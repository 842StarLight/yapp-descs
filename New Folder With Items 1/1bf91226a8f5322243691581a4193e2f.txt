<b>Your Impact:</b><br>
------------
<br><br>
Handshake is looking for a Salesforce Administrator to support our rapidly growing revenue team. You will report directly to the Sr. Director of Sales Operations &amp; Enablement, on the Employer Team here in San Francisco, CA. In this role you will become an expert in how our company operates and how we leverage our core sales and marketing systems to create efficient processes across our revenue teams. This includes improving, operationalizing, and adding both data and technologies to Handshake's existing sales infrastructure. You will have opportunities to work alongside Data Analysts, Product &amp; Engineering, Marketing, Finance, Security and Customer Success to build efficient and scalable solutions across the entire company. You will also assist with the procurement, implementation, management, automation, and roll out of new systems and enterprise software for the sales organization. Ultimately, you're responsible for ensuring that Handshake's rapidly growing revenue team has the right tools they need to succeed.
<br><br>
---------
<br>Your Role
<br>---------
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Daily administration and support of Salesforce.com custom objects, fields, record types, workflow rules, process builders, customized views and validation rules</li>
<li>Work directly with our Sales and SDR team to improve their workflows and usage of Salesforce.com</li>
<li>Support our Sales architect on larger Salesforce projects (e.g. CPQ implementation)</li>
<li>Keep our Salesforce data clean and well organized</li>
<li>Provision new users, manage workflows and system configurations for our key sales systems like Salesloft, Clari, Discover.org and Troops</li>
<li>Assist, develop and maintain training materials and training curriculum</li>
</ul>
----------------
<br><br>
<b>Your Experience:</b><br>
----------------
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>1-3 years experience working in Salesforce Sales Cloud</li>
<li>Salesforce Administrator (ADM101) strongly desired</li>
<li>Used SQL, data loaders or other integration/exporting tools in a business environment</li>
<li>Experience operating in a high-growth business environment like Handshake</li>
<li>Strong collaboration skills as you're going to work alongside colleagues from Sales, Customer Success, Sales Development and Marketing</li>
<li>You have a love for new technology and are on top of the latest trends regarding enterprise sales solutions</li>
<li>You have strong empathy for sales reps</li>
<li>Experience working with Sales Loft is a plus</li>
</ul>
