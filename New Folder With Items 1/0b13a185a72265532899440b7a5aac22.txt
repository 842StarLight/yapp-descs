<h2>Marketing Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Overview:</b><br>
Myriad Autoimmune seeks an experienced Marketing Manager (Digital) to function as a key member of the marketing team. The ideal candidate would come to understand the key product and therapeutic area information and drive content development for digital marketing campaigns. Plan and manage directly or via agency the web, SEO/SEM, marketing databases, email, social media, and display advertising campaigns for patient and health care provider initiatives. Maintain our social media presence. Measure and report performance of all digital marketing campaigns, and assess against goals (ROI and KPIs). Identify trends and insights, and optimize spend and performance based on the insights. Utilize strong analytical ability to evaluate customer engagement and experience across multiple channels and customer touch points. Develop and optimize user funnels. Brainstorm new and creative growth strategies. Plan, execute, and measure conversion rates. Evaluate emerging technologies. Provide thought leadership and perspective for adoption where appropriate. Collaborate with and manage vendor partners. A portion of the product managers' role may require some support of the patient marketing effort beyond digital. Develop a collaborative relationship with various departments including Sales, National Rheumatology Science Liaisons (NRSLs), Medical Affairs, and. The Associate Product Manager will report directly to the Director, Product Marketing. This position requires up to 10% travel annually.
