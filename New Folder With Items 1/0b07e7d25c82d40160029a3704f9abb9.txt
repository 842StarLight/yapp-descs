<h2>Vice President, Client Services</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>SUMMARY</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>The Vice President, Client Service provides strategic leadership, direction and support to the Impact XM Account Director team and is directly responsible for the performance and results of the team. This role supports the nurturing and growth of existing client base - at all revenue levels. Ultimately responsible for developing and implementing strategic sales action plans that will promote sales growth, achieve customer satisfaction and satisfy or exceed the stated goals of the organization. Reporting to the Senior Vice President, Client Development, the Vice President, Client Service has over 10 years of direct industry experience.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities </b>include but are not limited to the following. Other duties may be assigned. The duties and responsibilities as outlined in this Job Description may be changed from time to time by the Corporation, as it deems necessary, without further notice to the Employee.</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Work with Account Directors to create and manage a client account plan that ensures client revenue spent with Impact XM drives toward client objectives and growth for the account</li>
<li>Responsible for all aspects of team forecasting – team, individual, by account</li>
<li>Ultimately responsible for contract negotiations and project margins</li>
<li>Ensure assigned clients are on a master services agreement (MSA’s) or multiyear agreement using our services to their full benefit (new divisions, new product categories, new marketing channels).</li>
<li>Direct the implementation and execution of new policies and practices.</li>
<li>Develop and implement strategic plans to accommodate company goals for growth</li>
<li>Lead or support the creation and execution of Impact XM presentations for existing clientele</li>
<li>Acquaint clients and facilitate relationships with their personal Client Services Team</li>
<li>Meet with team members as needed to review account plan</li>
<li>Report on performance, variances, team updates, client updates, and initiatives</li>
<li>Conduct Performance Reviews for all team members in accordance with company policy</li>
<li>Approves all Account Director travel requests, expense reports and vacation requests</li>
<li>Adhere to all Impact XM processes and policies accurately and efficiently</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Supervisory Responsibilities
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>Directly supervises 6-15 employees in the Client Development Department. Carries out supervisory responsibilities in accordance with the organization's policies and applicable laws. Responsibilities include interviewing, hiring, and training employees; planning, assigning, and directing work; appraising performance; rewarding and disciplining employees; addressing complaints and resolving problems.</p>
