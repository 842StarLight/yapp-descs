<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Introduction</b></h2>
<p>ASML brings together the most creative minds in science and technology to develop lithography machines that are key to producing faster, cheaper, more energy-efficient microchips. We design, develop, integrate, market and service these advanced machines, which enable our customers - the world’s leading chipmakers - to reduce the size and increase the functionality of their microchips, which in turn leads to smaller, more powerful consumer electronics. Our main headquarters are located in Veldhoven, the Netherlands, and we also have 18 office locations around the United States- including main offices in Wilton, CT, Chandler, AZ, San Jose, CA and San Diego, CA.</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Mission</b></h2>
<p>ASML is seeking an experienced data analysis engineer with expertise in processing of optical images for metrology process development of ultra-high precision flat optics to join the Optical Sensor and Metrology Department in Wilton, Connecticut. The main job function is to develop, improve and integrate image processing, correction algorithms and deliver software packages in MATLAB for engineering and manufacturing teams to solve challenging engineering problems associated with achieving nanometer scale precision.
</p>
<p>ASML is a world leader in the manufacture of advanced technology systems for the semiconductor industry.ASML’s corporate headquarters is in Veldhoven, the Netherlands. Manufacturing sites and research and development facilities are located in Connecticut, California and the Netherlands. Technology development centers and training facilities are located in the United States, Japan, Korea, the Netherlands, and Taiwan. Additionally, ASML provides optimal service to its customers via over 50 sales and service organizations in 16 countries. Founded in the Netherlands in 1984, the company is publicly traded on Euronext Amsterdam and NASDAQ under the symbol ASML.</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Description</b></h2>
<div>The main focus of this position will be:<br>
<ul>
<li>Develop / improve image processing algorithm to extract nm flatness information from interferometer data.</li>
<li>Integrate algorithm from developers in to image processingsoftware package for analysis and process development cycles for engineering and manufacturing users.</li>
<li>Maintain Version controlled software package for multiple product generations.</li>
<li>Perform software testing to identify application, algorithmand software bugs.</li>
<li>Validate/ verify / regression test software to ensure it meets the business and technical requirements.</li>
<li>Develop &amp; execute roadmap of analysis, software, and systems, to mitigate product &amp; process risk and prevent software performance issues.</li>
<li>Lead the design team in software analysis tool development to find solutions to difficult technical problems in an efficient manner.</li>
<li>Develop/ maintain /improvedatabase structures and capabilities.</li>
<li>Write Matlab scripts to search analyze and plot data from Database.</li>
<li>Support query code to interrogate data for manufacturing and engineering needs.</li>
<li>Support image analysis on data and derive conclusions.</li>
<li>Some travel ( ~ 10%) to Europe, Asia, and within the US can be expected.</li>
</ul>
</div>
