<p><b>Position Summary: </b><br>Determine and implement accounting procedures and methods and ensure that data is recorded and allocated correctly. Assist in a variety of monthly closing and financial reporting tasks including the preparation and posting of journal entries and preparation of account reconciliations. Assist in compilation of process documentation.</p>
<p><b>Primary Duties and Responsibilities: </b><br>To perform this position successfully, an individual must fulfill the knowledge, skill, and/or ability requirements listed below.</p>
<p>1. Compilation, analysis, reconciliation, review, recording, and reporting a large assortment of financial data to assist communication with divisional personnel to support and provide accurate and relevant integral financial reporting in an efficient and timely manner.<br>2. Reconciliation and analysis of general ledger accounts, posting general ledger journal entries and document imaging of journal entry substantiation for retention.<br>3. Prepare internal financial reporting including monthly financial statement compilation, sales revenue, labor analysis, and inventory trends for management review.<br>4. Examine, review, compile and maintain records and develop estimates for the annual plan including estimates of changes for the following year as well as assist in the development and implementation of organizational and departmental financial planning, budgeting, cost management and performance evaluation programs.<br>5. Complete required regulatory and compliance business filings and tax reporting in a timely manner.<br>6. Assist in maintenance of sales tax tables in ERP system in accordance with jurisdiction rate changes in a timely manner.<br>7. Research and propose issue resolution using root-cause analysis.<br>8. Gather information requested by internal and external auditors during quarterly reviews, taxation jurisdiction audits and financial year-end audits.<br>9. Participate in rotation of duties among team staff accountants to support cross-training initiative.<br>10. Prepare estimates for new products and product changes.<br>11. Review inventory cycle count reports for variances and monitor inventory levels for unusual activity and errors.<br>12. Reconcile perpetual inventory records to general ledger inventory balances on a monthly basis and prepare the monthly inventory turns report.<br>13. Assist in all general ledger transaction postings, standard journal entries and account reconciliations.<br>14. Act as a resource to operations regarding accounting issues, reporting and analysis as requested by operations management.</p>
