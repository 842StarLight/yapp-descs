<h2>Business Development Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>We’re looking for the right person to join our team who’s experienced in selling software solutions to the commercial real estate industry. RealDash is an all-in-one software platform that streamlines the deal process for commercial real estate professionals. Our easy-to-use platform increases productivity for individuals and teams by combining powerful features all in one place.</p>
<p>
As a Business Development Director, your objective is to strategically grow our sales pipeline and close deals with brokerages, big and small.</p>
<p>
The market for CRE tech is growing fast. If you’re motivated, smart, and a proven deal maker, then this opportunity may be for you.</p>
<p>
Your responsibilities and duties will include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Drive and scale outbound campaigns with our marketing teams.</li>
<li>Build and track a pipeline using our CRM.</li>
<li>Meet / pitch potential new clients.</li>
<li>Participate in community events.</li>
<li>Respond to inbound leads.</li>
<li>Give online demonstrations.</li>
<li>Participate in sales calls.</li>
<li>Prepare proposals.</li>
<li>Negotiate contracts and licensing.</li>
</ul>
<p>Some of the qualifications and skills we are looking for:</p>
<ul>
<li>At least 5 years selling tech services and/or products in the commercial real estate industry.</li>
<li>Understanding of the digital / tech landscape.</li>
<li>Track record of performance. Must be able to verify through references.</li>
<li>Have existing relationships in the commercial real estate industry.</li>
<li>Experience interfacing with C-Level decision makers.</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills essential for conducting business in the tech space.</li>
</ul>
