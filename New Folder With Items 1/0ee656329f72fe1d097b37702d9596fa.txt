<h2>Product Manager - Instrument</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div><div><div>Our Menlo Park, CA based start-up is looking to hire a Product Manager for a Direct Hire Opportunity.<br>
<br>
They are looking for a Product Manager with experience in Hardware, Software and Instrumentation<br>
<br>
Responsibilities:<br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Prepare an integrated product development plan that includes all components of the platform: instrument, software and reagents.</li>
<li>Collect voice-of-customer input and perform market research to define product requirements, competitive positioning and go-to-market strategies.</li>
<li>Work collaboratively with hardware, software and reagent teams in the product development process and long-term product roadmap for the platform.</li>
<li>Develop content for marketing collateral and work closely with sales, technical support and service to ensure messaging is accurate and effective.</li>
<li>Provide ongoing product sustaining efforts post launch including continual improvements to entire platform and determine upgrade paths.</li>
<li>Prepare and maintain unit forecasting and pricing models.</li>
<li>Continually monitor target market and competition in order to address threats and opportunities with updated strategy and messaging.</li>
<li>Establish and maintain key opinion leader relationships to ensure successful adoption of products and strong advocacy in the market.</li>
<li>Communicate with board members, executives, senior managers and frontline across all company functions.</li>
<li>Interview, hire and manage consultants and employees required to meet product objectives.</li>
</ul>
<br>
Requirements:<br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>10 years’ experience in scientific instrument product management.</li>
<li>PhD or B.S. plus 5 years’ experience with study or research in immunology, oncology or related life science field.</li>
<li>Acumen with complex hardware, software and reagents systems, preferably involving tissue imaging.</li>
<li>Proven success defining product requirements and working with internal cross-functional teams to develop commercial solutions meeting needs of customers/market.</li>
<li>Ability to lead, train, motivate, and work effectively within a team.</li>
<li>Previous launch of instrument platform and customer-facing experience.</li>
<li>Strong business skills including ability to identify new accessible applications/markets to derive additional sources of revenue.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to work in fast-paced, dynamic startup environment.</li>
<li>Ability to travel up to 25%.
</li>
</ul>
