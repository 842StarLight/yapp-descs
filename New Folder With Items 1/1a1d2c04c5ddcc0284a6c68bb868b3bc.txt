<h2>Full Charge Bookkeeper</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Who Are We?</p>
<p>Petra &amp; Holum is a Chicago-based 85-year-old company providing outstanding and award-winning service, design and manufacturing of cutting-edge custom presentation packaging. Our reputation allows us to service clients such as NBC, Nissan, Kohler, Mahindra, Loreal and Disney.</p>
<p>The Candidate</p>
<p>We are looking for a bright, detail oriented, full-charge Bookkeeper to join our team! The ideal candidate should possess exceptional financial, computer, organizational and time management skills with the ability to work independently as well as in a team environment. It is essential to have the ability to work within a fast-paced environment hitting tight deadlines. Attention to detail and high accuracy is critical. Familiarity with Visual software is preferred.</p>
<p>The Role</p>
<p>Job duties include working closely with our CEO and outside accounting firm to assume primary responsibility for maintaining the Company general ledger. Additional duties include recording and paying vendor invoices, generating accounts receivable and sales reports, filing sales/use tax returns, other compliance tasks and payroll, banking and cash management.</p>
<p>Responsibilities</p>
<ul>
<li>Record day to day financial transactions and ensure a complete monthly closing process.</li>
<li>Perform certain monthly closing account reconciliations.</li>
<li>Record and pay expense and commission reports, per diems, credit card charges, etc.</li>
<li>Prepare selected sales/use tax forms</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Requirements</li>
<li>3-5 years of full charge bookkeeping experience</li>
<li>Minimum Associate degree or equivalent education in Accounting or Finance</li>
<li>Solid understanding of recording cash receipts/application to open customer receivables, entering and paying vendor invoices, recording payroll (summarized) entries and bank reconciliations</li>
<li>Hands on experience with MS Office 365 products</li>
<li>Clear communication skill/proficiency in English.</li>
</ul>
