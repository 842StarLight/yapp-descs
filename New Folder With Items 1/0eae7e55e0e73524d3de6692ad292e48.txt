<div><b>
Job Description:</b></div>
<div>
The Salesforce Account Executive will be responsible for working with pre existing internal relationships at Salesforce. The goal of our company is to deliver stellar results to clients in a timely fashion. Our ideal candidate is a creative problem-solver who thinks outside the box, willing to take on any challenge, with the ability to scope a project and write the Statement of Work (SOW). Office culture is extremely important, so you must love what you do, drive results, and be fun to work with.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
You will be working with clients, Salesforce Account Executives, and internal GSD team members to provide suggested solutions after thoroughly analyzing and understanding the business requirements of new clients.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Responsibilities Include:</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Develop and leverage internal relationships with Salesforce AEs and AVPs</li>
<li>
Meet and exceed targeted sales goals of &gt;$1.2mm per year</li>
<li>
Produce and consistently manage a healthy pipeline with accurate next steps and close dates</li>
<li>
Create and Generate SOWs to accurately encompass projects</li>
<li>
Abide by GSD Company scoping methodology to uncover business processes, user stories, and use cases for Salesforce</li>
<li>
Meet and exceed monthly and annual revenue targets</li>
<li>
Build and maintain relationships with Salesforce employees</li>
<li>
Remain responsible for lead and pipeline generation targets</li>
<li>
Travel when required to your territory and visiting key salesforce contacts and customers.</li>
<li>
</li>
</ul>

Basic Qualifications:</b></div>

3 Years experience full life cycle in B2B SAAS or Software Services sales</li>
<li>
1 year experience knowledge of salesforce as a super user.</li>
<li>
1 year experience delivering comprehensive proposals to various audiences.</li>
</ul>
