<p><b>
SUMMARY</b></p>
<p>
The Enterprise Triage &amp; Development team is responsible for all MasterControl software after release. We manage all Triage and Escalation projects, along with customer and production defects. We are the lifeline between our customers and our software.
</p>
<p>The ETD Software Engineer is integral to and active throughout the entire software lifecycle. They actively communicate with any and all affected parties product managers, product owners, scrum masters, developers, sales, services, managers, and customers to assure that the software is being built correctly, and that the right solution is being created for the customer. They design, develop, test, debug, troubleshoot and maintain MasterControl software applications, applying principles and techniques of computer science, engineering and mathematical analysis. Normally receives minimal instruction on routine work, general instructions on new assignments. Considered skilled and proficient in discipline. Works under minimal supervision with wide latitude for independent judgment. Work is generally independent and collaborative in nature. Creative minds and superb organization are important traits for ETD Software Engineers to possess. They should be analytical and methodical individuals who enjoy problem solving and challenges. The candidate will need to understand business and technical requirements and translate this into reporting designs and deliverables. The position also requires supporting the SDLC process in terms of testing, deployment, and post production support.</p>
