<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilties</b></h2>
<b>
Job Description</b><br>
<p>At Wells Fargo, we want to satisfy our customers’ financial needs and help them succeed financially. We’re looking for talented people who will put our customers at the center of everything we do. Join our diverse and inclusive team where you’ll feel valued and inspired to contribute your unique skills and experience.</p>
<p>
Help us build a better Wells Fargo. It all begins with outstanding talent. It all begins with you.</p>
<p><b>
Wells Fargo Technology</b> sets IT strategy; enhances the design, development, and operations of our systems; optimizes the Wells Fargo infrastructure footprint; provides information security; and enables continuous banking access through in-store, online, ATM, and other channels to Wells Fargo’s more than 70 million global customers.</p>
<p>
The Enterprise Contact Technology team delivers solutions that allow customers to perform self-service functions through Interactive Voice Response and Chat solutions or directly interact with customer service and sales agents to meet their financial needs. This Sr. Apps Systems Engineer role will be responsible for overseeing End to End testing approach across all inter-connected contact center applications, and participating in automation design guidance and consultation, utilizing a thorough understanding of applicable technology, tools and existing designs. This role will influence test environment installation and maintenance to match production and integrations. This role will champion the utilization of enabling technologies that will help drive continuous automation, continuous integration, and continuous delivery to complement an agile release delivery model. Span of awareness and influence will be across all applications in the contact center area, including Carrier routing, Interactive Voice Response, Customer Interaction Routing and Soft phone integration (inbound/outbound), Interaction Recording and Analytics, passive and active fraud detection, Workforce Management, Operational Reporting and Data Marts, Collaborative services (Chat, Co-Browse), and Customer Relationship Management systems.</p>
<p>
The ideal candidate will have will have strong leadership skills and experience, significant experience in development and testing methodologies, processes, tools. Technical knowledge across numerous technologies (e.g., Web/Mobile, Web Services, Cloud, Java / Selenium, .NET, Empirix, Nuance, Genesys, , Cisco, CA DevTest, Jenkins, etc.) is critical, with emphasis on systems integration development and testing. Strong need for Test Automation and/or software development experience is desired to drive the transformation of Development and QA teams toward test automation with continuous integration/continuous delivery to better enable agile project delivery. The candidate should be able to demonstrate a strong understanding of server infrastructure and application/data integration between Enterprise-scale systems. The selected candidate will work closely with the application development, line of business, other interfacing technology teams, performance testing, and regression automation teams to meet the business objectives and goals of the department.
</p>
<p><b>Key Responsibilities include:</b>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Serve as a primary escalation point for testing support/troubleshooting for less experienced staff and the overall platform.</li>
<li>Provide oversight and participate in overall project/systems testing, code automated test cases and maintenance of existing test cases, conduct code reviews, peer reviews, setup and maintain test data, metrics reporting to overall platform leads and managers to ensure platform release changes are deployed into production with high quality and defect free.</li>
<li>Work closely with application development leads/managers, QA leads/managers from other areas, and business groups to ensure successful collaboration.</li>
<li>Application Platform expertise to support Failure Analysis, Regression Failures analysis for every release, and support throughout the entire application lifecycle.</li>
<li>Deferred Defect Review and Dispositioning (Release/Regression).</li>
<li>Final Test Release Status Report.</li>
<li>Ensure the TC status is changed in QC/ALM to Regression (Manual and Automated Regression).</li>
<li>Post Release Audit - Project Artifacts, Regression Handoff, test case data quality.</li>
<li>Project Dashboard quality &amp; Enterprise Test Methodology/policy adherence.</li>
<li>Attend Key Learning Meetings hosted by Release Management (For release).</li>
<li>Root Cause Analysis/Key Learning for fallout items (RCA Dashboards).</li>
<li>Utilize tech enablers, shift left concepts to align with overall test strategy.</li>
<li>Risk assessment to the business across all projects, Maintenance Releases, etc. that impact the current release.</li>
</ul>
