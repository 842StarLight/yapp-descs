<h2>Store Team Member</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Come work at a place where we take pride in creating a workplace environment that values hard work, commitment, and growth.</p>
<p></p>
At Academy Sports + Outdoors, our Purpose is to make it easier for everyone to enjoy more sports and outdoors. Our Store Team Members are an essential part of customer service in any Academy store. He/she is responsible for ensuring a fast, friendly customer service, to be able to navigate the abundance of choices Academy has to offer, in addition to impacting customer satisfaction, store profitability, and Academy’s Mission.<p></p>
<p>
The application process will require completion of a video introduction through the HireVue Digital Assessment that will allow you to share more than what is presented on your resume or application. We want to give you the opportunity to express your passion for sports and outdoors, and helping our customers. Follow these steps to complete your application:</p>
<p></p>
<p>
1. Complete online application, you will receive an email from Academy confirming submission.</p>
<p>
2. Once your online application is complete, you will receive an email from HireVue with instructions to complete the HireVue Digital Assessment</p>
<p></p>
<p>
Should your background and experience align with the qualifications for the position, you may be contacted by a member of store leadership.</p>
