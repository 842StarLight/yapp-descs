<h2>Planner</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Position Overview</b>:</p>
<p>
The Planner will have the ability to strategically manage and coordinate the Planning function with Quality, Materials, Engineering, Manufacturing, Financial, and Sales functions. She/He will be creating manufacturing plans using thorough understanding manufacturing practices, opportunities, and limitations to determine the most efficient methods for production.</p>
<p><b>
SHIFT</b>: 07:00 a.m. – 3:30 p.m. (Mon-Fri)</p>
<p><b>
Duties and Responsibilities</b>:</p>
<ul><li>Investigates and resolves technical problems relating to assembly operations</li></ul>
<ul><li>Generates work instructions from engineering drawings and specifications</li></ul>
<ul><li>Participates in the implementation of lean practices</li></ul>
<ul><li>Implements continuous improvement opportunities</li></ul>
<ul><li>Adheres to all workplace safety guidelines, policies, and laws determined by the company, state, and federal authorities</li></ul>
<ul><li>Performs other duties as assigned</li></ul>
<p><b>
Required Attributes</b>:</p>
<ul><li>Proven ability to multi-task and prioritize activities within an environment of fast-paced periods of competing deadlines and fluctuating workload</li></ul>
<ul><li>Team player with a can-do attitude</li></ul>
<p><b>
Preferred Education, Skills, and Experience</b>:</p>
<ul><li>Two to four years’ experience in aerospace manufacturing or similar area with specific experience in lean manufacturing, quality control, inventory control, general manufacturing, production planning software and ERP systems</li></ul>
<ul><li>Familiarity with ISO9000 and AS9100</li></ul>
<ul><li>Knowledge of customer RFQ document types and languages</li></ul>
<ul><li>Extensive knowledge of various materials, hardware, and tooling as well as machining, assembly, heat treat testing, and related outside processes</li></ul>
<ul><li>Knowledge blueprint reading and interpretation – including Catia and Enovia</li></ul>
<ul><li>Excellent communication and presentation skills necessary to converse with inter-departmental staff, including senior management</li></ul>
<ul><li>Proficiency with Microsoft applications and MRP systems</li></ul>
<p><b>
Physical Demands and Work Environment</b>: The physical demands and work environment characteristics described here are representative of this job. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</p>
<ul><li>Working conditions are normal for that of a manufacturing/machine shop/office environment</li></ul>
