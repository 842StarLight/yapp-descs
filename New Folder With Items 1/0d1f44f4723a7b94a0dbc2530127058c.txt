<p><b>Job Description

<p>The Regional Sales Director - Customer Base Large Enterprise manages a team of senior Large Enterprise and Majors Account Executives in the Greater Metro NYC area. This opportunity is focused on leading, coaching and mentoring Senior Account Executives that are cross selling and up selling into existing Workday customers.
