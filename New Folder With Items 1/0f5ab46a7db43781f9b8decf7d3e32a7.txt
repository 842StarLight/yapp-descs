<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Cyber Risk Consultant</b></h3>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Tysons Corner, VA</b></h4>
<p>
Nehemiah Security is in search of an experienced Business Risk Consultant to help us support pre and post sales implementation of its security product, Risk Quantifier (RQ). The Business Risk Consultant will report to the Director of Client Services. Nehemiah desires a consultative person with a deep business understanding that thrives on managing customer engagements from beginning to end. You will be responsible for translating business objectives into an action plan and executing it across technical and business stakeholders.</p>
<p>
The Business Risk Consultant is the face of Nehemiah Security throughout an engagement, so knowledge of the cybersecurity field is essential in this role. The person best suited for this role is highly motivated and energetic with excellent communication skills. This person aspires to solve problems in a dynamic environment while paying attention to detail and focusing on client satisfaction. This person must have the ability to communicate effectively with customers.</p>

<ul>

<li>Develop and communicate implementation plans to meet customer objectives.</li>
<li>Gather business input through research and/or guided interviews.</li>
<li>Prepare/Refine collateral to facilitate stakeholder understanding of engagement process.</li>
<li>Configure and operate the RQ product.</li>
</ul>
