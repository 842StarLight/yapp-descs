<div><b>
Summary of the Position:</b></div>
<p>
The Buyer position plans and purchases MRO supplies (maintenance, repair &amp; operating supplies), packaging, buy-resell products, component parts, and outside services. This position is responsible for the timely planning, scheduling of receipt, and inventory control of the products as assigned.</p>
<div>
The duties performed require a knowledge of and ability to apply purchasing procedures and policies. The job requires considerable contact with various company personnel and suppliers.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>
Key Deliverables:</b></div>
<div>
1. Maintain safe and injury-free work place</div>
<div>
2. Generate cost savings ideas and completion of projects</div>
<div>
3. Generate accurate and timely request for quotes</div>
<div>
4. Generate accurate and timely purchase / credit card orders</div>
<div>
5. Achieve first-time supplier delivery acceptance</div>
<div>
6. Evaluate supplier performance</div>
<div>
7. Resolve quality issues with suppliers</div>
<div>
8. Expedite and follow-up on open orders</div>
<div>
9. Resolve supplier invoice discrepancies</div>
<div>
10. Supervise buyer/planner position</div>
<div>
11. Compliance with International quality standard ISO 9001 / Environmental management standard ISO 14001</div>
<div>
12. Global purchasing procedures</div>

<div><b>
Key Responsibilities and Essential Functions:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for setting correct levels of purchased materials to support production schedules and customer orders. The Buyer must work closely with supply chain, sales and operations of such facilities. (25%)</li>
<li>Prepare and issue purchase orders in a timely manner to suppliers. (20%)</li>
<li>Identify and negotiate opportunities for cost, process and supplier performance improvements. (15%)</li>
<li>Develop/Identify alternative sources of supply. (10%)</li>
<li>Track and follow-up on open purchase orders. (10%)</li>
 <li>Develop/Identify alternative sources of supply. (5%)</li>
 <li>Work with management to establish and adhere to global purchasing procedures and objectives (4%)</li>
<li>Prepare and issue Request for Quotes. Analyze quotes, determine best option and negotiate. (4%)</li>
<li>Promptly resolve supplier invoice discrepancies. (3%)</li>
<li>Evaluate supplier performance for quality, delivery and commercial value. (2%)</li>
<li>Promptly resolve quality issues with suppliers. (2%)</li>
 <li>Other duties may be assigned.</li>
<br>
</ul>

 <li>Analytical with a proactive “Hands On” approach to problem solving and continuous improvement.</li>
