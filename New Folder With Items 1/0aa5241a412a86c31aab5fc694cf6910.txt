<h2>Customer Service Rep - TMS</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Description:</b><br>
The Marine Corps Association &amp; Foundation (MCA&amp;F) is the professional Association for all US Marines. Founded in 1913, MCA&amp;F supports professional development among today’s Marines, recognizes outstanding accomplishments and serves to remind all of the rich history and legacy of the USMC. MCA&amp;F owns and operates The MARINE Shop (TMS) in Quantico, VA. TMS reflects the quality and value of the professional association by providing uniform items, books, gifts and apparel that represent a uniquely professional shopping experience.
<br><br>
Summary of Position
<br>TMS is seeking to fill a part-time CSR position (20-30 hours/per week) to assist the Assistant Store Manager with day-to-day operations by delivering exceptional customer service. The CSR provides customers with information about products and services, processes sales orders using the point-of-sale (POS) software, processes returns and assists in the fitting and sale of custom tailored uniforms. .
<br><br>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
Essential Duties &amp; Responsibilities
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Greet customers both in person and over the phone in a polite and professional manner</li>
<li>Address all questions concerning price, availability, and use of merchandise</li>
<li>Process all sales transactions through the POS software cash, check, credit card or gift card</li>
<li>Assist master tailor(s) while they are custom fitting uniforms and be familiar with use of control cards and the process and procedure of fitting custom tailored uniforms</li>
<li>Assist customers with selecting other uniform articles and accessories that complement planned purchases and are in alignment with the customers’ needs</li>
<li>Be familiar with all special services offered (medal mounting, engraving, calling/business cards, name tapes, dog tags, custom coins, and sword repair)</li>
<li>Assist in receiving, checking in, and putting away weekly orders from the MCA&amp;F warehouse</li>
<li>Restock sections or areas of the store as assigned by management</li>
<li>Conduct inventory cycle counts as directed by the General Manager or Assistant Manager</li>
<li>Process MCA&amp;F membership requests for customers through the POS system/updates and Association Management System</li>
<li>Answer questions, provide status updates, pack and ship mail order uniform packages</li>
<li>Provide solutions related to damaged/defective, missing or in accurate products or services</li>
<li>Other duties as may be assigned by management</li>
</ul>
