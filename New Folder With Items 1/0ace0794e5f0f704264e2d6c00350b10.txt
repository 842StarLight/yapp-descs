Access &amp; Pricing Strategy (APS)
<br><br>
<b>How We Do It :</b> Ensuring pricing, contracting, and customer partnership excellence that sustains profitability and research investment
<br><br>
Key Responsibilities
<br>Develop and manage GNE product pricing strategies that support product access while maintaining profitability
<br>Meet customer needs while driving net revenue for Genentech
<br>Create innovative ideas and approaches to address customer challenges and build strong, strategic relationships
<br>Increase Genentech value proposition to complex customers
<br>Provide account-specific tools, programs, and/or initiatives for the Genentech portfolio
<br>Proactively monitor the competitive and policy environment to support the future health of the portfolio
<br>Develop novel pricing strategy and customer partnership approaches that best position Genentech for the future landscape
<br>Provide an integrated (marketplace trends, cross-portfolio, cross-account) point of view on all topics related to access
<br>Identify and synthesize access-related market trends to be leveraged for product strategies, to include potential implications, risks, and benefits
<br>Provide cross-portfolio, cross-enterprise point of view for all access-related issues
<br>Develop comprehensive, thoughtful contract analytics that outline the financials for account-specific contract decisions
<br>Partner with internally to create account-specific strategic approaches and partnerships
