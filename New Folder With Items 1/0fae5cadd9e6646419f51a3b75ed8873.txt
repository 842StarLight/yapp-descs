<div><b>Enterprise Sales Executive</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
We are looking for a high-energy individual with an entrepreneurial spirit and proven<br>
track record of successful selling in the marketing technology &amp; automation space to<br>
join our team as an Enterprise Sales Executive.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Key Responsibilities</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Manage a Sales territory with an associated sales quota.
</li>
<li>Create a sales strategy and market plans to over-achieve your revenue target.</li>
<li>
With the help of our talented marketing team, you will uncover new leads to build a 4x pipeline to meet your target and orchestrate the sales cycle from inception to closure.</li>
<li>
Develop new corporate clients and establish long-term relationships with defined target accounts.
</li>
<li>Identify and develop new streams for long-term revenue growth and maintain relationships with customers to achieve repeat/referral businesses.</li>
<li>
Maintain accuracy of forecasts in the CRM system and provide sales forecasts in a timely manner.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Pre-Requisites</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
You are a "solution-selling" sales hunter and a self-starter in the marketing automation space with 8+ years of experience selling to C-level into large Enterprise customers.
</li>
<li>You should have closed multi-million dollars deals in the past 3 years.
</li>
<li>Have a record of over-achieving your revenue targets in software and services.</li>
<li>
Have a rolodex of contacts at the C-level within large Enterprise accounts.</li>
<li>
Should have great presentation &amp; negotiation skills and be a phenomenal communicator</li>
<li>
Regional travel is required for this role (approx. 50%).</li>
<li>
Ad agency experience will be an added advantage.</li>
<li>
Must be a great team player</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Experience and Academic Qualifications</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
8+ years of experience selling to C-level into large Enterprise customers</li>
<li>
Bachelor's Degree or higher</li>
</ul>
