<h2>Technical Support Professional II, SMB</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>
<div><b>THIS JOB PAYS $16 AN HOUR</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
THIS POSITION IS FOR OUR LEWISTON, MAINE LOCATION</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Respond to and troubleshoot incoming customer support requests via phone, chat or email</li>
<li>
Support Carbonite data protection software on PC’s, Servers and a wide variety of infrastructure</li>
<li>
Provide technical and non-technical product support to Carbonite customers</li>
<li>
Upselling and cross selling customers based off of their needs</li>
<li>
Achieve high levels of customer satisfaction</li>
<li>
Mitigate customer confusion and frustration through effective communications</li>
</ul>
<div>Prior technical support experience and aptitude for learning are important. However, the most successful Technical Support Professionals at Carbonite share exceptional customer support skills, strong work ethics, an extreme passion for technology and also have superb troubleshooting skills.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Specific Objectives and Responsibilities:</b></div>
<br>
<br>
<div><b>Your First Two Months in Training:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Successful completion of five week in office product/process training</li>
<ul>
<li>
Solve testing scenarios</li>
<li>
Complete daily content assessments
</li>
<li>Pass test calls and technical scenarios</li>
</ul>
<li>
Successful completion of two week in office ‘nesting’ period taking real customer calls</li>
<li>
Learn and execute documented processes to assist in issue resolution.</li>
<li>
Learn and demonstrate ability to execute Carbonite software troubleshooting steps</li>
<li>
Demonstrate proficiency with all support tools (SalesForce, CRM, Knowledge base)
</li>
<li>Learn and demonstrate ability to successfully execute sales techniques such as upselling, cross-selling and lead generation</li>
<li>
Answer open questions on tools and processes</li>
<li>
Demonstrate reliability and strong work ethic</li>
<li>
Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Three Months and Beyond – Post-Training:
</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Support company and partner’s enterprise cloud-based data backup and recovery offerings by working with our customers (consumer customers through IT professionals)
</li>
<li>Seek successful resolution of customer technical inquiries received through multiple communication channels</li>
<li>
Supports, logs, categorizes, prioritizes, and tracks incidents reported by customers</li>
<li>
Escalate cases to senior resources and follow documented escalation procedures</li>
<li>
Continued Sales excellence (upselling, cross-selling, lead generation)</li>
<li>
Assist in documenting new and known issues</li>
<li>
Collaborate with internal resources to improve processes</li>
<li>
Pursue additional company-funded Microsoft certifications</li>
<li>
Constant pursuit of technical education, proficiency and excellence</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to properly use all tools to process all Incidents</li>
<li>
Meet quality and customer satisfaction metrics
</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>

<div><b>
Key Challenges to Overcome:</b></div>
<br>
<div>
</div>
<div>In order to succeed the candidate will need to demonstrate the ability to learn new processes and server base products as they are presented. Vital to this role is maintaining a positive attitude in all interactions and ensuring customer issues are fully resolved. Daily activities and processes can be dynamic as are the needs of the customer. Being able to positively adopt to a changing environment is essential for success. A successful agent will be able to manage the various daily duties with a smile. Success at Carbonite is transparent as comprehensive metrics measure daily performance.</div>
<br>
<div>
</div>
<div><b>What will make you successful at Carbonite:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>A track record of good judgment and decision-making in positions with significant responsibility
</li>
<li>Highly autonomous and able to independently identify high value projects and drive them to completion</li>
<li>
Demonstrated leadership capability in cross-functional team environments</li>
<li>
High degree of comfort with complex technical environments</li>
<li>
Having and being able to articulate/defend an informed opinion on important topics</li>
</ul>
