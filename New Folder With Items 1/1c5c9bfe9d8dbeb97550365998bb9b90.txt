<h2>Field Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><b>The team:</b></div>
<p>
We are a fast-moving, analytically driven B2B marketing team. We show innovative companies how they can improve their applications and get to market faster. We do hundreds of events, thousands of marketing campaigns, research and evangelize ideas that move our industry forward, and more.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
The opportunity:</b></p>
<p>
Datadog is looking for a tenured Field Marketing Manager to design and build field marketing programs for our North America-West region. This role is a new position on Datadog’s field marketing team and will be a key player in fostering the growth of Datadog’s regional marketing practice.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
You will:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Contribute to the development and implementation of the field marketing strategy for the West region as part of the overall North America GTM strategy</li>
<li>Work closely with North America west coast sales teams to understand specific market priorities and inform and shape customer and prospect-facing programs contributing to pipeline and opportunity creation</li>
<li>Support field programs and events with a heavy content angle and work with relevant teams and customers to deliver in a live environment including executive breakfasts/dinners, meetups, customer-led panels, and partner events</li>
<li>Oversee promotion and outreach efforts pre/post event, including direct mail, email and cold calling</li>
<li>Track, measure and communicate program results, and seek to generate new global best practices through experimental efforts</li>
<li>Ensure programs and event details are on brand, on message, and fit the Datadog aesthetic</li>
<li>Support on larger events in the North America region such as AWS and GCP tradeshows, roadshows, summits, etc.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of five years of experience working in marketing, preferably in the B2B technology space</li>
<li>Equal parts creative thinker and result-driven</li>
<li>Ability to plan and deliver against hard deadlines</li>
<li>Consistent record of building successful partnerships with sales teams to craft programs that are results driven in their approach</li>
<li>Work independently, maintain strong attention to detail and take calculated risks all while juggling many projects</li>
<li>Clear and thoughtful communicator (verbal and written). Other foreign languages are a plus</li>
<li>Willingness to travel up to 50%</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
