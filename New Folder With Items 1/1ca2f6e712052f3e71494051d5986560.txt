<h2>Manufacturing Equipment Engineer (1st Shift)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Veeco (NASDAQ: VECO) is a leading manufacturer of innovative semiconductor process equipment. Our proven MOCVD, lithography, laser annealing, ion beam and single wafer etch &amp; clean technologies play an integral role in producing LEDs for solid-state lighting and displays, and in the fabrication of advanced semiconductor devices. With equipment designed to maximize performance, yield and cost of ownership, Veeco holds technology leadership positions in all these served markets.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>Overview:
</b></p>
<p>Ultratech a division of Veeco is hiring a Manufacturing Equipment Engineer with primary responsibilities to participate in the assembling of newly manufactured systems and sub systems on the manufacturing floor.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Integrate setup and test complex optical and electro-mechanical subsystems into a higher level system or process module
</li>
<li>Perform mechanical and software installations, and execute precision set-ups and adjustments following detailed work instructions
</li>
<li>Diagnose and repair system performance issues using high precision electrical, optical and mechanical test equipment
</li>
<li>Troubleshoot testing activity discrepancies, and design and conduct formal test plans
</li>
<li>Work with Development Engineering as required resolving diverse &amp; complex issues found during Final Test
</li>
<li>Work with Manufacturing Engineering and Assembly Test Technicians to resolve and close issues
</li>
<li>Manage all manufacturing test data associated with source acceptance testing of a process module
</li>
<li>Compile statistical reports for each system during Final Test
</li>
<li>Participate in Continuous Improvement. Identify and provide improvements of test methods for existing products and collaborate with Engineering to develop methodologies for new products/components<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications:
</b></p>
<ul><li>2-4 years of hands-on experience testing and troubleshooting complex systems<br>
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Knowledge, Skills and Abilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Knowledge of hardware, software and control systems
</li>
<li>SAP skills that range from simple inquiries to complex reports and transactions (MM, PP modules)
</li>
<li>Proficient with EXCEL and able to understand and apply statistical data analysis
</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal skills to interface with technicians, engineering, management, sales and marketing
</li>
<li>Willingness to work overtime, holidays and weekends as required to meet company revenue requirements
</li>
<li>Military experience is a plus<br>
</li>
</ul>
