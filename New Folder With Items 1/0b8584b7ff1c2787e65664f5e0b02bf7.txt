<b>Job Description:</b><br>

<br><br>
Cars.com ( http://cars.com/ ) has been featured as one of the top places to work in Chicago by The Chicago Tribune, Built in Chicago, Computer World and Chicago Inno, making us the premiere online destination connecting car shoppers to local automotive groups since 1998. We have prided ourselves on being an innovative tour-de-force within the automotive industry by offering top of the line digital media solutions to car shoppers and dealers alike through our expert content, mobile app features, millions of new and used vehicle listings, our comprehensive set of research tools and the largest database of consumer reviews in the industry.
<br><br>
Proudly standing as one of the largest digital automotive platforms on the market with our recent acquisition of DealerRater, Cars.com have experienced a lot of exciting now growth within our organization. In 2016 and 2017 alone, Cars.com ( http://cars.com/ ) moved into our brand new headquarters located in the South Loop of Chicago's business district, and spun off into a publicly traded entity on the New York Stock Exchange (NYSE). We can honestly say there has never been a better time to join the Cars.com ( http://cars.com/ ) family!
<br><br>
The Account Executive is responsible for developing and consulting clients within his/her market. This is a career position for a person who wants to contribute to the long-term success of a high growth and progressive company. Our business-to-business sales representatives are responsible for selling a value-based online advertising solution for automotive clients. This position will focus on growth of existing accounts, speak with decision makers, analyze their needs, and effectively deliver value-added solutions.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities include:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Retain and grow sales through upselling current customers, and relationship development.</li>
<li>Meeting with key decision makers and negotiate rates in defined territory.</li>
<li>Ensure customers have a detailed understanding of our products through a consultative selling approach.</li>
<li>Ensure current customer satisfaction by responding quickly and accurately to problems, concerns or needs.</li>
<li>Maintain a high level of customer service through training and customer support.</li>
<li>Develop relationships with dealers. Leverage these relationships to maximize revenue opportunities.</li>
<li>Proactively market the company to customers and convey the advantages of our services.</li>
<li>If requested, manage and monitor all contracts, as well as accounts receivables.</li>
<li>Collaborate with team members, managers and cross-functional departments when necessary.</li>
<li>Meet revenue and account retention goals.</li>
<li>Must live in market area while working out of home office and have a valid driver's license.</li>
</ul>
