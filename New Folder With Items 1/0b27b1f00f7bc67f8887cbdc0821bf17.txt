<h2>Network Installation Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Role Overview</p>
<p>Wilco is actively seeking dedicated &amp; talented network installation engineers to join its team that is creating &amp; deploying the next generation of smart building technology. As a network installation engineer, you will be responsible for designing, installing, testing, and maintaining Wilco smart access deployments.</p>
<p>Key Responsibilities</p>
<p>· Conduct site visits for network topography and smart access product offerings</p>
<p>· Prepare accurate technical network diagrams.</p>
<p>· Install reliable networks to include <b>WiFi,</b><b>ZWave</b>, <b>Zigbee</b>, <b>LoRa,</b>and more. Network equipment will include security gateways, network switches, mounting equipment, access points, hubs/gateways, security boxes, cabling (fiber &amp; Cat), and modems. Buildings will range from new development to retrofits/refurbs, and will range in type from garden style to high-rises.</p>
<p>· Identify and resolve device and network issues &amp; challenges.</p>
<p>· Conduct repairs and maintain preventative maintenance schedules for our sites.</p>
<p>· Monitor and manage networks at our sites. knowledge of infrastructure device installation, configuration and maintenance (i.e. firewalls, routers, switches)</p>
<p>· Answer any and all questions of the Sales team and/or client with regards to our technology, network design, supported devices, installation methods, installation/site activation timelines.</p>
<p>· Understand every key aspect of the software platforms’ features &amp; limitations, know every devices’ core functions, understand network deployment strategies for both new development projects &amp; retrofit projects, maintain relationships with our installation integrators, and advise the Sales Team to ensure clients receive the right solution for their needs.</p>
<p>· Provide excellent client service and be able to articulate technology and product positioning to both business and technical users. Be a technical resource for the sales team.</p>
<p>· Review site layouts, electrical diagrams, site images, and other site information to understand and advise others on the installation / site activation requirements.</p>
<p>· Conduct prequalifications &amp; site surveys in coordination with the Sales Engineering team as necessary.</p>
<p>· Actively drive and manage installation &amp; scalability methodology improvements.</p>
<p>· Completely own the network design &amp; installation process; ensure all aspects of a network activation are completed on-time, at a high quality. Consistently refine this process and own it from initial prospective client engagement to passing the fully installed &amp; activated site to the Welcome Team.</p>
<p>· Prioritize tasks well and synchronize with team, project management, the client, and the sales team.</p>
<p>· Be digitally competent; able to use/learn Slack, Email, Word, Excel, Google Sheets &amp; Docs, Adobe Acrobat, web forms, Salesforce, and other common applications.</p>
<p>· Love what you do &amp; be an integral part of our family that is forging a new path in IoT technology.</p>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<p>· <b>2+ years network installations.</b></p>
<p>· <b>Low voltage certified in at least one state.</b></p>
<p>· <b>Willing to be based out of / near Philadelphia. </b></p>
<p>· Knowledge of infrastructure device installation, configuration and maintenance (i.e. firewalls, routers, switches).</p>
<p>· Understanding of network connectivity requirements and troubleshooting.</p>
<p>· Capable of installing network Cat cabling or fiber in challenging retrofits. Able to conquer projects that other contractors would shy-away from.</p>
<p>· Excited to work in a growing company that always has a full project pipeline.</p>
<p>· Capable of performing with minimal supervision.</p>
<p>· Capable of engaging with clients, partners, distributors, integrators, and customers in a professional manner.</p>
<p>· Quick to learn new, complex systems and technologies.</p>
<p>· Excited to impact real people every day through your work.</p>
