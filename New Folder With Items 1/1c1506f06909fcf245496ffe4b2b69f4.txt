<h2>Analyst III Sourcing, Trends and Insights</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Retail Business Services, LLC</b>, is the services company of Ahold Delhaize USA, currently providing services to six East Coast grocery brands, including Food Lion, Giant Food, GIANT/MARTIN'S, Hannaford and Stop &amp; Shop, as well as the country’s largest online grocery retailer, Peapod.</p>
<p><b>Retail Business Services</b> leverages the scale of the local brands to drive synergies and provides industry-leading expertise, insights and analytics to local brands to support their strategies with services including Commercial Services and Strategy, Operations, Information Technology, Financial Services, Legal Services, Communications, Supply Chain and People Systems and Services.</p>
<p><b>Position Title: Analyst III Sourcing, Trends and Insights</b></p>
<p><b>Position Location: Salisbury, NC or Quincy, MA</b></p>
<p><b>Position Summary: </b></p>
<p>Lead all innovation, trend insights, market analyses, and merchandising insights for GM/Seasonal. Lead development of new items. Responsible for understanding competitive landscape, researching trends, researching/developing innovative products, and developing relevant merchandising recommendations.</p>
<p><b>Principle Duties and Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Create insightful topic-based reports that combine consumer data with secondary research on food and general merchandise industry trend and innovations to create a full picture of the market</li>
<li>Translate data (consumer, market, manufacturer) to actionable insights and forward-looking analyses</li>
<li>Research and identify key market trends, white space opportunities and analyze data holistically</li>
<li>Create trend, color, print/pattern, material story boards to visualize the themes for stake</li>
<li>Deliver visually compelling presentations of consumer data, trends and competitive analyses</li>
<li>Build strong relationships with a wide range of stakeholders, i.e. category, sourcing, private brands, vendors</li>
<li>Regular interactive and communication with key partners</li>
<li>Represent Sourcing team as trend and insights expert</li>
</ul>
<p>- Deliver consistent, accurate and efficient analysis data points.</p>
<p>- Assist in developing overall strategy for General Merchandise approach at each Great Local Brand.</p>
<p>- Complete market analyses and develop insights.</p>
<p>- Work with suppliers and internal/external resources to develop trend analyses and insights.</p>
<p>- Research and develop innovative products to recommend to Sourcing, Category, and Private Brands teams.</p>
<p>- Develop competitive intelligence and provide analyses and insights to Sourcing, Category, and Private Brands teams.</p>
<p>- Develop Merchandising strategies and recommendations to include category adjacencies, product planograms, etc.</p>
<p>- Develop understanding of the market for each Great Local Brand and provide analyses and insights to assist Sourcing, Category, and Private Brands teams with understanding performance within marketplace.</p>
<p>- Be primary interface with CM and CM+2 group</p>
<p>- Develop and support insights for top to top meetings and executive updates.</p>
<p><b>Basic Requirements: </b></p>
<p>- 4+ years experience in a relevant research, trend, consumer insights, branding or consulting role within food and general merchandise</p>
<p>- Bachelor’s Degree in related field or 8 years of equivalent experience</p>
<p><b>Skills and Abilities: </b></p>
<p>- Broad knowledge of retail marketplace and general merchandise</p>
<p>- Understanding of visual merchandising</p>
<p>- Collaboration</p>
<p>- Influencing</p>
<p>- Analysis</p>
<p>- Excellent writing ability</p>
<p>- Experience collaborating and communicating with cross-functional teams and internal stakeholders</p>
<p>- Ability to explain complicated concepts in a clear, concise manner</p>
<p>- A talent for seeing the patterns behind the data, understanding what these patterns mean from a business perspective</p>
<p>- Visually presenting data and translating it to the category</p>
<p>- Experience with sales data such as IRI or Nielsen</p>
<p>- Ability to analyze multiple data sets and write insightful, forward-looking analyses</p>
<p>- Ability to multi-task and handle the details while keeping an eye on the big picture</p>
<p>- Ability to travel</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
