<h2>Truss Designer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Description/Job Summary</b></p>
This description applies to the following Design positions: Design Trainee, Designer-Level 1, Designer-Level 2, Designer-Level 3; Senior Designer.<ul>
<li>
These associates must read architectural prints to create roof and floor truss placement layouts conforming to current 84Components design practices</li>
<li>
Design of roof and floor trusses in engineering software ensuring designs are accurate, optimized, and conforms to the current P&amp;P</li>
<li>
Specify all necessary supplied hardware</li>
<li>
Maintain complete and updated Job Tracking Sheet</li>
<li>
Send approval letter to customers according to P&amp;P procedures when necessary</li>
<li>
Provide sales and customer support when necessary</li>
<li>
Ensure office and plan are maintained in a neat and orderly fashion and all plans are logged properly</li>
<li>
Attend required meetings and participates in committees as requested by supervisor(s).</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Required Skills</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Computer Skills: Word Processing System, Design Software, Internet Software, Database Software</li>
<li>
Ability to read and comprehend simple instructions, short correspondence and memos; write simple correspondence; effectively present information to others one-on-one.</li>
<li>
Ability to apply common sense understanding to carry out instructions furnished in written, oral or diagram form; deal with problems involving several concrete variables in standardized situations.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Required Experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
General knowledge of construction industry. Knowledge of floor and roof truss design process</li>
<li>
Ability to read and interpret architectural and structural blueprints</li>
<li>
Ability to calculate tributary loads, concentrated loads, and load transfer through a building.</li>
<li>
Ability to operate a computer and current software used by company Knowledge of current local codes associated with truss design (i.e. TPI, UBC).</li>
<li>
Good written and oral communication skills are required.</li>
<li>
Ability to read, understand, and follow oral and written instructions.</li>
<li>
Ability to deal courteously with customers, co-workers, and construction personnel</li>
<li>
Good math skills including, but not limited to architectural units, fractions, geometry, and basic trigonometry.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Preferred Education</b></p>
Associate or 4-year degree and 2+ yrs. Experience in design.</div></div>
