<h2>First Business Hire: Sales</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>We are seeking our first business hire to focus on sales and business development (to act as a player now and a coach later). Ideally, we’re looking for someone with agency / consulting services and software sales experience. This position calls for a startup aficionado with experience taking new products to market and achieving company revenue goals.</p>
<p>As the first business hire, you will be responsible for helping the company build on its early success and find product-market fit and continue to hit revenue goals by targeting and converting new customers. Although you strive to meet and exceed overall company revenue milestones, you are inherently helpful and will always do what is necessary to help your customers be more successful.</p>
<p>BOS Framework is a revolutionary software development framework-as-a-service that enables developers to start on “Sprint 23” and get to market with unprecedented efficiency and with a product that seamlessly scales with the business. For non-technical founders, the BOS enablement team builds and operates the product until and transfers it in-house when the company is ready.</p>

<ul>
