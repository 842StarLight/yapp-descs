<b>POSITION SUMMARY</b><br>
The <b>Spectrum Telesales Representative</b> position is an Outbound Telemarketing Sales position within the <b>Spectrum </b>Residential team. Individuals in this position are responsible for understanding <b>Spectrum</b> Residential products and services (Internet, Voice &amp; Video) for the purpose of selling to new acquisition prospects and upselling existing <b>Spectrum </b>Residential customers via outbound calling in a call center environment.<br>
<br>
<b>MAJOR DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b><br>
Effectively sell Charter products and services to new and existing prospects<br>
Consistently achieve a monthly sales quota by adding new lines of service (Primary Service Units; PSUs).<br>
Meet established activity targets and key performance indicators, including outbound calls per day, follow up calls, quotes sent, sales, and connects, etc.<br>
Handle all prospects with courtesy and professionalism; Establish and maintain a high level of prospect satisfaction in all interactions.<br>
Act as a product consultant when promoting, recommending and selling the value of Spectrum Business products and services.<br>
Inform prospects of our product pricing model and Spectrum Business’ competitive advantage compared to other service providers.<br>
Effectively balance new lead generation versus follow up efforts. Create a strong follow up plan that will allow for greater efficiency and increased sales conversion rates.<br>
Ensure database information is accurate and complete.<br>
Perform other duties as requested.<br>
<br>
<b>REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS</b><br>
<b>Skills/Abilities and Knowledge</b><br>
Ability to communicate orally in a clear, concise and professional manner.<br>
Ability to listen and interpret the needs of the prospect.<br>
Ability to conduct a needs analysis (consultative approach), overcome objections, develop a strong sales pitch and effectively close sales.<br>
Ability to show proper judgement and initiative.<br>
Ability to manage multiple prospects, pay close attention to detail, respond quickly to changing business needs and prioritize and organize effectively even when volume and workload exceed expectations.<br>
Ability to effectively use and multi task with a personal computer; utilize job-specific software applications (e.g., CSG, ICOMS, Salesforce, survey software) and other office equipment such as telephone/dialer, headset, copier, fax and calculator.<br>
Must effectively work independently as well as with others<br>
Knowledge of commercial internet, video and data and telephone products and services.<br>
Proficient in MS Office and related software applications.<br>
