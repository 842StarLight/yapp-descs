<h2>Senior Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div><div><div><div>
<p>US - CareerBuilder</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>What’s The Role?</b></p>
<p>The CareerBuilder Sales team is looking for a skilled Senior Account Executive to join the CB family! The Senior Account Executive will act as a trusted advisor to clients by identifying and consulting with key decision makers and become a subject matter expert on human resources, recruitment landscape, and how the economy impacts today’s workforce.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Encourage clients to evaluate business needs and recruiting strategies in new ways. Constructively challenge client perceptions and the status quo.</li>
<li>Customer experience comes first. You must have an innate ability to engage, support, and service your customers.</li>
<li>Sales must be your passion. A competitive spirit and extreme focus to achieve and exceed sales goals is the CareerBuilder culture.</li>
<li>Each day your focus will be to identify opportunities, create the business case, and close the sale. You are able to own the entire sales lifecycle and have complete control over your success.</li>
<li>CareerBuilder’s salesforce are hunters! You must have the ability to pipeline development through a combination of cold calling, email campaigns, and industry knowledge in efforts to hunt new business.</li>
<li>You are able to articulate the value of new services to your existing client base and continue to expand the business with a set of nationwide accounts who currently partner with CareerBuilder.</li>
<li>With growth comes change, a good candidate must thrive on change while remaining highly organized, optimistic, and coachable.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Job Requirements:</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Minimum Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Experience with conceptual sales, solution selling and value-based selling</li>
<li>Able to self-motivate and work independently</li>
<li>High activity sales cycle for selling new solutions</li>
<li>Maintain a positive attitude and strong work ethic</li>
<li>Strong sense of initiative and desire to achieve</li>
<li>Excellent written, verbal, interpersonal communication, and organizational skills</li>
<li>Ability to effectively manage time, prioritize tasks and work within deadlines</li>
<li>Ability to travel up to 10%</li>
<li>3-5 years of relevant sales experience</li>
<li>Experience working named account lists</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Preferred Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Experience with B2B sales</li>
<li>Experience selling software or services</li>
<li>Experience selling to C-Suite buyers</li>
<li>Experience selling to HR buyers</li>
<li>Experience selling into a named account set of 1M+ annual spend with an average annual deal size between 25k-100K</li>
<li>Experience in multi-level relationships within accounts</li>
<br>
</ul>
