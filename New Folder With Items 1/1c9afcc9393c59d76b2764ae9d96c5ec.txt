<h2>Public Relations Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Job Overview:</b></p>
<p>Are you enthusiastic about all things sustainable? Interested in catalyzing the work of a company that has been on the cutting edge of sustainability for three decades, and is truly making a difference for people and the environment? We’re looking for an energetic Public Relations Manager to join our team and help grow our business.</p>
<p>The Public Relations Manager is responsible for planning and executing a multi-pronged PR strategy designed to build overall awareness of SCS Global Services, its thought leadership and innovations in the field of sustainability, and its many service offerings. This position will report to the Senior Vice President, and work closely with the sales and marketing, executive and program management teams.</p>
<p>The ideal candidate is a seasoned professional, preferably with experience on both the PR and journalism side – a superb writer who knows how to translate scientific and technical information into compelling PR content of all kinds, including one or several of the specific business areas in which we operate (see www.scsglobalservices.com). You are a self-starter and very hands on, and comfortable working with all kinds of digital, print and broadcast media channels. This is not a junior position. <b>Candidates without a cover letter will not be considered.</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Relevant Services/Schemes:</b></p>
<p>This position will provide PR support for company-wide programs and services.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Roles and Responsibilities:</b><br>
</p>
<p>Corporate Public Relations and Communications</p>
<ul><li>Assess company-wide Public Relations needs, execute a company-wide PR plan aligned with corporate marketing and brand-building objectives, and take responsibility for tactical execution.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Reinforce SCS client PR initiatives to promote their SCS-certified accomplishments, and build relationships with key SCS clients (major brands and thought leaders) to explore added opportunities to generate positive PR and visibility.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Prepare or edit content (e.g., articles, collateral, press releases, white papers, case studies, reports, videos) for distribution across multiple media channels, and manage communications within these channels.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manage company blog.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Foster relationships and spearhead outreach to key media and influential communities.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Support SCS marketing campaigns and new service launches</li></ul>
<ul><li>Provide media training, coaching and messaging support to company spokespeople.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manage publicity for live events (e.g., speaking opportunities, tradeshows, special events, webinars).</li></ul>
<ul><li>Coordinate with SCS digital marketing team to build and sustain successful SCS social media presence.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manage PR-related vendors and contractors.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Crisis communications management.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Track and report PR analytics.</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Minimum Qualifications:</b></p>
<p>? Bachelor’s degree in Public Relations, Communications, Journalism or related field.</p>
<p>? Minimum 10+ years of relevant experience.</p>
<p>? Exceptional writing and editing skills.</p>
<p>? Well-developed professional communication and presentation skills, with ability to adjust communication style appropriately for various audiences.</p>
<p>? Strong traditional media, social business, and social media skills.</p>
<p>? Passion for sustainability issues.</p>
<p>? Complete fluency in English in written and oral communication.</p>
<p>? Proficient with business software including Outlook, Word, Excel, etc.</p>
<p>? Strong customer service and teamwork orientation.</p>
<p>? Self-starter with good judgment, and strong organization, prioritization, planning and project management skills.</p>
<p>? Ability to work independently under pressure, and to manage multiple projects and priorities in a highly dynamic environment.</p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Physical Requirements</b></h3>
<p>? This is an office-based job. Sit 6+ hours per day. Keyboarding 6+ hours per day. Must be able to speak and listen in person and on the phone.</p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Work Environment</b></h3>
<p>? This is an office-based job for the majority of time.</p>
