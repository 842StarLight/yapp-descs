<h2>Sr. Capture Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>B3 Group Inc. is looking for an energetic Sr. Capture Analyst to join our growing organization. The Sr. Capture Analyst is responsible for working with the Capture Manager to meet the sales targets for the company. The Sr. Capture Analyst is responsible for assisting with the development of strategic business development plans based on company goals that will promote sales growth and customer satisfaction for the organization.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Functions</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</li>
<li>Assists the Capture manager on the sales capture approach in support of organization strategy and objectives.</li>
<li>Ensures communications are coordinated, supports sales plan objectives and meets organizational expenditure requirements.</li>
<li>Researches business development strategies for improvement based on market research and competitor analyses.</li>
<li>Manages multiple channel selling strategies.</li>
<li>Performs other related duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>To be successful in this position, we expect you to have the following skills and experience:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Problem Solving/Analysis.</li>
<li>Strong Business Acumen.</li>
<li>Strategic Thinking.</li>
<li>Results Driven.</li>
<li>Leadership.</li>
<li>Customer Focus.</li>
<li>Technical Capacity.</li>
<li>Communication Proficiency.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Required</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in business administration, sales and marketing or related field.</li>
<li>At least 5 or more years relevant experience</li>
<li>Previous experience working as a business analyst with proven research and analysis skills</li>
<li>Previous experience working in a sales organization with a federal government contracting organization.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p><br>
</p>

<br>
</p>

<li>You take care in learning your customer’s business and drivers in order to ensure solutions meet not only technical needs, but also support their mission properly in a way that meeting their client's mission.</li>
<li>You like to make your client look good.</li>
<li>You find solutions, where others only see problems.</li>
<li>You are proactive, you make and meet commitments, and you perform your duties exceedingly well.</li>
<li>You are aware of the client’s needs at all times and are well-versed in what both your team and B3 Group can offer them as a whole.</li>
<li>You have the ability to work with diverse, integrated, deliverable-driven teams to accomplish the larger mission.</li>
<br>
</ul>
