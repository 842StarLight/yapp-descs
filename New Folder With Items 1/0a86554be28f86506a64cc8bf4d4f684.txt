<div><b>Job Summary/Basic Function</b></div>
<div>
<ul>Student worker position available in Study Abroad Office. Selected applicant will be tasked with creation of promotional material, general office work, as well as maintaining social media presence and promoting Study Abroad on Campus. Promotions will include graphic design for a variety of promotional material. The successful applicant will be proficient in various forms of editing software and Additional time as needed for general office assistance.<br>
This position requires a very high level of attention to detail and a passion for producing quality work. This position has a set amount of duties and tasks that are critical to the overall success the Study Abroad Office. These task include, but are not limited to:<br>
<li>Preparation of marketing materials for study abroad programs, with targeted message for specific colleges</li>
<li>Maintain social media presence for office</li>
<li>Create and update program websites as needed</li>
<li>Quality control. Reviewing promotional material before final printing.</li>
<li>Communicating with students and other offices. Customer Service experience a must.</li>
<li>Send weekly count updates to program leaders</li>
<li>Create and update program websites as needed</li>
<li>Classroom presentations about Study Abroad</li>
<li>Other general office duties as needed</li>
</ul>
<p>
**This is a pooled position. Applicants are selected from the pool as positions become available. After a period of time, the pool will expire and applicants will be notified.</p>
</div>
</div>

<div><b>Required Qualifications</b></div>

Customer service experience.</p>
