<h2>Sales Trainer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Sales Trainer
</b></p>
<p><b>JOB LOCATION: New York, Chicago, Costa Mesa, Austin, Atlanta
</b></p>
<p><b>Position Summary:
</b></p>
<p>Are you interested in working for the hottest company in one of the world’s most innovative industries?
</p>
<p>GTT is disrupting the telecom/networking industry with its breakout growth and was recently named to the inaugural "Future 50" - Fortune Magazine's list of the fifty public companies best positioned for even greater success in the coming years. We are looking for a decisive and results-oriented manager who can motivate, mentor and guide our inaugural inside sales team of Sales Development reps and contribute to our regional market strategies.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>This is a highly visible position in one of our largest global offices, and critical to the company’s long-term growth strategy.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The Sales Development Trainer will be charged with building and delivering sales training content for GTT’s next generation of sales professionals. Topics will include: Technology, Product, Systems, and Soft Skills training. This individual will coordinate with the Director of Sales training, subject matter experts and sales leadership to achieve these goals.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The candidate we select will know what it takes to develop high-impact training events that impact results.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>This is an exciting time at GTT. We have recently launched our industry-leading SD-WAN service and we are moving fast. If you are someone who enjoys rolling up their sleeves, diving in and building a team of great sales people, apply today.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop and deliver Sales Skills training that fits into GTT’s sales framework and methodology
</li>
<li>Collaborate with sales leadership to identify gaps, recommend, and implement training solutions
</li>
<li>Assist in coordination and delivery of new hire training programs, including classroom training and coaching
</li>
<li>Tracking and communicating training progress
</li>
<li>Report on training program effectiveness and make recommendations for improvement
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>5+ years of Sales experience preferably in Telecom or data networking
</li>
<li>3+ year of experience creating and delivering training and ongoing coaching on sales methodology in complex, multi-level, large enterprise sales
</li>
<li>Strong presentation/coaching skills
</li>
<li>Quota-carrying sales experience
</li>
<li>Experience building and conducting blended learning training combining in-person and web-based components
</li>
<li>Familiar with adult learning principles and coaching concepts; certifications preferred
</li>
<li>Demonstrated record of successful collaboration with sales leaders, product managers and subject matter experts
</li>
<li>Strong Excel, Word, and PowerPoint skills
</li>
<li>Ability to travel and flexible working hours
</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree
</li>
<li>Self-motivated and organized with a strong desire to succeed and grow as we grow.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>To apply:
</b></p>
<p>If interested, submit a resume and cover letter addressing the above points. We look forward to hearing from you.
</p>
<p>GTT is headquartered in the Washington, DC metro area with offices all over the globe. We have built highly successful sales development teams in our Chicago, New York, and Southern California offices, and are recruiting to form a fourth team in Austin.
</p>
