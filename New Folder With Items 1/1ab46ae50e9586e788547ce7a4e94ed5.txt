<p><b>JOB TITLE:</b> Senior IT Process and Integration Analyst - Quality</p>
<p><b>
LOCATION: </b>Baraboo</p>
<p><b>
REPORTS TO: </b>IT Business Engagement Manager
</p>
<p><b>CLASSIFICATION: </b>Exempt</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
POSITION SUMMARY</b></p>
<p>
This position is responsible for creating a working relationship with business users and IT to identify opportunities for process improvement, collection of business requirements, creation of a business case for action and working with key resources to deliver a solution that meets and/or exceeds expectations. The role coordinates development and support for new and existing applications and resolves system problems as needed. It is also responsible for managing and directing the activities of contractors who support and provide services for Oracle R12 ERP and related third-party applications. This individual must apply creative thinking, communication, analytical and problem-solving skills to help maximize the benefit of our IT system investments, in accordance with company policies and procedures.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS</b></p>
<p>
To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions. The job description is not designed to cover or contain a comprehensive listing of activities, duties or responsibilities that are required of the employee. Other duties, responsibilities and activities may change or be assigned at any time with or without notice. The essential functions of this job include, but are not limited to:</p>
<ul>
<li>Eliciting software requirements from business users utilizing requirements gathering methods such as document analysis, customer interviews, working sessions, and site visits.</li>
<li>Assisting in the development of strategic partnerships with internal and external business partners to develop systems and processes to increase automation and efficiencies.</li>
<li>Participating in planning, testing and implementation of system enhancements.</li>
<li>Assisting with the research and resolution of system issues.</li>
<li>Providing users with application level documentation of programming enhancements.</li>
<li>Participating in project work, including analysis, requirements definition and testing.</li>
<li>Assisting in monitoring system and operational controls, identifying areas of risk and suggesting possible improvements.</li>
<li>Maintaining documentation of changes, test results and training.</li>
<li>Reviewing forms and reports and conferring with management and users about format, distribution, and purpose, and to identify problems and improvements.</li>
<li>Conferring with personnel concerned to ensure successful functioning of newly implemented systems or procedures.</li>
<li>Monitoring progress of team members to ensure project completion.</li>
<li>Preparing reports for management and defining and evaluating problems; ultimately recommending solutions.</li>
<li>Developing and maintaining a high level of technical expertise in Oracle R12.</li>
<li>Regular and punctual attendance and physical presence in the workplace.</li>
<li>Timely and accurate completion of duties.</li>
<li>Occasional travel for work, up to 33% of the time.</li>
<br>
</ul>
