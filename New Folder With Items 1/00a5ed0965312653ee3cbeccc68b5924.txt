<h2>Bookshop Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Casual Part Time (20-35 hours/week) - The Bookshop Assistant sells and promotes books, as well as other Bookshop retail items, while keeping the Bookshop clean and orderly to ensure the Bookshop provides the best customer service in a clean and welcoming environment and in a manner consistent with MHA mission, vision and values.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Key Job Accountabilities:<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>
Greet and assist Bookshop guests and complete sales transactions at point of sale to create a hospitable shopping environment for customers.</li>
<li>
Receive and stock books and merchandise in a timely manner to ensure a neat and orderly shop.</li>
<li>
Operate computer software to reconcile daily sales.</li>
<li>
Additional duties as assigned by supervisor.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Supervisory Responsibilities:</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul><li>
None</li></ul>
<div></div>
<br>
</div></div></div>
