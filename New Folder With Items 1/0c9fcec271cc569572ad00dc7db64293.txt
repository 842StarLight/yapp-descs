<p><b>
What Will You Do:</b></p>
<p>
The Salesforce Administrator will play a lead role in managing Seamless Salesforce system configurations, activities, programming, and reporting in support of multiple business functions including Sales, Operations, Marketing, Finance, and Senior Leadership. The Salesforce Administrator will be integrally involved in supporting users across the US, and Canada.</p>
<ul>
<li>
Provide day-to-day end-user support on Salesforce including system configuration and system maintenance</li>
<li>
Support users with reporting, analyses, and dashboards, from intake of requirements through development and delivery against the requests</li>
<li>
Assist users with proactive best practices to enhance and increase their knowledge of Salesforce</li>
<li>
Provide day-to-day production support to users who have questions and issues</li>
<li>
Maintain user roles and profiles, security settings, access settings etc. (User Profiles, Role Hierarchy, Sharing Rules and Security)</li>
<li>
Create and manage custom fields, formulas, validation rules, custom workflow, and approval processes</li>
<li>
Perform data integrity and manipulation tasks such as merging duplicate records and establishing proper ownership of existing accounts and contacts in accordance with sales territories</li>
<li>
Mass upload new data and amendments</li>
<li>
Help support integration and related process flows with Salesforce.com</li>
<li>
Develop, provide, and maintain custom reports</li>
<li>
Lead / Assist with user requirement sessions and document user requirements to address changing business needs.</li>
</ul>
