<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p>Responsible for directing the relationships with Brookdale Paid Partners and key Service Provider Partners, as well as, internal stakeholders. Works closely with the Business Development teams, marketing teams, sales teams, functional groups and the Executive Leadership team to create and administer compelling and high-impact partner programs in conjunction with our strategic partners. Creates strategic communications, events, and campaign strategy to recruit new partners and to grow partnerships with 3rd-party referral sources and service providers. Directs the Paid Partner corporate team to grow existing partner relationships.</p>
<p>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Develops strategies to drive occupancy growth across multiple service lines through the strategic use of referral partners and service providers.</li>
<li>Leads contract negotiations with key partners. Speaks on Brookdale’s behalf at partner conferences to grow partner referral volume.</li>
<li>Works with the analytics team to track and analyze the performance of the partner, monitor performance and make modifications, as appropriate.</li>
<li>Develops strategies along to drive occupancy growth across multiple service lines through the strategic use of referral partners.</li>
<li>Serves as the primary lead in establishing agreements/contracts with Paid Partners, monitoring terms, renewal dates; engages in contract negotiations.
</li>
<li>Manages day-to-day relationships with referral partners, monitoring program performance, sharing results and recommending modifications, as appropriate.
</li>
<li>Integrates referral partner strategies into overall direct response stimulation plans, ensuring alignment of objectives at the market level.</li>
<li>Develops matrix for referral partners to propose and test new approaches and go-to-market strategies that maximize effectiveness and efficiency in cost of acquisition.</li>
<li>Oversees invoicing process, ensuring accuracy of invoices.</li>
<li>Collaborates with marketing, sales and operations partners to strategically use referral partners in growth plans and identify and nurture new referral partners.</li>
<li>Leads cross-functional team to execute programs, while ensuring appropriate budget management, financial oversight and accountability.</li>
</ul>
