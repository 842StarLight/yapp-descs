<h2>Content Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Job Description</b><br>
<p>A newly created role reporting to the Sales Enablement Lead, you will collaborate closely with the rest of the Sales Enablement team and sales asset stakeholder community (e.g. Marketing, Proposition, Sales Learning, Sales Operations) to deliver impactful content to sales teams through curation, structuring and analysis of knowledge assets that drive sales effectiveness. Being a key contributor to the overall vision and success of the Advisor sales enablement platform (powered by Seismic technology), content quality control to ensure the best possible user experience &amp; impact will be at the core of your responsibilities. This includes (but not limited to) metadata &amp; taxonomy governance, content alignment (tagging/retagging), gap and impact analysis, and asset owner training, compliance and best practice.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
The ideal candidate will have previous experience in sales enablement and/or enterprise-level information architecture or content/knowledge asset management. Also, the successful candidate will be able to demonstrate a track record of managing multiple projects and workstreams with multiple stakeholders and competing priorities. You will possess strong analytical and problem-solving skills and an ability to translate ambiguity and incomplete information into insights and bias for action. You will also have a customer (end-user) focus and collaborative approach to working in matrix teams. Lastly, a strong desire to learn, curiosity, and a willingness to roll up your sleeves and get things done are essential qualities.<br>
</p>
<p><b>
KEY ROLES &amp; RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Contribute or lead the following team goals &amp; objectives related to Advisor content management: audits, alignment, content migration, QC/retagging, gap analysis, content standards &amp; templates and taxonomy.</li>
<li>
Work with content strategy leads to evaluate business (sales rep) requirements. Design, develop and curate custom content sets and aggregation solutions, such as predictive, playbooks, landing pages and article layouts.</li>
<li>
Help define goals/benchmarks for asset usage and success criteria for curation.</li>
<li>
Contribute or lead the development of mechanisms that assess content relevance, accuracy, timeliness.</li>
<li>
Support content strategy leads on analysis and communication of content utilization and impact.</li>
<li>
Contribute or lead Advisor search engine optimization (SEO), including keyword optimization, content tagging, and metadata management.</li>
<li>
Contribute or lead Advisor UI/UX enhancements, particularly as it relates to content search, navigation and sales workflows.</li>
<li>
Contribute or lead training, advising and coaching of asset owner and super-user communities on Advisor capabilities, content governance, quality assurance and best practices. Monitor compliance.</li>
<li>
Work with technology &amp; operations team to develop a smooth Advisor “change management” process, providing content requirements/impact analysis when necessary.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
DESIRED QUALIFICATIONS &amp; EXPERIENCE:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
3+ years experience in content management or sales asset creation/adoption, preferably in a B2B sales enablement, marketing, sales training, sales operations, information architecture and/or digital communications role.</li>
<li>
Experience developing “portal” pages, structuring files and folders, and/or managing knowledge asset content and designing navigation to such content through “portal pages” or files structures.</li>
<li>
Experience with engaging and understanding users to design interfaces, navigation and searches, and processes for a user-friendly experience and adoption</li>
<li>
Experience with developing, testing, editing, and improving content</li>
<li>
Experience with content inventories, content distribution models, message matrices, content migrations, asset management, and/or taxonomies</li>
<li>
Knowledge of B2B buyers’ journey and sales process and aligning content to the journey</li>
<li>
Experience in developing strategies and processes to capture and codify knowledge.</li>
<li>
Experience in knowledge/content management taxonomies, facilitating content/asset audits, content mapping and needs assessments.</li>
<li>
Excellent oral and written communication skills</li>
<li>
You are highly self-motivated with strong time management and organizational skills.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Special consideration will be given to candidates with these additional qualifications &amp; experience:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Sales experience or familiarity with established customer-driven sales processes and methodologies.</li>
<li>
Content and sales enablement applications such as Seismic, Showpad, Oracle Marketing Cloud, Highspot, Pitcher or Sharepoint.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of Salesforce.com and/or driving adoption of other sales tools and applications such as Tableau, C9 Forecasting, etc.</li>
<li>
Developing content in various formats downloadable content, blog posts, video, pod-cast and web.</li>
<li>
Supporting a global sales team.</li>
</ul>

OTHER:</b></p>

Based in Minneapolis (Eagan), Dallas, or Toronto.</li>
<li>
B.A./B.S. Degree – Business or Systems related preferred</li>
<li>
Excellent organizational and project management skills with a track record of leading very challenging, complex projects successfully, working across large and diverse project teams, maximizing resources and overcoming setbacks</li>
</ul>
