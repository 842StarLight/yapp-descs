<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>About the job</b></h2>
<p>
The Google Cloud Platform team helps customers transform and evolve their business through the use of Google’s global network, web-scale data centers and software infrastructure. As part of an entrepreneurial team in this rapidly growing business, you will help shape the future of businesses of all sizes use technology to connect with customers, employees and partners.</p>
<p>
The PSO Strategy and Operations team is the in-house dedicated team that supports the Professional Services Organization (PSO) within Google Cloud. This team fully embeds alongside Cloud, working directly with the PSO leadership on a mixture of strategic and operational issues.</p>
<p>As Strategy and Operations Manager, you will manage Professional Services data, analytics and reporting capabilities and shape business strategy. This role will support PSO leadership and work closely with stakeholders. This is an entrepreneurial business inside Google, moving fast with a lot of challenges still to resolve.</p>
<p>You will need to be comfortable with managing multiple data sources, building dashboards and reports, developing strategic insights and monitoring business performance on key metrics. You will help people, solve problems and analyze large amounts of data. Finally, you also need to be strong at cross-team collaboration as you will need to partner with stakeholders across multiple functions in Google.
</p>
<p>The Go-to-Market Strategy and Operations organization partners with our Google Cloud Sales organization to develop Go-to-Market strategies, providing business critical insights using analytics, ensuring cross functional alignment of goals and execution, and helping teams drive strategic partnerships and new initiatives forward. We stay focused on aligning the highest-level company priorities with day-to-day operations, and help evolve early stage ideas into future-growth initiatives. Our team of creative visionaries thrives in deconstructing and re-creating businesses to meet changing strategic and operational needs, enable, incubate and scale innovation, establish more efficient practices, align functions and stakeholders to help the company continue to achieve its vast potential.</p>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Responsibilities</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Drive strategic initiatives by developing a project plan, gathering and synthesizing relevant data, leading analyses and developing final recommendations.</li>
<li>Structure and perform analyses independently and conduct research to contribute to the overall Google Cloud PSO strategy.</li>
<li>Assess business needs to drive design, implementation, and improve visibility and accuracy of operational data for scalable and efficient operations.</li>
<li>Conduct sessions with subject matter experts, business users and sponsors to discover and define business needs.</li>
<li>Use effective data visualization and analysis to simplify complex business flows and enable faster decision making. Build, maintain and improve data infrastructure to support Professional Services efforts using SQL.</li>
</ul>
