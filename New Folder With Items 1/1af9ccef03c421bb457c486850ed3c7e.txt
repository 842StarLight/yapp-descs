<h2>Senior Staff Accountant - Westfield, IN</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Position Overview
</b></p>
<p>The primary purpose of this position is to perform accounting, credit and collections, inventory management, and analysis for the company. The secondary purpose is to provide the back-up of the Payables and Receivables duties for the Finance function.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Job Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Works to supply products and services that meet or exceed the customers' expectations. Follows the documented quality systems and processes that are continually being improved to satisfy our customers' needs.</li>
<li>
Perform duties within the accounting process for accurate general ledger information. This includes posting journal entries, analyzing and reconciling general ledger accounts, preparing financial statements, and maintaining fixed asset records.</li>
<li>
Perform duties within the credit process to facilitate establishing credit to customers. This includes obtaining and analyzing credit information. Investigating issues with customer accounts. Contact with existing and potential customers may occur.</li>
<li>
Assist with duties in the banking process. This includes clearing checks daily, reconciling bank and lockbox accounts, analyzing and forecasting cash flow weekly, and preparing the revolver borrowing base certificate.</li>
<li>
Prepare financial reports with reliable conclusions that management can use to implement more effective operational strategies. Forecast financial models for changes in performance.</li>
<li>
Conduct analysis on financials and company operations to evaluate performance, determine areas of potential cost reduction, and carry out process improvement with time, labor, and capital.</li>
<li>
Perform duties within the tax process. This includes federal and state income taxes, various state sales and use taxes, property taxes, payroll tax, etc.</li>
<li>
Assist with the completion of inventory management tasks including physical inventory cycle counting.</li>
<li>
Provide back-up to the receivables process. This includes billing of sales, collection and posting of cash, analyzing customer accounts, working with customers to expedite payments, and reporting to management.</li>
<li>
Provide back-up to the payables process. This includes scheduling and paying vendors in a timely manner, filing related documents, and analyzing any problems with vendors or suppliers.</li>
<li>
May assist Controller or President in other duties or special projects as needed.</li>
</ul>
