<p>
The Manager, Demand Generation is responsible for managing a team of six vertical-specific marketing managers to drive demand for Relias’ solutions that meets or exceeds pipeline goals and, ultimately, drives revenue growth. This is a cross-functional role within the organization, working closely with Strategic/Product Marketing and Sales teams to significantly impact our high-growth business through multiple channels.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>To be successful, you must be a results-oriented marketing professional and performance-focused people manager with experience in integrated marketing campaigns. This role will focus on ensuring your team is creating effective marketing campaigns that drive enough leads through the marketing funnel (from new names to qualified leads to new qualified opportunities) to meet the needs of their assigned sales teams.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>WHAT YOU’LL BE DOING:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Structuring high-performing, multi-channel marketing campaigns that align with business goals and market dynamics. Helping the team create marketing plans that include audience targeting, paid/promotional placements, trade show strategy, nurture stream creation and optimization.</li>
<li>
Developing a deep understanding of our customers across the healthcare continuum, their buying process and our solutions.</li>
<li>
Working in close collaboration with other teams within Marketing (Digital, Creative, Events, Product Marketing, Content Marketing, Marketing Operations) to build cross-functional cohesion.
</li>
<li>Understanding demand generation programs that support both Inside Sales and Enterprise Sales teams.
</li>
<li>Using marketing and sales data to analyze results and drive priorities for your team, setting goals, and identifying opportunities for improvement.</li>
<li>
Collaborating closely with product marketing peers to ensure that a clear, effective go-to-market strategy is in place for every new product release and product update.
</li>
<li>Setting launch goals and building marketing campaigns programs to reach those goals. This can include strategy, channel optimization, marketing programs and message testing
</li>
<li>Taking ownership and initiative to support the marketing team and company through a pro-active and positive attitude</li>
<li>
Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
