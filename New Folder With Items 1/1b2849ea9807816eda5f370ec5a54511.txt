<h2>AV/IT Technician</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<b>JOB DESCRIPTION</b><br>
Creation Networks is seeking a skilled and motivated AV/ IT Technician to join our team. The ideal candidate for this position has a strong understanding of Audio Visual &amp; computer, operating systems and applications, routinely thinks outside the box to solve problems quickly and has a strong focus on providing excellent customer service. The AV/IT Technician will be responsible for ensuring that all devices receive updated software/firmware, applications and performing technical and system updates and diagnostics as required. This position supports both in-house and external clients. If you are a CompTIA A+ certified professional who’s interested in joining a collaborative team, please submit your resume today.<br>
<br>
<b>DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES (Other duties may be assigned on an individual basis.)</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>
Populate AV equipment racks and upload equipment configuration/code</li>
<li>
Perform AV hardware updates and firmware, IP addressing</li>
<li>
Install software and hardware systems, including new equipment, anti-virus programs, CRM programs, and other tools or applications commonly used by a company’s customers and team members</li>
<li>
Uninstall old programs</li>
<li>
Strive to resolve malware and virus issues within personal workstations and the company’s network</li>
<li>
Repair and maintain servers, personal computers, scanners, printers, and other technology equipment</li>
<li>
Work with team members to troubleshoot and solve both complex and routine technical issues</li>
<li>
Contribute to the overall operations of a company by writing and distributing memos about best practices for computer, applications and network use</li>
<li>
Perform one-on-one computer coaching on technology when necessary</li>
<li>
Configure computers and data networks, understanding network scheme and IP addressing</li>
<li>
Communicate with individuals to solve technical problems - face-to-face, web meetings, by phone and email</li>
<li>
Stay abreast of current trends and technology and make recommendations that will help Creation Networks keep up with technology standards in our industry</li>
<li>
Adapt quickly to changing circumstances, new standards, and requests by team and company management</li>
<li>
Install AV equipment and wiring at Creation Networks and in client facilities</li>
</ul>
