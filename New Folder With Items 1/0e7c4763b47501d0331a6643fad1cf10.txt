Maesa is looking for a Social Media Manager. In this role you will be responsible for developing and executing overall social media strategy for some of Maesa's portfolio of exclusive beauty brands. The Social Media Manager leads and helps execute the content strategy across channels for each brand, as well as oversees Social Advertising campaigns globally. You will take part in a cross-channel marketing experience as you lead the social media aspect of the consumer journey for key activations..

<li>Collaborate with E-Commerce, Marketing and Design for content and campaign development that align with overall brand identity, goals and budget.</li>
<li>Ensure the brand voice and identity is carried out across social media channels while driving performance, reviewing with each brand founder/celebrity as necessary.</li>
<li>Responsible for community management across platforms for each brand, ensuring flawless execution of response times and content delivery in an authentic brand voice.</li>
<li>Manage paid social strategy and campaigns against defined KPIs such as driving traffic, engagement, leads and DTC conversions.</li>
