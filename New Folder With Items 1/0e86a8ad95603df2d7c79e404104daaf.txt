Essential Duties and Responsibilities
<br><br>
<b>Operations:</b><br>
Research, identify, and communicate key variances relating to financial and operational metrics
<br><br>
Provide ad-hoc financial and operational analysis to support business decisions
<br><br>
Improve upon existing reporting as requested by management
<br><br>
Process improvement to include analysis of improvement areas, improvement plan development, plan presentation and in some situations management of plan implementation.
<br><br>
Work closely with new M&amp;As to integrate/convert systems
<br><br>
Monitor daily overdue HFC activities Benefit Point Staff and work with team member on how to resolve the activities.
<br><br>
Through monitoring the above, recommend and in some cases deliver training needed for frequently occurring issues
<br><br>
Assist in correcting HFC activities as needed
<br><br>
Create and distribute Advisor Reports on a monthly basis
<br><br>
Create and distribute COI Commission Reports on a monthly basis
<br><br>
Audit commission splits on routine basis
<br><br>
Enter all commission corrections as well as change commissions when BOBs are adjusted and/or re-distributed
<br><br>
Create Benefit Point Reports as requested by COO for Sale Leaders or Office Managers
<br><br>
Additional Projects as Identified by COO and other Duties as assigned
<br><br>
<b>Cash/Deposits:</b><br>
Complete Daily Deposit Log required by HFC as needed for all Employee Benefit locations in Enterprise BMS Systems
<br><br>
Coding Branch RDSA Deposits and updating the required daily Deposit Log
<br><br>
Research EPIC to provide specific invoicing for cash application
<br><br>
Research unapplied cash
<br><br>
<b>Account Receivables:</b><br>
Review and follow up on all past due items (debit or credits); including reaching out to collect past due monies
<br><br>
Reconciliations- provide detail reconciliation to Carolina Employee Benefit teams on accounts as requested.
<br><br>
Audit pre-determined reports to determine missing payments for all clients on a routine basis
<br><br>
Identify missing commissions through analysis of commission reports. Contact carrier to find reason for nonpayment and then resolve root issue.
<br><br>
Identify and review licensing issues as it pertains to missing payments. This will include creating a database to manage carriers and agents as well as working with corporate Hub on Hub Carolinasâ€™ licensing needs.
<br><br>
<b>Accounting Issues:</b><br>
Review and confirm invoicing is correct as needed for producer commissions
<br><br>
Assist HFC in collection of difficult Direct Bill Statements for entering into BMS System
<br><br>
Enter commissions into BP for new clients
