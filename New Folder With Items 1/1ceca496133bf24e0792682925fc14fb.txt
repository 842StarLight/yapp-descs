<h2>Pre Sale Solution Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><b>POSITION PURPOSE</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>
<b><i>Support sales of QAD software solutions by developing and delivering solution presentations to customers and prospective customers during the sales cycle</i></b>; participates in sales opportunities with sales account executives, other solution consultants, service consultants, and other QAD personnel. Answer complex questions on product functions and usage and solve business process issues by effectively applying QAD software solutions. Promote business relationships and develop sales leads with high-level contacts through face-to-face and client site visits. Develop and maintain skills to promote QAD trusted advisor status with customers and effectively deliver solution messages throughout the QAD Sales and Customer Engagement Process.</div>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>DUTIES &amp; RESPONSIBILITIES:</b><br>
<ul><li>Develop and maintain skills to promote QAD software solutions and effectively deliver solution</li></ul>
messages throughout the QAD Sales and Customer Engagement Process. Provide subject matter<br>
expertise in support of customer process improvement, and compliance with business procedures.<br>
<ul><li>Develop and execute customer facing solution demonstrations consistent with QAD corporate</li></ul>
guidelines and expectations for completeness and quality of the engagement process. Prepare<br>
documentation necessary to ensure the fulfillment of the customer engagement process<br>
<ul><li>Conduct discovery and competitively position our solutions and showcase new solutions where</li></ul>
appropriate, prepare and deliver application solution demonstrations as required.<br>
<ul><li>Develop and maintain a high level understanding of the functional capabilities across the</li></ul>
breadth of QAD's solution applications in order to provide customers and sales reps with product and/or<br>
solutions advice and/or demonstrations during engagement discussions. Understand product road map<br>
and direction.<br>
<ul><li>Maintain an up-to-date understanding of trends that impact QAD targeted vertical markets by</li></ul>
networking with QAD's corporate industry focused personnel, maintaining a presence in relevant<br>
professional associations, and subscribing to internal and external industry focused distribution lists or<br>
publications. Additionally, maintain an understanding of how industry trends effect competitive<br>
positioning.<br>
<ul><li>Maintain an active presence in the LA Region by assisting other team members. Proactively</li></ul>
communicate industry trends and QAD solution knowledge to other team members, as needed..<br>
<ul>
<li>Other duties as assigned.</li>
<li>Willingness to travel – This position is expected to involve travel time overall</li>
</ul>
