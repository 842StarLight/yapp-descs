<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Description
</b></h2>
<p>We have world-class cross-functional staff across the globe including sales and marketing, regional medical liaisons, oncology nurse specialists and customer account teams to ensure our discoveries go from bench to bedside.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The Amgen Oncology team is committed to advancing oncology at the speed of life. Join us in Amgen’s ongoing commitment to serve hematology and oncology patients.
</p>
<p>The Sr. Marketing Manager will play an important role in maximizing the value of AMG510, a novel Kras G12c molecule developed in Lung cancer, by leading a range of strategic, analytical and promotional executional efforts to prepare and execute the US launch.
</p>
<p>The primary focus for this position is to develop launch materials, programs and initiatives for AMG510. This is inclusive of personal and non-personal tactical programs to help healthcare providers and patients understand the value of AMG510 in approved patients. Other responsibilities include sales force interaction and field communication, sales force training initiatives, and shaping and leading go to market strategy and execution. This role will have heavy cross-functional interaction and exposure to the US Value &amp; Access team, data and analytics, and contracting.
</p>
<p>If you are looking to gain experience working on launch products in a new and dynamic market place, this may be a role for you. This position will report to the Commercial Director for AMG510 and will be a part of overall brand leadership team.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Responsibilities:
</p>
<ul>
<li><p>Partner with Global marketing to ensure activities are aligned to brand goals, growth drivers, and key messages (compliant with MAC guidance)
</p></li>
<li><p>Develop disease state education, healthcare provider and patient promotional campaigns, messages, programs and sales tools for multiple products.
</p></li>
<li><p>Coordinate the work of multiple external vendors and internal departments including marketing, market research, contracting, V&amp;A, legal, regulatory, medical affairs, corporate compliance, and IS.
</p></li>
<li><p>Ensure that Budget management is a priority with all projects/budgets within approved limits
</p></li>
<li><p>Lead projects through the legal, regulatory, and medical review process.
</p></li>
<li><p>Manage program budgets and developing metrics to ensure success of the promotional programs.
</p></li>
<li><p>Help to guide strategic decisions and ensuring flawless execution of promotional campaigns and programs for Oncology healthcare providers.
</p></li>
<li><p>Engage consistently with the sales force to ensure the creation and execution of high impact, high value marketing resources that drive the appropriate use of AMG510.
</p></li>
<li><p>Collaborate with sales training to ensure effective, impactful, world class training on promotional tools and programs.
</p></li>
<li><p>Coordinate and drive market research planning.
</p></li>
</ul>
