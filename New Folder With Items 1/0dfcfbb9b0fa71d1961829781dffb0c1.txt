<p><b>Job Summary: </b></p>
<p>We’re currently looking for a Business Development Rep to focus on our Data Protection (DP) and Endpoint Systems Management (ESM) solutions. This is a pure prospecting role, heavy outbound calling to prospective clients.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Generate business through proactive outreach to new customers via cold calling and strategic email campaigning</li>
<li>Quickly develop value-based business relationships with B2B customers</li>
<li>Identify customer needs and requirements</li>
<li>Promote and position the strategic &amp; tactical values of data protection solution offerings</li>
<li>Schedule concrete next steps with the customer and the territory specialist, typically a product demonstration</li>
<li>Seamlessly transfer sales opportunities to the territory specialist</li>
<li>Research, qualify, manage and track leads and related sales activities</li>
<li>Develop internal sales relationships with sales specialist and sales engineers</li>
<li>Function competently and competitively in a fast-paced, highly transactional call-center environment that requires project and time management skills</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelors Degree preferred</li>
<li>2+ years of outbound B2B sales experience OR a combination of work experience and education</li>
<li>1+ years of customer service experience is required</li>
<li>Demonstrable verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Ability to learn quickly and think analytically</li>
<li>2+years of successful quota-carrying sales experience preferred</li>
<li>Experience utilizing CRM systems, preferably Salesforce.com preferred</li>
<li>Working understanding of data protection technologies preferred</li>
</ul>
