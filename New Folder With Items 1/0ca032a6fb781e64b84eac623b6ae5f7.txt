<h2>Resell Contracts & Compliance Manager (Enabling Manager)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div><div>
<div><b>Resell Contracts &amp; Compliance Manager (Enabling Manager)
</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>Are you a Contracts &amp; Compliance Manager who has strong negotiating skills, pays attention to detail, has outstanding oral/written communication skills, and the ability to work in a fast-paced environment across a matrixed organization? If so, Deloitte is currently seeking candidates for a Resell Contracts &amp; Compliance Manager (CCM) role.
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>Work You Will Do:</b></div>
<div>
 The CCM will focus on formalizing agreements and relationships with key vendors in the ecosystem whose products are offered by Deloitte pursuit teams to our clients, and adherence to all the contractual and compliance elements that are required when conducting business. This role involves:</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
Team leadership</b></div>
</div></div>
<ul>
<li>
Oversee and manage resell activities performed by team members.</li>
<li>
 Provide feedback and coaching to team members.</li>
</ul>
<b>
Vendor and contract management</b><br>
<ul>
<ul><li>Support vetting and onboarding new vendors whose products Deloitte can resell.</li></ul>
<li>
Understand vendors’ resell programs, discount rates and criteria, commission fees and requirements, and any other margin levers and requirements available with that vendor.</li>
<li>
Understand the business case and justification for establishing a resell relationship with a new vendor.</li>
<li>
Have a firm grasp of and be able to articulate to vendors and Deloitte leadership the nuance that leads to a successful reselling relationship.</li>
<ul><li>Provide guidance regarding Independence considerations to the Resell and pursuit teams.</li></ul>
<li>
Understand, recognize, and articulate terms that must be present in every vendor agreement, and terms than cannot be accepted in any vendor agreement, and why.</li>
<li>
Review proposed agreements and provide comments to assist the Office of the General Counsel (OGC) in their preparation of redline documents.</li>
<li>
Review contractual documents and act as primary liaison between DCPS, Deloitte Engagement Teams, OGC, and Delivery Excellence to negotiate and manage multiple agreements while ensuring adherence to company policies and conformance to Deloitte Agreements, including Reseller, Resale, Teaming, Subcontract, Software License, and Nondisclosure, and other related Agreements.</li>
<li>
 Interpret, understand and communicate key contractual requirements and obligations</li>
</ul>
<b>
Crossfuctional teaming</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Lead daily and/or frequent interaction with Deloitte’s senior management, vendors’ executive management, sales management, and cross-functional teams.</li>
<li>
 Provide contract guidance and support to senior business leaders within the Firm.</li>
<li>
Effectively and efficiently facilitate status updates, communications, and coordination between the vendors’ management team members and internal customers at all management levels within Deloitte.</li>
<li>
Take the lead role in implementing compliance policies, procedures and best practices.</li>
<li>
 Oversee performing of independence reviews to vendors to ensure the Firm’s adherence to AICPA and SEC Auditor Independence Requirements.</li>
<li>
Proactively work with Deloitte’s National Office of Independence and National Risk Management to resolve issues that might conflict with DCPS’ business relationships with vendors.</li>
<li>
 Provide guidance and training to Deloitte personnel regarding independence compliance policies, process and tools to establish marketplace business relationships, and application to policy related to independence.</li>
</ul>
<div><div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
 The Team</b></div>
<div>
The CCM is a member of Deloitte’s Resell team, working closely with a broad spectrum of product vendors and Deloitte practice leadership. The CCM’s focus is on securing the relationships and agreements with vendors needed for Deloitte to successfully offer technology to our client, while adhering to all compliance requirements. Additionally, this individual ensures consistency with agreements in all the associated documentation associated with a product sale.</div>
