<h2>Executive Chef</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Job Summary:</p>
<p><b>
Unity in Diversity
</b></p>
<p>Our team members are as diverse as our music venues. With its owned/operated/managed venues, Live Nation Clubs and Theaters, a division of Live Nation Entertainment, is wide-ranging, including the legendary Fillmore venues and the intimate House of Blues clubs throughout the United States. Live Nation Clubs and Theaters is the country’s preeminent group of intimate music venues, featuring superior sound and lighting technology in one-of-a-kind custom-designed environments aimed to bring fans and artists together in unparalleled musical environments.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
Our mission is to create a profitable and principled global entertainment company. To celebrate the diversity and brotherhood of world culture. To promote racial and spiritual harmony through love, peace, truth, righteousness and non-violence. Our venues bring fans as close to artists as possible and our aim is to create teams that are just as close.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Who are you?</b></p>
<p>
Passionate and motivated. Driven, with an entrepreneurial spirit. Resourceful, innovative, forward thinking and committed. At Live Nation Clubs &amp; Theaters, our people embrace these qualities, so if this sounds like you, then please read on!</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
The Role:</b></p>
<p>
The Executive Chef is responsible for all Food &amp; Beverage operations, related costs, procedures, quality, and all back of house labor percentages &amp; budgets. An effective Executive Chef is able to generate revenue, control costs and lead and develop a team.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
What You Need:</b></p>
<p><b><i>
Required:
</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 5 years of kitchen management and fine dining cooking experience in leadership role</li>
<li>Skilled in cooking, cost controls, management, crew training and development</li>
<li>Working knowledge of kitchen and dishwashing procedures</li>
<li>Knowledge of food and beverage, labor and product costs</li>
<li>Computer savvy – Working knowledge/experience with Excel worksheets, databases, word processing and Micros</li>
<li>Ability to run all kitchen operations</li>
<li>Able to lift at least 50 lbs. and stand for long periods of time (10 – 12 hours)</li>
<li>Tolerant of extreme heat</li>
<li>Comfortable working in a fast-paced and loud environment</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b><i>
Preferred:</i></b></p>
<ul><li>Knowledge of operations and procedures of a multi-faceted entertainment venue</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
What You’ll Do:</b></p>
<p><b><i>
I. Financial Performance of Department:</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Meet or exceed budgetary expectation</li>
<li>Control Food, Beverage and Labor costs</li>
<li>Manage Operating Accounts</li>
<li>Monitor, maintain and control inventory bi-weekly to House of Blues standards</li>
<li>Develop menu engineering program to project and monitor trend and predict cost</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b><i>
II. Supervision of the Department:</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ensure proper execution of all corporate mandates, SOP’s, and specifications</li>
<li>Assist and support kitchen management in any of their job functions as needed</li>
<li>Ensure positive and creative team environment within department</li>
<li>Facilitate open communication with the Sous Chefs, supervisors and staff</li>
<li>Facilitate proper inter-departmental communications and organization</li>
<li>Conduct staff evaluations (training, 90-day, annual). Set written development and performance goals for all team members and monitor progress</li>
<li>Ensure complete and satisfactory on-going staff training per corporate training program</li>
<li>Maintain appropriate staffing levels. Recruit and hire staff/supervisors and ensure that hiring/disciplinary/termination standards follow all HOB guidelines</li>
<li>Oversee performance evaluations and raise recommendations to AGM and/or GM</li>
<li>Maintain open door policy and build strong professional relationships with staff</li>
<li>Make business decisions that are information based and follow up on efficiently</li>
<li>Information is gathered, shared and used appropriately</li>
<li>Hold monthly department meetings to ensure proper communication and teamwork</li>
<li>Hold individual performance counseling meetings with each Sous Chef and Supervisor on a regular basis</li>
<li>Hold weekly Kitchen Manager meetings</li>
<li>Ensure adherence to safety guidelines</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b><i>
III. Build Sales;</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Work with Corporate Food and Beverage to create cost-effective specials and menu changes to achieve maximum sales</li>
<li>Ensure that bi-annual Restaurant menu roll-out is executed to specifications</li>
<li>Maintain food quality level that provides excellent value and increases guest visit frequency</li>
<li>Participate in fundraising events and community appearances to increase House of Blues awareness</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b><i>
IV. Maintain Operation Supplies:</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ensure that staff has supplies needed to facilitate job</li>
<li>Provide staff with proper equipment training when hired</li>
<li>Maintain all equipment</li>
<li>Organize and plan all events to House of Blues specifications</li>
<li>Ensure housekeeping standards are compliant with inspection guidelines and Local Health Code</li>
<li>Utilize House of Blues Back of House tools and systems consistently to use results and data to improve business</li>
<li>House of Blues specification forms are updated and used to ensure consistency</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b><i>
V. Maintenance and Update of Micros, Labor Systems and Crunch Time:</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Enter employee data in system prior to any work is performed</li>
<li>Ensure that all employee data is correct in system – job class, current wage data, etc., and that accurate employee file information is maintained</li>
<li>Maintain and update menu item counts</li>
<li>Audit and enter employee time card adjustment sheets into labor system for accuracy and tracking</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b><i>
VI. Food:</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Uphold food standards per House of Blues specifications. Obtain proper approvals for any changes</li>
<li>Monitor food portions and provide recommendations for any necessary revisions to Corporate Food and Beverage</li>
<li>Provide quarterly new menu item recommendations</li>
<li>Report guest and crew feedback and ensure issue resolution</li>
<li>Ensure 100% recipe adherence</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Our Benefits:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Competitive compensation and bonus plans</li>
<li>Professional career development</li>
<li>Additional benefits</li>
</ul>
