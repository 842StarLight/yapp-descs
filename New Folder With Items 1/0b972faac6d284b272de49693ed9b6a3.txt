The Aberdeen IronBirds are seeking a highly motivated and energetic professional to join our Ticket Services team as a Membership Services Coordinator. <li>Offseason season seat member events</li>
<li>Seat visits at games</li>
<li>Minor League Baseball Responsibilities, including but not limited to, pulling tarp, moving furniture, mascot performing at ticketing-related events, non-gameday season seat member events, public team appearances, and more</li>
