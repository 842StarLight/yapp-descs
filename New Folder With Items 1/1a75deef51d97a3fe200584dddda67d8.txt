WHAT YOU'LL DO
<br><br>
As the Partner Marketing Manager, you will play a key role in growing Braze Alloys (our Technology Partner Program) . Our Partner Program produces mutually beneficial events, case studies, webinars etc. This role will ensure our partners gain increasing value from their Braze relationship, and ultimately, contribute to one of Braze's most significant revenue channels.
<br><br>
As a Partner Marketing Manager, you will conceptualize and create marketing activities to support our partner ecosystem. You will have a wide range of responsibilities including field marketing, enablement materials creation, partner communications strategies and relationship building. Braze works in concert with many partners to form a best-in-class technical marketing ecosystem for our customers, and you will be tasked with leveraging that ecosystem to design and achieve shared goals just as seamlessly. You will work closely with our channel and sales teams as well as our trusted partners on conceptualizing field events and will manage them independently end to end: logistics, invitations and post event management. Additionally, you will work cross functionally with teams at Braze to ensure that Sales and Success teams have all necessary resources to "go to market" for all of our top partners.
