<p><b>What you’ll be doing...</b>
</p>
<p>Responsibilities associated with the position include:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Identifying and qualifying business opportunities and identifying key client technical objections and developing the strategy to resolve technical blockers.</li>
<li>
Owning the technical relationship with clients, including managing product and solution briefings, proof-of-concept work, coordination of additional technical resources, and obtaining Client requirements and translates these into a (client-specific) Solution Design and/or Verizon Enterprise offer.</li>
<li>
Leading solution definition and solution design both internal within Verizon Business Group as well as towards the client.</li>
<li>
Leading the prioritization of client solution / technical requirements and championing the resolution of solution / product gaps internally (also towards Product Groups).</li>
<li>
Working with potential clients to analyze IT infrastructure for potential opportunities to demonstrate Verizon Business Group solution's unique capabilities.</li>
<li>
Articulating the Verizon Business Group vision on technologies and solutions towards the client; positioning Verizon Business Group as thought leader on key technological developments.</li>
<li>
Developing individual skill set and expanding product knowledge to maximize value and to deliver quality services to our clients. This includes attaining designated certification requirements and attending product-specific training, reviewing technical literature, and obtaining hands-on experience.</li>
<li>
Traveling to client sites, conferences, and other related events as required.</li>
</ul>
