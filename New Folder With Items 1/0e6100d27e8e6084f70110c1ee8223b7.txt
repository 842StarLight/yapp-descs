<h2>Marketing Communications Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>COINS is a global provider of software solutions to the construction industry. We are a values led organisation and looking for like-minded people who can add value to our team. The position we are looking to fill is for a Marketing Communications Specialist. This role will be responsible for planning and implementing COINS marketing communication strategy in the USA in order to build the company brand and generate sales opportunities.</p>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Job Role</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Create all the marketing materials that are distributed to customers, including newsletters, emails, and brochures. They may also work with design teams to create other materials such as videos and white papers.</li>
<li>Research both online and offline channels to learn how to best distribute marketing materials for maximum exposure.</li>
<li>Distribute marketing materials through the best and most appropriate channels.
</li>
<li>Track marketing campaign performance such as customer engagement, lead generation and conversion rates to identify ways to improve response rates on future marketing campaigns.</li>
<li>Report on campaign performance.</li>
<li>Setting up interviews and press conferences as part of a marketing campaign. This involves contacting media outlets or industry experts.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<p><b>
Marketing knowledge</b><br>
Possesses knowledge of marketing trends and strategies.<br>
Direct experience of how to utilize the latest trends in social media strategies and management.<br>
Knowledge and experience of database marketing and management.<br>
Practical experience of implementing multi-channel marketing campaigns
</p>
<p><b>Creativity</b><br>
Know how to take seemingly uninteresting concepts and translate them into campaigns that drive customer engagement, lead generation and conversion.</p>
<p><b>
Writing skills</b><br>
Present marketing ideas in a clear manner that even the least knowledgeable customers can understand.<br>
Translate complicated technical jargon into customer-facing materials.</p>
<p><b>
Presentation skills</b><br>
Ability to present information and concepts in a manner that the identified target audience can easily understand.</p>
<p><b>
Communication skills</b><br>
Ability to work with a variety of teams within a company to create comprehensive marketing strategies and campaigns and also often communicate with clients.</p>
<p><b>
General</b><br>
At least 5 years experience in a similar role.<br>
Bachelor s degree in marketing, communications, or a related field.<br>
Marketing software (Campaign Monitor, Google Analytics, Hubspot).<br>
Email marketing software (Mailchimp, ContentCal, Campaign Monitor).<br>
Microsoft Office Suite (Word, PowerPoint, Outlook).<br>
Data analytics tools (Excel, Power BI).<br>
Experience of working in a software environment is desirable but not essential.<br>
Experience of managing corporate web site content is desirable but not essential.</p>
