<b>The Role:</b><br>
---------
<br><br>
NS1 is looking for a Senior Manager, Growth Marketing to own lead generation and conversion optimization within a highly technical, senior management (director and above) audience. This role will report to the Senior Director of Growth and collaborate with a talented team of marketing, product marketers, engineers, and salespeople to generate new leads and drive qualified opportunities with enterprise accounts.
<br><br>
The ideal candidate has hands-on experience using a variety of digital marketing channels (such as ABM, email, display advertising, social media, SEO, etc.) and has a proven track record of managing integrated campaigns that focus on generating qualified leads and converting pipeline.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities may include:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Manage marketing campaigns and demand gen activities (webinars, content, product launches, etc..) across digital channels, with a strong focus on display, paid social, and search</li>
<li>Plan, create, implement and oversee all campaigns, including creative, copy and landing pages</li>
<li>Own process for iterative testing to optimize performance of campaigns and content</li>
<li>Proactively engage with the go-to-market and sales team to coordinate, communicate, and optimize the impact of campaigns marketing activities to support opportunity creation, pipeline and bookings goals</li>
<li>Identify and evaluate emerging platforms and channels to lead new tests and growth initiatives</li>
<li>Continuous testing and optimization of messaging, content, creative and user funnel</li>
<li>Optimize marketing automation and lead nurturing across email, content, and social</li>
<li>Helping manage marketing attribution platforms</li>
</ul>
