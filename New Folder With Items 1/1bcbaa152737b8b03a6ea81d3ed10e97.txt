<h2>Golf Professional</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<div>Golf Galaxy is seeking a specialty retail Golf Professional in <b>Downers Grove, IL</b>.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
A division of DICK’S Sporting Goods, Golf Galaxy’s merchandising strategy offers a comprehensive selection of competitively priced brand name golf equipment, accessories, apparel, golf services, and golf instruction by on-staff certified PGA or LPGA professionals. As a Golf Galaxy Golf Professional, you will be responsible for establishing and maintaining customer service standards relating to lessons and fittings, including maximizing sales and profitability and controlling expenses.<br>
</div>
<div> #LI-SK1</div>
<div>
<b>
Essential Functions of this position include:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Drive and sustain lessons and fittings sales intensity and profitability by setting high expectations and monitoring and measuring positive results
</li>
<li>Meet or exceed sales goal objectives for lessons and fittings
</li>
<li>Give golf lessons and perform fittings (ball launch monitor, putter, driver, iron/wedge)</li>
<li>Conduct preliminary interview with customer, record customer’s swing, provide swing analysis and provide feedback to customer, and schedule customer for on-going lessons
</li>
<li>Develop, design, recommend and implement instructional programs, customer clinics and other promotional activities as directed
</li>
<li>Maintain thorough knowledge of products, services, merchandise promotions, test merchandise and advertising
</li>
<li>Promote Golf Galaxy by participating in community organizations, PGA or LPGA of America and other service organizations to promote and enhance Golf Galaxy’s standing in the business community</li>
<li>Create a climate within the store in which staff are motivated to do their best, strong morale and spiriting in his/her team; share wins and successes; foster open dialogue; and create a feeling of belonging within the team</li>
<li>Other tasks/responsibilities as business needs dictate</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div>At Golf Galaxy, our goal is to be recognized by our customers as the number one golf specialty retailer through the relentless improvement of everything we do. Our formula for success is simple: we offer a wide selection of golf merchandise and services in a convenient, shopper-friendly environment.
</div>
</div>
<div>
<b>Qualifications</b><p></p>
<div><b>
Success Profile:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Class A PGA of America or LPGA member in good standing
</li>
<li>3+ years golf teaching experience
</li>
<li>3+ years knowledge of club-fitting through experience and/or OEM training
</li>
<li>Ability to successfully meet goals and objectives for lessons and fittings
</li>
<li>Excellent leadership, interpersonal and communication skills
</li>
<li>Effective and productive time management skills to maximize lesson time
</li>
<li>Superior customer service orientation, excellent interpersonal and presentation skills
</li>
<li>Proficiency in MS Office</li>
<li>Flexible availability – including nights, weekend, and holidays</li>
</ul>
