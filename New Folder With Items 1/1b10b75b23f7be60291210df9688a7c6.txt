<h2>Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Buildpro is seeking a <b>Project Manager</b> for our <b>Dallas, TX.</b> location! This is a fast-paced leadership position overseeing all day to day operations and overseeing multiple installation crews. The Project Manager is ultimately responsible for organizing, motivating, and overseeing the installation crews as well as ensuring the safety, quality, and efficiency of the crew, and ultimately the customer experience.</p>
<p><b>Duties &amp; Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ensure sub contractor crews on site and working</li>
<li>Manage subcontractor schedules and time reporting</li>
<li>Interact and support the sales team</li>
<li>Schedule projects and service work</li>
<li>Ensure sub contractor crews are working safely</li>
<li>Acquire necessary project materials, tools and equipment</li>
<li>Inspect job sites of each installation for quality and accuracy</li>
<li>Resolve project, customer, and staff escalations</li>
<li>Review project packets for completeness and accuracy</li>
<li>Oversee inspections</li>
<li>Update appropriate statuses in project management software</li>
<li>Communicate and coordinate with territory sales manager on a regular basis</li>
<li>Oversees P&amp;L for territory</li>
<li>Manage multiple jobs in multiple territories each day</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications &amp; Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>2+ years construction management experience</li>
<li>Roofing experience/knowledge required</li>
<li>Residential construction experience</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Excellent organizational and time management skills</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to manage projects; installation, operations, and troubleshooting</li>
<li>Excellent people skills, able to manage, motivate and discipline if necessary</li>
<li>Professional demeanor when working with customers</li>
<li>Experience with MS Excel, MS Word and project management tools (MS Project) preferred</li>
<li>Salesforce experience a plus</li>
<li>Ability to manage a $1-10M P&amp;L</li>
<li>Strong work ethic and grit</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What’s in it for You?</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Competitive Pay and Incentives</li>
<li>Opportunities for Self-development and Career Progression</li>
<li>Health Benefits, Allowances, 401k, STD/LTD, Life Insurance</li>
<li>Paid Holidays &amp; Vacation</li>
</ul>
