National and Alamo has an exciting opportunity for an ASE Certified Automotive Technician. The Automotive Technician performs car and bus service, repair, preventive maintenance, in-fleeting service, and vehicle reconditioning service according to company policy and procedures. This position requires the exercise of reasonable judgment and initiative. Performs all responsibilities with a focus on the needs of our customers and in accordance with our Quality Standards. Also spend time doing warranty and recall repairs in the body of the message</div>
<div>
The successful candidate will be required to work a flexible schedule to include weekends, nights, and some holidays depending on business demand.</div>
