<p><b>Job Description:</b></p>
<p>
The <b>Junior Energy Engineering position </b>will work closely with the sales team, the project management team, and clients and partners to design, manage and develop building energy improvements to reduce energy costs. The technologies include all facets of</p>
<p>
The successful candidate will support AEC in project development and management or commissioning in various manners. These support functions include by gathering and or entering data from on-site audits, performing electrical or engineering calculations for efficiency and facility improvement measures, and supporting other work.</p>
<p>
You will work on new and retrofit projects that improve facility operations, especially energy and/or water efficiency.
</p>
<p>Our projects also incorporate renewable energy whenever possible.
</p>
<p>The projects often address the following systems: lighting, lighting controls, HVAC, HVAC controls, water fixtures, ancillary systems and equipment (distributed generation, cooking kitchens, pools, etc.), renewable energy (solar PV, solar thermal, etc.) systems, and metering systems (energy, water, sub-metering, etc.). These projects improve the occupancy conditions inside the facilities while reducing operating and maintenance costs and the use of natural resources.</p>
