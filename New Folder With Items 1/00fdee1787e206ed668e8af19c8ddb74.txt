<h2>Marketing, Sales & Game Operations Internship - Winter/ Spring Term</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>The Marketing &amp; Sales internship program with the Cincinnati Cyclones and U.S. Bank Arena is a hands-on program assisting the Marketing and Sales departments in responsibilities regarding advertising, public relations, promotions and sales efforts for all events at U.S. Bank Arena. This includes games for resident hockey team the Cincinnati Cyclones, concerts, family events and even some local club shows promoted by Nederlander Entertainment. Interns will assist with projects pertaining to these aspects of our organization to increase ticket sales, awareness of events and overall implementation of marketing and sales efforts.</div>
<div>
The internship program is primarily compiled of, but not limited to three areas of work including sales and promotions preparation in our offices at the arena, grassroot promotional efforts around the city and near your educational institution, and game day operations experience.</div>
<div>
Game day is a high-energy environment. The best candidates for this internship will be able to learn and adapt quickly, multitask effectively, communicate clearly, keep a high level of positive energy, and show an overall passion for the industry and our brand.<br>
<br>
<b>Assigned tasks may include:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Compiling information for our advertising campaigns</li>
<li>Assist in the creation and implementation of promotional plans for upcoming events</li>
<li>Grassroots Marketing</li>
<li>Qualifying sales leads</li>
<li>Database management</li>
<li>Working events to ensure proper implementation of promotions and sponsorship fulfillment.</li>
<li>Developing and distributing flyers, posters and other promotional materials to locations around Cincinnati and Northern Kentucky</li>
<li>Researching promotional and sales opportunities</li>
<li>Fulfilling various clerical duties</li>
<li>Assist with game day operations</li>
<li>Office Organizational tasks</li>
<li>In-game game operations<br>
<br>
</li>
</ul>
<div>
<b>Candidates must be in a school certified internship program to qualify.</b> All positions are unpaid and candidates must be willing to work flexible hours, including evening and weekends. Please submit hours required from your educational institution with application.</div>
<div>
Candidates must be willing to work each event as required including nights and weekends.<br>
<br>
Candidates must be enthusiastic and willing to learn, marketing experience a plus.</div>
<div>
Internship Interviews will take place late October and early November. Jobs offers will be extended before Thanksgiving. Internship Term will be January until May. We have openings for Full Time and Part Time Internships.</div>
</div></div>
