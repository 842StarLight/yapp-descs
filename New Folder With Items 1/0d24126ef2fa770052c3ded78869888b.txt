<div>What you will be doing<div>
Under the general direction of the Warehouse Systems/Operations (WSO) Director assists with the implementation of the Warehouse Systems/Operations software manages the post-implementation operation of the software in an assigned divisional territory and assists with other territories as needed. (Note WSO applications includes but is not limited to: PkMS (WM) Slot Optimization (SO) WorkInfo (LM) Supply Chain Intelligence (SCI) Extended Enterprise Management (EEM) Jira and Confluence).</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Implement:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Assists in the development and coordination of various schedules and ensures DC participation in required project tasks.</li>
<li>Manages performs and coordinates DC tasks related to go-live preparation including but not limited to: site prep configuration testing training cut-over planning and go-live support.</li>
<li>Projects could include the initial deployment of the WSO software upgrades or significant new functionality being deployed such as Track and Trace manifesting PMA reports/dashboards and SlotInfo/LM integration.</li>
<li>Acts as primary facilitator between the DC and the corporate implementation team for operational process meetings functional gap analysis and configuration requirements.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Manage:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for the consistent utilization and performance of the WSO applications to adhere to AmerisourceBergen principles and practices or local business requirements.</li>
<li>Ensures that supervisory and system activities are getting completed in a timely manner; includes but not limited to: a) work tasks completed using standard processes b) system process or data exceptions are identified and resolved and c) routine data or configuration maintenance.</li>
<li>Responsible for set-up and utilization of system enhancements functionality reports/queries and processes.</li>
<li>Provides on-going leadership and training to DC management on proper work processes and functionality.</li>
<li>Develops contingency plans for the divisions in the event of WSO application issues or outages to mitigate operational disruption.</li>
<li>Manages the Change Control log and SOX compliance for the WSO application operation.</li>
<li>Creates management tools to best manage the WSO applications and shares such tools with other WSO Managers.</li>
<li>Responsible for systems management of all WSO software applications.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Monitor:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Identifies and communicates issues which impact cost configuration schedule and operation of WSO applications.</li>
<li>Monitors key metrics and performs periodic audits of system configuration and operational processes; develops and implements action plans for improving any unsatisfactory results.</li>
<li>Monitors inventory sync reports and investigates discrepancies for root causes.
</li>
<li>Ensures that PkMS configurations support desired WorkInfo engineered standards Standard Operating Procedures Slot Optimization requirements and SCI/reporting requirements.</li>
<li>Provides issue/status reporting as appropriately assigned by the WSO Director.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Support:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Provides ongoing functional and system support acting as first level support for DC Supervisors and Management; including but not limited to periodic after-hours support routine on-site visits to other sites within primary territory (minimum of 4 days ever 2 months) and periodic on-site visits to sites outside primary territory for activities such as go-live physical inventory and emergency support.</li>
<li>Executes the necessary WSO application configurations and functionality to manage physical inventories in the local territory working with the division management as required.</li>
<li>Assumes leadership and ownership of assigned issues and tasks through completion.</li>
<li>Conducts WSO system enhancement and business scenario testing as required.</li>
<li>Identifies investigates and resolves WSO application/technical issues.</li>
<li>Interacts with operational users to answer questions and to ensure that they understand how to use the system to complete their job functions.</li>
<li>Acts as liaison between distribution center management and other parties involved in WSO applications including IT/IBM and other AmerisourceBergen teams.</li>
<li>Supports implementation of new WSO application releases and modifications.</li>
<li>Supports WSO application implementations at other ABC locations as needed.</li>
<li>On a weekly rotating basis with other WSO Managers provides on-call support for the ABDC distribution centers running the WSO applications (inside and outside the local territory).</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Improve:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Identifies opportunities for system or process improvement.</li>
<li>Provides input concerning needed/desired system functionality processes interfaces and enhancements.</li>
<li>Defines requirements for system or operational process improvements.</li>
<li>Collaborates with other WSO Managers to compare and develop best practices functionality and metrics.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Slot Optimization Specific Duties:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for ensuring product is stored and arranged in locations that optimize total production efficiencies to include order fulfillment put away and replenishment tasks; location types include but are not limited to static shelving carton flow rack A-Frames and case pick-to-belt modules.</li>
<li>Ensures that put away and picking rules are in sync with the desired slotting profile.</li>
<li>Utilizing the “Slot Optimization” software tool and other user-created queries/tools define requirements for existing or projected storage configurations and provides item placement/move reports to facilitate product movement.</li>
<li>Working with applicable warehouse departments creates business goals objectives and constraints to configure the Slot Optimization tool to drive optimal and obtainable slotting results.</li>
<li>Comprehends the Slot Optimization software tool reports and PkMS reports in order to make sound operational storage decisions and facility rework decisions as necessary.</li>
<li>Executes PkMS functionality in order to update and retrieve data within the Slot Optimization software.</li>
<li>Plans coordinates and schedules the various slotting inventory moves to maximize operational results and minimize operational cost and disruption.</li>
<li>Develops and implements new processes and procedures when necessary; develops recommended changes to PkMS rules to optimize warehouse performance.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Other:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Manages repository of information.</li>
<li>Owner of site specific documentation such as operations guide system configuration issue tracking and change control.</li>
<li>Complies with all appropriate policies procedures safety rules and regulations.</li>
<li>Interacts directly with divisional management resources such as the Operations Manager Inventory Control Manager and Vice President / Distribution Center Manager.</li>
<li>Interacts directly with other AmerisourceBergen management.</li>
<li>Must be able to work overtime when necessary.</li>
<li>Performs related duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
If in a Lead WSO Manager role performs the additional duties and responsibilities:</b></div>
<div><b>
Manages WSO Managers:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Ensures that WSO Managers are sufficiently supporting their assigned divisions.</li>
<li>Ensures that WSO Managers are recording tracking and addressing division WSO issues and needs in a timely manner. Resolve gaps and/or escalate gaps to higher management as necessary.</li>
<li>Ensures that WSO Managers are conducting site visits to their assigned remote sites in an acceptable periodic time schedule.</li>
<li>Ensures that WSO Managers are deploying new WSO functionality as it becomes available and in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Tracks and manages the overall WSO Manager weekly schedule of business-as-usual and special projects.</li>
<li>Communicates with VPDCMs on a periodic basis to obtain their feedback about the WSO Manager process and performance.</li>
<li>Conducts annual performance reviews and performance plans with WSO Managers.</li>
<li>Ensures that division physical inventories are sufficiently supported.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Proactive management of multiple WSO Territories:</b></div>
<ul><li>Collaborates with other Lead WSO Managers to share and address standardization processes best practices iss</li></ul>
