 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Technical Inside Sales Representative - Software &amp; Peripherals
</b></p>
<p><b>Competitive salary</b></p>
<p><b>
Round Rock, TX</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>
If you are fascinated by emerging software and eager to further your career in technical sales, Dell is the place to be. Within <b>Inside Product</b>, we have a growing Software &amp; Peripherals (S&amp;P) business which includes third party software and client peripherals. Now, we are seeking a <b>Technical Inside Sales Representative </b>to drive and support the business from our <b>Round Rock, TX</b> office.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
Dell is a collective of customer-obsessed, industry-leading visionaries. At our core is a commitment to diversity, sustainability and our communities. We offer unparalleled growth and development opportunities for our team members. We believe that technology is essential for driving human progress, and we’re committed to providing that technology to people and organizations everywhere, so they can transform the way they work and live.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Key responsibilities</b></p>
<p>
Your role will involve delivering technical solutions that address customers’ varying needs, either working independently or with a subject matter expert on more complex solutions. You will:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Provide expert guidance and direction to the Core Sales team on Dell’s infrastructure and enterprise solutions</li>
<li>
Achieve or exceed targets by selling S&amp;P third party software solutions, developing strong relationships with our partners and distributors</li>
<li>
Challenge, manage and extend the existing pipeline, ensuring the right solution is created to meet customers’ requirements</li>
<li>
Respond to customers’ queries and calls</li>
<li>
Execute sales campaigns accurately and promptly, providing appropriate feedback</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Essential requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree in Business Management, IT or a related discipline</li>
<li>
At least two years’ experience in a sales function that shows a high level understanding of the value proposition of main software solutions</li>
<li>
Knowledge of third party software</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Desirable requirement</b></p>
<ul><li>
Previous experience working within multi-national software solutions sales environment</li></ul>
