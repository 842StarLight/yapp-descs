<h2>Enterprise Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Company Overview:</b><br>
Archibus and Serraview recently merged to form Archibus-Serraview. Archibus-Serraview is a global provider of cloud based, Integrated Workplace Management Solutions for today's employee-centric workplace. Our solutions are designed to increase real estate portfolio utilization, streamline operations, and enable smarter, more productive work environments .
<br><br>
<b>Position Summary:</b><br>
This is an outside sales role. Enterprise Account Executives are responsible for managing a territory in a specified geography with a focus on building new business. This is primarily a "hunter" role targeting Fortune 1000 prospects who manage large real estate portfolios. Archibus-Serraview is aggressively seeking top tier, "A" players in North America to accommodate our rapid growth.
<br><br>
<b>Essential Functions/Primary Duties:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Territory Management</li>
<li>RFP/Proposals writing</li>
<li>Customer presentations</li>
<li>Pipeline development with a target of 3x annual quota</li>
</ul>
<b>Skills and Experience:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 8+ years in direct software sales</li>
<li>Proven "A" player with high Sales IQ</li>
<li>Enterprise sales/complex sales cycle experience</li>
<li>Self-starter who works well in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>Strong verbal, written, and presentation skills</li>
<li>Demonstrated past performance against sales quota in similar role</li>
<li>Corporate Real Estate industry experience a plus</li>
</ul>
<b>Education:</b><br>

<li>Bachelor's degree</li>
</ul>
