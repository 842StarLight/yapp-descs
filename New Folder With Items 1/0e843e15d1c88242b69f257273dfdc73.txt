<div><b>
The Position:</b></div>
<p>
Accenture is seeking a strong thought leader with deep category expertise for the role of <b>Procurement Category Advisor</b> within the Technology Procurement Category Advisory practice. This position is focused on sourcing and category knowledge in the one or more of the following technology categories:
</p>
<ul><li> Cloud</li></ul>
<ul><li> Infrastructure</li></ul>
<ul><li> Telecom</li></ul>
<ul><li> Software
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Services</li></ul>
<p>
 As a Procurement Category Advisor, you will harvest market intelligence content in the key categories; infuse category insights and strategies into opportunity assessments, sourcing projects, and sales pursuits; and coach and guide sourcing project managers with strategic oversight on projects within respective areas.
</p>
<p><b>Key Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop and maintain category-specific knowledge assets and market intelligence that capture expertise / best practices
</li>
<li>Provide strategic sourcing oversight and category knowledge in support of the Sourcing Project Management function on projects within categories of expertise
</li>
<li>Infuse market insights into the sourcing process on projects within categories of expertise
</li>
<li>In collaboration with Sourcing Project Management and/or Procurement Practice Advisory, engage with customers’ senior management team members to provide perspective on current market trends to inform client strategic decisions/sourcing initiatives in line with business goals and objectives
</li>
<li>Leverage category expertise to support new sales and drive significant client business value
</li>
<li>Establish, grow, and leverage network of suppliers, industry practitioners, and procurement professionals within categories of expertise
</li>
<li>Work collaboratively with suppliers and customer stakeholders to identify continuous cost and performance improvement strategies
</li>
<li>Provide educational and learning opportunities to internal colleagues and external clients (ex. Webinar, Spend Trends publications) on industry trends, supplier landscape changes, and market intel within categories of expertise
</li>
<li>Coach and develop sourcing project managers in their professional development and pursuit of building category knowledge within area of expertise</li>
</ul>
</div>
