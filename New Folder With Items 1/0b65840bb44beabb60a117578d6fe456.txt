<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>This role plays a key role in supporting the daily business and people operations of a YMCA location or group of locations. This includes responsibility for invoicing, cash management, accounts receivable, accounts payable, vendor setup and supporting employee onboarding and timekeeping.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Scope of Responsibilities:</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Accounting</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Supports cash operations and transactions including daily batches, bank deposits and cash management. Processes daily contributions received at location.
</li>
<li>Processes accounts payable invoices, as well as purchasing and fuel card activity in a timely and accurate manner.</li>
<li>
Ensures proper coding for all expenditures.</li>
<li>
Resolves billing issues with vendors including sales tax refund requests</li>
<li>
Assists with month-end accruals and distributes month-end reports for end users</li>
<li>
Provides guidance to location supervisors and employees where needed; supports location operations and recordkeeping as assigned.
</li>
<li>Participates in professional development to further enhance skills and efficacy.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Employment Support</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Supports timekeeping including data input, reports, and other miscellaneous support.
</li>
<li>Provides assistance with employment eligibility verification</li>
<li>
Assists with maintaining accurate, electronic, employee records</li>
<li>
Assists with new openings and new IT access for employees</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Child Abuse Prevention</b></div>
<div>
Support the YMCA’s commitment to child abuse prevention by:</div>
<div><ul>
<li>
Report any items that may provide a health or safety hazard to staff, members or guests to your supervisor or the person responsible for those items at your site.</li>
<li>
Following all policies and procedures related to keeping children safe in our facilities and programs, including those related to managing visitors and guests at our facilities.
</li>
<li>Reporting any suspicious behavior and violation of policy and procedures to your supervisor.</li>
<li>
Completing all child abuse prevention training as required.</li>
<li>
Supporting the YMCA’s mission and center operations as needed.</li>
</ul></div>
<div><b>
Requirements:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Associate’s degree or equivalent with 1 to 3 years prior experience in an accounting function.
</li>
<li>Intermediate PC skills (Microsoft Office and Google documents) with intermediate skills in Microsoft Excel a must; ability to quickly learn and adapt to new technology and software</li>
<li>
Customer orientation with the ability to support multiple stakeholders both at location and in other departments/units; demonstrated verbal and written communication skills are essential.
</li>
<li>Strong attention to detail, accuracy and efficiency; demonstrated ability to take initiative, audit own work and manage multiple, competing priorities with minimal supervision.
</li>
<li>Ability to hold confidentiality and maintain discretion</li>
<li>
Ability to travel locally within region on a frequent basis. Travel may also be required locally to other YMCA locations including corporate office.</li>
</ul>
