<h2>Structures (Bridge) Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>
<div>Our Structures (Bridge) Manager will serve on a lead technical role on bridge design projects and perform design for all structural aspects of transportation projects on local, regional and national projects. We are looking for a self-motivated professional with strong technical and business development skills and that have a desire to grow personally as well as professionally.
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>Applicants for this position would have four primary functions – 1) lead/manage the technical execution of bridge design and study projects, 2) provide leadership and mentorship to junior structural staff, 3) work closely with client account managers and sales professionals to identify project opportunities and grow our bridge design services in Iowa, and 4) work closely with our National Bridge practice.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<br>
<div>Our Structures (Bridge) Manager:
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul><li>Serve as Lead Bridge Engineer and/or Project Manager on various bridge projects
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Lead successful delivery of quality projects
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Work in all aspects of structural design for bridges and other transportation structures
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Lead bridge teams in all aspects of bridge design, from conceptual and preliminary design to final design and construction
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Train, direct, and mentor junior engineers
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Assist in business development, proposal efforts and project pursuits to win work
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Have strong written and oral communication skills and a team-oriented attitude
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Network with bridge and transportation engineers throughout Jacobs and the local community
</li></ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>This position will be based out of our Des Moines office and may include limited travel.
</div>
</div>
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2>
<ul><li>Bachelor of Science Degree in Civil/Structural Engineering required, Masters’ preferred
</li></ul>
<ul><li>10 years minimum bridge design experience (5 years minimum Iowa DOT experience)required
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Iowa PE required
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Solid understanding of Iowa DOT practice, procedures, and standards required
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Solid understanding of and practical experience applying the AASHTO LRFD Bridge Design Specifications to transportation structures required
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Existing Iowa DOT and/or local county/city/municipal relationships preferred
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Active involvement in local engineering societies preferred
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Project management experience preferred
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Design-Build project experience preferred
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong experience in structural analysis and design software preferred<br>
</li></ul>
