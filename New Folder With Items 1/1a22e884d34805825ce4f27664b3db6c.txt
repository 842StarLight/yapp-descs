Hillrom is a $2.8B leading worldwide manufacturer and provider of medical technologies and related services for the health care industry, including patient support systems, safe mobility and handling solutions, non-invasive therapeutic products for a variety of acute and chronic medical conditions, medical equipment rentals, surgical products and information technology solutions. Hillrom's comprehensive product and service offerings are used by health care providers across the health care continuum and around the world in hospitals, extended care facilities and home care settings to enhance the safety and quality of patient care.<div>
</div>
</div>
<div>
<b>
Description</b><p></p>
<br>
<p>
</p>Hillrom is seeking a Commercial Marketing Manager to assist in the management of downstream marketing activities in the US for our mobile care team collaboration and communication tool. S/he will be responsible for the strategy and execution of product launch, digital marketing, content creation, as well as sales training, support and enablement activities resulting from our acquisition of Voalte. The Marketing Manager will collaborate and lead cross-functionally to help deliver continued growth for the assigned product line(s). This is an individual contributor role reporting in to the Senior Manager, Commercial Marketing Care Communications.<p><br>
</p>
<p></p>
<div>This position would sit in the home of our IT and Software division in Cary, NC in the heart of the Research Triangle area. Local candidates preferred.</div>
<p><br>
</p>
<p></p>

<p><br>
</p>
<ul><li>Develop annual Marketing strategy and execute selected tactics to drive financial growth for the assigned product line(s).</li></ul>
<p><br>
</p>
<p></p>
<ul><li>Be an internal subject matter expert for the assigned product line(s) including market trends, voice of customer and competitive activity.</li></ul>
<p><br>
</p>
<p></p>
<ul><li>Represent Commercial Marketing on product release cross-functional team(s). Develop and execute go-to-market strategy and launch plans for product line enhancements or feature additions.</li></ul>
<p><br>
</p>
<p></p>
<ul><li>Partner with Digital Center of Excellence to provide digital marketing strategy inclusive of website, social media campaigns, SEO/SEM, and digital ad placement.</li></ul>
<p><br>
</p>
<p></p>
<ul><li>Create and introduce innovative content including sales tools, digital marketing assets, and customer-facing content aimed at driving demand generation and funnel acceleration for the assigned product line(s).</li></ul>

</p>

<ul><li>Train Sales team on mobile product line messaging and positioning, targeting strategy, and market intelligence to support achievement of annual orders goal for the assigned product line(s).</li></ul>
