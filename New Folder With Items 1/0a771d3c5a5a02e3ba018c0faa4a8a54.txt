<h2>Publisher Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">The Publisher Account Manager will be responsible for managing the relationship with a portfolio of digital publishers across multiple platforms. The ideal candidate for this position will have experience with digital publishers, video ad platforms and/or ad networks, as well as a keen understanding of digital advertising and related technologies.
<br><br>
Skills
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>1 to 3 years of working experience in a technical account manager role within the digital advertising area.</li>
<li>A strong knowledge of the adtech ecosystem and technical market standards (VAST, VPAID, ad-serving, SSP, DSP, header bidding, etc.).</li>
<li>Experience with web standards (HTML, JS / JSON, etc.) and enterprise ad technology tools including Ad Manager.</li>
<li>Experience analyzing and drawing recommendations from large datasets using analytics software including Excel.</li>
<li>Knowledge of key industry partners for fraud and viewability.</li>
<li>Strong technical written and verbal skills with the ability to convey technical concepts in a way that is easy for clients, internal teams and non-technical partners to understand</li>
<li>BA / BS degree in Computer Science, Statistics, Mathematics, Engineering or Digital Marketing.</li>
<li>A previous experience in the programmatic industry is a plus (Trading Desk, DSP, ad-networks, etc.).</li>
<li>Passionate about media, data, analytics and technology.</li>
<li>Outstanding analytical problem solving skills.</li>
<li>Fluent in Spanish (Native Tongue) and Bi-lingual in English is required.</li>
</ul>
Responsibilities
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Manage existing publisher accounts and create new accounts.</li>
<li>Build strong relationships with publishers, providing recommendations to increase qualified inventory.</li>
<li>High-end monitoring (integration rates, inventory sources, etc.) and reporting on a daily basis.</li>
<li>Troubleshoot discrepancies and technical issues.</li>
<li>Provides support to publishers as subject matter expert on campaign, deals and integration insights.</li>
</ul>
