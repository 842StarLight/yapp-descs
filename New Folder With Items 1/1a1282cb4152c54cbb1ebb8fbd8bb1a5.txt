<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Details
</b></h2>
<p>Your mission is to be a Pardot customer champion: to listen closely and respond thoughtfully to their needs, working with our partners in Technology Marketing Product, Alliances, Customer Success Group, and beyond to drive customer success and growth. We leverage our relationships and expertise to scale our knowledge and inspire action across Salesforce, creating an ecosystem that drives Pardot adoption and growth to new and unprecedented heights.
<br>
<br>
The Salesforce Pardot Product Manager is a Subject Matter Expert on B2B marketing products, programs, and processes. This person drives the relationship and alignment to their respective Technology and Product stakeholders and helps to ensure that CSG Field and CSG Go-To-Market professionals have the tools and information they need to be successful in their jobs as it relates to Pardot products. The Pardot Product Manager is laser-focused on solving for the most relevant Pardot customer challenges, which may include early adopter customer use cases, Enterprise customer success, developing and distributing product and feature implementation best practices content, adoption programming, and field readiness.
</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Key Accountabilities (Impact)
</b></h2>
<p><br>
<b>STRATEGY
</b><br>
We are deeply invested in every aspect of the Pardot customer experience. We ensure that our customers’ voice is represented by serving as the linchpin across the CSG landscape, surfacing feedback and trends to our business partners and advocating directly for solutions that improve the Pardot experience.
<br>
<br>
<b>CUSTOMER ENGAGEMENT
</b><br>
We put our customers’ success first, building trust and delivering value in every engagement. We listen deeply, accelerate adoption, and help transform our customers, celebrating their success stories. We are all responsible for ensuring that the vision created meets our customers’ business value expectations. We measure success for our customers based on their metrics, not our own.
<br>
<br>
We maximize value for our most strategic and highest-potential Pardot customers, working alongside our Sales, Services, and Success teams to drive clear business outcomes. We bring architectural guidance, product expertise, innovation, and knowledge capital to our customers - and in return, they provide us with valuable perspective on our product, services offerings, and success strategy.
<br>
<br>
<b>SCALE
</b><br>
We scale Pardot expertise by creating relationships across the business, making intentional investments in the areas that benefit our customers the most. We work cross-functionally with our partners in the field to develop and transition our expertise across CSG, building a sustainable and self-powered ecosystem that drives success for all Pardot customers.
<br>
<br>
We empower, inspire and celebrate our Trailblazer community to help us deliver success at scale, integrating them into our own success programs.
</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Jobs To Be Done
</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Establish mutually beneficial relationship with Pardot product owners and stakeholders.
</li>
<li>Collaborate with the Technology, Marketing &amp; Product organization, and Customers to execute high touch and scale adoption programs.
</li>
<li>Identify business needs and opportunities within the CSG organization and develop solutions to better augment the field and solve for Pardot customer needs.
</li>
<li>Support the launch of the Pardot Ambassador program to scale expertise across the business .
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Develop Pardot based solutions and/or customer success programming using Salesforce technology for common customer use cases.
</li>
<li>Lead formal presentations, webinars and other learning events tailored to field delivery stakeholders (advisory, delivery, GTM, etc.) to showcase Pardot customer use cases and reference architectures.
</li>
<li>Provide ad hoc subject matter expertise as warranted via customer needs and business demands including but not limited to practice operations, pre-sales support, field assistance, Red Account guidance, etc.
</li>
<li>Collaborate, strategize, and solve for Pardot’s top strategic customer use cases via Pardot Evergreen (a customer success program) to encourage adoption of the Pardot platform and strategic features/products.
</li>
</ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Basic Requirements
</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Pardot Specialist, Pardot Consultant, Salesforce Administrator certified.
</li>
<li>Passion for strategizing and developing solutions for Pardot Enterprise customers’ specific use cases.
</li>
<li>Implementation experience with Pardot and associated products.
</li>
<li>Thorough understanding of the fundamentals of Pardot products and how our customers use the platform to manage their business.
</li>
<li>Interacts well with both technical and non-technical customers, attains relevant technical and business requirements, analyzes information and designs comprehensive solutions.
</li>
<li>Develops visually rich and professional conceptual design documents targeted to varying audiences of business and technical aptitude.
</li>
<li>Conducts solutions presentations and obtains customer acceptance to solution design.
</li>
<li>A particular desire to continually study new technologies and functionality, as well as be involved in projects that push the capabilities of existing technologies.
</li>
<li>Strong communication skills, both written and verbal. Able to effectively develop materials that are appropriate for the audience and evangelize best practices.
</li>
<li>Strong presentation skills. Able to effectively present and defend point of view to a variety of audiences.
</li>
<li>Demonstrates resourcefulness. Able to navigate ambiguity effectively to develop creative solutions to customer and business challenges.
</li>
<li>Coordinates effectively between stakeholders. Able to network across the business to align priorities and mutually accomplish goals.
</li>
<li>Ability to work independently and be a self-starter, prioritize, multitask and perform effectively under pressure.
</li>
</ul>
