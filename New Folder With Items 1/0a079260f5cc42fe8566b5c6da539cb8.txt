<h2>Operations Leader</h2>

<p><b>JOB SUMMARY: </b></p>
<p>As an OperationsLeader for Action Resources you are the pulse of the terminal responsible for growthand development of a profitable transportation operation. Coordinate and manage day to day operations todrive profitability and growth. Work withall Fleet Leaders, Operations staff, Drivers, Customers and Terminal Managersto increase the productivity and effectiveness of the fleet. Engage customers,team members and peers across the organization to drive business results. They will be proactive in their approach,continuously look for innovative ways to streamline processes and offercreative solutions to meet the needs of the business and our customers. Collaborate with other terminals anddrivers to meet the needs of the company with primary focus on their assignedmarket.</p>
<p>· Driverretention and development</p>
<p>· Safetyfocus every day in the areas of terminal and on the road</p>
<p>· Creatingand maintaining a great culture success and team development</p>
<p>· AssetManagement</p>
<p>· Buildtrust and relationship with existing and new customers</p>
<p>The OperationsLeader will be the driving force for the terminal in meeting and exceedingtheir terminal Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) for Operations, Retention,Safety &amp; Compliance, Customer Service, Financials and annual goals andobjectives.</p>
<p><b>DUTIESAND RESPONSIBILITIES: </b></p>
<p>· Focused on key areas of Operations: Safety, Service, Revenue, Assets andCosts</p>
<p>· Implement and support all company continuousimprovement and safety efforts.</p>
<p>· Serves as the liaison for company drivers, owneroperators and terminal associates</p>
<p>· Interact with both internal and external customersin a cooperative and professional manner to meet their need. Require the same levelof cooperation from the driver force; ensure superior customer service</p>
<p>· Be an effective change leader, as it relates toprocesses, structure and business modeling t.</p>
<p>· Driver advocate, involved with retaining,recruiting and hiring of drivers; treating drivers fairly, respectfully andhonestly.</p>
<p>· Coordinate daily terminal activities: supportingterminal specific operating objectives.</p>
<p>· Effectively handle escalation and time criticalissues, perform transactions in a timely and accurate manner.</p>
<p>· Collaborate and support the sales team inmanaging existing, targeting and supporting new business opportunities</p>
<p>· Develop and mentor staff through performanceevaluation and effective coaching</p>
<p>· Promotes and develops safe work behaviors througheffective communication, implementation and follow-up of the company safetyprograms and objectives</p>
<p>· Identify process breakdowns and develop improvementplans with partners to resolve.</p>
<p>· Responsible for fostering a positive culture</p>
<p>· Assist in effectively onboarding drivers andassociates to terminal processes</p>
<p>· Initial point of contact for personnel problems andtroubleshooting.</p>
