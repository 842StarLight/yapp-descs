<p></p>
<br>
<p>
PSE's Supply Chain &amp; Contract Management team is looking for qualified candidates to fill an open <b>Buyer</b> position!</p>
<div>
<div>
<div>
<b>
Job Description</b>
</div>
<div><p>The Buyer performs a variety of moderately complex purchasing functions necessary to procure materials, equipment and services, and negotiates one time and long term agreements with suppliers. Additionally, the Buyer is responsible for admininstering sourcing projects in order to achieve the best value on the purchases of commodities and services.</p></div>
</div>
<div>
<div>
<b>
Job Responsibilities</b>
</div>
<div><ul>
<li>Performs a variety of moderately complex purchasing functions in a fast paced environment necessary to procure materials, equipment and services.<ul>
<li>
Provides procurement information, including analysis of price trends and lead times for capital projects and O &amp; M programs.</li>
<li>
Interprets and applies existing guidelines and sourcing principles, practices, and theories toward the completion of assignments.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Ensures compliance with codes, standards and design, operation, and maintenance requirements as required by PSE or pertinent laws or regulations.</li>
<li>
Responsible for developing and managing competitive bid solicitations. Ensures achievement of the best value to the company by coordinating the competitive bid process and making recommendations for purchase order awards.</li>
<li>
Advises and supports various business units in contract administration and compliance consistent with corporate and risk management policies.<ul>
<li>
Negotiates long term agreements, amendments, contract change orders and settlements with suppliers.</li>
<li>
Assists in the administration of contracts including compliance with contract terms, dispute resolution, and coordination with other contracting agents.</li>
<li>
Assists with ongoing contract matters while maintaining positive, professional relationships with suppliers and other PSE departments.</li>
<li>
Coordinates and resolves contractual discrepancies with suppliers through collaboration and partnership to negotiate and implement mutually acceptable resolutions.</li>
<li>
Resolves invoicing and payments problems and resolution of preliminary disputes.</li>
<li>
Conducts research of supplier contracts to address inquiries from project managers regarding key contract provisions and terms.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Responsible for supplier management activities such as tracking service level agreements, coordinating delivery milestones, performance reporting, and assisting business leadership to resolve supplier non-performance issues.<ul>
<li>
Follows up with orders to ensure the delivery of materials and services are on time and to the right location.</li>
<li>
Serves as primary liaison and team coordinator between the supplier community and internal stakeholders.</li>
<li>
Establishes and maintains positive working relationship with outside suppliers, contractors, and consultants.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Collaborates and coordinates work with various internal stakeholders including Project Managers and Standards Engineers.</li>
<li>
Performs other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul></div>
</div>
<div>
