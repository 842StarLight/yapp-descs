<div><b>
Responsibilities</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Develops and maintains product lines in accordance with industry standards and individual customer requirements.</li>
<li>
Responsible for the engineering database (specifications, drawings, procedures) for assigned product lines.</li>
<li>
Consults with and coordinates the engineering effort with customers, Marketing, Manufacturing and Quality Assurance/Quality Control.</li>
<li>
Develops engineering specifications for customer special valves.</li>
<li>
Oversees the design and drafting in product development, and ensures layouts, drawings and documentation are prepared in accordance with company procedures and industry regulations.</li>
<li>
Generates procedures for product testing, repair and maintenance, cleaning, packaging, etc.</li>
<li>
Performs product testing to establish and validate product performance to specifications.</li>
<li>
Maintains a working knowledge of all Codes and Standards applicable to the assigned product lines. Reviews Code addenda and documents changes affecting Emerson products.</li>
<li>
May perform other special duties or projects as requested by Product Engineering Manager.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Position can base in either Stafford, TX or McKinney, TX.</b></div>
<div>
</div>
</div>
<div>
<div><b>
Requirements</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor of Science degree in Mechanical Engineering from an accredited university.</li>
<li>
Working knowledge of mechanical engineering practices, standards/specs, computers, engineering software, planning, communication, presentation, facilitation, and analytical skills.</li>
<li>
Prefer experience in design for welding piping systems, level measurement devices, pumps and valves. Demonstrated strengths in fluid dynamics; knowledge and experience in fluid flow.</li>
<li>
Prefer experience in SAP variant configuration (or ORACLE equivalent), AutoDesk Inventor vault, Teamcenter, NX</li>
<li>
Prefer experience working with products needing IBR, S stamp and PED certification.</li>
<li>
Experience in a manufacturing environment, preferably a metal cutting environment, with knowledge of machining methodologies and practices.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div></div>

Must have outstanding interpersonal, communication and leadership skills along with demonstrated ability to work in a diverse team environment throughout all levels of the organization.</li>
<li>
Candidate should be results oriented with good project management, organizational, prioritization, and team-based skills.</li>
<li>
Listening and conflict management skills are also important.</li>
<li>
Good organizational skills and problem solving abilities are necessary.</li>
