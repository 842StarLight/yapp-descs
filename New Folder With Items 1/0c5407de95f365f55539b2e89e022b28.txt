<h2>Manufacturing Engineer - CMM</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job description</b></h2>
<div>
<p>Position Summary:<br>
The position is responsible for programming, validating, and troubleshooting automated geometric measurement equipment and measurement processes. Additional responsibilities will include data analysis and process automation.</p>
<p>
Principal Duties and Responsibilities (*Essential Functions)</p>
<p>
1.* Program Coordinate Measuring Machines (CMMs) for high volume production inspection using Quindos 7 metrology software<br>
2.* Analyze data using Excel, Minitab, R, Cloud Compare, GOM Inspect and other applicable analysis tools<br>
3.* Troubleshoot CMM operation, data acquisition, and data processing issues<br>
4.* Validate new equipment, programs, and processes including designing and analyzing gage reliability and reproducibility studies<br>
5.* Formalize conclusions in technical notes, written reports, slide presentations and other written and visual formats<br>
6.* Ownership and responsibility for maintaining and improving Overall Equipment Effectiveness measures related to CMM equipment<br>
7. Provide written and verbal feedback to support product non-conformity treatment<br>
8. Write and update operator work instructions and general procedure documents<br>
9. Ensure accurate tracking of program changes through the diligent maintenance of program revision blocks and use of revision control and file management software<br>
10. Manage urgent work deadlines and high profile deliverables in an efficient manner<br>
11. Identify opportunities for process improvement and implement automated solutions<br>
12. Dependent upon business needs, some overtime may be required<br>
13. Ability to travel both domestically and internationally up to 10% of time</p>
