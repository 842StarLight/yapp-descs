Responsible for building and maintaining strong internal and external referral relationships, counseling mortgage customers around solutions to their lending needs, providing excellent customer service, and supporting all key channel initiatives and sales activities. ( Cross Sell Initiatives, External Sales Activities, Customer Satisfaction Results, and Loan Quality.) Responsible for meeting or exceeding personal production and strategic activity goals.
