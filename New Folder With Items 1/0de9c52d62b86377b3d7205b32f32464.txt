Position Purpose (Summary)
<br><br>
<b>The function of this position is to:</b><br>
This is a 120-day training position, after which the employee may be offered the opportunity to apply for a Service Plumber Technician position. There is no promise that a position shall be available or that the trainee will be offer employment after the 120-day training period. At the company’s discretion, it may alter the training period as it sees fit.
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Be willing participate following, helping, and assisting his assigned mentor learning and preparing for becoming a tech.</li>
<li>Perform other tasks as requested, including running parts, maintenance, janitorial, and clerical.</li>
<li>Eventually provide routine maintenances and basic services regarding plumbing repairs and installations.</li>
<li>Assist consumers by providing information on options for any plumbing and/or electrical need.</li>
<li>Be able to identify and report all issues that arise that would keep the plumbing from functioning properly, present hazards, or can be changed to improve the client’s experience.</li>
<li>Communicate to the consumer on the proper operation and owner’s maintenance of the installed equipment. Answer related questions as to operation, features, and functions to the homeowner.</li>
<li>Perform required documentation for each call, recording recommendations, repairs, accurate readings and observations. Have client sign necessary paper work and collect payments as directed.</li>
</ul>
