<p><b>About Workforce Insight</b><br>
Workforce Insight is a world leader in workforce management and analytics services. We believe in a People First<br>
Approach to help our clients and partners optimize and accelerate business results. Our solutions leverage expertise<br>
from the best talent across the industries we serve, including Healthcare, Manufacturing, Business and Consumer<br>
Services. The workforce management services we deliver utilize a holistic and time-tested approach, addressing<br>
Technology, Best Practices and Analytics to help our clients enable and sustain success. Our implementation and<br>
optimization services span across workforce management domains, including scheduling &amp; staffing, time &amp; attendance,<br>
labor cost &amp; productivity management. Our people and the services we deliver earn world class customer satisfaction<br>
ratings unmatched by anyone in the workforce management industry.

The Senior Consultant, Application Solutions works closely with teammates to ensure the maximum value of our clients’<br>
workforce management investment is achieved. Specifically, the primary objective for this role is to understand clients’<br>
current state and use expert knowledge and best practices to deliver solutions on time, within budget and with<br>
exceptional quality. The Senior Consultant, Application Solutions leads large, complex workforce management strategic<br>
and implementation engagements and manages these projects to successful completion.
