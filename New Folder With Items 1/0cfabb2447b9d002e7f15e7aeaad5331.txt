<h2>Senior Director, Global Customer Experience Strategy</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><b>SUMMARY</b></div>
<div>As the Senior Director of Global Customer Experience Strategy, partner with the business to create a deeper understanding of customers, create best in class customer journeys, understand customer and company objectives and together strategize and design end-to-end customer experiences to achieve these objectives. This role focuses on translating brand strategy and customer understanding to innovative, impactful content and resources that are used to drive positive customer experience and business results also acting as the business sponsor for master data management (MDM) customer and product master strategy. This role will be responsible for leading and coordinating the management of customer facing content as it relates to the specific stage a customer is at in their journey with ANSYS and will lead the effort to tie the appropriate content to the appropriate journey stage, helping our customers be more successful with our products. This role has a broad sphere of influence, touching multiple different business units, teams and digital touch points.
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>RESPONSIBILITIES</b></div>
<ul><li>Set an inspirational vision and establish clear objectives, goals and milestones for the customer experience strategy
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Lead the marketing team and business units through the process of identifying user needs, developing a vision for the ideal experience by researching, conceiving, sketching, prototyping and user-testing experiences for digital + non-digital customer journeys</li></ul>
<ul><li>In partnership with Sales and Customer Service, establish, refine and publish definitive key performance indicators (KPIs) to track and monitor customer experience</li></ul>
<ul><li>Develop new analytical frameworks, data science techniques, and predictive analytics to better understand our customers/users and the impact of our investments, including marketing attribution, churn prediction, lifetime value modeling, and segmentation
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Collaborate with ANSYS teams on Voice of Customer research and insights including NPS tracking, Customer Experience (CX) Feedback and custom quantitative and qualitative research, deepening our understanding of what delights our customers (and partners), driving improvement in the customer experience across channels and creating a culture of customer obsession throughout the organization</li></ul>
<ul><li>Liaise with Commercial Operations, Information Technology and Master Data teams to align road maps to journey map improvements</li></ul>
<ul><li>Partner with Commercial Systems Operations, Product Development &amp; IT to deploy improvements in products and infrastructure facilitating improved ease to install and support (ultimately improving adoption).
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Collaborate with Digital (Center of Expertise) CoE team to develop, evolve, and communicate digital strategies, standards, processes, and best practices. This includes creating, implementing, maintaining and socializing a digital roadmap and supporting business case development and investment validation; monitoring industry and competitive trends, creating digital UI/UX/visual standards and best practices, and/or digital analytics dashboards, targets, and KPIs
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Lead persona, journey and content mapping process to generate touchpoint level content strategy development and global deployment plan
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Identify areas for improvement in business processes and technology tools and work directly with commercial systems operations to build backlog for commercial systems technical stack improvements</li></ul>
<ul><li>Develop/support strategic planning, including positioning, market and competitive analysis, customer segment selection and penetration plans, and related solution positioning</li></ul>
<ul><li>Develop/optimize client advisory boards to identify key trends and execute upon initiatives that enhance the client experience</li></ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>MINIMUM QUALIFICATIONS</b></div>
<ul><li>Bachelor’s Degree</li></ul>
<ul><li>10+ years’ B2B experience with emphasis on web and/or application development methodology</li></ul>
<ul><li>Confident and decisive leader who models a team-oriented, collaborative approach across all levels of the organization and acts as a change agent</li></ul>
<ul><li>Broad understanding of customer experience processes and systems including customer insights and market research (including Net Promoter Score), journey mapping and persona development, experience design (including UX &amp; UI), finance, sales and marketing</li></ul>
<ul><li>High acumen for both the aesthetic and structural aspects of UX design and measurement with exceptional problem-solving abilities</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to lead, design, produce, and consult on a range of commercial resources to be used in end-to-end, integrated customer experiences</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to manage multiple projects at various stages of user experience design</li></ul>
<ul><li>Technologically-savvy, including expertise in traditional, digital and content creation and publication</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to deal with ambiguity, strategic agility, manage diversity and drive for results</li></ul>
<ul><li>Persuasive verbal and written communication skills to influence senior leaders in a fast-moving and highly-matrixed environment</li></ul>
<ul><li>Excellent project leadership skills to hold team members accountable for delivery on goals and expectations</li></ul>
<ul><li>Deep passion and expertise managing and analyzing all types of data including survey, text analytics and metrics that provide insight into consumer loyalty, lifetime value and digital behaviors.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Proven track record of building, influencing and developing highly-motivated, empowered, and high-performing teams</li></ul>
