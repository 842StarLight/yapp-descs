<h2>Senior Mortgage Loan Pricing Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Job Summary:</b></p>
<p>
The Senior Pricing Analyst will be a key member of the Capital Markets team. The objective of this role is to support and enhance our pricing and margin infrastructure so that NewRez achieves optimal profitability on every loan.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Functions:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Maintain internal pricing engines and execute margin and pricing changes.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with IT on the testing and enhancement of internal pricing engines.</li>
<li>
Manage the daily creation, distribution, maintenance, and enhancement of rate sheets and external pricing in a timely and accurate manner. Create solutions for rectifying discrepancies.</li>
<li>
Serve as the main point of contact in Capital Markets for all product development initiatives.</li>
<li>
Manage relationships with external pricing engines and vendors.</li>
<li>
Develop, design, and automate new reports and analytical tools to meet the needs of Capital Markets, senior management, and the production sales force on an as needed basis.</li>
<li>
Continually devise ways to automate and simplify all pricing activities and functions.</li>
<li>
Monitor all pricing systems and models for accuracy and consistency.</li>
<li>
Identify and eliminate margin leakage through system enhancements.</li>
</ul>
