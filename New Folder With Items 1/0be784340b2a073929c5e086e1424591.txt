<h2>IT Technician</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Company Summary: </b></p>
<p>PriorityWorkforce is one of largest privately held staffing companies in California. Headquartered in Tustin, PriorityWorkforce is a sales driven company that prides itself in creating dynamic business solutions. With over 25 years of expertise specializing in Light Industrial and Skilled Labor staffing, we strive to consistently deliver that “perfect match” between our clients and our candidates.</p>
<p><b>Position Summary: </b></p>
<p>Provides technical support to users and clients by researching and answering questions; troubleshooting problems; maintaining workstations, LAN performance, smartphones, and Microsoft products.</p>
<p>Pay: $14.50-$16.50/hr DOE</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities and Duties: </b></p>
<p>Fundamental functions of an IT Technician include but are not limited to:</p>
<p><b><i>1. </i></b><b><i>Maintain clear and proactive communication with clients &amp; customers</i></b></p>
<p>· Rapidly establishing a good working relationship with customers and clients.</p>
<p>· Installing our Time&amp;Attendance hardware at client locations.</p>
<p>· Negotiating with the client on ideal time of appointment.</p>
<p>· Troubleshooting the clients internal network and assuring compatibility with our hardware.</p>
<p>· Driving to and from Client locations to install Time&amp;Attendance hardware as well as troubleshooting software issues.</p>
<p><b><i>2. </i></b><b><i>Provide Level 1 support to troubleshoot, diagnose and resolve user/client software, hardware, and network problems.</i></b></p>
<p>· Installing and configuring computer hardware operating systems and applications.</p>
<p>· Monitoring and maintaining computer systems and networks and printers.</p>
<p>· Support users in the use of smartphones and other IOS devices.</p>
<p>· Assisting staff or clients through a series of actions, either face-to-face or over the telephone, to help set up systems or resolve issues.</p>
<p>· Troubleshooting hardware/software and network problems.</p>
<p>· Following diagrams and written instructions to repair or establish something new.</p>
<p>· Supporting the roll-out of new applications as well as testing within our environment.</p>
<p>· Setting up new users' accounts and profiles on AD.</p>
<p>· Prioritizing and managing many open tickets at one time.</p>
<p>· Rapidly establishing a good working relationship with customers and clients.</p>
<p><b>Ideal Traits for Success: </b></p>
<p>· Leadership</p>
<p>· Excellent written and verbal communication skills.</p>
<p>· Ability to thrive in a fast-paced environment and remain positive and energetic.</p>
<p>· Superior organizational skills and the ability to prioritize tasks.</p>
<p>· Effective problem solving- ability with the capacity for critical thinking.</p>
<p>· Ability to work flexible hours.</p>
<p>· Be Professional and dress Polished.</p>
<p><b>Requirements: </b></p>
<p>· Must know basic computer setup and cabling.</p>
<p>· Ability to setup a local printer.</p>
<p>· Valid driver’s license</p>
<p>· Ability to Drive to branches and clients in different regions.</p>
<p>· Strong computer literacy with proficiency in Microsoft Office and operating systems.</p>
<p>· High School Diploma or equivalent.</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Salary: $14.50 to $16.50 /hour</p>
