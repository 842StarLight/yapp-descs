<h2>Marketing Content Producer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p>The Graphic Systems Division of FUJIFILM North America Corporation is the largest graphic distributor in the US. We deliver solutions for workflow, consultative services, remote monitoring, color standardization and G7 implementation, pressroom products, CTP, proofing, digital printing, wide format and more. FUJIFILM also manufactures and distributes Sericol inks and is a leader in the development of UV inks for the screen printing, narrow web, and large format digital printing industries.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<div>
Support Fujifilm product managers and sales teams by producing marketing content with primary focus on shooting, developing and editing marketing videos that help meet product branding and sales goals and objectives. Oversee all facets of branded video production, including concept and story development, shooting video and sound, editing/post-production, and maintaining our video archive.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>External US
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Responsibilities:</div>
<ul>
<li>
Create compelling content that supports the campaign area to be applied toward GSD websites, Showpad, social media and email nurturing promotions</li>
<li>
Produce short promotional video content for Fujifilm’s Graphics Systems Division printing products that contribute to trackable results</li>
<li>
Primary marketing content producer point of contact for all product managers</li>
<li>
Consult with the sales team and subject matter experts to create and produce marketing videos needed to meet the business objectives of the organization</li>
<li>
Shooting, developing and editing high-quality product marketing videos based on direction supplied by product managers and marketing department</li>
<li>
Determine lighting needs, shooting angle, white balance, set or location details, and sound levels</li>
<li>
Work with press operators and product managers to develop shot sheets, determine product highlight to be captured and coordinate shooting schedule</li>
<li>
Direct people to capture specific action and events for camera</li>
<li>
Proofs projects, edits final video, ensures consistency and quality of product, and delivers video production</li>
<li>
Monitors and executes social media initiatives</li>
<li>
Analyze and measure usage and effectiveness of sales and marketing content and optimize as needed</li>
<li>
Set up and break down recording equipment before and after use including taping down cables for safety</li>
<li>
Perform routine video and audio equipment maintenance, recommending new and/or replacement pieces as necessary</li>
<li>
Ideate trade show video displays and messaging</li>
<li>
Stay current with trends, research new tools and brainstorm new ideas for campaigns</li>
<li>
Keep abreast with the knowledge of competitors and their marketing strategies</li>
<li>
Actively participate in meetings, seminars, conferences as required</li>
<li>
Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
