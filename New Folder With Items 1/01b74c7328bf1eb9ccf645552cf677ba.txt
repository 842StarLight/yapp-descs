<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Strategic Account Manager</b></h2>
<p><b>
Who</b> <b>is</b> <b>Equifax?</b></p>
<p>
Equifax is a global information solutions company that uses trusted unique data, creative analytics, technology and industry expertise to power organizations and individuals around the world by transforming knowledge into insights that help make more informed business and personal decisions.</p>
<p>
Regardless of location or role, the individual and collective work of our people makes a difference in our business.</p>
<p>
We are looking for individuals who can help us disrupt the marketplace. You will do this by delivering leading-edge technology to build and deliver unparalleled customized insights that enrich both the performance of businesses and the lives of consumers.</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Your Focus:</b></h2>
<p>
You will lead and drive revenue growth for the most prestigious Equifax relationships within the Workforce Solutions operating segment. Understand the client’s industry, business issues, and short / long range needs in order to develop and apply beneficial solutions; identify business problems or needs and formulate effective, valuable solutions and alternatives to the situation. Establish high level relationships; offer creative ideas that will enable the client to take advantage of all products offered by Equifax and grow existing revenue annually.</p>
<p><b>
You Key Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
You will establish, maintain and handle key customer relationships; renewing all business</li>
<li>
You will lead Equifax sales efforts for targeted account areas</li>
<li>
You will work seamlessly across Equifax with his/her cross-functional team in order to smoothly engage and coordinate all functional areas as required</li>
<li>
You will have assess the client's needs and leverage appropriate Equifax expertise/solutions for profitable sales</li>
<li>
On behalf of the target account, develop business cases to support solution development that include the "voice of the customer"</li>
<li>
You will Lead negotiations, sales pipeline, pricing and customer contracts</li>
<li>
You will represent Equifax at key customer meetings or customer sponsored events</li>
<li>
You will work with the NPI team to build applicable solutions</li>
<li>
You will collaborate with Equifax partners for integrated solution development</li>
</ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Requirement:</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>
3+ years of experience in a client facing, strategic sales role and/or account management role with software/cloud and or consulting services</li>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree required, advanced degree preferred; experience in lieu of a degree considered</li>
<li>
Strong understanding of large client accounts and ability to partner and influence the agendas of multiple client partners</li>
<li>
Knowledge of existing and emerging regulatory/compliance requirements, to leverage that knowledge to proactively drive sales</li>
<li>
Superior skills to present and communicate effectively with c-level executives</li>
<li>
Proven sales/solution selling experience, including the ability to diagnose the impact of trends, client data and MBOs and translate that into rapid sales</li>
<li>
Experience working with senior-level account employees, including an ability to immediately establish credibility with executives and build/maintain those relationships</li>
<li>
Extraordinary written and oral communication skills; familiarity/comfort in public speaking</li>
<li>
Shown excellent negotiation skills</li>
<li>
Experience working with cross-functional teams, demonstrated understanding of key skills across Equifax competencies</li>
<li>
Ability to drive urgency in the sales cycle and the execution of solution strategies; extraordinary ability to run a robust pipeline</li>
<li>
Excellent planning, organization, multi-tasking, prioritization and meeting facilitation skills</li>
<li>
Proven experience in driving significant revenue growth with highly complex strategic clients</li>
<li>
Travel up to 60% of the time</li>
</ul>
