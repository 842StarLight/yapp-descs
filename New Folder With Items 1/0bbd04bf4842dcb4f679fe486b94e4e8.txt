<h2>Laura Mercier Cosmetics Counter Manager, Full Time: Bloomingdale's Ala Moana</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div><div>
<div><b>Job Overview:</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>The Cosmetic Counter Manager is responsible for supervising the complete operations of the cosmetics line to ensure a high level of productivity and customer satisfaction through extensive product knowledge and demonstrating exceptional Selling Skills. The associate is also responsible for fulfillment and performing other duties as necessary.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><div>All hourly associates are eligible to receive a quarterly bonus incentive, colleague discounts and the opportunity to be a part of an iconic brand. Certain criteria must be met to receive the quarterly bonus incentive.</div></div>
<br>
<div>
</div>
<div><b>Essential Functions:</b></div>
<div></div>
<ul><li>Be proficient in POS and MPOS systems including Search and Send, and B*connected</li></ul>
<ul><li>Proactive in engaging the customer in using devices and technology to enhance the shopping experience.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Assist customers in all aspects of total store fulfillment and have the ability to satisfy the needs of our digital and physical customers by making appropriate partnerships when necessary.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Provide an exceptional customer experience by ensuring the customer is always the priority</li></ul>
<ul><li>Drive top line sales by consistently demonstrating exceptional Selling Skills
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Acknowledge customers in a friendly and helpful manner upon their entry into the area</li></ul>
<ul><li>Assist Beauty Advisor in converting potential return into a sale through courtesy and professionalism</li></ul>
<ul><li>Partner with Sales Staff to complete sales and add on items to increase IPT</li></ul>
<ul><li>Assist in training, coaching, motivating, and developing new and incumbent associates on company policies and procedures, product knowledge, and department productivity goals</li></ul>
<ul><li>Review and analyze business performance of daily, weekly, monthly, seasonal, and annual sales results</li></ul>
<ul><li>Review advertising calendar and ensure proper execution of events</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ensure accuracy of promotional presentation; communicate advertising and events information to associates</li></ul>
<ul><li>Assist in the coordination of operational tasks as required such as, putting out new merchandise, sales &amp; event set-up &amp; damages</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ensure timely processing of new receipts, damages, testers, and return to vendors through delegation to self and staff</li></ul>
<ul><li>Develop and implement business driving events to achieve department goals</li></ul>
<ul><li>Supervise proper presentation, organization, storing, and replenishment of stock</li></ul>
<ul><li>Sell merchandise through effective customer service techniques and professional selling skills</li></ul>
<ul><li>Communicate regularly with vendors, planners, distributors, and buyers regarding stock needs, customer preferences, &amp; special events</li></ul>
<ul><li>Develop a repeat-business customer base in order to regularly communicate: upcoming events, new merchandise receipts and product replenishment to increase incremental sales</li></ul>
<ul><li>Participate in physical inventory process</li></ul>
<ul><li>Demonstrate a professional, can-do attitude</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintain high customer readiness standards: deliver a clean, neat and easy-to-shop store environment</li></ul>
<ul><li>Adhere to Asset Protection and inventory control and compliance procedures
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ensure shortage prevention initiatives are executed</li></ul>
<ul><li>Perform these functions in an efficient manner, as directed by the Supervisor</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>Regular, dependable attendance and punctuality</li>
<li>Perform other duties as necessary</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Qualifications:</b></div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Education/Experience:</b></div>
<div></div>
<ul><li>High School degree or equivalent.</li></ul>
<ul><li>A minimum of one year of previous selling experience is required, preferably in cosmetics.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Previous supervisory is also preferred.</li></ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Communication Skills:</b></div>
<div></div>
<ul><li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to interpret instructional documents such as safety rules, operating and maintenance instructions, and procedure manuals.
</li></ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Mathematical Skills:
</b></div>
<div></div>
<ul><li>Basic math functions such as addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Able to use a calculator.</li></ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Reasoning Ability:</b></div>
<div></div>
<ul><li>Self-starter</li></ul>
<ul><li>Able to work independently and as part of a team.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must have good time management skills</li></ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Physical Demands:</b></div>
<div></div>
<ul><li>This position involves constant moving and standing.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Involves standing for at least two consecutive hours.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Involves lifting at least 30 lbs</li></ul>
<ul><li>May occasionally involve reaching above eye level, stooping, kneeling, crouching, and climbing ladders.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Involves close vision, color vision, depth perception, and focus adjustment</li></ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Other Skills:
</b></div>
<div></div>
<ul><li>Superior organizational and time management skills.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must be able to multi-task in a fast-paced environment.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must be able to build relationships and influence others.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must possess a thirst for knowledge.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must be able to lead by example.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to collaborate and function as a member of a team.</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>Must possess a strong sense of urgency</li>
<li>Should be comfortable with the use of computers and frequent use of RF equipment.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Work Hours:
</b></div>
<div></div>
<ul><li>Flexible with scheduling and available to work retail hours, which may include day, evening, weekends, and/or holidays based on department and store/company needs.</li></ul>
