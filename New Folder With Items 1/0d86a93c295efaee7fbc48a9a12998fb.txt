<h2>Marketing/Content Creator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Brief description: </b></p>
<p>The position of Content Creator consists of traveling to specified events and capturing marketing material to be edited and used in various ways. It also consists of working across departments to revitalize and expand brand recognition through graphic design promotional efforts. The content creator will be responsible for the contribution of information to any media, especially to digital media. They will target a specific end-user/audience in specific contexts as directed by the Sales Director. Their contributions include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Blog posts</li>
<li>Email newsletters</li>
<li>Social media copy</li>
<li>Video and Photo marketing/editing</li>
<li>Graphic design</li>
<li>Etc.</li>
</ul>
<p>They will also create offline content such as brochures, client packets, SDS/TDS, etc.</p>
<p><b>Tasks: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Work with Sales Director to develop new product release literature to ensure successful launch into the market</li>
<li>Work with print shop to update, freshen existing literature, catalogs, product labels in line with the agreed brand strategy as directed by marketing manager</li>
<li>Management of Social Media to ensure maximum returns</li>
<li>Gain market share</li>
<li>MAP pricing management</li>
<li>In line with overall established brand strategy</li>
<li>Maximize lead potential and management</li>
<li>Travel to trade shows and power sports events w/ sales force to capture photos and videos to be used in marketing efforts (will have the expertise to edit said photos and videos to be used in marketing materials)</li>
<li>Manage social media accounts (wipe all social platforms clean and create a unified theme from the ground up)</li>
<li>Daily posts to all pages on all forms of social</li>
<li>Respond to all inquiries on all forms of social (work hand-in-hand with sales and customer service to respond appropriately)</li>
<li>Create one YouTube video per month on both the Braap and Seymour pages (Hi-Tech to be included as the project gains traction)</li>
<li>Create promotional / how to videos for customers who desire them (i.e. Iowa 80 who wanted a Plasta Wrap promotional video)</li>
<li>Work hand-in-hand with Sales Director in creating marketing campaigns for new and existing product lines</li>
<li>Work hand-in-hand with the print shop in creating new and exciting labels and marketing materials</li>
<li>Work hand-in-hand with Regulatory Affairs and Sales Director in designing SDS and TDS sheets to be better utilized in sales</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications and requirements: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Preferably college graduate, but not required</li>
<li>Must be proficient in camera skills (taking photos, video, flying drones, etc.)</li>
<li>Must be proficient in editing said photos and video into promotional materials using the appropriate software</li>
<li>Must be able to adequately manage and build all social media platforms</li>
<li>Must have adequate writing skills</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Competencies: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Dependability — Job requires being reliable, responsible, and dependable, and fulfilling obligations.</li>
<li>Attention to Detail — Job requires being careful about detail and thorough in completing work tasks.</li>
<li>Cooperation — Job requires being pleasant with others on the job and displaying a good-natured, cooperative attitude.</li>
<li>Integrity — Job requires being honest and ethical.</li>
<li>Leadership — Job requires a willingness to lead, take charge, and offer opinions and direction.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Working conditions: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Full-Time</li>
<li>Travel often for trade shows, power sports events, and other promotional / marketing activities</li>
</ul>
