<h2>Spectrum Store Greeter</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><p><b>JOB SUMMARY</b><br>
The <b>Spectrum Store Greeter</b> will actively and consistently support all efforts to enhance the customer experience within the <b>Spectrum </b>Retail Store environment by being the first point of contact for customers, providing a welcoming presence as customers enter the premises and displaying a positive attitude.<br>
<br>
<b>MAJOR DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES OF THE POSITION</b><br>
Responsible for providing prompt and courteous customer service within the <b>Spectrum </b>Retail Store, while supporting other Store employees and Management<br>
<br>
Greet all customers in a friendly, professional and timely manner to ensure every customer feels welcomed and appreciated<br>
<br>
Manage customer traffic to afford customers the opportunity to browse the store without losing their service position<br>
<br>
Direct customers to <b>Spectrum Retail Store Associates</b><b>and Specialists </b>as availability and needs dictate<br>
<br>
Promote all <b>Charter</b> products and services with each customer interaction. Educate and engage customers through product demonstrations as appropriate and respond to customer questions as needed<br>
<br>
Provide support to Store Associates with sales related administrative duties, including assisting with inventory counts, maintaining store appearance and work stations<br>
<br>
Consistently demonstrate adherence to all Company and Store policies and procedures, while maintaining the confidentiality of customer and business data at all times<br>
<br>
Actively engage in training programs and demonstrate associated skills and competencies<br>
<br>
Perform other duties as may be requested by management<br>
<br>
<b>REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS</b><br>
Ability to read, write and speak the English language to communicate with other employees and customers in a clear, straight-forward, and professional manner<br>
Detail oriented and a good problem solver<br>
Possess a self-motivated, competitive spirit and desire to provide a good sales and customer service experience<br>
Positive and professional demeanor<br>
Flexibility to work in other locations as the needs of the business dictate<br>
Basic math skills<br>
Knowledge and ability to use computer and software applications<br>
Ability to multi-task in a fast-paced team environment<br>
<br>
<b>PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS</b><br>
Current knowledge of latest technology and devices<br>
<br>
<b>Education</b><br>
High School Diploma or equivalent required<br>
<br>
<b>Related Work Experience</b><br>
1+ yr.: Sales experience<br>
3+ yrs.: Customer Service experience<br>
<br>
<b>WORKING CONDITIONS</b><br>
Retail environment<br>
Exposure to moderate noise level<br>
Work scheduled shift hours and overtime as needed<br>
Ability to handle a physically demanding job, often times lifting up to 35 lbs.<br>
Ability to stand for prolonged periods of time<br>
Required to wear a uniform</p></div>
