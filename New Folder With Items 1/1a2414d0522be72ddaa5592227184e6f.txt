<p><b>POSITION SUMMARY:</b></p>
<p>Responsible for job assignment, technician tracking, and job rescheduling functions using technical workforce management tools in the Cable TV services industry. Also, responsible for relaying and recording information to field personnel via telephone, messaging or other forms of communication using all tools available.</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS OF THE JOB INCLUDE, BUT ARE NOT LIMITED TO:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Dispatching service requests to available installation or service technicians</li>
<li>Assist in driving field productivity through efficient call handling and support.</li>
<li>Record information into computer to provide accurate reporting as needed</li>
<li>Assist with requesting locates for some or all crews as needed, daily</li>
<li>Use CSG, WFX, ACSR, PenguinData, and Manage Drop Board</li>
<li>Close work orders using proper solution and clear codes as needed</li>
<li>Perform other duties as requested by supervisor.</li>
</ul>
