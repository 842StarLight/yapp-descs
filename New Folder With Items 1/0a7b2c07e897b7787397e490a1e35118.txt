<h2>Freight Broker</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Pro Star Logistics is one of the nation’s leading third party logistics providers, headquartered in Salt Lake City, UT., and our Operational Offices are growing across the <i>US</i>. We are a licensed and bonded LTL and TL freight broker building personalized relationships worldwide for over 20 <i>years</i>. Our company is dedicated to the growth and success of our employees, customers and corporate values.</p>
<p>
Pro Star Logistics currently has five TL Operational Offices, and is looking for experienced Brokers to join our <i>team</i>. Pro Star has an experienced <i>team</i> of Sales Professionals currently growing our customer base, so a book of business isn’t required. However, a customer base is a definite positive.</p>
<p>
Pro Star Logistics’ <i>team</i> of Brokers offer a diverse, multi-modal offering, allowing <i>us</i> to Broker a wide variety of commodities. We are especially looking for an experienced Intermodal Broker with a book of business, as our customers have many IM needs.</p>
