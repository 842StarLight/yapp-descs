<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Description</b></h3>
</div>
<div>
<p>Suburban Electric is looking to hire a Service Coordinator to assist in our Pre-Construction department. The ideal candidate will have strong communication, and organizational skills while being self-motivated. Join the team and let Suburban help you reach your highest Potential!</p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
WHAT YOU’LL BE DOING:</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>Assists with analyzing data and creating reports for bid history, market analysis, and sales</li>
<li>Works with the PCS team or individuals on creating process improvements for group and company</li>
<li>Sets up projects with projected costs, logs change orders, creates invoices, and works with payroll for PCS revenue area</li>
<li>Prepares bid and plan documents, assists with takeoff, and provides needed support for estimating and engineering projects</li>
<li>Works with Director of Preconstruction on contract review process and tracking of contracts</li>
<li>Tracks all aspects of estimate requirements in CRM, M-drive, Box and/or accounting software, maintain all relevant logs and spreadsheets</li>
<li>Works alongside estimators, project managers and senior managers to assist with developing customer presentations</li>
<li>Work under deadline pressures in an efficient, composed and calm manner</li>
<li>Support, assist, organize, and communicate with PCS team members on various projects and systems</li>
<li>Creates and assists with new and existing initiatives to drive business development, including social media, marketing material, sales tools, account management, expo/conventions, and branding</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>WHAT YOU’LL BRING TO THE TABLE:</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>A high school diploma or GED is required</li>
<li>Must have strong verbal and written skills</li>
<li>Must be proficient in Microsoft Office including Word, Excel, and PowerPoint</li>
<li>Familiarity with estimating software a plus</li>
<li>Able to read construction drawings a plus</li>
<li>Team player, self-motivated, gets along well with supervisors and peers</li>
<li>Demonstrates a natural ability for striving to bring in business for our company</li>
</ul>
