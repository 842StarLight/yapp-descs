<h2>Sr. Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>JOB OVERVIEW</p>
<p>The Sr. Marketing Manager provides<br>
leadership within global marketing for strategy development and key initiative<br>
project management. This position develops and leads both US and OUS-focused<br>
initiatives to support the objectives and growth goals for all country markets,<br>
initially prioritizing US strategies and tactical execution. The Sr. Marketing<br>
Manager is the primary commercial owner of MediLynx arrhythmia diagnostics systems<br>
and services, performing both upstream and downstream modes of marketing. All<br>
strategic initiatives recommended by and then executed by the Sr. Marketing Manager<br>
will be supported by vigorous research of all served and targeted country<br>
markets, including competitive, internal, customer, patient and technology<br>
drivers. This position will set, commit to and meet key performance metrics for<br>
all key initiatives, with a bias for sustainable, exponential and profitable<br>
growth.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>ESSENTIAL<br>
FUNCTIONS</p>
<ul><li> Drive</li></ul>
annual strategic marketing planning for the US and OUS markets
<ul><li> Lead</li></ul>
directed market research for all served and targeted country markets
<ul><li> Provide</li></ul>
marketing support for all international country markets and distributors to<br>
ensure achievement of individual country goals
<ul><li> Regularly</li></ul>

<ul><li> Support</li></ul>

matter expert (SME)
<ul><li> Owner</li></ul>
of industry tradeshow and customer event initiatives
<ul><li> Develops</li></ul>

<ul><li> Drive</li></ul>
the external and internal communications process for all owned initiatives
<ul><li> Owner</li></ul>

<ul><li> Develop,</li></ul>

<ul><li> Develop</li></ul>

<ul><li> Drives</li></ul>
