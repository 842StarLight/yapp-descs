<p>
As part of the Toyota family of companies, Toyota Industries Commercial Finance (TICF) creates extraordinary customer experiences through our Brand Promise to be the partner of choice in the commercial finance industry.<br>
Since 1985, TICF has offered an extensive line of commercial financing programs for Toyota forklifts and Hino trucks through our network of Toyota Industrial Equipment and Hino Motor Sales dealers to their customers. Our people-focused team of professionals are constantly striving to provide always better solutions. Because of that we have experienced substantial growth and evolution. We are a new organization with a proud legacy, <i>Are you interested in becoming part of it?</i></p>
<p><b>
TICF Vision Statement</b>: To be the premier captive finance company that champions Toyota Industries Corporation, Hino Motors and dealers' prosperity.</p>
<p><b>
TICF Mission Statement</b>: Provide financial solutions to sell Toyota Industries, HINO Motors &amp; Dealer products.</p>
<p><b>
Major Duties and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p>
We're looking for an experienced Marketing Manager to develop and manage all company marketing and communications initiatives, oversee all digital and social media strategies including ongoing content development and maintenance. Management of campaigns across various channels required in support of strategic business objectives, lead acquisition, customer retention, business development and extensive company brand building.</p>
<ul>
<li>Provide marketing leadership across the organization with demonstrated adaptability and collaboration skills.</li>
<li>Lead the execution of end-to-end marketing campaigns and initiatives driving collaboration with internal stakeholders, external agencies and vendors.</li>
<li>Work with sales team to lead in the conceptualization, development and execution of marketing communications and promotions in support of sales objectives.</li>
<li>Develop formal company Marketing Plan based on short and long-term business objectives, market trending and competitive analysis.</li>
<li>Responsible for company web design and development.</li>
<li>Develop formal company value proposition along with associated communication materials and messaging.</li>
<li>Oversee company brand development and management, tactical design, and external marketing vendor management.</li>
<li>Assist in the development and execution of internal and external sales force training materials and platforms.</li>
<li>Actively participate in the annual budgeting and planning processes.</li>
<li>Manage and make recommendations for company participation in industry conferences, trade shows and OEM events while maintaining strong relationships with key stakeholders/vendors.</li>
<li>Conduct consumer and dealer satisfaction research where necessary.</li>
<li>Develops communication plan for various dealer incentive contests, including regular results cadence and ROI metrics.</li>
<li>Stay current on market, competition, and trends including analyzing current marketing tactics, concepts, practices and procedures.</li>
<li>Responsible for Dealer and Customer Survey activities</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Who You Are</b>
</p>
<p><b>Marketing Manager Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's Degree</li>
<li>Minimum 8 years in marketing, advertising and/or brand management preferably in a creative agency environment.</li>
<li>Comprehensive knowledge and competency in all areas of the marketing spectrum including but not limited to digital marketing, lead generation, SEM, SEO, social media, branding, email marketing and creative development.</li>
<li>Must have a track record of successfully executing marketing initiatives on-time and on-budget.</li>
<li>Proficiency in measuring campaign performance and associated ROI</li>
<li>Experience preparing executive level presentations and interacting with leaders at all levels of the organization.</li>
<li>Proven track record of developing and implementing marketing strategies and concepts, particularly in partnership with sales management.</li>
<li>OEM / Brand Partner relationship building experience a plus</li>
<li>Excellent creative writing and verbal skills are a critical requirement.</li>
<li>Able to travel as necessary (up to 20%) with occasional weekend travel required.</li>
</ul>
