 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Could be remote for the right candidate - Must be willing to travel up to 25% - Must still live in the USA. This is a full time salaried benefited position I am unable to utilize a Corp. to Corp. relationship on this position.</p>
<p>
As an XTIVIA DevOps Engineer, you will provision, enhance, and maintain cloud and on-premise infrastructure for our clients on both small and large-scale projects spanning a number of vertical markets. Responsibilities will include working with identity and access management, network architecture, application security, logging, monitoring, billing, and more. You will help to develop the optimal design for secure, scalable infrastructure for customers, including networking, encryption, load balancer setup, caching, compute optimizations, continuous integration, and delivery pipelines. You'll leverage automation tools like Ansible, Terraform and CloudFormation and platforms like Kubernetes, Fargate, and ECS to deploy and maintain cloud compute environments as well as on-premise infrastructure.</p>
