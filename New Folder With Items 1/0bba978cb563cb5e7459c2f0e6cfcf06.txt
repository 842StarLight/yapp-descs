<h2>Outside Sales Professional (Cloud Managed Services)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>SUMMARY:</b></p>
<p>
As an Outside Sales Professional, you will cultivate new customers to grow and promote the sale of our Microsoft Azure cloud based managed service offering. The Outside Sales Professional is the first line of contact with all new customers and along with their Technical Sales Team, will establish business relationships. This role requires a self-starter, motivated to grow business in the SMB market in Washington and Oregon. The Outside Sales Professional will work regularly with other team members to develop a sales strategy and has the opportunity to grow with the organization.</p>
<p><b>
KEY RESPONSIBILITIES</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Develop new business</li>
<li>
Close and grow sales through professional communication with potential clients in our marketplace</li>
<li>
Manage, interpret customer requirements by speaking with clients to understand, anticipate their needs</li>
<li>
Communicate sales or service opportunities and customer concerns or suggestions</li>
<li>
Expand market awareness of our products and our industry experience by communicating features and benefits of our products and services effectively</li>
<li>
Maintaining records of customer communications and contact information as required within CRM</li>
<li>
Making presentations to Decision Makers and Prospective Buyers</li>
<li>
Preparing responses to RFP (request for proposals) and RFI (request for information)</li>
<li>
Attending and participating in trade shows, conferences and other marketing events</li>
<li>
Maintain awareness of pertinent client information, future plans; communicate any client information that may affect company decisions as needed</li>
<li>
Researches market requirements and market data</li>
<li>
Maintain knowledge of competitor products and identify and report intelligence on competitor pricing or marketing strategies</li>
<li>
Submit a variety of sales status reports as required, including activity, closings, follow-up, and adherence to goals</li>
<li>
Submit prospective customer engagement plans; schedule client meetings and action plans for follow-up</li>
<li>
Is chiefly responsible for setting the customer's expectations, for any sold product and service</li>
</ul>
<p>
KEY PERFORMANCE INDICATORS:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Meet Monthly Sales Quotas, as defined.</li>
<li>
Perform and Document a Minimum of One Hundred (100) Cold Calls per week</li>
<li>
Participate in Sales Accountability Meetings</li>
<li>
Schedule and attend Customer meetings</li>
</ul>
<p>
QUALIFICATIONS:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Three (3) to Five (5) years of proven Sales Experience, in actively closing Net-New Business Prospects</li>
<li>
Technology sales and/or HaaS, SaaS experience a plus.</li>
<li>
Strong interpersonal, communication (verbal and written) and organizational skills</li>
<li>
Previous experience with delivering Presentations to Potential Clients</li>
<li>
Valid Driver's License</li>
<li>
Microsoft Office proficiency required, especially in Excel, PowerPoint, and Outlook</li>
<li>
CRM Experience</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
SKILLS &amp; KNOWLEDGE:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Perceived capacity to exude Personal Credibility and Professional Integrity</li>
<li>
Positive attitude, towards people and problems</li>
<li>
Ability to self-start, work independently and achieve high standards to meet multiple deadlines</li>
<li>
Explicitly proficient communicator, including writing, speaking, editing, proofreading and computer skills</li>
<li>
Above-par working capacity, to work in small and large projects with great attention to detail.</li>
<li>
Work effectively with staff in a team-oriented environment with a positive, can-do attitude.</li>
<li>
Adept in developing and maintaining strong relationships with management, staff, vendors and clients.</li>
<li>
Exhibit a high degree of professionalism, balanced business judgment, tact and diplomacy.</li>
<li>
Excellent problem-solving skills, in both mundane and highly-sensitive, albeit complex situations.</li>
</ul>
