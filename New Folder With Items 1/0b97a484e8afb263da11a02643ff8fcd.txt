<p><b>This is a junior enterprise-level business development position, designed to develop an individual to a full scale role as an Account Executive, Sponsorship Sales Manager, Sponsorship Marketing Manager, or Market Response/Vendor Relationship Representative within 3-6 months. <li>Be paid fairly and transparently for everything you do with uncapped commissions plus a guaranteed base salary and residuals on your book of business</li>
<li>Speak with Top executives at Fortune 1000 companies</li>
<li>Develop global business expertise and global career opportunities</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>This role is perfect for recent college graduates with little to no sales/marketing experience, </i></b><i>who are interested In learning how to speak to and negotiate complex deals with C-level executives at SME and F-1000 companies; </i>Learning about various different industries, and eventually becoming a subject matter expert in those fields.<br>
