<h2>Experienced Healthcare Insurance Verification Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Looking for an exciting opportunity with a fast growing company? ABC Home Medical Supply, Inc. is currently seeking an experienced Insurance Verification Manager to join our team!</p>
<p>SUMMARY:</p>
<p>The Insurance Verification Manager is responsible for developing, supervising, guiding, motivating, and evaluating ABC Medical’s Insurance Verification team while implementing department policies. The Insurance Verification Manager is responsible for overseeing that all customer insurance eligibility verifications are timely, complete and accurate and will report directly to the Director of Revenue Cycle Management.</p>
<p>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</p>
<p>Include the following as well as other duties and responsibilities, which may be assigned.</p>
<ul>
<li>Develops plans to structure ABC Medical’s insurance eligibility processes to comply with the company’s current and changing needs.</li>
<li>Develops policies and procedures for the handling of new and existing customer insurance eligibility verifications.</li>
<li>Proactively monitor the payor coverage guidelines to identify areas of risk that could lead to noncompliance and/or bad debt.</li>
<li>Monitors the accurateness of customer insurance information.</li>
<li>Drives continuous improvement of internal processes.</li>
<li>Continuous achievement of SLA’s.</li>
<li>Coaching and Mentoring of the Insurance Verification team members.</li>
<li>Supervise, guide, motivate and evaluate Insurance Verification team personnel.</li>
<li>Orients new staff to the relationship and interaction of the Insurance Verification team with other company staff.</li>
<li>Commitment to personal development to ensure you enhance your leadership, management, planning, and communication</li>
<li>Any other tasks or deliverables that are assigned to you by the management team as they see</li>
<li>Adheres to departmental and company policies and procedures.</li>
<li>Develop and Maintain relationships with Sales team, Referral sources, and Internal departments.</li>
</ul>
<p>QUALIFICATIONS:</p>
<p>To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily and must possess the ability to: manage, develop and support a team of Insurance Verification Specialist; interact professionally and ethically with customers, staff, payors and providers; handle multiple tasks simultaneously; provide clear, concise oral and written directives/communications; quickly assess situations and respond appropriately; handle special requests in a sensitive, professional manner. The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill, and/or ability required. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</p>
