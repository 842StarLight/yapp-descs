<h2>Customer Marketing Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>SEMrush</b> is a leading digital marketing toolkit for SEO, PPC and content marketing professionals worldwide. With over 30 tools within the software, search data for 140 countries and seamless integration with Google services, SEMrush provides solutions for in-house marketing teams and agencies working with clients in any industry.</p>
<p>
In its niche and pricing segment, SEMrush is an absolute leader in the number of tools available per one account and the richness of data. By ensuring the easy workflow between the marketing teams members, SEMrush helps them become front-runners in their industries.</p>
<p>
SEMrush’s Customer Marketing Specialist position will be measured on the ability to actively recruit and develop top-tier customer advocates and broadly leverage success stories at strategic levels (events, media, analysts) and for tactical activities.</p>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Our expectations</b></h4>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Work closely with the Sales department to identify and recruit SEMrush customers as advocates to participate in marketing and public relations activities showcasing various business benefits and outcomes that are generated thanks to our tool.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Create a consistent and stable pipeline of references.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Proactively schedule and conduct professional interviews with customers, create written record of interview.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Work closely with Paid Social/Digital Marketing team to ensure new cases are well-promoted and generate first payments as well as new registrations.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Work closely with designers to create engaging visual content based on the cases (static, video, gifs etc).</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Ensure our brand is represented accurately and messaging is appropriate in all reference materials.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Ensure Geo participation and increase program adoption and visibility.</p>
<br>
</li>
</ul>
