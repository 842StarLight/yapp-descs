<h2>Lead CNC Programmer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>The ideal candidate will be a highly detail orientated and multi-tasking able Machine Tool Setter or experienced CNC operator looking to grow. Leadership opportunities are available for those candidates that have excellent interpersonal skills and are looking to forward their career in a small business environment! Availability for both First and Second shifts available.</p>
<p><b>Primary Responsibilities: </b></p>
<p>· Program and design automated manufacturing processes and CNC machine operations.</p>
<p>· Train and mentor lower level machinists and CNC operators.</p>
<p>· Define and improve routing times and design of routing flow between machines.</p>
<p>· Work with management on process and operation improvement initiatives including 5S, SMED and TAKT time.</p>
<p>· Create and maintain setup and change over instructions.</p>
<p>· Consult with management on production machine acquisitions.</p>
<p>· Work with sales to evaluate opportunities and qualify manufacturing capability of new parts for sale.</p>
<p>· Other miscellaneous duties as required.</p>
<p><b>Qualifications: </b></p>
<p>· 4 years of experience with CNC setup of lathes, mills, swiss and mill-turn machines</p>
<p>· Working knowledge of writing and evaluating G-Code. Experience with Mastercam and CAD software is a plus.</p>
<p>· Understanding of materials and manufacturing processes.</p>
<p>· Technical writing style and supervisor level communication.</p>
<p><b>Requirements: </b></p>
<p>· Applicant must be a US citizen.</p>
<p>· Job is located in central Connecticut.</p>
