<h2>Head of B2B Marketing</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Head of B2B Marketing</b></p>
<p>
The Head of B2B Marketing is responsible for leading and contributing to projects that drive growth with our retailers and other corporate verticals. This leader will be responsible for the development and management of broad-based B2B marketing programs, implementation and results. They will develop marketing plans and quarterback marketing efforts across the organization including lead generation, digital development, strategic sponsorships/events, and content creation, and sales collateral. This high-impact role will work across functions as well as interact with clients, partners, and senior leaders.</p>
<p>
 <b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p>
Collaborate with leadership to develop and execute our B2B annual marketing strategy (including vertical programs) in order to support our customer acquisition/retention goals and the company’s business objectives. Collaboration with an array of cross-functional team members is essential to success. This position requires a candidate with a blend of strategy, technical, creative, analytic and project management expertise.</p>
<ul>
<li>Accountable for B2B marketing strategy and all B2B marketing initiatives: provide short and long-term marketing plans to both guide and support sales teams with B2B clients/verticals</li>
<li>Focus on acquiring and retaining new B2B clients through the management of all online and offline marketing programs that could include: marketing automation, lead generation, SEO/SEM, digital advertising, webinars, content creation/distribution, email, industry conferences/events, social media, lead generation/nurturing, channel marketing, website updates and analytics</li>
<li>Develop a program to further our authoritative voice in this space and within key verticals</li>
<li>Institute lifecycle marketing/pipeline management best practices and continuously monitor campaign activity; establish A/B tests, monitor results and make proactive course corrections to ensure that initiatives meet expectations</li>
<li>Hands-on database marketing experience with solutions like Salesforce to nurture, track and optimize the funnel</li>
<li>Write briefs that inspire the creative team to bring our brand to life in integrated marketing campaigns. Maintain a marketing calendar for B2B and roadmap with major activities, milestones, etc.
</li>
<li>Manage multiple projects and campaigns at the same time, and ensure clear and proactive communication with internal and external partner teams</li>
<li>Maintain an intimate understanding of our clients and their needs; own the brand positioning within our verticals to ensure messaging is relevant and resonates.
</li>
<li>Perform industry and competitive analysis to inform the sales process and help positioning and messaging</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in Business, Marketing or Communications; MBA preferred but not required
</li>
<li>7+ years experience directly authoring and managing B2B integrated marketing programs
</li>
<li>Intimate understanding with mid-marketing and small businesses with time spent acquiring prospects to use a software or join a program</li>
<li>Proven track record managing pipeline and demand generation, including managing campaigns from concept to ROI analysis</li>
<li>Be scrappy and self-directed - you'll have to be the roll up your sleeves type and work autonomously
</li>
<li>Understand data and use it as your guiding light. You'll be good at running a/b tests, digging through funnels, and optimizing them</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills – strong written and oral communication skills</li>
<li>Strong analytics and business acumen including experience reviewing performance results and making recommendations for future actions</li>
<li>Dynamic, motivating, and entrepreneurial-minded and team player with an internal drive to hit goals and deadlines. Ability to multi-task, prioritize and meet deadlines in a dynamic fast-paced environment</li>
<li>Proactive mentality with curiosity and desire to learn.</li>
<li>Must be very comfortable with continuously changing opportunities and priorities</li>
<li>Exceptional organizational and project management skills; ability to manage multiple projects, meet deadlines
</li>
<li>Experience with B2B CRM (Salesforce or similar solution), Marketo, Hubspot, Eloqua, Pardot, Google Analytics etc. - you should be pretty familiar with the standard marketing tools</li>
<li>Solid track record with B2B client acquisition, nurturing, and segmentation processes</li>
<li>Strong background with established marketing practices, both online and offline</li>
<li>Some travel required</li>
</ul>
