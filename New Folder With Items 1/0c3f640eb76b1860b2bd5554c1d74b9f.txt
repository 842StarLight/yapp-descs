<h2>Marketing Media Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>The Marketing Specialist will be responsible for the development of marketing strategy and plans that meet TAP’s brand goals and objectives. This includes developing multi-channel marketing communications that strengthen the brand and drive growth through consumer awareness. This position will lead in developing communications and promotions based on consumer’s needs, motivators and behaviors, while working collaboratively with internal and external brand business partners.
<br>
<br>
<b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES &amp; RESPONSIBILITIES :
</b><br>
<br>
Plan &amp; Execute Media &amp; Advertising
<br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Develop a plan across various media outlets; traditional media, online media, social media
</li>
<li>Maintain multi-channel purchase funnel marketing campaigns by working in conjunction with outside agencies and internal product management team.
</li>
<li>Support development &amp; presentation of media plan recommendations, evaluations, POVs and pre/post media analyses.
</li>
<li>Actively manage all aspects of media buying through the planning, scheduling, negotiation, buying, implementation and invoicing processes. Proactively negotiate media prices capturing the lowest rates possible.
</li>
<li>Monitor performance of all marketing vehicles on an ongoing basis and, where necessary, make recommendations on opportunities to optimize spend
</li>
<li>Drive effective and compelling marketing elements, including print collateral, social, email, in-store POP, digital media, advertising, public relations, training, and presentations.
</li>
<li>Partner with graphics specialist on all phases of dealer and consumer online and standard catalogs including creative, layout/pagination, studio and location photography, production, inventory and field distribution.
</li>
<li>Write high quality, SEO optimized product descriptions for web and mobile use and ensure accuracy of web and catalog content.
</li>
<li>Work in conjunction with leadership to develop annual and monthly budgets
</li>
<li>Lead planning and execution of events including dealer shows, customer/partner and industry events.
</li>
<li>Lead plans for and/or integrating with retail events and promotions with regional offers communicated via email and direct mail, social and email, dealer advertisements, and in-store POP.
</li>
<li>Build strong relationship with key brand business partners related to marketing efforts.
</li>
<li>Support consumer insights generation and analysis via focus groups, surveys and other measurement.
</li>
<li>Track and analyze competitive marketing efforts to optimize communication methods, mediums, and technologies to engage with various audiences.
</li>
<li>Keep current with emerging digital marketing channels (i.e. social, mobile, email, search). Research new media vehicles and make recommendations for testing.
</li>
<li>Track and report on all campaign metrics.
</li>
<li>Manage agency interaction, schedules, budgets and production of materials.
</li>
</ul>
</div>
