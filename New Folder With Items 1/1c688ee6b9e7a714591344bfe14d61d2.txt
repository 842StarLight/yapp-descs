<div><b>The Role</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>As the Sr. Financial Analyst in Retirement Plan Services, you will consult/analyze and deliver on more complex assignments/projects for assigned area(s) of Financial Analysis responsibility. You will also act as a resource to applicable internal/external stakeholders. You will prepare, analyze and report more complex financial information and provide management with a more complex financial perspective for assigned area(s) of responsibility.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>Requirements</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>Financial Analysis</div>
<ul>
<li>Understand, edit and manage the financial plan model; Key contact during the annual financial planning process</li>
<li>Own the 3-year financial planning; Forecast changes to overall profitability dynamics based upon different sales and pricing assumptions</li>
<li>Analyze changes to our investment and crediting rate strategies and the financial impact of different interest rate environments to understanding how our earnings respond to different macro-economic scenarios</li>
<li>Prepares, analyzes and reports on more complex financial information for assigned area(s) of responsibility</li>
<li>Evaluates financial information to develop and deliver more complex standard, and ad-hoc financial analysis to management</li>
<li>Provides support to the financial analysis team in the preparation and delivery the analysis of financial information including more complex financial analysis for assigned area(s) of responsibility</li>
<li>Identifies financial analysis issues/concerns and recommends mitigation solution(s) to management</li>
<br>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div>Business Acumen</div>
<ul>
<li>Maintains knowledge on current and emerging developments/trends, assesses the impact, and collaborates with management to incorporate new trends and developments in current and future solutions</li>
<li>Champions and enhances organizational initiatives by positively influencing and supporting change management and/or departmental/enterprise initiatives</li>
<li>Serves as a resource to team members and applicable internal/external stakeholders on more complex assignments/projects</li>
<li>Identifies and recommends process improvements that significantly reduce workloads or improve quality</li>
<li>Consults/Analyze on more complex assignments and/or projects Remains current in profession and industry trends</li>
<li>Makes a positive contribution as demonstrated by: - Making suggestions for improvement - Learning new skills, procedures and processes</li>
<br>
</ul>

<div>Experience</div>
<ul>
<li>5-7+ Years’ experience in financial analysis that directly aligns with the specific responsibilities for this position</li>
<li>Proven financial planning and analysis experience</li>
<li>Ability to read, analyze and interpret both internal and external documents such as general media/publications, professional journals, technical procedures, governmental regulations, policies, proposals, and standard operating procedures</li>
<li>Confident, comfortable communicator with strong written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Apply concepts such as probability, statistics, modeling, percentages, ratios, and proportions to practical solutions</li>
<li>Works with mathematical concepts such as averages and/or variances</li>
<li>Ability to analyze information and to evaluate the implications of a course of action or solution</li>
<li>Ability to interpret and apply common sense understanding to carry out a variety of instructions furnished in written, oral, or diagram form</li>

<li>Evaluate trends in data or information</li>
<li>Ability to perform under stress in cases of emergency, critical or hazardous situations.</li>
<li>Ability to work with others in a team environment</li>
<li>Demonstrated strong relationship management skills with internal clients (e.g. management, peers and colleagues); proven ability to develop collaborative approaches</li>
