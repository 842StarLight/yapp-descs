<h2>Business Development & Sales Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Business Development &amp; Sales Manager is responsible for the growth and development of new opportunities as well as the management and maintenance of existing accounts in federal, state, municipal and commercial contracting environments for 5 distinct Social Enterprises lines of business. Environmental Services including Janitorial, Landscaping and building maintenance/management, Food Service and Catering, Document Management including data capture, storage and destruction, Supply Chain including Warehousing and Distribution, Inventory management, Order Procurement and Fulfillment.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Functions:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Build and maintain relationships with key customers and suppliers in the existing and new Social Enterprise lines of business of local, regional and national relevance and grow sales and profit margin year-over-year, with a focus of selling goods and services provided by a diverse and untapped workforce.</li>
<li>Lead the design and implementation of strategies to improve sales results which may include online data mining software, collaboration with other similar entities for prime and subcontract opportunities and opening new markets for existing and future operations.</li>
<li>Work closely with the Project Management Office (PMO) on the development of proposals and RFP responses.</li>
<li>75% of time to be allocated to new business development / 25% to account maintenance.</li>
<li>Develop effective business plans aimed at achieving set objectives and introducing new lines of business to the organization.</li>
<li>Develop technical product and service knowledge; Conduct Market Analysis(es), establish and adjust selling prices by monitoring costs, competition, supply and demand.</li>
<li>Negotiate prices and terms with both suppliers and customers and close profitable deals.</li>
<li>Prepare an annual sales budget, monitor product sales, achieve financial objectives and explain/correct variances. Supervise all staff, volunteers and interns to ensure continuous operation during store hours; coordinating hiring, evaluation and professional development.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Requirements:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in Marketing, Business or related field.</li>
<li>Three years of experience in business development and sales.</li>
<li>Valid Driver’s License and must maintain a satisfactory driving record.</li>
<li>Depending upon assignment, must be able to work in a variety of settings, weather conditions and locations.</li>
<li>Proficiency in Windows, database programs, Microsoft Office and the internet.</li>
<li>Move weights up to 10 lbs. on an occasional basis</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Satisfactory driving record, current CT drivers’ license, and access to daily use of a reliable, registered, and insured vehicle for frequent in-state driving.<br>
</p>
