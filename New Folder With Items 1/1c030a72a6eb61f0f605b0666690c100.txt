<h2>Exhibit Designer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Metro Exhibits, a fast-growing leader in the exhibiting/tradeshow industry, is currently looking for a seasoned Exhibit Designer to join our team at our headquarters in Pine Brook, NJ. Metro Exhibits is a turn key exhibit house which provides custom/rental/portable solutions globally.</p>
<p><b>Only applicants that supply a portfolio and resume will be considered for this position</b></p>
<p>This position will be offered a competitive salary based on experience, along with a generous benefits package. Our benefits package boasts a zero-day waiting period for health benefits along with a generous employer contribution. In addition, we also have a matching 401K plan up to 5%, 25K life insurance policy, PTO policy along with numerous company perks.</p>
<p>The ideal candidate possesses 5+ years of exhibits design experience in the trade show industry or related industry, ability to work as a team, the responsibilities of an exhibit designer is to seamlessly assist our clients in a successful designing a tradeshow booth for our clients that is above and beyond our competition. Our employees also understand our standards of topnotch customer service and work as a team to ensure that every client feels as if they are our only client.</p>

</ul>
