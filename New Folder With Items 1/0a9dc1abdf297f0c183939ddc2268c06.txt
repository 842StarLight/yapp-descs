<p><b>Primary Responsibilities</b><b>:</b></p>
<p>Business Strategy</p>
<ul>
<li>Act as an adviser to the senior management team and assist in facilitating effective decision-making</li>
<li>Assess inquiries directed to the President, determine the proper course of action and delegate to the appropriate individual to manage</li>
<li>Ensure all staff issues are addressed properly, efficiently and judiciously, and keep track of progress until resolved</li>
<li>Directs the operation of key internal initiatives and incorporating them into business plans</li>
<li>Assists with the assimilation of leading practices in relevant industries
</li>
<li>Oversees the account teams periodic updating of account planning for mid and long-range business development goals and provides analytical reporting
</li>
<li>Oversees maintenance of knowledge management center and improvements in knowledge, process, and deliverables template sharing across the organization</li>
<li>Develop and manage methods to streamline and enhance existing processes and reporting tools</li>
<li>Develop executive level briefings, communications, presentations, and content</li>
<li>Create and maintain tracking and reporting tools as necessary (e.g., spreadsheets, databases, and reports) to efficiently, effectively and timely report milestones achievements and challenges confronted</li>
</ul>
<p>Training and Culture
</p>
<ul>
<li>Lead continued development and implementation of live and online training program including team off-site meeting development</li>
<li>Serve as liaison between staff, executives, senior leaders, and CEO, regarding company climate, employee well-being, project updates, proposals, and planning</li>
<li>Assist the human resources department with new hires, including documentation and on-boarding, and collaborate to address and resolve all employee concerns</li>
</ul>
<p>Operations</p>
<ul>
<li>Oversee daily operations through collaboration with senior management and department leaders, performing an array of administrative tasks from managing calendars, generating correspondence, maintaining hard copy and electronic files, planning and coordinating annual corporate meetings, and scheduling facilities</li>
<li>Review, design, and execute on improvements to org structure and processes. Find knowledge and skills gaps and help address them, coordinate organizational procedures for optimized efficiency and productivity</li>
</ul>

<li>Minimum of 3-5 years relevant business/project management experience with excellent people skills.
</li>
<li>Experience with Sales Force Automation, CRM, Reporting &amp; Analytics and other applicable supporting technology and systems such as expense management, learning management, etc.</li>
<li>Experience developing analytical models and structuring methodologies.</li>
<li>Excellent quantitative analysis, project management, research, and core consulting skills.</li>
<li>Solid proficiency in Microsoft Office software.
</li>

<li>Ability to initiate and follow through on projects, prioritize tasks and work independently with minimal supervision required. Able to prioritize tasks and work independently with minimal supervision.</li>

<li>Strong aptitude towards usage of automated systems; technology focused</li>
<li>The drive for self-development, the ability to collaborate, and an action-oriented work ethic</li>
<li>Strong attention to details and accuracy.</li>
<li>Recognize sensitive information and utilize professional discretion/judgment</li>
</ul>
