<h2>Regional Manager, Customer Integration - Sales</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>The Regional Manager reports to the VP of Customer Integration and is based in the field, works remotely, and travels throughout the network in a defined region. The position will provide support and coaching designed to increase the effectiveness of our sales teams around Installing, Connecting and Engaging customers with Estes’ transportation management system (TMS). The Regional Manager will work closely with District Sales Managers (DSM’s), Account Managers (AM’s) and Corporate Account Executives (CAE’s); and with regular collaboration with our TMS’s (Kuebix) sales team.<ul>
<li>
Travels with DSM’s/AM’s/CAE’s and is part of the business development process</li>
<li>
Makes joint calls on customers who have a need for TMS</li>
<li>
Closes enterprise deals and assists local and corporate sales employees to close deals</li>
<li>
Trains, coaches and develops CAE, DSM, AM capabilities around Estes’ TMS (Kuebix)</li>
<li>
Drives adoption and engagement of Estes’ TMS ; develops and manages metrics and results sharing with key stakeholders to improve performance</li>
<li>
Communicates/collaborates with TMS’s sales team on customer feedback and opportunities</li>
<li>
Participates in or leads coaching and development plans and performance reviews related to TMS success, with feedback to sales leadership on districts/regions/individuals</li>
<li>
Responsible for lead management to and from TMS to assigned region</li>
<li>
Conduct competitive analysis on TMS marketplace and communicate appropriately</li>
<li>
Regular attendance is required</li>
<li>

</ul>
