 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Full-Time Position (36 - 40 hours/week)</p>
<p>Flexible with day/evening and weekend shifts; however frequent weekends are required.</p>
<br>
<p>
</p>
<p>** No Phone Calls or Walk-Ins Please **</p>
<br>
<p>
</p>
<p>We are seeking a fun, smart, dedicated, ambitious, open minded, and driven person to join our small company full of unique individuals. The Studio Manager is the leader of our staff and studio activities. This front of house position maintains a positive and welcoming atmosphere in the studio every day for our students and team. Key responsibility areas of this leadership position include coordination of all classes, allotting resources, increasing sales, team performance, quality of service and training.</p>
<br>
<p>
</p>
<p>Tasks Include</p>
<p>Leading by example and maintaining consistent accountability for staff by training and coaching, ensuring adherence to policies, values and goals</p>
<p>Hiring and training new employees and instructors</p>
<p>Creating new and innovative ways to increase sales and improve processes</p>
<p>Responsible for meeting performance metrics (KPI's) and managing operational budgets</p>
<p>Answering phone calls, emails, attend to customer needs, respond to feedback and resolve issues</p>
<p>Creating and updating schedules for staff, instructors and classes</p>
<p>Organizing and leading studio meetings and training</p>
<p>Creating a fun and effective work environment</p>
<p>Being knowledgeable on all policies and procedures put forth in the studio</p>
<p>Ensuring facility is prepared, maintained and light cleaning</p>
<p>Managing inventory – count, analyze, investigate as needed, place orders, stock shelves</p>
<p>Motivating others, setting expectations and ensuring team are providing exceptional customer service</p>
<p>Maintaining and updating studio documents, forms, signs, and charts</p>
<p>Updating and/or writing new procedures/instructions as needed</p>
