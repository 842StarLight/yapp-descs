<h2>Human Resources Generalist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Human Resources Generalist</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
We’re disrupting a niche $3B legal support industry through innovative mobile software and tech-enabled logistics. We’ve doubled in size in the last 24 months and are infusing additional capital to accelerate our growth - our focus is to expand our technology lead, acquire and integrate less efficient competitors, and tap into new segments through an integrated inbound marketing and sales approach. To get there we need smart, capable talent. This is where you come in.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Your overriding goal as a Human Resources Generalist is <b>to improve the current processes around recruiting, manager development, and company-wide training. </b>You’ll be partnered with competent, experienced, and energetic leaders, provided freedom to experiment, and shown a solid vision for what comes next.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Job Requirements.</b> You have the following experience and are interested in learning more every day.</p>
<ul>
<li>Creating, maintaining and improving processes while continuously evaluating alternate options.</li>
<li>Being a culture leader within the organization and helping make the company a place where employees want to be.</li>
<li>Working with managers to streamline and document expectations and helping them build skills to be successful leaders.</li>
<li>Maintaining high performance expectations for yourself – you have a sense of urgency.</li>
<li>Thriving in a fast paced, ever changing environment.</li>
<li>Demonstrating attention to detail, assertiveness, consistency, and timeliness.</li>
<li>A minimum of 5 years of experience, specially focused on prior recruiting and improving HR processes.</li>
<li>Professional HR certification is preferred</li>
</ul>
