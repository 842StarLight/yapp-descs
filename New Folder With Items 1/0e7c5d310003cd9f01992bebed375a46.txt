<h2>Restaurant Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Operating since 2003, Mina Group currently manages more than 35 concepts around the world. At Mina Group, impeccable service is not just our ideal, it is ingrained in everything we do. Rooted deep in our foundation is the concept of Kaizen, the art of making great and lasting change in small steady increments. By embodying our values of adaptability and fluidity, we cultivate a whole new sense of hospitality that is uniquely Mina. #ZR</b></p>
<p><b>
About Trailblazer Tavern:</b></p>
<p>
Inspired by the lifestyle and culture of Hawaii, Trailblazer Tavern marries the unmatched talent and expertise of the Hawaiian-born-and-bred husband and wife chef duo with MINA Group's innovative approach to upscale dining and Salesforce's "Ohana" culture. This culture embodies the spirit of family, including employees, customers, partners, and the community. The team will draw inspiration from their backgrounds and travels to create a lively gathering place for locals and visitors to dine, imbibe and revel in the heart of San Francisco.</p>
<p><b>
Assistant Manager</b></p>
<p>
The Assistant Manager trains and assists in the operations of MG-350, LLC. Work in conjunction with Mina Group and Host Partners policies. Oversee and coordinate the planning, organizing, training and leadership necessary to achieve stated objectives in sales, costs, employee retention, guest service &amp; satisfaction, food quality, cleanliness and sanitation.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
GENERAL EXPECTATIONS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Act with integrity, honesty and knowledge that promote the culture, values and mission of Mina Group</li>
<li>
Ensure that all guests feel welcome and are given responsive, professional and gracious service at all times</li>
<li>
Perform all aspects of the position with efficiency, respect, professionalism, teamwork, knowledge, patience, awareness, compassion, courtesy and initiative.</li>
<li>
Understand the philosophies of management. Display consistent attention to detail and follow through of all Mina Group policies.</li>
<li>
Represent the restaurant professionally through effective communication, cooperation and relationships with the all business partners.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with the management team to create a culture and restaurant work environment based upon respect; foster opportunities for the team to learn, grow and develop their abilities.</li>
<li>
In partnership with the management staff, ensure that service standards are on par with all outside accreditation sources, including but not limited to: Zagat, Gayot, Mobil, AAA, Wine Spectator and Michelin. Adhere to the service foundation and philosophies established by MG-International Smoke, LLC.</li>
<li>
Understand all programs, procedures, standards, specifications, guidelines and training protocols.</li>
<li>
Offer positive solutions to problems or issues and be a voice that is part of the decision-making team.</li>
</ul>

REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS:</b></p>

2-3 years of culinary &amp; wine experience, preferably in a fine dining environment.</li>
<li>
Bachelor's Degree and /or Culinary degree required</li>
<li>
Excellent communication and organization skills. Ability to interact professionally and diplomatically with a variety of business partners.</li>
<li>
Fluency in Microsoft Office Suite: Word, Excel (Advanced ability), PowerPoint</li>
<li>
Prior experience with accounting software, POS systems, and payroll/HRIS systems.</li>
<li>
Minimum of 2 years' experience in the restaurant/hospitality industry. Must have a working knowledge of restaurant management and operations. Must have strong computer skills with working knowledge of Microsoft Excel and Word. Four-year college or culinary degree required.</li>
</ul>
