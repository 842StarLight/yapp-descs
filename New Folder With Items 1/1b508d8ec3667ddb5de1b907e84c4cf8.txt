As a Manager in the Test Engineering team, you will be responsible for the delivery of testing engineering projects including, planning, delivery, scope of work creation and approval. Do you love determining why a software solution is not working per the need of the business?</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Responsibilities will include but not limited to:</div>
<div><ul>
<li>
Engage with Technical teams and Business stakeholders to identify appropriate services required for an implementation, plan and execute the services with appropriate business value including high level quality at a competitive cost.</li>
<li>
Ensure vendor resources develop the risk based required testing artifacts as expected per the J&amp;J Technology policies, procedures and trainings</li>
<li>
Analyze need for testing different solutions/systems including Supply Chain (e.g. SAP/ERP) and other packaged applications, systems functionality and business processes;</li>
<li>
Provide testing oversight from a strategy and delivery perspective for major engagements;
</li>
<li>Ability to generate and build end-to-end test scenarios and test scripts, ensuring all test package is complete</li>
<li>
Ensure quality checks are being adhered to and effective testing coverage is taking place;</li>
<li>
Assist SMEs with User Acceptance Testing for data generation, execution and defect reporting;</li>
<li>
Work with Business and Supplier partners to identify the scope of the work; develop a scope of work documents and process them through approval; working with the vendor partners to provide experienced and skilled resources at the required locations to deliver work.</li>
<li>
Lead the overall Test engineering activities for projects</li>
<li>
Act as the point of contact with the development / support teams as well as the Technology Services team to resolve project related issues.</li>
<li>
Work with and educate the delivery project team on the services, strategy and process to engage application services</li>
<li>
Proactively determine and resolve issues that would present a risk to delivery of the service;</li>
<li>
Ensure alignment to SDLC and other J&amp;J Standards</li>
<li>
Measure SLAs and critical metrics as required by the teams</li>
<li>
Define project plans to deliver the project, monitors and reports the status of the execution of the projects ensuring projects are delivered within the agreed upon scope, budget and meeting compliance and quality requirements</li>
<li>
Work with SAP &amp; other platform teams to define sustainable regression test strategies with a high focus on automated testing;</li>
<li>
Work to define and execute the right level of performance testing based on release changes; provide guidance to the project teams in the development of testing strategies, testing process and standard methodologies.</li>
<li>
Additional responsibilities include educating and supporting project teams in the use of testing methodologies and tools such as JIRA-XRAY, Automation frameworks, HP ALM, &amp; other automated tools.</li>
</ul></div>
