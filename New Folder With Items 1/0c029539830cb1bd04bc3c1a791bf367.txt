<h2>KBB ICO Regional Sales Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>The KBB ICO Regional Sales Manager will be responsible for increasing revenue on new and existing products within their territory. The scope of responsibility will include managing a pipeline of new business and existing book of business, developing informational presentations and leading in-store dealership meetings to drive revenue.<br>
<br>
Specific Responsibilities:<br>
<ul>
<li>Use the Challenger based selling approach to manage and successfully close complex sales opportunities.</li>
<li>Engage your cross-business unit partners to drive mutual productivity.</li>
<li>Use insight and consultative selling techniques to provide feedback to clients on industry trends, strategies and challenges.</li>
<li>Leverage your managers and sales tools to gain a competitive edge.</li>
<li>Partner with client stakeholders to build consensus for KBB ICO products within their organization.</li>
<li>Independently and collaboratively strategize for solving deal-level challenges.</li>
<li>Develop customer relationships with new and existing dealers.</li>
<li>Facilitate sales and service support to the regions and major corporate offices of dealer groups with a focus on specific products.</li>
<li>Facilitate and coordinate the delivery of information supporting new products.</li>
<li>Develop strategies within the business market to identify market needs.</li>
<li>Develop and continually strengthen relationships with Customer Service to ensure that dealers are provided unparalleled service.</li>
<li>Meet or exceed assigned monthly, quarterly, and annual revenue sales goals as set by the region and or division.</li>
<li>Participate in weekly meetings with the sales organization.</li>
<li>Frequent travel is required. 80-90% travel.</li>
</ul>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Qualifications:</b></h4>
<ul>
<li>Software sales or automotive sales experience a strong plus.</li>
<li>5 or more years of experience in the automotive sales, automotive advertising sales industry or equivalent experience.</li>
<li>3 or more years of successful sales account management experience.</li>
<li>Ability to represent KBB ICO and Cox Automotive to the automotive industry and Model Company values by creating a positive impression of the company.</li>
<li>Understanding and demonstration of Challenger skills preferred.</li>
<li>Ability to systematically solve problems and hypothesize possible client pain points, expectations, and needs.</li>
<li>Knowledgeable in various technologies.</li>
<li>Ability to tailor communications to the client’s needs.</li>
<li>Strong written and verbal presentation, communication, interpersonal, and problem-solving skills.</li>
<li>Experience with MS Office required.</li>
<li>Salesforce Experience highly preferred.</li>
<li>High school diploma or GED required.</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree preferred.</li>
</ul>
