<p><b>
SUMMARY:</b></p>
<p>
This position is responsible for maintaining a high degree of professionalism and leadership while assisting in the daily operations of the Dispatch Office. The Dispatch Manager will oversee all daily operations of the dispatch process while ensuring policies and procedures are followed. They develop processes that will maximize stewardship, safety, quality and productivity. Their primary role is to support the Dispatchers who are tasked with routing our fleet of Company drivers and the procurement of outside carriers all to support Flyers sales of our Fuel and Lubricant products.</p>
<br>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
</b></h1>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Planning, organizing, directing and running optimum day-to-day operations to exceed our customers’ expectations.</li>
<li>Responsible for production output by Company Dispatchers who manage on-time shipping and delivery of our Fleet of Company trucks based on optimal routing.</li>
<li>Allocate vendor (Carrier) resources effectively and fully utilize Company assets to produce optimal results</li>
<li>Implement strategies in alignment with initiatives and provide a clear sense of direction and focus – be a leader.</li>
<li>Collect and analyze data to locate time waste or unnecessary overtime</li>
<li>Work with internal and external customers to resolve disputes and problems.</li>
<li>Manage the Dispatch Team to most effectively use their experience, specific knowledge and proprietary software to monitor and predict fuel level at responsible locations and build a driver dispatch which accounts for site history, traffic conditions, driver capability, market trend pricing and fuel availability and ensures that fuel is delivered where and when required.</li>
<li>Must be able to work under limited to no supervision in a 24/7 work environment</li>
<li>Must be willing to be on call after their normally scheduled shift.</li>
<li>Must possess the ability to work calmly and methodically under pressure.</li>
<li>Must possess excellent organizational skills and high degree of creativity and flexibility.</li>
<li>Maintain a high-level relationship with other departments and department Managers</li>
</ul>
<p><b>GENERAL RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Promote and support company image standards.</li>
<li>Cross train and/or act as back up for other positions in the department.</li>
<li>Adhere to all city, county, state and federal regulations.</li>
<li>Stay familiar with company Handbook and company policies.</li>
<li>Communicate effectively with TMs in the other departments to resolve problems and provide information as needed.</li>
<li>Work in safe manner at all times and report all accidents immediately.</li>
<li>Schedule and attend Dispatch meetings for the purpose of resolving problems, issues, safety procedure.</li>
<li>Create and monitor, for efficiency, Dispatcher daily work schedules based on Company needs.</li>
<li>Manage Dispatcher Time Clock entries to include the management of sick and vacation time and vacation scheduling.</li>
</ul>
