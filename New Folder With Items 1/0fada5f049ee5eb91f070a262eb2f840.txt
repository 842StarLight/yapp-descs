<p><b>
JOB DESCRIPTION:</b></p>
<p>
ASM Global, the leader in privately managed public assembly facilities, has an excellent and immediate opening for Administrative Project Coordinator for <b>The Dow Event Center &amp; Huntington Event Park</b>. The part-time (28 Hours Per Week) Administrative Project Coordinator assists all departments in projects and day to day duties such as answering phones, greeting guests, organization of contracts, bookings and reports as well as assisting the Marketing, Operations, Box Office, Human Resources &amp; Food and Beverage Departments with projects and reporting.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES &amp; RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Reports directly to General Manager</li>
<li>Represents themselves and the venue with the highest level of integrity and professionalism, a service focused approach and a caring, sincere attitude at all times.
</li>
<li>Maintains communication and effective relationships with booked and potential clients, tenants and colleagues.
</li>
<li>Assists clients to ensure effective delivery of expected goals.
</li>
<li>Effectively communicates with various departments to ensure interdepartmental communication for events.
</li>
<li>Develops strong customer relationships through appropriate client communication and the use of professional, courteous and ethical interpersonal interaction.
</li>
<li>Responsible for administrative office, ordering of supplies &amp; equipment as well as responding to general building and event related inquiries from guests and general public within one business day (General Company Email &amp; Information Phone Lines)</li>
<li>Creates correspondence, reports, agendas, and completes reporting forms as directed.
</li>
<li>Assist with developing effective processes to enhance work flows.</li>
<li>Operations Department:
<ul>
<li>Assisting with various projects and special assignments as needed for large events.
</li>
<li>Conduct facility tours</li>
<li>Assists in updating a maintenance database.
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Food &amp; Beverage Department:
<ul>
<li>Process deposits and contracts from new clients.</li>
<li>Collect and distribute suite orders for hockey &amp; concerts.</li>
<li>Create signage and menu boards for use around the building.</li>
<li>Assist in updating weekly &amp; monthly reports.
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Marketing Department:
<ul>
<li>Assist with group ticket sales and event lead generation.</li>
<li>Distribute materials, send out press releases, update marquees, and process &amp; document donations.</li>
<li>Assist with organizing marketing bills and creating purchase orders.
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Human Resources:
<ul><li>Assist employees in completing employment paperwork.
</li></ul>
</li>
<li>Box Office:
<ul><li>Learn how to operate the current box office system.
</li></ul>
</li>
<li>General Manager:
<ul>
<li>Assist in preparing contracts, proposals &amp; insurance for upcoming events.</li>
<li>Maintains well documented, accurate, organized and up-to-date file management to assist employers and clients in the most efficient and organized manner.
</li>
<li>Represent the facility to all potential promoters.
</li>
<li>Accurately maintains attendance and other records on a consistent basis.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned by General Manager.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>

</li>
