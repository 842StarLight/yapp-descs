<p>
Northfield, a CRH company, is a division within Oldcastle APG. APG is North America’s leading manufacturer and supplier of concrete masonry, dry mix, and hardscape products. With over $2B in sales, APG operates across 36 states and 5 Canadian provinces through a network of over 195 operating locations and more than 6,500 employees.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
The Customer Service Lead/Supervisor will provide general supervision to the Customer Service/Inside Sales Team, as well as provide customers with pricing, delivery, and product information. Follow established guidelines to receive and answer customer/outside sales representatives’ questions, comments, or complaints. Must have outstanding people skills, high energy and a positive “can do” attitude. Answering phones, taking and following up on sales quotes and contracts. Coordinate with production scheduling materials to meet the needs of the customers. This is an important position as many times you are often the first point of contact with our customer both in person and on the phone. The successful candidate for this position will train, develop and lead the Customer Service/Inside Sales Team.<br>
<br>
<b>Supervisory Responsibilities</b><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
Assist in hiring, training, development, the promotion of and discipline of the Customer Service/Inside Sales Team</li>
<li>
Oversee the day to day operations of the Customer Service/Inside Sales Team</li>
</ul>
<p>
<br>
<b>Primary Responsibilities</b><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
Manage the daily functions and priorities of the Inside Sales/Customer Service Team</li>
<li>

<li>
Act as the point person for training and development of the Team</li>
<li>
Enter customer orders and deliveries in computer system</li>
<li>
Be proactive and innovative in developing or refining processes to improve service levels and delight customers</li>
<li>
Respond to customer requests</li>
<li>
Work on quotes and sales contracts</li>
<li>
Handle incoming calls and provide customer service to all customers</li>
<li>
Provide customers with product information and literature</li>
<li>
Identify, research, and resolve customer issues</li>
<li>
Assist outside sales representatives with quotes, sales contracts and product availability</li>
<li>
Follow-up on customer inquiries not immediately resolved</li>
</ul>
