<h2>Coordinator - Executive Programs</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div><div><div>
<div>
<div>
<p>UVU Executive Education is seeking a dynamic, driven leader to run executive programs for c-suite and international business and government professionals. The coordinator will be responsible for high revenue growth and exceptional execution of leadership workshops, courses and custom programs.</p>
<p>
Strategic Activities<br>
Assists in strategic planning and evaluation and assessment of executive education programming. Supports departmental outreach efforts, attending events, convening meetings and networking with businesses and regional organizations to build professional relationships and research executive program needs.</p>
<p>
Program Coordination<br>
In collaboration with other departments, assists director with premium high revenue programming and projects for domestic and international business clients. Manages instructor recruitment, retention and assignments for programs. Coordinates with the event producer, department staff, and other departments on logistics for premium educational experiences. Assists with contract management for grants, contracts and custom programs, providing metrics, reports, and fulfillment of terms. Tracks course development and business relationships in Salesforce and other department technology tools.</p>
<p>
Marketing<br>
Collaborates on marketing and sales of programming to achieve growth and success of the programs.</p>
</div>
</div>

<div><b>Required Qualifications</b></div>
<p>Graduation from an accredited college or university with a bachelor’s degree and 3-5 years experience related to the summary of duties or a combination of education and work totaling 5 years.</p>
