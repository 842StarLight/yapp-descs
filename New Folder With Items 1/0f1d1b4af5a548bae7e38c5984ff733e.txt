<h2>Account Executive, Mid Market - Mid Market</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>We are looking for an Account Executive to join our Sales team and focus on our Mid Market customers. This segment represents a huge opportunity for Slack, and we want someone who is eager to do the best work of their lives while supporting others in doing the same.</p>
<p>
You will be responsible for driving the sales process with prospective customers as well as the expansion of existing accounts. You will work closely with our customers to understand their business objectives and act as their trusted guide for using Slack most effectively. You’ll also work with cross-functional teams to help align our product roadmap and marketing materials with customer feedback. If you enjoy working with customers from a variety of industries in a rapidly changing environment, come and join us! We’re nice.</p>
<p><b>
What you will be doing</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Educate customers on the value of Slack in a thoughtful way</li>
<li>Provide recommendations based on a customer’s business needs and usage patterns</li>
<li>Manage multiple customers simultaneously at various stages of the Slack buying cycle</li>
<li>Handle inbound customer communication and organize/escalate issues appropriately including billing, legal, security, and technical inquiries</li>
<li>Drive revenue through proactive outreach to existing customers</li>
<li>Get into territory and conduct onsites, at minimum 5 onsites per quarter</li>
<li>Prospect and cold call into a mixture of outbound, inbound, upsells, and expansions</li>
<li>Meet with C-level executives across all departments within a company</li>
<li>Serve as the voice of the customer and collect feedback to drive continuous improvement across all areas of the product</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What you should have</b></p>
<ul>
<li>3+ years of closing experience, preferably in SaaS</li>
<li>Needs little oversight in preparing for meetings</li>
<li>You are competitive and driven to achieve goals - you meet and exceed your number</li>
<li>You are articulate and poised with a clear and concise spoken and written communication style</li>
<li>You take an active interest in opportunities to increase customer satisfaction and deepen customer relationships</li>
<li>You have a consistent track record of identifying customer needs and successfully implementing solutions</li>
<li>You are flexible, adaptive, and resilient</li>
<li>You have a post-secondary degree</li>
</ul>
<p>Slack has a positive, diverse, and supportive culture—we look for people who are curious, inventive, and work to be a little better every single day. In our work together we aim to be smart, humble, hardworking and, above all, collaborative. If this sounds like a good fit for you, why not say hello?</p>
