<h2>Social Media Campaign Manager (Recent Grads Welcome)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Linqia combines the science and accountability of online media with award-winning service to deliver guaranteed influencer marketing results for our clients. Our AI-driven platform and team of experts are leading the transformation of influencer marketing into a scalable media channel. At Linqia, we value intelligence, recognize talent and have instilled a culture that supports career development and growth for our employees. We thrive on innovation and accountability, with a customer first attitude that adds true value to everything we touch. Our team members are smart, hard-working, have integrity and love to have fun as we play to win.

Founded in 2012, Linqia works with over 300 leading national brands including Kimberly-Clark, McDonald's, Nestle, Unilever, and Walmart. Linqia is headquartered in San Francisco, CA, with offices in New York, Chicago, and Bentonville.
<br><br>
<b>Role:</b><br>
The Social Media Campaign Manager will utilize excellent client relationship building skills, knowledge of online marketing best practices, and profound attention to detail to help plan, build, and execute Linqia campaigns from start to finish. As the main point of client contact, the Social Media Campaign Manager will manage the day-to-day operations and thrive in a fast paced, self-starter environment. The Social Media Campaign Manager is the #1 client advocate within Linqia and plays an integral role ensuring client success and driving satisfaction with Linqia's services.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
Act as principal liaison between client, agency, and Linqia internal teams to:
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Handle all customer requests in a timely and courteous manner</li>
<li>Gain a deep understanding of client product and marketing goals to proactively propose campaign content, strategy, and optimization ideas</li>
<li>Own and facilitate all aspects of the Linqia campaign planning, execution, and analysis process for clients. Including managing campaign calendar, monitoring and optimizing campaign results, writing creative briefs, and developing campaign analysis reports</li>
<li>Partner with the Influencer Success team to facilitate and support Linqia Community Leader selection, activation, and participation</li>
<li>Identify trends to enhance the customer support process and to improve team efficiency</li>
<li>Assist sales team to develop proposals and marketing materials</li>
<li>Process brand and community billing/invoicing</li>
<li>Schedule and lead client campaign update and status calls</li>
</ul>
<b>Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>6 - 24 months office experience or account management experience</li>
<li>Knowledge of how to effectively use various forms of digital/social media advertising to drive traffic and brand awareness. Experience with blogger communities is a plus.</li>
<li>Team player with the ability to prioritize and manage time effectively.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to take ownership and accountability.</li>
<li>Strong troubleshooting skills, with creative ability to lead internal teams to overcome challenges and achieve resolution.</li>
<li>Ability to thrive in a rapidly growing start up environment that is developing and growing quickly.</li>
<li>4-year college degree</li>
<li>Passionate about digital and social marketing</li>
<li>Currently located in San Francisco Bay Area</li>
</ul>
