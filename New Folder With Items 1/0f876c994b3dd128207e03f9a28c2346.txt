<p>
Oldcastle Infrastructure, a CRH company, is the leading manufacturer of precast concrete, polymer concrete, and plastic products in the United States. Our products range from utility vaults, reinforced concrete pipe, catch basins, drainage and septic tanks, to retaining walls, storm shelters, wall panels, concrete barriers, a variety of prestressed concrete products and more. With more than 80 locations nationwide and 3,000 plus employees, Oldcastle Infrastructure is committed to upholding core values of reliability, quality, and service in cutting edge ways.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Oldcastle Infrastructure is currently seeking a Civil/Structural Engineer (EIT). The work assignment will be located at the Precast Plant in Telford, PA . This position reports directly to the Regional Engineering Manager and involves performing engineering designs in support of estimates, preparing submittal packages, and review of production precast drawings for manufacture.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Job Responsibilities</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Work with CAD Operators and assist Engineering Manager prioritize work in support of sales/production</li>
<li>
Support CAD Operators as required to balance flow of submittals and production drawings.</li>
<li>
Work on various engineering projects in support of sales as well as continuous improvements in manufacturing, process, shipping, handling and installation</li>
<li>
Prepare calculations and drawings for precast products</li>
<li>
Work with the Quality Control Department to ensure quality precast products are manufactured</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Requirements</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelors of Science Degree in Civil or Structural Engineering or similar field of study</li>
<li>
Engineer in Training registration in any state</li>
<li>
0-3 year(s) industry experience and previous internship or design experience</li>
<li>
Ability to read and interpret drawings and fundamental knowledge of reinforced concrete and steel design.</li>
<li>
Ability to use a variety of computer programs, including AutoCAD, Inventor, Microsoft Office Products, Mathcad, RISA 3D, or other finite element software</li>
<li>
Excellent organization and communication skills</li>
<li>
Self-motivated and able to take initiative</li>
<li>
Ability to interact with customers, manager, engineers, drafters and other employees to resolve engineering and management related issues</li>
</ul>
