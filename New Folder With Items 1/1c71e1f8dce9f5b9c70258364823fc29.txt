<h2>Assistant Dispatcher</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>The primary focus of this position is to assist the <b>Dispatcher</b> in ensure the Mobile Mini Storage Units are successfully delivered and picked-up from customer sites and ensuring the drivers are following all safety and DOT regulations.<br>
<br>
<b>Primary Responsibilities:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>
Assist the Dispatcher in scheduling and assigning drivers and vehicles to convey Mobile Mini Units. This includes:</li>
<li>
Taking calls from the Drivers in the field and assist them with problems and delays</li>
<li>
Ensuring each Driver has the appropriate paperwork for deliveries and pick-ups and collects and records paperwork when they return</li>
<li>
Communicates with customers and branch/service center sales for daily/weekly deliveries and pick-ups</li>
<li>
Notifies customers of delivery and pick-up times and coordinates any delays</li>
<li>
Handles customer inquiries regarding delivery and pick-up</li>
<li>
Notifies branch/corporate sales of any delays, changes or problems with delivery or pick-up</li>
<li>
Maintains daily log files and preventative maintenance files and records the information into the appropriate systems. Ensures all daily/weekly/monthly paperwork is completed and recorded</li>
<li>
May schedule service calls for branch equipment and any outside hauling agencies</li>
<li>
Other duties as assigned. Regular attendance is required to fulfill the essential functions of the job.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
<b>
Equipment Used and Responsibility:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>
Computer, telephone, radio and electronic office equipment.</li>
<li>
MS Office Suite.</li>
<li>
GPS and tracking software.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
<b>
Education and Experience:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>
High School Diploma or equivalent preferred.</li>
<li>
2+ year of experience in the field or related area preferred.</li>
<li>
Exposure to standard concepts of scheduling and dispatching.</li>
<li>
1+ year of Customer Service Experience.</li>
<li>
Exposure to DOT and state specific safety regulations.</li>
<li>
Communicate clearly: verbal and written.</li>
<li>
1-2 years of data entry experience.</li>
</ul>
