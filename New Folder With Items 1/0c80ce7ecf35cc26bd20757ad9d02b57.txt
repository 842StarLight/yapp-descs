GENERAL SUMMARY
<br><br>
Under the direction of the sports program director, the Badminton Professional is responsible for all Badminton activities as assigned and directed by the Club's management. The Badminton Professional provides leadership to the Badminton professional team providing the best customer service and hospitality to all members and guests. The Badminton Professional assumes the duties of Badminton Director in his/her absence as directed by the Club's management is responsible for achieving personal revenue goals each month while providing the best customer service and hospitality to all members and guests.
<br><br>
Nothing in this job description restricts management's right to assign or reassign duties and responsibilities to this job at any time.
<br>PRIMARY JOB FUNCTIONS
<br><br>
<b>Promote and teach:</b> Badminton clinics, private lessons, and adult/junior/senior programs as assigned by management.
<br><br>
Responsible for organizing and maintaining daily teaching schedule, including but not limited to, private lessons and group lessons.
<br><br>
Responsible for achieving personal &amp; program revenue goals
<br><br>
Provide new member Badminton orientations and evaluations
<br><br>
Attend Badminton Department staff meetings and General Staff Meeting or Associate Orientations as directed by Club Management
<br><br>
Schedule permitting, be available for posted Badminton Clinics or other job functions throughout the Club in emergency situations.
<br><br>
Be on time for all scheduled appointments
<br><br>
Maintain accurate, current records on all client exercise programs and progress
<br><br>
Keep track of each client's sessions and fee payments
<br><br>
Enforce all Badminton department and club policies
<br><br>
Assist in the maintenance and cleanliness of the Badminton area. Maintain your teaching court in a professional manner. Ensure the environment is safe for all those who use the facility.
<br><br>
Accurately record each day worked, the actual time worked and client sessions in the appropriate tracking system
<br><br>
On-court instruction may not exceed a maximum of 20 hours per week
<br><br>
Encourage students to participate in the Club's Badminton events.
<br><br>
Represent the club in a professional manner through image, dress, communication, and immediate follow through and response
