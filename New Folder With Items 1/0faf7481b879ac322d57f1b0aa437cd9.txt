<h2>Project Engineer II</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Description:</b><br>
Nikkiso Cryo, Inc., is one of the world’s foremost suppliers of submerged motor liquefied gas pumps. We offer unmatched reliability, quality and safety. As part of the Nikkiso Company global organization, our “original technologies” provide our customers with the confidence in knowing they are receiving the latest technology and the highest standards of engineering available.
<br><br>
We are located in North Las Vegas, Nevada and we offer a full range of submerged pumps for LNG, LPG, LEG, LN2, liquid propylene and many other liquefied gases. With design, production and test facilities in both the United States and Japan, sales offices in Las Vegas, Houston, London and Tokyo, Nikkiso Cryo offers prompt, total support for all of our customers worldwide. For more information please visit www.nikkisocryo.com.
<br><br>
Position Summary
<br><br>
Working within the Engineering department and reporting to the Director of Engineering. The Project Engineer completes projects by planning, organizing and controlling all elements of the project. The Project Engineer supervises all development and implementation of a project and is actively involved in all project-related work.
<br><br>
Essential Functions
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Review project plans and proposals, develops project objectives</li>
<li>Review customer specification and deviation for clarifying the project requirement</li>
<li>Confer with document originators or engineering liaison personnel to resolve discrepancies and compiles required changes</li>
<li>Assure integrity of Engineering BOM to ERP Shop Floor BOM as released and revised.</li>
<li>Advise status of revisions and potential actions to maintain parts flow</li>
<li>Studies product design, customer requirements performance standards, then determines project specifications.</li>
<li>Interface with other departments to ensure internal and external customer requirements are met</li>
<li>Ensure all documents are filed in a timely manner and filed correctly</li>
<li>Release documents in accordance with department standards</li>
<li>Maintain project integrity through compliance with industry regulations and best practices</li>
<li>Travel between the NCI facility locations as required</li>
<li>This list is not comprehensive and tasks not mentioned here may be required.</li>
</ul>
