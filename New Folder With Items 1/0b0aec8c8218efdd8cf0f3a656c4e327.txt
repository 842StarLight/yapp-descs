<div>Job Description<br>
<br>
<p>We are looking for a tenacious <b>Business Development Representative</b> who is intellectually curious, enjoys prospecting and has a passion for engineering analysis, the product development process and technology advancements<b>.</b></p>
<p>
In this role, you will focus on new business development around ANSYS software sales and simulation consulting services in the Central region, including Ontario Canada and the following states â€“ MI, IN, IL, WI, MO, IA, MN, KS, NE, SD, ND.</p>
<p><b>
Your responsibilities will include:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Lead generation and early stage sales opportunity qualification</li>
<li>
Actively nurturing and prospecting into new named accounts through outbound calls, emails and marketing campaign follow-ups</li>
<li>
Identifying, researching and connecting with prospective clients to maintain a high volume of early-stage sales activity needed for pipeline development and achieving territory sales objectives</li>
<li>
Conducting needs assessments, identifying key decision makers and outlining relevant solution options sufficient for setting value definition and solution development appointments for territory sales staff</li>

Serving as a trusted business advisor to establish initial thought-leadership, relationship development and stakeholder interest with prospective clients across all levels of the organization</li>

Coordinating sales efforts, messaging and campaigns with the regional sales manager</li>
