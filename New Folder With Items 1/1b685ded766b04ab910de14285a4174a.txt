<h2>Typesetter/Graphics Technician</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Cenveo, a world leader in the management and distribution of print and related offerings is seeking a Typesetter position in its Amarillo, TX facility.
<br><br>
The ideal candidate will have a High School diploma or equivalent and thorough knowledge of graphic equipment and processes.
<br><br>
Cenveo offers a competitive salary and a comprehensive benefits package including, but not limited to medical, dental, vision, life insurance, short &amp; long term disability, 401(k), PTO and Company paid holidays.
<br><br>
Job Summary
<br><br>
Under close supervision, completes tasks related to the preparation of customer material for the print process utilizing a variety of design and layout applications and graphics software. This may include preflight and prepress functions, color correction, typesetting, and preparing or producing printing plates and proofs.
<br><br>
Primary Duties and Responsibilities (*Essential Functions)
<br><br>
These are functions a qualified incumbent or applicant must be able to perform with or without reasonable accommodation.
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>*Uses various graphics and proprietary software packages to prepare customer files for printing.</li>
<li>*Reviews work orders to ensure conformity to written and verbal instructions</li>
<li>*Coordinates customer supplied art with sales personnel for each order.</li>
<li>*Communicates order problems with supervisor, customer service representatives and sales staff.</li>
<li>*Monitors print quality for accuracy in color and placement.</li>
<li>*Advises customer regarding format and technical issues for best quality printing.</li>
<li>*Documents order actions and customer contacts clearly and concisely.</li>
<li>Performs other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
