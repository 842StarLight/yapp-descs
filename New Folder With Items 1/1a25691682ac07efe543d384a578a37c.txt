<h2>Aerodynamic Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>FS-Elliott is seeking an Aerodynamic Engineer at our Export, PA headquarters responsible for designing, analyzing, and testing centrifugal compressor products. This position will be part of an interdepartmental team dedicated to processing aerodynamic information from order entry through testing. They will utilize engineering software to ensure the successful testing of aerodynamic performance.
<br>
</div>

</li></ul>
<ul><li>Will be the main point of contact for processing aerodynamic information from the Sales &amp; Project Engineering departments to the Shop Floor.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Will analyze and adjust existing stage performance ratings based on test results.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Should be able to apply knowledge of engineering principles and have experience using computational fluid dynamics (CFX) and have the ability to research and apply technical subject matter to create engineering processes or solve complex issues.

<ul><li>Will be able to use new or existing designs to meet aerodynamic performance criteria and support Sales and Project management to ensure successful performance quotations.
</li></ul>
