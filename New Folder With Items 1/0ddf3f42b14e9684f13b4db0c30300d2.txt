<h2>Trade Show & Operations Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Heska is dedicated to quality pet care. Through innovation, invention, and strategic partnerships with worldwide leaders in diagnostics and treatments. Heska provides the benefits of latest generation technologies to pet healthcare providers. Heska’s range of complete blood diagnostic solutions include Chemistry, Hematology, Blood Gas &amp; Electrolytes, Immunodiagnostics, Heartworm, and other blood testing. Treatments and testing include Immunotherapy and complete Allergy solutions.</p>
<p><b>
JOB SUMMARY:</b> The Trade Show &amp; Operations Coordinator is responsible for the coordination and general oversight of all Heska tradeshows and sales meetings. This position also maintains data generated from these events in the CRM software (Salesforce.com).</p>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:
</b></p>
<p><b>Tradeshow Coordination:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Coordinates all tradeshows and wet labs including national, state, local conferences and sales meetings.
</li>
<li>Creates and maintains working relationships with directors and coordinators of associations that conduct trade shows attended by Heska.</li>
<li>Organizes tradeshow records, documents, schedules and deadlines via various systems, including Salesforce.com.</li>
<li>Maintains an ongoing, calendar (generated through Salesforce.com) for all meetings, available to sales and marketing teams.</li>
<li>Working closely with accounting, completes registration, payment, and booth placement for Heska attended events.</li>
<li>Orders and coordinates materials provided by decoration company and tradeshow location, including tables, chairs, electrical requirements, labor, and pre-show marketing materials.</li>
<li>Coordinates with the Tradeshow Logistics Coordinator to insure that in-house collateral materials, literature and promotional items are shipped to the proper locations. Coordinates additional booth labor as needed.</li>
<li>Coordinates with Sales Management to determine booth attendees, booth activities and focus.</li>
<li>Coordinates with Sales and Product Managers to assure the appropriate instruments are shipped.</li>
<li>Secures hotels based on conference install/dismantle times and flight information.</li>
<li>Communicates with the booth attendees about show details, booth activities, additional exposures and expectations via Salesforce.</li>
<li>Imports conference leads and communicating the leads to the appropriate owner in Salesforce.com.</li>
<li>Coordinates with Imaging to assure that Acquisition workstations QC process and repairs are completed in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Coordinates with Product Managers and EVP for Digital Imaging to ensure that all products are up to date prior to conferences.</li>
<li>Provides remote support for on-site attendees, in conjunction with the Trade Show Logistics Coordinator.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Salesforce.com Tradeshow Maintenance:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Creates and manages Trade Show Campaigns for all tradeshows and events.</li>
<li>Generates and reports core report on bi-monthly basis.
</li>
<li>Imports all tradeshow leads into Salesforce and assigns/communicates them to the appropriate owner.</li>
<li>Records all conference information and communications via calendar events and tasks with sales team.</li>
<li>Records clinic competitive demographic information received from tradeshows.</li>
<li>Records “Consent to solicit” signatures in Salesforce account or lead, scans and attaches the form into their account and giving all hard copies to Legal.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Other duties as assigned:</b></p>
