<p><b>Job Title: </b>Export Logistics Coordinator</p>
<p><b>American Trading International, Inc </b>(ATI) is currently seeking an experienced Export Logistics Coordinator to join our team in Los Angeles, CA.</p>
<p>ATI has recently expanded our beautiful Westside office and is looking for sharp, smart, like-minded individuals to help take us to the next level!</p>
<p>ATI is a 20+ year old Los Angeles, CA based export trading company specialized in USA food &amp; beverage products, both branded and private label. We are currently seeking to fill a new position in our fast growing, dynamic work environment. ATI is a 45+ employee / $50+ million operation exporting to over 80 markets worldwide. We take pride in our state-of-the-art facilities, resources, friendly atmosphere, with a multi-cultural / multi-lingual staff speaking more than 20 languages. We now also operate on SAP. Please feel free to visit our web site at: www.american-trading.com to learn more about us and this exciting opportunity.</p>
<p><b>JOB DESCRIPTION: </b></p>
<p>Daily activities will be the organizing and overseeing of international transactions from start to finish for export sales. Corresponding and working with domestic suppliers/manufacturers, domestic trucking companies and warehoused, international shipping carriers, and foreign buyers.</p>
<p><b>KEY JOB RESPONSIBILITIES ARE AS FOLLOWS: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Coordinate air or ocean shipments for export transactions.</li>
<li>Track order progress daily using company operational software.</li>
<li>Work with manufacturers to produce orders correct and timely.</li>
<li>Work to ensure safe transport of goods to overseas buyer.</li>
<li>Update all parties to the transaction on dates and information needed to execute order correctly.</li>
<li>Prepare Bill of Lading instructions and File AES reports.</li>
<li>Update customer of all order issues as needed.</li>
<li>Prepare export documentation per transaction instructions and customer requirements.</li>
<li>Collaborate with other company departments to ensure timely shipping.</li>
<li>Handle Customer Service Issues as needed.</li>
<li>Enter all order information in SAP operating system timely and with a very high level of precision.</li>
</ul>
