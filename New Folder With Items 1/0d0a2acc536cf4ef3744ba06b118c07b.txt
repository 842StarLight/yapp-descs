<p><b>Job Description</b></p>
<p></p>
<div>
Engineers in this general category are concerned with determining the optimal manufacturing methods and process for producing company products. Understanding of productivity improvement methodology, LEAN processes, ergonomics, time studies for reducing waste and excess, line balancing, 6S, Kaizans, etc.</div>

<div></div>
<ul>
<li><div>Design background of electromechanical, RF technology and or microwave switches and components</div></li>

Assists with the quoting process as assigned</div></li>
<li><div>
Updates labor hours and manufacturing lead time information on new jobs as released from sales</div></li>
<li><div>
Works directly on the production floor in support of direct employees needing assistance for production manufacturing engineering issues</div></li>
<li><div>
Updates and matinees travelers with up-to date information to minimize redlining of travelers</div></li>
