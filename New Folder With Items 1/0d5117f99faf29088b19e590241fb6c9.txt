<h2>Financial Analyst, FP&A</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Summary: </b></p>
<p>Reporting to the Director, Sequel Financial Planning &amp; Analysis, the Financial Analyst will provide support for the reporting of Sequel Business Unit results as well as execution of the forecast and annual budget processes. This position will have exposure to and work with wholesale, distributor, and subsidiary business models. The role will be responsible for support in the consolidation, reporting, and analysis of all P&amp;L line-items: sales through gross profit, and expense areas including overheads, marketing and creative commitments, and general and administrative costs. They will be asked to assist in a variety of ad hoc projects and/or analyses to achieve departmental objectives as well as foster the growth and development of the individual. This position will receive clear guidance and direction from the Director of FP&amp;A regarding execution of key responsibilities.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities: </b></p>
<p>· <b>Monthly Reporting</b></p>
<p>o Update and maintain multiple financial sets by compiling, verifying, and backing up data</p>
<p>o Ensure consistency and accuracy of all financial information across reporting packages and deliverables</p>
<p>o Update monthly FX rates used for monthly reporting packages</p>
<p>o Run month-to-date and year-to-date P&amp;L line-item variance reporting versus budget and prior year, used to ensure accuracy in financials and prepare for monthly results review with Corporate Finance</p>
<p>o Build Excel pivot tables for all business entities down to the general ledger account level by leveraging existing financial systems</p>
<p>o Utilize pivot tables to build and update expense recaps by department for functional areas and global business partners</p>
<p>o Utilize pivot tables to build various P&amp;L line-item reporting packages for senior leadership (e.g. labor costs; advertising and promotions; travel and entertainment)</p>
<p>o Support the monthly Business Review process by assisting with the consolidation of management P&amp;Ls and the development of PowerPoint presentations to be reviewed with senior executives at both the business unit and group levels</p>
<p>· <b>Forecasting</b></p>
<p>o Support the monthly and quarterly financial and management P&amp;L forecast processes by leveraging monthly expense reporting packages</p>
<p>o Work closely with functional areas and global business partners to understand business flow and key drivers, help identify and articulate risks and opportunities</p>
<p>o Consolidate and present financial forecasts based on inputs from functional areas and global business partners</p>
<p>· <b>Budgeting</b></p>
<p>o Support the annual budget process through department level expense template creation/modification and distribution to all functional areas and global business partners, holding them accountable to communicated deadlines and requested deliverables</p>
<p>o Verify and upload all expense budget submissions to relevant financial systems, confirming accuracy and consolidation at all levels</p>
<p>o Perform various operational analyses for budget presentation to and review with business partners and executives</p>
<p>· <b>Financial Analysis</b></p>
<p>o Assist with financial modeling improvements in reporting, planning, and forecasting to enhance process efficiency, support new initiatives, and to accurately capture changing business economics and structure</p>
<p>o Prepare various strategic, ad-hoc reports and analyses upon request while supporting the day to day efforts of the department</p>
<p><b>Requirements: </b></p>
<p>· Bachelor’s degree in Accounting or Finance</p>
<p>· 0-3 years of FP&amp;A experience in a retail or wholesale organization</p>
<p>· Previous exposure to and understanding of basic accounting principles</p>
<p>· Advanced Microsoft Excel and PowerPoint skills</p>
<p>· Comfortable working with consolidation software and systems (Oracle and Tagetik preferred)</p>
<p>· Highest standards of accuracy and precision; highly organized</p>
<p>· Articulate with excellent verbal and written communications skills</p>
<p>· Proven ability to multi-task, problem-solve, and thrive in a fast-paced environment</p>
<p>· Relevant experience with senior-level business partners outside of the Finance department</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
