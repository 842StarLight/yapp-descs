<h2>Estimator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>We are seeking an assertive, dynamic, ambitious individual who thrives in a conceptual and detail based environment. The <b>Commercial Roofing Estimator</b> is responsible for preparing accurate, timely estimates and contract negotiation on potential projects.</p>
<p>
Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Estimate, compile bids and generate proposals so that they are completed on time.</li>
<li>
Make site visits to collect bidding information.</li>
<li>
Work in tandem with sales staff to prepare proposals for completion before prospective customer due date.</li>
<li>
Review/prepare proposed project bids in accordance with company bidding procedures, with essential overall cost accuracy.</li>
<li>
Review contracts for accuracy in price and scope.</li>
<li>
Prepare submital packages.</li>
<li>
Prepare notice of award for manufacturer guarantees.</li>
<li>
Prepare change order estimates for approval.</li>
<li>
Prepare purchase orders request.</li>
<li>
Participate in pre-construction meetings.</li>
<li>
Participate in pre-job meetings with hand-off from estimating to project management on assigned projects.</li>
<li>
Coordinate project activities to attain successful goals on all projects by focusing attention to on time and in budget project completion.</li>
<li>
Complete project closeout documents.</li>
<li>
Participate in post-job review/analysis of actual costs to estimated costs through job cost system for use on future projects.</li>
</ul>
<p>
Requirements</p>
<ul>
<li>
Knowledge of various roof systems, materials, construction process and design, code compliance and manufacturers requirements for warranty.</li>
<li>
Adept with data base estimating, digitizer or computerize estimating software.</li>
<li>
Ability to analyze blueprints, specifications, proposals and other documentation.</li>
<li>
Estimate Roofing Projects on Excel Spreadsheets (Edge Estimating Experience a plus).</li>
<li>
Proficient in Microsoft Office Programs. (Excel, Word, Outlook, Power Point)</li>
<li>
Outstanding communication and time management abilities.</li>
<li>
Ability to give instructions and clearly explain problems or situation.</li>
<li>
Valid drivers license and clear driving record</li>
</ul>
<p>
Benefits: Full Benefits Package to include PTO</p>
<p>
Job Type: Full-time</p>
