<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Primary Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div><div>The Growth Solutions Manager supporting Fresh Thyme is responsible for being a subject matter expert in market dynamics affecting the industry. By leveraging analytics and marketplace knowledge, this role is responsible for helping KeHE’s strategic customer increase sales and trade area relevance though assortment and merchandising. As with all positions at KeHE, we expect that all actions will be consistent with KeHE’s Mission, Vision and Values.<br>
</div></div></div></div>
<div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Essential Functions
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Identify product trends specific to Fresh Thyme store locations and regions by analyzing:<ul>
<li>
Category, attribute and marketplace trends</li>
<li>
Consumer behavior shifts and shopper purchase patterns</li>
<li>
Market visits, trade shows, industry events</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Curate consumer, demographic, syndicated, and internal data to create optimum, innovative, and actionable assortment and merchandising solutions.</li>
<li>
Effectively present best in class presentations to Fresh Thyme category management and merchandising teams showcasing category insights, hot trends, new items, and merchandising concepts.</li>
<li>
Translate data into actionable and innovative insights to create turn-key, influential solutions for KeHE’s sales team and Fresh Thyme.
</li>
<li>Establish and maintain relationships with key decision makers at Fresh Thyme based on trust, marketplace knowledge, and results.</li>
<li>
Collaborate cross-functionally with Category Management, Sales and Sales Enablement teams.</li>
<li>
Develop ROI reporting by analyzing implementation rate at retail, product success rates, and sales growth in Fresh Thyme store locations.</li>
<li>
Support training and development of Growth Solutions team members.</li>
<li>
Maintain safeguards of confidential company information.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
