<div></div>
<div><b>How you will do it</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Plans, executes and controls assigned projects.
</li>
<li>Oversees the commissioning process to insure that it is performed in accordance with Johnson Controls standard practices.
</li>
<li>Resolves issues where other parties impact commissioning process.
</li>
<li>Facilitates escalation of product related problems.</li>
<li>Secures new work and change orders from the existing customer base.
 </li>
<li>Estimates work and presents proposals to the customer.</li>
<li>Acts as a technical resource for Sales on large projects.
</li>
<li>Assists sales in project development efforts and performs pre-bid reviews.</li>
<li>Recruits, hires and develops resources to staff the project team.</li>
 <li>Interfaces with the Truck Based team(s) to control manpower to proper staffing levels.</li>
<li>Reviews and interprets contract Terms and Conditions.</li>
<li>Coordinates the drawing layouts / detailing for proper job installation.</li>
<li>Analyzes financial reporting systems and project schedules to proactively address potential problems and manage risk. Investigates solutions and establishes project recovery plans.</li>
<li>Provides ongoing cost and estimate analysis reporting. Manages allocation of costs to financial reporting systems.</li>
<li>Uses Johnson Controls standard tracking and reporting tools to effectively communicate project progress and financial status to management.</li>
<li>Proactively investigates opportunities for improvements in the project scope that will result in value added change orders.
</li>
<li>Performs associated cost estimates, prepares proposals and secures customer acceptance.</li>
<li>Responsible for asset management including collections, cost control, progress billings and payables.</li>
<li>Oversees assigned projects for compliance with specifications, local codes and installation techniques.</li>
<li>Uses negotiation skills to consistently resolve disputes.</li>
<li>Manages material procurement, warehousing and logistics processes.</li>
<li>Provides high quality written communications.
</li>
<li>Ensures project document controls are in compliance with contract requirements and Johnson Controls standards.</li>
<li>Develops and maintains viable long-term relationships with customers and subcontractors.
</li>
<li>Attends job progress meetings as required.</li>
<li>Ensures subcontractors understand expectations of the project.</li>
<li>Ensures transition to appropriate Johnson Controls Truck-based team is achieved.</li>
<li>Coordinates performance of owner training requirements.</li>
<li>Adheres to safety standards.</li>
<li>Insures that Johnson Controls team members, and subcontractors adhere to safety standards.</li>
