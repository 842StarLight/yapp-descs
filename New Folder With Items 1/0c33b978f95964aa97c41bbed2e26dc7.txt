<h2>Store Operations Retail Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div><div><div>
<p></p>
<div><div><div><div><div><div>
<div><div>The Store Operations Retail Analyst contributes to the success of Sur La Table by compiling, analyzing, and interpreting data to support decision making related to store operations. The Analyst will support business reporting processes as well as develop and maintain analyses, projections, reports and presentations. This position requires constant exercise of discretion in prioritization of work and reports to the Director, Store Operations.
<br>
<br>
Job Duties and Responsibilities:<br>
<li>Using a broad range of data sources, provide accurate and timely operational reports to store operations leaders. Verify accuracy and integrity of data.</li>
<br>
<li>Interpret data and provide insights and recommendations to store operations leaders.</li>
<br>
<li>Support forecasting and budget development processes; assist in the preparation of budgets for store operations.</li>
<br>
<li>Communicate complex financial and KPI information in a way that is easily understood by and actionable for store operations leaders.</li>
<br>
<li>Use extensive information technology skills and systems to evaluate, extract, and organize data.</li>
<br>
<li>Construct models from data for reporting.</li>
<br>
<li>Consolidate, reconcile, validate and obtain clarification on financial information from multiple sources to develop forecasts and budgets.</li>
<br>
<li>Assist with design and implementation of management tools including business performance reports, sales (?) analysis and executive summaries.</li>
<br>
<li>Assist in ongoing and ad hoc business analysis.</li>
<br>
<li>Represent the Store Operations team on cross-functional projects and provide information to project teams to optimize training, service levels, customer experience and marketing strategy.</li>
<br>
<li>Additional responsibilities as assigned by manager.</li>
<br>
</div></div>
<br>
<div>
<div><b>Essential Functions</b></div> <div>
<li>Ability to communicate verbally and work cooperatively with employees and customers.</li>
<br>
<li>Ability to remain in a stationary position (sit or stand) for extended periods of time.</li>
<br>
<li>Ability to able to use computer and other office equipment to accomplish work tasks.</li>
<br>
<li>Ability to think critically and communicate complex thoughts and/or ideas in clear and concise manner.
</li>
<br>
<li>Regular and predictable attendance.</li>
<br>
<br>
<br>
</div>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<div><b>Experience and Required Qualifications</b></div> <div>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Business, Marketing or equivalent field. Related work experience considered in lieu of education.
</li>
<br>
<li>3+ years’ experience providing operations analysis and insights.</li>
<br>
<li>Demonstrated understanding of key business drivers.</li>
<br>
<li>High level of aptitude in algebra, retail math and modeling.</li>
<br>
<li>Proven ability to use data to develop business cases and complete root cause analyses.</li>
<br>
<li>Ability to work successfully in a complex and rapidly changing environment; retail industry experience strongly preferred.</li>
<br>
<li>1-2 years of SQL database and query design experience.</li>
<br>
<li>Experience with data modeling preferred.</li>
<br>
<li>Advanced MS Excel skills and proficiency in MS Access, Word, Outlook and PowerPoint.</li>
</div>
