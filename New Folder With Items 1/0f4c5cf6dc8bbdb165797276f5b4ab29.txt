Account Manager Relief<br>
<br>
Our Account Managers work in local stores, large or small, helping our customers keep Coca-Cola products fully stocked and available to Refresh the World and Provide Moments of Optimism and Happiness. Our Coca-Cola Account Managers are our front-line ambassadors bringing happiness and refreshments to consumers in their communities. Would you like to be a Coca-Cola Ambassador?<br>
<br>
<b>Essential Responsibilities:</b><br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for selling and ordering product into existing accounts</li>
<li>Ensure consistent adherence to merchandising and customer service standards</li>
<li>Executes and closes all sales calls for assigned (relief) sales route(s)</li>
<li>Maintains and sells incremental displays, as needed; and secures incremental equipment placements</li>
<li>Sell in promotional programs and ensure customer compliance</li>
<li>In connection with a sales call, maintain appropriate inventory levels, maintain company assets and point of sale, ensure account meets Company merchandising standards, determine stores' product needs, place and transmit appropriate orders</li>
<li>Communicate account activities to appropriate parties</li>
<li>Occasionally assist with merchandising in existing accounts ensuring Right Execution Daily meeting company standards for product displays</li>
<li>Building, changing and removing product displays; maintaining product signage; cleaning product space and securing damaged or defective product</li>
<li>Transport, replace and maintain point of sale advertising as appropriate for accounts</li>
<li>Periodic lifting of 50+ pounds, bending, reaching, and kneeling</li>
<li>Act as an Ambassador by providing customer service to Consumers and Store personnel by answering questions, locating product, and providing assistance as needed</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>Position Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>High School or GED (General Education Diploma) required; College or University graduate preferred</li>
<li>Must have a personal vehicle for use during working hours</li>
<li>A valid driver's license with a clean driving record with no major violations over the last three (3) years</li>
<li>Must have current vehicle liability insurance</li>
<li>1+ years of sales experience</li>
<li>Food/beverage industry experience preferred</li>
<li>Ability to multitask handling multiple customer accounts</li>
<li>Strong attention to detail and follow-up skills</li>
<li>Excellent planning and organization skills</li>
<li>Proficient computer application skills</li>
<li>Ability to create and conduct sales presentations</li>
</ul>
