<h2>Sr. Product Marketing Manager, Unstructured Data Storage Services</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Sr. Product Marketing Manager, Unstructured Data Storage Services</b><br>
<br>
San Jose, CA</p>
<br>
<p>
Nutanix has an opportunity for an entrepreneurial product marketer who will plan, execute and measure our global go-to-market efforts for its emerging unstructured data storage solutions, namely Nutanix Files and Nutanix Objects. This is an opportunity to disrupt the established data storage industry and drive growth for a strategically important area for the company.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
In this role, you will be responsible for overall positioning and messaging, launch strategy, demand generation and sales enablement. You will serve as a spokesperson representing the company with prospects, customers and analysts. Prior product marketing experience in the areas of IT infrastructure, file and object storage, and cloud computing is a plus.</p>
<br>
<p><b>
If this is you, you will:</b></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Identify addressable market, target segments, customer needs, key personas, use cases and competitive landscape</li>
<li>
Craft messaging and positioning that deeply resonates with target audiences</li>
<li>
Develop focused and measurable GTM plans and RTM recommendations</li>
<li>
Launch products and work with global marketing teams to build, scale and measure global campaigns</li>
<li>
Enable and equip inside, direct and channel sales teams</li>
<li>
Represent the company at customer executive briefings, speaking engagements, conferences, press interviews, analyst briefings, etc.</li>
<li>
Be a thought leader and share best practices via blogs, videos, webinars, etc.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<p><b>
Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
B.S. in engineering or computer science, and preferably an MBA</li>
<li>
3-8 years experience in companies delivering software target IT organizations</li>
<li>
Experience positioning technical products to both business and technical buyers</li>
<li>
Strong desire to work in a fast-paced, high-growth environment</li>
<li>
Ability to think strategically and execute effectively as an individual contributor</li>
</ul>
