<p><b>
Mission:</b></p>
<p>
Assure all properties on assigned portfolio meet profitability goals, optimize performance of portfolio and achieve all other company goals.</p>
<p><b>
Accountabilities:</b></p>
<p>
Supervise and manage the operations of a portfolio of multiple properties</p>
<p>
Maximize operating revenue and net operating income for assigned properties, accountable for the financial performance of each property.</p>
<p>
Monitor portfolio's performance through company's APS system</p>
<p>
Conduct regular site visits</p>
<p>
Evaluate status of each property within the portfolio and implement and drive improvements</p>
<p>
Recruit, develop and retain A players</p>
<p>
Oversee the maintenance of physical assets, recommend necessary and value added capital projects.</p>
<p>
Review and approve expenditures</p>
<p>
Maintain working knowledge of Fair housing laws, eviction laws, employment laws and other legal issues including knowledge of governmental regulations.</p>
<p>
Analyze local market trends and vendor and contract management</p>
<p>
Develop and foster relations with Community Residents</p>
<p><b>
Critical Success Factors:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Self-Starter - ability to execute and implement change</li>
<li>
Passion – for the company's mission</li>
<li>
Highly energetic personality – a motivator</li>
<li>
Critical Thinking – strategic and highly analytical</li>
<li>
Leadership – easily move others to action by coaching, planning, motivating, organizing and controlling work being done</li>
<li>
Goal Oriented – naturally motivated to reach goals</li>
<li>
Interpersonal/Communication Skills – an innate ability to channel different points of view; Able to establish and maintain excellent relationships and credibility quickly; Create team atmosphere with internal staff while achieving key objectives; Excellent sales and consulting skills as well as technical writing and public speaking</li>
<li>
Maturity – provide a good balance of risk taking and judgment; Is aggressive and confident; Able to operate independently of a large staff</li>
<li>
Professional – unquestionable integrity, credibility, and character; Who has demonstrated high moral and ethical behavior</li>
<li>
Travel- able to travel up to 50% or as needed.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Education, Training and Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor's degree is desired</li>
<li>
Minimum 5 years' experience as a Multisite Manager or Regional Manager level in the multifamily industry</li>
<li>
Experience in Class C and D Properties is required</li>
<li>
Must be proficient in Microsoft Office, word, excel and power point</li>
<li>
Advanced knowledge of AppFolio or any other property management software is required</li>
</ul>
