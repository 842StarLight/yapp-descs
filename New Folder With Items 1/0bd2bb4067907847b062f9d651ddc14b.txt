<h2>Legal Assistant - Foreclosure</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>Our Cincinnati Law Firm is currently seeking a new Legal Assistant on the Real Estate Default/Foreclosure team! He or she will be responsible for a wide range of functions up to and including the processing and filing of judgments and complaints. If you are an experienced legal professional and consider yourself to be self-motivated and highly organized with strong attention to detail, we invite you to read on and apply to be considered.<br>
<br>
RESPONSIBILITIES:<br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Assist attorney with management of cases and foreclosure timelines.</li>
<li>Prepares and files legal documents with the appropriate court.</li>
<li>Arrange for payment of court cost and fees and sets up billing in accordance with the client billing schedules.</li>
<li>Conducts research including case history, asset location, skip tracing and title review.</li>
<li>Review electronic files, mail and standard legal documents to gather information and locate missing or incomplete records.</li>
<li>Contacts clients, consumers, attorneys, and courts for further instructions, status updates and any additional information and documentation.</li>
<li>Maintain and update client specific e-commerce websites in accordance with their guidelines.</li>
<li>Obtain local counsel coverage for court hearings and sale dates.</li>
<li>Forward accounts to network local counsel; serves as liaison between client, local counsel and firm attorney</li>
<li>Assists with special projects.</li>
<li>Complete all required training applicable to assigned position.</li>
</ul>
</div>
