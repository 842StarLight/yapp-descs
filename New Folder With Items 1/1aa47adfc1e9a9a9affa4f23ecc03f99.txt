 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Job Description: Email Marketing </b></p>
<p><b>Salary range: 70-90K</b></p>
<p>Seeking a motivated, goal-oriented, results-driven <b>Email Marketing Manager </b>to join our exciting start-up in North Miami Beach, FL. Our team is growing and if you are passionate about e-commerce and career growth, this may be a great match for you! In this position, you will be responsible for the strategy approach, execution, and measurement across all email marketing campaigns that increase customer engagement and drive sales. This position will work with a team of marketers, developers, and analysts who are passionate about creating exceptional customer experiences that drive engagement and growth.</p>
<p>Being in a startup environment, you will work side by side with intelligent, passionate, highly motivated entrepreneurs. We're looking for someone who has personality, passion, excitement and a drive to succeed!</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Primary ownership of the email strategy and programs across B2C and B2B audiences.</li>
<li>Partner with business units to ensure email is an effective channel for conversion and revenue.</li>
<li>Lead and develop an email marketing team inclusive of email marketers and developers outside the USA.</li>
<li>Work closely with the Senior Management, Merchandising and Copywriters teams to develop comprehensive plans that support brand, consumer and business goals.</li>
<li>Manage all aspects of email strategy, planning and segmentation process.</li>
<li>Build and refine event-based triggers and lifecycle messaging across the customer lifecycle, including post purchase.</li>
<li>Work closely with Marketing Analytics to leverage and develop audience segmentation strategy.</li>
<li>Develop experiments, A/B testing to continually optimize email performance against brand and business KPIs</li>
<li>Oversee email operations including coding, testing and deployment activities.</li>
<li>Grow the email list and maintain its by managing deliverability, contact frequency and content.</li>
<li>Partner with the Copywriters and Marketing teams to continually test and improve email content and subject lines.</li>
<li>Create relevant reporting and analytics which provide insight into issues and opportunities leading to action.</li>
<li>Manage the relationship with Email Service Provider and all other email vendors.</li>
<li>Thoroughly QA and proofread email campaigns and internal briefs to find typos, errors, and customer experience improvements.</li>
<li>Develop and maintain a marketing tracker system which is used to track creative placements in various promotional and lifecycle emails.</li>
<li>Process unsubscribes daily and assists with pulling lists for segmentation.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in advertising, marketing or a related discipline</li>
<li>+ 5 years’ experience of email and/or direct marketing experience</li>
<li>Ability and proven track record of working and prioritizing across multiple projects and business units</li>
<li>Data driven mindset and ability to quantify success</li>
<li>Enterprise level ESP and project management tools (JIRA) required</li>
<li>Strong communication skills with ability to clearly articulate information that influences data-driven business decisions.</li>
<li>Proven experience leading a campaign management function and outstanding attention to detail.</li>
<li>Be knowledgeable on email best practices, technologies, and trends.</li>
<li>E-commerce experience is a plus.</li>
</ul>
