<h2>Customer Advocacy Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><div><div>
<div>The Customer Advocacy Marketing Manager is responsible for creating, developing, implementing, and executing a robust customer advocacy program with a portfolio of strategic customer references and assets that help accelerate the sales cycle, increase market awareness, and help articulate the customer journey. The Customer Advocacy Marketing Manager will help define the program attributes, build best-in-class templates, and drive necessary system and process investments. They will work closely and partner with multiple internal departments including Sales, Product, and Customer Success to ensure that customer advocates become a key element of Planview’s demand creation.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Develop and manage a scalable and trackable customer reference program that includes: written case studies, customer videos, speaking opportunities, social participation, webinar participation, press releases, and sales references</li>
<li>
Manage vendor relationships and influence strategy for peer review sites</li>
<li>
Develop customer co-marketing campaigns to drive user engagement with and brand awareness of Planview</li>
<li>
Represent the voice of the customer to the rest of the Planview organization</li>
<li>
Work with Customer Success, Product and Sales to build relationships with existing customers and develop a repeatable, streamlined process to identify customer evangelists</li>
<li>
Ensure programs are scalable across products and geographies</li>
<li>
Track and measure key customer advocacy and engagement metrics and present to internal stakeholders</li>
<li>
Partner with Corporate Marketing and Sales to promote successful and engaging customer events, such as Customer Forums and user groups</li>
<li>
Engage with customers on a daily basis, continually working to build strong relationships and identify new advocates</li>
<li>
Support the sales team’s customer requests by becoming the expert on Planview customer stories, having a strong knowledge of what our best content is as it pertains to company attributes (company size, product, and competition), so as to help expedite the sales cycle</li>
<li>
Secure customer references and develop plans for engaging customers based on their ability to validate Planview’s business value, establish confidence in Planview as the best in the industry, and act as thought leaders</li>
<li>
Identify opportunities to acknowledge and reward strategic customer references by providing them with speaking opportunities, executive meetings, and thought leadership platforms in a programmatic fashion</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>4+ years of relevant B2B marketing experience and a proven track record of success. Additional experience in reference or referral marketing, communications, advertising, or customer success a plus</li>
<li>
 Bachelor's degree in English, Journalism, Marketing, Communications, or Business</li>
<li>
Strong presentation and customer-facing skills</li>
<li>
Excellent communication skills (verbal and written)</li>
<li>
Strong analytical skills</li>
<li>
Ability to lead, motivate and direct the work of others</li>
<li>
Ability to work effectively under pressure in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>
Ability to quickly understand and articulate Planview’s solutions, value propositions, and key messages</li>
<li>
Working knowledge of Salesforce</li>
</ul>
