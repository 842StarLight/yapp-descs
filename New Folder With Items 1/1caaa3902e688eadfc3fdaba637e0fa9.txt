<div>
<b>Business &amp; Technology Integration</b> professionals advise upon, design, develop and/or deliver technology solutions that support best practice business changes.</div>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>Technology Consulting</b> provides consultative Business and System Integration services to help clients implement effective solutions. Understands and translates customer needs into business and technology solutions via the analysis, design and/or development of best practice business changes.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Job Description</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>Industry Applications-RESOURCES </b>professionals design, implement and deploy packaged software solutions that have been developed to accommodate unique industry business and management processes, regulatory requirements and other business needs which cross multiple industries.</div>
<div>
<b>Resources Industry Application</b>s professionals analyze, design, build, test and deploy innovative Resources industry-specific solutions - including solutions for the Chemicals, Natural Resources, Energy and Utilities industries - that enable industry standards and processes.</div>
<div></div>
<div>A professional at this position level within Accenture has the following responsibilities:</div>
<ul>
<li>Identifies, assesses and solves <b>complex business problems</b> for area of responsibility, where analysis of situations or data requires an in-depth evaluation of variable factors.
</li>
<li>Closely <b>follows the strategic direction </b>set by senior management when establishing near term goals.
</li>
<li>Interacts with <b>senior management </b>at a client and/or within Accenture<b> </b>on matters where they may need to gain acceptance on an alternate approach.
</li>
<li>Has<b> some latitude </b>in decision-making. Acts independently to determine methods and procedures on<b> new assignments</b>.
</li>
<li>Decisions have a major day to day impact on<b> area of responsibility</b>.</li>
</ul>
