<h2>Business Analyst & Systems Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Business Analyst &amp; Systems Manager</b></p>
<p><b>
ROLE:</b> This position is responsible for supporting Pillar Managers and core team members by providing accurate reporting and analysis of the manufacturing operations performance for Victory Millwork. This position prepares and analyzes operations performance reports and works with various team members to understand the data at a level that results in the ability to drive improvement. This position works as a liaison between all departments.
</p>
<p><b>HOURS:</b> Monday-Friday, Regular Day Hours, Full-Time</p>
<p><b>
RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Oversees the SKU Schema, including Divisions and Classes through tools such as the SKU Builder.</li>
<li>
Maintains integrity of the business systems through careful review of the data being processed.</li>
<li>
Completes ongoing analysis of material and labor variances on manufacturing orders. Works with Operations to assist in process improvements to gain operational efficiencies.
</li>
<li>Supports core team leaders in analysis of Direct Labor, Machine Efficiency, Overall Equipment Effectiveness OEE, and Asset Utilization.</li>
<li>
Analyzes Waste Variances by Operation and works with core teams to plan improvements in areas with the most opportunity.</li>
<li>
Supports Operations to review and adjust Bills of Material and Routings.</li>
<li>
Performs various month-end responsibilities including: communication of results to Pillar Managers, investigation of variance drivers and development of variance explanations with core team leaders.</li>
<li>
Manages and performs physical inventories.</li>
<li>
Calculates and Reports Monthly Success Sharing Metrics to Pillar Leadership Team and core teams.</li>
<li>
Calculate, report and assist with the application and distribution of other key performance indicators to all levels of the organization.</li>
<li>
Supports Business Service Manager &amp; core team leaders in preparation of inputs for budget schedules including Direct &amp; Indirect Labor, Waste, Materials, &amp; Cost Center Spending. i.e. # of heads, workdays, and hours by operation for budgeted volume</li>
<li>
Supports Business Services Manager &amp; core team leaders in preparation of inputs for forecast exercises including Direct &amp; Indirect Labor, Waste, Materials, &amp; Cost Center Spending. i.e. analysis of cost center spending and proposed balance of year spending adjustments</li>
<li>
Supports Business Services Manager &amp; core team leaders to understand performance, negative impacts and potential opportunities and aids in daily operational decision making.</li>
<li>
Identify accounting and business problems, research possible solution, recommend and implement improvements.</li>
<li>
Perform other tasks as required.</li>
</ul>

Dedicated and hard-working.</li>

</ul>
