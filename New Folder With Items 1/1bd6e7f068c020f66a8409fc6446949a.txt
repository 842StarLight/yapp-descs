<h2>Solution Architect</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><p>Design, develop, and articulate solutions based on our business user’s strategic business and technical requirements. The candidate will work with a variety of people (program managers, technical peers and business partners) to ensure that maximum value is delivered to clients.<br>
<br>
<br>
</p></div>
<p><b>Role &amp; Responsibilities﻿</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrated track record of major solution architecture contributions in a large enterprise.</li>
<li>
Lead the implementation and execute the delivery of the architecture designs and solution strategy.</li>
<li>
Experience with Salesforce Sales Cloud and Service Cloud (application design, architecture and development using Salesforce.com), certifications preferred.</li>
<li>
Use deep business knowledge of Salesforce products to drive solution architecture and analysis engagement for major initiatives.</li>
<li>
Applied knowledge of SFDC and CRM processes including Sales, Services and Marketing functions.</li>
<li>
Very strong leadership and influence skills – demonstrated ability to influence program partners to meet project and program objectives.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Education</b></p>
<p>Applicant must have Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or related field and 5 years direct experience in all aspects of developing and implementing CRM or Salesforce platform applications.<br>
<br>
Location: Manchester, NH, or remote.<br>
Applicant must be a U.S. citizen or permanent resident.
</p>
