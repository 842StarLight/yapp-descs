</p>

<p><i>
Atlantic Contracting and Specialites</i> is currently seeking an <b>Account Manager</b> <b>Trainee</b>. As a trainee, you will work with leaders in the industry and learn all aspects of estimating in our industry.
</p>
<p>Base location: <i>Hicksville, NY</i></p>
<p>
Area or Region supported: <i>Greater NY.</i></p>
<p>
Main industries supported: Mechanical Insulation, Asbestos and Lead Abatement, Hazardous Waste Remediation, Fire Stopping, Noise Control, Specialized Fabrication Services, Lagging, Scaffolding, Certified Energy Assessments and Smart Sensors</p>
<p>
The following are some of the essential duties of an Account Manager Trainee:</p>
<ul>
<li>You will be trained to perform initial specification interpretation, take-off of quantities from drawings or by field measurement.
</li>
<li>You will learn how to prepare cost estimates on assigned projects.
</li>
<li>You will also learn to coordinate the accumulation of labor &amp; material cost data, while recording and maintaining such information for guidance in future estimating projects.
</li>
</ul>
<p>A full job description will be given to candidates at the time of their interview.</p>
<p><b>
REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Associates or Bachelor s Degree in construction management, finance, business administration or a related field</li>
<li>A combination of at least 3 years field construction experience plus a high school diploma will be considered in lieu of a degree</li>
<li>Microsoft Office Suite of products such as Word and Excel</li>
</ul>
<p><b>PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Basic knowledge of construction industry and estimating practices</li>
<li>Knowledge of on-screen takeoff software such as (OST, Imagenation, BlueBeam, etc.) and scheduling software (MS Project, Suretrak, P-6, etc.)</li>
<li>Exhibit a spirit of cooperation &amp; teamwork</li>
<li>Enthusiasm and strong work ethic</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task, work under pressure and meet deadlines required</li>
<li>Strong organizational, verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>This position requires travel across a territory</li>
</ul>
