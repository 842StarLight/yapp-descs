<h2>Purchasing Agent</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>General Summary:</b> Obtains requirements by verifying, preparing, and forwarding purchase orders; verifies receipt of items; authorizes payment.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Job Functions:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Processes order requisitions into purchase orders (POs) according to local, state and federal policies and procedures</li>
<li>Purchase goods, materials, components or services in line with specified cost, quality and delivery targets</li>
<li>Act as an interface between suppliers and other relevant departments on purchasing processes, new projects and activities</li>
<li>Provide analysis on costs, new and existing and review cost reduction activities using Smartsheets and Excel sheets</li>
<li>Weekly updates and report preparation for Invoice vs. PO scoreboard, Yearly savings reports using Smartsheets, excel, and Power BI</li>
<li>Work closely with the Purchasing Manager in the procurement function and review opportunities for continuous improvement and business improvements</li>
<li>Work closely with accounting team (accounts payable) to help optimize vendor relations, payable practices, etc.</li>
<li>Follow-up on executed purchase orders and update end-user on the schedule of order delivery as needed</li>
<li>Ensure that a professional and consistent approach is taken in relation to all supplier relationships by keeping excellent and clear communication at all time</li>
<li>Conduct research for new products and suppliers</li>
<li>Compile data relating to supplier performance to enable vendor evaluation</li>
<li>Contact suppliers to resolve price, quality, delivery and work directly with AP on any invoice discrepancies</li>
<li>Support the purchasing function and other relevant departments and communicate any supply problems which may impact on business operations</li>
<li>Manage deliveries and receiving of goods and services by physically receiving and finalizing the receiving process within the purchasing system</li>
<li>Distribute all deliveries to end-user after effectively receiving purchased product</li>
<li>Manage the monthly credit card report for the Purchasing Department</li>
<li>Maintain and manage all purchasing files (e.g. all purchase orders, purchase requests, credit card receipts, receiving slips)</li>
<li>Implement three bid process with preferred vendors while simultaneously pushing cost savings</li>
<li>Maintain our office supply and pantry accounts to ensure all orders are received and pricing is accurate</li>
<li>Organize and maintain inventory within company purchasing closet</li>
<li>Periodically set up presentations for departments with potential new vendors</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Essential Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 2+ years of experience in a purchasing role</li>
<li>Computer literate, with advanced Microsoft Excel, Word, PowerPoint, OneNote, Smartsheet, Interchange skills/abilities, Power BI</li>
<li>Preferable knowledge in Nexus, MRI, Oracle or any other purchasing software</li>
<li>Assertive, resilient and welcomes to change</li>
<li>Strong communication, negotiation, and persuasion skills</li>
<li>Analytical and mathematical capabilities in order to compare prices and quantities and consider vendor contract terms</li>
<li>Ability to evaluate market conditions and various suppliers</li>
<li>Actively committed to the development of the company stakeholders in the use of Policies &amp; Procedures and ordering requests system</li>
<li>Good communication, negotiation, interpersonal and influencing skills</li>
<li>Able to manage time effectively, priorities tasks and achieve set targets</li>
<li>Able to work well under pressure and handle emergency and stressful situations</li>
<li>Keen attention to detail and accuracy</li>
<li>Previous experience of working in a purchasing team preferably within multifamily residential, commercial development construction, hospitality, and technology environments</li>
<li>Good knowledge of purchasing, negotiation, market understanding, and cost breakdown</li>
<li>Willingness to make quick and thoughtful decisions on behalf of the business</li>
<li>Effective relationship building skills</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Essential Physical Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Must be physically able to do assigned work as outlined above</li>
<li>Must be physically able to sit and use the computer for hours at a time</li>
<li>Must be able to lift up to 30 pounds</li>
<li>Must be able to communicate in English effectively with other individuals</li>
<li>Must be able to maintain good relations with employees, supervisors and the public</li>
<li>Must be able to follow written and verbal instructions</li>
</ul>
<p><i>This job description in no way states or implies that these are the only duties to be performed by this position. The employee will be required to follow any other instructions and to perform any other duty requested by the supervisor.</i></p>
