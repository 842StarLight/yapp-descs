<b>Description</b><br>
This role will require the leader to build a team of enterprise sales executives that will be responsible for selling and expanding Deluxe’s footprint to our most strategic accounts within a respective industry. The expectation is that the leader will harness the resources across a matrixed organization to build solutions that uniquely meet the client’s needs. A strong understanding of the client’s business/industry will be needed to understand industry challenges and trends. You will be responsible for developing opportunity pipelines, building proposals, closing new business, expanding relationships and maintaining strong client references. Strong industry skills, strategic thinking, needs based and consultative selling, high sales and business acumen, ability to lead sales teams and achieve quotas are required.<br>
<br>
Accountabilities:<ul>
<li>
Strategic planning around industry GTM strategy, building compelling value propositions and solutions for your clients from Deluxe’s portfolio and alliance partners</li>
<li>
Reviewing pipeline, proposals and sales coaching to make sure that our sales/deal strategies are on point and raising the level of sales exec performance</li>
<li>
Client relationship building and active selling, building pipeline and insuring the team meets its goals</li>
<li>
Supporting sales team to facilitate their collective success</li>
<li>
Forecasting, budgeting and other organizational support</li>
</ul>
