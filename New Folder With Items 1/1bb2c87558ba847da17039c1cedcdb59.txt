<h2>Production Planner</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>PolyPeptide Laboratories Group</b> – One of the world’s largest companies specializing in the manufacture of peptide and related compounds for research and pharmaceutical applications, has an opening in our Torrance, California office where you’ll be valued for your experience, input and teamwork.</p>
<br>
<p>
<b>
Job Title: Production Planner:</b><br>
<br>
We are looking for an experienced production planner, with <b><i>extensive knowledge In ERP planning </i></b><b>software (i.e. SAP) and other ERP</b><br>
<b>Systems, i</b>n a hemical manufacturing environment.</p>
<p>
The production planner is responsible for independently planning PPL’s production and scheduling, and responsible for the coordination of resources and employees, in order to ensure timelines are adhered to. Must be able to consult with designated site management team as needed to ensure production goals are met.</p>
<br>
<p><b>
</b></p>
<p><b>Essential Functions:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for the S&amp;OP process, wherein the purpose is to create a long-term planning (Rolling 12m) and to inform about changes in the established plan.</li>
<li>Short term planning (1m &amp; 3m ) and tracking of deliverables</li>
<li>Responsible for production planning and updating and informing the persons concerned of changes in the established plan.</li>
<li>Keeping the business ERP system updated in real time with regard to planning of production batches.</li>
<li>Responsible for entering and updating of planning parameters in ERP System</li>
<li>Coordinate development material demands with CRM and ensure demands are entered in ERP System</li>
<li>Providing Supply Chain support for material needs planning including correction of obsolete demands or change in demands in conjunction with production planning schedule changes</li>
<li>To support OTIF / RCA / QIRM and deviation investigations for raw materials / shipping / planning etc. when needed.</li>
<li>To coordinate all other planning that affects delivery to the customer such as:</li>
<li>Contact and planning with Sales &amp; Marketing for planned order dates and forecast.</li>
<li>Contact and planning with CPM for planning time for projects and production.</li>
<li>Contact and planning together with QC and QA for analysis and release of both raw materials and produced batches.</li>
<li>Assist in handling impurities and standards in accordance with the applicable manual.</li>
<li>Ability to calculate and apply descriptive statistics in a manufacturing environment</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree in life science or other related field</li>
<li>3 – 5 years of relevant industry experience</li>
<li>Extensive knowledge of MS Dynamics AX application or other ERP Systems</li>
<li>Excellent organizational and time management skills</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Experience in MS Office Suite - Advanced</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>PolyPeptide Laboratories, offer competitive salaries (commensurate with skills and experience), quality work environment and excellent benefits (Medical and Dental Insurance, and 401 k) and opportunity to grow your skills. An Equal Opportunity employer.</p>
