<h2>SAP Systems Analyst II - Sales (NC)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Greenheck’s Systems Analyst II – Sales is responsible for formulating and defining system scope and objectives through research and fact-finding to develop or modify moderately complex information systems. They will prepare detailed specifications from which programs will be written and analyzes/revises existing system logic difficulties and documentation as necessary. Systems Analysts work on most phases of applications systems analysis activities, but requires instruction and guidance in other phases.</p>
<p><b>
As the Systems Analyst II – Sales, you will:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Document business processes. Meets with users, project leaders and management to discuss and plan guidelines and criteria for documentation of a system, program, project plan, and timelines.</li>
<li>
Create effective documents that are clear, succinct, accurate, and appropriately detailed.</li>
<li>
Formulate and define system scope and objectives through research and fact-finding to develop or modify moderately complex information systems.</li>
<li>
Understand basic root-cause of problems and propose possible solutions within the scope of current Greenheck systems, test and identify best solution, create solution document and describe/sell chosen solution.</li>
<li>
Create standard to moderately complex technical design specifications from functional requirements including: system use case diagrams, data flow diagrams, system design models, pseudo code, etc. Some programming knowledge is required. May need to work with programmers to clarify during development.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with project teams. Manage tasks assigned to them and provide estimated time of completion. Understands and can apply project management methodologies.</li>
<li>
Build solid relationships with peers and customers and train end users on processes and transactions.</li>
<li>
Recognizes change management issues and works with others to resolve.</li>
<li>
Business Unit specific duties include:</li>
<li>
Work closely with Sales IT and Digital Solutions teams to assist in the definition of system requirements for improvement across the company.</li>
<li>
Understand business process management and business requirements of internal and external clients and translate them to functional requirements for Microsoft Dynamics CRM, SAP, or any order management systems (OMS).</li>
<li>
Assist to identify, define, and prioritize product configuration projects.</li>
<li>
Engage in activities to support and maintain current systems, including issue management, user acceptance testing, communication, and change management.</li>
<li>
Assist to manage, prioritize, and implement SAP, CAPS and CRM work tickets</li>
<li>
Act as an interface between business units, technology teams, and support teams.</li>
<li>
Gather feedback from key stakeholders to influence solution design and approach, and lead prioritization of requirements</li>
<li>
Maintain strong knowledge of industry trends, advancements in technology, and Microsoft’s product offerings and enhancements</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Associate degree with a minimum of 4 years of related work experience or a four-year degree with a minimum of 2 years of related work experience.</li>
<li>
Strong knowledge of Sales and Distribution systems and their integrations with Finance in a manufacturing environment is required. Additional experience with materials management, production planning, and business intelligence and reporting is preferred.</li>
<li>
SAP experience is required</li>
<li>
Experience with reading and debugging system code</li>
<li>
Ability to learn new programming languages in order to be able to analyst and decipher logic</li>
<li>
Knowledge of Agile project management</li>
<li>
Knowledge of MS office, with Intermediate level of Word and Excel.</li>
<li>
A solid understanding of data modeling, mapping and ETL processes</li>
<li>
Experience with customer service, sales and business unit support is essential</li>
<li>
Excellent verbal and written communication skills and demonstrated ability to communicate through all levels of the organization.</li>
<li>
Ability to manage deadlines and handle multiple priorities in a fast-paced environment with critical deadlines</li>
<li>
Experience with reporting tools</li>
<li>
Experience with new approaches for user interface applications</li>
</ul>
