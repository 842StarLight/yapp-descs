<h2>Employment Specialist (Department of Rehabilitation)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>JOB SUMMARY</p>
<p>This position Employment Specialist-DOR (Department of Rehabilitation) exists to assist clients with severe mental illness with or without co-occurring disorder in training for, seeking, finding, and keeping viable jobs in a Department of Rehabilitation funded employment services program.</p>
<p>RESPONSIBILITIES</p>
<p><i>Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions of the job. </i></p>
<p>DOR Service/Specific Duties: Employment Specialists provide Vocational Assessment, Employment Services (Intake, Employment Preparation, Job Development/Placement, Employment Retention, and Non-Supported Job Coaching Services under this contract by:</p>
<p>· Interviewing authorized MHS Inc., Employment Services SDCBHS/DOR consumers to determine work interests, availability, employability and the need for further employment qualifications.</p>
<p>· Providing assessment by identifying strengths, transferrable skills and barriers to employment to assist consumers in career exploration and set overall rehabilitation goals.</p>
<p>· Working with the Department of Rehabilitation Counselors to ensure a productive job match for each individual.</p>
<p>· Presenting Case Service Contract program to SDCBHS regional staff and update DOR Counselors on program changes and consumer progress.</p>
<p>· Participating in MHS Inc., Employment Services supervision meetings with the Program Manager and Job Developer.</p>
<p>· Facilitating Informational Sessions in assigned region to present the Case Service Contract to potential Cooperative Program consumers.</p>
<p>· Providing employment preparation services to authorized consumers including resume development, interviewing skills, work-related behaviors, grooming, and labor market research.</p>
<p>· Working closely with MHS Inc., Employment Services Job Developer to secure appropriate job placements in accordance with consumer’s interests, skills, abilities and qualifications.</p>
<p>· Identifying job openings which match the training and vocational goals of consumer.</p>
<p>· Educating employers about Americans with Disabilities Act and work with MHS Employment Services Job Developer in identifying reasonable accommodations or develop on-the-job-training (OJT) for work candidates if necessary</p>
<p>· Providing employment retention services to consumers placed in employment to ensure job retention</p>
<p>· Consulting with treatment teams and family members monthly, or as often as necessary, to initiate and maintain a liaison with educational, governmental, and community agencies involved with the Employment Services Program to help consumer achieve their employment goal</p>
<p>· Providing culturally sensitive, gender-responsive, trauma-informed vocational assessment, employment preparation and coaching services with women and men job seekers who may have substance use and co- occurring disorders using a strengths-based approach</p>
<p>· Emphasizing a wellness, resiliency and thriving approach following San Diego County’s “Live Well San Diego” Initiative with all client services.</p>
<p>· Maintaining timely, accurate, and legible documentation using assigned forms and formats for each client in caseload; record documentation in accordance with contract.</p>
<p>QUALIFICATIONS</p>
<p>· A minimum B. A. degree in a social service or business-related field, or two years of experience as a Vocational Counselor in a related field.</p>
<p>· A minimum one-year experience providing either employment preparation, job coaching, job counseling, or job placement or two years of experience in vocational rehabilitation, employment placement, or sales.</p>
<p>· Knowledge and experience with Microsoft Office and EHR software.</p>
<p>· Ability to set priorities, problem solve, and react appropriately to emergency situations.</p>
<p>· Valid California driver’s license and current auto insurance per contract requirements.</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Salary: $22.00 to $24.00 /hour</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul>
<li>job counseling: 1 year (Required)</li>
<li>vocational rehabilitation: 2 years (Preferred)</li>
</ul>
