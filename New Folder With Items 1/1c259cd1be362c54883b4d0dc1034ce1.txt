<h2>Customer Support Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Socio is a venture-backed SaaS company that helps SMBs and enterprises optimize their events. With clients like Google, Microsoft, PepsiCo, and Hyundai, Socio is being used in thousands of events globally while facilitating millions of interactions every day.</p>
<p>As we grow, we are building a team that does awesome work and has an amazing time doing it. If you're looking to be challenged every day, make a huge impact, and help define the future of live events, we would love to have you join our team!</p>
<p></p>
<p>At Socio, our customers' success is essential to our own. As a Customer Support Representative, you will quickly become a valued resource for customers who need a helping hand putting together amazing events using Socio's products. Our team works together to deliver a human and helpful support experience through creative problem-solving, a consultative approach, and a deep and thorough knowledge of how Socio works.<br>
</p>
<br>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities:</b></h3>
<ul><li>Provide Support through three main channels: chat (primary channel), email, and phone.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Handle multiple customer issues at the same time in a fast-paced environment</li></ul>
<ul><li>Partner with our customers to quickly and effectively resolve their issues, from answering simple how-to questions to diagnosing complex software bugs</li></ul>
<ul><li>Adapt and tailor your support style to customers from a myriad of different types of events. The events they are putting on can be any of the following: Conferences, Trade Shows, Expositions, Corporate Events, Association Events, Government Events, Academic Events, and more!</li></ul>
<ul><li>Develop and communicate thoughtful, customized solutions that help customers move forward with being successful</li></ul>
<ul><li>Take full ownership of any technical issues from beginning to end in order to usher each customer to a timely and successful outcome including reporting detailed tickets to our engineers and working with them to notify the customer throughout the process.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Actively and productively team up with other Socio employees, such as sales and marketing team, to drive better results for our customers, including identifying opportunities for customers to benefit from using more of Socio products and/or services.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Document and report software issues to the product and engineering teams at Socio using our established processes and channels, replicating the issue when possible and offering any needed context and research</li></ul>
<ul><li>Demonstrate composure, resilience, adaptability, and urgency as customer needs and case volumes change. Operates with professionalism and integrity.</li></ul>
<br>
<br>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>You're an ideal fit if you:</b></h3>
<ul><li>Are an above-and-beyond teammate who will do whatever you can to make us a better team and keep customers engaged, knowledgeable, and productive</li></ul>
<ul><li>Enjoy flexing some serious problem-solving skills as a customer-facing professional</li></ul>
<ul><li>Confidently use the resources at your disposal to resolve challenges, especially when you don't know the answer.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Are a self-motivated, intellectually curious learner</li></ul>
<ul><li>Flourish in a dynamic environment which thrives on collaboration and in which you can grow.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Set goals for yourself that you achieve and are always pushing yourself to improve</li></ul>
<ul><li>Function extremely well in situations that require flexibility, good judgment, and sound autonomous decision-making based on limited information or in undesirable situations</li></ul>
<ul><li>Adapt quickly to changing priorities and manage time, multiple tasks, and deadlines effectively</li></ul>
<ul><li>Have a clear, personable, kind, and informative communication style in an email, on chat, on the phone, and in-person</li></ul>
<ul><li>Readily help others and are very comfortable asking for help for yourself when you've exhausted all of your resources</li></ul>
<br>
<br>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Requirements</b></h3>
<ul><li>1+ years experience in a customer-facing role</li></ul>
<ul><li>Excellent communication skills, both written and verbal</li></ul>
<ul><li>Your friends describe you as independent and reliable</li></ul>
<ul><li>You thrive in a versatile and dynamic team environment</li></ul>
<ul><li>Always ready to help others and be helped</li></ul>
<ul><li>You can drive your own boat: the ability to fearlessly use the resources at fingertips to resolve technical challenges especially when you don't know the answer</li></ul>
<br>
<br>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Pluses:</b></h3>
<ul><li>Prior experience in customer support and specifically customer support software (Intercom, Zendesk, etc.)</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experience working with SaaS (Software-as-a-Service) software</li></ul>
