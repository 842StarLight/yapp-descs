<h2>Project Manager, International Accounts</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>We are looking for a Project Manager for our international department. This individual thrives in a fast paced collaborative environment. The Project manager is responsible for managing the scheduling, budgeting, resource management, logistics and is the primary client communication for mid to high complexity projects utilizing PEMB (Pre-Engineered Metal Buildings) and conventional steel framing systems. This role requires a person who can build strong relationships, has the ability to develop a strong technical foundation, and is highly functional.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Verifies pre contract project activities as part of establishing project action plans (order clarity)</li>
<li>Reviews and understands projects by conducting thorough reviews of project documents, drawings, specifications, customer requirements and expectations and contracts</li>
<li>Manages aspects of the project finances: budgeting, cash flow, schedule of values, payment milestones, invoicing, change orders, back charges, reporting and final reconciliation (project closeout) – must be able to protect or increase project margins</li>
<li>Drive project action items in accordance with project plans, both internally and externally, for maintaining clarity and understanding of requirements, expectations and objectives throughout the project life cycle</li>
<li>Responsible for driving project clarity of requirements and objectives to design and engineering teams</li>
<li>Maintains consistent communication with the customer regarding work product, drawings, submittals, schedules, processes and expectations</li>
<li>Establishes fabrication production slots, under supervision and in accordance with the project schedules and action plans by coordinating directly with external vendors, suppliers and factory partners</li>
<li>Facilitates purchase orders and work orders with external resources, vendors, suppliers, factory partners and logistics companies.</li>
<li>Engages technical consultants, advisors and sales managers as needed</li>
<li>Helps coordinate project delivery requirements, including material compliance and inspection, logistics, freight forwarding, shipping container programming, customs compliance and material inventory</li>
<li>Creates technical documentation as needed and report on progress of project</li>
<li>Resolves conflicts with projects by demonstrating leadership and appropriate decision-making competencies</li>
</ul>
