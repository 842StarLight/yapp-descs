<b>Enterprise Product Lifecycle Management and Mechanical CAD/CAM Sales Executive</b><br>
<br>
3 HTi, LLC is a privately held systems integrator that is the Global Leader for Digital Transformation for the Manufacturing Enterprise. We provide technology platforms and enterprise applications for smart and connected products, operations, and systems.<br>
<br>
We are looking for an <b>Enterprise Product Lifecycle Management (PLM) and Mechanical CAD/CAM Sales Executive to sell PTC’s Windchill and Creo software. </b>This highly visible role will be responsible for establishing executive relationships and supporting clients. You will execute large enterprise-wide sales pursuits and orchestrate multiple cross-functional teams in this process. The successful candidate will have at least some prior experience in selling enterprise software into the discrete manufacturing sector.<br>
<br>
Our enterprise applications serve manufacturers and other businesses that create, operate and service products. We are looking for remotely based sales professionals in Los Angeles, San Francisco, Philadelphia, Baltimore, Boston, Worcester, Providence, New York City, Dallas/Ft. Worth, Houston, San Antonio, Austin, Atlanta, Charlotte and Orlando. We are headquartered in Mount Laurel, NJ USA<br>
<br>
<br>
<b>Job Description:</b>
<ul>
<li>Territory Planning, Prospecting and generates interest through cold calling, email campaigns, webinars and other activities.</li>
<li>Services existing accounts, obtains orders, and establishes new accounts by planning and organizing daily work schedule to call on existing or potential clients</li>
<li>Establishes overall account plans and maintains a sales pipeline to achieve targets and quotas.</li>
<li>Establishes and maintains forecast accuracy for sales pipeline</li>
<li>Adjusts content of sales presentations by studying the type of prospect.</li>
<li>Under broad direction, applies strategic value selling methodology,</li>
<li>tools, and processes to improve sales effectiveness on complex projects<br>
and pursuits</li>
<li>Quotes and submits orders by referring to price lists and product literature.</li>
<li>Keeps management informed by submitting activity and results reports, such as daily call reports, weekly work plans, and monthly and annual territory analyses.</li>
</ul>
