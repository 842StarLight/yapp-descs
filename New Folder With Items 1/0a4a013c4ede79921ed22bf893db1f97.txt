<p>
Fasturtle seeks an experienced Digital Marketing Manager for our fast-paced, growing Phoenix office. The Digital Marketing Manager will lead all digital marketing efforts on behalf of the agency. Candidates must possess exceptional client and team management skills, as well as a proven track record of success with marketing/advertising campaigns across various digital channels. Fasturtle is a Top 10 Ranked (Ranking Arizona) AZ Website Design and Internet Marketing firm. We have a boutique feel with a small team but provide digital marketing services to 100’s of accounts across the country.</p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Responsibilities:</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>
Serve as the agency’s lead authoritative expert on all aspects of digital marketing</li>
<li>
Be directly responsible for the creation, execution and analysis of digital marketing programs through various channels including digital advertising (paid search, paid social, display and video), SEO, and eCommerce, among others</li>
<li>
Work creatively and collaboratively across multiple agency teams to build digital programs that align with clients’ brand, budget and business objectives</li>
<li>
Implement, monitor, evaluate and refine strategies/executions for optimal results</li>
<li>
Build and implement reporting frameworks to track KPIs and ROI, analyze key data, and uncover insights/trends</li>
<li>
Stay up to date on digital trends, tools, technologies and resources relevant to client categories to keep the agency at the forefront of digital marketing developments</li>
<li>
Oversee the selection, implementation and management of new digital tools as needed</li>
<li>
Support and or lead prospecting pitch meetings, account monthly call/F2F review meetings and quarterly campaign analysis meetings.</li>
<li>
Monthly speaking engagements with local associations on all aspects of digital marketing</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Requirements:</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>
6+ years’ experience creating, executing, managing and optimizing strategic digital marketing programs</li>
<li>
Proven results with sales-driving digital campaigns</li>
<li>
Hands-on experience working with Google digital advertising tools and platforms (Analytics, Ads, Data Studio, Search Console, Tag Manager, etc.), social media ads (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, etc.), eCommerce integrations (e.g., Shopify, WordPress eCommerce) and other applicable resources</li>
<li>
Ability and flexibility to manage competing priorities, responsibilities and several projects simultaneously while meeting deadlines</li>
<li>
Strong problem solving, project management, budgeting, attention to detail, and organizational skills</li>
<li>
Proactive nature and pride in a job well done</li>
<li>
Exceptional client management skills</li>
<li>
Excellent leadership, communication and presentation skills</li>
</ul>
<p>
We offer outstanding professional growth opportunities, a fun and supportive environment, training and development, mentoring in a team-oriented environment, competitive compensation and excellent benefits.</p>
