<h2>Assistant General Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Under the direction of the General Manager, the AGM is responsible for managing property operations on a day to day basis to assure optimum performance and continual improvement in the Key Result Areas (guest service, team members, housekeeping, maintenance, accounting and controls, property appearance, and profit/financial control). Coordinates, directs and manages the staff and everyday hotel operations to achieve profitability and efficiency while maintaining standards set by the company assuring 100% guest satisfaction.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b><i>
Essential Functions:</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrates and promotes a 100% commitment to providing the best possible experience for our guests and team members
</li>
<li>Complete weekly walkthroughs of public areas, guest rooms, back of house with Executive Housekeeper and Chief Engineer
</li>
<li>Inspects and documents repairs and cleanliness of the property with the Chief Engineer and Executive Housekeeper to ensure optimum upkeep and repair, room cleanliness and overall property appearance
</li>
<li>Attends all staff and departmental meetings
</li>
<li>Walks hotel to ensure areas are clean and maintained
</li>
<li>Be in public areas during peak business times to assist in improved customer assistance
</li>
<li>Directly oversees and manages the Front Desk team<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Financial</b></p>
</li>
<li>Responsible for maximizing revenues and flow through to GOP (Gross Operating Profit) to meet or exceed budgeted EBITDA (Earnings Before Interest, Taxes, Depreciation and Amortization)
</li>
<li>Assists in preparation of property budget and forecasts in overseen areas
</li>
<li>Manages labor standards and property level expenses to achieve maximum flow through to the bottom line profit
</li>
<li>Explains and manages financial activities. Reconciles areas of responsibility financial accounts
</li>
<li>Monitors collection of in-house guest balances, direct bill receivables, and issuance of refund checks
</li>
<li>Participates in and monitors monthly inventory of supplies and equipment. Ensures purchases made are within budget and by approved vendors
</li>
<li>Ensures proper room tax guidelines are followed and assists with monthly tax reporting<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Guest Satisfaction</b></p>
</li>
<li>Promotes 100% guest satisfaction throughout the property. Instills the 100% guest satisfaction objective to departmental and hourly associates
</li>
<li>Ensures that all guest related issues are resolved in a manner consistent with the company’s goals and objectives
</li>
<li>Ensures that all Guest Surveys are responded to in a timely manner<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Revenue Management</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Works closely with the Revenue Manager to ensure we are maximizing our revenue as best as possible
</li>
<li>Promptly communicates rate strategies and requests for changes to the Revenue Manager
</li>
<li>Ensure all Room Departments are up to date with selling strategies<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Employee Management</b></p>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Mentors and develops Departmental Managers; provides learning opportunities by assigning new tasks in all Areas Management, evaluates Rooms Department’s strengths and weaknesses and provides training and on the job tasks to prepare all Rooms Managers opportunities
</li>
<li>Performs duties in all aspects of hotel operations whenever needed
</li>
<li>Motivates and gives direction to all employees
</li>
<li>Communicates all policies and procedures to entire staff
</li>
<li>Assists with the planning and preparation of quarterly all team meetings to provide various information including company communications, policy reviews, local property activities, goals, etc
</li>
<li>Follows all Human Resources procedures with the help of the HR Manual
</li>
<li>Adheres to federal, state and local laws employment related laws and regulations
</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Directly oversees the Front Desk team
<ul>
<li>Recruits qualified applicants
</li>
<li>Trains employees in accordance with company standards
</li>
<li>Manages employee personnel forms, including hiring, performance evaluations, payroll and benefits related information, required Federal and State postings, etc
</li>
<li>Utilizes Vibe Software for applicant recruitment and employee management
</li>
<li>Conducts coaching/counseling sessions; performance evaluations; prepares performance improvement plans, disciplinary documentations; conducts terminations
</li>
<li>Ensures that employee related issues are resolved in a manner consistent with company policies<br>
</li>
</ul>
</li></ul>
<p></p>

<p>
In addition to performance of the essential functions, this position may be required to perform a combination of the following supportive functions.</p>
<ul>
<li>Perform other duties and responsibilities as requested (i.e. special guest requests)
</li>
<li>Adheres to all Federal, State, and Corporate policies in all functions
</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned<br>
</li>
</ul>

<p><b><i>Specific Job Knowledge, Skills and Ability:</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Thorough knowledge of the hotel, services and amenities available
</li>
<li>Ability to maintain a high level of professionalism at all times
</li>
<li>Operate with a high degree of autonomy, requiring excellent time management skills and self-motivation
</li>
<li>Ability to motivate employees and give clear directions
</li>
<li>Ability to serve as Manager-On-Duty
</li>
<li>Ability to organize and manage multiple projects, prioritize multiple tasks and meet deadlines
</li>
<li>Ability to provide relief or back-up duties as needed at the hotel including front desk coverage, housekeeping, maintenance, food &amp; beverage and other duties to ensure the optimum operation of the property<br>

Ability to effectively communicate professionally with guests, associates, supervisors, and others as required for optimum operation of the property. Conversational Spanish would be beneficial.<br>
