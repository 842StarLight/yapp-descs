<h2>Engineering Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Perform duties required to manage and direct the product development and test engineering functions of the company; test all valves, centrifugal and axial pumps; perform related engineering functions; supervise and direct the activities of engineering and test personnel.</p>
<p>Including but not limited to:</p>
<p>Oversee engineering personnel and processes to ensure accuracy and integrity required to conduct and certify performance tests on all Lewis products</p>
<p>Conduct weekly meeting with company managers to develop and review production and testing schedules; monitor work schedule of the Engineering department to ensure strategic plans and shipping deadlines are met</p>
<p>Manage a team of engineers and technicians to test work-in-progress; coordinate with Sales Department to ensure on-time delivery; coordinate with product development to test new product designs</p>
<p>Define and implement improvement plans related to test equipment, procedures and software used throughout all aspects of testing and new product development process</p>
<p>Develop detailed test specifications and procedures to assess conformance to product specification and provide the Certificate of Compliance as required to satisfy customer requirements</p>
<p>Ensure proper procedures are established and in use to ensure integrity of all historical and in-process test and development data</p>
<p>Define metrics and track performance to meet organizational goals including on-time-delivery to request, on-time-delivery to promise, cycle time, productivity, and instrument statistical performance against specification</p>
<p>Work with Sales and Marketing to develop the new product strategy and participate in the companyâ€™s strategic planning process</p>
<p>Lead monthly meetings to ensure ongoing management of product development projects and strategic goals</p>
<p>Schedule, coordinate and manage multiple development projects thru collaboration with other weir companies and resources, leadership and mentoring engineers and project managers</p>
<p>Review landscape of existing IP and coordinate within Weir and with external IP services to determine potential issues, competitive assessments, as well as generate new IP during the course of development projects</p>
<p>Help create a culture of cross functional, team-oriented product development, using a phase-gate product development process</p>
<p>Manage group and individual goals, coach and develop employees to achieve success</p>
<p>Promotes a safe work environment, address corrective actions where hazards are identified and provide recommendations to correct any unsafe practices</p>
<p>Education and/or Experience: Bachelor's degree with one to two years related experience and/or training; or equivalent combination of education and experience. (Preferred )- Master's degree with three to five years related experience and/or training; or equivalent combination of education and experience. Pump or valve experience desired.</p>
<p>Mathematical: Ability to comprehend and apply principles of advanced calculus, modern algebra, and advanced statistical theory. Ability to work with concepts such as limits, rings, quadratic and differential equations, and proofs of theorems.</p>
