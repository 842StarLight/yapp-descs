<p><b>General Description:</b><br>
The Director of Relationship Development will be in charge of leading an RDM Sales Team. RDMs upsell all of the Granite products to the existing Enterprise client accounts. These products include all forms of internet access such as Granite On Net layer 2, LEC provided DIA, Broadband, Wireless, MPLS protocol or a fully managed SD WAN solution. In addition RDMs are responsible for upselling our analog customers to VOIP in whichever form is optimal for the customer: SIP or some form of hosted PBX. The Sales Director will also train, and coach their sales team so they are properly self-motivated and constantly developing improved sales skills.</p>
