<h2>Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>The Ascentis Account Executive team is made up of dedicated sales professionals who came to Ascentis because they wanted something different than the big box experience. They wanted to be more than a number and make a true impact in their organization. As an Account Executive you are responsible for driving sales initiatives into an assigned geographic territory. Centered around our cultural norms, you will work in a fast-paced, innovative team environment where you are empowered to sell Ascentis solutions to achieve and exceed monthly and annual sales quota and business targets. Our Account Executives have a huge impact on the growth of our organization and their contribution creates a ripple effect that helps propel Ascentis forward in the marketplace.</p>
<p><b>
Cultural Norms:</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b><br>
What is a day in the life of an Account Executive?</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage sales opportunities through account prospecting (you love the hunt), lead qualification, opportunity management, forecasting, resource allocation, and account strategy.</li>
<li>Deliver corporate presentations and demonstrations via the web to mid and senior level business owners and executives.</li>
<li>Utilize CRM (Salesforce) to track all pertinent information related to the opportunity and account to effectively manage your territory.</li>
<li>Participate in the development, presentation and sale of a complete value proposition primarily via telephone and web contact.</li>
<li>Collaborate with the broader account team (Business Development, Pre-Sales, Professional Services, Account Management).</li>
</ul>
