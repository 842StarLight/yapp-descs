<h2>IT Service Company Office Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Norco Tech’s mission is to provide Virtual CTO and Security Services to our clients. This is accomplished by providing both remote and onsite support. The Office Manager’s primary responsibilities includes: 1- Monitoring, managing, and dispatching incoming client service requests to the appropriate resource to ensure maximum utilization of internal and field resources 2- Assisting and supporting customer invoicing, financial agreements, and financial reporting through our CRM software 3- Coordinating with the President and other to keep track of ongoing projects and events.</p>
<ul>
<li>Prepare all invoices through an automated process based on billing cycle.</li>
<li>Establish and update customer agreements that correspond to service or project contracts.</li>
<li>Prepare sales reports for all products, services, and projects.</li>
<li>Prepare various financial reports for management to forecast service and support costs.</li>
<li>Perform financial activities as required (Invoicing, Sales Reports Financial Reports).</li>
<li>Maintain the Schedule of the President and inform the team of how they can properly support him when appropriate</li>
<li>Act as the single point of contact to the customer for all types of service requests.</li>
<li>Schedule internal and field resources on the dispatch portal to ensure full utilization of resources.</li>
<li>Monitor Service Board to ensure timely resource assignment and follow-up.</li>
<li>Monitor resource schedules to ensure prompt time entry on service requests.</li>
<li>Manage the dispatch process of service requests to ensure full utilization of resources.</li>
<li>Review completed tickets for accuracy and invoice preparation.</li>
<li>Drive problem investigations and resolution as required.</li>
<li>Escalate service requests that cannot be scheduled within agreed service levels.</li>
<li>Identify areas for improvement and make constructive suggestions for change.</li>
<li>Good working knowledge of Microsoft Office.</li>
</ul>
