<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>The Production Associate provides assistance to a designated Loan Officer(s) in originating and processing loans.</div>
<div>
Duties include:</div>
<ul>
<li>
Representing the company in a professional manner and providing an exceptional level of customer service in all interactions and functions of the position</li>
<li>
Following up on leads for potential borrowers</li>
<li>
Coordinating receipt of a complete and accurate loan application package</li>
<li>
Communicating effectively and quickly to all parties, both internally and externally, about timelines and expectations to ensure all loans close on time</li>
<li>
Following up with borrowers for items needed for loan submission</li>
<li>
Tracking the loan throughout the entire loan application process</li>
<li>
Assisting with Email marketing, Social media marketing and event Event coordination</li>
<li>
Ad hoc assistance to support the unique, individual marketing needs of the team</li>
<li>
Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
