With electric vehicles (EVs) expected to be 25% of vehicle sales by 2025 and more than 50% by 2040, electric mobility is in the midst of a tipping point and becoming reality. Supported by more than half a billion dollars in funding from investors, including Quantum Energy Partners, GIC, Clearvision, Daimler Trucks &amp; Buses, Daimler, Siemens, Linse Capital, American Electric Power, Canada Pension Plan Investment Board, Chevron Technology Ventures, Rho Capital Partners, BMW i Ventures and Braemar Energy Ventures, ChargePoint offers a once-in-a-lifetime chance to be part of creating an all-electric future and shaping a trillion-dollar market. What You Will Be Doing
<br>----------------------
<br><br>
In this highly visible role, you will be the resident factory expert in manufacturing process, yields and overall factory
<br>readiness for mass production. As a technical lead role, your ability to combine knowledge of charging station
<br>manufacturing with mechanics and manufacturing process / process controls and deliver results on a tight timeline is
<br>critical. The TPM will work in conjunction with the New Production Introduction group as well as a multi-functional
<br>team of Design, Supplier Quality, Test, Service and Technical Program Engineers to ensure top quality products and
<br>services from the CM and drive excellence into everything we do.
