<h2>Clinical Applications Specialist – OB/GYN, Vascular and/or General Ultrasound</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Description</b></p>
<p><b>
Description</b><br>
Digisonics is a leading provider of structured reporting and image management systems for many clinical areas including OB/GYN, radiology and cardiology. Digisonics is currently seeking a Clinical Applications Specialist with expertise in OB/GYN, MFM, Vascular and/or General Ultrasound.</p>
<p>
The Clinical Applications Specialist is an integral position in Digisonics. The Specialists work closely with clinical personnel at hospitals and other healthcare entities to configure Digisonics applications to meet the sites’ needs for reporting imaging studies. They also prepare training materials and deliver end user training to clinical personnel at customer sites. Specialists provide educational sessions to in-house personnel on company products in the area of their clinical expertise. They partner with Sales personnel to provide product demonstration to potential customers, participate in product design input, product testing, system configuration and creation of product documentation.</p>
<p>
Professionalism, strong clinical knowledge, positive attitude, technical (PC) adeptness and good people skills are all desired qualities in a successful Clinical Applications Specialist applicant.</p>
<p><b>
Qualifications</b></p>

<li>RDMS or RVT credentials</li>
<li>Minimum of four years clinical experience in OB/GYN, MFM, Vascular and General Ultrasound imaging</li>
<li>Industry experience as an Applications/Product Specialist or equivalent desirable</li>
<li>Training experience preferred</li>

</ul>
