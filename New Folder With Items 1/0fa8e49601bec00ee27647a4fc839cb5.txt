<h2>Sales Operations Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>The Sales Operations Manager is responsible for revenue generation by supporting the Regional Sales Team and has complete ownership of all inside sales operations. The ideal candidate is marketing savvy and driven to increase market share all while satisfying demand for sales analytics. The Sales Operations Manager understands the pulse of the business and is the main point of contact for Marketing, Sales, Operations and Aftermarket. Customers and team members should find the Sales Operations Manager as a credible and knowledgeable source of information on new products, parts and services.</p>
<p><b>
Key Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Drive the lead generation efforts and qualify customers via websites, calls, advertising etc.</li>
<li>Oversee and execute initiatives that optimize the rental fleet with the goal to sell every machine</li>
<li>Plan and execute best approach for marketing equipment to current and future customers using optimal marketing distribution channels</li>
<li>Implement and own the CRM system - train new users, oversee data input, perform analysis and leverage CRM to drive better sales closure ratios and better predictability around sales funnel</li>
<li>Shape the marketing plan for the organization, including tradeshows and customer events with a goal to increase the customer experience</li>
<li>Develop social media outreach, sales materials and marketing collateral</li>
<li>Provide sales analytics to executives through the use of sales reporting to be used for high level strategy development</li>
<li>Plan and deploy accurate forecasting models for new and used equipment sales</li>
<li>Ensure tight connection between sales forecast and financial forecast as well as connections with inventory and production schedules – Owner of the sales funnel and forecast</li>
<li>Analyze and provide strategic feedback regarding pricing and demand for new equipment</li>
<li>Validate and ensure compliance as designated by company sales processes and practices with regards to agreements and PO’s</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Knowledge,Skills and Abilities:</b></p>
<p>
Education</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree or equivalent combination of education and experience required</li>
</ul>
<p>Experience</p>
<ul>
<li>5+ years of experience in marketing, inside sales or equivalent experience</li>
<li>2+ years working with a CRM – SalesForce or Microsoft Dynamics, or similar</li>
</ul>
<p>Technical Skills</p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to negotiate and influence outcomes</li>
<li>Ability to define problems, collect data, establish facts, draw valid conclusions and negotiate or influence appropriate outcomes</li>
<li>High proficiency in standard business applications included in Microsoft Office Suite, Adobe PhotoShop and InDesign</li>
</ul>
<p>Behavioral Attributes</p>
<ul>
<li>Thorough communication, interpersonal and organizational skills</li>
<li>Ability to hold themselves accountable for driving results all while achieving high customer satisfaction</li>
<li>Desire to win</li>
</ul>
<p>Travel</p>
<ul>
<li>This position requires some travel, no more than 25%. Most travel is outside the local area and overnight.</li>
</ul>
<p>Working Conditions</p>
<ul>
<li>Working is primarily light duty and requires the ability ti regularly lift and carry objects weighing up ti 20 pounds. this position requires the ability to sit, stand and walk unassisted. Requires the use of hands and fingers to handle or fell objects, tools or controls. The employee is required to talk and hear. Normal correctable vision is required.</li>
<br>
</ul>
