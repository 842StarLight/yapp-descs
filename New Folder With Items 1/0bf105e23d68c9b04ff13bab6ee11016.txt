<h2>Sr. Product Marketing Manager for Video Interface Products 1</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>Synaptics is the pioneer and leader of the human interface revolution, bringing innovative and intuitive user experiences to intelligent devices. Synaptics’ broad portfolio of touch, display, biometrics, voice, audio, and multimedia products is built on the company’s rich R&amp;D, extensive IP and dependable supply chain capabilities. With solutions designed for mobile, PC, smart home, and automotive industries, Synaptics combines ease of use, functionality and aesthetics to enable products that help make our digital lives more productive, secure and enjoyable. (NASDAQ: SYNA ) www.synaptics.com .<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>Synaptics is growing our Video Interface team and so we are looking for a Sr Product Marketing Manager to drive product definitions and business development. As a key leader in the IoT business unit, recognizing the unique attributes and strengths of the Synaptics’ solution you will both promote existing products to customers and partners and drive strategic product planning. You will work with sales leadership across the US and Asia to define and promote new products and drive design wins for Synaptics’ next generation connectivity products.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>The Sr. Product Marketing Manager role is customer-facing. Focus will be on further strengthening Synaptics’ position in the market with key partners and provide insight into evolving market trends.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<p><b>Key Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Own, develop and drive strategy for semiconductor interface product marketing, product management, and business development
</li>
<li>Develop new product business plans based on market analysis and customer inputs
</li>
<li>Identify new business opportunities as well defining product roadmaps with differentiated products
</li>
<li>Develop &amp; execute Go to Market strategy and plans, drive product adoption and business growth
</li>
<li>Manage key customer relations and customer projects from concept to production
</li>
<li>Focusing on audio and video applications you will work closely with customers, silicon development, platform &amp; customer engineering, program management, and sales/FAEs.
</li>
<li>Collaborate closely with cross-functional teams to ensure successful, on-time delivery of products<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>BSEE and 10-15 years of industry experience or a MSEE and 8-10 years. The preferred candidate will also have a minimum of 5 years’ experience in product marketing, or product line management.
</li>
<li>Knowledge of interface technology and implementations in embedded processors, mixed signal devices and/or embedded software - high-speed serial protocols like USB-C, Thunderbolt, DisplayPort, PCIE, HDMI and/or MIPI high-speed audio and video interfaces
</li>
<li>Experience developing and leveraging ecosystem partners, particularly for joint technology platforms and go-to-market execution.
</li>
<li>Experience conceiving, negotiating, and closing contractual agreements with partners (with legal support).
</li>
<li>Demonstrated Ability to formulate creative strategic and tactical plans and justify them, and excellent communication skills to convince and motivate others to participate.
</li>
<li>Effective at building support for projects throughout large organizations: C-level, engineering, and other stakeholders (both internally and at customers)
</li>
