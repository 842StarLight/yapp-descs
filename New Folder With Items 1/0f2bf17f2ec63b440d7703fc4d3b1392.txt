<div>
<b>Who we are</b><br>
EdCast is an AI-powered knowledge cloud for unified discovery, knowledge management, and personalized learning. Our award-winning Learning Experience Platform (LXP) is used globally by Fortune 500 companies and government organizations to solve the discovery, curation and recommendation problems of content fragmentation across external, internal, and tacit knowledge sources. In addition to the LXP, our company’s solutions include SalesU sales enablement suite, GuideMe's multi-language in-app content authoring tools and Leapest By EdCast Corporate Learning Marketplace.</div>
<div>
<br>
Our mission is to democratize learning. Often called the “Netflix of Learning” our goal is to provide frictionless access to knowledge for our enterprise and association customers making learning “the way we work”. By doing so, our goal is to help upskill the globe to meet the demands of a rapidly changing digital world in the 4th Industrial Revolution.<br>
<br>
The headquarters of EdCast is located in Mountain View, California, in the heart of Silicon Valley, with growing offices in Rotterdam and Mumbai. We are excited that in December 2018 we have closed an additional $33.6 million from investors including Softbank, REV Venture Partners, London Fund, Crescent Enterprises, and others.<br>
<br>
<b>What the role is about</b><br>
As a Senior Sales Director you are responsible for growing our client base with a focus on Global 2000 clients. You are committed to win and taking advantage of the untapped customer base that could benefit from EdCast product offering. You should have a proven track record of developing and executing key growth sales strategies, tactics and action plans; hitting annual targets, building relationships and understanding customer trends.</div>
<div></div>
<div>This position is virtual and open to East Coast locations.</div>

<div>If this is you, and you want to make a difference and contribute to the success of our scale-up business, you came to the right place. We also offer an attractive compensation package to who is ready to join this journey.<br>
