<h2>Marketing Associate (Paid Ads)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Are you an impact-driven marketer with experience or interest in creating and managing paid advertising and looking to learn more?
<br><br>
We’re looking for a Marketing Associate to help manage our paid ads. You’ll need the desire to learn how to create amazing ads and the analytical ability to learn to optimize them. You’re going to need to dig deep and be ready to grow into a true paid ads professional to help us make a real dent in our mission, which is, “To make world-class marketing easy for everyone”.
<br><br>
Tailwind is a visual marketing tool loved by over 400,000 brands, businesses, and influencers the world over. Today, our software makes marketing on Instagram and Pinterest easier and more effective.
<br><br>
<b>What you’ll love about this role:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>You’ll be learning to create and manage ad campaigns that provide value to small businesses and entrepreneurs, helping them to pursue their dreams and thrive financially using our platform.</li>
<li>Our marketers are a tight-knit, hand-picked team who want to help you learn and learn from you!</li>
<li>You’ll have the freedom and support to challenge yourself with difficult problems without being afraid of failing, with opportunities to learn from mistakes.</li>
<li>We’re nimble and fast-moving, without corporate red tape, approval layers, or politics.</li>
<li>You’ll get ownership shares, company-paid insurance premiums and extremely competitive benefits package, salary refreshes twice a year with annual bonuses and an unlimited vacation policy</li>
</ul>
<b>About You:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Empathy: You’re an empathetic marketer who takes the time to understand our customers and the problems those customers need to solve.</li>
<li>Hungry to learn: You’re hungry to master a variety of ad platforms (For example: Facebook, Twitter, Snap, Google Adwords, Google Display, Bing, Reddit, Quora, etc.)</li>
<li>Results-oriented: You’re focused on moving the needle, yet adept at balancing short-term gains with long-term strategy and brand objectives.</li>
<li>Natural collaborator: You’re able to work with stakeholders across Tailwind to test positioning and get distribution for our marketing campaigns, product launches and more.</li>
<li>Iterative: Always testing and iterating.</li>
<li>Competitive: Our strongest competition are world-class ad buyers, so you’ll need to bring your A-game.</li>
<li>Team-centric: An authentic, positive, and energetic team-player without the burden of an oversized ego.</li>
<li>Creative and passionate: You love to push the boundaries of what’s possible, to figure out cool new stuff, to get an edge.</li>
</ul>
Our ideal candidate would also have an interest in growing in the following areas:
<br><ul>
<li>Ad creation: You have the desire to devise creative and copy so resonant that it can’t be ignored.</li>
<li>Ad placement: You want to hone your ability to serve the right ad to the right audience on the right network at the right time.</li>
<li>Long-term thinker: You’re looking to learn how to nurture strangers through a satisfying customer journey all the way to advocacy.</li>
</ul>
<b>Desired skills &amp; experience:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Experience in marketing: Either through an undergraduate degree in a relevant field (example: marketing, design, public relations, data science) or experience working in the marketing field.</li>
</ul>
Requirements
<br>Please provide at least one example of some successful marketing that you’re proud of. Embed your example in a Google Doc, provide a little context around the goals and any other pertinent information you think we should know, and then include the link to the Google Doc in your application.
<br><br>
This position is based in our Oklahoma City office.
<br><br>
<b>Note:</b> Please don’t talk yourself out of applying if you don’t “check all the boxes.” We support each other’s growth and celebrate our differences at Tailwind. If you’re passionate about growing as a marketer in the paid ads space, feel free to apply. We’d love to hear from you.
<br><br>
If you have any questions, you can also email careers@tailwindapp.com. We look forward to hearing from you!
