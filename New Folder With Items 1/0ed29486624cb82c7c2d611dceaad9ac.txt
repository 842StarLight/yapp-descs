<h2>Director, Sales Enablement</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">We are a fast-growing company in the healthcare technology space with a suite of intuitive patient engagement solutions that streamline the responsibilities of hospital staff, increase patient involvement and satisfaction, and positively influence outcomes.
<br><br>
Our team is incredible. We are an award-winning company that strives to do better every day. Besides our fun team activities, company masseuse, and a cornucopia of healthy snacks, the work ethic, commitment to core values, and firm knowledge that we help people across the country are really what ties us all together.
<br><br>
We are currently looking to hire a Director, Sales Enablement to join the team.
<br>-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
<br><br>
The Director, Sales Enablement will play a critical role in driving the success of our sales team, ensuring every team member has the knowledge, skills, and tools to drive our sales goals. This is a highly cross-functional role, giving you the opportunity to collaborate to develop a systematic approach that equips all sales teams to be successful in every interaction with prospects and customers. You will be responsible for defining, developing and training on sales methodology, process, tools and best practices. You will assess and make recommendations on sales content and drive unified communications to the sales team about CipherHealth's solution portfolio.
<br><br>
We are looking for someone who is business focused, sales minded, collaborative, entrepreneurial and will thrive in our fast-paced team.
<br><br>
You will report directly to the CRO and work closely with the Sales Leadership, Sales &amp; Business Development, Sales Operations, HR, Product Management and Marketing teams.
<br><br>
Key Responsibilities
<br>--------------------
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Manage and reinforce our sales methodology and sales processes to understand and enhance the skills, knowledge, process and tools that are required to increase velocity and conversion rates at each stage in those processes</li>
<li>Conduct an inventory of current skills, processes, knowledge and tools and work with the sales leadership team to identify strengths and areas for development</li>
<li>Development and management of the Sales Enablement roadmap</li>
<li>Translate messaging into actionable phone pitches, email templates, cheat sheets and other tools for specific segments, use-cases and buyer personas, enabling the business development and sales teams to have consistent, effective and engaging sales conversations with prospects and customers at each stage of the sales cycle</li>
<li>Develop innovative sales tools like day-in-the life demo scripts, videos, whiteboards and simple PowerPoints that make it easier for sales teams to explain the portfolio of products</li>
<li>Collaborate with sales to develop relevant and engaging training, especially for new hires, new products and/or solutions</li>
<li>Ownership of the new hire onboarding process for all Sales &amp; Business Development new hires, supported by the Sales Management team</li>
<li>Manage the rollout, adoption and knowledge transfer of best practices on how to leverage CRM and other sales enablement tools and ensure alignment to sales processes</li>
<li>Enable sales managers to effectively engage with and maximize the productivity of their teams</li>
<li>Help in the selection and deployment of technologies to drive sales efficiency and effectiveness</li>
<li>Collaborate cross-functionally with Product Management &amp; Marketing to manage a content repository of sales assets to ensure a centralized repository of materials that meets the needs of the sales organization</li>
<li>Implement a continuous improvement program for our comprehensive on-boarding and ongoing training programs and establish measurable success metrics</li>
</ul>
