<h2>Leadership Giving Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div>
<p><b>SUMMARY</b></p>
<p>Working in a team environment, this position is dedicated to meeting and learning from potential donors of their interest in supporting local health care.</p>
<p><b>ENTRY REQUIREMENTS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in communications, business, or related area.</li>
<li>A minimum of two years’ experience in fundraising, sales, direct marketing or related field, preferably health care related.</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication and interpersonal skills.</li>
<li>Proficiency with Microsoft Word, e-mail, excel and other software programs.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to work in fast-paced environment with deadlines.</li>
<li>Comfortable with reaching out to potential donors.</li>
<li>Ability to manage several priorities at one time.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>ORGANIZATION</b></p>
<p>This position reports to the President of Munson Healthcare Foundations.
</p>
<p><b>POPULATIONS SERVED, COMPETENCIES, INCLUDING AGE OF PATIENTS SERVED</b></p>
<p>No direct clinical contact with patients.</p>
<p><b>SPECIFIC DUTIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Supports the Mission, Vision and Values of Munson Healthcare.</li>
<li>Embraces and supports the Performance Improvement philosophy of Munson Healthcare.</li>
<li>Promotes personal and patient safety.</li>
<li>Uses effective customer service/interpersonal skills at all times.</li>
<li>Supports the mission of Munson Healthcare Foundations to inspire charitable support to enhance health care for the people in northern Michigan.</li>
<li>Contacts and visits potential donors to discover possible interest in supporting Munson Healthcare with gifts of $15,000 or more.
</li>
<li>Composes correspondence and proposals to potential donors.</li>
<li>Provides staff support to board/volunteer committees. Demonstrates success in working with board committees to develop and implement goals and work plans.
</li>
<li>Attends and supports various Munson Healthcare and community events.</li>
<li>Quickly responds to requests made by various team or community members.
</li>
<li>Tracks, monitors, and communicates progress towards goals by entering data into Raiser’s Edge as outlined in written protocols. Prepares written reports as needed.
</li>
<li>Performs other duties and responsibilities as assigned.</li>
</ul>
