<h2>Field Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Are you ready to join a high growth company with an aggressive vision for the future?! If you are at a position in your career that sees you poised for explosive growth, we want to hear from you! We are International Car Wash Group, the largest car wash conglomerate in the world! ICWG has an exciting opportunity within our Field Marketing Manager.</p>
<p>The Field Marketing Manager develops and executes ICWG local marketing strategy within assigned markets/regions of the U.S. The role is responsible for flawlessly executing local marketing tools with a goal to attract and elevate consumers’ interest and desire to wash their cars more frequently driving profitable car wash traffic at the local level, build brand awareness, and grow local community engagement in a measurable way.</p>
<p>The Field Marketing Manager must possess a drive to understand the distinctions of different DMAs and various local marketing tactics to include traditional and non-traditional community engagement. Field marketing plans will be designed to serve our car wash customers more personally, at scale, by fully integrating digital mediums, strategic partnerships and commercial sales team collaboration. With this regional focused growth, this newly updated offense will connect the ICWG Regional Brands and car wash products with consumers in new and more powerful ways across the United States.</p>
<p>ICWG U.S. Field Marketing managers are passionate about winning more consumers washing their cars, more often with each of our Regional Car Wash Brands. Tangibly activating ICWG’s dedication to being the world’s best local car wash in every market we serve.</p>
<p>This position reports into the Vice President of Marketing.</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b> include, but are not limited to:</p>
<p><b>Field Marketing Expertise</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develops, executes, and manages local marketing strategies, goals, and objectives. Specifically dedicated to promotions that drive consumer car wash volume and loyalty.</li>
<li>Provides thought leadership and processes to the local marketing initiatives and marketing spend</li>
<li>Implements, monitors, and measures local programs to support broader marketing initiatives including attending trade shows, festivals and sponsored events in priority markets.</li>
<li>In-depth understanding of wash products/chemicals, vacuums and any new products specifically introduced to ICWG express car washes and their difference from competition</li>
<li>Explore and identify emerging new ways to drive on site traffic, upsell car wash packages and add-ons at the local level</li>
<li>Own local marketing toolbox execution and real time feedback how to improve process and impact in the local markets</li>
<li>Support local site relationships with the community through community events, cross promotions with site neighbors, school programs and local charities that are on strategy with Global Community Outreach strategy</li>
<li>The Field Marketing manager is the main local marketing point of contact within the region and owns end to end execution of Festivals and 3rd party events in the region, as well as communication with local and regional sites about all promotions, sponsorships, etc.</li>
<li>Conducts consumer qualitative research at local sites regularly, including routine reports back to ICWG corporate and regional brands.</li>
<li>Stays current and assesses local market competitive activity</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Strong Relationship Builder and Partner</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Partners with local site staff to ensure local marketing execution is fully communicated at all times as well as strategy aligns with national marketing and overarching business goals</li>
<li>Fully connected in real time with national marketing team to guarantee one marketing voice</li>
<li>Monitors local toolbox usage and needs as well as gives feedback on how to adjust accordingly to make more impactful going forward</li>
<li>Confirms all local marketing adheres to regional brand standards</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Measurements and Reporting</b></p>
<p>Prepares comprehensive reports on local marketing by connecting performance, sales and learnings from all programs to drive proactive recommendations and strategies for future planning.</p>
<ul>
<li>Provides counsel on local marketing strategies</li>
<li>Maintains a consistent system of reporting and metrics by DMA</li>
<li>Develop effective conversion strategies from field activation to accelerate driving car wash volume and service upgrades.</li>
<li>Track market performance and improve forecast performance of local marketing activation through understanding demand and redemption assumptions.</li>
<li>Monitors marketing spend by market on a monthly basis</li>
<li>Handles one off requests, tracking results as needed</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Performs Other Responsibilities as Assigned</b></p>

<li>Ability to communicate complex concepts in clear concise language; to demonstrate active listening while engaging others; and to establish, implement, and clearly communicate strategies that align with business needs and objectives to gain buy-in from stakeholder</li>
<li>Ability to develop and maintain positive business relationships (e.g., senior management, stakeholders, local carwash sites, other departments, etc.) and foster an environment of mutual respect, understanding, trust, and support</li>
