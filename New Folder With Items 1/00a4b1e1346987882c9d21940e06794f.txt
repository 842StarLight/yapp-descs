<h2>Underwriting Assistant, Commercial Insurance</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<b>AmWINS</b> is looking for an <b>Underwriting Assistant </b>for their Orlando location. The Underwriting Assistant will be responsible for providing support to an Underwriter by preparing information for a variety of reports and documents, processing inquiries and forms and communicating with outside sources.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>The successful candidate will possess a vibrant personality, excellent communication skills, great motivation, capacity to understand concepts and drive to succeed in a team environment. As an Underwriting Assistant, you will have the opportunity to grow your career with one of the largest independent wholesale insurance brokers and general agencies in the U.S. insurance industry, and work with industry leading Surplus Lines Carriers.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>To the qualified candidate we offer:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Compensation includes a salary commensurate with experience</li>
<li>Full benefit package including: Medical, Dental, Life, Disability and 401(k)</li>
<li>Collaborative, continuing education focused work environment</li>
<li>Opportunity for advancement</li>
<li>Opportunity for formal sales and leadership training</li>
<li>Generous Paid Time Off (PTO)</li>
<br>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Underwriting Assistant duties:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Preparing information needed to market new and renewal business</li>
<li>Monitoring various reports for consistency</li>
<li>Potential for policy issuance and endorsement processing</li>
<li>Make and follow through with request for missing information from outside sources</li>
<li>Address inspection report issues
</li>
<li>Assist in accounting procedures such as invoicing</li>
<li>Assist the team renewal underwriter</li>
<li>Data entry into multiple internet based systems
</li>
<li>Use Microsoft Office software, (e.g. Outlook, Word and Excel)</li>
<li>Answer incoming calls</li>
<li>Multi-Tasking</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned<br>
</li>
</ul>
<div></div>

<b>Underwriting Assistant requirements</b>:</div>
<ul>

</li>

<li>Excellent computer skills including typing proficiency, Microsoft Office proficiency with an emphasis in Excel spreadsheets</li>
<li>Strong written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Excellent attention to detail</li>
<li>High-level of motivation</li>
