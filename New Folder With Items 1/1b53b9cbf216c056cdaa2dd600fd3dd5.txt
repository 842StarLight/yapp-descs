<h2>Lead Generation Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>TransNational Payments is a credit card processing and payroll solutions firm who’s mission is to provide the very best experience to both our customers and employees. TransNational believes in fostering career and personal growth, as well as, an environment that is high performing and fun. We are seeking a Lead Generation Specialist for a high growth opportunity to break out into our Sales department.</p>
<p>The Lead Generation Specialist will be identifying lead opportunities for all of TransNational’s services and businesses: Credit Card Processing, Payroll, Software Sales (SaaS), and IT services. The Lead Generation Specialist is responsible for outbound B2B prospecting, qualifying, and scheduling meetings between our customers and sales team.</p>
<p><b>Deliverables: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop new business via the telephone and mass communications to introduce TransNational’s solutions and identify decision-makers within specific target markets; Proactively contact B2B prospects (80+ calls and emails per day)</li>
<li>Conduct a needs analysis to identify prospect’s pain points and determine how TransNational’s solutions will speak to those needs</li>
<li>Build and cultivate prospect relationships in order to move opportunities through the sales funnel</li>
<li>Enter and update lead data into our CRM database</li>
<li>Collaborate with sales on new and projective lead pipeline activity weekly</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Conduct investigative research on target companies and different verticals</li>
<li>Deliver weekly reports on call activity, lead, and opportunity generation</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Who you are: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>1 year of lead generation, sales prospecting, or sales experienced preferred; if you are motivated to start a new sales career than we’d like to speak with you too!</li>
<li>Must have exceptional phone communication skills, with thick skin - working with a sense of urgency</li>
<li>Task-oriented, taking great notes and efficiently entering data into our CRM database</li>
<li>Initiative, organization, team skills, and a desire to learn are critical and could make or break this opportunity</li>
</ul>
