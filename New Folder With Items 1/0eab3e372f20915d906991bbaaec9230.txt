<h2>Business Development Reprensentative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>General Summary</b>
<br>
<br>
The Dealer Support Representative is responsible for the selection, growth, development, and continuous improvement of the dealer network through an evolving global service and support network. The position will coordinate with both internal and external organizations to ensure delivery of consistently high levels of customer service through an international network of Dealers, Authorized Service Centers, and fleet operators
<br>
<br>
<b>Duties and Responsibilities</b>
<ul>
<li>Work closely with Honda Aircraft Sales, Marketing, and Customer Service organizations in the development and analysis of data to identify market needs, demands and growth potential of the global service and support network.</li>
<li>Assist in the identification, assessment, evaluation, and selection of aircraft service partners and development of presentations and business analysis for consideration of the Distribution Committee.</li>
<li>Facilitate the development and expansion of a fully integrated service and support network.</li>
<li>Coordinate with FSRs in accomplishing audits and monitoring the performance of Dealers/Authorized Service Centers in achieving published metrics for reporting and service operations, identify areas for improvement, and then collaborate with the Dealer/Authorized Service Center to implement process improvements and correct any deficiencies.</li>
<li>Assist FSRs in developing tools to facilitate communication with the Dealers/Authorized Service Centers to enhance fleet reliability and reduce operating costs.</li>
<li>Assist in development and presentation of customer meetings and training programs.</li>
<li>Develop metrics to manage performance of service network.</li>
</ul>
