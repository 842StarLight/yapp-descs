<h2>Bookkeeper</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><b>JOB SUMMARY</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Performs a variety of basic accounting, payroll and bookkeeping services for small businesses. Assists with providing advice, delivering advice products and client based products to small businesses and small business owners.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Performs a variety of basic accounting, payroll and bookkeeping services for small businesses.</li>
<li>
Assists with providing advice, delivering advice products and client based products to small businesses and small business owners.</li>
<li>
Identifies opportunities to increase the level of service provided to small businesses and small business owners as their requirements become more complex.</li>
<li>
Identifies client satisfaction issues and reviews the issues on a timely basis to the Bookkeeper Supervisor for resolution.</li>
<li>
Identifies the schedule of charges for assigned clients and forwards the charges for processing and collection.</li>
<li>
Tracks and documents chargeable and non-chargeable hours accurately and on a timely basis.</li>
<li>
Provides guidance and assistance to other bookkeepers, as needed, to ensure the timely and accurate completion of duties.</li>
<li>
Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
QUALIFICATIONS</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
A minimum of six hours of college-level accounting coursework or an equivalent combination of education and experience.</li>
<li>
A minimum of one year experience providing accounting and payroll services.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of accounting principles, practices and techniques.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of sales and payroll tax laws, payroll practices and applicable regulations.</li>
<li>
Ability to write simple journal entries and accrual entries. Ability to prepare clear, concise financial records and reports.</li>
<li>
Ability to collect, analyze, and summarize financial data and to assimilate into a useful format</li>
<li>
Demonstrated organizational, prioritization, and detail-oriented abilities to meet established work output timelines. Ability to work with limited supervision.</li>
<li>
Possess a passion to work with small business owners.</li>
<li>
Ability to develop and maintain effective client and employee relationships</li>
<li>
Ability to work under pressure; possesses the stamina to meet the time deadlines inherent in the tax and accounting profession.</li>
<li>
Effective oral, written and interpersonal communication skills.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Preferred Qualifications</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Strong command and use of the English language and other languages required in the assigned geographic area</li>
<li>
Certified in Quickbooks</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Technical skills
</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Understanding and previous use of a Windows-based computer system.</li>
<li>
Proficiency in MS Word, Excel, and Outlook.</li>
<li>
Proficiency in accounting software such as Creative Solutions Accounting, Quick Books Pro, Peachtree, Great Plains, etc.</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
