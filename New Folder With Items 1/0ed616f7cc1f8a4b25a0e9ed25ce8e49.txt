<b>Job Description</b><br>
<p>he Sales and Marketing Group (SMG) exists to deliver world class customer experiences in ways that maximizes our revenue, design- wins, as well as our brand and reputation. We are customer obsessed, amplifying their voice, providing personalized support and scale, in order to accelerate Intel's transformation to a data- centric leader.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
Inside SMG is Intel's Olympic Technology Group (OTG) who brings the latest Intel technology to the Olympic games in order to showcase a broad range of products/capabilities and drive business opportunities.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
As a Product Development Intern you will be responsible for:</p>
<ul>
<li>Assisting the OTG staff in designing and building out sports performance AI platforms for the athletes and audiences of the Tokyo 2020 Olympic Games.</li>
<li>Developing coaching and training products to be used by elite athletes and training facilities</li>
</ul>
