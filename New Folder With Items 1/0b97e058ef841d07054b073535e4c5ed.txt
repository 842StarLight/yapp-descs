<h2>Lead Analyst, DMO</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">The Lead Analyst DMO position is responsible for Master Data Management (MDM) strategy and tactics for materials, customers and vendors across the enterprise. This leader contributes independently, through others and organizationally. Lead, coach, and train team members, end users, business partners and stakeholders on MDM processes and master data lifecycle to enable accurate, efficient, data-based processes and decisions. Influence, improve and facilitate documentation and use of the MDM data standards/definitions, process flows and supporting systems in an effort to advance our master data governance maturity. Create, measure and analyze master data content &amp; quality to find and drive opportunities to improve the quality, accuracy and usability of the master data. Project Management and development of business and IT projects that impact master data, which include but not limited to putting new processes and/or software solutions in place for master data management
<br><br>

<li>Minimum of 5 years of experience with SAP or equivalent ERP system</li>

<li>Subject matter expert in one or more domain(s) – Material Master, Customer Master and or Vendor Master  Experience with SAP tools Master Data Governance (MDG), Data Services (DS), Information Steward (IS) and/or LSMW a plus</li>
<li>Experience as a project team member or business process team member on a business or technology project a plus</li>
<li>Experience in business process design, documentation and implementation, which can include workflow design and automation a plus</li>
<li>Experience in project management a plus</li>

<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Provide master data leadership, training and change management to internal team members, end-users, business partners and stakeholders</li>
<li>Manage the full master data lifecycle with little or no supervision</li>
<li>Build strong relationships and partnerships with business stakeholders, leaders and end-users, including but not limited to, Finance &amp; Accounting, HR, Customer Care, Manufacturing Operations, Global Sourcing, Distribution &amp; Logistics, Engineering, Information Technology, and Sales &amp; Marketing</li>
<li>Develop thorough understanding of the core business processes &amp; system transactions and how master data impacts business activity and performance upstream and downstream</li>
<li>Facilitate discussions and activities with our business partners to help them evolve in their MDM knowledge, skills, adoption and maturity which includes developing best practice master data designs in support of business processes</li>
<li>Create, design, expand and implement process improvements, data standards &amp; definitions, process flows, and documentation</li>
<li>Research, recommend, design and implement improvements to supporting tools and systems which include identify tool gaps, assess software solutions, vendor partnership, sell concepts internally, develop and manage projects</li>
<li>Analyze and audit master data content and quality; identify, recommend and put new solutions in place to improve the quality, accuracy and usability of the data</li>
<li>Keep current of MDM practices outside of the company for trends, tools and best practices; identify gaps with our practices, make recommendations and implement improvements</li>
<li>Drive, create, measure and communicate KPIs and metrics – includes negotiation and tracking service levels, cycle times and data quality levels that ensure best in class data quality</li>
<li>Facilitate an atmosphere of continuous improvement</li>
<li>Hold team and business partners accountable to our data quality targets and processes</li>
<li>Responsible for managing projects</li>
<li>Supervision of junior team members to tasks/projects</li>
</ul>
