<h2>Site Operations Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>View’s mission is to create delightful human environments.</p>
<p>
In the great tradition of challenging the way we think about everyday essentials, the team at View has reinvented something that hasn't changed for centuries – the simple window – and in doing so, is transforming the largest industry in the world: real estate.</p>
<p>
We manufacture View Dynamic Glass, a new generation of smart windows that let in natural light and views, and enhance mental and physical wellbeing by significantly reducing headaches, eyestrain and drowsiness. In addition, View’s windows reduce glare and heat, improving the energy efficiency of buildings by 20 percent. View’s windows are digital, cloud connected, and can be controlled from anywhere, including your phone. We are also leveraging advances in AI to develop smart building and IoT applications that make buildings healthier, smarter and more productive than anyone ever imagined.</p>
<p>
View is already installed in over 50 million square feet and is growing rapidly. If you dream of changing the world to make it a better place – join us.</p>
<p>
View is looking for an experienced and talented Site Operations Engineer who will provide service and support for the commissioning and operation of View Dynamic Glass control systems. You should be comfortable designing, deploying and sustaining processes required to operate enterprise class systems &amp; services. You should be able to create and leverage tools and release automation so that deployments are painless and require minimal intervention. You should enjoy collaboration with developers to refine build, test, and release procedures across the organization.</p>
<p>
The ideal candidate has a strong technical acumen coupled with excellent customer service, enabling 100% occupant delight. You are a creative problem solver, comfortable with providing subject matter expertise and rapidly responding to escalated issues.</p>
<p>
The Site Operations Engineer will be an individual contributor. This individual will work closely with leasing agents and building occupants, as well as Field Operations, Engineering, Sales, Marketing, IT to ensure system uptime, feature capability, and continuous improvement of the View Dynamic Glass control system. We seek a very personable, self-directed, self-motivated professional who is passionate and enjoys the challenges of working in a dynamic, onsite environment.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Essential position responsibilities:</b><br>
<b>
</b><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Ensure safety of all team members and customers at all times</li>
<li>
Production issue remediation onsite at specific installation, and/or on new &amp; existing View Dynamic Glass installations as appropriate, ensuring systems continue to meet building occupancy requirements</li>
<li>
Maintain and constantly improve continuous integration capabilities</li>
<li>
Responsible for programming of control systems and commissioning of control systems relating to building intelligence &amp; automation</li>
<li>
Strong ability to troubleshoot systems, interpreting both user feedback and system data, leveraging deep knowledge of system to provide necessary corrective action</li>
<li>
Strong ability to understand technical material from engineers and document them in the appropriate format: SOP, Reference, Process, template, or any other format as required</li>
<li>
Build a culture of customer satisfaction based on operational excellence, big picture understanding, continuous improvement, and proactive thinking</li>
<li>
Work closely with other departments to assure that the Voice of Operations is appropriately represented in product strategy and implementation strategy</li>
<li>
Provide detailed documentation of the services performed using internal systems and tools; ensure all pertinent details of the service call are recorded for comprehensive post-analysis</li>
<li>
Provide detailed documentation of identified anomalies or defects using internal systems and tools; ability to assist engineering team in assessing said anomalies.</li>
<li>
Routine professional communication directly with field, engineering, sales, internal, customer, and 3rd-party personnel, up to and including executive leadership, in order to satisfy the specific needs of what is required to solve the issue on a particular site</li>
<li>
Incorporate feedback for continuous improvement</li>
</ul>
