<div><b>Video Specialist</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Position Description:</b></div>
<div>
Stearns Lending is looking for an experienced and creative Video Specialist to join an internal video production department. As a Video Specialist, you will be responsible for capturing, editing, and producing photo and video content for internal and external usage.</div>
<div>
<b>
Responsibilities</b><b>:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>The Video Specialist supports the sales team by executing on the video marketing strategy through special events, advertising, and local brand awareness.</li>
<li>
Produce quality content in accordance with marketing strategy with high production value.</li>
<li>
Capture content onsite and in the field and around the world.</li>
<li>
Camera operation and editing.</li>
<li>
Work very closely with the Director of Content Production to execute the creative and project vision and objectives.</li>
<li>
Regularly collaborate with the marketing team.</li>
<li>
Assist in the development of the video brand messaging strategy.</li>
<li>
Maintenance of Camera equipment.</li>
</ul>
<ul><li> Support the marketing team in the development of sizzle videos and graphics supporting home loans, integrated marketing campaigns and sales training/support.</li></ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
 <b>Skills &amp; Experience</b>:
</div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree preferred, preferably in film or visual communications.</li>
<li>
1+ years of recent Video Specialist or similar role experience. Film production camera department experience a plus. Knowledge and good understanding of motion graphics is preferred.</li>
<li>
Technically proficient in Adobe After Effects, Adobe premiere.</li>
<li>
Familiarity with production equipment.</li>
<li>
Creative thinker and problem solver, highly organized, and comfortable with a dynamic and fast-paced environment.
</li>
<li>Manage multiple projects, juggle priorities and confident coordinate with multiple team members to see projects through to completion.</li>
</ul>
