<p><b>About the team</b></p>
<p>As a Cloud DevOps Engineer, you will significantly contribute to identifying best-fit architectural solutions for one or more projects; develop design of solutions, provide regular support/guidance to project teams on complex cloud technologies, issue resolution and execution.</p>
<p>You will collaborate with and lead some of the best talent in the region to create and implement innovative high-quality solutions, participate in Sales and various pursuits focused on our clients' business needs.</p>
<p><b>How you will be successful</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Design, Develop and Implement cloud solutions on AWS / Azure</li>
<li>Accountable for the quality development of enabling automation, application development, infrastructure development and overall IT service improvement</li>
<li>Manage CI CD tools and pipeline as a partner to development teams</li>
<li>Assess, deploy, and manage numerous infrastructure management tools including infrastructure optimization and orchestration solutions</li>
<li>Build integrations with cloud monitoring / alerting systems and processes</li>
<li>Produce technical specifications for custom development and systems integration requirements</li>
<li>Take a lead, design, develop and implement Proof-of-Concepts for automation and CICD technologies to support the cloud infrastructure including server, storage, network, and security.</li>
<li>Providing guidance and direction in regard to Cloud implementation, DevOps processes and technical pipeline standards</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What Experience you will need</b></p>

<li>At least 3 years of experience with DevOps in Cloud Technologies.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Preferred: </b></p>

<li>Experience with programming and infrastructure automation and Infrastructure as Code (IaC) (e.g., Terraform, Cloud Formation, Azure Resource Templates)</li>
<li>Experience with automation in dynamic environments such as IaaS and PaaS (on premises, hosted and hybrid)</li>
<li>Strong Experience with CICD tools</li>
<li>Experience with container-based applications and tools (e.g. Docker, Kubernetes)</li>
<li>Experience with configuration management tools (e.g Ansible, Chef)</li>
