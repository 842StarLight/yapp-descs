<h2>Marketing Rep</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div><div><div>
<p><b>MAJOR RESPONSIBILITIES:
</b></p>
<p>The Outside Marketing Representative will be responsible for a designated territory and managing all aspects of the independent insurance agent/broker relationship by providing best in class service with an emphasis on meeting or exceeding state, territory and account level business plans.</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><b>Territory Financial Results</b></li>
<ul>
<li>Meet or exceed expected revenue and profitability goals in assigned territory.</li>
<li>Use Agency Management software to determine plan vs. actual revenue and profitability.</li>
</ul>
<li>
<b>Agent quality</b>
<ul>
<li>Work with producers ensuring they have acceptable loss ratios and claim frequencies.</li>
<li>Manage agent NSF and delinquent accounts.</li>
<li>Manage audit results.</li>
<li>Manage quality assurance and discount management from producers.</li>
<li>Rehabilitate producers and increase user acceptance.</li>
<li>Grow and graduate producers to the next tier.</li>
<li>Measure, communicate and promote all incentives and promotions.</li>
<li>Manage out producers not meeting performance expectations.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<b>Product and system knowledge</b>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrate a complete understanding of Multi-State Insurance products and systems.</li>
<li>Fully understand Multi-State Insurance’s products strengths and weaknesses to help in overcoming objections.</li>
<li>Add value to Multi-State Insurance systems and products with new ideas and by bringing awareness to what competitors provide.</li>
<li>Demonstrate a complete understanding of Multi-State Insurance products and systems.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<b>Territory Knowledge</b>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrate market awareness by knowing key competitors, their products, their strengths, weaknesses and regulatory issues that may impact production.</li>
<li>Add to and keep the Feature and Benefit analysis current (i.e. a document detailing features of Multi-State and competitors in the state).</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<b>New appointments</b>
<ul>
<li>Continuously source and recommend for appointment. Aggressively appoint potential Tier I and Tier II producers who will add to the overall growth and profitability of Multi-State Insurance.</li>
<li>Work with In-house training to on-board new agents and ensure alignment with Multi-State’s expected quality and processes.</li>
<li>Concentrate on getting new producers production ramping upward as quickly as possible.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><b>Agency Management</b></li>
<ul>
<li>Accurately record calls, visits and other activity.</li>
<li>Consistently log in to use reports helping route visits and increase production out of the designated territory.</li>
<li>Maintain current critical agency data as required, validating and updating at least quarterly.</li>
<li>Maintain acceptable levels of agency contacts as compared to with peers understanding that seasonality and system issues impact all reps and all states equally.</li>
<li>Work to improve constant contact bounce rate to be less than 5% with Tier I and Tier II producers.</li>
</ul>
</ul>
</div></div></div>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Requirements</b></h2>
<p><b>QUALIFICATION REQUIREMENTS:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Outgoing personality with excellent interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to bond with agency/sales leadership from top to bottom</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to overcome objections</li>
<li>The ability to successfully train on products and systems</li>
</ul>
<p><b>EDUCATION and/or EXPERIENCE:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Sales and Customer Relationship Management experience preferred</li>
<li>Geographical Territory Management experience preferred</li>
<li>1-2 years industry experience a plus</li>
<li>High School Diploma or GED required; Some college preferred</li>
<li>P&amp;C license in the state which you work a plus but not required</li>
</ul>
<p><b>LANGUAGE SKILLS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bilingual English/Spanish capabilities a plus but not required</li>
<li>Excellent oral and written communication skills</li>
</ul>
<p><b>MATHEMATICAL SKILLS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to calculate figures and amounts such as proportions and percentages</li>
</ul>
<p><b>REASONING ABILITY:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to define problems, collect data, establish facts and draw valid conclusions</li>
<li>Ability to interpret an extensive variety of technical information</li>
</ul>
<p><b>OTHER KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS and ABILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Proficiency with Microsoft Office Suite with specific knowledge of Excel, Word and Outlook</li>
<li>Ability to maintain the highest level of discretion, diplomacy and confidentiality</li>
<li>The ability to work Saturdays is required</li>
<li><i>The ability to travel without aid within the designated territory</i></li>
</ul>
