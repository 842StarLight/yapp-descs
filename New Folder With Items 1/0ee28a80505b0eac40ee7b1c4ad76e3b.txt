<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Summary
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p><b>Position summary:</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<div>
The Principal IT Information Security Analyst will be responsible for supporting the development, enforcement, maintenance and compliance of security program within the Nuance Enterprise business unit. This is an autonomous role where the successful candidate will take ownership of supporting relevant Enterprise compliance programs, policy and procedure development/management/compliance, pre-sales, legal collaboration and audit management.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Principal duties and responsibilities:</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Lead efforts in industry standards and regulatory compliance such as ISO 27001/27002, PCI, and others</li>
<li>A good understanding of the Data Protection and its implications for business. Able to provide appropriate advice and practical support for current and emerging data protection requirements</li>
<li>Participate in the broader Information Security governance process with Nuance Corporate and Divisional Security leaders, as required</li>
<li>Lead and support internal/external audits, facilitate customer-driven and Third-party security audits/assessments</li>
<li>Provide pre-sales support for Enterprise products and services, as needed</li>
<li>Develop remediation plans in partnership with Legal, IT, Operations and other relevant groups</li>
<li>Perform tasks related to securing and keeping the products, tools, and processes that you are responsible for securing</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<b>
Knowledge, skills and qualifications </b>–</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<b>
Education: </b>4 Year / Bachelors Degree; Computer Science, Management Information Systems, Information Technology or a related discipline.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<b>
Minimum years of work experience: </b>8 years</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Required skills:<br>
</b></div>
<div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>8-12 years of related experience.</li>
<li>Knowledge and experience specific to managing and reporting internal controls</li>
<li>Must be knowledgeable about ISO standards and PCI requirements, prior work experience in a PCI-compliant environment highly desirable</li>
<li>Prior policy development and enforcement experience in a regulated environment</li>
<li>Ability to translate business requirements and risks into policy and technology implementation</li>
<li>Ability to understand technical implementations with proven technical experience</li>
<li>Networking and infrastructure knowledge</li>
</ul>
<br>
</div>

Preferred skills:<br>
</b></div>
<div>
<br>

<li>Excellent oral and written communications skills with the ability to prepare and deliver concise, understandable reports and presentations</li>
<li>CISSP, CISM and/or CISA (or equivalent)</li>
</ul>
