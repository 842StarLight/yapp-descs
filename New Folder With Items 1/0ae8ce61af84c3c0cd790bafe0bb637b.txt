<p></p>
<p><b>Mortgage Loan Originators</b> are responsible for soliciting and procuring new residential mortgage business, maintaining contact with their existing customer database, developing a reliable network of referral sources, and assisting the customer and the Underwriting, Processing, and Closing departments of the Company through each loan transaction. Our Mortgage Loan Originators help their clients choose the best loan for their needs by being knowledgeable about all our product offerings, as well as having a working understanding of underwriting guidelines. They are licensed by state and federal authorities and keep this accreditation through continuing education.</p>
<br>
<p><br>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities include the following. Other duties may be assigned.</p>
<ul>
<li>Solicit new and former mortgage customers for referrals and loans (“Borrowers”).</li>
<li>Originate loans in accordance with Company policies and procedures.</li>
<li>Advise customers on product options.</li>
<li>Assist funding department staff by obtaining complete, accurate documents from borrowers.</li>
<li>Lock loan rates and schedule loan closings.</li>
<li>Perform any other or additional duties that are assigned by GSF from time to time or that are contained in the Loan Originator Employment and Compensation Agreement.<br>
</li>
<li>Assist with any post-closing information or documentation required for loan sale and/or servicing.</li>
<li>Mastery of all current applicable software products, including but not limited to loan operating systems (LOS) for forward and reverse mortgages, CRM, and current pricing engine; detailed knowledge of GSF loan products; thorough knowledge of policies and procedures as they relate to marketing, procuring and closing mortgage loans.</li>
<li>Maintain positive contribution to branch revenue stream by meeting minimum production standard as established in their latest signed Compensation Agreement.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Certificates, Licenses, Registrations Required:</b><br>
Loan Originator license through NMLS.</p>
