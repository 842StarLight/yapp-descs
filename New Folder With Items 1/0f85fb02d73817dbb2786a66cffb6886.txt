<h2>Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>OSM Worldwide is a leading provider of domestic and international package shipping services that reach millions of addresses nationwide and around the globe. With today's consumer ordering product online there is a large market for business to consumer shipping. Because of this we are growing fast!</p>
<p>
Our Account Executives are primarily responsible for selling package delivery solutions to businesses, industrial establishments, E-retailers, mail service providers, associations, financial institutions, etc. We are a highly successful and growing company and this is truly a "hunter" sales environment with the ability to obtain significant financial reward. We offer a competitive base and lucrative commission program. This position is home-based with regular travel throughout the West Coast area to visit with potential customers. The ideal candidate will reside within the greater Los Angeles area. Compensation includes a competitive base and commission schedule along with an auto allowance and other reimbursable expenses.</p>
<p><b>
Job Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Consistently identify new sources of business while achieving sales quotas within an assigned territory.</li>
<li>
Facilitate meetings with current clients and prospects in order to analyze needs and present applicable solutions.</li>
<li>
Overcome technical and business objections of prospective customers.</li>
<li>
Meet/exceed corporate objectives/goals for product and service sales on a quarterly basis.</li>
<li>
Oversee client implementation.</li>
<li>
Provide product training to customers.</li>
<li>
Research and analyze prospective customers in order to identify appropriate prospects.</li>
<li>
Actively participate with industry organizations and trade shows in order to network with potential clients and interact with current clients.</li>
<li>
This position has account management responsibilities to all current clients in the assigned area of responsibility. Applicant must be able to handle inbound calls that are related to existing accounts and provide solutions to client inquiries.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
4 year college degree preferred or an equivalent combination of education and experience.</li>
<li>
At least five years of industry specific experience (parcel shipping, mail consolidation, logistics).</li>
<li>
At least seven years of previous outside sales experience.</li>
<li>
Thorough understanding of marketing and negotiating techniques.</li>
<li>
Hands-on experience with CRM software (Sales Force) is a plus.</li>
<li>
Ability to work in a team environment in high pressure situations.</li>
<li>
Ability for overnight travel as business needs dictate.</li>
</ul>
