<h2>Product Category Manager Packaging (BPD Riverside, MO)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Product Category Manager for Packaging will include overall management and success of our packaging materials product lines. Packaging materials include those materials used in food processing applications and controlled atmosphere packaging machines. This person will work with manufacturers to maximize product profitability throughout its lifecycle. The objective of the Product Category manager is to plan and specify a product portfolio in line with the long term strategic plan, and within the scope and capabilities of the company.</p>

<p><b><i>Responsibilities:</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Work to develop "Best Value" product opportunities that include best performance, customer acceptance, and continuous improvement for the users</li>
<li>Monitor market needs, competitive products, and the changing production schedules of approved manufacturers to improve competitive positioning</li>
<li>Provide sales support and product training material to field sales, and inside sales staff</li>
<li>Develop product pricing policies and negotiate best cost vendor programs to improve payment terms, and improve margin</li>
<li>Rationalize and consolidate product lines to improve inventory turns and create volume cost improvement opportunities</li>
<li>Support sales staff with special product requirements and prices. Work to improve go-to-market strategy and speed of new product implementation</li>
<li>Support sales staff by problem solving and recommending new and different packaging materials as they become available</li>
<li>Negotiate cost and terms of sale with all packaging machinery suppliers participating in our programs. Machinery includes stretch wrapping, taping, bagging, banding, lidding, and vacuum packaging</li>
</ul>
<p><i>Requirements:</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree required</li>
<li>5+ years experience in purchasing or relevant business experience is required; preferably in a distribution environment</li>
<li>Must have proven experience negotiating with vendors</li>
<li>Working knowledge of the raw materials used in food processing is preferred; also an existing working relationship with packaging material manufacturers is preferred</li>
<li>A thorough understanding of basic film structures and performance is preferred</li>
<li>An Understanding of packaging machines and their film requirements is preferred, as well as experience with designing custom packages for most food packaging applications</li>
<li>Must possess strong planning, organizational and time management skills as well as strong analytical problem solving skills</li>
<li>Must be able to multi-task with the ability to change direction quickly and often</li>
<li>Must be proficient in Microsoft Office (Excel, Word, PowerPoint, Outlook, etc.)</li>
<li>Enterprise purchasing system experience is highly preferred</li>
</ul>
