<h2>Leasing Agent</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Thursday-Saturday 8 AM- 5 PM</p>
<p>
Albany, Oregon</p>
<p>
Customer Service Experience Required</p>
<p>
Leasing:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Greet prospects and qualify by covering all criteria (ask questions; utilize guest cards etc.)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Immediately record all telephone and in-person visits on appropriate reports.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>File your own guest cards and maintains them according to established company procedures.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Inspects models and available “market ready” units. Communicate maintenance/cleaning needs to Property Manager.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Tours community and apartment/model while applying product knowledge to clients’ needs by communicating the features and benefits: CLOSE THE SALE!</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Have prospects complete application and secure deposit in accordance with the company procedures and Fair Housing requirements</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Update vacancy sheet, process application for approvals. (i.e., credit check, rental history, etc.) Submit processed application to the Property Manager for approval. Follow up with the applicant regarding status.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Ensure apartment is ready for resident to move-in on agreed date.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Immediately follow-up on prospects that did not close and attempt to close sale again. If unable to help prospect, refer them to our sister communities and try to meet prospects needs.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Secure new resident’s signatures on appropriate paperwork and receive all monies owed prior to move-in. Establish new residents to the community by touring their unit, the property and amenities.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Assist in monitoring renewals. Distribute and follow-up on renewal notices and phone calls.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Monitor advertising effectiveness. Gather information about market competition in the area and share with Property Manager</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Shop one competitive properties every month.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Represent the company in a professional manner at all times.</p>
<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p>Administrative:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Accept rental payments and put in proper rent collection bag for Property Manager to enter into Appfolio.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Type leases, lease renewals, complete appropriate paperwork and input information in Appfolio accurately and in a timely manner. Review with the Property Manager prior to obtaining signatures.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Maintain current resident files.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Maintain and record daily inspections for the community.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Distribute all company or community issued notices.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Maintain accurate monthly commission records on lease renewals for bonus purposes.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Assist management team with other various tasks as required</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Consistently implement policies of the community.</p>
<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p>Resident Retention</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Receive all telephone calls and in-person visits. Listen to resident requests, concerns and comments, respond appropriately or pass on to Management as needed.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Quickly complete maintenance Service Request and inform the maintenance team by entering into Appfolio and printing request. Answer questions for residents about community, repairs, rent, rules, etc. Follow up on a timely basis if unable to respond to residents on all matters.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Ensure all maintenance repairs are handled satisfactorily by contacting residents with completed Service Requests on a daily basis.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Maintain open communication with Property Manager, Assistant Manager and Maintenance Supervisor.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Contribute to cleanliness and curb appeal of the community on continuing basis.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Assist in planning resident functions. Attend functions and participate as host for any functions as directed by the Property Manager.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Neighborhood Marketing</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Advise residents of referral concessions (if permitted).</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Assist in placing, removing/updating banners, balloons, bandit signs, flags, etc.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Distribute newsletters, pamphlets, flyers, etc.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Conduct market surveys and shop competitive communities.</p>
<br>
</li>
</ul>
