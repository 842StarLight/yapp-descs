<h2>Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<ul>
<li>Meet and exceed monthly, quarterly, and annual budgets including spot, digital, and new direct business. Prospect new businesses on a weekly basis.</li>
<li>Prepare and complete sales presentations with decision makers.</li>
<li>Make presentations to include a customer needs analysis, research and supporting documentation, and creative business solutions.</li>
<li>Follow up and close all pending business in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Update Sales Manager weekly on progress of pending business.</li>
<li>Work closely with other departments in creating effective promotional sales solutions for clients.</li>
<li>Create and make presentations to agency personnel, including selling and pre-selling the station audience attributes.</li>
<li>Provide exceptional customer service to direct and agency accounts. Personally contact each active account regularly- defined as an in-person meeting, including the presentation of pertinent sales information.</li>
<li>Obtain credit approval for new accounts.</li>
<li>Ensure all accounts are paid in a timely manner. Follow up and assist in the collection process for past due accounts.</li>
<li>Utilization of and proficiency with Sales software.</li>
<li>Attend special events, such as remotes and broadcasts, which involve clients. These events may occur after business hours and on weekends.</li>
<li>Complete all written business requirements within established deadlines. These requirements include but are not limited to: forecast reports, pending business updates, written orders and advertising copy.</li>
<li>Attend and participate in weekly sales staff meetings, individual meetings with manager and other necessary meetings and training as required by management.</li>
<li>Have a thorough understanding of rates, promotions, inventory, and personnel. Continue to learn and stay abreast of trends in digital and broadcast advertising industry.</li>
<li>Maintain regular, reliable attendance.</li>
<li>Work cooperatively and collaboratively with others. Build working relationships with co-workers, customers, vendors and the general public.</li>
<li>Complete other duties as requested and needed.</li>
<li>Previous sales, advertising, digital sales, or media experience preferred.</li>
<li>Knowledge of, and familiarity with, (or ability to learn) broadcasting and digital terminology and methodology preferred.</li>
<li>Ability to understand the features and benefits of advertising and competitive media.</li>
<li>Familiarity with Microsoft Word for Windows; sales software knowledge helpful.</li>
<li>Excellent written and oral English language communication skills.</li>
<li>Experience developing presentations for the purpose of direct-business selling and ability to effectively present and communicate.</li>
<li>Ability to respond to questions appropriately and think on feet.</li>
<li>Establish and maintain customer relationships; communicate effectively with a wide range of personalities in a professional and courteous manner.</li>
<li>High level of initiative and ability to work independently required.</li>
<li>Must have reliable transportation, a valid driver's license and a safe driving history as determined by the Company for the purpose of meeting clients and attending events.</li>
<li>Demonstrate effective negotiation and closing techniques.</li>
<li>Strong analytical skills, good judgment, and a "positive can do attitude" are required.</li>
<li>Ability to read, hear and speak clearly and follow both oral and written direction.</li>
<li>Ability to think critically, analytically, creatively and logically; and to articulate information in clear, concise manner to others.</li>
<li>Ability to work evenings and weekends.</li>
<li>Physical Requirements: Manual dexterity and fine motor skills to manipulate computer keys and general office equipment (telephone, copier, etc.). Sit and/or stand for extended periods of time. Average pushing and pulling, bending and stooping.The Company may make reasonable accommodations to facilitate the ability to perform essential job function.</li>
</ul>
