<h2>Sales Engineer (Spanish)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>At ServiceSource, we strive to find and grow exceptional people like you.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Working at ServiceSource, you will be a part of an engaging, energetic, and entrepreneurial work environment while having the stability and influence of a nearly 20-year industry leader behind you.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>ServiceSource is a place where you can GROW, both personally and professionally, while expanding your skills in sales, customer success and people management.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The Sales Engineer position involves technical consulting for sales opportunities across a broad portfolio of products, channels, segments and regions for the Google Cloud Team. By combining technical expertise and sales skills, the Sales Engineer helps customers evaluate and adopt Google Cloud products to create business value.
</p>
<p>Ideal candidates thrive in a fast-paced, goal-oriented environment, strive to hit and exceed sales targets with a positive attitude and are motivated by closing deals and generating their pipeline.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>The Position:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Collaborate with inside sales SMB team to explore opportunities from the technical perspective, and collaboratively qualify the prospect.
</li>
<li>Take responsibility for the technical aspects of the sales cycle including general teaching, introductory meetings, supporting bid responses, product and solution briefings, proof-of-concept work, and the coordination of additional supporting technical resources. Identify and handle key customer technical objections, prescriptively guiding them to success.
</li>
<li>Work hands-on with Google Cloud products to demonstrate integrations in customer environments.
</li>
<li>Prepare and deliver product messaging to highlight Google Cloud value proposition using techniques to include slide presentations, product demonstrations, white papers, and documents.
</li>
<li>Make recommendations on integration strategies, enterprise architectures, platforms, and application infrastructure required to successfully implement a complete solution providing best practice advice to customers to optimize Google Cloud effectiveness.
</li>
<li>Articulate solutions and value into meaningful conversations with targeted prospects.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>What you should have:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>In-depth understanding of the technology and cloud computing market, and a passion for Google Cloud products (G-Suite, Google Cloud Platform).
</li>
<li>BA/BS/CS degree or equivalent practical experience.
</li>
<li>Understanding of solution architecture within web and mobile environments and technical experience of web/internet related technologies, architecture across SAAS, PAAS and IAAS and competitive cloud productivity suites.
</li>
<li>Understanding of data and information management and hands-on experience using and managing databases.
</li>
<li>Working knowledge of Linux, Web 2.0 development platforms, solutions, and related technologies (HTTP, Basic/NTLM, sessions, XML/XSLT/XHTML/HTML), understanding of DNS, TCP, Firewalls, Proxy Servers, DMZ, Load Balancing, VPN, VPC).
</li>
<li>Working knowledge of cloud computing environments.
</li>
<li>Web application development and integration a major plus. Software development platforms and solutions to include J2EE, Java Servlets, JavaScript, Python, GO, PHP, ASP, CGI, Ajax.</li>
</ul>
</div>
