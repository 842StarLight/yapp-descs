<h2>Market Development Manager - Massachusetts, Rhode Island, Connecticut, Vermont, New Hampshire, Maine</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Job Description
<br><br>
The Market Development Manager (MDM) is responsible for achieving sales plan by growing the Independent Business through New Business.
<br><br>
<b>Key Job Responsibilities are as follows:</b><br>
Driving sales and growth for a specific geography by Prospecting/Hunting new and competitive prospects
<br>Gain Commitment of Single- Multi Stores from competitive banner programs
<br>Assist customer store changeover process with the Independent Director of sales
<br>Assist the Independent Director of sales with Succession Planning
<br>Identifying potential new entrepreneurs and gaining commitment from them to join the CARQUEST Store program
<br>Assist with new store growth opportunities
<br><br>
<b>Key Skills Sets:</b><br>
Strong ability to build relationships and a high level of trust with the customer
<br>Bachelor’s Degree in Marketing, Communications or related field
<br>10+ years’ B2B sales experience
<br>4+ years in leadership sales position
<br>Track record of superior performance metrics
<br>Excellent negotiation skills
<br>Strong decision-making abilities
<br>Able to travel up to two weeks per month
<br>Familiarity with CRM software
<br><br>
<b>Territory:</b><br>
Boston, MA
<br>Travel to Massachusetts, Rhode Island, Connecticut, Vermont, New Hampshire, Maine
