<p><b>
The Role:</b></p>
<p>
The Simon-Kucher Data Science team is a group of data scientists who develop best-in-class approaches and models to solve complex business problems using Machine Learning and Advanced Analytics.</p>
<p>
As a Data Scientist at Simon-Kucher &amp; Partners, you will be an integral part of the project teams working to drive top-line growth for our clients. You will be responsible for the core components of the model development process, from data wrangling / pre-processing to Machine Learning model development, testing, and implementation. Throughout this process, you will also gather and communicate meaningful data insights to your project team.</p>
<p>
In addition, you will keep an eye on the fast-changing market of advanced analytics and help further develop Simon-Kucher’s data science offerings. Your continued involvement and efforts will enable Simon-Kucher &amp; Partners to become a thought-leader on the applications of Machine Learning and AI to pricing, sales and marketing strategies.</p>
<p><b>
Key Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Data wrangling, cleaning, and pre-processing</li>
<li>
Conducting exploratory data analysis and communicating insights through clear descriptions and visualizations</li>
<li>
Developing, testing, and implementing Machine Learning models</li>
<li>
Conducting research on recent developments in machine learning and AI, with a focus on topics related to pricing, sales, and marketing</li>
<li>
Being a topic expert on Machine Learning and AI for your project team, enabling strong project planning and team performance</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
<b>
Basic Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Degree in a quantitative field, such as computer science, engineering, statistics, operations research, data science, or equivalent experience</li>
<li>
Experience with Machine Learning and statistical modelling techniques</li>
<li>
Strong programming skills in R and/or Python</li>
<li>
Experience applying advanced analytics and Machine Learning to solve business problems</li>
<li>
Experience with data visualization software / libraries (Tableau, PowerBI, Matplotlib)</li>
<li>
Comfort working both on a team and autonomously</li>
<li>
Strong written and verbal communication skills, ability to simply and concisely explain complex analytical topics</li>
<li>
Entrepreneurial spirit—we are a fast-growing team with vast opportunities for growth</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Preferred Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>

Implementation experience with Machine Learning models and applications</li>
<li>
Knowledge of cloud-based Machine Learning engines (AWS, Azure, Google, etc.)</li>
<li>
Experience with large scale data processing tools (Spark, Hadoop, etc.)</li>
<li>
Ability to query and program databases (SQL, No SQL)</li>
<li>
Experience with distributed ML frameworks (TensorFlow, PyTorch, etc.)</li>
<li>
Familiarity with collaborative software tools (Git, Jira, etc.)</li>
<li>
Experience with user interface libraries / applications (Shiny, Django, etc.)</li>
<li>
Domain expertise in pricing, demand forecasting, or time-series data</li>
</ul>
