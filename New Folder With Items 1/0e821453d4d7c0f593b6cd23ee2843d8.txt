<h2>Product Manager Skilled Services</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>This role leads the development and support of BrightStar Care’s Skilled services line of business focused on providing skilled home health services for non-Medicare payers. The product manager is responsible for partnering with the clinical, operations and technology teams to define the skilled services business model used by BrightStar’s 330 independently owned and operated agencies to deliver a consistently superior customer experience.<br>
</p>
<p><br>
This position is located at BrightStar’s Corporate Headquarters in Gurnee, Illinois. With easy on-off access from I-94, we are located approximately 60 minutes North of Chicago and 45 minutes South of Milwaukee .
<br>
<br>
RESPONSIBILITIES <b>:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>Work with senior leadership to translate company strategy and objectives into a vision of the BrightStar Care skilled services offering and then serve as the business stakeholder on change initiatives to implement the vision.
</p></li>
<li><p>Establish and maintain key aspects of the model, including clear ownership of business process, best practices, business requirements
</p></li>
<li><p>Define and document key franchisee business processes that incorporate best practices and have input and buy-in from franchisees
</p></li>
<li><p>Simplify, Streamline and Improve the BrightStar Care model; provide the glue that keeps cross-functional teams working effective together to deliver the model consistently to our franchisees to drive business results.
</p></li>
<li><p>Partner with the National Accounts team, Sales Team and Operations to develop programs that are market focused and drive revenue and expanded commercial opportunities
</p></li>
<li><p>Align BrightStar Franchising’s delivery of the model to franchisees with the approved processes in a way that is consistent, cohesive and easy to find, understand and implement
</p></li>
<li><p>Define business process necessary to complete business requirements for technology initiatives in the BTG roadmap so that technology solutions align to process, have franchisee buy in and are suitable for use by BrightStar locations
</p></li>
<li><p>Lead and project manage process improvement initiatives, technology pilots and roll out of future state processes to existing franchisees, with effective change management and measurable metrics to drive adoption and ensure consistency across BrightStar locations
</p></li>
<li><p>Ensure team focus and successful delivery of key projects strategic priorities
</p></li>
<li><p>Represent BrightStar externally within the home health/post-acute care industry and keep BrightStar’s strategy aligned with industry trends, opportunities and regulatory changes.
</p></li>
<li><p>Curriculum owner and subject matter expert for training tools, Start Team checklists and is the backup instructor for Boot Camp that includes content and technology components;<br>
<br>
</p></li>
</ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>POSITION REQUIREMENTS:
</b></h2>
<ul>
<li><p>3-7 years experience in Home Healthcare or Post-Acute Healthcare industry
</p></li>
<li><p>Ability to build strong relationships with key stakeholders
</p></li>
<li><p>Outstanding communication, presentation and leadership skills to communicate with stakeholder communities, inform key decision makers and influence the user community to drive change
</p></li>
<li><p>Understanding of software development methodology with previous experience working with agile development teams
</p></li>
<li><p>Experience with process definition, process improvement and reengineering, including formal training in LEAN, SixSigma or similar methodology.
</p></li>
<li><p>Bachelor’s degree in business, technology or healthcare management;<br>
</p></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
