<h2>Materials & Process Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job description</b></h2>
<div>
<p>Candidate will provide materials and process (M&amp;P) definition, qualification, and support the design, manufacture, and continuing operational safety of pilot controls and actuation systems. This may include opportunities in mechanical testing, metallurgical analysis, failure analysis, and design review.</p>
<p>
Candidate will also work with design and supply chain partners to achieve business objectives and ensure safety as it relates to assigned hardware.</p>
<p>
Primary Responsibilities:<br>
</p>
<div><ul>
<li>Review and approve all mechanical drawing notes for material and process compatibility and adherence to aerospace certification standards.</li>
<li>Work closely with subcontractors and suppliers to ensure acceptable processes are employed.</li>
<li>Provide M&amp;P technical support to design engineering, stress analysis team, customer, and other necessary functions</li>
<li>Test and evaluate materials and fasteners in relation to design, manufacturing, and safety of the aircraft</li>
<li>Perform failure analysis to support engineering and production activities</li>
<li>Coordinate cross functionally to develop, revise, and maintain engineering processes and specifications</li>
<li>Review and write material and process specifications</li>
<li>Monitor material performance and evaluate material deterioration to ensure required specifications are met</li>
<li>Design, develop, and test metallic and non-metallic structures or materials and their related fabrication processes for use in engineering designs</li>
<li>Perform material analyses that include, but are not limited to, failure analysis of components, systems and subsystems, and requirement specifications</li>
<li>May review and approve subcontractor material processing procedures</li>
</ul></div>
<p></p>
</div>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job requirements</b></h2>
<div>
<p>Qualifications:<br>
</p>
<div><ul>
<li>BS or higher in Engineering or a related technical field plus 8 or more years of related experience.</li>
<li>US Citizen or Permanent Resident, required</li>
<li>Subject Matter Expert (SME) in a materials field, for example metals, polymers, adhesives, lubricants, composites, etc.</li>
<li>Historical aerospace industry experience that will allow for further development of approved materials and processes list.</li>
<li>Experience in material selection and qualification of metals, polymers, adhesives, composites, including a working knowledge of aerospace material specifications</li>
</ul></div>
