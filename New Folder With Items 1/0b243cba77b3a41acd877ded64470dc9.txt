<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Description
</b></h2>
<div>
<b>SUMMARY: </b>The General Sales Manaer assumes responsibility for customer retention and profitability in the new- and used-vehicle profit centers. Fulfills responsibilities primarily through effective personnel management, knowledge of market potential, established performance standards, and a keen awareness of each department's break-even data.
<br>
<b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES:
</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Hires, trains, supervises, and monitors the performance of the new and used vehicle department managers.
</li>
<li>Participates in the preparation of the annual dealership forecast by projecting unit sales, gross profits, expenses, and operating profits for the new and used sales departments.
</li>
<li>Meets with sales managers (new and used) to establish annual and monthly objectives for unit sales, gross profits, expenses, and operating profit.
</li>
<li>Ensures that sales managers (new and used) understand dealership policies, procedures, and sales systems.
</li>
<li>Works with the dealer and general manager to determine appropriate days supply for new and used vehicles and orders/acquires vehicle inventory accordingly.
</li>
<li>Establishes standards for displaying, merchandising, and maintaining new and used vehicles.
</li>
<li>Establishes procedures for quick disposal of averaged vehicles.
</li>
<li>Issues all demonstrators and ensures that appropriate dealership records are maintained.
</li>
<li>Checks the condition of all demonstrators monthly.
</li>
<li>Audits repair orders for used vehicles as needed to ensure prompt reconditioning.
</li>
<li>Meets with the general manager to review monthly forecasts, commission sheets, productivity reports, and the profit performance of each department as a whole and each salesperson individually.
</li>
<li>Studies local market conditions, demographics, and past sales history.
</li>
<li>Makes recommendations to the general manager regarding short and long-range advertising plans, sales promotions, staffing needs, lease promotions, and compensation plans.
</li>
<li>Conducts major sales promotions and advertising as needed (i.e., used car classifieds, new display ads, radio, etc.). Â·
</li>
<li>Approves all sales incentives in writing before submitting to the office.
</li>
<li>Addresses customer complaints to ensure a high level of customer satisfaction.
</li>
<li>Creates systems that ensure ongoing sales training, including weekly sales meetings.
</li>
<li>Audits all appraisals of trade-in vehicles.
</li>
<li>Attends auction at least once monthly.
</li>
<li>Reviews and initials all deals before they are posted.
</li>
<li>Facilitates new vehicle pre delivery with the service manager.
</li>
<li>Makes sure facility is secure, well lit, and professional in appearance.
</li>
<li>Attends managers meetings as requested.
</li>
</ul>
<b>QUALIFICATIONS:
</b><br>
<ul>
<li>6+ years in the automotive industry with increasing responsibility in a high volume brand
</li>
<li>Must have previous Sales Management experience in a dealership, F&amp;I Manager and/or Used Car Sales Management experience is a plus
</li>
<li>High energy and positive work ethic
</li>
<li>Ability to train and motivate
</li>
<li>Must be able to manage a large sales staff with big inventories
</li>
<li>Must have strong computer skills and knowledge of how the internet is driving our business
</li>
<li>Needs to maintain a high energy level
</li>
<li>Must be able to lead by example
</li>
</ul>
