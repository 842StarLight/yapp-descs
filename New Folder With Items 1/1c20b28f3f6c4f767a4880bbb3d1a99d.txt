<h2>Chief Financial Officer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<p>Join one of the fastest growing, exciting and dynamic young companies in the US. Our goal at WALTis to knit the framework for the future of apparel through product design, software innovation, and group collaboration. We consider ourselves big thinkers that are driven <i>by</i> consumer needs.
</p>
<p>WALT Account Executives and the Brand Management team as a whole are responsible for the positioning and ongoing development WALT – the next great company of our generation.</p>
<p>
As the successful applicant you must successfully sell into and develop relationships with customers across multiple channels with varied business processes and organizational objectives.
</p>
<p>The roles and responsibilities will include, but not limited to:</p>
<ul>
<li>Managing a portfolio of accounts</li>
<li>Maintaining and expanding relationships with existing clients</li>
<li>Using an existing network contacts to generate new business</li>
<li>Delivering sales presentations to high-level executives</li>
<li>Achieving sales targets</li>
<li>Managing client communications, conflict resolution, and compliance on client deliverables and revenue</li>
<li>Establishing and expanding key relationships across the customer organizations</li>
<li>Helping Develop the WALT roadmap and strategy that aligns with the customer’s business initiatives to further expand WALT offerings within each account</li>
<li>Working closely with the customer to gain insights into business pains and <i>opportunities</i>.</li>
<li>Working with Marketing Team on brand positioning across various channels
</li>
</ul>
<p>The ideal candidate will have demonstrated the ability to <i>work</i> with senior and executive level management across multiple channels. You will have the ability to build and develop customer relationships in addition to developing strategic, effective and actionable marketing and sales plans.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelors degree or equivalent</li>
<li>
<i>Experience</i> creating and managing relationships with customers</li>
<li>Able to <i>work</i> in a fast-paced, self-directed, entrepreneurial environment</li>
<li>
<i>Excellent</i> verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Able to manage time effectively and stay organized</li>
<li>Able to multi-task in a collaborative environment with shifting priorities</li>
<li>
<i>Experienced</i> and adept at getting people working towards a common goal</li>
<li>Knows how to deliver on promises</li>
<li>Is creative/innovative</li>
<li>Proficient computer skills
</li>
<li>
<i>Experience</i> with CRM software tools</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
This <i>position</i> is <i>located</i> in the heart of Chicago, IL.</p>
