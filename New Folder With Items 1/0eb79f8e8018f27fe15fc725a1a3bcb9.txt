<p><b>The opportunity:</b>
</p>
<p>The Director of Cloud Technical Program Management is responsible for coordinating and driving the development and implementation of system-wide strategic initiatives and processes that achieve tangible organization results for the Cloud Service Delivery (CSD) function. This role is for highly technical program managers who can provide oversight for multiple complex Cloud Service and operational initiatives that require cross-functional team alignment across OpenText.
<br>
<br>
The ideal candidate is a senior technical leader with a strong program management background, a head for business, and a pragmatic, data-driven, strategic approach to problem-solving. She/he will be non-political, with the ability to influence leaders across different business and functional teams and will have demonstrated examples of leadership defining and rolling out successful Cloud Service or Product delivery initiatives and processes. She/he will have proven ability to establish credibility at leadership levels and experience gaining consensus and approval for service / operational planning efforts across multiple functional leaders.<br>
<br>
The role collaborates with other functional project management offices and other corporate leaders including Product, Sales, Marketing, Finance and HR to ensure high performance and to promote the alignment of initiatives toward achievement of strategic results. The Director of Cloud Program Management should then be proficient in motivating and leading others, through vision, strategic positioning, and leadership characteristics.<br>
<br>
The role will work with the Head of PMO, Cloud to prioritize and implement the vision of the organization, being responsible for timely delivery of quality project outputs while performing appropriate scope management. This position requires strong leadership, excellent communication, and “hands-on” program management skills.
<br>
<br>
As OpenText’s Cloud business grows, you will alternate between working with and developing a small project management team and being the single point of contact for projects, taking projects from original concept through final implementation. She/he facilitates the definition of project scope and objectives with a team.</p>
<p><b>You are great at:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Is Accountable for the successful launch and delivery of new, key OpenText Cloud initiatives (e.g., expanding and scaling the OT Cloud footprint, including new capabilities and certifications; launching new Cloud-ready service offerings, etc.)</li>
<li>Helps create, populate, automate and manage metrics dashboards on an on-going basis, identifying and following through data-driven business and operational improvement opportunities
</li>
<li>Manages CSD’s collaboration and on-going operational and business dependencies with Product, HR, Finance and other key areas as may be needed by the business, becoming the de facto CSD point of contact for those areas</li>
<li>Provides insightful analyses on the efficiency, quality and economics of CSD’s operation based on data, identifying data gaps and working with stakeholders to fill those gaps</li>
<li>Develops detailed work plans, schedules, project estimates, resource plans, budgets and status reports</li>
<li>Recommends/takes action to direct the analysis of and solution to problems</li>
<li>Provides visibility to team impediments/risks and helps eliminate them</li>
<li>Leads small team of project managers / analysts (1-2)</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What it takes:
</b></p>
<div>10+ years of industry experience for a global, high-tech environment, with at least 5 years in a management or program management position<br>
Proven track record of successful project/program management of mid to large size projects<br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Results-oriented, project management of several concurrent projects for enterprise software and technology industries</li>
<li>Passionate about winning in the Cloud business / industry</li>
<li>Ability to work across multiple silos within the organization and lead cross functional teams to successful outcomes, including an ability to address complex situations into actionable plans</li>
<li>Strong ability to interpret data quickly and communicate to executive / business sponsors</li>
<li>Flexible and adaptable to time sensitive situations and work in a high-pressure environment</li>
<li>Strong business acumen and proficiency in budget planning, financial management and resource management.</li>
</ul>
