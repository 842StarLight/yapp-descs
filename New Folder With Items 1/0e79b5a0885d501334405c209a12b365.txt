<h2>Client Partner III- Credit Union Solutions</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>This role can be remote located anywhere in the United States.</b></h2>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
What does a great Client Partner III do?</b></h2>
<p>
They provide comprehensive solutions to Fiserv’s credit union clients. Serving as the primary contact for the client’s executive and/ or senior level managers, you will be responsible for managing and growing the overall relationship with key decision makers and influencers at assigned clients to build customer loyalty and increase revenues. This position will use Fiserv solutions to improve client’s growth and profitability, utilizing various company resources to ensure client happiness, retention and increased profitability for Fiserv.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
As a Client Partner III, you can look forward to:</b></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Client Relationship</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>
Build and maintain effective relationships with the CEO/President and other key senior executive and operational decision makers.</li>
<li>
Understand the politics of client’s organization in order to appropriately influence decision makers.</li>
<li>
Understand the credit union’s business challenges, strategic objectives, competitive standing, and critical success factors to determine the best Fiserv solutions.</li>
<li>
Use Fiserv internal resources to build and maintain an effective relationship with the client.</li>
<li>
Develop and document an Account Plan at least annually that includes the integration of other Fiserv business divisions, establish a course of action to accomplish specific goals, and take the lead on the Account Plan with other business divisions.</li>
<li>
Promote client attendance at Fiserv sponsored events such as annual client conferences, regional client conferences and user group meetings.</li>
</ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Client Retention</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>
Develop a plan to actively secure timely contract renewals.</li>
<li>
Monitor client happiness, facilitate improvement as needed, and regularly stay in touch with client’s key stakeholders.</li>
<li>
Demonstrate industry trends and Fiserv competition knowledge to demonstrate Fiserv value proposition.</li>
</ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Revenue Maintenance &amp; Growth</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>
Achieve annual sales quotas based on add-on products and services and renewals.</li>
<li>
Understand and promote Fiserv capabilities, solution sets and match those with client’s needs, ensuring that Fiserv increases wallet share with each client through compelling business unit cross sales and Fiserv Enterprise Solutions sales. Enterprise Solutions include Digital Bundle (such as Online Banking, Online BillPay, and Mobile platforms), Card Services, and EMV.</li>
<li>
Develop formal proposals for additional products &amp; services that address the client’s needs.</li>
<li>
Ensure a fair return on Fiserv services rendered by following up on any client receivables and negotiating a fair resolution on any items in question</li>
</ul>

</ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Preferred qualifications for consideration:</b></h2>
