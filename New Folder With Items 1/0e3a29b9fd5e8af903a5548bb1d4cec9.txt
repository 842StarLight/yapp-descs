At Apple, we work every day to create products that enrich people’s lives. Our Ad Platforms group makes it possible for people around the world to easily access informative and imaginative content on their devices while helping publishers and developers promote and monetize their work. Our technology and services power advertising in Apple News and Apple Search Ads on the App Store. Our platforms are highly performant, deployed at scale, and set new standards for enabling effective advertising while protecting user privacy.
The Associate Creative Director (ACD) for our group will lead the creative efforts for the Ad Platforms organization. This position will manage the creation of innovative and compelling design solutions that can scale across all of our channels as well as maintain consistency across all deliverables and campaigns. Excelling in this position will require a deep understanding of the Apple brand, B2B marketing, and effective customer communications.
In this role, you will manage a small studio team of designers, writers, and production designers—validating and directing their work as well as taking responsibility for their career development. You will oversee the day-to-day responsibilities of the studio and work with a Producer to develop creative solutions on time, in response to creative briefs. You will work collaboratively with other team leads to ensure the team is delivering great work. This is a hands-on management role.</div>
