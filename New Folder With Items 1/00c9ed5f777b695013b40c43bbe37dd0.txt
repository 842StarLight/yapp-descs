<p><b>Store Director Trainee Selection</b></p>
<p>
Store Director Trainees are selected through the Albertsons posting process. Selection is based the candidates interview, performance reviews, and district manager recommendation. The number of positions is based on projected store director openings within the district.</p>
<p>
Relevant Experience, Education, Certification, Knowledge, Skills and Abilities</p>
<ul>
<li>Assistant Store Directors should have completed the Assistant Director Training or equivalent training requirements prior to posting for the Store Director Training Program.</li>
<li>Store Director Trainees must complete the store rotations prior to starting the District Rotations.</li>
<li>The District Rotations must be completed prior to attending the Store Director Training - Capstone: Operations Decisions Class.</li>
<li>Experience as an Assistant Store Director.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>College Degree preferred.</li>
<li>Requires a thorough understanding of store operations and Company practices, policies and procedures.</li>
<li>Ability to work varying shifts and days of the week (may include evenings, weekends, early morning or late shifts).</li>
<li>Requires the ability to interpret profit and loss statements. Requires the knowledge and ability to implement emergency procedures in the event of equipment and computer software malfunctions.</li>
</ul>
<p>Physical Environment</p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to stand and walk for long periods of time. Ability to reach, lift, stack and maneuver objects of varying dimensions and weights up to approximately 55 lbs. Ability to push and pull fully loaded hand trucks and pallet jacks. Manual dexterity and good eye-hand coordination are necessary. Requires the physical ability to be able to cover for various positions throughout the store as required.</li>
<li>May occasionally be exposed to temperatures of approximately -20o Fahrenheit, 28o Fahrenheit, and 35o Fahrenheit, while handling products in freezers, coolers and display cases. Temperatures of approximately 150o Fahrenheit while handling products in hot cases. Most work is performed in a temperature-controlled store environment.</li>
<li>Incumbent may use calculators, keyboards, telephone, and other office equipment in the course of normal workday.</li>
<li>Incumbent may sit for periods of time at desk or computer terminal.</li>
</ul>
