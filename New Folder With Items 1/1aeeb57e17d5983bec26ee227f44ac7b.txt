<p>
As the Supply Chain Demand Planner, you will be responsible for developing statistically based forecast information for individual items within certain product lines. You will develop forecasting models as required to analyze and product forecast information. One of your goals will be accountable for achieving annual inventory turn, product availability and forecast accuracy goals.
</p>
<p>
You’re an assertive, proactive person and possess the ability to think ahead and move forward to attain goals. You’re diplomatic and know how to collaborate. You’re an influencer. You’re analytical and you like to problem solve.<br>
Required Experience:<br>
</p>
<p>
Analyzes historical information for the purpose of developing statistically based forecast information for individual items within certain product lines. Develops forecasting models as required to analyze and produce forecast information. Participates in demand and inventory planning activities and develops proficiency in the use of the current tools being used by the Supply Chain Management department for forecasting and procurement. Interfaces with other business units as is appropriate to perform the essential duties and responsibilities as outlined below.
</p>
<p>Essential Duties and Responsibilities include the following: (additional duties may be assigned)
</p>
<p>Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.
</p>
<p>Perform statistical analysis on historical product demand information to analyze and predict future demand.
</p>
<ul>
<li>Develop statistical models using means, standard deviation and trend lines to produce product demand profiles.</li>
<li>
Present forecast information in a manner suitable for use by Forecasting, MRP processes and Marketing/Sales.</li>
<li>
Develop proficiency in the use of the tools in use by the Supply Chain Management department for forecasting and inventory procurement.</li>
<li>
Research and collect intuitive information required to forecast demand for product not having statistically significant demand history.</li>
<li>
Take the actions required to facilitate the acquisition of inventory to meet demand on a timely basis.</li>
<li>
Be a contributing member of the Supply Chain Management Team.</li>
<li>
Lead the Monthly Demand Review meetings. Collaborate with Sales and Product Development partners to gather data needed to develop an accurate forecast.</li>
<li>
Accountable for achieving annual Inventory Turn, Product Availability and Forecast Accuracy goals</li>
</ul>
