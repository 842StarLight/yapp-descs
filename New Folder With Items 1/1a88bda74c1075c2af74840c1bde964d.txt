Quality Engineering Intern
<br>Work to improve Quality performance measures and improve the Quality Management system.
<br><br>
Responsibilities
<br><ul>
<li>Collect and analyze quality data, particularly from the Qualifying department</li>
<li>Work on projects that decrease the Cost of Quality</li>
<li>Use principles of Statistical Quality Control to maintain process control</li>
<li>Work with employees to maintain and continuously update documentation throughout the business</li>
<li>Assist with audits by collecting results, entering data and following up on corrective actions</li>
<li>Participate in the Fast Response process</li>
<li>Create and improve documents used for collecting and analyzing data.</li>
<li>Advanced skills with Excel and VBA are a plus.</li>
<li>Work with quality lab to improve processes with the aim of reducing paperwork and making data/records more accessible</li>
<li>Follow all Safety, Environmental, and Quality policies and guidelines.</li>
<li>Other duties may be assigned or required for this position.</li>
</ul>
Qualifications
<br><ul>
<li>High School Diploma</li>
