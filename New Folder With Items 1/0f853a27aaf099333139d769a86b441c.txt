<h2>Finance Analytics Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>What you’ll be doing...
</b></p>
<p>The Finance Analytics Analyst uses technical expertise to provide enablement and analytic assistance to Finance and the rest of Tableau. Success in this role means that our business partners have access to the information and the support they need to see and understand their data.
</p>
<p>Collect, validate, secure, and share all types of company data. Collect data via a wide range of methods and curate data sources for end users. Develop and track Key Performance Indicators and metrics in order to enhance understanding of the business and to improve strategic decision making. Collaborate with our business partners to automate manual tasks and increase operational efficiency. Conduct quantitative analysis and take responsibility for the integrity of data relating to all aspects of Tableau.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Some of the things you will be doing…
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Build and maintain processes to extract and load data into reporting databases.
</li>
<li>Transform and enrich data into easy to use data sources.
</li>
<li>Help the Development team build the best products possible by testing and providing feedback on new and existing products.
</li>
<li>Create highly scalable content to be leveraged by sales and customers.
</li>
<li>Develop, track, analyze and report on Key Performance Indictors and metrics.
</li>
<li>Employ visual best practices to present analyses in the form of Tableau worksheets and dashboards.
</li>
<li>Develop highly effective ways of using data to influence decisions and suggest business actions.
</li>
<li>Use appropriate techniques and channels to summarize findings, publicize results, and engage with senior leadership and other stakeholders.
</li>
<li>Document data sources, to promote their use more widely within the organization.
</li>
<li>Develop and promote uptake of best practices in data acquisition.
</li>
<li>Collaborate with teams to define and promote processes for data capture, data integrity and reporting functionality.
</li>
<li>Use Tableau expertise to design and implement programs that promote an analytical culture.
</li>
<li>Act at all times as a champion of Tableau products, and as an acknowledged expert in their use and in the use of data sources.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Who you are…
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Project Manager. </b>Ability to develop programs from requirements-capture to launch, with limited guidance.
</li>
<li>
<b>Detail Oriented. </b>Ability to juggle and prioritize a high-volume workload while handling details accurately, in a timely manner, and under pressure. Ability to work effectively with stakeholders with competing priorities, including senior managers and executives.
</li>
<li>
<b>Strong Communicator. </b>Ability to present complex ideas to a broad audience.
</li>
<li>
<b>Problem Solver. </b>Ability to build solutions in a customer-first environment.
</li>
<li>
<b>Curious. </b>Desire to use Tableau in new and different ways. Seeks out information and resources to share with others. Passion for creating scalable and repeatable analytics. Ability to rapidly discover and utilize data preparation and statistical analysis tools/languages.
</li>
<li>
<b>Collaborative. </b>Ability to collaboratively exchange ideas and thrive in a team-based environment
</li>
<li>
<b>Technically Savvy. </b>Exposure to the Tableau Product Suite, Alteryx, Data Robot, R and Python helpful. Experience with systems like NetSuite, Egencia, Concur, Coupa and Salesforce preferred. Use of APIs, Web Data Connectors and Cloud based data warehouses like Redshift and Snowflake a plus. Certification: Analysts at Tableau are expected to complete internal and external product certification programs within agreed upon timeline.
</li>
<li>
<b>Experienced. </b>5+ years of work experience, in a software environment preferred. Business Analytics/Programming and Accounting/Corporate Finance experience. Working knowledge of relational database technologies.
</li>
<li>
<b>Educated. </b>Bachelors or higher degree in an analytical field such as Data Science, Data Engineering, Computer Science, Business, Mathematics, Statistics or Economics.
</li>
</ul>
