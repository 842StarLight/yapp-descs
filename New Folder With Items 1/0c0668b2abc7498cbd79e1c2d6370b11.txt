<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>About the team</b></h2>
Zillow Group’s mission is to build the world’s most trusted and vibrant home related marketplace. Zillow Group’s largest business unit, Premier Agent, introduces consumers who are looking to buy or sell homes to real estate agents who can help them do so! We are in the process of transforming this business from an advertising model to a transaction model to improve outcomes for all parties involved and to drive top-line growth. Our agent partners’ success is of utmost importance in this model as we are aligning our business outcome to the transaction success of our partners. We are looking for a Senior Director of Partner Success who is responsible for setting up and managing a partner-facing team of product specialists and performance coaches that enables our partners to maximize their success with the Premier Agent platform.<br>
<br>
The Senior Director of Partner Success is a leader within the Premier Agent Strategy &amp; Operations team. This team runs the Premier Agent business, setting strategic direction, figuring out how strategy gets implemented, and owning the results that flow from strategy. We are heavy-hitters who move fast, think big, and team up to deliver results. Our closest partners are the sales force, product org, and marketing team.<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
About the role</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>
<b>
Strategy: </b>Define the partner success program strategy. Develop a cohesive partner success approach across all teams involved, incl. partner onboarding, product setup and maintenance, on-demand coaching.</li>
<li>
<b>
Team leadership:</b> Lead a team of success managers and product specialist. Drive employee engagement and morale. Hold team members accountable for performance delivery. Scale and grow the team in line with business needs.</li>
<li>
<b>
Partner success:</b> Ensure effective delivery of product support and coaching services to maximize partner transaction conversion outcomes and the overall agent experience.</li>
<li>
<b>
Operations:</b> Continuously refine responsibilities, workflows, curriculums and delivery mechanisms of each role in order to optimize the team’s productivity and effectiveness.</li>
<li>
<b>
Analysis: </b>Define, track, and communicate key metrics that help us understand the performance of this function and the value delivered to our partners.</li>
<li>
<b>
Collaboration:</b> Work closely with the Premier Agent Sales, Product, and Strategy &amp; Operations leadership to ensure a seamless and productive partner experience</li>
<li>
<b>
Cross-functional leadership: </b>Communicate with the larger Premier Agent business to drive understanding of partner success elements and identify opportunities to improve the overall business model.</li>
</ul>
