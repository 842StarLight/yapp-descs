<p><b>Job Description
</b></p>
<p>We seek a lawyer who will be engaged fully, communicate openly and forge close working relationships throughout our company. We seek a confident self-starter who can prioritize and manage multiple tasks and responsibilities and who enjoys working in a fast-paced and high-energy environment. We seek a leader, who can use his or her exceptional judgment and strong critical thinking and interpersonal skills to be comfortable, effective and persuasive in dealing with Sales teams, prospective customers and senior management.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Essential Duties and Responsibilities
</p>
<ul>
<li>Draft, review and negotiate various types of software / technology agreements
</li>
<li>Analyze and interpret complex provisions in multi-part contracts to ensure compliance and consistency
</li>
<li>Provide general legal advice on a variety of topics
</li>
<li>Negotiating and drafting corporate documents and other general corporate work
</li>
</ul>
<p>Required Skills &amp; Experience
</p>
<ul>
<li>JD from a top tier law school, excellent academic credentials
 </li>
<li>Bar Admission, preferably California
</li>
<li>5+ years of combined law firm and in-house legal department work experience, focused on negotiation of significant technology transactions, preferably with software / SaaS experience
</li>
<li>Outstanding organizational skills
</li>
<li>Critical thinker, problem solver Detail-orientated and meticulous practitioner
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Personal Attributes
</p>
<ul>
<li>Strong leadership ability, including outstanding influence and communication skills
</li>
<li>A strong team player who can work well with Workday colleagues in finance, sales, operations, human resources and other functions
</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task in a fast-paced, high-output, team oriented, practical environment
</li>
<li>Comfortable working with and supporting alpha-type personalities
</li>
<li>Demonstrates enthusiasm for solving problems and adding value to the company
</li>
<li>Committed to his or her personal growth and development by actively seeking feedback and responding with action
</li>
</ul>
