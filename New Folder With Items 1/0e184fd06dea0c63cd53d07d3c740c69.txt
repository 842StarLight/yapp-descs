<h2>Regional Head of Partner Sales, New York</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>As a Regional Head of Partner Sales You Will . . .</b><ul>
<li>
Identify, qualify, recruit and onboard the right partners for SaleCycle across the Americas, Canada (and potentially further)</li>
<li>
Create and execute against a business plan for partner sourced business across the region</li>
<li>
Build relationships at the highest partner level to understand business needs</li>
<li>
Identify and develop a go-to-market strategy that leverages the strengths of the partners to create a differentiated value proposition</li>
<li>
Confirm and communicate the SaleCycle value to partners and their customers</li>
<li>
Educate partners about product portfolio and complementary services offered. Communicate up-to-date information about new products and enhancements to partners</li>
<li>
Understand the formal and informal buying process in the customer's environment</li>
<li>
Work with partners to develop sales proposals, quotations, and pricing</li>
<li>
Deliver customer presentations and attend sales meetings and partner conferences.</li>
<li>
Assist in partner marketing activities such as campaigns and other promotional activities.</li>
<li>
Build trust to encourage success with both partner sourced and partner driven, opportunities</li>
<li>
Own partner forecasting and KPIs for your region on a weekly, monthly and quarterly basis</li>
<li>
Evaluate partner sales performance and recommend improvements</li>
<li>
Address partner related issues, sales conflicts and pricing issues in a timely manner</li>
<li>
Develop process improvements to optimize partner management activities</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
 <b>You Will Have the Following skills &amp; Competencies . . .</b></p>
<p>
To perform the job successfully, an individual should demonstrate the following competencies:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Ambitious and Entrepreneurial Self-starter, requiring little supervision; wants to own their area of the business; follows sales plan and engages necessary resources to meet set goals</li>
<li>
Interpersonal Skills Positive outlook; takes responsibility; strong self-confidence; comfortable talking about commercials; maintains confidentiality; listens to others; keeps emotions under control; open to ideas; tries new things; strong desire for success</li>
<li>
Communication Speaks clearly and persuasively in all situations; listens and clarifies; responds well to questions; confident group presentation skills; participates in meetings. Writes clearly and informatively; takes care with spelling and grammar; varies writing style to meet needs</li>
<li>
Judgement Displays willingness to make decisions; exhibits sound and accurate judgment; supports and gives reasons for decisions; includes appropriate people in decision-making; makes timely decisions</li>
<li>
Empathy Demonstrates exceptional empathy for partners and their customers, which helps build more authentic and mutually beneficial relationships</li>
<li>
Curiosity Has a genuine interest in developing a deep and broad understanding of partner organisations. Uses this knowledge to deliver dramatically more value</li>
<li>
Sense of Urgency Demonstrates a desire to identify and solve problems now, recognizing that SaleCycle solutions cannot deliver value unless and until they are purchased and implemented</li>
<li>
Approach Views selling as a service and sees this role as key to helping businesses make more informed, intelligent buying decisions. Differentiates themselves from other sales people throughout the client engagement by delivering more value and expertise</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
You Will Have the Following Experience . . .</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
(5+ years) of relevant Partner sales experience, preferably in SaaS, Marketing tech, ecommerce tech (or similar industry) and a demonstrated track-record of consistently meeting or exceeding performance targets</li>
<li>
Existing network of partner contacts in the performance marketing, marketing technology or ecommerce space is highly desirable</li>
<li>
Experience with recruiting new partners like software vendors and system integrators</li>
<li>
Ability to define, develop and lead partner programs in a fast-paced, rapidly changing environment.</li>
<li>
Able to establish, build and grow strong relationships.</li>
<li>
Sales oriented candidate who drives rapid change and growth.</li>
<li>
Exceptional collaboration, relationship-building and interpersonal skills with the ability to develop working relationships at all levels of management</li>
<li>
Excellent presentation skills; a highly effective influencer</li>
<li>
Willing to travel where beneficial to relationships</li>
<li>
Fast learner who thrives in a high growth, continuous change environment.</li>
<li>
Comfortable with G-Suite, MS Office and Salesforce</li>
</ul>
