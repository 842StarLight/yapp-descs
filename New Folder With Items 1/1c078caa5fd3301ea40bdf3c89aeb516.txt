About the Solutions Operations Project Analyst</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>
HPE Pointnext Solutions Operations is currently seeking highly qualified and motivated candidates for a position in Roseville, CA, for a Solutions Operations Project Analyst. No relocation assistance available.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
Within the HPE Pointnext organization in support of Advisory &amp; Professional Services projects, the Solutions Operations Project Analyst role provides end-to-end value-add consultancy and project management from Presales through the Delivery phase. In this role, you will be controlling and managing the administrative aspects of projects in the areas of deal assessment; deal governance and approvals; multinational compliance; SOX compliance and order acceptance; customer invoicing; legal; tax; and operational and financial matters.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
The nature of the role is such that it will demand additional time commitment at monthly and quarterly financial closes.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
You will demonstrate strong business and financial knowledge which will ensure projects are compliant per HPE policies. You will complete deal assessment activities to determine the appropriate level of consultancy and ownership on a deal-by-deal basis (i.e.: Standard vs. Complex). For Complex Deals, you will act as an advisor and consultant with Sales, Delivery, and other business partners during the crafting of a project proposal and onwards through ongoing project performance.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
You will be able to perform in a dynamic environment with the ability to make decisions when information is not clearly defined.</p>
