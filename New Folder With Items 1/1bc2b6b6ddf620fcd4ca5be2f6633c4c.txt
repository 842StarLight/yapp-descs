The Software Engineer has responsibility for developing and programming solutions for SurfMax and VoluMax technology platform. He/she provides support during project phase by creating all necessary programming routines and subprograms working in their optimum performance. Software Engineer is continuously improving the existing software platform as well as developing new software modules to meet the needs of our customers and maintain a robust, modern software environment.</p>
<div>
<div>Primary Duties and Responsibilities</div>
<ul>
<li>To create software solutions optimal for application</li>
<li>Develop requirements document collaboratively with rest of project team</li>
<li>Create software solutions and programs for part inspection application</li>
<li>To provide post proposal support
</li>
<li>Follow up with customer or applications team to address questions and obstacles</li>
<li>Reengineer solution as necessary</li>
<li>To provide software expertise for necessary project related activities – post sales</li>
<li>Ongoing solution development coordination</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>
<div>Experience and Education Requirements</div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in electrical engineering, computer science, and computer engineering (MS preferred).
</li>
<li>Minimum of 1-2 years’ experience developing software following software development methodology.</li>
<li>Expertise with C#, C/C++, and Objective Orient Programming, Microsoft Visual Studio, and .NET.</li>
<li>Excellent verbal communication skills and be comfortable presenting technical information to internal Zeiss team</li>
<li>Able to communicate the requirements across different technical and managerial levels at customer sites</li>
</ul>
