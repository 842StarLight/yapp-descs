<h2>Switchgear & Electrical Services Technician - Richmond/Roanoke Area</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Position Overview:<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>Start-up/commissioning, maintenance, and troubleshooting emergency electrical issues on medium &amp; low voltage electrical power systems. These power systems include, but are not limited to transformers, circuit breakers, switchgear/switchboard, protective relays, motor control centers, VFD’s, cable, and metering and backup power supplies.
</p>

<p>
§ Overall understanding of electrical systems and products.
</p>
<p>§ Executes digitalization and standard service operations strategies.</p>
<p>
§ Works with multiple internal service team members including Service Sales, Client Service Manager, Onsite Specialists and in AM Digital Service.
</p>
<p>§ Ability to develop strong working relationships with internal and external customers, identify needs and resolve issues.</p>
<p>
§ Follows all safety requirements as outlined by Siemens as well as customers.</p>
<p>
§ Good verbal and written communications skills. Skilled in the PC applications and in the use of word processing and spreadsheet programs.
</p>
<p>§ Ability to use Siemens technology and devices.</p>
<p>
§ Successfully complete assigned training as outlined in the training path.
</p>
<p>§ Follows the mandatory policies, procedures, and standards.</p>
<p>
§ Ability to work remotely.</p>
<p>

<p>

<p>

<p>

<p>

<p>
§ Leads from basic to complex installation and checkout of electric and electronic components for systems and service installed jobs and service agreements. Tasks are associated with maintenance and testing of: switchgear/switchboard, motor controls, circuit breakers, panel boards, transformers, and surge protection devices. Responsible for vibration analysis and metering, battery maintenance and testing, power monitoring, power quality, and protective relay maintenance.</p>
<p>
§ Must have experience in troubleshooting &amp; repair of electrical control circuits. Knowledge or capability to obtain thermography certification.</p>
<p>
§ May need to install wiring and connect to the power source. Performs emergency troubleshooting of power distribution products and 3rd party equipment. Performs complex service upgrades, reconditioning, retrofits, etc. for its low-voltage power distribution products and systems.
</p>
<p>§ Performs on-site technical and operational support in the design, development, installation and maintenance of equipment and systems of a complex nature. Performs complex site surveys to develop base or installation design plans.
</p>
<div>§ Provides instructions to customer personnel regarding proper operations and routine inspections and assists with training and system acceptance. Train customers to maintain and adjust complex equipment. Provides expert advice, training, and technical assistance to support staff and train lower level employees. Completes and submits reports covering all job activity. Responsible for maintaining complex tools, test equipment, calibration items, etc.

Performs the review of complex task plans, drawings, and installation schematics and provides continual guidance throughout the project duration.</p>

<p>

<p>
§ Ability to inspect, troubleshoot and evaluate equipment.</p>
<p>

<p>

Familiar with the Arc Flash and Shock Hazards of electrical equipment.
</p>
<p>§ Proficient with Microsoft Office suite, especially Word, Excel &amp; PowerPoint, experience with project management software preferred.</p>
