<p><b>Job Summary &amp; Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>The Solution Design team is a highly critical, specialized and fast-growing team within GEP. In this role, your work will involve assisting the sales and procurement specialists with the needed analytics during demo discovery and guiding him/her towards a solution fit during this discovery. This includes crafting a solution, creating pilots and demos/presentations. These are regional positions based in the Northeast, Southeast, Midwest and Southwest.</p>
<p><b>Duties and Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Create and own responses to Request for Proposal (RFPs), Request for Information (RFI), POCs, SoWs or Work Orders while working on assigned accounts</li>
<li>Work with respective Onsite/Regional Sales Team members in a highly collaborative fashion to be their extended arm for any ongoing Sales Engagements/Discussions</li>
<li>Exhibit strong technical and functional expertise during the entire sales process</li>
<li>Manage accounts where 50-75% of your time would include travel to existing/potential client sites</li>
<li>Showcase deep understanding of our platform from implementation &amp; integration perspective</li>
<li>Conduct Platform Demonstrations, IT/Platform Workshops and related trainings during entire Sales Cycle</li>
<li>Knowledge Management, Pipeline Tracking/Updates &amp; Continuous Process Improvement would be one of the primary activities/expectations</li>
<li>Work in collaboration with PMG (Product Marketing Group) to create case studies, marketing collateral (White Papers, Research Reports etc..) and represent GEP in various industry events &amp; conferences</li>
<li>Demonstrate ability to understand &amp; articulate client environment, challenges &amp; requirements to provide solution/proposal to address client needs</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualification Criteria: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>3+ years of software solution design for cloud-based solutions (ideally source to pay, procure to pay, procurement, sourcing, spend, contract management related solutions). Enterprise application software, SAAS companies and Product companies would be a must</li>
<li>Knowledge of procurement processes and environment desired and helpful to excelling at this job</li>
<li>Experience in systems applications selling or implementation of these applications</li>
<li>Dynamic positive personality, very strong presentation skills, and ideally experience with procurement, spend, sourcing related industry experience</li>
<li>Willingness to travel 50%-75%</li>
</ul>
