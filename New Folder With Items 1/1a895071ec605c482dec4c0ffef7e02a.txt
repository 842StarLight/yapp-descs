<p><b>Brief Summary of This Position:
</b></p>
<p>This individual contributor position is accountable for support of assigned individuals within the Customer Service Team and a main point of contact on assigned accounts. The position will coordinate and provide comprehensive Customer Service support for assigned areas including: order processing which may include items such as: entering new orders, entering and tracking anchor bolt pre-shipments, entering part shortage requests, requesting Proof of Deliveries, entering production part numbers, determining customer numbers, issuing Invoices, and general filing and E-filing for the Project Administrators. As a member of the Customer Service Team, this role serves as a coordinator on assigned accounts. Coordination takes place between Valmont and our OEM’s / representatives and customers in areas of requests for quotations, accepting and processing orders, computing accurate prices, maintaining management directed margins, negotiating price and delivery to obtain orders, handling all quote and order related issues for our customers. The incumbent is part of a broad cross-functional team consisting of Regional Sales Managers, Project Administrators, Engineer, Drafters, and Field Service Specialist.
</p>
<p></p>
