<h2>Engagement Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Job Description</div>
<p></p>
<p>
Our Engagement Managers are the calm, consistent customer force that gather customer business requirements and design a solution that meets their needs. We flawlessly follow our Customer Success methodology, encouraging utmost confidence along the way. In this highly versatile position we are responsible for the creation of the project plan, configuration of a customer’s Zendesk environment as well as transferring knowledge of the customer’s vision, goals, and completed project information both internally and amongst project team. As Project Managers we also complete project milestones, document and prescribe the Success plan, and assist in managing the go-live phase of the customer</p>
<p></p>
<p>
Description:</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
You will become proficient in customer support workflows and Zendesk’s business applications and services offerings. Ultimately you will apply this knowledge to Zendesk customer business needs to design high value services engagements.</li>
<li>
You will support our Sales team with a focus on large enterprise customers. You'll create services proposals, statements of work, and contract negotiations.</li>
<li>
Leading partner resources from assignment to completion, you'll ensure the customer has a positive Zendesk experience.</li>
<li>
You will work with external partner resources to ensure delivery methodologies are consistent with the Zendesk principles.</li>
<li>
You will manage executive level client relationships and provide team oversight during the delivery of services engagements.</li>
<li>
You will support account management team with minor customization requests from strategic accounts.</li>
<li>
Manage internal customer migrations using internal tools.</li>
<li>
Contribute to practice development initiatives through the development of service offerings, best practices, methodologies, and other intellectual capital.</li>
<li>
Parter effectively with other parts of the organization, including Support and Product Management to address and resolve customer issues.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with Zendesk partners during the sales process and in delivering joint customer engagements.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>
Requirements:</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelors Degree</li>
<li>
Fluent in English and Spanish</li>
<li>
Excellent client management instincts and abilities</li>
<li>
Strong written and verbal communications skills and experience in facilitation.</li>
<li>
Some experience with RESTful and JSON API implementation</li>
<li>
Knowledge of HTML, XML, JavaScript and CSS</li>
<li>
3-5 years experience delivering support and/or consulting services, preferably involvement in selling services</li>
<li>
Project management experience (SOWs, contract management, risk management, staffing)</li>
<li>
Experience selling and/or delivering support services, or experience with products and/or processes related to customer support groups.</li>
<li>
Ability and willingness to travel up to 40%</li>
</ul>
