<p><b>General Job Duties:
</b></p>
<p>This position is responsible for procuring and managing all supplies, equipment, implants, instruments and services. In addition, this position is responsible for the receipt of all material shipped into and out of TOA, as well as, assisting in maintaining adequate inventory levels of stock supplies by updating the inventory management program daily. This includes monitoring, ordering supplies and stocking the various storage areas at the facility. In addition, this position assists in monitoring scanned data to ensure product scans correspond with products issued in order to ensure each patient charge item is captured. This position must be flexible enough to assist in other areas when needed including but not limited to; assisting OR team with room turnover, patient transportation between facility departments, and identifying and pulling supplies for upcoming surgical cases. Will be required to travel to other locations.</p>
</div>
<br><div><p>High school diploma or equivalent, valid NC Driver License and strong computer skills are required. Two to five years of inventory experience preferred. Experience with SAP and/or other inventory software preferred, knowledge of durable medical equipment preferred. Must also have good communication skills and occasionally lift and/or move up to 50 pounds.</p></div>
