<h2>Senior Account Manager - Healthcare- Oncology</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Ipsos Marketing for the Health &amp; Pharmaceuticals sector</b> is a worldwide specialization that focuses on understanding the motivations, experiences, interactions and influences of the multiple stakeholders who impact the success of a product within the pharmaceutical industry. As a Account Manager, you will provide strategic insight to our clients in the delivery of research services. You are conversant in quantitative research design, interpretation and analysis of results, and have an exceptional flare for building and maintaining client relationships. In addition, you are comfortable working with large data sets and have a passion for finding answers through monitoring, analyzing and understanding results to determine trends.</p>
<p><b>
Key Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Independently manage and coordinate all aspects of the research process from proposal writing and research design, analysis and interpretation of results through to preparation of reports and delivery of presentations</li>
<li>Managing key operational partners (data collection, data processing and results reporting) to ensure that reliable results are delivered to client in a timely, cost-efficient manner</li>
<li>Maintain a project budget and work with team to ensure all aspects of the study continue to operate smoothly</li>
<li>With oversight from the VP, foster strong client relationships and work to develop those relationships through frequent client contact including travel to client sites, leading meetings/presentations, and delivering insightful, thoughtful research.</li>
<li>Provide training, guidance and support to junior staff</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Required Skills:</b></p>
<p><b>
Ideal Candidate has the following Skills and Experience
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>6-8 years of direct market research experience (supplier side experience an asset)</li>
<li>Proven experience building and managing successful client relationships</li>
<li>Knowledge of the health and pharmaceutical industry is desired</li>
<li>Knowledge of Oncology is a plus, not necessary</li>
<li>Ability to manage complex, multi-faceted project budgets and to create, manage, and commit to a project plan, schedule and budget</li>
<li>Data savvy with a strong attention to detail and accuracy</li>
<li>Sound understanding or research principles, designs, methodologies</li>
<li>Resourceful, pragmatic and flexible but always standards-oriented</li>
<li>Enjoy working in a team environment</li>
<li>Excellent written &amp; verbal communication skills with well-developed interpersonal and presentation skills</li>
<li>Excellent organizational and problem-solving skills with the ability to prioritize multiple projects and tasks</li>
<li>Strong computer skills including Microsoft Office Suite and other business-related software systems; SPSS or similar a plus, but not necessary</li>
<li>Bachelors Degree or higher</li>
<li>Travel is mandatory and will range from 5%</li>
</ul>
