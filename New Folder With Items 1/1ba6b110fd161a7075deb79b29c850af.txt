<h2>Local Sales Manager - Cox Media Inc</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>At Cox Media, we believe in recognizing outstanding work, investing in our teams and building authentic relationships. With nearly 30 offices in 13 different states, Cox Media covers 19 markets and 6 million households across our aggregated footprint, connecting advertisers of all sizes to audiences across multiple screens. Using everything from cable TV to the latest digital products, we create multi-platform advertising campaigns to exceed our clients’ marketing objectives.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Cox Media is looking for an exceptional <b>Local Sales Manager</b> to lead, manage and coach a team of Account Executives who sell innovative television and digital advertising solutions to local businesses.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
Responsibilities:</b></div>
<div>
</div>
<div><ul>
<li>Manage local sales team and oversee all sales activities creating a high-performing, fun and successful team environment</li>
<li>
Spends significant time with Account Executives in the field and attend client meetings to enhance account relationships and provide coaching opportunities</li>
<li>
Attract, hire and develop new sales talent who thrive in a fast-paced, creative culture</li>
<li>
Use Cox Media cable television and digital advertising solutions as the foundation of marketing campaigns to grow revenue in local and regional markets</li>
<li>
Provide on-going training to further enhance team performance and ensure team meets and/or exceeds goals</li>
<li>
Communicate sales activities with senior leadership and report on sales metrics, forecasts, budget variance and other key sales metrics</li>
<li>
Represent Cox Media in the business community by attending local events and building relationships with key decision makers</li>
</ul></div>
<div></div>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Qualifications:</b></h4>
<div>
<div>
<div><b>Minimum</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
</div>
<ul>
<li>
3-5 years of media, cable, or digital advertising sales experience</li>
<li>
Strong leadership, motivational and sales skills with a proven track record of success</li>
<li>
Highly organized and able to multi-task in an extremely fast paced environment</li>
<li>
Ability to communicate effectively with clients and all levels of personnel</li>
<li>
Valid driver’s license and a clean driving record</li>
</ul>

2+ years experience leading a team of 5 or more people</li>
</ul>
