<h3 class="jobSectionHeader">
<b>
Position Overview</b>
</h3>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Reporting to the Accounting Manager, the Staff Accountant will help perform a wide variety of accounting functions requiring understanding and application of fundamental accounting principles and practices. This is a great opportunity for someone looking to get into the software space and learn the accounting functions from the ground up at a rapidly growing SaaS tech company with a strong international presence. If you are looking for an opportunity to combine your knowledge, passion, and energy to play a meaningful part in our growth, then Repsly might be a great fit for you.</p>
<br>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities</b></h4>
<ul>
<li>Assist with the monthly and annual close including preparation of monthly journal entries and performing reconciliations of general ledger accounts.</li>
<li>Reconcile bank, credit card, and cash accounts.</li>
<li>Support accounts receivable process by managing collections, and invoicing customers for contract overages and renewals.</li>
<li>Drive the accounts payable process by tracking approvals and managing vendor relations.</li>
<li>Reconcile cash to revenue and internal KPIs reporting.</li>
<li>Assist in processing payroll and related employee benefits.</li>
<li>Interface with sales team to set up and oversee customer accounts.</li>
<li>Assist with annual audit planning and related preparation.</li>
<li>Assist with ad hoc financial project work and other responsibilities as needed.</li>
</ul>
