<h2>Senior Technical Product Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Senior Technical Product Manager, Nashville, TN
<br><br>
<b>Who We Are:</b><br>
As the market leader in enterprise contracts intelligence, Knowable gives corporate clients revolutionary visibility into the relationships that govern their businesses. By converting archaic legal language into structured data, Knowable helps its clients find, search, organize, and analyze their contracts, providing - for the first time - portfolio level views of risks, obligations, and entitlements. As the world's leader in contract data analytics, with more than 25 million contract data elements analyzed each quarter, Knowable combines proprietary machine learning tools with scalable facilities for human quality control that, together, enable a full contracts intelligence solution for the enterprise.
<br><br>
We are seeking a Senior Technical Product Manager to join our R&amp;D Team based in Nashville, TN. Reporting to Knowable's Chief Technology Officer, this role will define the software features and functionality that will bring to life Knowable's ambitious vision for machine learning and artificial intelligence. Come be a part of it!
<br><br>
<b>The Role:</b><br>
Are you passionate about building leading-edge technology that will transform an industry? Does taking one of the last analog worlds to digital get your pulse up? Is building enterprise-class solutions using the latest cloud technologies and current development best practices something you think is cool?
<br><br>
If you like to create new solutions from scratch and follow that solution throughout all phases from production to support, we want to talk to you. If you are an independent self-starter, work well within an integrated team, have a strong sense of ownership, and are obsessed by solving customer use cases and scenarios using best practices and cloud technologies, we want to talk to you!
<br><br>
<b>Specifically, this role will:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Develop and foster key cross-functional relationships across Knowable's Product, Technology, Commercial (Sales &amp; Marketing), and Client Service teams.</li>
<li>Develop innovative software that will bring Machine Learning insights to bear in a variety of contract negotiation scenarios.</li>
<li>Partner cross-functionally to define Knowable's next-generation product roadmap. Multiple Knowable products will go to market on top of Knowable's new Contract Intelligence Platform, requiring exceptional cross-product technical coordination, as well as coordination with business stakeholders including Product Managers and Client Service SMEs.</li>
<li>Drive an agile software development process incorporating user experience, software engineering, and cross-company stakeholders to deliver on the Contract Negotiation product roadmap. Act as the glue that brings a complex landscape of Knowable contributors and third-party partners together as a cohesive unit.</li>
<li>Act as scrum master of an agile development team, lead release planning, and drive product releases activities.</li>
<li>Write user stories and detailed functional specifications for all aspects of Knowable's products. Manage user experience and software development contributions to functional specifications.</li>
</ul>
<b>Successful Candidates:</b><br>
Successful candidates must be fits for Knowable's unique operating environment and culture: high-growth, innovative, lean, and values-driven. As such, successful candidates must be highly capable in each of the following dimensions (among others): adaptability, curiosity, resourcefulness, analytical thinking/problem solving, proactivity, collaboration, technological savvy, and operating with/through a lean team (for people leaders: must be comfortable in a player/coach role.)

Beyond these characteristics, here are the required skills, knowledge, capabilities, and education for this particular role:

<li>BS in Computer Science or other relevant field of study. Knowable is an equal opportunity employer and committed to a diverse workforce.</li>
