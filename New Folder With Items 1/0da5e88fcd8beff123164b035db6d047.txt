<h2>Solution Architect IBM Power</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Summary</b></p>
<br>
<div>
</div>
<div>Provides pre-sales technical engineering support to Account Executives and clients as integral part of full sales process. Focuses on technical solutions for mid-size and enterprise accounts and works closely with clients to determine how Logicalis solutions can best serve their needs including both on-premise and Logicalis Enterprise Cloud solutions, Managed Services, and Professional Services. Conducts in-depth assessments and functions as subject matter expert to design complex client solutions focused on IBM Power Systems utilizing both AIX and Linux operating systems. Expert level with components such as, LPAR, LPM, HA, NIM, VIO, and various Scripting solutions.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
