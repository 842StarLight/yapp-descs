<h2>Fitness Yoga Studio General Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>DESCRIPTION</b></p>
<p>Job Title: General Manager</p>
<p>Reports to: Franchise Owner</p>
<p><b>COMPANY OVERVIEW: </b></p>
<p>YogaSix believes everyone deserves the mind-body experience of yoga. YogaSix offers six different class types designed to accommodate students of all levels in a way that is empowering, encouraging, and fun. YogaSix was established in 2012 and is the fastest growing boutique yoga company in the United States.</p>
<p><b>POSITION: </b></p>
<p>We are looking for a driven, sales-oriented, team player who will help create brand awareness and drive sales for this brand new YogaSix studio. YogaSix is new to the Bay Area so the GM's primary responsibilities during the first few months will be marketing and sales. The GM needs to live for and excel in outside sales! We are looking to hire starting December 2019.</p>
<p>In addition, the GM is responsible for overseeing the designated studio(s) to ensure the studio is well-maintained and that customer service standards are upheld to YogaSix standards. He/she will oversee all aspects of studio functionality including Sales and Operations as well as the direct supervision of Wellness Advisors and Teachers. He/she must have strong customer service skills, knowledge of PC/Mac computers, and able to communicate in an effective and efficient way.</p>
<p><b>JOB REQUIREMENTS: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>2+ years of retail/service sales or fitness sales experience</li>
<li>Experience supervising people successfully</li>
<li>Proven experience in generating and following up on leads</li>
<li>Confident in generating personal sales and training Wellness Advisors in sales</li>
<li>Ability to manage and drive all revenue streams: memberships, retail, and teacher training</li>
<li>Must be fluent in English and have excellent communication and strong interpersonal skills in person, on the telephone and via email</li>
<li>Strong customer service skills</li>
<li>Ability to excel in a fast changing, diverse environment</li>
<li>Ability to recognize areas of improvement and make changes using good judgement</li>
<li>Solid writing and grammar skills</li>
<li>Highly organized, proficient in data management, ability to prioritize and meet deadlines</li>
<li>Professional, punctual, reliable and neat</li>
<li>Strong attention to detail and accuracy</li>
<li>Trustworthy and ability to handle confidential information</li>
<li>Ability to work harmoniously with co-workers, clients and the general public</li>
<li>Ability to stay calm at all times and act respectfully and professionally, even with customers who may become angry or raise their voices</li>
<li>Proficient with computers and Studio software</li>
</ul>
<p><b>DUTIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Lead Generation, Membership Sales and Retention</li>
<li>Manage and grow all revenue streams including Memberships, Retail and Teacher Training</li>
<li>Manage studio budget, spending, and reporting</li>
<li>Create and execute ongoing marketing plan based on studio needs with a focus on new client acquisition, activation, retention and loyalty</li>
<li>Schedule/participate in Grassroots Marketing partnerships on a weekly basis to drive new guest traffic</li>
<li>Work with Studio Owner and Director of Education to optimize class schedule on an ongoing basis</li>
<li>Recruit, hire, train, supervise, coach, mentor all studio staff</li>
<li>Recruit, hire, onboard, coordinate training, and terminate Teachers (with support of lead teacher)</li>
<li>Manage maintenance issues, inventory and cleaning</li>
<li>Participate in studio, regional and company-wide meetings and trainings as needed</li>
<li>Work closely with Studio Owner and National Sales Director to ensure health and profitability of the studio</li>
<li>Social media management</li>
</ul>
