<div>Cloud computing is the kind of major shift in information technology that only comes around once a decade. If you want to be part of history, this is your opportunity. Amazon Web Services is a high-growth, fast-moving group within Amazon with a start-up mentality where new and diverse challenges arise every day, but a group that operates with the full backing of one of the world’s most successful web companies.<br>
If you knew the state of every thing in the world, and could reason on top of the data: What problems would you solve?<br>
AWS Finance seeks a world-class Finance Manager to collaborate with the AWS IoT Services team. This position will work closely with finance management, as well as key business partners across AWS, including Product Managers and Engineers, to drive thoughtful analysis and controllership.<br>
To learn more about IoT, watch the video! https://aws.amazon.com/iot/<br>
The ideal candidate will have experience implementing and driving the core financial processes, including Annual Operating Plans, Service P&amp;Ls, Monthly/Quarterly Business Reviews, identifying and quantify financial/operational Risks and Opportunities (R&amp;O) to the business, and driving ad-hoc business projects. Additional responsibilities include helping refine the capacity planning process and modeling improvements to help drive positive Free Cash Flow.<br>
This role requires a self-starter with a strong financial modeling background, problem solving skills, a keen attention to detail, ability to work in a fast-paced environment with ambiguous situations, and solid skills in project management. This role has regular interaction with various business units across Amazon and requires strong people skills.<br>
Key Responsibilities:<br>
<ul>
<li>Serve as the key business partner working directly with AWS senior management and other internal stakeholders to provide financial recommendations.</li>
<li>Build out and enhance P&amp;L models for new and existing services, develop pricing proposals, deliver business insights and KPI dashboards, and lead ad hoc analyses.</li>
<li>Analyze new business ideas, make analytical judgments, and provide data-intensive analyses to drive meaningful change.</li>
<li>Financial planning and forecasting: identify business levers and variables that drive the P&amp;L, and develop the business/operational metrics to provide key guidance to the organization.</li>
<li>Drive continuous improvements in profitability, free cash flow, and operational efficiency.</li>
<li>Manage financial controllership, lead reviews of financial metrics, analyze variances, synthesize key business drivers, and communicate them to senior management in business terms and influence them through presenting actionable drivers.</li>
</ul>
