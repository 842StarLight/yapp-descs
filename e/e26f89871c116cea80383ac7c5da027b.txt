<h2>CNC Mill Machinist and Programmer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Program, perform set-up and run low volume/high mix (everyday is something new) machined parts primarily for Aerospace and Defense industries utilizing new Okuma and/or Haas mills within a clean, temperature controlled shop. Programming involves utilizing Autodesk PowerMill, training is available. Immediate opportunity to learn 5 axis milling.</p>
<p>The position is full time Monday through Friday. Openings available on both first shift and second shift with a $1.50/Hr premium. Hastreiter Industries strives to provide career opportunities for continual growth.</p>
<p><b>Job Duties: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Perform programing of CNC mills.</li>
<li>Perform job Set-up and verification of machine feeds and speeds.</li>
<li>Operate CNC machines making parts with a variety of materials.</li>
<li>Delegate machine operating when necessary, such as with higher volume runs.</li>
<li>Perform Machine adjustments (tool and work piece offsets).</li>
<li>Locate parts and/or tooling using probes and indicators.</li>
<li>Perform necessary initial and in-process inspections in conformance to product specifications.</li>
<li>Assist Sales department in quoting process.</li>
<li>Assist in material selection and processing.</li>
<li>Assist in tooling selection.</li>
<li>Perform and document routine machine and preventative maintenance.</li>
<li>Support Quality Management System (QMS) to ISO 9001/AS9100 standard.</li>
<li>Support company Vision, Mission and Values.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Job Requirements: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>2-3 years minimum experience with CNC Mill set-up and operating.</li>
<li>Prior experience in programming with any one of the following: CAM, Conversational or G-Code (manual).</li>
<li>Ability read, interpret and apply product drawings, specifications and layouts.</li>
<li>Ability to make program adjustments if necessary.</li>
<li>Must be able to utilize calipers, micrometers or other types of measuring equipment.</li>
<li>Ensure parts conform to specifications.</li>
<li>Complete necessary documentation as required.</li>
<li>Work independently or within a small team.</li>
<li>Must be organized with good communication skills.</li>
<li>Work collaboratively with other departments to identify and resolve production issues.</li>
<li>Must be a “Team Player” and support company vision, mission and values.</li>
<li>Must be a US Citizen or lawful permanent resident (i.e. green card holder) <i>per ITAR requirement.</i>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Education: </b></p>
<ul><li>Education: 2 year degree in machine tool or equivalent work/apprenticeship experience.</li></ul>
<p><b>Preferences: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Experience producing High Tolerance parts (±0.001-0.0005”) for Aerospace, or Military sectors.</li>
<li>Experience with 4 axis CNC machining is a plus.</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Working knowledge of a variety of materials including titanium and high-nickel alloy metals are a plus.</li></ul>
