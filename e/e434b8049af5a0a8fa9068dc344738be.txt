<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Key Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Understand, document and troubleshoot the environments within which our products exist and perform</li>
<li>
Provide senior level technical leadership in support of Imagine’s systems and platforms (both internal and customer facing)</li>
<li>
Direct reporting on open issues and future engagements to both Imagine and customer senior leadership</li>
<li>
Perform hands on and/or lead troubleshooting of systems and networks both internally and at customer locations</li>
<li>
Work closely with internal and customer development, support, infrastructure, and managed services teams to drive resolution of issues and solutions</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Key Requirements
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>10+ years in technical roles – demonstrating progressively more senior and leadership responsibilities</li>
<li>
Must be comfortable working in and with a multitude of systems and networking technologies</li>
<li>
Understand how workflows, process, general networking and third party applications can impact applications.
</li>
<li>Strong background with application management, general networking and troubleshooting – Java, .Net, etc.</li>
<li>
Admin level understanding of Windows Server and Linux Operating Systems and environments</li>
<li>
Proficient with SQL Databases – Oracle and MS SQL a bonus</li>
<li>
Exceptional knowledge of all levels of infrastructure – switches, routers, storage, compute – both in physical and virtual environments</li>
<li>
Experienced working in one or more CSPs (Cloud Service Providers – e.g. AWS, Azure)</li>
<li>
Strong knowledge of monitoring, alerting, shared service architecture, and how these enable support and operations organizations</li>
<li>
Capable security practioner</li>
<li>
Must be adept at moving tactically while always thinking strategically – in challenging and fluid environments</li>
<li>
Excellent communicator – must be capable of communicating tech and tasks to all levels of the Imagine and its customers (from sysad to C suite)</li>
</ul>
