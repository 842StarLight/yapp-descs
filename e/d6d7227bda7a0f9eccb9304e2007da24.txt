<h2>Senior System Engineer - Fire and Life Safety Systems - Cypress CA</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Overview:</p>
<p></p>
<p><br>
Siemens is seeking a Senior Systems Engineer to support our Fire Solutions team in Cypress CA. This office-based role designs Life Safety Systems (focusing on fire alarms) utilizing company products to meet requirements of the Siemens, local authorities, UL, and NFPA Standards and Guidelines. These designs are created for the incorporation of other low voltage systems including security systems, card access systems, sound systems, nurse call, intercom systems, and CCTV/MATV systems as needed.</p>

Responsibilities:<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li> Designs fire detection and security systems and verifies documentation produced by a Drafter.</li>
<li> Verifies the sales estimate for equipment requirements and system design and configuration.</li>
<li> Ensures the implementation and utilization of the Engineering Best Practices.</li>
<li> Develops drawings, wiring diagrams, operating instructions, sequence of operations, and assembly or installation instruction for the installation and commissioning of systems.</li>
<li> Ensures technical accuracy, content, assembly, and coordination of technical submittals to architects, engineers, and authorities having jurisdiction. Maintains records relating to the design, installation, programming, and commissioning of the systems designed.</li>
<li> Provides technical support to field technicians in solving technical, programming, mechanical, or other problems relating to the commissioning of Life Safety systems.</li>
<li> Maintains library of technical information and other related data pertaining to all fire codes and standards, other local codes, and all systems available to the branch.</li>
<li> Communicates with Fire Departments and other local AHJs for system design and installation approvals.</li>
<li> Performs OTC (Over-The-Counter) plan reviews and plan submittals for projects.</li>
</ul>
