<div><b>Welcome to Sherwin-Williams</b></div>
<div>Founded in 1866, The Sherwin-Williams Company is a global leader in the manufacture, development, distribution, and sale of paints, coatings and related products to professional, industrial, commercial, and retail customers. The company manufactures products under well-known brands such as Sherwin-Williams®, Valspar®, HGTV HOME® by Sherwin-Williams, Dutch Boy®, Krylon®, Minwax®, Thompson’s® Water Seal®, Cabot® and many more. Sherwin-Williams® branded products are sold exclusively through a chain of more than 4,100 company-operated stores and facilities, while the company’s other brands are sold through leading mass merchandisers, home centers, independent paint dealers, hardware stores, automotive retailers, and industrial distributors. Sherwin-Williams supplies a broad range of highly-engineered industrial and OEM coatings for wood and general industrial, coil, packaging, protective and marine, and transportation applications worldwide. Our 60,000 employees are diverse, innovative and passionate. With a variety of rewarding and challenging opportunities, Sherwin-Williams is a great place to find a career that takes you places.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>POSITION SUMMARY:</b></div>
<div>As a Corporate Audit Intern within the Global Audit Department, you will have the opportunity to gain exposure to many of our operating business units, interact with leaders of the organization and positively impact change associated with the business’ existing initiatives and processes. You will be charged with properly managing risk associated to various projects as our team explores improvement opportunities all while establishing the foundation to grow within the Audit Department’s career path and the organization’s finance job family.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>CORE RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>Have access to gain visibility to a wide range of company processes, functional areas, and personnel. Assist with completion of Sarbanes-Oxley (SOX) annual assessment and execution of annual financial statement audit. Complete the following under the supervision of a team lead:</div>
<ul>
<li>Execute assigned transaction testing of account balances</li>
<li>
Conduct process walkthroughs to understand, learn, and assess risk within critical company processes</li>
<li>
Test controls over key financial business processes and communicate identified deficiencies to team lead
</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div>Gain exposure to a wide variety of work within the broader Internal Audit function, beyond SOX testing, including:</div>
<ul>
<li>Participation in a domestic audit of a Sherwin-Williams Plant, Distribution Center or Enterprise-wide business process</li>
<li>
Store Level Audits to understand and assess retail operations across the Company’s 4,500 stores</li>
<li>
Information Technology systems and related audit work</li>
<li>
Other special projects within the department that further align with financial and operational processes across the broader organization</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div>Personal Career Development</div>
<ul>
<li>Develop and maintain productive working relationships with business process personnel throughout the internship</li>
<li>
Be provided with a mentor to utilize as a resource and provide professional insights</li>
<li>
Obtain real-time coaching and development throughout the internship from team leads</li>
<li>
Use technology and resources to learn Sherwin-Williams’ business operations</li>
<li>
Adhere to the highest degree of professional standards and strict confidentiality</li>
<li>
Participate in Summer Experience intern programming events and final group presentation</li>
</ul>
