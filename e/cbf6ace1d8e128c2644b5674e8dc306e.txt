<p>Description</p>
</div>
<div>
<div>
<p>We are looking for a dynamic individual to join our team as a Log Editor at the corporate office in Camarillo, CA. This is a full-time position. Duties and Responsibilities include:</p>
<ul><li>Daily reconciliation of broadcast commercial logs for stations</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manage commercial inventory and complete the daily logs for multiple stations</li></ul>
<ul><li>Communications with stations to ensure instructions are clear and appropriately reflected in system</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintain electronic filing systems</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintain rapport with sales executives and management</li></ul>
<ul><li>Complete projects within deadlines</li></ul>
<ul><li>Abide by organizational procedures and support organizational goals</li></ul>
<ul><li>Remain calm and professional through stressful circumstances</li></ul>
<ul><li>Regular and timely attendance</li></ul>
<ul><li>Order and Material entry</li></ul>
<ul><li>Other duties as assigned</li></ul>
<p>
Minimum Qualifications:</p>
<ul><li>High School diploma required; College Degree preferred</li></ul>
<ul><li>Requires working knowledge of Microsoft Word, Excel and Outlook software</li></ul>
<ul><li>Working knowledge of WideOrbit Traffic software a plus</li></ul>
<ul><li>Requires excellent communication and interpersonal skills</li></ul>
<ul><li>Requires highly motivated team player capable of working in a fast-paced environment</li></ul>
<ul><li>High level of analytical and problem solving skills and an ability to work on multiple tasks and prioritize in order to meet deadlines.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Traffic experience a plus</li></ul>
<p>
Salem Media Group offers a competitive salary and a comprehensive benefit package as well as 10 paid holidays and paid vacation. Salem Media Group is an equal opportunity employer</p>
