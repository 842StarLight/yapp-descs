<h2>Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>JOB SUMMARY:</b></p>
<p>As an Account Executive, you drive the standard that every interaction is unique, and the well-being of the customer is paramount. You recognize that relationships matter and that the key to any relationship is to truly understand the customer’s perspective. At Atronix, we ensure our focus is understanding the needs of our customers and you’ll be instrumental in making this happen.</p>
<p>Your primary role will be to identify and sell control systems and software systems to end users and systems integrators.</p>
<p>Your role will entail driving business development and sales to increase market share by developing successful partnerships with new and existing Atronix customers. As a key collaborator, you’ll build relationships across the business, including with our marketing team and other internal resources. You’ll serve as the main point of contact with our customers. This role gathers initial customer needs and collaborates with Atronix engineering and solution development teams to provide the best solution to the customer.</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Proactively identify controls &amp; software needs with new and existing customers</li>
<li>Identify, create, and implement sales strategies focused on growing existing business, accessing new opportunities within existing accounts, and pursuing new accounts.</li>
<li>Work collaboratively with engineering, solutions and peers to develop the best solutions for the client</li>
<li>Travel to customer sites in order to engage with and understand their needs</li>
<li>Manage execution of strategic sales plan including prioritization of activities.</li>
<li>Perform other duties as may be assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree (engineering or business a plus)</li>
<li>Minimum 1-year experience in a Technical Sales role</li>
<li>3-5 years’ experience in the automated material handling industry or related industrial businesses</li>
<li>Experience and understanding of PLC’s, HMI’s, control systems, etc.</li>
<li>Ability to travel when required</li>
</ul>
<p><b>SKILLS/ABILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Possess or obtain intimate knowledge of products, services, distribution, customers, and competitors</li>
<li>Strong self and social confidence and tenacity; polished &amp; persuasive communicator and presenter</li>
<li>Solid written and verbal communication skills combined with executive presence</li>
<li>Robust analytical and strategic planning and execution skills</li>
<li>Creative decision making and problem-solving skills</li>
<li>Taking initiative and working with limited direction</li>
<li>Understanding of the competitive landscape</li>
<li>Strategic selling</li>
<li>CRM familiarity</li>
</ul>
<p><b>WORK ENVIRONMENT</b>: This job operates in a professional office environment. This role routinely uses standard office equipment such as computers, phones, etc.</p>
<p><b>TRAVEL</b>: travel will be required for this position.</p>
