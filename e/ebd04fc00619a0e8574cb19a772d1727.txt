<h2>CHANNEL MARKETING ASSOCIATE</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Description:</b><br>
<b>Core business hours:</b> 8:30am - 5:00pm
<br><b>Gross Wage:</b> $21.00 per hour, payable Bi-Weekly
<br><b>Benefits:</b> Medical, Dental, Vision, FSA, Life &amp; AD&amp;D, Sup. Life, 401K, 12 Holidays, Vacation, Sick time
<br><br>
Primary role is to create customized channel and customer promotions, products and packaging (based on understanding of channel shoppers and customers) and associated sales presentations/collateral for customer sell-in by account Sales Managers.
<br><br>
<b>Essential Duties:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Product/Package Opportunity Analysis. Conduct market product and packaging “gap analysis” (using syndicated data and</li>
</ul>
visual store checks) to determine opportunities at individual accounts.
<br><ul>
<li>Product Packaging/Program Customization. Development of channel, customer customized programs and packaging configurations with assigned members of the Sales Team through Oracle processes.</li>
<li>Profitability Analysis. Analysis of new items and displays for cost and profitability in a specific customer or channel per company profitability goals.</li>
<li>Project Management. Manage projects from idea to commercialization interacting with cross-functional team using company project management system.</li>
<li>Packaging and Display Execution. Develop and manage execution of proposed new item mixes and new custom displays including graphics, spec information, product mix, item#, and UPC.</li>
<li>Sales Collateral Development. Develop sales collateral to help sell-in of new programs, products and promotions.</li>
<li>Manage and generate proposed new item samples and mock ups as it pertains to assigned channel of the business working with outside vendors and Pentel Buyer</li>
<li>Sales Partner Collaboration. Partner with sales team to attend and participate in new product POG and BTS account meetings and other account meetings as necessary</li>
<li>Consumer Promotion Development. Participation in strategic development of new promotions, new product mix ideas and participation in new product naming brainstorming meetings</li>
<li>Sales Presentations. Compose PowerPoint presentations for sales account meetings and marketing department presentations as requested</li>
<li>Cross-Functional Coordination. Coordinate, execute and manage marketing details of account accepted new item and display programs with a wide range of cross-functional departments internally and externally</li>
<li>Attendance and punctuality are an essential job duty</li>
</ul>
<b>WORK ENVIRONMENT:</b> Office.

</ul>
