<h2>Software Configuration and Implementation - Technical Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>InTouch Health is a leading telehealth solution company enabling remote physician care anytime, anywhere. Our solutions are robust, secure, and provide excellent physician-patient experiences to maximize care and minimize response time, and ensure the right doctor makes the right treatment decision every time.</p>
<p>
Under the general direction of the Sr. Director, Software &amp; Configuration Management, the Software Implementation Project Manager will be responsible for managing all aspects of implementation and service projects for InTouch products and the organization. The Software Implementation Project Manager collaborates with customers and leads Software Implementation Project Teams on planning, scheduling and execution of all Software deliverables within the scope of the project or program.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Lead, facilitate, coordinate, and track day-to- day activities required to ensure the project is completed on time, successfully, and in a manner consistent with organizational goals, departmental policies, established processes, and/or the standard terms and conditions of the contract</li>
<li>
Full understanding of how to implement and manage ITH integration products</li>
<li>
Create and use of project plans for assigned projects, which include clear milestones and assignment of project task responsibilities</li>
<li>
Successfully manage the scope of the project, including customer priorities</li>
<li>
Demonstrate a proactive approach to identify risks to project success; develop effective action plans for resolution</li>
<li>
Appropriately manage / escalate project issues or risks</li>
<li>
Manage customer expectations of product and services</li>
<li>
Provide leadership and direction for customers and the project team</li>
<li>
Demonstrate effective communication skills, verbal and written, with customers, project teams and leadership</li>
<li>
Ensure customer satisfaction</li>
<li>
Demonstrate ability of leading project teams and act as an effective team leader for all matrixed staff</li>
<li>
Accurately forecasting current and future quarter revenue for each assigned project</li>
<li>
Use of all governance tools (scope document, project status reports, risk and change logs, tollgates, etc.), for all assigned projects, to effectively manage all projects</li>
<li>
Provide constructive performance appraisal input through peer review process</li>
<li>
Technically support cross-functional (internal &amp; external) teams and customers through the technical implementation of ITH products (for all software engagements that may include hardware configurations)</li>
<li>
Lead technical discussions with clients and internal stakeholders regarding integrations/interfaces (HL7 standards, Integration Engines and VPN Connectivity)</li>
<li>
Support implementations in system HL7 messaging (Implementation/Conversion/Projects) integrations with 3rd party EMR's, systems, applications, etc.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Knowledge, Skills &amp; Ability</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Demonstrated knowledge of industry-recognized project management methodologies</li>
<li>
Possesses healthcare project management experience and demonstrates thorough knowledge and understanding of project management methodologies and relevant clinical and IT operations</li>
<li>
Thoroughly understands customer acute care, post-acute care, and community-based care workflow</li>
<li>
Demonstrated understanding of implementation project governance expectations (scope management, tollgate, and risk &amp; change management processes)</li>
<li>
Has responsibility of orders, medium or large sized projects or teams</li>
<li>
Project scope requires significant impact to customer workflow (multi-location)</li>
<li>
Manages project scope of multiple InTouch products</li>
<li>
Responsible for maintaining project schedule and timeline</li>
<li>
Compliant with forecasting and backlog management processes</li>
<li>
Participates in pre-sale process for medium or large size projects</li>
<li>
Provides coaching, mentoring, and training for project team and peers</li>
<li>
Demonstrates effective communication and engagement with customer</li>
<li>
Able to effectively work across multiple departments (e.g. distributor, operations, sales, finance)</li>
<li>
Ensures complete project closure including, customer acknowledgement, equipment reconciliation, and transition to Technical Assistance Center (TAC)</li>
<li>
Demonstrates the professional and soft skills necessary to be an effective project manager</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to train physicians and other clinicians</li>
<li>
Proficiency with email, Web browsers, Wi-Fi and cloud applications</li>
<li>
Ability to interact with internal and external stakeholders at all levels and able to lead technical discussions to influence decisions or come to resolution</li>
<li>
High-level understanding of HL7 standards, integration engines (including EMRs) and VPN connectivity, A MUST</li>
<li>
Ability to understand workflows and use cases to optimize to technical implementation process</li>
</ul>

Knowledge of hospital administrative and procurement processes for the public and private sectors</li>
