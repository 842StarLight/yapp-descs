About H-E-B</b><div>
</div>
H-E-B is one of the largest, independently owned food retailers in the nation operating over 400 stores throughout Texas and Mexico, with annual sales generating over $25 billion. Described by industry experts as a daring innovator and smart competitor, H-E-B has led the way with creative new concepts, outstanding service and a commitment to diversity in our workforce, workplace and marketplace. H-E-B offers a wealth of career opportunities to our 109,000+ Partners (employees), competitive compensation and benefits program and comprehensive training that lead to successful careers.<div></div>
<br>
<div>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>
In addition to our stores, HEB operates 13 manufacturing Plants across Bakery, Dairy, Snacks, Meat Processing, Meal Prep, Floral, Print and Packaging industries.</div>

<br>

<div></div>
<div>Do you have a:</div>
<div>HEART FOR PEOPLE... ability to lead / influence stakeholders?</div>
<div>HEAD FOR BUSINESS... expertise to collaborate / lead with technically diverse, cross-functional project teams?</div>
<div>PASSION FOR RESULTS... drive to champion a strategic technology roadmap that can transform H-E-B Manufacturing?</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>We are looking for:</div>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of a Bachelor’s degree in an Engineering or Computer Science field with at least 10 years of experience working in MES and Controls systems, preferably in Food &amp; Beverage manufacturing.</li>
<li>
Five or more years of experience working as a Developer or Integrator of MES systems or Controls systems, both is preferred.</li>
<li>
Project management experience required.</li>
<li>
Experience in implementing MES solutions and experience in integrating these systems with ERP technology. Experience integrating with Cloud solutions preferred.</li>
<li>
Experience in the adoption and application of ISA-95 and connecting enterprise IT systems to shop-floor systems.</li>
<li>
Experience in database design, development, and support.</li>
<li>
Demonstrated leadership skills and experience working in diverse, multi-disciplined work groups.</li>
<li>
Familiarity with Wonder Ware and GE Plant Apps considered an advantage.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div>What you’ll do:</div>
<ul>
<li>You will recruit, coach and grow high performing MES and Controls engineers, supporting operations across 13 diverse food and bev plants.</li>
<li>
You will focus on your team’s technical skills and delivery but also be able to participate in the development of in-Plant maintenance teams.</li>
<li>
You will provide project management for your team and take ownership of your team's delivery.</li>
<li>
You will manage third party systems integrator’s quality and ensure that H-E-B can be self-sustaining in MES and Controls.</li>
<li>
You will drive iterative and on-time delivery of software.</li>
<li>
You'll guide team members to make sound technical decisions.</li>
<li>
You will guide your engineers to deliver technology solutions to improve user experience and drive business value</li>
<li>
You will champion engineering best practices and innovation such as code reviews, test automation, test lab design, agile methodology, secure coding practices and use of Cloud capabilities.</li>
<li>
You'll identify recurring issues and opportunities to improve the engineering process, team success, and business success.</li>
<li>
You will change the Manufacturing Division’s use of technology and lead a reinvention of how we feed Texas with H-E-B’s signature Own Brand products.</li>
</ul>
