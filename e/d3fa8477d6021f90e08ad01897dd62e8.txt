Responsibilities:·</p>

<li>
Demonstrating and clearly differentiating Syncrofy with a focus on our strong and compelling product.</li>
<li>
Driving new product sales.</li>
<li>
Experience in SaaS sales with medium and small size businesses.</li>
<li>
Ambitious individual who embraces the growth mindset and change of a startup.</li>
<li>
Forecasting and delivering against substantial revenue targets and achieve the assigned sales volume.</li>
<li>
Identifying new business opportunities and follow-up the defined business opportunities.</li>
<li>
Working with various leads in order to maintain and expand CoE’s business within assigned customer accounts.</li>
<li>
Responsible for expertly prospecting, selling and closing new business to new and existing customers.</li>
<li>
Expertly land, adopt, expand, and deepen sales opportunities.</li>
<li>
Working with Marketing and Business Development teams to drive lead generation and awareness.</li>

Enterprise sales professionals with a hunter mentality.</li>
