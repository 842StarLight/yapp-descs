<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>We continue to experience rapid growth as we invest in resources and create new opportunities, as a result, we have exciting opportunities for a Recruiter to join our Talent Acquisition team. This position will work with Hiring Managers supporting the Sales, Manufacturing, and Engineering departments within the organization. This is a fast paced environment. Seeking a background of agency and corporate experience with an emphasis on technical recruiting.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>A strong relationship builder and influencer with candidates and hiring managers alike</li>
<li>
Work with our hiring managers to understand their needs to accurately define the role we are looking to fill; crafting a process that will lead us to the right hire in a timely, efficient manner</li>
<li>
Source, recruit, conduct interviews, checks references, qualify and promotes best fit candidates for a wide range of roles</li>
<li>
Effectively source candidates using internet search engines, recruitment tools, and social media websites
</li>
<li>Participate in intake meetings with recruiters to understand the business and specific capabilities we are looking to hire</li>
<li>
Research companies and industry trends to stay abreast of new potential sourcing channels. Conduct competitive intelligence research related to recruitment and staffing</li>
<li>
Develop talent pipelines for future hiring needs</li>
<li>
Promote our company brand online and offline</li>
<li>
Maintain candidate databases (e.g. via our Applicant Tracking System)</li>
<li>
Create a positive candidate experience</li>
<li>
Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree in Human Resources, Business Administration or other related field</li>
<li>
Minimum 10 years of full lifecycle recruiting experience including at least 3 years as a corporate recruiter supporting a complex sales, engineering organization</li>
<li>
A solid understanding of a recruitment lifecycle including headhunting (via LinkedIn, calling and referrals), advertising, selection, assessment, offer management and on boarding.</li>
<li>
Must have 5 years of proven experience in influencing and partnering with business leaders; experience in a complex business environment with multiple entities is a plus</li>
<li>
Have worked across different verticals including engineering, product, design, marketing, business systems</li>
<li>
Ability to work with highly sensitive material and possess good business judgment as it pertains to confidential information
</li>
<li>A strong sense of organization and detail for task follow-up</li>
<li>
Ability to communicate with all levels of staff/management
</li>
<li>Ability to work well under pressure to meet deadlines and adapt to changing priorities
</li>
<li>Strong privatization and multitasking skills</li>
<li>
Ability to positively present our company and open roles to potential candidates</li>
<li>
Works with general direction regarding tasks to be performed while possessing a drive to succeed and work independently.
</li>
<li>Develop relationships with candidates, network groups, social media involvement to generate an applicant pool that meets business needs.
</li>
<li>Improvement of action plans</li>
<li>
Experience with iCIMS a plus, experience with an ATS required</li>
<li>
Understanding of employment law, compliance, and an interest in contributing to building a best in class recruiting function is required.</li>
<li>
Microsoft Office proficiency (Outlook, Excel, Word and PowerPoint)<br>
</li>
</ul>
