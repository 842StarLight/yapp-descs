<h2>Network Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>General Summary:</b></p>
<p>
The Network Analyst is responsible for all onsite service and support needs for our customers. This relates to all technology, to include: phone systems, workstations, servers, printers, switches, routers, networks, and vendor specific hardware and software. Regular work hours are Monday – Friday 8 am to 5 pm. Night, weekend and on call work will be required as the business need dictates.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Job Functions:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Analyze, design, install, configure, monitor, maintain and repair all server systems and network devices including SAN, thin clients, printers, switches, routers and handheld computers to ensure optimum operation.</li>
<li>Provide IT support for any technical environment – software or hardware platforms such as Microsoft, Citrix, VMWare, Mail Applications, etc.</li>
<li>Provide support for Microsoft related technologies: Windows Server, Exchange, Active directory, etc.</li>
<li>Implement DRN Backup product and disaster recovery solutions.</li>
<li>Perform server maintenance such as patching, upgrading, rebooting, active directory maintenance, security permissions and other related server activities.</li>
<li>Work closely with IS team members, aiding where needed.</li>
<li>Perform network and general data center maintenance.</li>
<li>Research and analyze new software solutions as needed.</li>
<li>Project manage projects for managed service customer installs of new software or hardware. Ensure delivery of timely results that meet customers’ timeline and budget needs.</li>
<li>Track and maintain hardware and software licensing as well as ensuring continuity of software support contracts.<br>
Involved in regular internal IT projects for DRN.</li>
<li>Assist in protecting DRN’s network via Cyber Security policies, processes and procedures.</li>
<li>Enforce security policy in daily activities as it relates to the IS team functions.</li>
<li>Document IT processes and information as implemented</li>
<li>Goal &amp; team focused<br>
Attendance is an essential function of this position.</li>
<li>Regular, reliable, consistent and punctual attendance is required.</li>
<li>Responsible for all other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Knowledge of company policies, procedures, products and services.</li>
<li>Knowledge of Microsoft Office Suites</li>
<li>Knowledge of Microsoft Active Directory, Exchange Server, IIS, Sharepoint server, System Center Configuration Manager, Terminal Services</li>
<li>Knowledge of the following protocols/standards: SMTP, POP, FTP, DNS, DHCP</li>
<li>Ability to create and adapt in a fast-paced environment.</li>
<li>Ability to communicate with customers, co-workers and various business contacts in a professional and courteous manner, keeping them informed of progress, notifying them of impending changes or scheduled outages, etc.</li>
<li>Ability to organize and prioritize multiple work assignments.</li>
<li>Ability to make sound decisions using information at hand.</li>
<li>Ability to develop in-depth knowledge of the service catalog and how it relates to customer’s needs.</li>
<li>Ability to keep current with future technologies emerging in the industry by attending conferences, reviewing IT publications and online materials, etc.</li>
<li>Ability to document internal processes and procedures related to duties and responsibilities.</li>
<li>Ability to develop in-depth knowledge of the service catalog and how it relates to customers’ needs.</li>
<li>Ability to contribute in a sales environment.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Education, Experience and Qualifications:</b></p>
<p>
BS in Information Technology or related computer degree. 3-5 years of working experience in a Network and Server environment.</p>
