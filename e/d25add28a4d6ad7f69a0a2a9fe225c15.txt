<h2>RVP Sales & Marketing - Hospitality</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>BASIC FUNCTIONS: </b>Work with property General Managers and DOSM's for total property top line revenue generation and sales and marketing expense management. Oversee and support the Directors of Sales and Marketing and property-based sales teams. Insure basic effective selling processes are in place:</p>
<ul>
<li>First contact with the customer and effective needs analysis</li>
<li>Matching customer needs with features and benefits</li>
<li>Effective selling skill implementation</li>
<li>Effective sales correspondence</li>
</ul>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS: EDUCATION, KNOWLEDGE, TRAINING &amp; WORK EXPERIENCE</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or equivalent</li>
<li>College degree or Associates degree with experience preferred</li>
<li>Minimum ten years experience in the hospitality field with overseeing multiple (5-10) properties. Independent and/or luxury hotel experience strongly preferred.</li>
<li>Excellent written and oral communication skills</li>
<li>Strong supervisory and personnel management experience</li>
<li>Willing to be entrepreneurial and take risks</li>
<li>Ability to handle multiple ongoing projects in a fast-paced team environment</li>
<li>Ability to work independently on projects and also collaborate as a strong team member</li>
<li>Excellent attention to detail, flexible and adaptable to change</li>
<li>Ability to operate under pressure and meet or exceed deadlines</li>
<li>Excellent organizational, time-management and analytical skills</li>
<li>Imagination and creativity skills, focusing on improving financial performance through aggressive sales and marketing initiatives</li>
<li>Positive, outgoing and self-motivating individual with pleasant personality</li>
<li>Ability to maintain a professional manner and appearance in all situations</li>
</ul>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Quarterly Visits to each property to coincide with the Quarterly Individual Sales Managers Reviews</li>
<li>Active Participation of all DOSM annual performance reviews</li>
<li>Ongoing attendance of 1 - 2 sales meetings monthly for each property (via telephone or during quarterly visit)</li>
<li>Ongoing review of GRC and conversions of prospect/tentative to definite</li>
<li>Participate in scheduled ongoing outside sales calls with property-based sales managers during quarterly visits</li>
<li>Review of sales correspondence</li>
<li>Review of sales contracts to insure proper adherence to cancellation and attrition policies</li>
<li>Participation in STAR lead goal setting and monitor ongoing results</li>
<li>Insure effective pattern and rate management</li>
<li>Review individual sales productivity monthly</li>
<li>Review and monitor booking pace</li>
<li>Proactively assist with recruiting of DOSM and all property sales positions</li>
<li>Insure ongoing daily involvement of DOSM in the sales process</li>
<li>Review annual marketing plan and budgets and property reforecasts</li>
<li>Conduct Annual Sales and Marketing Operational Review</li>
<li>Ensure follow-up on all sales, revenue management and DMPE operational reviews</li>
<li>Monitoring of monthly marketing action plans</li>
<li>Insure proper lost and turn away business reporting</li>
<li>Monitor and insure branding compliance</li>
<li>Oversee support and assist with proper revenue management at the property level</li>
<li>Interact with Ownership as appropriate</li>
<li>Interact with Regional VP's of Operations as required</li>
<li>Approve sales goals and incentive plan calculations</li>
<li>Provide sales training oversight</li>
<li>Develop strategies to teach and increase individual sales manager performance</li>
<li>Ensure effective advertising/direct mail campaigns and measurement tools are in place</li>
<li>Ensure an effective public relations program is in place</li>
<li>Ensure all properties are in compliance with Our Town Hospitality and Sotherly Hotels standards</li>
<li>Ensure effective electronic marketing campaigns and measurement tools are in place</li>
<li>Oversee and support property customer relationship management program</li>
</ul>
