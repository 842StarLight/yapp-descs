<p><b>Job Description Summary:
</b></p>This position will play a key role in growing carrier participation in our loyalty program. The primary responsibility will be creating relationships with Carriers in a meaningful way that encourages growth and retention. The incumbent will work to drive faster adoption, higher ultimate usage and greater retention in the program which will impact the organization to increase benefit realization, value creation, and the achievement of results and outcomes.
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Description:
</b></p>
<p><b>Duties:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Contact new and existing leads to build effective relationships to sell them on the usage of JBH Loyalty/Perks program and maintain a robust pipeline that will drive carrier retention.
</li>
<li>Inform carriers about the benefits of the program and assist them through the sign-up process for discounts/services to help carriers save time and money.
</li>
<li>Analyze data using query reports on carriers in pipeline to better understand carrier needs to improve the sales process and assist with retention.
</li>
<li>Create lead generation strategy for building and maintaining pipeline of carriers from our internal database and contacts.
</li>
<li>Assists carriers in resolving issues by ensuring the carrier connects with the right department to research and implement a solution.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>The successful candidate for this role:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Uses his/her time effectively and efficiently; values time; concentrates his/her efforts on the more important priorities; gets more done in less time than others; can attend to a broader range of activities.
</li>
<li>Can be counted on to exceed goals successfully; is constantly and consistently one of the top performers; very bottom-line oriented; steadfastly pushes self and others for results.
</li>
<li>Is dedicated to meeting the expectations and requirements of internal and external customers; gets first-hand customer information and uses it for improvements in products and services; acts with customers in mind; establishes and maintains effective relationships with customers and gains their trust and respect.
</li>
</ul>
