<h2>Drafting Technician</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div><div><div><div>
<p>Electronic Contracting Company, one of the leading systems integration firms in the Midwest, is looking for a qualified drafter to expand its engineering department. We want candidates who are driven and can help continue our company's upwards growth.<br>
<br>
Our technicians draft a wide variety of low voltage systems such as: Access Control, Audio/Visual Systems, Fire Alarm, Nurse Call, Sound Reinforcement, &amp; Video Surveillance. As a new hire, you’ll train with our lead drafting technician, as well as the department manager on a multitude of different systems to learn the basics. Once you learn the ropes, you’ll start drafting projects from start to finish. You'll become a familiar face by interacting with our other dedicated departments such as install, sales, purchasing, shipping/receiving, and custom/service. You'll help our sales team with preliminary drafting design, as well as completed submittal drawings. Sound like a fit? Here’s what the job entails:</p>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Requirements</b></h4>
<ul>
<li> Proven work experience as a drafter or similar role</li>
<li> Associates Degree in CAD/Drafting or 2+ years experience in using CAD software</li>
<li> Hands-on experience with computer-aided design (CAD) software</li>
<li> Strong problem solving skills</li>
<li> Ability to manage several tasks at a time and effectively prioritize duties</li>
<li> Ability to handle and meet deadlines</li>
<li> Willing to work overtime/weekends when necessary</li>
</ul>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Benefits</b></h4>
<ul>
<li> Health, Dental, &amp; Vision Insurance</li>
<li> Paid Vacations</li>
<li> Paid Holidays</li>
<li> 401K/Profit Sharing</li>
</ul>
