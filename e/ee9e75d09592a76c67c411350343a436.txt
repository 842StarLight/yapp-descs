<h2>Outside Sales Representative/Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Outside Sales Representative/Account Manager</b></p>
<p><b>Lapham-Hickey Steel Corporation </b>is seeking career-minded focus to join our team as an OUTSIDE SALES REPRESENTATIVE/ACCOUNTAGE MANAGER in the INDIANAPOLIS INDIANA area. We are looking for a candidate that shares our vision of creating value for our customers, employees, suppliers and shareholders through the highest quality steel products and strong partnerships. We are a family owned, financially secure company with a storied history dating back to 1926. Lapham-Hickey’s business has been built by nurturing long-standing relationships with our customers. Those relationships have afforded us the opportunity to service customers for over 90 years. We support this vision through our values; The R.I.T.E. Way (Respect, Integrity, Teamwork, Excellence).</p>
<p><b>Purpose/Summary: </b> The Outside Sales Representative/Account Manager will present and sell LHS products and services to existing and potential customers. This entails maintaining and building positive business relationships as a representative of our organization. Customer service, problem-resolution, public-speaking, presentation skills and excellent follow up is a must. Ability to travel assigned territory covering southwest and central Missouri and southeast Kansas. Salary to commensurate experience and skills. Ideal candidate will meet the following criteria outlined below.</p>
<p><b>Primary Responsibilities: </b></p>
<p>1. Present and sell company products and services to current and potential clients.</p>
<p>2. Prepare action plans and schedules to identify specific targets and to project the number of contacts to be made.</p>
<p>3. Follow up on new leads and referrals resulting from field and marketing activities.</p>
<p>4. Identify sales prospects and contact these and other accounts as assigned.</p>
<p>5. Prepare presentations, proposals and sales contracts.</p>
<p>6. Develop and maintain sales materials and current product knowledge.</p>
<p>7. Establish and maintain current client and potential client relationships.</p>
<p>8. Prepare paperwork to activate and maintain contract services.</p>
<p>9. Manage account services through quality checks and other follow-up.</p>
<p>10. Identify and resolve client concerns.</p>
<p>11. Prepare a variety of status reports, including activity, closings, follow-up, and adherence to goals.</p>
<p>12. Communicate new product and service opportunities, special developments, information, or feedback gathered through field activity to appropriate company staff.</p>
<p>13. Coordinate company staff to accomplish the work required to close sales.</p>
<p>14. Develop and implement special sales activities to reduce stock.</p>
<p>15. Be present at sales meetings in a timely manner</p>
<p>16. Be present for all training sessions that are required make optimal use of time during working hours</p>
<p>17. Other duties as assigned.</p>
<p>Additional Responsibilities:</p>
<p>1. Participate in marketing events such as seminars, trade shows, and telemarketing events.</p>
<p>2. Follow-up for collection of payment.</p>
<p>3. Coordinate shipping schedules and delivery of merchandise and services.</p>
<p>4. Provide on-the-job training to new sales employees.</p>
<p>5. respect the policies and written directives of management</p>
<p>6. Display a positive attitude and maintain a cooperative working relationship with co-workers</p>
<p>7. conduct him/herself in a professional manner at all times</p>
<p>8. respect and protect company information and owner privacy.</p>
<p>Job Requirements:</p>
<p>1. Bachelor’s Degree in business, marketing or related program.</p>
<p>2. Ability to travel assigned territory covering southwest and central Missouri and southeast Kansas.</p>
<p>3. Ability to persuade and influence others.</p>
<p>4. Ability to develop and deliver presentations.</p>
<p>5. Ability to create, compose, and edit written materials.</p>
<p>6. Strong interpersonal and communication skills. Knowledge of advertising and sales promotion techniques.</p>
<p>7. Visibility requires maintaining a professional appearance and providing a positive company image to the public.</p>
<p>8. Work requires significant local travel to current and potential clients. This requires the possession of a valid state drivers license within 60 days of employment.</p>
<p>9. Work requires willingness to work a flexible schedule and occasional overnight travel.</p>
<p><b>Working Conditions: </b>Normal for an office environment. Requires significant local travel and may require occasional overnight travel and weekend and/or evening work.</p>
<p>Resumes should accompany completed application. No phone-calls please. We encourage you to learn more about our organization, please visit: https://www.lapham-hickey.com/.</p>
