<h2>Customer Service Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Particle is looking for an Customer Service Associate to join our Operations team in Las Vegas. As a Customer Service Associate at Particle, you will be responsible for coordinating shipments, invoicing and otherwise making sure our business customers are happy. You will focus on meeting quoted deadlines, ensuring the customer experience is positive and fulfillment operations move smoothly.
<br><br>
We are an Internet of Things (IOT) startup making Wi-Fi and cellular components for product creators of internet connected products (among other things). We are growing exponentially and we have seen our orders more than double in the last six months!
<br><br>
The Customer Service Associate job is exciting opportunity because it is the front line of our operations. You will be the face of Particle to our most important customers and you will directly enable a large variety of product creators to build various IOT products (from fitbits for cows to rainwater monitoring systems)! This job is also an excellent intro to the tech industry as you will need to work well with every part of the company (engineering, sales and customer success among others) in the course of your work.
<br><br>
Investment in our people is critical to who we are as a company. From extensive onboarding to 360 feedback to your very own Culture Ambassador, you'll have the tools navigate your own success from the start. From there, you'll work with your manager to create your own growth path and the coaching to achieve it.
<br><br>
Requirements
<br>------------
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>College degree or 2+ years of work experience in customer service or logistics</li>
<li>Currently in Las Vegas area or willing to relocate</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Office, Google Suite</li>
<li>Working knowledge of online tools and systems - Microsoft Office, G-mail, G-docs, G-sheets, online customer support systems and CRM systems</li>
</ul>
Responsibilities
<br>----------------
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Place orders for resellers, sales/enterprise contacts and other customers</li>
<li>Correctly and quickly invoice customers</li>
<li>Answer customer questions regarding products, lead times, pricing, order status and other issues</li>
<li>Perform various A/R tasks</li>
</ul>
Ideal Candidate
<br>---------------
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Customer oriented. You are excited to help people and willing to go out of your way to make experiences exceptional for our customers.</li>
<li>Action-oriented. You can get things done. You can move quickly and smartly. You love to set challenging goals and then develop plans to deliver and achieve them.</li>
<li>Organized and efficient. You can communicate clearly and concisely. The moment you see a problem, you quickly come up with solutions and then implement them in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Flexible. You are excited to wear a lot of hats and to learn new things (even if they are out of your particular job description). You are a team player.</li>
<li>Self sufficient/Independent. You can use our tools to investigate things yourself. You love learning even if it's outside your specific scope.</li>
<li>Fun. We're looking for a good fit on our team. If you don't think photos of Kevin's dogs in parkas are funny, then this is not the team for you.</li>
</ul>
