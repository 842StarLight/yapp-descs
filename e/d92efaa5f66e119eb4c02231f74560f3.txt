<h2>Sr. Partner Marketing Manager, Demand Generation</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<ul>
<li>6+ years of related experience in demand generation, marketing communications, product marketing or channel marketing,</li>
<li>Technical writing experience.</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in marketing, business, communications, or relevant field of study.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<div>Are you looking for a role that challenges both your creative skills as well as your analytical abilities? If so, look no further! Amazon Web Services (AWS) is seeking an experienced marketing manager to build creative solution campaigns that increase AWS product and service adoption through the AWS partner ecosystem..<br>
<br>
In this role you will be responsible for designing and delivering solution and use case focused integrated campaigns with strategic partners, including creating the campaign strategy and driving execution from from creation to completion. You will identify the needs and goals of the program and build related content/messaging to generate marketing and sales qualified leads for AWS and our partner ecosystem. You will work with AWS Product Management, Product Marketing and Services teams to identify focus areas and work with select partners who can deliver solution offerings to our customers. You will effectively project manage the campaign through multiple marketing channels, including website, online advertising, digital advertising, email, social media, etc. You will develop a deep expertise in Amazon’s products, policies and processes and be capable of both efficient and accurate campaign execution as well as identifying opportunities to improve our workflows and implement best practices across verticals.<br>
<br>
The ideal candidate will have a proven track record of working with cross-functional teams and managing multiple high-profile marketing projects simultaneously. The role requires the ability to analyze campaign effectiveness, modify campaigns based on conclusions, and clearly articulate results to stakeholders.<br>
<br>
Key Responsibilities:<br>
<ul>
<li>
<li>Work with Product and Services teams to develop solution based campaign strategy with select AWS Partners.</li>
<li>
<li>Develop detailed campaign project plans, including campaign goals, messaging, call to action, marketing distribution channels, and global campaign schedule.</li>
<li>
<li>Facilitate the creation and approval of compelling and effective copy that fits the appropriate audience and marketing channel while maintaining Amazon Web Services’ brand and voice highlighting the unique value proposition of each APN Partner participating in the campaign.</li>
<li>
<li>Oversee concept and creative development of campaign materials.</li>
<li>
<li>Manage the webinar channel for the campaigns.</li>
<li>
<li>Forecast, track, and report campaign effectiveness metrics as well as overall ROI to internal stakeholders. Plus, continually monitor, analyze and tune campaigns to maximize visit-to-registration conversion.</li>
<li>
<li>Work closely with Partner Team, AWS Segment leads, Product Marketing, Design, Digital, and Web teams to meet deadlines and execute campaign to your specifications.</li>
</ul>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<ul>
<li>Proven success in managing complex and multi-faceted marketing programs with the ability to drive projects through completion. Strong project management skills and experience, including managing internal stakeholders and external agencies, and multiple campaigns and projects at once.</li>
<li>Analytical tendencies with a natural curiosity to measure, test, learn and iterate in order to get the best results possible.</li>
<li>Experience with Marketing Automation Platform and CRM systems; Salesforce.com and Marketo is preferable.</li>
<li>Superior oral and written communication skills with an innate attention to detail.</li>
</ul>
