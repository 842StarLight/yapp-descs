<p><b>
RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<p>
Phalen Leadership Academies is seeking a savvy wordsmith and marketing genius to join our team! The Digital Marketing Coordinator works across the PLA Network to support headquarters and school campuses in multiple locations in internal and external marketing and communications. Candidates must have a gift and love for writing, a comprehensive understanding of multi-media storytelling, experience in content creation to achieve organizational goals, and a desire to make a difference. This role requires a high level of creativity, attention to detail, and strong project management skills.</p>
<p><b>
REQUIREMENTS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
2-3 years digital marketing experience</li>
<li>
Organized and self-motivated</li>
<li>
Strong writing skills required- blog posts, press release, or similar</li>
<li>
Solid business, technical, and problem-solving aptitude</li>
<li>
Strong skills in Microsoft Office and experience with project management software</li>
<li>
Excellent writing, editing, and proofreading skills</li>
<li>
Some experience with Adobe Creative Suite or similar design suites a plus (Photoshop, InDesign)</li>
<li>
Strong collaborator, able to forge productive working relationships inside and outside the organization</li>
</ul>
