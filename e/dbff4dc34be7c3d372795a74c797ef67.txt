<h2>Associate Software Product Analyst Co-op</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>OVERVIEW</b><p>
At Sentry, we realize that getting valuable, hands-on experience is important for you and your career. We’re looking to recruit talented professionals for our Associate Software Product Analyst co-op program. In this program you’ll have the opportunity to work part-time while attending classes and full-time during summers. This distinguished and cutting-edge unit is a part of our Software Product Analyst team in Information Technology (IT). Our co-op program is designed to provide you the opportunity to gain valuable experience while working in our downtown Madison office on State Street.</p>
<b>
WHAT YOU'LL DO</b><p>
You’ll gain valuable real-life, hands-on experience while working on a series of projects that involve coordinating development, implementation, and maintenance between business units and developers. We’ll look to you to work with Sentry’s analysts and business users to write functional and technical specifications for use by developers and others involved in the projects. In addition, you’ll:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Obtain project scope through unit interviews, workshops, site visits, scenarios, documents, tasks, and workflows</li>
<li>Analyze recommendations for system modifications/enhancement to meet the needs of the end users</li>
<li>Develop test plans and participate in system implementation of new or revised systems</li>
</ul>
<b>
WHAT IT TAKES</b><p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Current full-time college student</li>
<li>Majoring in Business-related or Information Systems-related field</li>
<li>Ability to work between 12-19 hours per week during the school year in a minimum of 2-3 hour increments</li>
<li>Ability to work 40 hours per week in the summer</li>
<li>The desire to expand your communication, facilitation, documentation, and interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Sentry does not offer employment in this program to holders of F-1, J-1 and H-1 Visas for the purpose of obtaining practical experience</li>
</ul>

</p>
<ul>
<li>

</li>
