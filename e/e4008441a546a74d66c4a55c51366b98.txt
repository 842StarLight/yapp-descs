<h2>Manager of Business Development, Central FL</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Summary/Objective:</b> The Manager of Business Development for the Orlando market will work to improve our market position and achieve financial growth. They must build key customer relationships, identify business opportunities, negotiate and close business deals and maintain extensive knowledge of current Orlando market conditions. They must collaborate with the internal team, marketing staff, and project managers to increase sales opportunities and thereby maximize revenue for the organization. They will also help manage existing clients and ensure they stay satisfied and positive.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Functions:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>A true business developer that will garner new design projects to the office from the hospitality, mixed use, multi-family residential, commercial and education sectors.</li>
<li>Develop annual business development/marketing budget and plan.</li>
<li>Continuously networking to cultivate and maintain positive relationships with sources for lead generation.</li>
<li>Maintain active participation in relevant professional organizations to promote RGD representing the firm at key industry events, conferences, market specific meetings, etc.</li>
<li>Conduct market research to include industry trends, background on potential clients and monitor the competitive marketplace staying abreast of industry changes and trends.</li>
<li>Gather market/competitor intelligence that translates into effective positioning of RGD.</li>
<li>Identify and respond to RFQs, RFPs and other leads on targeted projects.</li>
<li>Monitor, organize and evaluate public sector RFP's on a regional basis using Onvida, Florida. Administrative weekly and other internet based resources.</li>
<li>Prepare and review design service proposals, review brochures and related communication pieces as needed.</li>
<li>Ability to user In Design for preparation of Statement of Qualifications or similar marketing materials.</li>
<li>Ability to prepare Business Development Reports for all of RGD Orlando Pursuits and Business Development Activities.</li>
<li>Devise strategies that will lead to successful marketing efforts and help to define criteria for “go/no-go” decisions.</li>
<li>Liaise with other firms/consultants regarding team partnering opportunities. Empower and work well with others while promoting a business development-oriented mindset amongst the design staff.</li>
<li>May be assigned other duties as needed.</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
<b>Supervisor Responsibilities:</b> Ability to lead Marketing Coordinators and administrative support staff as required to implement successful business development activities.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>The ideal candidate will have the following qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in Business, Building Construction, Engineering, Architecture or related field.</li>
<li>7-10 years of experience directing business development/marketing and communications programs in the engineering, architecture or construction industry or related to it with a successful track record.</li>
<li>Broad and in-depth business background with a reasonable working knowledge of the technical aspects of design/build construction.</li>
<li>An entrepreneurial spirit who is adept and possesses “worldly” knowledge and keen passion for architecture design.</li>
<li>An existing network in Orlando of potential clients in the following sectors: Mixed-Use, Multi-family, Hospitality, Commercial, Education and Healthcare.</li>
<li>Proven success in networking, business development, and cold-calling to develop new markets/clients through closing of a deal, while maintaining a high percentage of existing clients and their business.</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills and proficiency in presentation software programs.</li>
<li>Proficiency in creative writing.</li>
<li>Outstanding negotiation and interpersonal skills.</li>
<li>Excellent client management skills with strong business acumen and professional demeanor that enables this person to interact with the highest levels both inside and outside the Firm.</li>
<li>Clear approach to problem solving and good decision-making.</li>
<li>Comfortable managing the efforts of marketing support staff.</li>
<li>Highly motivated, resourceful, and winning attitude.</li>
<li>Willing to travel as needed.</li>
</ul>
<p>RGD Consulting Engineers participates in E-Verify and is an Equal Opportunity Employer.</p>
