<h2>Senior Sales Manager</h2>

<div>
<p><b>Sr. Sales Manager</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>DESCRIPTION/SUMMARY</b><br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The Sr. Sales Manager role requires a self-driven, ambitious, goal-orientated person with an entrepreneurial, can-do attitude. The successful candidate will have built a history of developing start-up level sales organizations into global powerhouses, growing revenue and market share. We’re looking for a people-first manager who lives on responsibility, accountability, and execution, while making a positive difference in the lives of our customers, consumers, and team members.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>PRIMARY DUTIES &amp; RESPONSIBILITIES</b>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The following is a list of primary job duties that the Sr. Sales Manager would be responsible for. This list is not exhaustive and may include other tasks as assigned by management:</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul><li> <b>Revenue Growth &amp; Grow Existing Customers: </b>Drive revenue and gross margin growth by (a) hiring, training, managing, and retaining top-performing sales team members; (b) developing and growing channel partners; and (c) co-creating effective marketing (advertising, social media, and public relations) programs with customers.</li></ul>
<ul><li> <b>Sales Strategy &amp; Game Plan:</b> Develop and implement DHI’s field sales strategy and plan. Set goals, inspect achievement plans, determine metrics, track and report sales activity, and help analyze</li></ul>
results and make recommendations.
<ul><li> <b>Sales Leader &amp; Team Development:</b> Hire, develop, and retain a high-performing sales field team. Ability to search for leads, negotiate &amp; close deals; simultaneously developing a team that will be able to do the same in the field (different regions of the world, i.e. France &amp; Chile).</li></ul>
<ul><li> <b>Direct Reports:</b> Report to VP of Marketing &amp; Sales and work co-jointly with Senior Customer Success Manager; liaise with key members of the company’s leadership team. Our inside and outside sales team and sales support staff report to this position.</li></ul>
<ul><li> <b>Sales Tools/Messaging/Approach:</b> Ensure that our sales team is equipped with well-designed sales tools (CRM, presentation materials, proposals, brochures, etc.) and uses consistent messaging and sales</li></ul>
approach.
<ul><li> <b>Integrate Marketing &amp; Sales:</b> Work closely with the Senior Marketing Managers to ensure that the sales and marketing departments are synchronized in messaging, target markets, leads and sales pipeline</li></ul>
development, etc.
<ul><li> <b>Key Account Sales Leadership:</b> Meet, develop relationships, and negotiate programs with key accounts, either as part of a team with other executives or solo.</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS</b><br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The ideal candidate would have the following combinations of skills, education, abilities and attributes:</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul><li> Bachelor’s degree in Business Administration, Sales and Marketing or related field.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Minimum 20+ years’ Sales experience; technology field a plus.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ability to travel often internationally to close deals and manage field team.</li></ul>
<ul><li> <b>Results-oriented:</b> Built and managed a national sales team using inside and territory-based sales staff. A track record of leading sales teams to consistently exceed sales targets. Ability to monitor and track results from marketing initiatives as well as sales performance, forecast and pipeline.</li></ul>
<ul><li> <b>Change Agent:</b> Demonstrated ability to initiate change and energize others through data driven decisions.</li></ul>
<ul><li> <b>Collaborative: </b>History of building supportive environments that encouraged idea sharing and winning together.</li></ul>
<ul><li> <b>Knowledge/Skills:</b> At least mid-level expertise with CRM and other technical tools, specifically Salesforce.</li></ul>
<ul><li> <b>Leadership: </b>Prior people management experience. Exposure to or a history of active executive level leadership and participation in strategic planning.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Superior verbal and written communication skills.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Superior time-management, organization, and attention-to-detail.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Good understanding of a test and learn approach</li></ul>
<ul><li> Willingness to operate out of your comfort zone in a new, hyper-growth industry</li></ul>
<ul><li> Great communicator using technology (PowerPoint, teleconferences, etc.)</li></ul>
<ul><li> Capability to discern important factors from noise in a busy role</li></ul>
<ul><li> Proficiency with and willingness to learn new sales technology and data analytics</li></ul>
<ul><li> Positive attitude in a hyper-growth, fast paced, continually evolving environment</li></ul>
<ul><li> The ability to take initiative and be creative while simultaneously taking direction</li></ul>

<p><b>PHYSICAL REQUIREMENTS</b><b>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>Working conditions are normal for an office environment. Work may require occasional overnight travel and weekend and/or evening work.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Description</b>: This position requires a moderate amount of stamina with the ability to perform the above essential job functions and within timetables. Some overtime work may be required.</p>
<p><b>Lifting:</b><b> </b>The employee must be able to lift up to 10 lbs. frequently and up to 20 lbs. occasionally for short durations.</p>
<p><b>Carrying:</b><b> </b>The employee must be able to carry up to 10 lbs. frequently and up to 20 lbs. occasionally for short distances.</p>
<p><b>Pushing/Pulling:</b><b> </b>The employee must be able to push/pull up to 10 lbs. frequently and up to 20 lbs. occasionally usually from desk level to floor or floor to desk level.</p>
<p><b>Other Physical Requirements:</b> Upper extremity usage, motor coordination, range of motion, vision, hearing and balance should be in a range to safely preclude the harm or injury to the employee in this position or the harm or injury to other employees.</p>
<p><b>Considerations:</b> The employee will in most cases work in a central air-conditioned building; however, may be exposed to dust, fumes or gases and to changes of weather when exposed to outdoors at job sites or other locations.</p>
