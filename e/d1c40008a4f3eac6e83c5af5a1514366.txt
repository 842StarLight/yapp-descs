<h2>District Sales Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Description
</b></h1>
<p></p>
<p>Masco Cabinetry has an immediate opening for a District Sales Manager! This is a remote position and is responsible for the management of dealer sales Account Managers in a defined region.
<br>
<br>
Masco Cabinetry manufactures five nationally recognized cabinetry brands, KraftMaid®, Merillat®, QualityCabinets®, Cardell Cabinetry®, and One Two One Cabinetry®. Offering an unmatched selection of stylish, high-quality products at a variety of price levels, Masco Cabinetry brands are favored by builders, dealers, distributors and home centers throughout North America. A leader in environmental stewardship, Masco Cabinetry products have been granted an Environmental Stewardship Program certificate by the Kitchen Cabinet Manufacturers Association.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>JOB SUMMARY:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>Manages assigned region’s revenue, market share, staffing, expenses &amp; profitability. Participates in the development and the implementation of strategic business plans for the Dealer channel. Develops and maintains a productive and talented sales organization to “win” in the Dealer channel.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>PRINCIPAL FUNCTIONAL RESPONSIBILITIES:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for P&amp;L management and makes adjustments as needed to ensure success on budget commitments.
</li>
<li>Responsible for cultivating and maintaining business relationships with key customers to support departmental goals and objectives.
</li>
<li>Research, analyze and monitor Dealer’s business, sales trends, sales history, advertising/ marketing and make recommendations for increased sales.
</li>
<li>Implement and execute sales policies and practices within area of responsibility.
</li>
<li>Analyze and monitor sales against defined metrics, goals and objectives.
</li>
<li>Establish and manage performance goals for sales team.
</li>
<li>Lead cross-functional teams to increase revenue and market share with existing Dealers, new Dealers distribution.
</li>
<li>Participate in forecasting process and oversee the expense budget to ensure compliance with organizational expenditure requirements.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL QUALIFICATIONS AND SKILLS:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in business, marketing, sales or related field; or 8 years of selling experience with a minimum of a High School diploma or GED.
</li>
<li>8 years of experience leading a sales team to include P&amp;L responsibility.
</li>
<li>Proficient computer skills required including Microsoft Office Suite.
</li>
<li>Demonstrated successful ability to lead people and get results through others.
</li>
<li>Demonstrated successful ability to build positive partnerships and work collaboratively with department, cross-functional business teams, and customers.
</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills with the ability to interact with external customers.
</li>
<li>Excellent problem solving, critical thinking and decision making skills.
</li>
<li>Demonstrated successful ability to prioritize and multi-task various and conflicting responsibilities.
</li>
<li>Proven negotiations skills.
</li>
<li>Excellent project management and budgeting skills with a deadline orientation and focus.
</li>
<li>Ability to travel up to 75%.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS AND SKILLS:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>BA/BS degree
</li>
<li>Experience in the building industry.
</li>
</ul>
