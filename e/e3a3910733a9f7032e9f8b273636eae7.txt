<h2>Scheduling Support Agent</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader">
<b>If </b><b>you are a </b><b>current </b><b>Vivint Solar employee, please apply to positions using the "Find Jobs" task in Workday.
</b>
</h1>
<p></p>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"></h1>
<p><b>We are in search of a Scheduling Specialist that will be a solid contributor and ensures accurate scheduling of appointments. They will provide great customer service to customers, Operational Managers and sales representatives.
</b></p>
<p></p>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Key Job Duties:
</b></h1>
<ul>
<li><b>Manage Scheduling Software for Vivint Solar
</b></li>
<li><b>Maintain dialogue with managers and technicians in the field
</b></li>
<li><b>Maintain phone line for any scheduling related issues
</b></li>
<li><b>Resolve issues for Vivint Solar and help to promote savings and efficiency for the company
</b></li>
<li><b>Audit technician schedules
</b></li>
<li><b>Contact customers regarding scheduled installation and service appointments
</b></li>
<li><b>Optimize technician productivity through active schedule monitoring and coordination with local technician leadership
</b></li>
<li><b>Be the first point of contact for same-day schedule changes
</b></li>
<li><b>Function as the juncture between corporate and field operations
</b></li>
<li><b>Coordinate technician movement with local operation managers
</b></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Necessary Skills and Experience:
</b></h1>
<ul>
<li><b>Communication skills
</b></li>
<li><b>Analytical skills
</b></li>
<li><b>Understanding of scheduling processes and how they work
</b></li>
<li><b>Ability to meet deadlines as well as set and hold others to deadlines
</b></li>
<li><b>Organizational skills
</b></li>
<li><b>Flexible approach to working hours
</b></li>
<li><b>Ability to communicate effectively both verbally and in writing
</b></li>
<li><b>Ability to handle multiple tasks simultaneously in a fast paced environment
</b></li>
<li><b>Ability to work effectively with other departments and within own department
</b></li>
<li><b>Intermediate to advanced MS Excel skills
</b></li>
<li><b>Scheduling Software knowledge a plus
</b></li>
<li><b>Proficient at Microsoft Office Word, Outlook and MapPoint
</b></li>
<li><b>Competent in Windows 7 or above
</b></li>
<li><b>Experience or education in analysis is preferred
</b></li>
<li><b>Self-motivated with a desire to create new projects and work with little supervision
</b></li>
</ul>
