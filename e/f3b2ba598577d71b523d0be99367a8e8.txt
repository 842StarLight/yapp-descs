<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Key responsibilities include, but are not limited, to:</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>
Create, manage and timely deliver data deliverables (SDRLs and CDRLs) to various customers in accordance with contractual schedules and provide seamless customer service and support throughout the process.</li>
<li>
Coordinate performance of engine contract requirements from contract award to engine sales, and schedule sales in SAP, in accordance with the contract terms and the MRP system.</li>
<li>
Lead the Sales &amp; Distribution (S&amp;D) efforts for the F135 and Tanker Programs and partner with other functions across the enterprise to meet our customer’s data deliverables (CDRL) and S&amp;D requirements.</li>
<li>
Coordinate software-related development changes in multiple locations, ensuring that the software solutions remain compliant with government finance reporting and distribution requirements and aligned with contractual requirements.</li>
<li>
Work closely with the various teams to define process opportunities, including those enabled by technology.</li>
<li>
Collaborate, interact and interface with cross-functional teams across the enterprise to achieve coordination, alignment and integration with the project demands, schedules and other requirements.</li>
<li>
Perform tactical tasks with a high degree of quality and with a focus on customer satisfaction.</li>
<li>
Manage and adhere to project plans and schedules, monitor and track project progress.</li>
<li>
Conduct meetings, present plans and progress reports relating to each project.</li>
<li>
Maintain comprehensive project documentation of each project step in accordance with project requirements.</li>
<li>
Identify, manage, and escalate project risks and effectively manage any required changes to the project scope and schedule.</li>
</ul>
