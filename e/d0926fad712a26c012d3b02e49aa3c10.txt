<p><b>

<p><b>

Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p></p>

<li>
Participates in the scoping of proposed improvements, and works with Content Managers, Product Managers, or other Project Managers to turn business requirements into actionable developer specifications.</li>

Strong SQL skills and familiarity</li>
