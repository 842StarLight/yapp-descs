<h2>Director of Engineering</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Gill - Maryland</b></p>
<p><b>Job Description</b></p>
<p><b>Job Title: </b>Director of Engineering</p>
<p><b>Department: </b>Engineering</p>
<p><b>Reports To: </b>President</p>
<p><b>FLSA Status: </b>Exempt</p>
<p><b>Summary </b></p>
<p>Plans and directs all aspects of engineering activities and projects within the organization. Ensures all engineering projects, initiatives, and processes are in conformance with organization's established policies and objectives. Utilizes best practice engineering methods and provides expert technical guidance for engineering initiatives. Incorporates components, materials, and tools that result in cost-effective and quality output. Manages overall engineering budget and costs. Develops major goals to support broad functional objectives. Approves policies developed within various sub-functions and departments. Comprehensive knowledge of the overall departmental function. Typically requires 8+ years of managerial experience.</p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities </b>include the following. Other duties may be assigned.</p>
<ul>
<li><b>Leadership/Management</b></li>
<li>Must be an agent of change, driving continuous improvement in all daily activities</li>
<li>Identify top and bottom performers and execute growth strategies or improvement plans as required.</li>
<li>Using all available data establish the skill type, skill level and number of technical resources required to support the business.</li>
<li>Maintain a level of continuous improvement to increase overall efficiency of personnel.</li>
<li>Develop, Execute and Communicate business plans effectively throughout the organization to ensure proper buy in and approvals are met.</li>
<li>Provide overall direction, guidance and support to department engineering resources and support staff</li>
<li>Responsible for development and implementation of long range skills development plan and onboarding of new associates.</li>
<li>Develop and execute on key performance indicators. Develop recovery plans and actions to achieve key business metrics.</li>
<li>Provide routine feedback to direct reports utilizing performance management techniques to improve the overall performance of the group.</li>
<li>Coach, mentor and train department employees in support of Gill-Maryland goals.</li>
<li><b>New Products Management</b></li>
<li>Direct and manage all aspects of new program introduction from RFQ through First Article shipment.</li>
<li>Interface with Sales team and Customer Service during quoting phases to provide accurate and timely quotations.</li>
<li>Be the face of the company during New Product Introductions and drive design for manufacturability with the customer.</li>
<li>Develop and track NPI metrics and establish continuous improvement goals.</li>
<li>Interface with R&amp;D team and customer on prototyping process where applicable.</li>
<li>Drive standardized NPI process throughout the organization focused on engagement and communication.</li>
<li><b>Project Management</b></li>
<li>Maintain Gill - Maryland project portfolio and cost reduction efforts and work with other directors/managers to determine and drive priorities.</li>
<li>Direct and manage the capital improvements strategy. Interface with all departments to ensure a robust 3-5 year capital strategy exists for the organization.</li>
<li>Create and implement a standard project reporting structure to allow for best practice sharing between departments.</li>
<li>Identify opportunities for improvement and waste elimination</li>
<li>Manage the Gill-Maryland technical software suite. Ensure the most up to date software packages are being used to drive engineering efficiency in the organization.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Gill - Maryland</b></p>
<p><b>Goals and Objectives</b></p>
<p><b>Engineering department Goals and Objectives</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Zero recordable injuries or near misses</li>
<li>100% Training on ALL Federal, State and locally required Safety topics</li>
<li>Cost reduction of 2.5% of revenue</li>
<li>Scrap of less than 3%</li>
<li>Maintain customer return below .75% of sales</li>
<li>Change request turnaround time of less than 2 weeks</li>
<li>95% on time review and acceptance of RFQ’s and PO’s</li>
<li>Complete projects on time and to budget to scope sheet established the inception of the project</li>
<li>Achieve expected deliverables set forth in scope sheet established the inception of the project</li>
</ul>

<p><b>Education and/or Experience </b></p>
<p>Bachelor's degree (B. S.) from four-year College or university with Mechanical or Chemical Engineering Degree or equivalent; and ten years related experience and/or training; Masters degree in engineering or business is a plus. Must have 8+ years leading/managing engineering resources in a manufacturing environment.</p>
<p><b>Communication Skills </b></p>
<p>Ability to read, analyze, and interpret general business periodicals, professional journals, technical procedures, or governmental regulations. Ability to write reports, business correspondence, and procedure manuals. Ability to effectively present information and respond to questions from groups of managers, clients, customers, and the general public. Ability to work with all levels of the organization and effectively layout and communicate strategic plans. Ability to read, analyze, and interpret CAD data. Heavy interaction with customers is required.</p>
<p><b>Mathematical Skills </b></p>
<p>Ability to work with mathematical concepts such as probability and statistical inference, and fundamentals of plane and solid geometry and trigonometry. Ability to apply concepts such as fractions, percentages, ratios, and proportions to practical situations.</p>
<p><b>Reasoning Ability </b></p>
<p>Ability to solve problems and deal with a variety of variables in situations where only limited standardization exists. Ability to interpret a variety of instructions furnished in written, oral, diagram, or schedule form.</p>
<p><b>Computer Skills </b></p>
<p>To perform this job successfully, an individual should have knowledge of 2D or 3D design software such as AutoCad or Solidworks/Catia. High proficiency in PLM, ERP and CAD products is a must.</p>
