Control Expenses</b></p>
<ul>

Protect Company assets within guidelines of LIDS Retail policies.</li>

Prepare store schedules and provide for proper store coverage at all times, within the guidelines for wage control set by the company.</li>

<p><br>
<b>
Supervise Associates</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Manage store associates through thorough use of LIDS Training Programs, goal setting (for sales and tasks), and regular follow up.</li>
<li>
Recruit, develop, and train store personnel to achieve Operations Objectives as well as adhere to Operational policies and guidelines.</li>
<li>
Administer the progressive steps of discipline to include verbal and written warnings. In addition, the Store Manager carries out employment terminations following approval from the District Sales Manager and Human Resources.</li>
<li>
Encourage direct compliance of all store associates established company policies, procedures and guidelines including, but not limited to, safekeeping of company inventory, funds and property.</li>
<li>
Performs work of subordinates, as needed.</li>
<li>
Communicate with employees at all levels of the company.</li>
<li>
Other duties as assigned.</li>
