<h2>Data Analytics Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div>
<p>IQVIA™ is the leading human data science company focused on helping healthcare clients find unparalleled insights and better solutions for patients. Formed through the merger of IMS Health and Quintiles, IQVIA offers a broad range of solutions that harness the power of healthcare data, domain expertise, transformative technology, and advanced analytics to drive healthcare forward.</p>
<p><b>
Overview of Team:</b></p>
<p>
The US Data Integrity team comprises over 50 employees across multiple geographies. Our team enhances the value of IQVIA’s syndicated offerings by serving as a trusted partner to our clients. We provide expertise, timely insights &amp; solutions, enabling clients to appropriately use our data to formulate better business decisions.</p>
<p><b>
Overview of Role:</b></p>
<p>
Based in our IQVIA office in Parsippany, NJ, the successful candidate will be responsible for analyzing &amp; responding to queries raised by clients who purchase one or more of IQVIA’s syndicated prescription and/or sales data offerings (e.g. NPA, Xponent, DDD, NSP, WSP etc.). The candidate will also be responsible for completing &amp; presenting complex data analysis/validation to internal key decision makers &amp; external clients, leveraging their experience to provide custom data solutions.</p>
<p><b>
Principle Accountabilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Analyzes &amp; responds to complex queries raised by clients using in-depth understanding of the client and IQVIA syndicated offerings (data), ensuring a comprehensive and timely response via remote client access</li>
<li>
Provides additional insight and interpretation of the data to enable appropriate resolution of business questions and/or issues, identifies customized solutions</li>
<li>
Coordinates and manages online meetings and discussions (both internally &amp; externally) related to project work/data analytics</li>
<li>
Independently completes quantitative analysis and research to gain more in-depth understanding of trends</li>
<li>
Runs standard queries for data extraction</li>
<li>
Utilizes various systems/tools to work through issue identification and follow the standard escalation process through resolution</li>
<li>
Provides formal and written communications both internally and externally regarding issue status and resolution</li>
<li>
Verifies data for accuracy and completeness</li>
<li>
Performs audits of own work or that of others to ensure conformance with established procedures or to resolve routine issues</li>
<li>
May manipulate and transform data to optimize analyses</li>
<li>
May construct additional reports or tools to assist in analysis</li>
<li>
Identifies ways to improve and streamline business operations, driving process improvements aligned with desired customer outcomes</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Minimum Education, Experience, &amp; Specialized Knowledge Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Mathematics, Statistics, Economics, Life Sciences, or related field(s)</li>
<li>
Experience using Microsoft software tools (Excel, PowerPoint, Word, and Access)</li>
<li>
Knowledge of the industry and an understanding of the marketplace</li>
<li>
Knowledge and understanding of the fundamental processes of the business</li>
<li>
Minimum 3-5 years experience with data analysis</li>
<li>
Basic knowledge of SQL a plus, but not required</li>
</ul>
