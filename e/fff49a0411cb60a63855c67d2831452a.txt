<h2>Social Media Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>If you live and breathe social media and want to help small to medium size businesses grow through social campaigns, submit your resume and cover letter for this exciting marketing agency opportunity. Working closely with the digital marketing department, you will be responsible for curating content, developing an audience, and helping our clients achieve their social goals.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Keep current with social trends and best practices.</li>
<li>Create and implement social media strategies to align with business objectives.</li>
<li>Create original, relevant content for pages to reach clients’ target audiences.</li>
<li>Manage paid social and advertising and budgets.</li>
<li>Produce monthly client content calendars.</li>
<li>Participate in client onboarding meetings.</li>
<li>Maintain positive relationships with clients and regular communication.</li>
<li>Educate and coach clients on social media best practices.</li>
<li>Identify key metrics and generate reports to present results to clients.</li>
<li>Respond to reviews and comments in a timely and professional manner.</li>
<li>Engage with customers to resolve customer service issues.</li>
<li>Collaborate with cross functional teams</li>
<li>Monitor social activity both during and outside of office hours.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in marketing or related field with emphasis on social media and other digital channels.</li>
<li>Expertise in Facebook including algorithm and scheduling tool</li>
<li>Excellent written and oral communication skills.</li>
<li>Proven experience in social media platforms.</li>
<li>Excellent computer software skills including the entire Microsoft suite and multiple SaaS as necessary.</li>
<li>Excellent organizational skills and attention to detail necessary to function well in a busy, high-volume department.</li>
<li>Willing to learn new industry in great detail and depth.</li>
<li>Graphic design, photo and video editing skills.</li>
<li>Crisis management experience a plus.</li>
<li>Public speaking experience is highly desirable.</li>
<li>Agency experience is advantageous.</li>
<li>Grace under pressure.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>About Warm Thoughts Communications: </b></p>
<p>Warm Thoughts Communications is a rapidly growing, national, full-service marketing agency serving the residential energy industry. We are conveniently located in Northern New Jersey and just a few miles outside of Manhattan. We are growing at a faster rate than at any time in our 28-year history and we are looking for "A" players who are excited about being an important part of our growth and overall success. We create marketing solutions across a broad range of channels including digital, social, print and direct response, advising our clients on business development, revenue growth and profitability. Our vision is to solve our clients’ most important sales, marketing and business challenges.</p>
