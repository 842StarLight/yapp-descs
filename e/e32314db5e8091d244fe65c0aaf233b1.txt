<b>
Job Summary</b>
</div>
<div>
<p>At Atonix Digital, we're always looking for passionate, talented people who are leaders, innovators and eager to build best-in-class solutions. People work here because we can make a difference. We are driven by client success and passionate about data. Are you up for the challenge?</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Atonix Digital helps companies simplify asset performance management by putting data to work — to detect emerging risks, enhance efficiency, improve accuracy of planning, and provide an easily justifiable return on investment. The ASSET360 platform and solutions are purpose-built to solve client issues by bringing together disparate data sources, applying real-time analytics, and enabling dynamic and adaptive planning capabilities. Visit www.atonix.com to learn more.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<div>The ASSET360 software platform consists of many reusable data analytics and visualization components that</div>
<div>solve different problems for different customers. At Atonix, Implementation Engineers help perform and guide configuration of ASSET360 to match platform capabilities with customer opportunities. An Implementation Engineer is being sought to configure the ASSET360 software platform for customer use. This often includes connecting data sources, designing asset registries, codifying business processes, and configuring mathematical models. The Implementation Engineer position will introduce the professional to many business sectors, challenge both technical and interpersonal skills, and will serve as the basis for career growth in an exciting and dynamic business.</div>
</div>
</div>
<div>
<div>
<b>
Key Responsibilities</b>
</div>
<div><ul>
<li>Configure the ASSET360 platform post-sale to ensure customers see full value. Organize asset information to leverage platform capabilities, build analytic visuals and define engineering calculations and machine learning models.</li>
<li>Work with product and industry managers to ensure project awareness and inform strategy</li>
<li>Resolve front or back-end problems and instruct users remotely or in-person using basic troubleshooting and technical skills</li>
<li>Respond to situations where standard procedures have failed in isolating or fixing problems with software installation and configuration</li>
<li>Maintain technical manuals, quality assurance documentation, and knowledge base
</li>
<li>Perform limited application systems administration/analysis work</li>
<li>Assists in the evaluation, recommendation, testing, and implementation of new software features</li>
</ul></div>
</div>
