<h2>Cyber Identity & Access Management Senior Consultant - Okta</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>Are you interested in working in a dynamic environment that offers opportunities for professional growth and new responsibilities? If so, Deloitte &amp; Touche LLP could be the place for you. This is an unparalleled time of change with new information security challenges arising each day. Our team of Cyber risk professionals bring industry experience, confidence, and technical knowledge to help our clients tackle those unique challenges.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>Identity and Access Management (IAM) solutions</b> leverage Deloitte’s IAM Methods 2.0 methodology to help clients control which employees, customers, partners and suppliers access sensitive corporate resources, and streamline the process of managing the explosion in “digital identities.”</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>Work you’ll do</b></div>
<div>As Identity and Access Management (IAM) solutions team Senior Consultant, you will:</div>
<ul><li>Demonstrate advanced understanding of business processes, internal control risk management, IT controls and related standards</li></ul>
<ul><li>Identify and evaluate complex business and technology risks, internal controls which mitigate risks and related opportunities for internal control improvement</li></ul>
<ul><li>Understand complex business and information technology management processes</li></ul>
<ul><li>Execute advanced services and supervise staff in delivering basic services</li></ul>
<ul><li>Responsible to install, integrate and deploy Okta products in client environments.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Communicate to clients and partners aspects of both the product and the implementation at the technical and functional level appropriate for the situation.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Post-sales requirements gathering, analysis and documentation.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manage project scope, schedule, status and documentation.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Build and nurture positive working relationships with the clients with the intention to exceed client expectations.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Identify opportunities to improve engagement profitability.</li></ul>
<br>

<div>Required:</div>
<ul>
<li><ul><li>3+ years plus experience in developing, implementing or architecting information systems.</li></ul></li>
<li><ul><li>3+ years plus with technical architecture experience integrating identity management, access management and access governance software into clients' infrastructure and applications.</li></ul></li>
<li><ul><li>2+ years plus experience with installation, integration and deployment of Okta in client environment.</li></ul></li>
<li><div>
Identity Management familiarity in one or more of the following areas:</div></li>
</ul>
<div>
<div>o Single Sign On</div>
<div>o Identity Federation</div>
<div>o Enterprise Directory Architecture and Design including directory schema, namespace and replication topology experience</div>
<div>o Resource Provisioning</div>
</div>
<ul>

<li><div>
2+ years’ experience with the following programming languages: Java, JavaScript, JSP/Servlets, SQL.
</div></li>
<li><div>2+ years managing projects through the full system development lifecycle</div></li>

Must be willing to travel up to 80% within North America</div></li>
