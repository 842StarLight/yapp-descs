<b>Job Summary</b><br>
<p>Act as the operations business owner to ensure successful revenue cycle transition of new facilities into Parallon Shared Services. Transition projects include, but are not limited to; HCA acquisitions, new external clients, HCA or client divestitures (due to sale or disengagement), health information systems implementation/upgrade and also new facilities. Act as the leader for the Parallon Revenue Cycle Transitions team and the primary contact for Shared Service Center Leadership, and critical stakeholder departments such as Information Technology, Project Management, executive leadership and HCA and client counterparts. Drives continuous process improvement to methodology and project plans based on experiences with each transition project completed. Seeks additional ways to leverage staff and knowledge base in support of other departments and business units managing transitions (e.g. facilitates cross training and resource utilization as opportunities arise).</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>Moderate travel will be required both to project sites and to the Parallon corporate office.</p>
<p><i><br>
</i></p>
<p>Duties (included but not limited to):</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li><p>Establishing the department’s procedures and authoring toolkits.</p></li>
<li><p>
Management and mentoring of Revenue Cycle Transition staff to assure quality of work and integration of team members’ work.</p></li>
<li><p>
Managing detailed projects to ensure a smooth transition for the client which includes a detailed assessment of current practices and business needs of the client ensuring closure of associated gaps</p></li>
<li><p>
Actively collaborating with representatives from IT, Project Services, Parallon Subject Matter Experts, Shared Service Center Leadership, Client Leadership and Staff to ensure a smooth transition to operations.</p></li>
<li><p>
Act as an escalation point for project decision making, issues and risks.</p></li>
<li><p>
Creating and maintaining the on-boarding process for new Parallon revenue cycle transition team members</p></li>
<li><p>
Monitoring timeliness and quality of work performed by revenue cycle transition team</p></li>
<li><p>
Collaborating with VP of Client Relations to ensure all revenue cycle transition teams are properly resourced for the success of each project
</p></li>
<li><p>Overseeing the user access process to ensure all users of Parallon systems receive the correct system access and the necessary training. Managing the pre-go live activities for HCA acquisition Phase 2 and external client system conversions.</p></li>
</ul>
