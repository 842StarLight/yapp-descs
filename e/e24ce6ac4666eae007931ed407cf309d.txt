<h2>National Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Makita is a worldwide leader in the professional power tool industry. Over the past 100 years we've built a reputation for using the finest raw materials, the most advanced manufacturing equipment, and the most rigorous quality testing in the industry. Headquartered in La Mirada, CA, Makita U.S.A, with offices in Chicago, Illinois and Buford, Georgia, has been bringing Makita's best-in-class engineering advantage to professional power tool users in America since 1970.</b></p>
<p><b>
Summary:</b> <i>Responsible for maintaining and expanding established national account/accounts through direct sales. While overseeing the development of sales revenue and account base and for promoting Makita’s “Best-In-Class Engineering &amp; Innovation” brand position and the extensive Makita product line.</i></p>
