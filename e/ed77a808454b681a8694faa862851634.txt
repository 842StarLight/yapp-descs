<h2>Property Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>We are seeking an individual with exceptional management skills to fill a full-time Property Manager role at two of our apartments communities which include a 32 unit Senior Community in Azle, TX and a 32 unit family complex in Springtown, TX.</p>
<p>The starting salary for this position is $25K to $30K per year, depending on experience, and will include 32 hours each week with Fridays off. We have a very competitive compensation package that includes excellent PTO benefits, and employer paid health insurance.</p>
<p>In this role, the ideal candidate will be customer service and team oriented, self-directed and motivated, and will possess excellent communication and computer skills. You will be responsible for the financial performance and physical condition at assigned properties. Some after-hour work for emergency calls is required.</p>
<p>Integra-Peak Management has built a solid reputation on over 30 years of providing the best in affordable apartment living. Our residents depend on us to be observant, interested in their lives, and quick to respond to their needs. We enjoy working with them and with each other. Every day is different – demanding, rewarding, and fun. Integra-Peak is a growing company that fosters a professional attitude and a strong team spirit. We are committed to training our employees well, honoring their achievements, and promoting from within.</p>
<p>In this role, the ideal candidate will be customer service and team oriented, self-directed and motivated, and will possess excellent communication, marketing, and computer skills. Some after-hour emergencies may be required.</p>
<p>Successful Managers come from various backgrounds including: apartment management, sales positions, office administration, and real estate school graduates.</p>
<p><b>JOB RESPONSIBILITIES: </b>As a Property Manager, you will be a valuable member of the management team. A manager of an Integra-Peak property has responsibilities that fall into four (4) primary categories:</p>
<ul>
<li>Collecting rent</li>
<li>Attracting residents to property and helping make sure they remain</li>
<li>Overseeing the property’s physical condition</li>
<li>Properly completing paperwork and other required compliance tasks</li>
</ul>
<p>In addition to the skills listed above, Managers are responsible for budget and key control as well as enforcing occupancy policies and procedures, preparing occupancy and compliance reports, and continuing apartment management education/training. You will deal directly and consistently with the residents at our properties to analyze and resolve issues. Resolving issues must be done on time and in an efficient manner – both timely and accurately with a sense of urgency and with resident &amp; owner/management focus.</p>
<p><b>JOB REQUIREMENTS: </b>The successful Property Manager must have apartment and / or residential management experience. This individual must be professional with excellent communication skills. Proper conduct is essential. The ideal candidate possesses:</p>
<ul>
<li>3+ years of experience in Property Management</li>
<li>High level of competency and efficiency in all aspects of management operations</li>
<li>Capable of meeting financial objectives</li>
<li>May require overtime, holiday, and weekend work</li>
<li>Must be on-call</li>
<li>Strong sales approach with advanced negotiation abilities</li>
<li>Communicate directly with owner/management company</li>
<li>Great time management skills</li>
<li>Background checks are an employment requirement</li>
<li>Oversight of employees</li>
<li>Proficient in computer programs, including Microsoft Office &amp; property management software</li>
</ul>
