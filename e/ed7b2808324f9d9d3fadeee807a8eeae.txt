<h2>Hospitality Operations Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>JOB DESCRIPTION</p>
<p><b>
Hospitality Operations Manager</b></p>
<p>
TIER 3+ MANAGEMENT</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Summary of Position:</b></p>
<p>
The Operations Manager is responsible for the overall operations of a restaurant, brewery, and entertainment complex. Increases revenue streams for the organization. Creates a team environment that provides exceptional guest service while ensuring the management team and all staff members perform at a high level and meet performance goals. Must Enjoy the Ride!</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Duties &amp; Responsibilities: </b>(not meant to be an exhaustive list and may change at any time, as business needs demand)</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Leadership &amp; Team Development:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Build the Crooked Hammock management team;</li>
<li>
Uphold core values and code of ethics in our Team development plan; Reinforce “How We Roll” throughout the hiring, on-boarding, training, coaching and evaluating for growth process;</li>
<li>
Be an effective coach, elevating the team for growth and ensuring team members have the right tools, knowledge and equipment required to do their jobs safely and effectively;</li>
<li>
Develop management team to ensure that all Company policies, procedures and standards are being followed;</li>
<li>
Provide ongoing feedback, establish performance expectations and conduct performance reviews in a timely manner;</li>
<li>
Maintain strong presence in local community;</li>
<li>
Maintains professional and technical knowledge by tracking emerging trends in the industry; attending educational workshops; reviewing professional publications; establishing personal networks; participating in professional societies;</li>
<li>
Live the brand and be a brand ambassador.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Operations:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Oversight of operations by enforcing policies and standard operating procedures; implementing production, productivity, quality, and guest service standards; determining and implementing system improvements;</li>
<li>
Establishes and communicates sales and labor goals;</li>
<li>
Ensure management team is scheduling staff as required by anticipated business volume, while ensuring that all positions are staffed, when and as needed, and labor cost objectives are met;</li>
<li>
Oversee and manage all areas of the unit(s) and make final decisions on matters of importance to guest service;</li>
<li>
Maintain safe, secure, and healthy facility environment by establishing, communicating, and enforcing sanitation standards and procedures; complying with health and legal regulations; maintaining security systems;</li>
<li>
Ensure positive guest service in all areas. Respond to complaints, taking any and all appropriate actions to turn dissatisfied guests into return guests.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Financial:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Meets financial objectives by collaborating with ownership to create forecasts &amp; budgets, analyze and react to performance, analyzing variances, initiating corrective actions, establishing and monitoring financial controls, developing and implementing strategies to increase revenue;</li>
<li>
Oversee purchasing and labor management by working with management team, reviewing and evaluating reports, analyzing variances, and taking corrective actions to meet goals;</li>
<li>
Ensure that all financial (invoices, reporting) and personnel/payroll related administrative duties are completed accurately, on time and in accordance with company policies and procedures;</li>
<li>
Financial management through oversight of COGS, staffing, and spending to optimize profits.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Marketing:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Sales Growth through marketing of the business at the unit level and engagement at the brand level;</li>
<li>
Oversight of Unit Level Marketing Plan;</li>
<li>
Collaborate with ownership to develop and implement marketing, advertising, public and community relations programs; evaluating program results; identifying and tracking changing demands;</li>
<li>
Works with community organizations to identify partnerships that will benefit the business and build good will.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
College degree is preferred. Bachelor of Science degree in hotel/restaurant management is desirable;</li>
<li>
10+ years experience leading $10+ million hospitality complex with multiple revenue streams;</li>
<li>
Excellent leadership skills with proven ability to lead and motivate a team and maintain a positive workplace culture;</li>
<li>
A strong understanding of process controls. Experience with project management and budgeting;</li>
<li>
Strong experience increasing revenue and managing costs;</li>
<li>
Eligible to work in the United States;</li>
<li>
Ability to satisfactorily pass background and credit check;</li>
<li>
ServSafe certified;</li>
<li>
Ability to balance personal &amp; professional life;</li>
<li>
Must work nights and weekends;</li>
<li>
Professional appearance, punctual, reliable;</li>
<li>
Hospitality soul;</li>
<li>
Advanced written, verbal, and non-verbal communication skills;</li>
<li>
Well organized;</li>
<li>
Proficiency in Microsoft Office skills, Google G Suite products, internet, POS;</li>
<li>
Frequently walk and occasionally bend over, twist, kneel, climb, stoop and crouch. This position requires the ability to lift up to 55 pounds and maneuver 170 pounds.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Notice</b></p>
<p>
Employment with LaVida Hospitality or any of its’ companies (Crooked Hammock Brewery, Fork &amp; Flask, Taco Reho, Big Chill Surf Cantina, Big Chill Beach Club) is “at will,” meaning that employment may be terminated at any time, with or without notice, and with or without cause, by either the employee or the Company.</p>
