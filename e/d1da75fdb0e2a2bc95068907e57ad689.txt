<p><b>Job duties and responsibilities include but are not limited to the following: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Becoming knowledgeable about all aspects of the company’s retail and distribution operations and applications</li>
<li>Implementing online Point-of-Sale inventory management</li>
<li>Responsible for understanding and configuring business requirements in the new POS system</li>
<li>Configuring, testing, implementing and supporting features of POS system</li>
<li>Developing interfaces with other systems</li>
<li>Lead in testing, converting and implementation of a new distribution software system</li>
<li>Providing technical understanding, support and troubleshooting of systems</li>
<li>Providing support for escalated calls from retail stores</li>
<li>Troubleshooting issues and determining resolutions</li>
<li>Working closely with our 3rd party POS/Distribution software company</li>
<li>Assisting with team members and providing proactive assistance wherever needed</li>
<li>Assisting with training and communications</li>
<li>Recognizing opportunities to improve the company’s operations through software applications</li>
<li>Maintaining applications’ features and upgrades</li>
</ul>
