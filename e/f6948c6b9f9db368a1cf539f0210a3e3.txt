<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Description</b></h2>
<div>
<p>
As a member of our Front Desk team you must be friendly, outgoing, and ready to provide a true Badlands feel for our customers. Great candidates will be excited to step into a fast-paced, dynamic position and take ownership of the Badlands experience right where it starts, as you will play a major role in ensuring an organized, smooth, enjoyable entry for our guests. Your individual personality should shine through in order to thrill our guests and motivate our staff.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Greet guests with a friendly, helpful demeanor and be able to provide information as needed</li>
<li>
Remain familiar with all aspects of Badlands and stay up-to-date with any operational changes</li>
<li>
Maintain a warm, customer-first focus during all interactions with guests</li>
<li>
Provide waiver and general membership information</li>
<li>
Book ticketing/party/event reservations as needed through facility software</li>
<li>
Provide prompt and courteous service to all guests, encouraging repeat business</li>
<li>
Determine guest needs and expectations in order to resolve conflicts and complaints as they arise</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications</b></h2>
<p></p>
<div><ul>
<li>
Passion and excitement for working with children and families</li>
<li>
Cashier experience preferred, including cash handling and point-of-sale software</li>
<li>
Strong communication, customer service, and teamwork skills</li>
<li>
Creative individual who is adaptable and able to quickly learn new skills</li>
<li>
Comfort working in a fast paced, high volume, and energetic environment</li>
<li>
Available to work a flexible schedule, including weekends, evenings and some holidays</li>
<li>
Physical requirements include bending, stooping, crawling, crouching, carrying/lifting (up to 30 pounds), pushing/pulling, reaching, repetitive motions, sitting, standing, and walking.</li>
</ul></div>
