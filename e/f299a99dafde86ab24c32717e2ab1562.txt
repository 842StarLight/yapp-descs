<h2>Client Solution Specialist Sr-PNCI Advanced Services(F)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Position Overview</b><br>
<br>
At PNC, our people are our greatest differentiator and competitive advantage in the markets we serve. We are all united in delivering the best experience for our customers. As a Client Solution Specialist - PNCI Advanced Services (F) within PNC Investments organization, you will be based in Pittsburgh, PA, Charlotte, NC, or Cleveland, OH. FINRA Series 7,63 licenses required.<br>
<br>
<b>Job Profile</b><br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Provides sales, service and execution support for new and ongoing client relationships. May serve as a customer solutions resource.</li>
<li>
Gathers required documentation. Prepares, reviews and verifies documents and relevant information for accuracy. Performs common processing and ensures compliance with standard regulations and processes. May administer complex implementation plans and related client interactions.</li>
<li>
Acts as a point of contact for clients, client teams or service partners and may act as an escalation point for complex client implementation and servicing issues.</li>
<li>
Initiates, updates and verifies client, account, or transaction details in relevant systems/applications. Provides reports as needed.</li>
<li>
Serves as a peer resource and may perform general administrative support.</li>
</ul>
