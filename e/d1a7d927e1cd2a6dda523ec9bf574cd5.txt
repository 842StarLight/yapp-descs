<h2>Program Manager, Advertising - Hiring & Development</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<ul>
<li>4+ years in program management, product management or recruiting program development (brand management experience considered)</li>
<li>Demonstrated strategic thinker who can see around corners and develop structured plans, with a passion for process improvement as it relates to hiring and development</li>
<li>Highly organized, able to prioritize and work under pressure. Ability to manage ambiguous situation and projects and deliver clear results that have impact</li>

<br>
<div>Amazon Advertising is dedicated to driving measurable outcomes for brand advertisers, agencies, and entrepreneurs. Our ad solutions—including sponsored, display, video, and custom ads—leverage Amazon’s innovations and insights to find, attract, and engage intended audiences throughout their daily journeys. With a range of flexible pricing and buying models, including self-service, managed service, and programmatic ad buying, these solutions help businesses build brand awareness, increase product sales, and more. If you’re eager to help to accelerate Amazon Advertising’s International growth by supporting the hiring and developing of our International Sales teams, you’ve come to the right place.<br>
Our environment is very fast-paced, and requires someone who is highly enthusiastic, entrepreneurial, flexible, detail-oriented, analytical, and comfortable working with different teams across multiple locales. As an individual contributor, you’ll be supported by our International Expansion Sales, Recruiting, and Training teams, and will focus on dramatically accelerating hiring and development programs for our newest Advertising locales. You will act as the Program Manager within Sales, partnering with other internal teams and Advertising Country Managers to support the development and implementation of hiring practices and talent development that will have direct impact on Advertising’s International growth.<br>
<br>
Specific responsibilities<br>
<ul>
<li>Partner with Advertising Recruiting to proactively plan and execute all phases of successful hiring and onboarding talent in our International locales</li>
<li>Develop process improvements to positively impact results in areas such as speed to hire, candidate quality, and sourcing volume</li>
<li>Partner with the Advertising Sales Enablement, Sales Training, and Recruiting teams to identify and define best-in-class talent development programs for International locales</li>
<li>Identify and develop new frameworks to improve Sales talent development, onboarding, and playbooks for International locales</li>
<li>Provide visibility and reporting on progress against hiring targets and hold local teams and owners accountable to deliver results</li>
<li>Support hiring of International Sales teams (Account Executives, Account Managers, Creative Campaign Managers, Programmatic, and Product teams) via phone-screening and interviews</li>
</ul>
