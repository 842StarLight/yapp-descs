<h2>Litigation Claims Specialist- Latent Claims</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>OVERVIEW</b><p>
Sentry’s dedication to customers is embodied by our best associates. At Sentry, our associates take care of our customers with a passion for service and delivering business results. As one of the largest mutual insurance companies in the US, we’re completely focused on offering exceptional claims service and protection for our policyholders. To help uphold our premier standards we are currently looking for an Environmental Claims professional where you’ll be responsible for managing the litigation for some our largest customers. In this role, you’ll provide prompt claim investigation, evaluation and settlement or denial of claims.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<b>
WHAT YOU'LL DO</b><div>
In this high profile role you’ll:<br>
<ul>
<li>Manage insurance coverage litigation, latent bodily injury, environmental contamination and construction defect claims</li>
<li>Interact with executive management, the company's internal legal department and as well as the defense and plaintiffs’ bar across a national jurisdiction<br>
</li>
</ul>
</div>
<p></p>
<b>WHAT IT TAKES</b><ul>
<li>Minimum of five years of commercial liability claims and claims litigation experience or similar background</li>
<li>Environmental contamination, latent bodily injury and/or multi-state (mass tort) litigation claims experience and knowledge of Comprehensive General Liability coverage forms a plus</li>
<li>Bachelors Degree or equivalent work experience; a law degree is helpful but not necessary</li>
<li>You must possess strong analytical abilities to review, exercise judgment, and evaluate claims to make sound decisions</li>
<li>Highly effective oral and written communication skills and human relations skills are critical</li>
<li>Knowledge of Microsoft Excel program is a plus<br>
</li>
</ul>
