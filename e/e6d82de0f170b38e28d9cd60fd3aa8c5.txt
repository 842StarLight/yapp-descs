<h2>Manager, Developer Network</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">---------
<br><br>
<b>Overview:</b><br>
---------
<br><br>
Guru is looking for a highly motivated, results-oriented self-starter to expand Guru's ecosystem by supporting our Developer Network. We will target existing and prospective customers, Consulting/SIs/BPOs and ISVs that wish to develop against Guru's APIs, as well as continuously track the various ways these valued developers are building against our APIs. The Manager of the Developer Network will be responsible for identifying, educating and engaging developers, as well as Guru Line of Business owners to cultivate strategic mind-share and collaborate on initiatives to serve and drive adoption within this emerging community.
<br><br>
Residing on the Business Development team, this role is not only about how well you nurture and expand our network; it's about how you lend your positivity and presence, combined with your skill-set to an energized environment and highly interactive team. Strong sense of humor required, sarcasm detection skills a plus.
<br><br>
We are creating the developer network and serving this community as a part of our evolving ecosystem and partnership strategy, which requires us to expand and grow our team. We need a driven individual who can effectively execute and establish strong relationships.
<br><br>
---------------------
<br><br>
<b>Key Responsibilities:</b><br>
---------------------
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>You will drive the structure and ongoing expansion + scalability of Guru's Developer Network</li>
<li>You will own all GTM activities and work with all key stakeholders to drive awareness and educate the market on the value of integrating with Guru's knowledge network</li>
<li>You will identify and drive developer relationships that align to Guru's ecosystem strategy</li>
<li>You will define, track and report on key metrics that are aligned to annual, quarterly and monthly goals to produce measurable outcomes/results</li>
<li>You will establish and sustain yourself as a trusted advisor within the developer community, with well-defined objectives, in-depth understanding of developer's requirements &amp; working internally to scale and devise the optimal means of supporting them</li>
<li>You will help to uncover new opportunities for this community, devise an internal business plan to review with internal constituents and work effectively against the plan</li>
<li>You will work with all internal stakeholders (sales, engineering, marketing, customer success/support and the executive team) to ensure success of the developer network and Guru's Corporate SMART goals are aligned and achieved</li>
</ul>
-------------
<br><br>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
-------------
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>The right person will possess 4+ years of technology partner management, technical evangelist, API technical evangelist, and/or developer community management experience in the software-technology industry</li>
<li>Strong business and technical acumen</li>
<li>Experience presenting and articulating complex both business and technical concepts to cross-functional audiences</li>
<li>Experience engaging and influencing business and technical engineers + executives</li>
<li>Expertise in creating and managing output metrics on customer and partner API use</li>
<li>Consistently exceeded key performance metrics</li>
<li>Experience working with strategic partners and customers</li>
<li>Strong verbal and written communication skills are a requirement, as well as the ability to work effectively across internal and external organizations</li>
</ul>
