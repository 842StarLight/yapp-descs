<h2>SVP, Data Solutions</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">About Us
<br><br>
MediaMath's mission is to help innovative marketers delight their customers and drive real business outcomes. Over 9,500 marketers in 42 countries use our enterprise solution to reach the right user with the right message for the right price, wherever they are. These are the leading brands and agencies in the world who rely on MediaMath to provide technology, services and leadership to help them continue to transform their marketing practices and compete in a quickly changing industry. Founded in 2007 as a pioneer in "programmatic" advertising, MediaMath has been recognized as a leader in the space in four independent evaluations over the last two years. MediaMath has offices in 16 cities worldwide and is headquartered in New York City.
<br><br>
Key Responsibilities
<br><br>
MediaMath is home to industry-leading data solutions, which include a Data Management Platform, Audience solutions, and ConnectedID, MediaMath's non-PII targeting solution, connecting our own probabilistic and deterministic targeting signals with those of almost any other party powering meaningful engagement across devices and browsers
<br><br>
Some of the world's largest advertisers and agencies depend on our data solutions portfolio to onboard data, create audiences, activate those audiences, effectively target, and more deeply understand and analyze audience behavior and profiles.
<br><br>
We're looking for a senior leader for our Data Solutions, who can lead the next phase of product evolution, as MediaMath continues to invest in these areas. This leader will drive the development and strategy for DMP, Identity and Audiences. The right candidate brings deep domain expertise, a clear point of view on the direction of data management, digital identity and audience activation; and has an expert handle on the needs of the clients we delight (not only advertisers and agencies, but also valuable industry partnerships).
<br><br>
<b>You will:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Own and manage and set the product strategy for MediaMath's data solutions portfolio: DMP, Identity and Audiences</li>
<li>Drive and be accountable for the success of MediaMath's integrated data solutions</li>
<li>Serve as a subject matter expert, internally and externally, working with our teams, our clients, industry analysts and our executive team to drive maximum value</li>
<li>Lead data solutions strategy, including business case creation, assessment and build, buy partner decisions with partner strategy ownership</li>
<li>Lead the teams to set and deliver the product roadmap</li>
<li>Ensure policies and procedures in place as needed for product development; including Legal compliance</li>
<li>Partner with Engineering on the development, QA, and release of products and features</li>

<li>Experienced managing a team directly and through influence in a matrixed scenario</li>
<li>Strong manager of people and projects across reporting lines; passion for building and leading high-functioning, engaged teams</li>
</ul>
