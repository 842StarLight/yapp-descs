<div>
<p>Technical Services Analyst</p>
<p>The Technical Services Analyst will provide support for PC hardware configuration/installation, Microsoft software setup, network connectivity, application/network troubleshooting, video conference troubleshooting/support, cellular smartphone setup/support and telephone system support. The analyst will troubleshoot reported problems in a timely and accurate manner and provide end user training/assistance when required.</p>
<p>Essential Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li><div>Install, configure, setup, support and maintain all personal computers and peripherals according to enterprise standards.
</div></li>
<li><div>Perform basic troubleshooting, assist user personnel with issues and conduct training as needed.
</div></li>
<li><div>Provide support for Office 365, Microsoft Active Directory, Office Professional, Exchange, Outlook, Lync/Skype and System Center.
</div></li>
<li><div>Provide basic support for Enterprise applications.</div></li>
<li><div>
Maintain enterprise security standards - Active Directory, virus protection, browser controls, cell phones.</div></li>
<li><div>
Troubleshoot routine problems associated with local LAN and WAN connectivity.
</div></li>
<li><div>Provide support of Atlanta video conference systems including training as necessary.
</div></li>
<li><div>Configure, install, support and maintain local telephony hardware, software and peripherals.
</div></li>
<li><div>Setup and support enterprise standard smartphones and other cellular devices.
</div></li>
<li><div>Maintain familiarity and compliance with company technical standards, policies and procedures.
</div></li>
<li><div>Handle assigned tasks related to documentation, reporting, etc.
</div></li>
<li><div>Perform tasks on projects of all sizes and scopes as assigned by senior staff.
</div></li>
<li><div>Insure that all problems and associated resolutions are entered into the Remedy Force ticketing system.
</div></li>
<li><div>Prepare management reports and metrics as required.</div></li>
<li><p>
Handle other duties as assigned. Work after hours as necessary to minimize business interruptions (system outages, troubleshooting, system upgrades, etc ).</p></li>
</ul>
<p>Required Qualifications:
</p>
<ul>
<li><div>Minimum of 3 years of experience as a Technical Services Analyst, Help Desk technician or other position of equal or higher job grade in the IT Department.</div></li>
<li><div>
Strong PC hardware setup and troubleshooting skills</div></li>
<li><div>
Strong user skills with Microsoft Office Professional (Outlook, Word, Excel, PowerPoint)</div></li>
<li><div>
Strong Microsoft Active Directory(AD) skills
</div></li>
<li><div>Microsoft certification(s) desired</div></li>
<li><div>
Two or four year technical or related degree preferred or equivalent experience</div></li>
<li><div>
3+ years general experience with Microsoft Office and Microsoft operating systems
</div></li>
<li><div>Strong interpersonal and verbal/written communication skills with co-workers, vendors and customers.</div></li>
<li><div>
Ability to solve and document problems quickly and completely.</div></li>
<li><div>
Ability to work independently with little supervision.</div></li>
<li><div>
Demonstration of strong decision making skills.</div></li>
<li><div>
Ability to multi-task and adjust priorities based on workload.</div></li>
<li><p>
Excellent coordination and time management skills.
</p></li>
</ul>
