<h2>Chief Financial Officer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div><div><div>
<div>
<div>Colony Brands, Inc. is one of North America’s largest and most successful direct marketing catalog and e-Commerce companies. Our enterprise has grown from a premier mail-order cheese company to include a diverse portfolio of subsidiaries, brands, and products ranging from apparel and kitchen appliances to food and home décor, while maintaining our roots as a private, customer-centric business based in Monroe, WI. We work together with several affiliate businesses ranging from IT and financial services to corporate aviation to contribute to the continued success of our enterprise.
<br>
<br>
Our continued success is dependent on our intelligent, highly skilled, and motivated workforce. We are seeking a Chief Financial Officer who can build on that success by not only developing financial strategies to meet company sales and profit objectives, but by also building strong relationships and teams. Are you an engaged and seasoned leader who brings out the best in others? Do you relentlessly pursue excellence from yourself? If so, then Colony Brands may be the place for you to significantly impact our business and your career!<br>
<br>
What You’ll Do:<br>
The Chief Financial Officer is primarily responsible for maintaining the strong financial position of our enterprise and advising the leadership team of financial strategies to meet company goals. You’ll have the opportunity to positively impact a stable, family-owned, people-oriented company in business since 1926. You will be accountable for partnering with the organization’s varied companies, lines of business, and key stakeholders to ensure all financial objectives, plans, initiatives, and activities are aligned to meet and adapt to the goals of the organization in a continually changing market. You will have a critical role in the execution of key business initiatives.<br>
<br>
You’ll also be responsible for:
<br>
<ul>
<li>Developing both long- and short-term strategies to manage the complexities of the business within an appropriate level of financial risk</li>
<li>Providing trusted subject matter expertise as a partner to the leadership team and strategic guidance to business leaders at varying levels</li>
<li>Analyzing financial plans, reports, and projections at all levels of the enterprise and directing the preparation of all financial and government/regulatory reporting</li>
<li>Cultivating a culture that rewards and recognizes success and encourages curiosity, entrepreneurial spirit, and continuous improvement</li>
<li>Sustaining our commitment to generously support our employees and communities</li>
<li>Developing competent, productive, successful teams and individuals and providing direct leadership to the Accounting, Payroll, and Finance functions</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>
<div>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<div>What It Takes:<br>
We need a seasoned, successful, yet humble leader with business experience, financial acumen, and a strong work ethic. This person must thrive in a dynamic, cross-functional, collaborative team environment. Ideally you will have a master’s degree in business, finance, or a related discipline and experience within the consumer products, retail, or credit industries.
<br>
In addition, we are looking for:<br>
<ul>
<li>10+ years’ strategic leadership of business and/or financial functions</li>
<li>Proven ability to lead and function in a practical, solutions-oriented, hands-on environment</li>
<li>Acute knowledge of the retail and/or credit marketplace</li>
<li>Experience evolving critical business capabilities</li>
<li>Recognized ability to lead people and achieve positive results through others</li>
<li>Demonstrated analytical, problem-solving, and change management skills</li>
<li>Unwavering integrity and commitment to ethical business practices</li>
<li>Exceptional communication skills</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>
