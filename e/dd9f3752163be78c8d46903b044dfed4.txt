<h2>Director of Marketing</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Chirp Microsystems, a TDK Group Company, is bringing ultrasound to everyday products. Chirp’s ultrasonic sensor solutions enable products that perceive our three-dimensional world. Combined with Chirp’s embedded software library, these sensors enable new user interfaces for wearables, IoT and smart-home, virtual reality and augmented reality (AR/VR), and many more products. Learn more at www.chirpmicro.com.</p>
<br>
<p>
Chirp is currently searching for a <b>Director of Marketing.</b> In this role, you will direct the development of market requirements for Chirp’s ultrasonic product line, including product strategy definition, requirements analysis, and<br>
pricing. You will monitor the development of business plans, assesses market penetration and product positioning to drive competitive advantage, revenue and market share. You will reduce broad concepts and business strategies into<br>
structured product marketing plans. You will oversee market research, monitor competitive activity, identify customer needs, establish pricing strategies and develop marketing tools for successful product introductions. You will<br>
communicate customer feedback to engineering, manufacturing and sales to enhance existing products, collaborate with sales and engineering to lead new product definition and business plans.</p>
<p><b>
Job Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Lead all product line business planning activities: market/technology trends, market sizing (TAM, SAM, SOM), key customers to win, competitive analysis, product positioning and pricing.</li>
<li>Define a compelling product roadmap with clear value propositions that are validated and endorsed by leading customers.</li>
<li>Lead all product MRD and PRD activities to ensure engineering and cross-functional teams are all in-sync to execute what is needed to secure key design-wins.</li>
<li>Drive entire product life-cycle from cradle to grave.</li>
<li>Develop product strategies with sales and cross-functional teams that yield design wins at targeted applications/accounts.</li>
<li>Manage key ecosystem and technology alliances for product line success.</li>
<li>Partner with sales to develop key customer account plans</li>
<li>Collaborate with engineering to develop reference design solutions for leading use cases and architecture engagements with leading data center customers and their platform partners.</li>
<li>Educate/train Sales and FAEs on market and product line plans.</li>
<li>Help corporate marketing develop product line digital marketing and social media messaging.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Required Skills:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Excellent communication, interpersonal and presentation skills.</li>
<li>“Can-do” self-starter with strong cross-functional leadership and teamwork skills.</li>
<li>Strategic analytic mind who has had success in the conception and launch of new products.</li>
<li>Demonstrated product life-cycle management across whole semiconductor product NPI process.</li>
<li>Demonstrated experience coordinating programs across multiple organizations</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Experience:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>5+ years of Semiconductor Marketing experience</li>
<li>10+ years of relevant Semiconductor IC development (e.g. Application, Design) experience</li>
<li>Working with or in cross-functional and international team experience</li>
<li>Product pricing and revenue forecasting experience</li>
<li>Experience managing product line managers</li>
</ul>
