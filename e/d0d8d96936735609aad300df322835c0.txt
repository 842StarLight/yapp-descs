<p><b>SUMMARY</b> - As our Content Guru, you'll work closely with senior leadership to direct the operational planning and delivery process for all content internal and external (from web copy and blogs to reporting and story-telling).</p>
<p>You will partner closely with teams across digital brand, commerce, loyalty, product management, customer support, technology and global brand to advance Sunstone’s long-term strategic operational agenda for content creation and delivery.</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES </b>other duties as assigned…</p>
<ul>
<li>Displays excellent communication skills including writing, editing, presentation, persuasion, and storytelling.</li>
<li>Lead (and produce most of) the content curation, development, and delivery across all channels.</li>
<li>Partner with senior leaders to ensure the brand marketing and content delivery functions are set up for success to deliver quarterly objectives across all channels.</li>
<li>Be the Content Librarian for Sunstone (from building how it is stored to knowing where each piece is).</li>
</ul>
<p><b>JOB DUTIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Measure and enhance the implementation/operation of content planning and follow through across Sunstone’s suite of channels, from a seasonal perspective, and day-to-day.</li>
<li>Work with cross-functional partners to ensure milestones are met and workflows adhered to strategic planning and vision.</li>
<li>Proactively interact with Sunstone’s marketing, customer service, sales, and product management to improve overall communication (written and verbal) and to insure that it stays within Branding Guidelines.</li>
<li>Provide tailored responses for various written communication needs (public relations, scripts, bios, storytelling, etc.)</li>
<li>Participates in and project manages various campaigns and marketing projects.</li>
<li>Looks for opportunities to share customer “good news” with the company or with specific departments or individuals as it arises.</li>
<li>Research and present Industry Trends, Customer Experience ideas, and other marketing type projects.</li>
<li>Develop positive relationships with other employees in Customer Support, Manufacturing, Finance, Marketing and other departments as needed.</li>
<li>Ability to understand and communicate safety instructions, company polices and operational instructions in English.</li>
<li>Maintains punctual, regular and predictable attendance.</li>
<li>Works collaboratively in a team environment with a spirit of cooperation.</li>
<li>Respectfully takes direction from Manager.</li>
</ul>
