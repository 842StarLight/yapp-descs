<h2>Real Estate Transaction Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>We are looking for a professional, dedicated, passionate, and hard-working individual to join The Craig Team of REMAX Professionals. You must be friendly, energetic, self-motivated and extremely detail oriented with excellent organizational skills. It is critical that you have excellent phone/communication skills and the ability to multi-task. Real estate office experience is preferred.</p>
<p>You will be part of a Top Producing Real Estate team that provides high quality service to their clients. If you thrive in a fast-paced environment, like responsibility and the ability to make things happen, and have excellent communication skills this may be the position for you!</p>
<p>Responsibilities:</p>
<p>Provide concierge level customer service.</p>
<p>Be the first point of contact in handling customer inquiries and concerns.</p>
<p>Oversees all aspects of buyer and seller transactions from executed purchase agreement to closing.</p>
<p>Coordinate title/escrow. Mortgage loan and appraisal processes.</p>
<p>Coordinate inspections, assist in negotiations regarding repairs, and coordinate completion of repairs.</p>
<p>Responsible for keeping the entire team updated on the progress throughout the escrow and advises team members when they need to become involved in negotiations or problem resolution.</p>
<p>Regularly update and maintain communication with clients, agents, title officer, lender, etc.</p>
<p>Coordinate paperwork/disclosures, scheduling, client care, tracking, critical dates for property sales transaction and commission requests.</p>
<p>Submit all necessary documentation to office broker for file compliance.</p>
<p>Schedule and coordinate the closing signings for client and agent.</p>
<p>Coordinate moving/possession schedules.</p>
<p>Input all client information into client database.</p>
<p>Schedule 30 Day, 90 Day and 120 Day client service follow up calls to assist with any home improvement provider recommendations and to ask for referrals.</p>
<p>Qualifications:</p>
<p>Prior Real Estate Administration or Transaction Coordination experience is beneficial.</p>
<p>Well-versed in Microsoft Office programs (Word, Excel, Outlook, etc.) and able to use real estate databases and Internet-based software.</p>
<p>Ability to coordinate and communicate with staff and agents in a warm and helpful manner using exceptional customer service skills, over the phone and in person, with our agents, maintaining a professional positive service-oriented attitude.</p>
<p>Ability to work under pressure and handle multiple projects at once with regards to communication, timely task completion, prioritization and accuracy while being cost-effective, resourceful and solutions oriented.</p>
<p>Ability and willingness to work as a team player, assisting others when needed.</p>
<p>Extremely detailed-oriented and exceptional organizational skills.</p>
<p>Professional demeanor and presence, highly-motivated and passionate, trustworthy and dependable.</p>
