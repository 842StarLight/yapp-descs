<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Account Executive (Inside Sales) at ClientSuccess</b></h1>
<p>If you’re in sales and love developing genuine relationships with prospects, diagnosing their needs, and helping solve them, then you could be the right candidate for us.<br>
ClientSuccess is looking for team-oriented inside sales reps who have proven success in exceeding quotas in a previous sales role. We’re an early stage startup and we’re looking for rockstars to help build the next elite sales team in Utah!</p>
<div>
<b>
Key Responsibilities</b><br>
<b>
</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Successfully build, manage, and close new business through relationship based selling</li>
<li>Master the ability to evangelize the ClientSuccess story</li>
<li>Achieve and exceed monthly and quarterly quotas</li>
<li>Align ClientSuccess solutions with prospect business objectives/needs</li>
<li>Successfully manage and overcome prospect objections</li>
<li>Collaborate effectively with the account team and others to drive new business</li>
<li>Proactively keep abreast of our industry and the technology landscape to ensure you are a trusted resource to prospects and customers</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>
<b>
Required Qualifications</b><br>
<b>
</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Recent B2B SaaS selling experience (hard requirement)</li>
<li>Passion for client success</li>
<li>Demonstrated leadership ability (we want natural team players)</li>
<li>Tenacious hunger to win business and close deals</li>
<li>Demonstrated experience and success in a sales role, preferably software sales</li>
<li>Experience with quota and commission compensation structure</li>
<li>Ability to confidently and effectively speak with prospects of all levels (including CEO’s &amp; VPs)</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communications skills</li>
<li>Highly organized and strong time management skills</li>
<li>Ability to work in fast paced, changing environment with minimal direction</li>
<li>Self driven, motivated and results oriented</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>
<b>
Preferred Qualifications</b><br>
<b>
</b><br>
<ul>
<li>4-year degree preferred in Business or related field</li>
<li>Two years B2B software sales experience</li>
<li>Proven success in penetrating new markets and closing new business</li>
<li>Consistent over-achievement in past positions</li>
<li>Salesforce.com or other CRM experience</li>
<li>Proven prospecting and sales cycle management skills</li>
</ul>
</div>
