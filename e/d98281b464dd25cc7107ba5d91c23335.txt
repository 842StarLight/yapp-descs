<h2>Branch Collection Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Your Impact</b></p>
<p>
At OneMain, Branch Collection Specialists empower customers – listening to their needs and providing access to friendly, fast and affordable financing for life’s expenses. In this position, you will have the opportunity to change lives on a daily basis while developing valuable skills that elevate your career.</p>
<p><b>
About You
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>You are motivated, goal orientated, and enthusiastic</li>
<li>
You believe in a workplace that fosters growth opportunities for those who are willing to earn them</li>
<li>
You have exceptional communication and follow-up skills</li>
<li>
People find you engaging and outgoing</li>
<li>
You thrive in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>
You’re willing to listen and learn, building sales competencies and strategies that can help you advance your career</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
In the Role</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
Utilizes advanced negotiation skills to resolve delinquency issues.</li>
<li>
Utilizes computer software to document calls, analyze customer problems, and resolve basic issues.</li>
<li>
Utilizes all available tools and technology to influence customer behavior with established procedures.</li>
<li>
Uses clear procedures and instructions to make choices among alternative choices of action.</li>
<li>
Works under direct supervision and established procedures.</li>
<li>
May support a range of products/services.</li>
<li>
Applies knowledge of broad work area procedures and activities; and most of the impact is related to the accuracy of tasks performed.</li>
<li>
Applies attention to detail and task accuracy.</li>
<li>
Shares knowledge with less experienced collectors.</li>
<li>
Has increased opportunity to interpret procedures/instructions by choosing among alternative courses of action to resolve delinquencies.</li>
<li>
Understands how duties relate/integrate with others in the team/unit.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Other Requirements
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree or some college preferred</li>
<li>
Relevant work experience in sales or customer service a plus</li>
<li>
Valid Driver’s License and Reliable Transportation is strongly recommended</li>
</ul>
<p>
OR</p>
<ul><li>
HS Diploma/GED; and</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Over one year of full-time, relevant work experience<ul>
<li>
Sales experience (e.g. retail sales, positions with sales goals, commission sales, account executive); or</li>
<li>
Management or supervisory experience in a sales or services field</li>
</ul>
</li></ul>
