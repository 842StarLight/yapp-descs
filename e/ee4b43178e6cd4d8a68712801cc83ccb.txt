<h2>Bookkeeper</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>POSITION SUMMARY</b></p>
<p>-This position will serve as a bookkeeper for Nicolosi Foods Inc. in Union City, New Jersey. The company is an established, family owned business that has been successfully serving the community for over 60 years.</p>
<p><b>MAJOR DUTIES</b></p>
<p>-Perform all sales and associated record keeping</p>
<p>-Perform all billing and associated record keeping</p>
<p>-Conduct financial deposits</p>
<p>-ability to run reports</p>
<p>-build costing and yields for the owner</p>
<p>-Generate and maintain reports and charts of accounts</p>
<p>-Perform customer collections</p>
<p>-Reconcile and post all credit cards</p>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS &amp; EXPERIENCE</b></p>
<p>-A minimum of 5 years experience in accounting practices and procedures</p>
<p>-Proven experience in professional customer service</p>
<p>-Excellent ability to communicate both orally and in writing</p>
<p>-Demonstrated ability to be resourceful and work independently</p>
<p>-Demonstrated sound time management skills</p>
<p>-Demonstrated detailed-oriented skills</p>
<p>-Thorough knowledge of computers and software programs to include but not limited to QuickBooks, MS Word and Excel</p>
<p>-Must be legal to work in the United States</p>
<p><b>SALARY/WORK SCHEDULE</b></p>
<p>-Salary is commensurate upon experience</p>
<p>-It is a part time, hourly position but may lead to full time employment</p>
<p>Job Type: Part-time</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul><li>Quickbooks: 5 years (Preferred)</li></ul>
