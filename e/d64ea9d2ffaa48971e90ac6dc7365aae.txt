<h2>Purchasing Agent</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>AirTrim is one of the leading suppliers of pneumatic waste and dust handling systems in North America. For almost 30 years, AirTrim has grown as a proven, competent, single source supplier who designs, manufactures, installs and supports a full line of patented products and systems to a wide variety of industries. Our objective is to become the leading source for this type of technology in North America.</p>
<p>AirTrim's mission is to attract and develop talented and committed people by creating a workplace where in a teamwork environment everyone contributes and is rewarded for AirTrim's success.</p>
<p>Primary role will be Purchasing Agent with backup assistance in Financial and Human Resources as needed.</p>
<p><b>Purchasing responsibilities include: </b></p>
<p>Release work order jobs to manufacturing through E2 Shop Tech System (will train)</p>
<p>Develop multiple, positive vendor relationships</p>
<p>Follow up vendor purchase orders to insure on time receipt</p>
<p>Insure compliance to all mandated OSHA requirements</p>
<p>Inventory control and analysis</p>

<p>Medical insurance evaluation and communication</p>
