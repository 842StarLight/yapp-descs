<p><b>
Product Designer</b></p>
<p>
We are looking for a qualified and talented Product Designer to join our team. As a Product Designer, you will be an important part of the Product Design team and specifically focus on designing exceptional user experiences for our suite of Events.com products.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities and Duties</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Understand business objectives, be really team-oriented, and guide product through iterations</li>
<li>Create high-quality user-centered design concepts for mobile, tablet, desktop, native or web</li>
<li>Collaborate with other designers and team members on concept generation</li>
<li>Develop interactive prototypes for user testing and rapid iteration</li>
<li>Write user stories based on designs and user requirements</li>
<li>Define user task and interaction flows</li>
<li>Define and refine the user experience of features and products to achieve company goals</li>
<li>Communicate conceptual ideas and design rationale to other members of the product design, engineering, customer success and sales teams</li>
<li>Identify user research needs and coordinate studies based on need</li>
<li>Create designs that work simply, elegantly, intuitively and engage users</li>
<li>Develop your role at Events.com as we grow and succeed as a company</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Minimum Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>BA or BS Degree or higher in Human Computer Interaction (HCI) or design related-field</li>
<li>Experience with SAAS products</li>
<li>Passion, experience and talent for user-centered interaction design</li>
<li>Portfolio that demonstrates outstanding proficiency in native application and web application interaction design, including iOS and Android platforms</li>
<li>Knowledge and experience with the software design processes, UI design patterns, user-centered design principles and best practices</li>
<li>Agile development process knowledge and story writing experience</li>
<li>Experience with user-centered design methodologies, including information architecture, wire framing, prototyping, interaction flows and user research</li>
<li>Demonstrated proficiency with design tools (including Adobe Suite and Axure)</li>
<li>Understanding of software programming (i.e. HTML, Java script, and other computer software languages) in order to liaise with the software implementation team</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously</li>
<li>A portfolio of your work must be included in your resume to be considered</li>
<li>Excellent time management skills</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal, verbal, and written communication skills</li>
