The team
<br>Our Product &amp; Program Management teams work on some of the most impactful and challenging problems in the Software industry. We are a highly collaborative cross-border group that operates on the edge between Engineering and the business and helps to design solutions from concept to launch – all while focusing on and advocating for what's best for our customers. <br><br>
This position
<br>As an Associate Product Manager, you will be given impactful projects, while working closely with some of the best product leaders in the industry. The candidate will have the opportunity to build new features and products that touch millions of users and impact 10's of thousands of corporations in a wide range of industries around the world. In this role, you will work on discovery, conceptualization and launch of key new features and products that impact how DocuSign customers engage with our UI as well as API. You will work cross-functionally to develop the roadmap, vision, definition, design and delivery of delightful customer experiences and growth of the business.
<br><br>
This position reports to the Senior Director, Product Management.
<br><br>
Responsibilities
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Assist in building the strategy and roadmap for various DocuSign product offerings</li>
<li>Assist in developing requirements and translating them to functional specifications</li>
<li>Leverage primary and secondary research to deepen understanding of customers and their use cases</li>
<li>Stay abreast of industry trends, events, and channels to anticipate customer needs</li>
<li>Drive insights on the markets and segments in which DocuSign product offerings compete</li>
<li>Evaluate and prioritize requirements in partnership with cross-functional teams (sales, marketing, product development, etc)</li>
<li>Facilitate requirements definition with design and engineering partners while identifying dependencies and relative priority with other product managers</li>
<li>Partner with Program Management, Design and Engineering to ensure product ships on time and to quality</li>
<li>Define metrics and quantify success for features and products for which you are responsible</li>
</ul>
