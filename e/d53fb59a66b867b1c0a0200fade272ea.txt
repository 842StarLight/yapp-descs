<p></p>
<p>The Client Support Engineer position primary responsibility is to provide the on-site leadership and technical support for the TradeRev office location. They are the “face” of the KAR IT organization outside the Downtown Indianapolis location. In addition, the Client Support Engineer will be responsible for but not limited to PC/Laptop installation/repair, local telephony and systems management, asset inventory and customer support through our web-based ticket system. Perform a full range of IT tasks e.g., server administration tasks, administering end-point management desktop tools, upkeep of local telephony systems, and provide local desktop and asset inventory support. Must know, practice and ensure that company policies and procedures are followed at all times. Ensure that no company, employee or customer information is inappropriately divulged to unauthorized parties. The Client Support Engineer will participate in maintaining local systems that require high availability. The position reports to the Supervisor of Support Services.
</p>
<p></p>
