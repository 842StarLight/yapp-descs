<p><b>
Responsibilities</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Lead cross-functional projects using advanced data modeling and analysis techniques to discover insights that will guide strategic decisions and uncover optimization opportunities.</li>
<li>
Recruit, train, develop and supervise analyst-level BI employees.</li>
<li>
Provide managerial / expert guidance on Business Intelligence (BI) skills and technologies.</li>
<li>
Gain insight from customer stories, financial data, and operational data to uncover and quantify key pain points created throughout the customer journey to drive continuous improvement in the customer experience across the organization.
</li>
<li>Build strong partnerships and breadth of knowledge across multiple functions, regional locations, and key stakeholders including Supply Chain, New Equipment, Sales, Finance, Marketing, and Service.
</li>
<li>Provide functional and technical expertise and understand the business needs and objectives to guide analytics activities across the organization</li>
<li>
Partner with Global Data &amp; Analytics team in the design, development and delivery of analytics solutions and insights to ensure business needs and data are incorporated and in line with overall Enterprise strategic direction</li>
<li>
Oversee advanced analytics projects to extract, manage, and analyze customer data from multiple data sources and locations to drive business insights and operational efficiencies .</li>
<li>
Apply statistics and data modeling to gain actionable business insights and boost sales and service productivity and revenue generation .</li>
<li>
Examine, interpret and report results of analytical initiatives to stakeholders within financial, sales, service, marketing and product teams.</li>
<li>
Communicate and track key performance metrics across departments.</li>
<li>
