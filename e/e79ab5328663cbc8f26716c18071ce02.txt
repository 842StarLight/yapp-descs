<h2>Food Demonstrator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Food Demonstrator / Flexible Hours</b></p>
<p>A food demonstration agency has part-time positions (full-time potential) available in the Seattle area working inside a food-service wholesale chain.</p>
<p>We need outgoing people to cook and demonstrate food products, create relationships with business owners and chefs and gather relevant feedback pertinent to sales of the products. Previous cooking, brand ambassador or sales experience is a plus, but we'll train the right people. <b><i>We offer a competitive rate of pay!</i></b></p>
<p><b><i>No weekends!</i></b></p>
<p>This is great for anyone looking for flexible, permanent, part-time work. Work is conducted on weekdays between the hours of 7:00 am and 4:00 pm, but hours are flexible. This is a contract position. Shifts are typically 5 hours long.</p>
<p><b>RESPONSIBILITES <i>MAY</i> INCLUDE: </b></p>
<p>· Positively engage customers to promote and increase sales of product</p>
<p>· Maintain a friendly positive image, attitude and appearance</p>
<p>· Maintain a professional, clean, healthy work environment</p>
<p>· Serve and demonstrate products enthusiastically, providing helpful hints and uses of product</p>
<p>· Set up and break down of the work area, product preparation and sampling</p>
<p>· Ensure products are on the shelves</p>
<p>· Work with store staff for POS placement</p>
<p>· Adhere to all Food Safety, Handling and Sanitation training and guidelines</p>
<p>· Provide daily, accurate and timely reporting of time worked, sales results and meaningful customer comments by required deadlines</p>
<p>· Cleaning equipment after use</p>
<p><b>EXPERIENCE REQUIREMENTS: </b></p>
<p>· Interact in a friendly, enthusiastic and outgoing manner with management and customers</p>
<p>· Must be able to stand the duration of the shift and perform routine tasks with minimal supervision</p>
<p>· Able to work independently and as a motivated team player</p>
<p>· Requires daily access to a PC with internet/email access</p>
<p>· Requires experience with photo editing, Microsoft WORD and Excel software</p>
<p>· Requires bending, walking, stooping, reaching, kneeling, twisting, pushing and pulling</p>
<p>· Must have reliable transportation</p>
<p>· Part-time, permanent schedule</p>
<p>· Excellent communication skills</p>
<p>· Neat appearance and good grooming</p>
<p><b>ADMINISTRATIVE WORK</b></p>
<p>· Study product materials to develop product knowledge</p>
<p>· Complete reports</p>
<p>· Check voicemails, texts and emails</p>
<p>· Participate in scheduled calls with Supervisor/others as needed</p>

<p>The above statements are intended to describe the general nature and level of work being performed by people assigned to this position. This is not intended to be an exhaustive list of all responsibilities, duties and skills required.</p>
<p>Job Type: Part-time</p>
<p>Salary: $18.00 to $20.00 /hour</p>
