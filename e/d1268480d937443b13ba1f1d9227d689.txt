<div><b>The Role: Senior Analyst, Billing Resolution<br>
</b></div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Purpose
</b></div>
<div>The Billing Resolutions Senior Analyst will be responsible for, but not limited to, project management on behalf of the billing and collections team, developing, supporting and enhancing the products that we sell, applicable tax rules as we enter into new markets, regulatory compliance requirements, and the ability to troubleshoot and resolve customer concerns that impact billing accuracy. This position will also require self-managing the accurate and timely completion of routine tasks that affect contract pricing, financial reporting and data integrity. The Senior Analyst will be expected to contribute to key billing functions within independent and cross functional team projects. This role will provide training and support to peers for billing and controls related inquiries. The Senior Analyst will work with our third-party vendors, both independently and in a team environment as needed, to provide best in class customer service for our internal and external customers.<br>
</div>

<div><b>Essential Job Functions
</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Build an understanding of the business at a level of detail that enables them to identify and address critical issues
</li>
<li>Analyze complex business problems and issues using data from internal and external sources to provide insight to analysts and decision makers
</li>
<li>Develop strategies and write up business cases to implement system and process changes that will impact billing and its interaction with other teams, both internal and external
</li>
<li>Present status updates to management and billing team
</li>
<li>Subject matter expert for various projects that involve system enhancements, product development, market changes, and system testing
</li>
<li>Provide training and/or documentation to peers for successful completion of billing and controls related tasks.
</li>
<li>Research, correct or delegate the correction of identified discrepancies between our internal data warehouse and external billing system.
</li>
<li>Review and approve requests received from billing vendor and provides instructions to ensure accurate billing and payment application.
</li>
<li>Point of contact for review, approval and delegation of billing and processing related action items involving pre-deal and active deal setup and inquiries.
</li>
<li>Perform customer account reconciliations and provide instructions on necessary adjustments
</li>
<li>Create and manage manual action items and estimated billing statement requests.
</li>
<li>Provide cross-functional support to peers and departments to develop satisfactory end-to-end delivery processes.
</li>
<li>Create and/or manage ad-hoc control reports in Excel, Access, Power BI or other enterprise systems as necessary.
</li>
<li>Maintain open lines of communication with Compliance group to assure all Business Control regulatory and internal requirements are met or exceeded. Manage customers on prepayment arrangements by calculating the amounts to be billed by the vendor and reconciling the account balances to ensure proper credit assurance.
</li>
<li>Prepare governmental regulatory reporting and provide to the legal team for filing.
</li>
<li>Manage Sales Tax Exempt Process, reviewing certificates, facilitating audit requests from tax department as well as determining if refunds are needed.
</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned<br>
</li>
</ul>
