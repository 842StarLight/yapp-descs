<h2>Customer Experience Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Location:</b> New York City
<br><b>Reporting into:</b> Head of Customer Experience
<br><br>
The Customer Experience team is in many ways the heart of the organization, representing the client perspective and regularly interfacing with every part of the Contently. You'll have a chance to work with some of the largest companies in the world including F100 organizations. Though we certainly know when it's time to get to work, we have a ton of fun together and are known to frequently head out of the office after hours for some social time. Our team has a collaborative spirit and loves to be helpful, meaning you'll be growing with the team as we are constantly looking to improve
<br><br>
<b>What You'll Do:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Serve as the primary relationship owner for an assigned group of top tier client accounts with responsibility for retention and growth</li>
<li>Understand the dynamics of each client's business and execute account plans to maximize Contently's penetration throughout the organization</li>
<li>Sell Contently's technology and services across existing enterprise customers</li>
<li>Ensure clients recognize maximum value from the Contently solution; technology and services</li>
<li>Identify new upsell/cross-sell opportunities within existing accounts to grow revenue</li>
<li>Deliver weekly, monthly, and quarterly status and results presentations to internal and external teams</li>
<li>Prepare and deliver effective client presentations, including stakeholders at all levels of the organization up to C-Suite</li>
<li>Role requires minimum of 20% travel</li>
<li>Ensure a deep enough understanding of clients' individual experiences and issues to maximize their satisfaction</li>
<li>Develop expertise in Contently technology and content marketing methodology in order communicate long-term roadmap and represent client needs internally</li>
</ul>
<b>Who You Are:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>5 years experience in Account Management or Customer Success, ideally for a Martech, Adtech or content oriented B2B solution, supporting F500+ clients</li>
<li>A sales mentality for identifying revenue growth opportunities wrapped in a consultative relationship building body</li>
<li>Dynamic personality able to effectively engage and influence a variety of audiences at all levels of a business</li>
<li>Confident communicator (written and oral) skills, and a demonstrated ability to work collaboratively with all levels of internal and external organizations</li>
<li>A great relationship manager, able to gain trust through communication, expectation setting, and completion of planned deliverables</li>
<li>Business acumen, sound decision making, analytical, and organizational skills in a fast paced environment; a consultative approach to managing complex client relationships</li>
<li>Project and program management experience; knowledge of key concepts including phases, plans, deliverables, scope, and tasks</li>
<li>Ability to prioritize multiple responsibilities, balancing client deliverables on multiple projects as well as internal obligations</li>
<li>Passion about business and dissatisfaction with status quo – always thinking of ways to improve/grow assigned clients</li>
</ul>
