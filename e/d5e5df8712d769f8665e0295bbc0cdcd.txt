<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>About the job</b></h2>
<p>
The Online Partnerships Group (OPG) is a partner-focused organization that prides itself in providing great service, in creating innovative solutions, and most importantly, in having amazing people. We value having an open, inclusive and collaborative culture, where Googlers feel supported and able to do their best work. In offices around the globe, we foster and celebrate an environment where all team members feel welcomed and respected. OPG partners with online publishers and app developers around the world to grow their businesses using our portfolio of products including the AdSense, AdMob, and Google Ad Manager platforms. We offer a solutions-based approach across advertising, analytics, and site or app design, and pride ourselves in finding new opportunities for our partners to better engage users and advertisers. Over a million web publishers and app developers rely on our solution globally, and ultimately, we're proud to enable Google to be one of the foremost funders of digital content in the world.</p>
<p>
As part of the Platforms team, you will be primarily focused on developing a deep understanding of market/customer needs across channels (mobile, video, desktop, etc) and verticals (news, commerce, gaming, etc), partnering across product and front-line partnerships teams to accelerate adoption of Google Ad Manager, AdMob, and AdSense. You will take responsibility for managing and renewing products of an existing book of business while identifying new business opportunities within those strategic accounts. You will be a key member of a larger team to ensure Google’s top partners receive excellent account management and strategic consulting.
</p>
<p>
Google helps our partners find better ways to reach their audiences and manage their digital businesses. In return, our partners provide great content for our products, and they syndicate Google products to enhance their sites and apps. Global Partnerships team members expand and support these relationships by continually finding new opportunities and solving technical problems for our partners. The wider Global Partnerships team includes a variety of different organizations, including CorpDev, Emerging Business Development, Strategy and Operations, Google for Startups, Area 120 and our Display Advertising Business.</p>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Responsibilities</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Work closely with Account Managers to gain an in-depth knowledge of clients’ businesses and organizational structures to identify and execute opportunities for existing clients.</li>
<li>Act as strategic advisor with deep product and industry expertise. Consult partners on industry best practices, advanced platform capabilities, and yield management strategies, including implementation and optimization.
</li>
<li>Understand the full scope of clients’ monetization business. Optimize usage of Google’s monetization suite in consultation with partners based on performance analysis.</li>
<li>Become a trusted advisor to C-level contacts. Expand Google’s platform and business growth by identifying new inventory, emerging channels, and products that support partner growth goals.</li>
<li>Analyze monetization and platform performance and present findings to key clients, influencing partner strategy by highlighting key opportunities to impact business growth through additional product usage, and bring data driven insights to support recommendations.</li>
</ul>
