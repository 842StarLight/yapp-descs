<h2>Leadership Positions</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>We are Located near Hardy and Southern in Tempe, Arizona.</b></h3>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Full time hours are 7-3:30 Monday-Friday. During the summer season some extended hours and overtime are available as needed/desired.</b></h3>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Benefits:</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>Our starting wage is between $15 and $20/hour. We have some flexibility in start pay and offer generous increases based on performance and responsibility levels. Your wage is only limited by your ability to grow.</li>
<li>Health Insurance covered offered the first of the month after 60 days of employment.</li>
<li>Retirement benefits available after 2 years of employment.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>General qualifications:</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>Ability to utilize Microsoft Office (mostly word and excel) proficiently.</li>
<li>Possess strong mathematical, analytical and problem-solving skills.</li>
<li>Work well with diverse types of people.</li>
<li>Able to positively guide the work culture to be upbeat, encouraging, and motivating, with effective yet considerate communication between all employees, including those in leadership roles.</li>
<li>Willing to be unforgivingly honest when addressing the strengths and weaknesses of employees and the organization with management.</li>
<li>Have a genuine interest in the welfare of the entire organization, including employee welfare and company profit levels.</li>
<li>Willing to compromise and sacrifice during the summer peak season and adjust to company demands during this time.</li>
<li>Flexible with tasks and duties, leaving pride aside to respect “grunt work” as much as higher level functions.</li>
<li>Be able to work with long periods of standing, sitting, moving around, going up and down 12-15 step ladders, lifting 40-80 pound boxes, moving pallets of books, and similar tasks.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Successful applicants for all positions will be trained and expected to perform numerous positions which may include:</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>Book Conditioner—Accurately assess the quality of a book per our standards.</li>
<li>General Laborer—Move books around in the warehouse as necessary to neatly organize them.</li>
<li>Packager—Correctly package ordered books per our standards.</li>
<li>Picker—Correct pick ordered books per our standards.</li>
<li>Postage Printer—Accurately print postage at the lowest cost and most effective delivery in order to satisfy customer’s speed demands while ensuring profit margin on orders is optimized.</li>
<li>Checker—Enforce quality control measures to ensure the correct item(s) in the correct condition are shipped to the correct person.</li>
<li>Lister—Accurately input books into our inventory system for sale.</li>
<li>Listing Manager—train and supervise listers to ensure high levels of quality and efficiency are maintained.</li>
<li>Trainer—train and supervise new employees including book conditioners, pickers, and packagers to ensure high levels of quality and efficiency are maintained.</li>
<li>Fulfillment and Customer Service Manager—Oversee fulfillment process for orders, and respond to customer’s emails related to concerns or questions they may have pre or post order.</li>
<li>Administrative Support Staff—</li>
<ul>
<li>Receive inbound calls from customers wanting to purchase items or discuss difficulties with or questions about orders.</li>
<li>Assist in calling other businesses and inquiring about status of incoming orders or placing orders as needed.</li>
<li>Working with Excel and Google spreadsheets and proprietary software to manage large purchases of textbooks.</li>
<li>Physically audit packing of small and large orders of books to ensure accuracy and on-time delivery.</li>
<li>Issue Refunds for returns according to company standards.</li>
<li>Receive and respond to emails from customers about potential or past orders.</li>
<li>Do administrative work to assist sales and purchasing agents in closing deals.</li>
</ul>
<li>Operations Manager—</li>
<ul>
<li>Oversee all aspects of the operation and correct inefficiencies noted by Quality Assurance Manager (the owner).</li>
<li>Responsible for hiring, training, evaluating, and (as needed) discharging employees.</li>
<li>Ensure that employees maintain a fast and steady pace without losing necessary accuracy.</li>
<li>Assess employees’ skills and motivation in order to optimally deploy labor.</li>
<li>Switch from task to task as needed, even when not particularly expected. This includes not forgetting to eventually complete a task that was interrupted.</li>
<li>Maintain holistic awareness of the operation, even during highly attention-demanding experiences or when urgent issues arise.</li>
<li>When unexpected issues do occur, problem solve effectively and efficiently to determine a solution that diminishes the least amount of the company’s capital while attaining the company’s overarching goals</li>
<li>Weigh and implement changes that affect the company’s costs and operations long-term.</li>
<li>Self-aware and able to understand when help or input is needed in order to complete tasks or make decisions.</li>
<li>Keep calm in inevitable hectic moments to preserve productivity. Also sustain personal motivation in the slow and uneventful moments to preserve the same level of productivity.</li>
</ul>
</ul>
<br>

If you have experience driving a box truck or driving a forklift please mention this as well.</p>
