<h2>Customer Service Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>To connect with a recruiter on current openings, text "ADS" to 44844.</b></p>
<p><b>
POSITION SUMMARY</b><b>:</b></p>
<p>
Respond to and resolve customer service inquiries, requests or complaints; work directly with sales personnel to maintain customer accounts as necessary.</p>
<p><b>
PRIMARY DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<p>
This list of duties and responsibilities is not all inclusive and may be expanded to include other duties and responsibilities as management may deem necessary from time to time.</p>
<ul>
<li>Respond effectively and courteously to customer service inquiries, requests or complaints. Return all customer calls in a timely manner</li>
<li>Notify the appropriate personnel to handle the customer's problems and concerns</li>
<li>Research and resolve issues in a timely manner</li>
<li>Review daily reports with the Customer Service Manager/Office Manager</li>
<li>Enter service and route data into computer for billing and route scheduling purposes</li>
<li>Service all Residential and/or Commercial accounts by setting up new accounts from Service Agreements and maintaining proper flow of paperwork from sales to operations</li>
<li>Route customers for recycling and trash pick-ups</li>
<li>Work closely with dispatch to ensure quality assurance</li>
<li>Assist with collections by reinstating customers, processing reinstatement paperwork and processing collections letters for mailing</li>
<li>Review and make any necessary changes to weekly MSAR</li>
</ul>
<p><b>KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS, AND ABILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Excellent customer services skills</li>
<li>Basic customer skills required for inputting and retrieving data</li>
<li>Strong written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Ability to handle multiple tasks</li>
<li>Ability to work effectively with others in a team environment</li>
</ul>
<p><b>MINIMUM REQUIREMENTS:</b></p>
<p><b>
Education: </b>High School Diploma, GED and/or equivalent work experience</p>
<p><b>
Years of Experience: </b>One year customer service experience</p>
<p><b>
Preferred: </b>6-12 months experience with company procedures, products and service, and experience working in a team environment</p>
<p><b>
Certifications: </b>none</p>
<p><b>
MACHINES, TOOLS, EQUIPMENT AND SOFTWARE USED:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Usual office equipment including calculator, telephone, fax, computer and copier</li>
<li>Microsoft Office: Word, Excel, PowerPoint and Outlook</li>
<li>Company information systems applications</li>
</ul>
<p><b>WORK ENVIRONMENT:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Usual office environment</li>
</ul>
<p><b>TYPICAL PHYSICAL DEMANDS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to organize various office systems through adjusting, connecting, lifting, pulling, pushing, bending or folding for the purpose or proper records development and/or management</li>
<li>Ability to use a computer and related devices, such as a keyboard or mouse or other related equipment, throughout the workday while seated at a desk or workstation</li>
</ul>
<p>Possess speaking skills and hearing ability to interact with customers on the telephone</p>
