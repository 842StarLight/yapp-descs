<h2>Mail Processor Sr</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Under the direction of the Manager, Distribution &amp; Logistical Services, performs a variety of responsibilities in the Post Office. The Post Office is a contract branch of the US Post Office and is expected to follow federal policies and procedures as well as as well as complying with all policies of the University of California. Coordinates and directs (1) FTE and student staff, staying within the policy guidelines of the University.
<br><br>
Minimum Requirements
<br><br>
3+ years working in mail operation.
<br>Familiar with US Postal Service services, postage rates, basic mail and package requirements and standards.
<br>Experience operating postage machines.
<br>Ability to work well in a team environment.
<br><br>
Desirable Requirements
<br><br>
Experience driving pick up truck with box cargo compartment and working with mechanized lift gates.
<br>Certification(s) from mail industry associations, and/or US Postal Service.
<br>3-5 years working in a college/university mail operation.
<br><br>
Special Conditions of Employment

<br>Satisfactory criminal history background check
<br><br>
Other Special Conditions of Employment
<br><br>
Occasional special work hours on weekdays and weekends may be needed as directed by manager. Ability to lift 50 lbs.

General Duties/Training/Supervising
<br><br>
Serves as a working lead to (1) FTE, student employees and student leads in post office. Ensures FTE is trained on all assigned duties and responsibilities working in the UCen Post Office. Trains all student staff in their duties and ensures compliance with UCen policies and the requirements of USPS while working USPS service window. Works with the Manager, Distribution &amp; Logistical Services, regarding staffing for holidays, Saturdays and throughout each school quarter. Along with the student leads, evaluates student employees to decide which students perform effectively, contribute and are ready to assume increased responsibilities including being trained to work at the USPS Service window training and may be eligible for pay increases or incentive awards. Informs students who are not working up to expectations and designs plans to work with them for performance improvement or dismissal.
<br><br>
Ensures customer service standards are met. Interacts with peers, subordinates and manager to assure a smooth operation in area. Responsible for the training of the main post office customer service window which offers all domestic and international mail services. Training includes: Following the Post Office regulations and knowing the various postal rates; selling all types of postage, including certified, registered, insured, express transactions, money orders, package distribution, renting PO boxes, collecting money for PO keys and setting up/closing cash drawers. Know and meet daily deadline for USPS audit reports and ensure Start of Day and End of Day duties are completed.
<br><br>
Teaches non-window transactions such as tax forms, voter registration, passport forms and selective service info. Shows how to sort, box and forward by computer, all classes of incoming mail. Trains FTE and student staff on use of package tracking software, monitoring its use and ensures all software updates are obtained. Notifies publishers of address changes, collects postage due and COD's. Handles telephone inquiries that are for post office or the office.

Window Service
<br><br>
As needed, serves at customer windows, trains and assists student employees on working USPS and package service windows. Maintains adequate cash on hand for USPS transactions. Updates scales with new postage rates as needed; orders and adjusts stamp inventory &amp; USPS shipping stocks to keep up with customer demand.

Mail Pick Up
<br><br>
Picks up incoming mail from the USPS processing center. Moves cages from dock areas to processing area. Operates the University Center truck. Monitors and adjusts the inventory of USPS mail handling carts and cages. Lifts and handles parcels weighing up to 70 lbs.

Troubleshooting
<br><br>
Interacts with customers, coworkers, main post office and residence hall administrators to resolve problems. Researches missing, late or undelivered mail or packages and assists customers with claim forms for refunds and tracking. Works with post office on issues that impact UCen PO (late deliveries, mail not bundled by sections, wrong mail delivered). Works with Manager, Distribution &amp; Logistical Services to correct any of these issues through office restructure, schedule changes, contacting the proper postal authorities, or working with the mail distribution center. Handles questions that the Manager, Distribution &amp; Logistical Services, has regarding the final closeout reports or other issues impacting the UCen Post Office operation.

Planning/Projection/Goals
<br><br>
Works with the Manager, Distribution &amp; Logistical Services to get competitive prices, meets with sales reps and decides what equipment should be purchased. This includes updating/increasing boxes, researching new products, etc.
<br><br>
Works with post office headquarters on procedures regarding mail delivery and pickup expectations. Keeps all USPS records in order and remains prepared for USP financial and service audits and acts on corrective recommendations in a timely manner. Maintains records of student and private PO Box key assignments and reports those who fail to return keys for key assessment charge to BARC. Forwards mail, maintains and updates student permanent addresses lists, maintains lists of assigned PO boxes in alpha and numerical order, and update forwarding files as needed. Keeps boxes labeled with current residents and processes changes as residence halls reassign rooms or "private" boxes are rented or closed. Gives update of area at staff meetings. Discusses issues impacting UCen Post Office with involved parties and escalates to manager.
<br><br>
Works with peers and management to change or amend office policies when necessary. Assists Manager, Distribution &amp; Logistical Services on special project pertaining to UCen Post Office operations. Performs other duties as assigned. Maintains a positive attitude; strives to meet individual and staff performance goals; seeks innovative mail and package handling practices, and pursues customer service goals and expectations.</div>
