<div><b>ROLE OVERVIEW</b></div>
<div>Global Rescue is looking for a highly-motivated individual to help support the day-to-day operations of our busy sales department. The ideal candidate has an entrepreneurial spirit, drive and work ethic; and is willing to “do what it takes” to get the job done. This role will provide support to the sales team by handling client requests, account maintenance, troubleshooting issues, and maintaining CRM. You should have a positive attitude, strong interpersonal skills, and enjoy the idea of working on all different types of projects. Perfect entry-level role for an individual looking to get their foot in the door in sales.</div>
<div></div>
