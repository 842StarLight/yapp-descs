<h2>Purchasing Agent</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<ul>
<li>Coordinating activities involved in procuring goods and services such as raw materials, equipment, tools, parts, and supplies.</li>
<li>Receiving and reconciling invoices.</li>
<li>Hands on working in a manufacturing environment.</li>
<li>Mechanical aptitude is required.</li>
<li>Using Macola software to place orders and handle receipts of incoming materials and supplies.</li>
<li>Negotiate pricing, terms and delivery of needed materials.</li>
<li>Obtain material cost estimates and/or quotations.</li>
<li>Identify, evaluate and select/recommend sources of supply for direct and MRO materials.</li>
<li>Measure and evaluate supplier performance and initiate corrective action where appropriate.</li>
<li>Assist with identifying opportunities for cost reductions and/or productivity improvements.</li>
</ul>
<p>Qualifications:</p>
<ul><li>Three to five years related experience in a manufacturing environment, competent in the use of Internet, Microsoft Word, Excel, and basic manufacturing systems.</li></ul>
