<h2>Solutions Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Overview:</b><br>
At SearchSpring, our technology helps over 600 retailers across 10+ retail verticals create engaging shopping experiences for their customers. Our new Relevancy Platform provides retailers with the tools to make their marketing/merchandising teams more effective, deliver a contextually relevant customer shopping experience across all devices, and empowers employees across all departments with actionable insights.
<br><br>
<b>Job Description:</b><br>
SearchSpring is looking for a Solutions Engineer. In this role you will support our sales team by serving as a technical resource in discussions with prospective clients. You will help to ensure a successful implementation of SearchSpring on our customers’ e-commerce stores by understanding their needs and reviewing their product data. We’re looking for someone who loves helping people to solve problems, working with data, and wants to play a major role in ensuring the success of our clients.
<br><br>
<b>Skills and requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>3+ years in sales, solutions engineering, technical consulting or software development</li>
<li>Experience working with web technologies such as HTML, CSS and JavaScript</li>
<li>Top-notch written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Ability to simplify complex technical subjects</li>
<li>Great problem solving abilities and eager to learn</li>
<li>Comfortable interfacing directly with prospective clients during the sales process</li>
<li>Positive, team-first attitude with the desire to help others</li>
<li>Comfortable in a fast-paced environment with the ability to think on your feet</li>
<li>Experience working with B2B SaaS and/or e-commerce companies</li>
</ul>
