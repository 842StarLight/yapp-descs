<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>The Company is one of North America’s leading sales and marketing agencies specializing in outsourced sales, merchandising, category management and marketing services to manufacturers, suppliers and producers of food products and consumer packaged goods. The Company services a variety of trade channels including grocery, mass merchandise, specialty, convenience, drug, dollar, club, hardware, consumer electronics and home centers. We bridge the gap between manufacturers and retailers, providing consumers access to the best products available in the marketplace today.</p>
<p><b>Position Summary</b></p>
<p>The Client Services Coordinator is responsible for providing key support to the planning, management, and oversight of events and other special projects. This position will use Integrated Marketing Services' proprietary software, PTS, as well as proprietary Client software, and Microsoft programs to generate work requests, coordinate cross-functional tasks, and complete associated duties. Results will be obtained through effective planning, influence building, and use of resources.</p>
<p><b>Essential Job Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>Protecting Accounts</p>
<ul>
<li><p>Document account plans and forecasts: Assess activities to plan; monitor sales forecasts and event requirements in PTS to ensure accuracy; log activities to track progress against the plan</p></li>
<li><p>
Manage and/or monitor deployment readiness and resource alignment: Ensure accurate understanding of mutually agreed client deliverables (e.g., terms and</p></li>
<li><p>
conditions, service-level agreements) and ensure knowledge is dispersed amongst personnel engaged in post-sale activity (e.g., operations, field execution); when needed; act as the focal point for event education and preparation within individual geographies; create client communication to ensure operational alignment</p></li>
</ul>
<p>Maintaining Accounts</p>
<ul>
<li><p>Prepare standard and ad hoc reports on account status: Analyze account performance and contractual status data (e.g. compliance, out of stock, consumer engagement data, etc.); research customer or account business health and other qualification variables that quantify risk or opportunity (e.g. retail market trends, individual shopper trends, etc.); generate report on event readiness and business performance as scheduled or on demand (e.g. capacity reports, product procurement reports, etc.)</p></li>
<li><p>
Provide task substitution assistance in order to facilitate sales: Act in parallel to customer-facing sales in executing any back-office or operational tasks involved with aggregating data that will advance or formalize new deals; enter data using appropriate systems and processes and track processing to completion</p></li>
<li><p>
Craft agreements and formal documents in securing new business: Assemble standard agreements for use by others as boilerplates for non-complex opportunities (i.e. insert form example); communicate formalized expectations of work to stakeholders to ensure understanding of the opportunity and attendant responsibilities in execution; ensure that contracts and statements of work move through sanctioned approval process and are archived appropriately upon signing</p></li>
<li><p>
Troubleshoot customer operational issues: Revise inaccurate data entry; solicit and gain required approval, expedite customer involvement, or identify internal operations issues for escalation</p></li>
</ul>
