<h2>Part time Sales Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>POSITION: Part time Sales Coordinator</p>
<p><b>DEPARTMENT</b>: <b>Sales &amp; Marketing</b></p>
<p>
REPORTS TO: Director of Marketing and Sales</p>
<p>FLSA : Hourly Non Exempt</p>
<p>SMG, the leader in privately managed public assembly facilities has an excellent and immediate opening for a Sales Coordinator at the CenturyLink Center. The Sales Coordinator will assist all marketing personnel with tasks and projects associated group sales, contracts, insurance, sales prospects, and administrative tasks associated with arena sales.</p>
<p><b>
MAJOR RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Maintain databases include bulk mailing addresses and customer files (i.e. Ticketmaster, signage, promoter contacts, media, and building stakeholders).</li>
<li>Execute group sales marketing goals, both short and long range, to ensure the profit growth and expansion of group sales for facility tour events.</li>
<li>Organize and execute group sales advertising and promotion activities.</li>
<li>Prepare group sales expense and sales reports with appropriate backup documentation for show settlement.</li>
<li>Maintain event attendance database.</li>
<li>Make weekly prospecting sales calls and arrange sales meetings for Director of Sales and Marketing.</li>
<li>Maintain databases with relevant group sales information such as forms with price, number of tickets sold and repeat business.</li>
<li>Renew Centertainer (VIP) memberships and maintain database.</li>
<li>Keep accurate and up-to-date contact information for members of each respective program.</li>
<li>Keep accurate correspondence information for members of program including calls, letters, original order forms, problems, etc.</li>
<li>Assists clients with questions pertaining to Luxury Suites and VIP program.</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Disseminate monthly customer service survey responses to staff members and compile survey results into a monthly survey report.</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>Mail out/ deliver tickets, parking passes and other items to suite holders and/or VIP members.</li>
<li>Process and submit department requests and purchase requisitions/orders.</li>
<li>All other duties assigned</li>
</ul>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High School Diploma or equivalent. Degree in Business or related subject preferred or the equivalent in work related experience.</li>
<li>Proficient in MS Word, Excel and PowerPoint.</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal skills and orientation to customer service.</li>
<li>Professional presentation, appearance and work ethic.</li>
<li>Good communication, problem solving and organizational skills.</li>
<li>Excellent customer service and public relations skills.</li>
<li>Ability to work under limited supervision and to interact with all levels of staff including management.</li>
<li>Ability to work long and irregular hours that may vary due to functions and may include days, evenings, weekends and holidays. Some travel as needed.</li>
<li>Reliable Transportation</li>
<li>Possess strong organizational skills.</li>
<li>Give attention to detail.</li>
<li>Maintain an effective working relationship with staff, clients, associates, patrons and others encountered in the course of employment.</li>
<li>Organize and prioritize work to meet deadlines. Work effectively under pressure and produce accurate results.</li>
<li>Remain flexible and adjust to situations as they occur.</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Walk/Stand for extended periods</li></ul>
