<h2>Director of Field Operations, Roofing</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>In search of…</b><br>
<br>
<b>Director of Field Operations, Roofing</b><br>
<b>Do you have deep expertise in the roofing field? Are you a strong leader? Do you inspire? Care deeply about workplace safety? Join our team at Antis to help manage the “boots on the ground”. There is no limit to what we can do together.</b><br>
<br>
It is an exciting time at Antis Roofing &amp; Waterproofing as we celebrate 30 years in the roofing business. We are seeking a leader for Roofing Field Operations. As a member of our leadership team we need someone with strong business and field operation acumen that comes from years field expertise – someone who has spent their career doing the work and then has managed teams of roofers at all job levels – new to the business through those in supervision roles. The ideal candidate is SUPER motivated and motivates others, gains the trust of internal and external customers, exudes positive energy and is “just tough enough” to handle the details and follow through to meet production deadlines and financial margins.<br>
<br>
<b>We seek people with purpose…and our ideal candidate will be…</b>
<ul>
<li>Deeply dedicated to safety protocols</li>
<li>A leader who inspires and motivates and works in sync with all departments in the organization</li>
<li>Must be tech savvy and willing to embrace new technology, Microsoft suite, drones, salesforce and smart phones are a norm for you</li>
<li>Must be highly organized, positive, energetic, flexible, and persistent in getting things done</li>
<li>Must enjoy serving others, and thinks like the external and internal customer</li>
<li>Ready and willing to roll up their sleeves and pitch in where needed</li>
<li>Truly enjoys being a roofing professional</li>
<li>This Director role is a full time exempt position and will be centrally located in our Irvine Campus, overseeing Southern California field operations in Los Angeles, Riverside, Orange and San Diego counties.</li>
<li>Ready to run the Roofing Field Operations department while the Customer Service, Finance and Accounting, Human Resources, Safety, Marketing, and Sales teams support you.</li>
</ul>
<b>Key responsibilities:</b><br>
<br>
Field Operations duties include but are not limited to:<br>
<br>
<ul><li>Providing leadership to field operation managers, supervisors and technicians to perform work safely and productively per specifications and to the standards of the company and customer.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Effectively manage all aspects of production to meet company and project goals.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Complete jobs on time and within established margins.</li></ul>
<br>
<b>Requirements:</b>
<ul>
<li>A college degree and or a combination of experience and education with a <b>minimum</b> of 10+ years of management experience supervising multiple teams and 10-15+ years of roofing experience including flat and slope and multiple roofing materials.</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills. <b>Must be bilingual</b>, Spanish and English</li>
<li>Proficiency with computers and software that include MS Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook, Salesforce, and OneNote</li>
<li>Ability to work with communication tools including modern office technology, printers, copiers, and other related office machinery</li>
</ul>
<b>About Us:</b><br>
<br>

Each month one of our employees is selected to receive a charitable giving card that they use to donate to the charitable organization of their choice.
