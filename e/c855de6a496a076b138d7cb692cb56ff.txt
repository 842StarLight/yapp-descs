<h2>Accounts Payable Clerk</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">AECOM is seeking an Accounts Payable Specialist to join our team in Germantown, MD. The Accounts Payable Clerk will complete payments and controls expenses by receiving, processing, verifying, and reconciling invoices.
<br><br>
<b>Essential Responsibilities:</b><br>
+ Reconciles processed work by verifying entries and comparing system reports to balances using Deltek CostPoint.
<br><br>
+ Charges expenses to accounts and cost centers by analyzing invoice/expense reports; recording entries.
<br><br>
+ Pays vendors by monitoring discount opportunities; verifying federal id numbers; resolving purchase order, contract, invoice, or payment discrepancies and documentation; insuring credit is received for outstanding memos; issuing stop-payments or purchase order amendments.
<br><br>
+ Verifies vendor accounts by reconciling monthly statements and related transactions.
<br><br>
+ Maintains historical records by scanning and filing documents.
<br><br>
+ Disburses petty cash by recording entry; verifying documentation.
<br><br>
+ Reports sales taxes by calculating requirements on paid invoices.
<br><br>
+ Protects organization's value by keeping information confidential.
<br><br>
+ Updates job knowledge by participating in educational opportunities.
<br><br>
+ Accomplishes accounting and organization mission by completing related results as needed.
<br><br>
