<p><b>Job Title:
</b></p>VP, Category Lead, Retail
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Description:
</b></p>
<p>JOB SUMMARY:
</p>
<p>The Vice President, Category Lead, Retail will be a driving force of Retail and eCommerce leadership at 360i. The role will carry direct oversight of a portfolio of leading brands and indirect leadership for driving vision and strategy for retail across the agency. This role is responsible for building strategy and fostering senior client relationships to support and grow existing client relationships. The role will be active in setting agency vision for the Retail category and forging new business relationships. The VP, Category Lead, Retail, will lead a broad team of Group Directors/Directors and many specialized agency services to deliver best-in-class service, while driving innovative and measurable client solutions. The right candidate must carry deep-expertise – and passion - for retail, the consumer, and developing leading retail solutions.
</p>
<div>ESSENTIAL ACCOUNTABILITIES:
<br>
<ul>
<li>Retail Business Leader that transcends account leadership to consult and advise on current retail clients’ business, with added focus to strategically guiding and growing their businesses.
</li>
<li>Retail and eCommerce category Lead: - Be the senior most expert for Retail sector and vertical across 360i, helping to influence and establish portfolio that already includes many leading retail brands.
</li>
<li>Join and be part of a visible and local office leadership group as a senior representative of the agency.
</li>
<li>Assumes responsibility for an account portfolio, senior/executive client relationships and contributes as an agency leader.
</li>
<li>Responsible for the financial stewardship of a direct account portfolio, accountable to executive management for quarterly and bi-annual business reviews.
</li>
<li>Works in partnership with clients and their teams to identify client issues, expectations and requirements.
</li>
<li>Leads the tough conversations required in any client relationship from time to time, with the ability to balance client expectations and the best interests of the agency.
</li>
<li>Seeks opportunities for organic growth across the retail portfolio.
</li>
<li>Champions and understands the clients’ business at the highest level.
</li>
<li>Fosters company culture by leading internal agency initiatives as well as contributes to training/ongoing education programs.
</li>
<li>Responsible for the performance of direct reports and ultimately for the performance of the client-facing team members.
</li>
<li>Serve as a New Business pitch lead across cross-channel teams, from RFP to final contract discussions.
</li>
<li>Expected to identify and deploy key team members who can uncover opportunities and cultivate new business.
</li>
<li>Collaborates with the agency leadership team on key decisions impacting the staff and office.
</li>
</ul>
<br>
EDUCATION/EXPERIENCE:
<br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree required; account management or sales and marketing accreditation's are a plus.
</li>
<li>10+ years of industry relevant experience; retail experience and leading within an agency – or similar environment are required; strong understanding of integrated and digital media/advertising, also required.
</li>
<li>5+ years of demonstrated account or client partner leadership, as well as significant P&amp;L management experience working within a global organization.
</li>
</ul>
<br>
KNOWLEDGE/SKILLS/COMPETENCIES:
<br>
<ul>
<li>Retail, retail, retail - must have retail experience and passion for understanding the current opportunities shaping retail business and the consumer.
</li>
<li>Commands all aspects of managing a large, complex account with minimal oversight from agency executive management.
</li>
<li>Demonstrates an exceptional understanding of media markets, including industry issues, trends and direction as well as a mastery of competitive profiling and positioning.
</li>
<li>Creates a vision and business strategy to drive organic growth and profitability across the client portfolio.
</li>
<li>Exemplifies a strong focus on multi-channel digital education.
</li>
<li>Sets expectations and standards to which all are held equally accountable, with firmness, authority and encouragement.
</li>
<li>Leads by setting the example of professionalism, work ethic, honesty and humility.
</li>
<li>Inspires, motivates and mentors employees across the company.
</li>
</ul>
