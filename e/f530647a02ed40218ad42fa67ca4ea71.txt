<h2>Loan Processor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Job Summary</b></p>
<p>Under the direction of the Branch Manager, Operations Manager and/or Processing Supervisor, evaluates all information supplied on the mortgage loan application, verifies the validity of the information and assembles a completely documented file to Underwriting and Closing. Acts as the primary liaison between the loan officer, loan officer assistant, realtors, builders and the customer(s).</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Functions</b></p>
<p><b>1.</b> Maintains knowledge of all policies and modifications to the guidelines and standards of the company, federal housing programs, investors, and private mortgage insurers.</p>
<p><b>2.</b> Maintains and updates loan application information within loan origination software and other applications.</p>
<p><b>3.</b> As applicable, orders appraisals, title, survey, verifications, and any other items required for loan approval; conducts regular follow-ups with outside sources regarding outstanding documents.</p>
<p><b>4.</b> Verifies the data collected, analyzes, and decides whether it meets guidelines or if other documentation is needed; documents communication through the conversation log; ensures loan application is in compliance with underwriting, investor, RESPA, and HMDA guidelines.</p>
<p><b>5.</b> Calculates income, reviews assets and liabilities.</p>
<p><b>6.</b> Reviews all disclosures for completeness and compliance.</p>
<p><b>7.</b> Completes final evaluation and analysis of completed application packages and submits eligible files specifying any applicable conditions.</p>
<p><b>8.</b> Monitors rate lock for data integrity and expiration dates; communicates discrepancies to the Loan Officer.</p>
<p><b>9.</b> May maintain accurate pipeline to ensure proper projections, closings, and regulatory compliance with Company, Federal, and State standards.</p>
<p><b>10.</b> May prepare, when applicable, “Declination Letters.” Ensures accurate disclosure of the reason(s) for the adverse action as soon as it is determined that applicants do not qualify.</p>
<p><b>11.</b> May schedule closing appointments with respective parties (title companies, closing attorneys, corporate closing department and customers) Meets customer service standards as outlined by management.</p>
<p><b>12.</b> May facilitate training of junior or newly hired processors regarding specific Prime operations including file flow, loan origination software, and other applications.</p>
<p><b>13.</b> Updates loan officer, loan officer assistant, realtors, builders and customers on loan status.</p>
<p><b>14.</b> Maintains communication with customers throughout the loan process, assisting them with any questions and notifying them of any additional requirements.</p>
<p><b>15.</b> Maintains a professional image and adheres to standards consistent with company policies and procedures.</p>
<p><b>16.</b> Other functions as needed.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Requirements</b></p>
<ul><li> High school diploma or equivalent.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Minimum of 1 year mortgage banking experience as a processor.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Must have working knowledge of Fannie Mae/Freddie Mac and FHA/VA policies and guidelines, as well as, Regulatory Mortgage Compliance, state, federal, local government, private investors and insurers.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Must have working knowledge or ability to learn all operating systems and ability to facilitate training.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Proficient in Excel, Word, Outlook, with the ability to learn other mortgage-related programs.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Excellent written and verbal communication skills.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Excellent organizational and time management skills.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Demonstrated ability to interact effectively with branch, regional and corporate personnel, as well as realtors, sales agents, builders, appraisers, title companies, etc.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Demonstrated judgment and decision making ability.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Must be available to work all regularly scheduled hours; arriving at scheduled reporting time and at the prescribed time after any breaks or lunch periods.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Must be able to work overtime, as required.</li></ul>
