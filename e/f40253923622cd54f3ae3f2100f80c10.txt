<h2>Manager, Applications & Technology - Chemicals</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Sulzer Chemtech, a division of Sulzer Ltd, has leading positions in the fields of process technology, separation towers and polymer and crystallization. With locations for sales, engineering, production as well as installation and maintenance service, Sulzer Chemtech maintains a worldwide presence for its clients in the oil and gas, hydrocarbon processing and other industrial markets.</p>
<div>
Sulzer Chemtech is growing and is seeking a Manager, Applications &amp; Technology - Chemicals to join our team in Tulsa, OK.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>SUMMARY: </b>Responsible for managing the company’s mass transfer components related process/chemical engineering activities for the Fine/Specialty Chemical Markets and for providing technical service to sales personnel and customers.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Liaises with Sales to determine the priorities for, and assigns customer requests for proposals, and monitors performance in relation to scheduling requirements.</li>
<li>Meets with customers on-site, as required, to review specifications and requirements, present and discuss the company’s proposal and to participate in project initiation.</li>
<li>Provides technical leadership to the company and within the industry, participates in customer technical meetings and makes formal presentations on the technical aspects and capabilities of company product lines.</li>
<li>Produces quotes and/or proposals as expected of Applications Engineers and works closely with team to provide training and development.</li>
<li>Responsible for tracking and monitoring trends within the market.</li>
<li>Communication with management on day-to-day activities.</li>
<li>Provides technical support and direction to customers; responds to questions and issues from customer personnel; also responds to questions and inquiries from the customer regarding technical problems and issues that arise and provides related technical support or assistance.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>SECONDARY FUNCTIONS</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Identifies opportunities for the optimization and automation of programs and practices within the department, and interfaces with other departments, especially as required to improve efficiency, and manages activities aimed at achieving LEAN principles.
</li>
<li>Responsible for periodic reports.
</li>
<li>Plans, develops and supervises implementation of policies, systems and programs to guide applications engineering and product development functions and activities.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>SUPERVISORY RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Determines and recommends the organizational alignment and staffing requirements for the department, selects and assigns key/subordinate personnel, and provides for the on-going training and development of department staff, especially immediate subordinates.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>QUALIFICATIONS:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor or Master of Science Degree in Chemical Engineering</li>
<li>10+ years related engineering experience working within the mass transfer equipment industry for the fine and specialty chemicals markets
</li>
<li>In-depth knowledge of Fine and Specialty Chemical Applications</li>
<li>Leadership and managerial capability, as required in evaluating and selecting qualified personnel, developing their capabilities, evaluating and improving their performance, assigning work, setting priorities, evaluating and driving team performance.
</li>
<li>Providing leadership to sales personnel in both the technical aspects of their work.</li>
<li>Communication and interpersonal skills, as required in working effectively with customer personnel to interpret their requirements, develop new business, and resolve problems and issues that arise.</li>
<li>Demonstrate industry-leading understanding of the application of mass transfer equipment in the refining and petrochemical markets.</li>
<li>Needs to possess the ability to develop analytical and projective techniques for use in product development activities, including computer models.</li>
<li>Basic understanding of the financial and economic considerations and constraints involved in research and development activities.</li>
<li>Analytical ability and resourcefulness, as required in developing and implementing progressive policies, programs, standards and procedures to govern/guide engineering and product development work.</li>
<li>Ability to motivate staff towards achieving desired results</li>
<li>Oral communication skills, as required in interacting effectively one-on-one with all levels of personnel and delivering professional presentations to groups.</li>
<li>Written communication skills, as required in preparing concise, understandable business reports and presentations.</li>
<li>Resourcefulness and innovation, as required in designing and implementing improved and more efficient practices and procedures.</li>
<li>Proficiency in the use of personal computers, as required in building and applying models to simulate product operating conditions, analyzing financial information with spreadsheet applications, compiling and analyzing quantitative information with database applications, and preparing reports and presentations with word processing software.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div>
