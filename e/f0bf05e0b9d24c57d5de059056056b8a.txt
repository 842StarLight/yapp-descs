<p><b>Job Announcement
</b></p>
<p><b>Business Manager</b></p>
<p>The Middle East Broadcasting Networks, Inc. (MBN) is seeking a Business Manager for our Global Administrative Operations Team at our Headquarters office in Springfiled, VA. MBN is a non-profit grantee of the federal government that directly communicates with the people of the Middle East and North Africa through television, radio, and digital media. MBN's mission is to provide objective, accurate, and relevant news and information to the people of the Middle East about the region, the world, and the United States in support of U.S. public diplomacy.</p>
<p>The Business Manager is responsible for providing financial management, budgeting and procurement for assigned departments. Serves as an advisor on all administrative matters.</p>
<p><b>The primary duties and responsibilities include, but are not limited to:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Providing financial management, budgeting and procurement for assigned departments.</li>
<li>Managing vendor relationships and processing vendor payments as needed.</li>
<li>Initiating, maintaining and fostering productive working relationships to facilitate and improve communication inside and between departments.</li>
<li>Anticipating and meets informational needs of senior management on key issues.</li>
<li>Advising and updating management and staff on significant administrative developments.</li>
<li>Providing analysis that is both detail-oriented and has a strategic, corporate focus.
</li>
<li>Drafting proposals, policies, statements of work and other administrative documents.</li>
<li>Reconciling bank accounts.</li>
<li>Providing bi-weekly reports for expenditures.</li>
<li>Coding all expenditures based on guidelines.</li>
<li>Maintaining a list of fixed assets; check inventory as required.</li>
<li>Providing month-end reconciliation statement.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Candidates are required to have:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>BA/BS in accounting, finance, business, or related field</li>
<li>5-10 years progressively responsible work experience in a business environment.</li>
<li>Proficiency with accounting systems as well as ability to work with presentation, database, and spread sheet software required.</li>
<li>Knowledge of budgeting techniques.</li>
<li>Strong problem solving and analytical skills essential.</li>
<li>Excellent command of English grammar with strong verbal and written communication skills preferred.</li>
<li>Strong time management and planning skills and be able to coordinate and prioritize multiple projects and assignments with little supervision.</li>
<li>Ability to work and communicate in a professional manner and with a professional demeanor essential.</li>
<li>Ability to adapt to changing work assignments and manage impact of shifting priorities.</li>
<li>Ability to work effectively in a deadline oriented environment.</li>
<li>Ability to maintain absolute confidentiality in all business matters.</li>
</ul> <p><b>MBN is an equal opportunity employer committed to workforce diversity.</b></p>
