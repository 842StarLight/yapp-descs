<h2>Account Executive - Outside Security Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>VideoSurveillance.com secures facilities and at-risk assets for our clients. Our team manages a rapidly-growing business that is widely considered an innovator in our industry. We offer a dynamic work environment and excellent compensation and benefits alongside world-class people. Our company is backed by a leading investment firm, providing stability and future growth opportunities for our team.
</p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>The Position</b></h3>
<p>VideoSurveillance.com is hiring energetic and professional Account Executives to serve our clients in Portland. Our Account Executives are outside security consultants, working with our clients in the field to assess their risks and implement security measures to prevent losses and criminal activity before they happen. Successful Account Executives make a great first impression, work tirelessly on behalf of clients, and thrive in a competitive environment.
</p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities:</b></h3>
<p>Our Account Executives' specific responsibilities include:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Generating profitable revenue and meeting sales targets.</li>
<li>Managing the business and sales relationship with key client accounts.
</li>
<li>Developing and qualifying leads within territory to close sales opportunities.</li>
<li>Coordinating client services with key company personnel, including Operations, Support, Central Station, and Leadership teams.</li>
<li>Continuously learning and applying new technologies, products and services, security approaches, and career development.</li>
</ul>

<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Required:</b></h4>
<ul>
<li>Excellent interpersonal skills which can be used in a sales and client service capacity.</li>
<li>Effective verbal and written communication skills.</li>
<li>Extreme customer empathy, since customers pay our bills!</li>
<li>Field-based outside sales success.</li>
<li>Sales mentality; "Fire in the Belly".</li>
<li>Proficient computer skills and technical understanding if not excellence.</li>
<li>Ability to work cooperatively with others in a team environment.</li>

<li>Valid driver's license and clean driving record.</li>

</ul>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Preferred:</b></h4>
