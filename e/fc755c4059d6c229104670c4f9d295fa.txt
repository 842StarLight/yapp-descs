<br>Design at Remind
<br>----------------

A position of leadership. As an experienced member on the team you'll help shape quality at Remind while others will look to you to help mentor them and grow. As a lead at the company, your cross-functional partners will rely on you to drive the narrative from the ambiguous early stages of a problem to making sense of what we learn after shipping a solution.

A voice for design. As a member of the design team you'll advocate for user needs while iterating through solutions, across Remind's product, process, and organization, to influence the entire customer experience through Sales, Marketing, and Customer Success.
