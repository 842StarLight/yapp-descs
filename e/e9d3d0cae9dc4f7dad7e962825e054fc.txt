<p><b>Sales Development Representative New York, NY </b></p>
<p>We are looking for a driven sales development representative (SDR) that can help us build on top of our solid base of satisfied customers. We want to make Watchwire the standard energy management platform in every facility across North America and eventually, beyond. On a daily basis, you will be prospecting property managers and facility operations personnel, contacting them to generate interest in Watchwire, and qualifying them as potential customers. Your goal is to get qualified leads to take the next step and speak with one of our Account Executives. In addition to communicating with prospects, you’ll also collaborate daily with the rest of our marketing and sales team.</p>
<p>This is a sales development position with room for growth for the right candidate. Quality teammates are very important to us and we will invest in developing your sales skills during on-boarding and beyond. Those with proven sales ability will have the opportunity to become an Account Executive, where they will lead demos, proposal negotiations, and closing deals with clients - all while increasing their earning potential.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Build and maintain lists of potential clients (leads) utilizing internet research and the assistance of our marketing team</li>
<li>Engage prospects by via both phone and email every day</li>
<li>Ensure proper reporting of client outreach efforts and insights gained by fully utilizing our CRM Salesforce</li>
<li>Book meetings with qualified leads (decision makers), handing them off to our Account Executives (Goal: 1-2 meetings booked per day)</li>
<li>Effectively communicate what our product does and how it benefits our clients</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Are you a good fit for this role?</b></p>
<ul>
<li>At least 1 year of experience in sales or business development. Ideally you’ve spent some time as an SDR or BDR in another startup.</li>
<li>Must have superior interpersonal skills as well as oral and written communication.</li>
<li>Enjoy learning &amp; using software tools (Salesforce, Zoominfo, Linkedin Sales Navigator, etc...)</li>
<li>Organized. You rely on systems rather than solely your personal memory to ensure great follow-through on tasks</li>
<li>Motivated. You have high initiative, creativity, and constantly looking for ways to win!</li>
<li>Driven. You see this role as the next step in your successful sales career</li>
<li>Fearless. You’re a charismatic people person with no hesitation for starting conversations with complete strangers.</li>
<li>Ethical. We take pride in our company ethics and culture, and hold our employees to the same standards.</li>
<li>Local. You must be able to work from our office in Midtown Manhattan.</li>
<li>Bachelor's Degree in business, marketing or related discipline preferred.</li>
</ul>

<p><b>About EnergyWatch</b></p>
<p>EnergyWatch is an entrepreneurial, private equity backed New York City-based provider of energy management SaaS solution to large commercial and corporate real estate, retail portfolios, and industrial energy consumers. The Company’s established, well-regarded solutions are anchored by its proprietary, cloud-based software platform, which helps companies reduce energy expenses, simplify energy reporting, and measure and verify performance. By combining various streams of utility, weather, commodity market, metering, and building data in our integrated energy management platform, we provide a data-driven software platform that saves time and money while increasing the value of real estate assets.</p>
<p>At a key inflection point in our company growth, EnergyWatch is seeking a Sales Development Executive with the drive, determination and integrity to join EnergyWatch’s growing sales team.</p>
