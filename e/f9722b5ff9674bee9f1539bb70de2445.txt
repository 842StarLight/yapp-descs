<h2>Marketing Associate - Chandler, AZ</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b><i>OUR VISION</i></b></p>
<p>
To accelerate innovation by democratizing and decentralizing the way ideas are shared and products are manufactured. We invite the world to help us create vehicles that inspire, empower and nurture humanity.</p>
<p><b><i>
OUR VALUES</i></b></p>
<p><b>
Community</b></p>
<p>
Drive community engagement above all else. Ensure open dialogue and transparency with community members in order to facilitate innovation and empower the individual maker.</p>
<p><b>
Local</b></p>
<p>
Engage and empower global communities of designers, engineers, fabricators and automotive enthusiasts to solve local problems, locally, through distributed making.</p>
<p><b>
Open</b></p>
<p>
Foster open collaboration in order to drive faster, more cost-effective, more thorough product development to drive improved safety and quality.</p>
<p><b>
Sustainable</b></p>
<p>
Make transportation more sustainable globally by empowering maker profitability, defending and advancing the environment, and improving safety and well-being.</p>
<p><b>
Excellence</b></p>
<p>
Strive to succeed in the face of failure; meet deadlines regardless of obstacles; exceed expectations in the design, development and distribution of critical tools, services and finished products.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Summary</b></p>
<p>
As a Marketing Associate, you will be responsible for executing a wide range of marketing programs including, but not limited to customer engagement, events / trade shows, digital &amp; print marketing, and public relations. You will work cross-functionally with inside &amp; outside sales, deployment, product and IT departments.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Functions</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Assists in the composition, editing and publishing of brand and product communications, including but not limited to, sales &amp; marketing collateral, website content, presentations and social media posts.</li>
<li>
Assists in customer engagement activities via onsite interviews and management of experience software to include survey development, analysis and recommendations of action items based on findings.</li>
<li>
Coordinates event logistics including securing sponsorships/booths and advertising opportunities, as well as providing support for deployment.</li>
<li>
Assists with managing content calendar, event calendar and other marketing databases.</li>
<li>
Assists with the budgeting and purchasing of brand merchandise and maintaining inventory.</li>
<li>
Assists with collecting data and reporting on marketing campaigns using analytics.</li>
<li>
Works with Marketing Vice President on data quality, audience segmentation, agency scoring, among other projects, to ensure marketing projects are scalable for future reporting.</li>
<li>
Performs administrative support tasks relevant to marketing activities assigned by Marketing Vice President.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Requires minimum of a bachelor’s degree, preferably in marketing or communications.</li>
<li>
Interest in automotive or transportation industry, a plus.</li>
<li>
Experience with customer engagement and social media.</li>
<li>
Demonstrates strong interpersonal skills, prioritizes and plans work activities, exhibits sound and accurate judgment, demonstrates accuracy and thoroughness, follows through on commitments.</li>
<li>
Ability to articulate information clearly and concisely.</li>
<li>
Excellent organizational skills.</li>
<li>
Thrives in an entrepreneurial and fast-paced business setting.
</li>
<li>Ability to work in a team and alone.</li>
<li>
Ability to travel up to 25%, when needed</li>
<li>
Other duties, as assigned.</li>
</ul>
