<h2>Assistant Manager (Part-Time), Madewell, Towson Town Center</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>THE MADEWELL ASSISTANT MANAGER<br>
[Hourly Part-Time, reports to a store director]</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
What The Role is All About</p>
<p>
Our Assistant Managers:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Set the pace for all associates as selling and service leaders.</li>
<li>
Are energized by creating a customer experience that exceeds expectations and drives loyalty.</li>
<li>
Own the selling floor, placing the right people in the right place at the right time.</li>
<li>
Know their neighborhood: the events, artists, local influencers and style-makers.</li>
<li>
Motivate the team to achieve their SPH and KPIs.</li>
<li>
Master and own their DOR.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
What We Expect You To Bring</p>
<p>
Starting on day one, you should:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Enjoy working hard and always be up for a challenge.</li>
<li>
Have a track record of setting and achieving goals and always see things through from start to finish.</li>
<li>
Make smart decisions by: actively listening, understanding data and looking beyond the obvious.</li>
<li>
Know your strengths and limits and balance knowledge of process and judgment to guide actions.</li>
<li>
Address tough situations quickly, providing complete and actionable feedback.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
What You Can Expect</p>
<p>
To be part of a team of the best and brightest that:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Believes in our products and services.</li>
<li>
Knows the customer, are denim experts and are passionate about wardrobing.</li>
<li>
Maximizes opportunities and questions what can be done better.</li>
<li>
Generates ideas to evolve the business.</li>
<li>
Is market-aware, forward thinking and global.</li>
<li>
Fosters collaboration and productivity.</li>
<li>
Knows when to act and when to partner.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
How We Measure Results</p>
<p>
We expect that you will:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Lead productive selling floor shifts and achieve daily sales and KPI goals.</li>
<li>
Look for omni solutions to exceed customer expectations.</li>
<li>
Provide the communication and tools needed for the team to drive incremental denim sales and achieve their KPIs.</li>
<li>
Capture and create profiles during each transaction to increase customer loyalty (except California + Massachusetts).</li>
<li>
Achieve Enterprise Selling targets.</li>
<li>
Successfully complete facilitated, on-the-job and mobile learning.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Fine Print</p>
<p>
Our Assistant Managers:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Have a high school diploma or equivalent combo of education and experience.</li>
<li>
Have 2 or more years of experience with similar scope, specialty retail preferred.</li>
<li>
Communicate effectively and confidently.</li>
<li>
Process information and operate store systems accurately.</li>
<li>
Assist in processing and replenishing and keeping the store looking great.</li>
<li>
Are available when we are busy, including: nights, weekends and holidays.</li>
<li>
Are facile with technology and apps and familiar with industry-related blogs and feeds.</li>
<li>
Must bend, reach, stretch for product as well as lift, carry and move at least 40 pounds |18 kilos.</li>
<li>
Must regularly move around all store areas and be accessible to customers.</li>
</ul>
