<h2>Account Executive (North America - Central U.S)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Copperleaf's C55 software helps some of the world's largest energy firms make better strategic decisions. We have a track record of delivering award-winning, industry-changing solutions that enable our clients to build more resilient and sustainable infrastructure.
<br><br>
We have an opening for a consultative Enterprise Sales Account Executive to join our team in North America.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
As one of Copperleaf's Account Executives you will be assigned customer accounts, primarily in the central states of the U.S and drive the identification and qualification of opportunities while executing account strategy and generating license, support and services revenues.
<br><br>
You will be facilitating and maintaining relationships with customers, which will be measured by their reference-ability, customer satisfaction levels, increased revenue levels and overall account penetration.
<br><br>
<b>Your role will include the following activities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Identify, pursue and close new sales opportunities through the successful execution of the sales process</li>
<li>Developer relationships with prospective clients and stakeholders at a senior executive level with key decision makers</li>
<li>Work with business consultants to understand prospect's current business practices and strategic drivers, develop and identify pain points and create a vision for solving these key pain points</li>
<li>Continuously gather knowledge of competitors and how to effectively position our solution</li>
<li>Drive a sales process that will highlight our solution as a strategic advantage to the prospect</li>
<li>Routinely discuss and communicate opportunity plan sales strategy with other members of the account team, as well as field and corporate management</li>
<li>Maintain an accurate and documented pipeline of opportunities (prospects and suspects) within CRM</li>
</ul>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>You're an expert in solution selling and value based selling methodologies</li>
<li>You have experience and are comfortable selling enterprise software to C level executives</li>
<li>You know how to navigate complex organizations and have experience with the Complex Sale methodology</li>
<li>You can position new business within the account by developing, communicating and driving effective selling strategies that are based on valid, customer-specific value proposition</li>
<li>You're an enthusiastic self-starter with proven problem-solving skills</li>
<li>You have dynamic presentation skills, ability to articulate customer problems and challenges, and then link them to the value proposition</li>
<li>You have general familiarity with consultative selling training methodologies</li>
<li>You enjoy travelling and are willing to spend 50% of your time visiting clients or attending industry events in the region</li>
<li>Experience in asset intensive industries such as Utilities, Oil &amp; Gas, Mining, Public Sector or Transportation is desirable</li>
<li>Engineering or analytical Bachelor's degree</li>
</ul>
