<p>Responsibilities:<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Troubleshoot, maintain, service, and repair solar PV systems and associated equipment
</li>
<li>Assists higher level Technicians in performing maintenance.
</li>
<li>Performs other technical and administrative duties associated with operating and maintaining a power facility.
</li>
<li>Ensures work is performed in accordance with Environmental and Safety requirements.
</li>
<li>Participate in required training, including On-the-Job Training, Self-Study, Computer Based Training and Classroom Training.
</li>
<li>Performs other duties as assigned.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Knowledge &amp; Skills:<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Possess demonstrable mechanical aptitude and basic electrical knowledge.
</li>
<li>Understanding of general workplace safety and the ability to follow safety standards, as well as help the company improve upon the standards in place.
</li>
<li>Will drive company vehicle; must have a valid state driver’s license and good driving record
</li>
<li>Ability to work overtime and non-standard working hours (nights), when needed.
</li>
<li>Professionalism and excellent communication skills
</li>
<li>Ability to operate a computer with technical proficiency of standard business software.
</li>
<li>Knowledge of applicable safety and environmental regulations.
</li>
<li>Able to work both independently and as part of a multi-tasking team.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Education/Experience:<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>A high school diploma or GED is required.
</li>
<li>Knowledge of electrical and mechanical fundamentals of PV systems preferred.
</li>
<li>2 year technical degree or equivalent Journeyman certification and qualifications preferred.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Working Conditions:<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Normal plant and/or office environment, including working at heights, high/low ambient temperatures and high humidity areas.
</li>
<li>Requires standing, sitting, walking, kneeling, twisting, climbing stairs and ladders, and entering plant equipment to access, inspect, or collect data on plant equipment.
</li>
<li>Requires use of wear personal protective safety equipment (respirators, safety glasses, hard hat, appropriate footwear, ear plugs, etc.).
</li>
<li>May be required to work around rotating and energized equipment, hazardous materials and chemicals.
</li>
<li>Occasionally requires lifting up to 70 pounds, to perform duties and responsibilities.</li>
</ul>
