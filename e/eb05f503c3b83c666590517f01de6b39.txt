This position includes prospecting, pre-sales consultations, closing, and post-sales customer care. The ideal candidate will possess a quantifiable record of success in business to business sales. The candidate must understand ASP industry and subscription services, be able to correctly identify and aggressively pursue decision makers, be adept at developing and maintaining long-term customer relationships. The successful candidate must:<ul>
<li>
Have successful track record in business to business sales</li>
<li>
Possess the ability to function in a small team atmosphere</li>
<li>
Have excellent prospecting and cold-calling skills</li>
<li>
Be able to manage short sales cycles, high sales volume</li>
<li>
Be highly motivated, organized and self-disciplined</li>
<li>
Possess strong analytical and problem solving skills</li>
<li>
Demonstrate the ability to exceed predetermined sales goals</li>
<li>
Have personal motivation for success, creativity, and a sense of humor<br>
</li>
</ul>Sales Professional with 5+ years sales experience in business markets. Candidate must have excellent verbal as well as written communication skills. Software, Information Technology or Telecommunication Services background is a plus. BS or BA required. Local candidates only.<br>
