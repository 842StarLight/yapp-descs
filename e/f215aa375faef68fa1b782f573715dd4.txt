<h2>Financial Analysis Manager, Named Accounts, Worldwide Sales Finance</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>We are searching for an enthusiastic and self-motivated Finance Manager with experience in the software industry, preferably at a SaaS-based company, to join the Worldwide Sales Finance team. In this role, you will lead &amp; mentor the Global Named Account (NA) Financial Analysis (FA) team and partner closely with NA leadership to meet their strategic financial objectives.<br>
<br>
This position requires focus on existing and evolving financial metrics consistent with Autodesk’s overall business model transition (i.e., Annual Contract Value, Billings, Revenue, Annual Recurring Revenue etc.) It is a unique and interesting opportunity for an exceptional leader to interface with the NA leadership team and influence strategic decisions with insightful data-driven analysis. The role reports into the Senior Finance Manager, WW Sales Finance.<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Lead, inspire, develop highly accountable NA FA team (2 direct reports) that consistently delivers proactive reliable, insightful analysis to support and improve business performance</li>
<li>
Develop collaborative partnerships &amp; build trust with key stakeholders including NA Sales VP, Sales Finance Senior Directors, and Finance Business Partners</li>
<li>
Build smart, scalable and flexible financial models for forecasting, planning, and ad hoc analyses</li>
<li>
Provide strategic and tactical support on annual planning and budgeting processes</li>
<li>
Advise stakeholders on weekly &amp; end-of-qtr FCST trends and provide insights into FCST performance, risks/upsides; ownership of various elements of the Enterprise revenue FCST</li>
<li>
Support ad hoc analysis requests (i.e., SPIFF programs, cash-flow reviews, revenue recognition)</li>
<li>
Drive Long Range Plan updates (out-years 2-5) for consumption-based enterprise business models</li>
<li>
Deliver quarter-end executive reporting and presentations, support quarterly operations review, and quarterly business reviews</li>
<li>
Communicate effectively to key stakeholders around process updates, key deliverables, and timelines</li>
<li>
Work cross-functionally to drive efficient processes and leverage best practices</li>
<li>
Liaise with Sales Finance HQ, corporate FP&amp;A, Treasury, Investor Relations, and other cross functional teams as required</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
Minimum Qualifications</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s in Accounting or Finance</li>
<li>
Minimum 5+ years of related experience</li>
<li>
People management experience; the ability to work with multiple personality types</li>
<li>
Strong leadership experience developing world class talent/teams</li>
<li>
Advanced skills in Microsoft Excel and proficient in PowerPoint</li>
<li>
Strong aptitude for and experience with financial software (e.g., Business Objects, Anaplan, Qlikview, SFDC)</li>
<li>
Proven track record of managing large datasets with the ability to tackle complex issues using financial models and quantitative analysis</li>
<li>
Highly energetic and self-motivated, capable of working to firm deadlines</li>
<li>
Enjoy supporting a global business</li>
<li>
Ambassador of the growth mindset and strives for continuous improvement</li>
</ul>
