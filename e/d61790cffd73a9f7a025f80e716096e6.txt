<h2>Office Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>OFFICE ASSISTANT </b></p>
<p>American Industrial Sales of Berlin is currently seeking a part or full-time, experienced and qualified individual to add to their office team. We are looking for a candidate who is professional in both attitude and appearance, organized, reliable, self-motivated, works well with others, and has excellent customer service to join our team. Duties of this job includes performing various duties for the management team.</p>
<p><i>Essential job functions: </i></p>
<p>· Answering and directing phone calls</p>
<p>· Researching</p>
<p>· Purchasing products and supplies</p>
<p>· Shipping (creating BOL, creating LTL shipments and shipping via FedEx/UPS)</p>
<p>· Organizing roadcrew hotels, rental equipment, supplies, etc.</p>
<p>· Organize and maintain files</p>
<p>· Coordinate records</p>
<p>· Assisting Accounting in the reconciliation of financial statements</p>
<p>· Order office supplies and maintain office supply inventory</p>
<p>· Contact and communicate with customers, service providers, and management team, by telephone, e-mail, and/or fax</p>
<p>· Listing items for sale on online stores and following through with delivery of orders</p>
<p>· Taking care of the company dog</p>
<p><i>Education and Requirements: </i></p>
<p>· High school diploma</p>
<p>· Two-year college degree is preferred, will consider an actively enrolled student for part-time work that can develop into full time.</p>
<p>· Working knowledge of Microsoft Office, including Microsoft Word and Microsoft Excel</p>
<p>· Working knowledge of Adobe Acrobat software</p>
<p>· Working knowledge of internet search engines</p>
<p>· Must have good grammar and proofreading skills</p>
<p>· Excellent customer service and communication skills</p>
<p>· Ability to multitask</p>
<p><b>Compensation: </b></p>
<p>Based upon experience. Co-Pay insurance, Simple IRA with Employer Paid Contribution, Holiday pay, Paid Time Off, Flexible work environment. This is either a full-time or part-time position. Business office hours are from 7:30 am to 4:00 pm, Monday through Friday.</p>
