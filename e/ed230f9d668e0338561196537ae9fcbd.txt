<h2>Sr. Program Analyst FMS</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Precise Systems is a professional services company currently working for the U.S. Navy, U.S. Marine Corps, and U.S. Air Force; competing as a Service Disabled Veteran Owned Small Business (SDVOSB) under several NAICS codes. Our core competencies include systems engineering, program and project management, logistics, information technology and cyber security, and software development services. We were founded in 1990 and since our establishment we have been providing cutting edge solutions and support to our warfighters. We understand and provide expert consultation on network and weapons systems acquisition programs, maintenance/modernization programs, and sustainment programs.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
We are currently seeking a Senior Program Analyst to support our Patuxent River, MD customer. The Senior Program Analyst will provide Foreign Military Sales (FMS) Case support to the FMS Team. The Senior Program Analyst must have an in-depth knowledge of TTSARB, International Policy Procedures, and an ability to assemble policy briefs. Additionally, they must have the ability to track key policy documents and create customer briefs tailored to customer requirements. The Senior Program Analyst will work with multi-disciplinary IPT members including program analysts, financial/budgetary analysts, engineers and logisticians to support the requirements of an international acquisition case.</p>
<br>
