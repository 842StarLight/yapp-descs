<p><b>Engineer Project IV</b><br>
Engineer Project IV for Honeywell International Inc., Houston, TX<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Lead and implement projects engineering and addition/modification for Experion PKS control System, third party programmable logic controllers (PLCs), and third party and SCADA systems.</li>
<li>
Act as technical interface with customers and define the control/safety systems specifications based on project requirements.</li>
<li>
Ensure that required prerequisites (e.g. design inputs, tools, documentation, deliverables, schedule, budget) are in place prior to commencing work.</li>
<li>
Produce functional and concept technical design specifications and provide technical leadership, support and expertise for single discipline.</li>
<li>
Perform required integration of multiple automated systems such as Safety System, power distribution systems, AC/DC systems, PLCs and distributed control systems.</li>
<li>
Provide control improvement and create/modify control strategies and implement new control routines.</li>
<li>
Estimate man-hours required for work packages and requisition hardware and software needed.</li>
<li>
Perform hardware and software integration, configure system instrument databases and control system custom graphics and reports.</li>
<li>
Develop FAT and SAT procedures and perform loop checks, continuity tests, factory acceptance tests, site acceptance tests, start-up support, and performance tests for process control systems.</li>
<li>
Ensure that the engineered solution meets project requirements in terms of functionality, standards, quality, and supporting documentation and implementation process at minimum cost by allocating work assignments to project team.</li>
<li>
Ensure that work assignments are executed to appropriate standards and quality systems (implementation, test and documentation).</li>
<li>
Monitor and control assigned work scope against the project plan, ensure effective change control and identifies risks/opportunities.</li>
<li>
Proactively recommend potential quality and efficiency improvements within the engineering group.</li>
<li>
Share technical knowledge with project team, supervisor, own discipline and other engineering disciplines.</li>
<li>
Report progress and technical problems to project and engineering supervisor.</li>
<li>
Actively contribute in the pre-engineering phase of a project.</li>
<li>
Identify to the customer which information is required before project starts.</li>
<li>
Assist sales organization in defining and estimating the engineering/technical scope of work.</li>
</ul>
