<h2>Software Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>One of our very fast growing software clients in San Mateo, CA is seeking to hire an Account Executive.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
60% of your appointments will be set for you.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
The base salary is $70,000-$85,000 and $140,000-$170,000 total.</b></p>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Exceed monthly, quarterly, and annual sales goals</li>
<li>Prospect, build pipeline and sell the software to strategic clients</li>
<li>Maintain strong sales activity with a focus on qualifying and closing</li>
<li>Manage a pipeline of 20+ opportunities</li>
<li>Align value proposition targeted towards the C-Level decision maker in target accounts</li>
<li>Engage with C-level prospects to position software and take it to closing</li>
<li>Adopt value selling and the Challenger Sale sales model</li>
<li>Co-sell with established resellers</li>
<li>Accurately forecast</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Requirements:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>2+ years of B2B selling in software</li>
<li>Track record of exceeding sales quota</li>
<li>Strong executive presence, especially CEOs and CFOs.</li>
<li>Expertise in managing a pipeline with the ability to close</li>
<li>Ability to identify pain points and develop unique and value propositions</li>
<li>Great at building relationships</li>
<li>Excellent oral and written communication skills</li>
<li>Spend management or finance is a plus</li>
</ul>
