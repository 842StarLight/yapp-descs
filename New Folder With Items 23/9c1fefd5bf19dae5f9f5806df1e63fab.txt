<h2>Cyber Security SOC Transformation Subject Matter Expert</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<p><b>Job Description:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>The selected candidate will be responsible for supporting Cyber services presales efforts and engagement execution across a variety of large commercial organizations. This individual should be a motivated self-starter and have a strong foundation and understanding in Information Technology and Security Architecture as well as be familiar with roles and security functions of large organizations, specifically Security Operations Center (SOC) operations and how automation, orchestration, and artificial intelligence can be leveraged to streamline and automate operational tasks and workflows.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>This candidate should have excellent communication skills, both written and oral, be willing to learn and execute on any client requests, and have the ability to interact with all levels of customer staff from the executive level down to highly technical analysts in structured and unstructured situations. The ideal candidate should be willing to work to help optimize SOC-related portfolio offerings and deliverables for Capgemini customers.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
This mission of Capgemini Center of Excellence (CoE) is to leverage established capability with a powerful cohesive and holistic cyber CoE to maximize Capgmeni’s Cyber growth trajectory and market capture in the US.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
 <b>*** This position is 100% remote ***</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
 <b>In summary, the responsibilities of the role will include the following:</b></p>
<p> 1. Presales: The SOC Transformation SME will work collaboratively with Direct Cyber Sales team to ensure holistic coverage on all qualified cyber deals. They will provide technical leadership and content development for client and prospect needs such as proposal responses, RFIs, Statements of Work, and other similar requests</p>
<p> 2. Portfolio Optimization: Work with the Global Portfolio Hub to support portfolio optimization and service expansion efforts based on market trending and opportunities recognized cross-sector.</p>
<p> 3. Thought leadership: Provide thought leadership by engaging in publication of white papers, articles, podcasts, and participating in key industry events.</p>
<p> 4. Delivery Support: Maintain flexibility so that SMEs are enabled to take on short duration delivery responsibilities to accelerate time to revenue recognition.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Basic Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Self-Starter with ability to spin up quickly on technologies, issues, topics, and advances in the cyber security field</li>
<li>“Can do” attitude willing to take on challenging opportunities and deliver with excellence for all customer projects and programs</li>
<li>Minimum of 5 years working in the Information Technology/Information Security industry</li>
<li>Minimum of 2 years working in a SOC environment and/or experience evaluating large corporate enterprise networks and cyber security organizations</li>
<li>Experience with multiple Information Security domains: Cyber Intelligence Analysis, Threat Monitoring, Incident Response, Malware Analysis, Computer Forensics, Cyber Architecture, Endpoint Protection, Network Security, Infrastructure Security, Application Security, Platform Security, Identity &amp; Access Management, Policy &amp; Governance, Cloud Security, End User Education &amp; Awareness, Penetration Testing, Vulnerability Scanning &amp; Management, and Compliance &amp; Risk Management</li>
<li>Experience with leading industry leveraging automation and orchestration platforms such as Rapid7, Swimlane, Demisto, Siemplify, Alienvault, etc.</li>
<li>Experience with cyber security processes, tools, and technologies and extensive knowledge of best practices regarding their implementation</li>
<li>Knowledge of the cyber threat landscape to include Advanced Persistent Threats, Cyber Crime, Hacktivism; specifically, the tactics, techniques and procedures they apply to a cyber-threat attack.</li>
<li>Knowledge and understanding of attack method types and their usage in targeted attacks such as phishing, malware implantation, perimeter vulnerabilities, application vulnerabilities, lateral movement, etc.</li>
<li>Prior experience working with the Kill Chain, Diamond Model of Intrusion, and similar frameworks and concepts.</li>
<li>Cyber security certifications such as CISSP, CEH, GCIA, GCIH, GCFE, and/or Security+</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal, verbal, and written communication skills to successfully accomplish client-facing interactions</li>
<li>Ability and willingness to travel 40%+, including potential travel outside of the US</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Typical Minimums:</b></p>
<p>Bachelor’s degree from an accredited college in a related discipline, or equivalent experience/combined education, with 5 years of professional experience; or 3 years of professional experience with a related Master’s degree. Considered an emerging authority.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Desired Skills:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Prior experience leading an assessment or evaluation of a SOC or large security enterprise to include execution of the assessment, analysis of results, and formulation of a final report for dissemination to key stakeholders.</li>
<li>Experience developing content-rich final reports and associated deliverables with results of subject matter expert analysis and improvement recommendations</li>
<li>Experience presenting the results of applied analysis to client stakeholders to include senior or executive leadership</li>
<li>Experience with execution of assessments and evaluations of cyber security operations projects to include the development of strategic vision roadmaps, baseline or gap evaluations, and SOC Strategy and Concept of Operations reports</li>
<li>Knowledge of industry best practices and standards to include but not limited to the following; NIST 800-61, NIST Core Security Framework, NIST 800-53, NIST 800-171, ISO 27001/2, and ISO 27035, Financial Standards and Compliance, Cloud Security.</li>
<li>Knowledge of Large Fortune 500 organizations security programs and their related functions to include the SOC function, vulnerability assessment, penetration testing, security policy and procedure, security infrastructure management, network and host based defense, cyber security metrics, security engineering, etc.</li>
<br>
</ul>
