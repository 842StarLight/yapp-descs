<p>A GREAT OPPORTUNITY</p>
<p>An innovative, local CPA firm seeks another dynamic team member eager to grow in their career and deliver outstanding client service in a flexible, relaxed, and family-friendly environment. <b>We are currently seeking an experienced professional who has spent 1 year in a public accounting environment. </b> Ideal candidates will possess strong leadership and communication skills with proven experience in the delivery of quality client service. Key attributes sought include specialization in the areas of accounting, business tax preparation and/or individual tax preparation. Part of your job will be daily interaction with clients; answering questions and addressing concerns. <b>Experience in QuickBooks and Income Tax Preparation is a definite plus!</b></p>
<p>RESPONSIBILITIES * Preparation of federal, state and local income tax returns for individuals, corporations, S corporations, LLCs and partnerships. * Preparation of GAAP and OCBOA compiled and reviewed financial statements, including transaction data entry, account reconciliation, general ledger write-up and journal entry preparation as required. * Preparation of payroll, sales tax, and other miscellaneous business filings as requested. * Effective workpaper preparation in compliance with firm standards and procedures. * Work as an effective team member to complete project components and assignment tasks. * Build strong working relationships within team, as well as peer client level. * Daily time tracking through firm billing software and sensitivity to maintenance of an appropriate billable time ratio. * A commitment to work extended hours during the months of January through April (with the option of taking flex-time/comp-time in the months outside busy season).</p>

<ul><li>Proven history in delivery of quality client service. * Excellent computer skills including proficiency in MS Excel, MS Word, as well as financial statement and tax preparation software -- experience in QuickBooks and Creative Solutions/UltraTax (Thomson Reuters) or other relevant accounting/tax software.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Highly effective verbal and written communication skills. * Strong interpersonal and relationship building skills.</li></ul>
