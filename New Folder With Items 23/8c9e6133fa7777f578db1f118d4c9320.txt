<p><b>What does a great Principal Consultant of Professional Services do?</b></p>
<p>
You will provide analysis, consultation and recommendations to credit union and bank clients during deployment of DNA Core Financial Software, in order to ensure clients’ business needs are met. In addition, you will support post live clients with both strategic consulting and implementations of post live items/modules. Engage with clients and internal business partners to ensure delivery and implementations of their suite of products and provide the best-in-class solutions to the clients’ needs.</p>
