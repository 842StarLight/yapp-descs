<p>Tribus Services Inc. provides a wide array of services and solutions for electric, gas and water utilities, municipalities and cooperatives that streamline functions, increase operating efficiencies and result in direct cost savings.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Tribus Services Inc. has an immediate opening for a highly motivated, collaborative and committed individual to fill the position of <b>Project Manager.</b></p>
<p><b>This position located in Tacoma, WA </b>and will support our City of Tacoma, WA. AMI electric and water meter project. The Project Manager will lead our Electric meter Install pilot project which could evolve into a 4-year smart meter project.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Direct procedures and policies of their project. Analyze and/or evaluate practices and systems to ensure daily operations, prioritizes work to meet timelines, manages continuous improvement of project, procedures and operational methods.</li>
<li>Purchase/obtain all needed equipment and materials to complete the project. Work with Fleet Manager to secure transportation for project. Compile, schedule and issue routes daily utilizing routing and work order management software.</li>
<li>Ensure proper training of all field staff and uphold safety standards.Takes immediate steps to resolve any unsafe working conditions.Collaborate with office and field staff on route schedules, invoicing,errors, training, payroll, employee status changes and other employee related issues.</li>
<li>Verify that all routes are completed ensuring quality and timeliness. Schedules re-reads shut offs and other special consideration customers as needed. Visit route sites and performs quality checks. Track daily completions, labor hours, etc. Runs reports and supplies production information as requested by Director, Corporate or Customer.</li>
<li>Perform staff management functions including: Monitor and resolve on-the-job problems, set appropriate performance standards, analyze staffing levels, interview and selects new employees; facilitate staff training, coaching, cross-training and mentoring; perform performance evaluations and salary adjustments, completes new job profiles, resolve employee concerns; performs disciplinary counseling, and performs terminations with Human Resources assistance. Delegate tasks and responsibilities to staff when appropriate.</li>
<li>Maintain a strong working relationship with customers, keeping them informed on a regular basis. Work with Director of Sales and Operations to introduce the client to Tribus Services Inc. additional services and grow business.</li>
<li>Manage and maintain the project budget. Review operating costs become familiar with project revenue projections and all project related contracts.</li>
<li>Keeps records, tracks project performance and produces reports as requested.</li>
</ul>
