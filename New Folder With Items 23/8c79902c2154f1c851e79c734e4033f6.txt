<p>
At Maverik the Lead (Cashier) Adventure Guide assists the Store Director in maximizing sales and net profit through effective management, operational, and financial methods. The Lead Adventure Guide assists in training and certification of all Adventure Guides. Provide prompt, efficient, effective and courteous quality service to the customer, as well as maintain the conditions of the store during the work shift up to the quality and safety standards set by the Company. The Lead Adventure Guide may be called upon to assume Manager on Duty responsibilities.</p>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
About Maverik:</b></h2>
<p>
Maverik was founded by Reuel Call in Afton, WY., in 1928, and has grown to over 315 stores in 12 states to become the largest independent convenience store chain in the Intermountain West. Maverik currently employs 5,000 Adventure Guides and has bold growth plans, including expansion into markets west of the Mississippi. As Adventure’s First Stop, Maverik lives up to its purpose to build the coolest convenience-experience on the planet.</p>
