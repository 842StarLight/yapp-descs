<p>
In this role, you will have the:</p>
<ul>
<li>Opportunity to shine working alongside a team of experienced, forward-thinking team members who are down to earth, like to have fun, but take winning seriously</li>
<li>Enthusiastic support and a systematic approach for building your sales and corporate travel knowledge</li>
<li>Chance to make your mark as our company expands while enhancing our reputation in the travel community</li>
<li>Ability to drive your career to the next level as part of our Training &amp; Development Program</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Cooperate with clients to determine their needs and advise them appropriate of destinations, modes of transportation, travel dates, costs and accommodations</li>
<li>Book transportation, make hotel reservations and collect payment/fees</li>
<li>Utilize preferred vendors to maximize profit, and ensures compliance with the clients’ travel policy.</li>
<li>Enter data into our software and maintain client files</li>
<li>Stay fully informed on all airline rules and regulations, tariffs, and fare requirements</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Proven work experience as a corporate travel agent</li>
<li>Excellent knowledge of computer reservations systems, Sabre GDS system and e-travelling</li>
<li>Strong working knowledge and understanding of pricing rules, exchange transactions, and procedures.</li>
<li>Understanding of travel requirements (visas, passports and immunization requirements)</li>
<li>Sound knowledge of domestic and international travel trends</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Benefits</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Employer-Sponsored Medical and Dental Insurance with Employee Contribution</li>
<li>Fully-Funded Life Insurance (1x salary) &amp; LTD (Long-Term Disability Insurance)</li>
<li>Optional Employee-Paid Vision Insurance and STD (Short-Term Disability Insurance)</li>
<li>HSA (Health Savings Account)</li>
<li>401k (up to 4% Company Match)</li>
<li>Paid Time Off</li>
</ul>
