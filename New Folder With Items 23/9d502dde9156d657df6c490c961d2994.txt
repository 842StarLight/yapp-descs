<p><b>Marketing Specialist
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Get Inspired by the Work You’ll Do!
</b></p>
<p>SYNNEX’ STRATEGIC PROCUREMENT Group is growing, and with it comes the opportunity for a motivated professional to grow with the business. SYNNEX Strategic Procurement is focused on providing reseller partners with the resources and services they need to deliver the best solutions to their clients, all while increasing their profitability and maximizing their business.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Position Objectives:
</b></p>
<p>Responsible for driving sales of assigned vendors through the creation, coordination, planning and organization of marketing projects and strategic marketing initiatives for the Strategic Procurement line or strategic company initiatives
</p>
<p>Work closely with new vendors and the Product Management team to develop and create incremental proposals to grow vendor sales.
</p>
<p>Directly responsible for the coordination and planning of promotional activities for STRATEGIC PROCUREMENT vendor marketing strategies utilizing a variety of communication vehicles (i.e., website, newsletters, reseller events, All-Star/blitz days).
</p>
<p>Responsible for the identification of market opportunities to propose new ideas to new and existing vendor lines. Help manage to accrued and incrementally-funded budgets for proposal implementation and executes marketing related activities to support both vendor goals and the Company’s business plans.
</p>
<p>Develop business plans to include strategy, program mix, ROI goals and tracks marketing trends for STRATEGIC PROCUREMENT and SYNNEX
</p>
<p>Monitor market and vendor forecasts and the regular tracking of top distribution customers
</p>
<p>Obtain ideas, suggestions and approaches from the vendor. Develop a budget with the vendor(s).
</p>
<p>Perform market research on the product(s) and in-depth Data Analysis to help promote the SYNNEX/STRATEGIC PROCUREMENT partnership
</p>
<p>Collect, analyze and present ROI statistics to vendor and company executives continuously throughout the SYNNEX and STRATEGIC PROCUREMENT fiscal year to assess strategic vendor investments
</p>
<p>Budget assessment and management to maintain acceptable profit expectations
</p>
<p>Assist in scheduling of the marketing activities
</p>
<p>Develop the content and instructions to Marketing Communications Department
</p>
<p>Submit proper information to the VCM and/or Finance Department
</p>
<p>Assist vendor assigned Marketing Specialist with ROI assessment and Tracking
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Demonstrated verbal and written communication skills, including but not limited to negotiation and presentation skills
</p>
<p>Ability to develop and maintain good rapport with internal and external personnel at all levels of stature<br>
<br>
</p>
