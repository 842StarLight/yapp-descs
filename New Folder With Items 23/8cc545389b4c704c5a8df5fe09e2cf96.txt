<p><b>POSITION SUMMARY</b></p>
<p>Lot Porters are responsible for the appearance of the new and used RV lots, including the cleanliness of the RV’s and the general appearance of the sales area.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>PRIMARY DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<p>This list of duties and responsibilities is not all inclusive and may be expanded to include other duties and responsibilities as management may deem necessary from time to time. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Transport RV’s to and from service bays, stores, gas stations, repair facilities, and locations</li>
<li>Keeps new- and used-vehicle lots neat and orderly, moving RV's as directed by the general manager and in accordance with dealership display standards</li>
<li>Make a daily inspection of showroom and service entrance doors and wash as necessary</li>
<li>Keep units in a “sale-ready” condition</li>
<li>Keep sidewalk along street free debris</li>
<li>Maintain Dealership premises as directed by Supervisor/Manager</li>
<li>Detail and make units ready for delivery, assist with pre-delivery inspections</li>
<li>Fill vehicles with gas</li>
</ul>
<p><b>KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS, AND ABILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to multi task in a fast paced environment</li>
<li>Attention to detail</li>
<li>Reliable and strong work ethic</li>
<li>Pride of workmanship and follow-through are essential</li>
<li>Commitment to superior customer service</li>
<li>Ability to work independently as well as on a team</li>
</ul>
<p><b>MINIMUM REQUIREMENTS</b></p>
<p>Education: High School Diploma or equivalent</p>
<p>License or Certification: Valid driver’s license and good driving record is required</p>
<p>Years of Experience: A minimum of one year detailing experience preferred. Experience in RV or automotive dealership environment is preferred.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>MACHINES, TOOLS, EQUIPMENT AND SOFTWARE USED</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Tractor and other towing vehicles</li>
</ul>
<p><b>WORK ENVIRONMENT</b></p>
<p>Lot Porters are exposed to the following conditions</p>
<ul>
<li>Slippery floors</li>
<li>Occasional exposure to extreme temperatures and weather</li>
<li>May be exposed to solvents and chemicals</li>
<li>Other risks associated with working around machines, tools and trucks</li>
<li>This is a safety sensitive position</li>
</ul>
<p><b>TYPICAL PHYSICAL DEMANDS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to move RV’s of various sizes as needed</li>
<li>Ability to organize various physical tasks through adjusting, connecting, lifting, pulling, pushing, or bending</li>
<li>Ability to climb ladders, bend or stand for long periods of time. In addition, individuals must be able to reach above shoulders and lift a minimum of 50 pounds</li>
</ul>
