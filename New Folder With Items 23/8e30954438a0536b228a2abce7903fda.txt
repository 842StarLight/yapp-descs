<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job description</b></h2>
<div>
<p>A glimpse at the job:</p>
<p>
In the role of Mechanical Engineer, will work closely with other members of engineering to define the equipment architecture, bearing selection and definition of materials. You'll have the opportunity to conduct static, fatigue and dynamics analyses of mechanical components and mechanisms using both classical methods and FEA software. If you're looking for the opportunity to apply your talents and demonstrate your enthusiasm for the aerospace industry, this could be the position for you!</p>
<p>
This position contributes to our vision by:<br>
</p>
<div><ul>
<li>Works closely with mechanical design, engineering hardware design, and product management to drive new development of programs from concept to production;</li>
<li>Interprets equipment requirements to translate to design of mechanical components;</li>
<li>Conducts equipment failure analysis for equipment, including mechanical and electrical components;</li>
<li>Performs component and mechanism analytical calculations such as but not limited to rotor dynamics, thermal analysis, bearing selection;</li>
<li>Assists team members in the design of the equipment including material selection, equipment architecture and design for manufacturing;</li>
<li>Creates drawings of mechanical components.</li>
</ul></div>
<p></p>
</div>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job requirements</b></h2>
<div>
<p>Safran Ventilation Systems looks for service-oriented team players with strong interpersonal skills, the right attitude and adaptability to an innovative and evolving environment.</p>
<p></p>
<div><ul>
<li>Requires a Bachelor's Degree in Mechanical or Aerospace Engineering and 5+ years of related work experience.</li>
<li>Clear and effective written and oral communication with co-workers, management, manufacturers and customers - Advanced</li>
<li>CAD; 3D Solid Modeling using Pro-E (CREO) preferred</li>
<li>Familiarity with FEA software and calculation methods</li>
<li>Basic understanding of aerodynamic principles</li>
<li>Familiarity of asynchronous and synchronous motors</li>
<li>Familiarity with circuit boards, processors, chips and electronic equipment</li>
<li>Understating of bearings architecture, failure modes and applications in electromechanical equipment</li>
<li>Strong analytical skills of applicable discipline , FMEA, Risk analysis</li>
<li>Working knowledge of ASME Y14.5 Geometric Dimensioning and Tolerancing Design and Drafting Standards, ASME Y14.100 Engineering Drawing Practices</li>
<li>Knowledge of aerospace test standards such as RTCA DO-160 and MIL-DTL-810</li>
<li>Familiarity with product validation (Vibration, Shock and Environmental)</li>
</ul></div>
