<h2>Cost Estimator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Delta Gear is looking for hard-working, reliable, responsible, and dependable individuals that take pride in their work and are committed to establishing a career in the manufacturing industry.</b></p>
<p><b>Summary/Objectives</b></p>
<p>The Cost Estimator supports the Delta Gear team and is accountable for the estimates on large and complex projects as required by client’s documents. This role will lead estimating efforts, through our ERP, to ensure optimum costing and coordination of projects and programs to ensure Delta Gear meets customer expectations and manufacturing requirements.</p>
<p><b>Tasks:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Complete an extensive training program relating to Delta Gear Systems, software and process understanding</li>
<li>Respond to inbound requests from our established sales team for product and pricing information, quote status or quote date confirmation.</li>
<li>Send RFQ’s to material, heat treatment suppliers and specialty coating suppliers in order to adequately understand overall costing of materials and outside sources.</li>
<li>Prepare cost estimates, for product manufacturing, by collecting historical cost data to estimate costs for current or future products.
</li>
<li>Review customer specifications including inspection requirements, first articles and final documentation requirements prior to quote.</li>
<li>Analyze blueprints and other documentation to prepare time, cost, materials, and labor estimates.</li>
<li>Confer with engineers, managers, owners, regarding changes and adjustments to cost estimates.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Skills/Key Competencies:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Strong communication skills both written and verbal</li>
<li>Superior planner and organizer</li>
<li>Demonstrated achievement orientation</li>
<li>Works effectively and productively with others</li>
<li>Strong overall leadership skills/interpersonal skills</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of five years’ experience in cost engineering, cost estimating, planning, scheduling,</li>
<li>estimating and other areas of project controls in a manufacturing environment.</li>
<li>Advanced level of proficiency in MS Excel and other MS Office programs.</li>
<li>Functional knowledge of accounting, financial accounting.</li>
<li>Knowledge and experience in ERP systems.</li>
</ul>
