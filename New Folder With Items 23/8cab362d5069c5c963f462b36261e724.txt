<h2>Branch Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Description:</b></h4>
<div>
<p>As Branch Manager at United Rentals, you'll be the leader of a major business enterprise. You'll have the opportunity to hire and motivate an amazing team in various roles, from sales and service to maintenance and drivers. You'll ensure enthusiastic and informed customer service. And you will build a profitable location with your business and management skills, ambition and competitive spirit.</p>
<p>
Sound challenging and fun? Consider getting on the management track at United Rentals, the largest equipment rental company in the world. We'll provide the tools, the technology and the support you need to do the job right. You'll be proud of your employees, your branch and the work we all do in providing the equipment that helps build our communities.</p>
</div>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Requirements:</b></h4>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree or equivalent experience</li>
<li>5+ years of successful business management experience at a branch or other individual profit center, with profit and loss responsibility</li>
<li>1+ years of supervisory experience and proven sales and customer service success</li>
<li>Proficient computer skills and experience using Microsoft Office</li>
<li>Effective leadership, motivational, organizational and communication skills</li>
<li>Valid driver's license with acceptable driving record</li>
</ul>
<div>United Rentals, Inc. is an Equal Opportunity Employer of women, minorities, protected veterans and individuals with disabilities.</div>
