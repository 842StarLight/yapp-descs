<h2>Key Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Key Account Manager - (19000131)</div>
<div>
Description<div>
</div>
<div>The Customer Success Manager owns and manages key account programs, working as part of the global key account management efforts to establish,
</div>
<div>maintain and grow business partnerships with the key accounts.</div>
<br>
<div>
<br>
Responsibilities: This position is responsible for, but not limited to, the following:</div>
<ul>
<li>Lead/Collaborate with Customers Service &amp; OPS team to achieve top industry performance</li>
<li>Develop/Maintain key account programs to ensure maintaining current business with customers and identifying potential new business oppty’s</li>
<li>Build and maintain strong business relationships with existing accounts</li>
<li>Develop key account team capabilities</li>
<li>Coordinate and support customer events and activities, including site visits, customer or industry events and entertainment</li>
<li>Conduct regular business reviews (MBR/QBR/YBR)</li>
<li>Timely address service issues and new requirements</li>
</ul>
<div>Key Results:</div>
<ul>
<li>Budget</li>
<li>Strategic initiatives</li>
<li>Internal Communications</li>
<li>Drive annual budget achieve at CM level for the customer</li>
<br>
</ul>
<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>Qualifications<div>
</div>
<ul>
<li>BA/BS or equivalent combination of education and experience.</li>
<li>Minimum 8 years experience in international logistics</li>
<li>Strategic development of programs</li>
<li>Strong business acumen</li>
<li>Strong business communication skills; including speaking, written, communicating across cultures and geographies</li>
<li>Ability to engage and develop relationships with customers at various levels within their organization</li>
<li>Ability to adapt / adopt new information technology</li>
<li>Ability to work independently</li>
<li>Strong English language skills, written and oral</li>
</ul>
<div>Additional Knowledge, Skills and Abilities</div>
<ul>
<li>Teamwork – showing a commitment to teamwork and collaboration</li>
<li>Respect – extending dignity and respect to all people</li>
<li>Integrity – fostering trust and a positive work environment</li>
<li>Innovation – bringing creativity to the workplace</li>
<li>Customer Focus – achieving Company, department, and personal goals through a strong customer focus</li>
<br>
</ul>
