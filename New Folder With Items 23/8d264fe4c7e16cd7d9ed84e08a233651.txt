<h2>Account Manager - $100K+ at Target</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Job Overview</b></p>
<p>We are looking for a energetic, high-performing Account Executive with experience in industrial sales. You will be responsible for managing our clients, and proactively reaching out to ensure client satisfaction and retention while continuously hunting for new business. The ideal candidate will bring new business to the company, hit sales quotas, and build lasting great rapport with our clients.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities for the Account Executive</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Create detailed business plan to promote and execute the goals and quotas of the business.</li>
<li>Manage the entire sales cycle from bringing on a new client to securing and closing the deal.</li>
<li>Explore new sales opportunities through networking, and then turning them over to long-term clients.</li>
<li>Educate prospective clients on our products</li>
<li>Interact extensively with customers to clarify their needs, research, and to recommend parts.</li>
<li>Prepare and deliver excellent proposals and quotes.</li>
<li>Plan and schedule one domestic, outside sales visit per month to visit with key customers.</li>
<li>Provide excellent customer follow-up to ensure we are retaining their business</li>
<li>Remain in consistent contact with clients; it is your responsibility to understand their needs to recommend products.</li>
<li>Respond to customer concerns and resolve any issues that are interfering with the retention of that client, and to preserve the company’s reputation in the marketplace.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications for Account Executive</b></p>
<ul>
<li>4 years of Industrial sales experience</li>
<li>Mechanical and mathematical aptitude; most be comfortable with technical conversations regarding various industrial applications.</li>
<li>Previous experience in the fields of hydraulics, tube fittings, or other industrial fitting application is highly encouraged.</li>
<li>Most have excellent client follow up, and strategic/ creative marketing</li>
<li>Strong computer skills, CRM software (Salesforce) Preferred</li>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree</li>
</ul>
