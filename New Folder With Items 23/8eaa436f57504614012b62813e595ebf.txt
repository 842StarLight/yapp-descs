<h2>Data Entry Clerk</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Position Summary</b></p>
<p>The Greater Los Angeles Zoo Association is seeking anexperienced data processor for a full-time position. The candidate must have an established history with data entry. He or she must be accurate,fast, and consistent, with an ability to quickly learn our system ofcoding. The candidate must have the ability to work steadily andconsistently, to take on new and varied tasks, and to handle new challenges asthey present themselves. Quiet, hardworking, responsible and personableindividual preferred. Typing speed is required to be 80WPM or higher.Experience with Raiser’s Edge database a big plus and highly preferred. Proficiency with Excel and MS Office programs expected. Familiarity with Omaticprograms or Blackbaud NXT a plus.</p>
<p><b>Essential Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for entering membership/donations from direct mail campaigns, website transactions, telemarketing charges, on-site membership sales and renewal mail into the Raiser’s Edge database.</li>
<li>Verifies cash, checks and charges are balanced before posting and prints reports.</li>
<li>In response to mail, returned mail, or requests from Development/Membership, makes name, address and email changes, additions, corrections and coding changes to member/donor records.</li>
<li>Verifies database information and performs cleanup as needed.</li>
<li>Upon request, initializes processing/batching of all membership cash receipts, including maintenance of batch tracking log.</li>
<li>Upon request, processes credit card charges and enters data in member/donor software.</li>
<li>Supports the Membership Department as it pertains to Database operations.</li>
<li>Imports data from external data sources into the Raiser’s Edge database on a regular basis, as needed.</li>
<li>Performs other duties, as required.</li>
</ul>
<p>This is an hourly, full-time position; weekdays 8:30 a.m. to 5:00 p.m.</p>
<p><b>Competitive Compensation and Benefits: </b></p>
<p>GLAZA provides competitive compensation commensurate with acandidate’s experience, as well as a full array of generous employee benefitsincluding paid vacation and sick leave, health, dental &amp; vision insurance,an FSA plan, employer paid life insurance, long-term disability insurance, atax-deferred annuity plan with employer match and contributions, and over 13 paid holidays annually. Additionally, GLAZA’s location is at the beautiful LosAngeles Zoo and Botanical Gardens.</p>
