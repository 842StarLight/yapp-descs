<h2>Beverage Supervisor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>POSITION: Beverage Supervisor<br>
DEPARTMENT: Beverage<br>
REPORTS TO: Beverage Director<br>
FLSA STATUS: Hourly-Non-Exempt
<br>
HOURLY RATE: $19-$20 P/H<div>
</div>
Summary: This position will provide supervisory support for Beverage in the execution,<br>
achievement and will maintain the core functions of SAVOR...Chicago's distribution of water<br>
coolers and water jugs throughout the McCormick Place Convention Center and WINTRUST Arena.<br>
This position will provide supervisory support for Beverage in handling all of the water cooler orders<br>
and restocking for events.<div></div>
<br>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities<br>
<ul>
<li>Oversees Water Porters; sets assignments and directs activities, ensuring deliveries and re-fresh orders are delivered timely and accurately.</li>
<li>Print and review all Water Cooler orders in the Catering Sales System (Reserve) on a daily/ weekly basis.</li>
<li>Insure all staff is scheduled to cover all orders on a weekly basis.</li>
<li>Input and review all orders for water coolers and jugs and handle placement of all orders with Distributors and coordinate deliveries with Warehouse in conjunction with the Beverage Manager.</li>
<li>Follow up to ensure all orders are filled and 100% complete</li>
<li>Communicate changes in orders to the appropriate Managers in a timely manner</li>
<li>Create BEO Packets and distribute to Porters</li>
<li>Print out paperwork for each Porter and distribute</li>
<li>Functions as a liaison between Sales ensuring last minute pop-ups are communicated accordingly and timely</li>
<li>Maintain all equipment and storage area by ensuring coolers/jugs are properly serviced, placed on racks, shelves or pallets</li>
<li>Must be able to account for beverage consumptions each day and turn in within 2 days</li>
<li>Respond to Banquet Captain calls regarding additional product needed, confirmation of request by Sales and or Clients</li>
<li>Direct the daily/weekly show driven stream of administrative paperwork necessary to execute all events</li>
<li>Must be able to handle multiple projects at a time and very detail oriented</li>
<li>Edit ADP E-Time daily to correct punches</li>
<li>Track daily attendance log</li>
<li>Complete Borrowed Labor for each week</li>
<li>Ensure proper paper work is filled for sick, vacation, bereavement days</li>
<li>Fill in as a Beverage Manager when required</li>
<li>Effective communicator, have flexibility with scheduling, a sense of urgency, able to multi-task, sharp with a professional demeanor</li>
<li>All other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<br>
