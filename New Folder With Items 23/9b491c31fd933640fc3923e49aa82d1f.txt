<h2>Forecast Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Job Summary: </b></p>
<p>Manage the demand forecast using the Company’s forecasting software (Demand Solutions) and incorporate input from the sales organization and Category Managers so that forecast accuracy is maintained at or above the Company objective. Critical information such as market trends, impact from competition, advertising, new product launches, seasonality, product life-cycle stage, and historical outliers must be considered and accounted for in the forecast, where appropriate. Implement metrics to monitor and track forecast performance and actively participate in the S&amp;OP process.</p>
<p><b>Essential Functions: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage demand forecast and appropriate planning parameters in forecasting software and ensure new forecast is transferred to ERP system monthly.</li>
<li>Participate in monthly forecast meetings with sales team to review prior month performance, identify forecast outliers and incorporate market intelligence into forecast.</li>
<li>Actively participate in monthly sales and operation planning process.</li>
<li>Monitor and follow up on demand forecast metrics to ensure continuous improvement.</li>
<li>Actively participate in establishing annual sales plan; work with sales managers to forecast upcoming year’s sales for top accounts and market channels.</li>
<li>Work closely with Category Managers to forecast demand for new products and report on sales performance.</li>
<li>Work closely with supply team so abnormal demand is planned for and supplied as necessary.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Skills &amp; Abilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of three (3) years’ experience managing demand forecasts using a forecast software package, preferably Demand Solutions.</li>
<li>Two (2) years’ experience in inventory management and demand planning in an operations or distribution environment.</li>
<li>Must have experience with ERP Systems.</li>
<li>Must have the ability to interact and work with various functions (sales, marketing, finance and operations), influence forecasting decisions and report results to company management.</li>
<li>Must be able to prioritize tasks and communicate effectively with internal customers, external customers and senior management.</li>
<li>Proficient with Microsoft Office</li>
<li>Results-oriented, self-starter with a high level of integrity and customer focus.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Education Requirements: </b></p>
<p>Bachelor’s Degree in Business (Accounting, Finance, Logistics, Supply Chain, etc.) or related field of study.</p>
