<p><b>Position Summary </b></p>
<p>WDVM25 in Germantown, MD is recruiting for Account Executives to join our team! We are looking for passionate, motivated, goal-oriented individuals with a positive work ethic and the desire to WIN and EXCEL. This is a fast-paced sales environment with unlimited income potential. We offer a unique opportunity if you are looking for a challenging environment, with the tools, and resources to help you succeed as a sales leader. In this role you will prospect and develop new customers for both TV and digital advertising business. You will create and execute plans and strategies that follow industry best practices while keeping an eye on the competition.</p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Prospects, qualifies, closes and grows new business</li>
<li>Develops new and non-traditional revenue through consistent and creative prospecting</li>
<li>Owns the client relationship as the television and digital marketing services sales lead and subject matter expert</li>
<li>Establishes, maintains and grows meaningful client relationships</li>
<li>Continues to learn and implement new strategies and products to grow revenue</li>
<li>Responsible for order entry, contracts, and follow-up</li>
<li>Advocates for our products and programs</li>
<li>Treats coworkers, prospects, and clients with a service-centric attitude</li>
<li>Performs other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
