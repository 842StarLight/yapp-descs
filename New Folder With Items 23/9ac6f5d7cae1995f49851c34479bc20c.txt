<h2>Technical Field Support Manager-Construction Products</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Chase Corporation in Pittsburgh, PA is looking for a Technical Field Support Manager.</p>
<p>This position is responsible for field and lab support of Bridge &amp; Highway products (including Rosphalt) including providing customer technical support on-the-bridge and at asphalt plants, performing Job Mix Formulations (JMFs), leading product development work, providing technical support and testing for internal and external customers, and providing technical direction and support to manufacturing personnel. The position is also responsible for the successful qualification and certification of the above products to customer and industry specifications. Specifically, these include supporting all Bridge and Highway product lines for Expansion Joints, Membranes and Rosphalt (with primary emphasis on Rosphalt).</p>
<p><b>PRINCIPLE ACCOUNTABILITIES – ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES: </b> <i>(Other duties may be assigned)</i></p>
<p>1. Work with contractors, departments of transportation (DOTs), engineers and other customers on-site, at bridges, asphalt plants and other locations as a technical resource for Bridge &amp; Highway products.</p>
<p>2. Supervise third party contractors and engineers in their duties of inspection of Chase work on bridges and other locations as may be necessary.</p>
<p>3. Perform Job Mix Formulations (JMFs) in accordance with standard industry practices and confirm JMFs in coordination with customers.</p>
<p>4. Provide technical support and testing to the manufacturing operation and supply chain group with regard to all Bridge and Highway products and other product segments, as required.</p>
<p>5. Plan and formulate product development proposals such as objective or purpose of projects, applications that can be utilized from findings, cost of projects, and equipment and human resource requirements</p>
<p>6. Provide technical support and testing to customers and sales personnel on application-specific inquiries.</p>
<p>7. Review and analyze proposals submitted to determine if benefits derived and possible applications justify expenditures</p>
<p>8. Manage and document project activities though use of Chase stage gate design control process. Provides progress updates to Construction Business Unit Director through weekly updates, project reports, presentations etc.</p>
<p>9. Interface with consulting firms for possible joint research studies, etc.</p>
<p>10. Lead our customer technical service and product certification efforts.</p>
<p>11. Participate in, and represent company’s interests before, technical societies, trade organizations and code authorities, for example TRB and ASTM, Underwriters Laboratories (UL), and other Bridge and Highway oriented associations.</p>
<p>12. Supervise scale up manufacturing of new products.</p>
<p>13. Work with Plant Engineering at various plants on process development for new products and process optimization for existing products.</p>
<p>14. Provides other technical support to Sales, Manufacturing, Operations and Customers as required.</p>
<p>15. Includes up to 50% to 75% travel.</p>
<p>16. Lead contractor training and certification of asphalt companies and contractors.a</p>
<p>17. Assist in (Perhaps actually be responsible for) preparation of customer project specifications to include Chase Bridge and Highway products</p>
<p>KNOWLEDGE AND EXPERIENCE:</p>
<p>· Engineering degree preferred (relevant Professional Engineer’s license a plus) with BS or MS and demonstrated relevant experience in asphalt industry.</p>
<p>· Experience in the asphalt industry with background in paving or asphalt plant operations, and preferabley, Job Mix Formulation capability. Comfortable interacting with contractors and General contractors.</p>
<p>· 8-10 years experience with an asphalt binder company desirable (but not necessary). Background knowledge of asphalt products and additives a plus.</p>
<p>· Knowledge of equipment and processing in the paving and asphalt industry</p>
<p>· Experience and understanding of construction and contracting practices in the paving and asphalt industry as well as specification practices.</p>
<ul><li>Background of participating in industry-oriented events.</li></ul>
<p>· Attention to detail and excellent written and oral reporting and communication skills.</p>
