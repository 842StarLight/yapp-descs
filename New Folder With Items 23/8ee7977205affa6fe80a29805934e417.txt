<p>
This position reports to the <b>Chief Marketing Officer.</b></p>
<p><b>
What will you do?</b></p>
<p>
The Vice President, Brand &amp; Digital Marketing is a critical role in the development and delivery of MDLIVE customer and marketing strategies. This includes ownership of the MDLIVE customer strategy, brand/marketing strategies, product marketing, and digital marketing. This role is also responsible for marketing analytics including ongoing measurement, optimization, mix management and competitive analysis, in partnership with MDLIVE finance and data science teams. With a strong focus on the key business metrics including revenue, profit, demand, traffic, site conversion, retention, and engagement, this role will lead the marketing strategy to deliver increased utilization among MDLIVE current and potential customers.</p>
<p>
In addition, you will:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Manage customer strategy, with increasingly sophisticated expertise of target customers to inform marketing and product strategies</li>
<li>
Develop and optimize ongoing marketing strategy, including mix management, marketing calendar, measurement, competitive analysis, and reporting; manage marketing spend and performance to budget</li>
<li>
Build and manage marketing plans for major initiatives including new product launches and new capabilities</li>
<li>
Set strategy and lead agency partners, internal digital/email team; implement processes to streamline execution</li>
<li>
Develop, execute and optimize digital marketing plans to increase traffic and engagement with new, prospective customers; manage all digital channels including but not limited to display advertising, paid search campaigns and aggregators</li>
<li>
Manage email strategy and ongoing execution/optimization, in coordination with other channels, aligned to overall marketing strategy</li>
<li>
Lead the thinking for mobile platform development including website content, consumer research, marketing, and website technology</li>
<li>
Grow and upgrade organizational capability in the digital area and seamlessly integrate digital marketing into each marketing program</li>
<li>
Actively partner and collaborate with cross-functional partners including sales, account management, public relations, creative, and go-to-market and campaign teams to ensure a consistent message and focus across all customer touch points</li>
<li>
Partner to develop creative strategies that provide meaningful engagements and build the brand among customer targets</li>
</ul>
