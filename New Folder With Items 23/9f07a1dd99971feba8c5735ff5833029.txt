<p>Security Consulting Engineer
</p>
<p>Aurora Systems Consulting<br>
Torrance, CA 90505
</p>
<p>Do you strive for excellence? Love a rewarding challenge? Have what it takes to provide cutting<br>
edge technology along with unparalleled customer service? Love working with a great team? If<br>
so, you might just belong at Aurora.
</p>
<p>Aurora is a cybersecurity firm working with mid to large enterprise customers nationwide with<br>
their ever-growing cybersecurity needs. The exponential growth in cybersecurity has kept us<br>
busy and we’re looking for engineers like you to help us grow. Our core competencies include<br>
security assessments, encryption, network security, endpoint security, and data loss<br>
prevention. You will perform on-site and remote client consulting projects, from a pre-sales and<br>
post-sales perspective, in support of Aurora’s Security Consulting Services. You will also be<br>
responsible for positioning; demonstrating; assessing; designing; developing and implementing<br>
enterprise security solutions; as well as providing ongoing support to Aurora's clients.
</p>
<p>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIESinclude, but not limited to, the following:

<p>Security Consulting Services:
 </p>
<p>Perform security risk assessments for customer<br>
 Supports Aurora's Sales team in the role of a pre-sales engineer adhering to Aurora's
</p>
<p>Sales process, while always striving to provide top quality solutions and services to our<br>
clients
 </p>
<p>Assesses, designs and architects solutions based on Aurora’s proven security solution<br>
set.
 </p>
<p>Communicate technical capabilities of proposed solutions based on customer business<br>
and technical requirements.
 </p>
<p>Demonstrate Proof of Concepts of proposed solutions based on customer business and<br>
technical requirements.
 </p>
<p>Executes onsite and remote IT security projects and services adhering to Aurora’s<br>
Professional Services process and quality standards.
 </p>
<p>Provides pre/post-sales updates and reports detailing project deliverable status<br>
including challenges and other opportunities to meet customer’s business<br>
requirements.<br>
