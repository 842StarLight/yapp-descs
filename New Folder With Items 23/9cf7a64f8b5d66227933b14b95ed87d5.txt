<p>AFI is searching for an experienced, detail-oriented, and professional individual to serve as a Business Operations Manager. AFI seeks to continually operate on a nationwide scale by utilizing a lean manufacturing model. In order to achieve this goal, the Business Operations Manager will be tasked with continual analysis of current procedures in order to identify areas of improvement. Success in this role means developing new strategies to lower costs, establishing global income goals while monitoring expenses, coaching other team members on proper procedures, and collaborating with key stakeholders on how to achieve KPIs.
