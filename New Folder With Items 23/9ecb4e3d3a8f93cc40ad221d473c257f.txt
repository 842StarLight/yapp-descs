Acumen Solutions is currently hiring a<b> Strategic Alliances Manager</b> to join our team in <b>McLean, VA</b>. The primary accountability is development of strategies and relationship expansion to grow revenue and market presence for both Acumen Solutions and the assigned partners. This role is focused on cultivating the alliance relationship and execution across Acumen Solutions.<br>
