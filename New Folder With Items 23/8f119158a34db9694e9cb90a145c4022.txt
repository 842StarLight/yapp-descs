<h2>Summer 2020 Internship Program</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>AutoZone’s Summer Internship Program is designed to give students a challenging, hands-on experience in the corporate retail industry. Depending on your educational background and interests, we offer opportunities in a variety of departments that serve to support our stores throughout the United States, Puerto Rico, Mexico and Brazil. No matter which area you choose, you'll attend weekly presentations with senior leadership, gain exposure to different areas of our business, and work on business-critical projects – all while experiencing AutoZone’s corporate culture and life in Memphis.<br>
<br>
<b>Program Overview</b><br>
o 10 - 13 Week Paid Internship<br>
o Networking and Community Service Events<br>
o Business Mentorship Program<br>
o Program runs from May – August 2019<br>
o All internships are located in Memphis, TN<br>
o Top100 Internship Program in 2018 &amp; 2019<br>
<br>
<b>Qualifications</b><br>
Actively enrolled in a college or university AND have at least one semester remaining at the completion of the internship program<br>
</p>
