<h2>Installation Operations Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>Essential Functions and responsibilities</b></p>
<p>
The Installation Operations Manager leads project installations to service the clients and support Furnitureland Contract Sales and Support team. This position develops the costs for projects, provides the quotation to the sales member that is applied to all Sales Quotations and follows the project through the complete installation process.</p>
<p><b>
Competencies</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Interface with Sales staff for preliminary project requirements and identification of client services that are needed for each opportunity on a weekly scheduled meeting; a face to face with each Sales Consultant producing Install and operation calendar and workforce schedule on a shared drive.</li>
<li>
Manage installation department for profitability by costing man hours/cost to all projects with job costing report within 72 hours after job/project is completed.</li>
<li>
Recording all data in Shared File for communication and coordination of all projects on a continual day to day process.</li>
<li>
Expert level of Accounting System (<b>Hedberg/TeamDesign</b>/or other), <b>project management software</b> and <b>Plangrid</b>
</li>
<li>
Expert level of Microsoft Office – EXCEL, WORD, POWER POINT, SHARED DOCS</li>
<li>
Expert level for reading furniture drawings, plan and 3-D; specifications, finishes, validations, installation drawings and BOM breakdown for driving punch free installations.</li>
<li>
Teach and educate FLS CONTRACT Technicians and build the team.</li>
<li>
Schedule vendor training, updates and presentations to installation technicians.</li>
<li>
Responsible for ordering and maintenance of installation library materials, tools, equipment and products</li>
<li>
Responsible for collecting sign off paperwork and preforming walkthroughs at installations; producing a punch list within 48 hours from completion.</li>
<li>
Responsible for the scheduled plan of delivery within 5 – 7 working days of order entry.</li>
<li>
Complete time sheets and other administrative responsibilities of yourself and lead technicians with administrative responsibilities.</li>
<li>
Installation planning requires use of 3rd party sub-contractors – responsible for adding as vendors and seeking their technical abilities to fit the project on a national basis with FLS Clients/</li>
<li>
Produce all punch lists, warranty orders and service call for prompt solutions for the client.</li>
<li>
Adaptable to changing working priorities.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Required Education and Experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Four to five years of commercial furniture installation and operations.</li>
<li>
Expert level of Accounting System (<b>Hedberg/TeamDesign</b>/or other), <b>project management software</b> and <b>Plangrid.</b>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Preferred Education and Experience</b></p>
<ul><li>
Associates or bachelor’s degree</li></ul>
<p><b>
Working conditions</b></p>
<p>
This job operates in a professional office environment, both client and Furnitureland. This role routinely uses standard office equipment such as laptop computers, photocopiers and smartphones.</p>
<p><b>
Physical requirements</b></p>
<p>
This job requires the ability to carry 50 lbs, and competent use of dollies and hand trucks. Also, the ability to use professional tools for installation of furniture in a commercial setting.</p>
