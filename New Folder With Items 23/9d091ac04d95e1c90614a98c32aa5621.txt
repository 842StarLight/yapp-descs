<h2>Assistant General Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">A national leader in fitness, Club Pilates is currently expanding the team and seeking a qualified Assistant General Manager that is focused on driving sales and service excellence for our luxury fitness brand. This is the perfect opportunity to interact with clients on a daily basis to create a supportive and meaningful community!
<br><br>
<b>POSITION:</b> The Sales Manager will oversee all studio sales functionality
<br><br>
<b>REQUIREMENTS:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>1+ years of fitness sales or relevant sales experience preferred</li>
<li>Confident in generating personal sales and training Sales Reps</li>
<li>Ability to manage and drive multiple revenue streams including memberships and retail</li>
<li>Previous management or supervisory experience required</li>
<li>Must have excellent communication and strong interpersonal skills in person and over the phone</li>
<li>Must be solution-based and results oriented, competitive spirit</li>
<li>Ability to recognize areas of improvement and make changes using good judgment</li>
<li>An affinity and passion for fitness</li>
<li>Professional, punctual, reliable and neat and organized</li>
<li>Strong attention to detail and accuracy</li>
<li>Trustworthy and ability to handle confidential information</li>
<li>Ability to work harmoniously with co-workers, clients and the general public</li>
<li>Proficiency with computers and Studio software</li>
<li>This is a full-time position. Candidate must be able to work a flexible schedule with nights and weekend availability.</li>
</ul>
<b>DUTIES:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Lead generation including Grass Roots Marketing and networking</li>
<li>Membership and retail sales</li>
<li>Manage staff schedule</li>
<li>Supervise Sales Representatives</li>
<li>Collect out-standing dues</li>
<li>Maintain cleanliness and organization of the studio</li>
<li>Enforce studio policies and procedures</li>
<li>Provide exceptional level of customer service at the studio to existing members via face-to-face service, phone and email</li>
<li>Become proficient at ClubReady software to troubleshoot client account inquiries and possible sales opportunities</li>
<li>Contact prospects/leads to schedule barre class and enroll them in a membership</li>
<li>Drive and retain retail and membership sales</li>
<li>Maintain organization of staff, cleanliness and organization of the Studio</li>
<li>Enforce policies and procedures relating to customers</li>
<li>Ensure all forms, administrative supplies and studio literature is stocked and visible</li>
<li>Participate in as many networking/community events in the surrounding community and studio promotions as possible to be scheduled with Corporate</li>
<li>Execute light Operational duties such as: ordering supplies, scheduling staff, aid with social media and anything else Owner sees fit</li>
<li>Any other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<b>COMPENSATION &amp; PERKS:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>This position offers hourly base rate plus incentives</li>
<li>Unlimited Club Pilates Membership while employed</li>
<li>Employee Retail Discount</li>
</ul>
</div>
