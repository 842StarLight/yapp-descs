<h2>Marketing Automation and Production Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">--------------
<br>About the Role
<br>--------------
<br><br>
Are you passionate about developing creative, effective digital marketing programs? Would you like to play an integral role within a rapidly-expanding company? If so, this role may be a perfect match.
<br><br>
The Marketing Automation and Production Specialist is first and foremost, a Marketo production extraordinaire. In this role, you'll work with the marketing team to develop campaigns that fuel our business. Using existing templates in Marketo (and to a lesser extent, Wordpress), you'll create top-quality landing pages, emails and other digital assets. Please note, this is not a designer or developer role--this is a digital marketing production role.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Using templates within Marketo (and sometimes Wordpress), to create digital assets (landing pages, emails, etc.)</li>
<li>Ensuring deliverability, user experience, and rendering best practices</li>
<li>Reinforcing and supporting marketing automation best practices</li>
<li>Creating the various elements of Marketo programs including: smart campaigns, forms, smart lists, triggered alerts, email tokens, A/B testing, etc.</li>
<li>Ensuring timeliness and quality of all deliverables</li>
<li>Coordinating regularly with our in-house Design team to ensure brand consistency</li>
<li>Providing a technical point of view and system knowledge to support marketing and other internal teams to ensure effective design of digital programs</li>
<li>Maintaining effective communication with all stakeholders regarding project status, including project plans, issues and escalations</li>
<li>Understanding and helping improve our marketing automation strategy and process</li>
<li>Offering your insights and ideas as part of the collaborative planning process</li>
</ul>
<b>Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree or equivalent experience</li>
<li>Highly proficient working in Marketo to create marketing assets and programs</li>
<li>1+ years Marketo experience</li>
<li>1+ years digital marketing experience</li>
<li>Extremely detail-oriented with excellent time management skills</li>
<li>1+ years Wordpress admin experience preferred</li>
<li>Experience using Hotjar, Google Analytics and other measurement tools a plus</li>
<li>Experience working in B2B tech industry a plus</li>
<li>HTML / CSS familiarity a plus</li>
<li>Knowledge of standards for websites and mass email programs, including compliance (legal/regulatory), performance, security, data back-ups, etc. a plus</li>
</ul>
---------
<br>About You
<br>---------
<br><br>
You are a person who thrives in a fast-paced, collaborative environment. You have a keen eye for detail and are fast to execute. You are a good listener and communicator. You enjoy creating, testing and continually optimizing digital marketing assets and campaigns.
