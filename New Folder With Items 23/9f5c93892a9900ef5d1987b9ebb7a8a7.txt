<p><b>
Industry Advisor</b></p>
<p>The Industry Advisor is a critical role supporting our North America Utilities business to grow sales pipeline and engage with our customers to secure revenue. The Industry Executive Advisor provides industry perspective, strategic solution advice, and thought leadership to support sales cycles and customer co-innovation programs, drive pipeline growth and quality through demand generation strategies, and contribute to building and maintaining SAP’s leadership position in the industry and associated industry segments.</p>
<p><b>Duties and Responsibilities</b><br>
<b>Area 1 – Market Analysis and Strategy:</b><br>
Analyze the industry’s potential and help craft a go-to-market approach for the industry defining the big sales plays for the industry<br>
Combine SAP go-to-market strategies with industry perspective to develop and execute programs designed to build high-quality pipeline<br>
Work closely with industry and regional marketing management to ensure that we align marketing dollars to big bet areas in the industry<br>
<b>Area 2 – Business Development:</b><br>
Lead initial meetings with customer executives and educate the customers on industry trends, successes at other companies in their industry and SAP Portfolio<br>
Partner with field sales teams to identify revenue opportunities, create account strategies, and execute sales plays<br>
Build and maintain a customer network and link customers together to help build and secure pipeline<br>
Develop customer references in the industry to showcase SAP’s customer successes and educate the ecosystem on these references<br>
<b>Area 3 – Thought Leadership:</b><br>
Write whitepapers and other thought leadership pieces to illustrate important industry / cross-industry topics<br>
Develop content for conveying the SAP strategy and industry solutions (including new innovations) while working closely with other global industry teams<br>
<b>Area 4 – Industry Brand Management:</b><br>
Development of SAP's brand through thought leadership, content creation, appearances in industry events, articles and social &amp; traditional media interviews, etc.<br>
Represent SAP externally on a regional and national level on industry sector, innovation and value management related topics<br>
<b>Area 5 – Customer Engagement:</b><br>
Help shape the engagement by advising the account teams to ensure a right industry message is delivered in deal engagement and execution deliverables like RFP Responses, Business Cases, Product Demos etc.</p>
