<h2>Leasing Consultant / Agent (Part-Time)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>At TI Communities, people are our most valuable asset. We are actively seeking individuals who want to create a better life for themselves and the residents we serve. If you are ready to join a team of passionate, impact-driven, professionals, TI Communities wants to talk to you. TI Communities manages workforce housing in densely populated communities and provides enrichment programs for the residents of the communities. The ideal candidate for this position:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Has multifamily experience</li>
<li>
Has customer service experience</li>
<li>
Has a passion for serving others; exceptional customer service and is wildly passionate about helping people.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
WHAT WE WILL DO FOR YOU:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Good work life balance.</li>
<li>
Opportunity for growth</li>
<li>
Create an autonomous, engaging, and rewarding environment where you will be valued.</li>
<li>
Reward and recognize your achievements and contributions.</li>
<li>
Be transparent and open.</li>
<li>
Provide time with family and friends with Company paid holidays.</li>
<li>
Support and encourage your own personal development and growth.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
WHAT YOU WILL DO FOR US:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Conduct business in accordance with company policies, Fair Housing laws, Americans with Disabilities Act, Fair Credit Reporting Act and all other laws pertaining to multifamily housing.</li>
<li>
Greet prospective residents and handle all phone and email correspondence in a polite and professional manner.</li>
<li>
Take responsibility for curb appeal and leasing of the community; open and inspect vacant apartments noting any items needing to be corrected.</li>
<li>
Be familiar with the surrounding area and resources available to enrich the lives of our residents.</li>
<li>
Accurately prepare and be able to explain the residential lease and all related documents to residents.</li>
<li>
Maintain files in a neat and secure manner.</li>
<li>
Provide excellent customer service to all residents, supplier partners, and business partners.</li>
<li>
Communicate with property managers regarding property issues.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
EXPERIENCE:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Excellent interpersonal communication skills; must be collaborative and adaptive.</li>
<li>
Experience with Entrata software a plus but not required</li>
<li>
Bilingual Preferred</li>
</ul>

Job Type: Part-time</p>
