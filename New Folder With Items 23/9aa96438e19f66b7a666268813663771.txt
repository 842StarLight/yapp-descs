<p><b>Responsibilities
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Verifies and keeps records on incoming and outgoing shipments.
</li>
<li>Process all incoming inventory and corresponds with shipper to rectify damages and shortages.
</li>
<li>Maintain inventory of shipping materials and supplies &amp; maintain inventory control.
</li>
<li>Make arrangements for customer’s orders to be processed and delivered according to customer specifications.
</li>
<li>Maintain work area and equipment to adhere to all safety standards.
</li>
<li>Fill customer orders according to standard operating procedures.
