<h2>Administrative Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Functional Area:
</p>AS - Administrative Support
<p></p>Estimated Travel Percentage (%): No Travel
<p></p>Relocation Provided: No
<p></p>American General Life Insurance Company
<p></p>
<p><b>Position Summary:
</b></p>
<p>An industry leader in financial services is looking for an energetic and detail oriented individual who enjoys making a difference, being part of a team, and working in a fast paced environment.
</p>
<p>The Administrative Assistant’s primary purpose is to support the Internal Sales organization. The ideal candidate will be able to demonstrate, under minimal supervision, the ability to perform clerical, administrative, travel arrangements, and general office duties requiring advanced skills and significant experience. Assist with general site activities and schedule facility maintenance as necessary.
</p>
<p><b>Performance Objectives:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Support Internal Sales leadership with a variety of administrative functions, such as presentation preparation, solve moderately complex problems that address day to day issues, monitor daily office services, and other various administrative tasks as needed
</li>
<li>MS Word Processing, Excel spread sheets, PowerPoint presentations and some data entry
</li>
<li>Support management on compliance and regulatory audits (internal and external), contracting and vendor governance.
</li>
<li>Ensuring administrative processes and office operations run smoothly. Point of contact for maintenance, information technology, and other external service providers.
</li>
<li>Setup, coordinate, manage and coordinate travel arrangements as needed
</li>
<li>Handles vendor invoices and payments
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What we are looking for:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Skilled in verbal and written communications
</li>
<li>Strong sense of urgency and problem solving skills
</li>
<li>Must possess strong organizational skills and attention to detail
</li>
<li>Must be proficient with MS Office applications, and other software applications
</li>
<li>Strong anticipation and forward thinking skills.
</li>
<li>Ability to take a proactive approach to tasks and be comfortable working with minimal instruction and guidance.
</li>
<li>Self-starter, demonstrated aptitude in learning quickly, problem solving, and flexibility to adapt to varying responsibilities including the ability to handle multiple tasks and projects simultaneously in a deadline driven environment
</li>
<li>Prior experience in financial service company preferred
</li>
</ul>
