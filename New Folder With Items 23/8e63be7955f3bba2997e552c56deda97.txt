<h2>Marketing Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div><div><div>
<p>JMH Companies is a New Orleans based company that operates across multiple industries including technology, services, development, restaurants, and retail stores. We have an immediate opening for a talented <b>Marketing Coordinator </b>to join the marketing team to help develop creative messaging, marketing and editorial campaigns for JMH’s hospitality brands including Mahony’s Po-Boys &amp; Seafood, Belladonna Day Spa and more.</p>
<ul>
<li>Develop editorial content and creative recommendations, create branded story ideas and execute with proper style, messaging and tone</li>
<li>Identify ways to further enhance the branding of products and services of JMH Companies and its subsidiaries</li>
<li>Partner with management to develop campaigns and collateral for specific departmental needs</li>
<li>Conduct market/product analysis and research pertaining to the industry to identify customer trends and opportunities</li>
<li>Develop and update marketing materials</li>
<li>Manage projects with accuracy, efficiency, and a sense of urgency</li>
<li>Skills/Tactics: Content marketing, email marketing, content development, collateral development, special projects, public relations, community events, and sponsorship outreach, brand strategy and creative development, digital ads, social media marketing, media strategy, and ad design, analytics</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<div>Qualifications</div>
<div><div>
<div><ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree from an accredited 4-year college or university in the fields of communications, marketing, advertising, or journalism</li>
<li>2-3 years of experience at a branding/advertising agency, consulting firm, corporate marketing department or demonstration of similar work experience</li>
<li>Able to work well independently (self-starter), as a member of a team, and cross-functionally with multiple teams</li>
<li>Comfortable managing multiple assignments in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>Detail-oriented with the ability to check one’s own work and correct errors</li>
<li>Comfortable working with ambiguity and making decisions independently</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Office (Excel, Word, PowerPoint, Outlook)</li>
<li>Plus: Proficiency in graphic design software such as InDesign, Acrobat, etc.</li>
<li>Plus: proficiency in social, email and web marketing tools like Google Ads/Analytics, Facebook Ads Manager, Mailchimp, etc.</li>
</ul></div>
