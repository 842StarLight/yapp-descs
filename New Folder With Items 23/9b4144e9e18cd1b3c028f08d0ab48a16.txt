 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Job Description: Project Manager/ Technical Business Analyst</b></p>
<p><b>Location: Metro NYC</b></p>
<p>Key Position Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Business Analysis &amp; Requirements Definition: Analyze business operations to determine opportunities to automate business processes and functions; Define and document project objectives and business requirements to be translated into functional specifications for systems development, product evolution and new technology</li>
<li>Project Planning: Create and manage detailed project task plans, budgets, and timelines</li>
<li>Project Management: Coordinate and manage project activities, and ensure projects are delivered on time, within budget, adhere to high quality standards, and meet customer expectations</li>
<li>Client Management &amp; Communication: Responsible for serving as liaison between business team and development team to provide technical solutions to meet business needs that enhance the enterprise’s competitive advantage</li>
<li>Testing and Quality Control: Manage testing activities to refine and debug programs</li>
<li>Training and Deployment: Prepare user documentation and participate in end-user training; Support development deployment activities</li>
<li>Reporting: Monitor and summarize progress of project and prepare and distribute status reports</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<p>Skills/Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>5 + years of Project Management and/or Business Analyst experience</li>
<li>Bachelor's Degree in information technology, computer science, business administration, or related discipline</li>
<li>Strong analytical skills to interpret and translate business needs into application and operational requirements</li>
<li>Demonstrated experience planning and managing projects of various sizes</li>
<li>Demonstrated experience analyzing and documenting business processes and requirements</li>
<li>Strong Organizational Skills; Very detail oriented – while fully understanding the big picture</li>
<li>Excellent Written, Presentation and Verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Strategic Thinker with Strong Analytical and Problem-Solving Skills</li>
<li>Professional and Personable; ability to work constructively and collaboratively with people at all levels</li>
<li>Strong Time Management skills; enjoys a fast-paced, challenging environment; can be effective quickly</li>
<li>Willing to take initiative, deal with ambiguity and assume responsibility</li>
</ul>
