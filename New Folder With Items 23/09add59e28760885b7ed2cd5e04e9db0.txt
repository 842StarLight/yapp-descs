<h2>Senior Business Development Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Who we are:</b></p>
<p>
 We are growing quickly at Efficient Forms. Founded in 2004, we are a company who specializes in automated electronic solutions for dynamic and cost-effective forms completion, data collection and process workflow. In 2008, we launched our EfficientHire Solution. Along with being a full-service WOTC Processor, our EfficientHire Suite allows our clients to maximize their Corporate Tax Credits while seamlessly completing all necessary new hire information from Applicant Tracking to Payroll.</p>
<p>
 <b>The Role:</b></p>
<p>
 As a Senior Business Development Representative, you will be responsible for qualifying leads inbound and outbound and generating partner awareness for Efficient Forms across core market sectors Food &amp; Beverage, Staffing, Restaurants, Manufacturing, Construction, etc.. You will assist in direct and partner pipeline generation as well as maintain an accurate database in the company's CRM system. You will utilize principally outbound techniques around a set territory or industry.</p>
<p>
 <b>What you'll do:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Prospect, educate, qualify and develop target accounts from lead-generation activities, such as outbound cold-calling, emailing, InMail and direct mailers</li>
<li>
Research accounts and contacts to develop compelling and tailored value propositions</li>
<li>
Maintain meticulous notes and tracking of accounts and engagement, leveraging tools such as Zoho and Groove</li>
<li>
Seamlessly handle prospect's objections and educate them on Efficient Forms</li>
<li>
Route qualified opportunities to the appropriate teams for further development
</li>
<li>Book meetings that convert to opportunities to achieve quotas</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Desired Qualifications, Skills, and Experience:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
At least 3 years of experience in lead generation role</li>
<li>
History of quota over-achievement</li>
<li>
Thrive in a fast-paced, high-growth, rapidly changing environment</li>
<li>
Highly organized with excellent follow up</li>
<li>
SaaS experience a plus</li>
</ul>
