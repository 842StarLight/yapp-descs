<b>Summary:</b><br>
The Food Service Assistant I, with the Kitchen Satellite Lead, assists in preparing salad bar setup and maintenance during service, service line set up and presentation of serving meals, and kitchen cleaning as part of maintaining a safe food production and service environment. Food Service Assistants are also responsible, as assigned, for recordkeeping of temperatures and production, inventory counting and cashiering duties including recording meal sales, record keeping, money collection and all other functions of QSP including processing advanced payments.<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
-Understands and is able to communicate the philosophy of the BVSD Food Services Program<br>
-Assist in the proper reheating of main course items and side dishes<br>
-Ensures the freshness of all prepared items<br>
-Participates and maintains the cleanliness and sanitation of all kitchen, cafeteria and storage areas<br>
-Participates in the meal service periods assigned either as server or cashier<br>
-Assists in accurate inputting of all production and meal count data required for compliance with the National School Lunch Program<br>
-Assists Kitchen Satellite Lead in accurate reporting of all sales<br>
-Records and maintains temperature logs for equipment<br>
