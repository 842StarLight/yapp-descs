<p><b>Product Pricing Analyst, Project Atlas</b></p>
<p>
The primary function of this position on the Atlas Product Pricing Management (PPM) team will be to assist with pricing standardization initiatives and the creation of a Product Pricing Catalog across the enterprise to ensure proper alignment with Project Atlas guidelines, technology and infrastructure. This role will collaborate with various project, local business unit, and other internal teams in support of Project Atlas related efforts, and then ongoing, to facilitate and ensure the utilization of the Product Pricing Catalog by the business units and other systems where pricing is consume.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
This role will report to the Atlas PPM Team Leader.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
As the Product Pricing Analyst, you will:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Collaborate with global business unit team members and other subject matter experts across Pricing, Product, and Finance, to facilitate the compilation of various product and pricing information required to champion the enterprise transformation required as part of Project Atlas</li>
<li>
Provide tactical support and oversight of discovery, rationalization, and alignment tasks which help in the development of a go-to-market Product Pricing Catalog</li>
<li>
Align the Product Pricing Catalog and sales, marketing, legal, pricing, data, billing, finance, and data teams will be a key focus for this individual</li>
<li>
Advocate the need to balance the flexibility available via new technology/functionality with the requirement of global standardization and consistency – managing expectations accordingly</li>
<li>
Monitor timelines and manage assigned projects to meet deadlines and Project Atlas guidelines</li>
<li>
Provide status updates for business unit initiatives as assigned</li>
<li>
Facilitate implementation of common methodology and language for Project Atlas</li>
<li>
Provide process support to project management and/or initiative project leads</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
