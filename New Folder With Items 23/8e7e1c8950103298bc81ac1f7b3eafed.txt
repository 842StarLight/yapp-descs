<h2>Brand Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div><div><div>
<p>Brand Manager will work closely with sales and marketing to help execute a brand strategy that drives brand awareness, engagement and conversion for our promotional campaigns. The ideal candidate will coordinate the efforts to increase the value and performance of our brands. They will focus on our image, positioning and responsible for providing information and insights regarding customers, markets, competitors, brand reputation and overall trends. The ideal candidate will participate in complex research projects that examine, analyze and predict consumer behavior.</p>
<p>Duties and Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Primary liaison to execute and project manage our brand strategies and submit data on brand exposure, reputation and health.</li>
<li>Focus on Improving brand recognition and reputation for our brands and create go-to market campaigns for brand to build brand loyalty.</li>
<li>Ability to understand, analyze and tell a story on current consumer trends and insights collected from third party data and demographic software and compare to our customer data to define opportunities, identify our target consumer and predict consumer trends.</li>
<li>Responsible for utilizing a diverse set of information and data to develop recommendations for brand and consumer development. Pivot Tables and advanced Excel functions in a day are essential. Charts and tables are preferred.</li>
<li>Researching consumer markets, monitoring market trends and identifying potential areas in which to target, based upon consumer needs and spending habits</li>
<li>Ability to create surveys to capture market data and customer satisfaction analysis</li>
<li>Collect and interpret data regarding consumer behavior, the development and implementation of online advertising and promotional strategies, website analysis and search engine optimization to identify KPI’s.</li>
<li>Administer our marketing compliance process and procedure to ensure changes regarding compliance standards are met and monitoring marketing material and communication to ensure standards are met.</li>
<li>Manage marketing assets and brand guidelines for our multiple brands.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>

<p>Technical Competencies</p>

</ul>
