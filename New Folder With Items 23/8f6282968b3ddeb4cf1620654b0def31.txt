<h2>Customer Service Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Customers across North America depend on ID Technology to design, build, and install the most effective labeling, coding, and marking systems for their product lines. As a leading manufacturer and integrator of labeling, coding, and marking equipment, ID Technology provides our customers with six separate label converting plant locations across North America to efficiently and quickly produce quality labels and tags. ID Technology also manufactures the LSI line of integrated labeling systems. As part of the ProMach Labeling &amp; Coding business line, ID Technology helps our packaging customers protect and grow the reputation and trust of their consumers. ProMach is a family of best-in-class packaging solution brands serving manufacturers of all sizes and geographies in the food, beverage, pharmaceutical, personal care, and household and industrial goods industries. ProMach brands operate across the entire packaging spectrum: filling and capping, flexibles, pharma, product handling, labeling and coding, and end of line. ProMach is headquartered near Cincinnati, Ohio, with manufacturing facilities and offices throughout North America, Europe, South America, and Asia. For more information about ProMach, visit http://www.ProMachBuilt.com and for more information on ProMach Careers, visit http://www.ProMachCareers.com.</p>
<p>
 ID Technology, Fresno, CA seeks an experienced, positive individual who will contribute to the success of the company by establishing and maintaining good client relations. This position receives and processes to completion customer inquiries, orders and concerns. An ability to blend excellent communication skills with organizational and detail orientations are keys to success.</p>
<p><b>
JOB DESCRIPTION</b></p>
<p>
In this important role, you will:</p>
<ul>
<li>Process, track &amp; expedite customer orders, verifying for accuracy</li>
<li>Address shipment &amp; delivery inquiries</li>
<li>Conduct phone work with clients, internal sales and service representatives</li>
<li>Enter and manage data in software system</li>
<li>Follow up as appropriate, internally and externally, providing excellent communication and service</li>
<li>Maintain excellent records</li>
<li>Partner with others throughout the company to ensure successful processes</li>
<li>Coordinate special project tasks</li>
</ul>
<p><b>JOB REQUIREMENTS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Two year degree or relevant certification preferred</li>
<li>Experience in sales &amp;/or customer service</li>
<li>Ability to understand customers’ needs &amp; relate to production processes</li>
<li>Strong written and verbal communication and human relations skills</li>
<li>Demonstrated excellence in organization and time management skills</li>
<li>Must be computer literate</li>
<li>Strong working knowledge of Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop</li>
<li>Must maintain a positive attitude</li>
<li>Excellent project coordination skills</li>
</ul> <p><b>Other Skills/Abilities which will ensure success</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Commitment to service</li>
<li>Ability to assess client needs</li>
<li>Willingness to learn</li>
<li>Ability to work well with ever changing priorities and/or situations</li>
<li>Must be high energy, self-starter</li>
<li>Willingness to go above &amp; beyond to ensure superiors support of both internal &amp; external customers</li>
</ul>
