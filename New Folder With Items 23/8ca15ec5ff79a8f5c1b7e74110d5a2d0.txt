<p><b>Résumé du poste
</b></p>
<p></p>Position Summary
<br>
Responsible, under limited budget/Sales order supervision, for financial reporting and analysis for SEA C&amp;S Division. This role will participate in month-end closing, driven revenue recognition by all C&amp;S division and work for hoc accounting and various financial duties. This role will also assist in budgeting and non-trader inter-company transactions between other subsidiaries.
<br>
Works independently. General work direction is given and completed assignments and/or work is reviewed to ensure compliance with processes, standards and policies of the organization. It may instructor coach other colleagues.
<p></p>
<p><b>Rôle et Responsabilités
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Duties and Responsibilities
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provide financial management Revenue pre and actual reports as needed to management.
</li>
<li>Analyze actual operating results of each department through the year and compare to
<p>Monthly forecast and yearly plan and interpret data and presents results to management
</p>
</li>
<li>Processes vendor invoices within the SAP business enterprise accounting system and R/S calculation of TV Plus and process payment to the partners.
</li>
<li>Create sales order process to recognize in the system for Smart TV Apps and Mobile revenue.
</li>
<li>Receive monthly confirmed revenue updates from BD team and external partners to summarize in monthly sales master file.
</li>
<li>Work for yearly business plan and template creation to be used in communication for all departments.
</li>
<li>Monitor Invoices status of existing customer and responsible to report the sales results to controller.
</li>
<li>Respond to budget related requests for financial data. Perform fiscal and variance analysis on a variety of issues in support of and to facilitate budgetary decision making.
</li>
<li>Reconcile PG data and CSMP billing system to confirm the tax posting, accrued income balance.
<br>
Prepare monthly social/event budget analysis reports for each department and analyze variance amount.
</li>
<li>Resolve budget error problems, including trouble shooting and reminding users of company policy.
</li>
<li>Facilitate and guide Vendor Management. Perform other finance or administrative support projects and tasks assigned by financial management.
</li>
<li>Provide bank details for vendor / customer / ACH transfer registration
<br>
Manage communication with customer/vendor directly if needed, but should be managed primarily by biz team.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Skills and Qualifications
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Experience
</b></p>
<ul><li>Bachelor's Degree with 2-5 years of experience is required. Prior experience in accounting,
</li></ul>
<p>Budgeting and/or audits preferred.
</p>
<ul>
<li>Experience in SAP R/3 software or similar business management and general ledger system required.
</li>
<li>Must be proficient MS Excel, MS Word, and PowerPoint PC software. Ability to work in an open system environment with multiple software applications.
</li>
</ul>
