<h2>MATERIAL HANDLER 2</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Founded in 1925, MacLean-Fogg is a worldwide enterprise currently operating 40 global manufacturing facilities across 5 continents with annual sales in excess of one billion (USD) and a workforce of over 3,500 people. MacLean-Fogg is a privately held enterprise headquartered in Mundelein, IL USA and is comprised of two primary businesses, MacLean-Fogg Component Solutions and MacLean Power Systems. MacLean-Fogg Component Solutions (MFCS) is a leading manufacturer of fastener components, engineered components and engineered plastics for automotive, heavy truck, and other diverse industries. MacLean Power Systems (MPS) is a leading manufacturer of products for electric utility, telecommunications and civil markets.
<br><br>
MATERIAL HANDLER - The objective of this role is to support the goals and objectives of the warehouse.
<br><br>
<b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Ensure strict adherence to all applicable rules and procedures within the assigned area</li>
<li>Ensure that targets for the assigned areas are met through:</li>
<li>Maintaining accurate inventory that is free from damage</li>
<li>Shipping, receiving, and delivering supplies, materials and equipment, and performing related work</li>
<li>Ensure correct material flow plant-wide by supporting and following the appropriate systems:</li>
<li>FIFO</li>
<li>Warehouse SOP</li>
<li>Operating System</li>
<li>Maintain the work area and equipment in a clean and orderly condition and follow prescribed safety regulations &amp; 5S back lot outside</li>
<li>Must report defective materials or questionable conditions to the department supervisor</li>
<li>Fill in for any needed functions due to absence, short term work load requirements</li>
<li>Follow all Safety, Environmental, and Quality guidelines and procedures</li>
<li>Other duties may be assigned or required for this position</li>
</ul>
<b>REQUIRED SKILLS:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>A strong work ethic</li>
<li>The ability to provide excellent customer service</li>
<li>A high degree of accuracy and attention to detail</li>
<li>Must be able to work weekend hours</li>
<li>Perform all other duties and responsibilities as requested</li>
<li>Ability to perform but not limited to the following tasks, be familiar with and able to interpret packing slips.</li>
<li>Must be familiar with various forms used and required by the major freight carriers.</li>
<li>Must be familiar with proper packaging methods and able to interpret visual aids used to convey specific packaging requirements.</li>
<li>Must be familiar with the most common material handling equipment and be a licensed forklift truck operator.</li>
<li>Experience with UPS/FEDEX online software is desirable.</li>
</ul>
<b>REQUIRED EDUCATION AND QUALIFICATIONS:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>2 years’ work experience in a manufacturing or warehouse environment preferred</li>
<li>Required to read and write in English, follow verbal instructions, and use simple math.</li>
<li>Must have a valid driver’s license and a good driving record</li>
<li>Experience in a team-oriented environment</li>
<li>High School Diploma or GED</li>
<li>Must be able to lift up to 45lbs (20.4kg)</li>
<li>Must be able to walk several miles per day (distance is dependent upon assigned area)</li>
</ul>
Must qualify for forklift license and operate forklift as required
