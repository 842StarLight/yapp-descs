<h2>E-Commerce Listing Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Are you a person who has an excellent eye for finding that diamond in the rough item that could be sold on an e-commerce platform? We’re launching our first ever e-commerce initiative and we need your skills! Come be a part of something worthwhile. When you work with Goodwill of North Georgia you’ll receive valuable training, experience and benefits.</p>
<p>
What we’re looking for:</p>
<p><b>
Requirements:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>A High School Diploma or equivalent.</li>
<li>Possess intermediate and proven knowledge with Microsoft Office</li>
<li>Possess effective interpersonal skills as well as oral and written communication skills</li>
<li>Detail oriented with the ability to work in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>Ability to work independently as well as in a team environment</li>
<li>Possess strong organizational skills and the ability to demonstrate sound judgement</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p><b>
</b></p>
<p><b>Preferences:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>E-commerce research and listing experience preferred</li>
<li>Prior military or non-profit experience</li>
<li>Bi-Lingual (Spanish)</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What you’ll be doing:</b></p>
<p>
As an E-Commerce Listing Specialist, you will be responsible for identifying and selecting donated products for sale online through the e-commerce platform. You’ll thoroughly examine product conditions, capture clear product photos and document product descriptions necessary to maximize e-commerce sales. You’ll also:</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Identify rare, collectible and/or unique items for sale through the e-commerce software</li>
<li>Examine product condition; test and clean products to ensure products are in working order and appealing to our customers</li>
<li>Photograph all products selected for sale online; ensure all photos are clear and/or visible for online listings</li>
<li>Prepare detailed written descriptions of products, identify and document manufacturer information, and other pertinent criteria to accurately describe products listed online</li>
<li>List all selected products and maintain product listing documentation as needed</li>
<li>Modify product listings as needed</li>
<li>Ensure a clean, organized work and storage area</li>
<li>Assist and shares knowledge and information with e-commerce team, management and other employees as needed</li>
<li>Comply with the Goodwill of North Georgia International Organization for Standardization process (ISO – Quality Management System) and the agency’s safety and security policies and procedures</li>
<li>Assist with shipping products as needed</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Working Environment Conditions/Requirements:<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Safety work shoes are required on 1st day of employment</li>
<li>We need you to be able to work in all types of conditions including heat, cold and dust.</li>
<li>Have the ability to meet all physical requirement to perform the duties of the position including the ability to stand and /or walk for extended periods of time, bend, reach, grasp, and tolerate environmental issues such as dust, heat, and cold.</li>
<li>Ability to work flexible hours, including weekends</li>
</ul>
