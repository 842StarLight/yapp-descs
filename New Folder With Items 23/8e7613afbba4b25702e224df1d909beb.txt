<b>Position Summary:</b> The marketing manager will be responsible for leading our talented marketing team in an effort to bolster revenue, increase attendance, and drive fan engagement. This position will work with other team executives to develop the strategic marketing plan for the team with an emphasis on social and digital media strategy, promotional events.</div>
<div><b>
</b></div>
<div><b>
Essential Functions:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Develop and implement annual integrated marketing plan – driving key marketing objectives and strategies</li>
<li>Work with other team executives to establish overall annual creative campaign while keeping club on-brand through all communications</li>
<li>Develop key marketing partnerships to help build brand awareness &amp; drive revenue</li>
<li>Work with executive management to create and manage marketing budget</li>
<li>Manage traditional, digital, and social marketing campaigns, with a focus on tracking and maximizing ROI</li>
<li>Responsible for digital and social media content including in-bound campaigns and content aimed at driving traffic, lead generation, online visibility, and user engagement</li>
<li>Ensure marketing obligations are met for corporate partners</li>
<li>Identify new technologies that help reach organizations’ overall business objectives</li>
<li>Oversee grass-roots initiatives throughout the year</li>
<li>Oversee email, marketing automation, and database marketing to drive lead generation and ticket sales with measurable ROI.</li>
<li>Generate ideas and implement activations to grow the teams’ database and email distribution list.</li>
<li>Conduct digital market research surveys to help understand fan behaviors</li>
<li>Use appropriate research platforms to provide data on purchasing behaviors, media usage across platforms, and lifestyle trends in the local market</li>
<li>Provide weekly/monthly analytics reports on digital and social media buy performance and ROI</li>
<li>Support all other departments through appropriate marketing channels and platforms</li>
<li>Assist executive management with additional projects as assigned</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>
Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Advertising, Marketing, or related field</li>
<li>Minimum two (2) years full-time directly related work experience</li>
<li>Experience in sports industry preferred</li>
<li>Knowledgeable in digital and social advertising</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Office</li>
<li>Experience with Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator required</li>
<li>Excellent problem-solving ability</li>
<li>Ability to work effectively on an individual basis and within a team framework</li>
<li>Ability to handle multiple projects, meet deadlines and achieve objectives</li>
<li>Strong planning and organizational skills</li>
<li>Strong communications skills, both verbal and written</li>
<li>Superior attention to detail</li>
<li>Ability to maintain a professional business image</li>
</ul>
