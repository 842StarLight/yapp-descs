<h2>Dispatcher</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Papillion Sanitation, a Waste Connections company, has an immediate opening for a Dispatcher to join our team at location in Omaha, NE.
<br><br>
Hours are 8:00 am to 5:00 pm and every 3rd Saturday
<br>15.50 per hour
<br><br>
<b>DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b><br>
Work in conjunction with other departments to resolve customer disputes &amp; demonstrate the ability to become proficient in the use of proprietary operational systems.
<br>Analyzing and resolving customer inquiries for missed pick-ups, misplaced containers and property damage.
<br>Assisting driver(s) when a pick up or delivery is in question before servicing.
<br>Scheduling and dispatching work to various employees and departments throughout the day.
<br>Demonstrate effective use of software applications, at a minimum MS Word and Excel proficient.
<br>Deliver services to customers in a manner that promotes goodwill. Interacts with customers daily.
<br>Work in a fast paced, cooperative, high call volume environment. Solicit sale of new or additional services.
<br>Must have excellent attention to Detail.
<br><br>
<b>MINIMUM JOB REQUIREMENTS:</b><br>
High school diploma or equivalent is a must.
<br>2 years of Dispatcher experience in a transportation or logistics atmosphere preferred.
<br>Strong typing skills
<br>Must have working knowledge of MS Office Suite (Excel, Word, Outlook, Etc.
<br>Excellent organization and communication skills, both orally and written.
<br><br>
<b>We offer excellent benefits including:</b> medical, dental, vision, flexible spending account, long term &amp; short term disability, life insurance, 401K retirement and unlimited opportunities to "Connect with Your Future".
