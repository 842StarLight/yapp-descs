<h2>Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Requisition Number: </b><b>74403</b></p>
<p>
As Project Manager for Insight, you will enter a high-energy and tech savvy world. We are looking for someone who is a team player, an excellent communicator and has client engagement experience. If you’re a strong leader who is self-starting, a great problem-solver, and motivated, then you could be perfect fit!</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
 As a Project Manager, you can expect to:</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
<b>
Multi-task </b><b>–</b> You will develop and implement project schedules to meet deadlines, all while ensuring your project maintains profitability. Plus you will engage in ongoing client meetings to ensure client satisfaction.</li>
<li>
<b>
Take Ownership</b> – You will manage, direct and oversee all aspects of your projects.</li>
<li>
<b>
Be a Leader</b> – You will motivate your team to find successful solutions.</li>
<li>
<b>
Be Challenged</b> – Your job is never done as a Project Manager. You will be presenting Insight to prospective clients, managing your current clients, and assisting the sales team with business development activities; all in a single day’s work.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
What we are looking for:</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Combination of 5-8 years of education and IT services experience.</li>
<li>
Experience in specific technology areas including networking, storage, infrastructure lifecycle services, enterprise systems. and application development projects</li>
<li>
Project Management Professional (PMI) certification is highly preferred within 6 months of placement in Project Manager role.</li>
<li>
Proficient in Microsoft tools including, but not limited to, Microsoft Office, MS Project, MS Project Server, SharePoint.</li>
</ul>
