<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Responsibilities</b></h2>
<div>
In this role, you will help to significantly grow one of the largest businesses at Microsoft by developing and executing marketing strategies, programs, and campaigns to grow our Compliance business. Compliance is a key investment area for Microsoft 365, as it is top of mind for customers today. This role is an amazing opportunity to grow your network as it requires close collaboration with many teams across Microsoft!</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Key responsibilities include:</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Marketing planning</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Leveraging worldwide go-to-market guidance, you will develop a US marketing plan for Microsoft 365 Compliance, incorporating key Marketing moments and feature releases.</li>
<li>
You will support US scorecard metric owners building plans to achieve targets. You will help to identify gaps in performance and collaborate to help develop corrective actions</li>
<li>
Provide insights to leadership team during regular business reviews</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Program/ campaign execution</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
You will drive demand in the US for Microsoft 365 E5 leveraging the full menu of global programs and content</li>
<li>
Collaborate across Microsoft to adapt and land central marketing programs, plans, and activities to drive demand and customer acquisition</li>
<li>
Lead a virtual team to orchestrate the rollout of products, features and offers with internal sales teams, partners and customers across MSUS</li>
<li>
Act as the voice of the field and customer</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Product Evangelism</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Be the product expert – be able to deliver the value proposition and compelling demo</li>
<li>
Be the product advocate - evangelize products at marketing events, webcasts, campaigns, and sales engagements</li>
<li>
Be the product trainer - plan field and partner readiness</li>
<li>
Be the product voice - provide the engineering teams with product feedback to remove sales blockers and drive customer value</li>
</ul>
<div>
To be successful in this role, you will develop and maintain a strong connection with worldwide teams, counterparts in other subsidiaries, and all your key US stakeholders to develop and share insights and best practices on growing the M365 business.</div>
