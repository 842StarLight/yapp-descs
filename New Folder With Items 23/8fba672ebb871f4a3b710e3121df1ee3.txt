<h2>Digital Account Manager</h2>

<p>Here is your chance to work at Restore Hyper Wellness, one of the premier startups in Austin.</p>
<p>
We coined the phrase Hyper Wellness because it exemplifies our approach: offer cutting edge services that have shown results, we strive to make our customers feel better every time they visit, and hyper stimulate the body's natural defense mechanisms. For us, Hyper Wellness is a lifestyle that we believe improves the lives of our customers.</p>
<p>
Restore's unique retail locations are the antithesis of a traditional doctor's office. We are efficient, fun and social. We educate and provide information in a completely transparent manner so that you can manage your wellness better. Every day, we help thousands of customers achieve their goals around sports performance, pain management, recovery, beauty and overall wellness.</p>
<p>
We were awarded the Employee Health &amp; Wellness Award by the Austin Chamber of Commerce for the 2019 Greater Austin Business Awards!</p>
<p>
We've also made the Inc. 5000 list of fastest-growing companies in the United States! Coming in at #113 overall, #11 in Texas business and #1 in the Texas health industry! We've got a bright and busy future ahead of us!</p>
<p>
The Digital Account manager is a critical role in our in-house agency - this person works closely with the Franchise Launch Manager, Performance Specialist and Director of Digital Marketing to develop strong relationships and clear goals with our Restore franchise clients. We treat Restore franchise businesses as if they are our own, constantly reevaluating, tweaking and optimizing to get the best possible results for our franchise locations.</p>
<p>
The right candidate is quick to learn and quick to problem-solve. Digital marketing can be a moving target, and our best candidate will be agile and flexible to adjust to the changes. The candidate will be committed to building campaigns that give results and developing reports that detail the success of those campaigns. We are looking for someone who is equally creative as they are technical.</p>
<p>
