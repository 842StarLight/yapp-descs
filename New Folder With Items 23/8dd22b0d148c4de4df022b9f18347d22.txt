Collins Aerospace is a pioneer in the design, production and support of innovative solutions for our customers in aerospace and defense. Our expertise in flight deck avionics, cabin electronics, mission communications, information management, and simulation and training is strengthened by our global service and support network that spans 27 countries. Working together, our global team of 20,000 employees shares a vision to create the most trusted source of communication and aviation electronics solutions, applying both insight and foresight to help our customers succeed.
<br><br>
This position is responsible for identifying, analyzing, evaluating, recommending, pursuing and obtaining business opportunities that are consistent with the goals and objectives of the Army Aviation Avionics portfolio within the Collins Aerospace Avionics business unit. This position requires interfacing with Government customers at Army installations and potentially other locations. It also requires interfacing with and cultivating relationships with industry partners in a variety of locations. The primary focus is to grow the Collins Aerospace Army Aviation business with the US Army, with specific focus on the Future Vertical Lift program. This position also includes developing an understanding of the customer's most important requirements and operational needs, and then developing a coordinated strategy that leverages Collins Aerospace avionics products, systems, support services, expertise and customer affinity from across the Collins Aerospace enterprise. This position requires the ability to work with minimal supervision. This position will be located in Huntsville Alabama.
<br><br>
Specific Duties and Responsibilities
<br><br>
Develop and execute plans to capture avionics and mission system integration business on Army Aviation platforms with specific focus on the Future Vertical Lift program
<br>Primary face to the customer that establishes and maintains customer business relationships
<br>Grow Army Aviation business as measured by increased orders and sales
<br>Develop and implement strategies to expand existing and/or win new business
<br>Cultivate customer affinity/insight; build/execute contact plans; coordinate customer visits
<br>Cultivate relationships with industry partners; recommend acquisitions and alliances (teaming)
<br>Prepare and deliver Collins Aerospace capability briefings
<br>Identify market trends and opportunities; assess the impact on portfolio objectives and plans
<br>Shape requirements; identify the customer's most important requirements
<br>Lead Pursuit and Order Capture (POC) activities IAW Collins Aerospace processes
<br>Lead competitive assessments; build knowledge base of competitor strengths and weaknesses
<br>Lead development of price-to-win targets for competitive pursuits
<br>Conduct internal gate (Decision Point) reviews
<br>Track and document customer account activities in designated information systems
<br>Maintain accurate, timely orders forecast within a rolling 18 month window
<br>Assist development of the Strategic Financial Plan and perform assigned actions
<br>Support achievement of Annual Operating Plan objectives, goals, and priorities
<br>Manage activities within allocated budget and resources
<br>Support Marketing development of business plans, marketing material, and trade shows

Be a US citizen and capable of obtaining US DOD security clearance; having an existing Secret or higher Security Clearance is highly desired
