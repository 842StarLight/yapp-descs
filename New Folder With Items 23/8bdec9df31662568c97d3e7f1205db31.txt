<h2>Self Storage Property Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Self Storage Consulting Group (SSCG) is the leading provider of self-storage third party management. We have an exciting opportunity for a full time Property Manager at our soon to open self storage property in Brighton, CO.</p>
<p>The ideal candidate will be an experienced and outgoing self-starter with a drive to meet company goals and their own personal goals. As Property Manager, you will be responsible for achieving and exceeding sales goals and providing customers with exceptional customer service in a clean, well maintained, and secure storage facility. We are passionate about training our employees and cultivating a culture of growth coupled with a focus on life/work balance.</p>
<p><b>Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provide exceptional sales and service to our new and existing customers</li>
<li>Ensure a neat, clean, safe and secure facility at all times</li>
<li>Recommend appropriate unit sizes and services based on individual customer’s needs</li>
<li>Understand and explain the rental process to customers</li>
<li>Maintain complete and accurate tenant files</li>
<li>Accurately manage cash and balance receipts, perform collections, post payments and process daily bank deposits</li>
<li>Conduct daily visual inspections of entire property</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of 2 years management experience - Experience in the self-storage industry a plus!</li>
<li>Strong work ethic with exceptional decision making skills</li>
<li>Able to work independently and with minimal supervision</li>
<li>Willing to work in all outdoor conditions/elements</li>
<li>Excellent customer service, sales and marketing skills</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal, written and oral communication skills</li>
<li>Excellent organizational skills</li>
<li>Basic computer skills</li>
<li>Ability to learn and navigate new software quickly</li>
<li>Valid driver’s license, automobile insurance, and reliable transportation</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Schedule</b></p>
<p>Monday – Saturday, 9:00am to 6:00pm, with a mid-week day off. Closed Sunday.</p>

<p>· Monthly bonus plan</p>
<p>· Dental and Vision Insurance</p>
<p>· Two (2) weeks PTO</p>
<p>· Paid Holidays</p>
<p>· Free use of a 10 x 10 storage unit after 90 days of employment</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Salary: $15.00 to $16.00 /hour</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul>
<li>Management: 2 years (Required)</li>
<li>Customer Service: 2 years (Required)</li>
</ul>
