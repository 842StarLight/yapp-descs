<h2>Salaried Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>POSITION SUMMARY:</b> This position is under the direct supervision of the General Manager and/or Assistant General Manager and provides restaurant leadership in accordance with established company standards.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND TASKS:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Assists in managing Restaurant Environment during scheduled shifts with discretionary management decision making required:<ul>
<li>
Ensures prompt, friendly service according to company guidelines</li>
<li>
Builds a culture using the Vision / Mission statement to guide People, Profits, and Guest Experience</li>
<li>
Ensures the immediate response and correction of all verbal guest complaints</li>
<li>
Ensures a high-quality guest experience by employing tools such as table touching and proactivity in resolving potential guest issues</li>
<li>
Teaches and enforces alcohol awareness</li>
<li>
Assist in maintaining adequate inventory levels</li>
<li>
Ensures product preparation and presentation uncompromisingly meeting company standards</li>
<li>
Effectively oversees/schedules associates to meet sales demands</li>
<li>
Maintains effective safety and security programs according to company policy and government standards</li>
<li>
Promotes and manages restaurant organization, cleanliness and sanitation</li>
<li>
Assist in executing all incentive contests and educates associates on promotions and new menu offerings</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Assists in managing Team Leaders and Team Member performance</li>
<li>
Assists in maintaining Team Member and administrative functions as related to a manager role</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
Effectively assists in managing Front of the House and Back of House operations during assigned shifts</li>
<li>
Responsible for all opening and closing procedures of the restaurant during assigned shifts</li>
<li>
Assists in managing standards and commitment to quality</li>
<li>
Assists in managing daily and weekly reports</li>
<li>
Fosters teamwork by building effective teams committed to restaurant and organizational goals</li>
<li>
Operates with a high degree of Professionalism, Ethics and Integrity, maintaining a role-model image</li>
<li>
Development of Team Leads, and Team Members for growth within the company</li>
<li>
Performs other related duties as required and assigned</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
EQUIPMENT/ COMPUTER SKILLS:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Computers</li>
<li>
Phones</li>
<li>
Scanners</li>
<li>
Photocopiers</li>
<li>
filing cabinets</li>
<li>
fax machines</li>
<li>
Microsoft Suite</li>
<li>
Applebee’s systems and software</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
SUPERVISORY RESPONSIBILITY: </b>Back of House and Front of House Team Leads and Team Members</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
MINIMUM QUALIFICATIONS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
High School Degree or equivalent education and/or experience;</li>
<li>
One to Two (1-2) years of guest service experience;</li>
<li>
One to Two of prior supervisory experience, preferably in a casual dining setting</li>
<li>
Demonstrated progression towards growing sales and managing a Profit &amp; Loss Statement</li>
<li>
Positive attitude and the ability to handle highly difficult guest interactions;</li>
<li>
21+ years old</li>
<li>
ServSafe Alcohol and Food Certification a plus</li>
<li>
Reading, writing, basic math and verbal communication skills in English is required</li>
<li>
Bi-lingual experience a plus</li>
<li>
Ability to pass company background check</li>
<li>
Reliable transportation and government issued driver’s license required</li>
<li>
Ability to work 50-55 hour work weeks and flexible schedule consisting of mornings, mid-day shifts, evenings, weekends and holidays</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
KEY COMPENTENCIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Passion for hospitality</li>
<li>
Highly trusting</li>
<li>
Organized</li>
<li>
Detail oriented</li>
<li>
Demonstrates care and concern</li>
<li>
Developer of others</li>
<li>
Goal orientated</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
PHYSICAL DEMANDS:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Mobility required during the entire shift</li>
<li>
Ability to stand and exert fast-paced mobility for periods of up to four (4) hours in length</li>
<li>
Must have a good sense of balance, be able to reach, bend, stoop, and kneel and frequently lift as much as 50 pounds, several times over</li>
<li>
Must be able to speak clearly and listen attentively to guests and other employees</li>
</ul>
