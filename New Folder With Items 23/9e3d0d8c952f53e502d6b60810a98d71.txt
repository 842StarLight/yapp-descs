<h2>TOC - Help Desk Supervisor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>SYNQ3 is the restaurant industry’s leading technology and automation-solutions provider. SYNQ3’s technologies enable restaurants to enhance order-processing, provide better customer service for to-go and catering orders, and increase sales and profits. As the globally recognized leader in restaurant technology, SYNQ3 services numerous top brands with thousands of restaurants, such as Red Lobster, Red Robin Gourmet Burgers and Brews, Chipotle Mexican Grill, Noodles &amp; Company, and P.F. Chang’s China Bistro, to name a few.</p>
<p><b>
Job Purpose:</b></p>
<p>
The Tactical Operations Center Supervisor will be responsible for managing the Tier 1 and Tier 2 Help Desk teams to achieve the SYNQ3 objectives and behavioral standards, in-line with the company objectives, policies and guidelines. The Supervisor will be responsible for the growth and development of assigned team members.</p>
<p><b>
Job Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Coach, develop and mentor the Help Desk team to meet required objectives</li>
<li>Maintain a thorough understanding of the technical and troubleshooting process to assist with inquiries and escalated calls</li>
<li>Ensure that Synq3 is represented at the highest level of professionalism when communicating with internal and external customers</li>
<li>Provide feedback and suggestions on specific needs for continual improvement to immediate manager</li>
<li>Make supervisory decisions in line with company policies, procedures and other relevant guidelines to ensure consistency</li>
<li>Serve as an escalation point and incident manager for Severity 1 tickets</li>
<li>Create and manage schedules for the Help Desk team</li>
<li>Complete Manager Appraisals as required to make recommendations for internal applications</li>
<li>Conduct Monthly Performance Reviews to maximize performance</li>
<li>Monitor Analysts and review completed tickets as a coaching tool to ensure quality work</li>
<li>Coach by identifying performance gaps and developing and implementing action plans to assist team members in meeting or exceeding requirements</li>
<li>Conduct team meetings on a regular basis to develop and maintain effective team spirit and update agents with relevant company information</li>
<li>Address Specialists performance or behavioral issues in line with the company policy to ensure Specialists are meeting requirements</li>
<li>Special projects as required</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>2+ years of experience in an IT Help Desk or technical Customer Service Call Center environment preferred.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>1-2 years previous Supervisory experience preferred</li>
<li>Experience coaching, developing and mentoring team members preferred</li>
<li>Strong verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Strong Conflict Resolution and Problem-Solving Skills</li>
<li>Experience with Microsoft Office software applications</li>
<li>Ability to assist with troubleshooting</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
