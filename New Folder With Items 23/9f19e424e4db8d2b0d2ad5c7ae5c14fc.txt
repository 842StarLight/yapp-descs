<h2>Associate Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>VinSolutions - Associate Project Manager
</p>
<p>**Please note this position is not remote and is located in Mission, KS**</p>
<p>
Responsible for project implementation meetings, calls with clients and maintaining software implementation timeline. Serve as the primary point of contact for the client and internal stakeholders. Manage project timelines, dependencies and integration points. Distribute timely project status reports and ensure timely escalation of issues or potential high-impacting risks. Coordinate client data entry projects and schedule clients team training on how to effectively use software. Work with sales, development and client teams to provide dynamic customer solutions and implementation strategies.</p>
<p>Specific responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Manage Implementation and training timelines</li>
<li>Working with dealers to get data and information in a timely and efficient manner</li>
<li>Construct and communicate weekly status reports for the overall project status to the dealer stakeholders; managers, performance managers and training department.</li>
<li>Manage risks and issues</li>
<li>Managing and ensuring customer expectations are met through the implementation and training cycle</li>
<li>Act as a liaison between other departments (example: communicating with Sales regarding missed products, communicating to Performance Managers on the overall feeling of the Dealership, and handling the Final Training Recaps to the Trainers arriving on-site)</li>
</ul>
<br>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications:</b></h4>
<div>
Required Qualifications:</div>
<ul>
<li>3 + years experience in a customer facing, client centric position with proven track record of managing projects and consistently meeting deadlines. Prefer project manager background or experience leading software implementation projects, but not required.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to multi-task and prioritize effectively.</li>
<li>Self-directed</li>
<li>Strong knowledge of good customer service practices and project management</li>
<li>Customer oriented and service minded.</li>
<li>Capable of understanding and overcoming different cultural and language obstacles to provide solutions that satisfy corporate, regional and local objectives</li>
<li>Time management and troubleshooting skills</li>
<li>Ability to communicate both written and orally, to all levels of management and across different cultural backgrounds</li>
<li>Ability to identify problems and initiate corrective actions and preventative actions</li>
<br>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div>Required Education:</div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree or equivalent combination of education and work experience</li>
<br>
</ul>
