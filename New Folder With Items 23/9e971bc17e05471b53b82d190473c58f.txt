<p>InVerve Marketing is a Lansing marketing firm that specializes in web design and development, inbound marketing, and graphic design. Through inbound marketing software and innovative, creative marketing strategies – we ensure our clients get found on the web. We are a smart and slightly nerdy bunch of creative professionals that love to learn and grow.</p>
<p>With InVerve Marketing you will be joining a team where our values are front and center. We strive for these values in ourselves and in our culture:</p>
<p>· Smart</p>
<p>· High Emotional IQ</p>
<p>· Continuous Improvement</p>
<p>· Fidelity</p>

<p>InVerve utilizes EOS (Entrepreneurial Operating System) and Teamwork project management software. We use the agile marketing approach with clients and are a Gold Certified HubSpot partner. Our shop is equipped with MAC and PC computers and uses Adobe Creative Cloud.</p>
<p>As a content specialist you will work with design, digital, and web account managers to produce the written content for a variety of projects. You will attend meetings to collaborate on new projects including campaign concept development, traditional and digital marketing projects and campaigns, events, website content, social media, proposal responses, email, and other deliverables. You will be part of work sessions throughout the production process from whiteboarding ideas to final client presentations. Once the project is assigned and developed, you own the content creation deliverable, timelines and details through to completion.</p>
<p>Your day could include:</p>
<p>· Writing and managing all aspects of social media projects</p>
<p>· Reviewing or assisting in developing service proposals</p>
<p>· Meeting with management teams to determine need</p>
<p>· Facilitating creative meetings as needed with team members to ensure content is tracking in both audience and accuracy</p>
<p>· Participate in brainstorming sessions with creative teams</p>
<p>· Account accurately for time used against time allotted to complete a task</p>
<p>· Oversee your own daily tasks tied to creative production’s timelines</p>
<p>· Troubleshooting issues that could slow down project processes relating to content</p>
<p>· Providing content that is in line with audience and persona profiles</p>
<p>· Setting and working towards quarterly objectives</p>
<p>· Training and development of content and technological skills</p>
<p>· Hands-on work on a project</p>
