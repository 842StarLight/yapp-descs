<h2>Marketing Specialist, Part Time</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<b>About us:</b> Commonwealth Pain and Spine (CP&amp;S) is one of the fastest growing, progressive, comprehensive pain management groups in Kentucky and Southern Indiana. Our team of friendly physicians are all board certified in pain medicine and anesthesiology and take the time to educate our staff. Our facilities are brand new with the latest technology. We have a team/family-oriented culture with great hours, competitive pay, benefits, and open interaction between our staff and management.</div>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>Summary: </b>This position calls on health care offices in an assigned territory and communicates our company guarantee and plays a vital role in fostering the Company’s reputation and image. This position is responsible for increasing company referrals in a highly professional, ethical and knowledgeable manner. Position will report to Marketing Coordinator.</div>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>Assigned Territory: </b>New Albany, IN, to Bowling Green, KY, and the surrounding areas</div>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>Hours: </b>Part-Time 24 hours, 3 days a week</div>
<div>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Builds and maintains referral relationships by nurturing referral leads and provide a point of contact and authority for referrals by personally visiting offices.</li>
<li>Manage marketing calendar to inform Marketing Coordinator and executive staff of marketing plan, strategy and ensure execution is maintained.</li>
<li>Achieves or exceeds referral number objectives within assigned territory.</li>
<li>Submits complete reports on-time in marketing software.</li>
<li>Review territorial reports and maintains frequent correspondence with Marketing Coordinator.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Knowledge of modern office equipment and procedures.</li>
<li>Strong verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Interpersonal/human relations skills</li>
<li>Organizational/planning skills</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task</li>
<li>Ability to type and operate word processing programs</li>
<li>Ability to prioritize duties, delegate responsibilities, and evaluate the work of others</li>
<li>Ability to maintain confidentiality and professionalism</li>
<li>Ability to work efficiently and cooperatively</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Minimum Qualifications, Education, and Experience equivalent to:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma required, college degree preferred.</li>
<li>1+ years’ experience with computer software, required.</li>
<li>1+ years’ experience in sales or marketing role a plus.</li>
<li>Manage stress well, with ability to multi task and ensure projects are running on time.</li>
<li>Very consistent, pleasant, cheerful personality that offices and co-workers will warm up to immediately.</li>
<li>Must be incredibly punctual. Great attendance, required</li>
<li>Active, valid Driver’s License and state minimum coverage- vehicle insurance, required at all times</li>
<li>Must be able to pass state(s) driving history/motor vehicle check, required upon initial hire and may be re-checked annually, or for suspicion or cause</li>
</ul>
