<h2>Accounting Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Job Title</b><b>:</b> Store Accounting Coordinator <b>FLSA</b>: Non-Exempt<br>
<br>
<b>Department:</b> Grocery <b>Revision Date</b>: 11/2013<br>
<br>
<b>General Function</b>:<br>
<br>
Provides prompt, efficient and friendly customer service. Responsible for store accounting which includes posting sales, scans checks for back office conversion, cash accountability (completes deposit and balances system), accounts/expense payable, accounts receivable, and some inventory procedures.<br>
<br>
<b>Reporting Relations:</b><br>
<br>
Accountable and Reports to: Store Director; Assistant Store Director; Managers GM, Perishables, and Store Operations; Lead Store Accounting Coordinator<br>
<br>
Direct Reports: None<br>
<br>
<b>Primary Duties and Responsibilities</b>:<br>
<br>
1. Provides prompt, efficient and friendly customer service by exhibiting caring, concern and patience in all customer interactions and treating customers as the most important people in the store.<br>
<br>
2. Smiles and greets customers in a friendly manner, whether the encounter takes place in the employee's designated department or elsewhere in the store.<br>
<br>
3. Makes an effort to learn customers' names and to address them by name whenever possible.<br>
<br>
4. Assists customers by: (examples include)<br>
<br>
<ul><li>escorting them to the products they're looking for</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>securing products that are out of reach</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>loading or unloading heavy items</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>making note of and passing along customer suggestions or requests</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>performing other tasks in every way possible to enhance the shopping experience.</li></ul>
<br>
5. Answers the telephone promptly and provides friendly, helpful service to customers who call.<br>
<br>
6. Verifies registers, as needed.<br>
<br>
7. Counts the safe, gift cards, gathers Western Union and other utility reports and verifies with prior day sales with their computer software.<br>
<br>
8. Print necessary reports from each store locations servers.<br>
<br>
9. Scans checks and balances cash and checks, prepares deposit.<br>
<br>
10. Prepares and reviews check out and cash accountability report.<br>
<br>
11. Distributes sales and prints sales ledger.<br>
<br>
12. Evaluates money needs on a daily basis and keeps appropriate amounts in the safe.<br>
<br>
13. Reconciles invoices, enters into system, and sends to corporate.<br>
<br>
14. Prepares unpaid invoice report; reconciles Electronic Funds Transfer (EFT) and store accounts receivable with corporate on a monthly basis. Completes Third Party reconciliation for pharmacy as needed.<br>
<br>
15. Reconciles FTD/Teleflora items monthly, and posts to ledger.<br>
<br>
16. Maintains strict adherence to department and company guidelines related to personal hygiene and dress.<br>
<br>
17. Adheres to company policies and individual store guidelines.<br>
<br>
18. Reports to work when scheduled and on time.<br>
<br>
<b>Secondary Duties and Responsibilities:</b><br>
<br>
1. Assists in balancing reconciliations monthly to corporate.<br>
<br>
2. Prepares payroll where necessary; pulls time-clock; edits punches, runs daily report of prior date hours; prepares new employees, terms, transfers and wage increase information. Sends to corporate payroll system and prepares checks for employee pick up.<br>
<br>
3. Collects bad checks when necessary.<br>
<br>
4. Calls customers with charges 60 days old or older to get payment.<br>
<br>
5. Prepares invoices held at store for corporate.<br>
<br>
6. Assists in other areas of store as needed.<br>
<br>
7. Performs other job related duties and special projects as required.<br>
<br>
<b>Education and Experience:</b><br>
<br>
High School or 6 months to 1 year of similar or related work experience<br>
<br>
<b>Physical Requirements:</b><br>
<br>
<ul><li>Must be able to physically perform medium work: exerting up to 50 pounds of force occasionally and 20 pounds of force frequently, and 10 pounds of force constantly to move objects.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Visual requirements include vision from less than 20 inches to more than 20 feet with or without correction, depth perception, color vision, and field of vision.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Must be able to perform the following physical activities: Climbing, balancing, stooping, kneeling, reaching, standing, walking, pulling, lifting, grasping, feeling, talking, hearing, and repetitive motions.</li></ul>
<br>

<b>
Working Conditions</b>:<br>
<br>
This position is continually exposed to money for reconciliation purposes. There is occasional pressure to meet deadlines.<br>
<br>
<b>Equipment Used to Perform Job:</b><br>
<br>
Calculator, computer, telephone, cash register, intercom, and fax machines.<br>
<br>
<b>Financial Responsibility</b>:<br>
<br>
Responsible for all money and the purchasing of money from the bank.<br>
<br>
<b>Contacts:</b><br>
<br>
Has daily contact with customers, suppliers/vendors, and the general public.<br>
<br>
<b>Confidentiality</b>:<br>
<br>
Has access to confidential information including wages, sales, safe code, and money.</div></div>
