<h2>Credit Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Job Description<br>
<br>
<p>Primarily responsible for developing credit relationships in assigned markets with customers, sales team and Service Center Leaders. Work closely with Director of Credit to ensure timely and accurate processing of credit applications. In addition, supports the timely collection of account receivables by working closely with customers and Summit’s sales teams educating each on the importance of timely payments. Drive increased sales by analyzing credit decisions and identifying up sale credit opportunities. Develop new lines of business working closely with the service center team and proactively arranging credit lines in preparation for sales opportunities. Exceptional service to external and internal customers is critical to the success of a Credit Specialist.</p>
<p><b>
Principle Accountabilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Monitor key metrics including DSO and Bad Debt and surpass goals set by department and Director of Credit.</li>
<li>
Collect from receivable accounts in a timely manner.</li>
<li>
Timely interaction with customers to establish/update credit ratings and update account information. Analyze to mitigate risk.</li>
<li>
Research and resolve credit issues with customers as needed and verify transactions.</li>
<li>
Assist Sales, Operations Managers, Accounts Payable, Finance, and other Summit departments as needed with credit issues and when additional assistance is needed by customers.</li>
<li>
Set up and track job accounts.</li>
<li>
Prepare lien notices per state statutes and create lien releases as necessary.</li>
<li>
Prepare for annual and other audits as necessary.</li>
</ul>
</div>
