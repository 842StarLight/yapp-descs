<h2>Wireless/IoT Sales Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Because you belong at Twilio
<br>The Who, What, Where and Why
<br><br>
Twilio is looking for an Enterprise Account Executive to sell into high value prospects and customers. The candidate will be responsible for sourcing new opportunities. Responsibilities will span from driving the sales process from internal leads to qualifying new prospects and managing deals to closure.
<br><br>
Who?
<br><br>
Twilio is looking for a Wireless Account Executive for our growing Global Sales Team. The right person for this role is a go-getter, within the IoT/Wireless space that comes with a strong book of business. You'll be working with enterprise customers and some of the world's hottest startups to the largest enterprise companies. As a Wireless Sales Specialist, you will selling connectivity to C-Level, software engineers, device manufacturers, and product managers. You enjoy talking to not only engineers and product managers but also build new relationships with senior business owners and decision makers (CIOs, CTOs, VPs of Product / Chief Product Officers, Business Unit owners) about building their business on Twilio and managing the overall commercial relationship with these customers (including white space identification, contract negotiations, etc).
<br><br>
What?
<br><br>
A great day for you would include talking with potential customers, where you spend time learning about their needs, and then through consultation, develop a strategic plan to help their service thrive through the use of Twilio's IoT connectivity &amp; communication platform. At the same time, you develop a new use case for a long-time customer that will bring their product to a new level, and create new building blocks for future customers. You understand the IoT connectivity/wireless business model, enjoy selling to a technical audience and can build trust externally with partners, but also internally with the solution engineering, product management, and customer success resources that support your territory.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Achieve quarterly revenue and new reporting partner targets</li>
<li>Position and match IoT Connectivity &amp; Platform value to organizations looking to take the journey from non-connected to connected products.</li>
<li>Develop and execute an outbound plan to drive sales in the North America region</li>
<li>Lead qualification for all leads and sales opportunities</li>
<li>Align with internal Solution Engineering, Use Case Sales, Product Management, and Customer Success resources to support the territory</li>
</ul>
<b>Account planning and execution skills:</b><br>
<ul>

<li>Strong technical aptitude and an ability to thrive in IoT connectivity sales</li>
<li>A solution-oriented approach to selling, ensuring your customers receive value from the products they buy from you</li>
<li>Understanding of the IoT connectivity business model, enjoy selling to a technical audience and building mutual trust</li>
<li>At least 12 years of overall technology sales experience</li>
<li>5 years of solution sales experience specific to IoT</li>
<li>Proven track record of achieving quota in technology sales</li>
<li>Disciplined and demonstrates excellent time management skills</li>
<li>World-class interpersonal and communication skills - you have the ability to effortlessly make complex contractual, technical, and financial details sounds simple</li>
<li>Proven consultative sales solution skills, including the ability to articulate a clear, concise return on investment value statement</li>
<li>Ethical, hands-on, passionate, persistent, creative, easy to deal with, highly productive - you are a doer first</li>
<li>Bachelor's Degree</li>
</ul>
<b>Experience/Skills Desired:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Passion and commitment to driving results</li>
<li>Ability to maintain a high level of productivity, manage multiple competing priorities, and work effectively under the pressure of time constraints in a fast-paced, complex, collaborative, and team-oriented environment</li>

Twilio is a company that is empowering the world's developers with modern communication in order to build better applications. The Global Sales team plays an integral role in building out our customer base and bringing Twilio to developers, non-profits and enterprise to make an impact on their services. Twilio is truly unique; we are a company committed to your growth, your learning, your development and your entire employee experience.
