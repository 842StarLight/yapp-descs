<h2>Insurance Account Manager - Personal Lines</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>RSS Insurance </b>has an exciting and rewarding opportunity for a <b>Personal Lines Insurance Account Manager</b>! RSS is an independent insurance agency located in Chattanooga, TN. The successful candidate will be enthusiastic about providing professional and friendly help to prospects and clients and working in a team environment. The successful candidate will maintain a high level of client satisfaction, perform marketing and placement of accounts and achieve agency account retention goals through proactive account rounding, up-selling and providing extraordinary client service.
</p>

<li>Familiarity with risk assessment and risk management techniques preferred.</li>
<li>Experience and expertise in operating in electronic environment; excellent automation skills, including MIS, Outlook, Word, Excel, document management software, carrier proprietary systems, and phone systems.</li>
<li>Excellent time management, organizational and verbal and written communication skills - ability to work 8:30 to 5 Monday through Friday</li>
<li>High degree of self-motivation and self-direction
</li>
</ul>
