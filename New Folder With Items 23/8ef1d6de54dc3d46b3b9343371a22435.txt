<p>Are you a pro at selling software security products? Carbon Black is hiring a Civilian Federal Field Sales Representative who will be responsible for identifying, developing and closing new business and expanding market share with a focus on Civilian accounts. This is a career changing opportunity, a pivotal role in driving our company’s expansion in the region with lots of visibility. Think you’re up for the challenge?

</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop GTM demand creation strategy for your respective Civilian Territory
</li>
<li>Leverage account knowledge and market intelligence to drive actions that generate sales outcomes
</li>
<li>Develop and execute account strategy for civilian accounts and opportunities
</li>
<li>Exceed pipeline creation, booking and revenue quota targets
</li>
<li>Track, forecast, co-ordinate and report on sales opportunities
</li>
<li>Establish relationships with key decision makers, typically at the CIO and CSO level
</li>

</li>
<li>Qualify and understand prospect security priorities and provide compelling , differentiated association to Carbon Black solutions
</li>
<li>Co-ordinate local strategic marketing activities tied to the business's goals.
</li>
<li>Develop, Implement and Optimize Strategy for partnering with select Federal Systems Integrators
</li>
<li>Coordinate and Lead Key U.S. Federal program capture strategies to and through select U.S. Federal Systems Integrators in conjunction with Cb Federal team
</li>
<li>Fully conceptualize and evangelize Carbon Black value proposition to prospects, VAR, OEM, FSI, Security ecosystem and be the lead strategist for partnering to maximize customer success through the Federal channel
</li>
<li>Develop repeatable process to Recruit, Qualify, Validate, Grow and Retain key VAR contribution for Carbon Black Federal sales team
</li>
</ul>

<p><b>What you’ll bring
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>U.S. Citizenship
</li>
<li>Existing U.S. government clearance and or clear indication of eligibility for U.S. government clearance
</li>
<li>Reside in Washington, D.C Metro Area
</li>
<li>
<b>7-10 + years </b>of sales experience with significant experience selling software security solutions to Civilian agencies
</li>
<li>Demonstrable success aligning software security solutions to U.S. Federal Civilian Government Cyber mission imperatives
</li>
<li>Significant and relevant Cloud Service Provider SaaS solution sales to U.S. Government
</li>
<li>Knowledge of FedRAMP CSP program and associated controls and data governance
</li>
<li>Demonstrable success exceeding sales targets in past roles selling Cyber security software solutions to U.S. Civilian Agencies
</li>
<li>Demonstrable success communicating Cyber value proposition to Civilian CXO’s in a manner reflective of the target prospects KPI’s and compliance drivers
</li>
<li>Experience with DHS CDM program and past success capturing large Cyber wins through this program
</li>
<li>Expertise selling through Federal Systems Integrators
</li>
<li>Demonstrated history of exceeding booking and revenue targets
</li>
<li>Significant and proven experience developing relationships with senior executives
</li>
<li><i>BA/BS or equivalent combination of education and experience
