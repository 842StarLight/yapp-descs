<p><b>
Business Development Representative</b></p>
<p>
The Business Development Representative (BDR) interacts with those individuals most important to us – our customers! This is why the BDR must be an excellent communicator and become the voice of the dealership in our community.</p>
<p><b>
 Key Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Answers incoming sales calls; schedules and confirms daily appointments.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Promptly follows up on internet sales leads using the customer’s desired method of contact and sets appointments.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Conducts follow-up phone calls on lease retention, marketing campaigns, etc.</p>
</li>
<li>Acquires a complete knowledge of product information related to the makes and models of the Subaru brand.</li>
<li>
<p>Maintains excellent customer service scores.</p>
</li>
</ul>
