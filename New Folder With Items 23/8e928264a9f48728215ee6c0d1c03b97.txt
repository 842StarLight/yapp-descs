<div><b>Job Description
</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>Accenture Technology – Salesforce Team professionals demonstrate skills in Salesforce which is the leader in CRM solutions for customer relationship management software and cloud computing.
</div>
<div></div>
<div>The successful candidate will be able to design technical architecture solutions that span multiple platforms and include integration and authentication across systems; manage the development lifecycle to ensure the delivery of highly secure solutions that are optimized for performance and built to scale; articulate design considerations, trade-offs, benefits, and recommendations for a technical architecture. The Technical Architect will have support from the Global Delivery Group as well as from local support resources provided by the global team.
</div>
<div></div>
<div>We can hire for this role anywhere in the US, as long as you are located near an Accenture office, and willing and able to travel domestically approximately 80% of the time to meet client needs.
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Key Responsibilities</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Collaborate with Functional team to determine how best to implement solution (decisions around declarative vs programmatic vs AppExchange, etc).
</li>
<li>Escalation Point for technical issues.
</li>
<li>Solution components that are outside of the Salesforce platform:
<ul>
<li>Expert platform knowledge.
</li>
<li>Security.
</li>
<li>Integration (including a technology/not just) and data Migration.
</li>
<li>End to End Technical Solutions.
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Play TA role on projects: typically, part-time on more than 1 project at a time.
</li>
<li>People Manager have regular 1-on-1s, perform annual reviews, etc
</li>
<li>Sales Support: Help scope, solution, and estimate sales opportunities alongside a delivery lead. Assist in SOW creation, etc.
</li>
<li>Practice Development: Mentor resources, take on leadership roles to expand overall practice capabilities, etc.</li>
</ul>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<div><b>Basic Qualifications</b></div>
<ul>
<li>A minimum of 2 years of experience developing Salesforce customizations (Apex/VF), integrations, and developing and executing data migrations.
</li>
<li>A minimum of 2 years of experience creating the technical architecture for complex Salesforce implementations.
</li>
<li>A minimum of 5 years of experience in technology implementation for full lifecycle enterprise software projects.
</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree or equivalent (minimum 12 years) work experience. (If Associate’s Degree, must have minimum 6 years work experience).</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Preferred Skills
</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Strong background in Cloud Computing solutions – cloud computing strategy, systems design, implementation and management.
</li>
<li>Consulting experience with medium to large consulting firms focusing on cloud computing related consulting (storage, SaaS, multi-tenant computing, large scale computing environments, etc).
</li>
<li>Demonstrated knowledge of XML, SQL, HTTP/HTTPS, and EAI processes.
</li>
<li>Familiarity with multiple implementation standards and methodologies.
</li>
<li>Programming Languages – APEX, VisualForce, Familiarity with ANT and Eclipse tools for working with the IDE.
</li>
<li>Salesforce and other certifications, required.
</li>
<li>Very strong work ethic with a customer service focus (high intensity).
</li>
<li>15+ years of consulting experience preferred; executive level sales support experience in consulting/professional services.
</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in a related field preferred.
</li>
</ul>
