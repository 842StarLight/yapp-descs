<b>About the Role:</b><br>
We are looking for a Customer Success Manager to join our Customer Delivery and Support team.<br>
Customer Success Managers are primarily accountable for ensuring their customers are getting maximum value from our products and achieving their desired business outcomes working closely with our Technical Consulting, Support, and Sales teams. Customer Success Managers also actively champion the customers and their needs within our company to ensure we remain a customer-obsessed team.<br>
<br>
<b>Typical Customer Success Managers activities include:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Pro-active regular customer check-ins to ensure they are happy and being successful.</li>
<li>Acting as the reliable single-point-of-contact for their customers.</li>
<li>Creating and driving an Adoption Plan with new customers.</li>
<li>Ensuring any issues impacting the customer are resolved quickly and effectively.</li>
<li>Ensuring customers are aware of all our company resources.</li>
<li>Creating case-studies and blog posts about their customers.</li>
<li>Bonus Skills:</li>
<li>Experience working with the different types of software testing methods and methodologies.</li>
<li>Experience working in a customer facing pre-sales or post-sales role.</li>
<li>Experience with the entire range of products</li>
<br>
</ul>
