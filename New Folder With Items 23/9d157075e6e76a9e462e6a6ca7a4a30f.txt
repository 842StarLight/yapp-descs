<h2>Full Charge Bookkeeper/Office Manager</h2>

<p><b>Company Overview</b></p>
<p>
With over three decades as an industry leader in Massachusetts, Landscaping by J. Michael provides high quality landscape services to the Commercial and Residential market. Our vast experience allows us to take on each project with confidence and expertise.</p>
<p><b>
Job Summary</b></p>
<p>
At Landscaping by J. Michael, our success depends on our people, productivity and procedures, the Full Charge Bookkeeper/Office Manager is the supportive piece within each of these areas.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities and Duties</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Perform all A/R &amp; A/P functions; bi-weekly payroll via Paychex,</li>
<li>
Bank reconciliations, General Journal Entries, P/L Analysis and Insurance Audits.</li>
<li>
Provide office support to Sales, Production and GM; customer service and other administrative duties.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Qualifications and Skills</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
5 years proven administrative experience preferably in a construction or landscaping office.</li>
<li>
Superb written and verbal communication skills.</li>
<li>
Proficient in MS Office, with aptitude to learn new software and systems.</li>
<li>
A minimum of 5 years of current experience with QuickBooks.</li>
<li>
Ability to multitask, prioritize daily work flow and meet various seasonal deadlines.</li>
<li>
Comfortable handling confidential information.</li>
</ul>
