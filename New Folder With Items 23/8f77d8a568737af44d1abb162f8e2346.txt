<h2>Automotive Quick Service Advisor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>Quick Service Advisors are responsible for managing the Fast Lane Services such as oil changes and other maintenance services. Quick Service Advisors must communicate customers concerns to quick lane technicians and for recommending preventative maintenance service to customers based on their vehicle’s needs.</div>
<div>
</div>
<div><b>
POSITION REQUIREMENTS:</b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Minimum high school diploma or equivalent</div></li>
<li><div>
Experience preferred, but not required</div></li>
<li><div>
Ability to maintain a positive attitude and demeanor</div></li>
<li><div>
Valid Michigan driver’s license and an insurable driving record</div></li>
<li><div>
Ability to read, speak and write English in order fulfill job functions in an understandable way<b>
</b>
</div></li>
<li><div>Ability to operate a computer, tablet, and telephone system</div></li>
</ul>
<div><b>
ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Greet customers in a timely and friendly manner</div></li>
<li><div>
Perform four point walk around on the customer’s vehicle</div></li>
<li><div>
Schedule appointments using dealership software when necessary</div></li>
<li><div>
Utilize dealer management system for repair order creation, booking, and cashiering</div></li>
<li><div>
Actively listen to customers to determine the customer’s concerns and identify preventative maintenance needs</div></li>
<li><div>
Obtain and record accurate customer and vehicle data</div></li>
<li><div>
Advise customers on the care of their cars and the value of maintaining their vehicles in accordance with manufacturers’ specifications, using maintenance menus and the dealer management system</div></li>
<li><div>
Address manufacturer warranty and recall concerns in accordance with manufacturer policy and procedures</div></li>
<li><div>
Work proactively with the sales department when a customer indicates interest in obtaining a new or used vehicle</div></li>
<li><div>
Maintain Customer Satisfaction Index (SPSI) rating at least comparable to that of the manufacturer, zone, or branch average</div></li>
<li><div>
Maintain dealership-prescribed standard for “hours per customer repair order written”</div></li>
<li><div>
Ensure that all documents are accounted for and signed as required</div></li>
<li><div>
Maintain effective communication with technicians to understand vehicle and customer needs</div></li>
<li><div>
Accomplish the forecast that has been established by the Service Director</div></li>
<li><div>
Provide and communicate estimates for labor and parts to the customer in a timely fashion</div></li>
<li><div>
Establish “time promised” and communicate any deviations in a proactive manner</div></li>
<li><div>
Follow up with progress of repair and any changes</div></li>
<li><div>
Compare final invoice with original repair order</div></li>
<li><div>
Deliver vehicle to customer and answer any questions</div></li>
<li><div>
Achieve and maintain manufacturer certifications</div></li>
</ul>
<div><b>
OTHER DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Understand and follow federal, state, and local regulations such as those concerning the disposal of hazardous wastes, OSHA right-to-know, etc.</div></li>
<li><div>
Deliver vehicle to customer and answer any questions</div></li>
<li><div>
Follow up with customers after visits</div></li>
<li><div>
Maintain follow-up program on additional items found in need of repair or any other declined services</div></li>
<li><div>
Be responsible for quality control, to ensure that work is completed as requested and to reduce comebacks</div></li>
<li><div>
Relating positively and courteously with diverse groups of people (customers, visitors, and staff)</div></li>
<li><div>
Encourage a positive and upbeat team environment</div></li>
<li><div>
Assist co-worker’s customers when necessary to ensure an optimal customer experience</div></li>
<li><div>
All other duties as assigned</div></li>
</ul>
<div><b>
PHYSICAL AND ENVIRONMENTAL DEMANDS:</b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Fast-paced environment</div></li>
<li><div>
Ability to work in the service drive which may experience, but is not limited to conditions such as: varying temperatures, noise, dust, and exhaust fumes</div></li>
<li><div>
Ability to work weekends</div></li>
<li><div>
Ability to stand for 6-8 hours at a time</div></li>
<li><div>
Ability to lift 50 pounds</div></li>
</ul>
</div>
