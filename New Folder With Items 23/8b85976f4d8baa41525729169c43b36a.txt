<h2>Marketing Campaign Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>PrismHR is expanding their newly formed Demand Generation team and is looking for a talented marketing individual to help fuel their growth. PrismHR is searching for a Marketing Campaign Manager to create, develop, execute and optimize integrated campaigns. This position requires someone who is results-driven, inquisitive, strategic, flexible, and collaborative. There is a lot of freedom within the strategic creation for each program so being able to project manage the moving pieces to execution will be important.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
SPECIFIC DUTIES and RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>Research, develop, and execute integrated marketing campaigns that are delivered across different marketing channels</p></li>
<li><p>
Collaborate with internal stakeholders and work with the Field Marketing &amp; Events Manager (and at times with other Marketing Campaign Managers) to create segmented audiences and robust campaigns through a combination of efforts that help to deliver a solid prospective customer experience, and ultimately generate hand-raising interest</p></li>
<li><p>
Take on total ownership of campaigns within your designated market, from creative strategy to sales development follow-up</p></li>
<li><p>
Ensure accurate reporting on the effectiveness of each program and channel regarding results and associated costs</p></li>
<li><p>
Flexibility and desire to be a team player who is open to new ideas</p></li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>SKILLS and EXPERIENCE:</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>5-7+ years’ experience in marketing, ideally working within the software industry</p></li>
<li><p>
Extremely strong communication skills, especially business writing</p></li>
<li><p>
Ability to run Programs on time and on budget, with a proven performance of consistently meeting or exceeding marketing program goals</p></li>
<li><p>
Bring masterful execution of details and have a sense of urgency in completing tasks</p></li>
<li><p>
Ability to accurately segment your audience, create content for emails and any additional needs (campaign dependent)</p></li>
<li><p>
Ability to plan and execute on direct mail and help with events that tie into your campaign, and are able to tie into social and paid campaigns</p></li>
<li><p>
Ability to work with the Sales Development team and Sales team to articulate campaigns, audience segmentation, messaging, and follow-up activities to ensure results are carried to fruition</p></li>
<li><p>
Be a master of tactics; but, also enjoy creatively identifying opportunities to uplevel marketing activities, from innovative events and campaigns to high impact communications</p></li>
<li><p>
Proven experience to constantly juggle and prioritize a number of key tasks, collaborate with other members of the Marketing and Sales team, while always thinking and working towards the strategic goals of the business and team</p></li>
<li><p>
Experience with GoToWebinar, Salesforce and Marketo</p></li>
<li><p>
Knowledge or understanding of the Human Capital Management, Payroll Service Bureau, or HR Outsourcing Industry is a plus</p></li>
<li><p>
Bachelor’s Degree required</p></li>
</ul>
<p><b>PrismHR is an EO/AA/VEV/Disabled Employer</b><br>
<b>Diversity Candidates are encouraged to apply</b></p>
