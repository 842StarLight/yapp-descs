<p><b>About the Role</b></p>
<p>The engineering team at Enjoy builds the core technology that brings our product and the expert together. As a Partner Solutions Architect, you will be tasked with representing enjoy to our business and technology partners by architecting creative technology and operations solutions that allow our partners to meet their strategic objectives.</p>
<p>You will collaborate with your internal and external stakeholders to integrate the Enjoy platform within our partner's systems to achieve 5-star experiences for our customers. This includes but not limited to leveraging Enjoy API’s, data platform, point of sale, etc. You will foster strong relationships with our partners’ business, operations and engineering orgs, uniquely positioning you and your team to create integration solutions tailored to their specific needs. You will communicate with our partners on a daily basis to ensure alignment on internal timelines for successful launch, vision, strategy and tactical execution.</p>
<p>You will work closely with our product managers and engineers to build and optimize algorithms and systems. Additionally, you will collaborate with Field teams to run experiments and with the Data team to understand the results. Together with our business and experience teams, you will help create what success means for our Experts, partners, and customers.</p>
