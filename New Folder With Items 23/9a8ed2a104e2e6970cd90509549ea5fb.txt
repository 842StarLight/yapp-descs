<h2>Marketing Graphic Designer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Job Summary</b></p>
<p>A local healthcare company is seeking a Market Graphic Designer. The Marketing Graphic Designer is a vital part of our organization. This position will strategize and implement marketing, branding, and advertising plans throughout the year. Day to day, he or she may create or coordinate content, track results, coordinate social media campaigns, build relationships with sales teams, maintain promotional materials inventory, attend sales meetings, assist with trade shows or events and otherwise support all marketing strategies. This position assists with administration and communication to support our sales teams and customers in all Divisions. This individual stays informed about the market, industry trends, and the competitive landscape,.</p>
<p>Qualified individuals will be able to work well in a fast-paced, rapidly changing environment; self-motivated; flexible; extremely organized; very detail-oriented; confident and diplomatic in dealing with competing departmental objectives and varied personalities; a strong problem solver; able to work well under pressure in order to meet project deadlines; and will have a positive attitude.</p>
<p><b>Essential Responsibilities Include but Not Limited To</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Be the ultimate owner in making sure our site is up to date, reflecting new product details, merchandising strategies, and seasonal goals - we move quickly here and you’ll be in charge of making sure the site always adapts to our ever-evolving business needs</li>
<li>Developing and implementing short and long-term marketing strategies and advertising campaigns in order to create and increase brand awareness</li>
<li>Supporting marketing project development</li>
<li>Fostering and executing marketing and advertising campaigns</li>
<li>Managing on-going marketing and advertising campaigns and plans, while ensuring that every initiative is aimed at increasing brand awareness</li>
<li>Online marketing strategies through social media accounts</li>
<li>Giving ideas, recommendations, and plans to contribute to the optimization of marketing strategies</li>
<li>Scheduling promotional presentations, maintaining an annual project calendar, and ensuring deadlines are respected</li>
<li>Researching, analyzing and summarizing market trends</li>
<li>Studying competitors market behavior and strategies</li>
<li>Keeping up-to-date on industry activities and trends</li>
<li>Overseeing marketing material:</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Coordinating, editing, and optimizing any material concerning advertising, branding, and marketing</li>
<li>Ensuring that every material is consistent with planned marketing strategies</li>
<li>Monitoring and maintaining promotional materials inventory, when necessary.</li>
<li>Providing assistance to the Sales team in order to achieve the company’s goals:</li>
<li>Giving marketing advice and support for a better understanding of the brand’s voice and personality to determine the most profitable mean of distribution</li>
<li>Experience in Social Media Management, Review Management, Email Marketing, Branding, Creating Marketing Material, Website Updates and Blog, CRM/Lead Data Entry</li>
