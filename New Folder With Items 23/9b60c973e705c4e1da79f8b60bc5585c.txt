<h2>Channel Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Position Summary:</b></p>
<p>
A channel account manager is responsible for identifying, developing, and expanding relationships with distributors.
</p>
<p>This entry level inside sales role allows for a person to gain valuable sales experience from a leading games manufacturer and after achieving certain goals/tenure allows for career progression to outside sales. Duties of a channel account manager may include training, mentoring, and motivating channel partners in the assigned territory; generating additional demand through partner events; studying the market for newer trends and reporting the same to the management; monitoring pricing policies; making sure that channel partners sign proper contracts/agreements; managing channel conflicts; training or mentoring trainee channel account managers; and fostering relationships between channel partners and sellers.
</p>
<p>The person assists with all sales-related duties including coordinating sales team activities, providing clerical support, tracking reports, and managing leads. The Channel Account Manager reports to the SVP of Global Sales &amp; Business Development of LAIG and works directly with the LAI Games Sales Team. You will perform several duties focused on customer satisfaction and the management of sales documents and product availability.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
