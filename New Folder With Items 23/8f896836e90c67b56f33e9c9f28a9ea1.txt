<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Who We Need:</b></h2>
<p>Document Management Specialist duties include borrower document intake, scanning and parsing of loan documents, document analysis, and accurate data input into the loan system of record. These duties include ensuring a smooth handoff of quality work to loan partners inside the organization.</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
What You Will Do:</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Retrieval, intake, parsing, and scanning of loan documentation</li>
<li>Upload of loan documents into applicable areas within the Loan Origination System</li>
<li>Preparing, shipping and tracking allonges to notes for warehouse approval and shipping</li>
<li>Understand how the Document Management Specialist role fits into the larger organizational context and ensure the smooth handoff of work to loan partners</li>
<li>Process documents in an accurate and timely manner to ensure proper audit procedures are followed</li>
<li>Meet deadlines and quality standards</li>
<li>Effectively manage multiple demands and competing priorities</li>
<li>Develop effective professional partnerships with all colleagues</li>
<li>Ensures exceptional customer service by maintaining a thorough knowledge of lending programs, policies, procedures and regulatory requirements, demonstrating a commitment to professional ethics, complying with all Federal and State compliance policies and adhering to RESPA requirements</li>
<li>Ensure you meet daily, weekly and monthly internally specified requirements surrounding successful productivity requirements without compromising loan quality
</li>
</ul>
