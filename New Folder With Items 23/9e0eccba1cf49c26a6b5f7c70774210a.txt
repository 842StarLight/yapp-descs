<p><b>
JOB RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
As a Risk Management Specialist, you will support risk management team on activities within the Information Technology department. The incumbent will assist in conducting internal risk assessments, review of controls and coordinating remediation activities within the overall IT department.</p>
<p>
Your specific duties in this role will include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Advise and assist department management in matters of risk management.</li>
<li>Identify and advise management of issues that may impact the department and/or company as a whole through full participation in the risk management review processes.</li>
<li>Fully utilize the department's Governance Risk and Compliance System to manage identified risks.</li>
<li>Audit all department controls to ensure alignment with established risk management framework.</li>
<li>Assist with vendor audits as necessary.</li>
<li>Support the Risk Management group on policy and procedure management.</li>
<li>Collaborate, coordinate, and communicate across disciplines and departments.</li>
