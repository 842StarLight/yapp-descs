<p><b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Handling all aspects of leasing apartments, including greeting prospective residents, following up with prospects, touring the community, processing applications, preparing leases and closing the sale</li>
<li>
Understanding the community and its amenities, rents, and specials - as well as those of the community???s competitors</li>
<li>
Conduct off-site marketing and assist in preparing marketing plans</li>
<li>
Screen all incoming residents by objective qualification standards</li>
<li>
Achieve an acceptable leasing closing ratio</li>
<li>
Maintain activity reports by recording traffic daily</li>
<li>
Assist with resident programs and retention</li>
<li>
Assist with coordination of and participate in resident functions</li>
<li>
Processing Recertifications</li>
<li>
Perform all other duties assigned by the Assistant Manager/Community Manager</li>
<li>
LIHTC &amp; HUD property management experience, a MUST</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Job Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
2+ years of prior leasing, customer service, or sales experience, a MUST</li>
<li>
Excellent and premiere customer service orientation</li>
<li>
Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>
Attention to detail and ability to work independently on assignments</li>
<li>
Proficient in Word, Excel, Outlook, One-Site Property Management Software and Internet</li>
<li>
Ability to work in a fast paced environment</li>
</ul>
