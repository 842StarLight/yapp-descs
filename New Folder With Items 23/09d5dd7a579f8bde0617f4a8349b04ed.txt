<h2>Sales and Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Summary: </b></p>
<p>Accomplishes business development activities by researching and developing marketing opportunities and plans; implementing sales plans; managing sales and marketing staff. Implements sales and marketing strategy. Monitors and analyzes sales and marketing activity against goals.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities &amp; Duties: </b></p>
<ul><li>Promoting the company's existing brands and introducing new products to the market.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Analyzing marketing budgets, preparing annual sales and marketing budget plans, scheduling expenditures, and ensuring that the sales team meets their quotas and goals.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Researching and developing marketing opportunities and plans, understanding consumer requirements, identifying market trends, and suggesting system improvements to achieve the company's sales and marketing goals.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Gathering, investigating, and summarizing market data and trends to draft reports.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Implementing new sales plans and advertising.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Recruiting, training, scheduling, coaching, and managing marketing and sales teams to meet sales and marketing objectives.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintaining relationships with important clients by making visits, understanding their needs, and anticipating new marketing opportunities.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Staying current in the industry by attending educational opportunities, conferences, and workshops, reading publications, and maintaining personal and professional networks.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Represent the company at various industry and/or business meetings to promote the company.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Supervise the preparation, issuance, and delivery of sales materials, exhibits, and promotion programs.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Promote positive relations with partners, vendors, and distributors.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintains relationships with advertising agencies, public relations firms and market research firms.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Recommend policies and procedures to enhance operations.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Other duties as assigned.</li></ul>
<p><b>Skills and Qualifications: </b></p>
<ul><li>A Bachelor's degree in Marketing, Mathematics, Business Administration, or a related field.</li></ul>
<ul><li>3-5 years' experience in marketing or sales.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experience in management may be advantageous.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Understanding and knowledge of sales and marketing.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong analytical, organizational, and creative thinking skills.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Excellent communication, interpersonal, and customer service skills.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Knowledge of data analysis and report writing.</li></ul>
<ul><li>The ability to understand and follow company policies and procedures.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experience in working with marketing strategies, advertising campaigns, and successful public relations efforts.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Able to understand and utilize financial reports and legal documents to conduct business.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Regular travel by airplane and automobile in conducting business is required. Ability to communicate orally with customers, management, and other co-workers, both individually and in front of a group is crucial.</li></ul>
<ul><li>The ability to work under pressure, with a willingness to work a flexible schedule.</li></ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
</div>
