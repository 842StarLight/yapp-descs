<h2>Associate Director Biostatistics</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader">
<b>Responsibilities</b><b>
</b>
</h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Provides statistical input in the clinical development plan and protocols and conducts statistical analyses and summary of clinical studies and integrated database to help advance the drug development process. This is a strategic and critical role in effectively and efficiently developing the process for approval of drug products.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
60%</div>
<ul>
<li>
Protocol development<ul>
<li>
Provides statistical analysis plan, including study design, sample size, primary and secondary endpoints, and statistical analysis method.</li>
<li>
Reviews protocol outlines and protocols.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Produces randomization codes.</li>
<li>
Reviews CRF.</li>
<li>
Reviews and approves database structure and edit-check specifications.</li>
<li>
Reviews and approves other clinical documents, such as clinical operation manual, investigator brochure (I), etc.</li>
<li>
Develops final tables and listings (and figures) for final study report (FSR).<ul>
<li>
Develops Table of Contents (TOC) and Tables &amp; Listings templates.</li>
<li>
Develops a detailed Statistical Analysis Plan (SAP) to document technical considerations.</li>
<li>
Provides specs to SAS programmers for generating analysis datasets.</li>
<li>
Programs efficacy analysis and produces final efficacy tables and figures.</li>
<li>
Reviews final tables, listings, and figures.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Develops tables and listings (and figures) for other regulatory submissions.<ul>
<li>
Performs statistical analyses for PK/PD parameters.</li>
<li>
Provides safety tables and listings for IND annual reports and PSUR.</li>
<li>
Provides tables and listings for FDA meeting briefing packages.</li>
<li>
Performs exploratory analyses.</li>
<li>
Performs statistical analyses for marketing and Phase IV studies.</li>
<li>
Performs statistical analyses for abstracts, posters and publications.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Develops final study report (FSR).<ul>
<li>
Provides Statistical Methods section.</li>
<li>
Summarizes Efficacy Results.</li>
<li>
Reviews FSR.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Handles FDA or other regulatory agencies’ contracts and regulatory meetings.<ul>
<li>
Drafts response to questions from regulatory agencies.</li>
<li>
Participates in preparing FDA briefing packages.</li>
<li>
Attends meetings with Regulatory Agencies.</li>
<li>
Interfaces with FDA statistical reviewers and serves on the rapid response team to address inquiries during the NDA review.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
NDA submissions<ul>
<li>
Provides Integrated Summary of Efficacy (ISE) and Integrated Summary of Safety ISS) tables.</li>
<li>
Reviews ISS and ISE reports.</li>
<li>
Prepares briefing packages for FDA Advisory Meetings.</li>
<li>
Participates in responding to Advisory Committee questions in NDA defense.</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<div>
40%</div>
<ul>
<li>
Provides statistical inputs on Clinical Development Plan regarding primary efficacy end point, go/no-go decision criteria, size of studies, and statistical methodologies.</li>
<li>
Discusses strategies with Global Clinical Development and Regulatory Affairs regarding study design and complex analysis issues, FDA issues, and general drug development.</li>
<li>
Reviews clinical, statistical, and regulatory literature for current clinical trial results, regulatory trends and new statistical methods to be applied on his/her assigned therapeutic area.</li>
<li>
Represents clients (internal OPDC, OFRI, OPJ-C, and external partners) at FDA meetings, FDA advisory boards, or other scientific meetings.</li>
<li>
Evaluates new statistical methodologies and AI/Machine Learning software and their potential applications in our clinical trial design and analysis as well as HEOR, RWD/RWE insight and analytics.</li>
<li>
Serves as the Biometrics project team leader regarding the statistical analysis, tables and listings, data collection and quality, and overall timeline.</li>
<li>
Assists in departmental planning and resource allocation and mentoring staff. Provides support for administrative duties as needed to the Senior Director.</li>
<li>
Assists in proposal development, FTE allocation, budget projections, and corporate presentations.</li>
<li>
Provides technical supervision over other biostatisticians assigned to his/her therapeutic area and assigns them the statistical tasks.</li>
<li>
Takes full responsibility in managing biostatistical tasks (as listed below) for one therapeutic area and provides strategic technical support to other clinical projects.</li>
<li>
Provides technical direction to supporting statisticians working on projects on a day-to-day basis.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader">
<b>Overview</b><b>
</b>
</h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Otsuka Pharmaceutical Company is a global healthcare company with the corporate philosophy: “Otsuka-people creating new products for better health worldwide.” Otsuka researches, develops, manufactures and markets innovative products, with a focus on pharmaceutical products to meet unmet medical needs and nutraceutical products for the maintenance of everyday health.</div>
<div>
In pharmaceuticals, Otsuka is a leader in the challenging area of mental health and also has research programs on several under-addressed diseases including tuberculosis, a significant global public health issue. These commitments illustrate how Otsuka is a “big venture” company at heart, applying creativity in everything it does.</div>
<div>
Otsuka Pharmaceutical Company is a subsidiary of Otsuka Holdings Co., Ltd. headquartered in Tokyo, Japan. The Otsuka group of companies employed 50,000 people worldwide and had consolidated sales of approximately USD 11 billion (€ 9.9 billion) in 2016.</div>
