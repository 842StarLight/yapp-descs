<h2>Architectural Vinyl/Aluminum Window Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Crystal Window &amp; Door Systems, Ltd. is an award winning manufacturer of a full production line of quality, energy efficient vinyl and aluminum windows for residential and commercial use. We are currently looking for Senior Architectural Vinyl/Aluminum Window Engineer to join our fast-paced and innovative team. The ideal candidate will be able to conduct analytical studies on engineering proposals, to prepare shop drawings and provide technical supports to customers, manufacturing and sales teams.</p>
<p><b>Essential duties and responsibilities include the following. </b><b>Other duties may be assigned.</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Use computer assisted drafting (CAD), Solid Works, and design software and equipment to perform engineering tasks</li>
<li>Analyze test data and report to determine if design meets functional and performance specifications</li>
<li>Prepare product or system layout and detailed drawings and schematics.</li>
<li>Make orthographic or isometric illustrations required for sales aides</li>
<li>Prepare bills of material and specifications.</li>
<li>Evaluate engineering test results for possible application to development of system or other uses</li>
<li>Analyze research data and proposed product specifications to determine feasibility of product proposal</li>
<li>Coordinate with research personnel to clarify or resolve mechanical and fabrication problems and develop design.</li>
<li>Confer with engineering personnel and prepare design modifications as required</li>
<li>Direct and coordinate manufacturing or building of prototype product or system.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications and Experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>A degree in Civil, Structural, or Mechanical Engineering</li>
<li>Minimum of three years of architectural drafting experience in window &amp; door or architectural industry.</li>
<li>Minimum of three years of window field testing experiences is a plus</li>
<li>Experiences in construction industry preferred</li>
<li>Proficient in Auto CAD for engineering graphics or related field</li>
<li>Understanding and compiling the manufacturing fabrication process</li>
</ul>
<p>Crystal Window &amp; Door Systems, Ltd offers the comprehensive benefit package including 401K matching plan, FSA, health &amp; dental insurance plan, paid vacations, holidays, sick/personal leaves and other benefit plans. Crystal Window &amp; Door Systems, Ltd is proud to be an Equal Opportunity Employer.</p>
<p>For immediate consideration, please reply this advertisement with your resume and salary expectation.</p>
