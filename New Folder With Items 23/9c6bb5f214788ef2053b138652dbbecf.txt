Onsite Account Representative
<br>
20 King St, Port Jervis, NY 12771
<br>
Full-Time Shift(s): Monday-Friday 7am-5pm
<br>
<br>
OVERVIEW:
<br>
Working as an Full-Time Onsite Account Representative, you will be responsible for all aspects of the customer relationship and will work with the customer's upper management to ensure business success and compliance with any national or local service agreements.
<br>
<br>
RESPONSIBILITIES:
<br>
The duties and responsibilities of this position include, but are not limited to:
<br>
o Working directly with multiple customer contacts and building relationships with upper management
<br>
o Receiving product; inspecting it for quality control, staging, scanning, and packing; and making deliveries
<br>
o Purchasing product, quoting, sourcing, and managing inventory
<br>
o Developing and executing a sales plan to reach and exceed business goals
<br>
o Communicating business statistics &amp; progress toward goals
<br>
o Developing continuous improvement programs
<br>
o Demonstrating and maintaining cost savings programs
<br>
o Creating a unique environment of program offerings
<br>
o Handling day-to-day needs of the customer
<br>
<br>
REQUIRED POSITION QUALIFICATIONS:
<br>
The following skills and qualifications are required for this position:
<br>
o 18 years or over
<br>
o A valid driver's license and the ability to meet our driving record requirements
<br>
o Possess a degree in Business/Marketing OR equivalent industry experience and knowledge of the local market
<br>
o Possess strong computer skills and math aptitude, especially with Microsoft Office Suite
<br>
o Excellent written and oral communication skills
<br>
o Demonstrate strong organization, planning, prioritizing abilities and attention to detail
<br>
o Exhibit strong problem solving and deductive reasoning skills
<br>
o Highly motivated, self-directed, and customer service oriented
<br>
o Demonstrate our core values of ambition, innovation, integrity and teamwork
<br>
o Ability to learn and perform multiple tasks in a fast paced, dynamic team environment
<br>
o Ability to work independently as well as in a team environment
<br>
o Ability to lift, slide and lower packages that typically weigh 25lbs-50lbs and may weigh up to 75lbs
<br>
o Ability to pass the required drug screen (applicable in the US, Puerto Rico, and Guam ONLY)
<br>
<br>
PREFERRED POSITION QUALIFICATIONS:
<br>
o Experience and success in Strategic/Key account or National Account sales
<br>
o Prior vending and implementation experience
<br>
o Knowledge and experience using Timeline software
<br>
o Prior process mapping and DMAIC experience
<br>
o Experience speaking and presenting to groups of people.
<br>
