<h2>Assistant Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>We are a non-profit ministry, primarily supported by area churches, that provides food, clothing, and financial services to needy residents in a specific service area.</p>
<p>The Assistant Director will provide executive level administrative support to the Executive Director (ED) and, on occasion, to the board of directors, when needed. S/he will assist the ED and/or perform the following daily, weekly, and monthly duties:</p>
<p>* Establish and maintain an authoritative role over both staff and volunteers in all departments, while giving management-level employees the encouragement and support to make first-line decisions. Must make clear and definitive decisions pertaining to all functions of the organization in the absence of the ED.</p>
<p>* Establish and maintain close relationships with church, social, and business funding partners , which includes visits and presentations.</p>
<p>* Maintains all donor information for prompt acknowledgement and reporting to the ED, and by extension, to the board of directors.</p>
<p>* Prepares daily bank deposits of thrift store sales and monthly state sales tax payments.</p>
<p>* Prepares and/or pays all monthly operating expenses and reconcile all QuickBooks-to-bank entries and reports.</p>
<p>* Collects and maintains employee time sheets and benefits. Submits data to payroll service and pays all related vendors as scheduled.</p>
<p>* Work with coordinator of volunteer drivers to ensure prompt and efficient food donation pick-up.</p>
<p>* Oversees the purchasing and reporting process pertaining to the Atlanta Food Bank.</p>
<p>* Order, purchase, and maintain office, pantry, and store supply levels.</p>
<p>* Promote the organization via various social media platforms.</p>
<p>* Assist in planning donor, staff, and volunteer recognition, and client empowerment events</p>
<p>* Assist ED in researching funding opportunities and submitting the necessary applications..</p>
<p>Qualifications:</p>
<p>* Minimum of an Associate degree plus two or more years office management/executive-level administration responsibilities. Bachelor degree preferred.</p>
<p>* Must possess excellent oral and written communication skills, and have previous experience presenting to small and large groups; ideally, church congregations.</p>
<p>* Proficient in Office Suite, QuickBooks, and other relevant software, as well as the ability to learn new client database software.</p>
<p>This is not an all-inclusive listing of responsibilities, as some duties may be handled by the ED or other employees, when necessary. However, the selected employee must, at all times, display a compassionate manner when serving low-to-moderate income clients. S\he will, in the absence of the ED, or as directed by the ED, assess client eligibility for both food and financial services, as well as counseling and referring them to other community and government resources.</p>
<p>This is a key holder position with financial oversight. Must past criminal and driver background, as well as credit checks. Must be of high moral character and comfortable relating to senior church, business, and social service leaders as a prominent face of our ministry. This is an "all hands on deck" working environment. All staff "jump in" as needed. Persons that are unable or unwilling to work outside normal business hours on occasion, including a periodic weekend day, need not apply.</p>
<p><b>PREFERENCE GIVEN TO QUALIFIED CANDIDATES WHO LIVE IN, OR NEAR, THE FOLLOWING ZIP CODES: 30047, 30087, AND 30084 </b></p>
<p>Salary range is $28,000 - $30,000, depending on experience. 4 1/2 day work week, sick leave, paid vacation, short-term disability, and an optional health insurance employee participation package is available.</p>
<p>Interested applicants should submit a resume and cover letter with salary requirements for immediate consideration and initial telephone interview, as this will be an immediate hire. No phone calls, mailed, or hand delivered resumes will be considered.</p>
