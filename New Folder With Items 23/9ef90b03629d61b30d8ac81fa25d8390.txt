<h2>Hotel Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>POSITION: Hotel Manager</b></p>
<p><b>
LOCATION: Cahuilla Casino Hotel, Anza, California</b></p>
<p><b>
SALARY: 55k - 65k</b></p>
<p><b>
POSITION SUMMARY</b><b>
</b></p>
<p><b>Oversees all aspects of hotel operations for our 58-room hotel at Cahuilla Casino Hotel, including guest service, front desk, reporting, market retail, housekeeping, maintenance, budgeting / finances, teambuilding, and staff development. Responsible for all phases of hotel management, including operations, sales, employee relations and payroll. Empowers Team Members to provide Sky’s the Limit service to all guests.</b></p>
<p><b>
DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li><b>Recruits, selects, trains, and manages Team Members to deliver Sky’s the Limit guest service and quality products that will lead to maximizing revenue and profitability.</b></li>
<li><b>Creates and maintains guest-driven operations, empowering hotel staff to excel in superior guest service.</b></li>
<li><b>Assists in the development of the hotel's annual budget including capital expense budget and long-term business plan. Implements and manages approved annual fiscal year budget. Achieves budgeted sales and maximum profitability.</b></li>
<li><b>Monitors and contributes to the management of room inventory and rate strategies through effective Yield Management strategies.</b></li>
<li><b>Creates new programs in response to market conditions and revenue opportunities.</b></li>
<li>
<b>Provides regular direction and oversight for hotel operations as follows:</b>
<ul>
<li><b>Front desk, revenue and reservation functions to ensure the delivery of superior guest services, the security of monies, credit card transactions, guest information and that established goals are achieved.</b></li>
<li><b>Market area within the front desk to ensure proper display of retail items, sundries, and quick grab and go food options to drive incremental revenue for the hotel.
</b></li>
<li><b>Housekeeping and maintenance functions to ensure compliance with quality and brand standards in all areas of the hotel as it relates to appearance, cleanliness and levels of maintenance and repair; ensures preventative maintenance programs are in place to protect the physical assets of the hotel.</b></li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><b>Able to inspect multiple rooms on a daily basis.
</b></li>
<li><b>Protects the hotel’s financial assets by properly administering policies and procedures for cash handling, credit cards, accounts payable, accounts receivable, payroll and all other financial transactions. Also, properly administers policies and procedures for protecting the safety of guests and other Team Members.</b></li>
<li><b>Develops internal procedures, processes and policies.
</b></li>
<li><b>Prepares various daily, weekly, monthly reports for management.</b></li>
<li><b>Ensures regular assessment and review of all hotel personnel by appropriate staff. Coordinates internal training and development programs.</b></li>
<li><b>Acts as Manager on Duty on assigned shift/day.</b></li>
<li><b>Maintains a high personal visibility throughout the property.</b></li>
<li><b>Creates and maintains revenue management processes.</b></li>
<li><b>Develops and implements sales strategies.
</b></li>
<li><b>All other duties assigned</b></li>
</ul>
</div>
<br>
<ul>
<li><b>Bachelor’s degree in Hotel/Restaurant Management or Business or equivalent combination of education and experience.</b></li>
<li><b>Minimum of three years of hotel management or supervisory experience.</b></li>
<li><b>Knowledge of local competition and general industry trends.</b></li>
<li><b>Strong leadership and a professional image.</b></li>
<li><b>Strong interpersonal, written and verbal communication skills.</b></li>
<li><b>Excellent decision-making ability and analytical skills.</b></li>
<li><b>Knowledge of a variety of computer software applications.</b></li>
<li><b>Ability to work a flexible schedule, including evenings, weekends and holidays.</b></li>
<li><b>Ability to stand for long hours at a time.</b></li>
</ul>
