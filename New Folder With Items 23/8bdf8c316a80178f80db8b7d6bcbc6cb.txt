<h2>Client Success Partner - Healthcare & Life Sciences Division</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Job Title:</b> Client Success Partner
<br><br>
<b>Position Type:</b> Full-time
<br><br>
<b>Location:</b> Independence, OH, NY, NY or Fort Collins, CO
<br><br>
Are you an Account Management professional Do you love the thrill of developing client relationships while delivering measurable results and success If so, we have an opportunity that you need to look at.
<br><br>
Advance your Client Success Career at Sterling
<br><br>
We’re a fast-moving world leader in cloud-based background screening and identity services. Our clients use our services to create safer workplaces, build trusted brands and protect vulnerable populations. We are financially thriving, investing in growth and grounded in authenticity. If you are motivated and passionate, you will love working here. Apply today!
<br><br>
We have great people here and are looking for more. Come join us - you will love it!
<br><br>
As a successful Client Success Partner at Sterling, you are responsible for managing the day to day screening program for your book of business. As a key adviser to your accounts, you have the opportunity to expand the Sterling footprint with your customers and work diligently to develop and enhance resilient client relationships. Our primary goal is to provide hiring peace of mind by delivering a simple, smarter background screening and on-boarding experience for employers worldwide, and we'd be honored if you'd join us. Every day, our amazing Client Success Team provides solutions to their clients, gaining their trust while building long-term partnerships. When you join us, you'll be part of a high-performing team that truly values your contributions. If you're a self-starter who can meet the challenge, keep reading!
<br><br>
<b>This Is the Job For You, If:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>You're a relationship builder who connects with people and values friendship and fun.</li>
<li>You're a natural people person and had the best lemonade stand on the block as a kid.</li>
<li>Insatiable appetite to learn and driven by a hunger to grow, become, do, share, and give more.</li>
<li>Trusted adviser that lives integrity and delivers on promises ... every time.</li>
<li>Competition drives you…you may have excelled in sports, love a good game of trivia, and you won't be beat at a friendly game of Monopoly.</li>
<li>You'll talk to anyone at a party, are a natural networker and can build rapport with everyone.</li>
</ul>
<b>This Is What You'll Do:</b><br>
Relationship Cultivation and Strategic Management
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Maintain full ownership of a book of business including up to 30 accounts</li>
<li>Act as a strategic adviser to your client base by being the "go to" person for strategic account needs</li>
<li>Become an industry expert on your assigned vertical to better understand each client's organizational dynamics</li>
<li>Identify and develop multi-level relationships with key decision makers to ensure retention and relationship stability</li>
</ul>
Metrics, Reporting and Client Strategy
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Conduct regular business reviews using value add propositions to mitigate client risk and enhance background screening programs</li>
<li>Monitor and analyze development and strategic progression for all client accounts</li>
</ul>
Consultative Selling to Existing Business
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Create account plans as a road map to grow customer revenue and deliver world class client satisfaction</li>
<li>Grow customer book of business to target vs. prior year, driven by existing revenue retention and closing new business</li>
<li>Implement long term contract agreements with clients to review and grow revenue</li>
<li>Propose additional products and services to expand footprint within existing accounts</li>
</ul>
<b>This Is What We're Looking For:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Proven success in account management/client success/sales/project management experience with at least 3 - 6 years of experience</li>
<li>Experience managing enterprise level customers with more than $250,000 annual revenue and the ability to show evidence of this</li>
<li>Experience relative to background screening and/or compliance is a huge plus</li>
<li>Is a creative problem solver who has a track record of success in this area</li>
<li>Someone who can easily engage with and understands changing dynamics when working across all levels both from a client perspective and internal Sterling perspective</li>
<li>Someone who works well in an environment where a senior book of business requires extra involvement from Sterling leadership at all levels and in all areas of managing and growing the client relationship</li>
<li>Ability to effectively communicate with senior level decision makers within organizations and successfully handle objections</li>
<li>Must have experience leading face-to-face client presentations with middle to executive level management</li>
<li>Candidate must be polished with experience working for mid-large sized organizations</li>
<li>Strong program management, negotiation skills and the ability to solve problems</li>
<li>Proficiency using CRM Platform is ideal</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree strongly preferred</li>
<li>Lastly, we need someone who understands what we do, is excited to be a part of something big, has a positive outlook, is happy to travel whenever is necessary (up to 25%), is looking for a career (not a job) and understands that this is not a 9-5 job, and is a get the job done type</li>
</ul>
