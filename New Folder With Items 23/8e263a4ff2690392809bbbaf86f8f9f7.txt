<h2>Sr. Salesforce Administrator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree, high standard of education, or equivalent work experience.</li>
<li>5+ years Salesforce.com Admin experience and configuration of 100+ users.</li>
<li>Certified Salesforce.com Salesforce Administrator, Sales Cloud Consultant, and Platform App Builder or other equivalent qualification.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<div>Ring is looking to hire an experienced Sr. Salesforce Administrator to join our Neighbors team. The Neighbors team is revolutionizing public safety by utilizing innovative Ring technology. This leader will partner with sales, account management, business development, sales enablement, finance, product, marketing, and business intelligence teams to design and deliver solutions that automate and drive productivity of our sales and account management teams.<br>
<br>
This candidate will define, design and deliver sales processes and accompanying reporting for new initiatives, markets and segments the business pursues and will provide ongoing support and continuous improvements. The successful candidate must have a strong Salesforce technical skillset, prioritize multiple work streams, communicate with partners, execute in a fast-paced environment, and be eager to learn.<br>
<br>
Responsibilities<br>
<ul>
<li>Define and deliver sales operations and Salesforce.com strategic roadmap. Budget and resource the efforts accordingly. Drive consensus with stakeholders on the roadmap and deliverables.</li>
<li>Hands-on configuration of all new and existing Salesforce.com features including user management, security, custom objects, page layouts, validations, workflows, flows, process builders, Einstein Analytics, and other 3rd party applications.</li>
<li>Manage Salesforce.com CRM application including support requests and escalated administrative needs of users by providing prompt and complete resolution to technical challenges and business support issues.</li>
<li>Partner and consult with the business to identify and define business requirements and initiate and prioritize enhancement, gap analysis, and prepare user stories.</li>
<li>Partner with business stakeholders to proactively identify, drive, and build improvements, enhancements, and system customization that solve business needs.</li>
<li>Own and deliver new process updates and projects, including ideation, requirement gathering, build, support, maintenance, and success tracking.</li>
<li>Identify, procure and manage technology tools required to drive efficiency and productivity on marketing, sales, and account management teams; partner with legal and information security to execute contracts and ensure compliance with security policies.</li>
<li>Educate sales and marketing teams about process changes and opportunities to self-serve in Salesforce.com by leading in-person and online trainings</li>
</ul>
</div>
