<h2>Digital Campaign Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Main responsibility will be to act as the digital project manager and coordinate between Account Executives and the Central Operations team.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
Duties and Responsibilities:</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<ul>
<li> Act as a digital project manager, a coordinator between Account Executives and the Central Ad Operations team ensuring accurate order placement and fulfillment of client digital campaigns.</li>
<li> Enter insertion orders in workflow tool for trafficking and creative production.</li>
<li> Oversee the implementation and continued success of campaigns.</li>
<li> Identify optimization opportunities for digital campaigns (including search marketing (SEO/SEM), targeted display/ video, retargeting, and mobile optimizations.)</li>
<li> Develop in-depth/custom campaign reports for Account Managers and clients to effectively show campaign success and explain optimizations (through use of campaign dashboard and client provided Google Analytics.)</li>
<li> Coordinate digital creative fulfillment, working with Account Managers and the Central Ad Operations team.</li>
<li> Act as a digital product expert for clients and the entire Cox Media Group sales team.</li>
<li> Expertly navigate search marketing (SEO/SEM), targeted display/ video, retargeting, and mobile marketing at varying client levels of understanding.</li>
<li> Be hands on in clients' success, the campaign optimization process, and be eager to share and explain campaign results and improvements in-person on a consistent basis.</li>
<li> Miscellaneous other duties upon request.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Qualifications:</b></h4>
<div><b>
Experience and Education:</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<ul>
<li> Bachelor’s Degree or Higher.</li>
<li> Proven success at multitasking, hitting deadlines, and working in a fast paced environment</li>
<li> 1+ years of experience in processing and executing complex orders in any setting, digital advertising operations experience preferred</li>
<li> Demonstrated success in processing orders accurately with strong attention to detail</li>
<li> Strong understanding of search marketing (SEO/SEM), targeted display/ video, retargeting, and mobile marketing</li>
<li> Demonstrated record of success working with teams</li>
<li> Experience in providing in-depth analysis on client results</li>
</ul>
