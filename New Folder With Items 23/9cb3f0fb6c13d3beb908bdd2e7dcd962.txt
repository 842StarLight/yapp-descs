<h2>Senior Vice President, Business Development</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>SR. VICE PRESIDENT, BUSINESS DEVELOPMENT</b><br>
Orange County, CA<br>
</p>
<p>
Mobilitie is the largest privately held wireless infrastructure company in the United States. Connectivity is a vital component of daily life, and Mobilitie works with wireless carriers and venues to ensure our customers are better connected. Mobilitie is a leader in the wireless industry and has been rated one of the largest and fastest growing private companies by the Orange County Business Journal. Mobilitie owns and operates cell towers, DAS, small cell, fiber networks, and Wi-Fi systems and advises carriers on network operations, performance and improvement opportunities. As a global provider of complete wireless solutions, we design, build and operate networks and infrastructure to deliver optimal coverage in the most demanding and challenging of environments.</p>
<p>
We are currently seeking a <b>Sr. Vice President, Business Development</b> to join our team. This position will work collaboratively with multiple business units across the organization and will be responsible for managing and growing relationships and new business in the marketplace. This position is responsible for identifying and closing new business opportunities and mentoring other senior staff. The evolving product portfolio includes but is not limited to DAS networks, Wi-Fi, Small Cells, Tower assets, and professional services.</p>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Create business development strategy to meet revenue and investment goals</li>
<li>
Identify, cultivate and close business opportunities within the carrier, venue, and enterprise customer segments</li>
<li>
Promote various Mobilitie solutions in the marketplace, inbuilding DAS, small cells, Wi-Fi, CBRS, communication towers, and professional services</li>
<li>
Promote the Mobilitie brand and corporate strategy through networking, vendor relationships, partners and OEMS</li>
<li>
Identify all key decision makers and develop deep relationships in multiple business units within the carriers</li>
<li>
Develop and build upon existing wireless industry relationships to identify and pursue opportunities to grow Mobilitie’s asset base</li>
<li>
Work with the sales teams to identify, validate and close locations for DAS network coverage including sports stadiums, college campuses, convention centers, shopping malls, hospitals, and key areas of coverage deficiencies</li>
<li>
Identify new business opportunities within wireless carriers, tower owners, consultants and other new prospects through research, calls, meetings and presentations</li>
<li>
Develop new business models and opportunities within the carriers</li>
<li>
Work closely with multiple business units to support existing business development activities with the major wireless carriers</li>
<li>
Develop new relationships across the wireless industry and expand services provided to the wireless carriers</li>
<li>
Responsible for carrier negotiation of rates and flexible pricing approaches with the wireless carriers to ensure revenue metrics are obtained while also achieving investment metrics</li>
<li>
Report on budget, performance metrics, and goals monthly related to overall strategy and staff</li>
<li>
Matrix manage across the organization and business units</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s Degree required from accredited university, with a minimum fifteen (15) years of business development experience in the technology industry, MBA highly desired</li>
<li>
Excellent relationship history and references from the wireless carriers (T-Mobile, AT&amp;T, Sprint, and Verizon Wireless)</li>
<li>
Strong senior executive relationships, creative, driven &amp; self-motivated, with the ability to work independently and with a team</li>
<li>
Skilled negotiator including the ability to take direction or resolve issues independently</li>
<li>
Innate ability to prioritize, qualify, and manage business opportunities to achieve sales targets</li>
<li>
Superior organization skills with a strong initiative to lead customer sales presentations with consistent follow through until deal close</li>
<li>
Excellent closing skills with the innate ability to overcome objections and obstacles throughout the sales process</li>
<li>
Proven history of results</li>
<li>
Travel required</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
The ideal candidate will possess strong oral and written communication skills with the instinctive ability to be adaptable to an ever-changing environment. This person will also have a strong client facing sales aptitude with an inherent problem-solving capability.</p>

If you are interested in working in the exciting and growing world of telecommunications real estate, please contact the SVP of HR or submit an application at Mobilitie’s Careers page!</p>
