 Description
<p>Reporting into the VP, Corporate Communications &amp; B2B Marketing the <b>B2B Marketing Director</b> will direct all B2B marketing functions for MJH Life Sciences’ Truth Serum division to drive profitable and sustainable growth in support of our strategic business plan. This position will play an integral role in growing brand awareness and increasing market penetration for all agency businesses. Our ideal candidate will have extensive marketing experience and a comprehensive knowledge of branding. The position is responsible for the planning, development, implementation, and evaluation of all the Truth Serum B2B marketing strategies, marketing communications, and liaise with public relations teams, for external and internal efforts.</p>
 Position Requirements
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Drive B2B growth of MJH Life Sciences Truth Serum brands by building relevant tools, developing and executing marketing campaigns to build client retention and foster industry reputation.</li>
<li>Create high-impact, go to market strategy to support our sales process by developing marketing collateral and industry presentations, thought-leadership, etc.</li>
<li>Engage with key stakeholders among the executive team and Truth Serum brands to drive successful product and campaign launches and ongoing profitable sales growth.</li>
<li>Build brand awareness and primary demand creation including social media, search engine optimization, mass emails, and other traditional marketing sources.</li>
<li>Create and maintain content and digital assets to use and leverage across Truth Serum.</li>
<li>Develop marketing budget and track ROI</li>
<li>Collaborate with internal teams to identify key business needs and opportunities.</li>
<li>Be on top of trends of the market and direct the market research efforts of the company</li>
<li>Think strategically and contribute to new ideas and best practices.</li>
</ul>

<li>Experience building, nurturing and retaining a productive and engaged team.</li>
<li>Comfortable and effective interacting with senior management and key stakeholders</li>
<li>Ability to multitask and work in a fast-paced environment</li>

<li>Knowledge / experience with healthcare agency business models a plus.</li>

</ul>
