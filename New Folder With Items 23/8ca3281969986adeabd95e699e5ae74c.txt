<h2>Account Manager - Entertainment (Direct Sales)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div><div><div>
<div>The Account Manager for the Entertainment team (Direct Sales) is responsible for the sales relationship within assigned accounts to organically increase Vectorworks’ depth and breadth of services.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>You will work closely with the Entertainment Team Manager to understand important KPIs and the team strategy in order to maximize sales results within their assigned accounts while offering customers a seamless experience from first purchase through annual upgrades and renewals.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>If you are looking for an opportunity to have a lasting impact on a growing team, we hope you'll take the time to learn more about this role!</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div><b>In this role, you should expect to:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Function as a consultative salesperson (direct sales); accurately conveying Vectorworks’ software and services to clients as a strategic solution to improve their business operations
</li>
<li>Prospect within assigned accounts to uncover new opportunities for Vectorworks to partner with existing customers</li>
<li>
Analyze and understand the customer’s business strategy, future growth plans and expansion activities and strategically align Vectorworks’ product roadmap where mutually beneficial</li>
<li>
Work cross functionally with marketing, technical support, training, engineering, sales operations and other departments to provide outstanding customer service</li>
<li>
Make recommendations on product enhancements based on feedback from assigned accounts</li>
<li>
Have firm understanding of Vectorworks’ Entertainment product portfolio and the competitive posture of the company; keep up to date with industry knowledge and price trends</li>
<li>
Participate in industry trade shows</li>
<li>
Prepare sales forecasts, budgets, and other projections as needed
</li>
<li>25% travel required to existing and potential customers with on-site client visits</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>

</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>
<b>Why do you want this job? </b><b><i>Because you are:</i></b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>
Competitive with a tenacious attitude and the ability to think critically, multi-task, and keep up in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>
Self-motivated and confident presenting information over the phone to business decision makers</li>
<li>
Able to take direction and work with multidisciplinary teams and independently when necessary
</li>
</ul>

</li>

<li>
Excellent communication skills, both written and verbal</li>
<li>
Experience in the entertainment industry (lighting design, scenic design, theater, etc.)</li>
</ul>
