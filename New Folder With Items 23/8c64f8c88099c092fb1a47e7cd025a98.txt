<h2>Customer Service Representative, Entry Level</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Req ID#: </b>14234BR-1<br>
<br>
<b>Description</b><br>
Accountabilities:<ul>
<li>
The primary purpose of the CSR in Base Connection is to provide quality sales to Safeguard Distributor/customers in upselling and cross-selling product offerings. Receives inbound customer calls via phone, mail, and fax. Utilizes strong selling and influencing skills to achieve or exceed revenue targets, retention, accuracy and customer satisfaction goals.</li>
<li>
Consistently provides prescribed customer experience on every customer interaction. Customer service experience is defined in current performance metrics and goals. Achieves monthly sales goals by utilizing sales/call management skills.</li>
<li>
Consistently educates customer on improvements and new opportunities for their business needs. Asks for sales and consistently resolves customer stated and unstated needs.</li>
<li>
Consistently utilizes current sales approach and questioning techniques with each product or service offered. Provides appropriate responses on pricing and identifies/helps overcome pricing objections. Leverages and maximizes all available promotions.</li>
<li>
Instills confidence by processing error-free orders. Takes responsibility for thorough verification and quality, checking incoming quotes and orders to ensure consistency in the pricing, options, imprints and composition files.
</li>
<li>Utilizes customer fulfillment process and tools (computer monitor, keyboard, printer, software, etc.) to ensure timely and accurate translation of customer transactions; including pricing products, initiating proofs, coordination with Artwork Services, releasing orders to manufacturing, and following through with the customer.</li>
<li>
Partners with functional experts to fulfill customers's orders when needed, i.e., purchasing, manufacturing plant.</li>
<li>
Implements coaching/feedback and takes action to make changes as appropriate.</li>
<li>
Performs with full adherence to service compliance requirements.</li>
<li>
Participates in a positive work environment by complying with policies, procedures and safety rules.</li>
<li>
Position may include other duties as determined by the company.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<br>
<br>
<b>Required:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>High School (or equivalent)</li>
<li>
2 or more years of call center experience with proven sales abilities to achieve sales objectives</li>
<li>
Experience in printing or banking industry</li>
<li>
Knowledge of the sales process</li>
<li>
Able to address and convert customer inquiries into sales</li>
<li>
Good listening skills to gather what customer is saying to the customer need</li>
<li>
Excellent communication skills</li>
<li>
Order entry process experience</li>
<li>
Experience working on PC w/ accurate keyboarding<br>
</li>
</ul>
