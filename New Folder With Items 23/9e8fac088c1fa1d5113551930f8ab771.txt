<b>JOB SUMMARY:</b><br>
Responsible for locating, researching and contracting new land suitable for acquisition and development.<br>
<br>
<b>PRIMARY RESPONSIBILITIES</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Locate land suitable for acquisition and development by conducting the necessary market research and due diligence</li>
<li>Determine land values in the specific market by checking like property sales by means of public records, developers and other professional affiliates Negotiate and contract land acquisition</li>
<li>Coordinate land entitlement and planning activities with Development personnel</li>
<li>Coordinate governmental review</li>
<li>Review and monitor purchase agreements.</li>
<li>Coordinate joint venture agreements or joint venture management agreements, as appropriate</li>
</ul>
