<h2>Business Communication Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div><div>Are you looking to make a difference in a patient’s life? At AmerisourceBergen, you will find an innovative culture that is patient focused and dedicated to making a difference. As an organization, we are united in our responsibility to create healthier futures.<br>
</div></div>

<div>What you will be doing<div>
<div>
<p>Under the direction of the Director, Business Communications, this role is responsible for associate communication and enablement related to AmerisourceBergen’s U.S.-based human health distribution network and related business segments, as well as operational customer communications.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>This role will work collaboratively with all areas of the business, communications, human resources, sales, executive leadership, operations and other departments to develop and execute internal communications strategy that drives business performance, enables associates and supports the company and business unit strategy and annual financial plans.</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
Both develops and executes the annual strategy for business communications aligned to business unit strategy and goals—this may include key messages, leadership communications, sales direction and support materials, customer communications and associate enablement tools</li>
<li>
Creates connectivity to and alignment with the AmerisourceBergen enterprise including brand, messaging and channel strategy
</li>
<li>Defines and manages internal communications process and tools for the business units including communications policies and engagement strategy, distribution lists, channel technologies (such as Salesforce Marketing Cloud), digital signage, associate newsletters, and an internal mobile communications application</li>
<li>
Creates and manages a monthly reporting dashboard for communications activities</li>
<li>
Keeps abreast of associate enablement and communications best practice, trends and technologies</li>
<li>
Builds rapport with internal stakeholders and becomes a trusted advisor within the business units and at the enterprise level</li>
<li>
Performs related duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<br>
<br>
</div>
</div>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>What your background should look like (minimum qualifications)<div>
Requires a minimum of three years experience in communications and broad training in fields such as communications, marketing and business administration generally obtained through completion of a four-year bachelor’s degree program; BA or BS with a concentration in Communications or Marketing preferred.</div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>
<br>
Ability to work autonomously with remote supervision in a matrixed environment; resolve issues efficiently and effectively</li>
<li>
Excellent written and oral communications skills; Ability to take highly technical information and translate it for broad and diverse audiences</li>
<li>
Superior organizational skills; attention to detail</li>
<li>
Ability to work in a fast-paced environment, managing multiple projects while meeting deadlines</li>
<li>
Strong creativity; ability to partner with graphic design colleagues to integrate visual elements into all communications</li>
<li>
Excellent interpersonal and professional skills; ability to interact with senior leadership</li>
<li>
Strong analytical skills</li>
<li>
Experience with e-mail automation platforms, mobile communications technology and intranet platforms a plus</li>
<li>
Strong knowledge of Microsoft Word, Excel, PowerPoint and Outlook</li>
<li>
Ability to travel in the United States 15-20% annually</li>
</ul>
