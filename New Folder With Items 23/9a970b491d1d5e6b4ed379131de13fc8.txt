<h2>Account Executive, Corporate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Everbridge is seeking a hunter/closer looking to advance their career in software sales. This is a full selling role that will include prospecting, qualifying, managing, and closing new sales. This candidate will work remotely in the designated region or based out of our Burlington, MA headquarters.</p>
<p><b>
About the Team:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Everbridge’s Account Executive team is an extremely collaborative, results-driven, high performing sales team that is 100% responsible for the net new business, and therefore the revenue engine of the company. We work in an open sales floor environment in a world class headquarters located in Burlington, MA.</li>

<li>Meet the Sales and Account Management team here .</li>

<p><b>Job Duties:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop a strong understanding of target buyers, domain knowledge, products and competitors.</li>
<li>Prospect and identify new opportunities to build and maintain a healthy pipeline.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to identify and sell to multiple buyers within an organization.</li>
<li>Implement a consultative, solution selling, methodology.</li>
<li>Ability to develop and close sales exceeding $100,000 in annual value</li>
<li>Work with Marketing and Business Development to execute campaigns to top buyers.</li>
<li>Exceed assigned sales objectives and quarterly bookings quotas.</li>
<li>Accurately manage and forecast your business using SF.com.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Required Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of 5+ year software sales role, primarily selling into mid market or enterprise level commercial accounts.</li>
<li>Product or solution experience selling into Software, IT Ops, SW Development, Communications, Compliance, Security, IT Recruiting, Business Continuity, Disaster Recovery, HR, Facilities Management, Hardware or IT Services buyers.</li>
<li>Excellent internet research and networking skills</li>
<li>Experience conducting product demos</li>
<li>Strong hunting, prospecting, and cold calling skills.</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written interpersonal, presentation and communication skills including PowerPoint.</li>
<li>Must have a verifiable history of meeting or exceeding sales quotas.</li>
<li>Must be fluent in English.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Preferred Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>2+ years in a field based sales role selling a complex SaaS solution into the above industries.</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree or equivalent in Business, Communications, Computer Science, Organizational Behavior, Security, Facilities Management or HR.</li>
<li>Accurate forecasting ability</li>
<li>Salesforce.com power user skills</li>
<li>Knowledge of LinkledIn, Salesloft, Rainking, Data.com, Inside View.</li>
<li>Knowledge of IT Operations, QA and Application Development workflow.</li>
<li>Formal Sales Training (Sandler, Dale Carnegie, etc)</li>
</ul>
