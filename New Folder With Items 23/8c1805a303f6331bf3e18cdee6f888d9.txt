<h2>Business Development Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>The primary focus of a Business Development Representative (BDR) is to generate demand for Smartbridge solutions and services through a mix of inbound calls and outbound prospecting within an assigned territory or vertical.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Job Description:</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The BDR will prospect into targeted business accounts in an effort to increase the productivity of Account Managers by qualifying accounts as potential revenue opportunities. As a BDR you will act as the first point of contact for many prospects. You will be responsible for growing a sales pipeline through demand generation activities. The BDR will work closely with Account Directors and Marketing teams to establish a pipeline expansion plan and to leverage specific campaigns to penetrate target accounts. Sales activities will include research on assigned prospect lists, proactive cold calling to engage target prospects, email marketing, and ongoing lead nurturing and relationship development to support the creation of qualified opportunities.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Additionally, the BDR will:</div>
<ul>
<li>
Conduct market research; generating qualified sales leads for the sales organization through cold calling and email campaigning</li>
<li>
Set meetings with Director, VP, and C level Executives</li>
<li>
Identify new prospects for our customer profile, profile accounts, identify solutions and key contacts</li>
<li>
Handle the execution of multiple sales campaigns simultaneously</li>
<li>
Be able to manage time and work efforts with minimal supervision</li>
<li>
Contribute to marketing and sales content creation</li>
<li>
Drive business through cold demand generation, follow up on warm leads, and existing account management</li>
<li>
Attend trade shows and support sales efforts at conference exhibits, and visit customers/prospects.</li>
<li>
Attend local networking and professional events</li>
<li>
Work with our partner network to set meetings and drive additional business</li>
<li>
Clean, maintain, and update content in CRM</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Requirements:</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Minimum 4-year college degree with 1-3 years of sales experience. Software or technology related sales experience is preferred.</li>
<li>
Willing to travel up to 10%.</li>
<li>
Familiarity with enterprise solution providers such as Oracle, Microsoft, Workday, NetSuite, MicroStrategy, UiPath, M-Files, etc. is preferred, but not required.</li>
<li>
Ideal candidate should have good knowledge and experience with some or all of the following. ERP, Business Intelligence, Data Integration or Automation tools.
</li>
<li>Familiarity with CRM Systems, Market Research/Market Intelligence Tools, and Sales Automation Tools is strongly preferred.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The ideal candidate will also have:</div>
<ul>
<li>
Growth-minded thinking and an eagerness to rise within an organization</li>
<li>
Goal-oriented, self-motivator, independent, cooperative, flexible, creative</li>
<li>
Persistence, energy, passion, and enthusiasm</li>
<li>
Ability to be successful in a fast paced, high energy environment</li>
<li>
Great interpersonal skills that make others around you want to work with you</li>
<li>
Excellent verbal and written communications skills and effective presentation skills
</li>
</ul>
