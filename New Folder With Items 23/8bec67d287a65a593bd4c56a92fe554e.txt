<h2>Government Account Manager, Houston, TX</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>T-Mobile's Government Sales organization is expanding and seeking a qualified Government Account Manager to develop relationships and drive business in our State and Local Government Accounts. Our ambitious and resilient Government Account Managers specifically implement solution-based sales strategies and uncover new business opportunities. In addition to selling T-Mobile's existing wireless voice and data solutions, the Government Account Manager will help launch a new array of fixed-mobile convergence solutions in the region.<br>
<br>
<b>T-MOBILE: MOBILITY LEADER</b><br>
Ranked #1 in the US in business wireless customer satisfaction, T-Mobile is growing rapidly in the enterprise segment. T-Mobile is leveraging this market position to launch the most cutting edge solutions in the fixed-mobile convergence category. These solutions will change communications significantly as they will allow our customers to eliminate their voice landlines completely in favor of a single converged device.<br>
<br>
<b>CHALLENGING AND EXCITING ROLE</b><br>
Our Government Account Managers are self-motivated and entrepreneurial; however T-Mobile will provide all the training and support you need to do your job well. In this role, you will develop new business within your assigned base of accounts through a consultative approach. As you develop your account module, you will help your customers with strategic direction on mobility solutions, deployment planning and cost analysis. As one of the top wireless companies in the world, T-Mobile is an exciting place to work. T-Mobile highly values its employees and offers a very competitive compensation package.<br>
<br>
The nature of the Government Account Manager position is high-spirited and entrepreneurial. It offers substantial growth and opportunity to those who thrive in a competitive and fast-paced work environment. Proactive and responsible, these team members also relish the autonomy required to prioritize and troubleshoot on their own in an ever-changing professional field. The best Government Account Managers are aggressive, knowledgeable, and persuasive sales pros who don't easily take "no" for an answer. They make providing the very best customer service priority one.<br>
