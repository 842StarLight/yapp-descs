<h2>Sales Coordinator-PGE Hospitality</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>We are looking for a well-organized and responsible individual to provide support to the sales team. This individual will be coordinating with the sales and banquet teams and assist management where needed.</p>
<p>Responsibilities:</p>
<p>Process incoming leads via phone or email.</p>
<p>Assist with completion of banquet event orders.</p>
<p>Filing of important documents and assist with file organization.</p>
<p>Assist in the preparation and organizing of promotional materials for sales calls and events and assist in creation of marketing for advertising.</p>
<p>Individual will act as liaison to sales and banquet team to ensure events are properly set up and functioning.</p>
<p>Process reservations and support the front desk when necessary.</p>
<p>Requirements:</p>
<p>Experience as a sales coordinator or in the other administrative positions will be considered a plus.</p>
<p>Great computer skills including MS Office.</p>
<p>Well organized and responsible with an aptitude in problem solving with excellent verbal and written communication skills.</p>
<p>A team player with a high level of dedication.</p>
<p>Full time position. May work some weekends.</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul><li>Relevant: 1 year (Preferred)</li></ul>
