<b>Job Summary:</b> (Overall purpose and objective of the job)
<br><br>
The Marketing Specialist will organize and implement corporate marketing efforts in partnership with other senior Marketing Staff. Some of these efforts include custom materials, various marketing collateral, etc.
<br><br>
<b>Essential Functions:</b> (The reason this job exists is to perform these functions. These functions are performed regularly, seriously impacts other jobs, and there are a limited number of employees to whom performance of the function can be distributed).
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Write, design, and produce custom materials based on corporate brand standards, including but not limited to proposals, presentations, flyers, corporate collateral materials, templates, and custom sign designs</li>
<li>Create custom branding and designs for high-profile properties, including marketing collateral, logos, flyers, eflyers, offering memorandums, signage, and website coordination</li>
<li>Assist/coordinate high-level presentations including custom layout development, banner development/coordination, coordination of content population in partnership with brokerage staff</li>
<li>Write, design and develop concepts for print and digital advertising for newspapers, sponsorships, and other publications</li>
<li>Assist with design of tradeshow booths, company &amp; corporate events, and other promotional items as needed</li>
<li>Assist with interior design projects for office locations, coordinate &amp; track project details, and assist with furniture selections</li>
<li>Train brokerage staff on brand standards and technical skills</li>
<li>Coordinate outside print projects</li>
<li>Research print, web, technology, resources, and marketing tools/opportunities</li>
<li>Assist with corporate marketing initiatives as needed</li>
</ul>
