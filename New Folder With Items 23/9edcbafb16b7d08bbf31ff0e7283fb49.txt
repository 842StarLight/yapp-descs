<p><b>
Maintenance Technician Position Summary:</b></p>
<p>
The Maintenance Technician is responsible for ensuring equipment reliability by repairing and maintaining a wide range of processing equipment across the plant/bakery.</p>
<p><b>
Maintenance Technician Key Job Responsibilities:</b><b>
</b></p>
<ul>
<li> Ensure safety and food-safety by following all company, OSHA, and government rules and procedures including; Lock-out / Tag-out procedures, Good Manufacturing Practices (GMP’s), personal safety, and food safety/SQF Program standards.</li>
<li> Effectively support the Preventative Maintenance (PM) program with a goal of continually reducing unscheduled downtime.</li>
<li> Troubleshoot, diagnose, and develop sustainable solutions for issues with equipment to include; PLC controlled equipment, plant electrical, hydraulic/pneumatic, and mechanical systems including motors, instrumentation, distribution, and feed control systems.</li>
<li> Complete all assigned work orders within the time allotted. Maintain complete records of work performed using software, work orders and detailed logs.</li>
<li> Assist with equipment change overs and line startups, troubleshooting issues as needed.</li>
<li> Identify required parts and maintain current inventories, and parts reorder lists.</li>
<li> Act with a Continuous Improvement mindset seeking areas of opportunity for improvement and participating in cross-functional CI focused projects.</li>
</ul>

<p><b>
Maintenance Technician Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul><li> High School Diploma or GED is preferred</li></ul>
<ul><li> Associates degree and/or Technical School certification or training in basic electricity is preferred</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ability to troubleshoot and repair electrical systems up to 480v, 3 phase motors, hydraulic and pneumatic systems, and read and interpret electrical drawings</li></ul>
<ul><li> Working knowledge of and ability to troubleshoot PLC’s, VFD’s and HMI’s is preferred</li></ul>
<ul><li> Preferred - 3 years’ experience maintaining manufacturing systems and equipment in an automated plant as well as training/experience with compressed air systems, boilers, pneumatics, and hydraulics.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ability to work and communicate effectively in a fast-paced environment and adapt to frequent changes.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ability to understand and follow both written and verbal instructions with high level of detail and accuracy.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Willing to work irregular and/or rotating shifts, including split days off (non-consecutive days off), and holidays when needed IF NEEDED</li></ul>
<ul><li> Physical requirements: ability to work 8+ hour shifts, lift 50+ pounds, stand, climb, and/or stoop for prolonged periods of time, perform repetitive motions, and work in an environment with varying temperatures and humidity.</li></ul>
