<h2>Interactive Banking Specialist - Owensboro, KY</h2>

<div>
<p>Your career is here! Independence Bank is the fastest growing bank in the state of Kentucky.
</p>
<p></p>
<div>We’ve always treated our customers as friends and neighbors and we believe that it is just as important to treat every one of our employees the same way. We’ve created a unique environment where people love coming to work. <p></p>
<p>Come join this new and exciting role in our revolution and play a vital part in this newly created department.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The <b>Interactive Banking Specialist</b> is responsible for providing exceptional customer service via video interaction. This innovative new position will process and balance electronic customer transactions from our Interactive Banking Department located in Owensboro, KY.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Flexible shift options are available including 8, 10, or 12 hour shifts, Full or Part-time status and some Saturdays on a rotational basis ensuring department coverage from 6:00am-8:00pm CST Monday-Friday and 8:00 am-1:00pm CST Saturdays.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Essential Functions, Duties and Responsibilities include, but are not limited to:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul><li> Greet all customers via video/telecommunications in a timely and professional manner</li></ul>
<ul><li> Process all ITM transactions via video efficiently and accurately while providing quality service</li></ul>
<ul><li> Cash checks and payout money after verifying that signatures are correct, that written and numerical amounts agree, and that accounts have sufficient funds</li></ul>
<ul><li> Explain, promote, and sell specialized products</li></ul>
<ul><li> Responsible for following Independence Bank Security procedures</li></ul>
<ul><li> Responsible for maintaining confidentiality of customer accounts</li></ul>
<ul><li> Perform as a team member in allocating and coordinating workflow
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Answer inquiries regarding checking and savings accounts and other bank related products</li></ul>
<ul><li> Assist customers in solving account errors or problems</li></ul>
<ul><li> Promote and maintain positive relations with all contacts, customers, and potential customers</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions of this job.</p>

<p><b>
Knowledge Required to Perform Essential Job Functions:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul><li> <b>Customer and Personal Service</b> — Knowledge of principles and processes for providing customer and personal services. This includes customer needs assessment, meeting quality standards for services, and evaluation of customer satisfaction</li></ul>
<ul><li> <b>Economics and Accounting</b> — Knowledge of economic and accounting principles and practices, the financial markets, banking and the analysis and reporting of financial data</li></ul>
<ul><li> <b>Computers and Electronics</b> — Knowledge of electronic equipment and computer hardware and software, including applications and programming</li></ul>
<ul><li> <b>Sales and Marketing</b> — Knowledge of principles and methods for showing, promoting, and selling products or services. This includes marketing strategy and tactics, product demonstration, sales techniques, and sales control systems</li></ul>

<p><b>
Education, Experience</b> <b>and Qualifications:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul><li> High school diploma or equivalent required</li></ul>
<ul><li> Clerical, administrative, cash handling, or customer service experience preferred</li></ul>
<ul><li> Prefer background in Teller processes, Electronic Banking Services, and/or Customer Service/telephone skills (will have hands-on development to learn culture and core system that is unique to the Bank).</li></ul>
