<ul>

<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>About the job</b></h2>
<p>
Businesses that partner with Google come in all shapes, sizes and market caps, and no one Google advertising solution works for all. Your knowledge of online media combined with your communication skills and analytical abilities shapes how new and existing business grow. Using your influencing and relationship-building skills, you provide Google-caliber client service, research and market analysis. You anticipate how decisions are made, persistently explore and uncover the business needs of Google's key clients and understand how our range of product offerings can grow their business. Working with them, you set the vision and the strategy for how their advertising can reach thousands of users.</p>
<p>Google Customer Solutions sales teams are trusted advisors and competitive sellers who maintain a relentless focus on customer success by bringing the best Google has to offer to meet our advertisers’ and agency partners’ objectives. As a New Business Manager, you will empower agencies to strengthen and accelerate new business growth. This sales enablement will be achieved through a combination of avenues, including managing relationships, developing pipeline, establishing joint business plans, and optimizing sales processes. You will work closely with business development leaders and C-level executives at assigned agencies to improve their overall sales structure. You will identify areas for improvement in the customer success processes, training and development, pitching and negotiating, and overall leadership commitment.</p>
<p>
When our millions of advertisers and publishers are happy, so are we! Our Google Customer Solutions (GCS) team of entrepreneurial, enthusiastic and client-focused members are the "human face" of Google, helping entrepreneurs both individually and broadly build their online presence and grow their businesses. We are dedicated to growing the unique needs of advertising companies. Our teams of strategists, analysts, advisers and support specialists collaborate closely to spot and analyze customer needs and trends. Together, we create and implement business plans broadly for all types of businesses.</p>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Responsibilities</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Manage and grow relationships with agency business development teams, executives, and C/B-level leaders with the goal of developing joint business and action plans and driving business/sales process improvements.</li>
<li>Deliver product sales skills training embracing a mix of sales methodologies and processes.</li>
<li>Identify growth areas for agencies to drive new business and develop long-term pipeline; includes partnering to build new program structures, and product go-to-market strategies.</li>
<li>Synchronize your assigned agencies' customer acquisition plans with the Google Agency team and internal cross-functional teams.</li>
<li>Meet quarterly targets assigned to a portfolio of top agency partners, while delivering outstanding partner and advertiser satisfaction.</li>
</ul>
