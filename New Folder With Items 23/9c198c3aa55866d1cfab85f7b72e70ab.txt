<h2>SAP Business Analyst Senior</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><p>As a Senior SAP ERP Business Analyst, this means that you will bring your SAP experience in the Aerospace and Defense sector to facilitate the development, implementation, and sustainment of the SAP/ERP system and processes working across multiple project teams. You would provide the following capability to the project team;<br>
Analyze supply chain program business processes and procedures for improvement and system applicability.<br>
Provide clearly defined solutions supporting Performance Based Logistics contracting methodology for configuration of SAP and supporting business processes.<br>
Perform gap analysis and system re-engineering of SAP supply chain management activities including; Sales &amp; Distribution, Procurement, Inventory Management, Maintenance Repair &amp; Overhaul, and Packing Handling Storage &amp; Transportation.<br>
Responsible for system design, development, configuration, unit testing, operational assessment readiness for SAP.<br>
Will author functional specifications for enhancements, conversions, master data records, reports, and custom transactions.<br>
Responsible for unit testing, integration testing, documentation and training of the end user community.<br>
Develop training documentation and scripts using SAP methodologies<br>
We have begun an Agile transformation and agile experience is appreciated but not necessary<br>
<b>Basic Qualifications:</b><br>
Experience with SAP modules (Sales and Distribution or Plant Maintenance or Material Management or Logistics Information Systems) or working in the aircraft product support field (sustainment or post delivery modification).<br>
<b>Desired Skills:</b><br>
Experience working with Air Force, Navy and Marine corps customers<br>
Experience working with international customers<br>
Experience Facilitating change and learning activities<br>
Experience working with Fiori and/or Personas<br>
Experience working with Enterprise Asset Management (EAM) or Enterprise Warehouse Management (EWM)<br>
Agile, Scrum, Version One<br>
