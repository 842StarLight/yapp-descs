<h2>Senior Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>We are looking for experienced sales candidates that are comfortable educating executives and decision makers on the benefits of the Planalytics service in the prospect’s business. We seek candidates with a multi-year track record of success building and converting sales opportunities.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop an integrated sales and marketing approach to increase the pipeline of qualified leads to amplify volume and speed of deals</li>
<li>Manage your business inside Planalytics focus on the sales process from qualified lead to close</li>
<li>Visit prospective and existing clients to position and sell the company’s products and services</li>
<li>Coordinate and prepare business proposals, as well as deliver effective sales presentations</li>
<li>Share the learnings from the market back to management</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Proven new business development skills</li>
<li>An excellent track record of presenting, negotiating, and closing business</li>
<li>5-10 years of demonstrated success selling business SaaS products or related services</li>
<li>Experience or Knowledge in Retail, Manufacturing or Supply Chain Planning and Forecasting</li>
<li>Enthusiasm, confidence, “Hunter” attitude, business solution/ROI sales approach, and strong professional presence</li>
<li>An ability to travel nationally</li>
</ul>
