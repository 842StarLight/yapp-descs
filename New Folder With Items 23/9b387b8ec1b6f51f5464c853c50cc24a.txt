<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Position Description
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Serco NA is currently looking for a FMS Senior Combat Systems Engineer to provide expertise and direction in the development or modification of combat system hardware and software. Responsible for the instruction, assigning, and overseeing the performance of less experienced engineers. Coordinates activities with other sections of the Program Office.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Experience in supporting Foreign Military Sales (FMS) on U.S. Navy programs is highly desired.</li>
<li>Experience working with foreign military personnel is preferred.</li>
<li>Experience with Foreign Military Sales (FMS) and Direct Commercial Sales (DCS) to foreign militaries is preferred.</li>
<li>Experience supporting NAVSEA PMS 326, PMS 325, PMS 333, or SEA 21I is preferred.</li>
<li>Familiarity and experience with ITAR, FCPA, and other regulations and laws associated with international work is highly preferred.</li>
</ul>
