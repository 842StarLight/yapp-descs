<h2>Estimator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Salary based on experience: range $45,000 - $65,000 per year</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Cost estimators collect and analyze data in order to estimate the time, money, materials and labor required to manufacture a product or provide a service.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Duties:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Identify and quantify cost factors, such as production time, materials, and labor expenses.</li>
<li>Read blueprints and technical documents in order to prepare estimates.</li>
<li>Collaborate with engineers, carpenters, purchasing department, project managers, clients, and creative team on estimates.</li>
<li>Consult with industry experts to discuss estimates and resolve issues.</li>
<li>Use computer software to calculate estimates.</li>
<li>Evaluate a product?s cost-effectiveness or profitability.</li>
<li>Recommend ways to make a product more cost effective or profitable.</li>
<li>Work with sales teams to prepare estimates and bids for clients.</li>
</ul>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<p><b>Skills:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Strong math skills including basic arithmetic, algebra and geometry.</li>
<li>Knowledge of Microsoft Office products with a focus on Microsoft Word and Excel.</li>
<li>Strong typing skills.</li>
<li>Knowledge of building code.</li>
<li>Ability to read and navigate plans and specifications.</li>
<li>Good interpersonal and written communication skills.</li>
<li>Must be detail-oriented.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High School Diploma or equivalent; college degree preferred.</li>
<li>Three-five years of experience with estimating in the construction and/or manufacturing fields.</li>
<li>Experience in trade show/custom event industry a plus.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Physical Demands:</b></p>
<p>
This is primarily a sedentary office position which requires the ability to sometimes bend, stoop, reach, lift, move and carry material weighing 25 pounds or less. Finger dexterity is required to operate computer keyboard, calculator and telephone equipment.</p>
</div>
