<h2>Sr. Scaled Agile Framework (SAFe) Coach</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>As 321 Gang’s Scaled Agile Framework (SAFe) training and consulting business continues to grow, we need to help our compliance-driven hardware, software and firmware engineering clients meet their challenging program and product objectives. We are looking for experienced, highly independent and self-motivated technical professionals to help our clients succeed with their SAFe implementations.</p>
<p>
This high-leverage position is for those who can work with 321 Gang’s sellers to help communicate the value of SAFe to prospects, as well as successfully lead and manage large scale SAFe implementations through a combination of training and services.</p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Position Responsibilities:</b></h3>
<p>
SAFe Coaches are critical members of 321 Gang’s technical team whose primary role is to provide strategic consulting, training, and services to 321 Gang’s customers. Specific responsibilities include the following:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Work closely with the 321 Gang sales teams to develop Agile transformation plans to meet the customer objectives.</li>
<li>
Define and present to customers product and technology training on both tool solutions and modern software engineering best practices.</li>
<li>
Work on customer engagements to assist in the successful transformations</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Required Qualifications:</b></h3>
<p>
The ideal candidate for this position is an experienced customer-facing professional with a very strong technical and consulting background:</p>
<ul>
<li>
US resident with the legal right to work in the United States</li>
<li>
8+ years of experience with working within an Agile framework, including Scrum, Kanban and/or Lean</li>
<li>
3+ years of experience as a Scrum Master, Product Owner or Agile Coach</li>
<li>
Scaled Agile SAFe Program Consultant (SPC) certification</li>
<li>
10+ years’ experience providing pre-sales support, solution consulting and/or training for large software and/or systems engineering organizations</li>
<li>
Ability to oversee multiple clients’ SAFe implementations, managing on-site coaches</li>
<li>
Familiarity with mainstream engineering practices/processes including requirements management, V&amp;V, model-driven design (MDD), Model-Based Systems Engineering (MBSE), configuration and change management</li>
<li>
Knowledge of software/product ship cycles, release planning and tracking</li>
<li>
Independent self-starter able to work efficiently with minimal oversight in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>
Adept at customer interactions, including presentations, training, and software implementations</li>
<li>
Excellent presentation, analytical, problem-solving and communication skills (written and verbal)</li>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree or equivalent in engineering, computer science, mathematics, or a related field</li>
</ul>
