<h2>Social Media Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Works to increase brandawareness and customer acquisition through active management of onlinepaid ad campaigns including Facebook and GoogleAdwords. This position is responsible for campaign strategy, execution,analytics and reporting. We are looking for abold, data-driven individual who is not afraid of taking calculated risks togrow the business to new heights.</p>
<p>This position alsoparticipates in Social Media team’s daily activities such as publishingmessaging to all applicable social media channels, content development,trending topic research, identifying influencers, and responding to communitymember inquires.</p>
<p><b>R*</b>ESPONSIBILITIES AND ACCOUNTABILITIES - *essential job functions include but are not limitedto:</p>
<ul>
<li>Build, manage, and scale Facebook ad campaigns that meet or exceed designated KPIs</li>
<li>Strategize and manage lead acquisition strategy and create a robust traffic system/sales funnel for inbound marketing and advertising efforts</li>
<li>Utilize advanced features within Facebook Ad Manager and Google Analytics to improve campaign performance: Pixel, Retargeting, Ad Placements, Ad Targeting, Custom Audiences, and Look-a-like Audiences</li>
<li>Manage media buy on various advertising channels, primarily focused on Facebook Ads and Google Adwords, but may also include other acquisition channels</li>
<li>Develop and maintain a complete knowledge of (ever-changing) Facebook and Google tools for building campaigns, audiences, conversion tracking, and other tools used to deliver results</li>
<li>Leverage communication platforms such as Email and Facebook Messenger to augment lead acquisition strategy</li>
<li>Collaborate and support the Social Media team and other designers and videographers to produce creatives</li>
<li>Run A/B split tests to optimize campaign performance and gain valuable insights about our customers’ through a data-driven approach</li>
<li>Analyze ad performance and demonstrate a complete understanding of all available numbers in the Facebook Ad Manager and Google Analytics dashboards to optimize towards KPIs</li>
<li>Monitor and optimize campaign performance by clearly reporting results, trends, problems, and opportunities – with focus on ROI</li>
<li>Work with the Social Media team to develop conversion assets (emails, content and landing pages) to align with paid acquisition and marketing funnel strategy</li>
<li>Gather data on consumer, competitor, and market conditions</li>
<li>Prepare reports and present results to management that include data from the ad campaign paired with sales data from our CRM platform.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>MINIMUM QUALIFICATIONS: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree with social media experience.</li>
<li>Highly analytical, data-driven individual</li>
<li>Detail-oriented, Organized, Self-motivated and Focused on Success</li>
<li>Excellent Verbal and Written Communication Skills (including proofreading), Reporting, Multi-tasking</li>
<li>Paid Facebook Advertising experience (Blueprint Certification a plus)</li>
<li>Website analytics and conversion rate optimization (Google Analytics Certification a plus)</li>
<li>eCommerce advertising experience</li>
<li>Salesforce Dashboard and Reporting experience a plus</li>
<li>Experience with Excel</li>
<li>Familiar with Photoshop</li>
<li>Video creation/editing experience a plus</li>
</ul>
