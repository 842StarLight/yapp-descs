<h2>Digital Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Diray Media is looking for a talented individual with experience in programmatic advertising, ad tech or digital media. This role will work closely with clients and the digital team to oversee the execution and optimization of programmatic advertising campaigns across desktop, mobile, social and television platforms, as well as provide critical day-to-day support and service and find opportunities to create additional value for our clients. As a key member of our team, the Digital Account Executive will regularly work with all divisions including sales, business development and operations. Candidates must be comfortable taking risks and have an entrepreneurial spirit that will allow them to be successful in a dynamic, fast paced environment. Applicants must also exhibit effective project management skills; keep account team abreast of timelines and deadlines.</div>
