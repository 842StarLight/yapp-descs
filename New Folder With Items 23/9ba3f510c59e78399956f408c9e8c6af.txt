<h2>Production Planner</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Saint-Gobain Performance Plastics is one of seven businesses within the Innovative Materials Sector, which is spear-heading the advancement of innovation within the Group. Performance Plastics is a recognized leader in the advanced polymer technology market with access to state-of-the-art manufacturing and research &amp; development facilities around the world.
<br><br>
Through close partnerships with our business leaders, our team works to support the achievement of business goals while emphasizing the PPL values of accountability, trust, initiative, respect and helping.
<br><br>
Saint-Gobain High Performance Solutions in seeking an experience Production Planner for our Coated Fabrics operation in Merrimack, NH. The Production Planner is responsible to implement, monitor, measure and report production schedules to assure that matched sets of materials and capacities are available to meet the needs of customers and that production schedules are met. This includes management of all planning and production execution data including the creation of work orders, identification and management of priorities, capacity constraints, production monitoring, etc.
<br><br>
Properly execute planning through our ERP system
<br>Create, review, amend, and maintain attainable and effective material and production plans
<br>Work in conjunction with Customer Service, Sales, Production and Purchasing to support the production plan.
<br>Review and plan transfer orders to subsidiary sites.
<br>Attend the daily manufacturing execution meeting and follow-up on any open action items from the meeting.
<br>When necessary, prioritize schedules to ensure customer orders are meet.
<br>Work with customer service to manage customer order priority.
<br>Provide production completion dates to customer service for expedites and one off orders.
<br>Evaluate and maintain safety stock levels.
<br>Update/manage lead times in ERP system
<br>Participate in developing best practices for the planning department.
<br>Support and execute continuous improvement in all processes of the planning department with focus on lean manufacturing.
<br>Communicate with document control group to insure BOM and Route accuracy in order to manage a capacity planning.
<br><br>
<b>Essential Functions:</b><br>
Develop and implement the production schedule for the manufacturing work cell, and purchasing function taking into consideration available capacity, material availability, customer order requirements, best production order sequence, and above all, the financial targets established for revenue.
<br>Review material/purchase order due dates to ensure late deliveries are expedited in accordance with production schedule requirements. Verify status of components and delinquent material, maintaining shortage records.
<br>Research, analyze and interpret sales, bookings, and backlog data; documenting and reporting status, root-causes, and/or recommended corrective actions to management as necessary.
<br>Identifies and brings to resolution any production demand/supply imbalances in both volume and mix.
<br>Work as team member on continuous improvement projects, including but not limited to visual factory floor controls, 5S, and lean enterprise solutions.
<br><br>
The successful candidate will have the following qualifications:
<br><br>
Bachelor's Degree in Supply Chain, Logistics, or Business - consideration may be given for someone with a lesser degree and 5+ years of experience
<br>3+ years of production planning or scheduling in a manufacturing environment, preferably continuous production
<br>Experience working in an ISO certified environment
<br>In-depth knowledge of MRP/ERP systems, preferably QAD/Factivity
<br>Strong communication skills and follow-through
<br>Experience in Contract Manufacturing operations
<br>Strong computer skills to include Microsoft Office
<br>Strong analytical aptitude and problem solving skills
<br>Must be collaborative, with a focus on win-win
<br>Must be results-oriented, self-motivated, flexible, have a strong sense of urgency and proactive
<br>APICS CPIM certification is preferred
