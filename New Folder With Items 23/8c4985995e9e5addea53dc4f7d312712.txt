<p><b>MBM ENTERPRISE STAFFING</b></p>
<p><b>- SALES POSITION </b></p>
<p><b>OVERVIEW: </b></p>
<p>MBM Enterprise Staffing is looking for a part-time salesperson to help our business expand its customer base and obtain MSA’s from companies that require 3rd party contracting services. Oil/Gas/Construction experience is preferred. The main objective of this role is to open new accounts and obtain MSA's.</p>
<p><b>COMPENSATION: </b></p>
<p>This is a commission only based position. Once a new client and MSA is obtained, you will make the first $1,000 of the weekly profit. To put this in perspective; with just one profitable MSA obtained, you will make $52,000 a year. Once you obtain your second profitable MSA, you will make $104,000 a year. Once you obtain the third profitable MSA, you will make $156,000 a year. The weekly pay commission maxes out at $5,000 a week ($260,000/YR). After you are maxed out at $5,000 weekly, you are eligible for a bonus plan for any future MSA’s obtained.</p>
