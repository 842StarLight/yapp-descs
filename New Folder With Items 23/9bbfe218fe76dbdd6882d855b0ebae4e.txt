<p><b>
General:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Plan and execute the credit union's annual educational programs in conjunction to align with CCF's strategic plan to ensure appropriate ongoing development of all employees to meet changing organizational needs.</li>
<li>
Participate in meetings, seminars, and training sessions to obtain information useful for designing and facilitation effective training programs; and integrates information into programs.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Coordinator (15%)</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Maintain annual training calendar of events and communicate required attendance. Coordinates internal presenters and presentations.</li>
<li>
Manages online training systems to include vendor recommendations, site administration, staff training plan development and monitoring training progress.</li>
<li>
Develop and tracks training effectiveness and employee knowledge retention.</li>
<li>
Track the progress of trainees through online tests, observation, and class participation. Updates, maintains and track employee participation in training sessions and correlated pre and post work training requirements.</li>
<li>
Coordinate training for the Board of Directors' required annual compliance training.</li>
<li>
Provide feedback to managers regarding employee class performance, their display of the knowledge learned in the class, and any after-class support that is needed for them to become proficient in the skills.</li>
<li>
Deploys annual required compliance related training and maintains scores; addresses performance concerns with appropriate manager.</li>
<li>
Conducts proactive training need-assessments, gap analysis, and evaluations of current training effectiveness through surveys, solicited feedback, and observations.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Instructional Designer (35%)</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Develop, prepare, coordinate, organize, implement and evaluate training resources and materials.</li>
<li>
Develop and maintain in-house product knowledge materials and coordinate e-learning modules in conjunction with these materials.</li>
<li>
Construct educational curriculum with instructional end goals, including engaging learning activities and compelling course content.</li>
<li>
Understand and execute use of instructional design methods, including ADDIE or SAM, to develop training programs.</li>
<li>
Develop learning content utilizing a variety of training modalities and creativity to support the diverse needs of learners.</li>
<li>
Work with subject matter experts to identify target audience's training needs.</li>
<li>
Determine the criteria used to judge learner's performance and develop applicable assessment instruments</li>
<li>
Explore alternative deliverable types including micro-learning, video, and burst/boost to engage the modern learner and drive retention.</li>
<li>
Evaluates and recommends appropriate external instructional materials such as printed and electronic modules, visual aids, videos, etc., by monitoring the offerings available from outside sources.</li>
<li>
Support and structure new hire, continuing education, and career progression development initiatives.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Facilitation (40%)</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Facilitates new employee orientation sessions and provides on-the-job training, including both in-person and virtual training as it relates to the core processor system upgrades, teller transactions, lending related processes, and new accounts processes, Business Accounts, and IRA systems, etc.</li>
<li>
Ensure new hires have a good working knowledge of Outlook, phone system, Intranet, Time and Attendance system, Online training systems, compliance requirements, and general business programs/software at their designated workstation.</li>
<li>
Assist with and/or conducts training sessions both in-person and virtually on new products, policies and procedures and implementations of designated systems.</li>
<li>
Assist with coordination and facilitation of offsite training events including employee/team development days.</li>
<li>
Create and facilitate innovative learning solutions for referrals, sales solutions and member service expectations.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Minimum Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Excellent organized and engaging communication and presentation skills.</li>
<li>
Strong attention to detail.</li>
<li>
Ability to act as a role model as demonstrated by professional business appearance and enthusiastic tone and demeanor.</li>
<li>
Bachelor's Degree in Education, Training and Development, or equivalent.</li>
<li>
Intermediate working proficiency in Word, PowerPoint and related training software.</li>
<li>
Minimum 2 years' experience in financial services/banking/credit union preferred.</li>
<li>
Minimum 3 years' experience in employee development/training design and facilitation.</li>
<li>
Ability to be bonded.<br>
</li>
</ul>
