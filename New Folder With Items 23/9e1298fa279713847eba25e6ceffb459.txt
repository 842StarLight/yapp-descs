<h2>National Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>National Account Manager</b></p>
<br>
<p>
<b>
</b><b>The Opportunity</b></p>
<p>
The National Account Manager must build and maintain positive, trust-based relationships at all levels of the account, including senior executives. Successful individuals in this role are able to discuss business strategies and understand and explain complex technical solutions. The National Account Manager must coordinate and leverage multiple functions across Axcient, yet remain personably accountable for everything related to his/her partners. Success is measured by the total penetration of Axcient and customer feedback.<br>
<br>
</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Building and maintaining senior management/C-level relationships with key partners to gain an understanding of their businesses and key priorities to identify joint regional sales strategies and tactics for achievement of sales targets</li>
<li>Influencing the adoption of Axcient, Business Continuity and Disaster Recovery (BCDR), Office365 Backup, and Secure File Sync &amp; Share solutions through strong relationships with identified targeted partners</li>
<li>Actively supporting targeted named Managed Service Provider (MSP) partners and Value Added Resellers (VARs) through all stages of the sales cycle including sales planning, delivery of sales programs/incentives to promote awareness and stimulate demand for<br>
 Axcient</li>
<li>Conducting ‘deep dive’ strategic account campaigns to support your accounts, enabling your partners to experience the full Axcient value proposition, allowing them to deploy deeper into their organization</li>
<li>Building and managing a sales pipeline tracking and activities through the various stages of the sales funnel process</li>
<li>Developing and delivering various knowledge transfer activities, support technical product training and information to named MSPs and VAR partners</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree or equivalent,</li>
<li>5 years or more outside enterprise software sales experience</li>
<li>Experience developing and implementing account plans aligned to corporate strategy</li>
<li>Successful experience as a new-business "hunter", able to introduce technologies and maintain long-term relationships</li>
<li>Track record of successful personal sales pipeline management and results</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to establish and build rapport in senior customer relationships</li>
<li>Strong business acumen and technical knowledge to succinctly align customer needs with the Axcient value proposition</li>
<li>Proven ability to respectfully engage multiple functional areas across an organization</li>
<li>Strong technical aptitude and ability to keep it simple</li>
<li>Personal goal setting, time management, and drive to succeed</li>
<li>Integrity, humility, team-orientation, and drive for performance</li>
</ul>
