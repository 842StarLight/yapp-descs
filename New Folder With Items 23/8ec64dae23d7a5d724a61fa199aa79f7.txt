<h2>Technical Support Analyst - YouTube</h2>

<p>Do you like solving complex problems? Are you an analytical thinker with a knack for non-linear logic? Do you enjoy interacting with people? YouTube is looking for a Technical Support Analyst who can provide technical user support for one of Youtube’s core internal business tools.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provide technical support to users of Youtube’s internal business tools.</li>
<li>Optimize existing processes, identify areas for improvement, measure progress.</li>
<li>Create and maintain technical documentation for support and tools integration.</li>
<li>Develop web applications, scripts, and back-end services for Youtube’s internal stakeholders.</li>
<li>Manage time effectively with minimal supervision to deliver complex projects on-time.</li>
<li>Document technical processes for training purposes</li>
<li>Meet SLA requirements for problem resolution</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Minimum qualifications: </b></p>

<li>BS degree (CS or equivalent technical degree</li>
<li>1-2 years experience in previous front line support role</li>
<li>Provided technical support to a large team</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills (English)</li>
<li>Running scripts in a Linux environment</li>
<li>Working knowledge of how databases functions</li>
<li>Experience triaging and escalating bugs and tickets</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Preferred qualifications: </b></p>
