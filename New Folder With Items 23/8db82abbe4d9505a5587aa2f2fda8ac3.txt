<h2>Office/Warehouse Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Job Description</p>
<p>Medical Supply Distributor seeks qualified and energetic candidate for Entry Level Office and Warehouse helper position. Monday to Friday 9AM to 6PM</p>
<p>Position Description</p>
<p>Helping warehouse department with tasks, picking, packing, receiving. Responsible for interacting with customers, company sales and/or service representatives to handle a variety of sales or post-sales service functions. Determines the status of equipment returns, repairs, replacements, sales orders, delivery schedules, etc. May schedule field service repair calls. Handles requests for additional company materials. Maintains records of returns, schedule changes, product enhancements or changes, product pricing and resolves return credit problems. Enter customer orders using company software system.</p>
<p>Additional Knowledge &amp; Skills</p>
<p>Reading, writing, arithmetic, good communication skills, PC and word-processing skills, and some knowledge of the company products</p>
