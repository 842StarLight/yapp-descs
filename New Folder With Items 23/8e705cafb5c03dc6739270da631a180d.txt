<h2>Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Our team is looking for a <b>Project</b><b> Manager.</b> The Project Manager for Support the PMO's mission through successful directing, planning, execution, and delivery of EagleView internal and customer Projects.</p>
<p><b>
Primary responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Pre-Contract
</b><ul>
<li>Work with Sales to determine project viability
</li>
<li>Support proposal, quote, and contract generation with preparation of maps, shapefiles, related geospatial data, and use of project management tools such as Gantt charts</li>
<li>Develop and garner alignment on project requirements, including scope, schedule, and deliverables</li>
<li>Assist with RFPs (Requests for Proposals/Pricing) as needed</li>
<li>Review and sign off on contract parameters</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Project Management</b>
<ul>
<li>Manage the flow of information to internal and external customers including, but not limited to contract details, deliverables, project status, project history, timelines, and data quality</li>
<li>Provide clear and frequent communication and updates both internally and externally to drive a positive project outcome</li>
<li>Develop and maintain a consultative relationship with clients</li>
<li>Coordinate project details and requirements, expectations, and issues internally with Sales, Contracts, and Production departments</li>
<li>Proactively manage risks and propose solutions</li>
<li>Provide and oversee build materials and resources for internal departments (Flight Operations, Geomatics, et cetera as applicable to project type)</li>
<li>Ensure quality and completeness of project deliverables through direct review</li>
<li>Support Program Managers’ overall reporting by providing project-level detail and status</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<b>Project Delivery and Ongoing Support</b>
<ul>
<li>Ensure delivery to established project schedule and requirements</li>
<li>Manage customer concerns and inquiries until resolved</li>
<li>Support and/or present at in-person customer visits/meetings</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<b>Salesforce Management</b>
<ul>
<li>Update, document and maintain project milestones and communications in Salesforce</li>
<li>Provide building instructions and product specifics for internal stakeholders</li>
<li>Maintain project health reporting statuses
</li>
<li>Ensure quality and completeness of data supporting PMO KPIs</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<b>Product Expertise</b>
<ul>
<li>Develop a strong working understanding of the company’s technologies and processes</li>
<li>Have working knowledge of the EV Products and Markets, including but not limited to:
<ul>
<li>Imagery/AccuPlus/Ortho Only/IR</li>
<li>LiDAR and elevation products</li>
<li>HCA/Planimetrics</li>
<li>ChangeFinder/Building Outlines</li>
<li>Government</li>
<li>Infrastructure/Energy</li>
<li>Federal</li>
<li>Insurance</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<b>Training and Documentation</b>
<ul>
<li>Active participant in new hire and Sales training</li>
<li>Write, update, review, and assist with managing the PMO’s training materials specifically pertaining to Project Management</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><b>Other duties as assigned</b></li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Previous Project Manager experience preferred</li>
<li>Previous customer service and/or experience with customer engagement preferred</li>
<li>Excellent organizational and planning skills - ability to keep accurate detailed records and communicate project needs in a timely manor</li>
<li>Ability to work efficiently and respond quickly to internal and external inquiries</li>
<li>Demonstrates a strong work ethic – completes work in a timely fashion, takes on challenges willingly
</li>
<li>Trustworthy and an excellent team player – Pitches in to help others without question, shares tips/skills to help the team work more efficiently, and promotes a supportive environment for all colleagues</li>
<li>Demonstrates flexibility and adaptability – ability to switch gears when priorities and workloads change in a dynamic work environment</li>
<li>Resourceful - Ability to quickly learn applications such as ArcGIS (i.e., ESRI's ArcMap), database programs, and Salesforce.com</li>
<li>Punctuality and willingness to work until the task is completed</li>
<li>Complete comfort with Microsoft Windows operating systems and Office software</li>
<li>BA/BS in Business, Communications, GIS, Imaging Science or related disciplines with 1-2+ years’ experience preferred</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
