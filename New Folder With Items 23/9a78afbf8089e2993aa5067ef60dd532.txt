<h2>High School Outbound Marketing Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>At CIEE, our mission is central to what we do, but committed, inspired people make us who we are. The work we do helps people learn more about themselves, grow, and bridge differences with others. We help people develop new ways to support themselves as they push their own boundaries, make new connections, and gain fresh perspectives.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Our success depends on recruiting and retaining the very best professionals. If you are passionate about international education, a self-starter with high levels of energy and autonomy, and dedicated to exceeding expectations at every challenge, we have an opportunity that could be right for you.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Summary of Position:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>The Marketing Coordinator will help support the High School Outbound (HSOB) marketing team deliver the overall HSOB marketing strategy and enrollment goals. More specifically, the Marketing Coordinator will coordinate with the entire HSOB team to oversee accurate program representation throughout our digital eco-system and print material, and help us improve communication with students and parents in the post-acceptance cycle to improve customer retention rates, enhance the customer experience and help the team set our participants for success in their study abroad experience across all all high school outbound businesses (summer abroad, semester abroad, gap year abroad).</p>
<p></p>
<p>The Marketing Coordinator is a newly created position, reporting director to the HOSB Marketing Director and working alongside another Marketing Coordinator, in charge of digital campaign delivery and new material development.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Together as a team, we’ll be able to take our program representation to the next level, help the HSOB team achieve ambitious growth goals, and ultimately allow expand the horizon of hundreds of new U.S. high school students every year. We’re looking for candidates with strong verbal and written communication skills, excellent organizational and project management skills as well as experience in managing blogging and social media platforms. The ideal candidate will also have a personal study abroad or travel experience he can draw from,and demonstrate a true passion for our mission</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Primary Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>? Program Representation:</p>
<ul>
<li>Coordinate with internal teams (HSOB Portland team, CIEE Web team, CIEE study centers) to monitor and update program representation across our digital ecosystem and print material (including but not limited to: CIEE website, CIEE program sell sheets, CIEE blogs, 3rd party review sites…etc.).</li>
<li>Manage the curation, production and distribution of existing and new content across owned and earned digital platforms. Content could include original web/social copy, blog posts, tweets, status updates, pins, photos, videos, and infographics.</li>
<li>Identify internal opportunities to develop original content.</li>
<li>Help manage cataloging of existing and incoming photos/videos on Asset Management platform</li>
<li>Add in about flow of communication with parents and any other important post-confirmation constituents</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>? Customer Retention:</p>
<ul>
<li>Help HSOB team enhance communication towards program participants and their parents in the post-acceptance and pre-departure phases of our sale cycle, to limit attrition and set up our participants for success.</li>
<li>Support HSOB team with the conception, production and distribution of new participant pre-departure resources and materials (including but not limted to predeparture readings or newsletter development and digital delivery, participant “swag” production and delivery…etc.)</li>
<li>Leverage measurement tools to develop goals, provide progress reports complete with insights, and use results and learnings to improve on those metrics through testing and new initiatives.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>? Participant engagement:</p>
 <ul>
<li>Collaborate with the HSOB Marketing team to develop and manage the editorial calendars for all three lines of business (high school summer abroad, high school abroad and Gap).</li>
 <li>Help manage our online community by posting material, but also monitoring audience response, developing replies and adjust editorial calendar to best cater to the audience’s response.</li>
 <li>Develop and manage regular participant engagement contests to harvest fresh user generated content and fuel our editorial and promotional calendars ? Drive blogger recruitment efforts across HSOB businesses and manage blogger relationships</li>
 <li>Manage alumni outreach to mine content and organize opportunities for collaboration</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>General Knowledge &amp; Skill Requirements:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>BA or BS with focus on communication (Advertising/Marketing)</li>
<li>1-2 years of relation professional experience in marketing, public relations, or advertising.</li>
<li>Experience managing social media accounts including - but not limited to - Facebook, Instagram, Pinterest, Twitter, YouTube, Snapchat, Google+ or LinkedIn.</li>
<li>Experience writing, editing and crafting content for blogs and social media</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills, both verbal and written</li>
<li>Strong attention to detail</li>
<li>Candidate should be web savvy</li>
<li>Proficiency in the full Windows Office Suite (especially Microsoft Excel, Microsoft Word, Microsoft Power Point and Microsoft Outlook)</li>
<li>Basic understanding of media planning and buying strategy options in integrated communications</li>
<li>Basic understanding of the video production process to be able to coordinate effectively with copywriters, videographers and editors</li>
<li>Desire and enthusiasm to learn new skills and processes</li>
<li>Ability to work both independently with little supervision, as well as within a team environment and across departments</li>
<li>Excellent time management skills with the ability to manage multiple tasks simultaneously in a dynamic, fast-paced, deadline-oriented environment</li>
<li>Basic understanding of Adobe Photoshop to allow to effectively coordinate with designers and print production professionals</li>
<li>Experience leveraging social media management and analytics tools, including, but not limited to HootSuite, Radian 6, or Google Analytics</li>
<li>Study abroad or travel experience is a plus</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
