<h2>Product Marketing Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Works on the planning and implementation of projects that support the marketing function. Interacts directly with product managers to identify new marketing opportunities, prepares regular marketing activity reports and responds to questions from the sales organization. Works individually or with a team in recommending action, scheduling and planning projects, estimating cost and managing projects to completion. May identify, evaluate and recommend marketing opportunities consistent with product line objectives; provides pre- and post-sale marketing support to distributors, OEMs and resellers; and supports public relations through researching, writing and editing press releases. Responsibilities may also include: coordinating trade show activities, website development, developing future e-commerce strategies, and tracking of marketing campaigns to evaluate results and provide recommendations for future marketing promotions.
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Draw on and coordinate resources of corporate marketing group, as needed, to plan, oversee, develop, execute, track and be accountable for assigned product-group based marketing campaigns and related activities.
</li>
<li>Provide relevant campaign content to the corporate marketing calendar.
</li>
<li>Participate in and provide regular input regarding responsibility area (i.e. product group) to Itron's strategic marketing planning process.
</li>
<li>Serve as member of Corporate Marketing Team to ensure overall coordination of marketing efforts in accordance with established processes and standards.
</li>
<li>Work with corporate marketing team and the product line management team to ensure related marketing activities and programs are executed in a timely and effective manner.
</li>
<li>Identify, plan and provide budgetary requirements for product-group related marketing needs and activities.
</li>
<li>Provide continual tracking, analysis and reporting of product-group specific marketing efforts.
</li>
<li>Serve as corporate marketing group's primary subject matter expert on product group needs and opportunities.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<b>Experience: </b>This position requires a minimum of 5 years of related experience<br>
<b>Education: </b>Bachelor's degree in related field or equivalent experience.<br>
<b>Certification(s):</b><br>
