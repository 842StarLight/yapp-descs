<div>Property Frameworks, the property management subsidiary of NRT LLC, is currently seeking an Accounts Payable Coordinator for its single-family residential business. If you are organized, have a strong attention to detail and thrive on providing exceptional service, this position may be for you!<br>
<br>
Essential Job Responsibilities:<br>
<ul>
<li>
Operate effectively within a full cycle accounts payable environment</li>
<li>
Process invoices and provide other clerical support necessary to pay client obligations
</li>
<li>Clarify any questionable invoice items</li>
<li>
Obtain proper support and/or information regarding invoice payments</li>
<li>
Assemble invoices to be completed for payment</li>
<li>
Review invoices for satisfactory payment approval</li>
<li>
Maintain copies of invoices, support documents, and approvals necessary for files</li>
<li>
Paying vendors by scheduling checks and electronic payments</li>
</ul>
Job Requirements:<br>
<ul>
<li>
Minimum 1-year accounts payable experience</li>
<li>
Strong organizational skills and ability to prioritize duties</li>
<li>
Excellent attention to detail and ability to multi-task</li>
<li>
Excellent communications skills both verbal and written</li>
<li>
Highly accountable and have proven follow-through skills</li>
<li>
Must be able to work well under pressure</li>
<li>
Must be dependable and willing to do what it takes to get the job done</li>
<li>
Experience with Property Management industry and/or PropertyWare software is a plus</li>
