<p>In order to build our company and make it as strong as it can be, we're always looking for people just like you - top, qualified talent eager to join our team. If you're interested in a rewarding career at one of the most innovative and fastest-growing fiber cement companies in the world, we're interested in you. We currently have an opening for a Commercial Sales Manager in <b>Atlanta, Charlotte, Orlando, Birmingham, or Raleigh/Durham.</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>GENERAL FUNCTION</b></p>
<p>The Commercial Sales Manager is an outside sales role that achieves maximum sales profitability, growth and account penetration within the commercial segment in an assigned region by effectively selling the company’s products and/or related services. Reporting to the Regional Sales Manager, this position personally contacts and secures new commercial business accounts/customers in the assigned region. That commercial business includes, but is not limited to, manufactured housing and multifamily.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>DUTIES / RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul><li>Promotes/sells/secures orders from existing and prospective customers through a relationship-based approach</li></ul>
<ul><li>Demonstrates products and services to existing/potential customers and assists them in selecting those best suited to their needs</li></ul>
<ul><li>Establishes develops and maintains business relationships with current customers and prospective customers in the assigned region/market segment to generate new business for the organization’s products/services</li></ul>
<ul><li>Makes telephone calls and in-person visits and presentations to existing and prospective customers</li></ul>
<ul><li>Enters prospect/customer interactions and account information into the CRM system on a regular basis</li></ul>
<ul><li>Researches prospective customers and information to determine their potential</li></ul>
<ul><li>Develops clear and effective written proposals/quotations for customers</li></ul>
<ul><li>Expedites the resolution of customer problems and complaints</li></ul>
<ul><li>Coordinates sales effort with marketing, sales management, accounting, logistics, and technical service groups</li></ul>
<ul><li>Analyzes the region/market’s potential and determines the value of existing and prospective customers value to the organization</li></ul>
<ul><li>Identifies advantages and compares the organization’s products/services</li></ul>
<ul><li>Supplies management with oral and written reports on customer needs, problems, interests, competitive activities, and potential for new products and services</li></ul>
<ul><li>Keeps up-to-date on product applications, technical services, market conditions, competitive activities, advertising, and promotional trends through the reading of pertinent literature and consulting with marketing and technical service areas</li></ul>
<ul><li>Participates in trade shows and conventions</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>EDUCATION AND QUALIFICATIONS</b></p>
<ul><li>Minimum of 5 years of experience in fiber cement, building materials, or related industry.</li></ul>
<ul><li>High school diploma or equivalent. Degree preferred.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Results-oriented and able to work both independently and within a team environment.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Proficiency in using Microsoft Office Suite applications and CRM software is essential.</li></ul>
<ul><li>A valid driver’s license is required.</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>COMMENTS</b></p>
<ul><li>This position will require up to 75% travel within the territory/region.</li></ul>
