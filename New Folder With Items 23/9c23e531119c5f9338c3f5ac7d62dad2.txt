<h2>Multimedia Sales Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">GateHouse Media is an industry-leading media organization, investing in cutting-edge technologies, products, services and partnerships to revolutionize how we serve our 200,000 small-and-medium-sized business customers in a digital world. We also invest in our employees, offering premier sales training providing you with the tools you need to become an expert in our marketing solutions, prospect and manage your customer portfolio and reach your full potential (salary + commission!). Enjoy the opportunity to expand your professional abilities by pairing sales skills with your professional verbal and written communication skills to assist our customers with their advertising campaigns.
<br><br>
Summary
<br><br>
The primary role of the Multimedia Sales Executive (MMSE) is to increase print and digital advertising revenues by achieving or exceeding the revenue goal in their assigned territory. The MMSE will develop and present audience based, multimedia sales campaigns to meet the diversified needs of our customers and maximize customer ROI.
<br><br>
Key Responsibilities
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Consistently meets/exceeds all print and digital revenue targets</li>
<li>Develops and demonstrates expert knowledge and understanding of all GateHouse Media sales and marketing assets, including all newspaper products, digital products and digital marketing services</li>
<li>Represent and embrace new non-traditional products and services that the company may introduce as we look to diversify new revenue streams that capitalize on our customer base and scale</li>
<li>Follows GateHouse prescribed best practices on Pipeline Management and Gap Analysis and completes all required sales process activities</li>
<li>Regularly and customarily engaged in person with customers</li>
<li>Drives customer retention through excellent customer service</li>
<li>Develops new business by understanding customer needs and selling products and services that provide value to the customer</li>
<li>Effectively communicates with management to provide accurate revenue forecasts and market intelligence that may impact revenue (+/-)</li>
<li>Maintains the highest levels of personal and professional behavior in the workplace and in the market</li>
</ul>
The above statements are intended to describe the general nature and level of the work being performed by employees assigned to this job. This is not an exhaustive list of all duties and responsibilities. GateHouse Media management reserves the right to amend and change responsibilities to meet business and organizational needs as necessary.</div>
