<h2>Leasing Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Specialized Property Management Group, LLC is a Memphis based company specializing in the management of Class A Multi-Family Apartment Communities. We base our success on listening and understanding the needs of our residents, owners, and employees. All of these components must be considered in all decisions for a property to operate at its full potential. This requires the management company to understand the goals of the ownership group/developer, resident expectations, and professional development of its employees.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Leasing</p>
<ul>
<li>Greet prospects and qualify by covering all criteria (ask questions; utilize completed guest cards, etc.).</li>
<li>Immediately record all telephone and in-person visits on appropriate reports.</li>
<li>Files own guest cards and maintain according to established procedures.</li>
<li>Inspect models and available “market ready”, communicate related service needs to Property Manager.</li>
<li>Demonstrate community and apartment/model and apply product knowledge to clients’ needs by communicating the features and benefits; close the sale.</li>
<li>Have prospect complete application and secure deposit in accordance with the company procedures and Fair Housing requirements.</li>
<li>Update availability report, process applications for approvals. (i.e., credit check, rental history, etc.) Submit processed applications to the Property Manager for approval. Follow up with applicant regarding status.</li>
<li>Ensure apartment is ready for resident to move-in on agreed date.</li>
<li>Immediately follow-up on prospects that did not close and attempt to close sale again. If unable to help prospect, refer them to sister communities to meet prospect’s needs.</li>
<li>Secure new residents signature(s) on appropriate paperwork prior to move-in. Orient new residents to community.</li>
<li>Assist in monitoring renewals. Distribute and follow-up on renewal notices.</li>
<li>Monitor advertising effectiveness. Gather information about market competition in the area and file.</li>
<li>Represent the company in a professional manner at all the times.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Administrative</p>
<ul>
<li>Accept rental payments and give immediately to Assistant Property Manager.</li>
<li>Type lease and complete appropriate paperwork and input information in the ESITE System accurately and on a timely basis. Review with the Property Manager prior to obtaining signatures.</li>
<li>Maintain current resident files.</li>
<li>Maintain and record daily inspections for the community.</li>
<li>Distribute all company or community-issued notices.</li>
<li>Maintain accurate monthly commission records on leases and renewals. Assist management team with other various tasks as required.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Resident Retention</p>
<ul>
<li>Receive all telephone calls and in-person visits. Listen to resident requests, concerns and comments.</li>
<li>Quickly complete maintenance Service Request and inform the maintenance team. Answer questions for residents about community, repairs, rent, rules, etc. Follow up on a timely basis if unable to respond to residents on all matters.</li>
<li>Ensure all maintenance repairs are handled satisfactorily by contacting residents with completed service requests on a weekly basis.</li>
<li>Maintain open communication with Property Manager and Maintenance Supervisor.</li>
<li>Contribute to cleanliness and curb appeal of the community on continuing basis.</li>
<li>Assist in planning resident functions. Attend functions and participate as host for any functions as directed by the Property Manager.</li>
<br>
 </ul>
<p></p>
<p>Drug Test and Background Screening Required</p>
