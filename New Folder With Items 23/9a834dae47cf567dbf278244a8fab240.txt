<h2>Product Solutions Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">---------------
<br><br>
<b>About This Job:</b><br>
---------------
<br><br>
As a member of the ICE Technology team, the Product Solutions Director is responsible for leading, managing, and coordinating product and project management for the development of our products. The Product Solutions Director plays a liaison role between Business Development, Sales, Marketing, Customer Service and Technology teams from ideation to operational execution. Working with the ICE teams, the Product Solutions Director participates in the development of travel/membership products by identifying potential products, generating product requirements, determining specifications, production timetables, and time-integrated plans for product introduction and defining execution strategies. As we transform our current production systems to the NexGen Platform, the Product Solutions team plays a critical role in project management to deliver quality products for our partners, members, and ICE employees.
<br><br>
Please note, we are currently unable to sponsor candidates and preference will be given to local candidates.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
-----------------
<br><br>
<b>Duties include, but are not limited, to the following:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Provide full product ownership over a vertical of products, which includes having an in-depth understanding of ICE product offerings, business model and dependencies.</li>
<li>Provides insights to sales teams to develop product sales strategies.</li>
<li>Creates and delivers presentations for new product solution opportunities in conjunction with Business Development and Account Management.</li>
<li>Keep abreast of competitive market.</li>
<li>Provides information for management by preparing product costs, forecasts, and technology operational execution.</li>
<li>Leads, plans and executes within the Development and Product Lifecycle of assigned products bringing new products to market or enhancements to existing.</li>
<li>Review and manage the products for an assigned area of responsibility, to understand and ensure achievement of service level agreements set, and direct vendors or internal support to achieve operational excellence.</li>
<li>Provides assistance to Account Management in monthly reporting and as necessary for special report requirements.</li>
<li>Manage Root Cause Analysis to identify and eliminate the cause of break/fix issues resulting in improved system performance.</li>
<li>Design a yearly roadmap for the products assigned, and execute according to company goals and key initiatives.</li>
<li>Execute the implementation of the product strategy for undertaking projects across your assigned area of responsibility, implementing standards that will ensure quality and consistency throughout the business in regards to new projects. This includes integration planning, scope planning, schedule planning, cost planning, quality management, resource management, communication management, risk management and procurement management.</li>
<li>Own all business issues relevant to keeping the project on track; provide an escalation point for business users for resolution of project issues and conflict; assists in managing the customer relationship, networking throughout the organization to build user support.</li>
<li>Coordinate resource management to ensure that the appropriate resources are assigned to new project efforts.</li>
<li>Facilitates project initiation decisions and assesses build/buy/reuse/outsource alternatives, develop business cases to evaluate and prioritize projects (this includes researching market data, interviewing stakeholders to determine business requirements and translating those requirements into key performance indicators), recommending those projects where the customer's expectations can be fully met or exceeded.</li>
<li>Prepare initial time and cost estimates, managing feasibility studies, assist stakeholders in understanding and obtaining project entitlements. Take ownership and accountability for the performance of pro-forma against budget and commitments, and the project schedule.</li>
<li>Participate with the Product Solutions team to estimate impacts or changes to existing applications and costs resulting from new projects.</li>
<li>Provides leadership and mentoring to matrix organization of other Product Solutions Managers, Project Managers and technology staff.</li>
<li>Continuously evaluate emerging trends related to IT solutions to incorporate improved features and functionality as technology allows.</li>
<li>Maintain a good working dialogue with peers, both internally and externally.</li>
<li>Communicate effectively and regularly with the inter-departmental IT Directors and promote effective communication amongst the Product Solutions team.</li>
<li>Continuously evaluate support costs in order to maximize efficiencies.</li>
<li>Solicit, negotiate and review application licensing and support agreements with vendors.</li>
<li>Write and review Requests for Information, Quotes and Proposals.</li>
<li>Conduct project post mortems and create a recommendations report in order to identify successful and unsuccessful project elements.</li>
<li>Performs other related duties as assigned/required.</li>
</ul>
