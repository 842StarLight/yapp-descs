<h2>Coordinator Consumer Promotions</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>The Coordinator Consumer Promotions is responsible for the execution of national and tactical revenue-driving promotions and initiatives that support the promotions strategy. The Coordinator will amplify campaigns through a mix of digital channels such as email, social media, website, app, paid search, display and others. The Coordinator is process-oriented and able to maintain a very high attention to detail when carrying out tasks and does so with speed. The Coordinator is comfortable working against deadlines in a fast-paced environment.<br>
<br>
<b>Essential Responsibilities</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Execute the sales promotions plan comprised of a mix of tactics to increase profitability, improve price perception and drive network and product awareness</li>
<li>Work cross-functionally with Market Planning, Revenue Management, Network Planning and CRM teams to gain market, consumer and competitive insights and translate into actionable plans</li>
<li>Think creatively and contribute ideas to address business needs</li>
<li>Fully own the sales promotion process with an ability to flex when changes arise and proactively identify and manage the impact of changes to the promotion</li>
<li>Develop promotional plans and timelines to ensure on time deliverables from stakeholders</li>
<li>Manage ongoing stakeholder communication including launch plans with Customer Support teams to ensure a seamless launch and customer experience</li>
<li>Identify and facilitate synergistic opportunities through the integration of TrueBlue and ancillary products including but not limited to Vacations, JetBlue Card, Mint, Even More Space, etc.</li>
<li>Thorough proofing of all digital assets and manage stakeholder approval process</li>
<li>Work closely with the Analyst Consumer Promotions to evaluate campaign results and execute test plans to optimize promotional effectiveness</li>
<li>Continuously identify process improvements that increase go-to-market speed</li>
<li>Seek out opportunity gaps and barriers in technology and resources that can further expand the promotional toolkit and develop business cases that highlight innovative solutions</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
Minimum Experience and Qualifications</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in Marketing, Business, Communication, or a related field</li>
<li>One (1) year of experience in Marketing, Promotions, Project Management, Communications or working at an advertising agency</li>
<li>Basic understanding of promotions, revenue management principles and marketing analytics</li>
<li>Must possess a strong sense of urgency with an ability to prioritize, meet deadlines and handle multiple projects simultaneously</li>
<li>Strong attention to detail and highly organized</li>
<li>Effective communication (written, verbal, presentation) and negotiation skills</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal skills with an ability to motivate others</li>
<li>Must possess strong thought leadership and be able to think critically</li>
<li>Excellent at proofing written concepts and an ability to provide constructive feedback on visual creative concepts</li>
<li>Must be able to thrive and work cross-functionally in a dynamic, face-paced, matrix work environment</li>
<li>Proficient with Microsoft Office suite</li>
<li>Ability to work non-traditional hours as needed (nights/weekends)</li>
<li>In valid possession of travel documents with the ability to travel in and out of the United States</li>
<li>Pass a ten (10) year background check, and pre-employment drug</li>
<li>Must be legally eligible to work in the country in which the position is located</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
Preferred Experience and Qualifications</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Two (2) years of experience in Marketing, Promotions, Project Management, Communications or working at an advertising agency</li>
<li>Previous airline or travel sales promotions experience</li>
<li>Knowledge of consumer behavior as it relates to promotions</li>
<li>Experience working with design on the creative process and writing insightful briefs</li>
<li>Experience creating compelling and effective messaging across all marketing channels including digital, email, social media, display and paid search</li>
</ul>
