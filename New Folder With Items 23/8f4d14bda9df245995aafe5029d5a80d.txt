<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Description
</b></h1>
<p><b>Senior Copywriter, CNX
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>CNX: The agency of Condé Nast
</b></p>
<p>We are an agency that defies typical classification. As the agency of Condé Nast, we have a front row seat to culture as it is being created. Our job is to turn those cultural insights, along with Condé Nast’s talent and brand relationships, into award-winning creative work that can exist in any medium, inside and outside Condé Nast’s properties. We think beyond the norm to advance branded creativity into new and emerging spaces while building meaningful partnerships with our clients. We believe that a well-formed culture (at work and at large) is an essential ingredient for producing work that changes the way we look at things.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Who are you?
</b></p>
<p>As a senior copywriter, you are responsible for coming up with ideas that go beyond any single medium or format. You’ll support a team of creative directors on a range of projects that span categories (e.g. beauty, tech, finance, fashion, luxury, travel, CPG, etc).
</p>
<p></p>
<p>You’re a creative problem solver who strives to make thoughtful and beautiful work no matter the context. You’ve worked in a variety of settings in your career, and a strong candidate will have both editorial and agency experience. You have a love for culture and cultural brands and an interest in driving the future of storytelling which will help frame the larger context we are working within, including the nature of an advertising-driven business.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>You have passion and expertise for storytelling and the ability to work well with cross-functional team members in a collaborative way. You have a desire to contribute not just as a copywriter, but also as an advocate for the entire creative department. You’re confident in your skills and don’t mind being challenged on your ideas.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>Respond to briefs, present concepts internally, develop presentations, pitch directly to clients.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Manage all copy-related tasks associated with a project from pre-sale through post-production.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Be an enthusiastic teammate and mentor to more junior team members on the copy team.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Solid experience pitching and defending creative concepts to clients and senior stakeholders.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The ability to work fast, prioritize your workload and switch gears between multiple projects simultaneously.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Contribute to the development of the creative team culture, and the CNX identity.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Strong communication skills; articulate your perspective and creative rationale against each assignment.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Knowledge of and natural interest in the Condé Nast culture, and the range of editorial voices of the entire portfolio. *not just fashion*
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Experience working on experiential activations and events is a plus.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Specific Requirements:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>A love of the written word, and the ability to adapt your writing to different brand voices.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>A love of popular culture and a desire to create work that impacts it.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>An ability to think outside traditional ad formats.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>An analytical mind that can articulate an argument and adjust its presentation depending on its audience.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>A desire to explore creativity outside of the traditional advertising agency context.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>A portfolio with a range of work that demonstrates your personal taste level as well as your ability to solve creative challenges.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Education:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>6+ years of creative advertising experience
