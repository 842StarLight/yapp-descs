<h2>Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Tremor Video is the leading programmatic video platform in the market. We are hiring an Account Executive to join our sales team in Atlanta. We are looking for an individual with a proven record of revenue generation and a deep interest and passion for digital video and creative storytelling.
<br><br>
<b>What You Do:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Continually build a deep understanding of video advertising and the Tremor Video demand-side platform. Learn our unique &amp; exclusive data offerings, and market-leading creative offerings. Establish ability to strategically position products externally to help drive revenue</li>
<li>Help drive both managed &amp; self-service revenue</li>
<li>Strong knowledge of Atlanta based brands and agencies in Retail, QSR, Travel, and Automotive</li>
<li>Existing agency/brand relationships within Atlanta</li>
<li>Own and lead the sales process to enhance existing partnerships (through up selling of product and services and sales optimization)</li>
<li>Provide regular forecast of your business</li>
<li>Meet and exceed revenue goals defined by your manager</li>
<li>Showcase ability to get client-direct meetings to push forward partnership conversations</li>
<li>Prepare proposals and respond timely to online RFP's with a high degree of professionalism and quality</li>
<li>Work with Account Manager to ensure seamless post sales process</li>
<li>Prospect new business opportunities based on assigned territory and provide updates and reports to management</li>
<li>Work with cross-functional teams internally to ensure campaign success and growth of partnerships</li>
<li>Have the ability to look at suite of video solutions (Managed vs. Self, Exclusive data, Custom creative) to determine which solution works best for your client</li>
<li>40% travel to outer markets required</li>
</ul>
<b>How You Do It:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>3-5 years of digital sales experience in the Atlanta region. Client based relationships are a plus</li>
<li>Strong understanding of agency-based relationship navigation</li>
<li>Experience uncovering new business opportunities through diligent prospecting</li>
<li>You are experienced with building relationships with both internal and external business partners</li>
<li>You are highly organized and focused multi-tasker with strong attention to detail</li>
<li>You are able to creatively and strategically sell, with strong analytical and project management skills</li>
<li>You have excellent client service and presentation skills</li>
</ul>
