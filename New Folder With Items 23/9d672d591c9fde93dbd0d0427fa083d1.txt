<h2>Principal Field Solution Architect – Cisco Software Defined Networking</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>The Principal Field Solution Architect (FSA) role focuses on pre-sales solution design. The incumbent analyzes the sales environment including customer expectations, competitive environment as well as the customer's technical environment and define actions to be performed in the sales cycle timeframe.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>
The Principal FSA develops and maintains strategic relationships with Sales Management of the teams they support and seen as a trusted advisor to grow business. The Principal FSA is expected to be subject matter expert in Cisco Software Defined Networking including Cisco ACI products along with VxLAN, MP-BGP, Multicast protocols, as well as Network Analytics tools such as the Cisco Tetration solution.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>
The Principal FSA is expected to be subject matter expert in multiple families of solutions.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>
The Principal FSA takes a lead role in defining, developing, and supporting solutions and new technologies; prepares the team to be successful through proactive training, sharing knowledge, and communicating best practices.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
