<h2>General Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>General Manager</b></p>
<p><b>
Summary</b>: Manages all functions of the restaurant to ensure customer service and high quality products are delivered and ensures restaurant profitability. Responsible for the successful execution of fast and accurate sandwiches, world class customer service while maintaining a clean and organized shift.</p>
<p><b>
Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Must meet all requirements and perform all duties listed in the In-Shopper, Driver and Assistant Manager job descriptions</li>
<li>
Manages a staff of 3 Assistant Managers and approximately 25-30 employees. Assigns, oversees and evaluates work for compliance</li>
<li>
Manages hiring, training, evaluating, discipline and termination of employees</li>
<li>
Provides on the job training for new employees</li>
<li>
Delegates and is responsible for the ordering receipt, storage and issuing of all food, labor, equipment, cleaning and paper supplies for the unit to ensures a minimum loss from waste or theft</li>
<li>
Responsible for the supervision, preparation, inventory, sales and service of food</li>
<li>
Forecasts food items by estimation what amount of each food item will be consumed per shift</li>
<li>
Supervises food preparation and service operations while on duty. Assists In shopper during rush periods to ensure the maintenance of restaurant efficiency.</li>
<li>
Assists In-shoppers with greeting customers, talking orders, using the cash register, assembling orders and checking for completeness and correctness</li>
<li>
Ensures that every customer received world class customer service</li>
<li>
Must be able to actively participate in teamwork and get along with others</li>
<li>
Must be open and receptive to feedback</li>
<li>
Must consistently demonstrate high levels of reliability, punctuality, accuracy, sense of urgency, responsibility, integrity and initiative</li>
<li>
Routes deliveries and supervises drivers to maximize delivery business and speed</li>
<li>
Completes daily food preparations (opening procedures, meat and vegetables slicing, bread production)</li>
<li>
Executes systems and procedures with 100% integrity and completeness</li>
<li>
Completes daily, weekly and period paperwork with accuracy</li>
<li>
Controls inventory by using weekly inventory system and maintain regular inventory ordering schedules</li>
<li>
Conducts Weekly Manager meetings</li>
<li>
Audits system and procedures as well as shift ending paperwork</li>
<li>
Completes preventative maintenance and upkeep on stores equipment and supplies</li>
<li>
Performs other related duties as required</li>
<li>
Responsible for 100% of the cash drawers during the shift</li>
<li>
Manages deposits and change orders per Deposit Operating Procedure</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Position reports to</b>: Area Manager</p>
<p><b>
Knowledge, Skills, Abilities &amp; Work Environment:</b> Must be able to speak, read and write the English language fluently and clearly. Must have strong math abilities in addition, subtraction, fractions, and percentages. Must lead by example. Understand use of personal computer of various software such as Excel and Word.</p>
<p>
Required to stand, walk, sit, use hands, reach with hands and arms, talk, hear, close vision, distance vision, peripheral vision and depth perception. Occasionally required to climb, balance, stoop, kneel, crouch or crawl, lift up to 50 pounds.</p>
<p>
The physical demands described here are representative of those that must be met by an employee to successfully perform the essential functions of this job. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</p>
<p>
While performing the duties the employee is occasionally exposed to mechanical parts. The noise level in the work environment is moderate.</p>
<p><b>
Special Requirements:</b> Willing to work overtime, holidays, weekends, days and evenings, as business dictates. Must attend and successfully complete Jimmy John's corporate certification class and food safety certification class. Must be 18 years of age, have a valid driverâ€™s license, reliable transportation, current automobile insurance, clean driving record and must have physical stamina to work 50-60 hours per week.</p>
