<h2>Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Job Opportunity: </b>Project Manager</p>
<p><b>Job Category: </b>Client Services</p>
<p><b>Company: </b>Pinpoint</p>
<p><b>About Our Company: </b></p>
<p>Pinpoint is an award winning full-service promotional marketing agency with in-house production capabilities. We aren't your typical marketing company - we’re all about people and process, not products and profits. Though products are important to our business, they come secondary to the amazing customer experience we strive to deliver each day to our clients. We focus on the possible transformation and experience first, then dig deep to find the perfect match of tailored services, creative designs, and custom products around those needs. We have recently moved to a larger office facility due to our steady growth. We’re a great fit for someone who is always eager to learn, seeks a challenge, has a passion for creativity, has diverse experience with marketing and thrives in a close-knit team environment.</p>
<p><b>Job Description Summary: </b></p>
<p>Pinpoint is seeking a driven and dynamic individual with a history of success and passion for how great marketing and ideas can create meaningful connections between consumers, companies, and brands. You will be expected to have the right people in the right place at the right time, to enable the agency to “move it forward” and produce great work—on time, on budget and as efficiently as possible. As Project Manager, you must be able to manage a mixture of small and simplistic projects, along with large multi-faceted projects for well-known brands, events, campaigns and activations at the same time.</p>
<p><b>So, what will you actually be doing?</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Be the communications hub and liaison between internal teams, clients and agency partners.</li>
<li>Ensure the companies processes and workflows are being followed by owning and managing the communication, creative, and production process to guarantee projects are on schedule and within the client’s business requirements.</li>
<li>Working with internal teams to process data and clarify the specific needs for a project in order to define the next steps, key milestones, time allocation and the specific tasks required to move the project forward.</li>
<li>Initiate, develop and revise schedules monitoring the progress of the deliverables while updating the status of projects via the company’s internal project management platform.</li>
<li>Facilitating internal kick-off meetings for larger and more involved projects to onboard team members.</li>
<li>Working with the respective department leads to manage and allocate creative and production resources.</li>
<li>Think ahead and proactively engage in contingency planning by recommending solutions for possible future hurdles.</li>
<li>Alerting the team in real time if a project strays out of the originally set parameters.</li>
<li>Tracking time spent on specific tasks; submitting a formal report on a weekly basis communicating time allocation(s).</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Skills and requirements needed to succeed: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>4+ years of proven and relevant experience with project management, agency work, or account management.</li>
<li>Knowledge of the advertising, marketing, agency or sales industry required.</li>
<li>Ability to manage and organize multiple projects simultaneously across several departments.</li>
<li>A deep level of strategic thinking, the ability to ask the right questions, and the intuition to think ahead to prevent any issues or bottlenecks.</li>
<li>Well-developed interpersonal skills and experience in interacting with senior level stakeholders.</li>
<li>Ability to create, compose and edit written materials, especially visually attractive presentations.</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Office and related computer skills.</li>
<li>A confident attitude that fuels a genuine desire to want to help businesses and people improve and succeed.</li>
<li>Keen focus on the “why” for any action; from articulating insights for creative briefs to the rationale for holding a meeting, or writing an email.</li>
<li>Strong organizational skills, time management skills and attention to detail.</li>
<li>High-energy, yet composed personality with a ‘whatever it takes’ attitude and approach to work.</li>
<li>A flexible schedule is required depending on project and client needs. Some travel may be required.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Who this job is not right for: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Someone who does not play well with others.</li>
<li>Someone who has a sense of entitlement.</li>
<li>Someone who says one thing yet does something else.</li>
<li>Someone who focuses more on their job title than the required task at hand.</li>
<li>Someone who is always quick to think and say why something <i>cannot </i>be done.</li>
</ul>
<p>We’re looking for someone special to help grow our brand. Tell us why you would make a great addition to our team.</p>
