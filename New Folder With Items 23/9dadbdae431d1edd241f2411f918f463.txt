NorthShore University HealthSystem (NorthShore) is a premier integrated healthcare delivery system consistently ranked as a Top 15 Teaching Hospital in the U.S. Headquartered in Evanston, IL, NorthShore is comprised of four hospitals – Evanston, Glenbrook, Highland Park and Skokie. The system includes a 900 physician multispecialty group practice with 130 locations across Chicagoland. NorthShore has annual revenues of $2.1 billion and employs over 10,500 people.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b><i>
Position Overview</i></b></div>
<div>We are currently seeking a Senior Applications Developer of CRM Systems for our Health Information Technology Department located in Skokie, IL. This is a full-time position (Mon-Fri 9-5) and is benefits eligible. This person will serve as the lead developer on our CRM Applications Services Team. The NorthShore CRM is a cloud services platform based on Salesforce applications (Sales/service cloud and Salesforce Marketing cloud) as well as Evariant. NorthShore is currently engaged in an initial build of this environment and related experience is a plus.</div>
<br>
<div>
</div>
<div><b><i>Responsibilities</i></b></div>
<ul>
<li>Responsibility for the management of two or more moderately complex applications</li>
<li>Development of CRM platform including data integrations and/or API creation &amp; usage
</li>
<li>Design and implement Salesforce.com solutions using best practices through standard configuration and custom development.</li>
<li>Develop clear requirements and specifications when custom development is required.</li>
<li>Support for additions of new modules and features which augment or add to these existing applications</li>
<li>Support the analysis of CRM information system needs throughout NorthShore including requirements, product evaluations, recommendations for system selection, implementation, enhancements and support</li>
<li>Support applications that require high availability (24x7)</li>
<li>Salesforce Evangelism to other members of NorthShore</li>
<li>Work within the CRM team to understand business partners’ requirements, define, and optimize processes and help drive process improvements to meet business needs in Salesforce.</li>
</ul>
<div><b><i>Qualifications</i></b></div>
<ul>
<li>5+ years of experience in CRM Systems (Salesforce is a major plus)
</li>
<li>Salesforce Certified with Platform Developer II and active Trailhead account a bonus</li>
<li>3+ experience with CRM development languages (Apex Code, Visualforce)</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree or equivalent work experience</li>
<li>Experience with marketing automation platforms such as Salesforce Marketing Cloud, Pardot, Eloqua or Marketo is a major plus</li>
<li>Ability to solve highly complex technical, organizational, operational and people problems</li>
<li>Programming logic skills and proven ability to maintain and understand complex applications</li>
<li>Excellent technical troubleshooting, code debugging, and problem solving skills
</li>
<li>Knowledge of healthcare industry regulations a major plus</li>
</ul>
