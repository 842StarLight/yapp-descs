Forward thinking and experienced in selling, the SaaS Account Executive is responsible for selling Nextiva's cloud-based business communication products. The SAE will be responsible for adhering to a monthly quota. With a positive attitude and a highly, self-motivated desire to constantly learn and grow within the organization, the SAE is a critical role within the Nextiva family. If you are an overachiever and want to contribute to a team that will truly appreciate your dedication and hard work, then this is role is for you!
<br><br>
<b>Position Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Sales experience, ideally with a CRM, telecommunications, technology, or SaaS company</li>
<li>Proven ability to communicate effectively in presentations via telephone and in person with prospective Customers</li>
<li>Strong emotional intelligence and intuition for steering conversations that move the sales forward</li>
<li>Able to work in fast-paced, self-directed entrepreneurial environment</li>
<li>Highly energetic, self-starter with a purpose driven, results oriented attitude</li>
<li>Decision-making, problem resolution and creative thinking skills</li>
<li>Demonstrable and quantifiable success generating and closing new business</li>
<li>Ambitious, career oriented, energetic, self-motivated and results driven</li>
<li>Excellent presentation, negotiation and organizational skills</li>
<li>Ability to learn and apply new concepts quickly</li>
<li>Ability to exercise sound independent judgment</li>
<li>Demonstrated maturity, integrity and the ability to perform in an entrepreneurial environment, both individually and as a team player</li>
<li>Competitive, driven, and accountable personality</li>
</ul>
<b>Position Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Develop and apply an understanding of Nextiva's products and how to effectively position Nextiva as the best choice for the Customer</li>
<li>Persuasively communicate strategies for meeting customer needs</li>
<li>Master and quickly adhere to Nextiva's sales methodology, processes and prospecting techniques</li>
<li>Own a pipeline of valuable opportunities with the responsibility and expectation of building, maintaining and closing</li>
<li>Communicate appropriate product and technical information to prospective customers and conduct follow-up according to department standards</li>
<li>Interact with Customers at the C level to form relationships and drive long-term commitment and buying decisions</li>
<li>Build and manage your own book of business and drive the full sales cycle, from initial contact to close</li>
<li>Conduct live product demonstrations over the phone, via webinar, and in person</li>
<li>Manage accounts with direct involvement with escalations and/or customer concerns which directly impact Nextiva</li>
<li>Utilize sounds judgement and discretion when selling solutions, including pricing and contract terms that will have a major impact on Nextiva's profitability</li>
<li>Manage and professionally document all steps of the selling process, including but not limited to marking sales stages, coordinating complex decisions, and overcoming objections at close</li>
<li>Attend trade show and industry events, as needed</li>
<li>Complete required SalesForce.com sales process and pipeline activity tracking with detail and accuracy</li>
<li>Identify sales trends, document prospect feedback and contribute constructive feedback to our internal partners regarding marketing, product and systems</li>
<li>Continue training and education to keep pace with product modifications, industry changes and sales knowledge</li>
<li>Demonstrate commitment and engagement through seeking out additional responsibilities, volunteering for special projects, leading by example and sharing knowledge and best practices, with flexibility and adaptability</li>
<li>Perform other duties as assigned to contribute to overall sales and team results</li>
</ul>
