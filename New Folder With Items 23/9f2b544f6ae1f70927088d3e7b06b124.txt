<h2>Regional Sales Director - Federal</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>SpyCloud is the leader in account takeover (ATO) prevention</b>, protecting billions of consumer and employee accounts either directly or through product integrations. Our award-winning solutions proactively defeat fraud attempts and disrupt the criminals' ability to profit from stolen information.</p>
<p>SpyCloud is searching for a <b>Regional Sales Director, Federal </b>to build our presence within the Federal Government and Department of Defense (DoD) Community. Your executive level contacts in Federal agencies and partners mean you have the ability to drive short- and long-term results. You are customer focused, and build internal relationships and external partnerships. You can identify, develop and execute account strategy to close new business opportunities and expand revenue.</p>
<p><b>Job Description and Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Deep knowledge of Federal Sales process, and experience including pipeline development, territory planning, account planning, forecasting, quota attainment, sales presentations, and short term, mid-term, long-term opportunity management.</li>
<li>Establish access and maintain active relationships with key Federal and DOD decision makers (across all IT Security and senior executive levels) in industry, partners, and customers to drive all pertinent issues related to sales strategy and goal attainment.</li>
<li>Scope, negotiate and bring to closure agreements to exceed booking and revenue quota targets.</li>
<li>Target and gain access to decision makers in key prospect accounts in the assigned territory.</li>
<li>Collaborate across functions (channel, marketing, sales operations, sales engineering, customer success, and product management) to create visibility with target accounts and drive engagement of target prospects at both the individual contributor and executive level.</li>
<li>Work cooperatively with partners to leverage their established account presence and relationships.</li>
<li>Capture, maintain, and disseminate accurate and relevant prospect information using Salesforce.com</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications, Experience and Skills</b></p>
<ul>
<li>10+ years of successful technology sales experience with at least 5+ years’ experience selling cybersecurity solutions and/or security infrastructure products within the Federal government and DOD Community.</li>
<li>Demonstrated success working within the Federal government’s procurement process, regulations, GSA schedule and other GWACs.</li>
<li>Active DOD clearance with US Government badge preferred. *Must be eligible to obtain a US Government clearance.</li>
<li>Strong understanding of cybersecurity, cloud and SaaS technologies and competitive offerings in the marketplace.</li>
<li>History of exceeding booking and revenue targets. Successful experience with target account selling, solution selling, and/or consultative sales techniques.</li>
<li>Aptitude for understanding how technology products and solutions solve business problems and an ability to explain complicated concepts to a variety of audiences and skill levels.</li>
<li>Excellent communication (written and verbal) and presentation skills</li>
<li>Strong time management, organization and decision-making skills.</li>
<li>Ability to work remotely and be able and willing to travel on short notice.</li>
<li>Self-motivated ability to work independently and as part of a team..</li>
<li>BA/BS or equivalent combination of education and experience.</li>
</ul>
