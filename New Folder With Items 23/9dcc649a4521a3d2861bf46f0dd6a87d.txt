<h2>Account Coordinator, International Nursing</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>The Account Coordinator, International Nursing,** executes a variety of tasks in support of the International Nursing Account Management team to assist them in managing day-to-day processes. Tasks may include candidate screening, candidate profile review and submission, candidate interview preparation, candidate profile uploads and confirmations in all Vendor Management Systems, enter and maintain orders, billing and collections follow up and resolution activities as well as various other support tasks.</p>
<p>Job Tasks: **</p>
<ul>
<li>As directed by Brand President and OGP AM’s, provides initial screening of candidate submittal on the OGP-1 Ready for Placement report.</li>
<li>Helps to prepare candidate profiles based on high demand specialties and assists with AMIE profile completion, skills checklist retrieval and references.</li>
<li>Sends marketing material on open positions that match the candidate’s specialty and determines candidate the candidate’s location preference, if any.</li>
<li>Assists in updating status of pending candidates; coordinating between Account Management and Sales.</li>
<li>Creating and maintaining candidate profiles in vendor systems technology</li>
<li>In collaboration with AM, create and/or maintain internal job descriptions, unit descriptions and Facility contacts within open AMIE orders.</li>
<li>Assists with candidate on boarding activities as directed by AM’s, i.e. Candidate Bio’s, Pre-arrival appointments, First Day Instructions, orientation information.</li>
<li>Coordinates and/or responds to requests for assistance with timecards, billing (including UBR) and collections.</li>
<li>Partners with account management in varying capacities to ensure client expectations are met on a daily basis.</li>
<li>Sends The Service Center (TSC) information following placement status at “Booked”/PIN’s and ensures job offer completion</li>
<li>Confirms that notice has been given following issuance on green card interviews (GCI’s) and confirms candidate start dates by phone and email.</li>
<li>Ensures pre-placement candidate files are ready for submission to Clients.</li>
</ul>
