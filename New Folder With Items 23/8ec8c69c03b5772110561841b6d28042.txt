<h2>Leasing Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Job Description</b></p>
<p><b>Overview</b></p>
<p>The overall objective of the Leasing Consultant position is to maintain a high physical occupancy by focusing their efforts on the attraction and retention of qualified residents to his or her community.</p>
<p>Responsible for leasing, marketing and maintaining positive resident relations with the assistance of Assistant Manager.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>Assist in lease transactions by typing leases, gathering applicant history and credit approvals</p>
<p>Provide general clerical assistance to the community office (i.e., answer telephone, writing work orders, typing and filing)</p>
<p>Tour property by showing model or ready vacant apartments. Highlight amenities (i.e., clubhouse and fitness center) and close the sale. Have prospect complete all application paperwork and secure deposit and application fee.</p>
<p>Update all social media with resident events, current promotions and local area information.</p>
<p>Assist with promoting community events, resident referral programs and conducting outreach.</p>
<p>Responsible for compiling, reporting and sending accurate monthly renewals offers to residents and outcome reports to supervisors.</p>
<p>Assist in daily upkeep of community clubhouse.</p>
<p><b>Education And Mandatory Skills</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Proficient computer skills.</li>
<li>Experience using Microsoft Office applications including Word, Excel, PowerPoint and Outlook.</li>
<li>Basic knowledge of Fair Housing laws preferred.</li>
<li>Onesite software experience preferred.</li>
<li>Experience and industry knowledge preferred.</li>
<li>One-year experience in customer service-related industry preferred.</li>
<li>We are willing to train you to have a successful career in Multi-Family Housing!</li>
</ul>
