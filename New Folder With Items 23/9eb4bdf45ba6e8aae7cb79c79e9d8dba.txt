<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>This is a great opportunity to join a growing and highly successful team within Waters/ERA in the Golden facility as a Material Handler II in our Shipping/Receiving Department. You will represent Waters/ERA as the person putting together customer orders and shipping them on time and accurately. You will be the last individual to ensure the quality and integrity of our product and providing the first impression our customers have when they receive their order. You will work with multiple teams/groups to deliver on time products and services.</div>
</div></div></div>

<div><div><div>

<li>Processing and Printing Daily and Future orders</li>

<li>
Inventory Cycle Counts</li>
<li>
Quality focus on accuracy to review orders to comply with error free order fulfillment</li>

Shipping area housekeeping and cleanliness</li>

Continuous Improvement</li>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Company Description
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>For over 50 years, Waters Corporation has developed innovative analytical science solutions to support customer discoveries, operations, performance, and regulatory compliance. Specifically, the company designs, manufactures, sells, and services high performance liquid chromatography, ultra performance liquid chromatography, and mass spectrometry technology systems and support products primarily in the United States, Europe, Japan, and Asia. Our innovations enable significant advancements within the pharmaceutical, life science, biochemical, industrial, food safety, environmental, academic and government industries. Waters is a publicly traded corporation (NYSE:WAT) with more than 6,000 employees and is represented in over 100 countries around the world.</div>
