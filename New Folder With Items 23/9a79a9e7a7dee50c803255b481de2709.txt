<p>Job Description:</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
About Loop Commerce</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>
Do you have a passion for building delightful consumer products? Do you want to be part of a game-changing and category-defining company and team that will transform commerce? Are you a strong technical product manager with the analytical experience and ability to understand and influence technical teams and concepts? If yes, join us at Loop Commerce.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Acquired by Synchrony in June 2018, Loop Commerce is a transformative force in the $400 billion Gift-Commerce category. Currently based in Menlo Park, CA, we help leading brands and retailers like Target, Saks Fifth Avenue and Neiman Marcus eliminate the traditional obstacles their customers face when trying to purchase items as gifts. Our flagship product is GiftNow®, that integrates seamlessly into retailers' stores to offer gift givers a simple and thoughtful way to gift online, and gives gift recipients, options to change the size or color of an item before it ships. This creates a more confident, faster and hassle-free end-to-end experience for the gift senders and receivers.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Loop Commerce and GiftNow have been profiled by numerous news organizations including CNBC, CBS, USA Today, Chain Store Age and Forbes, as changing the game for commerce and leading the category called G-Commerce (Gift-Commerce). Loop Commerce defines its vibrant culture as being a family-like and fun-loving atmosphere. While maintaining it’s start-up culture and fast-pace innovation, Loop Commerce now has the full backing and support of Synchrony to fuel and accelerate its growth and success to create something meaningful for millions of people worldwide.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
What You’ll Do:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Define clear and detailed product requirements and wireframes for high level flows</li>
<li>
Create and manage product roadmaps that turn customer needs and pain points into innovative products.
</li>
<li>Develop key insights by conducting user, market and competitor research that guide product decisions.</li>
<li>
Analyze and prioritize customer problems and define innovative and simple solutions
</li>
<li>Lead agile product development teams to deliver iterative solutions</li>
<li>
Define, monitor, measure and report product metrics and KPIs</li>
<li>
Continuously evolve and improve the product by incorporating feedback based on user testing, experimentation, data and analytics</li>
<li>
Develop launch plans in collaboration with field teams such as Sales, Partnerships and Marketing, to optimize product launches. Develop release notes for the launches
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications/Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Electrical Engineering or any other engineering discipline
</li>
<li>3+ years of experience working for a software product development company building consumer facing products</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
What We’re Looking For:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Technically adept. Demonstrated ability to understand and discuss technical concepts, create technical product requirements and definitions, discuss and evaluate technical solutions with engineers and other stakeholders, manage tradeoffs and communicate with stakeholders at all technical and managerial levels</li>
<li>
Strengths in problem solving, issue-resolution, ability to work in a deadline-driven work environment, attention to detail, and ability to multitask</li>
<li>
Excellent oral and written communication skills, as well as ability to think clearly, analyze quantitatively, problem-solve, support scope of requirements and prioritize, with a demonstrated experience engaging and influencing senior executives</li>
<li>
Proven track record of delivering projects within scope, time, budget and quality</li>
<li>
Demonstrated experience planning and managing complex project schedules.</li>
<li>
An entrepreneurial mindset, with the tenacity to develop ideas independently and thrive in fast-paced environment is crucial</li>
<li>
Hands-on knowledge of agile product development processes
</li>
<li>Can-do, positive attitude.</li>
</ul>
