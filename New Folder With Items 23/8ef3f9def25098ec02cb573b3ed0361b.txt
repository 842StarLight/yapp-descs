<h2>National Account Executive, Cloud & Cyber Security</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Expedient is a provider of multi-cloud platforms, hosting, security and data center infrastructure as a service (IaaS) solutions. Our technical solutions enable our clients to focus on their own business innovation, while our team handles the information technology needed to support it. This is a high-growth industry offering layers of opportunity to grow your career.</div>
<ul>
<li>
You will be part of a team responsible for opening new markets for Expedient by selling to anchor tenant clients in targeted cities</li>
<li>
You will have the opportunity to market and sell new services from cutting edge, best of breed products and technologies</li>
<li>
You'll enjoy a competitive salary, uncapped earning potential, stellar culture and employee benefits in a fast-paced competitive environment</li>
<li>
You can expect the average commission to range from $50k - $150k, per sale</li>
</ul>
<div>
This is a great opportunity for someone with a minimum of 7 years of experience in an outside B2B regional or national sales role focused on market penetration and building an Enterprise level client base. The successful candidate will be able to show examples of their own sales success and achievements by way of exceeding sales quotas, and personal recognition through sales awards or acknowledgments.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Direct applicants only; sponsorship is not provided</b></div>

<div><b>
Responsibilities</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Partner with dedicated Lead Generation and Partner Alliance team members to prospect and develop plans of action based on industry trends, client analysis and competitive landscape</li>
<li>
Utilize your existing client relationships and networking events to prospect and build pipeline</li>
<li>
Develop, prepare and deliver sales proposals, presentations and quotes on Expedient solutions and technology to Enterprise level companies across the country</li>
<li>
Assess and evaluate prospects' needs and connect them with the tools, resources and data necessary to support and enhance their business</li>
<li>
Manage multiple prospects and client sales opportunities from qualification to close</li>
<li>
Nurturing and growing the sales pipeline of new business, forecasting future sales and meeting or exceeding quarterly sales quota</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Required</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Experience selling solutions in an outside B2B market, in the technology arena</li>
<li>
Proven track record as a hunter and closer of new business at a regional or national level; able to partner with Lead Generation and Partner Alliance team to prospect new leads</li>
<li>
Knowledge of the latest product, market and industry trends and the ability to apply that knowledge to each prospects' unique business needs</li>
<li>
Ability to actively listen and ask probing questions to better understand the prospect's needs</li>
<li>
Superior sales, business development and client engagement skills with C-level executives</li>
<li>
Highly motivated sales professional able to drive business to meet/exceed goals</li>
<li>
Strong communication, time-management skills, responsiveness and attention to detail</li>
<li>
Skilled in navigating complex business relationships and the ability to become a strategic advisor and thought leader for our prospects and clients</li>
<li>
Strong comfort level with video conference technology to maximize time with prospects and clients, balancing face-to-face meetings and travel time</li>
<li>
Bachelors' degree or a minimum of 7 years of relevant sales experience</li>
<li>
Proficiency with Salesforce and Microsoft Office and other sales tools</li>
<li>
Willingness to travel domestically
</li>
<li>Must be able to pass pre-employment screens</li>
<li>
Must have a valid driver's license and provide proof of current personal automobile insurance to comply with company policy</li>
</ul>
