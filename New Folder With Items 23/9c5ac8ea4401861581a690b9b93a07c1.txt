<h2>Product Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>As a Product Marketing Manager for Procore, you will aid in the development and roll out of product go-to-market campaigns. You will champion our products, be an evangelist of product positioning in the market, and enable other Procore departments to do so as well. You have strong experience in market research, polished presentation skills, and can develop strategies around how to bring your products to market.
<br>
<br>
You have a true curiosity around personas, their challenges, behaviors, as well as data trends that draw conclusions and insights from those trends. This position requires a blend of skills needed to develop and tell clear stories, identify innovative ways to deliver content, rally people behind your vision, and motivate cross-departmental collaboration in an effort to successfully launch a campaign.
<br>
<br>
If you're looking for a hands-on role in a fast-paced company with an opportunity to grow your career, this role is for you!
<br>
<br>
<b>What you'll do:</b>
<ul>
<li>Drive go-to-market strategies for your product and the marketing efforts to support and drive go-to-market delivery and engagement</li>
<li>Ensure internal cross-departmental groups are aligned with your go-to-market strategies and have the necessary information to successfully execute (understand the primary personas, are able to properly position Procore, and have the necessary sales tools)</li>
<li>Launch new product features and manage the cross-functional implementation of the launch plan</li>
<li>Track efforts of go-to-markets and strategize how to achieve key metrics in an ever agile environment</li>
<li>Become a market expert on Procore's current and future buyers, how they buy, and their buying criteria</li>
<li>Become an expert in positioning Procore's solutions against the competition</li>
<li>Create and nurture customer and thought leader relationships to develop references, drive case studies, identify champions</li>
<li>Identify market trends/challenges and inspire content meant to drive engagement around these discoveries (compelling webcasts, written articles, infographics, and social messaging)</li>
<li>Produce and present compelling presentations at various events including sales meetings, roadshows, and industry trade shows.</li>
</ul>
<b>What we're looking for:</b>
<ul>
<li>3 years experience in product marketing or related experience</li>
<li>Experience developing results-driven strategies</li>
<li>Desire to create stories from data and behavioral trends</li>
<li>Experience working in a B2B SaaS environment is a plus</li>
<li>Creative thinker with a high degree of initiative and self-motivation</li>
<li>You believe in ownership of your role and take initiative to find opportunities, develop plans that inspire others, and ensure execution through measured results</li>
<li>You have the ability to disagree while encouraging openness and collaboration along the way</li>
<li>Knowledge of the construction industry and its emerging technologies are highly desirable, but not mandatory</li>
<li>Some travel required</li>
</ul>
