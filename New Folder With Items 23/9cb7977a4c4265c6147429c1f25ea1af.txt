 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">

<p><b>Job Title:</b> Leasing Consultant | Sea Castle - Santa Monica, CA
</p>
<p>At Windsor Communities, it's all about people. Our residents depend on us to be observant, interested in their lives, and quick to respond to their needs. We enjoy working with them and with each other. Every day is different: rewarding, challenging, and fun. Join an environment where your team becomes your family and your profession becomes your passion.</p>
<p>
As a Windsor Leasing Consultant you are the first person a prospect or resident meets when they come home. For you it’s not just a job, it’s a career and you understand that for our residents it’s not just an apartment, it’s their home. You love interacting with all types of people and have a passion for creating unique experiences.</p>
<p><b>
Day to Day – the nitty, but not gritty:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Lease apartment homes by selling the benefits of living at a Windsor Community
</li>
<li>Always be selling – over the phone, through email and in person. You are the subject expert on the product, the property and the local neighborhoood
</li>
<li>Generate leads by conducting effective follow up on the phone &amp; through email
</li>
<li>Provide exceptional and consistent customer service to our residents &amp; prospects
</li>
<li>Create &amp; implement promotional programs, including creative online advertising, direct marketing, social media outreach and resident events
</li>
<li>Contribute to the desired occupancy levels by renewing current resident leases
</li>
<li>Maintain accurate resident files
</li>
<li>Work weekends to be available when prospective residents are out looking for their new home. 2-3 weekends per month required
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Your Skills – show us what you got:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Strong sales with a desire to exceed company leasing requirements
</li>
<li>Outstanding communication skills, both written &amp; verbal
</li>
<li>A customer-focused mentality
</li>
<li>Proficiency with various software, apps and computer programs
</li>
<li>A polished, professional appearance
</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task and shift priorities quickly in a fast-pace leasing &amp; sales office
</li>
<li>Qualified candidates will have 1-2 years sales experience in a retail, hospitality, or multi-family environment, and Microsoft Office skills. Undergraduate degree is preferred
</li>
</ul>
