<p>
The Role:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Support the Box Office Manager with printing in house ticket orders, selling tickets to patrons, testing ticket links and pre-sale codes, and processing promotional ticket orders.</li>
<li>
Support the Premium Experience Manager with processing ticket orders, filter upgrade inquiries via phone or walk up, and prep night of show materials for show nights</li>
<li>
Answer the box office phone and direct the call where necessary or assist the caller with their needs
</li>
<li>Sell tickets for all performances at the venue</li>
<li>
Sell and/or distribute upgrade and future show tickets</li>
<li>
Monitor future events and ticket sales.</li>
<li>
Learn the Ticketmaster host system for sales and ticket orders</li>
<li>
Provide assistance with guests' special needs, such as helping those with wheelchairs.</li>
<li>
Settle seating disputes or help solve other guest concerns</li>
<li>
Become knowledgeable of all seating charts</li>
</ul>
