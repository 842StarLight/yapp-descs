<h2>Brand Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>The Brand Manager plans, organizes, directs and provides the leadership to achieve the Company's fiscal year volume/profit objectives; develops &amp; executes plans that will ensure the viability of assigned brand(s); &amp; contributes to the improvement of the Department &amp; the Company as a whole. Duties include but are not limited to:</p>
<ul>
<li>Initiates, recommends &amp; secures management/interdepartmental agreement to marketing strategies, plans &amp; budgets which include New Products, Promotion, Cost Savings, Package Changes, Pricing, Ad Strategy/Copy Development, Media Planning &amp; Stewardship, Product Changes.</li>
<li>Ensures timely &amp; high quality execution of plans in all areas outlined above &amp; makes appropriate plan changes as dictated by ongoing business conditions</li>
<li>Proposes, tests &amp; evaluates new strategic &amp; tactical approaches to all areas of the marketing/product development mix.</li>
<li>Maintains processes to continuously develop new product ideas, leads research and system evaluation of new ideas to determine their consumer, trade, financial &amp; operations viability.</li>
<li>Leads annual brand planning and budgeting efforts.</li>
<li>Manages &amp; maintains specific activities of advertising/media agencies &amp; oversees public relations brand campaigns.</li>
<li>Positively motivates &amp; clearly communicates to all departments &amp; appropriate agencies to ensure agreement &amp; timely/high quality execution of new initiatives.</li>
<li>Provides ongoing data-driven analysis &amp; definition of key opportunities for maximizing volume &amp; profit potential for assigned brands &amp; ensure consistent pursuit of these objectives.</li>
<li>Assists Sales in developing brands' general merchandising strategies, financial guidelines, etc. on a data-driven basis</li>
<li>Exercises administrative control over the personnel assigned to him/her (i.e. interviewing, training, departmental orientation, salary reviews, performance appraisals, etc.) in conjunction with the Vice President, Marketing.</li>
<li>Through individual guidance, mentors and coaches brand personnel to succeed and grow in their positions, develop their skills and future potential, maintain a high level of knowledge in the marketing industry and understand/adhere to established Company policies, procedures, philosophies and business practices.</li>
<li>Orients new employees from other disciplines on Marketing, product line and promotional issues related to assigned brands.</li>
<li>Identifies, recommends and contributes to systems and process changes that will improve productivity, capability, cooperation and/or morale of own department and other disciplines.</li>
<li>Takes affirmative action in all conditions of employment to ensure that equal opportunity efforts and results are consistent with Company policies and procedures.</li>
<li>Strengthens the Marketing Department by contributing innovative ideas, expertise, knowledge, high professional standards, Team spirit and pride in the Company</li>
</ul>
<p>A Masters' degree in Marketing or MBA or its equivalent is required with 2 to 3 years real world experience &amp;/or advanced academic Marketing environments. Proven strengths in leadership, analytical, strategic &amp; tactical thinking, managing, developing &amp; working with others &amp; motivation &amp; drive are essential. This position has supervisory responsibility of Sr. Category Associate &amp; Administrative Supervisor.</p>
</div>
