<h2>Account Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Learn and work on meaningful initiatives with some of the best and brightest in the market research industry. The NPD Group provides the world’s most successful brands with leading market research, combining consumer and retail point-of-sale data with analytic solutions to interpret today’s market trends while anticipating tomorrow’s. In addition, we offer a career filled with innovation and growth to the forward-thinking problem solvers who join our team.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Position Overview
</b></p>
<p>The Beauty Practice at NPD works with some of the most innovative companies in the industry. This team is currently seeking an Account Specialist to be an integral team member dedicated to leading global beauty companies. The Specialist will contribute to the engagement, satisfaction, and growth among our clients by providing value-added insights for decision making. The position provides the opportunity to make an impact through existing analytical skills, while developing the knowledge and skills required for continued success in client development and market research solutions.<br>
<br>
</p>
<p>This position can be based in our NYC or Port Washington office
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Responsibilities include but are not limited to:
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Analytics &amp; Insights:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Leveraging Point of Sale (POS), Consumer survey data and other NPD data software and information sources to develop analysis which help our clients grow their business and gain market share
</li>
<li>Proactively searching for meaningful trends within the data and sharing findings with clients and Beauty team members
</li>
<li>Being an expert in NPD’s retail point-of-sale data, methodology and tools for accessing data + other NPD product offerings (i.e. consumer, Store Level, etc.)
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Client Service:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Providing support to the team in managing the day to day activities to enhance client relationships, including but not limited to, sharing analyses on brand specific topics, answering relevant client questions, training/educating clients in order to drive adoption and usage of NPD’s strategic data sets, etc.
</li>
<li>Partnering with account leads and cross-functional departments to drive initiatives which support the sale and delivery of NPD’s solution
</li>
<li>Understanding our Manufacturer Clients’ organizations and their business strategies, objectives, and challenges
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Qualifications:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree
</li>
<li>2 to 4 year(s) of prior experience in a role which utilizes data and analysis to add value to clients
</li>
<li>Strong expertise in all MS Office products, Excel, PowerPoint, &amp; Word
</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability work across various data sets and reporting tools
</li>
<li>Advanced interpersonal skills and strong orientation towards building client relationships
</li>
<li>Excellent time management and organizational skills
</li>
<li>Able to set priorities and meet deadlines in a fast-paced, multitasking environment
</li>
<li>Very strong oral and written communication skills; Confident in presenting to groups
</li>
<li>Highly detail oriented – with commitment to total quality and data accuracy
</li>
<li>Knowledge of, and passion for, the Beauty or Fashion Industry is a plus
</li>
</ul>
