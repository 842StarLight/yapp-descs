<div>As the<b> Indirect Sales Manager,</b> you will be responsible for Choice indirect retail natural gas sales via a variety of indirect sales partners and/or channels. The role is responsible for delivering customer and financial performance and optimizing margin opportunities. This position will work closely with marketing, pricing, brokers, partners, and operations to deploy sales programs.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>In February of 2019 CenterPoint Energy completed an acquisition which added nearly 7,000 employees. Now you’ll have the opportunity to work with a dynamic sales team to support our $27 billion-dollar company which has a footprint in 40 states.</div>
<div>Specifically, you’ll have the opportunity to:</div>
<ul>
<li>Identify, qualify, engage, and manage indirect sales partners and enhance partner yield.</li>
<li>
Develop performance management tools for tracking and enhancing partner performance.</li>
<li>
Enforce goals and KPIs, implement improvement plans.</li>
<li>
Develop and implement training and actionable sales coaching practices as called for.</li>
<li>
Ensure sales team is fully acquainted with relevant market and product details, training modules, and policies.</li>
<li>
Communicates effectively with sales partners on an on-going basis.</li>
<li>
Provide customer feedback to marketing and operations to assist in product and customer communications development and enhance customer support.</li>
</ul>
<br>

<li>
Minimum 2 years experience in commodities, channel and/or broker sales.</li>
</ul>
