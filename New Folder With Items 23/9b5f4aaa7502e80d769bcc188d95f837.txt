<p>JobCategory: Marketing/Communications</p>
<p>Job Title: Marketing Manager</p>
<p>ReportsTo: Marketing Director</p>
<p>Position Summary:</p>
<p>Thisposition executes day-to-day marketing initiatives (outside of digitalmarketing) working with contracted partners and Visit Williamsburg team members to ensure brandcontinuity. This position is focused on consumer advertising and corporatecommunication.</p>
<p>Responsibilities:</p>
<ul><li>Support all marketing/communications initiatives:</li></ul>
<p>-Contribute to brand strategy development, contractordeliverables, and internal/external tactics</p>
<p>-Develop, review, and edit all creative content (e.g.consumer ads, visitors guide, collateral marketing material, photo and video)</p>
<p>-Ensure brand standards are adhered to throughout all categories</p>
<p>-Act as the “keeper” of the brand and advise management on those matters</p>
<ul><li>Primary contact for third-party contractors (ad agency, writers, photographers)</li></ul>
<ul><li>Review contractor invoices for accuracy and compliancewith contract terms before approval by the Marketing Director</li></ul>
<ul><li>Create on-brand presentations, sales materials, andother items as requested</li></ul>
<ul><li>Coordinate the writing and production of the annualreport</li></ul>
<ul><li>Participate in meetings, public events, press conferences, etc.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manage the organization’s photography and video library; stay on top of best practices forstoring and managing this content.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Keep abreast of trends in tourism promotion andmarketing trends</li></ul>
<ul><li>Other duties as assigned</li></ul>
<p>Qualifications and Experience:</p>

<ul>
<li>Minimum of five years progressive CVB/DMO marketing/brand management experience</li>
<li>Ability to coordinate multiple projects in various channels</li>
<li>Experience in vendor management, a hands-on approach to managing agencies to meet contract deliverables and timelines</li>
<li>Entrepreneurial team player who can multitask</li>
<li>Self-reliant, strategic, good problem solver, and results oriented</li>
<li>Exceptional written verbal communication skills</li>
</ul>
