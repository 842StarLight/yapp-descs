<br>
<b>360 Oncology Product Marketing Manager – Palo Alto, CA</b><br>
<br>
The Oncology Software Solutions (OSS) team is looking to grow our Oncology Care Management business portfolio. We have a startup culture, but part of an established global medical market share leader. Our team is looking to bring in an experienced professional in a product marketing capacity. Must love technology and blazing new trails! Has unique combination of hands-on strategic and tactical marketing abilities. Must have a passion for healthcare! Bonus if you have an agency background and want to work towards a world without fear of cancer.<br>
<br>
Key Responsibilities<ul>
<li>
Positions the 360 Oncology business line for worldwide sales growth and market penetration.</li>
<li>
Execute product marketing strategy for the oncology care coordination and SaaS product portfolio</li>
<li>
Grow sales through market development and marketing program for demand generation, thought leadership and client engagement.</li>
<li>
Generate awareness, differentiation and demand in the market through clear and differentiated value propositions, innovative marketing programs</li>
<li>
Develops and implements direct marketing programs to generate awareness, crafts go-to-market messaging, establishes pricing and adoption, conducts competitive analysis, supports the sales and services teams, and engages with current and potential customers.
</li>
<li>Create and maintain marketing collateral including customer presentations, field training presentations, online and printed marketing content and demos.</li>
<li>
Communicates with multiple audiences including internal stakeholders (employees) and external stakeholders (customers such as providers/physicians, 3rd party vendors, etc.).</li>
<li>
Ensure synergy with technical product management team, business development team and marketing team</li>
<li>
Monitor industry innovations, perform competitive analysis and prepare business and marketing requirement documentation
</li>
<li>Solicit customer, prospect and partner input (via customer calls, user groups, site visits, tradeshows, surveys, win/loss analyses, etc.) to understand market challenges and requirement</li>
<li>
Assist with developing and executing channel and partner strategy</li>
</ul>
