<p><b>Product Marketing - Media and Entertainment</b></p>
<p>
Veritone has developed a proprietary platform that unlocks the power of AI-based cognitive computing so that unstructured audio and video data can be processed, transformed and analyzed in a seamless, automated manner to generate actionable intelligence. The cloud-based open platform integrates an ecosystem of a variety of best-of-breed cognitive engines, which can be orchestrated together, to reveal valuable multivariate insights from vast amounts of audio and video data. Founded in 2014, Veritone has been recognized by some of the industry’s most prestigious forums, including the Red Herring Top 100 Award, the Edison Awards, and the SIIA Business Technology CODiE Award for Best Native Advertising Platform or Service.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Senior Product Marketing Manager - Media and Entertainment</b></p>
<p><br>
As the Product Marketing Manager for Media and Entertainment, you will lead the effort to develop our go-to-market strategy and enablement resources for a suite of products serving the world’s largest brands producing movies, TV, radio and sports programming. Our products help our Media and Entertainment customers find new ways to manage, monetize and analyze their content. As a result, you must possess a relentless curiosity in technology. We’re doing things that have never been done before. This is not a gig for someone who is not willing to dig deep and understand how AI can help our customers. After all, you will be the one defining the messaging and product positioning.<br>
<br>
<b>Key duties and responsibilities include</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Insights: Create strategic insights into how we can impact potential customers. Collaborate with Product Management and Sales to develop targets, personas and insights.</li>
<li>
Strategic Positioning: Develop product positioning and messaging that differentiates our products and services in the market. You’ll work with the field, partners, and analysts to position our products not only on the high level but also around individual new technologies or features that we bring to market.<br>
</li>
<li>Commercial Readiness: Oversee and lead all external launch readiness efforts for product introduction. This includes assisting Marketing and Agency resources with demand generation programs, Product Management with buyer and user journeys, and Customer Success, Sales and Channel partners with required education programs or resources.
</li>
<li>Content Creation: Deliver impactful customer and partner facing product assets and collateral that supports our key messages.</li>
<li>
Field Enablement: Deliver sales, partner and user enablement tools to help with acquisition and sales conversion.</li>
<li>
Assess Competitive Landscape: Oversee the monitoring and active reporting of the competitive landscape including SWOT analysis of key competition.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Mandatory skill-set:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>
 </b>At least five years of previous product marketing experience in Enterprise B2B software market, preferably in a SaaS-based business</li>
<li>
 Demonstrated experience establishing product positioning and unique selling propositions</li>
<li>
Sales enablement: A solid vision for what sales tools are needed to support a product and how to get them produced and to our sellers</li>
<li>
Demonstrated international product launch experience</li>
<li>
Exceptional communication skills – both written and verbal (English)</li>
<li>
Strong finance skills including determining product price, SaaS metrics expertise (CAC, ACV, churn analysis … you get the idea)</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
<b>
Preferred:</b></p>

<b>
 </b>Strong working knowledge and experience in the Media and Entertainment fields including, but not limited to Broadcast Networks, Studios, TV and Radio content production, ad sales and related processes</li>
<li>
 Previous Product Marketing for an enterprise class product in any of the following segments: media asset management, content management, business process automation, RPA, video and/or audio production</li>
<li>
Experience working with trade and industry analyst firms such as Gartner, Forrester, IDC, etc.</li>
<li>
Familiarity (or better!) with machine learning, artificial intelligence</li>
</ul>
