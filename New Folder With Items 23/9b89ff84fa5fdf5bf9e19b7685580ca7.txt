<p>We are looking for individuals who truly enjoy helping customers and love working as a team. <p><b>Required</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Excellent customer support skills – you should be <b>Empathetic </b>and <b>Patient </b>as many users might not be skilled in using their computers and mobile devices.</li>
<li>You must be able to <b>Accurately </b>document the issues, since our development team or other support team members may need to help this customer in the future. <b>Compassion </b>and <b>Understanding </b>for your customers is important as they may be stressed while communicating with you. <b>Responsiveness </b>is key; getting information back to your customers as quickly as possible as well as other team members makes a huge difference to our customers. You are the <b>Resource </b>that customers are relying on, so you should know the answer to their questions and know when to ask other team members for help.</li>
<li>Excellent organizational, written and oral communication skills.</li>
<li>You should quickly learn how to evaluate, troubleshoot, and follow-up on customer issues. If it needs to be escalated to the development team, you must be able to replicate the issue and document it for them clearly.</li>
<li>You should have a desire to learn, master and even teach everything you know about Vagaro.</li>
<li>High School Diploma</li>
</ul>
