<h2>Sales Contract Administrator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Description Summary</b></h1>
This role will be part of a team that manages the contract administration functions for Reagent Rental Contracts, Reagent Plus Agreements, Reagent Purchase and Service Coverage Contracts, and Government Contracts.<br>
<br>
You will be expected to execute best in class, innovative, enterprise contract proposal preparation and administration, and assist with developing policy, process and documentation to ensure the effective implementation of complex, critical contract activities, managing business opportunities and risks appropriately. Specifically, this role will consult, structure, negotiate and finalize effective, executable, strategic sales contracts and distribution agreements.<p></p>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Job Description</b></h1>
<p>
Duties and Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Work directly with Sales Contracts Manager to ensure contract terms and conditions are negotiated in accordance with established BD contract standard T&amp;C policies and procedures, as well as legal and regulatory requirements</li>
<li>
Manage the sales agreement contract lifecycle including (but not limited to) planning, analyses, drafting/redlining, negotiation, facilitating internal approvals, execution, amendment, extension and retirement</li>
<li>
Facilitate both internal and external calls, in order to review and resolve conflicts and finalize contracts</li>
<li>
Respond accurately to customer and field sales personnel inquiries; providing pricing, terms, availability, terms and conditions, etc.</li>
<li>
Ensure customer and material master data is maintained accurately and completed</li>
<li>
Support BD sales team on issue resolution and deal management regarding purchase order and Reagent Rental Contract T&amp;Cs</li>
<li>
Maintain high level of customer support-responsiveness, timeliness, customer service (contract questions, issues)</li>
<li>
Coordinate post-deal activities and ensure smooth transition with post-deal team counterparts in Instrument Order Management and/or Sales Contract Administration</li>
<li>
POC for Contract Consultant with first review of non-standard language provisions. Assist in drafting and seeking approval of new language when necessary</li>
<li>
Act as subject matter expert in preparation and analysis of proposals</li>
<li>
Develop and maintain standard policies and procedures to drive operational efficiencies and standardization of terms &amp; conditions</li>
<li>
Ensure that risks are mitigated, and provisions are preferred or acceptable per company standard; and that contract terms &amp; conditions are negotiated in accordance with established BD</li>
</ul>
