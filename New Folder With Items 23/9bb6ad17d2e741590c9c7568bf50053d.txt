<h2>Engineering Automation Developer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>We are looking for engineers who also have programming skill. An engineering degree, CAD experience and skills, strong English communications skills and some programming experience are absolutely required for all applicants. Job experience as an engineer, programmer or consultant is a plus.</p>
<br>
<p><br>
Experience with: Inventor ETO, Autodesk Intent, Siemens PLM Rulestream a strong plus</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Engineering Intent, Inc. provides enterprise-scale engineering automation solutions for manufacturing companies throughout the world. Engineering automation solutions take customer requirements and follow embedded design ‘rules’ to automatically design a ‘custom’ product such as an elevator, custom door, transformer, plastic mold, cranes, etc. For many products that are custom engineered, an automation solution can provide automatic proposal documents, pricing, sales drawings, bills-of-material, manufacturing drawings, CNC, etc. saving our customers hundreds of engineering hours.</p>

<p>Our primary office is in the Boston area, but we allow work-from-home options as well.</p>
