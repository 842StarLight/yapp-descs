<div>
POSITION</div>
<div>
This role will manage the development and delivery of IT security standards, best practices, architecture and systems to ensure information system security across the enterprise.</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Enhance and update information security policies and compliance initiatives</li>
<li>
Implements processes and methods for auditing and addressing non-compliance to information security standards.</li>
<li>
Facilitates migration of non-compliant environments to compliant environments.</li>
<li>
Conducts studies within and outside the organization to ensure compliance with standards and currency with industry security norms.</li>
<li>
Manages and participates in the planning and implementation of security administration for all IT projects. Responsible for evaluation and selection of security applications and systems.</li>
<li>
Makes recommendations and assists in the implementation of changes to work methods and procedures to make them more effective or to strengthen security measures.</li>
<li>
Manages and participates in the planning and implementation of disaster recovery and business continuity for the enterprise.</li>
<li>
Perform risk assessments and drive improvements</li>
<li>
Manage and monitor device and system security technology</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
