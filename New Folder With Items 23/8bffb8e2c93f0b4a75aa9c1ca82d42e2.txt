<h2>Product Support Technician</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><div>Armstrong Hot Water Division is looking for an individual to fill the Product Support Technician opening. Responsibilities include product support via customers and sales reps in the field and internally as questions and issues arise.

Attention to detail, follow through, and solid communication skills are all requirements and must be displayed and proven for consideration. Also required is a high level of calmness and confidence in pressure situations that demand resolution and direction.</div></div>
