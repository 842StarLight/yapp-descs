<p>Job Description</p>
<p>We are seeking to fill a full-time and part-time position at our independent insurance office in Mountlake Terrace, Washington. The position will initially serve as receptionist for the office and provide essential support to our team of experienced personal, commercial, and commercial insurance agents. Position duties will include: answering high volume of incoming calls, greeting clients, mail distribution, and data entry. Responsibilities will progress to include additional customer service and bookkeeping duties, leading to an opportunity to become a licensed insurance agent.</p>
<p>Qualifications: The successful candidate will be detail-oriented, with excellent communication skills and a friendly telephone personality. Insurance experience preferred. Other qualifications include: Proficiency with Microsoft Office software (Word, Excel, Outlook) required Experience with office support, marketing, and sales Ability to connect with people and build strong customer relationships Knowledge of insurance operations is a plus.</p>
<p>Shift 1: Approx 25 hours a week / 4-5 hours a day / 12:00 pm to 5:00 pm M-F</p>
