Description<div>
</div>
<div>
Position is responsible for supporting the sales reporting, tracking and business analysis needs of the senior leaders and sales teams within the ISG Distribution &amp; Sales (ISG D&amp;S) organization. Position is responsible for the development of data specifications, complex ad-hoc programs and reports/dashboards to meet ISG D&amp;S's management information needs. Position has knowledge of many of the data systems needed to be used in the solution of problem areas, testing, reconciling and investigating the data which underlie the results. Uses analysis of business managements to explain trends in business. This individual will have oversight of a small team of staff to insure all daily responsibilities are completed. Knowledge of the Individual Life Insurance or Annuity business is a plus. This individual is expected to be a subject area expert in one or more specific skill sets, business areas or products.
