<h2>Director, Commercial Enablement</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>Job Description
<p>The <b>Director, Commercial Enablement </b>will help drive all efforts to ensure that Flexera’s commercial teams are equipped with the knowledge, skills, and tools to achieve the extraordinary. This position will focus on the design and delivery of development programs and the processes and tools to support the commercial team’s goals and objectives. This includes the implementation and ongoing management of the Flexera selling approach, commercial messaging and playbooks, manager coaching skills, onboarding program, and continuous learning.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><i>Key Focus Areas:
</i></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Commercial Effectiveness </b>: Identify and share best practices across the commercial org, build compelling and crisp communications (win/loss stories, motivation/inspiration, knowledge/best practices), channel feedback to other teams, and manage content geared towards driving improved sales productivity
</p>
<p><b>Sales Onboarding </b>: Utilize industry best-practices to build an effective multi-phase onboarding and ongoing training program to ensure initial development of the commercial team
</p>
<p><b>Ongoing Skill Development </b>: Build out a calendar of formal and informal sessions covering product launches, competitive updates, M&amp;A, objection handling, skill reinforcement, internal process updates, etc.
</p>
<p><b>Manager Coaching &amp; Developmen </b>t: Monitor manager coaching skills, levels, expectations; launch assessment, training, and development for managers of the commercial organization
</p>
<p><b>Messaging </b>: Partner with Marketing to improve our commercial messaging e.g. pitch decks, talk tracks, scripts, cadence, and develop a program to launch it to the field
</p>
<p><b>Industry Knowledge </b>: Become an expert on our market, customer personas, and competition to increase impact and effectiveness of enablement resources
</p>
<p></p>
<p><i>Partner with the Head of Commercial Enablement to accomplish the following:
</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Create and implement a scalable end-to-end enablement and training program designed to take us to the next level
</li>
<li>Refine commercial playbooks and processes, and uncover critical skills and competency gaps for success
</li>
<li>Organize and lead monthly and quarterly development sessions for the revenue &amp; customer success teams
</li>
<li>Refine and deliver our New Hire onboarding program; drive highly engaging and interactive content with built-in assessments to ensure an effective ramp time and improve productivity
</li>
<li>Develop additional learning opportunities (both formal training and on-the-job experiences) for skill
</li>
<li>development at different stages of career path (SDR, Inside sales rep, Major Account Manager)
</li>
<li>Leverage technology to measure performance and drive improvement; e.g. CRMs, video learning software, etc.
</li>
<li>Gauge the ongoing evaluation and performance of the commercial team; identify skills gaps based on performance and coach the entire team on an ongoing basis
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><i>What you’ll need:
</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Proven commercial enablement and training success including ability to demonstrate impact on a high growth commercial organization
</li>
<li>BA/BS degree and at least 5-7 years of related experience in commercial training and enablement
</li>
<li>Experience working with B2B sales teams
</li>
<li>Exceptional written and verbal communication skills
</li>
<li>Ability to prioritize tasks effectively and be a team player with strong interpersonal skills and ability to inspire and motivate others
</li>
<li>Ability to thrive in a fast-paced, unpredictable environment
</li>
<li>Adept at project management and cross-functional collaboration</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
