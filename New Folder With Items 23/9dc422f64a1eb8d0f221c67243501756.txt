<p><b>About the Role</b></p>
<p>
As the Global VP of Marketing at Brivo, you will drive lead generation, brand marketing, product marketing, and sales enablement. With a 15+ year record of innovation and industry leadership, we are a self-sustaining high-growth company that develops both the software and hardware components of our service offering. We currently serve over 40,000 B2B clients worldwide with a significant push toward further international expansion. Your mission will be to work with the critical technologies that enable our vision, and lead execution with a team of talented engineers.</p>
<p>
You will be a motivated, high-energy marketing executive that will help drive our next phase of rapid growth. You will lead a team of marketing professionals to drive both channel and end-user opportunities. You will also help extend the Brivo brand outside the U.S. as we become the global standard for cloud-based physical security.</p>
