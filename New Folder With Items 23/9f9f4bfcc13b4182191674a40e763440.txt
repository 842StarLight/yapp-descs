<div><b>About the Role</b></div>
<div>
Provide support and coordination for the Buyer and Associate Buyer in specified merchandise categories for all channels of sales. Input, proof and analyze data for all areas of merchandising management.<br>
</div>

<div><b>You're excited about this opportunity because you will...</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Print and distribute weekly sales reports</li>
<li>Order and track photo samples and coordinate movement of merchandise throughout photo process
</li>
<li>Enter and maintain all price changes for all categories related to promotions and markdowns on a weekly basis
</li>
<li>Work in PLM system to set up and maintenance of all skus</li>
<li>Prepare analysis as directed</li>
<li>Assist in quarterly retail floor sets both for line review and Burlingame and Roseville set ups</li>
<li>General administrative duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
