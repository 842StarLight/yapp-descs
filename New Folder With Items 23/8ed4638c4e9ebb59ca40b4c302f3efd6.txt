<p>We are seeking an<b> Assistant Community Manager</b> to assist in all phases of the operation of the property: general administration, maintenance, leasing, resident relations, collection of rent, and control of all personnel and resources, so the property is maintained at all times in good physical condition and with a stabilized fiscal operation.</p>

<ul><li> Collect rents and work delinquent accounts;</li></ul>
<ul><li> Make rent deposits, post rent payments;</li></ul>
<ul><li> Process invoices for payment;</li></ul>
<ul><li> Maintain records, reports and files;</li></ul>
<ul><li> Assist with eviction in rent agreement violation cases;</li></ul>
<ul><li> Process utility reads;</li></ul>
<ul><li> Prepare and process leases and related forms;</li></ul>
<ul><li> Resolve customer and public complaints;</li></ul>
<ul><li> Assist in planning resident activities;</li></ul>
<ul><li> Move-ins/Move- outs, giving special attention to mobile home inspections prior to move-in;</li></ul>
<ul><li> High School Diploma or equivalent is required;</li></ul>
<ul><li> 3 years of related management experience in retail, hospitality or property management preferred;</li></ul>
<ul><li> Knowledge of collection process preferred;</li></ul>
<ul><li> Knowledge of computer systems is a must. Proficiency in rent management based software preferred;</li></ul>
<ul><li> Must be flexible, willing to work Saturdays, and able to adapt in a fast paced and ever-changing environment;</li></ul>
<ul><li> It is expected and required that all business practices are conducted in accordance with company policy and procedure;</li></ul>
<ul><li> Adhere to all applicable fair housing laws governing real estate sales, leasing and management maintenance services.</li></ul>
