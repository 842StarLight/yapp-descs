<p><b>Title: </b>Principal Analyst/Director, Retail Insights
</p>
<p><b>Division: </b>Consulting - Retail

<p></p>
<p>If finding the deeper meaning behind data excites you and if a curiosity and passion for retail drives you, then you belong on our Retail Insights team! Kantar is seeking a Principal Analyst or Director, Retail Insights to join our team. As a thought leader, you will have a foundational understanding of how retailers operate and compete, while beginning to draw connections to the broader marketplace.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Kantar is a specialist growth consultancy. With over 1,000 analysts, thought leaders, software developers and expert consultants we help clients develop and execute brand, marketing, retail, sales and shopper strategies to deliver growth. Kantar owns market leading assets including PoweRanking, GrowthFinder, Global Monitor, RetaiI IQ, RichMix, XTEL and Marketing, Insights and Purpose 2020. We track 1200 retailers globally, have purchase data on over 200 million shoppers and forecast social, cultural and consumer trends across the world. Join us and you’ll be part of 30,000 colleagues and 12 world leading research, data and insight brands at Kantar. Our unique consumer insight inspires clients to flourish in an extraordinary world.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>KEY OUTCOMES
</b></p>
<p>Demonstrate excellent written and verbal communication skills
</p>
<ul>
<li><i>Conduct independent and original research with mastery over the subject area of retail, with an emphasis on how grocery (i.e., in-store and online) and mass retail (i.e., Target) is evolving
</i></li>
<li><i>Begin to manage insights and analysis for your coverage area, including forecasts, website content, presentations and more customized deliverables
</i></li>
<li><i>Demonstrate team collaboration by identifying and contributing to key topical and thematic narratives that cut across coverage areas
</i></li>
<li><i>Draw implications that have meaning and relevance for Kantar clients (i.e., suppliers, retailers, agencies and brands) in your core coverage area(s)
</i></li>
<li>
<i>Develop an external client-facing brand, and establish business development skills and capabilities </i><i>Mentor more junior analysts about retail and thought leadership approaches<br>
</i>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>CAPABILITIES
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Background in retail, market research, finance, economics, marketing, and/or consulting
</li>
<li>Possess effective project management skills, with the ability to perform independently and as part of a team
</li>
<li>Proven ability to analyze and draw actionable insights from qualitative and quantitative research
</li>
<li>Proven analytical or financial acumen
</li>
<li>An understanding of CPG, retailer or shopper/consumer needs
</li>
<li>Direct Target or grocer background/experience a plus
</li>
<li>5 or more years of experience required<br>
</li>
</ul>
