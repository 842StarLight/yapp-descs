<h2>Initiative - Associate, Paid Social</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div><div>
<p>We are looking for you - dynamic, best-in-class talent - to join the Initiative team as an Associate, Paid Social. As an Associate, Paid Social you will assist in the day-to-day stewarding of both account and media activity on assigned campaigns for our top clients, and help take social media planning to the next level. This entry-level opportunity will kick-start your career in media and provide a platform to partner with the world’s leading consumer brands.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Functions</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Work closely with Account/Media team on the development, implementation and optimization of campaigns to successfully execute the defined strategy and achieve business results</li>
<li>Serve as resource in terms of gathering and distributing research around the industry or our clients</li>
<li>Develop a strong working relationship with the media sales community, specifically our partners, to become the point person for day to day needs and account maintenance for all active campaigns:</li>
<li>Investigate new sites/media vehicles relevant to clients’ media campaigns</li>
<li>Work with sales reps to develop new tactics (placements, creative units) to improve performance of clients’ advertising</li>
<li>Use research tools to develop profile of target audiences with view into their social/online behaviors, media consumption, etc.</li>
<li>Ensure agreed upon pacing and delivery of campaigns, prompt delivery of reports to clients and accurate/useful performance reports</li>
<li>Become proficient with 3rd party ad-serving, tracking &amp; analytic systems to be able to accurately input media plan details and launch campaigns</li>
<li>Maintain detailed files of all pertinent records (latest media plans, performance reports, etc.) relevant to clients’ business with Society</li>
<li>Reconcile all campaigns to ensure that client receives all planned media and is invoiced/credited properly</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<p>Education:</p>
<ul>
<li>Associate's degree or work related experience</li>
</ul>
<p>Work Experience:</p>
<ul>
<li>Previous internship in related field strongly preferred</li>
<li>0-2 year experience in advertising or interactive digital media preferred</li>
</ul>
<p>Skills:</p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrated enthusiasm for interactive media / marketing with strong working knowledge of the Internet – and an active user / participant in social media a plus.</li>
<li>Proactive ability to multi-task, strong written and oral communication skills, excellent organizational aptitude, attention to detail, and accuracy when working with numbers</li>
<li>Strong desire to learn and build knowledge of: social media, native advertising, marketing principles, marketing platforms (e.g., Facebook and Twitter Advertising), interactive media, and direct response strategies and tactics</li>
</ul>
