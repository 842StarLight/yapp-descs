<h2>Application Manager, Enterprise Integration</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>
<b>Job Summary</b>
<p>This Application Manager is responsible for technical leadership of a group of developers, testers and analysts maintaining the overall health and soundness of the Enterprise Integration application suite that includes mainframe and distributed solutions. The ideal candidate has to be able to lead a diverse group of individuals to facilitate system solutions that supports our business roadmap and growth expectations. The candidate is expected to be a self-starter, requiring minimal oversight, is results-oriented, with a strong sense of teamwork. The role requires proven success in leading development teams supporting both mainframe and distributed applications. The roles also requires the ability to build and maintain working relationships with team members, vendors, and other departments within Alliance Data.
</p>
<p><b>Essential Job Functions</b></p>
<div>
<div><div>Lead a team of highly engaged associates and contractors, ensuring that team meets or exceeds the defined role expectations. Ensure that direct reports understand role expectations.</div></div>
<div><div>
Provide development team with ongoing coaching and feedback, performance management, career development and administrative support.</div></div>
<div><div>
Act in a leadership role to provide guidance to the development team and act as a liaison for multiple areas of the business and external clients. As necessary, work across the organization and function as Project Development Lead for enterprise-scale efforts.</div></div>
<div><div>
Use recognized industry models and methodologies to develop, execute, and continuously improve development practices in collaboration with all major stakeholder and functional roles.</div></div>
<div><div>
Actively participate in the development of technology and integrated application roadmaps.</div></div>
<div><div>
Coaching and mentoring development staff to enable them to reach their peak potential and cultivate high-performing teams.</div></div>
<div><div>
Appropriately manage resource capacity and demand to meet business delivery and production support expectations.</div></div>
<div><div>
Develop and maintain organizational strategies that support current and future workforce, business, and financial needs.</div></div>
<div><div>
Utilize the software development lifecycle to implement technology solutions that create and enhance applications.</div></div>
<div><div>
Continuously improve applications and processes with a focus on quality, efficiency, and user experience.</div></div>
<div><div>
Cultivate relationships across roles and organizations to maximize collaboration, partnership, and efficiency.</div></div>
<div><div>
Provide peer coaching and feedback formally and informally. Request and utilize feedback to foster personal development.</div></div>
</div>
