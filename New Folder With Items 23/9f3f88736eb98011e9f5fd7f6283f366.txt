<h2>Business Development Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Whereoware is hiring a Business Development Representative to act as the liaison between our Marketing and Sales teams. Your role will be to seek new business opportunities by contacting and developing relationships with potential customers.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Day-in-the-life</b></p>
<p>
Whereoware is an agile digital agency with clients across industries and verticals. Day-to-day projects for the Business Development Representative will vary, but may look like:</p>
<ul>
<li>Qualify leads from marketing campaigns as sales opportunities</li>
<li>Contact potential clients through cold calls and emails</li>
<li>Presenting Whereoware to potential clients</li>
<li>Identify client needs and suggest appropriate products/services</li>
<li>Proactively seek new business opportunities in the market</li>
<li>Set up meetings or calls between (prospective) clients and Sales Team</li>
<li>Stay up-to-date with new products/services and new pricing/payment plans</li>
<li>Ensure proper after-sales service</li>
<li>Use communication skills to cultivate strong relationship with customers</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p><b>
 </b></p>
<p><b>The Business Development Representative will have:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Have a B.S. or B.A.</li>
<li>1-5 years of experience in Business Development/Sales</li>
<li>Cold-Calling experience</li>
<li>Salesforce experience is a plus</li>
<li>Previous experience developing leads from marketing campaigns</li>
<li>Ability to effectively change focus in a rapidly evolving environment</li>
<li>Self-starter, but also collaborates well with team members</li>
<li>Highly communicative team player</li>
<li>Fluent English and strong writing and communication skills</li>
<br>
</ul>
