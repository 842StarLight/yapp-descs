<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Company Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Dematic is a leading supplier of integrated automated technology, software and services to optimize the supply chain. Dematic employs over 7,000 skilled logistics professionals to serve its customers globally, with engineering centers and manufacturing facilities located around the world. Dematic is one brand under the KION Group of companies and has implemented more than 6,000 integrated systems for a customer base that includes small, medium and large companies doing business in a variety of market sectors.<br>
<br>
Headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia, Dematic is a member of KION Group, a global leader in industrial trucks, related services and supply chain solutions. Across more than 100 countries worldwide, the KION Group designs, builds and supports logistics solutions that optimize material and information flow within factories, warehouses and distribution centers. The company is the largest manufacturer of industrial trucks in Europe, the second-largest producer of forklifts globally and a leading provider of warehouse automation.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>The Role
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>The Manager of Global Commercial Finance will report to and support the Director Global Commercial Finance in partnering with the Global Commercial Leadership Team (GCLT) to drive global business development and vertical market strategy. The ideal candidate will have strong communication and relationship management skills that are essential when coordinating globally and making recommendations to the management of the company. They will be a strong change-agent and implement changes quickly to drive improved results.</div>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Key Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Supports and provides additional transparency to global pricing strategies, including developing new strategies and governance in alignment with global partners</li>
<li>
Evaluate effectiveness of pricing strategies and recommends modifications to pricing structures as needed to align to divisional and corporate strategy</li>
<li>
Partner closely with the sales team to provide thoughtful and strategic solutions that will drive profitable growth with key customers</li>
<li>
Improve reporting in order to increase visibility to and understanding of cost drivers in order to identify solutions</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
Supports GCLT in modeling financial impacts and outcomes as requested</li>
<li>
Work in collaboration with wider finance leadership team to drive optimum margin and growth</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
Evaluate profit and cost efficiencies in the Commercial organization</li>
<li>
Identify trends and analyze key metrics and present findings to senior management</li>
<li>
Provide in-depth analysis of opportunities, sales performance, pricing trends and business direction</li>
<li>
Develop and/or mentor one or more analysts</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>
Partner with key stakeholders to solicit feedback to develop and implement step level improvements to enhance efficiency and productivity of reporting.</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Improve on tools utilized by site management to run business; Recommend and implement corrective actions</li></ul>
