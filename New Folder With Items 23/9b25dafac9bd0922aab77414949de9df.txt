<p>
 <b>JOB SUMMARY:</b></p>
<p>
The Sales Development Representative will focus on setting qualified appointments for our Sales team. This is achieved through managing the lead lifecycle including, but not limited to: pre-call research and planning, cold calling, creating email-based communications, and following up on marketing generated leads. The SDR will qualify all leads to meet basic qualification standards with the goal of closing for a meeting with aligned sales reps. This position can be located in either our Hopkinton, MA or Sarasota, FL offices.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
SPECIFIC DUTIES and RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>

Qualify potential opportunities through consultative conversations</p></li>
