<h2>Manager in Training</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>IMMEDIATE OPPORTUNITIES - MANAGER IN TRAINING POSITIONS !</b></p>
<p>With over 2,250 locations across five countries, FirstCash is the leading international operator of pawn stores. At FirstCash, Inc. (First Cash Pawn, Cash America Pawn, Cashland, &amp; other brands), we take pride in our employees because they are competitive, passionate salespeople. We are committed to building a brand that offers our employees work-life balance and our customers a peace of mind. We recognize that inclusion makes us stronger.</p>
<p><b>Reports to: </b><b>Store Manager</b></p>
<p><b>Supervisory Responsibility: </b><b>Shift Leader(s), Pawnbrokers or Sales &amp; Loan Associates in absence of Store Manager</b></p>
<p><b>Position Summary: </b></p>
<p>Accountable for the day-to-day operations of the store, promotes a positive and productive environment. The Assistant Store Manager partners with the Store Manager to lead and develop store employees, and recruit and retain top talent. This role partners with the Store Manager to maximize financial goals and manage Customer Relations and Customer Satisfaction. Leading by example and promoting our company values the Assistant Store Manager sets the tone for a customer-centric environment. Alongside the Store Manager, analyzes store’s financial statements to ensure financial goals are met, makes adjustments as necessary. Commits to company asset protection through inventory control, following policies and procedures and securing customer’s loans. With the use of internal and external marketing tools promotes store and targets new customer acquisition.</p>
<p><b>General Duties and Responsibilities: </b></p>
<p>(This is a representative list of the general duties the position may be asked to perform and is not all-inclusive.)</p>
<p><b>Management Training Program</b></p>
<p>For the first eight weeks of employment, the new hire will participate in our Management Training Program. This fast track leadership development curriculum is designed to get the trainee up to speed in the most efficient way possible. This program combines on-the-job experience with formal training (including classroom and e-Learning) in an eight week program. In addition to their direct supervisor, the new hire will be paired with a mentor to develop their skills. The program is designed to provide the knowledge, skills and abilities needed to help ensure success at FirstCash.</p>
<p><b>Partners with Store Manager to: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Facilitate a customer-centric environment by engaging customers in a friendly and professional manner and rapidly addressing customer issues</li>
<li>Monitor and assist in the training of Pawnbrokers or Sales and Loan Associates so their development meets the prescribed FirstCash standards</li>
<li>Recruit top talent, sourcing candidates through company-prescribed channels</li>
<li>Administer processes and procedures within the store, which include inventory, loan management, merchandising, store presentation, store systems, and enterprise-level reporting</li>
<li>Evaluate store financial statements and drive profitability through communication and goal setting</li>
<li>Ensure that operating standards meet or exceed FirstCash standards</li>
<li>Monitor loan qualification and buy processes to ensure value and integrity of all loans issued</li>
<li>Complete assigned tasks and training for self-development as requested</li>
<li>Communicate clearly and precisely to employees to ensure that they understand store mission and profit objectives</li>
<li>Remain up to date on product knowledge</li>
<li>Act as manager on duty in the absence of Store Manager</li>
<li>Other related duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Minimum Qualifications: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>High School Diploma or GED</li>
<li>Minimum 18 years of age</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to effectively supervise others</li>
<li>Entrepreneurial spirit</li>
<li>Strong desire to work, serve customers and work with people; team oriented</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>Cash handling experience required</li>
<li>High energy sales personality with knowledge of jewelry and general merchandise</li>
<li>Detailed oriented with strong organizational skills</li>
<li>Solid written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Analytical skills</li>
<li>Ability to work independently with minimal supervision</li>
<li>Excellent work ethic and strong business sense</li>
<li>Computer literate and able to use common office machines</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Work Location: </b></p>
<p>Primarily indoors. Employees may be asked to assist a customer on the exterior of the building (within our parking lots) or to retrieve products stored in exterior storage buildings or other areas.</p>
<p><b>Work Postures/Requirements: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Standing</b>– Frequently throughout work shifts as customers are assisted at the sales counter or on the sales floor; and in non-customer areas for purposes of inventory maintenance and management.</li>
<li>
<b>Sitting</b>– Occasionally throughout shift in order to complete sales documentation and other paperwork.</li>
<li>
<b>Bending</b>– Frequently bending in all directions, subject to proper bending techniques as products are brought into or out of pawn or sold (from customer, to storage, to sales floor, to counter).</li>
<li>
<b>Reaching</b>– Frequently as products are retrieved from storage shelves, carts, and customers.</li>
<li>
<b>Walking</b>– Frequently within the store as customers are assisted and stock retrieved.</li>
<li>
<b>Climbing</b>– Frequently to access elevated storage locations.</li>
<li>
<b>Kneeling</b>– Occasionally.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Physical Demands/Requirements: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Lifting/Carrying</b>– Due to the nature of the pawn industry, the types of products and materials that may be lifted, carried or handled during a work shift varies from day-to-day and week-to-week. On average, a pawn store employee will handle jewelry, firearms, tools, bicycles, and electronics (stereo, televisions, speakers) on a daily basis. The frequency of these lifts and the weight of the items handled will also vary. Employees may not lift or carry products or materials above the employee’s safe lifting and carrying amounts, or above any medical restriction. The maximum weight one person will handle ranges from 50-70 pounds. Employees must be capable of lifting 50 pounds. Carts and dollies may be used to limit the carrying distance of any product.</li>
<li>
<b>Tool Usage</b>– No tools are used on a regular or routine basis during a normal workday. Jeweler’s tools and other power tools are used occasionally.</li>
<li>
<b>Chemicals Usage</b>– Occasional.</li>
<li>
<b>Work Hours</b>– Assistant Store Managers will work 40+ hours a week. Hours worked are based on the needs of the business.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Definitions</b>:</p>
<p>Occasional = 20% of time or less</p>
<p>Frequent = 20-50% of the time</p>
<p>Continuous = 50% of the time or more</p>
