<h2>Operations Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>As Project Manager for Insight, you will enter a high-energy and tech savvy world. We are looking for someone who is a team player, an excellent communicator and has experience managing successful projects. If you’re a strong leader who is self-starting, a great problem-solver, and motivated, then you could be perfect fit!</p>
<p>As a Project Manager, you can expect to:</p>
<p>* Multi-task – You will spend 90% of your time leading and managing projects, and about 10% of your time on Business Development.</p>
<p>* Take Ownership – You will manage, direct and oversee all aspects of your projects. You will be monitoring and maintaining client and internal project schedules and engaging in regular status meetings.</p>
<p>* Be a Leader – You will motivate your team to find successful solutions. You will work with internal leadership to help drive best practices.</p>
<p>* Be Challenged – Your job is never done as a Project Manager. You will be assisting sales in business development activities, helping author Statements of Work (SOW) and presenting Insight’s Project Management capabilities to prospective clients; all in a single day’s work.</p>
<p>What we are looking for:</p>
<p>* Combination of 3-5 years of education and IT services experience.</p>
<p>* Experience in specific technology areas including networking, storage, infrastructure lifecycle services and enterprise systems.</p>
<p>* Project Management Professional (PMI) certification is required within 6 months of placement in Project Manager role. Active PMI membership is preferred.</p>
<p>* Proficient in Microsoft tools including, but not limited to, Microsoft Office, MS Project, MS Project Server, SharePoint.</p>
