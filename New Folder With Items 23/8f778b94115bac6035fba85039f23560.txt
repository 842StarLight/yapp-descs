<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Are you looking for a career with a strong, local business with the backing and support of a brand people recognize and trust? Working at a local Allstate dealership insurance agency may be your answer!</div>
<div></div>
<div>
This is not your typical insurance agent sales job. As a Licensed Sales Professional working inside of a thriving car dealership in Tacoma, you will work in a fast paced, high energy atmosphere. You'll partner with the car sales staff who walk warm leads directly to you through out the day. Meetface to face with customers who need your services and don't be afraid to pull a customer into your office for a quote. Be part of a sales team focused on providing property &amp; casualty and life insurance products that help customers protect their homes, cars, and retirement incomes, and live a good life.</div>
<div></div>
<div>
With Allstate Auto Dealership Agency Owners across the U.S., there are opportunities to start a career as a Licensed Sales Professional all over the United States. If you have a passion for helping others and an interest in building your career with a strong local business, this is the perfect opportunity for you!</div>
