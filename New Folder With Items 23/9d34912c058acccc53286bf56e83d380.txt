<h2>Regional Director - General Commercial Sales West</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Job Description<br>
<br>
<p>The Regional Director of General Commercial Sales role is an exciting opportunity to lead and grow a team of Territory Managers either in our Broomfield, CO location or remotely within assigned region.</p>
<p>
The primary responsibility of this role is to hire, coach, motivate, and mentor a scaling team to achieve sales goals. This person will be a passionate, data-driven self-starter who is not afraid to jump in themselves. A leader with a proven track record of success in both managing complex sales cycles and leading high-performing sales teams.</p>
<p>
This person will be responsible for managing a team that is focused on executing net-new logo acquisition strategy, expanding our existing customer portfolio, and driving revenue within the assigned territory of our general commercial segment (customer size 1,000-5,000 employees). This is an opportunity to be a change-maker by influencing the market, the company, and our customers on their digital transformation journey.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Manage and coordinate the tactical and strategic direction of Territory Managers engaged in promoting and selling solutions</li>
<li>
Develop a strong understanding of key differentiators, internal/external systems, sales methodologies and process in the sales cycle.</li>
<li>
Drive revenue by helping the sales team navigate the customer buying process, engaging key stakeholders, and ultimately closing the deal</li>
<li>
Execute against bookings plan by helping sales team navigate the customer buying process, engage key stakeholders and support the close of deals.</li>
<li>
Deliver a sales function that drives customer affinity and scalable, sustainable growth</li>
<li>
Partner with Customer Success and Sales Engineers to ensure our customers receive a solution that fits their needs.</li>
<li>
Provide strong coaching, mentoring and adherence to Conga best practices and methodologies</li>
<li>
Ensure your team is keeping accurate notes in Salesforce.com to ensure we have accurate data to grow the business</li>
<li>
Upgrade current sales techniques or procedures based on team performance and new selling techniques</li>
<li>
Empower team to achieve sales at or above stated quota and goals month over month</li>
<li>
Help the team accurately forecast opportunities</li>
<li>
Monitor, support, and coach Territory Managers with prospecting within existing account base as well as new logo acquisition, deal control, and closing business</li>
<li>
Ensure sales, finance, and legal policies and procedures are met</li>
<li>
Build a trusting, open-communication environment for your team</li>
<li>
Analyze regional market trends and discover new opportunities for growth</li>
<li>
Responsible for territory value growth and customer satisfaction within assigned territory</li>
<li>
Establish, build and maintain executive relationships to form strategic partnerships and influence long-term strategy</li>
<li>
Demonstrate subject matter expertise on all Conga solutions and services. Understand and communicate the business value and ROI of our solutions</li>
</ul>
</div>
