<b>Position Overview</b><br>
<b>
</b><br>
For our Service Sales Department based in Orlando, FL, we are looking for a highly motivated individual to join our Proposals team who will work with the Technical Sales leads to develop life cycle cost models for wind opportunities in the Americas region with focus on the US &amp; Canada, and execute the steps of the sales process including cost modeling, approvals, proposals, contract negotiations and contract exhibits with the respective target customers.<br>
</div>
<div>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Support the Technical Sales leads with customer bids, contract exhibits and contract negotiations</li>
<li>Prioritize the activities of a multi-functional team in the areas of specification review, service product selection, commercial strategy, cost/price development and proposal generation.</li>
<li>Coordination of proposal development process</li>
</ul>
</div>
<ul><li>Lead development of calculations for cost and price models for wind turbine service contracts</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>Coordinate and distribute high quality Sales Business Approval packages to management</li>
<li>Develop customer offers, and subsequent contracts</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Align with Wind Power Onshore Operations, Supply Chain, EHS, Engineering and other Product Lines, regionally and globally, to develop service offerings</li></ul>
<ul><li>Support internal and external processes, initiatives and presentations for management</li></ul>
<ul><li>Utilize Salesforce.com for opportunity and CRM management</li></ul>
