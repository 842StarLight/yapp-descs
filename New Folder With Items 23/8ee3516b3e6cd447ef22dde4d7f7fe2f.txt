<h2>Integration Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Date:</b> Aug 20, 2019<br>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Summary:</b></h3>
<p>We are now looking for an Integration Engineer to analyze, prepare, implement and verify the configuration and integration of a node, network and/or system. Your scope of work could include the scenarios of introduction, upgrade expansion, functionality and capacity. Your work will in part form our customer legacy.</p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities:</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>You will support pre-sales activities, including pre-studies</li>
<li>Plan the implementation of the product configuration / integration work</li>
<li>Execute product configuration</li>
<li>Execute integration and migration work</li>
<li>Prepare system test, module test and accept test</li>
<li>You will work to identify and drive improvements</li>
<li>Post project activities</li>
<li>E2e technical understanding</li>
<li>Execute test</li>
<li>Scripting &amp; coding</li>
<li>Knowledge sharing and collaboration skills</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Key Qualifications:</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>Education: Academic degree, minimum on bachelor level, in engineering (IT, Telecom) or</li>
<li>3-5 years’ experience of deploy system test and lead testing team.</li>
<li>Min years of experience: (Recruiter to supply)</li>
<li>Domain experience: (Recruiter to supply area of expertise – e.g.: Cloud, BSS, OSS etc.)</li>
<li>Creating &amp; innovating</li>
<li>Applying expertise &amp; technology</li>
<li>Analytical learning and researching skills</li>
<li>Delivering results &amp; meeting customer expectations</li>
<li>You will need excellent planning and organizing skills</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Additional Requirements:</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>If you have ISEB/ISTQB software testing qualifications that would be an advantage</li>
</ul>
