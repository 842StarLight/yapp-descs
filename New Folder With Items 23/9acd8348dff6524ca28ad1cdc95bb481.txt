Structural Designer, Burbank, CA
<br><br>
<b>The opportunity:</b><br>
Create and design innovative product packaging to ensure
<br>product integrity and appeal under varying shipping and display conditions for multiple
<br>market segments. Test, analyze, and select materials for packaging based on durability
<br>and cost effectiveness.
<br><br>
<b>How you will impact WestRock:</b><br>
Creates innovative design solutions and produces
<br>samples to meet customer requirements.
<br>Reviews sample requests for layout and planning
<br>of project with key stakeholders.
<br>Produces white samples and comps in accordance
<br>with customer requirements/specifications.
<br>Effectively manages multiple projects and
<br>provides ongoing status reports to key stakeholders.
<br>Develops specifications and diagrams to be used
<br>for production orders.
<br>Creates set-up instructions and/or animations for
<br>end user.
<br>Supports sales team including interaction with
<br>customers and/or vendors.
<br>Maintains stock inventory and evaluates possible
<br>stock alternatives.
<br>Brings in new substrates for packaging.
<br>Reviews in-process production orders to assure
<br>conformance to design functionality.
<br>Facilitates or executes product testing as
<br>required.
<br>Participates in industry trade shows and events
<br>including set-up and/or professional development opportunities.
<br>Performs other job duties as assigned.
<br><br>
<b>What you need to succeed:</b><br>
Must have a minimum of three years experience in structural design, special packaging and entertainment media
<br>preferred.
<br>Must have a minimum of three
<br>experience in CAD program Artios or similar software.
<br>Experience in 3D programs (i.e. Solidworks) strongly preferred.
<br>Must have knowledge of approaches and methods for
<br>manufacturing products or product components.
<br>Must have knowledge of methodologies, tools and
<br>techniques for designing, building and managing the manufacture of new
<br>products.
<br>Must have knowledge of the methodology and
<br>associated phases, activities and deliverables for the design of new
<br>products and the enhancement of existing products; ability to apply the
<br>methodology in diverse scenarios.
<br>Must have knowledge of the methodology and
<br>associated phases, activities and deliverables for manufacturing new or
<br>enhancing existing products.
<br>Must have ability to design, plan and execute
<br>testing strategies and tactics to ensure product quality.
<br>Must be proficient in the use of current design
<br>software (i.e. Adobe Creative Suite ).
<br>Must be able to perform all essential functions
<br>of the job with or without reasonable accommodation.
