<h2>Administrative Professional (Audit Support Technician)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>TMA is looking for a self-motivated, personable, team player to join the Special Projects Department in our Charlotte, NC office in the full-time role of OTC Audit Support Technician. Requires a team spirit with excellent communication skills and advanced data entry and excel skills. Must be detail oriented and able to multi-task. College degree preferred.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Requirements for Audit Support Technician</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Be personable and efficient.</li>
<li>Have strong working knowledge of Microsoft Word.</li>
<li>Have strong working knowledge of Excel.</li>
<li>Data entry experience.</li>
<li>Be well organized and have the ability to multi-task and prioritize.</li>
<li>Be eager to take on new responsibilities and work as a team.</li>
<li>Attention to detail a must.</li>
<li>Previous administrative experience ideal.</li>
<br>
</ul>

<p>Tax Management Associates is a national property tax consulting and audit firm. TMA works exclusively with state and local government clients to provide services related to this area of taxation and tax administration. In business for over thirty years, TMA's growth has created opportunities across the country. TMA sales professionals create and maintain client relationships all over the United States and assist in the development and delivery of all of TMA's products and services.</p>
