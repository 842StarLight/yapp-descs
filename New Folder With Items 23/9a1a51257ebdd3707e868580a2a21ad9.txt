<p><b>Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Coordinates with warehouse to support daily shipping activity; tracking the location and movement of goods to ensure that goods are shipped in a timely and cost-effective manner</li>
<li>Facilitates relationships with our carrier partners; monitors freight pick up/deliveries, audit of freight bills, POD requests and filing of claims</li>
<li>Assist in the generation of documentation for export shipments; creating documents that meet governmental standards and help ensure goods can cross the border and be cleared by customs upon arrival at destination</li>
<li>Familiar with governmental regulations pertaining to NAFTA, CTPAT and HTS /Schedule B determination</li>
<li>Maintenance and input of data into the TMS/WMS including carrier set up, routing tables for both pre-paid and collect customers and order status monitoring</li>
<li>Responsible for the timely investigation and mitigation of customer chargeback claims</li>
<li>Partner with warehouse ops, sales and finance in the development of strategies aimed at reducing exposure to chargeback claims</li>
<li>Be able to back-up, support, and train with other transportation support team members</li>
<li>Respond, collaborates &amp; communicate effectively with other departments in the organization pertaining to transportation operations</li>
<li>Support and assist in the creation of SOP’s, training, KPI metrics and continuous improvement methodologies</li>
<li>Participate as an effective team member with the ability to multi-task in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>Establish and maintain all necessary procedures to assure a clean, properly organized and safe/healthy environment for all personnel</li>
<li>Other job duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in Business related field, or equivalent work experience required</li>
<li>3+ years of Traffic, Transportation, Order Delivery related experience in distribution, warehouse and production environment</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Logistics or Supply Chain or General Business preferred</li>
<li>Experience with WMS software including JDE preferred</li>
<li>Experience creating SOPs</li>
<li>Strong Microsoft Office experience including, Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook</li>
<li>Demonstrated written &amp; verbal communication skills</li>
</ul>
