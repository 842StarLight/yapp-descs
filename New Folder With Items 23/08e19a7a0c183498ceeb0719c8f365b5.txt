<p><b>Title:</b> Membership Advisor<br>
<b>Department:</b> Membership<br>
<b>Reports to:</b> Membership Director<br>
<br>
<b>Summary:</b><br>
The Membership Advisor is responsible for generating memberships to promote club sales efforts to achieve and surpass sales goals.Membership Advisors are given access to our state of the art CRM system, HubSpot, to access and endless amount of leads to help grow your business. Membership Advisors are responsible for continuously educating themselves on each <b>acac</b> department so that they may address the needs of potential members. Membership Advisors are given a generous commission structure and <b>acac</b> provides the opportunity for them to establish a thriving business out of the gate.<br>
<br>
</p>
<p><b>Essential Functions and Responsibilities (other duties may be assigned):</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Makes a consistent effort to meet and exceed established sales goals</li>
<li>Meets and greets all <b>acac </b>members, guests, and prospects with a smile and exhibits positive attitude at all times</li>
<li>Generates creative ideas for ways to market self and/or membership sales/programs</li>
<li>Creates a win-win situation between <b>acac</b> and prospective members by finding the right membership options for them and <b>acac</b>
</li>
<li>Keeps work areas neat and organized with sales tools always available</li>
<li>Systematically follows up with prospective members who have visited the club or expressed interest in <b>acac</b> membership</li>
<li>Resolves member issues within <b>acac</b> policies</li>
<li>Interacts with members and team members in an effort to build a referral base</li>
<li>Tracks daily statistics related to sales process</li>
<li>Maintains constant knowledge of <b>acac</b> membership pricing and costs</li>
<li>Maintains knowledge of all <b>acac</b> policies and procedures related to membership sales</li>
<li>Promotes ongoing membership referral programs</li>
<li>Learns how to operate Club Automation (computer software) and HubSpot (crm system) and ensures working knowledge of the program</li>
<li>Checks HubSpot on a daily bases to insure leads are up to date.</li>
<li>Collaborates with <b>acac </b>Team Members in the spirit of outstanding customer service, teamwork, and communication</li>
<li>Prior Sales Experience Preferred</li>
<br>
</ul>
