<h2>Customer Service Representative - Propane</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>If you are interested in having a positive impact on EnergyUnited Propane customers through assisting their needs directly, we’d like to meet you! We are seeking hard-working individuals that have a passion for customer service and working with customers to resolve their inquiries in the Creedmoor and Lexington areas. The perfect candidate will have a positive, open mind and considerate attitude with the ability to implement work plans and make sound decisions.</p>
<p><b>
Responsible for:</b><br>
The Customer Service Representative is responsible for handling inbound and outbound sales and service related calls, overseeing front counter operations, and collaborating with other staff to resolve customer sales and service inquiries.</p>
<p>
(These positions form a regular progression of training and experience with each position being accumulative of the previous positions regarding broad scope, key accountabilities and duties and position specifications.)</p>
<p>
**Level to be determined by experience**</p>
<p><b>
Broad Scope of Accountability &amp; Duties:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Maintain sound knowledge of EnergyUnited Propane products and services, customer account and billing processes, new customer information, and various policies and procedures related to product delivery and service.</li>
<li>Answer and qualify inbound sales calls and question customers tactfully to determine needs and explain appropriate energy products and services.</li>
<li>Ensure customer service quality, efficiency, and accuracy for all call transactions.</li>
<li>Coordinate and cooperate with other personnel in performing assigned responsibilities.<br>
<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Customer Service Representative 1 - Key Accountabilities &amp; Duties</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Utilize proactive sales approach with every customer interaction to uncover additional sales and service opportunities along with assist customers with in-store item sales.</li>
<li>Develop good relationships through all client contacts through presenting self professionally, with poise, confidence and enthusiasm.</li>
<li>Document customer calls thoroughly and accurately as consistent with Company procedures.</li>
<li>Complete daily cash sheet, reconcile cash drawer, prepare daily bank deposit and balance credit card payments as required as well as manage petty cash.</li>
<li>Handle customer service inquiries regarding billing information, service requests, maintenance, sales, and tank run-outs as well as set up new accounts.</li>
<li>Post deliveries and payments, scan invoices, bills, and safety checks, and manage LEAP accounts.</li>
<li>Assist with electric payments as needed.</li>
<li>Handle high volume of incoming calls.</li>
<li>Resolve customer complaints in a friendly and tactful manner, even when caller is irritated or emotional.</li>
<li>Reach out to idle customer base to generate revenue and initiate outbound sales calls to targeted customers such as automatic delivery scheduling, “We-Call” program, and installation follow-up and cross selling.</li>
<li>Follow-up on service or delivery failures to reassure the customer and ensure customer satisfaction.</li>
<li>Administer “Move In / Move Out” and similar processes as well as track customer location and asset movements for inactive sites.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Customer Service Representative 1 Requirements:</b></p>
<div>
<div>
<div><div><ul>
<li>Must be a high school graduate or equivalent training and experience.</li>
<li>Six months to one-year related experience and/or training.</li>
<li>Passion and care for customer service and our customers.</li>
<br>
</ul></div></div>
<br>
</div>
</div>
<p></p>
<p><b>Customer Service Representative 2 - Key Accountabilities &amp; Duties</b></p>
<div>
Same as Customer Service Representative 1, plus the following:<br>
<ul>
<li>Facilitate and complete in-store sales.</li>
<li>Resolve complex customer issues.</li>
<li>Make unsupervised decisions concerning pay arrangements and adjustments within policy established by Manager of Customer Service along with set up new complex accounts.</li>
<li>Provide back-up support for dispatchers as determined by Manager of Customer Service.</li>
<li>Support store/regional managers as determined by Manager of Customer Service.</li>
</ul>
</div>
<p><b>
Customer Service Representative 2 Requirements:</b></p>
<div>
<div>
<div><div>
<p>Same as Customer Service Representative 1, plus the following:</p>
<ul>
<li>Preferred 12 months as a Customer Service Representative 1 or 4 years of customer service experience.</li>
<li>Calls rated as Satisfactory or Exceptional quality by Manager of Customer Service.</li>
<li>Must meet or exceed defined expectation for availability, and attendance.</li>
<li>Ability to handle various service requests, new tank installations, sales of various products and services.</li>
<li>Supervisor recommendation.</li>
<br>
</ul>

<p></p>
<p><b>Position Specifications for Customer Service Representative:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Be a high school graduate or equivalent training and experience with legible handwriting and be able to read comprehensively.</li>
<li>Minimum six months to one-year related experience and/or training.</li>
<li>Must possess a valid driver’s license.</li>
<li>Understanding of customer service concepts, procedures and practices attained through some related experience.</li>
<li>Understanding of basic computer applications including MS Office, able to learn and apply multiple business software applications.</li>
<li>Must be a self-starter with an attitude of neatness, timeliness, orderliness and accuracy towards the job.</li>
<li>Must possess superior mathematical abilities and a strong ability to communicate in both verbal and written form.</li>
<li>Must be able to work under pressure and meet deadlines along with work in harmony with many different personality types.</li>
<li>Must be able to work with very little supervision and be proactive in responsibilities.</li>
<li>Must be able to retain information of a confidential nature, project a good public image, and be able to function cooperatively with peers as a team member to meet department and system objectives.</li>
<li>Must be available to emergency work during all weather conditions.</li>
<li>Performs all assignments safely assuring compliance with all safety related rules, regulations and policies.</li>
</ul>
