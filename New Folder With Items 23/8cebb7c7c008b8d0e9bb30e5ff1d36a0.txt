<p><b>Account Manager (Industrial) - Schaumburg, Illinois</b></p>
<p><b>Efficient Engineering is when a PLAN becomes EPLAN</b></p>
<p><b>EPLAN Software &amp; Services LLC</b> provides software and service solutions in the fields of electrical, automation and mechatronics engineering. The company develops one of the world’s leading design software solutions for machine and panel builders. We continually evolve the business with help from our Marketing, Sales and Professional Services Teams as we grow our presence in the North American Markets.</p>
<p>As the <b>RegionalAccount Manager</b>, you will be selling EPLAN Software &amp; Services. You will need to balance the management of existing customers while building a pipeline of new business. You will work closely with our national consulting engineering team to provide detail specific solutions for our customers. We need someone who enjoys identifying opportunities, providing solutions and closing business. Our success comes from selling with a customer-first mentality and solution-driven methodology.</p>
<p><b>You will contribute </b>by analyzing customer facilities and requirements to help drive their business needs through the <b>EPLAN</b>platform. Additionally;</p>
<p>· You identify new EPLAN prospects and industries and are willing to cover all components of the sales process. What that entails are; prospecting, cold calling, negotiating complex requirements and maintaining high customer satisfaction (<b>NPS</b>).</p>
<p>· You develop sales plans and respond to proposals for specific customer requirements and help provide industry-specific solutions with our engineers.</p>
<p>· You participate in global initiatives to introduce products in new markets, including educating the customer on resources available and elevating our brand.</p>
<p>· You present at local sales marketing events while actively upselling and highlighting solutions to new, current and old customers.</p>
<p>· You will be resourceful, customer and service-oriented, creative and driven to produce measurable results within your region.</p>
<p>· You should have a high sense of urgency, strong business ethics and be innovative, self-motivating and self-aware.</p>
<p><b>What we are looking for is </b>a <b>Regional</b> <b>Account Manager </b>(<b>Industrial</b>)with the ability to passionately promote and display their industry knowledge to an expansive clientele as we strive to grow our business.</p>
<p>· We are looking for someone with a Bachelors degree in Electrical/Mechanical Engineering or a degree in business administration/Marketing along with relevant experience</p>
<p>· We are looking for 5 years of experience selling Enterprise SaaS platforms</p>
<p>· You have strong knowledge of CAE/CADmarketplace and engineering applications</p>
<p>· We preferred candidates with experience in the <b>industrial automation industry</b></p>
<p>· You have strong interpersonal skills and are going to show your proven ability to develop and build strong relationships with c-level and senior management of clients</p>
<p>· We need someone who is highly proficient in their computer skills including internet, CRM, email, MS Word, Excel, PowerPoint and has a complete understanding of consultative and solution-selling strategies</p>
<p><i>Do you have strong verbal and written communication skills?</i></p>
<p><i>Are you creatively curious and thoroughly enjoy finding solutions to unique problems?</i></p>
<p><b>What we offer </b>is an opportunity to be a part of a team that increases the optimization and efficiency of <b>CAD</b>/<b>CAE</b> professionals worldwide. Our goal is to develop long term and meaningful loyal relationships where our inspiration and creativity result in our competitive advantage. We enjoy all the benefits of an International company quickly growing in the US.</p>
