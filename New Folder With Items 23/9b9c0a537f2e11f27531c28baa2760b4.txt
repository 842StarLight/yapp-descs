<h2>Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>The Account Manager is responsible for managing the retention and expansion of Masergy’s existing account base through building and maintaining strategic relationships with key stakeholders and users through proactive outreach, quality business reviews, and strategic advising by aligning the relevant Masergy resources with the customers’ needs and initiatives. The Account Manager will be expected to drive strong Net Promoter Scores, increased cross sell and up sell, as well as optimal renewals at time of term expiration through proactive management and care of assigned book of business.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Target Market<br>
<ul><li>“Strategic Accounts” defined as existing customers billing greater than $50k MRR</li></ul>
</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Meet all assigned sales revenue and base growth targets</li>
<li>Coordinate and lead Quality Business Reviews with customers to ensure they maximize their investment and relationship with Masergy</li>
<li>Proactively ensure customers are educated on Masergy’s entire value proposition across different products and services</li>
<li>Utilize direct and channel networks to increase footprint within the install base</li>
<li>Develop and maintain detailed account plans including relationship maps</li>
<li>Develop and grow upsell and cross sell pipeline in territory</li>
<li>Act as the primary point of contact for assigned customers, and coordinate internal teams to service customer need</li>
<li>Work with Sales Engineers and lead technical discovery and design</li>
<li>Work with Pricing, Contract Admin, Sales Leadership and Finance to create and gain approval on sales quotes and proposals.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Related Activities included:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Present, negotiate, and gain customer signature on sales quotes and proposals</li>
<li>Understand and utilize Masergy’s internal order systems for sales, pricing and procurement</li>
<li>Proactively plan retention and churn management strategies within assigned account base</li>
<li>Work with account executives, inside account managers and retention management team to properly prepare for transfer of accounts into/out of assigned base each fiscal year.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<b>
Related Activities include:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Proactive outreach to existing customers</li>
<li>Preparation and presentation of materials for QBRs</li>
<li>Coordination with support, implementation, accounting for customer inquiries and issues</li>
<li>Record and progress sales activity within CRM tool</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Minimum (5) years of enterprise account management experience selling $50k+ MRR deals</li>
<li>Proven track record of managing a large or strategic base of accounts</li>
<li>Energetic, personable and responsive</li>
<li>Willingness to learn technology and be able to educate our customers</li>
<li>Excellent oral and written communication skills</li>
<li>Excellent sales skills</li>
<li>Excellent presentation skills</li>
<li>Independent self-starter</li>
<li>Experience with CRM, sales processes and basic reporting</li>
<li>Must have attention to detail, a commitment to quality, be results driven and customer focused.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Language Skills</b></div>
<div>
Ability to read, analyze, and interpret basic business documents. Ability to respond effectively to customer requests for information. Ability to write business acceptable and professional emails. Ability to create proposals, negotiate contracts. Ability to effectively communicate and build rapport with prospects.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<b>
Computer Skills</b><br>
To perform this job successfully, an individual should have knowledge of basic CRM functionality, Spreadsheet software, PowerPoint and Word Processing software.</div>
