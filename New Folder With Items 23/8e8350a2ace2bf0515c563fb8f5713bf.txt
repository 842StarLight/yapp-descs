<p>
The Cyber System Security Group in the United Technologies Research Center has an opening at the level of Senior Research Scientist in trusted design and implementation of cyber physical systems. Candidates meeting a substantial subset of the criteria listed below are encouraged to apply.</p>
<p><br>
Cyber-Physical Systems or "smart" systems are co-engineered interacting networks of physical and computational components. Other terminologies related to this emerging technology trend include: Internet of Things (IoT), Industrial Internet, Smart Cities, Smart Grid, etc. Those are large systems that connect and integrate industrial control systems, machines, sensors and actuators with enterprise systems, business processes, analytics and people. The Cyber System Security group at UTRC is developing novel research and technology to address this large-scale and complex system of systems, and provide integrated and adaptive solutions.</p>
<p></p>
