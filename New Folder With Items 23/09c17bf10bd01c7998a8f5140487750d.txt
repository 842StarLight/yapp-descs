<h2>Salesperson</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>SK Dental Lab, an independent full-service dental laboratory, is looking for an energetic, in-house salesperson to maintain, develop, and grow client relationships to improve sales and name recognition. SK Dental lab offers a unique work-experience to start your career in the dentistry and sales job industry.</p>
<p>
You will interface with clients nationwide, focusing on cultivating clients for both traditional and digital dentistry lab services, advertising products including dentures, crowns and bridges, CAD/CAM milling and more. Sales work will be completed from in the office - no door to door travel!</p>
<p>
This position will play a critical role in the continued growth of the laboratory, as we transition to offering digital services that can be provided to clients throughout the United States. As such, candidates that are able to demonstrate an ability to positively boost sales and client awareness will have ample opportunity to grow with the company.</p>
<p>
The ideal candidate will:</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Utilize Salesforce to gain further insight into customer needs, active projects, and to support team selling strategy</li>
<li>Schedule sales meetings and deliver solutions on immediate customer requests via phone</li>
<li>Leverage marketing &amp; sales tools to identify solutions and sell to new customers</li>
<li>Recover declining &amp; dormant customers via sales tools and marketing campaigns</li>
</ul>
<p>Qualifications:</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelors Degree in Marketing, Business, or related field</li>
<li>1 year experience in online or mobile marketing/lead generation preferred</li>
<li>Self motivated, goal oriented, and dependable</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills as well as presentation skills</li>
<li>Ability to handle multiple projects at the same time in a fast-paced environment. Must be detailed oriented</li>
<li>Ability to work effectively independently and as part of a team</li>
<li>Strong computer skills including advanced knowledge of Microsoft Office Products (PowerPoint, Word, Outlook and Excel) and the ability to learn required business systems</li>
<li>Experience working with Salesforce.com preferred</li>
<li>Knowledge in the dental industry is a plus</li>
</ul>
<p>Current students are encouraged to apply</p>
