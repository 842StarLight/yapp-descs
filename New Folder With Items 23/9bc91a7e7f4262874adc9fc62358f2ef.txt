Bill.com is a leader in financial process automation for small businesses and mid-size companies. Making it simple to connect and do business, the Bill.com Back Office Cloud digitizes, automates and simplifies legacy payment and financial processes. With an integrated, end-to-end platform, Bill.com leverages artificial intelligence to reduce manual work, and provides a cloud workspace to help run your business anytime, anywhere. The company partners with four of the largest U.S. financial institutions, more than 70 of the top 100 U.S. accounting firms, and major accounting software providers. Bill.com manages more than $70B in annual payment volume across ACH, virtual cards, checks, and international payments. The company has offices in Palo Alto, California and Houston, Texas. <div><b>
What you’ll be doing:</b></div>
<div>
Bill.com is seeking a Senior Product Manager to join our core experience product team. He/She will be responsible for the vision &amp; development of the design language system. His/Her team will own the development of common components across the app that’d help other feature teams scale quickly without compromising quality. Additionally he/she will also own shared product experiences such as Search, Messaging that span across the app, Feature discovery/adoption experience &amp; more...</div>
</div>

<div><ul>
<li>
Customer Focused: Designs and brings to market revenue generating, customer-driven software product experience. Gathers insights from customers, data, research and industry trends and convert them into compelling product ideas</li>

Entrepreneurial mindset: Able to balance thinking big &amp; dividing the vision into bite-size milestones. Self-aware with a strong desire to learn &amp; continuously improve.</li>
