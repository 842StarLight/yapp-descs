<h2>Merchandiser</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div><div>
<p>We are looking for <b>seasonal Merchandisers</b> who will be a key part of our success in our peak Spring season.</p>
<p>
The<b> Merchandiser</b> will report directly to the Sales Manager and work within our big box retailers, such as Home Depot and Lowe's. As part of the day-to-day responsibilities the<b> Merchandiser</b> will execute merchandising directives and re-sets, establish a positive rapport with store personnel and customers, and relay information to Scotts Sales team regarding inventory opportunities.</p>
<p><b>
In this role you will:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Merchandise products inside and outside stores</li>
<li>Provide customers and store associates with product knowledge on Scotts brands</li>
<li>Ensure product is stocked and presentable at all times</li>
<li>Assist in identifying opportunities for solution selling and incremental space</li>
<li>Provide support to Sales Manager and store employees as needed</li>
<li>Work effectively independently and as a team to perform all tasks assigned</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What we do for our associates:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Competitive retirement program with up to 7% 401K match</li>
<li>Provide valuable, hands-on experience within merchandising, retail and customer service</li>
<li>Provide a competitive hourly wage</li>
<li>Paid on-the-job training including in-depth product knowledge on the Scotts products</li>
<li>Fast tracked career advancement opportunities for high performing associates</li>
<li>Compensation for commuting expenses</li>
</ul>
<p><b>The ideal person will have:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or equivalent years of experience</li>
<li>Experience in retail, merchandising and/or stocking</li>
<li>Problem solving and service oriented attitude</li>
<li>Ability to lift, push and pull 45 pounds</li>
<li>Ability to work a flexible schedule, including holidays and weekends</li>
<li>Ability to operate company provided technology</li>
<li>Current, valid driver's license and reliable transportation</li>
<li>Ability to tolerate work in an outdoor environment (heat, cold, rain, etc.)</li>
<li>Must be at least 18 years of age</li>
<li>Ability to read, write and speak English</li>
<li>Ability to climb ladders</li>
<li>Ability to move pallets of product</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>

Scotts Miracle-Gro has helped to grow the nearly $7 billion global consumer lawn and garden market through product innovation, industry-leading advertising efforts and its trusted brands.</p>
<p><b>
The brands and products within our company include: </b>Scotts, Miracle-Gro, Tom Cat, Ortho, Round Up, Bonnie Plants, Osmocote, Earthgro and Fafard.</p>
