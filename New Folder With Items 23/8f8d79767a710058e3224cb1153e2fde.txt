<br>
<p>
Bon Appétit Management Company’s philosophy is “food service for a sustainable future,” but don’t be misled by the food service part. Bon Appétit is a restaurant company that has been revolutionizing contract food service since 1987. Our leadership has been honored by the James Beard Foundation and Chefs Collaborative; covered by <i>The New York Times</i> and <i>Fast Company</i>; and we’re proud to count renowned chefs José Andrés, Traci Des Jardins, Susan Feniger, and many others as our partners.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
We operate more than 1,000 cafés around the country for corporations, universities, and museums as well as award-winning public restaurants. Our chefs make everything from scratch — stocks, sauces, soups — from fresh, seasonal, mostly local ingredients. We also believe in changing the world through food. We’ve led our industry in socially and environmentally responsible sourcing, from sustainable seafood to cage-free eggs and pork raised without antibiotics.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
The Marketing Manager for Bon Appétit Management Company at Chase Center
</b></p>
<p>The Bon Appétit Marketing Manager plays an integral role for Chase Center, San Francisco’s new sports and entertainment arena, helping to craft and execute marketing initiatives that support the overall strategic priorities of both Bon Appétit and the Golden State Warriors, including driving participation, engaging guests, ensuring customer satisfaction, and providing relevant education.</p>
<p>
No two days are the same in this business, and the ability to be nimble and adaptable is key to success. This marketer must be adept at prioritizing multiple requests, have strong visual design and graphic skills, a clear communicator, enthusiastic collaborator, and a tech-savvy, effective influencer.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b><i>This position is full-time, on-site. A cover letter and resume are required. Writing samples will be requested.</i></b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Key Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Coordinate with multiple teams to create timely on-site marketing for various initiatives and client needs.</li>
<li>
Maintain the voice of our brand through all materials, collateral, and social media.</li>
<li>
Write succinct, sensible descriptions and blurbs for a variety of audiences</li>
<li>
Copyedit menus for consistency, accuracy, and house style</li>
<li>
Create professional, attractive designed templates quickly, on the fly for menus, signage, and other collateral</li>
<li>
Collaborate with designers on larger, longer-term projects for planned promotions and collateral.</li>
<li>
Handle high-profile social media (taking photos, writing captions, posting regularly including Instagram Stories, developing a calendar) to support all strategic priorities</li>
<li>
Oversee/direct photo shoots of food and beverages</li>
<li>
Build a venue-wide marketing plan for food operations and ensure all stakeholders buy in to the plan and commit to its successful execution.</li>
<li>
Work with all managers and supervisors to assure that all food displays are organized, signed, and merchandised professionally.</li>
<li>
Perform administrative and logistical tasks associated with events and promotions.</li>
<li>
Develop new strategies and tactics for promoting the food programs to enhance sales and customer satisfaction.</li>
</ul>
