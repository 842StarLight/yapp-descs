<h2>Consulting Solutions Architect</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div>
<div><div>
<p><b>Requirements/Job Duties</b></p>
<div><div>
<p>World Wide Technology, Inc. has an opportunity available for a Consulting Solutions Architect to support our Federal team with their DellEMC projects. This role involves leading business relevant engagements within Federal assisting with business transformation efforts related to DellEMC technology solutions (Compute, Storage/DCS to include Enterprise Storage portfolio, MDC, UDS, and DPS solutions, Client, Networking, Security, Services, S&amp;P, OEM) in addition to supporting additional Dell Tech SAB's where applicable, including: VMWare, Pivotal, RSA, SecureWorks, etc.</p>
<p><b>
RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Continually develop and expand personal knowledge of the validated DellEMC reference architectures</li>
<li>Build and foster relationships with the DellEMC Federal DCAE and ASE's and continuously enable them on WWT capabilities and extreme value of leveraging the ATC</li>
<li>Align the DellEMC Federal DCAE and ASE's with the WWT CSE, CSA, and CTA's</li>
<li>Provide DellEMC technical training and enablement to the WWT Federal CSE, CSA, and CTA's</li>
<li>Support the WWT Pub Sector Innovation &amp; Technology team from a DellEMC technical perspective as they build customized and repeatable WWT IP solutions</li>
<li>Translating key business requirements into DellEMC focused Data Center/Enterprise solutions within the Public Sector</li>
<li>Design and propose next generation DellEMC solutions that meet the needs and functional requirements of the customer</li>
<li>Present complex technical concepts in a manner that can be easily understood by non-technical or semi-technical audiences</li>
<li>Act as a consultant to the customers, striving to gain a trusted advisor status through honest but humble interactions</li>
<li>Perform technology demonstrations utilizing the WWT Advanced Technology Center, Partner and Vendor Labs or customer provided equipment</li>
<li>Contribute to the WWT technology body of knowledge through the creation of written documentation, white papers, internal blogs, WWT communities or knowledge base articles</li>
<li>Join and be an active participant in one or more WWT Virtual Teams representing a technology of relevance to you and the region</li>
<li>Develops and fosters relationships with strategic original equipment manufacturers (OEMs) and key partners who are local to your region</li>
<li>Support the account team through identification, development and ongoing support of net new opportunities</li>
<li>Join account teams in key customer meetings and EBC's to represent WWT's solutions and capabilities</li>
<li>Take a leadership position in proposal responses within your domain of expertise</li>
<li>Works with clients and WWT Sales team to obtain technical priorities, challenges, and initiatives that can be translated into WWT opportunities</li>
<li>Ensure effective coordination and support between account teams and supporting post sale technical resources</li>
</ul>
</div></div>

<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<div><div>
<p><b>REQUIRED SKILLSETS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>DellEMC technology in a presales consulting capacity</li>
<li>Ideal candidates will have extensive experience with DellEMC Data Center Solutions and the ability to design and implement integrated solutions within Public Sector</li>
<li>Experience with Federal procurement process</li>
<li>5 years of technical enterprise pre-sales experience with a focus on solution sales</li>
<li>Ability to create and present sales and technical training material for field enablement</li>
<li>Outstanding communication and organizational skills required</li>
<li>Bachelor's Degree in a related field (e.g. Business Management, Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, Information Management, Program Management, etc.) or equivalent industry experience preferred.</li>
<li>DellEMC technical certifications and any other related certifications are highly desired</li>
<li>Travel requirement between 50+%</li>
</ul>
