<h2>Marketing Manager - International</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Marketing Manager - International</b><br>
<b>Chicago, IL</b></p>
<p>
The AMA is a unifying voice and powerful ally for America’s physicians, the patients they care for, and the promise of a healthier nation. To be part of the AMA is to be part of our Mission to promote the art and science of medicine and the betterment of public health. Join the AMA team as the Marketing Manager II in Health Solutions. As part of the team, you will: conceive and execute integrated international marketing strategy and campaigns for the AMA Current Procedural Terminology (CPT®); Build awareness, facilitate adoption, and support licensing; Collaborate with the Director of Marketing to develop international value proposition(s), message(s), supporting marketing materials and trade shows; Track campaign effectiveness and provide recommendations for optimizing messaging and marketing efforts.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b><br>
<br>
<b>Conceptualize and implement international marketing strategies</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Develop campaign strategy and messaging; lead discussions of campaign concept and execution</li>
<li>
Execute marketing plans including traditional and digital marketing project timelines, content/message writing, production oversight, ongoing evaluation and analysis</li>
<li>
Provide guidance regarding campaigns to stakeholders and colleagues across the department and the AMA, and manage vendors to meet timelines and budget requirements</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Plan and execute events</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Manage planning and execution of international events and tradeshows to drive awareness, adoption and commercialization of the core business line</li>
<li>
Plan and execute all aspects of events from inception to completion, including collaboration with subcontractors</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Develop, proof, and troubleshoot print and digital marketing materials</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Develop marketing strategy, messaging, and content for printed and digital sales enablement materials and tools; build a marketing budget and track expenses</li>
<li>
Write and edit new web content (including text, infographics, video, webinars, social)</li>
<li>
Create presentation pitches under the guidance of the Director of Marketing</li>
<li>
Monitor and analyze key website and email metrics to improve traffic and conversions</li>
<li>
Develop, evaluate and oversee the implementation of A/B testing plan, protocols and procedures</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Assess international market conditions</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Advise the Director of Marketing and Director of Sales on the drivers that influence the core business internationally</li>
<li>
Research consumer insights for each country/region</li>
</ul>
<p>
May include other responsibilities as assigned.</p>
<p><b>
Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree required; Marketing or Business and/or MBA or related Master’s degree preferred</li>
<li>
5+ years’ work experience including global marketing, global advertising or related field: 2-3 years of healthcare experience is preferred</li>
<li>
Proven ability to develop marketing strategy and messaging to drive leads that generate sales</li>
<li>
Exposure to tradeshow event planning and execution; international trade show experience a plus</li>
<li>
3+ years’ experience in digital and social media marketing</li>
<li>
Experience developing and executing international marketing campaign preferred</li>
<li>
Willingness to travel up to 20%, including internationally to conventions and tradeshows</li>
<li>
Proven ability to implement marketing campaigns including meeting deadlines, working within budgets, and ensuring materials meet business requirements</li>
<li>
Well-developed interpersonal skills; experience in a politically sensitive organization preferred</li>
</ul>
<p>
The AMA offers competitive salaries, including an incentive plan; excellent benefits and progressive technology. Our office is a business casual environment and we respect work-life balance. The American Medical Association is located at 330 N. Wabash Avenue, Chicago, IL 60611 and is convenient to all public transportation in Chicago.<br>
