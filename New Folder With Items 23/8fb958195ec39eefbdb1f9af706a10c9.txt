<h2>Manager, Integration, Brand Marketing</h2>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>General Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p><b>About the Role</b></p>
<p>
We are looking for a Media and Customer Integration Manager who is passionate about driving our rental car brand to success through continual data-driven insights, customer reach strategies, and delivery of revenue-driving media. The focus for this role will be responsibility for delivering strategic media and full-funnel integration plans for the brand, aligned to financial plans and brand goals. This role will support the brand team in designing and executing the strategy to reach target customers and grow market share based on data-driven insights. This is a position for those who love working in a fast-paced and dynamic environment.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The primary mission of the Manager, Integration role is:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Champion customer reach and media innovation strategies and find new ways of reaching / engaging customers</li>
<li>
Responsible for leading the development and execution of these customer reach strategies, including the appropriate media mix and tactics that are measurable and optimizable</li>
<li>
Manage the intersection of customer reach, non-digital / digital tactics, and digital brand platforms to maximize success in reaching and retaining customers</li>
<li>
Drive analysis through data and competitive landscape information to establish business reach and investment objectives in partnership with the entire brand team</li>
<li>
Coordinate within brand team and marketing teams to align execution and optimization of efforts to reach customers aligned to brand’s product and program strategies</li>
<li>
Liaise with teams across the organization, including Marketing, Sales, Pricing, Operations, Finance, and others. Also coordinate work with external agencies as needed</li>
<li>
Foster consistent brand results measurement, robust reporting and a focus on results to continuously improve effectiveness and ROI for the brand</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Key Result Areas</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Drive strategy to successfully reach key customers segments with brand investments and executing to meet revenue targets</li>
<li>
Support growth of brand market share through innovative and disruptive strategies</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
