<h2>Director, Solutions Product Management</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Functional Area:
</p>SM - Sales &amp; Marketing
<p></p>Estimated Travel Percentage (%): Up to 25%
<p></p>Relocation Provided: No
<p></p>American General Life Insurance Company
<p></p>
<p>The Director, Product Solutions Management will work to drive initiatives which increase assets under management in retirement accounts by assuring that Retirement Services has the right products and services for key markets and constituents we serve and may want to enter. S/he is responsible for driving the successful implementation of all aspects of critical third-party solutions initiatives and ongoing support of the current solutions. It is expected that this person will be recognized within the organization as an expert.
</p>
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Provide leadership in identifying and driving forward innovative product/solutions opportunities as well as tactical product plans and execution for Retirement Services products and services, interfacing closely with Group Acquisition, Relationship Management, Market Management, Field sales, Marketing, Operations, IT, Pricing, Risk Management, and Market research teams.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Take the initiative to work with Group Acquisition, Relationship Management, Market Management, Field sales, Marketing, Operations, IT, Pricing, Risk Management, and Market research teams to drive research, develop and prioritize product concepts.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Interface with marketing on the development of collateral and sales materials for
</p>
<p>Retirement Services products &amp; Solutions.
</p>
