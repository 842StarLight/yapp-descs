 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Working at Rockit Science:</b></h4>
<p>
We're an award-winning advertising agency that works with brands across the United States. We believe in good work for good clients. We are highly selective on the clients we work with to ensure our team members have the best opportunities to work with great brands with realistic timelines and expectations. It’s an environment where creativity flourishes and individuals can express their opinions and influence project and client success.<br>
</p>
<p>
We're looking for a Marketing Coordinator to join our growing team. As Marketing Coordinator, you will assist day-to-day agency operations, marketing and account assistance. Candidates should have strong organization, strategy and time management skills possess the ability to communicate effectively with all levels of an organization. The position reports to the Account Director and is assigned task as needed included or beyond what is included in this job description. The applicant must be dedicated, confident, decisive, hard-working, and energetic, have the ability to prioritize and multitask, have excellent organizational, interpersonal, problem-solving and communications skills.<br>
</p>
