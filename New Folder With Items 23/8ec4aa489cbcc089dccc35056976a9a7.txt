<h2>Advertising Production Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>If you're motivated and looking to build your career and experience - this position has tremendous growth potential. You HAVE to be smart, proactive and computer savvy. As the business owner, I will personally train and invest in you. As a small business - this role is for a person that is willing to go the extra mile. The candidate should be comfortable managing all the incoming order details, for company headquarters, from behind the computer (full-time) and speaking with clients over the phone as well as email. This person will ideally grow into a management role.</p>
<ul>
<li>This position requires an individual who is ambitious, task oriented, extremely detailed and deadline oriented. It is very important for this individual to be self-managing and to be able to thrive under pressure.</li>
<li>A successful Production Assistant candidate must enjoy wearing multiple hats in all administration, production and communications aspects. This candidate must also be reliable, focused, confident, highly organized, and self-motivated. This role requires excellent problem solving skills and the individual must have a consistent, professional, positive and proven performance track record in similar work environments.</li>
<li>We're looking for a self-managing, energetic, detail oriented individual with a customer service mindset who works well in a fast paced, deadline driven environment.</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Highly focused on production schedules and work orders to departments</li>
<li>Detailed review of communications, disclaimers, and product orders</li>
<li>Responsible for performance, scheduling, cost and customer satisfaction of each contracted job</li>
<li>Assess resource requirements for each job</li>
<li>Must always be aware of key dates related to a job</li>
<li>Determine quality control standards – see those standards are met</li>
<li>Oversee job production processes – keep them true to their contracted timelines</li>
<li>Re-negotiate timescales or schedules as necessary – professionally communicate changes to all parties affected</li>
<li>Work closely with production and logistics team to ensure timely delivery</li>
<li>Knowledge of invoicing and comprehensive pricing</li>
</ul>
<p>General Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Keep filing up-to-date: digital central file repositories and hard copy file cabinets</li>
<li>Maintain sample library of all work in process for both development and production</li>
</ul>
<p>Communication Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Act as the liaison with client, vendors, marketing and sales staff</li>
<li>Encourage and build positive relationships with all co-workers, outside vendors and customers through effective and professional communication</li>
<li>Clearly communicate job requirements and key dates with upper management, clients and third party vendors</li>
<li>Maintain and form excellent relationships with clients</li>
</ul>
<p>The Advertising Production Assistant candidate must:</p>
<ul>
<li>Thrive in a high capacity, fast-paced, deadline oriented environment</li>
<li>Geek out on the details; you must be analytical, highly organized and task oriented</li>
<li>Possess a driven attitude with creative thinking skills</li>
<li>Work well under pressure; you must have an elevated ability to work in a deadline-driven environment with overlapping product development and production goals</li>
<li>Be a clear communicator; clearly communicate key job information with all parties involved, speak up when you are unsure, and maintain a positive working relationship with vendors, clients, and co-workers</li>
<li>Routinely demonstrate ownership and awareness of job duties, key dates and delivery goals</li>
<li>Contribute to creating a helpful and positive work culture</li>
<li>Have a quality-consciousness and “pride-in-quality” attitude</li>
<li>Be ambitious and self-motivated; initiate or suggest plans to increase and achieve work goals</li>
<li>Have a customer service oriented mindset; committed to forming excellent relationships</li>
<li>Be self-managing; we do not micro-manage</li>
<li>Be easily accessible by phone or email, even outside of traditional work hours</li>
</ul>
<p>Education, Experience and Skill Qualifications:</p>
<ul>

<li>Possess advanced knowledge of CMS systems – Salesforce highly preferred</li>
<li>Possess knowledge of managing electronic central repositories and hard copy file cabinets</li>
