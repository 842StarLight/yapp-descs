<h2>General Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>General Manager Opportunity with Salata Restaurants</b></p>
<p>
Solidify a successful career in the fast-paced food service industry by joining Salata. We are committed to the highest quality in everything we do — especially our people!</p>
<p>
We're looking for a General Manager who strives for the best in life — who values a spotless, supportive and flexible work environment, and who cares about our guests and the healthy and fresh food we serve.</p>
<p>
At Salata, there is no menu — our salads and wraps are fully customizable. In that spirit, we offer flexible schedules for a diversity of styles and personalities, along with competitive pay and benefits.</p>
<p>
This approach to flexibility in food means you will need to be great at communicating with guests to work at Salata, making them feel comfortable in exploring new combinations. You should also be keen to make your mark on the local community, by leaving lasting impressions with our guests of the excellent service they receive in our restaurants.</p>
<p>
Salata General Managers are chefs, bankers, trainers, schedulers, administrators, and leaders. They both lead and choose the restaurant teams – managing, training and scheduling all staff – and oversee all restaurant administration, maintaining the restaurant’s operational systems, overseeing financial reporting and banking, and ensuring Salata’s food quality is of the highest standard and guests are happy. Our General Managers keep the guests happy and the restaurants humming with excellent customer service skills, an eye for perfection and cleanliness, excellent organizational skills and a positive attitude.</p>
<p>
Our General Managers …</p>
<ul>
<li>
Conduct interviews with employment candidates and hire guest-oriented staff.</li>
<li>
Ensure proper follow through and execution of all restaurant operations standards, procedures, and policies, as defined by Executive Leadership and in accordance with Salata brand operational standards.</li>
<li>
Responsible for restaurant's safety program, to include ensuring all employees are fully trained in safe work practices, conduct safety meetings and safety inspections each period and forward corresponding reports to Loss Prevention, and ensure employee and/or guest reports are timely and accurately completed and processed.</li>
<li>
Responsible for managing and attaining all sales and financial targets for food, labor and controllable costs.</li>
<li>
Review/modify/approve Team Member schedules on a daily and weekly basis to control labor costs.</li>
<li>
Conduct frequent evaluation updates and a formal, annual performance review for all Managers and staff outlining strengths, opportunity areas, and merit recommendations.</li>
<li>
Investigate, respond, and resolve employee concerns and/or complaints.</li>
<li>
Manage the inventory process.</li>
<li>
Administer all disciplinary action, inclusive of termination.</li>
<li>
Hold frequent management and team member meetings to keep staff informed of important policy changes, new product rollouts, and operational updates.</li>
<li>
Oversee the employee onboarding process (new hire orientation).</li>
<li>
Develop Management and staff utilizing developmental guidelines and forms.</li>
<li>
Demonstrate high energy, an enthusiastic personality, and leadership skills that inspire a culture of hospitality in a fast-paced environment.</li>
<li>
Ensure the restaurant is accountable for the successful execution of all quality operating systems using daily/weekly/monthly duties checklists.</li>
<li>
Build a restaurant culture that is guest-service oriented.</li>
<li>
Drive all aspects of sales, promotions, advertising, and catering sales initiatives.</li>
<li>
Meet the requirements of an Authorized Driver as outlined in Company policy (must be 18 years of age or older, and have a valid in state Driver License, reliable transportation, current automobile insurance, and qualifying driving record).</li>
<li>
Must have open availability for work hours and work days as needed to support the business operations, up to 50 work hours/week.</li>
</ul>
<p>
We look forward to speaking with you regarding your qualifications and interest in our brand.</p>
