<p>Job Overview</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
The Field Marketing Manager is responsible for generating new business by selecting and optimizing event sponsorship and hosting. This includes trade shows, conferences, expos, regional events, partner events, and meetups. By preparing well for an event, the manager will drive more traffic to the booth, optimize the booth experience and SaltStack’s presence, and ensure prompt and targeted follow-up. Additionally, the role will require some knowledge of the marketing funnel, optimizing throughput via nurture and digital campaigns, and optimizing web conversion rates for each event or campaign. Good communication skills are a must.</p>
