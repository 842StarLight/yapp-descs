<h2>General Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Do you have a proven track record of success in hotel management with a desire to grow, taking your career to the next level? As <b>General Manager</b> for Seva Hospitality, you'll have the opportunity to apply what you know, strengthen as a leader, and much more.<br>
<br>
Due to recent growth and future development, Seva Hospitality is looking to expand our bench strength of highly qualified General Managers within our hotels! These are key management positions, which provide partnership, strategy and support to our guests, associates, and Corporate Officers. Our priorities are to Drive Revenue, Control Cost, and Provide Outstanding Guest and Associate Satisfaction.<br>
</div>
<div>
We offer a very competitive package of benefits and compensation, including Health, Dental, Vision, and a rewarding incentive plan.</div>
<div>
<br>
<b>
As General Manager, you will:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>
Oversee all aspects of operations by exercising demonstrated knowledge and experience in all phases of hotel management, including sales and marketing, human resources, food and beverage, budget and forecast management, rooms, housekeeping and maintenance.</li>
<li>
Provide the vision, leadership and strategy to inspire your associates to deliver exceptional service and drive financial success. You have a strong hotel management background and an obvious passion for the hospitality industry. Your passion for service is contagious and you are a natural coach that loves to build effective teams and inspires individuals to do their best.</li>
<li>
Enable innovation and drive results. You execute successful sales and marketing campaigns, understand dynamic revenue management and can drive profit to the bottom line through strong financial controls.</li>
<li>
Build a rapport with ownership. You partner closely with your owner and work to understand their priorities and exceed their expectations.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
<b>
Fundamentals:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>
To be successful in this role, you must be able to read, write, and verbally communicate effectively and professionally in English with associates, guests, and ownership.</li>
<li>
You must be able to deal with difficult situations and people, while exhibiting a consistent level of professionalism. You will regularly use a computer and different software.</li>
<li>
Occasionally you will have to lift or move up to 25 pounds.</li>
</ul>
