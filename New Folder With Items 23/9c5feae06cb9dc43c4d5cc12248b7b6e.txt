<h2>Telemarketing Rep</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>We’re thrilled that you are interested in joining us here at the Amynta Group!
</p>
<p></p>Outbound call center agent for Telemarketing Sales
<p>Position Overview
</p>
<p>The Direct Sales Representative will be responsible for making outbound telephone sales contacting existing customers to renew extended service contracts or to offer new service.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Essential Job Functions
</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Execute outbound calls as required;
</li>
<li>Assess and consult customer needs accordingly;
</li>
<li>Documents transactions by completing forms and record logs;
</li>
<li>Explain product features and benefits;
</li>
<li>Exposed to confidential information and expected to maintain confidentiality at all times; must adhere to compliance and regulations;
</li>
<li>Contributes to team effort by accomplishing related results as needed;
</li>
<li>Be able to achieve set daily goals with high call volume.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Requirements
</p>
<ul>
<li>High School Diploma or general education degree (GED);
</li>
<li>Minimum of 6 months of telesales experience, 1+ years preferred;
</li>
<li>Self-motivated; organized, detail oriented, strong desire to learn and grow;
</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication, interpersonal, and telephonic sales skills;
</li>
<li>Goal oriented and focused on meeting production goals;
</li>
<li>Ability to operate PC-based software programs and automated business systems databases “Dialer” is a plus;
</li>
<li>Must be extremely flexible with your work schedule as business needs require.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Benefits
</p>
<p>Warrantech offers a truly comprehensive, competitive and affordable benefits package.
</p>
<ul>
<li>Competitive starting salary;
</li>
<li>Exceptional benefits package including medical, dental, and vision after 30 days of employment;
</li>
<li>Vacation and Personal time.
</li>
</ul>
