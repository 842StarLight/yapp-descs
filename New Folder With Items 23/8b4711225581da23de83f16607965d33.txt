<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Opportunity Overview</b></h2>
<p><b>
OVERALL SUMMARY</b><br>
<br>
Partnering with executive leadership and operating areas, the WarnerMedia Entertainment (WME) Strategy team facilitates and supports strategic / operational planning. As part of the larger Finance organization, the team executes on detailed qualitative and quantitative analysis to further WME’s strategic priorities including the launch of a direct to consumer streaming service.<br>
<br>
We are looking for a Manager, based in New York, who will provide strategic support for our wide-ranging portfolio of entertainment properties (e.g. tbs, TNT, truTV, HBO, HBO MAX). This role requires a high level of collaboration with internal partners in various business units across the enterprise.<br>
<br>
This position reports to the VP of WarnerMedia Entertainment Strategy.<br>
<br>
<b>PRIMARY RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Support major initiatives to achieve the strategic goals of the WarnerMedia Entertainment business:</li>
<li>
Develop and adhere to work plans for enterprise-wide collaboration</li>
<li>
Collaborate with internal partners such as Finance, Content, Product, Technology, Network Operations, Ad Sales, Distribution, and Research to develop these initiatives</li>
<li>
Build detailed financial models to evaluate strategic options</li>
<li>
Create executive-ready presentations to summarize key findings and recommendations</li>
<li>
Build operating plans for implementation across the business</li>
<li>
Collaborate with Content teams to identify and evaluate strategic opportunities (e.g. audience development, potential partnerships, and new businesses)</li>
<li>
Create long term strategic plans and support the creation of long term financial plans for current and future WM Entertainment business areas</li>
<li>
Provide support for both WarnerMedia corporate and senior management, including strategy reviews, board presentations, and other management meetings</li>
<li>
Produce and maintain advanced knowledge of industry trends and competitive activity</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
REQUIREMENTS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
4 – 6 years of work experience that includes one or a combination of the following:</li>
<li>
Management Consulting within the Media, Technology, or Telecom practice</li>
<li>
Investment Banking, VC, or PE within the Media, Technology, or Telecom practice</li>
<li>
Corporate development, strategy, business development, or corporate finance at a media or technology company</li>
<li>
Mastery of Excel and PowerPoint skills:</li>
<li>
Superior quantitative modeling skills and a sound understanding of finance</li>
<li>
Ability to distill complex concepts and analyses into clear, easy-to-understand presentations</li>
<li>
Strong organizational skills with the ability to manage multiple simultaneous projects with several deliverables under tight deadlines</li>
<li>
Passionate about media and entertainment with a voracious appetite for industry news (e.g. on content, streaming services, linear ecosystem, advertising evolution)</li>
<li>
High level of integrity, strong work ethic, deep intellectual curiosity, and willingness to think outside the box</li>
<li>
Strong interpersonal skills with a collaborative DNA</li>
<li>
Ability to thrive and take initiative in a fast-paced and high-pressure environment</li>
<li>
Experience interfacing and presenting to senior executives and management teams</li>
<li>
Must be able to travel as needed</li>
</ul>
