<h2>Training Facilitator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Location(s)
</p>Dallas TX
<p></p>
<p><b>Details
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>The Training Facilitator is responsible for preparing and coordinating training programs that support employee on-boarding, internal systems training, new processes instruction, as well as product training for Kemper reps and agents. The ideal candidate will have experience conducting training sessions on a variety of subjects and will be responsible for developing tools that support—employee on-boarding , internal systems, new processes, and product training for Kemper reps and agents.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Position Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Partner with stakeholders and sales reps to assess training needs and identify opportunities for improvement.
</li>
<li>Work collaboratively with the Professional Development team to design and ensure the delivery of successful, engaging and effective learning experiences.
</li>
<li>Work with the Professional Development team to develop and outline sales processes.
</li>
<li>Organize training activities—presentations, job simulations and role-playing activities on sales related topics.
</li>
<li>Conduct ride-a-longs with field reps and shadow internal sales reps to assess skills. Manage administrative duties for training sessions—including creating training agendas, scheduling, logistical preparation and tracking.
</li>
<li>Assist in managing Kemper’s Learning Management System—including content management, course enrollment, etc.
</li>
<li>Monitor, assess, and evaluate sales and agent training programs.
</li>
<li>Provide ongoing training support for sales reps and agents.
</li>
<li>Perform other duties as assigned.
</li>
<li><p>50% of domestic travel during first four months of employment and up to 30% thereafter.
</p></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Position Qualifications:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>BA/BS degree in Communications, Instructional Design, Education, HR or related area or equivalent in education/experience.
</li>
<li>2-4 years of demonstrated experience facilitating Instructor-Led Training (ILT) and Virtual Instructor-Led Training (VILT).
</li>
<li>Experience designing curriculum for corporate employees.
</li>
<li>Experience with publishing and tracking learning objects in an LMS.
</li>
<li>Knowledge of ADDIE and a variety of adult learning/training methodologies.
</li>
<li>Experience teaching others how to use software is highly desired.
</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Office (PowerPoint, Excel, and Word), SharePoint, Webex, Skype for Business, Adobe Connect, Camtasia, Snagit, and video editing/whiteboard animation software (e.g., Adobe Premiere, PowToon, etc.).
</li>
<li>1+years insurance experience preferred
</li>
<li>Experience developing sales training preferred
</li>
<li>Proficiency with Adobe Creative Suite and eLearning software platforms is highly desired (e.g., Adobe Captivate, Articulate Storyline, Camtasia, etc) preferred
</li>
<li>Understanding of SCORM preferred
</li>
<li>Bilingual in English/Spanish preferred
</li>
</ul>
