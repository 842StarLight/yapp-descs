<h2>Corporate Risk and Compliance Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>
<div>We are looking for a passionate Risk Manager full of ideas to make valuable contributions to a high growth, innovative software company! Do you want to be a true business partner and not a "check the box" compliance manager? If so, this may be the perfect role.</div>
<div>
We are open to candidates in San Francisco, California.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
More about you</b></div>
<div>
You are comfortable working with management and SMEs to understand the business, analyze processes, business and operational models, and discover risks as well as recommend approaches to balancing them (six-sigma black belts and business analysts available for support). You have experience in risk management or internal audit and have high level of understanding of the general corporate risks - related to Finance, HR, Marketing, Sales, Legal. You are comfortable supporting business units in defining their risk strategies and articulating it to internal or external auditors. SOX, FCPA, GDPR, etc. - you don't only understand them but have great ideas how to implement effective and efficient processes and controls that align with the business goals.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Ability to collaborate with and influence various global teams with some level of ambiguity and uncertainty is a must.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
More about our team</b></div>
<div>
Risk &amp; Compliance is a high visibility function working with Atlassian business and technology teams globally. Our responsibilities include risk discovery and assessment, advisory, mitigation and controls oversight, compliance oversight, and reporting. Our purpose is to provide methodologies, structures, processes and oversight to allow effective identification, analysis, reporting, and management of risk throughout the environment. We work with virtually every team within the organization - Legal (we are part of it), Security, Marketing, Finance, Customer Support, Engineering, all product groups, HR, the executive team, etc. We also collaborate with business partners, vendors, consultants and customers.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Key Responsibilities:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Analyze the environment, discover, assess and monitor risksAssist in developing controls and mitigation plans, and lead aspects of their implementation</li>
<li>
Drive and participate in policy and standards development and management activities</li>
<li>
Advise senior management on techniques for optimizing the risk profile of their function</li>
<li>
Facilitate reporting on risks, findings, mitigation plans, and controls performance</li>
<li>
Drive compliance related initiatives</li>
<li>
Participate in internal and external audits and properly articulate the business function risk profile, risk management strategies and controls appropriateness</li>
<li>
Interact with customers and business partners to understand and respond to their compliance and assurance needs and concerns</li>
<li>
Work with senior management to design their compliance strategy and optimize their controls portfolio</li>
<li>
Report on the regular basis to the Head of Risk &amp; Compliance on the risk profile and controls status of assigned business groups</li>
<li>
Own the Business Continuity Management efforts for assigned business units</li>
<li>
Responsible for regular risk assessment and maintaining Key Risk Indicators for your business functions</li>
<li>
Contribute to the risk and compliance awareness activities by presentations to executives, creating vibrant communication channels for monitoring risks, etc.</li>
<li>
Engage in the community by participating in conferences, forums, publishing articles, etc.</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>On the first day, we'll expect you to have:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
5+ years of experience within corporate Compliance, Risk Management or Internal Auditing</li>
<li>
Understanding of core business functions - Finance, Accounting, HR, Legal, Sales &amp; Marketing</li>
<li>
Experience with control and risk frameworks, performing compliance and risk assessments, designing controls and overseeing mitigation projects</li>
<li>
Ability to apply abbreviations like COSO, FCPA, OFAC, HIPPA, SOX, ISO 31000 in a highly dynamic environment</li>
</ul></div>
