<h2>General Manager Extended Stay Hotel</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">A General Manager for an EXCL Hospitality managed hotel has overall responsibility for the sales and operations of an extended stay, limited service hotel and small team. A successful EXCL Hospitality General Manager is a hands on leader who knows how to drive revenue by exceptional customer service and quality room stays, while creating a team that enjoys coming to work each day. The GM focuses on Quality, Compliance, Service, Team, Profit and Sales. Their primary responsibilities are to execute existing EXCL systems to maximize profitability, ensure superior service, drive brand standards, quality, guest satisfaction, safety and security processes, hotel performance, and the development of people.
<br><br>
Our motto is Happy Teams. Happy Guests. Happy Owners.
<br><br>
We will support you and give you the resources you need to build and lead a great team. Each of our hotels is award winning for quality and customer service excellence. If you are motivated by being the best, please apply today.
<br><br>
A more detailed list of the types of responsibilities of a GM is below:
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Lead our full team EXCL Morning Room Preparation process each morning (a physical and energetic time of group effort to set the day's housekeeping and maintenance team up for success). The GM is in every room that is cleaned each day.</li>
<li>Participate in a daily and direct hands-on way, leading the operations of the housekeeping and front desk teams.</li>
<li>Facilitate sales lead management processes within the hotel. Provide inspiration (or motivation) to the sales support to team.</li>
<li>Oversee tracking of leads and results, follow up to ensure sales leads are actively managed and developed.</li>
<li>Maintain relationships with target accounts. Sustain and retain pieces of business that are core business.</li>
<li>Take ownership of sales calls on accounts.</li>
<li>Ensure a super clean room by inspections and training. No room is sold until it has been verified to meet quality standards.</li>
<li>Lead our sales efforts by building a culture of extraordinary customer service.</li>
<li>Ensure that all safety and security processes are executed to keep our employees, guests and building safe and secure.</li>
<li>Administer and the hotel revenue and expense budgets, vendor relationships, sourcing of contracts etc.</li>
<li>Embrace the EXCL Winning Ways systems and be the first to bring forth areas of opportunities to improve.</li>
<li>Analyze Profit &amp; Loss and General Ledger statements.</li>
<li>Manage the daily, weekly, monthly duties for the hotel's online Property Management, Rate Management, Accounting, Human Resources and Sales Software systems,</li>
<li>Manage schedule, and review and approve the hotel payroll.</li>
<li>Review room rate and inventory management to ensure maximization of room revenue.</li>
<li>Recruit, hire, train, counsel and motivate team members. Happy Teams = Happy Guests. Happy Owners.</li>
<li>Ensure proper delivery of guest special requests.</li>
<li>Drive superior guest satisfaction and manage online reviews and feedback.</li>
<li>Inspect rooms according to quality standards for cleanliness and proper preventative maintenance.</li>
<li>Execute hotel programs to assure that the quality program criteria are met.</li>
<li>Ensure all team members are trained to act competently in the event of an emergency or accident at the hotel.</li>
<li>Ensure a viable key control program is in place in all hotel departments.</li>
<li>Ensure team members follow appropriate cash control procedures.</li>
<li>Ensure the security needs of the property and guests are met.</li>
<li>Conduct regular team and sales meetings seeking feedback and providing motivation with solutions.</li>
<li>Respond to requests from immediate supervisor. Weekly conference calls and monthly site visits from owners and management company representatives.</li>
