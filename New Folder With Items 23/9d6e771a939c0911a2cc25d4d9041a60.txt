<h2>Customer Service Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<b>TWO MEN AND A TRUCK®</b> is NOW HIRING enthusiastic, customer focused individuals for our brand new franchise location! In this role, you will use your strategic selling skills and in-depth knowledge of company services to build relationships with our customers. This is a great role for those who are self-starting and enjoy a fast-paced work environment.</div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Provide outstanding customer service on all calls and online inquiries</li>
<li>Recommend moving services and products based on cost, availability, and customer specifications</li>
<li>Input information into internal move software to provide accurate moving estimates and schedule moves</li>
<li>Obtain customer feedback through follow-up calls, both during and after moves; calmly and professionally handle customer concerns</li>
<li>Prepare move-related paperwork and documents</li>
<li>All other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Requirements</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or GED; associate’s degree in a related field preferred</li>
<li>At least 1 year of sales and/or customer service experience</li>
<li>Excellent customer service skills: effective listening and communication</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task and enter customer data into move software while on the phone</li>
<li>Confidence to initiate customer contact and comfortable asking for the sale</li>
<li>Willing to submit to pre-employment background check and drug screen</li>
<li>Creative problem solving skills</li>
<li>Exceptional organization and time management skills</li>
<li>Strong team player who works productively with a wide range of people and personalities</li>
<li>Demonstrated integrity, ethical standards, and commitment to <b>TWO</b> <b>MEN</b> <b>AND</b> <b>A</b> <b>TRUCK</b> Core Values</li>
<li>Bilingual is a plus</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
