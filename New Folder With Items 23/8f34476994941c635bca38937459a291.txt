<h2>Field Marketing Specialist 2</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<b>Sodexo K12 segment</b> has an exciting <b>Field Marketing Specialist 2 </b>opportunity to support our dining program at <b>Richland School District 2 located in Columbia, SC.</b>
</div>
<div></div>

<div>
This Field Marketing Specialist will oversee the marketing program development, implementation of Sodexo and client programs, communication and evaluation designed to drive sales and increase customer satisfaction. Conducts market research and market analysis. Designs, develops and refreshes communication strategies including web, digital and print. Manages and measures social media.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Key Responsibilities:</div>
<ul>
<li>
Coordinates and implements all unit marketing initiatives to drive sales growth</li>
<li>
Coordinates unit specific strategic business plan/marketing plans and its implementation, communication and evaluation</li>
<li>
Maintains and tracks research, customer feedback and satisfaction and reviews statistics and trends with appropriate personnel</li>
<li>
Gather, track, analyze and report on results of marketing initiatives</li>
<li>
Responsible for daily posts/tweets and monitoring of all applicable social media outlets for multiple conference center units</li>
<li>
Identifies needs and makes recommendations for increasing sales</li>
<li>
Identify and clarify intern/external customer needs and expectations, taking actions to ensure that Sodexo and customer goals are aligned and met</li>
<li>
Prepare plans for projects or major assignments (i.e., Tasks, Resources and Time Frames)</li>
<li>
Creates and distributes monthly marketing calendar</li>
<li>
Participate in marketing conference calls and training</li>
<li>
Targets product compliance as it relates to marketing efforts such as printing, graphics and advertising.</li>
</ul>
