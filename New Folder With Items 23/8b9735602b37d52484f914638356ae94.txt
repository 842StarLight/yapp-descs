Job Description</p>
<p>

<p>
Join CoStar Real Estate Manager, a division of CoStar Group, a $1.4B fast-growing provider of cloud-based data and applications for the commercial real estate industry and corporations leasing real estate (https://www.costar.com/about). CoStar Real Estate Manager (https://costarmanager.com/), a division of CoStar Group, has had rapid growth of almost 100% annually since 2017, and is seeking a Solutions Consultant for the Eastern Region to join our team in Atlanta to continue our successful track record. This is a new position. As a fast-growing industry leader, CoStar has strong professional growth potential, competitive compensation, and industry-leading employee benefits.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
The Solutions Consultant works with sales people and partners to help sell the CoStar Real Estate Manager solutions and services to customers and new prospects (mostly in the Fortune 1000) in a high-growth, competitive environment. This work will include presentations and demonstrations through web meetings, conference calls, and in-person meetings along with various other sales support activities. Such additional activities include RFP’s, competitive analysis, marketing collateral development, presentation development and sales strategy development.</p>

Our Software Products</p>

Lease Accounting is a new, high-growth market, important to Fortune 1000 companies as they require compliance with this change in the accounting and finance regulations. To learn more about CoStar Real Estate Manger and the new accounting changes go to https://www.costarmanager.com/</p>
