<h2>Regional Sales Director San Francisco</h2>

<b>Executive Overview:</b><br>
JASK, founded in 2015, is led by recognized industry leaders with decades of successful experience solving real-world SOC issues.
<br><br>
Frustrated with the way SOC's operate today and burn out good, hard working analysts, SIEM's of yesterday inability to narrow the aperture on the alerts/threats that matter most, we are driven by a mission to solve the security gaps that impede security operation modernization. JASK's world-class engineering, data science and services teams apply decades of hands-on experience to drive advancements in autonomous SOC technology and operation workflows.
<br><br>
JASK is modernizing security operations with products and services to reduce organizational risk and improve human efficiency. We are empowering the SOC analyst to focus on investigative and response work, rather than the onerous data ingestion, normalization, parsing, and alert discrimination that is required to simply determine what is important and take action.
<br><br>
<b>Position Description:</b><br>
JASK is seeking seeks to hire a seasoned and accomplished sales executive. This Individual will report to the Regional Vice President. Notwithstanding there will be signification interaction and cooperation with the executive team, finance, marketing, and partner organizations who will all participate in the selection process. For focused individuals who thrive in an environment of change and who have demonstrated a highly successful track record, this position provides high visibility and an ability for financial rewards through the monetization of their stock options and an uncapped compensation plan. While JASK seeks an independent, autonomous and creative performer, this individual must also be a "team player" who can successfully partner within the company as well as with JASK's partner ecosystem.
