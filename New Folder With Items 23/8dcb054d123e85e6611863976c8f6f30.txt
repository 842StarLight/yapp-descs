<div><b>Who We Are:</b></div>
<div>
Spring Venture Group is a leading digital direct-to-consumer sales and marketing company with product offerings focused on the senior market. We specialize in distributing Medicare Supplement, Medicare Advantage, and related products via our family of brands and dedicated team of licensed insurance agents. Powered by our unique technologies that combine sophisticated marketing, comparison shopping, sales execution, and customer engagement – we help thousands of seniors across the country navigate the complex world of Medicare every day.</div>
<div>
</div>
<div><b>
What We Want:</b></div>
<div>
Spring Venture Group is looking for a talented, passionate, and curious Python Developer to help support both the data science and analytics teams. Your primary objective in this role is "data science enablement". The analytics team you will be working with is in charge of optimizing company profits by determining the price we pay for leads, price we pay for ads, and determining which sales agents get which leads and when. This position works will all facets of the IT organization to insure that the data science and analytics team has all the technical requirements met in order to "make use of" their data science models.</div>
<div>
</div>
<div>
Some of our past projects include: Working on data science shared library, building a visual configuration tool using python, flask, css, setting up a service to stream changes from our third party hiring vendor into a database, and Salesforce records reconciliation.</div>
<div>
</div>
<div>
Technologies that we use include: Python, Java, Angular, Python machine learning libraries, Pandas, Tableau, Mysql, AWS</div>

Responsibilities:</b>
</div>

<li>
