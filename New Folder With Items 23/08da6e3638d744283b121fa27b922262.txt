<h2>Software Engineer II</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Storage Commander Cloud is the next level of self-storage management software.</p>
<p>With an intuitive user interface, tiered pricing to fit any operator's budget, and a variety of unique features, Storage Commander scales seamlessly to meet the needs of any self-storage operation.</p>
<p>As one of our Software Engineers, you will design, develop, and maintain computer software for in-house and customer facing applications. Provide support to the Technical Support and Sales Departments on complex cases or providing information on system capabilities. Administration of in-house and customer data storage systems and communication system. Perform hardware and software testing of customer facing products.</p>
<p>Duties Include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Develop and maintain software programs for in-house and customer facing programs.</li>
<li>Perform data migration from competitor’s software to the company’s products.</li>
<li>Troubleshoot and repair software, hardware, and data issues escalated from Technical Support.</li>
<li>Provide technical assistance to the sales and marketing team.</li>
<li>Perform quality assurance testing of software products.</li>
</ul>
<p>Knowledge and Skills:</p>
<ul>
<li>Proficient at maintaining source code in C#, Java Script, Angular and ASP languages.</li>
<li>Good understanding and practice developing Structure Query Language and Microsoft SQL database servers.</li>
<li>Good written and verbal communication skills required.</li>
<li>Strong critical thinking skills.</li>
<li>Work well independently and within software development teams.</li>
</ul>
