<h2>Front Desk Receptionist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Division:</b> Orlando Location</p>
<p><b>
Department:</b> Front Office</p>
<p><b>
Reports To:</b> Office Manager</p>
<p><b>
FLSA Status:</b> Non-Exempt</p>
<p><b>Summary:</b> Answers inquiries and provides information to the general public, customers, visitors, and other interested parties regarding activities conducted at CHILL Crynosauna. Performs general office duties such as answering phones, scheduling appointments, filing, processing payments, performing cryptotherapy sessions, and light office cleaning by performing the following duties.</p>
<p><b>Duties and Responsibilities</b> include the following. Other duties may be assigned.</p>
<ul>
<li>Operates telephone, answers and forwards calls, provides information, takes messages, or schedules appointments.*</li>
<li>Greets persons entering establishment, determines nature and purpose of visit, and directs or escorts them to specific destinations including changing rooms or cryptotherapy chamber room.*</li>
<li>Transmits information or documents to customers using computer, mail, or facsimile machine.*</li>
<li>Hears and resolves complaints from customers or the public.*</li>
<li>Performs administrative support tasks such as proofreading, transcribing handwritten information, or operating customers or computers to work with any records, invoices, or other documents.*</li>
<li>Files and maintains records.*</li>
<li>Provides information about establishment such as location of offices, employees within the organization, or services provided.*</li>
<li>Collects, sorts, distributes, or prepares mail, messages, or courier deliveries.*</li>
<li>Processes and prepares memos, correspondence, or other documents.*</li>
<li>Receives payment and records receipts for services.*</li>
<li>Schedules appointments and maintains and updates appointment calendars.*</li>
<li>Analyzes data to determine answers to questions from customers or members of the public.*</li>
<li>Keeps a current record of staff members whereabouts and availability.*</li>
<li>Takes orders for merchandise or materials and sends them to the proper departments to be filled.*</li>
<li>Enrolls individuals to participate in programs and notifies them of their acceptance.*</li>
<li>Responsible for light cleaning and maintenance including laundry, mopping of floors, cleaning of restroom, and general cleaning duties.*</li>
<li>Performs cryotherapy duties as specified in CHILL Crysoauna policy guidelines.*</li>
<li>Performs assigned marketing duties including emails and social media posts.*</li>
<li>Performs administrative activities including maintaining records, reports, or files, types letters or correspondence, and maintains appointment calendar.*</li>
<li>Works directly with the public including collecting payment, answering questions from employees or public, providing clerical assistance to customers, and greeting customers, guests, or visitors.*</li>
</ul>
