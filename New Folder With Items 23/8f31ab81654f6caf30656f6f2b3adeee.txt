<h2>Financial Analyst III</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Responsible for Capital Assets:</b> record asset additions and deletions, construction in progress, depreciation, and reconcile balances.
<br>Record Journal vouchers and reconcile general ledger accounts.
<br>Prepares and files monthly sales and use tax returns.
<br>Tracks payroll disbursement and maintains outstanding checklist.
<br>Assists in the preparation of the annual financial statements.
<br>Other duties/projects as assigned.
<br><br>
Minimum Qualifications/Experience
<br><br>
Requires baccalaureate degree in Accounting, Business Administration or related field; minimum of three years of experience involving general accounting principles and practice; knowledge of PC-based word processing and spreadsheet software; ability to manage multiple projects and time effectively; and excellent interpersonal and communication skills.
<br><br>
Banner experience preferred.
<br><br>
Special Instructions to Applicants
<br><br>
Employment is contingent upon successfully completing a criminal background check as mandated by Tennessee State University.
<br>Official transcripts for positions which require a post-secondary undergraduate degree, advanced degree and/or certification must be presented upon hire. An unofficial transcript may be attached in the "Transcript" section.
<br><br>
You will be required to provide names and contact information for three references.
