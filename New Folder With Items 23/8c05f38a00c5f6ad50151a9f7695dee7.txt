<h2>Marketing Litigation Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div><div>
<div>Skadden is seeking a Marketing Litigation Specialist who will collaborate with attorneys on litigation-related new business pitches and serve as primary contact for litigation experience requests in connection with pitches/proposals, thought leadership and directory and other recognition, media outreach, etc. Oversee coordinator who researches and tracks litigation matters handled by the firm; work with coordinator and other members of the Marketing Department to draft and distribute matter information and news; coordinate with litigation attorneys and Litigation PR manager on thought leadership projects. Additionally, the Marketing Litigation Specialist</div>
<ul>
<li>Drafts pitches and proposals in collaboration with lawyers in the Litigation, Securities Litigation, Government Enforcement/White Collar Crime, Mass Torts Insurance and Consumer Litigation groups, among others in the litigation practice.
</li>
<li>Serves as primary Marketing contact for litigation-related experience requests in connection with new business pitches, thought leadership, directory and other recognition, media outreach, etc.
</li>
<li>Works in conjunction with senior coordinator to research/draft and track/distribute litigation matter information and news handled by the firm.</li>
<li>Work with litigation attorneys and Litigation PR Manager in the Marketing Department to develop media pitches and topics for lawyer-authored articles.</li>
<li>Ensures that the marketing materials for each Litigation practice group are current, accurate and complete.</li>
<li>Identifies best practices for utilizing experience database to obtain targeted litigation information; trains colleagues on using Foundation marketing database.</li>
<li>Collaborates with other department members, including editorial, PR and design specialists to produce a variety of products in connection with individual litigation partner and practice business development efforts; and liaises with other Firm administrative support departments (such as Client Accounting, Business Development and Technology) to obtain client relationship data for new business proposals and presentations.</li>
<li>Performs other related duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
<div></div>
