<h2>Retail Manager Vape Store</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Craving Vapor is looking for an experienced Retail Manager to provide oversight for all operations at one of our Austin locations! The Retail Manager leads all operations at the location and is responsible for making sure the store functions smoothly while optimizing profits. The Retail Manager will direct restocking efforts, the floor merchandising plan, as well as conduct hiring, scheduling and training. In this position, the Retail Manager must be able to garner respect and motivate personnel to perform at their maximum capacity, keeping the store in good working order. The ideal candidate will have a working knowledge of sales, staff training, merchandising, ordering, and marketing as well as strong people-skills.</p>
<p>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</p>
<ul>
<li>Supervises other employees, including the hiring, training, scheduling, promoting, recommending of raises, and firing of staff. Reconciles differences among staff and maintains a level of satisfaction among employees.</li>
<li>Plans and directs the day-to-day operations of the store.</li>
<li>Handles customers’ grievances and attempts to restore the customers’ goodwill toward the store if there are negative feelings.</li>
<li>Develops strategies to improve customer service</li>
<li>Keep accurate and up-to-date records of store inventory; ensure that new arrivals are recorded, priced and displayed or shelved.</li>
<li>Works with Top Management to make decisions on advertising, promotions, and which products to put on sale.</li>
<li>Supervises cash and accounting procedures, verifying expenditures and receipts.</li>
<li>Monitor and analyze sales, determine store- wide/market trends.</li>
<li>Keep up-to-date with respect to industry trends</li>
<li>Develops strategies to drive store sales, determine profitability of goods and services.</li>
<li>Secure merchandise by implementing security systems and measures</li>
<li>Protect employees and customers by providing a safe and clean store environment</li>
<li>Maintain the stability and reputation of the store by complying with legal requirements</li>
<li>Manage all controllable costs to keep operations profitable</li>
<li>Tour the sales floor regularly, talking to colleagues and customers to identify or resolve urgent issues</li>
<li>Delegate appropriate tasks to staff members to help accomplish the mission and goals of the organization</li>
<li>Responsible for developing employees to ensure that they are well trained, to identify future leaders, and to prepare their own successor should they ever move up or move on to other organizations.</li>
</ul>
