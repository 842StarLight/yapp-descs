<p><b>Title: Director of Business Solutions/IT</b></p>
<p>
The Onni Group is a leading real estate firm based in Vancouver British Columbia with offices in Los Angeles, Seattle, Chicago, Phoenix and Toronto. This diverse company is vertically integrated with in-house departments responsible for execution of the company's activities including property development, construction and management as well as leasing, sales and marketing.</p>
<p><b>
Position Summary</b></p>
<p>
Provides technical support, training, and direction to departmental staff using computer software and applications. Troubleshoots and resolves problems with business systems; Trains internal staff using various mediums to ensure staff is up-to-date and knowledgeable about all business systems.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>
The essential functions include, but are not limited to the following:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Managing a technical team focusing on development and operational support of IT solutions</li>
<li>
Set goals/expectations, drive project prioritization and resource allocations for projects</li>
<li>
Deliver information technology projects on-time, on-budget, within scope, high quality</li>
<li>
Foster innovation within the organization to create new solutions that enable business results and to improve effectiveness of Business Application delivery</li>
<li>
Manage ongoing projects, from integration of new acquired companies, to incremental change management, to development of new systems components in support of business operations</li>
<li>
Lead and oversee activities of assigned projects to ensure goals and objectives are achieved within approved time frames, scope and budget</li>
<li>
Responsible for resource planning</li>
<li>
Ability to prioritize individual and group tasks to meet conflicting demands</li>
<li>
Proactively anticipate project deviations and escalate the need for immediate corrective action</li>
<li>
Will be expected to contribute to business strategy utilizing Information Technology &amp; Business Systems to create business value, operational efficiencies,</li>
<li>
This role will be responsible for the business systems and applications that run the enterprise. These include: Yardi, SharePoint 2013 and SharePoint 365, Nintex Workflow and Nintex Forms, MS Office 365 applications (including Flow, PowerApp, Power BI), SQL Server 2018, SSIS/SSAS, PSICapture, RSI, Layer2 Cloud Connector.</li>
<li>
Gather requirements from operational departments to develop reports, processes, &amp; efficiency</li>
<li>
Develop and manage an Enterprise application roadmap and target application architecture</li>
