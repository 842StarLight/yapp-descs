<p><b>COMPANY</b></p>

<p><b>
DESCRIPTION</b></p>
<p>
We are currently seeking a full-time Business Development Specialist to contact state and local government officials and arrange an online demonstration of the GovQA Compliance Suite. This idea person will be able to assess government websites, navigate complex government organization and qualify government prospects for a 30-minute demonstration.</p>
<p>
In this role, the Business Development Specialist will be a “consultative hunter” that is quite adept at making more than 50 contacts each day with government officials and talking with them about issues in their work environment so as to arrange an online demonstration of the GovQA Compliance Suite. Arranging demonstration may require considerable email communication and calendar coordination among parties.</p>
<p>
The Business Development Specialist will be supported by multiple products that are market leaders, hundreds of references and a team of professionals in our Woodridge, Illinois offices.</p>
<p>
We are specifically looking for a PROVEN solution-oriented Business Development Specialist who is comfortable with web and SaaS technologies, can pioneer new relationships over the phone, and is familiar with the latest CRM technologies.</p>
