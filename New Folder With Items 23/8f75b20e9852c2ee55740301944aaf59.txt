<h2>Senior Product Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>Do you want to help an internet pioneer enhance modern digital experiences for the world’s largest consumer brands and enterprises? Akamai’s intelligent edge platform surrounds everything, from the enterprise to the cloud, so customers and their businesses can be fast, intelligent, and secure. We are looking for a dynamic, creative, and driven product marketer to advance the go to market strategy for Akamai’s Web Performance Solutions. The successful candidate will be intensely collaborative, working closely with product management, campaign and field marketing, direct and channel sales and our enablement team. As the Sr. Product Marketing Manager, you will lead the charge for product and solution success through the creation and execution of a strategy comprised of positioning, messaging, content, sales tools and training, while serving as a credible subject matter expert for the solutions and their buyers.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
 As a Senior Product Marketing Manager for the Web division, you will be responsible for:</div>
<ul>
<li>Developing and collaborating on go-to-market strategies that direct the business towards the highest potential market opportunities to drive growth and engage buyers with compelling value propositions and offers</li>
<li>Crafting positioning, messaging and marketing content that demonstrates differentiation compared to market competitors</li>
<li>Developing thought leadership messaging and campaign materials that leverage insights derived from data to drive awareness of Akamai's visibility into internet trends</li>
<li>Planning and executing cross-platform market launches in collaboration with a cross-functional team to communicate positioning and drive awareness and demand for Akamai’s latest capabilities in the market</li>
<li>Communicating the value proposition for web performance to the sales teams and developing sales tools that leverage Akamai’s data and insights as a unique solution differentiator</li>
<li>Collaborating with Campaign and Field Marketing to support the creation of campaigns that are well connected – from messaging to the underlying campaign tactics and field execution</li>
<li>Continuously monitoring, analyzing, sharing and developing responses to market developments, customer behavior and competitor's activities</li>
<li>Creating sales tools and collateral (presentations, datasheets, white papers, customer case studies, demos, and training materials) to communicate Akamai’s value proposition to customers and partners</li>
<li>Traveling up to 10-20%</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
 About the Team</div>
<div>
The digital experiences you and I connect to every day have become richer and more interactive while at the same time our expectations for how fast they should perform across all of our devices has never been higher. The performance team is driving innovation in how organizations build and deliver modern digital experiences to drive efficiency in their operations while ensuring exceptional customer experiences for users all around the world leveraging the power of data to identify and remove critical barriers to web performance.</div>
<div>
This role will report to the Director of Product Marketing as part of the Global Product and Industry Marketing team. In this position the individual will work closely with web performance product management, Akamai's field sales and marketing teams, and corporate marketing communications to ensure the proper market positioning and messaging for Akamai’s products and solutions. You will work as part of an experienced global team of product and industry marketers responsible for all Akamai products and will gain visibility to and help craft market aligned solutions comprised of products within and outside of the web portfolio.</div>
