<h2>Canvassing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>PURPOSE: The purpose of this position is to cost effectively drive non-media lead generation and local awareness through the local canvassing program while managing to a set budget.</p>
<p>PRIMARY RESPONSIBILITIES:</p>
<p>Manages a team of canvassers with a direct hands-on approach, provides motivation and coaching to help team reach daily, weekly and monthly goals.</p>
<p>Recruits, hires, train canvassers through cost effective recruiting tools including partnering with temporary agencies when appropriate.</p>
<p>Oversees all canvass field activities, including training, role playing, daily meetings, field supervision and motivating team members.</p>
<p>Create and develop the culture of the local canvassing team, through personal role modeling desired behaviors and techniques.</p>
<p>Monitors and coaches every team member to ensure they are following proper procedures, scripts and customer service guidelines.</p>
<p>In partnership with Marketing leadership team, creates annual budget with the sole purpose of generating qualified leads and appointments through canvassing efforts.</p>
<p>Administrative tasks include: coordinating canvass logistics with mapping and tracking, permit logistics, script and flyer maintenance, organizing field lead sheets, verifying / validating leads gathered during each shift.</p>
<p>Provides timely tracking and entering of marketing data into corporate database.</p>
<p>Provides information to supervisors, co-workers, and subordinates by telephone, in written form, e-mail, or in person on neighborhoods and cities where Renewal by Andersen’s products and services can be demonstrated with the intent of generating qualified leads and appointments.</p>
<p>QUALIFICATIONS:</p>
<p>High School diploma, College degree preferred or equivalent work experience .</p>
<p>Requires 3+ years of experience in management experience in canvassing / face to face marketing.</p>
<p>Valid Driver’s license with a clean driving record</p>
<p>Excellent training and coaching skills</p>
<p>Requires weekend work</p>
<p>Ability to think strategically, with strong project management skills, including attention to detail</p>
<p>Strong verbal and written communication skills</p>
<p>Ability to self manage resources (time and budget) to achieve objectives</p>
<p>Ability to analyze effectiveness of canvassing program and canvassing team</p>
<p>Ability to understand the role of marketing communications, sales promotion and advertising</p>
<p>Ability to demonstrate strong leadership skills</p>
<p>Demonstrated analytic skills</p>
<p>Demonstrated ability in Microsoft Office programs, specifically Excel</p>
