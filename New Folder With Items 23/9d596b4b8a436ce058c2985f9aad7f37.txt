<div>Fountain is the hiring automation software that powers the new service economy to source, screen, hire and onboard nearly 1 million applicants per month, while helping 150,000 people get hired each month. Since inception, teams across 50 countries use Fountain to save millions of work-hours and communicate with more than 10+ million applicants. Our mission is to open opportunities for the new global workforce. Fountain's customers include Uber Eats, Safeway, Lime and Shipt. What we build fundamentally affects the way people across the world work and our mission is to maximize efficiency and unlock economic opportunity in the labor market.</div>

We are looking for colleagues who are excited about making an impact in a fast-paced and growing company with an extremely open and collaborative culture!

<div>As an Account Manager, you'll be responsible for building trust and relationships with a portfolio of existing customers. You'll be managing a sales pipeline as well as negotiating and closing contracts. You should be adept at navigating organizations to identify and manage relationships with the landscape of decision makers, detractors, and champions. Above all, you should be driven, a self-starter, and scrappy. This role will report to the VP of Sales.</div>
