<h2>Staff Accountant (Entry Level)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Hondros Family of Companies is looking to add a staff accountant to its growing accounting team. This is an entry level position. The Staff Accountant is responsible for: account payables, receivables, monthly closing and financial reporting, including analyzing variances in actual results to plan; maintaining inventory; and fixed Assets. This position is also involved in the organization’s annual budget and forecast reporting.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Job Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for weekly accounts payable process; include data entry of weekly invoices and processing weekly checks</li>
<li>Maintain vendor file information</li>
<li>Responsible for sales and receipts reconciliation and posting to the general ledger</li>
<li>Responsible for all monthly journal entries</li>
<li>Responsible for monthly Financial Reports and variance reporting</li>
<li>Responsible for account analysis on major accounts</li>
<li>Responsible for reconciling and invoicing inter-company accounts</li>
<li>Performance of Financial Analysis as needed</li>
<li>Responsible for month-end Bank Reconciliation</li>
<li>Assisting in the preparation of the Annual Budget</li>
<li>Assisting in Forecasting Sales and Profit as needed throughout the year</li>
<li>Create and maintain Accounting Policies and Procedure manual</li>
<li>Maintain fixed assets</li>
<li>Year-end Close preparation</li>
<li>Assist in audit preparation</li>
<li>Attend meetings, events, training, etc. as needed.</li>
<li>Perform other duties as assigned.</li>
 </ul>

<p><b>Education/Experience:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>A Bachelor’s degree in accounting or related field or an equivalent combination of education and experience required. A minimum of 1-3 years of experience in accounting and financial analysis required. Experience with databases, Excel and various financial software packages required. Experience with Accounts Payable, Accounts Receivable, Inventory, fixed assets, and strong knowledge of basic accounting principles is required. ACCPAC Financial Package experience is a plus. Strong organizational skills are required and the ability to work well under pressure also required. The successful candidate should be eager to learn and grow with our company.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Communication Skills/Requirements:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Able to effectively communicate verbally and in writing as to work requirements, work in progress, work completion and status of departmental goals. Communicates verbally and in writing with employees, other members of management, vendors and customers. Able to utilize active listening skills to ascertain areas of need, define problems, and determine possible solutions to meet expressed needs. Demonstrate leadership, tact, diplomacy, professionalism and a positive demeanor in performance of all duties.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Mathematical Ability:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Able to work with mathematical concepts such as probability and statistical inference. Able to apply concepts such as fractions, percentages rations and proportions to practical situations. Must have a good understanding of general accounting principles.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Reasoning Ability:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Most work is complex and requires a high level of judgment. Requires the ability to decide on a course or sequence of action and to direct others in that course of action. Must have continuous visual and mental attention while performing responsibilities of the position. Must be able to define problems, collect data, establish facts and draw valid conclusions. Must be able to reasonably envision future needs and plan accordingly.</p>
