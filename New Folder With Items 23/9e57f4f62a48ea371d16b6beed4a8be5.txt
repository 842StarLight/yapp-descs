<h2>Automotive Technician</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><i>The Detroit Garage</i> auto family is currently seeking a motivated and experienced Automotive Technician! We currently have opportunities in our Whitmore Lake, Downtown Farmington, and Livonia locations. The Automotive Technician is committed to providing the highest quality of repairs and service necessary to satisfy the customer. They should be self-motivated, dependable, hard-working and work well in a team environment.We have built our business to change the way people think of auto repair. Concentrating on customer service, convenience, and an all-around exceptional experience is something we strive for on a daily basis. Together, we have made a huge impact on our customers and are looking for you to help grow our team! At <i>Detroit Garage</i> we are always innovating, improving processes, and setting the bar higher for ourselves, the automotive service industry, and our customers.</p>
<p></p>
<div>
<b>Duties and Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Diagnosis of customer vehicles</li>
<li>Properly secure vehicle to lift/alignment machine to ensure safe operation</li>
<li>Interaction between employees and/or customers; explaining diagnosis and needed repairs as required to assist sales staff and/or clarify for customers</li>
<li>Providing the highest quality repairs to customers' vehicles</li>
<li>Staying updated on most current technology, requirements and processes regarding automotive service and repair</li>
<li>Maintains an organized and safe work environment</li>
<li>The ability to be ready to start work by scheduled time</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned by supervision</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned by retail store manager or retail assistant manager</li>
</ul>
</div>
<p></p>
<p><b>Minimum Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>State of Michigan Mechanic Certification; (ASE Master Certification strongly preferred)</li>
<li>Must be at least 18 years of age</li>
<li>Valid driver's license</li>
<li>Ability to communicate clearly and professionally with co-workers and management</li>
<li>Ability to read and write English</li>
<li>Demonstrates a commitment of high quality service to internal and external customers</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<div>
<b>Experience:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>A minimum of 2 years of automotive repair experience with emphasis on under-car repairs, braking systems and alignments</li>
<li>Ability to diagnose and repair drivability issues, A/C systems, and light engine repair is a plus</li>
</ul>
</div>
<p></p>
<div>
<b>Education:</b><br>
<ul><li>High School Diploma/GED or equivalent experience</li></ul>
</div>
<p></p>
<div>
<b>Physical Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>The ability to be on your feet for long periods of time</li>
<li>The ability to safely lift of up to 75 pounds to chest level</li>
<li>Bending, twisting, grabbing, reaching and any other activity normally associated with an Automotive Mechanic position</li>
</ul>
