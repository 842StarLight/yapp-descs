<h2>Leasing Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Ewald Fleet Solutions in Waukesha is searching for a Leasing Coordinator!</b></p>
<p>The Leasing Coordinator is a key position within Ewald Fleet Solutions, providing support to all sales activities and ensuring that the company image is represented in a very professional manner. This position would be responsible to manage and complete a variety of projects and duties as well as provide assistance to other people within the sales department when assigned. Processing lease contracts, tracking orders, and account posting are also part of this very detailed position.</p>
<p><b>Essential Job Functions: </b></p>
<ul><li>Build quotes, specs, and assist with locating and acquiring vehicles from dealer stock.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Secure upfitting and or graphic quotes.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Issue purchase orders.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Audit purchase orders and verify credit standing on existing lease customers. Request updated credit information as needed.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Issue payment for vehicles.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Prepare and audit lease contracts, invoices and any other necessary documents. Verify all data on the lease worksheet is accurate and tax rates are correct.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Audit and review signed lease contracts, upload lease information into lease tracking software.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Order any parts or graphics on stock or ordered vehicles.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Prepare courtesy delivery packets and send out to dealers.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Follow up on delivery paperwork and Lease Contracts.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Post receivable payments into Deposit Edge, ALS, and Sage 50.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintain and balance the borrow certificate.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Coordinate drivers for deliveries and for off lease vehicles.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Reconcile vehicle payables account daily.</li></ul>
<p><b>Essential Job Requirements: </b></p>
<p><b>Education: </b> High School Diploma</p>
<p><b>Experience: </b> 3 –5 years general office and bookkeeping experience</p>
<p><b>Required Skills: </b> Computer literate with experience in Word, Excel, and data entry/accounting software. Must possess basic bookkeeping/accounting skills and above average written and oral communication skills. Proficiency, accuracy, and expediency with processing documents. Automotive industry familiarity beneficial.</p>
<p><b>This is a Monday-Friday 8am-5pm position with weekends off!</b></p>
