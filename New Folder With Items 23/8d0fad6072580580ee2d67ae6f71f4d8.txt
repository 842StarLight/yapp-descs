<h2>Sales Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>POSITION: </b><b>Sales Manager</b></p>
<p><b>
DEPARTMENT: </b><b>Sales and Marketing
</b></p>
<p><b>REPORTS TO: </b><b>Sr. Sales &amp; Marketing Manager</b></p>
<p><b>
FLSA STATUS: </b><b>Salaried Exempt</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Summary</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
SMG, the leader in privately managed public assembly facilities has an excellent and immediate opening for a <b>Sales Manager</b> at the SMG Shreveport Convention Center.Primary responsibility is booking large, high-revenue producing events at all venues within the Shreveport Convention Center including, but not limited to, the following event types: private, corporate, association, conferences, non-ticketed, open to public and community-related events, as well as sporting events.Responsible for working in conjunction with other members of the Sales staff to market the venues and initiate specific event-related promotions to increase revenue.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Include the following. Other duties may be assigned.</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Assist Sales team in accomplishing sales and marketing goals</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Use Sales and Marketing principles to promote the facility including prospecting</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Send client pre-event information and follow-up information that affects booking</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Quote rates and assists with formal license agreements; prepare contracts</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Handle pre-event correspondence and meetings until account is be turned over to an Event Coordinator</p>
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Assist Sr. Sales and Marketing Manager in developing Marketing Plan to include goals and advertising</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Carry out specific marketing plan relating to events using support of advertising, as requested by the Sr. Sales and Marketing Manager</p>
<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Supervisory Responsibilities<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>This position has limited supervisory responsibilities for employees or interns in the Sales and Marketing Department. Sales Manager carries out supervisory responsibilities in accordance with SMG's policies and applicable laws. Responsibilities include interviewing, hiring, and training employees; planning, assigning, and directing work; appraising performance; rewarding; disciplining employees in conjunction with Human Resources; addressing complaints and resolving problems.</p>
