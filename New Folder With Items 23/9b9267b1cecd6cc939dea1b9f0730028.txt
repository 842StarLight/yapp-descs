<h2>Marketing Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Join our highly collaborative, dynamic team and help magnify the impact of Acenda programs and services by strengthening and inspiring community engagement.</p>
<p><b>
Overview:</b></p>
<p>
The Marketing Coordinator supports the Development &amp; Marketing Division in advancing the organization's mission and goals, promote its successes and achievements, inform and engage its stakeholders, and ensure the integrity of its brand. As a key teammate in the division, the candidate will be creatively nimble while bringing strategic ideas to take our creative efforts to the next level. The successful candidate will be a positive-minded, creative marketer with both project management and communication skills as well as the ability to leverage data for decision-making.</p>
<p><b>
Work Performed:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Conceptualizes, designs, and creates strategic marketing materials for use across various media platforms.</li>
<li>Crafts audience-focused communications based on data, best-practices, and emerging trends.</li>
<li>Creates compelling and consistent narratives that shape public perception and drive our engagement outcomes.</li>
<li>Works to achieve brand consistency and the highest standards for communications.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Drafts and distributes content for news releases and maintains media lists and relations.</li>
<li>Experiments with various organic and paid acquisition channels.</li>
<li>Produces valuable and engaging content for our website and blog that attracts and converts our target audiences.</li>
<li>Develops and coordinates thought-leadership content to highlight internal expertise to external audiences.</li>
<li>Interfaced with donors, community partners, sponsors, stakeholders, and volunteers.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Assists in the planning of internal and external events and other mission-driven initiatives</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor's degree in a related field.</li>
<li>Tremendous attention to detail.</li>
<li>Excellent written, computer, and verbal skills.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Ability to organize and prioritize work.</li>
<li>Ability to work independently with little supervision.</li>
<li>Proficiency in marketing tactics and graphic design principles.</li>
<li>Ability to build and foster relationships with individuals with a variety of ages, backgrounds, and experiences through a variety of targeted communications.</li>
<li>Event planning, public relations, or nonprofit related experience is a plus.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Competency with Adobe Creative Cloud software preferred.</li>
<li>Must have and maintain a valid driver's license, use of an insured vehicle and an acceptable driving record.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Characteristics Required:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Must demonstrate initiative, creativity, dependability, and work well independently.</li>
<li>Must be able to exercise independent thinking and good judgment under all circumstances.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Must be able to analyze work, set goals, develop plans, and utilize time effectively and efficiently.</li>
<li>Maintain a positive and welcoming attitude</li>
<li>Attitude of continual improvement and learning</li>
<li>Provide service above and beyond what guests anticipate</li>
<li>Be an active, contributing member of the Development &amp; Marketing Team</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Respect and understand of the differences between positions</li>
<li>Be willing to pitch in and help in areas that do not fall directly within your position's responsibilities.</li>
<li>Must regularly be able to see, speak, and hear. Frequently required to stand, sit, walk, bend, use hands and arms and must occasionally lift up to 20 pounds.</li>
</ul>
