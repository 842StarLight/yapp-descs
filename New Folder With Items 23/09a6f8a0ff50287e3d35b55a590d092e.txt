<h2>Business Development Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Company Overview</b></p>
<p><b>
You must have behavioral health experience to be considered for this position.</b></p>
<p><b>
Billing Solutions </b>is a billing and practice management service company for behavioral healthcare needs. Billing Solutions operates as an extension of the client facility by working closely with its management, clinical, and administrative staff to address their billing issues head on. Whether a large residential center, an individual practitioner, a startup or established treatment center, Billing Solutions can manage the facilities medical billing needs. Billing Solutions specialists bring over 50 years’ experience in a variety of billing and practice management services.</p>
<p><b>
Position Summary</b></p>
<p>
We are seeking<b> </b>a National Business Development Representative with deep industry knowledge, connections and a ‘book of business’ in behavioral health, particularly having experience with facilities, and other in the substance abuse segment of behavioral health. This individual will appreciate the elegance of the Billing Solutions combined software and revenue cycle management offering. The proper candidate will not only have deep connections in this industry, but they will have extensive customer on-boarding and sales experience converting those that outsource their billing, or do it inefficiently in house, to the Billing Solutions platform.</p>
<p>
The candidate will need to be relatively well known in this industry. The proper candidate will be motivated and have excellent follow up and communication skills. The proper candidate will also have experience with search engine optimization and digital marketing techniques and ensure that marketing material and websites are relevant and effective to drive results. Customer communication, continuous contact and openness is of paramount importance.</p>
<p><b>
 </b><b>Primary Responsibilities, Requirement and Representative Tasks:</b></p>

<ul><li>Manage key customer relationships and participate in closing strategic opportunities</li></ul>
