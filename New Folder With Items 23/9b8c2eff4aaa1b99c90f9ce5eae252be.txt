<h2>Senior Datacenter Systems Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<b>JOB SUMMARY:</b><br>
The Sr. Systems Engineer is a Pre-Sale and Post-Sale position. This position is responsible for the design and deployment solutions in Datacenter technologies. The Engineer is responsible for high-level technical support of related issues. The Engineer is responsible for capacity planning and utilization assessments of Datacenter technologies. The Engineer works with vendors for related support and services and works with clients to assess their technical needs, propose a solution, and lead the design and implementation of products and services.<br>
<br>
<b>REQUIRED SKILLS/QUALIFICATIONS:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Computer Information Systems or similar preferred.</li>
<li>
Knowledge and experience performing migrations of applications to the public cloud (preferred AWS).</li>
<li>
Knowledge and experience of VMware Cloud on AWS.</li>
<li>
Minimum of seven years as a systems engineer or consultant in a datacenter environment.</li>
<li>
Proficient level of post-sales installation, configuration, troubleshooting with guidance from senior technical staff.</li>
<li>
Proficient level knowledge of the fundamental concepts, practices, and procedures of a particular field of specialization with the ability to apply concepts. (Software/Applications, Server, Storage, Networks, Security, Mobility).</li>
<li>
Working knowledge of business systems and outcomes with the ability to understand the impact of solution design and implementation on customer's business strategic objectives and requirements.</li>
<li>
Excellent verbal communication skills with the ability to translate technology-related jargon to diverse groups of technical and none- technical users.</li>
<li>
Excellent written communication skills with the ability to develop technical documents.</li>
<li>
Demonstrates a drive for achievement, adaptability/flexibility, dependability, persistence, and tolerance of stressful situations.</li>
<li>
Self-motivated with the ability to perform quality work within deadlines and budget with or without direct supervision.</li>
<li>
Strong analytical thinking, problem-solving, appropriate judgment, and decision-making skills.</li>
<li>
Requires a minimum of one of the following discipline certification: Servers, Storage, Networking, Software, Mobility, Security or AWS.</li>
<li>
Working knowledge of Microsoft Office Suite with the ability to create a document or simple spreadsheet.</li>
<li>
Proficient level in SharePoint technology.</li>
<li>
Proficient level in the development of Product Bill of Materials and basic system designs.</li>
<li>
This is a remote position with up to 50% travel to client sites in the US only.</li>
</ul>
