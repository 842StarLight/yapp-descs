<p><b>Position Objective</b><b>
</b></p>
<p>The Mobility Services Marketing Manager will be responsible for managing and coordinating marketing activities for Mobility Services within the United States. Responsibilities include sales funnel management, communication and advertising, events, and sponsorships. The role will collaborate with colleagues within PCNA, Porsche AG, Porsche Digital, as well as external partners to research, pilot, and implement smart and future mobility concepts to support Porsche’s strategy to provide sporty and exclusive mobility offers for its customers.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Primary Responsibilities</b><b>
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Works closely with senior management to define the direction of future mobility concepts and activities related to the US market
</li>
<li>Build and implement market growth strategies with support from cross-functional team at headquarters
</li>
<li>Execution of nationwide marketing campaigns in conjunction with key vendors and stakeholders
</li>
<li>Definition and execution of market specific marketing strategies and campaigns
</li>
<li>Responsible to track and report campaign and event performance metrics while using that data to creatively solve problems, find ways to improve
</li>
<li>Definition and upkeep of Marketing Toolbox which provides dealers with the tools necessary for the execution of their campaigns
</li>
<li>Leads in developing strategic partnerships with vendors, clients, and sister brands
</li>
<li>Leads the procurement process for marketing vendors and ensures that all contractual documents are reviewed and executed in a compliant manner
</li>
</ul>
