<h2>Account Executive - Northeast</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Do you have a passion for winning and a consistent track record of success in selling enterprise security and cloud infrastructure solutions?
<br><br>
As an Account Executive, you will maximize sales in the assigned market territory by working directly with customers and partners to sell Instart's products and services. The primary focus is aimed at sales opportunities and accounts which are achieved by driving strategic wins through medium and large organizations. You will increase deal win rates as well as accelerate time-to-close on key opportunities. You have full quota responsibility for their assigned territory.
<br><br>
Responsibilities
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Be the primary driver for revenue within your territory</li>
<li>Build end-user demand by hunting for new accounts in the territory</li>
<li>Do what it takes to help set up and sustain new sales efforts for a startup company</li>
<li>Sell to existing customer base and new prospects</li>
<li>Manage forecasting and account/opportunity detail in Salesforce.com CRM</li>
<li>Be a high-energy, motivated self-starter and great teammate</li>
<li>Craft sales campaigns and participate in developing the sales process while working closely with an extraordinary management team.</li>
</ul>
Qualifications
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>At least ten years of sales experience selling security and/or cloud infrastructure technology</li>
<li>Experience in building/generating a geography-based or vertical business</li>
<li>A strong network in media, SaaS, gaming or eCommerce vertical</li>
<li>Experience with subscription-based business models</li>
<li>Significant track record of quota over-achievement</li>
<li>Knowledge in selling high-end solutions that involve both Line of Business and IT ownership</li>
<li>Ability to be flexible, open-minded and able to wear many hats</li>
</ul>
