<h2>Heavy Equipment Territory Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><b>Rental Territory Manager</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>Looking for a fast-paced environment on a winning team with plenty of room to grow personally and professionally? Look no further. If you’re willing to work as smart and hard as you can while demonstrating a positive attitude, welcome to the family!</div>
<div>
<br>
Founded in 2012, Synergy Equipment draws on decades of construction equipment experience from our team members to provide a level of service that is unmatched in our industry. As one of the fastest growing construction equipment companies in the country, we are continually expanding our business to exceed our customers’ expectations while offering a great workplace for our team members to provide for themselves and their families.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Main Position Responsibilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Successfully manages assigned territory by developing business opportunities at active or future jobsites and offices with existing customers and develop new accounts through effective planning and strong team marketing skills</li>
<li>Uses Turn Key activity enhancement tools. (Salesforce.com loaded nightly with Dodge Construction Data specifically for your territory, Territory management via data mapping and planning tools for jobsites and offices, integrated equipment quoting tool, electronic credit applications)</li>
<li>Embraces technology to ensure most efficient and effective territory management</li>
<li>Researches and stays in tune with local market conditions and developments</li>
<li>Ensures industry leading customer experiences by conducting business with integrity and working as a strong team player</li>
<li>Continuously demonstrates a Strong Work Ethic, Highly Energetic, Motivated, and Organized.</li>
<li>Attends and participates in applicable sales training events, seminars and conferences throughout the year with some overnight travel required</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Required Education, Experience &amp; Qualifications:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree or equivalent work experience</li>
<li>2+ years equipment rental experience preferred, however industry related skill sets, knowledge or similar experience will be considered</li>
<li>Excellent Communication and Presentation Skills to customers and team members</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task, prioritize and manage time efficiently and effectively</li>
<li>Strong Ability to Motivate and Influence others</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Office (Word, Excel and Outlook), using the internet and the ability to learn new software programs</li>
<li>Valid Driver’s License</li>
<li>Must be physically able to work in a jobsite environment for extensive periods to include lifting up to 50lbs, bending, squatting, crouching, and reaching.</li>
</ul>
