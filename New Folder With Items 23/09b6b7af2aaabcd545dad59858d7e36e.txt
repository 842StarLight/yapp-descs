<b>
Description</b><div>
</div>
The Customer Service Representative for Hillrom Respiratory Health is a key contact during the patient transition from hospital to home, and to the Home Care environment. This role assigns devices to patients and is the hub of communication for each patient's needs within Provider and Customer Operations department of Respiratory Health. The Customer Service Representative interacts via email and phone with patients, caregivers, external healthcare teams, and sales; and communicates internally with several internal Hillrom teams to answer questions and resolve a wide variety of inquiries during the transition to home.The Customer Service Representative attempts to resolve product and technical complaints from the patient or caregiver over the phone; ensures that proper insurance coverage and/or payment terms are in place; determines the cause of failure, recommends an appropriate course of action and authorizes shipment or return of warranty related issues for all devices and accessories in the Home Respiratory Health portfolio.<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:
</b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>Establish rapport over the phone quickly, and remain positive and upbeat
</div></li>
<li><div>Responsible for interfacing with customers/patients, account executives, and internal departments, such as clinical, financial, reimbursement, operations, and other patient services representatives
</div></li>
<li><div>Quickly and accurately identify and assess individual customer needs, determining appropriate action, within Hillrom Respiratory Heath guidelines</div></li>
<li><div>
Deliver front-line phone support on products and electronic devices, explaining technical procedures to customers in an easy-to-understand manner, at times summarizing negative trends to Quality and Research &amp; Development engineers.
</div></li>
<li><div>Learn ongoing technical support for Respiratory Health devices and portal connectivity products</div></li>
<li><div>
Properly identify order types for correct billing, collaborating with the internal billing teams</div></li>
<li><div>
Utilize multiple systems, investigation techniques, and problem-solving skills to attempt to first-time resolution on each call with speed and accuracy
</div></li>
<li><div>Employ detail-oriented processes in multiple Hillrom systems to process new accounts, route, track, retrieve, and maintain proper information in correct fields</div></li>
<li><div>
Responsible for shipping-related activities, verification for rental agreements, and returned items</div></li>
<li><div>
Adhering to prescription and insurance requirements in all re-supply orders</div></li>
<li><div>
Responsible for timely and accurately processing of complaints received from patients/caregivers
</div></li>
<li><div>Responsible for after hours phone and laptop, which includes occasional evening and weekend coverage</div></li>
