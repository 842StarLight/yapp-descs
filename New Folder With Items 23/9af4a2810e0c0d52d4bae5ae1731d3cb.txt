<h2>Channel Partner Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<div>The Channel Partner Manager will be responsible for managing all key relationships with our Content Management Partner. S/he will work closely with our Clients to build and deliver successful solutions that leverage these products to drive revenue. This position requires a background in both digital and content management, credibility in front of sales executives and C-level audiences, and experience managing channel relationships.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div></div>
<b>What Will You Do?</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Drive sales and marketing relationships</li>
<li>Drive joint marketing initiatives</li>
<li>Serve as liaison to both our Content Management Partner and our Clients</li>
<li>Work with Partner managers to identify opportunities and jointly target accounts</li>
<li>Coordinate with MRM//McCann account teams to identify opportunities among existing clients</li>
<li>Educate partners on MRM//McCann capabilities and success stories to help stimulate partner based lead referrals</li>
<li>Serve as internal trusted advisor to Partners, ensuring proper internal product management as well as managing access to early release programs and product road maps.</li>
<li>Perform Partner needs assessment to identify training courses required to ramp-up on the platforms and solutions</li>
<li>As necessary, deliver introductory training courses and supplementary materials (e.g., overview presentations and hands-on exercise) to illustrate platform-specific features and functionalities</li>
<li>Work closely with internal trainers and content development teams to improve content and delivery for educational programs</li>
<li>Identify technical best practices for content management platforms and share them with Partners</li>
<li>Conceive and implement development of Partner platform demos, from ideation to proof of concept</li>
<li>Make recommendations on integration strategies, along with the platforms and application infrastructure required to successfully implement and extend Partner platforms</li>
<li>Work with assigned Partner manager to identify, prepare and deliver product messaging in an effort to demonstrate the MRM//McCann value prop position and promote MRM//McCann as a trusted systems integrator</li>
<br>
</ul>
<br>
Required Skills<br>
<div><b>What Skills Do You Need?</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Strong presentation skills</li>
<li>Solutions selling skills</li>
<li>Conversant in enterprise product, solution and technology strategies with the ability to convert current knowledge and skills to Content Management Platform Partners</li>
<li>Ability to communicate effectively across teams with strong verbal, written and interpersonal skills</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div>THIS POSITION CAN BE BASED OUT OF EITHER NY OR BOSTON</div>
<br>
<div></div>
Required Experience<br>
<div><b>We Are Seeking Someone With…</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Significant knowledge of Acquia + Drupal, or other similar content management platforms</li>
<li>Previous experience with content management platforms and digital marketing solutions or web content management solutions</li>
<li>Demonstrated success developing and maintaining client relationships</li>
<li>A high energy level and passion</li>
<li>The ability to work independently</li>
<li>The ability to travel as much as 50%</li>
<li>A Bachelors degree, preferably in the areas of computer science or technical</li>
<li>15+ years related experience</li>
<br>
</ul>
