<h2>Salesforce Business Application Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Abiomed, an innovative medical device company, is focused on recovering hearts and saving lives. We have a single guiding mission: “Patients First.” With corporate headquarters in Danvers, Massachusetts, offices in Aachen &amp; Berlin, Germany and Tokyo, Japan, Abiomed’s 1,300 employees form one of the fastest growing medical technology companies in the world. Abiomed attracts and retains exceptional talent with our collaborative culture, passion for our work, and a strong commitment to employee professional development.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Opportunity Summary:
</b></p>
<p>In this new role, the Business Applications Manager of the Salesforce Application Support team will own the global development and support of the Salesforce CRM system and other applications that support our Commercial and Clinical Support teams. As a Business Applications Manager, this person will manage a small but growing team of internal employees and external consultants to provide superior service to our internal teammates and manage the lifecycle of Salesforce enhancement requests This role will work closely with commercial team leadership to ensure alignment and drive process improvements. This is a <b>critical, highly-visible role </b>within the organization, that will drive our ability to provide the best outcomes to the most patients possible! If you are excited by that challenge and responsibility, we are excited to hear from you.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>As an Business Applications Manager with ABIOMED you will </b>:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Partner with business functions (Commercial team leaders, etc.) to determine priorities and requirements - this is a true partnering role!
</li>
<li>Track and help the business prioritize application enhancement requests
</li>
<li>Manage Salesforce production support and development teams
</li>
<li>Manage relationship with outside partners for Salesforce consulting and development
</li>
<li>Partner with corporate application teams to ensure technology and resource alignment
</li>
<li>Drive project execution and potentially lead projects
</li>
<li>Lead and enhance software development lifecycle process
</li>
<li>Travel to ABIOMED's German &amp; Japanese office locations annually or bi-annually (~10% travel)<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Required Qualifications:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree in Information Systems (or related discipline), or significant directly-related work experience
</li>
<li>Significant (5+ years of) experience with Salesforce application
</li>
<li>Experience successfully leading &amp; managing teams
</li>
<li>Experience defining technical strategy
</li>
<li>Ability to express complex technical concepts in terms understandable to the business
</li>
<li>Experience in business process re-engineering or process improvement desirable<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>To be successful in this role, one must:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Be highly motivated, have a great work ethic, and enjoy working as part of an EXCELLENT team!
</li>
<li>Have strong teamwork skills and the ability to develop effective working relationships
</li>
<li>Be able to communicate effectively with all levels of management, as well as to employees in the business<br>
</li>
</ul>
