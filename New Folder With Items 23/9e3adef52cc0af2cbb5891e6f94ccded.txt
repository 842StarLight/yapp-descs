<h2>Sales Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>SME is seeking a Regional Sales Executive to join their Tooling U-SME team. The Regional Sales Executivewill focus on selling within a given geographical territory generating new opportunitiesthrough prospecting, in-person meeting, WebEx sessions, trade shows, etc. This person will be responsible for generating newbusiness as well as maintaining continuing renewal efforts for a specific setof clients within the region through portfolio, cross selling, and programgrowth. They will be responsible formeeting an annual quota and isexpected to maintain a pipeline of three times the annual goal at alltimes. The job requirements entailcompleting the necessary sales metrics of sales revenue, prospecting, prospectdevelopment, and sales presentations. This role’s success is heavily measuredby the achievement of all sales metrics (sales revenue, customer retention andincremental growth, product mix, prospecting calls and new business/logos) on acontinual basis, driving enough new business and renewal activity to achievethe targeted revenue quota for a 12-month period.</p>
