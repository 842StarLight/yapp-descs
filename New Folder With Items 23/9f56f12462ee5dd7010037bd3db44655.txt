<h2>New Partnerships Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>
<div>Aware3 is seeking a positive, motivated individual to engage with potential customers and close new partnerships. This role will provide software demos self scheduled and provide all necessary follow-up to close new business. Responsibilities include executing daily prospecting tasks, actively managing your sales pipeline, and communicating why prospects will benefit greatly from our industry leading software platform.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
The position is full-time and based in our Kansas City, Missouri office in the Crossroads District. We have a fun and personal work environment with an exceptionally strong team dynamic. Each team member needs to be ready to shift between working independently and collaboratively daily to achieve results.</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Provide online software demos and presentations for marketing qualified leads</li>
<li>
Use these appointments to establish credibility, trust, and clear next steps</li>
<li>
Meet or exceed daily and monthly sales targets</li>
<li>
Follow up with prospects via online meetings, phone calls, and emails to answer questions and close sales</li>
<li>
Own your pipeline and reporting inside Salesforce and other prospecting systems</li>
<div>
<br>
</div>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>

<div><ul>
<li>
High emotional intelligence to read prospects well to uncover their key challenges</li>
<li>
Ability to explain complex concepts simply</li>
<li>
An innate sense of urgency and ability to prioritize effectively</li>
<li>
Readiness to communicate and to take action when faced with obstacles</li>
<li>
Experience working in a fast paced, startup environment</li>
<li>
Passion for achieving aggressive goals</li>
<li>
Familiarity with mobile technology and cutting edge software</li>
<li>
Proven track record of sales execution, ideally 2+ years</li>
<li>
Commitment to continuous personal growth</li>
<li>
Established confidence speaking effectively with a broad range of stakeholders including C suite profiles</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
