<div></div>
<div><div><div>
<p><b>Ambius Line of Business Services Product Manager</b></p>
<div><b>
Purpose of Position</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The Ambius Line of Business Services Product Manager is a business leader focused on delivering unique and compelling services to meet the constantly changing demands of internal and external stakeholders. This position will play a pivotal role in defining premium scenting and green wall program requirements, go-to-market strategy, and tirelessly championing the needs of our customers. This role will require coordination across many internal and external teams in order to achieve success. The person in this role will work with our customers, partners, colleagues, and operational stakeholders to research new product and service opportunities, spearhead the definition, development and deployment of new capabilities, prioritize roadmap features and manage the success and life cycle of new and existing products/services.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
In addition to service development and planning, this position will be responsible for the forecasting, pricing, and P&amp;L optimization of developed products and services for Ambius. The Ambius Line of Business Services Project Manager will own marketing in positioning, promotion, and sales enablement. This role requires working through ambiguity, demonstrating good judgment under pressure and the ability to thrive in a fast-paced, dynamic environment.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Our ideal candidate is a self-starter, conceptualizing and leading complex initiatives from end to end, and has an entrepreneurial spirit. This candidate should be comfortable working both at the strategic level and diving deep into details as needed to ensure high quality execution. This candidate will take ownership and be able to influence others to deliver on goals while working collaboratively with a diverse set of internal teams to achieve success for existing and new products and services that solve real problems for our customers and internal stakeholders</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The ideal candidate must have exceptional problem solving skills, strong business judgment, and demonstrated experience leading cross-functional teams. We are looking for a proven ability to execute both strategically and tactically, and someone who is excited to take on new, ambiguous projects that will be industry defining.</div>
