<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job summary
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Use your planning, problem-solving, and project management experience to make a difference at Red Hat. The Red Hat Products and Technologies Operations (PnT Ops) team is looking for a business operations consultant to join us in Raleigh, NC. In this role, you will be part of a team responsible for guiding process improvements, enabling collaboration, and converting insight into operation improvement for the PnT and other Red Hat’s team. You’ll work with multiple roles and stakeholders in Red Hat on a variety of projects. You will be expected to bring value back into the business through solid leadership and implementation skills. As a Business Strategy Consultant, you will also collaborate and be a partner in broader Red Hat’s efforts to ensure an end-to-end alignment and optimization of go-to-market plans. Help our Investments and Planning team guide fundamental changes in how we plan and conduct our business. You will play an important role in how Red Hat evolves and guides adoption of operational planning processes that have enterprise-wide impacts.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Primary job responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Collaborate with colleagues to examine the current state, identify areas for improvement, and help create plans, communication, and education content required to realize the desired future state</li>
<li>
Provide practical advice to colleagues about implementing long-term, portfolio, operational, and budget planning</li>
<li>
Differentiate strategic investments from other work, so that the business can react with flexibility and speed to market opportunities</li>
<li>
Create alignment between the product teams and the Sales, Marketing, and Services go-to-market plans through the alignment of goals and clear, repeatable planning processes that result in actionable operational plans</li>
<li>
Cascade business team strategy and goals into a well-defined set of R&amp;D investments, incorporating engineering, recruiting, facilities, DevOps, and other needs into a unified operational plan</li>
<li>
Deconstruct complex challenges to identify value propositions, set stakeholders’ expectations, and create an implementation plan that will result in achieving success</li>
<li>
Build trust and productive relationships across Red Hat; reduce difficulties and issues associated with planning</li>
<li>
Increase collective expertise about how to collaborate and align across teams to make our everyday work rewarding and enjoyable</li>
<li>
Provide practical advice about implementing long-term, portfolio, operational, and budget planning</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
