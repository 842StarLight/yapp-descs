<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Position Summary</b></h2>
<p>We are seeking a Shipping/Receiving and Warehouse Associate for 2nd Shift, 2:30pm-10:30pm M-F. in this role, they are responsible for scanning product both into and out of stock, pull/stack finished goods to fulfill orders, create bills of lading, storing, and distributing material, tools, equipment, and products within establishments. This person <b>must have 2-4 years experience in a manufacturing warehouse environment, along with prior knowledge of Inventory Software and scanning products both into and out of inventory</b>. Pay Range dependent upon experience: $11 - $13 to start, top rate is $16.75/hr + shift premium.</p>
<p>
</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Essential Responsibilities</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Reads customer order, work order, shipping order, or requisition to determine items to be moved, gathered, or distributed.</li>
<li>Conveys materials and items from receiving or production areas to storage or to other designated areas.</li>
<li>Sorts and places materials or items on racks, shelves, or in bins according to predetermined sequence such as size, type, style, color, or product code.</li>
<li>Fills requisitions, work orders, or requests for materials, tools, or other stock items and distributes items to production workers or assembly line.</li>
<li>Assembles customer orders from stock and places orders on pallets or shelves, or conveys orders to packing station or shipping department.</li>
<li>Marks materials with identifying information.</li>
<li>Opens bales, crates, and other containers.</li>
<li>Records amounts of materials or items received or distributed.</li>
<li>Weighs or counts items for distribution within plant to ensure conformance to company standards.</li>
<li>Uses computer to enter records.</li>
<li>Drives the Walkie, Rider Pallet, Reach Truck, Sit Down Rider, Side Loader, or Roll Grab to transport stored items from warehouse to plant or to pick up items from several locations for shipment.</li>
<li>Maintains inventory records.</li>
<li>Receive and count stock items, and record data manually or using computer</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Required Qualifications</b></h2>
<ul>
<li><b>2-4 years experience in a warehouse setting.</b></li>
<li>High school diploma or equivalent</li>
<li>Ability to read and comprehend simple instructions, short correspondence, and memos.</li>
<li>Higher level math knowledge (including adding, subtracting, multiplying, and dividing)</li>
<li>Knowledge of Inventory Software</li>
<li>Basic understanding and use of computers.</li>
<li>Physical Demands: stand, bend, reach, crouch, frequently lift/move up to 50 lbs.</li>
<br>
</ul>
