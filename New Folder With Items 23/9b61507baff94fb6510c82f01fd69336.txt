<h2>Corporate Accounting Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Candidates must have a working knowledge of accrual and cash basis accounting practices, as well as policies and procedures for privately-held corporations and real estate partnerships. Ideal candidates will possess substantial prior experience in commercial real estate accounting, with broad exposure to real estate leases, leasing and brokerage commissions, management fees, cash planning, intercompany reimbursements, property taxes, property insurance, construction accounting, lease administration, rent rolls, accounts receivable, accounts payable, pro rata allocations, interest calculations, financial reporting/ analysis and special projects. Candidates must have excellent Microsoft Excel spreadsheet skills, possess experience with modem accounting information systems and demonstrate proficiency with Microsoft Outlook. Prior experience with corporate real estate and property accounting/ property management is preferred. He/she should possess attention to detail with the capability for autonomous work and a demonstrated ability to work with a leadership team. The candidate should be hands-on and willing to handle a variety of clerical duties, be flexible yet thorough and possess the ability to manage multiple tasks efficiently while working productively in a fast-paced, team-oriented environment. This is a position that has potential for career growth and additional responsibility.</p>
<p><b>
Duties</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Prepare monthly cyclical spreadsheet schedules</li>
<li>Prepare advanced reporting and run financials for analysis reporting</li>
<li>Prepare analysis schedules</li>
<li>Prepare, analyze and execute multiple types of intercompany reimbursements</li>
<li>Collaborate with team and prepare allocations for corporate and intercompany recoveries</li>
<li>Track and reconcile expenses to leasing, brokerage and investment sales activity</li>
<li>Assist with corporate accounts receivable data entry</li>
<li>Enter journal entries on corporate entities</li>
<li>Research reconciling items</li>
<li>Assist with monthly corporate payable preparation and data review/verification</li>
<li>Assist with deployment of software system setups-new entities, workflows, chart of accounts entries, bank accounts and other software setups, primarily within accounting and accounts payable systems</li>
<li>Work with accounts payable staff and serve as backup for weekly cyclical AP functions including batch uploads and check printing</li>
<li>Assist with the development of new tools, workflows, reporting methods</li>
<li>Collaborate with Controller, Corporate Accountant, Treasury Administrator and other team members on multiple monthly tasks and special projects as needed</li>
<li>Create, edit and publish written Standard Operating Procedures</li>
<li>Prepare ad-hoc reports and support special projects</li>
<li>Work in a list-driven management system and observe/ enforce separation of duties</li>
<li>Ensure records are maintained in compliance with established policies and procedures</li>
</ul>
