<h2>SVP, Global Services</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Ellucian designs technology that powers the educational mission of colleges and universities around the world. Inspired by that goal, Ellucian employees thrive as part of a high-performing global team committed to delivering a modern, connected, industry-leading experience for today’s students and higher education institutions. More than 2,500 institutions in 50 countries around the world look to Ellucian for the ideas and insights that will move education forward, helping people everywhere discover their futures through learning.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
SVP of Global Services</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
**OPEN TO RELOCATION TO RESTON**</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
SVP of Global Services will be a critical leader at Ellucian and will be responsible for leading our Professional Services Organization through profitable revenue growth and delivery of an exceptional customer experience. The successful candidate will have a highly innovative and transformational approach- not just to the team and company- but as it relates to the broader industry and how top software companies <i>do</i> Services, breaking down regional siloes and implementing bleeding-edge automation, mobile, and delivery solutions.</p>
<p>
Higher education customers are transforming their campuses, leveraging technology and solutions to enable student success. The SVP of Global Services will partner with our customers in enabling their transformation to cloud/SaaS solutions and ensuring successful adoption of these solutions. Our services team is instrumental in consulting with our customers and delivering value-add services that provide the quickest path to success.</p>
<p><b>
Key Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p>
Ownership &amp; accountability for building, maintaining and growing a portfolio of SaaS implementation services</p>
<p>
Provide the vision &amp; direction to implement strategies, processes and technologies to deliver best in class customer experience while enabling profitable revenue growth</p>
<p>
Innovate and modernize the current function by implementing latest automation and mobile technologies to enhance customer experience &amp; realize global efficiency gains
</p>
<p>Standardize the global delivery methodology; promoting and ensuring the adoption of global best practices in all regions</p>
<p>
Break down regional siloes, transforming the greater global team to act as one cohesive unit vs. the separate and self-contained current state</p>
<p>
Provide the Executive Leadership necessary to ensure that we hire, train, and develop our teams, such that they consistently set the highest standard for the experience they give our customers and are personally and professionally fulfilled by their work</p>
<p>
Partner closely with Sales, Marketing, &amp; Product Management to support Ellucian go to market strategies for all SaaS solutions</p>
<p>
Interface with Cloud Services and Software Development teams to drive enablement requirements and specifications into SaaS solutions</p>
<p>
Be central to defining the services innovation needed to ensure that the operation remains, and is seen to remain, at the leading edge for higher education and the greater software industry
</p>
<p>Develop annual capital and expense plans that support the attainment of strategic and financial objectives</p>
<p>
Closely work with the Sales team, Finance team and Regional Services leaders ensuring that financial and capacity planning is accurate</p>
<p>
Analyze and manage capacity and demand to meet the needs of future implementations, and build strategic contract relationships to serve as a bench of resources to staff spikes in demand</p>
<p>
Devise rapid deployment strategies for new products and services on the Ellucian Cloud Platform</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Requirements:</b></p>
<p>
A minimum of 15 years successful experience as executive leader; including experience managing SaaS implementations</p>
<p>
Experienced leading Global teams; rising through the consulting/ PS ranks, and having accountability running a ~400+ person, ~$100M P&amp;L</p>
<p>
Customer Focus: Deep passion for customer experience, coupled with a driving motivation to solve their problems and continually improve their experience
</p>
<p>Experienced in modernizing a Service group by driving the digital adoption of process &amp; service models</p>
<p>
Seasoned executive leader possessing a strong balance of EQ/IQ with expertise in taking new solutions to market using industry best practices and cloud concepts</p>
<p>
Proven ability to develop strong working relationships in a matrix, global environment and establish a high level of credibility across functional lines of business and organizational levels</p>
<p>
Management skills including development of talent, strategic planning, budgeting and creative solutions</p>
<p>
Ability and experience to maintain a detailed and up to date knowledge of advances in Cloud and SaaS technology methodology</p>
<p>
Proven experience enabling delivery readiness for new SaaS solutions and bringing new SaaS service offerings to market</p>
<p>
Results focused with a strong bias toward thoughtful action</p>
<p>
Transformational Driver: not a steady-state or cruise-control type of leader</p>
<p>
Highly organized, self-directed business style, with strong ability to prioritize</p>
<p>
Ability to juggle multiple projects and initiatives simultaneously as well as effectively collaborate across teams and to work in a diverse, fast paced environment</p>
<p>
Demonstration of excellent communication skills, both written and verbal, communicating effectively with customer, executives, leaders, program/initiative owners, stakeholders, and other audiences as well as delivering presentations before large audiences</p>
<p>
Bachelor’s degree with Master’s preferred</p>
