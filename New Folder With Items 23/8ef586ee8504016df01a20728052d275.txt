<br>The Role
<br>--------
<br><br>
LeafLink is seeking a Creative Project Manager to join our fast-growing team. In this role, you will support the marketing team in all day-to-day operations including assigning projects, communicating with other departments, and managing external resources. Success in this role is about creating efficiencies to empower the marketing team to deliver work that meets our high creative standards while working against tight deadlines. The position is highly collaborative and cross-functional, with dependencies on our Sales, Client Experience and Product teams. You need to be a self-starter who is comfortable with change, enjoys a fast pace, and thrives on managing many tasks, deadlines, and resources.
<br><br>
The ideal candidate for this role has built and scaled marketing project management processes in the past, including intake forms, work delegation, prioritization and schedule management. You will be the bridge between our design, content, and digital marketing functions and will be instrumental in bringing campaigns to life. As an early member of a growing team, you should thrive on flexibility and taking on new projects outside of your comfort zone.
<br><br>
-----------------
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
-----------------
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Manage the workflows scopes, resources, timelines and budgets associated with all marketing projects</li>
<li>Traffic creative projects to ensure we are delivering high quality work on time</li>
<li>Implement tools and processes across the marketing organization, including an intake form for external requests</li>
<li>Communicate with third party vendors related to creative projects, including printers and freelancers</li>
<li>Represent the creative team in cross-functional meetings</li>
</ul>
