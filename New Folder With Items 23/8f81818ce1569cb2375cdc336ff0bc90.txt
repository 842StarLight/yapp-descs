<p><b>
Position Overview:</b></p>
<p>
As Global Digital Marketing Manager, you’ll be responsible for driving key business objectives, inclusive of achieving a multi-million dollar sales plan, retaining customers, and growing new customers via digital marketing campaigns. You are a digital channel expert and as such you will plan and execute digital media across Paid Search, Shopping Engines/Product Listing Ads, Social Media (Facebook, Instagram), Affiliates, Display, and SEO as well as optimize an omnichannel product feed to be used across marketing channels.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The core function of this role is to lead the execution and optimization of digital marketing campaigns. Data-influenced strategies are at the crux of your campaign execution, optimization, and technical approach. You will architect campaigns in a way to achieve optimal data points and provide actionable next steps using this data.<br>
<br>
You are naturally curious and as such will rigorously analyze data and metrics, and report performance to the team and management, providing follow-up strategies and actionable recommendations when possible. You will proactively partner with cross-functional teams (<i>including but not limited to: Global Analytics, Buying, Planning, Digital Commerce, Brand &amp; Social Advertising, CRM, Digital Technology/IT, Retail Marketing, Email Marketing, and relevant global counterparts</i>) to uncover trends and obtain data to provide meaningful insights.<br>
<br>
You must have strong analytical ability, be detail-oriented and extremely organized, capable of handling multiple projects at once, able to both take direction and be a decision maker, capable of dealing with ambiguity and rapidly-changing priorities, and comfortable working with and presenting to international teams, leadership and external partners. You will be the day-to-day point person for multiple global media agencies and act as the liaison between agency/client. You have a passion for continuous marketing testing and process improvements.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>This role will support the Global Digital Marketing Director in driving the overall performance digital marketing program. You will manage a Digital Marketing Coordinator.</p>
