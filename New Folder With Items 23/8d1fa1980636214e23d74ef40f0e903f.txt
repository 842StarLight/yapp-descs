<h2>Veterinary Receptionist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Looking for an ENTHUSIASTIC Veterinary Technician to work in a FRIENDLY and CARING environment, where opportunity for GROWTH is here!</p>
<p>The Veterinary Receptionist is the client’s first contact when calling or visiting the practice and as such represents the professional image of the practice to callers and visitors. The Veterinary Receptionist answers telephones, greets clients, prepares patient files and escorts clients and patients to exam rooms, receives and relays client correspondence, updates client financial records. The exceptional Veterinary Receptionist has the ability to diffuse negative client situations and foster client bonding.</p>
<p><b>PERSONAL REQUIREMENTS</b></p>
<p><i><b>The Veterinary Receptionist must be able to: </b></i></p>
<ul>
<li>· Be flexible in attitude and work habits.</li>
<li>· Quickly learn how to pronounce, know the meaning of and spell commonly used veterinary terms.</li>
<li>· Perform basic computer skills, type 30 words per minute.</li>
<li>
<b>Physical Effort</b>: Work requires lifting and carrying records and equipment weighing up to 25 lbs; requires sitting and standing for extended periods of time.</li>
<li>
<b>Working conditions</b>: May be exposed to unpleasant odors, noises and animal feces. May be exposed to bites, scratches and contagious diseases.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>PERFORMANCE EXPECTATIONS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Can answer client’s inquiries about basic animal care questions and routine procedures.</li>
<li>Can educate clients on over-the-counter products such as shampoos and nutraceuticals; has full knowledge of heartworm and external parasite preventives to promote client education and sales.</li>
<li>Can guide client’ to make appropriate decisions regarding optimum pet care.</li>
<li>Can admit patients and handle medical records entries accurately.</li>
<li>Can accurately handle payment transactions.</li>
<li>Can accurately and empathetically communicate estimates/treatment plans to clients.</li>
<li>Can keep the front desk a positive area even in the event there is a client wait time.</li>
<li>Can bond with clients during interactions.</li>
<li>Can work to maintain an effective veterinarian appointment schedule, screening emergencies and prioritizing cases that need to be seen.</li>
<li>Answers telephones and handle calls quickly, efficiently and in a professional and friendly manner using a multi-line system.</li>
<li>Can Enter and update client and patient information into practice computer software.</li>
<li>Has good computer skills and shows accuracy in inputting details.</li>
<li>Receives and relays telephone, fax, and email messages accurately and promptly.</li>
<li>Can display tact and respect with team members even when busy or hectic.</li>
<li>Can maintain positive, cooperative relationships with other employees.</li>
<li>Feel and express a genuine liking for animals and their owners and for working in an animal care field.</li>
<li>Can conduct oneself in a confident and professional manner with clients even when situations are stressful and/or focused on individual tasks.</li>
<li>Can deal intelligibly, pleasantly and efficiently with clients, often doing several things at one time.</li>
<li>Can greet clients with poise and natural effort.</li>
<li>Can outline costs and fees in a positive light while supporting hospital payment policies.</li>
<li>Can turn future clients (phone-shoppers) into clients through relationship building and bonding.</li>
<li>Can easily remember clients and patients names and uses them.</li>
<li>Can handle client correspondence including procedure reminders, thank you cards, welcome cards, sympathy arrangements and cards, and A/R statements.</li>
<li>Can maintain a professional/welcoming appearance of the front office through cleaning and organization.</li>
<li>Can offer proper upkeep for and troubleshoot office equipment including pc’s, telephones, copiers, and fax machines.</li>
</ul>
<p>At Forever Vets Animal Hospital, we believe in giving the best service to our community in whatever way we can serve. As a Veterinary Receptionist, you are expected to follow the hospital’s mission in providing the best customer service possible to all our clients while performing all previously-stated duties.</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul>
<li>Veterinary Receptionist: 1 year (Preferred)</li>
<li>Customer Service: 1 year (Required)</li>
</ul>
