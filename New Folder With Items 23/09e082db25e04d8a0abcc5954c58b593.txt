<h2>Customer Success Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Customers are the lifeblood of SearchStax and we are looking for a results-driven, relationship-oriented, strong communicator &amp; master juggler Customer Success Manager to engage, onboard, retain, and enable our customers to fully utilize the SearchStax platform. The Customer Success Manager will be responsible for designing, implementing, and scaling the entire post-sales experience for our customers.</p>
<p>You will be the primary advocate for each customer, guiding them along a path to success and engaging resources across SearchStax to accelerate the adoption &amp; expansion of SearchStax within each customer. The ideal candidate will be as passionate about SearchStax products as they are about providing an exceptional experience for every customer.</p>
<p>Please fill out the application here: https://searchstax.bamboohr.com/jobs/view.php?id=33</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul><li>Own the ultimate success of SearchStax customers, including customer onboarding, project success, retention, and renewal. You ensure that customers derive maximum value from their investments in SearchStax and fully leverage their subscriptions and services on an ongoing basis.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Identify and develop new opportunities for expansion across the customer’s business and ensure subscription growth and increased solution footprint.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Drive seamless onboarding processes and work cross-functionally with our support and technical solutions team to proactively manage each customer’s successful deployment.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Develop and implement tailored programs that provide continued value to the customer, execute product demos for their teams, ensure renewals and drive long-term account growth.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Participate in SearchStax Customer Support channels answering to Non-Technical requests and facilitating communication &amp; action to achieve high customer satisfaction.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Proactively manage each customer’s business needs and environments – actively seek opportunities to up-sell, cross-sell and generate referrals</li></ul>
<ul><li>Drive and execute customer renewals</li></ul>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<ul><li>3+ years Customer Success or Account Management</li></ul>
<ul><li>Passion for delivering customer delight with the demonstrated ability to drive execution</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experience leading client relationships and building relationships in software as a service, Web Content Management or marketing environments</li></ul>
<ul><li>Cross-functional experience working across teams like Engineering, sales, product, and marketing</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong analytical and problem-solving skills combined with the ability to provide a quick resolution to problems.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Highly organized project manager with acute attention to detail.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Empathy and a unique ability to understand customer needs.</li></ul>
<ul><li>BA/BS degree</li></ul>
<p><b>Nice to have</b></p>
