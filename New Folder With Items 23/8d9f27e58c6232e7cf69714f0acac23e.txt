<h2>Manager Business Development Strategy Planning</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>What you’ll be doing...</b>
</p>
<p>The Sales and Business Development position plays a meaningful role in building long-term business relationships within assigned enterprise accounts. This is a high-impact role working closely with the Enterprise Innovation and 5G Solutions team, Sales, other internal teams, and customers to identify accelerate innovation and use cases related to 5G.</p>
<p>
You are a highly effective communicator and listen with both your eyes and ears and people come to you for advice and counsel. You value diverse perspectives and seek out different points of view when trying to solve a complex problem. You’re not afraid to speak your mind and know it’s better to call attention to something before it gets out of hand. You have a sense of urgency and deliver on-time, on-budget results. You keep the big picture in focus as you manage the details on the way to the finish line.</p>
<p>
This position is responsible for driving a portfolio of activities to enhance the success of customers who have implemented 5G labs at their locations, called Sister Labs. You will help define and design the post implementation support required, programmatic activities that Verizon will offer and recommend continuous improvement for Sister Labs. You will also be responsible to execute the activities at multiple assigned customer accounts and seek to build deep, consultative technical relationships with your customers, effectively becoming a trusted adviser. As the primary Verizon contact for a Sister lab, you are ideally positioned to understand the customer's high-level objectives, know the focus areas of innovation and match these to Verizon strategy for 5G.</p>
<ul>
<li>
Understand and know the customer use cases under development.</li>
<li>
Recognize when there is synergy between the customers’ focus and what Verizon product strategy.</li>
<li>
Liaison with customer on 5G use cases, development and testing of applications or solutions.</li>
<li>
Keep customer informed on new advancements and devices and consult on what may be applicable to them.</li>
<li>
Point of contact for customer to connect with applicable Verizon team.</li>
<li>
Manage customer expectations.</li>
<li>
Interface with Technology Development team on applicable use cases of interest to Verizon.</li>
<li>
Report updates to the team and leadership.</li>
<li>
Interface with Verizon marketing and communication team to publish success stories.</li>
<li>
Help promote the customer success stories.</li>
<li>
Travel to customer sites, conferences, and other related events as required.</li>
<li>
Obtain feedback from lab customers and apply to program for continuous improvement.</li>
</ul>

</p>

<li>

<li>
Willingness to travel.</li>

Even better if you have:</p>
<ul>

<li>
Ability to deliver results in a fast-paced environment.</li>
<li>
Eight or more years of relevant experience in the tech industry with experience in pre-sales, customer-facing consulting or other relevant technical experience.</li>
<li>
Experience and knowledge about Verizon 4G network, and familiarity with concepts of 5G technology.</li>
<li>
Ability to quickly learn, understand, and work with new emerging technologies, methodologies and solutions related to 5G.</li>

Experience or knowledge of advanced technologies related with for instance Artificial Intelligence (AI), Machine Learning (ML), Augmented Reality / Virtual Reality, DevOps, etc.</li>
