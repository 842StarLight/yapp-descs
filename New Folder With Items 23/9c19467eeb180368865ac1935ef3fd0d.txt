<h2>Office Manager/Dispatcher</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Job Summary:</b><p>
The primary focus of this position is to ensure the Mobile Mini Units are successfully delivered and picked-up from customer sites, ensuring the drivers are following all safety and DOT regulations. This role will supervise the team of drivers and assign their daily duties.</p>
<b>
Principal Responsibilities:</b><ul>
<li>
Promotes safety first for all areas of dispatch. Ensures drivers are following all safety policies and procedures and will conduct safety meetings</li>
<li>
Supervises team of branch and operations yard Drivers, which includes:<ul>
<li>
Reviews next day/week deliveries and pick-ups and develops a dispatch plan</li>
<li>
Enters all assignments into database (Descartes)</li>
<li>
Notifies drivers of daily assignments</li>
<li>
Tracks Drivers out on delivery/pick-up</li>
<li>
Assists Drivers in troubleshooting when necessary</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Sets daily and weekly priories for the team</li>
<li>
Conducts performance discussions and trainings</li>
<li>
Ensures Drivers have collected all appropriate documentation and signatures related to delivery/pick-up of units and offices and will work with the Driver and Customer if the unit/office is damaged</li>
<li>
Communicates with customers to assist in scheduling daily/weekly activities<ul>
<li>
Informs customer of delivery/pick-up times</li>
<li>
Answers customer questions about delivery/pick-up</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Maintains daily log files and preventative maintenance files and records the information into the appropriate systems. Ensures all daily/weekly/monthly paperwork is completed, recorded and filed</li>
<li>
Maintains branch’s budgeted trucking profit margin</li>
<li>
Coordinates with outside vendors regarding trucking and deliveries. This will include outside hauling services, as needed, and assisting Drivers with obtaining required annual, monthly and daily city/state transportation permits</li>
<li>
Works with branch and corporate sales to determine driver schedule availability, and when available, can schedule deliveries/pick-ups outside of current dispatch schedule</li>
<li>
Manages, assigns and monitors office services for the branch, such as, time keeping/payroll, accounts payable/receivable, purchase orders, filing, and reception</li>
<li>
Defines and maintains office/administrative procedures. May manage a team of Branch Administrative employees to assist with the administrative duties, if not, will be main point of contact for Branch administrative duties and responsibilities</li>
<li>
Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<b>
Equipment Used and Responsibility:</b><ul>
<li>
Computer, telephone, radio and electronic office equipment</li>
<li>
MS Office Suite</li>
<li>
GPS and tracking software</li>
<li>
Salesforce</li>
<li>
SAP</li>
<li>
Decartes</li>
</ul>
<b>
Supervisory Responsibility:</b>
<ul><li>Yes – Drivers and in some branches Assistant Dispatcher</li></ul>
<b>
Education and Experience:</b><ul>
<li>
High School Diploma or equivalent, some college preferred</li>
<li>
2-4 years’ experience with dispatching preferred</li>
<li>
Familiar with standard concepts of scheduling and dispatching</li>
<li>
2-3 years of Customer Service Experience</li>
<li>
Ability to multi-task effectively</li>
<li>
Knowledge of DOT and state specific safety regulations</li>
<li>
Strong attention to detail and ability to problem solve</li>
<li>
Communicate clearly: verbal and written</li>
<li>
Valid Driver’s License</li>
</ul>
