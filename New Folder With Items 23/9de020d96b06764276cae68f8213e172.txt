<h2>Product Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Job Description
<br><br>
BCV is looking for an experienced Product Manager who is passionate about building software products that our employees and customers love. You will join a dynamic and fast-paced environment and work with cross-functional teams and clients to design, build and roll-out products that deliver the company's vision and strategy.
<br><br>
Responsibilities
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Gain a deep understanding of our internal and external customer experience, identify and fill product gaps and generate new ideas that grow market share, improve customer experience and drive growth</li>
<li>Create buy-in for the product vision both internally and with key external partners</li>
<li>Develop product pricing and positioning strategies</li>
<li>Translate product strategy into detailed requirements and further into commercially deployable products</li>
<li>Develop product roadmaps and production timetables, and time-integrated plans for product introduction</li>
<li>Scope and prioritize activities based on business and customer impact</li>
<li>Work closely with software engineering team to deliver quick time-to-market releases</li>
<li>Drive product launches including working with our public relations team, executives, and other management team members</li>
<li>Develop and track key performance metrics pertaining to a SaaS product based model</li>
<li>Act as a product evangelist to build awareness and understanding</li>
<li>Represent the company by visiting customers to solicit feedback on company products and services</li>
<li>Assess market competition by comparing the company's product to competitors' products</li>
<li>Obtain product market share by working with sales director to develop product sales strategies</li>
<li>Provide information for management by preparing short-term and long-term product sales forecasts and special reports and analyses; answering questions and requests</li>
<li>Contribute to team effort by accomplishing related results as needed</li>
</ul>
Qualifications
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Proven work experience in software product management</li>
<li>Proven track record of managing all aspects of a successful SaaS-based product throughout its lifecycle</li>
<li>Proven ability to develop product and marketing strategies and effectively communicate recommendations to executive management</li>
<li>Ability to understand customer requirements, pricing, new product launch, roadmap planning, competitive analysis, and sales planning</li>
<li>Solid technical background with understanding and/or hands-on experience in software development, cloud, and web technologies</li>
<li>Strong problem solving skills and willingness to roll up one's sleeves to get the job</li>
<li>Skilled at working effectively with cross functional teams including executive management</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>MS/BS degree in Computer Science, Engineering or equivalent preferred</li>
</ul>
</div>
