<div><b>Description:</b></div>
<div>The Escrow Operations Manager manages and coordinates operation/business strategies and activities related to escrow operations.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>
Responsibilities:</b></div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for an effective and efficient operation of the Escrow Department.
 </li>
<li>Coordinate office workflow systems, incorporating use of data processing, including all phases of the escrow file, abiding by all laws, rules and regulations which apply to the business of escrow under the umbrella of title insurance.
</li>
<li>Hire, train and develop staff as it relates to escrow procedures, practices and policies.
</li>
<li>Interpret escrow policies and assist in the complicated transactions.
 </li>
<li>Implement efficient process flow for the department, as needed.
</li>
<li>Conduct staff meetings on a regular basis.
</li>
<li>Confer with subordinate personnel to discuss operational problems or explain procedural changes or practices within the Department/Branch offices.
</li>
<li>Prepare work schedules and assign duties to personnel to ensure efficient operations of the Department/Branch offices.
</li>
<li>In the absence of an Escrow Officer/Processor, be available to assist with the daily functions of the unit.
</li>
<li>Audit escrow files on a monthly basis.
</li>
<li>Work in conjunction with the Title Department in resolving title issues.
</li>
<li>Help generate new business and maintain good public relations with existing customers. This may include attending business luncheons and seminars for business development purposes.
</li>
<li>When necessary, work closely with sales to help develop new business, maintain existing customers and work together to solve problems that may arise on escrow file.
</li>
<li>Work with Division President to achieve profitability of the Department.
</li>
</ul>
