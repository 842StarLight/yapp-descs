<p><b>
The Team You'll Impact</b></p>
<p>
Our Business Development team is lean and efficient, working closely to start the sales process with prospective clients and ultimately set our sales team up for success. The Sales and Marketing leadership team emphasizes opportunities for growth and professional development inside and outside of the department, as well as additional recognition and rewards for exceptional contributions.</p>
<p><b>
The Work You’ll Do</b></p>
<p>
Our Business Development Representatives are an integral part of the Marketing and Sales team whose responsibilities include inbound/outbound executive demand creation to companies conducting business in the Tax Information Reporting market, qualifying potential customers, and increasing awareness of Sovos' platform and services.</p>
<ul>
<li>Qualify inbound leads, provide high-level overview of products and services, update Salesforce.com, fulfill literature requests, and transition lead to Sales through identification of BANT (Budget, Authority, Need &amp; Timeframe)</li>
<li>Prospect into named, targeted accounts with the goal of generating a qualified opportunity to be handed off to Sales</li>
<li>Execute around 50 outbound activities a day - divided between inbound follow-up &amp; outbound prospecting to C-level executives</li>
<li>Support integrated marketing activities and promotions with prospect and customer base primarily through outbound calling campaigns</li>
<li>Maintain accuracy and integrity of information in database</li>
<li>Keep abreast of corporate objectives and product updates</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What We Need From You</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree</li>
<li>Great team player – identify and execute ideas that will make the Business Development and Sales teams better</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>A desire to learn and grow within an organization</li>
<li>Minimal travel is required</li>
</ul>
