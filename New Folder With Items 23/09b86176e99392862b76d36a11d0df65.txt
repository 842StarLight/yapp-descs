<p><b>
Your Career</b></p>
<p>
As Sales Operations Help Desk Supervisor, your role is to oversee the entire Help Desk staff and ensure that end-users are receiving the appropriate assistance. This includes the responsibility of managing all procedures related to the identification, prioritization, and resolution of end-user help requests, including the monitoring, tracking, and coordination of Help Desk functions. You also will contribute to problem resolution by giving in-person, hands-on support to both staff and end-users. The Help Desk will include services for several functions, including Channel Operations, Analytics, Compensation, Deals Desk, Data Governance, and any other Sales Operations Help Desk services.</p>
<p><b>
Your Impact</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Establish and enforce Help Desk service-level agreements (SLAs) in consultation with end-users to establish problem resolution expectations and time frames</li>
<li>
Analyze performance of Help Desk activities and documented resolutions, identify problem areas, and devise and deliver solutions to enhance service quality and prevent future problems</li>
<li>
Manage process for incoming requests to Help Desk via email and Service Now ticketing system to ensure courteous, timely, and effective resolution</li>
<li>
Design and enforce request handling and escalation policies and procedures</li>
<li>
Monitor and validate resolutions to ensure problems have been adequately addressed</li>
<li>
Track and analyze trends in Help Desk requests and generate statistical reports</li>
<li>
Oversee development and dissemination of help sheets, usage guides, and FAQ lists for end-users</li>
<li>
Oversee creation and implementation of Help Desk staff training procedures and policies</li>
<li>
Work closely with sales stakeholders to ensure timely and appropriate action for requests</li>
<li>
Respond to staff inquiries and concerns regarding operational processes and procedures</li>
<li>
Ensure staff is accountable for achieving performance objectives and establish SLAs for managing future requests</li>
<li>
Serve as escalation and resolution contact for client concerns and executive-related inquiries</li>
<li>
Coordinate daily staff schedules and coverage, and cross-train staff to cover areas of need</li>
<li>
Conduct regular team meetings and provide performance feedback</li>
<li>
Run daily reports of work orders in various ticketing systems</li>
<li>
Raise the bar for the team and develop staff to add value and support</li>
</ul>
