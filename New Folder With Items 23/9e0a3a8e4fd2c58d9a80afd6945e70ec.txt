Description</div>
<div>
<div>
Prepress Application Engineer- Dayton, OH Candidates must reside within the US.<br>
Main role / Job Objective:<br>
</div>
<div>
The Prepress Application Engineer trains system operators on the Esko Packaging software products, in different delivery methods: classroom (currently approx. 20%), on site (currently approx. 80%) and e-training. Must be able to travel up to 80%. This is an entry level role.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
Main responsibilities and key activities:<br>
<ul>
<li>Training the customer operators to make efficient use of their Esko Packaging solution, either in one of our training centers or at the customer’s site (in their production environment).</li>
<li>Install and configure Esko Packaging software solutions at customer sites.</li>
<li>Help the customer to integrate the system in their existing workflow. This includes:</li>
<li>System setup &amp; configuration</li>
<li>Defining and setting up the necessary production workflows</li>
<li>Report and follow-up on activities.</li>
<li>Keep your own knowledge up to date by attending courses, self-study and in-depth preparation.</li>
<li>Make software Preventive Maintenance visits (if applicable).</li>
<li>Keep track of reporting, such as training and service reports and expenses.</li>
<li>Provide input to define the content of training programs based on customer feedback.</li>
<li>Provide suggestions to help improve the quality of training material and training courses, certification and e-learning.</li>
</ul>

Qualifications</div>
<div>
<div>
As an ideal candidate you will possess:<br>
<ul>
<li>Degree in Packaging or similar experience in the industry</li>
<li>Good understanding of Packaging design industry.</li>
<li>Must be able to travel up to 80%</li>
<li>Knowledge of the Esko Packaging software solutions is preferred (Automation Engine, Imaging Engine, Deskpack tools, Esko Editors and WebCenter)</li>
<li>Familiar with the Esko hardware products.</li>
<li>Basic understanding of graphical processes and software applications</li>
<li>Basic knowledge of relevant operating systems.</li>
<li>Strong communication, listening, teaching and facilitation skills.</li>
</ul>
