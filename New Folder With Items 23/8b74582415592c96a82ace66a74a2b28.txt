<h2>Account Supervisor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>
<div>FCBCURE is looking for an Account Supervisor with promotional experience in therapeutic categories such as:<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>Women’s Health, Oncology, Epilepsy, Medical Devices, Urology, Gastro, and rare disease.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>These are fun, integrated accounts with print, digital, and broadcast. These are also just a part of what you will be working on. As an Account Supervisor your will be responsible for working with clients and account executives to develop long-term advertising/marketing plans and strategies. You'll have overall responsibility for agency output while providing perspective leadership in the development of the highest quality client service.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Client Management/Relationship – day-to-day contact for client, build trust and rapport, work directly with client with or without supervision
</li>
<li>Managing junior account team members – supervise and provide mentorship for AAE, AE and/or SAEs on business
</li>
<li>Internal hub for particular piece of business – go-to person for cross-functional team(s), collaborate effectively with Strategic Planning, Scientific Services, Creative, etc, foster positive internal relationships and ensure everyone understand deliverables and clients’ needs<br>
</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>RESPONSIBILITIES:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Provide strategic recommendations and manage all agency resources to ensure the client’s marketing needs are met</li>
<li>Conceive, develop and execute marketing communications and promotions on client’s behalf, which match their marketing objectives and strategies</li>
<li>Recommend sound business initiatives pertaining to the product and markets that enhances sales and marketing objectives while enforcing the agency’s image as a vital resource to the product team</li>
<li>Daily communication with client to establish and maintain positive working relationships</li>
<li>Maintain strong relationships with all levels of client management</li>
<li>Manage all jobs relating to account</li>
<li>Act as team leader to ensure integration of agency disciplines (scientific, media, art, copy, accounting, production, creative, library services)</li>
<li>Financial management of accounts (determine annual fee, prepare contracts, oversee job hours and expenses, budget for future estimates)</li>
<li>Manage, motivate, and develop account team<br>
</li>
</ul>
<br>
