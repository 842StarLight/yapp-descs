<h2>Salesman for Combine Heat and Power Solutions</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Position: <b>Salesman for Combine Heat and Power Solutions </b></p>
<p>Reports to: <b>Sales Manager</b></p>
<p>Department: <b>Sales</b></p>
<p><b>Job Summary</b>:</p>
<p>An industry leader in Combined Heat and Power solutions is looking to add a top tier sales associate. This sales position will be tasked with overseeing sales to new and existing customers as well as reengaging former customers. As our product offering is diverse – from CHP units, engine control equipment, switchgear and heat recovery systems, the ideal candidate will have a solid technical (field) or electrical engineering background and a strong desire to create solutions for the customer’s needs. Applicants should be familiar with the competitive product offerings and have knowledge of the CHP industry in the US and Canada.</p>
<p>The primary functions of this position include; effectively communicate product value proposition to customers and bring saleable opportunities to the company, providing accurate pre-sale application data and technical support, manage daily phone and email activity while planning future sales calls, develop customer sales strategies, assist other sales team members and marketing efforts as needed. This position’s success will be based upon the ability to close sales with enough profit margin while meeting customer expectations</p>
<p><b>Essential Functions: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for generating sales of CHP and related equipment</li>
<li>Develop and maintain effective working relationships with our existing customers and vendors, as well as developing new customers and opportunities in the CHP market.</li>
<li>Support and maintain the Company sales strategy while maintaining sales department budget goals.</li>
<li>Provide prospects with technically accurate information through project or product quotations in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Conduct customer presentations/host customers at our facility and at customer sites.</li>
<li>Prepare and present bids and quotations to customers developed in conjunction with our inside sales team</li>
<li>Weekly communication at team sales meetings and use of Salesforce software.</li>
<li>Potential travel 25%</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Other Functions</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Communicate and coordinate activities with the company’s management team.</li>
<li>Keep the Sales Manager and Accounting Department aware of any changes in customer credit worthiness</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Job Qualifications: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>A neat, professional appearance and demeanor is mandatory.</li>
<li>Electrical Engineering degree or equivalent work experience in the electrical control and power distribution field.</li>
<li>Proficiency in all Office Suite products with strong proficiency in Excel</li>
<li>This position will require general computer savvy.</li>
<li>Strong written and verbal skills.</li>
<li>Demonstrate knowledge of integrated electrical systems from controls through power distribution equipment.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Experience: </b></p>
<p>A minimum of two to five years of industry related sales experience.</p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrate knowledge of Combined Heat and Power systems, controls, power distribution equipment and switchgear.</li>
<li>A basic understanding of UL, ASME, CSE and other related industry standards.</li>
</ul>
