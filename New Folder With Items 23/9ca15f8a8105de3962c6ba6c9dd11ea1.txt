<b>COMPANY BACKGROUND:</b><br>
Morgan Corporation is the largest manufacturer of medium duty freight and refrigerated van and truck bodies in North America. Founded in 1952, as a small welding shop in Morgantown, Pennsylvania, on the site where Conestoga wagons were manufactured through the late 1800s, we have experienced tremendous growth and continue to look for additional expansion opportunities around the world. With a total of 14 manufacturing and five service facilities strategically located across the U.S. and Canada, Morgan is able to provide the quality, service, and prompt delivery our customers expect.

<b>POSITION SUMMARY:</b><br>
The Telesales - Outbound Sales Associate will generate sales for Morgan’s Bailment Chassis Program by making outbound sales calls based upon company-supplied leads or answering customer inquiries in a fast-paced environment. Responsibilities include influencing existing or potential customers to buy Morgan products. The goal is to promote business growth by expanding the Morgan Corporation’s customer base.
<br><br>
Our ideal candidate is friendly, persuasive and enjoys customer contact via phone. The Telesales - Outbound Sales Associate must be able to understand the customer’s requirements in a short time and present solutions that meet their needs. In addition, patience and understanding to deal with customer feedback and complaints are key to success in this role.
<br><br>
<b>KEY DUTIES &amp; RESPONSIBILITIES:</b><br>
1. Make outbound calls (and answer incoming calls) to existing customers and cold call prospective customers to promote sales of Morgan truck bodies and Morgan’s Bailment program.
<br>2. Using scripts to provide information about product’s features, prices etc. and present their benefits as it relates to Morgan’s Bailment Program and truck bodies.
<br>3. Ask pertinent questions to understand the customer’s requirements
<br>4. Persuade the customer to buy by demonstrating how Morgan products meet their needs
<br>5. Record the customer’s information accurately in a computer system, using our quoting tool and web site.
<br>6. Deal with complaints or doubts to safeguard the company’s reputation
<br>7. Go the “extra mile” to meet sales quota and facilitate future sales
<br>8. Keep records of calls and sales and record useful information
<br>9. Other duties, as assigned.
<br><br>
<b>EXPERTISE REQUIRED:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or GED; College degree preferred.</li>
<li>Proven outbound calling experience in an inside sales or customer service role.</li>
<li>Proven track record of successfully meeting sales quotas preferably over the phone.</li>
<li>Persistent and results oriented and skilled in negotiation and dealing with complaints. Patient and able to handle customer rejection.</li>
<li>Excellent phone etiquette. Well organized with solid communication and presentation skills.</li>
<li>Working knowledge of computer programs such as Microsoft Office and CRM software.</li>
<li>Proficient in English.</li>
</ul>
<b>PHYSICAL REQUIREMENTS:</b><br>
Working in an office environment in a seated position a minimum of eight hours per day is required. Individuals may need to sit or stand as needed. May require walking primarily on a level surface for periodic periods throughout the day. Reaching above shoulder heights, below the waist or lifting as required to file documents or store materials throughout the workday. Proper lifting techniques required. May include lifting up to 25 pounds for files, computer printouts on occasion.
<br><br>
The performance of this position may occasionally require exposure to the manufacturing areas where under certain areas require the use of personal protective equipment such as safety glasses with side shields and mandatory hearing protection. Primary environment: ambient room temperatures, lighting and traditional office equipment as found in a typical office environment.
