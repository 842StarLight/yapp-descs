The Proposal Analyst takes responsibility for the entire proposal process from RFP receipt to high-quality proposal creation and delivery in a high-volume environment. The Proposal Analyst works closely with Business Development, Program Management, Channel, Operations, Legal, Finance, Content Development, Measurement, Test Publishing, Global Business Solutions, and other teams as required to develop and deliver high-quality, compelling proposals. The Proposal Analyst is also responsible for facilitating bid strategy sessions and assisting the Director, Global Proposals with research and process improvement projects.
