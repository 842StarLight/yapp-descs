<h2>General Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>General Manager</b></p>
<p>Here’s your opportunity to join a growing organization that is revolutionizing the craft brewing industry!</p>
<p>Sixpoint Brewing Company seeks a <b>General Manager</b> at our soon-to-be innovation brewery and tasting room in the heart of downtown Brooklyn! This facility will serve as Sixpoint’s headquarters and a location where consumers can experience the Sixpoint brand as well as sample and celebrate Sixpoint beers.</p>
<p>Sixpoint Brewing Company was founded in 2004 and is owned by Artisanal Brewing Ventures, a top 10 regional craft brewer in the United States. Based in Brooklyn, the brewery is one of the New York-area’s longest-running and most well-regarded craft breweries and produces more than 50,000 barrels of craft beer a year, with four core brands: Sweet Action, Bengali, The Crisp, and Resin, as well as limited-release and seasonal products. Sixpoint has been named best craft brewer in New York on multiple occasions.</p>
<p>We are looking for world class candidates who share in our vision towards becoming a world class organization.</p>
<p><b>Position Summary</b></p>
<p>We’re hiring for a strong leader who has had experience managing a team of service and kitchen staff. To be successful in this role, you will be focused on delivering an exceptional guest experience and leading your team to achieve a high standard of service each and every day.</p>
<p><b>Key Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Create a positive and productive working environment by taking an active role in managing and developing staff and making employment decisions for your team</li>
<li>Manage and strive to achieve all company objectives in sales, costs, employee retention, guest service and satisfaction, food quality, cleanliness and sanitation</li>
<li>Continually strive to develop your staff in all areas of managerial and professional development, and take an active role in the performance review process</li>
<li>Develop, plan and carry out restaurant marketing, advertising and promotional activities and campaigns</li>
<li>Be an active part of Management meetings; share successes and improvement areas with leadership</li>
<li>Control cash and other receipts by adhering and overseeing all cash handling and reconciliation procedures in accordance with restaurant policies and procedures</li>
<li>Oversee and abide by all restaurant procedures including submitting required paperwork, effectively completing reports, and understanding how best to use those reports to increase revenues and create outstanding guest experiences</li>
<li>Schedule labor and ensure all positions are adequately staffed. Make recommendations as necessary based on any business or seasonal changes to effectively meet labor cost objectives</li>
<li>Fully understand and comply with all federal, state, county and municipal regulations that pertain to the health, safety and labor requirements of the restaurant, employees and guests</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>A minimum of 3+ years of front-of-the-house operations and/or Assistant Management positions in the food and beverage industry is required</li>
<li>You must be at least 21 years of age</li>
<li>Knowledge of craft beer and/or serious interest in understanding craft beer styles and the industry</li>
<li>Some experience or education in food service and understanding the restaurant’s predominant language</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Skills/Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Possess technology skills including MS Office, Aloha (preferred), and other industry related software</li>
<li>Possess excellent basic math skills</li>
<li>A basic understanding of financial statements is preferred</li>
<li>Be able to work in a standing position for long periods of time (up to 5 hours)</li>
<li>Be able to reach, bend, stoop and frequently lift up to 50 pounds</li>
<li>Must have the stamina to work 50-60 hours per week</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Perks</b></p>
<ul>
<li>A comprehensive insurance plan including medical, dental, vision, life, and disability insurance</li>
<li>401(k) plan with company match offered after a waiting period</li>
<li>Competitive pay</li>
<li>Employee discount programs</li>
</ul>
