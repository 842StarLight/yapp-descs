Part of Worldpay's success and rapid growth is a result of our teams functioning like well-oiled machines that strive for continual improvement. The Implementations Consultant understands the underlying mechanisms between customer need and technical implementation of our products and services. He/she is able to translate those needs to our development teams efficiently in order to achieve a seamless and intuitive experience for the client. They're also a knowledgebase, equipped with the skills to assist customers through issues ranging from installation, operation, maintenance to product application or compatibility matters.

The Day-to-Day
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Works closely with customers to identify user's business requirements.</li>
<li>Interprets business needs and translates them into system requirements.</li>
<li>Writes business specifications and forwards to technical staff for system development.</li>
<li>Acts as the conduit between a user group and an IT development team.</li>
<li>Responds to customer product inquiries via telephone or in written internet-based email or chat sessions.</li>
<li>Resolves customer concerns raised during installation, operation, maintenance or product application or compatibility matters.</li>
<li>Troubleshoots problems with malfunctioning electro/mechanical equipment or software applications and recommends corrective action.</li>
<li>Documents customer information and recurring technical issues to support product quality programs and product development.</li>
<li>Provides technical support to field engineers, technicians and product support personnel who are diagnosing, troubleshooting, repairing and debugging complex electro/mechanical equipment, computer systems, complex software or networked and/or wireless systems.</li>
<li>Reports design, reliability and maintenance problems or bugs to design/software engineering.</li>
<li>Acts independently or in collaboration with more senior Implementations resources to manage and complete moderate-to-complex implementations.</li>
<li>Developmental ownership of all assigned implementations and partner engagements (partial to limited oversight required).</li>
<li>Developing payment processing industry expertise; internal and external to Worldpay.</li>
<li>Developmental understanding of the complete functioning of the applicable Worldpay platform and payment processing as a whole.</li>
<li>Works on implementations and projects up to moderately complex scope.</li>
<li>Works as a Project Manager on all assigned implementations (partial to limited oversight).</li>
<li>Author documentation of all implementation-specific details for new and existing customers.</li>
<li>Works both independently and collaboratively within the Implementations department and Worldpay as a whole.</li>
</ul>
<b>Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Typically requires a Bachelor's degree and 1-2 years of professional experience; or advanced degree without experience; or equivalent work experience.</li>
</ul>
