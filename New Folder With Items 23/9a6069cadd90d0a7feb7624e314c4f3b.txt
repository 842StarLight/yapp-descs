<h2>General Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Position:</b> General Manager
<br>SUMMARY OF RESPONSIBILITIES
<br><br>
Responsible for all functions of the restaurants in his/her control, including but not limited to maintaining and increasing sales and profitability; recruiting, screening, hiring, training, and developing managers and key employees; sales forecasting and marketing; conducting risk/safety, and RER inspections; managing inventory and the facility; store upkeep and maintenance.
<br>ESSENTIAL RESPONSIBILITIES
<br><br>
1. Works closely with Director of Operations and District Manager in developing and implementing company strategy.
<br>2. Helps monitor store budgets and makes recommendations to improve and enhance the companyâ€™s image and sales.
<br>3. Monitors, evaluates, and enforces cleanliness standards per RER guidelines.
<br>4. Monitors maintenance requirements of stores, including care and upkeep of equipment, building and grounds.
<br>5. Assist in training, turnover, and retention of employees.
<br>6. Evaluates and monitors customer service and satisfaction. Takes corrective action as needed.
<br>7. Monitors quality of products and store system operations.
<br>8. Performs miscellaneous job-related duties as assigned.
<br>9. Be available some holidays, weekends, nights, and in crisis or emergency situations.
<br>10. Works a minimum of 48 hours per week to include 2 close shifts, 2 open shifts and 1 mid shift.
<br>11. Ensures quality experiences for all guests.
<br>12. Supervises and motivates employees.
<br>13. Provides leadership by keeping things fun and encouraging enthusiasm and a positive mental attitude and commitment toward Company objectives.
<br>14. Meets with the District Manager regularly to discuss financial results, customer satisfaction, labor issues, company culture issues and any other topic of relevance.
<br>15. Oversees the inventory levels of food and equipment, adjusts levels based on store needs and schedules timely deliveries with the appropriate suppliers.
<br>16. Actively participates in local marketing and promotional campaigns.
<br>17. Supervises 2-4 assistant managers per store and 10-20 employees.

<br>2. Moderate reading and writing skills including ability to create, compose and edit written materials.
