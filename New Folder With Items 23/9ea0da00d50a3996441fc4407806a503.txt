<h2>Intern Administrative - Customer Service</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>
<div>The Customer Service intern provides order entry and general administrative support within the Customer Service department. This position provides broad exposure to the daily operations of the department. Duties may include:</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Entering customer orders using our proprietary software.</li>
<li>
Answering incoming customer calls</li>
<li>
Working with existing customers, sales and service teams to problem solve utilizing inside resources</li>
<li>
Reporting</li>
<li>
Customer service focused projects as assigned by the service team leads</li>
</ul>
<div>
.</div>
<div></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<b>
Position Specific Details</b><div>
</div>
<div>
Primarily responsible for order entry and answering emails in the customer service box.</div>
<br>
<div>
</div>
</div>
<div>
<b>
Qualifications</b><div>
</div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Sophomore level student or above, pursuing a degree in business, marketing, or similar field from an accredited college or university preferred.</div></li>
<li><div>
Basic computer skills and working knowledge of Microsoft Office suite and Google G-suite products.</div></li>
<li><div>
Strong written and oral communication skills, must be able to work in a team environment as well as independently, and have strong interpersonal skills.</div></li>
