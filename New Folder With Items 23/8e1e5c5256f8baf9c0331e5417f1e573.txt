<h2>Administrative Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Position Overview
<br><br>
<b>Position Overview:</b> Reporting to VP, Payroll Operations Support Services this role will assist and support in the day-to-day operations of the VP, POSS. In addition, this position assists the Labor Relations department in providing support and information to the various departments at the company as well as to clients.
<br><br>
Essential Functions
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Answer and manage incoming calls for multiple phone lines. Takes and distributes accurate messages when necessary</li>
<li>Prepare Agendas for One on One Meetings</li>
<li>Record, transcribe and distribute minutes of meeting when necessary</li>
<li>Manage calendars for both VP, Payroll Operations Support Services and VP, Labor Relations</li>
<li>Assists with preparation/submission of expense report</li>
<li>Provides back-up for other Administrative Assistants within Operations</li>
<li>Assists with travel arrangements when needed</li>
<li>Locates research material and gathers information for grievances</li>
<li>Drafts letters, memos, and other internal and external client correspondences</li>
<li>Maintains storage log on Show Folders</li>
<li>Creates and maintains internal Show Folders</li>
<li>Creates and distributes show folders to Labor Relations for each new project, both physical and electronic</li>
<li>Files all department related material.</li>
<li>Creates negotiation binders for VP, Labor Relations</li>
<li>Orders office supplies</li>
<li>Creates and distributes "Wrap" Reports to all Unions and Guilds, as required</li>
<li>Creates and maintains electronic storage data file on Home/Away Plans</li>
<li>Maintain and gather CRA Waivers</li>
<li>Coordinate Assumption Agreements (Send out permission requests, draft AAs, save agreements on file)</li>
<li>Provide research &amp; answer inquiries from Guilds/Unions/Majors.</li>
<li>Distribute L724 3 Day Notices</li>
<li>Provide Address requests from Major studios and/or MPIPH</li>
<li>Performs other related duties and assignments as assigned</li>
</ul>

<li>Is tactful and uses diplomacy</li>
<li>Makes others comfortable and puts them at ease</li>
<li>Builds constructive and effective relationships</li>
</ul>
Initiative
<br><br>

Productivity
<br><br>

<li>Follows through on commitments and agreements</li>

Communication Skills
<br><br>

<li>Effectively communicate issues that are complex in nature, both verbally and in writing, through various mediums</li>
</ul>
Requirements
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>High School graduate</li>
<li>At least 2 to 5 years experience as executive, administrative or sales assistant</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills, both verbal and written</li>
<li>Must have good working knowledge of Excel, Microsoft Word, Outlook, Power Point, Web EX and various other programs</li>
<li>Must have strong organizational skills with attention to detail</li>
<li>Must be able to multitask and prioritize</li>
<li>Self-motivated, must be able to work independently</li>
<li>Good follow-through skills</li>
<li>Must demonstrate adherence to discretion and high ethical standards.</li>
</ul>
