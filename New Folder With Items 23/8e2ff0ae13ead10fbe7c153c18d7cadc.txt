<p></p>iHeartMedia Stations<br>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Job Summary:
</b></p>Job Description Summary
<br>
Identifies and develops new business opportunities; keeps and grows existing client relationships; offers solutions that help clients achieve their business goals; closes business; meets set sales target.<br>
<p>
</p>
<p>Responsibilities<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>• Identifies and solicits new business through prospecting and cold calling; builds and maintains a full pipeline of sales prospects.
</p>
<p>• Services and grows relationships in existing client base.
</p>
<p>• Identifies client/agency needs and develops persuasive proposals to meet needs and opportunities.
</p>
<p>• Delivers creative and effective sales presentations.
</p>
<p>• Steers clients based on market, platform and station information.
</p>
<p>• Maintains client communication and ensures client satisfaction.
</p>
<p>• Monitors competition to continually find new account leads.
</p>
<p>• Negotiates rates based on iHeartMedia ’ s budgets.
</p>
<p>• Works collaboratively with internal partners to drive revenue.
</p>
<p>• Ensures prompt payments.
</p>
<p>• Follows all station procedures for preparing orders, resolving billing issues, submitting regular reports regarding sales, pipeline lists, forecasts and competitive analysis.
</p>
<p>• Creates effective marketing campaigns in cooperation with iHeartMedia resources.
</p>
<p>• Generates revenue and meets/exceeds established sales targets.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Qualifications<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>• Proficient in Microsoft Office suite and social networking platforms
</p>
<p>• Adept at prospecting and using effective consultative selling principles and practices
</p>
<p>• Strong client service relationship-building skills
</p>
<p>• Ability to plan and organize, set priorities and multi task in a fast-paced environment
</p>
<p>• Negotiation and closing proficiency
</p>
<p>• Persuasive communication skills: verbal, written and presentation
</p>
<p>• Independent; self-motivated; competitive; assertive
</p>
<p>• Strong problem-solving and analytical skills
</p>
<p>• Understanding of market dynamics including demographics
</p>
<p>• Stress tolerance especially with tight deadlines and financial pressures
</p>
<p>• Flexibility and creativity
</p>
<p>• Professional appearance
</p>
<p>• Strong interpersonal skills<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Work Experience
</p>
<p>• 1-2 years ’ sales experience
</p>
<p>• Experience in Media/Advertising Sales with proven success is preferable
</p>
<p>• SalesForce experience is a plus<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Education
</p>
<p>• 4-year college degree, preferably in a related field<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Certifications
</p>
<p>• None required<br>
