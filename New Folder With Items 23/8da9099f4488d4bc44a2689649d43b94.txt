<h2>Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>POSITION SUMMARY:</b></p>
<p>
This position combines traditional marketing/advertising/public relations (marketing) with sales lead generation responsibilities.</p>
<p>
The Marketing Manager is a member of the Business Development Team responsible for clearly communicating and executing the various marketing and campaigns for Bluebird Network. This position will develop the day-to-day strategies, design content needs for the Bluebird Carrier and Enterprise Sales Teams, including the Bluebird Underground Data Center. This position will oversee complex data analysis to inform strategic decisions by the Director, Business Development. The Marketing Manager will be required to regularly engage with various members of the Bluebird Sales, Operations, Engineering and Network teams for collaboration.</p>
<p>
This position requires a broad understanding of Bluebird’s capabilities, customers, relationships and networking technologies and protocols (Internet, Ethernet, etc.). This position requires strong customer focus, strong organizational, project and matrix management skills and the ability to complete tasks in a multi-disciplinary team environment with little supervision.</p>
<p><b>
DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Create and manage the Bluebird marketing strategies, including advertising, public relations and digital marketing campaigns</li>
<li>Create and manage the annual marketing budget</li>
<li>Write articles, blogs and other marketing material, and provide feedback to other contributors, and editing other writers’ content
<ul>
<li>Ensure critical messaging has been included in all communications</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Work with external marketing, advertising and public relations firms.</li>
<li>Establish and maintain measurable processes that ensure best practices in campaign and lead generation.
<ul>
<li>Create, determine and monitor lead source generation opportunities and monthly reporting.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Collaborate with the Business Development, Carrier, Enterprise, Sales Engineer, Engineering, Operations and Network teams in support of special projects as needed.</li>
<li>Provide support for customer appreciation events and other marketing events (e.g. trade shows).
</li>
<li>Other tasks as may be needed from time to time.</li>
<li>Inform the marketing, sales, and customer support teams about our customer behaviors and buying preferences and patterns.</li>
<li>Evaluate new technologies and add-on applications to improve and optimize marketing team performance</li>
</ul>
<p>Manage responsibilities of support staff:</p>
<ul>
<li>Company website</li>
<li>Lead source reports from website and marketing campaigns.
</li>
<li>Metrics reports on marketing and sales activities, effectiveness and business impact.</li>
<li>Marketing and sales data analyzation/recommendations on areas for optimization.</li>
<li>Competitive research on other products and companies in same industry.</li>
<li>Customer surveys to gather marketing data and insights.</li>
<li>Social media channel activity with relevant company and industry content.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's Degree in marketing or advertising or equivalent working experience.</li>
<li>Software/platform experience, Microsoft Excel, PowerPoint, Word, SalesForce, SalesGenie, LinkedIn</li>
<li>Experience in creating and managing social media accounts.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>SKILLS AND ABILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to create and manage Bluebird marketing tactics
<ul>
<li>Ability to effectively communicate Bluebird tactics with external resources for marketing, advertising, and public relations support.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Highly motivated, with the ability to interact with customers and be attentive and responsive to customer needs and concerns.
</li>
<li>Exceptional writing and editing skills, as well as the ability to adopt the style, tone and voice of our business’ various types of content.</li>
<li>Ability to think analytically and interest in using data to optimize/scale marketing strategies and tactics.
</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task, work independently and maintain professional composure in a demanding work environment.</li>
<li>Understanding of Adobe's Creative Suite (Photoshop, InDesign, Illustrator, Muse) and Word Press.</li>
<li>Carrier and Enterprise telecommunications and Internet Service Provider experience is a plus.</li>
<li>B2B (business-to business) marketing preferred.</li>
<li>Ability to project for the future strategies to differentiate Bluebird from competitors.</li>
<li>Occasional overnight travel required.</li>
</ul>
