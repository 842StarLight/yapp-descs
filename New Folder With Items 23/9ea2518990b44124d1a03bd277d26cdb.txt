<h2>Service Billing Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Service Billing Specialist </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bill Sales Order and send invoice to customers</li>
<li>Process customer payment and deposits</li>
<li>Update customer account contacts.</li>
<li>Assist in the collection of past due accounts</li>
<li>Communicate with customer and some vendors related to customer transactions</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Responsibilities </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bill sales order and send invoices to customer either via email, regular mail or customer portal</li>
<li>Create Sales Order and Purchase Order as needed from time to time</li>
<li>Process customer payments and deposits by running customer credit cards</li>
<li>Manage the status of invoices in the accounts that were sent via the customer portal to ensure that invoices had been received properly</li>
<li>Follow up payments on invoices that were send via the customer portal</li>
<li>Update accounts receivable database with new account information, customer communication, missed payments and other pertinent information that will assist in the collection of past due accounts</li>
<li>Ensure all customers remain informed on their outstanding debts and due dates</li>
<li>Provide solutions to any relative problems of customers</li>
<li>May need to contact some vendors to schedule and ensure the service provided to customers are met</li>
<li>Create credit memos as needed</li>
<li>Write thorough notes on billing activity with clear and reliable data</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Proven experience as billing specialist (1-2 years relevant work experience)</li>
<li>Adherence to laws and best practices in regards with dealing with customers and data</li>
<li>Comfortable dealing with numbers and the processing of financial information</li>
<li>Proficient knowledge of MS Office (particularly Excel) and ER software (NetSuite)</li>
<li>Proficiency in English</li>
<li>Results-driven and patient</li>
<li>High degree of attention to detail and trustworthiness</li>
<li>High School Diploma required; BS or AA degree in Accounting or Finance or related</li>
</ul>
<p>Business Degree preferred</p>
