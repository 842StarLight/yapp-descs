<h2>Enterprise eTime Operations Manager</h2>

<p>
<b>RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Sales Support</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Support with pricing</li>
<li>Attend sales to implementation handover meetings, gather all the input material to then create a central repository per client</li>
<li>Work with regional managers and the resource management team to ensure required staffing is in place</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Management of Hardware, Licenses and Clocks</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ensure processes are clearly documented and the organization is trained in executing the process</li>
<li>Manage exceptions in a timely manner to ensure project commitments are met</li>
<li>Ensure clear guidance to sales on types of licenses, clocks and accessories</li>
<li>Establish deep relationships within different ADP business units that play a part in the process</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
 </p>
<p><b>Financial &amp; Operational Reporting</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Facilitate the financial forecasting process and ensure accuracy of reporting (cost by project, capacity planning...)</li>
<li>In liaison with Project Managers and resource manager ensure forecast effort by project is managed within PlanView</li>
<li>Performs periodic communications to associates targeting key areas where data is not accurate or timely</li>
<li>Support proactive financial reporting</li>
<li>Maintain cost deferment report - highlight main projects where costs cannot be deferred</li>
<li>Ensure report accuracy and delivery according to the agreed audience and distribution schedule</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p> <p><b>Client Billing</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for accurate and timely billing of implementation and recurring revenue in line with the contract</li>
<li>Maintain regional financial dashboards</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
<b>Aptitudes</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to prioritize tasks, strong time management skills, ability to work effectively across various regions and initiatives</li>
<li>Works collaboratively to achieve group objectives and business results</li>
<li>Ability to understand and gain knowledge and application of key processes with a view to improving them</li>
<li>Ability to adapt to changes in the organization and support others with impact they have on responsibilities</li>
<li>Demonstrates "One ADP" thinking and considers the impact of their actions on functions and business units</li>
<li>Applies critical thinking and a data driven, analytical approach to decision-making and problem solving</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Reporting line:</b></p>
<ul>
<li> Reports to EeT Sn Director for Implementation</li>
<li>Works closely with Implementation Directors, Project Managers and implementation managers</li>
<br>
</ul>
