<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>This position will involve interface implementation services with the IPS Computer Aided Dispatch (CAD) System. Under minimal supervision this individual will perform the following responsibilities:<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Plan, organize, coordinate and participate in the development and implementation of Mobile solutions and CAD related interfaces to meet internal and external customer objectives</li>
<li>
Provide expertise, support, assistance, ad-hoc consulting and guidance to internal and external customers</li>
<li>
Independently performs responsible analytical work in support of customer and management goals and objectives</li>
<li>
Independently develop required information, including approach and analytical techniques as well as provide sound analyses and recommendations</li>
<li>
Consulting and implementation services with the Intergraph CAD Interfaces and mobile solutions</li>
<li>
Travel to customer sites throughout the US (70%)</li>
<li>
Requirements gathering and documentation</li>
<li>
Installation and testing of the CAD system Interfaces and mobile solutions, as well as making sure the environment is ready for system training, cutover and acceptance</li>
<li>
Provide custom development work where applicable to CAD Interfaces and mobile solutions</li>
</ul>
