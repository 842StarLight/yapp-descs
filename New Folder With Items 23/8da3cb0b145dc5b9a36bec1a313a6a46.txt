<h2>Process Improvement Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<b>Description</b><br>
<div>The Process Improvement Analyst (Service) is tasked with analyzing data from various systems, documenting current</div>
<div>
backoffice business processes across multiple states and identifying efficiency opportunities through either business</div>
<div>
process changes or system updates/enhancements. The Process Improvement Analyst (Service) works with the National</div>
<div>
Call Center (NCC)/Customer Advocacy Team, Local Divisional Teams and the Proof Service Technical Team. These efforts</div>
<div>
should drive us forward in delivering world-class Service capabilities to Customers and Sales and increasing overall customer</div>
<div>
service levels.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
<div>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities include the following. Other duties may be assigned.</div>
<ul><li>Collaborates with and helps enable the Service organization (Customer Advocates/National Call Centers) to provide world-class</li></ul>
<div>
Customer Service:</div>
<div>
oMoving Customer Advocacy from “Deploy” to “Optimize” by performing Root Cause Analysis, digging through the Case</div>
<div>
Management data, identifying opportunities for improvement and working with functional leaders (Supply Chain, Commercial Ops,</div>
<div>
Revenue Mgt, etc.) and divisions on corrective action.</div>
<div>
oFacilitate the transition of customer support as we move them to a more self-service model.</div>
<div>
oFacilitate the transition of certain non-selling activities (e.g., “firefighting”) from Sales to Service to allow our Sales Consultants</div>
<div>
to spend more time “selling”.</div>
<div>
oDeliver improved overall customer satisfaction by providing more self-service options and better visibility into customer issues</div>
<div>
and timelier resolution.</div>
<ul>
<li>Gather information, perform discovery and document current backoffice/operational processes.</li>
<li>Analyze current processes to identify opportunities that will allow us to provide better levels or service and/or become more efficient.</li>
<li>Recommend and present ROI business cases for various system and process improvement opportunities.</li>
<li>Gather business requirements and translate into functional specifications.</li>
<li>Communicate systems and procedural changes to end users through excellent written and oral communications.</li>
<li>Support the education and training of end users, working to increase end user proficiency and adoption of implemented solutions.</li>
<li>Demonstrate and maintain expertise in writing SQL queries, reporting, automation, and presentation tools.</li>
<li>Create automated processes to gather data from various sources and format into reports, scorecards, etc.</li>
</ul>

</ul>
<div>

</ul>
<div>
various levels of management</div>
<ul>
