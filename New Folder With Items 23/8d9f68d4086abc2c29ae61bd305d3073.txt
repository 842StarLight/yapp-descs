<h2>Associate Business Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>About the Associate Business Manager role:</b><br>
Beekeeper Marketing’s Business Management team members act as the account lead for our clients’ eCommerce business and are responsible for harnessing internal, client, and channel resources to achieve eCommerce objectives. The Associate Business Manager (ABM) will work closely with and support other Business Managers, eventually managing his/her own portfolio of clients.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities include:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Managing clients’ e-commerce accounts (vendor and seller central) to deliver world class growth by planning &amp; executing all Amazon sales levers - products, pricing, promotions, content, relevancy, etc.</li>
<li>Onboarding new clients</li>
<li>Managing new account and product set-up</li>
<li>Communicating with and presenting to clients regularly as their account executive and main point of Beekeeper contact - we are strategic business partners for our clients and each one varies in size, category and growth.</li>
<li>Conducting brand audits to lay the foundation for a strong growth strategy</li>
<li>Creating and managing the execution of client promotional calendars</li>
<li>Executing marketing plans</li>
<li>Working with Beekeeper’s Media Team to set media strategy and oversee media execution</li>
<li>Ensuring ample stock and that clients meet creative deadlines</li>
<li>Working with Beekeeper’s Operations Team to ensure smooth supply chain for clients</li>
<li>Business analytics and ROI analysis</li>
<li>General troubleshooting</li>
</ul>
<b>Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Must be authorized to work in the United States</li>
<li>4 - 7 years relevant work experience in at least some of the following areas (willing to train in areas to fill gaps):</li>
<li>eCommerce channels</li>
<li>Cross-functional roles with consumer packaged goods companies (grocery, pet or health and personal care)</li>
<li>Marketing (online advertising experience a plus)</li>
<li>Prior experience working with Amazon is strongly preferred.</li>
<li>Detail oriented and strong organizational skills.</li>
<li>Client/account management experience.</li>
<li>Demonstrated experience as an effective team member with strong communications skills, reliability/integrity/maturity, and ability to self-start.</li>
<li>Must be comfortable with the learning curve/constant change that comes with operating in the fast moving, ever changing world of eCommerce.</li>
<li>Must be able to prioritize and comfortable with ongoing projects</li>
<li>Must be a team player. We are making strides in terms of process and efficiency, but we also need people willing to pitch in when needed and help us build processes.</li>
<li>Comfortable in spreadsheets and doing analysis.</li>
<li>Bachelor's Degree or higher.</li>
<li>We operate on G-Suite (Gmail, G-Drive, Sheets, Presentations, Calendar, Drawings, etc); experience using G-Suite is a plus. At a minimum, must be adept at Excel, Word, and PowerPoint and willing to make the transition to G-Suite.</li>
<li>Must have a dedicated office at home or elsewhere with access to secure high-speed internet and phone, and sufficient space for two monitors. This is not a job that can be done at a coffee shop or while traveling (appropriate equipment will be provided).</li>
<li>Must be ok to travel to expos, clients &amp; company offsites.</li>
</ul>
<b>About Beekeeper Marketing:</b><br>
Beekeeper Marketing was founded in 2007 to help great natural/organic/specialty consumer packaged goods companies (grocery, pet, and health/personal care) reach their sales potential on Amazon. We are a full service agency who partners with clients on the retail, marketing and supply chain portions of their Amazon Vendor and Seller Central businesses. We were recently acquired by Advantage Solutions and Marketing, but still remain scrappy and entrepreneurial. Our entire team of 50+ works remotely from their homes, but gets together often and keeps connected via Slack.
<br><br>
<b>Compensation Overview:</b><br>
Competitive compensation depending on experience that includes base salary, a bonus, 401K eligibility, health insurance and monthly stipends for phone/internet.</div>
