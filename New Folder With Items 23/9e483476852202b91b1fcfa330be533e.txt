SHOULD YOU ACCEPT THIS CHALLENGE…
<br><br>
The Subscription Services Representative is a key individual contributor on the World-Wide Renewals team. The Subscription Services team is a culmination of individuals who are passionate about customer loyalty committed to bringing the right people together with the intention of securing and growing our revenue. This role will support an assigned territory of customers in coordination with our channel to maintain and grow renewal rates and establish relationships with a focus on renewing subscriptions on-time, adding new products &amp; services and providing the highest level of customer service throughout the entire quote-to-cash process.
<br><br>
Your ability to drive business process, sustain and renew subscriptions, resolve customer issues/problems, and effectively manage your time will contribute to our mutual success.
<br><br>
The Evergreen Subscription Services team is responsible for closely working with Sales, Finance, and Support while managing subscriptions, co-terms for up-sell opportunities, and supporting the company's strategic subscription renewal strategy &amp; process.
<br><br>
<b>In this role you will:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>A point of contact with Channel Partners &amp; Account Managers to proactively market subscription opportunities to partners and/or resolve issues that may hinder them from maintaining a relationship with Pure Storage</li>
<li>Effectively communicate company messages, value propositions, etc. verbally and in writing</li>
<li>Uncover accounts which may be at risk and develop plans to mitigate and bring them to closure</li>
<li>Accountable for reviewing and validating installed base reports</li>
<li>Manage existing programs to maximize company revenue and resolve customer-related contract issues</li>
<li>Ability to research original business transactions and prepare detail that will lead the account team to make quick recommendations on how to position a renewal</li>
<li>Goal is to optimize renewal rate, attach rate and term</li>
<li>Quote all subscription renewal opportunities for assigned territory</li>
<li>Develop overall renewal strategy and process for assigned territory</li>
<li>Manage &amp; forecast the subscription pipeline for territory</li>
<li>Create programs with Marketing Manager to proactively encourage renewal activity with partners</li>
<li>Develop and communicate process change and improvements to optimize programs and enhance relationships with necessary company external customers and internal support groups</li>
<li>Track all activity in Salesforce</li>
</ul>
