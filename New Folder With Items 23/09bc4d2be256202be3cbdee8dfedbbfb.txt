<h2>Marketing Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div><div>
<p>T he American Nurses Association (ANA) is the premier organization representing the interests of the nation's 4 million registered nurses. ANA advances the nursing profession by fostering high standards of nursing practice, promoting a safe and ethical work environment, bolstering the health and wellness of nurses, and advocating on healthcare issues that affect nurses and the public. ANA is at the forefront of improving the quality of health care for all.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>BUSINESS UNIT: American Nurses Association
</p>
<p>DEPARTMENT: Enterprise Marketing
<br>
SALARY GRADE: 7
<br>
EMPLOYEE REFERRAL BONUS AMOUNT: $800.00<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The American Nurses Association Enterprise is a family of companies working together to achieve the vision of a healthy world through the power of Nursing.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Executes promotional marketing activities for the entities within the ANA Enterprise to meet (and exceed) goals.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>DUTIES:
</p>
<p><b>1. </b>Assists with implementation by coordinating activities of traditional and electronic marketing projects to meet program goals and to maximize revenue. May include project management, writing, graphic coordination, distribution, audience/list research, print production, data processing and/or analysis, or other activities to support the execution of a wide variety of promotional materials for programs, products, and web sites across the Enterprise.
</p>
<p><b>2. </b>Develops, maintains and tracks schedules and/or budgets for marketing projects. Assists with preparation of marketing departmental budgets (correlating response rates and revenue projections). Controls expenditure in assigned budget areas.
</p>
<p><b>3. </b>Implements and utilizes systems for tracking results and reporting of marketing activities. Monitors internal and external activities to discover new ways of maximizing campaign effectiveness. Suggests and implements changes in marketing tactics based upon results of analysis, strategic objectives, ISO, or other feedback.
</p>
<p><b>4. </b>Develops and maintains expert knowledge of programs, products, and services across the ANA Enterprise. Keeps informed of activities of assigned organizational units.
</p>
<p><b>5. </b>Assists with departmental administrative responsibilities and special projects (may include financial and shipping requests).<br>
</p>
