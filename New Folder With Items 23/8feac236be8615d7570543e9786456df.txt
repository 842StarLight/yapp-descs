<h2>Operations Delivery Manager-Cloud Solutions</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>Reporting to directly to the Vynamic Cloud Solution Manager, the Vynamic Cloud Operational Delivery Manager holds the responsibility to Design, Build, Facilitate and manage the operational functions relating to the Vynamic Cloud Business Model of Diebold Nixdorf. In this model, Diebold Nixdorf will be the Cloud operated SaaS provider, Operation both Banking an retail software portfolio and offer these in a SaaS model that can be used by the Financial institutions Cost and Price Management and a meticulous approach to Continuous Service Improvement will be key functions of this role to drive profitability. This position is responsible for the growth and management of this model and will act as a program director responsible for sub-contracting integrated services both internally to Diebold Nixdorf and externally in order to achieve the objectives of the Business Model. The development of processes and process management is key to delivery of this model, optimising cost and driving continuous improvement in the operation. Service level management is a key role as The Vynamic Cloud Operational Delivery Manager will act as a liaison with senior management to provide support and direction when required.
</div>
<div></div>

<div><b>Essential Functions:</b></div>
<div><ul>
<li>
<div>The Vynamic Cloud Operational Delivery Manager will lead a team to develop, deploy and manage this operation model in specific markets and sectors. The team will be sized based upon business volume.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>The Vynamic Cloud Operational Delivery Manager will have responsibilities for the Operational Readiness of the Vynamic Cloud model in new markets as defined from time to time.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>The Vynamic Cloud Operational Delivery Manager will engage and manage an internal Diebold Nixdorf Supply Chain and oversee any sub-contractors under that model. Where internal services and support functions do not currently deliver the required services, under the Standardized Services Portfolio, The Vynamic Cloud Operational Delivery Manager will be responsible for the development, deployment and incubation and management of new solutions or handover to the Solution Delivery Team.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>The Vynamic Cloud Operational Delivery Manager will develop plans to accompany growth, address service delivery issues, and take these plans to the field level and facilitate the roll out.</div>
</li>
<li><div>On an external basis (external sub-contractors for any function of the business) to facilitate and operationalize such contractual requirements.
</div></li>
<li><div>Hold financial obligations with respect to facilitating any project changes and filtering them, both to the operational and financial organization confirming accurate capture of revenues agreed upon by the parties. Internal budgeting is a must, as the operational cost center must be kept running efficiently in order to facilitate the requirements of its customers and the needs of Diebold Nixdorf to drive profitability and margin growth.
</div></li>
</ul></div>
