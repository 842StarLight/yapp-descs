Position Objective</b>
</div>
<div><p>Konica Minolta currently has an exciting opportunity for a <b>Sales Manager!</b></p></div>

<div>
<div>
<b>
Essential Job Functions</b>
</div>
<div><ul>
<li>Attract, hire, train, develop, motivate and manage members of a world-class sales organization.</li>
<li>
Aggressively stimulate, review and monitor all business activity for the branch and ensure achievement of activity goals.</li>
<li>
Ensure achievement of branch sales and profitability objectives.</li>
<li>
Accurately forecast annual, quarterly, monthly and weekly revenue streams.</li>
<li>
Provide quarterly evaluations of sales organization productivity.</li>
<li>
Understand each employee’s strengths and weaknesses with recommendations to develop these areas of performance.</li>
<li>
Evaluate the effectiveness of the sales organization and implement changes.</li>
<li>
Develop solid business relationships with new or existing customers.</li>
</ul></div>
</div>
<div>
<div>
<b>
Competencies (Knowledge, Skills and Abilities)</b>
</div>
<div><ul>
<li>Proven ability to directly manage and track performance.</li>
<li>
Motivate and direct sales teams in both the commercial and major account sales arena.</li>
<li>
Excellent leadership, interpersonal, communication, management and organizational skills with a proven track record.</li>
<li>
Strong business acumen managing to “bottom-line” profitability.</li>
<li>
Experience in accurately forecasting and managing the sales of a high growth business.</li>
<li>
Proven track record of having excellent internal and external customer relationships.</li>
</ul></div>
</div>

Experience, Educational Reqts and Certifications</b>

Demonstrated leadership and professional sales coaching/development experience.</li>
</ul></div>
