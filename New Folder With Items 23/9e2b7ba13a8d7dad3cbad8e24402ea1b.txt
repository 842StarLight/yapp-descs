<h2>Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Account Executive at Microsoft serves as the face of Microsoft leading our most valued clients into the digital age. This role is vital to Microsoft’s competitive ambitions. Traditional sales engagement methods have evolved, and clients are seeking consultative partnerships, not vendor, client relationships. Seeking candidates with mix industry experience.</p>
<div></div>
<br>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Responsibilities</b></h2>
<div></div>
<br>
<p>
Armed with world class cloud solutions, your role is to unlock the potential of enterprise clients by demonstrating clear business value accelerating their digital transformation and success. High level client satisfaction is a key measure, as are revenue/consumption targets.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The most successful Account Executives are ultra-resourceful, master orchestrators of virtual teams, and exceptionally curious to listen and learn from their clients. These attributes allow them to advise confidently and elevate their clients’ business.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
This role offers incredible opportunity to accelerate your career by driving business impact that can change the future. Be a part of a transformative, learning culture that drives innovation. It has never been a better time join Microsoft and see up close the digital revolution.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Leadership</b></p>
<p><i>
Plan and present clear vision of client success</i></p>
<ul>
<li>
Build and own holistic client plan detailing critical insights and new business opportunities. Critical that plan is made to evolve and continually vetted ensuring stated objectives. Strive for bold ambition and challenge convention.</li>
<li>
Build coalition of support at C-level with client and internally at Microsoft. Define digital success and path to attain it.</li>
<li>
Develop and lead team centric approach with extended teams. Direct clear opportunity ownership and accountability among virtual account team.</li>
<li>
Increase technical and industry acumen delivering innovative ideas to accelerate client success in the cloud.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Execution</b></p>
<p><i>
Laser focus on business outcomes that propel client satisfaction</i></p>
<ul>
<li>
Consistent, repeatable achievement of revenue and consumption targets through disciplined pipeline, forecast management.</li>
<li>
Leverage usage of key business insights to elevate client conversation and action.</li>
<li>
World class client service through alignment of client objectives and plan to achieve them.</li>
<li>
Deliver tangible solutions to client solving for greater efficiencies, cost savings, and deep regard for Microsoft partnership.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Profile</b></p>
<p><i>
Capabilities &amp; Behaviors Required</i></p>
<ul>
<li>
Sales Achiever – Achieve results by understanding digital transformation drivers and client priorities. Identify new opportunities and help clients accelerate their move to the digital age for their own benefit as well as their clients.</li>
<li>
Digital Transformation Driver – Anticipate market changes to drive industry-relevant solutions for clients, influencing BDMs and ITDMs, building trust with stakeholders and translating Microsoft solutions into business impact and outcomes that accelerate the clients’ digital transformation.</li>
<li>
Sales Challenger – Continuously build industry and technical knowledge. Bring innovative industry solutions, benchmarks and references to C-level executives that drive greater efficiencies, optimization and increase client satisfaction.</li>
<li>
Deal Orchestrator – Orchestrate virtual selling team and all Microsoft resources maximizing revenue opportunity. Lead and drive entire sales cycle and leverage repeatable offerings to accelerate and win deal.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Qualifications</b></h2>
<div><b>
Experiences Required: Education, Key Experiences, Skills and Knowledge:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Strong growth mindset. Seeks to solve for difficult client challenges.</li>
<li>
Proven ability to lead, build trust and be highly credible across levels of organizations.</li>
<li>
A strong sales and business background, with 2+ years of technology-related experience</li>
<li>
Knowledge within a mix industry environment and ability to make confident recommendations at C-level, business decision makers.</li>
<li>
A Bachelor’s degree with exposure to Information Technology (or equivalent) is preferred; MBA is preferred</li>
</ul>
