<h2>Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>WhiteSource, the leading provider of open source management solutions, is looking for a talented, Customer-driven Account Manager who will join WhiteSource growing Sales team!</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ownership of the subscription renewal process with existing customers.</li>
<li>Meet and exceed renewal ratio targets.</li>
<li>Identify upsell opportunities and maximize the customers' lifetime value.</li>
<li>Developing strong relationships with customers, connecting with key business executives and stakeholders.</li>
<li>Work closely with Customer support, Engineering, Product &amp; Customer Success teams to ensure customer success.</li>
<li>Responding to customers’ requests for information about proposals and quotes.</li>
<li>Develop business and technical proficiency with all WhiteSource solutions.</li>
<li>Monitor customer satisfaction across service lines.</li>
</ul>
<br>
Requirements:<br>
<p>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelors’ degree in a related field.</li>
<li>Proven work experience as an Account Manager, Key Account Manager, Sales Account Manager, or Customer success.</li>
<li>Experience in managing commercial activities (contracts, quotes, negotiations, proposals, payments and so forth).</li>
<li>At least 2 years of experience in B2B SaaS companies.</li>
<li>Experience delivering client-focused solutions to customer needs.</li>
<li>Excellent communication and presentation skills.</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
