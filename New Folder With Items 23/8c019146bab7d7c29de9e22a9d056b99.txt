<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Position Overview
</b></h2>
<p></p>At PNC, our people are our greatest differentiator and competitive advantage in the markets we serve. We are all united in delivering the best experience for our customers. As an Investment Solutions RM I (Associate Retirement Plan Advisor) within PNC's Institutional Asset Management Retirement Solutions organization, you will be based in Philadelphia, PA or Pittsburgh, PA.
<br>
<br>
In this role, you will provide centralized investment and client support for PNC Retirement Solutions products and services. You will work closely with the Investment Director, Retirement Plan Advisors and Business Development team. Responsibilities include:
<br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Coordinates ongoing investment due diligence processes for the FIS (Fiduciary Investment Services) Investment platform including quarterly monitoring and new fund selection.
</li>
<li>Reviews quarterly investment monitoring results, provides investment commentary, updates PNC’s Covered List, ensuring all information is accurate, and resolves any discrepancies.
</li>
<li>Coordinates internal and external client communication for investment platform changes.
</li>
<li>Provides investment analysis and investment platform support.
</li>
<li>Provides investment lineup reviews, mapping proposals and benchmark reports for clients and prospects.
</li>
<li>Manages client requests, client deliverables, client communications and other centralized processes and services related to our FIS products.
</li>
<li>Updates internal and external vendor systems.
</li>
<li>Manages relationships with internal and external vendors and asset managers.
</li>
</ul>
<br>
Position preferences/skills:
<br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Investment experience/knowledge
</li>
<li>Pursuing AIF, CFA or CIMA
</li>
<li>Experience with asset management, trust and/or related financial services, especially servicing Defined Contribution plans or other Employee Benefit plans
</li>
<li>Creative problem solving skills, ability to meet deadlines and strong client focus desired
</li>
<li>Experience with Morningstar Direct
</li>
<li>Strong skills in Microsoft Office, particularly Excel
</li>
<li>Strong verbal and written communications skills.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Description
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>As directed, develops, implements, manages and provides ongoing review on client investment strategies based on thorough understanding of client needs and objectives. Owns the client relationship by monitoring customer satisfaction service levels and managing revenue streams to meet relationship profitability. May implement plan design.
</li>
<li>Manages a book of business and establishes and executes the investment management and review of relationships in accordance with client objectives and PNC's solutions. Provides industry/market insights and perspectives. Retains and grows assets under management and\or revenue for assigned relationships through the delivery of an exceptional client investment experience. May be responsible for the retention of the plans. .
</li>
<li>Acts as a conduit to the clients for all products and services by referring clients to other lines of business. Accountable for working with internal partners where appropriate to acquire new clients and expand services in a proactive manner. May serve as a technical expert to internal partners. May lead the sales process for their clients.
</li>
<li>May support the Business Development Officers during the sales process and client onboarding.
</li>
<li>Executes risk management practices through the adherence to PNC's policies and procedures. May also act as a liaison for both internal and external partners in their business segment.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>PNC Employees take pride in our reputation and to continue building upon that we expect our employees to be:
</p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Customer Focused </b>- Knowledgeable of the values and practices that align customer needs and satisfaction as primary considerations in all business decisions and able to leverage that information in creating customized customer solutions.
</li>
<li>
<b>Managing Risk </b>- Assessing and effectively managing all of the risks associated with their business objectives and activities to ensure they adhere to and support PNC's Enterprise Risk Management Framework.
</li>
</ul>
