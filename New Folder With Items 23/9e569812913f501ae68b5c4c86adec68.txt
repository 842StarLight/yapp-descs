<h2>Enterprise Cloud Sales Executive</h2>

At Redapt, as part of an entrepreneurial team in this rapidly growing industry, you will help shape the future of businesses of all sizes that use public cloud solutions.

As an Enterprise Cloud Sales Executive, you'll help grow Redapt's business with a focus on Azure oir Google Cloud Service offerings. Minimum Qualification

<li>Ability, confidence, and experience to communicate with C level clients.</li>
