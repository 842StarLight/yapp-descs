<h2>Enterprise Sales Development Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>This person will be supporting out outside sales team (Enterprise Account Executives) with the confirmation, qualification and development of sales leads. The successful candidate will play a fundamental role in achieving our ambitious customer acquisition and growth objectives. You must be comfortable making dozens of calls per day, working with channel partners, generating interest, qualifying prospects to assist in closing sales.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Source new sales opportunities through inbound lead follow-up and outbound cold calls and emails
</li>
<li>Understand customer needs and requirements
</li>
<li>Work to plan, implement, and lead the new business sales expansion in a given geographic market.
</li>
<li>Route qualified opportunities to the appropriate sales executives for further development and closure
</li>
<li>Enter applicable data into CRM database
</li>
<li>Follow-Up on Request for Information (RFI)
</li>
<li>Assist with pre-event confirmations and post-event follow up
</li>
<li>Establish basic lead grading for each prospect
</li>
<li>Communicate product features/benefits clearly, consistently and with integrity
</li>
<li>Track, analyze and report against opportunities funnel.
</li>
<li>Ensure a culture of "can do" creativity and flexibility working to find better ways to work with our customers, partners, and fellow team members
</li>
<li>Research accounts, identify key players and generate interest
</li>
<li>Maintain and expand your database of prospects within your assigned territory
</li>
<li>Team with channel partners to build pipeline
</li>
</ul>
