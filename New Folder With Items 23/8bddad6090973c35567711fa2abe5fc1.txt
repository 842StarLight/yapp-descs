<h2>Rental Agent</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Seeking a talented Rental Agent to join our thriving<b> boutique </b>team! <b>Real Estate license a must!</b> The ideal candidate for this position will enjoy fast-paced work, show creativity and have a high degree of professionalism when working with our residents. The rental Agent is responsible for successfully creating and maintaining successful relationships with landlords and closing the rental inquiries efficiently.</p>
<p>
If you’re an enthusiastic, perceptive individual who’s eager to make an impact in the lives of others and aspires to build a career around the creation and cultivation of meaningful relationships, our dynamic, customer-oriented position is perfect for you!</p>
<p>
Self-assured and open-minded individuals who have a passion for selling and are willing to step outside their comfort-zones will thrive in this position.</p>
<b>
Compensation:</b><p>
$35,000+ (commission based)</p>
<b>
Responsibilities:</b><ul>
<li>
Answer incoming phone calls and respond to emails in a timely fashion</li>
<li>
Follow up with all leads on a daily basis</li>
<li>
Communicate with landlords in a clear and effective way</li>
<li>
Uphold company policy and professionalism when scheduling and performing apartment tours</li>
<li>
Processing paperwork in a timely fashion</li>
<li>
Approaching every day with an open-mind and unwavering enthusiasm</li>
<li>

<li>

<li>
Advertise available properties using MLS and a variety of media and promotional materials</li>
<li>
Determine the needs, living standards and economic viability of prospective customers</li>
<li>
Confirm rental application data and personal references</li>
<li>
Stay knowledgeable of the property market status</li>
<li>
Provide information on all aspects of properties</li>
