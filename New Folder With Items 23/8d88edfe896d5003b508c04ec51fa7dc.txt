They need better tools and building blocks so they can stay focused on innovating. One of these building blocks is identity: authentication and authorization. That’s what we do. Our platform handles 2.5B logins per month for thousands of customers around the world. From indie makers to Fortune 500 companies, we can handle any use case. We like to think that we are helping make the internet safer.

Join us on this journey to make developers more productive while making the internet safer!</div>

<div>
We are looking for a Corporate Counsel to join the Auth0 Legal team in Bellevue, WA (HQ). This is an exciting opportunity, requiring professional experience with SaaS and/or PaaS sales contracts, services agreements, licenses and intellectual property agreements. Come join our small internal team.</div>
</div>
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Responsibilities:</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>
Draft and negotiate a wide variety of commercial contracts, including agreements relating to SaaS and/or PaaS sales contracts, services agreements, licenses and intellectual property agreements.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with the company’s sales, channel, compliance, security and financial teams to ensure consistency of agreement terms with the company’s business, financial and risk management objectives.</li>
<li>
Advise internal clients regarding legal and business issues, including identifying, managing and communicating risks.</li>
<li>
Work closely with the internal legal team to improve process and legal strategy.</li>
<li>
Handle other various legal and ad hoc projects as necessary.</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Qualifications:</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>
3-5 years experience in tech transactions at a national or large regional firm.</li>
<li>
Experience drafting and negotiating SaaS and/or PaaS sales contracts, services agreements, licenses, privacy and intellectual property agreements, and with US and EU privacy and data protection requirements.</li>
<li>
In-house experience is a plus.</li>
<li>
Experience with cyber security issues is a plus.</li>
<li>
Ability to understand complex contractual, technological and data protection issues, assess risk, and provide concise business-focused legal advice.</li>
<li>
Strong organizational skills and the ability to prioritize and manage multiple simultaneous projects with tight deadlines.</li>
<li>
Highest integrity and ethical standards.</li>
<li>
Location: Onsite in the Bellevue Headquarters location.</li>
</ul>
