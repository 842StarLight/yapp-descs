<h2>Business Program Manager - Talent Acquisition</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>The Global Talent Acquisition (GTA) Excellence Programs Team is comprised of a group of folks who are passionate about making sure Microsoft hires the best talent for the company. Our goal is simple: bring in transformative talent for Microsoft, and we are looking for another energetic PM to join our team to help us achieve our goal.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Our team serves to acquire, develop, and retain the talent that will push Microsoft to grow and thrive as a company. We know that acquiring the best talent is the biggest asset to help shape an aspirational, inclusive, and global culture that enables people across the company to do their best work.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
We contribute to the all-up Microsoft mission by enabling productivity and impact through global programs. We deliver the overall programs strategy and roadmap for the organization &amp; then see that strategy through to execution. We don’t do it alone, so be ready to work closely with excellent partners! We are a collaborative and creative organization and we enjoy spending our time on ways to continue transforming Microsoft through talent. You’ll need to bring a creative and problem-solving appetite to join the fun!</div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Responsibilities</b></h2>
<div>
The responsibilities of this role include:</div>
<ul>
<li>
Managing multiple programs &amp; projects end-to-end (strategy to execution) using our standard project management framework, such as:<ul>
<li>
Holding core team/project team meetings</li>
<li>
Identifying &amp; managing project risks to mitigation and closure</li>
<li>
Communicating project status &amp; milestones to management &amp; stakeholders</li>
<li>
Conducting postmortems/after-action reviews and sharing learnings with the team</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Developing the project plan to drive for completion of the project goals</li>
<li>
Designing to-be processes (for the projects in your book of work)
</li>
<li>Designing to-be experiences – customer mapping (for the projects in your book of work)</li>
<li>
Creating success metrics and driving the project team to meet that criteria</li>
<li>
Delivering all programs &amp; projects on time and on budget (be ready to manage the project budget and make well-informed trade-offs)</li>
<li>
Managing internal processes for the team (as needed to support proper portfolio management)</li>
<li>
Following our organizational readiness practices (e.g. creating communications to stakeholders &amp; ensuring they are aligned to the team’s larger communication plan, etc.)</li>
<li>
Diving in to understand the staffing organization – learn how we staff at Microsoft – to help make better decisions and trade-offs for projects</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The successful candidate will have proven experience delivering on time &amp; high quality projects, the ability to think strategically at a global level &amp; then turn that strategy into a plan and execute on that plan. Experience with creating organizational readiness plans for projects &amp; programs is key to this role, as is the ability to build successful relationships with key partner organizations. Operating in a complex, matrix environment with diverse geographic, cultural and business needs, requires strong consultative skills, ability to influence, high adaptability, and a focus on driving toward results.</div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Qualifications</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>
Minimum 3 years driving programs/projects and deadlines as a project or program manager</li>
<li>
Minimum 3 years of proven experience in risk management (identification &amp; mitigation plans) and change management processes.</li>
<li>
Demonstrated track record of managing and leading people, teams, and multiple programs &amp; projects end-to-end (strategy to execution).</li>
<li>
Ability to communicate technical information to a non-technical audience. Experience with communicating effectively, being highly organized, possessing strong problem-solving skills and solving complex issues that may arise.</li>
<li>
Ability to recognize areas for internal improvement and develop plans for implementation.
</li>
<li>Excellent prioritization and organization skills required to meet deadlines.</li>
<li>
Able to work well in tight deadline-driven, cross-group situations to develop solutions and communicate common results.</li>
<li>
Ability to work effectively in ambiguous situations and respond favorably to change.</li>
<li>
Proven creative problem solving with a lens on candidate or customer experiences.</li>
<li>
Staffing or recruiting experience a plus, but not required.</li>
<li>
Experience with Agile Software Development a plus, but not required</li>
<li>
PMP Certification or equivalent project management experience a plus, but not required</li>
</ul>
