<h2>Promotional Products Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<p><b>Organization Summary</b></p>
<p>O’Brien Corporation is a leader in providing an integrated approach to printed materials, promotional products, marketing, and logistical support. Our client list includes many of the leading healthcare facilities throughout the Mid-West – our growth a direct result of our staff’s commitment to service, quality, and integrity.</p>
<p>O’Brien Corporation offers full-time employees a benefit package including profit sharing, vacation, and personal time. Work hours are Monday through Friday from 8:00am to 5:00pm including a 1-hour scheduled lunch break. Salary for this position is based on knowledge, skill, and experience.</p>
<p><b>Job Summary</b></p>
<p>Under general guidance, an individual in this position will be expected to perform customer service responsibilities within a dynamic and growing part of the organization. A Promotional Products Specialist serves both internal &amp; external customers while helping to coordinate the creative, logistical, and financial aspects of promotional product projects.</p>
<p>Success in this position is largely dependent on an individual’s ability to self-manage, think creatively, and focus on the needs of new and existing customers. The O’Brien work environment is fast paced, social, and full of challenging work assignments. A Promotional Products Specialist will be expected to be a consistent contributor to O’Brien values and the shared enthusiasm for organizational excellence.</p>
<p>General Duties include: Researching &amp; selecting products, liaising with suppliers &amp; sales representatives, developing proposals, placing and managing orders for new and existing accounts</p>
<p><b>Essential Job Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Communicates with sales representatives to identify project requirements (including, but not limited to: theme, quantity, budget, artwork, and timing)</li>
<li>Performs product &amp; supplier research according to project requirements and prepares proposals for customer review</li>
<li>Communicates artwork specifications to graphic artists and reviews completed proofs for quality &amp; accuracy</li>
<li>Manages order logistics including job tracking, order follow-up, details of inquiries, complaints, and/or comments, as well as actions taken</li>
<li>Keeps abreast of industry trends, research, and changes</li>
<li>Perform general administrative activities including, but not limited to: answering telephones, photocopying, faxing, mailing, and filing</li>
<li>Performs other related duties, as assigned</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>
<p><b>Job Requirements / Minimum Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Entry level position but applicants with ASI customer service, ASI sales, Marketing, Advertising, or related experience will be given preferential review</li>
<li>Proficient with Microsoft Office Suite, SAGE, and/or DemandBridge software</li>
<li>High School diploma or equivalent</li>
<li>Valid driver’s license</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Ideal Candidates Will Emphasize:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Vision, originality, and creativity</li>
<li>Organizational &amp; time management skills</li>
<li>Numerical and mathematical reasoning</li>
<li>Attention to accuracy &amp; detail</li>
<li>Communication and problem resolution</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Work Environment</b></p>
<p>Typical performance of the job requires the following physical demands:</p>
<ul>
<li>Significant contact with others including face-to-face, electronic mail, and telephone communication</li>
<li>High frequency of fast-paced, high-pressure, and/or cognitively demanding tasks &amp; responsibilities</li>
<li>Significant time spent using office technology including computers and research programs</li>
</ul>
<p>Generally, the job requires 80% sitting, 15% walking, and 5% standing. The job is performed under moderate temperature variations in a generally hazard free environment.</p>
<p><b>Required Pre-Employment Testing</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Telephone and in-person interviews</li>
<li>Background clearance &amp; reference check</li>
</ul>
