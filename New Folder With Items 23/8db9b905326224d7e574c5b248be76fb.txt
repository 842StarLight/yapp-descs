<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
The Customer Success team ensures that our clients grow their revenue by optimizing how they utilize BrightTALK products</li>
<li>
The CSM works with clients who have purchased innovative BrightTALK solutions to address their marketing challenges</li>
<li>
CSMs translate program strategies developed by the client and the sales team into manageable projects; The CSM is the customer’s point person for all program-related inquiries and becomes a trusted advisor</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>

Experience with webinar and video production and related processes</li>
