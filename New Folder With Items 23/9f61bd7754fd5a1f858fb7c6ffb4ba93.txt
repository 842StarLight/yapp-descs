<h2>Senior Analyst, Reporting & Analytics</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Position Summary
</p>
<div><div><div>
<div>The Senior Analyst, Reporting &amp; Analytics will partner with business stakeholders and IT to create, refine and visualize actionable KPIs. A successful candidate will design and develop dashboards and routine reports, and support ad-hoc analyses that drive strategic business initiatives in multiple business areas.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The Senior Analyst, Reporting &amp; Analytics should be intellectually curious and enjoy using data to inform answers to broad, unstructured business questions in a rapidly changing environment. This is an excellent opportunity for someone with solid technical skills, innate curiosity and a desire to gain technical experience while also building change management, communication and consensus building skills.</div>
</div></div></div>
<p>Responsibilities
</p>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Partners with business leaders on the design, development, testing, implementation, and evaluation of data assets.</li>
<li>
Participates in reporting requirements, feedback and prioritization sessions with various teams to ensure end-user expectations for reporting are satisfied.</li>
<li>
Automates recurring dashboards and reports on a continuous basis to ensure timely distribution.</li>
<li>
Identifies reporting processes that are inefficient and create/change processes that will lead to measurable gains in data quality and processing time.</li>
<li>
Manages a robust library of flexible analytical tools and dashboards.</li>
<li>
Designs and builds new reports, retire low value reports and consolidate redundancies.</li>
<li>
Creates reports regarding key performance indicators (KPI’s) and investigates reporting trends.</li>
<li>
Creates process for ad hoc report requests. When green-lighted, determines if report should be institutionalized and automated.</li>
<li>
Produces accurate, consistent and timely reporting by extracting information from several systems, databases, and manipulating data as needed.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<p>Qualifications
</p>
<div><div><div>
<div>Required</div>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree in Finance, Engineering, Statistics or related field</li>
<li>
3 - 5 years of experience in reporting and working with relational databases, business intelligence and data visualization software essential.</li>
<li>
Power BI or Tableau, SQL, Advanced Excel, and Microsoft Office</li>
<li>
Excellent communication skills (Verbal and Written) with the ability to interview all employee levels.</li>
</ul>
<div>
Preferred</div>
<ul>
<li>
Master’s degree in related field</li>
<li>
Experience in Consumer Lending</li>
</ul>
