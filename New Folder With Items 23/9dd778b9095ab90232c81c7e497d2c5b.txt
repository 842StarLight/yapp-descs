<h2>Automotive Accounting Office Administrator/Clerk</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Olathe Kia is looking for a Accounting Office Administrator/Clerk to join our busy dealership. This is a great opportunity for someone with some dealership experience and AP/AR experience that wants to learn and grow in an accounting role.</p>
<p>The Office Administrator/Clerk position is responsible for the day-to-day accounting and office administration functions.</p>
<p>Job Responsibilities include but are not limited to:</p>
<ul><li>Management of inventories, receivables and other controlled accounts</li></ul>
<ul><li>Reconciliation and disbursement of Accounts Payable</li></ul>
<ul><li>Process vehicle tag and title paperwork</li></ul>
<ul><li>Compliance with all title laws and filing requirements</li></ul>
<ul><li>Track, record and file vehicle sales deals</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>Troubleshoot and determine resolution for issues specific to titles, odometer discrepancies, power of attorney, etc.</li>
<li>Other daily accounting and office administration functions as needed</li>
</ul>
<p>The ideal candidate must have a strong desire to succeed.<br>Additional preferred requirements include:</p>
<ul><li>ADP/ADP Drive Experience</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experience in Dealership Accounts Payable/Receivable</li></ul>
<ul><li>Billing/Tag &amp; Title experience</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must have strong communications, customer service, attention to detail and organizational skills.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Proficiency with Microsoft Office applications, or the equivalent, is expected and familiarity with additional accounting software programs is valued.</li></ul>
<p>Full time M-F position with excellent benefits.<br>Please reply to this job opening with a Resume.<br>Seeking qualified applicants with Automotive Industry Office Experience</p>
