About the Automotive Industry
<br>More than 45 Million used cars are bought and sold annually in the US and over 90% are sold to consumers through dealers. As one of most highly considered and expensive purchases made by consumers over the course of a lifetime, the $2 Trillion Global Automotive Market is one of the largest yet least modernized industries in the US.

About the Role
<br>Autolist is looking for a results-driven Inside Sales professional to assist in growing our market share by increasing the dealer customer base subscribing to our platform. You will be part of a fast pace entrepreneurial culture - we are growing extremely fast and you’ll be getting in on the ground floor as we continue to grow our team rapidly. You will be collaborating with Marketing, Product and Dealer Success teams and will work directly with Sales Leadership to refine our sales efforts as we continue to scale. This is a customer facing front line role at Autolist, directly responsible for building relationships with prospective customers, demonstrating our product, signing up new customers and achieving revenue targets.
<br><br>
Who You Are
<br>You have the profile of a 'hunter' and love searching for the right decision makers, making connections, and winning new business. You are excited to connect with new prospects and learn about them, surface problems they face and help them meet challenges head on. You are naturally curious and unafraid to ask questions, a tinkerer, and are always learning to help drive your success. You work and live in the Bay Area because technology and the digital world excites you and you like to use technology to help you succeed.
