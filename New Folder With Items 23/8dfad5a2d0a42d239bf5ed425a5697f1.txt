<h2>Bartender - Bogart's</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Job Summary:</p>
<p><b>
The Role:</b></p>
<ul><li>
Serve alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages and food to guests in a music hall atmosphere with a cheerful attitude and in an efficient, professional manner.</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Interact with guests in a natural, friendly demeanor</li>
<li>
Cultivate regular guests and repeat business</li>
<li>
Successfully complete a pour test prior to every shift, where applicable</li>
<li>
Suggestively sell both beverages and menu items to guests at the bar</li>
<li>
Deliver food items and beverage orders promptly</li>
<li>
Prioritize and organize drink orders</li>
<li>
Consistently prepare beverages according to standard recipes and venue specifications, abide by all control systems</li>
<li>
Record any equipment failure and/or safety hazards in bar log</li>
<li>
Recognize when guests are becoming too intoxicated and refuse further service of alcohol in a courteous and safety-minded manner, bringing it to the attention of a manager</li>
<li>
Ensure responsible alcohol service per local ordinance alcohol awareness training and HOB alcohol policies</li>
<li>
Request proper identification for anyone appearing under 30 years of age</li>
<li>
Maintain HOB safety and sanitation standards</li>
<li>
Perform Opening/Running/Closing duties according to HOB policy</li>
<li>
Prepare guest checks (on point of sales terminal) and process payment according to HOB policy</li>
<li>
Maintain bartender bank</li>
<li>
Perform cash pull / check out procedures</li>
<li>
Maintain appropriate Comp and Spill checks</li>
<li>
Required to occasionally work Special Events that are scheduled throughout the venue</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Qualifications:</b></p>
<p><b><i>
Required:
</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Food handler card, if State applicable</li>
<li>
Responsible Alcohol Awareness Training Certification or Equivalent</li>
<li>
Ability to work late hours</li>
<li>
Flexible schedule</li>
<li>
Cash handling experience</li>
<li>
Must be able to lift or move up to 25 lbs using proper lifting techniques</li>
<li>
Skill in interpersonal communication and teamwork</li>
<li>
Physically able to pass a pour test and a mixology drill (timed)</li>
<li>
Ability to deal with intoxicated guests in a positive and safety minded manner</li>
<li>
Tolerance of all cultures, music and art forms</li>
<li>
Responsible Alcohol Awareness Training Certification or Equivalent</li>
</ul>
