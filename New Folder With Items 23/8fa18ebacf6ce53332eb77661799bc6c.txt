<h2>Cloud Customer Success Manager (Sales Engineer)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Identify and develop sales opportunities leveraging strong technical engineering background to promote expanded sales and adoption of VMware’s cloud computing solutions. Work independently in an area of specialization to analyze customer technical requirements and strategic IT goals and promote sales opportunities for enterprise Software-as-a-Service customers. Develop and/or customize innovative solutions for complex problems in cloud computing environments. Confer with customers and engineers to assess equipment needs, determine system requirements, and drive critical issues to resolution. Leverage extensive technical knowledge to sell products, solutions and services and support installation, proof of concept, use and training as required. Map and modify product configurations to customer requirements and track progress of Customer Success Plans. Serve as technical product expert and technical lead on major projects and proactively monitor project health. Prepare and deliver technical presentations to demonstrate the value proposition of assigned products and solutions. Research and maintain knowledge of competing technologies and vendors in order to promote and sell VMware solutions. Provide technical leadership to junior developers/sales engineers. Act independently to determine methods and procedures or special assignments. In connection with the above duties, apply knowledge and/or skills of Mobile Device Management (MDM); Application Virtualization; Application Packaging; ITSM/ITIL Operations; and IT Monitoring. Up to 30% domestic travel required.</p>
