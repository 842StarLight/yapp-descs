<h2>New Home Sales Advisor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Ashton Woods is one of the nation’s largest private homebuilding companies, blazing new trails in design and personalization to build homes as unique as the people who live in them. Collaborating with homeowners for over 25 years, the company and its team of world-renowned designers look beyond the conventional to draw inspiration from unexpected sources, resulting in exceptional design in every Ashton Woods home. One of America’s most Trusted Builders for the fourth consecutive year, Ashton Woods has been ranked among the top three for Most Trusted Builder in America according to the Lifestory Research Most Trusted Builder in America Study.</p>
<div>
<p>Ashton Woods Homes has emerged as a homebuilder of choice, driven by a strong entrepreneurial spirit and commitment to world-class product design and customer satisfaction. The company is focused on creating an innovative &amp; differentiated customer experience via the web, digital media and CRM that pioneers new ground and surprises-and-delights our customers.</p>
</div>
<div>
<p>We are looking for a New Home Advisor to join our Austin division. The primary role of the New Home Advisor is to match prospective home buyers with Ashton Woods communities and schedule meetings with our Community Sales Managers. By serving the role of trusted guide throughout the purchase process and identifying the communities which most closely align to the prospect’s needs, the New Home Advisor plays a pivotal role in driving traffic to the sales office and ultimately in driving sales for the division.</p>
</div>
