<p><b>Residential Construction Field Manager</b></p>
<p><b>Vanir Installed Sales, LLC</b></p>
<p>*<br>Who we are:</p>
<p>Vanir Installed Sales is a construction management company which specializes in the installation of exterior siding. Based in the Raleigh-Durham, North Carolina area, our clients include local, regional and national homebuilders as well as a rapidly-expanding client base of commercial contractors.<br>*<br>*<br>We’re a well-financed startup company with 3 years of operational experience in central and eastern North Carolina. Backed by our team of knowledgeable, highly-qualified and enthusiastic construction management professionals, we’ve rapidly grown our organization into a group of 25 full-time staff members, a handful of temporary employees and partnerships with dozens of vendors and labor crews. Our team members are our greatest resource—fostering and maintaining a friendly, open and supportive work environment is critical to our success. We recognize your input will shape the future of our company, and for that reason we’re dedicated to enabling your success. We’re looking for someone driven and energetic who shares these values and recognizes the room for growth as we expand into neighboring markets in North Carolina, South Carolina and Virginia.<br>*<br>*<br>The Field Manager is responsible for ensuring the quality, speed, and safety of the installations. This includes coordinating subcontractors and material deliveries/pickups.<br>*<br>*<br>Who we’re looking for:</p>
<ul><li>Experienced in Construction: Our team is fast-paced and has a reputation for providing quality installations. Our new team member should already have some experience in construction in order to hit the ground running.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Skilled Communicator: You will be expected to communicate the status of your job sites clearly to our customers as well as be in frequent contact with the subcontractors completing the installations. Additionally, the Field Manager must keep the office staff constantly updated on the activity in the field through phone calls, emails, and our software: BuilderTrend.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Tech-Savvy: Field Managers are given cell phones, laptops, and mobile printers to help them stay organized in the field as well as communicate with our customers and office staff. Being able to use this technology effectively is essential to the Field Manager’s success.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Self-Starter: You will be responsible for your own schedule and are expected to be capable of prioritizing your tasks and completing your jobs without much interference from a superior.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Respectful of Diversity: Many of our subcontractors are Hispanic and typically speak English as a second language. Many also have cultural perspectives that differ from the culture of the American South-East. A respect for diverse perspectives will be beneficial to working effectively with the subcontractors.</li></ul>
<p>*<br>*<br>Specific responsibilities include:</p>
<ul><li>Ensuring the correct material has been delivered to the jobsites</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ordering and transporting additional material as needed</li></ul>
<ul><li>Inspecting the jobs continuously for quality and coach subcontractors on what aspects they must improve upon</li></ul>
<ul><li>Enforcing all safety standards on the jobsites</li></ul>
<ul><li>Proactively identifying which houses in the subdivision(s) will be ready for siding and communicating that information to the warehouse and office staff</li></ul>
<ul><li>Scheduling the subcontractor teams to perform the installations</li></ul>
<ul><li>Updating the status of the jobs in BuilderTrend</li></ul>
<ul><li>Communicating the status of the jobs with the customer</li></ul>
