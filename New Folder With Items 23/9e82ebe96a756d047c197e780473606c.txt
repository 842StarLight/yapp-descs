<h2>Sr. Manager, Inside Sales</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>The Sales Manager is a leadership-level position that is responsible for managing and growing the Company's sales pipeline and leading, coaching and managing a team to successfully deliver on their respective annual sales goals, and achieving high customer satisfaction.<br>
<ul>
<li>Delivering a sales capability that drives customer affinity and scalable, sustainable growth.</li>
<li>Managing a team of 8-20 sales representatives. This team consists of Account Managers and Sales Executives selling solutions and providing account management across North America.</li>
<li>Increasing product revenue market share within the Independent Agent Insurance markets.</li>
<li>Managing and coordinating the tactical and strategic direction of sales representatives engaged in promoting and selling solutions and account management.</li>
<li>Strong mentoring and adherence to set Vertafore best practices and methodologies.</li>
<li>Recommends changes to current sales techniques or procedures based on team performance and new selling techniques.</li>
<li>Achieving sales at or above stated quota and goals.</li>
<li>Monitoring, supporting and coaching sales representatives with cold calling, prospecting, web-ex sales presentations and demonstrations, follow-up calls and closings.</li>
<li>Responsible for territory value growth and customer satisfaction within assigned territory.</li>
</ul>
</div>
<p><b>Skills &amp; Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Successfully mastered the following skills: Sales Management, aggressive cold/warm calling, prospecting, product demonstration and closing.</li>
<li>At least 3 years of related experience with at least 1 year of management experience</li>
<li>Proven success in professional sales, preferably in enterprise solutions selling within the Financial Services industry, which includes insurance.</li>
<li>Operational knowledge of various Windows based application programs and current Internet technologies.</li>
<li>Self motivated, ability to work independently and motivate a team.</li>
<li>Management of pipelines, territories, forecasting and processes.</li>
<li>Experience with coaching and mentoring.</li>
</ul>
</div>
