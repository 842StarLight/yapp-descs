<h2>Global Category Manager - Professional Services</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Bristol-Myers Squibb is a global Biopharma company committed to a single mission: to discover, develop, and deliver innovative medicines focused on helping millions of patients around the world in disease areas such as oncology, cardiovascular, immunoscience and fibrosis.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Join us and make a difference. We hire the best people and provide them with a work environment that places a premium on diversity, integrity, collaboration and personal development. Through a culture of inclusion, we create a better, more productive work environment. We believe that the diverse experiences and perspectives of all our employees help to drive innovation and transformative business results.
</p>
<p>This role is accountable for the successful development and execution of sourcing strategies supporting and enabling the objectives and goals of Bristol-Myers Squibb (BMS):
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Collaborate with GP SLT and serve as a trusted advisor to leaders across the enterprise for their external talent needs Develop and maintain category strategy plans annually to ensure we drive to high usage of preferred suppliers and continue to bring strong procurement capabilities to our stakeholders Orchestrate the voices of applicable subject matter experts within Finance, Legal, Audit, Treasury, and other groups. Identify and manage market, supply, financial, compliance, legal and other forms of risk. Ensure compliance with the letter and the spirit of BMS policies. Maximize the value BMS receives from suppliers. Facilitate the continuous improvement of sourcing strategies. Drive supplier relationship management efforts across Professional Services firms
</p>
<p></p>
<p>This role is accountable for the successful development and execution of sourcing strategies in an inclusive manner, by a matrixed team of sourcing professionals from different geographic, ethnic, cultural, personal, and professional backgrounds. This role develops awareness of Global Procurement’s strategies and ensures that the teams execute sourcing projects in a manner consistent with our strategies and consistent with BMS’ standards of business conduct and ethical behavior. This role is responsible for planning activities including the development and maintenance of a 3-year strategic sourcing plan with associated savings projections and for anticipating demand for external Talent services and projecting associated resource needs. This role brings external perspectives and insights aligned to business objectives and maintains an awareness of market developments. This role is responsible for directly leading complex business transactions, negotiating associated contracts and resolving complex contract and service issues with suppliers. This role will execute a supplier relationship management program as needed.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>This role reports to the Director of HR and Professional Services
</p>
<p></p>

</p>
<ul>
<li>B.S. / B. A.
</li>

</li>

</li>
<li>An understanding of the development of statements of work (time and material, fixed fee, milestones, deliverables and acceptance criteria);
</li>
<li>An understanding of professional services firms approach and value proposition
</li>
<li>Experience leading/influencing individuals/teams and establishing performance expectations;
</li>
<li>Ability to effectively communicate with a diverse audience using a variety of mediums;
</li>
<li>Project management skills and experience;
</li>
<li>Procurement/sourcing process knowledge;
</li>
<li>An understanding of the end-to-end procurement process and operations (desirable);
</li>
</ul>
<p>An understanding of financial valuation techniques and concepts including management of working capital, cash flow, accrual accounting and revenue recognition (desirable).
</p>
