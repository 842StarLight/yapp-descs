<h2>Senior Copywriter (Scottsdale, AZ)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Identity Studios, an Arizona-based B2B marketing agency serving high-tech clients across the United States, is currently seeking to add a Senior Copywriter to its team.</p>
<p>
The Senior Copywriter’s primary responsibility is to provide full copy support for assigned agency projects, as well as internal writing and editing for company marketing, sales and social media initiatives. Job responsibilities will include researching and developing material for multiple technology clients and business projects, participation in strategic development and brainstorming sessions, and writing for a broad spectrum of media, ranging from print and direct mail to datasheets, articles, blog posts, web copy and social media.</p>
<p>
———————————————</p>
<p><b>
Please Note:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
You must have 3-5 years of copywriting experience in B2B sales and marketing environments</li>
<li>Experience working in the technology industry or with technology clients is highly desired</li>
<li>Must have strength writing for both the web and print</li>
<li>Include salary requirements with your resume</li>
<li>Include samples of your 3 favorite writing/campaign projects</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Duties &amp; Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Writes and edits engaging, relevant, original copy</li>
<li>Performs independent research and works with a variety of materials to develop a range of promotional and informational pieces</li>
<li>Works as part of agency team to achieve client objectives through strategically sound concepts and impactful message strategies</li>
<li>Maintains consistently high standards of writing with minimal supervision</li>
<li>Uses presentation skills to enhance the agency-client relationship</li>
<li>Proactively monitors the progress of assigned projects through each phase of development, i.e., research, draft, layout, and production</li>
<li>Participates in client-agency meetings, when appropriate</li>
<li>Assesses day to day copywriting needs and alerts supervisor to coverage gaps within the agency</li>
<li>Works with our telemarketing team to develop calling scripts</li>
<li>Works to stay current with the B2B technology industry, its key players and trends</li>
<li>Mentors, trains and inspires less experienced writers and other team members</li>
<li>Manages outsourced/freelance copywriting resources and deadlines as needed based on workload</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Desired Skills &amp; Experience:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
3-5 years of copywriting experience in B2B sales and marketing environments, including print, interactive, video, social media</li>
<li>Established editing and proofreading skills</li>
<li>2-3 years experience in the technology industry, including working knowledge of key terminology, marketing and sales best practices, product marketing, vertical markets, etc.</li>
<li>Ability to write messaging to multiple levels of audiences, from system administrators to CXOs</li>
<li>Ability to effectively translate complex product information into a format that creates meaning for the prospect/end user so that the communication drives the intended action</li>
<li>Proven ability to manage and organize multiple tasks and meet deadlines</li>
<li>Ability to write both long, narrative marketing copy, as well as short, catchy campaign copy</li>
<li>Familiarity with current advertising and design trends, and practical knowledge of Internet and multimedia technology</li>
<li>A strong portfolio of well written work and concepts</li>
<li>Must be detail- and deadline-oriented and a quick study in new accountsMust be able to participate articulately in internal and client-facing meetings and presentations</li>
</ul>
<p>Please send resume, cover letter and salary requirements to hr@identitystudios.biz.</p>
<p>
The chosen candidate will receive a base salary dependent on experience and track record. Health and life style benefits are also available, in addition to a 401(k).</p>
