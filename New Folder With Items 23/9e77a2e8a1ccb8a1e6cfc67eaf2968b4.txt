<h2>Patient Engagement Marketing Specialist - Medical Groups</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Symetria Recovery® </b>is an innovative behavioral health company focused on improving substance abuse treatment outcomes and strengthening communities by increasing awareness, advocacy, and implementing best practices in the coordination of patient care. Our unique blend of clinical vision and medical innovation is transforming the face of substance abuse therapy to more effectively address the increasing challenges in substance abuse disorder treatment today.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Symetria Recovery is seeking a Patient Engagement Marketing Specialist to focus his/her efforts on the medical group segment. In this role, you will help develop and implement marketing and sales strategies to identify prospective targets or sources of patient referrals within the medical group segment. This is an outside sales position, requiring travel to various locations within a defined territory to initiate contact, develop and maintain relationships, and maximize referral potential. You will assist in the development and execution of strategic plans aimed at increasing awareness and referrals to Symetria’s outpatient and medication assisted treatment facilities, while always promoting Symetria’s services and reputation in a professional and inventive manner.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>As a member of our Illinois Patient Engagement Team, you will contribute to team targets and goals focused on driving patient referrals to Symetria’s facilities and growing patient census. Your performance and compensation will be measured in part by your ability to help the team meet its goals as well as organizational goals.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>As our internal expert, you will be assisting the Regional Director of Patient Engagement Marketing in performing medical group Segment market analysis and developing corresponding strategies to develop business from within this discreet Segment, throughout the Chicagoland area. In addition, you will manage medical groups and ensure these accounts are provided exceptional customer service.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Duties and Responsibilities include but are not limited to:</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Thorough understanding and firm knowledge of medical group setting and ability to use the knowledge and understanding to build relationships and increase patient referrals.</li>
<li>Research and analyze medical group trends, preferences, and needs.</li>
<li>Create and sustain positive impression and relationships with professionals in the medical group segment; including professional demeanor, neat physical appearance and professional verbal delivery of our agency messages and methods.</li>
<li>Ability to plan and organize daily activities to optimize the use of time and maximize the achievement of organization’s strategic goals for this segment.</li>
<li>Works collaboratively with team and other departments to meet all organization’s strategic goals.</li>
<li>Focus on medical group segment targets to meet or exceed organization’s strategic goals.</li>
<li>Ability to demonstrate a strong and clear understanding of the organization’s structure, method and medication assisted treatment (MAT) products and utilize this information to increase patient referrals from medical group targets.</li>
<li>Assist in design and implementation of successful marketing campaigns for medical group segment.</li>
<li>Assist in development and maintenance of tracking systems for ground marketing activities.</li>
<li>Ability to record and analyze progress, areas of opportunity, and needs with Regional Director.</li>
<li>Identify and analyze medical group segment trends, areas of opportunity and needs.</li>
<li>Prepare reports by collecting and analyzing data as directed by management.</li>
<li>Collaborate with the Marketing Director and advertising/ media team to produce promotional materials as needed.</li>
<li>Organize promotional activities to enhance professional engagement with medical group segment.</li>
<li>Exceptional verbal and written communication skills including but not limited to accurate and timely reporting, documentation of activities, response to emails, phone calls or other messages.</li>
<li>Must have excellent computer skills, including MS Office, Excel, and other forms of technology.</li>
<li>Ability to work within Salesforce or similar software.</li>
<li>Strong attention to detail.</li>
<li>Must be motivated and able to work with minimal supervision.</li>
<li>Maintain and protect all company provided equipment.</li>
<li>Proven work experience as a Marketing Coordinator, Outreach and Marketing Coordinator, or like role.</li>
<li>Must be willing to adhere to all organization compliance policies and procedures as written or communicated.</li>
<br>
</ul>
