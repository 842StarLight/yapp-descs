<br><br>
A Senior Digital Designer to design and build digital experiences for Rue La La. We're looking for someone with the desire to be a part of something big, the training and foundation to create outstanding work daily, a sophisticated aesthetic sense, and the interest to learn and succeed in a dynamic retail fashion environment. You'll be joining a group that prides itself on the excellence of everything they do, that respects and protects the integrity of the Rue La La brand and the world-class brands we feature, all while having fun together doing it. This position reports to the Senior Design Manager.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Maintain our brand style guidelines and ensure design consistency across projects through edits and approvals of Designer and Associate Designer work.</li>
<li>Produce, evolve and consistently innovate the design treatments, templates and visual content of our site and emails.</li>
<li>Manage Associate Designer and Designer with focus on developing their craft and career growth.</li>
<li>Brainstorm and collaboratively create new engaging concepts for our Members to increase brand affinity, visitation, and sales.</li>
<li>Develop and maintain requirements, documentation and processes for all digital projects.</li>
<li>Work collaboratively and constructively on each project with others on the team including UX Designers, Project Managers, Product Managers, Art Directors, Designers, Copywriters, Developers, Marketing, etc.</li>
<li>Produce digital assets as appropriate for new templates and emails</li>
<li>Partner with UX Designers, Product Managers and Marketing on QA and testing</li>
<li>Work with Information Technology staff to deliver final assets into production environment</li>
<li>Develop partnerships with Marketing and Information Technology team and be the go-to person for all online projects.</li>
<li>Develop designs in a fast-paced environment without compromising quality</li>
<li>Receive and give constructive feedback on each project</li>
</ul>
<b>This position requires:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Design chops - we're looking for someone with a passion for and mastery of design in the digital and social space, and the desire to both direct and be hands-on.</li>
<li>Sophisticated sense of color</li>
<li>Great understanding of current online design best practices such as Responsive Web Design and Email Templates.</li>
<li>Strong self-motivation and drive to succeed</li>
<li>Excellent attention to detail</li>
<li>Ability to manage and meet deadlines</li>
<li>Ability to manage, lead, and mentor Associate Digital Designers</li>
<li>Excellent collaboration, communication and interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Strong skills with the entire Adobe CS5 suite (InDesign, Photoshop, Illustrator, Bridge), and a deep understanding of CSS/HTML.</li>
<li>Experience creating and optimizing graphics for the web</li>
<li>3+ years of relevant work experience as a designer in an agency or in-house creative group with a focus on online projects.</li>
<li>Desire to learn new things and keep abreast of the world of fashion, style, design, retailing, social media and ecommerce</li>
<li>A strong portfolio showing both training and potential</li>
<li>BA in Graphic Design with educational training in typography</li>
</ul>
We can't wait to hear from you, so please submit your resume and online or pdf portfolio for consideration. Submissions without portfolios will not be considered.
