<h2>Business Development Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Lighter Inc, is a cutting edge digital health technology company that is disrupting the healthcare delivery market. We empower healthcare providers to leverage personalized meal planning and tracking software to prescribe food as medicine to prevent and reverse chronic disease. Lighter has been featured in Forbes, is a MassChallenge Digital Health-tech finalist, and works with bad-ass world influencers. We hire people that are passionate about their work and want to solve real problems.</p>
<p>
The Business Development Representative (BDR) is a sales role that is tasked with uncovering opportunities and building pipeline for our Director of Partnerships and LighterPRO product. We're growing our team and need self-motivated people to join Lighter Inc. This individual should be excited to take on new challenges, meet aggressive goals and work both independently and with a small, motivated team. We are seeking hard-working and mission-driven individuals who want to drive growth and help build a company while accelerating their career.</p>
<p>
The BDR role will have sales training directly from leadership and be expected to hit immediate pipeline targets as well as prepare for and grow into a larger role. This is a very unique and exciting role for a young hungry professional to join an exciting health and wellness technology company with a sales team based out of NYC.</p>
<p><b>
About You:
</b></p>
<ul><li>Quick learner, highly inquisitive, and emotionally intelligent.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Bring a lot of energy, enthusiasm, and ambition to life with a strong desire to get better.</li></ul>
<ul><li>High acumen for and desire to learn sales.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Coachable, natural people person, team player, and seeking professional growth.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong record of success.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Exceptional verbal and written communications skills.</li></ul>
<ul><li>1-3 years sales/BD experience, particularly in software / SMB markets. Experience using SFDC is a bonus.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Bachelor's degree in business, exercise science, nutrition, or equivalent experience</li></ul>
<ul><li>Personal interest in health, fitness, food as medicine, or related industry.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong critical thinking skills and the ability to deliver independently</li></ul>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul><li>Act as the front line for Lighter Inc. by engaging with prospective customers and work directly with the Director of Partnerships to close deals.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Sourcing pipeline via direct outreach (phone and email) based on our prospect database and your own research.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Communicate professionally and build relationships with C-level and VP level contacts at companies across the health and wellness industry.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Meet and exceed set monthly opportunity generation goals.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Adapt quickly to our fast-paced, dynamic organization, and continue to show improvements.</li></ul>
