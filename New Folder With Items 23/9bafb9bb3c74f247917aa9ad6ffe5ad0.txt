<h2>Process Control Technician</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>ME Elecmetal is a globally competitive supplier, recognized by its excellence and leadership in the delivery of integral solutions which add value to the mining processes and to other target markets. Our fast-paced production environment is recruiting for a talented Process Control Technician in its Duluth, MN foundry.</p>
<p><b>Process Control Technician: </b> The position will serve as a liaison between quality and other departments while taking a proactive approach to process procedures and initiating changes to improve quality and consistency. The position will involve the development of new processes and products; reviewing and documenting the effects throughout production and evaluating the results. A candidate must be able to recognize issues and variations in new and existing work and make recommendations for resolution. Responsibilities will include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Review new and existing patterns and prepare a sample packet detailing specific processing procedures when needed and enter these details into the Management Information System.</li>
<li>Compile the results of samples and obtain releases for all work prior to production runs. This includes review of production and inspection results.</li>
<li>Produce and maintain Finishing/Inspection process sheets in conjunction with the Sales department for use in the proper processing of production castings.</li>
<li>Act as a liaison between and a consultant to the Production, Pattern and Marketing Departments.</li>
<li>Review non-conforming products for appropriate corrective and preventive actions.</li>
<li>Provide assistance to various departments in analyzing and recommending corrective action for casting or production related problems.</li>
<li>Work as assigned with individual production departments to establish and maintain a set of standard operating procedures. Assist in training of employees to procedures.</li>
<li>Supervisor coverage as assigned in production or other departments.</li>
<li>Work as assigned on development and implementation of projects involving process improvement, cost reduction or research and product development.</li>
<li>Conduct and update internal audits and assist in auditing compliance to the ISO 9001 standards.</li>
<li>Assist with customer audits or first article inspections as assigned.</li>
<li>Perform required non-destructive testing including ultrasonic, magnetic particle and dye penetrant testing for samples, production inspection and plant equipment.</li>
<li>Maintain and adhere to ISO standards, including but not limited to the Quality Policy Statement and the Quality Control Procedures Manual.</li>
</ul>
<p>Position Qualifications</p>
<p>This position must have demonstrated work experience or education in process or quality control or a closely related field. Candidate must be an effective communicator, both written and verbal, at all levels of the organization. The incumbent must be computer literate in word-processing, spreadsheets and other computer related software.</p>
<p>Salary dependent on qualifications. Must pass a pre-employment physical, background check and a drug test. Benefit package includes medical &amp; dental insurance, life insurance, pension &amp; 401k and tuition reimbursement.</p>
<p><b>All qualified candidates must be: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>committed to creating a safe work environment</li>
<li>adhere to high performance standards</li>
<li>able to work in a hot/cold environment</li>
<li>committed to producing high quality products</li>
<li>willing to work off-shift</li>
<li>willing to work in an environment that expects excellence.</li>
</ul>
