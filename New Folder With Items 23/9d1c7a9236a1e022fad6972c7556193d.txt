<h2>Media Operations Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>ConsumerTrack™ is unique in the digital marketing and media industry - we combine marketing, digital, content and fintech. Our performance based approach increases brand awareness and generates targeted audience engagement on our internal web properties and partner sites.</p>
<p>
Learn More About What We Do</p>
<p>
ConsumerTrack is looking for a media operations manager to own inventory management with the goal to maximize client budget fulfillment across owned and operated (O&amp;O) properties and partner placements. This role will work with the partnerships and media teams to manage publisher relationships, supporting through publisher onboarding, inventory management, and campaign performance.</p>
<p>
Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Manage Media Ops team to support the media launch &amp; lifecycle (Salesforce updates, ad hoc reports for publishers, ad unit audits, invoicing, etc).
</li>
<li>Own inventory management (ad units on O&amp;O and external sites) &amp; yield management on inventory.
</li>
<li>Management of publisher supply/inventory (banner impressions) with the market demand, at the best price (CPA/RPM) while ensuring highest possible fill rates.
</li>
<li>Lead in strategizing and identifying new ways to maximize ad revenue and give recommendations to Media, Partnerships, and Sales teams to hit goal.
</li>
<li>Ensure maximum budget fulfillment (Media Plans) and direct Sales on what we can fulfill.
</li>
<li>Be the subject matter expert for product performance optimization and measurement from a publisher lens.
</li>
<li>Develop Salesforce to ensure publisher and campaign records reflect the most accurate and up-to-date information.
</li>
<li>Support ad unit specs and mocks for (O&amp;O) properties and partner placements.
</li>
<li>Lead client performance measures and provide performance updates to internal teams (revenue, impressions, gross profit margins by publisher/campaigns).
</li>
<li>Utilize experience to develop solutions and document internal procedures (workflow, requirements, QA process) regarding inventory management.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>5+ years’ experience in a yield optimization, inventory management, sales operations, or media/publisher operations role. Digital experience is a MUST.
</li>
<li>3+ years’ experience managing a team and delivering results
</li>
<li>2+ years’ analytical experience in a role requiring heavy data analysis.
</li>
<li>Experience developing processes and improving tool management for large teams, and rolling out these tools at scale
</li>
<li>Experience optimizing CPA, CPC, CPM campaigns with post-click quality metrics
</li>
<li>Proven business sense and problem-solving, critical thinking, project management, and analytical skills
</li>
<li>Ability to answer Publisher and Partner questions and prepare recommendations that proactively address any concerns
</li>
<li>In-depth knowledge of digital advertising sales and extensive experience in SalesForce a must
</li>
<li>Ability to initiate and drive projects to completion with minimal guidance
</li>
<li>Excellent written, verbal, and interpersonal skills
</li>
<li>Comfortable dealing with changing environments and making decisions for the team
</li>
<li>Able to work independently as well as collaboratively within a team environment, handling multiple projects, and meeting tight deadlines under pressure
</li>
</ul>
