<p><b>Job Description</b></p>
<p>Unitrends, a Kaseya company, is rapidly expanding and is looking to add a Partner Marketing Manager with a passion for channel marketing to assist in the development, management, and execution of channel initiatives in support of revenue targets for North America. The position requires an understanding of marketing concepts, marketing program development and the ability to track and measure the impact of those programs and campaigns.</p>
<p><b>Roles and Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Build and maintain relationships with Unitrends sales teams, regional and national channel partners, customers and third-party vendors</li>
<li>Execute multi-touch demand programs that build partner pipeline to meet quarterly goals</li>
<li>Coordinate and manage partner events (ie, seminars, tradeshows, lunch and learns, etc)</li>
<li>Monitor and track all marketing activities to maximizing ROI of marketing spend</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Success Metrics</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Number of campaigns delivered and activated in region</li>
<li>Percentage of partners engaged and executing partner-ready campaigns</li>
<li>Contribution to channel sourced pipeline</li>
<li>Deals registered from channel marketing influenced programs</li>
<li>Demand program performance/ROI, MDF, co-operative funds or volume incentive utilization rate</li>
</ul>
<p><b>The ideal candidate has</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Experience in corporate marketing, with at least 3 years working directly with, or at, a Regional or National Partner</li>
<li>Ability to execute channel programs and tools (MDF, Partner Portals, etc.)</li>
<li>Hands on experience with Salesforce</li>
<li>An energetic and willing attitude; Must be a self-starter, not afraid to ask questions</li>
<li>Highly analytical and creative thinker, with an ability to use data to inform decisions.</li>
<li>Excellent writer and communicator (in both written and verbal form); Comfortable presenting to peers and executives</li>
<li>Effective project manager with the ability to work under deadlines and manage multiple projects simultaneously</li>
<li>Willing to be humble and learn</li>
</ul>
