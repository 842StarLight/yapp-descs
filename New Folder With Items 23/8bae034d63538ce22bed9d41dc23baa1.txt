<h2>AV SYSTEMS PROGRAMMER</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Job Summary: </b></p>
<p><b>Batts Audio, Video &amp; Lighting, Inc.</b> designs, installs, and services commercial sound, video, lighting, and rigging systems for businesses, churches, education, entertainment, government, and performing arts centers. Established in 1997 with its principal office in Denison, Texas, Batts AVL services a 250-mile radius of Denison including much of Texas and Oklahoma while maintaining a national online sales presence.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities and Duties</b></p>
<p><b>Description: </b></p>
<p>Batts AVL, Inc. is seeking a systems designer and programmer for AV control systems that includes manufacturers like AMX, Strand Lighting, BSS, and Interactive Technologies. Prior experience with designing audio, video, and lighting systems as well as programming control systems including control system GUI's is desired. Also desired is the ability to design and program graphics using the following software: PhotoShop, HTML, .NET, and C++. Seeking candidates with leadership ability, high standards for personal integrity, excellent work ethic, professional presentation, and good communication skills.</p>
<p><b>Qualifications and Skills</b></p>
<p><b>Qualifications: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to evaluate client requirements and provide comprehensive designs of AV systems for projects of $200k or less</li>
<li>Knowledge of standard software for DSP set-up, switcher set-up, system integration, etc.</li>
<li>Ability to troubleshoot audio, video, lighting, and control system problems</li>
<li>Strong computer skills (Microsoft Office, Word, Excel, PowerPoint)</li>
<li>High standards for personal integrity, work ethic, professional presentation, and quality of work</li>
<li>Ability to program in C++, visual basic, HTML</li>
<li>Personal interest in acoustics, electronics, music, sound, lighting, video, and/or rigging systems desired</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Benefits</b></p>
<p><b>Compensation &amp; Benefits: </b></p>
<p>Batts AVL offers competitive salary and benefits including medical, paid vacation, paid holidays, and SIMPLE IRA w/ matching, as well as opportunities for advancement.</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
