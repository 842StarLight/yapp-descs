<h2>Media Sales Rep</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Clarion UX is a rapidly growing trade show management company with a long history of success in the United States. Headquartered, in Trumbull, CT Clarion UX has offices in Atlanta, GA, Boca Raton, FL, Fairlawn, NJ, Tulsa, OK and Tacoma, WA. If you are looking to advance your career working among forward-thinking professionals in a fast-paced, entrepreneurial environment built on service and excellence, Clarion UX could be the right place for you.</p>
<p>
Fire &amp; Rescue Group is a division of Clarion UX, owned by Clarion Events. Our Fire &amp; Rescue Group is looking to expand its sales team in our Atlanta, GA office. We are seeking a Media Sales Rep who will oversee the South Eastern US region and exceed sales targets, establish and build relationships.To be successful in a Media Sales Rep role with the Fire &amp; Rescue Group, the candidate must be motivated to prospect and generate leads, close sales, provide superior customer support and deliver great results for our market-leading events.</p>
<p><b>
In general, the Media Sales Rep is accountable to:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Consult with marketing and advertising personnel to explore customers' needs</li>
<li>
Develop a relationship with customers to ensure sales, marketing successes, marketing problems and, fears are being communicated</li>
<li>
Discuss and create print/digital media solutions for customers' sales, marketing problems, and fears</li>
<li>
Develop advertising proposals combining print and digital media products designed to solve customers' problems and generate interest in customers products and services</li>
<li>
Visit customers throughout the year in their location office(s) to investigate additional customers' needs, and develop media solutions to further meet those needs</li>
<li>
Visit customers prior to the beginning of their new fiscal year, to ensure monies are budgeted for our existing and/or new product launches</li>
<li>
Attend national and regional fire service trade shows, industry meetings, and conferences</li>
<li>
Weekly cold call new advertisers and/or potential customers</li>
<li>
Prospect for new business in competitors and/or from other industries media products</li>
<li>
Write follow-up e-mails following specific phone conversations, sales calls in customers/locations, and meetings at industry conferences</li>
<li>
Send copies of advertising proposals to Sales Managers</li>
<li>
Communicate with other territorial managers on split accounts</li>
<li>
Participate in weekly telephone conference calls with other members of sales team</li>
<li>
Provide weekly progress report of sales activities and orders</li>
</ul>
