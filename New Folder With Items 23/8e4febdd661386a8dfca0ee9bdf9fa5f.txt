<h2>Member Service Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>Are you looking to advance your career in a fun, supportive environment? Interested in developing relationship building skills while strengthening your financial knowledge?<br>
<br>
Currently a Teller ready for the next step?<br>
<br>
We want to talk to you!<br>
<br>
<b>Schedule:</b> Full time 40 hours. Schedule may vary, and you must be able to work branch hours.<br>
<br>
<b>The Role:</b><br>
Provide members and potential members with product and service information in a helpful efficient manner, build and retain member relationships, through service quality and member needs satisfaction; open and close member accounts, promote the Credit Union’s products and satisfy members’ financial needs through selling additional credit union services; originate loans and increase deposits by meeting identified member needs; cross sell other NECU products and services and ancillary products as appropriate.<br>
<br>
Adhere to and uphold Northeast Credit Union's Values and Service Standards.<br>
<br>
<b>A Day in the Life:</b><br>
<br>
Meet members' needs by providing outstanding service, problem resolution and needs based selling; interviewing loan applicants, closing loans, cross-selling, referring mortgages or prospecting and seeing the mortgage process from application to loan closing.<br>
<br>
Meet individual sales goals along with cross-selling credit union products and services which include Credit Life/Credit Disability; Debt Protection; GAP; and Route 66.<br>
<br>
Accurately processing all transactions, maintaining proper security and exercising good judgement utilizing thorough knowledge of products and services and related procedures.<br>
<br>
Adhere to operational controls including legal &amp; regulatory procedures to ensure the safety and security of members and Credit Union assets.<br>
