<h2>Accounting Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Goodwill Industries of Northern Illinois is seeking a full-time Accounting Associate. The Accounting Associate ensures that accounting records are maintained accurately and information is reported in a timely manner. Provides support to the entire organization with a focus on confidential work. The ability to manage details and follow processes is essential.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for processing bi-weekly payroll, resolving issues related to payroll, and providing strong customer service to employees with questions or requesting assistance regarding their pay.</li>
<li>Timecard maintenance for certain groups of employees.</li>
<li>Responsible for monthly sales tax filing and weekly pre-payments.</li>
<li>Completes general accounting tasks such as general ledger entries.</li>
<li>Processes accounts receivable and grant invoices and cash receipts.</li>
<li>Records and reviews e-commerce revenue.</li>
<li>Assists with review of processes for accuracy and compliance.</li>
<li>Assists with period end processing and audit preparations as needed.</li>
<li>Processes, tracks, and records fixed assets.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<b>Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>An Associates degree in Accounting or Finance is required.</li>
<li>Knowledge of GAAP, Sales Tax and other accounting related skills.</li>
<li>Functional use of Microsoft applications such as Word, Excel, and Outlook. Familiarity with Sage MAS accounting software is preferred.</li>
<li>Ability to focus on details and maintain high accuracy in work.</li>
<li>Maintains a high level of confidentiality.</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills.</li>
<li>Empathy for and ability to work with individuals with disabilities and barriers to employment.</li>
<li>Ability to take direction and effectively prioritize activities/tasks/initiatives.</li>
</ul>
Additional Information
