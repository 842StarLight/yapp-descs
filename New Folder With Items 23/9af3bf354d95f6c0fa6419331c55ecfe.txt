<div><b>Duties &amp; Responsibilities</b></div>
<div>
<p>JOB SUMMARY</p>
<p>
This position counsels clients on business development related matters.</p>
<p>
MAJOR DUTIES</p>
<ul><li>Counsels existing and prospective small business entrepreneurs on business-related issues, including marketing, management, finances, taxes, business planning, operations, bookkeeping, etc.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Reports details of counseling sessions; inputs details into the database.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Initiates and reports on quarterly follow-up contacts with clients.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Reviews and provides input on client business plans.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Reviews and analyzes client financial statements; provides guidance to established businesses related to the resolution of financial or accounting issues.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Develops marketing materials in support of center programs and services; prepares fliers and newsletters; provides program and event information on the state website.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Conducts market research.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Develops and instructs workshops.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Completes required continuing education training.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Performs related duties.</li></ul>
<p>
KNOWLEDGE REQUIRED BY THE POSITION</p>
<ul><li>Knowledge of college policies and procedures.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Knowledge of small business development principles.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Knowledge of business plan development principles.</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>Knowledge of budget management principles.</li>
<li>Knowledge of marketing principles.</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Skill in the operation of computers and job related software programs.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Skill in decision making and problem solving.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Skill in interpersonal relations and in dealing with the public.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Skill in oral and written communication.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Skill in working effectively with a wide range of constituencies in a diverse community.</li></ul>
<p>
SUPERVISORY CONTROLS</p>
<p>
The Director of SBDC assigns work in terms of very general instructions. The supervisor spot-checks completed work for compliance with procedures and the nature and propriety of the final results.</p>
<p>
GUIDELINES</p>
<p>
Guidelines include college and center policies and procedures. These guidelines require judgment, selection, and interpretation in application.</p>
<p>
COMPLEXITY/SCOPE OF WORK</p>
<ul><li>The work consists of varied advising and consulting duties. The unique needs of each business contribute to the complexity of the position.</li></ul>
<ul><li>The purpose of this position is provide services and counseling to clients of the Small Business Development Center. Success in this position contributes to the successful development of area businesses.</li></ul>
<p>
CONTACTS</p>
<ul><li>Contacts are typically with co-workers, other college personnel, faculty, staff, students, business owners, lenders, economic development personnel, and members of the general public.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Contacts are typically to give or exchange information, to resolve problems, to provide services, or to motivate or influence persons.</li></ul>
<p>
PHYSICAL DEMANDS/ WORK ENVIRONMENT</p>
<ul><li>The work is typically performed while sitting at a desk or table or while intermittently sitting, standing or stooping. The employee occasionally lifts light and heavy objects and distinguishes between shades of color.</li></ul>
<ul><li>The work is typically performed in an office.</li></ul>
<p>
SUPERVISORY AND MANAGEMENT RESPONSIBILITY</p>
<p>
None.</p>
