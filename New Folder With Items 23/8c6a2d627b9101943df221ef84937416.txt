<h2>Business Manager, Health and Natural Products</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>About the Organization
</p>
<div><div><div>
<div>Sun Chemical, a member of the DIC group, is a leading producer of printing inks, coatings and supplies, pigments, polymers, liquid compounds, solid compounds, and application materials. Together with DIC, Sun Chemical has annual sales of more than $7.5 billion and over 20,000 employees supporting customers around the world.</div>
</div></div></div>
<p>Description
</p>
<div><div><div>
<div>As a key member of Sun Chemical Performance Pigments/Advanced Materials group and DIC Health Care group, the business manager is responsible for managing existing and new business development activities for food colorants and other food additives, dietary supplement ingredients, dietary supplement finished goods and feed ingredients such as Spirulina colorants, Spirulina and other allied health and nutritional products promoted by DIC and its subsidiary, Earthrise Nutritionals in North America and Europe, and managing the sales operation of Earthrise Nutritionals.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Duties and Responsibilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Focus on growing the segment including new product development, acquisitions, partnership, expanding channel to market and market share.</li>
<li>
Manage resources and channel to market for the segment.</li>
<li>
Prepares and implements the business development plan for food colorants and other food additives, dietary supplement ingredients, dietary supplement finished goods and feed ingredients to customers such as color houses, food and beverage brand owners, dietary supplement companies.</li>
<li>
Consults relevant DIC business managers and develop strategic plan to define market opportunities, competitive environments, target segments, etc. for above mentioned product lines</li>
<li>
Works collaboratively with relevant DIC business managers to ensure alignment with strategy.</li>
<li>
Prepares and communicates visit reports for all customer visits and shares them with DIC.</li>
<li>
Creates direct business accounts, if not through color houses and distributors, with food and beverage brand owners, dietary supplement companies.</li>
<li>
Develops effective supply chain for food colorants and other food additives, dietary supplement ingredients, dietary supplement finished goods and feed ingredients in consideration of sourcing material from group companies.</li>
<li>
Builds and maintains good relationship with customers.</li>
<li>
Maintains existing business by ensuring service levels are being met and problems are quickly being solved.</li>
<li>
Optimizes price and profit in each individual customer, and pricing is regularly checked to ensure pricing policy is consistently applied.</li>
<li>
Manage sales and marketing group of Earthrise Nutritionals for budgeting, sales forecasting, reporting on monthly budget variances, meeting budgetary objectives for Spirulina extract and Spirulina (dietary supplement ingredients and finished goods) in North America and Europe.</li>
<li>
Keeps an eye on markets such as voice of customer hearing, price point checking, product needs analysis, and competitive analysis.</li>
<li>
Coordinates with DIC technical, Earthrise production and DIC supply chain group to manage technical service issues.</li>
<li>
Works closely with DIC for new product launch as required.</li>
<li>
Gains brand owner’s and consumer’s visibility by presentation in exhibition and advertising in the most cost-effective way.</li>
<li>
Provide customer support based on prior experience and coordinates with DIC.</li>
<li>
Maintains an active role in industry trade associations representing DIC / Sun Chemical in a professional manner.</li>
<li>
Perform other duties in relation to principal functions as required.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>

</p>
<div><div><div>

<ul>
<li>
Compliance with laws and regulations.</li>
<li>
Excellent business ethics and integrity.</li>
<li>
Excellent business development skills in food, beverage, dietary supplement industry.</li>
<li>
Excellent marketing, problem solving, analytical, entrepreneurship, business acumen skills.</li>
<li>
Excellent selling questioning, negotiation, influencing, collaboration, project management and organization skills.</li>
<li>
Excellent interpersonal skills together with the ability to coach and mentor others.</li>
<li>
Computer literacy with Microsoft Office software.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Education:</b></div>
<ul><li>
Bachelor’s degree in Business, marketing, food science or other scientific discipline. MBA prefered.</li></ul>
<div><b>
Experience:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Preferred 10+ year of working experience in business development and product management with color and food ingredients manufactures and distributors.</li>
<li>
Preferred 10+ year of working experience in product/application development with global brand-owners for food.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>

Position may be located in Parsippany (NJ), Cincinnati (OH), Dusseldorf, Vienna, or Brussels.</b></div>
