Pax8 is looking for a high caliber creative who is obsessed with creating! Join a winning marketing team full of swag and help spread the voice of our brand throughout the industry!</div>

<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>As the Senior Content Marketing Manager, you will:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Be responsible for managing and executing the Pax8 go-to-market strategy.</li>
<li>
Manage the creative process from concept to completion</li>
<li>
Translate marketing objectives into clear creative strategies</li>
<li>
Lead and direct the creative team in the production of all marketing collateral</li>
<li>
Ensure visual communication and brand standards are met</li>
<li>
Oversee deliverables and timelines</li>
<li>
Review work, troubleshoot and provide feedback to creative team</li>
<li>
Remain actively involved in hiring and training creative staff</li>
<li>
Develop and maintain a deep understanding of the evolving cloud industry and the Pax8 value proposition in the IT channel.</li>
<li>
Analyze and audit content readiness to ensure field and digital messaging is consistent and accurate.</li>
<li>
Partner with sales, engineering, and product leadership on the creation of industry and vertical-specific content.</li>
<li>
Help refine our brand voice/messaging and maintain consistency of our voice across channels.</li>
<li>
Develop ways to strengthen our brand and voice across content channels through the creation of compelling persona-driven content.</li>

</div>
</ul></div>

<div><ul>

4-6 years of experience in copywriting, editing and proofreading experience.
</li>
<li>A bachelor’s degree, preferably in Communications, Journalism, or English or related field or equivalent experience.</li>
<li>
Agency background desired.</li>
<li>

Excellent writing, editing, and proofing skills with ability to produce own materials (blog posts, whitepapers, emails etc.) including rules of composition and grammar, spelling, and punctuation.</li>

Demonstrated experience developing persona-focused content for a wide variety of channels and mediums (web, blogs, email, social, video, etc.)</li>
<li>
Detailed knowledge of inbound marketing approaches, tactics, and processes.</li>
<li>
Strong project management skills, ability to multitask, and manage multiple projects in a cross-functional work environment.</li>
<li>
Proven ability to prioritize and manage multiple tasks and projects in response to shifting initiatives.</li>
<li>
Excellent organizational, problem-solving, and interpersonal skills.</li>
