<p><b>Jamba Juice General Manager</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>Be part of a fresh, fun team that has tons of healthy energy and a smile with every smoothie.</p>
<p>Join Jamba. Make a Difference. Your first day with us will be the first day of your healthy journey with Jamba Juice.<br>
When you put truly good, straight from the earth ingredients in your body, you become a better you - and a better you makes for a better world. Team up with Jamba for a Healthy Whirl'd!</p>
<p>We are an independently owned and operated franchise of Jamba Juice.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Jamba Juice General Manager</b></p>
<p>Experience our fun and energetic culture that is full of opportunities to advance your career, while receiving a discount on our healthy drinks and food items. As a General Manager, you will manage Store Level Profit and Loss, including Sales and financials. You will be responsible for maintaining operational excellence in Service and demonstrate exceptional leadership behavior.</p>
<p><b>General Manager Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Recognizing outstanding work performance while providing necessary constructive feedback</li>
<li>Training, coaching, counseling, and disciplining your Team Members and Shift Managers</li>
<li>Ensuring that all business and productivity metrics, including sales, budgets, and staffing are met</li>
<li>Reviewing business practices, determining the needs of the customers and team members and identifying performance opportunities to create strategies that improve overall business performance.</li>
<li>Adhering to all Company and Brand food safety, cash handling, and operational policies and procedures and ensuring that all Team Members are in compliance as well</li>
<li>Developing and maintaining positive relations with the surrounding community through excellent guest services, effective marketing, community involvement, and sponsorships</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>General Manager Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>21 years of age or older</li>
<li>High School degree or equivalent required; Bachelor’s degree or equivalent certification in restaurant management or hospitality a plus</li>
<li>Must have reliable transportation with clean driving record</li>
<li>Minimum 1 year management experience, including Profit and Loss management</li>
<li>Available to work 45 hours per week</li>
<li>Ability to manage workplace location change within a reasonable area</li>
<li>Proficiency with technology including POS software, Microsoft Office products, email, as well as other additional web based programs such as inventory, hiring, and recruiting software</li>
<li>Ability to stand, bend, scoop, and regularly lift up to 40 pounds</li>
<li>Comfortable working occasionally in walk-in coolers and freezers</li>
</ul>
<p></p>

<li>Competitive Health, Life, disability and Dental Insurance</li>
<li>Paid Holidays</li>
<li>Book Scholarship Program –If you are a student please ask for further details!</li>
<li>Birthday and Anniversary Recognition</li>
<li>Incentives &amp; Promotions throughout the year</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
