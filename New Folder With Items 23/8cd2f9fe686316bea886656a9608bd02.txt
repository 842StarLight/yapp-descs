<h2>Leasing Professional</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>

Are you ready to join our team? To research us in more detail, visit www.irtliving.com.</p>
<p>
Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Perform all aspects of leasing apartments which include; Welcome prospective residents; Tour the community; Follow up with prospective tenants and close tenant deals.</li>
<li>Enthusiastically and professionally answer incoming calls from potential tenants or current residents. Provide accurate answers or transfer calls to Assistant Manager or Community Manager.</li>
<li>Complete lease application forms, assist with application verification, screen all incoming residents by objective qualification standards and notify prospective residents of results.</li>
<li>Be an expert on our community offerings, rents and amenities, as well as those of the community’s competitors by maintaining awareness of local market conditions and trends.</li>
<li>Perform off-site marketing research and assist in preparing marketing plans.</li>
<li>Ability to explain lease agreement and obtains signatures on all lease paperwork prior to move-in.</li>
<li>Organize and file appropriate reports, files and other community related paperwork.</li>
<li>Maintain activity reports by inputting daily traffic including telephone calls, internet leads, tours and follow-up.</li>
<li>Walk and inspect models, “market ready” vacant apartments, and common area daily to ensure cleanliness.</li>
<li>Assist with coordinating resident programs and functions to assure resident retention.</li>
<li>Maintain courteous &amp; helpful demeanor with owners, residents, prospective residents, coworkers, vendors, etc.</li>
<li>Participate in company training classes, meetings, and other events intended to introduce staff to new ideas and improve overall performance.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<p>Requirements:</p>
<ul>
<li>High School Diploma or equivalent. Some college level courses preferred.</li>
<li>Previous experience in a professional position involving heavy customer service or sales.</li>
<li>At least one year experience working within a multi-family community preferred.</li>
<li>Experience and proficiency in using a variety of software programs including MS Office Suite, Word and Excel. Yardi preferred. In addition, ability to use a typewriter, calculator and fax machine.</li>
<li>Possess excellent customer service skills and excellent written and verbal communication abilities.</li>
<li>Demonstrate sales and marketing skills.</li>
<li>Possess or ability to obtain a working knowledge of Fair Housing laws, rules and regulations concerning apartment leasing and management.</li>
<li>Must be detail oriented, an independent thinker, and be able to prioritize and shift focus as necessary.</li>
<li>Strong work ethic and ability to work as part of a team.</li>
<li>Must be able to obtain licenses when required by state</li>
<br>
</ul>
