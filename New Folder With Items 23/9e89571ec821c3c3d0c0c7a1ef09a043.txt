<h2>Change of Occupancy Maintenance Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">WinnCompanies is searching for an organized and proactive Mainenance Coordinator to join our team at Fort Knox. Primary responsibility is to coordinate and faciliate the administration of the Change of Occupancy Maintenance (COMs) in accordance with time, budget and quality expectations. Works closely with COM Manager, maintenance contract manager, Leasing Manager and Community Managers.
<br>Responsibilities
<br><ul>
<li>Scheduling of COM work (internal and contracted).</li>
<li>Ensures consistent and accurate communication between maintenance department and community/leasing offices.</li>
<li>Verify that labor and parts entries are entered on all work orders.</li>
<li>Perform data entry duties.</li>
<li>Reconcile technician labor hours in Yardi.</li>
<li>Ensure scope and tasks required are identified for COM including accurate estimations of time and cost to complete repairs.</li>
<li>Utilize the YARDI database to manage COM process</li>
<li>Generates reports and maintains accurate filing system, as required.</li>
<li>Updates daily work activity.</li>
<li>May be responsible for purchase order creation or receipt for COM contracted work.</li>
<li>May be responsible for duties of COM Manager, Other Department Managers, Resource Manager and Field Supervisors in their absence or as required.</li>
<li>May schedule and adjusts technician’s daily work load.</li>
<li>Adhere to company policies and procedures.</li>
<li>Performs other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
Requirements
<br><ul>
<li>High school diploma or GED equivalent required. Vocational or college education preferred.</li>
<li>3 years administrative experience preferred. Maintenance background preferred.</li>
<li>Proficiency in Microsoft Office is required and YARDI or other similar industry software is preferred.</li>
</ul>
