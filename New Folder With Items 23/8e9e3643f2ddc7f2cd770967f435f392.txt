<p><b>Sr. Pharmacy Program Manager PHS</b></p>
<p>
The Senior Manager of Pharmacy Programs is responsible for pharmacy program development, marketing and successful implementation within Omnicell PHS payor and network pharmacy channels, and serves as a strategic and tactical subject matter expert across pharmacy programs. This role will provide reliable insight regarding overall strategy and outcomes related to program innovation and lead complex, cross-functional projects.
</p>
<p>Content areas for the Senior Manager of Pharmacy Programs may include, but not be limited to: pharmacogenomics, transitional care, medication adherence, MTM, HEDIS / Stars, chronic care management, and behavioral health. This will require building relationships with a variety of stakeholders.
</p>
<p>This individual will develop and cultivate relationships with stakeholders to maximize profitability and growth; additionally, will collaborate with innovative partners to drive shared business goals and objectives; will interact with sales, account executives, business partners, and customers to understand customer needs and how Omnicell can best satisfy those needs.
</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provide subject matter expertise in pharmacogenomics (PGx); maintain, further develop, and enhance components of Omnicell’s PGx services:</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Research scientific and regulatory developments in the field of PGx</li>
<li>Advocate for the rational and routine use of PGx testing</li>
<li>Collaborate with the Clinical Pharmacist Specialist as a subject matter expert for the company's PGx clinical services</li>
<li>Support and participates in activities that guide and accelerate the application of PGx to pharmacy practice</li>
<li>Support provision of effective PGx-related education to patients, caregivers, health care professionals, and students</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Develop, market, and implement new programs to meet emerging customer needs around value-based care and pharmacy network performance in the payor market</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Provide functional and industry expertise to support analysis and review of business development opportunities</li>
<li>Plan, prioritize and communicates the strategy for pharmacy programs innovation
</li>
<li>Support the creation and tracking of key performance and operational metrics
</li>
<li>Manage relationships with a matrix of leaders across internal stakeholder groups</li>
<li>Review the competitive marketplace and recommend adjustments to operational activities based upon identified information. Make recommendations for the formulation of the strategies and operating plans.</li>
<li>Deliver against business goals/objectives; identify operational issues and develop, recommend, and implement strategies to resolve them</li>
<li>Partner with Director to drive alignment with other development value streams partnering with key stakeholders
</li>
<li>Work collaboratively with product innovation team to achieve revenue targets, profitability and growth of new products and solutions</li>
<li>Sell internally and externally to gain leadership support for new opportunities</li>
<li>Lead/support product concept validation</li>
<li>Provide support to the product management team to bring from idea to generally available solution</li>
<li>Contribute directly to the profitability and growth of the Omnicell pharmacy network</li>
</ul>
