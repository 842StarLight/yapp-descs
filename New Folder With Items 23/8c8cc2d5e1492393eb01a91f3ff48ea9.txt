<h2>Portfolio Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Description</b></p>
<div>
The Portfolio Management Group (PMG) is responsible for the day to day management of investment portfolios for clients of Clarfeld Citizens Private Wealth. A Portfolio Manager provides portfolio management services for high net worth and institutional client accounts within the firm, while meeting all compliance and governance requirements for managing portfolios. A Portfolio Manager works most closely with Advisors and other client facing and sales colleagues to design and maintain portfolios in accordance with developed strategy and specific client circumstances. This position has some limited client interaction, and is responsible for managing the day/day strategy implementation within each client’s objectives for risk/return, cash and other liquidity needs, asset location, and client specific constraints. Quarterly, semi-annual, or annual rebalancing, regulatory and governance account reviews and exception item management for tracking errors to benchmarks, asset allocation, strategy implementation, and various other concentration and portfolio construction metrics will be key measures of Portfolio Manager performance.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
Essential Duties/Responsibilities</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><ul>
<li><div>
Manage the investment, trust and institutional relationships within Clarfeld Citizens Private Wealth. The Portfolio Manager is singularly authorized to initiate investment transactions in client portfolios.</div></li>
<li><div>
Rebalance accounts according to the client’s investment policy statement and in line with guidance of the strategy team and client Advisor</div></li>
<li><div>
Understand and manage specific client circumstances related to client investments including asset allocation, concentrated positions and developing a long term investment plan</div></li>
<li><div>
Responsible for the investing of accounts, initial and semi-annual account reviews, regulatory review requirements, and divestiture plans</div></li>
<li><div>
In addition to managing client investment portfolios on an ongoing basis, the Portfolio Manager is directly involved with Advisors and other sales colleagues in new client onboarding activities. Works directly with Advisors and other client facing and sales colleagues to develop comparative portfolio analyses, investment implementation strategies, inclusion of non-managed assets, IPS structure, etc. for communication to new and prospective clients</div></li>
<li><div>
The Portfolio Manager also handles day to day inquiries regarding transactions, performance, and similar portfolio specifics from colleagues across the division.</div></li>
<li><div>
Maintain and update the client’s investment policy statement as clients circumstance change</div></li>
<li><div>
Portfolio Managers sometimes also provide support for the Investment Strategy Team regarding third party manager due diligence and portfolio implementation considerations. Less of this type of activity for this role is anticipated as the Clarfeld integration progresses over time.</div></li>
</ul></div>
