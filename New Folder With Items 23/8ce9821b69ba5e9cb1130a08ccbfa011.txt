<h2>Sr. Analyst, Program Development</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Element Fleet Management is seeking a Sr. Analyst, Program Development to join a dynamic team supporting a rapidly growing new fleet program with a last mile delivery client. You will contribute to client satisfaction by driving the implementation of new programs and program modifications, setting appropriate expectations across stakeholder groups, and delivering agreed upon results on time and without service interruption. By demonstrating initiative, you ensure the success of the cross-functional team in deploying program changes and enhancements.</p>
<p>
Being the ideal candidate, you will thrive in a fast-paced environment, seek to continuously improve the client experience, and consistently deliver strong results. You will interact with a variety of stakeholders and collaborate with a strong team to implement new technology solutions, supply chain innovations, and service offerings. What makes this role exciting is that you will execute a strategic program for our client while gaining valuable experience and exposure to accelerate your career.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Duties &amp; Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Work with clients, vendors, and internal stakeholders to scope solutions and create implementation strategies to deliver value on time, (e.g., facilitate solution creation, identify milestones and risks, establish timelines)</li>
<li>
Modify implementation strategy/approach based on observed results</li>
<li>
Identify cross functional process linkages and facilitate coordination and improvements</li>
<li>
Ensure compliance to project deliverable's, standard operating procedures, business practices, compliance and/or regulatory requirements</li>
<li>
Perform follow-up and validation for task completion, trouble shooting, and resolving issues to ensure timely execution</li>
<li>
Record project progress and provide proactive report-outs to internal and external stakeholders</li>
<li>
Participate in prospecting and sales cycle (visits, proposals, presentations); educating customer on products, services and processes</li>
<li>
Serve as ‘voice of the customer’ for product management, operations, sales, and proposal teams</li>
<li>
Lead or participate as a Subject Matter Expert in cross-functional projects; as requested by the business</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Required Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree or equivalent business experience</li>
<li>
Minimum 2 years project or program management experience</li>
<li>
Change management, managing work teams, and/or driving activities</li>
<li>
Strong PC proficiency (including Microsoft Suite including: Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and Outlook; and project management software Visio, MS project)</li>
<li>
Able to travel up to 35%</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Preferred Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Self-starter who takes initiative and can work independently and with a variety of internal and external teams with minimum guidance</li>
<li>
Project leadership skills: requirements gathering, communication management, risk management, issue resolution, scope management, and resource management</li>
<li>
Demonstrates proper judgment regarding decision making, self-reliance and problem resolution</li>
<li>
Ability to implement and adapt to change effectively</li>
</ul>
<p></p>

In the process, we help customers reduce costs and improve productivity. Our employees play a critical role in delivering value to customers and ensuring an exceptional customer experience. Element is an exciting organization, offering many opportunities for you to grow your career!</p>
