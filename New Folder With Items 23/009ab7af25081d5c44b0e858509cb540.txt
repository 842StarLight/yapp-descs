<h2>Senior Graphic Designer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>The <b>Senior Graphic Designer</b> is responsible to work with members of the strategy and creative teams to visually convey a particular message, idea or concept. They rely on their knowledge and proficiency with current graphic design programs to produce graphic art and visual materials for promotions, advertisements, websites, films, packaging and other mediums. Must have exceptional attention to detail and follow through, as well as the ability to collaborate cross-functionally and with a variety of stakeholders. In addition, should provide coaching and guidance to the team members about every aspect of the creative process.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities include but not limited to: </b></p>
<p>· Design interactive websites and supporting materials</p>
<p>· Create emails using email templates</p>
<p>· Ability to develop campaign branding.</p>
<p>· Concept high level thinking ideas for client campaigns</p>
<p>· Create on screen prototypes / mockups</p>
<p>· Push the boundaries and share ideas</p>
<p>· Collaborate with clients and colleagues</p>
<p>· Support your work with thoughtful and sound reasoning</p>
<p>· Participate in client presentations and discovery meetings</p>
<p>· Deliver successful print and digital design solutions within corporate brand guidelines.</p>
<p>· Able to work independently and own projects through completion.</p>
<p>· Put together disparate elements of a design created by another professional, such as the icons, photographs and other components necessary to create a cohesive design.</p>
<p>· Work effectively with other designers and writers and be willing to assist other team members to complete projects.</p>
<p>· Work collaboratively with a variety of internal stakeholders and steward the usage of approved design guidelines and templates.</p>
<p>· Consult with clients’ marketing, copywriting and sales teams to create cohesive designs that reflect our clients’ corporate cultures and goals.</p>
<p>· Assist in implementing improvements in departmental procedures to increase efficiency, quality and usability of current prototypes.</p>
<p>· Demonstrate expertise in using Adobe Creative Cloud software for both print and digital designs.</p>
<p>· Proficiently use Microsoft Office products: edit PowerPoint presentations, create charts using Excel and create publications in Word.</p>
<p><b>Experience and skills needed to be successful such as: </b></p>
<p>· Bachelor’s Degree in Graphic Design or Digital Media and/or Certificate in Graphic Design</p>
<p>· 7+ years’ experience of graphic design or any combination of years of experience and education.</p>
<p>· Mastery of Adobe Creative Suite, Adobe XD, Sketch, Microsoft Office Products (Excel, Word, PowerPoint, Project, Outlook).</p>
<p>· Expert level in WordPress templates</p>
<p>· In-depth understanding of UX/UI</p>
<p>· Experience working with WordPress templates</p>
<p>· Portfolio that demonstrates strong layout skills and understanding of UI and UX concepts</p>
<p>· Efficient at working on short turnaround projects</p>
<p>· Positive attitude</p>
<p>· Strong understanding of color theory and typography</p>
<p>· Ability to function well in a fast-paced environment under tight deadlines</p>
<p>· Good team collaborator with strong writing skills.</p>
<p>· Strong multi-tasking skills and the ability to rapidly shift priorities.</p>
<p>· Enthusiastic, open-minded and approachable</p>
<p>· Ability to develop innovative design approaches</p>
<p>· Adaptive design eye and skill</p>
<p>· Excellent communication skills</p>
<p>· Ability to absorb and apply constructive criticism from peers and clients</p>
