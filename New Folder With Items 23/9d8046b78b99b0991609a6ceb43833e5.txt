<div><div>Account Manager Heavy Equipment<br>
<br>
Would you like the opportunity to write your own paycheck? Do you believe in the saying “you get what you put into it”? Or just looking for a place with unlimited income potential?<br>
<br>
Then I have the perfect career for you!<br>
<br>
I am in search of an outstanding customer service driven, results orientated sales professional.<br>
You will be responsible for selling and depending on the location, renting heavy equipment. This is an outside sales position; with an uncapped earnings potentially. The majority of your time will be spent in the field. You will conduct sales calls, over the phone and in person; by going to varies job / construction sites.<br>
<br>
Do you think you have what it takes? If so, then go ahead and apply!<br>
<br>
Description in detail:<br>
<ul>
<li>Use systematic planning and prospecting to generate sales leads.</li>
<li>Conduct daily cold calls to generate leads / sales appointments.</li>
<li>Conduct daily job site visits to generate leads / sales appointments.</li>
<li>Conduct daily sales presentations and demonstrations in order to create brand awareness.</li>
<li>Identify your territory and create an effective marketing campaign.</li>
<li>Create and promote brand awareness in your territory.</li>
<li>Enter your activities into the CRM on a daily basis.</li>
</ul>
<br>
Educational/Experience Requirements:<br>
<ul>
<li>Applicants must have at least 3 years of outside sales experience, prior experience in heavy equipment sales is a plus.</li>
<li>Must be able to analyze and bid jobs</li>
<li>Ability to effectively interact with customers</li>
<li>Valid State Driver’s License and acceptable driving record.</li>
<li>Must be able to travel on a regular basis to include overnight stays.</li>
<li>Must successfully pass a criminal background check and drug screen.</li>
</ul>
<br>
ADMINISTRATIVE/COMPUTER SKILLS:<br>
<ul>
<li>This position requires daily proficient use of a company-provided personal computer. Incumbent will be trained in and must be capable of using the Road Machinery eXtend software.</li>
<li>Knowledge of Microsoft Office Outlook for email required.</li>
<li>Thorough understanding of MS Excel, knowing how to manipulate spreadsheets.</li>
</ul>
<br>
Work Environment:<br>
<ul>
<li>Sales environment with exposure to heat, cold, dust, dirt and noise.</li>
<li>Field environment with exposure to adverse weather conditions including heat, cold, wind, rain, dust and dirt.</li>
<li>Office environment. When visiting the corporate office, business professional appearance at all times.</li>
</ul>
<br>
Base plus commission, and business expense reimbursement. Full Company benefits package including medical, life, dental, vision, sick time, vacation, and 401(K).<br>
