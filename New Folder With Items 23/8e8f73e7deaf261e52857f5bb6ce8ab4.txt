Tax Training School Field Instructor
<br>Seeking seasonal instructors to prepare, facilitate, and train for a variety of courses and students at H&amp;R Block. Qualified candidates will exhibit strong tax preparation, communication, and presentation skills.
<br><br>
H&amp;R Block is the industry’s largest consumer tax services provider¹ and we have been focused on client service for over 60 years.
<br><br>
<b>Tax Training School Field Instructor duties include:</b><br>
Effectively teaching various courses and training classes while maintaining respect for students with different levels of experience and comprehension.
<br>Utilizing course materials, including Instructor Guides, Participant Guides, handouts and other teaching aids to deliver course content as designed.
<br>Presenting and facilitating training classes, using a variety of techniques such as exercises, case studies, skills practice along with question and answer sessions.
<br>Recording attendance and scores in a timely manner, as directed by District or Franchise General Manager
<br>"
<br><br>
Minimum Qualifications
<br><br>
"
<br><br>
<b>Required Skills &amp; Experience:</b><br>
Sufficient experience as a tax professional or education to master content of all courses being taught. Mastery is demonstrated by answering student questions in a comfortable/confident manner and per all tax law or factual information.
<br>Ability to read, write, and clearly communicate in English and/or other languages as required by needs of the district.
<br>Effective presentation and facilitation skills as demonstrated in their words, voice, pace, delivery and non-verbal communication. Instructor should have the ability to teach in an energetic and enthusiastic manner.
<br>Excellent interpersonal skills demonstrated through conversation with, and preparation of tax returns of, H&amp;R Block customers.
<br>Ability to explain complex laws and regulations in an easy to understand manner.
<br>Above average reading and math skills to understand information and explain course information to students.
<br>Travel to training locations in and out of district as scheduled/assigned by District General Manager.
<br><br>
<b>Preferred Skills &amp; Experience:</b><br>
Two to three years of combined experience as an H&amp;R Block Tax Professional or Client Service Leader (one year of experience within the most recent tax season).
<br>Understanding and demonstrated proficiency of BlockWorks and other applicable software.
<br>Additional state and local requirements may apply.
<br>H&amp;R Block is an equal opportunity employer.

1 Based on annual revenues for sales of tax preparation products and services.
