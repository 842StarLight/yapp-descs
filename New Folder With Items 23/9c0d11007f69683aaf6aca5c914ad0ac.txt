<h2>Implementations Specialist - AP</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Purpose:</b><b> </b>Implementations Specialist – AP, will work with and between sales operations, implementations team and Data Dimensions customers to assist with the implementation and client roll out of Data Dimensions 360 Purchase To Pay Suite and associated services.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities </b>include the following. Other duties may be assigned as deemed necessary per management.</p>
<p>
Provide routine support for end user with IT-related issues.</p>
<ul>
<li>Understand and articulate the full accounts payable cycle from receipt of invoices to payment; including coding invoices, matching purchase orders, obtaining approval, and entering the invoices into an accounting system and processing of employee expense reports; including verification of receipts and coding.</li>
<li>Be familiar with batch check runs, wire transfers, and ACH transaction process functions.</li>
<li>Understand the need for monthly accounts payable journal entries and balance sheet reconciliations.</li>
<li>Create and manage project timelines and deliverables lists.</li>
<li>Comfortable discussing accounts payable policies and compliance including HIPAA, SOC, and GDPR.</li>
<li>Understand the concepts of imaging, workflow and record retention of accounts payable documents.</li>
<li>Comfortable working with accounting software.</li>
<li>Partner with a client’s Corporate Controller to identify and implement process improvements.</li>
<li>Train clients on software and systems at client sites and through web meetings.</li>
<li>System configuration and testing.</li>
<li>Configure and present software demonstrations for prospective clients.</li>
<li>Other duties as required by management</li>
<li>Purchasing experience is a plus</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualification Requirements:</b><b> </b>To perform the job successfully, an individual should the following:</p>
<ul>
<li>Adherence to tight deadlines and quick turnaround for project deliverables.</li>
<li>Ability to effectively present information and respond to questions from client management, vendors, and associates.</li>
<li>Strong organizational skills and the ability to develop and maintain an organized structure.</li>
<li>Ability to work collaboratively in a dynamic environment where adaptability is imperative.</li>
<li>Accurate with a strong attention to detail.</li>
<li>Strong written and oral communication skills.</li>
<li>Previous experience using a popular ERP is a plus</li>
<li>Understanding of general accounting principles</li>
<li>Willing to travel frequently and work in a multifaceted sales and project implementation environment.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Minimum Required Education and Experience:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree and 3-5 years of related experience (or equivalent combination of education and experience)</li>
<li>Must be at least 18 years of age.</li>
<li>Able to read, write, and speak English.</li>
<li>Successfully pass and maintain applicable background checks and security clearances.</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
