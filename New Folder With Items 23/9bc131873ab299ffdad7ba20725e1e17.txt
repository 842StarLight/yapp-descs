<p>Position Overview:</p>
<p>Russelectric, A Siemens Business, is a leading U.S. manufacturer of power control systems including paralleling switchgear and automatic transfer switches. As a growing business, we are looking for an After-Market Project Engineer to join our Hingham, MA team. The Project Engineer will be responsible for the development of, and modification to, custom Low &amp; Medium Voltage Switchgear Control &amp; Distribution Systems.</p>

<p>Responsibilities:</p>
<ul><li>Review Sales Order Entry Packages (Specifications, Drawings, Bill of Materials, etc.) to develop Project</li></ul>
<ul><li>Submittals (Physical Detail Drawings, System Descriptions, Bill of Materials, Etc.).</li></ul>
<ul><li>Participate in Pre-Submittal and Submittal Review Meetings as required to obtain Project releases.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Development of Project Schematics.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Partner with members across various departments to provide releases to the Production Floor</li></ul>
<ul><li>Provide ongoing support to the Test and Field Service teams</li></ul>
<ul><li>Provide an estimated completion schedule for every project and assignment and ensure projects are completed by set timelines</li></ul>
<ul><li>Proactively identify areas for continuous improvement as it relates to department standards</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Qualifications and Education Requirements:</p>
<p>1. Bachelor’s Degree in Electrical Engineering or related field</p>
<p>2. Minimum of 3 years of design experience, focused on electrical power control circuits with a strong understanding of diesel generator control and switchgear system operation</p>
<p>3. Extensive knowledge in PLC based and relay control logic, electrical metering, protective relaying, and SCADA systems</p>
<p>4. Ability to interpret schematics, wiring prints, point lists, and other documentation.</p>
<p>5. Working knowledge with Windows, Word, AutoCad, and Promis e software.</p>
<p>6. Good organizational skills, able to establish priorities, while managing multiple projects at once</p>
<p>7. Excellent verbal and written communication skills</p>
<p>8. Ability to thrive in a diverse environment with minimal supervision</p>
