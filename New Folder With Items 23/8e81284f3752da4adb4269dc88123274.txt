<p><b>SiteLock Needs an Affiliate Marketing Sales Manager</b></p>
<p>SiteLock is the Global Leader in business website security and is the <b>only </b>web security solution to offer complete, cloud-based website protection. We find and fix threats, prevent future attacks, accelerate website performance and meet PCI compliance standards for businesses of all sizes (basically like Batman if Gotham City was the internet). Our mission is to protect every website on the internet (yes, we know that’s a colossal goal, but we are THAT good), that’s why we need talented people like yourself to join our ever-growing team!</p>
<p><b>That’s Nice, But What’s The Job?</b></p>
<p>The Affiliate Marketing Sales Manager will develop, grow and manage the company’s marketing affiliates, aggregators, and other marketing partners helping to meet aggressive growth goals. The Affiliate Marketing Sales Manager will work closely with leaders across sales and marketing to support continued channel and organizational growth. They will work closely with our partners and internal team members to maximize sales funnel efficiency while delivering upon planned lead and sales results. This position requires a high level of collaboration and will be responsible for coordinating internal and external efforts to grow the affiliate marketing channel.</p>
<ul>
<li>Manage partnerships including relationship development and ongoing communication with aggregators, affiliates and marketing partners.</li>
<li>Evaluate pricing and commission modification opportunities quantitatively and qualitatively to determine potential expected results and return.</li>
<li>Grow the channel. Develop new partnerships with partners that will contribute to our growth, expand our reach to new audiences, and drive new business. Explore, assess, and execute new approaches with existing partners.</li>
<li>Develop overall plans, and review campaign performance using internal and external reporting and analytics tools. Distill data into actionable insights which will help drive change and growth</li>
<li>Manage the affiliate program budget and sales forecasting.</li>
<li>Consistently innovate, explore, learn, test, execute and iterate. Develop and maintain a culture and discipline of testing, research, competitive analysis and data-driven decisions to optimize future plans.</li>
<li>Closely monitor the competitive landscape, evaluate new technologies, platforms, strategies, and tactics, bringing recommendations back to the team to ensure our continued evolution and growth.</li>
<br>
</ul>
