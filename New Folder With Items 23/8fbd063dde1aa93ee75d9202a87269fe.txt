<h2>Sales and Marketing Strategist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Division: </b>Marketing &amp; Business Development</p>
<p><b>Reports to: </b>Human Resource Director</p>
<p><b>Classification: </b>Contract Appointment / 1099</p>
<p><b>FLSA Status: </b>Commissioned-Based Independent Marketer</p>
<p><b>Position Objective: </b> The primary function of this role is to market and generate new business for the organization. The successful candidate in this role will set goals to increase the organization’s visibility, viability, sales, and revenue. The candidate for this role must have a proven track record of producing and delivering marketing strategies that increase awareness and visibility of the organization’s services, area(s) of expertise and possess a strong knowledge in the Senior Healthcare industry. The candidate in this position must adhere to a strict Non-Solicitation/Non-Compete Clause and will report directly to the Human Resource Director.</p>
<p><b>OPPORTUNITY </b></p>
<p>The Boomer generation is the fastest-growing market segment in health care. And we are one of the unique businesses in the country dedicated to serving the aging population and their needs. Up for the challenge of a lifetime? Join a team of the best and the brightest to find bold new ways to proactively improve the health and quality of life. You'll find a wealth of dynamic opportunities to grow and develop as we work together to navigate through the complex health care system.</p>
<p>Today’s growing aging population is the reason you can build your own senior care marketing business. We’re collaborating a team of independent business owners into this community to help seniors select the right care and assist in long term care planning and asset protection planning. The opportunity is enormous. Just think, before you know it, you could be an expert in one of the fastest-growing market.</p>
<p><b>PRIMARY DUTIES and REQUIREMENTS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>2+ years sales, marketing, Medicaid or other health care experience preferred.</li>
<li>Identify current marketing and business development trends to increase the organization’s customer base and revenue while improving outreach systems and implementing or enhancing current marketing and/or advertising campaign strategies.</li>
<li>Consistently maintain working partnerships through marketing outreach to organizations that promote the health and wellness of elders and seniors.</li>
<li>Direct market to urgent health care and skilled nursing, assisted living, senior care facilities, and hospitals.</li>
<li>Possess a working knowledge of care management, including basic knowledge of Medicaid Asset Protection planning and Medicare plans.</li>
<li>Establish a network of realtors for property management, residential sales, apartment leasing, and other housing considerations.</li>
<li>Create partnerships and listings of local churches, synagogues, and other faith-based organizations.</li>
<li>Meet marketing and sales financial objectives by forecasting projected new business opportunities and establishing target market share.</li>
<li>Prepare weekly sales updates with budget impacts, marketing plan(s), new client contacts, contact results, for the review and approval of the Human Resource Director .</li>
<li>Accomplishes marketing and sales objectives by planning, developing, implementing, and evaluating advertising, merchandising, trade promotion programs and developing field sales action plans.</li>
<li>Improves product marketability and profitability by researching, identifying, and capitalizing on market opportunities and improving the overall appearance of the organization’s marketing and outreach literature, brochures, web design, product packaging and new product development.</li>
<li>Recognize, target and neutralize potential marketing/advertising issues and work cohesively to resolve any issues and/or problems that can may create negative public image to the organization.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>ADDITIONAL DUTIES &amp; REQUIREMENTS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Sustains rapport with existing partners and key accounts by making periodic visits or other methods of contact as needed.</li>
<li>Provides information by collecting, analyzing, and summarizing data and trends.</li>
<li>Protects organization's value by keeping all information confidential and adhering to the organization’s Non-Disclosure Agreement.</li>
<li>Possess current knowledge of senior and elder care laws and regulations by participating in educational opportunities, reading professional publications, maintaining personal networks, and participating in professional organizations.</li>
<li>Create verbiage for and review all print communications and assist with preparing, proofing and printing all marketing materials.</li>
</ul>
