<h2>Program Administrator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>World Energy Efficiency Services (WEES) is an energy efficiency service company focused on assessing, developing, engineering, and managing commercial energy efficiency projects. Specifically, projects include commercial building energy audits, project scope development, equipment specifications and ROI analyses. We work with local utilities and their incentive programs, present project analyses and provide turnkey energy efficiency project solutions for our customers. Our focus is on high ROI lighting, mechanical and energy management systems.</p>
<p>Headquartered in Worcester, Massachusetts, with branch office located in Cromwell, WEES is primarily focused on serving commercial, industrial, and institutional customers in Southern New England.</p>
<p>We are seeking a Program Administrator who will support project data entry and report generation for commercial and industrial customers. Energy industry, lighting or utility background in helpful. This is an office-based position in Cromwell, CT that requires extensive work with the utility (Eversource) Data Management System and internal WEES software.</p>
<p>The ideal candidate will support field staff by accurately providing database data entry and preparation of proposals, specifications, project invoicing and reports. We are looking for a team player that is an effective communicator, possesses good mathematical skills, eager to learn and develop new skills and knowledge, and will thrive in a fast paced, dynamic, entrepreneurial environment.</p>
<p>Key Responsibilities</p>
<ul>
<li>Decipher energy assessment data provided by technical sales staff and prepare for data entry</li>
<li>Input technical data into utility (Eversource) Data Management System</li>
<li>Provide QA/QC of data received and entered</li>
<li>Generate reports from Data Management System for sales staff</li>
<li>Manage a large number of projects through the input, review and approval processes</li>
<li>Liaison between WEES and Eversource</li>
<li>Assist in project close-out and invoicing</li>
</ul>
<p>Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>Strong skills in Microsoft Office, especially Excel is a must</li>
<li>Understanding of lighting efficiency products and technology is helpful</li>
<li>1-3 years of project coordination experience</li>
<li>Prior experience with energy services firm working directly with utility programs, an electrical contractor, or an electrical distributor is a plus</li>
<li>Strong organization, verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Strong organizational skills is a must</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to effectively manage multiple priorities and business relationships</li>
</ul>
<p>Employee Benefits: We recognize people as our most valuable asset. Our competitive salary and benefits package includes medical &amp; dental insurance, prescription drug coverage, confidential employee assistance program, 401(K) plan with company match, life insurance, 10 paid company holidays and attractive vacation policy.</p>
<p>World Energy is an Equal Opportunity Employer</p>
