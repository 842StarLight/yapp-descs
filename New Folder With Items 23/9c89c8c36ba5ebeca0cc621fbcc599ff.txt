<h2>Regional Manager - Cleaning</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><p><b><i>Cleaning Excellence Regional Manager
</i></b></p></div>
<p><b>Join us in creating an exceptional experience and be a part of a cutting edge environment providing world class service in technology, operations and functional teams.
</b></p>
<p><b>JOB DESCRIPTION:
</b></p>
<p>The Regional Manager is responsible for quality assurance processes within ISS North American portfolio. Activities performed include quality systems implementation and adherence, process improvement programs, audits and service delivery of ISS standard operating procedures.
</p>
<p><b>PRINCIPLE DUTIES
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>Identifying and addressing client needs with major regulatory agencies (e.g. FDA, EMA, PMDA, etc.)
</p></li>
<li><p>Developing and sustaining client relationships using networking, negotiations and persuasion skills to help identify and sell potential new service opportunities
</p></li>
<li><p>Preparing and presenting written and verbal materials including RFPs
</p></li>
<li><p>Ensure all client requirements, guidelines, regulations and expectations are adhered to by employees and vendors to maintain the ISS experience
</p></li>
<li><p>Develop and maintains strategic client relationships
</p></li>
<li><p>Support Key Account managers within the parameters of the CBA with local 32 (where applicable); ensuring best practices are followed
</p></li>
<li><p>Survey building for staffing purposes and cost evaluations
</p></li>
<li><p>Maintain labor, projects, forecasting reports
</p></li>
<li><p>Examines work completion in accordance to contract deliverables
</p></li>
<li><p>Monitor quality control throughout assigned facilities in North America
</p></li>
</ul>
<p><b>MINIMUM REQUIREMENTS
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>Requires a minimum of 5+ years of quality assurance management, in the pharmaceutical industry; and/or 5+ years of quality assurance management in general cleaning
</p></li>
<li><p>Energetic and result driven leader with a passion for service and commitment to safety and security awareness
</p></li>
<li><p>Proven record of accomplishment in customer service satisfaction and outstanding customer service skills.
</p></li>
<li><p>Ability to communicate with all level’s within a client’s management structure
</p></li>
<li><p>Takes a team focus approach in leading
</p></li>
<li><p>Able to command credibility and be able to influence
</p></li>
<li><p>Proficient in PC applications, such as Microsoft suite, property management software and payroll systems
</p></li>
<li><p>Strong organizational skills; able to create plans and put into action
</p></li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>High level of expertise in methods used to clean buildings, tools and equipment
</li>
<li>Knowledge of occupational hazards and safety rules and regulations
</li>
<li>Ability to execute work schedules making the most efficient use of personnel
</li>
<li>Ability to work in a demanding, fast paced, high volume environment
</li>
<li>Must be a team player and committed to working in a quality environment
</li>
<li>Bilingual in Spanish is a plus<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS
</b></p>
<ul><li><p>Experiential guidance, subject matter specialty, and optimized standard industry practices with Quality Management Systems relating to Good Manufacturing Practices (GMP), Good Clinical Practices (GCP), Good Pharmacovigilance Practices, (GPP) and or Good Laboratory Practices (GLP)
</p></li></ul>
