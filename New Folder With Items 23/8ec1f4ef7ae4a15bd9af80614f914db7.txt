<h2>Workforce Coordinator (Atlanta, GA)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">The Workforce Coordinator will evaluate staffing levels, schedule adherence, call patterns, and work volume on a real-time basis to ensure service and operational goals are met. The Workforce Coordinator is responsible for creating and distributing daily reports throughout the organization. Strong Excel skills are required with this position. This position works closely with sales leadership, operations teams, HR, and other internal business teams both locally and across our other locations.
<br><br>
<b>Major Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Manage and monitor call volume and staffing data.</li>
<li>Create and distribute daily reports, accurately and according to deadlines.</li>
<li>Analyze and interpret the information to make recommendations for adjustments to staffing levels to ensure service level requirements and financial efficiency measurements (to be done continuously throughout the day).</li>
<li>Maintain and update the workforce management systems with daily exceptions.</li>
<li>Ensure the accuracy and timeliness of data by meeting departmental turn-around time goals in responding to requests.</li>
<li>Develop strong working relationships with other groups within the organization to ensure efficient and effective problem solving and issue resolution.</li>
<li>Analyze historical trends of contact distribution, AHT, contacts per hour, agent productivity, etc.</li>
<li>Perform on-going analysis including tracking and analyzing performance by individual and by team and reporting actual performance to standards; analyze performance trends and impact on goal achievements.</li>
<li>Communicate with management to aid in resolutions to issues affecting Sales Professionals relating to staffing or scheduling.</li>
<li>Process requests that come in to scheduling via the scheduling line as well as the scheduling mailbox.</li>
<li>Assist the WFM team as needed to achieve goals and deadlines.</li>
</ul>
