<p><b>
JOB SUMMARY:</b></p>
<p>
The Senior Accountant maintains primary responsibility for the general ledger and the month-end close. This position will work with the Director of Finance in overseeing bi-weekly payroll processing, revenue reporting, inventory and accounts payable. This position also performs analysis, reconciliation, month-end closing procedures and reporting on all General Ledger accounts.</p>
<p><b>
JOB DESCRIPTION:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Revenue Reconciliation</li>
<li>Reconcile monthly revenue for all programs in general ledger. Including all deferred revenue and unbilled revenue.</li>
<li>Process monthly billing and revenue processing for marketing division.</li>
<li>Accounts Receivable</li>
<li>Reconcile accounts receivable invoices, cash receipts and other cash flow documents and information.</li>
<li>Reconcile general ledger and accounts receivable subledger.</li>
<li>Manage bi-weekly accounts receivable aging reporting and analysis.</li>
<li>Purchasing/Inventory/Vendor Management</li>
<li>Manage vendor credit applications.</li>
<li>Oversee/manage the inventory pricing, physical count process and general ledger reconciliation/accounting for usage.</li>
<li>Partner with CMC’s Operations department personnel for vendor selection, unit price monitoring, and part and procurement changes.</li>
<li>Train employees on the purchasing processes.</li>
<li>Accounts Payable</li>
<li>Reconcile vendor invoices ensuring proper accounting classifications of costs and accounting periods.</li>
<li>Oversee the preparation of sales and use tax filings and remittances, as needed.</li>
<li>Reconcile general ledger and accounts payable subledger.</li>
<li>Fixed Assets</li>
<li>Manage fixed assets module including additions, disposals and depreciation.</li>
<li>Reconcile general ledger and fixed asset subledger.</li>
<li>Assist with fixed asset audits.</li>
<li>General Ledger/ Financial Statements</li>
<li>Prepare and review revenue, expenses, invoices, and other accounting/accrual entries as required.</li>
<li>Compile and analyze financial information to prepare entries to general ledger accounts.</li>
<li>Monitor and review accounting and related system reports for accuracy and completeness. Perform accounting analysis to identify anomalies and ensure utmost accuracy.</li>
<li>Maintain sub ledger accounts and needed data tracking to reconcile and explain the general ledger for management decision making.</li>
<li>Resolve accounting discrepancies.</li>
<li>Assist in overseeing fiscal activities ensuring the accurate and timely processing of revenue, accounts receivables, accounts payable, inventory, fixed assets, core employee expense reports, cash control, payroll processing and total corporate payroll tax compliance.</li>
<li>Assist with the activities of the corporate accounting department for the accurate and timely dissemination of financial management reports including, but not limited to, internal and external monthly financial statements and annual audits and annual budgets.</li>
<li>Participate in a wide variety of special projects and compile a variety of special reports.</li>
<li>Communicate with co-workers, management, clients and others in a courteous and professional manner.</li>
<li>Provide status of financial condition by collecting, interpreting, and reporting financial data.</li>
<li>Prepare special reports by collecting, analyzing, and summarizing information and trends.</li>
<li>Assist with coaching and mentoring accounting staff to ensure the corporate accounting department is performing effectively and at their highest potential to achieve program and company organizational goals.</li>
<li>Protect operations by keeping financial information and plans confidential.</li>
<li>Contribute to team effort by accomplishing related results as needed.</li>
<li>Perform other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>JOB QUALIFICATIONS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree required. Accounting or finance major preferred.</li>
<li>CPA a plus.</li>
<li>5+ years in accounting environment preferred.</li>
<li>Strong knowledge of finance, accounting, budgeting, and cost control principles including Generally Accepted Accounting Principles (GAAP).</li>
<li>General ledger experience required.</li>
<li>Knowledge of financial and accounting software applications. Knowledge of Great Plains and SalesForce a plus.</li>
<li>Knowledge of federal and state financial regulations, sound business practices, and good record keeping methodologies.</li>
<li>Ability to analyze financial data, statements and projections. Proactive analysis and suggestions for continuous process improvements and efficiencies is expected.</li>
<li>Resourcefulness to meet tight deadlines and flexibility with fluctuating priorities.</li>
<li>Strong ability to communicate clearly and effectively, in speech and writing, with customers, staff, managers, supervisors, contractors and clients.</li>
<li>Strong analytical, organizational, administrative and communication skills.</li>
<li>Ability to handle confidential information with the highest level of integrity and ethics.</li>
<li>Although this position is based in Fort Washington, PA, travel to other offices may be occasionally required.</li>
<li>Knowledge, practical experience and competency in Microsoft Office Suite applications including Word, Excel and Outlook (PowerPoint a plus). Advanced knowledge of Excel.</li>
<li>Perform the essential functions and physical demands of the position with or without accommodation.</li>
</ul>

<li>Visual capacity enabling frequent use of computer equipment.</li>
