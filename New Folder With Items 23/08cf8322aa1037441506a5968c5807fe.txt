<p>We are currently seeking a <b>Manager of Customer Success &amp; Inside Sales</b> to join our team.</p>
<p><b>
Job Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage customer support team to ensure an outstanding customer experience and increased customer satisfaction</li>
<li>Manage an inside sales team to drive sales opportunities and effectiveness</li>
<li>Manage processes focused on improved customer engagements during visits to AV.
</li>
<li>Provide in house support to Business Development to complete sales</li>
<li>Help Set up and maintain accounts in AV Capture (AV’s Customer Relationship Management tool)</li>
<li>Work cross-functionally with Finance, Contracts, Trade Compliance, Program Managers, Quality, Shipping, etc. to coordinate and support business objectives.</li>
<li>Serve as key customer liaison between Fulfilment, Account Manager and Customer</li>
<li>Work with SCARA team and Sub Contracts team to assist in getting agreements executed in a timely manner
</li>
<li>Conduct follow-up calls/ meetings as needed to support Business Develop</li>
<li>Facilitate introduction to customer service support team</li>
<li>Lead the team in the development of effective sales presentations tailored to the customer</li>
<li>Establish, track and analyze program performance metrics &amp; schedule and take action to address issues and/or opportunities</li>
<li>Comprehend, communicate, and manage programs in alignment with business unit priorities</li>
<li>Lead with rapid agility to deal with shifting priorities, demands, and timelines</li>
<li>Make sound/data-driven decisions to ensure program cost, schedule and performance targets are adhered to/met</li>
<li>Submit reports in timely manner</li>
<li>Communicate regularly with management</li>
</ul>

</li>

</li>

<p><b>
Who We Are</b></p>
<p>
Based in California, AeroVironment (AVAV) is a global leader in unmanned aircraft systems (UAS) and tactical missile systems. Founded in 1971 by celebrated physicist and engineer, Dr. Paul MacCready, we’ve been at the leading edge of technical innovation for more than 45 years. Be a part of the team that developed the world’s most widely used military drones and created the first submarine-launched reconnaissance drone, and has seven innovative vehicles that are part of the Smithsonian Institution’s permanent collection in Washington, DC.</p>
<p>
Join us today in developing the next generation of small UAS and tactical missile systems that will deliver more actionable intelligence to our customers so they can proceed with certainty – and succeed.</p>
<p><b>
What We Do</b></p>
<p>
Building on a history of technological innovation, AeroVironment designs, develops, produces, and supports an advanced portfolio of unmanned aircraft systems (UAS) and tactical missile systems. Agencies of the U.S. Department of Defense and allied military services use the company’s hand-launched UAS to provide situational awareness to tactical operating units through real-time, airborne reconnaissance, surveillance, and target acquisition.</p>
