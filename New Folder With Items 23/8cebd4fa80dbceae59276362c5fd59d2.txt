<p><b>Account Manager</b><br>
</p>
<p>
Cision, a leading global provider of software and services to communications and marketing professionals, is looking for an Account Manager based out of our Chicago office. Our flagship product, the Cision Communications Cloud, is an industry-leading comms platform that delivers best-in-class earned media monitoring and analytics, a comprehensive media and influencer contact database, newswire distribution, business results attribution capabilities, and much more.</p>
<p>
Cision's recent acquisitions of startups TrendKite and Falcon.io provided the company with additional innovative technology and a focus on user experience that set the company's software apart from anything else in the comms industry. With these additional capabilities, the next generation Cision Communications Cloud platform delivers an improved user experience, more intelligent and interactive reporting, and better campaign execution for PR and comms teams.</p>
<p>
By investing in our brand, our team, and our technology, we are leading a revolution in the PR and communications industry. We invest in our people through training and professional development and we empower them to grow their careers - because the most important measure of our success is yours.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>
As an Account Manager, you will work with an established client base and be responsible for maintaining and growing sales from these accounts.</p>
<ul>
<li>
Maintain high on-time renewal rates within a portfolio of current customers.</li>
<li>
Grow business through cross-selling and up-selling.</li>
<li>
Provide expertise and solutions based selling with the entire suite of Cision products and services.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
The ability to sell value at all levels over the phone.</li>
<li>
Sold On-Demand, SaaS (Software as a Service) solution or subscription-based product to a diverse customer base.</li>
<li>
A proven track record of growing business from an established client base.</li>
<li>
Strong time management skills.</li>
<li>
A consistent and documented track record for achieving monthly/quarterly/annual target metrics.</li>
<li>
2-5 years of sales experience focused on growing existing business or new business</li>
<li>
Large account management or project management experience</li>
<li>
Proven track record for closing large and mid size companies</li>
<li>
A communications, public relations or journalism background preferred</li>
<li>
Ability to adapt, overcome objections, and learn complex solutions</li>
<li>
Experience selling in the PR vertical is preferred</li>
</ul>
