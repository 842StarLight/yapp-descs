DIRECTOR OF OPERATIONS</p>
<p>
Summary:</p>
<p>
The Director of Operations is responsible for the daily leadership and operational oversight and the hotel. Position is directly responsible for the following departments: housekeeping, front office, night audit, kitchen, banquets, restaurant, bar and Starbucks. This position is responsible for overall results, as well as the development and execution of strategies that support and strengthen the property. Responsibilities include driving Operational Excellence which is defined as upper quartile performance in financial, individual guest, meeting planner, STR market, TripAdvisor, Associate and Management Opinion survey as compared to the appropriate benchmarks or comp sets.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Duties/Responsibilities:</p>
<ul><li> Develop and manage execution of Rooms/Food and Beverage division budgets and revenue forecasts. Develop and implement controls for expense management. Ensure staff is utilizing labor management tools to schedule and control labor costs.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Oversee Manager on Duty program.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Supervise and execute timely communication to various departments.</li></ul>
