<h2>Project Accountant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>What we're looking for:</p>
<p>
The Project Accountant handles the day-to-day financial operations of Superfly agency and IP experiences on a project basis. This person is an extremely organized, detail-oriented, and has experience in project accounting. Our ideal candidate thrives in a fast-paced environment, has the ability to work with lots of different personalities, and is able to provide accurate information against tight deadlines. This role requires an enormous amount of discretion as well as strong communication and financial accounting skills.</p>
<p>
Here's what you'll do:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Process all Accounts Payable for Superfly's live experience and agency projects upon receipt of a proper invoice, vendor W9, and fully executed vendor contract</li>
<li>
Provide consistent reporting of financial activity of various projects to all staff stakeholders</li>
<li>
Develop and manage project-wide reporting procedures</li>
<li>
Coordinate and process all temporary employee payrolls leading up to and through the conclusion of the event</li>
<li>
Work with Project Managers, Superfly Producers, and any other appropriate project leads on a daily basis</li>
<li>
Assist with cash management and project-based accounting</li>
<li>
Manage on-site cash handling and cash control functions</li>
<li>
Ensure all financial records are accurate, complete, and in compliance with Superfly and project accounting principles</li>
<li>
Produce regular labor reports, by department, to update and support the management team.</li>
<li>
Support internal finance team with timely reporting and royalty calculations</li>
<li>
Calculate and report royalties</li>
<li>
Assist Manager, Finance Operations with employee reimbursements, company Amex expense reports, and Accounts Receivable</li>
<li>
Assist finance team with monthly close</li>
<li>
Assist finance team with creating new policies, procedures, and controls for the department and company</li>
</ul>
