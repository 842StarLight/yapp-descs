<h2>Marketing Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>Landry’s has an open position for a <b>Marketing Assistant </b>at our Corporate Headquarters in the Galleria area of Houston, TX. We need high caliber, experienced individuals to join our organization. If your skills and talents are a match for our needs, we can open the door to career opportunities!<br>
<br>
<b>Job Description:</b> Assist and support the day to day marketing needs of the Landry’s Restaurant division.<br>
<br>
<b>Requirements:</b> Previous marketing/advertising and restaurant experience is required. Ideal candidate will have experience managing the ad process, knowledge and experience with digital media campaigns/promotions, excellent phone communication skills, great organizational skills, and excellent writing skills. Individual needs to be very detail oriented, self-motivated, highly organized and conscientious on follow through.<br>
<br>
<b>Essential Duties/Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>
Open work orders and trafficking jobs</li>
<li>
Support execution of all promotions, working with other marketing team members to formulate plans to utilize various media including print, broadcast, outdoor and internal collateral pieces</li>
<li>
Research opportunities and proposals</li>
<li>
Assist the Brand Manager in facilitating the production of promotional materials; ordering collateral supplies and working with managers on developing and implementing local marketing opportunities</li>
<li>
Alongside other marketing team members, work with vendors and agencies to manage creative process through final implementation, including production, photo shoots for TV and print</li>
<li>
Communicate with other department heads, Purchasing, Training, IT, Culinary, etc. to fully review all action plans related to quarterly promotions to ensure all aspects of the promotion are executed fully and successfully.</li>
<li>
Field sales calls</li>
<li>
Keeping track of production costs and expenditures</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>Education: </b>Bachelor's degree in Business/Marketing or a related discipline from an accredited college or university is required.<br>
<br>
<b>Additional Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities: </b>Professional personal presentation with cordial one-on-one presence; ability to negotiate with the media; comfortable in working with all levels of employees; capable of additional responsibility and growth in the Marketing department; able to work in a fast-paced, multi-task, hands-on environment; ability to establish and maintain a positive and professional working relationship with all individuals; listens carefully and understands various points of view; ability to perform as a team member and possess strong computer skills.<br>
