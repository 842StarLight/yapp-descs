<h2>Account Executive in Training</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Come join a fast paced, growing team where your career potential is limitless!</p>
<p><b>
In this role, you will:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Generate and acquire new business through aggressive lead generation and outbound freight inquiry.</li>
<li>Knowledgeably negotiate rates with customers for transportation services that fit customers’ needs.</li>
<li>Respond to sales inquiries and use the team’s knowledge of the market to provide strategic pricing.</li>

<p><b>What are we looking for?</b></p>
<ul>
<li>1+ years sales experience preferred (cold calling, relationship building over the phone or via email, tracking of leads through spreadsheets or CRM software)</li>
<li>Hunter mindset</li>
<li>Comfortable with computers and even more comfortable with dialing phones.</li>
<li>Proactive follow up and communication abilities</li>
<li>Team oriented with a spirit for fun!</li>
</ul>
<p><b>The Good Stuff:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>For the first 16 weeks (or less!), earn a base salary and be a part of a top quality training and mentorship program to prepare you for success.</li>
<li>After “graduating” from our training program, unleash your potential with a base salary + commission program.</li>
<li>We offer health, dental, and vision coverage options.</li>
<li>Our Paid Time Off (PTO) program has quick growth with work/life balance in mind!</li>
</ul>
