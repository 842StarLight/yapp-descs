<h2>Senior Network Solutions Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>What you’ll be doing...</b>
</p>
<p>The Senior Consultant will be responsible for the design, implementation, and handoff of complex network solutions spanning multiple technologies for a wide range of customers, from small to large enterprise, while providing senior technical leadership within the immediate team and the PS organization as a whole. Additional responsibilities include high level design, level of effort scoping, pricing, and management of partner selection in support of Professional Services presales activities.</p>
<p>
In this role, you'll be responsible for:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Develop trusted technical advisor relationships with internal organizations</li>
<li>
Consistently demonstrate the value of Verizon Network PS by identifying areas of improvement for the Network PS team, maintaining a high level of quality and professionalism within assigned tasks, and increasing organizational exposure through direct engagement with pre and post sales organizations in multiple capacities.</li>
<li>
Provide senior leadership both within the Network PS team as well as within assigned engagements.</li>
<li>
Evolve and support the growth of the network PS practice through offering development, engagement with partner vendors to identify and vet new technologies and proposed solutions, and assisting with the vetting of potential team members.</li>
<li>
Sustain a leadership role within team through mentorship, sharing experience and knowledge through technical sessions, and leading by example, both through professional interactions and the continued pursuit of expert level knowledge within multiple fields.</li>
<li>
Accept individual ownership of critical issues, creatively thinking through to develop resolutions.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
What we’re looking for...</b>
</p>
<p>You’ll need to have:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree or four or more years of work experience</li>
<li>
Four or more years of relevant work experience</li>
<li>
Sixyears of technical experience and increasing responsibility (team lead, mentoring, and technical lead for large, multi-technology deployments)</li>
<li>
Data Center design and implementation experience in large scale Enterprise (Technologies include: Cisco, Arista, NSX, ACI, Palo Alto, Fortinet, F5, NetScaler, etc)</li>
<li>
Cloud design and implementation experience (Technologies include: AWS, Azure, Google, etc)</li>
<li>
Programming/Scripting knowledge (Technologies include: Perl, Python, Java, C#, C++ etc)</li>
<li>
Working knowledge of computer platforms (Technologies include: VMware, Windows/*nix servers, etc)</li>
</ul>
<p>
Even better if you have:</p>
<ul>
<li>
A Degree</li>
<li>
Demonstrated expertise in correlating business drivers and needs (financial drivers, impacts, and restrictions) into technical solutions, on proposed solutions</li>
<li>
SDWAN experience a plus (Technologies include: Versa, Viptela, Silverpeak, Velo, etc)</li>
</ul>
