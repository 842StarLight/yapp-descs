<h2>Materials Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>This position oversees activities of a staff of three comprising the Purchasing Department and Engineering Release Coordinator who procure materials, equipment, and services, monitor and maintain inventories of raw materials used in production assembly and service part sales, and integrate engineered product documentation into the rest of the organization.</p>
<p>Responsibilities include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Insuring contracts with suppliers are consistent with corporate objectives.</li>
<li>Establishing and maintaining appropriate policies and procedures for the assigned functions.</li>
<li>Participating in the management teamâ€™s Strategic Planning Process.</li>
<li>Meeting fluctuating seasonal demand by flexing inventory levels supplied through a global supply chain that harmonizes with cash-flow targets.</li>
<li>Executing Buy America financial calculations and issuing accurate, auditable certifications.</li>
<li>Operating within a wide array of government regulations.</li>
<li>Standard Cost forecasting.</li>
</ul>
<p>Position Requires:</p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of 8 or more years manufacturing purchasing management experience in a multi-site environment operating under ISO Quality architecture.</li>
<li>Excellent computer skills including integrated manufacturing software systems and a thorough understanding how all modules link and interact across the enterprise.</li>
<li>College degree with strong preference for a sciences-oriented program. Additional inventory and/or procurement professional certifications are a plus.</li>
<li>Ability to solve complex supply chain issues and effectively communicate solutions to all stakeholders.</li>
<li>Knowledge of and ability to interpret and apply complex government regulations, specifically:</li>
<li>Importing (U.S. Customs documents, HTS, record keeping, tariffs and duties, brokerage POA, import bonding)</li>
<li>Transportation (FMVSS302, Buy America, Refrigerant Handling)</li>
<li>Department of Defense FAR flow-downs</li>
</ul>
