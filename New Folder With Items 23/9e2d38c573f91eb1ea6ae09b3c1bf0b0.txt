<h2>Business Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>Description</b><br>
The Clinic Manager will be responsible and accountable for leading and managing the clinic. The manager is instrumental in defining the mission, goals, budget and performance standards for the clinic and communicating it to staff.</p>
<p><b>
Planning and Prioritizing</b></p>
<ul><li>Ensure that office policies are followed, and consistency is obtained through monthly team meetings, and evaluations with team members on a yearly basis.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Establish quarterly and annual department goals and strategic objectives; establishes priorities, defines resource limitations, participates directly in various projects, monitors projects and results.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Monitors operations and capital expenditure budgets.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Recommends short- and long-term goals, develops objectives and the framework for long range planning.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Schedule team members work hours.</li></ul>
<p><b>
Information Gathering</b></p>
<ul><li>Oversee verification and authorization process with insurance companies for all out-patients.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Gather data/information from team and other departments to implement changes where necessary.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Oversees daily office operations and delegates authority to assigned team members</li></ul>
<p><b>
Decision-Making</b></p>
<ul><li>Monitor and measure resource needs for department and adjusts where necessary.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ensure financial policies are followed regarding upfront collections.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ensure that policies are followed, and consistency is attained through meetings with management and team.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Determine appropriate operational changes and coordinate procedural and systems changes as necessary.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Interview and hire new team members for clinics.</li></ul>
<p><b>
Communication</b></p>
<ul><li>Interacts and communicates with physicians, support team, and other health care professionals to facilitate department operations.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Addresses and responds to concerns or grievances proffered by physicians, team and patients.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Answer or source patient inquiries to the appropriate party.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Assist team members and other hospital departments with questions.</li></ul>
<p><b>
Teamwork</b></p>
<ul><li>Coordinate and collaborate with Network leadership and management team.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Work with various departments and physician clinics to optimize the patient experience.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Organize and facilitate Practice Operating meetings.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Meet with hospital leadership team monthly and join in committees as needed.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Oversees web sites and advertising for departments and works in conjunction with the marketing team.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Develop relationships with community members and surrounding communities.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Evaluates team, provides counsel, monitors performance, directs orientation, provides guidance and constructively influences team morale.</li></ul>
<p><b>
Quality</b></p>
<ul><li>Establishes workflows to effectively meet Quality Metric criteria, using the current software program.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Complies with mandatory regulatory requirements and participates in continuous improvement activities as assigned to ensure health care service quality. Monitors and evaluates staff member adherence to these requirements as related to quality improvement efforts.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Assists in developing and implementing short- and long-term work plans and objectives for clerical and clinical functions.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manage patient records within the IT system (practice perfect)</li></ul>
<ul><li>Negotiate equipment expenses</li></ul>
<ul><li>Support finance and accounting</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>
Providing goals vs actuals monthly hours</li>
<li>
Payroll</li>
<li>
Accounts Payable and Accounts Receivable</li>
<li>
Billing and Collections</li>
<li>
Update weekly division billable hours tracker</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Manage corporate calendar</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>
Fundraisers</li>
<li>
Events</li>
<li>
Networking</li>
<li>
Team Building events</li>
<li>
Retreats</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Work Effectiveness</b></p>
<ul><li>Ensure productivity by your team as well as quality of work.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Streamline processes between clinics and hospital departments, as well as clinic to clinic.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Establishes and maintains an efficient and responsive patient flow system</li></ul>
<p><b>
Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities</b>:</p>
<ul><li>Entrepreneurial Spirit. We are a growing organization and need team members who thrive in a rapidly changing environment. Flexibility and a demonstrated ability to deal with ambiguity, while managing multiple priorities and projects in a fast-paced, innovative organization is required.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Client Focus. A demonstrated track record of proactively engaging leaders and employees at all levels with a strong degree of professional maturity and the ability to influence outcomes is essential.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong Communication Skills. Excellent verbal and written skills with an ability to effectively work with all levels of the organization and leadership styles. Strong business acumen with proven experience translating business needs into actions that will move the business forward.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Enthusiasm: High energy level and a driving since of urgency with a continuous improvement mindset along with a strong desire and ability to mentor and coach employees is required.</li></ul>
<p><b>
Preferred Education and Experience:</b></p>
<ul><li>BS/BA in Management, Business, Organizational Development or equivalent experience.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Minimum of 5 years-7years clinic management</li></ul>
<ul><li>Exceptional interpersonal skills, emotional intelligence and ability to maintain a high level of confidentiality</li></ul>
<ul><li>Solid proficiency in Microsoft suite (Word, Excel, Powerpoint, Outlook &amp; Sharepoint)</li></ul>
<ul><li>Excellent oral and written communication skills</li></ul>
<ul><li>Exceptional problem-solving capability</li></ul>
<ul><li>Self-starter who can work independently, seeking answers when necessary and motivated to seek out areas of improvement and opportunity</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must be able to work in a fast-paced environment while managing workload and adapting to changes</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to prioritize multiple projects simultaneously</li></ul>
<ul><li>Prior management experience leading professional administrative staff</li></ul>
