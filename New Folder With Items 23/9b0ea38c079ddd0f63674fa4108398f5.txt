<div><b>Student Programmer Overview</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Student programmers learn our business operations by taking on challenging projects supporting the Commercial Sales Organization at Anheuser-Busch. We recruit forward-thinking, results-driven students to find innovative ways to help take our business to the next level. Employment objectives usually focus on hours up to full-time (40 per week) in summer and part-time (10 to 20) during non-summer periods. We have a long history of talented performers leveraging their strong experience into full-time careers with the world’s #1 brewer.</div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
<div><b>
Responsibilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Develop report prototypes, create processes to gain data insights, and suggest approaches for data extraction, front-end report models, and sales analyses</li>
<li>
Support the Commercial Sales and Marketing departments for Anhesuer-Busch InBev North America with market insights, trend analyses, forecasting, and ad hoc reports to meet customer requests</li>
</ul>
<br>

<b>Job Qualifications</b><div>
</div>
<div><b>
Requirements:</b></div>
<div><div>
<div><div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree IN PROGRESS in MIS, Information Technology Management, Computer Science, Data Science, Computer Engineering, or related field</li>
<li>
Proficiency with Microsoft Office applications, especially strong Excel skills with complex formulas and macros</li>
<li>
Proficiency with object-oriented and statistical programming (VBA, SAS, R, etc.) a plus.</li>
<li>
Experience with relational databases (Teradata, SQL Server, Oracle, etc.)</li>
<li>
Experienced designing data visualization and BI tools in Power BI, Azure Data Factory, Qlik Sense, Tableau, etc. a plus.</li>
<li>
Ability to quickly learn new software applications and tools, grasp complex business processes, and implement process improvements - particularly automation efforts</li>
<li>
General knowledge of profit and loss statements, revenue generation and margin calculations
</li>
<li>Strong analytical, problem solving, and math skills</li>
<li>
Strong written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>
Ability to multi-task and prioritize workload in a high-pressure and changing work environment</li>
<li>
Ability to work within a team environment and have strong organizational skills to support multiple business functions</li>
</ul>
