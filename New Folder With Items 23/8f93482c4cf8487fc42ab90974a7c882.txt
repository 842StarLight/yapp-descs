<h2>Customer Success Manager- Business Applications Healthcare</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>Microsoft envisions a world where passionate innovators come to collaborate, envisioning what can be and taking their careers places they simply couldn’t anywhere else. This is a world of more possibility, more innovation, more openness, and sky’s-the-limit thinking – a cloud-enabled world.<br>
Microsoft aspires to help our customers achieve their own digital transformation, leveraging the power of Microsoft Cloud solutions and services. To this end, Microsoft is investing in a dedicated Customer Success team that will help Microsoft customers successfully adopt Microsoft Cloud solution and services.<br>
We are looking for a Business Applications Customer Success Manager for our Healthcare business to drive successful adoption and expansion of Business Applications Dynamics and Power Platform workloads within her/his accounts. The CSM role will collaboratively drive change management and adoption activities with key Business Decision Makers, help reduce implementation risk, drive usage of existing Dynamics workloads, and identify opportunities in her/his accounts. The CSM is also a key part of our customer facing sales teams and will be called on frequently to leverage their deep knowledge of CRM or ERP and the Power Platform to support sales growth.</div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Responsibilities</b></h2>
<div>
The Business Applications Customer Success Manager role in the Healthcare team will enable our customers to realize business value from their Dynamics investment and make our customers passionate advocates of Microsoft. Key responsibilities include:<br>
<ul>
<li>Create value for customers by ensuring they clearly define business outcomes and then build a “success plan” with the appropriately identified objectives, stakeholders, milestones, risks and metrics needed to achieve them</li>
<li>Be accountable to drive high usage of Dynamics within customers by driving the creation and execution of a thorough and action-oriented adoption Plan</li>
<li>Influence customers’, partners’ and colleague sponsorship and governance to ensure that customer, partner, FastTrack, and support resources align for expedient and predictable D365 deployment and upgrades</li>
<li>Leverage D365 functional and technical use-case demonstration expertise to increase the customer’s usage of existing workloads; and to clear technical, competitive and security blockers to D365 and Power Platform usage.</li>
<li>Proactively, using Challenger skills, align D365 roadmap with customer needs to identify new workloads and expansion opportunities for D365 and Power Platform in each account to drive value both for the customer and for Microsoft</li>
<li>Orchestrate leads for cross-workload solutions with Technical Sales aligned to other Microsoft products and services, such as Azure, Office 365, Enterprise Mobility, and Productivity.</li>
<li>Build, maintain and leverage strong relationships with Business Decision Makers (BDMs) and IT Decision Makers (ITDMs) within each customer to influence solution adoption, create strong support for new opportunities and to secure their willingness to advocate on Dynamics and Microsoft’s behalf</li>
<li>Orchestrate and gain strong buy-in with multiple external (e.g. customer’s Partner) and internal Microsoft sales and services teams and be highly connected to Microsoft Product Team for closed-loop feedback throughout the customer’s Dynamics lifecycle</li>
</ul>
</div>
