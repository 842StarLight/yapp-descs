<h2>Program Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Delta Gear is looking for hard-working, reliable, responsible, and dependable individuals that take pride in their work and are committed to establishing a career in the manufacturing industry.</b></p>
<p><b>Tasks:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Serve as the liaison between the company and customer for all program matters and is the key customer contact during the new product introduction process</li>
<li>Obtain all customer program requirements, targets and dates</li>
<li>Lead the planning and implementation of project and direct their support team</li>
<li>Facilitate the definition of project scope, goals and deliverables</li>
<li>Develop full scale project plans and provide all aspects of customer support as required</li>
<li>Lead the cross functional team to ensure customer milestones are met, manage open issues, clear roadblocks and raise major program issues to appropriate senior management internally as well as the customer</li>
<li>Ensure program costs meet financial targets prior to launch</li>
<li>Track project deliverables using appropriate tools</li>
<li>Provide direction and support to their assigned project team</li>
<li>Constantly monitor and report on progress of the project to all stakeholders</li>
<li>Implement and manage project changes to achieve project goals</li>
<li>Provide program closure lesson learned document with assessment of results for future projects</li>
<li>Responsible for training the program team on procedures and processes as well as enhancing the NPI program structure</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Skills/Key Competencies:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Proven ability to learn customer-specific applications and processes</li>
<li>Proof of hands-on practical experience leading and coordinating organization resources and teams</li>
<li>Must have the ability to lead and influence to get work done through others</li>
<li>Proven ability to coordinate activities between engineering, manufacturing, quality, procurement, sales and outside customers</li>
<li>Proven resourcefulness and presentation / communication skills</li>
<li>Component manufacturing and processing knowledge</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelorâ€™s degree in engineering or business. PMI certification is a plus</li>
<li>Minimum 5 years work experience in sales or Engineering environment with significant customer contact, including supervisory experience; experience with aerospace OE or supplier is preferred</li>
<li>Significant customer contact requires the ability to foster and maintain positive internal and external relationships</li>
<li>Must have highly developed organizational skills including the ability to prioritize, handle multiple project simultaneously and meet deadlines</li>
<li>Knowledge of both theoretical and practical aspects of project management</li>
<li>Knowledge of project management techniques and tools including risk and change management</li>
<li>Proficient in project management software (MS Project, Word, Excel)</li>
</ul>
