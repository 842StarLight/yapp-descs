<h2>Dir, Retail Technology & Innovations</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Visionworks needs an IT Director to modernize Retail platform for over 700 stores. We need a hands-on leader who enjoys rolling up their sleeves and working with the team to implement best in class modern, scale-able and robust solutions to increase patient satisfaction. The successful candidate will be passionate about technology with an obsession for best customer experience and extremely knowledgeable about cloud based critical solutions in Retail and Vision care industry. You will be responsible for our Point Of Sale (POS) and Managed Vision Care (MVC) team and will report directly to the VP of Retail Technology and Innovations. Your operations and system maintenance experience with large scale products will help us deliver best in class experience to patients, doctors and our team member with modern technology.
<br><br>
<b>Essential Responsibilities:</b><br>
1. Coach, mentor and invest in team members to be the best engineers
<br>2. Communicate across functions and drive engineering initiatives
<br>3. Be a subject matter expert in a product domain
<br>4. Define and influence product strategy for Retail in regards to POS and Managed Vision Care.
<br><br>
5. Develop and instill a strong foundation for Application lifecycle management including governance, development, and maintenance of the Retail application software.
<br>6. Develop and implement architectural standards in partnership with Architect team.
<br>7. Drive technical design and decisions in partnership with Architect team.
<br>8. Partner with business to prioritize and deliver new capabilities.
<br>9. Define standards and methodologies for best practices; ensure compliance of these practices.
<br>10. Work closely with store operations and Managed Vision Care to ensure that Information Technology has goals that are aligned with and support broader Company objectives.
<br>11. Communicate business objectives, direct staff and perform activities to achieve business objectives.
<br>12. Responsible for hiring, payroll tracking/performance reviews, mentoring, coaching, and developing information technology associates within area of responsibility.
<br>13. Communicate company values; provide leadership, tools, training for enhanced growth and productivity.
<br>14. Identify, recommend and implement new technologies and/or upgrades as they become available.
<br><br>
<b>Education:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Preferred bachelor's degree in related field or equivalent combination or experience and education</li>
</ul>
<b>Experience &amp; Background:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Five (5) plus years of experience in leading Information Technology functions.</li>
<li>Prior experience with large scale critical cloud based solution in Retail and/or healthcare industry.</li>
<li>In-depth understanding of Micro services, open source and agile development practices.</li>
<li>Leadership experience in defining the IT Technology vision, strategy and roadmap.</li>
<li>Leading transformational programs and coaching team to deliver results.</li>
</ul>
