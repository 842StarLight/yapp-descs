<h2>Customer Service Rep - Express Agent</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>General Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>
<b>The Express Agent</b> is responsible for processing car rental transactions for Express customers, utilizing approved sales techniques to offer and sell incremental car rental products, and providing customer service with the car rental experience.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Responsibilities/Essential Functions:</b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Process car rental transactions for Express customers, and prepare and create rental agreements through use of computer system.</div></li>
<li><div>
Greet customers, and disclose rates, terms and conditions of rental while using approved sales techniques to encourage customers to utilize incremental car rental products; obtain customer signatures when needed.</div></li>
<li><div>
Assists customers with directions, and review and resolve rental contract and vehicle issues; process vehicle exchanges.</div></li>
<li><div>
Instruct customers on how to update their profiles.</div></li>
<li><div>
Organize and maintain cleanliness of rental office/cashier booth area.</div></li>
<li><div>
Enter and maintain information in location logs, as required.</div></li>
<li><div>
Distribute keys.</div></li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Mandatory Requirements
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div><b>Required Qualifications:</b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Knowledge and skill in the operation of car rental software and hardware</div></li>
<li><div>
Knowledge of car rental operations</div></li>
<li><div>
Ability to manage stressful and unusual situations while maintaining good customer relations</div></li>
<li><div>
Good organizational skills with the ability to write clearly and concisely</div></li>
<li><div>
Ability to speak and understand English</div></li>
<li><div>
Car rental product knowledge and ability to use effective and approved sales techniques, as required</div></li>
<li><div>
Ability to type and perform accurate data entry</div></li>
<li><div>
Ability to process all paper according to policies and procedures</div></li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Required Education/Experience:</b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>
High School Diploma or Equivalent</div></li>
<li><div>
Prefer 9 to 12 months car rental or sales experience</div></li>
<li><div>
Valid driver's license as required by state.</div></li>
<li>
At least 20 years old</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Special Job Dimensions:</b></div>
<ul><li>
Varied hours. Job involves a high level of customer contact, occasionally dealing with disagreeable or irate customers</li></ul>
</div></div></div>
