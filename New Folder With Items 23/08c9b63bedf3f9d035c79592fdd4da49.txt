<p><b>JOB TITLE</b><b>:</b><b> Technical Account Manager</b><br>
<b>REQUISITION: </b><b>DSA32726</b><br>
<b>Location: Redmon WA.</b></p>
<p><b>
OVERVIEW &amp; IMPACT:</b><br>
<br>
This position is to manage the technical account for SSD customers in WA regional region related to Enterprise and Datacenter business to maintain the close customer engineering relationship and SSD product technology leadership to maximize sales contribution.</p>
Create global demand by identifying business opportunities, providing optimal SSD technologies and solutions for future designs for the specific assigned accounts, and managing all technical related issues.<br>
<p><b>Job Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Manage the customer(s) to assure that technology leadership is maintained and sales are enabled and maximized by acting as an escalation path on occasions where there are (internal or external) obstacles impeding key design wins</li>
<li>
Provide strategic input to Samsungâ€™s Headquarters in Korea regarding the industry or application technology trend in order to create new product offerings or to improve the strategic partnership with the customer(s)</li>
<li>
Visit and interact directly with the customer(s) to maintain customer product roadmap updates.</li>
<li>
Track and report project details, design-in, and qualifications plan as well as the competitive analysis in the roadmap, process technology and qualification status.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Required Skills:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
BA/BS required (BSEE or MSEE preferred)</li>
<li>
5+ years of memory/storage engineering experience required</li>
<li>
Prior experience in a technical customer interfacing role required</li>
<li>
Hardware architecture knowledge (hardware and software) preferred</li>
<li>
Ability and willingness to travel ~ 15%</li>
<li>
Strong communication skills</li>
<li>
Prior semiconductor or storage experience preferred</li>
</ul>
<p>
 <b>Preferred Skills:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Basic On-site Debugging Capability (ex. Bus Analyzer, Oscilloscope, etc)</li>
<li>
Test Tool Management (ex. Linux or Windows-based FIO or equivalent test tool)</li>
<li>
Microsoft Project or equivalent schedule management tool</li>
</ul>
