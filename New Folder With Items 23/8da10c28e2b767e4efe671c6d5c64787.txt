<p>Marketing and Communications Specialist - Full-Time, Days - 400 W. River Woods Pkwy, Glendale, WI 53212</p>
<p><b>
Why Join Ascension?</b>
</p>
<div>
<p>Ascension is a faith-based healthcare organization dedicated to transformation through innovation across the continuum of care. As one of the leading non-profit and Catholic health systems in the U.S., Ascension is committed to delivering compassionate, personalized care to all, especially to those most in need. In FY2018, Ascension provided nearly $2 billion in care of persons living in poverty and other community benefit programs.</p>
<p>
Ascension includes approximately 156,000 associates and 34,000 aligned providers. The national health system operates more than 2,600 sites of care – including 151 hospitals and more than 50 senior living facilities – in 21 states and the District of Columbia, while providing a variety of services including physician practice management, venture capital investing, investment management, biomedical engineering, facilities management, clinical care management, information services, risk management, and contracting through Ascension’s own group purchasing organization.</p>
</div>
<p><b>
What You Will Do</b>
</p>
<p>As an Associate with Ascension, you will have the opportunity to initiate, plan and coordinate marketing and/or public relation functions for the organization.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Coordinates and manages events, programs, and screenings sponsored by the organization.</li>
<li>
Coordinates and distributes content for marketing and advertising materials.</li>
<li>
Oversees the logistics and production of marketing materials, campaigns and special projects.</li>
<li>
Organizes events and delivers event materials in a timely and cost-effective manner</li>
<li>
Analyze data to determine marketing campaign efficiency.</li>
<li>
Ensure creative marketing materials undergo regular review and adhere to brand guidelines.</li>
<li>
Develop and communicate marketing plans, campaign results, and project recommendations for senior management team.</li>
<li>
Adept with basic elements and principles of graph design for Brand Central software support.</li>
<li>
Monitor and respond to social media and reviews/engagement and inbound messages for each region, including managing budgets for boosted social media posts and campaigns based on local needs</li>
<li>
Create events around community initiatives on Facebook and promote market stories during health awareness occasions and other seasonal/timely opportunities</li>
<li>
Enhance provider profiles with key access points for a robust online presence, draft and schedule social media posts around market priorities and provider availability/promotion</li>
<li>
Track online campaigns and present summary findings to team</li>
<li>
Engage with PR and Talent Acquisition teams/colleagues to enhance online brand and messaging across all channels</li>
</ul>
