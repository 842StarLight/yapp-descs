<h2>Associate Manager - Account Services</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div><div><div><div><div>We at Best Buy work hard every day to enrich the lives of consumers through technology, whether they come to us online, visit our stores or invite us into their homes. We do this by solving technology problems and addressing key human needs across a range of areas, including entertainment, productivity, communication, food, security and health.<br>
<br>
Within the Media Network at Best Buy, we work with brands and advertisers to plan, execute, and report on media campaigns leveraging digital products that help the customer shop. We use targeting, high-impact ads, and industry leading reporting to create meaningful touchpoints that marry client objectives and shopper needs.<br>
<br>
Our team culture is indicative of a media publisher and client-focused ad agency. We value building relationship with clients and partners, campaign accuracy and speed to market, and industry-leading media capabilities. We are a team that supports one another to ultimately drive success for our clients, customers, and the Best Buy brand. We try new things, we care about the details, and work hard to ensure we support each other and our partners.<br>
<br>
The Associate Manager oversees the post-sale campaign execution of a key category and oversees a team of direct reports. In addition to role modeling a high standard of excellence in campaign management, the Associate Manager will identify and build out operational strategies to help the team work better (internally and cross-functionally) in support of our long-term goals and visions.<br>
<br>
<b>Role Expectations:</b><ul>
<li>
Ownership of strategic initiatives and programs end-to-end.</li>
<li>
Strategically tailor communication to the audience and business need/opportunity.</li>
<li>
Remove obstacles, evolve approaches, and define operations processes.</li>
<li>
Navigate Best Buy across various cross-functional expertises, leveraging ones understanding of the marketing and media organization to define new ways of working.</li>
<li>
Understand the positioning and execution approach of other vehicles across media to position operational improvements or campaign performance improvements.</li>
<li>
Translate leadership vision of marketing and media monetization to actionable operations and processes for the team.</li>
<li>
Strategically plan cross-functionally with media leaders to advise on the development, rollout and implication of new products to the execution and creative workstreams.</li>
<li>
Develop the campaign execution approach including establishing the creative strategy and overseeing the activation of the media plan (ex. kickoff creative brief, route creative for approval, verify deliverable accuracy, relay information to trafficker, QA campaign elements).</li>
<li>
Manage the execution of campaigns post-commitment in a manner that supports the team purpose and values (ex. maintain organized records of campaigns and flights, request inputs from partners, keep partners on track for timing constraints)</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>A successful candidate will have:</b><ul>
<li>
Have marketing, brand, media, publisher or retail experience.</li>
<li>
Ideally have worked in or with an advertising agency.</li>
<li>
Have experience with digital campaigns (content creation, deliverable creation, ad publishing, inventory planning).</li>
<li>
Understand the creative process and partners involved in creating custom creative.</li>
<li>
Have the ability to manage multiple projects at once and stay organized.</li>
<li>
Be able to summarize updates on projects or roadblocks for leadership.</li>
<li>
Communicate clearly (written and verbal) taking into account upstream impacts of timing and decisions.</li>
<li>
Critically consider the impacts of execution decisions on creative, deliverables, linking destination, versions, time in market etc.</li>
<li>
Have a passion for learning process and execution approaches</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>Basic Qualifications:</b><ul>
<li>
6 or more years overall experience in a Retail, Marketing, Media Sales Support Role or Project Management.</li>
<li>
Extremely strong, proactive project planning and management skills.</li>
<li>
Proficiency with Microsoft Office Suite.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>Preferred Qualifications:</b><ul>
<li>
Bachelor's Degree.</li>
<li>
Solid understanding of digital advertising concepts and terms, agency terminology and general practice/process.</li>
<li>
3 or more years work experience within agency or marketing.</li>
<li>
2 or more years work experience digital ad sales organization.</li>
<li>
Experience working with digital advertising agencies implementing / managing complex campaigns, as well as pre- and post-sales experienceMedia Network Sales Associate Manager.</li>
<li>
Solid understanding of general marketing project milestones and digital campaign execution best practices.</li>
<li>
Salesforce Salescloud experience.</li>
</ul>
