<h2>Sales and Investment Center Coordinator - Part Time</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Provide 'whatever it takes' service in the completion and coordination of activities which allow the Sales and Investment Center area to run efficiently and achieve their goals.</p>
<p><b>General Requirements: </b></p>
<p>Financial background helpful</p>
<p>Minimum of 2 years in an administration support position</p>
<p>Professional in appearance, upbeat, energetic, strong communication skills (written and verbal)</p>
<p>Handle multiple commitments and meet deadlines in a fast paced envrionment</p>
<p>Exceptional customer service skills</p>
<p>Excellent computer skills</p>
<p>Strong organization skills</p>
<p>Great attention to detail</p>
<p>Ability to conduct presentations to groups</p>
<p>Ability to work Saturdays and evenings as needed</p>
<p><b>General Duties: </b></p>
<p>Assist Investment Center Consultant with seminars and business development activities</p>
<p>Coordinate any sales or investment center presentations</p>
<p>Schedule and confirm initial and annual review appointments for Investment Center clients</p>
<p>contact potential Investment Center clients to schedule appointments and follow up as needed</p>
<p>maintain securities and insurance advertising logs and securities blotter according to record retention and compliance requirements</p>
<p>Prepare various reports, charts as needed</p>
<p>Coordinate the distribution and revision of all sales and operational materials</p>
<p>Compile and verify information for weekly/monthly reports: sales, member service activity</p>
<p>Job Type: Part-time</p>
