About the job…..<br>
<br>
As an Assistant Property Manager at Pinnacle you are an important piece of the onsite management team. You help guide the ship, so to speak, of a Pinnacle community under the direction of the Property Manager. The Assistant Property Manager is a business leader who focuses on resident customer service and assists in managing operations, leasing activity, renewals, collections, financial reporting, supplies, and communications of a multi-million dollar apartment community. This position requires unmatched customer service and individuals who thrive in a customer-centric, fast-paced environment. Be ready to be busy!</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL JOB FUNCTIONS</b><b>:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Address the concerns of current and prospective residents in a friendly and professional manner.</li>
<li>Helps set the standard on how other staff member engage prospective and current residents.</li>
<li>Tours and leases apartments as necessary.</li>
<li>Helps with training staff as necessary and models effective sales techniques on a daily basis.</li>
<li>Leads rent collection efforts, specifically with delinquent residents. This may involve lease termination and legal action if necessary.</li>
<li>Assist in managing the property’s budget by making sound fiscal decisions to increase the net operating income of the community.</li>
<li>Help maintain the accuracy of resident ledgers and files.</li>
<li>Inspects apartments during move-in and move outs, walking apartments and the community as needed.</li>
<li>Fills the role of acting Property Manager in their absence.</li>
<li>Perform any other related duties as required or assigned.</li>
<br>
</ul>
