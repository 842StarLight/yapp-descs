<h2>Project Manager - Contract to Hire - New York</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<div>Entercom is seeking a self-motivated and passionate Project Manager that will manage digital streaming, video and ad technology project plans for the Radio.com website, mobile app and +100 news, sports &amp; music sites. This Project Manager will coordinate between business and technology teams and serve as the primary point of contact for multiple internal stakeholders, third-party vendors and partners.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
In this role, you will be primarily responsible for coordinating and orchestrating the planning, execution, delivery, and rollout of video, streaming and ad technology solutions. The ideal candidate has hands-on experience with managing deliverables, timelines, and expectations for technology projects.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
This opportunity can sit in our Denver or New York offices.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Responsibilities</div>
<div><ul>
<li>
Coordinate with stakeholders and managers from multiple departments including advertising, sales, product, engineering, and marketing as well as with third-party vendors and partners to prioritize, plan and execute technology solutions that will meet revenue and product goals</li>
<li>
Work with stakeholders and venues to determine and document deliverables, implementation plans, risks and release schedules</li>
<li>
Ensure that project scope and objectives are well defined and technically feasible and facilitate problem-solving and mitigation discussions to address risks</li>
<li>
Manage project status through a tracking system while following project management principles and collaborating on an evolving system of project governance</li>
<li>
Help with day-to-day task management to ensure teams are working and communicating efficiently</li>
<li>
Manage and communicate expectations and status to internal stakeholders and third-party vendors and partners</li>
<li>
Monitor, escalate, and communicate project risks and roadblocks and proactively manage the downstream impact of issues and delays</li>
<li>
Help to guide third-party vendors and partners towards decisions that achieve business objectives and optimize value delivery</li>
<li>
Represent the company in project meetings with third-party vendors and partners</li>
<li>
Help identify opportunities to enhance process, operations, and culture</li>
</ul></div>
</div>
<div>Qualifications:</div>
<div><div><ul>
<li>2+ years of project management experience, directly involved with managing and delivering technology projects</li>
<li>
Excellent oral and written communication skills and experience interacting with both business and technology individuals</li>
<li>
Proven ability to multi-task and proactively follow up on multiple concurrent tasks and high-priority requests</li>
<li>
Experience with project management tools and approaches for tracking statuses and timelines</li>
<li>
Ability to understand different complex digital solutions at a high level</li>
<li>
Strong leadership skills, and the ability to manage partner relationships</li>
<li>
Proven strong problem-solving skills are a necessity</li>
<li>
Experience working with technology teams in an agile environment is a plus</li>
<li>
PMP Certification is preferred</li>
</ul></div></div>
