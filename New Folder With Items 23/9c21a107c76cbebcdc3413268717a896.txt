<h2>Associate Security Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Your Role:</b><br>
<br>
Tenable is currently seeking an Associate Security Engineer to support product demos and evaluations of small and medium sized sales opportunities. The Associate Security Engineer is responsible for identifying and matching technology opportunities with the customer’s business issues and objectives. Assist in developing business value propositions around Tenable products and solutions. Drives sales with technical expertise, account management skills, sales ability, and a superior customer focus. Participates as part of a sales team to achieve or exceed revenue targets.<br>
<br>
<b>Your Opportunity:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Deliver technical presentations and demos of Tenable products</li>
<li>
Manage software trials and "Proof of Concept" evaluations</li>
<li>
Answer technical questions and provide consultative security &amp; compliance expertise</li>
<li>
Up to 10% travel</li>
</ul>
<b>
What You'll Need:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Experience with Nessus or other network scanning technologies</li>
<li>
Solid foundational knowledge of TCP/IP and network security concepts</li>
<li>
Experience with Redhat Enterprise Server at the command-line level</li>
<li>
Knowledge of compliance standards (e.g., PCI, FISMA, SOX, NERC)</li>
</ul>
<b>
And Ideally:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree</li>
<li>
Sales experience</li>
</ul>
