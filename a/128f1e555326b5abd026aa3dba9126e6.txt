<p><b>Job Title – </b><b>Controlled Material Account Manager-Industrial Security.</b><br>
<b>Job Location – Rochester, NY</b><br>
<b>Job Code – CS20191309-33352</b><br>

<p><b>
Job Description:</b></p>
<p>
The organization is looking to fill the Security Support position to perform a variety of procedural tasks enacting the Sensitive Controlled Items (SCI) program under the security organization. The incumbent will perform primary duties as the Selective Availability Anti-Spoofing Modules (SAASM) account manager (SAM) responsible for consistent collaboration and coordination with cognizant U.S. Government agencies to ensure compliant accountability for SAASM controlled items within manufacturing, product service, demo and engineering operations as mandated by government regulatory policies. The SAM performs business enabling responsibilities ensuring total accountability and compliance of SAASM related development activities that enables implementation of business manufacturing operations and facilitates new business opportunities.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
Primary business representative responsible for collaboration and coordination with cognizant U.S. Air Force Global Positioning Systems Directorate (GPSD)</li>
<li>
Researches, interprets and implements government regulatory guidance while maintaining knowledge and awareness of interdisciplinary compliance requirements impacting enterprise sales, finance, trade compliance, engineering and production.</li>
<li>
Obtains rulings and acceptable deviations for regulatory compliance from government cognizant agency (GPSD).</li>
<li>
Develops, establishes and manages intra-business procedures for handling, storing, accounting and record retention of SAASM material across enterprise.</li>
<li>
Will assist industrial security manager with implementation of SAASM asset control education and training for all involved facets of business.</li>
<li>
Performs audits, investigations/inquiries of controlled asset violations and prepares reports specifying preventive action to be taken; collects, assimilates, interprets and disseminates to appropriate stakeholders or authority for corrective action.</li>
<li>
Prepares and submits Access Control Plan (ACP) to government cognizant agency (GPSD) establishing and maintaining authorization for L3Harris CS to procure and control SAASMs in direct support of business execution; includes preparing ACPs for all required L3Harris subcontractors and segment managed off-sites.</li>
<li>
Principal manager of business’ Authorization Control Number (ACN) submittal process enabling the acquisition and transfer of SAASM material between L3Harris and customer organizations.</li>
<li>
Reviews Direct Commercial Sales (DCS) Section 30 documentation; engages with Procurement personnel and Program Managers ensuring validity of all SAASM associated nomenclatures within the export licenses are correct prior to submittal.</li>
<li>
Principal for business’ submission of change authorizations for form, fit and function of SAASM enabled devices to government cognizant agency (GPSD); confirms new nomenclatures are compliant with National Security Agency (NSA) approved configurations.</li>
<li>
Prepares SAASM material for disposal by confirming serials and part numbers against records. Request approval from the Key Loading Initialization Facility (KLIF) via generation of Traveler report (SAASM inventory control document)</li>
<li>
Facilitates the Procurement process by obtaining pre-approval of quantities authorized for Engineering tests via GPSD; manages and maintains control of prior authorized SAASM quantities to preclude excess material requests against government allotted quantities.</li>
<li>
Provides guidance and assistance to support level employees.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Qualifications:</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul><li>
High School Diploma or equivalent and a minimum of 8 years of prior relevant experience or 2 years post-Secondary/Associates Degree with a minimum of 4 years of prior related experience.</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Preferred Additional Skills:</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
Current active DoD Secret security clearance or ability to obtain and hold a Department of Defense (DoD) Secret clearance.</li>
<li>
Strong knowledge and experience with Microsoft Office Software packages including Word, Excel Access, PowerPoint and Outlook.</li>
<li>
Strong interpersonal, verbal, organizational and written communication skills.</li>
<li>
Excellent work ethic and the ability to work and get along well with others.</li>
<li>
Must have the highest integrity and demonstrate complete accuracy in a compliance-oriented, high volume, fast-paced environment.</li>
<li>
Experience with Enterprise Resource Planning Systems (ERP) such as EBS (Oracle solutions) and MERCURY.</li>
<li>
Experience in a COMSEC or SAASM-controlled facility.</li>
<li>
Experience in managing, transfer and destruction of SAASM material in manufacturing and engineering operations.</li>
</ul>
