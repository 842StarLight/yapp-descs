<b>
Work you’ll do</b><br>
</div>
<div>
The Manager- Business Systems Analyst SWIFT will work within the SWIFT team to coordinate and document requirements and processes. This role will be primarily focused on Deloitte’s Global SAP S/4 HANA ERP rollout.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Primary Responsibilities:</div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Maintains a solid knowledge base of the functional capabilities of and is a Subject Matter Expert on the various areas and products within SWIFT</div></li>
<li><div>
Assists the Deployment Manager in leading, directing, and reviewing all activities associated with delivering technology products. Responsible for product quality, project timeliness, and customer satisfaction.</div></li>
<li><div>
Interfaces with key stakeholders and process leads to define and document resource requirements, delivery goals, and desired outcomes for identified software solutions and global template projects.</div></li>
<li><div>
Creates documentation including key data metrics and analytics data, resource metrics, financial metrics, executive level PowerPoint decks, instructional material, and newsletters.</div></li>
<li><div>
Coordination and support of project teams related to the full lifecycle of projects</div></li>
<li><div>
Interact with SAP S4 GFS personnel, developers, and end users during design and implementation of business process or system enhancements.</div></li>
<li><div>
Recordation and tracking of member deliverables and provide timely status and updates to immediate managers, project members and member firms.</div></li>
<li><div>
Manage elicitation workshops</div></li>
<li><div>
Provide recommendations and options for team considerations</div></li>
<li><div>
Writing design documents and flow charts</div></li>
<li><div>
Developing functional specifications</div></li>
<li><div>
Documenting and executing test scripts</div></li>
<li><div>
Writing training documents and delivering user training</div></li>
<li><div>
Facilitating Hyper care support tracking</div></li>
<li><div>
Support the development of costing models including adjustments based upon actuals captured.</div></li>
<li><div>
Effectively manage projects using an agile methodology.</div></li>
<li><div>
Possesses overall understanding of appropriate business processes and workflows.</div></li>
<li><div>
Leads and is responsible all data metrics and analytical analysis across the work channels for the Deployment Team.</div></li>
<li><div>
Provides feedback to other team members, leadership and process leads.</div></li>
<li><div>
Works with the Engagement Manager and Program Leadership to plan and execute FY Budgets and Resource models.</div></li>
<li><div>
Participates in reviews of plans associated with program execution.</div></li>
<li><div>
Assists Deployment Manager with status reporting as required by the ITS leadership.</div></li>
<li><div>
Responds proactively to both business and program issues and escalates appropriately.</div></li>
</ul>
