<h2>Implementation and Support Technician</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>M.M. Hayes Co., Inc., seeks highly motivated individuals who are interested in the Quickcharge Implementation and Support Technician position. This is a challenging and fast-paced role which consists of installing, configuring, and supporting the company’s payroll deduction and point of sale (POS) hardware and software solutions. This is a full-time, in-house position. M-F, 8-5.</p>
<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Provide technical and operational support for customers’ payroll deduction and point of sale (POS) hardware and software</li>
<li>Provide excellent customer service via phone, email, and in-person communications</li>
<li>Identify, research, and resolve technical issues in a timely manner</li>
<li>Maintain records of all open issues, including documentation of all communications and actions taken</li>
<li>Develop and maintain customer training and installation schedules</li>
<li>Perform installation and configuration of payroll deduction and point of sale hardware and software</li>
<li>Provide field training for customers regarding uses, functions, and applications of hardware and software</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Strong customer service and communication skills</li>
<li>Strong technical problem-solving skills</li>
<li>Dedication to providing an excellent customer experience</li>
<li>Ability to adjust priorities and manage time effectively in a fast-past environment</li>
<li>Must be independent, reliable, and able to display good judgment</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Requirements</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Information Technology, Computer Science, or a related field or related work experience</li>
<li>Demonstrated experience providing excellent customer service</li>
<li>Experience with point of sale (POS) hardware and software preferred</li>
<li>Experience with Windows operating system, networking, and applications</li>
<li>Basic knowledge of database queries in Microsoft SQL server and Oracle database server preferred</li>
<li>Proficient with Microsoft Office Suite (Word, PowerPoint, Excel, Outlook)</li>
<li>Ability to lift up to 45 lbs.</li>
<li>Must possess a current driver’s license</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Company Description</b></h2>
<p><b>MM Hayes is a software company based in Albany, NY. </b>Since 1971, MM Hayes has provided world-class customer service and support to organizations across the United States for Kronos workforce management, POS, Quickcharge cashless payment and access control solutions.</p>
<p>
We offer our employees competitive pay, health benefits, a 401(k) plan, paid time off, and a place where they can learn and grow within our company. We believe a company is only as good as its people, and that motivated employees will promote a culture of collaboration and cooperation to achieve our common goals. We strive to create a challenging yet fun work environment with a cohesive goal in mind: to lead our industry with innovative workforce solutions that help our customers succeed.</p>
