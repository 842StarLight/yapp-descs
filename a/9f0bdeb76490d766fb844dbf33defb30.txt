<h2>Membership Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>A Career with a Cause:</b></p>
<p>Our mission and core values are brought to life by our culture. In the Y, we strive to live our cause of strengthening communities with purpose and intentionality every day. Our mission is to put Judeo-Christian principles into practice through programs that build healthy spirit, mind, and body for all. The Y strengthens the foundations of communities and families through our key areas of focus; youth development, healthy living, and social responsibility and our core values of caring, honesty, respect, and responsibility. We are committed to this cause because a strong community is achieved when we invest in our children, health, neighbors, and values.</p>
<p>We are welcoming: we are open to all. We are a place where you can belong and grow. We are genuine: we value you and embrace your individuality. We are hopeful: we believe in you and your potential to become a catalyst in the world. We are nurturing: we support you in your journey to develop your full potential. We are determined: above all else, we are on a relentless quest to make our community stronger beginning with you.</p>
<p><b>Position Summary:</b></p>
<p>This position supports the mission and work of the Y, a leading nonprofit, charitable organization. The Membership Coordinator at the Downtown YMCA assists in the management and supervision of the Member Service Desk. Maintains a supportive positive atmosphere that welcomes and respects all individuals, responds to member and guest needs, and promotes memberships and programs. The Membership Coordinator provides direct leadership and assures the well-being for each participant and staff in line with YMCA of the USA guidelines and association policies in accordance with their training.</p>
<p><b>Essential Functions:</b></p>
<p>Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</p>
<p>The essential functions of this position include, but are not limited to the following:</p>
<ul><li>Models and teaches the YMCA core values of caring, honesty, respect, and responsibility with members, colleagues, staff, guests, volunteers, and vendors.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Develops, maintains, and facilitates positive relationships with and between volunteers, members, program participants, staff, and colleagues at all levels of the organization; helps participants connect with each other and the YMCA.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Assists with all supervision of Membership staff, including: recruitment, hiring, training, evaluation, human resources and payroll functions, recognition, and discipline of employees; scheduling and facilitating staff meetings, and trainings; addresses complaints; supports staff in achieving their goals through motivational support and guidance; and resolves problems effectively with the support of their direct supervisor.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Assists with overseeing Membership Services including: risk management in accordance with YMCA guidelines, supports and directs staff in promoting program and membership enrollment, and interactions with members and guests. Coordinates program registration with marketing efforts to maximize enrollments and provides ongoing support to Program Directors on related issues.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Stays up to date and assists with all facility changes including but not limited to events, holidays, schedule changes, and facility closures.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Follows all Membership procedures and guidelines including those pertaining to health and safety, member information, membership management, cash handling, bookkeeping, sales, forms, records, reporting, and staff expectations.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Communicates with staff, participants, and members in a timely and professional manner and answers questions that will support a positive experience with the Y.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ambassador of all YMCA programs with a focus on Membership and member engagement that fosters loyalty among those we serve.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintains physical presence at all times while on duty, monitors and administers safety checks, remains aware of surroundings and participants ensuring a pleasant, professional, and safe Membership environment. Up to 75% of work schedule will be in direct service to the Member Services.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintains Member Service area, equipment, and supplies in an orderly and safe manner. Assists in maintenance and upkeep as required by the YMCA of Central New York, local and county health departments, and The YMCA of the USA.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Knows, follows, and enforces all YMCA policies, rules, regulations, and procedures, including emergency and safety</li></ul>
<p>procedures, and those for the prevention of child abuse; and supports other staff to do the same.</p>
<ul><li>Pursues training opportunities that support the work of the YMCA.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Assists with sound fiscal management and meets deadlines in assigned areas.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Is an active leader in membership retention goals and the Annual Giving Campaign.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Serves as Manager on Duty.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Attends all mandatory meetings and trainings.</li></ul>
<p><b>YMCA Leadership Competencies:</b></p>
<ul><li>Engaging Community: Builds bridges with others in the community to ensure the Y’s work is community-focused and welcoming of all, providing community benefit.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Communication &amp; Influence: Listens and expresses self effectively and in such a way that engages, inspires, and builds commitment to the Y’s cause.</li></ul>
<div>
<p>Emotional Maturity: Demonstrates ability to understand and manage emotions effectively in all situations.</p>
<ul><li>Inclusion: Values all people for their unique talents, and takes an active role in promoting practices that support diversity, inclusion, and global work, as well as cultural competence.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Developing Self &amp; Others: Develops self and supports others (e.g., staff, volunteers, members, program participants), both formally and informally, to achieve their highest potential.</li></ul>
<p><b>Experience, Education, and Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul><li>High School Diploma, equivalent, or relevant work experience</li></ul>
<ul><li>Minimum of three years of customer service experience and staff supervision</li></ul>
<ul><li>Associates Degree in a related field preferred</li></ul>
<ul><li>Proven track record of building program participation and relationships with members, participants, and volunteers</li></ul>
<ul><li>Excellent interpersonal skills are critical and essential to the success of this position</li></ul>
<ul><li>Demonstrates excellent planning, organizational, attention to detail, and time management skills</li></ul>
<ul><li>Proficiency in a variety of software applications including but not limited to Microsoft Office</li></ul>
<ul><li>Possesses and demonstrates excellent customer service, decision-making, problem solving, team oriented, and critical thinking skills</li></ul>
<ul><li>Demonstrates leadership skills in an outgoing, friendly, assertive, professional, and mature manner</li></ul>
<ul><li>Effective communication skills necessary to inform, counsel, motivate, and support members and staff</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must be observant, safety-conscious, and able to react calmly and quickly in an emergency</li></ul>
<ul><li>Able to work flexible schedules</li></ul>
<p><b>Trainings &amp; Certifications:</b></p>
<ul><li>Must complete New Employee Orientation, Child Abuse Prevention, Blood borne Pathogens, Employee Safety, and Hazard Communication trainings prior to start of position.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must obtain YMCA Member Service Trainings within 60 days of hire.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must hold and maintain CPR, AED, and First Aid certifications prior to initial assignment to position.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must complete Emergency Oxygen Administration Certification within the first 90 days of employment.</li></ul>
<p><b>Effect on End Results:</b></p>
<p>The role requires that the Membership Coordinator be committed to delivering high quality results, building positive relationships, maintaining a safe environment, and fostering a healthy community for all.</p>
<ul><li>Growth is seen in the program enrollment and quality in accordance with annual targets.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Members, Participants, and Staff are confident and comfortable with the YMCA.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Participants and staff set and achieve personal goals.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Membership is conducted according to the YMCA of the USA standards.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Membership staff embody the mission and values of the YMCA.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Positive image of the Central Connecticut Coast YMCA is achieved.</li></ul>

This role routinely uses standard sports and recreation equipment such as gym equipment, sports equipment, and office equipment such as phones, photocopiers, filing cabinets, and fax machines. At times, employees may be exposed to undesirable working conditions, communicable infectious diseases, and risk of injury from others. All employees are required to follow the preventative health policies of the YMCA at all times. The noise level in the work environment is moderate to high.</p>
