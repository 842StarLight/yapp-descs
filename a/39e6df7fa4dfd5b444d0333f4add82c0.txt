<h2>RETAIL ASSOCIATE</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b><i>Who We Are</i></b><i>:</i> Goodwill of Southeast Georgia makes a difference in the communities we serve. At Goodwill, we believe work builds strong families, and creates the economic energy that builds strong communities. And at Goodwill, everything - from donations, to the revenue they generate, to the specialized training it provides - everything stays in our community. Goodwill, headquartered in Savannah, Georgia, has grown into a multi-million dollar operation with locations throughout Southeast Georgia.</p>
<p><b><i>What You Will Be Doing:</i></b> Performing all day to day operational functions of the store (e.g. running the register, stocking shelves and counters, hanging garments, selling merchandise and rotating goods.)</p>
<ul><li> Answering customers’ questions concerning location, price and use of merchandise.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Transport items to and from the sales floor.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Answering the telephone and making announcements over the store’s paging system.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Setting up advertising signs and displays or arranges merchandise.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Stocking storage areas and displays with new or transferred merchandise.</li></ul>
<p><b><i>Who We Are Looking For:</i></b></p>
<ul><li> Customer service persons with flexible schedules including evenings, weekends and holidays.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Persons with the ability to relate to a diverse shopping population .</li></ul>
<ul><li> Communicators that are effective in speaking and writing.</li></ul>
<ul><li> The ideal candidate that will embrace our values of Commitment, Learning, Accountability, Integrity, Service to Others, and Teamwork.</li></ul>
<p><b><i>What you’ll receive:</i></b></p>
<ul><li> Competitive compensation</li></ul>
<ul><li> Employee/associate discount</li></ul>
<ul><li> Benefits</li></ul>
<ul><li> PTO (Paid Time Off)</li></ul>
