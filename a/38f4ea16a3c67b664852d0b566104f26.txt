<h2>Store Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Responsible for driving sales and managing total operations in a retail store and a territory.</p>
<p>We are looking for leaders that enjoy creating an engaging and fun experience for our customers and a rewarding opportunity</p>
<p>for their team.</p>
<p>Successful managers are interactive, KPI driven, high energy, and consumer focused.</p>
<p>Essential Functions:</p>
<ul>
<li>Drive sales performance.</li>
<li>Must be able to communicate effectively with team members and corporate support.</li>
<li>Recruit, interview, and hire service oriented associates.</li>
<li>Skilled at coaching and training your teams, strategic planning, customer service, conflict resolution, business acumen, and time-management.</li>
<li>Must have experience and be proficient in general retail software</li>
<li>Manage multiple seasonal locations during 4th quarter. This includes build assists, weekly sales management calls, and expense control.</li>
<li>Assist employee managers with closing their kiosks and inlines stores on time.</li>
<li>Solution driven problem solver.</li>
<li>Manage staffing plan and control payroll for assigned stores.</li>
<li>Accountable for cash handling and deposits. Partner with Loss Prevention to protect assets and reduce cash loss.</li>
<li>Manage inventory by executing receiving, markdown, and POS processes</li>
<li>Must be available to work a flexible schedule to meet the needs of the business, which may include closing, weekend shifts and holidays</li>
<li>Travel and overnight stay up to 50% of the time during the fourth quarter may be required.</li>
</ul>
<p>Preferred Experience</p>
<ul>
<li>Retail Management experience</li>
<li>Proven ability to drives sales.</li>
<li>Customer service experience</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul>
<li>retail management: 2 years (Preferred)</li>
<li>relevant: 2 years (Preferred)</li>
<li>management: 2 years (Preferred)</li>
<li>retail: 2 years (Preferred)</li>
</ul>
</div>
