<h2>Enterprise Territory Manager - State and Local Government - North Central</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Your Role:</b><br>
<br>
We are currently searching for an Enterprise Territory Manager – State and Local Government. The Territory Manager shall develop business through existing and new state &amp; local government and higher education clients in their region.</p>
<p><b>
Your Opportunity:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Maintain and develop the existing client base through regional marketing and direct sales activities</li>
<li>
Identify, qualify and develop new opportunities through prospecting and networking</li>
<li>
Manage and maintain your region's sales pipeline and target key accounts and vertical markets for development</li>
<li>
Achieve assigned monthly, quarterly and annual sales budgets and other regional objectives established by the Area Sales Director</li>
<li>
Provide monthly and quarterly sales forecasts</li>
<li>
Manage the day to day activities of sales personnel dedicated to your region. This includes inside sales representatives and sales engineers</li>
<li>
Consistently achieve and/or exceed your assigned quotas and revenue objectives</li>
<li>
Utilize channel sales resources in your region as necessary</li>
<li>
Stay abreast of competitive industry and market intelligence and provide feedback in this area to executive management</li>
<li>
Performs other related duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
What You'll Need:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor's Degree and 5+ years of related experience</li>
<li>
Ability to take on a consultative style when presenting ideas and products</li>
<li>
Competency with sales tracking tools</li>
<li>
Outstanding oral and written communication skills</li>
<li>
Must be detail oriented and capable of accurate work with minimal supervision</li>
<li>
Readiness to travel extensively</li>
</ul>
