3 HTi, LLC is a privately held systems integrator that is the Global Leader for Digital Transformation of the Manufacturing Enterprise. We provide technology platforms and enterprise applications for smart and connected products, operations, and systems. Our enterprise software solutions serve manufacturers and other businesses that create, operate and service products. We provide PTC’s award-winning ThingWorx technology platforms that help companies achieve a “new reality” in smart, connected things and systems - deriving new value emerging from the “Internet of Things” market. This market is exploding and is deemed the “4th Industrial Revolution” We are looking for Sales Professionals in Los Angeles, San Francisco, Philadelphia, Boston, New York City, Atlanta, Charlotte and Orlando We are headquartered in Mount Laurel, NJ USA<br>

<b>Job Description:</b>
<ul>
<li>Prospects and generates interest through cold calling, email campaigns, webinars and other activities.</li>
<li>Established a value proposition to obtain orders, and establish new accounts by planning and organizing daily work schedule to call on existing or potential clients</li>
<li>Establishes overall account plans and maintains a sales pipeline to achieve targets and quotas.</li>
<li>Establishes and maintains forecast accuracy for sales pipeline</li>
<li>Adjusts content of sales presentations by studying the type of prospect.</li>
<li>Under broad direction, applies strategic value selling methodology, tools, and processes to improve sales effectiveness on complex projects<br>
and pursuits</li>
<li>Quotes and submits orders by referring to price lists and product literature.</li>
<li>Keeps management informed by submitting activity and results reports, such as daily call reports, weekly work plans, and monthly and annual territory analyses.</li>
</ul>
