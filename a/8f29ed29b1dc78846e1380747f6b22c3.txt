 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">

<p><b>Ideal Candidate Location: </b>Hoboken, Elizabeth, Newark NJ</p>
<p><b>
SUMMARY</b></p>
<p>
The category management analyst will work directly with the Account Manager and with the customer’s Deli Category Manager as an assistant providing support in preparing for sales meetings, building category reviews, assessing advertising results, and reviewing item selections. The analyst is responsible for delivering impactful summaries that highlight opportunities for growth for the client, customer, and category.</p>
<p><b>
For immediate consideration, send an email with resume/summary of experience to Bret Abramowitz at babramowitz@dietzandwatson.com</b></p>
<p><b>
JOB DESCRIPTION</b></p>
<p>
The category management analyst will develop Category Management plans for key customers with knowledge of A.C. Nielsen, IRI syndicated data and Spectra. He/she will ensure data integrity and develop standardized templates for ongoing market/consumer updates. He/she will team with the Account Executives and Headquarter Sales Team in the annual sales/marketing planning process and also serve as a liaison to the account’s management team. The candidate will assist in monitoring pricing, tracking competitive activity, opportunity gaps vs. plan and highlight possible causal factors. He/she will also be key in creating plan-o- grams for key customers as well as being responsible for the development and updating of planograms.</p>
