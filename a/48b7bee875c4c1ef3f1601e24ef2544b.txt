JOB SUMMARY
<br>
<br>
The Brinks Company is looking for a Web Implementation Manager to oversee the revamp and implementation of Website redesign and activity tracking by working with the regional leads to drive localization that aligns with global standards.
<br>
<br>
ROLE RESPONSIBILITIES
<br>
<br>
The Web Implementation Manager will be responsible for Implementation, management and governance of the Brink’s websites globally. This individual will work closely with the regional teams and identify how to best target localization needs with consideration to global consistency. The ideal candidate will collaborate across functions to deliver an effective strategies to maximize the effectiveness of the web presence to meet the business objectives in the most efficient and cost-effective manner. This position works closely with regional teams to understand the systems and data points applicable to each site and advise on areas of opportunity and collaboration to ensure global consistency is maintained while supporting localized needs.
