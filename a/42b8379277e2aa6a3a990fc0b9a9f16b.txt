<p><b>Marketing Coordinator/Administrative Assistant - We offer paid time off, medical benefits and opportunity for growth.</b></p>
<p>We are a computer services company that offers its employees paid time off, medical benefits and opportunity for growth.</p>
<p><b>The Company</b></p>
<p>Intelligent IT Designs is a small growing local high-tech service company that is committed to the success of our employees and our clients and is known for the great care we take with clients and employees alike.</p>
<ul>
<li>We believe in a strong team spirit and we develop this by building a strong working team culture and social culture.</li>
<li>We believe in taking care of our employees and giving back to the community.</li>

<p><b>The Position</b></p>
<p>We’re looking for a Marketing Coordinator. The pay range we’re offering is 40K to 50K plus bonus.</p>
<ul>
<li>We offer paid time off, medical benefits and opportunity for growth.</li>
<li>We also offer the opportunity to learn more about business, technology and sales.</li>
</ul>
<p>We understand the importance of good marketing. To build on this belief, we’re searching for a highly qualified marketing coordinator/administrative assistant to work closely with. From day one, this individual will contribute to marketing programs, while assisting with the development and execution of new initiatives aligned with company and client goals. The ideal candidate will have prior experience with a wide range of marketing functions, including communications, advertising, branding, digital marketing, print marketing, direct response marketing and social media. The marketing coordinator must be an organized multitasker able to handle many diverse projects at once and meet tight deadlines.</p>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communications skills</li>
<li>Working knowledge of CRM database software</li>
<li>Strong Interpersonal Skills (e.g. communication skills, active listening, and customer-care)</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task and adapt to changes quickly</li>
<li>Keyboarding skills to ensure quick and accurate entry of data</li>
<li>Organized, detail oriented, and self-motivated</li>
<li>Ability to adapt to shifting priorities, demands, and timelines for projects</li>
<li>Experience in Marketing or Sales</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Marketing Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Execute various marketing efforts such as direct response marketing, digital marketing, online marketing, print marketing, email, phone calls, etc.</li>
<li>Manage CRM database and maintain prospects data, validate and correct data</li>
<li>Research accounts, identify key players, add data to CRM, validate data</li>
<li>Work with freelance designers to generate new marketing materials</li>
<li>Schedule appointments and make follow up calls to support marketing campaigns</li>
<li>Understand customer needs and requirements</li>
<li>Route qualified opportunities to the appropriate sales team member for further development and closure</li>
<li>Team with channel partners and vendors to cultivate relationships and increase sales pipeline</li>
<li>Attend company sponsored industry, educational, or community events</li>
<li>Regular meeting and reporting of activity</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Administrative Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Invoicing</li>
<li>Collections</li>
<li>Organizing and filing</li>
<li>Ordering/purchasing materials</li>
<li>Answering phone</li>
<li>Other errands and responsibilities</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Skills and Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Degree in marketing, advertising, or communications</li>
<li>Past work experience as a marketing coordinator or similar role</li>
<li>Knowledge of traditional and digital marketing, content marketing, direct response marketing and social media marketing</li>
<li>Experience with research</li>
<li>Excellent writing, communication, and presentation skills</li>
<li>Proficiency in full Microsoft Office suite, especially with Excel and PowerPoint</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Preferred Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>

<li>Working knowledge of HTML and design and email software</li>
<li>Experience working with budgets and forecasting</li>
</ul>
