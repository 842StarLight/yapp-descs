<h2>Content Marketing Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>As the Content Marketing Specialist, you will be responsible for the creation and execution of Concord’s content strategy in support of the organization’s brand awareness, thought-leadership and lead generation goals.</p>
<p>
In this role you will develop messaging and content to elevate Concord’s profile in the marketplace, increase demand for its products, and equip the sales organization with the tools to close more business.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>
Collaborate with sales, marketing, and product strategy leadership to create and deliver effective messaging tailored for specific buyer segments.</p>
<p>
Assume a key role in defining and executing marketing’s content development plan.</p>
<p>
Develop a variety of digital and print content:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Lead assets (infographics, buying guides, quote-tools, ebooks etc.)</li>
<li>
Social proof (customer quotes, video testimonials, written case studies etc.)</li>
<li>
Web site and landing page content</li>
<li>
e-mail newsletter and campaign copy</li>
<li>
Blog articles and social media posts</li>
<li>
Print collateral (one-sheets, product overviews, flyers etc.)</li>
<li>
Company and product presentations</li>
<li>
Internal communications</li>
<li>
Publish content and track performance across a variety of channels</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Expertise</b></p>
<p>
Content strategy – you have a deep understanding of what it takes to make content work whether that means creating awareness, converting leads or closing business. You define your approach based on target buyer segment, audience profile, funnel position and channel. You track the performance of your content to answer the question: what’s working?</p>
<p>
Technical aptitude– you are highly adept at translating product capabilities and other technical concepts into non-technical, business-oriented content. You go out of your way to learn the technology you write about. You make a point of building relationships with subject matter experts. If you aren’t sure how something works – you jump into the software to see for yourself.</p>
<p>
Exceptional writing skills– the content you create is letter-perfect, packs a punch and delivers the message in way that’s simple for intended audience to understand. You are also a keen copy editor.</p>
<p>
Strong verbal communication and relationship building– you connect quickly with people both inside and outside of the business. Customers enjoy talking to you about how they use products, and business partners enjoy collaborating to projects.</p>
<p>
Agency management – you frequently work with external firms to deliver on projects the marketing team needs to outsource.You know what it takes to find the right firm, nail-down a clear brief, track progress and ensure projects are delivered on-time. You enjoy building lasting relationships with external agencies and have a black-book of resources you can call-on.</p>
<p><b>
Experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
5+ years’ experience in a content creation / copy writing role</li>
<li>
3+ years’ working for a B2B software or technology company</li>
<li>
Experience developing content for the healthcare industry highly desirable</li>
</ul>
<p>
Tagged as: North America</p>
