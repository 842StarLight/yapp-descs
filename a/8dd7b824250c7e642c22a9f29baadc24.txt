<h2>Senior Product Manager, Data Catalog and Pipeline</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>Wayfair's Product team attracts entrepreneurial, value-oriented decision makers who drive the development of the world-class software products that power Wayfair's transformation of retail for home. We create the web and app experiences that make Wayfair a delightful place to shop, establish new and innovative services for customers, and build the backbone of Wayfair's pricing, merchandising, supply chain, and enterprise operations. Whether on desktop, mobile web, or app, or any of our proprietary internal platforms, we leverage data, user-centric design, machine learning, and Agile to exceed customer expectations and achieve operational excellence, fueling company growth. <br>
Wayfair's S&amp;OP function sits at the center of the company's supply chain. We work closely with the demand-planning teams to project Sales volumes for each of the 14 million SKUs in Wayfair's catalog. We then turn that projection into a supply chain plan across more than 100 facilities, projecting the impact of that volume on all network assets: warehouse yard space, door utilization, warehouse rack space, ocean freight, rail and trucking, labor, and more. Our work helps to identify potential bottlenecks and risks and delivers a smooth flow of goods throughout our entire supply chain.<br>
<br>
This Sr. Product Manager role will focus on S&amp;OP for Wayfair's Data Pipeline and Catalog team. Wayfair's Data Pipeline and Catalog team was designed with the Supply chain stakeholders in mind: creating a product that leverages our scale, expertise, and advanced tech capabilities to have access to data in a standardized and secure fashion at the required grain. Supply chain team uses this data to generate advanced predictive insights that drive our Demand, Throughput, Capacity and Labor forecasts taking into account the ever changing realities on the ground reflected by the latest data. This role is global in scope, spanning operations in Asia, North American, and the EU.<br>
