<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>About the job</b></h2>
<p>
Google's projects, like our users, span the globe and require managers to keep the big picture in focus. As a Program Manager at Google, you lead complex, multi-disciplinary projects. You plan requirements with internal customers and usher projects through the entire project lifecycle. This includes managing project schedules, identifying risks and clearly communicating goals to project stakeholders. Your projects often span offices, time zones and hemispheres, and it's your job to keep all the players coordinated on the project's progress and deadlines.</p>
<p>
As the Pricing Program Manager, you will support the Pricing leads and Pricing Analysts with the ongoing evolution of price changes across our portfolio of YouTube products. You will take lead on running the overall reserve pricing cycle ensuring that timelines are created and commercialized with all stakeholders, setting agendas and running cross-functional working group meetings, owning the creation of pricing materials that culls together the appropriate level of information from the various products the Pricing Leads work on, into a cohesive and succinct set of materials for an array of different audiences. You will also look after competitive and marketplace briefings for both the Pricing leads and the cross-functional counterparts on an ongoing basis, as well as basic analytical work. You have organizational and project management skills as well as analytical capabilities.
</p>
<p>Global Solutions is the link between Google products and business. Your focus is to help turn innovation into complete, packaged tools that allow our customers to get the most that they need from our products. As part of the Global Solutions team, you'll help to ensure that Google has adopted the right strategy for our products. Working in one of three specialized areas – Performance, Brand, or Publisher &amp; Platform – you'll use your expertise to help front-line Sales partners to work their magic quickly and effectively. You are passionate about all things digital, and want to help shape the world of online advertising.</p>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Responsibilities</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Serve as lead for organizing the cross-functional team for the start and execution of pricing cycle, including ownership over setting timelines and all material creation.
</li>
<li>Review pricing within the product portfolio to ensure cohesive price ladder across products; Identify key outliers for further review and potential price tuning.
</li>
<li>Collect marketplace intel, and ensure team is up to date on latest competitive information and landscape/marketplace knowledge; Create competitive overview ahead of pricing cycle to be shared with each Pricing lead and cross-functional global pricing teams.</li>
<li>Support pricing and broader teams with analytical work.
</li>
<li>Apply pricing methodology to international markets, operationalize rate cards, and develop executive material for sign off.
</li>
</ul>
