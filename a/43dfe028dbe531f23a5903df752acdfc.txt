<p><b>Associate Consultant </b><b>(Enterprise Software) Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop in-depth expertise in the company's solutions and demonstrate how they can be applied to solve business needs</li>
<li>Implement the company’s solutions with customer project teams and in Pre-sales Proof-of-Concept scenarios.</li>
<li>Deliver on-site training for clients.</li>
<li>Contribute in submitting written Tender responses e.g. RFPs, RFIs, Questionnaires etc.
</li>
<li>Serve as liaison between clients and other internal technical experts and convey customer requirements to Product Management teams.</li>
<li>Provide technical support through to your clients</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<p><b>
ABOUT YOU:</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in MIS, Computer Science, Engineering or technical field, or equivalent combination of education and on-the-job training.</li>
<li>Able to travel overseas as required by the role.</li>
<li>Self-motivated, comfortable and responsible in a position that grants autonomy.</li>
<li>Proven or demonstrably outstanding communication, presentation and writing skills.</li>
<li>Proficient in platforms and technologies which are complementary to the solution a plus, particularly: MS Excel, MS Word, MS Access, MS Visio, MS Visual Studio/C#/VB.NET, Subversion and XML.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Ideally you will also have:</b></p>
<ul>

</ul>
