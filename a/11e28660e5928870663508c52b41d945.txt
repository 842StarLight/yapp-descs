<h2>Senior Sales Engineer, MSO</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">POSITION OVERVIEW
<br><br>
Oversees integrations with key MSO clients to ensure that they are operating efficiently. The Sales Engineer will travel to a client for the duration of the integration and will oversee the deployment remotely. The goal of this position is to ensure that clients are closely tied to the Alarm.com backend so they can create workflows based on information from Alarm.com (regarding best practices, trouble conditions, etc). Lead/manage/accountability (to MSO partners and internally at Alarm.com)
<br><br>
RESPONSIBILITIES
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Provides technical support for the development and implementation of complex products/applications/solutions.</li>
<li>Uses in-depth product knowledge to provide technical expertise to sales staff and the customer through sales presentations and product demonstrations.</li>
<li>Assists the sales staff in assessing potential application of company products to meet customer needs and may prepare detailed product specifications for the development and implementation of customer products/applications/solutions.</li>
<li>Creates detailed design and implementation specifications for complex products/applications/solutions.</li>
<li>Builds a mini-application based on customer requirements to demonstrate feasibility of the application, often requiring rapid prototyping and/or product demo for client.</li>
<li>Provides consultation to prospective users and/or product capability assessment and validation.</li>
</ul>
