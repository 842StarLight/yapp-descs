<h2>Representative, Cust Svc I</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>Responsible for ensuring all calls are answered and entered into the company system in a timely manner. Responsible for providing the best possible customer service to customers. Must be able to handle level 1 call types.
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><ul>
<li>Manages large amounts of incoming requests.</li>
<li>Identifies and assesses customers' needs to achieve satisfaction.</li>
<li>Builds sustainable relationships and trust with customer accounts through open and interactive communication.</li>
<li>Provides accurate, valid and complete information by using the right methods/tools.</li>
<li>Meets personal/customer service team production targets.</li>
<li>Documents customer complaints, provides appropriate solutions and alternatives within the time limits; follows up to ensure resolution.</li>
<li>Keeps records of customer interactions, processes customer accounts and files documents.</li>
<li>Follows communication procedures, guidelines and policies.</li>
<li>Takes the extra mile to engage customers.
</li>
</ul></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>Typically requires general education and/or vocational training but little to no experience.<br>
<ul>
<li>Strong phone contact handling skills and active listening.</li>
<li>Customer orientation and ability to adapt/respond to different types of characters.</li>
<li>Excellent communication and presentation skills.</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task, prioritize, and manage tasks effectively.
</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
