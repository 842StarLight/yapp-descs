<p><b>
Your role in a nutshell</b></p>
<p>
Check Point has a long standing history of having an exceptionally strong partner base. If you are keen to establish in-depth relationships with our key partners, our Channel Account Manager position could be what you are looking for! From ensuring that our partners possess the highest level of technical and commercial knowledge, to supporting them in gaining respectable margins whilst maintaining the integrity of the Channel, you will always be in for a challenge!</p>
<p>
Reporting to the local Area Manager</p>
<p><b>
You will be responsible for</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Create and deliver partnership development plans with key managed partners</li>
<li>Develop and implement quarterly and yearly business plans with the partners</li>
<li>Meeting/exceeding a unique sales quota for new customers, to drive with Partners</li>
<li>Maintain strong relationship with partners at all levels and recruit new resellers</li>
<li>Increase brand awareness and demand through targeted local sales &amp; marketing campaigns along with the partner community</li>
<li>Manage and facilitate the training and enablement of managed partners</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<div></div>Desired Background
<p><b>What you bring to the table</b></p>
<ul>
<li>3-8 years of proven track record in relationship based channel account management</li>
<li>Possess good business acumen in channels sales management</li>
<li>Experience in the IT industry</li>
<li>Knowledge of the Cyber Security industry – highly preferred</li>
<li>Excellent communication and presentation skills</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What you'll get in return</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Exposure to exciting and award winning technologies in the Cyber Security industry</li>

<li>Work with and build long-lasting relationship with a team of brilliant people</li>
</ul>
