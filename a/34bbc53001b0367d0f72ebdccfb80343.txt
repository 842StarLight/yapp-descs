<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Retail Wireless Consultant</b></h3>
<p>We are looking for a part-time <b>Retail Wireless Consultant</b> to join our team. As a Retail Wireless Consultant, your work will be highly valued as you are the face of our organization! Your work is critical to driving results that will grow our business which means that <i>your success is our success.</i></p>
<p>
If you are looking for an opportunity to get your foot in the door at a Top Workplace or are simply looking for flexible part-time hours while earning a competitive wage- our <b>Retail Wireless Consultant </b>may be the right position for you!</p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Responsibilities</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>Positively impact new and existing customers lives by helping them find the perfect solution for their wireless needs.</li>
<li>Meet and/or exceed individual and team sales goals.</li>
<li>Coordinate, schedule, and participate in community events.</li>
<li>Creating the ideal customer experience with each customer interaction.</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>The Ideal Candidate</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>High School diploma/GED</li>
<li>Excellent communication and time management skills</li>
<li>Flexible to work nights, weekends and holidays</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Things You Can Expect</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Flexible Hours: </b>We have the ability to offer our part-time associates flexible hours. As a part-time associate, you can expect to work 20-30 hours per week.</li>
<li>
<b>Competitive Pay: </b>On top of a base wage, your hard work will pay off with a competitive commission plan. On average, Retail Wireless Consultants earn <b>$16.00-$22.00 all in per hour</b>, depending on location. Associates who exceed their targets have the opportunity to earn even more.</li>
<li>
<b>Work That Matters:</b> We want you to feel empowered to offer your customers solutions that will positively impact the way they live their lives.</li>
<li>
<b>Development Opportunities: </b>If advancement is what you desire you will be happy to hear that at NGW you will have an opportunity to learn and grow both personally and professionally. Your Leaders will work hard to help you be the best you can be! While you are learning and ramping up in your 1st year with us you will have the ability to earn an additional <b>$1,000.00 in bonuses!</b>
</li>
<li>
<b>Community Involvement:</b> We believe in giving back to the communities we work and live in through volunteer and charitable efforts. All NGW associates receive paid volunteer time off.</li>
<li>
<b>Lasting Relationships:</b> Our associates find an immense amount of pride that comes from the relationships they build on a daily basis with both their customers and team members.</li>
<li>
<b>Fun Work Environment:</b> Sure, we work hard, but we have a lot of fun doing it.</li>
</ul>
