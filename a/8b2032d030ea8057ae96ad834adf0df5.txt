<h2>Enterprise Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">The Account Executive at Prometric will be a strategic advisor to one of Prometric’s largest clients. Possessing a solid understanding of our clients business and industry, and the challenges the client faces is critical. Your ability to develop and close opportunities that deliver global solutions to enable the customer’s desired outcomes is key. As an Account Executive, you will be responsible for managing a consultative solution sale with a 6 to 18 month purchasing cycle as well as moving the sale through the entire sales process, taking an active role in the proposal process, and at the conclusion of the sale owning the subsequent relationship with the help of a Customer Success Manager. This involves making prospecting a part of the regular routine and listening to a prospect’s needs and goals and matching an appropriate Prometric offerings as well as targeting and closing “green-field” opportunities.
<br><br>
Prometric is a trusted test development and delivery provider to more than 300 organizations worldwide. On their behalf, we securely deliver an average of 7 million exams per year to people who are seeking to improve their lives. Whether by starting a new career, further developing their skills to improve on an existing one, adding to their qualifications for a promotion, taking school entrance exams or simply for professional development, people taking tests want a reliable, convenient and hassle-free experience. Prometric serves as an industry gatekeeper, ensuring that people legitimately earn the credentials they seek to achieve, and thereby guaranteeing a fair testing experience for all who come through our doors.
