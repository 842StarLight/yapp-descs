<h2>Accounting Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div><b>Position Overview</b></div>
<div>
The Accounting Associate is responsible for recording various journal entries for general ledger.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Who would I interact with?</b></div>
<div>
This position interacts daily with support staff, Sales Associates, Sales Managers, Financial Services Managers, and customers, just to name a few.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b><br>
What are the day-to-day responsibilities?</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Preparing various accounting statements and reports - Reviewing and verifying the accuracy of journal entries and accounting classifications assigned to various records</li>
<li>
Coordinating accounting matters with other departments, locations and divisions</li>
<li>
Utilizing computer to input, retrieve or display accounting information</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>
What are the requirements for this job?</b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>
High school diploma or equivalent</div></li>
<li><div>
Two years experience in accounting</div></li>
<li><div>
Strong written and verbal communication skills</div></li>
<li><div>
Experience with Microsoft Office software</div></li>
<li><div>
Strong acclimation to mathematics and analysis</div></li>
<li><div>
Proficient in Microsoft
</div></li>
</ul>
