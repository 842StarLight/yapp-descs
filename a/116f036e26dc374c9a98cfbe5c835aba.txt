<h2>Senior Automation Design Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Universal Robotics, a state of the art A.I. robotics company, is seeking a senior automation design engineer to become part of a team implementing automation solutions that support and enable Universal’s software products. Must have passion for the effort and a strong work ethic.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities: </b></p>
<p>· Develop reliable, efficient mechanical designs for precision custom automated equipment in a variety of industries from retail to manufacturing</p>
<p>· Organize, document, and present technical design reviews and project management overviews with minimal assistance</p>
<p>· Work closely with Application Engineers to develop concepts and support sales engineering approaches as needed</p>
<p>· Work closely with Electrical Engineers to apply appropriate controls technology into the mechanical automation design</p>
<p>· Work with vendors and customers to correctly deploy process or test equipment into the mechanical automation design</p>
<p><b>Minimum Experience/Education</b>:</p>
<p>· 5+ years of professional experience designing and developing industrial automation products and systems</p>
<p>· BS Engineering with demonstrated experience</p>
<p>· Strong knowledge and experience with engineering principles and procedures that apply to machine design, part handling, robotic end-of-arm-tool design, semi-automated work cell layout, robotic work cell layout, process flow, operator ergonomics, and machine aesthetics</p>
<p>· Proficient in the use of OnShape, AutoCad, or Soldworks to generate 3D solid model designs from concept to release to fabrication is a requirement.</p>
<p>· Familiarity with industry policies and procedures to ensure safety, environmental, and regulatory compliance</p>
<p>· Experience in machining, cutting, and grinding of metals and polymers</p>
<p>· Candidate should be proficient in ANSI/ASME and GD&amp;T standards</p>
<p>· Basic understanding of machine controls and systems integration</p>
<p>· Basic understanding of motion control technologies and mechanical drive train design including the application of stepper motors, servo motors, pneumatics, and hydraulics</p>
<p><b>Relevant experience in</b>:</p>
<p>o Design for manufacturing</p>
<p>o Design for automation</p>
<p>o Machine tools</p>
<p>o Precision tooling</p>
<p>o Special purpose machinery</p>
<p>o Industrial robots</p>
<p>o Industrial machine control systems</p>
