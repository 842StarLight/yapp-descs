<h2>Property Manager - Large Class A Community</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><i><b>Trio Properties, LLC is seeking dynamic leaders with experience in the property management industry to oversee and effectively manage the operation of a Class A Trio managed, apartment community in Manchester, CT.</b></i></p>
<p><b><i>The Property Manager is directly responsible for creating extraordinary experiences of home and community for residents. From developing innovative, cost-effective, and revenue-driving tactics to building solid owner and resident relationships, they are in-charge of establishing brand recognition for the properties within the marketplace and for exceeding resident satisfaction and retention goals.</i></b></p>
<p>As a Property Manager, you are in an excellent position to showcase your business know-how. By using your proactive and persuasive leadership to aid in the mentoring of fellow staff and the execution of strategic partnerships, and your interpersonal savvy to resolve customer issues and create positive experiences, you will earn the opportunity to progress in your career.</p>
<p>Trio Properties was awarded <i>2014, 2015, 2016, 2017 &amp; 2018 Property Management Company of the Year</i>from the Connecticut Apartment Association. Come join our growing team!</p>
<p><b>Job Responsibilities Include: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Create and manage the property budget, marketing strategy, and business plan.</li>
<li>Hire and retain the best industry talent; actively motivate, train, and develop employees.</li>
<li>Oversee the overall financial and operational performance of the property.</li>
<li>Establish effective promotion initiatives that position the property as a sub-market leader.</li>
<li>Direct aggressive sales efforts to exceed occupancy and resident retention targets.</li>
<li>Develop and maintain relationships with all constituents; owners, residents, vendors, etc.</li>
<li>Supervise the filing of legal proceedings in accordance with State of CT codes.</li>
<li>Guide team members to ensure their comprehension of the current sub-market.</li>
<li>Lead by example to sustain a high-performing, customer-focused team.</li>
</ul>
