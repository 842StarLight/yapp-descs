<p><b>TITLE: SOLIDWORKS Electrical/Software Support Engineer</b></p>
<p><b>Why Join Us?</b> CADimensions, Inc. is a growing Value-Added Reseller of Mechanical Engineering Software. Our primary offering includes the 3D CAD product line from SOLIDWORKS. We have sold to well over 5000 companies and trained several thousand users in 3D Computer Aided Design.</p>
<p>Our clients range from one-person firms to large companies with thousands of employees. The mission of our team is to put the proper engineering tools in the hands of our customers so they can realize a shorter time to market, and a reduction in design, manufacturing, and overall operational costs.</p>
<p>We are searching for a <b>SOLIDWORKS Electrical/Software Support Engineer</b>. The successful candidate will troubleshoot and support computer software including but not limited to SOLIDWORKS, SQL, Windows, as well as an ability for electrical schematic and design.</p>
<ul>
<li>Install and configure SOLIDWORKS CAD, SOLIDWORKS Electrical and SQL Products for our customers</li>
<li>Resolve incoming requests from clients with timely solutions to questions/issues</li>
<li>Provide technical assistance to team members on customer challenges</li>
<li>Training customers in SOLIDWORKS Electrical Schematic and SOLIDWORKS Electrical 3D Software</li>
</ul>
<p><b>MINIMUM REQUIREMENTS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Associate or Bachelor degree in Electrical Engineering, Electrical Engineering Technology or related field</li>
<li>At least 2 years of directly related experience of electrical drafting/schematic design (some experience may be considered in lieu of advanced education).
</li>
<li>Prior experience generating complete drawing sets to document System Schematics</li>
<li>Ability to read and understand engineering drawings, electrical schematics, and wiring diagrams. Ability to create projects in SOLIDWORKS Electrical</li>
<li>Knowledge of the following hardware/software technologies: AutoCAD Electrical, SOLIDWORKS Electrical, ProE, or CREO,
</li>
<li>Experience with implementation/support/use of CAD systems</li>
<li>Familiar with engineering design, production and general business processes</li>
<li>Knowledgeable in computers, servers and understanding of networks</li>
</ul>
