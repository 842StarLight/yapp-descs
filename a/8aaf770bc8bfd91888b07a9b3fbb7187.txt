<h2>Resident Services Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Description</b></p>
<div>
<div>Based in Normal, Illinois, the Fairlawn team member will be responsible the resident services of Pinecrest Apartments, a 313-unit residential complex. The Fairlawn team member will work directly with the Property Manager and receive support as needed from a Regional Operations Manager and Fairlawn’s corporate office in Champaign, IL.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Responsibilities include:</div>
<ul>
<li>
Handle resident accounting tasks such as rent collection, posting rent and preparing deposits</li>
<li>
Prepare leases, resident files, move-in paperwork, confirm residences are move-in ready and perform move-in orientations with new residents</li>
<li>
Plan and host resident events</li>
<li>
Maintain excellent rapport with current and prospective residents</li>
<li>
Process maintenance requests and follow up with residents to confirm completion</li>
<li>
Assist in setting market rents, conducting market research, defining leasing goals and running renewal process</li>
<li>
Handle daily operations including: answering calls, scheduling appointments, greeting visitors, showing residences to prospective residents and any necessary paperwork, filing, etc.</li>
<li>
Become knowledgeable on our properties versus competition and effectively communicate features to prospective residents</li>
<li>
Apply established sales and marketing strategies to increase property traffic, maintain closing ratios and meet or exceed budgeted occupancy</li>
<li>
Run and approve credit checks and lease applications</li>
<li>
Ensure resident files are properly maintained</li>
<li>
Have a thorough knowledge of lease terms, specifications and policies</li>
<li>
Support other departments as needed (e.g. maintenance, property operations)</li>
<li>
Learn, audit and ensure team’s compliance with all Fair Housing Laws including local, state and federal housing regulations</li>
</ul>
</div>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<div>
<div>Skills and Knowledge Requirements:</div>
<ul>
<li>
At least three years property management experience</li>
<li>
Previous direct customer service experience required</li>
<li>
Comfort using a variety of computer applications required (e.g. Microsoft Office Suite)</li>
<li>
Experience with property management software required (e.g. Buildium, Yardi, AppFolio)</li>
<li>
Excellent written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>
Superior attention to detail, high energy and positive attitude</li>
<li>
Ability to work independently and also to work closely with other team members</li>
<li>
Ethical and professional conduct and integrity beyond reproach</li>
</ul>
<div>
Education Requirements:</div>
<ul>
<li>
Highschool Diploma or GED</li>
<li>
Undergraduate Degree preferred</li>
</ul>
<div>
Additional Requirements:</div>
<ul>
<li>
Valid driver’s license and dependable vehicle. Limited in-town usage of personal vehicle for errands and property visits required, any out-of-County personal mileage reimbursed at the Federal rate</li>
<li>
Availability to work evenings and weekends (mainly for special resident events)</li>
<li>
Must be able to perform the physical functions of the position, not limited to: ability to walk properties, complete physical inspections, deliver resident communications and show properties</li>
</ul>
