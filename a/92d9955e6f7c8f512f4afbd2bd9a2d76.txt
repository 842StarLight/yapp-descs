<h2>FPGA Engineer II</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>KARL STORZ SE &amp; Co. KG based in Tuttlingen, Germany, is a family-owned, global company committed to benefiting humanity by advancing medical technology through innovation and education.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>For more than 70 years, KARL STORZ has been dedicated to earning its international reputation as a leader that designs, engineers, manufactures, and markets all its products with an emphasis on visionary design, precision craftsmanship, and clinical effectiveness.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Located just north of Santa Barbara on the scenic Central Coast of California, KSI designs and manufactures high tech medical imaging equipment including video cameras and image enhancing devices. We employ technical staff with expertise in electronics, optics, mechanical systems and software. Our lean manufacturing operations include an in-house machine shop which enables the complete on-site assembly of our products. To become a part of this dynamic team, we invite you to learn more about our opportunities.</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Summary:</b></h2>
</div>
<p>Design, develop, test, integrate, and verify FPGA designs for medical video camera systems.</p>
<div><p><b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS</b><b><i>:</i></b> <i>List the essential duties and responsibilities of the position, in order of importance and/or amount of time spent.
</i></p></div>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Create synthesizable Verilog HDL code for FPGAs</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Create Verilog or System Verilog code to simulate and verify FPGA designs</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Troubleshooting FPGA designs</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Analyze resource utilization, power, and timing.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<div>
<p><b>ADDITIONAL RESPONSIBILITES: </b><i>List other important functions that are secondary duties but may be time consuming.</i></p>
</div>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Implement small software designs for embedded processors</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Performs other related duties as assigned by supervisor</p>
</li>
</ul>
<div>
<p><b>KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS, ABILITIES: </b><i>What does a person in this position need to be able to do?</i></p>
</div>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Intermediate expertise using (System) Verilog or VHDL for implementation and verification of FPGA designs.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Basic expertise writing and verifying Embedded C for microcontrollers closely interfacing with FPGA.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Basic knowledge of video timing and interface standards.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Intermediate expertise in troubleshooting electrical systems focusing on the FPGA but including software and hardware.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Intermediate ability to find, recommend, and implement innovative and effective solutions to design issues.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Basic expertise analyzing FPGA power, performance, resources, and other tradeoffs relative to system requirements</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Basic expertise architecting FPGA designs to meet system requirements. This includes contributing to system requirements development</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Intermediate understanding of the internal structure of FPGAs and the behavior of FPGA design tools</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Basic expertise improving and influencing the direction and process of FPGA design within Karl Storz</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Basic expertise leading other engineers in a technical role.This includes mentoring and knowledge sharing.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Intermediate expertise writing clear, accurate, and thorough requirements, analysis and design descriptions.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Knowledge of medical device quality system regulations and standards (eg, U.S. FDA Quality System Regulation 21CFR Part 820, ISO13485, ISO9001)</p>
</li>
</ul>
<div>
<p><b>EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE:</b></p>
</div>
<p>BS in Electrical or Computer Engineering and 2-5 years years experience in FPGA design or related field.</p>
<p>MS in Electrical or Computer Engineering and 0-2 years years experience in FPGA design or related field.</p>
