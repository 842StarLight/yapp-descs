<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage the marketing strategy and servicing of commercial insurance business in accordance with procedures outlined by the organization</li>
<li>Develop and produce submissions for marketing on new business/renewal account to carriers</li>
<li>Prepare proposals for new business policies in accordance with coverages and limits</li>
<li>Attend weekly sales meetings, discussing sales goals</li>
<li>Bind new business/renewal policies</li>
<li>Provide ongoing service to clients assisting account manager when needed, including but not limited to binders, invoices, endorsements, and certificates</li>
<li>Check policies to assure that coverages and limits are as ordered and that they match agency management system</li>
<li>Analyze and compare quotations</li>
<li>Review policy forms to ensure accuracy</li>
<li>Maintain account file in agency management system, including incoming and outgoing e-mail correspondence - Perform in a manner that will prevent errors and omissions</li>
<li>Be well-rounded with regards to various lines of coverage and be able to review potential exposures that can be addressed through risk transfer</li>
<li>Specialize in evaluating risks and consulting on missing coverage to ensure our clients are adequately insured - Assist in expanding existing accounts by rounding out and cross-selling additional insurance products</li>
<li>Assist in developing a service plan tailored to clientsâ€™ needs and work with account managers and others to service clients</li>
<li>Conduct periodic coverage analysis and risk reviews to ensure the adequacy of the client classification, coverages, limits, etc</li>
<li>Work with clients during audits and educate the client on the audit results</li>
<li>If needed help account manager process changes and renewals through communication with the insured and insurance companies</li>
<li>Pursue a program for personal and professional experience (continuing education classes, seminars, workshops) in combination with individual study programs to expand your knowledge and skills</li>
<li>Maintain and communicate up-to-date knowledge of market conditions, pricing strategies and market product options</li>
</ul>
