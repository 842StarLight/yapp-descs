<h2>Sales Data Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Summary:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>Our New York office is looking for an Associate to join the Sales Data Management Team. The team is responsible for the acquisition, attribution, maintenance and integrity of sales data for our distribution organization. The role requires close partnership with Sales Reporting &amp; Analytics, CRM, Finance, Technology and external vendors as we consider enterprise-wide improvements to our data and analytics capabilities.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Partner with Management, Sales Reporting, CRM and Finance teams to drive data processes for sales reporting and compensation purposes.
</li>
<li>Lead in the assessment of issues and opportunities, and in the development and implementation of complex workflows using Alteryx and Tableau for data integrity and analytics.
</li>
<li>Work with key stakeholders across all levels to communicate goals clearly and concisely and build consensus across teams to drive projects forward.
</li>
<li>Conduct analyses and tests of sales data requirements in partnership with Technology that conform to enterprise data management standards.
</li>
<li>Lead efforts in defining, documenting, and maintaining process flows that are fit for purpose; responsible for maintaining relevant policies and procedures.
</li>
<li>Focus on enhancing, streamlining, and automating processes. Be the subject matter expert for relevant processes and outcomes.
</li>
<li>Utilize various sources to verify asset and transaction details for exception management and resolution.
</li>
<li>Build robust reconciliation processes.
</li>
<li>Provide routine global sales reporting and ad hoc analysis focused on client coverage platform.
</li>
<li>Liaise with external vendors and sales firms to expand data coverage and enhance processes.
</li>
<li>Mentor junior team members; demonstrate and encourage broad adherence to best practices in data management processes and oversee procedures to ensure data integrity.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Requirements:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Business Administration, Finance, Computer Science or related field of study.
</li>
<li>Four to six years’ progressive experience in Asset Management with a focus on Distribution and Operations.
</li>
<li>Demonstrable proficiency in data management tools such as Alteryx and Tableau required.
</li>
<li>Experience with business intelligence analysis and project management skills.
</li>
<li>Attention to detail and the ability to manage multiple complex tasks within strict timeframes.
</li>
<li>Desire to work in a team-oriented environment yet able to work independently.
</li>
<li>Strong written and verbal communication skills.
</li>
<li>Knowledge of DST TA2000, Salesconnect/SalesVision, and Salesforce desirable.
</li>
<li>Experience in sales management and sales attribution is a plus.
</li>
</ul>
