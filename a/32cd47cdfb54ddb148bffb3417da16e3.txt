The Role
<br>--------
<br><br>
We're looking for a passionate writer, storyteller, and colleague to join Arundo as Marketing Communications Specialist. This role will drive content strategy and creation for sales and marketing initiatives across our products and general branding activities.
<br><br>
The Marketing Communications Specialist works with marketing, sales, product, and presales colleagues to define and deliver commercial content, including slides, blog articles, white papers, case studies, video scripts, landing pages, product sheets, press releases, and other written materials used in sales and marketing. The position reports to the VP Marketing and works closely with Product Marketing, Digital, and Demand Generation functions within Marketing.
<br><br>
The ideal Marketing Communications Specialist combines industrial or enterprise software experience and deep intellectual curiosity with persuasive writing experience. A passion for working with highly talented people in a fast-paced environment is a must.
<br><br>
We value critical thinking, self-motivation, and the ability to deal with ambiguity. The ideal candidate is willing to learn quickly about complex technical issues and produce high-quality content in short time-frames. Successful candidates will be strong communicators, and enjoy working closely with talented people to deliver extraordinary results. We want you to be passionate about delivering great products and achieving excellence in all you do. A strong interest about developments in AI, data science, and the industrial internet of things is a major plus.
<br><br>

<br><br>
