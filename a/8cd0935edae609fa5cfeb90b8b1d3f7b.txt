<h2>Process Improvement/Employee Development Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>DH Pace Company is seeking to hire an <b>Area Support Specialist</b> in Atlanta GA! You will be responsible for performing a combination of program administration and compliance functions related to the company's employee development initiatives, operational and administrative policies, procedures, and processes. You must be available to travel up to 65% of the time, to satellite locations in the Southeast region, as well as to other regions as required. Our Atlanta office will be relocating to Norcross before year's end.</p>
<p><b>
Job responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Review and follow up on audit findings, recommend training as needed.</li>
<li>Develop additional compliance topics to improve over-all performance.</li>
<li>Act as the Audit point person responsible for developing Remediation Task list, ensuring meetings are held, tasks are completed and are effective.</li>
<li>Review the functional tasks in each office for effectiveness.</li>
<li>Ensure consistency within all offices (size dependent) in the District, Region and Area.</li>
<li>Monitor COD/Problems, Credit Request, Contract tracking, Web invoicing, Commission plans audit (by spot auditing reports, fully auditing compliance on payment, sign off and approval process including a review of reductions for draws) Tool deducts, Concur-to ensure they are being maintained.</li>
<li>Coordinate with other Area Business Support teams to ensure consistency.</li>
<li>Identify and propose potential Process Improvement projects.</li>
<li>Work with all Senior Managers to ensure processes that are Best Practices, are documented, implemented and efficiently utilized.</li>
<li>In conjunction with Local Market Managers and Corporate Training Department, assist with developing Training Plans for all positions</li>
<li>Must be able to communicate the philosophy and culture of the DH Pace organization with a positive, out-going, and friendly manner</li>
<li>Ensure the Ambassador Program successfully welcomes each new employee professionally and provides the opportunity for successful acclimation into the organization.</li>
<li>Develop a new hire checklist which includes ensuring the employee has all necessary equipment, required software and supplies on their date of hire.</li>
<li>Assist Team Impact and others with coordinating employee events, community outreach, team building events</li>
<li>Coordinate with Training Department on LMS training opportunities-have off-the-shelf ready training plan for each new employee</li>
<li>Include development of consistent measurements, understanding differences in offices and including those differences in the measurements</li>
<li>Develop a cycle of ‘refresher’ courses for experienced employees</li>
<li>Participate in Process Improvement initiatives, ensuring the communication is rolled out accurately, timely and to the correct employees</li>
<li>Be responsible for other forms of communication, especially as it involves training requirements – ie: Risk Management initiatives, LOB changes, etc.</li>
<li>Participate in the project to update/clean-up/refresh SOPs</li>
<li>Be available to travel up to 65% of the time, within the Area to Satellites as well as to other Areas (Regions) as required</li>
<li>Ability to work additional hours as required</li>
<li>Other duties may be assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelors degree and a minimum of three (3) years of related experience/training, and process improvement required OR an equivalent combination of education and experience</li>
<li>Proficiency in MS office</li>
<li>Excellent time management skills, ability to multi-task, and prioritize work</li>
<li>Must have a valid drivers license</li>
</ul>
