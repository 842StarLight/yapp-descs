<div>
<p><b>Job Responsibilities</b></p>
</div>
</div>
<br>
</div>
</div>
<p>The Sales Operations function is responsible for driving an efficient sales process by optimizing sales activities including sales opportunity planning, customer proposals, and prospect engagement tracking. Assistance is also required in the development, implementation and analysis of marketing campaigns. Development of appropriate databases, resources, and lists for the sales team are also required.</p>
<div>
<div>
<div>
<div>
<p><b>Essential Duties &amp; Responsibilities</b></p>
</div>
</div>
<br>
</div>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Ensure optimal utilization of the Sales Opportunity Planner</li>
<li>Create proposals and RFPs using CRM automation, InDesign templates and Readz digital platform</li>
<li>Manage and enforce the proposal request process</li>
<li>Ensure digital and printed material that are used in board interactions are accurate, on brand and aligned with our sales methodology</li>
<li>Architect, update and polish various sales presentations, modifying as needed</li>
<li>Help RVP, Business Development to execute Regional sales pursuit strategy</li>
<li>Proactively seek regular feedback from sales leadership and field on customer problems, priorities and opportunity areas</li>
<li>Proactively seek regular feedback from marketing leadership on customer problems and operational changes to ensure proposal accuracy</li>
<li>Field ad hoc content and support requests from sales team</li>
<li>Manage a sales enablement content repository</li>
<li>Consistently analyze opportunities to accelerate sales cycle through sales tools</li>
<li>Assist with events - includes planning, setup and tear down</li>
<li>Regular attendance is required</li>
</ul>

<p><b>Education &amp; Experience</b></p>
</div>
</div>
<br>
</div></div>

<li>3+ years' experience in corporate marketing or communications environment, with direct responsibility for researching and writing content for a variety of topics and audiences.</li>
</ul>

<p><b>Knowledge, Skills &amp; Proficiencies</b></p>
</div>
</div>
<br>
</div></div>
<p>To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill and/or ability required.</p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrates excellent oral and written communication skills. Ability to write with persuasion</li>
<li>Must be goal minded and possess a self-starting drive to get the job done.</li>
<li>Urgency and time management skills with ability to manage and set expectations</li>
<li>Experience working with CRM platforms or similar systems. (Microsoft Dynamics CRM a plus.)</li>
<li>Proficient knowledge of Microsoft Applications, including Word, PowerPoint and Excel.</li>
<li>Intermediate knowledge of Adobe Creative Suite, InDesign</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Ability to monitor, analyze campaigns and report findings to RVP</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Experience with sales enablement software a plus.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Ability to maintain a positive attitude and work well under pressure</li>
<li>Excellent problem solver; demonstrates ability to use creative alternatives.</li>
<li>Works effectively with coworkers, clients, customers, and others by sharing ideas in a constructive and positive manner; addresses opportunities constructively to find mutually acceptable and practical business solutions.</li>
</ul>
