<h2>Marketing Specialist, Full Time</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>
<div>
<b>Summary: </b><b>R</b>esponsible for assisting with overall marketing responsibilities, including marketing campaigns, promotions, events, media planning and other general needs of the marketing plan. This position will primarily also handle the administrative needs of the department which includes billing, expense reports, sponsorship/donation/ad-request forms and implementation (including gift needs), rate sheet updates and competitive analysis. This position will have a special focus on Banterra Center and the partnership opportunities with SIU; while the entire marketing department will work on this, this position will be the point person on coordinating the tasks and developing brand exposure opportunities on campus and with the Saluki fan base.</div>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>All employees are expected to protect the information and assets of the organization through heightened awareness of information security, cyber security, and risk management best practices, as well as complying with all applicable laws, regulations, and organizational policies.</div>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>Responsibilities: </b>include but are not limited to the following. Other duties may be assigned.</div>
<ul>
<li>
<div>Work closely with Marketing Director on the branding and promotional opportunities with Banterra Center, serving as point person for coordinating promotion on campus and to Saluki fan base.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Assist in implementing and administering procedures for the organization’s business development, advertising, public relations and marketing functions.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Assist the Marketing Director in preparation, design and distribution of in-branch advertising pieces such as flyers, signs, hand-outs, and banners. Work on graphic design and copywriting.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Assist Marketing Director in negotiating and planning media buys.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Assist with online marketing needs including online banner placement; web, mobile, and tablet development; email programs; social media; SEO (Search Engine Optimization) and PPC (Pay Per Click) media opportunities.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Create presentations for meeting needs and in-branch marketing opportunities.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Assist with administrative tasks including processing invoices and request forms, preparing reports, posting interest rates, and archiving ad materials.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Process sponsorship, donation and advertising requests from the branches to include supplies, advertising promotional items, gift basket requests, and other requests as warranted.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Use marketing software and provide report needs as assigned.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Maintain inventory of promotional items and assist Marketing Director in the purchase and budget tracking of these items.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Provide graphic design, copywriting, editing and proofreading tasks for various marketing and company needs.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Assist HR and Marketing Director with talent recruitment needs by evaluating digital opportunities and targeting appropriate opportunities.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Assist with Customer Survey process, which includes online surveys; as well as further review and recommend a digital survey process for Banterra team members.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Maintain consistency of messaging to enhance and reinforce branding across multiple platforms.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Assist with writing needs which includes blogs, website copy, ad copy, press releases and newsletters.</div>
</li>
</ul>
