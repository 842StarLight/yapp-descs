<h2>CMM Calibration Field Service Technician</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Flexible Start Date.</b></p>
<p><b>2 Years CMM calibration and/or CMM inspection experience preferred.</b></p>
<p><b>Job Description</b></p>
<p>Calibrating, certifying and repairing coordinate measuring machines (CMM's) at customer facilities. We perform service on all major brand CMMs. Most jobs will be within 2 hours of your home, some will have overnight travel. Approximately 50% travel required. In addition to calibration and repair, technicians perform installations and/or retrofit CMM's with new hardware/software and provide training on equipment application.</p>
<p>http://cmmtechnology.com/employment/</p>
<p><b>Company Description</b></p>
<p>CMM Technology, Inc has been in business for over 25 years performing coordinate measuring machine (CMM) calibrations, repair, retrofits and sales. We've built our reputation on providing exceptional value and the highest quality service and support to clients across the country.</p>
<p><b>Required Skills</b></p>
<p>· Excellent customer service and professionalism</p>
<p>· Strong math skills and electrical and mechanical aptitude</p>
<p>· Knowledge of AutoCAD or similar 3D part creation software is a plus</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>· Calibration of various brands of CMMs at customer sites</p>
<p>· Service calls to diagnosis, troubleshoot and repair of CMMs</p>
<p>· Installation of new hardware and software</p>
<p>· Provide technical support to customers and other technicians</p>
<p>· Ensure prompt reporting of service to customers and the office</p>
<p><b>Training</b></p>
<p>Complete on-the-job training is provided for approximately 2-4 weeks (based on experience), working daily, at full salary, with an experienced CMM Technology technician.</p>
<p><b>Compensation</b></p>
<p>Salary $50K. Company vehicle, paid vacation (10 days) / holidays (7 days) and health insurance (Health Net PPO).</p>
<p>All required calibration equipment and supplies including laptop and cell phone provided.</p>
