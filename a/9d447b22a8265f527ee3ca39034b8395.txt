<h2>Credit Union Relationship Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Member Business Financial Services MBFS</b>, a credit union-owned company, is looking for a Credit Union Relationship Manager for the Pittsburgh Municipal area. This position is responsible for acquiring and retaining a portfolio of commercial credit union clients. This position will conduct relationship review meetings where opportunities are identified for commercial loan and/or deposit products and traditional credit union products and services. This role has day to day responsibility for the delivery of superior customer service, operational integrity and adherence to credit union policy as well as all state and federal regulations.</p>
<p><b>
Requirements</b><b>:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
BA/BS, MBA required.</li>
<li>
5-8 years of experience in business or financial sales.</li>
<li>
Work requires knowledge of small business and consumer loan policy; credit granting criteria, interest rates and how they are computed, and required documentation for each type of loan.
</li>
<li>Knowledge of loan interviewing skills and techniques also required.</li>
<li>
Must have experience in analyzing business financial statements and assessing the viability of businesses; must have excellent communication and PR skills; ability to use related software required.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of financial statement interpretation as applied to business accounting and financial principles.</li>
<li>
Demonstrated problem solving and analytical ability, and strong oral and written communication skills.</li>
</ul>
<p>
Our commitment to your success is enhanced by our competitive salary commensurate with experience and an extensive benefits package including paid time off, health benefits, 401k with a generous company match, and future growth opportunities within the company. We work to maintain the best possible professional and environmentally friendly atmosphere for our employees.</p>
<p>
To be considered, correspondence should include a resume listing job history and salary expectations. Please be advised that credit, criminal, employment, drug/alcohol testing and education background checks are conducted on potential candidates. All resumes will be reviewed, however, only competitive candidates will be contacted for further conversation.</p>
