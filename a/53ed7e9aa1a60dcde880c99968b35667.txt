As a leading provider of solutions across the continuum of care, we offer a path to fully automated medication management infrastructure, powered by a cloud data platform that supports improved patient care, fewer errors, enhanced safety, and new opportunities for growth.</p>

<p><b>Implementation Manager</b></p>
<p>
The Implementation Manager plays a crucial role in managing the installation and on-going servicing of our systems. After the sale of a system is complete, the Implementation Manager works in conjunction with the Project Deployment Manager is the primary customer contact onsite during implementations.
</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage all project administrative activities of multiple simultaneous projects</li>
<li>Manage and report to various project budgets and timelines</li>
<li>Develop, and mentor Implementation Consultants and project team members</li>
<li>Provides input to IT strategic and tactical planning and high level staffing plans</li>
<li>Plan all aspects of complex software implementation projects and other assignments.</li>
<li>Manage project scope, forecast, resources, and deliverables within specified deadlines while continuously assessing the client’s ability to change relative to the agreed scope</li>
<li>Work directly with the client project management team throughout all project phases</li>
<li>Conduct customer webinars related to Omnicell products</li>
<li>Proactively identify significant project risks, develop mitigation strategies, escalate to leadership, and implement approved mitigation steps</li>
<li>Provide internal and external project status reporting, ensuring all stakeholders receive the required level of detail.</li>
<li>Actively lead status meetings with the client and project team. Complete weekly reports for customer projects and internal updates</li>
<li>Review documentation and verify customer assets to provide reliable BI (Business Intelligence) to Sales, Operations and Service</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Required Knowledge and Skills:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Possess a wide product based knowledge, strong base in interfacing and databases</li>
<li>Ability to facilitate in-person and via webinar with a high level of learning effectiveness and satisfaction by participants</li>
<li>Strong negotiation/problem solving skills</li>
<li>Outstanding interpersonal, written and oral communication skills</li>
<li>Ability to handle multiple projects simultaneously</li>
<li>Detail-oriented, independent and able to adjust to changing priorities</li>
<li>Able to quickly learn new software and functionality of new products</li>
<li>Ability to work collaboratively and enjoy working in a fast-paced technical environment</li>
<li>Proven track record of success in managing multiple projects/multiple presentation deadlines at one time</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task and manage time efficiently</li>
<li>Excellent skills with Microsoft Project, Word, Excel, and PowerPoint</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Note:</b> The selected individual must have a valid driver license with an acceptable driving record, be able to meet company’s insurance coverage requirements and have their own vehicle in acceptable working condition.</p>

Basic Qualifications:</b></p>
