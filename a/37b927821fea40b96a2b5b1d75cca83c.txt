<h2>Account Executive 1</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Job Title: </b> Account Executive</p>
<p><b>Department: </b> Sales - Account Executives</p>
<p><b>Manager: </b> Sales Manager</p>
<p><b>Status: </b> Full Time, Exempt<br><b>Compensation: </b> $50,000 base salary with $100,000 OTE (on-target-earnings), uncapped</p>
<p><b>WHAT YOU’LL DO: </b></p>
<p>NAVEX Global provides a full suite of ethics and compliance software products. We’re an industry leader with exciting plans to continue growing, and we’re looking for driven, enthusiastic Account Executives to be a part of this growth. If you’ve been looking for a company where you can feel like the product you sell is making a positive impact in the world, then look no further! We help companies protect their people, their reputation, and their bottom line.</p>
<p>In this role, you’ll partner with existing customers to identify opportunities for cross-sell, while taking a proactive approach to bring in new customers with your prospecting prowess. You’ll identify and engage with the decision makers within an organization to uncover their needs, demonstrate the value of our products, and assist them through the implementation process. And don’t worry, you’re not alone in this! Our team offers a collaborative work environment where you’ll partner with Marketing, Solutions Engineers, Product Specialists, and your Sales</p>
<p>Manager to ensure you have the resources you need to be successful.</p>
<p><b>WHAT YOU NEED: </b></p>
<ul><li>1-3 years of B2B (business-to-business) sales experience. SaaS experience a plus, but we’ll teach you what you don’t already know!</li></ul>
<ul><li>A competitive edge with a drive for results, desire for success, and a passion for learning.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong planning, organizational, and time-management skills. This is a fast-paced role and the ability to stay on top of your day is key!</li></ul>
<ul><li>Communication skills are a must. The ability to be persuasive yet patient, and compelling yet compassionate. You’ll be forging strong relationships with customers and must be able to earn their trust.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Technical ability to be proficient with Salesforce and Microsoft Office products.</li></ul>
<p><b>WHAT WE OFFER YOU: </b></p>
<ul><li>Well-defined career path in sales. Move from small market, to mid-market, to Enterprise level sales as you demonstrate success. Or decide you want to move laterally into an entirely different department someday, we’ll help with that too.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Crazy good training program. You’ll spend your first two weeks in a sales intensive class, where you’ll learn all about our products and processes.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Work-life balance, unlimited PTO (used wisely, of course), health benefits, and a 401K. All the things you need for successful adulting.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Great culture, NAVEX family of peers, team lunches, happy hours, and lots of fun activities!</li></ul>
<ul><li>Industry leading name brand recognition, a strong product offering, and the opportunity to join a growing company.</li></ul>
