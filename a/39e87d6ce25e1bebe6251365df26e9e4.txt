<h2>Business Sales Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Job Description:</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>
The Builder Sales Manager role is responsible for driving new construction business for the division(s) they support. This is an outside business development role responsible for devising the overall new construction strategy for the division(s), cultivating new opportunities with builders/developers, recruiting new construction Lending Officers from other organizations, managing new/existing accounts, and training our internal sales force. Ideal candidates have extensive outside business development experience, can deliver dynamic presentations, and will drive accountability through top tier management skills.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Primary Responsibilities</b><b>:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Partner with Division Executive and support staff to devise and implement new construction strategy for the division(s)</li>
<li>
Recruit new Lending Officers and managers who focus in new construction</li>
<li>
Partner with other channels within the enterprise (SBB, CREB, ML, UST, etc..) to deepen relationships with builders/developers</li>
<li>
Up skill Retail Sales and Builder FFT associates through live training on varies new construction topics. Further training regimen to improve overall new construction execution and client delight</li>
<li>
Drive incremental volume through intensive management routines with builder partners</li>
<li>
Target new construction developments in priority markets located in Low to Moderate Income Tract (LMICT) and Multicultural Census Tract (MCCT) area to drive more LMICT/MCCT volume. Additionally, provide Neighborhood Lending with more activity data to report to OCC.</li>
<li>
Increase client capture and asset acquisition by driving incremental volume through new builder relationships</li>
</ul>
