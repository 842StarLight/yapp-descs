<h2>Advertising Sales Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>The Inside Sales Representative will work out of our Providence, RI office and will be responsible for achieving assigned sales goals, executing a comprehensive sales strategy, and maintaining relationships with clients. The ideal candidate would have at least one year of sales experience and be comfortable dealing with issues of an adult nature.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Accountabilities:</b></p>
<p>
*Servicing &amp; maintaining existing client base with weekly phone calls</p>
<p>
*Providing customer service to walk-in business &amp; new clients via the phone</p>
<p>
*Meeting sales goals
</p>
<p>*Prospecting new business from other media in order to expand client base</p>
<p>
*Coordinating layout of section, working with art &amp; traffic department on ad proofs</p>
<p>
*Handling and processing payments from customers &amp; clients</p>
<p>
*Tracking advertising trends and adjusting sales plan accordingly</p>
<br>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Job Requirements:</b></p>
<p>
* BA/BS preferred but not required</p>
<p>
* 1 year sales experience preferred</p>
<p>
* Must be able to work with advertising of an “adult” nature</p>
<p>
* Ability to confidently sell over the phone</p>
<p>
* Strong attention to detail</p>
<p>
* Must be able to work independently</p>
<p>
* Must have strong computer skills</p>
<p>
* Must have effective account management skills</p>
