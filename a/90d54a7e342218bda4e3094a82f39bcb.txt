<h2>Senior Systems Engineer (Digital Media Group) - Kenilworth, NJ</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">This Kenilworth based Senior System Engineer is responsible for technical design and performance of electronic systems in Digital Signage, A/V integration, and Experiential Design for our Digital Media Group.
<br><br>
What part will you play?
<br><br>
The Senior System Engineer must have proficiency in specifying and building wide range of equipment configurations to support multiple types of projects, as well as troubleshooting and maintaining a range of electronic and digital equipment.
<br><br>
What will you be doing?
<br><br>
The job responsibilities of the Senior System Engineer include the following:
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Determines system requirements for installations, ranging from simple to complex</li>
<li>Designs new systems and equipment configurations for DOOH (Digital out of home), including detailed schematic diagrams and cost analyses</li>
<li>Fabricates (mock up), installs, troubleshoots and tests equipment and systems.</li>
<li>Researches and recommends capital purchases to maintain or increase capacity of operations where applicable</li>
<li>R&amp;D of new products that can improve our offering to our clients</li>
<li>Manage their time appropriately</li>
<li>Self-sufficient team player responsible for engineering budget of a project and the successful implementation</li>
<li>Researches and implements procedures to increase effective maintenance</li>
<li>Upgrades operational software and hardware</li>
<li>Work directly with clients and project team to develop budgets, timelines and manage expectations.</li>
<li>Directly mentor other engineers, technical personnel and/or support staff.</li>
<li>Consistently maintain the privacy and confidentiality of information, protecting the assets of the organization, acting with ethics and integrity.</li>
<li>Assist sales with project estimates as needed</li>
<li>Develop BOM for system designs</li>
</ul>
What do we require from you?
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>5+ years relevant and related experience</li>
<li>Occasional travel required with the potential of some international travel</li>
<li>Ability to work in a fast-paced and deadline oriented environment</li>
<li>Strong verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Experience managing complex Audio and Video projects; design, construction, installation and integration</li>
<li>Scripting a plus</li>
<li>AMX/ Crestron programming a plus</li>
<li>Object Orientated programming a plus</li>
<li>Adaptability to changes in schedule or project needs</li>
</ul>
