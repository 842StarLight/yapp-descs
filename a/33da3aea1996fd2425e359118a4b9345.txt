<h2>Sales Recruiter</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Connection, a Fortune 1000 Global Technology Solutions Provider is adding to our family of passionate individuals. We work together to connect people with technology that enhances growth, elevates productivity, and empowers innovation. If you would like to discover how over 35 years of collaboration has shaped our culture of integrity, teamwork, and mutual success, we would love to speak with you!<br>
<br>
If you are an experienced, creative and dynamic Sales Recruiter then we are looking for you! As our Contract Recruiter, you will help us reach our goal of recruiting and hiring Professional Inside Sales Account Managers. You will enthusiastically promote Connection's mission while you actively seek qualified candidates through a variety of recruitment methods and build a pipeline of qualified talent. Your success is enabled by knowing our Account Managers start with a competitive base salary and with the opportunity to earn uncapped variable compensation. This is a great opportunity to use your creative recruitment strategies and be an integral part of Connection's growth!<br>
<br>
Your responsibilities will include but will not be limited to:</div>
<ul>
<li>
<b>
HEAVY Sourcing</b> and recruiting of qualified talent using a variety of recruitment methods to include all web based tools and methods, networking referrals, cold calling/direct sourcing of passive candidates, job fairs, and driving of the employee referral program.</li>
<li>
Establish and grow relationships with professional networks, the community and college contacts and promote Connection's mission.</li>
<li>
Build and maintain effective relationships with the management team to ensure understanding of our goals and hire talent that will find success for themselves and Connection.</li>
<li>
Work with the management team to brainstorm and deploy recruitment strategies and participate in all recruitment activities.</li>
<li>
Facilitate the hiring process to include phone screens, coordinating all pre-hire assessments, interviews, offers, and orientation while documenting all candidate activity in our applicant tracking system while ensuring compliance with all laws regarding employment.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
<b>
Qualifications:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor's degree preferred but will consider relevant work experience.</li>
<li>
3-5 years recruiting experience to include outbound call center and/or sales recruitment.</li>
<li>
Solid track record of setting high standards of performance; assuming responsibility and accountability for success.</li>
<li>
Experience utilizing all current recruitment practices to include social media and deep web sourcing.</li>
<li>
Proficiency with the Microsoft Office suite. Ability to work independently and as a collaborative team member.</li>
<li>
Ability to budget time efficiently. Superior customer service skills.</li>
<li>
Proven ability to utilize appropriate interpersonal styles to establish effective relationships with all candidates and colleagues.</li>
</ul>
