<h2>Business Development Specialist (M&A)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Overview
</b></p>Are you driven to win? Are you energized by the challenge of “getting to yes”? Does the prospect of working for a company with an aggressive growth profile sound exciting?
<br>
<br>
Harris is seeking a Business Development Analyst, to work closely with the senior management team on mergers and acquisitions (M&amp;A), as we build portfolios of mission-critical software solutions.
<p></p>
<p><b>Details
</b></p>
<p>The selected candidates will work directly with a Harris Portfolio Manager or Managers in sourcing and executing M&amp;A transactions in their portfolio. Our desired candidate is located in the Buffalo/Niagara Falls or Pittsburgh, PA area or willing to relocate. Remote candidates will be considered but office-based is preferred.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Primary Functions </b><b>:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Lead generation and mining of new M&amp;A business opportunities.
</li>
<li>Directly contact and engage companies for acquisition; identify, research, and cold-call target companies. Successful candidate will have strong proficiency in Business Development with an aptitude for lead generation, sales and account management.
</li>
<li>Career growth (1+ year) into providing financial analytical support, building financial models and performing other acquisition related analysis as necessary. To that end, candidate will have strong proficiency in Excel with a desire to grow into a blended Business Development / Financial role.
</li>
<li>Special projects &amp; ad hoc reporting as required.
</li>
<li>Some travel will be required, must be able to travel in the U.S. and Canada.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications </b><b>:
</b></p>
<p>To meet this challenge, you must possess a high level of initiative and energy, and a desire to flourish in a fast-paced evolving environment, as well as <b>several </b>of the following skills:
</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrable experience in Business Development with an aptitude for sales, lead generation and mining of new business opportunities; use of a CRM to track and analyze activity.
</li>
<li>1-3 years of direct experience in a similar role, business development, inside sales. Experience in corporate development, corporate finance, investment banking, investment analysis or M&amp;A preferred, but not required.
</li>
<li>Advanced knowledge of and proficiency with Microsoft Excel coupled with excellent analytical skills.
</li>
<li>Excellent written and oral communication skills.
</li>
<li>Ability to meet deadlines in a rapidly changing business environment.
</li>
<li>Strong initiative to work independently.
</li>
<li>Undergraduate degree in Business, Finance or related discipline..
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Company Overview </b><b>:
</b></p>
<p>Harris Computer (“Harris”) is a leading provider of mission critical software to the public sector in North America. As a wholly owned subsidiary of Constellation Software Inc. (“CSI”, symbol CSU on the TSX), Harris has become of the cornerstone for CSI’s investment in utility, local government, school districts, public safety, and healthcare software verticals. Our success has been realized through investment in our proprietary software and market expertise. This focus combined with acquiring businesses that build upon or complement our offerings has helped drive our success. Harris will continue to growth through reinvestment – both in the people and products that we offer and making investments in acquiring new businesses.
</p>
