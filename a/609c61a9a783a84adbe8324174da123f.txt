<h2>Sr. Manager, Brand Design (Ecom/Hive)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Who We Are…<br>
We are a creatively driven team that leverages the power of Design Thinking to solve brand and business problems that drive meaningful value and growth. Our team’s mission is to lead the innovation agendas in order to accelerate PepsiCo’s Global brands and businesses and transform beyond its core. We innovate with intent; to solve real problems, for real people, in the real world. We innovate to incite positive change – for people, brands, business and the world around us.<br>
<br>
What You’ll Do…<br>
The Senior Manager, Brand Design will provide clear leadership on brand design strategy. This role is a senior designer responsible for the management and execution of comprehensive brand and graphic design projects in support of new and existing Ecommerce/Hive brands, establishing standards and ensuring design excellence across all work. You will leverage the power of Design Thinking and demonstrate superior ability to inspire and engage cross-functional teams to drive projects from ideation to execution with quality brand design solutions, directly executing them or in collaboration with external partners.<br>
<br>
The main objective of this role is to execute major brand design projects, strategies, and innovations. This means planning, organizing, and implementing consistent design principles and processes to ensure that the design, strategy and production guardrails are met.<br>
<br>
Accountabilities:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Design Project Management: Responsible for partnering across disciplines within PepsiCo and external suppliers to ensure design assets are developed and delivered with quality, value, and on-time. Lead project team from idea phase to commercialization. Ensures each stakeholder understands his/her role and responsibilities throughout the creative process. Regularly reviews project status with appropriate leadership to verify that Company and Division expectations are met</li>
<li>
Brand Design Development: Develops and directs total visual brand identity for specific brands in the portfolio, working in collaboration with the Chief Design Officer and other design leaders cutting across brand, product, packaging, marketing equipment, and advertising. Generates creative design proposals (visions, scenarios, concepts) in order to visualize the desired output and share it with internal and external team members. Participates in research activities and effectively translates customer needs into actionable design requirements and solutions. Responsible for final design recommendations, decisions and design approval to enable artwork execution / production phases. Ensure thorough consideration of all project variables and that the final solution will perform as planned</li>
<li>
Design R&amp;D and Innovation: Identifies and promotes self-initiated design programs that generate critical business value for one or more divisions</li>
<li>
External Design Network: Supports the PepsiCo Design Organization in building and cultivating a strong external design network</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Qualifications/Requirements</b></h2>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s Degree in Communication Design, Experience Design, Brand Design, or Graphic Design required. Master’s degree in a Design discipline preferred</li>
<li>
A minimum of 10 years of work experience in a hands-on brand/graphic design role</li>
<li>
Talk and think about design in all its forms</li>
<li>
Have a 360 degree understanding of the world - an excellent sense of pop culture, music, art, retail, tech, and design trends</li>
<li>
Expertise in using the Adobe Creative Suite software</li>
<li>
Experience participating in human-centered research and defining design actionable principles</li>
<li>
Ability to work in an ambiguous and dynamic work environment; is comfortable with risk and uncertainty</li>
<li>
Effectively build relationships and influence senior levels in the organization across regions and functions in order to deliver on project plans</li>
<li>
Drive for results, able to influence and accomplish tasks through formal and informal channels</li>
<li>
Effectively communicates (verbally and written) with internal and external stakeholders across all levels. Must have compelling presentation and proposal skills.</li>
<li>
Must be willing and able to travel 15-20% of the time</li>
</ul>
