<h2>Senior Enterprise Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Udacity's mission is to power the enterprise through next-generation technology education. We're an immersive online learning platform, powering corporate technical training in fields such as Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Data Science, Autonomous Systems, Cloud Computing and more. We are backed by some of the best-known investors: Andreessen Horowitz, Charles River Ventures, Bertelsmann and Drive Capital, among other leading VC firms. Udacity is helping the enterprise market bridge the talent shortage and skills gaps during their digital transformation journey. We are providing a powerful and differentiated solution inside of a $32b total addressable market opportunity. If you are inspired to be a core component of this transformational opportunity... read on!
<br><br>
As a Senior Account Executive, focused on Enterprise Sales, you'll be responsible for driving our fast growing enterprise business through new client acquisition. You will help leading companies empower their teams with training in the latest technologies so that they can develop powerful new products and services on a global scale. We're looking for someone who has a proven track record selling to and/or consulting with C level executives at Fortune 1,000 companies, and can excel in a start-up fast paced environment.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
-----------------
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Aggressively prospect, identify, qualify and develop sales pipeline.</li>
<li>Close business to exceed monthly, quarterly and annual bookings objectives.</li>
<li>Leverage industry trends to understand customer pain points and tailor solutions based on customer needs.</li>
<li>Conduct meetings with C-suite and confidently speak as a leader in this space.</li>
<li>Partner with product team to provide feedback on trends and identify opportunities to deliver greater value to customers.</li>
<li>Grow the team as we scale.</li>
</ul>
<b>Qualifications:</b><br>
---------------
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Has 8+ years in enterprise sales -- start-up or early stage company experience is preferred.</li>
<li>Uses a consultative and solution/value selling approach to closing new business.</li>
<li>Demonstrates strong business development, negotiation, and communication skills at the client C level.</li>
<li>Gathers in-depth knowledge of client's business, organizational structure, and business processes to drive sales.</li>
<li>Has an exceptionally strong track record in advanced enterprise sales with proven over-performance.</li>
<li>Is forward-looking, flexible, creative, and proactive.</li>
<li>Experience with corporate training products/services a plus.</li>
</ul>
We are an equal opportunity employer and value diversity at our company. Women, people of color, members of the LGBTQ community, individuals with disabilities, and veterans are strongly encouraged to apply.</div>
