<h2>Senior Industrial Hygienist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Job responsibilities include but are not limited to:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Perform project hygiene work.</li>
<li>Technical Review of Hygiene proposals/projects.</li>
<li>Preparation of technical proposals/approaches/strategies, executing IH work, internal team consultant, project management and Senior review of IH reports.</li>
<li>Assist with management of national account work.</li>
<li>Presentations and sales contact for new clients.</li>
<li>Timely billing and management of WIP.</li>
<li>Safety training and mentoring.</li>
<li>Abide by ATC’s health and safety policies and procedures.</li>
</ul>
<b>Basic qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>B.S./M.S. in Industrial Hygiene or related science program.</li>
<li>Minimum 7-10 years experience in Environmental Health and Safety Consulting, Industrial hygiene and Asbestos/lead/microbial consulting services – commercial, residential, school environments.</li>
<li>Strong technical experience needed in indoor air quality, emergency response assessments, disaster claim related assessments and other industrial hygiene services in diverse industrial, commercial and health care environments.</li>
<li>Current CIH and or PE Certification. IDPH accreditations for Asbestos and Lead Work would be beneficial.</li>
<li>USEPA AHERA, USEPA NESHAP, OSHA 29cfr1926.1101, OSHA 29cfr1926.62, HUD title 10, IDPH Asbestos and Lead Regs, CDPH regs.</li>
<li>Familiarity with MS office software, Client Relationship Management software, basic CAD software, typical IH Equipment (Q-trak, RMD XRF, Jerome, etc) software.</li>
<li>Some out of town travel (as needed) is expected.</li>
<li>Valid driver’s license</li>
</ul>
<b>Desired qualifications:</b><br>
*
<br><br>
Good interpersonal communication skills and project management skills a must. Business development and client management experience is a plus.
