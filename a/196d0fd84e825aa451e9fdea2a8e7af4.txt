<div><b>Position Overview</b></div>
<div>The Curriculum Academic Project Manager is a strategic role in Siemens global academic program. Working with strategic stakeholders internally and external, this role leads key areas of enablement for the program, including curriculum and certification, to deliver a shared value to academic institutions, Siemens customers and internal stakeholders.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Responsibilities</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Improve penetration of Siemens software across academic institutions.</li>
<li>Lead an external Curriculum and Certification Advisory Board.</li>
<li>Align with sales, product and learning/development teams to drive academic requirements.</li>
<li>Deliver new content and curriculum to academic partners globally.</li>
<li>Drive certifications for the academic market to include micro credentials.</li>
<li>Analyze industry needs and competitor offerings to drive differentiation.</li>
<li>Drive strategies to publish content in external MOOCs for widespread adoption.</li>
<li>Manage project plans to ensure all materials are delivered on time, in budget and with high quality.</li>
<li>Effectively manage team members assigned to projects and communicate progress.</li>
<li>Increase content usage through proactive promotion.</li>
<li>Assist the team to define, plan, and implement improvements to the program.</li>
</ul>

<li>Bachelor's Degree in STEM (preferred) or Academic/Education or Instructional Design fields</li>
<li>
Experience developing and implementing successful programs</li>
<li>3+ years' of proven project management skills</li>
<li>Strong collaborative problem-solving skills to overcome obstacles</li>
<li>Effective communication and presentation skills</li>
<li>Willingness to travel 25-50% of the time</li>
<li>Team player, able to juggle multiple work streams with matrixed resources and goals in a dynamic, growing company</li>
<li>Flexible and adaptable to changing requirements</li>
<li>Ensures focus on measurable outcomes and value to the business</li>
<li>Establish appropriate measurement criteria and report to management quantifiable progress over time</li>
<li>Experience working with vendors and subcontractors, including budgeting and purchasing, monitoring and driving to firm schedules and results</li>
</ul>
