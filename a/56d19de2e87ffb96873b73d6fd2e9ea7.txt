<h2>Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Back Bay Roasters is changing the way people caffeinate with its fresh, never-heated, true cold brew coffee that is fully manufactured in-house. With distribution in over 1,000 stores nationwide, Back Bay Roasters is one of the fastest-growing coffee companies in the country. Alongside our brand, Back Bay manufactures cold brew coffee products for several top brands in the specialty coffee industry.</p>
<p>
Back Bay is looking for a Marketing Manager to lead all marketing initiatives for the brand, including digital marketing, branded content, packaging, customer marketing and field activation. We’re looking for someone who is experienced in brand positioning, packaging and consumer research and can also lead social media and digital advertising. This person is a key member of Back Bay’s growth team and will report directly to the founders. The ideal candidate has experience working as a brand manager at a leading consumer products company, and also has small-company experience.</p>
<p>
This role is for someone who thrives in a fast-paced, entrepreneurial environment and wants to have a big impact at a fast-growing company. Back Bay’s work environment is collaborative, team-oriented and fun. And there is plenty of free cold brew coffee!</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
PRIMARY RESPONSIBILITIES:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Develop Back Bay Roaster’s marketing strategy across all consumer and customer touch points to increase awareness and trial</li>
<li>
Lead and manage a team of interns and brand ambassadors to implement Back Bay’s marketing strategy</li>
<li>
Create cohesive customer marketing programs in collaboration with sales team to exceed velocity goals at retail locations</li>
<li>
Build national marketing partnerships that will increase awareness and trial with key consumer targets</li>
<li>
Craft and oversee the implementation of Back Bay’s digital marketing strategy to drive brand awareness and deepen engagement with consumers</li>
<li>
Develop advertising that stands out and gets noticed by our consumer base and is authentic to the Back Bay brand story</li>
<li>
Manage the end-to-end development of all consumer and customer-facing marketing materials including packaging, merchandising vehicles, sell sheets, coupons and more</li>
<li>
Serve as the champion of our consumer base. Conduct consumer closeness activities to understand drivers of brand loyalty, affinity and usage occasions</li>
<li>
Track and analyze key metrics for all marketing initiatives. Make recommendations to improve conversion rates and increase efficiency of spend</li>
<li>
Play a key role in the strategic planning process and in goal setting as part of the management team</li>
</ul>
<br>
<p>
SKILLS AND EXPERIENCE:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Minimum 5 years of experience as a Senior Brand Manager or Director of Marketing at a consumer products company</li>
<li>
Experience developing brand positioning statements, testing innovation concepts and overseeing market research</li>
<li>
Solid knowledge and experience in digital marketing, including social media advertising, SEO and web analytics</li>
<li>
Bachelor’s Degree required</li>
<li>
Proven track record in moving the needle on a brand’s awareness, trial and sales</li>
<li>
Self-motivated, entrepreneurial and a creative problem solver</li>
</ul>
