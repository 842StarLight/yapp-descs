<h2>Restaurant General Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Oversee and coordinate the planning, organizing and training necessary to achieve stated objectives in sales, costs, employee retention, guest service and satisfaction, food quality, cleanliness and sanitation.</p>
<p>
Duties &amp; Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Ensuring all aspects of daily operations conform with company polices and standards</li>
<li>Develop and foster a work environment where all employees are free of harassment of any kind</li>
<li>Act as community ambassador, creating goodwill toward BurgerFi brand</li>
<li>Perform and/or oversee regular inventory procedures, order taking, and food waste control</li>
<li>Ensure that all guests feel welcome and are given responsive, friendly and courteous service at all times</li>
<li>Ensure that all food and products are consistently prepared and served according to the restaurants recipes, portioning, cooking and serving standards</li>
<li>Achieve company objectives in sales, service, quality, appearance of facility and sanitation and cleanliness through training of employees and creating a positive, productive working environment</li>
<li>Control cash and other receipts by adhering to cash handling and reconciliation procedures in accordance with restaurant policies and procedures</li>
<li>Make employment and termination decisions</li>
<li>Continually strive to develop staff in all areas of managerial and professional development</li>
<li>Supervise and participate in kitchen and dining are cleaning</li>
<li>Prepare all required paperwork, including forms, reports and schedules in an organized and timely manner</li>
<li>Compile and balance cash receipts at the end of day/shift</li>
<li>Ensure all equipment is kept clean and in excellent working condition through personal inspection and by following the restaurant's preventative maintenance program</li>
<li>Ensure all products are received in correct unit count and condition</li>
<li>Schedule labor as required by anticipated business activity while ensuring that all positions are staffed when and as needed and labor cost objectives are met</li>
<li>Be knowledgeable of restaurant policies regarding personnel and administer prompt, fair and consistent corrective action for any and all violations of company policies, rules and procedures.</li>
<li>Fully understand and comply with all federal, state, county and municipal regulations that pertain to health, safety and labor requirements of the restaurant, employees and guests</li>
<li>Resolve customer complaints regarding food service</li>
<li>Assist Marketing team in developing an carrying out restaurant marketing, advertising and promotional activities and campaigns</li>
<li>Schedule and conduct periodic meetings with employees to discuss and/or correct problem areas or areas of concern</li>
</ul>
<div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Skills &amp; Requirements</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>Three or more years of restaurant management experience</li>
<li>Outstanding leadership and coaching abilities</li>
<li>Excellent communication and people skills</li>
<li>Assess staffing needs, staff scheduling, and training</li>
<li>Monitor inventory and purchasing</li>
<li>Assessment and maintenance of COG's and Labor Costs</li>
<li>Marketing development and advertising knowledge</li>
<li>Maintain and enforce sanitation standards and procedures</li>
<li>Experience with Micros POS system, Microsoft Office based financial reporting</li>
<li>Be able to reach, bend, stoop and frequently lift up to 50 pounds</li>
<li>Must be able to work in a standing position for long periods of time (up to 8 hours)</li>
<li>Must have the stamina to work a minimum of 50 hours a week</li>
<li>Must be able to travel out of state for a 2 1/2 week training course</li>
<li>Must have valid driver's license and be ServSafe &amp; TABC certified</li>
</ul>
