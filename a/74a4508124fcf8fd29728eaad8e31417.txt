<h2>Office Manager/Executive Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>We currently have an excellent opportunity for a highly-skilled and motivated Office Manager/Executive Assistant at a growing company. The Office Manager/Executive Assistant is responsible for coordinating and managing the office function, and assist general manager in daily work.</p>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Under the leadership of the General Manager, be responsible for the daily management of the office, formulate and implement the office work plan and provide administration and logistics services (10%).</li>
<li>Supervise the logistics work of each department and propose suggestions for improvement (10%).</li>
<li>Manage and maintain corporate files and seals, establish internal file and seal keeping and management rules and regulations, and supervise the implementation (10%).</li>
<li>Organize company meetings per instruction by the General Manager, maintain meeting minutes, distribute corporate resolutions to each department and assist the General Manager to supervise implementation of the corporate resolutions (10%).</li>
<li>Be responsible for the company's reception work, establish and maintain good relationship with various local governments and communities (10%).</li>
<li>Assist the General Manager in handling daily affairs and work schedules (10%).</li>
<li>Assist the General Manager in handling business negotiations, data translation, paperwork, etc. (10%).</li>
<li>Be responsible for the classification and submission of all kinds of documents, present to the General Manager for approval and transfer to the relevant departments for processing (10%).</li>
<li>Assist the General Manager to investigate, research, and understand the company's operation and management, and offer comments or suggestions for the General Manager to make decisions (10%).</li>
<li>Complete other work assigned by the General Manager (10%).</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Experience as office manager</li>
<li>Working knowledge of Excel, Word, and PPT otherOffice Software</li>
<li>Must possess solid mathematical skills, strong organizational skills, and be detail oriented</li>
<li>Must demonstrate the ability to work responsibly as a team member as well as an individual</li>
<li>Must be honest, fair, dependable, respect confidentiality and the rights and privacy of others</li>
</ul>
