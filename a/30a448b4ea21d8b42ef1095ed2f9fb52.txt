<h2>Strategy & Growth Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Position</b></h3>
<p>Reporting to the Director of Strategy &amp; Growth, North America, and based out of the Mountain View HQ, the Strategy &amp; Growth Manager will be a key player in driving the revenue growth for the company. You will be assigned a part of the North American market, and you will work actively with the Product, Marketing, Sales, Customer Success and Regulatory teams to drive growth efforts in your region like a true GM at strategic and tactical levels. You will also collaborate with the Global Strategy and Growth teams to ensure cross pollination of ideas and best practices.
</p>
<p>If your track record of success gives you confidence in your ability to scale businesses rapidly and keep clients happy, if you want your work to make a difference in the energy industry, and if you want to grow into a P&amp;L owner, we’d love you to join our world class team.
</p>
</div>
<div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities</b></h3>
<p><b><i>Revenue Growth (New Customers)</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Collaborate with the NA Sales team to drive growth efforts by tracking every deal and opportunity. Tactically providing sales support as a product and data science expert. Strategically helping the team identify the right opportunities and take the right approach. You will bring the brains and the drive to the Growth process.
</li>
<li>Acquire a deep understanding of utility market, and how Bidgely can play a disruptive role in introducing artificial intelligence to the market.
</li>
<li>Work with Sales Leaders to frame proposal response strategy and timelines. Lead collaborative process by engaging stakeholders, following-up on requests, and obtaining executive review and approval. Write, revise, edit proposal drafts to produce a final, high quality polished product that has a consistent structure, format, and tone.</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>Revenue Growth (Existing Customers)</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Build and foster relationships with stakeholders at our customer and partner organizations; goal is to generate additional and recurring revenues via cross-sell / upsell in collaboration with the Sales team.</li>
<li>Drive technical workshops to help customers define their business case and go-to-market approach.</li>
<li>Bring ideas and feedback to our product and innovation teams based on your interactions with our customers.</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>Product/ Marketing/ Analytics</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Be an expert on Bidgely’s Products and Data Science; lead key workshops with clients as an Expert</li>
<li>Collaborate with Product and Marketing to conceptualize new solutions that will help penetrate markets/ regions; drive go-to-market launch for these solutions.</li>
<li>Be a data junkie who can interpret, manipulate, and explain data.
</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>Strategy and Planning</i></b></p>
<div>
<div><ul>
<li>Collaborate with the CEO and Head of WW Sales to work on Board/ CEO requests.</li>
<li>Identify strategic initiatives based on industry and market trends that will help the company grow. Evaluate M&amp;A opportunities.</li>
</ul></div>
</div>
</div>
<div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Requirements</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor in Science, Arts or equivalent degree; MBA / JD from a top tier school strongly preferred</li>
<li>Management consulting and/or investment banking experience from top tier firms strongly preferred</li>
<li>Utility/ energy industry experience must have; SaaS experience nice to have</li>
<li>Analytical and able to interpret, manipulate and explain data trends
</li>
<li>Strategic, big picture, out-of-the-box thinking</li>
<li>Demonstrable ability to communicate, present and influence key stakeholders at all levels of an organization, including executive and C-level</li>
<li>Experience delivering client-focused solutions to customer needs</li>
<li>Proven ability to multitask, while maintaining sharp attention to detail</li>
<li>Excellent listening, negotiation and presentation abilities</li>
<li>Strong verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Bright, relentless, good, and bold; loves to work with people; can travel.</li>
</ul>
</div>
