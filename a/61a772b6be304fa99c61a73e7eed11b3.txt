<h2>Field Manager - Wine</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Responsible for the development of the entire portfolio from a volume as well as SKU stand point. A Wine Field Manager is expected to work with suppliers and sales people, targeting Off-Premise account development and assisting with special projects. This position will be based out of our office in Elizabeth, New Jersey, but is expected to spend 80 to 90% of their time on the road visiting accounts and growing market share.<br>
</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Open specific number of quality new accounts.
</li>
<li>Increase, maintain account base in revenue and distribution of key supplier brands.</li>
<li>
Penetrate undersold account base and increase sales and distribution of key supplier brands.</li>
<li>
Sell and market defined portfolio of premium wine and spirit products.</li>
<li>
Comply with federal, state and local legal requirements by studying existing and new legislation, anticipating legislation, enforcing adherence to requirements, and advising management on needed actions.</li>
<li>
Call on accounts and cover a daily route by creating an established and efficient routing pattern.<br>
Analyze entire account base by visiting each account and determining the specific needs of the individual accounts.</li>
<li>
Establish sales and merchandising objectives relative to brand priority for each account by understanding company priorities.</li>
<li>
Prepare and present professional sales presentation to customers by combining both the company’s priorities and customer's need.</li>
<li>
Understand company pricing and product programming information on a monthly basis by maintaining accurate and up to date records.</li>
<li>
Understand competitive pricing and product programming on a monthly basis by reading Beverage Media and other trade related publications.</li>
<li>
Obtain appropriate distribution and volume goals of supplier brands throughout account base by representing and selling based on the brands features and benefits.</li>
<li>
Must participate in all supplier meetings.</li>
<li>
Ensure maximum brand visibility within accounts by using effective point of sale materials when appropriate.</li>
<li>
Administer staff training seminars by educating the account's staff to build product knowledge.
</li>
<li>Organize special events to maximize brand visibility within accounts by promoting specific brands to account's customer.
</li>
<li>Keep current on inventory and out of stock situations by communicating with management as to problem situations.</li>
<li>
Communicate with management as to needs of specific accounts by maintaining organized territory and account records.</li>
<li>
Maintain professional relationships with all suppliers by participating in effective supplier work with sales calls.</li>
<li>
Attend and actively participate in weekly sales meetings by asking questions relative to current supplier activities.</li>
<li>
Maintains professional and technical knowledge by attending educational workshops, reviewing professional publications, establishing personal networks, and participating in professional societies.</li>
<li>
Contribute to team effort by accomplishing related results as needed.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in related field and/or equivalent training and work experience.</li>
<li>
A minimum of two years’ experience selling a wine portfolio is required.</li>
<li>
Broad based wine knowledge with commitment to continue to upgrade wine knowledge.</li>
<li>
WSET L2 required, WSET L3 preferred.</li>
<li>
Ability to hold a Solicitor’s Permit required.</li>
<li>
Valid driver’s license and clean driving record.</li>
<li>
A sound understanding of wine and spirits is required.</li>
<li>
Strong customer service and interpersonal skills required.</li>
<li>
Results oriented professional with excellent verbal/written communication skills; uses diplomacy and discretion.</li>
<li>
Ability to multi-task, work independently and/or within a team, pays attention to detail and meet deadlines.</li>
<li>
Proficient PC skills using MS Office and other various computer systems including presentation software.</li>
<li>
Familiarity with assigned territory is a plus.</li>
</ul>
