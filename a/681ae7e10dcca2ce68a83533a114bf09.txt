<h2>Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Company Description
<br>-------------------
<br><br>
At American Well, we believe digital care delivery will transform healthcare. Our mission is to connect and enable providers, insurers, patients, and innovators to deliver greater access to more affordable, higher quality care. We do this by partnering with our clients to deliver video visits over mobile and web. We have doctors, therapists, and specialists on staff to help people get care when and where they need it most.
<br><br>
<b>Brief Overview:</b><br>
The Project Manager will play a critical role in interfacing with clients, as well as internal sales, technology, product, and marketing organizations. Specifically, the Project Manager will be responsible for driving a successful implementation of the American Well telehealth platform, which meets specified objectives and requirements set by the client, and positioning the client for success with its telehealth service.
<br><br>
<b>Core Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Serves as an expert in all aspects of the American Well platform</li>
<li>During the initial client implementation, serves as the primary client contact and managing all aspects of the implementation project. Coordinates with internal technical, QA, marketing, and visual design teams to ensure delivery according to the agreed upon scope and schedule</li>
<li>Manages client expectations by building relationships, communicating project status and open issues, preparing reports, managing change, and facilitating weekly meetings</li>
<li>Provides functional and technical guidance to the client's project team relative to workflow and solutions design</li>
<li>Manages the overall requirements process to ensure the platform is configured to meet the client's needs</li>
<li>Facilitates training sessions for the client's project and operational teams, and end users of the platform, specific to each client's business model</li>
<li>Communicates project status with team members and and senior business stakeholders</li>
<li>Utilizes sound judgment in assessing project health; identifying, mitigating and resolving project issues and risks to ensure on-time project delivery</li>
<li>Provides ongoing support and management of new business initiatives, software upgrades, and enhancements</li>
<li>Partners with the Account Director to support client account growth and drive ongoing project activities in support of the client's strategic objectives</li>
<li>Contributes to continuous process improvements within the Professional Services organization</li>
</ul>
<b>Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>4-6 years of project management and/or consulting experience</li>
<li>Ability to concurrently manage multiple large projects in a technical and cross-functional environment, leading clients and team members through complex system implementations with aggressive delivery deadlines</li>
<li>Exceptional communication, leadership, team building, and people management skills</li>
<li>Outstanding time management and organization skills</li>
<li>Proven history of managing project teams to meet aggressive deadlines, utilizing sound project management methodology (processes, best practices, tools, and templates)</li>
<li>Ability to utilize effective communication skills to mitigate project risk</li>
<li>Proven experience managing project scope and schedule</li>
<li>Fluency with Microsoft Office Project, Excel, Word, PowerPoint, and Visio</li>
<li>Project Management Professional (PMP®) certification highly desired</li>
<li>Flexibility to travel up to 30% (overnight)</li>
</ul>
