<h2>Senior Director, Demand Marketing — Insight</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Sr. Director, Demand Marketing is a newly created position that reports to the CMO. A key member of the Marketing Leadership Team, this person will be responsible for development and execution of integrated demand marketing campaigns and coordination with the field to drive marketing contribution to pipeline that results in revenue for Gigamon Insight.</p>
<p>
 This person will be responsible for leadership and management of the Integrated Demand Marketing for Gigamon Insight , including campaign development, execution, field coordination and marketing lead qualification in conjunction with Sales. Campaigns need to be highly scalable and repeatable across multiple channels and regions, and consist of multi-phase outreach that utilizes a portfolio approach of online and offline marketing tactics including cross-media communications, email, web, thought leadership as well as online and in-person events, leveraging owned, earned and paid channels.</p>
<p>
Working with Sales and other Marketing leaders, this person will own the entire demand marketing process from developing segmentation and target lists to creating and implementing campaigns, ensuring timely follow up and hand off to sales, measuring results and making adjustments to optimize resources and outcomes.</p>
<p><b>
Key Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Develop, execute, coordinate and measure integrated demand marketing programs that drive qualified leads and marketing contribution to pipeline</li>
<li>
Manage the full lifecycle of integrated demand marketing programs including sales and marketing “playbooks” that articulate goals, messages and activities and help foster team alignment</li>
<li>
Develop iterative marketing campaigns and use control group testing to optimize lead acquisition</li>
<li>
Work with product/solutions marketing experts to develop messaging that targets audiences with the optimal messages, using a variety of marketing channels</li>
<li>
Work with internal/external resources to create compelling content and assets</li>
<li>
Work closely with the sales organization to gain buy in for the approach and to align with their goals</li>
<li>
Leverage marketing automation tools to bring scale to demand generation programs</li>
<li>
Establish and report on campaign metrics and ROI; communicate goals/results to organization</li>
<li>
Measure and monitor sales funnel activity, making recommendations to address coverage gaps</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Required Skills and Experience:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
7 to 10 years experience in building and/or managing cyber security focused marketing programs</li>
<li>
Previous experience marketing SaaS ( cloud-based ) products a must.</li>
<li>
15 to 20 years experience in technology marketing. At least 10 years in a high-growth or enterprise software company with direct responsibility for demand marketing strategy and execution.</li>
<li>
Experience managing a team of senior marketing professionals and interacting cross functionally with Sales leadership. Experience managing a lead qualification team or vendor a plus.</li>
<li>
Track record of success in demand marketing at a high growth technology company with multiple product lines, regions and distribution channels.</li>
<li>
Experience leading the development of exciting and effective demand marketing programs that influence CxO and functional buyers, utilizing the talents of a cross-functional group of directly-managed and influenced resources to achieve success and market impact.</li>
<li>
Ability to determine, implement and adjust the mix between marketing tactics to efficiently utilize those that best connect with the variety of buyers involved in an enterprise technology sale</li>
<li>
Knowledge and experience using a wide range of marketing tactics including cross media communications, email, web, PPC, SEO, social media, thought leadership and events to connect with target buyers</li>
<li>
Experience managing lead scoring and nurturing programs</li>
<li>
Expert knowledge of list management, cleansing, de-duplication, segmentation and targeting</li>
<li>
Executive level communication skills - both written and oral - including writing marketing campaigns</li>
<li>
Experience managing budgets</li>
<li>
Strong familiarity with Marketo, Eloqua or similar types of software</li>
<li>
Experience using and tracking leads in Salesforce.com</li>
<li>
A passion for technology</li>
<li>
Strong goal orientation and work ethic</li>
<li>
Ability to work in a team environment</li>
<li>
Self-starter that can hit the ground running and leverage their experience to elevate existing in-house demand marketing capabilities</li>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree required</li>
</ul>
