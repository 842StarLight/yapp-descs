<b>RETAIL STORE MANAGER</b><br>
<br>
Soccer Village is seeking a Retail Store Manager to join our team. At Soccer Village, we want to provide the best customer service experience possible. All Soccer Village Retail Store Managers play a vital part in the day to day operation of the store. If you are a soccer minded individual with customer service experience and a great attitude this is the job for you.<br>
All Store Locations Now Hiring<br>
<br>
<b>The Daily Duties will include the following:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Oversee Soccer Village Store operations</li>
<li>Maintain store staff by recruiting, selecting, orienting and training employees</li>
<li>Maintain store staff job results by coaching, counseling and disciplining employees; planning, monitoring and appraising job results</li>
<li>Identify current and future customer requirements by establishing rappor with potential and actual customers to understand service requirements</li>
<li>Ensure availability of merchandise and help ot maintain inventory</li>
<li>Market merchandise by studying advertising, sales promotion and display plans</li>
<li>Secure merchandise by implementing security systems and measures</li>
<li>Protect employees and customer by providing a safe and clean store environment</li>
<li>Maintain the stability and reputation of the store by complying with legal requirements</li>
<li>Maintain operations by initiating, coordinating and enforcing program, operational and personnel policies and procedures</li>
<li>Contribute to team effort by accomplishing related results as needed</li>
<li>Uphold Soccer Village's code of conduct and business model</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
<b>Skills/Qualifications</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Customer service focus</li>
<li>Tracking budget expenses</li>
<li>Pricing</li>
<li>Market knowledge</li>
<li>Staffing</li>
<li>Results driven</li>
<li>Strategic planning</li>
<li>Management proficiency</li>
<li>Client relationships</li>
<li>Verbal communication</li>
</ul>
