SS&amp;C Institutional Outsourcing, a division of SS&amp;C Technologies, Inc., is an investment processing service group that provides middle and back office operations, accounting and reporting support to Asset Management, Insurance, Pension, Wealth Management, nonbank lenders, and mortgage REIT clients. SS&amp;C is searching for a senior loan administrator with experience in the commercial loan industry.
<br><br>
<b>Job Description:</b><br>
Senior Loan Administrator’s responsibilities include performing loan servicing functions on assigned commercial and mortgage loan portfolios, as well as help manage the day to day operation tasks that are assigned to the staff members. The Individual will be responsible for assist in management of supervising completion of tasks assigned to staff, as well as performing the loan operation functions such as uploading loan onto SS&amp;C’s loan servicing software, process loan payment, generate billing statement, process escrow disbursement, generate payoff letter and handle loan inquiries from both loan borrower and client.
<br><br>
<b>Position Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Assist team manager to oversee loan operation tasks assigned to staff member are completed accurately and on time</li>
<li>Provide feedback to team manager on staff performance, as well as any outstanding issues with various client account</li>
<li>Perform daily and monthly loan servicing processes, loan balance / GL reconciliation, and reporting functions.</li>
<li>Upload newly originated / purchased loan onto SS&amp;C loan servicing software and send welcome package to borrower.</li>
<li>Generate various loan notices such as billing statement, maturity notice, late payment notice, payoff statement, etc.</li>
<li>Process loan cash transactions such as payment processing, loan payoff, escrow disbursement, investor sale, etc.</li>
<li>Monitor upcoming water / Real Estate taxes on escrowed loans, and report tax delinquencies on non-escrowed loan to client.</li>
<li>Reconcile daily / monthly GL transactions to ensure all loan transactions are processed and recorded accordingly.</li>
<li>Monitor cash account and reconcile loan payment received from borrower.</li>
<li>Produce month end servicing reports and any other ad hoc management reports as requested by client</li>
<li>Perform critical self-review of work product prior to submission to client and/or team manager.</li>
<li>Contribute to other ad hoc inquiries and assist on internal projects.</li>
<li>Liaise with clients and internal contacts on a day to day basis.</li>
</ul>
<b>Position Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of a BS degree</li>
<li>5-7 years of experience in commercial and mortgage loan administration or equivalent work experience in the loan servicing industry</li>
<li>1-3 years of experiences in supervisory role preferred</li>
<li>Self-starter and ability to work independently to meet daily / monthly deliverable</li>
<li>Ability to identify processing issue and formulate resolution</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task effectively</li>
<li>Strong ability to communicate professionally with internal / external contacts.</li>
<li>Strong analytical skills and ability to remain highly organized in a deadline driven environment while maintaining strict attention to detail</li>
<li>Proficiency with Microsoft Office suite, with advanced working knowledge of Excel</li>
</ul>
