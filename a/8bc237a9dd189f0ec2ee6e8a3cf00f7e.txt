<h2>Business Analyst, Senior</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">BUSINESS ANALYST, Senior - SALES ANALYTICS
<br>AAA…we're one of the largest Membership organizations in the country and you’ve probably heard of us or seen our stickers, but there’s a lot to us that you don’t know about:
<br>We’re big – We have a presence in 7 states, serve over 6 million Members, and generate $4 billion in sales
<br>We’re diverse – We have many products and services including roadside assistance, auto repair, insurance, travel advisory, DMV services, and home security

<br>We’re service oriented – The selling, the innovation, the size, it’s all in an effort to better serve our members, which is the underlying motive in all our decisions
<br>We care – AAA is committed to communities and have many community based programs including AAA School Safety Patrol, Car Seat Inspection, and disaster preparedness
<br><br>
Essential Functions
<br><ul>
<li>Performs financial evaluations on operations / products and develops/implements financial strategies covering a wide range of issues affecting business unit decision, profits and financial strength. Determines possible implications on various divisions/ departments and makes recommendations as needed.</li>
<li>Reviews and provides recommendations on the business unit’s operational / product / financial reports. Interprets the contents for management, alerts them to significant issues and / or opportunity trends.</li>
<li>Based on findings of identified trends and analysis conducted, participates in the</li>
</ul>
development of division’s business plan in support of corporate objectives.
<br><ul>
<li>Reviews the tracking and reporting methodologies and recommends better ways to track and trend collected data to provide management with more meaningful ways to review the data.</li>
<li>Reports on key metrics and provides recommendations to improve the division’s efficiency.</li>
</ul>
Knowledge/Skills/Abilities
<br><ul>
<li>Strong mathematical skills needed</li>
<li>Advanced analytical skills to effectively identify problems and develop solutions</li>
<li>Advanced organizational skills</li>
<li>Has strong business writing skills</li>
<li>Project management experience skills to accurately complete detail data assignments, understand and interpret broad operational / product / financial concepts and their application to the business unit and AAA</li>
<li>Proficient in the use and development of operational / product / financial concepts as they apply to business unit and AAA</li>
<li>Advanced interpersonal and inter-departmental skills</li>
<li>Advanced understanding of AAA business strategy</li>
<li>Strong understanding of the use and development of operational models and other related reporting software (preferred)</li>
</ul>
Education &amp; Experience / Licenses &amp; Certification
