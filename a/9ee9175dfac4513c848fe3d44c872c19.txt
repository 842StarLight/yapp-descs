<h2>Real Estate Buyer Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Job Description</b></p>
<p>Are you a current real estate agent looking to promote your success? Do you have the desire to join a team with growth opportunities? The Royston Team at Keller Williams Realty is currently seeking decisive and self-motivated candidates who have a competitive spirit and high-performance standards. The ideal candidate is a persuasive, outgoing, people person who seeks win-win agreements. If you have a positive attitude and a desire for continuous growth and learning, we should talk!</p>
<p><b>Who are we looking for?</b></p>
<p>This professional individual will have strong interpersonal skills, a sense of urgency and a winning attitude. The Buyer Specialist develops, prospects for, follows up with and services buyers at a high level while sharing their expert knowledge regarding mortgage financing, neighborhoods, schools, home maintenance, etc. They must coordinate a time-efficient home search process and effectively negotiate in the buyer’s best interest.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Prospect for both buyer and seller leads and convert leads to appointments.</li>
<li>Complete 10 hours of lead generation per week.</li>
<li>Manage new leads and work through existing leads.</li>
<li>Call past clients and your sphere of influence to ask for referrals.</li>
<li>Follow-up quickly on all sign calls, internet inquires and other leads.</li>
<li>Research MLS listings for homes that meet Buyer’s needs and specifications.</li>
<li>Preview homes that are in your target area. Gather and answer buyer questions about potential properties and local community information.</li>
<li>Educate buyers about the buying process.</li>
<li>Write and negotiate sales contracts.</li>
<li>Attend inspections.</li>
<li>Create a sense of comfort and familiarity through their ability to build rapport.</li>
<li>Able to diffuse difficult situations and resolve to the satisfaction of all parties.</li>
<li>Attend training offered by The Royston Team or Keller Williams to stay learning based for increased productivity.</li>
<li>Attend all bi-weekly script practice meetings.</li>
<li>Learns and internalizes scripts as well as dialogues.</li>
<li>Track all of your key business activities. Set weekly, monthly, yearly goals.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Key Benefits of Working with Us</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High net-income with minimal overhead or risk</li>
<li>KW profit sharing and residual income opportunity</li>
<li>Success Tools: Personal desk &amp; office space, office supplies, printing/copying/faxing, transaction coordinator, listing management, professional photography, supportive team atmosphere, professional staging consults, reliable vendor relationships, and team coverage.</li>
<li>Marketing: Database Management System, Business Cards, Mailings, Social Media Ads, Flyers, Promotional Items, Signs, Client Presentations, Strong Branding, Website, Client Care Systems.</li>
<li>Business Growth: Lead Opportunities, Client Events, Community Events, Open Houses, Networking Events, Individual Coaching, Team Script Practice, Training Program, Business Planning Support, High Accountability, Incentive Programs.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Washington Real Estate License (required)</li>
<li>Experience using CRM software (required)</li>
<li>2 years of real estate experience, or 24 total transactions preferred</li>
<li>You must have a "client first" mindset</li>
<li>Strong communication and negotiation skills</li>
<li>Great people skills</li>
<li>Computer savvy</li>
<li>Positive attitude</li>
<li>Willing to work hard</li>
<li>People oriented</li>
<li>Excellent organizational skills</li>
<li>An excellent problem solver</li>
<li>Motivated to help build a team</li>
<li>Valid Driver’s License and reliable car</li>
<li>Ability to spend a large amount of time driving in his/her dependable car</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Compensation: </b> Commission</p>
<p><b>About Company: </b></p>
<p>The Royston Team is a group of Real Estate Agents with Keller Williams Greater Seattle and services buyers, sellers and investors in Kitsap, Jefferson, Mason and Pierce county areas. Our intention is to grow our market share in the Olympic Peninsula each year and use our successes here to expand by using a world-class operation, marketing and lead generation hub. We sold over 100 homes last year and are on track to sell even more this year. The person we are searching for must be able to keep up with our growth and be excited about it!</p>
