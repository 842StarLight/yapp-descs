<h2>Senior Marketing Manager - Events</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The David A. Straz Center is seeking an Senior Marketing Manager - Events. Here at the Straz Center we aim to inspire, educate and enrich our entire community through the transformative power of the performing arts. We envision the Straz Center as a welcoming international cultural institution renowned for innovation, creativity and celebration of the human spirit. Come be apart of the show!</p>
<p>
Senior Marketing Manager, Events is responsible for bringing maximum awareness and ticket sales to all Straz Center events and programs through the creation, execution and management of all marketing and promotional campaigns for both internal and external clients, including most Broadway tours and other large-scale productions, as assigned.
</p>
<p><b>Primary Duties and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Create and implement marketing and promotional plans for all events assigned, including but not limited to creative input and supervision of all event-specific collateral - electronic, digital and hardcopy.</li>
<li>
Identifies target audiences and appropriate media available to gain maximum return on investment for the marketing and promotion of all Straz events, specializing in, but not limited to those in the following genres:<ul>
<li>
Broadway</li>
<li>
Broadway Encores</li>
<li>
Other large touring productions
</li>
<li>High-profile and/or high-risk events</li>
<li>
Broadway Education Projects, talk-backs, Broadway Family Nights</li>
<li>
Interactive, community, destination and festival-style events</li>
<li>
Other genres as necessary ex: dance, music, comedy, plays, concerts</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
Marketing activities include but are not limited to the management, strategy, budgeting, planning, negotiation and execution of the following:<ul>
<li>
Media planning</li>
<li>
Media buying</li>
<li>
Electronic</li>
<li>
Print</li>
<li>
Out-of-home</li>
<li>
Digital</li>
<li>
Media promotions utilizing RFP process</li>
<li>
3rd party promotions</li>
<li>
Event planning and execution</li>
<li>
Special offers</li>
<li>
Dynamic pricing initiatives</li>
<li>
Collateral creation and distribution</li>
<li>
Social media</li>
<li>
Grassroots audience development</li>
<li>
Production requests and execution collateral and electronic media</li>
<li>
Obtaining media sponsors
</li>
<li>Community partnerships and strategic alliances</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Provides effective, efficient, data-driven management of assigned events to ensure financial success:</li>
<li>
Pulls and reviews relevant ticketing reports via Tessitura, RMA, Segmentation Engine and associated programs</li>
<li>
Based on historical data and data gathered via Tessitura, RMA and Segmentation Engine, pro-actively directs pricing adjustments</li>
<li>
 Monitors ticket sales and sales goals, continuously reviewing daily sales patterns and evaluating both advertising and promotions goals and results.</li>
<li>
Daily sales recording is executed by the Marketing/Programming Assistant</li>
</ul>
<p>
Coordinates regularly with Development team</p>
<ul>
<li>
Secures company participation for donor/sponsor cultivation events</li>
<li>
Ensures all sponsorship commitments and associated benefits are clearly communicated to the company</li>
<li>
Maintains assigned budgets in a fiscally responsible manner.</li>
<li>
Creates detailed budgets and maintains up-to-date spreadsheets of all planned and processed expenditures by event or project.</li>
<li>
Tracks and records all promotional value negotiated for event, submitted a promo vs. paid report 2x per season or as requested.</li>
<li>
Codes, records and submits invoices and p-card purchases for payment in a timely fashion.</li>
<li>
Prepares and submits financial advertising settlements, on time, for all assigned events.</li>
<li>
Ensures that all event materials and communications conform to accepted standards of accuracy, clarity, impact, sponsor recognition, timeliness and style of both the show and The Straz.</li>
<li>
Participates in industry conferences and conference calls such as the Broadway League Spring Road conference and Broadway League Road Marketing calls, respectively</li>
<li>
Expects duties to include some nights and weekends.</li>
</ul>
