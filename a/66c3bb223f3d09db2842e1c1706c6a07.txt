<h2>Service Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>General Description</b><br>
</p>
<p>
The Service Manager is responsible for the performance all technical staff including support, repair and installation/project teams.</p>
<ul>
<li>Evaluate and prioritize IT support response</li>
<li>Review assignment of Service Tickets, remote and onsite support and onboarding</li>
<li>Monitor Service Board to ensure service level compliance is within company policies</li>
<li>Coordinate and receive escalation requests of unresolved or emergency support issues</li>
<li>Work with clients to ensure client satisfaction and resolve complaints</li>
<li>Oversee projects and installations</li>
<li>Monitor service personnel schedules to ensure compliance with company policies</li>
<li>Review all Service Tickets and Time Entries with Service Coordinator that did not meet company guidelines</li>
<li>Coordinate with Purchasing Coordinator and Bench Technician to schedule equipment purchase and repair</li>
<li>Recruit, interview, hire, onboard, train and evaluate support staff</li>
<li>Conduct staff meetings and training</li>
<li>Responsible for financial performance of the department</li>
<li>Perform disciplinary or termination action of service personnel if needed</li>
<li>Review sales procedures with sales staff to ensure correct service presentation in sales efforts</li>
</ul>
<p><b>WORK EXPERIENCE REQUIREMENTS / SKILLS / QUALIFICATIONS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>2 plus years in a managerial role</li>
<li>6 credit hours of accounting coursework OR 3 years P &amp; L responsibility</li>
<li>4 plus years in a professional services environment</li>
<li>Ability to pass test demonstrating superior written and verbal communications skills</li>
<li>Ability to pass vocabulary test on current business hardware, software and mobile devices</li>
<li>Ability to multitask and make decisions in complex, dynamic work environment</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES</b></p>
<p>
Evaluating support and project status 40%</p>
<p>Assisting support staff and coordinating support escalation 30%</p>
<p>Customer Service 20%</p>
<p>Internal meetings, communication and training 10%</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>MEASUREMENTS OF ACCOUNTABILITY</b></p>
<ul>
<li>100% service requests properly processed and assigned</li>
<li>95% compliance with company SLA procedures</li>
<li>75% support delivered within company SLA guidelines</li>
<li>95% customer satisfaction</li>
<li>100% of staff evaluations performed on time unless extenuating circumstances</li>
<li>Meets 90% of GP % goal for support</li>
<li>Meets 90% of project GP % goal</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>SALARY RANGE:</b></p>
<p>
$65,000 - $85,000 plus benefits and professional development opportunities</p>
