<h2>Bilingual Leasing agent</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Well-established property has an immediate opening for a Leasing Consultant in the Lawrence Township area. This is a terrific opportunity to work with a company that is growing and has great benefits.</p>
<p><b>Duties and Responsibilities: </b></p>
<p>Rents apartment homes to prospective residents.</p>
<p>Answers phone and email inquiries from prospective residents; uses standard sales techniques to encourage a visit to the community.</p>
<p>Meets with prospective residents and checks the availability of units that best fit their desires and budget.</p>
<p>Shows apartments and the overall community to prospective residents; explains other amenities of living at the community; uses standard sales closing methods to sell the prospective resident on the property management company.</p>
<p>Takes all needed information from prospective residents after they have indicated a desire to move to the property; completes all paperwork and computer entry associated with leasing activities.</p>
<p>Contacts prospective resident of approval/disapproval; assists them in completing the lease and receives initial rent and deposit payments; assists new resident with the move in process. Prepares and maintains a file for each resident.</p>
<p>Contact residents with expiring leases to discuss renewals and encourages the renewal of the lease.</p>
<p>Assists others in marketing efforts in the community to promote the property positively and to help bring in prospective residents. This includes implanting and carrying out marketing efforts such as outside marketing calls.</p>
<p>Answers outside phone calls and greets visitors.</p>
<p>Performs other miscellaneous duties as assigned.</p>
<p><b>Required Qualifications: </b></p>
<p>Must be willing to work some weekends.</p>
<p>High School education and one year of working experience position that primarily requires customer service. Basic personal computer skills.</p>
<p><b>Preferred Qualifications: Bilingual.</b>Experience in leasing or other sales positions.</p>
<p><b>Skills, Knowledge, and Abilities: </b>Above average ability to communicate with clients and with co-workers both verbally and in writing. Basic personal computer skills and ability to learn specialized software use. Skill to convince prospective residents of the advantages of living at a community by sales closing methods. Knowledge of the work of other associates at a property. Ability to organize work. Ability to work a varied schedule, including weekends and some holidays as required. Ability to learn and comply with industry legal requirements, especially related to federal and local Fair Housing Laws. Ability to travel off-site for training courses and outside marketing.</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
