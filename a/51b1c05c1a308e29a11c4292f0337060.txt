<h2>Mgr Supply Chain - Military Procurement</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Summary Statement:</b><br>
Manages the supply chain related functions of procurement and material management, (traffic, planning, scheduling, warehouse and inventory management). Provides leadership to institutionalize the processes of Continuous Improvement in accordance with organization goals and objectives.<br>
<br>
<b>Essential Functions and Key Responsibilities:</b><br>
Performs typical responsibilities of management requiring planning, evaluating, organizing, integrating, and coaching. Accomplishes results through subordinate supervisors or through experienced exempt and nonexempt staff. Work is reviewed for adequacy in meeting cost, schedule and quality objectives.<br>
<br>
Procurement<br>
Responsible for overall management of suppliers. Develops standardized procurement processes to improve efficiency and reduce costs. Negotiates procurement agreements with suppliers and monitors compliance of organization to policy and contracts. Works with TNG commodity management and strategic sourcing to support overall commodity strategy; supports the business unit and supplier reduction activities.<br>
<br>
Screens and commercially qualifies new suppliers. Solves complex procurement and supplier relationship issues. Oversees the procurement of materials in the right amount and at the right time to meet the requirements of the Master Production Schedule.<br>
<br>
Materials<br>
Manages and coordinates activities engaged in planning and scheduling the manufacturing operation. Ensures the flow of materials, parts and assemblies throughout the operation is available, organized and efficient. Determines recommended quantities and delivery schedules of inventory via Material Requirements Planning (MRP) and other forecasting techniques. Forecasts inventory support requirements by analyzing marketing forecasts, past usage and shop capacity. Provides leadership for the SIOP process and ensures alignment with sales and manufacturing. Understands inventory management as a key element for the business to meet its required annual goals.<br>
<br>
Monitors manufacturing schedules, including material planning, procurement and manufacturing schedules. Ensures adherence to production and shipping schedules by taking necessary action to eliminate delays. Creates and implements annual plans to maximize inventory turns and return on inventory assets.<br>
