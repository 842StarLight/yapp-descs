<h2>Director of Channel Programs</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>About the Position
</b></p>
<p>The Director of Global Channel Marketing is responsible for defining and driving program initiatives with current and prospective WatchGuard partners. This individual is the channel programs leader developing plans and executing on a wide range of marketing and program initiatives to drive channel success. The responsibilities include oversight of all corporate partner marketing functions, including global partner recruitment, productivity, and retention activities, in addition to ownership of the WatchGuardONE Channel Partner Program.</p>
<p>
This role requires someone who has an in-depth understanding of the IT channel and cyber security, including how varying IT solution providers leverage products services as managed service providers. You should enjoy working in a fast-paced environment and have demonstrated a diverse skillset, creativity, and strength with large-scale channel marketing initiatives. Above all, WatchGuard is looking for someone who is comfortable working cross-functionally to make ideas and platforms come to life, has a proven track record of meeting deadlines and exceeding expectations, brings a high level of energy, and has a can-do attitude no matter the task at hand.</p>
<p><b>
Position Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>
You are the face of WatchGuard’s channel programs and will work closely with counterpart marketing departments, Sales, IT, Product Management, and Executive Leadership to lead key company initiatives.</p>
<ul>
<li>Spearhead the constant evolution, upkeep and messaging of the WatchGuard channel partner program.</li>
<li>Create specialization tracks within the partner program consistent with the current go-to-market model and promote these tracks to new or existing channel partners. Have developed expertise to expand channel programs as needed to include system integrators, managed service providers, technical partners and ecommerce options.
</li>
<li>Assist in delivering partner learning modules for upcoming product launches or releases, and promote certification to partners and their customers.</li>
<li>Research and select appropriate vendors to ensures successful and timely project completion.</li>
<li>Develop and implement “channel onboarding” programs.</li>
<li>Effectively use data to recognize trends and anticipate challenges in the partner ecosystem.</li>
<li>Collaborate with Sales channel partners to develop strategic short and long-term marketing strategies and plans that address current and future revenue objectives. Work with Regional Sales leaders to understand local dynamics/include their inputs, plus execute the global channel strategy with/through the regions.</li>
<li>Lead the development, launch, and maintenance of all partner tools and platforms, including the delivery of sales enablement, business visibility, marketing development, and information dissemination. Lead channel marketing strategy, initiatives and program execution across all product and solutions lines/segments</li>
<li>Develop innovative go-to-market programs for channel that drive pipeline and revenue including sales and product promotions.</li>
<li>Manage WatchGuard’s partner communications schedule that spans monthly trainings and webinars, weekly email newsletters, and daily notifications within the WatchGuard Partner Portal and Support Center.</li>
<li>Oversee the planning and execution of WatchGuard’s annual partner conferences with major events across the Americas, Europe, and Asia Pacific.</li>
<li>Head partner recruitment marketing initiatives, including program and product positioning, content development, and delivery.</li>
<li>Effectively manage the WatchGuard Partner Advisory Council, consisting of partners from around the world who represent WatchGuard’s channel community. Constantly source feedback from members and relay it to internal stakeholders.</li>
<li>Develop and manage the WatchGuard Technology Alliance Program, which supports key product integrations with joint go-to-market activities.</li>
<li>A strong collaborator and partner with the product development team, sales teams, operations team, and other stakeholder teams, to drive and build the Company brand, sales profitability, and customer excellence.</li>
<li>Represent the business to the senior leadership teams across channel, sales and offering management from the product segment perspective.</li>
<li>Provide strategy, analysis, support, ideas and guidance to regional geography channel leadership teams.</li>
<li>Identify metrics and monitors ongoing program performance, making adjustments as necessary.</li>
<li>Provides periodic updates on program performance to executive staff, including analysis of performance and recommendations of changes as necessary.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Skills and Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Must have strong attention to detail, and the ability to deliver in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>Global Channel management expertise within a high tech, 2 tier distribution company</li>
<li>Ability to grow and scale upward with the company (500 million and beyond); second line management experience a plus</li>
<li>Strong executive presence and polish with the ability to negotiate at the executive level</li>
<li>Ability to build business cases and meaningful relationships with the right partners and internal customers</li>
<li>Strong champion building experience</li>
<li>Ability to coach and develop peers and superiors alike</li>
<li>Exceptional management, interpersonal, written and presentation skills</li>
<li>Thrives in a fast-paced, high growth, rapidly changing environment</li>
<li>Able to work independently and remotely from other members of your team and corporate</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree, or equivalent combined education and experiences</li>
<li>10+ years of experience in Channel Marketing</li>
<li>Two Tier distribution experience</li>
<li>4+ years of managing employees</li>
