<h2>Senior Client Executive (Sales)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>A technology services company is seeking a proven, quota-carrying sales performer. This individual will be responsible for generating new business within the New England region, focused primarily on the Greater Boston area along with Rhode Island, Southern New Hampshire, and Northern Connecticut. This company offers a strategic, business value driven sale that requires a strong understanding of the operational and financial side of IT, and demonstrated competency in solution-selling.</p>
<p>The Sr. Client Executive is primarily responsible for leading a coordinated sales approach with the organization's internal consulting and engineering team. The mission of this group is to broaden the company's reach in New England and to strengthen their foothold across the entire market segment by driving our solutions into existing and new accounts.</p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Description</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for developing a sales pipeline and directly selling the company's solution portfolio within the specified region.</li>
<li>Responsible for building and managing long-term, profitable relationships with client organizations across multiple internal contacts.</li>
<li>Responsible for managing opportunities through the entire sales process.</li>
<li>Exceed quarterly sales goals.</li>
<li>Match the appropriate solution to the customer’s business needs, challenges, and technical requirements.</li>
<li>Develop and manage a partner strategy for their respective region.</li>
<li>Represent the company well in presenting and articulating value proposition to all levels of IT including executive level.</li>
<li>Accurately forecast bookings on a regular basis. Manage pipeline in Salesforce CRM.</li>
<li>Have a thorough understanding of your client’s business and market. Be able to identify problems and opportunities in advance.</li>
<li>Exercise overall quality control for your accounts. Demonstrate a commitment to excellence in everything you do.</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Experience</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>7+ years of direct sales experience in successful enterprise software, solution provider or consulting companies.</li>
<li>Proven track record of exceeding goals in a competitive environment.</li>
<li>Experience managing complex technology sales process and in leading a team of professionals in sales cycles (SE’s, Marketing, Inside Sales, Project Managers, Services, etc).</li>
<li>Experience selling hardware, software, services and cloud-based based solutions.</li>
<li>Excellent written, verbal, and presentation skills.</li>
<li>Self-motivated with proven ability to work in a team environment.</li>
<li>Experience with Salesforce CRM preferred.</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Education/Certifications</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>Ongoing vendor certifications of major IT manufacturers including VMware, Dell, NetApp, EMC, etc., are preferred</li>
<li>Company is an Equal Opportunity Employer.</li>
</ul>
