<h2>Vice President, Global Marketing</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Akcea Therapeutics is a biopharmaceutical company focused on developing and commercializing drugs to treat patients with serious rare diseases. Our priority is to bring important new therapeutics to patients in need. In order to do so, we must drive clinical program execution, understand patient and physician needs and work with regulators to gain global approval and market access to these new therapeutics. Akcea has a portfolio of development-stage drugs covering multiple targets and disease states using advanced antisense therapeutics.</p>
<p>
 Akcea was established by Ionis Pharmaceuticals in late 2014 and was listed on the NASDAQ stock exchange in July 2017 under the symbol “AKCA.” Akcea is a global company headquartered in Boston, MA with offices in Carlsbad, CA and across Europe and Canada.</p>
<p><b>
Vice President, Global Marketing</b></p>
<p>
The Vice President, Global Marketing is responsible for establishing a vision and leading cross-functional and global teams in the development and implementation of potentially multiple products through world-class launches. This is an opportunity to work at the forefront of innovative science, defining and executing innovative and patient-centric solutions for both commercial-stage and pipeline programs.</p>
<p><b><i>
This position could be based in either our Carlsbad, CA or Boston, MA offices. Expected travel 50% (domestic and global).
</i></b></p>
<p><i>Responsibilities:</i></p>
<ul>
<li>
Lead and develop team of marketers responsible for commercial leadership of products ranging from in-line and commercially available across multiple countries to investigational programs at various stages of clinical development and regulatory review</li>
<li>
Ensure strategic, seamless development and execution of disease and brand strategies and tactics on a global basis</li>
<li>
Establish a best-in-class approach to global marketing through ongoing partnership with all geographies and scalable, innovative approaches to tactics and programs that achieve global brand priorities and improve country performance</li>
<li>
Oversee and ensure flawless execution of US marketing, including partnership with all aspects of US commercialization, including field sales, market access, patient support and medical affairs</li>
<li>
Ensure integration of internal and external data sources to continually improve marketing effectiveness</li>
<li>
Design and execute programming to optimize the complete customer experience for both prescribers and patients</li>
<li>
Determine brand and market KPIs and implement and manage trackers and monitoring practices to measure and continually improve effectiveness</li>
<li>
Partner with cross-functional colleagues in development and execution of strategy to engage medical thought leadership, partner societies and patient advocacy groups</li>
<li>
Lead the development and execution of Brand Planning and budget and supports the delivery and implementation of the core business planning activities</li>
<li>
Ensure timely and efficient review of promotional materials and complies fully with all legal, regulatory, ethical and company guidelines</li>
<li>
Manage external agencies as collaborative, strategic partners to ensure excellent execution of marketing programs aligned with brand strategic imperatives</li>
<li>
Other projects or activities as assigned</li>
</ul>
