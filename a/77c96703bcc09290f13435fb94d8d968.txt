<h2>CRIMINAL RESEARCH SPECIALIST</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>The Criminal Research Specialist processes routine employee background screening reports according to client guidelines and monitors reports to ensure execution of client requirements, including compliance, reporting guidelines and accuracy of information.</b></h4>
<div>
<p>Specific Duties:</p>
<ul>
<li>Ensure accurate and prompt turnaround on all adjudication results</li>
<li>Provide and maintain in-depth working knowledge of all adjudication and client guidelines</li>
<li>May input thorough and accurate notes when determining the adjudication result on an applicant</li>
<li>Provide timely follow-up and follow-through on all applicable client questions and/or concerns stemming from an adjudication result</li>
<li>Meet personal and team qualitative and quantitive targets</li>
<li>Follow company procedures, guidelines and policies with full understanding</li>
<li>Tracking, routing and redirecting information to the correct resources</li>
<li>Criminal research, understanding dispositions verbiage and reporting guidelines</li>
<li>Understanding employment background screening compliance</li>
</ul>
