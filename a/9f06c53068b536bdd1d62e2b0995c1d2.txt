<div><b>Business Development Representative</b></div>
<br>
<b>About the Job</b><br>
In this newly created position, the Business Development Representative will be a key member of Knowland’s Sales team, responsible for collaborating closely with Sales and Marketing to generate and develop new leads and ultimately new customers. As a candidate, you are enthusiastic, resilient and creative, and you are truly driven by the ‘hunt’ of Sales. Successful candidates will not be afraid of a new challenge, will be proactive in their approach to learning and improving, and will collaborate well across departments. If you’re up for the challenge of helping to establish a new role in a growing company, we’re excited to hear from you.
<div>
<br>
<i>Key Responsibilities:</i>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Proactively seek new opportunities within an assigned territory of potential customers</li>
<li>Contact potential customers through prospecting phone calls and email communication</li>
<li>Understand prospective customers’ needs and pain points, and effectively present our company to them; accurately communicate information learned to Sales Managers to hand over opportunities</li>
<li>Arrange and confirm meetings and Webinar demonstrations between Sales Managers and prospective customers</li>
<li>Use systemic sales methodologies and sales scripts to support progression of deals through the early stages of the sales cycle</li>
<li>Use Salesforce.com to manage database of prospects and customers</li>
<li>Report to Sales Management on activities, results and outcomes</li>
<li>Learn and stay up-to-date on Knowland’s products, value proposition and positioning</li>
</ul>
<div><i>Minimum Qualifications:</i></div>
<ul>
<li>At least one year of work experience, ideally in a Business Development or Inside Sales role</li>
<li>Previous experience qualifying leads from marketing campaigns</li>
<li>Proven track record of achieving sales quotas</li>
<li>Experience with CRM software (Salesforce.com experience preferred)</li>
<li>Understanding of Sales Performance Metrics</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills, particularly in an inside sales environment</li>
<li>Strong contract negotiation and closing skills</li>
<li>Proficiency with Microsoft Office, particularly Excel</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree strongly preferred</li>
<li>Experience in SaaS software or the hotel industry preferred</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>About Us</b><br>
We believe that there is a better way to sell group business. Our suite of hospitality solutions harnesses the power of data and analytics to help our customers <i>know</i> their buyers and win in the group and meetings marketplace. With the industry's largest historical database of actualized events, we deliver insights that win more share, optimize revenue strategies, and accelerate sales cycles. That’s the Knowland Advantage. See why thousands of customers trust Knowland to sell smarter and maximize their revenue. Knowland operates globally and is headquartered in Rosslyn, VA.<br>
