<p><i>We're looking for a Human Resources Generalist that is ready for the next challenge in their career. Join our HR team at our shared services office in Southfield, Michigan where you will be supporting our Multi-Family regions with employee and business needs. This position will report to the HR Director and you will be given all of the tools and resources necessary to become successful in this role. We look forward to your application!</i></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Administers various human resource plans and procedures for all organization personnel; assists in the development and implementation of personnel policies and procedures; assists with preparing and maintaining the employee handbook and the policies and procedures manual.</li>
<li>Participates in developing department goals, objectives and systems.</li>
<li>Administers the compensation program; monitors the performance evaluation program and assists with revising as necessary.</li>
<li>Performs benefits administration, such as communicating benefits information to employees, open enrollment activities, new hire enrollment activities, and other activities identified by senior HR management.</li>
<li>May assist with compliance activities and reporting, such as EEO-1 reports / records, ACA reports / records, employment posters, etc.</li>
<li>Conducts recruitment effort for exempt and nonexempt personnel, which includes managing recruitment and retention practices, use of creative and social media sourcing, drafting ads, conducting phone interviews, candidate management, pre-employment screening and assessments, conducting new-employee orientations, and monitoring career-pathing programs.</li>
<li>Handles employee relations counseling, outplacement counseling and exit interviewing.</li>
<li>Participates in administrative staff meetings and attends other meetings and seminars.</li>
<li>Maintains HRIS system with accurate employee information, compiles necessary reports, company organization charts and the employee directories / company intranet information pages.</li>
<li>Assists in evaluation of reports, decisions and results of department in relation to established goals. Recommends new approaches, policies and procedures to continually improve efficiency of the department and services performed.</li>
<li>Maintains compliance with federal, state and local employment and benefits laws and regulations.</li>
<li>Other duties, as assigned.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Desired Skills and Experience:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Human Resources or related field
</li>
<li>Minimum 2 years’ experience in Human Resources, with 1-year experience in employee relations
</li>
<li>Must have a valid driver’s license</li>
<li>Proficient in communicating in English and Spanish, preferred</li>
<li>SHRM-CP or PHR designation, preferred</li>
<li>Knowledge of the principles and practices of HR concepts</li>
<li>Strong analytical, organizational and time management skills
</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills
</li>
<li>Team oriented personality with commitment to excellent customer service
</li>
<li>Ability to develop effective relationships with managers</li>
<li>Ability to work independently, think proactive and resolve problems
</li>
<li>Proficient with Microsoft Office Applications, especially Word and Excel
</li>
<li>Knowledge and prior use of Paycom software(s) or similar HRIS systems required
</li>
<li>Experience within a multi-state environment and 300+ staff is a plus</li>
<br>
</ul>
