<div><b>Job Description</b></div>
<div>Looking for: A problem solver. A creative thinker. A brilliant force.
</div>
<div>This experienced and creative design professional will develop strategy-driven visual content for sales proposals, presentations, and related business development documents for the Risk and Financial Advisory function’s most important clients. This individual is accountable for creative, brand-compliant, and timely design, formatting, production, and delivery of professional documents. The graphic designer is responsible for managing his/her projects independently, as well as working effectively with a team and communicating with others. This position requires proficiency in multiple software packages and the ability to produce large volumes of work consistently and accurately. Reports to the Risk and Financial Advisory graphic design leader.</div>
<br>
