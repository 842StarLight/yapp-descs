<div><b>Imagine new horizons….</b></div>
<div>
The Solutions Consultant on the Creative Client Services team is responsible for generating creative and innovative visualization solutions and content to prepare the 3DEXCITE Sales team for success. Each of our clients need 3D visualization, but in many unique ways. The Solutions Consultant ensures that each customer is approached with best solution to meet their requirements, developing unique and strategic experiences for our clients, as well as educating the customer in new technology and available products that they may not have known existed.</div>
