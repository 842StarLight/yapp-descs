<p><b>
Enterprise Finance </b>drives financial management for the company and maintains and enhances risk and financial controls. Key functions within Enterprise Finance include finance and accounting; Treasury; corporate development, mergers, and acquisitions; Data Management and Insights, the Customer Remediation Center of Excellence, Enterprise Shared Services, Business Process Management, and Corporate Strategy. Enterprise Finance informs shareholders, regulators, taxing authorities, team members, and leaders of the company’s financial performance through earnings releases, investor meetings and conferences, and meetings with regulators and credit rating agencies, following appropriate reporting guidelines. They also maintain and enhance risk and financial controls and lead many of the company’s shared services functions including corporate properties, security, and global services.</p>
<p>
Wells Fargo Strategic Sourcing is currently seeking a Consulting Vendor Manager who will be responsible for developing the scorecarding process/methodology and identifing the key performance indicators for approved/preferred consulting suppliers. They will also execute quarterly scorecards and business reviews as wells as any required corrective action plans in coordination with Supply Chain Management leadership and TRIMs relationship managers. Additionally they will establish and execute a method to assess how consulting firms perform on an engagement level and incorporate these results into the quarterly scorecarding process and manage the process for updating this list of approved/preferred consulting suppliers. This process includes removing underperforming suppliers from the program, adding new suppliers with an emphasis on diversity and niche consulting suppliers, and training the sourcing team on the core competencies of the suppliers.</p>
<p>
Additional responsibilities include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Lead the establishment of approving consulting contracts for non-preferred suppliers with the goal to eliminate non-preferred spend.</li>
<li>Establish and distribute regular reporting to measure progress on the elimination of non-preferred consulting spend, including exception levels across the enterprise.</li>
<li>With the guidance from team manager and Professional Services COE, provide day-to-day direction to the 3 other team members focused on this activity.</li>
</ul>
