<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Details
</b></h2>
<p>Salesforce is looking for an amazing Senior Art Director to join our Creative team, full-time.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The Salesforce Creative team is part of our Global Brand and Cross-Product Marketing organization. We’re experienced storytellers and thinkers, writers and designers, ad folk and content creators. We combine high-level creativity, craftsmanship, strategic smarts, and business IQ to bring engaging concepts to life. We aim to create remarkable work that inspires customers, attracts prospects, and delivers results.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The Senior Art Director will drive creative for all advertising, demand gen, and event initiatives for Sales Cloud and related solutions. Working closely with the Creative Director, you’ll create inspiring work that meets the strategic marketing and business objectives for the world’s #1 CRM for sales. In the process, you’ll set the team standard for craft and excellence.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>You’ll need strong organization skills. You’ll manage multiple projects and aggressive deadlines in a fast-paced environment. You’ll build and maintain strong relationships with partners in other organizations. You’ll often present and sell your work to executive audiences, providing a clear point of view and articulating the strategy behind the work.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>You’ll also lead and mentor a team of 3-5 art directors, designers and copywriters, taking a hands-on approach to their work. And you’ll lead external vendors to provide clear creative direction and timely and helpful feedback to propel the creative process forward.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>JOB SKILLS:
</p>
<ul>
<li>8+ years of advertising or design agency experience
</li>
<li>Strong command of integrated marketing/advertising campaigns, including traditional advertising (print, OOH, online, video) and digital (web, UI, interactive).
</li>
<li>A compelling portfolio of conceptual, results-driven, original work
</li>
<li>Expert knowledge of Adobe Creative Suite, especially Photoshop and InDesign
</li>
<li>Familiarity with Google Docs, Google Slides, and Keynote
</li>
<li>Presentation and written communication
</li>
<li>Relationship-building
</li>
<li>Organization and time management
</li>
<li>Strategic POV
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>About Salesforce: Salesforce, the world's #1 CRM, makes technology that brings companies and customers together. The company was founded on three disruptive ideas: a new technology model in cloud computing, a pay-as- you-go business model, and a new integrated corporate philanthropy model. These founding principles have taken our company to great heights, including being named one of Forbes’s “World’s Most Innovative Company” six years in a row and one of Fortune’s “100 Best Companies to Work For” nine years in a row. We’re the fastest growing of the top 10 enterprise software companies, and this level of growth equals incredible opportunities to grow a career at Salesforce. Together, with our whole Ohana (Hawaiian for family) made up of our employees, customers, partners and communities, we are working to improve the state of the world.
</p>
