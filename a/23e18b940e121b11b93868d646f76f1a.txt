<h2>Inside Sales Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Cyber Security is a big deal. It’s in the news, growing rapidly, a critical tool for every company, and our specialty.<br>
</div>
<div>
Imperva is a cybersecurity leader that delivers best-in-class solutions to protect data and applications on-premises, in the cloud, and across hybrid environments.<br>
</div>
<div>
Our customers include leading enterprises, government organizations, small businesses, and service providers.<br>
</div>
<div>
<b>
The role: Inside Sales Executive, location, Redwood Shores HQ, California</b><br>
Reporting into the Regional Inside Sales Director, The <b>Inside Sales Executive</b> is directly responsible for Imperva’s Net New Logo Sales in a designated territory, utilizing advanced marketing automation tools.<br>
</div>
<div>
You should be experienced in methodically screening the market for new sales opportunities, have a deep understanding of emerging industry trends and leverage your expertise to broaden your network of industry contacts through customer referrals, use of social media, appearances at conferences, etc.<br>
</div>
<div>
This is a classic “hunter” role, and we are looking for someone resilient, self-driven, and who enjoys and prefers a high risk, high reward comp model.<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Target Account planning</li>
<li>
Develop an account-specific value proposition</li>
<li>
Demonstrated success navigating complex sales cycle</li>
<li>
Knowledge of navigating business organization structures, buying influences and purchasing processes</li>
<li>
Achieve monthly, quarterly, yearly revenue goals, and defined objectives. Provides weekly forecast metrics and updates to direct manager</li>
<li>
Contact customer on an ongoing basis Liaise with internal stakeholders, e.g. Technical Services, Customer Success, Senior Management, when appropriate</li>
<li>
Make expansion/cross-sell proposal based on account plan – in collaboration with, ISR’s Channels/VARs, Marketing, Order Entry, ProServ, Legal, etc.</li>
<li>
Collect and capture customer and competitive insights during a negotiation</li>
<li>
Negotiate and close the deal/ensure renewal</li>
<li>
Work closely with the Regional Teams to plan and execute high impact demand generation activities such but not limited to email campaigns, PPC, targeted banners, 1st response notifications, etc</li>
<li>
Serve as the 1st point of contact to inbound inquiries</li>
<li>
Maintain accurate customer relationship and knowledge base in management systems</li>
<li>
Ability to create, forecast and close deals - proficient in tracking/forecasting deals in SFDC</li>
<li>
Provide product demos remotely</li>
<li>
Develop &amp; build pipeline via Up / X-sell activities within installed accounts</li>
<li>
Experience selling enterprise software licenses / SaaS / On-premise &amp; virtual products</li>
<li>
Experience in a selling methodology e.g. (Challenger, SPIN, Solution Selling, etc.)</li>
<li>
Experience participating in customer discovery/technical calls that expedite the sales cycle</li>
<li>
Effectively understand &amp; successful usage of a sales quote tool (Apptus, etc.)</li>
<li>
Might request occasional travel and out-of-hours activities</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Qualifications:</b></div>
<ul><li>
Minimum 4-5 years of sales experience</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree preferred</li>
<li>
Must have excellent written communication skills</li>
<li>
Must have excellent verbal and interpersonal communication skills and present a professional image in person and over the phone</li>
<li>
Must maintain a positive attitude, be a self-starter, dependable and take pride in work product</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to take the initiative to get things done and work collaboratively with others; strong team player</li>
<li>
Efficient, organized, and have the ability to set priorities and meet deadlines; able to work independently</li>
<li>
Strong organizational and time management skills; ability to work with a high sense of urgency within established timelines, exercising consistent follow-through/follow-up when necessary</li>
<li>
Trusted to handle details of a highly confidential and critical nature and use excellent judgment at all times.</li>
<li>
A meticulous eye for detail, accuracy, high standards of presentation and the ability to anticipate, react and thrive in an ambiguous environment</li>
</ul>
