<p>Maintenance Technician</p>
<p>
Hydro is a fully integrated aluminum company with 35,000 employees in 40 countries on all continents, combining local expertise, worldwide reach and unmatched capabilities in R&amp;D. In addition to production of primary aluminum, rolled and extruded products and recycling, Hydro also extracts bauxite, refines alumina and generates energy to be the only 360° company of the global aluminum industry. Hydro is present within all market segments for aluminum, with sales and trading activities throughout the value chain serving more than 30,000 customers.</p>
<div>
The Maintenance Technician will report directly to the Maintenance Supervisor of Hydro Connersville, IN.<br>
<br>
Job Responsibilities:<br>
<ul>
<li>Inspects operating machines and equipment for conformance with safety/operational standards.</li>
<li>Works on projects as assigned by the Maintenance Manager, Maintenance Supervisor and or Cell Leader.</li>
<li>Performs preventative maintenance as required.</li>
<li>Maintains building, grounds, and utility systems.</li>
<li>Aids in the training of new maintenance employees.</li>
<li>Other duties which may be assigned from time to time.</li>
<li>Provide a safe working environment for all in the department and enable personal growth.</li>
</ul>
<br>
Key Requirements:<br>
Understanding and experience with<br>
<ul>
<li>Hydraulics</li>
<li>Electrical</li>
<li>Combustion systems</li>
<li>Thermocouples</li>
<li>Mechanical</li>
<li>Welding</li>
<li>Microsoft Word and Excel</li>
<li>Two years of Technical school including electrical, PLC’s, hydraulics &amp; pneumatics preferred but not required.</li>
<li>Two years of maintenance experience, preferably in an industrial setting. Emphasis on PLC’s, hydraulics and mechanical</li>
<li>High level PLC experience, preferably Allen Bradley</li>
<li>SLC 500's systems, RSLogix 500 software</li>
<li>PLC 5's systems, RSLogix 5 software</li>
<li>ControlLogix Systems, RSLogix 5000 software</li>
<li>Panelview operator terminals, Panelbuilder software</li>
<li>Extrusion back ground preferred but not required</li>
<li>Ability to communicate with all levels of employees.</li>
<li>Any combination of education, experience, and training which provides the above will be considered.</li>
</ul>
