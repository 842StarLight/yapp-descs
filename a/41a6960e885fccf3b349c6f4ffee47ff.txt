<br>Job Description
<br>The Client Services Group and this position are an essential part of retaining Blume Global's client base. As a Client Services Specialist, you provide front line support to Blume Global clients and users. This involves resolving customer inquiries accurately and within a timely manner as well as supporting bi-monthly billing by rectifying any unsolved disputes, charges, etc. for clients.
<br>You will be successful if you enjoy and want to be part of …
<br><ul>
<li>A high performing company and team, with a focus on quality, accuracy, and responsiveness</li>
<li>Solving problems and working with customers</li>
<li>Helping our customers move over 3 million shipments a year on the railroad</li>
<li>Working in a team environment with the opportunity for team projects and collaboration</li>
<li>Having a voice and participating</li>
<li>Managing a variety of support tasks and engaging with the users and customers</li>
</ul>
Responsibilities
<br><ul>
<li>Front line support for all Blume Global products and services – through online ticket help system, email, phone, or face-to-face</li>
<li>Research and resolve customer billing disputes</li>
<li>Draft and send announcements tailored to Blume Global's brand and values regarding updates to the client base</li>
<li>Ensure your interactions and resolutions align with company SOP's (standard operating procedures)</li>
<li>System configuration and Analysis – update the way the system is configured to meet individual customer needs or analyze and provide recommendations to customers</li>
<li>Policy advocacy – enforce client program policies</li>
<li>Knowledge management – update the internal and external user knowledge base</li>
<li>Help the product team with product design, and perform beta testing</li>
<li>Training - Onboard new customers and provide training, Blume Global system instruction, consulting and coaching</li>
<li>Act as a consultant to our clients and users to teach best practices and make sure they can take full advantage of everything we have to offer</li>
<li>Support implementation efforts around product or service changes - Operate collaboratively to solve internal and external challenges</li>
<li>Act as a data and information detective; digging into and researching client and customer challenges - Identify support trends, recommend and spearhead process and operational improvements - Identify and raise potential sales and growth opportunities for the sales team</li>
</ul>
