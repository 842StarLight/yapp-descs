<h2>Business Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>Haymarket Media, Inc. is seeking a <b>Business Analyst</b> to join the <b>Medical Communications</b> team. This position is located in our <b>New York, NY</b> office.<br>
<br>
<b>Job Overview:</b><br>
This is an exciting opportunity to join Haymarket’s growing Medical Communications Group. With an aggressive growth plan that builds on thirty years of healthcare market leadership, and 18 award winning media brands, this role will be a key contributor to Haymarket’s ongoing success. The Business Analyst will work closely with the business leadership and finance teams to achieve our growth objectives.<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<b>Sales Forecasting/Reporting</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Monitor Salesforce.com reports throughout the month and work with the sales and operations teams to ensure accurate sales data</li>
<li>Collaborate with business leadership, sales and finance to create monthly forecasting reports that identify forward sold revenue and sales pipeline</li>
<li>Produce interim reports to identify potential revenue at risk and campaign changes affecting revenue</li>
</ul>
<b>
Revenue Recognition</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for the accuracy of revenue recognition and reporting across Haymarket Medical Communications Group for print and digital media, event sponsorships, direct mail and custom projects</li>
<li>Reconcile delivery reports from both Haymarket’s ad server and third-party reports with the billing system and client contracts</li>
<li>Ensure that all contracted and delivered non-ad server revenue is captured in Salesforce.com (i.e. Email, direct mail and engagement based programs)</li>
<li>Review monthly print advertising orders with production and reconcile discrepancies</li>
<li>Collaborate with finance team on monthly close process for the business – pre-close statements and monthly reconciliation of finance system postings</li>
<li>Coordinate the collection and integration of custom projects revenue</li>
</ul>
<b>
Billing &amp; Invoicing</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Work with accounting to confirm recognized revenue to be invoiced/billed, accrued and reconcile receivables</li>
<li>Resolve customer discrepancies and questions regarding invoices and billing</li>
</ul>
<b>
Expense Reconciliation</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Coordinate with production, circulation, billing, editorial, and sales to ensure all expenses are recognized</li>
<li>Create purchase orders for accounts payable as needed</li>
</ul>
<b>
Forecasting, Trend analysis and Optimization</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Produce analyses and reports for management, as needed; including sales, yield, customer churn, revenue mix, etc.</li>
<li>Collaborate with management to implement new systems and processes to improve order management, forecasting, reporting, and reconciliation</li>
<li>Work with business and finance leadership to prepare monthly forecasts and financials for monthly financial review meeting.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
Skills and Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree required; business, accounting, math, or finance preferred</li>
<li>1-3 years professional experience working with data analysis, budgets, and/or financial management highly desired.</li>
<li>Excellent analytic and communication skills required</li>
<li>Significant experience with Excel is a must and Salesforce.com experience a plus</li>
<li>Highly organized, focused, and a very strong attention to detail</li>
<li>Experience with digital media or publishing industries is preferred</li>
<li>Must be able to multitask in a fast paced environment</li>
<li>A strong ability to perform independently and proactively while working in a team environment</li>
<li>Must be willing to work in New York City</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
About Medical Communications:</b><br>
Haymarket Media is a channel agnostic media company with some of the leading brands in select markets. This exciting opportunity is in our Medical Communications Group, which provides healthcare professionals practical clinical information to improve patient outcomes. We have 18 top ranked brands across a variety of medical professions and specialties, including the leading drug reference, MPR, and our award winning Advisor brands for specialty healthcare professionals. Through digital resources, print publications and live educational events we deliver valuable information that improves patient care.<br>
<br>
<b>About Haymarket:</b><br>
Haymarket has its heart and soul in publishing and media. Since the company was founded half a century ago, Haymarket has always prided itself on being a highly creative business, with an unrelenting focus on the quality of the products and the people. The philosophy has always been quite simple: only by having the highest quality individuals can you produce the highest quality products, combining the best in content, design, production and customer services. Haymarket has offices around the world and many of the titles are truly global brands. Haymarket serves a broad spread of business markets, from marketing to medicine to technology along with exhibitions and live events. Predicting the shape of the business in 3, 5, or 10 years is almost impossible; and the unpredictability is part of the appeal. Haymarket aims to be the perfect company to work with or for – we have the processes and attitude that ensure quality and consistency, and an entrepreneurial spirit that makes every day rewarding.<br>
