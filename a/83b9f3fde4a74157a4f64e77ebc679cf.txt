<h2>Account Executive (Hartford)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div><div>ENTERCOM HARTFORD IS HOME TO WTIC-AM, WTIC-FM, WZMX-FM &amp; WRCH-FM AND WE ARE LOOKING TO ADD NEW ACCOUNT EXECUTIVES TO OUR SALES TEAM.<br>
<br>
 DUTIES INCLUDE:<br>
<ul>
<li> Contact prospective customers to sell radio packages for broadcasting station inclusive of commercial spot inventory, digital and streaming packages and non-traditional revenue.</li>
<li> Spend at least 50% of work time engaging in sales and solicitations outside of the office.</li>
<li> Responsible for exceeding sales budget and collection of sold accounts.</li>
<li> Maintain account lists while soliciting new business.</li>
<li> Prepare promotional plans, sales literature, and advertising proposals.</li>
<li> Develop and maintain relationships with clients by providing top quality service.</li>
<li> Investigate and resolve customer problems.</li>
<li> Communicate with other departments to assure quality service in placing orders and arranging promotional events.</li>
<li> Attend sales meetings, training sessions, and client remotes.</li>
</ul>
Performs all other tasks perceived, assigned, and/or required that contribute to the smooth running of the department and to the achievement of departmental and station goals</div></div>
<div>Qualifications:</div>
<div>High school graduate; college preferred. Minimum 2 years experience in sales/marketing; previous radio sales experience preferred; must have basic knowledge of Microsoft Office. Requires valid drivers license, vehicle insurance, and dependable transportation. Must possess excellent organizational/time management skills. Must be self-motivated, self-disciplined, have a positive attitude and be an effective communicator.<br>
</div>
