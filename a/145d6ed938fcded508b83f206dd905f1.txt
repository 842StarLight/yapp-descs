<p><b>
Position Summary:</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Reporting to the Division Controller, this role is an instrumental part of the finance team for Dover Food Retail (DFR). This role is responsible as the key business support to the engineering systems operations partnering with supply chain, manufacturing, and quality organization and will perform various financial accounting activities as required by the Company, including maintaining the general ledger accounts in accordance with GAAP regulations, account reconciliations and analyses, and assisting in the year-end audit process. Additionally, this role is responsible for acting as central resource for DFR Operations finance organization. As a central resource, this role will regularly support the executive team, including the CFO and the Vice President Operations for Dover Food Retail.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Specific Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Responsible for the monthly preparation and understanding of journal entries and account reconciliations with required back-up.</li>
<li>
Responsible for maintaining the general ledger records in accurate condition and in accordance with GAAP regulations.</li>
<li>
Evaluates business changes and implement updated control mechanisms as needed to establish policies and procedures to maintain internal controls.</li>
<li>
Investigate and troubleshoot accounting issues as needed and lead/represent Finance in discussions on various issues.</li>
<li>
Responsible for providing complete account analysis and transaction research upon request by the Division Controller.</li>
<li>
Responsible for the monthly processing of outside sales commissions; including maintenance of commission module/customer rates and data.</li>
<li>
Responsible for the monthly processing and accurate recording of freight bills and accruals and reconciliation of that data back to third-party sources.
</li>
<li>Responsible for the monthly and quarterly recording and tracking of warranty related reserves.</li>
<li>
Responsible for the documentation and maintenance of accounting records for the general ledger accounting system in an organized manner; i.e. monthly/quarterly checklists and questionnaires, month-end close binders, etc.</li>
<li>
Assist in the creation and distribution of monthly financial statements and supporting schedules including but not limited to sales, margin and cost reporting.
</li>
<li>Assist in the interim and year-end audit process through the preparation of audit schedules, data, and account analysis related to auditor requests for Sarbanes-Oxley and financial audits.</li>
<li>
Responsible for the preparation of various monthly account report and analysis schedules.</li>
<li>
Analyze actual costs versus standardized costs and prepare variance reports.</li>
<li>
Assists in preparation of budgets and forecasts as requested by the Division Controller; additionally, reviews the Company’s operating results and reports on variances.
</li>
<li>Responsible for all other duties and ad hoc reports as needed and assigned by the Division Controller as deemed necessary.</li>
</ul>
