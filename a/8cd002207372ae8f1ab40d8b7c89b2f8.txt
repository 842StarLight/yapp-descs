<h2>Sr. Product Manager - Cloud Native</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>We believe work is not a place, but rather a thing you do. Our technology revolves around this core philosophy. We are relentlessly committed to helping people work and play from anywhere, on any device. Innovation, creativity and a passion for ever-improving performance drive our company and our people forward. We empower the original mobile device: YOU!</p>
<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
What we're looking for:</b></h2>
<p></p>
<p>
We are creating a team to incubate cloud-native networking opportunities. The environment is fast paced with dedicated engineering support. The technology stack changes every three months. Our customers are looking to move quickly by adoption cloud native platforms like Kubernetes backed by an organization that can provide enterprise grade support and features. The Senior Product Manager role is a product leader within the next generation networking product team</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Must have to qualify for this role:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Working with a list of customers to insert Kubernetes in their deployment while leveraging new or existing Citrix ADCs and controllers</li>
<li>
Contribute to or write blogs, competitive notes, deployment guides, YAML files</li>
<li>
Work as a strong team player with a PM and Engineering team providing grounded-observations and feedback</li>
<li>
Experience designing and deploying open-source platforms end-to-end</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Role and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
This functional role will be focused on driving customer adoption of Citrix ADC products, working with a list of customers to insert Kubernetes in their deployment while leveraging new or existing Citrix ADCs and controllers</li>
<li>
Work closely with customers to act as trusted advisor and resource for modernized apps and microservices</li>
<li>
Update / refine and create sales tools to create awareness and preference for Citrix cloud native stack.</li>
<li>
Drive or modify product requirements for both North-South and East-West use cases for Citrix ADC controllers and proxies</li>
<li>
Drive product requirements with engineering and use customer data to justify the business rationale</li>
<li>
Work closely with Citrix ADC analytics team to add new cases tied to micro services</li>
<li>
Work closely with the Product Marketing team to promote Citrix next generation solutions through official channels – blogs, events, online demos</li>
<li>
Work closely with partners to integrate Citrix ADC solutions</li>
<li>
Keep track of competitive open source solutions and devise develop differentiation leveraging the broad Citrix networking portfolio that includes analytics, visibility and ADCs</li>
<li>
Contribute or write blogs, competitive notes, deployment guides, YAML files</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Qualifications and Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Operational knowledge of web-scale platforms with SaaS or cloud providers and/or enterprise Kubernetes platforms</li>
<li>
Passion to learn new technologies, can work in an environment where the stack changes constantly and can look for markers that are the beginning of new trends</li>
<li>
Experience designing and deploying open-source Kubernetes platforms end-to-end</li>
<li>
Experience in DevOps practices and stacks such as CICD and orchestration</li>
<li>
Experience with open source software such as NGINX, HA Proxy and Cloud Native Compute Foundation projects</li>
<li>
Experience presenting to a wide range of audiences from developers and DevOps to business leaders</li>
<li>
Experience writing product requirements documents and driving them to product release</li>
<li>
5 years product management or related experience from technical support, sales engineering, technical product marketing, IT/operations or DevOps</li>
<li>
Hold a BS Computer Science or related degree or equivalent technical experience</li>
</ul>
