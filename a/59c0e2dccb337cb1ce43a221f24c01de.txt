<h2>Sr. Recruiter</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<div>WalkMe pioneered and invented the Digital Adoption Platform (DAP) to transform the user experience in today’s digital world. As digital transformation becomes essential for the success of leading companies, users and employees are overwhelmed with technology. Join WalkMe and be part of digital history as we create an industry category and help the world’s leading companies bridge the gap between humans and technology.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
</div>
<div>
As a Sr. Talent Acquisition Recruiter, you will report to the Talent Director as a member of the Talent Team. You'll partner closely with your Hiring Managers to help us grow WalkMe as you provide a positive interview experience for our Candidates and our Teams. This role is based in our New York and we have Team Members in Raleigh, NC and San Francisco.</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What You’ll Own</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Manage full life-cycle recruiting efforts as a trusted partner to your Hiring Teams to bring new hires into WalkMe</li>
<li>
Troubleshoot issues as they come up and use your experience to see around the corner to avoid setbacks</li>
<li>
Source top candidates through multiple channels and use your relationship building skills to create a pipeline of future candidates
</li>
<li>Seek out trends and share ideas with team members to help us create a learning environment where we all grow our skills
</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What You Need to Succeed:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
4+ years of full-lifecycle recruitment, ideally a mix of agency and in-house. Established relationships with SaaS Sales candidates is a real plus</li>
<li>
Passion to provide a interviewing experience based on respect and honesty</li>
<li>
Curiosity to learn what makes a candidate excited about their next move and the insight to know if WalkMe is right for that candidate</li>
<li>
Positivity, humor, and an indomitable desire to persevere through any odds</li>
<li>
Commitment (or at least willingness) to maintain accurate data in our ATS. Knowing Lever is a plus.</li>
<li>
Belief that Diversity and Inclusion is central to organizational success</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
