<h2>Production Assembler Technician</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<ul>
<li>Works on assembly problems of unusual complexity under minimal supervision. Recommend practices and procedures. Confer with supervisor only on unusual or complex problems. Participate indetermining objectives of assignment. Most work not verified by another and errors may create substantial loss of funds, accounts, cause delays, or affectCompany image. Independent contacts with internal customers requiring discussion, interpretation, and approvals.</li>
<li>Proficiency in complex assembly supporting scientific/calibration or optical technology product lines, processes and procedures. Determines method of assemblywhere complete information is not available. Assists in development, and analyzes/critiques assembly methods, processes and procedures; demonstrates expertise in all or majority of assembly products, processes, and lines; is utilized in prototype design applications.</li>
<li>Proficient in highly complex processes which are non routine, non repetitive, multiple process with limited documentation, difficult rework, etc., of a particularline or product.</li>
<li>Test, troubleshoot, and repair all types of complex electronic equipment which may include new production specials, prototype design models, hard models, andfactory test systems.</li>
<li>May assist engineering in evaluating new product designs or changes in existing designs, Collect, analyze, and report data from production processes.</li>
<li>Provide training to lesser experienced personnel, Perform other duties as assigned.</li>
<li>2-4 years of experience.</li>
<li>Experience working in lean, continuous improvement manufacturing environment.</li>
</ul>
<b>Working Conditions and Basic Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Manufacturing, environment</li>
<li>Position may require occasional heavy lifting (&gt; 50 lbs) or movement of objects up to 40 lbs.</li>
<li>Safety glasses may be required.</li>
<li>Repetitive use of upper extremities.</li>
<li>Must be able to read, speak and understand English fluently in order to communicate and provide directions to various team members in operations.</li>
<li>Available to work a minimum of 10% OT.</li>
</ul>
