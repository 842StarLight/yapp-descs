 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Portfolio Business</b><b><i>:</i></b> CP Kelco</p>
<p>
CP Kelco is a leading producer of specialty ingredients which are added to improve texture, consistency, and stability in food, pharmaceutical and industrial products. Our products are derived from renewable raw materials found in nature for use in jam, fruit and milk drinks, confectionery products as well as industrial applications. We work across the whole value chain from natural raw materials to customers’ end products. CP Kelco is part of the JM Huber Corporation, one of the largest privately held, family owned companies in the United States. With approximately $2.3 billion in sales and 4,000 employees worldwide, we have a combined material presence in more than 20 countries.</p>

<div>
<div>
Position Summary
</div>
<div><div>The PSM Process Engineer leads and directs the process safety engineering function for the Okmulgee Plant. This position optimizes CP Kelco’s process safety performance by helping the development and implementation of process safety standards and initiatives to assist the Okmulgee Plant in achieving world class Process Safety excellence. In this role you will identify, manage, direct, and audit the process safety systems required to limit risk factors leading to process related incidents, including: environmental mishaps, compliance violations, job-related injury or illness, property damage, and adverse public relations. In order to perform this job successfully, you must support operation’s process safety efforts at Okmulgee with the direction of the plant manager and Platform Process Safety Manager while acting as a functional expert and resource for the plant as it relates to the process safety related functions.
</div></div>
</div>

<div>
<div>Principal Duties &amp; Responsibilities
</div>
<div>
<div>To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform the fundamental functions and responsibilities of the job as defined below;</div>
<ul>
<li>
Provides expertise in the area of process safety related hazard assessment and risk management including (but not limited to) fire, explosion, toxic gas release, and chemical reactivity hazards</li>
<li>
Plant level subject matter expert on Process safety management standard HSMS 230 (Identification, Assessment, and Prioritization of Risks), as well as process safety management of capital projects, safety critical equipment and controls (HSMS 400)</li>
<li>
Provide expertise in the area of mechanical integrity, process safety inspections and testing protocols, preventative maintenance systems, and risk mitigation</li>
<li>
Provide expertise in the selection of safety critical equipment including containment venting and suppression, fire and explosion protection and safety related alarms, interlocks, and detection systems</li>
<li>
Develop and assist locations in the implementation of process safety standards including PSM200</li>
<li>
Develop and/or assist in the development and implementation of process safety training programs designed to enhance the skill level of site and other key personnel including providing PHA/HAZOP training at the site to ensure sufficient personnel are trained for effective process reviews.</li>
<li>
Coordinate and participate as the team lead at the plant for process hazard analysis</li>
<li>
Development and active tracking of action plans to address hazard analysis findings and to assure identified risks have been mitigated to an acceptable level</li>
</ul>
