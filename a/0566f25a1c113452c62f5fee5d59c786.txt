<div><b><i>
POSITION SUMMARY</i></b></div>
<p>
The Enterprise Account Executive manages the full sales cycle to organizations with IT security needs. Identify prospects, understand their challenges and highlight how Thycotic solutions will enhance and simplify their IT security efforts. Close deals and get our award-winning product suite into the hands of IT Admins across the identified region.
</p>
<p>Based in a specific geographic area, the candidate may be required to work from a remote home office and travel approximately 30-40% throughout the assigned territory. This role will report to the Regional Vice President of Sales for the assigned region.</p>
<p><br>
<b><i>
KEY RESPONSIBILITIES</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Prospect to and create contacts within Named Accounts in a geographic region.</li>
<li>
Proactively pursue existing leads who have expressed interest in our services.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with and engage the right Thycotic technical experts to provide an accurate and compelling story on our products’ strengths and capabilities to win deals.</li>
<li>
Consistently meet/exceed sales targets.</li>
<li>
Maintain an accurate forecast of sales pipeline.
</li>
<li>Cultivate and manage relationships with Channel partners.<br>
</li>
</ul>

<li>
