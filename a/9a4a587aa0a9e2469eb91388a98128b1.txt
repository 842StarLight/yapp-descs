<h2>B2B Software Sales</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>We are looking for a B2B software sales professional experienced in ERP /accounting system software sales for both on-premise and SaaS Cloud solutions. As a Software Solutions Advisor, you will be based out of our office in Edgewater, NJ.</p>

<p>WHAT YOU NEED TO SUCCEED:<br>- 5+ years experience in selling enterprise and/or accounting software with a proven track record of achieving set goals.<br>- Bachelor's Degree<br>- A high-energy self-starter with drive to succeed and high level of credibility and integrity<br>- Excellent presentation, written and oral communication skills<br>- Time management with organization skills and attention to detail<br>- Strong ability to actively listen and determine customer's needs<br>- Proven opener and closer with the ability to lead multiple customer sales cycles</p>
<p>COMPENSATION:<br>Competitive base salary + commissions &amp; bonuses<br>Health &amp; Life Insurance (optional dental &amp; visions)<br>401K Plan - company matching<br>Paid Time Off</p>
<p>We are a drug free, Equal Employment Opportunity Employer</p>
