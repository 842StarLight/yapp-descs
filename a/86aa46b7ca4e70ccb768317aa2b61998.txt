<h2>Project Manager - Level 3</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">A CAREER AS A RAYMOND PROJECT MANAGER...
<br><br>
Project Managers at the Raymond companies are dedicated individuals who enjoy leading a team to implement a project and meet the customers' expectations. They determine ways to move the project ahead even when challenges appear. The Project Manager will be responsible for the successful installation of complete material handling systems or modifications to existing conveyors, sortation and automated storage systems along with integration of racking, shelving, mezzanines, picking systems and supporting software. Our Project Managers work closely with the Sales team and other Systems staff to oversee project development and installation while maintaining Raymond's reputation, integrity and customer-first approach.
<br><br>
The Project Manager is responsible for the system review, final counts, ordering, schedules, budget and implementation of Storage and Automation projects. They communicate directly with vendors, customers, sales and credit personnel as well as the Systems support team of Project Engineers and Site Supervisors.
<br><br>
Experience and Skills
<br><br>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES include the following. Other duties may be assigned.
<br><br>
Manages all assigned projects without supervision, to include contract review, engineering calculations, permit administrative functions, order entry, shipping/installation coordination, installation, commissioning, and acceptance.
<br><br>
Achieves project implementation with resulting customer satisfaction while maintaining as-sold gross profit margin and minimizing or eliminating cost overruns.
<br><br>
Establishes and maintains project development and installation schedule. Creates timetable and milestones for project implementation, ensures that project remains on schedule.
<br><br>
