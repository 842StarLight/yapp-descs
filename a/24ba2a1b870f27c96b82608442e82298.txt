<h2>Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>The IMA Group is looking for an Account Executive to find business opportunities and manage customer relationships in Occupational Health. The position may be located Columbus, Dallas/Forth Worth, Philadelphia, Pittsburgh, Sanford(FL), or St. Louis.
</p>
<p>The Account Executive will locate business opportunities, preserve, expand and manage customer relationships in occupational health and other related lines of business.</p>
<p>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Establish new business and continue to develop current business</li>
<li>Manage the entire sales cycle from finding new clients to securing the closing the deal</li>
<li>Maintain records of sales and data</li>
<li>Present products (services) to prospective clients</li>
<li>Cultivate relationships, provide professional after sales support to enhance customer dedication</li>
<li>Respond to customer concerns and resolve issues aiming for customer satisfaction and preserving the company’s reputation.</li>
<li>Develop and maintain current line of business knowledge</li>
<li>Create detailed business plans to facilitate the attainment of goals &amp; quotas</li>
<li>Assist with the RFP &amp; proposal process</li>
<li>Responsible for formulating and submitting proposals in conjunction with team members</li>
<li>Communicate to internal groups the outcome of proposals and conveying the expectations set forth.</li>
<li>Travel is 50% +</li>
</ul>
</div>
