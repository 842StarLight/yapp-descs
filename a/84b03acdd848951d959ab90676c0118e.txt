<h2>Senior Data Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>The Opportunity:</b><br>
Shutterstock is growing by leaps and bounds and our data is too! We are looking for a bright and driven data analyst with a passion for data, data mining and data analysis to support our rapid business growth. The Data Analyst is responsible for contributing to creation of KPIs and dashboards, conducting business impact analysis, evaluating product usage and trends, and using data to find business growth opportunities. To enable your success you will have access to massive structured and unstructured datasets including searches, downloads, clickstreams, user behaviors, transactions and marketing spend along with a sophisticated data analytics platform to perform historical and real-time insight generation. This position reports to the Manager, Analytics and will collaborate with an extremely talented and passionate team of researchers, data engineers and data scientists from around the world working for Shutterstock.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Build and maintain dashboards and reports that enable the Executive Leadership, Sales, Product, and Marketing teams to drive business performance</li>
<li>Tell data stories to stakeholders by drawing insights from data analysis, bringing insights together to formulate the story and making recommendations for actions</li>
<li>Conduct channel, partner and conversion performance analysis</li>
<li>Analyze trends in asset usage using asset metadata such as asset type, category and keywords</li>
<li>Work closely with Product, Sales, Marketing and Operations to setup and provide data, insights and ad hoc analyses for business decision making</li>
</ul>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>10+ years professional experience of consultation to internal groups or departments</li>
<li>Strong knowledge of SQL required</li>
<li>Knowledge of key digital marketing KPIs (CPC/CACs, LTRs, etc.)</li>
<li>Excellent analytical skills including cohort analysis and lifetime value analytics</li>
<li>Knowledge of visual techniques for data analysis and presentation</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Exceptionally organized self-starter who can work independently and as part of a team</li>
<li>Ability to work in a fast-paced environment with flawless customer-centric focus</li>
<li>BS/BA in Math, Statistics, Physics, Engineering, Economics, Finance or related fields</li>
</ul>
<b>Preferred Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Strong understanding of Google Analytics preferred</li>
<li>Tableau, Python, R, or similar statistical, visualization and/or dashboarding tools</li>
<li>A/B and Multivariate testing techniques and measurements is a plus</li>
<li>Strong understanding of both statistics and regression</li>
<li>Understanding of database structures and data mining techniques</li>
<li>Experience with subscription products is a plus</li>
</ul>
