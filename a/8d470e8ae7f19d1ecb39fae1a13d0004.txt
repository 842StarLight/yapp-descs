<p>
The Pricing organization is responsible for price creation, implementation and lifecycle management in all countries where services are sold on Enterprise Servers, Storage, and Networking products. As an extension of our responsibilities, the Pricing team leads projects to improve operational or financial impact of pricing including analytics and new models.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
We are hiring a Service Segment Pricing Manager. This individual will be responsible for specific portfolios of products and services and/or for driving pricing projects. The Pricing Manager is expected to collaborate with key stakeholders including the HIT product business teams and PN Worldwide and Regional business teams and to understand the competitive market landscape.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
General expectations of this level job include:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Leads and develops product support pricing over entire lifecycle, including conformance to pricing strategies, sales readiness, compliance and associated processes</li>
<li>
Represent pricing on product or service development core teams and provide pricing requirements into development stages/phases</li>
<li>
Performs business analysis, identifies root cause, and develops recommendations/solutions to drive business improvements</li>
<li>
Collaborate with regions/worldwide regarding pricing planning, implementation and performance</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
