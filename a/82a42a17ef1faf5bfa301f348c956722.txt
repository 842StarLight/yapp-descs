<h2>Contract and Fixed Asset Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Requirements / Qualifications</b></p>
<p><b>EDUCATION, TRAINING, AND EXPERIENCE</b></p>
<p>Any combination equivalent to:</p>
<p>a minimum of a Bachelor’s degree in Business from an accredited institution, and course work in purchasing, business administration, accounting technology, or program management, and three years’ experience in accounting, purchasing, or contracting. Additional education, training, or experience which demonstrates the ability to perform the required duties may be substituted for year of experience and degree.</p>
<p><b>LICENSES AND OTHER REQUIREMENTS</b></p>
<p>Possession of a valid California Class C Driver’s license, and proof of insurance is required.</p>
<p>** $1,000 FOR M.A. OR $2,000 PhD (only 1 stipend will be credited)</p>
<p>QUALIFICATIONS</p>
<p><b>Essential Job Functions: </b></p>
<ul><li>Research and develop contracts for the more complex District projects.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Management accounts and services such as leases, contracts, maintenance agreements, and license renewals.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Compile and reconcile accounts for District contracts, leases, and agreements as appropriate.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Adhere to various codes and legal requirements, including but not limited to Public Contract Codes, State Education Codes, Government Codes, California State Board of Education, and District Board Policies.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Assist administrators and department heads in matters pertaining to contracting and procurement.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Assure fiscal accountability of District contracts, leases, and agreements to which assigned.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Development, monitor, and maintain all fiscal reporting and budgeting requirements of <b>District contracts, leases, and agreements </b>
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Track fixed assets in BPlus System</li></ul>
<ul><li>Track and maintain location of districtwide fixed assets.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Perform other related duties as assigned.</li></ul>
<p><b>Desired Job Requirements: </b></p>
<ul><li>School districts purchasing policies and procedures</li></ul>
<ul><li>Applicable sections of State Education Code and Public Contract Code.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Proficient in Excel, Word, PowerPoint, Access, Outlook, and Internet searches.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Recordkeeping and report preparation techniques.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Operations of computer and assigned software.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Research and development skills/methods.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Oral and written communication skills.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Interpersonal skills using tact, patience, and courtesy.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Principles and practices of administration and training.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Correct English usage, grammar, spelling, punctuation, and vocabulary.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Modern office practices, procedure, and equipment.</li></ul>
