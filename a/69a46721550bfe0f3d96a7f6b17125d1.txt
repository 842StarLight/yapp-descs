<h2>Fitness Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Job Summary:</b> The Fitness Manager Training Manager ensures that the club delivers a high
<br>quality fitness experience to all members.
<br>Responsible for communicating and upholding company standards and
<br>leading by example. Ensure that team members consistently execute the
<br>basics in punctuality, dress code compliance, friendliness and cleanliness.
<br>Hires, trains and develops a strong team of Personal Trainers.
<br>Responsible for the successful attainment of department targets, including
<br>revenue and member retention.
<br>Is the point of reference for fitness expertise within the club.
<br><br>
<b>Essential Duties &amp; Responsibilities:</b><br>
The FM is responsible for performing the following activities for the club:
<br>Staffing and Development [40% of time]
<br>Maintain a fully engaged and high performing fitness team that aligns with
<br>company values and goals. To achieve this objective, the FM will:
<br>§ Hire develop and manage performance of qualified Personal
<br>Trainers
<br>§ Train &amp; develop Membership Counselors regarding fitness and
<br>supplementation presentations, emphasizing the impact of these on
<br>membership retention
<br>§ Conduct weekly (or as-needed) meetings with direct reports to
<br>review performance and offer direction, motivation and guidance
<br>toward achieving individual and company goals. Offer career growth
<br>and advancement opportunities as warranted
<br>§ Manage the Personal Trainer schedule including staying within
<br>budgeted non-session hours as
<br>submitted to and approved by the CM
<br>§ Mediates club employee relations matters for all club fitness
<br>employees
<br>§ Discipline staff under the guidance of your direct supervisor
<br><br>
Fitness Management [40% of time]
<br>Ensure that all club members have access to a highly trained and professional
<br>fitness staff able to provide fitness counseling, training and programs within
<br>Crunch standards. The Head Coach (HC) is responsible for the successful
<br>attainment of fitness and supplement revenue goals. To achieve these objectives
<br><br>
<b>the HC will:</b><br>
§ Ensure that all Crunch Coaches are delivering high quality programs to
<br>their clients
<br>§ Develop weekly work schedules for fitness staff. Ensure that staff
<br>properly records their Time &amp; Labor,
<br>ensure that staff complie with Time and Labor guidelines, and manage
<br>overtime in accordance with
<br>company guidelines.
<br>§ Provide member service and support related to fitness servicing issues.
<br>Assist members and
<br>encourage their involvement in fitness services.
<br>Ensure accurate administration of Personal Training including usage of dotFIT,
<br>measurement tracking, workout programs etc.
<br>§ Responsible for achieving monthly, quarterly and yearly revenue
<br>objectives set forth by the Company
<br>with regards to CPT and Supplements
<br>§ Train and develop new Crunch Coaches
<br>§ Regularly train staff on industry updates and teach Crunch Coaches to
<br>be continually successful to
<br>ensure that all clients are trained professionally
<br><b>Sales and Service [20% of time:</b> club specific]
<br>Participate and provide direct execution support in the fitness area.
<br><br>
<b>QUALIFICATIONS:</b><br>
Participate and provide direct execution support in the fitness area.
<br><br>
<b>Knowledge, Skills &amp; Abilities:</b><br>
Knowledge of Crunch Certified Personal Training Program including program
<br>software (dotFIT, Google Calendar, MASS).
<br>Knowledge of Personal Training technique and program design.
<br>Must be able to adjust and operate all club equipment.
<br>Experience in coaching/motivating groups.
<br>Strong interpersonal &amp; communication skills.
<br>Able to generate, maintain &amp; demonstrate a friendly, enthusiastic &amp; positive
<br>attitude.
<br>Possesses a strong member service focus.
<br>Responds professionally to requests and inquires from guests, members and
<br>staff.
<br>Leadership skills
<br><br>
<b>Education Level:</b> High School Diploma or GED required
<br>Bachelor’s Degree preferred
<br>Current Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation (CPR)
<br><b>Certifications:</b> (One or more of the following certifications)
<br>Participate and provide direct execution support in the fitness area.
<br><b>Qualifications:</b> American College of Sports Medicine (ACSM)
<br>§ Certified Personal Trainer
<br>Health Fitness Specialist
<br>American Council on Exercise (ACE)
<br>§ Personal Trainer Certification
<br>The Cooper Institute
<br>§ Personal Trainer Certification
<br>International Fitness Professionals Association (IFPA)
<br>§ Personal Fitness Trainer Certification
<br>National Academy of Sports Medicine (NASM)
<br>§ Certified Personal Trainer
<br>§ Corrective Exercise Specialist (CES)
<br>§ Performance Enhancement Specialist (PES)
<br>National Exercise and Sports Trainers Association (NESTA)
<br>§ Personal Fitness Trainer Certification
<br>National Federation of Professional Trainers (NFPT)
<br>§ Personal Trainer Certification
<br>National Strength and Conditioning Association (NSCA)
<br>§ Certified Personal Trainer
<br>§ Certified Strength and Conditional Specialist (CSCS)
<br><b>Experience:</b> 1-2 years as a Personal Trainer/Crunch Coach preferred
<br><b>Preferred:</b> Prior management experience in retail/hospitality industry leading 3-10
<br>employees
<br><b>Preferred:</b> Consultative sales experience
<br><br>
<b>Physical Requirements:</b><br>
Ability to work in club office; move about club floors and rooms; review, revise,
<br>create club paperwork; communicate with employees, members, and the public
<br>Regularly required to demonstrate or explain proper physical fitness activities,
<br>techniques and procedures. Regularly required to lift up to 50 pounds
<br><b>Travel:</b> Must be able to travel by car and airplane up to 5% of the time</div>
