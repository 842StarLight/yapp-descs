<ul><li>Confer with engineering and controls personnel as well as key vendors to develop sound equipment concepts</li></ul>
<ul><li>Delegate needed research, or drawings for proposals to draftsman, engineers, vendors</li></ul>
<ul><li>Prepare technical write ups of proposed machine designs</li></ul>
<ul><li>Facilitate and contribute to estimating costs and budgets for equipment proposals</li></ul>
<ul><li>Prepare dynamic technical and commercial sales proposals</li></ul>
<ul><li>Create project Gantt charts for equipment proposals</li></ul>
<ul><li>Make proposal presentations to clients</li></ul>
<ul><li>Steward and nurture customer contacts to grow key client relationships</li></ul>
<ul><li>Negotiate contractual requirements and close sales with customers</li></ul>
<ul><li>Assist in prioritizing and qualifying quote inquiries, along with regularly communicating status to customers</li></ul>
<ul><li>Communicate concepts and specifications to engineering and project managers for successful proposals</li></ul>
