<h2>Regional Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
The essential functions of the position include, but are not limited to the following tasks, duties, and responsibilities consistent with the function. The employee is expected to perform all other duties as requested, directed, and/or assigned.
<br><br>
Relationship Building and Account Service
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Creates and implements a corporate partnership program that provides value to the professional education partners and to the College through the continuous development and maintenance of relationships with partners that appropriately benefit from the educational service model, offerings and abilities of Collegis Professional.</li>
<li>Develops and conducts informational presentations and special events with prospective and existing corporate partners that promote the value of Collegis Professional educational programs and services.</li>
<li>Provides excellent customer service to corporate partners and effectively resolves challenges in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Identifies areas of improvement in the partnership and assists in creating and implementing solutions.</li>
<li>Collaborates with Career Services, Faculty, Program Coordinators, Advisory Boards, and campus management to discuss trends, identify potential concerns and strategize ways to overcome them; leverages community relationships that exist with campus staff members to aid in partnership creation.</li>
<li>Shows passion for improving the delivery of services to corporate partners with a commitment to continuous improvement.</li>
</ul>
Market Orientation, Strategy Generation, and Business Development Effectiveness
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Develops and maintains partnerships by leveraging and utilizing knowledge of the corporate sales process and cycle, and creating innovative ways to connect with challenging accounts.</li>
<li>Recommends and implements strategies to increase account penetration, including establishing a process for securing and tracking leads and account activity.</li>
<li>Maintains market awareness and pursues opportunities; actively seeking information on competitor offerings, programs, and market plans to share information with management and marketing department and provides and/or assists in building an offensive and/or defensive response.</li>
<li>Identifies and investigates growth opportunities for Collegis Professional including leveraging a national and/or regional partner to gain traction with prospective accounts in assigned region; develops, presents, and implements plans for the acquisition of new corporate partners.</li>
</ul>
Problem Identification, Data Analysis, and Decision Making
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Utilizes the appropriate data management protocol, including applicable CRM software, to record, update, and track all relevant business development interactions with potential and existing corporate partners to maximize efficiency and to analyze data and marketing activity on a weekly basis.</li>
<li>Identifies the information needed to solve a problem effectively, presents problem analysis, and thinks “outside of the box” to generate creative, workable, and useful solutions.</li>
<li>Gathers data and others’ input when making decisions; considers lessons learned from experience, differing needs, and the impact of the decision on others.</li>
<li>Demonstrate advanced skill and insight in gathering and sorting key information while maintaining persistence, skill, and resilience through the process.</li>
<li>Appropriately incorporates internal and external professional network and other additional resources to establish a shared ownership when faced with challenging problems and utilizes the ideas of others to help develop solutions.</li>
<li>Adhere to the appropriate information security policies based on the sensitivity of company data and report any security related issues</li>
</ul>
Planning and Organizing
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Creates an organized and systematic quarterly territory plan for effectively engaging potential corporate partners; maintains clear, detailed records of activities related to the accomplishment of stated objectives.</li>
<li>Creates and utilizes sound methods to plan and track work, appointments, and commitments; prioritizes tasks by importance and deadline and adjust priorities as situations change; manages time effectively to deliver projects, reports, requests for information in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Orchestrates multiple activities at one time in a professional manner and appropriately identifies the resources necessary to complete tasks and achieve goals; anticipates obstacles and develops contingency plans; demonstrates excellent judgment, professionalism, and flexibility in adjusting and adapting initial strategies as required.</li>
<li>Establishes a systematic course of action for self and/or others to ensure accomplishment of a specific objective; sets priorities, goals, and timetables to achieve maximum productivity.</li>
<li>Creates action plans for achieving performance expectations.</li>
</ul>
Effective Communication and Professionalism
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Establishes productive linkages and regular communication with program managers to appropriately disseminate prospective students’ requests for information on the various programs and services of Collegis Professional.</li>
<li>Practices good hygiene and presents oneself in an appropriate and highly professional appearance.</li>
<li>Sets standards for the highest level of professional behavior; leads by example and models success behaviors, a high performance work ethic, and constant self-improvement.</li>
<li>Engages in active listening with corporate partners, students, campus management, and others and formulates relevant responses.</li>
<li>Exercises tact, discretion, and diplomacy in dealing with sensitive circumstances; recognizes others’ nonverbal indicators and cues and adjusts tone and style accordingly.</li>
<li>Demonstrates a high degree of self-awareness and actively strives to develop and maintain positive working relationships with co-workers by being punctual, fulfilling all commitments made, and maintaining a pleasant work attitude.</li>
<li>Engages in professional development activities, including but not limited to: memberships in professional organizations; attendance at conferences, workshops, and training sessions.</li>
</ul>
Delivering Results
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Sets and maintains high performance standards for self that support the overall strategic plan and vision of the department, campus/region, and organization as a whole, and holds self accountable for achieving results.</li>
<li>Recognizes, capitalizes, and pursues activities and opportunities with energy and drive.</li>
<li>Demonstrates a sincere positive attitude towards completing tasks and achieving individual, team, and organizational goals.</li>
<li>Devises and executes new methods and strategies to reach challenging goals and persists until individual and team objectives are achieved.</li>
<li>Consistently meets and/or exceeds expectations with role responsibilities, tasks, and goals based on own personal initiative and with minimal direction from others.</li>
<li>Demonstrates consistent attainment of qualitative and quantitative performance expectations.</li>
<li>Seeks out and/or accepts additional responsibilities in the context of the role.</li>
</ul>
Team Orientation
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Cultivates and maintains a positive team environment.</li>
<li>Develops and nurtures collaborative relationships with peers and managers to support the team effort for student and College success.</li>
<li>Attends campus meetings, functions, and events to provide information and input for operations and growth.</li>
<li>Provides constructive and appropriate input and ideas to assist the College in decision-making and process improvement.</li>
<li>Encourages team unity through the sharing of information or expertise, working together to solve problems, and putting team success first; promotes the value of and builds team continuity and cohesiveness and acknowledges and shares wins and successes for the team.</li>
</ul>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in Business, Communication, Education, or related field preferred.</li>
<li>Minimum of three years of relevant experience and/or training in business to business sales, or equivalent combination of education and sales experience, preferably in educational sales and/or services, admissions counseling and/or customer service.</li>
<li>General knowledge of the higher education industry preferred.</li>
<li>Ability to work a flexible schedule as the College, partners, and students needs dictate including evenings and weekends. Must be able and expect to work in the field a minimum of three days a week.</li>
<li>Proven customer service experience in a professional environment.</li>
<li>Strong multi-tasking, organizational, planning, and coordination skills.</li>
<li>Must be self-motivated, flexible, patient, goal-oriented, and enjoy working in a fast-paced and dynamic environment.</li>
<li>Strong presentation, persuasion, and negotiation skills and the ability to build good rapport with a variety of constituents.</li>
<li>Ability to set ones’ own priorities, work effectively under pressure, and meet frequently occurring deadlines with minimal supervision.</li>
<li>Possess excellent interpersonal skills to interact effectively and professionally with corporate partners, students, management, and peers with the highest levels of integrity.</li>
<li>Possess a spirit of enthusiasm, energy, and multi-cultural awareness.</li>
<li>Demonstrate knowledge and good judgment in matters of College policy and procedures.</li>
<li>Working knowledge of computers and networking systems in a professional environment.</li>
<li>Must have strong command of the English language, in both oral and written form and possesses excellent written and verbal communication skills.</li>
<li>Must be proficient in Microsoft Office Suite, with an emphasis on Outlook, Word, Excel, and Power Point.</li>
<li>A professional appearance and telephone manner is essential.</li>
</ul>
