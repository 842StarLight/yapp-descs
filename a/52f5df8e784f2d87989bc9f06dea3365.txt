<b>Description:</b><br>
WESTMINSTER CHRISTIAN ACADEMY
<br>JOB DESCRIPTION
<br>FRONT DESK / ACCOUNTS PAYABLE COORDINATOR
<br>September 2019
<br><br>
<b>General Description:</b> The Accounts Payable Coordinator demonstrates a spiritual maturity in attitude, actions, and speech, showing a consistent walk with Jesus Christ to the praise and glory of God. This is a full-time position.
<br><br>
<b>Contracted By:</b> The Board of Directors upon recommendation from the Head of School
<br><br>
<b>Responsible To:</b> The Business Manager
<br><b>Supervises:</b> N/A
<br><b>Observable Actions:</b> The Accounts Payable Coordinator shall exhibit integrity as a Christian leaderstriving for continuous improvement in the job requirements and exhibiting all related skills in the areas below.
<br><br>
<b>Spiritual Requirements:</b><br>
Models a daily walk with Jesus Christ in attitude, speech and actions
<br>Shows by example the importance of Biblical knowledge and study, prayer, witnessing, and unity in Christian fellowship
<br>Follows the Matthew 18 principle
<br>Demonstrates brotherly love within the Body of Christ for students, parents, and staff
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
A. Front Desk / Accounts Payable Duties
<br><br>
Friendly and helpful point of contact for all callers and guests to the Business Office and provides administrative assistance as needed
<br>Responsible for ordering/stocking of department supplies
<br>Oversees the processing of invoice and vendor payments
<br>Enters all invoice information and ensures vendor payments are posted to correct expense accounts by analyzing invoice/expense reports
<br>Calculates and remits sales taxes by calculating requirements on paid invoices, including preparing the journal entry for posting
<br>Reviews vendors’ statements for correctness, taking appropriate action so that bills do not become past due or are paid more than once.
<br>Verifies that all purchase requests and invoices have proper approval
<br>Ensures timely payment of all invoices.
<br>Initiates weekly check runs, which includes processing, mailing, and filing
<br>Works closely with Business Manager to monitor expenses according to budget
<br>Organizes all A/P documentation and ensures all documentation is filed in a timely and orderly fashion.
<br>Assists with processing and posting of credit card receipts and timely reconciliation and payment of credit card statement
<br>Reviews accounts payable for completeness and correctness and reports any unusual activity to Business Manager
<br>Assist with monthly AP financial close process by running applicable reports and reconciling
<br>Track, verify, and prepare yearly 1099s
<br>Assist with various school-related event reconciliations
<br>Ensures that at the end of each business day all unused checks are locked and secured
<br>Prepares reports as needed for the Business Manager
<br><br>
B. Miscellaneous Duties
<br><br>
Any other duties not mentioned which are deemed necessary per the Business Manager
<br><br>
<b>Professional Requirements:</b><br>
Utilizes educational opportunities for professional growth
<br>Seeks and/or accept the counsel of the Business Manager and colleagues, while maintaining a teachable attitude
<br>Provides input and recommendations for administrative and managerial functions in the business office
<br>Attends and participate in scheduled orientations, devotions, conferences,school functions, seminars, retreats, in-service, and scheduled meetings i.e. Committee, faculty, and P.T.F.
<br>Contributes to the general improvement of the school program
<br>Refuses to use or circulate confidential information inappropriately
<br>Studies and knows the procedures for crisis management issues including, but not limited to, fire and tornado drills
<br><br>
<b>Personal Requirements:</b><br>
Demonstrates the character qualities of enthusiasm, courtesy, flexibility, integrity, gratitude, kindness, self-control, perseverance, and punctuality
<br>Meets everyday stress with emotional stability, objectivity, and optimism
<br>Develops and maintains rapport with staff by treating others with friendliness, dignity, and consideration
<br>Shows respectful and faithful submission to constituted authority
<br>Maintains a personal appearance that is a role model of cleanliness, modesty, good taste, and in agreement with school policy
<br>Recognizes the need for good public relations.
<br>Represents the school in favorable and professional manner to the constituency and general public
<br>Attends regularly and faithfully support a local church whose fundamental beliefs are in agreement with the WCA Statement of Faith.
<br><br>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
<b>Skills/Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 2-3 years’ experience in an Accounts Payable role</li>
<li>High School degree required. Associate’s degree preferred.</li>
<li>Strong knowledge of Accounts Payable principles and processes</li>
<li>Experience with sales tax preferred</li>
<li>Attention to detail</li>
<li>Must be computer literate. Proficiency in Microsoft Excel and Word software required.</li>
<li>Must have basic business office skills including typing, filing, and financial skills.</li>
<li>Protects organization’s value by keeping information confidential</li>
<li>Ability to work independently and pro-actively</li>
<li>Possesses the ability to multitask and good organizational skills</li>
<li>Excellent customer service skills</li>
</ul>
