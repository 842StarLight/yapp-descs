<h2>Leasing Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">The Leasing Consultant role is primarily a sales position with an emphasis on customer service, meeting goals and achieving results. As a Leasing Consultant, you will manage the sales process from start to finish; presenting apartments and the community in a compelling way, overcoming objections, building relationships and creating excitement. You will maintain these relationships by providing excellent customer service and helping current residents resolve problems as they arise. A successful Leasing Consultant must demonstrate strong sales and customer service, organizational, multi-tasking and communication skills.
<br>Essential Functions
<br><ul>
<li>Demonstrates an understanding of the property, its residents, the geographic area, and the competition.</li>
<li>Develops and maintains effective telephone, greeting, demonstration, and closing and follow up skills.</li>
<li>Answers incoming telephone calls, responds to all email leads, tours all scheduled and walk-in traffic, conducts follow up with all traffic and records all activity into the Management Information System (e.g. Yardi).</li>
<li>Maintains relationships with area corporations and real estate brokers to generate new business.</li>
<li>Keeps current with local events and hiring trends, which may have an impact on the property.</li>
<li>Ensures the property exterior and interior common areas and vacant apartments are presentable.</li>
<li>Knowledgeable in operating policies and procedures specific to the property, including, but not limited to, security deposit policy, lease break policy, photo ID policy and pet policy.</li>
<li>Handles resident service requests and follows up with the resident to ensure the work has been completed to their satisfaction.</li>
<li>Manages the lease expiration and renewal process, under the direction of the Property Manager.</li>
<li>Ensures that the site-specific resident retention cycle is properly executed.</li>
<li>Coordinates the planning of resident social events.</li>
<li>Prepares the property's monthly newsletter as directed by Property Manager.</li>
<li>Responsible for administrative duties pertaining to processing rental applications, general correspondence, and resident files.</li>
</ul>
Job Requirements
<br><ul>
<li>High School Diploma and/or equivalent is required. Some college is a plus.</li>
<li>1 to 2 years of sale, leasing, or hospitality related experience</li>
<li>Proficiency with Microsoft Office software. Yardi experience a plus</li>
</ul>
