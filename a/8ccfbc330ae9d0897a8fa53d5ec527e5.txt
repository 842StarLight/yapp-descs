<h2>Senior Business Systems Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Senior Business Systems Analyst</b></h1>
<p></p>
<p>The Natural History Museum of Los Angeles County is seeking a <b>Senior Business Systems Analyst</b> for our Guest Relations department. You will work closely with the Director, Guest Relations to analyze existing systems for improvements, define business requirements, provide solutions and recommend best practices at the Natural History Museum. It is an exciting time as we embark on new journeys of becoming a museum of, for, and with L.A.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities for this position include, but are not limited to:</b></p>
<ul><li> Responsible for the day-to-day operations, maintenance and future use of the Museum’s Ticketing Software, online ecommerce, related hardware and data warehouse.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Analyze existing systems for improvements, define business requirements, provide solutions and recommend best practices.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Provide data driven insights, implement system integrations and advance Museum’s mission and strategic goals.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Lead the business intelligence initiative (DEXIBIT) providing technical support, reporting and analysis around data</li></ul>
<ul><li> Be a key member of the team to implement a future Customer Relationship Management (CRM) system.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Provides input, assists with analysis and research as requested. Raises issues to Director overseeing the Division regarding implementation challenges for the Division and its programs/functions in implementing the strategic goals.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Evaluates the effectiveness of program/function through established outcome measures and metrics and input from Supervisors and other Staff Members.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Develops procedures for the consistent execution of the program/function. Ensures procedures are written, communicated to all staff affected and stored in an accessible fashion for employees to reference in the future. Informs the Director of any significant changes to operations.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Conducts ongoing configuration and monitoring of ticketing system and its custom integrations with the Museum's fundraising and financial software. Maintains ticketing test environment and develops test scripts. Assists with the execution and analysis of test data output. Serves as the primary contact with the ticketing software vendor. Provides project management for ongoing sustainment tasks for system.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Works with the Museum's internal user group to identify and prioritize reporting needs and develop custom analytics and serves as project manager for external consultant based reporting and analytics services. Documents business requirements and translates these to specifications and design. Reviews and evaluates product modules and upgrades when necessary.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Assists the Director with coordinating with the Finance Division with respect to ongoing reporting and reconciliation of operating and financial data collected in the ticketing system.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Participates as an active member of Galaxy User Community to identify and implement best practices. Recommends system improvements, troubleshooting, document and report systems concerns to software manufacture and appropriate NHM staff.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Configures and maintains the online sales channel, and improves the guest experience by identifying and implementing improvements to the presentation of options and optimizing the purchase path.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Develop and maintain the organization’s Business Intelligence tool and continue to implement integrations to enhance the ticketing system and data consolidation.</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>This position has the following requirements:</b></p>
<ul><li> Understands basic planning processes. Is familiar with the setting of goals and is capable of developing staff goals and work assignments to support the accomplishment of departmental goals. Is capable of monitoring implementation of goals at this level, making necessary adjustments and informing higher levels of management regarding significant changes.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Has demonstrated ability to work with others on teams, across an organization and external audiences. Ability to collaborate effectively with all levels within an organization.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ability to read, analyze, and interpret general business periodicals, professional journals, technical procedures, peer reviewed primary literature, or government regulations.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ability to effectively present information to and respond to questions from groups of managers, clients, customers, and the general public</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ability to work with mathematical concepts such as probability and statistical inference.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ability to analyze data related to a specific program or function</li></ul>
<ul><li> Has the capability to assess complex situations and make decisions and/or recommendations for specific programs/functions and/or across departments.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Has experience in using programming languages such as TSQL, SSRS, C#, MySQL, PHP, Python/R, CRM and BI Tools for data analysis.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Has proficiency is using web related programming languages such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript and jQuery.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Proficient in POS configurations and functionalities.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Understanding of domains, network securities, virtual servers and PCI requirements.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Bachelor's degree in computer science or information technology preferred.</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p>The <b>Natural History Museum of Los Angeles County</b> is especially interested in candidates whose background and experience have prepared them to contribute to our commitment to engagement and inclusion at culturally diverse audiences in museum and in sciences.</p>
<p></p>
<p>The Natural History Museum of Los Angeles County is seeking applicants who have demonstrated experience and commitment working with a diverse community. Salary is commensurate with experience plus excellent benefits. This is a full time regular, exempt position.</p>
