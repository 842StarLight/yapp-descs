<b>About the Position:</b><br>
The Ceridian Customer Success Team is responsible for the overall business and executive relationship with Ceridian’s Dayforce customers. The Client Relationship Executive (CRE) strives to build trusted advisor relationships with our clients and help them build better teams that achieve more utilizing our Dayforce solution. The CRE quarterbacks the strategic planning, managing, and measuring the ongoing success of our client base via on-site business reviews, product roadmap presentations, customer community events, and overall advocacy on behalf of the client. The Client Relationship Executive is outcome-focused and driven to help define and achieve client success.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Attains retention goals by creating and executing a business plan that effectively demonstrates strategies to exceed client retention goals, expand Ceridian’s value, and promote Customer Advocacy.</li>
<li>Maintain the highest level of client loyalty by developing relationships at all levels within the client organization, particularly the C-level executives.</li>
<li>Conduct ongoing business reviews that measure client success and build plans to achieve client success including:</li>
<li>Provide information about Ceridian’s company vision and product strategy as well as release readiness activities</li>
<li>Share best practices and connect clients to other clients to share ideas and industry best practices</li>
<li>Identify areas where solution optimization is needed and bring in the product subject matter expert to help</li>
<li>Solicit client feedback and work with internal partners to help drive product and operational improvements to enhance the overall Customer Experience</li>
<li>Partner with Support Management to continuously improve efficiency and quality of service to clients</li>
<li>Work closely with operational counterparts inside Ceridian (Hosting, Product Development, Implementation, Support, Billing) to drive issues to closure</li>
<li>Continually advocate for Clients and find new ways to add value to the Client</li>
<li>Drive client engagement by connecting clients to the broader Ceridian community of clients with attendance at events such as Insights and local events; providing opportunities to share their success stories and network with other clients for best practices collaboration</li>
<li>Manage and negotiate the renewal process when needed.</li>
<li>Identify additional solution expansion opportunities and refer to sales</li>
</ul>
<b>Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>5+ years experience with Human Capital Management space</li>
<li>Solid business acumen</li>
<li>Familiarity with HCM software features and current competitive landscape</li>
<li>Managing large enterprise accounts (i.e. over 2,000 employees)</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills, including the ability to chair meetings or host webinars</li>
<li>Customer relationship management experience in the HCM space</li>
<li>Proven effectiveness with executive relationships, comfortable and confident in the board room</li>
<li>High emotional intelligence and ability resolve conflict wherever it arises</li>
<li>Highly self-motivated and team oriented</li>
<li>Ability to work virtual in a professional manner</li>
<li>Proficient with the tools of the trade (laptop, e-mail, Office apps, smartphone, Salesforce)</li>
<li>Highly organized and efficient with exceptional follow through</li>
<li>Desire and ability to negotiate and communicate with clients</li>
<li>Ability to manage a territory of clients and prioritize your time and travel</li>
<li>Bachelor degree required, at a minimum. Business or technical degree preferred.</li>
<li>Travel Requirement: Up to 50%</li>
</ul>
