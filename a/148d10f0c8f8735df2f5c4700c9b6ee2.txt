<h2>Producer – Individual Medical (GHB Insurance)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Acrisure has been named the fastest growing insurance brokerage firm three years in a row by Business Insurance Magazine. We have over 300 locations across the US and continue to grow every month.</p>
<p>To fuel that growth we have another exciting opportunity as a Producer – Individual Medical with GHB Insurance, an Acrisure Agency Partner. This position is responsible for the production and management of individual medical accounts.</p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Achieve objectives established in annual producer sales goals.</li>
<li>Prospect for new business from existing clients, referrals, and identified target groups.</li>
<li>Contact prospects for the purpose of scheduling appointments or telephone consultations.</li>
<li>Consult with prospects to determine insurance needs, provide quotes, and make appropriate recommendations.</li>
<li>Maintain client database and facilitate enrollment process and submission of enrollment applications through electronic enrollment tools or paper applications.</li>
<li>Stay informed of all claims or service issues and become involved in the resolution when required.</li>
<li>Provide continued service and participate in renewals by contacting clients, reviewing options, and making recommendations.</li>
<li>Attend all sales meetings as required.</li>
<li>Participate in professional organizations, educational opportunities, community events and personal networks.</li>
<li>Keep informed of industry developments through review of trade press and by attending insurance carrier training meetings on new products, etc.</li>
<li>Perform other specific duties and projects as assigned by agency management.</li>
</ul>
<p>This description is not meant to be all-inclusive and may be modified from time to time at the discretion of management.</p>
<p><b>Competencies:</b></p>
<p>To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill, and/or ability required. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</p>
<p>Education and/or Experience:</p>
<ul>
<li>High School Diploma or the recognized equivalent is required. Bachelor's degree is preferred.</li>
<li>Life and Disability Insurance License is required.</li>
<li>Minimum of one (2) year experience working in individual medical insurance is preferred.</li>
</ul>
<p>Computer Skills:</p>
<ul>
<li>Advanced computer skills including Microsoft Word, Excel, Outlook, Explorer and web based applications</li>
<li>Ability to learn additional software and systems.</li>
</ul>
<p>Other Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>Must be organized, detail oriented, and efficient with excellent verbal and written communications skills and the ability to effectively work with others.</li>
<li>Must be able to work proactively and independently in a fast-paced environment and have the ability to prioritize and work on multiple projects.</li>
<li>Must have a high level of customer service and problem-solving skills.</li>
<li>Ability to maintain a professional demeanor and positive attitude</li>
</ul>
