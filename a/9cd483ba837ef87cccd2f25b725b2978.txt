<p>Kyriba empowers financial leaders and their teams with award-winning solutions for cash and risk management, payments and supply chain finance. Kyriba delivers a highly secure, 100 percent SaaS enterprise platform, superior bank connectivity and a seamlessly integrated solution-set for tackling today’s most complex financial challenges.
<br>
<br>
Thousands of companies, including many of the world’s largest organizations, rely on Kyriba to streamline key processes, protect against loss from fraud and cybercrime, and accelerate growth opportunities through improved decision support. Technology analyst-firm IDC recognized Kyriba as a global leader in its MarketScape for SaaS and cloud-enabled treasury and risk management applications for 2017-2018. Kyriba is headquartered in New York, with offices in San Diego, Paris, London, Tokyo, Dubai and other major locations.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>We currently have a rewarding and challenging <b>Business Development Representative </b>(BDR) opportunity for extraordinarily talented and motivated individuals who thrive in a fast-paced, entrepreneurial work environment. Your main responsibilities will include identifying, qualifying and nurturing prospects; building trust, uncovering compelling events and presenting Kyriba’s unique business value with the goal of developing a sales-ready opportunity.
<br>
<br>
Prospects are identified through marketing campaigns as well as direct prospecting efforts from the BDR. Interaction with the prospect involves comprehensive discussions surrounding their current Treasury operations to identify their current situation, problems and pain all while positioning Kyriba’s unique business value. Throughout the interaction, the BDR records detailed notes and finds opportunities to ask qualifying questions. This results in a trusting interaction that speeds up the buying process.
<br>
<br>
Additionally, nurturing activities take place during which resources and other educational materials are offered. Once the prospect is qualified and the timing is ideal, the BDR introduces the right sales personnel to the prospect and the opportunity is transitioned to the sales force.
<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities include:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Educate contacts through comprehensive discussions surrounding their current Treasury operations and Kyriba’s product offerings
</li>
<li>Become fluent with all scripting, and speak with general proficiency regarding basic Treasury terminology and concepts
</li>
<li>Understand where the prospect is in the buying process and qualify accordingly
</li>
<li>Nurture non-qualified prospects by offering resources and educational materials
</li>
<li>Follow-up on inquiries from marketing campaigns, online ads, events and webinars
</li>
<li>Perform high volume outbound calling activities combined with e-mail marketing
</li>
<li>Gather key information to update SalesForce.com in order to effectively prospect specified territory
</li>
<li>Enhance product knowledge through regularly scheduled training sessions and self-education
</li>
<li>Become part of a very collaborative team in terms of training and sharing education, and best practices
</li>
<li>Establish and maintain strong relationships with the sales executive in assigned territory
</li>
<li>Provide qualified sales-ready opportunities to the field sales organization to engage in our sales cycle
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Preferred qualifications:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Highly successful track record in inside sales or cold calling environment
</li>
<li>Ability to handle high outbound call volume with corresponding high talk time
</li>
<li>Must be articulate, organized, detail-oriented, and have the ability to multi-task in a dynamic, fast-changing entrepreneurial environment
</li>
<li>Ability to learn complex Treasury concepts and incorporate into daily interactions and conversations
</li>
<li>Ability to build rapport with prospects and transition inquiries over time to the sales force
</li>
<li>Strong phone, prospecting, sales, customer service, and interpersonal skills
</li>
<li>Understand the customer’s environment and introduces new concepts to solve the customer’s problems
</li>
<li>Effective listener to understand prospect needs
</li>
<li>Ability to effectively communicate with internal stakeholder
</li>
</ul>
