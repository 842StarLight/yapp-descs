<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>The Marketing Manager will be responsible for overseeing the promotion of the company's brands utilizing all marketing venues. The position will be responsible for developing pricing strategies, identifying new customers, supporting lead generation efforts, and creating promotions. The Marketing Manager will be tasked with developing budgets and expenditures, as well as R&amp;D appropriations, organizing trade shows and developing and managing the company's advertising campaigns.</div>
