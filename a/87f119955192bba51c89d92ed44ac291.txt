<h2>Marketing and Data Strategy Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div><div>
<div><b>Description</b></div>
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<b><i>The position will remain open until filled</i></b><br>
</div>
<div>
The Marketing and Data Strategy Manager analyzes and executes Visit Grand Junction's marketing through advertising technologies and multi-channel platforms. The manager is a motivated self-starter with a passion for leveraging data, progressive marketing tools, and analytics to support travel and tourism in the Grand Junction area. This position reports to the Visit Grand Junction Director.<br>
</div>
</div>
<p></p>
<div><b>Duties</b></div>
<div>
<p><b>Manage &amp; execute data-driven branding, marketing, advertising and technology strategies:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develops, creates, and implements annual data-driven marketing, media, and ad-tech strategies and plans for local (in-market), regional, national, and international visitor markets in earned, owned, and paid media.
</li>
<li>Leads and manages marketing vendor relationships and creative agency partners.
</li>
<li>Plans and manages budgets and keeps accurate accounting records.
</li>
<li>Leverages data to provide analysis and insights on marketing performance, makes recommendations and adjusts as needed.
</li>
<li>Maintains and stays up to date on the latest ad-tech, AI &amp; BI tools and trends.
</li>
<li>Monitors metrics and collaborates with the marketing team to analyze the business impact of the marketing and data strategy and helps implement optimization recommendations.
</li>
<li>Organizes annual stakeholder meeting.
</li>
<li>Develops Annual Report and Marketing Plan.
</li>
<li>Keeps abreast of Colorado Tourism Office, US Travel Association, Brand USA, and other Colorado DMO marketing research and initiatives.</li>
</ul>
<b>
Paid Media/Advertising-</b><ul>
<li>Works with marketing team and contractors to develop, implement, monitor, and evolve creative and advertising campaigns utilizing ad tech tools, data collection and analysis.
</li>
<li>Ensures agencies are referencing available data and adjusting campaigns accordingly on a regular basis.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
Earned Media/PR-</b><ul>
<li>Plans and hosts media/influencer visits, including international and domestic group media FAM trips, working with local tourism partners.
</li>
<li>Works with marketing team and contractors to develop, execute, and assess public relations and influencer programs, media campaigns, PR plans, pitch calendars, and other media relations activities.
</li>
<li>Writes &amp; distributes press releases for events and organizational milestones.
</li>
<li>Updates and maintains crisis communications plan.
</li>
<li>Develops and maintains relationships with local and regional media and PR contacts.
</li>
<li>Maintains database (CRM) of media contacts, writers, editors and influencers.</li>
</ul>
<b>
Owned Media-</b><ul>
<li>Works with department team and contractors to formulate, assess and evolve content strategies, production, and calendars.
</li>
<li>Oversees the creation, acquisition, and production of creative assets.
</li>
<li>Works with content production team members and contractors to manage a strategic content calendar.
</li>
<li>Ensures proper tracking, data collection, and ROI reporting for content programs.
</li>
<li>Assists with managing website, social media, email marketing, SEO, CMS, and CRM contractors/services.</li>
</ul>
<b>
Integration and consistency with marketing programs:</b><ul>
<li>Works closely with staff and local tourism partners to ensure integrated branding, campaigns, communication and messaging.</li>
</ul>
<b>
International:</b><ul>
<li>Develops annual international travel trade sales and marketing programs.
</li>
<li>Assists with planning, scheduling, and hosting international travel trade FAMS.
</li>
<li>Seeks out, assesses, and books opportunities for international marketing and consumer communication, including contractors for writing/developing content and collateral.</li>
</ul>
<b>
Grant Management</b>:<ul>
<li>Writes, executes and tracks grant applications, awards, and programs.</li>
</ul>
</div>
<p></p>
<div><b>Qualifications</b></div>
<div>
<p><b>Knowledge, Skills &amp; Abilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Must be data-oriented with a passion for innovative applications.
</li>
<li>Knowledge of and experience in business intelligence, data analysis and visualization.
</li>
<li>Knowledge of and ability to use ad-tech and other marketing related software platforms.
</li>
<li>Strong analytical skills with experience in translating data into insights.
</li>
<li>Proficiency with data visualization tools and capabilities, such as Tableau or Power BI.
</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills with expertise in press writing and press relations.
</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Office (Outlook, Excel, Word, PowerPoint).
</li>
<li>Experience with photo and video design software. Acrobat Pro, Adobe Creative Suite â€“ Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign preferred.
</li>

</li>

</li>

</li>

</li>
<li>Proficiency in social media marketing, promotions, communications, analysis &amp; functionality.
</li>

Education and Experience:</b><ul>
