<h2>Advertising Director / Lead Multimedia Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>The Palestine Herald-Press seeks an accomplished hands-on advertising director/lead multimedia consultant to head its print and digital sales team serving the daily newspaper’s market in scenic east Texas.</p>
<p>The advertising director/ lead multimedia consultant develops and executes marketing strategies across multiple platforms to maximize sales and enhance our market share. This person works with the publisher and department managers to ensure profitability by meeting revenue and expense targets. We expect a manager who quickly spots trends and adjusts to maximize gains or minimize losses.</p>
<p>Candidates should have a background in sales management that includes strategic planning, analysis functions, budgeting and project management. Also essential are excellent organizational and communication skills, a creative approach to revenue development and a strong focus on customer service.</p>
<p>The Palestine Herald-Press is a five-day per week newspaper and seven days per week online. In addition to the newspaper, we publish a community magazine, a weekly TMC, digital products, numerous specialty publications and a website, www.palestineherald.com.</p>
<p>The Herald-Press is Texas’ second oldest newspaper at 170 and serves the scenic Anderson and Houston County areas located in the beautiful piney woods of east Texas. The area features a range of opportunities for the outdoor enthusiast and is located just 60 miles south of Tyler, Texas and 110 miles southeast of Dallas.</p>
<p>Interested candidates should send their resume and salary history to Publisher Jake Mienk.</p>
