<h2>AWS DevOps Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The <b>Software Engineer - Infrastructure</b> position is responsible for maintaining, enhancing, and growing applications developed by Konica Minolta's Solutions Engineering Center (SEC). Qualified candidates should have experience working with the latest versions of JavaScript (ES2015+), DevOps, and AWS services such as Lambda, S3, and DynamoDB. In addition to a strong technical background, the ideal candidate should show the ability to think critically, gather information, and problem solve in a team environment. This is a great opportunity for someone who wants to join a team that takes Agile development seriously. As a member of a small team, the successful candidate will be able to play an important role in the evolution of the Konica Minolta MarketPlace while working with the latest technologies.</p>
<p><b>
Competencies (Knowledge, Skills and Abilities)</b></p>
<ul><li>Work in a small development team to complete assigned tasks by the specified deadline using the Agile development methodology</li></ul>
<ul><li>Create specification documents for new features</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manage AWS infrastructure and configuration, including integration with GitLab CI/CD pipelines and Docker</li></ul>
<ul><li>Assist in the design, development, and deployment of new Lambda functions written in Node.js using the Serverless framework</li></ul>
<ul><li>Analyze, diagnose, and implement fixes for reported infrastructure issues</li></ul>
<ul><li>Schedule and execute system component upgrades</li></ul>
<ul><li>Monitor and evaluate system performance and identify and address potential bottlenecks</li></ul>
<ul><li>Expert-level knowledge in modern JavaScript (ES2015+) and Node.js</li></ul>
<ul><li>Expert-level knowledge of Git and Docker</li></ul>
<ul><li>Expert-level knowledge of AWS services, especially Lambda, DynamoDB, Elastic Beanstalk, EC2, and CloudFormation</li></ul>
<ul><li>Proficient on Linux/Unix operating systems from a terminal</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong written and verbal communication skills</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to work in a team environment</li></ul>
<ul><li>Self-motivating and self-learning</li></ul>
<p><b>
Experience, Educational Reqts and Certifications</b></p>
<ul><li>Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or a related degree concentration &amp; 10+ years of proven experience as a backend developer or infrastructure engineer</li></ul>
<ul><li>5+ years of proven experience working on DevOps with continuous deployment to AWS</li></ul>
<ul><li>Personal or Professional experience in Python and Go</li></ul>
<ul><li>Proficient with various web browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari)</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experience developing for multiple browsers and devices</li></ul>
<ul><li>Open source code you are proud of</li></ul>
