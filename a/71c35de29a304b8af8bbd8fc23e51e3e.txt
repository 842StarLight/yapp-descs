<h2>System Designer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<b>Summary</b> : The System Designer is responsible for:<br>
<ul>
<li>Producing drafted PV system design documents using on-site data</li>
<br>
<li>Providing engineering deliverables that meet contractual requirements for PV projects</li>
<br>
<li>Working with Operations Team and Engineering Subcontractors throughout the project life cycle to provide as accurate of a deliverable as possible</li>
</ul>
<br>
<br>
<b>Duties and Responsibilities of the System Designer</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Draft preliminary designs to support sales proposals and contracts using HelioScope, Aurora, or AutoCAD software</li>
<br>
<li>Use site information collected to design residential solar electric systems including roof mounts, fixed ground mounts, pole mounts and custom installations</li>
<br>
<li>Provide diagrams showing PV equipment and Point of Interconnection</li>
<br>
<li>Draft construction plans and other design exhibits using AutoCAD software</li>
<br>
<li>Select optimal equipment for site and customer-specific requirements</li>
<br>
<li>Provide Bill of Materials for Procurement and Construction Teams</li>
<br>
<li>Track design progress using workflow management platform</li>
<br>
<li>Other tasks as assigned</li>
<br>
</ul>
<br>
<b>The System Designer's Skills, Abilities and Background:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>BS in Engineering; or Associates Degree in related field</li>
<br>
<li>Experience with AutoCAD, Microsoft Office, Salesforce, HelioScope, and Aurora</li>
<br>
<li>Desire to learn in a fast-paced and results driven environment</li>
<br>
<li>Strong attention to detail with high standard for quality of deliverables</li>
<br>
<li>Willing to put in the time to reach goals</li>
<br>
<li>Ability to accept feedback and grow professionally</li>
<br>
<li>Sense of humor</li>
<br>
</ul>
