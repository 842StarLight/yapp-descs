<h2>Director of Marketing</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Director of Marketing for our Medical Division will be responsible for developing and executing strategic marketing plans for Vyne’s Medical Division in order to meet growth objectives and develop market awareness for the company. Works with company executives, sales leadership and product teams to develop and execute supporting programs to attract, win and retain clients; as well as working closely with industry partners and key customers to ensure successful brand direction and excellent industry relations. Working with key departmental resources, the Director will drive successful, cross-functional marketing initiatives and is responsible for the successful development, maintenance and reporting of key performance indicators to realize goal performance.<br>
</p>
<p><b>DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<div>
<ul>
<li>Lead the development of the overall marketing strategy for the Medical Division</li>
<li>Lead the Medical Marketing team in successful execution of marketing strategy and tactics</li>
<li>Act as the Medical Division’s primary Marketing liaison to the:</li>
<ul>
<li>Industry, trade and professional associations</li>
<li>Executive Leadership Team</li>
<li>Product Teams</li>
<li>Sales Teams</li>
</ul>
<li>Drive Medical Division brand strategy</li>
<li>Lead messaging and communications strategy for Medical brands and ensure consistency across the company</li>
<li>Design sales kit strategy and components, ensure effective training of customer facing staff in the use of these tools to “operationalize” use for consistency and effectiveness</li>
<li>Create and manage brand awareness strategy to maximize market awareness of all medical brands, to include website, trade shows and events, thought leadership initiatives, webinars, white papers, case studies, client quotes, and eCampaigns</li>
<li>Quantify and qualify impact of marketing efforts by establishing success metrics, measuring marketing efforts, and providing recommendations for changes in strategy based on marketing program performance</li>
<li>Assist Chief Development Officer and Sales Directors in Medical Division Partner strategy and management</li>
<li>Lead Medical Division Advisory Board strategy, selection, relations and management</li>
<li>Develop and maintain speakers list and reference list for brand</li>
<li>Work with product teams on pricing strategy, tools development, market forecasts</li>
<li>Facilitate the flow of information throughout the organization with a proactive internal communication program, including promoting the Company’s strategy and Core Values</li>
</ul>
</div>
<p><b>EDUCATION, KNOWLEDGE AND SKILLS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>4 Year College degree required; MBA a plus</li>
<li>8+ years in marketing and at least 4 years in Healthcare</li>
<li>A proven track record of developing and executing effective marketing strategies, campaigns, and plans with demonstrable results</li>
<li>Planning and executing integrated marketing programs from inception through completion and measurement of success</li>
<li>Competence in Marketing tools such as eMarketing systems, graphics software, presentation systems, demand generation tools and MS Office software</li>
<li>Superior interpersonal abilities and strong communications skills (oral and written) are required in order to work with a broad array of personnel across functions and at all levels within the company including sales, product development, and operations personnel</li>
</ul>
<p><b>SECURITY ROLES / RESPONSIBILITIES:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>This position may have access to and be responsible for securely administering user access to corporate applications.</li>
<li>This position may have access to and be responsible for the security of personal information including salary, insurance, reviews, resumes.</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
