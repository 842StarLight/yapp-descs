<h2>Attorney-Real Estate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Keystone Property Group</b></p>
<p>
Founded by William Glazer in 1991 as a real estate brokerage firm, Keystone Property Group (“KPG”) grew into an investment firm in 1993, to a development company in 1997, to a real estate private equity firm in 2003, and to a full-service real estate investment and development operating enterprise in 2014.</p>
<p>
KPG is actively involved in the acquisition and development of commercial office properties, facilities management, and fund management. These opportunities are pursued by each KPG regional team with the cooperation and support of other dedicated groups operating within the firm, including Finance and Accounting.</p>
<p>
KPG prides itself on the success of its transactions and relationships with lenders, investors and real estate partners. The successful candidate must be a skilled real estate attorney with significant experience representing institutional clients. The newly created role requires a market-minded attorney who can work independently but seeks collaboration with other team members in support of KPG’s business growth. The position requires an attorney with the ability to run deals from inception to closing while managing outside counsel, making decisions on legal issues and providing counsel to business teams.</p>
<p><b>
Principal Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Legal support for transactional work arising out of KPG, as well as all real estate transactions that may arise from strategic partnerships/joint ventures created by KPG</li>
<li>Negotiation of loan documents for complex real estate transactions, financings and refinancing</li>
<li>Creation and/or review of real estate investment structures</li>
<li>Creation and/or review of acquisition, investment, sale/purchase, financing, development, re-development and joint venture agreements</li>
<li>Management of outside counsel providing primary support and review of transactional documents</li>
<li>Establishment of forms and policies to facilitate KPG goals and objectives, while protecting the firm’s legal interest</li>
<li>Support and manage preparation of leases</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Required: JD and admission to the bar of Pennsylvania</li>
<li>Required: Ten + years’ experience with one or more major law firms in the real estate department (representation of lenders would be a plus)</li>
<li>Knowledge of Real estate joint ventures, Real estate acquisitions/dispositions, Advice concerning title and land use issues, Leasing, Construction &amp; permanent financing (representing the borrower) and Construction/development documentation</li>
<li>Experience acting as the primary attorney responsible for both drafting and negotiating transactional real estate documents including purchases, sales and financing</li>
<li>Experience in a real estate development or construction environment preferable</li>
<li>Able to assess legal risks in practical ways and to adjust quickly to ever-changing business needs and priorities</li>
<li> Knowledge of multiple real estate product types and partnership documents a plus</li>
<li>Able to delegate work to outside counsel, manage their costs, and hold that team accountable.</li>
<li>KPG is open to an arrangement that is part time and that permits some telecommuting</li>
</ul> <p>Keystone is an Equal Opportunity Employer</p>
