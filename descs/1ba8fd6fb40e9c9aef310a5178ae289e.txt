<h2>Senior Product Manager - Vulnerability Risk Management</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Your </b><b>Role</b><b>:</b></p>
<p>
Tenable is looking for a Senior Product Manager that will play a significant role in the company vision of helping organizations understand and reduce their Cyber Exposure. The Senior Product Manager will be responsible for conducting analysis on unmet and underserved customer needs in support of our nascent product offerings.<br>
<br>
This individual needs to be highly organized and adept at leading and influencing across cross-functional teams. S/he will provide indirect and informal leadership to other product managers as part of a larger, distributed team. This person should have a demonstrated track record of driving change informed through data, analysis, collaboration, and a strong understanding of the customer and their use cases. The ideal candidate should have excellent communication skills with the ability to drive outcomes across product, design, engineering, business, and enablement teams.</p>
<b>
Your Opportunity:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Drive the product strategy for our Cyber Exposure offerings through the software development life cycle</li>
<li>
Work closely with customers to understand their needs, prioritize features accordingly, and education customers on the vision of the solution.</li>
<li>
Proactively assess the market and align the long-term product roadmap accordingly.</li>
<li>
Continuously assess and highlight competitive differentiation to make and retain the product in a market leading position.</li>
<li>
Work closely with design and engineering to discuss, brainstorm, re-assess and re-align short- and long-term roadmaps based on customer feedback and market evolution.</li>
<li>
Participate in regular meetings with Sales, Marketing, Support, and other departments to align with customer needs and keep them informed of product execution, messaging, and direction.</li>
<li>
Assist with creation, maintenance, and dissemination of the product roadmap</li>
</ul>
<b>
What You'll Need:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Proven track record of strategy creation, development, alignment, execution, and delivery</li>
<li>
5+ years of experience building enterprise cybersecurity products, particularly threat and vulnerability management (TVM)</li>
<li>
5+ years of enterprise product management experience</li>
<li>
Strong oral, written, and presentation communication skills</li>
<li>
Strong project, time management, and team collaboration skills</li>
<li>
Light travel (~10%) to customers, company offices, security conferences, etc.</li>
</ul>
<b>
And Ideally:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Data Science experience</li>
<li>
Experience working with remote/distributed teams</li>
<li>
Broad knowledge of IT security markets and products</li>
<li>
Hands-on experience with enterprise security solutions and practices</li>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, or related (or equivalent experience)</li>
</ul>
