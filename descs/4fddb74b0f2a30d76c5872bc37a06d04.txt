<h2>Multimedia Content Writer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Academies of Math and Science – 2016 Arizona Charter School of the Year</b></p>
<p><b>For Today’s Scholars – Tomorrow’s Global Visionaries</b></p>
<p><b>Job Location: Tucson</b></p>
<p>We are looking for a master storyteller who loves to write and edit. We are a fast-paced environment that always strives for excellence and efficiency while making room for autonomy, new ideas, and creativity. As a member of the marketing team, you will read metrics and benchmarks that measure the impact of digital outreach, identify key metrics and audience trends, then use that data to alter strategies as needed. You’ll be challenged to make a measurable impact with your writing, to track results, to help write and edit blog posts, ebooks, datasheets, video scripts, and more. We need a creative mind to pitch new ideas and devise a content strategy that aligns with enrollment goals.</p>
<p><b>Our ideal candidate has:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>A bachelor’s degree (preferably in journalism, communications, marketing, or a related field)</li>
<li>Strong verbal and written communications skills</li>
<li>Experience with editing professional publications</li>
<li>Proficiency in Microsoft Outlook, Excel, PowerPoint, Photoshop, and general PC operations</li>
<li>Experience with posting and creating content on multiple social media platforms and analytics</li>
<li>Proficiency in research and an interest in learning technical material</li>
<li>Excellent command of written English</li>
<li>Deep knowledge of how target audiences consume content in 2019 and nuances of digital distribution</li>
<li>Experience with technical writing, copywriting, and multimedia</li>
<li>Experience with Adobe creative software, including Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign and Premiere Pro preferred but not required</li>
</ul>
<p><b>This job is for you if you are an expert in:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Publicizing programs, activities, and events through the production of news releases, publications, and multiple social media channels</li>
<li>Using messaging that is compelling, easy to understand, and in alignment across the current and future portfolio of programs, products, and services, ultimately driving adoption and expansion within current and new classrooms, schools, and entire districts</li>
<li>Developing and deploying compelling, creative content with specific objectives across different channels and segments; recognizing when to use visuals, images, and graphics to motivate the intended audience to engage, capturing both the heart and mind of the reader</li>
<li>Social media marketing and strategy as well as a thorough understanding of social network algorithms, especially Facebook</li>
<li>Sales funnels creation</li>
<li>A/B testing and tracking and reporting of the efficacy of marketing campaigns</li>
<li>Content marketing &amp; growth hacking techniques and distribution strategies</li>
</ul>
<p>Why work for us? We are the fastest-growing and one of the highest performing charter school networks in the state of Arizona serving low-income children. We transform the neighborhoods we move into through college-prep education. We have grown by 30% for each of the past few years and have our targets at expanding to multiple states over the next few years, becoming change agents in every neighborhood we serve.</p>
<p>This is your chance to get in on the ground floor.</p>
<p><b>Does this interest you?</b></p>
<p>If so, apply by submitting your resume and a cover letter that clearly demonstrate your outstanding success in all things marketing.</p>
<div>
<div><b>Compensation: $40,000-60,000/year, DOE.</b></div>
