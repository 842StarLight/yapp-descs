<p>Automotive Finance Managers help our customers arrange the financing of purchases and present additional products that enhance vehicle and ownership experience. They are are proficient at structuring deals for maximum profitability and collect ability.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Sell Insurance and Financing products to customers</li>
<li>Fully proficient with title laws and registration process</li>
<li>Maintains proficiency and certifications as required for the position</li>
<li>Accurately desks deals, submits deals to lenders for approval, makes credit decisions, and effectively closes deals</li>
<li>Ensures all deals are fully compliant with local, state and federal guidelines</li>
<li>Prepares paperwork and contracts, and delivers deals</li>

<li>Upsells vehicle service contracts and aftermarket items</li>
</ul>
