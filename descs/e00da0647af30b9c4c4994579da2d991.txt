<h2>Commissioning Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Hillphoenix is a leading manufacturer of Display Cases, Specialty Products, Refrigeration Systems, Power Systems and Comprehensive Services. The story of Hillphoenix is built on creativity and customer-centric innovation delivered by people invigorated by a strong sense of responsibility to help our customers win in their marketplaces. The relationships we build with our customers are as important to our success as the products we manufacture. This collaborative environment delivers FRESH THINKING, reassuring our customers that they choose the best when they choose to work with the people of Hillphoenix. Hillphoenix is part of the Refrigeration and Food Equipment segment of the Dover Corporation® (NYSE: DOV). Dover is a diversified global manufacturer with annual revenues in excess of $7 billion.</p>

<p><b>
Commissioning Specialist Job Purpose:</b></p>
<p>
The Commissioning Specialist oversees the planning, implementation, and tracking of a specific short-term/long term refrigeration Project (primarily, may include some additional trades). Knowledge of commercial and industrial refrigeration system service, startup, project management, work place safety, customer relations, bid development &amp; sales is required.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Commissioning Specialist Job Duties and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Understand the entire scope of the project.</li>
<li>
All communication amongst the team to foster an open environment of positive communication and teamwork to proactively solve issues and successfully execute the project scope.</li>
<li>
Determine the resources (time, money, equipment, etc) required to complete the project.</li>
<li>
Overseeing start-up projects and training others to do a correct start-up.</li>
<li>
Overseeing commissioning/re-commissioning programs so that we hand over a quality product to the customer.</li>
<li>
Assist in observing and inspecting construction to ensure adherence to specifications and contract documents.</li>
<li>
Provide energy efficiency advice to customers.</li>
<li>
Training internal associates about operation of systems presently used across the business.</li>
<li>
Evaluate and recommend alternatives to existing systems and processes.</li>
<li>
Develop and manage a cost budget to meet estimated margin</li>
<li>
Develop a schedule for project completion that effectively allocates the resources to the activities</li>
<li>
Review the quality of the work completed with the project team on a regular basis to ensure that it meets the project standards</li>
<li>
Enhance client relationship through proactive and positive communication, and by recognizing and delivering on client key issues and expectations.
</li>
<li>Travelling to jobs sites for job visits and documenting reports for customers and internal office team.</li>
<li>
Maintain ongoing client relationship and recognize future business opportunities.
</li>
<li>Updates job knowledge by participating in educational opportunities; reading professional publications; maintaining personal networks; participating in professional organizations.</li>
<li>
Enhances department and organization reputation by accepting ownership for accomplishing new and different requests; exploring opportunities to add value to job accomplishments.</li>
<li>
Be able to travel 70% to 80% of work time.</li>
<li>
Other duties and responsibilities as assigned.<br>
</li>
</ul>

This includes experience performing new system startups, servicing parallel rack systems, secondary cooling (glycol) systems and refrigerated supermarket cases.
