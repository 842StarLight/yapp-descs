<h2>Customer Success Coordinator - Healthcare IT Industry</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Job Description</b></p>
<p>ScriptDrop is looking for a Customer Success Coordinator (CSC) to provide inbound and outbound phone, email and text support for our pharmacy and courier partners. We are currently looking to staff evenings and weekends (see below); both full and part time positions are available. As the CSC, you will own the relationship with our pharmacy and courier partners, who are critical to the company’s network. You will also act as a liaison internally for all resources who interact with the customer (Sales, Projects, Support, Product, etc.).This role is the foundation to our organization and provides a diverse experience and potential to move up quickly. You will utilize an array of soft skills and technical aptitude to ensure we are delivering value to our customers, while helping the team solve problems to improve the product for the entire ecosystem. We are looking for a team player willing to wear different hats on a daily basis, thrives with changes and is driven by challenge. You are the voice of ScriptDrop to our customers, which is a powerful responsibility and will provide you with a great foundation in healthcare. You will be challenged, empowered and encouraged to grow with the company.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Serve as the lead point of contact for all customer support matters (inbound and outbound support via phone, email, chat and text)</li>
<li>Develop relationships with our customers including pharmacies, courier companies and pharma</li>
<li>Managing day-to-day courier and pharmacy operations including scheduling, communicating and problem solving any outstanding concerns</li>
<li>Communicate customer needs and objectives clearly and provide solutions to improve the product and services</li>
<li>Track key account metrics (e.g. daily deliveries, volume fluctuations, etc.)</li>
<li>Complete daily reconciliations of orders with our courier partners</li>
<li>Collaborate with leadership team to identify and grow opportunities within territory</li>
<li>Assist with challenging client requests or issue escalations as needed</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrable ability to communicate effectively through phone, email, chat and text interactions</li>
<li>Ability to think outside the box and create solutions and improvements for our partners with minimal guidance</li>
<li>Solid experience with MS Office, MS Excel and Powerpoint</li>
<li>Experience delivering client-focused solutions to customer needs while interacting with them</li>
<li>Proven ability to juggle multiple projects at a time, while maintaining sharp attention to detail and being able to pivot to next situation quickly</li>
<li>Excellent listening and presentation abilities</li>
<li>Strong verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to learn new products and services very quickly</li>
<li>Strong adaptability to our constant changing environment and business needs</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree or equivalent is required</li>
</ul>
