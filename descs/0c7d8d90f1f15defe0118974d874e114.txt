<h2>Small Business Account Executive</h2>

<div>Auth0’s mission is to help developers innovate faster. Every company is becoming a software company and developers are at the center of this shift. They need better tools and building blocks so they can stay focused on innovating. One of these building blocks is identity: authentication and authorization. That’s what we do. Our platform handles 2.5B logins per month for thousands of customers around the world. From indie makers to Fortune 500 companies, we can handle any use case. We like to think that we are helping make the internet safer.

<div>
The Small Business Account Executive acquires new accounts through direct sales of the companies core identity-as-a-service solution. This individual will be the lead point of contact for all customer interactions and related decisions. The role requires qualification skills, ability to manage the sales process to close, including technical assessment, and expert negotiation skills.</div>
</div>
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Responsibilities:</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>
Close business through proper solution assessment and management of customer interaction including technical assessment.</li>
<li>
Properly forecast monthly and quarterly revenue through attention to opportunity pipeline.</li>
<li>
Qualify inbound opportunities and prospect for new accounts.</li>
<li>
Create win-win sales proposals matching our subscribers' use cases.</li>
<li>
Manage sales steps through CRM and other systems.</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Skills and Abilities:</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>
1-2 years of SaaS sales experience carrying a quota AND hitting (or exceeding!) your number.</li>
<li>
Excellent listening skills and engaging personality.</li>
<li>
Strong negotiation skills with win-win mindset.</li>
<li>
Impeccable track record of quota attainment.</li>
<li>
Solid organization capabilities to manage multiple opportunities and customer requests</li>
</ul>
