<h2>Broker Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<b>AmWINS Brokerage</b> is looking for a <b>Broker Assistant</b> for our office in The Woodlands. The Broker Assistant will be responsible for providing support to to a seasoned Broker by preparing information for a variety of reports and documents, processing inquiries and forms and communicating with outside sources.<br>
<br>
The successful candidate will possess a vibrant personality, excellent communication skills, great motivation, capacity to understand concepts and drive to succeed in a team environment. As a Broker Assistant, you will have the opportunity to grow your career with one of the largest independent wholesale insurance brokers in the U.S. insurance industry, and work with industry leading Surplus Lines Carriers.<br>
</div>
<div><b>Broker Assistant duties include by not limited to:
</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Learn company programs and proprietary software</li>
<li>Checking and processing policies and endorsements</li>
<li>Providing status reports broker</li>
<li>Setting up online account folders</li>
<li>Preparing quotes, cover letters, binders, and invoices</li>
<li>Review carrier quotes and binders for consistency</li>
<li>Sorting and prioritizing work flow</li>
<li>Verifying accuracy of completed documents</li>
<li>Data entry into multiple systems</li>
<li>Learning special projects including loss summaries, etc.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Broker Assistant requirements:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 2 year of experience in an office environment</li>
<li>Excellent computer skills including typing proficiency, Microsoft Office proficiency with an emphasis in Excel spreadsheets</li>
<li>Strong written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Excellent attention to detail</li>
<li>High-level of motivation</li>
<li>Ability to prioritize important and pressing tasks</li>
<li>Innate organizational skills, willingness to take on challenges, and focus on personal responsibility</li>
<li>Property &amp; Casualty Insurance License; or must take offsite insurance course and obtain insurance license within a year of hire (expense paid by AmWINS)</li>
</ul>
<div>
<div><b>To the qualified candidate we offer:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Compensation includes a salary commensurate with experience</li>
<li>Position eligible for annual bonus</li>
<li>Full benefit package including: Medical, Dental, Life, Disability and 401(k)</li>
<li>Collaborative, continuing education focused work environment</li>
<li>Opportunity for advancement</li>
<li>Opportunity for formal sales and leadership training</li>
<li>Generous Paid Time Off (PTO)</li>
</ul>
<div>No direct inquiries please.</div>
