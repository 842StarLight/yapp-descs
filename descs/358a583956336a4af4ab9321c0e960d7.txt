<h2>Sales Communication Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Sales Communications Specialist develops, executes and manages the successful deployment of communication strategies and tactics to enable and maximize selling time for the sales organization. This role is responsible for all phases of the communications and creative process from developing strategic objectives and aligning key messaging to writing communication materials including web content, email bulletins, newsletters, brochures, fact sheets, blog posts, proposal content and measuring their effectiveness. This role also interacts with senior level management to assist in the development of communication plans based on sales needs and priorities. Additionally, this position will maintain and control the sales communications editorial calendar, provide key event management for sales conferences and meetings and benchmark best practices across the organization.</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
General Duties and Responsibilities</b></h2>
<p>
(This is a representative list of the general duties the position may be asked to perform, and is not intended to be all-inclusive)</p>
<ul>
<li>
Define sales communication project scope, goals and deliverables that support business objectives in collaboration with cross-functional teams
</li>
<li>Create, maintain and support communications and content development for executive level presentations, proposals, newsletters, sales messages, and video content</li>
<li>
Review communication requests and determine if communication is needed. If so, then provide optimal timing and best method for delivery, factoring in audience workload and previous communications sent
</li>
<li>Plan, develop and execute strategic, integrated communication programs in support of business activities including new product introductions, customer wins, sales meetings, recognition calls, events, etc.</li>
<li>
Read sales proposal content and create collateral to visually and succinctly tell a story
</li>
<li>Enhance the layout and appearance of sales communications to create visual stimulation to hook, hold and entice customers to take action</li>
<li>
Possess an eye for making communications of all types appealing to the end user</li>
<li>
Demonstrate an understanding of the sales support model and best practices</li>
<li>
Perform ongoing project management of sales initiatives
</li>
<li>Manage communications and incorporate content points from internal customers and subject matter experts into program deployments, respond to support needs and deliver ongoing executable solutions</li>
<li>
Manage day-to-day execution of proactive communications programs that support sales business priorities and growth
</li>
<li>Collaborate with business partners, including Internal Communications, on company-wide or enterprise wide initiatives as needed to proactively understand business needs prior to production to minimize last minute communications</li>
<li>
Draft and edit a wide range of written communication materials to ensure content is easily understood and free from errors, including but not limited to proposals, presentations and sales messages</li>
<li>
Ensure all communications and supporting tools are archived for easy retrieval</li>
<li>
Responsible for leading cross functional teams and ensuring all Sales field deployments maximize selling efficiencies</li>
<li>
All other duties as needed or required</li>
</ul>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Minimum Qualifications and Requirements</b></h2>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree in Journalism, Communications, Public Relations, Marketing or related field or equivalent experience</li>
<li>
5+ years’ experience developing and implementing communications plans and programs
</li>
<li>2+ years sales, sales support or customer-facing communications experience
</li>
<li>Advanced proficiency in Adobe Creative Suite (In Design, Illustrator Photoshop, etc.) and Microsoft Office</li>
<li>
Experience creating and managing a range of communications media including print, online, mobile and multimedia
</li>
<li>Exceptional writing and editing skills, as evidenced by past experience and writing samples</li>
<li>
Strong verbal communication skills as well as presentation skills</li>
<li>
Proven ability to deliver visually appealing action oriented communications of all types</li>
<li>
Excellent project management, problem solving skills and ability to prioritize and manage multiple tasks successfully</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to propose creative solutions to complex communication challenges using a variety of communication channels</li>
