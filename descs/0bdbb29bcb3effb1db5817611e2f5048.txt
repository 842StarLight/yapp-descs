<div><b>
Role Overview</b></div>
<div>
The Sales Development Representative (SDR) is responsible for prospecting and setting qualified appointments for the Account Executive team. The SDR will leverage cold calling, email, social networking, sales and lead generation tools, events, programs and various platforms, to identify and qualify prospects.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Responsibilities</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Set qualified sales appointments for Account Executives for further development and closure</li>
<li>
Daily outreach via phone, email or social networking / media to new prospects within the existing Salesforce database and maintaining active engagement with prospects until they are qualified</li>
<li>
Perform research on companies as a component of lead generation and qualification activities utilizing the web and other research tools available</li>
<li>
Accurately enter, update, and maintain daily activity and prospect information in Salesforce, including comprehensive and up-to-date client information</li>
<li>
Attend and engage prospects at networking events, IT meetups and other local programming opportunities</li>
<li>
Work with the Market Vice President to identify key targets within their assigned geographies for outreach campaigns</li>
<li>
Work collaboratively with our Demand Generation Team in Marketing to identify opportunities not currently within the Salesforce database</li>
<li>
Utilize social network tools to push Expedient news, press releases, and other public content for business development purposes</li>
<li>
Review analytics from monthly email campaigns and follow up on areas of opportunity</li>
<li>
Consistently achieve or exceed your monthly and quarterly performance targets for activity metrics and qualified appointments</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Required</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
1 – 2 years of prospecting experience in a B2B market, preferably in technology</li>
<li>
Bachelors' degree preferred but not necessary</li>
<li>
Proficiency with Salesforce, Microsoft Office and other sales tools</li>
<li>
Knowledge of social networking (LinkedIn, Twitter)</li>
<li>
Exceptional written and verbal communication skills; organized and detail oriented</li>
<li>
Must successfully pass pre-employment screens</li>
<li>
Must have a valid driver's license and provide proof of current personal automobile insurance to comply with company policy.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Qualifications</b></div>
<div>
A successful candidate will exhibit the following traits, skills and knowledge:</div>
<ul>
<li>
Natural ability to connect with people via phone and in person</li>
<li>
Ability to be persistent and answer objections</li>
<li>
Strong willingness to learn and adapt – turning training and feedback into action and self-improvement</li>
<li>
Good research skills and the ability to translate information into resonating statements</li>
<li>
Highly organized and timely with follow up</li>
</ul>
