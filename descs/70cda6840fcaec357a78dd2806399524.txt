<div>
<b>
What </b><b>we are looking for</b><br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>As a Software Engineering Manager, you will lead, manage, and inspire the software engineering team to provide effective solutions that meet or exceed customer expectations. The ideal candidate may also lead software development projects and contribute technically as needed in a fast-paced cross-functional design environment. This includes working collaboratively with other cross-functional engineering teams. The ideal candidate can provide valuable insight to support marketing and sales activities. The ideal candidate has a drive for excellence and desire to improve continuously by keeping current on new software technologies, design practices, and methodologies.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b><b> </b>include the following. Other duties may be assigned. To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Lead software teams responsible for software development from requirements through commercial launch, participating as an active development team member when needed and ensuring that effective software development methodologies are followed.</li>
<li>
Provides strategic and tactical solutions to complex technical or organizational problems</li>
<li>
Collaborates effectively within a broad cross functional organization</li>
<li>
Develops a team culture that consistently improves the quality and execution of project deliverables</li>
<li>
Develops technical leaders through mentorship, training, and leadership opportunities</li>
<li>
Manages resource allocation across multiple ongoing and upcoming projects</li>
<li>
Participates in project planning</li>
<li>
Actively monitors software development process and drives process improvements</li>
<li>
Participates in the analysis of new software technologies, design practices, and methodologies</li>
<li>
This is a supervisory role</li>
</ul>
