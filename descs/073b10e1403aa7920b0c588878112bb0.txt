<h2>Contract Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Requisition Number: </b><b>74783</b></p>
<p>
As a Contract Specialist for Insight, you will enter a high-energy and tech savvy world. We are looking for someone who is a team player, an excellent communicator and has exemplary organizational and technical writing skills.</p>
<p>
As a Contract Specialist, you can expect to:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Multi-task – You will support various Services contracts and Statements of Work (SOWs) to ensure all the required information is in place and accurate. You will be interacting with people at all levels of the company including Sales, Technical and Services Organizations and Business Units.</li>
<li>
Take Ownership – You will develop a practical knowledge of Insight’s offerings including Hardware, Software, and Services. You will manage the overall process for your documents, ensuring we meet deadlines.</li>
<li>
Be Challenged – Your job is never done as a Contract Specialist. This role requires the critical thinking ability to produce a customized document for a particular client when predefined processes and templates will not work.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
What we are looking for:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Prior SOW development, contract administration and/or technical writing experience.</li>
<li>
Must have strong organization and time management skills.</li>
<li>
Must be able to multi-task and manage multiple contract implementations simultaneously.</li>
<li>
Able to apply writing skills to communicate complex concepts in clear, concise language.</li>
<li>
Proficiency in Microsoft Office including Word, Excel, Outlook, and PowerPoint.</li>
<li>
A Bachelor’s Degree in a business-related field preferred.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Duties include:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Creating documents, ensuring appropriate templates are used (ex: SOW vs. Standalone) and all required agreements are in place.</li>
<li>
Proofreading and editing all content, ensuring language is clear and concise, and meets Insight requirements.</li>
<li>
Creating pursuit timeline emails to communicate clear deadlines to all participants.</li>
<li>
Following up and managing the workflows/creation of documents, escalating when necessary to meet deadlines.</li>
<li>
Obtaining all necessary internal approvals.</li>
<li>
Working independently and with little supervision, managing work assignments in workflow tool, and addressing any concerns/requests by the deadlines.</li>
<li>
Applying critical thinking to input, determining best approach for each document</li>
</ul>
