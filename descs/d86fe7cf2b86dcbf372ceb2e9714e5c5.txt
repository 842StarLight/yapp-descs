<h2>Analyst, Marketing and Valuation</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><i>CAE is seeking a Marketing and Valuation Analyst to play a critical role within our global physical commodities trading platform. This is an entry level position at a company with a demonstrated history of promoting from within. The Marketing and Valuation Analyst will be responsible for maintaining the quality of the firm’s marketing processes in accordance with the firm’s objectives. This includes both written text and numerical analysis, as it pertains to pricing and valuation of semiconductor capital assets on the secondary market. This individual should be comfortable analyzing and drawing conclusions from large data sets and graphs. The Marketing and Valuation Analyst will work closely with many members of the team across various departments and will report to the Vice President of Sales and Trading.</i></p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Analyze daily marketing plans, processes, and campaigns of the firm’s Trading Team.</li>
<li>Audit marketing campaigns to ensure conformity with the firm’s objectives.</li>
<li>Perform pricing and valuation analysis of marketed assets / requirements against the firm’s propriety valuation software and data sets.</li>
<li>Edit and improve the marketing language used in individual Traders’ marketing campaigns.</li>
<li>Track marketing campaigns firmwide and generate reports for Management.</li>
<li>Efficiently manage and prioritize workflow with minimal oversight.</li>
<li>Coordinate between multiple departments (Trading, Data Acquisition &amp; Analytics, Software Developing) to optimize marketing efficiency and effectiveness.</li>
<li>Assist the Data Acquisition &amp; Analytics Department in maintaining data integrity and accuracy.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications/Experience:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree required; Master’s Degree in Economics or similar field preferred but not required</li>
<li>0-2 years’ work experience</li>
<li>Forensic attention to detail</li>
<li>Advanced proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite strongly preferred</li>
<li>Proficiency with database systems and CRM software</li>
<li>Ability to learn quickly, self-start, and demonstrate versatility in a dynamic environment</li>
<li>Strong written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Advanced proficiency in editing text for grammatical errors, structure, and tone</li>
<li>Advanced proficiency analyzing and drawing conclusions from data sets and graphs</li>
<li>Experience and/or familiarity with the semiconductor industry is NOT required</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Compensation and Benefits Include:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Competitive base salary and bonus program</li>
<li>Full Medical, Vision, Dental insurance</li>
<li>401k program</li>
<li>Gym membership</li>
</ul>
