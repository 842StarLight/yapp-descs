<p><b>Position Overview:
</b></p>
<p>This position is primarily responsible for managing the compliance of the TPM broker-dealer. The fully licensed supervisory incumbent interacts with the TPM to provide oversight of licensing and sales practices regarding retail non-deposit products. The incumbent reviews, tracks and monitors the broker and dealer compliance program, tests and documents wealth management compliance and compiles management reports supporting required committees and oversight. The incumbent works with the TPM and program management to resolve any required client or security inquiry or complaint.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Primary Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<p>Communicates with the brokerage program provider to monitor compliance program status, outstanding issues for resolution and remediation of any outstanding issues. Communicates brokerage program compliance status, information, changes and inquiries to appropriate parties. Communication with the brokerage program provider includes the onboarding of licensed bankers and Financial Consultants.
</p>
<p>Coordinates internal needs and status of the brokerage compliance program. Assists the Manager of Wealth Management Compliance, compliance management and lines of business leaders in the development of policy and procedure manuals.
</p>
<p>Interacts with third party brokers and dealers to understand and assist with the implementation of changes to internal policies and procedures as needed. Elevates unique requests and issues to management. Monitors third party brokers and dealers for compliance issues and risk indicators via reporting protocol and monitors their compliance programs for reporting and responsiveness.
</p>
<p>Prepares or assists in preparation of data and compliance reporting for management, committee and board presentations. Monitors lines of business action plans regarding remediation of identified compliance and risk issues. Completes compliance functions and other duties as assigned by the Manager of Wealth Management Compliance.
</p>
<p>Coordinates information requested by internal and external partners and examiners. Monitors completion of TPM audit and examination program.
</p>
<p>Performs other related duties and projects as assigned.
</p>
<p>All employees have the responsibility and the accountability to serve as risk managers for their businesses by understanding, reporting, responding to, managing and monitoring the risk they encounter daily as required by F.N.B. Corporationâ€™s risk management program.
</p>
