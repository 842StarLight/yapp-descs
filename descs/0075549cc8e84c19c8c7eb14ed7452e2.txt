<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>The Role
</b></h2>As the market leader in software solutions for the design, operation and maintenance of process industries, we are seeking a high energy technically oriented professional to work marketer to work as a Product Marketing Specialist for the Engineering, Chemicals business. This global position has primary “go-to-market” responsibility for market campaign strategy, packaging, positioning, messaging, and the commercial success of our solutions serving the Engineering chemicals vertical segment.
<br>
<br>
The Senior Product Marketing Specialist will assist in developing and implementing the “go-to-market” activities for the Engineering Chemicals business and collaborates closely with globally distributed cross-functional teams in Field Marketing, Sales, Product Management and other internal and external functions to drive adoption of AspenTech’s ENG applications. The right candidate will be a quick learner and able to absorb industry and product knowledge rapidly to make immediate contributions to the organization.
