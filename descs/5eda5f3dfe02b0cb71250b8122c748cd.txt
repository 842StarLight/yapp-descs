<h2>Solution Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Our Solution Engineering team is responsible for providing the technical leadership throughout the sales process. This is a highly dynamic, customer-facing, and hands-on role within the Solution Engineering team where you will partner with the account management team on revenue generating opportunities.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b><i>Responsibilities:
</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Partner with sales representatives, solution engineering and services to drive revenue for Flexera’s software across the Corporate NAM territories
</li>
<li>Provide pre-sales technical support for the sales team and sales process via telephone, email, webinars, and face-to-face communication
</li>
<li>Produce and deliver customer demonstrations, technical presentations and proof of concept activities
</li>
<li>Collaborate with the sales teams to determine the best solutions and present the capabilities of Flexera’s technology, support, and value
</li>
<li>Understand and communicate the value proposition and competitive advantages of Flexera solutions
</li>
<li>Demonstrate excellent customer service skills through all levels of communication
</li>
<li>Contribute to RFI/RFP responses as the technical account lead
</li>
<li>Provide insight from the field back to product management and engineering
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b><i>Qualifications, Experience and Requirements:
</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Presales Solution Engineering background in one or more of these areas:
</li>
<li>Cloud Optimization and Management
</li>
<li>Data normalization for IT Asset Management
</li>
<li>Software license optimization
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b><i>Competencies:
</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Highly technical and analytical with minimum 2+ years technical experience
</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills
</li>
<li>Masterful at presenting over the phone, in person, and via web conferencing
</li>
<li>Knowledge and/or pre-sales experience with Software Asset Management / IT Asset Management (SAM/ITAM) is a plus
</li>
<li>Ability to map customer’s high-level business problems to our product solution and value
</li>
<li>Hands-on experience with Amazon Web Services, Microsoft Azure, Google Cloud
</li>
<li>Platform, or other IaaS cloud service provider is strongly desired
</li>
<li>Ability to vet and propose cloud computing architectures and strategies is strongly desired
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b><i>Other Requirements:
</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>2+ years of customer-facing Presales/Solution Engineer experience
</li>
<li>Strong background in demonstrations, proof of concept development and delivery, and business presentations
</li>
<li>Ability to travel at least 25% of the time
</li>
<li>B.S./B.A. is required; technical background in CS or MIS is strongly preferred</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
