<h2>Customer Success Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Customer Success Manager along with the Associate Director runs a multidisciplinary team that is responsible for ensuring that our customers receive maximum value from L2TMedia products and services. The Manager will drive the revenue growth and satisfaction of their assigned accounts, and will manage and develop junior team members, establishing a culture of continuous improvement and customer service excellence. Whether advising clients on best practices, offering category specific ideas or consulting on how to best leverage our platform to achieve business goals, success will be recognized through building long-term customer relationships and growing the business organically.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
PRINCIPAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<p>
The Customer Success Manager is responsible for, but not limited to, the following tasks:</p>
<ul>
<li>Ensure Customer Success Team identifies and develops multi-level relationships with key client decision makers and influencers to ensure retention and relationship stability.</li>
<li>Grow customer book of business to target vs. prior year, driven by existing revenue retention and closing new business through cross-sell and upsell approaches.</li>
<li>Understand each client’s organizational dynamics and probe to discover opportunities to further support their business goals by leveraging L2T’s full suite of products and services.</li>
<li>Manage team workflow to ensure that regular client deliverables are high quality and delivered on-time.</li>
<li>Develop structure, professional skills and advancement of career growth within the Account Strategist team.</li>
<li>Coach and develop team members by accurately accessing their strengths and development needs, giving timely and specific feedback.</li>
<li>Maintain and improve a level of product or service knowledge by collaborating with cross-functional teams across disciplines such as Field Sales, Product Delivery, Compliance and Finance and ensuring effective cross training and mentoring.</li>
<li>Drive a culture of continuous improvement by establishing and maintaining a high performing team where new ideas and solutions are encouraged, evaluated, quantified and implemented.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>KNOWLEDGE, SKILL AND EXPERIENCE REQUIREMENTS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>5+ years of business development/account management experience with an expertise in consultative selling methods</li>
<li>3 years of work experience in Advertising, Media, Digital Marketing or a related area</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to lead and delegate to staff both in and out of own department and get results through others</li>
<li>Experience identifying opportunities for process improvement and retraining team members</li>
<li>Proven time management skills in an environment where you may be managing several customer engagements simultaneously</li>
<li>Ability to think and act strategically as well as tactically to execute against your customers’ business goals</li>
<li>Innovation-minded and decisive, with a strong results orientation</li>
<li>Must be a quick learner, self-starter, and have the ability to work with minimal supervision in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills with the ability to convey complex ideas to non-technical parties</li>
<li>Strong analytical and problem solving skills</li>
<li>High knowledge bandwidth and intellectual curiosity</li>
<li>Experience with CRM systems (ideally Salesforce), Google Apps, and Microsoft Office, in particular Excel and PowerPoint</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree from an accredited university</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p>In addition to the above, ideal candidates will possess one or more of the following:</p>
<ul>
<li>Experience working within the automotive industry</li>
<li>Experience in a role with direct senior customer interaction</li>
<li>Certification in Google AdWords</li>
<li>Experience with Facebook and other social media platforms</li>
<br>
</ul>
