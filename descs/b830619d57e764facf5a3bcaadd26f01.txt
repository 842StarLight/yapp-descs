<h2>Management Systems Analyst - Accounting</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div><div><div>
<p>Seattle Parks and Recreation (SPR) is a national leader and innovator in the services and programs we provide to our residents who enjoy our public spaces such as parks, community centers, specialty gardens and swimming pools. Our mission is providing welcoming and safe opportunities to play, learn, contemplate and build community and promote responsible stewardship of the land. We promote healthy people, a healthy environment and strong communities. We strive to deliver excellent customer service to each visitor and participant. SPR is also deeply committed to advancing the City of Seattle's Race and Social Justice Initiative (RSJI) by working to end institutional and structural racism.</p>
<p>
SPR Accounting group provides accounting, financial reporting, and payroll services department-wide and is seeking to hire a part-time (80%) Accounting Management Systems Analyst (MSA) to join the team. This position will serve in a critical role performing technical liaison between users of various Point of Sale Systems (POS), Accounting, and Citywide Treasury Operations.</p>
<p>
SPR uses POS systems to transact business with the public. Community centers, pools, event scheduling, athletic scheduling and a few other operations are currently using the ActiveNet system, while golf uses a separate POS that integrates into STRATAGO.</p>
<p></p>
<h5 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Responsibilities:</b></h5>
<p>The Management Systems Analyst will be the Accounting subject matter expert for POS integration into the Citywide Financial System (People Soft 9.2. In this role, the MSA will interface with Department staff, as required, to design and prepare upload files, and ensure that these are accurately reflected in People Soft. The MSA produces reports that verify the accuracy of the data and investigates/resolves any issues and maintains work flows and processes.<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities include:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Perform duties as Subject Matter Expert (SME) for POS systems that upload into or interface with City of Seattle financial systems (People Soft 9.2).
</li>
<li>Prepare, review, analyze and submit daily upload files to Treasury for transactions originating in ActiveNet, and Stratago.
</li>
<li>Monitor and review files uploaded in People Soft 9.2 for timeliness and accuracy.
</li>
<li>Design and coordinate tools and processes to streamline operations.
</li>
<li>Prepare, distribute, and explain analytical reports that reconcile the various systems.
</li>
<li>Interface with operations staff to troubleshoot issues and resolve discrepancies.
</li>
<li>Develop testing protocols and implement same to confirm that the financial reporting elements of operational systems are working as required and/or designed.
</li>
<li>Develop and maintain detailed work flows and processes for all elements of operational systems that interface with People Soft 9.2.
</li>
<li>Interface with users of POS systems to ensure timely and accurate processing of refunds, payments, fee reductions, scholarships, and adjustments.
</li>
<li>Interface with Treasury to develop and maintain allocation codes as required
</li>
<li>Reconciliations between bank statements and POS systems.
</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
