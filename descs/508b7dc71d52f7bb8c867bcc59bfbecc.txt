Compensation:</b><p>
$60,000+ at plan commission (based on experience)</p>
<b>
Responsibilities:</b><p>
As an Inbound Lead Generation Specialist at Dave Friedman Team, you tenaciously prospect for, qualify and follow up with seller and buyer leads. This includes:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Conduct 300+ inbound and outbound calls per day to source and qualify prospective buyers and sellers.</li>
<li>
Quickly respond to all inbound leads sourced from several of our marketing efforts, including radio/print advertising, online leads, social media, client referrals, and more.</li>
<li>
Transfer qualified leads to the appropriate Listing and/or Buying Specialists for follow-up and face-to-face appointments.</li>
<li>
Measure and track your daily lead generation activity against weekly, monthly, quarterly and annual production goals.</li>
<li>
Document all customer interactions, follow up activity and buying decisions in our CRM system.</li>
<li>
Use a variety of communication mechanisms including calling, texting and emailing to generate new business.</li>
<li>
Build and manage our qualified leads through the sales pipeline.</li>
<li>
Act as an integral part of the entire real estate team serving our clients in the Charleston market.</li>
</ul>
<b>
Qualifications:</b><ul>
<li>
Career-minded individuals who are highly motivated and looking to expand their skills to achieve both professional and personal growth.</li>
<li>
2-3 years professional experience on a sales team (new business development, field sales, inbound/outbound sales, account management) is desired but not required.</li>
<li>
High-volume Call Center experience in a tele sales or customer service role is also desired but not required.</li>
<li>
Baseline knowledge and proficiency using CRM systems and technologies for account/contact management. Must be highly adaptable to leveraging technology in the work environment.</li>
<li>
Candidate must be team oriented, learning based, possessing sheer grit and the will to succeed.</li>
<li>
Real estate experience or licensing is not required.</li>
<li>
We provide training, coaching and an abundance of resources to help you achieve your professional and personal goals.</li>
<li>
College graduate or equivalent.</li>
</ul>
