Job Summary:<br>
Provide product technical advice and warranty support to Club Car sales offices, branches, and dealer/distributor representatives. Handle/Resolve incoming inquiries via phone calls, emails, or site visits in a timely and professional manner. Provide any needed support to end customer, such as training in repair and maintenance processes.<br>
<br>
Responsibilities:<br>
1. Set strategic vision for channel technical development/training<br>
2. Develop entry and advanced level training for new and existing product releases<br>
3. Develop and Deploy FSR role/responsibilities<br>
4. Develop training of FSRs soft skills and SME knowledge<br>
5. Develop assessment tool and benchmarks to assess FSR skill sets<br>
6. Create assessment model, and evaluate FSR skill sets per select criteria<br>
7. Develop tiered FSR model based upon expertise, knowledge, and experience<br>
8. Update knowledge base of FSR and channel materials<br>
9. Assist in New FSR Orientations and Technical conferences as needed.<br>
10. Participate in product review meetings.<br>
11. Work with video photographer in the development of technical videos to support new products, technical troubleshooting, and kit installation.<br>
12. Visit dealer/distributor facilities to evaluate technical expertise, quality of service, assess maintenance abilities, etc.<br>
13. Create and publish an annual training schedule for dealers and distributors.<br>
14. Set-up and provide regional schools. Assist those in ESA, Latin America and Asia Pacific with Technical Training needs.<br>
15. Provide structured feedback on issues that arise and engage with engineering to drive resolution.<br>
16. Participate in product review meetings.<br>
17. Assist in yearly planning and budgeting of all aspects of the Technical Training dept.
<br>
18. Manage Training expenses and ensure revenue goals are meet/exceeded yearly.<br>
19. Participate in product review meetings to provide input on practical use, training, and budget requirements.<br>
20. Work with outsourced OEM's to learn and access training materials and product knowledge to ensure Club Car employees and channel partners are educated to the proper levels to service outsourced OEM's product.
<br>
21. Provide at least two Factory/Augusta based classes per year for channel and major accounts.<br>
<br>
