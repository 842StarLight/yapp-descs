<p><b>POSITION PURPOSE</b></p>
<p>This individual is responsible for managing all administrative activities as they relate to Bona’s eLearning development efforts and site platforms; including individual and team registration, site management and usage assistance, analytics, and project coordination while working independently and with the US and global eLearning teams. In addition, this person will help research, develop, implement, maintain and manage the eLearning modules, courses, learning paths, training videos, certification and badging programs for the use of Bona colleagues, Certified Craftsmen, other contractors, distributor personnel, the A&amp;D community and consumers. This individual will be part of the U.S. and global eLearning teams, supporting Bona’s annual strategic objectives, while contributing to a positive team and company culture.</p>
<p><b>POSITION QUALIFICATIONS AND JOB DUTIES</b></p>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS AND EDUCATION REQUIREMENTS: </b></p>
<p>1. Bachelors or Associate degree preferred; High School Diploma / GED required</p>
<p>2. Must be proficient with Microsoft office programs</p>
<p>3. Must be a self-starter, creative and able to work with minimal supervision.</p>
<p>4. Ability to multi-task</p>
<p><b>JOB DUTIES: </b></p>
<p>1. Site administrator for BonaTraining.com (SAP Litmos platform); team/individual set-up, site assistance, communication, course launches and communications, compliance, and analytics</p>
<p>2. Complete mass user uploads through Excel and Litmos/Acclaim platform functionality</p>
<p>3. Coordinate course projects for U.S. and global teams</p>
<p>4. Review course modules for content and functionality</p>
<p>5. Help create content/guidelines/tutorials to increase site usage and understanding</p>
<p>6. Create course reference materials (Key Take-Aways, Best Practices, etc) with Word and design software; edit and publish as pdf’s</p>
<p>7. Become proficient with eLearning authoring and design tools (Articulate Storyline, InDesign, etc); while increasing knowledge of adult learning theory and eLearning best practices</p>
<p>8. Collaborate with product management, marketing, and sales personnel to create needed content</p>
<p>9. Update course modules and content as products/processes change</p>
<p>10. Create and launch marketing communications to help draw users back into system</p>
<p>11. Site administrator for Bona badging site (Acclaim); badge set-up, issuance, communication, analytics</p>
<p>12. Participate in hands-on Bona Training events to become knowledgeable about Bona products and processes</p>
<p>13. Perform same duties above for global eLearning efforts with EMEA and APAC team</p>
<p>14. Serve as Mindtouch (knowledge base) coordinator and authoring expert</p>
<p>15. Ensure system security and confidentiality of sensitive and or protected content and materials</p>
<p>16. Able and willing to work necessary hours to meet all project deadlines, travel when necessary.</p>
<p>17. Other Duties- Please note this job description is not designed to cover or contain a comprehensive listing of activities, duties or responsibilities that are required of the employee for this job. Duties, responsibilities and activities may change at any time with or without notice.</p>
