<div><b>Key Responsibilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Works with customer or company released drawings and/or CAD models, engineering BOMs, and other engineering specifications to develop, implement and improve manufacturing processes.</li>
<li>Develops and documents the manufacturing process in detail with an emphasis on visual media, ease of training, and standardized format.</li>
<li>Develops methods for inspection, testing, sampling, and training. Identifies tooling requirements and coordinates the manufacturing of tools/fixture.</li>
<li>Manage the process flow through the Quality Clinic</li>
<li>Review Clinic data to identify root cause and corrective actions along with disposition of internal non-conformance's in Epicor (ERP).</li>
<li>Utilize the Clinic team and the appropriate team members to drive root cause and corrective actions</li>
<li>Manage the Clinic Data and communicate the success stories to management site wide.</li>
<li>Completes any other projects or tasks as assigned by their direct supervisor.</li>
</ul>

<li>CAD software experience (Unigraphics preferred, will consider others)</li>
<li>Aerospace experience a plus</li>
</ul>
