<b>Your Role and Responsibilities</b><br>
<p>IBM is seeking professional positions to work within an agile team environment. This job will be based in the respective markets, focused on tangible achievements through a vast array of challenging opportunities. Those considering these positions should be able to handle / assist/ support different internal and external stakeholders.<br>
<br>
The professional will work as a team member in an agile squad supporting sales on Q2C processes, including: Contract Management, Billing, Disputes, AR.<br>
<br>
They will serve as the key contact person specially for sales team to interlock with Q2C different teams. Organizational and interpersonal skills also leadership operating as a team player, and willingness to work under tight deadlines, are additional requirements.</p>

IBM will not be providing visa sponsorship for these positions now or in the future. Therefore, in order to be considered for this position, you must have the ability to work without a need for current or future visa sponsorship<br>
