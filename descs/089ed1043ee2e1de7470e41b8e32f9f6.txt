<h2>District Manager</h2>

<p><b>Airgas offers a full benefits package that includes: Medical, Dental, Vision, Life, AD&amp;D, Short Term and Long Term Disability Insurance, Vacation, Sick, Paid Holidays, 401(k) Retirement Plan with company match, Tuition Assistance and much more!</b></p>
<p>Discover an exciting Career with Airgas! Airgas USA, LLC, an Air Liquide company, is the nation’s leading single-source supplier of gases, welding, and safety products. Known locally nationwide, our distribution network serves more than one million customers of all sizes with a broad offering of top-quality products and unmatched expertise. Airgas’ customer base includes but is not limited to: manufacturing, health care, construction, energy, hospitality, food service, and so on…</p>
<p><b>JOB SUMMARY: </b></p>
<p>The District Manager position is directly responsible for the overall management and sales activities of industrial, medical, and specialty gases and welding supplies in assigned district; overall responsibility for the critical issues of branch operation and profitability, including sales, sales growth, budgeting, gross margins, operating expenses, P &amp; L responsibilities, safety, customer service, inventory, equipment, building and vehicles, community relations, accounts receivable and resolution of personnel issues, while complying with all regulatory agencies and requirements including, but not limited to OSHA and FDA, and Airgas policies and procedures at the facility.</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND ACCOUNTABILITIES</b>:</p>
<p><b><i>Safety and Regulatory Compliance</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrates and ensures staff alignment with Airgas Safety Pledge and 12 Live Saving Rules.</li>
<li>Exemplifies, promotes and develops safe work behaviors. Provides direct coaching to improve associates safety behaviors resulting in an improved safety culture.</li>
<li>Performs safety incident investigations recommending corrective actions to eliminate recurrences.</li>
<li>Develops training tools to support Airgas’s safety, regulatory, continuous improvement and operational efficiency initiatives.</li>
<li>Ensures all applicable regulatory (OSHA, FDA, DOT, EPA) requirements are met and applicable training and recordkeeping is performed to meet departmental and location deadlines.</li>
<li>Ensures compliance with all Specialty, Medical and Food Gas Standard Operating Procedures and ISO requirements.</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>Personnel / Personnel Development</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provides supervision of direct reports, in accordance with the organization’s policies, procedures and applicable laws</li>
<li>Ensures sufficient operations staffing levels; works with the Human Resources Department to develop recruitment, staff development and retention strategies</li>
<li>Accountabilities include interviewing, hiring, training associates, planning, assigning and directing work; appraising and developing performance; rewarding and disciplining associates; addressing complaints and resolving problems</li>
<li>Ensures subordinate matters/concerns are effectively handled.</li>
<li>Consults with Human Resources regarding personnel issues including excessive absences and performance management.</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>Customer Service / Business Development</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ensures all customers receive quality customer service from company associates.</li>
<li>Ensures customer inquiries are promptly and courteously handled, providing follow-up to ensure complete customer satisfaction.</li>
<li>Identifies, prioritizes and penetrates target accounts.</li>
<li>Maintains existing accounts and develops new business with existing accounts.</li>
<li>Assist outside sales representatives in all areas, including but not limited to preparing quotes, maintaining all literature required, providing customer follow-up, and gathering sales leads.</li>
<li>Oversees completion of sales-related paperwork (shippers, invoices, cylinder audits, month end reports, etc.) in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Coordinates sales promotion activities and prepares or directs preparation of merchandise displays.</li>
<li>Prepares market and competitor information, annual sales analysis and forecast for the Area Vice President.</li>
<li>Maintains / upgrades selling skills and technical/product knowledge.</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>Business Support</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Coordinates sales distribution by establishing sales territories, quotas, and goals and advises branch managers, distributors, vendors, and customers concerning sales and advertising techniques.</li>
<li>Reviews market analysis to determine customer needs’, volume potential, price schedules, and discount rates; develops sales campaigns to accommodate goals of the company.</li>
<li>Serve as liaison between sales department and other sales-related units.</li>
<li>Relates gross profit dollars and margins to gross profit requirements and budget; relates actual net income to budget.</li>
<li>Reviews actual gross profit to guidelines, as evidenced by monthly reports.</li>
<li>Regularly reviews operational expenses with the ability to explain variances and recommend appropriate actions; prepares/executes budget optimization.</li>
<li>Adheres to a sustainable process for operational files and records; completes all required paperwork accurately, timely and in compliance with various company policies and procedures, to ensure full regulatory compliance.</li>
<li>Continuous improvement of all applicable safety, environmental, quality and compliance programs</li>
</ul>
