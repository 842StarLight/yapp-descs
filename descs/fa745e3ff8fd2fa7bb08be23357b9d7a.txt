<h2>Business Intelligence Developer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>The Business Intelligence Developer is responsible for designing, developing and creating data visualizations to support the needs of Sales, Marketing, Supply Chain and Finance areas. The Business Intelligence Developer is an excellent communicator responsible for aggregating data from multiple sources and presenting data visualizations to a variety of audiences, including marketing, sales, operations, finance and others.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Build, iterate, and validation of predictive models using multiple statistical techniques</li>
<li>
Translate business needs to technical specifications</li>
<li>
Design, build, deploy and present BI solutions (e.g. PowerBI, Tableau, etc.)</li>
<li>
Develop and execute database queries and conduct data analyses</li>
<li>
Create visualizations and reports for requested projects</li>
<li>
Develop and update technical and end user training documentation</li>
<li>
Designing, developing and maintaining business intelligence solutions</li>
<li>
Conduct system testing and troubleshooting</li>
<li>
Collaborate with development teams and automate, test and maintain solutions</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Key Performance Measures</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Impact on business performance due to delivery of strategic systems solutions</li>
<li>
Leveraging business intelligence tools and dashboards across the enterprise</li>
<li>
Performance to budget and performance to plan of strategic system solutions</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
