<h2>Account Manager/Inside Sales Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>The Company</b></p>
<p>Halfpenny Technologies (an Accumen Inc Company) is a leading provider of healthcare clinical data exchange solutions. Through its proprietary Integration Technology Framework (ITF-Hub), Halfpenny Technologies enables the exchange of clinical data such as medical record, lab, radiology and admission/discharge/transfer (ADT) data between disparate technologies within the healthcare environement, including physician practices, laboratories, hospitals, pharmacies, analytics/care management vendors, ACO’s, CIN’s, and health plans.</p>
<p>Visit us online at www.halfpenny.com or www.accumen.com to learn more about us.</p>
<p>We are actively searching for an Account Manager/Inside Sales Representative <b>for our Blue Bell, PA</b> location</p>
<p><b>Summary: </b></p>
<p>Reporting to Sr. Vice President of Sales, the Account Manager/Inside Sales Representative will interact daily with both current and prospective Halfpenny Technology Inc. (HTI) customers to build relationships, solve problems, and create an environment of client satisfaction and revenue generation. The primary responsibility of the Account Manager/Inside Sales Representative is to use key skills such as client communication at all levels of a client, expertise in HTI’s solutions and how they solve clients’ needs, and active listening to culture and harvest new business opportunities from existing customer in key markets.</p>
<p>You will have a creative approach to your work, seeking to create and refine processes. In daily interactions with clients, you will combine intellectual curiosity and tenacity to advance projects to exceed expectations. Additionally, you will work with the leaders of our Health Plan, Health System and Strategic Lab markets, as well as operations and marketing, to identify, participate-in, and help close new sales opportunities.</p>
<p><b>Duties and Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Create revenue opportunities for existing hospital and commercial laboratory clients by ensuring:</li>
<li>A high degree of customer satisfaction</li>
<li>Excellent follow-up on price quotes, product demonstrations, trouble-shooting and general program-related issues</li>
<li>Account mapping &amp; relationship building for assigned accounts, in partnership with the Vice President, map-out key influencers and decisions makers and execute a ratified strategy to drive success</li>
<li>Listening to the client, and internal resources that interact with the client, to identify areas of opportunity</li>
<li>Work with EMR vendors to obtain quotes, packaging them with our SOW for delivery to the Client for consideration</li>
<li>Client status – Participate in routine meetings as well as Quarterly Business Reviews (both, as assigned)</li>
<li>Maintain our CRM as per company policies and practices, making suggestions for process improvement wherever possible</li>
<li>Flex your intellectual curiosity with discovery calls for new opportunities, both for new and existing logos</li>
<li>Provide updates and reports to internal colleagues as required</li>
<li>Create Opportunities, SOW’s &amp; Sales Orders in our systems of record</li>
<li>Become the internal Go-to for client concerns bringing business maturity to problem identification and problem solving</li>
</ul>
