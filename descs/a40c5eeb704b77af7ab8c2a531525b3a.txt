<h2>SALT Staff Accountant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Cherry Bekaert</b> has been proudly providing Elite Accounting and Advisory services to our clients for over 70 years. As a State and Local Tax (SALT) Staff Accountant in our Atlanta, DC Area, or Charlotte or Coral Gables office, you will have the opportunity to work within the Specialty Tax Practice of a Top 25 accounting firm and help us continue our success.</p>
<p>
The SALT Practice is a national practice, and serves all Cherry Bekaert offices firm wide. This candidate will have the opportunity to work with and learn about Income/Franchise Tax, Sales and Use Tax, Credits &amp; Incentives, and Property Tax services.</p>
<p><b>
What your day looks like:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Income tax consulting services including nexus reviews, sourcing analyses and credits/incentives implementation.</li>
<li>Preparation of multi-state income tax returns for corporate and pass-through entities. Other SALT tax preparation as required.</li>
<li>Sales/use tax consulting services including nexus reviews, taxability determinations and other related issues.</li>
<li>Research state and local tax issues using state statutes, regulations, case law and other relevant authorities/guidance.</li>
<li>Keep abreast of significant current developments and new legislation within multi-state taxation</li>
<li>Perform other accounting duties as needed in engagements and as assigned by supervisory personnel</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What you need for this role:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>1-3 years of experience in public accounting or other industry experience</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree in accounting. Masters preferred</li>
<li>Proficiency with computers, spreadsheet software programs, and calculators</li>
<li>CPA certified/eligible or CMI</li>
<li>Ability to manage multiple responsibilities simultaneously (multi-task)</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What you can expect from us:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Shared values, including uncompromising integrity, a passion for excellence, and mutual respect</li>
<li>Collaborative environment focused on career advancement and professional development</li>
<li>Opportunity to focus your career in specialty taxes</li>
<li>Extensive training</li>
<li>Flexible work arrangements with generous PTO</li>
<li>Competitive salary and bonus program</li>
<li>“Dress for your Day” policy</li>
</ul>
<p>Our mission is to make a difference for our people and our clients. If these values align with yours, we’d like to hear from you.</p>
