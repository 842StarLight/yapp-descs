<h2>Marketing Specialist Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<div>
<div>Position Description
</div>
<div><p>Implements marketing projects across multiple business functions with responsibilities that include merchandising, social media, website content to improve brand engagement, preference and sell-through.</p></div>
</div>
<div>
<div>
Job Responsibilities
</div>
<div><ul>
<li>Monitors and communicates project timelines and budgets</li>
<li>
Collaborates with management to develop communication strategy and executes across an integrated marketing communications plan.</li>
<li>
Writes, edits, and proofs copy for marketing communications such as product packaging, printed collateral, merchandising, digital content, advertising, customer presentations and press releases</li>
<li>
Executes budget, strategy, and shot list for photo and video shoots</li>
<li>
Distributes prepared marketing materials to internal and external stakeholders</li>
<li>
Assists in the planning and execution of trade shows and events</li>
<li>
Performs other duties as assigned</li>
</ul></div>
</div>
<div>
<div>
Job Skills Requirements
</div>
<div><ul>
<li>General understanding of business-to-business and business-to-consumer marketing practices, digital marketing and customer online experience, durable goods and retail sales channels</li>
<li>
Organized with ability to handle multiple projects independently</li>
<li>
Ability to communicate (verbal, written) and collaborate cross-functionally in a fast-paced environment.</li>
<li>
Ability to take initiative</li>
<li>
Team-oriented</li>
<li>
Fluent in English and primary language used in area of responsibility and/or location</li>
</ul></div>
</div>
<div>
<div>
Education
</div>
<div><div>Bachelor's Degree in Marketing or related field, or equivalent education and experience</div></div>
</div>
<div>
<div>
Experience Requirements
</div>
<div><ul><li>Prior experience in Marketing or related field preferred</li></ul></div>
</div>
<div>
<div>
Physical &amp; Environmental Requirements
</div>
<div><div>Domestic and/or international travel as required up to 15%</div></div>
</div>
</div>
