<div>
What are the details of this position:</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
As Bilingual Product Support Specialist, your key area of responsibility will be creating customer loyalty and satisfaction through one-contact resolution of Geotab related inquiries. You will also assist new and existing customers via the deployment and implementation of devices and databases. You will need to work closely with Operations, Fleet Division, Sales, and other internal support teams. To be successful in this role you will be a self-starter with strong written and verbal communication skills, and have the ability to quickly understand complex, technical concepts. In addition, the successful candidate will know and demonstrate practices and processes for establishing and retaining customer relationships and creating customer satisfaction. You will also understand the Geotab devices, accessories and portals including troubleshooting protocols and applications.</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Duties and Tasks/Essential Functions:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Support client, carrier, and sales team by providing phone, chat, and email assistance relating to device errors, network communications, portal configuration and navigation, product implementation, and troubleshooting</li>
<li>
Support carrier demo and product trial programs</li>
<li>
Coordinate with all departments in the management of the return/exchange process for Geotab devices and accessories from case origin to completion, including communication to the customer.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of business writing, grammar and mechanics used in speaking and writing with customers and co-workers</li>
<li>
Customer feedback and follow-through</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Experience/Skills Required:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Associate's degree and/or Bachelor's degree preferred</li>
<li>
Minimum 2+ years of customer service experience (experience in a call center environment is preferred)</li>
<li>
At least intermediate-level Excel skills, including creation of pivot tables and reports</li>
<li>
Internet savvy and accustomed to the use of automated systems</li>
<li>
Experience working with wireless or telecommunication technologies preferred</li>
<li>
Exceptional relationship-building skills</li>
<li>
Demonstrated professionalism and poise (especially when dealing with frustrated or disgruntled customers)</li>
<li>
Able to work varying shifts, including weekends and holidays</li>
<li>
Able to work in a cubicle environment with multiple distractions and interruptions throughout the day</li>
<li>
Able to sit for prolonged periods of time and to listen to customers via phone and review customer history and information via computer</li>
<li>
Ability to read, write and speak in Dutch</li>
<li>
A passport for international travel may be required for this position (at employee's expense)</li>
</ul></div>
