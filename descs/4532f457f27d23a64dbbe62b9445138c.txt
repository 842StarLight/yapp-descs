<h2>Business Development Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Job Description<br>
<br>
<p>RESPONSIBILITIES</p>
<ul>
<li>
Drive quarterly and annual sales and revenue targets for a defined territory</li>
<li>
Generating new business through prospecting, cold calling, scheduling appointments and building relationships</li>
<li>
Responsible for end-to-end business development of the assigned area; includes planning and building short-term and long-term sales pipeline</li>
<li>
Cross-functional sales and implementation of new accounts through coordination with multiple internal teams</li>
<li>
Utilize CRM software daily to track sales calls and sales funnel</li>
</ul>
</div>
<br>
<div>Qualifications<br>
<br>
<p>REQUIRED SKILLS</p>
<ul>
<li>
Strong organizational and time management</li>
<li>
Excellent written and verbal communication</li>
<li>
Must be self-motivated and have the ability to work independently in a fast paced, multi-tasking environment</li>
<li>
Focused and possess sales mindset</li>
<li>
Driven, enthusiastic, and self-motivated</li>
<li>
Must be comfortable dealing with ambiguity and able to work in undefined situations</li>
<li>
Previous hospitality experience is a plus</li>
<li>
Experience in early-stage startup environments is an advantage</li>
<li>
Drivers License required.</li>
</ul>
