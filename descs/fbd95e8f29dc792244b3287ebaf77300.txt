<h2>Customer Service Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Snapshot of the Position…</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>The Weber Customer Service Team is looking to add a talented, detail-oriented, multi-tasker to our team! This role works closely and on a daily basis with our Field Service Engineers by dispatching work to our field service teak and closing out service tickets (preventative maintenance or repair calls). This primarily involves invoicing activity and at times and working with our customers to gather additional information. This role may also involve the coordination of customer returns. This position involves a lot of detailed work; checking several databases, running reports, creating service contracts and creating service quotes as needed. This role also serves as a back up to our B2B customer service team.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>This position requires initiative, attention to detail, strong multi-tasking ability, an ability and willingness to learn quickly and a talent in managing internal relationships as well as customer relationships. Additionally, this role requires strong verbal and written communication skills and the ability to manage and prioritize your own work.</p>
<br>
