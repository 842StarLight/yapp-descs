<p><b>Professional Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>
4-year degree (or work experience equivalent)</p></li>
<li><p>
Data Warehouse Institute “Certified Business Intelligence Professional” credential preferred
</p></li>
<li><p>MBA Required</p></li>
<li>
10+ years of experience with business intelligence and analytics technologies in at least one of the following four areas: business analysis, enterprise data warehousing, business intelligence, and predictive analytics.</li>
<li>
Expert analysis skills backed by experience with big data, cloud computing, ERP systems, and modern business intelligence and data visualization tools.
</li>
<li>Experience executing and managing projects.
</li>
<li>Expert knowledge of descriptive, predictive, and prescriptive analytics techniques and algorithms.
</li>
<li>Expert knowledge of common information systems, metrics, and reports for Financial, Workforce, Sales &amp; Marketing business functions.
</li>
<li>Expert knowledge of common business modeling problems including: customer/employee retention, upsell/cross sell, risk management, collections management, fraud detection, etc.</li>
<li>
Able to create a budget plan, account balance, exception, and other common report types.
</li>
<li>Expert understanding of dashboarding, mashups, pivot tables, and lists, bar graphs,<br>
line graphs, box and whisker plots, tree maps, scatterplots, histograms, Pareto<br>
charts, control charts, small multiples, sparklines, and when such<br>
visualizations should be used.
</li>
<li>Must have a proven track record of implementing at least one of the following: SAP Business Objects, Oracle Hyperion, IBM Cognos, Insight, Microsoft Excel Services, Microsoft Power BI, Tableau or DOMO.
</li>
<li>Expert understanding of relational, dimensional, and entity modeling techniques<br>
including Kimball data warehousing and willingness to develop expertise.</li>
<li>
Proven ability to interpret and query relational databases, data warehouses, OLAP cubes, and Microsoft tabular models.</li>
<li>
Expert level knowledge of Microsoft Excel.<br>
</li>
