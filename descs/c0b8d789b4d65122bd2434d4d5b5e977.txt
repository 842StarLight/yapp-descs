<h2>Project Manager (Signage)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Be a part of something visible!</b> Whether it’s a sign atop a high rise building, or channel letters on a store front, our work stands out as some of the best in the industry. Jones Sign Company has grown immensely over the past 100 years with high profile projects which span the nation. Recent projects include the Hard Rock Hotel &amp; Casino, Milwaukee Bucks Arena, and Daytona Motor Speedway.<br>
</p>
<p><b>Job Description:</b></p>
<p>When nearly every job we do is a custom project, our Program Managers are at the core of our success, owning each job from the moment it is sold through final installation. As a Project Manager, you will assertively work towards a timeline while managing relationships with both the customer and internal resources, and keeping the costs within budget.</p>
<p>Successful candidates have exceptionally strong organization and time management skills, as well as the confidence and negotiation skills to tackle issues head-on with professional communication. Detail-orientation is a must-have to thoroughly and accurately manage project plans and handle customer invoicing. The ideal candidate will have project management experience in the signage or construction field.<br>
</p>
<p><b>What it takes to excel:</b></p>
<ul><li> Associate’s Degree with coursework in business, construction, manufacturing, logistics and project management; Bachelor’s Degree preferred</li></ul>
<ul><li> 2 years’ experience in project management</li></ul>
<ul><li> Permitting or B2B sales background a plus</li></ul>
<ul><li> Sound business acumen and understanding of what drives company profits</li></ul>
<ul><li> General knowledge of blueprints and reading contractual language</li></ul>
