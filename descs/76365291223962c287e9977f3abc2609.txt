<h2>Senior Marketing Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Charlotte Radiology is the area's imaging experts. Having served Mecklenburg and surrounding counties in both NC and SC since 1967, Charlotte Radiology is one of the largest radiology groups in the country with more than 500 employees and doctors and is a founding partner of US Radiology Specialists. Charlotte Radiology owns and operates 15 breast centers, a mobile breast center program, two vein centers, several vascular and interventional radiology sites and jointly owns five free-standing imaging centers. Greater than 90% of our employees directly touch patient care and are comprised of 95+ radiologists, 25 advanced care professionals, 160+ technologists and nurses and 150+ outpatient support staff professionals. Backed by an administrative team of more than 65 employees, we together consistently earn an overall patient satisfaction score of greater
<br>than 95%. Our radiologists’ training span a wide range of subspecialties from mammography and musculoskeletal imaging to pediatrics and interventional radiology, and they interpret and/or perform more than 1.5 million studies annually for 18 hospitals, 30+ imaging centers and a multitude of health care providers. For more information visit: www.charlotteradiology.com.

<b>Our Vision:</b> To be recognized as the provider of choice – where people come first.
<br><br>
<b>Our Mission:</b> Through collaborative teamwork, we will provide quality-driven, innovative, customer-focused services that exceed expectations.
<br><br>
<b>The Opportunity:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>The Marketing Manager is responsible for the coordination of assigned marketing projects and materials (direct to consumer, patient education, and physician marketing) across all service lines, including material development, field team materials, vendor management, and project/data analysis and optimization, to assist in the growth of the business</li>
<li>Implement and analyze assigned components of multichannel marketing plans across the service lines and various audiences (patient and referring practices)</li>
<li>Lead the development of marketing communications and field sales tactics aligned to the strategic plan and guided by brand standards and customer insight</li>
<li>Traffic advertising to appropriate channels to ensure on time flighting</li>
<li>Manage budget and collateral inventory to ensure on budget spending, appropriate on-hand inventory levels, and optimization of collateral (e.g. inventory quantities based on utilization)</li>
<li>Majority of work takes place in an office setting. Availability must be flexible throughout the week to accommodate occasional evening and weekend events</li>
</ul>
<b>What We Need:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in Business, Marketing, Advertising and/or Communications</li>
<li>MBA preferred</li>
<li>At least 3-5 years of experience in Marketing/Communications</li>
<li>Experience in Digital and CRM marketing, Multichannel Marketing and/or Healthcare, preferred</li>
<li>Strong computer skills- all Microsoft Office applications, website and social media platforms and analytics; helpful to have knowledge of Charlotte Radiology software interfaces (i.e., SCI, Xiris)</li>
<li>Knowledge of industry best practices for tracking and analyzing data and reports</li>
<li>Marketing, Advertising and Public Relations knowledge to include: ad and marketing campaign development and management, multichannel marketing execution, collateral development and management, website and social media content development, SEO/SEM knowledge and CRM</li>
<li>Experience working with creative agencies and freelancers, and production vendors</li>
<li>Strong marketing fundamentals, including: creative brief writing, utilizing market research insights, budget/vendor management skills, and evaluating creative content development, and data analytics</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal and communication skills (written/verbal, creative brief writing, presentation development)</li>
<li>Collaborative working style to effectively work with marketing team, vendors, agencies, and leadership</li>
<li>Self-starter that excels in a performance driven environment, detail oriented with multiple ongoing projects</li>
<li>Strong problem-solving skills and analytical skills for budgeting, campaign and collateral optimization</li>
<li>Basic understanding of radiology services for all service lines and a working knowledge of Charlotte Radiology's operations, preferred</li>
</ul>
