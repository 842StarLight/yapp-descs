<div>
<p>Altice USA (NYSE: ATUS) is one of the largest broadband communications and video services providers in the United States, delivering broadband, pay television, telephony services, proprietary content and advertising services to approximately 4.9 million Residential and Business customers across 21 states through its Optimum and Suddenlink brands.</p>
<p>
From high-speed broadband and ultra-HD video, to digital advertising solutions, local news and voice offerings, we are making connections possible around the country - to businesses, to consumers, to communities, to schools, to hospitals, and everyone in between.
</p>
</div>
<p></p>
<p>The Analyst will be responsible for providing direct support to the Manager of Sales Operations. This role will be responsible for providing support for recurring and ad-hoc reporting and analysis as it relates to Altice USA’s B2C operational metrics. This position will also assist in driving market level insights of Altice USA’s footprint. The ideal candidate possesses the ability to understand the data needs of the business and provide clear &amp; logical analysis to help drive growth. The Analyst will coordinate details behind reporting and analysis related to Altice USA’s B2C Sales organization. Extract data from multiple sources, organizing into understandable information to provide to management staff up to C-level executives. Collaborate across organizational lines to solve business issues based on insights gained. Develop automated processes to streamline reporting and data distribution.
</p>
