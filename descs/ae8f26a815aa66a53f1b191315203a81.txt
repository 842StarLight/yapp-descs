<h2>Account Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><b>Account Coordinator</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>
<div>The Account Coordinator is responsible for managing marketing territories, onboarding new clients, communicating with clients during their campaigns, and remaining in close communication with the Success Coach team on all assigned campaigns. This includes researching audiences, communicating with advisors, responding to territory requests, and supporting the Success Coach team in other ways.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Overview</b></div>
<div>Do you love consulting successful sales professionals but are tired of prospecting and cold calling? Would you like to work with an existing, pipeline of some of the top sales professionals in the financial services and insurance industry from all around the country? Do you find yourself wondering what it would be like to work for a company that was named one of the, “Top 100 Companies in Colorado” to work for?</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Experience</b></div>
<div>We value hands-on digital marketing experience. We have found success investing in and training lesser-experienced professionals or professionals with no experience to execute our programs with confidence. We place a premium on people who exude a keen desire to learn and grow.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>What to Expect</b></div>
<div>A supportive, work-hard, play-hard culture with people who are passionate about what we do for one another and our clients. An innovative approach to marketing that never stops improving. A casual and fun work environment (jeans and t-shirts). Incredible opportunities for career advancement. We love promoting from within and our benefits are exceptional.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Responsibilities</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Perform audience research to determine if territory requests are within Steep’s best practices</li>
<li>Communicate with clients on their pending territory requests</li>
<li>Communicate with Success Coaches on pending requests involving the relationships they manage</li>
<li>Support the Success Coach team by playing an active role in onboarding conversations</li>
<li>Respond to all territories effectively to achieve maximum usage of exclusive markets</li>
<li>Maintain communication with clients on all active assigned campaigns</li>
<li>Work independently and with both the Digital Marketing and Traffic teams on mid-campaign optimization</li>
<li>Analyze under-performing campaigns and implement solutions to increase performance</li>
<li>Report mid-campaign optimization both internally and with clients</li>
<li>Record all campaign data in shared spreadsheets</li>
<li>Maintain accurate records of territories in shared spreadsheets</li>
<li>Work with our traffic team to record data from completed seminars</li>
<li>Take 2 Digital Marketer certification courses related to this job function</li>
<li>Managing online advertising campaigns including monitoring the vital stats of each campaign, gathering data and results for campaigns, making simple revisions to digital marketing strategies such as email designs and copy and landing pages</li>
<li>Offer excellent written and verbal communication both with Steep clients and team members</li>
<li>Performs additional duties as assigned by Management</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Skills and Experience Needed</b></div>
<ul>
<li>High School diploma/GED required; Bachelors’ degree preferred</li>
<li>Minimum of two (2) years’ experience in a related field</li>
<li>Prefer knowledge of landing page tools (Lead Pages, Unbounce) or Wordpress</li>
<li>Prefer knowledge of InfusionSoft or other email automation tools</li>
<li>Strong customer relationship skills, verbal and written</li>
<li>Ability to work independently with little or no supervision</li>
<li>Ability to interact with all personality types and with all levels of staff</li>
<li>Time management and the ability to handle multiple tasks in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>Exceptional problem-solving skills with a high attention to detail</li>
<li>Professional approach and appearance</li>
</ul>
