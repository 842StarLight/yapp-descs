<br><br>
This position
<br><br>
The Field Strategy and Operations Team is chartered with making and enabling continuous productivity improvements for the field organization.
<br><br>
We are seeking a highly motivated and innovative person to join the team to actively manage, build and evolve Sales Systems of Scale for worldwide sales forecasting and planning tools and to cement a center of excellence focused on Sales Planning and Performance Management.
<br><br>
This is a leadership position that will collaboratively drive the roadmap, the development and the evangelism of a portfolio of core business applications for in quarter sales forecasting, out quarter forecasting, territory modeling as well as quota and capacity modeling and management.
<br><br>
Success in this role will be measured on accelerating development and delivery of value-added functionality as well as accelerating adoption and usage by Sales Management and SalesOps teams.
<br><br>
The ideal candidate is a team player who possesses very strong analytical and technical building skills and enjoys taking solutions to scale. This ideal candidate is also resilient and resourceful when solving complex cross functional complex problems – and they will often look at challenges as opportunities.
<br><br>
Attention to detail paired with mindfulness of the big picture is essential for success in this role. The successful candidate is also someone who thrives in a fast-paced, and at times demanding environment and sees themselves as having ownership and accountability over their own performance and responsibilities – they are not afraid to go to extra mile to make things work out.
<br><br>
This position is a People Manager and reports to Sr. Director Ops and Analytics.
<br><br>
Responsibilities
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Will be the functional leader and primary force of change for evolving Anaplan models roadmap (Sales Forecasting, Sales Outlook, Quota Model) inside the sales organization</li>
<li>Will build out the team to rapidly develop, evolve and manage existing models to drive better, faster planning and modeling solutions that scale.</li>
<li>Drives improvements to refine upstream processes that needs to be "fixed" in order to deliver scalable solutions</li>
<li>Actively consumes and curates the model's contents to support the ongoing development and management of the business</li>
</ul>

<li>Minimum three years relevant business experience consulting on Anaplan implementations, preferably in the software / high tech</li>
<li>Minimum two years relevant business experience working in a SalesOps or finance in a managerial role supporting Sales</li>
</ul>
Preferred Qualifications
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Knowledgeable about sales systems within the SaaS based Industry</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>

<li>Accountable, Goal-oriented and organized team player; able to lead cross functional teams</li>

</ul>
