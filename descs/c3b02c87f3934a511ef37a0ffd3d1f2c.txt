<h2>Hi-Tech Customer Strategy/Lead-to-Cash Senior Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div><b>Hi-Tech Customer Strategy/Lead-to-Cash Senior Manager</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Our Telecommunications, Media and Technology (TMT) industry clients include some of the most valued companies in the U.S. and many of the world’s top global brands. We are poised to help clients shape their evolving business models, scale and transform their operations, modernize their processes and technologies, and constantly improve their customer experience. Our <b>Technology </b>sector includes the world leaders in software, semiconductor, Cloud, analytics, cyber, networking, enterprise and personal computing, and technology services.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
The team</b></div>
<div><b>
Customer Strategy &amp; Applied Design</b></div>
<div>
Our Customer Strategy &amp; Applied Design team focuses on helping clients discover, design, and prototype customer growth strategies and innovation portfolios to create and drive change in a digital world. We bring knowledge and offerings to uncover the behaviors, motivations and preferences of our clients’ customers and develop new experiences for them. Our team of professionals has deep capabilities in strategy, customer experience, digital strategy and innovation.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>We deliver set of customer focused work:
</div>
<ul><li>
<b>Customer Strategy</b>| Help clients build capabilities to better understand customer value drivers and make choices about the markets and segments they serve through digital, social, and mobile channels; optimize pricing decisions and shape the most strategic issues on the CMO agenda</li></ul>
<ul><li>
<b>Customer Experience</b> | Help clients improve the quality, nature and power of interactions between the customer and the organization across the lifecycle of sales, marketing and service at each and every touch point.</li></ul>
<ul><li>
<b>Applied Innovation</b> | Help clients rapidly design, prototype and launch new businesses and experiences, translating insights on human behavior from design-led methods into offerings and technologies that delight customers and drive growth</li></ul>
<ul><li>
<b>Digital Strategy</b> | Help clients understand, envision and articulate “digital” as a business strategy aligned with their CEO’s agenda – and then craft a plan to embed everything they do with the latest technology solutions and business models to compete in the marketplace for customers and talent</li></ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Work You’ll Do</b></div>
<div>
As a <b>Senior Manager</b>, you will lead and deliver large, complex client engagements that identify, design, and implement creative business and technology solutions for large companies. You will contribute to the firm's growth and development in a variety of ways including:</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Engagement Management:</div>
<ul><li> Lead engagement planning and budgeting; mobilize and manage engagement teams; define deliverable structure and content; facilitate buy-in of proposed solutions from top management levels at the client; direct on-time, quality delivery of work products; manage engagement economics; manage engagement risk.</li></ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Relationship Management of executive clients and sponsors:</div>
<ul><li> Participate in Business Development, develop and maintain contact with top decision makers at key clients, organize and lead pursuit teams, participate and lead aspects of the proposal development process, contribute to the development of proposal pricing strategies.</li></ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Practice Development &amp; Eminence:</div>
<ul><li> Develop practical solutions and methodologies, develop "thoughtware" and "point- of-view" documents, participate in public speaking events, get published in industry periodicals.</li></ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
People Development:</div>
<div></div>
<ul><li>
<br>
 Perform the role of counselor and coach, provide input and guidance into the staffing process, actively participate in staff recruitment and retention activities, provide leadership and support for delivery teams and staff in local offices.</li></ul>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>Qualifications</b></div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>
10+ years of experience working with <b>Hi-Tech sector firms or for clients including software, semiconductor, cloud, analytics, cyber networking or personal computing.</b> Industry experience is a must.</li>
<li>
8+ years’ experience working in <b>customer strategy, customer transformation, lead-to-cash, ecommerce customer experience, customer growth strategies.</b>
</li>
<li>
Knowledge of helping clients make choices about markets and segments through digital, social and mobile channels.</li>
<li>
Experience helping clients improve interactions between customer and organization across sales, marketing and service.</li>
<li>
Prior experience assisting clients align digital business strategy with technology.</li>
<li><b>
Ability to travel 80%</b></li>
<li>
Must be legally authorized to work in the United States without the need for employer sponsorship, now or at any time in the future.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>
Preferred:</b></div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>
Strong project management and delivery experience, including project planning, budget oversight and staffing of project teams including time management.</li>
<li>
Strong problem solving and troubleshooting skills with the ability to exercise mature judgment.</li>
<li>
Experience with selling to C-level executives and other business development activities.</li>
<li>
Previous experience mentoring, training and developing junior members of the team; experience in employee performance reviews.</li>
<li>
Ability to work independently and manage multiple task assignments.</li>
<li>
Strong oral and written communication skills, including presentation skills (MS Visio, MS PowerPoint). Working knowledge and experience of MS Office applications and training development tools.</li>
<li>
Located within a commutable distance of a Deloitte office.</li>
</ul>
