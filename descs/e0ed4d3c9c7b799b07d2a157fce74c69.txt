<h2>Network Solutions Architect</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Accudata Systems is looking for a Network/Wireless Solutions Architect in the DFW area. This is a technical sales support role for our account management team. Your ability to clearly articulate network solutions to our existing customer base and prospects is essential.</p>
<p>
Your involvement in our opportunities will start with the initial customer meeting and continue through the design phases of the solution. You must be a self-starter, require minimal supervision, and possess strong technical and design skills – including in-depth knowledge of multi-site, mixed technology enterprise environments. This position requires excellent communications skills (written and verbal), as well as the ability to work as part of a team.</p>
<p><b>
 Skills:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Experience working as a pre-sales Solution Architect or Cisco Sales Engineer</li>
<li>
8+ years of experience in a network or solutions engineering level role that includes route/switch, Cisco wireless and Cisco Nexus</li>
<li>
Extensive experience architecting Cisco Enterprise Wireless networks</li>
<li>
Experience with architecting complex Cisco wired networks (Route/Switch).</li>
<li>
Strong working knowledge of Cisco Enterprise Networking Solutions and capabilities (Cisco Catalyst 9000, ISR 4000, ISR 1000 and Cisco Viptela SD-WAN).</li>
<li>
Strong working knowledge of Cisco Nexus (Spine/Leaf and ACI) is preferred</li>
<li>
Certified Ekahau Specialist or extensive experience working with Ekahau wireless survey software.</li>
<li>
A technical understanding of RF design.</li>
<li>
CCNP or CCIE certifications preferred</li>
<li>
Desire to be in a sales-driven, 100% customer-facing role</li>
<li>
Ability to deliver well-crafted and detailed Statements of Work</li>
<li>
Ability to deliver accurate Bills of Materials in short timeframes</li>
<li>
Able to effectively manage your own time and calendar</li>
<li>
Must be able to quickly learn several products across a wide range of enterprise and data center networking technologies</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Sales Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Customer presentations and seminars/conferences</li>
<li>
Develop high-level designs and architectures – create and document architectures that address client business problems that can be used to ensure a smooth transition from presales to delivery</li>
<li>
Create Statements of Work</li>
<li>
Create Bills of Materials</li>
<li>
Developing technical solution presentations.</li>
<li>
Diagraming of proposed solutions and detailing solution requirements</li>
<li>
Ability to lead evals, demos, or proof-of-concepts for customers</li>
<li>
Develop/maintain sales solution kits</li>
<li>
Assist customers with understanding, selecting, and architecting appropriate network and/or security solutions</li>
</ul>
<p>
This position will require local travel within the greater DFW area. Occasional overnight trips may be required for training, seminars, or vendor events.</p>
<p><b>
Apply for this Position:</b></p>
<p>
DFW residents preferred.</p>
<p>
 Accudata offers an excellent performance-based compensation package with outstanding earning potential, a benefits package that includes medical coverage, 401k, and a flexible spending account.</p>
