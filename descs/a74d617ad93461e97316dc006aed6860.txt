<p><b>Detailed Description and Job Requirements</b></p>
<div>Manages and develops a team of sales representatives including recruiting, hiring, and training new reps on the Oracle sales process. Develops and execute a territory plan to maximize revenue. Generates and achieves monthly territory forecasts, and accurately predicts bookings on a monthly basis. Manages escalation. Conducts weekly progress meetings with sales team. Maintains a persistent focus on customer success and satisfaction, resulting in referenceable accounts and positive brand awareness. Provide leadership and guidance to all stakeholder organizations including product management, pre-sales consulting, renewals sales, and customer support, establishing an effective "virtual team" driving towards a consistent set of common goals.</div>
