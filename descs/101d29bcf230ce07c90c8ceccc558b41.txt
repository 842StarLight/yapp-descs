<h2>Mechanical Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b><i>Who We Are</i></b></p>
<p>Binsky, a large-scale mechanical contracting and service provider located in Piscataway NJ is seeking a Project Manager. The Project Manager will report to the Director of Field Operations</p>
<p><b><i>Why We Need You!</i></b></p>
<p>We need to talented individuals, like you, who care about the company and our customers, and can help us continue to grow and succeed!</p>
<p><b><i>The Big Task</i></b></p>
<p>The Project Manager will lead major projects, develop client relationships and deliver projects per client needs and budgets. The position requires high-level client service and establishing long-term partnerships.</p>
<p><b><i>Key Sub Tasks</i></b></p>
<p>Accountabilities:</p>
<p>Project Operations</p>
<ul>
<li>The PM has overall control of all project activities</li>
<li>To manage overall safety of all personnel on a project during construction activities</li>
<li>Enforce company safety policies and procedures. Review client’s specific safety procedures and develop a site-specific manual to be utilized. Oversee and ensure weekly safety meetings are conducted and documented in the field</li>
<li>Monitor coordination and design efforts for required scope of work</li>
<li>Manage labor goals and affirmative action plans; awareness of labor relations with unions regarding jurisdiction over a project</li>
<li>Review design documents prior to field installation</li>
<li>Implementation of B&amp;S QA/QC program</li>
<li>Identify and communicate opportunities for pre-fabrication on the project; review union requirements for fabrication and compliance; monitor progress on, and coordinating installation of, pre-fabricated sections in the field</li>
</ul>
<p>Change Order Tracking</p>
<ul>
<li>Coordinate and monitor cost events pricing</li>
<li>Communicate monthly billings with accounting</li>
</ul>
<p>Scheduling and Labor Tracking</p>
<ul>
<li>Generate, update and maintain overall construction schedule</li>
<li>Review productivity control plan for fab shop and field activities and implement metrics</li>
<li>Review purchasing status and equipment expediting</li>
<li>Supervise and schedule all subcontracts work under their direction, and work with other trades impacted by it</li>
<li>Monitor overall job performance by tracking labor and materials costs timely, and adjusting schedules and performance as necessary to achieve budgeted goals</li>
<li>Coordinate staffing on construction projects</li>
<li>Client Relations</li>
<li>Process and review project invoicing with owner and subcontractors.</li>
<li>Identity problem areas early and develop contingency plans for the successful delivery of the project and schedule</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>Desired Skills and Experience</i></b></p>
<p>Skills</p>
<ul>
<li>A solid understanding of construction means and methods associated with the renovation and construction of commercial and industrial buildings</li>
<li>Strong computer and technology skills including project management software, Microsoft Outlook, smart phones, and other software used in the construction industry</li>
<li>The ability to decipher various project schedule formats and impacts to mechanical work; working knowledge of civil, architectural, mechanical and electrical scopes of work</li>
<li>Ability to handle multiple tasks and projects at the same</li>
</ul>
<p>Education and Experience</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree Preferred</li>
<li>Experience in construction, sales or business development a plus</li>
<li>Successful management of large and complex projects for sophisticated client’s healthcare, laboratory, infrastructure, higher education, industrial or R&amp;D facilities desired</li>
<li>Lean IPD experience a plus</li>
</ul>
<p>Physical Requirements</p>
<ul><li>Physical activity not limited to climbing, bending, kneeling, crawling, lifting&lt; 20 lbs.</li></ul>
<p>If you can achieve the above and you find it fun and challenging - you have just the right amount of skill and experience.</p>
