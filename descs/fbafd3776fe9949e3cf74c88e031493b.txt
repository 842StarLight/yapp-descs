<div>We are seeking a Sales Executive to join our motivated, and talented, elite client services team. </div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Responsibilities</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Hit quarterly sales goals</li>
<li>Create or bring relationships that drives growth and builds client trust</li>
<li>Identify and sell new media solutions to businesses and agencies, helping them connect with their customers/users</li>
<li>Enter daily sales activity, meetings, calls and emails into Digital Remedy CRM</li>
<li>Enter detailed sales opportunities, pipeline and deals into Digital Remedy CRM</li>
<li>Manage campaign launch process from start to finish, inclusive of client kick-off call, strategy recommendation, and up-selling</li>
<li>Provide accurate revenue forecasts and account plans required on cadence</li>
</ul> <div><b>Our ideal candidate</b></div>
<ul>
<li>5+ years of experience working at media sales organization selling brand advertising campaigns, mobile experience a plus</li>
<li>2+ years of experience selling programmatic advertising or educating brands, agencies and media companies about programmatic advertising</li>
<li>A strong set of existing relationships with brands and agencies at all levels</li>
<li>Proven track record of building relationships with the media/advertising industry</li>
<li>Deep understanding of the programmatic and advertising ecosystems</li>
<li>Drive new managed service revenue and meet quarterly revenue targets as set by management</li>
<li>Identify, target, and acquire new programmatic or media business opportunities</li>
<li>Work with internal teams to connect the needs of our various customer constituencies</li>
</ul>
