<h2>ITSM Software Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div><div><div><div><div><div><div>
<p>At TBC, our core values are Passion, Partnership and Innovation. Every day our team of highly qualified engineers and administrators work hard to empower our clients to transform and grow their companies.</p>
<p>
The role of the <b>Software Engineer</b> is to combine business acumen and analytic insight with programming skills to develop applications to meet specific requirements. <b>Note:</b> Application development at TBC is primarily, but not exclusively focused, on development in the ServiceNow application.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>
Includes the following. Other responsibilities may be assigned.</p>
<ul>
<li>
Customer Service &amp; Delivery</li>
<li>
Product Build &amp; Platform Support</li>
<li>
Solution Delivery Support</li>
<li>
Technical Documentation</li>
<li>
Process and Standards Adherence</li>
<li>
Sales Support (product demonstrations, information, etc.)</li>
<li>
Provide ongoing maintenance, support and enhancements in existing systems and platforms</li>
<li>
Collaborate cross-functionally with business users, project managers and other engineers to achieve secure, reliable and scalable solutions</li>
<li>
Provide recommendations for continuous improvement</li>
<li>
Collaborate with business analysts, test analysts, project managers and other engineers to deliver excellent solutions and to consistently apply best practices</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Qualifications/Your Background:</b></p>
<p><b>
Required Skills/Experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
A Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering, IT or related discipline/experience</li>
<li>
A strong technical background having direct experience with ServiceNow application and portal development as well as a strong understanding of web development including HTML5, Angular, CSS3, JQuery, and JavaScript</li>
<li>
2+ years’ experience with ServiceNow is required with an emphasis on application, workflow, and portal development</li>
<li>
Hand’s on ServiceNow experience in these areas:<ul>
<li>
Custom development including workflows and applications</li>
<li>
ServiceNow Studio and the Automated Testing Framework</li>
<li>
Scripting incl. Business Rules, Script Includes, UI Policies/UI Actions and Scheduled Jobs</li>
<li>
Reporting</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Experience translating designs into high quality code</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to formulate and define application scope and objectives, and to implement complete solutions</li>
<li>
Experience with Scrum/Agile development methodologies</li>
<li>
Consulting or similar client-centric experience</li>
<li>
Capable of delivering on multiple competing priorities with little supervision</li>
<li>
Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Recommended Skills/Experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Experience with modern programming languages like Java, Python, etc.</li>
<li>
Experience with building APIs and services using REST, SOAP, etc.</li>
<li>
Experience with MID Server integrations including Web Services and Orchestrations</li>
<li>
Hand’s on experience with ServiceNow ITOM suite</li>
<li>
ITIL Foundations certification</li>
<li>
Experience with<ul>
<li>
Scripting languages like Perl, Shell, etc.</li>
<li>
Infrastructure automation technologies like Docker, Vagrant, etc.</li>
<li>
Build automation like Maven Jenkins</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
How You Know if This Job Might be For You</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
You are motivated and driven to deliver value. You take ownership of your responsibilities and follow through on all client and team member requests and questions.</li>
<li>
You are a proficient developer, able to develop high quality code and to provide trouble-shooting support for the ServiceNow application/platform.</li>
<li>
You like meeting and working with new people. You are comfortable engaging with people at all levels in an organization.</li>
<li>
You are comfortable with change and multi-tasking. You enjoy learning new concepts and are quick on your feet. When things change, you know how to “roll with the punches”.</li>
</ul>
