<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p><b>Product Manager I</b></p>
<p><b>
Portland, OR</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<div>
Lives, and breathes consumer mobile solutions that delight users. Focused on the technical problems and the distinctive competencies for direct to consumer healthcare mobile offering. Supports Senior Product Management with all phases of new product development and management activities including defining business requirements, leading product development, implementing and sustaining the strategic product roadmap, pricing and budget models, competitive positioning and product design for both existing and next-generation product offerings.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
These roles are inherently cross-functional – and require close work and collaboration with counterparts in product development, data science and data engineering, user experience, operations, program, and strategy to ensure all teams are delivering high-quality products that solve real market needs. Expectation is you can define a vision, and successfully articulate that vision to a cross functional audience.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
At Cambia, our values are fundamental to achieving our Cause of transforming the health care industry. They guide our actions and bring diverse perspectives together to improve the health care journey better for those we serve. All eight values are equally important and linked to the others: Empathy, Hope, Courage, Trust, Commitment, Innovation, Collaboration and Accountability. These values are not just words on paper - we live them every day.</div>

<div></div>

<div><b>
General Functions &amp; Outcomes:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Manage the product roadmap and prioritization process, including defining the deliverables into discrete commitments (sprint planning).</li>
<li>
Directs the product management activities for products to meet business, market segmentation and financial goals.</li>
<li>
Produces product requirements for and interfaces and documents initiatives with marketing, UX, engineering and category management teams.</li>
<li>
Understands the target market and primary user needs for a product.</li>
<li>
Synthesizes business requirements from multiple sources including or may include customer segments, healthcare product experts, brokers, and sales teams into requirement documents and agile project plans.</li>
<li>
Define the feature sets, and business requirements from multiple sources, focusing on measures of success, and product roadmap.</li>
<li>
Contribute to the long term product vision.</li>
<li>
May conduct market research by working with internal teams and visits to customers and other relevant companies</li>
<li>
Manage a products life-cycle from planning to tactical activities to cross company go to market planning.</li>
<li>
May specify product requirements in the form of functional requirements, user stories, wireframes, and their objectives.</li>
<li>
Define and drives the solutions across all departments including the development of companywide go to market plans.</li>
<li>
Collaborates with team to develop and deploy strategies for improving customer acquisition, engagement, and retention.</li>
<li>
Writes accurate and useful functional specifications for multiple projects simultaneously.</li>
<li>
Ensures that all issues are identified, reported, tracked and resolved in a timely manner.</li>
</ul>
