<h2>Marketing Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Identity Studios, one of Arizona’s fastest growing marketing agencies, is currently seeking to add a qualified Marketing Project Manager to its team.</p>
<p>
The ideal candidate will have 5+ of years experience in marketing or advertising, preferably in a B2B ad agency or an in-house marketing capacity with a technology company.</p>
<p>
———————————————</p>
<p><b>
Overall Job Duties:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Engages directly with clients to help determine their business needs, develop and execute effective programs that meet their goals, and manage their relationship</li>
<li>Responsible for the preparation of marketing-related project deliverables throughout the client engagement</li>
<li>Understands client dynamics and mobilizes internal resources to effectively meet requirements</li>
<li>Works with team members to ensure a clear vision of project outcomes</li>
<li>Tracks campaign successes and project profitability</li>
<li>Sets and manages client expectations</li>
<li>Executes and maintains project work plans and schedules for projects of various sizes</li>
<li>Manages client budgets and billings</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Responsibilities Include:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Delivers the utmost level of customer service and support and lives up to our reputation for superior responsiveness and quality</li>
<li>Manages end-to-end marketing campaigns, from strategy and design to completion and reporting</li>
<li>Engages with Identity Studios’ creative team, including copywriters, graphic designers and web developers, with responsibility for scheduling workflow, receiving deliverables and presenting deliverables to clients</li>
<li>Develops cost outlines and timelines for client marketing campaigns</li>
<li>Updates project status daily in our internal project management system</li>
<li>Creates client status reports</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Skills Required:</b></p>
<br>
<p><b>
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Marketing, Advertising, Communications or related field</li>
<li>5-years of proven experience managing successful multi-media marketing campaigns in a B2B agency or for a technology company</li>
<li>Superior understanding and commitment to project management, client service and account management</li>
<li>Strong analytical skills</li>
<li>Ability to succeed and meet deadlines in an extremely fast-paced environment</li>
<li>Proven self-starter with results-driven personality</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communications skills</li>
<li>Proven project management and execution skills</li>
<li>Strong understanding of B2B marketing concepts</li>
<li>Expert knowledge of Microsoft Word, Excel, PowerPoint and Outlook</li>
<li>Knowledge of key marketing topics and tactics including email marketing, direct mail, online marketing and print advertising, telemarketing, etc.</li>
<li>General understanding of the principles of website development and management</li>
<li>Experience working with creative teams including copywriters, graphic designers, web developers, etc.</li>
<li>Understanding of the marketing/sales relationship</li>
<li>Understanding of marketing ROI and reporting principles</li>
<li>Working knowledge of graphic and web design terminology</li>
<li>Ability to use project management software</li>
</ul>
<p>Please send resume, cover letter and salary requirements to hr@identitystudios.biz.</p>
<p>
The chosen candidate will receive a base salary dependent on experience and track record. Health and life style benefits are also available, in addition to a 401(k).</p>
