<h2>Maintenance Production Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>MAINTENANCE PRODUCTION MANAGER</b></p>
<p>The Maintenance Production Manager upholds the mission of the company, “<i>Providing outdoor environments that enrich people’s lives”</i> by serving as the assistant to the operations manager. The individual in this position facilitates all operations and assures crews are meeting hour goals while also completing work deemed important by the operations manager. This position acts as the intermediary between customers, vendors, subcontractors, employees, and the Operations Manager.</p>
<p><b>Key Responsibilities</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Daily Crew Sendoff – 6:45 am- 7:15 am to ensure departure by all crews at 7:15 am. Have 7:05 am morning huddle to discuss any final details for the day to facilitate this very important company procedure</li>
<li>Job Handoff – Attend the Job Handoff Meeting weekly with crew leaders and designers to assure we are making the right decisions at the right time and to ensure all details of the job are known prior to starting the job</li>
<li>Crew Optimization - Train and develop crews under his/her direction. Meet daily to review, revise and /or set daily plans with each crew so they can stay on schedule.</li>
<li>Daily Crew Planning to be completed with the operations manager each day</li>
<li>To be done daily, planning out the current day and the next day to ensure we are always a day ahead in planning</li>
<li>Work daily on job sites where most applicable to beat projected job hours and ensure quality and building accuracy to the job plan (20% of your time spent)</li>
<li>Quality Control to ensure all jobs are being completed to the job plan</li>
<li>Job Change Orders – Responsible for all job change orders by working with the crew leaders and customers and following the process of getting information to the sales/design team to coordinate pricing and getting information to the Operations Manager for scheduling purposes</li>
<li>Warranty - Ownership of the Warranty process from start to finish. Communicating with clients and documenting in shared documents for the rest of the staff to see.</li>
<li>Pick up materials and equipment when necessary</li>
<li>Notify the Operations Manger when common stock items need ordered</li>
<li>Notify the Operations Manager of additional materials needed on jobs</li>
<li>Ensure compliance with company’s safety and behavior policies and procedures on landscape jobsites</li>
<li>Maintain tool inventory for each crew</li>
<li>Maintain and care for nursery stock</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Other responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Advance the company’s image through involvement in an organization that supports company goals</li>
<li>Assist with shop/ yard duties as required by Operations Manager</li>
<li>Maintain active involvement in a community service organization</li>
<li>Execute other assignments as directed by Operations Manager or President of the company</li>
<li>Snow removal and management as required</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum three years’ experience in the green industry with a focus in lawncare, mowing, maintenance.</li>
<li>Three years experience managing people, equipment and operations</li>
<li>Prefer 2 or 4 year degree in an accredited landscape-related program</li>
<li>Basic understanding of Microsoft software programs including Word, Excel, Outlook and financial/ accounting software</li>
<li>Strong work ethic, embracing accountability and consistency</li>
<li>Effective written and oral communication skills</li>
<li>Ability to prioritize and multi-task in a fast-paced, quickly-changing environment</li>
<li>Insurability and ability to operate company’s vehicles</li>
<li>Ability to push, pull, lift or move up to 50 pounds unassisted</li>
<li>Knowledge of and ability to operate requisite landscape machinery</li>
</ul>

<p><b>Work Schedule:</b> 6:30a – 5p, Monday-Friday, some overtime as required.
</p>
