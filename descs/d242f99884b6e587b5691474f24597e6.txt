<h2>Front Desk Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">This position is responsible for checking in members and guests upon their arrival to the Club, creating a positive first impression, and maintaining a clean and inviting reception area. Associates are responsible for providing exceptional customer service, creating an environment of hospitality and luxury, and consistently demonstrate a positive and professional attitude.
<br><br>
Customer Service
<br><br>
Greet members and guests upon arrival to the Club
<br><br>
Check members in by swiping their membership card
<br><br>
Ensure all guests to the Club fully complete a Guest Registration Waiver and pay any applicable Guest Fees
<br><br>
Book appointments, program and class reservations for members
<br><br>
Provide information regarding Club programming and facilities
<br><br>
Deliver exceptional service experience to members, guests, and Associates with a goal of retaining guests for repeat business
<br><br>
Reception Desk Oversight
<br><br>
Operate cash register
<br><br>
Process payments
<br><br>
Manage/handle cash and balance cash drawer each shift.
<br><br>
Perform opening and/or closing duties, as required
<br><br>
Answer and transfer phone calls
<br><br>
Cleanliness
<br><br>
Assist in maintaining the cleanliness and stock levels of Sports Desk, Lobby area and lobby retail and/or marketing campaign displays
<br><br>
Communication and Relationships
<br><br>
Communicate timely and follow up, as appropriate
<br><br>
Provide membership sales team with guest/prospect lead contact information
<br><br>
Active participation in required events, required programs, required meetings and required trainings
<br><br>
OTHER JOB FUNCTIONS
<br>Flexibility in work schedule, as job may require day and evening shifts, weekends and holidays
<br><br>

SKILLS &amp; KNOWLEDGE
