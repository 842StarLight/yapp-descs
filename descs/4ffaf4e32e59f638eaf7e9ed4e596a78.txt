<div>
<b>
Cedar Fair</b> is seeking a <b>Corporate Marketing Communications Manager</b>, based at the <b>Charlotte, NC</b> corporate office. The Corporate Marketing Communications Manager leads strategy development and execution of employee communications, executive communications and public relations, media relations and social media programs. This position works alongside Investor Relations, Human Resources and Park Communications staff overseeeing communications strategy and integrated planning for our park brands, media relations, crisis communications, and public policy, brand-building and promotions. This role will also participate in setting strategic direction for Corporate messaging and provide counseling on issues that impact the company’s reputation.</div>
</div>
<div>
<b>
Qualifications</b><p></p>
<ul>
<li>
7+ years’ experience successfully leading and managing Corporate Communications/Public Relations strategy and program execution with recent experience leading digital/social media efforts.</li>
<li>
Bachelor’s Degree in Communications, Public Relations, or Journalism and/or experience equivalent; MBA or MS in business desired.</li>
<li>
Hands on, focused, consultative experience with solid business acumen combined with excellent written and verbal communication skills.</li>
<li>
Experience in building relationships and managing complex projects on time.</li>
<li>
Crisis Communications background.</li>
<li>
Sound judgement to know when to elevate issues to leadership.</li>
<li>
Proficiency in managing and influencing executive management and peers.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of current best practices on understanding and managing social media behaviors and channels (beyond Facebook); Working knowledge of media monitoring and social listening platforms (e.g., Cision, Meltwater, Crimson Hexagon).</li>
<li>
Lifelong learner and storyteller with natural curiosity about people, products and processes who can influence others through effective persuasion.</li>
</ul>

Responsibilities</b><p></p>
<ul>
<li>
Experience delivering strong employee engagement and consumer awareness of high-impact initiatives including new event and product launches, promotions, and other integrated marketing campaigns.</li>
<li>
Extensive Integrated Communications Planning and Execution experience collaborating with other key marketing stakeholders, agency partners and vendors is a requirement.</li>
<li>
Experience leading crisis communications including conducting on- and off-camera media interviews, as well as providing direction and counseling to internal stakeholders and resources.</li>
<li>
Experience in successfully leading across organizational boundaries, including strong relationship management skills.</li>
<li>
Experience in working with top leadership and C-suite with diplomacy and discretion.</li>
<li>
Strong writing experience including press releases, byline articles, blogs and scripts.</li>
<li>
Ability to understand and recognize emerging issues across social media channels with ability to develop and implement plans to address.</li>
<li>
Experience in measuring and reporting communications impact.</li>
<li>
Works directly with CMO to create the company’s external and internal messages, ensuring integration and consistency of messaging.</li>
<li>
Coordinates closely with Park Marketing teams, internal SMEs, and executive management to ensure all communications (including Social Media) are aligned with company positions, plans and objectives.</li>
<li>
Leads the effort to align and integrate park communications across channels including paid, earned and owned content including blog, influencers and UGC for timely and consistent messaging.</li>
<li>
Lead efforts to source and select SAAS tools as needed for media monitoring, content publishing, editorial calendars and others as needed.</li>
<li>
Serves as principal day-to-day Corporate Communications Contact.</li>
<li>
Works directly with Marketing, Sales, Corporate Alliances and HR on creation and maintenance of content on Corporate Website, news releases and other corporate communications. As appropriate, assists Investor Relations on website material, publications and announcements.</li>
<li>
Collaborates with Parks on news releases and media relations supporting new product announcements, special events and promotions.</li>
<li>
Proactively uses media relations to support key corporate business objectives</li>
<li>
Leads frequent professional development training (e.g., media training, writing, social media best practices) and process improvement for communications and other staff as needed, ensuring best practices in implementation, and consistency throughout company.</li>
<li>
Monitors and measures effectiveness of corporate communications programs.</li>
<li>
Budgeting and forecasting responsibility as it relates to both Corporate and Park resources.</li>
<li>
Adheres to and enforces all Cedar Fair and specific Park policies and procedures, including safety, attendance and EEO policies, and demonstrates a commitment to customer service in all aspects of employment.</li>
</ul>
