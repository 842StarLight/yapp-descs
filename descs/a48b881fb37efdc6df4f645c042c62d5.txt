<h2>Data Architect</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><div>Concurrency, Inc. is seeking a Data Architect which requires both broad and deep technology knowledge and the ability to architect data solutions by mapping customer business requirements to end-to-end technology solutions. Demonstrated ability to engage in senior level technology decision maker discussions related to agility, business value, data warehousing, and cloud-oriented data solutions is also a must. Data &amp; AI Architects are key enablers for other consultants and partner staff by sharing deep knowledge in large enterprise implementations, best practices, and frameworks or patterns. They oversee delivery of the largest, most complex, and most strategic projects for enterprise customers.</div></div>
<div><div>

<div><ul>
<li>

<li>
Author functional requirements and technical design documentation</li>
<li>

<li>
Lead strategic planning and roadmap development with clients</li>
<li>
Work with BA and PM teams to plan project sprints, scope and resource allocation</li>
<li>
Manage at project milestones to ensure successful solution delivery and client satisfaction</li>
