<h2>Outbound Customer Service Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Alarm New England is looking for a Customer Service Professional who specializes in outbound customer care to join our Customer Experience team. This role is part of an exciting new team being built within Alarm New England that will deliver world class service to our incredible customers. Your days will be varied and challenging with great satisfaction coming from being an expert in so many areas of customer care. Ample training will be provided to help you learn new skills and there will be a strong leadership backstop to help you grow to your fullest potential.</p>
<p><b>Your Day-to-Day Essential Functions: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Proactively reach out to existing customers that are experiencing trouble on their alarm system.</li>
<li>Help them with issues like setting up a pass code, troubleshoot why their system is not communicating to us, resolve incorrect phone numbers, gather eMail addresses.</li>
<li>Send review requests to customers who are happy!</li>
<li>Create service tickets for the rest of the customer experience team when we have to send a technician.</li>
<li>Take detailed notes on all customer interactions and input them into our systems.</li>
<li>Hit daily call number goals.</li>
<li>Working off daily lists, track clearly which customer issues have been resolved and which are still outstanding.</li>
<li>Ensure consistently a positive customer experience.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What you bring to the Team: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Technically inclined and computer proficient.</li>
<li>The ability to organize and manage multiple priorities with strong time management skills.</li>
<li>Strong attention to detail and notetaking to record all customer activity in our systems.</li>
<li>Delivery of outstanding customer service and troubleshooting experience.</li>
<li>Master our company’s software systems, processes and customer experience standards.</li>
<li>Desire to be part of a high-performance team and a strong contributing team player.</li>
<li>Wish to learn and grow in your career and contribute to team’s process innovation.</li>
<li>Associates degree and/or a minimum of 2 years of customer service experience, dispatch or technical background.</li>
</ul>
