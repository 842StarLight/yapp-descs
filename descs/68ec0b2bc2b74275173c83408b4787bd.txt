<p><b>Product Manager for ODM Projects</b></p>
<p>
 Working location: Irvine, CA
 </p>
<p>Advantech Corporation (www.advantech.com)</p>
<p>
Advantech (www.advantech.com), the leading Embedded Platform Hardware provider, has been an innovator in the development and manufacturing of high-quality, high-performance embedded solutions in the industrial computing and automation markets since 1983.
</p>
<p>For over thirty years, Advantech has been refining what is possible in the ePlatform services market, offering comprehensive system integration hardware, software, customer-driven service, and global logistics support. Advantech cooperates closely with our partners to help provide complete solutions for a wide array of applications across a diverse range of industries.</p>
<p>
 <b>Duties:</b></p>
<p>
The Product Manager will be focused on ODM hardware products situated in many different vertical markets including projects in healthcare, automotive, signage, industrial, gaming, surveillance, and IOT for tier 1 global market leaders. The Product Manager would be responsible for the project management, product planning, life cycle management, and market penetration for Advantech solutions.</p>
<p>
The Product Manager would need to be successful at developing expert domain knowledge of a multiple markets through identifying key market players, competition, channel partners, and technology requirements.</p>
<p>
The Product Manager is expected to:</p>
<ul>
<li><p>Develop Hardware Based (x86 and RISC) customer-specific solutions (ODM/OEM) by providing pre-sales and post-sales technical support, feasibility verification, product proposal, and project management throughout the entire development process.
</p></li>
<li>
<p>Manage the customer’s needs through understanding their business objectives and technology requirements and providing Advantech’s solution offerings, product roadmaps, and resource capabilities.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Capable of working with customers and cross-functional colleagues in all relevant departments across the organization to successfully manage Advantech’s standard and custom products.</p>
</li>
<li><p>Manage the product life cycle from NPI (New Product Introduction) to EOL (End of Life), component Phase-Out, Engineering change notifications, pricing, revenue and profit.
</p></li>
<li><p>Develop effective marketing, product strategy and expert domain knowledge of target markets in terms of product offering, technologies and market positioning.
</p></li>
<li>
<p>Create and provide product/market training materials for sales and customers.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Participate in trade show and customer visits. Periodical travel is required</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Other duties as required or assigned.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Bachelor’s degree in Electrical Engineering, Computer Science or a related technical degree; Advanced business degree/MBA is highly desirable. PMP Certification is a plus.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>At least five years of experience in product management, project management, product engineering or technical support in the PC industry or related fields.</p>
</li>
<li><p>Must have working knowledge of x86 and RISC architecture.
</p></li>
<li>
<p>Experience in one or more of the following fields are a plus: Digital Signage, Medical Computer Products, Surveillance, Tablet and Handheld devices, Fleet Management Hardware, Display Technologies or Servers.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Excellent English written and verbal communication skills is a must</p>
</li>
<li><p>Excellent teamwork skills and capable of working independently in multinational organizations.
</p></li>
<li><p>Bilingual (English and Mandarin) is a plus in order to communicate with factories and vendors in Taiwan and China more effectively.
</p></li>
</ul>
<p><b>Key Characteristics</b><b>:</b></p>
<p>
Quick learner, team oriented, good listener, positive ‘can-do’ attitude, energetic, innovative, consultative mindset. Must be a problem solver, with a strong customer focus. Motivated by results and excellence.</p>
