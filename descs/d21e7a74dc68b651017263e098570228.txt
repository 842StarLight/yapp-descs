<h2>Project Lead - Enterprise Technology</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>The Company:</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>
WeWork is building the future of workplace experience. We provide the software platform that many of the world’s most dynamic and forward looking organizations use to improve the interaction between employees and their workplace, through meeting room scheduling, beacon and sensor technology integrations, and space utilization analytics and insights. We drive workplace productivity, efficiency and happiness for our incredible customers worldwide.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
The Role:</b></p>
<p>
As a Project/Functional Lead in the We Work Professional Services team, you will play a key role in creating solutions that achieve customer’s business outcomes and lead the We Work services team, customer teams and 3rd party vendor teams from project kick off to successful go live.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>You will be working with C-level executives, IT leaders, real estate groups, facility management teams and more, to demonstrate how WeWork capabilities will add value to their workplace experience. You will establish credibility and confidence from the first meeting and manage and exceed customer expectations throughout the project/program journey.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>You will be the leader, a functional expert, keeper of project budget, guardian of the project scope and a trusted advisor who has the customers’ best interests at heart, sometimes that may include convincing customers that what they want may not be what they actually need nor what the industry best practices recommend.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Primary Responsibilities Include:</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>
Project management</p>
<ul>
<li><p>
Provide oversight and governance to enterprise customer implementations</p></li>
<li><p>
Develop and keep up to date project plans</p></li>
<li><p>
Build and maintain strategic customer relationships at all levels of the organization</p></li>
<li><p>
Manage the activities of the WeWork project team. Be a mentor to members of the team and delegate effectively.</p></li>
<li><p>
Coordinate with cross functional teams including Product Management, Engineering, Sales, Customer Success Management and Support</p></li>
<li><p>
Publish internal and customer facing status reports at the day to day level and at executive steering committee levels</p></li>
<li><p>
Manage all project escalations including internal and customer-related</p></li>
<li><p>
Actively manage project financials, including project team utilization, budget vs burn hours, project margin, project travel and other expenses</p></li>
<li><p>
Actively seek to optimize resource utilization and project billable targets</p></li>
<li><p>
Refine, improve our implementation methodology and execute projects accordingly</p></li>
<li><p>
Check compliance of solution delivery against our quality and stage gate checklists and drive for 100% compliance and adoption of recommended processes and tools.</p></li>
<li><p>
Conduct knowledge gathering, best practices development and sharing sessions and assist to develop training and enablement materials.
</p></li>
<li><p>Track issues and articulate the impact of different resolution options in the client’s terminology, escalating to management and cross functional teams in a timely manner</p></li>
<li><p>
Proactively raise and document risks, develop mitigation plans and track dependencies.</p></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>
Professional Service Sales</p>
<ul>
<li><p>
Articulate professional services offerings, value proposition, pricing and other parameters in relation to the overall sale of WeWork Enterprise technology products</p></li>
<li><p>
Estimate and size pipeline projects and tailor Statement of Work for each enterprise customer.
</p></li>
<li><p>Negotiate terms of the statement of work in the joint best interest of client and WeWork and close services deals.</p></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>
Functional and Process Analysis, Analytics and Problem Solving</p>
<ul>
<li><p>
Quickly grasp customer’s business problems and objectives</p></li>
<li><p>
Lead analysis of the workplace metrics and productivity data collected from WeWork and other vendor implemented solutions.
</p></li>
<li><p>Identify insights and recommendations on increasing real estate utilization, team productivity and team communication</p></li>
<li><p>
Lead the project team to document analysis and other deliverables</p></li>
<li><p>
Stay up to date with each release of our SaaS products and able to relate new feature/functionality with real customer use cases.</p></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Required Skills/Experience:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Technical consulting experience with a US or global system integrator, service provider, delivery center preferred.</li>
<li>
Minimum 5 years experience managing enterprise-level projects for technology product companies</li>
<li>
Competent in project schedule, scope, budget control, accounting and reporting</li>
<li>
Functional understanding of Software as a Service as a product delivery mechanism, web, database, object oriented software technologies, and salesforce.com or Force.com</li>
<li>
Familiarity with business process analysis and data analytics</li>
<li>
Expertise with office productivity tools such as MS Office or G Suite
</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills in verbal, written and presentation formats</li>
<li>
Ability to travel approximately 50% of the time.</li>
</ul>
