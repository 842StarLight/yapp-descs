<h2>Sr. Business Development Director, New York, USA</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div><div>
<p><b>The Role</b></p>
<p>
We are currently looking for an experienced sales professional to drive our direct selling efforts. The Senior Business Development Director (aka Account Executive) will be the face of our company to top executives of prospective clients. You will be evangelizing our brand and cold calling key decision makers at targeted companies. This is an entrepreneurial and self-directed role that will play a large role in the growth of the company.</p>
<p><b>
What you’ll do</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Build a scalable sales process to replicate and drive revenue</li>
<li>Create and prioritize a pipeline of prospective new clients</li>
<li>Build relationships with prospective clients through cold calls/emails, in-person meetings, attending trade shows and other industry events.</li>
<li>Take ownership of the entire sales cycle from initial contact, demo, proposal, RFP response, negotiation, to execution and handover to our client services team.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>How you’ll deliver it</b></p>
<ul>
<li>These will be determined by you, in collaboration with your manager, and you’ll update them regularly to keep your contribution relevant as we evolve. You’ll do this via your Personal VOS.</li>
<li>This role will be aimed at driving the team towards new verticals and revenue streams. Experience building strategies, working cross-functionally, and executing these strategies will be extremely important.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>To help you be successful, we’re looking for</b></p>
<ul>
<li>10+ years of mid-market or enterprise sales or business development</li>
<li>Track record in identifying new revenue streams, developing new value propositions, and selling into new verticals</li>
<li>Proven success bringing in new business and driving revenue growth</li>
<li>Superior pipeline management skills</li>
<li>Demonstrated negotiation skills with ability to write formal proposals, respond to technical RFPs and structure high value contracts</li>
<li>A self-motivated, scrappy, high energy, and hardworking attitude</li>
<li>The ability to travel to meet client executives</li>
</ul>
