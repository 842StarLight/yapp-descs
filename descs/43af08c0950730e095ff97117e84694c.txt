<b>About the Role</b><br>
<br>
Uber Eats is seeking a customer centric, resourceful, and driven leader to oversee our strategic partnerships for the largest national enterprise accounts across North America. The Head of National Enterprise Sales will oversee a team of managers and multiple markets, focusing on people management and development, as well as best-in-class sales with local, regional, and national cross-functional team collaboration. The ideal candidate has experience focused on sales and customer success in the enterprise space and is a data-driven strategic thinker with experience in both mature and start-up environments.<br>
<br>
You'll succeed in this role if: you embrace rolling up your sleeves with your team, you enjoy finding the balance between short-term needs and long-term investments, your working style is collaborative, process-driven and you are decisive with creative problem solving skills.</div>
<div><b>What You'll Do</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Directly recruit, manage, and mentor the National enterprise sales team responsible for acquiring, retaining, and developing the key US/CAN partnerships</li>
<li>
Responsible for establishing a customer obsessed culture and developing and operationalizing sales strategy to achieve sales critical metrics</li>
<li>
Own the full sales cycle from outreach to close to customer success post-sale; engage and align effectively in C-level meetings with restaurant partners, actively listen to understand business objectives and challenges, and align Eats solutions to accomplish partner goals</li>
<li>
Manage and report accurate forecast and pipeline, achieve sales goals on a quarterly basis, and coach account executives and customer success managers on strategies to reach their goals</li>
<li>
Retain and grow existing restaurant partner base by delivering excellent customer experience via strategic account management</li>
<li>
Establish an effective partnership with operations, product, engineering, and marketing teams<br>
</li>
</ul>
