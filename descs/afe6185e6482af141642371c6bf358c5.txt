<h2>Qualified Purchasing Agent</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">The Housing Authority of Bergen County (HABC) is seeking an experienced Qualified Purchasing
<br>Agent to lead its Purchasing/Procurement Department. The HABC is seeking an energetic, wellorganized, and proactive professional leader, committed to good work habits and a positive attitude.
<br><b>Duties include, but are not limited to:</b> oversight of the procurement process in accordance with NJ
<br>Local Public Contracts Law; public bidding; purchase orders; contracts; Board resolutions;
<br>contractor management; vendor relations and project management.
<br>This is a full-time, exempt position with excellent benefits package and participation in the N. J.
<br>Public Employees' Retirement System (PERS).
<br><br>
<b>PRINCIPAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Establishes and develops accepted purchasing methods and records in accord with</li>
</ul>
prescribed laws, rules, regulations, standards, policies and procedures.
<br><ul>
<li>Plans, prepares, and coordinates Public Bidding, Requests for Qualifications (RFQs),</li>
</ul>
Requests for Proposals (RFPs)
<br><ul>
<li>Reviews submitted bids for content and after thorough analysis makes</li>
</ul>
recommendations for acceptance or rejection of bids.
<br><ul>
<li>Reviews and issues contracts for goods and services, operating and maintenance</li>
</ul>
equipment, materials and supplies.
<br><ul>
<li>Communicate with vendors and contractors in a professional and efficient manner.</li>
<li>Handles complaints by taking appropriate action.</li>
<li>Develops and maintains a centralized purchasing system including all necessary</li>
</ul>
procedures, forms, electronic data, and filing.
<br><ul>
<li>Prepares periodic reports and statements.</li>
<li>Maintains bidder’s list and calendar to ensure timely contract management.</li>
<li>Assigns, supervises and reviews work of the Procurement Department.</li>
<li>Confers with other department representatives on the standardization of supplies used</li>
</ul>
and the need for specialized equipment.
<br><ul>
<li>Responsible for contracting with licensed professionals, consultants, and attorneys as</li>
</ul>
needed
<br><ul>
<li>Will be required to learn various types of electronic and/or manual recording and</li>
</ul>
information systems used by the Authority.
<br><ul>
<li>Performs other Authority activities, as requested.</li>
<li>Business conditions or Authority needs may require from time to time, at employer’s</li>
</ul>
discretion, changes in job duties, responsibilities or location.
<br>KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS AND ABILITIES REQUIRED
<br><ul>
<li>Graduation from an accredited college or university with a Bachelor’s degree.</li>
<li>Minimum of five (5) years of experience in the purchasing/procurement field.</li>
<li>Minimum of three (3) years of experience in a managerial capacity.</li>
<li>Knowledge of laws, rules, and regulations affecting purchasing, specifically the New</li>
</ul>
<b>Jersey Local Public Contracts Law &amp; Regulation N.J.S.A. 40A:</b> 11-1 &amp; N.J.A.C. 5:34.
<br><ul>
<li>Proficiency with Microsoft Office software</li>
<li>Experience related to technical specifications, contractor management, purchasing of</li>
</ul>
goods and services, equipment, materials and supplies on a large scale.
<br><ul>
<li>Knowledge of accepted preparation methods for commodity pricing methods.</li>
</ul>
<b>Posted:</b> 06/21/2019
<br><ul>
<li>Ability to interpret market prices and trends and apply such interpretations to HABC</li>
<li>Ability to establish and maintain harmonious working relationships.</li>
<li>Ability to review and prepare specifications.</li>
<li>Ability to recommend suitable action on bids.</li>
<li>Ability to establish and maintain purchasing and other records.</li>
<li>Ability to read, write, understand and communicate in the English language.</li>
</ul>
Multilingual a plus.
<br><br>
<b>WORKING CONDITIONS AND PHYSICAL REQUIREMENTS:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Must be able to physically perform the basic life operational functions of climbing,</li>
</ul>
stooping, kneeling, crouching, reaching, standing, walking, pushing, pulling, lifting,
<br>fingering, grasping, feeling, talking, hearing and performing repetitive motions.
<br><ul>
<li>Must be able to perform light work exerting up to 10 pounds of force occasionally.</li>
<li>Persons with mental or physical disabilities are eligible for this position as long as they</li>
</ul>
can perform the essential functions of the job after reasonable accommodation is made
<br>to their known limitations. If the accommodation cannot be made, because it would
<br>cause the employer undue hardship, such person may not be eligible.
<br><br>
<b>CERTIFICATION:</b><br>
<b>In accordance with N.J.S.A. 40A:</b> 11-9, applicants will be required to possess a valid
<br>Qualified Purchasing Agent (QPA) certification issued by the State Division of Local
<br>Government Services, in the Department of Community Affairs.
