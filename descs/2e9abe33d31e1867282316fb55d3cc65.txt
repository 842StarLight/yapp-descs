<h2>AM 1450 – News/Talk Brand Manager and Morning Host</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Townsquare Media Group
<br>Bozeman, MT
<br><br>
Townsquare Media Bozeman includes well-known brands like 100.7 XL Country, AM 1450, 95.1 The Moose, 96.7 Kiss FM and My 103.5.
<br><br>
<b>POSITION OVERVIEW:</b><br>
A Townsquare Media Brand Manager is the brand captain for their radio station. You will develop relationships with your on air team, the community, other Brand Managers and the Digital Managing Editor, sales department, management, engineering and clients. You will be the leader of your station and its content moving forward on air and in digital. You will, through your day-to-day efforts, be an exemplary example of the execution of the vision of Townsquare Media.
<br><br>
<b>CANDIDATE MUST:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Show by example the importance of the relationship between station and the local community.</li>
<li>Be extremely creative in the development of unique content, promotions and multi-media strategies.</li>
<li>Positively balance your vision for a radio station with the needs of the business.</li>
<li>Help extend your brands in a marketplace and understand the significance of that for station performance.</li>
<li>Breath personality, understand the right balance of topics, news and lifestyle for the target and consider social networking and digital platforms as the key to the future and leverage that to the station's advantage.</li>
<li>Relentlessly create an environment of constant creation &amp; implementation of dual purpose ideas that grow ratings and revenue.</li>
<li>Win in a competitive landscape with enthusiasm, high standards for yourself and those who work for you.</li>
<li>Develop solutions for issues and problems, then execute them.</li>
<li>Have creativity, heart, passion, intelligence and a sense of team across all Townsquare Bozeman stations.</li>
</ul>
<b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Quickly become proficient on NexGen, if not already, both in studio and production rooms</li>
<li>Attend periodic meetings with the Townsquare Brand Captain for your format</li>
<li>Host Morning drive radio program and be live on air 6am to 9am Monday – Friday</li>
<li>Spend time each day doing daily show prep</li>
<li>Do commercial production daily/as necessary</li>
<li>Report directly to Market President</li>
<li>Attend programming and sales meetings as requested</li>
<li>Work with station "voice" to keep station's imaging fresh including timely promos for upcoming events, contests and promotions</li>
<li>Total direct responsibility for the overall sound of your radio station</li>
<li>Work with nationally syndicated programs for proper execution and producing localized imaging and content</li>
<li>Develop and execute local service elements of station, i.e. local news, weather, traffic, alerts, road reports, etc.</li>
<li>Responsible for all aspects of station lineup, including syndicated talent</li>
<li>Responsible for management of any additional live, local shows including weekend shows</li>
<li>Execute and host all off-site live broadcasts and station events, whether originated by sales or programming, including set up and tear down</li>
<li>Learn the Townsquare blogging procedures</li>
<li>Blog a minimum of 12 blogs per week on the station website – local and regional content (national content must be approved prior to posting)</li>
<li>Facebook daily posts before and during your show (this does not count towards blogging requirements)</li>
<li>Able to voice track or go live on one or more Townsquare radio stations when needed, vacation relief, sick days or in case of emergency</li>
</ul>
<b>SUGGESTED QUALIFICATIONS/EDUCATION:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>College Degree preferred</li>
<li>Previous successful experience as broadcast show host required; news/talk preferred but not required</li>
<li>Previous experience with technical platforms for broadcast delivery; NexGen experience preferred</li>
<li>Previous on-air programming experience required</li>
</ul>
BENEFITS

</ul>
