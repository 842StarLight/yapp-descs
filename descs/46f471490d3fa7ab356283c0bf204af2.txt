<h2>Production Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Design, produce and install all types of high quality signage. Handle call-ins and walk-in prospects and customers in a courteous and professional manner. Demonstrate a professional image of the SIGNARAMA brand always.</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li><b>Design and create all types of signs.</b></li>
<li><b>Mask, weed and apply vinyl to various substrates.</b></li>
<li><b>Print, mount, trim and finish various digital graphics and signage.</b></li>
<li><b>Must be able to use an X-Acto knife and ruler efficiently.</b></li>
<li><b>Properly load and use a cold pressure laminator following standard safety procedures.</b></li>
<li><b>Load and cut vinyl on a plotter.</b></li>
<li><b>Load media, inks and generate quality prints from digital print equipment.</b></li>
<li><b>Can lift 80lbs!!!!!</b></li>
<li><b>Move raw materials, sheets and roll goods for manufacturing.</b></li>
<li><b>Operate a vertical panel saw using standard safety procedures.</b></li>
<li><b>Maintain machinery in good working order.</b></li>
<li><b>Greet customers and answer phones in a professional and knowledgeable manner.</b></li>
<li><b>Accurately fill in sign layout forms, price estimates and invoices.</b></li>
<li><b>Assist in operating and managing the store when needed.</b></li>
<li><b>Travel to different locations within the community to preview work areas, give estimates and install various types of signs.</b></li>
<li><b>Apply vehicle and window graphics.</b></li>
<li><b>Keep a running inventory of all stock and supplies.</b></li>
<li><b>Complete in store training utilizing the following: Front Counter Sales, Sign Production Techniques, Key Lines and the Owner's Manual.</b></li>
<li><b>Maintain an organized, clean and professional store appearance at all times.</b></li>
</ul>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Must be computer knowledgeable and willing to learn to be proficient in various software programs.</li>
<li>Must also pass basic math and spelling tests.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>PHYSICAL DEMANDS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Must be able to climb a ladder while lifting signs.</li>
<li>Must be able to retrieve various types of substrates from a substrate rack including the upper shelves.</li>
<li>Must be in good physical condition and can lift over 80lbs.</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
