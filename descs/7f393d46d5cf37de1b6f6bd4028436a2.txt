<h2>Marketing Manager (For Smart Cities Expansion)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Utility Metering Solutions (UMS) is a product-independent firm specializing in the design, build, integration, and maintenance of utility programs, including Advanced Metering, Billing Systems, Smart City Design &amp; Integration, Network Lighting &amp; Controls, and Water Conservation. By combining the best technology, solutions, and support, we enable our clients to improve customer service, manage workforce's more effectively, recover lost revenue, and conserve resources. UMS offers a wide range of standalone services—from feasibility and design, to meter installation, to systems integration—to comprehensive end-to-end utility programs. This allows us the flexibility to partner with utilities and municipalities at any stage of a project. Though UMS is a product-independent firm, our knowledge, respect, and strong industry connections enable us to build custom solutions that meet the specific needs of our customers. Our team is committed to working with each and every community that we come in contact with. Likewise, we are seeking individuals, who are highly motivated, dependable, positive, and have good work ethic, to become a part of our growing company.
<br><br>
Utility Metering Solutions is seeking a highly motivated, creative, and successful individual to fill a full-time position as a Marketing Manager (For Smart Cities Expansion). The successful candidate will be responsible for designing, creating, and delivering marketing programs to support the growth and expansion of smart cities across the United States.
<br><br>
The UMS Marketing Manager, for Smart Cities Expansion, will research market conditions in local, regional, or national areas, gather information to determine potential sales, create marketing campaign content, and convey brand messages.
<br><br>
ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS
<br><br>
The purpose of the Marketing Manager, Smart Cities Expansion , position is to help UMS plan, coordinate, or shape the future of public utilities and city governments. We are expanding and in rapid growth mode, so you will wear many hats, but your primary responsibilities will include the following:
<br><br>
Manage our content marketing strategy, including compilation, development, and improvement of promotional content (website, presentations, and other social media channels).
<br><br>
Technical writing for external communication such as website content, white papers, presentations, videos, blog posts, forums, etc.
<br><br>
Assist sales with content development for RFP responses. Revamping content and messaging for custom proposals will be a high priority.
<br><br>
Stay abreast of changes in the services offered by UMS, and translate those updates into content, to ensure internal and external information remains accurate.
<br><br>
Manage the Marketing/PR calendar.
<br><br>
Ensure that UMS has a steady drumbeat of customer announcements, speaking events, and thought leadership.
<br><br>
Drive inbound traffic strategy. This could be SEO, paid search, PR, traditional advertising, etc. Write the plan and execute. Work with outside consultants where appropriate to develop and maintain a strong brand identity.
<br><br>
Monitor industry growth rates, relevant trends, and gather market intelligence.
<br><br>
Work closely with the Vice President to educate both internal and external stakeholders on our services and their benefits. Articulate the benefits of our products and services, and promote the story of UMS, and how our customers benefit.
<br><br>
Talk to our customers, and represent UMS in the market, at all levels.
<br><br>
Overall, be scrappy. Do whatever you can to overhaul and expand our image, build an audience and then turn them into qualified sales leads.
<br><br>
Develop and monitor trends, and conduct analysis to identify advantages/disadvantages to help better position the company for future opportunities.
<br><br>
