<p><b>Who We’re Looking For</b></p>
<p><b>
Managing Editor – Performance Marketing, Salt Lake City</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>As a Managing Editor on the Performance Marketing team, you will directly manage a team of Copywriters and work to ensure copy aligns with business goals and adheres to brand voice and tone guidelines, internal marketing scopes, and marketing channel best practices.</p>
<p></p>
<p>In addition to providing clear, consistent editorial feedback on all copy projects, you will work with writers to develop their skills. You’ll also hold weekly 1:1s with Copywriters and meet regularly with marketing and functional leadership to align Copy team development, capabilities, and availability with marketing plans and content needs.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Beyond team management and craft development responsibilities, you’ll support the Copy Director on high-level projects and contribute to relevant company-wide efforts.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Breaking Down Your Day-to-Day</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Manage, supervise, and mentor a team of Copywriters and Editors</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Own and maintain onboarding processes and documentation</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Provide clear and consistent feedback designed to reinforce strengths, improve skills, and boost copy efficacy</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Work with the Copy Director to establish and communicate Copy team expectations</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Prioritize editing queue based on competing Brand team requests and deadlines</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Connect Copywriters with channel stakeholders to provide clarity on channel goals, metrics, and best practices</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Identify opportunities to improve both efficiency and quality through process</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Edit for organization, clarity, voice, tone, grammar, and punctuation</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Plan writers’ workshops based on opportunity and forthcoming content needs as established by content plans</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Hold regular 1:1s with Copywriters to discuss craft, feedback, career development, and other timely issues</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Work with marketing leaders to align on Copywriter feedback, understand Brand team goals, and position Copywriters for success</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Collaborate with functional and channel stakeholders to ensure the Copy team is meeting project needs, aware of channel best practices, and contributing to strategic goals</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Work cross-functionally to coordinate large projects that require the entire Copy team</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Meet regularly with Copy Director to share team-related updates and align on priorities</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Champion the strategic direction of Performance Marketing</p>
</li>
</ul>

</li>

</li>

<p>4+ years’ experience in writing and editing</p>
</li>

<p>Basic knowledge of SEO, PPC, Email Marketing, Paid Social, Content Strategy, and CRO</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Experience in direct-response marketing preferred</p>
</li>

<p>Ability to adhere to brand compliance guidelines</p>
</li>

</li>
</ul>
