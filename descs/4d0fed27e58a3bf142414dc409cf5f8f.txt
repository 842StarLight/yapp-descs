<h2>AI Workload Strategist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>We're hiring AI Workload Strategists throughout the U.S. to advise companies and government agencies on how they can remodel their business value through artificial intelligence technology. The Dell AI Workload Strategist’s primary function is to support the expansion of the AI Workload business for Dell Technologies (new markets, new buyers, deeper share of wallet) based on deep expertise in data science, advanced analytics / AI. While this is not a sales position, compensation is tied to the results of the AI Workload business. The role focuses on Commercial customer base (Fortune 2000-5000), State, Local, &amp; Federal governments. You will be required to understand trade-offs between on-premises and cloud implementations as well as the role of security and privacy. It requires the ability to persuade at the most senior levels, negotiate workload architectures and drive many stakeholders in a single direction. Key customer contacts will be diverse: c-suite; workload specialists (e.g. application developers), business owners; and IT system administrators.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
You will be expected to keep up to date on the latest technologies and how they are implemented. This includes open source frameworks (e.g. TensorFlow, Caffe, PyTorch, Spark), accelerator technologies (GPU, FPGA, ASIC), programming languages (e.g. Python, Java), Machine Learning (e.g. boosted tree, clustering) &amp; Deep Learning (e.g. CNN, RNN, LTSM) techniques and the particular demands around data governance / privacy, data cleansing, lifecycle model management. Likewise, you need to be well versed in Dell EMC infrastructure products: servers; storage; networking; data protection; and systems management.</p>
<p></p>

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES:

<p>This role is supported by Dell AI Workload Specialists, Dell AI Workload Architects, Dell Professional Services and Systems Consultants from Dell EMC. This role is a highly visible one outside of the company. AI Workload Strategists will be expected to a have well-honed public voice. They will be speaking at conferences, blogging / vlogging regularly and authoring thought leadership papers. They will interact and share information both ways with the Office of the CTO and the Dell AI Workload Engineering teams and Product Group with a 1 to 3 year vision when advising customers.</p>
