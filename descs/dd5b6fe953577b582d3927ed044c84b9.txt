<h2>IT Project Manager- Vendor Enablement</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<div><b>Role Summary:</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>This role will take direction driving execution and benefit realization on the relationship between Finance and Digital Technology. S/he will be responsible for the operational management and day-to-day execution of technical projects in support of the Accounting global finance centers and transformation teams. Serve as the liaison between our vendor partners and internal stakeholders while working to create processes, methodology, and governance framework to support productivity targets.</div>
<br>
<div>
</div>
<div><b>Essential Responsibilities:</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>In the role of IT Project Manager- Vendor Enablement, you will:</div>
<ul>
<li>Work with technical teams to deliver feature and significant products that meet the Finance function’s needs
</li>
<li>Be essentially the vendor manager for the Accenture relationship. They manage accounting operations for finance
</li>
<li>Drive results through a 3rd party, serve as the single point of contact for all DT related support required by Accenture, Navigate the DT org for project execution and ticket escalation
</li>
<li>Demonstrate expert level skill in problem decomposition and ability to navigate through ambiguity
</li>
<li>Organize project teams of internal and external technical and functional experts based on budget, scope and timeline
</li>
<li>Manage day to day execution of multiple interdependent projects by working directly with global technical teams.
</li>
<li>Engage frequently across stakeholder groups (technical and non-technical); facilitate discussions, provide clarification, author documentation, testing and validation; contributes to design activities and decisions.
</li>
<li>Manage project plan, budget, spend, risk / issues, scope changes and other non-standard events throughout the life of the project</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Qualifications/Requirements:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’ Degree in Computer Science or in “STEM” Majors (Science, Technology, Engineering and Math) from an accredited college or university or equivalent knowledge/experience
</li>
<li>Minimum 5 years of experience in Information Technology
</li>
<li>Minimum 2 years of experience using project management methodologies to deliver business value and drive significant change across an organization</li>
</ul>
<div>Eligibility Requirements:</div>
<ul>
<li>Travel 20% of the time, as required
</li>
<li>Work out of an office located in Houston, TX</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Desired Characteristics:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrated ability to lead in a highly matrixed environment
</li>
<li>Ability to work and communicate with cross-functional teams to drive execution plans w/ shared outcomes
</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability working with 3rd party vendors
</li>
<li>Experience using project management methodologies to deliver business value and drive significant change across an organization</li>
</ul>
<div>Business Acumen:</div>
<ul>
<li>Exposure to at least one enterprise resource planning (ERP) package (preferably Oracle 11i or SAP), Data analytics and visualization tools experience a plus
</li>
<li>Strong problem solving, analytical skills and demonstrated ability to assimilate new information and understand complex topics
</li>
<li>Experienced with agile/iterative project methodology such as Scrum, preferably with experience using agile project management tools such as JIRA or Rally</li>
</ul>
<div>Leadership:</div>
<ul><li>Excellent influencing, interpersonal and communication skills (both written and verbal) with all levels of the organization and with other Baker Hughes businesses</li></ul>
<div>Personal Attributes:</div>
<ul>
<li>Able to work under minimal supervision, adaptive; agile approach to delivery
</li>
<li>Self-starter with ability to manage multiple priorities in a fast-paced work environment with minimal direction to accomplish goals</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Locations:</b></div>
<ul><li>Houston, TX</li></ul>
<div>This is your opportunity to learn more, do more, live the career you have imagined and be part of a truly diverse organization.</div>
