<p><b>Pacific Office Automation</b> is a recognized leader in office management solutions and currently expanding its footprint within the IT Field in <b>Phoenix, AZ.</b> Want to be a part of a team that strives to be a leader within the valley for Managed IT solutions? We are currently looking for talented individuals that are passionate about technology and want to use their skills to help Pacific Office Automation provide an exceptional customer experience for Managed IT customers. Pacific Office Automation provides an environment where employees can expand their current skill set while preparing for future growth in both work and life.</p>
<p><b>IT Field Support Engineer at Pacific Office Automation </b>is the front line when it comes to hands-on hardware and software troubleshooting. The position supports Pacific Office Automation MIT (Managed IT) clients in a variety of roles from troubleshooting client network and system issues to assisting the sales team with planning and implementing new solutions. IT field support provides hands-on support for client hardware and software on Windows workstations &amp; Servers. They also assist in support for networking and local server resources for our customers. They are the face of Pacific Office Automation for their local customers, they serve as a local resource when remote troubleshooting is not an effective way of resolving an IT issue. Regular activities include troubleshooting client operating systems, PC imaging and repair, network troubleshooting, project management and mentorship of junior technicians.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Setup and deploy new equipment to customers in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Assist SE\TAM with assessments for current and potential new clients.</li>
<li>Assist with special internal or external projects</li>
<li>Assist helpdesk with onboarding New customers</li>
<li>Assist helpdesk with deploying new networking equipment (Firewall &amp; Switches ect ).</li>
<li>Assist helpdesk with implementing backup systems (Datto).</li>
<li>Be an escalation point for IT issues and requests that cannot be resolved by the helpdesk.</li>
<li>Document tickets within CRM (ConnectWise) &amp; MWAi.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>AA degree in related field OR 4+ years of professional experience.</li>
<li>Net+ Cert minimum, MCP, MCA, OR MCSE</li>
<li>Good understanding of Windows server’s environment and common operating systems (Windows 7, 8, &amp; 10). With a full understanding of core network technologies.</li>
<li>Good understanding of Microsoft applications.</li>
<li>Good understanding of Email systems and configurations.</li>
</ul>
