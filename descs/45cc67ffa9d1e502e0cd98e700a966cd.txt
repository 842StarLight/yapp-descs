<p>Description</p>
</div>
<div><div>
<p>As the world’s leading water experts, we are dedicated to delivering high-quality water solutions to residential, commercial and industrial customers. Headquartered in Rosemont, Illinois, Culligan International offers technologically advanced, state of the art water filtration products, including: water softeners, water filtration system, industrial water treatment solutions, drinking water systems and whole-house filtration systems.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<p>The Senior Engineer – Industrial Controls provides expert judgment, analysis and direction in the design, installation and troubleshooting of control systems for custom water purification equipment. This individual is competent across all the company’s major product lines and possesses a solid comprehension of complex engineering concepts, practices, and procedures. This role reports to the Manager of Project Engineering but works independently, relying on experience to plan and accomplish goals.</p>
<p><b>Specific Job Function:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Create standardized control systems as required</li>
<li>Develop, install and test all PLC programs for customized systems</li>
<li>Develop, implement and maintain a process to revision control all PLC programs, and enables rapid location of the most current PLC program, both standard and job-specific.</li>
<li>Manage relationship with control panel manufacturers to insure proper documentiaton, scope of supply and cost competetivness</li>
<li>Posses a solid knowledge of applicable regulatory standards (UL, NFPA, local codes, etc…), and ensure that products meet the required specifications</li>
<li>Independently perform PLC system start-ups, and troubleshooting complex PLC issues at on-site customer locations</li>
<li>Mentor junior engineers</li>
<li>Provide controls training as required for other functional areas (Sales, AE’s, Mfg, PE, etc…)</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<b>Requirements</b>
<p><b>Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelors of Science degree in electrical engineering; Masters of Science in engineering a plus</li>
<li>5+ years of experience in PLC and HMI hardware specification &amp; software development, programming from a written specification for Allen-Bradley PLC-5, Control Logix, Compact Logix, Micro Logix systems</li>
<li>Able to read, interpret and utilize mechanical prints, electrical schematics, and P&amp;IDs to drive electrical component &amp; instrument specification and control system design.</li>
<li>Independently convert sequences of operations into automation codes.</li>
<li>Able to select PLC hardware, design/layout/build PLC panels, and program and troubleshoot PLC systems</li>
<li>Comprehensive knowledge and experience in the use of industrial communications protocols including: RS232, RS485, Modbus, TCP/IP, BACnet, Profibus, and Ethernet</li>
<li>Up to 40% travel</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Competencies:</b></p>
<p>To perform the job successfully, an individual should demonstrate the following competencies in this position.</p>
<p><i>Creativity &amp; Innovation Detail Oriented Organization/planning</i></p>
<p><i>Technical Excellence Integrity Oral &amp; Written Communication</i></p>
