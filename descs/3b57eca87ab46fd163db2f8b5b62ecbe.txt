<h2>Government - Land Planner</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Imagine a place where responsibility is shared equally among all employees. Where leaders are approachable, and the open-door policy is the rule. Where employees are tasked to be innovative and work with an entrepreneurial spirit.
<br>Welcome to Manhard Consulting. We see problems as an opportunity for creativity, and we are looking for problem solvers to join our dynamic team. Our firm is organized into small teams to give you a strong sense of accountability and to achieve great results. The dress is business casual, the work environment is pleasant, work hours allow for flexibility, and we have a lot of fun.
<br><br>
Assisting with creating, reviewing, interpreting, and implementing multiple plan and ordinance types. Responsible for drafting of maps, site plans, subdivision plans, and other land planning documents.
<br><br>
<b>Accountability and Duties:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Assist with creating, reviewing, interpreting, and implementing Comprehensive Plans, Neighborhood Plans, Corridor Plans, Downtown Plans, and other such plans.</li>
<li>Assist with creating, reviewing, interpreting, and implementing Zoning Ordinances, Subdivision Ordinances, Sign Ordinances, and other such development ordinances and regulations.</li>
<li>Drafting and design of maps, site plans, subdivision plans and plats, and other such land planning documents.</li>
<li>Drafting and review of technical review memos and reports regarding proposed developments designs, conditional/special uses, variances, PUDs, and other associated development proposals.</li>
<li>Develop, review, and interpret demographic/socio-economic data, fiscal and economic analysis, market data and other such quantitative research information regarding plans, developments, population projections and forecasts, governmental structures, municipal and governmental budgets, and other such applicable instances.</li>
<li>Working knowledge of AutoCAD, ArcGIS, Photoshop, Sketch-Up and other such design software.</li>
<li>Prepare and present testimony and information at public hearings, municipal board and committee meetings, trials, and all other such public forums.</li>
<li>Participate on multi-disciplinary teams and providing planning and design support to those teams.</li>
<li>Participate in company special purpose committees.</li>
<li>Review contract and proposals for structure, liability and fees.</li>
<li>Maintain key client relationships.</li>
<li>Works closely with other disciplines on multi-discipline projects.</li>
<li>Assist in mentoring of key staff and ensure staff within service line are mentored.</li>
<li>Responsible for project schedules and timely completion of projects for self, team members, and subordinates.</li>
<li>Identify inconsistencies or weaknesses and recommend standards and procedures to increase performance/cost and quality.</li>
<li>Keeps supervisor informed of work load and any changes in project scope or additional services.</li>
<li>Provides an accurate accounting of time spent on projects with descriptions for each time entry on a weekly basis.</li>
<li>Participates in marketing, sales and business development efforts where or when requested by supervisor.</li>
</ul>

Bachelor’s degree in relevant area with 5-7 years’ experience
<br><br>
<b>Education and/or Experience:</b><br>
Bachelor’s degree, Master’s degree preferred
<br><br>
<b>Certificates, Licenses, Registrations:</b><br>
Valid driver’s license
<br>Member of the American Planning Association is recommended

<li>Ability to make independent decisions within a general framework.</li>
<li>Strong public speaking and presentation skills.</li>
<li>Knowledge of industry related software.</li>

</ul>
