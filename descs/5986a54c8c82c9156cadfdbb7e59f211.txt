<h2>Jewelry Designer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>JOB SUMMARY: </b></p>
<p>Gennaro Inc. has an exciting opportunity to join our RI based in-house fashion jewelry design team. The candidate must possess superior time and project management skills with the ability to shift gears seamlessly while meeting deadlines and working in a fast-paced environment. Brand experience is preferred but qualified designers please apply. Must have at least 2 – 4 years of design experience and knowledge in Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop.</p>
<p>Responsible for working on a creative team, to design and develop relevant, trend-right product across all company accounts.</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL RESPONSIBILITIES &amp; ACCOUNTABILITIES: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Research style and design trends for each season, and interpret these trends into product that is appropriate for our customers</li>
<li>Create final technical illustrations and samples with specs that can be sent overseas for sample production</li>
<li>Work with the sales team to design product based on customer-specific requests</li>
<li>Work with the product development team to communicate with overseas vendors about resolving any design issues</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Design collections for market that are trend-right, and will appeal to a broad range of customers</li></ul>
<ul><li>Design/manipulate product that aims to meet the target cost</li></ul>
<ul><li>Assist with presentation boards</li></ul>
<ul><li>Collaborate with other designers to complete projects in a timely manner</li></ul>
<ul><li>Multitask on a daily basis</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manipulate with components to produce final samples</li></ul>
<ul><li>Use Illustrator and Photoshop for illustration designs</li></ul>
<p><b>Experience: </b></p>
<p>Bachelor’s degree or equivalent in Fashion Design, or a similar field, fashion jewelry specific preferred. 2 years of design/creative experience.</p>
<p><b>Specialized Knowledge and Skills: </b></p>
<p>Professional, action-oriented, detail-driven, excellent follow-through, problem-solving, high energy, integrity, and team player. Fluent in relevant software (Adobe CC, 3D CAD, MS Office, etc.), tools, materials, and jewelry manufacturing processes</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul><li>design/creative: 2 years (Required)</li></ul>
<p>Education:</p>
<ul><li>Bachelor's (Required)</li></ul>
