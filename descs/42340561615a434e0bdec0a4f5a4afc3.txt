<h2>CUSTODIAL WORKER</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div><div><div>
<div>
<div>UNDER GENERAL SUPERVISION, PERFORMS A VARIETY OF JANITORIAL AND CUSTODIAL DUTIES WHICH ARE RELATED TO THE GENERAL APPEARANCE AND CLEANLINESS OF THE INTERIOR OF EXCHANGE FACILITIES, USING INDUSTRIAL TYPE POWERED EQUIPMENT AND LADDERS/SCAFFOLDS.<br>
<br>
1. SWEEPS FLOORS, HALLWAYS, CORRIDORS, AND STAIRWAYS, AND MOPS, SCRUBS, WAXES, AND POLISHES FLOOR SURFACES USING HEAVY DUTY POWERED EQUIPMENT. PERFORMS NECESSARY MAINTENANCE SUCH AS CLEANING AND OILING EQUIPMENT.<br>
<br>
2. OPERATES HEAVY INDUSTRIAL TYPE VACUUM CLEANER TO CLEAN RUGS AND DRAPES, USING CHEMICALS AND CLEANING SOLUTIONS AS REQUIRED.<br>
<br>
3. WASHES WINDOWS, WALLS AND PARTITIONS, AND CLEANS AND REPLACES VENETIAN BLINDS AND LIGHT FIXTURES, USING LADDER OR SCAFFOLD.<br>
<br>
4. MOVES HEAVY FURNITURE, BOXES OR OTHER OBSTACLES IN ACCOMPLISHMENT OF CLEANING DUTIES. MOVES TRASH, SCRAPS, AND OTHER MATERIAL TO CENTRAL PICK-UP POINT.<br>
<br>
5. CLEANS, ADJUSTS, AND OILS TOOLS AND POWERED EQUIPMENT, AND CHANGES BRUSHES, ROLLERS, PADS AND BLADES.<br>
<br>
6. WASHES, CLEANS, DISINFECTS, DEODORIZES, AND MAINTAINS RESTROOMS IN A CLEAN, ORDERLY, AND SANITARY CONDITION. REPLACES TOILET TISSUE, TOWELS, SOAP AND DEODORIZER, AS REQUIRED.<br>
<br>
7. DUSTS, WAXES, AND POLISHES OFFICE FURNITURE; CLEANS SHELVES AND EMPTIES ASH TRAYS AND WASTE BASKETS.<br>
<br>
8. MAINTAINS STOCK OF RESTROOM AND JANITORIAL SUPPLIES.<br>
<br>
9. MAY DO LIGHT OUTDOOR WORK RELATED TO FACILITY CLEANLINESS SUCH AS PICKING UP LITTER AND SWEEPING STEPS AND SIDEWALKS.<br>
<br>
10. PERFORMS OTHER RELATED DUTIES AS ASSIGNED.<br>
<br>
ACTIVELY COMMUNICATES/PROMOTES THE BENEFITS OF THE IN-STORE CREDIT CARD TO CUSTOMERS IN ORDER TO DRIVE SALES GROWTH AND INCREASE CUSTOMER LOYALTY</div>

<p><b>Who We Are</b></p>
<div>The Army &amp; Air Force Exchange Service, also known as “the Exchange,” is the retailer on US Army and Air Force installations worldwide. An $8 billion enterprise ranked among the top retailers, the Exchange operates department stores, convenience stores, restaurants, movie theaters and an internet shopping site to serve Soldiers, Airmen and their families. In addition to US bases in Germany, Italy, England, Turkey, South Korea and Japan, the Exchange operates stores for US troops in Iraq, Afghanistan and other countries in the region.</div>
