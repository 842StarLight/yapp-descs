<b>The Job</b><br>
<br>
We're looking for a Designer to join our NCAA Digital Design team. As a Designer at NCAA Digital, you'll have the opportunity to work on marketing and sales campaigns for NCAA.com, the NCAA Sports App and Emmy Award-winning March Madness Live with its suite of products across 17 platforms.<br>
<br>
As a Designer, you'll get the chance to innovate, ideate and execute designs, collaborating with cross-functional teams including marketing, design, research, editorial, multimedia, engineering, product and program management. Working together, you'll shape the vision into reality, creating a world-class experience. Join us!<br>
<br>
<b>The Daily</b><ul>
<li>
Partner with the Marketing team to support NCAA's overall strategy, with a strong emphasis on supporting the different campaigns
</li>
<li>Conceptualize innovative ideas for Marketing campaigns, with a vision for creating compelling visuals
</li>
<li>Willingness to work within the established brand guidelines
</li>
<li>Create engaging, high-impact, and brand-consistent static and motion graphics to be used in banner ads, emails, social graphics, and other marketing collateral
</li>
</ul>
<b>Production of assets</b><ul>
<li>
Create and export multiple marketing asset sizes once a look and feel has been established
</li>
<li>Work with Sales to create mocks for sponsored features within our products
</li>
<li>Constantly seek out current and upcoming industry and technology trends and best practices
</li>
<li>Show work early and often.
</li>
<li>Seek out constructive feedback from others (Manager, Marketing, Sales and Creative teams) through presentations/critiques, and actively work with the Marketing Coordinator to filter and prioritize this feedback
</li>
<li>Practice effective communication techniques with all team members, and begin to build relationships with members of other groups on the team, such as Marketing, Editorial, Sales and Social
</li>
<li>Care deeply about the quality of the work, with a desire for improvement
</li>
<li>Willingness to jump in and help with any design needs for day to day tournament or championship operations
</li>
<li>Assist Sr. Designers with product design work if needed</li>
</ul>
