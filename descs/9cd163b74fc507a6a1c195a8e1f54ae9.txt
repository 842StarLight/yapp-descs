<h2>Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Summary:</b></p>
<p>
Looking for a highly motivated individual to work for our growing auction company. This position is a sales position with a base and commission draw. This individual will be responsible for finding, targeting and working with businesses in an effort to consign and sell their items in our weekly auctions. We are looking for someone to focus mainly on large assets like construction equipment, farm &amp; ag, industrial machinery, etc.</p>
<p>
 This position requires mostly local and state-wide travel.</p>
<p><b>
Qualifications &amp; Requirements
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Excellent Customer Service Skills</li>
<li>High School Diploma
</li>
<li>Proficiency in Microsoft Office (Word, Excel, Outlook)</li>
<li>Motivated, Self-Starter</li>
<li>Familiarity with sales experience in related field</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills</li>
<li>Highly organized
</li>
<li>Experience in buying and selling</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Essential Job Functions
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Understanding of how online bidding works and functionality of Auction Software</li>
<li>Knowledge of MAMG companies
</li>
<li>Repocast.com Admin system
</li>
<li>Sales techniques and procedures for an auction</li>
<li>Completing consignor contracts</li>
<li>CRM Software</li>
<li>Ensure all contracts are correct</li>
<li>Work with existing sellers to further sales and streamline labor with our check in departments
</li>
<li>Work with past sellers and noncurrent sellers to revive relationships and rebuild sales</li>
<li>Work with and call on existing sellers lists for specially scheduled auctions and monthly auctions</li>
<li>Handle auction sales leads to meet and provide auction proposals for auctions</li>
<li>Manage and work with marketing department for weekly advertising
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Typical Duties
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Stay up-to-date on Repocast.com auction schedule</li>
<li>Input consignor info for new customers, generate a consignor ID</li>
<li>Effectively communicate with other divisions of Miedema Asset Management Group to develop the best course of action based on the needs of the customer.</li>
<li>Attend all Sales and Marketing meetings, when required</li>
<li>Develop effective working relationships with Sale Coordinators in order to ensure that customers are satisfied</li>
<li>Assist the check-In department when needed</li>
<li>Manage Consignor Drop off Location</li>
<li>Start contract and get all necessary and proper information on consignor's contracts.</li>
<li>Consignor information and consignor id # if person has sold with us in the past</li>
<li>Verify information in system is correct</li>
<li>Make any necessary edits to consignor info in system</li>
<li>Create New consignor in system with all necessary info for new sellers</li>
<li>Organize and communicate items that come in with guys writing items up</li>
<li>Make sure that all items are being added by the end of each day and communicate with General Manager what things still need to be completed</li>
<li>Answer questions to items that are on auction</li>
<li>Answer all emails to Question email link online</li>
<li>Answer all phone calls to Question phone line posted online</li>
<li>Assist in watching items on auction</li>
<li>Assist with loading customers items when needed</li>
<li>Work with specific customers to assist with arrival, point of entry and coordinate customer needs with other account specialists.</li>
<li>Effectively communicate with Sales Manager, Title Department, and Operations Manager regarding equipment to be auctioned off and any potential issues.</li>
<li>Any other duties requested by immediate supervisor</li>
</ul>
