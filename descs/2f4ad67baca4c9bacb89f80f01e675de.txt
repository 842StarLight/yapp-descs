<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>This is an opportunity to lead and innovate in a fast-moving, dynamic company environment. The Sr. Software Project Manager is client facing, confident and consultative. Our ideal candidate will be a proven leader in the pharmaceutical industry, or as a consultant, with a strong track record of working with teams and high level customers to bring new solutions to the healthcare and life-sciences market. The position is based out of San Diego and requires up to 20% travel, domestically and internationally.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Provide hands on expertise in the areas of program management, software implementation, customer support and client account management.</li>
<li>
Lead AGILE software development process acting as scrum master/team leader for a cross functional software development team.</li>
<li>
Act as liaison to clinical trial program management team for representation on solution delivery for a specific clinical trial solution</li>
<li>
Responsible for client satisfaction and delivery metrics (on time / on budget).</li>
<li>
Ensure compliance with all contract deliverables, terms and service level agreements.</li>
<li>
Create and maintain detailed project plans; monitor, track and report on project progress; manage project scope, issues, risks and change orders.</li>
<li>
Direct individual and team weekly goals, and monitor progress through a positive, collaborative, and encouraging work environment.</li>
<li>
Ensure all projects remain on schedule. Items with scheduling risk (potential delays) are proactively reallocated, assigned additional resources or properly re-prioritized.</li>
<li>
Produce timely and accurate reporting against project timelines and other metrics.</li>
<li>
Clearly communicate priorities and status to internal stakeholders.</li>
<li>
Interface with internal and external stakeholders to understand requirements and issues.</li>
<li>
Manage and direct customer support activities.</li>
<li>
Lead client account management teams, post-sales support and organic growth initiatives.</li>
<li>
Develop and implement tailored programs that provide continued value to the customer, ensure contract renewals and drive long-term account growth.</li>
<li>
Participate in various phases of pre-sales support.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
