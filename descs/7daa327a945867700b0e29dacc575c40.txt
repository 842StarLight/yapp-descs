<h2>Corporate Enterprise Skills Transformation Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<div>Coursera was founded by two computer science professors at Stanford with a vision of providing life-transforming learning experiences to anyone, anywhere. It is the world’s largest online learning platform for higher education. 190 of the world’s top universities and industry educators partner with Coursera to offer courses, Specializations, and degrees that empower over 40 million learners around the world to achieve their career goals. Over 1,800 companies use the company’s enterprise platform Coursera for Business to transform their talent. Coursera is backed by leading venture capital firms such as Kleiner Perkins Caufield &amp; Byers, New Enterprise Associates, GSV Capital, International Finance Corporation, Laureate Education Inc., and Learn Capital.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
At Coursera, our Enterprise team plays a key role in increasing global access to a world-class education. We are working with organizations around the world to provide more learning opportunities for their employees and citizens that enrich their careers and drive impact within their company and countries. Over two years since launch, the Enterprise business has grown rapidly and now serves 1,800+ enterprises and governments around the world.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
The Skills Transformation Consultant is an Individual Contributor role. In this role, you will be part of a small, global team of high-performing individuals tasked with advising Coursera’s enterprise customers on how to develop their organization’s skills. Our customers include the world’s leading companies and governments. You will work closely with the Enterprise Sales and Customer Success team to interact with customers in order to understand their learning needs and strategy, help them develop skills transformation plans, and share recommendations from the Coursera catalogue. You will also work cross-functionally with the Marketing, Data Science, Services, and Product teams to ensure customers know how to effectively transform their talent through Coursera. You will also work with our Content Strategy team to identify exciting new areas of learning for Coursera to invest in to help our customers stay on the cutting edge of skill development.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
This role is ideal for someone who is excited about advising companies and governments on the skills of the future and who is willing and eager to learn constantly. You will have to quickly develop expertise in a wide variety of skill areas, from leadership and communication to AI, data science, software development, and cloud computing. You will have access to unlimited Coursera courses to help facilitate your professional development and effectiveness in this role.</div>
<div>
<br>

<div>
If you are genuinely passionate about learning and global skill development, then this is the role for you.
