<p><b>Who We Are</b></p>
<p>https://www.safariclub.org/</p>
<p>Hunting is an ancient tradition passed down from the earliest times of man to the present day. Hunters participate directly in the natural world and care enough to make sure that it continues in all of its awesome beauty and diversity.</p>
<p>Safari Club International is a U.S.-based organization of more than 50,000 hunters dedicated to protecting the right to hunt and to promote wildlife conservation worldwide. Between SCI and its sister organization, the SCI Foundation, we have put more than $70 Million on the ground for conservation since 2000. In the U.S. and abroad, hunters are part of a system that keeps the rivers, forests and fields intact and maintains the wildlife.</p>

<p><b>Summary</b></p>
<p>This position will assist with organizing all aspects of meetings and events as assigned. This will include learning to utilize event planning software and learning how to read vendor contracts.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul><li> Updates electronic meeting and event request forms (function space assignment and AV price list, menus and pricing). Communicates deadlines to staff and committee liaisons. Assist with gathering back-up support and preparing of Journal Entries.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Audits and reconciles hotel bills, vendor invoices and allocates expenses.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Analyzes and summarizes ticket sales/comp data for meal guarantees and event set up. Provides room block performance updates.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Tracks hotel rooming needs and transportation for staff and volunteers. Provides detailed rooming lists and transportation manifests to hotel management team.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Provides administrative support with production needs for the Convention evening events which includes audio visual requirements, décor &amp; floral needs, and speakers and entertainment needs. Assists with speaker &amp; entertainment contract fulfillment, payments, and expense reporting.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Sitting in on event production meetings and taking notes for future reference.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Taking on additional tasks or projects to learn more about event planning operations.</li></ul>
