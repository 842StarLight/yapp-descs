Description
<br><br>
Reporting to the CMO, the Director, Demand Generation will be a key leader in our Go-to-Market team, strategizing/implementing/measuring 360 marketing campaigns, and moving both internal and external customers toward desired actions and results.
<br><br>
Core responsibilities
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Conceive of, execute, and measure integrated marketing campaigns to drive outbound/inbound activity, and to generate/nurture leads and opportunities</li>
<li>Coordinate with CMO on strategy, Upstream Marketing and Science/Labs on value proposition, Marketing Communications on content and programming, and Growth on specific needs, execution, and operations</li>
<li>Assess sales/martech stack, owned media, and other related infrastructure, and recommend and implement comprehensive investment around marketing automation and analysis (a from the ground up endeavor)</li>
<li>Note: we're a hypergrowth early-stage firm that serves a dynamic and exploding market. Versatile, hands-dirty players, who love to learn, share, and act on what they find will especially thrive in our fast-paced, matrixed organization.</li>
</ul>
Qualifications
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Exceptional project planning and management, verbal and written communication, interpersonal and influencing skills</li>
<li>In-depth experience in programmatic B2B marketing; knowledge of/fluency in marketing SaaS solutions to multiple audiences within biopharma desired</li>
<li>Trusted and able partner to Growth; ability to work cross-functionally with all levels across the organization, including executive leadership</li>
<li>Skilled and smart in measuring what matters (e.g., can do the tricky work of parsing marketing influence across pipeline stages); develop reporting dashboards and provide analysis and insights to the business; devise tests to assess and optimize promotion response model</li>
<li>Passionate about the details, e.g., database cleanliness, list management/scoring, lead management from capture to follow-up, written communications, etc.</li>
<li>Knowledgeable re: marketing ops/automation and integration with Salesforce</li>
<li>Account-based marketing experience, including targeted content and communications</li>
<li>Thrive in open, collaborative startup environments; can readily/swiftly navigate through change and ambiguity; improve processes and productivity; provide recommendations to support an organization scaling at a rapid pace</li>
<li>A plus: solid understanding of regional differences and experience localizing tactics and messages for US, Europe, APAC</li>
<li>Intangibles: curiosity, versatility, GSD, bias toward action, service ethic, conscientiousness, player-coach orientation</li>
</ul>
