<h2>Loan Officer Assistant - Mortgage Lending</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><b>Loan Officer Assistant - Mortgage Lending</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>Summary Description</b><br>
The Loan Officer Assistant’s (LOA) primary function will be to coordinate and lead best in class communication and customer service between NASB borrowers and the applicable Loan Officer(s), throughout the loan application and loan file submission stages of the mortgage loan life-cycle. The LOA will be responsible for driving effective and efficient borrower relationship management by being the point of contact to the NASB customer, in support of the designated Loan Officer, to meet and exceed loan file submission service level agreements (SLA’s) and to allow Loan Officers to focus their attention on generating new business.<br>
<br>
<b>Duties/Responsibilities</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Provide high-quality customer service and support in order to guide the NASB borrower through the loan application process after the Loan Officer has secured a commitment from the borrower to proceed with the loan.</li>
<li>Communicate directly with the borrower to collect the necessary documentation needed to complete the loan application process, such as: income documentation, asset documentation and initial disclosures.</li>
<li>Track receipt of missing documentation and coordinate follow-up on any needed information within the prescribed time-frame to meet and exceed loan file submission SLA’s.</li>
<li>Accurately upload borrower loan file documents into electronic loan file as required, including properly labeling and organizing documents as prescribed.</li>
<li>Input required loan file data into NASB Loan Origination System (LOS).</li>
<li>Order required documentation from third party vendors, as required for each particular loan scenario.</li>
<li>Obtain contact info for realtors, insurance agents, and other relevant 3rd parties related to the loan.</li>
<li>Partner with the Loan Origination staff and Operations resources to ensure that all loans meet required SLA’s, are completed on-time and with high quality and accuracy.</li>
<li>Operate in compliance with all NASB Policies and Procedures</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Qualifications</b></div>
<div><ul>
<li>Ability to prioritize workload, while staying organized and being efficient</li>
<li>Ability to write clear, concise correspondence to customers</li>
<li>Ability to effectively present information verbally and in writing</li>
<li>Ability to effectively respond to questions verbally and in writing, from external and internal customers and management.</li>
<li>Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.</li>
<li>Strong customer service skills and ability to manage challenging conversations to a successful outcome and positive customer experience.</li>
<li>Strong Computer software skills – experience using Internet, Outlook (email), Microsoft Office, Loan origination software, paperless loan file software</li>
<li>Knowledge of the mortgage loan process preferred</li>
<li>
<div>Bachelor’s degree preferred, and a minimum 4+ years professional experience in a high-volume sales, customer service focused or related position/industry.</div>
</li>
<li><div>Mortgage Lending and/or Finance Services background preferred.</div></li>
</ul></div>
