<h2>Mixed Martial Arts Coach</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>
<p><b>GENERAL SUMMARY:</b> At UFC Gym, MMA Coach is responsible for ensuring a positive customer experience. Coaches are responsible for delivering great member service, Private Training &amp; achieving Supplement revenue. Coaches are responsible for the successful attainment of revenue goals pertaining to Private Training and Supplements. This position requires a minimum of one day on the weekend on either Saturday, Sunday or a combination of both days.</p>
<br>
</div>
<p></p>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES &amp; RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<p><b>
1. Sales Execution on Key Metrics
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Sell and Conduct Private &amp; Small Group Training with Members</li>
<li>Assist members and encourage member involvement in private training.</li>
<li>Ensure accurate administration of client programs, supplement purchases, measurement tracking and workout programs. Arrives on time, prepared and attentive for training appointments.</li>
<li>Assist in all revenue generating activities, including but not limited to: complimentary workouts, supplement booths, body fat tables, seminars, workshops, etc.</li>
<li>Sell training sessions, execute an effective prospecting strategy, conducting guest tours, and responsible for completing sales of when needed through the Turn-Over process.</li>
<li>Responsible for achieving personal monthly revenue objectives set forth by the Company with regards to Private Coaching and Supplements; Achieve all minimum standards set forth (sessions, PT and supplement sales)</li>
<li>Perform "Starter Sessions" and prospecting activities</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>2. Member Experience
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Focus on changing lives through Service, Science and Sales</li>
<li>Be a brand ambassador of UFC Gym and its "Train Different" philosophy</li>
<li>Bring T.R.A.I.N (Timely, Ready, Aware, Inspiring, Noble) to life</li>
<li>Conduct safe and effective private MMA training sessions</li>
<li>Maintain the organization and neat presentation of the fitness floor and training spaces at all times</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>3. Miscellaneous</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Follow all policies and procedures regarding payment transactions</li>
<li>Follow all guidelines in the UFC Gym Employee Handbook</li>
<li>Document daily workouts for liability (adherence to national certification standards)</li>
<li>Be knowledgeable of club facility and services</li>
<li>Adhere to agree upon schedule set forth by the MMA Manager</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>ORGANIZATION RELATIONSHIPS:</b></p>
<div>
<p>The MMA Coach will report directly to the MMA Manager. Duties require interaction with all club members and employees.</p>
</div>
</div>
<br><div>
<p><b>REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
1) Knowledge, skills &amp; abilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>In depth knowledge of Private Training techniques from assessment to program design.</li>
<li>Possess a working knowledge of the dotFIT program and software.</li>
<li>Must be able to adjust and operate all Fitness Equipment.</li>
<li>Knowledge of company policies, practices and procedures, including emergency and safety procedures.</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal and communication skills.</li>
<li>Able to generate, maintain and demonstrate a friendly, enthusiastic and positive attitude.</li>
<li>Possess a strong customer service focus. Responds professionally to requests and inquiries from guests, members and staff.</li>
<li>Understands and follow oral and written instructions.</li>
<li>Communicates clearly and concisely.</li>
<li>Possesses strong organizational skills. Understands basic record keeping practices and procedures. Demonstrates the ability to organize and prioritize multiple tasks.</li>
<li>Experience operating office equipment such as computers, calculators, fax and photocopiers.</li>
<li>Must be computer proficient with basic skills in Word and Excel.</li>
<li>Ability to work, setup and execute successful weekends</li>
<li>Extroverted/Self motivator/Customer service minded</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Adhere to meal and rest break periods and must clock in and out for all shift times</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>2) Required Experience:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Current CPR/AED certification</li>
<li>Certification from organization recognized by UFC Gym and/or Pro-Am fight background.</li>
<li>High school diploma or general education degree (GED).</li>
<li>4-year degree in a related field recognized by UFC Gym (preferred).</li>
<li>Current DotFIT certification (preferred)</li>
<li>Minimum 1 year of related experience</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>3) Physical Requirements:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>While performing the duties of this job, the employee is regularly required to stand.</li>
<li>Physical effort required for daily duties include lifting heavy weights, squatting, bending, reaching, spotting &amp; prolonged standing and walking. Must be able to frequently lift and /or moves up to 45+ pounds.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
