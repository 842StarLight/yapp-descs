<p>This role supports the creative design direction and implementation of digital consumer experience within Samsung US and global retail strategy. Through the development and execution of immersive, engaging digital content, software and applications experience, this role will drive strategies for greater consumer engagement and understanding of the benefits of Samsung products in order to extend Samsung’s brand across carrier and retail channels.
</p>
<p>Individual will develop and implement consumer content and interactions for Samsung’s on-device software experience for consumers in a retail/carrier environment, including experience on mobile phones, tablets and other electronic products.
</p>
<p>Individual will work closely with the Samsung Electronics America Retail Design &amp; Marketing organization, R&amp;D, and retail/carrier customers to define, create, sell-in, implement, monitor, maintain and measure Samsung’s in-store strategy, consumer experience and execution plan.
</p>
<p>Individual will also manage creative agencies that will provide support ranging from content development, installation, and maintenance of, software, and applications.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Content Development/Planning:
</b><ul>
<li>Responsible for creative agency management from a creative oversight standpoint, script development, video shoot oversight and User Interface layout.
</li>
<li>Partner with product marketing to determine and select all key content that will be deployed on devices including native content which emphasizes and supports the key features of the product.
</li>
<li>Managing multiple agencies to define, develop, implement, and evolve Samsung’s on device retail experience to maximize consumer attraction and engagement with our brand at POS within our carrier and retail partner stores.
</li>
<li>Partner with retail insights team to understand shopper insights, target demographics and recommend new research studies to investigate and validate new trends for digital content tailored to target audiences.
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<p></p>
<li>
<b>Implementation
</b><ul>
<li>Understand competitive implementations and ensure Samsung is best in class in its execution at retail.
</li>
<li>Define and partner with deployment and account teams to secure and load content onto live device, and manage fulfillment and updates with all customers.
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<p></p>
<li>
<b>Reporting
</b><ul>
<li>Provide regular business updates to key constituents on problems, opportunities, successes and key learnings related to consumer engagement and satisfaction of in-store experience related to Samsung products. Included in analysis should be: elements of launch performance, data on consumer usage, engagement and feedback on live device experience, and competitive analysis.
</li>
<li>Partner with analytics and agency teams to develop performance metric and measurements for program.
</li>
<li>Track and analyze consumer usage of digital content, and make changes to drive best in class experience.
</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>

<p><b>Background/Experience </b>to qualify for this position, the following minimal background and skill levels are required:
</p>
<ul>
<li>A Bachelor's Degree in business, sales or marketing from an accredited university.
</li>
<li>Solid background in video/digital asset planning, creation, production and deployment
</li>
<li>Minimum 6+ years of business experience, with majority of years in retail marketing, with most recent experience creating and driving implementation of the digital experiences of the shopper journey in a retail environment.
</li>
<li>Baseline understanding of technology, i.e., the technology infrastructure on how devices need to be connected to work together to create a seamless experience
</li>
<li>5+ years in wireless industry desirable, though not required.
</li>
<li>Strong knowledge of design principals and brand strategies
</li>
<li>High energy, strong work ethic
</li>
<li>Previous CPG or technology experience preferred
</li>
</ul>
