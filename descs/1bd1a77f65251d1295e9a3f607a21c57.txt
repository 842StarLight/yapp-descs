<h2>Federal Industrial Control Systems Cybersecurity Senior Technologist</h2>

<div></div>

<div>
<div>Jacobs Buildings, Infrastructure &amp; Advanced Facilities (BIAF) line of business is a global network of employees serving both the private and public sectors, including our nation’s vital Defense and Intelligence Community components. Our employees work to shape the communities in which we work and live by creating and delivering world-class projects that transform the future development patterns of those communities. We provide our clients award-winning planning, engineering, architectural and interior design, construction and program management, and design-build project delivery. The Jacobs business model allows us to deliver exceptional, integrated services to our clients, while providing growth opportunities to our employees working together to deliver global solutions to our clients’ programs.
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>Our BIAF Federal team is Headquartered in Arlington VA with another major office in Herndon VA. Collectively, these offices consist of over 700 employees dedicated to delivering built environment solutions to the Intelligence Community and other Federal agencies. Our services include planning, design, construction oversight, and commissioning for highly mission-critical building and infrastructure systems directly supporting and sustaining the Intelligence Community’s mission.
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>This position will be responsible for assisting in the sales, development, and execution of cybersecurity technology solutions for Jacobs Buildings, Infrastructure, and Advanced Facilities (BIAF) to include client positioning, opportunity capture, governance, risk, and compliance, Department of Defense Risk Management Framework specification development, National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) SP 800-82R2 and NIST 800-53R5 policy development and consulting across the BIAF global community. Primary focus will be Jacobs BIAF Department of Defense clients to include US Army Corps of Engineers (USACE), Navy Facilities Engineering Command (NAVFAC), and Air Force Civil Engineering Center (AFCEC). Other clients include DOT, DHS, TSA, FTA, FAA, and various federal agencies.
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>This position will provide practice leadership, senior technical cybersecurity design guidance with minimal direction and collaborate with various internal and external stakeholders as needed. Jacobs BIAF performs cybersecurity services for industrial control systems, building automation systems, transportation control systems, aviation control systems, and other various Federal Internet of Things (IoT) ecosystems. This is not an Information Technology (IT) position.
</div>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2>
<div>Minimum qualifications include:
</div>
<ul><li> Bachelor’s degree in an engineering discipline, cybersecurity, information systems, computer science or related field
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Experience with client development, positioning and sales capture
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Cybersecurity certification as Certified Information Systems Security Professional CISSP), Global Industrial Cyber Security Professional (GICSP) or related cybersecurity certification
</li></ul>
<ul><li> 10 years minimum experience working in the cybersecurity field which may include governance, risk, and compliance, penetration testing, design and architecture, security operations center, client consulting or other related cybersecurity experience.
</li></ul>
<ul><li> United States Citizenship.
</li></ul>
