<h2>Technical Supervisor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>We are a Leading Toxicology Laboratory in Indianapolis IN who is in the market to hire Technical Supervisor, Clinical Operations. This is a stable laboratory that boasts a team-oriented culture and provides it's employees with a positive environment. Our laboratory is a high-complexity, CLIA-certified, CAP-accredited facility. Our skilled team of lab personnel possesses over 70 years of clinical laboratory experience and review data a minimum of three times before reporting results to clients. Never compromising quality for an increased rate of production, all procedures are implemented following CAP standards of patient care and safety. Our lab yields results that enable clinicians to make informed decisions regarding care that will ultimately reduce abuse, misuse, and diversion of harmful drugs.</p>
<p>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES include the following.</p>
<ul>
<li>Fulfills the functions of general supervisor for regulatory requirements and provides 24/7 rotating on-call coverage.</li>
<li>Oversees the performance of testing within the department of expertise. Acts as a subject matter expert in one or more clinical/technical areas within the department. Develops and promotes technical and strategic opportunities within the area of expertise.</li>
<li>Develops and executes all of CLIA/CAP certified lab work ensuring that the laboratory meets state and federal guidelines and achieves excellence in quality at every level</li>
<li>Develops and maintains department Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) according to the Clinical Laboratory Standards Institute (CLSI) guidelines.</li>
<li>Establishes standards, normal values and methods of control for each laboratory test performed in accordance with regulatory and accrediting agencies and in conjunction with clinical/medical leadership.</li>
<li>A plan, designs, validates and implements installation of new or improved laboratory techniques. Reviews quality control data, recognizes and resolves problems as well as prepares or directs preparation of various qualitative and quantitative reports and records.</li>
<li>Performs ongoing departmental monitoring to improve quality, workflow, staff productivity, and employee satisfaction and is responsible for the mentoring, coaching and development of direct reports.</li>
<li>Designs and administers quality control program for each testing section.</li>
<li>Consults on work in progress and completed work to assure accuracy.</li>
<li>Directs the proper storage of hazardous materials and the laboratory safety program in conjunction with the safety officer.</li>
<li>Demonstrates the understanding of all duties and responsibilities of those supervised and serves as a technical specialist in the area of oversight. Must be able to perform all tasks and responsibilities of a technical specialist. Recruits and leads scientific laboratory personnel, ensuring that all employees are adequately qualified, trained and motivated to perform their duties.</li>
<li>Reviews current literature and attends meetings and seminars to keep informed of new developments in the field.</li>
<li>Coordinates and conducts education and training programs for personnel. Develops training/competency checklists and provides clinical orientation and/or training of new employees/students. Supports Education Department personnel/trainers and/or designee in the training/orientation of new staff.</li>
<li>Coordinates new business with the Sales Department and administrative management team.</li>
<li>Evaluates and recommends new instrumentation based on established testing parameters and efficiencies. Provides technical guidance and resolves problems associated with instrumentation.</li>
</ul>
