<h2>Chargeback Supervisor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>Position Responsibilities/Requirements<br>
Are you curious, motivated, and forward-thinking? At FIS you'll have the opportunity to work on some of the most challenging and relevant issues in financial services and technology. Our talented people empower us, and we believe in being part of a team that is open, collaborative, entrepreneurial, passionate and above all fun.<br>
<br>
<b>About the team:</b><br>
The Milwaukee FIS Chargeback Team supports our clients and their cardholders with resolving Debit and Prepaid Debit Merchant disputes and ATM/PIN disputes.<br>
<br>
<b>What you will be doing:</b><br>
In this role you will be Supervising and coaching your staff to ensure all duties are performed and completed in an accurate and timely manner.<br>
<ul>
<li>Assists staff with inquiries from external clients and other FIS departments. Resolves escalated issues for team.</li>
<li>
Compiles and analyzes dispute data to monitor department statistics and provide reports to management.</li>
<li>
Maintains extensive knowledge of Visa and MasterCard rules, federal regulations and internal systems.</li>
<li>
Ensures deadlines are met and reallocates workloads as appropriate.</li>
<li>
Works with manager to establish service and quality standards and ensures these are met.</li>
<li>
May assist manager with budgeting and financial forecasting for department.</li>
<li>
Coaches team on setting goals and conducts employee performance reviews.</li>
<li>
Works with management to make personnel changes.</li>
<li>
Addresses disciplinary issues with staff and escalates to management as needed.</li>
<li>
Approves timecards, overtime and time off requests.</li>
<li>
Monitors and implements procedural and system changes affecting the department.</li>
<li>
Trains new and experienced staff on procedures and systems.</li>
<li>
Assists with sales to new clients.</li>
<li>
Other related duties assigned as needed.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>What you bring:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Strong leadership skills</li>
<li>
Knowledge of the Debit or Prepaid Debit card operations environment</li>
<li>
Knowledge of Regulation E</li>
<li>
Considerable knowledge of dispute resolution processing procedures</li>
<li>
Knowledge of Visa/MasterCard operating rules and regulations and federal and state regulations</li>
<li>
Knowledge of processes and procedures that affect debit and pre-paid debit dispute processing</li>
<li>
Knowledge of MS Office software, such as Word, Excel and Access database</li>
<li>
Excellent customer service skills that build high levels of customer satisfaction</li>
<li>
Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>
Demonstrated analytical, organizational, interpersonal, leadership, problem-solving, decision-making, conflict resolution and negotiation skills</li>
<li>
Ability to manage multiple tasks</li>
<li>
Ability to maintain confidentiality</li>
</ul>
<b>
</b><br>
<b>Added bonus if you have:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Ability to delegate effectively</li>
<li>
Experience working with Microsoft Excel Pivot tables and V-Lookups</li>
<li>
Ability to effectively multi-task</li>
</ul>
