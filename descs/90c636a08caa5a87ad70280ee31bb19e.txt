<h2>Biocompatibility Engineer I</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Performs biological safety related tasks and assists with biomaterials characterization and analysis in our R&amp;D environment.</p>
<br>
<div>
<b>
DAY TO DAY:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Assists with developing test strategies and test plans for biomaterials used in Merit Medical products.</li>
<li>Assists with writing biocompatibility sections of 510(k)s, PMAs, IDEs, etc. for regulatory submissions, both in the U.S. and global markets.</li>
<li>Assists with writing no-test rationales for well known and understood biomaterials.</li>
<li>Maintains database of scientific literature references and testing.</li>
<li>Works with R&amp;D teams and engineers.</li>
<li>Works with other staff in resolving regulatory and technical problems.</li>
<li>Writes risk/benefit analyses when necessary.</li>
<li>Writes MDR's and assists with recall activities.</li>
<li>Performs other related biological safety tasks.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>WHAT WE ARE LOOKING FOR:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Education and/or experience equivalent to a Bachelor's Degree in Biomedical Engineering or related field with focus in biomaterials/chemistry.</li>
<li>A minimum of 1 year of related experience, (research lab, etc.).</li>
<li>Familiarity with ISO 10993 Biocompatibility standard series and FDA guidance biocompatibility documents and how your knowledge can be used to define biocompatibility test plans.</li>
<li>Capacity to assist writing rationales that are scientifically based, with scientific literature references when required.</li>
<li>Excellent writing skills.</li>
<li>Must have scientist knowledge of biomaterials.</li>
<li>Self-motivated, self-directing, strong attention to detail and excellent time management skills.</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal skills and the ability to communicate well - verbally and in writing - with others.</li>
<li>Ability to work well with people at all levels of the organization.</li>
<li>Excellent analytical and problem solving skills.</li>
<li>Demonstrated computer skills preferably spreadsheets, word processing, database and other applicable software programs.</li>
</ul>

<p><b>COMPANY HISTORY</b> Merit Medical Systems, Inc. (NASDAQ, MMSI) was founded in 1987 by Fred P. Lampropoulos. Merit develops, manufactures and markets single-use medical devices primarily for diagnosing and treating cardiovascular and vascular disease. Merit has been successful in introducing and marketing innovative, high-quality products through its expertise in new product design and its capabilities in injection and insert molding of plastics; the application of its proprietary electronic and sensor-based technologies; and wire, extrusion and catheter technologies. Merit also develops and markets embolotherapeutic products for the treatment of uterine fibroids, hypervascularized tumors, and arteriovenous malformations. Embolotherapy procedures are primarily performed by interventional radiologists and use bioengineered microspheres to create targeted vascular occlusion and drug delivery. The Company is utilizing its competitive advantages and technologies to develop and offer products that address a wide range of needs related to cardiology and radiology procedures performed in hospitals. Sales are made primarily to U.S. hospitals through a direct sales force. The Company markets its products in over 60 countries worldwide, utilizing a direct sales force in Europe, and distributors for the remainder of the world.</p>
