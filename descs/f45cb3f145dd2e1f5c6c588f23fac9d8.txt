<h2>Training Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>The Company
</b></p>
<p>CampSite, a division of EZFacility is quickly becoming an industry leader in providing Camp Management Software to the Summer and Day Camp Industry. With a cutting-edge and streamlined web-based solution, camps can now seamlessly collect online registration, process payments, manage medical and other important camper information, hire staff, track alumni, communicate with their clients and much more. As a growing company, CampSite thrives on a team of highly motivated, passionate and energetic employees.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>CampSite is located at Facility’s headquarters in Woodbury, NY. As a part of the Fitness and Sports Division of Jonas Software, our goal is to be recognized by customers and respective industry stakeholders as a trusted provider of “Software for Life” in the areas of technology, product innovation, quality, and customer service.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Responsibilities
</b></p>
<p>As a Training Specialist at CampSite, your responsibilities will include, but are not limited to the following:
</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Provide one-on-one training for all of your assigned Camps.
</li>
<li>Collaborate with the Sales team to understand a Camp’s needs, by reviewing their current processes and evaluating the best way to set up their Camp in CampSite.
</li>
<li>Consult with new Camps to review their database setup and make changes as necessary.
</li>
<li>Manipulate new Camp data for migration from their existing system and coordinate with the Development team to import this data into CampSite.
</li>
<li>Build strong relationships with customers, ensuring trust and 100% satisfaction.
</li>
<li>Receive and respond to customer support tickets and phone calls quickly and efficiently.
</li>
<li>Work with the Support and Development teams to diagnose customer issues and come to an expeditious resolution.
</li>
<li>Share customer feedback and functionality requests with the Product Management team.
</li>
<li>Create and maintain training materials and support documentation.
</li>
<li>Review and test new product functionality.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>A self-starter with a strong work ethic and superior attention to detail.
</li>
<li>The ability to work independently on multiple projects simultaneously with minimal direction.
</li>
<li>Excellent written, verbal and interpersonal communication skills.
</li>
<li>The ability to quickly diagnose customer issues, provide seamless resolutions, and effectively share this information with other team members.
</li>
<li>The ability to learn new programs quickly is essential.
</li>
<li>Strong knowledge of Microsoft Excel required.
 </li>
<li>Previous experience in a Customer Support &amp; Training Role.
</li>
<li>Technical Experience preferred. Friendly, outgoing, empathetic and customer focused.
</li>
<li>Knowledge of Day &amp; Residential Summer Camp administrative processes a big plus.
</li>
<li>Knowledge of web based software (SaaS) is a plus.
</li>
<li>Familiarity with ZenDesk, ScreenSteps and SalesForce or similar systems is a plus.
</li>
<li>Willingness to work variable hours during the busy summer support season.
</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree or equivalent experience.
</li>
</ul>
