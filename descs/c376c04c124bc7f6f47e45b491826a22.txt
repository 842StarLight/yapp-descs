<div>
<b>Overview</b>
</div>
<div>
<p>The US Head of Contracts is a critical role within the Offshore Business focussing on the delivery of large-scale offshore wind projects in the US, working with Multinational Joint Venture Partner(s) and Contractors ensuring that Avangrid position is suitably protected in any agreements.</p>
<br>
<p><br>
Belonging to the Project Services Direction and reporting to the US Project Services Director. Responsible for establishing a team of experts in US to define contracting strategies and plans, tendering, negotiating and managing multi-million offshore delivery contracts.</p>
<p>The US Head of Contracts would work closely with the supply chain and maintain high level relationships with the major suppliers and contractors. The purpose being to ensure that Avangrid maintains very effective and value adding relationships with the suppliers which will enhance our ability to deliver against our business strategy.</p>
<p>The individual will be working as part of a global business with activities in US, being well coordinated with the global offshore business contracts to implement processes and lessons learnt and to share experiences. The position may require travels to various locations in the US and overseas to Europe, and during development, contracting and delivery of projects (large packages) the individual should be prepared to manage teams working at different countries and project locations.</p>
</div>
</div>
<div>
<div>
<b>
Key Responsibilities</b>
</div>
<div><ul>
<li>All Avangrid offshore wind farms shall develop adequately balanced risk profiles through contract strategy and ongoing contract management.</li>
<li>Hold competent staff of all levels to matrix into each of the US offshore wind projects and develop resourcing strategies to manage resource demand peaks and troughs.</li>
<li>Manage the training and development of the contracting staff.</li>
<li>Develop, hold and control the necessary processes and procedures required to develop and contract each US offshore windfarm.</li>
<li>Oversee that projects comply and follow standard processes and procedure while developing or executing each US offshore wind farm.</li>
<li>Oversee the negotiation and completion of all key business contracts, including wind turbine contracts, infrastructure contracts, offshore wind supply contracts, grid connection contracts, power purchase agreement, sale and purchase agreements etc.</li>
<li>Project business cases are to be supported with detailed contractual assessments across all disciplines e.g. turbines, civil, structural, mechanical, electrical, marine, geological, weather, offshore installation, ports and logistics etc.....</li>
<li>Anticipate contractual and commercial risks, opportunities and threats to Avangrid offshore and implement control measures or actions.</li>
<li>Maintain value adding relationships with all contractor and suppliers.</li>
<li>Coordinate all legal and procurement activities within Avangrid in relation to projects.</li>
<li>Monitoring performance of all projects and contractor performance, to ensure quality, H&amp;S and cost targets are met and ensuring projects are implemented in a manner consistent with the contract terms and sound commercial and risk management principles.</li>
<li>Management of Supply Chain strategy:
<ul>
<li>Securing sufficient manufacturing capacity in accordance with programmes and budgets;</li>
<li>Devising the most economically advantageous multi-contract solutions;</li>
<li>Ensuring procurement best practice and sound risk allocation principles are maintained;</li>
<li>Provision for management of interfaces across several concurrent supply chain contracts in a highly risky marine environment; and</li>
<li>Taking account of changes in technology or industry practices (such as installation techniques)</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Encouraging supply chain growth in US. This will include working with senior executives in supply chain companies, senior government officials, regional development agencies, local authorities, key competitors and other stakeholders.</li>
<li>Lobby and influence US government, regulators and key stakeholders on a variety of topics such as supply chain development and infrastructure investment.</li>
<li>Project insurance management:
<ul>
<li>Responsible for developing and coordinating project insurance strategy (by coordinating with Avangrid Insurance Team and external insurance advisers).</li>
<li>Ensuring that the strategy takes account of best industry practice for insurance and risk management in the offshore wind sector and is properly co-ordinated across all supply chain and contracting arrangements.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Put in place systems so that all activities within contracting department are undertaken in compliance with Avangrid risk and governance policies.</li>
<li>Establish the necessary committees on a per project basis to influence direction and ensure knowledge transfer.</li>
<li>Drive continuous improvement in contractual strategies, commercial parameters and contractual terms and conditions.</li>
</ul></div>
</div>
