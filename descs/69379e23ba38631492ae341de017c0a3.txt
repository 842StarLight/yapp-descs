<h2>Parts Administrator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Description:</b></p>
<p><b>Position Summary:</b></p>
<p>A Penske Parts Administrator is responsible for coordinating daily inventory levels, executing shop administrative tasks, and organizing other related day-to-day functions.</p>
<p><b>Major Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul><li>Maintain optimal inventory levels through ordering parts according to sourcing procedure ensuring suppliers deliver purchases defect-free and on-time.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Receive and store parts and update PartsNet, maintaining inventory cycle counts and ensuring parts are available when needed.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ensure warranty items are shipped and received on time, and follow-up on returns and warranty-grams</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manage the new &amp; used battery inventory and ensure that tires are on hand in the right quantity and specifications</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manage all purchasing (not including office supplies)</li></ul>
<ul><li>Process and manage receipts, payables, and invoices from suppliers</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manage vendor performance for timely repairs and invoicing</li></ul>
<ul><li>Answer telephone dealing directly with customers and vendors</li></ul>
<ul><li>Other projects and tasks as assigned by supervisor</li></ul>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul><li>1 year of general office experience required</li></ul>
<ul><li>1 year of inventory control experience highly desired</li></ul>
<ul><li>HS diploma or equivalent required, degree preferred</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong customer service skills</li></ul>
<ul><li>Excellent phone skills</li></ul>
<ul><li>Understanding of engine, drive-train, and body parts</li></ul>
<ul><li>AS400 experience preferred</li></ul>
<ul><li>Proficiency with MS office Software as well as EP system navigation</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to work independently, developing people skills, organizational skills, good written and oral communication skills required</li></ul>
<ul><li>Regular, predictable, full attendance is an essential function of the job</li></ul>
<ul><li>Willingness to travel as necessary, work the required schedule, work at the specific location required, complete Penske employment application, submit to a background investigation (to include past employment, education, and criminal history) and drug screening are required.</li></ul>
