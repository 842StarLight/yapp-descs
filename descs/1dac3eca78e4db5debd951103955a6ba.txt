<div>Combined Insurance, a Chubb Company, is seeking a Senior Leader of Application Development to join our fast-paced, high energy, and growing company. Come build your career here, along with other positive, hard-working, talented professionals—just like you!
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Position Summary</b></div>
<div>The Senior Leader of Application Development position will be responsible for managing three legacy policy administration systems across North America, as well as the Combined IT PMO office. The position serves as the primary liaison providing strategic and tactical support for group and individual products as well providing support for the delivery of process and distribution improvements. The position will interact with several key business areas including sales, marketing, actuarial, and back office support. The impact on operations includes increasing efficiency, reducing expenses related to business process, while also enhancing customer service.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Key Accountabilities Include:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Working with all business areas as new business relationships are introduced, maintaining a high level of personal attention and accountability to the business with focus on quality and service.</li>
<li>Managing the implementation of Group and Individual supplemental insurance products interacting with various business units within the organization.
</li>
<li>Ensuring IT budget, Controls, Service Levels and Key Performance Indicators are fulfilled.
</li>
<li>Participating in annual budget planning process with Senior Business Leaders.</li>
<li>Conducting monthly budget review with direct reports to help manage and track project progress.</li>
<li>Assessing cross-channel conflict and challenges, prioritize work, challenge benefit cases, resolve and escalate issues.</li>
<li>Managing team capabilities, encourages a continuous learning and depth chart (on and off-shore).</li>
<li>Reviewing designs, anticipate new demands and assist with solutions.
</li>
<li>Tracking project progress and prepare status and conducts status update meetings with Senior OPS/Actuarial/Worksite Leaders weekly/monthly/quarterly.</li>
<li>Participating in Legacy System Modernization, New Product and TPA Assessment.</li>
<li>Managing other IT-related initiatives or project streams as assigned.</li>
</ul>
