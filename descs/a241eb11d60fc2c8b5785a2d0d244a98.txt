<h2>Underwriting Senior Manager, PBM</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>As a Senior Manager on our strategic Underwriting team, you will contribute directly to winning and retaining multi-million dollar PBM client contracts. This role is essential to developing financial models to determine optimum profitability to help people on their path to better health. This position is responsible for managing a team that creates, manages and reviews PBM pricing offers for current and prospective clients.
<br>
<br>
As part of the Underwriting Team, you will:<br>
<ul>
<li>Create, manage, and review PBM client pricing offers for current and prospective clients</li>
<li>Collaborate with internal partners in support of developing pricing offers, including Sales and Account Services, Proposals, Networks, Trade, Legal, and Specialty teams</li>
<li>Provide consultation on pricing strategy to assigned sales partners</li>
<li>Coach, train, mentor, hire, and lead a team of Underwriters</li>
<li>Assign work and manage deadlines across assigned team</li>
<li>Present pricing offers for review and approval by executive management</li>
<li>Develop and implement processes to ensure accuracy of pricing offers</li>
<li>Ensure Fee Authorization Forms are released accurately and in a timely manner after a pricing offer is accepted</li>
<li>Support enterprise goals and special projects as assigned</li>
<li>Contribute to development and implementation of new or improved pricing strategies or processes</li>
<li>Grow and maintain knowledge of PBM pricing, products and services, and the overall healthcare landscape</li>
<li>Collaborate closely with Underwriting and Analytic Consulting Services peers</li>
</ul>
<br>
Join the company helping people and their path to better health!
