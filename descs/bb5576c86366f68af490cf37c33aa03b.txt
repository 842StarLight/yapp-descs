<p><b>Qualifications and Skills</b></p>

<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills and is comfortable working with clients at a business level to understand their current and historic issues regarding their business and technology</li>
<li>Possesses strong organizational and time-management skills</li>
<li>Experience writing proposals for and implementation of technical solutions to fulfill business needs</li>
<li>Results-oriented, self-motivated, energetic, professional, reliable and a team player</li>
<li>Strong understanding of technology and business productivity systems</li>
<li>Experience with RMM (remote monitoring and management) tool a plus</li>

<li>Sales training/experience is a plus</li>
</ul>

</ul>
