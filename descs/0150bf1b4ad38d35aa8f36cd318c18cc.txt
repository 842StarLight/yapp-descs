<h2>Auto Technician</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>Get on the fast track to a more rewarding career with Enterprise Holdings including three car rental brands: Enterprise Rent A Car, National Car Rental and Alamo Rent A Car - teams that are committed to quality, innovation, customer satisfaction and employee development.<br>
<br>
Our philosophy is to take care of our customers and employees first. We know if we do this, success will follow - for both our company and our employees.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Enterprise, National and Alamo has an exciting opportunity for an Automotive Maintenance Technician. The Automotive Maintenance Technician performs automotive service, such as oil changes and tire rotations and preventive maintenance. This is a position of routine difficulty requiring the exercise of reasonable judgment and initiative. Having your NYS inspection license or eligibility to obtain one is preferred. Performs all responsibilities with a focus on the needs of our customers and in accordance with our Quality Standards.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
**This position is based near the Albany Airport in Albany, NY. This is a non-sales, team environment. Training and career path available along with flexible schedule**</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<b>
Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li><div>Ensure all repairs and preventative maintenance are completed according to the manufacturer's specifications and guidelines.</div></li>
<li><div>
Provide safe and reliable vehicles upon completion of repairs and preventative maintenance.</div></li>
<li><div>
Perform in-fleet servicing on new cars according to company procedures as required.</div></li>
<li><div>
Perform buy back/turn back servicing according to company procedures as required.</div></li>
<li><div>
Perform vehicle servicing/repairing to maintain company standards; maintain Out-Of-Service at or below 2%.</div></li>
<li><div>
Maintain safe and clean work area.</div></li>
<li><div>
Perform other related duties as required.</div></li>
</ul>
<div></div>

</p>

</b>
