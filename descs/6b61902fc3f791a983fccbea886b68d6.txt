<h2>Customer Service Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Job Description<br>
<br>
<p>The ideal Customer Service Representative will be responsible for performing the below job functions including but not limited to:</p>
<ul>
<li>Accurately enter and process purchase orders in a timely manner</li>
<li>Correspond with customers and internal and external company representatives on all matters related to the order</li>
<li>Diligently follow purchase orders through to completion, working with appropriate parties to quickly resolve issues that may delay timely processing</li>
<li>Perform general office and clerical tasks</li>
<li>Other related Customer Service/ Order Entry office duties as deemed necessary</li>
</ul>
</div>
<p></p>
<br>
<div>Qualifications<br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Prior experience entering purchase orders within a manufacturing setting/sales office environment</li>
<li>Ability to prioritize and multi-task</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Professional and pleasant phone manner to service incoming and outgoing calls</li>
<li>Proficiency in use of computers and related software</li>
<li>Excellent organizational and time management skills</li>
<li>Detail-oriented work ethic</li>
<li>Knowledge of SAGE100 preferred but not required.</li>
</ul>
<br>
Additional Information<br>
<br>
<p>Schedule: Full-time (9am-5pm), Monday-Friday</p>
<p>
Rate of pay: Starting at $18.00 per hour.</p>
<p>
We offer an excellent working environment, great benefits and on-site parking.</p>
