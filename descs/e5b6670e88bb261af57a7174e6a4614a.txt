<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>The Opportunity
</b></h2>
<p></p>
<ul><li>Senior Corporate Counsel will be part of the Commercial Transactions Group and will work with a team of other in-house counsel.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must have experience in drafting, negotiating, and reviewing commercial agreements including, without limitation: inbound/outbound licensing agreements, sales, facilities, services, and other types of commercial contracts relating to hardware and software products. Experience in drafting or reviewing government contracts and marketing, public relations, and human resource agreements also helpful.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Collaborate with IP counsel and compliance counsel to ensure that identified risk is managed properly.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Interface with, and, as necessary, conduct training of various business units domestically and internationally to understand key IP and commercial requirements, and to ensure commercial contract terms comply with corporate directives, policies and procedures.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Drive continuous improvement to existing legal templates, legal and business processes, and legal support models, including contracts administration procedures and processes.
</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>Handle and complete special assignments or projects at the direction of the Director of Commercial Transactions or the General Counsel.
</li>
<li>Build business relationships and gain trust of internal clients while maintaining adherence to legal standards.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Qualifications:
</p>
<ul><li>Candidate must be an active member of the California Bar.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Minimum experience: 8+ years drafting and negotiating commercial transactions, preferably with an emphasis on technology transactions (hardware and/or software) and government contracts in either a law firm or in an in-house corporate setting.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Semiconductor experience or related experience is preferred and experience with open source is helpful.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must possess effective verbal and written communication skills as well as excellent time-management and organizational skills.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must have proven ability to handle difficult situations and solve problems creatively by providing business clients advice on various legal risks, business strategies, and other issues related to commercial transactions, engineering, product development, and business operations in a fast-paced work setting.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must have ability to work independently and as part of a team, and possess superior work ethic and commitment to professionalism.<br>
</li></ul>
