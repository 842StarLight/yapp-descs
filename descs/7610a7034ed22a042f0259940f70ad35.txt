<h2>Manager - Demand Planning</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>
<div>The Demand Planning Manager role is responsible for all consensus forecasting activities associated with their Strategic Business Unit(s). This role has ultimate responsibility for delivering a solid monthly Demand Plan for their SBU(s), built on the combination of the sales forecast, statistical forecasting practices, and any appropriate macro trends in the environment or SBU strategic direction.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<b>Key Responsibilities and Duties</b>
</div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Collaborate with, and challenge where needed, relevant sales colleagues in their submission of the monthly sales forecast.
</li>
<li>Deliver the best possible monthly demand plan to the Supply Planning function, combining sales forecast input with appropriate statistical forecasting, SBU strategic imperatives, any relevant macro trends, and analyses driven by this role to deliver the best possible monthly Demand Plan for their SBU(s).</li>
<li>
Drive continuous improvement in the monthly demand plan by routinely conducting analyses to improve the predictability and accuracy of their monthly Demand Plan across sales trends, sales forecast accuracy, statistical forecasts, industry trends, and other demand related data.
</li>
<li>Support the launch of new products as well as movement of product due to sourcing optimization</li>
<li>
Proactively work with commercial organization to smooth customer demand where practical</li>
<li>
Collaboratively work with Supply Planning Manager, commercial leaders, and others to resolve supply problems</li>
<li>
Participate in budgeting and long-range planning activities</li>
<li>
Given this is a new role being implemented as part of overall process improvements at SI Group, the successful candidate will be a key team member in the development and rollout of those process and tool improvements across the enterprise.</li>
</ul>
