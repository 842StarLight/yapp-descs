<h2>Dell Inside Product Specialist 2</h2>

<div><b>Inside Product Specialist II</b></div>
<p></p>
<div><div><div><div>
<p>
Dell, Inc. is a worldwide provider of information technology services and business solutions to a broad range of clients. We offer powerful, yet easy-to-use, solutions that span the entire breadth of the information lifecycle, enabling companies to better manage data, improve data integration and amplify the benefits of data performance.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
As an <b>Inside Product Specialist</b> 2 you will provide pre-sales support to the inside sales teams during the sales process. You will be accountable for delivering technical solutions, products, and /or services that address customer needs of varying complexity (via telephone, chat, web, or e-mail); or working with subject matter expert to address a solution of even higher complexity.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Role Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Acts as a technical resource to support the sales organization to meet and/or exceed their objectives.</li>
<li>
May own account sets and/or be called into accounts as opportunities are identified-Identifies customer needs/ requirements, and recommends the appropriate products and solutions.-Promotes and positions key strategic service offerings that help drive solutions-Remains up-to-date on new product, technology, and services offerings.</li>
<li>
Stays current on industry and vertical solutions as well.</li>
<li>
Identifies appropriate services to meet the full range of customer needs around Client and Enterprise-Understands and articulates strengths and vulnerabilities of the competition.</li>
<li>
Works with internal sales teams to ensure Services pipeline accuracy.</li>
<li>
Point of contact for escalated issues.</li>
<li>
Recommends business solutions that consider customer needs and Dell interests-Consistently and proactively identifies significant ways to improve customer experience</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Preferences:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Assists the ongoing services education /development of the broader team.</li>
<li><p>
1 - 3 yrs. related work experience.</p></li>
<li>
Associates degree, technical college or some college course work.</li>
</ul>
