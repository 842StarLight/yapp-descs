<h2>Product Manager, Digital Marketing</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><b>Product Manager, Digital Marketing</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
About Us:</b></div>
<div>
Dealer Spike is a digital advertising company focused on helping dealerships in the power sports, RV, marine, and agricultural industries. We provide beautiful, powerful, distinctive web solutions and tools to our dealership clients. Our expertise comes from real-world dealership experience and a passion for responding to our dealers' needs in the marketplace.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
Summary:</b></div>
<div>
As the Product Manager you will grow KPIs for your product portfolio. This position reports to the Director of Product Management. You will drive effective product lifecycles including conception, research, design, build, pricing, marketing, sales, distribution and upgrade. This role will focus on SEM, digital advertising and managed digital marketing products to help our dealer clients drive demand and conversion on our multi-tenant dealer website platforms. This is a rapidly growing and changing area of the business and you have an exciting opportunity to use your experience and knowledge to build and partner your way to clear market leadership.
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Identify appropriate target markets and track market trends.</li>
<li>Develop a product vision, strategy and roadmap.</li>
<li>Deeply understand customer problems and deliver prioritized product requirements.
</li>
<li>Gather market research and other customer/prospect feedback to measure effectiveness of marketing efforts and predict future market conditions and potential.
</li>
<li>Assess partnership opportunities including third party programs.</li>
<li>Analyze competitive strategies and tactics.</li>
<li>Maintain product marketing plans (positioning, tactics, pricing, etc.) and communicate them as necessary to appropriate personnel.
</li>
<li>Oversee the work of cross-functional teams including marketing, product development, and technical groups to drive product vision, define product requirements, and manage products.
</li>
<li>Analyze market requirements and business opportunities in target markets.</li>
<li>Ensure successful release of product requirements.</li>
<li>Develop and test product concepts and ideas cross-functionally.</li>
<li>Ensure financial objectives for assigned product(s), including revenue and profitability are met.</li>
<li>Build business plans for products including opportunity assessment, positioning strategy, and financial analysis.</li>
<li>Develop marketing tactical plans for each product area in conjunction with other appropriate departments.
</li>
<li>Develop tactical plans to support successful adoption.</li>
<li>Plan and lead successful product launches.</li>
<li>Train sales teams and support selling activities, including development of sales tools and collateral.</li>
<li>Other responsibilities as assigned.</li>
</ul>
