<p><b>Mirador, Corpus Christi's </b>premier life care community, is currently seeking an individual to join our Health Services Sales and Marketing team as a Sales Counselor.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>The Sales Counselor provides sales activities and results necessary to achieve Assisted Living and Memory Support sales and move-in goals. Interacts with all leads whether provided through advertising, public relations, referral, or personal contact and turn those leads into residents of the community through the use of professional selling skills. Additional duties include tracking leads, keeping accurate records on leads and prospects and meet predetermined monthly sales goals.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>This role acts as a liaison with the local business and healthcare community for the purpose of developing and maintaining referral source relationships and to achieve and sustain the quality and quantity of leads and referrals to the community to achieve established occupancy goals. Ideal candidates must have a proven record of sales experience in Assisted Living and Memory Support.</p>
