<h2>Strategic Planning and Performance, Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>
<div>At Blue Shield of California, Markets manages membership and revenue growth and performance, serving the state’s commercial, individual and government markets. The Strategic Planning and Performance Consultant is a multi-faceted role reporting to the Strategic Planning and Performance Director, who supports the EVP Markets.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Position Summary:</b></div>
<div>The Strategic Planning and Performance Consultant (SPPC) position plays a vital role in growing membership, revenue &amp; operating income. The SPPC will support strategic decision making, including the management, implementation, and reporting of strategic initiatives within the Markets organization and Blue Shield of California enterprise wide. In this role, you will interface with Blue Shield leadership both within Markets and across stakeholder organizations, including Customer Experience, HCQA, and Finance. He/she will monitor and represent the business unit interests in programs and track progress against goals to measure success. This role is responsible for a wide range of duties that impact the health of the business.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>This role is strategic, tactical, and facilitative requiring a combination of focus, flexibility, and willingness to play an active, behind-the-scenes role. As a liaison to cross-functional business efforts, the role requires a resourceful, self-starter individual who is solution focused and possesses strong analytical and communication skills.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Responsibilities:</b></div>
<div><i>Strategic</i></div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li><div>Provides strategic advice and support business unit on matters relating to goals and initiatives.</div></li>
<li><div>
Supports strategic planning, goal setting, great place to work initiatives and business case development.</div></li>
<li><div>
In collaboration with the Director and the Competitive Intelligence Strategist, provide meaningful insights and recommendations on how BSC can expand market share.</div></li>
<li><div>
Collaborate cross functionally with BSC Markets leadership, product, marketing, and sales to proactively address competitive issues and to develop differentiating competitive strategies and execute competitive programs.</div></li>
</ul>

Assists in the coordination of management processes, including input and reports from cross-organizational teams, including but not limited to; leadership team meetings, executive dashboards, milestone reporting, and other ongoing meetings and reporting as needed.</div></li>
