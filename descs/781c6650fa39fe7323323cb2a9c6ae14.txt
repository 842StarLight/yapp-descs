<h2>Associate Manager, Digital Marketing</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Job Description
</b></p>
<p><b>POSITION SUMMARY </b>:
</p>
<p>As the Associate Digital Marketing Manager, you support the strategic development and execution of digital marketing initiatives across paid, owned and earned channels designed to build brand awareness, engagement, purchase preference and sales across a subset of our Wine &amp; Spirits brands. A natural collaborator, you partner with internal teams and external agencies to ensure alignment with and achievement of business goals as well as adherence to best practices. A strong project manager, you oversee programs from inception through execution and reporting, ensuring successful delivery against goals.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>RESPONSIBILITIES
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop and project manage digital programs, including but not limited to digital and social advertising, search marketing (SEO/SEM), email communications, and website design/development
</li>
<li>Collaborate with internal teams and external agencies and vendors to deliver and optimize digital programs
</li>
<li>Compile, review and communicate monthly/quarterly qualitative and quantitative reports on digital marketing performance, driving actionable insights for future efforts; communicate best practices and share lessons learned throughout the organization
</li>
<li>Work to gain efficiencies and scalability within processes and protocols as it pertains to digital marketing execution
</li>
<li>Monitor competitors’ successes and shortcomings, as well as relevant brands that exhibit best in class digital marketing programs
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Keep informed of industry activities as well as new digital approaches, resources, opportunities and best practices
</li>
<li>Ensure that all CBI digital marketing programs meet beverage alcohol legal requirements
</li>
<li>Drive innovation and work with agency partners to identify business opportunities in a “test and learn” culture
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree, plus 3-5 years of agency digital marketing experience
</li>
<li>2+ years of experience preferably within the programmatic space
</li>
<li>Demonstrable experience managing social media marketing efforts
</li>
<li>Detail-oriented with excellent critical thinking and problem-solving abilities
</li>
<li>Strong analytical and project management skills
</li>
<li>Excellent communication (written and verbal), presentation and interpersonal skills
</li>
<li>Self-starter, self-motivated and a team player
</li>
<li>Ability to manage several concurrent activities and thrive in a fast-paced, entrepreneurial environment
</li>
<li>Possesses a spirit of intellectual curiosity, creativity, and innovation
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Preferred Qualifications
</b></p>
<ul><li>Digital or interactive marketing/media agency experience a plus
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Physical Requirements/Work Environment
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Must be at least 21 years of age
</li>
<li>Must be able to sit and/or stand for long periods of time and work on a computer for extended periods. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.
</li>
</ul>
