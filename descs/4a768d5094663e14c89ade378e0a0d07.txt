<p><b>
ADMINISTRATIVE ASSISTANT</b></p>
<p>
We seek an experienced administrative professional who is organized and energetic, with superb interpersonal skills for our Boston, MA office. The Administrative Assistant performs administrative support functions and operations for multiple consulting professionals within the office.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Increase productivity of consulting professionals by handling the administrative tasks (such as, travel arrangements, expense reporting, calendar management, interacting with vendors and clients, email correspondence, etc.)</li>
<li>
Provide scheduling support to multiple individuals (appointment, calendar and meeting management)</li>
<li>
Complete expense reports within compliance guidelines, process invoices for payment, and assist with other administrative processes, as needed</li>
<li>
Prepare domestic and international travel logistics (itineraries, visas, etc.)</li>
<li>
Coordinate internal and external meetings/activities (i.e. venue selection/reservation and set-up, hotel accommodations, catering, etc.)</li>
<li>
Prepare and organize documents for client proposals and presentations as needed (contract tracking, printing, organizing and archiving meeting support materials)</li>
<li>
Address problems and troubleshoot with internal and external stakeholders</li>
<li>
Handle special projects with demanding deadlines</li>
<li>
Front desk reception back-up coverage (answering phones, greeting visitors, handling incoming/outgoing mail and deliveries)</li>
<li>
Participates in group learning and/or knowledge sharing</li>
<li>
Mentors new team members</li>
<li>
Hours are 8:30 am – 5:30pm.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
High school diploma required. Post-secondary education or Associate or Bachelor degree preferred.</li>
<li>
1 - 3 years of relevant work experience as an administrative/operations assistant, preferably in a professional services environment</li>
<li>
Fluent in MS Office (Word, PowerPoint, Excel and Outlook), travel and expense reporting applications</li>
<li>
Strong oral and written communication skills</li>
<li>
Professional appearance and demeanor with ability to exercise good judgment and discretion</li>
<li>
Attention to detail, dependability, punctuality</li>
<li>
Proactive self-starter with ability to manage a wide variety of tasks and meet deadlines</li>
<li>
Experience working both independently and with a team in a demanding environment</li>
<li>
Sense of humor appreciated</li>
</ul>
<p>
ZS is a global consulting firm; fluency in English is required, additional fluency in at least one European or Asian language is highly desired.</p>
<p>
Candidates must possess work authorization for their intended country of employment. An on-line application, including a cover letter expressing interest and a full set of transcripts (official or unofficial), is required to be considered. ZS offers a competitive compensation package with salary and bonus incentives, plus an attractive benefits package.</p>
