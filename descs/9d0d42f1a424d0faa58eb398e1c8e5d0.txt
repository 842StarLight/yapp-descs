Who We’re Looking For:</div>
<div>
We’re looking for a Manager of Global Talent Acquisition to optimize and lead our recruiting function. You will shape and drive our global recruiting strategy in support of our rapid growth. This role is highly collaborative with high visibility, reporting directly into the Chief People Officer and partnering closely with hiring managers and the executive team. As the head of talent, you will play a vital role in our success. Our team is the core of everything we do, and the selection process starts with you!</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What You’ll Do:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Build and foster strong relationships/partnerships with leaders &amp; stakeholders across the globe.</li>
<li>
Manage requisition load, perform in-person and phone interviews with candidates and make recommendations to hiring managers.</li>
<li>
Create, implement, and drive adoption of innovative and scalable recruitment policies and practices from the ground up.</li>
<li>
Roll out and champion global Talent Acquisition processes.</li>
<li>
Develop internal trainings on recruitment, interviewing and hiring best practices with the aim of providing hiring managers with the tools needed to make optimal hiring decisions and manage rollout of same.</li>
<li>
Continually assess processes to identify inefficiencies and drive effectiveness.</li>
<li>
Define consistent data-driven hiring metrics and goals.</li>
<li>
Create reporting dashboards which offer insights into hiring processes, brand reach and effectiveness of recruitment efforts.</li>
<li>
Review and revise recruiting strategies to ensure we are attracting a diverse pool of top talent.</li>
<li>
Be a champion of Madison Logic’s culture.</li>
<li>
Develop strategies to say ahead of potential hiring or retention challenges.</li>
<li>
Stay abreast of new and changing recruitment trends and laws.</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
