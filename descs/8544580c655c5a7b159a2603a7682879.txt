<h2>Director of Marketing Operations</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<div><b>Director of Marketing Revenue Operations</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>Flexential stands for something not often found in the world of IT transformation: the human touch. The best infrastructure solutions aren’t about infrastructure, they’re about people. With a mission to accelerate customer success through people and technology, we build trusted relationships and deliver tailored, value-added and reliable solutions to demonstrate the power of people in a technical world.</div>
<div></div>
<div>Utilizing our people, values and reliable performance, Flexential is deeply invested in the success of our 4,200+ customers, who trust us to deliver core data center solutions of colocation and connectivity, as well as cloud, managed solutions and professional services. Flexential’s robust suite of assets spans 21 domestic and international markets and comprises 41 highly redundant and connectivity-rich data centers.</div>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>Flexential is seeking an experienced Marketing Operations and Analytics guru to help build a world-class B2B Revenue Marketing engine. You will be responsible for the day-to-day management of our marketing technology systems (Marketo and Salesforce), with a focus on enabling the marketing team to build, measure and optimize Lead Generation, ABM, Upsell/Cross-sell and Nurture campaigns. This role requires a deep understanding of the full Lead to Revenue cycle, segmentation, Ideal Customer Profiling, Account and Lead Scoring. As the Director of Marketing Revenue Operations you will set the vision and strategy for Marketing to align its technologies with business objectives and priorities, serve as a liaison to cross-functional stakeholders (Sales, IT, Sales Ops) and decide on the optimal technology partners.</div>
<div></div>
<div>Reporting to the SVP of Marketing, the successful candidate will be a true change-agent and team player, have technical-depth (acting as the Marketing unit’s CIO) and possess a passion for accomplishing marketing’s mission through the implementation of new and emerging technologies. Moreover, the leader must bring a comprehensive end-to-end approach that extends to web development, data analytics and actionable business intelligence, and ensuring training/enablement are part of the journey. In this role, you will manage and build a team of passionate, action-oriented, data driven operational experts, design and manage the MarTech stack and Web Dev, owning optimization for the corporate website, lead flow, and integrated multi-touch campaigns.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Key Responsibilities
</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Define and lead the innovation, operations and health of the Flexential marketing technology stack (Marketo, SFDC, DemandBase, Conversica, Bizible, UberFlip, etc.)</li>
<li>Ensure the corporate web site is optimized for customer acquisition and delivers a seamless buyer’s journey.</li>
<li>Oversee campaign management and execution to drive new logo acquisition and growth of existing customer upsell/cross sell plays, with a rigor of A/B testing and guidance to the campaign managers to deliver high quality, multi-touch programs.</li>
<li>Work with regional marketing teams/field marketing to monitor all lead assignments, including “unassigned”, to ensure leads are getting the appropriate follow-up. Perform root-cause analysis to uncover issues that impact lead assignment and drive resolution in partnership with Regional Sales VPs, IT and Sales Ops.</li>
<li>Recommend playbooks optimized for region-specific promotions, Strategic Accounts, and solution selling.</li>
<li>Define and manage a marketing database management strategy that supports data unification and across SFDC, Marketo, Gainsight, and the Partner Portal, maintaining data quality and enforcing governance and measurement.</li>
<li>Support the CMO with executive level reporting and actionable insights.</li>
<li>Partner with marketing stakeholders to provide analytics, insights and intelligence that drives awareness and accelerates demand generation strategies at every stage of the sales pipeline, as well as serve as a change-agent to identify areas of success, opportunity and improvements.</li>
<li>Create and maintain currency of Marketing Revenue Operations documentation and insure relevant training across the Marketing and Sales organization.</li>
<li>Oversee the campaign calendar across all channels.</li>
<li>Manage internal and 3rd party resources for website development with a highly technical approach around analytics, SEO optimization, and delivery of quality traffic to the website</li>
<li>Contribute to paid social media campaigns and strategy, from awareness to retargeting, across multiple audiences and channels.</li>
<li>Research, recommend and oversee new areas and channels to expand digital marketing performance.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Qualifications and Required Skills</b></div>
<ul>
<li>BA/BS degree, MBA desired.8+ years of sales and marketing operations experience in the enterprise B2B market.5+ years of experience in salesforce.com and Marketo.5+ years of experience in data management and governance strategies.</li>
<li>Capabilities in evaluating, managing and maintaining relationships with 3rd party resources.</li>
<li>Ability to implement new marketing technologies, inherit and improve current technologies, and replace existing technologies.Extensive background in data management and health strategies inclusive of database unification, compliance, governance, quality, and measurement.</li>
<li>Knowledge of B2B customer sales cycle and how to identify opportunities to maximize demand creation, pipeline contribution and sales opportunity acceleration.</li>
<li>Varied background in marketing planning and operations strategy, workflows, business process design and review, and data mining and analysis.</li>
<li>Familiarity with Drupal, MySQL, and Qlik is a plus.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>You are:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Excellent with leadership, communication and relationship skills, both in developing relationships with the field, other cross-functional constituents, third-party resources, coaching and mentoring a team.</li>
<li>Exceptional technical and data analysis skills.</li>
<li>High business acumen with strong project/program management skills, particularly in managing multiple projects and deadlines</li>
<li>Highly motivated individual who thrives in a fast-paced team environment and is readily adaptable to changing market and organizational dynamics.</li>
<li>Natural curiosity, and excitement to stay abreast with the latest marketing technology trends and best practices.</li>
</ul>
