<h2>Senior Product Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div><div>
<div><div>Internet Creations is committed to delivering the perfect service experience, every time. The company is re-energizing and expanding its SaaS product line, and we are looking for a Senior Product Manager with a proven track record of success in product management specific to software, preferably related to the Customer Service industry. Ideal candidates will have a strategic mindset, a focus on quality, strong collaboration skills and a passion for exceptional customer service.
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities - Product Management</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Develop and Implement Product Strategies Consistent with the Company Vision and Product Philosophy</li>
<li>
Define &amp; Communicate the Philosophy and Vision for the Product</li>
<li>
Follow trends in Customer Service</li>
<li>
Regularly Perform Pricing and Competitive Analysis, Analyze Industry and User Trends, and Recommend Strategies to Improve Market Position, Improve Product Quality or Reduce Support Costs</li>
<li>
Accountable for Product Performance by Setting and Tracking Success Metrics that Inform Product Roadmap</li>
<li>
Manage Product Release Versions, Push Upgrade Schedule</li>
<li>
Quality Assurance for New Apps, New App Releases, and New Salesforce Releases</li>
<li>
Manage Product Roadmap for Specific Products [Shared with Director]</li>
<li>
Create/Manage Development Cases for Feature Requests and Bug Fixes</li>
<li>
Regularly Review Bug Fix Pending Cases to ensure timely delivery (per customer contract terms)
</li>
<li>Ambassador for Products with Salesforce Account Executives / Sales Engineers, giving presentations at private and public events</li>
<li>
Facilitate App Release Meetings with Sales Team
</li>
<li>Work with Marketing Team for Product Communication, Release Upgrades, New Features</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Responsibilities - Product Feedback</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Reviewing Product Ideas for feedback and feasibility, Internal IC &amp; Customer Community</li>
<li>
Manage Customer Advocacy Panels for Specific Products [Shared with Director]</li>
<li>
Manage Product Pilot Programs</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Responsibilities - Product Support / Adoption</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Maintain Product Documentation, Knowledge Base, How-To &amp; FAQ</li>
<li>
Create Written Walkthroughs &amp; Video Screencasts for Product Features</li>
<li>
Assist with and Understand Product Support Cases to Listen for Key Use Cases</li>
<li>
Mentor for Product Support Team - Product Features</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Requirements</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
5+ years Product Management experience</li>
<li>
Customer Support / Service related product management experience</li>
<li>
Technical platform experience a must</li>
<li>
Has successfully launched a new software product / significant feature
</li>
<li>Experience writing technical product documentation and release notes</li>
<li>
Strong presentation skills</li>
<li>
Salesforce experience a plus</li>
