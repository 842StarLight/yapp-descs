<h2>Delivery Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>The Delivery Manager is responsible for leading a team of front-line employees to deliver orders to key customers in a fast-paced direct store delivery environment. This includes, training and supervising the delivery department team members.
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Description:
</b></p>
<p><b>Position Duties:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage a group of 30 delivery drivers
</li>
<li>Ensures Transportation team members comply with local, state and federal regulatory agencies (i.e. DVIR, DOT, OSHA, etc.) and ensures that electronic logs are kept current and accurate
</li>
<li>Supervises labor hours and consumable supplies within budget; prepares work schedules including extra workdays and shifts as needed
</li>
<li>Assists with the training of new associates, including cross training of existing associates
</li>
<li>Reviews delivery functions and productivity reporting; suggests efficiency ideas, cost reduction
</li>
<li>Maintain relationship with fleet repair service provider
</li>
<li>Effectively communicate routing issues or concerns
</li>
<li>Scrutinize and approve truck repair quotes and invoices
</li>
<li>Measures and assists with implementation of delivery changes
</li>
<li>Interprets, trains and consistently enforces company policies, protocols and procedures including but not limited to fuel management, ELD Compliance, FMSCA, Fleet Maintenance
</li>
<li>Communicate daily with sales, warehouse and office departments to address any issues or concerns related to account sales or delivery
</li>
<li>Conduct team meetings and safety training to ensure that preventive measures are enforced
</li>
<li>Accompany drivers on routes to customer accounts to evaluate work performance, assist with sales, and ascertain any customer problems
</li>
<li>Foster a work environment that maximizes employee involvement, morale and engagement
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Position Requirements:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>General working knowledge of trucks and repair technics
</li>
<li>Ability to pass non-CDL, CMV requirements/ Ability to obtain CDL license in 1st year
</li>
<li>AS400 experience preferred
</li>
<li>Familiar with GPS/ELD Software
</li>
<li>3+ years of experience in delivery operations
</li>
</ul>
