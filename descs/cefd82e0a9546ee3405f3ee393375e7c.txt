<p><b>Multi-Media Sales Executive</b></p>
<p>National B2B multimedia publishing company is seeking a detail-oriented sales executive for print and digital magazines, websites, e-newsletters, eBooks, digital marketing platforms, webinars, roundtables and more.</p>
<p>We’re looking for a real sales prospector who knows that “no” is only a “yes waiting to happen.” Ideal candidate must have strong written and verbal communication skills, excellent organizational skills, the skills required to write proposals, create compelling email correspondence, manage multiple deadlines – and above all, the ability to close sales.</p>
<p>Proficiency in Microsoft Office, including Word, PowerPoint and Excel a must. Experience with Salesforce is extremely helpful. Marketing and project management experience preferred. Excellent opportunity for growth for a smart, energetic deal-closer.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>

<li>Coordinate sales activities with other sales team member</li>
<li>Close sales via phone and email</li>
</ul>

<li>Strong leadership qualities</li>
<li>Ability to build partnerships with clients</li>

<li>Familiarity with Salesforce</li>

<li>Superb listener</li>
<li>Detail-oriented with a high level of accuracy and organization</li>

<li>Relentless sales-closer</li>
</ul>
