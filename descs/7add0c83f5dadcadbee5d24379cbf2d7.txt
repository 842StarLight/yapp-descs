<h2>Make-Up Artist, Sahra Spa</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>As a Make-up Artist, you will bring out the best in clients, collaborating with them in a close, creative atmosphere where abstract visions become inspiring realities. Your artistic intuition and stylistic sensibility will give guests the chic elegance to truly engage in every urban adventure.</p>
<br>
<p>
<b>
PRIMARY JOB DUTIES:</b></p>
<p>
Duties include, but are not limited to, the following:</p>
<ul>
<li>Provide professional make -up application to guests</li>
<li>Upsell products and services.</li>
<li>Greet guest, offer beverages, including alcoholic options.</li>
<li>Reserve additional services through the concierge.</li>
<li>Direct guests on where to be for their next service such as spa check-in, etc.</li>
<li>Responsible for retail sales and assisting the department in making retail goals.</li>
<li>Monitor inventory levels and notifies manager when ordering is necessary.</li>
<li>Follow handbook guidelines in regards to proper usage of all spa equipment and spa products.</li>
<li>Follow all prescribed safety procedures; immediately notifies security and supervisor of accidents and potential safety hazards.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
</div>
<p></p>
<br><div>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS:</b></p>
<p><b>
Required:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>At least 250 hours experience in an upscale spa/salon setting.</li>
<li>Strong knowledge of various make-up products, brands and application techniques.</li>
<li>Possess, or be able to immediately obtain, a valid Nevada Aesthetics, or Cosmetology license.</li>
<li>High School diploma or equivalent.</li>
<li>Intermediate skills in Microsoft Excel, Word and Outlook as well as spa management software.</li>
<li>Strong written and verbal communication skills with the ability to effectively communicate in English.</li>
<li>Comprehensive knowledge of laws, codes and regulations, impacting Spa Management including OSHA.</li>
<li>Polished appearance and demeanor.</li>
<li>Excellent customer service skills.</li>
<li>Ability to obtain and maintain full knowledge and understanding of company and department rules and regulations, policies and procedures.</li>
<li>At least 21 years of age</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Preferred:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Experience in a high volume luxury salon environment.</li>
<li>Previous experience working in a large, luxury resort setting.</li>
</ul>

</ul>
