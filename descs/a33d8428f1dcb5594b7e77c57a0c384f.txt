<p><b>
Overview:</b></p>
<p>
This position is part of the Product Development Team responsible for supporting both the Life and Annuity product lines inside the Individual Life &amp; Annuity (ILAD) Actuarial department. Within the ILAD Actuarial department, an associate can expect to be part of a team environment, to work on a variety of projects across both product lines, and to gain exposure to all areas of the division. This position will primarily focus on product development for both the Life and Annuity product lines including product ideation, design, pricing, and product support for ILAD business areas and related actuarial functions.</p>
<ul><li>The decision on grade level will be made considering the applicant's qualifications and experience. Associates will be considered for higher grade levels based on the ability to:</li></ul>
<ul>
<li><p>Demonstrate subject matter expertise and lead product development and pricing.</p></li>
<li><p>Interact independently and confidently with individuals from across the Company, including senior management.</p></li>
<li><p>Manage and prioritize multiple projects at once.</p></li>
</ul>
</div>

<div><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
What you would do in this job:</b></h2>
<div>
<div></div>
<div>
<ul>
<li><p>Provide leadership, analysis, and decision making relative to ILAD product development balancing sales growth objectives, market needs, regulatory constraints, administrative and illustration system integration, risk management and profitability.</p></li>
<li><p>Create and/or oversee product pricing models and increase pricing capabilities to provide results on a Stat, GAAP, Capital, or Economic Value view, as appropriate.</p></li>
<li><p>Draft policy forms for products and riders to accurately describe features and benefits, taking into account all state and SEC regulatory requirements, as well as providing appropriate risk mitigation options and consistency with marketplace standards.</p></li>
<li><p>Oversee preparation of actuarial supporting documents for state filings and SEC filing process.</p></li>
<li><p>Apply holistic product and risk management knowledge to provide actuarial and business input to enhance consistency between administrative practices, policy form language, systems, sales materials, illustrations, etc.</p></li>
<li><p>Provide leadership and product expertise through participation in system development and testing for administration, illustration, and marketing systems to ensure values are accurate and consistent with product design.</p></li>
<li><p>Provide product expertise as liaison to the ILAD and corporate actuarial financial reporting units to communicate new product designs and to facilitate peer review of assumptions or product mechanics in models or tools used for financial reporting, capital analysis, or DAC.</p></li>
<li><p>Represent ILAD on one or more corporate committees.</p></li>
<li><p>Communicate results and drive approval from division leadership and other key stakeholders.</p></li>
<li><p>Actively participate in ILAD inforce management and experience study creation/review.</p></li>
</ul>
</div>
