<h2>Accounts Payable/Accounts Receivable Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Accounts Payable/ Account Receivable Specialist</b></p>
<p>Wyland Worldwide is looking for another hard-working, self-motivated, organized, detailed oriented person to process Accounts Payable and Accounts Receivable.</p>
<p>Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</p>
<p>Accounts Payable:</p>
<ul>
<li>Review code and enter accounts payable invoices</li>
<li>Match receipts to credit card statements</li>
<li>Post manual checks/ACH payments/voided checks</li>
<li>Generate aging report weekly</li>
<li>Print checks and prepare for mail</li>
<li>Maintain vendor files and vendor cards</li>
<li>Contact vendor as needed</li>
<li>Annually generate, mail and file 1099/1096 forms</li>
</ul>
<p>Accounts Receivable:</p>
<ul>
<li>Process daily sales envelopes: verify cash receipts, prepare credit card summary form</li>
<li>Invoice deposits on customer orders and post cash receipts for customer deposit on orders</li>
<li>Process internet orders</li>
<li>Post and email invoices for shipped art</li>
<li>Retrieve Amazon reports for Amazon Digital Media sales/royalties</li>
<li>Post all cash receipts (includes cash received from customer and other sources (ie. Royalty checks))</li>
<li>Process all financed customer orders</li>
<li>Process gallery cancellations/transfers, issue credit memos, gift cards</li>
<li>Process web orders; print settlement reports, print orders, collate and forward to customer service</li>
<li>Run/email monthly distributor account statements</li>
<li>Run/email monthly layaway –plan payments</li>
<li>Maintain distributor cash-in log and sales reports</li>
<li>Maintain records and files for accounts receivable documentation</li>
<li>Run/distribute gallery cash-in, orders taken and top consultant reports weekly/monthly</li>
</ul>
<p>Desired skills and experience:</p>
<ul>
<li>Associate degree preferred</li>
<li>Three to five years of experience in accounting</li>
<li>Must be computer literate with knowledge of Microsoft products</li>
<li>Knowledge of accounting software (Navision a plus)</li>
<li>Good communication and interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Work independently</li>
<li>Team player</li>
<li>Detail orientated</li>
<li>Self-motivated</li>
<li>Outgoing and positive demeanor</li>
<li>Understand and follow work rules and procedures</li>
</ul>
<p>Type of position: Full-time (flexible schedule)</p>
<p>Benefits:</p>
<ul>
<li>Paid vacatioin</li>
<li>401k</li>
<li>Health care</li>
</ul>
<p>No phone calls or walk in’s please. Considerations by Resume submission.</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Salary: $21.00 to $24.00 /hour</p>
