THE ROLE
<br><br>
The Strategic Account Manager role is all about making sure Simon's largest, and most sophisticated clients extract as much value out of our platform and services as possible. Strategic Account Managers are responsible for developing deep, trusted-advisor relationships as they work with clients across every phase of the client lifecycle from integration and onboarding, to campaign ideation, launch, and iteration, to partnership review and contract renewal.
<br><br>
Strategic Account Managers deliver value to our enterprise clients by leading strategic engagements, driving the deployment of technical solutions, and pushing process improvements. In doing so our Strategic Account Managers help marketing teams at some of the worlds largest brands to deploy campaigns that drive millions of dollars in incremental revenue for their businesses.
<br><br>

<br><br>
<ul>
