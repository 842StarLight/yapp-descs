<h2>Accounting Data Specialist- DC</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader">
<b>Overview</b><b>
</b>
</h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Performs Accounts Receivable function and creates various reports on detainees for statistical and billing purposes.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader">
<b>Responsibilities</b><b>
</b>
</h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Use of Detention Center JCMS to review each booking and release to determine which agency will be billed for housing the detainee.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
On a monthly basis, each agency shall be billed for the time of incarcerations during the month. This could be up to 17 different agencies.</div>
<div>
</div>
<div>
Keep general ledger with Finance Services on all agencies. Reconcile ledgers on a monthly basis with Finance Services on outstanding agency balances. Follow up with agencies regarding unpaid balances.</div>
<div>
</div>
<div>
Provide Detention Center Director with monthly financial reports on accounts receivable and accounts payable.</div>
<div>
</div>
<div>
Utilize spreadsheet to track adult and juvenile daily arrests. This data is utilized by the Director to track total bookings, arresting agencies, etc, as well as other statistical information.</div>
<div>
</div>
<div>
Review daily booking for errors that could affect the daily count, arresting agencies and proper billing. If errors are found, work with the Booking Lieutenant to ensure the corrections are made.</div>
<div>
</div>
<div>
Track and prepare annual graphic report on the top ten arrest codes and description of the code. This information is provided to the Detention Center Director and to the New Mexico Correctional Department.</div>
<div>
</div>
<div>
Compile the fiscal year book data. This report is updated daily and compiled on a monthly basis for use by the Detention Center Director. The monthly reports are then utilized to complete the fiscal year report.</div>
<div>
</div>
<div>
Detainee housing report is updated daily and compiled monthly for use by the Detention Center Director.</div>
<div>
</div>
<div>
Provide quarterly report on Traffic related bookings to the New Mexico Association of Counties Risk Management Division.<br>
<br>
<b>ADDITIONAL DUTIES. </b>Other duties as assigned</div>
<div></div>
<br>
</div></div></div>
