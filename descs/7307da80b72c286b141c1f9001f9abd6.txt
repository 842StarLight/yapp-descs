<div>
Hillrom (NYSE: HRC) is a $2.8B leading worldwide manufacturer and provider of medical technologies and related services for the health care industry, including patient support systems, safe mobility and handling solutions, non-invasive therapeutic products for a variety of acute and chronic medical conditions, medical equipment rentals, surgical products and information technology solutions.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Hillrom's comprehensive product and service offerings are used by health care providers across the health care continuum and around the world in hospitals, extended care facilities and home care settings to enhance the safety and quality of patient care.</div>
<br>
<div>
</div>
</div>
<div>
<b>
Description</b><div>
</div>
<div>
The Hillrom Acute Care Sales Executive is responsible for selling and implementing two new Hillrom product lines in the acute care and MedSurg environment. The territory will potentially cover Philadelphia, Boston and New York based on your location.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The role encompasses all aspects of developing sales for (1) the WatchCare Incontinence Detection Solution and (2) the EarlySense Continuous Monitoring Solution. Both systems are incorporated into Hillrom’s Acute Care and MedSurg bed offerings.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The role manages all aspects of the sales process for both solutions including but not limited to: lead generation, sales development, implementation, product training, post-sale healthcare economics programs to support, defend and grow the disposable sale components of both solutions.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Additionally, the Acute Care Disposable Sales Executive is responsible to work collaboratively with internal partners, especially Patient Support System (PSS) Account Executives (AE) and Senior Clinical Managers (SCM) to develop and support any and all local sales opportunities.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b><b>:
</b>
</div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Identify, qualify and sell WatchCare and EarlySense into installed base of Versacare and Progressa bed fleets in the territory</li>
<li>
Organize meetings with key stakeholders in targeted facilities: WOCN nurses, CNO’s, Directors of Quality, Clinical Nurse Specialists, Purchasing/Supply Chain/Materials Management and other stakeholders vital to a facility decision to purchase WatchCare and Earlysense</li>
<li>
Coordinate and Drive Early Adoption Sales Opportunities as identified by New Business Acclerator and PSS Salesteams.</li>
<li>
Explore and close new business opportunities</li>
<li>
Conduct WatchCare and EarlySense clinical sales calls pursuant to the Hillrom New Business Accelerator sales training methodology, adjusting sales messaging to appropriate audiences.</li>
<li>
Effectively utilize and coordinate internal/external resources to achieve sales and territory goal</li>
<li>
Complete company reports, plans and projects in accordance with company standards and expected deadlines</li>
<li>
Conduct effective and ongoing communications with all Hill Rom internal and external customers</li>
<li>
Understand and comply with company and regulatory/compliance policies and procedures</li>
<li>
Stay within company budget guidelines with business expenses</li>
<li>
Complete other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<br>

<div>
<b>Qualifications</b><div>
</div>
<ul>
<li>B.S./B.A. or equivalent related work experience required</li>
<li>2-5 years demonstrated experience as a top level sales performer, preferably selling premium priced disposable products or other “concept” sales within the medical device or healthcare industry</li>
<li>Outstanding presentation, organizational, selling and relationship development skills</li>
<li>Demonstrated clinical and “new concept” selling skills (strategy development and execution); medical disposable sales experience required</li>
<li>Must be able to work independently and in a team environment
</li>
<li>Exceptional written, verbal and interpersonal communication and presentation skills</li>
<li>Outstanding planning and organization skills</li>
<li>Strong analytical problem-solving skills and computer aptitude</li>
<li>Conversant w/Salesforce.com</li>
<li>Familiar with a variety of concepts, practices, and procedures related to field sales</li>
<li>Ability to travel as needed nationally based upon prioritization of WatchCare sales opportunities</li>
<li>Valid driver’s license required</li>
<li>Ability to lift up to 40 - 50 lbs</li>
<li>Clinical experience in a Medsurg or ICU environment a plus</li>
</ul>
