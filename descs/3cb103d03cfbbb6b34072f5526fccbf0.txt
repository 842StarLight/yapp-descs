<p>August Schell is an Enterprise Technology Solutions Provider specializing in the U.S. Public Sector market with immediate openings for Enterprise Account Managers.</p>
<p>
This is a great opportunity for a professional to thrive in the technology sales arena. This position will appeal especially to those who have a strong background in Public Sector sales and are looking for a company with the engineering, marketing and business expertise to support both personal and company growth.</p>

You will be involved in all aspects of the sales cycle process. You will be responsible for building strong positive relationships within your assigned territory, and meeting revenue targets.</p>
