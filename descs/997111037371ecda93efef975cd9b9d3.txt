<h2>Manager, Account Management</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>SUMMARY</b><br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The Manager, Account Management is responsible to ensure that the Account Management team is growing and maintaining profitable revenue from new and existing Impact XM clients. Collaborate with Global Sales on training procedures, development plans, and to ensure accuracy of CRM, forecasts, project status, project value, estimates and timeline invoicing of projects.Collaborate with Global Operations leadership on work order accuracy and detail, refinement or introduction of processes and systems, gather feedback from the Operations team, and participate in regular Production meetings.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b> include but are not limited to the following. Other duties may be assigned.The duties and responsibilities as outlined in this Job Description may be changed from time to time by the Corporation, as it deems necessary, without further notice to the Employee.
</p>
<p>The Manager, Account Management must drive effective and efficient account management of new and existing client business.Responsible for oversight and leadership of the Account Management team, ensuring that the team:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Adhere to all Impact XM processes, policies and procedures accurately and efficiently
</li>
<li>Promote growth within accounts by providing a high level of service and identifying opportunities for incremental revenue
</li>
<li>Manage all assigned client projects in accordance with Impact XM policies and practices to best meet client needs
</li>
<li>Complete final event costing within 12 days following the close of each event, and invoice accurately (with input from the Account Director)
</li>
<li>Support forecasting schedule of upcoming events and budgets for all assigned accounts
</li>
<li>Assess packing lists, final show services order packets and detail drawings for accuracy
</li>
<li>Manage multiple teams onsite for the event execution, while monitoring timelines and budgets
</li>
<li>Strive for continuous improvement through completion of Post Project Reports, regular 1:1 meetings, and knowledge sharing with the broader team
</li>
</ul>
<p>Manager, Account Management will need to perform the following:<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Management responsibility for Account Management team
</li>
<li>Develop Account Management objectives and strategies related to client program and strategy development
</li>
<li>Coach and counsel of staff
</li>
<li>Collaborate with Human Resources to recruit talent and develop retention strategies
</li>
<li>In collaboration with Vice President, Account Management, hire, mentor and build training programs for Account Managers &amp; Account Coordinators, as required to support company and department needs
</li>
<li>Establish regular 1:1 meetings with team members, to review project/program status, coaching &amp; development progress, and assess areas for improvement/training
</li>
<li>Consistent dialogue with Sales &amp; Operations individuals to assess training and development opportunities, new processes &amp; strategies
</li>
<li>Regular team meetings to review new processes, gather feedback for other departments, and communicate organizational updates
</li>
<li>Identify annual team performance metrics and individualized development plans for Account Management team members; work with each individual to accomplish
</li>
<li>Review team expenditures and implement strategies to reduce costs and maximize return on investment
</li>
<li>Ensure team supports the development of detailed post event reports, including margin, client ROI and other key metrics
</li>
<li>Review &amp; audit final quotes for accuracy and completeness based on the approved estimate and the client requirements
</li>
<li>Provide overflow Account Management support on clients and programs, as required
</li>
<li>Assign &amp; manage resources to new and existing clients, as required
</li>
<li>Support Sales team to develop annual forecasts
</li>
<li>Perform regular performance reviews for all team members
</li>
</ul>
