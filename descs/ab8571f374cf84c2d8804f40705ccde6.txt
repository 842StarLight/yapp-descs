<h2>Mortgage Loan Originator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Job Purpose
</b></p>
<p>The Mortgage Loan Originator originates mortgages via self-origination and in-house leads. The MLO increases mortgage loan portfolio by developing contacts, attracting potential customers, completing mortgage loan processing and closing.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Duties and Responsibilities</b><br>
</p>
<ul><li>Generate and pre-qualify potential borrowers, complete all loan applications and documents, obtain all required supporting documents and information, process loan applications and submit a complete loan file in a timely manner in compliance with company and lender procedures.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Provide company headquarters with neat and orderly accounting information, bank statements, pipe-line and closing reports on a periodic basis.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Provide applicants with accurate interest rate quotes and loan information.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintain the highest level of business and professional ethics and practices and strictly avoid any form of loan fraud or any other variation of fraudulent, illegal or unfair dealings.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Retain copies of all loan files originated , whether or not the loan was closed, rejected or withdrawn, for a period of time to comply with all applicable company policies and state and federal laws.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Attract new mortgage loan applications by developing relationships within the community and making sales calls to prospective clients.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Monitor collection, verification and preparation of mortgage loan documentation; schedule and complete mortgage loan closing.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintain strict client confidentiality.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Exhibit a high level of working knowledge of agency and government loan guidelines and packaging requirements, primarily with purchase transactions.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Educate customers on the different type of loans and credit options available to them, including the terms associated with each.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Continue education to remain compliant with NMLS requirements.</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
 <b>Qualifications</b>
</p>
<ul><li>NMLS licensed and requested to transfer license to company account within 48 hours of start date</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong communication skills</li></ul>
<ul><li>Proficient working knowledge of basic office software, including word processing, spreadsheets, e mail accounts and presentation software</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to multitask in a fast-paced environment</li></ul>
<ul><li>Work professionally with a wide array of personalities, both within and external of the company</li></ul>
<ul><li>High ethical standards</li></ul>
