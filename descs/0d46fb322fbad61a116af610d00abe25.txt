<h2>Solution Architect 2 - Cloud</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>Position Summary:</b><b><br>
</b></p>
<p><br>
The Solution Architect is client facing, pre-sales technical expert that works with designated Accounts and acts as a value-added technical advocate for clients. A Solution Architect is responsible for understanding clients’ business requirements as they pertain to IT infrastructure, qualifying opportunities, and creating designs that match the requirements with appropriate hardware, software and professional services. This role entails the following responsibilities: collaborating with other Line of Business (LOB) experts as required to address client needs, presenting the proposed solution to the client, ensuring the sale is made, and overseeing the delivery of these solutions.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
This role builds the required presales collateral to represent the solution including, but not limited to, At-A-Glance documents, HLDs (High Level Design), BOMs (Bill of Materials), Product Quotes (with pricing), LOEs (Level of Efforts), Client Proposals and SOWs (Statement of Work) Lastly, the solution architect role requires the person builds and maintains a healthy relationship with key business partners (OEMs, subcontractors, service providers) in the assigned districts and regions they support.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
The Solution Architect II (SA2) - is a client facing technical expert with deep subject matter expertise of virtualization solutions. Experience with Compute, Storage, Load Balancing, Networking, and Cloud is a plus. This technical expert would be an expert in “Art of the Deal” for sequencing activities and deliverables to the client to value sell, earn trust and enhance margin. The Solution Architect is considered a thought leader and develops Converged Infrastructure Solutions &amp; collateral. They are proficient at selling and positioning implementation and advisory services and are capable of understanding where strategic and advisory services fit for their clients’ needs.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Primary Duties &amp; Responsibilities</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Client Facing Sales Support – Work with sales account team and engineering technical expert, interfacing directly with clients. Build trusted advisor relationships, understand clients’ business challenges and propose solutions. These relationships will be with engineering, Mid-Level Management along with C Level Executives.</li>
<li>
Develop High Level Designs and Architectures – Create and document architectures that address client business problems and can be used to ensure a smooth transition from presales to delivery</li>
<li>
Create Proposals – Develop BOMs (Bill of Materials), Product Quotes, SOWs (Statements of Work), At-a-glance documents, business cases and RFP responses</li>
<li>
Sales Planning – Work with management and sales teams to develop account prioritization and support strategies that will ensure a long term trusted relationship status</li>
<li>
Partner Relationship Management – Develop healthy and trusted relationships with key partners in the geography</li>
<li>
Billable Consulting – Provide services as part of client projects that are billable. Usually in a project oversight mode</li>
<li>
Intellectual Property Development – Contribute to the development of intellectual property and industry recognition (white papers, speaking engagements etc.)</li>
<li>
Training – Develop and execute a professional plan that will enhance/build current skills along with foster skills in emerging technologies</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Travel – Travel to/from clients, corporate events and training</li></ul>
