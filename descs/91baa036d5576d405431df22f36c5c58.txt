<h2>Delivery Router</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>JOB SUMMARY</b></p>
<p>
To route in a cost effective manner without compromising our customer service expectations. To disseminate all reports through the proper channels once routing is complete.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li><p>
Run nightly route solution, paying close attention to any special delivery instructions received throughout the day.</p></li>
<li><p>
Send nightly report on number of stops, orders, bottles and cases to warehouse management team including Ashland Operations and Transportation management team.</p></li>
<li><p>
Complete the Atlanta based drivers dispatch report for the warehouse.</p></li>
<li><p>
Review nightly Load Order report with night managers.</p></li>
<li><p>
Review with warehouse any capacity issues we may have on any of the trucks dispatching that night and remedy any issues before leaving.</p></li>
<li><p>
Run and distribute nightly stop list for the Sales team.</p></li>
<li><p>
Run end of route session reports and send to Atlanta management team as well as Ashland based VP of Operations and Sr. Director of Transportation.</p></li>
<li><p>
Run nightly dispatch report for Warehouse and Transportation teams.</p></li>
<li><p>
Communicate any special delivery instructions with the drivers and leave copy of list with Transportation Manager and Driver Check-in Coordinator.</p></li>
<li><p>
Update and maintain all spreadsheets / database and miscellaneous reports</p></li>
<li><p>
Arrives to work, meetings and any work related functions on time and as scheduled.</p></li>
<li><p>
Meets agreed upon goals and objectives effectively and in a timely manner</p></li>
<li><p>
Forklift, Cherry picking experience</p></li>
</ul>
<p><b>
OTHER FUNCTIONS</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li><p>
Follows all safety policies and procedures; communicates hazards and or suggests improvements to manager.</p></li>
<li><p>
All other duties as assigned</p></li>
</ul>
<p><b>
WORKING CONDITIONS</b></p>
<p>
Prolonged sitting</p>
<p>
Ability to work spilt shift from 10:00 am - 9:00 pm Monday - Thursday</p>
<p><b>
EQUIPMENT / MACHINERY USED</b></p>
<p>
Computer, calculator, telephone</p>
<p><b>
MINIMUM REQUIREMENTS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
High School Diploma / GED</li>
<li>
3 years of routing or road experience</li>
<li>
Good working knowledge of geographical areas in Georgia</li>
<li>
Experience using delivery software systems, Roadnet / Mobilecast a strong plus</li>
<li>
Excellent organizational skills and detail oriented.</li>
<li>
Computer literate</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
PHYSICAL REQUIREMENTS</b></p>
<p>
Manual dexterity, visual acuity, reaching, bending, and lifting up to 50 lbs.</p>
