<h2>Technical Writer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Safran </b>is an international high-technology group, operating in the aircraft propulsion and equipment, space and defense markets. Safran has a global presence, with more than 92,000 employees and sales of 21 billion euros in 2018. Working alone or in partnership, Safran holds world or European leadership positions in its core markets. Safran undertakes Research &amp; Development programs to meet fast-changing market requirements, with total R&amp;D expenditures of around 1.5 billion euros in 2018.
</p>
<p><b>Safran </b>is ranked among the Top 100 Global Innovators by Thomson Reuters and is featured on the "Happy at work" rankings. The Group places fourth on the Universum ranking for the favorite companies of newly-qualified engineers in France.
</p>
<p><b>Safran Cabin </b>provides all elements of a seamlessly integrated Cabin Interior. From the overhead bins, lavatories and galleys to crew rests and cargo containers, either as independent world class products or as a fully integrated cabin. The company is headquartered in Huntington Beach in Southern California, USA and operates 30 sites in 11 countries. The over 13.000 employees across the globe serve virtually all the airlines, aircraft leasing companies and airframe manufacturers worldwide.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>SUMMARY: </b>Primary focus of this position is to support the need of creating and revising technical documents for Safran Cabin products (Aircraft Galley Inserts) for their continuous airworthiness, and meet customer requirements and requests.
</p>
<p>Documents types are Components Maintenance Manuals (CMM), Service Bulletins (SB), Service letters (SL), and User Manuals (UM).<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES </b>: include the following. Other duties maybe assigned.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Understand and apply Safran Cabin standards, processes and best practices
</li>
<li>Assist in developing new Safran Cabin standards, processes and best practices when required
</li>
<li>Plan, develop, organize, write and edit user manuals, maintenance manuals, service bulletins and service letters
</li>
<li>Research, develop and document technical design specifications and test scripts
</li>
<li>Produce electronic documentation in addition to hard copy manuals
</li>
<li>Analyse documents to maintain continuity of style of content
</li>
<li>Manage updates and revisions to technical literature
</li>
<li>Work with internal teams to understand product documentation requirements
</li>
<li>Create strong content that fulfils company standards and audience goals
</li>
<li>Write straight-forward user tutorials and interface text
</li>
<li>Analyse current content and make improvements as necessary
</li>
<li>Study drawings, specifications, mock-ups, and product samples
</li>
<li>Release the document following final approval.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Experience/Education and Certification Requirements:<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Must possess proven experience in creating technical documents and understand Safran Cabin products
</li>
<li>AS in Computer Science, Engineering, or equivalent preferred.
</li>
<li>Min 3 years of experience in Technical writing is required.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications: </b>To perform the job successfully, an individual should demonstrate the following competencies:<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrated ability as a self-starter
</li>
<li>Willingness to learn new skills and develop existing ones
</li>
<li>Proven experience working in a technical writing position
</li>
<li>Working knowledge of using STE (Simplified technical English) in creating technical content and explain scientific and technical ideas in simple language
</li>
<li>Solid working knowledge of Microsoft Office suite
</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills
</li>
<li>Basic knowledge of using a CAD software is preferred
</li>
<li>Familiarity with mechanical assemblies, exploded views, wire management and plumbing is highly preferred
</li>
<li>Strong attention to detail
</li>
<li>Skilled at prioritization and multi-tasking
</li>
<li>Knowledge of industry writing specifications (ATA iSpec2200)<br>
</li>
</ul>
