<p>Product Manager Job Description</p>
<p><b>About ThinkTank</b></p>
<p>ThinkTank’s Digital Engagement system is the leading B2B SaaS toolset used by global IT Services and Consulting firms to redefine how they win and deliver high-value business transformation projects.</p>
<p>Senior leaders of industry and technology practices (e.g. SAP, Oracle, Workday, HR, Supply Chain, Manufacturing, Life Sciences, Oil &amp; Gas) rely on ThinkTank as a differentiated way to engage clients in quality delivery of transformation projects, where co-creation, stakeholder alignment and buy-in is critical.</p>
<p>Using next generation client facing automation technology, ThinkTank supports firms in digitizing and standardizing their own delivery methodologies. Ensuring repeatable, high quality client engagements with higher margins. Project leaders also employ ThinkTank for high-impact digital workshops and live sessions, to direct large or distributed stakeholder groups or teams’ step by step to amazing outcomes.</p>
<p><b>Job Description</b><br><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b> include the following (other duties may be assigned)</p>
<ul>
<li>Support go-to-market activities associated to the ThinkTank Engage platform, including the development of market and customer facing materials.</li>
<li>Act as liaison between customer and technical teams to elicit, capture, analyze, refine, articulate and document business requirements, user stories, acceptance criteria and software documentation.</li>
<li>Manage requirement changes throughout the project lifecycle.</li>
<li>Balance business requirements with technical feasibility and sustainability.</li>
<li>Work closely with technology teams to ensure core platform technologies are being deployed in a way that maximizes their usability for the customer.</li>
<li>Ensure the sales, marketing, and customer service teams are receiving the support and training they need to successfully take products to market and help customers migrate to new platform.</li>
<li>Product launch – support the launch of new products and releases and manage the cross-functional implementation of the plan.</li>
<li>Measure and report on product performance and adoption.</li>
<li>Participate in all aspects of the release, including the management of a scrum board, sprint planning, launch activities such as internal education and training, product materials.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>SUPERVISORY RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<p>This position has no direct supervisory responsibilities</p>

<li>Experience with software-as-a-service (SaaS) platforms, JIRA.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>OTHER SKILLS AND ABILITIES</b></p>
<ul>

<li>Ability to work effectively in a highly collaborative and team-oriented environment</li>
<li>Strong work ethic with a high degree of flexibility to manage in an ever-changing environment</li>
<li>Relationship builder, both with internal and external contractors/customers.</li>
<li>Experience in deploying Enterprise Applications or ERPs and Consulting Experience with a thorough knowledge of the Professional Services industry is a plus.</li>
<li>Strategic technology vision and understanding with a strong marketing aptitude.</li>
<li>Consultative and problem-solving skills with customers and solution delivery partners</li>
<li>Ability to professionally represent ThinkTank in multiple forums</li>
<li>Willingness to laugh at CTO’s jokes a plus</li>
</ul>
