<h2>Senior Consultant, Enterprise Performance, SAP</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Deloitte Consulting LLP seeks a Senior Consultant, Enterprise Performance, SAP in Houston, TX and various unanticipated Deloitte office locations and client sites nationally.</div>
<div>
<br>
<b>Work You’ll Do</b>
</div>
<div>
Driving business transformation and on-going operations and maintenance services for the full suite of SAP software solutions. Synthesizing overall operations strategy by analyzing a wide variety of business solutions. Selecting the most relevant tools/techniques to meet specific client requirements such as business case, pricing, or complex financial analysis and interpreting and reporting on outputs. Participating in setting engagement objectives and scope, developing work plans for components of engagements, coordinating activities between workstreams and identifying changes in scope. Participating in various aspects of the full software development lifecycle for software applications, which may including requirements gathering, application configuration design, business process analysis, scripting, code deployments, enhancement validation, triage of defects and change requests, escalations and urgent issues. Evaluating clients’ technology needs, developing customized applications and draft technical and functional design documentation. Identifying and mitigating risks in process and technology for client engagements. Functioning as an integrator between business needs and technology solutions and helping to create technology solutions to meet clients’ business needs. Analyzing and determining the organizational structure and current business processes and providing business analysis of AS-IS process and TO-BE process. Gathering business requirements, conducting workshops with the business users, designing, configuring, testing and providing post implementation hyper-care support in SAP Finance. Designing the functional specifications for business requirements for Reports, Interfaces, Conversions, Enhancements, Forms and Workflows (RICEFW). Providing solution for SAP integrated processes between Finance and other modules, including Materials Management, Sales and Distribution, Project Systems and Controlling. Preparing end-user training documentation, providing trainings to end users and supporting users during User Acceptance Testing (UAT), Integration Testing, and Regression Testing in HPQC and HP ALM as well as developing and implementing processes using SAP S/4 HANA and SAP Central Finance as solution tool.</div>
