<p><b>
WHAT YOU'LL DO:</b> Responsibilities</p>
<ul>
<li>
Attain or exceed 100% of assigned revenue and product goals within assigned accounts.</li>
<li>
Develop annual business plan, determining sales and activity goals required for attainment of assigned revenue and product goals.</li>
<li>
Compile "Account Plan" documents for prospective clients, describing existing products and services used, potential product sales and related selling strategy to create robust pipeline.</li>
<li>
Maintain a current prospective business report identifying potential business and activities necessary to close client accounts.</li>
<li>
Develop prospective client sources utilizing various communication mediums, including personal contact mailings and planning client seminars.</li>
<li>
Utilize sales automation tools to define market penetration strategies. Identify and target competitive prospects and plan strategy to increase market share.</li>
<li>
Work with U.S. and Global National Accounts sales teams on joint and global client opportunities and relationships.</li>
<li>
Broaden knowledge of company products and their capabilities versus the strengths/weaknesses of competitive products.</li>
</ul>
