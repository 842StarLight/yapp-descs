<h2>Aftermarket Marketing Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<b>Overview</b><br>
This role will support the Aftermarket Marketing department. The Marketing Specialist will have generalist capability in marketing as well as assisting in developing, implementing and supporting key communications and projects using both digital and traditional tactics.<br>
<br>
<b>Purpose</b><br>
The Marketing Specialist role is responsible for enhancing and assisting in the management of Aftermarket Marketing initiatives and facilitating cross functional alignment.</div>
<br>
<b>Key Responsibilities:</b><br>
COMMUNICATIONS<ul>
<li>
Manage and post all communication on Trucks Dealer Portal (TDP)</li>
<li>
Create and manage Aftermarket Marketing bulletins</li>
<li>
Assist in management of digital assets and initiatives (internal and external)</li>
<li>
Support the Marketing team in evaluating and establishing the marketing plan and strategy by assembling and setting objectives, planning and organizing promotional presentations, and updating calendars</li>
<li>
Work with marketing team to coordinate day-to-day activities that lead to the completion of projects</li>
<li>
Manage communications with pertinent internal stakeholders, including, parts marketing, management, dealers, service, sales, IT, and business partners to ensure effective communications, coordination and messaging</li>
<li>
Responsible for scheduling, planning, coordinating and executing department webinars</li>
<li>
Create surveys and event follow-up measurement</li>
</ul>
<b>
DEPARTMENT MANAGEMENT</b><ul>
<li>
Responsible for consolidating budget information into a monthly budget update during staff meetings and taking meeting notes during key meetings</li>
<li>
Support with collection of Program Fees, communication of program and invoice creation/distribution</li>
<li>
Ownership of purchasing card transactions and monthly reconciliations with Manager</li>
<li>
Administration and management of internal vendor purchase order process (EBD) and check requests</li>
<li>
Audit all budget data from EBD, P-Card, each manager, etc.</li>
</ul>
<b>
EVENTS</b><ul>
<li>
Assist in event program planning and communication of annual and bi-annual dealer facing initiatives focused on dealer parts and service training. This includes development of main stage and breakout presentations, communication plan development, timeline management, workbook creation, budget assistance, onsite support and other event related support tasks</li>
<li>
Responsible for the planning and execution of multiple internal events including lunch &amp; Learns, department social events, Supplier Summit and Parts Sub-council meetings</li>
</ul>
<b>
Critical Competencies</b><ul>
<li>
Ability to manage a variety of projects and stakeholders simultaneously and still meet project deadlines and stakeholder expectations</li>
<li>
Detail oriented and able to multitask across a variety of types of projects</li>
<li>
Knowledge of basic accounting concepts and processes</li>
<li>
Basic knowledge of Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Illustrator required</li>
<li>
Strong communication skills - Both written and verbal (proven written communication skills)</li>
<li>
Expert software knowledge in the following: MS Office (Word, Excel, PowerPoint)</li>
<li>
Balances Stakeholders</li>
</ul>
