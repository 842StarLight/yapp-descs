<p><b>Job Title: Business Process Manager</b><br>
<b>Location: Santa Clara, CA</b></p>
<p><b>
This position reports to:</b>Sr. Manager, ACE Strategic Programs and Operations</p>
<p>
ServiceNow is changing the way people work. With a service-orientation toward the activities, tasks and processes that make up day-to-day work life, we help the modern enterprise operate faster and be more scalable than ever before.</p>
<p>
We’re disruptive. We work hard but try not to take ourselves too seriously. We are highly adaptable and constantly evolving. We are passionate about our product, and we live for our customers. We have high expectations and a career at ServiceNow means challenging yourself to always be better.
</p>
<p><b>What you get to do in this role:</b></p>
<p>
The Alliances and Channel Ecosystem (ACE) Strategic Programs and Operations team at ServiceNow is looking for a vibrant self-starter to fill the role of Senior Business Process Manager. This business process oriented professional plays a key support role to build consensus and help drive a single programmatic approach through multiple program managers and initiatives in a fast-placed, solutions-oriented environment. They will help mature and evolve our programs benefiting our technology, services and sales partners to support and grow those ecosystems, our business, and the businesses of our partners.</p>
<p>
The ideal candidate is able to meet and deliver their commitments across multiple timelines using virtual and direct teams and mentor and grow their direct team members into valuable resources and future leaders. They also know the meaning of work/life balance and while it isn’t always achievable, they never lose sight of it! We are looking for innovators, game-changers, and those people that refuse to be “status-quo”!</p>
<p>
What you’ll lead:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Formalize a training strategy including development and implementation for Alliances and Channel Ecosystem by asking the right questions and working closely with our sales and process leadership to enforce and establish training.</li>
<li>
Work synchronously with all or partner program managers and their programs and initiatives, our partner marketing, event management, enablement, procurement, vendors, and solution consulting teams, as well as work closely with leadership across all of Alliances and Channel Ecosystem.</li>
<li>
Communicate and collaborate effectively with our partners, as well as share results and feedback across Alliances and Channel Ecosystem.</li>
<li>
Create professional, concise and compelling presentations and templates.</li>
<li>
Ensure all program and process criteria are published and maintained, help drive the execution of strategic business initiatives including program launches, content creation, and executive updates and briefings.</li>
<li>
Help shape, evolve and implement our strategy through business process improvement, project management, requirements gathering, analysis, and market research.</li>
<li>
Deliver a world-class experience to our partners through every initiative, project, program and training you touch.</li>
<li>
Provide end-to-end partner events experience by creating compelling cases for partners to attend events along with events analysis and surveys.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
In order to be successful in this role, we need someone who has:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
5+ years of Project Management/Program Management experience required</li>
<li>
5+ years of business process improvement experience required</li>
<li>
5+ years of training and enablement experience</li>
<li>
5-7+ years working with Channel Partners</li>
<li>
Experience working with PaaS, ISVs, Consulting or Reseller organizations</li>
<li>
Outstanding organizational, communication, and interpersonal skills</li>
</ul>
