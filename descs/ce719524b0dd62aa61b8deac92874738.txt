<h2>Revenue Accountant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>What you’ll be doing...
</b></p>
<p>The Revenue Accountant is responsible for software revenue recognition and analysis. Additional responsibilities include maintaining and reconciling general ledger and financial statement accounts.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Some of the things you will be doing…
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Prepare monthly revenue recognition analysis and reconcile revenue and deferred revenue
</li>
<li>Execute monthly close accounting for several general ledger functions, including recording closing entries, accruals, and account reconciliations
</li>
<li>Perform monthly reporting and analysis
</li>
<li>Gain a solid understanding of ASC 606 software revenue recognition rules
</li>
<li>Review sales transactions to ensure compliance with Tableau business practices and accounting principles
</li>
<li>Perform accounting research and implementation of financial standards
</li>
<li>Proactively identify and implement process improvements
</li>
<li>Contribute to audit preparation by providing requested documentation and analysis
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Who you are…
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Knowledgeable. </b>Comprehensive understanding and working knowledge of revenue recognition, financial accounting principles and practices. Understanding of transactional processes including accounts receivable, accounts payable, purchasing, payroll, and related systems.
</li>
<li>
<b>Organized. </b>Excellent organization, project management, analytical and execution skills.
</li>
<li>
<b>Multi-Tasker. </b>Ability to work in both a team environment and independently and balance multiple projects and processes simultaneously.
</li>
<li>
<b>Communicator. </b>Excellent oral, written and interpersonal communication skills including the ability to interact professionally with all levels of the organization.
</li>
<li>
<b>Technically Savvy. </b>Proficient in Microsoft excel; must be able to prepare intermediate to advanced formulas (VLOOKUP, IF, pivot tables, etc.). Experience with NetSuite, NetSuite Advanced Revenue Management and/or Salesforce preferred.
</li>
<li>
<b>Educated. </b>Bachelor's degree with emphasis in Accounting. Minimum of two years of relevant accounting work experience. CPA and/or Big Four accounting firm experience preferred.
</li>
<li>
<b>You are a Recruiter! </b>Tableau hires company builders and, in this role, you will be asked to be on the constant lookout for the best talent to bring onboard to help us continue to build one of the best companies in the world!
</li>
</ul>
