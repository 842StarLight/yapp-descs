<h2>Network Sales Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Come join one of the fastest growing SD-WAN and SD-Security companies in the Silicon Valley.</p>
<p>
This Senior Network Sales Engineer will be developing, implementing, and supporting complex MPLS and SD-WAN networking solutions to address a variety of SP and Enterprise customers.</p>
<p>
They will need to excel in a fast-paced and dynamic environment with the ability to multi-task and work closely with peers in Engineering and Architecture for implementing new technologies.</p>
<p>
This person must be a collaborative team player that will work across cross-functional groups and have the ability and willingness to mentor others and have a sound business approach for supporting our global footprint.</p>
<p>
This person will need to obsess over the customer experience and drive operational excellence in their everyday work.</p>
<p>
Besides a strong technical role, this position also requires strong people skills and an individual who enjoys the dynamic nature environment.</p>
<p><b>
Specific Duties &amp; Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Advanced Routing Protocols &amp; Technologies</li>
<li>Carrier-class Routers and Technologies</li>
<li>Traffic Engineering</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>College degree (<b>Bachelors or higher</b>); technical degree preferred</li>
<li>6 to 8 years experience with Service Provider network design and support</li>
<li>Experience in operating a large scale WAN environment utilizing BGP, RSVP, MPLS, ISIS and/or OSPF</li>
<li>Experience and knowledge of BGP and Internet Routing Policies</li>
<li>Solid understanding of TCP/IP (both IPv4/IPv6)</li>
<li>Extensive knowledge of both Cisco IOS/IOS-XR and JUNOS</li>
<li>Advanced Network Certifications</li>
<li>Ability to script using PERL/Python/Shell a plus</li>
</ul>
