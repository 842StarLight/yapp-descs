<h2>Marketing Product Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>MTBC (Nasdaq: MTBC), a high-growth microcap company and global leader in the healthcare IT market, provides leading-edge healthcare information technology solutions and business services to community hospitals and healthcare practices throughout the U.S. Our corporate headquarters is in Somerset, New Jersey and we maintain branch offices throughout the country, as well as operational offices offshore.</p>
<p><b>
We have immediate openings for Marketing Product Managers at our corporate office in Somerset. </b>Marketing product managers will be responsible for managing the growth of one of the company’s product/service offerings and will have the opportunity to work with a passionate and successful team in a dynamic and growing industry. We have multiple cloud-based products/services and will consider candidates with 0 – 5 years of experience and varied backgrounds (e.g., marketing, business administration, health, law).
</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Become the company’s internal subject matter expert on your product, the vertical market, pricing/functionality, competition, and the characteristics and decision-making drivers of existing/prospective customers</li>
<li>Create/manage/implement marketing plans to support product road-maps, including product launch and product enhancements</li>
<li>Help establish and ensure achievement of product sales goals
</li>
<li>Develop/enhance product positioning/messaging and manage its implementation in all corporate/marketing/sales communications</li>
<li>Serve as the primary point of contact for all marketing activities with internal and external stakeholders</li>
<li>Track our competitors and their activities and take appropriate proactive steps to retain existing customers and win new business</li>
<li>Develop and launch ongoing surveys to benchmark and track customer satisfaction and opportunities for product enhancement</li>
<li>Meet regularly with customers to understand product opportunities/strengths/weaknesses/positioning and communicate findings and recommendations to stakeholders</li>
<li>Develop content marketing assets to increase product awareness and reputation: whitepapers, eBooks, articles, blog posts, info graphics, etc.
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Proactive, self-driven and hardworking</li>
<li>Strong attention to detail, written and verbal communication skills
</li>
<li>Ability to think strategically and implement with excellence</li>
<li>Desire and commitment to achieve product growth goals</li>
<li>Strong commitment to collaboration with internal and external stakeholders</li>
<li>0-5 years of product marketing experience</li>
<li>BS in business administration or marketing or equivalent experience; we’ll also consider individuals with varied backgrounds such as law school grads</li>
</ul>
<p><b>COMPENSATION AND BENEFITS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Competitive salary, with cash and stock incentives</li>
<li>Robust benefits package, including health/dental/life insurance, 401k with generous company match, flexible PTO</li>
</ul>
