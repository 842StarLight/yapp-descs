<p>Hungry for a fresh start? Look no further because one of the world’s most iconic brands can help you get there.</p>
<p>
The Product and Project Manager, Delivery Technology cultivates and executes a roadmap of functionality to support and enhance Subway's global convenience strategy, focusing on satisfying guests by providing convenient access to Subway's offerings through sales and distribution channels (e.g., third-party food marketplaces or "aggregators" offering delivery, or delivery "as a service" in brand-owned digital channels including mobile and online ordering). The Product and Project Manager, Delivery Technology liaises with ecosystem stakeholders, analyzing a business case and developing documentation supporting desired features and functionality, then matrixes with technology teams to build features, functionality, and services that satisfy the needs of business stakeholders and delight guests. The position carries managerial responsibilities, overseeing a Business Analyst to support execution of the functional roadmap for convenience technology.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Functions</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Gather and manage requirements (e.g., functional specifications, user stories, acceptance criteria, visual designs) for delivery technology advancements in a heavily matrixed environment, working with integrations/API, point of sale, mobile app, responsive web, UI/UX and data teams to ensure realization of enhancements within the delivery technology portfolio; oversee and participate in User Acceptance Testing to confirm that requirements have been met by development teams</li>
<li>
Liaise heavily with business and marketing stakeholders across Subway's global ecosystem to gain an understanding of enhancement needs, capturing them into a prioritized backlog of delivery functionality, actively building and managing the prioritized backlog for consideration in ongoing Agile release and Waterfall project planning</li>
<li>
Liaise with partners and vendors in the area of delivery technology (e.g., third-party food marketplaces and delivery providers), working with business stakeholders to understand and define contractual obligations, integration opportunities, and project scope of any collaborative initiatives</li>
<li>
Liaise with architectural leadership to ensure Subway's global technology roadmap is on the trajectory to meet the needs of the business</li>
<li>
Engage with customers, franchisees, Guest Care and Analytics to enrich understanding of guest behavior, ensuring that prioritization of delivery functionality on the roadmap is informed by data and supported by business case analysis</li>
<li>
Manage resources (e.g., Business Analyst) who contribute toward the effort of gathering functional requirements, interfacing with stakeholders, reviewing designs, and assessing business case value</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Basic Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor's in Marketing, Business, Communications, Information Systems or Equivalent Field; Master's in Business Administration preferred</li>
<li>
Agile/SAFe certifications preferred</li>
<li>
5+ years' relevant experience; 1-year relevant experience successfully managing staff or direct reports</li>
<li>
Deep eCommerce and digital marketing subject matter expertise</li>
<li>
A passion for working on the bleeding edge of technology and staying current with retail- and food-specific technology trends</li>
<li>
Comfort with contemporary product management and software development methodologies (Agile, SAFe, Scrum, Kanban)</li>
<li>
Ability to manage down (1+ direct reports), laterally (product managers of sales channels or integrations teams) and up (reporting status and surfacing key decisions to leadership)</li>
<li>
Strong influential communication skills (verbal, written and presentation), with the ability to exert influence and meet commitments in a highly matrixed organization</li>
<li>
Exceptional project management skills in a technical domain along with an ability to successfully juggle multiple initiatives and manage to change priorities</li>
<li>
Ability to thrive in a fast-paced, dynamic environment</li>
</ul>
