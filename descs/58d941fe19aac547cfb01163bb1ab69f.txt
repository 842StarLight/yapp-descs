<h2>Executive Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Mezzetta Foods is searching for an experienced, reliable and task-oriented Executive Assistant. The Executive Assistant will provide direct, day-to-day company and personal support to the company’s Owner and President. They will work directly with C-level executives and will be responsible for performing a number of administrative duties. The ideal candidate is highly self-motivated, professional, and capable of managing their work load and prioritizing tasks in a fast-paced corporate environment. Confidentiality and discretion are a must.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
The incumbent of this position will split time between our Larkspur and American Canyon offices. Flexibility is a must.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Coordinate executive communications, including taking calls, responding to emails and interfacing with clients.</li>
<li>
Prepare internal and external corporate documents for team members and industry partners.</li>
<li>
Handle confidential and sensitive materials, as well as those that require analyzing before composing detailed responses.</li>
<li>
Handle personal tasks for company Owner/President including home projects for residence, personal travel, and various reservations as assigned and requested.</li>
<li>
Schedule meetings and appointments, arrange and coordinate events, and manage travel itineraries.</li>
<li>
Arrange corporate events to take place outside of the work place, such as Leadership Team Meetings, business lunches/meetings, etc.</li>
<li>
Approve office supply orders.</li>
<li>
Uphold a strict level of confidentiality.</li>
<li>
Develop and sustain a level of professionalism among staff and clientele.</li>
<li>
Conduct research, collect and analyze data to prepare reports and documents.</li>
<li>
Document and distribute minutes of meetings.</li>
<li>
Liaise with internal staff at all levels.</li>
<li>
Prepare CEO with the right documents and information for his meetings.</li>
<li>
Act proactively and without much supervision, anticipating needs and following through.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Minimum of 7-10 years of experience as an Executive Assistant reporting directly to C Level management.</li>
<li>
Advanced Microsoft Office skills, with an ability to become familiar with firm-specific programs and software.</li>
<li>
Proficiency in collaboration and delegation of duties.</li>
<li>
Possess a high degree of initiative to take independent action and be able to exercise proper judgement at all times.</li>
<li>
Self-motivated and able to work with little or no supervision.</li>
<li>
Detail oriented, and able to consistently produce high quality, accurate work and results.</li>
<li>
Strong organizational and problem-solving skills with impeccable multi-tasking abilities.</li>
<li>
Ability to work effectively and efficiently under extreme pressure and strict time constraints and have the capacity to remain calm and composed at all times.</li>
<li>
Exceptional interpersonal skills.</li>
<li>
Friendly and professional demeanor.</li>
<li>
Information gathering and monitoring skills.</li>
<li>
Decision-making ability.</li>
<li>
Work management including planning, prioritizing and organizing tasks to effectively meet deadlines and achieve specific results.</li>
<li>
Strong communication skills including listening actively, expressing oneself in a clear and concise manner and appropriate written communication skills.</li>
<li>
Proven ability to handle confidential information with discretion.</li>
</ul>

<p><b>
Life at Mezzetta</b></p>
<p>
At Mezzetta, we select the finest ingredients the world has to offer and craft high-quality products to make mealtime more vibrant. When we share our products with our consumers, we share a part of who we are.</p>
<p>
Our vision is to inspire more people to add a little happiness to everyday life through food. Because when people share their food, they share a part of who they are.<b> </b>From our glass-jarred, globally inspired products to our spirited company culture, we believe in celebrating and sharing the little things that make life special.</p>
<p>
Our story began in 1935, when Giuseppe Mezzetta brought a taste of Italy to his new home in San Francisco. Today, four generations later, our passion for food and flavor is stronger than ever. Now led by CEO Jeff Mezzetta and headquartered in Napa Valley, the company has expanded nationally to showcase a wide variety of premium products from across the globe, and we’re proud to be the country’s leading brand of specialty olives and peppers.</p>
<p>
At our family-owned company, everyone is an honorary Mezzetta — from the farmers to the team on the factory floor, from the sales team who keep the shelves stocked to our loyal fans who seek a bit of adventure in every meal.</p>
<p>
As we write our next chapter, we’re looking for bright, qualified candidates to join the Mezzetta family. Our team is passionate about flavor, quality, and fun, and we think food made with love tastes better. From sales and marketing to operations and maintenance, every day is an adventure, and we’d love to have you join us.</p>
