<div><b>JOB SUMMARY
</b></div>
<div>Provides specialized knowledge and skills to enhance the operations of the Quotations. Performs takeoffs of bids and pricing functions. Proactively works with engineers and customers to meet their changing business needs and resolve issues . Develops quotations and requests bid and/or performance bonds for customers within company profit objectives and guidelines. <div><b>RESPONSIBILITIES
</b></div>
<div><i>Essential Functions
</i></div>
<ul>
<li>Performs takeoffs of bids and pricing functions that could include products such as motor control centers, motor drives, control components, fixtures, lighting, lamps, dimming systems, switchgear, duct systems, apparatus and specialty equipment.
</li>
<li>Utilizes senior level knowledge and experience to make judgment and discretionary decisions to determine what product specifications, applications, and pricing are necessary for a specific project.
</li>
<li>Proactively works with engineers and customers to meet their changing business needs and resolve issues.
</li>
<li>Provides guidance and may participate in the completion of the quotation process for each assigned project.
</li>
<li>Provides customers with timely follows-up on specialized or technical inquiries and requests.
</li>
<li>Develops quotations and requests bids for customers within company profit objectives and guidelines.
</li>
<li>Provides consistent, responsive, quality service to our customers.
</li>
<li>Actively participates in coordinating, analyzing, and acting upon information and feedback of customer needs, pricing, products, competition, and procedures.
</li>
<li>Coordinates with BSE staff to resolve customer billing issues resulting from errors in the quotations process, pricing, shipping, deliver or other customer service/credit areas.
</li>
<li>Maintains an active log of wins/losses for project bids that includes vendors and contractors quoted
<br>
<br>
<i>Non-essential Functions
</i>
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Provides input and market intelligence, as required.
</li>
<li>May place orders and hold orders on secured projects, prepare submittal drawings, and release hold orders after submittal approval.
</li>
<li>May obtain orders and provide sales support for the BSE outside sales force.
</li>
<li>May provide guidance in purchasing functions, product evaluations and code descriptions, inventory management, pricing development, and other related services.
</li>
<li>Attends sales meetings and training programs, as required.
</li>
<li>Participates in sales meetings and trade shows, as needed.<br>
</li>
</ul>
