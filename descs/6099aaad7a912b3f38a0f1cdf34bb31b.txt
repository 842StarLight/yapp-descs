<h2>Senior Solution Specialist, Customer & Marketing, Human Services Transformation</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Deloitte Consulting LLP seeks a Senior Solution Specialist, Customer &amp; Marketing, Human Services Transformation in Mechanicsburg, PA and various unanticipated Deloitte office locations and client sites nationally.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
<br>
<b>Work You’ll Do</b>
</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>Delivering large, complex systems development and transformation projects to Human Service agencies across federal, state, and local government agencies. Transforming how clients deliver services to customers. Directing large technology engagements and participating in projects including ideation, identification, planning and cost estimation through requirements, design, development, testing, implementation and production. Performing assessments of existing information systems, and identifying gaps between business requirements and current system capabilities. Functioning as an integrator between business needs and technology solutions and helping to create technology solutions to meet clients’ business needs. Defining systems strategy, developing system requirements, processing design, prototyping, and testing custom technology solutions, and supporting system implementation. Creating test cases and performing functional, non-functional, system integration, black-box, white-box, and end-to-end testing of software applications. Creating technical and functional specifications, including screen layouts, navigation flows, logic diagrams, data models, process models, and pseudo code, to be used for application development. Tracking application defects using defect tracking tools, coordinating defect resolution efforts, and documenting resolution details. Working across all phases of the Agile and Waterfall project delivery and software development lifecycle for the design, development, testing and implementation of Web Applications, Database design and Windows services solutions for enterprise-level clients. Interfacing directly with client stakeholders to gather functional and technical requirements, analyzing client requirements, and translating requirements into project designs to deliver solutions that meet client needs using MS Project and MS Office Tools. Using MS Visual Studio, Structured Query Language (SQL) Server, Oracle, Internet Information Service (IIS), Team Foundation Server (TFS), Model View Controller (MVC), Entity Framework, Web API, Angular, jQuery, JavaScript and Elasticsearch to support the design and development of project solutions. Tracking and reporting on project and milestone deliverables status to ensure timely project delivery and communication of updates to internal and external stakeholders using Remedy, GIT and Jira. Using MS Test, HP Quality Center, Team Foundation Server (TFS), Postman and Fiddler to support User Acceptance Testing (UAT) as well as Unit, Integration, System, and Regression Testing to ensure successful design and implementation. Analyzing and documenting current-state systems, processes and environments to support project design of future-state/to-be systems, processes and environments. Designing and delivering user manuals and training for solutions to ensure successful implementation, adoption and maintenance in client environments. Supporting business development activities, including proposal development and responses to Requests for Proposals.</div>
