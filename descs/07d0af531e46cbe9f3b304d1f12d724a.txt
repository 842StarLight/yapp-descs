<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Summary
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div><b>Marketing Manager</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The Marketing Manager is responsible for working with the Director of Marketing on planning, developing and implementing the organization’s integrated marketing strategies, communications, and public relations activities. As a member of the Waypoint marketing team, the Marketing Manager will drive digital strategy and execution, including digital content, corporate communications, brand building, website, social media, blog, sales enablement, and events.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Waypoint is a national sales and marketing agency primarily representing leading brands that enjoy top positions in their categories within the foodservice industry. Our mission is to be a seamless and flexible extension of our clients, unmatched in our market reach and business intelligence. We bridge the gap between manufacturers and the comprehensive and evolving foodservice marketplace across a broad platform of strategic and tactical execution methods with both expansive and direct impact. Our empowered sales force is equipped with the latest technology to stay on top of the changing local marketplace and drive immediate follow through and follow up.</div>
<div></div>

<div>
Advantage Waypoint LLC dba Advantage Solutions is proud to be an Equal Opportunity Employer</div>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Waypoint is the premier national sales and marketing agency for Foodservice and Non-Foods channels and beyond.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Succeeding in today’s competitive marketplace begins with gaining the advantage, and at Waypoint we stay ahead of the curve. Our partners rely on our unparalleled strategic insights, channel-specific expertise and resources, and innovative culinary support to deliver that competitive advantage. But helping our partners gain the advantage is not enough for us – we make it our business to maximize it through our pioneering technology and big data mining capabilities. We help position our partners to consistently beat their competition and to grow their business.</div>

<div><b>
Job Duty</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul><li>Partner with the Director of Marketing on digital marketing best practices and standards within the organization, including case studies, and content marketing. Use analytic tools to track, monitor and optimize digital presence and campaigns based on historical performance and industry benchmarks.</li></ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul><li>Develop specific digital strategies and identify requirements for all creative assignments, including: email, banner ads, landing pages/microsites, mobile sites/apps and website development for both the Company and Clients.</li></ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul><li>Collaborate on all marketing campaigns including strategy, content, execution, and analysis. Manage and report on marketing metrics and KPIs.</li></ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul><li>Demonstrate strong conceptual ability and create outstanding written content that meets the creative briefs for mediums including but not limited to: social media, digital, printed collateral, website content etc.</li></ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul><li>Drive development and consistent implementation of marketing processes to ensure consistency in brand visuals with brand guidelines.</li></ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul><li>Coordinate with sales and client team to create bundled synergistic selling tools for segmentation purposes, culinary created menu concepts and promotional operator-facing materials.</li></ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul><li>Demonstrate a high level of creativity and initiative, as well as the ability to understand research and use data-driven insights to write targeted and compelling copy.</li></ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul><li>Consolidate metrics and provide ongoing reporting and analytics to senior level management and Client partners and provides guidance for program optimization.</li></ul>
