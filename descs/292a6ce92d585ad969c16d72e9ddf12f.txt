<h2>Marketing Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>PURPOSE:</b></p>
<p>Assists the Marketing team with projects, campaigns, and administration to promote the Bank’s brand, increase sales, and support the business lines.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul><li> Responsible for designing, editing and proof reading in house collateral (i.e. brochures, flyers, posters, ads, etc.).</li></ul>
<ul><li> Assist in digital marketing daily tasks (i.e. PPC reports, SEO, creation of display ads, Facebook promoted posts, blog posting and sharing). Including managing the banks social media presence.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Manages vendor relationships – giveaways, digital displays, advertising and community organizations. Screens all requests for Bank sponsorships or donations.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Maintain calendar of events and Intranet calendar, coordinating participation in community events, including expos, trade shows, parades, seminars, and bank sponsored functions.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Responsible for processing marketing expenses and maintaining the marketing budget/actual.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Assists in maintaining the responsibility for Emergency communications, including social media and radio media.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Dress as bank mascot for events as needed.</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>SUPERVISION OF OTHERS:</b></p>
<ul><li> N/A</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>ECONOMIC IMPACT:</b></p>
<ul><li> Helps manage expenses to ensure department’s adherence to budget</li></ul>
<ul><li> Manages vendor relationships to control expense</li></ul>
<ul><li> Monitors sponsorships to ensure all benefits are realized</li></ul>
<ul><li> Assists in monitoring digital campaigns to calculate maximum return on investment</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS:</b></p>
<ul><li> Bachelor’s degree in Marketing or equivalent educational program preferred;</li></ul>
<ul><li> Proficient with Microsoft Office, including Word, Excel, PowerPoint</li></ul>
<ul><li> Experience using Adobe Illustrator, InDesign, Photoshop, Fireworks, and Dreamweaver</li></ul>
<ul><li> Experience with Salesforce and Kentico helpful</li></ul>
<ul><li> Knowledge of social media content management platforms</li></ul>
<ul><li> Strong verbal and communication skills, must be accurate and organized in presentation of materials</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ability to work independently and complete assigned tasks within identified timeframes</li></ul>
<ul><li> Must have strong organizational and multitasking skills</li></ul>
<ul><li> Must have a valid driver’s license</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>EFFORT:</b></p>
<ul><li> Transports and sets up displays.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Drives to and attends meetings as required, often using bank vehicle.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Moves around bank between levels or floors while performing duties.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Lifts books, boxes, supplies, etc. up to 40 lbs.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Frequently loads and unloads the Bank vehicle with supplies for events offsite.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Walks around bank location in the performance of duties.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Speaks and listens to customers and explains Bank services.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Hand dexterity required to operate computer terminal and/ or related equipment.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Eye Coordination required to operate computer/teller terminal.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Moves around bank between levels or floors while performing duties.</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>WORK ENVIRONMENT:
</b></p>
<ul><li> Frequent interruptions, requests from multiple internal and external sources.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Cube environment. The noise level in the work environment is usually moderate.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Frequent weekend work including event set-up, breakdown, clean-up and execution.</li></ul>
