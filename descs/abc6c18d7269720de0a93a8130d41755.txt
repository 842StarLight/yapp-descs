<h2>Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Join our team as an Account Executive!</p>
<p>As an Account Executive, you will be responsible for creating new businesses, targeting companies in various industries. This will involve visiting potential customers’ sites to present and familiarize them with our goods and services as well as collaborating with internal departments.</p>
<p>AmeriPride Services, an Aramark Company, is a nationally recognized leader in the uniform rental industry, has been delivering exceptional service to all of our customers since 1889. We have grown into a multi-national organization operating production facilities and service centers throughout the United States and Canada. We develop customized uniform and facility services programs for our customers that will enhance their employees’ image, provide brand identity apparel and products, and promote the safety of their employees and clients through a full portfolio on innovative products and services.</p>
<p>Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Generate and grow new business by successfully executing on a sales plan</li>
<li>Research potential customers and prospect in assigned sales territory</li>
<li>Build strong customer relationships and close sales</li>
<li>Conduct competitive market analysis and other sales-related research</li>
<li>Earn competitive base salary plus uncapped commissions</li>
</ul>
<p>Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree or equivalent experience</li>
<li>Minimum of two years business-to-business outside sales experience focused on new account generation, preferably selling a service</li>
<li>Demonstrated success in developing new business and generating sales leads within an assigned sales territory</li>
<li>Strong presentation and communication skills with a consultative selling approach</li>
<li>Proficiency in Microsoft Office and contact-management software such as Salesforce, ACT, etc.</li>
<li>A valid driver’s license</li>
<li>Successful completion of Criminal Background, Motor Vehicle Record, and Drug Screen checks</li>
</ul>
<p>AmeriPride Services, an Aramark Company, is an EOE/AA employer, the organization will not discriminate in its employment practices due to an applicant’s race, color, religion, sex, national origin, and veteran or disability status.</p>
