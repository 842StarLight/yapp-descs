<b>ERP Application Consultant</b><br>
<br>
<i>Aktion Associates is looking for a skilled ERP Application Consultant to plan and manage successful ERP application implementations for companies in the Wholesale Distribution and Manufacturing industries. This consultant is an ERP application expert, and a strategic thinker, problem-solver and key customer adviser.</i><br>
<br>
<b>About Aktion</b><br>
Aktion Associates is a national software reseller and IT infrastructure provider focused on the Construction, Distribution and Manufacturing industries. One of the largest VARs in the US, Aktion delivers industry-specific applications, Cloud and Managed Services, IT infrastructure and support, and next generation technology solutions. More than 4,500 clients choose Aktion as their technology advisor and nearly 200 professionals chose to be part of the Aktion team.</div>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>The Position</b><br>
The ERP Application Consultant is confident, organized, analytical and skilled at pre-sales support. The consultant has a proven track record of successful ERP configurations and implementations, and is knowledgeable about financial/accounting concepts and best practices.</div>
<div></div>
<div>Core Responsibilities</div>
<ul>
<li>Facilitate process discussions with customer and Aktion technical services team</li>
<li>Conduct discovery diligence, project analysis, and scope of work creation.</li>
<li>Follow established implementation standards and service delivery requirements.</li>
<li>Develop and/or maintain all project documentation and client reporting.</li>
</ul>
<div>Required Skills/Education</div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, Business Technology, Accounting or a related field of study.</li>
<li>Strong knowledge of accounting process including operations and financials.</li>
<li>ERP application installation and configuration experience.</li>
<li>Microsoft Office applications and presentation tools.</li>
<li>Experience with Microsoft SQL Server; SQL Server relational database concepts.</li>
<li>3+ years ERP software implementation experience – Acumatica or similar ERP software.</li>
</ul>
