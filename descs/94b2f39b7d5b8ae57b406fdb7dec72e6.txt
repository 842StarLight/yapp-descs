<b>Job Description</b><br>
<p>At Wells Fargo, we want to satisfy our customers’ financial needs and help them succeed financially. We’re looking for talented people who will put our customers at the center of everything we do. Join our diverse and inclusive team where you’ll feel valued and inspired to contribute your unique skills and experience.</p>
<p>
Help us build a better Wells Fargo. It all begins with outstanding talent. It all begins with you.</p>
<p>
Wells Fargo Technology sets IT strategy; enhances the design, development, and operations of our systems; optimizes the Wells Fargo infrastructure footprint; provides information security; and enables continuous banking access through in-store, online, ATM, and other channels to Wells Fargo’s more than 70 million global customers.</p>
<p>
The Specialized Services team within Enterprise Functions Technology (EFT) / Platform Management (PM) / Platform Engineering (PE) group is seeking a senior Application Systems Engineer to work in a lead role supporting Enterprise One Number Environment (eONE).</p>
<p>
Basic job responsibilities and individual expectations include:</p>
<ul>
<li>OFSAA, AgileReporter,and related application environment administration.</li>
<li>Technical relationship management efforts on multiple platforms.</li>
<li>Acts as primary contact for coordination, vulnerability compliance, release management, and production issue escalations.</li>
<li>Tracks application and batch performance and coordinates plans for remediation of issues.</li>
<li>Ensure PAC2000 tickets meet established SLAs.</li>
<li>Ensure application monitoring meets best practices.</li>
<li>Cross-team leadership efforts on resiliency advancement.</li>
<li>Align BCP plan for application; coordinate logistics for plan implementation, resource alignment.</li>
<li>Assures quality, security and compliance requirements are met for supported areas.</li>
<li>Leads, coordinates, and develops additional staff.</li>
</ul>
