<h2>Marketing and Events Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>A successful candidate will be well organized with the ability to manage the logistics of multiple events simultaneously. Responsibilities for the Marketing &amp; Event Coordinator include meeting with commercial team members to plan event details and strategy, create agendas and content, scout and book venues, and coordinate all logistics for each event.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Marketing &amp; Event Coordinator Responsibilities</p>
<ul>
<li>
Maintain a working relationship with key conference planners and venues.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with senior members of the business development team to identify new opportunities for sponsorships and speaking opportunities.</li>
<li>
Understand the complex needs of different events and webinars. Create a plan and activation timeline for each.</li>
<li>
Plan all event and sponsorship aspects, such as venue, contracts, invites, attendee lists, collateral development and shipping.</li>
<li>
Coordinate event content including presentation development, securing speakers and panelists.</li>
<li>
Oversee all aspects events on the day-of, including problem-solving, welcoming guests, directing event set-up, communicating with staff, and organizing vendors, and managing take-down.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with wider marketing team to create a dynamic plan to appropriately promote each event.</li>
<li>
Anticipate and plan for possible different scenarios.</li>
<li>
Track event success over time through RSVP and attendance rates, attendee-to-client journey, etc.</li>
<li>
General marketing and administrative work, as necessary.</li>
<li>
25% travel time expected, including international travel.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Marketing &amp; Event Coordinator Qualifications</p>
<ul>
<li>
1-3 years of event planning and/or general marketing experience.</li>
<li>
Strong communication skills, and the ability to present event ideas and secure buy-in across teams.</li>
<li>
Thorough understanding of webinar platforms such as GoTo Webinar.</li>
<li>
Strong knowledge of PowerPoint a plus.</li>
<li>
Social media and marketing automation software experience helpful.</li>
</ul>
