<h2>Sr. Director/Director, Product Management - AdTech</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">If you are a multi-talented individual who would enjoy bridging engineering and business, have a proven track record in the Programmatic AdTech space, can effectively interact with a broad range of customers, stakeholders, or constituents, and can represent Rubicon Project products and strategies across the industry, this could be the right opportunity for you.
<br><br>
The Rubicon Project has an exciting opportunity for a Sr. Director/Director, Product Management who would own and drive a portfolio of Rubicon Project's Products tied into our Header Bidding solutions. The right person will be the expert on how publishers integrate demand into their properties in a way that will lead us to transform the Ad Industry.
<br><br>
<b>What you will be doing:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Lead, guide, and align product initiatives from inception through delivery for Rubicon Project with goals of the business unit and overall company vision.</li>
<li>Analyze competitive products and gather feedback from publishers and internal teams to drive product roadmap.</li>
<li>Work both as an individual contributor and as a manager towards creating detailed product requirements</li>
<li>Collaborate with engineering and sales leadership to develop, prioritize, and drive product to delivery</li>
<li>Work closely with UI and UX team to shape product's interface</li>
<li>Collaborate with Product Management from other product teams at Rubicon Project</li>
<li>Represent Rubicon Project at industry events or functions</li>
</ul>
<b>What we look for:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>8+ years of Product Management experience in web applications; demonstrated success in building and launching products; B2B preferred</li>
<li>Experience with current programmatic advertising technologies, Header Bidding, Prebid or similar products; building core set of functionalities and extending them toward a future vision</li>
<li>Experience working and executing effectively in a global environment</li>
<li>Proven ability to articulate business opportunities and customer needs into product requirements</li>
<li>Demonstrated skill in writing concise and clear definitions of prioritized features for the technical teams to implement.</li>
<li>Strong organizational skills and attention to detail with proven experience managing multiple projects to successful completion</li>
</ul>
<b>What's in it for you:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Take time for yourself: Take what you need -- our vacation days are unlimited and we close down the week of 4th of July and last 10 days of the year that is paid for as well.</li>
<li>Perks/Benefits: We have a very low-cost medical insurance program for you and your family. In addition, you have access to very good 401K match program.</li>
<li>Enjoy your stay: Each Rubicon Project office enjoys a variety of benefits like daily catered lunches, a fully stocked kitchen with healthy snacks.</li>
</ul>
