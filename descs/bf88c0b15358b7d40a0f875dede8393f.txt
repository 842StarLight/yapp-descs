We’re on a journey to build the best analytics platform in the world, but we can’t do it without people like you, leading the way. Forget the stereotypical tech companies of the past. <p></p>
<p><b>Overview:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>Are you passionate about sales process and helping sales hit their number? Are you someone who can make order out of chaos? Do you have a keen attention to detail and aren’t afraid to get “in the weeds”? Then we want you on our team! As a Sales Operations Specialist, the candidate will be responsible for providing all the necessary support to ensure Sales is able to thrive. Whether it’s pulling together reports around Pipeline or Quota Attainment, or acting as a point person around CRM issues, the ideal candidate will be able to handle multiple projects at one time while delivering a high level of service to our internal Sales team. This role requires excellent communication and interpersonal skills and the ability to partner with various key-players across multiple teams and functionalities. Additionally, the candidate will be able to pivot quickly depending on the needs of our rapidly growing business, all while keeping focus on the big picture. In summary, we are looking for someone who is passionate about helping our Sales team function at peak performance and who isn’t afraid to get their hands dirty in the process.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Help build, maintain, and improve our data and reporting foundation
</li>
<li>Audit all new data added to Salesforce.com to ensure high data quality, specifically around our territory model and lead routing rules.
</li>
<li>Support our territory model by continually monitoring and organizing our Account database in Salesforce.com
</li>
<li>Maintain the Sales Operations email queue to act as frontline support to our Sales teams.
</li>
<li>Suggest ways to automate and improve scalable processes, tools, and dashboards to drive operational and business efficiency
</li>
<li>Analyze data and leverage insights to identify problems, implement recommendations, and track success
</li>
<li>Help develop business requirements to build and improve systems and reporting
</li>
<li>Assist with GTM Operations special ad hoc projects and analyses
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>2-3+ years in a sales operations role
</li>
<li>Advanced skills in Salesforce.com and Microsoft Excel
</li>
<li>Expertise in working with a CRM system, including experience around territory planning, lead routing, quota management, pipeline forecasting
</li>
<li>Strong analytical and creative problem-solving abilities
</li>
<li>Results-driven and self-motivated with the ability to multitask
</li>
<li>Excellent communicator, including both in-person and virtually
</li>
<li>Self-starter with excellent time and project management skills, with a willingness to learn new techniques and processes as required
</li>
<li>Positive “can do” attitude and growth mindset; comfortable and flexible working in a fast-paced environment
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Desired Skills &amp; Experience:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Experience working with software as a service (SaaS) or other recurring revenue businesses is a plus
</li>
<li>Alteryx experience is preferred</li>
</ul>
