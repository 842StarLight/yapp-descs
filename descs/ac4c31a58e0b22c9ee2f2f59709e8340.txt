<br><br>
The Regional Operations Manager is responsible for ensuring that all loan files in their region are processed, closed, and funded according to Guaranteed Rate’s established quality, efficiency and customer service standards. A successful ROM will partner with multiple departments (Closing, UW, Appraisal, Compliance, etc.) to proactively identify, isolate and resolve problems, and will ensure adequate staffing, strategic planning and load balancing on a branch and regional level. ROMs will support coordinated onboarding strategies with Production Managers, the Transition Team, Recruiting and Regional or Branch Sales Managers, and effectively leverage pipeline, production and quality reports to promote both employee and customer satisfaction.
