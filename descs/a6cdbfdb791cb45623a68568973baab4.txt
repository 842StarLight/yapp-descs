<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Essential Job Functions (Including % of Time for Each)
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Working under limited supervision:</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
80% - Processing of invoices:</div>
<ul><li>
Performs financial support in the area of accounts payable by following written instructions, verbal directions, and take direction from designated AP supervisor or lead.</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Prepares Accounts Payable Invoice data for entry which includes research of PO or receiving activity, adding proper receipt documentation on or attached to invoices, and other validation steps such as reviewing data entered for accuracy in matching PO invoice quantity, amount, miscellaneous lines and coding, etc.</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Processes accounts payable invoices into the accounting system, matching to PO Line and validates accounting distributions to ensure accurate vouching was performed.</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Follow defined exception handling processes.</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Track time or turn backs as directed for metric reporting.</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Perform SOX controls with attention to detail including 3 way match of check to voucher package data to ensure payments to suppliers are accurate and have proper approvals, attachments, and are mailed per instructions as directed or needed to complete payment processing on designated days.</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Good written and verbal communication skills sufficient to describe the issue and request the action, using punctuation and standard verbiage provided by work instructions.</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Performs specialized calculations on invoices such as imputing Sales and Use Tax, 1099 fields for tax reporting.</li></ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
20% - Additional Responsibilities:</div>
<ul>
<li>
Accounts Receivable postings.</li>
<li>
Performs other administrative duties such as opening and distributing mail, printing, filing, pulling vendor files for audit requests as required.</li>
<li>
Perform Weekly Payroll Processing.</li>
<li>
Interface with General Accounting management, internal customers, internal and external auditors to assist with identification and location of voucher information including payment, voucher, or invoice information in AP files.</li>
<li>
Other special projects as required by management.</li>
</ul>
