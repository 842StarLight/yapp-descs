<h2>Customer Success Manager - Enterprise</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<div>Managing our core customers is a strategic initiative that requires an experienced, knowledgeable, empathic employee to act as a central point of contact for managing the needs of WorkWave and our clients.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
This is not a sales role, it is a role that can identify the needs of our customers and coordinate with the appropriate subject matter experts of the broader WorkWave team. Responsibilities include coordinating large scale projects, changes in management, support issues, financials and business operations of the customer. The <b>Customer Success Manager</b> will be the principal point of contact through which communication flows to and from our customers.
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>We work hard to get customers in the door and onboarded successfully and your role as a Customer Success Manager is to keep that momentum going.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>WHAT YOU’LL DO:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Be the principal Customer Success Manager for enterprise accounts.</li>
<li>
Proactively monitor customer engagement (via Pendo and other reporting platforms) and work with Marketing on campaigns to encourage customers to use the services they are paying for.</li>
<li>
Maintain relationships with accounts and be aware of changes to their leadership, focus and/or organizational structure so that we can best support their continued growth and success.</li>
<li>
Recognize the opportunity for upsells and cross sells and engage the appropriate sales personnel.</li>
<li>
Ability to lead, manage or influence both internal WorkWave resources as well as customer resources to achieve successful outcomes.</li>
<li>
Be the services escalation point of contact for any questions as they relate to the customer, typically in a reactive manner, working with Customer Support, Onboarding, and Training.</li>
<li>
Weekly reporting of engagement activity, outreach, churn risks/mitigation and churn.</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>WHAT YOU’LL NEED:
</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>You must have a minimum of a Bachelor’s degree and at least 2 years of experience in customer success or account management. More specifically, we’d like to see evidence that you:
</li>
<li>Have a solid grasp of customer service and some experience developing client relationships, working with clients directly to find solutions.</li>
<li>
Possess a "big picture" perspective of customer service, and its impact on revenue, retention, marketing, and our client’s success</li>
<li>
Demonstrate excellent presentation and communication skills with particular ability to adapt to your audience (client, vendor, team member), knowing how to blend compassion, respect and authority.</li>
<li>
Prior experience in the SaaS, Tech or Software space is a must.</li>
<li>
Manage the needs around customer projects, ensuring the customer and WorkWave staff are completing their tasks in a timely manner.</li>
<li>
Ability to travel up to 25% of the year</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div>
<div><b>A FEW WORDS FROM THE HIRING MANAGER:
</b></div>
