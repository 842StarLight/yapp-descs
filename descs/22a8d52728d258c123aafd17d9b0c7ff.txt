<h2>Senior Accounts Payable Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Main Tasks</b><br>
<p>HOERBIGER American Holding, Inc. (HAH) is seeking a Senior Accounts Payable Analyst for our Financial Shared Services Center in Deerfield Beach. The Senior AP Analyst is responsible for processing payables in a shared service center environment. This senior position requires five years of experience and the ability to work independently.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
HAH, the holding company for the HOERBIGER North America entities, is a service provider and the business partner in the areas of Finance, Marketing, Benefits/Payroll and IT for the HOERBIGER group of companies in the Americas. Headquartered in Zug, Switzerland, the HOERBIGER Group is a worldwide leader in the fields of compression technology, drive technology, and hydraulic systems.</p>
<br>
<b>
Individual Tasks</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Follow the established protocol for ensuring invoices are processed in a timely manner in a digital environment (paperless organization)</li>
<li>
Process all incoming invoices using OCR Optical Character Recognition (ABBYY FineReader) and invoice verification program in SAP</li>
<li>
Ensure proper coding for all non-inventory payable items</li>
<li>
Work as a liaison between the local Purchasing department and the local Controlling department</li>
<li>
Ensure all vendor accounts are handled precisely and ensure accurate and complete information</li>
<li>
Ensure good working relationship with vendors and acknowledge any queries in a timely and professional manner</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
Requirements / Challenges</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of five years progressive Accounts Payable experience, preferably within the Service and or Energy Industry</li>
<li>
Strong background in the full Accounts Payable cycle within a corporate accounting or finance function; experience in high volume payables</li>
<li>
Experience with OCR and electronic invoice approvals</li>
<li>
AS or professional degree in Accounting or Business Administration</li>
<li>
Knowledge of applicable accounts payable/general ledger systems, internal controls, procedures, financial chart of accounts and corporate procedures</li>
<li>
Solid understanding of accounts payable topics including 1099 process, unclaimed property, GRIR, down payments, drop shipments</li>
<li>
Experience with purchase related taxes (sales tax/GST)</li>
</ul>
