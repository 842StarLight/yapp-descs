<h2>Design Drafter</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Job Summary:</b></p>
<p>
Design Drafters are ultimately responsible for the design of product according to project requirements. The Design Drafter develops submittals, layouts, schematics and shop details as part of a design team in accordance with project specifications and site conditions. The position requires knowledge of the Sales, Project Management, Engineering and Production Process along with a thorough understanding of the manufacturing and construction processes with the ability to work on multiple tasks concurrently. Design Drafters must possess a strong understanding of Materials, Building Codes and some Engineering Principles.</p>
<p><b>
Primary Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p>
Project Planning</p>
<ul>
<li>Thoroughly review project specifications and scope, placing emphasis on design requirements.</li>
<li>As part of a Project Team, prepare submittal drawings for customer approval based upon project specifications, scope and engineering requirements.</li>
<li>Coordinate with project team to determine potential value engineering opportunities for the project.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p>Project Execution</p>
<ul>
<li>Work in conjunction with the project strategy and schedule set forth by the Project Manager.</li>
<li>Prepare measuring templates, acquire field dimensions and construct as-built 3D models and layouts to ensure design feasibility and accuracy.</li>
<li>With the use of Autodesk Inventor and AutoCAD software, prepare accurate assembly and part drawings to assure proper fabrication and installation of product.</li>
<li>Prepare bills of materials and project routers to the Process Engineer and Production Manager.</li>
<li>Assist the Project Manager with quality control measures throughout fabrication and installation phases of the project.</li>
<li>Assist the Project Manager in the completion of all specified closeout documents required by the customer including final ‘as-built’ drawings.</li>
<li>Perform project review with thorough synopsis of project and opportunities for improved performance on future projects.</li>
</ul>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>General Duties</b></h4>
<ul>
<li>Provide material take-offs and estimates and assist in project forecasting.</li>
<li>Assist the Project Manager with change orders, project schedules and project evaluation.</li>
<li>Integrate educational and professional experience to optimize project efficiencies.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<br>
Required Skills<br>
<p><b>Position Requirements
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Associates degree in Mechanical Design/ CAD Technology or similar is required</li>
<li>5+ years of related experience. Manufacturing and construction experience preferred.</li>
<li>Proficiency with AutoCAD and Autodesk Inventor or comparable program is required.</li>
<li>Ability to read and interpret fabrication drawings.</li>
<li>Ability to maintain a high degree of integrity, loyalty and honesty; not divulge to outside parties’ information of a confidential nature pertaining to the company’s operations or bids</li>
<li>Ability to carry out the duties described in the job description in an efficient manner.</li>
<br>
</ul>
