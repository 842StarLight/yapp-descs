<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Logistics Account Executive job summary</b></h1>
<p>Our logistics company is looking for an experienced and dedicated Logistics Account Executive to join our team. This person should possess outstanding sales experience, honed customer service skill and passion for technology, new systems, and innovative business solutions in a variety of settings and inceptions. We value Account Executives who are passionate about growing existing accounts while proactively pursuing new accounts and identifying roadblocks and obstacles to increasing business. The ideal fit will be innovative enough to suggest tailored solutions to common account problems. Dedication to growing the business and facilitating smoother connections between our clients and our internal customer support team is a must.</p>
<p>
As you meet with clients and prospects, it will be your responsibility to not only shine light on a prospect’s supply chain inefficiencies and offer catered business solutions, but to also represent our company in a professional manner that will highlight our company values.</p>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Logistics Account Executive responsibilities/duties</b></h1>
<ul>
<li>Educating customers about our shipping solutions</li>
<li>
Conducting research on customers that have a need for our services</li>
<li>
Cold-calling, generating new leads and prospecting accounts</li>
<li>
Qualifying prospects for size, complexity, and overall fit for our company</li>
<li>
Using Salesforce automation tools to track progress and report goals</li>
<li>
Analyzing consumer needs and developing innovative solutions</li>
</ul>
