<h2>Technical Lead, Content Engineering</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><b>Job Overview:</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>The Content Engineering team is a dynamic and fast-paced team within the Platform and Architecture group within Digital Technology. The ideal candidate must be able to thrive in an environment where change is constant. Strong communication, interpersonal and mentoring skills are a must for this position. An ideal candidate for this role would be a strong Leader that would own the issue and see it through resolution. The Content Engineering team releases on a weekly basis, with 2-week iteration cycles, which requires strong prioritization skills as well as the ability to pivot quickly. These releases are marketing content designed to drive sales and SEO for Macys.com. Perform other duties as assigned.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>Essential Functions:</b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>Provide technical leadership to the team that develops the presentation layer of the website – responsive, error-free, cross-browser compatible and optimized for download performance.</div></li>
<li><div>
Set standards for development of responsive sitelets and content, with focus on performance, scalability, accessibility, and SEO.</div></li>
<li><div>
Stay current with the newest online technologies.</div></li>
<li><div>
Lead code review sessions with the team to set standards for clean, readable, usable code.</div></li>
<li><div>
Build and maintain collaboration and trust with all our partner teams – either in SF or in NY.</div></li>
<li><div>
Strong creative skills to be able to provide alternate design suggestions</div></li>
<li><div>
Regular, dependable attendance &amp; punctuality.</div></li>
</ul>
<div>
<b>Qualifications</b><b>:</b>
</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Education/Experience:
</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree required.</li>
<li>
At least 5 years of experience in building responsive web experience using the latest standards-based approaches (JavaScript, HTML5, CSS3) and Foundation, Bootstrap, or handcrafted CSS.</li>
<li><div>
Ability to demonstrate previous work experience via URLs.</div></li>
<li>
Experience in creating pages that conform to the principles of semantic markup.</li>
<li>
At least 2 years of experience using CSS templating and pre-processing tools (LESS, SCSS).
</li>
<li>At least 5 years of experience in developing MVC web applications using frameworks such as Backbone, Ember, or Vue.</li>
<li>
At least 5 years of experience with a Version Control system (Subversion, Git).</li>
<li>
Expert-level knowledge of cross-browser design constraints.</li>
<li>
Experience with JavaScript-based task runners such as Grunt.js or Gulp.</li>
<li>
Experience with JavaScript unit testing methodology and frameworks (Jasmine, Mocha).</li>
<li>
Familiarity with design software (Photoshop, Illustrator) and a strong understanding of image optimization techniques.</li>
</ul>
