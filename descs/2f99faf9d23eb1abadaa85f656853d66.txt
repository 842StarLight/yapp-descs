<p><b>Description</b><br>
</p>
The Account Manager will work on a team responsible for the success and growth of a fortune 500 program within the GSA revenue channel of the organization. This is your chance to play a significant role supporting an industry leader for an enterprise level customer with growing needs. This includes, but is not limited to all revenue generation, growth, and relationship management amongst an assigned portfolio. The Account Manager will work directly with the customer, vendors, and internal departments to insure the customer’s diverse and time sensitive projects are executed effectively, efficiently, and profitably. The key motivator to success within this role will be in defining lasting relationships with key buyers that lead to additional revenue based growth. This position may involve some travel.
<p><b>Duties &amp; Responsibilities</b><br>
Key Responsibilities</p>
<ul>
<li>Effectively lead presentations and meetings with the customer in person or via conference call communicating updates, determine needs, present ideas and solutions.
</li>
<li>Establish clear expectations with the customer and team members providing clear direction to the customer as to what is needed to ensure a successful partnership.</li>
<li>Manage a portfolio of buyers or decision makers within the customer; building relationships and effectively persuade or negotiate appropriately with each generating new revenue.
</li>
<li>Aggressively identify new outlets of opportunity to generate new revenue opportunities within your defined portfolio<br>
</li>
<li>The Account Manager must possess a deep knowledge of merchandise offerings and maintain relationships with a network of vendors.</li>
<li>Must be willing to work with the creative department to identify unique and exclusive offerings- think with a creative mind.</li>
<li>Responsible for sourcing and quoting new product options to the customer.</li>
<li>Effectively communicate with various team members and Touchstone staff members and departments to meet customer needs and make sure everyone is consistently on the same page.</li>
<li>Arrange and participate in internal and external client debriefs.</li>
<li>If appropriate, attend industry functions, such as association events and conferences, and provide feedback and information on market and creative trends.</li>
</ul>
