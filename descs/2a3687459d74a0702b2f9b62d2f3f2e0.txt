<h2>Agronomy Territory Manager - CA, OR, WA, based</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>It's fun to work in a company where people truly believe in what they are doing!
</p>
<p></p>Neogen is currently looking for an Agronomy Territory Manager to join our team out west.
<br>
The selected candidate will be responsible for territory sales management primarily through our channel partners in the California, Oregon, Washington and Idaho region. They will also aid in pull-through sales efforts at the crop production level within the territory. Applicant would need to have understanding of agriculture industry and familiarity with EPA and crop label claims. Need to possess computer/technology skills, and quick relationship building abilities. Significant travel in territory will be required. Ideal candidate will be home based in CA, OR, or WA.
<p></p>
<p><b>DESCRIPTION: </b>Promotes use of and sells Neogen’s Agronomics Products to the distribution channel.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES </b>include the following. Other duties may be assigned.
</p>
<ul>
<li>Calls on customers and prospects in the field and by telephone, fax and e-mail, informs them of new and existing products/services, and explains characteristics and benefits.
</li>
<li>Promotes and sells Agronomics products and services offered by the company.
</li>
<li>Communicates with existing customer base on a consistent basis to: (1) monitor customer satisfaction; (2) resolve customer problems and concerns; and (3) monitor competitive position.
</li>
<li>Develops formal sales plans and strategies to penetrate new markets and increase market share and profitability of territory. Provides written updates on a quarterly basis.
</li>
<li>Makes a minimum of 200 documented sales contacts and 15 customer visits per month. Reports must be turned in monthly.
</li>
<li>Quotes prices and persuades prospects and customers to buy.
</li>
<li>Works with sales team to develop marketing ideas.
</li>
<li>Meets or exceeds budgeted sales revenues.
</li>
<li>Secures, records and places orders with others for filling and arranges delivery dates.
</li>
<li>Supports sales efforts of other sales staff members.
</li>
<li><p>Collaborates with communications to develop and provide product marketing material and programs that are effective and profitable.
</p></li>
<li><p>Represents company and analyzes industry trade shows and meetings to: (1) promote products and services; (2) increase market and competitive knowledge; and (3) identify new products for distribution, OEM or development.
</p></li>
<li>Documents customer questions and answers.
</li>
<li>Documents product requests by customers or potential clients.
</li>
<li>Conducts market analysis and research as needed.
</li>
<li>Reads industry publications to keep abreast of industry; shares articles of interest with organization.
</li>
<li>Participates in annual budgeting process.
</li>
<li>Analyzes and controls expenditures to conform to budgetary requirements.
</li>
<li>Participates in the resolution of customer billing or collection problems.
</li>
<li>Operates office machines such as computer, facsimile, copier and adding machine.
</li>
<li>Copies data and compiles records and reports on sales activities. Records names, addresses, purchases, and reactions of customers and prospects solicited in CRM Software Program.
</li>
<li>Completes field sales call reports.
</li>
<li>Conducts customer training at sales meetings and grower meetings.
</li>
<li>Supports efforts of distributor sales representatives by riding with reps and telesales to detail products and programs.
</li>
<li>Executes marketing programs that have been approved by management.
</li>
<li>Maintains databases of distributor representatives, certified dealers and consumers.
</li>
<li>Develops long-lasting business relationships with key cus <b>t </b>omers and industry influences to support Neogen’s market position.
</li>
</ul>
