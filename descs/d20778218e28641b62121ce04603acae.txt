<p><b>Product Manager, Platform Products</b><br>
<br>
<b>The Organization</b></p>
<br>
<p><br>
At Kaufman Hall, we believe that sustained success is never an accident. It is the result of sound decision making, based on Platform-driven analysis and disciplined thinking, and guided by the fundamental principles of corporate finance.<br>
<br>
Kaufman Hall provides management consulting and software to help organizations realize sustained success amid changing market conditions. Since 1985, Kaufman Hall has been a trusted advisor to boards and executive management teams, helping them incorporate proven methods into their strategic planning and financial management processes, and quantify the financial impact of their plans and strategic decisions to consistently achieve their goals.<br>
<br>
Kaufman Hall services use a rigorous, disciplined, and structured approach that is based on the principles of corporate finance. The breadth and integration of Kaufman Hall advisory services are unparalleled, encompassing strategy; financial and capital planning; cost transformation; treasury and capital markets management; and mergers, acquisitions, partnerships, and joint ventures. Kaufman Hall’s Axiom Software provides sophisticated, flexible performance management solutions that empower finance professionals to analyze results, model the future, and optimize organizational decision making. Solutions for long-range planning, budgeting and forecasting, reporting and analytics, consolidations, capital planning, and profitability and cost management are delivered on a single integrated software platform. Kaufman Hall’s Clinical Analytics Software empowers healthcare organizations with clinical benchmarks, Platform, and analytics to provide a higher quality of care for optimized performance and improved patient outcomes.<br>
<br>
<b>The Position</b></p>
<br>
<p><br>
As a Platform Product Manager, you will define the platform strategy, roadmap and requirements to deliver Kaufman Hall’s products across multiple industries. You will collaborate with the product teams and technology development to identify the opportunities to develop common capabilities such as reporting and analytics as well as product specific features. Based on these requirements, the Platform Product Manager will prioritize product requirements for the development team, and ensure high value and product quality are provided to customers.<br>
<br>
<b>The essential functions of the position are:</b></p>
<br>
<div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Create vision and direction for Platform-related capabilities and products</li>
<li>Collaborative approach to balancing multiple priorities and stakeholders needs</li>
<li>Execute platform roadmap</li>
<li>Evangelize strategy for Platform-related products internally and externally</li>
<li>Analyze the market to understand opportunities, buyers, users, and competitive landscape</li>
<li>Coordinate product management and go-to-market activities for platform-related products</li>
<li>Run customer design, usability programs</li>
<li>Run beta and pilot programs with early-stage products and samples</li>
<li>Ensure effective product launches and stakeholder enablement</li>
</ul>
