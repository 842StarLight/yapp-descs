<h2>Member Onboarding Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Description</b></p>
<p>
The Member Onboarding Manager is a leader who is results driven and action oriented with a strong passion for member facing interaction throughout their day. This leader owns all aspects of business acquisition with LT Training through an organized and passionate engagement with both our new and existing members. The M.O.M. will lead and manage a cohort of on-boarding specialists and other high member engagement fitness professionals each day, assuring that all of our members at Life Time Fitness are given the opportunity to be connected to their area of passion and reach their Healthy Way of Life goals.<br>
<br>
<b>Major Duties and Responsibilities</b><br>
<b>Member Experience</b><br>
1. Conducts fitness professional file audits each month.<br>
2. Manages the phone call log of all new members each month.<br>
3. Conducts shadows of member experience interactions to assure a quality experience.<br>
4. Assure the fitness floor experience is at LT expectations daily<br>
5. Reach out to all non-connected members in their first 90 days.<br>
6. Assure all Work out workshops are properly set-up and cast for</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Sales &amp; Promotion</b><br>
1. Manages the set-up and execution of major in-club LT Training events.<br>
2. Attend the weekly Sales meeting<br>
<br>
<b>Financial &amp; Budgetary</b><br>
1. Plans and manages the execution of hitting the Sales-not-Service budget of location<br>
2. Plans and manages the execution of hitting new client acquisition goals.<br>
3. Maintain a personal book of business each week<br>
<br>
<b>Managerial &amp; Supervisory</b><br>
1. Acts as the Manager On Duty during designated time blocks<br>
2. Completes a thorough business review each month<br>
3. Attend weekly business and education meetings<br>
4. Attend weekly leadership meeting<br>
5. Attend National Leadership Calls<br>
6. Run weekly Cohort meetings<br>
