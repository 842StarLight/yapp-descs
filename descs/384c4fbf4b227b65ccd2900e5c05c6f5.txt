<h2>Senior Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<div>Position Overview</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
We are looking for an accomplished Account Executive to find and drive new business opportunities and manage customer relationships in Southern California. You’ll be directly responsible for the expansion of a customer base and preservation of that business.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
The ideal candidate will have 5+ years in technology sales and experience, and should come with an established network. We expect you to be a reliable professional able to manage the sales process, customer relationship, internal resources, and a results-driven approach.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
The goal is to find opportunities and turn them into long-term relationships based on trust, credibility, and mutual satisfaction.</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Principle Duties &amp; Responsibilities</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Create business plans to facilitate the attainment of goals.</li>
<li>
Manage the entire sales cycle from finding a client to securing an opportunity.</li>
<li>
Unearth new sales opportunities through networking and turn them into long-term partnerships.</li>
<li>
Present products and services to prospective clients.</li>
<li>
Provide professional post-sales coverage to enhance customers’ overall experience.</li>
<li>
Remain in frequent contact with the clients to understand their needs.</li>
<li>
Respond to complaints and resolve issues aiming to customer contentment and the preservation of the company’s reputation.</li>
<li>
Negotiate agreements and keep records of sales activity and data in NetSuite.</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Proven experience as an IT Account Executive, or in another sales/customer service role.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of market research, sales and negotiating principles.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of MS Office; knowledge of CRM software (eg. NetSuite), will be required to maintain an accurate forecast.</li>
<li>
Excellent communication/presentation skills and ability to build relationships.</li>
<li>
Organizational and time-management skills.</li>
<li>
Business acumen.</li>
<li>
Enthusiastic and passionate.</li>
<li>
Undergraduate degree.</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div>
<div>Southern California. vCORE office is located in Irvine.</div>
