<h2>Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<p><b>Sound Engineering</b>, a leader in integrated system sales in southeastern Michigan, has openings in our Lansing, Flint Livonia and Toledo offices for AV Project Managers. We require energetic professionals who can work closely with the architectural and consulting communities.</p>
<p><b>Sound Engineering</b> is customer focused and committed to the highest quality and ethical standards for our products and our people. Our engineering and sales professionals work with the latest computerized software to optimize system features and benefits along with on staff programming professionals. We have an excellent reputation in designing, installing, maintaining audio visual systems and sound systems for our customers over the last 33 years.<br>
We are a privately held corporation.</p>
<p><b>Project Manager Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manages major projects from technical details to troubleshooting installation challenges.</li>
<li>Works with purchasing and manufacturers to obtain required equipment for projects.</li>
<li>Coordinates with the Engineering &amp; CAD for accurate as built drawings &amp; project completion.</li>
<li>Liaison with sales, service staff and contractors to promote effective communications<br>
and project coordination.</li>
<li>Ability to work with staff in the resolution of system design solutions.</li>
<li>Synchronize available team members versus project required dates and equipment availability</li>
<li>Be willing to travel throughout within the local area supervising projects.</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>
<div>
<p><b>
Required Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Knowledge of the design, installation and troubleshooting of audio/video systems and knowledge of RF distribution systems.</li>
<li>One to three years experience managing field personnel in a growing business environment.</li>
<li>Customer orientation and associate involvement/team building skills.</li>
<li>Ability to utilize computer drafting program (preferable AutoCAD) to produce or edit drawings.</li>
<li>Capability to read blue prints.</li>
<li>Must be assertive, diplomatic and able to work on an independent basis with a minimum of supervision. Self-starter with a competitive drive for results.</li>
<li>Must possess multi-task skills and foster an entrepreneurial spirit.</li>
<li>Have excellent communication/presentation/interpersonal skills.</li>
<li>Proficiency with PC based applications such as Word, Excel and Outlook.</li>
<li>Knowledge of NEC requirements helpful.<br>
</li>
</ul>
