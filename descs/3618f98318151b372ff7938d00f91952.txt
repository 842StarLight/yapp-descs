<h2>Trademark Paralegal</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Stradley Ronon is currently seeking an experienced Trademark Paralegal to assist attorneys with the prosecution of trademarks in our Malvern, PA office. The Trademark Paralegal will need to be able to take initiative, think independently, write well, exhibit a sense of urgency, and perform sophisticated tasks of a highly sensitive nature.</p>
<ul>
<li>
Conduct and analyze trademark searches; prepare necessary clearance reports;</li>
<li>
Handle the day-to-day prosecution of domestic and foreign trademark applications and correspond with foreign trademark counsel;</li>
<li>
Provide guidance to clients regarding proper use of trademarks;</li>
<li>
Work with trademark counsel in reviewing advertising, point-of-sale and promotional materials for trademark issues;</li>
<li>
Conduct trademark portfolio and budget reviews;</li>
<li>
Assist in domestic and foreign trademark litigation and enforcement.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor's Degree or equivalent experience;</li>
<li>
5+ years of experience as a trademark paralegal with high-level responsibilities on a global trademark portfolio;</li>
<li>
Prior law firm or corporate IP department experience;</li>
<li>
Ability to consistently plan, organize, work independently, think critically, and manage multiple, competing deadlines;</li>
<li>
Adept at conducting online trademark searches;</li>
<li>
General knowledge of domestic and foreign trademark law, policies and processes;</li>
<li>
Proficiency with MS Office products, particularly MS Word, Excel and PowerPoint, and with trademark-related websites.</li>
</ul>
