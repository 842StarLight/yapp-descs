<h2>BEAUTY CONSULTANT</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Job Objectives</b><br>
<br>
Maximizes sales and brand awareness by providing exceptional customer care to all customers in a friendly, efficient, and professional manner; gives impartial, personalized, expertise and advice regardless of brand and budget.<br>
Demonstrates a passion for beauty and provides inspirational beauty knowledge and know-how.<br>
Provides customers with solutions for their beauty needs, including mini-makeovers and skincare consultations, with an emphasis on the customers’ total look.<br>
Models and delivers a distinctive and delightful customer experience.<br>
Oversees and maintains overall beauty department standards.<br>
<br>
<b>Job Responsibilities/Tasks</b><br>
<div>
<b>Customer Experience</b><br>
</div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Engages customers by greeting them and offering assistance with products and services. Resolves customer issues and answers questions to ensure a positive customer experience.</li>
<li>
Models and shares customer care best practices by proactively offering assistance, asking questions to determine customers’ needs, and informing customers of options.</li>
<li>
Greets, listens, and engages customers to identify their needs, making them aware of appropriate products and services, and providing warm transfers to other areas of the store when necessary.</li>
<li>
Provides expertise in product demonstrations and consultations utilizing tools such as the Matchmade Device, Skin Hydrometer, and tablet.</li>
<li>
Leads and delivers in-store events to promote brand awareness and loyalty of Owned Brands and total Beauty/Personal Care.</li>
<li>
Builds relationships with customers; provides authentic closure of customer interaction and builds and fosters relationships.</li>
<li>
Engages with omni-channel solutions to enhance customer engagement/experience.</li>
<li>
Locates products in other stores or online if unavailable in the store.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Operations</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Achieves or exceeds predetermined sales goals by assisting customers in the purchase of products and by modeling exceptional customer care.</li>
<li>
Reviews scorecard on a regular basis to understand performance within store and district; partners with store leadership and BAE to create improvement goals.</li>
<li>
Consistently focuses on link-selling and up-selling with customers by showing them sale items and complimentary offerings.</li>
<li>
Uses testers and sampling to demonstrate product application and leverage items from beauty promotional programs.</li>
<li>
Ensures testers are stocked and maintained in compliance with hygiene standards; maintains a clean and organized department.</li>
<li>
Implements company asset protection procedures to identify and minimize profit loss.</li>
<li>
Processes sales for customers and/or employee purchases on cash register.</li>
<li>
Participates in meetings and conference calls concerning programs and promotions, new items, brand launches, sales goals, and modeling exceptional customer care.</li>
<li>
Has working knowledge of store systems and store equipment.</li>
<li>
Ensures compliance with state and local laws regarding regulated products (e.g., alcoholic beverages and tobacco products).</li>
<li>
Complies with all company policies and procedures; maintains respectful relationships with coworkers.</li>
<li>
Completes special assignments as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Training &amp; Personal Development</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Seeks self-development by monitoring own performance, setting high personal standards, maintaining awareness of beauty trends, learning from others, and improving job performance.</li>
<li>
Maintains knowledge of competition, new product/brand launches, and overall industry trends.</li>
<li>
Attends and participates in meetings, seminars, and other knowledge and business-driving opportunities; educates store team on new beauty updates, information, and learnings from training.</li>
<li>
Attends training and completes e-learning modules requested by Manager or assigned by Corporate.</li>
<li>
Maintains professional appearance and image in compliance with company guidelines at all times.</li>
<li>
Models and shares behavior with other Beauty Consultants; provides training and assistance to new Beauty Consultants.</li>
<li>
Works collaboratively with the Beauty Area Expert to review goals and maintain product knowledge.</li>
</ul>

<b>Basic Qualifications</b><br>
<ul>
<li>High School Diploma/GED and at least 1 year of experience working in a retail sales environment that required meeting a defined sales goal OR at least 1 year of experience proactively selling beauty or cosmetic products and providing customer care.</li>
<li>
Experience in developing ways to accomplish goals with little or no supervision, depending on oneself to complete objectives and determining when escalation of issues is necessary.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of products and brands in order to engage and meet the needs of the customer.</li>
<li>
Experience building and maintaining relationships within a team.</li>
<li>
Basic level PC/tablet skills.</li>
<li>
Requires willingness to work flexible schedule including evenings, weekends, and holidays.</li>
<li>
Must be fluent in reading, writing and speaking English. (Except in Puerto Rico)</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
Preferred Qualifications</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Licensure in Cosmetology or as an Esthetician as granted by appropriate state licensing authority.</li>
<li>
Experience establishing &amp; maintaining relationships with individuals at all levels of the organization, in the business community &amp; with vendors.</li>
<li>
Experience demonstrating makeup application and providing makeovers to customers.</li>
<li>
Experiencing selling Prestige brands.</li>
<li>
Degree from Beauty School.</li>
<li>
Experience with another retailer in the form of an Externship.</li>
</ul>
