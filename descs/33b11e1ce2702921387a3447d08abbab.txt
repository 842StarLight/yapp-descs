<h2>Sr. IT Data Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>The Sr. IT Data Engineer will provide deep data knowledge and lead data investigations of questions or anomalies primarily for commercial data environment (CDE), business intelligence and data set queries from sources. This role will entail providing support for headquarters and commercial field organization data inquiries and be the subject matter expert for the CDE. This SME role will assist Business power users when they have questions on the data elements or integration.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The ideal candidate will have experience in supporting commercial pharmaceutical enterprise wide data environments that support multiple downstream application such as business intelligence, customer relationship management applications, etc. for multiple internal business groups. The key tasks for this role are following computer system development processes (including validation as appropriate), coordinating gathering system enhancement requirements from business areas, documenting and translating into solution designs, management of testing process, and implementing changes into production.</div>
