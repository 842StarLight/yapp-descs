<div>
ActiveCampaign is seeking an experienced <b>Head of Partnerships</b> to lead the strategic growth and ongoing management of key business partnerships. This role will build upon the vision and strategy for identifying key partner opportunities, prioritizing and executing on all partner-related programs and successes.</div>
<div>
This role will work closely with a range of internal stakeholders (such as Product, Engineering, Marketing and Sales) to maximize the partner opportunities and experiences and achieve ROI on partner investments and relationships.</div>
<div>
As Head of Partnerships, you will be a key force in advancing ActiveCampaign’s strategy that advances our mission to provide our SMB customers a comprehensive solution to their marketing and revenue success.</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Key Responsibilities</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Build upon the blueprint for ActiveCampaign’s partnership strategy and roadmap</li>
<li>
Execute upon this strategy, identifying key partner opportunities that are key to customer success and needs</li>
<li>
Build productive relationships with key internal and external stakeholders</li>
<li>
Own analyses that provides actionable insight into business metrics, issues, and business opportunities; develop and execute plans to realize these opportunities</li>
<li>
Work closely with internal teams and external partners to drive continuous growth while also improving customer experience</li>
<li>
Develop qualitative and quantitative data/research to identify opportunities; work with Product and Engineering teams to implement changes throughout the tech stack and product roadmap to ensure enablement of partner opportunities</li>
<li>
Build partner revenue models to inform decision making</li>
<li>
Provide clear reporting on partner strategy and successes to key stakeholders</li>
<li>
Advocate for product and engineering changes or advancements that would enable partner opportunities</li>
<li>
Conduct or add to market and customer research</li>
<li>
Ensure excellent and productive partner relationships, enabling these throughout the business</li>
<li>
Maintain ongoing education and involvement in the industry to ensure awareness of best partner opportunities</li>
<li>
Work effectively with the Marketing and PR teams on partner brand awareness</li>
</ul></div>
