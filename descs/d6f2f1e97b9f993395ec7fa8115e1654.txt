<div>
Description<div>
</div>
<div>Chubb is the world’s largest publicly traded property and casualty insurer. With operations in 54 countries, Chubb provides commercial and personal property and casualty insurance, personal accident and supplemental health insurance, reinsurance and life insurance to a diverse group of clients. The company is distinguished by its extensive product and service offerings, broad distribution capabilities, exceptional financial strength, underwriting excellence, superior claims handling expertise and local operations globally.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
We offer a comprehensive solution for the large, small and middle market commercial customers’ insurance needs. We underwrite and sell wide range of property, casualty, professional liability and management liability lines of insurance, including Package, Workers Compensation, Auto, General Liability, Umbrella, Directors and Officers, Errors and Omissions for worldwide exposures. Our multi-line approach and segment expertise create a unique offering that our customers and agents/brokers appreciate. Our segments are built on an intimate knowledge of our customers’ industry, enabling us to provide the best products and services to meet their needs.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
We are seeking a Private Not for Profit (PNP) Underwriter for the Los Angeles, California branch office. The successful candidate must have a track record of success in Financial Lines. See below for additional detail on responsibilities.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>
Personal Book of Business:</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li><div>Financial performance, including profit, rate, retention, and growth, of a personal book of business consisting of D&amp;O, EPL, Cyber, MPL, Crime, K/R and Fiduciary lines ranging from $3M - $5M GWP</div></li>
<li>
<div>Successful personal market penetration and agency management including building, maintaining and managing producer and customer relationships</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Developing personal agency strategy and goals with continual monitoring of progress</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Identifying cross sell opportunities within commercial products and services on personal book of business.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Soliciting, selecting and analyzing risk within PNP guidelines and ensuring proper documentation</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Developing and negotiating price, coverage, and terms and conditions for all new business and renewals and actively identifying account rounding opportunities with a goal of writing $1M+ in new business annually</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Meeting with producers and renewal customers to make sales presentations including product education and new product roll out</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Collaborating with underwriters, operations, claims, marketing and home office product management</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Consistently meeting service standards</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Collecting and sharing industry intelligence with team, including industry trending and development</div>
</li>
</ul>
