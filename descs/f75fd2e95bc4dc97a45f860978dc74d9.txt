<h2>Compliance & Reporting Analyst – Marketing</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Enterprise Holdings has an exciting opportunity for a Compliance &amp; Reporting Analyst. The Compliance &amp; Reporting Analyst leverages data to create, deliver and analyze reports for loyalty programs, compliance projects and digital marketing programs. This includes gathering, validating, investigating, analyzing and</div>
<div>
interpreting both high-level and detail-level data from a variety of sources to develop</div>
<div>
reports and tools that effectively display results and provide actionable information to ensure programs are operating effectively and efficiently and to answer ad-hoc management inquiries. Additionally, this position will coordinate with Marketing Accounting to ensure information is complete and accurate.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Enterprise Holdings is the largest car rental provider in the world as measured by revenue and fleet. The company and its affiliate Enterprise Fleet Management – which combined offer a total transportation solution that includes extensive car rental and car-sharing services, truck rental, corporate fleet management and retail car sales – accounted for $24.1 billion in revenue and operated 2 million vehicles throughout the world in 2018. Enterprise Holdings’ annual revenues also place it near the top of the global travel industry, exceeding all other rental car companies, many airlines, and most cruise lines, hotels, tour operators and online travel agencies. Enterprise Holdings’ regional subsidiaries and Enterprise Fleet Management currently employ more than 100,000 people worldwide.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Through its integrated global network of independent regional subsidiaries and franchises, Enterprise Holdings operates the Enterprise Rent-A-Car, National Car Rental and Alamo Rent A Car brands at more than 10,000 fully staffed neighborhood and airport locations. The Enterprise Holdings global network operates in more than 90 countries and territories, including North America, Central America, South America, the Caribbean and Europe, as well as parts of Asia-Pacific and the Middle East. Today, the company’s three brands serve more than 95 percent of the worldwide car rental market.</div>
<br>
<b>
Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Assist with analyzing data and developing tools and reports to enhance monitoring of loyalty programs to ensure they are operating as designed</li>
<li>
Develop and maintain standard reports for loyalty accounting; ensure data integrity for digital marketing programs reporting</li>
<li>
Automate reports and streamline processes as needed</li>
<li>
Determine data requirements and identify data available</li>
<li>
Collaborate with others to develop and support reporting needs (ad-hoc or routine) using a variety of reporting tools and techniques, including Excel, Access, and SQL; recommend alternatives as necessary</li>
<li>
Develop in-depth understanding of the source and content of transactions and consumer databases (Teradata, ECARS, Odyssey, etc.) in order to mine these sources effectively and efficiently</li>
<li>
Leverage expertise to identify data available in order to implement controls to mitigate risks</li>
<li>
Assist Business Management with risk/compliance model system user roles and functions, standards, procedures and business practices</li>
</ul>
