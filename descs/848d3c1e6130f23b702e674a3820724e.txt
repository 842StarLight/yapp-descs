<h2>Bilingual QA Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Quality Assurance Representative is a seasonal position that monitors the sales and enrollment activities of our marketing staff for compliance with legal and regulatory requirements, carrier-specific requirements and adherence to approved scripts by performing the following duties.</p>
<br>
<p><br>
<b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul><li>Navigate computer systems to efficiently locate specific customer data including call recordings, enrollment applications and scorecards.</li></ul>
<p></p>
<ul><li>Search various approved websites to confirm the accuracy of data being presented to the customer.</li></ul>
<p></p>
<ul><li>Maintain a full, functional understanding of the products and the compliance requirements of the plans you are assigned to audit.</li></ul>
<p></p>
<ul><li>Record/report findings and provide written factual explanations defining your conclusions.</li></ul>
<p></p>
<ul><li>Conduct sales/enrollment audits in response to allegations generated by both internal and external sources.</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Core Competencies</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul><li>Written Communication - Writes clearly and informatively; Edits work for spelling and grammar; Varies writing style to meet needs; Presents numerical data effectively; Able to read and interpret written information.</li></ul>
<p></p>
<ul><li>Ethics - Treats people with respect; Keeps commitments; Inspires the trust of others; Works with integrity and ethically; Upholds organizational values.</li></ul>
<p></p>
<ul><li>Organizational Support - Follows policies and procedures; Completes administrative tasks correctly and on time; Supports organization's goals and values.</li></ul>
<p></p>
<ul><li>Judgment - Displays willingness to make decisions; Exhibits sound and accurate judgment; Supports and explains reasoning for decisions; Includes appropriate people in decision-making process; Makes timely decisions.</li></ul>
<p></p>
<ul><li>Quality - Demonstrates accuracy and thoroughness; Looks for ways to improve and promote quality; Applies feedback to improve performance; Monitors own work to ensure quality.</li></ul>
<p></p>
<ul><li>Quantity - Meets productivity standards; Completes work in timely manner; Strives to increase productivity; Works quickly.</li></ul>
<p></p>
<ul><li>Adaptability - Adapts to changes in the work environment; Manages competing demands; Changes approach or method to best fit the situation; Able to deal with frequent change, delays, or unexpected events.</li></ul>
<p></p>
<ul><li>Attendance/Punctuality - Is consistently at work and on time; Arrives at meetings and appointments on time.</li></ul>
<p></p>
<ul><li>Dependability - Follows instructions, responds to management direction; Takes responsibility for own actions; Keeps commitments; Commits to long hours of work when necessary to reach goals. Completes tasks on time or notifies appropriate person with an alternate plan.</li></ul>

Ability to effectively present information in one-on-one and small group situations to customers, clients, and other employees of the organization.</li></ul>
<p></p>
<ul><li>Ability to add, subtract, multiply, and divide in all units of measure, using whole numbers, common fractions, and decimals.</li></ul>
<p></p>
