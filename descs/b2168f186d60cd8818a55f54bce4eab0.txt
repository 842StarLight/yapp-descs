 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Field Services Manager-Unit Franchise Development

For over 25 years, JAN-PRO has been providing customers with world-class commercial cleaning services through a network of dedicated, well-trained franchise business owners. Independent experts have consistently recognized JAN-PRO as a top choice for prospective franchisees, including recent rankings by an internationally renowned Entrepreneur Magazine as the #1 Fastest Growing Franchise and the #1 Commercial Cleaning Franchise.
<br><br>
With over 10,000 franchisees in more than 16 countries, JAN-PRO has successfully established territories throughout the world and is currently expanding in Central Florida. The ideal candidate will be responsible for customer account management and customer retention throughout their territory in Central Florida.
<br><br>
<b>POSITION SCOPE:</b><br>
The Field Services Manager collaborates with the Sales Team and our Unit Franchise Owner Operators to attain customer satisfaction for businesses that we contract with to perform their nightly janitorial services. We provide services to a variety of businesses, including banks, car dealerships, churches, department stores, hotels, medical facilities, office buildings, restaurants, and schools, among others. This role is expected to meet/exceed account retention goals, perform proactive monthly quality inspections and present proposed solutions for special service needs for our customers such as hard floor care, pressure washing, window cleaning, and carpet care. This is a full-time salaried position.
<br><br>
This role reports directly to company President/Owner.
