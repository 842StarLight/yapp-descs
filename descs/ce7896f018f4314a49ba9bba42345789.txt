<p><b>Job Title
</b></p>Associate Marketing Manager, GTM
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Description
</b></p>
<p><b>I. Job Summary </b>| <i>Major goals and objectives.
</i></p>
<p>This role is responsible for the development of strategy and materials for Meredith’s Corporate and Digital Marketing teams. The Associate Marketing Manager works across the company to compile information from internal stakeholders (data, insights, editorial, research, content, innovation, etc.) to then develop compelling go-to-market, category and senior client and agency presentations/materials that showcase Meredith’s strategic capabilities and offerings. The Associate Marketing Manager must have the ability to manage several projects at once and work in a fast-paced environment.
</p>
