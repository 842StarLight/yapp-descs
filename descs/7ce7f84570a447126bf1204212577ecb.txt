<h2>Sr. Copywriter - PHARMA</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>CLUTCH is looking for a Pharma Copywriter for a top agency in the Greater New York City area, to fill a position with an emphasis on candidates with previous experience in HCP and Pharma. The Copywriter must be team-oriented, able to write solid, brand-appropriate, and creative pieces in a variety of basic communication forms (sales aids, resource guides) for a variety of audiences (healthcare professionals, patients, consumers).</p>
<br>
<p><br>
What you can expect:</p>
<ul>
<li>Will need to develop multiple concepts/campaigns for assigned projects in partnership with other internal teams.</li>
<li>Coordinates with Account Services and Art Team in identifying and obtaining information needed to carry out assignments.</li>
<li>Will write copy for assigned products/projects and develop language that is original, compelling, proprietary, and synergistic with art/graphic elements.</li>
<li>Will prepare creative platforms/rationale for promotional concepts</li>
<li>Explores/develops/produces innovative ideas appropriate to the Client’s marketing strategy.</li>
<li>Presents creative work and properly prepares for all Client presentations.</li>
<li>Makes sound, timely decisions about changes to work in progress.</li>
<li>Organizes all creative materials to ensure their smooth transition to other departments.</li>
<li>Produce insightful and unique creative work that reflects the brand strategy.</li>
<li>Good knowledge of the assigned brands, indications, therapeutic implications, and competition as well as regularly research trends in the appropriate areas.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>What we expect:</p>
<ul>
<li>HCP experience</li>
<li>Great collaboration skills</li>
<li>Comfortable presenting to clients and internal account teams</li>
<li>Someone who is "science minded" is a plus</li>
<li>Confidence in pitching ideas and speaking up with suggestions and solutions to existing issues</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 5 years of experience in copywriting, specifically in healthcare advertising/marketing or medical writing.</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree or equivalent in Communications, Writing, English, Journalism, Advertising, Marketing, or related field.</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Word.</li>
<li>Professional attitude</li>
<li>Must be team-oriented.</li>
<li>Good organizational and superior attention to details.</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal and communication skills, both oral and written.</li>
<li>Must be a self-starting, independent and innovative individual capable of looking at old problems in new ways.</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
If interested in this role, please submit your resume today for immediate consideration!</p>
