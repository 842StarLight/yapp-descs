<h2>National Accounts Admin</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Job Overview</p>
<p>Various assigned office duties based on company needs. Frontline contact to all constituents including hiring managers, client HR and Talent partners, agency partners and contingent workers. Ability to determine a need versus issue, provide resolution or escalate to the appropriate member of management. Maintains accurate records for company, audit, and reconciling purposes.</p>
<p>Responsibilities and Duties</p>
<ul>
<li>Provide immediate front-line customer service to field management team, suppliers and clients, maintains thorough and accurate documentation on client and resource interactions.</li>
<li>Maintains and updates electronic records to include changes or status updates, accurate compliance fields and validates entries to ensure accuracy for company and audit purposes.</li>
<li>Responds, documents and answers inbound calls and emails in a timely manner and according to LMS utilizing systems, policies, processes and</li>
<li>Handles pre-employment background checks and non-VMS job orders</li>
<li>Maintains and continually increases knowledge of supported software solutions</li>
<li>Handles Reconciliation of Accounts Receivable and Accounts Payable for National Accounts</li>
<li>Participates in special projects and performs other duties as assigned by the National Sales Manager or National Sales</li>
</ul>
