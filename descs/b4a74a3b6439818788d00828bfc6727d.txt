<h2>Jr. Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>In this role, you will learn key strategies to build and manage meaningful client relationships. You will have the opportunity to work closely with various departments within the organization and gain a better knowledge of the financial industry.</p>
<p>The Company: American Financial Education Alliance (AFEA) is one of the fastest growing national 501(c)(3) non-profit financial wellness programs in the country. Based in Charlotte, North Carolina, AFEA offers its employees an excellent learning environment with great career advancement opportunities. Our mission is to empower Americans to take control of their finances by providing financial education in communities nationwide. We accomplish our mission by recruiting qualified financial advisers to teach our courses in local libraries, colleges, and universities. Although AFEA’s internal team consists of ~12 employees, we currently service over 180 members nationwide.</p>
<p>Who/What We Are Looking For:</p>
<ul>
<li>A self-motivated recent graduate interested in joining our team as a full-time Account Executive</li>
<li>Natural ability to stay organized, give attention to detail, and plan ahead</li>
<li>Someone with excellent communication skills, especially by phone and email</li>
<li>An energetic teammate who can handle a fast paced, high volume environment</li>
<li>Someone who is confident in their ability to handle various personality types</li>
<li>A positive sense of humor</li>
</ul>
<p>Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Serve as the main point of contact for our members</li>
<li>Creating detailed business plans to facilitate the attainment of goals and quotas</li>
<li>Provide professional after-sales support to enhance the customers’ dedication</li>
<li>Remain in frequent contact with the clients in your responsibility to understand their needs</li>
<li>Respond to complaints and resolve issues aiming to customer contentment and the preservation of the company’s reputation</li>
<li>Negotiate agreements and keep records of sales and data</li>
<li>Ensuring that clients pay on time - Reporting client agendas to other members of staff</li>
<li>Maintaining client relationships through regular check-ins and follow-ups</li>
<li>Using a Customer Relationship Management Software (CRM) to document and capture all related member activity in a timely and accurate manner.</li>
<li>Use problem solving skills to handle irregular issues and requests</li>
<li>Work with clients to coordinate event space rentals within the designated timeline</li>
<li>Support and back-up other team members as needed</li>
<li>General administrative duties</li>
<li>Other departmental duties as assigned by manager</li>
</ul>
<p>Requirements:</p>
<ul>
<li>Proven experience as an Account Executive, or in other sales/customer service role</li>
<li>Outstanding knowledge of MS Office; knowledge of CRM software (eg. Salesforce) is a plus</li>
<li>Excellent communication/presentation skills and ability to build relationships</li>
<li>Organizational and time-management skills</li>
<li>Enthusiastic and passionate</li>
<li>BS or BA in business administration, sales or marketing</li>
</ul>
<p>*<br>Job Type: Full-time</p>
