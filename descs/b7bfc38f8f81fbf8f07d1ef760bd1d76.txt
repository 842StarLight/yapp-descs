<h2>Stock Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>THE J.CREW SUPPORT ASSOCIATE<br>
[Hourly Full-Time, reports to a store director]</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
What The Role is All About</p>
<p>
Our Support Associates:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Drive store sales through accurate and efficient execution of all receiving, stock, visual and cashwrap activities.</li>
<li>
Respond to customers in the moment partnering with the team to deliver amazing service.</li>
<li>
Provide an engaging and efficient checkout experience.</li>
<li>
Use visual tools and resources to maintain and enhance in-store presentation.</li>
<li>
Utilize knowledge and skills garnered from requisite training.</li>
<li>
Are multi-taskers, jumping into any activity as needed to support the business.</li>
<li>
Share feedback, ideas and insights with the management team.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
What We Expect From You</p>
<ul>
<li>
Friendly, helpful and always smiling, for starters.</li>
<li>
Customer focused.</li>
<li>
Be the best at what you do by achieving all your goals.</li>
<li>
Bring your smarts; we want thinkers who always do the right thing.</li>
<li>
Pride in your appearance and your store.</li>
<li>
Technologically savvy while at the same time knowing that devices don’t dominate dialogue.</li>
<li>
An ability to be nimble and get on board with where we’re headed.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
How We Measure Results</p>
<ul>
<li>
Comply with merchandise handling guidelines and processes.</li>
<li>
Execute merchandising and visual direction to standard.</li>
<li>
Achieve Enterprise Selling targets by adhering to Standard Operating Procedures.</li>
<li>
Achieve or exceed task-specific time standards.</li>
<li>
Complete training with flying colors.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Fine Print</p>
<p>
Our Support Associates:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Are at least 18 years old.</li>
<li>
Communicate effectively and confidently.</li>
<li>
Process information and operate store systems accurately.</li>
<li>
Are available when we’re busy, including: nights, weekends, and holidays.</li>
<li>
Assist in processing and replenishing and keeping the store looking great.</li>
<li>
Can bend, reach, stretch as well as lift, carry and move at least 40 pounds |18 kilos.</li>
<li>
Can move around the store with ease and be accessible to customers.</li>
</ul>
