<h2>Account Executive - Louisville, KY</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>What makes Gartner Sales a GREAT fit for you? When you join Gartner, you’ll set your career on track for outstanding achievement with a company that knows no limits. We’re the world’s leading research and advisory company, achieving consistent double-digit growth by steering clients toward the right decisions with business and technology insights they can’t find anywhere else. Our sales associates earn a competitive base salary, uncapped commissions and exceptional benefits — along with top training and support, and all-expenses-paid luxury incentive trips for high performers. If you’re coachable, persistent, smart, executive-savvy, and looking for your next great adventure, Gartner is the place for you.<p></p>
<p><b>
This role is responsible for:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Consult with C-level executives to develop and implement an effective, enterprise-wide strategy that maximizes the value delivered by Gartner's products and services</li>
<li>
Account management with outcome of increased customer satisfaction and increase in retention &amp; account growth</li>
<li>
Quota responsibility aligned with specific accounts</li>
<li>
Mastery and consistent execution of Gartner's internal sales</li>
<li>
Proficient in account planning and understanding of territory management</li>
<li>
Manage forecast accuracy on a monthly/quarterly/annual basis</li>
<li>
Maintain competitive knowledge &amp; focus</li>
<li>
Fiscal responsibility with regards to expense management</li>
<li>
In-depth knowledge of Gartner's products and services</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
We're looking for:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
6+ years external experience with proven consultative sales, preferably experience in high technology (services, software or hardware), with evidence of prior success in Sales.</li>
<li>
Ability to prospect and manage C-level and senior level relationships within large multi-national organizations</li>
<li>
Strong demonstration of intellect, drive, executive presence, sales acumen</li>
<li>
Proven experience building excellent client relationships, offering benefited, insightful and strategic insight into their business</li>
<li>
Strong computer proficiency</li>
<li>
Excellent written and oral/presentation skills</li>
<li>
Ability to develop and conduct effective presentations with contract decision makers (c-level)</li>
<li>
Knowledge of the full life cycle of the sales process from prospecting to close</li>
<li>
Language requirements as determined by territory needs</li>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree, with strong evidence of success in school</li>
<li>
Masters or advanced degree a plus</li>
</ul>
