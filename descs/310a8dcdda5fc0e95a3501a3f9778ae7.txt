<h2>Senior Compliance Program Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What you'll be doing...
</b></h3>
<p></p>
<p>The Senior Compliance Program Manager, Data Privacy and Risk Management supports the company’s compliance program by defining and guiding the implementation and operation of the Security team’s Data Privacy and Risk Management programs to support continuous improvement as well as future compliance initiatives. This role is responsible for partnering with key business areas to translate regulatory compliance requirements into actionable business requirements.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Some of the things you’ll be doing include….
</p>
<ul>
<li>Act as subject matter expert for internal teams related to data privacy regulations such as GDPR, Privacy Shield, and CCPA.
</li>
<li>Develop and run a formal data privacy program to ensure regulatory requirements are included in Tableau’s Privacy by Design methodology to building our products.
</li>
<li>Develop and run a formal risk management program that centrally tracks data privacy and security risks and facilitates actionable steps to address identified risks.
</li>
<li>Evaluate and assist the business with implementing controls that mitigate security and data privacy risks.
</li>
<li>Track, measure, and report on the status of risk mitigation efforts.
</li>
<li>Maintain a current understanding of relevant security and data privacy compliance standards and regulations.
</li>
<li>Monitor the industry landscape to keep visibility on evolutions, trends, and best practices related to data privacy and risk management and integrate improvements into existing programs.
</li>
<li>Evaluate regulatory controls to identify opportunities to mitigate risk, strengthen controls, and reduce overhead.
</li>
<li>Assist in the testing of regulatory controls and processes to verify expected operation.
</li>
<li>Drive remediation efforts for deficiencies and develop and implement compensating controls.
</li>
<li>Prepare documentation related to remediation actions and testing outcomes utilizing content collaboration software.
</li>
<li>Track and report on security compliance activities utilizing process and project management software.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Who you are…
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>

<li>Maintaining continuous certification and compliance with relevant industry regulatory standards including GDPR, Privacy Shield, SOX, SOC 2, ISO 27001, NIST, and FedRAMP.

</li>
</ul>
</li>

<b>Go-Getter. </b>Flexible, dependable and self-motivated.

</ul>
