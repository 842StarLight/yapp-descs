<div><b>
Job Overview:</b></div>
<div>
The Underwriting Technician (UT) contributes to the success of the underwriting organization by partnering with Underwriters and Policy Service Representatives to drive growth, improve profitability, retain accounts and service producers.<br>
</div>
<div>The UT is responsible for providing Underwriters with information necessary to make prudent underwriting decisions and to support the underwriting lifecycle from pre-submission through policy issuance and renewal (marketing, underwriting analysis, sales, service and administrative).</div>
<div>
</div>
<div>
Acts as an integral member of the Underwriting team by providing support within established performance standards and priorities. Ensures all related activities are delivered in a manner consistent with customer service focus, quality and timelines.</div>
<div>
</div>
<div><b>
Responsibilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Partners with Underwriters to establish support needs for new and renewal policies (i.e., identify and gather relevant account information to quote or issue policies)</li>
<li>
Analyzes account specific information and summarizes information and findings for Underwriter review (i.e. loss information, description of operations, financials, risk control reports)</li>
<li>
Researches and provides underwriting analysis of account information and ensures overall regulatory compliance</li>
<li>
Works as a liaison between the field and processing centers to ensure timely account set-up, clearing, rating, issuance and renewal prep activities</li>
<li>
 Coordinates account processing and servicing</li>
<li>
Participates in pre-renewal meetings to build renewal schedule of key activities (missing information, updated applications, etc)</li>
<li>
Prepares underwriting/pricing exhibits (updated exposures, rating and pricing worksheets, updated account information)</li>
<li>
Executes pricing strategy on behalf of and in conjunction with the underwriter</li>
<li>
Manages account documentation and ensures file completeness and quality (correspondence, proposals, agreement letters, endorsements, cancellations, etc.)</li>
<li>
Communicates with external producers/customers and serves as the first-line point of contact on account related matters (Responds to questions and resolves issues in a timely manner)</li>
<li>
Provides administrative support and participates in ad-hoc activities as needed</li>
</ul>
