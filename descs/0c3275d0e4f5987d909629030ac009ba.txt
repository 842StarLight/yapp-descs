<h2>Clinical Program Consultant (HBS)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Description:</b><br>
Excellent opportunity for an experienced clinician with a passion for consulting and improving overall population health to leverage clinical, analytic and consultative skills to join our team of Clinical Program Consultants (CPC) who offer clinical insights and solutions to help improve health plan performance for our group commercial plan sponsors. This is a clinical transformation-type role to support clients with ACO-like needs; experience with joint ventures, provider collaboration, or Accountable Care organizations required. Highly collaborative, individual contributor position in a professional environment working with the account team, customer and ACO, Aetna's clinical operation teams and our Plan Sponsor Insights team of consultants. The CPC will support one of our most important customers - a large health system in Southeast Texas with a focus on helping ensure patients receive the right care at the right time and advancing health - working directly with the benefits team, onsite, at their location in Houston using analytics to identify opportunities for improvement.<br>
<br>
Clinical Program Consultants are key members of the Plan Sponsor Insights (PSI) business - a business focused on providing insights and solutions based on analytics to customers in the group commercial business. The work environment supports and thrives on out-of-the box thinking, creative talent and a consultative approach. Our team is highly valued by our internal partners as well as the customers we serve. 61708<br>
<br>
<b>Fundamental Components:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Provide clinical consultation based on analysis and identification of key<br>
drivers of results e.g. use data to identify clinical trends, evaluate performance results, monitor engagement and collaborate on opportunities for improvement</li>
<li>
Recommend clinical programs and services that align with the customer's<br>
strategy and help drive short and long term results e.g. assisting with development of health strategies, tactics, policies and programs (which could include network management, clinical coverage, care management, and compliance policies for data and information) that improve overall population health and encourage efficient and effective delivery of benefits.</li>
<li>
Development and presentation of clinical content and recommendations in<br>
customer facing meetings e.g. present findings and recommended actions to help improve performance and clinical outcomes, and work collaboratively to help track and resolve issues</li>
<li>
Act as a trusted clinical resource providing clinical program guidance</li>
<li>
Collaborate across the enterprise and with clinical operations and ACO to optimize collaboration, efficiency, and impact</li>
<li>
Provide input to the design of clinical solutions and workflows leveraging knowledge of workflow, policy, and procedures to help ensure teams can work successfully</li>
<li>
Stay current on, and share knowledge of, population health trends</li>
</ul>
