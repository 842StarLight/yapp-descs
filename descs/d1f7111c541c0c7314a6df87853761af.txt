<h2>SVP, Cloud Transformation</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>SUMMARY</b><b>:</b></p>
<p>
The VP/Senior Vice President of Cloud Transformation will serve as one of the primary public spokespersons for Intelisys in various public-facing capacities. This individual will be recognized as a thought leader around all things cloud and other emerging technologies. This individual will also be the executive in charge of the company’s education platforms, including Intelisys Cloud Services University and the Super9 program, and will own responsibility for the strategy and development of both the University and education plan. The VP/SVP of Cloud Transformation will be responsible for leading and managing the education (iCSU) team and collaborating with other stakeholders and resources in Sales and Marketing to expand those platforms. It is expected this individual will create new opportunities to grow the Intelisys brand and ecosystem through a passionate commitment to education and thought leadership, all of which will result in sales growth and increased profitability.</p>
<p>
This individual will collaborate with the Director of Marketing, VP of Marketing and other key executives and stakeholders on the future direction of Intelisys’ education, sales and marketing strategies. The right individual will be a strategic thinker and an innovator, but also a strong doer that can produce meaningful results.</p>
<p><b>
RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<p><b>
Thought Leadership</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Observing, analyzing and reporting on industry and technology trends.</li>
<li>
Must be an ongoing student of new technologies and emerging suppliers, and provide relevant interpretation to both internal and external audiences.</li>
<li>
Will serve as one of the primary evaluators of prospective suppliers to determine the viability and success probability if added to the Intelisys portfolio.</li>
<li>
Serve in advisory roles to existing suppliers as they continue to evolve their cloud strategies.</li>
<li>
Attend and serve on supplier advisory boards as requested.</li>
<li>
Analyzing key statistical data to derive critical observations relevant to the go-forward strategy of Intelisys.</li>
<li>
Advise senior leadership team on emerging trends, threats and opportunities.</li>
<li>
Will be looked to as lead “technologist” within Intelisys.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Education</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Lead, motivate and manage the iCSU team by creating the vision and goals that align with company objectives and values.</li>
<li>
Oversee the iCSU Program Manager and ensure that all certification tracks, education strategies, and lead generation and reporting duties are executed on time and according to company strategy.</li>
<li>
Perform annual and quarterly goals and evaluations, manage daily and weekly workflow for the department, and ensure it aligns with the ICSU and overall company vision.</li>
<li>
Collaborate closely with VP of Marketing to ensure education initiatives are in tight alignment with Marketing’s objectives and capabilities.</li>
<li>
Collaborate closely with the Manager of Video and the entire Video team in the creation and execution of meaningful and impactful video content as part of the larger education initiative.</li>
<li>
Determine the long-term strategy and ongoing evolution of Cloud Services University.</li>
<li>
Create innovative initiatives that keep the education strategy fresh, and industry leading.</li>
<li>
Increase Intelisys’ industry leadership position around education through innovation, leadership and strong execution.</li>
<li>
Obtain feedback from Sales Partners and other stakeholders on all programs and incorporate feedback into new initiatives and capabilities</li>
<li>
Continue and expand the Intelisys Super9 brand, leading Super9 sessions and creating new and innovative Super9 training initiatives, including development and delivery of new event curricula.</li>
<li>
Lead the iCSU team on maintenance of current certifications, establishing a schedule for content review, revision and delivery.</li>
<li>
Working with the iCSU and Marketing teams, conceptualize, develop, record and promote new iCSU certifications.</li>
<li>
Formalize an education strategy, in collaboration with the VP of Marketing and other senior leaders.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Supplier Relations &amp; Fundraising</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Serve as lead executive sponsor for all Cloud supplier relationships.</li>
<li>
Build relationships with key Cloud supplier executives in order to secure long term support.</li>
<li>
Create compelling value propositions for suppliers to fund our events and education strategy.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with Cloud suppliers on events and education initiatives that will result in increased sales partner adoption and revenue growth.</li>
<li>
Exceed annual fundraising targets while maintaining meaningful return on investment for participating supplier partners.</li>
<li>
Collaborate closely with Intelisys finance, marketing, sales and supplier management teams to achieve targets and execute strategies.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Public Speaking</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Collaborate closely with VP of Marketing and President, on key messages to be delivered at partner, industry and customer events as well as in front of internal audiences.</li>
<li>
Design and deliver speeches to small and large audiences that are engaging, challenging, thought provoking and inspire meaningful and desired actions.</li>
<li>
Strengthen our public brand and credibility by delivering industry leading speeches.</li>
<li>
Create cutting edge digital presentation content in support of public speaking engagements.</li>
<li>
Create and deliver impactful video content in tight collaboration with our video team.</li>
<li>
Design and execute effective webinars and digital broadcasts that produce measurable results.</li>
</ul>

Willingness to travel extensively.</li>
