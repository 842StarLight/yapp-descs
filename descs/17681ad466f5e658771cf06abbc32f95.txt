<h2>Heavy Equipment Design Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div><div><div><div><div><div><div><div><div><div><div><div><div>
<p>Southwest Products, located in Surprise, Arizona, is a leading manufacturer of quality service vehicles, commercial power generation equipment, and leading distributor of high-performance diesel and gas engines for many applications and industries. We are looking for a Heavy Equipment Design Engineer with hydraulic experience to help support our Vehicle team.<br>
Southwest Products offers a competitive total compensation plan which includes a comprehensive benefit package consisting of medical, dental, and vision insurance, flexible spending account participation, Paid Time Off (“PTO”), as well as company-paid holidays, and a generous 401K match program.</p>
<p><i>
This high profile position is responsible for providing Engineering Designs for Customer Driven Products and New Product Development.</i></p>
<p><i>
Primary Duties include :</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Design of mechanical systems with a variety of materials and hardware systems.</li>
<li>Design of custom sheet metal and structural design</li>
<li>Design of hydraulic circuits and pumped fluid systems</li>
<li>Comprehends complex engineering projects involving multiple functions.</li>
<li>Design of systems using modular methodology</li>
<li>Ensures effective and efficient system design for functionality and reliability.</li>
<li>Participates in the refinement of engineering systems and processes.</li>
<li>Communicates between departments: operations, purchasing, sales and suppliers.</li>
<li>Manages resources for desktop computer design work</li>
<li>Provides Engineering support to the shop floor for production, testing and operation.</li>
<li>Reviews, edits, and prepares documentation for projects in paper and electronic forms.</li>
</ul>
<p>What we need from you:</p>
<ul>
<li>High proficiency with Computer programs: Microsoft Word, Excel, Outlook, and Truck Body Builder References</li>
<li>Proficiency with SolidWorks or similar 3D modeling software.</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills, positive attitude, works with others</li>
<li>Make critical decisions with minimal supervision and information</li>
<li>Knowledgeable with Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standards</li>
<li>Five years experience in a mechanical design</li>
<li>BSME or Similar</li>
<li>Minimum five years experience in vehicle or heavy equipment Design and Operation</li>
<li>2+ years experience in hydraulic or fluid system design.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Working Conditions, Travel/Schedule expectations and Physical Demands:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<div>Traditional work schedule (Monday to Friday) to include occasional evenings and weekends to meet certain business demands</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Office setting with exposure to shop environment and equipment operations</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Safety standards and accommodations to enable a safe working environment both inside and outside of the office</div>
</li>
</ul>
