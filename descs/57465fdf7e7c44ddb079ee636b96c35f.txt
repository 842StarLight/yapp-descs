<h2>Product Manager, Integrations</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Datto, the world's leading provider of IT solutions delivered through managed service providers, is looking for a Product Manager to join a growing team. Datto is a creative company at its core and is an exciting and dynamic workplace. We're 100% focused on our managed service provider partners and believe that with the right technology, managed service providers can change how businesses around the world operate. Datto provides data protection, business continuity, networking, business management, and file backup and sync products that empower and protect the clients of our 14,000+ partners. We're headquartered in Norwalk, Connecticut and have 22 offices worldwide.
<br><br>
You will report to VP Product Management.
<br><br>
<b>Does This Describe You:</b><br>
You're a problem solver.
<br><br>
<b>A Look Inside The Job:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Own products or systems through entire product lifecycle -- from the initial proposal, production, marketing, and sales, through product retirement</li>
<li>Work with customers and internal stakeholders to understand what functionality and features they need/want, and use that input to define the feature roadmap</li>
<li>Keep stakeholders involved and informed throughout the planning, development, testing, and deployment lifecycle</li>
<li>Manage stakeholder and customer escalations when needed</li>
<li>Using product research input, create detailed business and technical requirements, working alongside engineering to design, develop, and bring concepts to market</li>
<li>Contribute to the strategic planning process and ensure continuing alignment of the product strategy with the overall company strategy</li>
<li>Collaborate with product marketing and sales on go-to-market strategy and execution</li>
<li>Communicate all product releases, additions, and updates with customers, partners, and internal stakeholders, sharing uses cases and benefits of new functionality</li>
<li>Establish success criteria for product usage and perform ongoing measurement and monitoring, using the input to inform roadmap and designs</li>
</ul>
<b>About You:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>2+ years of software product management experience</li>
<li>A track record managing a successful product throughout its lifecycle</li>
<li>A solid Product Owner background, with experience working with a team of technical experts in an agile environment</li>
<li>A technical background with an understanding and/or hands-on experience in software development and web technologies</li>
<li>Amazing skills communicating with peers and managers</li>
<li>Excellent analytical, collaboration, and written skills</li>
</ul>
