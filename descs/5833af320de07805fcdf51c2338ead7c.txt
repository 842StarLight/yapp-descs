<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Description<br>
</b></h2>
<p></p>
<div><div><div>
<p>Wheatland Tube, a division of Zekelman Industries, Inc., seeks an <b>Inside Sales Representative</b> based at our location in Sharon PA. Reporting to the Inside Sales Manager, the Inside Sales Rep will work with customers and our internal sales team, handling all aspects of inside sales from inquiry/quote to placement of order through shipment to customer.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
NATURE AND SCOPE:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Enter orders and make any changes to order for entry on mills, including requesting new part numbers</li>
<li>
Prepare and process quotes</li>
<li>
Contact customers to obtain orders for upcoming rollings</li>
<li>
Contact potential sales leads</li>
<li>
Expedite orders and advise customer of product availability or alternative products and delivery schedules</li>
<li>
Work with the product manager and outside sales team as necessary in fulfilling customer requirements as well as providing market and competitive intelligence from customers</li>
<li>
Work with shipping and traffic departments by providing customer releases and information to schedule timely shipments</li>
<li>
Resolve problems that arise on orders and shipments</li>
<li>
Assists management with other special projects as required and provide back-up for other customer service representatives</li>
</ul>
