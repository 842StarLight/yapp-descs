<div><b>
General Accountabilities</b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Holds full understanding of services and specials Island Spa offers</div></li>
<li><div>
Sell products and services offered and improve sales methods to help workflow</div></li>
<li><div>
Greets and directs guests to therapy areas.</div></li>
<li><div>
Occasional walk throughs of the facility during down time</div></li>
<li><div>
Schedules, cancels and confirms reservations for services.</div></li>
<li><div>
Makes self available for any guest need while in the therapy/service areas</div></li>
<li><div>
Upholds and enforces therapy/service area rules and regulations.</div></li>
<li><div>
Locate guests that are checked in but have not arrived for their appointment</div></li>
<li><div>
Accurately keeps track of services and records them on a Turning Sheet to ensure fair distribution for therapists</div></li>
<li><div>
Calls guests that are not checked in half an hour before their appointment</div></li>
<li><div>
Keeps records of occupied and vacant therapist rooms and guests' accounts.</div></li>
<li><div>
Accurately records guest number for payment at end of stay.</div></li>
<li><div>
Answers inquiries pertaining to spa services and accommodations of spa.</div></li>
<li><div>
Handles guest complaints or concerns, seeks help from Floor Manager if needed.</div></li>
<li><div>
Responds to inquiries and requests for information requiring knowledge of departmental, company policies, procedures and services</div></li>
<li><div>
Records services and accurately puts them into the POS</div></li>
<li><div>
Update Booker with assigned therapist</div></li>
<li><div>
Mark Turning Sheet for completion after assigning a guest to a room and charging them in the POS for their service</div></li>
<li><div>
Keeps all work areas up to Island Spa Standards of hygiene.</div></li>
<li><div>
Performs other duties as assigned or requested by Floor Manager or General Manager.</div></li>
</ul>
