<h2>Territory Sales Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Position Overview</b><b>:
</b></p>
<p>The Territory Sales Manager will be responsible for selling business product lines / systems and generating new growth and profitability within a specified geographical territory or specific assigned markets and meeting the required sales plan. They will also be responsible for leading and developing sales representatives in the Territory.</p>
<p>
This person can be based in in the 5 Boroughs, Long Island or Hudson Valley area.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Support the business in both maintenance of existing accounts and generation of new growth accounts in specified sales territory/markets</li>
<li>Lead and Develop a team of Sales Reps in specific Territory.</li>
<li>Develop and drive a sales strategy for a particular territory/market; conduct market research analysis</li>
<li>Develop sales forecasts by product line, markets and major customers.</li>
<li>Implement marketing programs to achieve sales and market share objectives</li>
<li>Monitor and make recommendations concerning market needs, changes in competitive policies and strategies, channels of distribution, advertising and promotion, pricing and sales activity; prepare and issue reports as required</li>
<li>Make direct customer calls on contractors/distributors/retailers to develop and increase sales through these channels; formulate job bids and negotiate contracts</li>
<li>Drive product/systems sales to support established price strategies and minimize price deviations</li>
<li>Assure legal compliance in the selling of all products/systems with applicable local, state and federal regulations</li>
<li>Manage customer needs through product development processes</li>
<li>Provide technical assistance and information to customers including project submittals and budget estimates</li>
<li>Respond to customer inquiries</li>
<li>Manage customer dissatisfaction and specific complaints fairly, promptly and effectively; recommend and if necessary develop and conduct training programs for customers</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Requirements</b><b>: (education, skills and abilities)</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree with a minimum of 5 years sales experience:</li>
<li>Product knowledge and product set-up process experience preferred</li>
<li>Knowledge of the building industry, including technical knowledge of products/systems</li>
<li>Sales ability to introduce new products/systems</li>
<li>Ability to read blue prints and architectural drawings</li>
<li>Knowledge of competitor product lines and price levels</li>
<li>Interpersonal, verbal and written communications skills.</li>
<li>Ability to build rapport and develop strong customer relationships</li>
<li>Ability to negotiate complex situations.</li>
<li>Problem resolution skills</li>
<li>Experience in third party (e.g. distributor) sales and working with architects and contractors to specify products</li>
<li>Ability to analyze and evaluate product lines, markets, and customers</li>
<li>Ability to analyze and evaluate marketing programs and sales strategies</li>
<li>Ability to estimate and monitor expenses for budgeting and territory/market operations</li>
<li>Ability to analyze customer credit information</li>
<li>Ability to work with minimal supervision.</li>
<li>Ability to drive a vehicle for long periods of time and carry a valid driver's license</li>
<li>Organization and time management skills</li>
<li>Ability to work in a team environment</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Work, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook</li>
<li>Heavy travel required (30 or more days per year)</li>
<li>May be required to lift, carry, push or pull up to and including 25 pounds</li>
<li>Primary work environment is typically in the field at a customer's facility or on a job site; may be exposed to environmental conditions of cold, heat, high noise levels, moving mechanical parts, heights, dust/mist/gas/fumes, extensive walking or climbing, etc.</li>
</ul>
