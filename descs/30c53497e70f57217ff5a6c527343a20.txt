<h2>Manager, Loyalty and CRM Marketing</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Do you know what it takes to develop and manage a successful loyalty program? Publix is looking for a dynamic, driven individual to lead our Loyalty &amp; CRM team.<br>
<br>
The Manager, Loyalty &amp; Customer Relationship Management (CRM) Marketing is responsible for contributing to the development of the overall Loyalty and Personalization strategy for Publix. This position develops and directs the Loyalty &amp; CRM Marketing function as well as provides strategic counsel to all levels of the Marketing department, business partner senior leadership, and outside suppliers.<br>
This position is also responsible for the hiring, training and retaining of a knowledgeable and motivated Loyalty &amp; CRM Marketing team to ensure we are providing premier service and support to customers and retail operations.<br>
The impact this position has on Publix is leading the strategic development, Marketing resource allocation and execution of Loyalty and Personalization programs intended to support retail business goals by driving increased customer loyalty which results in increased traffic, sales, market share and loyalty.<br>
