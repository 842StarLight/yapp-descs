<p><b>
Accountabilities</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
The responsibilities include (are not limited to):</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Reconcile processed work by verifying entries and comparing system reports to balances</li>
<li>Code operational invoices to G/L accounts and cost centers by analyzing invoice/expense reports; recording entries and posting month end accruals</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Resolving purchase order, contract, invoice, or payment discrepancies and documentation</li>
<li>Record and disburse T&amp;E reports, reconcile and pay credit card statements</li>
<li>Maintain accounting ledgers by verifying, posting and proofing account transactions</li>
<li>Verify vendor accounts by reconciling monthly statements</li>
<li>Review and validate credit card and out-of-pocket expense reports submitted by company employees against company policies and process for payment</li>
<li>Disburse and record EFT debit transactions; monitor leases and recurring transactions and setup payment schedules</li>
<li>Determine and calculate sales or use taxes on goods and services invoiced</li>
<li>Provide assistance to department administrators by answering inquiries about expenses</li>
<li>Maintain an efficient and well organized filing system for the department and ensures items are archived and disposed of per company guidelines</li>
<li>Protect the organization's value by keeping information confidential.</li>
<li>Update job knowledge by participating in educational opportunities and updating desk instructions.</li>
<br>
</ul>
