<h2>TRIOSE Enterprise Account Manager- West Coast</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>TRIOSE</b> is a nationwide non-asset based logistics management firm that drives to continually set the industry standard for providing world-class logistics management services for hospitals, health systems, and health care networks. TRIOSE accomplishes this by providing innovative products, services and solutions that meet all of our client’s logistics requirements by providing visibility of information, actionable and measurable results and control of the logistics processes.</p>
<p>
The <b>Enterprise Account Manager</b> is accountable for working with large hospital and health care systems to identify key personnel in the organizations who have responsibility for decision-making or have influence in strategic supply chain management. This position does not have supervisory responsibilities. This position deals with key company business processes that are unique and confidential. Due to the nature of this position, a non-compete agreement is required. A qualified candidate for this role must be located West of the Mississippi and near a major airport.</p>
<p><b>
You’d be having fun doing this and more throughout the week:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Signs a volume of business with new large hospital systems</li>
<li>Develops and tracks a sales pipeline status and pipeline value</li>
<li>Obtains commitments and / or signed sales contract from the prospect to advance</li>
<li>Maintains regular in person contact with prospects</li>
<li>Positions entire portfolio to prospects</li>
<li>Sells other business lines to new clients</li>
<li>Oversees the implementation process to ensure flawless execution</li>
</ul>
<p><b>We need you to have these skills and abilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Customer / Client Focus</li>
<li>Communication Proficiency</li>
<li>Performance Management</li>
<li>Business Acumen</li>
<li>Initiative</li>
<li>Results Driven</li>
<li>Organizational Skills</li>
</ul>
<p><b>You also need these qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree preferred; high school diploma or equivalent experience required</li>
<li>Minimum of 3 – 5 years of experience in selling solutions and services to “C” suite</li>
<li>A proven track-record in building strong business relationships having an established record of successful sales results on the “provider side” of Healthcare.</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Office Suite</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Physical Demands/Work Environment:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>This is a remote / home office position</li>
<li>Willing and able to travel within the U.S. (75%)</li>
<li>Ability to work evenings and weekends based upon the prospect / customer requirements</li>
</ul>
