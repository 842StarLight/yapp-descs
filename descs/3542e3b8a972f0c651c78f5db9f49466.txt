<div><b>Technical Data Analyst</b></div>
<br>
<b>About the Job</b><br>
Our market and SaaS products are highly data-driven, and we’re seeking a technical data analyst to play a pivotal role helping us clean, maintain, report on, and leverage our data. You’ll work as a member of our data operations team, analyzing and keeping our data up-to-date by creating formal and ad-hoc processes and tools. If you have a passion for quality and love getting your hands dirty with data, this is the ideal position for you!<br>
<br>
<i>Key Responsibilities:</i>
<ul>
<li>Implement data quality controls and reports, including the development of new metrics in accordance with business requirements.</li>
<li>Perform analysis and provide recommendations for modification or creation of business/technical rules based on data insights.</li>
<li>Work to identify root cause of any data inconsistencies and assist with developing long-term strategies to prevent future occurrences.</li>
<li>Create data automation tools to improve speed and efficiency for new data quality processes.</li>
<li>Gather, manage and synthesize large amounts of information efficiently and creatively.</li>
<li>Perform data load and mappings from different source systems, including batch processing.</li>
<li>Provide recommendations for cost-effective technologies and processes to enhance data quality.</li>
</ul>
<i>Minimum Qualifications:</i>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Information Systems, Information Technology, or related field with at least 2 years of work experience.</li>
<li>Very strong knowledge of MS SQL Server and SQL</li>
<li>Very strong knowledge of MS Excel - including Visual Basic to enhance automation</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task and manage multiple projects with varying timelines</li>
<li>Experience in data mining, analysis, reporting, and process implementation</li>
<li>Strong team player with the ability to work cross-functionally across the organization</li>
<li>Experience working for a SaaS organization preferred</li>
<li>Awareness of project management approaches and techniques</li>
<li>Excellent attention to detail and high standards for data quality</li>
</ul>
