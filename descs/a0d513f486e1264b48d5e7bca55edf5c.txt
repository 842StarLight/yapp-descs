<b>Position Summary:</b><br>
The Lead Generation Specialist is responsible for generating and qualifying leads on behalf of some of the nation's largest telecom providers by sourcing quality leads via cold calling, generating proposals, and correspondence to web inquiries. He/she will introduce potential partners to our Channel Management team while remaining part of the sales cycle as support.
<br><br>
<b>Essential Functions and Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for meeting daily, weekly and monthly sales goals.</li>
<li>Places outbound prospecting calls to potential customers (up to 300 calls per day).</li>
<li>Generates accurate proposals based on customer needs using Excel/Word/PDF.</li>
<li>Collaborates details and information to assigned National Channel Manager</li>
<li>Multi-tasking between various PC programs, carrier tools, and phone calls.</li>
<li>Entering correct customer data into NetSuite/CRM</li>
<li>Generates leads by explaining the benefits of the proposed pricing as well as the value of the telecom carrier.</li>
<li>Sells customers on features and benefits of services.</li>
<li>Provides kind and courteous service to customers.</li>
<li>Applies a professional and positive image and attitude towards all assigned tasks.</li>
<li>Performs with a high level of focus, organization, and competitiveness.</li>
<li>Adheres to all company policies, procedures, and selling practices.</li>
<li>Performs other related duties as assigned.</li>
<li>Provides continued support throughout the sales cycle for the assigned National Channel Manager.</li>
</ul>
<b>Job Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>High School Diploma required</li>
<li>Demonstrated basic computer proficiency including Word and Excel</li>
<li>1-2 years of Sales Experience, preferably lead generation</li>
<li>Excellent oral and written communication skills with a strong attention to detail</li>
<li>Ability to easily adapt to changes</li>
<li>Ideal candidate will demonstrate an energetic and "Can-Do" attitude</li>
<li>Thrives in a competitive environment</li>
</ul>
