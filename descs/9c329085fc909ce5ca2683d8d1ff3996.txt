<br>
We are Xaxis, the Outcomes Division of GroupM, the world’s leading media investment and management company. We believe in the power of digital media to transform the way business and consumers interact, and we are actively seeking a Product Manager to join our Global Business Operations team.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
You would hold responsibility for new product applications around Salesforce, our primary CRM system, while gaining exposure and maintaining other products that facilitate our financial reconciliation processes. You would also have an opportunity to influence innovative solutions for our visualization dashboards using the data ingested by these integrated products for global markets.<br>
</div>
<div>
<br>
As a member of this growing Product team at Xaxis, you will have the chance to collaborate with internal teams located all around the world to understand our product needs, define success metrics, construct product requirements and define the go-to-market strategies for existing products, including training and enablement.<br>
<br>
<b>YOUR IMPACT:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>
Proactively collaborate with engineering to determine the best technical implementation method for product enhancements and set reasonable timelines when needed</li>
<li>
Understand the business’ product integration needs in Xaxis and gather product requirements
</li>
<li>Collaborate with other product owners on the team and senior executives to help define Global Business Operations Roadmap for aspects of Salesforce, Manage, Radia, and Tableau
</li>
<li>Lead Product conversations and act as the SME for new market onboardings or integrations with global systems
</li>
<li>Maintain system integration initiatives within the larger product roadmap.</li>
<li>
Create and maintain a pilot user group as a sounding board for any new features or initiatives and overall feedback.</li>
</ul>

<br>
