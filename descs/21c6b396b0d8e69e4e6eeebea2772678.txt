<h2>Sr. Technical Analytics Lead / Martech Expert</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Directly responsible for managing the marketing analytics infrastructure for Collegis' partners – this involves auditing, implementing, onboarding/training, maintaining, as well as, build out reporting infrastructure from digital analytics tools (Google Analytics, Google Tag Manager, etc.) and marketing technology (A/B Testing tools, Marketing Automation, etc.)</li>
<li>Oversees the team responsible for the execution of technical analytics</li>
<li>Assists, supports the creation of technical specifications for connecting analytics and reporting systems, as well as, execute the implementation of marketing technology within internal and external digital properties</li>
<li>Directly participates in resource planning and partner meetings to help strategize current and future analytical needs to support marketing optimization efforts</li>
<li>Enables measurement and analysis of marketing initiatives through channel attribution and management</li>
<li>Find and support value-add and sales opportunities for new and existing partners within marketing technology and analytics</li>
<li>Provide training/onboarding to current and future partners, as well as, internal departments on maintaining data integrity and finding insights within the data</li>
<li>Ensure measurement and tracking on ongoing marketing initiatives that require advanced technical requirements</li>
<li>Innovate and experiment with new marketing and analytics technology</li>
</ul>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in technical field preferred but not required - equivalent experience considered</li>
<li>Proficiency in basic HTML and JavaScript, jQuery and ability to troubleshoot front-end/back-end site code is a must</li>
<li>Previous experience managing a team</li>
<li>2-5 Years with digital analytics systems (Google Analytics, Adobe Analytics/SiteCatalyst, etc.)</li>
<li>+2 Years of experience with Tag Management Systems (Google Tag Management, Tealium, Ensighten, etc.)</li>
<li>Familiarity with web content management systems</li>
<li>Experience deploying analytics and advertising tracking systems (IE: Google Analytics, Google Ads, Bing, etc. )</li>
<li>Experience with Google Analytics 360 a plus</li>
<li>Experience deploying and managing A/B and multivariate tools</li>
<li>Digital Ad Management experience is a plus</li>
<li>Web development experience is a plus</li>
<li>Working knowledge of databases and structure</li>
<li>Strong oral and written communication skills with a proven ability to interact professionally with partners, team members and cross-functionally within an organization</li>
<li>Strong organizational and interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Ability to translate analytics-related requests, analysis requirements into clear objectives</li>
<li>Advanced knowledge of Excel (Pivot Tables, Macros, etc.)</li>
<li>Possess high attention to detail and quick learn, self-starter</li>
</ul>
<b>The Company:</b><br>
Collegis Education is an enrollment growth management and technology services company that offers industry-leading solutions for colleges and universities of every size in every sector. Using a proactive and analytical approach, Collegis Education empowers education institutions to make a broader impact by providing insights that help grow enrollments, improve student outcomes and optimize expenses. With several decades of experience working within the higher education industry, the team at Collegis Education was founded within the walls of a college and expanded to help change more lives through education. Currently, the infrastructures established by Collegis Education support more than 40,000 students nationwide. For more information about Collegis Education, please visit www.CollegisEducation.com.
