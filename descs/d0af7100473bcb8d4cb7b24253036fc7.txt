<h2>Business Systems Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>Principal Responsibilities</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Responsible for planning and execution throughout the Reporting and Analytics lifecycle, including: gathering and prioritizing product and customer requirements, and working closely with developers, sales, product, and support teams to ensure revenue and customer satisfaction goals are met.</li>
<li>
Responsible for delivering functional specifications and stories with prioritized features and corresponding justification, run beta and pilot programs with early-stage products and samples, and act as a domain expert within the organization.</li>
<li>
Focus and deliver on the vision of Reporting and Analytics to build a scalable, sustainable and performing reporting product. Ensure that the product supports the company's overall strategy and goals.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with other business analysts and product managers to understand the user personas, write reporting specific user personas, functional specifications and help convert them into appropriate features for report development</li>
<li>
Assist report development teams in sizing and estimation of the product features</li>
<li>
Participate in planning activities, help the report development team to understand the user personas, product features to build the technical specifications for reporting</li>
<li>
Research and analyze market trends to adopt and communicate new concepts, ideas, and best practices specific to business intelligence reporting.</li>
<li>
Support the report development team to build proof of concepts. Present them to stakeholders to get their feedback and provide recommendations back to the team to improve the user experience.</li>
<li>
Mentor and develop junior resources in the team. Provide reporting training to external team members and end users.</li>
<li>
Assist in Product releases and new client system implementations, including user setup and training.</li>
<li>
Completes all responsibilities as outlined on annual Performance Plan.</li>
<li>
Completes all special projects and other duties as assigned.</li>
<li>
Must be able to perform duties with or without reasonable accommodation.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree in Business, Computer Science or other field demonstrating product managerial expertise</li>
<li>
Strong working knowledge of Healthcare, Pharmaceutical or Insurance industry with a minimum of 5 years’ experience partnering with internal and external Customers</li>
<li>
Strong presentation and communication skills to demonstrate reporting solutions to external Customers</li>
<li>
Proven ability to lead initiatives, plan activities and resources, work hands-on, and mentor junior team members</li>
<li>
1 year of experience as Agile Product Owner</li>
<li>
2-3 years working in an Agile environment (SCRUM or SAFE), preferred</li>
<li>
Proven ability to work closely with internal and external customers to understand and translate business needs and requirements into technical reporting specifications</li>
<li>
Proven ability to work with report development teams to ensure technical requirements are satisfied</li>
<li>
Strong analytical and problem-solving skills</li>
<li>
Experience in converting reporting requirements to visualized prototypes, preferred</li>
<li>
Must be self-motivated and passionate about delivering creative and value-driven reporting solutions</li>
<li>
Ability to work in a fast-paced, time-sensitive environment with competing priorities and ongoing deliverables</li>
<li>
Strong interpersonal, written and oral communication skills</li>
</ul>
