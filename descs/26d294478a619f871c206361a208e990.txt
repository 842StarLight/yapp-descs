<h2>Brokerage Technical Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>AmWINS </b>is looking for a <b>Technical Assistant </b>for their Satellite Beach location. The Technical Assistant will be responsible for providing support to multiple Brokers by preparing information for a variety of reports and documents, processing inquiries and forms and communicating with outside sources.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>The successful candidate will possess a vibrant personality, excellent communication skills, great motivation, capacity to understand concepts and drive to succeed in a team environment. As an Technical Assistant, you will have the opportunity to grow your career with one of the largest independent wholesale insurance brokers in the U.S. insurance industry, and work with industry leading Surplus Lines Carriers.</p>
<div><b>To the qualified candidate we offer:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Compensation includes a salary commensurate with experience</li>
<li>Position eligible for overtime</li>
<li>Position eligible for annual bonus</li>
<li>Full benefit package including: Medical, Dental, Life, Disability and 401(k)</li>
<li>Collaborative, continuing education focused work environment</li>
<li>Opportunity for advancement</li>
<li>Opportunity for formal sales and leadership training</li>
<li>Generous Paid Time Off (PTO) with Vacation and Personal days</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Technical Assistant duties:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Clearing and setting up new business and renewal submissions.</li>
<li>Learn company programs and proprietary software for submitting renewal submissions to markets</li>
<li>Submitting new business submissions to markets</li>
<li>Providing status of market clearances to marketing broker</li>
<li>Setting up online insured and new business folders</li>
<li>Preparing quotes, cover letters, binders, and invoices</li>
<li>Catching errors in carrier quotes and binders for the marketing broker</li>
<li>Building relationships with each producing and marketing broker</li>
<li>Determining home state for surplus lines taxes, and providing proper affidavits for tax filing compliance</li>
<li>Understanding the clearing of accounts</li>
<li>Sorting and prioritizing work flow</li>
<li>Verifying accuracy of completed documents (SOV, Acord Applications)</li>
<li>Entering data into internet based system (AmLINK)</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Office software, (e.g. Outlook, Word and Excel)</li>
<li>Multi-Tasking in a high paced environment</li>
<li>Learning how to run RiskMeter reports and batches for property data</li>
<li>Learning special projects including mapping, RMS scrubbing, loss summaries, etc.</li>
<li>Ordering loss runs from carriers</li>
<li>Able to deal with high volume and high stress</li>
</ul>
<div>
<b>Technical Assistant requirements</b>:</div>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 1 year of experience as support staff in commercial insurance</li>
<li>Excellent computer skills including typing proficiency, Microsoft Office proficiency with an emphasis in Excel spreadsheets</li>
<li>Highly organized</li>
<li>Able to deal with stress</li>
<li>Open to constructive criticism and instructions from others</li>
<li>Team minded</li>
<li>Detail oriented</li>
<li>Sense of urgency</li>
<li>Must be able to work overtime during peak times</li>
<li>Strong written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Excellent attention to detail</li>
<li>High-level of motivation</li>
<li>Ability to prioritize important and pressing tasks</li>
<li>Innate organizational skills, willingness to take on challenges, and focus on personal responsibility</li>
<li>Property &amp; Casualty Insurance License; or must take offsite insurance course and obtain insurance license within a year of hire (expense paid by AmWINS)</li>
</ul>
