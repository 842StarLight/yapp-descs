<p><b>Parker Hannifin</b>
</p>Founded in 1917, Parker Hannifin Corporation is a $14.3 billion, global company. With annual sales of $14.3 billion in fiscal year 2019, Parker Hannifin is the world's leading diversified manufacturer of motion and control technologies and systems, providing precision-engineered solutions for a wide variety of mobile, industrial and aerospace markets. The company has operations in 50 countries around the world. Parker has increased its annual dividends paid to shareholders for 63 consecutive fiscal years, among the top five longest-running dividend-increase records in the S&amp;P 500 index. Parker’s engineering expertise and broad range of core technologies uniquely positions the company to solve some of the world’s greatest engineering challenges. By partnering with customers, Parker improves their productivity and profitability and seeks new ways to solve humanity's biggest challenges.<p><b>
Responsibilities</b>
</p>The Production Coordinator provides scheduling and design support to all production lines within the Slater FF plant via the use of multiple software systems and various communication methods. In addition to supporting the productions lines, this position also maintains positive working relationships with various departments in the Overland Park office. • Maintain discrete jobs by entering data into ERP • Release, prepare and distribute production order packets • Process and book sample sales orders • Create/update fabric filter manufacturing drawings • Process fabric slitting orders via ERP • Requisition fabrics from consignment as needed • Scan and file paper drawings • Update and file closed sample orders • Update monthly closed sample order report • Schedule sample order production in coordination with Sales &amp; Order Management staff • Work in coordination with the Master Scheduler • Professionally communicate with Production Supervisors, Leads, Order Management staff • Work independently with minimal direction from Supervisor • Determine short &amp; long-term time frames so to organize priorities and meet demands<p><b>
Qualifications</b>
</p>REQUIREMENTS: • Microsoft Office: intermediate knowledge of Excel (formulas, pivot tables), Word and Outlook • Detail-oriented • Professional writing skills • HS Diploma or equivalent • Strong math and analytical skills PREFERRED: • Basic Auto-Cad knowledge • ERP skills • Data entry skills • Associates Degree or experience in a manufacturing environment<p><b>
Pay, Benefits, Work Schedule</b>
</p>Shift: 5:30 a.m. to 2:00 p.m., Monday – Friday, Overtime as required.<p><b>
