<div><div>
<p><b>Outline:</b> The Director of Customer Delivery will be responsible for leading and developing the relationship between his/her designated customers and the wider TrakCel project team to ensure project expectations are delivered. They will act as a customer and user advocate for product and service improvement as well as aiding delivery of the TrakCel solution as part of the project team.</p>
<p>As a member of TrakCel’s delivery team, you are responsible for the value delivered to TrakCel’s clients. The Director of Customer Delivery will orchestrate the customer journey through proactive engagement strategies which facilitate the delivery through the project team by spending time on-site with the customer for their journey. You will guide TrakCel’s clients to their desired outcomes while driving great value in their partnership with TrakCel. Monitoring the health of customer relationships and projects, you will predict and navigate customer challenges and offer solutions that drive satisfaction, retention, and additional enrichment opportunities.</p>
</div></div>
<br>
<div><div>
<p><b><i>Main Responsibilities:</i></b></p>
 <ul>
<li>Ensure assigned customers are delighted with TrakCel’s product, service and performance.</li>
 <li>Act as a local point of presence for in-region support and liaison. Where necessary, spend time working in client facilities in support of their internal team.</li>
 <li>Work with customer leadership teams and business line owners to define success criteria, project priorities and challenges to overcome.</li>
 <li>Deliver an exceptional delivery service to our clients in the US in close co-operation with our Head Office teams.
 </li>
<li>Capture customer and end user feedback and reporting requests for Product Management. Contribute to defining product and service vision.</li>
 <li>Support project delivery by customer coordination to ensure project deliverables, milestones and required tasks are completed by all stakeholders as planned.</li>
 <li>Provide regular status updates for customer leadership and business line owners.</li>
 <li>Facilitate working sessions to identify questions to ensure our work delivers against our clients’ needs and delivers actionable insights.</li>
 <li>Test, break and recommend improvements to the existing product from an end user perspective.</li>
 <li>Facilitate regular customer reviews of the project deliverables and system design to ensure success in delivery.</li>
 <li>Ensure management and internal teams are aware of any threats to our relationship in a timely manner so these can be addressed before they impact negatively on our business.</li>
 <li>Strengthen client relationships post sale to drive high engagement.
 </li>
<li>Orchestrate the quality and timely delivery of products or services purchased by working with the relevant ecosystem partners in the product, research, or consulting organizations to ensure that client success measures are met and that their value gained from TrakCel is maximized.</li>
 <li>Assist customers with onboarding of users and sites, understanding policies, and executing consultation calls to drive adoption and utilization. Assist the implementation of the platform for the customer, by managing the interaction with TrakCel’s project delivery teams.</li>
 <li>Support user training during roll-out.</li>
 <li>Work closely with TrakCel Solutions teams by providing oversight and management for issues and initiatives to ensure consistently-positive customer experiences. Escalate issues requiring resolution or sensitive decisions to TrakCel and customer leadership.</li>
 <li>Collaborate with Business Development on account strategies, identify opportunities for expansions. Identify upsell / expansion opportunities and partner with Business Development and Product teams to evaluate.</li>
 <li>Build trusted relationships with customer stakeholders.</li>
</ul>
</div></div>

<p><b><i>Skills and Experience- Essential</i></b>:</p>
 <ul>
<li>Proven track record in a client-facing role, driving client engagement, handling difficult situations and challenges, and delivering exceptional client service — for example, within a professional services organization (five to eight years).</li>
 <li>Relevant healthcare or pharmaceutical industry experience working in a B2B setting; familiarity with the typical challenges faced by clients in leading change and implementing new initiatives.</li>
 <li>Experience in delivery, balancing the needs of multiple stakeholders and working under tight timelines with resource constraints (five to eight years).</li>
 <li>Experience successfully building senior-level business relationships and facilitating conversations at all levels, including the C-suite.</li>
 <li>The ability to work in a high-energy, fast-paced environment and collaborate with other functions to achieve results as a high-performing, interdependent team.</li>
 <li>Relevant business experience demonstrating a commercial mindset and an understanding of selling moments.</li>
 <li>Cell / Gene therapy, supply chain and / or pharma commercial product experience.</li>
 <li>Willing to travel to visit clients and, if required, spend time working at client sites (estimate 30% of time will be spent out of home/TrakCel office). International travel may also be required.</li>
 <li>Bachelor's degree or equivalent work experience.</li>
 <li>Proven leadership and organizational skills.</li>
 <li>Excellent written, verbal and interpersonal communication skills. Proficiency in Microsoft Word, Excel and PowerPoint.</li>
 <li>Strong strategic thinking, problem solving and analytical / critical thinking skills.</li>
 <li>Capable of working closely with more than one client at a time.</li>
</ul>
