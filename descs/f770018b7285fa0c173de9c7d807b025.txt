<p><b>PURPOSE OF THE JOB</b></p>
<div>
<p>
The Composite Solutions Business is seeking an <b>Area Sales Manager (ASM) for the Fiberglass Rebar North America Team. </b>The primary accountabilities of the Area Sales Manager position are ensuring customer satisfaction, driving profitable sales growth of fiberglass rebar by increasing placement in 2-step and 1-step distribution, and gaining/maintaining OC market share by creating contractor pull through as well as successfully implementing a distribution strategy for the region to achieve the above. This position will be based in the western U.S. (west of the Rockies).</p>

JOB RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b><br>
JOB REQUIREMENTS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
The ASM will successfully seek out new accounts, grow current accounts, and convert targeted accounts.. Eighty percent of the ASM’s time is expected in to be in front of customers and distribution partners, as a result travel is a direct function of the role and where the ASM should be located within the territory</li>
<li>
This position will require building strong relationships with all customers and will be responsible for achieving the highest level of customer satisfaction and transactional excellence within the region. Managing perceived channel conflict well will be one of the keys to success.</li>
<li>
The ASM is supported by and works closely together with OC support partners (Customer Service, Customer Financial Services, Technical organizations, planning, etc.)</li>
<li>
For specific designated accounts, the ASM provides input to and works together with the Sales Leader in defining an integrated strategy for that account.</li>
<li>
The ASM will be responsible for creating and implementing a sales strategy for the territory. The strategy will focus on growing OC’s profitable share with distribution partners and direct accounts.</li>
<li>
The ASM will be responsible to capture the necessary market intelligence/forecasting data and communicate to the OC organization. This individual must possess strong business acumen and analytics as well as have strong planning and organizing skills.</li>
<li>
The ASM is a member of the Infrastructure Solutions organization and is expected to be a strong team player, excellent communicator and provide a positive impact to our fun, dynamic and successful team.</li>
</ul>

About Owens Corning
</b></p>
<p>Owens Corning is a global leader in insulation, roofing, and fiberglass composite materials. Its insulation products conserve energy and improve acoustics, fire resistance, and air quality in the spaces where people live, work, and play. Its roofing products and systems enhance curb appeal and protect homes and commercial buildings alike. Its fiberglass composites make thousands of products lighter, stronger, and more durable. Owens Corning provides innovative products and solutions that deliver a material difference to its customers and, ultimately, make the world a better place. The business is global in scope, with operations in 33 countries. It is also human in scale, with approximately 20,000 employees cultivating local and longstanding relationships with customers. Based in Toledo, Ohio, USA, the company posted 2018 sales of $7.1 billion. Founded in 1938, it has been a Fortune 500® company for 64 consecutive years. For more information, please visit www.owenscorning.com.</p>
