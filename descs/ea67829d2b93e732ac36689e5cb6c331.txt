</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Responsible for partnering with business leaders to align business strategies across Cambia, addressing complex issues and interactions that have strategic impact. The incumbent facilitates design of an enterprise-wide roadmap among senior business and technical leaders and manages the implementation of the design.</div>

All eight values are equally important and linked to the others: Empathy, Hope, Courage, Trust, Commitment, Innovation, and Accountability. These values are not just words on paper - we live them every day.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Achieving our ambitious Cause requires exemplary leadership. In addition to the Cambia values listed above, our leaders demonstrate the following leadership attributes: Cambia leaders think big, push boundaries, envision possibilities, inspire and empower others. <h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities &amp; Requirements
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Provide strategic consultancy support to technical and business partners in defining or designing business processes and researching and identifying enabling solutions based on consumer research, and/or business requirements.</li>
<li>
Direct projects and be responsible for the overall implementation and execution of project and performance criteria; maintain full knowledge of all phases of implementations.</li>
<li>
Assess near-term needs, utilizing structured interview processes to establish business priorities; consult with other subject matter experts and develop alternative solutions; advise on options, risks, costs versus benefits, and impact on other business processes and system priorities.</li>
<li>
Facilitate ideation discussions, and the brokering of services within the business and technical communities on behalf of consumers and the enterprise; coordinate multisystem solutions to clarify interdependence so that systems appear seamless to customers.</li>
<li>
Ally with business partners to remain apprised of project and available technology status and inform customer management of progress; conversely, keep management team aware of user issues and potential potholes or resolve conflicts.</li>
<li>
Facilitate development of cross-line of business (LOB) and multiple-technical discipline solutions that combine knowledge of particular business processes and issues, specific technologic options, and process facilitation techniques.</li>
<li>
Develop, motivate and direct staff to create a team environment and enable staff to fulfill Cambia’s operating and strategic objectives.</li>
<li>
Has regular contact with management and personnel of assigned department/unit on specific projects.</li>
<li>
Partner with the functional groups within the organization to communicate and clarify business needs, identifying creative applications of research, design, and technology to meet those needs; contribute to the development of long-range project plans, and ensure products and services are aligned with consumer, and business needs.</li>
<li>
Conduct business process and technology analyses, needs assessments, and preliminary cost versus benefit measurements; and communicate trends and requirements to and from customers and IT, with particular emphasis on meeting consumer needs, and strategic objectives.</li>
</ul>

Minimum Requirements:</b></div>

The Manager Business Integration Consulting would have a Bachelor’s degree (or higher) in Business Administration or related field and at least 8-10 years experience in project, program and initiative management execution of large scale, complete projects or initiatives (or co-management with IT or business unit/corporate department) or equivalent combination of education and experience.</li>
<li>
Management experience in which one or more of the following occurred: Demonstrated competency in strategic thinking and leadership, with strong abilities in relationship management; Gained experience in managing resources to meet goals across multiple projects and initiatives; Gained experience in leading multidiscipline, high performance work teams/groups; Demonstrated competency in developing efficient and effective solutions to diverse and complex business problems; Demonstrated high competency in project management. Demonstrated competency in consumer focused solutioning.</li>
<li>
Deep knowledge of industry business practices and direction.</li>
<li>
Recognized within and outside of Cambia as an industry expert in project, program and initiative management.</li>
<li>
Extensive knowledge and expertise in the use of project management, initiative management and consumer focused methodologies and tools.</li>
<li>
Strong project, program and initiative management skills; experience in organizing, planning, and executing large-scale efforts from vision through implementation, involving internal personnel, contractors, and vendors; ability to analyze initiative needs and determine resources needed to meet objectives.</li>
<li>
Strong teamwork and interpersonal skills and ability to communicate effectively with all management levels.</li>
<li>
Strong analytical and conceptual skills; a demonstrated track record in new concept development for a variety of projects and complex technical plans.</li>

Ability to solve problems involving remote and elusive systems often spanning multiple environments in a business area and spanning diverse technologies.</li>
