<h2>Intern - Digital Marketing</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>

<div><div>Description</div></div>
<div></div>
<div>Overview:<br>
Continental is searching for a Digital Marketing intern to support the Commercial Vehicle Tires team. The right candidate will be pursuing Digital Marketing or a related field of study and will exemplify a willingness to grow their digital and business skills. Must be a highly motivated, collaborative self-starter with the ability to accept constructive criticism and implement change with a positive attitude. This is a paid internship opportunity.<br>
<br>
Responsibilities:<br>
<ul>
<li>Independently complete or collaborate on digital projects including digital advertising design, metrics and analytics reports, asset management, SEO analysis, FTP uploads, CMS website edits, etc.</li>
<li>Be informed of the latest digital/web trends and best practices</li>
<li>Adhere to a set of established guidelines and practices</li>
<li>Understand how to work within a team environment</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
</ul>
</div>
<p></p>
</div>
<div>
<div><div>Qualifications</div></div>
<div></div>
<div>Required Qualifications:<br>
<ul>
<li>Must be enrolled in a US accredited degree program (Requirement for ALL intern candidates)</li>
<li>Must maintain a minimum GPA of a 2.8 (Requirement for ALL intern candidates)</li>
<li>Digital advertising or web design portfolio, or samples of work</li>
<li>Knowledge of a web CMS and/or HTML based software</li>
<li>Microsoft Office Suite including PowerPoint</li>
<li>Passionate about digital marketing</li>
<li>Ability to work at least 15 hours per week</li>
<li>Ability to dedicate 6+ months toward this internship</li>
</ul>
<br>
Preferred Qualifications:<br>
<ul>
<li>Proficiency in Adobe Creative Cloud for digital advertising design including one or more of the following: InDesign, Illustrator, Photoshop</li>
<li>Knowledge of Google Analytics or Google Web Tools</li>
<li>HTML, CSS, JavaScript skills</li>
<li>Ability to create animations</li>
<li>SEO optimization knowledge</li>
<li>Bilingual in French or Spanish languages</li>
</ul>
</div>
