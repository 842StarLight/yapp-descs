<h2>Administrative Assistant - Design and Development</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>DecoPac, Inc. is a high growth, high-energy company that designs, markets and distributes the most popular cake decorations and bakery supplies in the bakery industry. Located in Anoka, MN we sell and distribute our products to all supermarkets, retail bakeries, and ice cream stores throughout North America, Canada, and the US.
</p>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Summary:</b></h1>
<p>
This position will assist the Design &amp; Development and Product Management &amp; Licensing departments in day-to-day responsibilities to support the tactical implementation of DecoPac's product development and marketing goals. This position is responsible for tracking and trafficking new products in development, licensor presentations and meeting logistics as well as general administrative-related support. The Administrative Assistant must excel in a highly collaborative environment, balancing multiple projects and stakeholders across the organization positively and proactively.</p>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader">
<b>
Responsibility</b><b>:</b>
</h1>
<ul>
<li>
Ability to coordinate, track and manage non-licensed product development to support the needs of DecoPac's customers and meet business objectives.</li>
<li>
Complete item setup for new products.
</li>
<li>Complete tactical operational activities and support business objectives including licensor presentations, meeting logistics, print material proofing, catalog production planning and production, project management, brand updates, new product guides, and program support information and distribution.</li>
<li>
Create, edit, and manipulate support materials including presentations, product line lists and reports in preparation for presentations and meetings.</li>
<li>
Cross-functional team liaison supporting organization, communication and project management needs.</li>
<li>
Manage and coordinate new product samples and distribution, as well as set up new items in legacy program.</li>
<li>
Tactical support for the planning and execution of new products and programs</li>
<li>
Prepare schedules and portfolios for off-site travel and events licensing, vendor visits, trade shows, etc.</li>
<li>
General department administrative tasks, including calendar management, meeting requests, expense reports, etc.</li>
<li>
Open and willing to drive continuous improvement.</li>
<li>
Other daily tasks as assigned.</li>
</ul>
