<b>The Job: </b>Technical Product Manager<br>
<br>
<br>
We are looking for an experienced Technical Product Manager who is passionate about building products that customers love. You will work with cross-functional teams to design, build and roll-out new products that expand on the Agiloft no-code platform story and deliver the company’s vision and strategy. The ideal candidate will have a passion for, and history of, managing IT- or TC products in B2B markets throughout their lifecycle.</div>
<br>
<div>
<div>
<br>
<b>Job Responsibilities</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Analyze and monitor the relevant market for your products and use findings as input for roadmap planning, new product design and the planning of product improvements</li>
<li>Manage the product lifecycle for one or multiple product areas of the Agiloft platform</li>
<li>Write and negotiate product specifications</li>
<li>Manage the development of new products all the way from idea to market launch</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Requirements</b></div>
<ul>
<li>A Bachelor’s degree in Engineering or IT and an MBA or MS degree with a focus in Marketing.</li>
<li>3+ years of relevant work experience in fields such as technical marketing, technical pre-sales, consulting, or product management.</li>
<li>Experience with the writing of technical product specifications</li>
<li>Excellent leadership, communication, project management, and organizational skills.</li>
<li>High level of independence</li>
<li>Experience in working with distributed, multinational teams</li>
</ul>
<br>
*Accepting applications from the USA, Canada, The United Kingdom and Europe<br>
