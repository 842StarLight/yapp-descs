<h2>Sr. Sales Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Softweb Solutions is seeking a Sr. Sales Manager to join our Customer Success team in the Dallas Fort Worth Metroplex. The Sr. Sales Manger will manage the end-to-end relationship with our enterprise customers. As a Sales Manager you wil also present to the Sales Qualified Leads with Solutions and Platform offerings.
</p>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities:
</b></h4>
<ul>
<li>Customer Lifecycle Management for Customer Success</li>
<li>Manage customer success by owning the end-to-end customer lifecycle – including on-boarding, expansion phase, and renewals.</li>
<li>Provide technical leadership for customer onboarding projects at large Financial Services, Healthcare, Retail and other industry verticals.</li>
<li>Provide Technical Account management for a portfolio of enterprise customers.</li>
<li>Driver Customer Success via High touch and Low touch methods (automation tools).</li>
<li>Conduct QBRs for enterprise customers.</li>
<li>Drive successful customer renewals with low churn and work with sales ops to execute renewals.</li>
<li>Customer Onboarding</li>
<li>Ability to provide expert guidance for Softweb Solutions product deployment with strong skill sets in infrastructure architecture, cloud, IT operations, security, and development processes.</li>
<li>Create a frictionless onboarding process for the customer by working closely with Support, Services and Sales Engineering teams within Softweb Solutions.</li>
<li>Project Management
<ul>
<li>Project liaison support for managing product deliveries into customers – especially for custom deliverables.</li>
<li>Conduct regular requirements gathering reviews and project reviews.</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Requirements:</b></h4>
<ul>
<li>7+ years Technical Account Management, Customer Support experience or Sales Engineering experience or equivalent experience – practitioners are welcome as well.</li>
<li>Ability to provide expert guidance for Softweb Solutions product deployment with strong skill sets in infrastructure architecture, cloud, IT operations, security, and development technologies and processes.</li>
<li>Self-starter with proven track record of Customer facing roles and working with executives.</li>
<li>Expertise in open source and SaaS is a major advantage.</li>
<li>B.S. degree in an engineering or similar program from an accredited college / university preferred or equivalent experience</li>
<li>Proficiency in understanding concepts and technologies in DevOps, IT operations, security, cloud, microservices, containers, and scheduling platforms</li>
<li>Proficiency and/or knowledge of existing Softweb Solutions tools such as Vagrant, Packer, Terraform, Consul, Nomad, Vault and others</li>
<li>Experience with implementing software products or solutions to large and dynamic enterprise companies</li>
<li>Ability to work well in a highly dynamic / team environment that focuses on providing above industry standard customer service</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
