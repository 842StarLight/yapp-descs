<h2>Associate Account Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Job Description</b><br>
<p>Do you have a passion for partnering with customers to help drive their success? Are you a self-starter who, with the right tools and resources at your disposal, can identify, prioritize and close sales opportunities?</p>
<p></p>
<p>
As an inside sales <b>Associate Account Representative</b> at Thomson Reuters you will have an opportunity to be involved in all aspects of the sales cycle from prospecting through to demonstrating and selling our best-in-class products, while learning and using market-leading sales tools to help attain your success and advance your career.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
We provide a competitive compensation package with base salary and bonuses (uncapped!), as well as generous employee benefits, which include paid vacation/sick time annually, comprehensive health/vision/dental plan options, retirement savings with company match, education assistance, health &amp; wellness incentives, etc.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
Thomson Reuters is also consistently recognized as a ‘Top Employer to Work For’ across a broad range of categories, such as Work-Life Balance, Diversity &amp; Inclusion, Opportunities for Women, Culture, Volunteerism.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
About the Role:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Consult with clients to sell our integrated tax and accounting solutions to existing and potential customers over the phone and Cisco WebEx covering every aspect of a firm’s operations that compile client data to posting finished tax returns, from managing engagements to every aspect of a firm’s practice</li>
<li>
Take responsibility for the full sales cycle, from prospecting through to demonstrating our products</li>
<li>
You will have the confidence knowing you are representing an iconic trusted brand/organization that provides best-in-class products</li>
<li>
You will be trained on and given access to market-leading sales tools to enable your success</li>
<li>
Live the Thomson Reuters culture and values</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
We'd love to hear from you if you have/are:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Experience in developing new account prospects and clients through networking, referrals, cold calling and account development over the phone and online</li>
<li>
One or more years of business-to-business (B2B) sales experience</li>
<li>
An ability to explain complex and technical topics clearly to non-technical audiences</li>
<li>
Proficient with MS Office, sales automation, customer relationship management or web based software programs</li>
<li>
An entrepreneurial mindset and a proven track record of meeting or exceeding your sales quotas</li>
<li>
A college degree and/or relevant professional or academic certifications</li>
<li>
Previous experience selling financial services or software products and services is a plus</li>
</ul>
