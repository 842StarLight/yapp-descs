<div>Job Description<br>
<br>
<p>The Construction Center Administrator (CCA) is a full time position that involves working in one of our many local construction centers. Individual will be working with local sales/construction and corporate personnel on a daily basis. The Administrator will be accountable for a wide range of responsibilities to support and promote the local construction center and all of its activities. Many of the daily responsibilities will be achieved using state of the art computer programs and equipment. Solid computer and organizational skills are a must in maintaining the level of expectation that is required to achieve full potential for this position.</p>

Create and maintain a positive/professional attitude towards customers and co workers</li>
