<div><b>The Job: Business Control Advisor
</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<b>Purpose
</b><br>
Assist in the compilation and delivery of IFRS and/or US GAAP based financial statements, reporting packages and analysis to local management, parent company, and the US partnerships. Provides IFRS and US GAAP guidance on complicated business transactions. Develops processes, drives continuous improvement in financial reporting. Co-ordinate in external audits for ENGIE North America partnerships and JVs.
<br>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>Location/Department
</b></div>
<div>Houston, TX / ENGIE Partnership
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<b>Status
</b><br>
Salaried - Exempt
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>Essential Job Functions
</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Closely follow IFRS standard releases and assess the accounting and financial reporting impact for various partnerships in the US.
</li>
<li>Closely work with technical accounting to ensure financial statement packages are reported in compliance with IFRS and/or US GAAP based on the ENGIE Group and various partnership reporting requirements.
</li>
<li>Coordinate in the statutory and partnership audits and reviews for various partnerships and businesses in the North America region
</li>
<li>Coordinate external audit by providing working papers including consolidated statements template for various partnerships
</li>
<li>Assist in the budget presentation process for Asset Optimization both internally and to partnerships.
</li>
<li>Continuously engage with business units (operations, BD, LPM, technical services, corporate) to coordinate financial reporting deliverables.
</li>
<li>Manage internal activities of Asset Optimization that include responsibilities of gathering data, providing analysis, preparing reports, making recommendations, driving productivity initiatives and corrective actions.
</li>
<li>Lead or act as a high-level team member on special projects as needed.
</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.
<br>
</li>
</ul>
</div>
