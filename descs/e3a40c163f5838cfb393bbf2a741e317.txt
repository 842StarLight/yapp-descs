<br>
<b>The Why</b><br>
This diverse, purpose-driven Team at Stanley Black &amp; Decker shares a unified passion for creating products, tools and solutions for those who make the world. This role will give you a strong understanding of how a world class Fortune 200 organization creates and implements different strategies across our various commercial brands focusing on performance, customer satisfaction &amp; innovation.<br>
<br>
<b>The What</b><br>
Reporting directly to the Specialty Manager, the Industrial Construction Specialist is responsible for executing the End User Brand Marketing Strategy. As an Industrial Construction Specialist, you must be able to form strategic partnerships with the professional End Users to deliver jobsite solutions, world class customer service and product innovation. Some of your daily activities will include cold calling on industrial, commercial and institutional construction sites, calling on construction company head offices, planning jobsite events and conducting safety training seminars for our tool and accessory brands.<br>
<br>
<b>The How</b><br>
<ul>
<li>As the main point of contact for the company in the field, providing customer service, product knowledge, and sales expertise to our end users</li>
<li>This role will include visiting trade schools, trade training facilities and union sites</li>
<li>Assisting Sales Team at distribution sales events; developing new contacts and closing sales</li>
<li>Building and maintaining relationships with End Users and internal Sales Force</li>
<li>Coordination and management of top customer purchasing</li>
<li>Supporting our distribution network as it pertains to large user needs</li>
<li>New business development within assigned region</li>
</ul>
