<b>Job Responsibilities:</b><br>
A Shift Lead is responsible for the overall shift operations, sales performance and execution of brand excellence in a store in the absence of Assistant General Manager and/or General Manager. Shift leads typically supervise a staff of 1 to 8 team members and carry out management responsibilities consistent with Jamba Juice policies, procedures and legal requirements.
<br><br>
<b>Essential Responsibilities and Skills:</b><br>
Lead and manage shifts while acting as manager in charge of store. Deploy team members as required to meet business demands and make necessary staffing decisions.
<br>Communicate clearly, concisely and accurately in order to ensure effective shift operations.
<br>Work the line. Perform all tasks related to Jamba operational standards. Be able to effectively work and act as a role model at each station in the store.
<br>Ensure exceptional customer service is delivered on every shift.
<br>Follow all food safety, cash handling and operations procedures and policies.
<br>Ensure proper financial accountability during each shift.
<br>Delegation. Clearly and comfortably delegates both routine and important tasks and decision.
<br>Create an environment in which Team Members are motivated to do their best.
<br><br>
<b>Additional Requirements:</b><br>
Must be at least 18 years of age.
<br>High School Diploma or equivalent Certification
<br>Current or recent food/retail service experience
<br>Able to work minimum 20-25 hours per week, including nights and weekends
<br>Computer skills including some Microsoft software and register skills
<br>Able to lift 30-40 pounds regularly throughout a four to eight hour shift
<br>Ability to be mobile in walk-in refrigerator and freezer with temperatures ranging from 40° F to -10° F
<br>Ability to embody Jamba culture &amp; values – will make a good representative to the Jamba Brand (i.e. energy, fun, self-starter)</div>
