<h2>DIRECTOR OF NATIONAL SALES / CALL CENTER OPERATIONS</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>DIRECTOR OF NATIONAL SALES / CALL CENTER OPERATIONSWe are a leading disruptive insurtech operation looking for an experienced DIRECTOR OF NATIONAL SALES / CALL CENTER OPERATIONS to rapidly scale our call center operations.Description: This position will report to the COO and leads the National Sales department’s daily operations and ensures that best practices in an insurance call center service and sales environment are developed, implemented, and maintained. We are looking for a leader who has “been there, done that” and would like to now use those skills to scale our company in a highly visible role. As a key contributor of our team your role will be to double the size of our organization while optimizing the company’s Omni-Channel sales.Position Requirements:</p>
<ul>
<li>MBA or Bachelor’s Degree preferred.</li>
<li>Minimum 5 years experience in a management capacity for a 200+ person contact center.</li>
<li>P&amp;C sales experience preferred but not required with sufficient contact center experience.</li>
<li>Extensive knowledge of current call center technologies, best practices, budgeting, staffing, and operational metrics.</li>
</ul>
<p>Primary Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Recruit, hire, train, monitor and instill pride and esprit-de-corps in a professional national call center to consistently provide a sales experience that is personalized with trusted professional customer service and advice and assistance to our nationwide customers.</li>
<li>Provide leadership and effective communication across multiple levels of staff (agents, team leads and supervisors).</li>
<li>Develop and enhance sales force capabilities by demonstrating hands-on consistent management practices that identify areas of excellence while fostering an environment of continuous improvement through coaching, KPI development, goal setting, performance evaluations, and incentive systems.</li>
<li>Maintain a positive work environment that supports a quality driven team and identify any issues that may inhibit an individual's or the team's performance.</li>
<li>Effectively use all channels of communication to inform and motivate staff through one-on-ones, staff meetings and written communications.</li>
<li>Effectively recruit and hire qualified staff and provide for their training and development fostering a cohesive and supportive team environment.</li>
<li>Assign priorities and make decisions that are consistent with the company's mission and strategic goals.</li>
<li>Execute on sales strategies to achieve or exceed sales results and improvement initiatives.</li>
<li>Proactively share thoughts / concerns / issues with regards to the design and / or marketing of the company's product portfolio and consistently solicits input from staff.</li>
<li>Perform other related duties as required to achieve the goals and objectives of the company and department.</li>
<li>Continuously improve our customers overall experience via training, technology and other methods while meeting SLAs and KPIs including average call handling time, average talk time, wait time, follow-up calls, sales conversions, and overall productivity.</li>
</ul>
<p>Strategy:</p>
<ul>
<li>Understands the competitive landscape and assists in uncovering market niches, actively pursues growth opportunities, and creatively experiments with new ideas.</li>
<li>Investigate trends, surface issues, identify root problem and collaboratively work with specific team / individuals to resolve issues.</li>
<li>Provide ongoing evaluation of the strategy and effectiveness of call center programs, as well as communication of policies and procedures.</li>
</ul>
<p>Performance and Cross-Functional Management:</p>
<ul>
<li>Interact with Marketing, Finance, and Information Technology to ensure effective integration of system and operational procedures needed to support and grow he company’s national sales.</li>
<li>Work with senior management to develop the annual plan and P&amp;L for call center operations.</li>
<li>Source, maintain, and manage outside call center vendors and service providers.</li>
<li>Determine and ensure best practices and emerging technologies to drive profitable growth.</li>
<li>Project manage new initiatives and lead through change.</li>
<li>Responsible for partnering with relevant departments to monitor and ensure compliance from a legal, regulatory, tax, and accounting perspectives.</li>
</ul>
