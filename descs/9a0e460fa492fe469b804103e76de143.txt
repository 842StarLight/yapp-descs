<h2>IT Support Call Center Intern</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<b>General Summary
</b><br>
<p>Under direct supervision, the IT Support Call Center Intern will gain exposure to all functions performed by Store Support Services including: store operations, hardware, software and communication troubleshooting. Interns will assist with answering these questions from the support desk via phone, e-mail, and online tickets. Store Support Services provides 24 hour service to our stores, 7 days a week. Schedules including days, nights, over nights, and weekends.
</p>
<p><b>Essential Job Functions
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Answer calls from stores with regard to questions and issues they experience with their computer system.
</li>
<li>Assist in minor repairs to computer equipment that are performed in-house.
</li>
<li>Provide troubleshooting in WAN/LAN issues at the store.
</li>
<li>Load and maintain computer access passwords for the stores Communicate with store personnel to clarify and identify all errors and forward the issues to the appropriate personnel.
</li>
<li>Maintain excellent customer service in all customer or team member contracts.
</li>
<li>Provide after hours support for other corporate departments.
</li>
<li>Monitor and respond to e-mails from service providers, corporate users and stores.
</li>
<li>Ensure the computer hardware and software operates as efficiently as possible to prevent the interruption of business at all retail locations.
</li>
<li>Direct communication with store personnel, district managers, regional managers, territory sales managers, distribution center personnel, loss prevention auditors, corporate users, customers, vendors and job applicants.
</li>
<li>Additional projects as assigned.
</li>
<li>Update and maintain incidents using the ticketing software.
</li>
<li>Completion of required training as assigned.
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Skills/Qualifications/Education
</b></p>
<p>Required:
</p>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or equivalent
</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal skills, verbal and communication skills; is capable of explaining procedures in writing, verbally, and over the phone
</li>
<li>Exceptional problem solving skills, critical thinking, and troubleshooting skills
</li>
<li>Ability to think outside-the-box, perform research, and operate independently or in a group setting
</li>
<li>Basic technical and computer knowledge</li>
</ul>
