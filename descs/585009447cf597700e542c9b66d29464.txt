<h2>Strategic Account Manager - Denver</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Salsify is seeking a highly motivated, dynamic, and driven Strategic Account Manager who has previous SaaS experience and an affinity for solution selling. As a Strategic Account Manager at Salsify you will take our Product Experience Management (PXM) solution to the international marketplace. Strategic Account Managers are responsible for taking a strategic sales approach to further sell and expand Salsify's presence and relationships within a small group of named clients comprised of the most well-known brand manufacturers in the world.
<br><br>
We have found that the most successful Strategic Account Managers are masterful storytellers who can weave a complex solution into connecting with brands and optimizing how they are navigating the ever-changing e-commerce landscape. Salsify is seeing incredible success and growth both internally as well as with our clients. Our Strategic Account Managers are key drivers in Salsify's continued relationships with our clients on the worldwide stage. We are seeking those high achievers who continue to set the bar and are true evangelists in their sales approach and follow through.
<br><br>
Salsify loves a good success story and it would be our privilege to help write yours! We recognize that talent and potential come in all forms and our mission is empowerment. Our hiring process involves recognizing all facets of motivation, drive, success, and passion, not just check marks next to a job description. If you have interest in our roles please do not hesitate to apply - we would be happy to speak with you!
