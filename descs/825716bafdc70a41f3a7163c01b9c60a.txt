<h2>Fulfillment Client Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Fulfillment Client Coordinator is responsible for the growth of Bethel Media’s Fulfillment Center and management of current clients. This includes the maintenance, analytics, data reporting, client experience and customer service of the Fulfillment Center. This candidate should possess a strong sales background, customer service background and previous marketing experience. This person must be organized, detailed, and can manage multiple accounts at once. Time management is key. High drive for results, reporting, analytics, marketing and account management. This candidate should possess a high understanding of retail and shipping and have a high value for self-starting.
</p>
<p><b>Job Duties &amp; Responsibilities
</b></p>
<p>This position is a strategic account acquisition role. This position will Identify new business opportunities. The candidate will target, qualify and pursue prospects in accordance with Bethel's culture, strategy, and techniques. Building rapport and retention is key. You will be responsible for retaining current customers. This should be done by sharing your knowledge and enthusiasm about our pricing, service, and technology.
</p>
<p>Keep a pulse on the competitive fulfillment market
</p>
<p>Have a woo like personality to build relational partnerships with ministries and businesses on behalf of Bethel.
</p>

Developing new client relationships and looking for ways to acquire new clients for our fulfillment

</p>
<p>Sales forecasting and growth opportunities<br>
Create a compelling, well-balanced experience for our Fulfillment clients.</p>
