<h2>Director of Sales</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b><i>We Create Great Experiences! Come Join Our TEAM! We value and take care of our Team Members and provide a safe and friendly environment in which to work.</i></b></h3>
<p><b>
Summary</b></p>
<p>
This position is responsible for generating top line revenue by managing all aspects of the Sales and Marketing Department. This individual supports the General Manager . This position is responsible for providing leadership and conducting his or her own prospecting and outside sales calls.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Duties &amp; Responsibilities include the following:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Calls on prospects to solicit business, analyzes requirements of function, outlines available hotel facilities and services offered, and quotes prices.</li>
<li>
Cultivates lead sources, distributes leads to other hotels in the brand and Willow Valley properties.</li>
<li>
Conducts site tours of hotel to walk-ins and prospective clients.</li>
<li>
Uses sales system to make contracts for bookings, and documents weekly sales efforts.</li>
<li>
Maximize occupancy by contributing strategies and promoting yield management and using the RMS system to ensure total revenue, REVPAS, and ADR are on target.</li>
<li>
Coordinates and communicates meeting room set-up and catering needs with hotel staff.</li>
<li>
Attends and represents the hotel at Chamber functions and community events and follows-up and responds to all Hilton and CVB sales leads.</li>
<li>
Establishes and maintains close contact with assigned accounts and target accounts.</li>
<li>
Confers with customers and Team Members to plan function details such as space requirements, publicity, time schedule, food service and decorations.</li>
<li>
Responds to all RFP’s and sales lead through the OnQ system, and any other packages or special offers, as directed by the RDOSM.</li>
<li>
Assists the RDOSM in planning and coordinates advertising and packages for the hotel.</li>
<li>
Qualifies leads and prospects by using the Sales Process.</li>
<li>
Interacts with guests to solicit new business and gain feedback, alerts RDOSM to all opportunities for improvement.</li>
<li>
Assist in training GSA’s on the reservation/sales process, new packages, and hotel promotions.</li>
<li>
Reports on hotel sales as indicated on report chart.</li>
<li>
Completes OnQ Property Management System training and all other required Hilton Hotels and Willow Valley training.</li>
<li>
Participates in the development of the Sales &amp; Marketing Plan.</li>
<li>
Creates Action Plans and key account action plans as directed by the RDOSM. Once approved, accepts responsibility for assigned aspects of the plans.</li>
<li>
Must work minimum of one weekend a month as MOD.</li>
<li>
Sets-up lunch and breaks when needed.</li>
<li>
Assists the Front Desk with Sales training.</li>
<li>
Make necessary hotel purchases such as, food, gas, etc.</li>
<li>
Coordinates overbooking situations.</li>
<li>
Keeps the website up to date.</li>
<li>
Attends all Hilton training and yearly conference.</li>
<li>
Attends scheduled sales meetings, All Team Meetings and Focus Service Meetings.</li>
<li>
Is part of the leadership team which is responsible for the physical building as well as the care and morale of the Team Members &amp; Guests.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Supervisory Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>
Manages subordinate Team Members within the Sales Department. Is responsible for the overall direction, coordination, and evaluation of this unit.</p>
<p><b>
Education &amp; Experience</b></p>
<p>
Associates Degree; or 4 years related experience and/or training is the minimum education level required for this position. Bachelor’s degree preferred. Outside Sales Experience required.</p>
