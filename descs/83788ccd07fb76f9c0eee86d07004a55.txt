<p><b>ESTIMATING SUPERVISOR</b></p>
<p>
Metal Edge Business Unit, Roofing Products</p>
<p>
OMG Inc. is a leading manufacturer of fastening products and technologies for the commercial roofing and other construction industries worldwide. Based in Massachusetts, with manufacturing facilities outside of Springfield, MA, Chicago, IL, and Asheville, NC, OMG is an equal opportunity employer valuing integrity, teamwork, diversity, trust, respect, communication, accountability, proactive performance and a passion for excellence!</p>
<p><b>
Position</b></p>
<p>
OMG's Roofing Products division delivers products and services that improve contractor productivity and enhance roofing system performance. Our Metal Accessories Business Unit manufactures &amp; sells code-compliant edge metal systems, fascia, coping, and gravel stops which provide protection against wind and water infiltration. Reporting to the Sales Manager, the Estimating Supervisor drives a team of inside sales resources to accurately assess material needs related to a sales opportunity, help gather the data necessary to prepare an effective sales proposal and provide strong technical support to the Sales &amp; Marketing teams post bid. This key position will develop and implement processes that drive accuracy in the estimating process, reduce lead times from request to bid, and result in a higher percentage of estimates transitioning to closed sales. The Estimating Supervisor must possess strong leadership skills, the ability to identify metrics with which to measure the team's success and to implement countermeasures for goals missed, and the ability to utilize lean tools to maximize the accuracy and efficiency of the team's work product.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Promote Safety as the Number One Priority of all OMG employees.</li>
<li>Help Sales management to develop and implement processes by which success of estimating and related inside sales can be measured</li>
<li>Ensure inside sales resources provide timely and accurate pricing information and quotes to customers and remote sales team.</li>
<li>Utilize metrics and countermeasures to drive team to reach daily, weekly, monthly and annual close ratio targets.</li>
<li>Manage team to utilize CRM software and dashboard metrics to measure growth and communicate progress to sales and marketing teams.</li>
<li>Train, coach and develop inside sales resources on problem solving, customer relations, product knowledge, standard work and continuous improvement.</li>
<li>Strengthen relationship between Inside Sales, Estimating, Customer Service and Operations to balance customer needs and operational efficiency.</li>
<li>Assist with preparation of competitive negotiated pricing including negotiation spreadsheets.</li>
<li>Understand roofing applications and the complete product line and improve the team's ability to respond to basic product and application questions.</li>
<li>Work with Customer Service to identify roles and ensure processes are implemented by both teams that result in leads being transitioned to closed orders.</li>
<li>Lead by example and develop and maintain strong working relationships with key customers.</li>
<li>Support Marketing programs by leading the team to communicate available promotions and incentives to customers, and measuring the close on incentives offered.</li>
<li>Manage team performance, utilizing goals, development plans, reinforcing and redirecting feedback and regular assessments to achieve team goals and individual career objectives.</li>
<li>Perform other duties that may be assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Job Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or equivalent.</li>
<li>5+ years of experience in Sales and/or Marketing, preferably working with construction products or a similar complex technical product line.</li>
<li>2+ years of experience reading architectural drawings preferably tied to commercial construction.</li>
<li>Knowledge of commercial construction design, materials, pricing, fabrication and installation, preferably of metal edge products or metal edge application.</li>
<li>Ability to develop standard product pricing utilizing product line information.</li>
<li>Ability to understand sales forecasts and translate into estimating goals/strategies.</li>
<li>Success identifying issues and generating creative solutions.</li>
<li>Success utilizing process and performance metrics to achieve goals in a customer facing role.</li>
<li>Strong understanding of Microsoft Office products, specifically Excel.</li>
<li>Hands-on leadership style with success motivating and developing team.</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal and communication skills, with success using mutual understanding to achieve desired outcomes.</li>
<li>Ability to use hands to finger, handle or feel objects, tools, and controls and vision and depth perception necessary to review and analyze architectural drawings.</li>
<li>Positive attitude, good judgment and excellent time management skills.</li>
<li>High level of attention to detail and motivation.</li>
<li>Strong sense of responsibility and ownership!</li>
</ul>
