<h2>Retail Store Associate-Part-time (Seasonal)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Description:</b><br>
<b>Summary:</b> As a Retail Associate of Amain.com, you will foster business relationships with loyal hobbyists from all over the region. Striving to provide the best quality customer experience, the Retail Associate is charged with excellent customer service and interacts with anyone who visits our location. Professional engagement is essential and critical, as the Retail Associate is the forefront personnel for AMain.com. The primary objective is to ensure consumers are attended to in a quick and efficient manner, catering to their purchasing needs. Deliver WOW, have fun and create lasting memories that will inspire trust and keep shoppers within the RC Hobby.
<br><br>
<b>Essential Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Customer Interaction, building positive business relationships with everyone who walks in the establishment.</li>
<li>Offering assistance and suggestions to help shoppers make a formal purchasing decision.</li>
<li>Develop trust with our brand – building customer confidence, while creating transparency with our business model.</li>
<li>Describe merchandise and explain use or demonstrate operation and care of merchandise to customers.</li>
<li>Recommend, select, and help locate or obtain merchandise based on customer needs and desires.</li>
<li>Place special orders or call suppliers to find desired items.</li>
<li>Keep clientele informed by notifying them of preferred customer sales and future merchandise of potential interest.</li>
<li>Answer incoming telephone calls. Take special orders via phone, in an effective and timely manner.</li>
<li>Transfer customer calls to appropriate staff.</li>
<li>Outbound calls about special orders and other product related information</li>
<li>Communicate ideas for better organization, smoother workflow, suggestions, etc., to Supervisor or Manager.</li>
<li>Prepare sales slips or sales contracts, total purchases, and receive and process cash or credit payments.</li>
<li>Understand company return and exchange policies. Explain policies to customers and accept returns as needed.</li>
<li>Watch for and recognize security risks and thefts and know how to prevent or handle these situations.</li>
<li>Stock, arrange and display merchandise to promote sales.</li>
<li>Clean shelves, counters, windows, bathroom, floors and tables on a daily basis.</li>
<li>Inventory stock and requisition new stock through the Manager.</li>
<li>Contribute to team effort by accomplishing related store tasks as needed or directed by store Supervisor or Manager.</li>
<li>Suggest and promote our SD Raceway, inspiring consumers to become a member of our track program.</li>
<li>Process stock rotations to the Distribution Center, in addition to defective returns.</li>
</ul>
Non-Essential Responsibilities (Duties performed on an as-needed basis):
<br><ul>
<li>Receiving of products</li>

<li>Designing of internal product themes / displays</li>
<li>Till setup, End-Of-Day Deposit/Settlement</li>
<li>Generating PO’s for new products</li>
<li>Vendor Sourcing – Product hunting</li>
<li>Stock Takes (Inventory Management)</li>
<li>Other tasks and duties that may be assigned.</li>
</ul>
