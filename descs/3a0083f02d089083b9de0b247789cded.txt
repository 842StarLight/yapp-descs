<p><b><i>
Marketing &amp; Investor Relations (Associate, Investor Network):</i></b></p>
<p>
This is a fantastic opportunity for someone with the drive, initiative and talent to create and manage marketing materials, including presentations and special events. This internal, non-customer facing position works within the Investor Network team creating communications for external marketing and manages investor requests. Acts as the primary contact for events and with Equilibrium's third-party administrator.</p>
<p><b><i>
Responsibilities include:</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Investor communications - updating and creating presentations, reporting, and responding to investorsâ€™ requests in an accurate, consistent and timely manner.</li>
<li>Supporting Equilibrium offerings - responding to data requests and due diligence questionnaires as well as the building and maintaining the online investor portal and due diligence data rooms to reflect the most up-to-date documentation and marketing materials available.
</li>
<li>Coordinating Equilibrium events such as semi-annual Women In Asset Management dinners, Investor Round-tables, Strategy Team investor meetings, asset tours with investors and other events.</li>
<li>Primary contact and coordination person to work with our third-party administrator.</li>
<li>Maintains and administers Salesforce database, including updates and reports.</li>
