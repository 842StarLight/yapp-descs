<h2>Digital Media Buyer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>eLocal </b>is a profitable, 10 year old company whose mission is to connect consumers with local businesses, with a particular specialization in the home services and legal verticals. eLocal has been at the forefront of a changing advertising industry, being one of the only media companies in the country offering local businesses a performance-based pricing model. We use our software to route people to the appropriate service providers based on their needs.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>eLocal </b>has been at the forefront of a changing advertising industry; offering local businesses a performance-based pricing model for phone calls and email leads from prospective customers. We boast an exceptional customer retention rate, impeccable online reputation within our industry, as well as an A+ rating with the Better Business Bureau. Every day, we are selling a product that truly helps local businesses to succeed.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>What you’ll do:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>As a <b>Digital Media Buyer</b> you’ll support all of our industry verticals. The core role is campaign strategy, deployment, optimization and revenue generation so your background and skills should be paid media buying centric, you won't just analyze the data - you'll own your work; from idea creation and strategy to implementation.</p>
<p>Primary responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Manage digital media buying campaigns end-to-end, including planning, targeting, set-up, testing, execution, reporting and optimization.</li>
<li>Create paid media reporting dashboards and custom reports analyzing campaign performance in order to provide actionable recommendations and deliver continuous improvements to our marketing programs.</li>
<li>Champion continuous advancement of our digital mix and capabilities including targeting, personalization, template development in collaboration with multiple stakeholders: development teams, designers, business teams and more.</li>
<li>Stay current with best practices, strategies, and industry standards related to digital media while managing all the direct response touch points with consumers and clients whether that is Display, Social or Programmatic.</li>
<li>Take ownership of key operational initiatives to identify areas for improvement and increase profitability and do more than consult – you’ll immerse yourself in our industry verticals. You’ll drive daily performance, develop solutions and see them through as they are implemented.</li>
<li>Develop and manage new tools for measuring and understanding the health of our business operations</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Basic Qualifications</p>
<ul>
<li>BA/BS Degree</li>
<li>2+ years of digital marketing experience, including strategy, implementation, analytics and reporting. Knowledge of best practices for creative and templates, segmentation, dynamic content, testing methodologies.</li>
<li>Strong knowledge of Excel and media platforms and ability to learn technical systems and in-house tools quickly.</li>
<li>Strong project management skills: experience coordinating cross-functional teams, delivering results and meeting tight deadlines</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Preferred Qualifications</p>
<ul>
<li>Exceptional written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Knowledge of additional analytical and database tools (eg SQL, Access) a strong plus</li>
<li>Experience in lead generation, B2B &amp; B2C communications, media buying work flow and cadencing</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>What you’ll need:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>A strong analytical prowess and entrepreneurial mindset</li>
<li>Strong quantitative and financial analysis skills. You must be comfortable measuring, interpreting and making real-time decisions based on large volumes of sales and marketing performance data.</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in an analytically oriented discipline (e.g. business, economics, statistics, mathematics, etc.) or technology oriented discipline (e.g. computer science, MIS, etc.)</li>
<li>Experience building complex media models, conducting ROI analysis, and presenting data and recommendations to senior executives on a regular basis. Statistics, management consulting, or investment banking experience is more relevant to this role than traditional marketing or market research analysis.</li>
<li>The ideal candidate is proactive, has a positive attitude, and is well organized and adept at managing competing deadlines and priorities.</li>
<li>Eagerness to break records, create opportunities and take risks. Comfortable in a fast-paced and often changing environment.</li>
<li>Our employees behave like entrepreneurs, are incredibly driven, agile, goal-oriented, and always up for the challenge.</li>
<br>
</ul>
