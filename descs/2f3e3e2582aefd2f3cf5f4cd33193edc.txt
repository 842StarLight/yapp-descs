<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>REQUIREMENTS</b></h2>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>About the Role:</b></h3>
<p>You will spearhead our internal and external training and documentation efforts. We need a talented, motivated, and organized individual with a great mix of technical and communication skills. You will make sure our internal teams, including sales development representatives, sales executives, and customer success as well as our customers are well informed about our product capabilities and maximize the benefits from the product.</p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
You're a Perfect Fit If:</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>Great verbal and written communication skills with ability to author and edit presentations, product guides, documentation, etc</li>
<li>Strong understanding of technical and abstract concepts and ability to “get into the mind” of the user</li>
<li>Proven experience in designing multiple training classes</li>
<li>Adequate knowledge of learning management software</li>
<li>Self-motivated, able to work independently without close supervision</li>
<li>Organized and detail oriented, with ability to manage projects to strict timelines and deliver high quality results</li>
<li>Comfortable in a fast changing environment, which requires mental and emotional flexibility</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What you’ll own:</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>Collaborate with management to identify company training needs</li>
<li>Collaborate with our Product and Engineering teams to create and maintain training content, product guides, and documentation</li>
<li>Work closely with our partners to create and maintain training content and documentation for our joint offering</li>
<li>Build annual training programs and prepare teaching plans for new and existing customers, employees, and partners</li>
<li>Oversee and direct internal and external training sessions</li>
<li>Periodically evaluate ongoing programs to ensure that they reflect any changes</li>
<li>Stay abreast of the new trends and tools in employee development</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Experience needed:</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree, preferably in education, training, HR, or a related field.</li>
<li>A minimum of 3-5 years experience in a similar role at a B2B SaaS company</li>
<li>Certification from talent and training associations a plus</li>
</ul>
