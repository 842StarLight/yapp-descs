<p><b>Job summary<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>Under the supervision of the Vice-President, Human Resources, the selected candidate will provide strategic advice and will support the Vice President of Sales for the Americas region and its management team. The candidate will provide guidance and leadership, coach the Directors and Managers located in multiples sites throughout the USA and will be working in close collaboration with other Human Resources professionals around the world.
</p>
<p>You will promote the Human Resources agenda and best practices for that organization. You will play a role in key HR initiatives including employee and or labor relations, workforce planning, recruiting/staffing, organizational development, talent/performance management, diversity/inclusion, compensation and benefits, regulatory compliance and employee communications.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>You will be in charge of the implementation of various initiatives arising from the corporation and its global HR strategy. In addition, you will have to recruit for all Sales positions within the Americas organization.
</p>
<p>In collaboration with the Corporate Global Compensation team, you will manage the US benefits (Group insurance and 401(k)) in a cost-efficient manner and watch over developments and new legislations on that subject.
</p>
<p>You ensure that all programs are deployed and applied locally, such as performance management, salary reviews, commission plans, development plans, succession plans and ensures compliance with Human Resources policies.
</p>
<p>The Business Partner will make decisions on the application and/or interpretation of the laws, rules and regulations governing labor relations in America and when required, will take action in the settlement of issues.
</p>
<p>When required, you will perform any other task related to this position.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
