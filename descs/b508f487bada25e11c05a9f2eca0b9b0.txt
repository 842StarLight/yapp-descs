<h2>Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Description:</b><br>
The Account Manager role is a consultative sales and account management position to grow our partnership and revenue with Asurint clients. Responsibilities include working with clients to develop long-term profitable relationships and new revenue opportunities. The Account Manager manages a portfolio of clients, connecting with key business executives to solve their business issues, while managing a pipeline of new revenue opportunities. Account Managers are a liaison between our clients and internal teams to ensure the timely and successful delivery of Asurint’s services; and responsible for not only retaining client revenue but also driving revenue growth while maximizing profitability of their book of business.
<br><br>
<b>About Asurint:</b><br>
Asurint is a Cleveland, OH based national background screening company that focuses on innovation, technology &amp; service to deliver comprehensive screening solutions to businesses &amp; government entities across the country.
<br>Asurint’s proprietary data analytics platform &amp; application technologies improve the thoroughness, efficiency and timeliness of pre-employment screening for our clients.
<br><br>
Duties and Responsibilities
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Develop a precise and thorough account plan detailing specific and relevant information about all aspects of assigned accounts with the end goal of retaining and growing assigned client base.</li>
<li>Analyze industry trends and identify white space opportunities to present solutions to our clients to enhance their current background screening program and generate new business.</li>
<li>Monitor and analyze service levels on assigned account base to ensure on time delivery. Work cross functionally to maintain SLA’s and client satisfaction.</li>
<li>Responsible for portfolio management of a large book of clients.</li>
<li>Sets, tracks and exceeds sales targets, aligned with company objectives through Asurint’s defined sales cycle.</li>
<li>Demonstrates knowledge of Asurint’s products, services, processes and procedures to effectively communicate Asurint’s advantage to our clients and creates alignment between our clients and our internal teams.</li>
<li>Responsible for collaborating with the Sales and Operations teams to onboard and integrate new clients. Must be able to present solutions to ensure a timely implementation completion.</li>
<li>Develop and maintain knowledge base of client programs, processes, procedures, pricing, packages, etc.</li>
<li>Prepare and present business reviews to assigned client base on a semiannual and/or annual basis.</li>
<li>Establish Asurint as a strategic partner to our clients and key stakeholders, while delivering customer and industry intelligence to drive retention and revenue growth.</li>
<li>Possesses an in-depth understanding of the client market and their industries.</li>
<li>Identifies risks and presents developed solutions to internal and external stakeholders.</li>
<li>Monitors, evaluates and reports on account health and works proactively to ensure superior client satisfaction.</li>
<li>Drives solutions within the multifunctional teams.</li>
<li>Travels to client locations to resolve issues and collaborate on new opportunities and solutions.</li>
<li>Other duties as required.</li>
</ul>
