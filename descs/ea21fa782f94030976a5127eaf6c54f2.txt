<h2>Technical Instructional Designer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Universal Avionics, an Elbit Systems company (NASDAQ: ESLT), an industry leader in avionics systems technology, is seeking a Technical Instructional Designer to support our Customer Training department as we continue to grow.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Summary</b> – The Technical Instructional Designer will instruct and provide curriculum maintenance, development and revision support for classroom and online technical courses for the company’s advanced avionics systems. The primary audience for training is the company’s End Users, Dealers, Integrators and Installers.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b> <b>include the following. Other duties may be assigned.</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Plans technical training programs for Universal Avionics equipment with consideration of applicable regulatory guidance, and customer and company requirements.</li>
<li>Supports the instructional design process by working closely with subject matter experts, researching, and developing content for use in training materials, manuals, presentations, storyboards, test items, and exams.</li>
<li>Leads in the development and organization of web-based training content, interactive courseware, multimedia visual aids and other educational materials.</li>
<li>Participates in defining the requirements for, testing and supporting improvements of computer-based training software.</li>
<li>Coordinates with End Users, OEM partners and training centers to develop and support training programs involving Universal Avionics equipment.</li>
<li>Works collectively to improve curriculum and training processes by providing continual feedback and recommendations on existing curriculum.</li>
<li>Continually looks for new and alternate methods of instruction and recommends appropriate methods and facilitates implementation when approved.</li>
<li>Delivers structured training in the company classroom, during tradeshows, at conferences and, at the customer’s site, to include participating in both domestic and international travel.</li>
<li>Demonstrates procedures being taught, such as programming and operation of equipment</li>
<li>Observes trainees in classroom or training environment and answers trainees' questions.</li>
<li>Participates in meetings, conferences, seminars, and training sessions to obtain information useful to refining training processes and program.</li>
<li>Contributes knowledge-based articles for use in company print and digital publications.</li>
<li>Actively and positively promotes the company and its products and communicates leads to the sales team.</li>
<br>
</ul>
