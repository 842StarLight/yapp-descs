<p><b>
JOB SUMMARY</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>
This job is responsible for performing employee transaction fulfillment and escalations for Enterprise customers within HR Services Center (HRSC). Duties may include one or more of the following related administration functional teams (Health, Retirement, Compensation and Leave/Time-off) within the HRSC. This job is responsible for processing business as usual tasks (BAU) outlined in the Functional Operational plans, conduct various audits based on functional operations to ensure data integrity for internal human resources systems and external vendor systems, responsible to fulfill ad-hoc reporting requests based BAU deliverables, responsible for functional fulfillment tasks and service requests. Review and audit vendor eligibility and enrollment inquiries pertaining to benefits, retirement, leave/time-off and compensation programs. In addition, the incumbent supports functional project deliverables as needed.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Support the functional administration in one or more of the following areas; benefit, retirement, leave/time-off and compensation plans across the enterprise including fulfillment task.</li>
<li>
Maintain ongoing communication with carriers pertaining to current and former employees regarding enrollment eligibility and Service Request resolution.</li>
<li>
Generate and deliver reports for operational and performance metrics related to Functional Administration.</li>
<li>
Participate as a team member in testing of new systems, enhancements to existing systems, and production support.</li>
<li>
Support the Administration project tasks relating to benefit, retirement, compensation and leave/time-off plans across the enterprise.</li>
<li>
Other duties as assigned or requested.</li>
</ul>
