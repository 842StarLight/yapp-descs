<div><b>Job Description</b></div>
<div>The <b>Client Service Delivery Manager</b> is the single point of contact and accountability for service delivery operations and is responsible for:</div>
<ul>
<li>Managing all service management activities on behalf of the delivery organization</li>
<li>Accountability for commitments made on behalf of Accenture to the client, including service level agreements, contract scope management, demand management &amp; forecasting, and cost &amp; profitability of service delivery</li>
<li>Interacting with the Accenture Account Management team, IO Portfolio Lead, clients, delivery groups and Service Delivery teams responsible for delivering work to the client</li>
<li>Leveraging Service Delivery Managers (SDM) and towers leads to drive day-to-day operations, providing leadership, coaching and direction to delivery teams to ensure alignment with client and Accenture goals, expectations and commitments</li>
<li>Managing service delivery across all locations where services are provided, such as client locations, Global Delivery Network (GDN) and non-GDN delivery centers, external providers etc.
</li>
<li>Managing the delivery of agreed scope of service at agreed price
</li>
<li>Ensuring the feasibility of the solution and deliver the resolution by leveraging Accenture's full capabilities
</li>
<li>Working with Account Management team to commit to client(s) on behalf of Delivery organization on SLA achievement demand plan and delivery schedule
</li>
<li>Participating in strategic discussions with client and account team to provide outsourcing expertise
</li>
<li>Managing to reduce costs of standard offerings, assets, processes, technologies, licenses, facilities and subcontractors
</li>
<li>Working with delivery leaders to optimize delivery pyramid and mix and cost-to-serve including leveraging low-cost locations to optimize balance between cost and delivery targets
</li>
<li>Delivering efficiencies through standard processes and synergies
</li>
<li>Achieving defined SLA commitments and other financial/operational metrics
</li>
<li>Escalating performance issues to Account Management team
</li>
<li>Supporting sales opportunities and validate expected delivery capability
</li>
<li>Estimating of new services/contracts and sign off on commitment to proposed SLA
</li>
<li>Partnering with Mobilization leadership and consistently execute standard services
</li>
<li>Addressing supply demand gaps, confirm demand plan and delivery performance issues, communicate operational issues and continuous improvement ideas</li>
<li>Managing service quality, change orders and financial recovery plans within contract scope
</li>
<li>Working with appropriate delivery leaders to ensure resources are committed to deliver the work
</li>
<li>Managing the escalations between delivery, client and Account Management team
</li>
<li>Participating in account planning and forecasting sessions within the Account Management team</li>
<li>Supporting the achievement of contract controllable income (CCI) targets and cost-to-serve targets</li>
<li>Achieving year on year efficiency gains in conjunction with delivery service providers</li>
</ul>
