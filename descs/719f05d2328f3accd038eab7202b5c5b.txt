<b>
Preferred Qualifications</b><div>

<div>
As Senior Business Development Manager in one of the fastest-growing divisions of our company, you will drive deals with our Agency partners across all of ODC’s (Oracle Data Cloud) major products, including digital audiences, media and measurement solutions, DMP, Moat, and Grapeshot’s suite of context capabilities. Oracle provides a comprehensive and unified data solution to drive prospecting at scale, audience insights, and cross–channel marketing actions, and we are now partnering more and more deeply with Agencies to help them utilize these assets to drive ROI for their clients.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>The Senior Business Development Manager is responsible for negotiating and closing strategic deals with our Agency partners. As part of this role, you will perform market analyses to identify new strategic business opportunities and work closely with teams such as Legal, Finance, and Product to scope and implement these solutions. Specifically, as these deeper Agency partnerships are stood up and evolve, we are looking for a Senior Business Development Manager who can be a deal strategy specialist to solve complex business, organizational, and industry problems for the highest priority deals within the organization.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>Key responsibilities include:</b></div>
<div>Understand Oracle’s full suite of products and provide input on enhancements required for the Agency ecosystem</div>
<div>Identify business opportunities with Agencies and strategize with the team on the best way to structure each partnership (e.g. commercials, contract structure, product positioning etc.)</div>
<div>Identify opportunities for consistency across deals, create frameworks to be used across teams, train teams of other deal structures that may impact future deals, etc.</div>
<div>Own strategic projects that have the greatest impact for ODC and our Agency partners</div>
<div>In addition to the deal and strategic project focus horizontally across the team, you will also be responsible for the end-to-end management of Agency contracting alignment and execution</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>Skills and Qualifications:</b></div>
<div>5-7+ years’ experience negotiating contracts and closing deals</div>
<div>Experience working in technology contracting and with or within an Advertising Agency a plus</div>
<div>Excellent verbal, interpersonal, and written communication skills with the ability to lead client presentations and assist in contract negotiations</div>
<div>Demonstrated ability to self-motivate, stay focused, work in teams, be flexible, and keep a level head within a fast-paced and changing environment</div>
<div>Ability to effectively manage up and out to proactively ensure key stakeholders are up to speed</div>
<div>Always solutions driven – identifying a problem is not enough</div>
<div>Adept at navigating a complex marketplace and organization</div>
<div>Excellent organizational, time-management, and multi-tasking skills</div>
<div>Technical understanding of data, analytics, measurement, context, DMP</div>
<div>Deep understanding of general marketing practices, targeting practices, and online media</div>
<div>Advanced knowledge of online advertising techniques</div>
<div>Travel as needed, up to 30%</div>
<div>BA/BS required, JD a plus</div>

Builds long term, strategic relationships with named and vertical business alliance partners.<br>

Work is non-routine and very complex, involving the application of advanced technical/business skills in area of specialization. Demonstrated track record developing and managing Global Partnerships and driving Partner account activity. Prefer strong analytical, sales channel and marketing skills. Prefer 8 years of related experience with a secondary education in Marketing or a related field.<br>
