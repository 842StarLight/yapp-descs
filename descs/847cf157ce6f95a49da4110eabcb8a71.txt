<h2>Wealth Advisor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview<br>
</b></h2>
<div></div>
<div><div><div>
<div>Girard is a full-service, wealth management firm backed by the strength and stability of Univest Financial – a trusted institution with a 143-year history. We provide independent advice and customized wealth solutions to individuals, businesses, and charitable institutions, to help meet their investing and financial planning goals.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
This is individual is expected to serve as the primary relationship manager and investment professional for key client relationships. The relationship manager is expected to drive loyalty and focus efforts to expand wallet share and generate client referrals, incrementally.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>PRIMARY RESPONSIBILITIES:</div>
<ul>
<li>
The wealth advisor is the lead for all financial and planning services for our clients, leading the team in the delivery of services designed to retain clients and expand wallet share.</li>
<li>
Deliver exceptional service and create extraordinary experience with our clients while helping them reach their long term financial goals.</li>
<li>
Manage and execute client servicing standards by scheduling, preparing, and conducting periodic client meetings, which include the analysis and presentation for financial planning, investment recommendations and portfolio reviews.</li>
<li>
Assist business development advisor with final prospect meetings and communicate the investment process in sales presentations.</li>
<li>
Coordinate client events focusing on specific wealth topics of interest.</li>
<li>
Develop and present client focused advice solutions.</li>
<li>
Manage client portfolios to model and target allocation.</li>
<li>
Maintain up-to-date awareness of clients’ wealth management issues and needs, anticipate clients unspoken needs.</li>
<li>
Cultivate client 'next generation' and familial relationships.</li>
<li>
Provide coaching, mentoring, and development support to associate and support advisor roles</li>
<li>
Maintain records in customer relationship management system with all client interactions.</li>
<li>
Assume additional responsibilities as needed.</li>
</ul>

</ul>
