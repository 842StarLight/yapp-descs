<h2>Mortgage Loan Originator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Position Summary</p>
<p>
Originates both conforming and non-conforming residential mortgage loans. The applicant will participate in related business development activities, generating a network of referral sources.</p>
<p>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities</p>
<div>
1. Provides exceptional service to existing and prospective customers seeking residential mortgage loans, while ascertaining their needs and the programs for which they qualify.<br>
2. Conducts interviews with prospective mortgage loan applicants, pulls credit bureaus and prepares early TRID disclosures on all loan files before submitting to processing.<br>
3. Communicates daily with mortgage processors in order to stay abreast of any changes or updates to the loan file.<br>
4. Maintains prompt communication between all relative parties to the transaction.<br>
5. Remains informed of new trends and developments in the local real estate market, as well as changing rules and regulations within both the private and government sector of mortgage lending.<br>
6. Makes sales calls on potential and existing customers developing new business and increasing/retaining existing business relationships.<br>
7. Reviews documentation (i.e. credit report, appraisal, legal commitment, insurance, taxes, etc.) and ensures all information is correct before submitting to processing.<br>
8. Sells and refers bank products and services to customers and prospects.<br>
9. Review title requirements and exceptions for accuracy and conformance.<br>
10. Coordinates the loan closing with all principals to the transaction.<br>
11. Assists all bank lenders and loan assistants with questions regarding mortgage loan programs and products.<br>
12. Adheres to the BSA Policy and all other Bank policies.<br>
13. Other duties and responsibilities as assigned by the supervisor.<br>
Minimum Qualifications (Education, Experience, Skills)<br>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 3 years of mortgage loan experience.</li>
<li>Knowledge of mortgage processing, underwriting, and closing procedures.</li>
<li>Knowledge of conventional and government underwriting guidelines.</li>
<li>Knowledge of federal lending regulations governing real estate transactions</li>
<li>Ability to multitask in an ever-changing environment.</li>
<li>Good organizational skills.</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft office suite.</li>
<li>Ability to learn new software systems quickly.</li>
<li>Knowledge of Calyx a plus.</li>
<li>High School Diploma required.</li>
</ul>
