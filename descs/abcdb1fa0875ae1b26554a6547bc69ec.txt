<h2>IT Hardware Sales Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Job Description</b></p>
<p>BBC Technology Solutions is looking for a motivated, high performance IT hardware sales representative to join our growing team. If you can successfully resell IT hardware to new prospects, as well as, grow business within your existing network, then this is a great opportunity for you with endless earning potential. Work from home, anywhere in North America!</p>
<p>BBC, a Microsoft® Registered Refurbisher, provides certified, pre-owned business class computer equipment and IT LifeCycle Solutions, including data eradication, imaging, licensing, and asset auditing to the midsize marketplace.</p>
<p><b>Major Job Duties and Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop sales portfolio by contacting existing network, channel partner relationships, networking, social media, email, company generated leads, and prospecting new clients</li>
<li>Move leads through the sales process by generating interest, understanding needs/business objections, and strategically creating value to close sales</li>
<li>Communicate accurate product descriptions and use product knowledge to appropriately react to customer needs</li>
<li>Establish and maintain a trusted customer relationship throughout the IT Lifecycle with each developed account</li>
<li>Document all activities in company CRM system</li>
<li>Adopt BBC’s culture of assuring the highest level of quality, value, satisfaction, and customer service in every communication</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications and Desired Skills: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Must have at lease 5 years of previous IT hardware resale experience</li>
<li>Proven track record of sales success</li>
<li>Knowledge of computer products and specifications</li>
<li>Demonstrated proficiency in database management, Windows 10, Microsoft® Office (Excel, Word, and Outlook), LinkedIn and CRM</li>
<li>Experience with IT Asset Management and Remarketing</li>
<li>A desire to make more income as sales increase</li>
<li>Ability to travel on occasion</li>
</ul>
