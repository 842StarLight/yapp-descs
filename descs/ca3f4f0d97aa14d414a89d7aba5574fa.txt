<h2>Integrated Solutions Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>ASSA ABLOY Door Security Solutions provides end-users (healthcare facilities, schools, universities, military, commercial buildings, etc.) with unsurpassed security and life-safety solutions and the essential support services (LEED consultation, code compliance, access control system integration, product research and selection, specification writing services, technical support, etc.) to ensure well-functioning secure access control systems in the commercial building industry. This is all accomplished by combining the strengths of industry-leading door and hardware brands. Visit www.assaabloydss.com for more information.</div>
<div></div>
<div>Integrated Solutions Specialists (ISS) are business development security professionals for the suite of ASSA ABLOY Brand Electro- Mechanical and Access Control Solutions. ISS establish influential business relationships with the end user community, with security system integrators, security consultants, OEM channel partners, wholesalers and channel distributors. The ISS work as an embedded business development resource within the local ASSA ABLOY Door Security Solutions team, to promote and provide ASSA ABLOY's innovative suite of Electronic Access Control Opening solutions to our customers via our partner community.</div>
<br>
<div>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>
<div><b>Responsibilities Include:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Actively call on, train, educate, support and build ASSA ABLOY's business relationship with targeted end users, including strategic influencers, security systems integrators and related channel partners such as security/risk consultants, systems specifiers, OEM access control partners who utilize and/or buy and sell Electronic Access Control solutions, products and programs. Discover, develop, manage and grow sales of ASSA ABLOY Brand products and services with and through the partner community with a specialized focus on electro-mechanical offerings and solutions.</li>
<li>Become proficient in ASSA ABLOY's Security Continuum suite of innovative electronic and wireless access control solutions, and how they address customer challenges.</li>
<li>Provide end users, security system integrators and other customers technical, sales and marketing support, pricing information, new product data &amp; product enhancements.</li>
<li>Conduct joint end user site surveys, job walks, takeoffs, etc., with the partner community to effectively apply ASSA ABLOY solutions to the customer need.</li>
<li>Collaborate closely and transparently with ASSA ABLOY's local Door Security Solutions team.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Job Requirements:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of three years of experience selling complex security technology solutions to end users of access control system technologies, across various vertical markets.</li>
<li>Five years of practical sales and applications experience in the integration of security systems including electronic access control software and hardware, CCTV, electro-mechanical devices and related security products.</li>
<li>Experience must demonstrate consultative-selling techniques to major decision makers at all channel and target customer levels.</li>
<li>College degree preferred, High School Degree required.</li>
<li>Familiar with architectural and mechanical drawings.</li>
<li>Ability to read and comprehend wiring diagrams.</li>
<li>Member of American Society of industrial Security (ASIS) or eligible.</li>
<li>Qualified for ASIS Physical Security Professional (PSP) certification.</li>
<br>
</ul>
