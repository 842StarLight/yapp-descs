<h2>Part Sales & Customer Support Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Although you may not have heard of us, Pathfinder Manufacturing is a rock-solid, family owned and operated company based in Holland. We started 20 years ago with only 1 employee. As a growing company, today, we are an extremely knowledgeable yet humble group of 6, and we rarely see turnover. Our tenured staff stays because of our casual work environment, friendly team and professional growth opportunities, not to mention the fact that we treat our employees really well with 100% employer paid health insurance and profit sharing!</p>
<p>At Pathfinder, we support the construction industry as a parts supplier/distributor for concrete machines. Our customers are scattered through the United States and Canada and look to us for both parts and technical support. As for our location, we literally sit right in the heart of the action – among our inventory in our Holland warehouse. Each team member has their area of expertise,but as with any small business, we all wear many hats during the day. This means you get to experience something new every day, having plenty of opportunities to grow professionally.</p>
<p>Due to our continued growth, we have a new opening for a <b>Parts Sales and Customer Support</b> <b>Associate </b>to work in on our Parts Sales team. As a member of our Parts Sales team you will be the front-line support for our customers, Monday - Friday from 8:30 am - 5:30 pm. There are occasional Saturday hours once fully trained.</p>
<p>Daily, our <b>Parts Sales and Customer Support</b> <b>Associate </b>will:</p>
<ul>
<li>Manage our Parts email inbox and main phone line– personally handle routine requests and escalate the rest</li>
<li>Respond in a timely manner to routine requests with professional, effective communication - remembering that follow through is key</li>
<li>Enter orders into Sage (our order entry and inventory management system) with impeccable attention to <b><i>all</i></b> details</li>
<li>See basic orders through the order fulfillment process by physically pulling product and prepping basic documentation for shipping</li>
<li>Build your knowledge and troubleshooting capabilities when it comes to electrical, hydraulic, and pneumatic systems</li>
<li>When the need arises, work with our team to tear down, inspect and reassemble industrial machinery</li>
<li>Occasionally act as backup to our Shipping Department</li>
</ul>
<p>A successful <b>Parts Sales and Customer Support</b> <b>Associate </b>will already have foundational business skills. They will be a critical thinker who can anticipate needs and be a creative problem solver to meet customer needs. We believe they will also have the following to be successful in the position and in our Company:</p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 5 years of experience providing exceptional customer service, inside sales and/or inventory management – <b><i>required</i></b>
</li>
<li>Unwavering attention to details (every last one)– <b><i>required</i></b>
</li>
<li>Strong communication skills (verbal and written)– <b><i>required</i></b>
</li>
<li>Mechanically inclined mind – <b><i>required</i></b>
</li>
<li>Technical abilities (computer literate with Word, Excel, Outlook, and ability to learn enterprise software systems) – <b><i>required, </i></b><i>Bonus points if you have CAD experience</i>
</li>
<li>Valid Driver’s License and the ability to maintain – <b><i>required</i></b>
</li>
<li>Completed college degree – <b><i>not a requirement but preferred</i></b>
</li>
</ul>
