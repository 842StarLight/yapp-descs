<h2>Drafter/Detailer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Position Summary: </b><br>Responsible for detailing and drafting of belt, screw, and drag conveyors, and bucket elevators. Maintains direct contact with the design engineer. Generates general arrangement drawings for belt, screw, and drag conveyors, and bucket elevators. Generates fabrication detail drawings for all equipment manufactured by CSC. Interact as necessary with plant supervisor to provide clarity of detail during fabrication.</p>
<p>Job Duties:</p>
<ul>
<li>Works directly with design engineer to produce general arrangement drawings from sketches, existing drawings, and electronic images for equipment to be manufactured by CSC for customer approval.</li>
<li>Creates assembly and parts details from approved general arrangement drawing suitable for fabrication, welding and fitting, and final assembly.</li>
<li>Assigns part numbers to all assemblies, buy-outs, etc. that are required during the detailing process.</li>
<li>Provides final raw materials list to purchasing department in the format required for ordering from vendors.</li>
<li>Provides final buy-out list of materials required per equipment assembly to purchasing department for ordering from specified vendor(s).</li>
<li>Maintains complete, readily accessible files and records in the standard format designated by CSC.</li>
<li>Works with Operations, Sales/Marketing, and Administration to provide design and cost control of all products and processes.</li>
<li>Performs other duties as assigned by Supervisor.</li>
<li>Maintains the confidentiality of all information processed.</li>
<li>Performs related duties as assigned.</li>
<li>Regular and consistent attendance is required.</li>
</ul>
<p>Qualifications/Skills Required:</p>
<ul>
<li>Experience in operation CAD and SolidWorks computer drafting software.</li>
<li>Ability to read, interpret, and use applicable standards and customer supplied documentation.</li>
<li>1-3 years of experience in the design and detailing of mechanical components and mechanical equipment assemblies</li>
<li>Associates’ degree or completion of a technical trade school in CAD and SolidWorks computer drafting software.</li>
</ul>
