<h2>Sales Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Location: Various US Cities</p>
<p><b>
Job Description:</b><br>
Sales Executives are responsible for prospecting, identifying opportunities, building new business relationships and generating revenue for advertising media placements. Focusing on growing existing accounts and acquiring new clients, this position will be primarily placing outbound sales calls (to include cold-calls), conducting face-to-face meetings, making sales presentations and prospecting.</p>
<p><b>
Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>3+ years successful sales experience</li>
<li>Outstanding written, verbal and presentation skills</li>
<li>“Hunter” instincts</li>
<li>Drive and desire to win and thrive in a fast-paced challenging environment</li>
<li>Self starter with exceptional time management skills</li>
<li>Professionalism is imperative</li>
<li>Undergraduate Degree required</li>
</ul>
