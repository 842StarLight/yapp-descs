<b>Your mission:</b><br>
-------------------
<br><br>
In this role, you will be directly responsible for prospecting new business through heavy telephone sales (this is not a cold call, you will be calling leads who have filled out an application or have responded through one of our marketing campaign). Success in this role is measured by hitting and exceeding monthly sales goals, achieving daily metrics in a competitive yet collaborative environment.
<br><br>
---------------------
<br><br>
<b>What you'll be doing:</b><br>
---------------------
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Prospecting new business through heavy telephone sales</li>
<li>Diligently handle incoming calls -- understand the customer's needs, overcome objections, handle rejection in stride and ultimately close the sale</li>
<li>Guide prospects through the application to funding process</li>
<li>Become an expert in the SMB landscape, you will understand the cash flow trends and successfully coach SMB owners on how to grow their revenues</li>
</ul>
------------------------------
<br><br>
<b>What we're looking for in you:</b><br>
------------------------------
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Flexibility/Adaptability: Thrives in fast paced, start-up environments. Adjusts quickly to changing priorities.</li>
<li>Empathy: Demonstrates that you genuinely care about others.</li>
<li>Openness to New Ideas: Enjoys learning and seeks feedback. Comfortable with a rapidly-changing workspace.</li>
<li>Strategic Thinking: Able to see and communicate the big picture in an inspiring way; problem solver.</li>
<li>High Standards: Expects personal performance and team performance to be nothing short of the best.</li>
<li>Communication: Ability to communicate information, ideas, and concepts clearly.</li>
<li>Proactivity: Actively finds ways to make improvements at work. Has a positive "can do" attitude.</li>
<li>Focus: Ability to work effectively in an open workplace with frequent distractions.</li>
</ul>
---------------------
<br><br>
<b>What you should have:</b><br>
---------------------
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Previous sales experience in financial services or selling to SMBs</li>
<li>Telephone sales experience</li>
<li>History of achieving and consistently exceed monthly sales goals</li>
<li>Has a desire to grow personally and professionally</li>
<li>Strong negotiating and prospecting skills</li>
<li>Experience selling software, platform and/or SaaS solutions is preferred</li>
</ul>
