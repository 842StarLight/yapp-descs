 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Sr. Sales Engineer</b></h1>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Remote Opportunity in Seattle, Washington (or Remote Northern Cal or Portland)</b></h3>
<br>
<b>
The purpose of the Sr. Sales Engineer is to provide dedicated sales support to the field and inside sales teams:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>By proactively identifying &amp; addressing customer‘s technical needs and objections, to drive sales.</li>
<li>
By acting as the primary bridge to identify &amp; remove process problems early between Sales and Operations</li>
<li>
By protecting NICE inContact, as an organization, from projects where technical issues outweigh value to NICE inContact by being able to delineate between technically feasible and technically problematic opportunities</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
As a Sr. Sales Engineer, a Typical Day Might Include the Following:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Assist sales team through preparation and delivery of technical presentations and statements of work by matching specific client business requirements into effective technical solutions - with a focus on NICE inContact’s solutions.</li>
<li>
Maintain high level knowledge of competitive product lines, especially as it relates to technical strengths and weaknesses of the competitor‘s products and what strategy NICE inContact should develop to counter.</li>
<li>
Maintain high-level knowledge of all other NICE inContact products/services, competitive product lines, and industry through self-education and NICE inContact’s resources.</li>
<li>
Proactively and effectively identify customer needs and overcome objections to proposed solutions.</li>
<li>
Perform technical benefits assessment for customers by gaining detailed understanding of their environment.</li>
<li>
Provide continuous knowledge transfer to the sales force regarding technical aspects and benefits of the NICE inContact’s™ solutions.</li>
<li>
Support new and existing sales efforts via technical discovery, product demonstrations and presentations.</li>
<li>
Properly document and provide feedback to sales team.</li>
<li>
Assist in the preparation of responses to RFP’s and deliver technical and sales oriented presentations to customer’s technical staff and senior management.</li>
<li>
Meet with customers to understand their current technical environment, key business issues, and future benefits that could be realized through NICE inContact’s advanced contact center technology deployments.</li>
<li>
Work with the Sales Support to ensure orders are completed in an accurate and timely fashion.</li>
<li>
Create, based on customer needs, a detailed Solutions Overview (SO) document for each new customer which will be the basis from which all pre-turn up work will be performed. Instruct customer on costs associated with the work.</li>
<li>
Build and maintain relationships with financial, subject and technical decision makers and influencers in prospect base.</li>
<li>
Position will require significant travel up to 75%.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
To Land This Gig You'll Need:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in Business Information Systems, Computer Science, Communications or related field or equivalent work experience required.</li>
<li>
6+ years direct sales engineering experience in contact centers, telecommunications or software sales</li>
<li>
Strong sales skills</li>
<li>
Relationship management abilities</li>
<li>
Superior communication skills</li>
<li>
High integrity</li>
<li>
Experience in fast paced environment</li>
<li>
Experience meeting customer time constraints</li>
<li>
Ability to define problems, collect data, establish facts and draw valid conclusions</li>
<li>
Analytical/Quantitative abilities required. Must be able to interpret and explain financial and statistical information</li>
<li>
Ability to negotiate and up sell products and services</li>
<li>
In-depth knowledge of technical support issues related to Internet, video, and telephony. A hands-on background in engineering, technical support or field maintenance is desirable</li>
<li>
Broad knowledge of call center technologies, call traffic analysis, automatic call distributions and interactive voice response systems</li>
<li>
Excellent interpersonal and communication skills including verbal, written, negotiation and communications.</li>
<li>
Valid driver‘s license.</li>
</ul>
