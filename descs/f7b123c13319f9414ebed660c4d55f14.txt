<p><b>What makes the SimilarWeb Enterprise Sales team awesome?</b></p>
<ul>
<li>We love market intelligence! At SimilarWeb you have the opportunity to work with the world's biggest brands and offer a solution that has the power to have a huge impact on their business!</li>
<li>As part of our global team you will work alongside the industry’s top talent where you will have the chance to not only learn but also grow professionally</li>

<p><b>As an Enterprise Sales Executive at SimilarWeb you will:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>The Enterprise Sales Executive will be responsible for generating new revenue for SimilarWeb: creating, cultivating and developing accounts and increasing market share. S/he will be expected to exceed revenue goals by planning and executing successful sales within North America’s largest Enterprises in the digital world.</li>
<li>On a typical day you will:</li>
<ul>
<li>Meet and exceed monthly sales quota through outbound leads - strategically selling the company’s various propositions to new prospects in a consultative manner</li>
<li>Manage the entire sales cycle from prospecting, discovery, solution proposing, presentation, negotiating and closing</li>
<li>Work closely with the Sales Development and Marketing teams to target appropriate accounts</li>
<li>Build cross functional relationships within the prospect in order to penetrate the account further by focusing on director/ C-level engagement</li>
<li>Work with channel partners to identify, support and close opportunities</li>
<li>Develop accurate forecasts and manage sales activity in CRM (Salesforce.com)</li>
</ul>
</ul>
<p><b>What will I bring to the team?</b></p>
<ul>
<li>An established network of contacts</li>
<li>High integrity, energy and dedication. We are looking for uncommonly good sales professionals with a highly collaborative, value-driven, honest, and direct approach to delivering customer success</li>
<li>Excellent communication and presentation skills. Ability to persuade, lead, and confidently handle objections and resolve customer objections</li>
<li>Solid experience. Minimum 5 years online sales experience in digital software sales</li>
<li>Documented success in SaaS, enterprise software sales. The ideal candidate will have experience client-direct sales, and be highly successful at running consultative sales processes in large, complex prospect organizations. Experience in presenting to and interacting with senior management of prospect organizations.</li>
<li>Ideally a deep understanding of current digital ad technologies and online marketing including display advertising, SEO, SEM, Affiliates, Social, Email Marketing and data solutions</li>
<li>Ideally experience of working in a hyper-growth or start-up type of business</li>
<li>Ideally experience of taking a new, disruptive technology to market from a new sales perspective</li>
</ul>
