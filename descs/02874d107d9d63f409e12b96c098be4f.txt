<p>Secure Code Warrior is the global secure coding company that makes software development better and more secure. Our vision is to empower developers to be the first line of defense in their organization by making security highly visible and providing them with the skills and tools to write secure code from the beginning.</p>

<p></p>
<p>

Inside Sales Manager, Portland</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
We’re searching for an Inside Sales Manager to join our global sales team to build + guide our ISRs; responsible for generating, developing, and closing new business across the SCW platform. As our first ISR Manager for the Americas you will join your inaugural cohort of Portland sales warriors, responsible for educating and selling our platform to small and medium-sized enterprises. SCW has a strong community of developers, users, and customers, with a wide variety of existing security needs, all hungry to learn more about SCW and how it can continue to improve their developers skills within their organizations.</p>
