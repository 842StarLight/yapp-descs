<h2>Translation Business Development Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Precision Language &amp; Graphics, Inc. (www.plg-online.com), a fast growing Schaumburg, IL-based translation firm has an immediate opening for a full-time position in business development and sales.</p>
<p>Responsibilities:
</p>
<ul>
<li>New account development: Follow through on company generated leads via cold calls and emails, generate proposals and deliver value proposition</li>
<li>Identify further opportunities for leads via internet research, social media, trade shows, referrals, networking, various professional directories and personal investigation</li>
<li>Manage the entire sales pipeline, from prospecting through post-sales follow up</li>
<li>Involve key co-workers with larger deals or when needed to ensure full customer satisfaction</li>
<li>Cultivate the relationship with the client ensuring that we are meeting quality, schedule and price commitments with each project</li>
<li>Take an active role in learning about the client’s industry, goals, needs and collaborate with co-workers to help meet them – you are not pushing sales, rather finding solutions to your client’s multilingual needs</li>
<li>Find and identify new business ground and possibilities and convert them into a viable business avenue</li>
<li>Actively involves in company’s marketing activities including newsletter publication, company website enhancement, customer education and social media participation</li>
</ul>
<p>Requirements:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Fluent English speaker</li>
<li>Sales-driven career path</li>
<li>Minimum Bachelor’s degree preferred</li>
<li>Excellent spoken and written English communication skills</li>
<li>Outstanding telephone skills</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Experience in sales is strongly preferred, including internships and part time jobs</li>
<li>Translation industry experience a plus but not required</li>
<li>Highly motivated and assertive</li>
<br>
</ul>

<p><b>About PLG</b></p>
<p>
Established in 1994, Precision Language &amp; Graphics, Inc. (PLG) has provided translation and localization services to companies worldwide. PLG’s project management and technical team provides a full line of language services, including document translation, interpretation, website and software localization, subtitling, voiceovers, product label compliance review and international business consulting. During the past 5 years, PLG has seen rapid growth and market expansion as a result of its customer oriented business model and technology driven approach. We firmly believe employees are the most critical asset of a growing company. Leveraging our small business nature, we intend to hire the most talented candidates, who:</p>
<ul>
<li>Prefer to work with a close, supportive and friendly workforce</li>
<li>Desire to have access to and influence on all aspects of the business</li>
<li>Appreciate hard work and getting recognized for it
</li>
<li>Want to grow their career with a growth-oriented company</li>
<br>
</ul>
