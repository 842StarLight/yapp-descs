<h2>Lead IT Developer - Salesforce and B2B Commerce</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Come help us lead the digital transformation here at PepsiCo: a dynamic environment with cutting edge technologies. Where winning with purpose matched with a passion for creating products and programs of lasting value are expected and rewarded.<br>
<br>
PepsiCo has an aggressive goal to increase share of product sales and revenue via B2B and B2C eCommerce channels. In support of this goal PepsiCo IT has embarked on a modernization journey by utilizing salesforce commerce cloud (CloudCraze) platform to support the B2B commerce needs. Furthermore, a commerce integration hub based on java microservices is being developed to enable product information management(catalog), integration with a variety of eCommerce partners and third-party logistics companies, provide real-time visibility to product inventory and stock levels, allow for flexible orchestration and tracking of product fulfillment.<br>
<br>
The eCommerce Platform Delivery Sr. Specialist will be responsible for managing the design, development, and delivery of the Salesforce commerce cloud commerce portal and integration with the commerce integration hub. This position will work with key stakeholders to understand requirements, lead eCommerce implementations providing both oversight and hands on contributions to the software design, development and integration on the Salesforce platform.<br>
<br>
If you like working in an entrepreneurial environment while at the same time having the backing and resources of a large fortune 50 company, then this could be the ideal opportunity for you. You will be rewarded with an explosive growth opportunity as we forge the platform to power PepsiCo’s digital transformation.<br>
<br>
<br>
