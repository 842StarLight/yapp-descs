<h2>Global Partner Enablement Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><i>We are not accepting resumes from third parties and sponsorships are not provided for this role. This position also requires the employee to be on-site.</i></p>
<p><b><i>This is a direct-hire opportunity.</i></b></p>
<p>We’re seeking a <b>Global Partner Enablement Manager</b> This individual support execution of our global partner enablement program focused on North America and UK/EMEA. This is a fast-paced, high-growth startup environment -- we are looking for smart, high energy candidates who are looking to make a big impact, and who want to work alongside a great team to do so. We work closely together and put in startup hours, so you must be a team player!</p>
<p><b>Essential Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Engagement with senior management both in the company and our partners to grow the company’s partnerships through technical leadership</li>
<li>Work closely with the solution engineering team, product management, product marketing, and sales enablement team to identify technical enablement needs and develop training solutions</li>
<li>Collaborate with the global head of sales enablement and internal stakeholders to ensure timely and accurate deliverables</li>
<li>Assessing partner capability across business development, technical and delivery domains and maintain accurate records of current status</li>
<li>Increasing partner sales, solutions and delivery capability and competence through construction and measurement of enablement plans in collaboration with partners and the company’s partner management</li>
<li>Mentoring and assisting partners on Pre-Sales engagements through post-sale success planning, working closely with partner account managers, the company’s sales, services and customer success</li>
<li>Enable the company’s partners by delivering business level partner certifications, workshops and applied learning</li>
<li>Create joint partner enablement plans in collaboration with partner counterparts</li>
<li>Engage regularly with key partners to monitor enablement process, new requirements and future goals</li>
<li>Respond to concerns when partners fail to deliver to the expectation of customers when implementing the company.</li>
<li>Create a Partner Enablement Roadmap and help ensure that new partners are engaged in activities outlined in the roadmap</li>
<li>Execute on virtual and face-to-face training delivery (eLearning/virtual/traditional classroom)</li>
<li>Familiarity with software development, coding, and scripting.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Education/Experience: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Salesforce Lightning Platform Skills (Apex, Workflow, Customization, Configuration etc).</li>
<li>5 years of experience as a Consultant (Salesforce or SI), Sales Engineer or Developer.</li>
<li>Formal Salesforce certification is a bonus.</li>
<li>Good working knowledge of the software sales cycle, and partner account management and technical enablement to be able to effectively enable our partners.</li>
<li>You will have experience with one or more of the following:</li>
<li>SaaS Integration</li>
<li>API Management</li>
<li>Understanding of ERP, CRM and related application software domains</li>
<li>Strong partner management and interpersonal skills with the ability to develop relationships and build existing partner channels</li>
<li>Ability to prioritize and handle multiple requests concurrently, with the flexibility to adapt seamlessly to rapid shifts in direction/strategy</li>
<li>Ability to identify problems, quickly devise feasible solutions, disseminate the appropriate information to relevant internal partners and ultimately bring about a timely resolution</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills with an ability to tailor messages for both business and technical audiences; comfort in addressing informal team meetings and delivering prepared presentations to executives/external audiences</li>
<li>Experience collaborating with cross-functional teams on technical projects, specifically in translating business objectives/priorities for product management and engineering colleagues</li>
<li>Willingness to travel (10-15%)</li>
<li>Bachelor's Degree preferred</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Salary: $130,000.00 to $150,000.00 /year</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul><li>Consultant (Salesforce or SI), Sales Engineer or Developer: 5 years (Preferred)</li></ul>
