<h2>Network and Security Engineer</h2>

<div></div>
<div>
<div><div><div>
<p>PCM is currently looking for a Network Engineer to joing our Professional Services Organization to support some of our largest customers in Southern California. This is a hybrid role that will focus 50% on pre-sales and 50% on post sales.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>50% delivery/50% pre-sales</li>
<li>
Primarily focused on delivering long-term project-based, customer engagements.</li>
<li>
Act as a customer-facing Networking SME supporting PCM’s Account Managers and their Enterprise customers.</li>
<li>
Design and implement business-driven customer solutions involving Networking, Data Center, Security and SD-WAN</li>
<li>
Responsible for deploying, and maintaining security infrastructure including next generation firewall, monitoring solutions, correlation tools, and identity and access control systems in a highly controlled environment</li>
<li>
Assist in developing integration of network and security focused solutions into an enterprise network</li>
<li>
Assist various teams to ensure network appliances and enterprise services are configured with the correct security posture to support business requirements</li>
<li>
Provides technical recommendations to optimize performance</li>
</ul>
