<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview</b></h3>
<p>The “Green Power Guy” (www.GreenPowerGuy.com) is a truly innovative community outreach B2C and B2B online platform, which enables clients to connect with GPS and ultimately buy our solar energy products and services. The Online Marketing Manager is responsible to establish this platform as a successful sales channel by all means of online marketing, internet optimization, and social networking. Content creation and content marketing are especially important.</p>

<li>Driving content management as our internal Subject Matter Expert (SME)</li>
