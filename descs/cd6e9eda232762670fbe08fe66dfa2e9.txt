<h2>AR Implementation Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>RCM Enterprise Services™ (RCM) was founded to address the high costs and complexities that are often associated with accounts receivable management. RCM's services are designed to address the challenges of revenue cycle management, reducing DAR (days in account receivable) and improving cash flow.<br>
<br>
<b>POSITION SUMMARY:</b><br>
SoftA/R is a billing and accounts receivable application specifically designed for laboratories, clinics and hospitals.<br>
<br>
We are seeking an analytically minded self-starter with medical/hospital or clinical laboratory billing experience that can easily adapt to the high demands of managing multiple projects simultaneously. As the AR Implementation Specialist, you will be actively engaged with clients throughout all phases of their project. The position requires effectively communicating with the client, analyzing workflows and processes, providing guidance on system configuration, and conducting training remotely as well as at the customer site.<br>
<br>
<b>DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Provides professional product installation and support services to an assigned number of sites resulting in smooth, well-coordinated implementations, which meet targeted live dates</li>
<li>As required, may also assist with sales related activities including product demonstration and RFP support</li>
<li>Assists the Implementation Team Leader in coordinating all enterprise activities related to the system implementation to ensure that contractual obligations are met and that the implementation is performed within the defined project scope and time frame</li>
<li>Provides telephone consultation and support services for clients during the implementation process</li>
<li>Provides on-site support during system live</li>
<li>Provides on-site training and system file building to assure a thorough understanding of overall capabilities</li>
<li>Responsible for establishing complete familiarity with the project implementation work plan</li>
<li>Ensures that the system is designed to utilize existing capabilities as they relate to client requirements</li>
<li>Complies with all approved procedures, directives and guidelines</li>
<li>Performs other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>
<b>
JOB SPECIFICATIONS:</b><br>
<br>
<b>Education Required:</b><br>
A Bachelor’s Degree in Accounting, Computer Science, Healthcare Administration or related fields is preferred. Bachelor of Science degree in Medical Terminology is highly desirable. Must have at least an Associate's Degree.<br>
<br>
<b>Experience Required:</b><br>
Broad understanding of clinical laboratory environment and operations is essential. Five years of previous experience in the usage of file building and troubleshooting of an accounts receivable information system and/or three years of previous implementation, training, and billing in a hospital or medical office setting. Previous experience in a service related field, clinical laboratory, or related computer position desired.<br>
<br>

<li>Excellent communication skills (oral and written)</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Excellent analytical and problem solving skills</li>
<li>Should also possess excellent planning and organizational skills</li>
<li>Must have the ability to work effectively under stress</li>
<li>Intermediate computer skills required</li>
</ul>
