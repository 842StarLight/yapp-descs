<p>
Are you a digital marketing specialist with a technical background or quality-minded engineer who's is passionate about digital marketing and analytics tools? Do you believe in the power of data and analysis to drive informed decision making? Are you looking for an organization focused on delivering value? The NetSuite Customer Success team helps customers bring their eCommerce solutions to life. We launch hundreds of customer B2B and B2C sites across the global and lead integrations with customer’s marketing, brand management, loyalty, and in-store solutions to deliver engaging experiences. We build web and mobile eCommerce applications on top of the NetSuite ERP and CRM platform.</p>
<p>
Now, as part of Oracle, NetSuite is facing some new and exciting opportunities to help customers in successfully adopting our solutions. We are looking for creative, motivated, and innovative individuals to bring new approaches to our Digital Experiences practice to ensure successful delivery of our customer’s project and instill a quality-centric culture that strives for continuous improvement.</p>
<p>
In this role, you will bridge NetSuite solutions and various digital marketing, advertising, web analytics, and social media platforms to help customer gain the most business value from our platform. You will work with customers and other internal delivery teams to launch websites, consult on how best to achieve business goals, and design new offerings to help customer unlock the value of their investment.</p>
<p>
As a Digital Experience Consultant, you will partner with project delivery teams, support, sales, business partners, and product development to affect day to day customer experience and influence the strategic evolution of NetSuite Cloud platforms.</p>
<p>
You will be asked to bring your expert knowledge of technology and best practices to find elegant solutions in our daily delivery, but also shape our culture keep us on the trajectory of improvement. Here are some of the qualities we appreciate on our team:</p>

Customer Obsession: understand your customers and an advocate for them.</li>
<li>
Outcomes Driven: we all have our part, but our part is never more important than the overall goal.</li>
<li>

<li>
Fearless: Not afraid to ask a question or jump onto a big idea</li>
<li>
See the Whole: understand the context at hand, and how it connects everything around it</li>
<li>
Balanced Open minded and Assertive: be open to new idea or input from others, but balance when a decision must be made to move forward</li>
<li>
Many Hats: a consultant, a developer, a leader, a facilitator, mentor/coach</li>
<li>
Tech Savvy: we are a technology company, a passion and comfort to get in deep with our solutions or create your own to help improve our agility and engagement</li>
</ul>
