<h2>Content & Digital Marketing Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><b>About Little Earth:</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Little Earth is a small Pittsburgh-based accessory and apparel manufacturer with a nationwide presence. Our Company is a nationally recognized women's consumer product licensee and manufacturer for the NFL, MLB, NHL, MLS, and NCAA. At Little Earth, we make women's accessories, apparel and pet items for fashionable fans. We are currently looking for high-energy, collaborative, team-players to join our growing company!</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Who we are looking for:</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Little Earth is seeking a creative, pro-active and analytical Content &amp; Digital Marketing Associate. In this position, you will be responsible for producing and managing all content and digital marketing efforts including content creation, file management, digital marketing campaign implementation and monitoring. Our business relies heavily on e-commerce sales and the Content &amp; Digital Marketing Associate is critical to our continued success! You can find out more about our current product lines on our website: www.littlearth.com.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
What you will do:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Create content for all sales and marketing channels online and print.</li>
<li>
Photograph and edit blank goods and decoration methods, prepare for mass production or data merge with team logos.</li>
<li>
Prepare secondary images, model shots, and product use videos as needed.</li>
<li>
Upload, organize and maintain artwork and image files on server and vendor folders as appropriate. Create, edit, and update artwork &amp; packaging files per SKU per league.</li>
<li>
Communicate to required parties when images are finalized and ensure all images and artwork are up-to-date and approved.</li>
<li>
Obtain approval for all product images by management for each commerce channel (website, image site, Amazon, social media, etc).</li>
<li>
Produce artwork as needed to convey concepts for product development and/or sales purposes.</li>
<li>
Maintain company's website including uploading new items and images, editing and maintenance, troubleshooting and site app up-keep.</li>
<li>
Design and develop relevant content for website homepage including season changes and sales.</li>
<li>
Manage all digital marketing efforts including social media, email blasts, website, and online advertisements, etc. Create content (images, copy, links, product collections), update/segment subscriber list, test email campaigns, track sales and analyzing various campaign results.</li>
<li>
Curate site reviews and respond to comments.</li>
<li>
Manage advertising campaigns including constant monitoring, timely updates for team wins, creating ads for product development, developing ad budgets and monitoring ad spends.</li>
<li>
Coordinate photography, layout, and copy for catalogs, flyers, and special presentation projects.</li>
<li>
Research, propose and implement technology upgrades to automate processes and improve efficiencies.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
What you need:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor's Degree in Marketing, Graphic Design, related field or comparable work experience required.</li>
<li>
Minimum of 3 years of experience in a creative marketing role required.</li>
<li>
Experience using Advanced Photoshop, Illustrator, Excel, Google apps for business preferred.</li>
<li>
Excellent written and verbal communication skills.</li>
<li>
Strong creative and technical skills.</li>
<li>
Strong technical and pattern design skills in Adobe Illustrator &amp; Photoshop.</li>
<li>
Ability to observe, learn, and incorporate new ideas quickly.</li>
</ul>
