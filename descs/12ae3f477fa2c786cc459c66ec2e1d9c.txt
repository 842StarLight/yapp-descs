<h2>Manager of Market Intelligence and Strategy</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Position Overview</b><b>:</b></p>
<p>
Responsible for full market intelligence associated with specific geographies. This knowledge will translate to management level strategies in partnership with division Ops teams (primarily in the capacity of land acquisition, sales, product positioning, business/financial planning, etc.).</p>
<p>
This position must be located in one of the following locations: Dallas, San Antonio, Austin</p>
<p><b>
Relocation, if necessary, may be considered.</b></p>
<p><b>
Duties and Responsibilities: (Including but not limited to)</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Primary contact for Ops teams in several division(s)
</li>
<li>Managing and completing Annual Market Positioning Study for the assigned division(s).</li>
<li>Managing and completing feasibility studies for all potential land transactions.</li>
<li>In-depth fieldwork to understand market dynamics, the competitive environment, consumer preferences and submarket perceptions.</li>
<li>Monitoring of all competitive and market activity.</li>
<li>Strategic decisions based on divisions’ competitive market analyses.</li>
<li>Other research projects (i.e. qualitative and quantitative market research, survey development and implementation, developing and moderating focus groups, division level community reviews and repositionings, etc.) as may be required.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Relevant Experience: 5+ years of market research, market intelligence, and/or strategy capacity in the new construction/home building industry and/or real estate consulting industry.</li>
<li>Education: Bachelor’s degree (minimum) from an accredited college or university required with a preferred background in business, real estate, market research, economics, urban planning or construction management.</li>
<li>A valid driver’s license and insurance is required should an automobile be utilized for travel as required by this position.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Skills and Knowledge:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Knowledge of local economic activity and conditions.</li>
<li>Self-motivated and able to work well with little or no direct supervision.</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task/prioritize several tasks without compromising quality of work or deadlines.</li>
<li>Proven organization skills in a high paced work environment.</li>
<li>Ability to review, analyze and interpret information as well as trouble-shoot to solve problems.</li>
<li>Knowledge of business English and ability to communicate patiently and professionally with both internal and external customers both orally and in writing.</li>
<li>Ability to adapt in a changing workplace.</li>
<li>High proficiency with Outlook, Excel, Word, Power Point, Google Earth (or other mapping software) and ability to learn new programs as needed.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Typical Physical and Mental Demands:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Must be able to read, write, and speak fluently in English.</li>
<li>Must be able to perform calculations.</li>
<li>Employees in the position are required to be able to hear and have vision sufficient to utilize all office equipment.</li>
<li>Use of hands and fingers is required to utilize standard office equipment.</li>
<li>Requires occasional lifting up to 20 pounds.</li>
</ul>
<p>These physical requirements are representative of those required to successfully perform the essential functions of this position. Reasonable accommodation will be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform these functions if requested.</p>
<p><b>
Working Conditions:</b></p>
<p>
Work is split between driving to field locations to visit construction job sites and new home sales offices as well as being in a typical office environment using standard office equipment. Fieldwork may present hazards typical when driving an automobile and will also require standing, walking, and navigating stairs or uneven surfaces. Office work is typically sedentary in nature.</p>
<p>
Fast paced environment subject to numerous schedule and priority changes and short notice activity.</p>
<p>
Approximately 50 percent of the job will require out of market travel, by automobile or commercial airlines.</p>
