<h2>Leasing Agent</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Our company is looking for a self motivated and energetic Leasing Agent to work for our multi unit apartment community. This candidate should be professional, team player, have strong marketing skills and is motivated to provide excellent customer service and also has leasing experience.</p>
<p>You will support the Property Manager with daily operations of the property and must be able to work some weekend hours.</p>
<p>Key responsibilities include:<br>- Assist in answering phones<br>- Greeting and welcoming residents and prospective residents<br>- Schedule showings of apartments within the community.<br>- Processing leasing applications.<br>- Participate and assist in the move-in process to new residents.<br>- Work with maintenance to handle apartment turnovers, move-ins and move-outs and work order requests.<br>- Complete and maintain availability and marketing material for prospects.<br>- Some weekend hours required.</p>
<p><b>Qualification requirements: </b><br>- Previous experience in leasing, sales and/or customer service preferred<br>- Ability to work both independently and in a team environment<br>- Customer-service oriented.<br>- Written and verbal communication skills.<br>- Experience with property management tools, including Excel, Microsoft Word, Outlook and property management software.</p>
<p><i><b>We offer competitive hourly pay plus commissions; Medical and Dental coverage and 401k match.</b></i></p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Experience:</p>

<li>Customer Service: 1 year (Preferred)</li>
<li>Marketing: 1 year (Preferred)</li>
</ul>
