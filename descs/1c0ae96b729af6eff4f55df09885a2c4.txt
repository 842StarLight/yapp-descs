<h2>System Director - Creative Services</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div><b>Role Purpose:</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>Are you seeking to put your creative talents to work for one of the original social good organizations that has a legacy of caring and service? Have you led teams to do breakthrough creative for leading consumer brands, but you want a career with more purpose than simply adding to your portfolio? Let’s talk. We’d like to share our vision for transforming health care for our communities and explore how you can help amplify our brand into the hearts and minds of our coworkers and customers. You’ll be reporting to the System Vice President of Marketing &amp; Communications at SSM Health and be responsible for leading a team to conceptualize, develop and measure omni-channel campaigns with a strong focus on digital and retail. You should be a digitally savvy creative with a strong background in brand identity, brand architecture and integrated marketing campaigns that include content marketing and CRM/Marketing Automation.
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Responsibilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>Build, lead and review work of the creative team in the production of all web, print, and digital marketing collateral.</div></li>
<li><div>Lead and manage content strategy for both small-scale and large-scale projects, harnessing strategy, digital and brand design, compelling content and technology.</div></li>
<li><div>Collaborate with the marketing and sales departments to develop marketing plans, analyze results and identify opportunities.</div></li>
<li><div>Develop internal marketing campaigns that translate marketing objectives units into clear and motivating creative strategies.</div></li>
<li><div>Create a team environment that facilitates mutual respect, partnerships across functional teams and high-level of strategic and conceptual thinking.</div></li>
<li><div>Meet budget standards by forecasting and managing expenses.</div></li>
<li><div>Supervise a cross-functional team of graphic designers, copywriters, social media experts, photographers/videographers and project managers in the strategic development of messages and deliverables.</div></li>
<li><div>Prioritize work and resources across engagements based on short- and long-term needs, establishing production schedules by collaborating with designers, copywriters and production departments.</div></li>
<li><div>Conduct brainstorming sessions with creative team, maintaining strategic and creative thinking to develop innovative and actionable creative initiatives in a fiscally responsible manner.</div></li>
<li><div>Responsible for overall quality of work, review and approve art and copy developed by the team and provides clear, timely and constructive feedback ensuring that deliverables effectively address marketing goals and challenges.</div></li>
<li><div>Produce fresh, innovative work that translates complex ideas into compelling print materials and digital experiences for our customers.</div></li>
<li><div>Present or oversee presentation of final concepts and obtain approvals for deliverables.</div></li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Minimum Qualifications:</b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>Bachelor’s degree required</div></li>
<li><div>7 to 12 years of progressive marketing experience with at least 4 years of creative direction in agency setting.</div></li>
<li><div>At least 5 years of experience creating marketing/advertising campaigns from developing vision and the message platform to overseeing production on time and budget.</div></li>
<li><div>At least 4 years supervisory experience of 5 or more direct reports, demonstrating ability to mentor, lead and motivate others.
</div></li>
<li><div>Ability to see the big picture, determine the right course of action for the group, delegate responsibility to the right people and allocate the right resources.</div></li>
<li><div>Understanding and past experience creating integrated content across all marketing channels from digital, social media, paid media, mobile, but also understand offline and print.</div></li>
<li><div>Proven experience leading brainstorm sessions that produce strategic concept development.</div></li>
<li><div>Highly skilled with leading a team of creative talent.</div></li>
<li><div>Strong creative vision with a solid understanding of business objectives to create forward-thinking solutions.</div></li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Preferred Qualifications:</b></div>
<ul><li>Master’s degree preferred</li></ul>

</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>SSM Health is one of the largest Catholic health systems in the country and is dedicated to quality and compassionate care for anyone in need, regardless of ability to pay. Based in St. Louis, where its System Office is located, SSM Health operates 20 hospitals in Wisconsin, Illinois, Missouri and Oklahoma. We provide care in various settings: outpatient sites, physician offices, a pharmacy benefit company, an insurance plan, hospitals, nursing homes, home care, hospice, telehealth and a technology company. <i>Our Mission: Through our exceptional health care services, we reveal the healing presence of God.</i>
