<p><b>Position Summary: </b></p>
<p>TheClient Account Services Representative works with an assigned list of billingattorneys and their clients to accurately maintain client account balanceswithin the specified terms of engagement.</p>
<p><b>Essential Functions: </b></p>
<p>1. Reviews assignedaccounts and initiates activity to recover delinquent funds.</p>
<p>2. Keepsaccurate and detailed records regarding collection activities in the Firm’sAccounts Receivable Collection Software (ARCS) when any new activity takesplace (i.e. conversation with client, voice messages left, correspondence sent,etc.).</p>
<p>3. Utilizesboth written and verbal communication to inform clients of balances due and duedates (i.e. statements, letters, emails, phone calls, etc.).</p>
<p>4. Activelyparticipates in firm collection push periods.</p>
<p>5. Assistsbilling attorneys in determining when an account should be written off,discounted, or sent to an outside collection agency.</p>
<p>6. Whennecessary, assists billing attorneys with the preparation and submission of theproper write-off forms.</p>
<p>7. Providescopies of invoices and initiates updates when incorrect addresses, contactnames, and phone numbers are uncovered in the collection process.</p>
<p>8. Listensto client feedback and takes appropriate steps to resolve issues.</p>
<p>9. Participatesin department meetings and keeps the Client Account Services Manager informed on problem accounts.</p>
<p>10. Communicatesproperly and promptly with BFCA personnel and external contacts at all timesand must exhibit a high level of customer service at all times.</p>
<p><b>AdditionalResponsibilities: </b></p>
<p>1. Generatesreports and aids the Director of Client Account Services &amp; LPM and ClientAccount Services Manager with special tasks when necessary.</p>
<p>2. Performsother duties as assigned.</p>
<p><b>Qualifications: </b></p>
<p>The Client AccountServices Representative must have a high school diploma. Demonstrated advanced knowledge of collectionprinciples, accounts management and accounting is required. Three to five years of experience in a legal financeenvironment is preferred. Must beproficient in Excel. Flexibility to workovertime as needed. Must possess anexcellent customer service attitude. Dueto the year-end functions of this job, there are no vacation or Paid Time Offrequests in the month of December.</p>
