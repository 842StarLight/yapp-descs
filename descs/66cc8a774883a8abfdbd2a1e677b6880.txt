<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Details
</b></h2>
<p>The Program Architect is responsible for successfully designing and managing the delivery of cloud based solutions and technologies. These technologies include-but are not necessarily limited to Salesforce.com products and APIs, Apex/Visualforce, Lightning, Amazon Web Services, Heroku, mobile development technologies, and integration/ETL technologies. It also requires that the Program Architect maintain an ongoing comprehensive understanding of the cloud-computing ecosystem. Program Architects typically will specialize in a salesforce platform (e.g. Force.com (Sales, Service, Community, IoT, Einstein Clouds), Marketing Cloud, Commerce Cloud) but ability to develop solutions cross-cloud is becoming more critical.
<br>
Responsibilities
</p>
<ul>
<li>Become a highly certified and recognized Salesforce expert.
</li>
<li>Serve as a Trusted Advisor, drive conversations with our customer’s Enterprise Architects, IT Delivery Leaders, and Business Stakeholders armed with best practices for enterprise architecture functions such as Security, Performance, Development Process, and Application Governance.
</li>
<li>Working closely with Delivery Managers, Functional Solution Architects and directly with clients to architect technology solutions to meet client needs.
</li>
<li>Designing transformational technical solutions and working directly with customers to meet their business requirements in accordance with industry best practices
</li>
<li>Leading and mentor the development team on the project.
</li>
<li>Enforcing sound development practices and ensure the quality delivery of enterprise solutions.
</li>
<li>Provide guidance on application and integration development best practices, Enterprise Architecture standards, functional and technical solution architecture &amp; design, environment management, testing, and Salesforce Platform education.
</li>
<li>Create application and technical design documents which leverage Salesforce best practices and effectively integrate Salesforce into the customer’s infrastructure.
</li>
<li>Troubleshoot key customer implementation issues and demonstrate ability to drive to successful resolution. Lead the evaluation of business and technical requirements from a senior level.
</li>
<li>Review and correcting project/program direction, approach and key artifacts to keep programs on track and solutions extendable and maintainable going forward.
</li>
<li>Working collaboratively and having very strong communications skills, especially in teaching complex concepts, and creative, prescriptive thinking
</li>
<li>Identifying and leading internal strategic initiatives to grow the consulting practice; serving as an active contributor to the overall knowledge base and expertise of the community.
</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
Preferred Qualifications and Skills:
</p>
<ul>
<li>BA/BS degree or foreign equivalent
</li>
<li>8+ years experience in developing technology solutions.
</li>
<li>5+ years experience as a Technical Architect and/or Development Manager in a mid to large organization managing internal IT projects or external client projects in positions of increasing responsibility in the delivery of business consulting and systems development.
</li>
<li>Specializes in gathering and analyzing information and designing comprehensive solutions that are flexible and adaptable the client's needs.
</li>
<li>Experience overseeing development and/or project team members.
</li>
<li>Detail-oriented individual with the ability to rapidly learn and take advantage of new concepts, business models, and technologies.
</li>
<li>Ability to work independently and be a self-starter.
</li>
<li>Cutting edge innovator who continually studies new technologies and functionality, and is involved in projects that push the capabilities of existing technologies.
</li>
<li>Strong communication skills, both written and verbal. Able to effectively develop materials that are appropriate for the audience.
</li>
<li>Strong presentation skills. Able to effectively present and defend point of view to a variety of audiences.
</li>
<li>Travel to client site (average of 75%), but may be higher or lower based on the client engagement.
</li>
<li>Highly beneficial to have any Salesforce.com product certifications prior to joining. It is expected that you will gain the following certifications as part of the role requirement and onboarding process in the first 3 months:
<ul>
<li>SFDC Certified Administrator
</li>
<li>Sales Cloud Consultant
</li>
<li>Service Cloud Consultant
</li>
<li>SFDC Certified Advanced Administrator
</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
