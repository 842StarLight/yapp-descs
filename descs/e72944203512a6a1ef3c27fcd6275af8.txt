<h2>Brand Ambassador</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<div>
<b>Mosaic</b> North America is an event marketing agency that delivers brand experiences that bring our clients' products directly to the people who buy them. Advertising Age recently included us in their <i>Best Places to Work in Marketing and Media</i> list, and we are about to expand our team!</div>
<div></div>
<div>We are currently looking for <b>Brand Ambassadors</b> to join our innovative, growing company. This activation is an in-field program where teams will promote a national big-box retailer’s on-line home grocery ordering/pickup service in the retailer. Brand Ambassadors will excite, entertain and inform shoppers of everything our client has to offer.</div>

<div><b>The Brand Ambassador will:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Enthusiastically represent Mosaic and our client
</li>
<li>Educate and inform shoppers about the everything our client has to offer
</li>
<li>Engage shoppers and share key messages about our client's &amp; sponsor’s products to create a "buzz" to gain attention
</li>
<li>Enthusiastically create memorable brand experiences for shoppers
</li>
<li>Work with local vendors and public service officers to give the shoppers a fun event-filled experience
</li>
<li>Provide detailed feedback on shopper interactions and comments
</li>
<li>Present a professional appearance
</li>
<li>Wear specifically required apparel
</li>
<li>Attend in-depth and comprehensive brand/product training
</li>
<li>Maintain working knowledge of all client products in order to be a subject matter expert
</li>
<li>Set up &amp; Breakdown event equipment
</li>
<li>Must be available on specific event days</li>
</ul>
</div>
<p></p>
<div>
<b>
Qualifications</b><div>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Must be comfortable standing for the duration of the shift</li>
<li>Existing knowledge and experience with large big box stores is a plus</li>
<li>Previous promotional experience is required. Large scale event experience is preferred</li>
<li>Extremely outgoing, enthusiastic and energetic</li>
<li>Comfortable educating and sharing product feature and benefit information with shoppers</li>
<li>Strong communication skills</li>
<li>Dependable and reliable</li>
<li>Must be able to commit 100% to specific event days</li>
<li>Independent and motivated whose past experience clearly demonstrates team player abilities</li>
<li>Available transportation to get to and from the site is required</li>
<li>Must have access to computer and cell phone</li>
<li>Must be able to lift 30 pounds as some event equipment may be heavy</li>
</ul>
