<h2>Brand Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader">
<b>Healthcare’s helping hand.</b><b>
</b>
</h2>
<div><div><div>
<p>CHG shook things up in 1979 by inventing the locum tenens staffing model. We connect doctors with patients who need their care. As the largest physician staffing firm in America, our providers treat millions of patients each year.
</p>
<p>Our industry is growing and demand is high. This means you’ll have plenty of opportunities to grow and develop in your career. Keeping healthcare healthy can be as fun as it is rewarding.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
This position is responsible for supporting brand image and positioning including planning and executing the strategic marketing plans for one or more CHG Healthcare staffing brands. This at-will position will plan and lead execution of advertising and lead generation activities in support of the Director of Marketing. The position serves as a business partner to the staffing divisions while working within the Marketing Department.</p>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader">
<b>Your role:</b><b>
</b>
</h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Develop the advertising, sales support, and lead generation activities in accordance with the brand’s objectives, budget, and overall marketing strategy</li>
<li>
Develop a solid understanding of the business/industry and how key strategic issues impact objectives</li>
<li>
Deeply understand our target audience behaviors and beliefs</li>
<li>
Acquire thorough knowledge of the business so that marketing and advertising problems and opportunities may be anticipated</li>
<li>
Develop strategic briefs based on input from the Director and manage projects in accordance with department procedures</li>
<li>
Drive brand equity over time by evolving strategies and competitive positioning in the industry</li>
<li>
Analyze results, draw conclusions and develop strategic documents and proposals</li>
<li>
Evaluate copy, designs, media plans and production materials and make suggested revisions in keeping with agreed upon strategies</li>
<li>
Provide marketing team with timely and relevant feedback, objections, and current business needs of the brand</li>
<li>
Manage budgets, approve estimates and invoices ensuring all projects come in on time and on budget</li>
<li>
Communicate relevant business updates to stakeholders and marketing department leaders on a regular basis</li>
<li>
Manage partnership with our all marketing teams, including creative, email, media, lead gen, events, PR, social, content, and internal communications and help drive strategy</li>
<li>
Travel as required for training and ongoing marketing department activities</li>
<li>
Perform other duties as assigned by management</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader">
<b>Your qualifications:</b><b>
</b>
</h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Strong strategic marketing and analytical skills</li>
<li>
Ability to work under pressure while still meeting deadlines and objectives</li>
<li>
Superb communication and interpersonal skills</li>
<li>
Effective professional presentation skills</li>
<li>
Drive work forward through appropriate delegation and prioritization</li>
<li>
Ability to build relationships over the phone and in person</li>
<li>
Comfortably provide feedback and direction to others</li>
<li>
Ability to exercise discretion and independent judgment</li>
<li>
Ability to be persuasive and influential in verbal and written communications</li>
<li>
Ability to integrate individual efforts with team objectives and processes</li>
<li>
Ability to balance requests from the division with marketing department priorities, providing a consultative “no” when needed</li>
<li>
Teamwork is a key success factor: the brand manager must be able to ensure collaboration and buy-in within the marketing department, internal clients and throughout the organization</li>
<li>
Ability to learn new technologies and software quickly (i.e. Workfront, Salesforce)</li>
<li>
Desire to be a positive and professional team player who can collaborate with peers on decisions</li>
<li>
Strong ability to think strategically as well as creatively</li>
<li>
Aptitude to be tenacious in meeting deadlines, well organized, and adept at managing peak workloads</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Education &amp; Years of Experience:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s or master’s degree in business with an emphasis in marketing</li>
<li>
Extensive knowledge of direct response advertising</li>
<li>
Extensive knowledge of brand equity management
</li>
<li>Extensive knowledge and experience in strategic communication plan development</li>
<li>
Extensive knowledge of digital marketing strategy including social media, paid media, SEO and current consumer online trends
</li>
<li>Five or more years of marketing experience in an agency or account management structure
</li>
<li>Two to four years of indirect team leadership or large project leadership experience</li>
</ul>
