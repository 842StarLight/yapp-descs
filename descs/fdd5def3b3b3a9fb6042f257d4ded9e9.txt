<h2>Landscape Designe</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div><div>
<p>We are in search of a creative and passionate Landscape Designer to join our Design/Sales staff. At Groundscapes, we design and build beautiful outdoor living spaces and are always in search of new professionals to join our ever-growing team. As a Landscape Designer with us, you will be designing fully-custom outdoor spaces complete with swimming pools &amp; spas, outdoor kitchens, outdoor fireplaces, fire pits, and so much more.</p>
<p>
You will be able to improve your design skills while designing a variety of different projects that are large vs small, modern vs rustic, and simple vs highly detailed. You will be using leading industry software to communicate and estimate landscape design concepts for your clients. After a client decides to go forward with a landscape concept, your projects will then be constructed by experienced landscape construction professionals that will make you and your client's vision come to life.</p>
<p>
We value our employees and their personal well-being. Just as much as you want to find the right fit for your career - we want to be the right fit for you. We have a family-like office environment that thrives on working independently, while collaborating with team members as necessary.</p>
<p>
This is a salary position with the opportunity for UNLIMITED sales commissions.</p>
<p>
If you're interested in becoming a Landscape Designer at Groundscapes please submit your application. Please send a cover letter, resume, &amp; portfolio to <b>careers@groundscapesne.com</b></p>

IMPORTANT APPLICATION INFORMATION AND INSTRUCTIONS:</b></p>
<p>
Applications will be accepted on a continuous basis until a sufficient number of qualified applications have been received. You will be contacted if you're a finalist in consideration.</p>
<p><b>
Job Title:</b>Landscape Designer</p>
<p><b>
Department:</b>Landscape Design / Sales</p>
<p><b>
Position Summary: </b>Lead landscape designs and sales for residential projects, from conceptual planning to start of construction. Develop personal relationships with residential clients and keep an open line of communication with the client throughout the construction process. Perform various professional, administrative, and technical work in the design, development, and construction of residential landscaping, outdoor living spaces, pools/spas, and other projects as required.</p>
<p><b>
Supervision Exercised and Received:</b>Position works under the supervision of the Lead Landscape Designer.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Job Functions:</b></p>
<p>
Operations</p>
<ul>
<li><p>
Oversees planning and design efforts associated with residential landscape projects. Provides design direction and technical guidance to both the client and colleagues. Serves as a point of communication with industry professionals, including construction contractors, suppliers, and more.</p></li>
<li><p>
Responsible for estimating construction of Landscapes, Outdoor Living Spaces, and Pools/Spas.</p></li>
<li><p>
Must be able to build personal relationships with clients and communicate with the client throughout the construction of the project.</p></li>
</ul>
<p>
Estimating / Sales</p>
<ul>
<li><p>
Responsible for estimating construction of Landscapes, Outdoor Living Spaces, and Pools/Spas accurately.</p></li>
<li><p>
Must be able to build personal relationships with clients and communicate with the client throughout the construction of the project.</p></li>
<li><p>
Must be able to accomplish sales and performances goals</p></li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Core Attributes: </b>We are seeking an individual that displays the following key professional attributes:</p>
<ul>
<li><p>
Knowledge of site construction materials, processes, techniques and all technical aspects of developing residential landscaping projects.</p></li>
<li><p>
Ability to identify new design &amp; technology trends in the field of landscape design and subsequently implement trends into the residential projects.</p></li>
<li><p>
Ability to communicate effectively through email, written reports, and verbally.</p></li>
<li><p>
Ability to be self-motivated and ready to work independently without direct oversight.</p></li>
<li><p>
Ability to make sound recommendations to supervisors in response to situations when necessary.</p></li>
<li><p>
Ability to work effectively as part of a team so that we can all excel.</p></li>
<li><p>
Ability to identify problems and review related information to come up with solutions.</p></li>
<li><p>
Ability to effectively and efficiently provide great customer service and have the ability to take steps to help customers with their needs.</p></li>
<li><p>
Skill in managing complex and technical projects to ensure they are completed in a timely manner, withing the clients budget, and meet the overall objectives.</p></li>
</ul>
