<div><b>
Description</b></div>
<div>This position supports and assists the implementation of HCM (Human Capital Management) applications for Emerson Business Units in a global, multi-Business Group environment. This position provides assistance to the Project Managers in HRIS for implementations and other enhancements. In addition, this position will help the Operations team in resolving production issues, as needed.</div>
<br>
<div>
</div>
<div><b>Responsibilities</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li><div>Manage the configuration of the application based on Business Unit requirements and Emerson Global HRIS standards for Human Resources, Payroll, Benefits, Talent Management, Compensation Management, Time and Labor, Reporting, and other associated modules</div></li>
<li><div>Coordinate with cross-functional teams in different lines of business to collect requirements for Oracle HCM
</div></li>
<li><div>Assist in Oracle HCM implementations and on-boarding activities in the following areas:</div></li>
<li><div>Participate in requirements gathering sessions and maintain requirements documents</div></li>
<li><div>Convert legacy data into Oracle</div></li>
<li><div>Maintain other project management documentation such as fit/gap analysis</div></li>
<li><div>Participate in data validation</div></li>
<li><div>Assist with payroll parallel testing</div></li>
<li><div>Maintain reporting standards and coordinate reporting requirements across multiple Business Units</div></li>
<li><div>Provide support to the Business Units for HR, Payroll, Benefits, Talent Management, Compensation Management, Time and Labor, Reporting, and other associated modules</div></li>
<li><div>Research new Oracle functionality to enhance customer usage of Oracle and assist in testing the new configuration before it is migrated in production</div></li>
<li><div>Transition completed implementations to the HRIS support process team</div></li>
<li><div>Assist with the maintenance of end-user training documentation
</div></li>
<li><div>Provide support to the Operations team in resolving production issues</div></li>
<li><div>Other duties as assigned</div></li>
</ul>

Requirements</b></div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li><div>Requires completion of a four-year bachelor’s degree in a Human Resources, Information Technology Business, or related field</div></li>
<li><div>Up to five years configuration, implementation or business support experience with an HCM application preferred. Oracle HCM Cloud experience preferred; however Oracle E-Business Suite, PeopleSoft, ERP or similar system experience will be considered</div></li>
<li><div>Good business knowledge in HR, Payroll, Benefits and related processes
</div></li>
<li><div>Deadline oriented with a sense of urgency in meeting project goals</div></li>
<li><div>Ready and willing to share information, as necessary</div></li>
<li><div>Able to communicate with personnel in multi-disciplines from the clerical level to senior management
</div></li>
<li><div>Able to interact professionally with clients, contractors and vendors both internal and external</div></li>
<li><div>Experience with large change initiatives is a plus</div></li>
<li><div>Interacts with other team members to exchange information and creates a positive and productive team dynamic</div></li>
<li><div>Ability to effectively work in a team environment
</div></li>
</ul>
