<p><b>Job Description
</b></p>
<p>A strategic representative for Performance and Scalability across development engineering, Product Management, Project Management, Sales, Services and Support
<br>
A strategic representative for defining roadmap of key services offered by the Performance and Scalability team including a growing list of performance tools and automation frameworks
<br>
Responsible for reporting on the roadmap to stakeholders
<br>
Own prioritization of backlog requests based on stakeholders priorities, feedback, and needs
<br>
Function as a central performance and scalability resource for our product and tools engineering teams as they move through the software delivery lifecycle
<br>
Participate in customer and partner facing events, including conferences, as a performance expert
<br>
Ability to quickly come up to speed in Workday's Agile development model
<br>
Ability to write about and speak to key performance and scalability indicators and metrics
<br>
Flexible personality that is able to adapt to rapid change
<br>
Ability to collate many cross-team requests identifying business value and prioritizing it
<br>
Work with application and tools teams within Workday to champion performance and scalability priorities
</p>

Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or equivalent technical major
<br>
5+ years of product management, consulting, or development experience in SaaS
<br>
Passionate about technology as it relates to performance and scalability
<br>
Excellent written and verbal communication skills
<br>
Experienced with scrum and other agile development methods
