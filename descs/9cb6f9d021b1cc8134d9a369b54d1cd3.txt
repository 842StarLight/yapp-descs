<h2>Major Account Systems Engineer - Seattle</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Major Accounts Systems Engineer</p>
<p></p>
<p>
Aruba, a Hewlett Packard Enterprise Company, is a leading provider of next-generation networking solutions for the mobile enterprise:</p>
<p>
http://www.arubanetworks.com/company/about-us. We have an exciting opportunity for a Major Accounts Systems Engineer (SE) to join our team.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
The Major Accounts SE primarily provides pre-sales technical support for the development and implementation of complex network and security products/applications/solutions. As a Pre-Sales/Systems Engineer, the Major Accounts SE will be responsible for managing pre-sales technical / functional support to prospective clients and customers while ensuring customer satisfaction. With a proven track record of successful sales support activity, the Systems Engineer will present and articulate the capabilities and values of an Aruba Solution versus that of our competitors. The preferred candidate will have career level experience with networking infrastructure and access layer security technologies in all arenas.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
TheSE will work with an Aruba Major Accounts Manager in a 1:1 pairing to qualify opportunities, develop strategy and convert leads into successful engagements. TheSE must combine excellent sales support, and consultative skills with an expert understanding of advanced and emerging technologies with an emphasis on campus and distributed branch wired and wireless networks. The Major Accounts SE will consult with their assigned Account Manager to develop a comprehensive strategy around new and prospective clients. By combining technical expertise with business acumen this role provides vital, detailed information about how Aruba’s solutions deliver business value to our clients.</p>
<p></p>
<p><i>
Responsibilities:</i><br>
Opportunity analysis.</p>
<ul>
<li>
Gathers and assesses customer needs, both business and technical.</li>
<li>
Identifies related needs (lead generation, opportunity expansion).</li>
<li>
Identifies site-specific and corporate parameters and constraints that impact the solution.</li>
<li>
Identifies required project steps.</li>
<li>
Identifies likely problem areas that require attention.</li>
<li>
Identifies information on competitors and product roll- out data/training needs and proactively positions the company strengths as a relative value position in the account.</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
Solution Planning and Design.</p>
<ul>
<li>
Architects an appropriate technical solution to meet the customer's business requirements and account team strategy.</li>
<li>
Investigates and optimizes a solution's fit to the requirements of an opportunity, both current and future.</li>
<li>
Adapts solution design to new requirements.</li>
<li>
Establishes the validity of a solution and its components; creates proof of concept where required.</li>
<li>
Identifies the growth path and scalability options of a solution and includes these in design activities.</li>
<li>
Generates an implementation plan with timelines for the solution.</li>
<li>
Create appropriate test plan as required.</li>
<li>
Identifies potential challenges for the proposed project plan.</li>
<li>
Optimizes a solution to maximize the company's competitive advantages.</li>
<li>
Actively identifies opportunities to assist peers globally in area of expertise (e.g., writing white-papers).</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
Client/customer relationship.</p>
<ul>
<li>
Maintains excellent communications with customer executive management and business executives globally.</li>
<li>
Represents the company as a technical expert with customers; shares knowledge in area of expertise and links to related technology areas.</li>
<li>
Delivers customer workshops as appropriate for further understanding of issues and buy-in to the company's proposal.</li>
<li>
Advances opportunities through the use of effective consultative selling techniques.</li>
<li>
Builds customer loyalty through continuous innovation, becoming a trusted advisor.</li>
<li>
Partners effectively with others in the account to ensure problem resolution and customer satisfaction.</li>
<li>
Effectively communicates and articulates the details of their component roles in a proposed customer solution.</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
Team collaboration</p>
<ul>
<li>
Actively supports the account team with solution advice, proposals, presentations, and other customer communications.</li>
<li>
Analyzes and provides support to deals in the pipeline where needed.</li>
<li>
Transfers knowledge to presales peers via mentoring and playing leadership roles in peer education programs.</li>
<li>
Understands the roles and effectively directs other teams and resources within the company and partners globally.</li>
<li>
Identifies overlooked opportunities suggested by technical expertise.</li>
<li>
Facilitates smooth transition from sales to implementation by orienting the appropriate teams to the solution design.</li>
</ul>

Typically 12+ years experience in technical consultative selling and account management.</li>

Technical and solutions experience in either wired or wireless networking – both preferred.</li>
<li>
Experience in vertical industry preferred.</li>
<li>
Experience with working with large fortune 500 and fortune 100 accounts preferred. Ideally in a technical sales role.</li>
</ul>
