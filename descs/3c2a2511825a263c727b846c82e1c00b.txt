RESPONSIBILITIES
<br><br>
Reporting to the VP, Cloud Operations, the Network Operations Specialist will be responsible for the following:
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for flawless deployment and ongoing support of all network infrastructure including core and edge routers, firewalls, load-balancers, switches, DWDM systems, and any other related network infrastructure.</li>
<li>Work alongside the ops teams to stand up new racks, configure switches, run cables as necessary, and provide local support for network issues.</li>
<li>Provide support to sales and support for customer related issues (both pre and post sales).</li>
<li>Troubleshoot and resolve network issues as they arise.</li>
<li>Strong optical networking design/troubleshooting experience.</li>
<li>Ability to work closely with internal and external (customer/partner) DevOps organizations in implementation and troubleshooting scenarios.</li>
</ul>

REQUIREMENTS
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Certifications: CCNP, CCDP, CCIE, JNCIE, JNCP or other certifications desired.</li>
<li>Education: College degree or equivalent work experience.</li>
<li>Experience: 5 or more years working on large scale service provider networks.</li>
<li>Network hardware: Cisco 6XXX or 76XX series L3 switches, all Juniper hardware, Cisco Nexus and Catalyst switching hardware, Fortinet or other firewall appliances, Array networks or other load-balancing hardware, Infinera or other DWDM systems.</li>
<li>Network protocols/technologies: BGP, MPLS, OSPF, IPv4/6, MST/PVST, and many others.</li>
<li>Strong organization and time management skills.</li>
<li>Impeccable verbal and written communication skills.</li>
<li>Familiar with local marketplace, companies and the community within the assigned region.</li>
<li>Ability to work remotely with little or no direct supervision.</li>
<li>A strong understanding of customer service a plus.</li>
<li>Must be self-motivated and disciplined.</li>
</ul>
