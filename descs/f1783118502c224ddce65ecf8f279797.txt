<p><b>Sitecore Strategist/Configurator</b></p>
<p>
Slalom is seeking a self-driven individual with a “get it done” mentality that has a passion for helping organizations solve complex problems. The individual in this role will lead business and technical requirements sessions and help clients strategically select and leverage the best digital platform components for their needs. Working directly with clients and Slalom consultants, this person will help lead the strategy, design, and configuration of solutions that best utilize content management systems and related components including marketing automation tools, analytics, CRM systems, and more.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Translate business objectives and marketing plans into well-designed solutions that best leverage digital platform capabilities</li>
<li>
Recommend appropriate digital technology to support the customer’s needs end-to-end, including hosting and integration with existing technologies (CRM, marketing automation, analytics, etc.)</li>
<li>
Implement Sitecore marketing tools such as goals (CTAs and Page Level), campaigns (Email/Paid) and profiles on a large customer-facing website</li>
<li>
Design user journeys including strategies for personalization scenarios targeting specific user audiences</li>
<li>
Implement A/B testing determine top performing content</li>
<li>
Journey Mapping (Diagramming the user’s journey in Visio including all personalization scenarios, goals, and campaigns)</li>
<li>
Pattern Matching</li>
<li>
Lead business and technical design sessions; work with Slalom architects to design and document solutions that are aligned with client business objectives</li>
<li>
Conduct solution reviews to ensure the strategy is best meeting customer needs during design and build phases</li>
<li>
Adjust team direction as necessary in a collaborative fashion</li>
<li>
Be a credible subject matter expert to clients and partners on sales cycles and marketing events</li>
<li>
Provide hands-on, expert-level assistance to analysts, configurators, and other team members</li>
<li>
Demonstrate digital platform thought leadership</li>
<li>
Provide detailed estimates and participate in sales solutioning and proposal creation</li>
<li>
Own responsibility for ongoing development of team capabilities in the digital space</li>
<li>
Collaborate with Slalom team members and clients, promote a fun and supportive work environment</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Proven ability to design, configure, and deliver simple and complex digital solutions for Sitecore, SharePoint, Adobe, etc.</li>
<li>
4+ years of Web Content Management System experience, ideally in consulting or digital agency work</li>
<li>
3+ years of digital or marketing platform experience (5+ years preferred)</li>
<li>
Strong background in CMS, analytics, CRM, marketing automation, SEO</li>
<li>
Familiarity with agile software delivery methodologies such as Scrum</li>
<li>
Excellent client-facing written and oral communications skills</li>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree</li>
</ul>
