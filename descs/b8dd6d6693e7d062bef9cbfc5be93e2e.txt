<h2>Technical Support Engineer II</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>This position will provide troubleshooting and resolution of technical issues via in-person, phone and web-based communication for Ascom products (such as Nurse Call, Emergency Call, Mobility, and Unite Middleware Systems). The Technical Support Engineer II will own and resolve customer support issues while handling and exercising judgment in escalating issues within defined practices and policies.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Associates degree or 4 years Ascom product experience.Or, 5+ years’ experience working with Nurse Call, Middleware, telecommunications, or wireless technology (DECT or 802.11)</li>
<li>2 years’ experience in Customer Support role utilizing phone, e-mail and web-based systems</li>
<li>3 years’ experience with server hardware and Microsoft Windows Server operating systems</li>
<li>2 year experience with telecommunications, IT or WLAN</li>
<li>2 year experience with Desktop Operating systems</li>
<li>2 year experience with basic IP communication</li>
<li>Subject matter expert in one Ascom product line along with working knowledge in all Ascom products</li>
<li>Microsoft Server and Desktop operating systems</li>
<li>Microsoft SQL Server</li>
<li>TCP/IP Networking</li>
<li>Wireless Networking</li>
<li>PC skills (Microsoft Office)</li>
<li>Basic telephony knowledge</li>
<li>Excellent customer management skills</li>
<li>Escalation management skills</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal skills, including verbal and written communications</li>
<li>Excellent troubleshooting and problem solving skills</li>
<li>Detail oriented, highly responsive and customer focused</li>
<li>Ability to work independently and have a strong social presence with team interaction</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Training</b></p>
<p>Technical Support Engineer II must successfully complete within the first 9 months in position (obtain certification where applicable):</p>
<ul>
<li>All Technical Support Engineer I required training (IP-DECT, UNITE, VoWiFi, Telecare IP, Telligence)</li>
<li>UNITE Advanced</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provide level II advanced technical troubleshooting and problem resolution via in-person, phone, email and network tools in order to troubleshoot, fix or triage customer support issues</li>
<li>Investigate and isolate hardware and software problems using documented procedures and appropriate tools</li>
<li>Interface with customers and peers to collect information needed for troubleshooting and problem resolution while escalating issues within defined practices and policies. Collaborate with internal groups as needed</li>
<li>Establish and maintain advanced technical knowledge in networking, operating systems, and all Ascom products at the administrator level, with successful completion of Ascom technical training classes and industry training through self-education</li>
<li>Follow defined process and procedures for documenting, tracking, and managing customer support issues via the issue tracker</li>
<li>Identify opportunities and provide input to improve overall service delivery processes, tools/systems, documentation and support capabilities</li>
<li>Leadership in maintaining technical support lab equipment and records</li>
<li>Provide cross training and mentoring for more junior technical support engineers</li>
<li>Perform on-call coverage and after-hours duty as business needs dictate</li>
<li>Position will require travel up to 30% of the time, including overnight travel</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
