<p></p>
<p><i>Are you our “TYPE”?

<p></p>

Want t </i><i>o learn more about who we are and what we do, visit us at </i><i>www.monotype.com </i><i>.

<p></p>
<p>Reporting into Monotype’s Senior Director of Corporate and Product Marketing, the Sr. Product Marketing Manager will be responsible for developing and executing go-to-market strategy, messaging and positioning &amp; product launches for Monotype’s SaaS product offering and supporting services. The ideal candidate is a strategic thinker and storyteller who can create compelling value-based messaging and marketing collateral to serve Monotype’s global markets and channels. You will be responsible for understanding how our customers want to buy, and helping them to do so, by managing the product or feature launches, arming the sales channel to market and sell the product, and collaborating with our revenue marketing team to help calibrate campaigns to buyer personas and market opportunities.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>You will have deep understanding of our customers and be able to articulate the customer pain points and communicate clearly in their own language about the opportunities and solutions. You understand market dynamics, competitors and have experience applying that knowledge to meaningful and actionable go-to-market strategies.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>You are comfortable working closely in cross-functional teams (product management, finance, sales, etc.) and interfacing with company executives. Experience working with large brands and creative agencies in an enterprise B2B environment is a plus.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b><i>What you’ll be doing:
</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Work closely and collaboratively with cross-functional teams to shape our product messaging and value propositions.
</li>
<li>Plan the external campaigns around the launch of new products and releases and manage the cross-functional implementation of the product plan.
</li>
<li>Drive the creation and execution of product content and collateral.
</li>
<li>Own the creation of key buyer personas and target segments and collaborates with the Content and Revenue Marketing team to develop a content strategy to support the buyer’s journey.
</li>
<li>Monitor and update market trends and translate them into active points of view that helps drive organizational decision making.
</li>
<li>Create meaningful and measurable KPIs and metrics for programs.
</li>
<li>Equip Monotype’s sales organization with the tools, data, trainings and product demos that enable them to develop deep relationships with our current and future customers.
</li>
<li>Collaborate with product on market segmentation and product and pricing strategy
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b><i>What we’re looking for:
</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in business or marketing; MBA preferred.
</li>
<li>7+ years of software product marketing or related experience. Very strong written communication skills.
</li>
<li>Excellent people and management skills to interact with staff, colleagues and cross-functional teams, and third parties.
</li>
<li>Good understanding or willingness to learn about typeface design, brand equity and consumer solutions and related technologies.
</li>
<li>Excellent managing and analytical skills, with the ability to think creatively and solve problems; plus verbal, written and presentation skills in English to serve our globally operating organization.
</li>
<li>Experience developing and creating ROI-centric reports that align product back to corporate marketing goals.
</li>
<li>Experience with Salesforce.com and Microsoft PowerBI.
</li>
<li>A “team player/coach” with demonstrated abilities to both develop strategies that help achieve business goals, as well as to execute programs with excellence, down to the smallest details.
</li>
<li>Project management skills with demonstrated ability to manage multiple projects.
</li>
<li>Experience in the Pragmatic Marketing framework is a plus.
</li>
<li>A positive attitude, energy, integrity, accountability and a great sense of humor.
</li>
</ul>
