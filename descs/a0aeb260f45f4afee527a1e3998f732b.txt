<h2>Field Application Engineer (FAE)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Primary responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Collaborate with customers’ engineering design teams on new product development and growth of existing accounts.</li>
<li>Nurture existing relationships through regular engagement and support.</li>
<li>Based upon day-to-day engagement with our customers, products and applications, act as proactive liaison with our factory personnel as required.</li>
<li>Analyze customer problems, and quickly and efficiently offer best solutions.</li>
<li>Coordinate across internal and external engineer and support teams.</li>
<li>Provide regular project status updates to the manager and drive timely completion of tasks.</li>
<li>Travel to meet customers on site and Luxshare-ICT factories.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications &amp; Requirements:</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li><b>Bilingual in Chinese/English is a must.</b></li>
<li>Design experience with stamping, molding, FATP for Component/Module/Hardware development.</li>
<li>Minimum of Bachelor’s degree in Mechanical Engineering.</li>
<li>5-8 years of progressive experience in design, with some sales or customer-facing experience.</li>
<li>Proficiency in 3D software as Pro/E, Creo, Solidworks, and UG NX.</li>
<li>Self-motivated, driven personality with the ability to quickly analyze and assess situations.</li>
<li>Desire and ability to help customers solve complex problem.</li>
<li>Strong communication and interpersonal skills.</li>
<li>Comfort and skill presenting technical information to a variety of audiences.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to nurture customer relationships.</li>
<li>Demonstrated project &amp; program management skills</li>
<li>Persistent, goal/deadline driven work style</li>
<li>Strong communication and interpersonal skills.</li>
<li>Up to 25% international and domestic travel.</li>
</ul>
