<h2>Deputy Program Manager*</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<div>Ironclad Technology Services is a Virginia based Information Technology and Business Consulting services company focused on supporting public and private sector customers as they tackle their most daunting information technology and business challenges. We focus on solving real world business problems through innovative thinking, a talented staff, and hard work.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Ironclad Technology Services LLC, a Service Disabled Veteran Owned Small Business has an immediate opening for a Deputy Program Manager to join our growing company. The Deputy Program Manager will provide business and financial management support for ongoing efforts of the Program Executive Office for Integrated Warfare Systems (PEO IWS) in Washington, DC. Due to the nature of the work, only U.S. Citizens will be considered for this position. This position is contingent upon contract award.</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>RESPONSIBILITIES</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Assist in prime contract engagements with the Government.
</li>
<li>Support the Program Manager with individual program management, staffing, quality assurance, technical training, client interface activities, and project financial management.
</li>
<li>Contribute to developing and improving processes to effectively operate the organization and its infrastructure to effect strategic and tactical planning and execution.
</li>
<li>Support financial and business management operations using NAVSEA financial tools in areas of budget and reporting including, but not limited to, N-ERP, PRISMS, and STARS.
</li>
<li>Provide detailed reports regarding the financial status of programs.
</li>
<li>Provide expertise and cost models germane to Program/System type and acquisition phase.
</li>
<li>Assist in generating, updating, tracking, and consolidating Task Planning Sheets (TPS).
</li>
<li>Assist in the day-to-day budget execution process by preparing, processing, and tracking the status of funding documents for all appropriations managed by PEO IWS.
</li>
<li>Develop processes to monitor and ensure program is managed within Letter of Offer and Acceptance (LOA) budget and FMS Payment schedules.
</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>SKILLS AND QUALIFICATIONS</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Bachelor's degree required preferably in Business or Accounting; Masters of Business Administration (MBA) preferred.
</li>
<li>Minimum 15 years professional experience in program/project management required.
</li>
<li>Minimum 5 years experience in Navy program management preferred.
</li>
<li>Minimum 7 years supervisory experience and experience communicating with SES/Flag level personnel required.
</li>
<li>Specialized experience in DoD/DoN procedures and Policies (DoD 5000 process); Foreign Military Sales; US Navy Programs (PEO IWS Programs) preferred.
</li>
<li>Project Management Professional (PMP) Certification preferred.
</li>
<li>U.S. Citizen with a Current Secret security clearance or higher.
</li>
</ul></div>
