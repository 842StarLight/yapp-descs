<h2>Digital Ad Operations Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Job Description<br>
<br>
<p>The <b>Digital Ad Operations Manager</b> position will coordinate, monitor, and manage all aspects of online advertising. Including, but not limited to chasing ad creative, managing ad impression inventory, trafficking ad campaigns and coordinating with the Finance Team for invoicing. This position reports to the Director of Ad Operations, but works closely with Sales staff, Marketing staff, agencies and others to ensure ad campaigns deliver the desired number of impressions and are completed on time.</p>
</div>
<br>
<div>Qualifications<br>
<br>
<p><b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Digital Ad Campaign Management / Ad Trafficking (60%):</p>
<ul>
<li>
Manage digital ad campaigns via DFP and other ad servers as needed. On-Site and email</li>
<li>
Manage digital ad impression inventory and banner ad inventory for on-site and email products</li>
<li>
Diagnose ad serving problems and interfacing with team and/or clients to identify solutions</li>
<li>
Document and maintain digital ad campaign schedules</li>
<li>
Assist Sales and Client Services with RFP’s as needed</li>
<li>
Work directly with Product and Digital Engagement teams to optimize digital campaigns as needed</li>
<li>
Train and inform process documentation for the Digital Ad Operation team</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Analytics/Reporting (25%):</p>
<ul>
<li>
Report on digital campaign performance</li>
<li>
Report on impression pacing throughout the flight of a campaign</li>
<li>
Report on final campaign performance - Monthly</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Other (15%):</p>
<ul>
<li>
Provide support to Digital Ad Ops staff</li>
<li>
Complete data entry and analysis functions as needed</li>
<li>
Provide technical QA support on ad serving as needed</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
1-3 years of Ad Operations or Ad Tech experience</li>
<li>
Highly analytical and technology agnostic</li>
<li>
Knowledge of/experience with third party ad servers – DFP preferred</li>
<li>
Intermediate skills in HTML, familiarity web coding concepts preferred</li>
<li>
Ability to independently handle multiple tasks in a fast-paced environment under tight deadlines</li>
<li>
Strong verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>
High level of attention to detail and excellent organizational skills</li>
<li>
Demonstrated proclivity in Technology, Internet and/or Media/Advertising</li>
</ul>
