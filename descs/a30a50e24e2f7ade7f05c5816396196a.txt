<p>Bonnell Aluminum is North America’s premier manufacturer of custom aluminum extrusions with over $570 million in net sales. Through a commitment to customer excellence, Bonnell Aluminum’s production facilities serve many of our nation’s largest and most respected manufacturing companies through design solutions, unmatched extrusion capabilities and services. We are headquartered in Newnan, Georgia. In addition to our Newnan facility, we also have manufacturing operations in Carthage, Tennessee; Niles, Michigan; Elkhart, Indiana; and Clearfield, Utah. <p>Responsible for providing technical assistance and support related to computer systems, hardware, or software. Responds to queries, isolates problem, and determines and implements solution.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Primary Responsibilities
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Experience assisting users on phone and live chat, email and web form submissions.
</li>
<li>Proven analytical and problem-solving abilities.
</li>
<li>Ability to effectively prioritize and execute tasks in a high-pressure environment.
</li>
<li>Exceptional customer service orientation.
</li>
<li>Patience for dealing with difficult customer-service situations.
</li>
<li>Provide technical assistance and support for incoming queries and issues related to computer systems (Windows 7-10), software (Office 365 products, web browsers), and hardware (projectors, PCs, tablets, printers and other IT related devices).

<li>Respond to queries either in person or over the phone.
</li>
<li>Train computer users.
</li>
<li>Maintain daily performance of computer systems and peripherals.
</li>
<li>Respond to email messages and phone calls for customers seeking help.
</li>
<li>Ask questions to determine nature of problem.
</li>
<li>Walk user through problem-solving process.
</li>
<li>Install, modify, and repair computer hardware and software.
</li>
<li>Clean up computers.
</li>
<li>Troubleshoot technical problems with Local Area Networks (LAN) and other systems.
</li>
<li>Install computer peripherals for users.
</li>
<li>Follow up with users to ensure issue has been resolved.
</li>
<li>Possibility of writing training manuals/SOPs.</li>
