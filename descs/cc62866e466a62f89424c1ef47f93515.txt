<p><b>
The Role:</b> The Production Control &amp; Sales &amp; Operations Planning Manager is responsible for all demand, capacity and network planning activities for Xylem Americas Commercial Team. This position will serve as the key connection point that links and synthesizes activities of the Sales, Manufacturing and Procurement functions.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Duties/Principal Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Creates and lead communication of monthly meetings/interactions with functional business leaders. Leads day-to-day activities of S&amp;OP planning team for Bridgeport operations.</li>
<li>
Defines and releases the production plan including purchasing raw material and component parts; maintaining inventory levels/safety stock levels.</li>
<li>
Maintains procurement records, such as items purchased, costs, delivery, product quality or performance, and inventories.</li>
<li>
Supplies forecast to suppliers on a weekly basis. Works with suppliers to continually improve delivery performance.</li>
<li>
Develops and maintains the monthly department expense budget, while applying measures to continually decrease expenses.</li>
<li>
Develops and cultivates a culture of independent problem solvers who deploy LEAN tools to continuously improve fulfillment processes. Builds and maintains a process management approach through visual management, periodic inspections and development of countermeasures.</li>
<li>
Promotes and ensures conformance to the Company’s work standards, regulations and quality.</li>
<li>
Advocates and ensures corporate programs and initiatives are executed effectively and in alignment with Xylem’s strategy.</li>
<li>
Effectively implements and manages a collaborative demand planning process.</li>
<li>
Ensures effective manufacturing capacity, network and inventory optimizations are being achieved.</li>
<li>
Ensures KPIs are accurate and shared with the business in a regular, timely fashion.</li>
<li>
Coordinates special projects aimed at alleviating demand/supply imbalances and improving overall “order to cash” efficiency of the business unit</li>
<li>
Assists with the refinement of item master/item routing setup &amp; bill of materials for new and existing item codes.</li>
<li>
All other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
