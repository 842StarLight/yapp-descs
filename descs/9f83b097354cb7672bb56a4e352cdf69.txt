<p><b>Description – </b>Outside – B2B sales professional – responsible for the marketing and sales of office technology solutions including Copiers, printers and business software applications in a designated territory.</p>
<div>
<ul>
<li>Mission: Working with businesses to help them improve office efficiencies &amp; cost through more effective use of office technologies.</li>
<li>Specialize in eliminating or minimizing manual business process with the use of office automation hardware and software.</li>
<li>Our goal is to streamline business processes through connecting items like an office scanner directly to an existing software system. Through this process development we eliminate multiple touchpoints creating both time and cost saving efficiencies.</li>
</ul>
