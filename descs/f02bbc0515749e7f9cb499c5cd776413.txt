<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Oldcastle BuildingEnvelope®, a CRH company, is the leading supplier of products specified to close the building envelope. They include: custom-engineered curtain wall and window wall, architectural windows, storefront systems, doors, skylights and architectural glass.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
We are looking for a detail-oriented individual with a minimum of 5 years Accounts Receivable and Collection experience who will take full ownership of the Credit &amp; Collections position.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Job Summary</b></p>
<p>
Responsible for tracking and resolving outstanding payment issues and generate weekly aging reports in a timely manner. Must have knowledge of general accounting principles. Must be proficient at MS Office (especially Excel). Should display a positive and proactive attitude, strong organizational skills, the ability to prioritize, multitask and be a strong communicator.<br>
</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Make daily collection calls.</li>
<li>
Conduct account research and analysis.</li>
<li>
Prepare and maintain various reports.</li>
<li>
Send statements to customers.</li>
<li>
Interact with customers to resolve outstanding issues.</li>
<li>
Prepare collection letters, notice of intent to lien, and lien documents as required.</li>
<li>
Verify credit history information for new accounts.</li>
<li>
Recommend customer credit limits and credit status.</li>
<li>
Monitor customer creditworthiness and change status on customer master accordingly.</li>
<li>
Notify sales personnel when credit status changes.</li>
<li>
Respond to all customer inquiries relating to credit.</li>
<li>
Inquire as to problems or delays with payment of delinquent invoices.</li>
<li>
Notify salesperson of problems with unpaid invoices and request their assistance in resolving.</li>
<li>
Reconcile customer A/R accounts as needed.</li>
<li>
Follow up on short pays and credit with sales staff and customers.</li>
<li>
Maintain and resolve e-mail requests. (waivers, billing issues, short pays, references, etc)</li>
<li>
Work with attorneys on collection issues.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Ability to multi-task.</li>
<li>
Ability to work effectively in a team environment.</li>
<li>
Ability to communicate effectively with customers and salespeople.</li>
<li>
Ability to take detailed direction and follow through as instructed.</li>
<li>
Ability to handle large quantity of work with minimal mistakes.</li>
<li>
5 years of Collection or Business to Business Credit experience required.</li>
<li>
Regular and predictable attendance, safely perform tasks and adherence to all safety policies and procedures are essential functions of this job</li>
</ul>
