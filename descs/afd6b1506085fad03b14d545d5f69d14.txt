 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Req. ID: 145604</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Micron Technology’s vision is to transform how the world uses information to enrich life and our commitment to people, innovation, tenacity, collaboration, and customer focus allows us to fulfill our mission to be a global leader in memory and storage solutions. This means conducting business with integrity, accountability, and professionalism while supporting our global community.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
As an Associate Project Manager Intern, you will provide recommendations and efforts to continually improve the work processes and scheduling in the Customer Qualification Team (CQT), ensuring our Customers are receiving an outstanding qualification experience and their requirements are balanced from a strategic and product basis, ensuring Micron SSDs meet or exceed the needs of our Customers in the future!</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
To achieve this, you will focus your work in the following areas:</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Project qualification management and process efficiency – Work with Technical Customer Managers (TCM’s), Customer Application Engineers (CAE’s) and other Micron groups, to review processes, document and measure processes, lead meetings to develop project and customer qualification scheduling baselines and implementation of a tracking system, within the Customer Qualification Team.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Shadowing a Qualification Manager, TCMs - Participate in the Marketing Requirement Document process to ensure that hardware, software and encryption requirements are being driven into new SSD designs and that qualification testing and certification expectations are well understood.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Working with Partners and Micron Engineering to ensure that all new Micron’s SSD products are tested and can be qualified OEM customers and their ODM’s.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
We also encourage our intern to report to team and management on internship successes!</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Roles and Responsibilities:</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>
Process review, development / optimization of Technical Customer Managers activities around Customer engagements (e.g., concept reviews, design reviews, qualification readiness reviews and program qualification schedules)</li>
<li>
Documentation and design of training for new /revised schedule baselines</li>
<li>
Review, identification and documentation of standard methodologies in the group, for development of templates that will supplement/support the schedules</li>
<li>
Review of scheduling systems, including resource loaded schedules</li>
<li>
Potential implementation of scheduling / automation system(s)</li>
<li>
Monitor and work towards a comprehensive understanding of program and Customer schedule baselines</li>
<li>
Lead the key activities required to launch new products into the market, from a qualification perspective</li>
</ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Location:</b></h2>
<ul><li>
Position is based in Boise, Idaho or Longmont, Colorado</li></ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Qualifications:</b></h2>
<p>
Successful candidates for this position will have:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Passion for scheduling, process review and improvement from an internal consulting perspective.</li>
<li>
Able to evaluate business goals from a high-level and understand how processes support the achievement of business goals.</li>
<li>
Able to analyze data sets, draw insights, and communicate those insights effectively.</li>
<li>
Excellent interpersonal relationships and able to interview and gather insights from teams and individuals, including facilitation of process improvement meetings.</li>
<li>
Experience with Customer qualification a plus, but not required</li>
</ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Education:</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>
Pursuing a Bachelor’s Degree in Engineering, Business – Program/Project Management, Systems Engineering, Industrial Engineering, Computer Engineering or a related field or equivalent degree.</li>
<li>
Pursuing MBA or Master’s Degree in Business (desired, but not required).</li>
<li>
Graduation date should not be prior to September 1, 2020</li>
</ul>
