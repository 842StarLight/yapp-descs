<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div><b>Specific Tasks and Responsibilities:
</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Creates and manages all healthcare job postings</li>
<li>
Maintenance of job requisitions in ERP’s Applicant Tracking System, including progress notes and updates</li>
<li>
Pipelining of qualified candidates for on-going requirements</li>
<li>
Screens applications against job performance work statement qualifications</li>
<li>
Candidate first contact, follow-up, and close-out</li>
<li>
Initial collection of and primary source verification of core credentialing documents</li>
<li>
Verification and validation of candidate references</li>
<li>
Profile creation for submission of candidates</li>
<li>
Coordinates interviews with Hiring Managers and customers</li>
<li>
Completes incumbent capture and processing</li>
<li>
Networks with industry, references, and contacts to develop leads</li>
<li>
Solicits current staff and customers for referrals</li>
<li>
Conducts pre- and post-sales market salary analysis</li>
<li>
May attends career fairs, trade shows, etc…</li>
<li>
Communicates daily with Hiring Managers on status of requisitions, candidates, etc…</li>
</ul>
