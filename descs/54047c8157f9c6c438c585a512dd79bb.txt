<h2>Credit Analyst- Portfolio Management</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>The Credit Analyst assists the Lending team by scanning credit and financial files, assembling credit files, procuring credit reports, and analyzing a various financial statements and individual and business tax returns. The Credit Analyst is also responsible for assessing financial data to estimate degree of risk involved in extending credit to firms or individuals.
</p>
<p><b>PRIMARY FUNCTIONS </b>(and other duties may be assigned)</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Prepares and assembles credit files for review; assures basic information has been presented in a complete and accurate manner.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Performs scanning and callbacks to complete information shown in credit files; complete letters of requests for credit information.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Reviews credit files to ensure compliance with regulations and bank standards.</p>
</li>
<li><p>Prepares files for Cabrera audits and FDIC Examinations.
</p></li>
<li>
<p>Updates and maintains portfolio using tickler files</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Compiles returns and financial statements for new and existing credit requests.</p>
</li>
<li><p>Analyzes and spreads business financial statements, personal financial statements, and tax returns for underwriting packages.
</p></li>
<li>
<p>Prepares and audits Asset Based Lending – Borrowing Base Certificates from ABL Borrowers.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Completes pre-flights and assists with credit presentations for new loans.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Completes credit authorizations for renewals, extensions, and refinance of existing credit.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Runs due diligence (OFAC, Suits, Liens, Judgments, D&amp;B, UCC, and Consumer Credit Reports) for all TPPP Payment Solutions’, “Annual Reviews,” and “New Customers.”</p>
</li>
<li><p>Follows the same due diligence for loan renewals, extensions, and new credit requests.
</p></li>
<li>
<p>Performs industry analysis to be included in the credit authorizations.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Incorporates due diligence findings into credit write-ups.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Reads appraisals and writes-up the pertinent sections involved with Cost Approach, Income Capitalization, and Sales Comparisons.</p>
</li>
<li><p>Reads writes-up the environmental reports and Phase I reports.
</p></li>
<li>
<p>Proofreads Credit Authorizations that are completed, but not yet submitted to Credit Admin.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Performs site visits and completes site-visit worksheets.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Prepares annual reviews on Credit Administration approved template.</p>
</li>
<li><p>Assists in loan administration matters such as taking notes and preparing Loan Committee minutes.
</p></li>
</ul>
