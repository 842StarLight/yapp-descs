The Department of Veterans Affairs/Military Health Service (VAMHS) Account is looking for an Analyst/Consultant/Associate Manager to support with growth strategy and capture for the VA’s transformation work. The VA is a citizen-focused, complex agency with a range of large information technology and management consulting related opportunities that Accenture is working to shape. This role would involve direct day-to-day strategy work with the VAMHS’s Growth &amp; Strategy Managing Director and Manager. The account is looking for an entrepreneurial quick learner with interest in health &amp; human services, growth strategy, and federal sales.</div>
<div>
<br>
Role: The Senior Analyst/Consultant/Associate Manager would spearhead work to:<br>
<ul>
<li>Define growth campaigns for IDIQ style contracts</li>
<li>
Develop contract roadmaps</li>
<li>
Develop project plans, milestone charts, teaming matrices</li>
<li>
Build management approaches and pricing plans</li>
<li>
Build outreach strategies</li>
<li>
Create and own relationships with teaming partners</li>
<li>
Perform client and industry research on veteran affairs, military health, health &amp; human services, health IT and present to Accenture and external leadership</li>
<li>
Liaise with teaming partners and Accenture leadership</li>
</ul>
