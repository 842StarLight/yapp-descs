<p>
Jewell, a CRH company, is a division within Oldcastle APG. APG is North America’s leading manufacturer and supplier of concrete masonry, dry mix, and hardscape products. With over $2B in sales, APG operates across 36 states and 5 Canadian provinces through a network of over 195 operating locations and more than 6,500 employees.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Job Description Summary</b><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
Transport construction material to job site in a safe and timely manner</li>
<li>
Assist and supervise loading of truck to ensure proper order and weight distribution</li>
<li>
Receive from dispatcher invoice for materials, directions to job site and any special instructions and follow them to the detail</li>
<li>
Maintain complete and accurate records such as DOT drivers' daily logs, fuel receipts, delivery documentation and any other required documents according to state and federal regulations</li>
<li>
Build and maintain good customer relationships</li>
<li>
Perform pre-trip inspections of assigned vehicle and report immediately any concerns or problems</li>
<li>
Obtain shipping bills and necessary signatures and return completed paperwork to the shipping department</li>
<li>
Provide and document proper routine maintenance of assigned truck and loading/unloading equipment</li>
<li>
Survey job site and unload cargo in the best area available in a safe manner</li>
<li>
Develop very good knowledge of inventory, sizes, types, colors, and pallet package</li>
<li>
Monitor and immediately report problems with assigned product quality, customer satisfaction, and traffic</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Requirements<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma / equivalent</li>
<li>
Experience with Moffett</li>
<li>
CDL A required</li>
<li>
Must have at least 3 years of experience operating a CDL A vehicle - flatbed is a plus</li>
<li>
Detailed oriented and good communication skills a plus</li>
<li>
Must be at least 24 years of age</li>
<li>
Must have a clean driving record</li>
<li>
Forklift certified a plus</li>
<li>
Bi-lingual is a plus</li>
</ul>
