<p><b>WINDSTREAM ENTERPRISE</b><br>
<b>Major Account Executive</b></p>
<p>
Windstream Enterprise has an immediate need for a Major Account Executive interested in working alongside fellow WE employees in building a foundation for our customers’ success. We do this by acting as Pathfinders, pursuing a daily dedication to the following behaviors:</p>
<ul>
<li> We approach each situation with a responsive and highly agile style, always meeting customers on their own terms.</li>
<li> We take the time to listen, learn and acknowledge the context of a customer’s pain points.</li>
<li> We dig deeper into problems and seek new and better answers for our customers’ unique requirements.</li>
<li> We guide customers towards the right solutions by leveraging our deep expertise and portfolio of innovative next-generation technology.</li>
<li> We deliver a direct response to our customer’s needs, focusing on technologies that set the stage for growth.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
JOB DESCRIPTION</b></p>
<p><b>
As a Windstream Enterprise Major Account Executive, you will:</b></p>
<ul>
<li> Meet or exceed company sales metrics by gaining profitable business from both existing customers and new logo Fortune 1000 companies through a strategic and consultative approach and becoming a recognized expert in the marketplace.</li>
<li> Gain access to Fortune 1000 C-level executives at targeted companies in the area by prospecting deep and wide within the organization; leverage relationships with referral partners and key industry partners; communicate and collaborate with other internal sales teams; gain referrals from current customers.</li>
<li> Generate sales to new logo enterprise-sized companies by developing executive sponsorship with top-level influencers; build long-term, mutually beneficial relationships with all influencers; listen to learn all critical business issues (CBIs) and wins/losses of all influencers; develop and execute a strategic approach for selling multiple complex solutions with multiple buying influencers; challenge conventional thinking to enhance credibility and establish differentiation from competitors; present the company brand ; research public and private company information; gain insight of prospect’s business strategy; analyze prospect’s competitive market and compare it with similar companies.</li>
<li> Organize internal resources appropriate for each customer opportunity; deliver customized solutions which exceed customer expectations and address CBIs.</li>
<li> Generate additional sales to existing customers by enhancing and strengthening relationships with C-Level executives and earning “trusted advisor” status with all levels of the organization through a collaborative and consultative approach; influence future business and operational initiatives by continually updating the customer on how to leverage new product offerings to achieve desired results; develop and maintain a strategic plan for future sales; manage multiple complex opportunities at the same time.</li>
<li> Follow a disciplined, structured sales process for complex sales by deploying solution selling and strategic selling tools and techniques; submit activity and results reports on time; manage sales opportunity progress through Salesforce; submit accurate orders in a timely manner.</li>
<li> Achieve expert status in the marketplace by demonstrating Windstream Enterprise cultural beliefs; properly preparing for each customer interaction; applying superior salesmanship and persuasive presentation skills at the Executive Level; viewing customer objections as a natural part of the process and maintaining poise under pressure to seamlessly handle those objections; use analytics to track and improve performance; effectively manage time; stay abreast of industry trends to improve business acumen.</li>
</ul>
