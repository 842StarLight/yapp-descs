<p><b>WINDSTREAM ENTERPRISE</b><br>
<b>Major Customer Advocate
</b></p>
<p>Windstream Enterprise has an immediate need for an Major Customer Advocate interested in working alongside fellow WE employees in building a foundation for our customers’ success. We do this by acting as Pathfinders, pursuing a daily dedication to the following behaviors:</p>
<ul>
<li> We approach each situation with a responsive and highly agile style, always meeting customers on their own terms.</li>
<li> We take the time to listen, learn and acknowledge the context of a customer’s pain points.</li>
<li> We dig deeper into problems and seek new and better answers for our customers’ unique requirements.</li>
<li> We guide customers towards the right solutions by leveraging our deep expertise and portfolio of innovative next-generation technology.</li>
<li> We deliver a direct response to our customer’s needs, focusing on technologies that set the stage for growth.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
JOB DESCRIPTION</b></p>
<div><b>
Responsibilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Advises assigned accounts (multi-national/global accounts) by selling new or additional products and services to existing customers;</li>
<li>
Finding additional business in multiple areas within the existing customer;</li>
<li>
Identifying essential customer prospects and evaluating their position among competitors;</li>
<li>
Researching and analyzing sales options.</li>
<li>
Drives organizational management and effectiveness for customers by planning for evolutionary product development;</li>
<li>
Communicating with internal resources the project plan; and bridging any gaps within the product delivery process.</li>
<li>
Manages formal RFP responses by collaborating with internal and external stakeholders;</li>
<li>
Setting strategic sales objectives unique to the customer; and aligning sales goals and quotas.</li>
<li>
Manages customer projects and charters that relate to specific key accounts by overseeing project plans from conception to completion; ensuring project specifications are met;</li>
<li>
Facilitating customer proposals;</li>
<li>
Providing recommendations regarding setting metrics and tracking results.</li>
<li>
Manages customer satisfaction and service delivery by anticipating potential service problems;</li>
<li>
Continuing communication during difficult times; and monitoring satisfaction.</li>
<li>
Creates business development plans and forecasting by preparing efforts on behalf of the customer;</li>
<li>
Assessing customer needs on a regular basis;</li>
<li>
validating strategic sales model alignment;</li>
<li>
Maintaining accurate data for all sales activity.</li>
<li>
Processes product renewals and retention of existing customers by building customer rapport;</li>
<li>
Conducting specific customer product analysis for expiration;</li>
<li>
Following customer industry trends for enhancements to existing product platforms.<br>
</li>
</ul>
