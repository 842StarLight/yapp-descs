<h2>Modeler, Credit Analytics</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>
<div><b>Job Summary</b></div>
<div>
Develops insights into customer behavior in the areas of Underwriting (account acquisition and credit limit management), Collections/Recovery and Fraud through advanced analytics such as model building and R &amp; D (encompassing all product types and channels). Uses the insights to recommend actionable solutions that increase bottom line profit. Ensures accurate and timely implementation of solutions.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Reports To Sr. Mgr, Credit Modeling</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
Essential Job Functions</b></div>
<div>
Completes advanced analytics related to score/model building with the overarching goal of increasing bottom line profit, including the following:</div>
<div>
Extracts data, conducts exploratory data analysis, and addresses common data issues like missing and extreme values</div>
<div>
Develops predictive models/scorecards/segmentations that predict customer behavior such as delinquency, write-off, payment rate, revolve rate, profitability, etc.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Oversees outsourced models, including providing data, answering questions and providing feedback</div>
<div><div>
<div>
Provides support for implementation of models, including monitoring when applicable</div>
<div>
Provides recommendations/insights regarding how to use models</div>
<div>
Supports partners by providing forecasting assistance when needed</div>
<div>
Provides analytical enhancement (i.e., uses advanced tools to enhance analyses)</div>
</div></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Ensures that regulatory/administrative tasks are completed, including the following:</div>
<div><div>
<div>
Monitors and validates current scores regularly; documents results</div>
<div>
Makes recommendations regarding when scores should be redeveloped</div>
<div>
Documents modeling and analysis processes and results for internal and regulatory purposes</div>
</div></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Focuses on R &amp; D tasks, including the following:</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><div>
<div>
Researches industry trends in analytical approach and new techniques to enhance predictive modeling capability</div>
<div>
Tests/analyzes new methodologies</div>
<div>
May evaluate new scores from vendors by performing cost/benefit analysis</div>
<div>
May evaluate new products from vendors by performing cost/benefit analysis</div>
<div>
Translates analytical results into useful recommendations that increase bottom line profit</div>
<div>
Provides support for implementation of solutions</div>
<div>
Monitors impact of solutions that are implemented</div>
</div></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Acts as a consultant for the Analytics team, including the following:</div>
<div><div>
<div>
Collaborates with internal partners regarding business needs/problems</div>
<div>
Generates ideas regarding specific analyses, models, etc., designed to increase bottom line profit</div>
<div>
Designs and plans models/analyses</div>
<div>
Communicates/presents results to various audiences</div>
</div></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Ensures that up-to-date tools are researched, including the following:</div>
<div><div>
<div>
Evaluates new modeling software and completes cost/benefit analysis</div>
<div>
Educates department on tools that may assist them/provides support</div>
<div>
Makes recommendations regarding tool selection and usage</div>
</div></div>
<div>
