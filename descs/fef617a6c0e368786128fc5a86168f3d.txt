<h2>(Mortgage) Loan Processor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader">
<b>Job Summary</b><b>
</b>
</h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Under the direction of the Branch Manager, Operations Manager and/or Processing Supervisor, evaluates all information supplied on the mortgage loan application, verifies the</div>
<div>
validity of the information and assembles a completely documented file to Underwriting and Closing. Acts as the primary liaison between the loan officer, loan officer assistant, realtors,</div>
<div>
builders and the customer(s).</div>
<div></div>
<br>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader">
<b>Essential Functions</b><b>
</b>
</h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li><div>Maintains knowledge of all policies and modifications to the guidelines and standards of the company, federal housing programs, investors, and private mortgage insurers.</div></li>
<li><div>
Maintains and updates loan application information within loan origination software and other applications.</div></li>
<li><div>
As applicable, orders appraisals, title, survey, verifications, and any other items required for loan approval; conducts regular follow-ups with outside sources regarding outstanding documents.</div></li>
<li><div>
Verifies the data collected, analyzes, and decides whether it meets guidelines or if other documentation is needed; documents communication through the conversation log; ensures loan application is in compliance with underwriting, investor, RESPA, and HMDA guidelines.</div></li>
<li><div>
Calculates income, reviews assets and liabilities.</div></li>
<li><div>
Reviews all disclosures for completeness and compliance.</div></li>
<li><div>
Completes final evaluation and analysis of completed application packages and submits eligible files specifying any applicable conditions.</div></li>
<li><div>
Monitors rate lock information for data integrity and expiration dates; communicates discrepancies to the Loan Officer.</div></li>
<li><div>
May maintain accurate pipeline to ensure proper projections, closings, and regulatory compliance with Company, Federal, and State standards.</div></li>
<li><div>
May prepare, when applicable, “Declination Letters.” Ensures accurate disclosure of the reason(s) for the adverse action as soon as it is determined that applicants do not qualify.</div></li>
<li><div>
May schedule closing appointments with respective parties (i.e. title companies, closing attorneys, corporate closing department and customers etc.) Meets customer service standards as outlined by management.</div></li>
<li><div>
May facilitate training of junior or newly hired processors regarding specific Prime operations including file flow, loan origination software, and other applications.</div></li>
<li><div>
Updates loan officer, loan officer assistant, realtors, builders and customers on loan status.</div></li>
<li><div>
Maintains communication with customers throughout the loan process, assisting them with any questions and notifying them of any additional requirements.</div></li>
<li><div>
Maintains a professional image and adheres to standards consistent with company policies and procedures.</div></li>
<li><div>
Other duties as assigned.</div></li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader">
<b>Job Requirements</b><b>
</b>
</h2>
<div><div><div><ul>
<li><div>High school diploma or equivalent.</div></li>
<li><div>
Minimum of 1 year mortgage banking experience as a processor or junior processor, required.</div></li>
<li><div>
Must have working knowledge of Fannie Mae/Freddie Mac and FHA/VA policies and guidelines, as well as, Regulatory Mortgage Compliance, state, federal, local government, private investors and insurers.</div></li>
<li><div>
Must have working knowledge, or the ability to learn all operating systems and possess the ability to facilitate training.</div></li>
<li><div>
Proficient in Excel, Word, Outlook, with the ability to learn other mortgage-related programs.</div></li>
<li><div>
Excellent written and verbal communication skills.</div></li>
<li><div>
Excellent organizational and time management skills.</div></li>
<li><div>
Demonstrated ability to interact effectively with branch, regional and corporate personnel, as well as realtors, sales agents, builders, appraisers, title companies, etc.</div></li>
<li><div>
Demonstrated judgment and decision making ability.</div></li>
<li><div>
Must be available to work all regularly scheduled hours; arriving at scheduled reporting time and at the prescribed time after any breaks or lunch periods.</div></li>
<li><div>
Must be able to work overtime, as required.</div></li>
</ul></div></div></div>
</div></div>
