<h2>SVP Global Customer Success</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">KnowBe4, Inc. is a high growth information security company. We are the world's largest provider of new-school security awareness training and simulated phishing. KnowBe4 was created to help organizations manage the ongoing problem of social engineering. Tens of thousands of organizations worldwide use KnowBe4's platform to mobilize their end users as a last line of defense and enable them to make better security decisions, every day.
<br><br>
We are ranked #1 best place to work in technology nationwide by Fortune Magazine and have placed #1 or #2 in The Tampa Bay Top Workplaces Survey for the last four years. We also just had our 25th record-setting quarter in a row!
<br><br>
The SVP of Global Customer Success is responsible for overseeing all aspects of the organization's global customer success initiatives and work to drive results in Customer growth, satisfaction and retention. In addition, he or she will help improve and maintain all of the CSM Department's processes and functions for the global teams, and will ensure that the Global CSM Department meets and/or exceeds its monthly quotas.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Build, manage, and mentor a team of Customer Success Managers (CSMs)</li>
<li>Hire, fire, discipline, and promote subordinates on the CSM team</li>
<li>Increasing global customer health and retention</li>
<li>Drive operations for key stakeholders in the global Customer Success team</li>
<li>Work with Product, Marketing, Sales, Order Processing and Finance to build increasing customer health and net retention rates</li>
<li>Design and implement programs to increase effectiveness of the global Customer Success team</li>
<li>Oversee ongoing training, drilling and success of the individual Customer Success reps in coordination with Quality Control and Growth</li>
<li>Manage the Customer Success team's performance using proprietary data analytics tools.</li>
<li>Oversee and assist with the expansion of the CSM department</li>
<li>Thought leadership regarding account management, value management, customer onboarding, and customer success best practices</li>
<li>Create, implement, and maintain scalable procedures and policies to ensure that the CSM team has the ability to grow efficiently</li>
<li>Maximize the overall retention rate of existing customer relationships, with an 85% minimum standard.</li>
<li>Ensure customers are onboarded in a timely and efficient manner</li>
<li>Communicate with leadership in the CSM department to ensure that all procedures and policies are thoroughly understood and communicated to their subordinates in an efficient and effective method</li>
<li>Monitor statistics, dashboards, and other types of reports on an ongoing basis</li>
<li>Work closely with the Operations and Quality Control/Growth departments to update current processes and to administer any corrective action on employees who have areas of improvement</li>
<li>Coordinate with other departments to ensure all staff are aware of any changes to the CSM processes</li>
<li>Ensure accurate reporting of churn percentage to executive team</li>
</ul>
<b>Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree</li>
<li>Experience as an executive in SaaS industry required.</li>
<li>Demonstrated success in building and leading a Customer Success division in a Fortune 500 company with at least $350 million in revenues</li>
<li>Consistently deliver revenue numbers and produce accurate forecasts while maintaining high renewal rates</li>
<li>Strong understanding of SaaS industry and the business benefits</li>
<li>Ability to manage large groups</li>
<li>Salesforce Experience or CRM Experience</li>
<li>Experience with Gmail and Google Docs</li>
<li>Experience with MS Office (Word and Excel)</li>
<li>Experience with web browsers (Chrome, Internet Explorer, etc.)</li>
<li>Strong verbal and written communications</li>
<li>Excellent time management and organization skills</li>
<li>Previous management experience, VP level or higher preferred</li>
<li>SaaS or Security Software Experience a plus</li>
<li>Experience with Project Management a plus</li>
</ul>
