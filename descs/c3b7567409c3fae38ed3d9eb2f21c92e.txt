<h2>SDR, Partnerships Lead Generation</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<div>b8ta is a new company created to fix an old problem—brick-and-mortar retail. Our mission is to make retail accessible for all. We believe when makers have easy, direct access to physical retail, customers will in turn have unbiased access to what the world is creating.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
As a technology company building a better retail stores and software for the world’s most innovative brands, our team is full of extraordinary thinkers, creators, builders, early adopters, and rule-breakers. We're based in San Francisco and New York with multiple flagship and small format stores across the country—and we’re just getting started. Think you can help change retail? We'd love to meet you!</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
The ideal candidate should be a customer-centric, energetic, and highly-motivated individual who can thrive in a rapid growth environment. You have the ability to uncover new potential partners and communicate b8ta’s partner offerings to effectively drive an ongoing funnel of new partners for our sales team. Strong candidates will understand how to juggle volume every day, how to open doors, communicate clearly and sell interest in b8ta.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
We are always looking for the most innovative products from start-ups to enterprise brands. You, as a Sales Development Rep, would be at the forefront of finding the best by seeking out these brands/products and building relationships from scratch.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
You are responsible for producing a constant flow of qualified leads for the partnerships team across multiple verticals improving your efficiency over time. You will work closely with our Partnerships Managers to understand what type of leads are producing the highest impact to revenue. You will partner with our B to B marketing team to create outreach campaigns that generate interest in b8ta’s offerings and you will represent b8ta at relevant trade shows.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
This position is based in San Francisco or New York for the right candidate. This role reports to b8ta’s VP, Partnerships.</div>
</div>
