<h2>Sales Operations Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Kognito (www.kognito.com) is a health simulation company based in NYC. We develop role-play simulations where users engage in conversations with virtual humans and learn how to lead similar conversations in real-life that result in improved social, emotional, and physical health. We work with more than 300 health, education, and gov’t organizations in and outside of the U.S. To date, more than 1 million people have completed a Kognito simulation. Our team of 80+ people includes learning scientists, psychologists, software engineers, graphic artists, writers, and operation specialists.</p>
<p>
Kognito is a unit of Ascend Learning; LLC (www.ascendlearning.com). Ascend is a leading provider of technology-based educational, curriculum and assessment solutions for high-growth careers in a range of industries, including healthcare, fitness and wellness, and insurance and financial services. We provide outcomes-based solutions aimed at helping people enter, sustain and succeed in their chosen fields. Ascend employs more than 1,000 employees, with headquarters in Burlington, Mass., and offices in Kansas City metro, Phoenix metro, Minneapolis-St. Paul, Walnut Creek, California, Ann Arbor, Michigan, New York, and the U.K.</p>
<p>
We are searching for an individual who has a strong knowledge of Salesforce.com CRM and successful history with sales operations with a high growth, SaaS company. Contagious enthusiasm for Kognito’s mission and the passion to learn is essential in this role.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Own of all Sales and Client Experience metrics and reporting including pipeline, Marketing Qualified Leads (MQLs) and Sales Qualified Leads (SQLs) conversion rates, leads, aging, analysis, churn, territory and product and service analysis.</li>
<li>Salesforce reporting on the pipeline generation needs and variances for each segment. Work with sales leaders to uncover areas of revenue opportunity in our customer and prospect base.</li>
<li>Develop formal reporting packages for sales effectiveness and productivity. Conduct metrics reviews with sales leaders.</li>
<li>Partner with the key stakeholders to define market segment strategies and priorities based on opportunities and regional needs</li>
<li>Optimize the sales rep, manager experience in Salesforce.</li>
<li>Ensure sales processes &amp; policies are implemented effectively within the team</li>
<li>Manage the weekly, monthly &amp; quarterly cadence, including BoD reports, forecasting, planning, and opportunity management</li>
<li>Salesforce mastery of complex dashboards &amp; reports, custom report types, calculated fields, workflows needed to support MQLs through SQLs</li>
<li>A strong working knowledge of Marketo marketing automation software or similar application and associated rules is a real plus</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Skills and Experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to evaluate tools and applications to help further improve efficiency and effectiveness of the organization</li>
<li>A high comfort level with driving decisions through data and analysis, while influencing other departments to achieve immediate and midterm KPI’s</li>
<li>Deep experience modeling complex problems, both conceptually and tactically</li>
<li>A self-starter mentality that can operate independently with general direction and take appropriate action</li>
<li>Small company experience</li>
<li>Exceptional quantitative and MS Excel skills</li>
<li>Ability to run numerous activities and projects simultaneously</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree</li>
</ul>
