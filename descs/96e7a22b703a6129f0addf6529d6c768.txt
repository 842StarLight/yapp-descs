<h2>Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>TheHomeMag Minnesota is seeking a mature, honest, hardworking individual with an upbeat personality &amp; entrepreneurial spirit-to fill the role of account executive!</p>
<p>As a member of our Sales Team, you will manage the entire sales process, including prospecting, presentations, closing business and client retention using consultative methods. We work hard and have fun along the way! We are looking for a person with excitement, enthusiasm and excellent closing skills. We are dedicated to providing our employees with the tools needed to succeed on a daily basis and ultimately reach their long-term career goals.</p>
<p>TheHomeMag was founded in 2002 as a single magazine location in southwest Florida with a simple purpose to drive local business to local home improvement businesses through a high-end direct mail magazine. Today, we are a collection of 61 locations with the same common core purpose: to connect reputable local home improvement companies with the top single family owner-occupied homes in every market we publish by designing client-specific brand-to-action advertising.</p>
<p>TheHomeMag Minnesota was started in January of 2017 and has had a fast paced and exciting start! We're growing, and looking for an individual to grow with us!</p>
