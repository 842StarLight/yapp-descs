<h2>Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>The Northern California Division of Safeway has an opening for a Marketing Manager located at the Norcal Division Office in Pleasanton, CA.</p>
<p><b>
POSITION PURPOSE:
</b></p>
<p>Responsible for identifying and understanding consumer trends using available internal and external data sources. To create, implement and support marketing initiatives across our regions, which provide solutions that meet unique customer needs, build brand loyalty and drive incremental sales.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
KEY ACCOUNTABILITIES:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Provide excellent Customer Service for Store Management teams by working with them on marketing direction, questions, and strategy</li>
<li>Assist stores with total store Customer Service by developing marketing and merchandising strategies around customer needs/wants</li>
<li>Identify and understand customer trends in existing and emerging markets.</li>
<li>Create, implement and support all marketing initiatives for the Safeway Northern California Division of stores including the Andronico's banner of stores.</li>
<li>Develop yearly theme calendar in conjunction with internal business team objectives.</li>
<li>Negotiate with vendors for buy in on the marketing plan and for execution on digital, web, social, print and in-store platforms.</li>
<li>Coordinate with vendors and stores the execution of the agreed marketing plans.</li>
<li>Provide post-financial analysis and recommendations for future marketing plans.</li>
<li>Develops, communicates and oversees the content and execution of all social, web, mobile, digital, blog, and email campaigns for all banners of the Safeway Northern California Division.</li>
<li>Prepare financial projections for proposed marketing programs as well as updating the business on the status of existing programs.</li>
<li>Develop project plans for future marketing initiatives as needed to support the business and manage their implementation.</li>
<li>Manage the role(s) of Third-party vendors during the implementation of their marketing programs.</li>
<li>Work with the regional marketing teams to effectively implement and manage all programs and measure their effectiveness.</li>
<li>Visits stores as necessary to observe results of marketing plans and/or support the marketing plan.</li>
<li>Supports and implements corporate initiatives.</li>
<li>Performs other duties as assigned by supervisor.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
KNOWLEDGE AND EXPERIENCE:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Education Level:
</b><ul><li>Bachelor's Degree in business, Marketing or related field or equivalent experience. Post-graduate MBA preferred.</li></ul>
</li>
<li>
<b>Experience Level:
</b><ul>
<li>1-3 years Retail Grocery Store Management experience preferred</li>
<li>3-5 years marketing and/or merchandising experience preferred</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Skills and Experiences:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Previous store operations experience or similar experience where a strong understanding of operations and marketing approaches has been gained is preferred</li>
<li>Financial, mathematical, statistical, marketing, merchandising and consumer research background to read, interpret, and understand how internal and external factors influence financial results. Ability to forecast and analyze data to support a marketing decision.</li>
<li>Strong project development skills with the ability to execute and see plans through to completion.</li>
<li>Proficiency using Microsoft Word, Excel and PowerPoint; and the ability to quickly learn and use other software packages.</li>
<li>High degree of organizational skills with the ability to multi-task; detail oriented and flexible.</li>
<li>Possess strong analytical and problem-solving skills. Experienced in taking initiative and making decisive yet appropriate decisions.</li>
<li>Must have strong written and verbal communication skills as well as interpersonal skills to develop and maintain effective business relationships within and outside the company.</li>
<li>Strong negotiation skills required.</li>
<li>Ability to prioritize and manage changing requirements and short timelines, as well as multiple overlapping projects simultaneously.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
TRAVEL REQUIREMENTS:</b> Approximately 25% depending on projects assigned-expected to visit retail stores</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
PHYSICAL ENVIRONMENT:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Most work is performed in a temperature-controlled office environment.</li>
<li>Incumbent may sit for long periods of time at desk or computer terminal.</li>
<li>Incumbent may use calculators, keyboards, telephone, and other office equipment in the course of normal workday.</li>
<li>Ability to stand and walk for long periods of time.</li>
<li>Ability to reach, lift, stack, and maneuver objects of varying dimensions and weights up to approximately 55 lbs.</li>
<li>Ability to push and pull fully loaded hand trucks and pallet jacks</li>
<li>Stooping, bending, twisting, and reaching may be required in completion of job duties.</li>
<li>Workday is fast paced; holiday, evening and weekend work may be required.</li>
<li>Some travel may be required</li>
</ul>
