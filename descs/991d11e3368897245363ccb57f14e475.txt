<h2>Leasing Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Fairfield Residential is looking for a Leasing Consultant to float between two properties. Berkdale Apartment has 296 units and is located in Riverside and Hensley at Carona Pointe has 628 units located in Corona.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>From discovering the prospects needs and matching them with the perfect apartment, to nurturing the resident experience while supporting the property’s needs; our Leasing Consultants are critical to building our apartment communities. Every day is an opportunity to meet new people and turn them into a loyal customer.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>We are looking for dynamic, top performing sales people with high energy and superior sales and customer service experience. The ideal candidate must thrive in a fast paced leasing office, juggle various tasks and work well within a team.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>What you can expect to do in this role:
</p>
<ul>
<li>You will be the first point of contact with prospective residents, current residents, vendors or Fairfield associates.
</li>
<li>This position will tour, qualify and sell the property’s features to prospective residents.
</li>
<li>Manage the leasing, closing and move in process with our new residents.
</li>
<li>Initial questions most likely come through you first. You must be quick to determine the customer’s needs, resolve and/or escalate to a higher level as permitted.
</li>
<li>Fostering new relationships with our customers is vital to the success of this position.
</li>
<li>We look to you to use your customer friendly personality as well as your ability to diffuse/resolve difficult situations while nurturing relationships.
</li>
<li>Using your strong administrative and computer skills, this position will manage various tasks, such as managing lease files, updating necessary paperwork and computer systems, completing marketing/advertising tasks, updating social media sites, etc.
</li>
<li>Weekends are required; must be able to work a flexible schedule to include weekends, evenings and holidays.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>What you can bring to the role:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Superior sales and customer service skills.
</li>
<li>Prior leasing experience is helpful.
</li>
<li>Strong time management and organizational skills.
</li>
<li>Strong attention to detail and good follow through.
</li>
<li>Professional appearance and demeanor.
</li>
<li>Superior ability to help our customers and resolve any issues with professionalism and clear communication.
</li>
<li>Strong typing and computer skills as well as knowledge of Microsoft Outlook, Word and Excel.
</li>
<li>Experience using Yardi or other related property management accounting software is preferred.
</li>
<li>Your ability to manage several tasks at once while remaining detailed and well organized.
</li>
<li>Good follow through is necessary so our customers feel their needs are recognized and met.
</li>
<li>Strong adherence to ethical standards including, but not limited to, the ability to maintain confidentiality and maintain fiduciary responsibility.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>What is required:
</p>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or equivalent.
</li>
<li>Valid driver’s license.
</li>
<li>Real estate and/or other license as required by law.</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
