<h2>Technician - Body Sculpting</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>At <b>Looking Great Body Sculpting </b>we offer state of the art, non-invasive aesthetic services for your face and body that help you look and feel your best. Our goal is to help our clients achieve life changing results, whether it’s to prevent or address the signs of aging with healthier, more youthful skin, or to improve body shape. Being a part of the team gives you the opportunity to watch our clients change their lives for the better. All services are safe, effective, FDA approved procedures that are being administered across the country. This is a great opportunity for talented individuals looking for a lucrative career helping patients achieve their goals.</p>
<p>*Responsibilities<br>*After completion of training and certification, technician will perform facial and body treatments in accordance with established treatment protocols, scope of practice, organizational policies and procedures, and all other governing standards. They will drive clinic facial and product sales to hit monthly targets and are responsible for following up with clients to renew their interest in service offerings.</p>
<p>Essential functions include, but are not limited to, the following:</p>
<ul>
<li>Administer facial and body treatments in accordance with Looking Great Body Sculpting policies and procedures</li>
<li>Establish and maintain rapport with clients in order to ensure overall customer service satisfaction of clients</li>
<li>Provide consultations for clients and determine best treatment strategy to meet the client’s desired goals</li>
<li>Evaluate client’s suitability for treatment and perform treatments that same day</li>
<li>Comprehensive knowledge of all service offerings</li>
<li>Promotion and sale of services and packages</li>
<li>Educate clients on all treatment processes including pre and post-treatment care guidelines</li>
<li>Maintain cleanliness and appearance of treatment rooms between each client</li>
<li>Annotate and post appropriate information and maintain accurate client treatment records/data in the client’s medical chart</li>
<li>Sustain proficiency through continuous education and trainings</li>
<li>Collaborate with sales and ops teams to drive facial and product sales to achieve monthly clinic targets based on metrics set by the company</li>
<li>Participates in local marketing and networking events</li>
<li>Builds self-generated client base</li>
<li>All other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
