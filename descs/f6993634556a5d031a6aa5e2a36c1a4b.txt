<div><b>Corporate Functions – Marketing &amp; Communications</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>As part of this team, you’ll play a key role in helping Accenture execute its strategy. You’ll work with a diverse team of innovative marketing experts who focus on the following areas: global brand management and advertising; global events and sponsorships; internal communications; marketing research; media and industry analyst relations; corporate and financial communications; internet marketing; recruitment marketing; client-centric marketing; and industry, service line and geographic marketing campaigns.</div>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>YOUR ROLE</b>: <b>H&amp;PS Public Service Industry Marketing Manager</b>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>Manager responsible for managing and running a Global Public Service campaign. This person will be responsible for driving differentiation and creating a relevant client experience. Additionally, this role will drive collaboration with the North America public service national strategy.</div>
<div></div>
<div>Responsibilities:</div>
<ul>
<li>Act as the analytics lead the Health &amp; Public Service Marketing team
</li>
<li>Leverage quantitative analysis to analyze and optimize the client journey and experience
</li>
<li>Connect digital, cross-channel analytics with CRM to deliver closed loop reporting including client opportunities and revenue impact
</li>
<li>Measure the client journey across touch points to develop lifecycle marketing strategies
</li>
<li>Aggregate and analyze multi-channel data to develop actionable insights on client engagement with marketing touchpoints to optimize marketing messages and channel strategy
</li>
<li>Drive and evangelize key performance indicators and success metrics to inform, measure and optimize client marketing strategy and execution
</li>
<li>Partner closely with Health &amp; Public Service Marketing stakeholders and Campaign Operations teams to connect client insights strategy with business opportunities and sales execution
</li>
<li>Inform and educate teams on M&amp;C measurement strategy, KPIs and reporting tools as well as developing ways to evolve tools to meet their needs</li>
</ul>
