<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p>Join a team with 50 years of success in the real estate industry! Lincoln Property Company is currently hiring for a Development Analyst . We are looking for an enthusiastic person who enjoys being hands-on and helping fellow employees. Does this sound like you? Join us!</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Founded in 1965, Lincoln Property Company is one of the largest and most diversified property management companies in the United States. Lincoln maintains a presence in more than 200 cities in the U.S. and 10 countries throughout Europe. Our mission is to be a company for people, a company about people.</p>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p>Candidate will be responsible for assisting the Northeast Regional Development Team and will report directly to the Executive Vice President-Northeast. Primary responsibility includes helping to underwrite development and acquisition opportunities and prepare investment books for investors and lenders that include extensive pro-forma modeling, preparing market analysis reports, comparative rent studies and all related project specific information. This candidate will assist in all aspects of the development process from concept to completion. Additionally, the candidate will be involved in supporting the permitting and entitlement efforts, coordinating and preparing presentations to various municipal bodies including state and local agencies.</p>
<div></div>
<br>
<p><b>
In alignment with Corporate goals, specific responsibilities for the Development Analyst will include:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Analyze development and acquisition projects using Excel modeling spreadsheets.</li>
<li>
Support office development team members.</li>
<li>
Assist Northeast Executive Vice President with various development and financial analysis.</li>
<li>
Maintain region-wide market knowledge including tracking development and acquisition activity.</li>
<li>
Monitor construction scheduling, hard cost and soft cost through project completion.</li>
<li>
Monitor research of Regional and US markets regarding rent, employment, permits and supply and demand trends, including the use of CoStar, Axiometrics, and RealPage analytics.</li>
<li>
Assist the construction management team including site visits, monthly draws and punch lists.</li>
<li>
Due diligence research on rent, expense, utilities, taxes and pre-development fees.</li>
<li>
Preparation of presentation materials to investors, lenders and various municipal authorities.</li>
<li>
Provide Asset Management to projects under construction as well as owned assets.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<b>
Additional tasks include</b>:</div>
<ul>
<li>
Tracking deal status through monthly reports.</li>
<li>
Assist in property management operations budget analysis for completed development projects.</li>
<li>
Coordinating due diligence processes from feasibility through lease-up and sale of development projects.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
