<h2>Room Reservation Agent</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Location: Pala, CA</p>
<p>
Promptly answer incoming reservations calls, taking, recording, and canceling of reservations of Individual, Group and Casino guests; Consistently provide prompt, courteous service to the guest in an efficient and professional manner at all times; Possess a warm, friendly, smiling voice with all callers to ensure guest pleasure and satisfaction; Maintain a pleasant and professional attitude when taking reservations; Maintain constant awareness of services, promotions and events knowledge offered by Pala Casino Resort &amp; Spa; Reporting directly to Room Reservation &amp; PBX Manager and Room Reservations Supervisor.</p>
<ul>
<li>Room reservation experience six months minimum - 1 year preferred.</li>
<li>Should have excellent telephone presentation, sales skills and ability to upsell a plus.</li>
<li>Should have experience in Opera Reservation Software.</li>
<li>Should have the ability to accurately type approximately 40-50 WPM.</li>
<li>Should have proper phone etiquette, people and strong communication skills.</li>
<li>Should read, write, speak and understand English, Bi-lingual a plus.</li>
<li>Should have general office skills.</li>
<li>Should have computer and working knowledge of the Internet.</li>
<li>High School Diploma or General Education Degree (GED) required.</li>
</ul>
<p>Essential Job Functions:</p>
<ul>
<li>Achieve a maximum number of confirmed reservations while excelling in guest service standards.</li>
<li>Strive for maximum productivity through prompt, proficient and accurate action in the performance of all assigned tasks.</li>
<li>Consistently strive to improve job knowledge and performance, thereby assisting in maintaining an efficient and effective operation.</li>
<li>Use upselling techniques, when possible by providing information of higher room value options, the caller may not have been aware existed.</li>
<li>Establish an open and close working relationship with the Room Reservation/PBX Manager and the Room Reservation Supervisor.</li>
<li>Perform all duties relative to the job assignment in the most productive manner at all times.</li>
<li>Assume a fair share of the workload by the performance of all assigned duties.</li>
<li>Actively interface with all casino/hotel departments to ensure company goals are met and exceeded.</li>
<li>Constantly be alert for ideas and improvements that could increase productivity and revenues.</li>
<li>Perform all assigned tasks with enthusiasm and a pleasant attitude toward executives, guests and fellow team members.</li>
<li>Work with fellow team members for mutual improvement in job skills and performance.</li>
<li>Ensure that the highest standards are always maintained.</li>
<li>Inform supervisor’s of guests’ and team member needs which may require their attention.</li>
<li>Consistently report to work on time and as scheduled.</li>
<li>Understand that flexibility to work extended hours to meet the department’s needs may be required.</li>
<li>Cooperate with fellow team members to develop and enhance mutual respect, teamwork and the feeling of camaraderie between team members of all departments.</li>
<li>Possess a thorough knowledge of all hotel functions and guest services.</li>
<li>Possess a thorough knowledge of all emergency procedures.</li>
<li>Possess a thorough knowledge of community services and events.</li>
<li>Maintain a constant awareness of safety and accident prevention, immediately report and/or complete an appropriate accident report form if an accident does occur.</li>
<li>Perform other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
