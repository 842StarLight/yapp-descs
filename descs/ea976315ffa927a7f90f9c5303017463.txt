<h2>Field Service Technician</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><div><div>
<div><b>Commercial Kitchen Maintenance, Repair, Installation, and Customer Interaction</b></div>
<div><b>
Company vehicle</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<b>
Location</b><b>:</b> From where you live – to where your customers are! Your office is your company vehicle!</div>
<div>
<b>
Perks &amp; Benefits</b><b>: </b>Company Van (use for personal too), maintenance, company credit card, tools, cell phone reimbursement, gas card, uniform, medical, dental, vision, bonus, &amp; 401k matching.</div>
<div>
<b>
Route</b>: <b>Miami Metropolitan Area</b>
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<b>
Think commercial kitchen.</b> The beverage machines, dishwashers, and other equipment used within restaurants, schools, and hospitals require someone who can calibrate, troubleshoot technical issues, and install. Every day is a little different with a great blend of mechanical work and people interaction.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>That’s where you come in!<br>
</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>Also, in keeping with food service regulations, anywhere food is prepared for customers, it is required they use certain<b> chemicals and processes to comply with department of health. In this role, you'll support our customers</b> with their purchases and equipment used for this portion of food service operations as well.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
<b>Position requirements</b><b>:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>
<b>
Education: </b>High School Diploma or GED is required.</li>
<li>
<b>
Experience:</b> 2 years previous sales and equipment repair experience OR an equivalent combination of education, training, and experience.
</li>
<li>
<b>Valid state driver's license </b>(required)</li>
<li>
<b>
Clean driving record</b> (we will run an MVR)</li>
<li>
Must be <b>21 years of age or older</b>
</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<b>
Work Schedule</b><b>:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>
This is a<b> Monday – Friday </b>schedule</li>
<li>
<b>
Start time</b>: May be between 7–8 AM</li>
<li>
<b>
End time</b>: 5 PM or until finish</li>
<li>
From time to time, we might need you other hours / days of the week (flexibility is important)</li>
<li>
This position requires the ability to provide emergency services on a 24 hour basis during the week, as well as 8 weekends per year - when on call<br>
</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<div></div>
<div><div><div>
<div><b>A day in the life of our Technicians includes:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Coordinate and perform scheduled installations, preventative maintenance, service for our customers</li>
<li>
Collaborate with other Field Service team members and outside sales representatives as needed</li>
<li>
Build relationships with existing customer accounts in order to grow sales</li>
<li>
Performs cycle counts on parts and equipment inventory</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Other skills, abilities and requirements needed for this position:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Excellent communication skills</li>
<li>
Preferred bilingual in Spanish</li>
<li>
Ability to conduct successful Sales opportunities</li>
<li>
Demonstrates ability to manage multiple tasks in a professional and timely manner</li>
<li><b>
Computer Skills</b></li>
<li>
Knowledge of Metrix and Mobile software applications (<b>preferred</b>)
</li>
<li>
<b>Mechanical Aptitude</b> - electrical, plumbing, health and sanitation codes. Ability to correctly interpret maintenance manuals, schematics, and wiring diagrams.</li>
<li>
Ability to perform physical aspects of the job, including heavy lifting of machines</li>
<li>
Successfully <b>pass</b> the <b>drug screen, motor vehicle report (MVR)</b> and <b>background check</b> requirements (criminal background check – we do not check your credit score).</li>
</ul>
