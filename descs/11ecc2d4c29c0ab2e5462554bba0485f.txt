<h2>Senior Strategic Sourcing Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>SUMMARY OF POSITION</b></p>
<p>
The Senior Strategic Sourcing Specialist is responsible for all tactical sourcing and supplier management duties associated with assigned product categories. The position must support Marketing and Sales in new product introductions and innovations to existing products. The Senior Strategic Sourcing Specialist is also responsible for supporting Operations by pursuing improvements in supplier performance, controlling inventory and the overall reduction of product and supply chain costs.</p>
<p><b>
AUTHORITY</b></p>
<p>
The Senior Strategic Sourcing Specialist is empowered to make decisions regarding:</p>
<ul><li> Supplier selection with supervisor approval.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Managing and communicating supplier performance and key actions for improvement for the suppliers within the category/categories that they manage after review with direct supervisor.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Establish operating agreements with the suppliers they manage after review with direct supervisor.</li></ul>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b>.</p>
<p>
Responsibilities:
</p>
<ul><li> Responsible for creating tactical approach for engaging in supplier negotiations.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ensuring cost control across categories under their responsibility through RFQ’s, Product and Category reviews, researching the redesign of product/packaging to take out costs, etc.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Responsible for maintaining awareness and knowledge surrounding market trends and cost drivers.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Responsible for assisting in the development of secondary source of supply, where applicable.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Responsible for consistent execution and integrity in managing all transactions between suppliers; this includes: Purchase Orders, Receipt of Goods, Pricing, Terms &amp; Conditions, and Invoice Resolutions.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Responsible for understanding all legal requirements for purchasing while upholding ethical supplier/partner philosophy.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Responsible for supplier performance and cost controls related to each supplier and product.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Responsible for consistent execution of all internal procedures related to but not limited to: Item and Supplier Set Up, Pricing, Documentation, and Financial Controls.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Responsible for working with supervisor to understand supplier’s product make-up and processes in order to help facilitate solutions to quality and service problems.</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Duties (other duties as assigned):</p>
<ul><li> Leadership: Strategic Sourcing Specialist must assist in all sourcing, engineering and inventory replenishment projects and problem solving with regard to assigned product categories.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Negotiations: This position is responsible for establishing programs to regularly identify the lowest total cost through request for proposals and product category reviews. This scope includes participation in negotiations regarding price, terms, quality, inventory programs, and lead time for assigned suppliers/categories.
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Market Trends: The position is responsible for monitoring relevant raw material market prices and to develop knowledge of industry for assigned category.
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Sourcing: The Strategic Sourcing Specialist has responsibility for locating and assisting supervisor in the qualification of potential suppliers. Responsible for assisting in the development of secondary suppliers as needed.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Supplier Relations: This position is responsible for initiating and maintaining effective relationships with actual and potential suppliers for their area of responsibility. Provide regular communications of supplier performance through evaluations.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Supplier Management: Assist supervisor in the facilitation of solutions to supplier quality problems, delivery performance issues, supplier supply chain constraints, etc. by fully understanding supplier processes, supply chain and product make-up to the extent that the supplier will share this information.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Product Development: Responsible for coordinating the design and development of new products, leveraging supplier engineering capabilities together with internal technical resources.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Continuous Improvement/Q.A.: In conjunction with Q.A., the Strategic Sourcing Specialist is responsible for establishment and maintenance of supplier quality control and continuous improvement and the development of supplier certification programs.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Purchase Orders: Responsible for placing orders as needed.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Expediting: Support Buyer/Planners in expediting purchase orders for problem situations as requested or as dictated by conditions.</li></ul>
<ul><li> New Item Process: Responsible for item set-up as needed.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Purchasing Policies: Strategic Sourcing Specialist is responsible for understanding and implementing industry accepted purchasing policies and procedures.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Product Specifications: Support the development of product specifications for all products.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ethics: Maintain and uphold ethical supplier/partner philosophy.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Legal: Must have some knowledge of legal aspect of purchasing to support company decisions</li></ul>
