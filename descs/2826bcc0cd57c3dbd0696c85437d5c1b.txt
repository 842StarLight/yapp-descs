<b>Job Title:</b> Customer Service Specialist<br>
<b>Reports To:</b> Customer Service Manager<br>
<br>
<b>Role:</b><br>
We are looking for a talented, competitive customer-service oriented individual who can thrive in a fast-paced, quick turn environment. The successful candidate will play a fundamental role in achieving daily sales goals and providing an excellent customer experience. They will act as the first point of contact for existing customers and will be an integral part of converting new leads into members of the Blue Chip family. This position requires the candidate to be extremely detail oriented, hard-working and dedicated. They must be able to multi-task, have excellent communication skills and be comfortable dealing one on one with clients and vendors.<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Identify customer needs and accurately take custom orders to achieve satisfaction.</li>
<li>Accurately enter detailed information and orders into our operating systems.</li>
<li>Create and submit complete, effective and thorough art requests for custom orders.</li>
<li>Manage the entire cycle of the order process to ensure deadlines are met.</li>
<li>Keep a clear and detailed record of all transactions.</li>
<li>Process and apply payments.</li>
<li>Diligently follow up on and perform account receivables.</li>
<li>Create up-sell opportunities.</li>
<li>Call and email existing and potential customers to increase sales.</li>
<li>Build relationships through excellent customer communication and a sense of urgency.</li>
</ul>
<br>
