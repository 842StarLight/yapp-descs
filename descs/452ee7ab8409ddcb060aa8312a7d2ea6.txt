<b>JOB DESCRIPTION</b><br>
<br>
Member Service Staff is responsible for Customer Service, Membership sales and changes, program/class registration, tours of the facility, organization and completion of tasks, and communication liaison between the members and/or public.<br>
<br>
<b>Schedule</b>: Some evenings and weekends required.<br>
<br>
<b>Service Skills:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Greet members and guest</li>
<li>Membership Sales/Changes and Updates</li>
<li>Show interest in member and guests concerns</li>
<li>Know members’ names and use them in their presence</li>
<li>Listen to suggestions and respond promptly</li>
<li>Address of complaints/concerns immediately</li>
<li>Serve as liaison between members and program supervisors</li>
<li>Provide information on facility services and programs</li>
<li>Program/Class Registration and Cancellation</li>
<li>Tours (implementing and follow-up)</li>
<li>Monitoring Access In/Out of the YMCA facility</li>
<li>Phone Inquiries and Registration</li>
<li>Always look for way to improve performance</li>
<li>Emphasize fun, make the Y a happy place with positive relationships</li>
<li>Maintaining Neat/Clean Area (includes services such as making coffee etc.)</li>
<li>Able to remain alert for extended periods of time</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
<b>Relationship Skills:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Make members feel comfortable and at ease.</li>
<li>Be open, friendly and approachable to members</li>
<li>Initiate interaction with and between members</li>
<li>Frequently ask if there is any way you can help</li>
<li>Ask about family, job, hobbies, get to know personally</li>
<li>Ask about their goals; progress and satisfaction</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
<b>Member Involvement Skills:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Be knowledgeable about programs areas and share information with members</li>
<li>Be knowledgeable about the YMCA, its programs and policies</li>
<li>Be informed and up-to-date on latest developments in your field</li>
<li>Recommend participation in a variety of Y programs</li>
<li>Be able to articulate the YMCA’s Mission, values and benefits</li>
<li>Ask members to contribute to the annual campaign</li>
<li>Be able to articulate the role and importance of volunteers</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
<b>Technology Skills:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Become proficient in Member Service software</li>
<li>Retail Sales/Inventory</li>
<li>Close-outs/Balancing Daily Work</li>
<li>Proficient on General Business Equipment such as: Fax Machine, Copier, Mobile App, Credit Card Machine, etc.)</li>
</ul>
<div>
<b>BENEFITS</b><br>
<b>FREE </b>city-wide YMCA membership<br>
Discounted Program Fees<br>
Flexible Work Schedules<br>
Training Opportunities</div>

<div><b>JOB REQUIREMENTS</b></div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Certifications:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Certified in 1st Aid and/or obtain certification within 30 days of hire (YMCA provides on-going classes at no cost to you if hired)</li>
<li>Certified in CPR and AED and/or obtain certification within 30 days of hire (YMCA provides on-going classes at no cost to you if hired)</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
<b>General Requirements:</b><br>
This position requires strong organizational, computer, communication and interpersonal skills. The ability and willingness to learn, be multi-task with good problem solving skills, along with excellent customer service skills are essential. Individuals with previous customer service experience and/or a related field along with a strong initiative, adaptable to change, and responsive are encouraged to apply.</div>
