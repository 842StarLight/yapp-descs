<h2>Tasting Room Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Bainbridge Organic Distillers is a Grain-To-Glass organic distillery located on Bainbridge Island, making award winning whiskies, vodkas, and gins.</p>
<p>We have an immediate opening for a tasting room associate to pour samples for distillery visitors, conduct tours of the distillery operation, and to represent us pouring and serving samples and discussing our products and processes at various tasting events. Tasting room staff may also be required to perform bottling and other back-of-house duties to assist the production team on an as-needed basis.</p>
<p>We are looking for someone with a great attitude, is self-motivated, and who will be articulate and enthusiastic about our company and its products.</p>
<p>This position has a 4-day work schedule – Monday, Tuesday, Friday, Saturday, Sunday, 10:AM to 5:30 PM. This is a full time position. Starting wage is $17.00 per hour, and includes health insurance coverage. <b>Bainbridge Organic Distillers pays 100% of insurance premiums.</b></p>
<p>FUNCTIONS/DUTIES:</p>
<ul><li>Greet all visitors to the Tasting Room, pour and discuss the different distilled spirits, and respond to customer</li></ul>
<p>questions, educating visitors on the process of making distilled spirits and what makes our products unique</p>
<ul>
<li>Maintain a positive and enthusiastic attitude</li>
<li>Ensure that every visitor has the best distillery tasting experience possible</li>
<li>Strong sales skills</li>
<li>Handle purchasing transactions, including operating cash register, accounting of daily sales, and processing returns and discounts</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Restock spirits and merchandise displays as needed</li>
<li>Maintain order and cleanliness on site (will include daily cleaning)</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned by manager</li>
</ul>
<p>JOB REQUIREMENTS:</p>
<ul>
<li>Prior experience in alcohol tasting rooms, or other service or hospitality experience (preferred)</li>
<li>Gain a solid knowledge of distilled spirits, spirit types, the mashing and distilling processes</li>
<li>Consistently demonstrate outstanding customer service skills when working with the public</li>
<li>Possess professional appearance and demeanor</li>
<li>Proficiency with Microsoft Word, Excel, and point of sale software/systems</li>
<li>Flexibility, can manage changing priorities and tasks with positive attitude</li>
<li>Ability to work alone and with minimal direction</li>
<li>Must be at least 21 years of age.</li>
<li>Possess or obtain MAST Servers Permit Class 12</li>
<li>Possess a valid Washington Driver’s license</li>
</ul>
<p>PHYSICAL REQUIREMENTS:</p>
<ul>
<li>Able to lift &amp; carry 45 pounds</li>
<li>Ability to stand for long periods of time</li>
<li>Ability to effectively stock spirits and move larger quantities of spirits with help of a dolly</li>
</ul>
