<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
The Team</b></h2>
<div>
At Edovo, our mission is to help everyone connected to incarceration build better lives. We’ve created a secure digital platform to expand education behind bars, reduce recidivism and to support the vocational, rehabilitation and communication needs of the 2.2 million people in jails and prisons. Users have access to thousands of hours of self-guided learning materials. Upon release, our learners have the option to continue their progress on the outside. Edovo offers courses in subjects ranging from basic literacy and GED to career exploration and cognitive behavioral therapy. We’re also building tools to keep people in touch with jobs, social services, friends, and family. Our team is committed to supporting opportunities for justice-involved people.</div>

Don’t take it just from us. Awards, publications, grants, and partnerships all around the country are recognizing the momentum and impact of Edovo. A select few: Global EdTech Startup Champion in London out of 1,000+ competitors; GIIRS Platinum Rating for social impact; MacArthur Foundation Grant Award Winner; Forbes article feature; Chicago Community Trust grant award winner; Impact Engine portfolio member; SXSW Civic Tech Innovation Pitch Competition runner-up; Chicago Innovation "Up and Comer" Award winner; and so much more.</div>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
The Role</b></h2>
<br>
<div>
Edovo’s Infrastructure Team designs and operates the network services that deliver Edovo’s secure digital platform to jails and prisons across the United States. This includes backend hosting systems, wide area transport, customer premise installations, end to end security, automation, and management solutions.</div>
<br>
<div>
We focus on security, stability, scalability, fault tolerance, and automation. We strive to provide our customers and internal teams with 100% system and service availability.</div>
<br>
<div>
Working under Edovo’s VP of Engineering the Director of Infrastructure will define and execute our network infrastructure strategy. This includes responsibility over on premises hardware, datacenter hardware, and our initiatives in the cloud. This includes responsibility for defining the organization of the NOC group, individual responsibilities, and performance management.</div>
<br>
<div>
Leading a small NOC group means there’s plenty of opportunity to get your hands dirty and we’ll expect the ideal candidate to be a seasoned network engineer who’s comfortable troubleshooting in complex network environments.</div>
