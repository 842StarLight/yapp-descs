<p>P.E. P.E. Labellers provides solutions across numerous markets, including beverage, food, wine, cosmetics, and consumer goods. P.E. <p><b>
JOB DESCRIPTION:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
P.E, Labellers is currently seeking an Accounts Receivable Specialist at our Sharonville, Ohio facility. The Accounts Receivable Specialist will be responsible for invoicing customers, and follow-up with customers and other accounting and general office duties. The position reports to the Accounting Manager. Responsibilities include the following:</p>
<ul>
<li>Prepare customer invoices for previous day shipments after reviewing customer order and shipping data for completeness and accuracy.</li>
<li>Prepare customer invoices for service technician visits, includes entering expenses and hours in SAP.</li>
<li>Review sales tax calculations on invoices.</li>
<li>Send invoices to customers, via email, mail or customer portal.</li>
<li>Download daily deposit information via online banking and apply to customer accounts.</li>
<li>Responsible for collection of past due accounts. Requires maintenance of customer communication records and follow-up to ensure payment is received.</li>
<li>Responsible for effectively researching, tracking and resolving accounts receivable problems and discrepancies.</li>
<li>Assist with month end closing procedures and periodic audits as they relate to AR.</li>
<li>Cross train as back up to Accounts Payable Specialist.</li>
<li>Miscellaneous office duties, such as answering phone, order supplies, etc.</li>
<li>Perform additional duties and assignments as directed by management.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p><b>
</b></p>
<p><b>JOB REQUIREMENTS:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>2-year accounting degree preferred</li>
<li>2+ years AR or General Accounting experience, preferably in a manufacturing environment</li>
<li>Experience working with an ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) system, SAP preferred but not required</li>
<li>Intermediate or better use of Microsoft Office, specifically Excel, Outlook and Word</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communications skills</li>
<li>Professional, pleasant and calm demeanor required for dealing with customers</li>
<li>Good interpersonal skills and ability to build partnerships and work with all levels of the P.E. team in a positive manner</li>
<li>Ability to prioritize and meet deadlines</li>
<li>Self-motivated, task-oriented and able to self-direct daily activity</li>
<br>
</ul>
