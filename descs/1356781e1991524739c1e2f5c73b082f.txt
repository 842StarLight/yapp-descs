<p><b>Position Description:</b></p>
<p>Role Summary:</p>
<p>Flowserve Pump Division’s Commercial Operations Parts group is seeking an Interpretation of Parts &amp; Pricing Analyst for a role in our NA Organization located in Vernon, CA with a primary focus within the Nuclear, Decoking &amp; Ebullator markets.</p>
<p>Essential Responsibilities:</p>
<p>Responsibilities of this role will include, but not limited to:</p>
<ul>
<li><p>Reading &amp; understand technical documents, drawings, and metallurgy’s</p></li>
<li><p>
Preparation of material &amp; labor estimates to support Aftermarket quotation proposals</p></li>
<li><p>
Clear documentation of all proposals to allow for clean execution at order fulfillment stage</p></li>
<li><p>
Interfacing with internal/external sales force, distributors, engineering and localized support teams</p></li>
<li><p>
Daily management of high workload with varying complexities to support a fast-paced environment</p></li>
</ul>
<p><b>Position Requirements:</b></p>
<p><b>Qualifications/Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>College 4-Year Degree or equivalent work experience within a manufacturing environment (3-5 years)</p></li>
<li><p>
Mechanical aptitude, Engineering skillset and/or comparable work experience</p></li>
<li><p>
Ability to read drawings, technical documents and skillset to develop</p></li>
<li><p>
Detailed proposals</p></li>
<li><p>
Understanding of cost &amp; margin analysis</p></li>
<li><p>
Excellent communication skills, both written and verbal</p></li>
<li><p>
Computer proficiency including MS Office Software (Outlook, Excel, Word), Oracle, SAP</p></li>
</ul>
<p><b>Preferences:</b></p>
<p><b>Preferred Experience/Characteristics:</b></p>
<ul>
