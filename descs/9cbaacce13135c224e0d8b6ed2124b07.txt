Job Description &amp; Qualifications</div>
<div><div>
<div>
<p>At Miles Partnership, we hire smart people who are hardworking, resourceful, fun, friendly and driven to do more. We are passionate about travel and use the most effective, results-driven solutions to tell the stories of our destination clients and share experiences that inspire visitors.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>It’s an exciting time for us, as we continue to grow and add to our team. As such, we are currently seeking an <b>Account</b> <b>Director</b> who demonstrates the following skills and brings the following experience:</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>5+ years of directly related marketing/advertising work experience</li>
<li>2+ years of experience functioning at the Account Director level, managing large accounts</li>
<li>Experience managing a wide array of project challenges simultaneously; collaboration with other marketing disciplines in budgeting, planning, and execution</li>
<li>Understanding of financial and accounting practices</li>
<li>Proficient with marketing best practices and online advertising techniques, including social media, SEO/SEM, email marketing, Mobile, etc.</li>
<li>Thorough understanding of tracking, measurement and analytics platforms and metrics used to measure and track campaign performance</li>
<li>Solid understanding of B2C and B2B marketing principles</li>
<li>Proficient knowledge of UX/UI best practices, web application architecture, design and implementation. Strong sense of design fundamentals</li>
<li>Strong written and oral communications skills, including the ability to successfully communicate complicated information to both internal and external partners and stakeholders</li>
<li>Possess exceptional leadership skills and strong team management experience</li>
<li>Able to think strategically and work collaboratively with a team</li>
<li>Perform efficiently and well under tight deadlines</li>
<li>Able to respond positively to client and peer criticism and feedback</li>
<li>Able to effectively manage relationships, both internally and externally</li>
<li>Display a dependable, strong work ethic</li>
<li>Able to work in a fast-paced, professional office environment</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Snapshot</b>: Responsible for managing contracts and day-to-day relationships with Miles Partnership’s clients and leading Miles Partnership’s internal account team. Success is measured by evaluating client satisfaction, the delivery of innovative marketing programs, personnel development, and strong financial management.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Duties:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Leads the strategic planning with Miles Partnership’s clients across multiple marketing channels, (web, email, print and digital media, and social)</li>
<li>Represents Miles Partnership and client accounts at all client meetings</li>
<li>Oversees day to day deliverables in collaboration with the client</li>
<li>Ensures all contractual requirements are consistently being met with the client</li>
<li>Oversee all aspects of billing for the client contracts</li>
<li>Manages all aspects of Miles Partnership’s internal P &amp; L statements for the client accounts</li>
<li>Travels and builds relationships with stakeholders in the client’s tourism industry, as well as explores new business opportunities</li>
<li>Conducts project discoveries and gathers technical requirements</li>
<li>Implements plans for new campaigns</li>
<li>Identifies and provides ongoing key measurements. Analyzes campaign reports with corresponding adjustment recommendations to improve program performance</li>
<li>Communicates daily with Miles Partnership’s dedicated internal team regarding project(s) deliverables and priorities</li>
<li>Collaborates with the project manager to ensure internal processes are being followed by all team members and quality products are being delivered</li>
<li>Collaborates with the Vice President of Sales &amp; Vice President of Product Development to ensure programs are appealing to stakeholders and sales goals are met</li>
<li>Responsible all aspects of employee life cycle including management and development</li>
<li>Consistent communication with Senior Vice President</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Travel Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Frequent intra- and/or inter-state travel required, sometimes requiring an overnight stay</li>
<br>
</ul>
