<h2>USC Enterprise Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Overall management for a sales target within the US and Canada region. Based out of New Jersey. You will be responsible for driving the performance of assigned reseller partners in country while maintaining a high level of direct engagement with prospects to achieve personal sales goals.</p>
<p><b>
Purpose</b></p>
<p>
To drive consistent sales revenue targets</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Outcomes</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Achieve annual recurring revenue targets</li>
<li>Ensure our partners love working with us and are successful</li>
<li>Work as part of a team to create shared learnings and momentum</li>
<li>Drive First Customer Meetings to ensure that the top of the funnel is healthy</li>
<li>Maintain a healthy pipeline with consistent coverage for future quota achievement</li>
<li>The Purple brand will consistently become brand of choice in the region</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Key KPI’s</b></p>
<p>
Avg of 15 FCM’s attended per month</p>
<p>
4X Pipeline cover forward for the next 90 days</p>
<p>
Pipeline days in stage less than 60</p>
<p>
New ARR and TCV generation in line with targets</p>
<p><b>
Role Competencies</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Strong sales experience and capability (SaaS a benefit)</li>
<li>Personal ownership of goals</li>
<li>Love the detail on deals</li>
<li>Drive consistency in systems and processes</li>
<li>Understanding the channel landscape</li>
<li>Strong negotiator</li>
<li>Ability to work strongly with a local and a global team</li>
<li>3-5 years sales experience</li>
<li>IT solutions and Software sales (preferred)</li>
<li>Previous experience working for or with Verizon (preferred)</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Cultural Fit</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Curiosity and an enthusiastic learner</li>
<li>Have other people’s interests at heart</li>
<li>Contagious positive energy</li>
<li>No BS, no politics</li>
<li>Resilience to take the knocks and grow from the learns</li>
</ul>
