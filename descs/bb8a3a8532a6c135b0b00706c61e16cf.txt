<p><b>About Slalom Build</b></p>
<p>
Slalom Build is a highly-scalable, high-velocity Build as a Service firm. We work with clients to close the distance between dream and reality, imagined possibility and technical realization.</p>
<p>
We do this by blending design, product engineering, analytics, and automation to build the modern technology products of tomorrow.</p>
<p>
Nearly 1000 builders strong in seven Build Centers across North America, Slalom Build leverages a foundation of innovation inherited from Slalom Consulting. We’re intensely proud to partner with future-focused clients committed to disrupting their industries.</p>

<p><b>
About Slalom</b></p>
<p>
Founded in 2001 and headquartered in Seattle, WA, Slalom has organically grown to over 6,000 employees. We were named one of Fortune’s 100 Best Companies to Work For in 2017 and are regularly recognized by our employees as a best place to work. You can find us in 27 cities across the U.S., U.K., and Canada.</p>

<p><b>
Who You Are</b></p>
<p>
Slalom is looking for a talented and progressive leader to fuel the growth and success of our Slalom Build organization. As a Practice Area Leader, you will provide thought leadership to our clients, set strategic direction for our software engineering organization and work with the Build leadership team to assist in driving sales, recruiting, engagement, and operational efficiencies. You will also be accountable for ensuring the overall quality execution of technology services across a breadth of expertise areas that include Technology Strategy, Digital Innovation and Product Engineering (Modern Web, Mobile, Custom, Cloud, DevSecOps).</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Practice accountability support across sales, recruiting, account management, product engineering, and operational aspects, including:<ul>
<li>
Capability Growth – Help to drive overall growth of the Build Center engineering organization through a combination of business development, talent management, oversight of delivery work and thought leadership.</li>
<li>
Business Development – Support business development activities for prospective and existing customer sales pursuits. Understand client needs, share our value proposition and shape, estimate and propose solutions.</li>
<li>
Engagement Management – Manage engagement risk, project economics including planning and budgeting, running accounts receivable, defining deliverable content, and ensuring consensus of proposed solutions with our clients and their leadership teams.</li>
<li>
Team &amp; Individual Development – Improve team performance through an effective team approach that increases productivity and job happiness.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Find opportunities with both new and existing clients to generate sales pursuits within the Slalom Build Network and our Markets.</li>
<li>
Build and maintain executive and senior executive-level client partner relationships and expand Slalom's client portfolio within the region.</li>
<li>
Work with teams to bring together best practices and develop case studies and other core deliverables to build solution offerings.</li>
<li>
Participate in industry forums, events, and speaking engagements; handle production of engagement deliverables, budget, and presentations to client personnel and leadership.</li>
<li>
Mentor, develop, and actively support Build Center capabilities per market demand.</li>
<li>
Support recruiting and onboarding of new employees.</li>
<li>
Mentor and develop new employees within the organization.</li>
<li>
Display leadership presence internally and with our clients and partners.</li>
<li>
Travel Requirements: Willingness to travel up to 50% for client engagements and internal meetings and events.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
10+ years of experience focused in custom software engineering and/or product engineering.</li>
<li>
Experience in full stack software development and architecture and agile delivery.</li>
<li>
Ability to manage complex team delivery and navigate complex stakeholder environments.</li>
<li>
Proven ability to successfully lead cross-functional teams.</li>
<li>
Business operations experience (e.g., invoicing, SOW, margins, utilization, reporting and metrics).</li>
<li>
Strong client-facing skills including; networking and relationship building, delivering client presentations, leading meetings and negotiating delivery engagements.</li>
<li>
Validated history of using and growing an existing client base in addition to experience in growing new accounts/clients from the ground up.</li>
<li>
Deep experience with leading people, including mentoring and developing leadership skills.</li>
<li>
Excellent verbal and written communication skills to audiences of all levels.</li>
</ul>
