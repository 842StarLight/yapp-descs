<h2>Assistant Director - Client Solutions</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>We’re a global company of thinkers, designers, and doers. Freeman partners with our clients to build a connection with their customers by creating brand experiences that are seamless, innovative, and immersive. From strategy, creative, and digital to logistics and event technology, our teams place the client at the center, helping them connect to people in meaningful and personal ways. What makes us unique is our collaborative culture, intuitive knowledge, global perspective, and personalized approach developed from our 90-year legacy as an industry leader. Freeman connects people to a universe of opportunities that create a spark, build understanding, and inspire action.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>SUMMARY:
</b></p>
<p>The Assistant Director – Client Solutions is responsible for management of Client Solutions and support teams. Directs team to ensure goals on revenue and profit are achieved. Directs team to grow opportunities with existing accounts by selling additional products and services. Plays a key role on major accounts. Intermediate role that continues to develop a deeper understanding of the growth organization and the principles of genuine and authentic leadership.
</p>
<ul>
<li>Differentiators
<ul>
<li>Individual Volume - $2 million or more
</li>
<li>Team Volume - $4 million or more
</li>
<li>Strategic customers and complex accounts
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Leadership
<ul>
<li>Direct team in the successful and profitable production of revenue opportunities.
</li>
<li>Comply with strategies set by VP or SVP with the assistance of Director Client Solutions.
</li>
<li>Play key role on major accounts.
</li>
<li>Collaborate with Delivery leadership.
</li>
<li>Participate in meetings for team that allow for collaboration on goals to include, but not limited to E3 calls, production meetings, team meetings and delivery meetings as necessary.
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Client Solutions
<ul>
<li>Develop E3, order lead time, event profitability and budget goals with assistance of Director Client Solutions.
</li>
<li>Identify execution and profit challenges on accounts and develop actionable plans for improvement.
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Business Development
<ul>
<li>Direct team to improve organic growth on all accounts.
</li>
<li>Assist team members with understanding of business development strategies and pricing structure in local market.
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Budget
<ul>
<li>Ensure accurate budgets on all accounts in group.
</li>
<li>Manage within team operational budget.
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Talent Management
<ul>
<li>Recruit, develop and retain team members.
</li>
<li>Execute on training programs as determined by team leader to include mentoring new employees through Foundations.
</li>
<li>Assist with account and personnel succession planning.
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Analytics
<ul>
<li>Ensure Salesforce.com data integrity.
</li>
<li>Report areas of concern on show execution areas (order lead, E3, event profitability &amp; budgets) to Director Client Solutions.
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Miscellaneous
<ul>
<li>Support team on major proposals, pricing and presentations in tandem with Business Development.
</li>
<li>Participate in industry organizations on committees (national and local basis) with assigned direct reports.
</li>
<li>Conduct random invoice audits to verify accurate account billing.
</li>
<li>Perform other duties as assigned.
</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree (B.A.) from a four-year college or university.
</li>
<li>One (1) or more years of leadership experience in a sales related role is preferred.
</li>
<li>Four (4) or more years of related account management experience.
</li>
<li>Has proven the ability to manage their own book of accounts or the ability to lead teams in the successful and profitable production of revenue opportunities totaling approximately $2 million or more.
</li>
<li>Key role on major accounts.
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Knowledge of sales systems such as TSS, Passport, ECS, EMS and Salesforce.com is required.
</li>
<li>Working knowledge of all Microsoft Office Software applications.
</li>
<li>Excellent customer service and organizational skills are required.<br>
</li>
</ul>
