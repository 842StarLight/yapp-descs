<h2>Gainshare Technical Delivery Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Liveperson is an expert based culture, built on the drive and passion of smart people! Our business is growing and we are looking for people like you!
<br><br>
LivePerson is the global leader in conversational commerce solutions and we aim to make life easier by transforming how people communicate with brands. Our 18,000 customers, including leading brands like Citibank, HSBC, Orange, Virgin Atlantic, RBS and Liberty Global, use our conversational commerce solutions to orchestrate humans and AI, at scale, and create a convenient, deeply personal relationship — a conversational relationship — with their millions of consumers.
<br><br>
Over the next three years, our goal is to transform the 268 billion analogue phone calls between a brand and its consumers to digital on the LiveEngage platform. By doing this, we enable consumers to get back time and experience a more connected relationship with the brand in which sales, service, marketing, branches, stores, and contact centre's become a unified experience.
<br><br>
The successful candidate has an opportunity to join a highly outstanding team within a fast-paced and successful organisation.
<br><br>
<b>In this role you will:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Assist in shaping key metrics to guide performance of bots with business sponsors by understanding end-user requirements</li>
<li>Design and document systems and processes, including bot performance and tuning approaches</li>
<li>Collaborate with project, technology &amp; customer teams to provide business analysis from project conception to implementation</li>
<li>Manage detailed, customer level data and provide data-driven recommendations to on how best to deploy resources.</li>
<li>Shape operational improvement and metrics to drive results of automations in support of the Gainshare (outcome-based delivery) programs</li>
<li>Extract data from systems (SalesForce, BI reports, Spreadsheets, LivePerson Systems, etc) to run scenarios for delivery leadership to enable operational improvements and efficiencies</li>
<li>Work with business and technology leaders to identify opportunities to improve the operations of bots and coordinate improvement tasks</li>
<li>Be able to draw accurate conclusions with partial data-sets and manage process improvement through automation initiatives and execution.</li>
<li>Work with stakeholders to support go-live deployment as well as transition of the solution to BAU</li>
<li>Assess the risk of the proposed solutions and prioritise competing business demands</li>
<li>Deliver collaboratively with the Transformation Organization to ensure products are used effectively to drive improved operational effectiveness for customers in the Gainshare (Pay for Performance) model</li>
<li>Continuously improve methodologies and processes, contribute from your experience and insight and build best practice serving customers and working with teams across a global footprint</li>
<li>Work well in ambiguous settings to build technical delivery teams that create concrete, incremental value for customers</li>
<li>Develop and manage client delivery plans based on automation (bot development) roadmap to on-time / on budget delivery</li>
<li>Demonstrate the interpersonal skills to interface well with all other disciplines of the business at an executive, managerial, as well as a front-line team level</li>
<li>Effectively support critical business field sales efforts with customers and partners</li>
<li>Establish and maintain core metrics and reports to lead, manage, and reflect the value of the delivery of teams</li>
<li>Manage resource allocation and identify all delivery needs and assign resources in line with customer requirements and business priority</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related technical field (MBA Preferred)</li>
</ul>
<b>You should be an expert in:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Analytical thought process</li>
<li>Shaping, defining, and executing reporting functions for recurring consumption by management and decision makers.</li>
<li>Ability to communicate effectively with both technical and non-technical individuals</li>
<li>Operational and technical understanding of Contact Center environments and software</li>
<li>Bot design and delivery experience, including familiarity of Natural Language Processing delivery options to meet customer needs at a global scale</li>
<li>Knowledge of SaaS at scale, and have technical acumen that allows for a complete understanding of the fundamentals underlying messaging and automation technologies</li>
<li>Product and Software development expertise</li>
<li>People management, developing and attracting talent, coaching, and performance management</li>
<li>Collaboration with Client Service Managers, Client Partners and regional leadership to define priority and resource alignment</li>
<li>Effective communication and be comfortable communicating complex business and technical issues with executive presence</li>
<li>Excellent oral and written communication skills</li>
<li>Track record for being detail-oriented with a demonstrated ability to self-motivate and follow-through on projects</li>
</ul>
