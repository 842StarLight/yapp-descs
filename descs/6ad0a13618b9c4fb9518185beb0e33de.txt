<h2>Regional Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div><div><div><div>
<p>At Stratasys Direct, we’re all about finding the best for our customers. Join a growing team of boundary pushers who care just as much about innovative manufacturing as the people who will benefit from it.</p>
<p>A Stratasys Direct <b>Regional Account Manager </b>is the first-line champion of innovative manufacturing solutions for our key accounts and new prospects. By achieving a sales quota, promoting the value proposition of the products and services of Stratasys Direct, this position prospects with outbound sales and account development in the Western Region of the USA.</p>
<p>In this role, you’ll proactively manage a geographic territory for maximum sales by responding to opportunities, face to face client visits, prospecting, regular contact with the accounts in the territory and obtaining referrals from satisfied customers. Other responsibilities will include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Development of long-term, sustainable relationships with key territory accounts.</li>
<li>Creation and presentation of annual strategic territory plans, including key accounts, activity plans and growth targets.</li>
<li>Timely follow-up with inbound leads and opportunities, proactive work with customer base and prospecting via outbound phone calls and face-to-face client visits.</li>
<li>Successful completion of quotes, fulfillment of orders, and other customer requests.</li>
<li>Ability to work from a home-based office. Specific work schedule is coordinated with direct supervisor.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><i>As a part of Stratasys Direct Manufacturing, this position requires access to information and/or technology controlled under the International Traffic in Arms Regulations (ITAR) or the Export Administration Regulations (EAR). The successful candidate must be a U.S. Citizen, lawful permanent resident of the U.S. (or, green card holder), protected individual as defined by 8 U.S.C. 1324b(a)(3), or eligible to obtain the required authorizations from the U.S. Department of State.
</i></p>
<p><b>MINIMUM QUALIFICATIONS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in sales, engineering or related discipline.</li>
<li>5+ years’ experience in B2B sales/account management of technology products/services.</li>
<li>Work experience in an engineering/manufacturing environment.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Knowledge of 3D CAD software.</li>
<li>Prior formal sales training such as that from Miller Heiman, Wilson Learning or Achieve Global.</li>
<li>Knowledge of additive or subtractive fabrication technologies, particularly as used in rapid prototyping and manufacturing.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>

<p><b>Stratasys</b> (Nasdaq: SSYS), headquartered in Minneapolis, Minnesota and Rehovot, Israel, is a leading global provider of 3D printing and additive manufacturing solutions. The company's patented FDM® and PolyJet™ 3D Printing technologies produce prototypes and manufactured goods directly from CAD data for the aerospace, automotive, healthcare, consumer goods and education industries. Stratasys subsidiaries include MakerBot and GrabCAD, and the company operates the digital parts manufacturing service, Stratasys Direct. Stratasys has nearly 3,000 employees, holds over 1,200 granted or pending additive manufacturing patents globally, and has received more than 30 awards for its technology and leadership.</p>
