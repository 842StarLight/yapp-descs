<p>Anderson Auto Group located Arizona is seeking an experienced General Sales Manager to join our team. The ideal candidate will have experience coaching, training and leading a team. We are looking for 2+ years of GSM experience and 5+ years’ automotive sales management experience with a proven success record to increase unit volume, CSI and gross profit.</p>
<p>Our Auto Group currently has 5 separate rooftops with an annual retail volume of 7,000+ units. This opportunity is for a forward thinking, professional, career minded Leader. Previous sales, Digital understanding, F&amp;I, used/new car sales manager experience is a must. Leadership skills must be top tier in order to be considered.</p>
<p><b>Essential Functions</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Understand vehicle inventories and how to properly manage Turn, ROI and Capital.</li>
<li>Monitor &amp; manage a CRM system effectively</li>
<li>Using appropriate procedures for management, facilitate the Sales Department to generate expected levels of market share and sales efficiency.</li>
<li>Generate forecasted levels of gross and net profit.</li>
<li>Establish sales quotas for all Product Specialists, which meet their individual skill levels.</li>
<li>Supervise Sales Management and F&amp;I staff to ensure performance standards are met.</li>
<li>Help advance the Digital Sales team &amp; hold accountable for their performance.</li>
<li>Devise appropriate forecasts for variable operations with General Manager assistance.</li>
<li>Stay current with sales management through skilled use of appropriate computer systems.</li>
<li>Maintain process to complete all paperwork correctly and on time.</li>
<li>Keep a balanced new inventory in relation to sales.</li>
<li>Provide for an appealing merchandised showroom, set up with vehicles and accessories to draw the interest of customers.</li>
<li>Supervise employees to ensure that facilities are clean and efficient.</li>
<li>Maintain active programs to follow up sales.</li>
<li>Meet budgeted revenue and expense goals.</li>
<li>Attend pertinent training as available to keep current with the ever-changing industry.</li>
<li>Facilitate Advertising and Marketing along with keeping to a budget.</li>
</ul>
