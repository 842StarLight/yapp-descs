<div><b>
Job Summary:</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Leveraging licensing, and marketing/promotions experience, the Marketing Coordinator, Brand Partnerships helps the Brand Partnership team and assist where necessary, in developing and nurturing licensor and other external relationships on an ongoing basis.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>Areas of responsibility include assisting the Brand Partnerships team with:
</div>
<ul>
<li>Analytic thinking applied to industry trends (CES, Toy Fair, Licensing Show) and business sales (data base and PES Score analysis)</li>
<li>
Assist the Associate, Brand Partnership in the development of reports and presentations, helping to organize and prepare for meetings.
</li>
<li>Assisting the Associate in global communications related to Happy Meal Calendar Development.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Job Relationships:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
<b>
Reports to: </b>Associate, Brand Partnerships</li>
<li>
<b>
Relationships and Communications: </b>Regular and ongoing interactions with TMS colleague in various agency departments such as Planning/Strategy, Youth &amp; Family Account team, US Promotions Account team, Digital, Creative, Studio and TMS counterparts in Asia/Latin/Europe, Globa Havi Analytics Team.</li>
</ul>
