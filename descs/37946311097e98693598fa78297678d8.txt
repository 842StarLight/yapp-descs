<div>
The Sr. Database Administrator role will assist in the planning, coordination, and other related activities associated with the design, development, and implementation of database management systems and software applications in support of all E-commerce applications. The Sr. Database Administrator is responsible for maintaining, supporting, and upgrading existing database management systems and related applications. This individual will apply proven communication skills, problem-solving skills, and knowledge of best practices to help IT teams on issues related to the design, development, and deployment of mission-critical information and database systems. All project and support tasks must be completed consistent with Company goals, industry best practices and regulatory requirements while ensuring data protection and security in highly visible, mission critical environments.<br>
<br>
<b>Essential Functions</b>
</div>
<div>
<br>
<b>
Strategy &amp; Planning</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Participate and support management in the assessment and development of long-term strategic goals for databases technologies and related products in conjunction with application business requirements.</li>
<li>Support management in the assessment and development of long-term strategic goals for production databases in conjunction with application data owners and department managers.</li>
<li>Work with application development staff to develop database architectures, tuning/coding standards, and quality assurance policies and procedures.</li>
<li>Design and implementation of redundant systems, policies, and procedures for backup/recovery, disaster recovery, monitoring, alerting and data archiving to ensure effective protection and integrity of data assets.</li>
<li>Contribute to the design of data flow across different environments and down to developer local instances.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
Acquisition &amp; Deployment</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Support Management in the development and deployment of new database applications, systems software, and/or enhancements to existing applications throughout the enterprise.</li>
<li>Support Management in research and making recommendations on database and related software products and services in support of procurement and development efforts.</li>
<li>Coordinate feasibility studies for database software and related system products under consideration for purchase, and give advice based on findings, where required.</li>
<li>Ensure that any new database software integration into company systems meets functional requirements, system compliance, and interface specifications.</li>
<li>Collaborate with analysts, designers, and system owners in the testing of new database software programs and applications.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
Operational Management</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Provide direction for the development team in support of business operations.</li>
<li>Identify and resolve program and data errors where applicable.</li>
<li>Design, develop, and install enhancements and upgrades to database systems and application software.</li>
<li>Design, develop, and implement processes to ensure data is in sync across environments.</li>
<li>Liaise with network administrators, security engineers, architects and software engineers to assist with quality assurance, program logic, and data processing.</li>
<li>Provide guidance to junior members of the team.</li>
<li>Responsible for documentation requirements in the team’s knowledge base.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
Incidental Functions</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Assist with projects as may be required to contribute to efficiency and effectiveness of the work.</li>
<li>Attend management meetings on behalf of team.</li>
<li>Participate in hiring activities and fulfilling affirmative action obligations and ensuring compliance with the equal employment opportunity policy.
</li>
<li>Provide rotational 24/7 on-call support of database platforms.</li>
</ul>

Knowledge &amp; Experience</b><br>
