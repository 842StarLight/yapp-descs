<h2>Field Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>Job Title: Field Manager<br>
<br>
Reports to: Office Manager<br>
<br>
Location: Office<br>
<br>
FLSA Status: Seasonal, Non-Exempt<br>
<br>
Summary/Objective:<br>
<br>
The Field Manager oversees and evaluates employees who travel to customer sites and to ensure they are accomplishing their daily responsibilities.<br>
<br>
Responsibilities:
<ul>
<li>Generate leads/sales opportunities for self and team</li>
<li>Train new agents on policies, procedures, software, marketing and all relevant job responsibilities in order to meet the Company’s objectives</li>
<li>Provide customer service to agents by answering questions</li>
<li>Take team on marketing tours to acquire new clientele</li>
<li>Manage and oversee employees to ensure they are accomplishing the Company’s goals</li>
<li>Contributes to team efforts as needed in order to meet deadlines</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned</li>
<li>Complete all associated paperwork in neat and timely manner</li>
</ul>Qualifications:
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or GED; Associates degree in business or related field preferred</li>
<li>3-5 years customer service or sales experience</li>
<li>Exceptional customer service skills</li>
<li>Strong organization skills</li>
<li>Extreme professionalism at all times</li>
<li>Ability to communicate consistently and clearly</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
