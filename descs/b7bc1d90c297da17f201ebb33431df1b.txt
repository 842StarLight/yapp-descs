<h2>Solutions Specialist – Commercial Market Development</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Want to be part of a dynamic team providing state-of-the-art technology solutions to businesses, schools, and governments? Come join our collaborative team of professionals focused on delivering quality in everything we do. Unified Technology Systems is 100% employee-owned and proud to provide exceptional customer experiences. We make technology innovative and simple to use!</p>
<p><b>Job Title: </b> Solutions Specialist – Commercial Market Development</p>
<p><b>Reports to</b>: Regional Manager</p>
<p><b>Goals and Outcomes: </b> Increase Unified Technology System’s business in assigned market and/or clients by effectively establishing relationships with Prime Contractors, Influencers, End Users, Consultants, Engineers, and Specifiers, (PIECES) to promote the Unified Technology Systems brand and capabilities, identify opportunities where Unified can add value, generate new leads for projects, lead proposal development activities, and close business. Manage key accounts and prospects while offering clients a value-added resource for their organizations.</p>
<p><b>Essential Functions: </b></p>
<p>· Build relationships in the commercial real estate market and with developers to obtain early involvement in new projects.</p>
<p>· Active participation in key networking groups (IFMA, CREW, CoreNet, etc.) with the goal of promoting Unified Technology Systems brand and value.</p>
<p>· Establish credibility and long-term relationships that will support the growth of new business opportunities.</p>
<p>· Engagement with the community of PIECES in the assigned geographic area. The focus will be to promote the company brand and value while identifying projects.</p>
<p>· Schedule and lead Corporate Capabilities Presentations with appropriate PIECES personnel at Unified’s or PIECES member’s office.</p>
<p>· Ensure that all leads, relationships, activities, opportunities, and other associated information is maintained in the NetSuite CRM.</p>
<p>· Qualify opportunities to ensure that Unified applies it resources to high probability opportunities.</p>
<p>· Collaborate with Sales Assistant, Sales Engineer, and other team members to ensure development of impactful and winning proposals.</p>
<p>· Present the solution(s) proposed and the value that our solutions offer to the client.</p>
<p>· Participate in turnover meetings &amp; introduction of Project Manager to clients to ensure smooth transitions.</p>
<p><b>Qualifications/Requirements: </b></p>
<p>· Excellent verbal and written communication skills.</p>
<p>· Excellent presentation skills to support delivery of engaging Corporate Capabilities presentations.</p>
<p>· Excellent networking and interpersonal skills</p>
<p>· The ability to work within a team environment is essential.</p>
<p>· Strong computer skills with proficiency in MS Office.</p>
<p>· Experience with NetSuite (or other CRM/ERP) software.</p>
<p>· Knowledge of Unified’s AV/NSI products and services.</p>
<p>· Bachelor’s degree in Marketing, Business, or related field.</p>
