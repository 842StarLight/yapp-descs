<h2>Applications Specialist - Pressure Protection</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Job Summary:</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>The Applications Specialist role is the conduit through which our customers communicate their product needs to us and we translate these needs to winning product and/or service proposals for complex systems, applications and WIP jobs. Creating a winning proposal requires an ability to accurately ascertain the customer’s need, correctly size and select a solution, negotiate as appropriate a competitive price from our suppliers, and translate this in to a compelling offer to the customer. The Applications Specialist serves as an internal subject matter expert (SME) on specific product lines and/or applications, and is a partner in the sales strategy and execution with the Account Manager.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Primary Duties &amp; Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Build, maintain, and retain relationships with key buying influences and key management contacts by building effective long-term relationships and customer loyalty often in collaboration with the Account Manager</li>
<li>Develop and maintain a thorough knowledge of the Company’s available services, product lines, sales territories, product applications, and applicable pricing structures.</li>
<li>Develop and maintain a thorough knowledge of various fluid and chemical types, pressure, and the mechanical functions of products in order to size/select the appropriate Customer Solution</li>
<li>Develop and maintain a thorough knowledge of OEM and Global Alliance Agreements and appropriately apply to the Customer Solutions Process</li>
<li>On occasion, when needed and requested by the Account Manager, make Customer site-visits to serve as a product/application SME in the Sales process.</li>
<li>Serve as the technical product/application SME on Customer calls both directly and in collaboration with the Account Manager</li>
<li>Facilitate intake of Customer Solution requests and required specifications to then initiate the engineering of a Customer Solution quotation including:</li>
<ul>
<li>Sizing and selecting of valves, parts and applications and typically with oversight/input from the Sr. Applications Specialist</li>
<li>Communication with Jr. level Engineering and/or Buyer representatives at the Customer level to determine the appropriate solution</li>
<li>Set the appropriate quotation pricing and margin per the Setpoint BOA and typically with oversight/input from the Sr. Applications Specialist in order to provide a competitive Customer Solution</li>
<li>Sourcing of materials as needed</li>
</ul>
<li>Partner with the Operations team and Services Coordinators, when needed, to address Customer Service issues</li>
<li>Create and maintain Customer trust in Setpoint as a quality, value-added, solutions provider</li>
<li>Accurately capture quote data in quote log and order information into Oracle or effectively pass on to administration for entry according to branch standard procedures</li>
<li>Accurately place purchase orders on the suppliers and then follow up as appropriate to ensure on time delivery to meet commitments to customer</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Positional Requirements &amp; Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Education &amp; Experience</li>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in business or engineering preferred or equivalent technical school certification</li>
<li>Alternatively 5 or more years of experience in a customer-facing role identifying and addressing customer needs within relevant industry</li>
<li>Typically has 5 or more years of experience in a customer-facing role identifying and addressing customer needs within relevant industry</li>
<li>Strong market reputation with the ability to attract business to Setpoint through customer relationships</li>
</ul>
<li>Skills &amp; Abilities</li>
<ul>
<li>Advanced knowledge of valves and related products</li>
<li>Intermediate understanding of the chemical, refining and power markets</li>
<li>Intermediate knowledge of Oracle</li>
<li>Advanced relationship with relevant suppliers</li>
<li>Intermediate knowledge of Microsoft Office software</li>
<li>Mechanical aptitude</li>
<li>Ability to coach, mentor, develop other staff</li>
<li>Ability to perform job function without supervision</li>
</ul>
<li>Must be able to travel and have a clear driving record in accordance to Setpoint IS driving guidelines</li>
<li>Clear background check and drug/alcohol screening</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Essential Physical Functions:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Travel: less than 5%</li>
<li>Lifting up to 20 pounds</li>
<li>Bending, stooping, walking</li>
</ul>
