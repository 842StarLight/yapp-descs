<h2>Managing Applications Consultant - Pega Robotics Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>
<div>Responsibilities:<br>
<ul>
<li>Client-facing role responsible for implementing full life cycle Pega Robotics solutions</li>
<li>Serve as Pega Robotics SME for client deliverables, tool selection presentations, training of resources</li>
<li>Lead Architect for Pega Robotics (legacy OpenSpan) Automation Project with the goal of preparing consultants to guide clients through the design and implementation of automation solutions using Pega Robotics software</li>
<li>Leveraging industry best practices and experience, identify automation opportunities and recommend automation solutions including architectural and technical designs</li>
<li>Take business requirements, current and future state process designs and translate them into process flows within Pega Robotics Automation Studio</li>
<li>Coordinate activities within clients IT / Automation COE / business environments to develop, test and deploy RPA solutions</li>
</ul>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Qualifications:</li>
<li>Minimum of 3 years of experience with a Robotics Process Automation technology such as OpenSpan, Blue Prism, Automation Anywhere.</li>
<li>Minimum 3 years of experience in all phases of Software Development Life Cycle.</li>
<li>Experience with any robotic software, frameworks, and functional design.</li>
<li>Ability to convert business logic/req experience with Pega robotic software, frameworks, and functional design.</li>
<li>Possess one of the following certifications: Pega robotic automation (open span) certification.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>Candidates should be flexible / willing to work across this delivery landscape which includes and not limited to Agile Applications Development, Support and Deployment.</div>
