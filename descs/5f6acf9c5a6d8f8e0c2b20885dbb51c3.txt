<h2>Digital Sales Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Digital Sales Representative</b></p>
<p>Orange142’s Digital Sales Rep will develop relationships with new agency partners and direct advertisers to fulfill their digital media needs and maximize their potential on our platforms. The ideal candidate will have strong sales skills with a proven track record of exceeding revenue targets through active selling, account management, product implementation, and managing client terms and expectations. Additionally, successful candidates will showcase thought-leadership and intellectual curiosity to seek out innovative tactics to solve business challenges. Knowledge of digital media solutions and the ability to communicate from junior to top level executives is a must.</p>
<p>CORE RESPONSIBILITIES</p>
<ul>
<li>Develop a strong understanding of Orange142’s technology and media solutions, and take a solution first approach in helping clients and agency partners grow their business</li>
<li>Cultivate and develop high-level agency partner relationships and leverage a strong consultative approach within aligned region</li>
<li>Manage and exceed individual sales and revenue goals</li>
<li>Identify additional revenue opportunities with existing clients while continuously prospecting new business</li>
<li>Serve as an industry expert in digital advertising; specifically in digital advertising, search, eCRM, marketing strategy and social media</li>
<li>Work collaboratively with cross functional teams, including Orange142’s Media Services, Marketing, and Product teams, to drive revenue growth with new and existing clients</li>
<li>Drive industry knowledge by delivering high quality thought leadership</li>
<li>Provide ongoing client management to clients and agency partners including quarterly business reviews, proactive media packaging and marketing services, and client entertainment</li>
<li>Provide regular updates to management on pipeline and partnerships through company CRM</li>
</ul>
<p>QUALIFICATIONS</p>
<ul>
<li>The successful candidate must possess a minimum of 7 years of sales experience in digital marketing and advertising</li>
<li>Established relationships with agencies and clients that plan and purchase digital media programs and/or marketing services</li>
<li>Proven success in structuring and closing complex media and marketing deals for longer sales cycles (6 months+)</li>
<li>Established C-Level relationships within the digital industry and assigned territory</li>
<li>Strong ability to identify prospective clients challenges and formulate tailored strategies to alleviate pain points</li>
<li>Strong quantitative aptitude and the ability to communicate complex technical concepts in simple terms</li>
<li>Driven and goal-oriented with the ability to contribute to a highly collaborative team environment</li>
<li>Experience preparing and delivering formal proposals/presentations</li>
<li>Proficient knowledge of Microsoft Office</li>
<li>History of outstanding achievements and proven sales skills</li>
<li>Strong analytical, communication, and interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Strong work ethic and customer focus are required</li>
<li>Strong CRM experience (i.e. Salesforce, Pipedrive, Hubspot, Zoho, etc.)</li>
<li>Ability to travel</li>
<li>Experience at ad agencies, publisher, ad networks, SSPs, DSPs and ad exchanges preferred</li>
</ul>
<p>Successful candidates will be considered from Austin, Dallas, Houston, Denver, Chicago, or Atlanta regions.</p>
