<div><b>Job Description</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>This is an opportunity to join one of the fastest growing industry teams as a leader in the life sciences vertical. The Veeva Solutions Architect will drive solution architecture in sales pursuits, develop and refine delivery accelerators, be a trusted client advisor in the vertical and ensure consistent delivery for clients in the Veeva space. This person will also actively engage with industry partners across the Veeva landscape and will help drive campaigns and content to help grow pipeline.</div>
<div></div>
<div>We can hire for this role anywhere in the US, as long as you are located near an Accenture office, and are willing and able to travel domestically approximately 80% of the time to meet client needs.
</div>

<div><b>Basic Qualifications</b></div>
<ul>
<li>A minimum of 5 years of progressive experience in project management, business analysis, and technology implementation in full life-cycle Cloud/Digital engagements.
</li>
<li>A minimum of 3 years of experience leading Veeva CRM or Veeva Vault delivery, either through work at industry participants or consulting to the industry.
</li>
<li>A minimum of 3 years of experience in the Life Sciences OR Consumer Goods vertical is required
</li>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree or equivalent (minimum 12 years) work experience. (If Associates Degree, must have minimum 6 years work experience).</li>
</ul>

<div></div>

</li>

</li>
<li>Excellent leadership and management skills, including communication skills
