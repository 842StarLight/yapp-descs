<p>The content marketing consultant will be part of the long-term care marketing team and be responsible for writing marketing content across multiple mediums (Email, brochures, whitepapers).</p>
<p><b>During this role the candidate will gain</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Insight into the financial services industry and experience collaborating across multiple business units, such as: marketing, sales, product, compliance</li>
<li>Opportunities to gain insight and exposure in multiple areas of marketing, including: distribution, advertising, PR, automation, and lead generation</li>
<li>Working knowledge of multiple systems including: Salesforce; Online marketing center (OMC), Workfront (project management system)</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Write and develop content for different channels and audiences that aligns with overarching campaigns</li>
<li>Write and develop content for different marketing mediums (email, social, brochures, whitepapers, etc) and be able to tailor the tone for advisors, consumers, and wholesalers</li>
<li>Support the marketing strategy and campaign execution throughout the year</li>
<li>Identify opportunities to optimize campaigns and react to shifting market conditions or business priorities</li>
<li>Leverage centers of influence across the organization to enhance your go-to-market content strategy and execution plans</li>
<li>Lead the development of content for print, social and digital platforms that help support the entire portfolio<b>
</b>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Skills and Abilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Previous experience writing for financial services is required</li>
<li>Experience in B2B advisor marketing is required</li>
<li>Experience in insurance marketing is preferred</li>
<li>Extremely well-organized with ability to prioritize; appropriate sense of urgency</li>
<li>Proactive approach to problem-solving and attention to detail</li>
<li>Self-directed and resourceful with a strong work ethic and the ability to work well independently</li>
<li>Proven ability to multi-task and be flexible</li>
<li>Financial services experience preferred</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Education</b></p>
