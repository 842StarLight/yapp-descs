<h2>Regional Property Manager - (East Charlotte)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>As a Regional Property Manager, you will be responsible for: </b><br>Assisting in the development and monitoring of company policies and procedures related to property management; such as recruitment, training, development, and supervision of Property Managers, and shares responsibility for maintaining the physical asset and performance of assigned assets. This position directs and coordinates all leasing, marketing, operational, maintenance and financial activities of the assets within their portfolio to ensure that the business objectives are achieved.</p>
<p><b>Some things we require you to have in this role include: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of a high school diploma is preferred. also preferred.</li>
<li>5+ years current experience in multi-site property management and residential property operations, including all facets of managing the physical, operational, financial, and customer service performance of a wide variety of asset types under varying market conditions.</li>
<li>Proven ability to successfully manage multiple residential assets equal to or greater than the unit count for this position.</li>
<li>Broad operational and administrative experience within the full investment cycle in various asset classes including participation in acquisition due diligence, asset reposition and rehabilitation, stabilized operations, and asset dispositions.</li>
<li>Outstanding communication skills - both oral and written.</li>
<li>Exceptional time-management, organizational and creative abilities.</li>
<li>Ability to understand technical evaluations as they apply to the investment and/or operations.</li>
<li>Strong reporting and self-discipline skills; must have high sense of urgency, be able to prioritize and meet multiple deadlines.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to apply principles of logical thinking to define problems, collect pertinent data, establish facts, draw valid conclusions and initiate appropriate course of action.</li>
<li>Effectively convey ideas, images and goals to a diverse group of personalities.</li>
<li>A positive attitude and the ability to smile under all circumstances.</li>
<li>Knowledgeable of GAAP accounting practices as it relates to accrual based accounting.</li>
<li>Proficient in use of computer as well as basic business office software applications including MS Office, Excel, Word, PowerPoint, and Outlook. Experience with Yardi or similar products preferred.</li>
<li>Sharp skills in identifying and attracting the target market, determining market rents in order to optimize revenue goals and economic occupancy objectives.</li>
<li>Basic understanding of building maintenance and mechanical functions.</li>
<li>Valid driver’s license and able to provide reliable transportation.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>We would like you to have: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Industry certifications (e.g. CPM, CAPS, CAM, etc.)</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in business, finance, marketing, or related field</li>
<li>An existing network/pipeline of profiled small businesses</li>
<li>A passionate, driven work ethic who can teach, tailor and take control</li>
<li>Bachelor's Degree in a business or technical discipline and / or equivalent experience in related field</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Some things you will do daily include: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop and manage individual site business plans including investment objectives, compliance goals, marketing positioning, people, asset quality, and financial goals and objectives.</li>
<li>Acts as primary liaison between Senior Management and Property Management</li>
<li>Review and approve all property purchasing with complete adherence to the expense budget.</li>
<li>Perform a physical review of the portfolio bi-weekly, as needed, assessing general property appearance, condition, stability, and satisfaction of maintenance and capital programs.</li>
<li>Mentor, coach, supervise and develop site staff</li>
<li>Respond to and remedy all escalated complaints from residents in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Supervision of assigned residential assets, ensuring the financial reporting guidelines and timelines are being consistently followed</li>
<li>Oversee the overall Financial, Leasing, Resident Retention, and Administrative functions and responsibilities of the assigned region properties.</li>
</ul>
