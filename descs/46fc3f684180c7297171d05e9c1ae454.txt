<h2>Buyer 1/Inventory Control Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Carrier Great Lakes is a Heating, Ventilating, &amp; Air Conditioning (HVAC) wholesaler in Michigan and Northern Ohio. Carrier Great Lakes has over 20 years in the industry, offering the best brands in the business backed by the most experienced personnel in the industry. Our focus is on supporting our customers with our continued commitment to understanding their needs and finding the best solutions to service them.</p>
<p>There is an opportunity to join CGL’s Inventory Control Department in Livonia, MI.</p>
<p><b>What you will be doing in this position: </b></p>
<p>Prepare and enter purchase orders, verifies inventory availability, maintains database of vendors and product maintenance, solicit bid proposals and review requisitions for goods and services. Monitor shipments to ensure that goods come in on time, and in the event of problems trace shipments and follow up undelivered goods. Confer with staff, users, and vendors to discuss defective or unacceptable goods or services and determine corrective action.</p>
<p>Serve as liaison between department and outside vendors, answering questions and resolving any problems that arise relative to orders.</p>
<p>Instruct Sales department personnel in policies and procedures for obtaining material/products.</p>
<p>Facilitate on-going business relationships with suppliers while keeping focus on quality, productivity and cost targets.</p>
<p>Work with suppliers to obtain cost-saving contracts/material cost reduction.</p>
<p>Develop and support department analytics.</p>
<p>Analyze inventory needs.</p>
<p>Review backorders for purchasing needs</p>
<p>Process stock and customer drop shipment PO’s daily</p>
<p>Review Vendor acknowledgements for accuracy</p>
<p>Maintain and update ERP system with pricing changes</p>
<p>Inventory Management</p>
<p>Release replenishment backorders to ship daily</p>
<p>Process stock rotations as needed</p>
<p>Regular review and updating of stocking levels</p>
<p>Process inventory adjustments as needed</p>
<p>Manage physical inventory and cycle counts</p>
<p>Other duties as assigned</p>
<p><b>What you'll bring to the job: </b></p>
<p>Bachelor of Science degree in Business, Business Administration or Supply Chain Management or 3-5 years of supply chain / purchasing related experience.</p>
<p>Proficiency in Microsoft Office applications is a must, with a focus on Excel.</p>
<p>Experience in Enterprise purchasing systems- Infor, Infor SX.e, Trends, SAP is a plus.</p>
<p>Familiarity with purchasing/supply standardized methods and inventory control systems. Knowledge of purchasing policies and procedures and the ability to utilize procurement tool applications and techniques.</p>
<p>Good problem solving abilities</p>
<p>Excellent verbal and written communication skills</p>
<p>Must be self-motivated/self-directed, flexible, experienced working independently and thrive in a fast-paced, multi-tasking environment</p>
<p>Strategic thinker with unquestioned ethics</p>
<p>Desire to continuously contribute and improve</p>
<p>Business Acumen (Analytical, Project Management and Planning Skills)</p>
<p>Carrier Great Lakes offers an attractive salary and benefits package.</p>
<p>This position does not offer relocation and will be located in Livonia, MI.</p>
