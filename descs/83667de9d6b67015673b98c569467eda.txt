<h2>Regional Property Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Job Description</b></p>
<p>Atlanta-based real estate services company that consists of several wholly owned subsidiary companies that focus on acquisition, property management, and development/preservation of multi-family housing is currently seeking a Regional Manager to join our team. This position will be based out of the Orlando, FL area. The Regional Property Manager is responsible for overseeing all aspects of a portfolio of assigned properties. The Regional Property Manager will work closely with senior management, property owners, investors, asset managers, and internal departments to ensure that each property performs at a high-level.</p>
<p><b>Duties and Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Effectively lead geographically dispersed teams of property management professionals to operate a portfolio of assigned properties.</li>
<li>Complete regular site visits to each property to engage with the site teams, complete quarterly audits, recommend maintenance and capital improvements</li>
<li>Successfully recruit, train, and develop high-performing associates in all site level roles to maximize the performance of our sites and develop the future leaders of our company.</li>
<li>Provide performance coaching to under-performing site associates, initiate disciplinary action, and complete approved terminations as necessary.</li>
<li>Work directly with Property Managers to prepare aggressively achievable site budgets that meet or exceed the goals of the owner/investor.</li>
<li>Manage the financial performance of each property to ensure compliance with the approved budget, produce accurate financial reports, and provide advance notification of anticipated out of budget expenses.</li>
<li>Review and approve property invoices, resident refunds, leasing commissions, and monitor the use of credit cards issued to on-site associates.</li>
<li>Develop, implement, and continually evaluate a unique marketing plan for each property with the assistance of the marketing department and on-site team.</li>
<li>Continuously evaluate and communicate suggestions for improving site and/or corporate operations and participate in the discussion and decision-making process of enacting positive change.</li>
<li>Coordinate all construction and rehabilitation projects including planning, vendor engagement and bidding, progress inspections, timeline (scheduling) and approval of completed projects.</li>
<li>Ensure sites are prepared for hazardous weather situations in order to minimize damage to the property.</li>
<li>Maintain a positive attitude and provide excellent customer service to our residents, owners/investors, and other associates.</li>
<li>Follow established company policies and complete all work in an ethical, fair, and consistent manner.</li>
<li>Complete all other tasks as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Sound business acumen and the ability to gather information required to make educated decisions.</li>
<li>Must have the ability to manage multiple demands concurrently in an organized manner.</li>
<li>Excellent oral and written communication skills using the English language.</li>
<li>Excellent working knowledge of computers and the Microsoft Office Suite.</li>
<li>Knowledge of OneSite Property Management software a plus.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Experience, Education, and Certifications: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree from a 4-year college or university or job related experience required</li>
<li>At least 3 to 5 years of related supervisory experience with a proven track record is required</li>
<li>Valid driver’s license, reliable transportation and method of contact</li>
</ul>
