<h2>IS Technical Specialist (Senior Salesforce Developer)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Description
</b></h1>
<p>The Customer Sales and Service team is responsible for Huntington’s Enterprise Sales and Service platform (ESS) as well as Huntington’s Customer Relationship Management and pre-sales marketing platforms (Salesforce Sales, Service, and Marketing clouds). The team delivers and maintains high-quality applications, features, and capabilities to over a dozen business segments supporting all aspects of complex financial industry activities.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The Customer Sales and Service team is seeking an experienced Salesforce.com senior level developer with superior technical skill in Lightning development on the Salesforce Sales &amp; Service Clouds. The senior developer’s primary focus will be on strategic project efforts. In addition, the senior developer will provide assistance researching production defects on an as-needed basis. The ideal candidate for this position would be able to identify, assesses and solve complex business problems, where analysis of situations or data requires an in-depth evaluation of variable factors. This position would closely follow the strategic direction set by senior management when establishing near term goals, although has some latitude in decision-making. Decisions have a major day to day impact on area of responsibility.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Detailed Description:
</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Provides strong problem-solving, interpersonal and communication skills (both verbal and written)
</li>
<li>Owns and communicates clearly, design and project deliverables
</li>
<li>Develops working relationships with individuals at all levels of an organization
</li>
<li>Analyzes, designs, and develops systems based upon user specifications
</li>
<li>Maintains and demonstrates knowledge of technical industry trends in Cloud Computing, particularly as they apply to Huntington
</li>
<li>Assists with identifying training needs and/or with training of less experienced staff
</li>
<li>Assists with performance monitoring and troubleshooting for salesforce applications when necessary during production support and deployment exercises
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Basic Qualifications:<br>
<br>
</p>
<ul><li>Bachelor's Degree
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Minimum of 5 years technical and business work experience developing applications utilizing Salesforce.com platform Salesforce toolkit and Force.com platform technologies: custom objects, controllers &amp; triggers, and workflows, Apex classes, Lightning, Visualforce, SOQL, SOAP/REST based web services
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p>Preferred Qualifications:
</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Hands-on Salesforce Lightning migration strategies and development experience
</li>
<li>Web services API integrations
</li>
<li>Experience evaluating and executing a large-scale Classic to Lightning user experience migration
</li>
<li>Experience with java/J2EE/.Net object-oriented technologies
</li>
<li>Exposing apex classes as web services
</li>
<li>Experience working with ETL tools such as WebSphere DataStage highly preferred
</li>
<li>Has experience with applications utilizing SOA technologies such as WebSphere Enterprise Service Bus and calling web services from within Salesforce in a production environment
</li>
</ul>
