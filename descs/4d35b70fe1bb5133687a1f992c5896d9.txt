<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
What you will be doing</b></h4>
<ul>
<li>
Calling hotels to generate business for the sales team (primary role)</li>
<li>
Perform initial onboarding of strategic accounts</li>
<li>
Provide ongoing customer and technical support via phone, email, and ticketing systems</li>
<li>
Drive usage and product adoption with pro-active customer outreach</li>
<li>
Work closely with Sales team to share customer feedback that may inform product enhancement and or further sales opportunities</li>
<li>
Become an authority at using lead generation tools to routinely extract contacts and craft accurate and targeted lists of prospects</li>
<li>
Conduct sales development best practices with email, phone and to connect with new prospects.</li>
<li>
Utilize smart, targeted questions to speak knowledgeably with decisions makers such as VPs and Directors of Sales</li>
<li>
Expertly build interest and opportunities with new prospects</li>
</ul>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
The ideal candidate for this position</b></h4>
<ul>
<li>
Will not shy away from cold calling</li>
<li>
Can build a good rapport over a telephone conversation</li>
<li>
Will be persistent and can overcome barriers</li>
<li>
Strong written, verbal, and presentation skills</li>
<li>
Ability to understand customer needs and problem solve</li>
<li>
Develop tools, processes, and best practices to ensure customers realize the most significant possible value from Hospitality Online</li>
<li>
Experience working with a dynamic CRM</li>
</ul>
