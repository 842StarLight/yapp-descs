<h2>Revenue Operations Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div><div>
<div>
<div>Come help fuel growth for one of the fastest-growing startups in the digital marketing industry by joining our team!</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
AdHawk &amp; FloorForce are looking for a Revenue Operations Manager to join the Business Operations team, reporting to the Director of Business Operations.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
In this role, you will partner with key stakeholders on a variety of business initiatives, ranging from developing Sales strategies to accelerating deal velocity &amp; revenue growth, to improving tactical Marketing and Financial operations. You will provide robust data insights / analysis, systems support, training and change management to the organization. As a Revenue Ops Manager, you will also oversee management of third party applications, establish best practices for the Sales, Account Management, Marketing and Finance teams and mentor more junior Revenue Ops team members. Ultimately, you will be responsible for implementing and project managing new infrastructures to support the company's current &amp; future growth aspirations, and will be seen as a key member within the Business Operations team that is helping to accelerate growth for an amazing startup!</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
What You'll Do:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Collaborate closely with Sales &amp; Account Management leadership to design, execute, refine and implement strategic initiatives</li>
<li>
Partner with the Marketing department to, among other projects, refine lead qualification and provide analysis of campaign efficiency</li>
<li>
Oversee the administration of all software applications used by the Sales, AM and Marketing teams (e.g., Salesforce, SalesLoft, Marketo, HubSpot, etc.) and ensure utilization, timeliness and accuracy of these systems (e.g., reporting, account-level information, etc.)</li>
<li>
Provide recommendations on and evaluate, manage and train stakeholders on industry tools &amp; resources that can be leveraged to drive efficiency throughout the organization</li>
<li>
Work with Finance and BI functions to ensure consistent &amp; accurate data across systems, report on key company metrics and ensure specific activities (e.g., commissions) are handled properly</li>
<li>
Create and manage operational reports and dashboards to monitor team performance</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Who You Are:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree required</li>
<li>
3+ years of experience within Business Operations or Revenue Operations</li>
<li>
Experience with MS Excel, Salesforce, SalesLoft and Marketo is strongly preferred</li>
<li>
Experience with HubSpot, Google Suite, Google Analytics, NinjaCat and other reporting / data systems are nice to haves</li>
<li>
Track record of successful project &amp; people management</li>
<li>
Able to understand high-level strategic needs, develop insights &amp; recommendations and ensure proper execution and business impact</li>
<li>
Strong attention to detail with exceptional organizational skills</li>
<li>
Able to anticipate business needs and maintain positive relationships with key stakeholders</li>
<li>
Self-starter who is an expert problem-solver and can work independently &amp; collaboratively</li>
<li>
Excellent communication skills (both verbal and written) with the ability to synthesize complex data and deliver meaningful, easy-to-understand insights &amp; recommendations to stakeholders</li>
</ul></div>
