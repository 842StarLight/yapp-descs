<div><b>
Hardware Design Engineer Summer Intern</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>Want to work with the best and brightest in a company that takes on challenges Never Been Done Before? Emerson’s Machine Automation Solutions business develops electronic hardware and software used in factory and process automation. We are looking for students who are interested in gaining real-world experience in this dynamic environment.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>As a team member, you’ll share in the satisfaction of delivering industry-leading products and solutions that help provide our customers with state-of-the-art control systems. As an intern, you will be part of a hardware team that assists with new product developments and supports efforts to address field, manufacturing, and obsolescence issues. Many of the assignments will involve opportunities for hands-on interaction with PACSystems RX3i equipment including prototype validation, test fixture construction, and electromagnetic compliance (EMC) noise testing.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Responsibilities</b></div>
<div></div>
<p>The HARDWARE DESIGN ENGINEER Summer Intern will work with senior Emerson Technology team mentors and industry experts to:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Lead one or more projects that will be started and completed during the internship term.</li>
<li>
Work with advanced diagnostic equipment to help validate new prototype designs.</li>
<li>
Assist in setting up and conducting EMC tests.</li>
<li>
Provide engineering support to other departments.</li>
<li>
Present to the team periodically and at the end of the internship.</li>
</ul>
</div>
