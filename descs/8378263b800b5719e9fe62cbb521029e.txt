<h2>Restaurant Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>OVERALL DESCRIPTION OF JOB SCOPE AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b><b>:</b></p>
<p>
Responsible for managing daily restaurant operations and assists with menu planning, maintains sanitation standards and assists servers and hosts on the floor during peak meal periods. Strives to continually improve guest and employee satisfaction and maximize the financial performance in areas of responsibility.</p>
<p><b>
RELATIONSHIPS</b><b>:</b> This position reports to the Director of Food &amp; Beverage, retains relationships with peers and all other departments within the hotel/restaurant, maintains relationships with current and prospective guests, and maintains relationships with suppliers, vendors, and others serving the Company or the hotel/restaurant.
</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL JOB FUNCTIONS AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b><b>:</b></p>
<p>
Please note that the essential job functions listed here are not all-inclusive, rather they indicate the types of activities normally performed by this position and, therefore, may be modified and revised. Other duties may be required and assigned from time-to-time.</p>
<ul>
<li>
Oversee the dining area, supervises food and beverage service staff in accordance with operating policies.</li>
<li>
Creates a positive team atmosphere among Team Members.</li>
<li>
Provides feedback and coaching to the Team regularly.</li>
<li>
Understands building capability through cross training.</li>
<li>
Treats all Team Members fairly and with respect.</li>
<li>
Sets high standards for appropriate team behavior on shift.</li>
<li>
Works with food and beverage staff to ensure proper food presentation and proper food-handling procedures.</li>
<li>
Schedules periodic food and beverage service staff meetings to ensure correct interpretation of policies and obtain feedback from staff members.</li>
<li>
Maintain budget and employee records, prepare payroll, and pay bills, or monitor bookkeeping records.</li>
<li>
Check quality of deliveries of fresh food and baked goods.</li>
<li>
Meet with sales representatives to order supplies such as tableware, cooking utensils, and cleaning items.</li>
<li>
Arrange for maintenance and repair of equipment and other services.</li>
<li>
Total receipts and balance against sales, deposit receipts, and lock facility at end of day.</li>
<li>
Is capable of handling irate customers with a friendly/calm attitude.</li>
<li>
Ensures product quality and great service.</li>
<li>
Shows enthusiasm about guests within the restaurant.</li>
<li>
Effectively forecasts restaurant needs.</li>
<li>
Shifts priorities and goals as work demands change.</li>
<li>
Delegates and follows-up effectively.</li>
<li>
Taking ownership of issues or tasks and also give detail update to the Director of Food &amp; Beverage and the General Manager.</li>
<li>
Seeks, listens and responds to guest feedback.</li>
<li>
Coaches team on how to exceed guest expectations.</li>
<li>
Effectively identifies and resolves restaurant problems through reports.</li>
<li>
Proficiency in using computer software to monitor inventory, track staff schedules and pay, and perform other record keeping tasks.</li>
<li>
Proficiency in Point of Sale POS software, inventory software, restaurant guest satisfactory tracking software etc.</li>
<li>
Assists in planning regular and special event menus.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
JOB QUALIFICATIONS</b><b>:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Degree in restaurant management or equivalent education and experience.</li>
<li>
Three years of experience in a management position.</li>
<li>
Strong interpersonal and communication skills; ability to speak clearly and listen attentively.</li>
<li>
Strong organizational skills and attention to detail.</li>
<li>
Effective problem solving skills.</li>
<li>
Ability to handle pressure with poise and finesse.</li>
<li>
Positive attitude, outgoing and friendly personality.</li>
<li>
Strong leadership and a professional image.</li>
</ul>
