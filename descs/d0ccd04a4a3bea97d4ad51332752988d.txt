<p>Description</p>

<div><div>
<p>The Billing and Credit Specialist will process Customer billing and credit transactions to include billing of month end invoicing, working with Accounts Receivable Team to address customer inquiries and resolve customer billing disputes, and the review and approval of customer credits issued by other departments.</p>
<p>
Here at Ultimate Software, we truly put our people first. We strongly believe in teamwork, and we encourage and trust our people to reach higher, learn more, and live up to their potential. Ultimate is ranked #1 on Fortune's Best Places to Work in Technology for 2019 and #8 on the 100 Best Companies to Work For list in 2019. Ultimate is also ranked #2 on Fortune’s 75 Best Workplaces for Women and #5 on its Best Workplaces for Diversity list. Learn more about US here: www.ultimatesoftware.com/careers</p>
<p><b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p>
Essential duties and responsibilities include the following. Other duties may be assigned.</p>
<ul>
<li>Preparing, posting, and sending invoices to customers, including subscription fee and implementation fee invoices.</li>
<li>Communicating with clients and internal parties regarding invoices, payments, and agreement terms, providing exemplary Customer Service to clients and Ultimate Software customer teams via e-mail &amp; phone calls.</li>
<li>Providing timely responses to Customers and Accounts Receivable Team in relation to customer billing inquiries or disputes.</li>
<li>Reading and understanding contract terms, the Contract 360 module in Salesforce, and the Revenue and Billing schedules in order to address customer inquiries and assess any need for billing correction via additional invoices or credits.</li>
<li>Reviewing and approving Customer credits submitted by other departments, including review of credit reason codes and General Ledger account codes to each credit request.</li>
<li>Create statements for customers as needed.</li>
<li>Create miscellaneous invoices for required customers.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Knowledge and Abilities:</b></p>
<p>
To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. The requirements listed here are representative of the knowledge, skill and ability required.</p>
<ul>
<li>Strong organizational skills and attention to detail required</li>
<li>Ability to manage priorities and meet deadlines in a dynamic environment</li>
<li>Ability to research billing issues and communicate relevant conclusions, both verbally and in written format</li>
<li>Proficient in Excel</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Necessary Training:</b></p>
<p>
Billing and Credit Specialist needs to understand the Accounting System of Record. They need to understand Sales Force and Contract 360. They need to be able to use excel and understand existing formulas to accurately and properly prepare invoice batches for billing and credit calculations. They also need to be able to read contracts and understand the impact of billing and payment terms, employee count types, the functionality of the Revenue and Billing schedules, and the relationship between parent / children Customer accounts. Billing and Credit Specialist needs to understand the impact of taxation among the US, CAD and Europe.</p>
<p><b>
Education, Work Experience:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>BS in Accounting, Finance, Business or related fields is a plus.</li>
<li>Working knowledge of Epicor Software is a plus.</li>
<li>Working knowledge of Salesforce is a plus.</li>
<li>Previous experience in Billing/Crediting role is a plus.</li>
<br>
</ul>
