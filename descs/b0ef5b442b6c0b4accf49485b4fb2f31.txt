<h2>Client Service Representative - Viacom Ad Solutions</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview and Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Viacom Ad Solutions is a leading innovative advertising organization that delivers the advertising revenue across all Viacom media brands using cutting-edge research, data, partner marketing, and sales strategy approaches. We work across all media platforms – TV, digital, social, experiential/ live events, emerging platforms; and all audiences – from young kids with the Nickelodeon brands, to Millennials with brands like MTV and Comedy Central; as well fans of TV Land.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Viacom is always on the lookout for fresh talent at the Client Service Representative level to join our MPS team.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The CSR is responsible for supporting one or more sales teams for all aspects of account management during the sales and selling cycle. Maintain assigned accounts on a day-to-day basis after the close of a sale, from order booking through invoice clearance. The position has frequent contact with clients, high level industry executives, and a diverse group of internal constituents. Position is focused on exceeding our customers’ post sale needs, while adhering to all internal policies and processes.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Responsibilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Build effective relationships at agencies through on-going communication with various departments i.e. Buying, Traffic, and Accounting</li>
<li>
Coordinate with Sales, P&amp;I, and Client Service Management on the maintenance of agency accounts including:</li>
<li>
Upfront and Scatter order entry</li>
<li>
Timely delivery of advertiser schedules/flowcharts</li>
<li>
Input of all inventory adds, deletes, and moves per P&amp;I, and/or client direction (with appropriate approval)</li>
<li>
Maintain schedule changes, both internal and external, including follow-up with agencies</li>
<li>
Product/Brand allocations</li>
<li>
Discrepancy resolution</li>
<li>
Invoice Clearance</li>
<li>
Flight Changes</li>
<li>
Liability tracking</li>
<li>
ADU scheduling</li>
<li>
MSA post analysis</li>
<li>
EDI maintenance for applicable accounts</li>
<li>
Collaborate with sales teams in weekly meetings and though maintenance of account status reports</li>
<li>
Proficient use of WIDE ORBIT and PLAN-IT systems to manage deals</li>
<li>
Support and enhance agency relationships by monitoring requests and ensuring commercial spots are booked according to guidelines</li>
<li>
Monitor BOB and unit exception reports to ensure all contracted dollars are booked and all unschedulable units are placed</li>
<li>
Work with P&amp;I to flight deals, distribute weekly weights, maintain CPM guidelines, manage the effect of program changes on schedules</li>
<li>
Communicates daily with Commercial Operations to resolve any traffic issues and reconcile discrepancies</li>
<li>
Work closely with Billing and A/R teams to reconcile and clear invoices in a timely manner, monitor accounts receivable, and issue invoice adjustments</li>
</ul>
