<h2>Research Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Clariant is a focused and innovative specialty chemical company based near Basel in Switzerland. Last year the company recorded CHF 6.6 billion in sales harnessing the talents of its 18 000 employees across 53 countries.
</p>
<p>Clariant’s portfolio is optimized for future growth, with a focus in four business areas: Care Chemicals, Catalysts, Natural Resources, and Plastics &amp; Coatings.
</p>
<p>We believe that our growth is all about our customers and their success. Investment in innovation capabilities and developing inspiring environments for courageous and determined minds are therefore key to reaching our goal.
</p>
<p>Join us and help create sustainable value through chemistry for customers all over the globe.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The Research Engineer is responsible, as a member of the project team, for the evaluation of catalytic materials to develop new or improved commercial catalyst products.<br>
</p>
<p><br>
<b>Responsibilities<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Operate, maintain, and troubleshoot catalyst test units and analytical tools </b>used to discover new catalysts and processes that deliver value to SC businesses .
</li>
<li>Specifically, evaluate catalysts in high-throughput and conventional <b>fixed bed </b>and batch reactors using analytical hardware such as gas and liquid chromatography.
</li>
<li>Plan experiments in collaboration with the technical project team.
</li>
<li>Conduct equipment operational programming.
</li>
<li>
<b>Develop and implement GC methods </b>and maintain equipment.
</li>
<li>Characterize catalysts using BET, XRD, LS, SEM, etc. and interpret resulting data.
</li>
<li>May prepare or assist in preparing catalytic materials.
</li>
<li>
<b>Work-up, analyze, and present data </b>using appropriate specialty software packages such as DOE, visualization, and statistical analysis programs.
</li>
<li>Communicate results internally via reports, presentations, and informally.
</li>
<li>Generate IP and assist with patent preparation.
</li>
<li>
<b>Operate a safe laboratory </b>by working closely with the site safety committee and coworkers<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Supervisory Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<p>• May direct technical/research assistants when applicable.<br>
</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Requirements<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>BS in Chemical Engineering, Materials Science, or a related field with 3-8 years of industrial experience in heterogeneous catalysis, or (PHD or MS) in Chemical Engineering, Materials Science, or a related field with 1-5 years of industrial experience in heterogeneous catalysis
</li>
<li>Skilled in the evaluation of <b>solid catalysts in fixed bed </b>and batch reactor systems.
</li>
<li>Knowledgeable in the use of <b>automated laboratory hardware and software </b>, and in data analysis and reduction software and techniques.
</li>
<li>Experience with analytical methods such as XRD, SEM, TEM, XPS, BET, IR, TPD\TPR, HPLC, <b>GC </b>, MS, etc.; including method development.
</li>
<li>Record of success (commercialized products, patents, publications) conducting team-oriented catalyst research.
</li>
<li>Senior technical team member with responsibilities in one or more projects; ability to execute aspects of multiple projects simultaneously.
</li>
<li>Serve as a mentor and technical resource to other technical staff.
</li>
<li>
<b>Superior creativity and imagination </b>with respect to new products, processes, procedures
</li>
<li>
<b>Strong communication, interpersonal, and teamwork skills </b>with extended project teams on HT projects.
</li>
<li>Expertise in high throughput R&amp;D methods.
</li>
<li>Optionally experienced in catalyst synthesis<br>
</li>
</ul>
