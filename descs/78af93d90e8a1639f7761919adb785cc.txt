<p>Position/Title: BIM Piping Project Coordinator - VDC</p>
<p>
Reporting To: Lead, Sr. Project Coordinator -VDC</p>
<p>
Location (Facility): Easton, PA Global Headquarters</p>
<p>
**RELOCATION ASSISTANCE IS OFFERED FOR THIS OPENING***</p>
<div>
Victaulic, the global leader in mechanical pipe joining innovations, is searching for the best and brightest Building Information Modeling (BIM) talent in the industry. We're looking to add BIM Project Coordinators for Our VDC Virtual Design Construction team. Victaulic is the world's leading producer of mechanical pipe joining solutions. The company has 14 manufacturing facilities and 29 branches worldwide with 4,200 employees who speak 43 languages across the globe. With over 1900 global patents, Victaulic solutions are at work in more than 140 countries across diverse business lines including oil and gas, chemical, mining, power generation, water and wastewater treatment, military and marine, as well as commercial building and fire protection.</div>

<br>
<div>
<b>
Responsibilities:</b><br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>
1.<b> </b>Must have comprehensive piping project management skills (i.e. scheduling, budgeting, expediting, material coordination, customer interface) and be able to:<br>
2. Perform Quality Control Review of drawings and bill of materials (plans &amp; sections, isometric's, spool sheets, B.O.M.'s) produced both in-house and by the VDC office, utilizing VDC procedures and standards.<br>
3. Ability to prepare 3D drawings in Revit, AutoCAD &amp; CADmep as well as various other design modeling and coordination software (Navisworks) formats.<br>
4. Must have comprehensive piping layout skills, including:<br>
a.) Understanding of mechanical equipment rooms and systems (HVAC piping, steam, plumbing, industrial process piping, water and wastewater piping &amp; Fire Protection)<br>
 b.) Knowledge of other mechanical trades (Sheetmetal, Plumbing, Electrical, Maritime)<br>
 c.) Knowledge of construction techniques (Architectural, Structural)<br>
5. Prepare and coordinate complete BEP (Basic Engineering Package), adhering to established guidelines<br>
6. For projects that have been transferred to the VDC, provide assistance and comments as required, along with attending daily coordination meetings.<br>
7. Advise the VDC BIM Manager on a weekly basis on status of all assigned projects and assist with project close out and customer satisfaction reports.<br>
8. Utilize and enforce the VDC PM Division procedures and policies.<br>
9. Work with Victaulic's customer care department regarding the preparation of and/or changes to advanced bill of materials prepared by VDC Coordinators.<br>
10. Assist Sales with presentation of VDC drawing quotes (as required).<br>
11. Assist VDC Pre-Construction, Specification, and Software Development departments. (as required).<br>
12. Must have ability to travel to jobsites to obtain accurate field measurements and interact with customers and Victaulic salespeople as required.<br>
13. Assist in the training of Piping Coordinators, Draftspersons, Draftsperson Trainees, and Interns.<br>
14. Utilize and participate in Lean implementation and the Victaulic Quality Improvement Process.<br>
</div>
