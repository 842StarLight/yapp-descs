<div>Description<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>Are you a builder? Do you enjoy designing and enabling powerful programs that help guide business decisions? The IT Risk Management team is an integral part of the Enterprise Technology &amp; Operations division, providing IT risk governance and support across our key IT divisions. At Zions Bancorporation, we are looking for team members who want to share their experience and insight in developing and adhering to a strong risk culture.<br>
</div>
<p></p>
<div>In this Risk Governance Relationship Manager position, you will be a key partner to our IT lines of business, identifying risk impact to the business and organization, helping shape remediation efforts, and providing transparency to executive leadership on the IT risk environment. To be successful in this Technology Governance Relationship Manager opportunity it is important that you have broad Relationship Management experience developing and maintaining trusted business, risk, and audit partnerships. In this role we would count on your Information Technology and Risk Management experience. This opportunity is perfect for you, if you thrive on developing comprehensive risk strategies while driving execution and results.<br>
</div>
