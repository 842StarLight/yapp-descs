<p><b>Senior Manager, Consulting Partner Alliances – North America</b></p>
<p><b>
Profisee Group Inc. Background</b></p>
<p>
Profisee has enjoyed phenomenal growth in the United States, Canada, and Europe, positioning us to become one of the new global leaders in the Data Management software market. Profisee has recently been positively profiled in the Gartner Magic Quadrant for Master Data Management (MDM) solutions, we are the fastest growing MDM solutions provider in the industry, and we are already the number one global provider of Master Data Management software solutions on the Microsoft platform. Hundreds of companies leverage the Profisee Platform as their MDM technology of choice to solve enterprise data management problems. www.profisee.com</p>
<p><b>
Position Summary</b></p>
<p>
The Senior Manager, Consulting Partner Alliances is responsible for leading all aspects of Profisee’s engagement with consulting firms (aka “systems integrators” or “SIs”). The senior manager will lead our Consulting Partner Program to the next level, with an acute dual focus on generating incremental company software/subscription revenues and driving innovation and differentiation with the Profisee consulting partner ecosystem. This will be achieved by engaging consulting firms (some existing Consulting Partners, some targeted prospective partners) to:</p>
<ul>
<li> Create joint solutions (Profisee solution and partner’s expertise/IP) that will deliver business value to prospective customer in targeted market segments,</li>
<li> Drive thought leadership, points of view, and ‘door openers’ to influence targeted audiences to engage Profisee and the partner as a leading Data Management solutions provider, and</li>
<li> Galvanize and energize their sales and business development teams to seek and qualify for net new sales/project opportunities for Profisee and the partner.</li>
</ul>
<p>The role includes responsibilities for managing and steering our existing Consulting Partner relationships, ensuring our partners build Profisee-related competency and attain accreditations via our certification program, and developing/operationalizing focused joint business plans with individual partners. The overriding objective is evolve our Consulting Partner ecosystem such that it becomes a force multiplier for Profisee in the marketplace. This position reports to the Senior Vice President of Partner Alliances &amp; Channels for the company.</p>
<p><b>
Performance Objectives</b></p>
<p><b>
Lead and Guide – </b>fulfill the role of leading/steering/growing our market traction and business results with individual partners. Take a hands-on approach towards forging and ensuring successful execution of partner-specific business plans.</p>
<p><b>
Prospect and Recruit </b>– spearhead our efforts to identify, engage, recruit, and sign-on consulting firms that will generate additive value towards fulfilling our revenue, market share, and solution innovation goals.
</p>
<p><b>Coordinate and Lead</b> – leverage resources across our products, presales, sales enablement, and marketing teams to drive scale of newly signed partnerships.</p>
<p><b>
Align and Engage – </b>create and maintain active sales-focused collaboration with our field sales team. Partner with every Account Executive (AE) to create their location partner ecosystem to ‘extend their reach’ in their assigned territories. Work closely with our Sales VPs (AE managers) to ensure they build meaningful, impactful relationships with peers from our key consulting partners. Establish and own cadence between our Sales VPs and field/market executives from the partner ecosystem.</p>
<p><b>
Required Skills</b></p>
<p><b>
Courage and Tenacity – </b>the successful candidate must be resilient, persistent, and passionate about what we are doing. Help prospective partners see the tangible opportunity we are presenting to them.</p>
<p><b>
Self-motivated and Resourceful</b> – you must be able to execute to build and grow our Consulting Partner program. To be successful, a very hands-on approach is essential.</p>
<p><b>
Program Management/Process-oriented</b> – the ability to build a programmatic approach and scalable model for engaging, igniting, and sustaining a large set of Consulting Partners.</p>
<p><b>
Sense of Urgency – </b>a disposition of determination and grit to push, forge ahead, and ‘find a way’ to meet your business goals.</p>
<p><b>
Ownership and Accountability </b>– a person who had the mindset of ownership and that this is their business to make hugely successful.</p>
<p><b>
Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li> Influence, lead, and manage Profisee’s relationship with our Consulting Partners, with the bottom line goal being convincing them and motivating them to drive new joint business for Profisee and their company.</li>
<li> Meet assigned targets for signing up new partners and Consulting Partner-sourced Profisee license revenue.</li>
<li> Engage with highest potential Consulting Partners to spearhead the build of joint solutions/offerings plus aligned demand generation, market awareness campaigns and engagement of the partner’s and Profisee sales teams and drive incremental license sales.</li>
<li> Coordinate and leverage resources across our product management, development, sales, marketing, and customer delivery functions.</li>
<li> Evangelize and develop solid relationships with key contacts at all levels within our Consulting Partners.</li>
<li> Conduct regularly schedule business status reviews with key contacts and executives within Consulting Partners.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Required Experience and Education</b></p>
<ul>
<li> Relevant experience in establishing, managing and growing relationships with consulting firms that engage enterprise organizations to provide them transformation solutions, change management, and business strategy advice.</li>
<li> 10 years of partner sales and/or direct sales experience in the commercial software, including across a solution-selling, B2B sales environment.</li>
<li> Strong work ethic and relentless, with a proven track record of consistently meeting and exceeding your critical sales and initiative development goals.</li>
<li> Demonstrated experience with leading programs which require hands on work with partners.</li>
<li> Executive presence and excellent communication and presentation skills.</li>
<li> A college bachelor’s degree is required.</li>
</ul>
