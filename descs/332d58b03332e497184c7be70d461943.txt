<h2>Electrical Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Purpose of Position
</p>
<p>To design and create Powell standard products with reference of using customer specifications.
</p>
<p>Key Responsibilities
</p>
<ul>
<li>Design and consult on complex jobs.
</li>
<li>Read and interpret customer job specifications.
</li>
<li>Order materials.
</li>
<li>Create drawings.
</li>
<li>Transmit drawings to production and customer.
</li>
<li>Interface with project management, customer and production.
</li>
<li>Work leader responsibilities may include: task assignment, performance feedback, on-the-job training, directing lower level engineering personnel to appropriate sources of information, products, vendors, etc.
</li>
</ul>
<p>Relationships
</p>
<ul>
<li>Reports to Engineer Supervisor.
</li>
<li>Internal relationships include regular interaction: Engineering supervisors, project management, sales, engineering, and manufacturing.
</li>
<li>External relationships include regular interaction with customers and suppliers .
</li>
<li>Assist in the training of the lower levels of engineering.
</li>
</ul>
<p>Responsibilities, Skills, Experience, and Requirements
</p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 3-10 years of experience in an Engineering role
</li>
<li>BS Degree in Electrical Engineering or Electrical Technology
</li>
<li>Applicable computer skills using Word, Excel, Outlook, Lync
</li>
<li>Previous AutoCAD or drafting software skills preferred
</li>
<li>Develop Detailed Bills of material for Power Control Room using Agile, Oracle or other similar programs
</li>
<li>Ability to learn/use Primavera P6 to update project status and keep all assigned projects on schedule
</li>
<li>Ability to read customer specifications, compare to the quote and provide technical feedback to project management and the customer on differences
</li>
<li>Basic understanding to very proficient at job reviews (i.e., acquired knowledge of specs that Powell should take exception to and specs that require special attention; able to accurately interpret customer specs, etc.)
</li>
<li>Apply technical documents to develop building layouts, AC/DC panel board schedules, battery systems and cable tray layouts using AutoCAD
</li>
<li>Apply conduit, wire and breaker sizing per the NEC tables
</li>
<li>Meet all required NEC equipment spacing requirements for the PCR layout
</li>
<li>Able to apply NEC and various other codes as it applies to the design and energy efficiency of the PCR
</li>
<li>Basic to thorough understanding loop diagrams and relay panels in order to develop interconnect spreadsheets
</li>
<li>Apply cost competitive techniques to develop the most cost-effective solution for the engineered to order product
</li>
<li>Coordination with Project Management throughout the project
</li>
<li>Constant contact with production to answer questions and provide clarification and/or authorization on design changes
</li>
<li>Follow standard engineering processes and timelines.
</li>
<li>Worked on Multiple Project simultaneously and managed work load as per priority
</li>
<li>Available to analyze and answer technical questions from lower level engineers
</li>
<li>Able to understand, interpret and trouble shoot most customers’ needs and problems
</li>
<li>Provide technical assistance to sales, engineering and inspection on daily problems
</li>
<li>Assist with and solve problems of other production engineers on a daily basis.
</li>
<li>Check completed jobs of other engineers
</li>
<li>Learn policies and procedures, work instruction, ISO policies, Safety Rules and Regulations, and vendor products
</li>
</ul>

</p>

It is the responsibility of each employee to adhere to this policy, as well as to observe all safety practices and exercise every precaution for the safety of accidents.
</p>
