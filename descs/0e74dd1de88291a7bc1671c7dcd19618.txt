<h2>Leasing Consultant- Floater</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Looking to join a company that truly listens to its' team members and values teamwork? Want the opportunity to grow as the company grows?<b> LOOK NO FURTHER.</b>...<b>Stellar Equity Management wants you!</b> We are looking to invite customer focused, creative, dependable, self-starting, and self-disciplined individuals to join our team.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
The Position:</b> Leasing Consultant-Floater</p>
<p><b>
The Purpose:</b> To assist with leasing activities at the properties in our Stellar Equity portfolio, as assigned to. To represent our company in a professional and courteous manner when receiving residents, prospective residents, vendor, and the like. To help drive traffic to and lease up our community by incorporating efficiency, high energy, integrity, honesty, and a strong desire to make a difference in people's live. To help create a STELLAR community for our residents to call home across all of our Stellar Equity portfolio (including one LITCH community)!</p>
<p><b>
Leasing Consultant Floater Responsibilities, include, but, not limited to the below:</b></p>
<p><b><i>
A. Leasing</i></b></p>
<p>
1. Maintains a professional, and friendly, atmosphere in the leasing office and other areas in and around our apartment communities.</p>
<p>
2. Learn and maintain thorough product knowledge of the properties assigned to and its market competition.</p>
<p>
3. Assists the supervising property manager/assistant property manager/ team member in implementing effective marketing strategies to increase property traffic and internet leads.</p>
<p>
4. Convert phone calls to tours, property tours to leases and internet leads to tours and/or leases.</p>
<p>
5. Ensure guest cards are completed, entered into ResMan, and follow up is completed for prospects.</p>
<p>
6. Ensure leasing office, tour path, vacant apartments, amenities, and general curb appeal of the apartment community is pleasing to all 5 senses and impeccably clean. If necessary, spot clean to maintain fresh and clean appearance.</p>
<p>
7. Assist with various aspects of the Company's marketing plan for both assigned site, and the Stellar Equity portfolio, as needed.</p>
<p>
8. If assigned to our our LITCH community, assist with performing initial certifications. Assist with preparing new leases and securing tenant signatures. Assist with preparing re-certifications lease renewal, interim adjustments, and other documents. Assist in performing beginning inspections as to condition prior to leasing and report to ensure that units are being maintained according to HUD required housing quality standards.</p>
<p><b><i>
B. Resident Relations</i></b></p>
<p>
1. Develop and maintain stellar customer service relationships with prospects and residents.</p>
<p>
2. Assist with managing and help to resolve resident concerns. Engage the Property Manager, where necessary.</p>
<p>
3. Assist with monitoring work orders completion and communicate with maintenance team to insure timely completion of work orders. The goal is a 24-hour completion timeframe.</p>
<p>
4. Assist in planning, organizing, and conducting resident functions as needed and as directly by Property Manager.</p>
<p><b><i>
C. Administrative and Maintenance</i></b></p>
<p>
1. Do general clerical work including, but not limited to, answering phones promptly and courteously, taking messages and assuring follow-up, checking and responding to emails, recording all traffic in ResMan, managing work orders in ResMan, filing, receiving and assisting in coding vendor invoices, and assisting in ordering office and/or property supplies.</p>
<p>
2. Assist in preparing and delivering late notices.</p>
<p>
3. Assist with collection of rents in the absence of property manager or assistant manager.</p>
<p><b>
The Essentials:</b></p>
<p>
1. High School diploma or G.E.D equivalent preferred.</p>
<p>
2. Bilingual in English and Spanish required.</p>
<p>
3. National Apartment Leasing Professional (NALP) preferred.</p>
<p>
4. Position requires a minimum of 6 months experience in sales OR service industry.</p>
<p>
5. Proficient in reading, writing, grammar, and mathematical skills required.</p>
<p>
6. Must possess, and be able to use and apply common sense understanding and good judgement on a daily basis.</p>
<p>
7. Must possess a positive attitude and the ability to smile under all circumstances. Must be able to handle stressful, urgent, diverse situations in a calm and reasonable manner.</p>
<p>
8. Understand and adhere to all Fair Housing laws. Strive to achieve Fair Housing Certifications.</p>
<p>
9. Competence in personal computer skills, keyboard, internet search, math, Microsoft Office Suite as well as software applications, such as ResMan, BlueMoon, etc.</p>
<p>
10. Must be able to bend, stoop, sit, stand, walk, climb stairs, reach, lift and carry items weighing 40 pounds or less.</p>
<p>
11. Participate in training in order to comply with new or existing laws.</p>
<p>
12. Be able to work evenings and weekends as designated by a supervisor and determined by the needs of the property. Additional hours may be required due to business necessity.</p>
<p>
13. Present self in a neat, clean, and professional manner at all times throughout the workday and/or whenever present at the community or representing the company.</p>
<p>
14. Maintain reliable transportation daily. This position requires travel to all properties in the Stellar Equity Portfolio, as needs dictate. Start times may vary at each assigned site.</p>
<p>
15. Successfully pass a drug test.</p>
<p>
16. Comply with Company expectations.</p>
<p>
17. Accurately record daily work schedule as well as assigned work locations.</p>
