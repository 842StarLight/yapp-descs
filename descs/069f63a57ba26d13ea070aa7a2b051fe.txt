<div><b>
Description</b></div>
<div>
<div>
<p>The Emerson (A&amp;C) Customer Care team provides services to Emerson (A&amp;C) that enable them to attract and serve customers faster, with better quality. This includes a variety of responsibilities, including but not limited to quoting, order entry, resolving customer inquiries/issues, etc.</p>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>Essential Responsibilities</b></div>
<div></div>
<div><ul>
<li>
Accurately input orders from internal or external customers into SalesForce and/or other systems platforms</li>
<li>
Effectively retrieve, open, validate customer purchase orders prior to inputting in the SalesForce platform</li>
<li>
Perform timely requests to include processing returning material authorizations, credit and debit memo’s, address corrections and other customer inquires</li>
<li>
Support software fulfillment to ensure customer receipt of software via licensing platforms</li>
<li>
Support hardware fulfillment as it relates to communicating with customer the expected shipping dates and expedited order solutions</li>
<li>
Perform customer set up activities as needed to ensure accurate processing orders and delivery of goods and services</li>
<li>
Perform customer related financial transactions such as credit and debit memo’s per operating policies and procedures</li>
<li>
Respond to inquiries from various businesses in a timely manner in a solution orientated mindset &amp; manner</li>
<li>
Ability to resolve multiple customer facing inquiries from various mediums such as telephones, email and chat</li>
<li>
Able to perform in fast paced customer centric environment</li>
<li>
Navigate effectively within multiple tools, receivable systems and internal processes to deliver accurate and timely deliverables (SAP, Oracle, Salesforce, etc.)</li>
<li>
Other tasks assigned by Manager or applicable leadership</li>
</ul></div>
</div>
