<h2>Customer Experience Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Job Description:</b><br>
<p><b>Position Summary</b>:
</p>
<p>The Customer Experience Specialist (CES) serves as the first point of contact for customer inquiries via phone, email, and in person. The CES supports the daily operations of the Resource Center/shops.
</p>
<p>The incumbent is responsible for providing the highest level of customer service to all members/customers, both external and internal.
</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Answers incoming calls/requests; engages in problem-solving and provides solutions; manages and responds appropriately to all inquiries including but not limited to email, phone and walk-in customers.</li>
<li>
Maintains contact center database by entering information accurately and quickly.</li>
<li>
Use of point-of-sale systems in processing cash register sales and monies to support all council functions.</li>
<li>
Assist customers in their selection and purchase of merchandise, including phone and e-mail orders.</li>
<li>
Coordinates some aspects of membership and program registration.</li>
<li>
Maintains a customer-centric environment in public areas of council facilities.</li>
<li>
Ensures and provides quality service to both internal and external customers.</li>
<li>
Answers inquiries by clarifying desired information, researching, locating and delivering findings.</li>
<li>
Provides back-up administrative support across all departments as needed.</li>
<li>
Tracking inventory and receipt of merchandise to ensure that appropriate stock levels are maintained.</li>
<li>
Preparing reports that support the resource center functions and other council departments.</li>
<li>
Enhances organization reputation by providing a positive customer experience for all those contacted, either in person or by phone.</li>
</ul>

Position Requirements</b>:
</p>

</p></li>
<li><p>Strong phone contact handling skills and active listening.
</p></li>
<li><p>Customer-service oriented with ability to communicate in a friendly and professional manner with a wide range of people. Unequivocal commitment to pluralism.
</p></li>
<li><p>Proven customer support experience.
</p></li>
<li><p>Exercise discretion when handling potentially confidential or sensitive matters.
</p></li>

</p></li>
<li><p>Accurate, organized and detail-oriented, with the ability to work under pressure and shift priorities in response to changing needs.
</p></li>

</p></li>

</p></li>

</p></li>

</p></li>
