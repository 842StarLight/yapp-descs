<h2>Marketing & Events Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>The Marketing &amp; Events Coordinator organizes all aspects of internal and external meeting/event planning. The Coordinator assists with negotiations for space contracts, books event space, arranges food and beverage, orders supplies, equipment and signage, and ensures appropriate d√©cor to meet quality and brand expectations. This role is also responsible for ensuring digital event assets are accurate and functioning, producing/moderating webinars, and tracking registrants and attendees.</p>
<p>Non-event activities include promotional item procurement/inventory tracking, budget tracking, mailing fulfillment, proofreading, social media postings and other administrative duties.</p>
<p><b>Essential Functions</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage assigned projects (local and national) in the area of meeting/event planning for a variety of meetings, webinars, conferences, tradeshows and workshops</li>
<li>Track budgets to ensure expenses are kept within established boundaries and reconcile purchase orders/invoices</li>
<li>Ensure all logistical details, including room set-up, audio/visual, transportation, lodging, food and beverage, invitations, fulfillment, etc., for meetings/events are planned effectively</li>
<li>Participate in contract negotiations and vendor management</li>
<li>Monitor timelines for multiple events</li>
<li>Generate event reports, perform event-related follow-up and communicate with internal teams</li>
<li>Procure/track promotional items, performing routine inventory</li>
<li>Work effectively as part of a multi-disciplinary Marketing team</li>
<li>Ensure compliance and accuracy with the existing corporate communications strategy for uniformity in brand, message, templates, tag lines, and brand usage in all print, online and social media messages</li>
<li>Maintain and strengthen the brand through communication support and on-line presence</li>
<li>Carry out other marketing administrative duties, as needed</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Competencies and Qualifications</b></p>
<p>An ideal candidate will have:</p>
<ul>
<li>BS/BA degree in Marketing, Business Administration or Communications</li>
<li>5+ years of experience in planning and working directly with clients on events at least $10K and above</li>
<li>Working knowledge of event design and event planning concepts</li>
<li>Very strong writing/proofreading skills</li>
<li>Strong aptitude in marketing automation software (eg. Eloqua, Pardot, Marketo, or Hubspot), CRM, and web conferencing software tools (e.g. On24, WebEx, or GoToMeeting)</li>
<li>Proficient in the use of the most current versions of the industry software on a PC platform: Word, Excel, PowerPoint and Publisher</li>
<li>A keen eye for detail with a solid understanding of design and brand standards</li>
<li>Creative and strategic thinker</li>
<li>Entrepreneurial spirit, track record of initiative and ownership of work</li>
<li>Ability to handle and prioritize multiple projects, meet tight deadlines and remain resilient under pressure</li>
<li>Ability to fully develop ideas</li>
<li>Strong verbal communication and presentation skills
</li>
<li>Ability to work effectively in a team environment</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Conditions of Work:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Flexible work hours are necessary during event seasons and when facing deadlines</li>
<li>Occasional travel to regional offices and events</li>
<li>Ability to lift/move up to 30 lbs.</li>
</ul>
