<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Technical Accounting Manager</b></h2>
<p>
Location: McLean, VA</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Purpose and Principle Accountabilities of Role</b></h2>
<p>
This position reports to the Director of Accounting and works with an existing team of four Technical Accounting managers.</p>
<p>
This position is responsible for:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Technical accounting – This role will support the Corporate Financial Controller’s organization in evaluating non-routine transactions and researching, documenting and communicating accounting implications to the business for both external reporting and management accounting. This role will interact with Business Unit associates, corporate finance associates, consultants and external auditors.</li>
<li>
Investment accounting – This role will evaluate and record certain investments in non-core business activities for determination of accounting e.g. cost method investment, equity method investment, VIE and/or control consolidation as well as on-going impairment analysis.</li>
<li>
Accounting policy – This role will support the Corporate Financial Controller’s organization in evaluating Business Unit inquiries on accounting for transactions (routine and non- routine) and drafting the detailed updates of internal accounting policies in the Finance Manual including updating, adapting, and improving these policies to meet external and internal requirements.</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>
Special projects – This role will participate in the team’s technical accounting evaluation of Corporate initiated non-routine transactions (e.g., financing transactions, acquisitions, dispositions, leases).</li></ul>
<ul><li>
External reporting – This role will provide a supportive role during the year-end and quarter-end close to the External Reporting team to assist in the objective of an efficient and shorter close cycle.</li></ul>
