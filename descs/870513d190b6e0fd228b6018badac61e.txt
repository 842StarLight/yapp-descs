<h2>Talent Acquisition Sourcer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>As a Talent Acquisition Sourcer, you will partner with all members of our Talent Acquisition &amp; Business Partner teams to build candidate pipelines for positions across all business units nationwide. You will develop a deep understanding of QTS, our culture, and the desired qualifications in developing comprehensive sourcing strategies. This position plays a critical role in ensuring QTS has a high-quality talent pipeline available at all times.</p>
<p>
In addition to pipeline generation, you will gather, maintain and share market intelligence and knowledge of competitor companies in each QTS region.
</p>
<p>Willingness to think outside the box to help QTS find and attract top talent will lead to great success!
</p>
<p>This is opportunity focuses on results including lead generation and quality hires. Work location is open to any of our major markets (Atlanta/Suwanee, GA; Ashburn, VA; Overland Park, KS).</p>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES, </b>other duties may be assigned.
</p>
<ul>
<li>Source for high priority positions across the business to include Facilities, Finance, Sales, Corporate, and Technical Operations positions</li>
<li>
Use sourcing tools and techniques to identify top talent through social media, job boards, networking, referrals, and Boolean searches, including candidate contact information</li>
<li>
Lead Recruitment Marketing initiatives including lead generation, social media, paid search, and other creative strategies to identify and connect with passive candidates and leads</li>
<li>
Gain a strong understanding of hiring profiles across the business as well as urgent hiring needs</li>
<li>
Measure and track pipeline activity and share results</li>
<li>
Support recruiting agency program and other programs as assigned</li>
<li>
Serve as recruitment technology administrator for specialized recruiting tools</li>
<li>
Workday system testing as required to support recruiting module enhancements, break/fix etc.</li>
<li>
Craft custom and meaningful outreach to encourage engagement when needed</li>
</ul>
