<h2>Director of Sales and Marketing - Functional IT</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div>
<p><b>POSITION PROFILE</b></p>
<p>
This is the highest leadership role for the assigned functional/technical area in the IT organization which participates in strategic decisions regarding assigned functional/technical area. The Director implements and manages initiatives within assigned area with complete autonomy and accountability for results. This role reports to the VP, Enterprise Solutions.</p>
<p><b>
JOB DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>To direct all activities of a department that delivers best in class solutions to business problems through the use of information technology.</li>
<li>To align and calibrate organization's technology deployment strategy with its business strategy, including overseeing technology purchases, negotiating contracts and services.</li>
<li>To partner cross functionally within IT and/or external business function leaders in their efforts to deliver value to the organization.</li>
<li>To be the strategic leader with focus on continuous improvement by increasing efficiency, reducing costs or enhancing revenue for the organization.</li>
<li>To manage customer (internal/external) relationship and be the single point of contact within the supported function.</li>
<li>Manage department budget and project finances.</li>
<li>Manage complex projects/initiatives involving significant cross functional coordination.</li>
<li>Establish and manage a team that is responsible for delivering projects, achieving departmental metrics and day to day operations.</li>
<li>Establish and build strategic alliance with business unit leadership, partner with them to develop long term/short term strategies to meet business objectives.</li>
<li>Recommend new approaches, policies and procedures to effect continual improvements in the efficiency of the department and services performed.</li>
<li>Maintain compliance with audit requirements, SOX and operational controls.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS (Education, Experience, and Certifications)</b></p>
<p>
Typically Required:</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelors or Masters Degree in information technology or related field.</li>
<li>Min of 10 years of progressive experience managing Information technology for a large company in a cross functional environment.</li>
<li>Requires min of 5 years of experience and significant depth of knowledge in the business/technology function assigned.</li>
<li>Requires project management experience specifically managing large scale IT projects from inception to delivery.</li>
</ul>
