<h2>Director, Lighting - US</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><i>Founded over 40 years ago, Solotech is a world leader in audiovisual and entertainment technology with 13 strategic locations in Canada, the United States and Europe. Thanks to an inventory from over 300 renowned manufacturers, Solotech is the one-stop-shop for audio, video, lighting, rigging, soft goods, control and collaboration solutions &amp; services. The company is internationally recognized for its expertise in Live Productions and Systems Integration in various markets, namely music, sports, business, culture and education. Solotech employs 1,200 professionals in its offices located in Montreal, Quebec City, Saguenay, Ottawa, Toronto, Las Vegas, Nashville, DeKalb, Los Angeles, Heywood, London and Redditch.</i></p>
<p><i>To apply: https://carrieres.solotech.com/default.aspx?REQ_ID=00706&amp;Language=1</i></p>
<p><b><i>*Please note that the exact location of this position in the United States is to be determined*</i></b></p>
<p>Reporting directly to the President of Live Productions, this position will act as the senior lighting Project Manager for all US shows.</p>
<p><b>Main Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage all aspects of a lighting project from the design to the project delivery whether working on it directly or assigning it to a lighting advisor;</li>
<li>Meet with potential clients in order to assist them during the technical assessment process;</li>
<li>Develop technical solutions for equipment integration into the design</li>
<li>Advise clients on the development of new technology adapted to their needs;</li>
<li>Plan all necessary equipment for the project, and, if required, advise on purchase or the development requirements of customized equipment;</li>
<li>Collaborate with the R&amp;D team for any technology development;</li>
<li>Work jointly with all internal departments in order to ensure a high level of customer service, and respect the delivery timelines;</li>
<li>At all times, be sure to meet the expectations of clients in terms of customer service, including pre and post-sale communications. Identify, solve problems and recommend improvements;</li>
<li>Execute the lighting equipment integration during the preparation in cooperation with all technicians; Deliver the project by assisting the production team during the pre-production period, rehearsals, to job completion;</li>
<li>Advise, guide and share expertise with the technicians;</li>
<li>Act as a contact person for the client's Technical Director for questions, comments or changes related to the lighting projects;</li>
<li>Ensure that Solotech's standards are respected (cables, connectors, packaging, etc.) by the shop technicians and by the production teams;</li>
<li>Explore current industry trends and seek new products, software, suppliers and technologies within field of expertise and advise R&amp;D team;</li>
<li>Work jointly with the administration and the department's head to minimize subrental expenditures (special maintenance and purchases vs sub-rental);</li>
<li>Participate in all other projects identified by Management.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Academic training: </b> High School diploma or equivalent</p>
<p><b>Experience: </b> Have at least 20 years experiences within the lighting touring industry;</p>
<p>General technical knowledge in the following areas: sound, lighting, rigging; Very good communication skills both oral and written.</p>
<p><b>Required Skills: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to work with MS Office, Visio/ CAD/Drawing Software;</li>
<li>Able to work under pressure and meet deadlines;</li>
<li>Show perseverance, precision, and be detailed-oriented;</li>
<li>Act with diplomacy;</li>
<li>Excellent organizational skills;</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal skills;</li>
<li>Strong customer service orientation;</li>
<li>Ability to work on multiple projects at the same time;</li>
<li>Teamwork oriented and cooperative;</li>
<li>Demonstrate resourcefulness and autonomy.</li>
<li>Available for travel (Passport and international travel may be required).</li>
</ul>
<p><i>We thank all applicants for their interest in Solotech, however, only those selected for an interview will be contacted.</i></p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
