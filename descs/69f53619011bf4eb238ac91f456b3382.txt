<h2>Warehouse and Dock Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>DH Pace Company, Inc. of Milwaukee, is looking to hire a Warehouse and Dock Associate. You will have excellent organizational and communication skills and will be responsible for all aspects of warehouse functions including the receiving, storing, handling, shipping, and preparing of product for customer delivery. You will assure the warehouse is maintained in a safe and orderly fashion. Forklift certification is a plus. If interested apply today or call 800-507-0023 for more information.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities include:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Off-loading materials, verifying counts to purchase orders, and checking shipments for damage.</li>
<li>Sorting stock materials from job materials</li>
<li>Staging materials for installation, shipment, or customer pick up</li>
<li>Participating in physical inventory and cycle counts to maintain an accurate inventory</li>
<li>Utilizing company specific software, field devices, and paperwork to maintain accurate inventory information</li>
<li>Purchasing materials to ensure stock and order requests are placed in a timely manner</li>
<li>Utilizing hand tools, mechanical equipment, and motorized.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Successful candidates will:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Be team oriented with strong interpersonal skills and attention to detail</li>
<li>Have the ability to safely lift 75 pounds</li>
<li>Have 2 or more years warehouse and inventory experience</li>
<li>Familiarity with computers a plus</li>
<br>
</ul>
