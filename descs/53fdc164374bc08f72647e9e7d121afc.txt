<div><b>Position Overview</b></div>
<div>Will interact with business users within the financial and accounting areas and implement Oracle PBCS (Oracle Planning and Budgeting Cloud Service) applications based on Oracle Hyperion Planning that supports enterprise-wide planning, budgeting, and forecasting in a cloud-based deployment model. PBCS provides a rich Web and Microsoft Office enabled planning and modeling framework that supports driver-based planning to help connect operational assumptions to financial outcomes. The application supports a hierarchical planning process that encompasses both corporate finance and the lines of business within an enterprise. With PBCS, organizations can meet immediate financial planning, budgeting and forecasting needs while enabling a platform for future cross-functional expansion and automated process integration.</div>

<li>Participate in systems analysis and design, includes identifying and documenting the planning process and functional specifications for building planning and budgeting applications
</li>
<li>Consult and advise client on building rules and making technical changes to applications in the planning processes.</li>
</ul>
