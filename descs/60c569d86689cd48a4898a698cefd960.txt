Reports To
<br>----------
<br><br>
Sr. Director National Accounts
<br><br>
Candidate will be responsible for building and expanding key relationships with ChargePoint's most strategic accounts. This includes the penetration of existing National accounts, as well as the development of Fortune 500 targets. You will collaborate with &amp; support regional managers to increase sales and profitability. The successful candidate will effectively communicate ChargePoint's value proposition, strategies, services, and products to customers while selling ChargePoint's full portfolio of offerings. As a National Account Executive, you should be data centric, organized and possess the ability to develop the strategy &amp; execution that elevates your engagement into executive management at these accounts.
<br><br>
Job Specifications;
<br><ul>
<li>Meet and exceed individual revenue quota for the defined National Account's portfolio</li>
<li>Develop account portfolio strategy, including channel leverage and internal resource requirements, for each account</li>
<li>Qualify and develop new business opportunities while working with internal sales resources and cross functional support</li>
<li>Proactively positions a positive business outcome to the customer's current business problem</li>
<li>Perform sales presentations and demonstrations to customers and prospects in a manner that creates curiosity and credibility</li>
<li>Travel extensively into territory to develop maintain relationships with key customer contacts, present, articulate/clarify value and differentiation, and win the business in expected timeframe</li>
<li>Partner with field reseller partners and/or service partners to develop sales opportunities &amp; implementation activities that will enable scale of the National Accounts business</li>
<li>Accurately forecast revenue for territory and accounts, develop customer org charts, document business history and opportunity details in salesforce.com continuously</li>
<li>Develop a strong technical baseline through training and development milestones</li>
</ul>
Requirements;
<br><ul>
<li>7+ years documented success in enterprise software and/or business technology products and services sales</li>
<li>Demonstrated knowledge of EVSE industry dynamics and nomenclature is preferred</li>
<li>Demonstrated selling experience into Executive Management decision makers</li>
<li>Experience creating detailed sales strategy and market approach with forethought &amp; planning</li>
<li>Exemplary written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal, planning, and organizational skills</li>
<li>Demonstrated experience with data management and analysis of sales pipeline</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree required, Master's degree is a plus</li>
</ul>
