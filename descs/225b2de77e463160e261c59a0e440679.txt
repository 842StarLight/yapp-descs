<h2>Retail Development Internship</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Title: Retail Development Intern</p>
<p>
Location: Dallas, TX</p>
<p>
Reports to: Director of Retail Development</p>
<p>
Job Purpose:</p>
<p>
The Retail Development intern will be responsible for supporting the day-to-day activities of the team while gaining valuable experience and skills in the influencer marketing space. This position requires not only dedication to detail and world-class follow through, but also a personality that is up to the task of shouldering considerable responsibilities that may change day to day. This individual must ensure that information is presented accurately, appropriately, and that reporting requirements and deadlines are met in a timely fashion. This position requires an individual who can work well under pressure and is flexible and easily able to multi-task and re-prioritize when necessary. As there is a chance to get some client interaction, strong communication skills and a composed, professional demeanor are required. There is significant opportunity for growth and potential to secure a full-time position.</p>
<p>
Examples of Potential Projects/Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Assist teams with campaign planning, execution, and tracking</li>
<li>Become familiar with the influencer space in order to help cast campaigns</li>
<li>Create campaign recap documents to share with key clients</li>
<li>Collaborate with team members on new ideas, directions, and tools for marketing and communications</li>
<li>Reliably produce zero-defect work</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Support reporting needs through data entry and utilizing/manipulating numbers and data to derive insights</li>
<li>Answer customer service emails with professionalism, timeliness, and solution oriented responses</li>
</ul>
<p>Requirements:</p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum one year of experience in sales or related field</li>
<li>Strong communication skills, particularly in professional email and telephone communication</li>
<li>Superior organizational skills and ability to multitask and manage workload between multiple teams</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Strong problem solving skills</li>
<li>Team player attitude and proven ability to work with team members across business functions</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Willingness to take on all sorts of tasks, including less glamorous ones</li>
<li>General interest in social media, sales, and fashion</li>
</ul>
<p>Technical / Educational Requirements:</p>
<ul>
<li>College graduate</li>
<li>Proficiency with general office software (Excel, Keynote, Google Docs)</li>
</ul>
