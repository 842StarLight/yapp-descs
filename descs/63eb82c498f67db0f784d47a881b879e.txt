<h2>Influencer Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>*<br>Equilibria is a high-growth, omni-channel premium CBD platform for women. We are dedicated to restoring balance to our community – physically and mentally – through our high quality CBD products and personalized dosage &amp; delivery support. We are growing our team with proven, passionate, positive leaders and look forward to hearing from qualified candidates. Corporate Headquarters: River West, Chicago; Bioscience and Farm Operations: Longmont, CO.</p>
<p>Role Summary</p>
<p>Equilibria is seeking a best-in-class Influencer Marketer, a savvy digital growth hacker with demonstrated ability to leverage this explosive channel across social mediums, predominantly Instagram. As a premium CBD brand for women, Equilibria’s growth and market launches are driven by collaborative partnerships with influencers (micro, macro &amp; mega) as well as female-focused brands and wellness leaders. Each of these relationships requires thoughtful nurture and flawless execution and follow up. Successful candidates will have 2-4 of experience of Influencer &amp; Social Media management for a consumer-facing brand, either in-house or within an agency. This full-time role will report directly to Equilibria’s Head of Business Development.</p>
<p>Role Specifics:</p>
<ul><li>Manage and cultivate relationships from Micro to Macro level influencers across wellness, beauty, lifestyle, and fitness categories.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Design and execute influencer, press and celebrity mailings and seeded boxes nationwide</li></ul>
<ul><li>Help allocate growing monthly budgets to increase brand awareness, website sales, and solidify brand identity across various social media platforms.Help allocated growing monthly budget</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manage contract process for each relationship, including budget and timeline specifics.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Distribute merchandising and marketing materials – digital and physical – to partners.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Re-engage &amp; nurture Influencer network with compelling ongoing merchandising, content, and new product launch Campaigns throughout the year</li></ul>
<ul><li>Educate select Influencers on Equilibria revenue share opportunities. Create and manage unique codes for converted Influencers</li></ul>
<ul><li>Track health of partnerships based on new member conversions, and overall commissions.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Following up with partners to maximize their monthly efforts and commissions</li></ul>
<p>Qualifications</p>
