<h2>Member Service Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Core Responsibilities</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Represent the Credit Union to the members in a courteous and professional manner and provide prompt, efficient and accurate service;</li>
<li>Develop and maintain an understanding of the Credit Union philosophy, organization, bylaws and operational procedures;</li>
<li>Provide general and specific service-related information concerning Credit Union products, services or policies, in person and/or by telephone;</li>
<li>Respond to members’ requests, problems, and complaints, and/or direct members to the proper person/department for specific information and assistance;</li>
<li>Responsible for appropriate handling of member mail and incoming documentation as well as other incoming mail;</li>
<li>Develop good relationships with PSFCU vendors;</li>
<li>Attend, represent and actively participate in PSFCU community events as well as actively participating in branch sponsored events;</li>
<li>As per PSFCU Policy and Procedures perform routine transactions, including but not limited to:</li>
<li>opening / closing accounts and shares;</li>
<li>accepting wire transfer requests-domestic and international;</li>
<li>performing change of address, issuing temporary checks;</li>
<li>performing Harland Clarke share drafts orders;</li>
<li>processing ATM card and Debit card requests;</li>
<li>handling and processing IRA accounts;</li>
<li>handling and processing Share Certificates of Deposits;</li>
<li>handling and processing Club accounts;</li>
<li>direct deposit of Social Security and payroll checks to members accounts;</li>
<li>processing lost passbook requests;</li>
<li>performing Chex Systems checks;</li>
<li>set up and provide access to On-Line Banking, Mobile Banking</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Service existing accounts; set up new account files and provide members with all necessary information and assistance;</li>
<li>Provide members with information regarding consumer loans, business loans, and VISA cards and appropriately align the right loan product for the member’s financing need;</li>
<li>Assist members in the completion of the loan application (real estate loans excluded) and all associated forms;</li>
<li>Accept and process loan applications of all types(real estate loans excluded); review documentation for accuracy; and request all necessary documentation required to complete the loan application;</li>
<li>Notify members by phone or mail that their checkbooks are ready for pickup at the branch;</li>
<li>Conduct orientation interviews on Credit Union services and programs with each new Credit Union member;</li>
<li>Effectively offer Credit Union products and services through the practice of established sales and service techniques;</li>
<li>Post dividends prior to the end of the month for all closed member accounts;</li>
<li>Perform all functions of Teller when business needs dictate;</li>
<li>Maintain extensive knowledge of all products and services;</li>
<li>Monitor changes in interest rates and effectively communicate pricing to members</li>
<li>Maintain superior knowledge and full understanding of consumer loan products;</li>
<li>Act as a Safe Deposit Box Attendant, responsible for handling box rentals, providing access to the vault area and related functions;</li>
<li>Perform other duties or responsibilities as assigned.</li>
<li>Effectively cross-sell PSFCU products and services in order to achieve Branch Goals as per the Branch Goals Incentive Plan.</li>
<li>Travel to and cover at other branches if necessary and needed.</li>
<li>And other functions as needed and delegated by the Branch Manager.</li>
<li>Perform Teller function as needed.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>1 - 2 years of work experience;</li>
<li>1 year of experience as a Teller;</li>
<li>Basic computer skills and knowledge of computer software programs;</li>
<li>Ability to cross sell products;</li>
<li>Fluency in spoken and written Polish and English;</li>
<li>High School Diploma.</li>
</ul>
