<b>THE ROLE</b></p>
<p>
The Payroll Specialist will assist with day-to-day payroll operations and the accurate and timely processing for our multi-state weekly and biweekly payrolls. This position reports to the Director of Payroll.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL </b><b>FUNCTIONS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Data processing in Ultipro payroll system</li>
<li>
Review and process employee new hire/rehire information and terminations.</li>
<li>
Garnishment compliance and calculations</li>
<li>
401k setup and processing</li>
<li>
Payroll and tax reconciliation</li>
<li>
Daily communication with our various locations regarding payroll matters</li>
<li>
Create and run ad hoc Business Intelligence reports</li>
<li>
Multiple worksite and labor statistic reporting</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
QUALIFICATIONS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Must have solid understanding of payroll processes and procedures</li>
<li>
Must have proficiency in Microsoft Excel</li>
<li>
General knowledge of basic accounting practices</li>
<li>
Excellent Customer Service and communication skills</li>
<li>
Able to maintain a high level of confidentiality</li>
<li>
Must be able to work in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>
Ability to work effectively in a team environment</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Experience with Ultimate Software (Ultipro) Systems including IBM Cognos Reporting preferred</li>
<li>Knowledge of Canadian payroll a plus</li>
</ul>
