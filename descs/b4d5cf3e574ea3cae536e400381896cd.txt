<p>
Growth and innovation are imperatives for all financial services firms, but relentless compliance, operational and risk pressures can hinder progress. <p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Guided by brand and NTT DATA overall sales strategies pursue new sales opportunities within Banking and Financial Services industry domains</li>
<li>
Learn the NTT DATA portfolio of services (Applications, Business Process Outsourcing, Digital, Infrastructure, Cloud &amp; Security) via in classroom instruction and in partnering with senior Sales and Client Executives</li>
<li>
Assess potential sales opportunities and in conjunction with NTT Data Solution Architects so to develop solutions proposals that deliver value-add impacts across customer’s enterprise</li>
<li>
Interface and influence customer stakeholders at the Director level and above</li>
<li>
Develop a pipeline of new business contacts, opportunities and conduct regular progress reviews with sales leadership</li>
<li>
Work independently in accordance to Sales Plan focused on driving results</li>
</ul>

<ul>

3 - 6 years technology or solutions sales experience</li>
<li>
Experience working in formal sales structured organization measured against quota and/or target attainment goals</li>
<li>
Fluency in reading, writing, and speaking the English language</li>
<li>
Experience presenting to small or large audiences</li>
<li>
Proficiency with Microsoft Office products (Word, Excel, PowerPoint)</li>
