About Mars, Incorporated</b></p>
<p>
Mars is a family-owned business with more than a century of history making diverse products and offering services for people and the pets people love. Mars also provides veterinary health services that include BANFIELD® Pet Hospitals, Blue Pearl®, VCA® and Pet Partners™. Headquartered in McLean, VA, Mars operates in more than 80 countries. The Mars Five Principles – Quality, Responsibility, Mutuality, Efficiency and Freedom – inspire its more than 100,000 Associates to create value for all its partners and deliver growth they are proud of every day.</p>

<div><div><b>
Job Purpose/Overview</b></div></div>
<br>
<div><div>
The Employer Reputation &amp; Sourcing Manager role will lead the ER &amp; Sourcing team in building sourcing capabilities within a critical region where talent is a top priority, as well as overseeing implementation of the ER strategy across all recruiting channels. In this role, the ER &amp; Sourcing Manager will ensure Mars’ Employer Value Proposition resonates in the local market and use a cross-channel approach to build awareness and engage with prospective talent in the USA &amp; Canada with the aim of ensuring Mars is seen and selected as a top employer. This role will collaborate closely with Global ER Director and regional Corporate Affairs team to drive employer reputation externally and proactively engage Associates to enable them to become Mars ambassadors. Additionally, this role will be responsible for working closely with the Talent Acquisition Lead and Talent Acquisition full life-cycle recruiting teams to understand forecast demand and establish sourcing strategies for high-volume, hard to fill, or future pipeline needs. Creativity and innovation will be key skills that will enable the ER &amp; Sourcing Manager to successfully drive Mars’ growth ambition by sourcing and attracting top talent for the business.</div></div>
