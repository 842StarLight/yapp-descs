<p><b>Purpose and Objective:</b>
</p>
<p>SAP America Inc. seeks a Business Processes Senior Consultant for our Reston, VA office location and various client sites nationwide to provide application and process specific solution consulting (for a specific application and/or process area: creation of business blueprint, Implementation based on a blue print, creation of test cases, test scheduling and execution, key user training, go live support, and post go live support).</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Expectations and Tasks:</b>
</p>
<p>Support in escalated projects. Performance of feasibility studies/ solution reviews. Support of pre-sales activities. Taking over responsibility from time to time as a sub project team leader. Takes responsibility from time to time as team lead and supports respective line manager(s) in people development tasks. Detailed planning of process implementation. Define detailed blueprint for development requirements. Industry /corporate process implementation across all related applications (SAP/non-SAP, OnPremise and cloud-based). Develop and Assure quality of process models. Demonstrating profound knowledge of modeling standards and tools. Drive deployment of SAP Model Companies for specific area of business. 75% travel required to various client sites nationwide.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Education and Qualifications/Skills and Competencies:</b>
</p>
<p>Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering, Business Administration or related field and 6 years of experience required. Company will also accept Master's and 4 years of experience. Company will also accept a three-year degree or combination of degrees as meeting the Bachelor's degree requirement.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Work Experience:</b>
</p>
<p>Experience must include 4 years of experience involving the following: implementing solutions across complex landscapes including Cloud and OnPremise environments; SAP Cloud Technology and SAP integration technology including C/4HANA SAP Sales Cloud, C/4HANA Service Cloud, C/4HANA Service Center, SF Employee Central, SAP HANA, SAP Cloud Platform Integration and SAP Netweaver PI; end to end CRM (customer relationship management) processes; network connectivity, SSL connections and reverse proxy solutions; and SAP Activate Project Methodology. 75% travel required to various client sites nationwide.</p>
