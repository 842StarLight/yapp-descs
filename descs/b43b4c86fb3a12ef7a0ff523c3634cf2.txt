<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Performs non-surgical aesthetic treatments in accordance with established treatment protocols, scope of practice, organizational policies and procedures, and all other governing standards.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Essential functions include, but are not limited to, the following:</div>
<div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Administers treatments in accordance with Ideal Image policies and procedures.</li>
<li>Establish and maintain rapport with clients in order to ensure overall customer service satisfaction of clients.</li>
<li>Evaluate client’s suitability for all available services/treatments and perform treatments that same day.</li>
<li>Pursue opportunities to educate clients on additional services.</li>
<li>Educate clients on all treatment processes including pre and post-treatment care guidelines.</li>
<li>Perform maintenance checks on lasers to ensure systems are operating appropriately.</li>
<li>Maintain cleanliness and appearance of treatment rooms between each client.</li>
<li>Annotate and post appropriate information and maintain accurate client treatment records/data in the client’s medical chart.</li>
<li>Sustain proficiency through continuous education and trainings.</li>
<li>Collaborate with sales and ops teams to achieve company goals.</li>
<li>Travel between clinics, as necessary.</li>
<li>All other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
</div>
