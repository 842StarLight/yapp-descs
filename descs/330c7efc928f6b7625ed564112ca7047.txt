<h2>Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>MDC Interior Solutions is a leading wallcoverings and design solutions company headquartered in Glendale Heights, IL. One of the world’s largest sources for commercial wall designs and coatings, MDC was founded nearly 50 years ago. Today, a proven leader in commercial design, the company provides a broad array of products suiting a myriad of aesthetic preferences and functional needs.</p>
<p>MDC’s portfolio includes: eco-friendly products, digital imaging, acoustical solutions, architectural products, paint/coatings, hand-crafted wall designs, dry-erase coatings, decorative wall panels, luxury textiles, foils, grasses, woods and vinyl.</p>
<p>Known for its ability to produce solutions that meet the needs of the design community, MDC has earned a reputation for innovative products, value and service. New materials and technologies, along with its knowledgeable sales team, strengthen MDC’s industry leadership. MDC prides itself on being a committed single source, total solution provider.</p>
<p>MDC has more than 85 outside sales representatives, as well as additional sales support, reporting to four regional offices. The company’s sales efforts extend further through a worldwide distribution network. A staff of approximately 90 associates are located in MDC’s corporate headquarters.</p>
<p><b>Account Executive- Minnesota- Minneapolis, St. Paul, Rochester</b></p>
<p><b>Summary: </b> Sell MDC products to A&amp;D businesses, industrial establishments or individuals at sales office, store, showroom, or customer's place of business in a <b>well- established territory </b>by performing the following duties.</p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Service established MDC customers assigned to the territory</li>
<li>Develop and maintain relationships with all accounts</li>
<li>Manage the entire sales cycle from prospecting, presentations, quoting, and negotiations.</li>
<li>Travels throughout assigned territory to call on regular and prospective customers to solicit orders.</li>
<li>Displays or demonstrate product, using samples, keynote, catalog, and emphasizes salable features.</li>
<li>Prepares reports of business transactions and keeps expense accounts.</li>
<li>Investigates and resolves customer issues.</li>
<li>Attends trade shows.</li>
<li>10% travel</li>
</ul>
