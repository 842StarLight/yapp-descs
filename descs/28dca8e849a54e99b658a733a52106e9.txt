<h2>Assistant Parts Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Premier Truck Group </b>is headquartered in Dallas, Texas, with 26 locations throughout Texas, Oklahoma, Tennessee, Georgia, Utah, Idaho and Canada, making it the largest Freightliner dealer group. It offers a premium selection of new Freightliner and Western Star commercial vehicles, a large selection of previously owned commercial trucks and a full suite of maintenance and repair services with many of its locations open 24 hours every day, seven days per week. Premier Truck Group also offers a wide selection of retail parts and accessories. When you build your career here, you make a commitment to being the very best in the business. We are the pre-eminent group of Freightliner truck dealerships in the nation with serious plans for the future and we want you to be a part of it. We currently have an opening for our Assistant Parts Manager position.</p>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Directs and schedules the work of all parts department employees</li>
<li>Forecasts goals and objectives for the department including required inventory, pricing, ROI and profit margins</li>
<li>Prepares and administers an annual operating budget for the parts department</li>
<li>Analyzes sales, expenses, and inventory on a daily, weekly and monthly basis to maintain profit goals</li>
<li>Monitors employees daily productivity reports and corresponding payroll records</li>
<li>Maintains a balanced inventory consistent with the requirements of the service department, body shop, wholesale accounts, and the “self-service” retail customer</li>
<li>Establishes pricing parameters in each category that generate sufficient gross profit to produce a satisfactory profit while maintaining customer loyalty.</li>
<li>Implements aggressive marketing and sales promotions plans to increase parts department business for wholesale accounts and “self-service” retail customers</li>
<li>Takes advantage of manufacturers' inventory co-op advertising as part of promotions</li>
<li>Coordinates marketing and sales promotions with appropriate company personnel</li>
<li>Establishes individual parts inventory levels and manages the inventory to achieve maximum turnover in accordance with established goals</li>
<li>Monitors and adjusts inventory to minimize obsolescence of parts in inventory</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Ensures that all parts are tagged so that the dealership recovers credit from the factory</li>
<li>Controls accounts receivables for parts department</li>
<li>Ensures that the same high quality level of service provided to outside customers is also provided to internal dealership customers.</li>
<li>Works with the service manager and body shop manager to ensure a timely turnaround of parts needed for internal jobs</li>
<li>Analyzes departmental operations and storage layout and revises as needed for maximum effectiveness of inventory control, staff efficiency and public display</li>
<li>Directs parts delivery program to wholesale customers</li>
<li>Maintains delivery vehicles for delivery program</li>
<li>Directs shipping and receiving efforts to ensure timely processing of parts being received and parts being delivered</li>
<li>Establishes and maintains a program to ensure customer satisfaction and loyalty</li>
<li>Promptly follows up on customer concerns and complaints. Uses empathy, technical expertise and understanding of company policies to resolve complaints and ensure customer satisfaction and loyalty</li>
<li>Establishes and maintains good working relationships with other service drive departments and the sales department</li>
<li>Establishes and maintains good working relationships with wholesale customers to encourage repeat and referral business.</li>
<li>Develops promotions to increase wholesale business.</li>
<li>Follows up on parts department orders to ensure parts availability in inventory and ensures availably of all required parts and supplies to complete repairs in a timely manner</li>
<li>Checks quality of completed work by parts department employees to ensure quality customer service, inventory control and documentation.</li>
<li>Monitors paper flow to ensure that all documents are accounted for, filled out completely and legibly, and filed or distributed to the appropriate parties</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Review all accounts payables for department to ensure appropriate payments are made for parts inventory including parts are received and accounted for in accordance with dealership policies and departmental procedures</li>
<li>Understands, keeps abreast of, and complies with federal, state, and local regulations that affect parts operations including but not limited to hazardous waste disposal, emergency response and OSHA Right-to-Know</li>
<li>Ensures that proper safety equipment is available and being used properly by all employees in the parts department</li>
<li>Facilitates and/or conducts technical training and sends employees to appropriate training schools as needed</li>
<li>Keeps abreast of new equipment and tools available and recommends purchases</li>
<li>Ensures that the work areas and customer waiting area are kept clean</li>
<li>Follows all attendance and punctuality standards and adheres to timekeeping standards</li>
<li>Follows the Company Code of Business Ethics and Conduct</li>
<li>Understands and follows all work rules and follows lawful directions from Supervisors</li>
<li>Upholds the company’s non-disclosure and confidentiality policies and agreements</li>
<li>Maintains a professional appearance for self and subordinates in accordance with company policy</li>
<li>Attends pertinent training on request</li>
<li>Attends company meetings as required</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>

Valid Driver’s License</p>
<p>
Associate's degree (A. A.) or equivalent; and five to seven year related experience and/or training; or equivalent combination of education and experience. Minimum of one year’s experience working in the service drive or at an auto parts retailer or wholesaler.</p>
