<div>
<div>Grant Thornton is collaborative, entrepreneurial and on the move. As part of a dynamic global organization of 47,000 people serving clients in more than 140 countries, we have the agility and focus it takes to be a leader.<br>
<br>
State and Local Tax – Indirect Tax Senior Manager</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>Position Summary:<br>
</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><div>Grant Thornton’s responsive, partner-led Tax service teams help dynamic companies navigate complexity and grow with insightful, practical tax advice. In addition to tax professionals across the United States, our Washington National Tax Office provides deep technical knowledge and responsive support in local, state, federal and international tax. Our tax services include: compliance, human capital services, federal tax services, international tax services, state and local tax services, private wealth services, tax reporting and advisory, partnership taxation, not-for-profit tax, and mergers &amp; acquisition tax services.</div></div>
<div>
<br>
The Tax Senior Manager in the State &amp; Local Tax – Sales &amp; Use Tax group is responsible for the delivery of a full range of tax services for multiple clients in a wide variety of industries. Responsibilities include interacting closely with clients to provide innovative tax planning, consulting, and compliance expertise; developing and managing staff; working closely with partners and staff on client management, practice development and business development activities; and formulating and delivering innovative tax planning and tax savings strategies.<br>
</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</div>
<ul>
<li><div>Oversee the efforts of multiple client services teams on client engagements; plan, execute, direct, and complete tax projects in a wide variety of industries; and market, sell, design, and implement innovative tax-planning strategies for clients; and manage to budget.
</div></li>
<li><div>Manage, develop, train, and mentor staff on tax projects and assess performance for engagement and year-end reviews.
</div></li>
<li><div>Deliver a full range of State and Local Tax services including tax return preparation and review, tax research, preparation of technical memorandums, drafting client correspondence, and counseling client in defending against state audits.
</div></li>
<li><div>Conduct primary and secondary review of complicated or complex State and Local tax returns.
</div></li>
<li><div>Oversee multi-disciplinary, multi-state reviews that analyze income, franchise, sales and use, payroll, valorem taxes, and others to identify optimal filing options, exemptions, credits and incentives, and corporate structures that can reduce the client's tax burden.
</div></li>
<li><div>Consult, work with, and service client to make recommendations on business and process improvement and serve as a business adviser to client.</div></li>
<li><div>
Other duties as assigned.</div></li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
<b>Qualifications</b><br>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor's degree in Accounting. CPA, Masters in Taxation, LLM in Taxation, or JD.
</li>
<li>A minimum of eight years of progressive State and Local Tax consulting and/or compliance experience in public accounting or a combination of corporate and public accounting experience.
</li>
<li>Broad exposure to State and Local Taxes including knowledge in State Income/Franchise Tax, Credits and Incentives, Sales and Use Tax, with Property Tax a plus; must be eager to work on a variety of SALT issues and not specialize in one particular area.
</li>
<li>Superior analytical, technical, and tax accounting/technology skills with proficiency in US GAAP, tax compliance, corporate consolidated returns, and combined state tax returns.
</li>
<li>Computer expertise including knowledge of tax software and technology.</li>
</ul>
<div>Additional Information:</div>
<ul>
<li>Ability to work additional hours as needed and travel to various client sites.</li>
</ul>
