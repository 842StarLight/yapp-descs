<div>The Scheduling Coordinator is a key contact within the manufacturing organization and is primarily responsible for managing and completing the mill’s weekly department work schedules. The candidate will also be responsible for assisting the Training department, Safety, and the Paper mill with various administrative tasks, i.e. maintaining training records, upkeep of the training matrix, preparing various reports, and scheduling meetings.
</div>
<div><b>Requirements
</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Office software programs, i.e. Word, Excel, PowerPoint and Outlook
</li>
<li>Strong organizational skills
</li>
<li>Solid interpersonal, written, and verbal communication skills
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Scheduling experience is a plus, as is Kronos knowledge.
</li>
<li>Ability to interact with managers, coworkers and hourly personnel.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Responsibilities
</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Complete weekly employee scheduling for paper mill, pulp handling, pulp mill, utilities, and lab.
</li>
<li>Oversee the vacation scheduling process for most departments.
</li>
<li>Schedule employees for mill outage needs.
</li>
<li>Schedule employees for meetings, trainings, etc.
</li>
<li>Schedule training for Confined Space and 1st Responder teams.
</li>
<li>Track training and work with Training &amp; Development Coordinator to ensure schedules align with proper training for new hires and newly promoted
</li>
<li>Maintain call lists, phone lists and seniority lists
</li>
<li>Provide general administrative support to the Training department and the Paper mill.
</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned<br>
</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Competencies
</b></div>
<div>
<b>Core Competencies </b>: action oriented, approachable, conflict management, customer focus, functional/technical skills, integrity and trust, organizational agility, perseverance, priority setting and results oriented.<br>
</div>

Our mission is to expand the role of fiber-based solutions for sustainable everyday life. Our offerings include filter materials, release liners, food and beverage processing materials, decor papers, abrasive and tape backings, electrotechnical paper, glass fiber materials, medical fiber materials and solutions for diagnostics as well as a range of specialty papers for industrial and consumer end-uses.
</i></p>
<p><i>Our annual net sales are about EUR 3 billion and we employ some 8,000 people. The Ahlstrom-Munksjö share is listed on the Nasdaq Helsinki and Stockholm.</i></p>
