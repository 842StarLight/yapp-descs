<h2>Marketing Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>The Marketing Specialist will support the Senior Brand Manager, Clinician Marketing &amp; Sales Support, execute a professional business to business marketing strategy that grows GeneSight ordering and loyalty. Skilled at copy writing and editing and project management, the Marketing Specialist would focus on collateral &amp; print management, sales support and clinician advocacy work.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>MAJOR DUTIES/ RESPONSIBILITIES:</div>
<ul>
<li>
Manage our national and regional convention efforts</li>
<li>
Work with our internal medical team as a part of overall publication efforts</li>
<li>
Oversee collateral management including working our print vendor on logistics, inventory, online supplies portal, artwork, etc.</li>
<li>
Support work on new claims and professional advertising campaigns</li>
<li>
Organize &amp; support clinician advocates as part of overall Professional Advocacy plan</li>
<li>
Help run quarterly speaker trainings, onsite new speaker training</li>
<li>
Handle timelines and proper execution of ideas within fast-paced, dynamic environment</li>
<li>
Manage external agency relationships to maximize effectiveness and cost efficiencies.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p>BASIC QUALIFICATIONS:</p>
<ul>
<li>
BA – 0+ years’ experience with a focus on marketing &amp; project management</li>
<li>
Exceptional verbal and written skills</li>
<li>
Curiosity and drive</li>
<li>
Willingness to take on new/different work</li>
</ul>
