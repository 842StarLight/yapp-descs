<h2>Helpdesk Support Technician</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<p>Duties include phone based technical support as well as server and network support at all levels having a focus on customer service. Job functions are focused on the workstation, basic server, printing, and network installation and troubleshooting. To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily.</p>
<p>
A Microsoft Certified Professional certification will be required within 90 days after start of employment. Some daily clerical and sales support work will be expected.</p>
<p><b>
Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or equivalent required.</li>
<li>Working conditions are normal for an office environment.</li>
<li>Work may require occasional weekend and/or evening work.</li>
<li>Work will be performed at Duluth office.</li>
<li>Advanced working knowledge of personal computers and basic networking skills to include past and present software packages.</li>
<li>Strong virus detection and removal skills are required.</li>
<li>Proficient with multiple platforms and hardware; Mac and Windows XP and up.</li>
<li>Accommodating, quick to learn and work well with others.</li>
<li>Must have A+, Network + certification or Microsoft MCDST.</li>
</ul>
