<h2>Regional Facilities Manager - North America (NA)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div><div>We are seeking a highly motivated and collaborative individual to join the Global Workplace Experience team. You are a leader with positive energy, are solutions-focused and enjoy working on cross-functional teams. <i>We are in the process of defining the Facilities Management (FM) Playbook for Atlassian, join us if you want to be part of the design and implementation around the globe.
</i>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>In this role, you’ll get to:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li><i>
Own and evolve the Facilities Management approach for Atlassian in North America</i></li>
<li><i>
Partner with our major vendors, regional operations manager, regional real estate team and Head of Workplace Security to problem solve issues and drive projects in the North America region.</i></li>
<li><i>
Continually strive for alignment on the FM strategy with APAC FM regional manager &amp; EU regional lead
</i></li>
<li>Experiment with new and enhances processes and approaches that continually improve the way we operate our workplaces</li>
<li>
Act as an expert for local site teams and real estate project teams, transferring knowledge and experience by coaching and mentoring site managers and local FM Teams</li>
<div>
This will result in:
</div>
<li><i>Local site teams having increased confidence in the right way to run their sites</i></li>
<li><i>
Increased measurable value for Atlassian from existing vendors</i></li>
<li>
Ensuring that SME knowledge and strategy and planning is within Atlassian rather than vendors or partners</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>On your first day, we will expect you to have:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
10+ years of facilities management experience at an agile company</li>
<li>
A tireless focus on the end-users holistic Experience and interpret the company values into your programs and initiatives</li>
<li>
First-class communication skills, a resilient attitude and a proven ability to manage and implement change</li>
<li>
Have a track record of partnering with multiple senior internal stakeholders and working within a matrix organization to deliver the best outcome</li>
<li>
Has a clear vision of what operational excellence should look like across our workspaces
</li>
<li>Strong vendor partnership management experience, from communicating expectations, assessing performance through KPIs and innovating with partners who work side by side with us to deliver our services</li>
<li>
Confidence in a fast-paced environment and you are a good listener, who is open to experimenting with new ideas and thinking</li>
<li>
Previous experience in managing across multiple sites</li>
<li>
Passionate about team building, you enjoy investing time in people
</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>It's great if you have:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Subject matter expertise in Food and Beverage, Sustainability or Real Estate</li>
<li>
A well-developed network of contacts in the employee experience and customer service industries</li>
<li>
Prior knowledge of Atlassian products, budget setting and managing external partnerships.</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
