<h2>Store Associate (location: Hidalgo St.)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">

<p><b>Job Description: </b></p>
<p>Deliver top class customer service and build customer satisfaction and loyalty. Provide effective and timely resolution of a range of customer inquiries. Be part of a team in which associates promote sales results and customer service through teamwork that is exemplified by personal involvement in phone/email handling, customer questions, and problem resolution.</p>
<p><b>Key Metrics: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Sales</li>
<li>Daily cash drawer balancing.</li>
<li>Accurate and timely sales order finalization.</li>
<li>Punctuality &amp; Attendance</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Essential Duties &amp; Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Greet all walk-in customers immediately.</li>
<li>Strike a positive and cooperative tone with both customers and coworkers.</li>
<li>Balance the cash drawer and prepare the appropriate paperwork as required for all transactions.</li>
<li>Organize and file all sales paperwork daily.</li>
<li>Responsible for the daily completion of all transactions including finalizing sales tickets throughout the day, collecting all appropriate paperwork for finance transactions, and the proper completion of credit card transactions.</li>
<li>Review transfer distribution documents according to company policy. Review all documents for accuracy and completeness. Report all discrepancies to the Store Manager.</li>
<li>Assist in the training of all personnel on the proper use and operation of computer programs and proper paperwork handling procedures.</li>
<li>Maintain a neat and orderly appearance, dress and groom according to company standards.</li>
<li>Assist with the maintenance of the store including, vacuuming, dusting, and general cleaning as directed by the Store Manager.</li>
<li>Assist in the unloading of stock trucks as scheduled and directed.</li>
<li>Ensure the use of safe work practices in all your responsibilities to limit the opportunity for injury to yourself and others and damage to products and property.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Essential Skills and Experience: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Excellent customer service skills.</li>
<li>Effective communicator with strong organization and problem solving skills.</li>
<li>Enthusiastic and a quick learn</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills, both written and oral</li>
<li>Upbeat and friendly attitude with ability to work well with others.</li>
<li>Ability to thrive in a lively working environment and multi-task.</li>
<li>The flexibility to work evenings, weekends and holidays as assigned.</li>
<li>Ability to perform responsibilities in a safe manner for self and others.</li>
<li>Compliance with HKG's Drug and Alcohol Policy.</li>
<li>Basic Computer skills to use software and complete required paperwork.</li>
<li>This position is subject to pre-employment, random, reasonable suspicion, post-accident and other Drug and Alcohol pursuant to HKG's Drug and Alcohol Policy.</li>
</ul>
