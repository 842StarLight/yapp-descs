<h2>Big Fish-Outside Sales Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<b>Outside Sales Representative</b><br>
<b>Big Fish</b>
</div>
<div>
<br>
Big Fish Employer Services, a 14-year-old, southern California-based company. We offer first class Human Resources and Payroll services, supported by the iSolved Human Capital Management (HCM) platform. We're looking for someone who wants to make a big difference in a smaller organization by implementing new clients onto our platform and services while helping to support the clients who have already put their trust in us.<br>
<br>
<b>Typically, you'll be taking on responsibility for:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Selling our full suite of HCM and Payroll processing services using the iSolved HCM platform</li>
<li>Selling our proprietary HR Managed Services</li>
<li>Selling our other ancillary services including Applicant Tracking, Time &amp; Attendance, Paperless Onboarding, Benefit Carrier Connectivity, COBRA and FSA administration, Workers Compensation Insurance, etc.</li>
<li>Managing your territory by:</li>
<li>Identifying prospects (potential customers) through phone and networking activities</li>
<li>Tracking activity within and updating Salesforce</li>
<li>Learning and understanding prospect requirements</li>
<li>Communicating the features and benefits of available services</li>
<li>Gathering required documentation and required forms to complete setups</li>
<li>Offering and providing stellar customer service</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
<b>Other things you ought to know about this job:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>You’ll need to be able to travel independently to client locations for meetings. Relationships are a big deal here at Big Fish.</li>
<li>You’ll be developing our brand in a new-ish territory, so you gotta be able to work independently with minimal supervision.</li>
<li>We keep things business casual, including our dress code.</li>
<li>While we do want our sales folks to have experience with service and/or technology sales, we will be providing training on our HCM platform and related services—it’s not sink or swim.</li>
<li>We’ll be measuring your success using an assigned quota.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
<b>You may be able to swim with the Big Fishes if:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>You have solid experience with outside B2B software sales – this is a must!</li>
<li>You’ve got at least five years of experience managing large, complex sales engagements, specifically in service contracts, such as business services or SaaS sales.</li>
<li>You’ve earned your college degree.</li>
<li>You’re adept at creating solutions to fill prospect requirements, and then professionally presenting those solutions to multiple influencers in our prospects’ organizations.</li>
<li>You know your way around Excel, Word, and Salesforce.</li>
<li>You’re computer competent and willing to become a platform expert – a big part of our service is our software solution!</li>
<li>You can show us your success at your previous employers – we’re really looking for winners.</li>
<li>You’ve had prior success working with and leveraging third-party influencers, specifically, group health brokers and agencies.</li>
</ul>
