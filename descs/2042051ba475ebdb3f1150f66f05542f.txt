<h2>Sr Sales Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>Location Georgetown, Texas Job Information
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>I. General Summary of Responsibilities</b></h4>
<p>Responsible for sales account management by promoting, selling and supporting TiPS alarm management software and lifecycle solutions to process industry enterprises. A comprehensive understanding of the Alarm Management discipline will be the foundation of the representative’s activities. Account management will be through direct engagement and support of external partners.</p>
<br>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
II. Primary Job Responsibilities</b></h4>
<ul>
<li>Account management responsibilities include selling TiPS solutions, pursuing leads, utilizing Microsoft Dynamics CRM to forecast sales, penetrate assigned corporate accounts, support partner activities, travel as sales situations dictate, perform demonstrations of LogMate software, deliver presentations, scope solutions, create proposals, develop solution architecture diagrams and collaborate with prospects to develop justification.</li>
<li>The representative will be required to manage and review sales documentation, process purchase orders, resolve issues related to delivery of solutions, and monitor projects.</li>
<li>Develop knowledge of Miller Heiman Strategic Selling methodology, Alarm Management best practices and associated disciplines,related industry regulations, standards and guidelines, Microsoft Dynamics CRM, and thorough understanding of TiPS software to facilitate demonstrations.</li>
<li>With respect to licensees, the representative will foster relationships, monitoring for the expansion or contraction of unit operations within the licensee’s enterprise searching for new opportunities.</li>
<li>The representative will also support Marketing with feedback on products, event support, collaboration on strategy and tactics.</li>
<br>
</ul>
