<h2>Senior Manager, Marketing</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Are you an excellent communicator? Do you have a passion for marketing? Do you consider yourself a leader? Do you like fitness, sports, and wellness? Are you hungry to learn on the fly and build something meaningful? If the answer is yes to the above—and if you live in the greater Los Angeles county—this gig at Mamba Sports Academy might be right for you.</p>
<p><b>About Mamba Sports Academy </b></p>
<p>Mamba Sports Academy (MSA) is a full-service Human Performance Center that aims to help athletes unlock their full potential in sports and life. The Academy, which counts Kobe Bryant among its partners and trains stars across all major sports, enlists a diverse array of experts who facilitate leading-edge body, cognitive, and performance training. The Academy, which was founded in 2016, has a 100,000 square foot location in Thousand Oaks, CA, as well as a performance center, in tandem with the Dodgers Training Academy, in Redondo Beach, CA.</p>
<p>MSA is a fast-paced and team-oriented environment where no two weeks look alike and not many days go by without a fun or unique experience. It is a place where people work hard, are rewarded for their efforts, and get to see the fruits of their labor come to fruition in real time. And it is a place of individual and communal growth.</p>
<p><b>Job Description</b></p>
<p>Mamba Sports Academy is in the market for communicative, detail-oriented, motivated, organized SENIOR MANAGER, MARKETING, who thrives off of fast-moving projects and multitasking. The person who fills this position will be an integral part of the marketing team, and will report directly to the Chief Marketing Officer. Under direction of the CMO, the senior manager will play a vital role in development and implementation of monthly, quarterly, and yearly marketing strategy for Mamba Sports Academy. The senior manager will be the CMO’s point person in the office, helping to oversee task assignment and completion within the department. They will also be the point person for communication with various members of the fitness and events staff. Moreover, the senior manager will come up with creative solutions on the fly and will have enough autonomy to ensure the larger marketing strategy is adhered to.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilites</b></p>
<p>· Lead and manage the ideation and execution of category-specific marketing plans consistent with brand ethos for key monthly activations, camps, and tournaments</p>
<p>· Co-manage in-house creative departments in development of key art, advertising creative, sales support materials, web and point-of-sales materials</p>
<p>· Co-manage performance marketing personnel/agency and any external marketing agencies to plan, implement, and optimize media spends around digital, print, social, and more</p>
<p>· Work closely with community management team to plan, implement and track social media growth and sentiment</p>
<p>· Work closely with content creation team to plan, implement and track digital and social video performance and sentiment</p>
<p>· Minimal travel required, mostly within Southern California</p>
