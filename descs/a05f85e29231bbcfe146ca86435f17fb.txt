<h2>Web Marketing Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Under the direction of the Marketing Manager, this position creates, sources and maintains digital content that engages contractors, builders and homeowners. Content design is focused on answering and anticipating customer questions about cabinet hardware and finishing. The position shares content and engages with customers across multiple social media platforms.
<br>Responsibilities include -
<br>Maintain websites for hghhardware.com and avondalespecialtyhardware.com, including:
<br><ul>
<li>Update product data on website including taxonomy/classification, images, etc.</li>
<li>Coordinate marketing content including banners, promotions, vendor pages, resources, etc.</li>
<li>Provide site support and serve as technical point person for staff and customers</li>
<li>Work with internal resources to resolve web-related product/customer issues</li>
<li>Work with technical support to resolve website issues</li>
<li>Participate on monthly conference call with web software project manager to follow up on tickets, site progress, etc.</li>
</ul>
<b>Manage social media presence, including:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Develop content strategy aligned with short-term and long-term marketing targets</li>
<li>Develop and implement an editorial calendar</li>
<li>Interview subject matter experts, research, and write blog posts</li>
<li>Maintain social media channels</li>
<li>Maintain customer relationships via social media</li>
<li>Create and publish engaging social media content</li>
<li>Edit and proofread content for grammar, style, punctuation, and brand adherence</li>
<li>Stay up to date with web and social media developments and generate new ideas to draw customers' attention</li>
</ul>
Maintain customer email list, and send email newsletters, bulletins, updates, etc.
<br><br>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Deadline-oriented self-starter with excellent writing abilities, good understanding of data manipulation, and impeccable attention to detail</li>
<li>Excellent organizational and time-management skills</li>
<li>Experience with social media and blogging platforms</li>
<li>Basic technical knowledge of web publishing</li>
<li>High school diploma, relevant college degree preferred</li>
</ul>
Company benefits include vacation, holidays, health and dental insurance, life insurance, short- and long-term disability insurance, flexible benefit plan, and 401(k) with company match.
<br>Who Are We?
<br>HGH Hardware Supply is a family-owned wholesale and retail cabinet hardware and accessory and finishing supply company based in the Avondale neighborhood of Birmingham, Alabama. We've been serving cabinetmakers and woodworkers since 1963, have five branches in three states, and cover the better part of the Southeast.</div>
