SHOULD YOU ACCEPT THIS CHALLENGE...
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Define overall product and feature requirements - future product strategy with sustainable and compelling differentiation based on customer needs, competitive analysis, market and technology trends.</li>
<li>Support and enable the product and solutions teams to design cloud enabled storage and compute architectures.</li>
<li>Drive overall roadmap, feature tradeoffs, prioritization and requirements for enterprise-class storage array systems.</li>
<li>Drive roadmap based on market, technology, business and competitive analysis.</li>
<li>Field/Sales – engage key account field sales teams and channel partners. Work with team to support sales process and drive the overall customer engagement model</li>
<li>Foster an active ecosystem of customers and partners.</li>
<li>Responsible for competitive material development and SE/Sales support materials as well as customer presentations.</li>
<li>Customer/Market Research – gather customer feedback to understand key issues/needs and trends in customer base.</li>
<li>Work closely with the executive team to establish the scope and goals of the project and provide leadership for the project teams</li>
</ul>
WHAT YOU'LL NEED TO BRING TO THIS ROLE...
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>6+ years of combined experience in a product management and product development roles in any of the following areas:</li>
<li>Storage replication: Synchronous, Asynchronous, Continuous async</li>
<li>User interfaces: GUI, CLI, REST API and Policy based management, storage provisioning models, reporting capabilities.</li>
<li>Security: Certifications and Compliance, UI security features, and issues.</li>
<li>Strategic leadership and strong business insight</li>
<li>Deep, technical understanding of enterprise storage environments, deployments and use cases</li>
<li>Executive presence and excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Critical problem solving</li>
<li>Consistent track record of developing a vision, strategy and execution plan for a product line</li>
<li>Experience and a solid understanding of product life cycles, release milestones, from concept inception to product end-of-life</li>
<li>Ability to think both strategically and tactically</li>
<li>Ability to work in a rapid pace environment with multiple and shifting priorities</li>
<li>Consistent record of taking ownership and driving results</li>
<li>Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, or equivalent, Master's Degree a plus</li>
</ul>
