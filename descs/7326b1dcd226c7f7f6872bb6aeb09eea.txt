<h2>Product Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Position Description
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div><b>At a Glance</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<b>
Legrand </b>has an exciting opportunity for a <b>Product Manager</b> to join the <b>Wattstopper Team</b> in <b>Carlsbad, CA</b>. Responsible for developing, driving and implementing the product line strategic plan for BCS’s fixture integrated controls. Develops this plan in conjunction with the systems Product Managers and Product Marketing Managers.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
What Will You Do?</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
 Product Development: field visits, meet with customers (ECs, lighting manufacturers, specifiers, and end-users), initiate market requirements documentation, and collaborate on market problems with engineering, etc ... to maintain a constant flow of new fixture-integrated contorls and product enhancements.</li>
<li>
Launch Coordination: together with software product managers, project managers and engineering leads, bring together system releases comprised of hardware, software, firmware and cloud products.
</li>
<li>Sales support &amp; training: working with product marketing managers respond to sales questions, prepare major launches &amp; sales events (Nat Sales Conf, Trade Shows, etc.), support pricing strategy, travel with PMMs and sales team, collaborate &amp; respond to training requests</li>
<li>
Promotional activities: working with product marketing managers, technical writers and marketing communications, participate in catalog development, coordinate development of installation instructions, product sell sheets, product bulletins &amp; promotional campaigns to support product launches</li>
<li>
Long-term planning: lead the long-term vision for designated product line, ensuring sustainable revenue growth, market share and profitability, keep an eye on the market: attend trade events, competitive benchmarking, initiate &amp; conduct research into long-term trends &amp; customer needs.</li>
<li>
Budget planning: monitor &amp; review product line performance &amp; trends to evaluate budget</li>
<li>
Performs other similar and related duties as required.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<b>
Education</b>:
</div>
<ul><li>BS in Engineering, Marketing, Business or related</li></ul>
<div>
<b>
Experience</b>:</div>
<ul><li>
Min. 3 years experience in marketing or related field. Specific electrical or electrical industry knowledge a plus.</li></ul>
<div>
<b>
Skills/Knowledge/Abilities</b>:</div>
<ul>
<li>
 Demonstrated ability to understand customer’s needs and identify new ways to serve/satisfy customer.</li>
<li>
Requires strong organizational and time management skills, close attention to detail and be able to effectively handle multiple priorities.</li>
<li>
Must possess excellent project management skills, including demonstrated ability to develop and manage to a project plan, establish priorities, work to deadlines and control a number of projects at various stages of work.</li>
<li>
Experience with a variety of product development processes (different forms of agile, waterfall, etc) a significant plus</li>
<li>
Strong computer skills, proficient with Slack, JIRA, TFS, word processing, spreadsheet and presentation software, e-mail and meeting management software. Ability to use internet and web based resources efficiently and effectively.</li>
<li>
Must have excellent verbal and written communications skills and be able to communicate effectively across all levels of the organization. Must be an effective listener, able to maintain focus extract necessary information and validate understanding of the information.</li>
<li>
Must be highly collaborative and able to work in a team based environment. Promotes an environment that is encourages cohesiveness and fosters trust and open communication with team, peers and all business partners.</li>
<li>
Must have solid business acumen and understanding of a variety of functional areas within the business. Must be proficient in negotiation and influencing skills.</li>
</ul>

<div><b>About Legrand</b></div>
<div>
Every single day, Legrand brings power, light and data to millions of spaces around the world. <div><b>About Legrand’s Building Control Systems Division</b></div>
