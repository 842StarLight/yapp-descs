<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Answer technical support requests via email, chat, and phone</li>
<li>Help guide customers through the setup process</li>
<li>Collaborate with our engineering team to solve more complex issues</li>
<li>Identify, document and follow up with engineers on product bugs and features</li>
<li>Update knowledge base and troubleshooting documentation for internal and customer use</li>
<li>Act as a liaison between engineering, customer success and sales on technical issues affecting our customers</li>
</ul>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Fluent in English</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Previous experience in a client-facing technical role for a product business</li>
<li>Strong problem-solving, decision-making, and critical-thinking skills</li>
<li>Ability to make sense of something complex and explain it in plain English</li>
<li>Professional, courteous and committed to provide amazing customer support</li>
<li>Open-minded, positive and keen to learn</li>
<li>Great attitude, team player</li>
<li>Willing to work flexible hours</li>
<li>Keen to mentor and help train junior team members</li>
<li>College degree</li>
</ul>
<b>Bonus Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Degree in a technical subject</li>
<li>Knowledge of IP Telephony</li>
<li>Experience diagnosing QoS issues</li>
<li>Experience with call center technology such as IVRs</li>
<li>Experience with Salesforce as an admin or developer</li>
</ul>
