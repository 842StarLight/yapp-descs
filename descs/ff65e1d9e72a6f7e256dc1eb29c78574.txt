<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Who You'll Work With</b></h2>
<p>
Cisco's Internet of Things (IoT) Group builds intelligent networks and software platforms for many different IoT applications.</p>
<p>
Do you love challenging product boundaries in a fast paced, agile environment? Our product managers are entrepreneurs in their space, working in a small, innovative team with some of the sharpest product minds in the industry.</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Who You Are</b></h2>
<p>
We are looking for a product manager for software products that leads Cisco’s IoT hardware and software portfolio. In this role, the IoT Product Manager will lead our management software product definition, provide leadership and direction in developing and maintaining the larger IoT experience, be responsible for pricing, go to market planning and facilitate cross functional roadmaps with other product managers.</p>
<p>
You will have a deep, hands-on role in defining product capabilities and working closely with engineering and marketing teams.
</p>
<ul>
<li>5-8 years of overall work experience with a strong background in systems engineering, product development and/or product management.</li>
<li>
3+ years of Product Management experience in software, network management, IoT or M2M environment</li>
<li>
Develop project business cases and customer Return on Investment (ROI)</li>
<li>
Deep appreciation for the Customer mindset. Confirmed ability to think Customer-first; successful track record in building strategies and solutions to meet Customer needs</li>
<li>
Has demonstrated project leadership including successfully defining and launching product innovation</li>
<li>
Experience in network deployment and operations processes (eg ITIL) and tools (Prime Infrastructure, Nagios, etc.)</li>
<li>
Understanding of modern SW stacks, eg: Docker, Kubernetes, NiFi, Kafka, Flume etc.</li>
<li>
Understanding of infrastructure services, eg: RBAC, IAM, UI frameworks,</li>
<li>
Understanding of cloud offerings and ability to discuss cloud architectures; familiarity/Strength with AWS and related AWS services</li>
<li>
Understanding of manufacturing environment (Purdue Model/ISA-99, SCADA, PLC, CNC)</li>
<li>
Experience with Agile/Scrum development environments is desired</li>
<li>
Validated ability to influence cross-functional teams without formal authority
</li>
