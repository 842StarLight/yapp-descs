<h2>Accounting Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Job Description:</b><br>
<p>The Accounting Manager is primarily responsible for maintaining the General Ledger; preparing external and internal financial statements; managing Sales Tax; <b>and managing a great Accounts Payable/Receivable team!
</b></p>
<p><b>PRIMARY RESPONSIBILITIES INCLUDE:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Maintaining General Ledger by reconciling internal accounts; ensuring journal entries are made in a timely manner and conducting month end closing</li>
<li>
Day to day management of the Accounts Payable/Receivable team and the associated processes and activities</li>
<li>
Being responsible for management of Sales Tax filings, maintaining sales tax tables, and reconciliations</li>
<li>
Managing all Management Reporter financial reports</li>
<li>
Managing entire Intercompany process (Weekly Billings/Monthly Misc. Billings/Month-End Reconciliations)</li>
<li>
Assisting with Monthly Executive Financial Packet</li>
<li>
Preparing journal entries and account reconciliations for International entities (UK/Canada/Panama)</li>
<li>
Reviewing internal financial reporting and analyzing profit and loss (P &amp; L) statements for individual departments</li>
<li>
Overseeing various audits (Year-end, 401k) and tax prep for outside auditors/tax accountants</li>
<li>
Managing Fixed Assets</li>
<li>
Managing Inventory tracking process</li>
<li>
Preparing Warranty Reconciliation and Analysis</li>
<li>
Acting as backup for Payroll processing</li>
<li>
Researching various accounting/tax issues as appropriate</li>
</ul>
