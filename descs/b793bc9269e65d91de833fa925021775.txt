<b>Your Role and Responsibilities</b><br>
In this role, you will support IBM’s Journey To Cloud growth strategy by enabling our clients to plan and execute on their cloud platform, DevOps, and infrastructure strategies. You'll be responsible for building strategic relationships with executives and key business leaders to promote IBM’s portfolio of cloud platform solutions, including hybrid cloud and cloud native application development, and container management.
<ul>
<li>Sell Red Hat based cloud solutions while working within a defined market vertical</li>
<li>
Promote the value of IBM services around Red Hat as part of a larger cloud solution</li>
<li>
Advise clients on the best path to move from virtualization to a container based solution on a hybrid cloud using Red Hat technologies and services</li>
<li>
Gain trusted advisor status with senior IT execs within assigned accounts as part of pipeline development and efforts to develop long term relationships</li>
<li>
Work closely with the dedicated IBM client teams to identify opportunities and provide services sales support specific to Red Hat solutions</li>
<li>
Develop high level cloud strategies for IBM clients as part of a consultative sales cycle
</li>
<li>Take identified opportunities and drive them through a defined sales process to closure</li>
<li>
Lead technical sales discussions with clients from executive to C-level
