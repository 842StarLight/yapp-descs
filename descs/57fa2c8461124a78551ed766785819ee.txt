<h2>IT Sales Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>The Managed Network Services Specialist is a selling consultant with a geographic territory assignment within a region. They work in conjunction with the sales team to qualify Managed Network Services opportunities upon first meetings. The MNS Specialist will conduct network assessments and develop solutions to meet the client’s needs. They will also build existing business through prospecting and networking for new business opportunities as well as grow through existing clients that do not have MNS services from Proven IT. The MNS Specialist is the liaison between the sales engineer and IT team at Proven and the client’s IT team or executive team to manage the projects and communication to have the highest level of support and response time.</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Increase education of MNS services with internal partners and external potential clients.</li>
<li>Identify new potential opportunities through qualifying while prospecting, during meetings and conducting an assessment for MNS services to include but not limited to voice and data, security, phone systems, company policies, software, hardware, and disaster recovery.</li>
<li>Use sales activities &amp; database management to reach communicate and track project results: appointments, on-site workflow analysis, solution collaboration presentations, proposals, implementation of solutions and account reviews.</li>
<li>Collaborate with other sales professionals and engineers to develop solutions</li>
<li>Utilize a database and the internet to research prospects and qualify leads to increase contact to appointment ratio benchmarks.</li>
<li>Attend and participate in development seminars and company meetings to develop professional skills, business acumen and certifications.</li>
<li>Utilize resources and apply learning to teach and tailor technology solutions to client environment</li>
<li>Assess client’s business, analyzing data and costs to provide an accurate assessment of their environment to make educated decisions to support solution and future growth.</li>
</ul>
