<h2>Product & Channel Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>Overview</b></p>
<p>
The Product &amp; Channel Marketing Manager is a results-oriented individual who has strong experience bringing products to market and driving revenue through channel marketing programs. The role’s key function is to maximize new customer acquisition and delivering on the overall product and channel marketing strategy, with success in the role linked to achieving both product performance targets as well as delivering on key initiatives that will meet channel revenue targets. Learning from our customers, partners and sales team, the role will build and iterate programs to drive customer acquisition and evangelism through the channel.</p>
<p>
This person will closely align with the company’s sales team to build and manage to measurable key performance indicators designed to support driving revenue objectives. Role responsibility will also include building strategy for and executing on channel marketing plans and programs. Product marketing responsibilities will include go-to-market development, competitive analysis, sales enablement, and overall market/industry intelligence analysis.</p>
<p><b>
What You'll Be Accountable For</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage partner content, communications and demand generation programs with vehicles that deliver consistent/relevant information to partners ultimately creating new revenue.</li>
<li>Responsible for ensuring marketing programs are generating new revenue month over month and adjusting plans to accommodate a dynamic marketplace.</li>
<li>Develop and manage programmatic channel promotions, benefits and rewards.</li>
<li>Develop and support co-marketing programs for strategic partners.</li>
<li>Build and promote channel marketing and enablement tools to strengthen our partners’ technology acumen and understanding of the Ruby’s value proposition.</li>
<li>Empower partners to be our advocates: providing and promoting vehicles deliver partner communications and brand awareness.</li>
<li>Maintain online content across partner sites including copy, imagery, video &amp; brand pages.</li>
<li>Ensure all tactical aspects of marketing events, programs and branding are handled with impeccable attention to detail.</li>
<li>Own and build quarterly marketing plans with input from the partner team.</li>
<li>Create integrated multi-touch campaigns that may include email, online programs, social media, events and/or webinars to support the partner community.</li>
<li>Act as the primary evangelist for product solutions messaging and positioning and drive overall efforts to establish strong market differentiation and desirable value proposition for our customers.</li>
<li>Develops and executes comprehensive strategic product marketing plans and programs to support sales and revenue objectives of organization.</li>
<li>Collaborates with Product Management and Marketing to develop product positioning and messaging that resonates with our target customers.</li>
<li>Manage and execute product launch plans including the creation of sales collateral, sales training and business development support materials.</li>
</ul> <p><b>Your Work DNA</b></p>
<ul>
<li>You are a strong, articulate, natural leader, able to successfully motivate and challenge a team of talented and creative people at all levels of the organization.</li>
<li>You excel at building strong relationships to facilitate collaboration across multiple cross-functional stakeholders including product, customer happiness, sales and marketing.</li>
<li>You are a multi-channel marketing guru capable of building and executing compelling marketing campaigns and concepts, channel strategy and plan implementation and got-to-market plans.</li>
<li>You thrive in a role where the entire organization is counting on you and your team to achieve their goals.</li>
<li>You think both strategically and analytically, and are disciplined about measuring everything you do</li>
<li>You possess an internal competitiveness and natural curiosity to constantly strive for improved results</li>
<li>You love the fast-paced environment of a growth-stage company, and possess a scrappy, hands-on, roll-up-your-sleeves approach.</li>
<li>You understand the needs and purchase behavior of small businesses, and the value of the human connection in business success</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Your Background</b></p>
<ul>
<li>3-5 years’ experience in a marketing role, eCommerce, B2C &amp; B2B preferred. SaaS to SMB experience preferred.</li>
<li>Proven record of successful execution of go-to-market plans and channel marketing strategy development; strong strategic planning and program development skills.</li>
<li>Must have a strong analytical and quantitative skill set combined with a strength to analyze data, interpret and translate results to produce actionable changes.</li>
<li>Highly efficient team player, with the ability to also work independently in a fast-paced, fluid environment.</li>
<li>Proven track record collaborating with cross-functional internal parties and external agencies/contractors.</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in marketing, business, or related field.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Work Environment</b></p>
<p>
Work takes place in an open office setting. This position requires the ability to sit and work at a computer for extended periods of time and communicate effectively with a diverse audience in person, by phone and by computer. This position will travel occasionally.</p>
