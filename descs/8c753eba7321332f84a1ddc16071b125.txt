The team
<br>Our Product &amp; Program Management teams work on some of the most impactful and challenging problems in the Software industry. We are a highly collaborative cross-border group that operates on the edge between Engineering and the business and helps to design solutions from concept to launch – all while focusing on and advocating for what's best for our customers. <br><br>
This position
<br>The Program Manager will be responsible for managing the scope, impact, and complexity of 2 programs through all phases of the product life cycle. This role will manage interdependencies and integrations across projects, teams, and stakeholders. This position will work in conjunction with Product Management, Customer Experience, and Engineering to ensure the delivery of the team's roadmap, and deliver the underlying platform systems and applications necessary to support continued system scale, a broadening product portfolio, the basis for new innovative product capabilities, and improve operational efficiencies related to these industries. Your objectives will be directly linked to business metrics such as revenue, customer satisfaction, and operational efficiency.
<br><br>
This position reports to the Director of Program Management.
<br><br>
Responsibilities
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Drive the end-to-end delivery and sustainability of Commerce solutions</li>
<li>Partner with Product Management, Engineering, UX, IX, Marketing, Sales, Support, Legal, Finance, and IT to deliver best in-class experience for our customers</li>
<li>Work with scrum team on planning activities for scope commitments</li>
<li>Keep leadership and business teams updated on project timelines and results</li>
<li>Partner with Product Manager to define end to end launch plan ensuring Design, Research, IX, Marketing, Support, Legal, Finance are represented</li>
<li>Partner with Product Management and Engineering leads to facilitate continuous roadmap planning and quarterly planning</li>
<li>Define communication plan for program</li>
<li>Lead communication within the team, to stakeholders and at cross-functional meetings</li>
<li>Ensure adherence to PLC and SDLC while maintaining visibility to Release Management</li>
<li>Provide coaching to teams on Agile/SCRUM best practices to maximize value and efficiency</li>
<li>Identify and track risks to project delivery and overall program schedule</li>
<li>Work with cross-team leadership to conduct LOE estimation and resource planning</li>
<li>Evaluate existing processes and procedures. Make recommendations and drive process improvement and adoption of best practices to improve efficiency and effectiveness.</li>
<li>Conduct change management efforts of moderate complexity impacting development teams</li>
<li>Some travel required. (~10%)</li>
</ul>
