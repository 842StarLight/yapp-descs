<h2>Demo Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<div>WalkMe pioneered the Digital Adoption Platform (DAP) to transform the user experience in today’s overwhelming digital world. Using artificial intelligence, analytics, engagement, guidance, and automation, WalkMe’s transparent overlay assists users to complete tasks easily within any enterprise software, mobile application or website.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
About the role</b></div>
<div>
Here at WalkMe, our product demos are one of the most important assets, so we need an amazing team to take these demos to the next level. This is different than your traditional demo engineering job - you won't build demos for specific deals, but rather create demonstrations that are used by <i>everyone</i>, so it's a chance to affect our global success, and to wow customers, sales reps, and even our CEO!</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
You'll become a mega-expert on the WalkMe product and value suite; this is an excellent introduction to the WalkMe sales organization, where you can start building your career in Solution Consulting, Product Marketing, or similar.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
You will report to the Sales Demo Manager, and be part of the Solution Consulting organization.</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What You'll Own</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
You will configure and manage the underlying demo environments, which are required to show the WalkMe product features in a contextual way.</li>
<li>
You'll design, develop, QA, and maintain the WalkMe content for our global demo ecosystem.</li>
<li>
You will also create demo documentation and communication, working with our field readiness organization</li>
<li>
Additionally, you should collect requirements from multiple teams by paying close attention to new product feature releases, soliciting and listening to feedback, and monitoring demo use and success. You will use all of this information to inform your efforts.</li>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
</div>
<div>
You will uphold the four E’s of demo engineering:</div>
<li>
<b>
Expertise</b>: understand best what WalkMe does, and what it can and will do</li>
<li>
<b>
Exploration</b>: push the boundaries of the product by testing new features and discovering new use cases</li>
<li>
<b>
Execution</b>: bring those possibilities and future possibilities to life in demos</li>
<li>
<b>
Efficiency:</b> make it possible, quickly and easily, for others to demonstrate those things</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What You Need to Succeed</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
1+ years of software implementation experience</li>
<li>
Basic HTML, CSS, and Javascript experience highly preferred</li>
<li>
1+ years of experience creating or using software product demonstrations</li>
<li>
Knowledge of on-premise and cloud applications, across web, desktop, and mobile frameworks</li>
<li>
Exposure to the configuration of at least one other complex software tool</li>
<li>
Interest in software sales</li>
<li>
Consulting or customer-facing experience highly preferred.</li>
</ul></div>
