<h2>Program Manager - Acquisitions and Special Projects</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<div><b>Position Overview</b></div>
<div>The Program Manager – Acquisitions and Special Projects will assist the VP Program Manager-Acquisitions and Special Projects with analysis, organization and coordination of projects related to banking business line acquisitions and other strategic projects/initiatives as assigned by VP Program Manager-Acquisitions and Special Projects. The position requires attention to detail, critical thinking skills, good interpersonal communication, the ability to work individually and in a group setting, and self-motivation.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>Areas of Responsibility</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Assist in the day-to-day management of acquisition activities within the Banking business line to include supporting the Program Manager and IT Project Manager with potential needs, assist with coordination of the Enterprise level groups once the acquisition is announced and being available to conduct planning and execution on assigned tasks, as needed. Specific responsibilities would include: scheduling times/rooms for meetings; tracking/reporting expenses; legacy data conversion; working with HR regarding project employees; compiling mailing lists for customer communications; data gathering/analysis/reporting of conversion metrics; assist with conversion weekend activities. In the event of multiple conversions, fill in as Acting Program/Project Manager for parts of less complex acquisitions, executing assigned tasks against the Timeline/Milestone framework established.</li>
<li>
Management of all post conversion activities: obtain and verify the ingestion of the legacy data gap cut; ensure all paper records have been boxed and relocated to the appropriate Wintrust department; manage the turn down and decommissioning of all legacy servers; and any other milestones uncovered during the conversion process.</li>
<li>
Own the maintenance of the Wintrust Banking Business Line Acquisition Playbook; compile all “lessons learned” and incorporate any necessary procedural or systemic changes as determined.</li>
<li>
Manage other special assignments as required.</li>
</ul>
.
