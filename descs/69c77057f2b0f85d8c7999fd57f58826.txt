<h2>Group Creative Director, Retail</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<ul>
<li>10+ years of conceptual creative experience working with an agency or global brand</li>
<li>10+ years of experience as an art director with retail &amp; packaging design experience.</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in design, advertising, or related fields.</li>
<li>Online portfolio including campaigns that include 2D and 3D graphic design, environmental design, interactive, motion graphics, print, video or web.</li>
<li>Experience with four-color printing processes, and large format printing.</li>
<li>Experience with dieline creation and color, material and finish of paper products (corrugate, paper pulp, SBS)</li>
</ul>
<br>
<div>Ring is looking for a transformative Group Creative Director to drive Ring’s brand storytelling within the retail and packaging spaces. As a design leader, you have inspired and motivated both creative and cross-functional teams. In this role, you’ll inspire a team of internal resources, give direction to vendors, and drive outside agencies and partners. Above all, you are a master at telling strong brand and product stories on the real estate presented to you. - whether 10 feet away, 5 feet away, or 1 foot away.<br>
<br>
Responsibilities<br>
<ul>
<li>Ownership of the creative development and execution of the retail experience, packaging design, and unboxing across all Ring products and properties.</li>
<li>Research-driven approach, incorporating business, marketing, and customer insights to deliver impactful experiences.</li>
<li>Creative leadership on the end-to-end process, synthesis of consumer insights into creative actions, wireframe design, management of workflow and approvals, presentation of work for executive review and buy-on, leadership of projects through engineering and development and QA.</li>
<li>Develop relationships with suppliers, engaging in prototyping and oversee engineering. Have insight and oversight into cost, production timelines and workback schedules.</li>
<li>Lead consumer feedback studies on packaging, point of sale and displays.</li>
<li>Create concepts, sketches, graphics, and high-fidelity executions in support of, retail designs, packaging, brand, and marketing campaigns.</li>
<li>Oversee routine and complex duties in the preparation of printing specifications, working with vendors to verify print specifications.</li>
<li>Understand and respond to performance data (metric tests, usability studies), and steer the direction of future creative designs.</li>
<li>Lead and grow a team of designers and grow the capacity and the output of the retail group.</li>
<li>Manage multiple projects and coordinate with GTM, Marketing, Ring Taiwan and any associated vendors to ensure all projects are completed in a timely manner</li>
</ul>
</div>
