<p>
We specialize in all things glass.</p>
<p>
Glass Doctor offers fast &amp; friendly glass service to business, home, and auto owners across the DFW area.</p>
<p>
Our team of experts lives our code of values of Respect, Integrity, and Customer Focus while having fun in the process! We hire reliable, fun, customer service-focused professionals who are dedicated to creating an amazing customer experience.</p>
<p>
Glass Doctor is looking for an assertive, problem solving professional who is looking to grow their career and assume responsibility for the internal operations of our fast growing commercial services business.</p>
<p>
The ideal candidate is a detail oriented, impatient, and results oriented. They should prefer to work in an environment where they can make a big impact on the business, learn, grow their responsibilities, increase their compensation, and not be micro managed.</p>
<p>
We only hire winners, so a proven track record of winning and overcoming obstacles in life is required to be considered for the position.</p>
<p>
The position reports directly to the division manager. Responsibilities include estimating, procurement, logistics, scheduling and systems improvement.</p>
<p><b>
Specific Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Receive incoming calls in professional and courteous manner</li>
<li>Enter customer service requests into our point of sale system</li>
<li>Assist in scheduling, procurement, and invoicing</li>
<li>Perform other duties as needed which may include cross-training in related positions</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Job Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Prior experience is not necessary</li>
<li>Computer literate, with working knowledge of business software and spreadsheet applications</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills</li>
<li>Good organizational and time management skills</li>
<li>Professional appearance and personality</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Benefits:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Company health insurance, disability insurance</li>
<li>Dental and vision insurance is available</li>
<li>Two weeks of paid time off, 8 holidays</li>
</ul>
