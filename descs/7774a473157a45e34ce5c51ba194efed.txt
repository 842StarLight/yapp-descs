<h2>Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>At Signals Analytics, we help the world’s leading brands to make better decisions faster and with less risk. We’re looking for hard-working, passionate people to help us unite the art and science of sound decision- making!</p>
<p>
Our Commercial team will be growing significantly in 2020 and we will be looking for a talented and business-minded Sales professionals! You’ll identify opportunities, build significant relationships within the organization, drive and close business deals with your key accounts. You’ll be working with key decision makers and stakeholders in order to offer Signals’ value proposition. This includes coordination with multiple departments and executives at Signals as well as presentations, discussions, and negotiations with clients.</p>
<p>
We are looking for an Independent thinker, able to see the big picture, multi-task in different teams, take initiative and get things done, excellent time management skills with the ability to work under pressure and possess a fast learning curve</p>
<p>
 In addition to traditional sales skillsets, you will have a background in data analysis and the ability to present this data to potential clients and stakeholders.</p>
<p><b>
What you will be doing...</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Prospecting a defined list of companies to build a qualified pipeline</li>
<li>
Create polished presentations and present to senior executives (C-level, VP, Directors) to effectively demonstrate your learned knowledge of Signals solution and the associated value proposition</li>
<li>
Proactively identify and convert new leads while developing appropriate strategies to ensure successful attainment of sales quotas</li>
<li>
Working in partnership with various internal departments to gain a true understanding of prospects’ requirements in order to win business</li>
<li>
Acting as a true ambassador for the company</li>
<li>
Moderate travel (to meet with customers, prospects or to attend conferences)</li>
<li>
Negotiating pricing and contract terms and conditions</li>
<li>
Effectively coordinate all aspects of the sale from start to finish and achieving sales quotas
</li>
<li>Serving as primary contact and advisor for your clients throughout the sales cycle</li>
<li>
Team with the pre-sales team to product demos, and Proof of Concept presentations (POC),
</li>
<li>Prepare RFP responses; business justification in a pre-sales capacity to ensure the Signals value proposition is clearly understood</li>
<li>
Diagnose customer pain points and use cases of interest and accordingly translate areas of interest into tangible product and service requirements</li>
<li>
Coordinate with internal product teams to assess feasibility of configuration requests</li>
<li>
Maintain a deep understanding of industry trends and how those trends are reflected in the Signals product and services</li>
<li>
Proposal preparation</li>
</ul>
<br>
Requirements:<br>
<ul>
<li>3-6 years’ experience working for a consulting company, startup technology company or in house in a sales or business development role. Continuous, substantial, and demonstrable success in enterprise sales</li>
<li>
High energy and well-developed business acumen</li>
<li>
Experience with innovation processes, product development, marketing strategy – an advantage</li>
<li>
Exceptional ambition combined with great teaming skills</li>
<li>
Experience in consultative, enterprise solutions selling to line of business executives and data analytics software</li>
<li>
Deep and creative sales hunter skills</li>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree</li>
<li>
Consistently exceeded targets and objectives</li>
<li>
Excellent communication, presentation and negotiation skills</li>
</ul>
