<h2>OUTSIDE SALESPERSON</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>
<div>Hajoca Corporation is the nation’s largest privately held wholesale distributor of plumbing, heating and air-conditioning, industrial pipes-valves-fittings, pool and waterworks supplies for residential, commercial, industrial and infrastructure construction. We have approximately 400 locations, called Profit Centers, throughout the United States, representing the premier product lines in our industry.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
Since 1858, Hajoca has been blending the strengths and clout of a large company with freedom and opportunity for employees. The company will continue to grow and prosper because our fundamental business philosophy works: emphasis on entrepreneurial spirit, expert knowledge, strong incentives for our employees, and devotion to Hajoca’s proven business principles of “Service, Integrity, Reliability.”</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b><i>
A Hajoca Outside Salesperson is expected to:</i></b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li> represent the Profit Center in a professional and ethical manner, in accordance with Company policies and procedures</li>
<li> build strong customer relationships</li>
<li> take initiative, seeking always to improve share of wallet with each customer</li>
<li> be persuasive using excellent verbal communication and sales abilities</li>
</ul>
<div>
</div>
<ul><li> know the products and services they are selling to each customer.</li></ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Job Summary</b></h1>
<div></div>
<br>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Outside Sales personnel develop lasting, ethical customer relationships to maximize our share of the customer’s potential, to meet or exceed the established sales and gross profit goals, and to increase profit sharing. He/She becomes an integral part of each assigned account, understanding the customers’ business and needs, ensuring our role as their primary supplier.</b></h1>
<div></div>
<br>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Job Duties</b></h1>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<ul><li> Visit customer offices and job sites to assess customer needs and potential, to develop and maintain strong relationships, and to generate and close sales.<br>
</li></ul>
<div></div>
<ul><li> Analyze customer data, develop and execute strategies to grow market share and meet or exceed the monthly required GP$ target $ and GP% target %.<br>
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<ul><li> Process Sales Orders, Bids, Job Information packages, and perform all sales functions in accordance with Company policy and procedures.</li></ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul><li> Attend scheduled sales meetings, maintain weekly sales plans and call reports, and keep customer contact files current.</li></ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul><li> Prospect new customers and alert the Profit Center Manager and Credit Manager of viable prospects for screening.</li></ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul><li> Maintain product literature files and sample material to ensure the most current and accurate information is always provided to our customers.<br>
</li></ul>
<div></div>
<ul><li> Investigate and resolve customer problems; address pricing deductions and material returns timely, exceeding customer expectations and benefiting the Profit Center.</li></ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul><li> Work with the customer, Profit Center Manager and Credit Manager to facilitate payment of all accounts receivable; communicate to the Profit Center Manager and Credit Manager any changes in a customer’s business that might cause a credit risk.<br>
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<ul><li> Support Inside Sales and all Profit Center activities as part of our Profit Center Team.<br>
</li></ul>
<p>
</p>
<ul><li> Attend local and state meetings or conventions to learn about new products, market conditions, and the competition, and to build vendor relationships.<br>
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<ul><li> Perform other reasonably related duties as assigned by immediate supervisor and other management as required.</li></ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<div>
Although Hajoca is a large company, we work in a decentralized environment where each of our locations, called Profit Centers, is run by the Profit Center Manager as if it was their own small business. We give you the tools you need to succeed, investing in your personal and professional growth through targeted training programs, and reward team success through our profit sharing opportunities and generous benefits package which includes:</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<ul><li>Medical, dental, vision, and prescription coverage</li></ul>
<ul><li>401 (k) and retirement cash account</li></ul>
<ul><li>Life insurance</li></ul>
<ul><li>Pre-tax accounts for healthcare and dependent care</li></ul>
<ul><li>Paid vacation, holidays, and sick time</li></ul>

<div>
Hajoca Corporation is an Equal Opportunity/Affirmative Action Employer (EOE and M/F/Disability/Veterans.) We are a drug free workplace, with pre-employment drug screening required. Employment is contingent upon successful completion of a background investigation.</div>
