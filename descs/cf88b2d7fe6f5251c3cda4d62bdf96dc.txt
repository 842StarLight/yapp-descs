<p></p>
<div>
<p>Clariant is a focused and innovative specialty chemical company based near Basel in Switzerland. Last year the company recorded CHF 6.6 billion in sales harnessing the talents of its 18 000 employees across 53 countries.
</p>
<p>Clariant’s portfolio is optimized for future growth, with a focus in four business areas: Care Chemicals, Catalysts, Natural Resources, and Plastics &amp; Coatings.
</p>
<p>We believe that our growth is all about our customers and their success. Investment in innovation capabilities and developing inspiring environments for courageous and determined minds are therefore key to reaching our goal.
</p>
<p>Join us and help create sustainable value through chemistry for customers all over the globe.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Clariant is seeking a global process owner Order-to-Cash and the single point of contact for a dedicated Business Segment. The position manages and drives the order management in the regions together with sales und customers until cash is on hand by implementing blue prints and standardization across regions. They manage all operational interfaces to optimize the short-term financial results of the dedicated Business Segment. The position also participates in the operational S&amp;OP for dedicated supply chains and coordinates all respective team activities.<br>
</p>
<p><br>
<b>Responsibilities<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>Global process owner Order to Cash for assigned Business Segment
</p>
<ul>
<li>Drives backlog performance and order adherence together with the regions
</li>
<li>Improves performance towards customer, implements OTIF performance and QNS
</li>
<li>Ensure cross-regional information flow and accurate reporting
</li>
<li>Drives and leads improvement projects
</li>
</ul>
<p>Coordination of processes
</p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible to harmonize processes globally across regions according to global best practices
</li>
<li>Drives for continuously optimization of Order-to-Cash, logistics and operational processes
</li>
<li>Key member of Center of Excellence and responsible for regional implementation of global processes and standards
</li>
<li>Responsible to manage internal and external interfaces (e.g. Sales, GBS)
</li>
<li>Ensure compliance with standards, policies &amp; processes as per best practice defined
</li>
</ul>
<p>Increase of efficiency
</p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible to meet quarterly BU Sales targets as part of the Order-to-Cash cycle for dedicated Business Segment
</li>
<li>Responsible to meet operational KPIs to defined service levels and business objectives
</li>
<li>Ensure appropriate documentation, SOP, etc. to meet internal and external audit requirements<br>
</li>
</ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Requirements<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s in business management, chemistry or engineering degree
</li>
<li>Experience with Supply Change Management (SCM) related Enterprise Resource Planning processes around Order-to-Cash
</li>
<li>Minimum of 5 years operative experience in one function of Customer Service
</li>
<li>Project and operative SCM management experience required
</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills and ability to work in a team<br>
</li>
</ul>
