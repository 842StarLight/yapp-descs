Rosemount™ Measurement &amp; Analytical, a business unit of Emerson Automation Solutions, is currently seeking an IT Manager. Oracle experience is required for this role.
</div>
<div></div>
<div>Rosemount™ is a signature brand of Emerson, offering a comprehensive line of measurement instrumentation, analyzers and systems which are used in a wide range of applications found in critical processing facilities such as refineries, chemical plants, power plants, pipelines, and more.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>SUMMARY STATEMENT</b></div>
<br>
<div>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>We are seeking a professional Oracle IT Manager, with an emphasis in <b>Quote, Order Management, Pricing and Finance</b>. A strong candidate will have 8 plus years of Oracle experience, across IT and the business, in the mentioned modules. The successful applicant will serve as a team manager and liaison between the business and IT departments while supporting IT projects as well as daily task monitoring and issue resolution. The ideal candidate for this position is detail-oriented, self-motivated, highly innovative and can work in a fast-paced matrixed environment.<br>
</div>
