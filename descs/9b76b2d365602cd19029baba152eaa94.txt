<h2>Graphic Designer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Trader Interactive’s Recreation division is seeking an entry-level/intermediate Graphic Designer to support the Sales Enablement Team in the Norfolk, VA office.
<br><br>
The ideal candidate for this role would be a highly-motivated, proactive individual who has a keen eye for design, and an understanding of how to appropriately use the Adobe Creative suite to create unique, on-brand, and captivating collateral which will be used to help close deals. This individual will need to be a self-starter who shows initiative and takes pride in the work produced. A Bachelor of Arts or Bachelor of Science in Visual Arts or Graphic Design and 1 - 2 years of experience is preferred.
<br><br>
<b>Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>BA/BS in Visual Arts, Graphic Design, or equivalent experience</li>
<li>Expertise in Adobe Creative Suite</li>
<li>An excellent eye for design and typography</li>
<li>A thorough understanding and use of Mac computer systems, Microsoft applications, and the Google Suite (including G-Mail, Hangouts, Google Docs, Google Sheets, Google Slides, etc.)</li>
<li>Strong attention to detail with the ability to work independently and meet deadlines</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills, both oral and written</li>
<li>An ability to handle multiple tasks/projects within a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>Time management, planning, and organizational skills</li>
<li>Excellent customer service skills</li>
<li>Strong work ethic</li>
<li>A positive, professional attitude and the ability to work cooperatively within the team environment.</li>
</ul>
<b>Primary Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Oversee the design and localization of presentations (i.e, Town Halls, sales pitch decks,) collateral, signage, newsletters, The Garage, and promotional materials as requested by the Recreational leadership and sales teams</li>
<li>Support Major Accounts, OEM, and banner ad creation</li>
<li>Provide on-demand creative support for sales programs and events</li>
<li>Consistently meet deadlines as set out by stakeholders and managers to ensure materials are delivered on time</li>
<li>Collaborate with Marketing, Sales, Support, and other stakeholders on the creation of key projects</li>
<li>Ensure consistency and integrity behind our company’s brand and brand guidelines as set out by the Brand Management Marketing team</li>
<li>Provide support to Brand Management Marketing team initiatives as time allows</li>
</ul>
This full time position is based in our home office in Norfolk, VA and will report directly to the Sales Enablement Marketing Manager.
