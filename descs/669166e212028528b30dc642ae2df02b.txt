<div>Ring is currently seeking a Trade Compliance Analyst to join a rapidly growing team. You will be part of the Ring Supply Chain team delivering on complex Trade Programs. As a member of Supply Chain, we are responsible for the classification of products in our database which facilitate the importation of our products safely meeting all local laws and regulations. We also prevent the sale of items that are prohibited by law or regulations. In the global trade and direct import areas we are accountable for the import tariff classification and ensure imported products have had proper testing for safety and operability.<br>
<br>
Responsibilities<br>
<ul>
<li>Perform regulatory research and write product rules based on existing regulatory assessment and checklists and well communicate with business partner;</li>
<li>Leverage technology to manage compliance processes, create and maintain regulatory content database</li>
<li>Build and strengthen the connection with internal teams and service providers</li>
<li>Be familiar with the policy and guidelines of e-commerce and import/export tariff; with strong understanding of product categorization and search engine methodologies</li>
<li>The ability to interpret the concept into executable database queries to categorize products from different perspectives</li>
<li>Act as point of contact for process-related/operational queries for associates within the team and will also work on business and stakeholder correspondence.</li>
<li>Support Import and Export Operations</li>
<li>Audit, track, maintain and report out on quality metrics.</li>
<li>Proficiency in communication skills to work with different functional teams to create/modify/update trade rules/regulations</li>
</ul>
