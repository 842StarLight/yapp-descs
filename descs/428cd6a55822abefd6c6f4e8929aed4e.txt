<p><b>The Job:</b></p>
<p>This mid-level position is 70% execution and 30% new brand concept and other brand extensions. This role will often support the Production Development of Packaging Design including but not limited to Packaging, Print, POGs Little Tikes &amp; Boys Creative Packaging Dept. This person usually becomes the main point person for that particular brand. This person in this role is generally responsible for package design through production on 1 or 2 core brands 70% of the time and works on other brands and / or new brand concepts 30% of the time.</p>
<ul>
<li>Working under the direction of the Production Manager and / or Director of Packaging, you will create packaging design concepts that support brand strategy.</li>
<li>Work with production team to ensure integrity of design in final deliverables.</li>
<li>Manage the timelines of the projects assigned to you.</li>
<li>Maintain a consistently high level of quality, thoroughness and attention to detail.</li>
<li>Effectively communicate all project related progress/issues or concerns to the Design Manager and to Project Management in a timely and professional manner.</li>
<li>Solicit creative direction from the Design Manager and / or Director of Packaging in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Develop marketing support materials, creative boards and layout presentations for new and existing business development presentations and trade shows.</li>
<li>Develop digital marketing assets where required.</li>
<li>Collaborate with Structural Engineering, Marketing, Quality Control and Product Development teams as well as overseas agents, as necessary, in the package development and approval process.</li>
<li>Maintain knowledge of new technologies within your area of expertise</li>
</ul>
<p>The ideal candidate has a strong design sensibility with an understanding of current printing techniques, attention to detail and overall quality. This role will create production ready artwork that delivers on the brand aesthetic, ensures products are engineered to cost target goals while proactively providing solutions to design challenges. This individual will demonstrate the ability to work collaboratively with cross functional teams including Packaging Design, Product Design, Marketing, Sales and Operations, while exhibiting a strong degree of ownership and accountability to deliver packaging at a high level of execution on time.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Qualified candidates will 5+ years of related work experience, be able to provide a strong design, retail and/or CPG and print and TOY packaging experience. Must understand visual schematics and visual merchandising. <b>Strong knowledge of Adobe Creative suite is required.</b></p>
<p>Required Skills:<br>
<br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Prior experience working with assigned client is highly preferred</li>
<li>At least 3-5 years production experience in a Design environment.</li>
<li>Minimum 3-5 years of experience in marketing/advertising/design studio environment.</li>
<li>Superior decision making, troubleshooting, negotiating, influencing and interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Conflict Management: approaches conflicts as opportunities; understands others quickly; can find common ground without compromising total position.</li>
<li>Ability to work well within a team environment.`</li>
<li>Ability to take direction well and apply it.</li>
<li>Ability to work and function in ambiguity.</li>
<li>Strong attention to detail.</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills.</li>
<li>Expert Mac OSX-based knowledge of Adobe CC.</li>
<li>Expert knowledge of Mac OSX fonts, both PostScript and OpenType.</li>
<li>Knowledge of color, b/w printers, Fiery or Splash RIP.</li>
<li>Expert knowledge of printing processes and building mechanical art, including knowledge of color separations and common prepress workflows.</li>
<li>Self-motivated team player; must be able to work efficiently with minimal supervision.</li>
<li>Great organizational skills, both physically and electronically, with capability to prioritize and manage own time. Assist other staff in prioritizing projects. Work closely with other business partners to ensure timely transfer of project materials to HK or Ohio Teams.</li>
<li>Design skills required to execute iterative mechanical versions.</li>
<li>Must be able to interpret and communicate creative direction and revisions to production partners.</li>
<li>Must be physically able to use both digital and traditional production tools, including but not limited to, X-acto blades, Xyron, foam core and other mounting substrates/materials. Regular and predictable attendance is an essential duty of this position.</li>
</ul>
