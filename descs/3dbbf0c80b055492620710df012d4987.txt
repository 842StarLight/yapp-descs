<h2>Implementation Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Assists external clients in the <b>implementation</b> of technical systems, software, hardware or solutions. Evaluates client needs, develops configurations that support business processes. Tests and troubleshoots final system setups. Provides training and end-user support during and after the <b>implementation</b> process.</p>
<p>
Job Description:</p>
<ul><li>Responsible for the successful completion of all client implementation activities in the most client friendly, expedient, and efficient manner possible</li></ul>
<ul><li>Responsible for managing all client relationships from the point of sales hand-off to the point after which client is successfully implemented.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Responsible for working with client to obtain initial test files, managing through development process, setting expectations, testing/QA of development work, training, and monitoring the first three client submissions, and obtaining client sign-off on proofed output data, and training process.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Attend weekly sales pipeline/implementation status review; responsible for reporting on implementation status.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Log all implementation activities and client nuances in company CRM application.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Proactively identify additional areas of opportunity with existing clients or referral business</li></ul>
<ul><li>Continually look for opportunities to improve the client experience or internal operations</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintain expert knowledge or company software applications and processes</li></ul>
<ul><li>Know and/or gain expert HIPAA Compliance knowledge</li></ul>
<p>
Location: Castle Rock, Colorado</p>
<p>
Background &amp; Drug Testing Required: Yes</p>
