<h2>Tech Support Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>This exciting and diverse role involves:</p>
<ul>
<li>Reporting to the Technical and R&amp;D Manager</li>
<li>Providing technical support via telephone, email and online chat to end-users, distributors and colleagues for our full range of chemistry products, including, but not limited to:
<ul>
<li>Pre-sale product selection/specifications
</li>
<li>Post sales enquires</li>
<li>Identifying spares, accessories, add-ons etc.</li>
<li>Assisting with product installation/start-up/set-up issues</li>
<li>Application support</li>
<li>Diagnosing faults</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Communication with suppliers and/or QA regarding any technical difficulties with supplied equipment</li>
<li>Testing products and product applications:</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Debugging, functional, and performance testing of products
</li>
<li>Setting up and testing systems, including scientific instruments, glassware, and software</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Providing support to the Marketing department, including technical advice, copy writing, blogs, instructions, FAQ’s etc
</li>
<li>Helping out other departments as and when required<br>
<br>
</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>About you</b></h3>
<p>The ideal candidate will have the following skills and experience:</p>
<ul>
<li>Scientific background via degree or similar level qualification (e.g. in chemistry, physical sciences, or via practical lab experience)
</li>
<li>Experience of working in a technical support role is beneficial, but not essential
</li>
<li>Hands-on laboratory skills preferred</li>
<li>Fully IT literate with standard Microsoft Office packages (e.g. Word, Excel, and PowerPoint)</li>
</ul>
<p>And have the following qualities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Excellent communication skills, both written and oral, with the ability to effectively interact pleasantly and positively with a wide range of internal and external contacts both in the UK and overseas
</li>
<li>Proactive and well organised with good attention to detail and ability to maintain high levels of accuracy
</li>
<li>Enthusiasm and perseverance with a positive ‘can do’ attitude
</li>
<li>Good prioritising skills as role is multi-functional and hectic at times
</li>
<li>Forward thinking always looking for ways to improve current systems and suggest new initiatives
</li>
<li>Diligent attitude with the determination to see things through to completion
</li>
<li>Capable of working as part of a team and able to use own initiative when working individually
</li>
<li>Aptitude to meet challenges that may arise
</li>
<li>Willing to get stuck in with hands-on work
</li>
<li>Good time keeper and reliable</li>
<li>Adaptable and willing to learn new skills<br>
<br>
</li>
</ul>
