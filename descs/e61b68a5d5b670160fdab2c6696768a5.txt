<p><b>CAE SoftSys, Inc.</b> offers a full complement of specialized engineering, technical and management services to the Energy Systems, Aeronautics, Space Science, Information Technology and Multi-Disciplinary Industries. CAE SoftSys professionals are engineers, engineering group managers, manufacturing managers and program managers with proven track records of successfully working with local and international operators, prime contractors, subcontractors, service providers as well as research community.

<p><b>Job Description :</b>
<br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Support in CAE's inhouse Aerospace engineering design and finite element analysis work using Catia, Nastran, Patran, Matlab, Simulink, and Abaqus.</li>
<li>Conduct static and dynamic structural analysis using Ansys and Nastran&amp; Patran</li>
<li>Provide technical support to customers for Aerospace engineering design tools: Catia, Matlab/Simulink</li>
<li>Conduct Training for Catia, Enovia, Matlab/Simulink, Abaqus,Nastran&amp;Patran software for Mechanical and Aerospace Design and Simulation engineers.</li>
<li>Build sustainable relationships with customer's Champions to facilitate access to customer's Executive levels.</li>
<li>Support in Technical Sales by explaining technical details of Aerospace software tools in order to transform Marketing and Sales leads (by sales engineer) into Opportunities.</li>
<li>Able to review and understand engineering documentation (drawings, specifications, instructions, etc).</li>
<li>Demonstration, training and customization of Aerospace Product Design, Development, Simulation, Testing and Validation software and tools from leading software vendors.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<br>
<br>
<p><b>Required Skills and Experience:</b>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 2+ Years of experience in the field of Aerospace or Mechanical engineering design, FEA, and simulation software tools e.g. Catia, Enovia, Delmia, Abaqus in design, development, simulation, and verification and validation.</li>
<li>The candidate should be able to provide technical supports on the tools to the customers.</li>
<li>The candidate should have minimum a Bachelor in Aerospace or Mechanical Engineering.</li>
<li>Having a Masters in Aerospace or Mechanical Engineering will eliminate the need for the work experience.</li>
<br>
</ul>
