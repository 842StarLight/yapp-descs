<h2>Business Systems Analyst (MES)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>We are searching for an experienced Business Systems Analyst (MES) at our Newburyport, MA facility.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The Business Systems Analyst provides IT departmental support to software and process improvement projects. This role directly collaborates with groups such as finance, sales, manufacturing and warehouse to understand their procedures, process flows and implement new features in ERP, MES, MRP, CRM, etc. systems for greater efficiency, control, productivity and speed. This role is to focus on the engineering, assembly, and manufacturing areas, especially with Manufacturing Execution Systems (MES), interfaces to ERP (job status, MRP, parts, BOMs), and ancillary systems (Yield management, job control, process optimization, maintenance, machine interfaces, data collection and IoT).</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL JOB FUNCTIONS</b></p>
 <ul>
<li>Works directly with business users and software vendors to research, evaluate, prioritize, and implement new and better software and features</li>
<li>
 Working with developers, support and service providers tom install and support software, interfaces, customization's, and reports</li>
<li>
 Participates actively with users in software selection, design, and implementation efforts</li>
<li>
 Manages software implementation and upgrade efforts, and IT-related user training</li>
<li>
 Understands and assists in process improvement programs for the Engineering, Assembly, Manufacturing, and Test functions</li>
<li>
 Develops dashboards, reports, and analysis tools to understand and improve process flows.</li>
<li>
 Provides team support, working with project managers and project teams.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>OTHER DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
 <p>Manages smaller side projects as assigned to evaluate vendors and tools, e.g. document control, dashboard and reporting tools, imaging solutions, interfaces, IoT, etc.</p>
<p>
 Performs additional duties, as assigned.</p>
