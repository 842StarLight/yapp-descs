<div><b>A ROLE YOU WILL LOVE</b></div>
<div>This position is responsible for converting prospective customers into new loan applications by presenting customers with appropriate mortgage products and pricing based on their borrowing needs. Borrowers will be primarily sourced through the SLS servicing and subservicing portfolio. MLO will sell the loan products through the use of inbound and outbound sales calls. Once the customer has applied for a loan, the MLO will manage the customer experience from origination through closing, ensuring a positive borrowing experience.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Primary Accountabilities
</b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>Sell multiple mortgage loan products to prospective borrowers with the primary goal of converting them into closed loan accounts.
</div></li>
<li><div>Manage active pipeline from origination to closing. Follow up with borrowers in a timely and efficient manner.
</div></li>
<li><div>Provide excellent customer service</div></li>
<li><div>Determine and communicate loan level pricing with customers
</div></li>
<li><div>Work effectively with fulfilment departments to ensure loan requirements are satisfied and deadlines are met
</div></li>
<li><div>Analyze underwriting guidelines to determine the documentation and locking/pricing requirements for each borrower.
</div></li>
<li><div>Efficiently work in the necessary technology platforms to maximize time spend selling.
</div></li>
<li><div>Work with management to develop and execute on sales plans to ensure the success of the business.
</div></li>
<li><div>Continually develop sales skills through product training and educational opportunities.
</div></li>
<li><div>Adhere to all federal and state laws and compliance guidelines.
</div></li>
<li><div>Learn various loan programs, processes and stay current with changes</div></li>
</ul>
