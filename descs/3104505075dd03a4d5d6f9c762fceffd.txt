<h2>Senior Smart Home Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>About the role:</b></p>
<p>
The Sr. Smart Home Project Manager is accountable for delivery of a first-class experience to clients that subscribe to PayLease’s Smart Home solution. This role will lead the project from contract to completion and act as a client's primary point of contact throughout. The primary focus is project management of the implementation of Zego Smart products and services for our clients. The Smart Home Project Manager is responsible for training property managers on the administration of the software and on-site staff on the installation, proper use, and ongoing maintenance of smart home hardware.</p>
<p><b>
Why you should work here:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>You’ll learn a best-in-class smart home implementation methodology</li>
<li>We invest in technology to enable Project Managers to focus on the highest value activities</li>
<li>Merit based promotion</li>
<li>The company culture is incredible</li>
<li>Our goal is to help you develop your skills, advance your career and increase your income</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What you’ll do here:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Full accountability for implementing smart home projects for clients. Onboard new clients/properties, including setup of new accounts in our system.</li>
<li>Create a project plan and adhere to it in order to ensure the full project scope is delivered and project milestones are met.</li>
<li>Coordinate client communication throughout the project including project kickoffs, information gathering, project setup and scheduling, on site training, project launch, and support hand off.</li>
<li>Process orders for smart home hardware. Ensure the proper equipment is ordered and sent to the site on schedule.</li>
<li>Collaborate with clients to provide strategic advice and guidance; use interactions to configure our software to meet their needs and exceed their expectations.</li>
<li>Will host on site and virtual training for clients on the use and administration of the Zego Smart solution as well as on the installation of hardware. Travel to customer sites is required; up to 40% travel.</li>
<li>Project manage installation of equipment at properties. This may include scoping the installation, hiring a subcontractor, negotiating pricing, quality assurance of the installation, and coordinating the on site installation.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>About you:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>3+ years of proven project management, sales, IT or similar experience</li>
<li>Prior experience at a software/tech/multifamily company</li>
</ul>
