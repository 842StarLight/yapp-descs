<h2>Production Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Join Ecolab at our flagship manufacturing site in Greensboro, North Carolina as our <b>Production Manufacturing Manager</b>. You will lead our chemical blend operation to produce the cleaning and sanitation products Ecolab makes for the global market. We seek someone who will think bigger than Manufacturing Manager, someone eager to get up every day and come to work, driven to develop a well-oiled machine who craves the opportunity to make a lasting contribution. Be part of Ecolab’s journey from good to great while we improve in every shape and form to become a World Class Supply Chain. If you thrive in your current role but hunger to leave a legacy, share your own World Class supply chain experience and lead your team on an unforgettable journey, training and coaching them to thrive in a changing Supply Chain culture then this challenge is a good fit for you!. With laser focus on SAFETY, SAVINGS, &amp; SERVICE we are in the midst of an integrated project that will create better visibility and better metrics to drive performance and give customers what matters to them most.<br>
<br>
Ecolab is the global leader in water, hygiene and energy technologies and services. Around the world businesses in foodservice, food processing, hospitality, healthcare, industrial, and oil and gas markets choose Ecolab products and services to keep their environment clean and safe, operate efficiently and achieve sustainability goals. Ecolab is currently a $14B Fortune 500 Company on track to be $20B by 2020 – supply chain enabled sales will be a big part of that growth.<br>
<br>
Lead your team as you follow TPM strategies and use Toyota Production System tools as you drive production to meet our safety, quality and production goals. With established TPM pillars we need a manager with TPM experience who enjoys developing people and seeing them grow in their careers. The right candidate will have excellent TEAM building and engagement skills and will be able to drive HPO throughout the Greensboro facility. The position requires that you have a keen knowledge of financial measurements of Operations and strong computer literacy. Gain recognition for your ability to develop and lead a World Class team by providing new leaders to fill succession opportunities.<br>
<br>
Is this an exciting opportunity? It is for the driven person who thrives on challenge, is willing to relocate for advancement opportunities, and has experience in World Class operations with a foundation in Toyota Production Systems and the pillars of Total Productive Maintenance. It’s the perfect opportunity to share your knowledge, advance your career and leave a lasting legacy with a leading global company recognized as one of the most ethical in the world, devoted to offering solutions to the world’s biggest challenges. Knock on the door, we’d like you to come in!<br>
<br>
<b>What You Will Do</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Evolve Ecolab’s World Class manufacturing safety culture; lead by example and manage the transition from safety is important to having a team where 100% of the employees are engaged 100% as we continue our Journey to Zero.</li>
<li>
Provide inspirational leadership to our operating teams as we build a TPM based High Performance Organization, building flexible, stronger, and more engaged operating teams.</li>
<li>
Deliver KPI’s in safety, quality, delivery, and cost through execution of key projects, programs, loss reduction, and through engaging 100% of our team toward our 2020 vision.</li>
<li>
Inspire the manufacturing team as you embrace Lean practices and use your ability to listen and communicate transparently to encourage every single person to think Lean and act proactively to make changes that will save time and reduce manufacturing waste.</li>
<li>
Lead Autonomous Maintenance pillar activities as a fundamental culture shift in our TPM journey.</li>
<li>
Strengthen and foster front-line leadership capability, impact, and sustainable operations.</li>
<li>
Manage the integration of capital projects with cross-functional team and respond to opportunities for process improvements.</li>
<li>
Integrate business partnership focus in strengthening cross-functional collaboration with RD&amp;E, Logistics, Suppliers, and Kay Business leaders.</li>
</ul>
<p>
<br>
<b>Location Information:</b> As our Production Manager you will work at our flagship plant: 8300 Capital Drive Greensboro, NC 27409. The Manufacturing Manager leads a diverse team of Team Leaders and indirect reports. The plant is a 250,000 sq ft site that operates 24/5 and manufactures approximately 115 formulas and 2400 SKUs. Products include Liquid, Powder, Extruded and Pressed Solids products used by businesses for cleaning and sanitation to promote clean water, abundant energy and safe food. The plant is FDA and EPA regulated, and is Halal and Kosher certified. The role requires willingness to be on call around the clock for critical manufacturing. This is a highly visible role where you will report to the Plant Manager and have interaction with divisional business leaders, Global Supply Chain functional leaders, and peers on the plant leadership team to continuously improve and develop the processes in production, safety, quality, planning, procurement and distribution.<br>
