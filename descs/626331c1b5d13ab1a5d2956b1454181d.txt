<h2>Marketing Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>JOB SUMMARY:</b> The Marketing Specialist is responsible for promoting First US Bank through the development and management of marketing initiatives, including enhancing the bank’s image through professional communication efforts.
<br><br>
<b>POSITION REQUIREMENTS:</b><br>
1. Bachelor’s degree in Marketing, Business, or related field or equivalent experience
<br><br>
2. Minimum of four years of marketing experience or equivalent training
<br><br>
3. Strong creative ability to develop ads/collateral materials promoting bank products/services
<br><br>
4. Excellent communication, negotiation, interpersonal and problem-solving skills
<br><br>
5. Ability to be a team-player in a leadership and support role
<br><br>
6. Excellent skills in managing multiple tasks requiring strong attention to detail
<br><br>
7. Results-oriented, flexible and demonstrated ability to organize and prioritize work assignments
<br><br>
8. Exceptional ability to write, proofread and edit written work
<br><br>
9. Computer aptitude, including knowledge of Microsoft Office Suite programs, graphic design programs and other software
<br><br>
<b>DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b><br>
1. Develops a marketing strategy supporting strategic plan initiatives across bank functions, including subsidiaries and related lines of business.
<br><br>
2. Develops an in-depth knowledge of the bank's products and services, including digital transaction and communication channels.
<br><br>
3. Facilitates the creation and production of marketing, collateral and business development packaging materials:
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Includes strong graphic design expertise for print ads, promotional collateral material and online/digital graphic promotional advertising.</li>
<li>Ensures a consistent branding image is maintained in all FUSB advertising/collateral materials.</li>
<li>Within the creative development scope, welcomes input for revisions based upon alternative objectives.</li>
</ul>
4. Coordinates advertising schedules and placement, as required for ads placed market publications, on-line and community program books, newsletters, etc.
<br><br>
5. Creates and distributes press releases highlighting noteworthy news events within the bank.
<br><br>
6. Reviews specifications sheets, obtains and prepares bids, and acts as Specialist with vendors when delivering marketing material and scheduling proofs and meetings.
<br><br>
7. Communicates with staff and outside vendors (e.g. designers, printers, mail houses, copy writers, and media ad providers) in coordinating the implementation of marketing promotions and projects.
<br><br>
8. Compiles and produces sales and marketing reports for assigned programs.
<br><br>
9. Assists in ongoing process improvement within First US Bank’s online website and creates online content for FUSB website.
<br><br>
10. Creates strategies and ideas to promote the bank through social media.
<br><br>
11. Tracks, collates and maintains inventory of marketing materials, as required.
<br><br>
12. Maintains inventory of photo work, artwork and media work, and maintains marketing collateral material, archive files and sample files. Ensures all photos and artwork are properly licensed.
<br><br>
13. Manages an ongoing budget to ensure prudent expense control.
<br><br>
14. Plays an active role as called upon for internal and external event planning and management. Assists with presentations as needed for bank meetings.
<br><br>
15. Assists Line of Business heads (Retail, Commercial, Mortgage) with the knowledge of ongoing community events, including but not limited to: market business development opportunities, chamber events, expositions, school business development opportunities and other client acquisition programs.
<br><br>
16. Works in close conjunction with Compliance to insure all marketing materials are compliant following regulatory guidelines. Ensures regulatory signage is compliant within the bank and banking centers.
<br><br>
17. Assists with marketing support for the mortgage service subsidiary.
<br><br>
18. Contributes to a positive team working experience within the bank.
<br><br>
19. May assist with additional projects as requested.
