<h2>Sales & Customer Success Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>DESCRIPTION OF DUTIES</b></p>
<p>At Buncee (https://www.edu.buncee.com/), our goal is to provide classrooms with a creation platform that makes learning fun, encourages creativity, and empowers student voice. Through Buncee (https://www.edu.buncee.com/), your goal is to help encourage teachers and students to reach their full potential and create a fun learning environment in the classroom. As Buncee's (https://www.edu.buncee.com/) Sales &amp; Customer Success Manager, you will be a primary sales advocate of Buncee's (https://www.edu.buncee.com/) suite of products to users especially in the education space. As the head of the sales team, you will also act as a primary point of contact for direct sales related initiatives and manage members of the sales team to convert qualified leads into lifelong customers. With your teammates, your role is to coordinate and execute strategic approaches to generate and close prospective clients worldwide.</p>
<p><b>Primary Responsibilities</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Strategize and manage sales targets and performance metrics in line with Buncee's business needs.</li>
<li>
Also act as an Account Executive &amp; Customer Success representative.</li>
<li>
Generate financial and/or sales analyses and prepare performance and sales reports for upper management.</li>
<li>
Work with the sales team to conduct and strategize various outreach communications (eg. phone, emails, social media) to generate qualified leads, opportunities, and introductory meetings in order to onboard users onto Buncee for Schools and Districts (as well as Buncee Classroom and Buncee Premium).</li>
<li>
Conduct product demonstrations with prospective clients either by hosting online training sessions, in-person meetings or in-class demonstrations, such as Professional Development (PD) Sessions.</li>
<li>
Actively seek and attend events as well as perform product pitches as needed.</li>
<li>
Coordinate with the Marketing Team to create online conversion strategies that onboard and convert users that sign up for Buncee's web products: Buncee Premium and Buncee Classroom.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with the Marketing Team to develop strategies that will cultivate enriching experiences for prospective clients to learn how to effectively use and implement Buncee's suite of education tools.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>CULTURE AND PERKS</b></p>
<p>We're looking for creative and fun individuals dedicated to empowering student voice and making learning fun. Every individual working at Buncee becomes an integral part of the team, and we support and rely on each and every one of our teammates. With offices located at WeWork in Manhattan and on the East End of Long Island in Calverton, we offer our employees the best of both worlds, whether it's taking advantage of the perks offered in our WeWork space in the city, or getting those creative juices flowing while tossing the football around in the tranquility of the East End. Our employees love company gatherings, whether it be at the beach, the arcade, or the bowling alley, and we ALL love birthdays....and cake!</p>
