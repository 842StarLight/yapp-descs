<h2>Software Sales Specialist - Red Hat Ansible Automation, CloudForms, and Satellite</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>At Red Hat, we connect an innovative community of customers, partners, and contributors to deliver an open source stack of trusted, high-performing solutions. We offer cloud, Linux, middleware, storage, and virtualization technologies, together with award-winning global customer support, consulting, and implementation services. Red Hat is a rapidly growing company supporting more than 90% of Fortune 500 companies.</p>
<br>
<p>
The Red Hat Management Sales Specialist team (formerly Ansible Automation Sales Specialist team) is looking for a Software Sales Specialist to join us in the Southern California or Phoenix area. In this role, you will be responsible for Red Hat Ansible Automation, Red Hat CloudForms, and Red Hat Satellite "management" software subscription sales offerings and services to prospective and current accounts. These accounts are both strategic and enterprise, primarily located in Southern California, Arizona, and Nevada. You’ll need to have the ability to understand customer needs and challenges and link them to a set of Red Hat Management offerings. This is a senior level sales position. Your tasks will include creating sales opportunities in both new and existing customer accounts through well-planned demand generation efforts. You’ll enable Red Hat's customers to define, develop, and deploy modern management strategies by collaborating with account executives, solutions architects, and our partner ecosystem. You will also produce success stories about key strategic projects that will be referenced internally throughout Red Hat. Successful applicants must reside in a state where Red Hat is registered to do business.<br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
Work within the sales teams to plan the overall automation and multi-cloud strategy within a defined set of accounts</li>
<li>
Gain the trust of senior IT and business executives within key accounts and create long-term partnerships resulting in customers becoming public references</li>
<li>
Ability to lead a consultative sales cycle, based on solutions selling, which includes the whole of Red Hat’s portfolio rather than specific offerings</li>
<li>
Build growth go-to-market strategies including partner and solutions ecosystem</li>
<li>
Meet sales expectations and set high standards for the communication and presentation of key messages to C-level executives, both internally and externally</li>
</ul>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
8+ years of experience in a solutions sales role focused on strategic and enterprise level accounts with a balance of strategic and tactical skills, as well as creative thinking skills</li>
<li>
4+ years of experience focused on enterprise automation systems like BladeLogic, Chef, or Puppet</li>
<li>
Broad technical experience with enterprise IT, focused on automating network and infrastructure services</li>
<li>
Excellent written and verbal communication skills, including experience presenting to C-level executives at large firms</li>
<li>
Ability to sell solutions in a multi-product environment</li>
<li>
Ability to work well in a matrixed-selling environment</li>
<li>
Proven ability to work with global cross-functional teams and achieve success for customers</li>
<li>
Ability to collaborate with Consulting and Training Services teams on large projects</li>
</ul>
