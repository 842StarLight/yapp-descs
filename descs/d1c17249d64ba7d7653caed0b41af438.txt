<p><b><i>Enterprise Performance - Project Delivery Specialist - SAP BPC Developer<br>
</i></b></p>
<p></p>
<div>Are you an experienced, passionate pioneer in technology – a solutions builder, a roll-up-your-sleeves technologist who wants a daily collaborative environment, think-tank feel and share new ideas with your colleagues - without the extensive demands of travel? If so, consider an opportunity within our Project Delivery Model (PDM).</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<p><b>Work you’ll do/Responsibilities
</b></p>
<ul><li> Deliver Finance Planning projects</li></ul>
<ul><li> Lead and support Finance pursuits and practice development initiatives</li></ul>
<ul><li> Develop external eminence across the sub-capabilities in Finance</li></ul>
<ul><li> Promote and participate in forums for sharing Finance expertise, strengthening Deloitte’s collective Finance knowledge and helping resolve our clients’ Finance challenges</li></ul>
<ul><li> Collaborate on innovative solutions to support the future of Finance</li></ul>
<ul><li> Develop your knowledge of and gain project experience in SAP’s new S/4HANA Finance and future solution</li></ul>
<br>
<div></div>
<p><b>The Team</b></p>
<p>Our SAP team focuses on design, implementation, and on-going operations and maintenance of SAP applications and technology. Professionals will have hands-on access and exposure to the latest technologies and thought leaders.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>We work with clients who want to improve their ability to react on digital world and enable scalability, remove performance barriers, enable innovation, and modernize core systems and reimagine their business with SAP digital solutions. Applications include SAP S/4HANA and Cloud (Hybris, Ariba, IBP, SuccessFactors, and SAP S/4HANA Public Cloud) across the following capabilities:</p>
<ul><li> Finance
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Supply Chain</li></ul>
<ul><li> Customer and Sales Operations
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Technology (Mobility and User Experience, Cloud and Virtualization, Data Management and Analytics)</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<p><b>Required</b></p>
<ul><li> At least six (6+) years of SAP experience implementing SAP BPC
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Functional/technical proficiency with at least 2+ full lifecycle SAP implementations and 4+ years of industry and/or consulting experience</li></ul>
<ul><li> Experience defining systems strategy, developing systems requirements, designing and prototyping, testing, training, defining support procedures and implementing practical business solutions under multiple deadlines</li></ul>
<ul><li> Adept at implementing technology-enabled business solutions for clients as part of a high-talent team and as a team lead or Project Manager on at least 1+ full cycle implementation</li></ul>
<ul><li> Implemented 2+ end to end planning project using BPC as a planning system</li></ul>
<ul><li> Develop custom information system reports and financial reports using complex EPM formulas, dimension member formulas and local members</li></ul>
<ul><li> Design and develop custom code to attain the complex requirements such as workforce calculations, Intercompany transactions eliminations at group level, currency conversions and consolidations at the group level</li></ul>
<ul><li> Advanced Excel skills are required to build complex EPM reports/input forms</li></ul>
<ul><li> Able to collaborate with clients and have a strong desire to excel and be committed to gaining exposure to multiple industries while further developing your career</li></ul>
<ul><li> Strong configuration and design skills
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Bachelor’s degree or equivalent required</li></ul>
