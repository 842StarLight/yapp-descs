<h2>Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>JOB<br>
SUMMARY:</p>
<p>The Project Manager is responsible for coordinating and planning turnkey projects with Sales, Engineering, and Service,<br>
in a pre and post-sales environment.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>ESSENTIAL<br>
FUNCTIONS:</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Evaluate and select project management software that integrates with salesforce.</li>
<li>Develop Standard Sales /Engineering / Turnkey / Installation / Drawings / Open issues</li>
<li>Status / Accounting / Final Payment Template within PM software for all projects.</li>
<li>Manage all projects within the PM Software and work together with internal departmental team members to<br>
close projects &amp; keep up to date continuously.</li>
<li>Weekly meetings internally and with customers / sales/ service to discuss open issues and project status<br>
etc.</li>
<li>Evaluate Nexteer &amp; AAM drawing format / system process to increase efficiency and completion.</li>
<li>Develop &amp; work with local vendors to supply / make needed turnkey parts, chucks, jaws, tooling,<br>
sheet metal, machine painting etc.</li>
<li>Develop &amp; work with local vendors to supply turnkey peripheral device solutions. Special machines,<br>
loaders, unloaders, tables, gauges etc.</li>
<li>Keep track of all costs /problems / challenges/ Delays within software that can be queried to check profitability and elicit necessary change in a professional way to improve sales, internal processes &amp; profitability.</li>
<li>Support VP of Sales &amp; Service and sales &amp; service teams.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned by Manager.</li>
<br>
 </ul>
<p></p>
<p>EXPERIENCE<br>
AND BACKGROUND:</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Bachelor of Science degree in Mechanical Engineering preferred</p>
<p>Five+ years Project Management experience in a turnkey environment</p>
<p>Working knowledge of the machine tool industry</p>
<p>Technical knowledge of the function and applications of CNC lathes</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>GENERAL<br>
REQUIREMENTS:</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Verbal and Written Communication Skills. Mental requirements include but are not limited to the ability to concentrate, take<br>
initiative, cope with stress, adapt to and stay alert in a business environment, and to use independent judgment to accomplish results. Walking,<br>
balancing, standing, turning, climbing, stooping, crouching, kneeling, sitting, reaching, lifting (at least 25 pounds), carrying, pushing, handling, feeling,<br>
talking, hearing, seeing depth perception.</p>
