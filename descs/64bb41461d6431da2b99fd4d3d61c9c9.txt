<h2>Mortgage Loan Officer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div><div><div>
<p>First Northern Bank, a locally owned community bank established in 1910, has 10 banking offices serving Yolo, Solano, Sacramento, Placer, with a reach into neighboring El Dorado and Contra Costa counties.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The financial strength, longevity and product diversity, typically associated with larger financial institutions, combined with a superior level of personal service make First Northern Bank a perfect choice for individuals and small to medium-sized businesses, professionals, builders and agribusinesses.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Employees of First Northern Bank are committed to their customers and to the communities in which they live and work. The “First Northern Bank Difference” can be experienced everyday through friendly personalized attention, local decision making, customized financial solutions and a visible passion for community involvement.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Summary
</b></p>
<p>Responsible for originating residential real estate loans for First Northern Bank. Solicits new business relationships with local realtors/referral sources. Calls on loan customers and prospective customers to sell the bank’s real estate products and services.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Job Duties and Responsibilities
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Solicit residential mortgages through contacts with realtors, developers and other referral sources/centers of influence.</li>
<li>Demonstrate sound financial acumen to assist in determining whether a loan will be an acceptable risk by obtaining and analyzing pertinent financial and credit data.
</li>
<li>Ability to travel to and attend community and professional events during the day, evening and/or weekends.</li>
<li>Ability to prepare and effectively facilitate presentations.</li>
<li>Provide a high level of service to customers and prospects seeking residential mortgage loans.</li>
<li>Cross sell bank products.</li>
<li>Provide sales and product training to branches, as needed.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>

<div><div>

<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Proven business development skills and ability to meet sales objectives.</li>
<li>Strong verbal and written communication skills with the ability to tailor communications based on the audience.</li>
<li>Demonstrate understanding of various mortgage loan programs.</li>
<li>High school diploma or equivalent.</li>
<li>BA in Marketing, Sales, Real Estate or related field preferred.</li>
</ul>
