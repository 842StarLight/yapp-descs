<h2>Sales Director, West</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Utilant, the provider of the P&amp;C insurance industry’s premier loss control survey management SaaS platform, is seeking to hire four exceptional Sales Directors to accelerate our growth trajectory in the United States.</p>
<p>
We are looking for experienced Sales Directors who are dynamic, curious and passionate, willing to make a difference leveraging innovation, while achieving or exceeding quota by selling Utilant’s software and/or SaaS solution.</p>
<p><b>
Summary</b><b>:</b></p>
<p>
Loss Control and Premium Audit is an essential part of a P&amp;C insurance carrier’s underwriting program where they use a combination of physical inspections and virtual data sources to avoid claims, verify premiums and enhance the customer experience.</p>
<p>
Utilant’s <b>Loss Control 360</b> acts as a P&amp;C insurance carrier’s platform for streamlining the entire loss control and premium audit process. Seamless integration with core applications such as Underwriting, policy administration, claims, data warehouses, and imaging systems, allows for automated ordering of surveys, as well as in-depth data mining and the production of key metrics, to reduce losses and improve performance through the use of one global platform configurable to the needs of the P&amp;C insurance carriers, brokers and inspection firms.</p>
<p>
Since being founded in 2005 Utilant has developed an impressive customer list including Allianz, Aviva, Chubb, Nationwide, State Auto, the two largest global brokers and many of the leading inspection firms. A key indicator of our momentum is that Utilant has signed four tier-1 carriers so far this year!</p>
<p>
Utilant is now inviting applications from experienced enterprise software Sales Directors who have a demonstrated history of overachieving targets and sales success to join the team and enjoy:</p>
<ul>
<li>Extra-large territories</li>
<li>A generous comp plan with aggressive accelerators</li>
<li>A great-looking product which demonstrates well</li>
<li>A highly satisfied customer base with extremely high renewal rates</li>
<li>An under-served market that is just realizing the benefits of analytics, data and digital</li>
</ul>
