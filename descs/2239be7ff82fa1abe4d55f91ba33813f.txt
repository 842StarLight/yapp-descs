<h2>Market President</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Description:</b><br>
<b>Summary:</b> As the senior person in the market, this individual manages the community bank office, and is responsible for all aspects of commercial lending activity. Directs business development, staff development, customer satisfaction, community leadership, promotes corporate culture, goals, and objectives. Assists bank President in corporate planning for market to set mutually agreed upon market position growth and profitability targets.
<br><br>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities include the following. Other duties may be assigned.
<br><br>
1. Creates and maintains a sales and referral focus to include the delivery of all products and services to customers. Direct and oversee the function to ensure that the goals and objectives are achieved.
<br><br>
2. Ensures effective day to day operation of the market including compliance with regulations, effective service, and profitable operations within budgetary guidelines.
<br><br>
3. Works closely with Administration to ensure effective delivery of banking services to the bank's customers.
<br><br>
4. Directs and oversees the Commercial Lending function for the market to develop a high quality and profitable portfolio.
<br><br>
5. Monitors market performance and provide input on pricing, target markets, and product mix for the respective market. Direct marketing efforts of subordinate staff to achieve market goals for loans and deposits.
<br><br>
6. Interprets, directs, or implements bank policies and procedures in accordance with applicable state, federal, and local laws and regulations.
<br><br>
7. Serves on bank committees as directed.
<br><br>
8. Identifies needs and assist with correcting problems in areas such as operations, Human Resources, service delivery, compliance, etc.
<br><br>
9. Provides leadership and participate in appropriate community/civic activities to enhance the bank's presence, image, and reputation in the community.
<br><br>
10. Develops and maintains a high level of employee morale in the market.
<br><br>
11. Exercises the usual authority in the management of assigned staff.
<br>a. Evaluate performance of immediate officers.
<br>b. Review subordinate recommendations/decisions on staff performance issues, salary issues, promotions, terminations, etc., in conjunction with Human Resources.
<br>c. Provide guidance, counseling, coaching, and motivation as necessary/appropriate.
<br>d. Develop personnel based on capacity, performance, initiative, and interest for future positions.
<br><br>
12. Maintains a good working rapport with other (operational) departments to assure smooth workflow.
<br><br>
13. Refers business to other functional areas as appropriate.
<br><br>
14. Maximizes the sales of all company products and/or service either directly or through referrals.
<br><br>
15. Accompanies staff on business development calls to generate new banking relationships, and to continually coach them on proper calling techniques.
<br><br>
16. Works with Retail Manager and Marketing department to define localized marketing requirements in the defined market area complementing the centralized marketing efforts of the company as a whole. This would include defining a plan to develop a high profile for the market by whatever means may be appropriate for the area, including localized direct mail, local sponsorships of events, representation in community organizations, etc.
<br><br>
17. Provides input and assists in the preparation of, and adherence to, annual budgets for activities conducted in the defined market area.
<br><br>
18. Adheres to all State and Federal Banking regulations.
<br><br>
