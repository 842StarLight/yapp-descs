<p><b>Position:</b> Site Marketing Coordinator</p>
<p><b>Location:</b> Capstone On-Campus Management (COCM) Headquarters</p>
<p><b>City / State: </b>Birmingham, AL</p>
<p><b>Reports To:</b> Vice President of Operations</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Compensation:</b> $50,000 - $60,000 (salary commensurate on experience) and competitive benefits package, including health benefits, dental benefits, vision coverage, and 401K.</p>
<p>Capstone On-Campus Management (COCM), with headquarters in Birmingham, Alabama, is a group of student housing professionals focused exclusively on the management of housing communities owned by non-profits and/or highly affiliated with a university.</p>
<p><b>Position Description:</b></p>
<p>The primary responsibility of the Site Marketing Coordinator is to assist with and oversee site level leasing and marketing initiatives for both new (development and takeover) and existing communities, where requested. Areas of responsibility include, but are not limited to, development of marketing plans and processes; competitive property survey processes; oversight of start-up development marketing/licensing initiatives; site level social media leadership and coordination; website review; online reputation management; site staff training and on-site support, when needed; marketing project planning and implementation; and, site level marketing material development oversight, review and approval.</p>
