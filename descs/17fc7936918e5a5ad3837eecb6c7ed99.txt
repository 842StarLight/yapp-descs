<h2>Assistant Ticket Office Manager/Ticket Office Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div><div><div>
<p>The Air Force Academy Athletic Corporation located in Colorado Springs, CO is looking for an Assistant Ticket Office Manager to join our team.</p>
<p>This position will perform the following duties:</p>
<ul>
<li>In cooperation with the Director of Ticket Operations and Assistant Director of Ticket Operations, supervises the overall operation of the Athletic Ticket Office by working with AudienceView computer ticketing system to assist with order entry (phone and walk up sales) and customer inquiries regarding tickets and donation accounts</li>
<li>Responsible for maintaining events and build reporting in ticketing software
</li>
<li>Manage game day Ticket Office for Volleyball and Wrestling</li>
<li>Supervises game day part-time staff and is responsible for the hiring, mentoring, training, scheduling, daily communication etc.
</li>
<li>Assist in managing the Ticket Office as needed in support of AFAAC outside activities (concerts, NHL Winter Classic, etc).
</li>
<li>Be a champion of the AudienceView ticket platform and train and/or assist other AFAAC users in using the platform to the best of their abilities</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned
</li>
</ul>
<p>Consistently conduct business in a professional manner, including dress and manner of speaking/writing when dealing with fans, donors, alumni, and students. Use time management skills to complete work assignments and meet deadlines before busy sales times and/or game days. Attend community events as needed with the marketing and sales staff to promote Air Force Academy Athletics.</p>
