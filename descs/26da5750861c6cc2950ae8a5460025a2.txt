<h2>Revenue Staff Accountant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Global provider of market leading solutions. Dynamic team culture. Competitive bonus programs. Collaborative cross-functional teams. World class benefits. Challenging and Rewarding work.</p>
<p>
At Aderant, we power the world’s leading law and professional services firms. We focus on delivering world class products and services which are developed, marketed, sold, and supported by superior talent. The expertise and talent of our people have made us the largest independent provider of law firm technology in the world, and our people will drive our success in the future.</p>
<p>
As the Revenue Staff Accountant for Aderant, you will help define Aderant's revenue through revenue recognition, customer contract review, order management, and revenue related analysis. This position calls for strong analytical skills, as you will be tasked with managing the revenue stream from client contracts utilizing the new revenue standards under ASC 606.</p>
<p>
<b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Keep current on the application of Revenue recognition pronouncements.</li>
<li>Prepare reports and analysis related to the Revenue function.</li>
<li>Prepare Balance Sheet account analysis/reconciliations.</li>
<li>Review customer contracts and approval of sales opportunities</li>
<li>Assist with account support</li>
<li>Ensure timely invoicing of license and maintenance to terms in contract</li>
<li>Responsible for maintenance renewals</li>
<li>Assist with month end closing and annual audit</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Skills &amp; Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in Accounting</li>
<li>Minimum of 2+ years of accounting experience</li>
<li>Accuracy and attention to detail</li>
<li>Analyze and interpret data</li>
<li>Plan work and meet deadlines</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal and communication skills</li>
<li>Ability to successfully deal with questions and resolve issues</li>
<li>Customer service orientation (both internal and external)</li>
<li>Ability to learn quickly</li>
<li>Problem-solving skills</li>
<li>Solid working knowledge of GAAP, SOX and accounting standards and practices</li>
<li>Intermediate Microsoft Office Suite (Excel, Word, Outlook) experience</li>
<li>Experience with computerized accounting systems; knowledge of SFDC, &amp; Intacct is a plus</li>
<li>Experience with software companies is a plus</li>
<br>
</ul>
