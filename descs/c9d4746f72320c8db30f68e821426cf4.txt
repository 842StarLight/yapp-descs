<h2>Digital Art Director NYC</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div><div>
<div><div>Businesses that work with Hylink come in all shapes and sizes. Our key partners range from agencies, advertisers, and major international brands. As the Digital Art Director, you will own the development and strategy for online advertising and digital marketing. You'll collaborate closely with senior clients and agency representatives, to guide and develop artistic production with innovative interactive tools, fresh visual graphics and effective ideas. Moreover, you will develop a strong understanding of your clients and agencies in order to redefine the digital world. Among your many attributes, you are persuasive, gregarious, a natural leader and skilled at solving problems and explaining complex issues. You possess excellent communication and presentation skills at the senior level as well as the ability to work closely and constructively with all areas of the business. You're particularly adept at collaborating with various elements putting new creations into the digital realm. Your knowledge of agency management, online media combined with your communication skills and analytical abilities shapes how new and innovative digital ideas will be. Using your influencing and relationship-building skills, you provide Hylink-caliber strategy, ideation, art direction and planning. You anticipate how decisions are made, persistently explore and uncover the artistic needs of Hylink's key clients and understand how our range of products offerings can grow their business. Working with them, you set the vision and the strategy for how their advertising can reach thousands of users.</div></div>
