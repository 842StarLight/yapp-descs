<b>Position Summary:</b><br>
Amobee is looking for a Product Manager with expertise in digital advertising, based in its Redwood City, CA office, to power the growth of its buying platform and DSP suite. This position involves working effectively in a cross functional environment to deliver high quality products. The product manager will work very closely with other product managers, sales, product marketing and an engineering team that is globally distributed.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Perform competitive analysis, gather market requirements, author market requirement and product requirement documents, drive product development with engineering teams, assist with product marketing, and provide pre-sales support and post-sales training while managing trade-offs and thoughtfully delivering solutions to complex problems.</li>
<li>Interface with the client-facing teams to understand the business requirements and use cases</li>
<li>Define product roadmap, success metrics and set expectations with all relevant stakeholders including engineering and internal leadership teams</li>
<li>Act as product lead in agile development teams, including sprint grooming, planning, feature/bug prioritization, and requirement definition</li>
<li>Advocate and educate internal teams regarding new product features</li>
<li>Build relationships with customers to garner feedback for development of product offerings. Present products to internal and external customers at a senior level.</li>
<li>Be the visible advocate for your products internally and externally.</li>
<li>Contribute to product, go-to-market, and corporate strategy</li>
<li>Define and prioritize user stories and product requirements for new features</li>
<li>Work closely with product marketing, sales, engineering, program management, and international teams to deliver the API products globally</li>
<li>Ensure smooth release process, release notes, user documentation, and sales enablement</li>
</ul>
<b>Required Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Self-starter, bias towards action, detail, punctuality and follow through</li>
<li>5-8 years of product management experience</li>
<li>BS in analytic or engineering-related field; MS in a quantitative or analytical discipline desired</li>
<li>Technical skills and demonstrated direct experience interacting with a technical audience, including software engineers and web developers</li>
<li>Experience in managing multiple projects in a fast-paced, entrepreneurial environment</li>
<li>Exceptional written, verbal communication and presentation skills</li>
<li>Strong organizational skills, with the ability to manage and collaborate with different groups and personalities to achieve progress</li>
</ul>
<b>Preferred Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Experience in Digital creative, API based buying, mobile, location based or Out Of Home (OOH) advertising</li>
<li>Prior experience with agile/incremental development approaches and cross-functional work environments</li>
</ul>
<b>Location:</b> Redwood City, CA
