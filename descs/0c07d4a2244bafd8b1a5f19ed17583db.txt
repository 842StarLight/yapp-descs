<div>Headquartered in Louisville, KY, Schneider Electric's Energy &amp; Sustainability Services (ESS) division addresses clients' diverse energy and sustainability management needs. Experts within the division provide integrated solutions to meet clients' energy supply, efficiency and sustainability challenges. With an expanding global footprint, Energy &amp; Sustainability Services includes a team of more than 2,000 energy professionals in offices around the globe. www.schneider-electric.com<br>
<br>
<br>
<br>
<b>Position Summary:<br>
</b><br>
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<p>The <b>Associate Client Development Manager </b>(CDM) will serve as part of the America’s National Sales organization and is responsible for helping future-Clients succeed by leading them on their Active Energy Management journey and working collaboratively with customers to develop Solutions and Business Cases focused on recurring energy management, data and reporting se rvices. This role will be based out of our Louisville, Kentucky office .<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The ideal candidate would be familiar with CRM tools such as Salesforce.com and possess strong consultative selling skills, with the proven ability to engage with influencers and decision makers to communicate the Schneider Electric value proposition around Active Energy Management.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Additionally, you will research, define and target specific market segments as assigned to enable a high level of sales success with targeted clients and prospects. Integrity, vision, passion and ability to excel in a collaborative sales environment are essential for this role.<br>
</p>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<b>What do you get to do in this position?<br>
</b><br>
<br>
</div>
<p></p>
<ul><li>You will have the opportunity to shadow a Client Development Manager and become familiar with the various aspects of the Client Development team at ESS.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Establish new business opportunities with targeted segments and accounts through prospecting, networking, qualifying and closing techniques.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Prospecting and converting leads into profitable and ongoing business relationships.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Working with Schneider Electric’s portfolio of offerings to develop customized solutions that address the customer’s business needs.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Understand customer long-term strategic business needs and increase level of contact– higher and wider – within customer’s organization to allow for selling of new opportunities.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Effectively advise clients on Schneider Electric’s software, services and product offerings.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Lead the development of sales proposals. Enlists the support of Sales Operations, implementation resources, delivery resources, and other sales and management resources as needed in response to RFP's.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Negotiating and closing contracts for energy management related services.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Collaboration with colleagues, including sales, marketing and operations teams, on ways to continuously improve offerings and go to market strategies.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Work collaboratively with customers to develop Solutions and Business Cases that incorporate ESS Services including Schneider’s award-winning Resource Advisor™ SaaMS offerings.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Communicate and interact with business decision-makers at all levels to identify needs and requirements as well as market opportunities.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Lead and move prospects through the sales cycle.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Close an agreed upon number of sales and revenue per year.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintain prospect (CRM – Salesforce.com) database information Essential Skills and Qualifications.
</li></ul>
