<h2>Inside Sales and Administration</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Company Description: :</b>Sampla Belting is an international manufacturing company headquartered in Italy with offices around the world and local operations in Lackawanna, NY. We manufacture, fabricate, and distribute lightweight conveyor belts and their accessories. The Lackawanna office is staffed by growth driven, youthful, energetic, and motivated employees.</p>
<p><b>Job Description: Inside Sales :</b>An Inside Sales associate sells industrial products and performs customer service to customers across the US via distribution in a business to business environment. The Inside Sales Associate services and develops existing accounts as well as acquires and grows new accounts. Other duties include phone work, customer service, order entry, invoicing, updating inventory, creating manifests for shipments, creating spreadsheets, updating the CRM system, running errands, and other duties as assigned.Integrity, reliability, good work ethics, taking pride in your work, drive, hunger for sales, and a positive attitude are the foundation of the position. Commitment to all company policies, including attendance and safety are essential. Reliable transportation to work is required.</p>
<p><b>Please be aware that a final step in hiring is for final candidates to arrange reference calls with former managers and others. Any job offer will be conditional on a criminal background check, drug test, as well as satisfactorily completing a number of employment aptitude tests including basic mathematics and writing.:</b> <b>Reports to:</b></p>
<div>
<ul><li>Operations Manager &amp; General Manager</li></ul>
</div>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<div>
<ul><li>Establish, develop, and maintain business relationships with current customers and prospective customers in the assigned territory to generate new business for the organization</li></ul>
<ul><li>Make sales and perform customer service via primarily phone and email</li></ul>
<ul><li>Research for prospective customers and determine their potential</li></ul>
<ul><li>Coordinate sales with workshop production, outside suppliers, and the inside sales team</li></ul>
</div>
<div>
<ul><li>Supply management with reports on customer needs, leads, problems, competitive activities, and potential for new products</li></ul>
<ul><li>Keep up to date product applications, technical services, market conditions, competitive activities, advertising and promotional trends through the reading of pertinent literature and consulting with marketing and technical service areas</li></ul>
<ul><li>Quoting customers, entering orders, updating the CRM, creating proforma invoices and shipping manifests, and other customer service functions</li></ul>
<ul><li>Any other duties as assigned</li></ul>
</div>
