<h2>Senior Director, Global Marketing Programs & Demand Generation</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Reporting to the Senior Vice President of Marketing, the Senior Director of Global Marketing Programs &amp; Demand Generation is responsible for leading the strategy, development and delivery of demand generation programs to all global target audiences to drive awareness and generate net new opportunities as well as cross-sell and account growth. He/she manages the following teams: Global Events, Content Marketing, and Digital Marketing - and partners closely with his/her counterparts in Product Marketing, Creative and Corporate Communications.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Duties/Responsibilities</b></p>
<div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Work with SVP Marketing and Sales leaders around the globe to help define the right top-line campaigns to support their business and revenue targets</li>
<li>Connect Product Marketing priorities with sales success by defining, instrumenting, measuring and executing end to end marketing campaigns.</li>
<li>Measure effectiveness of campaign activities and return on marketing investment (ROMI) and build integrated dashboards to share with peers and senior leadership.</li>
<li>Manage and optimize budget spend across demand generation and events</li>
<li>Manage Global events team to ensure smooth execution and optimization of demand generation potential.</li>
<li>Manage the Digital and Content Marketing teams to optimize the corporate website to increase inbound user engagement and conversion.</li>
<li>Work in partnership with Marketing Operations on lead flow and mapping, Pardot-to-Salesforce integration, quality assurance of marketing assets, segmentations, campaign setup and management.</li>
<li>Partner with channel marketing team to ensure seamless multi-channel execution</li>
<li>Manage team to develop and execute integrated marketing campaigns</li>
<li>Measure and monitor sales funnel activity to ensure predictable pipeline.</li>
<li>Facilitate a strong working relationship with all key counterparts including Sales, Product Marketing, the Creative Team, and Corporate Communications.</li>
<li>Analyze and segment customer data to help define prospects and customers with high engagement and revenue growth potential</li>
<li>Establish goals and KPIs for all demand generation programs based on historical or projected results, and tie back actual performance and revenue impact.</li>
<li>Test new tools to better automate and scale programs, with an emphasis on integrated marketing.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned
</li>
</ul>
</div>
<p><b>Knowledge, Skills and Abilities Required</b></p>
<div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrated expertise &amp; success in developing and executing high value marketing programs globally in support of Sales budgets greater than $250M a year</li>
<li>Demonstrate fluency in modern marketing practices including social media marketing, account-based marketing, SEO, SEM, retargeting, cross-sell and up-sell campaign planning, etc.</li>
<li>Demonstrated financial and budgeting acumen with corresponding financial accountability.</li>
<li>Demonstrated experience attracting, retaining, motivating and developing a high-performing marketing team</li>
<li>Demonstrated proficiency directing managers of multiple, diverse projects, and/or processes.</li>
<li>Demonstrated expertise to influence and negotiate with divergent internal and external contacts</li>
<li>Strong understanding of brand principles and audience segmentation.</li>
<li>Possesses strong competition and industry knowledge or proven ability to get up to speed quickly in new verticals</li>
<li>Strong data analysis skills</li>
<li>Previous experience in the security software or services space a plus.</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills and the ability to work with a broad range of stakeholders</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to maneuver political environment with high level of visibility.</li>
<li>Must thrive in working in a fast-paced, dynamic environment</li>
</ul>
</div>
<p><b>
Education and/or Experience</b></p>
<div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>8-10+ years of experience leading and managing global demand generation programs, preferably involving enterprise software and/or SaaS services.</li>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in Business, Marketing, or related field or an equivalent combination of education and experience. Master’s Degree preferred.</li>
<li>Data-driven marketer who possesses strong business acumen, attention to detail, and a bias for action</li>
<li>Experience working with Salesforce.com and Pardot</li>
<li>Experienced in driving website traffic and converting traffic into qualified leads.</li>
<li>Experienced in funnel optimization and lead scoring based on sales leadership collaboration.</li>
</ul>
</div>
