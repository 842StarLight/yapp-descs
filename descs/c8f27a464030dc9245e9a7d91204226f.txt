<h2>Sr Product Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Have you watched Game of Thrones on www.hbo.com or live broadcasting of FIFA World Cup on www.espn.com? If the answer is yes, you have already used Conviva technologies.</p>
<p>
Conviva is the real-time decisioning platform for optimized streaming entertainment. More than 250 industry leaders across 5 continents – including CBS, DAZN, HBO, Hulu, PlayStation™Vue, Sky, Sling TV, Turner, and Univision – rely on Conviva to maximize their consumer engagement, deliver the quality experiences viewers expect, and drive revenue growth. With a global footprint of 100 billion streams per year across 3 billion applications streaming on devices, Conviva offers streaming providers unmatched scale for continuous video measurement, intelligence, and benchmarking across every second of every stream on every screen.</p>
<p>
Conviva is seeking a Senior Product Marketing Manager (PMM) to develop, execute and bring to life product marketing strategies and initiatives for Conviva’s Ad Insgihts and Experience Insights products for TV publishers, advertisers and the digital video ecosystem. In addition to positioning, messaging and go-to-market effectiveness, you will be responsible for implementing a broad array of deliverables and initiatives in support of the products you own. You’ll also be part of a team ensuring alignment and promotion across our entire portfolio of data-driven solutions and artificial intelligence (AI)-powered platform. This role reports to the VP of Product Marketing.</p>
<p>
The ideal candidate for this role will be a revenue-motivated, strategic marketing professional, someone who can partner effectively across the organization to differentiate Conviva products, drive market demand and fuel customer acquisition, activation, engagement and retention. The successful PMM will be able to respond quickly to changing business needs, possess deep understanding of digital video content and the streaming advertising ecosystem, digital video delivery (CDNs, ISPs, networks) technologies and digital video advertising (ad delivery chain, ad servers, programmatic advertising, client and server-side ad insertion). Proven success launching and growing quality products and partnering with sales, business development, product management, corporate marketing, finance and senior management to achieve business goals are critical. Direct experience in streaming digital video publishing and monetization, delivery, measurement and advertising are a plus.</p>
<p><b>
What you get to do</b></p>
<ul><li>
<b>
Be the expert on your products</b>: Build deep knowledge of the market, customers, competitors, influencers and partners to guide content creation and GTM strategies. Be the thought leader on industry trends, key use cases, and business benefits. Establish yourself as the go-to source through product &amp; sales collateral, ROI analyses, presentations, web copy, whitepapers, demos, blogs, webinars, etc.</li></ul>
<ul><li>
<b>
Lead Go-To-Market.</b> For both new and next generation products, work closely with stakeholders &amp; peers across the organization to craft detailed launch plans including product fit to market, messaging, pitches, packaging and pricing, marketing materials and rollout schedules. Lead execution throughout the full new product introduction lifecycle, including early-access, public announcement, GA &amp; early market adoption.</li></ul>
<ul><li>
<b>
Enable Sales.</b> Develop the “what we make” and “what we make possible” stories that make our value propositions compelling. Arm sales with what they need to accelerate pipeline growth, close deals and shorten time-to-market. Ensure frequent two-way communication. Share critical knowledge and tools: demos, presentations and pitch decks, demos, competitive battle cards, training materials, FAQs and case studies. Join the field for customer pitches, meetings and QBRs to gather and act on new insights.</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>
<b>
Drive Growth-Market Strategies</b>: Define, refine and monitor product KPIs through critical growth and expansion phases. Work with solution consulting and product management teams to iterate on messaging and pitches based on customer and market feedback and progress against corporate/revenue goals. Develop new use cases, build customer references and success stories, represent Conviva at industry events, and take proactive steps to make it easier for customers to evaluate, choose and demand us.</li>
<li>
<b>
Define New Market Requirements</b>. Develop strong business cases for product initiatives with detailed market requirement documents (MRDs). Work closely with product managament team on roadmap planning.</li>
<li>
<b>
Analyze the Market.</b> Perform continual assessment of competitive landscape, develop and refine competitive positioning to inform all stakeholders in the company. Stay on top of digital video publishing and delivery ecosystem, ad tech and general technology trends; translate them into POVs, educate product and business teams, devise ways to assimilate trends internally.</li>
</ul>

What you bring to the role</b></p>

You’re exceptional at making findings actionable for sales, product and marketing, as well as using the information to inform your go-to-market and growth strategies.</li>

Commitment to results</b>: Ideal candidate is someone who takes initiative, hates to lose for any reason, and drives projects to completion with minimal supervision.</li>
