<h2>Vice President, Business Affairs - Unscripted</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>Featuring exclusive original movies, series, short-form and live events and a vibrant catalogue that spans classic Disney Animation to the most recent movie releases available to stream on demand, Disney+ will be a global, premium entertainment destination that connects viewers to the great stories of The Walt Disney Company. We’re looking for talented team-players with an entrepreneurial spirit, who thrive upon the incomparable legacy of our company yet embrace unconventional thinking, and who are passionate about contributing to The Walt Disney Company’s direct-to-consumer experience through strategic hard-work and determination.<br>
<br>
We are seeking an experienced film and television executive for the Vice President, Unscripted Business Affairs position. This individual will collaborate with Business Affairs teams across all segments of The Walt Disney Company, playing a critical role in structuring the overall strategic approach for unscripted original programming deals for Disney+ Direct-To-Consumer service. The ideal candidate will have experience in a comparable position navigating and shepherding Business Affairs for all unscripted feature and television programming for SVOD platforms. This role will serve as key point of contact and partner with TWDC Enterprise segments on full business, content, and production lifecycles for unscripted programming.<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities :</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Develop and suggest strategies, policies and procedures for managing unscripted programming for Disney DTC</li>
<li>
Draft, structure, and negotiate agreements and complex deals related to unscripted features and television programming, including unscripted pilot/series, international co-productions, unscripted features/documentaries, specials, short-form, live events, ancillary content, third-party acquisitions, etc.</li>
<li>
Meticulously analyze deal arrangements, and formulate business strategies, regarding overall direction, proposal framework, associated costs, talent relations, and any other aspects in respect to the various content development and production matters for unscripted programming</li>
<li>
Partner with each TWDC segment legal team to draft sophisticated business term sheets and agreements</li>
<li>
Regularly interface and consistently coordinate with production companies and studios to oversee day to day logistics as it pertains to unscripted content and production</li>
<li>
Work closely with internal team members in creative, scheduling, and finance to set content strategy, financial budget planning, and programming calendar for Disney SVOD platforms</li>
<li>
Act as a business liaison and build relationships between Disney+ and the unscripted creative programming community (e.g., talent agencies, producers, studios and independent production companies)
</li>
<li>Identify and pursue original-related new business development and alternative financing / production opportunities, including international partnerships
</li>
<li>Handle any day-to-day business affairs issues for current programming</li>
<li>

</li>
<li>Provide guidance and general business counsel in a rapidly changing business and tech environment<br>
</li>
</ul>
