<h2>Installation Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Provide remote installation and training to new merchant accounts in the use of credit card terminal or eCommerce gateway.</li>
<li>Analyze and resolve technical problems and issues associated with new installation of products or services.</li>
<li>Resolution of merchant and client requests in timely matter and responding to inquiries or requests via phone and e-mail.</li>
<li>Pre-Sales support of EMS products and services with internal clients, sales and vendor partners.</li>
<li>Utilization of several different software and web-based systems</li>
<li>Download of processing software and deployment of credit card terminals.</li>
<li>Organize and package marketing collateral and stickers to be shipped with equipment.</li>
<li>Participate in ongoing product and services training and effectively cross-train team members.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>Job Benefits:</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>40 hours per week</li>
<li>401K, Medical, Dental, Vision available after 90 days</li>
<li>On the job training program</li>
<li>Casual business dress code</li>
<li>Excellent location near highways</li>
</ul>
