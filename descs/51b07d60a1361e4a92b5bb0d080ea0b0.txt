Responsibilities
</div>
<div>
<div>The main responsibilities for the Master Data Manager role are:</div>
<ul>
<li>Ensure timely and accurate execution entry of enterprise master data RB, Hygiene/Home.</li>
<li>Develop an understanding of RB’s global business strategies.</li>
<li>Lead definition of RB’s master data strategies and plans focusing on quality and governance needs and opportunities.</li>
<li>Communicate and collaborate with a matrix of data owners and business partners to ensure understanding and alignment of end-to-end data governance processes.</li>
<li>Influence and build alignment on business requirements and roles in the master data governance and quality processes.</li>
<li>Participate in prioritization and planning of quality improvement initiatives and process simplification.</li>
<li>Manage master data team members to define and implement master data standards, data management processes, and information quality controls, including determination of whether objects and attributes are centrally, regionally or locally governed.</li>
<li>Establish Master Data services SLAs with other functional departments.</li>
<li>Lead and participate on strategic projects and global initiatives to improve data quality.</li>
<li>Lead and influence across multiple levels of the organization and with internal and external team members.</li>
<li>Maintain awareness of industry trends and best-practices within the master data management environment to introduce innovation and ensure cost-effectiveness of RB’s data quality and governance standards and strategies.</li>
<li>Recommend changes to RB’s master data quality and governance strategies to improve the value of master data within RB.</li>
<li>Streamline and continuously improve processes to improve speed and agility for setup of new and evolving product development, financial processing and vendor setup.</li>
<li>Develop and maintain relationships with a variety of internal and external IT and business data experts and specialists (business data owners, solution architects, project managers, external solutions providers) to provide leadership and technical guidance, continuously expanding RB knowledge and capabilities of master data management.</li>
</ul>

<li>Demonstrated success delivering projects on time and on budget.</li>
<li>Demonstrated matrix management skills with internal and external partners.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to interface with all levels within the organization.</li>
<li>Solid understanding of financial, supply chain and manufacturing business processes.</li>
