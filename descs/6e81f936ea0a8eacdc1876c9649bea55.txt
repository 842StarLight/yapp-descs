<h2>Production Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Founded in 1936, Roseburg Forest Products is a privately owned company and one of North America's leading producers of particleboard, medium density fiberboard and thermally fused laminates. Roseburg also manufactures softwood and hardwood plywood, lumber, LVL and I-joists. The company owns and sustainably manages more than 600,000 acres of timberland in Oregon, North Carolina and Virginia, as well as an export wood chip terminal facility in Coos Bay, Ore. Roseburg products are shipped throughout North America and the Pacific Rim. To learn more about the company, please visit www.Roseburg.com .
</p>
<p><b>Key Responsibilities
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ensure safety of all employees and contractors working in operating unit or department by driving continuous improvement towards a true safety culture. This includes supporting all corporate safety programs; developing SOP's, identifying and eliminating hazards and enforcing all safety rules.
</li>
<li>Ensure compliance with all standards and programs (safety, environmental, quality, maintenance and manufacturing policies and procedures) within area of responsibility.
</li>
<li>Work closely with Technical Manager to ensure products meet expectations and ensure proper checks are being completed to ensure quality.
</li>
<li>Responsible for manufacturing operations through subordinate supervisors.
</li>
<li>Lead, plan and participate in process improvement, cost reductions and continuous improvement projects as part of the site business plan.
</li>
<li>Direct daily production to ensure production meets delivery schedules.
</li>
<li>Assist and participate with interviewing, selection and orientation process, has significant responsibility for the hiring process from recommendation, interview and hiring.
</li>
<li>Ensure and maintain established customer focus for employees, effectively maintain positive relationships with key strategic external and internal customers/suppliers.
</li>
<li>Responsible for staff training, performance management/development and setting daily work schedules. Provide coaching and counseling on performance feedback, up to initiating and assisting with corrective action.
</li>
<li>Manage production department budget and participate in forecasting activities to ensure sales targets are met and mill efficiencies are maintained.
</li>
<li>Ensure equipment supplies and mill facilities are in appropriate working condition to meet business needs.
</li>
<li>Prepare timely reports for distribution to internal customers and maintain accurate records and files.
</li>
<li>Perform all duties in accordance with safety rules and regulations.
</li>
<li>Perform other duties as necessary.</li>
</ul>
