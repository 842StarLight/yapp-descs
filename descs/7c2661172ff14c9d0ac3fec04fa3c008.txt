<h2>Customer Service Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>
<b>Summary</b>: Raise the level of customer satisfaction through elevated levels of service across the team. Optimize tools and processes to support the team in delivering results.</div>
<div>
<b>
Responsibility</b>: Daily management of Customer Service Team. Assume responsibility for the success and performance of the department.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>
<b>Essential Duties:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Develop and promote a culture of customer service excellence.</li>
<li>Provide leadership, guidance, direction and communication to the team in support of organizational strategic objectives.</li>
<li>Engage with, and influence, the managers of other internal support services to ensure all are aligned in providing the necessary service levels to contribute to a positive customer experience.</li>
<li>Review, develop and implement customer service practices and procedures.</li>
<li>Improve levels of communication – both internal and external – amongst the team.</li>
<li>Optimize Salesforce and other management tools.</li>
<li>Work with Operations Team in all manufacturing facilities to consistently deliver on aggressive – but realistic – deadlines.</li>
<li>Guide Production Scheduler to minimize adverse variances through optimizing manufacturing capacity.</li>
<li>Contribute to the improvement of the supply chain.</li>
<li>Work with Commercial Team to manage price book and maintain margins.</li>
<li>Work with Technical Department to ensure consistency in details and policies.</li>
<li>Identify areas for improvement in business processes and results.</li>
<li>Implement and manage effective department cross training and succession planning.</li>
<li>Present relevant weekly/monthly reports.</li>
<li>Work with Sales Team to problem solve as needed and support in a selling capacity.</li>
<li>Support new product development initiatives.</li>
<li>Possess the knowledge and ability to back up and support other internal departments as necessary.</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div><b>Education/Experience:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree or 10+ year’s applicable industry experience.</li>
<li>Experience with insulated/metal panel construction a plus.</li>
<li>Salesforce experience a plus.</li>
<li>Ability to read and interpret project specifications and all applicable project documents.</li>
<li>Ability to read and interpret structural and architectural drawings.</li>
<li>High degree of accuracy and meticulousness about details.</li>
<li>A pro-active personality with high energy, self-motivation, and strong decision-making skills.</li>
<li>Excellent time management, organizational and communication skills.</li>
<li>Self-motivated and possesses the ability to meet deadlines and problem solve.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Computer Skills:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Must be proficient in all Microsoft Office applications (Word, Excel, Power Point) and Outlook.</li>
<li>SAP or other equivalent ERP software knowledge is desirable.</li>
<li>Salesforce knowledge would be beneficial.</li>
</ul>
