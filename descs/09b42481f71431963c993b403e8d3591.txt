<h2>Solutions Advisor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>KAJABI SALES DIVISION SOLUTIONS ADVISOR</b></p>
<p><b>Do you love finding pain-points of business owners and helping them find the best solution? Do you listen first, and consult rather than close? Can you identify a customer’s needs and confidently direct them to the right product? Do you thrive in an environment where you can help others succeed and have the opportunity to be recognized for the results you create? If you said yes a whole bunch, you may be exactly who we need.</b></p>
<p><b>Let us introduce ourselves. Kajabi is a fast-growing company that is seeking passionate and ambitious individuals who are looking to establish and/or grow their career in software sales(SaaS). Kajabi is an all-in-one platform that makes it easy to create online courses, launch marketing campaigns, build landing pages, and design the perfect website. We are passionate about putting customers first, which demands a different type of sale. Our solutions advisors are responsible to engage and educate prospects in their decision-making when considering Kajabi, and driving results through a consultative sales process.</b><br>*YOUR ROLE WILL INCLUDE THE FOLLOWING<br>As a Solutions Advisor, you will work directly with the Head of Sales to engage visitors to our marketing site and optimize their experience by providing true guidance and explore all possibilities to help them achieve their goals. The Kajabi platform and its world-class support have fostered a heart-based community that empowers others to succeed. Our marketing team’s mission is to attract the right customer to the best all-in-one online business platform in the market. Every visitor has a notion of what Kajabi brings to the market, and you will be the instrument to help them solidify their decision in choosing Kajabi. Your assignment will be to meet sales quotas and manage leads from sales chats and/or booked meetings from our chat-bot through to closing the deal. In addition, you’ll have an opportunity to help develop efficient processes and procedures to make our team successful. As the team grows, you’ll have the potential opportunity to grow into a leadership role within the team.<br>*<br>*</p>
<ul><li>Convey Kajabi’s heart-based and product-focused brand to every single prospect Promote our world-class support, industry-leading product, and amazing global community</li></ul>
<ul><li>Work with clients and prospects to understand their business goals and help them find the best Kajabi option</li></ul>
<ul><li>Close deals with new and existing customers at or above quota level</li></ul>
<ul><li>Work with the Head of Sales to optimize sales strategies</li></ul>
<ul><li>Work closely with our partner referral division to attend company-sponsored events and provide live customer interaction.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintain and update sales interactions between Drift and HubSpot</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manage a pipeline of inbound leads (site visitors and referrals) to identify, engage, and develop relationships with potential purchasers</li></ul>
<ul><li>Assess and educate prospective customers on how Kajabi can help them achieve their business goals and scale</li></ul>
<ul><li>Propose new ways to promote our company’s mission and vision while maintaining our company’s objectives</li></ul>
