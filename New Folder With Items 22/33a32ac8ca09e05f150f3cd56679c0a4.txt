<b>Job Summary:</b> Production specialists are the front line of researching and coding products for our reporting service, GreenEdge. Production specialists investigate, resolve, classify, and enter point-of-sale data from cannabis retailers for all brands, products, and strains in to our proprietary database with accuracy and speed. With a keen eye for detail and an investigative mindset, Production Specialist ensure that we have the most reliable and accurate
<br><br>
<b>Essential Functions and Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Process large data sets of point-of-sale information by compiling, categorizing, tabulating, auditing and verifying information</li>
<li>Analyze data by identifying the underlying principles, reasons, or facts of information by breaking down information or data</li>
<li>Enter information into databases or software programs</li>
<li>Compile, sort, and verify the accuracy of data</li>
<li>Document and record information for standardizing categorization</li>
<li>Provide information to supervisors, co-workers, in written, email or in person</li>
<li>Compare data with source documents, or re-enter data in verification format to detect errors</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<b>Job-related Education and/or Experience:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>In-depth knowledge of Cannabis and related products</li>
<li>Prior experience in dispensary compliance and/or budtending is preferred</li>
<li>Typically requires a high school degree or equivalent</li>
</ul>
