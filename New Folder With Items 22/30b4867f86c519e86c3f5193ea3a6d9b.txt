<h2>Inside Account Executive | Higher Education</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Inside Account Executive is a key member of the sales team and works with the Strategic Account Executive, and Systems Engineer, to meet sales goals.</p>
<p>
<b>RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
 <li>Communicate regularly with a defined list of existing and new customers throughout the year to identify areas of need for business growth and new solutions
</li>
<li>Use prospecting tools such as DiscoverOrg and LinkedIn to find new contacts and new business units from a defined list of Education Accounts
</li>
<li>Qualify new leads and inquiries, conduct initial assessment, and determine prospect requirements to aid in the correct assignment of new opportunities
</li>
<li>Understand customer business needs and schedule technical demonstrations or face to face meetings with SE as required
</li>
<li>Execute campaigns to drive traffic to online and in-person events
</li>
<li>Support efforts of Account Executive in the field to sell the Whole Product Experience and procure purchase orders from new prospects and existing customers
</li>
<li>Enter new Opportunities and related information into SalesForce.com to document and manage sales activities
</li>
<li>Develop well-rounded understanding of Jamf sales philosophy, market and customers
</li>
<li>Know how to position Jamf solutions against competitor offerings
</li>
<li>Accurately process customer transactions such as price quotations and purchase orders
</li>
<li>Grow understanding of and ability to explain business value of Jamf solutions to prospects and existing customers
</li>
<li>Responsible for leading and closing sales opportunities for the Education Commercial team
</li>
<li>Performs all job responsibilities in alignment with the core values, mission and purpose of the organization
</li>
<li>Adheres to the highest moral, ethical and legal standards to deliver an environment that promotes respect, innovation and creativity
</li>
<li>Supports and promotes a positive, inclusive workplace one in which the talents and strengths of our increasingly diverse workforce are welcomed, further developed and manifested in our work</li>
</ul>
<ul>

<li>General Experience navigating and negotiating legal agreements (Preferred)
</li>
<li>General Experience in software sales within high-growth technology companies (Preferred)
</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal, organizational, and customer service skills
</li>
<li>Ability to work independently and coordinate well with other team members that are remote
</li>
<li>Ability to interact effectively with prospects and customers via phone and email
</li>
<li>Ability to balance areas of responsibility across multiple territories
</li>
<li>Mac experience preferred</li>
