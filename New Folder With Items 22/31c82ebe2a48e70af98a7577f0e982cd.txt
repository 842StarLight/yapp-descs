<h2>Senior Tax and Compliance Accountant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Senior Tax and Compliance Accountant - Minneapolis, MN</b></p>
<p><b>
Westwood Professional Services, Inc.</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Westwood Professional Services, Inc.</b> is seeking a Senior Tax and Compliance Accountant to join our corporate services team in our Minneapolis, Minnesota office. The successful candidate will be a detail-oriented tax professional who will administer and coordinate all aspects of the company’s state and local taxation and compliance, including sales/use, property, payroll and business/professional licensing. They will serves as primary liaison for the company with its external income tax firm. has a highly experienced and dynamic team of individuals dedicated to providing high-level professional services to our clients.</p>
<br>
<p>
<b>
Duties and Responsibilities:<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Accumulate, analyze, and process financial accounting data in order to prepare and file sales/use and property tax returns, process tax payments, and prepare forecasts and financial accounting tax estimates.</li>
<li>Complete all required tax filings in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Gather information and draft responses for various IRS and state tax notices, as needed.</li>
<li>Coordinate audits by various taxation authorities, and negotiate with them over tax payment issues, as appropriate.</li>
<li>Ensure adherence to compliance-related deadlines such as extensions, tax estimate requirements, tax return filings and other tax related services</li>
<li>Understand and research basic to moderately complex tax concepts and their application to company situations, and communicate results to Vice President of Finance, Controller and other leadership.</li>
<li>Develop new and enhance existing tax processes, such as tax calendars, research tools and training.</li>
<li>Update job knowledge by participating in training seminars and webinars.</li>
<li>Create a corporate tax database, and update as needed for tax rate and other changes.</li>
<li>Coordinate outsourced tax preparation work, including income tax filings and year-end tax provision.</li>
<li>Support Payroll and Benefits Coordinator to ensure accurate and efficient payroll tax processes.</li>
<li>Serve as primary contact with Legal teams maintaining the company’s business and professional licensing.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<br>
Required Skills<br>
<br>
Required Experience<br>
<div>
<p>
</p>
</div>
<ul>
<li>3+ years of state and local tax accounting experience.</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree in accounting preferred, or a tax concentration within an accounting program.</li>
<li>Must be detail oriented, and deadline driven.</li>
<li>Knowledge of accounting procedures.</li>
<li>Familiar with accounting software systems (Deltek Vision preferred).</li>
<li>Strong Microsoft Office applications knowledge.</li>
<li>Effective written and verbal communication.</li>
<li>Independent judgment is essential.</li>
<br>
</ul>
