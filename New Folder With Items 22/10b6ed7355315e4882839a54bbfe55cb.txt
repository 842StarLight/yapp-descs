<p><b>Straight Line Source is looking for new talent!</b></p>
<p>
We are the fastest growing providers of working capital in the Merchant Cash Advance industry. We cater to small and mid-size businesses across the United States and Canada. Our unique business structure offers a variety of products and services that provide financial solutions to business owners.</p>
<p>
We are seeking ambitious and motivated individuals who have the essential qualities to be successful Account Executives. If you enjoy being in a success driven sales environment and working towards achieving goals, this is the job for you!</p>
<p>
Account Executives act as the first point of contact with potential clients. They answer questions and increase client satisfaction. As an Account Executive you will contribute to building profitable and long-term relationships with our clients to reach our business objectives.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>

<li>Contact potential applicants to obtain application documents, bank statements and credit documents to seek business financing on behalf of prospect.</li>
<li>Provide customer service in a professional manner and facilitate the transparent disclosure of sensitive information using tact to:</li>
<li>Answer customer inquiries regarding small business lending,
</li>
<li>Update and maintain customer account records,</li>
<li>Update customers on new products, services, modifications, and enhancements.</li>
</ul>
<p>Attend training to enhancing knowledge of new and existing products, services, procedures, and tools. Utilize sales management tools to maximize sales effectiveness. Always portraying the company in a positive manner.</p>

</li>
<li>The ability to deliver an excellent customer service experience through strong verbal and written communication skills to build robust professional relationships.
</li>
<li>Effective presentation and negotiation skills.
</li>
