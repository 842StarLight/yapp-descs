<p><b>Company Overview</b></p>
<p><b>Brady Corporation is an international manufacturer and marketer of complete solutions that identify and protect people, products and places. Brady’s products help customers increase safety, security, productivity and performance and include high-performance labels, signs, safety devices, printing systems and software. Founded in 1914, the Company has a diverse customer base in electronics, telecommunications, manufacturing, electrical, construction, medical, aerospace and a variety of other industries. Brady is headquartered in Milwaukee, Wisconsin and as of July 31, 2019, employed approximately 6,100 people in its worldwide businesses. Brady’s fiscal 2019 sales were approximately $1.16 billion. Brady stock trades on the New York Stock Exchange under the symbol BRC. More information is available on the Internet at www.bradycorp.com..</b></p>
<p>SPC Overview</p>
<p>An industry leader for more than 30 years, Brady's SPC business manufactures high quality, polypropylene-based absorbents and spill control products for a variety of applications. Servicing customers in industrial, commercial, utility, hospitality, healthcare and emergency spill markets, SPC is fueled by two missions: creating innovative products that help maintain a clean, safe workplace for employees and protecting the environment from hazardous fluids. With an international customer base, a new state-of-the-art manufacturing facility and a dynamic team, SPC is a growth driver for Brady Corporation with great opportunities for professional growth and development.</p>
<p>Position Reports To: Production Supervisor</p>
<p>Position Summary:</p>
<p>The inspector &amp; packager is responsible for performing quality inspection and packaging of goods in the meltblown and converting workcenters under direct supervision.</p>
<p>Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</p>
<ul><li>Work in the meltblown or converting workcenters</li></ul>
<ul><li>Verify product meets specifications including dimensions, perforations, construction, etc</li></ul>
<ul><li>Perform quality tests as directed by operator</li></ul>
<ul><li>Record quality check data</li></ul>
<ul><li>Retrieve product from conveyor and package in boxes/bags</li></ul>
<ul><li>Assemble and label boxes</li></ul>
<ul><li>Palletize boxes/bags according to specification</li></ul>
<ul><li>Thread web</li></ul>
<ul><li>Gather, move and bale waste product</li></ul>
<ul><li>Operate boom, soc or pillow equipment.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Clean production area</li></ul>
<ul><li>Perform minor production maintenance on equipment as instructed (cleaning sheeters, etc)</li></ul>
<ul><li>Adhere to Brady's Attendance, Safety &amp; other Company policies</li></ul>
<p>Required Knowledge, Skills &amp; Abilities</p>
<ul><li>High school diploma or equivalent</li></ul>

<ul><li>Ability to work in a team environment</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to train others</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to read &amp; follow written directions</li></ul>
