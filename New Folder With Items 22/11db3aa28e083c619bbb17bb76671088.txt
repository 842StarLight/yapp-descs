<h2>Software Solutions Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Job Description</b></p>
<p>
As a Software Solutions Consultant you will be responsible for presenting the value proposition and benefits of our best in class health science cloud applications to prospective clients and customers while striving to deliver 100% customer satisfaction. You’ll work with and get training from our team of product experts to become a certified product expert in SureClinical’s health science cloud applications.</p>
<p><b>
Job Duties</b></p>
<p>
Share, evangelize and leverage your product expertise with customer prospects in the health sciences area through both online and in person engagements. Serve as a technical resource for other internal staff including other Software Solutions Consultant, Account Managers, Product Support Engineers, Product Development and QA. Deliver standard company presentations and demonstrations. Demonstrate standard application and platform features and benefits, and product advantages over competitor offerings. Support the sales team with minimal supervision.</p>
<p><b>
Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>BS degree or equivalent in computer science, sciences or technology; advanced degree desirable.</li>
<li>Consultative selling skills including customer needs analysis, product demonstrations, closing techniques</li>
<li>Knowledge of systems – PC’s including Windows, Apple Mac; mobile devices including Android, Apple IOS</li>
<li>Strong verbal and written communication skills. Email writing test is required of all applicants to insure that candidates have the ability to succinctly and clearly communicate with customers, prospects and partners</li>
<li>Knowledge of MS Office</li>
<li>Ability to travel as needed</li>
<li>Assist with completion of technical responses to customer questionnaires and requests for proposals</li>
<li>Previous cloud application software experience in one or more of the following desired: Veeva, Medidata, Oracle Healthsciences, Siebel, PeopleSoft, Salesforce, SAP or other enterprise applications in Pharma, life sciences or healthcare</li>
</ul>
<p>A business email writing test is required of all applicants. Candidates must demonstrate an ability to clearly communicate with customers, prospects and partners.</p>
<p><b>
Compensation</b></p>
<p>
Base salary depending on experience plus quarterly performance based bonus. SureClinical is on a pre-IPO track and offers stock options to selected candidates.</p>
