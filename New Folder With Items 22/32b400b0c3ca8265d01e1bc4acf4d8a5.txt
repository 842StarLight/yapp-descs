<h2>Accounting – AR Admin/Clerk</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>SHI International has an exciting role for an Accounts Receivable Clerk in our Accounting department. The focus of the job is on managing the company’s cash and invoicing processes. This is an entry level accounting role and training is provided to all new employees.</p>
<p>This position is required to report to the SHI Somerset office location.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities/Job Function</b></p>
<p>Responsibilities include, but are not limited to:</p>
<ul>
<li>Reconciling incoming payment and making sure necessary invoices are closed out</li>
<li>Utilizing all tools made available to you in order to work in the most efficient manner</li>
<li>Managing daily invoice printing and posting processes</li>
<li>Managing Credit Card processes from charging and crediting invoices, and reconciling payments</li>
<li>Submitting invoices via customer provided websites</li>
<li>Issuing general ledger entries to transfer funds between divisions or posting to specified accounts</li>
<li>Working with various departments to resolve any customer payment issues</li>
<li>May be required to work in different currencies</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications/Minimum Skills Required</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High School diploma</li>
<li>Microsoft Excel experience</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Comfortable working in a computerized accounting environment as part of a team</li>
<li>Ability to pay close attention to details and completes tasks with accuracy</li>
</ul>
