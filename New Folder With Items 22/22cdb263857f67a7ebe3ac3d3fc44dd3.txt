<h2>Franchise Sales Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Lift Brands, the parent company to Snap Fitness and a variety of other fitness franchise brands, is a dynamic and fast paced environment that values the integration of work and wellness. One of the largest global fitness franchisors, Lift Brands has over 2,000 gyms open or in development across the globe, in over 25 countries.</p>

Summary:</p>
<p>
The Franchise Sales Marketing Strategist will be responsible for all aspects of franchise sales marketing for Lift Brands globally including lead generation, lead nurturing, KPI reporting, and budget monitoring. This is a rare in-house franchise marketing opportunity that we are excited to offer to outstanding candidates! If you have a passion for changing lives, are seeking international experience, and bring positivity and energy with you wherever you go, this position is perfect for you!</p>

<li>Extremely strong writing skills and the ability to produce final marketing copy for sales collateral including presentations, advertisements, press releases, and articles</li>
</ul>
