<h2>Marketing Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Celebrity Theatre is seeking an organized, creative and energetic individual to add to our team to help advertise shows to drive sales for the venue.</p>
<p>This person would be the main point of contact for venue marketing needs and must have experience in Adobe Photoshop with the ability to re-size, layer images, and create ads.</p>
<p>Duties include but are not limited to:</p>
<p>Working closely with the office team and/or promoters to create strategic and creative marketing plans targeted to a show specific audience within specified budget.</p>
<p>Writing press releases for all shows consisting of artists’ background information and event descriptions</p>
<p>Executing and updating marketing plans following a show specific budget including print ad creation, social media campaigning, radio spots, and billboard design and scheduling.</p>
<p>Obtaining admats and approvals from artists agency reps and/or promotors prior to publishing.</p>
<p>Announcing shows via social media and press release via e-mail blast</p>
<p>Responding to all inquiries made via social media and creating scheduled campaigns for each event to targeted audiences</p>
<p>Adding and removing events from the website using wordpress</p>
<p>Writing and scheduling radio spots and negotiating price and or/trade with radio managers</p>
<p>Creating and scheduling print ads with various publications and negotiating contracts with same</p>
<p>Designing and scheduling billboards in strategic places throughout the valley</p>
<p>Designing and distributing flyers and other forms of marketing material</p>
<p>Monitoring and analyzing market and sales trends to determine effectiveness of ad placement and updating marketing plans accordingly. Weekly meeting regarding sales and marketing efforts.</p>
<p>Contacting and negotiating terms with sponsorships such as groupon, goldstar, vettix and other ‘seat fillers’ when necessary.</p>
<p>Tracking expenses on spreadsheets for all advertising and keeping within budget.</p>
<p>Saving copies of all ads, tearsheets and billboard proofs on company server for easy access</p>
<p>Saving copies of all invoices/proposals and corresponding ads in show ad packs to be given to artists on show day</p>
<p>Printing and posting artists’ dressing room signs and show schedules.</p>
<p>Sending out weekly show calendar e-mail blast via mailchimp</p>
<p>Updating in house digital signage and designing and updating in house print signage with event info when an event is added or has passed</p>
<p>Other marketing tasks as needed</p>
<p>Open availability is necessary due to short time frames in the industry. Pay to be determined/negotiated upon hire.</p>
<p>Please submit resume and portfolio to be considered.</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>

<ul><li>Store Discounts</li></ul>
