<h2>Branch Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div><div>The Branch Manager is responsible for the development, growth, profitability and smooth operation of the business.</div></div>
<div>
<p><b>
Tasks and Responsabilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Assume overall responsibility for sales growth and profitable operations.</li>
<li>
With the VP Retail Operations or owner, participate in the development and updating of a marketing plan for the branch.</li>
<li>
Conduct all marketing activities as prescribed by the current marketing plan.</li>
<li>
Recruit, hire, train, supervise and manage all sales, installation and administrative staff.</li>
<li>
Ensure the successful completion of installations.</li>
<li>
Ensure overall customer satisfaction.</li>
<li>
Meet or exceed sales projections.</li>
<li>
Manage inventory, maintain stocking levels, ensure inventory security and supervise accurate counts.</li>
<li>
Maintain vehicles, tools and equipment in good working order according to scheduled servicing and to meet state and insurance requirements.</li>
<li>
Maintain vehicles, tools and equipment in good working order according to scheduled servicing and to meet state and insurance requirements.</li>
<li>
Monitor various prescribed reports and initiate action plans as required.</li>
<li>
Ensure that all branch personnel follow all Bath Fitter policies and procedures.</li>
<li>
Ensure that all branch personnel follow all Bath Fitter safety policies and procedures.</li>
<li>
Conduct/participate in regular branch and/or regional meetings and training sessions.</li>
<li>
Realize the Branch Manager's role as the personal representative of Bath Fitter while in uniform and while driving a company vehicle and act accordingly.</li>
<li>
Perform other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>
<p><b>
Job Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Education and/or Experience – High school diploma, general education degree (GED) or equivalent education.</li>
<li>
Demonstration of managerial and leadership skills.</li>
<li>
Valid driver's license and maintain a clean driving record.</li>
<li>
Language Skills – Ability to read and interpret documents such as safety rules, operating and maintenance instructions, procedure manuals, identification catalogs and price lists. Ability to write routine reports and correspondence.</li>
<li>
Mathematical Skills – Ability to add, subtract, multiply and divide in all units of measure.</li>
<li>
Computer Skills – Knowledge of computer workstations and peripheral equipment; knowledge of database, spreadsheet and word processing software.</li>
<li>
Reasoning Ability – Ability to apply common sense understanding to carry out detailed written, illustrated or oral instructions.</li>
<li>
Communication Skills - Be able to effectively communicate orally and in writing.</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>
<p><b>
Demonstrated Skills</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Problem Solving – Identifies and resolves problem in a timely manner.</li>
<li>
Oral Communication – Speaks clearly and persuasively; participates in meetings.</li>
<li>
Written Communication – Writes clearly; able to read and interpret written information.</li>
<li>
Teamwork – Contributes to building positive team spirit.</li>
<li>
Leadership – Exhibits confidence in self and others; inspires and motivates others to perform well; gives appropriate recognition to others.</li>
<li>
Diversity – Promotes a harassment-free environment.</li>
<li>
Ethics – Treats people with integrity and ethically.</li>
<li>
Organizational Support – Supports organization's goals, values and code of ethics.</li>
<li>
Judgment – Exhibits sound and accurate judgment; supports and explains reasons for decisions; includes appropriate people in decision-making process; makes timely and appropriate decisions.</li>
<li>
Planning/Organizing – Prioritizes work activities; uses time efficiently. Sets goals and objectives; develops realistic action plans.</li>
<li>
Professionalism – Focuses on solving conflict, not blaming; treats others with respect and consideration; accepts responsibility for own actions.</li>
<li>
Adaptability – Manages competing demands; able to deal with frequent change, delays or unexpected events.</li>
<li>
Quality – Demonstrates accuracy and thoroughness; monitors own work to ensure quality. Looks for ways to improve and promote quality.</li>
<li>
Quantity – Meets productivity standards; completes work in timely manner; strives to increase productivity.</li>
<li>
Safety and security – Observes safety and security procedures; reports unsafe conditions; uses equipment and materials properly.</li>
<li>
Attendance/Punctuality – Is consistently at work and on time.</li>
</ul>
