<div><b>PURPOSE AND SCOPE:</b></div>
<div>
The Systems Developer performs complex design, implementation, and maintenance of multi product modules/sub-systems to ensure that Company needs are met. Assists in outlining new product/service business case justification to ensure that proposed solution is capable of delivering articulated business benefits.</div>
<div></div>
<div>
<div><b>
IT / Salesforce Healthcloud focus:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Solid understanding of SaaS platform concepts involving technical requirements, upgrades, system configuration, workflow management, reporting and user/role setup.</li>
<li>
Proven experience working with Salesforce.com platform for Service Cloud, Marketing Cloud, Sales Cloud and Analytics Cloud.</li>
<li>
Experience working with various implementation methodologies such as Waterfall, Agile, or hybrid models.</li>
<li>
Proven working experience in Salesforce administration, developing objects, record types, page layouts, workflow rules and approvals.
</li>
<li>Experience with Salesforce.com developer toolkit including Apex Classes, Controllers and Triggers, Visualforce, Force.com IDE, Force.com migration tool and Web Service.</li>
<li>
Experience integrating business processes across disparate systems using 3rd party tools.</li>
<li>
Experience with data migration using Data Loader or 3rd party tools.</li>
<li>
Comfortable working in a culturally diverse workforce and business environment.</li>
<li>
Ability to work cross-functionally in a matrix management environment.</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>
