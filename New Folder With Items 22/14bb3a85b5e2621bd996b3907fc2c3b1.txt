<h2>Infrastructure Architect</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><div><div>
<p>The Infrastructure Architect is responsible for the development and ongoing technical expertise and architectural solutions in infrastructure, hardware, hosting service and network areas. This role holds significant responsibility for providing robust and scalable technology platforms to ensure the ongoing performance of United Shore systems. This role will be focused on settings standard, designs and implementation of IT infrastructure and associated management tools. The Infrastructure Architect will work with other architecture roles in the company, including Solution Architects and Enterprise Architects as well as Infrastructure Project Managers.</p>
<p>
Other responsibilities may include creating prototypes to demonstrate product features and presenting product capabilities to the United Shore development community.</p>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>WHAT YOU WILL BE DOING
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Provide oversight and governance for all infrastructure technology and domains</li>
<li>
Develop an enterprise infrastructure architecture framework and implementation strategy</li>
<li>
Sets technical standards and procedures across the enterprise for all strategic platforms</li>
<li>
Document and communicate all infrastructure architecture designs and standards</li>
<li>
Creates reference architectures, position statements and technical design documents as technology guidelines</li>
<li>
Design software and hardware solutions that are of high quality, effectively meet business objectives, and support long-term IT strategy</li>
<li>
Ensure that software and hardware solutions are aligned with the company’s System Architectural strategy by understanding company objectives, key business and regulatory requirements, staying abreast of current and emerging technologies, and the System Architectural framework and future architectural direction</li>
<li>
Identify and specify interfaces between software components, applications, and systems and document their interactions in support of the solution’s functionality</li>
<li>
Define the functional attributes (performance, throughput, availability, capacity, security) of components and develop infrastructure standards</li>
<li>
Evaluate and document commercial software solutions, toolsets, and hardware</li>
<li>
Provide solutions and recommendations for application performance improvement</li>
<li>
Promote continuous improvement and strategic direction of the infrastructure</li>
<li>
Provide cost benefit analysis and estimates for proposed solutions</li>
<li>
Lead and participate in the modeling, prototyping, analysis, configuration, construction, and deployment of technology components solution</li>
<li>
Research, identify and recommend industry best practices for solving business problems</li>
<li>
Ability to act in the Technical Delivery Lead role for infrastructure projects</li>
<li>
Maintains knowledge of IT Infrastructure architecture, standards and practices and consistently applies these standards</li>
<li>
Review and assess infrastructure solutions for specific challenges or risks, and guide business enabling IT investment</li>
<li>
Facilitate discussions with business and IT leaders to ensure that enterprise infrastructure strategic initiatives are leveraged to drive IT simplifications, standardization and efficiencies</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>

</b></h2>

<p><b>Required:</b></p>

Computer-related degree or equivalent experience</li>
