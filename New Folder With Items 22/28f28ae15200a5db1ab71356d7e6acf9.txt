<h2>Senior Tax Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div><div>
<p>JOB DESCRIPTION</p>
<div>
<p>For almost 200 years, PQ Corporation has envisioned the future of performance materials, chemicals and catalysts through collaborative innovation, making products that touch all of us, every day.</p>
<ul>
<li>Cleaner Emissions - Our Catalysts and Refining Services products and solutions help companies produce vehicles with improved fuel efficiency and cleaner emissions.</li>
<li>Safer Travels - We produce highly engineered Performance Materials that help make highways and airports safer for drivers, pedestrians, pilots and passengers</li>
<li>Better Care - Our products are critical ingredients in consumer applications that help make teeth brighter and skin softer</li>
<li>Carbon-Free - With inorganic and carbon-free products, we contribute to improving the sustainability of our planet.</li>
</ul>
<p>POSITION DESCRIPTION</p>
<p>The Senior Tax Analyst will assist our Tax Managers and gain valuable experience in all aspects of the Corporate Tax Function:</p>
<ul>
<li>Income Tax Provision – Quarterly/Annually</li>
<li>Income Tax Compliance and associated audits</li>
<li>Sales Tax Compliance and associated audits</li>
<li>Assist with Property Tax Compliance</li>
<li>Assist with overall Tax Planning – primarily US but potentially Foreign</li>
</ul>
<p>More specific responsibilities include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Working with 3rd Party Service Provider to complete US Federal Tax Filings.</li>
<li>Preparing all US State Tax Filings</li>
<li>Calculating and processing quarterly estimated payments and year-end extensions</li>
<li>Assist Tax Manager with any Federal/State tax audits</li>
<li>Involvement with Sales Tax Process – monthly review of Sales Tax payments</li>
<li>Involvement with Property Tax – support 3rd Party Consultants in preparing annual budget</li>
<li>Involvement with Quarterly Provision and Domestic Annual Provision process.</li>
<li>Address questions in all areas of tax from both internal and external customers</li>
</ul>
</div>
