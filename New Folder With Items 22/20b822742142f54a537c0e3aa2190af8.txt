<h2>.NET Developer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Autodata Solutions is looking for a <b>.NET Developer</b> to join their growing Troy, MI team!</p>
<p><b>
Position Overview:</b></p>
<p>
As a .NET Developer with Autodata, you will work closely with the product team to develop new offerings under one of our largest divisions. You will drive new ideas that contribute to research, design, execution, documentation and modifications of software applications. The successful candidate will be a passionate C# programmer who is comfortable with the Software Development Life Cycle. Liaise with Architects, Business Analysts, QA Analysts and other senior technical teams involved in the delivery of our solutioning opportunities and engagements. Join a team where you will be encouraged to take strong ownership of your work, and given latitude to break ground on new ideas.</p>
<p><b>
Core Job Duties &amp; Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Design, build and maintain enterprise .NET web-based applications utilizing .NET frameworks (versions 4.0 and above) and established best patterns and practices</li>
<li>
Perform requirements gathering and analysis, systems design, development, documentation, testing and integration in an agile environment.</li>
<li>
Identify problems uncovered by testing or customer feedback and correct problems</li>
<li>
Work with business analysts, designers and other developers; contribute to research, design, execution, documentation and modification of software applications throughout the production life cycle</li>
<li>
Identify system deficiencies; recommend, schedule, and perform efficient software improvements and upgrades</li>
<li>
Maintain and improve code quality through peer reviews and unit testing</li>
<li>
Communicate with your team, share and document your work on a regular basis</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
In depth knowledge (3+ years of experience) in .NET (C#)</li>
<li>
Web service integration using technologies such as XML, SOAP, REST</li>
<li>
Experience with microservices design and .NET Microservices Architecture</li>
<li>
Experience in full life cycle of software development; requirements analysis, design, development, testing, implementation</li>
<li>
Familiarity with process modeling techniques</li>
<li>
Knowledge of code and release management</li>
<li>
Strong creative problem-solving ability</li>
<li>
Experience in creating software architecture documents or other design artifacts</li>
<li>
Stable work history and an ability to collaborate effectively with a distributed team (local &amp; offshore)</li>
</ul>
