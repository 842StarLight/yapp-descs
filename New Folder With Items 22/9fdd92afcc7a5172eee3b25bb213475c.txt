</div>
<div>
We are passionate about our customer’s experience and to deliver excellence, we ask our Okera colleagues to show up every day prepared to do their best work and to constantly enable teammates’ to do theirs. <div>

<div>
Role</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
We are seeking an experienced, facile product marketing leader who wants to build a great software company at the intersection of data, analytics, and infrastructure. The VP, Product Marketing will be responsible for driving key elements of vision, messaging, and execution of our overall go-to-market. This position reports to the CEO.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
The ideal candidate demonstrates success in developing compelling value propositions, messaging, content, go-to-market strategies, and marketing plans within large, global enterprises. The Candidate has previously developed new product messaging and driven usage expansion at a proven technology vendor. The candidate has a strong background in product marketing.</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
What You Get To Do In This Role</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Develop messaging, value propositions, and supporting collateral that describe the unique benefits of Okera for IT organizations and Analytics Center of Excellence.</li>
<li>
Partnered with product management to achieve a shared understanding for the future of IT and appropriate product direction for Okera that is executed through solutions, products, and messaging.</li>
<li>
Defined and execute product marketing programs and operational deliverables - including but not limited to product launches, product specific programs (i.e., campaigns), pricing updates, and enablement materials for sales and partners.</li>
<li>
Educate the industry analyst community so they understand how to properly communicate Okera value propositions to their clients and accurately represent Okera relative to the competition.</li>
<li>
Meet with Customers, Sales and Sales Engineers, Customer Success, and Partners to understand the needs of customers and partners for earning greater mindshare.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with Product Management, Sales and Sales Engineers, Customer Success, and Partners to produce content, curriculum, and other materials aligned with growth strategies.</li>
<li>
Help identify, support and manage key conferences and events.</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
In order to be successful in this role, we need someone who has:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
An understanding of data access challenges at large, global enterprises.</li>
<li>
The ability to generate ideas for how to message our value proposition.</li>
<li>
An understanding of potential partner ecosystem.</li>
<li>
Passion for how data agility will enable new ways of doing business.</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>

10+ years of experience with technology products</li>

<li>
Up to 25% travel to support sales efforts and industry events.</li>
</ul></div>
