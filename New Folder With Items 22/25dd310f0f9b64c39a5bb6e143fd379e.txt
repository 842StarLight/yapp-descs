<p><b>OUR ZELIS PURPOSE</b></p>
<p>We founded Zelis to help control the rising cost of healthcare and make healthcare more affordable for all of us.</p>
<p><b>POSITION OVERVIEW</b></p>
<p>Zelis is pursuing an aggressive organic and non-organic growth strategy focused on providing superior healthcare claim lifecycle solutions to payors, providers and individuals from pre-payment through payment. As the company executes its growth strategy, Zelis is seeking to identify an accomplished Director of Implementations.</p>
<p><b>KEY RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Understand pipeline reports and sales timeline and impact</li>
<li>Collect and organize pre implementation documentation and process flows.</li>
<li>Review and approve transition documents from sales</li>
<li>Assign implementations to project managers as needed</li>
<li>Lead complex and highly visible Client implementations</li>
<li>Lead assessments around functionality and/or processes. Including white boarding and determining business improvements.</li>
<li>Responsible for documenting &amp; communicating Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) instructions for all ongoing business and transitioning the Client to the Account Management &amp; Production Support team.</li>
<li>Work closely with IT Implementation Managers, Solution Architects and/or Account Managers to ensure appropriate configuration and deployment of customer</li>
<li>Provide on-line meetings and conference calls to orientate a new client, present new functionality, perform training, and collaborate with clients about concerns.</li>
<li>Provide executive stakeholder updates and escalate timely and appropriately</li>
<li>Track all progress, milestones and updates in salesforce</li>
<li>Support Jr. Implementation Specialists who support the overall client facing portion of implementations.</li>
<li>Manage Vendor Relationships – inclusive of project management, system integration projects, ongoing enhancements of interfaces, etc.</li>
<li>Provide strategic direction and manage business operating plans for long-term scalability of department and team.</li>
<li>Project Management: Responsible for providing Account Managers and/or clients with updated implementation plans, which may include project schedules, project plans.</li>
<li>Support Clients &amp; Account Managers on Post-Production, special projects, related to customization, development, enhancements, reporting, etc.</li>
<li>Build and maintain relationships system partners and other vendors.</li>
<li>Engages with Sales team/IT team early in the sales process to assist in customer communications and presentations.</li>
<li>Coordinates and directs all implementation activities; management of schedules and deliverables, the identification of special needs and the fulfillment of all requirements per the contract with partners and clients.</li>
<li>Works with assigned IT reps to validate the scope of work with the assigned account manager to handoff completed project.</li>
<li>Maintain awareness of and ensure adherence to Payments standards regarding privacy.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>PROFESSIONAL EXPERIENCE/QUALIFICATIONS</b></p>

<li>Familiar with common types of connectivity solutions</li>
<li>Experience with different file formats required</li>
<li>Experience working in a SQL environment preferred</li>
<li>Various claim adjudication knowledge preferred</li>
</ul>
