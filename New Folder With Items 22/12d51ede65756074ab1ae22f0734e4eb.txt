<h2>BIM360 Technician</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>BIM360 TechnicianU.S. CAD is a leading provider of software, services and support to the Architecture, Engineering and Construction(AEC) Industry.
</p>
<p>The BIM360 Technician role entails delivery of BIM360 related software, demonstration of otherAutodesk Products, and continued growing knowledge of Construction specific software solutions.
</p>
<p>U.S. CAD willperform on-the-job training and coaching to help assist the growth of this team member in BIM360 and otherrelated solutionsCandidates should be intimately familiar with construction process, specifically as it relates to the RFI/Changeorder procedures.
</p>
<p>BIM is at the core of our services to the AEC community and qualified candidates will understandthe application of BIM within the Construction industry.</p>
<p>
Major Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Interact with sales team, technical team, and management to successfully implement Autodesk BIM360software.</li>
<li>Prepare and deliver remote (web-based) and on-site demonstrations of BIM360 products to clients.</li>
<li>Assist in the development (scope and time estimates) of proposals with direction from sales and otherteam members</li>
<li>Maintain knowledge of competing and complimenting solutions to BIM360</li>
<li>Delivery of BIM360 training courses to customers.</li>
<li>Establish a high level of confidence with U.S. CAD accounts, maintaining a strong customer servicecommitment to accounts of all sizes.</li>
<li>Other duties and responsibilities that may be required to effectively support the needs of our customers,Autodesk and U.S. CAD.</li>
</ul>
<p>
Qualifications:</p>
<ul><li>High school diploma or GED and a minimum of 1-year of Autodesk BIM360 experience</li></ul>
<p>
• Delivered 6 or more BIM360 implementations• Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills</p>
<ul>
<li>Communication and presentation skills and the ability effectively communicate with our clients andteammates.</li>
<li>Proficient with Microsoft Word, PowerPoint, and Excel.</li>
<li>Minimum of 2-years of Autodesk Revit, Navisworks, and Collaboration for Revit experience</li>
<li>Understanding of Project Lifecycles, Construction processes, and project schedules</li>
<li>Maintain a good working attitude and a desire to be a key member of the U.S. CAD team and make adifference in the Architecture, Engineering and Construction (AEC) Industry</li>
<li>Ability to coordinate effectively when multiple project priorities are in conflict and provide viable solutionsto internal and external customersPreferred Qualifications:</li>
<li>Associates or Bachelor’s degree in a related field.</li>
<li>Experience with Autodesk Forge and/or Dynamo</li>
<li>2-years or more of construction-related experience</li>
</ul>
<p>
Job Type: Full-time</p>
