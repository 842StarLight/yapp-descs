<h2>Northwestern Territory Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The <b>Northwestern Territory Manager</b> will maintain and increase the publishing and online services customer base in the established territory in order to continue profitable growth. This position will cover seven states: CO, WY, MT, UT, WA, OR, ID</p>
<p><b>ABOUT THE POSITION:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Create sustainable customer focus in an assigned territory, contacting or visiting each customer at least once per year and documenting call details in Salesforce.com.</li>
<li>Through the process of creating and maintaining documented Opportunities in Salesforce.com, qualify potential new customers, deliver presentations on LPi’s products and services to key decision makers, and turn established prospects into contracted customers. Ensure the opportunity funnel is maintained and continually updated, and opportunities do not extend past their noted Close Date.</li>
<li>Negotiate and close successful agreements at or above the assigned annual sales plan for the territory for new and renewal LPi customers across all LPi product lines, assigned territory, and across all competitors.</li>
<li>Create and maintain records in Salesforce.com that document sales activities: calls, contacts, visits, and files that include all pertinent information on the account, current product, and existing advertisers for the assigned territory.</li>
<li>Review sales pipeline with the Manager to identify and eliminate any obstacles to contract signing.</li>
<li>Work with Stewardship Communications management in order to help set direction, effectively manage time in the field, and establish direct marketing support for assigned territories.</li>
<li>While maintaining accountability for each sales opportunity, advance certain opportunities to sales support teams as necessary for new closings and account retention.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>ABOUT YOU:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in Business or similar field preferred</li>
<li>Minimum five years sales experience</li>
<li>Strong sales personality traits including: optimism, drive, perseverance, personal accountability, and resilience.</li>
<li>Demonstrated flexibility and adaptability to adjust with ease to changing environment and circumstances.</li>
<li>Above average organization and time management skills</li>
<li>Thorough knowledge of marketing practices and skills, in addition to advertising and print industry standards.</li>
<li>Knowledge of church hierarchy, organization, and communication and engagement tools</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal skills and the ability to handle difficult situations with diplomacy and tact.</li>
<li>Above average judgement and decision-making skills.</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written language skills</li>
<li>Ability to effectively use computer and required software platforms as applied by LPi</li>
<li>Ability to work independently with minimal direction.]</li>
</ul>
