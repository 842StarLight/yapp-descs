<h2>Senior Mechanical Engineer Project Manager - Hyper Scale Data Centers</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<b>JOB SUMMARY</b><br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>
</div>
<ul><li> You are a Mechanical Engineer with a positive can-do attitude and seek to overcome unique design challenges.</li></ul>
<ul><li> You have significant CAD/BIM experience and are comfortable both designing and reviewing designs in Revit.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Professional skill set of how design affects construction and ability to learn from construction visits.</li></ul>
<ul><li> You have experience successfully performing QA/QC of design drawings on large scale projects +200k sqft.</li></ul>
<ul><li> You are a great communicator who can express complex ideas in a concise manner through all digital mediums (design drawings, presentations, video conference) both internally and with clients.</li></ul>
<ul><li> You are well organized and can meet changing project priorities to make sure your team and projects stay on schedule.</li></ul>
<ul><li> You have a strong work ethic that internal and external team members can depend on.</li></ul>
<ul><li> You have experience coordinating mechanical scope with multiple discipline trades (Architecture, telecom, electrical, plumbing, structural and civil)</li></ul>
<br>
<div>
</div>
<div><b>
Duties and Responsibilities:
</b></div>
<ul><li> Coordinate documentation of mechanical design, end to end scope, for data center builds</li></ul>
<ul><li> Work with internal stakeholders on mechanical system requirements and translate requirements into design drawings for distribution to external consultants.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Actively review production Revit models and collaborate with external consultants to ensure documentation is at an appropriate level of detail at each design milestone.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Implement automation tools to reduce the production time internally and externally</li></ul>
<ul><li> Establish standard Mechanical BIM content and QC content development for projects at each milestone.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Reviewing in-progress drawings &amp; Autodesk Revit models for coordination issues, and escalate to our consultants to prioritize their focus on critical items affecting schedule to close out during design meetings.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Escalate known design issues that have construction impacts to all parties so there is clear communication to all teams</li></ul>
<ul><li> Review in progress projects for owner standard compliance and provide summaries to owner on deltas for projects that deviate for owner standards due to local requirements</li></ul>
<ul><li> Update existing owner standard plan of record documentation as changes are made</li></ul>
<ul><li> Provide review comments in BIM360</li></ul>
<ul><li> Create discipline specific Revit family library</li></ul>
<ul><li> Provide discipline specific and timely update to overall Revit shared parameters maintained by central BIM team</li></ul>
<ul><li>Other duties as assigned.</li></ul>
