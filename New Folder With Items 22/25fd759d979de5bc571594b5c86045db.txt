Overview</div>
The Master Production Scheduler-ATS is responsible for developing and maintaining schedules, inventory and workload capacity to maximize resource allocations and drive on-time delivery targets. Assess and recommend resource allocations, based on data trends, lean techniques and scheduling needs to meet customer expectations and financial goals.<br>

Responsibilities:<br>
<ul>
<li>Develop schedules, monitor workload capacity and inventory. Optimize production resources to ensure that customer expectations, deadlines and internal financial goals are met</li>
<li>Create, maintain, and post value add metrics i.e.: OTD, CRD, WIP progress, etc.</li>
<li>Plans and provide milestone dates to engineering and Inside sales to meet customer commitments</li>
<li>Develop and maintain the Master product line schedule</li>
<li>Develop and maintain inventory levels for piece parts and sub-assemblies to meet financial plan and organizational goals</li>
<li>Generate shipping plans and coordinate with receiving site logistics</li>
<li>Facilitate production meetings as needed to ensure successful communication and execution to meet customer expectations and financial goals</li>
<li>Act as the custodian of work release packages ensuring completeness and accuracy of BOM</li>
<li>Partner with Purchasing, Sales, Engineering to ensure that materials and product release packages are available as needed during the production process to meet shipping plan</li>
<li>Support the S&amp;OP process meetings to review service levels, performance against schedule, inventory strategy, supply plan to meet demand, and resource implications.</li>
<li>Maintain a Master Production Plan and ensure data reflects updates in real-time</li>
<li>Assist Operations in developing weekly capacity model to drive execution to plan, full utilization of machinery and resources; while maintaining inventory and on time delivery targets</li>
<li>Assess and recommend resource allocations, in-source/out-source of selected parts and outside services to service up/ down swings in demand</li>
<li>Partner with MPS personnel to coordinate and synchronize action plan of feeder work centers to resolve resource constraints due to competing product demands</li>
</ul>
