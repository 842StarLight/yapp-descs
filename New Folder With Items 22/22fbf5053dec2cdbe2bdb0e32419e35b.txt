<p><b>
The Role in Brief:</b></p>
<p><b><i>
Senior Account Project Manager</i></b></p>
<p>
Senior Account Project Managers are responsible for ensuring client obligations are accurately met on schedule, as well as providing direct support for the creation, implementation and deployment of our client’s campaigns, all while displaying strong leadership qualities and expertise. Senior Account Project Managers organize the workflow of client teams and their members, working closely with Operations, Production, Engineering, Finance, Targeting, Strategic Analysts and more. The position also contributes to the development and implementation of organizational and departmental strategies and policies, as well as directly assists in the training and mentoring of team members.</p>
<p>
This position is located in Richmond, Virginia.</p>
<p><b>
Primary Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Attend project kickoffs, internal reviews, forecasting meetings and project status sessions; provide regular status updates; facilitate problem resolution</li>
<li>Reinforce specific guidelines and processes with team members regarding company workflow and client expectations</li>
<li>Work with client teams to develop internal and external project schedules and timelines for client campaign completion, including coordination of project requirements/components and routing type, as well as implementation of marketing direction</li>
<li>Oversee and troubleshoot workflow of client campaigns by using Workfront project management software, while informing team members on project status and progress</li>
<li>Oversee initial deployment of both digital and paper campaigns</li>
<li>Work with vendors to ensure campaigns are reviewed and deployed accurately and on time by serving as the main point of contact and ensuring purchase orders, files, instructions, specifications and approvals are sent in a timely manner</li>
<li>Manage campaign completion by shutting down programs at the vendor and internally updating Workfront and storing any physical documentation
</li>
<li>Manage inventory disposition and monitor inventory levels of printed materials for all campaigns</li>
<li>Work to continually improve and optimize company workflow, best practices and production solutions for new and existing clients</li>
<li>Create, implement and maintain account project management documentation and training materials from onboarding to refresher courses
</li>
<li>Maintain and manage yearly schedule of training and education and product reviews</li>
<li>Work with department management to participate in interview process</li>
<li>Coach and mentor junior account project managers</li>
<li>Provide training presentations, timely communication and direction throughout the project lifecycle on designated subject matter expert topic(s)</li>
<li>Work with account project manager SME’s to ensure training materials are continuously updated and accurate</li>
<li>Meet regularly with team members to discuss process updates, best practices and training opportunities for the operations function</li>
 <li>Lead special projects and assignments while providing updates to sponsors</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Basic Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Proficient with PC and Microsoft Office suite</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree</li>
<li>4 years + project management/planning/scheduling experience, including demonstrated knowledge and application of poject management practices, tools and technologies
</li>
<li>Proven ability to handle multiple campaigns concurrently under tight deadlines, exhibiting a high level of initiative, motivation and flexibility to meet ever-changing client and team demands</li>
<li>History of building relationships, collaborating and influencing others for the achievement of project success</li>
<li>Proactively identify risks, resolve issues, and remove impediments that jeopardize success of the deliverables</li>
<li>Execute effectively by using resources efficiently, meeting deadlines, and keeping others informed</li>
<li>Excellent written and oral communication skills</li>
<li>Demonstrated organizational skills and attention to detail</li>
<li>Proven ability to identify risks, resolve issues and remove impediments that jeopardize success of the deliverables</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal and client focus skills</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Ideal Qualifications:</b></p>

<li>Experience in a creative or marketing environment</li>
<li>Experience training and mentoring others</li>
</ul>
