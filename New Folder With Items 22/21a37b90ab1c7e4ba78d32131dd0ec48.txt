Our Fabrication/Tech Shop in Anderson, California, has an immediate opening for an Industrial Software Developer. This position utilizes state-of-the-art imaging equipment and processing to optimize production. This is a unique position that allows you to be involved from concept to commissioning.<br>
<br>
<b>About The Position</b><br>
<li>Engineer high-performance, low-latency software to optimize the production and yield during manufacturing.</li>
<br>
<li>Architect complex algorithms to mathematically model multiple dependent geometric axes.</li>
<br>
<li>Collaborate closely with Control Systems Designers and Mechanical engineers to create solutions that meet production requirements.</li>
<br>
<li>Develop and implement updates for a variety of existing computer programs.</li>
<br>
<li>Assess and define scope and timelines for assigned work.</li>
<br>
<li>Manage development efforts including pre-plan reviews, projected cost analysis, resource availability, and related documentation.</li>
<br>
<li>Provide some phone support for division technicians for new and existing software.</li>
<br>
<li>Some overnight travel to locations in California and Washington.</li>
<br>
<br>
<b>Qualifications</b><br>
<li>Minimum 2 years work experience in related field.</li>
<br>
<li>B.S. in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, or comparable degree.</li>
<br>
<li>Solid understanding of geometry and trigonometry.</li>
<br>
<li>Excellent analytic and problem-solving skills.</li>
<br>
<li>Excellent communication skills.</li>
<br>
<li>Thorough knowledge of Windows desktop programming using Microsoft Visual C++.</li>
