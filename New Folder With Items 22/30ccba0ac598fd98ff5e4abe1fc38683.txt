<p>*<br><b>About the Company: </b></p>
<p>Since 2017, Smilelove has been bringing the benefits of straight teeth and a confident smile direct to individual consumers. At Smilelove, we believe everyone deserves a confident smile. We also believe that you deserve a job you will love, and a job that will empower you with the opportunities to change peoples’ lives.</p>
<p>We’re on a mission to make a confident smile accessible to people in every town in the country. <p><b>Scope of Responsibilities</b><br>*</p>

<li>Electronic transmission of customer records</li>
<li>Genuine and enthusiastic face to face engagement with customers that builds connection and trust</li>
<li>Taking orthodontic photos</li>

</ul>
