<h2>Marketing “Help Desk” Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>The Marketing “Help Desk” Specialist is responsible for providing direct assistance to sales agents and employees to effectively utilize the company's Concierge agent product offering. This position will support rollouts of new marketing technology as well as provide continuous training, deliver guidance and support to agents and employees with new marketing/technology systems and infrastructure.<br>
<br>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES &amp; RESPONSIBILITIES<br>
<ul>
<li>
Serve as the point of contact for agents on marketing technology issues</li>
<li>
Provide clear support to agents using our company tools to help the agent build their business from a marketing perspective</li>
<li>
Assist Concierge teams in coordinating and executing all activities related to web, online and platform/tool initiatives</li>
<li>
Assist in roll outs and promotion of company marketing tools/initiatives</li>
<li>
Serve in critical quality assurance role on Concierge product suite</li>
<li>
Help support local marketing efforts where needed</li>
<li>
Support national initiatives and product roll-outs by participating on Go to Market Teams</li>
</ul>
REQUIREMENTS<ul>
<li>
Limited HTML experience is preferred</li>
<li>
Ability to learn new software programs</li>
<li>
Ability to troubleshoot technical issues and effectively train on marketing technology and tools</li>
<li>
Technologically proficient. Familiarity with various operating systems on PCs, and ability to navigate basic computer software and hardware issues</li>
<li>
Must be well-organized, process/detail oriented with ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously</li>
<li>
Ability to multi-task, prioritize, perform under pressure, and be flexible with changing business needs</li>
<li>
1-2 years digital marketing experience preferred</li>
<li>
Excellent interpersonal and customer service skills</li>
<li>
Ability to change gears quickly to support different customers</li>
<li>
Team player and an excellent communicator who can work with a variety of people at various levels throughout the company</li>
<li>
Can work autonomously, proactively identifying and impacting opportunities while also identifying and resolving issue</li>
<li>
Strong proficiency with Microsoft Office Suite, including Outlook, Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and Publisher</li>
</ul>
