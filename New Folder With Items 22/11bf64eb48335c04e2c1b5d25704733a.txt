<h2>Content Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Requisition Number: </b><b>74028</b></p>
<p>
As a Content Managert, for Insight, you will enter a high-energy and tech savvy world. We are looking for someone who is a team player, is an excellent communicator, and has exemplary organizational and technical writing skills.</p>
<p>
 As a Content Contract Specialist, you can expect to:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Multi-task – You will be responsible for creating and managing a variety of content for Statements of Work (SOWs) and other contractual documents to ensure all required information is in place and accurate.</li>
<li>
Take Ownership – You will own the process of updating the content and templates required to complete SOWs and contractual documents, interacting with people at all levels of the company including Technical and Services Organizations, Sales, and Business Units.</li>
<li>
Be Challenged – Your job is never done as a Contract Specialist. This role requires the critical thinking to be able to produce a customized document for a particular client when predefined processes and templates will not work.</li>
<li>
Use your knowledge – As a senior member, you will apply your knowledge to achieve additional tasks and responsibilities to support the team.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
What we are looking for:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Prior content management, technical writing, SOW development, and/or contract administration experience.</li>
<li>
Strong organizational and time management skills.</li>
<li>
Ability to multi-task and manage multiple projects simultaneously.</li>
<li>
Detailed, methodical, and accurate (high attention to detail).</li>
<li>
Expertly skilled in Microsoft Word, Excel and PowerPoint (particularly when it comes to formatting content).</li>
<li>
Excellent writing skills, including spelling and grammar, and ability to apply writing skills to communicate complex concepts in clear, concise language.</li>
<li>
Polished and professional in all communications, with ability to interact with all levels in the organization (and with external partners).</li>
<li>
A high level of digital skills and understanding (tech-savvy) with some experience using content management tools and systems.</li>
<li>
Solid understanding of all the processes that surround the content (industry/domain knowledge).</li>
<li>
Project management skills (to drive regular reviews and content intake/expiration).</li>
<li>
A Bachelor’s Degree in a business-related or English field preferred.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Duties include:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Creating/maintaining a repository of SOW content, including technical/service offering language and contract terms.</li>
<li>
Collaborating with other teams to write consistent, clear, professional content.</li>
<li>
Ensuring content is well-structured, well-categorized, and at the correct level of granularity to promote reuse and ease of searching.</li>
<li>
Creating a standard practice related to naming conventions and labelling (metadata) for efficient content management, using industry best practices.</li>
<li>
Creating and managing templates, making the best decisions regarding template structure to suit the business purpose.</li>
<li>
Creating and managing many standard style sets applicable to templates; need solid understanding of style sets and how they integrate/interact with stored content.</li>
<li>
Ensuring that content is reviewed, through software workflows, and that out-dated content is expired/archived.</li>
<li>
Proofreading and editing all content to ensure language is clear, concise, and meets Insight requirements.</li>
<li>
Obtaining all necessary internal approvals for content.</li>
<li>
Assessing search reports periodically to ensure that the search experience provides the best outcomes for all end-users.</li>
<li>
Assessing content usage reports periodically to ensure that all areas of content are identified and added to, and all unused content is updated or assessed for expiration.</li>
<li>
Working with Qorus and internal teams to improve the content management system environment where necessary, based on user feedback and reporting.</li>
<li>
Acting as Qorus expert for the team, assisting with documentation, training, future enhancements and support of end users, internally, to assist in finding the correct content and templates.</li>
<li>
Creating/maintaining process documents, ensuring team has latest information.</li>
<li>
Support for team, as needed, in creating SOWs and other contractual documents:<ul>
<li>
Creating documents, ensuring appropriate templates are used (ex: SOW vs. Standalone) and all required master agreements are in place.</li>
<li>
Proofreading and editing all content, ensuring language is clear and concise, and meets Insight requirements.</li>
<li>
Obtaining all necessary internal approvals.</li>
<li>
Working independently and with little supervision, managing work assignments in workflow tool and addressing any concerns/requests by the deadlines.</li>
<li>
Applying critical thinking t input, determining best approach for each document.</li>
<li>
Taking accountability for leading and managing a pursuit team in the SOW development process from deal kickoff to executive review.</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
