<h2>Equipment Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>
<div><p>JOB OVERVIEW</p></div>
<div>
<p>Manages the assignment, maintenance and repair of the organization's vehicles and equipment. Implements vehicle and equipment preventive maintenance and repair programs with guidance from Senior Equipment Management. Ensures processes are followed and all equipment management systems are updated timely and accurately. Familiar with a variety of the field operations’ concepts, practices, and work procedures. Analyzes life cycle and utilization rates making recommendations on replacement, addition and / or sale of assets as needed. Follows procedures for acquisition and divestiture of fleet assets including on-road and off-road equipment.</p>
</div>
</div>
<div>
<div><p>KEY RESPONSIBILITIES/ ACCOUNTABILITIES:</p></div>
<div><div>
<p>Interact with Equipment leadership to fill needs identified by Operations.</p>
<p>Develop and maintain adequate equipment pool to see that quick operational and project requirements can be met when needed.</p>
<p>Ensure systems are updated timely and accurately and that equipment location assignments are always accurate.</p>
<p>Oversee shop processes that ensure all Fleet assets are properly maintained at appropriate intervals.</p>
<p>Evaluate repair activities and makes decisions on whether corrective action is the best decision considering the ownership status, book value / lease life remaining, cost, etc.</p>
<p>Monitor and manage expenditures on staff, maintenance, equipment repairs and purchases.</p>
<p>Responsible for compliance to all Company policies and practices as well as any government regulations.</p>
</div></div>
</div>
<div>
<div><p>BASIC QUALIFICATIONS:</p></div>
<div><div>
<p>At least 5 years’ experience in fleet and equipment operations, preferable in the utility construction space.</p>
<p>Ability to follow instruction and communicate effectively.</p>
<p>Proven ability to multi-task.</p>
<p>Think and act strategically carrying out long-term plans to meet operational needs and departmental goals.</p>
<p>Management experience over staff of mechanics, drivers and warehouse personnel</p>
<p>Sound knowledge of computer software such as Access, Excel, Vista- Viewpoint.</p>
</div></div>
</div>
<div>
<div><p>COMPANY OVERVIEW:</p></div>
<div>
<p>Primoris T&amp;D provides engineering, construction, and maintenance of electric power delivery facilities and gas distribution installations, including substations up to 345kV. Ancillary services include wireless telecommunication installation, environmental cleanup, project management, and storm restoration services.</p>
<p>Primoris T&amp;D is a full-service contractor dedicated to serving the electric, telecommunication, and gas industry. With over 70 years of operating experience, our collective services include engineering and design, installation and maintenance, material and equipment procurement, and repair of electrical transmission, distribution, substation, wireless, and gas distribution systems. Our collective experience ranges from small engineering and consulting projects to multi-million dollar turn-key distribution, substation, and transmission line programs.</p>
