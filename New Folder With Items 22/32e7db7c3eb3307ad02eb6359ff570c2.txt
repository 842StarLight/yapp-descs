<div>
This position is responsible for supervising Inventory Control Coordinators, overseeing all elements of Store and Distribution Center perpetual inventory accuracy, ensuring perpetual inventory records reconcile to the general ledger, establishing new inventory control standards, addressing inventory data control exceptions due to process errors or system related failures, developing continuous improvement of inventory data flow by collaborating with Store and DC Operations Management and IT personnel to implement inventory control process and systematic solutions.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Supervise, direct, prioritize and measure the quantity and quality of the work of the WMS and Inventory Control Coordinators, who are primarily responsible for working to maintain accurate inventories by reviewing, analyzing and correcting data errors made by Store and DC personnel as they make inventory adjustments by keying Receipts, Junk, Theft, Store Use, Super-peg, Defectives, Vendor Buy-backs, etc. to the perpetual inventory along with analyzing data sync jobs, to ensure that any failures between Store or DC systems are identified and corrected timely.</li>
<li>
Manage ongoing reconciliations and evaluations of system interfaces between SAP and other systems, which are primarily the Store Point Of Sale (POS) system and the DC Warehouse Management System (WMS). Ensure perpetual inventory records reconcile to the general ledger. Identify data flow failure trends and root causes. Collaborate with Store, DC and Information Technology resources to establish, test, and implement system improvement solutions.
</li>
<li>Define, implement, and monitor compliance with policies and procedures relating to inventory process and financial controls to ensure proper valuation of all distribution center inventory, including the cycle counting program and all other inventory adjustments. Analyze distribution center inventory adjustment activities to identify root causes and partner with distribution operations leadership, merchandising, inventory management, vendors, and others to develop and implement actions to drive continuous improvement of inventory accuracy.</li>
<li>
Develop the business requirements and solutions to deliver the most efficient and accurate inventory movement protocols to support developing business trend with Omni-channel, warehouse management systems, inventory transfers, shipping and receiving accuracy and efficiency, and all other business developments that impact inventory transactions.
</li>
<li>Coordinate research of all inventory control movement abnormalities that are identified by other team members and by monitoring exception trends to execute a twofold actin plan:
<ul>
<li>Immediate response to store, distribution center, or support teams followed by appropriate inventory correction</li>
<li>
Research, identify, and implement solution to prevent future occurrence of a similar issue when feasible. Analyze inventory exceptions to identify trends, determine root causes, and lead testing of solutions to recommend best course of action</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Manage and review analysis of inventory status for all stores and distribution centers to identify and correct stagnant systematic inventory that does not reflect proper physical location. Coordinate efforts with appropriate cross-functional team members to develop and implement process improvements to eliminate root causes</li>
<li>
Organize activities and provide continuous feedback to direct reports. Identify, support, and plan career and skill development</li>
<li>
May perform other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
