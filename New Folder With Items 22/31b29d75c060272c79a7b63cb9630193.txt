<p>Scope of Position</p>
<p>The Beverage Cart Attendant will provide prompt, courteous and efficient food and beverage service on our golf course in a luxury brand inspired hotel environment, where attention to detail is key.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Position Requirements</p>
<ul>
<li>Professional demeanor appropriate for a luxury environment.</li>
<li>Basic knowledge of Micros or other Point of Sale (POS) Systems.</li>
<li>Previous food and beverage experience in a luxury hotel setting preferred.</li>
<li>Of minimum age to serve alcohol according to state law.</li>
<li>Food handling certificate preferred.</li>
<li>Knowledge of food and wine preferred.</li>
<li>Golf knowledge preferred.</li>
<li>Excellent eye for detail and the ability to multi-task.</li>
<li>Ability to anticipate prevent, identify, and solve problems as necessary.</li>
<li>Ability to maintain composure and objectivity under pressure.</li>
<li>Effective at listening, understanding, and clarifying the concerns and issues raised by guests.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Responsibilities</p>
<ul>
<li>Greet each guest promptly in a sincerely warm, friendly manner using the guestâ€™s name.
</li>
<li>Know the ingredients, preparation, and proper presentation of all menu items and specials and ensure that every product delivered to the guest adheres to the highest quality possible.</li>
<li>Remain observant and respond to each guest at all times.</li>
<li>Provide prompt, courteous and efficient food and beverage service.</li>
<li>Know layout of hotel and golf course and location of all equipment and supplies.</li>
<li>Check and stock cart with all food and beverage items.</li>
<li>Maintain cleanliness and condition of the work areas and equipment.</li>
<li>Implement standards/procedures of service for guest consistently.</li>
<li>Make appropriate suggestion of food and beverages (employs suggestive selling and up selling technique).
</li>
<li>Take guests' orders promptly according to JW Marriott Turnberry Miami Resort &amp; Spa standards.
</li>
<li>Perform wine service according to procedures.</li>
<li>Keys in (pre-checks) order on the Micros (POS) terminal promptly and accurately.
</li>
<li>Perform any other reasonable duties as required by management.</li>
<br>
</ul>
