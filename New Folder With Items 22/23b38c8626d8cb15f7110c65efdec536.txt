<h2>Client Solutions Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Client Solutions Specialist</b><br>eCare Vault is a privacy-compliant collaboration and communication platform that connects educators, family members, health and other professionals, to collaborate seamlessly and securely around a person or a project they are working on. Documents and discussions can be securely shared between team members to facilitate real time communication and sharing of information in support of a person or project. eCare Vault adheres to the highest of federal privacy standards, including HIPAA (health) and FERPA (education) and is available as a mobile app as well.</p>
<p>eCare Vault is growing and looking to add a Client Solutions Specialist to our team! The Client Solutions Specialist will be responsible for the overall product implementation, relationship management and retention of new and existing clients. This position will be integral when on-boarding new customers post sale and continuing after on-boarding is complete. Ideally, this candidate will have experience in the healthcare/pharma space. Come and join our fun and vibrant work environment in the heart of downtown Boston!</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities: </b></p>
<p><b>Relationship Management</b>. The Client Solutions Specialist is responsible for growing and building long-term relationships with the client base. Supporting clients to increase retention and maintain renewals along with additional module purchases.</p>
<p><b>Implementation / Client Onboarding</b>. Lead and support client implementation and adoption including data, training, analysis, change management, project management, etc. ensuring client’s full use of product to drive success. Ability to lead and document workflow mapping sessions with client and produce documentation and recommendations.</p>
<p><b>Oral Communication</b>. This role will speak to clients of all levels on a regular basis. Communication with the customer must be clear, empathetic and friendly. This role will also regularly communicate across teams within eCare Vault, sharing important customer issues or escalating concerns when appropriate. Comfort with communication over email, in person or over the phone is key!</p>
<p><b>Written Communication: </b> This position requires the production of client facing materials, including slides, user manuals and mocks of future-state functionality of our platform. Hence, The Client Solutions Specialist should have advanced skills in using Google Slides, Powerpoint, Figma, Lucid Charts etc, and a quick learner of technology solutions that enable project management and process flow mapping.</p>
<p><b>Decision-making</b>. The Client Solutions Specialist will take both requests and concerns from the customer or potential customer and determine how to triage the issue to reach the best resolution within an appropriate timeline. Decision-making skills are vital to this role.</p>
<p><b>Client Support. </b>The Client Solutions Specialist will be the bridge connecting clients to eCare Vault. They will be responsible for sharing helpful resources with the client, resolving client issues, alerting the client to features that may be helpful to them, and creating a customer comfort level when using eCare Vault.</p>
<p><b>Technology</b>. As a technology company, the Client Solutions Specialist will be expected to use email, social media, and be an expert with both the eCare Vault app and web-based product.</p>
<p><b>Organization. </b>This role will be responsible for appropriate documentation, tracking and follow up of each issue or concern that they receive. Along with proper documentation will be timely, professional responses to both the client externally and the team internally.</p>
<p><b>Product Design. </b>The Client Solutions Specialist will be responsible for listening closely to the customer’s needs and bringing product ideas back to the Product Team, by documenting use cases, mapping workflows and providing input for product design.</p>
<p><b>Customer KPI Metrics. </b>This role will be responsible for establishing the key metrics for customer success, tracking pre-post measurements and course-correcting as needed, to drive maximum customer satisfaction.</p>
<p><b>Feedback to eCare Vault leadership</b>: The Client Solutions Specialist will have a hand on the pulse of the client relationships and provide regular written updates, including a SWOT (Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, Threats) analysis for the likelihood of renewal.</p>
<p>Requirements:</p>
<ul>
<li>4-6 years in a customer service role, project management, or implementation</li>
<li>Experience working in the technology field, preferably in the sale of a SAAS solution</li>
<li>Proven ability to work efficiently while maintaining a calm and friendly demeanor</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree preferred</li>
<li>Ability to multitask and prioritize customer issues and needs</li>
<li>Flexibility to travel to clients sites on short notice</li>
<li>Comfortable working in both a team situation and autonomously</li>
<li>Massachusetts based in commuting distance to the Back Bay, Boston</li>
</ul>
