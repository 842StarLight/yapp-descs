<h2>Plant Controller</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>Careers across Quad are defined by a rich legacy of passionate people driven by innovation and motivated to create a better way. Here, you'll discover roles that provide the chance to help transform our business while providing insightful financial information needed to make critical decisions to help drive growth and improve financial results. Our values have guided us from day one, creating an intense pride in knowing that every day is an opportunity to thrill our clients, side-by-side with the larger community we all call Quad.<br>
<div>Quad (NYSE:QUAD), a global marketing services provider that helps brand owners market their products, services and content more efficiently and effectively, is seeking a highly-motivated professional to fill a Plant Controller opening at our Leominster, Massachusetts Plant. This location is part of our growing QuadPackaging business. QuadPackaging offers a wide range of advanced end-to-end packaging solutions, specializing in high-end paperboard folding cartons.<br>
<br>
The Plant Controller will join our Accounting and Plant leadership teams and be responsible for accounting, financial analysis and planning, and internal controls. We will count on this individual to help drive sales growth and margin improvement as a business partner to the business development and manufacturing teams.<br>
<br>
Job duties include but are not limited to the following:<br>
<ul>
<li>Complete financial understanding of the business/operation</li>
<li>Prepare timely and accurate analysis of financial and operational data, highlighting key financial indicators, trends, and business issues driving performance to targeted goals and initiatives</li>
<li>Participate in the monthly, quarterly and annual financial closing process, including preparing the detailed financial statements and supporting schedules.</li>
<li>Prepare annual Plan and Forecasts, including but not limited to, plant level consolidation and analysis of P&amp;L variances and key performance indicators</li>
<li>Complete required monthly variance explanation and documentation</li>
<li>Respond to day-to-day financial inquiries and analysis</li>
<li>Identify and implement profit improvement plans</li>
<li>Communicate monthly financial results to the leadership team</li>
<li>Communicate issues to the leadership team to ensure that they are operating on a consistent basis with the corporate goals and strategies</li>
<li>Benchmark key metrics with peer operations to identify improvement opportunities and establish goals</li>
<li>Ensure compliance with Sarbanes-Oxley and internal control documentation, testing, compliance and auditing</li>
<li>Ensure compliance with GAAP and Corporate Accounting Policies</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Must have a bachelor’s degree in Finance or Accounting, or equivalent working experience in the Accounting field</li>
<li>Five or more years of related Accounting/Finance experience in a manufacturing or printing environment is preferred</li>
<li>Must have the desire and ability to understand the business/operation</li>
<li>Must be detail orientated and process driven, with an ability to handle multiple projects simultaneously</li>
<li>Must have a solid knowledge of accounting principles, excellent analytical, communication and presentation skills, and be comfortable working independently, as well as acting as a change agent within a team</li>
<li>Must have strong computer and data analysis skills, and possess working knowledge of financial systems and Microsoft software (Excel, Word, PowerPoint), and an ability to quickly learn systems supporting the business.</li>
</ul>
