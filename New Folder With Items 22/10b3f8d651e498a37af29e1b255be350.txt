<h2>Field Solutions Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>The Field Solutions Engineer is a datacenter generalist that will possess prior experience in Infrastructure, Virtualization and/or Network architecture. SHI is passionate about providing our customers with the right solutions that enable Data Center optimization. As more customers are relying on SHI to architect and deliver these solutions, our Enterprise Solutions Group needs the advanced skill to continue to meet the demand and needs of our customers and is responsible for the Data Center sales attainment goals in supported territory.<br>This position supports the Richmond, VA territory.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities/Job Function</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for the Data Center sales attainment goals in supported territory</li>
<li>Pipeline management and ownership for Data Center opportunities</li>
<li>Account retention and penetrating multiple lines of Data Center business in existing accounts</li>
<li>Interface with multiple layers of contacts within an organization, including but not limited to CIO, IT Directors, CTO, Application Managers, etc.</li>
<li>Assist SHI Account Executives with customer facing meetings, conference calls &amp; presentations</li>
<li>Lead Data Center Solution positioning and qualification conversations with customers</li>
<li>Represent SHI at relevant industry events with speaking slots encouraged</li>
<li>Drive the sales process of the solution, including identifying the opportunity, qualifying, forecasting, decision making criteria, and closing</li>
<li>Build the region/territory based on strategic account planning done in collaboration with the account executive and extended team Continuously educate and develop the Sales Team’s technical sales acumen</li>
<li>Build deep relationships and trust with customers and owns the technical side of customer relationships.</li>
<li>Demonstrates leadership to ensure that the SHI’s recommended enterprise solutions are comprehensive, achieve customer expectations and meet customer business needs and desired outcomes</li>
<li>Support RFPs by aligning key internal personnel to assist with responses</li>
<li>Align SHI’s Professional Services portfolio with the solutions being developed</li>
<li>Establish relationships with preferred Delivery partners, vet their performance acceptability, and build integration within the region</li>
<li>Develop relationships with the local technology community (manufacturers and SI Partners) and the appropriate technical personnel to establish market credibility</li>
<li>Will travel as required by the role</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications/Minimum Skills Required</b></p>
<p>Bachelor's or equivalent relevant experience.<br>5 years + Experience working in complex Data Center sales opportunities</p>
<ul>
<li>Advanced knowledge of servers, storage, networking and virtualization regarding their impact on the business</li>
<li>Knowledge of Converged Infrastructure of servers, storage, networking and virtualization regarding their impact on the business</li>
<li>Expertise in mainstream storage technologies to include: Dell/EMC, NetApp, HPE, Pure Storage, Public and Private Cloud Solutions</li>
<li>Strong knowledge of Virtualization technologies, Hypervisors, server and desktop virtualization (i.e. VMware, Hyper-V, Citrix, VDI)</li>
<li>Deep Familiarity with the RFP process and possess a winning record</li>
<li>Experience with Disaster Recovery, Business Continuity and High Availability Solutions ( backup/recovery, mirroring, active/standby, active/active, clustering)</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Skills Required</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Ability and desire to work within a team environment, and at times independently</li>
<li>Proactive mindset. Sales mentality.</li>
<li>Highly organized individual</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication, listening, negotiation and presentation skills.</li>
<li>Ability to work effectively, add value as a team member and assume a leadership role for the team.</li>
<li>Demonstrated technical knowledge and consultative skills.</li>
<li>Strong documentation skills to include system/network diagrams and presentations (Visio, PowerPoint, Excel, Word)</li>
<li>Ability to train and disseminate information within an Area or Operation.</li>
<li>Ability to understand complex technical and selling situations and the ability to solve the problem or solicit the required resources.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Skills Preferred: </b><br>Preferred certifications: VMware VCP/VTSP, Hyper-converged: Nutanix, VxRail, vSAN. Hybrid/Public Cloud: AWS/Azure<br>5 -10 years Previous IT Administrator/Management experience desired</p>
<ul><li>Previous experience working with Large Enterprise, SLED and Higher Education organizations preferred</li></ul>
