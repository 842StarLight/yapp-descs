<div>Amazon Web Service (AWS) is a comprehensive, evolving, and growing business unit within Amazon.com which provides cloud services. <br>
The AWS Global Real Estate Team is looking for a talented, experienced Portfolio Manager based in our Seattle corporate office campus. In this role, you will be a key member of the Real Estate Team and will be responsible for coordinating and managing data center real estate approval processes and metrics; end-to-end ownership of financial requirements for real estate acquisitions; coordinate legal documentation requests from property owners and additional property portfolio management duties.<br>
<br>
The ideal candidate needs to be detailed oriented and a strong communicator that can work independently and will possess a high level of competence in performing a full range of portfolio responsibilities in an extremely fast paced environment with a high degree of ambiguity.<br>
<br>
Key Responsibilities include:<br>
<ul>
<li>Collect, coordinate and manage real estate approval processes</li>
<li>Collect, coordinate and manage real estate metrics via Sales Force</li>
<li>Collect and disseminate regular reports to finance on various metrics</li>
<li>Manage portfolio reporting and tracking, create dashboards, and perform analysis</li>
<li>Create and track key metrics, KPI’s and SLA’s</li>
<li>Responsible for creating and managing weekly, monthly, and quarterly business reviews</li>
<li>Drive process implementation and improvements</li>
<li>Identification of cost saving opportunities with existing sites and financial analysis</li>
<li>Assist in portfolio optimization and the creation of templates</li>
</ul>
