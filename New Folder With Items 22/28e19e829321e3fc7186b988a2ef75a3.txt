<h2>Client Account Manager, Tech/Gaming</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div><div><div><div><div><div>
<p>Twitter has changed the way people communicate. Now we have a substantial opportunity to change how marketers interact with our rapidly growing user base. We are creating a world-class team of media professionals, and seeking an experienced Account Manager to develop our business with advertisers and their agencies.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
If you’re an enthusiastic Twitter user with relevant account management experience, analytical skills, and a passion for learning, we invite you to talk to us about our advertising sales organization at Twitter, Inc.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Collaborating with Fortune 500 advertisers to understand their objectives, recommending best practices, and developing effective campaigns</li>
<li>
Educate and consult to demonstrate how to use Twitter’s advertising products, best practices, how to develop effective campaigns.</li>
<li>
Executing, optimizing, and analyzing advertising programs using internal tools and dashboards.</li>
<li>
Resolving campaign issues in a timely and productive manner, and identifying and implementing process improvements</li>
<li>
Managing contracts and renewals, and assisting our Sales Finance organization with payables and collections</li>
<li>
Providing feedback to our product and tools teams to help us scale our advertising platform</li>
<li>
Mentoring new team members as our organization grows</li>
</ul>
<br>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
4+ years of experience managing digital marketing programs for advertisers or their agencies</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to create, develop, and enhance customer relationships</li>
<li>
Ability to manage large customer relationships and manage through conflict, driving groups to collective business decision</li>
<li>
Proven track record translating marketing objectives into results for advertisers. Demonstrated experience effectively optimizing marketing campaigns by analyzing performance and recommending adjustments to key performance drivers.</li>
<li>
Excellent communication and presentation skills, attention to detail, and a bias for proactively resolving issues</li>
<li>
Aptitude, creativity, and a preference for working in small, collaborative teams with minimal supervision</li>
<li>
Must have strong understanding of digital marketplace and understanding of the competitive landscape in which we operate</li>
<li>
A passion for Twitter and our mission</li>
<li>
BA / BS degree</li>
</ul>
