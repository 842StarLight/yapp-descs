<h2>Implementation Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">-
<br>About the Role
<br>-
<br><br>
Do you have a knack for developing solutions to ambiguous technical software requirements? Do you enjoy working with enterprise customers to deliver world-class service? Then you may be the Implementation Consultant we're looking for on our team!
<br><br>
Our Implementation Consultants help to drive the post-sales execution of software development projects for end-clients (municipalities, agencies, universities, private operators). They establish expertise with the Passport systems and work with Project Managers to build out an awesome product for each client's specifications. The ability to problem solve, configure and train clients on their new system, and collaborate with Development, and Support Services teams to resolve issues is crucial.
<br><br>
Since our Implementation Consultants play such a critical role liaising between our clients and our software engineers, a background in technology (educational, professional, or otherwise) and an aptitude for solving ambiguous technical issues will be advantageous to a new hire!
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Assist Project Managers in onboarding a client onto the Passport platform. This typically includes: environment buildout &amp; system configuration and setting up third-party integrations</li>
<li>Build operational standards within proprietary framework, testing user scenarios, develop processes, and strategize with Support Services and Development teams to ensure a top-notch implementation/end product</li>
<li>Generate Scope of Work (SOW) documents for integrations using information gained from Solutions Engineering and customer intake meetings</li>
<li>Develop internal and client-facing user guides to be used post go live</li>
<li>Train clients on functionality, configuration, and troubleshooting of Passport software and hardware systems related to their operations</li>
<li>Provide transitional client support post-go-live, ensuring project success prior to Customer Support handoff for ongoing system support</li>
<li>Become a subject matter expert on the Passport system, helping to document and build up our knowledge base</li>
</ul>
