<h2>Healthcare Advertising Project Management Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Are you an experienced advertising account specialist looking for greater involvement in your campaigns' implementation and success, or for marketing position with a greater purpose than driving sales? This is a unique your opportunity to advance your career – one that you’ll find challenging and rewarding.</p>
<p><b>Who We Are</b></p>
<p>For three decades, BBK Worldwide’s pioneering vision has made us the global leaders in the field of patient recruitment, offering a full spectrum of marketing communications solutions that help pharmaceutical, biopharmaceutical, and medical device companies find participants for their clinical trials of products in development. Comprised of the most talented and dedicated professionals in the industry, we provide our clients with an unparalleled level of service and best-in-class solutions.</p>
<p><b>About the Position</b></p>
<p>To service new accounts, we are expanding our client services department and looking for an account specialist. The account specialist contributes to and coordinates team members’ and other advertising specialists’ activities related to the development and implementation of global and domestic client campaigns.</p>
<p>The ideal candidate possesses strong operational and communication skills. A four-year college degree is required, with liberal arts / critical thinking coursework a plus. One-to-two years of project management, clinical trial industry, and/or healthcare marketing experience or similar, is a plus.</p>
<p><b>Key responsibilities include: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Providing day-to-day operational support as part of an account team, such as attending internal and external meetings and client phone conferences, preparing agendas and call reports for meetings, circulating materials for review and approval, and maintaining schedules, critical paths, and project budgets within BBK's project management system.</li>
<li>Coordinating the timely completion of the work of specialists (e.g., creative, research, and production) involved in the development and implementation of campaigns.</li>
<li>Monitoring, reviewing, and reporting on data that tracks campaign progress and effectiveness.</li>
<li>Providing medical professionals with information about campaign programs, strategies, enrollment objectives, materials approval, and tactical implementation timelines.</li>
</ul>
