<h2>Digital Account Coordinator - B2B Marketing</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>This role will provide autonomy and responsibility with direction when you need it. And you will be working with some of the world's most dynamic companies, helping to transform marketing from an enabler to a driver of business success.</p>
<p>
This is a great opportunity for a recent college graduate or career-changer with an interest in B2B marketing to work side-by-side with passionate professionals and develop strong project management and client relationship skills. The role will incorporate being an enthusiastic and engaged member of a team; writing contact reports; developing project management skills; administrative support and more.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Perform administrative tasks related to account management, including scheduling of meetings, creating and publishing agendas, taking thorough call notes and publishing notes to clients</li>
<li>Assist in development of presentation materials</li>
<li>Track budgets, report on actual hours burned per project, and gather project status from delivery resources (internal and external)</li>
<li>Prepare budget reports for clients</li>
<li>Assist in creation of project plans and ensure all plans are updated</li>
<li>Set up resource plans in Netsuite, create content repositories in Basecamp, set up Jira structure for new projects, and create foldering structure for each new client/project</li>
<li>Coordinate POs for external resources</li>
<li>Perform monthly research into industry trends and share with the wider organization</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Essential requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree with a concentration in Marketing, Communications, or related field</li>
<li>Minimum of 1 year experience working at a digital marketing agency or at least 1 internship with an agency or company's digital marketing team or significant experience working in a professional business environment</li>
<li>Project management and/or customer service experience a plus</li>
<li>Superior organizational skills and ability to juggle multiple, conflicting tasks and deadlines</li>
<li>Comfort with Microsoft Office Suite, and aptitude for learning new software applications</li>
<li>Strong written and verbal communication skills, and an eye for detail and design</li>
<li>Intellectual curiosity, energy, enthusiasm, and analytical thinking</li>
</ul>
