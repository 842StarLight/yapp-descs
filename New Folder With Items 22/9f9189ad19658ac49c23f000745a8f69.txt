<div><b>Job Description</b></div>
<div>
<b>
Industry Applications-CMT</b> professionals design, implement and deploy packaged software solutions that have been developed to accommodate unique Communications, Media and Technology industry business and management processes, regulatory requirements and other business needs.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>
CMT-Industry Applications</b> professionals analyze, design, build, test and deploy innovative Communications, Media and Technology industry-specific solutions that enable industry standards and processes.</div>
<div>
A professional at this position level within Accenture has the following responsibilities:<br>
Identifies, assesses and solves <b>complex business problems</b> for area of responsibility, where analysis of situations or data requires an in-depth evaluation of variable factors.<br>
Closely <b>follows the strategic direction </b>set by senior management when establishing near term goals. Interacts with <b>senior management </b>at a client and/or within Accenture on matters where they may need to gain acceptance on an alternate approach. Has<b> some latitude </b>in decision-making. Acts independently to determine methods and procedures on<b> new assignments</b>. Decisions have a major day to day impact on<b> area of responsibility</b>. Manages <b>large - medium sized teams</b> <b>and/or work efforts</b> (if in an individual contributor role) at a client<br>

</b></div>

<ul><li>Cloud (“SaaS”) solutions and how fit into a client’s larger application ecosystem (on premise as well as cloud)</li></ul>
