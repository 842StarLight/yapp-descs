<h2>Product Manager I</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>THE OPPORTUNITY:</b></p>
<p>
The <i>VelocityEHS</i> work environment is dynamic, innovative and entrepreneurial. We have a results-oriented culture that demands intelligence, teamwork and follow-through. If you have these qualities and take pride in surprising and delighting clients then this is the job for you - check out the details below and apply now.</p>
<p>
We are looking for a motivated, analytical and effective business analyst with strong strategic thinking capabilities. The role is helping implement technology solutions by determining the requirements of a project or program, and communicating them clearly to all stakeholders, and requires a flexible and a highly adaptive approach to fluid start-up like conditions. The position requires exceptional collaborative, teaming and consensus building abilities, as well as proficiency in cross-discipline and cross-product group cooperation.</p>
<p>
Working with Sales, Professional Services, and Engineering, the Product Manager will help develop specifications, analyze competition, help position the products, and help develop marketing/sales collateral and tools.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
PRIMARY DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES INCLUDE BUT ARENT LIMITED TO:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Be the driving force behind the inception, planning, definition, development and delivery of new products and product releases to market</li>
<li>
Responsible for ensuring overall business success throughout the product life cycle; including definition and introduction &amp; transforming stakeholder requirements into winning products</li>
<li>
Work closely with engineering and product development teams to develop and enhance existing products based on stakeholder needs</li>
<li>
Support Director with cross-functional teams addressing key areas of concern for product line, new product launches and marketing campaigns</li>
<li>
Support multiple products or work on multiple projects at one time as needed</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
SKILLS/QUALIFICATIONS:</b></p>
<p><b>
Minimum Skills &amp; Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree in an appropriate discipline or equivalent work experience</li>
<li>
Minimum 3 years’ experience in product management or equivalent experience</li>
<li>
Advanced critical thinking and problem solving skills</li>
<li>
Ability to work as part of a team with specific direction, or as an individual project lead with only high-level direction</li>
<li>
Attention to detail, strong teamwork and communication skills.</li>
<li>
Strong Internet and computer literacy skills in Microsoft Office (Word, PowerPoint, Access, and Excel) as well as an ability to utilize various project management applications.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Preferred Skills &amp; Qualifications</b></p>
<ul><li>
Direct experience in SAAS and web development</li></ul>
