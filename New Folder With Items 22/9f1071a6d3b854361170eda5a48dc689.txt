<h2>Social Media Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Social Media Manager is responsible for the social media presence of all 38 brands of Sagora Senior Living. The goal of this role is to manage content and exposure of Sagora's online presence.</p>
<p>Sagora Senior Living is one of the top 50 senior housing operators in the United States offering independent living, assisted living, and memory care services. We are looking for compassionate and caring individuals to join our team to uphold our "Residents First" philosophy. We offer a competitive compensation and benefits package along with additional company perks.</p>
<p>If you are looking for a rewarding position and a great company culture, we urge you to apply!</p>
<p><b>Position details:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Location: Sagora Home Office</li>
<li>City, State: Fort Worth, TX</li>
<li>Community details: www.sagora.com</li>
<li>Status: Full Time</li>
<li>Shift/hours: Business hours</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Social media content creation and management for 28 different brands' social media accounts and corporate accounts on Facebook, LinkedIn, Instagram, Twitter, and YouTube</li>
<li>Monitoring and maintenance for all profiles on each social media platform</li>
<li>Integrated regular reporting</li>
<li>Combining data from Google Analytics, reports, and social media to optimize social targeting</li>
<li>Budget and planning for boosted social posts targeted to a specific audience to increase reach</li>
 <li>Research digital audience preferences and current trends</li>
 <li>Moderate all user-generated content and monitor user comments in line with our client's company policy for each state</li>
 <li>Design posts to sustain readers’ curiosity and creating buzz around new products</li>
 <li>Develop original digital content and suggest creative ways to attract more customers and promote our brand</li>
 <li>Increase web traffic and customer engagement metrics aligned with broader marketing strategies</li>
 <li>Measure web traffic and monitor SEO</li>
 <li>Stay up-to-date with changes in all social platforms ensuring maximum effectiveness</li>
 <li>Train co-workers to use social media in a cohesive and beneficial way</li>
 <li>Facilitate online conversations with customers and respond to queries</li>
 <li>Report on online reviews and feedback from residents and associates</li>
 <li>Develop an optimal posting schedule, considering web traffic and customer engagement metrics</li>
 <li>Oversee social media account layout</li>
 <li>Suggest new ways to attract prospective customers, like promotions and competitions</li>
 <li>Other special projects and duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Skills/Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Minimum of two years corporate or agency social media management experience – SAMPLES required</p>
 </li>
<li>
<p>Strong Social Media experience in a marketing capacity (not personal use) including all social media platforms (Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, SnapChat, LinkedIn)</p>
 </li>
<li>
<p>Bachelor's Degree in Marketing, Communications, Business, or related field required</p>
 </li>
<li>
<p>In-depth knowledge of SEO, keyword research and Google Analytics</p>
 </li>
<li>
<p>Experience with Hootsuite, Hubspot and Facebook Ad Platforms</p>
 </li>
<li>
<p>Experience with Live streaming is a plus</p>
 </li>
<li>
<p>1+ years of marketing experience in senior housing or related field preferred, but not required</p>
 </li>
<li>
<p>Working knowledge of sales and marketing concepts to include principles and methods for promoting, and selling products and/or services</p>
 </li>
<li>
<p>Familiarity with online marketing strategies and marketing channels</p>
 </li>
<li>
<p>Strong project management and organization skills during multitasking</p>
 </li>
<li>
<p>Working knowledge of Microsoft Office and design software including Excel, Adobe Creative Suite, and Google Analytics</p>
 </li>
<li>
<p>Professional communication skills</p>
</li>
<li>Ability to work effectively in a team setting</li>
</ul>
