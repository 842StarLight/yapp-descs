<h2>SharePoint Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>
<div>ON Semiconductor is seeking a self-driven and hard-working individual to join our Information Management Tools/SharePoint team to assist system administration and development opportunities in our Corporate Headquarter. Most of the Information Technology team operates from the Phoenix location, and you will have direct insight to the organization as a whole. The ideal candidate should have the experience we are looking for apart from technical skills, one should have a good interpersonal skills with strong sense of functional capabilities to work with internal customers on system requirements and problems. Analytical and critical thinking will be crucial in working with clients.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The IM Tools/SharePoint Analyst will provide 2nd and 3rd level support for various company systems, with the primary focus on SharePoint.<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities include</b>, but are not limited to:</div>
<ul>
<li>Diagnose and resolve application, configuration, and code level technical issues.</li>
<li>
Act as a point of contact for business related incident and request submissions, tracking, and resolution.</li>
<li>
Demonstrate the ability to articulate problems and solutions in a concise manner.</li>
<li>
Responsible for the development or customization of SharePoint web parts, workflows as well as contributing to the development of client-side customization.</li>
<li>
Aid in analysis and/or requirements gathering, design, configuration, implementation, testing, support of SharePoint solutions.</li>
<li>
Provide and conduct end-user training sessions.</li>
<li>
Support enhancement and upgrade maintenance procedures, performance monitoring, and ensure applications are compliant through patching and new releases.</li>
<li>
Support planned and unplanned outages.</li>
<li>
Works with staff to develop and maintain standard practices and policies to support the application configuration and usage, platform, and development solutions.</li>
<li>
Provides quality assurance, testing, and documentation for application configuration and solutions.</li>
<li>
Coordinates with IT Service Teams associated to the deployment and ongoing maintenance of the application and platform.</li>
</ul>
<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>
Qualifications</b><div>
</div>
<ul>
<li>
BS in Computer Science/Engineering (or equivalent) with minimum 2 years of analyst experience.</li>
<li><b>
MUST HAVE 3+ years of experience with Microsoft SharePoint environments.</b></li>
<li>
Applied experience in facilitating business requirements, research, and documentation.</li>
<li>
Knowledge and experience in implementing or supporting custom SharePoint solutions.</li>
<li>
Demonstrate the ability to quickly learn new technologies and adapt to business processes.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<b>Preferred Knowledge</b>:</div>
<ul>
<li>Experience administering SharePoint Online (O365).</li>
<li>
Experience using SysAid ticketing service.</li>
<li>
Intermediate knowledge of SharePoint Framework, React, Hangfire, Office Fabric, JavaScript, CSS, and HTML is highly desired.</li>
<li>
PowerShell SharePoint Designer, Sharegate knowledge is an advantage.</li>
<li>
Working knowledge of Agile methodology.</li>
<li>
Experience with web-based and client/server products in a Wintel environment is an advantage.</li>
</ul>
