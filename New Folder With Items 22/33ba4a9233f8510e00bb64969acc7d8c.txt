<h2>Channel Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Channel Manager</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Summary</b></p>
<p>Responsible for working with strategic partners and internal stakeholders to generate new business opportunities for Advantix. Net new business opportunities will be referred to as discovery calls or client discovery calls which represents the initial call with a client account.</p>
<p><b>Essential Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Establish productive, professional relationships with key within and outside of Advantix to ensure discovery call quotas are met.</li>
<li>Route qualified opportunities to the appropriate sales executives for further development and closure.</li>
<li>Developing and executing strategic plans for achieving and exceeding discovery call quotas.</li>
<li>Meets assigned discovery call targets.</li>
<li>As needed, works with internal sales project staff throughout the sales process to ensure applicable third-party interests are appropriately engaged and updated as needed.</li>
<li>Proactively works with key lead-generation sources to sales opportunities are maximized.</li>
<li>Remain up-to-date regarding updates to products and processes and ensures that applicable third-party resources are adequately informed.</li>
<li>Solution selling with the know how to translate product and service features into benefits that solve real enterprise customer problems.</li>
<li>Skills, Qualifications, and Core Competencies</li>
<li>Minimum of 2 years of sales and/ or account management experience.</li>
<li>Intermediate skill level in Excel and PowerPoint.</li>
<li>Ability to present to prospective client stakeholders and articulate Advantix's value proposition(s) relative to client needs.</li>
<li>Experience working with client and strategic partner stakeholders to perform a comprehensive needs analysis.</li>
<li>Excellent customer service, and oral and written communication skills. Comfortable working with clients, providers, partners, and internal stakeholders.</li>
<li>Detail-oriented. Exceptional time management and multi-tasking abilities.</li>
<li>Experience in telecommunications and/or familiarity with associated terminology a plus</li>
<li>Familiarity working with databases or other processes dependent on data integrity a plus</li>
<li>To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill and/or ability required. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</li>
<br>
</ul>
