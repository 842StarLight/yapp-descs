<div><b>Job Description</b></div>
<div>
<b>Security and Risk</b> professionals develop and deliver solutions that protect enterprise systems, applications and data by establishing policies, practices and tools that prevent unauthorized access, use, disclosure, modification or disruption. A professional at this position level within Accenture has the following responsibilities:<br>
Provides solutions to<b> complex business problems</b> for area(s) of responsibility where analysis of situations requires an in depth knowledge of organizational objectives. Involved in setting <b>strategic direction </b>to establish near term goals for area of responsibility. Interacts with senior management levels at a client and/or within Accenture, which involves negotiating or influencing on significant matters. Has<b> latitude </b>in decision-making and determining objectives and approaches to <b>critical assignments</b>. Decisions have a lasting impact on <b>area of responsibility </b>with the potential to<b> impact outside area of responsibility. Manages </b>large teams and/or work efforts <b>(if in an individual contributor role) at a client or within Accenture.</b>
