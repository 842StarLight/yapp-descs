<p><b>Who we are
</b></p>
<p>The Memorial Student Center (MSC) Box Office - Theatre Complex is conveniently located in the first floor lobby of Rudder Tower. Rudder Auditorium accommodates events such as Broadway productions, concerts, variety shows, movies, lectures, conferences, commencement ceremonies, and recitals. The MSC Box Office handles ticketing sales for events sponsored by Texas A&amp;M University student programming organizations, departments, MSC OPAS, and other surrounding community organizations.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>What we want
</b></p>
<p>The Business Associate I, is responsible for preparing money deposits and maintaining fiscal records for the MSC Box Office while keeping in compliance with PCI and University policies. The Business Associate will communicate effectively to clients and university staff regarding sales reports and settlements. Providing excellent customer service during office hours and during large ticketed events will be required to help the department run successfully. We need an individual who subscribes to and supports our commitment as stated above. If this job description sounds interesting to you, we invite you to apply to be considered for this opportunity.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Required Education and Experience:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High school graduation or any equivalent combination of education and experience
</li>
<li>Three years' experience in general office, bookkeeping or personnel operations
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Required Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Working knowledge of spreadsheet and word processing programs
</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal and communication skills
</li>
<li>Basic planning, organizational and time-management skills
</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task and cooperatively with others
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Preferred Education and Experience:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Associate's degree
</li>
<li>One year of experience with the preferred education, including task management experience
</li>
<li>Customer service experience, such as retail and sales as well as accounting responsibilities such as cash handling with deposits
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Prepares daily deposit of ticketing funds. Communicates with MSC Business Services regarding deposits, refunds, holding accounts, interdepartmental orders, etc.
</li>
<li>Operates ticketing system to sell tickets and run sales reports, settlements and billing for all ticketed events. Completes a weekly extensive performance sales report for MSC OPAS events.
</li>
<li>Purchases office supplies and reconciles box office expenses with Pro Card. Accounts for all online ticket sales. Maintains fund development records.
</li>
<li>Keeps a log of daily handling for budget reports. Performs daily audit of drawers and vault. Assists external auditors. Maintains change.
</li>
<li>Provides customer service over the phone, at the service windows and through email. Helps customers with more difficult inquiries and complaints, explains and applies policies, investigates complaints and assists in resolution.
</li>
<li>Assists with customer service during large ticketed events which will occur during nights and weekend s.
</li>
<li>Provides backup supervision and training to student workers. Assists students in balancing drawers and deposits.
</li>
<li>Operates Microsoft Word, Excel, Outlook and ticketing software. Performs other duties as assigned.
</li>
</ul>
