<h2>Contracts Administrator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>The Contracts Administrator is responsible for preparing, examining, analyzing, negotiating, and revising contracts that involve the distribution, reproduction, or translation of API’s Intellectual Property.</p>
<p><b>Contract Functions</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Oversee the preparation and revision of complex license and distribution agreements.</li>
<li>Participate and aid with negotiations for revenue-generating and vendor contracts for the IP Sales &amp; Distribution program.</li>
<li>Provide contract summaries and ensure contract execution in accordance with company policy.</li>
<li>Prepare contract change notices, monitor contractor performance, including the reporting and status of contractor and owner deliverables.</li>
<li>Conduct Due Diligence Assessment on new Vendors</li>
<li>Communicate effectively with external distributors, consultants, and committee members</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Support Functions</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Support activities related to the global distribution and protection of API’s standards, software and other intellectual property</li>
<li>Create guidance documents on department policies and procedures to provide staff training and education</li>
<li>Register existing IP with the US Copyright Office and monitor for instances of copyright infringement</li>
<li>Perform quality checks of marketing materials before disseminating to API distributors</li>
<li>Collaborate with various member funded and self-supporting departments throughout the organization</li>
<li>Upload API Standards to Government-cited and Safety Documents website.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Technical Functions</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Utilize expertise in various computer and web applications and technologies to maintain and upgrade multiple internal and public-facing websites</li>
<li>Create and customize SharePoint sites, libraries, lists and other content types including managing user and group permissions</li>
<li>Proficient in Adobe Professional, Microsoft Office 365, SharePoint, and DocuSign</li>
<li>Increase efficiencies in document and process management by migrating manual functions and obsolete databases to SharePoint for improved maintenance and tracking</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Project Oriented Functions </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage project to convert PDF standards to HTML and XML formats to provide flexibility and interoperability to users</li>
<li>Maintain up-to-date sales / royalty financial records, department lists, and files for review, analysis and reporting.</li>
</ul>
