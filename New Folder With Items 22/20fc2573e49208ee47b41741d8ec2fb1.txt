<p><b>Job Description</b></p>
<p><b>Title</b><br><i>Business Development Representative</i></p>
<p><b>Career Summary</b></p>
<p>As a Business Development Representative (BDR) you will be responsible for scheduling sales appointments with prospective internet and phone buyers. A BDR is our organizations first point of contact with more than 60% our customers and prospective customers.</p>
<p><b>Career Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>-Receive inbound internet form leads &amp; follow up with clients in a timely, courteous manner instilling in each customer that they are our first priority.<br>-Answer all inbound sales calls immediately, gathering accurate customer information, create urgency while providing information &amp; ultimately schedule a customer appointment.<br>-Communicate with the prospective customer with a clear pre-determined message until they are ready to schedule an appointment.<br>-Maintain clear and accurate customer files in our internal Customer Relationship Management Software (CRM).<br>-Maintain an organization standard for quality &amp; timely lead responses.<br>-Set up test drive and fact finding appointments with all opportunities. Keeping in mind that everyone will buy, if not now; in the future.<br>-Keep an active daily log tracking contact attempts &amp; success for; text, chat, email &amp; phone correspondence. Additionally for appointments set and shown.<br>-Handle minor customer complaints &amp; misunderstandings.<br>-Communicate actively &amp; openly with management team.<br>-Maintain an active training schedule staying up to date with products, current incentives &amp; industry best practices.</p>
<p><b>Required Experience &amp; Qualifications</b></p>
<p>-Must meet company requirements for employment<br>-Actively demonstrated customer service skills<br>-Previous automotive experience encouraged however not required<br>-Excellent verbal &amp; written communications skills<br>-Proficient in the use of Microsoft office and products such as Excel<br>-Professional appearance</p>
<p><b>Working Conditions</b></p>
<p>This position requires the ability to type with very high computer and phone usage. Additionally it will require extended periods of seated office work.</p>
<p><b>Compensation</b></p>
<p>Compensation is negotiable based on prior experience &amp; qualifications. This is a full time position with unlimited earnings potential including both hourly &amp; bonus pay.</p>
