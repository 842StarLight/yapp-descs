<h2>Revenue Management Data Analyst/Client Services</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Small data mining company looking for strong intermediate level revenue management data analyst. Candidate must have a significant level of experience with Microsoft Excel (pivot tables, macros, and VB code if possible) and Access (queries, macros). A working knowledge of queries is expected. Candidates should be familiar with inner joins, left joins, how to dedupe records in a query, and be comfortable worked with nested “If” statements.This is a work-from-home position; however, candidates located in Lynchburg, VA or Greensboro, NC are a plus in terms of ability to easily meet with other staffers when necessary.</p>
<p>Candidate would work with and manipulate large customer data files containing several million records. The data will be analyzed to address key strategic issues for large sales organizations including questions like: 1) what is the optimal size of the sales force, 2) who should they be calling on, and 3) where should the sales associates be located. Candidates will also be expected to interact with clients via video conference calls to assist in reporting/troubleshooting when necessary.</p>
<p>A 4-year college degree is preferred but not essential, as is a minimum of 2-3 years of work experience in a data oriented position. Experience in the hotel/hospitality industry is another plus. Compensation commensurate with experience.</p>
<p>This is a work-from-home position; however, candidates located in Lynchburg, VA or Greensboro, NC are a plus in terms of ability to easily meet with other staffers when necessary. Schedule is very flexible. Position is eligible for 401k plan and 2 weeks of vacation time after one year of employment. Medical insurance coverage is not available at this time.</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
