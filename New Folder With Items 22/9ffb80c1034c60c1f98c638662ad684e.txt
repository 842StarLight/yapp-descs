<div><b>Global Strategy &amp; Business Development Associate</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The Global Strategy &amp; Business Development Associate works closely with the Global Strategy Team to achieve LexisNexisâ€™ revenue and profitability goals and to maintain and enhance its industry leading position. Responsibilities include providing high quality and timely research and analysis to inform recommendations on strategy, acquisitions, divestments, market characteristics, and competitive dynamics. We operate in a collaborative and supportive culture and require the role holder to be a strong team player who is able to execute discrete workstreams of projects as well as rapidly shift to participate in additional projects as necessary across the business units. The role requires outstanding analytical, excellent communication and advisory skills, the ability to execute workstreams independently, the ability to support cross-functional teams within the organization, and the ability to build and maintain strong relationships across the business. In many instances, the Associate will also participate in the operational implementation of the plans and ensure best practices are leveraged.</div>

<div>
2. Support the development of internal reports on the state of the business or broader legal services industry</div>
<ul>
<li><div>
 Support the development of reports for CEO / Board of Directors on the legal services market working with the business unit strategy leads</div></li>
<li><div>
Support the development of quarterly reports assessing financial performance of the business</div></li>
</ul>
3. Support the delivery of projects to address and solve key strategic issues facing the business units, delivering high quality results to planned timescales<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li><div>
Support multiple concurrent projects, keeping the Global Strategy Team updated and timelines on track</div></li>
<li><div>
Conduct analysis and draw insights and recommendations from a variety of data sources such as sales data, market data, usage data, and front-end customer facing interviews / surveys</div></li>
<li><div>
Execute workstreams independently and support the Global Strategy Team in delivery of a variety of business performance improvement initiatives</div></li>
<li><div>
Example projects include:</div></li>
</ul>
<div><div><ul>
<li>Evaluate the attractiveness of new markets for LNLP and develop market entry strategies</li>

<ul>

</ul>
<div>
5. Be an active member of the Global Strategy Team</div>
<ul>
<li><div>
 Be a trusted sparring-partner to other Strategy team members</div></li>
<li><div>
Help with team-building initiatives</div></li>
<li><div>
Support the development of an effective Strategy team, including ensuring knowledge transfer within the Strategy team and best practice sharing across the business</div></li>

Work with strategy leads across LNLP to support the sharing of ideas and best practices</div></li>
