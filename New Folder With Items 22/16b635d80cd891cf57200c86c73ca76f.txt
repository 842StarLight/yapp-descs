<div><h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Description
</b></h2></div>
<div>The Leica Biosystems Imaging business is excited to be growing our R&amp;D capability in our Vista, CA office, just outside of San Diego! On our journey to Vision 24, enabling clinicians to efficiently provide patients a highly confident diagnosis within 24-hours from biopsy, the LBS Imaging business plays a critical part in the scanning, image management and image analysis parts of the overall integrated solutions we offer.
<div></div>
<br>
<div>As a Software Engineer, Imaging Solutions, you will be responsible for design, develop, and unit test image viewing, data workflow in support of the image analysis application. Components could be web, thick-client applications or micro-services. Collaborates with product managers and other developers to design and deliver needed functionality.
<div>
<br>
Major Responsibilities
</div>
<div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Collaborate with Product Managers and other Software engineers developing image viewing and analysis applications.
</li>
<li>Collaborate, present and defend design with broader development team.
</li>
<li>Develop software under Leica Biosystems development procedures.
</li>
<li>Document software specifications based on marketing requirements in accordance with Leica Biosystems quality process. These would include but not limited to SRS, SDS, SDD, FMEA.
</li>
<li>Unit test software prior to turnover to QA engineers.
</li>
<li>Member of team of engineers developing devices and platform.
</li>
<li>Provide third-level support for customer installations. This would include participation in CDT&amp;R activities
</li>
<li>Learn and apply DBS (Danaher Business Systems) tools and processes for problem solving, lean product development, inspection of code &amp; design review and ultimately for driving customer happiness.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<br>

<div><h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2></div>
<div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree in CS, Engineering or related field
</li>
<li>5+ years C++ or C# Windows development.
</li>
<li>2+ years’ experience with scripting language, preferably with JavaScript/PHP and related Web 2.0 technologies (CSS, AJAX, etc.)
</li>
<li>Experience developing multi-threaded applications.
</li>
<li>Experience developing Database applications including queries, views triggers using SQL
</li>
<li>Experience in estimating requirements and writing design documents
</li>
<li>Experience with client/server application architecture.
</li>
<li>Experience deploying and supporting software applications.
</li>
</ul>
<br>
Preferred Qualifications
<div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Experience working with SCRUM in an Agile team environment.
</li>
<li>Experience with Visual Studio and other Microsoft development tools.
</li>
<li>Familiarity with GUI design for non-technical users, especially for image viewing applications.
</li>
<li>Familiarity with image file formats (JPEG, TIFF, GIF) and with compression techniques (JPEG, LZW).
</li>
<li>Familiarity with COM.
</li>
<li>Familiarity with medical imaging applications, such as in Pathology or Radiology.
</li>
<li>Experience with TCP/IP socket programming.
</li>
