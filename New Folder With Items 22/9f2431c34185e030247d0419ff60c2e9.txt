<p></p>
<p>As a Field Technician, you will provide hardware/software/structure cabling technical service and support, installation, configuration, and problem resolution in a PC/network/infrastructure environment. You will complete proper inventory and asset recording processes prior to and during delivery and complete appropriate paperwork as required for customer acceptance. On a daily basis you will travel to customer locations within a region, so a flexible work schedule is a must. Structured cabling experience should include the ability to run all cable types via pathways (conduit, drop ceiling, wire mold, etc). Terminate cabling on the appropriate hardware (jacks, patch panel, both 66 type and 110 type). The ability to install voice and data connections, along with the ability to troubleshoot techniques to identify issues with structure cabling.</p>

Key Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>
The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill, and/or ability required. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</p>
<p>
The <b><i>Field Services Technician </i></b>is primarily responsible for:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Install, configure and support new hardware and software infrastructure</li>
<li>
Ensure that hardware and software systems are maintained</li>

Provide technical support to users with very different levels of IT knowledge and competence</li>

Perform onsite cabling tasks, including cable runs, terminations, installation, and troubleshooting</li>

Act as the liaison between nfrastructure team and the customer</li>

Travel to customer locations and possess a flexible work schedule, this may require overnight stays</li>
<li>
Maybe responsible for operating company vehicle</li>
<li>
Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
We are seeking candidates with the following experience and skills</b></p>
<ul>

<li>
Familiarity with standard Windows O/S and Office suites</li>
<li>
Ability to thoroughly and accurately perform inventory and asset recording and complete paperwork as required by customer, including vendor warranties</li>
<li>
Ability to perform final configuration tasks on customer premise</li>

2+ years’ experience with desktop hardware</li>
<li>
2+ years’ experience with structure cabling</li>

Excellent customer service and communication skills</li>
