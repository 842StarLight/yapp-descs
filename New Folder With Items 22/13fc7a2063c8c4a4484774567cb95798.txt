<h2>Client Services Representative II - Office Services</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>Advanced proficiency in site operations and procedures with ability to assign tasks to colleagues and manage responsibilities within the team.
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div>
<ul>
<li>Promptly informs supervisor of potential problems or customer concerns.</li>
<li>Promptly reviews and responds to management and client requests via emails, phone calls, text messages and verbal.</li>
<li>Strong focus on providing good customer service.</li>
<li>Contributes to the creation of the Site Procedure Guide to ensure all account processes are properly recorded.</li>
<li>Oversees workflow and job balance between staff and ensures tasks are completed within account SLA requirements.</li>
<li>Responsible for communicating and training team in changes to workflow or procedure.</li>
<li>Oversees and manages daily and monthly records on service activity.</li>
<li>Effectively communicates with the client and staff.</li>
<li>Where appropriate, may serve as main point of contact to the client for daily activity and participate in client meetings on account activity.</li>
<li>Responsibilities may include Copy/Print Production/Copy Center, Copier Maintenance, Mail/Courier Services, Reception/Office Services, Shipping/Receiving, Inventory Services, Warehouse, File Room/Records File Services based on assigned location.</li>
<li>Attends cross-functional trainings to ensure ability to provide coverage when short-staffed.</li>
<li>Site responsibility and location of coverage may change based on client and/or division needs.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<br>
Reception/Office Services: Responsible for performing general front office reception duties such as greeting visitors, answering phone calls, taking messages, scheduling conference rooms, entering data into computer. Responds to customer needs and requests, accepts deliveries and sets up food and beverages for meetings. May also be responsible for copy/binding/copier maintenance.<br>
<br>
Mail/Courier Services: Responsible for prompt and accurate receiving, sorting, delivery, pick up, and processing of all courier items, interoffice mail, newspapers, magazines and all classed of United States Postal Services mail. Tracks courier and accountable items (FedEx, UPS, Certified Mail, etc). Researches and routes unidentified and generic mail. Receives, logs, delivers and tracks messenger items. Responds to customer requests. Performs routine upkeep of equipment. Records and tracks customer inquiries and fulfillment of requests.<br>
<br>
Device Maintenance: Responsible for customer satisfaction through the performance and maintenance of reprographic equipment at customer location. Follows daily, established maintenance processes and procedures. Maintains routine upkeep of equipment including cleaning glass and surfaces, clearing paper jams, replenishing toner and loading paper. Evaluates equipment issues and notifies service department if unable to resolve. Delivers paper. Assists end-users in basic functionality of equipment. Records meter reads. Maintains service activity reports. Monitors supplies and restocks inventory.
</div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>High School diploma or equivalent required plus 1 to 2 years of related experience.<br>
<ul>
<li>Prior experience in a customer service environment.</li>
<li>Good computer skills/technical knowledge.</li>
<li>Ability to multitask and prioritize in order to meet deadlines.</li>
<li>Good customer service and communication skills.</li>
<li>Ability to work with minimal supervision.</li>
<li>Ability to work OT as needed.</li>
<li>May require driving between multiple client locations, may also require a personal vehicle (valid driver's license and acceptable driving record necessary).</li>
<li>Ability to lift up to 50lbs.</li>
<li>Ability to spend extended periods of time standing, bending, walking, reaching, and pulling while performing duties.
</li>
</ul>
