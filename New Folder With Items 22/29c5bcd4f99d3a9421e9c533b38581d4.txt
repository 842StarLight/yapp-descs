We have over thirty manufacturing and assembly plants worldwide servicing over 10,000 customers in more than 100 countries. We complement our extensive line of Motive Power and Energy Systems with a full range of integrated services and systems. <b>
Summary</b><br>
</div>
<p></p>
<div><p>The Buyer position ensures an uninterrupted supply of quality materials that meet targets for cost, inventory, and supplier management goals. Coordinates activities involved with procuring goods and services, such as raw materials, equipment, tools, parts, supplies, and advertising, in a timely and cost-effective manner.</p></div>
</div>
<div>
<div>
<b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b><br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>
<div>Include the following. Other duties may be assigned.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Research and evaluate potential suppliers to ensure they can produce the quality and quantity of goods needed at the most competitive total cost.</li>
<li>
Maintains strategic relationships with suppliers and execute purchasing agreements and contracts.</li>
<li>
Constantly monitors inventory levels, production scheduling demand and execute to the MRP or SAP exception messages.</li>
<li>
Verify demand, generate purchase orders and compare standard cost to the actual pricing.</li>
<li>
Mitigates and negotiates price increases.</li>
<li>
Plans and analyses inventory levels by verifying available on hand stock.</li>
<li>
Generate system purchase requisitions, place purchase orders and verify part number specifications and price. Obtains purchase order confirmation from suppliers confirming price, delivery and payment terms.</li>
<li>
Expedites material from suppliers to meet the master production plan.</li>
<li>
Verifies receipt of items by comparing items received to items ordered and resolves shipment discrepancies from suppliers.</li>
<li>
Manages optimal inventory levels and develops a “plan for every part” to increase material availability and increase inventory turns.</li>
<li>
Maintains Purchase Order information accessible by sorting and filing documents by supplier and date.</li>
<li>
Develops an intimate knowledge of the company’s ERP system in all relevant modules.</li>
<li>
Develops / Implements an efficient and accurate system for monitoring all Open Purchase Orders</li>
<li>
Ability to translate, statistically analyze data, and effectively report problems through written and/or graphical formats.</li>
</ul>
