<h2>Teacher or Master Teacher</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<p></p>
<div><div>
<div><div>This advertisement will be used to fill one of the following Pre-K 4 SA Teacher positions: Teacher: Starting salary $52,682.50 annually; Master Teacher: Starting salary $63,746.28 annually.<br>
<br>
Under general direction, is responsible for planning and implementing activities that promote the social, physical, and intellectual growth of four-year old children enrolled in the City's Pre-K 4 SA program at the Education Centers. Responsibilities include motivating students and their parents/guardians to develop the child's communication abilities, attitudes, skills in math and reading and knowledge needed to provide a foundation for further education. This position will be responsible for implementing developmentally appropriate practices that are aligned to the Texas Pre-kindergarten guidelines.<br>
<br>
This position is funded by sales tax that has been approved by the citizens of San Antonio for a limited time period. This position, therefore, is subject to at-will employment status, and is unclassified and has no disciplinary appeal rights to the Municipal Civil Service Commission.<br>
</div></div>
<br>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><div>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL JOB FUNCTIONS:</b></p>
</div></div>
<br>
<div><div>
<ul>
<li>Implements early age curriculum that is supportive and a language and numbers-rich classroom environment that sparks children's imaginations.<br>
</li>
<li>Plans and delivers engaging lessons, according to students' needs that showcase high quality teaching and learning.<br>
</li>
<li>Assesses developmental needs of children and works to incorporate individual students learning, physical, social, and emotional needs.<br>
</li>
<li>Presents academic content through a variety of engaging and rigorous instructional strategies.<br>
</li>
<li>Develops and maintains a classroom environment conducive to effective learning and appropriate for the physical, social and emotional development of all students.<br>
</li>
<li>Engages students in the learning process; plans and uses appropriate instructional and learning strategies, activities, materials and equipment, including instructional media.<br>
</li>
<li>Assesses student progress on objectives, expectations, and/or goals utilizing anecdotal notes and authentic assessment to provide feedback to students, parents, and administration at three designated checkpoints.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Collaborates with Teacher Assistant to provide an effective classroom program and address needs of individual students.<br>
</li>
<li>Organizes age-appropriate indoor and outdoor activities to ensure student participation in learning activities.<br>
</li>
<li>Communicates regularly with parents by means of parent meetings, newsletters, electronic communication, and/or individual parent conferences<br>
</li>
<li>Collaborate daily with teaching assistant to ensure effective implementation of curriculum and instruction, federal, state and local licensing standards, Pre-K 4 SA standards, and policies.<br>
</li>
<li>Maintains high standards of student conduct using guidance skills appropriate for young children, according to licensing regulations.<br>
</li>
<li>Works with Center staff and parents to address the needs of the whole child, including emotional, academic, physical, social, and nutritional needs.<br>
</li>
<li>Participates in all staff meetings, including those scheduled after-school and during the summer.<br>
</li>
<li>Performs related duties and fulfills responsibilities as required.</li>
</ul>
</div></div>

<p><b>Job Requirements for Teacher:</b><br>
<b>EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's Degree from an accredited college or university in education or related field.</li>
</ul>
<b>
LICENSES OR CERTIFICATES:</b><br>

<li>Valid teaching certificate with required endorsements to teach early childhood education from any State's Educator Certification Department and the ability to obtain certification in Texas.</li>

<ul>
<li>Valid Class "C" Texas Driver's License.</li>
</ul>
<b>
Job Requirements for Master Teacher:</b><p><b>
EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's Degree from an accredited college or university in education or related field.<br>
</li>
<li>Three (3) years of early childhood teaching experience in a private, charter, or public school.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
LICENSES OR CERTIFICATES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Valid teaching certificate with required endorsements to teach early childhood education from any State's Educator Certification Department and the ability to obtain certification in Texas.<br>
</li>
<li>Valid Class "C" Texas Driver's License.</li>
</ul>
<b>
PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS:</b><ul>
<li>Master's Degree from an accredited college or university with a focus in Early Childhood.
</li>
<li>Five (5) years of early childhood teaching experience in a private, charter or public school.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Experience in lesson plan/curriculum development.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Ability to read, write, and speak Spanish.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Experience with the following curriculums/approaches: HighScope, Creative Curriculum, Reggio-Emilia inspired learning, Project based learning.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Have completed at least 30 hours of college course credit in early childhood.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Ability to obtain a valid teaching certification in Texas prior to the first instructional day.</li>
</ul>
