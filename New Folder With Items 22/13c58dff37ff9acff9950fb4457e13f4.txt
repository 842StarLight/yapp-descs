<p><b>Job Description Summary:
</b></p>This Full-Time Truckload Transportation Specialist works in our original division, within a Truckload location in Tannersville, Pennsylvania. This position will be assigned tasks to help manage on-time service, customer service communication and truck utilization.
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Description:
</b></p>
<div><b>What does it take to be considered?
</b></div>
<div><div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Check call drivers in region to verify their time of availability and on-time service
</li>
<li>Contact shippers and receivers to modify pick-up and delivery appointments to improve on-time service and driver utilization
</li>
<li>Send regular service status update reports to critical customers
</li>
</ul>
