<h2>Office Administrator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Assists the General Manager with various administrative tasks to include: filing, running reports, setting appointments, scheduling meetings, gathering information, etc. Assists the HR Manager with daily HR functions in the store. To include: conducting training sessions for new and current associates; completing new hire paperwork; filing; data entry.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>. <b>Position responsibilities:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>1) Supports a strong commitment to world class customer service and ensures a pleasant and productive shopping experience for all customers
</p>
<p></p>
<p>2) Assists the HR Manager with the following:
</p>
<p></p>
<ul><li>Complete all “new hire” paperwork for all new associates
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<ul><li>Conduct orientation for all new associates
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<ul><li>Coordinate the S.T.A.R. Program to include: scheduling, training and reporting.
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<ul><li>Coordinates and schedules Associate training in all areas.
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<ul><li>Completes Data entry for new associates, pay increases, associate reviews, etc.
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<ul><li>Assists with planning and implementing Associate activities
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p>3) Gathers data &amp; information, and prepares certain reports as needed by the General Manager
</p>
<p></p>
<p>4) Maintains strict confidentiality
</p>
<p></p>
<p>5) Processes and codes invoices &amp; maintains Expense Tracking Worksheet
</p>
<p></p>
<p>6) Maintains store checkbook register &amp; P-card log
</p>
<p></p>
<p>7) Maintains the Competitive Shop program &amp; the decoy list
</p>
<p></p>
<p>8) Orders &amp; monitors store supplies
</p>
<p></p>
<p>9) Coordinates travel advances and expense forms
</p>
<p></p>
<p>10) Submits IT Help Desk requests promptly
</p>
<p></p>
<p>11) Must be highly organized.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>12) Handles customer and associate questions and requests
</p>
<p></p>
<p>13) Filing, typing and copying as needed
</p>
<p></p>
<p>14) Aware of advertised sales
</p>
<p></p>
<p>15) Keeps work area clean, neat and well stocked with supplies
</p>
<p></p>
<p>16) Follows all Company Policies and Procedures
</p>
<p></p>
<p>17) Provide a legendary experience for every customer, every time by assisting customers in making buying decisions by:
</p>
<ul>
<li>identifying and evaluating customers' needs
</li>
<li>making product recommendations based off of this analys
</li>
<li>promoting programs including, but not limited to CLUB Membership, VOC and In-Store Pick-up
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Education and/or Experience
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>High School education or equivalent experience
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Mathematical Skills / Reasoning Ability
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>Ability to calculate figures such as discounts and make change to customers
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Communication Skills
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>Ability to communicate in a friendly and professional manner to our customers and other associates. Must possess above average communication skills. Ability to facilitate training to groups of up to 25.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Physical requirements
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>Able to stand &amp; sit for extended periods
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Other Knowledge, Skills and Abilities
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>Ability to establish and maintain effective working relationships with Management, co-workers and customers.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Ability to operate computerized Point of Sale register system and computerized Phone system
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Ability to use Microsoft Office Word Processor and Spreadsheet software
</p>
