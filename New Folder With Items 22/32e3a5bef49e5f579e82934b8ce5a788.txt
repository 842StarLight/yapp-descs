<h2>Advertising Sales Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Advertising Sales Assistant </b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Type: </b> Full time job based in New York City (Midtown)</li>
<li>
<b>Location: </b> Midtown, New York City</li>
<li>
<b>Company: </b> Hemisphere Media Group owns and operates 5 Hispanic cable TV networks that are based in the U.S.</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Description</p>
<ul>
<li>Provide Advertising Sales Team (Director and National Account Executive) with support in the day-to-day management and servicing of accounts and advertising schedules across multiple Television networks</li>
<li>Act as liaison among Sales, Traffic, billing and our advertisers to ensure schedules run as ordered, instructions/creative materials are delivered, and payment is received</li>
<li>Respond to client requests in a timely manner (orders, flighting, revisions, allocations, discrepancies…)</li>
</ul>
<p><b>DAY-TO-DAY RESPONSIBILITIES: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Enter orders into Wide Orbit and/or Strata</li>
<li>Handle program and commercial changes (change notices, preemptions…)</li>
<li>Prepare and deliver pre-logs and post-logs and other reports to clients in a timely manner</li>
<li>Resolve account and billing discrepancies on a timely manner</li>
<li>Generate, track and distribute sales reports</li>
<li>Update and maintain contact lists</li>
<li>Keep filing system up to date for all accounts</li>
</ul>
<p><b>AS NEEDED/REQUESTED: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Update and maintain sales presentations and other sales material as needed</li>
<li>Assist with the development of proposals, presentations</li>
<li>Perform any additional duties as required (we’re a small team and we all are expected to pitch in)</li>
</ul>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>College degree</li>
<li>1+ year of administrative/sales assistant or advertising agency experience as assistant buyer/planner</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal and writing skills</li>
<li>Proficiency in Microsoft Word, Excel, and PowerPoint.</li>
<li>Ability to work independently and multi-task in a fast-paced, high pressure environment</li>
<li>Attention to detail a must</li>
<li>Moderate Spanish-language communication skills</li>
<li>Must be organized, efficient and team player</li>
<li>Must be flexible and willing to work overtime</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul><li>relevant: 1 year (Required)</li></ul>
<p>Work Location:</p>
<ul><li>One location</li></ul>
<p>Management:</p>
<ul><li>Team Lead</li></ul>
<p>This Job Is Ideal for Someone Who Is:</p>
<ul>
<li>Dependable -- more reliable than spontaneous</li>
<li>High stress tolerance -- thrives in a high-pressure environment</li>
<li>Adaptable/flexible -- enjoys doing work that requires frequent shifts in direction</li>
</ul>
