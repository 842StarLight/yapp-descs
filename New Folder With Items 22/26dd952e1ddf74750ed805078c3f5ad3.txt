<h2>Marketing Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Leading edge technology company seeks Marketing and Sales Assistant for their Gainesville location.</p>
<p>The Marketing assistant will report directly to and work closely with the Director of Marketing and will be responsible for marketing and sales efforts, including but not limited to marketing campaigns, website updates, social media management, sales performance tracking, and front office work.</p>
<p>The right candidate is a detail-oriented self-starter who processes information quickly, is highly organized, technically savvy, extremely creative, and can inspire compelling and convincing marketing campaigns to promote our company and products.</p>
<p><b>Duties &amp; Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Marketing: Create PowerPoint presentations for Sales team to use when presenting technology, concepts, and products to Customers. Create compelling email teasers to pique Customer interest in technology and products. Perform market research to gather lists and email addresses of potential Customers for our outreach campaign.</li>
<li>Website Management: Work with web developer to update and enhance our website. Create clear, concise, and compelling presentation of our company and products via our corporate website.</li>
<li>Social Media Management: Update social media accounts with company and product news and information. Integrate social media into our marketing outreach campaigns.</li>
<li>Trade show Coordinating: Assist with trade show planning, booth design, and scheduling.</li>
<li>Seek and analyze competitor marketing material and digital content</li>
<li>Create and maintain tracking reports of digital and traditional marketing efforts</li>
<li>Analyzing marketing metrics</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Skills &amp; Requirements: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Strong work ethic</li>
<li>Experience with marketing computer software, online applications</li>
<li>Extensive knowledge of business social media best practices and platforms</li>
<li>Data entry and computer skills a must</li>
<li>Strong verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Flexible work schedule</li>
<li>Willingness to learn on the job and share experiences with other members of the team</li>
<li>Photoshop and Illustrator skills are a plus</li>
</ul>
<p>Technical knowledge: Command of technology in a paperless office environment; cloud-based business management and scheduling software, tablet computers, smart phones, Windows desktop computers, Google Calendar and Docs, MS Word, Excel and Outlook, cloud sync and storage</p>
<p>Creation tools: Command of Microsoft PowerPoint. Should be able to create presentations from scratch as well as modify and enhance existing presentations. Communication: Should be able to communicate in a clear, compelling, and concise manner with coworkers and Customers.</p>
<p>Job Type: Part-time</p>
</div>
