<h2>Brand and Graphic Designer II</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Veritiv is a Fortune 500, business-to-business packaging company. From starts ups to over 80% of the Fortune 500 companies, Veritiv provides unique, scalable packaging and design-driven solutions. The Design Services group consists of design, engineering, testing and project management professionals, partnering together on a global scale to deliver exceptional customer experiences. The Design Services team seeks out talented individuals that have a passion for design, and are looking to build a great career.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The Brand and Graphic Designer I is responsible for executing brand and graphic-related projects to support the company's strategic business goals. These projects center mostly around packaging development. Positive team engagement and motivation is a primary focus of this role. As a Designer I serving our customers, this person will be expected to bring creativity, innovation and ownership to projects. They will collaborate with their functional partners to work through projects from concept to completion.
</p>
