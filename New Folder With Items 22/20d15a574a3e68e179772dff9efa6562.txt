<div>World leader in the field of in vitro diagnostics for over 55 years, bioMérieux conceives, develops, produces and markets diagnostics solutions (systems, reagents, software and services) intended for clinical and industrial applications, which determine the origin of a disease or a contamination to improve the health of patients and insure consumer safety.

<br>

<br>

<br>

<br>
<div>As Global Strategic Account Technical Support Manager, you will directly report to the Industry Customer Service director and be part of the Industry unit.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>bioMérieux Industry unit is a dedicated business unit having for aim to satisfy food cosmetic and healthcare industrial companies thanks to innovative microbiological diagnosis technologies.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>In this position, you will be responsible to establish and develop long term technical relationships with Healthcare strategic accounts to achieve sustainable high performance in terms of customer satisfaction, revenue and profitability.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Main Accountabilities:
</b></div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>put in place personalized and globalized technical support and tools to pilot customer satisfaction either by survey and direct contacts (emails, calls,…) after specific actions
</li>
<li>know the strategic accounts you are responsible for and identify the key contacts in their organization (Quality director, Microbial method Leader…) and support them on technical requests
</li>
<li>coordinate and involve the different bioMérieux departments at Global level (CSI, Investigation Unit, Field Actions, R&amp;D, Marketing, Supply Chain, QA sites) and at local level to solve customer issues and improve their satisfaction: reduce time to result, proactive and regular feedback, specific and adapted answers
</li>
<li>collaborate closely with the strategic accounts managers in order to be involved in the business development strategy and to bring technical expertise during internal business unit review
</li>
<li>prepare and attend Quality and Business review to present and comment Helpdesk KPIs, address technical topics and acknowledge customer development strategy and needs
</li>
<li>be the interface between the Strategic Accounts and the bioMérieux Project teams in order to make sure their specific needs are taken into accounts and aligned with the development and, in required, identify and put on place collaborative actions with the Strategic accounts: beta sites, ring trials…
</li>
<li>contribute to the bioMérieux solution deployment on customer: participate to the Strategic Accounts needs definition (products improvements, developments, implementation, follow-up and customer SOP update…), collaborating with marketing teams, R&amp;D, support and feasibility, and coordinate implementation and follow-up with local teams
</li>
<li>define a proactive communication with the Strategic Accounts and the local bioMérieux team in contact with the Strategic Accounts : Field Actions, new products or updates (protocols/applications, validations, documents…)
</li>
<li>Build the network link to the Strategic account activity (local, regional, corporate, sites…) in order to have an efficient organization
</li>
<li>Travels: 30% of time<br>
</li>
</ul>
