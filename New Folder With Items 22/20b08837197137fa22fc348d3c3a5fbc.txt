<p>
Corporate Risk helps all Wells Fargo businesses identify and manage risk. The team focuses on several key risk types, including conduct, credit, financial crimes, information security, interest rate, liquidity, market, model, operational, regulatory compliance, reputation, strategic, and technology risk. The group provides leadership, enhances communications, assists with problem identification and solutions, and shares best practices. In addition, the group provides an enterprise-wide view of risk, assists management and our Board of Directors in identifying and monitoring risks that may affect multiple lines of business, and takes appropriate action when business activities exceed the risk tolerance of the company.</p>
<p>
Conduct Risk is an Independent Risk Management function responsible for establishing, implementing and maintaining an enterprise-wide program to manage conduct risk; independently overseeing the identification, measurement, assessment, monitoring, aggregation and reporting of conduct risk; overseeing and independently and credibly challenging the Front Line’s execution of its conduct risk management responsibilities; and communicating the importance of conduct risk management throughout the Company, consistent with Wells Fargo’s Vision, Values &amp; Goals.</p>
<p>
We have a new and exciting opportunity for a <b>Conduct Risk Lead Consultant </b>to join Conduct Risk, Policy and Governance team. This team works across Conduct Risk and plays an integral role in conduct related monitoring governance and linkage with Enterprise Testing results for conduct risk owned policies and conduct risk indirect policies and regulatory requirements.</p>
<p>
The person selected for this role will be responsible for assisting with the establishment and assuring execution of operational risk, validation and monitoring programs. Maintains an awareness of the state of operational/compliance risk across the enterprise and provides subject matter expertise; ensures that all conduct related business issues are pro actively identified and addressed appropriately, escalating where necessary; maintains collaborative relationships across the front line assurance efforts, Independent Risk Management (IRM), and Conduct Risk oversight groups.</p>
<p>
Primary Responsibilities include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Partnering with Enterprise Testing &amp; Validation on Conduct Risk and Complaints related testing including determining testing scope and design as well as test script development. (Gifts and Entertainment, Code of Ethics, Conflict of Interest, Allegation Management, Customer Harm, Sales Practices, Insider Threat , Business and Personal Activities, Internal Fraud, Complaints Management )</li>
<li>Evaluation of business process and controls in partnership with Conduct Risk policy owners and Conduct Risk oversight for conduct related policies.</li>
<li>Supporting the development and execution of the annual testing plan (schedule and scope), leveraging results of the Conduct Risk risk assessment and ensuring timely completion of testing plan in accordance with Enterprise standards.</li>
<li>Assisting in the aggregation of results and report development in conjunction with Enterprise Testing.</li>
<li>Provide oversight, monitoring, and credible challenge of control environment in partnership with policy owners and Conduct Risk Oversight teams.</li>
<li>Support of regulatory and audit examinations as needed.</li>
<li>Review and escalation of issues, as needed</li>
</ul>
