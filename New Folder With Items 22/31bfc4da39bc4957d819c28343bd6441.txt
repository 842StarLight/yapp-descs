<h2>Retail Direct Loan Officer Sr</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>As a part of TIAA’s Financial Solutions business, TIAA Bank, a division of TIAA, FSB, provides full-service, nationwide banking and lending services to consumer, commercial and institutional clients through a variety of channels, including online, its Florida-based financial centers and at other business offices throughout the country.<br>
<br>
<b>Primary Characteristics</b><br>
The Retail Direct Loan Officer Senior is responsible for providing a high level of mortgage and loan sales converting prospective clients into new loan applications by presenting customers with a variety of mortgage products and pricing based on their borrowing needs. This position will sell multiple brands of lending products through the use of inbound and outbound sales calls. This role will manage the client experience from origination to closing; additionally, they will have the opportunity to cross sell banking and investment services offered through the partners in other TIAA divisions.
<br>
<br>
<b>Essential Functions</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Sell multiple brands of company loan products to prospects, effectively converting leads to borrowers<br>
</div></li>
<li><div>Proficient analyzing, communicating, and guiding consumers on decisions related to mortgage products as it relates to the specific needs of the customer and the loan applications process; handle mortgage loans of highest complexity, value, and risk<br>
</div></li>
<li><div>Determine and communicate loan level pricing with customers and management in order to maximize profitability<br>
</div></li>
<li><div>Follow underwriting guidelines to determine the documentation and lock/pricing requirements on loan level basis<br>
</div></li>
<li><div>Conversant in various types of borrowing programs and processes; stays current with changes<br>
</div></li>
<li><div>Responsible for mentoring, training, and in some instances managing the progress of new Retail Direct Loan Originators<br>
</div></li>
<li><div>Responsible for the general loan production; error free applications, the ability to foresee possible inadequacies in documentation prior to submission to processing<br>
</div></li>
<li><div>Efficiently work with various types of systems and software programs simultaneously to maximize time spent selling products<br>
</div></li>
<li><div>Knowledge of market fluctuations and expertise managing an active pipeline during those fluctuations<br>
</div></li>
<li><div>Manage active pipeline from origination to closing, work effectively with fulfillment departments to ensure loan requirements are satisfied and deadlines met<br>
</div></li>
<li><div>Adhere to all federal and state compliance guidelines relative to the position<br>
</div></li>
<li><div>Develop sales skills through attending systems and product training and other educational opportunities necessary for the position</div></li>
</ul>
