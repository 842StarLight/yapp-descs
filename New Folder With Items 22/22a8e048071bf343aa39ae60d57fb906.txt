<h2>Outside Sales Representative/AG Customer Account</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>The <b>AG </b><b>Customer Account Outside Sales Representative</b> is responsible for sales and relationships with key customer accounts in an assigned areas of responsibility (AOR). Key customers accounts represent those customer segments that have a significant impact on the dealer-business but develop and maintain relationships with the dealership enterprise through a trusted advisor.</p>
<p><b>Essential Functions: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manages key customer account relationships to provide a differentiated customer experience.</li>
<li>Proactively assesses, clarifies, validates and communicates key customer account needs on an ongoing basis</li>
<li>Provides value to key customer accounts by developing solutions that save time, reduce risk and increase profits.</li>
<li>Develops a contact plan that meets the individual needs of your key customer accounts</li>
<li>Influences customer account trade cycles and current and future needs.</li>
<li>Updates and retains relevant customer account information such as equipment and operation information in the customer relationship management systems</li>
<li>Engages with dealership personnel (AMS, consultant, Parts, Service, etc.) when needed in completing a sale, answering customer account questions and ensuring customer account needs are met.</li>
<li>Represents the company for the sale of equipment, parts, labor and technology-based products and services to assigned customer accounts; Work with Aftermarket departments to closely.</li>
<li>Maintains current product knowledge of all equipment parts, and services available to customer accounts.</li>
<li>Maintains current knowledge of financing and risk management options to assist customer accounts with securing the purchase of a solution.</li>
<li>Maintains and communicates current knowledge of customer account operational requirements, both agronomic/turf industry and / or business goals.</li>
<li>Monitors and timely communicates any competitive activity to management.</li>
<li>Coordinates new equipment field demonstrations.</li>
<li>Actively participates in local/regional industry associations.</li>
<li>Attends applicable sales training events/seminars/clinics.</li>
<li>Maintains assigned company vehicles and equipment.</li>
</ul>
