<p><b>SUMMARY</b> This positionis responsible for performing lab sample analysis in a surface sciencelaboratory, writing reports, and other lab-related tasks to support KRÜSS USA’sfeasibility and contract lab services.</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIALDUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES </b>include the following.</p>
<ul>
<li>Analyze samples in support of sales feasibility studiesor contract lab samples.</li>
<li>Documentanalysis results in a professional and timely manner.</li>
<li>Maintain strict confidentiality regarding all privilegedcustomer information associated with projects</li>
<li>Keep lab instruments in good working order</li>
<li>Adhere to all company and departmental processes,procedures, and safety guidelines.</li>
<li>Ability to work up to 30 hours per week.</li>
<li>Other duties may be assigned.</li>
</ul>

<li>Possess technical and mechanical aptitudes and greatattention to details.</li>
