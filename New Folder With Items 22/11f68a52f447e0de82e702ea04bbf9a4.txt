<b>You are:</b><br>
A subject matter expert with experience working on software solutions around electric vehicle managed charging and complex integration projects in the eMobility and/or Utility space. You are interested in working in a fast paced environment where technologies are evolving around managed Electric Vehicle charging and Distributed Energy Resource Management. You have the ability to design solutions around vendor software tailored to meet the customer business needs. You have strong communication skills for interacting with a variety of parties, including customers, vendors, upper level management, and team members.<br>
<br>
<b>The work:</b><br>
Within Industry X.0, the Connected Energy team is focused on supporting the energy transition with new IT solutions, from managing Distributed Energy Resources to leverage their flexibility to the charging of Electrical Vehicles.<br>
<ul>
<li>Take a leading role in providing subject matter expertise for both pre-sales and project work for software solutions around distributed energy resources, such as Electric Vehicle (EV) Managed Charging Solutions, Distributed Energy Resource Management Systems (DERMS), etc.</li>
<li>Provide a bridge between the customer business teams and the technical teams at Accenture and vendor partners</li>
<li>Lead functional requirements gathering and create associated requirement and high level design documentation including Fit/Gap analysis, Requirements Traceability Matrix, Use Cases, etc.</li>
<li>Provide oversight and guidance throughout the project to ensure functional requirements are met by the implementation</li>
<li>Participate in project management activities, including monitoring project scope and creating plan to handle deviations, identifying project risks and creating mitigation plans to address and helping develop project timeline and resource plan to staff a project</li>
<li>Contribute to Request for Proposal/Information (RFP/RFI) response development and support customer sales presentations</li>
<li>Support internal work to create new offerings and build the business</li>
</ul>
