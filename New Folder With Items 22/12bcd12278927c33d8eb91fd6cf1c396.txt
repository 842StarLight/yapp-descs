<h2>Buyer Small Formats</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>Description:
<p><b>JOIN US AS A SMALL FORMATS Buyer – A&amp;A</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>
Join us in working on a key enterprise initiative at an exciting time when we are learning, iterating and scaling the small format portfolio. Use your skills, experience and talents to be a part of groundbreaking thinking and visionary goals. As a Small Formats Buyer, you'll take the lead as you…</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
PRIMARY FUNCTIONS</b></p>
<p>
Work with Small Formats Sr. Buyer to develop and execute merchandising strategies for Small Format stores to produce planned financial results for assigned product classifications. Determine sustainable methods to build segmented apparel and accessories assortments for Small Format stores.</p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>
Merchandising and Assortment Strategy/Direction</b>: Works with Small Format Sr. Buyer to identify and execute merchandising strategies to deliver a relevant guest experience in Small Format stores. Combines trade area inputs and guest insights with core assortment knowledge to deliver locally relevant assortments. Major tasks include assortment analysis and completing planogram direction for New Stores/Transitions/Revisions.</li>
<li>
<b>
Assortment Planning &amp; Execution</b>: Analyzes category level sales and profitability to create locally relevant assortment strategies by trade area and floor pad size. Actively identifies meaningful guest segments beyond trade area to drive improved assortment curation. Manages assortment strategy execution, communication, and coordination with core merchandising.</li>
<li>
<b>
Education &amp; Insight sharing</b>: Shares small format learnings/considerations, field feedback and white space opportunities with core buyers to help improve format understanding and execution.</li>
<li>
<b>
Financial Accountability</b>: Analyzes and recaps current Small Format sales performance to inform assortment updates and future planning. Shares performance, learnings, and future updates quarterly with core merchandising teams.</li>
<li>
<b>
Process Development:</b> Helps develop sustainable processes for long term scalability of Small Formats process and execution.</li>
<li>
<b>
Field Partnership</b>: Partners with appropriate cross functional teams to address store requests.</li>
<li>
<b>
Cross-Functional Collaboration</b>: Works with multiple cross-functional partners from across the organization, including Core Merchandising, Store Ops, Space/Presentation, Localization, and VM. Partners with the IM Ops team to ensure inventory strategies are executed in Small Format stores.</li>
<li>
<b>
Special Projects</b>: Leads special projects as needed (examples: seasonal strategies and new segmentation opportunities).</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
REQUIREMENTS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
4-year degree or equivalent work experience</li>
<li>
3+ years previous Retail Merchandising or Merchandise Planning experience</li>
<li>
Apparel experience strongly preferred</li>
<li>
Strong analytical abilities</li>
<li>
Strategic orientation</li>
<li>
Thrives in ambiguity and a highly collaborative environment</li>
<li>
Innovates and challenges the status quo</li>
<li>
Flexible and solution oriented</li>
</ul>
