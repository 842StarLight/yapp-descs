<h2>Lead Shop Technician - Heavy Equipment</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Lead position – Generally has more than ten years of experience and/or demonstrated job-relevant knowledge/skills. A Lead Technician has a broad knowledge of the field’s standard and technical concepts, practices, and procedures. Relies on extensive experience and judgment to plan and accomplish goals. Performs a variety of standard, complex, and technical tasks; is self-starting and requires no supervision.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Functions:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Monitors physical requirements for work completion (facilities, tools, environmental) and communicates same on an ongoing basis to his/her supervisor</li>
<li>
Models and reinforces company policies/practices to ensure that work is performed in alignment with the company’s mission, vision, and values and in collaboration with other Holt functions</li>
<li>
Achieves labor sales and utilization expectations as set by supervisor/manager to ensure team revenue targets are met</li>
<li>
Performs job duties of a Service Supervisor during periods of his/her absence</li>
<li>
Troubleshoots and diagnoses equipment malfunctions and failures</li>
<li>
Provides quality control/assurance inspections of completed tasks</li>
<li>
Disassembles and assembles machine components to manufacturer’s specifications through the use of hand tools, pneumatic tools, and any specialized tooling</li>
<li>
Rebuilds, repairs, reconditions and maintains equipment and components</li>
<li>
Demonstrates good customer relations and support; communicates customer needs to shop management</li>
<li>
Plans and controls the cost of repair jobs</li>
<li>
Completes parts orders and identifies parts for return</li>
<li>
Provides a significant amount of task and behavior-oriented supervision, mentoring, and training to others</li>
<li>
Maintains all equipment and work areas – keeps in good, clean, and orderly condition</li>
<li>
Works safely at all times and adheres to all applicable safety policies; complies with all company policies, procedures, and standards</li>
<li>
Works overtime as required by workload and customers’ needs</li>
<li>
Updates job knowledge by participating in educational opportunities; e.g., ProTech Technician Training Paths</li>
<li>
Performs other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Extensive knowledge of machines and tools, including their designs, uses, repair, and maintenance</li>
<li>
Proficient with current computer software, related hardware, and machine electronic systems</li>
<li>
Quality control analysis skills - Conducting tests and inspections of products, services, or processes to evaluate quality or performance and communicate findings</li>
<li>
Operation and control skills - Controlling operations of equipment or systems</li>
<li>
Equipment selection skills - Determining the kind of tools and equipment needed to do a job</li>
<li>
Ability to operate a forklift and an overhead crane</li>
<li>
The ability to quickly and repeatedly operate the controls of a machine or a vehicle to exact positions</li>
<li>
Ability to operate hand tools, power tools, pneumatic tools, and equipment used in a mechanical repair</li>
<li>
The ability to make precisely coordinated movements of the fingers of one or both hands to grasp, manipulate, or assemble very small objects</li>
<li>
The ability to read, write, and understand information</li>
<li>
The ability to listen to and understand information and ideas presented through spoken words and sentences</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Additional Requirements:</b></p>
<ul><li>
Owns a large and complete assortment of appropriate hand tools and may have some specialized tools</li></ul>
<p><b>
Education and Experience:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
More than ten years of work-related skill, knowledge, or experience is preferred</li>
<li>
High school diploma or equivalent preferred</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Travel:</b></p>
<ul><li>
Some travel may be expected for this position</li></ul>
