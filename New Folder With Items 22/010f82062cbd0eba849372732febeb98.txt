<h2>Data Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>As a leading manufacturer and distributor of medical supplies and equipment for healthcare simulation and education, Pocket Nurse provides solutions and services that create hands-on learning experiences for the next generation of healthcare professionals. Our small business philosophy is, ?Use your best judgement every day.? Not only do we give team members the guidance and tools to succeed, we give them autonomy to do their best for the company to succeed</p>
<p>In this exciting new role, the Data Analyst will partner across the Supply Chain, Demand Planning, Distribution Center, Production, and Sales teams to provide financial planning, reporting, analysis and recommendations on in-stock and turn improvements to drive customer satisfaction, sales and profitability. As a trusted advisor to senior leadership and business partners, this role will be responsible and accountable for accurate and timely reporting and analytics to move Pocket Nurse forward based on our strategic goals.</p>
<p>The focus of this role centers around developing creative solutions to problems and decisions based on factual and technical data and communicating those solutions with confidence once sure of the information. A disciplined, conscientious, hands-on approach is central to this job and it requires work that is high quality and precise.</p>
<p><b>Key Responsibilities and other duties as assigned:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Produces, formats and analyzes reports/dashboards from multiple sources including the ERP system and other tools.</li>
<li>Business reporting on a weekly, monthly and ad hoc basis.</li>
<li>Uses business acumen and an ability to communicate findings to mine large amounts of data for useful insights and recommendations.</li>
<li>Applies innovative and analytical approaches to draw conclusions and make recommendations to answer business objectives including:
<ul>
<li>Sales and Financial analysis and planning.</li>
<li>Budget and forecasting.</li>
<li>In-stock and inventory turn improvement.</li>
<li>Distribution Center and Production metrics.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Maintains a high level of confidentiality in all proprietary, planning, development or operation processes, procedures, products or documentation.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Supervisory Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>This position has no supervisory responsibilities.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Education:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor?s Degree in Finance, Business, Economics, Statistics or a related field and 1-3 years of analytical experience with an emphasis on forecasting, actionable analysis, and insightful reporting or the equivalent of 7 years combination of education and experience.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Skills and Experience:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Strong analytics experience with a proven track record of using data to generate insight and drive business results.</li>
<li>Basic understanding of Inventory Management Systems and ERP/CRM software.</li>
<li>Proficienct in Microsoft Access Databases.</li>
<li>Advanced Excel skills including pivot tables, VLOOKUPS and Macros. Proficient in Word and PowerPoint.</li>
<li>Financial budgeting and forecasting experience.</li>
<li>Critical thinking and complex problem-solving skills.</li>
<li>Interpersonal, written and verbal communication skills with a strong ability to clearly and effectively communicate and present findings to all levels of the company.</li>
<li>Motivated, self-starter with an ability to work cross-functionally and develop a collaborative work environment across multiple teams.</li>
<li>Strong organizational skills with a demonstrated ability to manage multiple projects.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Benefits and Compensation:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Competitive wages and quarterly bonus opportunities.</li>
<li>Medical, dental, and vision insurance coverage options.</li>
<li>401(k) plan with company contributions.</li>
<li>Paid time off, paid holidays, and flexible work schedules.</li>
<li>Short-term disability insurance and voluntary long-term disability.</li>
<li>Voluntary life, accident, and critical illness insurance.</li>
<li>Company-wide cookouts, pot-lucks, and work-day events.</li>
<li>On-site fitness center, free parking, and dog-friendly.</li>
<li>Opportunities for career enhancements through training programs and educational programs.</li>
<li>The opportunity to make a difference in the ever-growing healthcare field.</li>
</ul>
