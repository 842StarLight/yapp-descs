<h2>Managing Editor for Plant-Based Lifestyle Brand - 25Madison</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Managing Editor for Plant-Based Lifestyle Brand
<br><br>
Townsquare Media is teaming up with startup studio 25Madison to develop a media brand focused on Plant-Based Lifestyle, and is looking to hire a Managing Editor to oversee digital content and audience development. The Managing Editor will have a passion for and deep understanding of the multiple facets of plant-based living, and how to create engage with the community. The role is accountable for the quality of the content on the website, and the growth of the audience to that content.
<br><br>
The ideal candidate will have at least 5 years of experience in online journalism or content creation, and the ability to nurture a strong editorial voice. Exceptional writing and editing skills are a must. The person filling this role should understand what makes content on the web and social media engaging, conversation-starting and good for SEO and social traffic-driving strategies. A track record of building a brand from scratch is very helpful.
<br><br>
The job also requires a sense for marketing and socialization of content, the ability to focus under fast-paced pressure, and the capacity for managing multiple tasks at once. Great communication skills and attention to detail are a requirement. Experience working with Wordpress, HTML, Photoshop and video is a plus, as is familiarity with AP style.
<br><br>
<b>Key responsibilities include:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Lead the day-to-day operation of the new brand and its staff of full-time and freelance writers and contributors to excellence</li>
<li>Create compelling and relevant digital news and editorial content for the plant-based lifestyle community. This content will consist of text, photo, video and interactive content across the site, mobile app and social media.</li>
<li>Develop content ideas for the community based on audience demand and search data</li>
<li>Top edit several posts and features a day</li>
<li>Manage the calendar of content for the site</li>
<li>Conceive of innovative ways to distribute the content across the web and grow the audience</li>
<li>Monitor and analyze web statistics with the goal of improving performance</li>
<li>Utilize and enforce best practices for Search Engine Optimization and Social Media to drive audience.</li>
<li>Creatively strategize new ways to increase site audience and engagement and be accountable to key metrics including Unique Visitors, Repeat Visits, and Pages per Visit.</li>
<li>Grow an active database for email, mobile app and social media</li>
<li>Partner with Sales Team to develop and execute sponsored content programs and other consumer offerings that can be monetized by advertising partners.</li>
</ul>
