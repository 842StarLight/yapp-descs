<h2>Leasing Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>We have an immediate opening for a Leasing Specialist with our community Gulstream Isles located in Fort Myers, FL. As a Leasing Specialist, you will become the public face of our community and will be the key liaison between the management office and our current and prospective residents. We are looking for a friendly, outgoing individual who preferably has experience in leasing for multi-family communities; sales experience, and great customer service.</p>
<p>
Our company offers excellent benefits including paid time off, 13 paid holiday schedule, 401K and commission pay.</p>
<p>
Key Responsibilities</p>
<ul>
<li>Meet with perspective residents and conduct tours of available units of interest</li>
<li>Prepare and execute leases in accordance with the community standards and regulations</li>
<li>Promote vacant units using all marketing and social media tools available</li>
<li>Conduct credit and other background verifications for qualification approvals</li>
<li>Collect application fees, security deposits and rent payments</li>
<li>Coordinate Service requests as needed</li>
<li>Communicate changes in rental agreements and community events to residents</li>
<li>Follow-up with prospective residents</li>
<li>Establish rapport with residents and provide excellent customer service</li>
</ul>
</div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>2-3 years of Leasing specialist experience with multi-family properties preferred</li>
<li>Sales and/or marketing experience</li>
<li>Customer Service Experience</li>
<li>Ability to work a flexible schedule including weekends or past 5:00 pm</li>
<li>Software experience should include Microsoft office suite</li>
<li>Experience using One Site software preferred</li>
</ul>
</div>
