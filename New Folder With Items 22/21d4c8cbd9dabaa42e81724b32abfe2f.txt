<h2>Operations General Manager Tulsa or Washington, DC Area (ID#15615)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Cherokee Nation Businesses – Federal Solutions, is looking for an <b><i>Operations General Manage</i></b><b><i>r</i></b><b> </b>for its businesses in the Environmental &amp; Construction sector. This exciting opportunity will leverage the candidate’s leadership ability and experience in Federal Construction. With a $120 million in annual revenue, 20% growth per year, approximately 200 contracts and 750+ employees, Cherokee Nation Businesses’ (CNB) group of Federal Contracting Section 8(a) companies is well positioned to deliver expertise needed to solve complex technical issues. Federal Solutions provides an array of services for 12 of the 15 cabinet-level departments and agencies – from applications and data services to cybersecurity and national defense.</p>
<p><b>SUMMARY</b></p>
<p>Under direction of the Executive General Manager the Operations General Manager will be responsible for direct development and the day to day operations and oversight of standards for construction projects, within CNCS. Ensures the highest levels of business practices are in place and continuously monitors and makes recommendations to operational controls and construction operations. Individual must be capable of interfacing with the various levels of the federal customers, and develop continued working relationships with same.</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<p>Includes the following, other duties may be assigned or tasked by the EGM:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Formulates LLC/ company policies and procedures.</li>
<li>Is responsible for managing daily operations.</li>
<li>Reviews performance against operating plans and standards. Provides top level reports to the EGM and carries out the direction of said plans with direct authority</li>
<li>Plans for the use of materials and human resources.</li>
<li>Directs people to various goals and targets.
</li>
<li>Monitors and tracks the performance and achievements of the employees as well of the entire company.
</li>
<li>Responsible for matters related to finance and transactions are within his supervision.
</li>
<li>Prepares contracts and bids; estimates costs and makes schedules; and ensures projects stay within budget.</li>
<li>Reviews and approves cost control reports along with cost estimates and manpower and workload requirements.</li>
<li>Maintains appropriate lines and methods of communication with defined area of responsibility.</li>
<li>Ensures performance on contracted efforts by executing approved plans, controlling and reporting progress relative to the established contract values and cost, schedule, quality and safety standards.</li>
<li>As a corporate representative maintains continuous and effective communication with customer organization to obtain and exceed their expectations.</li>
<li>As a member of corporate management. Plays a leadership role in the accomplishment of the E&amp;C Sector vision and defined by the EGM of that sector.</li>
<li>Recommends personnel actions to the EGM, for approval and follows lines of disciplinary related actions as directed.
</li>
<li>Maintains and fosters a working BD process with engaged clients and also seeks out new opportunities within the Federal construction market.</li>
<li>Has a high level proficiency with the Federal capture, proposal, and marketing, and contract, and PTW strategies used in the in the region</li>
</ul>
<p><b>SUPERVISORY/MANAGEMENT AUTHORITY</b></p>
<p>Manage multiple project and program managers while having direct supervisory responsibilities, as directed by the EGM. Lead and/or guide the work of other employees. Supervise the day to day construction operations and its efforts and provide authority over hiring/firing/performance decisions, along with operational objectives.
</p>
<p><b>EDUCATION and/or EXPERIENCE</b></p>
<p>Position requires a minimum of Bachelor’s Degree in Construction Management related field of study and 12 years’ experience, in various venues of construction such as, Federal, ANC, Tribal, 8a, Hubzone, OBO, GSA, DOD, and USACE. Certifications such as CCM and PMP, would be greatly beneficial.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS AND ABILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Well-developed management skills, both in principles, and public/business presentations with above average people skills, and team leadership experience.</li>
<li>Proven ability to recruit, train, mentor and motivate personnel in order to balance staffing strength with profitability and growth.</li>
<li>Proficiency in Project Management software required.</li>
<li>Familiarity with types and methods of construction cost, sales, revenue forecasting and reporting</li>
<li>Knowledge of PMBOK and its methodology is a definite plus.
</li>
<li>Knowledge and understanding of engineering principles and guidelines</li>
<li>Above average knowledge and ability to read and discern blueprints and plans</li>
<li>Ability to write and develop PWS, and Proposals, Capture processes.
</li>
<li>EM 385-1-1 background required
</li>
<li>Familiar with the regulations and working knowledge of the DFAR-FAR
</li>
<li>Working knowledge and experience with the SBA, 8(a), Hub zoned system of contract modeling
</li>
<li>Capable knowledge and understanding of JV, Mentor Protégé programs</li>
<li>Expert knowledge and understanding of the 1-48 Divisions within Federal construction</li>
<li>High level knowledge of scheduling, estimating, warranty and closeout procedures in contracting</li>
<li>Ability to chair and do presentations of a weekly, bi-weekly basis to sub-contractors and to top level customers in a professional environment.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>WORK ENVIRONMENT</b></p>
<p>This position involves a large percentage of an office (business casual) environment with occasional trips to the work sites/offices in the field under all types of conditions. Must be comfortable in required PPE if needed. This role also has a “high” interaction with the Federal customer/client in their office surroundings where decorum and professional demeanor, and attire is required.</p>
