<p>ABOUT THE OPPORTUNITY</p>
<div><div>
<b>Position Summary</b><br>
Produces graphic art and visual materials for packaging, displays, promotions and informative material. Generates and manipulates graphic images and/or animations into consolidated and seamless presentations. Uses creativity and latitude in performing job functions.<br>
</div></div>
<p></p>
<div><div>
<b>Essential to this Position</b>
<div>
<p><b>Primary Duties and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Creates innovative design solutions and produces samples to meet customer requirements, as required</li>
<li>Effectively manages multiple projects and provides ongoing status reports to key stakeholders</li>
<li>Supports development of specifications and diagrams to be used for production orders</li>
<li>Supports sales team including interaction with customers and/or vendors</li>
<li>Maintains stock inventory and evaluates possible stock alternatives, as required</li>
<li>Reviews in-process production orders to assure conformance to design functionality</li>
<li>Facilitates or executes prototyping, testing of new design, standardized specifications, creation of instructions and/or animations for end user to ensure product performance, quality and operational efficiencies</li>
<li>Develops, implements, and monitors new process and product performance parameters for new and existing products</li>
<li>Reviews sample requests for layout and planning of project with key stakeholders, as needed</li>
<li>Provides technical and professional leadership throughout the organization including assisting in root cause and failure mode analysis, vendor development, and education/training</li>
<li>Participates in industry trade shows and events including set-up and/or professional development opportunities</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Essentials for Success:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Technical degree required, Bachelor’s degree preferred</li>
<li>Proficient in the use of current design software (i.e. Artios, SOLIDWORKS, Adobe Illustrator, etc.)</li>
<li>Minimum 6 year of relevant experience required</li>
<li>Prior expeirence communicating directly with customers in-person</li>
<li>Technical knowledge about packaging materials and their applications</li>
</ul>

<p>ABOUT MENASHA PACKAGING</p>
<b>Menasha Packaging Company</b> is a leading designer and manufacturer of graphic consumer corrugated packaging, merchandising solutions, food packaging, shipping containers, material handling solutions, and pack-out and fulfillment services. As the largest independent retail-focused display and packaging company in North America we set new standards in sustainable packaging, and our printing and structural design capabilities are unrivaled in the industry. Our Retail Integration Institute (RII) Group uses an exclusive merchandising model with processes specifically designed to bridge the gap between consumer packaged goods companies and retailers. We currently operate seven RII campuses in geographically dispersed locations. Our 3,200+ employees include more than 100 designers and more than 50 project managers who, together with our highly skilled workforce, develop new, innovative and cutting-edge solutions for global customers. With over 40 locations we are geographically positioned to serve major food, beverage, consumer products, entertainment, electronics, healthcare and pharmaceutical companies with superior products and fulfillment services.<br>
<br>
As an operating company of <b>Menasha Corporation</b>, we celebrate over 167 years of unwavering dedication to a sustainable business strategy that focuses on keeping pace with change and market trends. <b>Menasha Corporation</b> is a privately held company comprised of two subsidiaries: <b>Menasha Packaging Company</b> and ORBIS Corporation. As one of America’s oldest family-owned manufacturing companies, <b>Menasha Corporation</b> employs over 5,000 in over 100 facilities in North America and Europe.<br>
