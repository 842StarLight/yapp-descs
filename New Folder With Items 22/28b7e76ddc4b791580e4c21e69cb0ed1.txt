<p>About the role:<br>
</p>
<p>
In this role you will design, develop, and modify new and existing curricula for IDEXX sales professionals, customer support technicians, field support representatives, and IDEXX customers, using your writing and instructional design skills to support e-learning, webinar and live presentation-based formats. You'll work closely with a variety of subject matter experts and business partners to continuously improve training programs and design learning experiences to educate internal and external customers about our products and services.</p>
<p>
Responsibilities include:</p>
<div>
<div>
Conduct needs assessments and strategic learning assessments to develop the basis for content development.</div>
<div>
Develop unique and compelling learning and/or instructional assets that support business objectives, usability, and generate results.</div>
<div>
Work with subject matter experts on design and development of learning solutions using a balanced combination of media (text, graphics, animation, audio, and video) to maximize engagement and successfully transfer instructional message.</div>
<div>
Through collaboration, understand business requirements to develop related training curricula for sales professionals, customer support technicians, field support representatives and customers. Identifies training program goals, overall program objectives, and module objectives.</div>
<div>
Consistently apply Adult Learning Theory and facilitation/communication methods to ensure reaching the broadest possible audience effectively.</div>
<div>
Work with product managers and subject matter experts to author and maintain product knowledge bases across several CRMs.</div>
</div>
<p></p>
<p>
Must haves/Desirables:</p>
<div>
<div>
Experience in executing all stages of the instructional design process - analysis, design, development, implementation, and evaluation - with an emphasis on new development and redesign. Work samples will be requested.</div>
<div>
Experience should include working with complex content and designing training for multiple delivery modalities (traditional classroom, virtual, and self-paced e-Learning modules).</div>
<div>
Demonstrates excellent written and verbal communication skills.</div>
<div>
Strong attention to detail.</div>
<div>
Has a broad knowledge of instructional technologies and is proficient in the advanced use of e-Learning tools that do not require programming such as Articulate Storyline and Rise.</div>
<div>
Proficient using photo editing and presentation tools such as PowerPoint, Adobe Photoshop, and SnagIT.</div>
<div>
Nice to have - Knowledge of video authoring and editing tools including Camtasia, Adobe Premier, and Audacity (animated video software).</div>
<div>
Nice to have – Knowledge of self-paced training authoring tools including Walk-Me.</div>
</div>
