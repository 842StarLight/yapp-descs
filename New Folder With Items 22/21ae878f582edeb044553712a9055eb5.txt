<div></div>
<br>
<div>
ACCO Brands is seeking a talented individual to join the Finance team as a <b>Accounts Receivable Specialist</b> at the Sidney, New York location. We are looking for organizational skills and candidates who are detail-oriented to manage and work as the contact with our customers as the Accounts Receivable (AR) service provider.</div>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Primary Duties and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Collect past due payments via phone and email.</li>
<li>
Prepare documentation for collection claims.</li>
<li>
Manage and monitor daily assigned workflow in order to obtain collections within expected timeframes.</li>
<li>
Work within the cash applications process for the Oracle and SAP systems to ensure accuracy and efficiency.</li>
<li>
Analyze customer and internal data establishing the cause of key issues; develops solutions and triggers appropriate action to avoid future occurrences. Implement process solutions and communicates with customer and internal departments.</li>
<li>
Ensure receipts are coded correctly and resolved within expected timeframe.</li>
<li>
Assist with deduction research and resolution.</li>
<li>
Work with customer care, sales and other ACCO Brands departments as needed to resolve issues.</li>
<li>
Maintain a high level of accuracy in collections by enforcing customer payment instructions.</li>
<li>
Perform other related duties as required for the efficient operation of the department, or as directed by the supervisor.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Position Qualifications
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div><b>Qualifications:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Minimum 2 years accounts receivable experience.</li>
<li>
College degree preferred or the equivalent of education, training and experience.</li>
<li>
Strong knowledge base of collections and accounts receivable processes.</li>
<li>
Ability to work in a time-sensitive and high volume environment.</li>
<li>
Strong organizational skills required due to high volume of transaction types per customer each with different processes, department structures and terminology.</li>
<li>
Persistent follow-up skills requiring multiple sign-offs on issues at different levels of authority at both ACCO and customer departments.</li>
<li>
Good verbal ability to effectively communicate with different departments at all levels of the customer and internal departments.
</li>
<li>Produces written communication containing all relevant information, clearly expressed reasoning and recommended actions or next steps to produce results.</li>
<li>
Able to perform effectively in a team environment.</li>
<li>
Demonstrated use of software with a variety of different system skills including a proficiency in Microsoft Excel and Word.</li>
<li>
Experience with Oracle or SAP preferred.</li>
</ul>
