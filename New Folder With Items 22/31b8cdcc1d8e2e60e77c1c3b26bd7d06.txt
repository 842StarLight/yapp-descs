<h2>MES Senior Developer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>MAVERICK Technologies is seeking a talented Sr. Developer to join our team.
</p>
<p>If you have an interest in helping manufacturers run more efficient by utilizing your software skills, then this is the opportunity for you!<br>
</p>
<br>
<p>We are flexible on location. Will require about 30% travel.
</p>
<p><b>Preferred Criteria:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science, Computer Engineering or equivalent experience
</li>
<li>8 or more years of experience in software project delivery in manufacturing environments
</li>
<li>Experience working on development teams using a technology stack like .NET, Java, J2EE, web services, ASP, SQL Server
</li>
<li>A minimum of 3 years of domain experience working with manufacturing execution (MES), manufacturing operations management (MOM), SCADA, or material resource/enterprise resource planning (MRP/ERP) systems or applications
</li>
<li><p>Experience with RA Products hardware and software product suites (ControlLogix, Logix 5000, FT View, FTBatch, FT Historian and FT VantagePoint.) is a plus
</p></li>
</ul>
<p><b>Technical Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrates a detailed knowledge and experience of software development techniques and principles along with ability to accurately estimate effort required to deliver software to meet design specifications
</li>
<li>Strong technical and functional experience in using and developing an “enterprise-wide” business application on a platform like MES, ERP, etc. (i.e. SAP, Apriso, CAMstar, Wonderware, FactoryTalk Production Centre, etc.)
</li>
<li><p>Analyzes customer's requirements to determine the optimal information solution and determines applicable configuration/architecture.
</p></li>
<li><p>Ability to interface information systems to the factory automation layer through data historians, OPC servers, or other mechanisms.
</p></li>
<li><p>Make recommendations for improvements and assist Project Manager to propose these to the customer.
</p></li>
<li><p>Plan, schedule, coordinate and monitor resources for projects including setting and managing time-scales and budgets.
</p></li>
<li>Experience in working closely with customers in defining the requirements and of key information solutions using a similar platform to one as listed above.
</li>
<li>Experience working on development teams in creating and managing XML and XSD structure for data transfer. (Knowledge of B2MML a plus)
</li>
<li>Knowledge of pertinent industry concepts and standards as they pertain to MES (i.e. MESA, ISA 95, etc.)
</li>
<li>Experience in creating software development templates and libraries to facilitate team development speed and progress in all above technology areas.
</li>
<li>Implementation experience on a minimum of 2 multi-site MES (or enterprise-wide) software projects utilizing a full software development lifecycle (Use Cases &gt; Design &gt; Development &gt; Testing &gt; Go-Live &gt; Support) preferred.
</li>
<li>Experience in interfacing MES systems to ERP and other “peer” external systems a plus.
</li>
<li>Experience in interfacing MES systems to the SCADA or Automation layer through Data Historians, OPC servers, or other mechanisms.
</li>
<li>Demonstrates a high level of knowledge and consistent performance in conversion of software design to effective implementation in multiple commercial software products including following MAVERICK's Project Complete® Methodology.
</li>
<li>Demonstrates team leadership through ownership of technical project deliverables and enhancement of sales support tools through case study development for assigned project work scopes.
</li>
<li>Performs and or directs the technical work scope for projects, and provides accurate status reporting of same to project management.
</li>
<li>Responsible for maintaining and enhancing personal technical skills as well as development of same for less experienced team members.
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Critical Competencies:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrates a proactive commitment to safety by taking the time to understand risks and completing all required safety training.
</li>
<li>Complies with safety requirements by following all safety procedures and by reporting/documenting safety incidences and near misses.
</li>
<li>Demonstrates personal accountability to learning and development of oneself as well as assisting with development of new employees and peers.
</li>
<li>Willingly accepts coaching and mentoring from more experienced resources and provides the same when working with entry level resources.
</li>
<li>Promotes teamwork by participating as a team player while also demonstrating effective communication skills with the team, management and the customer.
</li>
<li>Proactively works to build good relationships with peers, leaders and customers while also demonstrating the ability to meet customer expectations.
</li>
<li>Seeks out new responsibilities and stretch opportunities while demonstrating creativity in identifying better means of accomplishing a job.
</li>
<li>Displays ambition for learning new processes, platforms or other new offerings at MAVERICK.
</li>
<li>Displays leadership abilities including honesty, integrity and professionalism across all aspects of the business.
</li>
<li>Demonstrates a willing, can-do attitude by actively participating in teams.
</li>
<li>Exhibits proficiency and personal accountability for project performance and meeting customer needs.
</li>
<li>Demonstrates a business acumen that allows for effective utilization of tools and standards to ensure project deliverables are met.
</li>
</ul>
