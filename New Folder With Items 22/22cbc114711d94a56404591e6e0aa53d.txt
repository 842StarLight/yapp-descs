<h2>Branch Manager I</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div><div>
<div>
<div>This position is responsible for demonstrating a passion for excellence through providing leadership and management for a branch team ensuring profitability, growth, and sound operation.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Position Accountabilities</b></div>
<ul><li>Direct and lead branch activities and the customer experience (i.e. leading/managing the branch team, ensuring branch goals are achieved, ensuring customer service excellence, acting as an escalation point for customer problems, and adhering to, implementing, training, and enforcing branch policies/procedures/regulations).</li></ul>
<ul><li>Responsible for achieving individual and branch sales and lending goals and demonstrating ongoing improvement. Identify customer needs and promote/sell the Bank’s products/services; including, but not limited to, cross selling products/services to expand customer relationships, initiating branch calling efforts, promoting the Bank through community involvement, attending networking events, referring business to other branch employees and business partners, inspiring/energizing/training employees, and maintaining/ensuring adherence to partnership agreements.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Offer guidance to customers and provide a full spectrum of customer services, including: opening new business and personal accounts, explaining bank products and services which are appropriate to customer’s needs and gathering information needed to accurately and efficiently process new and existing accounts.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Enhance strong customer relationships through performing new account follow-up (i.e. Thank You letters and outbound calls) for business and personal accounts and performing account maintenance, including, but not limited to reviewing reports and making/confirming necessary maintenance requests in a timely manner.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Identify opportunities to grow the business and independently call on and develop new and existing business customer prospects.</li></ul>
<ul><li>View the business through the lens of the customer to set, achieve and exceed service standards by adhering to established Service Quality Standards.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Build strong teams through positive, proactive communication and coaching. Select and retain top talent, set clear performance expectations, keep employees informed, develop/motivate employees, mentor and assess performance (i.e. ongoing coaching, counseling, training, formal performance appraisals).</li></ul>
<ul><li>Create an environment that is passionate about meeting and exceeding sales, service, and operational goals by setting an example of professionalism, ownership, and initiative. Provide formal observation and coaching sessions.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Guide and assist consumer loan applicants through the lending process. Accurately enter application data into the loan origination system. Correspond with customers, applicants, and/or creditors to resolve questions regarding application information. Notify applicants of the loan decision. If necessary, schedule/hold a closing session and assemble the loan package.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Stay informed of industry trends, competitor information, and maintain knowledge of the Bank’s products/services, pricing, and the Bank’s operating systems, customer relationship management software; utilize software identify customers’ needs and actively refer to other lines of business.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Assist with branch functions and support other positions or job duties including, but not limited to, teller transactions servicing overdrawn/past due accounts, night depository responsibilities, servicing the branch’s ATM and vault, wire log, OFAC review, NSF processing, and Back Counter processing.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Complete arrangements and documents for special services such as foreign currency/items, direct deposits, the origination of outgoing wires, service government bond transactions, Notary and Signature Guarantee services, and other customer services duties, as necessary.</li></ul>
<ul><li>May oversee, prepare, and distribute branch schedules.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Responsible for ensuring satisfactory audit ratings are achieved.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Perform a variety of administrative functions to include conducting audits, approving customer transactions, analyzing customer lists, and monitoring the branch’s key performance indicators.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Perform other such duties as assigned.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintain compliance with and adhere to all state and federal regulations and bank policies and procedures, including, but not limited to Bank Secrecy Act, FACT ACT, Community Reinvestment Act, and EEO/AA/Fair Employment Practices.</li></ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Organizational Relationship</b></div>
<div>This position reports to the Market Manager or Area Manager.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Position Qualifications</b></div>
<div><b><i>Education &amp; Experience</i></b></div>
<ul><li>Bachelor’s degree or equivalent work experience</li></ul>
<ul><li>Minimum three years of retail banking experience in a sales environment</li></ul>
<ul><li>Minimum one year of supervisory/human resources experience</li></ul>
<ul><li>Some lending experience preferred</li></ul>
<div></div>
<div><b><i>Knowledge &amp; Skills</i></b></div>
<ul><li>Successful candidate must meet and comply with all requirements set forth in the SAFE Act, including, but not limited to successful completion of the required background checks and obtaining a Unique Identifier from the NMLS</li></ul>
<ul><li>Demonstrated leadership skills and the ability to inspire employees to excel, as well as, proven ability to lead employees to embrace a “needs based sales culture” and attain goals as assigned</li></ul>
<ul><li>Exceptional customer service skills with the ability to the ability to establish and maintain relationships with internal and external customers, including customer confidentiality</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to ask questions to uncover and identify varying customer needs; ability to present product features and benefits effectively and provide sound financial guidance</li></ul>
<ul><li>Demonstrated track record of meeting sales and service goals</li></ul>
<ul><li>Decision-making/problem resolution</li></ul>
<ul><li>Highly motivated and detail oriented individual</li></ul>
<ul><li>Excellent written and oral communication skills</li></ul>
<ul><li>Excellent computer skills relevant to Microsoft Office Suites (i.e. Word, Excel, Outlook) and industry related software</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to work all branch hours, including weekends and evenings required; demonstrate flexibility when schedule changes are necessary</li></ul>
