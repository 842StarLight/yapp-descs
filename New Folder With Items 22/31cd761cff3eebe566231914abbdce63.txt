<h2>International Studies Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>CIEE:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>A nonprofit, non-governmental organization, CIEE is the world leader in international education and exchange. For over 70 years CIEE has been committed to bringing the world together through meaningful international exchange programs to help humanize international relations – all students, all nations. Driven by this purpose, CIEE has helped thousands of people gain the knowledge and skills necessary to live and work in a globally interdependent and culturally diverse world by offering the most comprehensive, relevant, and valuable exchange programs available. Serving over 400 U.S. College and University consortium members, CIEE operates sixty-five study centers in forty-five countries that support study abroad programs for over 10,000 students annually. In addition, as the largest sponsor for U.S. Department of State J-1 Visa programs, CIEE helps nearly 30,000 international students participate in U.S. based exchange programs annually. CIEE programs and services span study abroad, teach abroad, international faculty development seminars, and inbound exchange study, work, and internship programs for today’s high school and university students, professionals, and educators.<br>
<br>
CIEE strives to lead a robust community of international program providers and sponsors through innovation, exemplary service, and exceptional efficiency. Our success depends on having the very best professionals. We are committed to recruiting and retaining the best people in the industry, particularly those who are passionate about international education, are self-starters with high levels of energy and autonomy, and are dedicated to exceed expectations in every challenge. We foster excellence through tapping the skills of a diverse workforce and promoting a dynamic environment where talented individuals of differing perspectives and cultural backgrounds work together to advance our mission.</p>
<br>
<p><br>
<b>Summary of Position:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>The International Studies Coordinator High School Summer Abroad role supports the team in achieving program enrollment goals through direct sales to students. The International Studies Coordinator assists the Manager, Director, and Sales team in promoting the High School Summer Abroad Programs and Global Navigator Scholarship Initiative to students and parents, working with them through the application process. The International Studies Coordinator achieves objectives through personal initiatives and successful coordination with customers: schools, teachers, students, and parents, and colleagues: High School Summer Abroad team, seasonal staff, Marketing, and Support staff.</p>
<br>
<p><br>
<b>Primary Responsibilities:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul><li>Communicate and coordinate with Sales team to achieve program enrollment goals in selling CIEE High School Summer Abroad programs and the Global Navigator Scholarship Initiative</li></ul>
<ul><li>Convert inquiries from prospective participants of CIEE High School Summer Abroad Programs and the Global Navigator Scholarship into sales</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>Coach participants through selecting programs that align with their goals for a summer abroad, and work with families to overcome barriers associated with studying abroad in high school.</li>
<li>Coordinate daily with temporary and field staff to manage workload and support participants during the busy sales season</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Host webinars targeting students and parents</li></ul>
<ul><li>Use reports to manage a high-volume pipeline of applications</li></ul>
<ul><li>Follow sales process for participant outreach</li></ul>
<ul><li>Monitor stock and ship marketing materials on behalf of field staff in coordination with Marketing</li></ul>
<ul><li>Conduct market research to help achieve program expansion goals</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintain accurate records in Salesforce (CRM software) and other internal databases</li></ul>
<ul><li>Document all communication with schools, teachers, students, and parents during the sales process</li></ul>
<ul><li>Provide support to Operations team as necessary, preparing students to travel abroad</li></ul>
<ul><li>Assist Manager and Director in ensuring high schools are prepared to promote CIEE High School Summer Abroad Programs and the Global Navigator Scholarship Initiative</li></ul>
<ul><li>Provide coverage for the CIEE emergency cell phone 1 week/year</li></ul>
<ul><li>Travel to, and represent CIEE at study abroad fairs and school events as needed</li></ul>
<ul><li>Other duties and projects as assigned</li></ul>
<br>
