<h2>Warehouse Associate - Lite Assembly</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>60:40 Warehouse / Light Assembly</p>
<p>Reporting to the Warehouse Manager, the Warehouse Associate is expected to safely meet goals and objectives for the company by assisting in the warehouse. He/she will be expected to perform a normal range of operations while paying attention to detail, etc. Meet safety objectives. Must be able to lift boxes up to 50 lbs. Learning and understanding of various types of products. Strong PC skills required. Working knowledge of shipping software preferred. Good communication skills required.</p>
<p>The right candidate will have a positive attitude and outstanding attention to detail; must be reliable, self motivated and able to work independently. Must possess the ability to multitask and manage priorities while maintaining accuracy and attention to detail.</p>
<ul>
<li>Assist shipping and receiving unloading trucks, checking in merchandise, matching purchase orders to sales orders and distributing to customer service for processing.</li>
<li>Read customer orders, work orders, shipping orders or requisitions to determine items to be moved, gathered or distributed and/or shipped.</li>
<li>Prepare incoming product for stock; labeling, repackaging, etc. as required.</li>
<li>Basic math skills required.</li>
<li>Must be willing to undergo a drug screening, in accordance with local law/regulations if required.</li>
</ul>
