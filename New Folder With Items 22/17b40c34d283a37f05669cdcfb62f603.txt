<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Description</b></h2>
<div>
This is a highly visible and exciting role within the Xandr Media Sales Operations team!<br>
<br>
Xandr is an industry leader in audience-based targeted advertising. At Xandr we aim to Make Advertising Matter by enabling advertisers to reach and engage a targeted audience with relevant messaging across screens. With one of the world’s largest collections of digital, film and TV properties, we provide a premium option for advertisers and publishers looking to reach specific audiences at scale in premium and brand-safe environments.<br>
<br>
To fulfill this mission Xandr is actively seeking a full-time and experienced Senior Business Analyst to join the Data Management and Business Intelligence Systems team. A successful Senior Business Analyst at Xandr will be a key individual contributor to the data management and business intelligence initiatives for the Xandr Media Sales organization. In this role, he/she will drive value through the design and implementation of business intelligence systems to transform the way Xandr Media Sales produces client reporting, processes internal information, and utilizes data to make critical decisions that guide the strategy, roadmap, and execution of the organization.<br>
<br>
This person will perform non-technical project and program-management work focused on internal Xandr Media products as well as company-wide projects or programs. The Senior Business Analyst will help coordinate delivery across multiple product managers and support teams and serve as a key liaison across stakeholders. This person will proactively identify issues, track KPIs and help manage project/program scope, quality, schedule, resources, and risk.<br>
