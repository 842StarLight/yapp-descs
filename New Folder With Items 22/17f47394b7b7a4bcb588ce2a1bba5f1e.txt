The Investment Relationship Manager (IRM) is the investment planning and investment and insurance products lead on the Private Client Group Team. The IRM is responsible for facilitating the delivery of strategies and capabilities including portfolio management, trust administration, investment advisory and insurance. IRMs partner with the team to assist clients and families by gaining a thorough understanding of their unique goals and objectives and constructing appropriate goals based investment portfolios. IRMs advise clients using U.S. Bank's Asset Management Group (AMG) or U.S. Bancorp Investment's open architecture where appropriate, to deliver economic and market views, investment strategy, manager/fund/security research and due diligence, and portfolio construction resulting in tailored investment management and insurance services based upon a client's unique situation.</div>
