<p><b>MARKET RESEARCH SPECIALIST</b></p>
<p>Mobility Lab is seeking to hire a Market Research Specialist to complement its research team and work on cutting-edge research projects in Arlington, Virginia.</p>
<p>The Market Research Specialist will leverage their research background and quantitative skills to find innovative ways to answer research questions around transportation behavior and travel demand management in Arlington, Virginia. The Market Research Specialist supports Mobility Lab’s in-house capacity for collecting and analyzing data, integrating transportation data into the programs Destination Sales and Marketing Group, Ltd. (DS&amp;MG) runs for various clients, and further bringing “research to practice” for DS&amp;MG and Arlington County Commuter Services (ACCS) programs. The Market Research Specialist reports directly to the Research Director and interacts with other Mobility Lab, ACCS, and DS&amp;MG staff members.</p>
<p><b>What is Mobility Lab?</b></p>
<p>Mobility Lab is a research center and information source for transportation behavior and travel demand management. Founded in 2010, Mobility Lab is managed by The Destination Sales &amp; Marketing Group, Ltd. (DS&amp;MG), and is an innovative initiative in advancing the field of travel behavior and Transportation Demand Management (TDM).</p>
<p>Mobility Lab, which serves as Arlington’s long-standing and extensive TDM research program, applies rigorous research methods and analytical frameworks to solve real-life transportation challenges focused on Arlington County, Virginia with best practices from around the United States. Examples include measuring the impacts of TDM services in Arlington County and the U.S., examining the drivers behind transit ridership and exploring the impact of new shared mobility devices on the transportation landscape. Mobility Lab’s research provides a basis for advocates of policy and investment in TDM at the local, state, and national levels.</p>
<p>Mobility Lab also acts a communications platform that disseminates its research and other literature (such as the benefits and successes of TDM) and best practices to a wide audience.</p>
<p>Mobility Lab collaborates with other programs within the DS&amp;MG including Arlington Transportation Partners, BikeArlington, WalkArlington, and Capital Bikeshare as part of a dynamic and innovative regional TDM program for ACCS.</p>
<p><b>Duties and Responsibilities: </b></p>
<p>The selected candidate will gain hands-on research experience in a fast-paced work environment, crafting and promoting original Mobility Lab research. Duties may include but are not limited to:</p>
<ul>
<li>Collaborates on research projects such as collecting, analyzing and modeling travel behavior and travel management data.</li>
<li>Reviews regional datasets such as regional household travel surveys and operational travel data, and analyzes the data for ACCS program needs.</li>
<li>Reviews the state of the practice and synthesizes the literature as is useful to the team</li>
<li>Advises staff on key messages to highlight from research.</li>
<li>Stays well-versed on research and TDM industry trends and brings ideas to team meetings, and occasionally writes for the website to build profile as a thought leader.</li>
<li>Collects, and analyzes attitudinal and behavioral data, such as conducting focus groups and online surveys to understand the determinants of bus ridership.</li>
<li>Exploring and modeling regional travel behavior using statistical software such as Stata, R or SPSS.</li>
<li>Spatially analyzes and visualizes travel behavior data in GIS.</li>
<li>Tracks key performance indicators and benchmarks for travel behavior and travel demand management.</li>
<li>Collaborates on support for research and data-related needs for ACCS programs.</li>
</ul>
