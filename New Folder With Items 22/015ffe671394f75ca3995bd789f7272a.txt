<h2>Software/Hardware Electronics Technician</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Manufacture, test and service automation products, including process control software/hardware,<br>remote printer terminals, self-service kiosks, camera systems and business integration software. We seek bright, people-oriented individuals with strong computer skills and a willingness to travel.</p>
<p>Specific Responsibilities:<br> Tests, troubleshoots and repairs process control software and hardware.<br> Performs field service, including installation, training and troubleshooting.<br> Provides customer support via telephone.<br> Equipment assembly and wiring.<br> Interfaces with engineering &amp; sales departments.</p>
<p>Education/Experience/Qualifying Skills:<br> Minimum of two-year technical degree – GPA of 3.3 or greater.<br> Related work experience beneficial but not required.<br> Strong software skills, including the ability to learn new software applications and updates.<br> Capability to read schematics, recognizing logic and analog circuits.<br> Thorough knowledge of electronic components and equipment including the use of<br>oscilloscopes and multi-meters for diagnostic and repair purposes.<br> Must be able to work well with all levels of personnel whether individually or in a team<br>environment, and especially must be able to interface and communicate with plant operators in<br>order to assist with repairs and troubleshooting.</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Salary: $20.00 /hour</p>
</div>
