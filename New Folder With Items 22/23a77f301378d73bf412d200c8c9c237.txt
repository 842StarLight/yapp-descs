<h2>DIRECTOR OF INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY SECURITY & COMPLIANCE</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>PURPOSE:</b><br>
The Director of Information Technology Security and Compliance will work as a primary operational lead for all Information Technology security and compliance activities. The Director will work closely with multiple cross-functional teams to understand customer and legal requirements, enforce security standards, implement and review designs, and provide solutions that support our business objectives. This role is a strategic leader while remaining hands-on technically.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop Security Architecture for highly scalable and fault-tolerant applications that adhere to expected standards and discipline from a security posture.</li>
<li>Identify security design gaps in existing and proposed architectures and recommend changes or enhancements.</li>
<li>Work with our product management organization to develop secure business requirements, develop the security architecture and integrate into our long-term platform strategy.</li>
<li>Own security systems planning and implementation.</li>
<li>Provide training and awareness on security and information technology compliance-related concerns to the extended team.</li>
<li>Work directly with project development teams to enable successful project implementation by applying the recommended security tools, technologies and techniques. Provide expertise to engineers as needed.</li>
<li>Drive comprehensive security policy development and enforce the policies.</li>
<li>Lead risk management activities, including risk models and analysis of weaknesses.</li>
<li>Lead incident response activities, tabletop exercises, penetration tests, and remediation efforts.</li>
<li>Lead penetration testing efforts.</li>
<li>Design and lead incident management efforts.</li>
<li>Ensure compliance with IT-related requirements, such as SOC, HIPAA, HiTrust, FIPS, FEDRAMP, and GDPR.</li>
<li>Arrange and coordinate customer and third-party audits.</li>
<li>Manage and lead vulnerability assessments.</li>
<li>Lead IT-related physical security measures such as badging, cameras, and monitoring.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>EDUCATION AND WORK EXPERIENCE:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in relevant technical field.</li>
<li>15+ years IT-related experience, including IT management, with 5+ years in the security area.</li>
<li>Proven success with security compliance management.</li>
<li>Experience implementing and managing IT security solutions.</li>
<li>Software Development environment experience.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>SPECIALIZED KNOWLEDGE AND SKILLS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Grasp of the “big picture” (company structure and key business initiatives).</li>
<li>Strategic mindset with a desire to be hands-on technically.</li>
<li>Sense of urgency, with a positive attitude.</li>
<li>Proven excellent organizational, coordination and negotiation skills.</li>
<li>Ability to effectively prioritize and execute in a high-pressure environment.</li>
<li>Team player with proven relationship building skills.</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal and verbal and written communication skills.</li>
<li>Proven ability to work across geographical locations.</li>
<li>Ability to interact and work effectively across all functional areas (e.g., Technology, Sales, Marketing, Finance, HR) and at all levels of the organization.</li>
<li>Effective leadership and influencing skills.</li>
<li>Sound project management skills. Must be able to chart a course of action, establish accountability, and follow through to ensure execution.<br>
Sound business judgment. Must be able to set priorities, make plans to organize work, and perform multiple tasks concurrently.</li>
</ul>
