<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Job Title</b></h2>
CRM Digital Marketing Senior Specialist<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
The Challenge</b></h2>
The CRM Digital Marketing Senior Specialist will be responsible for helping to develop and execute our overall value proposition and lifecycle messaging to our consumer base. This role will be charged with improving the understanding of Monogram and Café Appliances among prospective customers as well as new and existing customers and increasing our consumer engagement and lifetime value. The manager will work with cross-functional teams from Marketing, Product, Brand, Analytics, Research, Finance, and Merchandising Teams.<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
What you will Do</b></h2>
<p><b>
Essential Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Assist in development and execution of overall communication strategy for increasing the comprehension of Monogram and Café Appliances.</li>
<li>
Work with Analytics and Research teams to create deeper insights about consumer behavior to shape the strategy</li>
<li>
Work with Product, Service and Brand teams on developing new value propositions, language, and call-to-actions for those value propositions</li>
<li>
Leverage first, second and third-party data to develop enhanced segmentation of our customer base to drive more relevant messaging</li>
<li>
Lead the creative development of the email and content marketing strategy; partnering with the creative partners, analytics and research team. This includes developing positioning &amp; message design, segmentation focus, creative themes and consumer experience</li>
<li>
Manage the execution and refresh of our ongoing lifecycle marketing campaigns - driving impactful, fresh creative that further drives business goals</li>
<li>
Partner with other marketing team members to ensure smooth execution of marketing campaigns</li>
<li>
Develop a robust testing agenda to ensure continued evolution of our lifecycle marketing campaigns</li>
<li>
Highly cross- functional role; the CRM manager will work with teams across Marketing, Product, Brand, Analytics, Research, Finance and Merchandising.</li>
<li>
Consistently review analytics to gain insights and drive decision making.</li>
</ul>
