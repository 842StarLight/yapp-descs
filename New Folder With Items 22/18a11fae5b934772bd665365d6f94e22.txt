<div><div><div>It's an exciting time for the <b>Long-Term, Post-Acute Care </b>organization within Cerner! We're looking for a <b><i>Solution Strategist </i></b>for our Rehab Solutions, and you'd be given a great opportunity to join a growing team! As a Solution Strategist, you will develop solutions by identifying potential opportunities, conducting market research, generating solution requirements, pricing and creating integrated go-to-market strategies. Some other responsibilities include crafting, communicate and justify solution roadmap across the corporate portfolio; analyzing solution needs and define scope requirements to ensure it meets market needs, including best practice workflows, scope of capabilities and visual design; and collaborating to set priorities for development efforts based on alignment with business goals, strategic direction, voice-of-the customer feedback, timelines and client commitments. If you're looking to make an impact in health care solutions, we invite you to apply today!<br>
