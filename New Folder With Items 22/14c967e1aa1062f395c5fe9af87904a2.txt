<h2>Director of Marketing - Juice It Up!</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Position: </b> Director of Marketing</p>
<p><b>Job Scope: </b></p>
<p>While reporting to the President and CEO, this position manages all marketing related activities including all brand marketing and advertising initiatives, digital marketing plans, media relations, new and existing product promotions, web and mobile app services, demographic and customer research. The Director of Marketing will lead agency and marketing team to build and activate successful marketing plans/activities that achieve financial and strategic objectives of the Juice It Up! brand.</p>
<p>Specific key responsibilities will include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Oversees the process to develop short-term and long-range marketing strategies for the brand</li>
<li>Works with agency and team to develop local store marketing objectives and activation plans for the brand</li>
<li>Oversees the company’s media relations including social media and all methods of advertising</li>
<li>Supervises the company’s market and consumer research plans</li>
<li>Demonstrates a continual track record of sales success from month to month and year to year</li>
<li>Manages budgets to support all marketing functions (planning, media, PR, research, etc.)</li>
<li>Sets up and reviews all sales analytics (processes and metrics) to ensure ROI objectives are met</li>
<li>Drives daily execution and enhancement of the overall marketing plans/calendar</li>
<li>Analyzes the competition and reviews results of market research to guide marketing efforts</li>
<li>Compiles data/trends to assist in product development</li>
<li>Participates in research to look for new product ideas and to grow sales</li>
<li>Manages website and constantly updates information provided on the site to serve our guests</li>
<li>Handles PR communications to deliver a brand-consistent message</li>
<li>Heightens awareness of the company regionally and nationally</li>
<li>Leads and directs agency of record to support key business objectives</li>
<li>Partners with Operations to ensure that marketing plans and products are being successfully implemented in the field</li>
<li>Leads agency to develop impactful and creative and brand messaging to support all marketing efforts</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Experience: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Business undergraduate degree or greater</li>
<li>Experience within another Franchise system for 5 to 10 years</li>
<li>Experience in a restaurant/QSR Franchise system a plus</li>
<li>A track record of successes</li>
<li>Exemplifies an outgoing personality, a great attitude, high energy and a willingness to work as needed in a team environment</li>
<li>Strong communication, interpersonal, and organization skills</li>
<li>Business legal, contract, real estate, and negotiating experience</li>
</ul>
