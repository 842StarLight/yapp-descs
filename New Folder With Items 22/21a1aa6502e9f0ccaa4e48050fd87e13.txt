<h2>CLIQ System Specialist, Abloy USA</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>CLIQ System Specialist, Abloy USA</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><i>Abloy is a strong global brand, world's leading developer of solutions in the field of electromechanical locking technology, access control and a pioneer in detainer-disc cylinder technology. We serve customers in more than 90 countries worldwide. Our main focus areas internationally are professional end users within critical infrastructure and smart building sectors.</i><br>
<br>
We are now looking for a CLIQ System Specialist / Technical expert for CLIQ products and application range in US region for Abloy. In this role, you will work closely with sales teams and distribution partners in the region as well as Abloy factory's service team in Finland. The position involves regular traveling, good interpersonal skills and strong customer sensitivity. You will report directly to Customer Success Manager, Abloy USA.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Principal Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Accompany sales teams and distribution partners with sales activities, deployments and usage in regards to CLIQ projects at customers</li>
<li>Provide technical expertise, support and training of the CLIQ product, software and service range</li>
<li>Provide documentation and tools for sales teams, distribution partners and integrators for sales development</li>
<li>Communication related to CLIQ systems in the region about scheduled updates, new features, service outages, unplanned incidents and other notifications</li>
<li>Detecting customer needs and documenting solutions</li>
<li>Analysing the market and surrounding technical evolutions as well as competitors' features and movements</li>
<li>Defining the new development needs</li>
<li>Monitoring product development (following the development of solutions with the teams concerned and guaranteeing adequacy of the specifications)</li>
<li>Support alpha and beta tests as well as POCs in the region</li>
<li>Know, measure and share the value of the solutions produced throughout the economic chain (distributor, installer / integrator, end customer) by being in constant contact with customers and regional sales</li>
<li>Participate in the development of the strategic plan for the product range in collaboration with business development.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Education &amp; Experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of Bachelor's degree in Electronics Engineering / Information Technology / Computer Science or similar</li>
<li>Minimum 3 years of experience in Windows server operating system and Microsoft SQL Server, IT infrastructure (<i>such as firewalls, networks, DNS, proxy, domain</i>), IT security and technologies, in addition to software programming languages and integration technologies (SOAP, REST)</li>
<li>Experience in project management, business continuity and disaster recovery</li>
<li>Working knowledge of industries in Critical Infrastructure, commercial and petroleum are an advantage</li>
<li>Fluent in English, both written and spoken</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Who you are</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Self-imposed, rigorous and responsive</li>
<li>Results oriented</li>
<li>Able to meet the deadlines and work under pressure</li>
<li>You have a strong business acumen with the ability to translate business needs into technical specifications</li>
<li>Person with excellent interpersonal and communication skills, able to work in a multicultural team and across all organisation levels</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Position will be based in Irving, Texas and it includes regular travelling in United States. Therefore, the applicant must have a valid US Passport.</p>
