<h2>Senior Manager, Desktop Support</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>POSITION: Senior Manager, Desktop Support</p>
<p>
DEPARTMENT: Information Technology</p>
<p><b>
REPORTING SUPERVISOR:</b> VP, Information Technology</p>
<p><b>
DIRECT REPORTS:</b> Yes</p>
<p><b>
FLSA: </b>Exempt</p>
<p><b>
EMPLOYMENT STATUS:</b> Direct Hire</p>
<p><b>
TRAVEL REQUIREMENTS:</b> Up to 20% local travel and 10% overnight travel may be required</p>
<p><b>
SCHEDULE:</b> Standard business hours</p>
<p><b>
ABOUT THE TEAM:</b></p>
<p>
The Desktop Support team operates in a tight-knit, collaborative environment. IT, Systems Development, Product Management, Customer Care, Marketing &amp; Direct Sales all work closely together to align on creating value through health and wellness products and services. We also strive to maintain a strong bond and trust between our internal teams (architecture, development, test, configuration, operations, etc.). We encourage each other to work together and help one another make our work life at GreatCall more satisfying and rewarding. This is a team that constantly looking for ways to improve our customer oriented service. The team also fosters a culture of innovation and encourages everyone to contribute through new ideas and insights.</p>
<p><b>
ABOUT THE JOB:</b></p>
<p>
As a Senior Manager of the Desktop Support team, you will be responsible for developing and managing all aspects of the Desktop Support groups in our corporate office, remote users, and Caring Center agents according to industry best practices and company policies and procedures. You will be counted on for complex problem solving as well as encouraged to recommend solutions. As a member of the GreatCall team, you will have the unique opportunity to make a difference in people’s lives, developing and optimizing applications that improve the quality and stability of our health, wellness, and emergency response systems. Our customers and their loved ones come to depend on our products and services every day. Your job will be critical to our success in meeting their needs!</p>
<p><b>
RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul><li>Ensure Service Desk staffing, processes, and technology is aligned with all desktop support teams in corporate and Caring Centers</li></ul>
<ul><li>Coached and developed on-site and remote technical personnel, including onboarding, goal setting and professional development, performance improvement and succession planning.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Balance resource capacity and demand, directly overseeing ticket prioritization and escalation to increase first call resolution</li></ul>
<ul><li>Research the root cause of issue categories, implementing self-service, training/coaching, and process/system improvements</li></ul>
<ul><li>Analyze Desktop Support team performance using metrics and KPI’s (Key Performance Indicators) to meet department’s performance goals</li></ul>
<ul><li>Adhere and manage to company established Service Level Agreements to deliver excellent customer service and build strong internal relationships with all departments</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manage cross-functional deadline driven projects from proof of concept through to completion and maintenance mode.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintain department hardware and software inventories in support of onboarding, upgrades and special projects. Ensure adherence to IT asset management best practices for auditing and tracking purposes</li></ul>
<ul><li>Collaborate with other IT managers/leaders in deployment and support of new products and services. Participate in on call rotation and after-hours support and incident escalation on an as needed basis</li></ul>
<ul><li>Obtain IT Department HW and SW quotes, and contracts, generate purchase orders. Track software licensing, subscriptions, and HW and SW maintenance agreements to ensure compliance. Approve invoices for IT department purchases. Facilitate equipment repairs, returns , e-waste and hard drive destruction through parent company approved process and vendors.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manage onboarding of all employees through various systems and processes and optimize where needed</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ensure efficient operation of all laptops, desktops, and terminal hardware on Windows\MAC OS</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manage and support conference room solutions to ensure optimal performance.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Create/manage department documentation and end user instructions to meet internal compliance or training requirements.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Keep up to date with emerging technology to help facilitate onsite and remote support of users</li></ul>
<ul><li>Other duties as assigned</li></ul>
<p><b>
QUALIFICATIONS:</b></p>
<p><b>
Education:</b> Bachelor's degree preferred or equivalent experience</p>
<p><b>
Certification(s):</b> HDI Manager or ITIL training and certification preferred</p>
<p><b>
Experience:</b></p>
<ul><li>Minimum 6 years of experience directly managing a technical support team required</li></ul>
<ul><li>Minimum 6 years of experience working in a Caring Center environment or large user base (1,000 users +)</li></ul>
<ul><li>Minimum 6 years of experience managing a service desk required</li></ul>
<ul><li>Minimum 6 years of experience configuring, deploying, and managing workplace hardware, familiarity with networks, workstation, telephony, and mobility components required</li></ul>
<ul><li>Minimum 3 years of experience managing remote teams</li></ul>
<ul><li>Prior experience managing outside vendors required</li></ul>
<p><b>
Knowledge/Skills/Abilities:</b></p>
<ul><li>Proficiency in providing account support in a Microsoft Office 365 and Windows environment</li></ul>
<ul><li>Proficiency in identifying desktop and application issues and trends</li></ul>
<ul><li>Knowledge and configuration of Windows 10 and Apple OSX, iPhone, and Android devices in enterprise environment</li></ul>
<ul><li>Enterprise imaging and desktop deployment technologies (preferably WDS, MDT, and/or JAMF)</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experience with Jira, and/or Service Now,</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experience with enterprise management and troubleshooting of Office365 suite (Excel, Word, PowerPoint, Teams, OneDrive, etc.)</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experience with video teleconferencing equipment and systems.</li></ul>
<p><b>
Personal Attributes:</b></p>
<ul><li>Coach and Mentor</li></ul>
<ul><li>Interpersonally Savvy</li></ul>
<ul><li>Interrupt driven</li></ul>
<ul><li>Action Oriented</li></ul>
<ul><li>Drive for Results</li></ul>
<ul><li>Establish trust and maintain confidences when addressing company sensitive information and situations.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to develop positive, collaborative, reciprocal working relationships with peers</li></ul>
<ul><li>Committed to professional growth</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to set priorities and stay focused</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to learn new technologies quickly</li></ul>
<ul><li>Time Management skills</li></ul>
