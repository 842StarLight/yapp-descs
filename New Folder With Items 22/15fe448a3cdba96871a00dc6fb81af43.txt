<b>Position Summary:</b><br>
The Director of Marketing is a senior level creative and digital strategist that will provide input to our marketing approach, analyze data and translate it into actionable direct tactics. He/She will be directly involved with development of the overall marketing plan, have control of the marketing budget and will be involved with the day-to-day operations. The Director of Marketing is responsible for growing the department through new programs and tools and identifying efficiencies to be more productive. This individual must be able to work in a collaborative environment, collaborating with various department heads to execute on both internal and external initiatives, executing on inbound and outbound campaigns, generating leads and increasing company sales. The ability to articulate the specific value of TBI offerings and our competitive advantage is critical to the success of this role.
<br><br>
