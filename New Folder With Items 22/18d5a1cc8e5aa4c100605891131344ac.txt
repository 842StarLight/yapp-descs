<h2>2019-2020 Campus Graduate - Treasury Leadership Development Program (MBA</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Description
<br><br>
Dow is seeking MBA candidates majoring in Finance who are graduating in December 2019 or Spring 2020 , and who are motivated by the challenges and rewards of working for a company that is experiencing tremendous growth and change. Dow is a diversified chemical company and has a history of innovation and risk-taking.
<br><br>
Treasury is a global function within Dow that ensures the Company’s financial flexibility enabling the Company to meet business and corporate strategies in support of our strategic transformation. Treasury is responsible for the management of the Company’s liquidity, funding and capital and works closely with executive management. We are a process driven organization - aiming for excellence and innovation in each of the key financial areas: financial risk management , funding , cash management , financial planning , credit risk management , corporate risk management and financial asset management . We strive to manage risk holistically across the organization by utilizing an enterprise wide risk management methodology.
<br><br>
<b>The Treasury work processes are comprised of the following:</b><br>
<b>Financial Risk Management:</b> Management of a portfolio of foreign exchange, interest rate, and commodity exposures via hedge strategy development and execution.
<br><b>Funding:</b> Professionals participate in the development of capital structure and formulate funding strategies to meet the financing needs of the company.
<br><b>Cash Management:</b> Manage the day to day liquidity of the Company and its subsidiaries in support of the optimal short-term financing and investment decisions.
<br><b>Financial Planning :</b> Determine the Company's optimal risk adjusted capital structure and forecast cash flow.
<br><b>Credit Risk Management:</b> Customer credit risk analysis and mitigation in support of the business strategies to optimize the Company’s working capital management.
<br><b>Corporate Risk Management:</b> Risk analysis and mitigation options for various business loss scenarios.
<br><b>Financial Asset Management (Portfolio Investments):</b> Manage the Company’s global pension plan and insurance assets via the development of investment strategies and related asset allocation decisions.
<br><br>
We look for highly motivated individuals who are passionate about Treasury. We seek to build a diverse workforce of professionals who have the ability to work in a global team environment. Applicants must be able to demonstrate superior analytical, communication and problem-solving skills with a commitment to continuous improvement and life-long learning.
