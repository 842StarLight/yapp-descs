<h2>Infrastructure Support Engineer – Lowe’s Innovation Labs</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Lowe’s Innovation Labs is what’s next for home improvement retail. With a focus on emerging technologies – ranging from 3D visualization to mixed reality to robotics to things we can’t talk about right now – our team accelerates the experiences that customers expect today and develops the capabilities that power tomorrow.</div>
<div></div>
<div>
<br>
The Infrastructure Support Engineer will be responsible for cloud management, network engineering and troubleshooting, project management, data cabling, and systems administration in a variety of software and hardware environments.</div>
<div></div>
<div>
<br>
<b>
WHAT YOU WILL DO</b>
</div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>
Install, manage and troubleshoot on-Prem Linux &amp; Window servers</li>
<li>
Monitoring system performance and troubleshooting issues</li>
<li>
Manage cloud services and resources (GCP &amp; Azure)</li>
<li>
Automate deployment and resource provisioning (e.g: Ansible, Terraform)</li>
<li>
Implement automation tools and frameworks (CI/CD pipelines)</li>
<li>
Collaborate with team members to improve engineering tools, systems and security.
</li>
<li>Develop and maintain design and troubleshooting documentation.
</li>
<li>Setup, configure and monitor system and application alerts and health</li>
<li>
Mobile device management in a Hybrid corporate and BYOD environment</li>
<li>
Analyze issues &amp; create solutions for hardware &amp; software problems</li>
<li>
Travel and on-call support as needed</li>
<li>
The Infrastructure Support Engineer must have an excellent understanding of computers (both hardware and software) and a willingness to learn and apply new technology</li>
<li>
Must possess superior technical aptitude and the ability to deal effectively with people in a wide variety of situations. Additionally, they must possess strong analytical skills with demonstrated problem solving ability. Previous experience exercising high levels of initiative, judgment, and diplomacy required</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
<b>
BASIC QUALIFICATIONS</b>
</div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s Degree, CS, CIS, or related field</li>
<li>
8+ years of experience in an IT support environment with technical experience in distributed technologies and systems development</li>
<li>
3+ years of experience in a leadership role with or without direct reports</li>
<li>
2+ years of infrastructure engineering experience working across multiple domains, platforms, or specialty areas</li>
<li>
Experience using Mobile Device Manager solutions for tablets, iOS and Android device management</li>
<li>
Extensive knowledge and experience with Microsoft Windows 10, Mac OSX, iOS administration, and troubleshooting</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div>
<br>
<b>
Preferred Qualifications</b>
</div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>
Familiar with Perforce version control administration, GIT, and VSTS</li>
<li>
Experienced with cloud services such as Azure, Google Cloud platform, or AWS</li>
<li>
Automation framework such as Ansible, Terraform, and Chef.
</li>
<li>Excellent oral and written communication skills</li>
<li>
Experience with video collaboration tools and conference room technology support; Skype and MS Teams experience a plus</li>

This position requires domestic travel quarterly</li>
