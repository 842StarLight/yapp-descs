<b>Job Description:</b><br>
The administrative assistant position will be responsible for supporting our sales and admin teams with various administrative tasks with an emphasis on scheduling and coordinating sales appointments.
<br><br>
<b>Office Location:</b> North Phoenix, I-17/101
<br><br>
<b>Key Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Facilitates the timely completion of scheduling requests from sales team: -Includes confirming sales appointments and putting sales appointments on the calendar according to geographic location and staffing availability.</li>
<li>Maintains master schedule to ensure coordination between sales, customers, and project managers.</li>
<li>Gathers all detailed information for the customer in order to process the sales appointments/leads and enters all customer information in computer system.</li>
<li>Accurately verify and update customer demographics in the database as well as any changes or additions to the schedule.</li>
<li>Responsible for answering high volume of incoming calls and the scheduling and confirming of sales appointments.</li>
<li>Ability to modify, correct, cancel and overbook appointments as required.</li>
<li>Support our sales/admin team through misc administrative tasks.</li>
</ul>
