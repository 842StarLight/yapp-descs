<p><b>DESCRIPTION:</b></p>
<p>Division Overview:</p>
<p>Our team provides multi-platform promotional support for CBS and CBS All Access programming on CBS.com (desktop + mobile), the CBS App, and OTT. We also work cross-functionally with CBSi’s content strategy, social, editorial, and integrated sales marketing teams to execute homepage takeovers, email marketing, and social media campaigns.</p>
<p>Role Details:</p>
<p>This person will work closely with the Senior Marketing Manager to implement integrated campaigns across digital marketing channels including CBSi sites, email, search and display, OTT, social media, and mobile. They will also provide administrative support including meeting scheduling, taking meeting notes, and invoice processing for a range of high visibility CBS.com programs and campaigns.</p>
<p>Your Day-to-Day:</p>
<ul>
<li>Set up paid social media campaigns across Facebook, Instagram, and YouTube</li>
<li>Work cross-functionally with Design, Video, Ad Ops, and Network Marketing teams to assist in overseeing creative requests, managing project timelines, prioritizing and tracking deliverables, and facilitating stakeholder approvals</li>
<li>Traffic creative assets and flighting instructions to On-Air, VOD, CAN, and CBS All Access Audience Acquisition teams</li>
<li>Assist in conducting and monitoring analysis of campaign KPI’s including tracking against marketing goals</li>
<li>Manage master calendar of all key programming dates including premieres, tentpole events, specials, and finales for both CBS and CBS All Access shows</li>
<li>Help create campaign summary decks, marketing support plan documents, and KPI reports</li>
<li>Stay apprised of industry trends and conduct competitive market research</li>
<li>Execute special projects as needed</li>
</ul>
<p>Key Projects:</p>
<ul>
<li>Network Fall Premieres campaign assistance</li>
<li>Midseason and Summer season network campaign assistance</li>
<li>Live tentpole paid social support (ie. GRAMMY® Awards, ACM® Awards, Tony® Awards)</li>
</ul>
