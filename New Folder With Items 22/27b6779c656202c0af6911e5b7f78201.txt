<h2>Treasury Management Sales Officer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>SUMMARY</b><br>
<b>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>The Treasury Management Specialist is responsible for performing the best in class customer service activities relating to the Treasury Management products and services for the Bank. This role is a consultant and business partner to the Relationship Management team to ensure all Bank clients are offered the right products to fit their individual needs.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b><b>
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Handle in-bound customer service inquiries, training and support requests pertaining to Treasury Management &amp; Internet Banking products and services, while ensuring customer service quality.</li>
<li>Reviews and ensures the completeness, accuracy and adequacy of signed customer agreements, maintenance requests and cancelation forms.</li>
<li>Assists with completion of daily and monthly tasks that support the Internet Banking and Treasury Management functions.</li>
<li>Works with lending teams, branch staff, marketing and other departments in answering customer questions.</li>
<li>Responsible for adherence to all compliance programs, including but not limited to, completion of all required and assigned training modules by<br>
established due dates.</li>
<li>Understand and comply with requirements of all laws and regulations applicable to the position.</li>
<li>Timely responses and dedicated support for all incoming communications via phone, fax, email and online.</li>
<li>Complete new client setup and/or deletion requests and client training in all related products and services</li>
<li>Maintain relevant working knowledge of industry practices pertaining to the full suite of products and services offered.</li>
<li>Assist in fulfilling the department’s audit requirements and ensure the implementation and completion of agreed audit recommendations.</li>
<li>Provide support to department employees in all areas of Electronic Banking and Treasury Management products and services.</li>
<li>Provide remote technical assistance to clients</li>
<li>Serve as a subject matter expert on system capabilities, client onboarding process &amp; worksheets and bank agreements related to Treasury Management services</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>ADDITIONAL RESPONSIBILITIES</b><b><br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrates high degree of professionalism in communication, attitude and teamwork with customers, peers and management</li>
<li>Demonstrates high level of quality work, attendance and appearance</li>
<li>Adheres to all Company Policies &amp; Procedures and Safety Regulations</li>
<li>Adheres to local, state and federal laws</li>
<li>Performs any other duties that may be assigned by warehouse manager</li>
<li>Understands and complies with all company rules and regulations</li>
<li>Attends training and maintains a basic knowledge of procedures to ensure compliance with laws and regulations governing financial institutions, as they apply to your position and job responsibilities, with an emphasis on the BSA/AML/CIP, Consumer Privacy, Regulation CC, Information Security, Bank Protection Act, FACT-Identity Theft, Information Technology and GLBA.</li>
<li>Additional duties as assigned as it relates to the position</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>SUPERVISORY RESPONSIBILITIES</b><b>
</b></p>
<p>This position does not have supervisory responsibilities</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>MINIMUM QUALIFICATIONS</b><b>
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>2 to 3 years of related Treasury Management experience and/or training; or the equivalent combination of education and experience.<br>
Educational experience, through in-house training sessions, formal school or financial industry related curriculum, should be applicable to the financial industry.</li>
<li>Some knowledge of related state and federal banking compliance regulations, Bank operational policies and procedures, and the Bank’s products and services.</li>
<li>Exceptional verbal, written and interpersonal communication skills, with the ability to apply common sense to carry out instructions and instruct others, train personnel, write reports, correspondence and procedures, and speak clearly to customers and employees.</li>
<li>Ability to deal with complex problems involving multiple facets and variables in non-standardized situations.</li>
<li>Ability to work with no supervision while performing duties.</li>
<br>
</ul>
