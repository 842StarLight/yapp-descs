<h2>Clinic Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Academic Dental &amp; Orthodontic School Clinics located in Henderson, NV are seeking a Clinic Account Manager. The Clinic Account Manager is responsible for managing the work of all non-clinical business staff in coordination with clinician schedules to ensure service delivery in practice and operational aspects. The overall goal is to achieve excellent customer service, quality, cost and delivery performance in line with company strategy, goals, and values. The Clinic Account Manager will provide support and direction to enhance performance, skills and knowledge of Dental School Clinic business staff within the assigned environment. In addition, the Clinic Account Manager will work closely with the Academic Dental &amp; Orthodontic Clinics to ensure the delivery of quality dental and orthodontic care to patients.
