<h2>Assistant Retail Store Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Job Title: Asst. Retail Store Manager</p>
<p>
Job Category: (HR) HOURLY</p>
<p>
Company/Dept.: Sprint</p>
<p>
Job Code/ Req# (HR) ARSM</p>
<p>
Travel Required (Y/N): NO</p>
<p>
Level/Salary Range (HR): NE5A</p>
<p>
Position Type: FT or PT: FT</p>
<p>
Reports to: Store Manager</p>
<p><b>
Role and Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>
This position assists the Store Manager with daily store activities and oversees the store operation in the absence of the Store Manager. The Store Assistant Manager provides top quality service and assistance to customers; coaches store employees and works with the store manager to maintain a safe, cost effective and profitable store.</p>
<p><b>
PRIMARY RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>
Customer Service:</b> Provides and coaches store employees to provide excellent customer service in a courteous, friendly and attentive manner to ensure the customer has a pleasant shopping experience.
</li>
<li>Greets customers on entering the store and THANK them as they leave.</li>
<li>
Gives assistance and offers suggestions or recommendations to the customer.</li>
<li>
Resolves or assists to resolve routine customer concerns within established guidelines.</li>
<li>
Reports to work on time and follows the dress and appearance code.</li>
<li>
Lead by example.</li>
<li>
<b>
Store Condition:</b> Maintains an awareness of overall store and property conditions; notifies the Store Manager of any equipment failure or maintenance/supply needs; performs or directs store employees to perform a variety of general housekeeping duties including:</li>
<li>
Sweeping, mopping, and cleaning floors and driveways.</li>
<li>
Dusting and /or washing windows, counters, displays, food service areas and bathrooms.</li>
<li>
Picking up and disposing of trash, litter, debris in store, parking lot and/or surrounding grounds.</li>
<li>
Cleaning/checking equipment including coffee machines, gas pumps, telephones and other machines, tools and work aids used.</li>
<li>
<b>
Merchandising:</b> Monitors and ensures that store product area (including gasoline, merchandise and food service) are stocked, rotated, and displayed to ensure freshness, appeal and easy customer access.</li>
<li>
Prepares or assists in preparing merchandise orders.</li>
<li>
Prepares and ensures food items are prepared according to menus and recipes with proper weights, holding times and temperatures; practices and ensures safe food handling procedures are followed.</li>
<li>
Uses and coaches store employees to use suggestive selling and other techniques to maximize sales.</li>
<li>
Makes price changes on merchandise in accordance with Sprint Foods, Inc. price book or promotional planner; places advertising and pricing signage inside or outside the facility including marquees, walls, windows, pump toppers.</li>
<li>
Monitors gasoline competition and reports findings to the Store Manager.</li>
<li>
<b>
Sales Controls:</b> Ensures that all shift sales are completed in accordance with government regulations and company policies and procedures by performing and coaching store employees to perform:</li>
<li>
Verifications of customer eligibility to purchase age-restricted products. Age-restricted products include alcoholic beverages, tobacco products, lottery tickets, fireworks and adult magazines.</li>
<li>
Proper register transaction procedures and appropriate use of equipment for specialty product sales such as money orders, credit cards, checks processing and lottery sales and redemptions.</li>
<li>
<b>
Financial Controls:</b> Keep accurate cash, sales, food cost, payroll and inventory control records and accounts for variances.</li>
<li>
Assist with or completes all appropriate daily paperwork required to perform the banking function and daily deposit as directed by the Store Manager; ensures currency drops are made appropriately.</li>
<li>
Follows vendor check in procedures and accounts for quality and quantity of merchandise received during vendor deliveries.</li>
<li>
Notifies the Store Manager of any sales, cash, inventory or operating discrepancies.</li>
<li>
Follows company guidelines for taking items for store use (such as cleaning supplies) or for product breakage or spoilage.</li>
<li>
<b>
Staffing Controls:</b> Oversees the activities of store employees as directed by the store manager.
</li>
<li>Helps coach and train new employees to carry out their duties in accordance with Company operating, conduct and performance standards.</li>
<li>
Assist with scheduling and assigning duties to store employees.</li>
<li>
Assists with counseling of employees; provides feedback to help employees improve performance and gives input on performance reviews.</li>
<li>
<b>
Internal/External Controls:</b> Follows and ensures store employees follow all government laws, health and safety codes, food brand inspection standards, and the Sprint Foods, Inc. policies, procedures and standards that apply to the conduct of business. Seeks guidance from Store Manager and/or appropriate management areas about legal questions or obligations.</li>
<li>
<b>
Security Controls:</b> Assists the Store Manager in maintaining a safe environment for employees and customers and protecting Sprint Foods, Inc. assets such as information, cash, merchandise, gasoline, equipment and property.</li>
<li>
Reports all incidents (employee or customer injury, gas drive-off, theft, property damage, chemical release, improper waste disposal, etc.) to the Store Manager.</li>
<li>
Reports any situation in which unsafe, unlawful or unethical conduct is or might be occurring.</li>
<li>
<b>
Administrative Controls:</b> Prepares or assists in the preparation of periodic sales, merchandise or operating reports as directed by the Store Manager.</li>
<li>
Takes inventory of stock to identify items to be reordered or replenished; monitors environmental readings.</li>
<li>
Performs duties associated with shift change such as counting the cash drawer and completing end of shift/day reports.</li>
<li>
Reviews and verifies employee paperwork and timekeeping records to ensure accuracy and completeness.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
PRIMARY WORKING RELATIONSHIPS</b><b>:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
 Retail Store Manager and District Manager</li>
<li>
Capable of performing the Sales Associate Job Requirements.</li>
<li>
High school diploma or equivalent, plus 6 months to 1 year of retail or food service work experience or an equivalent combination of education, training and work experience.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of basic business and customer service principles including planning and organizing, resource management and leadership skills.</li>
<li>
Ability to communicate information and ideas in a clear and understandable manner.</li>
<li>
Ability to deal with customers, unanticipated problems or events using standard procedures.</li>
<li>
Ability to accurately operate a variety of electronic equipment that may include cash registers or scanners; money order and lottery machines; car washes, hot/cold beverage dispensers, hot dog grills and microwave ovens.</li>
<li>
Physical demands: work requires frequent standing and walking entire shift; occasional to frequent bending, stooping or crouching; frequent lifting/carrying of up to 50 lbs.; occasional climbing and descending ladders.</li>
<li>
Working Conditions: work can include variable hour on all shifts, weekends and holidays. Tasks are performed in all areas inside and outside of the store and can include exposure to extreme temperatures (both hot and cold), vapors/fumes, noise and household chemicals for routinely short periods and occasionally longer periods.</li>
<li>
Available to work at any location.</li>
<li>
Valid state motor vehicle license.</li>
<li>
Background Check.</li>
<li>
Vehicle and proof of vehicle insurance</li>
<li>
Home phone/cell phone.</li>
</ul>
