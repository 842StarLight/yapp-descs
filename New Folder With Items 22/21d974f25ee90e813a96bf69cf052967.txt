<p><b>DESCRIPTION:</b></p>
<p>Role Details:</p>
<p>The Account Manager will be responsible for supporting the sales team in selling, planning, executing and monitoring the digital advertising programs of CBS Interactiveâ€™s customer base. This individual will work with a designated sales team to ensure flawless campaign planning and execution as well as provide optimal client service support for assigned advertisers and agencies. The Account Manager will serve as the primary point of contact for client inquiries and requests pertaining to their digital programs, which includes: preparing proposals and client presentations, providing clients with pricing and inventory availability, and communicating regular campaign performance.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Your Day-to-Day:</p>
<ul>
<li>Partner with sales to build client relationships</li>
<li>Be main point of contact for all post sale client requests</li>
<li>Work with marketing, pricing and sales to create thoughtful media plans and proposals</li>
<li>Manage collection of creative assets, tracking and ad verification from clients / agency contacts and coordinate with Ops</li>
<li>Responsible for order creation and management</li>
<li>Process Insertion Orders and ensure they are compliant</li>
<li>Project Manage and coordinate with internal teams all necessary elements for campaign execution</li>
<li>Work with Ops &amp; Campaign Strategists to monitor and optimize campaigns</li>
<li>Work with internal teams on billing reconciliation</li>
<li>Help Sales upsell and renew business</li>
</ul>

<li>Exceptional communication and organizational skills.</li>
<li>Ability to manage stress well, and juggle multiple account challenges at once.</li>
<li>BS/BA degree, or equivalent training and experience.</li>
