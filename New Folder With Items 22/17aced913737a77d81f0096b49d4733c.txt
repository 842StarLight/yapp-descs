<div><b>Still interested? Let’s talk about the role</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>
<div>The Sr. Analyst/Manager, Digital Marketing Analytics will be a key contributor on the analytics team that will derive actionable business insights through data to help the organization gain efficiencies in online marketing channel analysis.</div>
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Here’s what you’ll be doing:</b></div>
<div></div>
<div><ul>
<li>Develop and ensure correct implementation of measurement plans for all media campaigns and create controlled tests to ensure data is tracked correctly.</li>
<li>Produce Digital Marketing reporting and insights across media and marketing channels (e.g. Paid Search, Paid Social, Video, email, websites, etc.).</li>
<li>Own the quality of our digital marketing performance data, combining product management and high-level analysis to identify opportunities for improvement and eliminate blind spots.</li>
<li>Ongoing pursuit of deep understanding of the web analytics, tagging, data management and site personalization/optimization tools to identify untapped opportunities and track/understand/take action on new features as they roll out; liaison with internal partners and vendors as needed for strong partnership vision and goals.</li>
<li>Act as a consultative partner to key stakeholders throughout the agency to drive strategic targeting and measurement plans across initiatives.</li>
<li>Collaborate with media and advertising partners where necessary to ensure common understanding and utilization of targeting, analytics and reporting.</li>
<li>Oversee the delivery of client weekly, monthly, ad-hoc performance reports (e.g. digital media dashboards) via Tableau and other like technologies.</li>
</ul></div>
<div><b>We’d love for you to apply if you have…</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>
<ul>
<li>3-5 years’ experience in digital marketing analytics at an agency or on the client side.</li>
<li>Demonstrated expertise in both digital and paid media strategy as well as digital marketing analytics.</li>
<li>Experience with digital analytics tools and technologies such as Google Analytics, Google Tag Manager, Tableau, R, Python, Alteryx</li>
<li>Strong understanding of data, outcomes and analytical way of driving results.</li>
<li>Must be able to direct and guide team. Build the team to be leading with data rather than a support team.</li>
<li>Knowledge of data sources, data flows – operational, digital and customer data, familiarity with experimentation and visualization tools.</li>
<li>Excellent communication and presentation skills</li>
</ul>
