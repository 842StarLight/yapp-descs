<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Duties</b></h2>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Summary</b></h3>
<div>
<p>The foundation for success of the Defense Commissary Agency mission is our people. It is DeCA's goal to have a workforce that is comprised of our nation's diverse cultures and backgrounds. DeCA operates a worldwide commissary system that provides quality grocery products to active duty military personnel and retirees, members of the Reserve and National Guard, and their families, as well as current DeCA employees. The agency employs over 15,000 people and its annual sales exceed $4 billion.</p>
<br>
</div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities</b></h3>
<p></p>
<div><ul>
<li>Maintains the Computer Assisted Ordering (CAO) system with current, accurate data to ensure optimal order quantities are calculated necessary to sustain customer demands.</li>
<li>Uses radio-frequency hand-held terminals to gather data necessary to update and adjust inventory</li>
<li>Records and updates Balance on Hand (BOH) data; maintains perpetual inventory and audits credits</li>
<li>Performs daily inventory audit of items not-in-stock and overwrite items</li>
<li>Evaluates stock levels of all CAO categories daily by physically reviewing the storage and display shelf locations, shippers and warehouse.</li>
<li>Interfaces with all levels of management and deals directly with commercial distributor/vendor stocking representatives on issues affecting CAO</li>
</ul></div>
