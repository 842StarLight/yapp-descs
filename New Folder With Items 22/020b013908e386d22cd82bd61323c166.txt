<h2>Business Development Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Elmington's commitment to our clients is to manage properties with a <i>Think Like an Owner</i> TLO mentality. With this in mind, the goal for each member of the Business Development team is to cultivate partnerships that are mutually beneficial for Elmington and our clients and to communicate and represent Elmington's core values. Your primary role will be to proactively engage with owners, brokers, and other sources of new management opportunities in order to support our overall growth objectives. Once a new relationship is secured, you will act as the primary client contact while maintaining a strong partnership with the operations team to ensure the properties' success.</p>
<p>
An effective Business Development Executive at Elmington is a self-motivated, passionate person. Because you're tasked with representing Elmington in every interaction you will have, expectations are high. At Elmington, you will be pushed to Forget The Ordinary &amp; Think Like an Owner. Every day.</p>
<p><b>
To be an effective Business Development Executive at Elmington, you will need to:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Have multifamily management experience at a regional level or higher, or the equivalent</li>
<li>
Have strong presentation skills</li>
<li>
Have a tireless work ethic</li>
<li>
Think BEYOND the box</li>
<li>
Be confident and smart</li>
<li>
Be self-sufficient and self-motivated</li>
<li>
Think creatively</li>
<li>
NOT accept the status quo</li>
<li>
Work with passion</li>
<li>
Take pride in your work</li>
<li>
Be a good listener</li>
<li>
Offer a solution with every problem</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Your Key Job Responsibilities will be to:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Source new business via client relations and broker partnerships</li>
<li>
Maintain positive relationships with existing and potential clients</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
Attend conferences and market update meetings to network and stay on top of trends</li>
<li>
Track progress and report results on a regular basis by performing market studies, tracking new construction trends, and understanding the supply, demand, and potential in our concentrated markets</li>
<li>
Have a clear understanding of complex financial statements and budget statements</li>
<li>
Positively represent Elmington with owners, brokers, vendors, and the community at large</li>
<li>
Effectively work remotely and independently</li>
<li>
Constantly learn and search for ways to improve your business</li>
<li>
Think creatively and go way beyond the box by conceptualizing new ideas to improve the business</li>
<li>
Be an effective listener who offers helpful solutions</li>
<li>
Possess great energy, enjoy challenges, and constantly set and reach new goals</li>
<li>
Oversee the successful execution of Elmington's growth initiatives</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Essential Functions of Your Role Include, but are not limited to:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Professional verbal and written communication</li>
<li>
Presenting a professional image and effectively representing the company</li>
<li>
Operating computers and other technological devices to access email, internet websites for business marketing, and company utilized software</li>
<li>
Preparing, locating and compiling information and documents</li>
<li>
Driving a motor vehicle and frequently traveling</li>
<li>
Purchasing items for business needs and submitting for reimbursement</li>
<li>
Following and enforcing all company policies and procedures</li>
</ul>
