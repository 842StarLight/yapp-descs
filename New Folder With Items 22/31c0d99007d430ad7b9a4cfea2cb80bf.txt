<h2>Inside Sales Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Account Manager / Inside Sales Representative</b></p>
<ul><li><i>Mediaworks Advertising Solutions (www.mediaworksnow.com) provides local and regional businesses with advertising opportunities located within the top supermarket chains nationwide. We are currently expanding and are now located in over 20 states nationwide. There's never been a better time to join the Mediaworks team!</i></li></ul>
<p><b>Position Summary: </b></p>
<ul><li>The Inside Sales Representative (ISR) will be responsible for cultivating new business relationships as well as managing renewal business to continually grow their sales pipeline. The primary focus of the ISR will be to prospect and consult business owners on how they can use the company’s products to develop new business. The ISR is required to meet sales objectives by qualifying customer needs, and selling advertising products/services that satisfy clients' needs. This is an Inside Sales position working from our Eagan, MN office.</li></ul>
<p><b>Technical Skills and Experiences</b> <b>: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Consultative professional with a successful track record in selling value over price in a high volume inside sales environment dealing with an intangible product.</li>
<li>Ability to assess multiple priorities and develop a plan to focus on the highest potential opportunities.</li>
<li>Demonstrates ability to learn and apply new technologies, specifically to utilize CRM effectively - we use SalesForce.</li>
<li>Takes a disciplined approach to sales, utilizing a multi-call, multi-step sales process.</li>
<li>Ability to apply business experience to help clients utilize pre-determined marketing platforms.</li>
<li>Minimum two years’ sales experience (Advertising Sales Strongly Recommended)</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in business, marketing or related.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Interpersonal and Soft Skills</b> <b>: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Excellent listener with strong presentation skills.</li>
<li>Not afraid of the phones! Must have strong phone presence!</li>
<li>Must be comfortable calling on business owners and asking them to buy our products!</li>
<li>Keeps well informed on industry changes, participates and continually works to improve sales techniques and sales knowledge.</li>
<li>Individual with exceptional integrity and the highest ethical values.</li>
<li>Proactive, energetic, focused professional with a results and success-driven mindset.</li>
<li>Genuine client-first service mentality.</li>
<li>Team player and excellent communicator committed to collaboration, practice development and execution.</li>
<li>Entrepreneurial, innovative mindset with a complementary sense of ownership.</li>
</ul>
