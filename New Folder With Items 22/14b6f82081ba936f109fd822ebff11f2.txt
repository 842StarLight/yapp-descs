At Concord we believe that agreements are the foundation of everything - progress, possibilities and potential. That is why our mission is in our name: to empower people to agree more. Concord was built to eliminate the countless frictions that stand in the way of winning agreements. Those frictions not only cost time and money, they are dividing us and halting opportunities for everyone.
<br>-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
<br><br>
With 95% of the world's companies still managing their agreements through an antiqued manual process, we are disrupting this final frontier and pioneering a new way forward. Our B2B SaaS contract management solution is already empowering over 400 organizations. These 400+ customers are driving agreements across 220,000 companies worldwide across a variety of industries. The breadth of company and industries speaks to the holistic nature of the platform - and that it really is geared to meet the needs of any organization - and equally importantly - any teams within a company.
<br>-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
<br><br>
Our Sales Team is passionate about product and lives and breathes it every day. Customer growth and managing accounts are both core functions of the team and the environment is fast paced and collaborative. Above all, the whole team operates as stakeholders in the business and treats our clients as partners. As the Sales Enablement Lead, you will be responsible for ensuring that the Sales organization has the knowledge and skills necessary to enable our team to effectively position and sell Concord. The ideal candidate is a high energy with experience in SaaS sales that has designed, developed, and delivered Sales training.
