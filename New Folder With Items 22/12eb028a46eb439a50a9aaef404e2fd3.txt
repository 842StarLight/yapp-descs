<h2>Solution Engineer - San Francisco</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<div>Scout Solution Engineers are trusted advisors to our customers and prospects when evaluating Scout platform. We work closely with our customer stakeholders, Scout sales teams, and Scout product development to provide guidance, insights, and rigor when considering our solutions. We are a small but very focused team that views itself as critical to growing Scout’s customer base and market share. <b>We’re looking for insightful, hands-on, and inquisitive people who can rapidly translate Scout’s innovative platform into effective solutions for procurement leaders.</b>
</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
We are a rapidly growing team and there is plenty of room to develop. We value individuality and we work with every member of our team to enable them to progress towards their career goals. As a Scout Solution Engineer, you will have our commitment to work with you directly to develop your skills, advance your career, and make an impact along the way. Scout’s customers – brands like Salesforce, Airbnb, Levi's, Zappos, Uber, and Starbucks - will challenge you daily with fascinating technical and business problems. As you mature in this role, you’ll have the chance to work with global leaders, learn from industry mavericks, and share your perspectives on a grand stage, and we will support you every step of the way.</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What you will accomplish:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Work closely with our sales teams, product stakeholders and executive leadership to assess, develop, and accelerate key opportunities</li>
<li>
Customize our solution packages and configure demos to effectively showcase the benefits and the value of our solutions</li>
<li>
Lead and manage technical POCs and user pilots while continuously improving the process for them</li>
<li>
Conduct technical solution and product training both to seasoned and new account executives</li>
<li>
Act as the liaison between our sales and product marketing teams to tailor and fine-tune our product roadmap and market messaging</li>
<li>
Build business cases and guide decisions regarding Scout product roadmap decisions and new investments</li>
<li>
Support field marketing events as a Scout expert and trusted presenter</li>
<li>
Will require some travel to engage with customers directly</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>

<div><b>About Scout</b></div>
<div>
Scout is the sourcing and supplier engagement platform trusted by procurement teams to streamline their process, manage a unified pipeline of projects, and collaborate with stakeholders and suppliers. Scout’s powerful platform drives profitability and makes enterprise commerce faster, less risky, and more transparent, giving CPOs the tools they need to transform their organizations.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Over 200 global brands trust Scout to provide greater visibility across stakeholders and suppliers, centralize core processes, measure effectiveness, and make procurement teams crazy successful by delivering better business outcomes. Our simple, effective interface enables companies to streamline supplier selection, manage vendors, centralize data, and make more informed purchasing decisions, faster.</div>
