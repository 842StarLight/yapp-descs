<h2>Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>Corcoran Sunshine Marketing Group is seeking a <b>Marketing Manager</b> for our New York headquarters. The Marketing Manager reports directly to the Chief Marketing Officer. The Marketing Manager will work with managing directors, marketing team members and sales teams in order to effectively service the client in areas of pre-development planning, branding, marketing, digital, print and social media and event-based promotions to bring new residential developments to market and throughout sales cycles.<br>
<br>
Responsibilities include, but are not limited to:<ul>
<li>
Develop and maintain marketing initiatives under the direction of the Managing Director
</li>
<li>Develop marketing plans, programs, timelines and budgets</li>
<li>
Track the performance of all marketing initiatives and provide data and analysis-based recommendations to the client and to the internal CS team.</li>
<li>
Obtain sign-offs and approvals for all advertising and marketing expenditures and maintain the marketing budget. Present marketing plans and initiatives to the developer and make recommendations based on project management material on hand.</li>
<li>
Interact and work directly with the developers, architects, creative agencies, public relations firms, media buyers, social media consultants and all other ancillary professionals brought on throughout the process</li>
<li>
Make suggestions and follow up for advertising in print publications, online channels, and cross marketing/branding partnership opportunities. Make design and copy recommendations and create daily project management related communications.
</li>
<li>Consult with development team on the sales office layouts and displays, property materials including brochures, floor plans, renderings, bags, websites and signage, assist as needed with the set up of a model apartment(s).
</li>
<li>Work with managing director and sales teams to define and adjust sales goals and initiatives throughout the development process.</li>
<li>
Create and implement broker outreach strategies and events.</li>
<li>
Make informed recommendations to enhance the value of each property.</li>
<li>
Liaise with internal team members in planning &amp; design and market research to research and present design options for amenity spaces, interior design and technology.</li>
</ul>
<br>
Qualifications:<ul>
<li>
5+ Years of marketing and/or project management experience</li>
<li>
Bachelor’s Degree</li>
<li>
Background in luxury marketing, media, architecture, design, fine art or real estate preferred</li>
<li>
Real Estate Salesperson’s license required (can be obtained during first year with the company)</li>
<li>
Excellent oral and written communication skills
</li>
<li>Ability to manage multiple tasks with shifting priorities and to interact effectively with diverse personalities in a fast-paced team oriented environment</li>
<li>
Must be extremely detail oriented, organized and self-motivated</li>
<li>
Proficiency with Microsoft applications, particularly Word, Excel, Power Point &amp; Outlook required. Photoshop, Illustrator and InDesign experience preferred.</li>
</ul>
