Job Overview
<br><br>
To sustain our explosive growth, we are looking for drivers—people who thrive on making an impact and live for the next big challenge. We seek to employ the brightest and most forward-thinking talent on the planet, we want to hire people who can lead by example and have a passion for customer success.
<br><br>
The Customer Success Manager is a highly strategic role that is responsible for engaging with our customers and acting as a trusted advisor to a portfolio of customers. In this role you will tasked with building strong partnerships, driving adoption of our products/solutions and ensuring our customers realize value from their investment. The successful candidate, will embody a high degree of professionalism, polish and business acumen to interact with our customers – from CFOs and Controllers to AP Managers.
<br><br>
In this role, you will also become part of the talented and ambitious Customer Success Manager (CSM) team dedicated to engaging, retaining, and enabling AvidXchange’s customers to achieve their desired business outcomes. You’ll develop a close and trusted relationship – through quarterly business reviews, assisting with escalations, conducting special projects and other customer touch points. You’ll cultivate an understanding of your customers’ industry and AP business processes, which may include a vertical focus such as Real Estate / Property Management and HOA. You’ll define and apply operational metrics to measure customer effectiveness and make recommendations for increasing the value that customers derive.
<br><br>
Importantly, you will be the single point of contact for the customer in a post-sales production environment, proactively engaging resources across Avid to resolve escalations, respond to requests and ensure our customers get the very best of AvidXchange.
<br><br>
Job Responsibilities
<br><br>
<b>Job Duties:</b><br>
Manage relationships with AvidXchange’s customers [50%, Primary Responsibility]
<br><ul>
<li>Build and maintain lasting relationships with assigned customers</li>
<li>Conduct recurring business reviews and health checks with assigned customers, obtaining candid feedback on our ability to help them achieve their desired outcomes with AvidXchange</li>
<li>Identify trends in cross-customer feedback from recurring business reviews and health checks</li>
<li>Conduct product and process trainings for assigned customers on an as needed basis</li>
<li>Monitor and relentlessly manage customer adoption, retention, and annual recurring revenue</li>
<li>Scope expansion opportunities with assigned customers and pass those opportunities to AvidXchange’s Strategic Sales team</li>
</ul>
Prevent and manage customer escalations [30%]
<br><ul>
<li>Leverage predictive analytics to proactively identify symptoms of unhealthy customers</li>
<li>Work cross-functionally to intervene with customers that display unhealthy characteristics</li>
<li>Work cross-functionally to drive resolution to customer escalations</li>
<li>Developing success plans for customers that outline their critical success factors, metrics for success, potential issues, and provide recommendations</li>
</ul>
Drive Customer Success Initiatives [20%]
<br><ul>
<li>Define customer success plans, targets and objectives; ensure that the appropriate level of resources and capabilities exist to achieve the goals</li>
<li>Influence future lifetime value through higher retention, adoption, customer satisfaction and overall health scores</li>
<li>Be the voice of the customer - synthesize and facilitate communicating cross-customer trends in feedback, escalations, and enhancement requests to AvidXchange’s leadership</li>
<li>Define and deploy playbooks and structure into customer success operations</li>
</ul>
