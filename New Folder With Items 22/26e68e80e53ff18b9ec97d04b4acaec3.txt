<h2>Shipping/Receiving/Inventory Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>American Lumber is looking for an experienced Shipping/Receiving/Inventory Manager</p>
<p>The idea candidate will possess the following:</p>
<ul><li>Strong Organizational Skills</li></ul>
<p>Ability to work in a non-climate controlled environment</p>
<ul><li>Excellent Communication Skills, both written and verbal</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to Professionally Manage Others</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong Computer Skills and Inventory Control Software</li></ul>
<ul><li>Excellent Time Management Skills</li></ul>
<p>Duties Include:</p>
<ul><li>Ensure all inbound and outbound freight paperwork matches BOL, Sales Order or PO</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ensure all Inbound and Outbound Freight is properly received and/or loaded in a timely and efficient manner</li></ul>
<p>Management and Inventory of lumber yard</p>
<ul><li>Manage Shipping Clerk and Forklift Operators</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manage preventative maintenance program for forklifts, ensuring all is performed in a timely manner</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ensure dust system is running properly</li></ul>
<ul><li>Create reports on inbound and outbound freight</li></ul>
<ul><li>Work closely with Production Plant, Transportation, Sales, Accounting and Costing</li></ul>
<ul><li>Other duties as assigned</li></ul>
