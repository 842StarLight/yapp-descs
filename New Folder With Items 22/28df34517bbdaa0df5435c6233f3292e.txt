<h2>Project Manager - Southern CA region</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div><p>The Project Manager is responsible for the safe completion of the project within budget, on schedule, to the Company’s quality standards and to the customer’s satisfaction. It will be the responsibility of this individual to initiate required action to achieve the project objectives and to ensure all project activities are consistent with contract documents and the Company’s policies.</p></div>
<br>PositionRequirements
<br><div>
<p><b>Essential job functions include:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Subcontractor management
</li>
<li>Pre-job planning and mobilization
</li>
<li>Subcontractor purchasing
</li>
<li>Scheduling
</li>
<li>Customer/Owner relations
</li>
<li>Quality control and support of our safety plan
</li>
<li>Submittal reviews
</li>
<li>Progress payments from owners
</li>
<li>Collaborating with team members
</li>
<li>Estimating
</li>
<li>Cost reports
</li>
<li>Project closeout
</li>
<li>Personnel evaluation and development
</li>
</ul>
<p>The position may require the new hire to be assigned to a project with specific badging or other security requirements, which may require the Company, Owner, or third party to conduct a criminal or other background check.
<br>
</p>
<p><b>Preferred qualifications:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Strong communication skills
</li>
<li>Computer software - Microsoft Office Suite, specifically Excel based templates, Prolog, Primavera and AutoDesk Suite
</li>
<li>Minimum of 5-10 years’ experience on large commercial construction projects</li>
</ul>
</div>
