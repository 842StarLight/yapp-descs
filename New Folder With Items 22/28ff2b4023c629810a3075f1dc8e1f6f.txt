<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Details
</b></h2>
<p>The person hired in this role will be responsible for executing centralized operations for the Customer Success Groupâ€™s (CSG) Data Governance Office effectively to drive efficient decision making and be the cornerstone for knowledge and updates impacting our domains. In this role, you are part analyst, engineer, and a trusted advisor to business teams and their leaders.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>You will be expected to learn and understand our data governance offering, identify opportunity for improvements and cater to data quality needs. You will be responsible for devising and executing a vision that aligns with business stakeholders' need for managing metadata and business quality rules.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Could be hired as a Senior Analyst or Manager
</p>
<p></p>
<p>In this role, you will...
</p>
<ul>
<li>Operate, define and implement cross-functional data quality improvement initiatives that support our business team's top priorities
</li>
<li>Run the Data Governance Councils effectively to drive efficient decision-making and be the cornerstone for knowledge and updates impacting our domains. Share Data Governance methodology, best practices, and accomplishments
</li>
<li>Refine and mature our data governance and data management practices and tools to enable future scale and improve engagement
</li>
<li>Collaborate with cross-functional business partners and IT to implement and enable our initiatives
</li>
<li>Improve our data governance maturity to streamline operations, improve effectiveness, and democratize our assets and information
</li>
<li>Provide stakeholders with Subject Matter Expertise related to data governance offering and capabilities
</li>
<li>Monitor and regularly audit data quality for CSG data governance councils
</li>
<li>Where appropriate and required, liaise with the project teams to ensure data standard compliance, acting as the escalation point cross business teams like CSG Success, Support, Red Accounts etc
</li>
<li>Proactively mitigate and manage critical escalations and at-risk engagements or related metric; communicate effectively and appropriately with internal leaders and executives
</li>
<li>Identify data governance process improvements while documenting proposed changes and user stories
</li>
<li>Simplify complex problems and find solutions collaboratively
</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
<b>Experience &amp; Qualifications
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>5+ years experience in a combination of data analysis, risk management, data analytics or an equivalent role
</li>
<li>Bachelors or Masters in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, or related fields
</li>
<li>Expert in SQL, and data mining tools. Experience in Python and other BI tools is a plus
</li>
<li>Ability to translate data and map to data quality dimensions for generating business insights
</li>
<li>Experience working with BI/Visualization tools like Tableau, Qlik, Domo, Einstein Analytics, etc
</li>
<li>Show apptitude and interest to learning the business and contributing to the business outcomes using data
</li>
<li>Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
</li>
<li>Functional knowledge balanced with a strong technical skillset and aptitude
</li>
<li>Excellent communicator - both written and verbal, with the ability to work with both executives as well as technologists
</li>
<li>Innovative mindset with an eagerness to learn
</li>
<li>Salesforce product knowledge is a big plus
</li>
</ul>
