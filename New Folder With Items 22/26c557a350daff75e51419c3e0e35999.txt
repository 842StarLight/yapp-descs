<h2>Violations Generalist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>General Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Donlen, a Hertz company, is one of the largest fleet management services companies in the US with headquarters in Bannockburn, IL. Every year Donlen processes title and registration transactions on thousands of vehicles across North America. There is an opening in the License and Title department to work with our title and registration team. The title and registration generalist is responsible for providing administrative support to the team. The title and registration generalist will be proficient in a variety of tasks including data entry, auditing registration data, filing, organization, and mailing.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Monitor and take action on assigned workload</li>
<li>
Mail completed registrations and other title and registration documents</li>
<li>
Audit completed title and registration documents for accuracy</li>
<li>
Enter registration data into FleetWeb</li>
<li>
File titles and other title and registration documents</li>
<li>
Retrieve titles and prepare for sale</li>
<li>
Sort and distribute mail</li>
<li>
Scan and index title and registration documents</li>
<li>
Escalate and assist in resolving issues and delays</li>
<li>
Identify, discuss, and implement process improvements</li>
<li>
Manage and update requirement reference materials</li>
<li>
Manage CARE</li>
<li>
Work with entire licensing team to cross train and cover volume and staff fluctuations</li>
<li>
Partner with Customer Service to ensure a quality customer experience</li>
<li>
Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>LI-KR1</li></ul>
