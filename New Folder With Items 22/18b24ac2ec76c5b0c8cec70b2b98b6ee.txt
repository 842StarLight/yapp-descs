<h2>Office Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Description/Job Summary</b></p>
The Customer Service Manager is responsible for all administrative and customer service related duties within a region including office organization, invoicing, sales support, product deliver, and general customer service.<p><b>
Essential Functions:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Hauler maintenance</li>
<li>
Reconcile POs</li>
<li>
Monitor quote schedule</li>
<li>
Daily and monthly number reporting</li>
<li>
Competitor purchase report follow-up</li>
<li>
Complaint, back-charge, short pay resolution</li>
<li>
Manage plant delivery schedules</li>
<li>
Manage all invoicing duties</li>
<li>
Develop and promote CSRs</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Required Skills</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Computer Skills: Word Processing Software, Payroll System, Design Software, Manufacturing Software, Spreadsheet Software, Order Processing System, Internet Software, Project Management Software, Human Resource System, Database Software</li>
<li>
Ability to read, analyze and interpret general business periodicals, professional journals, technical procedures and government regulations; write reports, business correspondence and procedure manuals; effectively present information and respond to questions from groups of managers, clients, customers and the general public.</li>
<li>
Ability to solve practical problems and deal with a variety of concrete variables in situations where only limited standardization exists; interpret a variety of instructions furnished in written, oral, diagram or schedule form.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Required Experience</b></p>
<ul><li><p>
Bachelor's degree from a four-year college or university; or at least 2 years of specific experience and/or training related to the essential functions of the job; or equivalent combination of education and training</p></li></ul>
