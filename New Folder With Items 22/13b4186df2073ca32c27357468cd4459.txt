<h2>Environmental Engineer 5</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div><div><div>
<div>
<div>With general direction, creates water and waste water treatment facilities, water and sewer systems, pipelines, pump stations, remediation sites, solid waste facilities, etc. engineering designs to meet client project requirements.<br>
<br>
<ul><li>Reviews draft designs and reports for compliance</li></ul>
with federal, state and local regulations and signs off<br>
on completed designs.<br>
<ul><li>Ensures that firm policies and practices are followed</li></ul>
on all designs.<br>
<ul><li>Performs site reviews and studies, as needed, to</li></ul>
ensure designs are aligned with location<br>
specifications. Updates design requirements as<br>
necessary.<br>
<ul>
<li>Performs water quality analyses and modeling.</li>
<li>Contributes to firm's TKM by developing white</li>
</ul>
papers and technical design documentation of new<br>
or special case designs, studies, etc.<br>
<ul><li>Submits technical papers and designs for publishing</li></ul>
to technical journals.<br>
<ul><li>Collaborates with sales staff to create proposals in</li></ul>
response to current and potential client requests for<br>
proposal (RFPs).<br>
<ul><li>Reviews draft proposals for adherence to firm,</li></ul>
industry, state, local and federal regulations and<br>
best practices.<br>
<ul><li>Meets with current and potential future clients to</li></ul>
review their current and future design needs.<br>
<ul><li>Attends conferences, symposia, etc. to improve the</li></ul>
visibility of the firm's products and services in<br>
collaboration with applicable sales staff.<br>
<ul><li>Provides technical guidance and training to more</li></ul>
junior staff.<br>
<ul><li>Mentors more junior staff and develops them for</li></ul>
future growth within the discipline and firm.<br>
<ul><li>May supervise the work of junior engineers on</li></ul>
project work.<br>
<ul><li>Performs other duties as required.</li></ul>
</div>
</div>
<div>
<p><b>Minimum Qualifications</b></p>
<div>Education:<br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree in civil, environmental or chemical engineering or related discipline.</li>
<li>Professional engineering (PE) license.</li>
</ul>
<br>
Experience:<br>
<ul><li>7 years of related experience.</li></ul>
<br>
Travel: Domestic and/or international travel may be required. The frequency of travel is contingent on specific duties, responsibilities, and the essential functions of the position, which may vary depending on workload and project demands.</div>
</div>
<div>
<p><b>Certification/License Requirements</b></p>
<div>Professional Engineer</div>
</div>
<div>
<p><b>Preferred Qualifications</b></p>
<ul><li>Experience working with ArcGIS, AutoCAD and EPANET.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Experience with designing and providing construction oversight of water mains, force mains, gravity sewers and pump stations.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Experience completing engineering designs that include specific erosion and sediment control plans and control practices according to the VA DEQ Standards.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Water main design and analysis that includes compliance with VDH-ODW standards and analysis with water modeling software (such as EPANET) to evaluate water distribution system pressure conditions to meet fire flow requirements.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Construction services experience that includes pump station improvements, piping, penstock modifications, electrical and SCADA control system, emergency power, architectural changes to buildings, HVAC systems, and structural modifications.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Experience preparing bypass design for pipeline improvements projects, including determination of design flow, bypass piping length and diameter, appurtenances, bypass pump model no., and number of crossing ramps, and a report to document bypass operations</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Experience with water and wastewater conveyance system assessment and rehabilitation experience that includes NASSCO PACP/MACP/LACP Certification, design of rehabilitation improvements, and preparing cost estimates for rehabilitation techniques.</li></ul>
</div>
