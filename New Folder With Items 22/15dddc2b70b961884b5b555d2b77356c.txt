<p><b>Canvasser</b></p>
<p>
CTI Contractors is a Premier General Contracting Company. We specialize in the restoration of residential and commercial properties affected by storms and other disasters. We have been serving clients for over 13 years, and have a growing presence in Arizona, Idaho, New York, South Dakota, Nebraska and Utah.</p>
<p>
Our company is built on the Core Values of Confidence, Trust &amp; Integrity.</p>
<p>
We are looking for an experienced Door to Door Canvasser to join our growing team in Salt Lake City, UT, Sioux Falls, SD &amp; Omaha, Nebraska</p>

JOB OVERVIEW AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>

Generates new leads through Door to Door Direct Marketing activities.</p>
<p>
Follows up on referrals and sets appointments for the Field Sales Representatives.</p>
