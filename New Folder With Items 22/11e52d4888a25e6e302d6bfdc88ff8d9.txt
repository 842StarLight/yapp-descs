<h2>Design Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Safran </b>is an international high-technology group, operating in the aircraft propulsion and equipment, space and defense markets. Safran has a global presence, with more than 58,000 employees and sales of 16.5 billion euros in 2017. Working alone or in partnership, Safran holds world or European leadership positions in its core markets. Safran is listed on the Euronext Paris stock exchange, and is part of the CAC 40 and Euro Stoxx 50 indices.
</p>
<p>In February 2018, Safran took control of Zodiac Aerospace, significantly expanding its aircraft equipment activities. Together with Zodiac Aerospace, Safran has more than 91,000 employees and would have around EUR 21 billion in adjusted revenue (pro forma 2016).
</p>
<p><b>Safran </b>is ranked among the Top 100 Global Innovators by Thomson Reuters and is featured on the "Happy at work" rankings. The Group places fourth on the Universum ranking for the favorite companies of newly-qualified engineers in France.
</p>
<p><b>Safran Aerosystems </b>is a world leader in aircraft electrical distribution, inflatable safety and water and waste management systems. It is also a major supplier of oxygen systems, telemetry and communication solutions, in-flight entertainment systems or fuel systems.
</p>
<div>
<p>Responsible for the creation of new AVOX product designs and design improvements of existing products. Ensure new product designs meet the established design requirements.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Duties &amp; Responsibilities:
</p>
<p>Duties and responsibilities include, but are not limited to:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Evaluate, select and define material for all parts in the product design.
</li>
<li>Define geometry of components while ensuring that design for manufacturability goals are satisfied.
</li>
<li>Perform tolerance stack – up analysis utilizing tolerance distribution that is consistent with manufacturing capabilities.
</li>
<li>Perform oxygen hazard analysis to ensure designs are inherently safe.
</li>
<li>Check detail drawings to ensure thoroughness and that design intent is captured and properly communicated.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Qualifications: Education
</p>
<p>Required – BS Mechanical, Electrical or Aerospace Engineering Preferred – MS in Mechanical Engineering<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Experience
</p>
<p>Required – 3 to 5 years design engineering in an aerospace environment<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Special Knowledge/Skills –
</p>
<p>Required – CAD design; MS Office suite of software<br>
</p>
