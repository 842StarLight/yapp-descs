<p>
Aravo Solutions delivers market-leading, cloud-based solutions for managing third-party governance, risk, compliance and performance. We help companies protect their business value and reputation by managing the risks associated with third parties and suppliers, and to build business value by ensuring that their third-party relationships are optimized.</p>
<p>
Since 2000, leading global brands across a diverse range of industries have counted on Aravo for their end-to-end enterprise supplier and third-party risk management. Brands including GE, Unilever, Procter and Gamble, Google, Salesforce and many more. Aravo has also distilled this experience and best-in-class technology into rapid time-to-value applications that help companies manage a wide range of programs including: anti-bribery anti-corruption, responsible sourcing, data privacy, information security, GDPR, and financial services regulatory compliance.</p>
<p>
Aravo supports a user base of 200,000 corporate users, managing more than 5 million third party users in 36 languages and 154 countries.</p>
<p>
Aravo was recognized as a leader by independent analyst research firm Forrester Research Inc., in The Forrester Wave™: Supplier Risk and Performance Management Platforms, Q1 2018. Aravo is the top-ranked vendor in the current offering category.</p>
<p>
Aravo has also been recognized with GRC 20/20’s Value Award for Third Party Management for providing measurable value in GRC efficiency, effectiveness and agility, the GRC 20/20 Innovation Award for Aravo for GDPR. Aravo was named as a Category Leader with the highest “Completeness of Offering” of any provider in the Chartis RiskTech Quadrant® for Third Party Risk Management Solutions, 2017, and was named a Challenger in the 2017 Gartner® Magic Quadrant for IT Vendor Risk Management.</p>
<p>
Aravo’s employees are inspired by contributing to a greater purpose through providing solutions that help eradicate corruption and social injustice from the world’s supply chains and third-party networks.</p>
<p><b>
Aravo is headquartered in San Francisco, with offices and partners across the US, Europe and Asia.</b></p>
<p><b>
The Role:</b><br>
</p>
<p>
Aravo is growing fast and recognizes the importance of building a world-class team to fuel our continued success. With significant investment and strong leadership to expand our business, it’s a tremendous time to be joining the Aravo team. In this role you’ll have the opportunity to:</p>
<ul>
<li>Support our clients who are building and managing world-class third party risk management programs by maximizing the value of their investment in the Aravo platform.</li>
<li>Provide a valued voice, where ideas and creative approaches are welcomed.</li>
<li>Feel a sense of accomplishment as you are empowered to deliver in a dynamic, fun and fast-paced environment.</li>
<li>Celebrate successes and grow with us!</li>
</ul>
<p>The Program Director is a senior level position on the professional services team for individuals who have demonstrated a strong combination of leadership, business acumen, and project management skills in support of successful project delivery with a particular emphasis on establishing executive level trust and credibility with customers. The Program Director oversees the coordination and administration of all aspects of projects including planning, organizing, staffing, leading, and controlling project activities. Direct management responsibility of project managers, senior consultants, and configuration specialists.</p>
<p><b>
Leadership:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage 3 to 4 implementation teams; consisting of Project Managers, Senior Consultants, and Configuration Specialists.
</li>
<li>Monitor scope, schedule and budget of large, complex and/or multi-phase software deliveries to ensure implementation teams are tracking on time/budget for project deliveries.
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Establish and foster governance structure within project implementation lifecycle.
</li>
<li>Model attributes of a leader when interacting with all internal team members and clients.
</li>
<li>Build relationships within client base at the project sponsor level.
</li>
<li>Develop new initiatives to support the strategic direction of the organization.
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Salesmanship:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Act as an integral part of the pre-sales team during the closing phase of new client acquisition.
</li>
<li>Accurately estimate work effort on large projects.
</li>
<li>Create Services Statements of Work (SOW) and coordinate with Director of Services, Legal, and Finance for sign-off on new project work.
</li>
<li>Communicate w/client stakeholders to gain support for Aravo and project (current/future phases).
</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Review/Create opportunities for extension/expansion.
</li></ul>
<p><b>Project Management:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Identify and evaluate the risks associated with implementation activities and take appropriate action to control/mitigate the risks.
</li>
<li>Provide immediate visibility into project risks and escalate internally and with customer’s executive sponsorship as appropriate.
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Coordinate with other Program Directors to ensure effective and efficient project delivery.
</li>
<li>Work within cross-functional teams to solve complex problems outside of one’s core expertise.
</li>
<li>Improve upon and/or create new project templates as appropriate throughout implementation life cycle.
</li>
<li>Proactively share insight with other departments gained from project experience.
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Administrative:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Supervise implementation teams by providing direction, input and feedback.
</li>
<li>Set, communicate, and evaluate quarterly business objectives with your direct reports.
</li>
<li>Supervise, mentor and provide performance feedback to internal and contract resources.
</li>
<li>Establish and coordinate resource allocation across the team and communicate/anticipate external needs with other Program Directors for projects with larger scope.
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Administrative activity related to project initiation and supervising time tracking and billing.
</li>
<li>Complete monthly financial status updates and billing.
</li>
</ul>
