<h2>Business Development Representative - Ft. Lauderdale/Miami, FL</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Job Description:</b><br>
<p>This position will be responsible for bringing profitable sales leads to the company. This position will create and build client relationships across the industry. This will predominantly be a prospecting and business development role. Performing at the highest level in all aspects of the job.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Lead generation responsibilities</li>
<ul>
<li>
Uncover and develop leads through networking groups, A&amp;D relationships, GC relationships, furniture dealers, affiliate companies, and other industry contacts.</li>
<li>
Monitor lead websites to discover new projects and track existing projects</li>
</ul>
<li>
Engage and update the A&amp;D Community with a project focused mindset</li>
<ul>
<li>
Present product updates/ lunch and learns/ ice cream tastings/ waffle breakfasts/ happy hours/ etc… for A&amp;D firms and GC’s with a focus on engaging these firms regarding current or upcoming projects in our markets</li>
<li>
Research and engage unfamiliar A&amp;D firms to develop a relationship and update them on our offerings / learn about their upcoming projects</li>
<li>
Develop and maintain relationships within the A&amp;D market: become the point of contact and go to person for any storage needs and provide info, specs, drawings as needed</li>
</ul>
<li>
Track projects and monitor pipeline for geographical territory</li>
<ul>
<li>
Track all projects within geographical market and develop a game plan in conjunction with sales reps on how to best pursue each new project (*exception of military/ protected accounts)</li>
<li>
Track projects as they progress and work with sales rep to ensure projects are moving down the pipeline without getting left behind</li>
<li>
Utilizing relationships in the A&amp;D community to assist sales reps in pursuing these projects</li>
</ul>
<li>
Marketing responsibilities</li>
<ul>
<li>
Engage with the marketing team to provide relevant content and ideas in an effort to better our presence with the A&amp;D community</li>
<li>
Provide digital assets and support for the sales team</li>
<li>
Update the marketing team on projects in your territory with case study potential</li>
<li>
Engage the A&amp;D community through social media platforms and assist the marketing team in content development</li>
</ul>
<li>
Investigate new markets within territories</li>
<ul>
<li>
Investigate untapped markets within your geographical territory in search of potential new projects</li>
<li>
Analyze changing trends in the market and innovative solutions to better position Patterson Pope</li>
</ul>
<li>
Assist sales reps in further pursuing and updating existing major accounts</li>
<ul>
<li>
Colleges and universities (athletics, archives, librarians, design department, planning department, etc…)</li>
<li>
Major corporations and company headquarters (facility managers, design departments, purchasing departments, etc…)</li>
<li>
City/ county managers and planning departments</li>
</ul>
<li>
Networking responsibilities</li>
<ul>
<li>
Investigate and partake in local industry networking groups</li>
<li>
Update sales reps on upcoming events and networking opportunities</li>
</ul>
</ul>
