<b>Belimed</b> is one of the leading global suppliers of innovative cleaning, disinfection and sterilization solutions for Medical and Life Science. <br>
</div>
<p><b>Customer Service Parts Specialist</b><br>
</p>
<div>
<b>Your tasks</b><b>
</b>
</div>
<div>Belimed is seeking a business-oriented <b>Customer Service Parts Specialist</b> to support our growing service operations team. This position will support customers with part identification, part order processing, and full repair part planning. The Customer Service Parts Specialist will play a key role in our ability to be an effective partner to our customers by providing top notch part planning.<br>
<br>
</div>
<div>
<b>How you will impact Belimed and our clients</b><b>
</b>
</div>
<div>
<ul>
<li>Assists internal and external customers with part orders</li>
<li>Manages customer expectations &amp; commitments in a professional manner</li>
<li>Responsible for providing timely and accurate part identification and delivery times</li>
<li>Reduces customer downtime by identifying and proactively suggesting additional parts that may be required for a project</li>
<li>Assists field personnel with inventory control</li>
<li>Communicates part issues quickly to Technical Support Specialist or management for resolution</li>
<li>Supports the Field Operations group by providing “backup” dispatch capabilities</li>
<li>Communicates to management any issues, concerns, or customer satisfaction problems</li>
<li>Keeps current on part documentation and regularly studies part diagrams.</li>
<li>Utilize service systems to research part application</li>
</ul>
<br>
</div>
<div>
<b>What you need to succeed</b><b>
</b>
</div>
<div>The ideal candidate will have:<br>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma/GED or equivalent</li>
<li>
1 - 2 years part department experience or part related customer service experience</li>
<li>Experience with ERP software</li>
<li>
Must be creative, innovative, and flexible, with the ability to work in a matrix environment.</li>
<li>Must be able to work on-call as business needs require including occasional nights, weekends and holidays.
</li>
<li>Must have excellent PC skills. Proficient in the use of word processors, spreadsheets, communication software, database applications.</li>
<li>Excellent Customer Communication skills (extensive phone work is required).</li>
<li>Strong analytical/problem-solving skills.</li>
<li>Proven abilities in the areas of:<ul>
<li>
interpersonal relations</li>
<li>
ability to interpret part diagrams</li>
<li>
customer satisfaction skills</li>
<li>
technical understanding of part functions</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>An outstanding candidate may also have:<br>
<ul>
<li>Experience in medical equipment part sales</li>
<li>
Experience with SAP software</li>
</ul>
