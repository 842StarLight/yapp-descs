<p><b>Purchasing Agent: Up to $60,000 </b></p>
<p>Express Employment Professionals is currently hiring for Direct Hire - Purchasing Agents. We are looking for dedicated, driven and goal-oriented people who are looking to join on with a premier manufacturing company in Lincoln. This schedule will be Monday-Friday, 8am-5pm and offers a full benefit package (401K plan, Health/Life/Dental and Paid Time Off).</p>
<p><b>Essential Job Functions: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Evaluate and select suppliers based upon quality, price and delivery to specific prints.</li>
<li>Interpret Instructions that are in either written, oral, diagram or schedule format.</li>
<li>Effectively present information to small groups of people. Customers, Clients or Peers.</li>
<li>Ability to problem solve where limited standardization exists.</li>
<li>Ensure that suppliers are following purchasing procedures manual and supplier quality assurance manual.</li>
<li>Create the purchase order using computerized system to insure accurate and timely payment.</li>
<li>Plan, schedule and coordinate projects</li>
<li>Support the product area and their goals</li>
<li>Gather and analyze data for reporting.</li>
<li>Solicits and analyzes quotations for new or non-standard items.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree with two years of related purchasing or manufacturing experience or equivalent combination of education and experience.</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft applications, email, AS400 and additional programs/software necessary.</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills, ability to multi-task in a fast-paced environment, ability to read blueprints and analyze data.</li>
</ul>
