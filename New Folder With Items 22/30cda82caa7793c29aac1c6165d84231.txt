<h2>Business Development</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Department: </b>Business Development</p>
<p><b>Job Status: </b>Full Time</p>
<p><b>Reports To: </b>Executive Team</p>
<p><b>Company Overview: </b> Mission Design &amp; Automation, LLC (“MDA”) is a Holland, Michigan based, provider of customized automation solutions to companies ranging in size from middle market companies to global concerns. MDA has expertise in conceiving, designing, fabricating, programming and assembling custom automation equipment to exacting quality standards. MDA largely serves customers in the following end markets: automotive, office furniture, medical device, transportation and building products.</p>
<p>Due to growth within the automation industry and Mission, we are seeking to expand our sales team with the right individual to help the company with business development, customer management, and solution concepting and costing.</p>
<p><b><i>Position Responsibilities: </i></b></p>
<ul><li>40%-60% of your day is spent traveling to grow current customer relationships, fostering new relationships, and listening to customer’s problems for potential solutions.</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>Present solution concept and associated quote proposal to customer’s engineering team and/or executive management.</li>
<li>Possess a winning and creative attitude toward finding customer solutions.</li>
<li>Attend trade shows, seminars, and other industry events to meet customers and vendors while learning about current and upcoming trends (and technologies) in the manufacturing and automation industries.</li>
<li>Track business development and quoting activity for presentation to Executive Team * 40%-60% of your day is spent with the Mission team concepting and quoting solutions for customers.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Ability to brainstorm with the Mission team to generate concept solutions for problems with no previous or standard solution.</li>
<li>Developing proposals that have a clearly defined scope, which includes specifying machine buy-off requirements, identifying customer part requirements at project level, and ensuring that the customer specification has been reviewed</li>
<li>Developing a comprehensive quote package that incorporates necessary documentation for internal and external audiences</li>
<li>Attend and present at internal kick-off meetings with project management and Team Leads of the Design, Build, and Electrical departments.</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>Position Requirements: </i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>3+ years of experience in the industrial automation industry selling and/or concepting custom high-end automation equipment.</li>
<li>Previous industry experience in machine design or application engineering role strongly preferred</li>
<li>Previous automation machine building experience a plus</li>
<li>Proven track record of identifying prospective customers and cultivating a relationship that results in an automation partnership.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to continually contact current and prospective customers based on leads from employees, vendors, centers of influence, and other external resources.</li>
<li>Ability during concepting/quoting to think outside the box with the Mission team to solve a problem with no standard solution.</li>
<li>Experience with SolidWorks or other mechanical design software preferred</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Office Suite</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>Why Mission?: </i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Attractive industry with growth expected to outpace others</li>
<li>Competitive pay with benefits package</li>
<li>Creative work environment with a chance to learn from others</li>
<li>Modern, clean facility with a positive workplace culture</li>
</ul>
