<p>Children’s Interiors Design Consultant – Flexa Design District, San Francisco, CA</p>
<p>Unique job opportunity in the heart of San Francisco. Take part in the launch of our <b>second</b> FLEXA store in the US market. We provide an exciting and rewarding work environment for those who enjoy being around children. The store is located in one of the absolute most affluent areas in the US.</p>
<p>A Little More About FLEXA</p>
<p>FLEXA® specializes in Scandinavian interior solutions for kids. Founded in Denmark in 1972, FLEXA has since developed a global footprint with now more than 140 stores located in over 20 countries.</p>
<p>FLEXA has developed complete, contemporary Scandinavian interiors for babies and children. All FLEXA products are designed to create a safe and stimulating environment which adapts, grows and transforms to meet children’s ongoing developmental needs. FLEXA creates the world best functional, playful and sustainable room solutions at affordable prices.</p>
<p>FLEXA knows kids</p>
<p>Kids are at the center of everything FLEXA creates. FLEXA has been in the forefront of furniture design for kids for more than 40 years and this can only be achieved through continuous drive and passion for kids and their development. At FLEXA we are always curious - we love to learn more about kids, their development and how we can provide the best possible environment for playing, studying or sleeping. Throughout the decades we have been creative - our ideas are our best asset, we encourage creativity and applaud innovation.</p>
