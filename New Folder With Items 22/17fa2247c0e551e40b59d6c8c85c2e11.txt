<h2>Sr. Accountant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>JOB PURPOSE</b>:</p>
<p>
Trans Lease Inc. is a subsidiary of Transwest Inc., a diverse holding company with business units involved in heavy, medium and light duty truck and trailer dealership activities, as well as manufacturing. Trans Lease serves customers throughout North America by providing financing of trucks and trailers. The Sr. Accountant will be responsible for the life cycle of financial products, which includes recording and terminating a lease and everything in between. This position will work in A/R, A/P and general maintenance of the portfolio including multi-state sales, use and property taxes and preparation of various reconciliations. The ideal candidate will have 1-3 years of public accounting or industry experience within the financial services or manufacturing markets.</p>
<p>
 We offer competitive wages and a full benefits package including medical, dental, life, disability insurance, 401(K), paid holidays, paid vacation/personal time, and an employee car discount program.</p>
<p>
 <b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES &amp; RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Maintain general ledger accounts by monitoring account transactions and preparation, entering amortizations, balancing costs and preparing monthly entries for fixed asset depreciation, accruals, amortization and cost allocations.</li>
<li>Summarize current company financial status by analyzing general ledger accounts, assisting with compiling and submitting financial statements.</li>
<li>Reconcile financial discrepancies by collecting and analyzing account information.</li>
<li>Assist in closing of month and year end and preparation of reports.</li>
<li>Prepare supporting schedules and documents for fiscal year audit.</li>
<li>Assist with various sales tax or related audits.</li>
<li>Secure revenue by verifying and posting receipts and resolving discrepancies.</li>
<li>Sales tax set up and verification on new leases in all 50 states and Puerto Rico.</li>
<li>Monitor changes in state sales tax laws.</li>
<li>Post customer payments by recording cash, checks and automatic withdrawal transactions.</li>
<li>Post revenues by verifying and entering transactions from local deposits.</li>
<li>Verify validity of account discrepancies by obtaining and investigating information from sales, customer service departments and customers.</li>
<li>Resolve valid or authorized deductions by entering adjusting entries.</li>
<li>Resolve invalid or unauthorized deductions by following pending deductions procedures.</li>
<li>Resolve collections by examining customer payment plans, payment history, credit line, coordinating contact with collections department.</li>
<li>Summarize receivables by maintaining invoice accounts, coordinating monthly transfer to accounts receivable account, verifying totals and preparing report.</li>
<li>Complete payments and controls expenses by receiving, processing, verifying and reconciling invoices.</li>
<li>Reconcile processed work by verifying entries and comparing system reports to balances.</li>
<li>Charge expenses to accounts by analyzing invoice/expense reports and recording entries.</li>
<li>Resolve purchase order, contract, invoice, or payment discrepancies and documentation.</li>
<li>Issue stop-payments or purchase order amendments.</li>
<li>Reimburse employees by receiving and verifying expense reports.</li>
<li>Maintain accounting ledgers by verifying and posting account transactions.</li>
</ul> <p><b>WORK ENVIORNMENT &amp; PHYSICAL ABILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Requires frequent sitting, standing, balancing, bending or stooping for prolonged periods of time.</li>
<li>Requires eye-hand coordination and manual dexterity sufficient to operate a keyboard, photocopier, telephone, calculator and other office equipment.</li>
<li>Ability to communicate by providing verbal feedback in a professional manner.</li>
<li>Normal range of hearing and vision.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Ability to receive and analyze data and input into the computer.</li>
</ul> <p><b>REQUIRED EDUCATION, EXPERIENCE, KNOWLEDGE &amp; SKILLS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or GED equivalent.</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree from an accredited university/college in Accounting.</li>
<li>1-3 years practical experience in finance, leasing or auditing experience.</li>
<li>Proficient with Microsoft Office applications.</li>
<li>Working knowledge of multi-state sales, use and property taxes.</li>
<li>Experience with long term motor vehicle leasing.</li>
<li>Knowledge of Lease Complete Software and Microsoft Dynamics.</li>
</ul>
