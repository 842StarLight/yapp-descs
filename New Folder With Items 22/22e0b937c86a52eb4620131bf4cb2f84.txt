<h2>Chief Marketing Officer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>This is a position for a seasoned Story Brand chief marketing leader and the marketing champion of SVO’s Mission, Vision, and Values. The primary responsibility of the position is to tell the Farmer Focus brand story through experience and execution and/or oversight day-to-day marketing tasks, and liaise with sales and product development leaders within the company to establish requirements for campaigns/programs. Secondary responsibilities include coordination of events, digital marketing, PR, and working closely with Sales Team to provide appropriate tools, materials and presentations. <b>A cover letter and work samples are required for consideration.</b></p>
<p><b>
Reasonable Accommodations Statement
</b></p>
<p>To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable qualified individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</p>
<br>
<p>
<b>
ESSENTIAL JOB FUNCTIONS/DUTIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop and execute product-specific marketing strategies and product-specific marketing plans that support SVO sales objectives, are informed by the SVO brand position, and meet senior team objectives and overall sales goals.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>In collaboration with the leadership team, develop an annual marketing and communications plan, companion product-specific marketing plans, and market-specific strategies that support all SVO initiatives and product sales goals.</li>
<li>Manage and grow the in-house marketing team while effectively coordinating with all external agency and creative partners.</li>
<li>Supervise the successful management and improvement of the SVO website and social media properties.</li>
<li>Develop and execute successful social media strategies and social campaigns and provide consistent reporting.</li>
<li>Supervise the formulation of all SVO event marketing strategies.</li>
<li>Supervise the creation and execution of the SVO event roadmap and the formulation and execution of all event strategies, with the goal of increasing SVO Farmer Focus brand exposure and increasing sales of all products.</li>
<li>Identify and implement essential and accurate marketing metrics to ensure SVO business objectives are met and re-evaluate and modify marketing metrics as appropriate.</li>
<li>Proactively share established metrics results, consistently seeking and implementing effective marketing iteration based on interpretation of results.</li>
<li>Establish clear SVO marketing objectives and form and execute plans with clear timelines and milestones; oversee all SVO marketing projects to achieve stated objectives in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Provide leadership for SVO content creation and optimization processes, utilizing both internal and external resources and partners.</li>
</ul> <p><b>Marketing Team Leadership and Inter-Departmental Relations
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop and motivate an effective marketing team and maintain strong interdepartmental, partner, and vendor relations.</li>
<li>Effectively coordinate with the in-house design and web specialist staff and all external advertising teams to ensure resources are used appropriately to achieve organizational goals in an efficient and professional manner.</li>
<li>Provide coaching and mentoring to all marketing staff. Establish clear expectations and hold all staff accountable for meeting goals and for providing exceptional customer service to all SVO stakeholders and departmental customers.</li>
<li>Educate and develop the marketing acumen and specific specialist skill sets of the SVO marketing team to further grow a high performing marketing team.</li>
<li>Create a strong sense of trust, mutual respect, and teamwork within the SVO marketing team and with all external partners, vendors, and other SVO stakeholders.</li>
<li>Act as liaison between various internal stakeholder groups, such as sales and product teams, as well as all appropriate vendors, with the end-goal of increased participation and efficiencies in the marketing process.</li>
<li>Identify and implement mechanisms that enable ease of use for SVO senior leadership and other departments relying on marketing collateral, presentations, etc.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Marketing Technology</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Experience with utilizing common CRM systems and their specific applications to marketing, event, and digital strategies.</li>
<li>Experience utilizing project management systems specific to a marketing ecosystem and demonstrate experience with project management methods specific to the organization and execution of the work of the SVO marketing team.</li>
<li>Coach the SVO marketing team on project management methods and consistent and timely execution of marketing jobs.</li>
<li>Experience with Google analytics, and other analytics reporting tools.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Budget Management
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>In collaboration with the leadership team, contribute to the development and management of the annual SVO marketing budget.</li>
<li>Manage and maximize the SVO marketing budget to align resources based on marketing volume and business or sales goals.</li>
<li>Track marketing budget spend against marketing plans and identify areas for mid-course corrections to remain within budget.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Communications and Publications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage all SVO corporate communications and publications to achieve stated communication objectives.</li>
<li>Provide assistance to the leadership team for all Board of Directors and Investor presentations and communication.</li>
<li>Coordinate messaging as needed with external PR partners to increase market exposure of the SVO brand and products.</li>
<li>Coordinate development of a communications plan that aligns with external PR partners and supports SVO business strategies.</li>
<li>Establish metrics for measuring the success of communication plans and modify strategies as appropriate.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Events and Engagement</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Supervise the Marketing and Event Coordinator to ensure the effective execution of all SVO branded and sales events to achieve defined objectives.</li>
<li>Establish an annual event plan and roadmap for all events.</li>
<li>Develop specific goals and budgets for each major event.</li>
<li>Supervise the Marketing and Events Coordinator to ensure a positive brand experience at all SVO events, including the development of all trade show booths and related marketing collateral.</li>
<li>Implement effective companion marketing efforts to support SVO events and increase market exposure and the successful lead generation at all events.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Work effectively within the SVO Management System.</li>
<li>Work to cultivate the SVO Culture.</li>
<li>Demonstrate a high degree of Customer service skills, with both internal and external Customers.</li>
<li>Must be able to communicate effectively regarding Product and Brand knowledge to our Customers as well as our internal management.</li>
<li>All other related duties as assigned by Management from time to time.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>EDUCATION/EXPERIENCE:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Recommended candidate experience: Master’s degree in Marketing, Business Management and/or related field or a relevant combination of education and experience.</li>
<li><b>Must be a Story Brand Marketer.</b></li>
<li>Progressive job history demonstrating a broad knowledge of major marketing roles.</li>
<li>Experience in planning and developing Marketing Strategies, Advertising Campaigns and successful Public Relations efforts.</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills.</li>
<li>Proven leadership skills.</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Office Suite, Adobe Creative Suite, and social media platforms.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Other Physical Requirements
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Vision (Near, Color, Depth Perception) Must be able to pass color vision test.</li>
<li>Ability to 1 – 2 flights of stairs 2 – 6 times daily.</li>
</ul> <p><b>WORK ENVIRONMENT: </b>Multiple and varying work environments will be encountered and be required to work in, e.g., Small/Large offices, and production environments. Must be able to tolerate the varying heat and chill of the plant that accompanies each season, hearing and eye protective equipment required. Required to wear hair net, beard net if applicable.</p>
