<h2>Inside Sales Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Are you a sales professional who can drive new business?</div>
<div>If you enjoy building relationships, talking to people and closing the deal, this may be the job for you!</div>
<div>As an Inside Sales Consultant, you will develop and maintain face-to-face relationships with customers by promoting products, providing day-to-assistance and generating sales for a building materials industry leader. From customers to contractors, you play an important role in recommending roofing materials and tools for projects of all sizes.</div>
<div>Beacon Roofing Supply, Inc. is one of the largest distributors of residential and non-residential roofing and complementary building products in North America. Beacon currently has over 550 locations in 50 US states and 6 Canadian provinces and more than 8,000 employees. We are known throughout the building supply industry for having quality people, quality service and quality building products.</div>
<div>Beacon Roofing Supply is publicly traded on the NASDAQ stock market under the symbol BECN. In June of 2006, Beacon Roofing Supply was named to the NASDAQ Global Select Market.</div>
<div>Job Description</div>
<ul>
<li>Receive and process requests directly from customers (via telephone, fax and mail) for price quotations, product availability, orders, material returns, billing corrections, product information, order changes, and cancellations</li>
<li>Plan and implement telemarketing and prospecting objectives</li>
<li>Quickly and smoothly resolve customer complaints and problems</li>
<li>Engage in specialty purchasing (when applicable)</li>
<li>Coordinate delivery and/or pick up of customer orders with operations teams</li>
<li>Provide support to Outside Sales force (also serve as a backup)</li>
<li>Generate price quotations in a timely manner</li>
<li>Responsible for adhering to policies of the customer financial services</li>
<li>Maintain accurate and up-to-date "open order" files in computer system</li>
<li>Maintain adequate supply of product samples and literature</li>
<li>Adhere to pricing guidelines established by Branch Manager</li>
<li>Review all orders to forecast additional customer needs</li>
<li>Interact with sister branches when necessary on inventory issues and deliveries</li>
<li>Work with Replenishers to achieve the best product line</li>
<li>Handle cash reconciliation of register tape and Trend reporting documents</li>
<li>Maintain adequate stock of Tool Rack items with accurate pricing</li>
<li>Maintain adequate product stock at branch location</li>
<li>Responsible for setup and maintenance of store displays</li>
<li>Sustain a working technical knowledge of all available Allied products</li>
<li>Review job plans and bidding documents for Taper quotes and developing project summaries for Outside Sales personnel</li>
<li>Perform other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<div>Requirements</div>
<ul>
<li>Previous front-line customer service experience</li>
<li>Previous sales experience (building products industry preferred)</li>
<li>Must be able to communicate and follow-up with co-workers, customers and vendors</li>
<li>Must be able to learn and retain vast amounts of product information</li>
</ul>
<div>Preferences</div>
<ul>
<li>Experience in a team-based environment</li>
</ul>
