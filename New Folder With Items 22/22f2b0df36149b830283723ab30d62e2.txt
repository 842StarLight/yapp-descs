<p><b>Job Description</b>: An excellent opportunity to be part of a leading International Trade practice group supporting a diverse team of lawyers across various time zones to accomplish deadline driven projects for ongoing international trade disputes. As a Trade Analyst, you will be offered the chance to be involved in all facets of some of the most significant international trade disputes across the globe in a wide variety of industries.</p>
<p><b>Primary Responsibilities</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Coordinate with teams of attorneys and other analysts to prepare responses in Antidumping and Countervailing Duty investigations and reviews under tight deadlines</li>
<li>Draft responses to Antidumping and Countervailing Duty questionnaires;</li>
<li>Work with international companies to collect information on sales and production processes;</li>
<li>Prepare and manipulate SAS databases; analyzing price and cost drivers.</li>
<li>Assemble and analyze trade flow data and assist with correspondence and extensive management of case documents</li>
<li>Work with senior attorneys and non-attorney professionals to ensure all deadlines are met.</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
<b>Job Qualifications</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's Degree with 3-5 years of professional experience, preferably in Antidumping and Countervailing Duties.</li>
<li>Must have strong organizational and communication skills as well as familiarity with Antidumping and Countervailing Duty policies, procedures, and methodology.</li>
<li>Proficient in SAS statistical software, Microsoft Office 2010, with advanced MS Excel.</li>
<li>Outstanding project management skills, including strong attention to detail and ability to execute multiple projects simultaneously.</li>
<li>Candidate must have the ability to work effectively individually and as part of a diverse team as well as an ability to prioritize, work under pressure with tight deadlines and ability make the appropriate tradeoffs as needed.</li>
<li>Some accounting background a plus</li>
<li>Language skills a plus (native/fluent) â€“ in Japanese, Korean, Vietnamese, or Spanish.</li>
<li>Travel is required.</li>
</ul>
