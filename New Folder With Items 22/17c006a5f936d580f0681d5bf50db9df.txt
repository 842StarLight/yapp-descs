<h2>Digital Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Plans and directs digital marketing strategy of the organization and client’s products and services by performing the following duties.</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b> include the following. Other duties may be assigned.</p>
<ul>
<li>Establishes strategic online marketing campaigns to achieve corporate objectives for products and services.</li>
<li>Develops and executes comprehensive marketing plans and programs, both short and long range, to support sales and revenue objectives of the organization and its clients.</li>
<li>Researches, analyzes, and monitors financial, technological, and demographic factors to capitalize on market opportunities and minimize effects of competitive activity.</li>
<li>Plans and oversees advertising and promotion activities including, online and electronic media.</li>
<li>Evaluates and recommends distribution channel development programs.</li>
</ul>
<p>DUTIES AND TASKS</p>
<ul>
<li>Develops and executes online marketing campaigns for clients and the organization including email marketing and SEM (paid search)</li>
<li>Recommends and assists with updates to company and client websites to increase overall website effectiveness and SEO by monitoring and testing campaigns to optimize effectiveness and ensure ROI.</li>
<li>Creates, implements and monitors SEO and PPC strategy to drive traffic and conversation rates</li>
<li>Ensures effective control of online marketing results, and takes corrective action to guarantee that achievement of marketing objectives falls within designated budgets.</li>
<li>Oversees and evaluates market research and adjusts marketing strategy to meet changing market and competitive conditions.</li>
<li>Monitors competitive products and marketing activities.</li>
<li>Analyze campaigns and identify trends to optimize the campaigns to produce the maximum ROI for the company and its clients.</li>
<li>Researches and evaluates market and adjust to new trends to meet changing market and competitive conditions.</li>
<li>Implement all tracking mechanisms on websites and monitor website analytics and online conversations.</li>
<li>Guides the customization and implementation of the company’s internal CRM system.</li>
<li>Establishes and maintains a consistent corporate image throughout product lines</li>
<li>Analyzes current research and statistical techniques, and evaluates the latest research developments to develop new market research methods.</li>
<li>Maintains up to date knowledge and expertise of SEM strategies.</li>
<li>Plans, organizes, and coordinates assigned programs to ensure accomplishment of financial/budgetary goals and optimum allocation of resources.</li>
<li>Manages resources to execute assigned programs.</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
</div>
