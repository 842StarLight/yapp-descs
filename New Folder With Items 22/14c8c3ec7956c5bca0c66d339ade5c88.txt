<h2>Robotics & Cognitive Architect</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div><b>S&amp;A-Analytics and Cognitive</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Robotics and Cognitive Automation Manager/Architect</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Work you’ll do</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
As a RCA Manager/Architect at Deloitte you will manage and deliver components of client engagements that identify, design, and implement technology and creative RCA solutions for large companies. Key responsibilities will include:</div>
<ul>
<li>Participate in pre-sales client workshops, provide solution architect /technical architect support to the teams in RFI/RFP process</li>
<li>Analyze client processes, identify automation opportunities, define RCA value proposition, reengineer process to improve automation potential and recommend RCA approach/strategy</li>
<li>Develop Detail As Is Process Description - PDD (Process Description Document), Detail To Be Automated Process Description - SDD (Solution Design Document)</li>
<li>Conduct RCA High Level Assessments
</li>
<li>Develop RCA client architecture and solution proposal focusing on scalability and extensibility
</li>
<li>Work with the Project Managers and Business Analysts to develop high level project plans for implementation projects</li>
<li>Have exposure to wide variety of technology problems and their solutions and use that to drive the adoption of best practices around coding, design, quality, and performance in the team</li>
<li>Provides continuous advice to RCA developers and Project Manager during project delivery</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Also, Manager key responsibilities include:</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Manage teams in the identification of business requirements, functional design, process design (including scenario design, flow mapping), prototyping, testing, training, defining support procedures.</li>
<li>Formulate planning, budgeting, forecasting and reporting strategies.</li>
<li>Manage full life cycle implementations.</li>
<li>Develop statements of work and/or client proposals.</li>
<li>Identify business opportunities to increase usability and profitability of information architecture.</li>
<li>Experience with program leadership, governance and change enablement.</li>
<li>Develop and manage vendor relationships.</li>
<li>Lead workshops for client education.</li>
<li>Manage resources and budget on client projects.</li>
<li>Assist and drive the team by providing oversight.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
The team</b></div>
<div><b>
Analytics &amp; Cognitive</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
In this age of disruption, organizations need to navigate the future with confidence, embracing decision making with clear, data-driven choices that deliver enterprise value in a dynamic business environment.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The Analytics &amp; Cognitive team leverages the power of data, analytics, robotics, science and cognitive technologies to uncover hidden relationships from vast troves of data, generate insights, and inform decision-making. Together with the Strategy practice, our Strategy &amp; Analytics portfolio helps clients transform their business by architecting organizational intelligence programs and differentiated strategies to win in their chosen markets.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Analytics &amp; Cognitive will work with our clients to:</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Implement large-scale data ecosystems including data management, governance and the integration of structured and unstructured data to generate insights leveraging cloud-based platforms</li>
<li>Leverage automation, cognitive and science-based techniques to manage data, predict scenarios and prescribe actions</li>
<li>Drive operational efficiency by maintaining their data ecosystems, sourcing analytics expertise and providing As-a-Service offerings for continuous insights and improvements</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Qualifications</b></div>
<ul>
<li>6+ years of relevant RCA Analytics consulting or industry experience</li>
<li>At least 3 years of experience in Robotic Process Automations (RCA) with one of the market leading RCA platforms : Automation Anywhere, BluePrism and/or UIPath</li>
<li>Minimum 3 years of process design and process improvement experience</li>
<li>Minimum 5 years of experience in programming (.NET, Java, ETL etc.)</li>
<li>Demonstrated expertise with at least 3 full life cycle analytics or systems integration engagements across strategy, design and implementation.</li>
<li>2+ years experience leading and managing project teams
</li>
<li>Bachelor's Degree or equivalent professional experience.</li>
<li>Ability to travel 80-100% of the time (Monday – Thursday/Friday)</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Preferred:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science</li>
<li>Certification in Enterprise Architecture (e.g. TOGAF).</li>
<li>Ability to work independently, manage small engagements or parts of large engagements.</li>
<li>Strong oral and written communication skills, including presentation skills (MS Visio, MS PowerPoint).</li>
<li>Strong problem solving and troubleshooting skills with the ability to exercise mature judgment.</li>
</ul>
