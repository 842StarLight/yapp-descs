<h2>Sr Manager, Data/Sys Bus Analysis</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<div>We are seeking a talented individual for a<b> Sr. Manager, Data/Systems Business Analysis</b> who acts in a senior management capacity, managing all activities of the Data/Systems Business Analysis team(s). Responsible for overseeing various data file and system development lifecycle projects. Provides direction to resources, monitors development efforts, reports project status and raises awareness on issues requiring escalation. Provides a strategy for continuous improvement to meet customer expectations.
<br>
<br>
<b>Essential Responsibilities:</b><br>
</div>
<ul>
<li>
Manages the activities of the Data/Systems Business Analysts including staffing levels, performance management, salary administration, training and development, and achievement of department goals.</li>
<li>
Acts as the main conduit for engaging the team and developing individual team members.</li>
<li>
Tracks KPIs, MBOs and individual goals. Sets goals for individuals and the team.</li>
<li>
Builds technical and soft skills training program for the team.</li>
<li>
Thinks strategically to build tools and innovative time saving solutions for resources while maintaining quality.</li>
<li>
Provides intelligence to management about the queue, what is aging and identifies major roadblocks / trends.</li>
<li>
Works with Project Managers to bring process improvement ideas to fruition.</li>
<li>
Provides direction to resources and monitors development efforts; highlights issues at risk.</li>
<li>
Assembles project team members, assigning individual responsibilities.</li>
<li>
Develops project schedules, manages team resources, creates work</li>
<li>
breakdown structures, estimates size and effort of new development, as well as develops detailed requirements for these projects as needed.</li>
<li>
Coordinates and prioritizes staff tasks and also coordinates with other departments.</li>
<li>
Acts as an escalation point for teams; assists multiple levels of staff with their needs and knowledge to complete their job with quality.</li>
<li>
Coordinates and facilitates meetings, presentations to senior management and users as needed.</li>
<li>
Prepares and presents status reports and other ad hoc report requests needed by upper management. Builds reports to highlight impact of monthly queue on product areas / develops a communication strategy to highlight value of the team.</li>
<li>
Provides feedback/input for budgetary process.</li>
</ul>

<ul><li>Performs other functions as assigned</li></ul>
