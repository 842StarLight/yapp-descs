<h2>Digital Marketing Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>GENERAL DESCRIPTION – PURPOSE OF ROLE:</b></p>
<p>
The Digital Marketing Coordinator will play an essential role in supporting Blue Buffalo’s Digital Marketing domestic and international initiatives. As coordinator, you will be responsible for managing and coordinating projects in a fast-paced, deadline driven environment, working together with internal marketing and creative resources (as well as external agency resources) to deliver email, social and website related initiatives.<br>
</p>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:</b></p>
<p><b>
Email Support (30%):</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Assist CRM manager with routing assets as part of email deployment process.</li>
<li>
Assist in sends of segmented test emails.<ul><li>
Gather feedback from stakeholders and communicate to 3rd party vendor responsible for email builds.</li></ul>
</li>
<li>
Evaluate competitive email and CRM programs and share findings with team members (quarterly).</li>
<li>
Support ways to incorporate user generated content into email communications.</li>
<li>
Manage the process of translating content for international emails.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Website Support (40%):</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Assist in updating and adding content across all US and International Blue Buffalo B2B and B2C websites.</li>
<li>
Assist with routing assets across marketing and creative departments, coordinate and communicate feedback with external agency partners.</li>
<li>
Perform complete QA during stage and production deploys &amp; works with appropriate resources to drive resolution of any issues found.</li>
<li>
Manage the process of translating content for new international website features as well as assisting with the translation of US article content to be featured on International websites, where applicable.</li>
<li>
Evaluate competitive websites and digital experiences and share findings with team members (quarterly).</li>
<li>
Conduct regular audit of the website from a content and imagery perspective.<ul><li>
Work with internal marketing and creative resources to resolve any issues found in a timely manner.</li></ul>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Online Community Management (20%):</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Moderate user-generated content submitted to BLUE’s various online communities, contests and campaigns.</li>
<li>
Maintain a library of UGC content that can be used in digital marketing communications.</li>
<li>
Assist in management and implementation of online ratings and UGC integration.</li>
<li>
Assist with social media moderation.</li>
<li>
Liaise with customer service team on customer service related inquiries received via social media.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Other (10%):</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Generate purchase orders via ERP system.</li>
<li>
Assist in ad-hoc digital marketing initiatives as needed.</li>
</ul>
Assist with reporting as needed.<br>
<br>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS:</b></p>
Must be able to work in an environment with free-roaming companion pets.<br>
<br>
<p><b>PREFERRED SKILLS, COMPETENCIES AND EXPERIENCE:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Basic understanding and interest in Digital Marketing principles, platforms and tactics.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of Content Management Systems and Email Management Systems (Salesforce Marketing Cloud, Oracle Cloud Services Constant Contact, etc.) or similar experience.</li>
<li>
Proficient in Excel and project management software &amp; methodologies.</li>
<li>
Strong analytical skills, experience using Google Analytics preferred.</li>
<li>
1-3 years of experience in a supporting role in a digital marketing environment.</li>
<li>
Strong attention to detail, as well as strong written and verbal communication skills required.</li>
<li>
Pet parent preferred.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
EDUCATION REQUIREMENTS:</b></p>
<p>
Bachelor’s Degree in related field required.</p>
