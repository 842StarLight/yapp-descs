<b>Summary:</b><br>
The Manager, Revenue Recognition will be responsible for ensuring proper revenue recognition in a fast-paced, shared services environment. In addition, they will be responsible for reviewing customer contracts and incoming sales orders, specific to the software and telecommunications industry, in accordance with ASC 606 and support any projects involving revenue recognition software implementation.
<br><br>
<b>Key Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Establish and maintain business partnerships with key stakeholders across the organization</li>
<li>Provide technical accounting advice to key stakeholders during the deal negotiation stage such that revenues are maximized where possible in accordance with external and internal accounting guidance</li>
<li>Review contracts for compliance with US GAAP ASC 606 and Nextiva's Revenue Recognition Policy to ensure timely and accurate financial reporting, identifying potential impacts on revenue and financial risk</li>
<li>Research and analyze complex revenue recognition issues as required</li>
<li>Design, manage, implement and execute key controls in the quote to cash process</li>
<li>Perform and manage all receivables, revenue and deferred revenue accounting activities including the monthly close processes, account reconciliations, revenue reporting, and revenue/contract analysis</li>
<li>Partner with the billing team to improve enterprise wide operational efficiencies</li>
<li>Implement accounting processes and procedures and improve internal controls</li>
<li>Adopt required and business driven accounting changes</li>
<li>Maintain an up to date and thorough knowledge of the technical accounting policies, including Nextiva's internal Revenue Recognition Policy</li>
<li>Develop relationships with our external auditors to ensure all audit requirements are met on a timely basis</li>
<li>Responsible for and/or assist with recruitment, performance management, conducting appraisals, and identifying training and development needs of direct reports and the wider team</li>
<li>Partner with IT, Development and Sales to successfully implement a new billing system</li>
</ul>
