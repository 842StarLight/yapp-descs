<p><b>
PRIMARY FUNCTION:</b></p>
<p>
Provides superior member service by accurately and efficiently performing teller related transactions in accordance with VACU policies and procedures. Builds and maintains member relationships by recommending VACU products and services.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
JOB DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Demonstrates appropriate knowledge of all VACU policies and procedures related to the teller area, including Security and Information Security</li>
<li>
Meets member needs by maintaining appropriate knowledge of all VACU products and services</li>
<li>
Makes appropriate referrals of VACU products and services</li>
<li>
Performs paying and receiving functions with accurate balancing and reporting, including but not limited to deposits, check cashing, loan payments, miscellaneous sales, cash advances, etc.</li>
<li>
Handles each transaction proficiently, exercising good judgment and seeking approval for any transaction exceeding their level of authority.</li>
<li>
Verifies and balances assigned cash drawer daily with minimal cash variances. Assists in reconciling errors or discrepancies. Maintains adequate cash and/or cash limits as required by VACU policy.</li>
<li>
Demonstrates basic knowledge of safe deposit box access procedures</li>
<li>
Provides quality service by following all member service expectations</li>
<li>
Meets expectations in all areas of teller performance documentation including service quality, operations, and sales.</li>
<li>
Comply with all published enterprise level policies and procedures including, but not limited to, Risk Management policies.</li>
<li>
Complete all required, ongoing enterprise level training including, but not limited to BSA, OFAC, and Information Security.</li>
<li>
Report all Risk Management Policy violations in accordance with policy.</li>
<li>
Other duties may be required and assigned by the supervisor</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>

JOB QUALIFICATIONS:<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Knowledge: </b>Good understanding of products and services offered by financial institutions preferred</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Skills:</b> Satisfactory computer skills preferred.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Abilities:</b> Math aptitude; professional appearance, manner and communication skills</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Minimum Education and Experience: </b>Minimum of high school diploma or equivalent. Cash handling/business experience, preferably at a financial institution.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
PHYSICAL REQUIREMENTS:</b></p>
<p>
This job requires the ability to sit and stand for long periods of time.</p>
<p>
This job requires occasionally lifting up to 50 pounds and/or up to 30 pounds frequently.</p>
