<h2>Sales Administrator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>ADA Site Compliance (“ADASC”) is a leading provider of website accessibility solutions for businesses. We help our clients make – and keep – their sites compliant with the Americans with Disabilities Act (“ADA”) and avoid costly litigation. We seek to hire an energetic, talented full-time Sales Administrator to join our team. The position offers interesting work and regular and reasonable hours in a friendly, dynamic, and casual work environment.</p>
<p>Businesses and public entities of all sizes and across nearly every industry trust our expertise in making their online brands usable and enjoyable for all. Our clients include global leaders in community management, retail, food and beverage, healthcare, education, travel, hospitality, banking, and insurance, as well as mid-market and boutique enterprises.</p>
<p>This is an entry-level position in a fast-growing startup enterprise. <i>Best of all,</i> <i>you will have the opportunity to “do well by doing good.”</i></p>
<p>Find us on the web: www.ADASiteCompliance.com.</p>
<p><i><b>Summary of Responsibilities: </b></i></p>
<ul>
<li>Provide sales and operational support to senior salespeople on the team</li>
<li>Leverage client relationship management (CRM) software platform to help satisfy client requests</li>
<li>Help maintain prospective client database</li>
<li>Assist with ADASC’s marketing efforts, including participation in conferences, webcasts and other events</li>
<li>Communicate with prospective clients via telephone, face-to-face and written correspondence, using the highest level of service</li>
<li>Attend both internal and client meetings</li>
<li>Follow up on outstanding proposals and contracts</li>
<li>Assist in responding to requests for proposals</li>
<li>Provide support to ADASC’s tech and operations teams as necessary for successful completion of projects</li>
<li>Assist in the identification and development of new product and service offerings</li>
<li>Perform ad-hoc work/special projects as necessary to support ADASC on various client and internal initiatives</li>
</ul>
<p><i><b>Compensation: </b></i></p>
<p>Compensation, which will be competitive and commensurate with experience, will include a base salary plus bonus.</p>
<p><i><b>Job Requirements: </b></i></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree preferred but not required</li>
<li>0-2+ years of previous sales experience</li>
<li>Excited to advance your career into more substantive sales roles</li>
<li>Proficient with Microsoft Office applications, Adobe Acrobat, and the Internet</li>
<li>Positive client service attitude with the desire to facilitate successful sales efforts</li>
<li>Demonstrated professional integrity</li>
<li>Dependable, flexible, and adaptable to new ADASC initiatives and changing client needs</li>
<li>Ability to work well in a fast-paced, small-team environment</li>
<li>Ability to work independently, multi-task and prioritize effectively</li>
<li>Ability to establish and maintain effective working relationships with colleagues, prospects, and clients</li>
<li>Volunteers for and shows initiative on both internal and external projects and tasks</li>
<li>Dedicated to upholding ADASC’s high quality standards</li>
<li>Strong organizational and problem-solving skills with attention to detail</li>
<li>Excellent oral and written communication skills</li>
<li>Infrequent travel required</li>
<li>Available for work Monday through Friday 9am to 5pm</li>
</ul>
