Business Intelligence Developer<br>
The MIS &amp; Analytics group within the Retail Payments Solutions Prepaid division of U.S. Bank is looking for a Business Intelligence Developer to support the sales, marketing, operations, and product teams. This technical developer position will be responsible for eliciting reporting and analytics requirements from business users; translating those requirements into dashboards and reports using the Prepaid data warehouse and associated technologies; and providing operational and training support for the reports. In addition, the developer will have operational responsibilities during on-call rotations, disaster recovery exercises, and other support tasks. The position will communicate with staff, peer managers, and senior managers across the business and supporting teams, including business operations, DBAs, development, enterprise data management, and project management teams.<br>
Qualified candidates will have business intelligence systems experience, and will develop, configure, maintain, and support dashboards, reports, and analytics for end users. The best candidates can analyze end-user requirements to identify user profiles, translate requirements into queries or reports, and then design, develop, troubleshoot, debug, test, and deploy dashboards/reports/queries. Experience in a financial company is helpful but not strictly required.<br>
