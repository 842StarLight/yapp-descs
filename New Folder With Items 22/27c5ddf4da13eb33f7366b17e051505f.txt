<div>
Unravel is seeking an <b>Account Development Representative (ADR)</b> with enterprise sales exposure and at least 1-3 years of experience. You will be driving pre-sales activities for Unravel's Big Data Application Performance Management platform offering. The ideal candidate has a passion, general experience and knowledge of software solutions specifically in data and cloud technology.</div>
</div>
<div>
<div>
About the Role</div>
<ul>
<li>
Capable of quickly learning new software product(s) and clearly communicating the value proposition</li>
<li>
Develop and execute territory account plans to achieve and exceed quota responsibility</li>
<li>
Responsibility to move the transaction through the entire pre-sales cycle and pass-off effectively to the outside account executive (i.e., candidate is a “hunter” not a “sales order” taker)</li>
<li>
Consistently build and deliver an accurate pipeline via Salesforce (SFDC)</li>
<li>
Able to develop strong relationships with key decision makers, influencers and partners within identified territory</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>
<div>
About You</div>
<ul>
<li>
1+ years of successful software pre-sales experience with focus on enterprise technology and solutions</li>
<li>
Successful track record of pre-sale activities promoting enterprise solutions</li>
<li>
Ability to establish relationships with C-level clients, business and technical buyers, and key project stakeholders</li>
<li>
Successfully sell the business value vs. product is key</li>
<li>
Ability to clearly articulate your point of view in a professional manner</li>
<li>
Possess entrepreneurial spirit or worked in a fluid (start-up) environment</li>
<li>
Experience working with a team of professionals in sales campaigns that include field AEs, SEs, Marketing, Services, etc.</li>
<li>
Bachelors degree required</li>
<li>
Pre-selling one or more of the following products a plus, but not required: Middleware, Analytics, Data Warehouse, Business Intelligence and/or Hadoop “Big Data”</li>
<li>
Team player, self motivated and able to take ownership on the assigned task</li>
</ul>
