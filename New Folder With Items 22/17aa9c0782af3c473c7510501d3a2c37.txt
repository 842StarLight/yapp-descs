<h2>RMA Data Administrator (Return Merchandise Administrator)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>JOB SUMMARY</b></p>
<p>
The <b>Return Merchandise Data Administrator,</b> RMA Data Administrator, will administrate all aspects of the customer RMA process. The postholder is responsible for Sales Order creation, RTV Return to Vendor management, and service parts inventory management. The postholder works closely with Operations and Service team under the guidance of the Operations Manager.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
JOB RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Collect and process all necessary information required to create an RMA number and/or SO advance replacement; forward all necessary information to customer and update systems accordingly</li>
<li>
Enter and process Sales Orders and RMAs daily</li>
<li>
Ensure timely and accurate data entry in ERP system Expandable and Salesforce</li>
<li>
Coordinate tracking of RMA with Service and update Salesforce as needed</li>
<li>
Update RMA statuses and correspond with stakeholders</li>
<li>
Coordinate with vendor to process monthly RTV transactions</li>
<li>
Monitor service parts usage level and submit purchase requests as needed
</li>
<li>Customer Master entry and maintenance when required</li>
<li>
Serial Number verification and maintenance</li>
<li>
Supply Chain transfer between warehouses</li>
<li>
Provide Operational support as required to all team members within the department including but not limited to Service, Scheduling, Logistics and Purchasing</li>
<li>
Document and maintain department procedures</li>
<li>
Provide RMA KPI and trend analysis as required</li>
<li>
Review audit reports for data integrity<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>REQUIRED SKILLS, KNOWLEDGE AND EXPERIENCE</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
An Associate's degree or equivalent education plus work experience</li>
<li>
Min 2 years of experience in manufacturing environment</li>
<li>
Min 2 years of experience in RTV or material management</li>
<li>
Familiarity with ERP system and CRM system</li>
<li>
Proficient with MS Office Suite, e.g. Word, Excel and Outlook
</li>
<li>Ability to work in a team environment and communicate effectively</li>
<li>
Strong verbal &amp; written communication skills</li>
<li>
Ability to work independently with minimum supervision</li>
<li>
Excellent attention to detail to ensure accuracy</li>
<li>
Ability to analyze situation and react quickly</li>
</ul>
