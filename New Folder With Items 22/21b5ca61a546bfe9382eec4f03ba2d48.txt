<h2>Program Manager, Prospect Marketing</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Southern New Hampshire University is currently seeking a Program Manager of Prospect Marketing. The Program Manager, Prospect Marketing is responsible for conceiving and building targeted marketing campaigns to move prospective students through the funnel and re-engage with lapsed prospects through a variety of channels.
</p>
<p>The Program Manager, Prospect Marketing will play a hands-on, critical role in developing, executing and tracking the impact of automated marketing and communication plans that align with the organization’s goals. In addition to automated and mass email communications, communications channels might include digital and social media, phone, chat and text messaging, among others.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Specifically, this position will work closely with Admission, Enrollment Operations, Data Analytics and Marketing Automation teams to develop and execute an overarching communications plan designed to generate new starts and re-engage with lapsed leads.
</p>
<p>This position reports to the AVP of Prospect &amp; Student Lifecycle Marketing and works collaboratively with other members of the Prospect &amp; Student Marketing team as well as others in Marketing and throughout the company such as Admission, Enrollment Operations, ITS, Data Analytics and other Student Support departments as part of a service organization.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential responsibilities of this position include the following:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Provide a unified view of all prospective student marketing and communication, with a focus on maintaining brand consistency and integration with message, tone and voice
</li>
<li>Drive growth via nurture marketing to prospects and remarketing strategies to “dusty” leads
</li>
<li>Develop a variety of targeted marketing content and campaigns – including email, phone scripts, live chat, direct mail and text – designed to nurture prospective students to the point of enrollment
</li>
<li>Ensure accuracy in campaigns, including content and audience, in partnership with the Marketing &amp; Email Automation Program Manager
</li>
<li>Track campaign results and impact across variety of performance metrics including engagement rates, conversion rates and new starts
</li>
<li>Manage an accessible editorial calendar and ensure shared understanding across department leaders
</li>
<li>Work closely with Admission and Enrollment Operations to enhance and adjust campaigns based on feedback from admission counselors, prospective students and changing business needs
</li>
<li>Demonstrate strong communication skills to ensure a shared understanding across department leaders and team members, an understanding of the marketing funnel, and a desire to grow as part of an integrated team
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Minimum Qualifications:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of seven years’ experience in marketing and/or communications, ideally within a non-profit or educational institution
</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in marketing, communications or related field
</li>
<li>Track record of developing and implementing marketing and communication strategies and campaigns
</li>
<li>Deep understanding of marketing processes including campaign execution, A/B testing and measurement
</li>
<li>Proven ability to understand and utilize data and metrics in decision-making
</li>
<li>Understanding or experience using CRM and Marketing Automation platforms
</li>
<li>Experience with email and project management software
</li>
<li>Client service oriented
</li>
<li>Ability to collaborate and build consensus across teams and departments
</li>
<li>Exceptional written and verbal communications skills
</li>
<li>Strong strategic thinking, creative problem solving and analytical skills
</li>
<li>Proven project management experience - including time management and attention to detail
</li>
<li>Robust understanding of the sales/marketing funnel
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Preferred Qualifications:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Master’s degree
</li>
<li>Experience in Salesforce, Marketing Cloud, Exact Target or Pardot
</li>
</ul>
