<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<p>As a Product Manager you're responsible for defining, prioritizing, planning and driving the execution of the vision for their product lines. You possess business acumen as well as the engineering wherewithal to strategize, drive and bring the product to market. You'll work extensively with customers and cross functionally with product marketing, engineering, sales, corporate marketing and alliances to ensure whole product success.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
As a Senior Product Manager for VMware Validated Designs, you will be responsible for helping deliver to market an expanded set of validated designs and solutions, which VMware customers will use to make sure their IT organizations deliver real business value. You’ll be part of the effort across VMware to develop infrastructure guidance and apply it to application run-times.<br>
You'll work across a number of different product teams and with data center architects and engineers to communicate requirements you’ve gathered from a variety of customers. You’ll be the voice of those customers in these conversations.You'll also spend significant time working to define solutions for VMware's Cloud Foundation software. Defining features, working with UX/UI teams, incorporating customer feedback, and helping to bring Cloud Foundation to market. Along the way you’ll get to wrestle with hard technical problems, smart software developers and architects, great writers, and a team of PMs who love technology.</p>
<p><br>
The Senior Product Manager for VMware Validated Designs are responsible for working to build a set of Validated Designs which pull together technologies from across the VMware portfolio. You’ll collaborate with product teams across VMware, a team of architects and engineers within the Integrated Systems BU, and most importantly, with customers to:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Define and drive user stories and features, user personas, product features/requirements, roadmaps and release plans for product features.</li>
<li>
Identify customer needs for the platform and architecture, drive feature prioritization and work closely with engineering to deliver successful products.</li>
<li>
Lead the product feature planning process; ensure that the product set meets the requirements of all users who will interact with it, be they IT experts or procurement departments.</li>
<li>
Understand customer (both external, internal and partner) needs and requirements and represent them in Epic definitions.</li>
<li>
Work closely with VMware customers, developers, and key VMware stakeholders including Engineering, Global Support Services, Product Marketing, and Sales.</li>
<li>
Understand the market and competition for use case support as a means of driving adoption of VMware technology.</li>
<li>
Support planning, execution and go-to-market</li>
</ul>
