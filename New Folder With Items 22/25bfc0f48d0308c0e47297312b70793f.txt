<h2>Designer - UI</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Mi9 Retail is hiring a <b>Jr. UI Designer</b> to join our Product Design Team. This position will focus on designing interfaces for ecommerce customers using industry best-practice design methodologies. The role will include work with external clients and internal project stakeholders. The ideal candidate should have some experience working in data-driven user experience and visual design in a fast paced, entrepreneurial environment. This position reports directly to the Director of Product Design.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Create high quality visual / UI design, primarily for retail web-based applications</li>
<li>
Design &amp; maintain visual style libraries for products</li>
<li>
Collaborate with other UX / UI Designers</li>
<li>
Work with product owners &amp; development leads to ensure UX/UI design is aligned properly with technical capabilities / within scope of project efforts</li>
<li>
Work with product management, business analysts, development, training and sales departments to gain an understanding Mi9 Retail’s core products and forward-looking design.</li>
<li>
Interpret sitemaps, wireframes and user flows into high fidelity designs</li>
<li>
Participate in stakeholder and user interviews</li>
<li>
Participate in user research &amp; testing</li>
<li>
Participate in competitive and/or heuristic analysis</li>
<li>
Participate in design sessions / design sprints</li>
<li>
Participate in design reviews with stakeholders</li>
<li>
Be the voice of the customer in understanding their behavior and needs.</li>
<li>
Keep up with the latest trends related to visual / UI design and usability studies</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor's Degree in digital design or related field</li>
<li>
Some industry experience</li>
<li>
Experience with Sketch app for wireframing and visual design</li>
<li>
Experience with Adobe CC suite</li>
<li>
Thorough understanding of responsive design principles / experience wireframing responsive design sites and/or native mobile apps</li>
<li>
Strong understanding of design trends; future-oriented mindset</li>
<li>
Ability to communicate effectively</li>
<li>
Self-motivated, organized, accountable, resourceful and goal-oriented</li>
<li>
Ability to multi-task and work efficiently under pressure with careful attention to detail</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
About Mi9 Retail</b><br>
Mi9 Retail is the fastest growing provider of enterprise software for retailers, wholesalers, and brands. Mi9 enables the world’s leading retailers to automate and optimize their entire Plan-to-Sell™ process, from planning to executing, influencing, and selling merchandise in-store, online, and on any device. Our corporate retail systems facilitate better planning, master data management, allocation, and replenishment, and our customer engagement and point-of-purchase systems boost revenue across digital and brick-and-mortar channels. Our solutions are connected via a common analytics framework that serves as the foundation of the system and speeds time to insight with role-based dashboards, KPIs, and governed self-service data discovery. Mi9 Retail is committed to helping retailers on their path to success, so they can maximize revenue and customer engagement while minimizing costs.</p>
