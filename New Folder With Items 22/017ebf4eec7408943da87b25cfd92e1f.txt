<h2>Call Center Representative I</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Call Center Representative I is an entry level position is responsible for being trained to perform duties associated with the following functional areas below with some assistance:</div>
<div>
<div>
Customer Service</div>
<div>
Billing</div>
<div>
Field Service Scheduling and Dispatching</div>
<div>
Service Administration</div>
</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Customer Service:</div>
<div>
<div>
Manage &amp; route all incoming calls and written requests Domestic &amp; International in a professional manner as outlined in the Customer Service standards.</div>
<div>
Analyze and provide problem resolution to Customers, International Affiliates, and internal personnel at all levels of the organization.</div>
<div>
Prepare Quotes and obtain applicable written approvals for service and part orders.</div>
</div>
<div>
Billing:</div>
<div>
<div>
Provide information related to billing &amp; repair status, service history, inventory inquires, warranty and agreement information.</div>
<div>
Perform research as necessary.</div>
<div>
Resolve billing discrepancies with customers, sales reps, and credit/collections.</div>
<div>
Perform complex service order billing of field repairs, depot repairs, 3rd party repair services, material stock requests, and order management orders for domestic and international customers.</div>
<div>
Review invoices for accuracy, resolve billing discrepancies, perform credit/rebills, maintain &amp; file service device history records.</div>
<div>
Assist corporate credit analyst to resolve aged invoices.</div>
</div>
<div>
Field Service Scheduling and Dispatching:</div>
<div>
<div>
Create and Update Cases and Service orders for Field and Depot services. - Capture &amp; record detailed Product failure information for reporting Product Complaints appropriately</div>
<div>
Dispatch and coordinate service events with Field Engineers and 3rd party Field Service Providers.</div>
<div>
Manage and monitor FE’s calendars and dispatch board.</div>
<div>
Perform cost effective &amp; independent decisions to schedule, prepare, and execute service events related to Capital equipment installations, upgrades, relocations, preventative maintenance and other service repairs as needed.</div>
<div>
Review 3rd party service invoices and approve for payment.</div>
</div>
<div>
Service Administration:</div>
<div>
<div>
Enter in system material requisitions and OM orders (domestic &amp; international) to process part requests, loaner requests, &amp; international freight charges.</div>
<div>
Ensure applicable pricing, inventory availability, and correct warranties/service agreements are applied.</div>
<div>
Coordinate International shipments with International Logistics group &amp; assist with preparing customs documentation for shipment.</div>
<div>
Create and maintain required spreadsheets</div>
<div>
Enter/Update/Correct Service Installation records in device relationship management system that tracks Equipment product ID’s, serial numbers, hardware/software revisions, and warranty information shipped/sold to Customers and all B&amp;L owned equipment.</div>
<div>
Manage files related to this activity.</div>
<div>
Monitor &amp; follow-up with Customers, Sales Reps, and FSE's on equipment returns to ensure timely receipt to fulfill Complaint evaluations.</div>
</div>
<div>
Additional Responsibilities include:</div>
<div>
<div>
Person MUST treat others with respect, demonstrate a positive can do approach, and be willing to work in whatever area is assigned.</div>
<div>
Remain open to change and support and assist projects when needed.</div>
<div>
Provide any data and/or reports to Manager as requested.</div>
<div>
Maintain a clean, neat, and orderly work area.</div>
<div>
Perform additional job duties as assigned</div>
<div>
Responsible for processing Field Service billings with accuracy</div>
<div>
Coordinate &amp; maintain the U.S. Field Service Engineers’ schedules. The primary scope of this position includes support for the Service organization for the U.S. market. Some support for markets outside the U.S. such as Canada and Latin America may be performed as necessary.</div>
<div>
Support Inter-company (Int'l) cost transfers, Distributors, &amp; 3rd party Service providers</div>
</div>
</div></div></div>
