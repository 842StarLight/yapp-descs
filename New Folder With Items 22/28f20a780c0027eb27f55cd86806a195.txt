<h2>Solutions Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">UserTesting enables companies to put their customers at the center of every business decision by leveraging the power of human insights. The most advanced on-demand customer insights platform, UserTesting enables product managers, UX researchers and designers, marketers, and digital executives to connect with their exact target customer in a matter of hours and uncover actionable insights that drive ROI. More than 35,000 companies have adopted UserTesting to make smarter business decisions throughout the design and development of their digital experiences as well as in their marketing messaging and competitive positioning.
<br>What makes the difference between a product that's engaging, compelling, and easy to use and one that's frustrating, broken, and complicated? The answer is user experience. Here at UserTesting, our mission is to create great experiences.
<br><br>
As a Solutions Consultant, you'll be an integral part of our sales organization, being a thought-leader throughout the sales process by articulating and demonstrating how UserTestings solution solves business challenges and delivers positive business outcomes.
<br><br>
This position can be located in Seattle or anywhere in the Northwest.
<br><br>
<b>What you'll be doing:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Collaborate with Enterprise Regional Sales Directors, Account Executives, and Customer Success Managers to identify customers business goals and initiatives, and uncover unmet needs and challenges</li>
<li>Consult and partner with various stakeholders from C-Suite Executives to Researchers to maximize the quality and value of the insights they will gather from UserTestings solution</li>
<li>Develop strategic solutions that map to customers business goals and initiatives</li>
<li>Provide recommendations and best practices on how UserTesting can address business goals and initiatives, unmet needs, and challenges</li>
<li>Deliver relevant and impactful product demonstrations that impress the value of UserTestings solution in relation to customers goals &amp; initiatives</li>
<li>Develop a deep familiarity of the UserTesting platform via hands-on training leveraging relevant use cases</li>
<li>Demonstrate understanding and value of customer experience research methodologies conducted via the UserTesting platform</li>
<li>Lead and/or consult on Proof-of-Concepts (POCs) to ensure results address and impact a strategic business goal or initiative</li>
<li>Partner with the Research and Marketing teams to develop collateral, content and training to enable the Sales team</li>
<li>Communicate effectively with Sales team regarding sales activities and strategies</li>
<li>Inform the Product teams of trends, use cases, and business impact of how specific capabilities are delivering / not delivering against customers business goals &amp; objectives</li>
<li>Engage in thought leadership and marketing related efforts, such as blogs, webinars, and public speaking</li>
</ul>
Requirements
<br><ul>
<li>Bachelors or Masters Degree in relevant field</li>
<li>3+ years within a customer-facing or consultative firm; preferably as a Solutions Consultant or Solutions Engineer</li>
<li>5+ years within a client-side environment leading research initiatives</li>
<li>Deep understanding of both qualitative and quantitative methodologies</li>
<li>Excellent written, oral, interpersonal, and presentation skills</li>
<li>Excellent analytical and problem solving skills</li>
<li>Ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously and be results oriented</li>
<li>Strong passion for CX practices, research, and technology</li>
<li>Experience with our product a plus!</li>
</ul>
Besides a great work environment and the opportunity to change the world, we offer competitive salary, benefits, plenty of perks, as well as stock options.We value diversity, and were proud to be an inclusive, equal opportunity workplace.
