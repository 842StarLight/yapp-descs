<p><b>
BRIEF POSITION SUMMARY:</b></p>
<p>The Inside Account Executive answers inbound calls to sell MSC products in accordance with MSC Culture and Customer Service Standards.</p>
<p><b>DUTIES and RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Exercises independent judgment to answer inbound calls to sell MSC products in accordance with MSC Culture and Customer Service Standards.</li>
<li>Resolves customer product and service issues by providing sales-related solutions utilizing common and alternative sources of information. Consults with other associates to locate the most effective sources of information.</li>
<li>Guides less experienced branch associates to resolve customer issues, increase product knowledge, and promote MSC Culture.</li>
<li>Provides customer service in accordance with MSC standards to ensure customer satisfaction, account retention, and revenue generation.</li>
<li>Communicates customer concerns to management to ensure the most effective and lasting problem resolution.</li>
<li>Builds customer relationships via exceptional levels of service to ensure customer satisfaction and retention.</li>
<li>Works closely with vendors to produce quotes, expedite orders, locate sources for product, and arrange product training.</li>
<li>Supports outside sales representative in various branch activities.</li>
<li>Resolves more complex credit and collections issues to enhance customer service.</li>
<li>Identify and upsell MSC products where applicable to satisfy customer needs.</li>
<li>Recommend team sales process improvements to drive profitability for each customer order.</li>
<li>Utilize WEBSOE and Salesforce.com software to increase sales and fuel customer satisfaction.</li>
<li>Provides customer service using all tools available including, but not limited to, email and the Internet.</li>
<li>Fosters the MSC Culture in the department and throughout the company to ensure fulfillment of MSC’s vision and unity of purpose.</li>
<li>Participation in special projects and performs additional duties as required</li>
</ul>
<p><b>EDUCATION and EXPERIENCE:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>A High School Diploma or the equivalent.</li>
<li>2+ years inbound/ outbound telephone sales, outside sales, or customer service experience required.</li>
<li>Industry experience preferred.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>SKILLS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Computer literacy and proficiency with email, various internet browsers and/or the Windows operating system required.</li>
<li>Knowledge of basic arithmetic and MSC-like products and the commercial procurement process is preferred.</li>
<li>Excellent customer service and sales skills are required.</li>
<li>The ability to work a varying schedule required.</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communications skills are also required.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>OTHER REQUIREMENTS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>A valid driver’s license is required.</li>
<li>The ability to lift up to 50lbs may be required based on branch location (not all branches)</li>
<br>
</ul>
