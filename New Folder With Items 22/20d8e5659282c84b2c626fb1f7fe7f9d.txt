<h2>Office Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Purpose: </b></p>
<p>Assure that all aspects of the business of this assisted living center are managed in a timely and accurate manner. Serves as a Good Will Ambassador for this living center.</p>
<p><b>Qualifications: </b></p>
<ul><li>Must possess a high degree of interpersonal relationship skills, and be capable of relating to a variety of people and personalities. Considerable initiative and judgment is required. Word processing and clerical skills, pleasant telephone manner and greeting skills as well as experience in residential services to seniors, leasing, sales or marketing preferred. Must have a positive and respectful attitude toward the care of the frail elderly, ability to read, write and carry out directions; maturity to deal effectively with the demands of the job in a professional and confidential manner.</li></ul>
<p><b>Essential Functions: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Business Practices</b>- Manage accounts payable and accounts receivable (rent collection) according to policy and within budget parameters; maintain appropriate files and documentation as directed. Make daily bank deposits; prepare and submit weekly packet to corporate office; Notifies Administrator and department managers of appointments or guests; maintains petty cash fund and accounting; compiles credit card receipts and reconciles to purchase statements; orders and maintains inventory of office and business supplies; Receives mail and assures it is distributed the same day for residents and managers; maintains spreadsheet of work orders and apartment maintenance;</li>
<li>
<b>Human Resources</b> – pre-screens applicants for employment, processes application and hire documents, including criminal history checks, maintains personnel files and records as directed. Process payroll in coordination with ORBIS. ON-SHIFT schedule creator and manager for all hourly personnel – including training staff in proper utilization of the systems within ON SHIFT. Coordinates workers’ compensation paperwork and reports; Prepares cover sheet and basic information in employee performance reviews (90 day and annual), provides to department manager and administrator to assure timely processing of reviews for employees; maintain spreadsheet of employee attendance –notifying managers of absences;; assure posting of compliance posters as required by local, state and federal labor laws.</li>
<li>
<b>Sales and Marketing</b> – greeting of guests to this living center (via telephone, in person, or email), provide information and tours to prospective residents and inquiries; may assist in outreach marketing as directed. Tracks response to advertising and marketing efforts; Tracks inquiries for residency – including referral source, contact name and number/address/ email; Follow up on inquiries as directed; Develop and maintain a mailing list of prospects and referral sources; may assist to participate in senior housing fairs and expos; Complies with Fair Housing requirements.</li>
<li>
<b>Resident information</b> – maintain resident data base (from initial inquiry through end of residence); maintain emergency information; prepare resident chart “face” sheets in ECP and in the data base; Prepares and distributes weekly or monthly resident assessments due, logging completed information into the resident data base;</li>
<li>
<b>Supervision and Delegation</b><i>: </i>The Office Manager is the supervisor of receptionist staff. Routine tasks may be delegated to the Receptionist providing adequate training and supervision is provided to assure consistent quality performance. Prepares and Processes Employee Performance Reviews: 90 day and annual.</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul><li>Office Management: 2 years (Preferred)</li></ul>
<p>Education:</p>
<ul><li>High school or equivalent (Preferred)</li></ul>
<p>Work authorization:</p>
<ul><li>United States (Preferred)</li></ul>
<p>Work Location:</p>
<ul><li>One location</li></ul>

<li>Dental insurance</li>

<li>Paid time off</li>

</ul>

</ul>
