<p>
This program will provide co-ops and interns with hands on technical training and mentorship by the Boomi professional services team. Our professional services team is a balanced effort of technical product and services knowledge as well as consultative skills. Dell Boomi is also building connections between our Boomi Unified Platform and our Military Community. We have teamed up with Onward to Opportunity (institute for veterans and military families) to develop a Foundational Certification Training program available to veterans who are pursuing a technical degree for either a co-op or summer internship.</p>
