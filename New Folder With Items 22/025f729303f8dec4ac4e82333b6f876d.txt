<h2>Product Support Technician</h2>

<p>Start your new career as a <b>Product Support Technician</b> with <b>MAU at Paxton Access Inc.</b>, in Greenville, SC. <p><b>What You’ll Do As A Product Support Technician: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provide a world class level of service to our customers at all times, through various means of communication</li>
<li>Use technical knowledge and expertise gained through training and/or prior experience to resolve customer queries and to trouble shoot to ensure similar issues are avoided.</li>
<li>Escalate customer queries to senior support colleagues where appropriate</li>
<li>Achieve targets as defined within the team Key Performance Indicators</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Taking a minimum of 40 calls per day * Log and process all communication, both inbound and outbound</li>
<li>Maintain a professional approach to work at all times</li>
<li>Assist with research and marketing projects for new and existing products following the Support service level agreements</li>
<li>Consistently receive positive customer feedback</li>
