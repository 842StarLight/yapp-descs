<h2>Business Data Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><b>POSITION PURPOSE:</b></div>
<div>
The Strategy department is responsible for understanding, developing, and planning the future of the infrastructure and flow of data within the organization, as well as being integral to the strategic vision of creating a culture of data-driven decision-making. The Business Data Analyst will support the Strategy team by leveraging internal and external data through exploratory data analysis, statistical modeling, and data visualization in order to provide insights into organizational performance and complex business problems. The role will report directly to the Director of Strategy and Analytics.</div>
<div>
</div>
<div>
<b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Work independently and collaboratively to understand business processes, underlying data, and reporting needs.</li>
<li>Create data visualizations, presentations, and reproducible reports that communicate methodology and key findings for internal use.</li>
<li>Create workflows to manage and integrate internal and external data sources.</li>
<li>Develop and monitor reporting of ticket sales, partnerships, and marketing performance.</li>
<li>Analyze effectiveness of marketing tactics and sales campaigns.</li>
<li>Assist in the implementation and maintenance of analytics and business intelligence software.</li>
<li>Analyze ad-hoc projects and other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>

SPECIFIC JOB KNOWLEDGE, SKILL AND ABILITY:</b></div>

<li>Ability to work independently and complete duties and projects with minimal direct supervision.</li>

<li>Ability to work under above-average pressure in meeting urgent deadlines.</li>
