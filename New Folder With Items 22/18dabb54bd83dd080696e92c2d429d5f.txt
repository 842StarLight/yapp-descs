<p><b>Morgan Bronze Products</b> is looking for a <b><i>Manufacturing/Quality Technician</i></b> to join our Quality Team!<br>

<p><b>JOB PURPOSE</b></p>
<p>This position serves multiple functions within the quality and sales department and include various administrative duties. This person must have the ability to multi-task through a variety of responsibilities. People skills are essential as there is interaction between all employees, customers and vendors.</p>

<ul>
