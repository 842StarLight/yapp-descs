<h2>Business Development Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Work for a company we're partnered with! Ntroduced.com is a free referral platform that introduces sales professionals directly to hiring managers at companies around Boston.</p>
<p>Our partner sells an award winning SaaS customer journey analytics platform which enables customer experience and marketing teams to easily connect the dots between customer interactions and business outcomes across channels, programs, and time.</p>
<p>As a Business Development Representative you’d be responsible for uncovering lucrative new business opportunities by researching target accounts and contacts, identifying key engagement opportunities, starting the conversation, creating qualified sales leads, and in some cases carrying them through to closing.</p>
<p>Ideally, you have the following skills:</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree (required)</li>
<li>1-2 years’ experience in a SaaS-based sales development position or selling to senior executives</li>
<li>Proven ability to aggressively prospect via both phone and email</li>
<li>Consistently meet and exceed quota (on activities such as campaigns responses, calls, meetings but also qualified opportunities)</li>
<li>Experience leveraging LinkedIn and other prospecting tools</li>
<li>Knowledge of CRM and opportunity management systems, preferably Salesforce.com</li>
<li>Strong capacity to articulate industry-specific value proposition to address common vertical pain points</li>
<li>Experience in the SaaS analytics space is a plus</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Experience working within a account-based (ABM) approach is a plus</li>
</ul>
<p>You will be a great fit if you:</p>
<ul>
<li>Are curious, passionate and eager to learn</li>
<li>Crave success and will stop at nothing short of excellence</li>
<li>Are a strong team player with a “can do” attitude</li>
<li>Have impeccable organizational skills and consider yourself a self-starter</li>
<li>Want to be a key part of a start-up looking to make a mark on the world</li>
<li>Thrive in an open, collaborative, and ever-evolving environment</li>
</ul>
<p>Our partner would be a great fit if you:</p>
<ul>
<li>Want to be a critical part of a dynamic and professional team that is taking a disruptive solution to the market</li>
<li>Are looking to challenge yourself in new ways and advance your career</li>
<li>Are seeking a competitive salary</li>
