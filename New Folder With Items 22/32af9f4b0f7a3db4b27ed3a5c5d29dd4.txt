<h2>Electrical Technician</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Evapco, Inc., an employee owned company, is currently seeking an Electrical Technician at our Greenup, IL manufacturing site. Our Greenup, IL site is a multi-shift facility that employs approximately 600 people and is a manufacturer of the highest quality products for the evaporative cooling and industrial refrigeration markets.</p>
<p><b>Position Summary: </b></p>
<p>The responsibilities of the Electrical Technician are to assist with wiring of units, as well as electrical testing and trouble shooting. This position may also be needed to work in various areas of the plant to assist with other electrical/maintenance activities.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Maintains positive, cheerful, and professional behavior on a daily basis</li>
<li>Meet the productivity/efficiency, quality, safety, and housekeeping/5S goals set by the department supervisor</li>
<li>Ensure units are wired correctly</li>
<li>Ensure units are electrically tested and all instruments and devices are in complete working order prior to shipment</li>
<li>Assist the team in problem-solving</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Position Challenges &amp; Latitude: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Punctual and dependable</li>
<li>Set an “example of excellence” in all areas of work</li>
<li>Effectively respond to “unplanned” events</li>
<li>Self-starter and motivated</li>
<li>Respected by all department employees</li>
<li>Effectively communicate job processes and expectations to team members</li>
<li>Adhere to all company policies and work rules</li>
<li>Stay abreast of process and work instruction changes and updates</li>
<li>Accountable for the quality of the electrical portion of the unit</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Knowledge, Skills, &amp; Experience: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum two years of electrical experience with controls/electronics with technical degree preferred</li>
<li>Reading and understanding of engineering drawings and BOM's</li>
<li>Reading and understanding of electrical one-line diagrams, wiring schematics, control schematics, instrument loop diagrams, P&amp;ID's (Process &amp; Instrumentation Diagrams), and PLC'c (Programmable Logic Controls)</li>
<li>Use and understanding of various electrical meters including volt meter, amp probe, ohm meter, and 4-20 mA</li>
<li>Understand basic I/O functions</li>
<li>Familiar with Lockout/Tagout rules</li>
<li>Ability to recognize and solve problems</li>
<li>Word and Excel knowledge and the ability to learn all Evapco software-based programs</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Work Environment &amp; Physical Requirements: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Industrial setting (i.e. factory, outdoor)</li>
<li>Temperature extremes. In general, from 20 degrees F to 110 degrees F</li>
<li>Requires very good agility (i.e. bending, standing walking, climbing, reaching, squatting, twisting, and lifting)</li>
<li>Normal work week Monday through Friday, Saturday work likely, some Sunday work possible</li>
</ul>
