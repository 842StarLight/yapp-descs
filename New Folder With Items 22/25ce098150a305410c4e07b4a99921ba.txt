<b>
Our Company:</b><br>
<br>
At Spectrum Enterprise, our goal is to foster an engaging work environment that encourages our team members to reach their full potential. We promote a culture of excellence that celebrates diversity, innovative thinking and dedication to consistently exceeding client expectations.<br>
<br>
Spectrum Enterprise is a national provider of fiber-based technology solutions that believes connectivity is fundamental to the progress of every organization. We are committed to helping our clients achieve their business goals by providing the digital infrastructure that enables them to outperform. Our portfolio includes fiber Internet, Voice, Video, TV and cloud Infrastructure solutions as well as network and managed services. For more information visit enterprise.spectrum.com<br>
<br>
<b>Highlights:</b><br>
<br>
As the Director of Sales Tools and Applications, you improve sales productivity and effectiveness by developing, coordinating and implementing sales enablement tools, automation and initiatives. You achieve revenue growth and maximize sales efficiency by serving as the liaison between Field Sales, Sales Operations, Marketing, Learning and Development, Operations and project management teams.<br>
<br>
You ensure successful tool and application implementations by monitoring key performance metrics and reporting to senior management. You have a keen ability to identify roadblocks and overcome obstacles to see projects completed on time and within budget. You thrive in an office environment and travel as needed to achieve company objectives. You report directly to the Sr Dir, Sales Tool &amp; Applications for goals and guidance.<br>

What you will do:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Promote company-wide success through active and consistent support of all efforts to simplify and enhance the client experience.</li>
<li>
Spearhead sales improvement initiatives by providing strategic and tactical support of the development of sales enablement tools and managing essential sales automation, implementation and execution.</li>
<li>
Drive projects by translating business needs into requirements and ensure Project Management and IT teams understand the business issues for solutions.</li>
<li>
Improve sales by leading the assessment, recommendation, selection and deployment of sales enabling tools and technologies.</li>
<li>
Mitigate risks by anticipating the impact on other tools and cross-functional teams.</li>
<li>
Ensure data integrity between client relationship management (CRM) tools and billing systems through an in-depth analysis.</li>
<li>
Enhance the CRM tools through management and direction of improvement projects.</li>
<li>
Optimize the effectiveness of sales and support tools by collaborating with Sales and Sales Operations.</li>
<li>
Identify process improvement opportunities and system solutions by engaging with cross-functional business leaders.</li>
<li>
Support complex enterprise initiatives by serving as the integration point.</li>
<li>
Improve the client experience and reduce durations and fall out through the identification and implementation of essential automation and productivity improvements.</li>
<li>
Lead change across the Sales department by preparing and articulating business needs to cross-functional teams and leadership.</li>
<li>
Ensure projects and system solutions are successfully implemented through the facilitation and engagement of leadership cooperation.</li>
<li>
Analyze gaps and root cause analysis of data integrity between CRM tools and other systems through decisive management of a team of analysts.</li>
<li>
Perform additional duties related to this position as assigned.</li>
</ul>
