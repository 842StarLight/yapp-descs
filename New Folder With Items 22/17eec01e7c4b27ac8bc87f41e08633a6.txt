<h2>Assistant Controller</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>QAD is seeking an <b>Assistant Controller.</b> This position will be responsible for the external reporting of financial results, including 10K/10Q and other SEC filings. They will also Interpret, apply, documents, and effectively communicates technical information relating to US GAAP, Federal Securities Laws, and SEC accounting rules.<br>
<br>
<b>What You’ll Do:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>External financial reporting, including the 10K and 10-Q and other SEC filings</li>
<li>Interpret, apply, document and communicate technical information including US GAAP, Federal Securities Law and SEC accounting rules</li>
<li>Primary contact/lead for quarterly and annual audits with our external auditors and other regulatory audits when applicable</li>
<li>Manage consolidations, foreign currency and global monthly/quarterly close</li>
<li>Management of global finance calendar and communication of monthly close</li>
<li>Global equity administration and reporting</li>
<li>Global treasury operations, including cash management, collections, debt, insurance and foreign currency management</li>
<li>Manage statutory reporting compliance</li>
<li>Manage intercompany transactions, transfer pricing processes</li>
<li>Establish, communicate and administer global policies and procedures</li>
<li>Acquisitions and other special transactions</li>
<li>Reporting of goodwill, intangibles, capitalized software</li>
<li>Audit committee preparation, board books, proxy and annual report</li>
<li>Build relationships with and provide ongoing guidance and training in finance, sales, legal and operations</li>
<li>Responsible for the Hyperion system</li>
<li>Responsible for Global Reporting and analytics</li>
<li>Design, implement and monitor SOX internal controls as necessary</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
<b>What You’ll Need:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>BA or BS degree with emphasis on Finance or Accounting – CPA or equivalent required</li>
<li>Minimum 10 Years of accounting experience in a public company – preferably within the last 5 years – SEC reporting as well as for a CPA firm</li>
<li>Advanced proficiency in MS Office Suite, particularly EXCEL</li>
<li>Prefer knowledge of Hyperion</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
<b>Who You Are:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Someone with strong interpersonal skills, effective ability to manage and influence others, analytical thinking</li>
<li>Proactive, positive, high performer</li>
<li>Likes working in a diverse corporate environment with a global presence</li>
<li>Someone who enjoys collaborative work and demonstrates our QAD core values of partnership with our customers, commitment to excellence, innovation, global teamwork, integrity, and respect for the individual</li>
</ul>
