<h2>Sales Associate, J.Crew Factory, Lee</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>SALES ASSOCIATE
</b></p>
<p><b>You’ve got to…</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Love the brand and ensure that passion is evident in everything you do.</li>
<li>
Make the best first impression; smile, welcome and connect with customers authentically.</li>
<li>
Bring your best to everything you do and achieve your goals and KPIs.</li>
<li>
Always be flexible, up for anything and ready to have fun along the way.</li>
<li>
Look under rocks, be curious, ask questions, and think boldly.</li>
<li>
Make the most of every moment and be energized by multi-tasking.</li>
<li>
Be technologically savvy, while also knowing that devices don’t dominate the dialogue.</li>
<li>
Build productive relationships with everyone on the team and always respect each other.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
We’ll want you to…</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Ace training, use product knowledge tools, participate in fit sessions and put those experiences to use.</li>
<li>
Stay on top of promotions and share product value on the selling floor and in the fitting room.</li>
<li>
Provide an engaging and efficient checkout experience.</li>
<li>
Introduce customers to Loyalty and open at least one J.Crew Card per shift.</li>
<li>
Achieve Enterprise Selling targets by adhering to Standard Operating Procedures.</li>
<li>
Achieve or exceed task-specific time standards.</li>
<li>
Learn our systems and gadgets and use them effectively.</li>
<li>
Assist in processing and replenishing – our customers should always see us at our best.</li>
<li>
Share feedback, insights, and ideas with the management team.</li>
<li>
Act in a manner that aligns with our values.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Oh, and by the way, you</b>…</p>
<ul>
<li>
Are at least 18 years old.</li>
<li>
Communicate clearly and with purpose.</li>
<li>
Are available when we are busy, including: nights, weekends and holidays.</li>
<li>
Can bend, reach, and stretch for product, as well as lift, carry and move at least 40 pounds |18 kilos.</li>
<li>
Can regularly move around all store areas and be accessible to customers.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Before we wrap, a word about a few of our way cool perks…</b></p>
<p>
Amazing discounts on clothes and accessories (even new arrivals), flexible days and hours and 401(k)*.</p>
