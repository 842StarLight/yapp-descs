<h2>Associate Product Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div><div><div><div><div>
<div><b>Role Summary:</b></div>
<div>
The Exclusive Brands (ExB) Associate Product Manager is responsible for holistic product category management of assigned product categories, ensuring that products developed under the area of responsibility meet or exceed Best Buyâ€™s unique customer needs and deliver the appropriate value proposition for the associated brand. This role will partner closely with Merchant teams for assigned product categories in the development and alignment of category strategies, and lead the ExB global cross-functional team, providing clear direction on priorities and objectives in executing the category strategy.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>
Key Responsibilities:</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>
<br>
1. Product Category Management:</div>
<ul>
<li>
Establish self as the category expert in assigned product categories, including a deep understanding of customer needs and expectations, competitors and their weapons, and related market/technology trends for products within area of responsibility.</li>
<li>
Develop strong working relationships with Merchant team partners, closely collaborating and aligning on category strategies, and ensuring that appropriate brand standards are adhered to.</li>
<li>
Develop and regularly update category strategies and product roadmaps, ensuring that all global stakeholders, including Merchant partners, regional Category Managers, global cross-functional team members and ExB senior leadership, are aware of and understand the strategy.</li>
<li>
Utilize established Product Development Process and related tools to effectively and efficiently move products under area of responsibility through the entire product lifecycle, from concept to end-of-life.</li>
<li>
Develop and maintain a master product development schedule, providing project status visibility to the global cross-functional team, regional country houses and ExB leadership, and ensuring that new products are developed on schedule and budget.</li>
<li>
Lead regular rhythm of business review meetings with all stakeholders to ensure maximum effectiveness of product assortments.</li>
<li>
Serve as a steward of our brands and evangelist of our product development capability across the enterprise.</li>
<li>
Identify product and/or category opportunities to drive incremental ExB and Enterprise growth.</li>
<li>
Manage third-party agencies and non-ExB enterprise support capabilities in executing packaging, documentation, marketing related activities, potential Legal resolutions, etc. for products within assigned categories.</li>
</ul>
<div>
2. Budgets:</div>
<ul>
<li>
Collaborate closely with Merchant partners and lead ExB cross-functional team in building category specific annual financial budgets.</li>
<li>
Responsible for ensuring accurate category financial forecasting in the enterprise MAPP system for all metrics, channels, processes and timelines.</li>
<li>
Closely monitor category financial performance through the monthly forecasting (version) process, identify and explain variances between budgeted/forecasted/actual sales results, and lead action planning with Merchant partners and ExB global cross-functional team for quick course correction in category strategies where needed to achieve financial goals.</li>
<li>
Manages costs associated with category product development (NRE, travel, third-party labor, etc.), with responsibility to deliver category strategies within budget.</li>
</ul>
<div>
3. People Leadership:</div>
<ul>
<li>
Lead the ExB global cross-functional team in execution of category strategies, clearly setting direction, expectations and priorities for the team to focus on.</li>
<li>
Lead the ExB global cross-functional team in following the established Product Development Process to ensure projects are delivered within schedule and budget, while continuously seeking ways to improve efficiency and effectiveness.</li>
<li>
Provide direct or indirect managerial direction to Product Management Analyst support team.</li>
<li>
Mentors Product Management Analysts and others outside of the Product Management team and ExB department.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Experience:</b></div>
<ul><li>
2+ years of product development or related experience</li></ul>
