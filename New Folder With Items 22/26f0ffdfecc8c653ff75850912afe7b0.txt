<h2>NCR Local Office General Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>About NCR
</b></p>
<p>NCR Corporation (NYSE: NCR) is a leading software- and services-led enterprise provider in the financial, retail, hospitality, telecom and technology industries. NCR is headquartered in Atlanta, Ga., with 34,000 employees and does business in 180 countries. NCR is a trademark of NCR Corporation in the United States and other countries.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>TITLE <b>: NCR Local Office General Manager
</b></p>
<p>LOCATION <b>: Denver, CO
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>We serve the most exciting and dynamic customers in the world – the North American hospitality SMB market where almost 8 out of 10 restaurateurs own a single location. And…we put them at the center of everything we do.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>In this role you will be responsible for the sales, services and operations of the newly established NCR Local Office Rocky Mountain team and the 60+ employees that work there to serve the hospitality technology needs to existing and new Aloha and Silver customers in the region
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Locally we operate many similar Local Offices in major US markets and are deeply committed to our SMB customer base.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>POSITION SUMMARY &amp; KEY AREAS OF RESPONSIBILITY:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul><li>Position is responsible for operationalizing and executing against NCR’s strategy to further extend the company's position and profile in the the Rocky Mountain region; Required to develop, lead and manager NCR’s direct organization in selling, deploying and servicing NCR restaurant solutions in the assigned geographies. A keen focus on ensuring world class customer service is critical.
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<ul><li>Full responsibility includes orders, revenue, profit, and effective management to organize and execute a complete business that drives maximum cash flow with the available resources. Everything is done with a strategy and approach that aligns to our people, culture, values and growth vision.
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<ul><li>Works with senior management to develop the local office strategy and with other local office leaders develop, share and implement best practices.
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<ul><li>Responsible for creating market potential and opportunities for our solutions, within NCR hospitality business; Providing the vision, inspiration and leadership necessary to drive commercial success; Providing input to strategic decisions that affect functional area of responsibility
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<ul><li>Responsible for implementing the strategic goals and objectives of the Company, giving direction and leadership towards the achievement of the organization's vision, mission goals and initiatives
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<ul><li>Provide leadership direction to all functions within the assigned area, to grow profitability; Develop, build, inspire and motivate sales and sales support teams through passionate leadership.
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<ul><li>Formulate and advocate a people management strategy and high performance culture which includes recruitment, development and retention of top talent; Foster and lead change, and create a mindset of innovation and efficiency.
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>BASIC QUALIFICATIONS:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Strong team management experience overseeing either Hospitality Software Sales and/or POS Technology.
</li>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree
</li>
<li>Minimum of 8 years of managerial experience as a second and higher level manager
</li>
<li>Proven track record in sales leadership
</li>
<li>Ability to work in an organized; action-driven fashion
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS:
</b></p>
<ul><li>MBA Degree preferred
</li></ul>
