<h2>UI/UX Designer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>WHO WE ARE</b><br>
Based in Los Angeles, CA, eatOS is a venture-backed, cloud-based Restaurant Software platform including Point of Sale, Self-Service Kiosks, Mobile and Online Ordering, Scheduling and Inventory Management.</p>
<p>
We build world-class software that empowers restaurants to succeed, from food trucks to enterprise restaurants with 100+ locations.</p>
<p>
We’re on a mission to disrupt a market of old, slow, expensive POS products and bring innovative products to worldwide restaurants.</p>
<p><b>
WHAT DO WE SEEK</b></p>
<p>
We are looking for an experienced and creative UI/UX Designer to join our team! As UI/UX Designer, you will be responsible for delivering the best online user experience, which makes your role extremely important for our success and ensuring customer satisfaction and loyalty.</p>
<p>
You will be designing ideas using various methods and the latest technology. You will be designing graphic user interface elements, like menus, tabs, forms, and widgets.</p>
<p>
Also, you will design, conceptualize, recommend, and implement design changes, content enhancements, and mobile/website layout including future community-building initiatives. You will work in a fast-moving, agile environment where you will coordinate your efforts with project managers &amp; developers.</p>
<p>
This is an entry/mid-level position.</p>
<p><b>
Key Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Conceptualize and build from scratch UI/UX for Mobile Apps</li>
<li>Conceptualize and build from scratch UI/UX for Websites and other digital material</li>
<li>Plan and run A/B tests to optimize user engagement</li>
<li>Plan and run usability testing on mobile and desktop applications</li>
<li>Propose and design new ideas for enhancing engagement</li>
<li>Review user research and/or user testing data to design improvements needed to the user interface</li>
<li>Participate, lead, and program all phases of the development process for user interface projects – from idea conception, to wire-frames and prototyping, and to design and implementation</li>
<li>Collaborate closely with Marketing, Management, and Technology teams to deliver hypothesis-driven designs and coding to improve the user interface</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Knowledge and Skill Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to design and implement beautiful user interfaces. Mastery of semantic Adobe products is a must</li>
<li>An advanced understanding of user-centered design, usability, as well as aesthetic design principles.</li>
<li>Minimum 2 years of interface design experience or related degree</li>
<li>A well-rounded understanding of online marketing and web usability best practices</li>
<li>A strong portfolio, available for viewing, showing the great sense of design, creativity, and good communication skills</li>
<li>Highly motivated, self-directed, enthusiastic, and truly passionate about user-centered design and user experience</li>
<li>Creativity with an eye towards detail and perfection of user experience</li>
<li>Professional, team player with strong interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Ability to learn new technology quickly</li>
</ul>
<p><b>A Plus if you have/you are:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Experience working at a startup</li>
<li>Undergraduate Degree from a top university or equivalent professional experience</li>
</ul>
