<h2>Creative Play Professional-On Call, Rockingham</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div><div><div>
<p><b><i>General Summary:
</i></b></p>
<p>Presents demonstrations, shows, programs, Museum activities, and performs admissions-related operations at the front desk.</p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities
</b></p>
<p>Conduct programs, presentations, demonstrations, and lead play activities in a variety of settings</p>
<ul>
<li>Interpret programs for children in various themes</li>
<li>Utilize Museum displays to augment standard teaching methods</li>
<li>Select and assemble materials to be used in daily demonstrations and programming</li>
<li>Adapt content and complexity to ages and interests of children</li>
<li>Plan content for a variety of programming and methods of presentation, and prepare outlines of materials to be covered for submission to the Program Specialist</li>
<li>Conduct public shows, programs, and demonstrations on a daily basis</li>
<li>Facilitate school classes, workshops, and camps as needed</li>
<li>Display a high level of enthusiasm, energy, and positive behavior to children and their caregivers</li>
<li>Evaluate success of experiences through feedback provided by guests</li>
</ul>
<p>Work as a front-line customer service representative at the Admissions desk</p>
<ul>
<li>Greet guests as they enter the Museum</li>
<li>Check in guests and sell retail items through the point-of-sale systems at the front desk</li>
<li>Accurately perform admissions transactions/reporting and follow cash handling procedures</li>
<li>Conduct group orientations</li>
<li>Inform guests of daily and special programming, learning opportunities, Membership, and special events</li>
<li>Provide input to management on feedback and information from customers</li>
<li>Monitor inventory levels of consumables at the front desk and within the Museum and notify management of needs when apparent</li>
<li>Process Membership sales</li>
</ul>
<p>Monitor the Museum exhibits, activities, and environment to ensure safety</p>
<ul>
<li>Perform exhibit checks and either correct problems or report them to management</li>
<li>Enforce safety and other policies/procedures within the Museum</li>
<li>Assist in emergency response procedures</li>
<li>Perform daily housekeeping routines for Museum activities and exhibits</li>
<li>Provide detailed cleaning, sanitization, and organization of exhibits and areas during down time</li>
<li>Maintain a clean and orderly work environment</li>
</ul>
<p>Provide a high level of customer service</p>
<ul>
<li>Approach and interact with guests in a respectful and professional manner</li>
<li>Display care and concern when dealing with any guest issue</li>
<li>Always communicate and maintain a positive attitude (verbally and nonverbally)</li>
<li>Respond promptly to customer needs</li>
</ul>
<p>Demonstrate behavior consistent with the organizational culture</p>
<ul>
<li>Continually maintain an outwardly positive demeanor toward coworkers and visitors</li>
<li>Maintain a team-oriented approach and can-do attitude</li>
<li>Assist other team members and departments in their responsibilities when help is needed</li>
<li>Display flexibility in an ever-changing environment</li>
<li>Assist in training and share business-related knowledge with fellow employees</li>
</ul>
