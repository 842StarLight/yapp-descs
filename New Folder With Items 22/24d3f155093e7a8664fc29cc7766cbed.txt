<p>POSITION TITLE: Member Services Team Member</p>
<p>DIRECT REPORT: Member Services Manager</p>
<p>LOCATION: RDV Sportsplex Athletic Club</p>
<p>DEPARTMENT: Member Services</p>
<p>POSITION STATUS: Part-time, hourly position</p>
<p>COMPENSATION: $9 per hour</p>
<br>
<p></p>

Primarily responsible for delivering exceptional customer service to existing and potential members to effect retention and new membership sales.</p>

<li>Assist members and guests with reservations, account billing questions, comments and concerns as appropriate.</li>
<li>Ability to provide superior customer service in a timely, professional, and courteous manner.</li>
<li>Develop and maintain a complete working knowledge of the entire operation; keep current on all programming, events, and functions at the facility.</li>
<li>Ensures telecommunications procedures are carried out in a professional and timely fashion.</li>
<li>Assist members and guests with registering for our programs in CSI and online.</li>
<li>Perform any point of sale procedures with CSI Software. Responsible for contents of cash drawer during a specified shift.</li>
<li>Effectively communicate membersâ€™ concerns, comments, and questions to appropriate Manager.</li>
<li>Perform all job functions at an exceptional level while adhering to areas of accountability.</li>
<li>Familiar with merchandise lines and can explain products to customers and members.</li>
<li>Knowledge of product and departmental policies regarding returns and other customer service issues and can explain to customer.</li>
<li>Perform duties ranging from lifting, stocking, reaching, climbing, and organizing stock.</li>
<li>Keeps a constant clean towel supply by washing, drying and folding towels as needed.</li>
<li>Assists with member laundry, by following the member laundry procedures.</li>
<li>Maintains interior and exterior appearance by picking up all trash, dirty towels, hangers and other debris as needed.</li>

<ul>

<li>Excellent organizational skills.</li>
<li>Ability to manage multiple tasks.</li>
<li>Must be able to work extra hours, weekends, and holidays.</li>
<li>Ability to stand 95% of the shift and lift weighted objects up to 40 lbs.</li>
<li>Experience with telecommunications.</li>
<li>Proven track record of accountability and integrity.</li>
<li>Ability to handle multiple tasks and functions while maintaining a low personal stress level.</li>
<li>Physical Requirements: Bend, stoop, crouch, climb, stand, sit and turn/pivot; Lift/lower, push, pull and carry up to 40 lbs; perform office tasks using simple hand grasping, fine hand manipulation and reach associated with assigned tasks such as paperwork, typing, and/or word processing, filing, calculating and use of telephone; See, hear, and speak with sufficient capability to perform assigned tasks and maintain proper job safety conditions.</li>
<br>
