<h2>Videographer/Editor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><b>Job Summary:</b></div>
<div>
The Videographer/Editor will be responsible for the creation and distribution of video content for the Tucson AHL Affiliate of the Arizona Coyotes. This position will be responsible for demonstrating and maintaining a high standard of video creation. The Videographer/Editor will coordinate production schedules and organize cataloging and storage of media. In addition, this position will recommend software, hardware, and networking solutions to insure the most efficient production model. The Videographer/Editor position calls for a combination of creative vision, organizational ability and technical proficiency.</div>
<div>
</div>
<div><b>
Tasks and Responsibilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li> Primary shooter/editor for videos needed by Media Relations, Game Presentation, Ticket Sales, Corporate Sales, Marketing and Community Relations.</li>
<li>Shoot all home games for team website and AHL.com.</li>
<li>Provide digital copy to home and away teams of all home games.</li>
<li>Shoot videos as needed with players and coaches for team website and FTP site.</li>
<li>Coordinate production schedule.</li>
<li>Develop policies to organize video content.</li>
<li>Oversee loading of game-day elements to control room video servers.</li>
<li>Assist with creation/design of supporting sales materials (video packages, etc).</li>
<li>Provide creative pieces for select advertising and marketing campaigns (digital).</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
