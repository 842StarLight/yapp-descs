<h2>Senior Accountant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<p><b>Summary of Responsibilities
</b></p>
<p>Perform a wide variety of diversified accounting, tax and audit assignments under the supervision of different partners and senior accountants. Engage in all areas of the firmâ€™s tax, consulting and audit practice. Work closely with Partner-in-Charge on engagements.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Functions
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Record transactions in ledgers; prepare trial balances and simple financial statements using computer software.</li>
<li>Reconcile accounts to control accounts.</li>
<li>Perform scheduled computations of accruals, property and equipment, and calculate depreciation expense.</li>
<li>Prepare business and personal income tax returns, including appointments with clients.</li>
<li>Assist in proofing financial statements, tax returns and other documents.</li>
<li>Assist with responses to various taxing agencies.</li>
<li>Perform QuickBooks Consulting and maintain proficiency with software.</li>
<li>Prepare, or direct preparation of, complete financial statements and related reports for clients, including reviews and compilations.</li>
<li>Perform other accounting and tax duties as needed in engagements and as assigned by Partners.</li>
<li>Prepare payroll and sales tax returns.</li>
<li>Perform all audit functions and tests as prescribed in applicable audit programs.</li>
<li>Delegate specific audit programs and duties, providing training to junior staff members, review workpapers, financial statements and reports prior to final review.</li>
<li>Prepare proposals and responses to bid requests for prospective audit clients as well as fee quotations for tax, payroll and accounting services.</li>
<li>Maintain a working knowledge of all current auditing laws, statutes, etc.</li>
<li>Provide insight and feedback to Partner-In-Charge of audit engagement.</li>
<li>Prepare, or direct preparation of, complete financial statements and related reports for audit clients.</li>
<li>Provide training to Staff Accountants as necessary.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Other Functions</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Participate in community and volunteer organizations</li>
<li>Perform other duties as assigned by Partners</li>
<li>Network with local business owners and managers to promote CDS.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Working Conditions</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Overtime will be required.</li>
<li>Travel to client sites, meetings, and seminars will be necessary from time to time.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Education, Experience, and Skills Required
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Three or more years of accounting experience required.</li>
<li>Accounting degree required.</li>
<li>CPA designation, or desire to obtain the CPA designation, preferred.</li>
<li>Well-rounded knowledge of accounting and auditing principles and personal income tax.</li>
<li>Experienced in QuickBooks preferred.</li>
<li>Ability to communicate effectively and professionally with clients and other staff.</li>
<li>Flexible, team player.</li>
<li>Maintain strict confidentiality.</li>
</ul>
