<h2>Compliance Team Member: Focus on eCommerce policy enforcement</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Canyonwall is a fast growing e-commerce consulting company. We need a dynamic, project oriented, analytical person to be part of an e-commerce compliance team, driving compliance efforts for several brands. We are located in Provo UT with offices right across the road from the BYU Creamery.</p>
<p>
The successful candidate will be responsible for utilizing technology and research to assist partner companies with investigations related to online sales. He/she will review and update data to ensure compliance with federal and online marketplace regulations.</p>
<p>
Responsibilities and Duties</p>
<ul>
<li>Maintain data integrity of reports and support all Compliance functions by updating and maintaining e-commerce seller data.</li>
<li>Communicate with customers to evaluate and report on compliance issues related to Amazon and supplier policy violations.</li>
<li>Implement best practices and procedures, identifying areas of improvement.</li>
<li>Develop &amp; execute daily, weekly &amp; monthly action plans that increase compliance results.</li>
<li>Analyze policy trends and work with other members of internal teams to support client needs.</li>
<li>Identify and develop solutions for removal of inauthentic inventory.</li>
<li>Present a unified enforcement experience to clients.</li>
<li>Qualifications and Skills.</li>
</ul>
<p>The Potential Applicant must have an</p>
<ul>
<li>Understanding of how the Internet works with an interest in innovation, compliance investigations, fraud risk management, and e-commerce.</li>
<li>Intermediate to advanced understanding of Excel.</li>
<li>Attention to detail, the ability to analyze information quickly, and communicate professionally.</li>
<li>Be self-driven, organized, and have a proactive mindset.</li>
<li>Ability to build relationships and work cross functionally within partner teams.</li>
<li>Excitement for working in a start-up environment with high level change.</li>
<li>Ability to quickly pick up various software systems and data analysis tools.</li>
<li>Strong project management and continuous improvement skills.</li>
<li>Customer service obsession.</li>
<li>Proven ability to quickly adapt and create the right results using data.</li>
<li>Highest levels of integrity and ethics.</li>
</ul>
<p>The successful candidate will be smart, organized, and able to drive results without being micromanaged. We offer a flexible, results-focused, people-oriented work environment.</p>
