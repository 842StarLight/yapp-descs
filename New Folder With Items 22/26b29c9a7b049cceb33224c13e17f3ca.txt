<p><b>JOB DESCRIPTION</b></p>
<p><b>Summary/Objective</b></p>
<p>The Senior Commercial Loan Officer is responsible for managing a portfolio of existing commercial loan customers, including cross-selling new loans, products and services and analyzing loan requests from the existing customer base and from referrals.</p>
<p><b>Essential Functions</b></p>
<p>To accomplish this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform, with or without reasonable accommodation, each essential function satisfactorily. Reasonable accommodations may be made to help qualified individuals with disabilities perform the essential functions.</p>
<p>1. Manage an existing commercial and consumer portfolio as assigned.</p>
<p>2. Maintain current beneficial loan relationships and seek additional financing opportunities with existing customers.</p>
<p>3. Perform collection activities on existing and future commercial and consumer credits as needed. To include both customer and bank legal counsel.</p>
<p>4. Develop a thorough understanding of the bank’s current credit policies and procedures, as well as the loan policy.</p>
<p>5. Attend weekly past due, Business Development Officer and bank loan approval committee meetings, as well as the monthly loan committee meeting.</p>
<p>6. Complete a weekly pipeline report to be discussed during the weekly Business Development Officer meeting.</p>
<p>7. Complete all required forms, including but not limited to, Classified Asset Status Reports, as needed for regulatory or internal reporting purposes.</p>
<p>8. Utilizes sales management training and tools to identify and refer current and prospective clients.</p>
<p>9. Perform collection activities on existing and future commercial and consumer credits as needed. To include both customer and bank legal counsel Prospect for clients independently by utilizing referral sources, existing customers, Top 100 and centers of influence.</p>
<p>10. Exhibit excellent interpersonal and communication skills, providing an environment of open and authentic communication.</p>
<p>11. Adhere to all Legence Bank and AFMFS Hoopla standards, serving as an example to other team members, as well as to our customers.</p>
<p>12. Represent and promote the Company within the community through participation in bank sponsored events, philanthropic opportunities and civic events.</p>
<p>13. All other duties as assigned</p>
