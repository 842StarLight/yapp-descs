<h2>Administrative Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Location:</b> Irving, Texas
<br>
<br>
<b>Contech Engineered Solutions</b> is seeking an <b>Administrative Assistant</b> to join our busy office in Irving, Texas. The Administrative Assistant provides support to Sales and Field Marketing within the region, provides quality customer service, and performs general administration and business functions. This is full time employment opportunity offering comprehensive insurance plans, PTO, 401(k) and paid holidays!
<br>
<br>
Primary Responsibilities
<ul>
<li>Greet and assist visitors.</li>
<li>Receive and facilitate incoming calls directed to the operator.</li>
<li>Administer and operate phone system and essential office equipment, including the setup and coordination of company presentation / projector equipment.</li>
<li>Distribute mail and faxes, post mail, ship materials, including marketing mailings.</li>
<li>Order and maintain office supply inventory and equipment.</li>
<li>Sales, marketing and customer service support.</li>
<li>Research disputes / errors and other special projects.</li>
<li>Provides assistance and support for Inside Sales Coordinators.</li>
<li>Validates and completes commission requests for dealer programs in region.</li>
<li>Maintains region files to include contracts, purchase orders, payables, etc.</li>
<li>Updates region directory and State Web pages.</li>
<li>Orders and maintains office supplies for region.</li>
<li>Processes expense invoices for contract employees and codes all regional bills and expenses for processing.</li>
<li>Travel, schedule, seminar and meeting coordination.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<b>Required</b>
<ul>
<li>Required minimum education: High School Diploma or GED</li>
<li>At least 2 years Administrative experience.</li>
<li>Front desk receptionist experience.</li>
<li>Ability to write routine reports and correspondence. Ability to speak effectively with groups of customers or employees of the organization.</li>
<li>Ability to apply common sense understanding to carry out instructions furnished in written, oral, or diagram form.</li>
<li>Proficient knowledge of Word Processing software; Spreadsheet software and Database software.</li>
</ul>
