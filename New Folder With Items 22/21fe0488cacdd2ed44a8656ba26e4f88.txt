<h2>AppD Sr. Technical Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Sr. Technical Project Manager, Professional Services</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
About Us</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
AppDynamics is an application performance monitoring solution that uses machine learning and artificial intelligence (AI) to provide real-time visibility and insight into IT environments. With our unique AIOps solution, you can take the right action at exactly the right time with automated anomaly detection, rapid root-cause analysis, and a unified view of your entire application ecosystem, including private and public clouds. Using AppDynamics, you’ll finally align IT, DevOps, and the business around the information that helps you protect your bottom line and deliver flawless customer experiences at scale.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
About the Role</b></div>
<p>
Leads the implementation of AppDynamics enterprise software solutions, provides guidance to customers and ensures the successful delivery of project engagements. A successful project delivers tangible business outcomes for our customers and delights both internal and external stakeholders whilst maintaining healthy financial metrics (on-time, on budget, profitability). We expect this individual to actively keep abreast of the latest trends and best practices in enterprise software to build credibility with customers and instill confidence in the proposed solution. <b>This role will include up to 30% travel.</b></p>
<p>
You will exemplify these traits</p>
<ul>
<li><p>
Curious – Have desire to truly and intimately understand our customer's business problems</p></li>
<li><div>
Creative – In finding a sustainable path to yes</div></li>
<li><div>
Partnership – To both the customer and to our colleagues outside of services organization</div></li>
<li><div>
Technology First Mindset – We sell software for technologists and we speak their language</div></li>
<li><p>
Empathetic – Connect at a human level with our customers and be able to walk in their shoes</p></li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Key responsibilities</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li><div>
Drive requirement gathering and scoping, mapping technical solutions to business objectives</div></li>
<li><div>
Create detailed project plan and own and drive the successful execution</div></li>
<li><div>
Have good understanding of the AppDynamics solution and steps involved to ensure our customers realize the full value of their investment in our technology</div></li>
<li><div>
Manage a number of parallel software deployment projects for our customers (project plans, project governance, action items, risk log) and complete projects on time and on budget while delighting the customer</div></li>
<li><div>
Establish and lead recurring project status or governance meetings including preparation of materials and working with customer project manager and key internal and external stakeholders to review key project information and maintain visibility and accountability</div></li>
<li><div>
Periodically report, in a concise and actionable manner, project status both internally as well as to the customer and document project progress as per established best practices (meeting minutes, project plan updates, weekly status reports</div></li>
<li><div>
Guide customer as well as project team members to a successful business outcome by managing the client relationship and setting and managing customer expectations during implementations</div></li>
<li><div>
Act as a liaison between customer, project team as well as the rest of AppDynamics (sales, customer success, support, product and engineering) throughout the duration of an engagement</div></li>
<ul>
<li><div>
Identify and advocate for new product features on behalf of the customers</div></li>
<li><div>
Identify account expansion opportunities and support Sales to close them</div></li>
</ul>
<li><div>
Contribute to constant improvement of AppDynamics’ project management practices</div></li>
<li><div>
Maintain the project within the PSA system as well other tools including revenue forecasting</div></li>
<li><div>
Autonomously create change orders and services upsells as required and negotiate to close</div></li>
</ul>
