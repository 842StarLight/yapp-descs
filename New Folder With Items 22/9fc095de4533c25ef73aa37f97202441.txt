Call Center Team Lead
<br><br>
<b>Position Purpose:</b><br>
A-MAX Auto Insurance is seeking an energetic, innovative and motivated individual with an entrepreneurial spirit to join as a Call Center Team Lead. As a Call Center Team Lead you will utilize your knowledge of the Insurance industry to better position your team to win. Team Leads at A-MAX Call Center work closely with the Operations Manager to develop sales strategies and ensure that the Call Center runs effectively and efficiently.
<br><br>
<b>Job Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Manage a team of call center agents</li>
<li>Educate agents on our insurance products and services while identifying opportunities to sell additional products.</li>
<li>Ensure that customers and prospective customers are treated with respect, courtesy and professionalism</li>
<li>Handle escalated issues forwarded by your representatives, as needed</li>
<li>Monitor call queues and track inbound leads coming in through multiple sources. Keep representatives aware of inbound calls, calls waiting, abandonment rate, hold times etc.</li>
<li>Track outbound call activity for quotes, payments, renewals, reinstatements etc.</li>
<li>Audit the underwriting/policy paperwork of agents for accuracy and ensure that agents are caught up, with no open suspense.</li>
<li>Print cancellations, call renewals and quote call backs, distribute among team members to ensure agents are following up on leads and working on retention of existing customers</li>
<li>Work on daily accounting reconciliation reports as per guidelines from accounting department.</li>
<li>Motivate and encourage representatives through positive communication and feedback.</li>
<li>Conduct team huddles each day to discuss daily goals and strategies</li>
<li>Conduct weekly team meetings to discuss weekly and monthly goals, product updates, changes in underwriting guidelines and any other policy matters</li>
<li>Disseminate new product information to the team members</li>
<li>Listen to live and/or recorded phone calls and provide coaching/feedback to agents to help improve.</li>
<li>Review agent performance based KPIs of the department</li>
<li>Keep track of attendance, daily statistics, production, reviews, paid time off, sick time, etc.</li>
<li>Present to the management regular reporting of the team’s performance and suggestions for improvement</li>
<li>Work with Operations Manager to assess staffing needs within the call center.</li>
<li>Administer training programs for new hires and existing staff. Train representatives on the proper use of the Agency Management System and how to cross sell different products</li>
<li>Develop and implement contests, awards and themes that increase agents’ output and keeps them engaged</li>
<li>Establish monthly meetings with other departments to review call center operations.</li>
<li>Review and discuss computer hardware, software and database needs in a timely manner with Operations Manager and work with other departments at Corporate to resolve any issues</li>
</ul>

<li>3 - 5 years of supervisory experience</li>
<li>1- 3 years of Call Center/Sales Experience preferred</li>
<li>Advanced knowledge of Insurance Industry</li>
<li>Ability to work flexible schedule</li>
<li>TDI licensed (P&amp;C preferred)</li>
</ul>
<b>Core Competencies:</b><br>

<li>Good organizational and interpersonal skills with a strong customer focus.</li>
<li>Demonstrated intermediate to advanced written communication skills (spelling, grammar</li>
