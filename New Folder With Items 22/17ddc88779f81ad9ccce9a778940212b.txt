<h2>Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Seeking a motivated and self-driven sales professional to fill a key role for an Azure, Apps and Infrastructure Specialist. As a Specialist, you will lead a virtual team of technical, marketing, Partner and internal consulting resources to advance the sales process and achieve/exceed quarterly budget targets in your territory. The ideal candidate will have a proven track record of developing new business, building strong business relationships, driving Azure Cloud Services and partner integration. The ability to effectively present to a wide range and size of audiences from IT Pro to CxO to business decision makers is a must.
</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities</b></h2>
<div><b>
Responsibilities
</b></div>
<div>Key responsibilities include:
</div>
<ul>
<li>Creating and executing a business plan to ensure your product’s revenue and unit goals are achieved.</li>
<li>
Developing a healthy Azure pipeline of qualified opportunities.
</li>
<li>Develop in-depth understanding of the platform and related solutions and the value they bring in solving business problems.
</li>
<li>Ensure appropriate resources are engaged to help customers evaluate Microsoft Windows, Management and Azure solutions.
</li>
<li>Drive customer and partner satisfaction for your products and solutions. Connect enterprise customers and partners with the product groups.
</li>
<li>Contributing to the recruitment, engagement and readiness of Partners who can help the Solution Sales Professional drive business.
</li>
<li>Ensure rapid value realization through robust customer success plans.
</li>
<li>Team effectively with other sales and technical representatives, Partners, Microsoft Consulting Services and Marketing to meet your goals and customer needs.
</li>
<li>Understand Microsoft strengths relative to competition and effectively communicate those strengths to customers in a positive manner.
</li>
</ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications</b></h2>
<div><b>
Qualifications
</b></div>
<p>Requirements and skills for this position:</p>
<div><b>
Account Management &amp; Sales Acumen:
</b></div>
<ul><li>7+ years of selling of software (preferably cloud based) solutions to medium or large enterprises or local governments</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>
Demonstrated experience and expertise in selling technology solutions to senior business and IT decision makers by reinforcing the value of the technology to the customer’s overall business pain and/or strategic opportunities, including financial acumen.
</li>
<li>History of holding and consistently exceeding quota - Strong communications, presentation, negotiation and interpersonal skills.
</li>
<li>Ability to articulate the business value of the Microsoft platform and Azure in competitive scenarios.
</li>
<li>Demonstrated strategic time management and multitasking skills.
</li>
<li>Effectively manages sales pipeline with predictable business results that exceed revenue targets.
</li>
<li>High degree of collaboration with multiple Microsoft sales personnel, as well as Microsoft Partners will be required for success.
</li>
<li>Local government industry knowledge is a plus but not a requirement</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>
Effective territory/account management: planning, opportunity qualification and creation, stakeholder and executive communication, needs analysis, value engineering, services/partner engagement, opportunity management, pipeline management, large dollar licensing and deal negotiation <b>required</b>
</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>
<b>
Executive Presence.</b> Experience and expertise selling to senior business decision makers by aligning &amp; reinforcing the value of the solution to the customer’s overall business pain and/or strategic opportunities and decision criteria.</li>
<li>
Orchestrate and influence virtual teams to pursue sales opportunities and lead v-teams through influence
</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Technical</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
<b>
Azure Platform. </b>Understanding of Microsoft server products and/or complementing solutions. The position requires the ability to articulate and present the business value of Microsoft's solutions and have a firm understanding of Microsoft's strategies and products relative to major Microsoft competitors <b>preferred</b>
</li>
<li>
<b>
Competitive Landscape.</b> Knowledge of enterprise software solutions and application and infrastructure competitor landscape</li>
<li>
Understanding of partner ecosystems and the ability to leverage partner solutions to solve customer needs.
</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Education. Successful candidates will have a BS/BA degree. MBA preferable</li></ul>
