<p><b>Why Join Altec?</b>
</p>
<div><div><div>
<div>
<p>Altec Industries is seeking <b>Engineers</b> for our Focus Factory located in Roanoke, VA.</p>
<p><b>
Applications, </b><b>Manufacturing, and Quality Engineering</b> opportunities are available.</p>
<p>
Our mission is to profitably design, manufacture, and sell innovative custom products, services, and solutions to users of mobile telecom and utility equipment. From customer interaction and new product design to the implementation of manufacturing processes, Altec Engineers are leading the industry. If you possess strong design skills, a knack for problem solving, and a mechanical troubleshooting aptitude, this is the job for you.</p>
<p><b>
Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree from an ABET accredited college in a related discipline, excludes Engineering Technology.</li>
<li>EIT registration or ability to obtain registration.</li>
<li>Willing to travel up to 25% of the time, potentially on short notice, which may include flying.</li>
<li>Internal Candidates will be an Associate in good standing.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Desired Skills:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Experience with 2D (AutoCAD) and 3D (SolidWorks) modeling.</li>
<li>Ability to work with team members and work with minimal supervision.</li>
<li>Excellent written and communication skills.</li>
<li>Experience with Teamcenter or similar PLM software.</li>
<li>Knowledge of Lean Manufacturing and principles.</li>
<li>Detail oriented.</li>
<li>Customer service oriented.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Learn Altec’s product lines and systems/rules/processes required for different Engineering roles at Altec.</li>
<li>Practice prudent engineering and effectively apply formal engineering training adding to the overall effectiveness of the Engineering Group and enhancement of Altec’s position within the product line.</li>
<li>Participate and contribute productively as part of Altec’s team-oriented design, manufacture and customer service/support efforts.</li>
<li>Operate with appropriate entry-level decision-making latitude within the scope of an assignment.</li>
<li>Know and apply fundamental concepts, practices, and procedures in the engineering field.</li>
<li>Communicate with customers on issues of technical specifications, product design and operation as appropriate.</li>
<li>Provide technical support to sales associates.</li>
<li>Support Operations, Sales, and Service.</li>
<li>Interface directly with Suppliers and Customers.</li>
<li>Learn and Utilize Lean principles to improve our products and processes.</li>
<li>Work within any of the Altec departments as assigned to grow knowledge and skill for future Altec positions.</li>
<li>Participate in Continuous Improvement Initiatives.</li>
</ul>
<p>The job level is able to be adjusted at time of hire, if the candidate meets the qualifications for jobs of a more experienced level in the career path.</p>
<p><b>
Our Company:</b></p>
<p>
Altec specializes in the manufacture, sale, and service of aerial devices, digger derricks, cranes, and specialty equipment for the electric utility, telecommunications, tree care, construction, and light and sign maintenance industries in more than 120 countries.</p>
<p>
At Altec, advanced technology efforts are aimed, without exception, at helping customers work “Safer and Smarter.” Altec invests more resources than any other manufacturer in the advancement of safety, reliability, uptime and low cost of ownership.</p>
<p>
Founded in 1929, Altec is an innovative, financially sound company that is setting the standard for excellence in design, manufacturing and service; and that’s why you can rely on us to provide you with the stability of a well-managed company.</p>
