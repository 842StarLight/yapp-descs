<div>OVERVIEW</div>
<div>
FieldEdge is the leading provider of Field Service Management SaaS solutions to the $1.2billion service contractor market. We have helped thousands of service-based organizations increase revenue, sales performance and overall workforce productivity by managing all aspects of their business and daily operations through our industry leading SaaS platform. We estimate that only 40% of the market current utilizes software, which should have you seeing $$$ as a sales professional.</div>
<div>
</div>
<div>
As a technical sales engineer, the number one goal is customer retention which is accomplished by setting the right expectations through product and industry expertise during the sales process. Sales Engineers at FieldEdge support the sales teams and customers through engagement activities. Sales Engineers are trusted advisors and product consultants to the customer focused on understanding individual business operations, identifying change in business workflow, and explaining the value FieldEdge will provide based on their goals.</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Leading customer discovery (pre-sale) through workflow analysis, high level training, and onsite survey activities to uncover business challenges and opportunities to optimize productivity</li>
<li>
Outlining and setting proper expectations for implementation timeline, training, and data conversion</li>
<li>
Managing the team to the leading indicators to ensure monthly targets are met</li>
<li>
Demonstrating technical expertise in FieldEdge and QuickBooks, acting as a resource across the organization transferring knowledge and developing alternative workflows</li>
<li>
Relaying product improvements, use cases, feature gaps, and customer requirements to the product team to lead to future development, higher customer satisfaction, and increased industry market share</li>
<li>
Detailed understanding of the product and constant desire to be learning more</li>
</ul></div>
