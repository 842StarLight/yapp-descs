<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
The Global Ecosystem Marketing (GEM) team is responsible for creating, executing and optimizing a portfolio of integrated marketing programs and campaigns designed to:</b></h1>
<ul>
<li>Deliver strategic value to SAP Concur clients through Partner-developed or Partner-delivered solutions and services that increase satisfaction, adoption and retention with the Concur platform</li>
<li>Grow the depth, breadth and health of the Partner Ecosystem by helping our Partners grow their businesses and technical innovation built on the Concur platform</li>
<li>Create and optimize joint go-to-market opportunities (pipeline), resell and co-sell revenues (ARR), and</li>
<li>Support the incubation and innovation of new business models.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>FSI / Bank Champion:</b> The FSI Ecosystem Marketing Manager is responsible for creating, executing and optimizing a portfolio of integrated marketing programs and campaigns to:</p>
<div><div><ul>
<li>drive growth and retention of existing and new FSI Partnerships across Banks, Card and Digital Payment Providers and FSI Alliance partners</li>
<li>support the growth and expansion of our Private Equity selling model in coordination with our PE Team and Field Marketers, and
</li>
<li>lead other strategic business objectives, as our business models and strategy evolve.</li>
</ul></div></div>
<p><b>Matrix Role: </b>Reporting to the Sr. Director of Global Ecosystem Marketing, he/she will work closely with FSI Business Development (BD) team, Pay Provider/ Fintech Partnerships, Field / Client marketing, product and corporate marketing teams.</p>
<div><div><ul>
<li>As the regulatory landscape and GTM strategy differs greatly across markets, direct day-to-day partner management will be largely led by the field marketing and BD relationship owners.</li>
<li>Given the rapid evolution of this channel, this leader will help develop the repeatable and scalable marketing playbooks that can be customized and adapted at a market level. To accelerate the creation of those assets, this leader will work directly with a handful of early adopter accounts to create the initial playbooks.
</li>
</ul></div></div>
<p><b>Strategy and Outcomes</b> – Responsible for developing scalable, integrated go-to-market (GTM) strategies for the entire FSI community, establishing repeatable and scalable programs, determining marketing mix and tactics in coordination with the field, analyzing sales pipeline trend data, including growth KPI’s of number of Active partners, Partner sourced pipeline, and closed/won revenue.</p>
<p><b>Brand alignment, content curation and activation - </b>Partner with corporate marketing, product marketing and other content creators to identify, curate and customize (where appropriate) high-performing assets and programs, including:</p>
<div><div><ul>
<li>Product and partnership messaging and positioning frameworks</li>
<li>Top of Funnel thought leadership and awareness content</li>
<li>Event content, promotion and follow-up strategy for field, industry and virtual events</li>
<li>Partner training, selling reference tools and programs in coordination with product and learning &amp; development teams</li>
<li>Partnership launches and market entry campaigns</li>
<li>Curation of inbound marketing assets and messaging for syndication to partner websites, newsletters and social media channels</li>
</ul></div></div>
<p><b>Measurement – </b>Ensuring that all GTM programs have clear and measurable success metrics, partnering with Marketing OPS to ensure the measurement plan and infrastructure is in place, and championing a “test and learn” optimization approach. Analyzes channel marketing program performance to increase yield, ROMI, and partner engagement.</p>
<p><b>Internal Advocacy and Global Best Practice Sharing </b>- Support and contribute to internal communication vehicles to update, educate and share success stories to Concur and SAP stakeholders. Shares and syndicates best practices, high performing programs and strategies with Concur’s regional field marketers around the globe.</p>
<p><b>Utilizing and activation of MarTech Tools </b>- The GCMM will leverage a variety marketing applications, including our Partner Portal (Averetek), CRM (Salesforce.com), Marketing Automation (Marketo) and other components of our MarTech stack.
</p>
<p>10-20% Travel is expected.</p>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Position Competency and Skill Requirements:
</b></h1>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Education, Experience &amp; Training</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>5+ years of experience in B2B marketing. 1-2 years hands-on experience in channel, demand generation or related B2B marketing discipline.
</li>
<li>Direct or relevant product, service and market knowledge and experience, ideally within the Travel, Accounting / Financial Services, or Cloud SaaS sector.</li>
<li>Hands-on experience with modern digital marketing tools and channels, including email marketing, landing pages, marketing automation, content portals, events, social media, and more.</li>
<li>Bachelors degree in marketing, business or related field. MBA or advanced studies a plus.</li>
<li>Power user of Word, Excel and PowerPoint. Design skills a plus.
</li>
<li>Experience working with CRM and Marketing Automation tools, ideally Salesforce.com and Marketo.</li>
<li>Ability to pass an SAP background check.</li>
</ul>

</li>

<li>High level of attention to detail and quality assurance.</li>
<li>Analytical thinking, with a “test and learn” mindset to measure and optimize program performance and return.</li>
