<p><b>
WHAT YOU WILL BE DOING:</b></p>
<p>
You will seek to build deep, consultative technical relationships with your customers, effectively becoming a trusted adviser. An SA is ideally positioned to understand the customer's high-level objectives and match these to Verizon solutions in order to satisfy business and technical requirements.
</p>
<ul>
<li>Integral part of a team to identify and qualify business opportunities and develop the strategy to resolve technical matters.</li>
<li>
Recommend integration strategies, enterprise architectures, platforms and application infrastructure required to successfully implement a complete solution using best practices.</li>
<li>
Own the technical relationship with Verizon customers, including managing product and solution briefings, proof-of-concept work, and the coordination of additional technical resources. Work directly with customers to create solutions and use cases that leverage Verizon product integrations that generate business value.</li>
<li>
Articulate the Verizon Enterprise vision on technologies and solutions towards the Client; positions Verizon Enterprise as thought leader on key technological developments.</li>
<li>
Lead prioritization of Customer solution / technical requirements and champions resolution of solution / product gaps internally (also towards Product Groups).</li>
<li>
Develop individual skill set and expand product knowledge to maximize value and to deliver quality services to our clients. This includes attaining designated certification requirements and attending product-specific training, reviewing technical literature, and obtaining hands-on experience.</li>
<li>
Travel to Client sites, conferences, and other related events as required.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<p><b>
What we’re looking for...</b>
</p>
<p><b>What we are looking for:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Four or more years of relevant experience in the tech industry with experience in pre-sales, customer-facing consulting or other relevant technical experience.</li>
<li>
BA/BS degree, specialized or equivalent experience.</li>
<li>
Experience architecting and developing software or infrastructure for scalable, distributed systems.</li>
<li>
Experience architecting and developing LAN/WAN or large infrastructure and network solutions for enterprise customers.</li>
<li>
Experience with networking concepts including: software defined networking, routing, VPNs, load balancers, and firewalls. (Can be customized by technology and experience)</li>
<li>
Experience in and understanding of data and information security - especially as it relates to issues within businesses.</li>
<li>
Ability to quickly learn, understand, and work with new emerging technologies, methodologies and solutions in the cloud/IT technology space.</li>
<li>
Gathered industry knowledge and experience in two or more of the following areas: Software Defined Networking (SDN), IT infrastructure and systems security, cloud or network systems management, and business continuity.</li>
</ul>

EVEN BETTER IF YOU HAVE:
</b></p>
<ul><li>MA/MS degree in engineering or computer science.</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>
Run multiple projects or deals under tight timelines.</li>
<li>
Security, architecture, and industry certifications such as AWS Solution Architect, Certificate of Cloud Security Knowledge (CCSK), CompTIA Security+, Cisco Certified Design or Network Associate, ITIL Foundations, or similar.</li>
<li>
Hands on experience. It could be implementing or designing two or more of the following: Data and IP Services Managed Operations; Security Services; Call Center Technologies and or IT Applications Management
