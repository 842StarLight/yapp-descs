<h2>Accounting Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Are you good with numbers and looking to work in an accounting department? Then we may have the perfect position for you. Allured Business Media is a business-to-business publisher in Carol Stream, IL, looking for an <b>Accounting Specialist</b> to handle the day to day accounts payable and staff expense reporting auditing and processing. Other duties include billing, soft collections, Credit Application processing, Sales Commission Calculations, and Reporting.</p>
<p><b>RESPONSIBILITIES: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Audit vendor invoices and employee expense reports.</li>
<li>Data entry and reconciliation of payables.</li>
<li>Weekly processing of voucher packages for payment.</li>
<li>Mail and distribute checks</li>
<li>Set up ACH / WIRE payments for approval.</li>
<li>File paid invoices.</li>
<li>Handle vendor inquiries.</li>
<li>Prepare 1099’s.</li>
<li>Contact vendors to clean up outstanding checks</li>
<li>Reconcile Corporate Credit Card activity</li>
<li>Generate invoices and send customer statements.</li>
<li>Process new customer Credit Applications</li>
<li>Perform collection duties.</li>
<li>Compute monthly commissions.</li>
<li>Other related AP/billing/collection duties.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Associates Degree in Accounting preferred.</li>
<li>Minimum 2 years’ experience in both accounts payable and collections.</li>
<li>Strong math skills.</li>
<li>Detail oriented.</li>
<li>Enjoys working with numbers.</li>
<li>Ability to learn quickly.</li>
<li>Multi-tasker that is very organized.</li>
<li>Good communication skills both verbal and written.</li>
<li>Proficiency in Excel and Word.</li>
<li>Familiarity with Intacct, Nexonia, and a2z software systems a plus</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>This is a part-time position working three days a week with Tuesday and Wednesday mandatory, the third day is flexible.</i></b></p>
<p>Job Type: Part-time</p>
