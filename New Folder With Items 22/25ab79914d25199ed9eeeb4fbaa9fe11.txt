<div><b>
ROLE OVERVIEW</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
As a Leasing Manager, you lead the leasing team in building partnerships with future residents and securing lease agreements for a multi-million dollar, multifamily apartment home community. Here are the roles you'll play:</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>The Team Captain</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Set your leasing team’s overall goals and daily sales objectives, mentoring them every step of the way
</li>
<li>Hire, train, and motivate a stellar team while keeping group drama to a minimum
</li>
<li>Create friendly competitions to incentivize your team – while always having their backs and holding yourself accountable for their success
</li>
<li>Maintain an open-door policy and a friendly, highly visible presence, so associates feel comfortable talking to you about nearly anything</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>The Tour Guide</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Discover future residents’ needs, show them around your community’s amenity areas and model homes, and highlight all the benefits that will make them want to call your community home
</li>
<li>Brag about your community’s unique features and benefits – and highlight what sets it apart from the competition
</li>
<li>Create added value by anticipating needs and addressing living concerns customers haven’t even thought about yet
</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>The Deal Sealer</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Put in extra effort to make prospective residents feel so at home, they’ll never want to leave
</li>
<li>Optimize occupancy and increase community visits by finding new ways to boost online engagement, update promos/advertising, and create new marketing strategies
</li>
<li>Scout the competition to stay one step ahead and keep bringing in the big bucks
</li>
<li>Communicate with such clarity and enthusiasm that customers are left with only one question: “Where do I sign?”</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>THE IMPACT YOU CAN MAKE</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Occupancy is at an all-time high thanks to your world-class tours, ability to edge out the competition, and tactful ability to meet customer needs.
</li>
<li>Community traffic and closing ratios have skyrocketed due to your strategic marketing.
</li>
<li>Resident retention keeps climbing because you and your team have such a loyal customer following and have landed so many renewals.
</li>
<li>You can rest easily at night knowing you and your team have improved people’s lives by providing a better living experience and homes that residents love.
</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>YOUR BUILDING BLOCKS OF SUCCESS</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Solid communication skills that allow you to connect easily with diverse groups of people
</li>
<li>Stellar track record of leasing/sales and team leadership
</li>
<li>Resourceful problem solving
</li>
<li>Enthusiastic and professional customer service</li>
</ul></div>
