<h2>Assistant Manager - ReStore</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Honolulu Habitat for Humanity is looking for a friendly, upbeat individual to join our ReStore team, full-time. Honolulu ReStore is a nonprofit home improvement store and donation center that sells new and gently used furniture, appliances, building materials and home accessories to the public at a fraction of the retail price.</p>
<p>This position is responsible for assisting the Manager with every aspect of Restore operations including the development and implementation of a strategic business plan, marketing strategy, operating plan, inventory procurement, financial management, day-to-day operations and staffing. The position requires strong problem-solving skills; the ability to work in a fast-paced team environment; multi-tasking; attention to safety and details; to work independently and able to interface with a variety of individuals in a consistent, professional manner.</p>
<p><b>Essential Duties (include but not limited to): </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Work closely with the ReStore Manager promoting day-to-day operations in ReStore</li>
<li>Ensure cleanliness and safety of the warehouse and sales floor</li>
<li>Provide excellent customer service</li>
<li>Provide and analyze weekly and monthly sales reports and updates</li>
<li>Direct the warehouse flow and establish day’s priority (e.g., monitoring the flow from drop-off and clean up to pricing)</li>
<li>Price products, stage inventory, and merchandize donations</li>
<li>Train, direct and supervise volunteers</li>
<li>Assist with donation screening and pick-ups</li>
<li>Serve as a back-up pickup truck driver</li>
<li>Manage the store in the absence of the Manager including but not limited to maintaining ReStore email account, reporting, staff scheduling, approving timesheets, adjusting days priorities as needed</li>
<li>Operate and service the Restore vehicle and other equipment</li>
<li>Identify gaps/problems/concerns and bring suggestions for correction directly to the attention of the ReStore Manager</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned by the Manager</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Key Competencies: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Possess an understanding and appreciation for the mission of Honolulu Habitat for</li>
<li>Relevant prior retail experience</li>
<li>Knowledge of construction building supplies, tools, and materials preferred</li>
<li>Strong working knowledge of Microsoft Office software a must</li>
<li>Data analysis, business development, and marketing experience a plus.</li>
<li>Be a team player who can take direction and complete work under</li>
<li>Outstanding interpersonal skills and enjoys work with others from diverse ethnic and social backgrounds.</li>
<li>Reliable, honest, mature, detail-oriented, and has good time management skills.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Physical Demands: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Performing work in a wide variety of settings and environmental conditions (office, warehouse, outdoors); primarily in a non-climate controlled warehouse, settings and temperatures vary based on Hawaii environment and minimal work in an environmentally controlled office setting</li>
<li>Computers, monitors, standard office equipment, and motorized equipment used in the acquisition and movement of heavy materials</li>
<li>Lifting and/or moving objects up to 50 pounds on a regular basis</li>
<li>Sitting, standing, walking, frequent bending, reaching, squatting, lifting, and repetitive motion</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Additional Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Meet one of the following Citizenship requirements:</li>
<li>Citizen, permanent resident alien, or nationals of the United States.</li>
<li>Non-citizens who are eligible under Federal law for unrestricted employment.</li>
<li>Possess a valid Hawaii driver’s license, with a clean driving record and the ability to be insurable under the organization’s general liability insurance</li>
<li>Reliable Transportation</li>
<li>Completion of a background check at time of employment</li>
<li>Submit to random drug testing as provided for in the personnel policy manual.</li>
<li>Solid communication and interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Availability to work flexible shifts Monday through Saturday and occasional Sundays</li>
<li>Flexibility to adapt to changing circumstances and priorities, and to be open to new ideas</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Salary: $15.50 to $16.00 /hour</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul><li>management: 1 year (Preferred)</li></ul>

<ul><li>Honolulu, HI (Required)</li></ul>
