<h2>Senior Web Strategist</h2>

Accolades such as "Manufacturing Marvel," "National Best and Brightest" and a feature in the "Wall Street Journal" for our innovation in training program represent the excellence that is Hu-Friedy. A global industry leader, we are committed to employee engagement and development, diversity and inclusion, wellness and social responsibility. We have a commitment to continuously developing our team and to promoting from within whenever possible. Our well-defined culture has strongly impacted the prosperity of our company. Our core values of Teamwork, Respect, Integrity, Excellence, Reliability and Social Responsibility are evident in daily life at Hu-Friedy and continue to shape the success of our business.</p>

<p>
Our 10,000 dental instruments and products are sold in over 100 countries, and we have close to 1000 employees representing and growing our brand around the world. We currently have offices in Frankfurt, Milan, Shanghai and Tokyo, and a sales force across 30 different countries.</p>

<p></p>
<p>
We are looking for a <b>Senior Web Strategist</b> to partner with our Senior Manager of Digital Marketing to drive strategic direction for all aspects of digital marketing, site merchandising, ecommerce best practices and customer experience mapping.
</p>
<p><b>Summary of Position</b></p>
<p><b>
Digital Marketing</b> : 55%</p>
<ul>
<li>Plan &amp; implement profitable digital initiatives, including defining goals, objectives and associated tactics.
</li>
<li>Experience with managing and optimizing content, UX, and conversion with focus on loyalty, communities and ecommerce.
</li>
<li>Brainstorm and generate new and innovative ideas and promotions for marketing campaigns and functionality to drive strong traffic, conversion and sales.
</li>
<li>Monitor and make recommendations for the expectation of continual improvement across all devices</li>
<li>Develop email test plans across devices, oversee analyze and share results with cross-functional partners</li>
<li>Work along with marketing and product management team to identify strong project stories for email campaigns, homepage, as well as promotional marketing messages.
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Site Performance, Testing and Site Improvement</b> : 30%</p>
<ul>
<li>Identify and promote opportunities for site improvements through technology and new features. Translate concepts, ideas and improvements into key business objectives which may include developing wireframes and generating mock ups with our agency.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Reporting and Site Analytics</b> : 15%</p>
<ul>
<li>Analyze data at a granular level, summarize concisely and report out key learnings. Share trends from raw data. Provide direction on site enhancements.
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Principal Accountabilities</b> :</p>
<ul>
<li>Leverage digital platforms to optimize community interactions, UX, and conversation, identifying new digital tactics and channels to drive traffic, optimizing the website and content for the search engines, and measuring effectiveness. Executing against all channel KPIâ€™s.
<ul>
<li>Optimize community technology platforms to ensure a compelling customer experience that drives engagement, advocacy and preference (e.g. loyalty &amp; rewards and recycling program).</li>
<li>Develop specific customer journey maps for the various Hu-Friedy customer communities that path community members to the next desired action.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Recommend and execute site merchandising best practices for optimal conversion</li>
<li>Identify trends, insights and optimize spend and performance based on learnings. Translate data findings into actionable recommendations for internal teams. Plan, execute, and measure experiments and conversion tests.</li>
<li>Brainstorm new and creative traffic &amp; conversation growth strategies. Evaluate emerging technologies. Provide thought leadership and perspective for adoption where appropriate.
</li>
<li>Measure and report performance of all digital marketing campaigns, and assess against goals</li>
<li>Collaborate with various teams across the organization to build processes and GTM calendars, to create merchandising plans, and build execution plan.</li>
<li>Utilize strong analytical ability to evaluate end-to-end customer experience across multiple channels and customer touch points.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>This position requires an individual with:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>BS/MS degree in Marketing or related field</li>
<li>Minimum of 5 years digital marketing experience</li>
<li>Proven experience developing online loyalty &amp; rewards and online communities in digital marketing and/or ecommerce company</li>
<li>Demonstrable experience leading and managing SEO/SEM, email, social media and/or display advertising campaigns</li>
<li>Highly creative with experience in identifying target audiences and devising digital campaigns that engage, inform and motivate</li>
<li>Experience in optimizing landing pages and user funnels</li>
<li>Experience with A/B and multivariate experiments</li>
<li>Solid knowledge of website analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics)</li>
<li>Working knowledge of ad serving tools</li>
<li>Experience in setting up and optimizing Google Adwords campaigns</li>
<li>Working knowledge of HTML, CSS and JavaScript development and constraints</li>
<li>Strong analytical skills and data-driven thinking</li>
<li>Up to date with the latest trends and best practices in online marketing and measurement</li>
<li>Some project management experience preferred.</li>
<br>
</ul>
