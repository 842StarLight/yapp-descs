<h2>Regional Sales Manager, META</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>The Role:</b> In this position you will develop a the META territory, drive engagement and build relationships with new prospects, resulting in bookings and revenue attainment in excess of your goals.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Develop and manage the META territory</li>
<li>Identify and close multiple opportunities and projects at the same time, shape our enterprise business in your territory ensuring accurate overview and forecasts</li>
<li>Target, educate, and persuade new customers to use Recorded Future products and technologies in new and creative ways that benefit their business</li>
</ul>
<b>Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>5+ years experience in software and/or SaaS sales to the Fortune 500/Global 1000</li>
<li>BA/BS or equivalent combination of education and experience</li>
<li>Track record of success consistently exceeding overall goals in security technology sales</li>
<li>Entrepreneurial drive and ability to sell creative solutions into new markets</li>
<li>Confidence and track record building a new territory</li>
<li>Comfortable calling at the senior executive level</li>
<li>Highly effective written, presentation, and closing skills</li>
<li>Ability to travel extensively</li>
<li>Dynamic, high-energy self-starter</li>
<li>Experience with information security or threat intelligence a plus</li>
<li>Fluency in English and excellent writing skills</li>
</ul>
