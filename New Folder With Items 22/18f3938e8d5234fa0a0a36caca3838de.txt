<h2>Sales Support Specialist III</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<b>Job Description</b><br>
<div></div>
<div>The Auto Club Group (ACG) provides membership, travel, insurance and financial services offerings to approximately 9 million members and customers across 11 states and 2 U.S. territories through the AAA, Meemic and Fremont brands. ACG belongs to the national AAA federation and is the second largest AAA club in North America.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>Primary Duties and Responsibilities:</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>Lead, coordinate and monitor various field sales projects to ensure the achievement of regional and corporate goals. Provide analytical skill and content knowledge in the development of recommendations and prepare reports, statistical information and communications for distribution. Research issues and respond to complex inquiries, providing management with a consistent approach regarding various processes (e.g. communication, training schedules, promotions, events) across regions. Develop, reviews and analyze various reports (e.g. sales volumes, retention results, commission, revenue, expense, service trends, etc) to identify opportunities for further training, improved marketing opportunities, tracking for contest standings, etc.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>Support projects/implementation of action plans designed to promote market growth and penetration. Develop and/or maintain branch/department budgets for management review. Support projects and/or drives local promotions and sales including the marketing of member and prospect events in branches and communities. May develop and contract local Show Your Card &amp; Save (SYC&amp;S) partnerships and identify, cultivate and execute local community-level sponsorships.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>PREFERRED:</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>Education:</div>
<ul><li>Bachelor's Degree in Business Administration, Marketing, Advertising, or a related field
</li></ul>
<div></div>
<div>Experience:</div>
<ul><li>Active participation in civic or community organizations.</li></ul>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>WORK ENVIRONMENT</div>
<div>Works in a temperature controlled office environment. Frequent travel (approximately 50% of work time) required with exposure to road hazards and temperature extremes.</div>
