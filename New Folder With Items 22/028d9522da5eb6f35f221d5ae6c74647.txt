 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Description
<br><br>
Position at Motion Industries
<br><br>
On-Site Solutions Project Coordinator
<br>The Mi On-Site Solutions (OSS) Project Coordinator role is to assist the Mi OSS Sales and Operations teams with their projects to implement programs at customer sites for industrial vending, Vendor Managed Inventory, RFID, Asset Repair Tracking (ART), and on-site solutions programs. The primary focus is to provide administrative and financial support, manage detailed information on equipment placed, and provide project management to improve order flow and processes. The position will coordinate directly with multiple departments within Motion and solution providers.
<br>Responsibilities
<br>Generate POs for equipment as required
<br>Provide Mi branches with information on their equipment lease details as required
<br>Maintain a detailed master database of all industrial vending and RFID equipment placed in eCOS
<br>Assist in problem solving and process improvements related to sales and operations
<br>Coordinate the set-up of vending account numbers for revenue reporting purposes
<br>Manage list of surplus equipment available for redeployment
<br>Manage the addition of vending equipment to the Bank of America lease program
<br>Review the quarterly Bank of America lease schedules of vending equipment for accuracy
<br>Process vending supplier invoices for payment
<br>Ensure the vending machines’ annual database license renewals are invoiced appropriately
<br>Handle department purchases via the department expense credit card
<br>Monitor equipment rolling off the Bank of America lease program, work with Finance to ensure payment is completed, and update our records
<br>Routinely interact with branches to obtain information necessary to update equipment database
<br>Interact with Mi Credit, Mi Finance, and Mi IT departments
<br>Interact with vending equipment suppliers regarding invoices, licensing, and order details
<br>Attend training sessions to learn the functions and capabilities of vending and software applications offered by Mi OSS
<br>Complete all Motion-required training
<br><br>
<b>Qualifications:</b><br>
Complete all Motion-required training
<br>Strong attention to detail required
<br>Assist with scheduling and planning meetings and project activities
<br>Able to support multiple projects with changing priorities
<br>Strong written and verbal communication skills
<br>Demonstrates poise and professionalism
<br>Excellent computer skills—Microsoft Office Suite; including Outlook, Excel, Word &amp; PowerPoint.
<br>Ability to work in a team setting, sharing responsibilities and goals
<br>Ability to learn procedures and concepts quickly
<br>Ability to work in a fast paced environment
