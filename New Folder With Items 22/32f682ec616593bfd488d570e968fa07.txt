<p><b>Who are we looking for?
</b></p>
<p>We have an exciting story to tell, which is where you come in! We’re looking for an experienced, collaborative and creative corporate communications professional to join us at our headquarters in Paddington, London.
<br>
<br>
Reporting to the Head of Communications, this is a varied role where you will have the opportunity to help devise and deliver a corporate communications strategy, to develop the corporate narrative and raise Finastra’s profile across a range of external audiences. Working with our appointed PR agencies, and focusing predominantly on external communications, this role will centre on clearly articulating Finastra’s business strategy and support our mission of becoming the number one platform for open innovation in the world of financial services.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>It goes without saying that you have an exceptional way with words and an inquiring mind, and are able to interpret and distil complex business concepts. The successful candidate will also need to be self-driven, with advanced time management skills, a can-do attitude, exceptional problem-solving capabilities, and excellent relationship management skills. Crucially, you will have the experience, resilience and gravitas to provide strategic, corporate communications leadership at the most senior level of the company.<br>
<br>
</p>
<p><b>Your role?
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop and implement a clear corporate communications strategy to raise the profile of Finastra, across a range of external audiences, via a number of channels, ensuring consistency of message.
</li>
<li>Elevate the profile of key members of the Executive Leadership Team through media relations, broadcast opportunities, high profile speaking engagements and maximizing their social media presence in appropriate channels
</li>
<li>Own the crisis communications plan, in conjunction with the Business Risk Operating Committee, to ensure the preparedness of the communications team (internal and external) to respond to any potential crisis situations
</li>
<li>Act as the point of contact for customer communications to enhance and protect our reputation
</li>
<li>Manage relationships with appointed external agencies across the globe, acting as a principal contact for corporate media relations.
</li>
<li>Collaborate with global PR team to draft key company announcements and aid in the development and dissemination of press releases
</li>
<li>Devise innovative and imaginative approaches to corporate communication campaigns.
</li>
<li>Provide strategic counsel to the Executive Leadership Team, briefing and supporting executives across a variety of communications activities.
</li>
<li>Regularly assess and report the effectiveness and impact of corporate communications strategies.
</li>
<li>Collaborate across to the business to ensure content and messaging is integrated and aligned with business priorities and other communications plans.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Required Experience </b><b>, Qualifications &amp; Skills
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in Communications, Public Relations, Marketing, English, Economics or related field required.
</li>
<li>15+ years of progressive strategic communications/public relations experience, operating within a large company and/or unit of a highly matrixed organization.
</li>
<li>Substantial experience across all areas of corporate communications, including media relations, crisis and issues management, change management communications.
</li>
<li>Strategic thinker with strong understanding of how communications strategies, channels and effective deployment can support and drive business performance
</li>
<li>Global experience of working at a senior level, across diverse regions and with remote teams and agencies, with the ability to influence and provide counsel to senior executives
</li>
<li>Extraordinary written and verbal communication skills – ability to craft meaningful and engaging content such as press releases, presentations, blogs.
</li>
<li>Excellent project management skills, with proven ability to deliver high quality work with tight deadlines, while managing multiple and competing priorities.
</li>
<li>Very strong attention to detail.
</li>
</ul>
