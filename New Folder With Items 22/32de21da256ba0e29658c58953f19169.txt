<h2>Rocky Mountain Vibes – Ticket Intern (Colorado Springs, CO)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<b>Rocky Mountain Vibes – Ticket Intern (Colorado Springs, CO)</b><br>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>Start Date: April 1st, 2020</b></div>
<div><b>
End Date: September 30th, 2020</b></div>
<div><b>
Compensation: $12/hr at 40 hours per week (hours will vary in-season) and 5% commission on sales made.</b></div>
<div><b>
</b></div>
<div><b>
JOB DESCRIPTION:</b></div>
<div><b>
</b></div>
<div>
The Rocky Mountain Vibes are the Pioneer League Rookie-Advanced affiliate of the Milwaukee Brewers. The season runs from mid-June through early September with a 38 home game season. This position will allow for insight into all operations of a Minor League Baseball organization in a fast paced and exciting environment.</div>
<div>
</div>
<div>
This position will primarily focus on the operations of the ticket office, reporting to the Director of Ticketing. Daily responsibilities include the selling of season ticket packages, group sales, advanced individual game sales, and walk up sales. Game day responsibilities include the management of the ticket office and game day staff, processing staff ticket requests, financial reconciliation, and ticket reporting. Position will also include sales training and regularly scheduled weekly sales calls.</div>
<div>
</div>
<div><b>
Off-Season Duties (April 1st – June 15th):</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Ticket software training and management</li>
<li>Season ticket and package sales</li>
<li>Membership sales</li>
<li>Customer prospecting</li>
<li>Data reporting</li>
<li>Outbound season ticket and mini plan sales calls</li>
<li>General stadium and team season preparation</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>
In-Season Duties (June 15th – September 30th)</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Manage box office and staff on game days</li>
<li>Process team Will Call and fulfill day-of ticket requests</li>
<li>Assist in selling walk-up game tickets</li>
<li>Reconcile box office financials</li>
<li>Outbound season ticket and mini plan sales calls</li>
<li>Assist in other game day operations</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>
REQUIREMENTS:</b></div>
<div>
Applicants must be able to work all Vibes’ home games. Applicants should also be detail oriented, have high customer service skills and a strong work ethic and desire to learn.</div>
<div>
</div>
<div><b>
Applications can be submitted through TeamWork Online at the link found at vibesbaseball.com.</b></div>
