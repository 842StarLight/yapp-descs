<h2>Operations Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>ESSENTIALDUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES: </b></p>
<p>Theduties noted below are considered to be essential job functions for one of thefollowing reasons: performance of the duty is why the job exists, failure toperform the duty may have serious consequences, limited number of employeesavailable to perform duty, or the duty requires specialized skills.</p>
<ul>
<li>Review financial statements, sales or activity reports, or other performance data to measure productivity or goal achievement or to identify areas needing cost reduction or program improvement.</li>
<li>Direct and coordinate activities of businesses or departments concerned with the production, pricing, sales, or distribution of products.</li>
<li>Direct administrative activities directly related to making products or providing services.</li>
<li>Prepare staff work schedules and assign specific duties.</li>
<li>Monitor suppliers to ensure that they efficiently and effectively provide needed goods or services within budgetary limits.</li>
<li>Direct or coordinate financial or budget activities to fund operations, maximize investments, or increase efficiency.</li>
<li>Establish or implement departmental policies, goals, objectives, or procedures in conjunction with board members, organization officials, or staff members.</li>
<li>Perform personnel functions such as selection, training, or evaluation.</li>
<li>Plan or direct activities such as sales promotions that require coordination with other department managers.</li>
<li>Set prices or credit terms for goods or services based on forecasts of customer demand.</li>
<li>Manage the movement of goods into and out of production facilities to ensure efficiency, effectiveness, or sustainability of operations.</li>
<li>Perform sales floor work, such as greeting or assisting customers, stocking shelves, or taking inventory.</li>
<li>Develop or implement product-marketing strategies, including advertising campaigns or sales promotions.</li>
<li>Recommend locations for new facilities or oversee the remodeling or renovating of current facilities.</li>
</ul>
