<h2>Project Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Who we are and what we do?</b></h2>
<p>Alchemy Systems (www.alchemysystems.com) is the global leader of innovative technology solutions that help food companies engage and train their frontline workforces. Over three million food workers at 50,000 locations use Alchemy’s learning management, performance management and communications programs to drive operational excellence.</p>
<p>Alchemy was named to the Inc. 5000 list of America’s fastest growing private companies. The Austin American Statesman has named Alchemy as one of Austin’s Top Workplaces for five consecutive years. Alchemy is now part of Intertek family since it’s acquisition in August 2018. Intertek is headquartered in London with over 40,000 employees throughout the world completing Assurance, Testing, Inspection and Certification services.</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What are we looking for?</b></h2>
<p>A highly motivated individual to manage, coordinate, train and execute multiple implementation projects for new and existing clients. You will work closely with support, training, sales, and account management to ensure clients have all product resources enabled and are able to fully utilize the product features. You will send routine project communication to appropriate team members with updates and action items.</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What you’ll do:</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrate expertise in project management</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Conduct customer facing Kick off calls</li>
<li>Manage deliverables &amp; timelines of the project – self and customers</li>
<li>Simplify complex information for understanding</li>
<li>Exhibit critical thinking and problem-solving skills</li>
<li>Flexibility</li>
<li>Trouble shoots issue, identify solutions and provide recommended course of action for customer</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Plan and execute the overall implementation for mulitple small to large scale accounts</li>
<li>Identify client’s roll out plan based on their specific needs and recommend the best practices to ensure a smooth and successful implementation</li>
<li>Safeguard customer satisfaction through prompt and professional communication including calls, emails, and follow-up timeliness with clients (internal and external)</li>
<li>Manage multiple clients and projects/tasks simultaneously</li>
<li>Practice sound judgement and decision-making skills when working with customers, team members, and other departments</li>
<li>Host internal Kick off Calls, and train on basic product functionality</li>
<li>Work with Sales Account Executive(s) and Account Manager(s) to Schedule Kick-Off Call</li>
<li>Order, configure, and ship hardware and software (this does not require knowledge of any development language)</li>
<li>Create and customize new client sites based on the client’s preferences and products sold</li>
<li>Track all implementation tasks in NetSuite</li>
<li>Manage time and prioritize project task requirements and issues</li>
<li>Schedule and coordinate customer training with Alchemy Learning Team</li>
<li>Communicate and escalate any implementation road blocks to Team Lead, Director of Implemetation and Learning, and any other appropriate internal partners</li>
<li>Work with cross functional teams to resolve any roadblocks to ensure a successful implementation</li>
<li>Use product knowledge expertise to troubleshoot common product issues general PC errors</li>
</ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What you bring to the table:
</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree (B. A.) recommended but not required.</li>
<li>Completion of a project management PMP, CAPM, or communications related college program</li>
<li>5 years related experience or training or equivalent combination of education and experience</li>
<li>A minimum of 5+ years project management experience</li>
<li>5 years of Customer Service/Client relations experience</li>
<li>Bilingual preferred (Spanish/French)</li>
</ul>
