<h2>Corporate Paralegal</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>With our strong investment in research and development and our culture of continuous improvement, Hexcel is the industry leader in the manufacturing of composite materials and industrial fabrics. We invite you to join the Hexcel team at various manufacturing sites, sales offices and R&amp;T centers around the globe. Become a part of the “strength within.”
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Hexcel is currently seeking a <b>Corporate Paralegal </b>for our <b>Stamford, USA </b>location. The ideal candidate will be experienced in handling a wide range of corporate and compliance tasks and will be able to work independently with little or no supervision. This person must be exceedingly well organized, flexible and enjoy the challenges of supporting a corporate legal department. The ability to interact with staff (at all levels) in a fast-paced environment, sometimes under pressure, remaining flexible, proactive, resourceful and efficient, with a high level of professionalism and confidentiality is crucial to this role. Excellent written and verbal communication skills, strong decision-making ability and attention to detail are equally important.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>The selected individual will be responsible for but not limited to the following obligations:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Oversee subsidiary management to ensure that Hexcel’s domestic and international subsidiaries comply with various legal requirements, including minute book, legal entity chart and electronic subsidiary database maintenance, preparation of organizational documents, board resolutions, written consents and secretary certificates, and working with professional services firm on service of process, good standing certificates, and certified documents, as needed.
</li>
<li>Prepare minutes, board resolutions, agendas and meeting calendars for Hexcel board and committee meetings, and otherwise assist with meeting preparation. Manage distribution of materials to attendees. Work closely with the CEO’s office with regard to Hexcel board meetings.
</li>
<li>Assist with various SEC filings (including proxy statements and Forms 3, 4 and 5), D&amp;O questionnaires, and compliance filings with the NYSE. Apply for and maintain EDGAR filings codes for Hexcel and its officers and directors.
</li>
<li>Assist with components of Hexcel’s annual meeting of stockholders, including preparing meeting materials, coordinating voting reports and managing the calendar of events for the annual meeting.
</li>
<li>Assist with management of Hexcel’s securities trading policy, including preparation and maintenance of trading window calendar, processing of trading clearance requests and distribution of quarterly trading window notices.
</li>
<li>Assist in the preparation for and closing of various corporate transactions, including M&amp;A and financing transactions.
</li>
<li>Manage professional services firm on implementation of company-wide training programs. Compose/run periodic and ad hoc compliance reports, prepare compliance reports for presentation, maintain and update various compliance databases, and update compliance-training records.
</li>
<li>Assist with intake, management and disposition of complaints to the Ethics and Compliance hotline, and provide support to program directors and investigators.
</li>
<li>Manage internal Law Department email boxes.
</li>
<li>Assist with special projects for the Law Department, as needed, which may include assistance with maintaining and updating corporate policies and procedures.
</li>
<li>Prioritize regular workload, special tasks and concurrent projects, allocating time and resources to ensure that work is completed accurately and efficiently within established time frame.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree required
</li>
<li>Paralegal certificate from an ABA accredited institution (preferred) or equivalent work experience
</li>
<li>5+ years’ experience working as a corporate paralegal at a top law firm and/or in-house legal department of a public company
</li>
<li>Experience with ’34 Act (including Section 16) and U.S. public company board governance, such as NYSE listing standards, director independence standards, corporate governance guidelines, etc.
</li>
<li>Strong knowledge of MS Office, including Word, Excel, PowerPoint and Outlook and an aptitude for learning and using new software tools
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Eligible candidate must be: U.S. citizen, U.S. national, a person lawfully admitted for permanent residence, a temporary resident under sections 210(a) or 245(A) of the Act, a person admitted in refugee status, a person granted asylum. Hexcel (NYSE: HXL) is a global leader in advanced composites technology, a leading producer of carbon fiber, and the world leader in honeycomb manufacturing for the commercial aerospace industry.
</p>
