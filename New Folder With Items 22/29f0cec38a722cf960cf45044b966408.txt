<div><b>
Overview
</b></div>
<div>In order to support our global Product, Sales &amp; Operations organization we are looking for a talented, driven and enthusiastic People Partner Director to support 2 of the global functions here at Criteo. This role supports two Executive Vice Presidents with global organizations.</div>
</div>
<div>
<b>What will you be doing?</b><div><ul>
<li>
Serve as a strategic partner to leadership to build a world-class organization and culture; develop and successfully implement strategic and innovative talent solutions to support business needs</li>
<li>
Leverage People data, metrics and analytics to support informed decision making and evaluate of key People programs</li>
<li>
Consult, collaborate, and implement strategy with business leaders on attracting, retaining, engaging, and motivating talent in an extremely competitive environment</li>
<li>
Collaborate globally across the People Team, and with all cross-functional business partners</li>
<li>
Work to inspire a culture of strong business collaboration and play a key role in developing an environment of teamwork and out-of-the-box critical thinking</li>
<li>
Coach and develop business line leadership with the aim of building high performing teams</li>
<li>
Partner with Centers of Excellence (Total Rewards, Talent Acquisition, Talent Development, Analytics) and local People Experience teams on building and driving customized People strategies focused on driving business priorities</li>
<li>
Drive strategy and partner with local HR on implementation of organizational transformation initiatives</li>
</ul></div>
<b>
What do we NEED?</b><div><ul>
<li>
10+ years HR generalist experience
</li>
<li>3-5 years as an HR Business Partner supporting global business lines
</li>
<li>Experience collaborating in a matrixed HR environment with local HR generalists and global Centers of Excellence</li>
<li>
Sound HR expertise in all generalist areas (Talent, Employee Relations, Engagement, Rewards)</li>
<li>
Demonstrated success leading change in a high-performing environment</li>
</ul></div>
