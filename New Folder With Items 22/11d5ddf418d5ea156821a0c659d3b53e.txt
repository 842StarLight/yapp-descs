<h2>Business Development Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Hitwise, a division of Connexity Inc., is a leading Saas company that accesses the world's largest sample of online behavior across desktop, tablet and mobile devices providing end-to-end understanding of how consumers behave online to optimize digital marketing campaigns.</p>
<p>
As part of our growth, we're seeking a Business Development Representative to join our marketing team. This is an entry level role well suited for someone looking to develop a career in sales. The successful candidate will be working on a diverse group of campaigns to drive lead generation and lead nurturing in key vertical markets in order to drive new business across the Hitwise portfolio.<br>
<br>
<b>On a daily basis, you will:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Proactively be involved in marketing lead generation, from a variety of sources (CRM /Website leads/LinkedIn) to identify and prioritize leads for new business</li>
<li>
Prospect outbound marketing generated leads and booking meetings for the new business team</li>
<li>
Qualify and process inbound marketing generated leads within 24 hours and booking meetings for the new business team</li>
<li>
Ensure quality of both outbound and inbound meetings by tracking opportunity creation and pipeline</li>
<li>
Data cleanse by outbound calling to validate contacts and their details within existing customers, prospects and partner accounts</li>
<li>
Strategy &amp; Reporting - participate and contribute to group strategy, reporting on key management metrics</li>
<li>
Meet &amp; exceeding KPI's - including prospecting activity (email, phone etc.) lead generation, meeting quality, conversion to opportunity</li>
<li>
Understand and participate in performance management processes</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
What we'd like to see from you:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Commercial awareness – the ability to understand commercial challenges, interpret data and identify associated business opportunities</li>
<li>
Strong personal organization - the ability to prioritize tasks and ensure key targets are met</li>
<li>
Excellent attention to detail – to ensure high levels of data quality</li>
<li>
Articulate and professional telephone manner - previous experience of sales or customer service an advantage</li>
<li>
Personal Drive – a highly motivated, self starter who is prepared to go ‘the extra mile’</li>
<li>
Self-driven, disciplined, consultative sales professional who consistently demonstrates resilience and commitment</li>
<li>
Thirst for knowledge – an interest and desire to learn about the wider digital marketing landscape</li>
<li>
Salesforce experience a MAJOR plus</li>
<li>
BA/BS degree or equivalent</li>
</ul>
