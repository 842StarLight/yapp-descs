<h2>E-Commerce Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>One of the world’s leaders in manufacturing high-end aftermarket motorcycle accessories has an immediate position open for <b>E-Commerce Assistant</b>. This position will enhance and be an integral part of our sales department. The right applicant will have a <b>passion for racing and motorcycles</b>.</p>
<p><b>Essential Duties &amp; Responsibilities: </b></p>
<p><b>Job Description: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for assisting e-Commerce customers with processing sales orders and ensuring a friendly, positive, and timely shopping experience.</li>
<li>Confirms and processes e-Commerce customers’ records, billing information, payment transactions, delivery date, and provides information on warranty protection plans.</li>
<li>Provides continued service and support to e-commerce customers.</li>
<li>Assists with maintaining website product information, and assists merchandising department as needed.</li>
<li>Excellent communication and writing skills.</li>
<li>Asset linking and verification in vendor tools.</li>
<li>Under the supervision of marketing/sales team members, coordinate creation of, and review, any necessary copy and keywords; ensure internal and vendor tools are properly populated; review products before go-live for accuracy and completeness.</li>
<li>Removal, modification and verification of custom categories, products and SKU attributes.</li>
<li>Product verification and publishing/un-publishing as directed by Merchandise Managers.</li>
<li>Assist in site auditing and maintenance.</li>
<li>Ensuring the daily workload is on schedule and delivered on time.</li>
<li>Proactively reports to team on outstanding production elements or unfulfilled production requirements.</li>
<li>Identifies opportunities for efficiencies in the processes as they relate to website production.</li>
<li>Other projects as assigned.</li>
<li>Develop expertise in internal and vendor tools that support product and catalog maintenance.</li>
<li>Troubleshoots issues and provides creative problem-solving ideas for short and long-term benefit.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Job Requirements: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage Google Ad Words and Website Optimization.</li>
<li>Experience with product detail, page optimization, keyword ranking, conversion and exposure.</li>
<li>Proficiency in Microsoft Excel, Word and Outlook experience is a plus.</li>
<li>Motorcycle Industry experience is a plus.</li>
<li>Experience working with MAS200 ERP software is a plus but not required.</li>
</ul>
