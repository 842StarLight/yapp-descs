<h2>Product Support Tech I (Afternoon Schedule)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<b>Hours-</b><br>
<b>2:00 PM-10:30 PM off Sundays &amp; Tuesdays</b><br>
<br>
<b>Pay is $13 per hour + potential $250 bonus monthly + Benefits Immediately</b><br>
<br>
This position serves as the principle point of contact between the Lottery Retailer, Lottery Sales Representative and Field Site Marketing Representatives. Their main focus will be to provide first call resolution for all Lottery Retailer needs over the phone, including but not limited to troubleshooting of technical issues. The tools used in this role will not allow TSR to perform any system level changes. They are limited to read-only diagnostic information and static knowledgebase articles designed to help resolve or escalate the customers’ issues.<br>
<br>
<b>Job Functions:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Properly identify caller issue, then guide the caller through a series of remedial steps by utilizing documented procedures over the phone</li>
<li>If unable to resolve over the phone, properly route ticket to next level support</li>
<li>Provide excellent customer service experience for all callers as outlined in the quality assurance program</li>
<li>Successfully meet departmental metrics (Scorecard)</li>
<li>Responsible for learning the hardware, software functionality, terminals including peripherals</li>
<li>Properly communicate any situations that may require escalation to Response Center/Site Level Management or, to the Service Desk Supervisor</li>
<li>Communicate directly with Operations and Dispatch departments to resolve the customers issue</li>
<li>Provide information on Arizona lotteries active games and promotions, including detailed steps on how to process transactions</li>
<li>Responsible for upholding all policies and procedures</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned
</li>
</ul>
</div>

<br>
<br>
<b>Position Requirements/Qualifications:</b><br>

<li>Basic knowledge of Microsoft Office applications including Excel, Power Point, Outlook, Word, phones, computers, faxes</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
Skills/Abilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Excellent communication skills</li>
<li>Ability to multitask (talk and type at the same time)</li>
<li>Maintain a positive and professional attitude</li>
<li>Ability to work under pressure in a constantly changing environment</li>
<li>Attention to detail</li>
<li>Ability to type 40 wpm, minimum</li>
</ul>
<br>

This isn't a traditional Monday-Friday 8-5 schedule. Candidates must be flexible on the 40-hour work week schedule which includes working one weekend day.</b>
