<h2>Director of Event Services</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>The Director of Event Services plans, manages, and directs the day-to-day event coordination operations of the Bert Ogden Arena and HEB Park. Controls and monitors the customer contact aspect of the Security, Parking and Front-of-House areas of the venue.</p>
<p>Responsibilities</p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for all aspects of event planning, scheduling, budgeting and event implementation</li>
<li>Produces events from concept to completion – involving strategy, management and oversight of all details for each event</li>
<li>Clearly communicates event project scope, schedules, deliverable dates, project status/action items and any other relevant information – both to the client as well as the internal team</li>
<li>Researches, reviews and recommends equipment, materials and supplies required in providing event services and planning.</li>
<li>Oversees all spending to ensure fiscal responsibility; organize and reconcile project expenses in a timely fashion in accordance with company policies</li>
<li>Coordinates outside service needs with food and beverage contractor, decorator and other vendors</li>
<li>Coordinates with marketing department all media entry into the building before, during or immediately following events</li>
<li>Meets with potential clients, advises them on the venue requirements, and negotiates sales of additional services</li>
<li>Manages client expectations, ensure delivery of the highest quality service, solicit and act on client feedback</li>
<li>Acts as a direct liaison to all event promoters and venue tenants in regards to show advancement, show production, facility usage, practice times and disseminates all show information to all venue departments</li>
<li>Represents the venue at tradeshows, community events, and industry meetings</li>
<li>Maintains the proper image and generates positive public relations with clients, patrons and staff</li>
<li>Prepares schedules and assigns events to the Event Managers</li>
<li>Develops policies and procedures for the Event Services, Security and Parking Department and monitors their compliance</li>
<li>Provides leadership to enhance staff development and promotes excellent guest service</li>
<li>Ensures event staff, and other personnel are properly trained in guest services, protective procedures, fire safety and other emergency procedures</li>
</ul>
<p>Supervisory Responsibilities<br>Directly supervises Event Managers and/or Event Coordinator, Security, Parking and FOH staff within the Event Services Department. Carries out supervisory responsibilities in accordance with policies and applicable laws. Responsibilities include interviewing, hiring, and training employees; planning, assigning, and directing work; appraising performance; rewarding; disciplining employees in conjunction with Human Resources; addressing complaints and resolving problems.<br>Qualifications<br>To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill, and/or ability required. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree from an accredited four-year college or university in Business, Sports or Hotel Management or related field or equivalent combination of education and experience</li>
<li>Minimum of 5 -7 years experience in Supervising and Managing events in a sports and entertainment venue in an operationally focused role</li>
<li>Demonstrate knowledge of principles and techniques of planning, implementing and supervising event staff</li>
<li>Possess knowledge of security and public safety issues related to a sports and entertainment venue</li>
</ul>
<p>Experience in AutoCad is preferred</p>
<p>Skills &amp; Abilities</p>
<ul><li>Exceptional organizational skills required. Must be able to oversee complex projects and schedules, maintain deadlines, work within budgets, with the ability to set priorities and manage several projects simultaneously required</li></ul>
<p>Excellent written, verbal, and interpersonal communication skills with the ability to synthesize and accurately present relevant information</p>
<ul>
<li>Must have the ability to manage in a fast-paced, diverse environment with focus on Client and Guest Services, entrepreneurship and building &amp; growing a strong business is essential</li>
<li>Ability to successfully manage a workload that balances long-range planning with the unanticipated daily demands of Event Services required. Work effectively under pressure and/or stringent schedule and produce accurate results</li>
<li>Handle conflicts, make decisions and exercise proper action during high tension and stressful situations. Remain flexible and adjust to situations as they occur</li>
<li>Maintain a professional, cooperative working relationship with City officials, clients, employees, exhibitors, patrons, and others encountered in the course of employment</li>
<li>Requires ability to work flexible and extended hours, including nights, weekends and holidays, in addition to normal business hours</li>
</ul>

<ul><li>Competent computer skills, including knowledge of Microsoft Office, spreadsheet programs, and familiarity with facility management and systems software required. Experience in Auto Cad preferred</li></ul>
