<p><b>
Overview/Description</b></p>
<p><br>
Do you love to be deeply involved in enabling R&amp;D to become even more impactful through managing and influencing top priorities and ensuring effective governance across business units?</p>
<p></p>
<p>
Do you have a passion for, and a track record of, pulling together cross-functional, cross-BU teams to deliver on key business results?</p>
<p></p>
<p>
Do you thrive on driving strategic focus areas that creates a sense of ownership and commitment with the rest of the team?</p>
<p></p>
<p>
If so, then VP, Operations role in VMware may be the right next step in your career.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
VP, PCS Operations position reports directly to the PCS COOs defining and driving a combination of operational and strategic initiatives. You could be working with product management, product marketing, engineering, finance, sales, channel, planning, global marketing, HR, legal, or the PCS leadership team. In other words, this is a high-exposure position in terms of breadth and upward visibility. You will make a direct contribution to PCS and the BUs in achieving business goals, and in the process, have an inside vantage point to how R&amp;D functions operate – invaluable experience for any aspiring business leader.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
Work with R&amp;D COOs to drive the cadence and content for business, operational, and strategic reviews</li>
<li>
Develop and execute on PCS staff agendas, PCS Operations reviews, MBOs, analyst briefings, action item follow-up</li>
<li>
Develop PCS R&amp;D strategy</li>
<li>
Drive 3 year and Annual PCS planning process</li>
<li>
Direct PCS Communications working with the Communications leader which includes PCS All Hands</li>
<li>
Lead initiatives and projects aimed at addressing operational and strategic challenges, driving for results with a bias for action</li>
<li>
Be a thought leader working with the business units</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Qualifications</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
Undergraduate degree required. MBA or graduate degree is preferred.</li>
<li>
Product or consulting experience is highly desirable (eg. product management, product marketing, technical marketing, management consulting, engineering, SE, professional services)</li>
<li>
Ability to define/drive both operational and strategic efforts</li>
<li>
Ability to influence without authority; partner with other functions</li>
<li>
Strong organizational skills</li>
<li>
Diplomatic, persuasive approach/style</li>
<li>
Strong and confident presence – solid presenter</li>
<li>
Clear, articulate written communication skills</li>
<li>
Strong analytical skills and a natural predisposition toward data-driven decision making, but with ability to make reasoned decisions in the face of uncertainty or imperfect data</li>
<li>
Financially literate</li>
</ul>
