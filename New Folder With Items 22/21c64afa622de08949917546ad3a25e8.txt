<b>About the Role:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Manage application software initiatives and staff, helping the organization further leverage technology to deliver services to our members and employees.</li>
 <li>Maintain high levels of availability for our mission-critical applications.</li>
 <li>Manage workflow and processes related to application systems and services.</li>
 <li>Work with department managers to analyze, design, and implement technical solutions and other systems.</li>
 <li>Identify what software is best for a business’ specific needs and tune it, upgrade it, and repair it when there are problem.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
<b>Some Roles and Responsibilities:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Manage and provide support to critical business application systems.</li>
<li>Continuously monitor industry trends, technologies, and standards and be able to research, recommend, and apply new technologies as they emerge.</li>
<li>Provide technical support and problem resolution related to business application software systems. Whenever there is a problem with the software, the Application Manager or a member of their team will review the issue and come up with a solution.</li>
<li>Lead a team of IT specialists responsible for implementing and upgrading application software. They ensure that the servers are all ready before implementing new systems, and then carefully monitor the rolling-out process</li>
<li>Builds and maintains an effective working relationship with all members of the division’s Management Team.</li>
<li>Monitor everyday operations of application development team and provide regular feedback.</li>
<li>Develop professional relations with customers and establish effective solutions to meet business requirements.</li>
<li>Analyze business requirements and develop enhancements to achieve same.</li>
<li>Prepare designs and maintain cost for various business application developments.</li>
<li>Work with business and IT teams and manage all project requirements.</li>
<li>Analyze current system processes and design functional requirements.</li>
<li>Manage efficient work of various teams and participate in associate activities.</li>
<li>Determine and analyze various processes and implement changes for improvement.</li>
<li>Performs research and develops new systems and applications for new and present systems.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
<b>Qualifications:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering or related discipline, or equivalent Information Technology and/or application support experience.</li>
<li>Minimum 10 years of experience in system implementation and business application support.</li>
<li>Proven leadership experience managing a software team on an ongoing basis is crucial to the role.</li>
<li>Good technical knowledge, with hands-on experience managing systems development in new computer architectures and environment; knowledge of relevant technology and tools.</li>
<li>Experience as the lead person in implementing a major software system.</li>
<li>Strong verbal and written communication skills, with an ability to express complex technical concepts in business terms.</li>
<li>Knowledge of system design, development, implementation, and user support principles and practices.</li>
<li>Experience with database management.</li>
</ul>
