<p>Job Title: Manager, The Milk Shake Factory<br>

<br>
About Edward Marc</p>
<p>
Edward Marc (www.edwardmarc.com) is a Pittsburgh-based, rapidly expanding manufacturer, wholesaler, and retailer of chocolate and caramel products. A family business since 1914, the company was rebranded in 2007 when the fourth-generation of chocolatier siblings assumed management. Under their leadership, the company has launched national and global brands including Edward Marc Chocolatier, The Milk Shake Factory (MSF), and Snappers. The management team values sustainable innovation, aggressive sales growth, and maintaining the companyâ€™s foundation as a family-owned enterprise.</p>
<p>
Position Overview</p>
<p>
As a key member of the retail team, the Manager will assume a key role in managing a major component of the business while creating the customer experience of the Milk Shake Factory. The Manager will have day-to-day responsibility for all retail activities at the store. This encompasses leveraging customer insights to drive sales, implementing seasonal campaigns, managing the retail team, identifying opportunities for improvement, and creating a safe and clean environment.<br>
<br>
Essential Functions</p>
<ul>
<li>Takes complete ownership of the front- and back-house store operations, merchandising, workflows, sanitation, organization and customer experience</li>
<li>Aids in the creation of systems for achieving store-wide operational excellence with regards to cash management, food safety, inventory control, and employee performance</li>
<li>In conjunction with the EMB HR team, oversees MSF human resources functions including staff training, coaching, motivating, feedback, and retention to ensure all team members have the tools to succeed</li>
<li>Schedule and staff adequately the retail store to ensure retail operations function smoothly while keeping labor costs in line with the retail budget</li>
<li>In conjunction with the company accounting department, provide accurate data that shape the MSF budget</li>
<li>Monitor opportunities to reduce waste on milk shake and confection inputs through continuous monitoring, documentation, and team member training on product quality and recipes</li>
<li>Work with the Maintenance Manager to ensure the MSF environment exceeds regulatory health and safety standards</li>
<li>Establish work plans and maintenance schedules that support and improve MSF equipment reliability</li>
<li>Leverage consumer insights and data sources to contribute to the creation of annual and seasonal marketing campaigns that resonate with customers</li>
<li>Act as the point of contact for MSF corporate and special event accounts including holiday gifts and weddings</li>
<li>Work with the retail team to ensure that all security cameras, alarms, locks, and security protocols are functioning and documented on a regular basis</li>
<li>Meet or exceed retail sales goals (i.e., units per transaction, dollar per transaction) through techniques that create value for the consumer and MSF (e.g., bundling, merchandizing)</li>
<li>Act as a brand advocate to consumers and employees by communicating the company story, history, vision, and products including those for corporate accounts and special events</li>
<li>With the Director of Retail create a system for managing the Sweet Rewards loyalty program</li>
<li>Ensure that all POS register systems are operational at all times</li>
</ul>
<p>Requirements</p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrated leadership ability with at least five years of experience in a customer-facing sales setting</li>
<li>Strong track record of creating an amazing customer experience while managing successful retail or restaurant teams, brands, operations and financials</li>
<li>Availability to work flexible hours that may include early mornings, evenings, weekends and/or holidays</li>
<li>College degree strongly preferred; however, extra years of experience may substitute</li>
<li>Composure, positivity, flexibility, and time management ability necessary to succeed in a performance-driven retail environment</li>
<li>Exceptional leadership, communication, operational, problem-solving, organizational and planning skills</li>
<li>Ability to plan and prioritize workload</li>
<li>Ability to handle confidential and sensitive information</li>
<li>Knowledge of Microsoft Office</li>
<li>Ability to lift and/or handle packaged goods and materials up to and including 25 lbs</li>
</ul>
