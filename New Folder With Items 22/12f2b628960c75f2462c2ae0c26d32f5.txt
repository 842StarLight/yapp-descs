<h2>Senior Software Engineer (Javascript, Node.js)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Are you passionate about building Web applications? Do you want to work with other smart people who really care about their craft? Do you excel in an environment of continuous self-improvement and learning? We are looking for someone who is motivated to build truly great web applications to lead our web development initiatives and develop high performing applications. The apps we build are used by Splunk’s sales &amp; customer success organizations and partners to help prospects and customers understand the value they can derive from Splunk, and provide actionable plans to achieve this. These apps have a measurable effect on Splunk’s bottom line.</p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Your Responsibilities:</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>
Write state of the art applications using a modern JavaScript stack (Node.js, React).</li>
<li>
Collaborate with UI experts and designers to implement responsive interfaces across multiple platforms.</li>
<li>
Build close relationships with teammates, product managers, and quality engineers.</li>
<li>
Break down feature requests into actionable plans and assist when needed with the creation and execution of test plans.</li>
<li>
Put your head together with key architects and managers to establish and implement standard methodologies for delivery of high quality software.</li>
<li>
Ensure development projects fulfill business requirements and goals and delight end-users.</li>
<li>
Create necessary documentation to ensure project objectives are met and that designs are well documented and communicated.</li>
<li>
Engage in hands-on, in-depth analysis, review, design, development and debugging of your applications.</li>
</ul>
