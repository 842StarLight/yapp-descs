<h2>Controller</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Job Summary
</b></p>
<p>Garavel Chrysler Jeep Dodge Ram is growing and seeking an experienced Controller to add to our team. Generous compensation package which includes medical, dental, Life, and STD insurance, PTO, and 401K.
</p>
<p><b>Full accounting responsibilities include but not limited to:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Month-end closing including financial statement preparation and presentation
</li>
<li>Calculate weekly and monthly commissions and bonuses
</li>
<li>Maintain accurate balance sheet accounts and schedules
</li>
<li>Track and analyze expense trends
</li>
<li>Prepare monthly CT sales tax returns
</li>
<li>Perform daily cash flow and monthly bank reconciliation
</li>
<li>Direct accounts payables/receivables process
</li>
<li>Oversee weekly payroll processing
</li>
<li>Compiles monthly financial statement and demonstrate financial accuracy and acumen
</li>
<li>Advanced Excel skills with building spreadsheets utilize formulas, data sort, export data from DMS, etc.
</li>
<li>Manage office workflow
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Previous dealership/automotive accounting experience as a Controller or Office Manager
</li>
<li>Understand generally accepted accounting principles, including familiarity with dealership accounts and financial statements.
</li>
<li>Experience managing a team of employees
</li>
<li>Excellent organizational skills and ability to multi-task.
</li>
<li>Advanced experience working with a DMS (dealership accounting software)
</li>
</ul>
