<h2>Human Resources and Finance Administrative Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><div>Sakon, www.sakon.com, simplifies how businesses around the world Understand, Manage and Transform their Communications and Cloud Ecosystems. One of the world’s top SaaS companies in our markets, Sakon is a leader in managing communication services and costs, mobility and cloud services. Sakon has 200+ large enterprise customers spanning virtually every country across the globe. Sakon is headquartered in Concord, MA with additional locations across North America, Europe and India.</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Human Resources Function:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Performs a wide range of duties relative to the maintenance and processing of personnel records and reports.</li>
<li>
Processes payroll changes using a computerized system.</li>
<li>
Assists with new employee on-boarding</li>
<li>
Provides clerical and operational support to other human resource staff.</li>
<li>
Maintains high standards of confidentiality of all employee records and information.</li>
<li>
Assists with HR projects</li>
<li>
General office administration</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Finance Function:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Monthly client invoicing – Back up
</li>
<li>Work with the Financial Administrator to become familiar with the clients and their individual billing needs.</li>
<li>
Be available as back up to send client invoices if Financial Admin is out.</li>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<li>
Calculating Sales commission – monthly</li>
<li>
Work with the Financial Administrator to become familiar with the different Sales commission plans.</li>
<li>
Pull reports of closed deals for each Sales person.</li>
<li>
Calculate monthly Sales commissions based on client payments that have been received.</li>
<li>
Keep detailed excel spreadsheet on each Salesperson’s commission.</li>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<li>
Calculating Agent commissions – monthly</li>
<li>
Work with the Financial Administrator to become familiar with the different Agents and their individual commission plans.</li>
<li>
Calculate monthly Agent commissions based on the client payments that have been received.</li>
<li>
Keep detailed excel spreadsheet on each Agent’s commission.</li>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<li>
Remote bank deposits – Daily</li>
<li>
Deposit any incoming checks via remote scanner and send deposit detail sheet to Financial Administrator.</li>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<li>
Transfer Checks – Random</li>
<li>
Write check from one bank account and deposit via remote deposit into the other.
</li>
<div></div>
<br>
<li>Scanning – Emailing – Random</li>
<li>
Scan and email paper invoices that come in the mail to the Financial Administrator as well as other miscellaneous notices.</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>

</ul></div>
