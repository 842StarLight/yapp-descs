<h2>Business Development Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>About Opal</b></p>
<p>At Opal, we believe enterprise software should be beautiful, simple and designed for the job at hand. The Opal platform empowers marketing teams to collaborate within a dedicated environment to plan, visualize and deploy campaigns across all marketing channels, reducing complexity and aligning teams for better marketing results.</p>
<p>Our team is deeply invested in encouraging a culture that promotes design thinking, collaboration, and a passion for excellence. We work together as a team to revolutionize the modern marketing organization.</p>
<p>We are seeking a <b>Business Development Director </b>who will lead the development and execution of the Opal Partner Program across technology and platform partners. Reporting to the VP of Sales &amp; Partnerships and working collaboratively across Sales, Marketing, Product, and Customer Success functions, this role will play an important part in executing Opal’s growth strategy.</p>
<p><b>What you’ll do:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Lead the Opal Partner Program - set direction, build to scale, and own the successful delivery of partner value propositions</li>
<li>Conceive, design, and execute strategic business development activities across existing and prospective partners</li>
<li>Balance strategic and tactical objectives to create data and insight backed business cases, to then directly manage the implementation of those plans</li>
<li>Recruit, develop, nurture, and expand new and existing technology and platform partners</li>
<li>Develop and negotiate partner agreements - managing the process from discovery, definition, contract, execution, and launch</li>
<li>Manage relationships with strategic global technology and platform partners</li>
<li>Partner across Product, Marketing, and Sales to develop and deliver comprehensive partner GTM plans and field activities to achieve business goals</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What you’ll bring:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>15+ years of experience in business development, partner management, or product management driving customer and revenue growth, preferably in B2B software or SaaS environments</li>
<li>Excellent communication and facilitation skills and an ability to convey technical concepts and product features in a relatable way</li>
<li>Ability to distill complex issues into structured frameworks and concrete plans</li>
<li>A penchant for the details with strong organizational, time management, and prioritization skills</li>
<li>Ability to thrive in a startup, fast-paced, constantly evolving environment</li>
<li>Highly analytical with the ability to consume, process and transform large amounts of information into actionable insights</li>
<li>Commitment to proactive collaboration and accountability - you are prepared, informed, and ready to go in all of your interactions</li>
<li>Value-focused growth mindset - you see every touchpoint as a chance to create a positive experience for the customer and your team</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to work effectively with individuals from diverse communities and cultures</li>
<li>Time-zone flexible for meetings, and willingness to travel domestically and internationally to meet with customers, partners, and attend Opal events</li>
<li>Undergraduate degree required; advanced degree is a plus</li>
</ul>

</ul>
