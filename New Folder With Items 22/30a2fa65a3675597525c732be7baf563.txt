<h2>Sales Director - Programmatic</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Zeta Global is a marketing technology company that is focused on the consumer journey, focused on customer acquisition, retention, and growth. We are looking for a dynamic Sales Director to join our sales team covering territory throughout the Midwest. This position will work to promote our technology and services to advertising agencies including both agencies with trading desks and independent agencies alike, with the intent of building and growing profitable relationships. In this role you will manage the sales process for your region from prospecting to close while ensuring excellent service and support to prospects and clients. The ideal candidate will be focused on monitoring lead follow-up for sales development and have a proven track record of meeting measurable sales goals. A successful candidate will have a contagious and insatiable desire to win and an inherent interest in the ad technology landscape.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Develop strategic plan to achieve revenue goals; hit or exceed these revenue goals</li>
<li>
Develop and maintain strong active relationships with key client stakeholders at both senior and mid-management levels</li>
<li>
Understand agency, advertiser, data and vendor landscapes and trends, reporting these to the organization; who are the major players and how they buy</li>
<li>
Understand and effectively communicate Zeta Global’s value proposition, technology, processes, partnerships as relate to growth of current and prospective client accounts</li>
<li>
Ideally has an in-depth knowledge of the industry and of the biddable media marketplace —past, present, and future</li>
<li>
Plans, directs and coordinates sales activities, including management of the sales pipeline</li>
<li>
Leverages external relationships and personal network to generate sales leads</li>
<li>
Regularly involved in all phases of the sales lifecycle including: identifying and developing leads; meeting with clients; developing value propositions; financial deal structuring; to contract negotiation and closing</li>
<li>
Advises pursuit team in crafting and positioning deals and closing. Shares</li>
<li>
Proven sales skills in complex deal development, financial structuring, negotiations and closing new and existing clients</li>
<li>
Has responsibility and is accountable for Win/Loss reviews on new accounts</li>
<li>
Oversee a portfolio of strategic accounts and monitor changes in key metrics, analyze performance trends and deliver on-going reviews of these items to clients</li>
</ul>

Positive attitude</li>

5+ years of proven experience in consultative sales process selling digital media, data and/or SaaS products</li>

<li>
Willing to travel</li>
