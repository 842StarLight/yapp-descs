<h2>Assistant Property Manager (Jamestown Square Apartments)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>We are looking for an exceptional <b>Part Time</b> <b>Assistant Property Manager</b> to join our team at our <b>120-unit JamestownSquare </b>apartment community!</div>
<div><b>
This is a Part Time position at 32 hours per week. It does provide benefits.</b></div>
<div>
</div>
<p>
Responsible for assisting the Property Manager with ensuring the community generates a satisfactory cash flow, the property is maintained in accordance with Company standards and assures customer satisfaction. Manage the property in the absence of the Property Manager.</p>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div></div>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Promptly answer telephone and e-mail inquiries with the goal of obtaining an appointment to show the community to the prospective resident.</li>
<li>
Greet prospective residents, demonstrate the features and benefits of the interior of the apartment homes and amenities, explain terms of occupancy, and provide information about local areas.</li>
<li>
Close the sale with prospective resident and obtain a holding deposit and application for an apartment or waiting list.</li>
<li>
Promptly follow up with prospective residents whom have not yet leased to continue the sales process and secure the sale.</li>
<li>
Determine and certify the eligibility of prospective residents, following government regulations and Company procedures.</li>
<li>
Assist Property Manager with all marketing and sales activities including advertising promotions marketing surveys of competitors’ activities.
</li>
<li>Pursue and collect rent payments and accurately record remittance.
</li>
<li>Investigate complaints, disturbances and violations and resolve problems following management rules and regulations.</li>
<li>
Prepare, submit and maintain accurate records, reports and documents.</li>
<li>
Ensure confidentiality of all data in the resident files (i.e. income, credit reports, assets, etc.).</li>
<li>
Provide information and reports in coordination with other departments as needed.</li>
<li>
Conduct all activities in accordance with HUD regulations and Fair Housing requirements.</li>
<li>
Process move in and move out documents.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>

</b></h2>
<div></div>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or equivalent combination of education, training or experience.</li>
<li>
Property Management experience preferred.</li>
<li>
Must be detail oriented and organized.</li>
<li>
Must have and maintain a valid Driver’s License in the state of residence.</li>
<li>
Able to work a varied schedule including weekends.</li>
<li>
Must be able to communicate effectively verbally and in writing.</li>
<li>
Must be able to read and comprehend the English language.</li>
</ul>
