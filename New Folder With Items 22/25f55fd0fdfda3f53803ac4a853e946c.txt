<p><b>General Summary</b></p>
<p>
Our team is looking for a talented, motivated, and high-performing Account Executive to join our team in Portland, OR. Account Executives are responsible for prospecting new business, upselling and cross-selling within our extensive install base, as well as delivering results against a quota.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Duties/Responsibilities</b></p>
<div>
The Account Executive is a solutions/consultative sales role where you will be solving your client's business challenges by offering relevant and compelling software, hardware, software as a service (SAAS) subscription services and professional services.</div>
<p><b>Required Skills &amp; Experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>1-5 years of sales experience</li>
<li>Strong customer service orientation</li>
<li>Solid computer skills such as email and Word required</li>
<li>Exposure to CRM systems such as Salesforce</li>
<li>Good driving record and reliable transportation required</li>
<li>Excellent communication, negotiation, and persuasion and partnering skills</li>
</ul>
