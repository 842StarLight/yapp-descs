<p><b>Position Summary</b></p>
<p>
The <b>Senior Marketing Specialist</b> will assist marketing staff in execution of effective marketing campaigns and tactics to achieve company business objectives. And act as lead for creation of marketing plans and campaigns to meet business needs and regulatory requirements. Mentor and train less experienced staff on various marketing duties.</p>
<p><b>
In this role you will:</b></p>
<p><b>
Marketing Projects (90%)</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Create, execute, and coordinate marketing plans, tactics, and campaigns. Develop project and/or campaign creative briefs and supporting documentation, and updates to business unit annual marketing plans, coordinating with Marketing Communications Manager.</li>
<li>Lead kick-off meetings and work with peers to ensure positive outcomes.</li>
<li>Manage requests from sales and other departments for refresh, redesign, or development of marketing collateral, applications, or forms.</li>
<li>Review and improve established project processes and platforms to track project deliverables, obtain appropriate approvals, and maintain timelines.</li>
<li>Review and proof marketing materials.</li>
<li>Analyze marketing data and team project metrics.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Administrative (10%)</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Monitor and update projects within InMotion, track due dates, and complete project close-out process.</li>
<li>Maintain Marketing Collateral Library and sample files.</li>
<li>Support Production team with tasks such as quote requests, work orders, obtaining approvals</li>
</ul>
