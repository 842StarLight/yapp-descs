<h2>Safety Services Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>OVERVIEW</b><p>
Sentry’s dedication to customer service is embodied by our best employees. At Sentry, our associates take care of our customers with a passion for service and delivering business results. We are currently seeking a, experienced, safety minded, and results driven individual to fill a Loss Control consultant position supporting our Regional business unit’s Irvine, California office. The ideal candidate will be located in the Southern, California area and able to conduct comprehensive safety and loss control assessments, providing our Underwriting team with a strong analytical opinion of risk to support our goal of profitable growth in the middle to large premium market segment. In addition, the successful candidate will develop close relationships with our agents and policyholders, conducting selected safety seminars and training, to build the value-added service that Sentry is known for. We are searching for an individual who is passionate about consulting on safety and risk management practices, can work from home, with excellent organizational skills to effectively manage their territory.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<b>WHAT YOU'LL DO</b><div>
The following are some of the responsibilities of this Loss Control consultant position.<br>
<ul>
<li>Conduct on-site risk evaluation surveys and complete detailed reports to assist our Underwriting groups with account acceptability.</li>
<li>Assist customers with development and implementation of safety polices, programs and procedures specific to each account &amp; loss exposure.</li>
<li>Provide advanced risk management and consultative services to assigned clients.</li>
<li>Conduct Job-site safety inspections and OSHA 10-hour seminars for our Regional construction line of business.</li>
<li>Maintain and demonstrate safety management and expertise in areas such as commercial property protection, general liability, product liability, fleet safety and employee safety.</li>
<li>Develop, coordinate and conduct safety/risk management seminars and training programs for accounts to promote awareness and reduce claim frequency and severity.</li>
<li>Develop and execute service proposals and plans to qualified accounts.</li>
</ul>
</div>
<p></p>
<br>
<b>
WHAT IT TAKES</b><ul>
<li>B.S. degree with emphasis in Occupational Safety &amp; Health, Engineering, or Physical Sciences preferred</li>
<li>5 or more years of insurance safety consulting experience or safety work in a private industry (construction and manufacturing)</li>
<li>Current safety designation recipient including CSP or ability &amp; desire to pursue</li>
<li>High level of independence, organization, and self-motivation to work from home</li>
<li>Experience in coordinating and servicing large or multi location accounts preferred</li>
<li>Knowledge of OSHA, ANSI, NFPA Standards, and DOT regulations</li>
<li>High level of verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Ability to learn use of specialized building valuation (MSB Express), diagramming and report software</li>
<li>Acceptable motor vehicle record for extensive travel (50%) including overnight travel</li>
</ul>
