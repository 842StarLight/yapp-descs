<br>
<p><b>
SUMMARY</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
The Technical Writer 3 develops technical manuals for the entire Completions product lines, to accompany all completions tools and parts for our customer base. Creates separate marketing and sales materials for tenders, quotes, bulletins, product information sheets, flyers, and brochures. Develops and maintains training and assessments for the Design Engineering Group.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Responsible for writing new and modifications of assembly, disassembly and test procedures of all equipment including step by step instructions with visual diagrams and inventory of parts and fixtures.
</li>
<li>Develops written content related to equipment functionality, purpose and limits including technical specifications, trouble shooting, and parts lists.
</li>
<li>Works with the Engineering department to gain functional knowledge of equipment and their assembly including receipt of part diagrams to structurally manipulate for use in technical manuals.</li>
<li>
Gains hands-on knowledge of manufactured products via the onsite warehouse and manufacturing personnel.</li>
<li>
Maintains a single-source documentation system.
</li>
<li>Mentors Technical Writer(s) I and Technical Writer(s) II positions within Superior Completion Services.</li>
<li>
Maintains the branding for all Superior Energy Completion Services documentation.</li>
<li>
Works with artists and illustrators to update and maintain the Technical Operations Manuals.</li>
<li>
Creates separate documentation for sales and marketing.</li>
<li>
The above statements are not intended to be an exhaustive list of all responsibilities, duties, and skills required of personnel so classified; nothing restricts the company’s right to change, assign, or reassign duties and responsibilities at any time or for any reason.</li>
</ul>
