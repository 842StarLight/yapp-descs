<p><b>Legends</b></p>

Founded in 2008, Legends is a holistic experiential services agency with more than 1,500 full-time and 30,000 season team members globally. Legends has three core divisions operating worldwide: Global Planning, Global Sales and Hospitality, offering clients and partners a 360-degree platform to elevate their brand and execute their vision. Currently, Legends works with marque clients across business verticals including professional sports; collegiate; attractions; entertainment; and conventions and leisure. We are the industry leaders in designing, planning and realizing exceptional experiences in sports and entertainment. A few of our esteemed partners include:</p>

<p><b>
San Diego State University
</b></p>
<p>San Diego State University is a major public research institution that provides transformative experiences, both inside and outside of the classroom, for its more than 36,000 students. The university offers bachelor's degrees in 95 areas, master's degrees in 78 areas and doctorates in 22 areas. The Aztecs are the 19 men’s and women’s NCAA Division I athletics teams representing the University as a member of the Mountain West Conference.</p>
<p><b>
San Diego State University Mission Valley</b></p>
<p>
On November 6, 2018 San Diego voters approved Measure G paving the way for the proposed SDSU Mission Valley campus expansion. As a result, SDSU will build a 35,000-capacity multi-use stadium to serve as the new home for Aztec football beginning with the 2022 season.</p>
<p><b>
The Partnership</b></p>
<p>
SDSU Athletics has partnered with Legends Global Sales to secure naming rights and founding partnerships for the new Mission Valley Stadium. They will also assist in the sales strategy and execution of ticket packages for Aztec football, men’s &amp; women’s basketball as well as premium and priority seating at the new Mission Valley Stadium.</p>
<p><b>
The Role</b></p>
<p>
The Coordinator, Collegiate Partnerships will assist the Collegiate Partnerships department in maximizing the productivity and efficiency of its team members. Primary responsibilities include sales prospecting and activation program research, CRM and reporting, database report creation, as well as the creation of partner proposals.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Functions</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
(20%) Work with software tools (e.g. Hoovers, Sports Business Journal, The List), publications, and third party vendors to compile data and analytics to assist Stadium Partnerships on front-end research phase of the sales process</li>
<li>
(10%) Development of partner briefs for due diligence and research phase of the Business Development sales process</li>
<li>
(50%) Coordinate the development of sales presentations – including brainstorm research, platform development, image curation, copywriting, etc.</li>
<li>
(20%) Analyze partnership deals and department activity metrics with detailed scorecards, leveraging Salesforce.com, KORE software tools, and Tableau</li>
</ul>
