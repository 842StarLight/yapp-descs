<h2>Data Center Infrastructure Engineer II</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>PURPOSE OF ROLE: The primary purpose of this role is to providing consultation and technical advice on IT infrastructure planning and engineering for assigned systems. This includes responsibility for translating business requirements and functional specifications into technical requirements that support integrated and sustainable designs for designated infrastructure systems.<br>
This role supports the deployment, configuration, and life cycle management of enterprise class IT infrastructure. Additional responsibilities include working with internal customers and peer organizations to create, update, and improve the processes that are used to deploy, configure, and maintenance the IT infrastructure; using service catalogs, automation tools, and asset databases.<br>
<br>
RESPONSIBILITY STATEMENTS<br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Provides consultation and technical advice on IT infrastructure planning and engineering for assigned systems by assessing the implications of IT strategies on infrastructure capabilities</li>
<li>Collaborates with IT business partners, solutions, and enterprise architects to help define and understand target strategies</li>
<li>Evaluates new service options, identifies issues and impacts, and makes recommendations on feasibility and ROI</li>
<li>Translates business requirements and functional specifications into technical requirements that support integrated and sustainable designs for designated infrastructure systems by partnering with Business Analysts to understand business needs and functional specifications</li>
<li>Collaborates with architects and software engineers to ensure functional specifications are converted into flexible, scalable, and maintainable designs</li>
<li>Writes, reviews, and validates clear technical specifications and documentation</li>
<li>Develops or modifies infrastructure solutions within designated computing environments by identifying technical solutions to business problems</li>
<li>Develops and validates system design prototypes</li>
<li>Builds hardware and software configurations and prepares system components for installation to the infrastructure</li>
<li>Develops, validates, and executes hardware and infrastructure software tests to identify system errors and ensure solutions meet functional specifications</li>
<li>Supports systems integration testing (SIT), component integration testing (CIT), and user acceptance testing (UAT) by providing insight to testing teams in order to ensure the appropriate depth of test coverage</li>
<li>Leads the implementation and maintenance of assigned enterprise infrastructure systems to ensure successful deployment and operation by developing and documenting detailed standards (e.g., guidelines, processes, procedures) for the introduction and maintenance of services</li>
</ul>
<br>
Qualifications<br>
<br>
Required Minimum Qualifications:<br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science, CIS, or related field with 6+ years of experience in an IT support environment with technical experience in distributed technologies and systems development. Additional equivalent work experience may be substituted for the degree requirement.</li>
<li>2+ years of experience in a leadership role with or without direct reports</li>
</ul>
<br>
Preferred Qualifications:
<br>
<ul>
<li>Master's Degree in Computer Science, CIS, or related field</li>
<li>2+ years of experience using ServiceNow</li>
<li>2+ years of experience working with asset database and/or CMDB</li>
<li>2+ years of experience leading technical or project teams with or without direct reports</li>
<li>2+ years of work experience in an IT role requiring interaction with senior management (i.e., Director level or higher)</li>
<li>3+ years of experience working in a large matrixed organization</li>
<li>3+ years of experience working with third party IT vendors and/or systems solution providers</li>
<li>3+ years of IT experience in the retail industry</li>
<li>3+ years of experience writing technical documentation in an infrastructure development environment</li>
</ul>
