<h2>Branded Content Program Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Gannett is a leading media and marketing company with unparalleled local-to-national reach, successfully connecting consumers, communities and businesses. With the iconic USA TODAY brand, 109 strong local media organizations in 34 states and Guam, more than 160 local news brands online and in the U.K. and ReachLocal, a digital marketing company, we provide rich content through hundreds of outstanding affiliated digital, mobile and print products.
<br><br>
The Branded Content Program Manager brings a passion and aptitude for branded content production, creativity, organization, and client service. He/She plays a key role on a fast-growing studio team to ensure that all projects and campaigns are efficiently and effectively executed and embody our point-of-view as a creative agency with an editorial voice.
<br>The Branded Content Program Manager is responsible for managing local branded content campaigns. He/She will be responsible for managing timelines, coordinating stakeholders, setting up campaigns using internal systems and maintaining campaigns throughout the lifecycle. He/She shall identify and implement successful strategies and practices as well as mitigate challenges and obstacles for the effective and efficient management of branded content campaigns. He/She may also be responsible for tracking individual and aggregate campaign performance to inform successful strategies.
<br>The Branded Content Program Manager works directly with advertising clients and across the organization to provide support for custom branded content products, campaigns, and serves as the internal expert and leader on branded content executions to advise on best practices, troubleshooting, and optimization. This position requires extremely strong communication skills and flawless project management and execution capabilities.

<br><ul>
