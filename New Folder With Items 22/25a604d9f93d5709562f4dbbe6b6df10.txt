<div>Project YX’s mission is to provide the most personalized online shopping experience in the world. Our highly client-centric approach uses deep data to take the infinite array of luxury goods available for sale online and to recommend the perfect product based on our clients’ lifestyles, preferences, and wardrobes. We define a luxury experience as one that is super-personalized—customized just for you. To our clients-a select and discerning group-it looks and feels like magic.</div>

We love our work. We look for individuals who are curious, humble, and driven to play a role in redefining the luxury goods industry.</div>

<div>
We are looking for on-call Photo Shoot Assistants who would like freelance work. This position will assist our Styling and Operations team at our client's closet photo shoots. We photograph and digitize all of their clothing, footwear, accessories, handbags, etc. We are looking for someone with photography experience or someone with knowledge of fashion to enter attributes in our app. If you have both that would be a plus! Please note the days could be long (8 plus hours) and you are on your feet, but the team is super-fun and focused!</div>
<div>
