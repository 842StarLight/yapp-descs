<h2>Interoperability Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>
<div><div>The Interoperability organization is one of Cerner’s strategic business units focused on enabling clients to better understand, manage and engage populations to deliver a higher quality of care at a lower cost. By developing innovative solutions that leverage clinical and administrative data across continuum of care including data from the Electronic Health Record (EHR), this organization helps build healthier communities, one person at a time. By aggregating data from multiple sources, health systems can view a more holistic record for their patients, which can prevent the duplication of tests, help avoid potentially dangerous drug interactions and lower costs that are associated with these adverse events. This approach ensures that health care facilities are delivering the best care to their patients effectively, efficiently, and rapidly.<br>
<br>
Cerner's Interoperability team is growing! Are you looking to join a fast-paced and highly collaborative team? As an Interoperability Project Manager you are responsible for the installation process and relationship with designated Cerner clients. You will provide support to the project team members and serve as the project manager deploying the Interoperability solutions. In this position you are the primary contact for all client-based activities. Additional responsibilities include: project planning and management, risk assessment, appropriate executive relationship management and project status review. We are glad you are considering joining our team and making your impact on health and care!
