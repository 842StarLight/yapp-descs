<h2>Staff Accountant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Angela L. Eyster CPA,LLC is searching for a staff accountant to join our team in our office in West York. We offer a flexible work environment, competitive salary, health insurance, paid time off, and a SIMPLEIRA.</p>
<p>The staff accountant willwork directly with our clients and be responsible for a wide variety of accounting duties including preparation of individual and business tax returns,payroll processing, sales and payroll tax, and bookkeeping.</p>
<p>Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's Degree in Accounting or work equivalent</li>
<li>Strong organization, oral and written communication skills</li>
<li>Ability to work independently and manage multiple projects and deadlines</li>
<li>Accuracy and attention to detail</li>
<li>Strong computer aptitude, including Microsoft Excel and Word as well as preferred experience with Ultra Tax software, Thomson Reuters CS, and Quickbooks and Sage accounting software</li>
<li>Must pass background check</li>
</ul>
<p>The successful candidatewill enjoy the diverse nature of our small business clients and appreciatebeing an integral part of their success. As part of our team, you will interactwith our clients throughout the year, providing personal service to help themgrow their business.</p>
<p>Job Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Address and resolve client related issues</li>
<li>Perform the accounting operations of client</li>
<li>Administers all aspects of payroll processing and tax filing for clients</li>
<li>Preparation of federal, state, and local income tax returns, estimates, extensions, forecasting, and analysis</li>
<li>Prepare and review bank reconciliations</li>
<li>Assist with monthly close process</li>
<li>Works on special projects</li>
<li>Performs month-end standard journal entries</li>
<li>Reviews balance sheet and income statement accounts</li>
<li>Prepare financial reporting for assigned clients</li>
<li>Comply with local, state, and federal government reporting requirements and work with third party for tax filings</li>
<li>Assist with new client set up for payroll and bookkeeping</li>
<li>Come to work on time</li>
<li>Be responsible to be productive</li>
</ul>
