<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What you’re good at
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>The Managing Director, Data Science will leverage statistical algorithms to provide decision makers across Schwab with insights and solutions to important business projects. This position will use a wide range of modeling techniques including but not limited to machine learning, deep learning, Bayesian modeling, time series and econometric modeling, design of experiments, marketing mix modeling, and other approaches. In addition, the position will also work on exploring most recent innovations in modeling techniques and applying those to structured and unstructured data across various business use cases.</div>
<div>
There will be a diverse set of business applications and project areas that this position will be responsible for. This includes areas such as:</div>
<ul>
<li>
Recommendation systems across different product, content and user levels</li>
<li>
NLP/NLU/NLG based Conversational assistants and other relevant use cases</li>
<li>
Anomaly detection for cybersecurity and other relevant use cases</li>
<li>
Predictive modeling for acquisition, retention and asset consolidation</li>
<li>
Business impact analyses to help the firm determine the ROI on strategic investments</li>
<li>
Customer lifetime value modeling</li>
<li>
Provide data science vision and leadership on using modeling and algorithms to uncover new insights that drive decisions to impact the business</li>
<li>
Support multiple business units and use cases on a wide range of advanced modeling projects</li>
<li>
Consult with business partners to understand and define their needs in terms of clear data science project deliverables</li>
<li>
Fully scope modeling projects including defining clear business value, determining modeling approach in discussion with data scientists, identifying right resources needed, development of execution plan and realizing business impact</li>
<li>
Work independently or collaboratively throughout the complete project lifecycle across a range of cross-functional teams including product managers, data engineers, architects, and other business plus engineering teams</li>
<li>
Clearly communicate methodologies and results to increase our business partners’ understanding and confidence in Advanced modeling capabilities and recommendations</li>
<li>
Work with UI/UX/market researchers, various business partners, engineering teams and other analytical teams to align and integrate modeling efforts in various end user applications</li>
<li>
Provide education and consultation to various cross-functional teams and decision makers to understand impact of AI to frame and solve complex business problems.</li>
</ul>
