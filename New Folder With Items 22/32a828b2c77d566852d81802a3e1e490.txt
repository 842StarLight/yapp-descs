<h2>PR Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>ACCOUNT EXECUTIVE </b><br>A Little About Giant Noise…<br>As a full-service public relations and social media agency, Giant Noise has a passion for generating creative and successful campaigns for our clients. We consider ourselves in the industry of interesting. Our unique roster and small firm size assures hands-on experience and synergy between all of our clients. Giant Noise is a fun, dynamic place to work. We are a small company that strives for big results. We work as a team and have a “whatever it takes” mentality to do a great job. We root for each other, respect each other, and appreciate each other, always. Giant Noise is seeking a public relations team player with excellent writing and communication skills to join our team.</p>
<p>A Little About The Job…</p>
<ul><li>Respond promptly to daily inquiries from media, acting as an accessible and credible representative of Giant Noise and the clients</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>Display thorough knowledge of media pitching and developing press materials</li>
<li>Write a variety of PR and digital content material, including but not limited to pitches, press releases, and social media posts</li>
<li>Actively participate in creative brainstorming sessions and offers original ideas</li>
<li>Assist in monitoring company, business, and industry trends and communicate effectively up the line keeping managers abreast of issues and opportunities</li>
<li>Contribute to client strategy planning with creative ideas</li>
<li>Effectively analyze data to provide recommendations on strategy and ROI</li>
<li>Develop network of local industry, editorial, and promotional contacts, with a focus on building effective media relationships to aid in securing coverage for clients</li>
<li>Effectively manage and delegate to Account Coordinators and interns</li>
<li>Lead various aspects of meetings with team leader</li>
<li>Plan and implement successful media or special events with supervision</li>
<li>Manage the process for drafting and oversee completion of agendas, recap reports, media coverage reports and monthly reports</li>
<li>Accurately track time on a daily basis and flag overages before they happen</li>
</ul>
<p>A Little About You…</p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrate strong writing, editing, organization, and proofreading skills</li>
<li>Demonstrate strong client and media relations skills</li>
<li>Demonstrate effective organizational skills: juggle multiple tasks at one time, are able to prioritize and meet deadlines, and keep managers aware of project status</li>
<li>Proficient using appropriate software (Word, PowerPoint, Excel, Cision, Google Suite, etc.)</li>
<li>Work well independently and collaboratively with team members</li>
<li>Understand and utilize research techniques</li>
<li>Present and interact professionally and strategically with clients and within the agency</li>
<li>Proactively seek out innovative growth opportunities and new projects</li>
<li>Solve problems effectively, efficiently, and creatively</li>
<li>Strive to set and achieve personal objectives and goals</li>
<li>Work to understand client’s industry, terminology and competitive set</li>
</ul>
<p>What It Takes…</p>
<ul>
<li>3 years agency experience</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree, preferably in Public Relations, Journalism, Communications, or related field</li>
<li>Able to present portfolio of writing samples that can be reviewed as part of the interview process</li>
</ul>
