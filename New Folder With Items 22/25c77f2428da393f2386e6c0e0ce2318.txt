<h2>Financial Reporting Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Has your paper napkin business plan turned into a billion-dollar reality? Ours has, and our employees have helped us get to where we are today; and as we continue to scale towards being the incomparable leader in our industry, we are looking for a <b>Financial Reporting Manager </b>to join our team to help write the next chapter with us.</p>
<p>
What started as a sketch on a napkin is now our world-class software platform that combines cutting-edge technology with outstanding client service and premier wealth management capabilities. <b>Shareworks by Morgan Stanley</b> is designed to simplify the complexities of equity plan management, while helping employees realize the full potential of their benefits.</p>
<p>
Located in <b>Tempe, Arizona</b> and reporting to the<b> Financial Reporting Team Lead, </b>the Financial Reporting Manager is responsible for providing expertise in financial reporting for Shareworks equity management! You will support our external clients, help internal teams with equity accounting and expensing questions, and support initiatives which further the Shareworks brand in the financial reporting community.</p>
<p><b>
Key Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Act as a specialist to provide financial reporting support for public clients</li>
<li>Share advanced expertise in Shareworks financial reporting capabilities</li>
<li>Provide thought leadership on industry trends and developments related to Canadian and US equity compensation plans</li>
<li>Lead education and training to internal teams on regulations, best practices, and product functionality related to financial reporting</li>
<li>Collaborate with internal experts on the development of financial reporting content for learning and development</li>
<li>Organize demonstrations, user or internal training sessions, and other client requests, as required and in-line with service level agreements</li>
<li>Provide feedback on product enhancements directly or on behalf of clients</li>
<li>Assist with key sales opportunities by demonstrating product reporting capabilities</li>
<li>Lead initiatives to promote Shareworks products and services in the financial reporting and accounting community (e.g., focus groups, educational forums, partner relationships)</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>University degree in finance, accounting, business management, economics, or related education is required</li>
<li>Certified Equity Professional (CEP) and/or Certified Public Accountant (CPA) designations considered an asset</li>
<li>Knowledge of equity based incentive plans and equity valuations, stock purchase plans, and financial reporting requirements for stock-based compensation for public and private companies considered an asset</li>
<li>Demonstrated success with training on software and complex financial concepts desired, especially with a diverse audience (administrative users, senior financial reporting analysts, CFO’s)</li>
<li>Demonstrated competency with common technology and technical concepts, including but not limited to Microsoft Office products</li>
<li>Minimum 2 years’ experience working in an accounting or financial reporting role</li>
<li>High degree of analytical, attention-to-detail, and problem solving skills</li>
<li>Excellent customer service focus and commitment to service standards</li>
<li>Ability to manipulate and convert data using various tools and data formats</li>
<li>Comfortable in a fast-paced and evolving environment which includes ongoing learning and training opportunities</li>
<li>Occasional travel to client meetings may be required</li>
<br>
</ul>
