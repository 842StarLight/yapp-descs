<p><b>SUMMARY</b></p>
<p>As a LoadUp Dispatcher, you are an integral part of team LoadUp offering support to both our driver teams and customer service team.</p>
<p>Some of your key duties include:</p>
<p>· Handle incoming service orders, directs drivers, and resolves service issues.</p>
<p>· Communicate with LoadUp drivers to assist with problem resolution in real time.</p>
<p>· Interacts with customer service, sales, and operational staff</p>
<p>· Uses LoadUp software to manage order information and monitor driver activity</p>
<p>Shifts vary as a member of the dispatch team is on site at our Atlanta Headquarters 8am to 8pm Monday to Saturday.</p>
<p><b>RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<p>To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each duty effectively and efficiently.</p>
<p>· Monitors and ensures all orders are assigned to drivers as they are received, using information on customer needs, drivers’ locations, and daily factors to ensure overall driver success</p>
<p>· Monitors Driver’s jobs throughout the day to ensure on time performance and successful resolution to customer requests.</p>
<p>· Handles fields incoming calls and texts from customer service and sales department; manages requests for pick-up</p>
<p>· Initiates outbound calls to drivers resolving pick-up and other issues</p>
<p>· Troubleshoots and resolves potential driver and scheduling problems before they result in service issues</p>
<p>· Records and documents information from Drivers and distributes to appropriate departments</p>
<p><b>REQUIREMENTS</b></p>
<p>The requirements listed below are representative of the qualifications necessary to perform the job.</p>
<p>A. Education/Experience</p>
<p>· Education: High School Diploma or GED</p>
<p>· Experience: 6 months of relevant work experience with working in a team setting, with the ability to communicate to several areas effectively. Multi-tasking in a fast-paced work environment with excellent organizational skills.</p>
<p>· Experience preferred: Experience in transportation, logistics, or a role requiring decision making and critical thinking.</p>
<p>C. Other Knowledge, Skills or Abilities Required</p>
<p>· Computer skills with ability to handle multiple programs and systems</p>
<p>· Must be able to communicate effectively and professionally via e-mail, telephone, and texting</p>
<p>· Must have the desire and ability to learn LoadUp proprietary software</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Salary: $30,000.00 /year</p>
