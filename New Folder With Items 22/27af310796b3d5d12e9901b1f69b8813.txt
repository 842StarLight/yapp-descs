Background</i></b>
</div>
<div>
We’re looking for a sales enthusiast who wants to deep-dive into our sales world as an SDR. Are you looking for a sales job that’s a bit out of the ordinary? Then you’ve come to the right place.</div>
<div>
<br>
<b><i>
Your Team</i></b><br>
Can you relate to the stereotypical sales role, doing anything for commission? No? Good! Because sales at Frontify is a bit different: more consultative, no commission, and many other benefits.<br>
<br>
<b><i>Your Job</i></b>
</div>
<div>
You'll be an SDR for the US market, working closely with the market’s Account Executive (AE).<br>
<br>
As an SDR you’re responsible for the first part of the sales cycle. This includes doing outbound sales; creating a great first impression with prospects, following up and qualifying inbound leads, develop customized pitches for different target accounts, working closely together with the market’s AE to drive outbound prospecting activities, accompany the AE to different trips, and much more.<br>
