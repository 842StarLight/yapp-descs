<div>
<div>
<b>
PURPOSE STATEMENT</b>
</div>
<div><p>Are you an experienced Maintenance Supervisor with drive and determination to grow and advance your manufacturing career? If you are, we would like for you to consider working for a stable, family owned, company with sales in 110 countries and a workplace with a great culture. Rich Products is seeking a strong Maintenance Supervisor for our Vineland, New Jersey operation that will bring strong leadership and organizational skills to our manufacturing process.</p></div>
</div>
<div>
<div>
<b>
KEY ACCOUNTABILITIES/OUTCOMES</b>
</div>
<div>
<p>What you'll be doing:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Coordinates the efforts of maintenance, electric, and hydraulic work teams and provides maintenance and mechanical engineering support to those teams.</li>
<li>
Mentors and motivates associates, providing training and development to optimize their performance and personal growth.</li>
<li>
Communicates performance standards to associates, recognizing and rewarding individual and team accomplishments and counseling performance problems.</li>
<li>
Analyzes mechanical and operational problems on assigned equipment, planning and implementing corrective action. Notifies department of recurring equipment malfunctions and corrective action taken, and quality problems or supplies needed.</li>
<li>
Schedules preventive maintenance checks and inspection of assigned equipment ensuring no disruption to the production schedule.</li>
<li>
Analyzes and records cause of production delays on daily production report. Maintains and reports other required operational information to management.</li>
<li>
Ensures that plant facilities and grounds are maintained in accordance with relevant regulations, company and regulatory standards and fiscal consideration.</li>
<li>
Assists in preparing major equipment bids, recommending purchase when replacement or new equipment is required. Represents the company’s position and interest in dealing with outside contractors, vendors, and engineering consultants.</li>
<li>
May have responsibility for plant safety including and coordinating annual fire safety inspections with local fire departments; arranging and coordinating internal safety inspections; and implementing solutions to identified safety deficiencies.</li>
<li>
Assists in the development of policies and procedures related to maintenance and safety.</li>
<li>
Assists in annual budget preparation as it relates to safety and maintenance of facilities, grounds, and equipment.</li>
<li>
May direct the machine parts storeroom operation in smaller facilities.</li>
<li>
Keeps abreast of latest manufacturing technologies and systems and engineering concepts.</li>
<li>
Performs other related responsibilities, as needed, to support Rich Products Corporation’s business objectives.</li>
<li>
Work directly with Process Improvement Teams and assist in preparation and execution of team charters.</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>
<p></p>
<br>
<div>
<div>
<b>
KNOWLEDGE/SKILLS/EXPERIENCE</b>
</div>
<div>
<p>What you'll need:</p>
<ul>
<li>
HS degree or GED and HVAC licensing; BA/BS degree in Engineering or related field preferred</li>
<li>
3 years supervisory experience in a manufacturing environment requiring knowledge of refrigeration, plumbing, industrial electrical and mechanical systems and maintenance, and automated manufacturing process controls and equipment</li>
<li>
Demonstrated knowledge and application of preventative maintenance techniques/scheduling and safety management</li>
<li>
Working knowledge of FA regulations, Good Manufacturing Practices (GMP), sanitation processes/procedures and HACCP</li>
<li>
Demonstrated knowledge of efficient and safe manufacturing operations to include product and associate safety, OSHA standards, product quality (success rates), cost control (minimizing downtime and waste, optimizing yield)</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to direct, coach, and train associates as well as plan, monitor and schedule work</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to analyze and resolve problems</li>
<li>
Demonstrated mechanical and spatial aptitude, including the ability to disassemble and reassemble equipment</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to formulate and understand complex mathematical equations</li>
<li>
Proficient using Excel or other spreadsheet software</li>
</ul>
