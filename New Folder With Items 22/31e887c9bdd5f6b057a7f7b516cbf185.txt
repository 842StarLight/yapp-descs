<h2>Financial Analyst, FP&A</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<p>The Financial Analyst (FA) will support the growing business at Corelle Brands as well as the recent acquisition of Instant Brands (makers of the Instant Pot®). The FA will work across multiple functions in a highly visible capacity including, but not limited to: Business Unit Leadership, Corporate Cost Center Owners, Sales, and will have direct interaction with the CEO/CFO.
</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What will you do?
</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Support the Annual Plan and periodic financial forecast processes. Analyze the data comprising the financial statements and prepare summary reports and presentations.
</li>
<li>Periodic P&amp;L’s and Customer Profitability reconciliation/tie-out.
</li>
<li>Provide timely, accurate and relevant information on profitability &amp; key metrics using budget, forecast, and prior year as a comparative.
</li>
<li>Learn and utilize Adaptive Insights (FP&amp;A tool) to create ad hoc financial reports and analyses.
</li>
<li>A&amp;P (Advertising &amp; Promotion) and NPD (New Product Development); Build models
</li>
<li>Partner with corporate cost center owners to assist in analyzing current/planned spend as well as identifying major drivers to budget, forecast, and prior year.
</li>
<li>Routinely engaging with business teams to support ad hoc analyses based on current trajectory and needs of the business
</li>
<li>Business analytics and support of strategic planning
</li>
</ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Required Qualifications:
</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>0 - 3 years’ experience in a finance role with a Bachelor’s degree in Finance, Accounting, or Economics
</li>
<li>Willing to put an effort to learn the business
</li>
<li>Advanced Excel &amp; PowerPoint skills
</li>
<li>Demonstrated capability to manage multiple competing priorities simultaneously and drive projects to completion without explicit direction or detailed instructions
</li>
<li>Proven ability to develop strategic relationships with business partners and influence decisions EEO Corelle Brands. participates in E-Verify
</li>
</ul>
<p><i>EEO Corelle Brands participates in E-Verify</i></p>
