<p><b>Position Overview</b></p>
<p>
Soundstripe is looking for a Content Marketing Specialist to help with the execution of web copy activities that fit within the broader scope of our digital marketing strategy. Reporting to our Director of Digital Marketing, you’ll be working closely with him as well as our creative team and VP of Marketing to help scale our digital strategy. As we continue to pursue organic search, paid media and email as channels for growth, we are looking for help with activities such as blogging, web copy with the intent to rank in search, ad copy, long-form assets such as ebooks, and email copy, among others.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>
As Content Marketing Specialist, responsibilities include but are not limited to:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>SEO-driven blogging.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Optimization of blog posts to rank in search.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Guest blogging with partnering publications.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Assisting with the location of new publications to partner with (temporarily).</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Ideating topics, strategies and content for the editorial calendar (managed by our Director of Digital and VP of Marketing).</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Email copywriting &amp; optimization.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Working with the Director of Digital Marketing to improve the conversion rates associated with our email campaigns.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Web copywriting for new webpages as well as pages that need to be refreshed or optimized.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Execution and ownership of bi-weekly newsletter from Soundstripe. This includes tracking engagement with the newsletter and signing up for goals aimed at improvement.</p>
</li>
</ul>
