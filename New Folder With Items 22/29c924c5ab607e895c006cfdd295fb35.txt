<h2>Corporate Counsel, Marketing Data and Demand Gen</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>Come join Autodesk’s Legal team as Corporate Counsel to partner with our marketing and demand generation colleagues as they grow the business and help our customers imagine, design and make a better world. We are passionate about a future where everyone can live well and sustainably by using cutting edge technologies like artificial intelligence and automation. Architects, engineers, designers, and manufacturers use our software solutions to design and build anything. We are also committed to building a diverse, inclusive and collegial culture and supporting world-class programs that give back to the community.<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Serve as the first point of contact for day-to-day legal support to our marketing operations and demand generation teams. This will include advising on technology solutions, digital and social marketing, promotional activities and performance-based initiatives to ensure consistency with applicable law and company policies and procedures</li>
<li>
Participate as a trusted advisor and legal partner to business leadership</li>
<li>
Engage proactively with product, marketing, sales and compliance legal team members as needed</li>
<li>
Provide regular updates on relevant industry standards, practices and laws and make recommendations in practice, procedures and policies where needed</li>
<li>
Help develop and provide tools and training to business stakeholders and legal colleagues in support of our marketing legal projects, programs, and policies</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
Minimum Qualifications</b><br>
<ul>
<li>JD from an accredited/recognized university and bar membership in good standing</li>
<li>
3 years of combined of law firm and/or in-house experience, preferably working with digital marketing technologies, programmatic or performance-based marketing activities of high tech, internet, media, and/or advertising companies</li>
<li>
Solid knowledge on relevant marketing issues and laws, ideally as to user privacy, data use, martech and/or advertising</li>
<li>
Clear, concise written, oral and interpersonal communication skills; ability to interact comfortably with senior leaders</li>
<li>
Demonstrated experience working independently and managing multiple projects at once to meet deadlines</li>
<li>
Collaborates well in cross-functional teams dealing with matrixed issues</li>
<li>
Exercises sound and practical legal judgment to help business solutions in fast-paced environment</li>
</ul>
