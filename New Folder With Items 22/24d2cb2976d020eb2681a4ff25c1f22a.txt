<div>
<p>Summary</p>
<p>Elements Massage Pearland, a locally owned small business is seeking an outgoing, competitive, sales-driven individual to join our fun and outgoing team as a Wellness Membership Advisor (WMA).</p>
<p>The most important responsibility of our Wellness Membership Advisor is selling massage services and memberships to our clients. The WMA will also focus on booking appointments/scheduling, maintaining our studio clean and inviting, answering the phone professionally and politely to ensure a flawless guest experience and completing tasks left by the manager and owner . WMA’s are the first people our clients interact with, they are responsible for ensuring a smooth visit from phone call to check out. If you don't love people, aren't comfortable with sales or motivated to reach goals, then this is not the position for you.</p>
<ul><li>Wellness and community are what we bring to the marketplace. Elements is the best part of our clients’ day, and that starts with our employees first.</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>We Provide:</p>
<ul>
<li>A comfortable atmosphere and environment where you are inspired to change lives – including yours</li>
<li>Free monthly massage</li>
<li>Monthly Sale Bonuses</li>
<li>Variety in your work that is both challenging and rewarding</li>
<li>The opportunity to be part of a team that is focused on the wellness of others</li>
<li>A warm, professional atmosphere with mutual respect among our team</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Position Requirements:</p>
<ul>
<li>Sales/Customer Service experience</li>
<li>Attention to detail and ability to follow instructions (both written and oral)</li>
<li>Ability to communicate effectively (both written and oral)</li>
<li>Ability to multitask / Keep calm and confident under stress</li>
<li>Computer skills / Experience with Microsoft office and ability to quickly learn and navigate our online scheduling software program</li>
<li>Must be able to work nights and weekends</li>
<li>Flexibility with schedule</li>
<li>Massage studio experience, a plus</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>This is a part-time position</p>
<p>Elements Massage is a smoke free environment</p>
<p>Elements Massage is closed on all major holidays</p>
