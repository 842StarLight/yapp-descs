<h2>Product Proposal Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div><div><div><div>
<p>When you’re part of the team at Thermo Fisher Scientific, you’ll do important work, like helping customers in finding cures for cancer, protecting the environment or making sure our food is safe. Your work will have real-world impact, and you’ll be supported in achieving your career goals.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
How will you make an impact?</b></p>
<p>
As a Product Proposal Specialist, you will utilize your product knowledge to support sales and to serve customers through product conversion related activities</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
What will you do?
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop and maintain cross reference data and tools that are essential to facilitate competitive and strategic alternative cross reference initiatives.</li>
<li>
Identify opportunities to improve current processes.</li>
<li>
Support the sales team in competitive bid situations to win new business.</li>
<li>
Consult with Sales to develop business strategies specific for each customer based on supplier and equivalency preferences.</li>
<li>
Recommend strategy-specific cross references.</li>
<li>
Leverage existing data and identify new cross references by researching product specifications and utilizing product knowledge.</li>
<li>
Identify alternative cross references to suggest margin improvement and customer savings options.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with Product Management to proactively identify portfolio gaps, enhance cross reference data, and provide meaningful data to assist with pricing projects.</li>
<li>
Prepare weekly project reports and update monthly metrics on profit improvement projects and other cross reference initiatives.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
How will you get here?</b><br>
<b>Education</b></p>
<ul><li>
Bachelor’s degree in biology, chemistry, or related scientific field.</li></ul>
<p><b>
Experience</b></p>
<ul><li>
2+ years of laboratory experience required.</li></ul>
<p><b>
Knowledge, Skills, Abilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Broad knowledge of laboratory equipment, instrumentation and consumables</li>
<li>
Familiarity with major laboratory supply brands, both internal and external to the company.</li>
<li>
Thorough knowledge of Microsoft office products. Proficiency with Excel, Access, and Cognos and an ability to quickly grasp new software tools preferred.</li>
<li>
Ability to work under tight deadlines and demonstrate flexibility with priorities.</li>
<li>
Must be detailed-oriented, analytical and have the ability to multi-task.</li>
<li>
Strong team player with good interpersonal skills.</li>
<li>
Willingness to learn and seek process improvement.</li>
</ul>
