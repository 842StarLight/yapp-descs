<h2>Senior Product Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">---------------------------------
<br>The brain behind the Go-to-Market
<br>---------------------------------
<br><br>
Coveo partners with some of the world's largest enterprise technology ecosystems where delivering a unified and relevant experience for customers, partners and employees is critical to achieving business results with each of the respective platforms. In order to give our products a clear and compelling voice while achieving our business targets, we are currently looking for a Senior Product Marketing Manager who will help develop and execute our go-to-market strategy across our Coveo for Salesforce and Coveo for ServiceNow product lines.
<br><br>
A story-teller &amp; a strategic mind
<br>-------------------------------------
<br><br>
You will need to be adept at building and telling a relevant and strategic narrative through the lens of customer personas and stories. You will need to be a credible partner with product management, marketing, sales, alliances and others to align product launches and growth with customer and partner needs, enable commercial teams, and position successfully against the competition.
<br><br>
<b>Your main responsibilities:</b><br>
---------------------------------
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Partner closely with product management and marketing teams to understand the product strategy and fit, define use cases, understand competitive offerings and articulate value propositions</li>
<li>Lead product launch plans that are fully synced with business &amp; revenue goals and aligned with pipeline generation targets</li>
<li>Collaborate with demand generation and corporate marketing teams to ensure integrated, market-tuned programs are building the product pipeline</li>
<li>Develop compelling messaging and feature benefits that lead to Coveo's differentiators and truly resonate with our target customers</li>
<li>Lead sales and partner readiness initiatives in collaboration with product management that significantly impact the sales cycle</li>
<li>Work directly with counterparts at the partner companies to further drive awareness, pipeline and awareness</li>
<li>Manage the creation of a wide variety of high-impact marketing collateral and compelling sales content including web pages, eBooks, presentations, product information, demo videos and more</li>
<li>Inform the product and marketing strategy by collecting and analyzing customer feedback and reviewing usage analytics</li>
</ul>
--------------------------------
<br><br>
<b>What it will take to impress us:</b><br>
--------------------------------
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>A passionate product marketer with 7+ years B2B software product marketing experience.</li>
<li>Proven ability to drive the go-to-market strategy and release readiness process with product management, marketing, sales and others.</li>
<li>Be able to distill highly technical concepts into persuasive marketing content for diverse audiences.</li>
<li>Ability to engage with and earn the respect of both deeply technical IT leaders, industry experts and business leaders alike.</li>
<li>Exceptional verbal and written communication skills to both internal and external stakeholder.</li>
<li>Prior experience at an enterprise search, personalization, content management, customer service or other marketing technology platform company.</li>
<li>Experience with Salesforce Clouds and/or ServiceNow a major plus.</li>
<li>BA or BS in marketing, business, computer science/engineering or related field.</li>
<li>Ability to work cross-organizationally to align multiple stakeholders and teams, especially with Sales, Marketing, and Product Management.</li>
<li>Highly collaborative and inclusive work style where you check the ego at the door.</li>
<li>Willing to travel 25% of the time.</li>
</ul>
We have a fit? In this particular role, you are dealt with an extremely good set of cards, it will be yours to play them well and win the pot. Send us your resume (and a cover letter) and convince us that you are a must have rather than a nice to have.
