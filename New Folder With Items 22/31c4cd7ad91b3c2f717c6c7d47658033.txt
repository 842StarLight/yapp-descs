<div><b>GENERAL DESCRIPTION</b></div>
</div></div>
<br>
<div><div>
<div>Handles invoices for multiple properties and maintains/updates payable information in the accounting software. Payable systems include: Sage, Docuware, Excel</div>

<br>
</ul></div></div>

<div><b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS</b></div>
</div></div>
<br>
<div><div>
<div><i>Primary responsibilities include, but are not limited to, the following:</i></div>
<ul>
<li>High level of comfort with various systems and applications</li>
<li>Basic knowledge of general accounting</li>
<li>Processing invoices and payables for multi-properties</li>
<li>Adhere to proper accounts payable processes ensuring invoices are appropriately routed through the electronic workflow system</li>
<li>Post approved transactions into the general ledger accounting software under routine and complex scenarios</li>
<li>Prepare payment batches on specific payment dates and other payments as needed</li>
<li>Respond to payment inquiries and other accounts payable requests</li>
<li>Reconcile monthly vendor statements and resolve outstanding invoices/issues</li>
<li>Reconcile accounts payable transactions</li>
<li>Perform accounts payable customer service support with vendors and in-house departments</li>
<li>Attention to detail to maintain accuracy and thoroughness</li>
<li>Ability to prioritize and plan to meet deadlines</li>
<li>Knowledge of sales and use taxes</li>
<li>Knowledge of government reporting for 1099 formâ€™s</li>
<br>
</ul>
