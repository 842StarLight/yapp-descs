<h2>Summer 2020 Business Process Technology (IT) Internship</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>IT and Computer Science students will work in our Business Process &amp; Technology (BPT) function and have challenging projects in a variety of disciplines such as Information Security, Applications Development Maintenance &amp; Integration, Information Management, Architecture and Infrastructure &amp; Operations.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>BUSINESS PROCESS &amp; TECHNOLOGY INTERNSHIP PROGRAM:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Leverage technology skills to help solve business problems and drive business decisions.</li>
<li>
Opportunities to interact with cross-functional business partners (i.e. Sales, Marketing, Finance, RD&amp;E) on challenging projects.</li>
<li>
Leadership Exposure; interaction with Business Process &amp; Technology leaders.</li>
<li>
Coaching; receive support from an experienced manager, technical lead and mentor.</li>
<li>
Professional and personal development encouraged throughout your internship.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
QUALIFICATIONS:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Full-time undergraduate student pursuing a degree or minor in Information Systems, Information Technology or Computer Science. Preference will be given to students of UW-Madison and UW-Whitewater.</li>
<li>
Cumulative GPA of 3.0 strongly preferred</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
DESIRED SKILLS:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to confidently interact with business partners and clients</li>
<li>
Can be successful in both an individual and team atmosphere</li>
<li>
Goal-oriented; results driven and intellectually curious</li>
<li>
Strong analytical, technical skills</li>
<li>
Problem solving, critical thinking skills</li>
<li>
Self-starter – seeks out challenge</li>
<li>
Time management and organizational skills</li>
<li>
Demonstrated leadership experience within or outside of school</li>
<li>
Excellent oral, written &amp; communication/presentation skills</li>
</ul>
