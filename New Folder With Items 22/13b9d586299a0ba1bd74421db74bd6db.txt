<b>Job Summary and Scope of Responsibility:</b><br>
The Business Systems Analyst is responsible for the system administration of Service Cloud for customer care, technical support and distribution teams by supporting the administration and enhancements of the Salesforce Service Cloud platform and SAP. This position identifies, owns and manages Customer Experience (CE) improvement projects related to these systems, delivering high-quality solutions without user interruption. The Analyst will be a designated SAP super user and oversee super user activity within the CE functions, including systems testing.Working with IT partners, he/she will refine requirements and design value-added functionality and reporting solutions for both SAP and within Salesforce Service Cloud as well as other internal tools to meet internal customer needs for information, process improvement and decision support. This position recommends and prioritizes projects based upon in-depth analysis and demonstrated ability to identify root cause, driving benefits to the CE users.
<br><br>
<b>Essential Functions:</b><br>
1. Maintain professional and technical knowledge by attending educational workshops, professional publications, and participating in professional societies.
<br>2. Protect Chamberlain’s reputation by keeping information confidential.
