<h2>Lead Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Qualitrol would like to invite an experienced engineer with proven leadership abilities to join our team! If you have a passion for engineering and a passion for leading and developing others in the field, this is the role for you!
<br><br>
The Lead Engineer is responsible for all product development and sustaining activities for the assigned product groups. This includes hardware, firmware and software design, as well as engineering services. The Lead Engineer will lead the activities of an organization in the sustainment of released products, components, and systems. The position provides strategic direction and tactical planning for released product improvement, project management and manufacturability issues. Day-to-day activities include:
<br>Manage internal and external resources to meet project demands
<br>Streamline and manage product customization efforts
<br>Create and implement tools and processes that improve time-to-market, product quality, manufacturability and customer satisfaction with Qualitrol products
<br>Support in building capabilities of the Engineering/R&amp;D team to provide sufficient technical resources legacy product support
<br>Provide technical assistance to Customers and Sales as required
<br>Drive product enhancements, derivatives, and specials as necessary to meet agreed upon customer needs
<br>Reviews new designs during the development phase and prior to the issuance of production drawings and recommends modifications where advisable to minimize cost and conform to production capabilities and limitations
<br>Determines methods for manufacture during design of products
<br>Recommends design modifications to improve ease of manufacturing Develops and ensures robust engineering processes in accordance with ISO and FDA requirements
<br>Performs duties in accordance with establish quality standards
<br>Manages direct reports as assigned
<br>Performs such similar, comparable or related duties as may be required
<br>BS in Engineering required, MBA or MS Engineering preferred
<br>Proven success in management and design3+ years of leadership experience, managing development organizations
<br>Prior experience of embedded software development and product development
<br>Strong oral and written communication skills
<br>Good interpersonal skills
<br>Ability to handle many tasks simultaneously
<br>Ability to work independently
<br>Ability to travel
<br>Product development and Manufacturing experience
<br>Knowledge of systems, hardware and software
<br>Knowledge of Continuous improvement tools
<br>Strong Customer Service skills
<br>Strong sense of urgency (to get things accomplished)
