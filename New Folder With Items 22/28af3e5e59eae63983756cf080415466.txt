 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">

<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Company Description</b></h3>
<p>
Hamilton Company is a global enterprise headquartered in Reno, Nevada with manufacturing facilities in Reno, Boston and Bonaduz, Switzerland. Branch sales offices are located in France, Germany, and the United Kingdom.</p>
<p>
We are the worldwide leader in the design and manufacture of manual, semi-automated and robotic products for precision fluid measuring. For over 56 years, Hamilton has been satisfying customer needs by combining quality materials with skilled workmanship, ensuring the highest level of performance of every precision fluid measuring device we manufacture.</p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Job Description</b></h3>
<p>
Provide application and technical support to customers for Hamilton products. Act as a technical liaison between customers and Hamilton Sales team to meet and maintain customer needs. Operate within a designated territory and provide onsite and remote customer support. Develop advanced application protocols and provide support for complex instrumentation. Provide technical support and training for Hamilton Sales and Applications team. This position will preferably be based in the New York / New Jersey area.</p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Job Functions</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>
Develop application-specific protocols on Hamilton products by interpreting procedures provided by the customer or Hamilton Sales team.</li>
<li>
Perform troubleshooting of application protocols and instrumentation.</li>
<li>
Optimize liquid handling performance of instrumentation.</li>
<li>
Provide basic and advanced software and hardware training. Develop and deliver training material for Hamilton Sales and Applications team.</li>
<li>
Develop application-specific protocols for complex automated instrumentation, which require integration with third party devices.</li>
<li>
Demonstrate usage of advanced programming skills and software functions to control instrumentation.</li>
<li>
Participate and contribute on instrumentation which requires Factory and Site Acceptance Testing.</li>
<li>
Contribute to company knowledge base.</li>
<li>
Maintain support for existing customers by providing additional application protocols, training, etc.</li>
<li>
Understand customer expectations and schedule work accordingly.</li>
<li>
Document application support work and customer visits within a company database or other required format.</li>
<li>
Work independently and make intelligent decisions based on experience and knowledge.</li>
<li>
Maintain professional relationships with customers and co-workers.</li>
</ul>
