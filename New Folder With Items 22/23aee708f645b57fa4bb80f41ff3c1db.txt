<p></p>
<p>
VMware is looking for a talented leader and team player to fill a highly visible role in our Worldwide Customer Strategy organization. The Manager, Global GTM Strategy, will be responsible for driving Go-to-Market Strategy, reporting to the Director, Global GTM Strategy. As a candidate for this role, you will have a proven track record of leading GTM strategy development and transformation in high-tech companies, specifically driving a shift from Product to Solution selling and a transformation to Cloud and SaaS business models. This is a critical role that will help VMware transform our GTM Strategy to support our growth to a $10Bn company.</p>
<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b><br>
Job Role and Responsibilities:</b></h2>
<p>
The responsibilities of this role include working within the Corporate Intelligence, Planning and GTM Strategy team to build strategies that will drive emerging product growth and drive business in new markets and new customer segments leveraging our routes to market as well as our existing and emerging strategic partnerships. This role will be involved in conducting the analysis, defining the strategy, defining the supporting sales, partner, coverage models and pipeline acceleration as well as defining any other programs that will be critical to realizing success. The successful candidate will be a ‘Strategic Doer’ with great energy and initiative with leadership qualities that inspire teaming and trust to influence cross functional teams in support of defining and evolving our GTM Strategy and Operations.</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Define GTM strategy for new VMware offerings, led by internal and external research, benchmarking and analytics for data driven decision making</li>
<li>Assess effectiveness of current GTM strategy across VMware offering portfolio, and refine and optimize accordingly</li>
<li>Support the evolution of GTM for Solution Selling and SaaS transformation</li>
<li>Evangelize GTM strategy by engaging cross-functional teams in a matrixed organization including Sales, Marketing, Services, Customer Success and Product Marketing/Management
</li>
<li>Perform business analysis and support real-time execution on high-impact strategic projects and mission-critical initiatives
</li>
<li>Support sales and partner organization with market analysis, capacity analysis, customer segmentation and predictive analytics and reporting</li>
<li>Drive mechanisms to gather feedback from customers and customer-facing teams at VMware to incorporate into strategy definition</li>
<li>Drive alignment across regions and shared functions to implement the sales and partner GTM strategy globally</li>
<li>Work with business operations functions to ensure that the GTM strategy can be effectively executed</li>
<li>Develop external benchmarking to develop a best-in-class strategic account sales structure to support our largest and highest potential customers</li>
<li>Evaluate and analyze effectiveness of sales models and programs. Determine what programs should start, stop or continue</li>
</ul>
<p></p>

<li>Great business acumen, financial insight and strategic thinking skills</li>
