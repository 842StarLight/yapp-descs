<p><b>JOB SUMMARY</b></p>
<p>
 Serve as a clinical member of the Dermatology Care Team by assisting in medical and cosmetic dermatology care. Role include administrative functions as well as serving as a medical scribe.</p>
<p><b>
RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<p><b>
 </b><b><i>Essential Functions</i></b></p>
<p><b>
Communication</b></p>
<p><b>
 Functions as a key member of the care team and provides exceptional patient service at all times. Answers phones, conveys messages, and coordinates appropriate communications between the provider and patient. Transfers related information to others as appropriate to maintain effective working relationships in the delivery of patient care.</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Greets all patients friendly, courteously and respectfully. Maintains a positive attitude with patients, physicians, and co-workers at all times.</li>
<li>Responds and delivers messages accurately and in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Uses appropriate etiquette and telephone procedures when placing, receiving and transferring calls.</li>
<li>Maintains the strictest patient and personal confidentiality.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p><b>
</b></p>
<p><b>Clerical</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Performs a variety of clerical/administrative duties as assigned to ensure efficient utilization of time within the health center.</li>
<li>Maintains patient electronic medical records and other information; files all incoming patient record documents in designated location in patient electronic medical record.</li>
<li>Assists with appointment scheduling of off-site tests and procedures, consultations, surgeries and treatments.</li>
<li>Completes Prior Authorizations, referrals and other care authorizations as assigned.</li>
<li>Monitors workload assigned to the individual and/or Clinical Work Group and complete actions, telephone and web encounters in a timely manner. Documentation of all actions taken using established workflows.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Medical Scribe:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Performs all clerical and information technology functions for a physician.</li>
<li>The duties of a Medical Scribe are to document the patient history, physical examination, social history, family history, past medical history, impressions made by the physician, and any additional information pertinent to the patient’s visit.</li>
<li>Enters appropriate prescriptions, laboratory, pathology and radiology order and referrals per provider’s instructions and care plan.</li>
<li>Computer System access is assigned based on manager’s access level request and on the minimum necessary described in this job description.</li>
<li>Accompany the physician into the exam room and accurately transcribe the patient’s history and physical exam.</li>
<li>Transcribe all pertinent diagnoses, symptoms, and any follow-up instructions.</li>
<li>Document performed procedures and treatment.</li>
<li>Prepare and print or publish to the patient portal patient education material.</li>
<li>Create referrals and letters as directed by the physician.</li>
<li>Perform other duties, including administrative duties, as needed or assigned.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Clinical Care:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Rooming of patients and conducting standard rooming and documentation as set forth by organization policies and procedures</li>
<li>Accurate capture of patient’s family and medical history, medications, allergies, and vital signs</li>
<li>Prepare exam rooms between patients and maintaining appropriate stocking of supplies</li>
<li>Prepares a clinical brief to provider before entering the room to communication chief complaints, history and observations</li>
<li>Ensure efficient through put of patients by working collaboratively with entire team</li>
<li>Collects and processes blood, tissue or other specimens for transport to appropriate labs with needed paperwork.</li>
<li>Sets up for all medical and cosmetic treatments based on patient scheduling and provider care plan.</li>
<li>Prepare patients for laser and cosmetic treatments including prepping treatment areas (ex. washing, applications of topicals and serums) based on treatment plan determined by the provider, taking pre- and post- photography, reviewing informed consent, screen for contraindications to treatments</li>
<li>Cleaning, QC and regular review of treatment equipment. Takes responsibility for notifying management of any issues with equipment.</li>
<li>Assisting providers during treatments and procedures and as instructed by providers.</li>
<li>Experience and knowledge of medical and cosmetic Dermatology procedures including but not limited to, Botox, chemical peels, ED&amp;C, Biopsy, Wart and Acne treatment, PDT, laser hair removal, etc.</li>
<li>Performing Chemical Peels as instructed by provider in accordance with established care plan</li>
<li>Promote and sell cosmetic products up to and including completing financial transaction and sales tracking.</li>
<li>Regular review of cosmetic and medical product and supply inventory.</li>
<li>Placing necessary orders using the established protocols and systems and being stewards for the responsible use of clinic resources.</li>
<li>Exercise knowledge of medical and cosmetic treatments to assist in explaining and relaying care instructions per providers care plan.</li>
<li>Clear and effective communication and team collaboration with Medical Office Scheduling and Registration team.</li>
<li>Other duties as trained and assigned.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p><i>
</i></p>
<p><i>Other Functions</i></p>
<p><b>
Environmental:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Participates in maintaining an environment that is conducive to patient care, comfort, and privacy, as well as, employee safety.</li>
<li>Recognizes hazardous conditions and responds either by correcting the situation or reporting it to the Practice Manager/ Administrator.</li>
<li>Demonstrates safe and proper usage of all health center equipment and attends equipment usage in services when offered.</li>
<li>Ensures waiting room, examination and treatment rooms, bathrooms, medical record area, kitchen and all areas of the health center are clean and clear of refuse.</li>
<li>Requires full range of body motion including handling and lifting of patients, manual and finger dexterity and eye-hand coordination 100% of the time.</li>
<li>Involves standing and walking 50% of the time. Sitting 50% of the time.</li>
<li>Occasionally lifts and carries items weighing up to 50 lbs. 5% of the time.</li>
<li>Requires normal visual acuity and hearing 100% of the time.</li>
<li>Potential risk to blood borne exposure 100% of the time.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Policy and Procedures:
 </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Observes department policies and procedure, environment and infection control standards.</li>
<li>Interprets hospital policies, regulations, standard to other staff, patients and visitors to ensure compliance.</li>
<li>Attends required all required in-services on system improvement, Safety, Infection control, etc.</li>
<li>Observes OSHA regulations including principles of standard precautions.</li>
<li>Cleans the exam/treatment rooms after each patient and/or procedure including equipment used in accordance with infection control guidelines.
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Professional Growth.
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Participates in departmental meetings, appropriate in-services and educational opportunities.</li>
<br>
</ul>
