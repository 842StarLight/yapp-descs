<h2>IT Technician II</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Employee Contract Type</p>
The Information Technology Store Support Helpdesk Technician II, provides a differentiated customer experience to Signet’s internal customers by supporting and performing the intake and triage functions of support for retail store operations. The position supports first level phone support agents, as well as, functions in the phone support role for Signet stores including resolving procedural inquiries; performing initial troubleshooting for all point-of-sale systems, corporate systems, and remote user systems; and thoroughly documenting each contact in the appropriate systems. In the support and Leadership function this position will create departmental processes; support departmental projects; support the team effectively; and support the agents with knowledge and training plans.<p></p>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES </b><i>include the following. Other duties may be assigned.</i></p>
<ul>
<li>
Handles all inbound customer support calls and provides initial troubleshooting of basic network issues, hardware and software issues pertaining to retail point-of-sale equipment; additionally, will relate and instruct through various process/procedural steps.</li>
<li>
Communicates with customers and gives them a comfort level that their issue is being properly addressed.</li>
<li>
Creates support tickets in ServiceNow for all technical support inquires via incoming calls or email.</li>
<li>
Accurately documents and records technical support calls and events (call receipt, documentation entry, tracking, follow-up, and escalations) in ticket and tracking software applications.</li>
<li>
Escalates or assigns tickets to other resolver groups/departments as required.</li>
<li>
Responsible for follow-up on all issues, maintains quality assurance of tickets, and monitors ticket status to ensure timely resolution.</li>
<li>
Support new store openings, remodels/relocations, and closings, as well as any planned project implementations and rollouts.</li>
<li>
Ensure all store sales data files are polled successfully every day. This involves processing files and following up on files that did not poll to ensure they complete and post.</li>
<li>
Consistently exceeds customer expectations.</li>
<li>
Performs according to documented metrics and defined goals.</li>
<li>
Adheres to established operations standards and procedures recommending appropriate modifications and process improvements.</li>
<li>
Continuously self-develops and self-improves technical knowledge and customer service skills.</li>
<li>
Attends required training.</li>
<li>
Performs other related duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
