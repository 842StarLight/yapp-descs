<div>Job Description – Program Manager<br>
Global IT seeks Program Manager for the Global IT Deployments team. This team is responsible to deliver right products to Amazonians at right state and right time. We deploy Minimum Lovable Product (MLP) at scale inside Amazon. We deal with product type such as software, services, hardware and hybrid (hardware &amp; software combo). Our program is scaled to deploy products from 100 to several thousands of Amazonians. The deployments team plays a critical role helping product teams test their products in Amazon to gather customer feedback before product becomes publicly available. The Deployments team is the gatekeeper for any new products to be tested/used by Amazonians and we advocate for our customers globally<br>
<br>
In this role you will work in conjunction with various internal and external stakeholders to guide the design/build, test, and deployment of complex and cross-functional technical solutions to address a wide variety of IT infrastructure gaps and problems. You will take large, complex projects and break them down into manageable pieces, help guide the development of functional specifications and draft project schedules and milestones, then work with engineers and project managers to ensure timely delivery of those solutions. You will work with stakeholders to identify technical and resource options to successfully deliver the designed architecture, assemble project teams, assign responsibilities, and ensure we are driving product adoption<br>
<br>
