 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">

<div>
<p>West Pharmaceutical Services, Inc. is a leading manufacturer of packaging components and delivery systems for injectable drugs and healthcare products. Working by the side of its customers from concept to patient, West creates products that promote the efficiency, reliability and safety of the world's pharmaceutical drug supply. West is headquartered in Exton, Pennsylvania, and supports its customers from locations in North and South America, Europe, Asia and Australia. West's 2017 sales of $1.6 billion reflect the daily use of approximately 112 million of its components and devices, which are designed to improve the delivery of healthcare to patients around the world.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Summary:
</b></p>
<p>In this role, you will Perform technical tasks within a laboratory setting to analyze and test the electro-mechanical functionality of West products.
</p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities:
 </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Uses critical thinking skills to analyze field failures to root cause and documents findings per procedures.
</li>
<li>Uses software tools (Salesforce, MasterControl, Microsoft Office Suite) to document and manage investigations.
</li>
<li>Has good writing skills and is able to communicate findings in a technical, concise, and effective manner.
</li>
<li>Is proficient in laboratory test equipment operation and maintenance including Instron, CT scanner, oscilloscope, power supply, multi-meter, microscopes.
</li>
<li>Is familiar with and utilizes a variety of root cause analysis methodologies to assess technical problems.
</li>
<li>Identifies and resolves potential safety hazards. Maintains a clean, neat, and safe work environment.
</li>
<li>Exercises creativity in developing new test / debugging methods.
</li>
<li>Uses ability as a skilled specialist to complete technical tasks in creative and effective ways.
</li>
<li>Takes immediate action after identifying critical product issues. Assists with corrective action when necessary. Contacts appropriate support and/or management to ensure issues are reported and resolved.
</li>
<li>Demonstrates a “Customer Service” approach to all activities and assignments.
</li>
<li>Facilitates teamwork, cross training, and collaboration between departments to maximize processes and resolve problems.
</li>
<li>Focuses on quality, timeliness, and continuous improvement.
</li>
<li>Is able to easily adapt to changes in priorities and can work independently to investigate complaints.
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Performs other duties as assigned based on business needs.
</li>
<li>Conforms with and abides by all regulations, policies, work procedures, instruction, and all safety rules.
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Exhibits regular, reliable, punctual and predictable attendance <b>.
</b>
</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned
</li>
</ul>
