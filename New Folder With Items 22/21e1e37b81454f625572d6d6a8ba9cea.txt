<h2>Commercial Solutions Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Commercial Solutions Engineer</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Expectations and Tasks</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>
VMware Solutions Engineer (SE)</b> is an experienced SE who works closely with the sales team, commercial territory manager and partner business manager to identify, develop, accelerate and win opportunities.</li>
<li>
Demonstrate deep understanding of VMware solutions and their appropriate industries in order to drive synergy and maintain credibility with prospective clients.</li>
<li>
Perform and support tasks such as technical sales calls, presentations, proof-of-concepts, capacity planning and technical ROI models.</li>
<li>
Ability to effectively present to customers via remote conferencing technologies such as Zoom / InExpo</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Qualities You Need to Have</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>
Memorable Presenter</b> - Prepare and deliver value-based software demonstrations/presentations in support of sales acceleration. Preparation includes personalization of materials to ensure delivery of a simple, appealing and compelling customer presentation.</li>
<li>
<b>
Creative Problem Solver</b> – Creativity is an important part to the success of the role, you must enjoy difficult challenges and know how to think out-of-the-box to formulate the best outcome for the customer.</li>
<li>
<b>
Post-Sales </b>– Provide limited post-sale support to key customers primarily to the project/implementation team to ensure a smooth transition. Also support one-to-many sales and marketing events on-site and remotely.</li>
<li>
<b>
Communication Skills</b> – Being an excellent communicator with both business and technical people is an essential skill for the role, while working independently and participate in the sales processes.</li>
<li>
<b>
Passionate about customer success</b> – helping customers to succeed and solving their problems is where you need to strive in, when customers are successful, we are successful, and you will do your utmost best in your capacity to help them realize their goals in the organization.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Work Experience / Education / Certifications / Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelors or Masters Degree in Information Systems, Computer Science, Engineering or equivalent</li>
<li>
At least 5 or more years’ experience in Pre-sales Organization in the software industry</li>
<li>
Relevant experience / certifications in cloud, storage, networking, applications will be considered</li>
<li>
Demonstrates 3-4 successful engagements leading small teams on small-mid-sized deals</li>
<li>
Proficiency in English as the main language is mandatory, additional proficiency in one of the following language Korean, Vietnam, Thai, Bahasa language are advantageous.</li>
<li>
Previous experience in working with channel partners on solution delivery advantageous</li>
</ul>
