<b>About BDP International:</b><br>
BDP International is a privately held, family-owned non-asset based global logistics provider with annual sales exceeding $2.8 billion USD. Founded in 1966 by Richard Bolte Sr. and based in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, BDP has grown to be one of the largest logistics providers in the world. BDP employs more than 4,900 people worldwide, and operates freight logistics centers in 270 cities across the world through a global network of wholly owned offices, joint ventures and affiliates in 135 countries.
<br><br>
The company serves more than 4,000 customers worldwide. Clients include Bayer, Dow, DuPont, Heineken USA, Honeywell, Johnson &amp; Johnson, Revlon, Trek Bicycle, Wacker, and others.
<br><br>
<b>Job Description:</b><br>
The Application Developer is responsible for analysis, design, estimation, development, testing and deployment of software solutions to meet project requirements. Additional responsibilities include defect resolution during the project lifecycle and support of existing production software applications. Utilizing established software development processes, the Application Developer will participate in solving complex technical problems. Additionally, the Application Developer will work with Business Analyst, Quality Assurance, Infrastructure and Project Management peers as well as business team members in a team environment to deliver projects on schedule and within budget. Must have good verbal and written communication skills and must be capable of communicating with both technical and non-technical team members.
<br><br>
<b>Job Responsibilities:</b><br>
Performs coding according to technical specifications and development standards.
<br>Participates in code reviews.
<br>Responsible for unit testing and debugging of code.
<br>Responsible for working with the user community, business analysts, and support specialists to confirm functionality of applications based on user specifications.
<br>Work with management to develop delivery plan and cost estimates.
<br>Responsible for the delivery of product according to established timelines.
<br>Responsible for after hours and weekend support as needed.
<br>Communicates status through the development cycle to manager and project manager.
<br>Required to keep abreast of new technologies through training and reading of reference materials.
<br>Problem-solver with an inquisitive and innovative mind working in an agile environment.
