<h2>Sales Business Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>
<div><b>Position Summary:</b></div>
<div>The Sales Business Analyst is responsible for supporting reporting, information analysis, KPI tracking and other business performance metrics as needed to support successful evaluation of selling activities. Timeliness and accuracy of reporting solutions and processes are critical to overall business success. This may also include participation in projects intended to enhance front-end sales processes, systems and programs as directed by business management and in accordance with established company processes and standards. Foundational commitment to employee core competency and organizational development, adherence to company code of conduct policies and overall business and personal integrity are required.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<p><b>Primary Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul><li> Works with management team to define and deliver required sales, orders, margin, backlog and other reporting needs in formats and with frequency as required</li></ul>
<ul><li> Manage collection, consolidation and cleansing of weekly and monthly reporting input from sales team and finance team in support of established weekly and monthly reporting requirements</li></ul>
<ul><li> Consolidates customer, sales, channel and other input to provide weekly forecast recommendations to VP of Sales in support of weekly business forecasting needs</li></ul>
<ul><li> Provides sales team weekly / monthly updates to order, sales and margin results in support of their planning and performance management efforts</li></ul>
<ul><li> Works with finance team to support review and validation of monthly Manufacturer’s Representative commission calculations, payment and reporting via the Partner Portal commission reporting system</li></ul>
<ul><li> Manages monthly updates to Manufacturer’s Representative and Channel Partner order and shipment reporting updates in the Partner Portal</li></ul>
<ul><li> Works with Sales team to ensure weekly updates to are completed to open opportunities in the CRM system per established CRM Process</li></ul>
<ul><li> Coordinate with Manufacturer’s Representative organizations to track and update status of open opportunities</li></ul>
<ul><li> Manage quarterly updates to Sales and Manufacturer’s Representative target account conversion plans</li></ul>
<ul><li> Maintains strong working relationship with immediate team members and provide support for RSM’s, reps, customers, etc.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Provides primary business input and direction for development of enterprise reporting and executive dashboards across front-end sales and management functions</li></ul>
<ul><li> Acts as liaison between Sales, Operations, Finance and Information Technology organizations to ensure systems and reporting are properly defined and provide accurate data and analysis in support of delivering business results</li></ul>
<br>
<div></div>
<p><b>Established Success Measures</b></p>
<ul><li> Successful and accurate management of all required reporting and information management per established schedule
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Improved order and sales forecast accuracy per defined objectives</li></ul>

<b>
Qualifications</b><div>
</div>
<p><b>
Position Skill and Education Requirements:</b></p>
<ul><li> Bachelor’s Degree or greater in Computer Science, Business Reporting, Management or related Analytical Sciences</li></ul>
<ul><li> 3+ working as a business analyst supporting complex reporting and analysis of sales, orders, forecasts and overall business profitability</li></ul>
<ul><li> Excellent interpersonal and communication skills to support both internal and external individual and team engagement</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ability to work independently and consistently meet deadlines</li></ul>
<ul><li> Proficient with corporate ERP, CRM and enterprise reporting
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Demonstrated advanced experience with Microsoft Excel and Microsoft Access</li></ul>
<ul><li> Excellent time management and multi-tasking skills required</li></ul>
