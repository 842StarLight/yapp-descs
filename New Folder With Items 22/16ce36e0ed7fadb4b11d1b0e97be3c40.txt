<h2>Entry Level Audio Visual Technician</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>If you have a passion for Audio-Visual focused systems in the Information Technology field and are an innovative problem solver interested in working in a team environment that seeks to provide the best audio-video solutions possible for the organization, this is the position for you!</p>
<p>
As an Audio Visual Technician, you will work as part of a team supporting the company’s increasing use of audio visual systems for video production, video conferencing, and sales/training presentation solutions. You will set up and support small to large-scale audio visual systems, troubleshoot issues, and seek to continually review and improve performance and capabilities. Additionally, you will review various departments’ audiovisual needs and assist in finding the right solutions to meet those needs.</p>
<b>
Requirements:</b><ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s in audio-visual technical field or equivalent experience</li>
<li>
Knowledge of computer hardware and networking required</li>
<li>
Experience in Windows, Mac and/or Linux platforms preferred</li>
<li>
Experience with web conferencing equipment and software (e.g. WebEx, Adobe Connect, GoToMeeting, etc.)</li>
<li>
Strong communication and organizational skills</li>
</ul>
