<h2>Financial Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>COMPANY DESCRIPTION:</b></p>
<p>Heska is dedicated to quality pet care. Through innovation, invention, and strategic partnerships with worldwide leaders in diagnostics and treatments. Heska provides the benefits of latest generation technologies to pet healthcare providers. Heska’s range of complete blood diagnostic solutions include Chemistry, Hematology, Blood Gas &amp; Electrolytes, Immunodiagnostics, Heartworm, and other blood testing. Treatments and testing include Immunotherapy and complete Allergy solutions.</p>
<p><b>
JOB SUMMARY:</b> The Financial Analyst will play an integral role in developing a growing, global finance organization with support of treasury and financial analysis functions. Responsibilities will include both specific periodic analysis and reporting, and special projects as identified and assigned. Multiple, concurrent projects will be expected of this role.</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:
</b></p>
<p><b>Financial and Business Analysis:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Assist with annual budget and reforecasting processes</li>
<li>Responsible for preparation and analysis of monthly, quarterly, annual financial reports with variance analysis to prior year, budget, and forecast; including but not limited to P&amp;L, Cash Flow, Working Capital, Capex and Expense analysis</li>
<li>Analyze sales and operational data to provide management with key conclusions and recommendations</li>
<li>Provide reporting and analysis on monthly forecasting, sales trends, product supply and demand, sell through and returns, margins, revenue calculations and profit impact</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Treasury and Cash Management:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Prepare, maintain and distribute periodic cash forecasts and liquidity analysis</li>
<li>Ensure investment activities are in line with the Investment Policy</li>
<li>Support Lender reporting requirements, including the coordination of interest/fee payments</li>
<li>Support Due Diligence processes for Capital Markets</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Other duties as assigned, including but not limited to:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Project management of process and/or system improvement that benefit the financial and business reporting of the company
</li>
<li>Ad-hoc finance and treasury support
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<p><b>
EDUCATION AND/OR EXPERIENCE:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree from four-year college or university in Finance or Accounting</li>
<li>Minimum 1-3 years finance or accounting experience in a corporate environment</li>
<li>Budget and reforecast experience required</li>
<li>Experience in analyzing sales, operations, supply chain and inventory management in a discrete manufacturing environment, with medical equipment background preferred</li>
<li>Treasury/cash management experience preferred</li>
</ul>
<p><b>KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS AND ABILITIES:</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Good analytical abilities around finance principles</li>
<li>Consistently detail-oriented and meticulous in number analysis and review</li>
<li>Excellent internal and external customer service skills and approachability</li>
<li>Ability to work in a high-stress environment with demanding deadlines with constantly changing priorities.</li>
<li>Strategic reasoning and tactical implementation skills of analytical projects.</li>
<li>Effective presentation skills to various department staff and corporate management</li>
<li>Ability to work in an independent, proactive and cooperative fashion with professionals at all levels of the company.</li>
<li>Strong technical and operational aptitude, including broad-based awareness of a variety of analytical, metric-oriented tools and procedures.</li>
<li>Advanced ability with Microsoft Office Suite, especially Excel.</li>
<li>Fluency with spreadsheets, accounting software, database software, and CRM software (Salesforce.com preferred)</li>
<li>Commitment to Heska’s Promise and Creed</li>
</ul>
