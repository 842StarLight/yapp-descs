<h2>Director of Customer Care</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Kerauno, LLC is seeking a driven, experienced Director of Customer Care who is interested in designing and building a world class customer support organization. This person will develop a team of highly skilled support engineers responsible for Tier 3 software issues and above, meeting the needs of Kerauno's growing network of reseller / implementer partners and their end customers.
<br><br>
The ideal candidate must be self-motivated, quick-thinking, both detail-oriented and strategic, a superior communicator, a proven leader and possess an executive presence. They must make sound decisions, work effectively with and obtain timely results through others. Success is defined by customer satisfaction, team development, KPI development, and managing team performance against those KPIs.
<br><br>
<b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES &amp; RESPONSIBILITIES:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Hire, train and develop a team of highly skilled technology problem solvers</li>
<li>Develop metrics to measure the quality of the partner/customer support experience, the efficiency and effectiveness of the team, and to drive capacity / hiring decisions</li>
<li>Coach and mentor team members with a focus on professional development</li>
<li>Utilize deep product and industry knowledge in the Unified Communications and business process management space to guide and direct the efforts of the team</li>
<li>Treat Support as a product at Kerauno, innovating and iterating on the customer experience</li>
<li>Gather and synthesize relevant data, leading improvements in the tools and techniques to help scale the team</li>
<li>Migrate the support function to a Salesforce.com based case management system</li>
<li>Develop and continually improve systems and processes</li>
<li>Organize and manage the day to day schedule of the team to meet set KPIs</li>
<li>Manage and own customer complaints and escalations, ensuring proper documentation, escalation and follow through</li>
<li>Ensure the creation and maintenance of internal trouble shooting and best practices documentation</li>
<li>Represent the support function with the Sales, Professional Services, Finance and other cross-functional teams</li>
</ul>
