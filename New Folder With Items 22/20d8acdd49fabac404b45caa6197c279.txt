<div>
InSinkErator, a division of Emerson, is looking for a highly motivated and results driven person to join our team. The Sr. Supplier Quality Engineer will participate in cross-functional project teams assigned to larger-scale projects regarding internal manufacturing processes, the development of new products or introduction of new products into manufacturing. This position is responsible for coordinating or completing project quality deliverables, such as, quality planning and verification through use of advanced quality tools. Will work closely with various functions at all levels to resolve quality issues and improve internal processes and products.
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>What You'll Do:</b></div>
<div>
</div>
<ul>
<li><div>Identify unfavorable quality trends and lead improvements to reduce Cost of Quality, improve yield or customer satisfaction.
</div></li>
<li><div>Lead or participate on continuous improvement or corrective action teams as assigned.</div></li>
<li><div>Determine root cause of quality problems and lead implementation of effective corrective action.
</div></li>
<li><div>Participate on cross-functional teams to qualify and approve new processes or new products.
</div></li>
<li><div>Prepare detailed quality plans for large-scale projects to reduce risk of producing nonconformances.</div></li>
<li><div>Provide or coordinate required project quality related deliverables, such as, PPA’s, D/PFMEA, capability studies, statistical analysis, inspection plans, quality records and workmanship standards.</div></li>
<li><div>Direct the completion of quality actions stemming from Design Reviews, DFMEA’s and PFMEA’s.
</div></li>
<li><div>Provide training and mentoring to promote proper use and understanding of quality tools.</div></li>
<li><div>Recommend design, manufacturing or control changes to increase product performance, quality or reliability.</div></li>
<li><div>Participate on Material Review Board. Coordinate inspections or tests if necessary to determine disposition.</div></li>
<li><div>Lead, mentor and train assigned personnel.<br>
</div></li>
</ul>
