<h2>Director of Strategic Partnerships</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Your Mission As Director of Strategic Partnerships
<br><br>
We're looking for an experienced Partnership Leader with a proven track record of success in B2B SaaS partnerships - a passionate, highly intelligent, focused and well-organized individual, who wants to be part of our fast-growing, SaaS company in the hot field of HR Technology.
<br><br>
As the Director of Strategic Partnerships, your mission is to define and execute the company's growth strategy, supporting both our commercial and inside sales business. You'll work across functions to discover new partnership opportunities and help drive existing ones forward.
<br><br>
<b>Accountability &amp; Impact:</b><br>
In this role you'll...
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Identify, grow, and nurture strategic partnerships with media, association, and technology organizations</li>
<li>Work with consultants, and business development partners to find new opportunities</li>
<li>Work with partners to generate new opportunities, and with our Account Executives on existing accounts and opportunities, leveraging partner relationships to help drive deals forward</li>
<li>Contribute insights on market analysis and critical thinking for partnership strategy</li>
<li>Become an Energage product expert, conducting awesome product demonstrations to interested partners</li>
</ul>
<b>What You Bring to the Role:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>You have at least 10 years of experience in a partnership role for a SaaS company</li>
<li>Ideally, you have experience with enterprise accounts and the HR Technology space</li>
<li>Track record of consistent over-achievement in past Sales/Partnership roles</li>
<li>You have outstanding people skills, both remotely, written and face to face</li>
<li>You are self-motivated and super-focused on getting results</li>
<li>You are able to work in a fast-paced sales cycle and dynamic environment</li>
<li>Candidates must be to work in the U.S. from our offices</li>
</ul>
<b>Perks and Benefits of Working at Energage:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>3 weeks paid time off plus the week between Christmas and New Year</li>
<li>Company pays a portion of individual health care premium</li>
<li>Option to participate in a company-sponsored 401(k), with company match dependent on employee contribution</li>
<li>A day off per year for community service</li>
<li>Training and education</li>
<li>Tuition reimbursement program</li>
<li>Working with Certified B-Corp (https://www.bcorporation.net/)</li>
</ul>
