<h2>Patient Intake Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><p>Collects demographic and insurance information on patients and acts as the primary contact for new and existing accounts.<br>
 Investigates type and level of insurance coverage to assess patient eligibility for the program.<br>
 Communicates with medical professionals and insurance company personnel to expedite patient eligible into program.<br>
 Resolves reimbursement issues with accounts, sales force, and management.<br>
 Completes data entry in CPRS to ensure accuracy of reporting and outcomes.<br>
 Investigates data entry issues to correct data warehouse reports.<br>
 Enters initial patient delivery ticket with appropriate billing information.<br>
 Monitors computerized reports to avoid delays in customer billing information.<br>
 Confirms the timely resolution to un-voucherables.<br>
 Performs other duties and responsibilities as assigned.<br>
<br>
<b>Required Qualifications</b><br>
Education or experience equivalent to a high school diploma.<br>
 Minimum one year of related experience.<br>
 Strong service orientation and customer service attitude.<br>
 Effective verbal and written communications skills.<br>
 Excellent detail orientation and time management skills.<br>
 Proficiency with Microsoft business software.<br>
