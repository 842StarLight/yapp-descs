<h2>Business Development Director - AX D365</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>RSM US LLP is looking for a dynamic Microsoft Dynamics 365 Finance &amp; Operations Business Development Director to facilitate the growth of our national Technology &amp; Management Consulting practice with a particular emphasis on the life sciences industry.</b></p>
<p>
The firm is looking for a dynamic individual to join one of our national business development teams. This Business Development role will concentrate on selling Microsoft Dynamics 365 Enterprise (Finance &amp; Operations) solution with a life sciences industry focus.</p>
<p>
This position is responsible for leading all aspects of the sales process, including systematic prospect targeting, development of opportunity-specific sales strategy, and selection of pursuit teams and "quarterbacking" the entire sales process. They are growth leaders and support the efforts of various Firm industry teams through direct prospecting, networking, attendance and participation with various industry and professional groups and networking associations.</p>
<p>
The individual in this position will have all the necessary resources to be set up for success for this career opportunity that provides a competitive base salary along with a lucrative incentive compensation plan. We are looking for a candidate that has a proven track record in selling the Microsoft Dynamics 365 Enterprise (Finance &amp; Operations) solutions.</p>
