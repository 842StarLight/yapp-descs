<p><b><i>
Position Summary:
</i></b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>Responsible for planning, assignment and coordination of people and materials necessary for the prompt and complete installation and servicing of all commercial jobs within an assigned territory, district or location.</p>
<p><br>
</p>
<p><b><i>
Duties and Responsibilities:</i></b></p>
<div><ul>
<li>
Tracks and records daily/monthly/quarterly commercial installation and service activity to include individual installers' and service tech efforts and results.</li>
<li>
Establishes, maintains and coordinates a high level of quality and timely installation and service to customers for maximum retention.</li>
<li>
Serves as the primary contact for the district installation work force to provide input and feedback related to Corporate Operations for Installation and Service.</li>
<li>
Assists commercial sales force by developing low installation costs and suggesting proper and varied applications of systems and systems designed.</li>
<li>
Conducts meetings monthly. Communicates current installation and service policies and procedures, techniques, demonstration of equipment, new products and services, sales promotions and area advertising.</li>
<li>
Develops and maintains an effective installation and service work force through recruiting, hiring, training, motivating and coaching techniques.</li>
<li>
Key interactions with General Manager, Regional Operations Manager, engineers, fire planners, estimators and sales organization.</li>
</ul></div>
