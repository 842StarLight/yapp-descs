<h2>Data Entry Clerk</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>As a Data Entry Clerk, you will be a member of a team that is responsible for entering or updating data into a company computer system supporting the pricing team. Data is entered into the computer system from paper documents or from any other source by using a keyboard and must be able to be entered into the system quickly and accurately. Create and manage spreadsheets with large numbers of figures by using various databases to store and manage data.
</p>
<p>Verifies data to ensure accurate information is input and obtains missing or incomplete data when needed.</p>
<p>
 <b>Essentials Functions</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Prepares source data for entry by opening and sorting documents through email and/or paper.</li>
<li>Create and manage spreadsheets with large numbers of figures by using various databases to store and manage data.</li>
<li>Update existing data to ensure accurate information is input and obtains missing or incomplete data when needed, cross reference data reports with source documents.</li>
<li>Records data into company computer system by using keyboard.</li>
<li>Keeps organizational data information confidential and secure.</li>
<li>Accomplishes department and organization mission by completing related results as needed.</li>
<li>Retrieve data as requested, scans documents and print files, when needed.</li>
<li>Comply with data integrity and security policies.</li>
<li>Answer department phone and directs callers to appropriate areas.</li>
<li>Works with sales and credit departments to verify entered customer an account data by reviewing correcting deleting or reentering data and combining data from systems when account information is incomplete or incorrect purging files to eliminate duplication of data.</li>
<li>Maintains parameters within corporate pricing for data entry and operations by following policies and procedures and processes.</li>
<li>Maintains customer confidence and protects operations by keeping information confidential.</li>
<li>Processes customer an account source documents by imputing to ensure accuracy resolving discrepancies by using standard procedures or returning incomplete documents to the team leader for resolution.</li>
<li>Supports pricing team in the initial review and research on new quote requests by data entry.</li>
<li>Verifies and processes debit memos to correct invoicing discrepancies secondary function.</li>
<li>Utilizing the software system enters approved pricing to maintain company files and records.</li>
</ul>
