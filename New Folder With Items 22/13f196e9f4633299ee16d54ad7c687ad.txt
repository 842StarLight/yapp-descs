<h2>Associate Product Marketing Manager (ADX-146-19)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div><b>POSITION SUMMARY:</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>The Associate Product Marketing Manager (APMM) works closely with both the marketing and product management teams to drive adoption by ensuring that the product value propositions are clearly communicated to the right audience. The APMM conducts competitor analyses, market research and field team interviews to define persona-based market positions within the context of the overall product strategy. The APMM articulates the customer-facing messaging and outlines all marketing efforts to achieve this goal through multiple channels, including web content, webinars, emails &amp; social, advertising, public relations and tradeshows. The APMM also aligns field teams with current messaging and expertise through team training and tight communication.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>Job performance will involve a variety of activities including:</div>
<ul>
<li>Perform market research and competitor analyses</li>
<li>Define marketing personas and develop targeted messaging</li>
<li>Implement and analyze customer surveys</li>
<li>Map out marketing and sales collateral needs</li>
<li>Build content that aligns with product messaging for marketing deployment</li>
<li>Review, understanding, and compliance with ArcherDX HIPAA Security policies and procedures.</li>
<li>Safeguarding the privacy and security of protected health information and European Union personal data.</li>
<li>Processing and handling of European Union personal data only as directed by ArcherDX for purposes of personalized molecular pathology.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>EXPERIENCE:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>A solid understanding of next-generation sequencing and the various methods and players in the targeted sequencing space</li>
<li>2+ years marketing experience</li>
<li>Experience developing personas, performing market research and competitive analyses</li>
</ul>

</ul>
</li>
</ul>
