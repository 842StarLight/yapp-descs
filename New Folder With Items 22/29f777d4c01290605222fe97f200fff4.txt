<h2>Regional Manager | San Antonio</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Job Description:</b><br>
<p>Vesta Corporation has an exiting, new opportunity for a Regional Manager, supporting affordable housing communities in San Antonio. The individual in this role would have complete responsibility for personnel, physical plant and economic performance.<br>
</p>
<p>
Job duties will include, but are not limited to:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Utilize recruitment techniques and policies to interview, hire, and on-board new property managers when vacancies occur.</li>
<li>
Oversee, support, and mentor Property Managers in your assigned region; offering guidance and leadership development. Identify development opportunities, including Property Managers who may benefit from coaching from an assigned Mentor, or may add value to the current team as a new Mentor.</li>
<li>
Ensure efficiency of team members through ongoing staff meetings/communications, training, development, counseling, regular performance management, and leadership.</li>
<li>
Ensure administrative processes involving personnel are handled timely (i.e., performance evaluations, salary reviews, Payroll, change of status, etc.).</li>
<li>
Meet with the PM’s regularly to:
<ul>
<li>Review financial performance and the preparation of projections/action plan for balance of the fiscal year.</li>
<li>
Review occupancy and develop action plan to address areas of concern.</li>
<li>
Review current rent collections and discuss action necessary</li>
<li>
Review current rent levels and adjust where necessary.</li>
<li>
Monitor annual rent changes (i.e., GRC), and work with compliance to properly process and implement proposed changes on time.</li>
<li>
Review status of capital projects. Check in with Property Managers, and review service contract/vendor work for proper completion of contracted duties (i.e., exterminator, landscaper, snow removal, etc.).</li>
<li>
Review all documented capital improvement needs/projects, including solicited bids, contractor references, and insurance liability certificates in accordance with Vesta standards.</li>
<li>
Review and oversee preparation for all regulatory compliance reviews and physical inspections.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Make sure that any changes to management fees are implemented accurately and on time.</li>
<li>
Participate in monthly financial review, and ensure that Property Managers are prepared to address any variances.</li>
<li>
Work with property teams to set financial goals and assist in preparing accurate property budgets annually.</li>
<li>
Work with property teams to consistently monitor actual results and adjust operations as necessary to achieve or exceed the budgeted financial goals.</li>
<li>
Network, establish and maintain positive relationships with third parties and regulatory agencies in the region. Handle escalated resident, community, or agency complaints or other issues as necessary.</li>
<li>
Monitor activity of resident retention initiatives (i.e., resident functions, promotions, monthly newsletters, etc.) and property-issued communications/notices (i.e., bad weather, emergency, etc.)</li>
<li>
Visit all communities in the region regularly, to ensure that physical appearance of grounds, common areas, vacancies, and any other amenities meet standards. Document and address with property teams any deficiencies/necessary repairs discovered, and follow up to ensure repairs are completed.</li>
<li>
Conduct random spot checks of resident files, new leases, renewals, recertifications, move-outs and corresponding paperwork to ensure completion, accuracy and timeliness.</li>
<li>
Verify that all community emergency teams are instructed of proper response and handling of emergencies with buildings, criminal activity, employee/resident injuries, fires, floods, freezes, etc., in accordance with Vesta’s SOP to minimize liabilities.</li>
<li>
Review and provide support to ensure properties are answering any internal department requests for information in a timely manner, ie, Acquisition and Development needs, accounting needs, physical plant needs, etc.</li>
<li>
Attend and participate in meetings that involve either existing or new properties in the Region, and make sure that all property management staff are apprised of the lease up schedule, tax credit delivery schedule, break even plan, and any other important information on any new properties in the Region.</li>
<li>
Hold regular meetings with the appropriate Property Managers for any property in active lease up. Keep Development and Senior Management apprised of progress.</li>
<li>
Keep supervisor apprised of any activity at the sites that are of concern, can negatively affect the operation of the site and in accordance with Vesta notification protocol.</li>
<li>
Other duties as assigned by management.</li>
</ul>
