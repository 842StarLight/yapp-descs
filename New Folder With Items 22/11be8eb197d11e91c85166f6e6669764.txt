<b>About the Role</b><br>
<br>
<p>Responsible for all technology business development activity in assigned States, including the introduction and sales of new Company products and services to prospective customers, and the growth of Company products and services to existing customers. Maintains ongoing communication with customers to develop and enhance positive relationships, promote Company products and services, and ensure customer satisfaction and retention.</p>
<p>Primary focus is new business development of accounts including new programs, take-away of competitor accounts, growth of assigned accounts and account retention.</p>
<br>
<br>
<br>
<b>What You'll be Doing</b><br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Identifies and closes sales opportunities in assigned existing and new accounts in order to meet and/or exceed assigned revenue quotas. Utilizes all Company resources, including management team, customer service, sales support and business development team members to achieve objectives.</li>
<li>Drives market share growth and focuses on customer retention.</li>
<li>Creates and maintains a strategic account plan for each account consistent with the customer business/strategic plan.</li>
<li>Negotiates pricing and contract terms with new and current accounts to close deals.</li>
<li>Develops and enhances ongoing relationships with key executives in each assigned account, including directors, commissioners, judges and department heads.</li>
<li>Forecasts new and existing account growth/revenue in assigned territory as requested by Regional Manager.</li>
<li>Utilizes Customer Relationship Management (CRM) software to deposit and maintain market share data in assigned territory.</li>
<li>Assists each assigned/prospective account with identifying, justifying and planning for information and service needs.</li>
<li>Acts as a communication liaison between Company internal departments and customer at all levels to ensure prompt handling of customer inquiries/issues and customer satisfaction.</li>
<li>Provides customer status reports, key prospecting data, trip reports and other sales reports as requested.</li>
<li>Represents Company at national/local trade shows and conferences, mandatory bidders’ conferences, and public hearings, as required.</li>
<li>Performs other duties as assigned.</li>
<br>
</ul>
