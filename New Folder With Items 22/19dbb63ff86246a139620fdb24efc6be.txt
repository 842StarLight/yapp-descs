Purpose of Position-</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Develops and oversees the brand marketing team development and execution of long-term strategic marketing plans. Ensures BR as a brand is differentiated and has distinctive position in the market place.</div>
<div>
Leads the field and franchisee portion of the enterprise in successful execution of Baskin-Robbins marketing initiatives, incorporating both strategic imperatives and field-based tactical needs in order to drive, meet and exceed sales and profit targets</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<p>
What You’ll Be Working On-</p>
<ul>
<li>
Lead and Manage the Consumer Engagement, Digital marketing, social media, Brand Marketing, Consumer Research and Field Marketing teams for Baskin-Robbins Marketing in:</li>
<li>
Brand Vision, strategy and tactics to drive sales, profitability and to reach long term goals</li>
<li>
Delivering sales and profit targets through an annual operating/marketing plan by leading internal, external and franchisees in its development and execution</li>
<li>
Developing the advertising strategy (local and national balance) that drives sales while concurrently building brand</li>
<li>
Directing the agency in the development and execution of brand-building marketing programs</li>
<li>
Leading the Marketing Leadership Team to set and approve the brand strategy, tactics, and calendar</li>
<li>
Attracting, develop, and retain top-quality marketing staff</li>
</ul>

At least 12 years progressively more senior field-based, marketing agency or operations experience in a franchisee distribution system</li>
