<h2>Chief Estimator (Water/Wastewater)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Haskell is currently seeking a Chief Water &amp; Wastewater Estimator to join our team and develop an Estimating Center of Excellence. This candidate will manage various design-build and/or CMAR preconstruction projects. The Chief Estimator will be responsible for bidding projects, establishing standardized procedures, methods and processes for estimating, in addition to managing and training estimating resources. This individual must have the ability to deliver high level detailed estimates at the initial project stage and efficiently function by reviewing design options, recommend best solutions based on cost, methods of construction, market trends, etc. Qualified individuals will have extensive estimating experience with Water &amp; Wastewater projects.</b></p>
<p><b>
Scope of Position</b></p>
<p>
The Chief Estimator is the primary member of the estimating team who has overall responsibility for the planning and development of estimates produced. The Chief Estimator is responsible for bidding projects, establishing standardized procedures, methods and processes for estimating, in addition to managing and training estimating resources.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Job Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manages estimating resources and assigns resources to specific projects within the group.</li>
<li>Assigns and/or manages preconstruction team project specific responsibilities.</li>
<li>Reviews proposal specifications and drawings to determine scope of work and required contents of estimate.</li>
<li>Ability to identify risk issues and their order of magnitude.</li>
<li>Ensures and monitors the solicitation of subcontractors and/or vendors, along with the review and/or management of scopes.</li>
<li>Prepares and/or supervises the instructions to bidders, bid form and other bid solicitation information as required.</li>
<li>Preforms and/or manages the estimating quantity survey and pricing effort.</li>
<li>Prepares estimates at all stages of the project design, from conceptual to 100% completion.</li>
<li>Coordinates preconstruction project specific insurance and tax requirements.</li>
<li>Responsible for project specific area labor and market pricing.</li>
<li>Coordinates and reviews the technical proposal requirements with Project Director and Marketing.</li>
<li>Assists the project team with change orders.</li>
<li>Effectively determine the bid strategies.</li>
<li>Ensure the flow of project information is maintained.</li>
<li>Analyze alternate means and methods to determine the most economical alternative. Review design options and recommend best solution based on cost, engineering quality, or availability of materials, etc.</li>
<li>Reviews all final estimate packages to insure accuracy and completeness.</li>
<li>Coordinates internal and external reviews at various stages of the preconstruction process.</li>
<li>May review design options and recommend best solution based on cost, engineering quality, or availability of materials.</li>
<li>Leads and/or manages the transfer of a successful preconstruction project to the project team.</li>
<li>Creates and/or manages adjusted estimates and budget estimates.</li>
<li>Establishes and/or manages the project budget development and reviews with project management.</li>
<li>Maintains estimate files of working documents as back-up for estimates, including current (accurate) information on prices from suppliers through direct contact, sales brochures, price lists and other sources.</li>
<li>Maintains and/or manages the maintenance of estimating software.</li>
<li>Participates in periodic reviews during construction and site visits to projects as appropriate.</li>
<li>Ability to build effective relationships with customers, design teams, suppliers, preconstruction personnel, etc.</li>
<li>Conducts and/or assists with employee annual reviews and participates with employee career development.</li>
<li>Preforms additional duties as assigned.</li>
<br>
</ul>
