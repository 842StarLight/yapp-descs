<h2>Senior System Security Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>In this role, you have the opportunity to</b></p>
<p>
Be a lead system security engineer working with and leading cross functional engineering teams to design secure hardware and software subsystems to meet customer requirements. Work directly with Mercury's customers in a lead technical role to shape requirements to meet customer needs. Oversees the technical planning, verification and validation of systems components. Performs total systems analyses to evaluate alternatives, costs and risks, and supportability. Builds, codes, tests and implements the system according to systems specifications.</p>
<p><b>
To succeed in this role, you should have the following skills and experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Working in pre-sales environments to identify critical needs with system features and performance issues</li>
<li>Direct experience validating, verifying, and deploying security harden systems and sub-components</li>
<li>Experience with FPGAs and how to map requirements to design implementations</li>
<li>Mapping customer specifications to system design requirements; analyzes system performance and identifies risks</li>
<li>Flow system level requirements down to detailed Hardware and Software specifications</li>
<li>Consults with customers on technical issues and technical presentations</li>
<li>Serve as the main technical contact for projects</li>
<li>Understand the main concepts and applications associated with specific, specialized company product design</li>
<li>Responsible for providing an accurate assessment of the scope of work for projects and accurate assessments of progress</li>
<li>Understands and addresses cost, schedule and performance impacts and provides trade-offs for programs decisions</li>
</ul>
<p><b>In this role you will be responsible for:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Builds technical relationships with customers and provides insight into improvement and unique value add.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Establishes relationships with critical leaders in the industry.</li>
<li>Generates major account technical proposals for both custom and standard products.</li>
<li>Identifies and understands the customer's technical requirements and recommends Mercury product solutions.</li>
<li>Manages the analysis of technical features of competitive architectures or solutions.</li>
<li>Performs system performance analysis and identifies potential implementation risks.</li>
<li>Provides technical evaluations on how to most effectively map target market applications to Mercury's current and future products.</li>
<li>Serves as a technical expert to customers and Mercury Applications Engineers, providing in-depth specialized knowledge in applications and techniques used in target markets.</li>
<li>Tracks technology trends and influences within the industry.</li>
<li>Works on the most complex issues that impact design/selling success or address future concepts, products or technologies.<br>
<br>
<b>Education:</b> BS or higher in Electrical Engineering, Computer Engineering, Computer Science or related field.<br>
<br>
<b>Work Experience:</b> 10+ Years<br>
<br>
<b>Travel Requirements:</b> 25-50%</li>
</ul>
