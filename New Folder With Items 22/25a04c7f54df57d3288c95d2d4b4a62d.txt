<h2>Sr Manager, Go to Market and Sales Operations</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>Manage and lead team of analysts &amp; program managers in support of the Virtual Retail business. This position will have primary responsibilities of: go-to-market planning &amp; communications; business support &amp; escalations for the Virtual Retail channel; sales operations including forecasting, supplier invoicing, and contracts. Will serve as the primary liaison between VR team and HQ support groups ensuring alignment of support for shared services, programs, and promotions. Will collaborate with Retail, Care, and VR groups to build &amp; maintain Virtual Retail playbook. Will play a pivotal role in working with Sr Manager, Digital Funnel &amp; Programs to align programs with field execution capabilities.<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Manage and develop a team of program managers and analysts in support of Virtual Retail Go-to-Market and Frontline Sales Operations. Interfaces with Headquarters teams including: GTM, Planning &amp; Execution, Finance, and others to ensure Virtual Retail is scoped for relevant programs and promotions and GTM plan is aligned to VR priorities &amp; capabilities.
</li>
<li>Manage GTM timing and communications with VR Frontline &amp; programs teams, ensuring strategic delivery of key initiatives. Serves as primary research and escalation team for Frontline, customer, and partner-related support needs. Assist field with getting answers for operational questions and issues impacting sales and partner relationships.
</li>
<li>Builds and supports quality assurance and ongoing, standardization efforts in sales &amp; service delivery. Supports accountability and team execution of GTM initiatives, including Customer Experience, Sales Interaction Model, and adherence to operational standards and procedures. Will develop &amp; maintain Virtual Retail sales &amp; operations playbook.</li>
<li>
Serve as Lead on channel projects that require project requirements gathering, documentation, reporting, financial exposure and consolidation (spiff programs, partner &amp; customer events, enterprise efforts, data and information validation). Uphold sales operations standards and ensure communication to all of field sales and Frontline impacted groups<br>
</li>
</ul>
