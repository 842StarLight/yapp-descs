<b>Responsibilities</b><br>
<b>Customer Service &amp; Sales</b><br>
- Be available to perform customer service and/or sales functions when needed. Greets customers in a timely and positive manner - create a positive sales/buying experience for the customer. Identifies the customer's needs and provide information related to Metro by T-Mobile products and services. Handles all administrative aspects of the sale including paperwork to establish service, accepting customer payments and filing completed orders. Is a "knowledgeable expert" to the customer.<br>

<ul><li>Assists store management in training store associates regarding operating procedures. Acts as a resource to store staff on products, and handset service issues. Know where to get support where needed and deliver operational results based on key performance indicators</li></ul>

Merchandising</b><br>
