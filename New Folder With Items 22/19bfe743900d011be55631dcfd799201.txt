<h2>Executive Director, MEMS & Sensors Industry Group</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>THE ROLE:</b><br>
The Executive Director of the MEMS &amp; Sensors Industry Group (MSIG) will provide association leadership for this vital, $50B+ electronics community. The Executive Director will be a high-level ambassador, building and sustaining relationships with key stakeholders, managing MSIG’s activity portfolio, identifying and evaluating business opportunities to support the industry’s interests, and creating partnerships that advance the industry’s growth and prosperity.
<br><br>
This is a full-time, exempt position and reports to the Vice President of SEMI Technology Communities at SEMI Milpitas office.
<br><br>
<b>THE IDEAL CANDIDATE:</b><br>
The ideal candidate will have high-level industry experience, possess an excellent technical background, bring energy and focus with a sense of entrepreneurship, and be capable of overseeing all MSIG operations, functions and activities. The Executive Director will be a charismatic individual, responsible for providing strategic directions and implementing a high-quality vision.
<br><br>
<b>CORE RESPONSIBILITIES:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Grow MSIG membership and service offerings world-wide.</li>
<li>Be the leader of SEMI HQ and regional offices with respect to the MSIG strategic plan, membership, communications, and technical programming.</li>
<li>Provide content guidance for regional events and work in collaboration with SEMI regional leadership, marketing, program management, and operations teams to market and communicate MSIG's value and ROI to existing and prospective members.</li>
<li>Accountable for the annual and long-range budgets, and other financial reports.</li>
<li>Provide advice to the MSIG Governing Council, to other industry committees, and to internal and external stakeholders.</li>
<li>Act as the industry’s ambassador and spokesperson to interact and build relationships/connections with SEMI members, the MSIG community, end users, media, and other stakeholders.</li>
<li>Engage with small through large groups on business and technical topics.</li>
<li>Be an enabler - bring disparate groups together to identify and solve common business and technical challenges.</li>
<li>Advocate for MSIG and help build stronger connections with members and their industries.</li>
</ul>
<b>WHAT SUCCESS LOOKS LIKE:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>You have developed and implemented strategies to advance the industry’s growth and prosperity.</li>
<li>You have fostered valuable collaborations and partnerships that help drive business development and increase MSIG membership world-wide.</li>
</ul>
<b>CRITICAL SUCCESS FACTORS:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Strategic thinker</li>
<li>Stellar management skills with experience in matrixed organizations</li>
<li>Outstanding customer service ethic</li>
<li>Superb communications skillset (verbal, written) to include compelling presentation skills</li>
<li>Ability to work with and manage industry committees, projects, and activities (R&amp;D projects, events, programs, meetings, etc.)</li>
<li>Self-starter with strong, proactive work ethic</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task in a fast-paced, dynamic environment</li>
<li>Collaborative team player</li>
<li>Expected travel approximately 35%, some international</li>
</ul>
<b>FOUNDATIONAL KNOWLEDGE OR PRACTICAL EXPERIENCE:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>BS degree in science, technology, engineering, or math required. Advanced degree a positive.</li>
<li>Increasingly responsible management positions (e.g., currently a Sr. Director and above) in a technology industry or technology trade association.</li>
<li>R&amp;D, manufacturing, product development and/or sales/business development experience in the MEMS and sensors industry or adjacent industry.</li>
<li>Experience in representing a company or being a volunteer in an association or professional society.</li>
</ul>
ABOUT SEMI
<br><br>
SEMI Is the global industry association representing the electronics manufacturing supply chain, connecting over 2,200 member companies and 1.3 million professionals worldwide. SEMI members are responsible for the innovations in materials, design, equipment, software, devices, and services that enable smarter, faster, more powerful, and more affordable electronic products. Since 1970, SEMI has built connections that have helped its members prosper, create new markets, and address common industry challenges together.</div>
