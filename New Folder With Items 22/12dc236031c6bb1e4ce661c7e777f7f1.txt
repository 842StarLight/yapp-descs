<h2>Technical Customer Support Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Job description</b></p>
<p>
The Technical Customer Support Engineer is responsible for providing customer support, troubleshooting, testing and maintenance for the MyoPro product line. Additionally, assist in the implementation of appropriate systems, policies and procedures to ensure quality and compliance and proactively develop improved processes. The ideal candidate is a hands-on engineer or technician with a passion for customer support with a fundamental understanding of electromechanical systems.</p>
<p><b>
Duties and responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Answer support calls</li>
<li>Schedule support and troubleshooting for Myomo product lines</li>
<li>Configure, troubleshoot, and update laptops and MyoPro devices</li>
<li>Update Google sheets, enter Cases and RMA’s in the product lifecycle management / quality management system software application (Arena)</li>
<li>Quote and process PO’s for repairs</li>
<li>Receive, package, and ship RMA’s</li>
<li>Ensure compliance to FDA requirements as required</li>
<li>Support for sales and field personnel</li>
<li>Perform testing while precisely following detailed instructions</li>
<li>Documentation of test procedures</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Customer support experience</li>
<li>Ability to learn new skills and adapt to a small rapidly changing team</li>
<li>Excellent written/verbal, interpersonal, and organizational skills</li>
<li>Comfortable in fast-paced environment with ability to prioritize</li>
<li>Ability to meet deadline with specified time constraints</li>
<li>Work well independently and in teams</li>
<li>Attention to detail and ability to follow written instructions</li>
<li>Test development and documentation</li>
<li>Experience working with electromechanical systems, including software</li>
<li>Ideally, some embedded software QA testing experience</li>
<li>Computer literacy, specifically, in Word, Excel, and PowerPoint and web based remote communication.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Working conditions</b></p>
<ul>
<li>The job requires a person to work some weekday evenings and weekend work.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>About Myomo</b></p>
<p>
Myomo develops mobility solutions for people with neurological disorders. The MyoPro arm brace helps increase movement in weak, impaired arms. The company combines technology developed at Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT) with orthotics and prosthetics (O&amp;P) professionals to help neurologically impaired people regain independence. For more information, visit www.myomo.com.</p>
