<h2>Customer Service Representative/Enrollment Agent I (Part-Time)</h2>

We are a distributed company leveraging the latest technologies to deliver world-class products in the private and public sectors of finance, telecom, identity, security, retail, sports entertainment, commercial, government, and IoT. In this ever-changing world, protecting your identity is paramount. Join the team that is ensuring one person- one identity.</p>

<p>
The Enrollment Agent I (EA I) supports Enrollment Solutions Operations through participation in commercial, state, federal programs; and/or consumer sales of identity related products and services. The employee performs electronic fingerprinting functions according to the commercial, state, or federal customer’s program requirements. This position is a trainee role and the incumbent will be introduced to various processes, taught how to operate various fingerprinting machinery, and trained to navigate the various add-on products and services offered through the retail program as well as through specialized computer software databases.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Job Functions (includes but is not limited to):</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provide superior customer service to internal and external customers</li>
<li>Perform basic maintenance and troubleshoot issues with fingerprinting and retail equipment, including computer hardware and software</li>
<li>Obtain successful customer fingerprint images and capture biometric information to meet or exceed program standards</li>
<li>Verify customer identity and scan required documents into database system</li>
<li>Successfully complete required biometric and data capture processes and paperwork on each customer</li>
<li>Identify win-win opportunities to provide more products and services to customers</li>
<li>Create opportunities to add value for customers through the upselling of retail products and services (if applicable)</li>
<li>Perform at or above Enrollment Agent performance benchmarks. Adhere to all company and departmental employment policies regarding security and confidentiality</li>
<li>Adhere to all company and departmental policies relating to Enrollment Center standards, including adherence to signage, branding, and employee attire policies</li>
<li>Performs Enrollment Center Sustainment responsibilities on a daily basis</li>
<li>Perform administrative duties such as time keeping, shift schedules, daily activity reports, card inventory, equipment inventory, and requisitioning supplies</li>
<li>Represents the company and the program(s) that are supported in a professional and customer oriented manner</li>
<li>Support Customer Service Registration and Reservation activities, as necessary</li>
<br>
</ul>
