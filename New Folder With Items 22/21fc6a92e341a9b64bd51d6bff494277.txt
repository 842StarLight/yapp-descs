<p>General Responsibilities</p>
<p>The Marketing Manager is responsible for all marketing related activities and results related to retail and commercial banking deposit growth, community involvement, sponsorships, advertising, and digital banking. This position is also responsible for setting the direction of the marketing plan for the Bank along with budgeting within the department.</p>
<p>Key Responsibilities and Accountabilities</p>
<p>The Marketing Manager's key accountabilities include, but are not limited to the following:</p>
<ul>
<li>Develops and implements marketing plans driven by the Bank’s Business Plan</li>
<li>Develop and deploy initiatives to protect, strengthen, and build the Bank’s brand and visibility</li>
<li>Sets strategy and oversees the implementation of Marketing and communications campaigns.</li>
<li>Develop and implement a cohesive plan to increase brand awareness and Bank visibility.</li>
<li>Manage a marketing budget to effectively gain a return on dollars utilized.</li>
<li>Conduct market analysis to identify challenges and opportunities for growth.</li>
<li>Build relationships with media and stakeholders through creative PR strategies.</li>
<li>Monitor all marketing campaigns</li>
<li>Achieve or exceed AxiomGO sales plan and net income.</li>
<li>Develop, and monitor marketing campaigns for LOBs that align with the division and brand requirements and overall marketing programs.</li>
<li>Source, present, and execute sales contracts of AxiomGO to third party business partners.</li>
<li>Oversee and maintain relationships with marketing, partners, external vendors, and other third-party resources to develop, execute and monitor discrete marketing campaigns including direct mail, email, digital, social and in-branch merchandising.</li>
<li>Oversees the creation of Marketing materials. Organize and distribute promotional materials for marketing campaigns to ensure thorough and timely communications to stakeholders.</li>
<li>Coordinate with product, sales and support teams to create and deliver campaign guides, info graphics and reporting. Assist with developing and maintaining tools to support sales, product knowledge and customer service.</li>
<li>Implement and manage customer experience analysis and measurement processes</li>
<li>Directs activities in market research, branding, advertising and promotion campaigns and public relations</li>
<li>Provides leadership and support for the design, development and implementation of new product lines and services. Monitor and evaluate customer requests for product improvements or enhancements.</li>
<li>Oversees and direct market research and analysis</li>
<li>Applies benchmark criteria to measure and continually improve the efficiency and effectiveness of marketing initiatives; Examines multiple data points to analyze and evaluate efficacy of marketing strategies and activities, and ensure highest ROI for the bank</li>
<li>Identifies, interprets, and capitalizes on marketing trends.</li>
<li>Develops, implements, and administers the annual marketing budget</li>
<li>Hires, coaches, develops and manages performance of Marketing specialist and AxiomGO RM.</li>
<li>Ensures the implementation of Marketing best practices</li>
<li>Maintain current, comprehensive knowledge regarding the division products and services; including benefits and advantages, rules and regulations, reports and price structures.</li>
<li>Understand and comply with bank policy, laws, regulations, and the Bank's BSA/AML Program, as applicable to your job duties. This includes but is not limited to; complete compliance training and adhere to internal procedures and controls; report any known violations of compliance policy, laws, or regulations and report any suspicious customer and/or account activity.</li>
</ul>
