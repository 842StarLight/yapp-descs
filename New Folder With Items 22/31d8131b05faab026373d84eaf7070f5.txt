<h2>Sr HR Business Partner</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>We’re looking for a high-energy, business focused, service-oriented Sr HR Business Partner to join our global HR team. We’re looking for a strategic, yet hands-on, business partner to support our Go-To-Market organizations including Sales, Operations, and Analytics and be one of our HR leaders in our Cincinnati office.<br>
<br>
This role will report to the CHRO and will be a change agent to drive organizational transformation, high performance, talent development, succession, and engagement, along with other progressive HR initiatives for the company. If you’re someone who thrives in a dynamic, fast-paced, team environment where you are empowered to drive change, we want to talk to you!<br>
<br>
<b>Key Accountabilities</b><b>:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Partner with leaders to shape their vision and align people practices to drive high performance</li>
<li>
Implement and facilitate talent management programs and initiatives to meet the business needs of the organization</li>
<li>
Lead talent processes, performance review, and compensation processes</li>
<li>
Ensure compensation programs and initiatives enable us to attract, reward and retain top talent</li>
<li>
Use data and analytics to drive improvement (e.g. retention, engagement, hiring)</li>
<li>
Identify training needs and facilitate training</li>
<li>
Proactively anticipate business needs, formulate and drive change initiatives</li>
<li>
Coach managers to pro-actively manage performance challenges and through other employee relations issues</li>
<li>
Lead and resolve Employee Relations issues, including investigations and resolution</li>
<li>
Facilitate the integration of new acquisitions</li>
<li>
Lead and participate in other HR team projects/programs</li>
</ul>
