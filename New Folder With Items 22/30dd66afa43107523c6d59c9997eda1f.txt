<h2>Proposal Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Proposals Manager will lead a team of business proposal writers whose primary responsibility is to respond to school district and state-wide RFPs, RFIs and catalog bids. The Proposals Manager will be responsible for balancing workload across the team, maintaining adherence to timelines/processes and ensuring a high quality final product to represent Istation.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Position Details</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Full-time position at our Dallas-based corporate office</li>
<li>Reports to Chief Financial Officer</li>
<li>Base salary</li>
<li>Group health plan and company-wide options</li>
<li>401(k) with company match</li>
<li>Building amenities</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p><b>
</b></p>
<p><b>Main Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Determine which RFPs, RFIs and catalog bids to respond to</li>
<li>Manage team of proposal writers, including determining work assignments, evaluating performance, recognizing key accomplishments and managing personnel issues</li>
<li>Ensure adherence to timelines, interim deadlines and standard processes</li>
<li>Interface with other departments on timelines, processes, etc.</li>
<li>Review proposals before submission</li>
<li>Write RFP responses as needed</li>
<li>Conduct comprehensive win/loss analysis</li>
<li>Ensure appropriate resources (e.g., subscription services, information storage software, etc.) available to the team</li>
<li>Perform other job duties as assigned</li>
<br>
</ul>
<br>
Required Skills<br>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrated leadership capability; able to set priorities and ensure achievement</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Ability to collaborate effectively with direct reports, department heads and subject matter experts across the organization</li>
<li>Commitment to developing and maintaining a standardized process across the team</li>
<li>Attention to detail and a commitment to accuracy</li>
<li>Ability to be flexible and multi-task in a fast-paced environment with fluctuating priorities</li>
<li>Ability to work as needed to meet tight deadlines and at peak periods</li>
<br>
</ul>
