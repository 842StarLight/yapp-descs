<div><b>Overview of position:<br>
</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>Summary:
<br>
</div>
<p>Is responsible for the purchase of a wide variety of technical and non-technical material and equipment required for the daily operation of Genesis HealthCare System. This involves working closely with vendors, clinicians, medical staff and management to obtain products that offer the greatest quality and value to GHS. Works with the Contract Consultant providing information for RFP’s for products.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<div><b>Essential Duties:<br>
</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>Responsibilities:
<br>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Review Group Purchasing contracts and utilizes these contracts when feasible. Maintains current knowledge of GPO contract availability and status.
</li>
<li>Recommends new products and presents them to the appropriate person.
</li>
<li>Assists in conducting product evaluations on patient care and non-clinical supplies as needed. Performs value analysis on products.
</li>
<li>Conducts financial analysis of benefits involved in the selection of product including the impact on existing contracts, service quality and delivery.
</li>
<li>Able to exercise judgment in contracting and for purchasing.
</li>
<li>Resolves vendor invoice discrepancies through appropriated individuals.
</li>
<li>Sources requisitions to purchase orders based on need.
</li>
<li>Reviews open order report and follows up on the status of outstanding orders.
</li>
<li>Documents cost savings
</li>
<li>Adheres to standards for cost effectiveness to insure the highest quality of goods is provided that maximize value.
</li>
<li>Stays abreast of current developments, trends, innovations and opportunities in all areas of responsibility.
</li>
<li>Interviews sales representatives to keep abreast of new and worthwhile products for Genesis.
</li>
<li>Provides product information for new items needing added to the MMIS System.
</li>
<li>Processes returns.
</li>
<li>Monitors Pricelynx tool and works on opportunities for cost reduction
</li>
<li>Assists with capital purchases and special requests.<br>
</li>
</ul>

</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>Other information:
<br>
</div>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or equivalent.
</li>
<li>Certified Materials &amp; Resource Professional (CMRP) Certification required, or obtained within 24 months of hire.

</li>

</li>
<li>Demonstrated excellence with listening, verbal and written communication skills.
</li>
<li>Ability to provide outstanding customer service while maintaining a communicative relationship with customers.
</li>
<li>Ability to handle multiple projects, and work independently and under pressure in a complex and changing working environment.
</li>
<li>Excellent computer skills and knowledge of computer software, including programs such as Word, Excel, Power Point, etc.<br>
</li>
</ul>
