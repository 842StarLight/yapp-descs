<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Requirements</b></h2>
<p><b>
Essential Role and Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Deliver on quota expectations</li>
<li>Manage all sales activities, including self-sourcing new business, uncovering customer challenges, painting the vision for NUVI’s value and negotiating contracts</li>
<li>Demonstrate an understanding in how to drive sales to CXO’s, Digital and Social Marketers in the Enterprise B2B space.</li>
<li>Exhibit proficiency at creating and nurturing executive relationships on their own while still relying on the NUVI team as needed</li>
<li>Consistently meet and exceed sales goals</li>
<li>Accurately forecast revenue goals using CRM and industry standards</li>
<li>Build pipeline both personally and using your inside sales and partner teams</li>
<li>Work with leadership, Account Development, Marketing and NUVI Product teams to create and execute on pipeline-generating activities</li>
<li>Collaborate with NUVI Account Management to successfully upsell/cross-sell into your existing customer base</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>The Experience You'll Need</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 4-5 years proven track record of Enterprise sales experience including to the Fortune 5000</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to sell SaaS in the complex Enterprise B2B space</li>
<li>Creative thinker, problem solver and self-motivated</li>
<li>Experience using Salesforce or similar CRM tool</li>
<li>Knowledgeable about the digital marketing space</li>
<li>Excellent communication and presentations skills</li>
<li>College degree, (MBA preferred)</li>
<li>Ability to work in a team environment internally and ability to create relationships with clientele</li>
</ul>
