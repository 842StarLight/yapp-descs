<h2>Store Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<div><b>What you will do</b></div>
<div>
The Store Manager is responsible for overseeing all aspects of the Source 1 HVAC Supply location including: store sales and customer support and satisfaction, order administration, inventory management, warehousing, employee development &amp; training and showroom appearance and promotion and marketing.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
<b>
How you will do it</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for store sales growth both in residential units and parts &amp; supplies</li>
<li>
Manage customer relationships, support and maintains an efficient operation.</li>
<li>
Leads, develops and trains the Counter Sales and Warehouse Associates</li>
<li>
Maintains inventory control to adequately serve customers while maintaining 98% cycle count accuracy and maximum turn rates</li>
<li>
Manages all shipping and receiving activities to assure that products are handled and stored in a safe and efficient manner.</li>
<li>
Trains warehouse personnel in material handling methods, and insuring that the applicable OSHA requirements are fulfilled including forklift certification.</li>
<li>
Manages the product selection function for the location.</li>
<li>
Administers the Factory Warranty policies and includes the processing of warranty claims and the proper disposition of defective items.</li>
<li>
Ensures that the facility team adheres to all branch policies and procedures.</li>
<li>
Responsible for ensuring that the building and its inventory is secure at all times.</li>
</ul>
