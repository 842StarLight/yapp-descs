<h2>Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Alpha Media – Far East Bay, CA </b>is seeking an Account Executive for our stations in Pleasanton. The ideal candidate must be a self-starter who is able to work with clients to meet marketing objectives, sell the value of Alpha Media local radio and digital brands, and have a proven record of building and maintaining ongoing relationships with existing clients through the entire sales cycle.</p>
<p>
Alpha Media is a diverse multimedia company sharing your favorite music, sports and news across a variety of platforms. Whether it’s on your phone, desktop, tablet, Alexa, Google Home or in your car, our stations will enhance your journey. We hire and retain top talent who are unique, innovative and vibrant. We believe in creating progressive products, world-class events, and building strong relationships in our communities.</p>
<p>
Headquartered in Portland, Oregon Alpha Media operates radio stations across the United States covering all formats.</p>
<p>
We are live, local, and can't wait to talk to you!</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities for this position may include:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Sell radio and digital advertising.</li>
<li>Provide valuable consultative services to small, mid-size, and large firms and bring a concentrated focus on business development for both you and your clients.</li>
<li>Prospect for new accounts and identify local businesses that have a need for our products and services.</li>
<li>Set appointments, meet with clients, make sales presentations and execute contracts.</li>
<li>Establish and execute weekly, daily, and monthly sales plans.</li>
<li>Assist with writing advertising copy as required by the client.</li>
<li>Coordinate the production and scheduling of advertising with the Production and Traffic Departments.</li>
<li>Coordinate with the Promotions Director to develop sales promotion programs that fit your client’s needs.</li>
<li>Meet established revenue requirements.</li>
<li>Ensure that company initiatives and tools provided are used and maximized.</li>
<li>Closely monitor the progress of all client campaigns to ensure maximum success.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements of this position include the following:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>A minimum five years’ outside media sales experience.</li>
<li>Goal oriented, competitive and have the confidence to approach local businesses with marketing plans to help their business grow.</li>
<li>Excellent written and oral communication skills.</li>
<li>Proficient prospecting and business development skills.</li>
<li>The role requires an enthusiastic and hardworking person who exudes passion for Alpha Media’s unique platform and value proposition.</li>
<li>This position requires a fully insured personal vehicle and valid driver’s license.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Preference may be given to candidates who have the above experience plus the following:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Previous broadcast media sales experience.</li>
<li>Experience building strategic presentations and dynamically presenting them to clients.</li>
<li>Experience and knowledge of Microsoft Office programs.</li>
</ul>
