<p>EXFO develops smarter network test, monitoring and analytics solutions for the world’s leading telecommunications service providers, network equipment manufacturers and webscale companies—and we love what we do! With nearly 1,900 employees in more than 25 countries, EXFO is no. 1 worldwide in fiber optic test solutions and has the largest active assurance deployment. Our broad portfolio of intelligent hardware and software solutions enable our customer’s network transformations related to fiber, 5G, virtualization and big data analytics. We’re always looking for top talent to help us lead the way in a thriving industry with boundless opportunities.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>Job summary
</b></p>
<p>EXFO is seeking a dynamic, self-motivated Sales Executive with a proven track record of increasing sales within Western USA. This individual will drive the business development and the customer engagement process. You will be part of a team which is responsible for the definition and implementation of sales plans. In parallel you will help integrate these programs into the overall Strategic Sales Plan. The ideal candidate must have the strategic relationships, experience and skills required to sell up to VP levels.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>What you’ll do<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Sales Focus:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for the overall revenue and margin growth of the complete line of EXFO products
</li>
<li>Anticipates and assesses new opportunities within their assigned region
</li>
<li>Effectively utilizing and motivating all available resources (sales account team, technical support specialists, and channel partners) to aggressively pursue revenue opportunities
</li>
<li>Communicates effectively to ensure the flow of information amongst the key internal (EXFO) stakeholders and the customer.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Customer Focus:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Understands the customers’’ business and is able to support them by aligning EXFO’s solutions to their business needs.
</li>
<li>Raise EXFO’s presence by positioning EXFO as a business partner who understands the accounts’ technologies and business needs<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Product Focus </b><b>:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Understands the customers’ vision, their business goals and metrics, their strategic direction and challenges, and can successfully communicate this to the EXFO team.
</li>
<li>Align the EXFO Product Management Team with accounts’ and engage the customer on road map decisions to align with upcoming customer requirements .<br>
</li>
</ul>
