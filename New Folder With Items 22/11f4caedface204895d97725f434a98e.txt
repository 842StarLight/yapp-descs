<h2>Media Account Manager -- Enterprise</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Read something interesting online today? There's a good chance it reached you because of our technology.</div>
<p>Taboola is the world's leading content discovery platform, serving 360B recommendations to over 1B unique visitors each month on the web's most innovative publisher sites, including NBC, USA Today, The Weather Channel, Tribune and Fox Sports</p>
<p>
We are hiring a <b>Media Account Manager</b> to join our NYC team and focus on our Strategic / Tier 1 advertisers. This person will be responsible for managing a large, dynamic book of business and developing strong relationships with our advertisers. We are looking for someone who is passionate about content, has strong client-facing experience, and is ready to hit the ground running at an exciting, growing company.<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities</b><br>
</p>
<div><ul>
<li>Manage client relationships during all post sale activities</li>
<li>Launch, monitor and optimize all campaigns on a weekly basis through dynamic and strategic thinking, problem solving and data analysis to convey optimal account positioning to meet client KPI's</li>
<li>Report on strategic insights, recommendations and new product capabilities to each client on a regular basis with a goal to upsell and seek new revenue opportunities</li>
<li>Conduct in-person quarterly business reviews with key accounts</li>
<li>Analyze book of business and identify opportunities for growth and improvement</li>
<li>Monitor and identify trends in network data</li>
<li>Upsell to hit quarterly revenue goals</li>
</ul></div>
<p><b>Requirements</b><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 3 - 5 years of online advertising experience</li>
<li>Proven results in a customer-facing role; ability to build strong relationships</li>
<li>Strong analytical skills and and the ability to learn quickly</li>
<li>Excellent time management and prioritization skills</li>
<li>Outstanding communication skills</li>
<li>A true passion for online content and in-depth knowledge of web marketing</li>
<li>The will and ability to work in a fast-paced, ever-evolving environment and learn, learn, learn!</li>
<li>Preference for candidates with a media agency or publisher background or both would be a plus</li>
</ul>
