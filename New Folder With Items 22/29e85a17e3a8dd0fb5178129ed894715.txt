<h2>Enterpise Account Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div><b>POSITION SUMMARY:</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
The Enterprise Account Director is responsible for all aspects of an enterprise client’s relationship with FacilitySource: account health, service delivery, relationship management, growth, and profitability. The incumbent will organize, lead and execute an overall account plan that delivers balanced sales growth while overseeing the delivery of current services at agreed-upon levels. They will be focused on profitable business growth while maintaining and improving customer satisfaction and have a long-term multi-year perspective on account management. Ultimately, this role is evaluated on the delivery of account and relationship growth, service levels, and appropriate profit levels.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>
DUTIES &amp; RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
The Enterprise Account Director will be responsible for all aspects of FacilitySource’s relationship with the client. This role will perform the following for FacilitySource and its clients:</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
Grow and maintain FacilitySource relationships within and across the client, both directly and across the broader FacilitySource team.<br>
</div>
<div>Identify opportunities for new business within the client. Use the full suite of FacilitySource services and capabilities to expand the ways in which, and the extent to which, we work for clients. Deliver profitable revenue growth<br>
</div>
<div>Lead and manage service delivery teams in meeting and exceeding our service commitments.<br>
</div>
<div>Own and deliver targeted P&amp;L performance.<br>
</div>
<div>Engage marketing, service line, or “trade” expertise from across the company to provide additional services to Clients.<br>
</div>
<div>Act as the ultimate “owner” of the company’s relationship with Clients<br>
</div>
<div>Support monthly metrics, quarterly business reviews with thought leadership and present growth opportunities in this forum. Deploy the full analytical capabilities of FacilitySource’s technology and team.<br>
<br>
Perform the necessary tasks to ensure robust sales, operations, client and management reporting, including but not limited to: Establishing goals, analyzing growth and providing accurate, complete, and timely submission of required progress reports, forecast, and status of opportunities in the company’s salesforce.com system.</div>
