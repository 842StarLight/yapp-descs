<h2>Channel Sales Manager, San Mateo</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>We currently have an opening for a<b> Channel Sales Manager </b>in our San Mateo, CA headquarter. Responsible for increasing company revenue by managing existing partners.</p>
<p><b>
Who are we looking for?</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of 5+ years quota-carrying sales experience in a direct or channel sales capacity</li>
<li>Experience collaborating and managing SaaS channel partners</li>
<li>Strong verbal, written and presentation communication skills, with ability to translate technical and customer information into functional requirements</li>
<li>Ability to think and plan strategically</li>
<li>Excellent communication and cross-functional teamwork skills (product management, support, sales and senior management)</li>
<li>Exposure to one or more of the following ecosystems: Salesforce, Shopify, Magento, Netsuite, Zendesk is a strong plus</li>
<li>Experience in sales or business development with web design agencies</li>
<li>Demonstrated organization and prioritization skills</li>
<li>College degree</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What would you do, if hired?</b></p>
<p>
After learning the basics of our business, the Channel Sales Manager will manage and recruit partners to refer and resell Celigo products and generate leads. Responsibilities will include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Carry a quota, working with sales reps and sales managers at channel partners to drive sales</li>
<li>Coordinate with marketing to generate and execute on outbound marketing campaigns to increase exposure and partner recruitment.</li>
<li>Accurate forecasting of partner relationships and revenue tied to the leads generated.</li>
<li>Report back to the business market trends, including competitive landscape or any other relevant information that will help us shape our success.</li>
<li>Executing outbound telephone and email communications to partners</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Why you’ll love it here</b></p>
<ul>
<li>We are solving a really hard problem that affects almost every business on the planet: integrating cloud apps</li>
<li>You get the best of both worlds: a start-up opportunity at a company that has already established itself in the market with thousands of happy customers.</li>
<li>You’ll have the opportunity to make a significant contribution to the company as well as the iPaaS industry as a whole.</li>
<li>We’re at an exciting crossroads as we re-imagine the Integration and iPaaS space. This will take teamwork, creativity, strategic thinking and a willingness to advance the market.</li>
<li>We offer a strong benefits package, 401k, pre-tax commuter expense reimbursement, fully stocked kitchen, snacks, and many other perks.</li>
</ul>
