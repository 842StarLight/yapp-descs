<h2>Seattle Commercial Pre-Sales/Systems Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><div><b>Job Summary</b></div></div>
<div>
<div>
<br>
The Sales Engineer is responsible for identifying and matching technology opportunities with the customer’s business issues and objectives, as well as channel partner training and enablement. The SE is a high impact, very prominent role responsible for establishing Nutanix as an innovative technical leader for commercial and closing commercial transactions within assigned territories.</div>
<br>
<b>
Responsibilities:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>
Engage as part of a sales team to achieve or exceed revenue targets.</li>
<li>
Drive sales with technical expertise, account management skills, sales ability, and exceptional customer focus.</li>
<li>
Present Nutanix Solutions value proposition to customers and partners.</li>
<li>
Participate in the consultation to prospective users and/or product capability assessment and validation as it applies to the technical sales process.</li>
<li>
Install, support, and maintain Nutanix solutions in pre-sales POC deployments.</li>
<li>
Build long-term business relationships and become a trusted advisor within each account to identify opportunities and sell Nutanix Solutions.</li>
<li>
Ability to leverage and maximize reseller/alliance/OEM/SI partner relationships to increase sales performance.</li>
<li>
Create and deliver technical product presentations and/or training sessions to partners and customers.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Requirements:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Hands on experience with Server Virtualization and Enterprise Storage</li>
<li>
Thorough understanding of one or more of the following areas:</li>
<ul>
<li>
Devops/Microservices</li>
<li>
Cloud operations</li>
<li>
Big Data</li>
<li>
Databases (SQL or noSQL)</li>
</ul>
<li>
Ability to motivate, train and conduct seminars within CSA partners to increase Nutanix revenues within assigned territory.</li>
<li>
Ability to maintain relationships with technical resources at all levels of a customer organization.</li>
<li>
Thorough understanding of technical sales techniques and processes including the ability to understand customer needs, overcome objections, assist in the development of business cases, and technically close deals.</li>
<li>
Flexible, dependable, self-starter, and capable of quickly learning new products and technologies.</li>
<li>
Excellent oral and written communications skills, as well as excellent presentation skills.</li>
<li>
Ability to travel within assigned territory (Portland).</li>
<li>
High level of specialized sales and product solution knowledge.</li>
<li>
Direct professional experience with data center virtualization</li>
<li>
Experience selling servers, storage hardware, enterprise software, or networking.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of Linux/UNIX.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Qualifications and Experience:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree or equivalent experience.</li>
<li>
3+ years prior Sales Engineering or Reseller experience in a high tech sales environment with a demonstrated track record of success in driving customer adoption of technology.</li>
<li>
Hands-on experience with Nutanix products (or similar) is strongly preferred.</li>
</ul>
