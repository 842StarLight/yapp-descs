<b>About FreshLime:</b><br>
FreshLime is the marketing platform for local businesses. Our proprietary technology platform integrates the core elements local business owners need to connect with more customers and keep them coming back. By integrating social, reviews, and customer engagement into a single platform we save business owners valuable time and help them make more money.
<br><br>
FreshLime was founded in 2015 by a team that has successfully helped over 100,000 local businesses with their most important marketing efforts. Today we provide analytics and insights to more than 60,000 local businesses in 103 countries around the globe.
<br><br>
Enjoy the security of a base salary and the thrill of an uncapped commission on new customer revenue. The majority of your time will be spent working with warm leads and referrals. Our Account Executives earn commission on new customer sign-ups.
<br><br>
<b>Job Responsibilities / Expectations:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Learn what we have to offer and understand how it will benefit local Businesses</li>
<li>Make daily prospecting calls on the leads provided or ones you find on your own</li>
<li>Manage a sales pipeline from Setting Appointments to Closing Deals</li>
<li>Identify customer needs using a combination of discovery and technology</li>
<li>Overcome sales objections with professionalism and expertise</li>
<li>Keep an open communication with clients and build a strong relationship</li>
</ul>
