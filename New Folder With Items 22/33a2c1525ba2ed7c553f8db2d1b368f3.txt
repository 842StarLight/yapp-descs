<h2>Nuisance Wildlife Technician</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>$19.00 Per Hour</b></p>
<p><b>At Orkin, our purpose is to help protect the world where we live, work and play.</b></p>
<p>Our <b>Wildlife Technicians</b> are full-time with benefits, a company vehicle, fully paid training, competitive wages and the opportunity for career growth!</p>
<p><b>Construction or roofing experience required</b></p>
<p>We are searching for both experienced <b>Wildlife Technicians </b>as well as candidates willing to learn through our company training. As an employee with us, you will be provided the opportunity to grow your career and control your financial opportunity with a successful and growing company.</p>
 <p><b>The Successful Wildlife Technician Will . . .</b></p>
 <ul>
<li>Drive company vehicle to customer locations</li>
<li>Meet with customers to explain service and service agreement and have customers fill out and sign appropriate paperwork</li>
<li><b>Perform exclusion and repair work on areas that nuisance wildlife enter the structure, using hand tools, including sealing, caulking, or installation of sheet metal and screening to soffits, gutter lines, roof ridges, vents, and voids etc.</b></li>
<li>Inspects the interior and exterior of the building to be treated</li>
<li>Provides uncompromised service to our wildlife control customers through exclusion, trapping, and removal of wildlife</li>
<li>Advise customer of the services provided</li>
<li>Conduct annual re-inspections of customer’s property as assigned</li>
<li>Maintain cleanliness of vehicle and equipment</li>
 </ul>
