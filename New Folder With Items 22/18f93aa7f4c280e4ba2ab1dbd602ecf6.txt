<h2>Customer Service Rep II</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview</b></h2>
<br>
<div>
Patterson Dental is seeking a Customer Service Representative to join our team. Our customer service representatives are responsible for providing and maintaining a professional standard of service to our customers by responding to inquiries in a courteous and helpful manner. As a CSR, you will assist the customers in determining their professional needs when receiving or placing telephone calls.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Responsibilities</b></h2>
<br>
<div><b>
Customer Service Representative Main Duties:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Responds to inquiries from customers and sales personnel utilizing enterprise resource planning (ERP) system.</li>
<li>
Determines the professional needs of customers when receiving or placing calls.</li>
<li>
Upgrades telephone orders.</li>
<li>
Introduces and promotes new products during calls.</li>
<li>
Researches and resolves customer problems relating to the merchandise department.</li>
<li>
Participates in and supports campaigns/promotions sponsored by the Marketing Department, Region and/or Branch.</li>
<li>
Expedites credits on merchandise returned from accounts.</li>
<li>
Coordinate meetings, accompanying logistics, and other Customer service representative duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Qualifications</b></h2>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
High school diploma or equivalent required.</li>
<li>
2-5 years as a Customer Service Representative in the dental industry.</li>
<li>
Dental background and product knowledge preferred.</li>
<li>
Computer experience is required.</li>
</ul>
