<h2>Corporate Counsel</h2>

<p><b>About INFICON:</b></p>
<p>
INFICON is headquartered in Switzerland and has world-class manufacturing facilities in Europe, the United States and China, as well as subsidiaries in China, Denmark, Finland, France, Germany, Hong Kong, India, Italy, Japan, Korea, Liechtenstein, Singapore, Sweden, Switzerland, Taiwan, the United Kingdom and the United States. INFICON registered shares (IFCN) are listed on the SIX Swiss Exchange. This position will be located at INFICON’s U.S. headquarters in East Syracuse, NY
</p>
<p>This is a ground floor opportunity for a highly motivated professional to expand and broaden a career in corporate law. You will collaborate with many areas of INFICON's business; direct, advise and research legal entities when required as stated below under Basic Functions. Some travel is required, depending on the business.</p>
<p>
For more information about INFICON please visit www.inficon.com.</p>
<p><b>
Basic Functions:</b></p>
<p>
INFICON Inc. has a new position for Corporate Counsel located in East Syracuse, NY. The ideal candidate will provide direction, guidance, and assistance on legal matters related to INFICON within areas including, but not limited to, contracts, taxation, intellectual property, warranty/recalls, acquisitions, employment/regulatory compliance, litigation and general corporate law.
</p>
<ul>
<li>Negotiate, write and execute agreements and contracts on behalf of the company</li>
<li>Review, draft, and negotiate a range of sales agreements, including distribution and reseller agreements, pricing agreements, group purchasing organization agreements, private label agreements, rebate agreements, marketing agreements, sponsorship agreements, evaluation agreements, license agreements, business associate agreements, and non-disclosure agreements; advise on contract status, legal risks, and legal liabilities associated with different deals; provide advice that balances business objectives and legal risk</li>
<li>Advise leadership within the company and offer counsel on a variety of legal issues and act as liaison with outside counsel on matters requiring external legal assistance; work closely with several business units; deliver accurate, creative, timely, and user-friendly legal advice in a fast paced environment
</li>
<li>Manage the continuous improvement of standard form agreements and contracting workflows, including updating templates and delivering training, to drive efficiency; maintain legal research database</li>
<li>Provide general regulatory advice and training, remain current on legal decisions, statutory changes, and other developments, and advise accordingly.</li>
<li>Research and anticipate unique legal issues that could impact the company</li>
<li>Review advertising &amp; marketing materials to ensure they meet legal requirements</li>
<li>Add value to the business by supporting new business initiatives and projects; positively influence outcomes through persuasion and collaboration</li>
</ul>
<p><b>EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Licensed attorney with J.D. degree from recognized law school; admitted to recognized bar</li>
<li>2-3 years’ experience in a law firm and/or in-house at a corporation</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to master wide ranging fields of law &amp; apply in complex problem solving in support of the business and to communicate critical information in an effective manner</li>
<li>Contract drafting, interpretation and negotiation preferred</li>
<li>Although not required, intellectual property and multi-national experience is a plus.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS AND SKILLS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Highly effective written and verbal skills with the ability to relate directly, openly, and effectively with internal and external parties</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal and presentation skills, along with ability to communicate effectively with all levels of the organization</li>
<li>Strong conceptual, analytical and negotiation skills and superb listening capabilities</li>
<li>High degree of organization with demonstrated attention to detail</li>
<li>Firm understanding of contractual terms and legal, regulatory, and compliance concepts</li>
<li>Proactive, practical business sense</li>
<li>Possesses a personal presence that is characterized by a sense of honesty and integrity</li>
<li>Ability to work independently on routine matters and exercise appropriate independent judgment and also know when to seek guidance and input from relevant stakeholders</li>
<li>Ability to work in a fast-paced, multi-tasking environment under tight deadlines</li>
</ul>
<p>INFICON empowers employees to be creative, goal oriented, self-directed with an open door policy to share and recommend ideas.</p>
