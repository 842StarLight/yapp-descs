<h2>Product Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Looking for the next step in your career? How does this sound: a high-impact role at a fast-growing startup in a brand new, rapidly emerging tech category disrupting the largest asset class in the world? We don't always use buzzwords, but in this case, they apply.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Real estate touches where every person lives, works and plays but until recently, tech was largely untouched in this industry. Now, real estate is becoming a user experience business, and companies building property technology (also known as "proptech") are seeing massive opportunities to enhance the physical world.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
At HqO, we are building a tenant experience platform that is already used in millions of square feet of commercial real estate across the US. The leading property owners and operators in the world are partnering with us on our mission to connect tenants to the places, experiences, and people that enrich where they live, work, and play.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
There are many ways to describe our team. We work hard. We're smart and passionate about the company we're building. We stay humble. We're obsessed with our customer. Oh, and we're very funny. Whether it's during our weekly lunch &amp; learns, happy hours or just an average day in the office, we always find time to make work fun. We eat our own dog food by creating a place that WE love. If you work with us, you will always be learning, improving, and teaching. Want to help us create places people love? Let's talk.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
The Role</b></div>
<div>
Your overriding objective will be to define and deliver differentiated, habit-forming, high-value products to customers, advance our leadership position in the space and fuel its growth. The successful Product Manager will possess the intellectual and interpersonal capabilities combined with the product management and business skills and experience necessary to meet that primary objective. This high-visibility role will give you the opportunity to make a significant contribution to the company's growth and success.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
What you will do</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Deeply understand and analyze our market and customer needs</li>
<li>
Talk to customers who manage commercial real estate space and tenants who use (or may use) the HqO platform to gather feedback and identify needs and opportunities</li>
<li>
Build business cases for new revenue streams and products</li>
<li>
Articulate the vision and define the roadmap for a key pillar of HqO's tenant experience platform and be responsible for the success of the product</li>
<li>
Define requirements and collaborate closely with UX and Engineering to build great products</li>
<li>
Drive the success of products by partnering with Sales, Customer Success, and Marketing</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
What you bring?</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
3+ years experience in a product management role (B2B SaaS or B2C)</li>
<li>
Excellent problem solving, organizational, and analytical skills</li>
<li>
You are a strong communicator. You thrive in the intersection of product, engineering, customer success and marketing</li>
<li>
You are a strategic thinker with strong execution skills and a "roll-up-your-sleeves" entrepreneurial attitude</li>
<li>
You are a natural collaborator who leads by influence</li>
<li>
Passion to deliver excellent end-to-end customer experiences</li>
<li>
Experience with agile development processes and rapid product release cycles</li>
<li>
Bachelor's Degree in an analytical discipline. MBA would be a plus</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Bonus Points</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
B2B2C product experience</li>
<li>
Experience with products whose market includes commercial estate owners</li>
<li>
Both mobile and web product experience</li>
</ul>
