<h2>Customer Service Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div><div><div>
<p><b>Innovative-IDM is known industry-wide for our "Legendary Customer Service"! We are seeking to add another "Legendary Player" to our Customer Service Team! </b>The Customer Service Representative is responsible for interacting with customer to assess needs, communicate product/cost information and enter orders/invoices to ensure product needs are met. This position is critical to Innovative-IDM; the Customer Service Rep functions as the liaison between the customer and all facets of the business to ensure products needed are ordered and received, all customer needs are met and that legendary service is provided.</p>
<p>You’ll serve as the customer’s primary point of contact and own customer issues, via phone and email, from start to finish. You must be a keen problem solver with a unique ability to think on your feet. You should be comfortable with taking initiative to help customers, and want to work within an energetic, fast-paced environment.</p>
<p>This is not a call center for robotic responses; this is a customer service group where people work together, own customer problems, and must decide what is best for the customer without looking at a script.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities include:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Promptly answers phones by the third ring, and responds to simple email inquiries within minutes.</li>
<li>Using our automated quoting system, provides customer quotes for parts within 2-3 hours of receiving the quote.</li>
<li>Many projects and tasks are assigned from a group-directed job queue. However, common sense and customer needs must prevail, so decision making is important and necessary.</li>
<li>Assists customers nationwide as well as locally.</li>
<li>Work closely with customer care reps at our suppliers. We use supplier intranets, websites.</li>
<li>Consults with new and existing customers (company buyers/inside sales) and provides information regarding products needed for customer application.</li>
<li>Provides cost efficient shipping information to customers to ensure delivery in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Researches and troubleshoots products to best fit customer needs and expectations.</li>
<li>Communicates with vendors to acquire parts to meet customer needs.</li>
<li>Prepares product quotes for customers requesting a specific item not carried in our warehouse.</li>
<li>Enters customer orders, purchase orders or billing information in system on a daily basis.</li>
<li>Processes credit card authorizations and creates invoices for products purchased.</li>
<li>Researches and resolves difficult customer service situations in a calm and efficient manner.</li>
<li>Functions as liaison between customer, technicians, warehouse and vendors to ensure needs are met.</li>
<li>Brings problems to the attention of a manager; is willing to ask for help.</li>
<li>Identifies and introduces new ideas and solutions to create efficiency in the operation.</li>
<li>Follows all procedures as detailed in customer service operations policies and guidelines.</li>
<li>Understands all parts stocked in the warehouse.</li>
</ul>
