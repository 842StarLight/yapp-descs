<h2 class="jobSectionHeader">
<br>
<b>Department Overview
</b>
</h2>Department provides financial support to the Supply Chain and Procurement operations team. Responsibilities include but are not limited to P&amp;L, Balance Sheet, and Budget planning and reporting as well as analyzing financial information to provide value through proactive collaboration.
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader">
<br>
<b>Job Description
</b>
</h2>
<ul>
<li>Record monthly journal entries during month end close
</li>
<li>Perform monthly account reconciliations
</li>
<li>Perform detailed monthly analysis of supply chain and procurement department cost actuals vs budget
</li>
<li>Prepare management actual to budget reporting for all of supply chain and procurement
</li>
<li>Prepare quarterly headcount reconciliation between the finance headcount and HR headcount and research deltas
</li>
<li>Prepare supply chain and procurement budget Plan for following year
</li>
<li>Perform budget revisions for supply chain and procurement, as needed
</li>
<li>Perform GDM system structure changes for supply chain and procurement
</li>
<li>Prepare management reporting for Freight and Duty
<ul><li>Work with operations to identify drivers for variances to forecast
</li></ul>
</li>
<li>Work with operations to prepare following year’s annual cost for freight and duty
<ul><li>To be updated every forecast cycle
</li></ul>
</li>
<li>Prepare sales and units management reporting
</li>
<li>Ad hoc reporting or analysis
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>

</li>
