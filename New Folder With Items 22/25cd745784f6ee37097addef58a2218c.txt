<div><b>Operations Coordinator (Contracts)</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>
Job Expectations/Performance</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
Contract Processing:</div>
<div>
<ul>
<li>Key and process new contract sales for WVR and WBW sales sites within established timeline
</li>
<li>Verify worksheet accuracy
</li>
<li>Validate sales figures of worksheet to SalePoint, resolving any discrepancies
</li>
<li>Audit contract quality
</li>
<li>Input sales information into CRS
</li>
</ul>
</div>
<ul>
<li><div>Provide timely feedback on processes helping strengthen business acumen for department
</div></li>
<li><div>Maintain effectiveness while experiencing constant change in new work structure, processes, requirements or culture.
</div></li>
<li><div>Quickly learn and achieve satisfactory technical and professional skills/knowledge; keep up with current development and trends.
 </div></li>
</ul>
<div>Accounting:
</div>
<ul>
<li><div>Ensures the timely and accurate processing of accounts receivable.</div></li>
<li>
<div>Works with accounts payables in order to maintain the financial integrity of the sales and marketing site. This includes processing payments, ensuring invoices are coded correctly, new vendors are set up and large check requests are submitted.
</div>
</li>
<li><div>Maintain several premium/ petty cash banks that require weekly validation submitted to site leaders.</div></li>
<li>
<div>Conduct monthly audits on all issued banks for all sales operations.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Maintain accurate records on a daily, weekly and monthly basis in regard to site related account information.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Perform random audits on all banks in accordance with company SOPs.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Maintain reconciliation of all cash submitted for deposit.</div>
</li>
</ul>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>
 This position requires working mainly late afternoon/ nights</b></div>
<br>
</div>
