<h2>Internet of Things (IoT)/Cloud Enablement, Senior Manager - Data Delivery</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<p>In case you missed it in the press, we’ve had some major success lately: our team was recently named a global leader in Internet of Things (IoT) services by Forrester. We're very proud of this honor, which supports Deloitte’s position as the #1 professional service provider in the IoT services market. Read more about the report here.</p>
<br>
<div></div>
<p><b>Work you'll do</b></p>
<br>
<div></div>
<p>Senior Managers are expected to contribute to the firm's growth and development in a variety of ways, including:</p>
<br>
<div></div>
<p>Engagement Management: Lead engagement planning and budgeting; mobilize and manage engagement teams; define deliverable structure and content; facilitate buy-in of proposed solutions from top management levels at the client; direct on-time, quality delivery of work products; manage engagement economics; manage engagement risk</p>
<br>
<div></div>
<p>Client Management: Manage day to day interactions with executive clients and sponsors</p>
<br>
<div></div>
<p>Business Development: Develop and maintain contact with top decision makers at key clients; organize and lead pursuit teams; participate and lead aspects of the proposal development process; contribute to the development of proposal pricing strategies</p>
<br>
<div></div>
<p>Practice Development &amp; Eminence: Develop practical solutions and methodologies; develop "thoughtware" and "point-of-view" documents; participate in public speaking events; get published in industry periodicals</p>
<br>
<div></div>
<p>People Development: Perform role of counselor and coach; provide input and guidance into the staffing process; actively participate in staff recruitment and retention activities; provide leadership and support for delivery teams and staff in local offices</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>The Team</b></p>
<br>
<div></div>
<p><b>Analytics &amp; Cognitive</b></p>
<br>
<div></div>
<p>In this age of disruption, organizations need to navigate the future with confidence, embracing decision making with clear, data-driven choices that deliver enterprise value in a dynamic business environment.</p>
<br>
<div></div>
<p>The Analytics &amp; Cognitive team leverages the power of data, analytics, robotics, science and cognitive technologies to uncover hidden relationships from vast troves of data, generate insights, and inform decision-making. Together with the Strategy practice, our Strategy &amp; Analytics portfolio helps clients transform their business by architecting organizational intelligence programs and differentiated strategies to win in their chosen markets.</p>
<br>
<div></div>
<p>Analytics &amp; Cognitive will work with our clients to:</p>
<br>
<div></div>
<p>Implement large-scale data ecosystems including data management, governance and the integration of structured and unstructured data to generate insights leveraging cloud-based platforms</p>
<br>
<div></div>
<p>Leverage automation, cognitive and science-based techniques to manage data, predict scenarios and prescribe actions</p>
<br>
<div></div>
<p>Drive operational efficiency by maintaining their data ecosystems, sourcing analytics expertise and providing As-a-Service offerings for continuous insights and improvements</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<br>
<div></div>
<p><b>Required:</b></p>
<br>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>
<p>10+ years experience with relevant technology consulting experience</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>1+ years hands-on experience in Cloud solutions architecture, including migration of existing applications to the cloud</p>
</li>
<li>Architect level implementation experience - for at least one project - with the design, implementation and operation of a full-scale IoT architecture data pipeline for housed assets, assets in motion and/or connected assets</li>
<li>Experience with one or more of the following:
<ul>
<li>
<p>AWS: IoT Core, IoT Greengrass, IoT Analytics; Kinesis streams, SageMaker, DynamoDB, Glue, Athena, API Gateway, TensorFlow</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Azure: IoT Hub, Stream Analytics (IoT); Machine Learning Services, Text Analytics, Async Messaging, Event Hub, Cosmos DB, NiFi</p>
</li>
<li>GCP: Cloud IoT Core, Dataflow, Dataproc, BigQuery, Machine Learning/ML Engine</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree or equivalent professional experience</li>
<li>Ability to travel 80-100% of the time (ie: Monday – Thursday/Friday)</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Preferred:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
<div>Knowledge of IoT market landscape including key players and competition in the market for IoT platform technologies, implementation partners, and networks</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Demonstrated experience driving both the sales cycle by taking the lead in framing out client opportunities and leading the pursuit team to create the proposal content for client discussions</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Experience with analytics platforms and best practices</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Certification: Designing and Implementing an Azure AI Solution (AI-100)</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Knowledge of Machine Learning, UI/UX Design, Network Architecture, API development, Edge computing, Design for Data (no SQL), Big Data Processing (Spark, Elastic Stack), Automation</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Demonstrated experience with management and delivery of multiple strategic engagements for large global organization</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Experience working in Top Tier Consulting firm</div>
<br>
</li>
</ul>
