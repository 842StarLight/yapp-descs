<b>What You'll Do:</b><br>
The Regional Sales Manager is responsible for managing a team of Account Executives supporting and selling into distinct sales territories across North America. This is a hands-on sales management role responsible for contributing to predictable new sales velocity, executive engagement with existing enterprise customers and a focus on the professional development of high-performing sales reps on the team.
<br><br>
<b>Here's a closer look at the duties in this key role:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Lead and support a team of direct reports by setting overall strategy, driving comprehensive territory and account planning and deal execution to achieve results</li>
<li>Ability to recruit, develop, and retain a high performing enterprise sales organization</li>
<li>Consistent and strong software sales experience in an individual contributor and management role, including sales management experience</li>
<li>Drive a consistent, measured, and managed sales process that ensures goal attainment and also improves AE productivity</li>
<li>Conduct a weekly forecast meeting with each AE</li>
<li>Coach direct reports on areas of improvement or course correction to achieve results</li>
<li>Provide detailed and accurate sales forecasting week over week to management</li>
<li>Measure, manage and drive individual and team activities, pipe generation and quarterly / annual goal attainment</li>
<li>Ability to lead a team in aggressively sourcing and creating pipeline by leveraging organizational best practices and assets.</li>
<li>Proven track record in Solution Selling with specific examples of business case value</li>
<li>Develop and execute in-territory travel plans encompassing on-site visits, sales presentations, networking events, LM field events and more</li>
</ul>
<b>What You'll Need:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's Degree or equivalent</li>
<li>5-7 years of related experience</li>
<li>2+ years experience managing high-performing technology teams</li>
<li>Experience selling cloud based enterprise applications</li>
<li>Proven track record as an individual sales contributor exceeding personal sales quota for more than 2 consecutive years</li>
<li>Ability to work in a fast-paced, high-growth environment, requiring superior skills in critical thinking, influencing, and managing multiple priorities under tight deadlines</li>
<li>Excellent presentation and listening skills</li>
</ul>
