<h2>Customer Support Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>The Company:</b></p>
<p>
AudienceView is an organization of people who are passionate about the business of Live Events. We create industry-leading software solutions that fuel attendee engagement, ticket sales and advertising solutions for sports, music and theatre.</p>
<p>
Through a combination of organic growth and key acquisitions we now have 3,000 clients, processing over 100M tickets annually across 8,000 venues that generates in excess $3B in ticketing and fundraising transactions across Europe &amp; North America. To support this continued expansion, we are seeking to add to our growing team.</p>
<p>
AudienceView employees share a vision to help entertainment organizations deliver exceptional experiences for people who love live events. We achieve this through innovative technology, popular media brands, effective distribution strategies and a dedicated team of experts that help create customer success every single day.</p>
<p><b>
The Position:</b></p>
<p>
We are looking for a passionate and knowledgeable contributor to our Customer Support team, responsible for helping our Vendini customers use their system daily. The successful candidate should be passionate about customer service and have a troubleshooting mindset. If solving problems and helping people is something that drives you, we are looking forward to hearing from you!</p>
<p><b>
Position Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Act as the first point of contact for our Vendini customer base and enjoy providing a delightful customer experience</li>
<li>Understand customer needs and provide assistance navigating them through how to use the system and troubleshooting any issues they encounter</li>
<li>Collaborate across internal teams and external stakeholders to provide expertise in assessment, analysis, evaluation of alternative solutions, and resolution of issues within the application</li>
<li>Understand industry best practices and suggest new approaches that optimize for customer success when answering technical and business questions</li>
<li>Troubleshoot hardware issues and iOS applications</li>
<li>Manage case queues ensuring response targets are met or exceeded</li>
<li>Participate as a key member in a rotating on-call schedule</li>
<li>Be prepared to work flexible shifts</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Hands-on experience in a customer-facing service or support role</li>
<li>Strong verbal and written communication skills in order to understand customer issues, communicate across internal teams, appropriately document cases and provide effective solutions or training</li>
<li>Excellent grasp of Mac and/or Windows operating systems, and internet applications</li>
<li>Critical thinking skills so when you don’t know the answer (and we know there are times you won’t) you can think about the problem and determine the steps you need to take to get results</li>
<li>Ability to multitask, and to set and manage dynamically changing priorities</li>
<li>Ability to work effectively as part of a team but also with minimal supervision</li>
<li>Ticketing industry knowledge is a plus</li>
</ul>
