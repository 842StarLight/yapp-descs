<h2>Revenue Operations Manager (LTG) Raleigh, US (3303)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>We are looking for a talented <b>Revenue</b> <b>Operations Manager</b> to join our team in our Raleigh offices.
</p>
<p>The primary job responsibilities include general oversight and supervision of the Revenue Operations team. The team is responsible for order management, billing and revenue in excess of $100m annually. This new role, created to support the Company’s growth and expansion strategy, will become a pivotal role within LTG’s Finance organization. It provides a career development path and exposure to key internal organizations within the Company such as Sales, Services, Legal and Sales Operations.
</p>
<p>Specifically the role will involve interaction with our Sales and Legal teams on deal structure as necessary, review and approval of sales quotes, assistance with the definition of the overall revenue recognition framework and any changes that may be required, and review and approval of the revenue process: sales order entry/invoicing/contract or deal analysis.</p>
<p>
Additionally, the role will ensure data integrity within LTG’s systems: NetSuite (financial), Salesforce.com (CRM), and Hive (time management). This role will help develop and optimize policies and procedures around the Salesforce.com opportunity close process and transfer of information to NetSuite and Hive.</p>
<p><b>
Why you will love it</b></p>
<p>
Based in beautiful, airy offices in the heart of Raleigh, you will get to work alongside others who are talented and have a passion for what they do. The environment is fast paced, enthusiastic and fun. Most importantly of all you will be a highly valued member of our team with great opportunities for personal growth.</p>
<p><b>
What you’ll be doing every day</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Supervise, lead and mentor Revenue &amp; Order Operations team members</li>
<li>Participate in the process improvement and automation project aimed at aligning Services, Sales and Finance and accelerating reporting.</li>
<li>Document the various processes where the Revenue &amp; Order Operations team is engaged and identify key controls.</li>
<li>Assist with monthly close and revenue analysis including providing explanation for actual to budget variances.</li>
<li>Drive continuous process and system improvements to increase efficiency of the end-to-end order management process</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<p><b>
About you</b><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>A minimum of 4 to 7 years of Order Operations and/or Revenue Management related work experience, preferably in the software industry.</li>
<li>College degree or comparable experience in finance or accounting</li>
<li>Revenue recognition for software companies with multiple elements under ASC 16 / IFRS 15 (SaaS, Professional Services, etc.)</li>
<li>NetSuite and Salesforce or similar ERP/CRM systems experience preferred</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to solve complex problems and strong written and oral communications skills</li>
</ul>
