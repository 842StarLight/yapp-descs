<h2>Call Center Representative - Bilingual English and Spanish</h2>

<p><b>Job Description</b>
</p>
<p>Call Center Professionals, if you’re passionate about providing world-class customer service and would like to grow your career with an industry-leading company, we may have just the perfect fit for you! Founded in 1957 and headquartered in Mahwah, NJ we are one of the nation's largest privately owned dedicated domestic call center companies with 20 call centers located throughout the United States.
</p>
<p>We are currently seeking Full Time and Part Time Call Center Representatives to assist our world-class clients and their customers with enrollments, renewals, cross sells and upsells. You’ll work in an environment that is rewarding and fun, with coworkers who are truly passionate about what they do.
</p>
<p>If this sounds like the opportunity you’ve been looking for and if you meet our qualifications, we invite you to bring your skills and expertise to our team and supply the fuel for growth. Contact us today!</p>
<p><b>
Job Responsibilities</b>
</p>
<p>As a Call Center Representative, you will ensure a memorable customer service experience by using questioning and listening skills to determine the customer’s needs and help them select the appropriate products and services.</p>
<p>
Specific duties for this CSR role include:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Answering customer requests or inquiries
</li>
<li>Upselling products and/or services to the customer
</li>
<li>Identifying and resolving customers’ issues using problem-solving skills</li>
<li>
Building a positive rapport across a variety of personality types</li>
<li>
Continually maintaining a working knowledge of our clients’ products, services and promotions
</li>
<li>Putting the customer first and remaining polite and professional at all times</li>
<li>
Documenting all customer information, communications and sales in CRM system</li>
</ul>
