<div><b>
Position Summary:</b></div>
<div>
The Account Executive, Furniture is responsible for initiating and closing profitable furniture sales. The Account Executive, Furniture serves as the primary contact for the client during the sales process and works with the other furniture support functions (design, project management, furniture sales support, etc.) to ensure that client needs are met and client satisfaction is achieved. As part of an integrated selling team, the Account Executive, Furniture collaborates with sales professionals from other Staples product categories to develop comprehensive sales plans for Staples accounts in an effort to maximize sales of all product categories. The Account Executive, Furniture reports to the Sales Director, Furniture.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>
Primary Responsibilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Prospect in the local marketplace for new furniture opportunities</li>
<li>Develop local business relationships with various influencers (A/D community, commercial real estate brokers, moving companies, etc.) in order to identify potential opportunities early</li>
<li>Manage the day to day relationship with existing clients for which the Account Executive, Furniture is assigned</li>
<li>Lead the development of creative and innovative solutions to meet client needs in a differentiated manner. Collaborate with Interior Designers to provide solutions that meet client functional, aesthetic and budgetary requirements</li>
<li>Lead the project strategy process. Involve stakeholders in the development of a winning sales plan to capture targeted accounts and/or opportunities</li>
<li>Work in a team selling environment to collaboratively develop account plans for existing accounts that maximize penetration and retention</li>
<li>Work with other Staples lines of business to provide cross-selling opportunities</li>
</ul>
