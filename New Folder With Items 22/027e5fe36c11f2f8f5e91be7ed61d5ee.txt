<h2>Director, Consulting - Enterprise Legal Management</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Wolters Kluwer is a global leader in professional information services. Professionals in the areas of legal, business, tax, accounting, finance, audit, risk, compliance and healthcare rely on Wolters Kluwer's market leading information-enabled tools and software solutions to manage their business efficiently, deliver results to their clients, and succeed in an ever more dynamic world. Every day, our customers make critical decisions to help save lives, improve the way we do business, build better judicial and regulatory systems. We help them get it right.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Ranked one of the world’s largest legal service organizations, Wolters Kluwer ELM Solutions serves corporate legal and insurance claims departments and their law firms worldwide with enterprise legal spend and matter management and legal analytics solutions. ELM Solutions products include Passport ® , the most powerful platform for Enterprise Legal Management; TyMetrix® 360° , the industry’s leading SaaS-based e-billing and matter management solution; and the LegalVIEW® portfolio of legal analytics solutions based upon the industry’s largest and most comprehensive legal spend database.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Direct and control activities on a portfolio of Enterprise Legal Management (ELM) Solutions services engagements overseeing the project managers to deliver on-time, on-budget, and profitable engagements to a high degree of client satisfaction
</li>
<li>Assess and mitigate engagement risk – presales, as well as project risk – post sales
</li>
<li>Lead the end-to-end design of a proposed solution, including the preparation of customer presentations, configurations, budgetary proposals, and detailed Statement of Work documents
</li>
<li>Manage delivery to project plans, including resources, schedules, milestones and budgets
</li>
<li>Understand contractual obligations outlined in the statement of work to ensure effective and timely scope management and staffing
</li>
<li>Lead rigorous process of inspection of portfolio of engagements to accurately track project status, quality, issues and task estimates to completion
</li>
<li>Assemble and manage multiple engagement teams in conjunction with services practice leads
</li>
<li>Conduct post-implementation assessments to document implementation success criteria and best practices and facilitate incorporation into ELM's implementation methodology
</li>
<li>Accountable for the services P&amp;L attainment which includes Order, Revenue, and Margin against targets
</li>
<li>Order, Revenue, Margin, and Resource forecasting for assigned portfolio of engagements
</li>
<li>Develop and manage strong customer relationships at senior levels and is an active participant in engagement in presales and delivery
</li>
<li>Act as a point of escalation for delivery issues for client
</li>
<li>Drive pull through services revenue post the initial sale
</li>
<li>Manage a direct team, including accountability for team effectiveness, people development and achieving utilization targets
</li>
<li>Contribute to knowledge management and best practices, drive for continuous improvement, and enhancements to the services offering catalog
</li>
<li>Identify and sponsor the ongoing development and improvement of delivery methodology, processes, and approaches for creating and leveraging intellectual capital
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>A minimum of eight years of experience in consulting and/or technical delivery services for external customers; preferably in corporate legal environment, insurance claims, or financial related solutions
</li>
<li>A minimum six years of experience in a leadership or senior position, with at least six years of P&amp;L management experience
</li>
<li>Experience managing multiple internal and external clients, as well as leading projects/implementations related to configurable products with simple to complex integrations with other applications used by clients
</li>
<li>Demonstrated understanding and proven track record designing client specific business solutions for end user applications
</li>
<li>Solid understanding of project management and consulting services methodologies
</li>
<li>Strong professional consulting skills including negotiation, client relationship management and scope management
</li>
<li>Experience with Agile implementation/development processes that serve as part of ELM's delivery implementation methodology preferred
</li>
<li>Ability to leverage internal tools and policies for optimal P&amp;L management
</li>
<li>Proficient with Microsoft Office Suite applications including Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Project and Outlook
</li>
<li>Demonstrated leadership attributes such as critical and strategic thinking, understanding and executing organizational goals, fostering positive attitude in the team and coaching project resources to delivery
</li>
<li>Ability to travel up to 35% at any given time
</li>
</ul>
