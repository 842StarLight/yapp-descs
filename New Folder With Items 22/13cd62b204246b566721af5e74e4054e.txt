<h2>Claims Technical Advisor- (Located in any NSA Claims Service Center: Scottsdale, Goldsboro, Davenport, or Stevens Point)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>OVERVIEW</b><p>
We're a people-focused business. And we pride ourselves on the promise to deliver efficient and effective services to our customers. At Sentry, we believe people are our competitive advantage. We strive to attract, train and motivate our employees to keep Sentry as a market leader in our industry. You could be the next key player to support our Non-Standard Auto unit. We are seeking an individual with technical knowledge of insurance policies and coverage, including thorough knowledge of claim handling to join our team as a Claims Technical Advisor. This position can be located in any of our Claims Service Centers. Locations include: Scottsdale, AZ; Davenport, IA; Goldsboro, NC; Stevens Point, WI.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<b>
WHAT YOU'LL DO</b><p>
You’ll provide exceptional guidance and direction to the Non-Standard Auto Claim Unit in order to help Sentry maintain its level of standards and ensure a high level of customer service is provided to our policyholders and claimants. In addition, you’ll:</p>
<ul>
<li>Continuously review pending claims for quality claim handling.</li>
<li>Authorize settlements for claims exceeding individual settlement limits.</li>
<li>Evaluate work of assigned claim staff to assure adherence to claim standards and to improve overall file quality.</li>
<li>Provide ongoing feedback to CSC leadership on the claims technical abilities and developmental opportunities for assigned staff.</li>
<li>Provide overall claims technical and file guidance to the assigned claim associates and actively participate in the training of new claim associates.</li>
<li>Participate in file review sessions to determine direction and monitors file progression through resolution.</li>
<li>Develop the claims technical skills of assigned claim associates as part of their assigned staff’s overall professional and individual development.</li>
<li>Ensure claims technology solutions / programs are utilized properly and the capturing of required claims data is timely, accurate and complete.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<b>
WHAT IT TAKES</b><ul>
<li>Bachelors Degree or equivalent work experience</li>
<li>Prior Non-Standard Auto claims handling experience required.</li>
<li>Professional designations such as the CPCU highly desired</li>
<li>5-8 years of related work experience in Auto Casualty / Bodily Injury and Litigation</li>
<li>Previous experience in claim handling, including investigations and settlements</li>
<li>Analytical ability to make appropriate decisions</li>
<li>Managerial/supervisory experience and/or training beneficial</li>
<li>Strong human relations and communications skills</li>
</ul>
