<div>
<b>Key Responsibilities:</b><br>
</div>
<div><ul><li>Provide basic support and assistance in all areas of Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) based Federal procurement.<br>
</li></ul></div>
<div><ul><li>Prepare or review pre-procurement packages.<br>
</li></ul></div>
<div><ul><li>Actively participate in developing and managing acquisition plans.<br>
</li></ul></div>
<div><ul><li>Prepare/research support documentation.<br>
</li></ul></div>
<div><ul><li>Make quality recommendations to the Contracting officer.<br>
</li></ul></div>
<div><ul><li>Handle and resolve problems, validating data, and otherwise completing actions needed to successfully advertise, solicit, construct, award, administer and or/or closeout Government contracts or task/delivery orders.<br>
</li></ul></div>
<div><ul><li>Monitor status of and support de-obligating undelivered orders (UDOs).<br>
</li></ul></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
<b>Required Experience &amp; Education:</b><br>
</div>
<div><ul><li>Must have been Federal Acquisition Certification in Contracting (FAC-C) Level I certified, Defense Acquisition Workforce Improvement Act (DAWIA) certified, or equivalent.<br>
</li></ul></div>
<div><ul><li>One (1) year of contract work experience based on the Contract Specialist (GS-1102) Qualification Standard.<br>
</li></ul></div>
<div><ul><li>Either a Bachelor's degree from an accredited institution or 24 semester hours of business-related college courses.<br>
</li></ul></div>
<div><ul><li>Experience in automated systems and software applications including Microsoft Word, Excel, Outlook, SharePoint, Adobe Acrobat, and teleconference applications.<br>
</li></ul></div>
<div><ul><li>US Citizenship is required.</li></ul></div>
