<p>Experience:
</p>
<ul>
<li>6-10 years experience in Business Continuity/Crisis Management/Disaster Recovery Program Management
</li>
<li>Experience working in the financial and/or technology industry
</li>
<li>Extensive experience in co-coordinating projects or programs, providing consultation and working with teams on multiple and complex projects under tight timelines.
</li>
<li>Experience in conducting business impact analysis, risk assessments, creating and maintaining continuity of operations plans, and emergency/crisis management/pandemic plans.
</li>

<p></p>
<p><b>Customer Growth:
</b></p>
<p>Analyze information and develop detailed requirements for tools and solutions needed to support Business Continuity Planning and Analysis.
</p>
<p>Monitor and manage BCM statistics and reporting.
</p>
<p><b>Financial Performance:
</b></p>
<p>Manage and monitor spend within given budget and optimize cost efficiencies.
</p>
<p>Make recommendations for cost efficiencies.
</p>
<p><b>Enhanced Service Delivery:
</b></p>
<p>Work in partnership with various departments, such as Technology Services and Operational units to develop and maintain currency of Business Continuity Plans and Maintenance Programs, ensuring synchronization with Disaster Recovery. Event/crisis management including gathering required information at time of event, activating call trees to engage appropriate individuals as required, tracking/monitoring/reporting on situation including minutes for conference bridges.
</p>
<p>Provide 24/7 on-call support when required.
</p>
<p><b>Leadership:
</b></p>
<p>Establish self as Business Continuity expert and ‘enabler’ to facilitate program development and engage with Vendors and Customers. Provide expert advice on the implications of changes or proposed changes of the Plans in place, coordinating changes as necessary. Provide training to all local managers and senior leaders for Business Continuity Planning.
</p>
<p>Provide BCP expertise on RFP proposals and during contract negotiations.
</p>
<p>Provide expertise on BCP program for third party vendors. Train staff and senior leaders in all sites.
</p>
<p><b>Risk Management:
</b></p>
<p>Oversee BCP compliance with contractual agreements (Customer and Vendor). Keep abreast of best practices in the Crisis Management and Business Continuity fields, including Pandemic planning. Mitigation and recovery strategy development.
</p>
<p>Coordination of regular testing of Business Continuity Plans.
</p>
