<h2>Advertising Sales Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>The Tyler Morning Telegraph is seeking an Advertising Sales Executive to sell print and digital advertising solutions to small and medium sized businesses in East Texas. The primary focus of this position is to develop new business sales through quality prospecting and relationship building while working with the client to determine the right mix of our products to create a solution that will bring them a solid return on their investment.</p>
<p>In this position, you will be responsible for:</p>
<ul>
<li>Creating persuasive sales presentations</li>
<li>Prospecting new businesses, appointment scheduling and building solid pipelines</li>
<li>Making a high volume of calls per day</li>
<li>Organizing and prioritizing daily activity</li>
</ul>
<p>Required qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>Previous media/internet/digital advertising sales a plus</li>
<li>Hunter mentality, strong closing skills and consultative sales approach</li>
<li>Proficient computer skills and strong understanding of the internet</li>
<li>Strong written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Valid drivers license and auto insurance, reliable transportation and cell phone</li>
</ul>
<p>This position pays base plus commission...how much you earn is up to you! We also offer a great benefits package, including medical, dental, vision, life and accident insurance, 401k retirement plan, paid holidays and a generous paid time off program.</p>
<p>Applicants must successfully complete a pre-employment drug screen and background check.</p>
