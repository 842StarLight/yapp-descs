<h2>Customer Success Ninja</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Job Summary</b><br><b>Intellectyx Inc</b> is a global software Services Company focused on #digital #transformation empowering business with innovative solutions. We develop innovative software solutions using web, mobile, social, business intelligence, artificial intelligence and analytics that traverse the organizations journey from data to decisions.</p>
<p>A global company established in 2008, Intellectyx is headquartered in Denver, USA with consultants working across the country onsite at client locations along with offshore development centers in Coimbatore,India.</p>
<p>We believe that when you put people at the center of digital experiences, you achieve better outcomes. Cool, calm and collected, your passion for technology is at the core of everything you do.<br>We are a diverse and inclusive workplace, and we believe people do their best work when they are in a supportive team. Our family-like company is rapidly growing, and we are looking for an outstanding Customer Success Ninja to join us.<br><b>Responsibilities and Duties</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Onboard clients by educating and setting expectations with clients on IX project scope, delivery commitments, client ownership, support interactions, communication flows, escalations, SLA, Privacy Breach etc.,</li>
<li>Creates, manages and sets , has a clear level of understanding on the high level scope for the project and takes total ownership of delivery across clients by working closely with PMs.</li>
<li>Pulls the right resources across all projects and ensures the delivery commitments are met with a long- term view of engaging clients, maintaining the relationship at the highest degree of satisfaction</li>
<li>First Level contact for any production, escalation, technical issues and bridges/facilitates interactions between client, client partners and internal teams for closure of issues along with RCA.</li>
<li>Responsible for ensuring that the client gets access to the right people within Intellectyx, and that they ultimately have their questions answered, issues resolved.</li>
<li>Demonstrate client commitment , show value and keep efforts coordinated by participating in regular meetings to stay connected with the customer.</li>
<li>Identify opportunities for adding more services that weren’t included in the original project’s scope – Upsell.</li>
<li>Total Cost Ownership of the resources utilized and ensuring each resource deputed is used at the highest level of velocity for a given customer – meaning high level management and working relation with all delivery resources - PMs, Delivery engineers and Test, Devops.</li>
<li>Manages P&amp;L of the projects and success of project delivery is measured by Margin, Delivery Quality, Slippage and Client Satisfaction.</li>
<li>Works closely with the HR and PM on identifying slack and fixing by working with the resources including direct conversations with resources on Behavioral, Cultural, Performance Plans etc.,</li>
<li>Possess the business acumen and people skills needed to work with clients at a strategic level, and to engage effectively Intellectyx resources across all levels of client.</li>
<li>Act as a client advocate and ensure that everyone is getting what they need to make the relationship a successful one.</li>
<li>Pursue a more strategic engagement, interviewing the client on initiatives (onsite as needed) and plans in the customer organization, and determining how Intellectyx can help the business accomplish its objectives.</li>
<li>Stay on top of renewals, billings and invoice payments</li>
<li>Uncover additional layers of understanding of the customer’s technical environment, and then continue to help the technical and support teams keep apprised of changes or intricacies</li>
<li>Primary Owner and takes accountability in terms of Go/No Go Release decisions.</li>
</ul>

<li>Exceptional communications skills, both verbal and written.</li>
<li>Social personality and genuine love of relationship-building.</li>

<li>Demonstrable track record of over-achieving quota.</li>
<li>Can-do attitude and the ability to think on your feet.</li>
<li>Ability to solve complex problems quickly, inventively and resourcefully.</li>
<li>Highly motivated and energetic team player, with excellent interpersonal skills.</li>
<li>Able to travel to client locations (25% travel)</li>
</ul>
