<b>Job Title:</b><b> </b>Lead Help Desk Support Specialist</div>
<div><b>Major Goals and Responsibilities:</b></div>
<div>This position provides computing support to customers of the company and staff. The position is responsible for problem determination, possible resolution, problem/incident recording, problem resolution, problem escalation for network or solutions related service requests with Lead role responsibilities of the Team.</div>
<div>Products and services supporting include but not limited to: networks, client/server systems, mainframe systems, PC and Macintosh desktops, desktop application software, intact, E-Copy, and Fleet View, Papercut and others.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Essential Activities:</b></div>
<div><b>General Administration/Help Desk Support (80%)
 </b></div>
<ul><li>Provide first-level contact and problem resolution for all users with hardware, software and applications problems.</li></ul>
 <ul><li>Resolve as many user-reported problems as expertise permits using available tools and following procedures and policies for the handling of support cases.</li></ul>
 <ul><li>Courteously obtain and convey concise problem information for external and internal customers.</li></ul>
 <ul><li>Provide accurate and timely logging of problems and resolution for problems in E-Automate.</li></ul>
 <ul><li>Escalate problems as appropriate following company procedures.</li></ul>
 <ul><li>Act as a liaison between customers and internal support staff to assure accurate problem interpretation.</li></ul>
 <ul><li>Maintain communications with customers during the problem resolution process. This includes connectivity metrics, policies, and procedures which provide a positive work environment.</li></ul>
 <ul><li>Take ownership for and make recommendations for improvements to connectivity support at ProSource.</li></ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Training and Quality Improvement (10%)
 </b></div>
<ul><li>Maintain in-depth knowledge of supported products and services.</li></ul>
 <ul><li>Work with the Software Solutions Specialist to identify available Help Desk training that will enhance and improve computing support delivered to customer.</li></ul>
 <ul><li>Perform coaching and mentoring for Connectivity Technicians as assigned.</li></ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Help Desk documentation, records and procedures (10%)</b></div>
 <ul><li>Review and update Help Desk documentation as assigned.</li></ul>
 <ul><li>Review and recommend modifications to procedures with Software Solutions Specialist and management.</li></ul>
 <ul><li>Gather and input data for regular reports distributed by Help Desk.</li></ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Behavioral Traits, Activities and Skills Required:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Assertiveness – </b>Provides current, direct, complete and actionable feedback to others. Deals with problems quickly, directly and is not afraid to take action when necessary.</li>
<li>
<b>Communication – </b>Conveys information accurately, efficiently, listens actively and incorporates the ideas of others, adapts written and oral style to the audience to enhance understanding. Appropriately maintains confidential information.</li>
<li>
<b>Decision Making – </b>Gathers necessary information, makes timely and good decisions by calling on varied resources and sound judgment.</li>
<li>
<b>Initiative – </b>Takes prompt action to accomplish objectives, goes beyond what is required and/or expected, acts on ways to improve existing conditions and processes, seeks to resolve problems without being asked.</li>
<li>
<b>Organization - </b>Effectively structures ideas, people or resources in ways that support the achievement of specific and broad objectives, works in an orderly, systematic fashion.</li>
<li>
<b>Problem Solving – </b>Analyzes interrelated elements of problems and works systematically to solve them, uses sound judgment to develop efficient and feasible resolutions to challenging issues.</li>
<li>
<b>Teamwork</b> – Acts as a valuable, collaborative team member by demonstrating willingness to support customers, sales and service groups. Develops cooperative relationships, exchanging ideas and preventing and resolving problems.</li>
<li>
<b>Training – </b>Further your knowledge to obtain an overall of all of our products offered today.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<b>Prerequisites (i.e… education, experience)</b><b> EDUCATION and EXPERIENCE</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>High School and Technical School graduate or equivalent experience.</li>
<li>10-15 years experience related to technical/repair of business equipment.</li>
<li>Ability to multitask service issues.</li>
<li>Excellent customer relations skills background.</li>
<li>Operate a motor vehicle and have a valid driver’s license at all times.</li>
</ul>
