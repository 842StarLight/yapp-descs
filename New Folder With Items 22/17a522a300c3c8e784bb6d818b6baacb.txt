<h2>Assistant Controller</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>
<b>Summary: </b>Maintains records of financial transactions by establishing accounts and posting transactions.</div>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities </b>include the following. Other duties may be assigned.</div>
<ul>
<li>Develops and maintains system to account for financial transactions while defining accounting policies and procedures in accordance with accounting standards.</li>
<li>Perform monthly close requirements, financial reporting, and assist in research/resolution of accounting issues for both US and foreign entities.</li>
<li>Prepares financial reports by collecting, analyzing, and summarizing account information and trends. Provides management with timely reviews of organization's financial status and progress in its various programs and activities.</li>
<li>Responsible for maintaining and processing all fixed assets in the fixed asset accounting system.</li>
<li>Complies with federal, state, and local requirements by studying requirements; enforcing adherence to requirements; filing reports; advising management on needed actions. Includes sales tax for various states, payroll, property tax, business licenses renewals/requirements, business and automobile insurance requirements, automobile tax and license requirements/renewals.</li>
<li>Prepares reports which summarize and forecast company business activity and financial position in areas of income, expenses, and earnings based on past, present, and expected operations.</li>
<li>Assist in the preparation of budgets, reviews budget proposals, and prepares necessary supporting documentation and justification.</li>
<li>Assist with annual audits for US and foreign entities.</li>
<li>Detail oriented and progressive thinking on process to better maintain records and perform daily tasks more efficiently.</li>
<li>Assist in the verification of financial metrics contained in strategic planning initiatives.</li>
<li>Perform other duties and special projects as assigned.</li>
<li>Contributes to team effort by accomplishing related results as needed.</li>
</ul>
