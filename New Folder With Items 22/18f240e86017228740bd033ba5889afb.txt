Position Description</div>
<div><div>
<div>
<p>Welcomes guests, seat them at tables or in lounge, and help ensure quality of facilities and guest service. Presents menus to guests and answers questions about menu items, making recommendations upon request. Is thoroughly knowledgeable in all aspects of the menu, ingredients, and prices.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b><b>:</b></p>
<p>NOTE: Traditions Spirits, Inc. ® may change the requirements of this job description at any time. These include, but are not limited to the following:</p>
<ul>
<li>Embodies Traditions Spirits Mission, Vision, and Core Values</li>
<li>Greet guests, escort them to their seats or waiting areas, and present them with menus and wine lists</li>
<li>Assign guests to tables suitable for their needs</li>
<li>Direct guests to coatrooms and waiting areas such as lounges</li>
<li>Ensure guests are seated promptly, be attentive at all times to guests seated in waiting areas or lounge</li>
<li>Inspect dining and serving areas to ensure cleanliness and proper setup</li>
<li>Explain how various menu items are prepared, describing ingredients and cooking methods</li>
<li>Maintain contact with kitchen staff, management, serving staff, and guests to ensure that dining details are handled properly and guests' concerns are addressed</li>
<li>Receive and record guests' dining reservations</li>
<li>Supervise and coordinate activities of dining room staff to ensure that guests receive prompt and courteous service</li>
<li>Speak with guests to ensure satisfaction with food and service, and to respond to complaints</li>
<li>Review work procedures and operational problems in order to determine ways to improve service, performance, and/or safety</li>
<li>Check with guests to ensure that they are enjoying their meals and take action to correct any problems</li>
<li>Explain how various menu items are prepared, describing ingredients and cooking methods</li>
<li>Inform guests of daily specials</li>
<li>Present menus to guests and answer questions about menu items, making recommendations upon request</li>
<li>Describe and recommend wines to guests</li>
<li>Serve food and/or beverages to guests; prepare and serve specialty dishes at tables as required</li>
<li>Prepare tables for meals, including setting up items such as linens, silverware, and glassware</li>
<li>Remove dishes and glasses from tables or counters, and take them to kitchen for cleaning</li>
<li>Stock service areas with supplies such as coffee, food, tableware, and linens, fill salt, pepper, sugar, cream, condiment, and napkin containers, etc.</li>
<li>Take orders from guests for food or beverages when necessary</li>
<li>Prepare checks that itemize and total meal costs and sales taxes</li>
<li>Present bills, accept payments, operate cash register</li>
<li>Prepare cash receipts after establishments close, and make bank deposits</li>
<li>Maintain personal health and sanitation standards (wash hands when using restroom, etc.)</li>
<li>Review work procedures and operational problems in order to determine ways to improve service, performance, and/or safety</li>
<br>
</ul>
