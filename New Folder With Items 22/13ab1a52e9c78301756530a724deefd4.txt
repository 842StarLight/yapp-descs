<h2>Account Executive (Washington Metro Area)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Unanet is seeking experienced, enthusiastic and motivated individuals to fill the position of Account Executive to cover the DC Metro Area. The Account Executive will be responsible for identifying opportunities for Unanets software solution, working with accounts to demonstrate how Unanet will deliver upon their expected outcomes, and closing new customer accounts. The successful candidate will have good business discipline and strong sales skills.</p>
<p>
As Account Executive, you will be responsible for the full sales cycle:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Cultivate the pipeline by acting on field marketing leads and conducting numerous outbound calls daily</li>
<li>
Attend industry networking events and trade shows to represent Unanet in the community and to generate leads</li>
<li>
Respond to inbound leads in assigned territory</li>
<li>
Understand and articulate the breath of Unanets product portfolio and demonstrate the software to qualified prospects</li>
<li>
Drive results and measure success by meeting or exceeding sales goals</li>
<li>
Participate in sales program training sessions, meetings, skill building and professional development classes</li>
<li>
Capture accurate and complete information in Customer Relationship Management CRM system</li>
<li>
Cooperate with sales management to determine strategic approaches for sales</li>
<li>
Maintain and expand the database of prospects for the organization</li>
</ul>
<p>
This full-time, exempt position reports to the VP, Business Development. Applicants must be authorized for non-sponsored employment in the US.</p>
<p><b>
You have many of the following:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor's degree from an accredited college/university</li>
<li>
3-4 years of enterprise software sales experience using solution selling approach</li>
<li>
Consistent track record of overachieving sales quotas</li>
<li>
Project management and/or accounting experience and understanding of project management and/or accounting software solutions</li>
<li>
Specific experience using and/or selling commercial software for commercial or government contracting firms</li>
<li>
Knowledge and understanding of government contracting companies, consulting organizations, life sciences, internal IT organizations, non-profits and/or advanced technology organizations</li>
<li>
Superior verbal and written communication and presentation skills</li>
<li>
Exceptional organizational and time-management skills, able to multi-task; detail-oriented</li>
<li>
Professional presence</li>
<li>
Proficiency in Excel, Word, PowerPoint and CRM</li>
<li>
High-level of initiative with strong work ethic</li>
<li>
Strong computer skills</li>
<li>
Ability to work in a team</li>
<li>
Knowledge of Salesforce.com preferred</li>
<li>
Prior experience with the Unanet product is desirable</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Cultural and Position Fit " You are:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Extraordinary at building authentic, long-term relationships while also outgoing and effective at networking to meet new prospects and evangelize the Unanet message</li>
<li>
Motivated and driven by the success of the team</li>
<li>
Comfortable multi-tasking and filling multiple roles, as is typical at a growing, entrepreneurial business</li>
<li>
Consistently achieving and growing professionally, as demonstrated by past results</li>
<li>
Consistently recognized for your positive outlook and approach to solving problems collaboratively</li>
<li>
Able to see the forest and the trees and can think both strategically and tactically to meet objectives</li>
<li>
Excellent at presenting information both virtually and in person</li>
</ul>
