<h2>Senior Administrator - Central Marketing</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>OVERVIEW
</p>
<div><div><div>
<div>The <b>Sr. Administrator - Central Marketing</b> is responsible for assisting in the planning, development and execution of TDS’s strategic and tactical marketing initiatives for acquiring profitable customers and driving demand to meet gross add and revenue targets. They will manage projects of medium scope and complexity.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
This role helps develop and oversee advertising, lead generation and promotional campaigns across TDS markets using traditional and non-traditional marketing tactics for new product/new market rollouts. This includes submitting job requests, list development and creative proofing.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
This position will organize and coordinate mailing schedules for pre-printed direct mail based on targeted launch dates and communicate changes to impacted teams. They will execute and manage lead nurturing campaigns to optimize customers experience and increase close rates.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
This role will be a key resource for the team in monitoring tactical success and reporting on such initiatives. They will serve as a supporting role to the marketing organization.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
This position is expected to maintain and understand TDS market areas along with product availability and competitive threats. This role will work with all functional areas such as marketing, sales and product management to help facilitate sales and access market opportunities</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
TDS Telecommunications LLC (TDS Telecom/TDS®) delivers high-speed internet, TV entertainment, and phone services to nearly 900 rural, suburban, and metropolitan communities across the U.S. With more than 1.2 million connections, TDS is one of the fastest growing technology companies in the United States. Powered by fiber-optics and new industry-leading technologies, TDS delivers up to 1 Gigabit internet speeds and offers internet-protocol based TV entertainment solutions along with traditional phone services. TDS also offers businesses VoIP advanced communications solutions, dedicated internet service, data networking, and hosted-managed services. Visit tdstelecom.com.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
TDS Telecom, headquartered in Madison, Wis., employs nearly 2,700 people and is a subsidiary of Telephone and Data Systems, Inc. [NYSE: TDS], a Fortune 1000® company. Founded in 1969, TDS Inc. employs 9,400 people and has approximately six million connections nationwide through its businesses U.S. Cellular, TDS Telecom, OneNeck IT Solutions LLC, and TDS Broadband Service. TDS Inc. has been named to several Forbes lists, including America's Best Employers for Diversity, Best Large Employers, and Best Employers for Women. Visit tdsinc.com.</div>
</div></div></div>
<p>RESPONSIBILITIES
</p>
<div><div><div>
<div>Provide support for marketing group:</div>
<ul>
<li>
Schedule and manage creation of marketing materials</li>
<li>
Oversee writing, design and production of materials</li>
<li>
Research and respond to customer inquiries</li>
<li>
Event coordination support</li>
<li>
Coordinate or prepare targeted mailing/calling lists</li>
<li>
Monitor marketing reports</li>
</ul>
<div>
Assist with the development of customer acquisition, retention and growth strategies. This includes new marketing campaigns, product promotions, product rollouts, and sales channel development to achieve growth targets.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Manage and coordinate pre-printed direct mail campaigns based on projected launch dates and communicate changes to impacted teams.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Execute and manage lead nurturing email campaigns to optimize customers experience and increase close rates.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Interact with sales and marketing leaders to help create, manage and implement marketing and sales tactical plans.</div>
</div></div></div>

</p>
