<h2>IT Analyst, DTC Operations</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div><div><div>
<div><div><div>
<p>Reporting in to the Manager, IT Operations, the IT Analyst, DTC Operations has responsibility for triaging and supporting the day-to-day operations of the Cole Haan website and associated omnichannel infrastructure as well as Cole Haan's Wholesale programs. This individual will work in partnership with the IT Operations Manager and various IT and business entities to maintain a continual focus on quality customer experience and successful engagement with the Cole Haan brand. This position will assist the IT Operations Manager with various tasks managing the daily IT operations, systems availability, incident troubleshooting, vendor management, and in driving Operational KPIs. This is a highly visible and user facing role, requiring leadership and partnership with many IT and Business functional areas.</p>
<p><b>
CORE ACCOUNTABILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for IT operations for customer-facing technology (DTC); managing application, computing, and incident management support functions ensuring availability of key systems 24 hours a day 7 days a week</li>
<li>Primary operations point of contact for Direct to Consumer (DTC) business and technology partners</li>
<li>Monitors key jobs and file feeds, investigates and researches discrepancies and disruptions with the flow of data/file feeds between Cole Haan and DTC partners</li>
<li>Responsible for triage and troubleshooting for DTC issues that are not able to be handled via SOP. Responsible for advocating for long-term remediation of chronic issues.</li>
<li>Performs support readiness assessments prior to DTC projects turnover to production</li>
<li>Commitment to collaborate with developers and subject matter experts to ensuring routine maintenance and technical upgrades are executed</li>
<li>Primary operational point of contact for strategic outsourcing partner Capgemini</li>
<li>Responsible for Cole Haan Service Delivery model</li>
<li>Responsible for Off-Shore Service Desk (Level 1) in support of infrastructure, network, security, Wintel team, SAP and BW teams</li>
<li>Vendor Management - service level attainment tracking &amp; reporting as a component of overall governance reporting and IT metrics</li>
<li>Manage appropriate capacity of the services to meet resource supply and demand specific projects .</li>
<li>Participates in the assessment of external and internal technology capabilities with a focus on suitability for integration with the existing IT operations environment</li>
<li>Contribute to process improvement by constantly refining service delivery model based on advancing technology</li>
<li>Direct efforts to create and enhance standards/best practices and drive adoption across the technical team</li>
<li>Convert business requirements into technology requirements.</li>
<li>Maintaining operations by utilizing a combination of internal resources as well as external vendors</li>
<li>Maintain committed service levels within defined budget</li>
<li>Responsible for ongoing continuous service improvement through coordination and communication between support and business functions.</li>
<li>Responsible for overall call tracking tool and hierarchy in a distributed environment</li>
<li>Developing the service strategy for IT Operations services, with annual updates and an updated technology plan in conjunction with Enterprise Architecture</li>
<li>Understand IT service cost drivers and connect cost of servic</li>
<li>Play key role in day to day operations and delivery of Cole Haan's IT services with focus on the Digital direct to consumer systems.</li>
<li>Lead incident management and escalation with both internal and outside partners.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<div>
<div>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<div><ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree or higher in Information Technology, Computer Science, or Engineering</li>
<li>5+ years of IT experience</li>
<li>5+ years vendor management experienc</li>
<li>Self- starter able to work independently under tight deadlines</li>
<li>5+ years technically supporting retail and e-commerce solutions. This should include the processes and systems as it relates to the following areas;</li>
<li>E-Commerce (Sales Force Commerce Cloud preferable)</li>
<ul>
<li>Merchandising and Pricing on E-Commerce sites</li>
<li>Mobile Technology</li>
<li>POS (Aptos preferably)</li>
<li>OMS (Aptos preferred)</li>
<li>CRM (related to both the Digital, Brick and Mortar and Corporate environments)</li>
</ul>
<li>Experience managing infrastructure in a DTC environment preferred.</li>
<li>Experience troubleshooting data I/O and data requirements connecting DTC systems.</li>
<li>ITIL Certification or equivalent experience</li>
<li>PCI Experience</li>
<li>PMP Certification or equivalent experience</li>
<li>Experience with COBIT framework a plus</li>
<li>POS Support and Accreditation Certification</li>
<li>Experience with IT service management, as well as emerging methods, such as DevOps, that are optimized for agility</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills, including the ability to explain technical concepts and technologies to senior leaders, and business concepts</li>
<li>Experience with IT operations teams in complex and dynamic environments requiring 24/7 support</li>
</ul></div>
