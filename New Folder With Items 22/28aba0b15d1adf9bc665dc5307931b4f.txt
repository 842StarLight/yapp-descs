Summary of Duties: Partner with customers, functional business analysts and other team members in driving appropriate design and implementation of technical solutions to support current or future Oracle ERP integrations. Responsible for building integration solutions with Oracle ERP and third party solutions using Oracle SOA technologies, work with ERP technical developers to provide end-to-end support. Research, design, document, and modify software specifications throughout the production lifecycle in accordance with the business goals and organizational architectural standards. Collaborate with business analysts, business users and external systems integrators in conceptualization and development of new technical objects to support current or future Oracle ERP integrations. Perform software development lifecycle activities. Evaluate requirements from functional specifications and create efficient technical integration designs. Analyze, evaluate and support impact of Oracle release upgrades in existing integrations. Adhere to and support the use of IS standards for code development and code promotion. Develop complex technical objects in accordance with the technical development standards and best practices. Mitigate significant risks associated with integration projects, which have a high technical complexity and/or involve significant challenges to the business. Create and resolve Service Requests in collaboration with Oracle Support or third party vendor integrations. Provide generalized production technical support. Gather business requirements, recommend industry best practices, and work with functional knowledge holders. Design, develop, deploy, test and maintain technical objects that exist in Oracle SOA production environment, e.g., web services, system designs and integrations. Design and develop ESB/BPEL processes (Synchronous and Asynchronous). Design and construction of web services. Create process flows, high level functional and detailed technical design specifications from business requirements. Provide customer support for business critical application problems and issues. Proactively work through service requests with vendor support as needed. Coordinate work efforts with the DBA and infrastructure teams for application change controls and system performance issues. Partner with business analysts and application super users to successfully complete integration projects. 25% international and domestic travel required.<br>
<br>
Qualifications: Bachelor's in Computer Science, Information Technology, Engineering, or a related field and five (5) years’ experience in the offered role or as a Programmer Analyst, Oracle Developer, or a related role. Experience must include five (5) years with the following: Building java applications and system integrations; Integration experience with any Enterprise Service Bus; BPEL; J2EE based development; SoapUI or similar web service testing tools; SOA principles, design patterns, application integration architectures; Building, configuring and deploying services on containers such as web logic and other application servers; and Define and maintain a common interface schema or canonical models.<div></div>
