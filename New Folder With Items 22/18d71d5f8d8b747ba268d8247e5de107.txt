<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Description</b></h1>
<p>Title: Commercial Sales Intern</p>
<p>Job Type: Internship</p>
<p>Location: Wayne-PA</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Opportunity:</b></p>
<p>The Commercial Sales Intern will work directly with sales leadership. This position will work on a variety of sales and marketing tasks in support of Sungard AS outreach programs. The Intern will assist the Business Development team with market research and ensuring the satisfaction of our customers. This position is a temporary assignment. Hours are flexible.
</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Assist and maintain the Customer Relationship Management (CRM) system’s data cleansing.
</li>
<li>Pull customer/company lists for data cleansing in support of outreach programs across North America.
</li>
<li>Conduct market research of companies and customer data by pulling information from the DiscoverOrg software program.
</li>
<li>Build email templates to send information to prospective clients.
</li>
<li>Design sales presentations for delivery to the Business Development team.
</li>
<li>Qualify prospective customer calls for sales leads.</li>
<li>Call current customers to inquire about their satisfaction with their Sungard AS services.</li>
<li>Lead a weekly sales team meeting.</li>
<li>Present on relevant industry topics during weekly team meetings.</li>
<li>Engage in weekly meetings with Sales Managers, Account Executives, and Business Development Representatives to discuss the Sungard AS value propositions and industry topics.
</li>
<li>Create and deliver a role play scenario to the Commercial Sales/Business Development Leadership team.
</li>
<li>Participate in various projects and other responsibilities as assigned.
</li>
<li>Work supportively with colleagues, operating in a collegiate manner at all times and in line with the Company’s values.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>December 2019/2020 degree graduates in Business or Marketing.</li>
<li>Proficient in the Microsoft Office (Word, Outlook, Excel, PowerPoint.</li>
<li>Self-motivated team player able to work in a fast-paced environment.</li>
<li>Excellent oral and written communication skills, with an aptitude for communicating effectively across multiple geographies.
</li>
<li>Must be able to learn quickly in a highly technical environment.</li>
<li>Desire to work in a global workplace.</li>
</ul>

</ul>
