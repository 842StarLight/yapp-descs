A DAY IN THE LIFE OF A SALES PROFESSIONAL</b></p>
<p>
As a Sales Professional, you will be <b>identifying prospects</b>, leading a team of Drain Technicians, making <b>presentations</b>, and <b>counseling clients</b> on their plumbing needs. From start to finish, you will take our customers through all sales steps. This includes initial interactions to final contract signatures as well as follow-up satisfaction calls. Often times you will be working with clients that have issues with their plumbing, so you will be ready to work with your hands to help solve their problems.</p>
<p>

However, this job is not always easy, so you will take charge of challenging opportunities as they arise and provide exceptional customer service. To our clients, you will be the expert as you review and guide them to the best products and services. Your <b>strategic proposals</b>, job contracts, and documentation will be <b>well-organized</b>, and you will find great <b>fulfillment meeting or exceeding sales quotas</b> and finding <b>financial success</b>!</p>
