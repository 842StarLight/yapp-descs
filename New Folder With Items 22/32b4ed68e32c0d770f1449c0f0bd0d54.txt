<p><b>Position Summary:</b></p>
<p>The Ecommerce Development Manager will be responsible for a four person Ecommerce development/QA team reporting directly to the CIO of the company.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Duties:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ensure “consumer-first” approach to developing the website, through smooth UX.</li>
<li>Both manage engineering staff and directly develop in Magento/PHP including requirements analysis, design, coding, testing, and support</li>
<li>Develop custom interfaces between the Magento ecommerce site and third-party applications as required</li>
<li>Ensure that all related documentation (business requirements, technical designs, etc.) are created, maintained and available in a central repository.</li>
<li>Coordinate cross-functionally with other departmental managers and Subject Matter Experts (SMEs) in Accounting / Finance, Marketing, Sales, Supply Chain, and Manufacturing.</li>
<li>Support corporate objectives and global growth strategies</li>
<li>Provides recommendations for improvements</li>
<li>Responsible for escalating support issues to third-parties, as needed</li>
<li>Provide regular project status reports</li>
<li>Predict project risk factors and address proactively</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<div></div>
<div><div><div>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Must have 5+ years of Ecommerce development experience</li>
<li>Must be proficient with a Magento 2.0 eCommerce platform</li>
<li>PHP, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Linux and SQL skills</li>
<li>Understanding of React and/or other client-side JavaScript MVC or View-Model frameworks</li>
<li>Experience in working in an Agile development environment</li>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in Information Technology or related field</li>
<li>Experience in hiring and supervising an ecommerce development staff</li>
<li>Excellent planning and project management skills</li>
<li>Self-starter with a high level of initiative and strong sense of ownership</li>
<li>Strong communication and interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Experience working in the CPG industry preferred</li>
<li>Experience with both B2C and B2B business models preferred</li>
<br>
</ul>
