<h2>Convenience and Fuel Retail Sales Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>About NCR
</b></p>
<p>NCR Corporation (NYSE: NCR) is a leading software- and services-led enterprise provider in the financial, retail, hospitality, telecom and technology industries. NCR is headquartered in Atlanta, Ga., with 34,000 employees and does business in 180 countries. NCR is a trademark of NCR Corporation in the United States and other countries.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>TITLE <b>: Convenience and Fuel Retail Sales Specialist
</b></p>
<p><b>Location: Texas (Virtual)
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>POSTION SUMMARY &amp; KEY AREAS OF RESPONSIBILITY
</b></p>
<p>Are you a software sales leader with the agility to thrive in a fast changing environment and the creativity to enable a sales team to reach and surpass tough stretch goals?
</p>
<p></p>
<p>We’re seeking a versatile leader with experience growing and leading Solutions Sales teams in the Convenience and Fuel Retail (CFR) industry. The primary objective of this position is to lead and drive software sales within North America.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>As a member of the CFR Specialist Sales team, your ability to participate in defining the strategy of a growing business will be critical for the team’s success. The ability to deal in ambiguity and a fast changing environment is table stakes for this role. Your leadership will help drive the software sales behavior of NCR’s direct quota carrying sales force, ensuring that sales force employs the most effective sales tactics and tracking to exceed the software objectives as defined by NCR Retail.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>If you have demonstrated experience in creatively driving customer solutions while delivering exceptional customer service, we want to talk to you.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Key Areas of Responsibility include:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Drive software sales results and behavior with the North American sales teams
</li>
<li>Lead a robust sales management system focused on funnel, orders, backlog, revenue, gross margin, and expense and profit contribution
</li>
<li>Create, communicate and inspire a vision for the business to customers and to the team
</li>
<li>Build strong relationships with account executives, key customer accounts and channel partners
</li>
<li>Negotiate multi-year, multi-solution software and services contracts
</li>
<li>Work with senior sales leadership to develop region/area strategy
</li>
<li>Operationalize and execute against NCR’s strategy to further extend the company's position and profile in the marketplace
</li>
<li>Create market potential and opportunities for NCR Software solutions and selected Solution Partners, within NCR core and new industries; work with Inside Sales to create campaigns
</li>
<li>Inspire, influence and lead as necessary to drive commercial success
</li>
<li>Plan and direct all sales activities including planning and implementing forecasts, providing executive updates and developing policies;
</li>
<li>Foster and lead change, and create a mindset of innovation
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>BASIC QUALIFICATIONS
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree or equivalent in a related discipline
</li>
<li>10 years + of related business and sales management experience along with a proven track record of success
</li>
<li>In-depth experience and understanding of the convenience and fuel retail market and related competition
</li>
<li>Background in SaaS and Enterprise Software sales
</li>
<li>Manage the sales process and develop a strategic roadmap which aligns customer needs, issues, opportunities with industry best practices and our company’s offerings.
</li>
<li>Proven track record in sales leadership
</li>
<li>Collaborate with customers and partners enabling them to experience greater business value from our company.
</li>
<li>Experience in developing and executing solution sales strategies.
</li>
<li>Strong communication and interpersonal skills, including establishing credibility and trust with partners and building influential relationships with the stakeholders, including Finance, Legal, Sales Ops, HR, Support Services, Marketing and Professional Services
</li>
<li>Ability to work and manage in a matrix management environment
</li>
<li>Lead, coach, influence and develop sales presentations and demonstrations for relevnt solutions
</li>
<li>Reports directly to the North Americas’ Software Sales Leader
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Strong Executive Presence – this role will have extensive executive visibility both internally and externally
</li>
<li>Leadership of a sales organization inclusive of complex solutions (HW/SW/Services)
</li>
<li>Project management skills; Client and Business plan management proficiency
</li>
</ul>
