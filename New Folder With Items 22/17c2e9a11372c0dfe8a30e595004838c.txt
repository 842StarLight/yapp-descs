<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p><b>CHA Hollywood Presbyterian Medical Center</b></p>
<p>
CHA Hollywood Presbyterian Medical Center (HPMC) is an acute care facility that has been caring for the Hollywood community and surrounding areas since 1924. The hospital is committed to serving local multicultural communities with quality medical and nursing care. With more than 500 physicians representing virtually every specialty, HPMC strives to distinguish itself as a leading healthcare provider, recognized for providing quality, innovative care in a compassionate manner.</p>
<p>
HPMC is part of a global healthcare enterprise which owns and operates general hospitals throughout Korea, numerous fertility and research centers in the U.S. and Korea including CHA Fertility Center, a medical university, and CHAUM (a premier anti-aging life center).</p>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Professional Requirements
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Position Summary:</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Position is primarily responsible for the maintenance of records of financial transactions and month-end processes in accordance with Generally Accepted Accounting Principles.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Position Accountabilities:</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Balance Sheet Account Analysis-and providing proposed adjustments as well as other Income Statement Account Analysis: Building Rentals/Leases.</li>
<li>
Timely completion of all Bank Reconciliation-variances are researched and identified.</li>
<li>
Maintenance and organization of Month-End Financial Reports.</li>
<li>
Timely distribution of Monthly Departmental Financial Reports.</li>
<li>
Distribution of Payroll Checks.</li>
<li>
Assist in preparation of financial statements in accordance with Generally Accepted Accounting Principles-Journal Entries and Statistical information.</li>
<li>
Verify intercompany/intrafacility accounts are balanced at month-end.</li>
<li>
Maintain information required for Medicare Year-End Cost Report information-Directorship Logs, Association Fees, Miscellaneous Revenues and Supply Issuances quarterly.</li>
<li>
Maintains information required for Annual Tax Package.</li>
<li>
Prepare Quarterly Reports: OSHPD and Sales Tax</li>
<li>
Provide analysis of miscellaneous accounts during the budgetary process as required.</li>
<li>
Perform Physical Inventory Audits and Summary of Audit Adjustments.</li>
<li>
Capital Project Maintenance in accordance with the Tenet’s Standards in processing CERs.</li>
<li>
Preparation of invoices for collection of non-patient receivables.</li>
<li>
Timely completion of Corporate’s volume schedule-ensuring accuracy of information.</li>
<li>
Participate in coordinating required information by outside/inside auditors.
</li>
<li>Complete other assignments and special projects as assigned by the Controller/CFO.
</li>
<li>Support the attainment of the goals of the Hospital and the Department by fulfilling annual employee requirements: reorientation, ethics, and health screenings.</li>
<li>
Meet the Standard of a positive Guest Relations Program of the Hospital demonstrated by positive attitude, professionalism and abiding with Tenet’s Code of Ethics.</li>
<li>
All other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
