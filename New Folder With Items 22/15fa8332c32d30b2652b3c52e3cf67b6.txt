<h2>Warehouse Associate (Part-Time)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>INTERMOUNTAIN FARMERS ASSOCIATION</b> IFA is a regional farm supply co-op with diverse operations including feed production, agronomy services, and retail stores throughout the Intermountain West. We are currently seeking a qualified individual to fill the position of <b>WAREHOUSE ASSOCIATE</b> at the IFA Country Store in Tremonton, Utah.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Must demonstrate basic material handling/warehouse equipment skills.</li>
<li>
Fill customers' orders for bagged feed, fertilizer and supplies accurately, carefully and timely into their vehicles.</li>
<li>
Unload freight trucks and stock products onto shelves.</li>
<li>
Help at the sales counter as a backup sales clerk.</li>
<li>
Calculate figures and amounts using basic math skills.</li>
<li>
Maintain a clean and organized work area.</li>
<li>
Able to use computer and company software.</li>
<li>
Work and communicate well with co-workers.</li>
<li>
Treat co-workers with respect.</li>
<li>
Perform other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
QUALIFICATIONS:</b> High School diploma, GED, or equivalent. Prior warehouse experience highly desired. Able to stand for extended periods of time during shift. Able to operate a sit-down forklift and hand carts in a safe manner. Able to lift/palletize various products weighing 50 pounds or more repeatedly. Must be age 18 or older to operate the forklift. Must be willing to work some Saturdays.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
BENEFITS:</b><b> </b>Competitive compensation, medical &amp; dental insurance, vision reimbursement, generous 401K matching, company-paid life &amp; long-term disability insurance, vacation &amp; sick time, holiday pay and employee purchase discounts. IFA is also closed Sundays, and has a family friendly work environment along with other incredible benefits.</p>
