<p><b>About the Company</b></p>
<p>
Founded in 1997 C&amp;B offers high volume construction of commercial and specialty mobile and modular buildings manufactured in completely enclosed facilities. We build our products on outrigger and steel perimeter frames with wheels and axles. We offer true modular wood framed floor systems and have our own fleet of modular carriers. We convert cargo shipping containers for a variety of uses. In addition we build steel skid type buildings. We also specialize in multiple unit configurations and multi-story designs.</p>
<p>
C&amp;B Custom Modular boasts 105,000 square feet of manufacturing space nestled on a 25 acre site just south of Bristol, IN. We are located in a premier industrial park situated right in the heart of Elkhart County which is rich with suppliers and partners for the modular business industries. C&amp;B has engineered and built thousands of modular structures and distributes throughout most geographic areas in the USA and Canada.</p>
<p>
C&amp;B performs all the engineering services with their own in-house engineering staff. We are capable of providing all the design specifications and drawings necessary to produce job specific engineering packages for the various states and countries in which we currently do business.</p>
<p><b>
About the Position</b></p>
<p>
C &amp; B Custom Modular has an immediate opening for a CAD Technician at our Bristol, IN office. Ideal candidate will have a minimum of 1-2 years' professional experience in the Modular industry is highly preferred, strong knowledge of commercial modular building codes (CSA/USA), structural calculations, plan submittal, review and approval process is required as well as proficiency with CAD software including modified and new plans. Communicate with R &amp; D, sales, production and quality control. This is an excellent opportunity to join a leading, growing organization.</p>
<p><b>
Duties/Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Create prints using AutoCad drawing software for layout diagrams, schematics and production documents.</li>
<li>
Prepare and maintain plans for new structures and processes.</li>
<li>
Communicate and collaborate with production team regarding print related items.</li>
<li>
Communicate and collaborate with our sales department.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Skills, Knowledge, and Abilities Required:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
High attention to detail.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of general construction standards and related codes.</li>
<li>
Ability to check over work to ensure accuracy and thoroughness of prints.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of standard computer techniques, CAD schematic design.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Education and Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
High school diploma or GED. Drafting degree or equivalent preferred.</li>
<li>
Proficiency in Microsoft Office.</li>
<li>
Experience with and general knowledge of constructions standards.</li>
<li>
1-2 or more years of experience using CAD systems.</li>
</ul>
