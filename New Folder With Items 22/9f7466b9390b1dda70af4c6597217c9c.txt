<h2>Asst Community Mgr</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>Summary of Position:<br>
Assist the Community Manager in the general administration of the property and perform all related functions. Responsibilities will include the areas of management, marketing and leasing, resident retention, and accounting.<br>
<br>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities:<br>
<br>
Management<br>
<br>
<ul><li>Assist the Community Manager and be prepared to act as the staff person in-charge in the event of the absence of the Community Manager in enforcing and adhering to all NPV policies</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Motivation and supervision of employees</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Inspecting the property, vacant and occupied units, and rent ready units on a daily basis; assist in the administration of make ready units and update of availability</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Work with attorney and Community Manager regarding legal proceedings, including evictions and collections</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Maintain inventories for the property, including all hospitality and office supplies (e.g., continental breakfast, brunch supplies, if applicable).</li></ul>
<br>
Marketing and Leasing<br>
<br>
1. Advertising and marketing, including outside marketing<br>
<br>
2. Showing and leasing apartments, processing applications and calling on lease renewals<br>
<br>
3. Preparation of Comparable Market Survey, intimate knowledge of the property, services and the general market area<br>
<br>
4. Maintain courteous communication with residents, applicants, and representatives of other companies. Represent the property and the company with local municipal and trade associations, including apartment associations and Chamber of Commerce<br>
<br>
5. Work closely with manager in the planning of resident functions, creating monthly newsletter and move-in packets<br>
<br>
Accounting and Administration<br>
<br>
1. Assisting with all financial matters by ensuring that financial transactions are within policies and procedures, including bank deposits, collections of delinquent rents and preparation of notices to residents<br>
<br>
2. Working within budgetary limits, under the direct supervision of the property manager<br>
<br>
3. All secretarial and administrative functions, including posting all receipts and preparing all back-up records. Responsible for tracking and reporting all daily activity of property, including move-ins, move-outs, rent billings, late notices, cancellations, work orders and final billings<br>
<br>
4. Prepare and maintain all office management files. Assist in preparing all required reports for supervisors, as well as all required reports for the maximization of on-site management<br>
<br>
5. Assist manager in processing of property P.O.s or input into computer and submission to corporate office for payment<br>
<br>
6. Other duties as required and determined by Community Manager<br>
<br>
Qualifications:<br>
<br>
<ul><li>High school education, CAM or similar designation</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Computer proficiency in Microsoft Windows and Microsoft Office (Word &amp; Excel); basic typing skills</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Experience and knowledge of bookkeeping functions</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Sales ability</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Professional appearance</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Pleasant telephone voice and excellent telephone techniques</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Ability to competently handle resident issues</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Able to perform leadership functions in the absence of the Community Manager</li></ul>
</div></div>
