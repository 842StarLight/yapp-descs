<h2>Technical Support Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Clear-Com, an HME company, is a trusted global provider of professional real-time communication solutions and services since 1968. We innovate market-proven technologies that connect teams together through wired and wireless systems. Clear-Com products facilitate the seamless coordination for all of the markets we serve – broadcast, live performance, live events, sports, military, aerospace, and government.
</p>
<p>Come be a part of the next chapter in the Clear-Com story.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
We are currently recruiting for a <b>Technical Support Associate.</b> In this role you will provide technical support and troubleshooting to end users, sales team, inside sales and 3rd party sales channels.</p>
<br>
<p>
<b>What you will do in this position:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Provides technical telephone support to resolve technical product issues.</li>
<li>Attempts to resolve problems with guidance of Technical Support Engineers, but quickly identify when escalation is necessary.</li>
<li>Maintains basic knowledge of RF and impact on Clear-Com wireless products.</li>
<li>Provides Assistance to the Applications Engineering team as required.</li>
<li>Provides technical support to Sales. As requested, provides product technical support and other product information to Clear-Com's in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Maintains basic understanding of Clear-Com’s product functionality as new hardware and software are released. Applies root cause analysis and standard troubleshooting techniques to resolve technical issues.</li>
<li>Records detailed information from the customer that will allow Technical Support Engineers, Product Management, or Engineering to understand and duplicate the reported problem.</li>
<li>Regularly contributes to the Company’s knowledgebase, enabling customers to find answers to problems on their own.</li>
<li>Provides administrative assistance to team by ensuring the CRM is maintained with current information, records are as complete as feasible, and other general database maintenance is performed as directed by GSS Management.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>What you need to succeed in this position:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Understanding of customer requirements within live performance and broadcast markets.</li>
<li>Technical audio expertise.</li>
<li>Strong analytical skills. Logical problem solver, able to apply standard trouble shooting and root cause analysis techniques, detail oriented, well-organized, coordinated and methodical approach to work.</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal and communication skills, able to resolve customer issues in a calm and professional manner.</li>
<li>Ability to create and decipher basic technical documentation.</li>
<li>Prior hands-on experience with audio equipment a plus.</li>
<li>Attitude, energy, and demonstrable success in a customer-focused role.</li>
<li>Good PC skills and experience with Office applications (Outlook, Excel, Word, PowerPoint, etc).</li>
<li>1+ years of related experience.</li>
<li>High School Diploma - Required</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p>HME is committed to building a company where highly talented, challenge-hungry people thrive. You will have the opportunity to learn and grow, while making a real contribution to our product. Our benefits package includes tuition reimbursement; 3 weeks paid vacation your first year, paid holidays, medical, vision and dental coverage, life insurance and 401K contributions.</p>
