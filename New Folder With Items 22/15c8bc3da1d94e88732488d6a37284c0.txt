Job Summary: The Marketing Operations Test Analyst I is responsible for supporting the customer data analytics team by providing campaign support, data validation and implementing/maintaining QA workflows. Additionally, the Marketing Operations Test Analyst will be a part of the broader customer data analytics team and play a key role in subscriber/channel data Integrity and campaign automation &amp; testing.<br>
<p></p>
Essential Functions / Process Responsibilities include the following; other duties may be assigned as necessary<br>
<br>
<ul><li>Perform pre-campaign analytics using primary and secondary data collection methods to determine optimal target and offer selection to achieve strategic objectives</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Analyze interim and post campaign analytics to measure overall performance and evaluate changes in customer behavior to recommend modifications to drive improved campaign ROI</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Provide customer insights and in depth analytics on targeted marketing campaigns that are launched across multiple channels</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Assist in project groups to answer emerging organization questions requiring in-depth customer analytics</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Provide ad hoc analysis as needed upon request</li></ul>
<br>
