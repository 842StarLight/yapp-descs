<h2>Director of Corporate Innovation Services, WeWork Labs</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>WeWork Labs</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Launched in early 2018, WeWork Labs is WeWork’s innovation platform that supports early-stage startups and corporations seeking to transform their industries. With a range of offerings, WeWork Labs helps growing startups and bridges the gap to enterprise organizations through our innovation services and programs.</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
The Role:</b></p>
<p><b>
WeWork Labs is looking for an innovation leader to help build and scale our Labs Innovation Services offering for enterprise organizations. The ideal candidate should have client-facing experience in innovation consulting, hands-on experience creating internal innovation programs at enterprise organizations, and a global lens for building and scaling teams and products.</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
You’ll succeed in this role if:</b><b> you have a successful track record for building innovation platforms, both internally and externally, you understand how to create platforms that scale on a global level, and you are comfortable with ambiguity and a culture of constant growth.</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p><b>
Lead the development and design of new products, services and platforms through internal and client discovery</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
Create a scalable, consistent execution and delivery processes to support our global clients</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
Create marketing decks and sales collateral with an eye for the Labs and WeWork brand</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
Drive conversations with potential clients to understand the business priorities</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
Partner with the Innovation Services BD team to </b><b>effectively scope project proposals, sign new clients, and ensure successful results</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
Build and maintain a strong internal and external network in service of building a portfolio of work that supports the strategic priorities of the business</b></p></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p><b>
8-10 years experience </b><b>working in an innovation role – at an innovation consulting firm, a design firm and/or within a corporate innovation team</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
Strong interpersonal and presentation skills with exceptional verbal and written communication skills</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
Passionate about the corporate innovation industry, following trends, attending events, etc.</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
In-depth understanding of the corporate innovation space, players, opportunities</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
A problem solver with the ability to make creative, forward-looking decisions</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
A network of decision-makers and influencers across the enterprise innovation space</b></p></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
You are:</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p><b>
Passionate about building lasting relationships with clients that help them reach their goals and objectives</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
A curious thinker who thrives in a dynamic, fast-paced, and collaborative environment</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
An expert in creating innovation programs that truly impact the teams, organizations and industries of focus</b></p></li>
</ul>
