<p><b>Associate Contracts Analyst (Interventional Urology)
</b></p>
<div><div>
<div><div>
<p>Coloplast has a new opportunity available for an Associate Contract Analyst to join our growing Interventional Urology (IU) team at our North America headquarters in Minneapolis.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>As an Associate Contract Analyst, you will be responsible for managing customer contracts for key customers to optimize our top and bottom line in partnership with our sales team. The responsibility covers all the steps from analyzing the initial contract proposal to following up on customer performance. You will work in a talented and dynamic team environment with frequent exposure to our Urology Care leadership team.<br>
<br>
</p>
<p><b>Major Areas of Accountability
</b></p>
<p><i>Contract Management
</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Collaborate with the Sales team to establish contractual relationships with key Group Purchasing Organization (GPO) Integrated Delivery Network (IDN), and local customers while upholding critical business initiatives.
</li>
<li>Analyze contract terms and conditions to determine compliance and assess corporate risk, while acquiring and documenting appropriate stakeholder approvals
</li>
<li>Serve as point of contact for Coloplast internal clients on contract negotiation and other contractual matters, including status of contracts in process and tracking of contracts in the contracts database and corresponding systems
</li>
<li>Build relationships with key internal clients, including Legal, Finance, Marketing, Regulatory, and Global Operations to facilitate understanding, proactively influence timely decision-making, and streamline execution of contractual terms &amp; conditions
</li>
<li>Assist with the negotiation of customer contracts on business terms and conditions when requested by the sales team
</li>
<li>Ensure corporate requirements are met while implementing contracts: notifications, documentation, price file uploads, record management, database maintenance, systems-related updates, and other follow up items as needed
</li>
<li>Process Letters of Commitment/Designation (LOC/LOD) efficiently and accurately to ensure GPO members receive the correct pricing.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><i>Contract Process Education
</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Educate internal business partners on Coloplast contract request and review process
</li>
<li>Translate detailed contract language into concise, clear communications in order to help internal customers and other external customers better understand contract related terms
</li>
<li>Design or improve upon processes &amp; tools to help the Contracts team facilitate accurate workflow management, contract price loading, KPI monitoring and reporting, data management, and record retention
</li>
<li>Provide timely responses to internal and external inquiries<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><i>Ad hoc Requests
</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop reports for senior management on contract performance and summaries of key terms &amp; conditions
</li>
<li>Design queries from various data sources to assist in research and analysis to contractual requirements
</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Qualifications
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in business or related area is required.
</li>
<li>Minnimum of one year experience working with contracts, negotiations or legal department; healthcare industry is preferred.
</li>
<li>Knowledge of healthcare compliance requirements is preferred
</li>
<li>Excellent understanding of corporate policies and procedures, coupled with ability to identify required improvements to established policies
</li>
<li>Solid understanding of the business needs and an appreciation of financial contractual implications
</li>
<li>Ability to prioritize, multi-task and complete tasks efficiently and independently with a high degree of accuracy and attention to detail
</li>
<li>Confidence to apply independent judgment and creativity to contract resolution issues, in partnership with legal and compliance stakeholders
</li>
<li>Comfortable with communicating and influencing at all levels within the organization
</li>
<li>Proven track record with consistent high performance both academically and professionally
</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills
</li>
<li>Experience with managing multiple initiatives / projects
</li>
<li>Technical proficiency with the following tools / systems: Excel, Access, Outlook, Enterprise One / One World, Cognos, PowerPoint
</li>
<li>A team player with the ability to use strong verbal reasoning skills to analyze elements of contract language<br>
</li>
</ul>
