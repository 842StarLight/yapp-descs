<h2>Building Automation Specialist II</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Building Automation Specialist III (BAS3) reports to the Manager of Projects &amp; Controls and is responsible for performing a range of Controls jobs for industrial and commercial customers.</p>
<p>
 Customer based activities involved in this position require an individual who is decisive, tactful, dependable, and who possesses initiative and the ability to communicate well with all customer and supervisory groups. A BAS3 must be able to accurately diagnose and efficiently accomplish a job to the mutual benefit of the customer and CES. Job related contact could occur with virtually all demographic groups, with the majority of the contacts being service and construction customers, Project Managers, other CES Employees including the Manager of Projects &amp; Controls and Dispatcher.</p>
<p>
Daily job stress will range from minimal to extreme, based on such factors as the weather, site consideration, time constraints, sales potential, travel requirements, job complexity, and customer needs. An average workday will be moderately stressful.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBLITIES:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Serving as a member of the Service Department Team to conduct Controls servicing of jobs, as well as construction controls installations and start-ups promptly and efficiently.</li>
<li>Performing DDC Control service and construction activities without direct oversight or on-site assistance.</li>
<li>Programming application specific controllers and ancillary devices. Setting up and programming system supervisory controllers and front end equipment, including, but not limited to, line code and graphics.</li>
<li>Commissioning systems and performing point to point checks.</li>
<li>Conducting point-of-work sales for added control services, unit components, unit replacements, control system upgrades, HVAC sales leads, and commercial service contracts.</li>
<li>Developing lead referrals for the sales staff.
</li>
<li>Performing related duties as directed by the Service Operations Manager.</li>
<li>Keeping vehicle in clean condition at all times and using vehicle only for company use.</li>
<li>Drive safe at all times and not utilizing the cellular phone while driving.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>
</p>
<p>REQUIREMENTS:</p>
<ul>
<li>High school graduate.</li>
<li>Seven or more years of successful experience, either as a commercial controls service or installation technician.</li>
<li>Good verbal and reading skills include the ability to read and apply manufacturers' instructions and to clearly communicate with customers, suppliers, and other CES employees.</li>
<li>Ability to read and understand mechanical blueprints, control drawings, and specifications.</li>
<li>Possess basic computer skills including MS Word and Excel.</li>
<li>Possess in depth knowledge of manufacturers control system software.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>JOB KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS AND ABILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Perform all duties noted above under the conditions, circumstances, and extreme weather conditions found in Florida.
</li>
<li>Possess good vision (either naturally or with correction).
</li>
<li>Lift and carry objects weighing up to 100 pounds occasionally and up to 60 pounds frequently.
</li>
<li>Stoop, kneel, crouch and crawl.
</li>
<li>Climb and balance on ladders and stand for long periods of time.
</li>
<li>Must not have fear of heights (acrophobia).</li>
</ul>
