<h2>Business Development Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><div>We are looking to hire a Business Development Representative (BDR) to join our growing Inside Sales team as we move deeper into the enterprise market. The Business Development team at Flock is charged with qualifying and producing high quality leads for our sales team. In this role, you will be the first sales contact that a prospect has with Flock. Using outreach emails, phone calls, and social media, you will connect with the right person using the best possible message to set meetings and qualify opportunities for our sales team. Additionally, you will help us farm our installed base of some of the top brands and companies in the world to upsell and cross-sell additional products and services. The BDR program at Flock is a great place to start your career in technology sales and is a great opportunity to learn the fundamentals of sales while working with a product and customer focused team.</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What is the Job like?</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
As a Business Development Rep, you will execute outreach strategies, research and qualify customers while identifying expansion opportunities within our paying customer base.</li>
<li>
Work closely with the Manager of Client Development to pursue deals within our paying customer base.</li>
<li>
Prepare yourself to close deals so you’re ready to become an Account Executive</li>
<li>
Conduct a high volume of prospecting hitting daily and monthly activities.</li>
<li>
Help the customer understand the value of Team Messaging and collaboration (85% of the world does not use a messaging app for work).</li>
<li>
Setting up meetings with qualified customers through calls, email, and social media</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
