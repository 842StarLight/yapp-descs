<h2>Experienced Technical Help Desk Technician</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>About Tech to Us</b></p>
<p>Tech to Us is a fully US-based and staffed provider of remote technical support services for home users and is also a managed IT services provider (MSP) to businesses in Connecticut and across the country.</p>
<p>At Tech to Us, we look for great people who are looking to grow with the company and, in turn, help achieve that growth. There is an excellent opportunity for advancement at Tech to Us since we are a small, yet growing company. Individuals, who are dependable, work hard and excel at their job, have the chance to be rewarded both financially and career-wise.</p>
<p><b>Position Summary</b></p>
<p>A Help Desk/Support Technician is responsible for handling calls, emails, and chats from both potential and current customer in regards to technical issues.</p>
<p>In addition to providing technical services to customers, a Technician is also required to proactively sell the proper add-ons such as cloud data backup and software when applicable. The position requires someone who can combine excellent customer service skills, exceptional technical ability, and basic sales skills into one.</p>
<p>Qualified individuals will display an ongoing drive and determination to continually improve and excel in all aspects of the position; including sales, customer service and technical ability.</p>
<p>Employees are required to work from our Hartford, CT office and open availability and flexibility are required. We are a 7 day a week operation with extended hours.</p>
<p><b>Standard Job Functions of All Technical Team Members</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Remote technical support in a multi-level help desk environment.</li>
<li>Provide quality technical support services and customer service on a consistent basis.</li>
<li>Troubleshoot a variety of computer, peripheral, networking or related software and hardware issues remotely.</li>
<li>Provide and apply accurate and efficient technical and practical solutions to solve customer issues.</li>
<li>Document detailed and accurate technical notes regarding each customer issue and steps taken to resolve it throughout the customer contact; ensuring completion before ending the contact.</li>
<li>Sell and up-sell all Tech to Us services. A proactive approach to sales is standard and required.</li>
<li>Consistently meet and exceed goals and service levels for customer service, technical quality, first call resolution, efficiency, and sales</li>
<li>Understand the needs of business customers and have a proper understanding of business IT environments such as server and networking knowledge.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Knowledge and Skills Required</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Computer Networking. Wired, Wireless, Knowledge of routing, DHCP, DNS, TCP/IP, switching and next-gen firewalls devices.</li>
<li>Operating systems. Microsoft Windows 7, 8/8.1, 10. Mobile and tablet operating systems such as Android and iOS.</li>
<li>Knowledge of Malware removal processes</li>
<li>Ability to properly install, configure and troubleshoot issues with peripherals such as printers, scanners, digital cameras, and webcams</li>
<li>Knowledge of Apple Hardware and Mac OS 10.x</li>
<li>Active Directory and General Windows Server knowledge</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>As a standard and regular part of the job, being comfortable selling services over the phone is highly important and necessary to achieve the required sales results.</li>
<li>Ability to continually learn, be trained and accept constructive feedback</li>
<li>Detailed and goal-oriented</li>
<li>Ability to handle a consistent volume of calls, emails, and chats</li>
<li>Ability to keep efficient and organized while handling multiple tasks or multiple customers at the same time</li>
<li>Strong and dedicated work ethic</li>
<li>Ability to work efficiently.</li>
<li>Ability to manage time effectively.</li>
<li>Company first focus and mentality.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Experience Required</b></p>
<ul><li>A minimum of 3-years of experience in a help desk or technical support environment is required.</li></ul>
<p><b>Education</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or GED.</li>
<li>Completed technical school or coursework, Associates Degree or Bachelor's Degree in Computer, Networking or IT related area preferred.</li>
<li>A+, Network +, MCSE certifications are a plus</li>
</ul>

<li>Paid Sick Time</li>
<li>401k</li>
</ul>
