<div>
Fluidigm is looking for a Field Applications Specialist I (Mid-west territory) who is the primary customer-facing role in supporting Fluidigm’s flagship proteomics platform the Helios™ Mass Cytometer, a CyTOF system. The primary responsibilities for this role will be supporting mass cytometry customers. Additionally, the mass cytometry FAS serves a critical Voice-of-Customer role in by providing relevant and timely feedback to internal colleagues in Application Support, Marketing, and R&amp;D.<br>
<br>
If you are inspired and driven by cutting-edge technologies and you work well in a flexible and fast-paced environment, then this position may be for you.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Description:</div>
<ul>
<li>Provide high quality sales support, engaging with customers to provide technical discussion, seminars, and demonstrations relating to mass cytometry.</li>
<li>Provide multi-faceted mass cytometry customer support including on-site and in-house training, email and phone inquiries, applications discussion, planning and troubleshooting, and data analysis.</li>
<li>Participate in conferences, trade-shows and symposia.</li>
<li>Collaborate with in-house teams to provide customer feedback and enhance future products.</li>
<li>Travel ~60% within your territory and other parts of North America as needed.</li>
<li>Enjoy a highly competitive compensation package, including better-than-average benefits.</li>
<li>Have fun, be fully engaged and promise to have a life beyond “work”.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Minimum Qualifications:</div>
<ul>
<li>M.S. in life sciences or equivalent experience background is required. Ph.D. and strong immunology background preferred.</li>
<li>3-5 years of hands-on experience with biochemistry, molecular biology, immunology or cell biology lab is required. Experience in flow cytometry is highly desirable.</li>
<li>Previous mass cytometry experience preferred.</li>
<li>Experience with tissue-based imaging techniques highly desirable.</li>
<li>High level bioinformatics skills highly desirable.</li>
<li>A keen interest in technology along with a high level of self-motivation and excellent organizational skills.</li>
<li>Excellent skills in training instrumentation and software as well as technical writing.</li>
<li>Strong presentation skills with a natural and influential style that comes across in both large seminars and small groups.</li>
<li>A strong desire to collaborate both with customers and colleagues.</li>
<li>Independent thinker with excellent problem solving and interpersonal skills</li>
<li>A passion for helping customers succeed.</li>
<li>Previous applications specialist or other customer-facing experience preferred.</li>
</ul>
