<div>Georgia-Pacific is seeking a <b>Building Envelope Manager </b>to join our Building Products Technical Services team. This position is based out of our Decatur Gypsum Technical Center (just outside Atlanta, GA). Georgia Pacific’s R&amp;D, Quality, and Technical Service teams work closely together at the Decatur Gypsum Technical Center a state of the art facility that includes an analytical lab and extensive application-based testing capabilities. If you aspire to utilize your subject matter expertise, experience &amp; technical aptitude, networking ability &amp; business acumen, along with proven interpersonal &amp; relationship building skills, we are interested in learning more about you!
</div>
<div></div>
<div>Georgia-Pacific creates long-term value by using resources efficiently to provide innovative products and solutions that meet the needs of customers and society, while operating in a manner that is environmentally and socially responsible, and economically sound. Headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia-Pacific LLC is one of the world's leading manufacturers and marketers of building products, consumer products, packaging, paper, cellulose and chemicals. The company employs more than 30,000 people at approximately 300 locations in North and South America.</div>
