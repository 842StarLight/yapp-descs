<p><b>Job Code: D31</b></p>
<p><b>
Job Posting Title: Acquisition Program Specialist, Journeyman*</b></p>
<p><b>
Job Location: Fort Walton Beach, FL (Eglin AFB)</b></p>
<p>
Are you looking for a rewarding and impactful new opportunity? Odyssey Systems has an exciting opportunity for an <b>Acquisition Program Specialist, Journeyman</b> supporting the <b>Direct Attack Division, AFLCMC/EBD</b>. This division consists of the <b>Massive Ordnance Penetrator (MOP), BLU-120, Joint Programmable Fuze (JPF), Hard Target Void Sensing Fuze (HTVSF), Medium Caliber Ammo, </b>and <b>Foreign Military Sales (FMS)</b> of multiple assets and a number of other US and FMS air-to-ground munitions and supporting systems in various acquisition phases. Their mission is to advance combat capability through life cycle management of direct attack systems and enterprise-level air armament mission planning capabilities, integrate existing standard munitions, and launch platforms and integrate with C41 capabilities.</p>
<p><b>
Odyssey Systems Consulting Group, Ltd.</b> is an innovative small business committed to providing world-class technical, management, and training support services to government and public sector clients. We focus on people, processes, and performance to deliver superior results. Since our inception in 1997, our commitment to mission success and customer satisfaction has been recognized with exponential growth and exceptional past performance ratings. We accept challenging assignments and drive projects from the planning stages, through implementation, and into operations and support.</p>
<p><b>
Job Responsibilities</b>:</p>
<p><i>
Duties include but not limited to</i>:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Provides the knowledge and experience in developing, maintaining, and updating acquisition reports including the Weekly Activity Reports (WARs), Monthly/Program Acquisition Reports (MARs/ PARs), Selected Acquisition Report (SAR), Defense Acquisition Executive Summary (DAES), Acquisition Program Baseline (APB), and supplemental reports as required</li>
<li>
Provides resources with knowledge and experience in developing, maintaining and updating program Integrated Master Plans (IMP) and Integrated Master Schedule (IMS); analyzing prime and subcontractor IMP/I MS; identifying opportunities to improve program execution; reviewing deliverable status together with milestone schedules, status reports, and cost performance reports; and determining accuracy of information and situation depicted.</li>
<li>
Plans, evaluates and implements program acquisition strategies to meet current guidance</li>
<li>
Develops program management documentation to support all phases of the acquisition life cycle</li>
<li>
Prepares Project/Program Management Reviews (PMRs) in conjunction with the PMs and IPTs for complete integration management, governance, and compliance at the Branch, Division and PEO levels</li>
<li>
Prepares and coordinates responses to requests for information from both internal and external organizations</li>
<li>
Develops briefings for stakeholder engagements and integration management</li>
<li>
Prepares decision-support information</li>
<li>
Develops IPT staffing plans supporting the PMs using LCMP and AFMC (ASU model)</li>
<li>
Collects data, prepares, and drafts program reports as required</li>
<li>
Collects data, prepares drafts and edits the annual history report</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
AA/AS + 10 years of relevant experience; OR BA/BS + 5 years of relevant experience; OR MA/MS + 2 years of relevant experience</li>
<li>
Secret Security Clearance</li>
<li>
Proficient in the Microsoft Office Suite</li>
<li>
Proficiency in DoD/OSD systems such as BQ, MOCAS, SMART, DAMIR, IDECS, FM-Suite, EDA, Comprehensive Cost and Requirement System (CCaR), and LCMP preferred</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to prepare briefings, graphics, and reports</li>
<li>
Effective communication skills both orally and in writing</li>
<li>
Experience in developing maintaining, and updating acquisition reports for a Government Program Office preferred</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Additional information:</b></p>

Travel required (approx. 20%)</li>
<li>
Must be able to rent/operate a rental car while on travel</li>
</ul>
