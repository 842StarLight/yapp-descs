<h2>E-Commerce & E-mail Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Under the supervision of the Marketing Director, the E-Commerce Manager is responsible for leading and managing email communications and e-commerce and online engagement with consumers through moodypublishers.com. This role also includes supervisory responsibility of outside contractor(s). This role is accountable for achieving consumer sales revenue targets as well as growing Moody Publishers’ audience and reach through email campaigns. This person will also work collaboratively with book marketing managers and others within the team to promote new and backlist titles and authors through email communications and our website.
</p>
<p><b>Essential Functions</b></p>
<p><i>
Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions of the position.</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Develops the annual e-commerce marketing plan, including qualitative and quantitative goals, strategic partnerships, messaging, and operational support.</li>
<li>Work with the marketing team to create and implement email marketing plans and campaigns. Create and implement email and automated marketing campaigns to increase consumer sales, reader engagement, and audience development.</li>
<li>Develop and track success metrics and goals for each email or e-commerce campaign. Monitor, analyze and report the metrics to Moody Publisher’s marketing team and leadership.</li>
<li>Provide thought leadership, best practices and procedures, and support to book marketing managers and authors regarding optimization of emails.</li>
<li>Collaborates with Customer Service, Business Systems, IT, and other department marketing teams to ensure seamless and coordinated service to consumers.</li>
<li>Active participation in weekly product review meetings to provide direct-to-consumer knowledge and drive discoverability and sales of Moody Publishers titles and authors.</li>
<li>Perform related and special duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>

<ul>
<li>Committed to Moody Publishers’ vision and mission</li>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in Marketing or related field</li>
<li>Three to five years of e-commerce and/or email experience</li>
<li>Proven experience and results increasing online sales significantly</li>
<li>Ability to report, draw conclusions, and present recommendations based on data</li>
<li>Results-driven project management skills</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal skills and emotional intelligence</li>
<li>Detail-oriented and organized</li>
<li>Excellent oral and written communication skills</li>
<li>Able to work in the US Legally, without sponsorship</li>
</ul>

Preferred Requirements</b></p>
