<h2>Cutlist Technician Custom Closet Manufacturing</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">We have experienced rapid growth and we are looking for a sharp, dynamic individual with a lot of initiative and drive to take charge of our custom cut list department. You must have great interpersonal skills to communicate with everyone from the owners of the company, sales designers to hourly employees and manage the cut list and job detailing efforts.
<br><br>
You will be a member of our management team helping to develop plans for the future. You must be able to identify areas of concern and develop processes and procedures to aid in our growth and the long term health of the business.
<br><br>
To be successful in this position, you will have the following duties and responsibilities:
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Oversee and direct all KCD design reviews and cut-list development</li>
<li>Ordering custom doors, drawers, custom parts and specification</li>
<li>Convert KCD designs to factory cut lists</li>
<li>Visit job sites to evaluate site conditions, field measurements and interact with clients and installers</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills with customers and management</li>
<li>Effectively communicate build/construction plans, performance goals with team members</li>
<li>Implement company policies and procedures effectively</li>
<li>Be highly focused and able to work with minimum supervision</li>
</ul>
The following are the requirements you must meet to qualify for the position:
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 2+ years experience in closet/cabinet specification and cut list work using KCD</li>
<li>Experience in the custom closet industry using the 32 mm manufacturing system</li>
<li>Strong computer experience with proficiency in Windows, Word and Excel</li>
<li>Experience with beam saw and CNC equipment</li>
<li>CAD design experience a plus, KCD Design software preferred</li>
</ul>
If you feel you have the right qualifications and want to take the next step in your career, please send your resume, salary history and references. We will be contacting qualified candidates immediately.</div>
