<p><b>SUMMARY </b>:
</p>
<p>As an Estimator you will examine damaged vehicles to determine the extent of structural, body, mechanical, electrical, or interior damage. You will be responsible for estimating the cost of labor and parts to repair or replace each damaged item.<br>
<br>
</p>
<div>
<b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES:
</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Reviewing repair cost estimates with a body shop manager or foreman
</li>
<li>Determining feasibility of repair versus replacement of parts
</li>
<li>Preparing insurance forms to indicate repair cost estimate and recommendations
</li>
<li>Estimating cost of repainting, converting to special purposed, or customizing undamaged vehicles
</li>
<li>Writing reports, business correspondence, and procedure manuals
</li>
<li>Presenting information and responding to questions from groups or managers, clients, customers, and the general public<br>
</li>
</ul>
</div>
