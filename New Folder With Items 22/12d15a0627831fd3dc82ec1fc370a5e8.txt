<p>Territory Account Manager: Maryland - Virginia - SUPERSTARS ONLY NEED APPLY</p>
<p><b>About the Employer</b>: Founded in 1986, Eastern Marketing. is one of America’s fastest growing luxury appliance distributors. We specialize in cooking appliances, kitchen ventilation, under counter refrigeration, wine cellars, ice makers, outdoor kitchens and more.. You may learn more about our company at www.easternmarketing.com</p>
<p><b>About the Role</b>: EMC is seeking a District Sales Manager calling on appliance dealers, builders and kitchen &amp; bath dealers. This position is charged with growing our existing relationships with appliance dealers, builders and kitchen &amp; bath dealers as well as establishing new relationships with companies which we do not currently do business.</p>
<p><b>Key Role Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Initiate and continually develop strong relationships with principals, influencers and salespeople at appliance and kitchen &amp; bath dealers</li>
<li>Secure premium merchandising space for EMC brands at these dealers</li>
<li>Conduct regular, impactful trainings for salespeople in your market to show the superior features and benefits of our products</li>
<li>Influence customer sales through strategic use of programs, promotions, incentives, advertising support and display allowances</li>
<li>Remove barriers to sales growth, which often includes working with the relevant contact at EMC to solve customer-service-related problems like credit limits, retail incentive claims, RMAs, and technical service, as well as other issues</li>
<li>Provide feedback to our marketing team on key competitor initiatives</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Ideal Candidate </b></p>
<ul>
<li><b>4 or more years of sales experience </b></li>
<li>Positive attitude with strong presentation and relationship building skills</li>
<li>Strong organizational skills with solid project management skills but not so “rigid” that can’t adapt to a fast-paced and/or an ever-changing competitive environment</li>
<li>Analytical abilities – ability to use sales reporting tools to analyze threats and opportunities</li>
<li>Effective communication and business writing skills</li>
<li>Thrive on working in a fast paced environment</li>
<li>Strong proficiency using Technology</li>
<li>Reside in the Baltimore area and be able to service EMC customers during normal business hours as well as be available for special dealer events that may occur evenings and weekends.</li>
<li>The territory requires 8 overnights per month to ensure proper coverage. We will invest in the territory and provide you with the tools to be successful. Displays, POP, Training, Service and back up office support are important keys to success</li>
</ul>
<p>Compensation for this position will be salary based with commission and bonus objectives.</p>
