<h2>Equipment Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Join the team as we build and start manufacturing in our new facility in Sherman, Texas where we will produce vertical-cavity surface-emitting lasers (VCSELs) used in Apple's iPhone camera to allow features such as Face ID, Animoji and Portrait mode selfies. What are VCSELs? It's an acronym that is pronounced "vixels" they are small semiconductor devices that emit light vertically and enable 3D sensing.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>KEY RESPONSIBILLITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Experience repairing and calibrating DC Power supplies.</li>
<li>Strong mechanical skills required</li>
<li>Maintain effective communications between equipment engineering, process engineering, quality/test and manufacturing to insure optimal equipment availability.</li>
<li>Develop and implement preventative maintenance programs to ensure optimal equipment performance.</li>
<li>Develop new concepts of maintenance to eliminate or reduce major equipment failures and downtime.</li>
<li>Identify and correct any chronic problem areas, using both on-site and off'site equipment.</li>
<li>Coordinate maintenance activities with EE Supervisor and Production Supervisors to minimums production delays.</li>
<li>Provide necessary data for decision making and work with equipment suppliers to improve data collection.</li>
<li>Maintain a high degree of expertise within the group by assisting in the training and development of EE Technicians who are responsible for the repair, maintenance and calibration of existing equipment.</li>
<li>Provide expertise and support in the selection, source inspection, and acceptance of new equipment.</li>
<li>Direct and supervise the installation of new equipment within the area of responsibility.</li>
<li>Develop and implement running PM programs to assist operations in proper tool setup and monitoring of critical parameters on non-automated equipment.</li>
<li>Participate in the Emergency Response program.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
SKILLS/QUALIFICATIONS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
BS or AA and 10 + years in Epi or equivalent work experience is desired.</li>
<li>Strong mechanical and electrical skills.</li>
<li>Experience in handling chemicals.</li>
<li>Board level troubleshooting skills.</li>
<li>Good communication, planning, organizational &amp; interpersonal skills.</li>
<li>Must be able to function in a fast-paced environment, carrying out multiple projects at a time.</li>
<li>Experience managing high profile projects.</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft project and other office software</li>
<li>Positive can do attitude with a lot of energy to break down barriers and tackle problems.</li>
<br>
</ul>
