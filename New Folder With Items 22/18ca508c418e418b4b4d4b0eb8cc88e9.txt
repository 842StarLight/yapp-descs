<div>We are looking for people who are keen to try something new:</div>
<div>The Energy Materials Applications Scientist will have responsibilities in all areas of applications development activities, primarily related to Energy Materials (e.g., batteries, fuel cells, photovoltaics, and nuclear materials) marketing. This individual will work extensively with customers, collaborators, and the rest of the Materials Science marketing team to define, execute, and promote novel applications of the ZEISS microscopy portfolio. The scientist will provide support to the marketing team through the development of collateral and participation at customer-facing events such as scientific conferences and workshops. The scientist will travel up to 50% for training and applications support to existing customers, as well as sales support activities. Excellent time management skills, communication skills, and the ability to work independently in a global team setting is a must. Conduct in accordance with company guidelines and standards is expected at all times.<br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Be an expert in using ZEISS microscopy products, e.g. the ZEISS Xradia Versa systems, and 2D/3D visualization software products</li>
<li>Promote and develop compelling Energy Materials solutions around the ZEISS microscopy portfolio</li>
<li>Collaborate with lighthouse customers and present research at conferences and workshops</li>
<li>Perform product demonstrations of ZEISS microscopy products both with and without customers present</li>
<li>Maintain demo excellence within the ZEISS microscopy customer centers for energy materials solutions across the product portfolio</li>
<li>Collect information from the sales group and the customer about what results for each sample are required to win the business</li>
<li>Create detailed reports, white papers, and presentations on the results that showcase product differentiation</li>
<li>Be able to professionally deliver technical presentations both internally and externally, in person and through web meetings</li>
<li>Train customer users regarding how to operate ZEISS Microscopy products – training sessions may be held either at the ZEISS facility or at the customer’s location</li>
<li>Gather customer inputs to product requirements by application and help translate those needs into future ZEISS microscopy solutions</li>
<li>Contribute to the development of marketing material</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>
<div>
<div>You are:</div>
<ul><li>B.S. in physical sciences or engineering – MS or PhD preferred</li></ul>
<br>
Research experience in Energy Materials<br>
<ul>
<li>Hands-on experience with 2D and 3D microscope characterization for materials science</li>
<li>Experience with X-ray Computed Tomography systems required – ZEISS Xradia Versa systems preferred</li>
<li>Experience with 2D/3D image analysis software (Dragonfly, Arivis, ImageJ, Avizo, etc.) including machine learning based segmentation and python scripting</li>
<li>Technical documentation authoring and editing skills</li>
<li>Self-starter with demonstrated ability to independently carry out and complete projects</li>
<li>Analytical thinking, aptitude and creativity to work on unfamiliar problems</li>
<li>Excellent communication and presentation skills, and the ability to work independently in a global team – multilingual a plus</li>
<li>Ability to travel up to 50%</li>
</ul>
