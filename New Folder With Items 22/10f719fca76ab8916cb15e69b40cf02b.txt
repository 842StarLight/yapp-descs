<h2>Java Developer (Application Support Analyst)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Herff Jones, A Varsity Achievement Brand, is looking to add a Java Developer (Application Support Analyst) to our team. As a member of the web development support team, the Java Developer (Application Support Analyst) will play an integral role in improving the level of support given to our customers. The Java Developer will work closely with the team to solve complex customer issues, work on bug fixes and help the team assess and improve the code.</p>
<b>
What You’ll Do On A Daily Basis:</b>
<ul>
<li>Work with a team of experienced developers to improve the level of support for the flagship SaaS of the Yearbook division.</li>
<li>Solve customer issues in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Develop code for regular maintenance releases.</li>
<li>Develop solutions to technical debt items, to improve app stability and maintainability.</li>
<li>Develop tools to help us better support our customers.</li>
<li>Review other Developers’ code to ensure the highest level of quality.</li>
<li>Assist support lead in improving processes and documentation across the team.</li>
<li>Challenge team leadership with new ideas and approaches to common problems experienced by our customers, both internal and external.</li>
<li>Guide less experienced Developers, to improve their code quality and find resources to solve problems.</li>
<li>Investigating and solving unique customer issues, which can’t be handled by the customer-facing technical support team.</li>
<li>Work closely with other support team members to discuss and solve issues.</li>
<li>Identify and fix software bugs.</li>
<li>Identify areas and solutions for future improvement to the application.</li>
</ul>
<b>What You’ll Bring To The Table</b>:
<ul>
<li>Successfully worked in a high tier of support on very large SaaS applications.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to work independently to solve complex customer issues.</li>
<li>Experience working in highly collaborative teams.</li>
<li>Shown an aptitude for working in a quickly changing environment, while maintaining a positive attitude.</li>
<li>Ability to hand-write SQL queries (Oracle preferred)</li>
<li>Experience with ticketing and bug tracking systems</li>
<li>Demonstrated knowledge of analytics tools (Splunk or New Relic preferred)</li>
<li>At least one year of experience writing HTML, JavaScript, or Angular.</li>
<li>At least 3-5 years of experience writing Java.</li>
<li>Experience with Amazon Web Services considered a plus.</li>
<li>Experience with Jenkins considered a plus.</li>
