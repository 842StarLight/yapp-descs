<h2>VP, Internal Audit</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Do you enjoy creating air-tight, well-structured audit practices to mitigate risk? Can you recite GAAP, IFRS and SOX principles in your sleep? You might be our next VP, Internal Audit at sgsco in Chicago, IL!</b></p>
<p><b>
Company Description</b></p>
<p>
Southern Graphic Systems (Sgsco) is a global collective of teams, talent and expertise, collaborating to help brands Own The Moment™ when a consumer’s desire can be converted into action. They believe in the power of the package to convert interest into purchase; it’s what they call The Moment of Sale™. The Sgsco collective delivers compelling package design and production consistently and quickly, with significant efficiencies, on a global scale.</p>
<p>
Sgsco adds value to the vast majority of the world’s largest consumer packaged goods (CPG) organizations through multiple entities (the collective) and almost 5,000 employees serving customers in more than 30 countries. They are not a holding company, they’re the team for the future, helping brands Own The Moment™! Sgsco is dedicated to driving results for clients. Rather than following a holding company model, they’ve united all of their companies with one bottom line, creating a collective team to drive powerful strategy, innovation and execution.</p>
<p><b>
Position Summary</b></p>
<p>
As a key member of the global finance team, the Vice President Audit will oversee a small team and will be responsible for designing, preparing and implementing a risk-based audit plan to assess, report on and make suggestions for improving the company’s operations, finance activities and internal controls.</p>
<p>
Reporting to both the CFO and the Audit Committee, the Vice President Audit will have entire responsibility for the strategy, design and overall program management of audit and internal controls for the Company. Leading the audit team, the Vice President Audit will have ultimate responsibility for staffing to meet the needs of delivering the required programs globally.</p>
<p><b>
Key Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>
Essential duties and responsibilities include the following. Other duties may be assigned based on business needs and abilities.</p>
<ul>
<li>Developing methodologies and test plans for the audit of compliance with internal control policies.</li>
<li>Designing solid audit plans as well as ensuring modifications are made as business processes and procedures change in the financial operating environment.</li>
<li>Implement and effectively execute an annual internal audit plan consistent with the priorities identified through the risk assessment process.</li>
<li>Providing recommendations for strengthening internal controls.</li>
<li>Review and implementation of new internal controls as necessary.</li>
<li>Partner with Sgsco management to implement recommendations resulting from internal audits.</li>
<li>Co-ordination and liaising with external auditors.</li>
<li>Remain current with all significant trends in internal controls and finance processes.</li>
<li>Assist in developing company policies and procedures to ensure the existence of a strong control environment.</li>
<li>Developing and overseeing the Company’s Enterprise Risk Management Program.</li>
<li>Ensuring the Company understands and is in compliance with Sarbanes-Oxley (“SOX”) 404 and National Instrument 52-109 (NI 52-109).</li>
<li>Monitoring compliance and reviewing ongoing effectiveness of Company’s Risk Management Policy.</li>
<li>Ongoing monitoring and assessment of risks including reviewing business models and contracts for risk issues on an ongoing basis.</li>
<li>Recruit, train, supervise and evaluate departmental staff. Reinforces the values of Sgsco and its brands to ensure employees understand the expected behaviors and competencies to make a great place to work.</li>
<li>Establish and maintain schedules and deadlines that allow the Company to meet its requirements.</li>
</ul>

</ul>
