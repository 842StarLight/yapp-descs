<p>This is a recognized world leader in the production and manufacture of large machines provide service and parts support for customers around the world. </p>
<p><b>JOB RESPONSIBILITIES
</b></p>
<p>This person would:
</p>
<p>? Have a `big picture` perspective regarding planning, controlling, tracking and reporting including the sales of all extrusion press machines and related parts.
</p>
<p>? Work with sales reps in developing quotations and continually communicate with customer maintenance after the sale.
</p>
<p>? Develop new relationships with potential customers and maintain relationships with existing customers.
</p>
<p>? Oversees and approves preparation of advertising materials including organizing trade shows, sales brochures and other related materials.
</p>
<p>? Have authority to approve quotes and purchases.
</p>
<p>? Communicate and negotiate with oversees parts suppliers.
</p>
<p><b>REQUIREMENTS
</b></p>
<p>? Bachelor degree in mechanical or technical fields with 4-6 years of related experience in the <b>extrusion field </b>or combination of education and experience.</p>
