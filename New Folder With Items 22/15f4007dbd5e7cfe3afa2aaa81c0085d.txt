<h2>Ad Inventory Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<div>Volta is building a nation-wide network of free-to-use EV chargers. We place our stations at the highest profile locations within a city and our advertising sponsors provide charging as a free, open-access service. The sponsored services model combined with digital, adaptive media brings real-world advertising and EV charging into the 21st century. With Volta, everyone wins.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
The Ad Inventory Manager is a pivotal role at Volta. You will have the opportunity to play a crucial part in transforming the way we operate as a business. You will work closely with cross-functional teams like Media Sales, Site Sales, Finance, Business Operations, Operations, and Legal. You will assist the Sales Operations Manager in delivering solutions to complex issues and identifying process breakdowns across the ad inventory management process.</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>You will own the end to end ad inventory process for both media sponsors and site partners hosting our stations</li>
<li>
You will work closely with the media sales account management team to ensure timely and accurate ad installments for digital, static, and programmatic campaigns</li>
<li>
You will assist the media sales team in creating custom campaigns based on customer requirements and station/ad inventory availability</li>
<li>
You will work closely with the Business Operations team to prioritize projects relating to ad inventory management</li>
<li>
You will enable Salesforce improvements and automate processes</li>
<li>
You will design and improve key ad inventory processes for efficiency and connect the dots with downstream functions</li>
<li>
You will build and manage reports and dashboards within Salesforce to provide insight and guidance around utilization per station, utilization per market/client, etc.</li>
<li>
You will work with cross-functional teams to identify and solve process inefficiencies</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
3+ years in ad operations at a fast-growing startup</li>
<li>
Experience administering &amp; improving Salesforce, Vistar, or Ayuda</li>
<li>
Experience as an operator working with cross-functional teams</li>
<li>
Exceptional independent problem solving skills, attention to detail, and adaptable in changing situations</li>
<li>
Excellent communication skills to collaborate with internal teams</li>
<li>
Strategic thinker, efficient &amp; execution driven</li>
<li>
Growth mindset: A desire to dive in and learn about new problems</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
