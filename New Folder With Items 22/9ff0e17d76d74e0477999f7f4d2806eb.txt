<p><b>
Description:</b></p>
<p>
The <b>Marketo Architect (MCSA)</b>, will report to the Digital Marketing Director and be responsible for leading the marketing automation part of cross-platform projects and/or owning the entire engagement in those instances where the project is a standalone initiative. You will also be expected to support hybrid delivery projects (onshore/ offshore teams), in collaboration with project managers, Business Analysts, and technical leads. Additional duties will include managing the development of proprietary digital marketing solutions developed by TA Digital.</p>
<p>The ideal candidate will possess a <b>MCSA (required)</b> along with a combination of development, configuration, marketing business analysis, and project management experience.
</p>
<p><b>General Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Create marketing automation implementation plans (sales and marketing alignment, lead-to-revenue strategies, database requirements, automation configuration and functional mapping of integrations).</li>
<li>As a member of a cross-functional team, oversee and execute marketing automation audits, implementations, integrations and rebuilds.</li>
<li>Conduct business process review meetings and define technical/functional requirements for marketing automation.</li>
<li>Develop functional expectations and business architecture, in collaboration with the digital marketing practice leader and digital strategists. Write marketing automation user stories and lead story refinement till final approval.</li>
<li>Design ABM programs, content personalization programs,</li>
<li>Support integrations, proof-of-concept (POC) development or new product development for the Digital Marketing Practice’s Innovation products/ services.</li>
<li>Collaborate with practice leadership to enhance the company’s profile with major software partners, as a personal contributor within their communities, custom content development, etc.</li>
<li>Provide Subject Matter Expertise for new Marketing Automation platforms within the practice, as directed by senior management. After becoming a SME, provide training and expertise to junior resources in the practice.</li>
<li>Support hybrid development efforts through daily, joint delivery with offshore teams.</li>
<li>Manage client relationships, in collaboration with project and practice managers.</li>
</ul>

</ul>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<p><b>
Experience and Education Required</b></p>
<ul>

<li>Certifications: The <b>MCE</b> (Marketo Certified Expert) along with the <b>MCSA </b>(Marketo Certified Solutions Architect) are required.</li>
<li>5+ years of experience with Marketing Automation platforms, including 3+ years of Marketo experience. Additional platforms of interest include: Adobe Campaign, Oracle Marketing Cloud (Eloqua), or Salesforce Marketing Cloud, Pardot.</li>
<li>Experience with software configuration processes (martech stacks) and familiarity with coding fundamentals. Hands-on experience with one or more of the following: HTML/CSS, JavaScript, webhooks or API calls.</li>
<li>Experience with delivery of marketing automation projects, especially implementations and integrations (CRM, CMS).</li>
<li>Experience in client-facing roles in a digital marketing agency or marketing technology consulting firms.</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal presentation skills and consultative relationships.</li>
