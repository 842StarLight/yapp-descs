<h2>Senior Accountant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Senior Accountant</b></p>
<p><b>Full-time</b></p>
<p><b>Major Duties and Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul><li>Perform accounting functions as assigned including, but not limited to:</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>Prepare and record journal entries as part of the monthly financial close.</li>
<li>Maintain fixed asset ledger; additions, deletions, monthly depreciation entries</li>
<li>Track and record advertising programs, SPIFFs, etc; interact with sales personnel to ensure programs are properly accrued, recorded and paid.</li>
<li>Reconcile balance sheet accounts on a monthly basis; follow-up and clear all reconciling items</li>
<li>Inventory reconciliation, re-costing and analysis * Prepare accurate, timely financial statements in accordance with Generally Accepted Accounting Principles</li>
<li>Facilitate and coordinate the annual budget process</li>
<li>Participate in the annual physical inventory</li>
<li>Analyze processes and recommend improvements for efficiency and internal controls.</li>
<li>Maintenance of financial systems as needed (e.g. add new accounts; update allocations)</li>
<li>Support management requests for financial information and analysis.</li>
<li>Assist in preparing assigned schedules for the annual and interim audit and the workers compensation audit; interact with auditors and provide additional information as requested</li>
<li>Document policies, procedures and workflow for assigned areas of responsibility</li>
<li>Back up support for sales tax filing and payroll processing.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Required / Preferred Qualifications: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Thorough understanding of Generally Accepted Accounting Principles (GAAP)</li>
<li>Strong analytical and accounting skills</li>
<li>Intermediate to advanced experience with MS Excel</li>
<li>Experience with JDEdwards World system preferred but not required</li>
<li>Excellent verbal, written communication and interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Display original thinking and creativity with the ability to work independently.</li>
<li>Effective time management skills and demonstrated ability to multi-task projects and responsibilities and meet deadlines</li>
<li>Demonstrate leadership abilities working within a team environment.</li>
<li>Strong organizational skills and attention to detail with a focus on accuracy</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Education /Experience: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelors Degree in Accounting or equivalent from an accredited university.</li>
<li>Four to six years progressively responsible experience in accounting, preferably in a manufacturing environment.</li>
</ul>
