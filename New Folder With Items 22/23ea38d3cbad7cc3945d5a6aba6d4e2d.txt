<h2>FORECAST ANALYST</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Description:</b><br>
<b>Core business hours:</b> 8:30am - 5:00pm.
<br><b>Benefits:</b> Medical, Dental, Vision, Life Ins. AD&amp;D, Sup. Life, Vacation, Sick, 401K, Hoildays
<br><br>
The Forecast Analyst effectively leads the link between Customers, Sales organization, Marketing, Logistics and Operations to optimize company assets and customer satisfaction. Monitors forecasts and compiles trend analysis. Reports directly to the Supply Chain Manager.
<br><br>
<b>Essential Duties:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Establish, review and maintain forecasts for assigned accounts at multiple levels of aggregation and ensure factors such as historical sales data, market trends, seasonality and promotional activities are reflected</li>
<li>Identify demand variances, issues and discrepancies; investigate root cause of issues, and make recommendations to improve forecast accuracy</li>
<li>Prepare both routine and ad-hoc data collection, reports, and analysis including but not limited to forecast accuracy, POS Data, sales vs booking trends, and SKU rationalizations</li>
<li>Communicates regularly with Sales, Marketing, R&amp;D and others to understand sales programs, customer expectations, and company strategies</li>
<li>Collaborate interdepartmentally for product life cycle management related activities and ensure significant forecast changes are communicated to the supply chain team in a timely manner</li>
<li>Make recommendations to Department Manager on areas of improvements based on analysis and findings</li>
<li>Attendance and punctuality are an essential job duty.</li>
</ul>
