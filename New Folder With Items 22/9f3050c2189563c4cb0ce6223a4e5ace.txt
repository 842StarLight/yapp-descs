<h2>MARKETING REPRESENTATIVE</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><p>Paratech Ambulance Service has an exciting full-time opportunity for a Marketing Representative to establish and maintain rapport with assigned accounts for the purpose of marketing the services offered by Paratech Ambulance Service. Weekly attention to special requests from accounts. Appropriate inquiry resolution within a client facing environment. The Marketing Representative must be efficient, adaptable, and thrive while balancing multiple priorities. If state licensed, provide EMS staffing to the Operations Department while still maintaining an elevated client facing persona.<br>
<br>
<b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES:</b><br>
Market existing clients to ensure that Company is meeting customer expectations.<br>
Market potential new clients in an effort to establish new client base to build future business interests.<br>
Develop robust knowledge of all company service offerings and capabilities.<br>
Attend relevant trade shows, health fairs and other external events.<br>
Assist in coordination and execution of internal special events.<br>
Assist in coordination of external special events.<br>
Managing and resolving inquiries from patients and facilities that have been assigned. Includes facility billing problems and education of ambulance regulations.<br>
Provide marketing perspective to Marketing Director when requested.<br>
Ability to manage time and organize workloads to ensure completion and accuracy of scheduled work.<br>
Track expenses in an organized fashion.<br>
Reports and/or Communicates weekly on the status of assigned marketing activities.<br>
Maintain a high degree of flexibility to allow for management of various issues as they are presented.<br>
Perform other duties as assigned by Marketing Director or Leadership Team.<br>
Potential to assist CTC with facility CPR courses.<br>
<br>
<b>MATERIALS AND EQUIPMENT USED:</b><br>
Understanding of Microsoft software products; i.e. Word, Excel, Power Point, Access and Publisher is desirable.<br>
Other related marketing software as used in company related business.<br>
General office equipment.<br>
Company owned vehicles/cell phone.<br>
<br>
<b>MINIMUM QUALIFICATIONS REQUIRED:</b><br>
<br>
<b>Education and Experience:</b><br>
High School diploma or equivalent. Some higher education courses are desirable.<br>
Current Wisconsin State Emergency Medical Technician certification or health care experience highly desired.<br>
One (1) year of prior marketing or customer service experience in a related field desirable.<br>
<br>
<b>Licenses and Certifications:</b><br>
Licensure as a Wisconsin Emergency Medical Technician Basic desired.<br>
American Heart CPR Certification recommended.<br>
ACLS Certification (if required for level of license) desired.<br>
AHA CPR Instructor Certification desired.<br>
Possess and maintain valid Wisconsin driver’s license required.<br>
Motor vehicle record must meet requirements of our auto insurance provider.<br>
<br>
<b>Knowledge, Skills and Qualifications:</b><br>
Experience in healthcare related to Hospital, Skilled Nursing or Assisted living facilities.<br>
Basic understanding of private ambulance industry desirable.<br>
Strong “people person” skills.<br>
Professionalism.<br>
Have an aptitude for spotting business opportunities.<br>
Good presentation skills.<br>
Excellent interpersonal and communicator skills.<br>
<br>
<b>PHYSICAL DEMANDS:</b><br>
Very heavy strenuous labor work is defined as, for example, exerting force sufficient to lift and carry up to 200 pounds of body weight and occasionally, as a team member, to lift and maneuver onto a gurney body weight up to 600 pounds, or as necessary lift whatever weight is required to handle the emergency. Often the lifting must be done in spaces where use of correct lifting techniques is not possible.<br>
Hearing ability sufficient, for example, to use stethoscope to assess heart and lung sounds where ambient noise is a factor.<br>
Sustained physical effort, standing sitting, squatting, kneeling, pushing, pulling and climbing.<br>
Climbing stairs or terrain in order to access patients and remove them from the location.<br>
Maintain the physical ability to drive company vehicle.<br>
