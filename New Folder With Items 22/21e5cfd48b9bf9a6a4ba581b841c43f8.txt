<div>
<b>Title:</b> Copy Supervisor</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<b>
Department/Discipline:</b> Copy</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<b>
Company/Location: </b>DDB Health / New York</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<b>
Core Competencies: </b>Creativity, Customer Focus, Interpersonal Savvy, Perseverance</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Responsibilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Writes and supervises solid, intelligent, brand-appropriate, creative pieces in a variety of communication forms (eg, sales aid, direct mail) for a variety of audiences (eg, healthcare professionals, patients, consumers)</div></li>
<li><div>
Supervises all copy in all communications related to his/her brand(s)</div></li>
<li>
Gives strong, clear direction to writers</li>
<li>
Exhibits strong creative ability, as shown in ads/other forms of communication, created in partnership with an art director</li>
<li>
Has an ability to understand and process healthcare information</li>
<li>
Thoroughly understands his/her assigned brand(s)—indication, therapeutic implications, competition—and regularly researches trends in the appropriate area(s)</li>
<li>
Contributes strategically to the long-term vision of the brand, internally and with client</li>
<li>
Works well independently while always making sure that all creative work s/he writes or supervises is approved by supervisor/CD</li>
<li>
Submits copy/concepts to supervisor and AE in a timely fashion, with enough time for revisions before copy goes to client</li>
<li>
Leads teammates; maintains a strong relationship with Editorial, Art, and Account Services and follows DDB Health procedures</li>
<li>
Presents points of view articulately and persuasively within DDB health and with client</li>
<li>
Is the “brand champion.” Makes contributions to the success of his/her brand(s) that the client sees as meaningful and proactive. Enhances client’s confidence in DDB Health</li>
<li>
Identifies opportunities to generate work with clients beyond already-contracted activity. Actively pursues these opportunities</li>
<li>
Exemplifies the values of DDB Health and contributes to the overall culture of the agency</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Qualifications and Experience:</b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Bachelor’s Degree</div></li>
<li>
<div>
Portfolio that demonstrates strong conceptual abilities and writing skills</div>
<div>
Previous work experience as a writer in advertising</div>
</li>
<li><div>
Team Oriented</div></li>
<li><div>
Presentation Skills</div></li>
<li><div>
Management Skills</div></li>
<li><div>
Pharma/Healthcare Exp</div></li>
</ul>
