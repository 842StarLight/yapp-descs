<h2>Senior Software Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div>
<div>
<div>
<div>Are you looking for a company that cares about people’s lives and health, including yours? At Olympus, we help make people’s lives healthier, safer and more fulfilling, every day. We’re currently looking for a Senior Software Engineer to join us in our Waltham, MA office.<br>
<br>
<b>Let’s inspire healthier lives, together.</b><br>
<br>
The Senior Software Engineer performs Software/Firmware development for new NPI (New Product Introduction) projects, and perform hardware/firmware integration, testing, and debugging. NPI projects will utilize 80% of work load; the remaining work effort will be in software support of related products. He/ She collaborates with other departments including Operations, Product Management, Customer Support, and Sales, in providing quick, robust system solutions. The incumbent works with a dynamic team of systems, software, electrical, and mechanical engineers to improve existing and creating new NDT products.</div>
</div>
<div>
<p><b>Job Duties:</b></p>
<div>In a typical day, you will:<br>
<br>
<ul><li>Design software for embedded systems, coding, unit testing and system integration on problems of diverse scope requiring judgment and creativity.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Work collaboratively in a team environment.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Mentor junior employees or interns.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Perform Design documentation and communication. Adheres to schedules and timelines.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Perform software/hardware system integration using debuggers, oscilloscopes, etc.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Make judgements/suggestions on hardware schematic reviews.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Develop and execute test plans.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Perform other duties as assigned.</li></ul>
</div>
</div>
<div>
<p><b>Job Requirements:</b></p>
<div>
<b>REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS:</b><br>
<br>
<ul><li>Bachelor’s degree in Engineering required (EE/CE preferred).</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Minimum 5 years of work experience with C/C++ coding required, or an Master’s Degree in Computer Engineering and a minimum 4 years of experience, or a PhD and a minimum 2 years of experience.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Strong knowledge of embedded development tools required.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>GUI development experience using off-the-shelve GUI components. (e.g., QT, PEG, Prisum, ZINC etc) required.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Working knowledge of source control usage (local and distributed) and software development best practices required.</li></ul>
<br>
<b>
PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS:</b><br>
<br>
<ul><li>Working knowledge with OS/micro-OS and multi-threaded application development desired.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Development experience using Embedded Linux/ UC Linux (TimeSys Linux) a plus.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Software development for Analytical instrumentation, hand-held electronic devices, or gaming a plus.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Working knowledge of digital hardware a plus.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Working knowledge of peripheral driver development experience a plus.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Working experience with Texas Instruments TMS320C6748 DSP processor a plus.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Working experience with Freescale iMX a plus.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Ability to work well individually and as a member of a team.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Strong attention to detail.</li></ul>
