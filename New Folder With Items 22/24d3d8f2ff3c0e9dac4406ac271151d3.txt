<h2>Graphic Designer to help build our presence in the US</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div><div>
<p><i>Are you a graphic designer with strong communication skills?</i></p>
<p>Join us and become Graphic Designer for the US Onshore team where you’ll be responsible for designing all new communication material and for redesigning existing material. We’re expanding our presence in the US and have acquired a US onshore wind company which is now being integrated into the Group. You’ll work together with the local communication team as well as the Headquarters’ global design team situated in Denmark.</p>
<p><b>Your key tasks will be to</b></p>
<ul>
<li>apply the company’s design guidelines to the touch points in need of rebranding</li>
<li>conceptualise and design local campaigns for our brand</li>
<li>be a part of project teams within our internal team and external agencies</li>
<li>continuously develop CVI and guidelines</li>
<li>contribute to creating a flexible and dynamic brand that can be used by all our stakeholders.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Your competences include that you</b></p>
<ul>
<li>have a bachelor’s degree in creative communication or graphic design</li>
<li>are an excellent and creative designer who has experience with creating graphic design that clearly communicates the intended message</li>
<li>are a dynamic team player with strong collaboration skills</li>
<li>have a great understanding of different media – from digital to spatial</li>
<li>have a proactive and positive approach to your tasks.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Working at Ørsted</b><br>
Our vision is to create a world that runs entirely on green energy. This ambitious goal relies on maximizing the talents of a truly inclusive workforce that brings diversity of thought, perspective, knowledge, and background to our business.</p>
<p>To be the frontrunner in the green energy transformation, we invest significantly in innovation and empower our employees to help shape the renewable energy technologies of the future. We cultivate a collaborative, dynamic, and diverse work environment and encourage career-long learning and development so our people can realize their full potential.</p>
<p><b>Would you like to help shape the renewable technologies of the future?</b><br>
Send your application and portfolio to us as soon as possible and no later than November 6, 2019, as we’ll be conducting interviews on a continuous basis.</p>

<p><b>About Ørsted</b><br>
Headquartered in Denmark, Ørsted’s 6,300 employees develop, construct, and operate offshore and onshore wind farms, bioenergy plants, and provide energy products to its customers. Ørsted Offshore’s 2,300 employees have developed and constructed the largest portfolio of offshore wind farms in Northern Europe, and we’re expanding with international activities in the US and Asia-Pacific. For more information on Ørsted, visit orsted.com.</p>
