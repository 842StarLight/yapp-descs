<h2>Lab Drafter</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>New England Lab is seeking a Lab Drafter for our Woburn, MA and Raleigh, NC office. You will join a highly-skilled and experienced team. Providing a team approach with a focus on detail, precision, and meeting deadlines in a positive can-do team atmosphere is essential for the success of every project and our business as a whole. Proactive planning, coordination and communication are all essential to a successful project and building repeat clients.</p>
<p>
<b>Role</b></p>
<p>
The position of Lab Drafter is responsible for developing floor plans, elevations, sections and details of laboratory casework and furniture systems using AutoCAD 2D software. You will work closely with estimators to determine customer’s requirements/scope of work and assist/work alongside project managers to ensure accurate drawings. You will need to check product orders and proactively manage project schedules. Also assist PM’s with changes and additions to drawings as needed. A Lab Drafter must be able to multi-task, prioritize and work within a fast pace, quick turn-around and deadline oriented environment. The Lab Drafter plays a key role in each project and helps determine the overall success. You will provide detail drawings for customer’s approval, fabrication and installation. As an integral part of each project you are assigned to, you will be responsible for the following:<br>
</p>
<p><b>Duties &amp; Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Prepare thorough floor plans, elevations, sections and details of cabinet, countertop and lab furniture system layouts for client approval and fabrication</li>
<li>Work with an existing team to coordinate drawings and provide quality design work to client’s specifications</li>
<li>Support project managers, estimating and sales department with submittals</li>
<li>Interpret architectural specifications and drawings</li>
<li>Work with various vendors in determining the correct product to be used on a project</li>
<li>Document, organize and maintain project information</li>
<li>Follow drafting schedule and adapt to changes promptly</li>
<li>Monitor and respond to phone or email messages promptly</li>
<li>Ensure project scope is being met in line with the estimated price</li>
<li>Effectively communicate product or schedule concerns with other departments<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Technical Diploma or Associates degree required</li>
<li>2+ years of experience using AutoCAD (2007-2010) to produce detailed shop drawings or architectural drawings.</li>
<li>Recognize issues and address</li>
<li>Ability to communicate professionally via phone and email with internal and external clients</li>
<li>Ability to multitask amidst local distractions</li>
<li>Keen eye for detail and ability to visualize and understand 3-D dimensions</li>
<li>Work in a team environment and receive feedback</li>
<li>Experience with cabinetry or construction a plus</li>
<br>
</ul>
