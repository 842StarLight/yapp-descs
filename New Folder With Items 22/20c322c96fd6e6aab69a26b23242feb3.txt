Meals on Wheels People<br>
Meals on Wheels People enriches the lives of seniors and assists them in maintaining independence by providing nutritious food, human connections and social support. With the help of more than 5,500 volunteers, our nonprofit organization serves over 5,700 meals daily and more than 1.3 million meals each year across Multnomah, Washington, and Clark Counties. Meals on Wheels People is an EEO employer committed to the inclusion of people of color, lesbian, gay, bisexual and transgender people, women and people living with disabilities in our workplace and programs.<br>
<br>
General Summary<br>
The Special Events and Fundraising Manager provides leadership and support to the development department by serving as a key fundraiser with responsibility for successfully executing annual events, meeting Stride for Seniors peer-to-peer fundraising goals and leading efforts for Donate Dinner. The Special Events and Fundraising Manager will support the Director of Corporate Partnerships with the Clark County Luncheon and other events throughout the year. The Special Events and Fundraising Manager will manage all logistical aspects of each event as well as work in collaboration with the Volunteer Manager to recruit, train and retain volunteers for each event.<br>
