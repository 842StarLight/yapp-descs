<b>Software Engineer (Full Stack)</b><br>
<br>
<b>SKILL LEVEL:</b> 3+ years of experience<br>
<br>
<b>JOB DESCRIPTION</b><br>
<br>
Are you a motivated and highly talented software engineer with a passion for building amazing products and the drive to quickly learn new technologies? Do you love to solve real world problems and make customers happy? Do you want to be a key contributor in creating an awesome solution for a growing market? We need your help!<br>
<br>
We are looking for a talented and eager mid to senior level full stack software engineer with great skills in Java and Javascript (especially Angular) as well as solid experience with relational databases. You will join our product engineering team to help us build new features, fix defects, and help make our product the all around best offering in the pest control industry.<br>
<br>
Our office is located in Lehi, UT. We are only considering local candidates at this time.<br>
<br>
<b>RESPONSIBILITIES</b><br>
<br>
As a senior software engineer at Briostack, you will be responsible for the following:
<ul>
<li>Collaborate with other team members and architects to design and develop new product features</li>
<li>Research and quickly learn new technologies</li>
<li>Debug and fix issues with existing code</li>
<li>Address customer issues quickly, thoroughly, and professionally</li>
<li>Work as part of an agile development team</li>
<li>Create clean, well designed, testable, and efficient code</li>
<li>Write unit and integration tests for new and existing code to ensure quality and stability</li>
<li>Create new code or enhance existing code, working through the entire stack, to implement features and address bugs</li>
<li>Work closely with stakeholders to understand and design requirements and maintainable architectures for new features</li>
<li>Mentor, train, and guide junior engineers to help the entire team be successful</li>
<li>Occasionally interact with 3rd parties to understand and develop integrations, troubleshoot issues, and coordinate deliverables *</li>
</ul>
