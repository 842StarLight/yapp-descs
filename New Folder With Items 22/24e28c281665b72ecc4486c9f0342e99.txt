<h2>Technical Product Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>LinkUp is a Minneapolis-based job search engine with a singular vision of delivering perfect job market information to our clients and users. We do this through proprietary and sophisticated technology that allows us to index millions of jobs directly from company websites around the world every day. As a result of this unique approach to the online job space, we deliver compelling job search, candidate sourcing, and job market data solutions to job seekers, employer advertisers, and data clients.</p>
<p>We are searching for an experienced Technical Product Manager to help us plan, build, launch, and improve products that help our partners and clients leverage the incredible power of our labor market data.</p>
<p><b>Job description</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Take ownership of LinkUp's Labor Market Data product line</li>
<li>Work with a range of stakeholders, including prospects, clients, sales, marketing, support, and engineering to understand everyone's needs and how your product will address them</li>
<li>Define the vision and the set the roadmap for the product</li>
<li>Document business and technical requirements</li>
<li>Assist with the creation of documentation, sales materials, and marketing copy</li>
<li>Develop processes to improve the way that products get planned, built, supported, maintained, and improved</li>
<li>Assist in the quality assurance and acceptance testing process</li>
<li>Analyze the competitive landscape to understand where we currently fit and help determine the most effective strategic initiatives to pursue</li>
<li>Define, analyze, and drive key metrics that accurately measure success</li>
<li>Act as the Product Owner in an Agile Scrum environment</li>
</ul>
<p><b>To be successful, you'll need the following</b></p>
<ul>
<li>The ability to listen intently and understand each individual's perspective and needs</li>
<li>A knack for interrogating people to tease out all of the important information</li>
<li>The ability to synthesize mountains of disparate information into a coherent whole</li>
<li>A deep understanding of the nature of the trade-offs necessary when designing and building software</li>
<li>A natural talent for persuading people through compelling written and verbal communication</li>
<li>A grasp of technical topics broad and deep enough to be able to participate productively in technical discussions and to establish credibility with the engineering team</li>
<li>The ability to weight multiple competing priorities, take decisive action, and focus relentlessly</li>
<li>The ability to think and act to impact short-, medium-, and long-term goals as needed</li>
<li>5 years or more of progressive experience in and around product management</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Someone that would be a great fit</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Understands how software and data products are built and used</li>
<li>Understands the processes required to both build the right things and to build them correctly</li>
<li>Has an innate sense for how users most effectively interact with technical products</li>
<li>A familiarity with the technologies and techniques used to build web applications</li>
<li>A familiarity with various database technologies and their designed usage patterns</li>
<li>Has a degree or commensurate experience in science, technology, engineering, or math</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Bonus points if you have experience in and around</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Scraping technologies and techniques</li>
<li>Large scale data analysis</li>
<li>Capital markets, investment strategies, financial analysis</li>
</ul>

Along with some of our office perks such as stand-up desks, volunteer opportunities, and free snacks, we offer competitive pay, health / dental benefits, paid time off, 401k, and employee stock options.</p>
