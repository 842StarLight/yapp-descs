<h2>Operations Coordinator - Part Time</h2>

<p><b>Deliver the dream</b></p>
<p>At Lyman Lumber, we’re not only in the business of selling building materials. We’re also helping to create and deliver the promise of someone’s dream. That’s why our sales force is so important—they are the front face of our entire company. The work itself includes not only sales, but also following the order through the process to on-site delivery. In other words, it’s about building relationships with our customers and multi-tasking all that comprises the scope of a project. If you can think on your feet and like to be a part of a team that solves solutions, there’s lots of room for professional growth for you at Lyman. You can be sure that your own dreams are fulfilled along the way too.</p>
<p>For over 100 years, <b>Lyman Lumber of Chanhassen</b> has been the areas leader in framing and exterior products and services to the professional builder and remodeler. Our core focus on framing and exteriors has enabled us to become specialists in providing the products, services and business practices that you depend on.</p>

<ul>
<li>We are all about teamwork! All positions are hands on and we band together when necessary.</li>
<li>We support each other. We have local and corporate team members to help you along the way and partner on projects as appropriate.</li>
<li>We are a relaxed atmosphere, this is not a suit and tie environment.</li>
<li>We work hard. We are a metric driven organization and we are focused on keeping organized and on task.</li>
<li>We recognize the importance of each and every customer.</li>
</ul>

<ul>
<li>401(k) with company match</li>
<li>Closed holidays</li>
<li>Employee discounts</li>
</ul>
<p><b>A quick summary of the job:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>You will support the Transportation Supervisor by checking out drivers, scheduling delivery changes, and other DOT administrative tasks</li>
<li>You will prepare schedules and map out job sites to visit for site inspections</li>
<li>You will travel to job sites and inspect for: delivery readiness including; job site safety, access issues, and any other issues the Drywall delivery team may encounter.</li>
<li>You are able to work 4-5 hours per day Monday-Friday</li>
<li>Pay: $16 per hour</li>
</ul>
<p><b>We want you to join our team if you can check most of these boxes:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>You have some experience in a building materials or construction related industry (preferred not mandatory).</li>
<li>You are organized, very attentive to details, have good math skills, knowledge of building materials such as lumber.</li>
<li>You are have good computer experience using MS Office products and can quickly pick up proprietary software.</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>We thank all applicants for their interest, however, only those selected for interview based upon experience will be directly contacted.</i></b></p>
