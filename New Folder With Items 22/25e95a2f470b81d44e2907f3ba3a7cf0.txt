<p>Our clinical trials group is seeking thoughtful and self-motivated individuals to help spread the word about our offerings to clinical research organizations and the pharmaceutical industry. The successful candidate will have quarterly and annual booking goals and will be integral in monetizing industry relationships to help achieve Company revenue goals. Travel is required in this role.</p>

The candidate must have a proven track record in selling strategic, multi-product programs.</p>
<div>
<h5 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Essential Functions</b></h5>
<ul>
<li>Act as an outside sales representative for the Company’s products and services created for pharmaceutical pediatric clinical trials</li>
<li>Effectively navigate the CRO and clinical pharma landscape</li>
<li>Manage and grow specific assigned accounts by researching, developing, and validating new prospects and existing clients</li>
<li>Build strategic plans for your clients that focuses on education, relationship building and driving recruitment and retention</li>
<li>Understand the Company unique selling proposition and how that translates to each customer's needs to provide real, effective solutions</li>
<li>Work with other team members, as needed, to propose solutions and close deals</li>
<li>Delight clients and prospects by delivering exceptional customer service</li>
<li>Be a team player by sharing knowledge and open dialogue with peers to ensure ongoing education</li>
</ul>
<h5 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Candidate Requirements</b></h5>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree in Communications, Marketing or related field required, MBA and/or medical-related degree preferred</li>
<li>At least 5 years experience working in pharma sales, medical communications, medical education, or advertising; selling into the Clinical Trial space (CROs, direct-to-pharma Clinical Trial Managers) = jump to the front of the line!</li>
<li>Past experience independently building and managing successful relationships with clients in a sales capacity or at an agency</li>
<li>Past experience working in, and being successful in a start-up environment a huge plus</li>
<li>PROVEN track record of success – specifically exceeding sales goals on a quarterly and annual basis</li>
<li>Demonstrates a deep understanding of health care, pharmaceutical marketing, clinical trials, and trends in digital marketing; adequate therapeutic area knowledge</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills (written and verbal), including ability to speak with a variety of stakeholders including CROs, pharmaceutical Clinical Trial Managers, physicians, patients/families, health care organizations, and advocacy groups</li>
<li>Self-starter who is able to roll up his/her sleeves, take initiative, think creatively, work independently, and be efficient/productive</li>
<li>Outgoing and friendly personality and the ability to connect with a variety of stakeholders</li>
<li>Results driven, understands how to effectively navigate the sales cycle and selling environment, and most importantly how to EXCEED quarterly and annual sales quotas!</li>
<li>High degree of integrity and business ethics</li>
</ul>

<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>ABOUT JUMO HEALTH</b></h4>
<p>Jumo Health develops age-appropriate, educational resources for patients and caregivers for use throughout their medical journey. By working with providers, manufacturers, and advocacy groups, we ensure our resources are available at the moment of diagnosis, during a treatment regimen, or while participating in a clinical trial. With the belief that an informed patient is a capable patient, Jumo Health designs its solutions for outcomes.
<br>
With experience providing resources in more than 60 countries, 50 languages, and covering more than 200 topics, our mixed media solutions range from comic books to animated videos, are evidence based and peer reviewed, and pay careful attention to health literacy and educational attainment to ensure patients can understand and take the prescribed actions.
</p>
