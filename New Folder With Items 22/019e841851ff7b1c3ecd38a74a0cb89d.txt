<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Description
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>
<b>Title:</b> Platform Configuration Engineer / App Dev &amp; Support Engineer II<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Position Overview:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>The Platform Configuration Engineer will manage the deployment and release process of StrataWare to production and pre-production environments. Position will serve as a technical expert in deployment and support of StrataWare environments across all platforms.</li>
<li>Responsibility also includes identification of StrataWare application interdependencies, configuration of staging/QA/DR environments, risk mitigation, application server configuration, and configuration documentation. The successful candidate will also work to minimize platform discrepancies between multiple environments to ensure successful and repeatable deployments.
</li>
<li>The candidate will be required to be on call and will work on some nights and weekends in accordance to StrataWare deployment and release schedules.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Main Duties/ Responsibilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for all configuration changes of the StrataWare platform in production, DR, staging, and pre-production environments.</li>
<li>Deploy StrataWare suite of products in accordance to our established Service Level Agreements and committed schedules.</li>
<li>Adhere to the company established guidelines for Incidents, Problems, and Change Management processes (ITIL), and meet all compliance requirements (SAS-70).
</li>
<li>Performs all StrataWare deployments including PSWI’s, Patches, MSR’s, and Majors.</li>
<li>Responsible for working closely with Development in order to produce and formalize the StrataWare deployment plan for production, DR, staging, and pre-production environments.</li>
<li>Prepare, coordinate, and implement deployment outage schedules with other departments in order to minimize the impact of critical business services.</li>
<li>Seek improvement opportunities by analyzing the current deployment model and methodologies and collaborates with other department leaders to improve and streamline the StrataWare deployment process.</li>
<li>Act as a senior to Platform Configuration Administrators.</li>
<li>Recommends capacity change and optimization of the StrataWare platform based on careful observation and analysis.</li>
<li>Participate in discussions regarding implementation of complex deployment scenarios as well as their backup and roll back procedures.</li>
<li>Responsible for application servers uniformity; ensuring that application servers within any clusters in preview, DR, and production environments meet configuration requirements listed in build documentation.</li>
<li>Act as the center channel for deployment upgrade notifications, ETA, discovered problems, and post mortems.</li>
<li>Interfacing with the development team for quick resolution of defects.</li>
<li>Work nights and weekends when deployments are typically scheduled. Provide 24/7 support.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Preferred Qualifications:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>BS or BA degree preferred but not required and three or more years of work experience in a Technical/Deployment/Configuration role at a software company, preferably SaaS (Software as a Service).</li>
<li>Superior technical aptitude, written and oral communications skills and the ability to deal effectively with people.</li>
<li>Strong analytical skills with demonstrated problem solving ability.</li>
<li>Proven ability to work under pressure and make difficult decisions under a wide set of deployment conditions.</li>
<li>Understanding of the Software Development Life Cycle, Change Management, and ITIL processes.
</li>
<li>Hands on experience and working knowledge of distributed systems and environments.</li>
<li>Proficient with Stored Procedures, T-SQL, and scripting.</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills.</li>
<li>Working experience with Windows Server Operating Systems, IIS, and Microsoft SQL server.</li>
<li>Working understanding of high availability concepts such as load balancing, clustering, and cloud computing.</li>
<li>Blade Logic experience highly desirable.</li>
<li>Informatica experience highly desirable.</li>
<li>Service-Now/Sales Force applications.</li>
<li>Experience with EDI and FTP.</li>
<li>Ability to query and run application reports off of MSSQL.</li>
</ul>
