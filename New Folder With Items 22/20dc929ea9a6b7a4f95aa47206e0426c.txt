<h2>Apartment Property Manager</h2>

<p>Pinnacle is a national real estate provider that manages and invests in multifamily residential communities. As one of the nation’s largest third-party managers of multifamily housing, Pinnacle serves 32 states. Headquartered in Dallas, Pinnacle has offices in 16 cities across the nation.</p>
<p>We are growing in the South Florida area and looking for Property Managers!</p>
<p>As a Property Manager at Pinnacle, you’ll put your outstanding leadership and savvy business skills to work at one of the most respected apartment companies in a management opportunity that offers real leadership, innovation and support.</p>
<p>Our Property Managers are the cornerstone of our team. They are responsible for keeping our communities in the top-notch condition our residents have come to expect, building motivated and trustworthy teams who consistently deliver a notably higher level of service and maximizing the operating performance of our community. Be ready to be busy!</p>
<p>If you have demonstrated leadership skills, top notch sale/marketing skills, financial acumen and a commitment to quality service for your residents, Pinnacle is the place for you!</p>
