<p><i>You thrive in a fast-paced, collaborative environment. You're an independent, driven, and self-starting hunter who also enjoys working as part of a team. You're able to shift gears at a moment’s notice and have the tenacity and dedication to close deals.</i></p>

<p><b>
Intro to </b><b>inzpire.me</b></p>
<p>
inzpire.me is a successful tech startup that was born in the Nordics, with the ambition to conquer Europe and the US. We're on a mission to humanize advertising. By connecting brands with influential content creators on social media, we let marketers everywhere work with local content experts. We've already worked with some of the most famous brands in the world, like Coca-Cola, Yoplait, Nestlé, Monster Energy, and Unilever. Now, we're looking for a Client Partner who has what it takes to lead the company’s expansion into the US.</p>
<p>
We're a team of bold doers, we love learning new things, and we strongly believe that we'll make the most impact when we work and win together.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
The role in a nutshell</b></p>
<p>
You'll be part of a highly motivated and supportive Sales team. You'll have a key role in driving new business for inzpire.me in the US market. Your primary responsibility will be to maintain and grow inzpire.me’s revenue by working with existing clients, as well as acquiring new ones. To achieve this, you'll work closely with the CRO (Chief Revenue Officer), the Growth Team, and the Customer Success Team. Together, you'll develop existing and new client relationships using in-depth knowledge of your client’s business and thorough understanding of inzpire.me’s offerings.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Serve as an industry thought leader in influencer marketing (specifically) and digital marketing (generally). You'll drive client success by leveraging inzpire.me’s ongoing product and technology developments.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Accurately and effectively develop and manage a sales pipeline to prioritize business opportunities and resources.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Develop and broaden collaborative relationships by identifying contacts who manage budgets in order to close large deals.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Reach and exceed sales quotas while contributing to overall team and company goals.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Help create strategies for our international expansion to ensure successful adoption of inzpire.me across the globe, and in particular, the US.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Systematic and continuous prospection and lead generation through LinkedIn, phone, email, your network of contacts, and relevant events to fill your sales pipeline with qualified leads and meetings.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Work closely with the Customer Success team to drive success for both customers and inzpire.me.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>In this role, you can be based in NYC, LA, or San Francisco. You can also work out of Oslo, but you'd be required to travel to the US on a regular basis for customer and sales meetings.</p>
<br>
</li>
</ul>
