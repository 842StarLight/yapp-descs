Responsibilities:<br>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for strategic brand planning, financial planning, analytics and reporting, promotional efforts. including television, digital, mobile, outdoor, radio, podcast, event, and print advertising.</li>
<li>Establish performance specifications, cost and price parameters, market applications and sales estimates.</li>
<li>Utilize a test and learn approach to quickly identify what works and what doesnâ€™t, optimizing to the former.</li>
