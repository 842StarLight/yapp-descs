<h2>Customer Service Representative - OEM</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>GENERAL DESCRIPTION</b><b>
</b></p>
<p>Fenix Group and our brands are the industry’s most trusted problem solvers for specialty performance coatings. It is our mission to be the best solution for all our customers’ needs and to provide the best customer experience from start to project completion. Our strength is the efficiency with which we deliver our solutions from our Minnesota, Florida, Utah and Washington locations.</p>
<p>The Customer Service representative provides the best possible customer satisfaction to the organization’s customers by addressing and resolving customer inquiries and problems, processing orders, preparing service orders, tracking shipments, and resolving shipping discrepancies in accordance with the company policies and procedures.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES &amp; RESPONSIBILITIES</b><b>
</b></p>
<p>This list of duties and responsibilities is not all inclusive and may be expanded to include other duties:</p>
<ul><li> Work collaboratively with outside sales reps and other customer service personnel.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Process Sierra and 3M batch tickets using BatchMaster Enterprise, and request labels to be made for these products.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Process 3M orders through 3M unique computer program.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Establish and maintain positive working relationships with customers to ensuring the best possible customer satisfaction.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Process customer orders and verify availability and pricing via telephone or electronic means, including confirming order with our company field sales representatives.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Develop knowledge of multiple brand products and provide same customer service for each.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Set up and maintain new credit card customers in accounting system.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Handle various customer inquiries such as pricing, freight cost, shipping information, inventory, and the like.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Stay in contact with customer through shipping process and assisting with tracking deliveries. Provide accurate information regarding the status of open orders.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Facilitates customer complaints including arranging product return and issuing credit to maintain customer goodwill.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Assist with pour-off requests, lab fulfillments, and other custom requests.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Process small batch and lab requests.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Manage certain specialized customer accounts as assigned by the company. These accounts can be complex and require special attention.</li></ul>
<ul><li> May provide limited inside support to the sales representatives as needed.</li></ul>
<ul><li> May make outbound cold calls to potential customers.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Complete special projects as assigned.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Prepare daily shipping paperwork for Finance team, including matching packing slips with orders, calculate shipping costs, verify totals.</li></ul>
<br>

<p><b>CULTURAL TRAITS &amp; SKILLS</b><b>
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Self-motivated and driven – strives to deliver great results and continuous improvement. Gets things done</li>
<li>Proficiency in Microsoft Excel and Outlook</li>
<li>Must demonstrate strong, effective communication skills</li>
<li>Demonstrate a high level of attention to detail, strong organizational skills and ability to manage several projects at once</li>
<li>Ability to communicate clearly and concisely to all levels of the organization</li>
<li>The employee must occasionally lift and/or move up to 10 pounds.</li>
<li>Experience with Sage ERP or other manufacturing integrated ERP system a plus</li>

</ul>
