<h2>Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p>Meritage Homes is looking for a Marketing Manager to develop and manage our marketing program in the Atlanta Division. This position is based in our Atlanta Division office.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Develop, prepare and execute annual marketing plan with division leadership and Regional Marketing Director</li>
<li>
Design quarterly and monthly strategies and tactics to drive qualified traffic to support the sales effort for existing communities and new community openings</li>
<li>
Work with sales leadership as well as community teams to develop targeted marketing tactics to maximize traffic and conversion for individual communities based upon sales performance and lifecycle phase
</li>
<li>Create strategic pre-opening marketing plans to ensure successful community openings; oversee and coordinate all new community marketing activities including signage, messaging, interest list development, model merchandising, sales center installation and special events; responsibilities include planning, oversight and regular site visits
</li>
<li>Create and maintain collateral materials for new and existing communities</li>
<li>
Oversee and coordinate all consumer and agent events including planning, execution and results-tracking</li>
<li>
Effectively implement national agent program and create local strategy for agent outreach efforts including event planning and messaging</li>
<li>
Regularly participate in key divisional meetings including sales, finance, budget and marketing</li>
<li>
Develop sales meeting presentations</li>
<li>
Prepare annual and quarterly budget forecasts</li>
<li>
Manage local budget for new community startups, model furniture and monthly advertising</li>
<li>
Create, audit and maintain community web presence on Meritage Homes website</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in Marketing or related field preferred</li>
<li>
5-7 years of experience in marketing, preferably in homebuilding</li>
<li>
Experience leading others and effectively managing relationships with internal stakeholders and external marketing-related vendors</li>
<li>
Strong technical aptitude and intermediate computer skills with expertise in MS Office applications (Outlook, Word, PowerPoint and Excel), eblast systems, photo editing and website content management systems
</li>
<li>Ability to influence others and overcome objections</li>
<li>
Excellent written, verbal and interpersonal communication and presentation skills with keen attention to detail</li>
<li>
Team player with a demonstrated ability to work well within cross-functional teams</li>
<li>
People management experience</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
