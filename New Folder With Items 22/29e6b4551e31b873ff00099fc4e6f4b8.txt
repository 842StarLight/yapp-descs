What you'll get to work on
<br>--------------------------------
<br><br>
Humu's Nudge Engine® deploys thousands of customized nudges—small, personal steps—throughout organizations to empower every employee, manager, team, and leader as a change agent. Over time, our nudges grow increasingly aware of the timing, messaging, and motivational techniques that inspire individual employees towards action.

The details
<br>-----------
<br><br>
<b>Role and responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>As a member of the People Science team, you will ensure that Humu is built upon solid science and research</li>
<li>You'll use research and data to derive insights and drive behavior change within organizations</li>
<li>You'll partner with Humu's clients to deploy the product and ensure success alongside partners in Sales, Customer Success, and Product &amp; Engineering</li>
</ul>
<b>Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Advanced degree (PhD or Masters) in a field that uses analytics to understand human behavior (e.g., Organizational Behavior, Social/Personality Psychology, Behavioral Economics, Industrial/Organizational Psychology)</li>
<li>Passion for applying social science research to solve real-world organizational challenges</li>
<li>Experience analyzing data from surveys and experimental research in a business or academic context (applied experience is a plus, but not required)</li>
<li>Demonstrated knowledge of statistics and statistical software (e.g., R, SPSS, Stata)</li>
<li>Strong communication skills with a growth and learning mindset</li>
<li>Ability to translate data into insights for a non-technical audience</li>
<li>Ability to navigate ambiguity and solve problems in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>Experience in text and/or social network analytics is a plus, but not required</li>
</ul>
