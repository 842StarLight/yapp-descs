<p><b>
Purpose of Role:</b></p>
<p>
The Technical Support Analyst (TSA) is primarily responsible for communicating and representing the technical aspects of Wisdom Health products to consumers, veterinarians and clinic staff via phone calls, e-mails, trade shows and in-clinic training events. The TSA will also support the customer service staff with general inquiries received into the call center and e-mail system while providing the highest level of service and customer satisfaction by assuring prompt, courteous and accurate responses to customers as well as internal inquiries. This role requires the ability to exercise independent judgment and provide accurate product information while adapting and applying the guidelines specified by the R&amp;D team and the Customer Service Manager &amp; Senior Operations Manager. The primary correspondence universe will be the veterinary community and pet owners. This role may also be responsible for drafting technical product explanations to be targeted to both consumers and veterinarians. The TSA may also be involved in R&amp;D projects as needed and defined by the R&amp;D team.</p>
<p><b>
Principal Accountabilities</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Develop and hone scientific explanations for new and existing genetic products targeted towards consumers and veterinarians.</li>
<li>
Address technical customer service escalations in a timely, professional and accurate manner with minimal supervision from science team.</li>
<li>
Perform manual data-analysis of test results where necessary.</li>
<li>
Completely and accurately answer phone calls, emails and letters from vet clinics, pet owners and distributors in accordance with performance standards.</li>
<li>
Accurate handling of complaints and refund requests according to department policies and procedures.</li>
<li>
Ensure continuous follow-ups until issues are resolved.</li>
<li>
Appropriately classify and track pertinent subject matters, ensuring timely follow up.</li>
<li>
Participate in ongoing product training to learn and maintain strong knowledge of product information.</li>
<li>
Work on special projects as assigned by the R&amp;D and Customer Service teams.</li>
<li>
Take part in any outbound call campaigns when necessary.</li>
<li>
Represent Wisdom Health at key trade shows and provide expertise to consumers and trade.</li>
<li>
Present technical aspects of products to veterinary staff.</li>
