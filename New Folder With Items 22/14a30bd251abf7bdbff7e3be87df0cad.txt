<h2>Thrift Store Worker</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>This position is primarily responsible primarily to run the cash register, assist customers, properly price when needed and display merchandise throughout the store, safely drive The Salvation Army trucks to pick up donations and make deliveries and loading and unloading of the truck and handling trash disposal. Also assists with other operations of the thrift store donation processing as needed. This is a regular part-time position with a workweek expectation of 25 hours per week.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>This job description is not all inclusive. It is intended to identify the essential duties of the position. Other duties, responsibilities and tasks may be assigned by corps officer/administrator.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Maintain a positive work atmosphere by behaving and communicating in a manner that fosters good relationships with customers, donors, co-workers and supervisors. This includes but is not limited to such actions as: resolution of conflicts in a professional manner; courteous treatment of staff, visitors, volunteers and customer; and respect for others and their property. Professional and appropriate communication to and about customers, donors, co-workers and supervisors.</li>
<li>Run the cash register.</li>
<li>Keep the area near the cash register.</li>
<li>Clear racks and remove empty hangers.</li>
<li>Help maintain neat appearance on sales floor.</li>
<li>Assist customers as needed.</li>
<li>Answer the phone in a business type manner.</li>
<li>There are times individuals receive assistance through our voucher system.</li>
<li>Confidentially and dignity must be maintained</li>
<li>Discard items that are not of showroom quality.</li>
<li>Assist with keeping the store clean. It is understood that it is the job of all store workers to help keep the showroom and backroom clean, neat and safe.</li>
<li>Help count the deposit at the end of the day, when needed.</li>
<li>Drive assigned routes to each address and evaluate donations for appropriateness and accept or reject as needed applying store policies and guidelines as appropriate</li>
<li>Load donations in a safe, secure manner to minimize damage and breakage to donations and property.</li>
<li>Issue donor receipts for donated items.</li>
<li>Deliver donations to store and unload truck taking donations to designated areas</li>
<li>Deliver purchased merchandise as scheduled taking care to assure safe handling and avoiding damage to merchandise and property.</li>
<li>Fill truck with fuel and check and fill with oil and washer fluid as needed at designated filling stations.</li>
<li>Report all maintenance and performance concerns to supervisor as needed to assure that trucks run safely and efficiently.</li>
<li>Maintain interior of truck in a clean orderly condition.</li>
<li>Assist with bailing and loading of bales on trailer.</li>
<li>Assist with maintaining furniture floor – moving furniture to display in best manner.</li>
<li>Assist customers with loading large purchases.</li>
<li>Assist donor with unloading their vehicles.</li>
<li>Assist with cleaning as needed.</li>
<li>All other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2>
<div><div><div><ul>
<li>High School Diploma with a minimum of 4 years relevant experience.</li>
<li>Must have excellent interpersonal, oral and written communication skills, and the ability to work independently and/or in a team setting.</li>
<li>Possess a positive attitude, works well with others, be trustworthy, especially in confidential matters and be well organized and efficient.</li>
<li>Ability to use Salvation Army approved word processing, spreadsheet, data base management, and graphics/desktop publishing software packages.</li>
<li>Valid Driver's License. (Must meet the guidelines provided by the State of residence and the automobile insurance provider as pertaining to corps’ passenger vans/buses.)</li>
<li>Must pass The Salvation Army background checks and meet the MVR driver’s qualifications.</li>
<li>Must be willing to support the mission of The Salvation Army.</li>
<li>It must be understood by the incumbent that the provisions of Safe From Harm will apply upon commencement of employment and also understood that the required Safe From Harm training be satisfactorily completed within the first thirty (30) days of employment.</li>
</ul></div></div></div>
