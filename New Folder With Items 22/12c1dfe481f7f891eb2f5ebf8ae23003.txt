<h2>Preditor, E! Creative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div><div><div>
<div><div>
<b>OVERVIEW – ROLE PURPOSE</b><br>
We’re looking for a Creative bursting with ideas, get up and go and motivation to develop and deliver stand out creative ideas and executions. Must love what they do, be hungry to be all they can be, passionate about creating work that gets noticed and talked about. Must be comfortable developing ideas that work across all media; on air, digital and social media.<br>
<br>
Reporting into the Vice President Creative, the role will be responsible for making innovate work, striving to continuously raise the creative bar through continuously wanting to push themselves and others with their creative. Able to adapt to the changing media landscape, media consumption habits and creative trends in order to sell and promote our shows in unique, innovative and effective ways.<br>
<br>
<b>RESPONSIBILITIES</b><ul>
<li>
Visualises, creates and produces original and truly engaging promos, campaigns and short form content across all media, according to a brief, timeline and budget as agreed.
</li>
<li>Oversees projects from conceptualisation through the various stages of execution, to include: Research/ Copywriting/ Storyboarding/Producing and Directing/audio post and voiceover direction.
</li>
<li>Consults and develops projects with the Vice President Creative throughout the production process.</li>
<li>
Contributes towards building the brand identity.
</li>
<li>Is flexible in the role within the team and within the company, including working on marketing reels, Ad Sales spots and other Off-Air work.
</li>
<li>Diligent about production paperwork, music reporting and adhering to the On-Air working practices.</li>
<li>
Communicates throughout the stages of production to other departments within the company as required.</li>
<li>
Is aware of the need to complete promo treatment / script before starting editing and making sure these have sign off from key stakeholders.</li>
<li>
Able to confidently pitch and present ideas to the team and stakeholders.</li>
<li>
Discusses and listens to feedback constructively and acts on it.</li>
<li>
Offers a range of creative routes, where appropriate, as part of the creative development process.
</li>
<li>Is an engaged and enthusiastic team member, proactively sharing ideas and best practice.</li>
<li>
Be creatively ambitious, willing to take risks and have fun.
</li>
</ul>
</div></div>

<p><b>Desired Characteristics</b></p>
<div>SKILLS AND EXPERIENCE<br>
<ul>
<li>Proven experience in a similar role within a television broadcast, advertising or creative agency environment. Though talent trumps experience!</li>
<li>A passion for television in general and popular culture in particular.</li>
<li>A clear understanding of current trends in the industry, on channel, social media, OTT, etc.
</li>
<li>A real and genuine desire to go that extra mile. To break down the barriers of creativity across mediums, experiment, have fun and win some awards in the process.
</li>
<li>Experienced on Avid and able to self-operate at high level.</li>
<li>Ability to write strong and distinctive copy.</li>
<li>Creative thinker with the skills and imagination to produce eye-catching promotions, shorts and digital content.</li>
<li>Good knowledge and understanding of post-production, editing and audio design techniques.</li>
<li>The ability to work under pressure, to tight deadlines and within a prescribed budget</li>
<li>Diligent about production paperwork, music reporting and adhering to the communications department working practices.</li>
<li>Good interpersonal skills, both in contributing to the creative team, the overall Creative and Marketing teams and in representing the department throughout the company.</li>
<li>Fluent English essential (spoken and written)</li>
</ul>
