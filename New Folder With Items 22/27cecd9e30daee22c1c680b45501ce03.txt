<h2>Night Warehouse Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div><div><div>
<div><b>Night Warehouse Work</b></div>
<div>
Connecticut Distributors Inc. is hiring a Night Warehouse Worker. This position receives, stores, and distributes products within the warehouse by performing essential duties.
</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div><b>Must be able to:</b></div>
<div>
Receives and disperses product merchandise or point of sale materials in accordance with assigned storage methods and handling procedures.</div>
<div>
Safely sorts and places materials or items on racks, shelves, or in bins according to predetermined sequence such as size, type, year of vintage, or product code.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Assembles customer orders from stock based on customer invoice and places orders on pallets or onto conveyor for loading into trucks for delivery.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>Safely operate warehouse material handling equipment such as forklifts and cherry pickers.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>Must be able to learn all warehouse software platforms including SAP and Lucas voice pick software.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>Must be able to learn Contech case waving system.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>Meets product picking accuracy and productivity standards as established by management.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Prepares split case orders by selecting product from stock based on customer invoice and placing product into shipping containers.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Arranges and rotates product in accordance with prescribed procedures. Takes inventory of stored product as scheduled by company.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Examine incoming and stored product and report any deterioration and or damage.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
May perform quality control function of checker to ensure product being loaded into delivery truck matches customer invoice and picking order.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Responsible for loading product safely using proper truck loading techniques in accordance with routing manifest in a prescribed manner so as to avoid breakage or damage. Closes and securely seals truck after loading is completed.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Obeying all safety rules, maintaining clean work area and immediately reporting accidents to supervisor.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
</div></div></div>

<div><div><div>
<div><b>QUALIFICATIONS
</b></div>

EDUCATION and/or EXPERIENCE</b></div>

<div><b>
LANGUAGE SKILLS</b></div>

<br>
<div><b>
MATHEMATICAL SKILLS</b></div>

Ability to add and subtract two digit numbers and to multiply and divide with 10's and 100's.<br>

Ability to apply common sense understanding to carry out detailed but uninvolved written or oral instructions.<br>
</div>
