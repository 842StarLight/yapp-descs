<h2>Accounting Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>Aras is an innovative, global software company that provides advanced Product Lifecycle Management (PLM) software solutions to business customers worldwide including General Motors, Airbus, Microsoft, Gulfstream, Motorola, General Electric, Honda, and more. With many significant recent wins and our recent Series C financing, we are growing fast and need to expand our finance team.
<br>
<br>
The <b>Accounting Manager</b> will assist in building a first-class financial infrastructure that scales with the growth of the company in the US and internationally. We have operations in Switzerland, Germany, France, Japan and plan to continue to expand. This is a fantastic opportunity to make a first-hand difference in many areas of operation. The Accounting Manager will report to the Chief Financial Officer and is responsible for the day-to-day accounting operations of our US business.
<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities</b>
<ul>
<li>Manage the monthly financial close, including analysis of balance sheet and income statements, account reconciliations and issue resolution</li>
<li>Process customer sales orders, generate invoices (subscription and consulting) and determine revenue recognition</li>
<li>Hand-on processing of accounts receivable and accounts payable transactions, including employee expense reports
</li>
<li>Maintain fixed assets including monthly depreciation</li>
<li>Process sales tax filings</li>
<li>Manage the preparation for external audits, including audit-ready work paper reconciliations of balance sheet and general ledger accounts</li>
<li>Drive process improvements</li>
<li>Other duties and ad hoc projects as assigned</li>
</ul>
<b>Qualifications and Requirements</b>
<ul>
<li>Bachelors degree in Accounting or Finance</li>
<li>4+ years of accounting experience</li>
<li>Strong proficiency in computer skills and software applications including Excel and Word</li>
<li>Experience with SaaS or subscription model</li>
<li>Excellent organizational, time management and communications skills</li>
<li>Enjoys working in a fast-paced, agile and collaborative start-up</li>
<li>Strong attention to detail, and a commitment to excellence</li>
<li>Ability to work in an independent, self-directed manner as well as in a team environment</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
