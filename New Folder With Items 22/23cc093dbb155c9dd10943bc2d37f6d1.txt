<div><b>
Job Summary:</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Reputation.com is seeking a Senior Director of Demand Generation. In this role you’ll guide your team to develop multi-touch, integrated campaigns around specific products, industry verticals, and themes. Collaborating with teams such as Content Marketing, Customer Marketing, Events, Sales, and Product Marketing, you’ll oversee outbound and inbound efforts to programmatize and scale email nurturing, webinars, advertising, gated content, etc.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Your team’s purpose is to build and scale an engagement and conversion machine that gets the right message to the right prospect at the right time. As such, your team’s campaigns will be central to our marketing mission: to raise awareness about Reputation.com, to drive qualified leads that convert to pipeline, to facilitate a prospect’s journey to purchase, and to promote customer loyalty.
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>This position requires a mix of strong analytical skills, solid organizational and problem solving skills and the ability to balance multiple campaigns and projects, while thriving in a very fast-paced, early-stage environment with great people and killer solutions. The ideal candidate will have a demonstrated history of rapidly learning and understanding the client base, purchase decision process and dynamics, and how lead generation should cater for an outstanding customer experience end to end to end.</div>
Responsibilities:
<ul>
<li>Provide vision and direction for the team, define goals, and analyze results.</li>
<li>
Drive the creation of new campaigns and content across all stages of a buyer’s journey.</li>
<li>
Set a high quality bar for marketing campaign materials, and develop systems to maintain this bar as we scale.</li>
<li>
Ensure accurate campaign attribution tracking, create new tracking systems as needed, and evaluate campaign effectiveness.</li>
<li>
Inspire your team to take on new challenges, move quickly, run productive experiments, and feel accountable to grow the business.</li>
<li>
Work seamlessly with inside and outside sales to optimize opportunity conversion and drive progression through the sales funnel to close, including creation of demand generation programs "in a box."
</li>
</ul>Qualifications:
<ul>
<li>5+ years of demand generation leadership experience at a fast-growing technology company, ideally driving B2B leads and new business to a sales organization.</li>
<li>
Skilled in marketing automation and related software such as Marketo and Salesforce, and knowledge of market trends in digital marketing technology.</li>
<li>
A demonstrated history of creating and executing ROI-driven marketing programs, including but not limited to email, website, PPC, SEO, SEM, etc.</li>
<li>
Exceptional written and oral communications skills, effective in both formal and informal settings.</li>
<li>
Strong analytical skills and experience with reporting and data analysis.</li>
<li>
Program management skills, with high attention to detail.</li>
<li>
Proven experience in developing trusted relationships with sales organizations and the ability to present persuasively to key stakeholders.</li>
<li>
Ability to balance, prioritize and deliver excellent results under tight deadlines.</li>
<li>
Collaborative, flexible and open mindset</li>
</ul>
