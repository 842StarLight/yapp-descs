<h2>Custom Order Specialist 2</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Description
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>
<b>JOB OBJECTIVE:</b> To accurately and expediently process custom inquiries, quotations, custom orders, and all related documentation to ensure timely and efficient service to our customers; to build relationships with customers and sales team by providing value-added service in the custom/bulk processing of orders.</div>
<div>
<br>
<b>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES:</b>
</div>
<div>
<br>
1. Manage the collection of all pertinent information necessary for the creation of responses to requests for Custom Orders.</div>
<div>
<br>
2. Create accurate and timely Quotation and Custom Order documents.</div>
<div>
<br>
3. Enter and maintain orders in Promega’s ERP system.</div>
<div>
<br>
4. Process Custom Orders – work with other departments as needed to assure product supply to meet customer requirements and obtain pricing.</div>
<div>
<br>
5. Maintain databases/files relating to Quotes and Custom Orders.</div>
<div>
<br>
6. Assist in handling customer service needs after the shipment of a Custom Order (such as returns, invoice adjustments, requests for PPI’s, etc).</div>
<div>
<br>
7. Report any customer complaints per Promega’s Complaint Handling Procedures.</div>
<div>
<br>
8. Assist in resolving problems with the processing of Custom Orders.</div>
<div>
<br>
9. Build relationships with regions/branches and/or CAS team.</div>
<div>
<br>
10. Ensure that customers receive the best possible service on all Custom Orders.</div>
<div>
<br>
11. Maintain and promote a high quality Custom Order philosophy within the department.</div>
<div>
<br>
12. Understands and complies with ethical, legal and regulatory requirements applicable to our business.</div>
<div>
<br>
<b>
OCCASIONAL DUTIES:</b>
</div>
<div>
<br>
1. Prepare for and conduct Custom Order meetings.</div>
<div>
<br>
2. Work on special projects for management, as needed.</div>
<div>
<br>
<b>
MANDATORY QUALIFICATIONS:</b>
</div>
<div>
<br>
1. Bachelor’s Degree in related field or equivalent experience with a minimum of 2-5 years of relevant experience.</div>
<div>
<br>
2. Experience using Microsoft Office products and general PC based systems.</div>
<div>
<br>
3. Strong oral and written communication skills.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
4. Strong organizational skills and an attention to details.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
5. Ability to work independently, manage multiple projects, and meet deadlines.</div>
<div>
<br>
6. 2-5 years experience with and/or knowledge of production processes – Quotation, customer master, order entry, bill of material, shop order, lots, purchase requisitions, quality system, customer complaints, etc.</div>
<div>
<br>
7. Custom production environment experience.</div>
<div>
<br>
<b>
PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS:</b>
</div>
<div>
<br>
1. 1-4 years OEM or sales experience, in the biotech/life science market place.</div>
<div>
<br>
2. Demonstrated negotiating skills.</div>
<div>
<br>
3. Working familiarity with SAP ERP software.</div>
<div>
<br>
4. Project management experience.</div>
<div>
<br>
<b>
PHYSICAL REQUIREMENTS:</b>
</div>
<div>
<br>
1. Ability to use the phone, computer and other general office equipment.</div>
<div>
<br>
2. Ability to sit at desk for long periods of time.</div>
