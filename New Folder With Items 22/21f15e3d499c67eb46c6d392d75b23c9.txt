Job Summary: </b>Responsible for storing, picking materials, supplies, components, and units. This position will also be responsible for forklift use and maintaining warehouse paperwork.</div>
<div></div>
<div>Primary Responsibilities</div>
<ul><li>Perform a variety of routine warehouse duties related to storing, picking materials, supplies, components, and units throughout the plant and yard areas.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Duties require the use of forklifts, hand trucks, cranes, hoists, floor dollies, scales, and various other power and hand tools related to the warehouse operations.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Requires the use of various documents relating to warehouse activities, such as receiving documents, etc.</li></ul>
<ul><li>General area maintenance such as housekeeping, sweeping, etc.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintain all related paperwork, perform accurate counts, report discrepancies, maintain stocks in quality condition and ensure that all materials are safely stored, stacked, etc.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Report problems to supervisor or lead man and follow safety rules and regulations.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Have general overview of National Oilwell Varco Quality program.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Participate and lead continuous improvement efforts.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Perform other related tasks as requested by supervisor.
 </li></ul>
<ul><li>Comply with all NOV Company and HSE procedures and policies.</li></ul>
