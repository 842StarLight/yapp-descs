<h2>Business Office Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>The Chelsea at Warren has an opportunity for an experienced Full Charge Bookkeeper to join our dynamic team. Experience with A/R, A/P, Payroll, and Excel, required. QuickBooks and knowledge of HR is preferred. Team player needed for this leadership role.</p>
<p><b>SUMMARY: </b></p>
<p>Applies principles of accounting to record, maintain and analyze financial information and prepare financial reports.</p>
<p><b>DUTIES AND REPONSIBILITIES: </b></p>
<ul><li>Compiles and analyzes financial information to prepare entries to accounts, such as</li></ul>
<p>accounts receivable, general ledger accounts and documents business transactions.</p>
<ul><li>Documents, coordinates and implements accounts payable system; prepares check runs</li></ul>
<p>and hand written checks for the Executive Director’s signature.</p>
<p>i Disburses and reconciles petty cash for outside purchases to departments as needed.</p>
<ul><li>Audits residents’ agreements and prepares monthly billing to residents and/or responsible</li></ul>
<p>parties.</p>
<p>i Prepares all family medical leave and disability paperwork for employees.</p>
<p>i Handles all employee enrollment in health and welfare plans, life insurance, 401(k) and supplemented insurance plans.</p>
<p>i Reviews monthly billing for insurance enrollment and submits payments.</p>
<p>i Handles collection of outstanding accounts.</p>
<p>i Devises and implements systems for general accounting and bank reconciliation.</p>
<ul><li>Establishes, modifies documents and coordinates the implementation of accounting and</li></ul>
<p>accounting control procedures.</p>
<p>i Computes and runs bi-weekly payroll; reconciles payroll on arrival at the residence.</p>
<p>i Tracks and records employee attendance.</p>
<p>i Compiles and produces reports for review by the Executive Director and Chelsea Senior Living executives including, but not limited to, payroll analysis of actual hours versus budget, disability calculations, quarterly tax report, census report, ancillary ration report, cash projection report.</p>
<ul><li>Creates databases and tracks expenditure and revenues through the use of accounting software</li></ul>
<p>and spreadsheets.</p>
<p>i Maintains and protects the confidentiality of resident financial information at all times.</p>
<p>i May supervise concierge and front desk staffing.</p>
<p>i Other duties as assigned.</p>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS: </b></p>
<p>The qualifications listed below are representative of the knowledge, skills and/or abilities required to perform the job successfully. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</p>
<ul><li>Education/Experience: High school graduate with accounting knowledge, post high school education in bookkeeping/accounting; 3-5 years bookkeeping with computerized payroll experience preferred; some health care experience preferred.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Language Skills: Able to read, analyze and interpret financial reports and effectively explain financial matters to residents, residents’ families, vendors, regulatory agencies and staff.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Mathematical Ability: Able to work with mathematical concepts such as probability and statistical inference; able to apply concepts such as fractions, percentages, ratios and proportions to practical situations.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Reasoning Ability: Able to apply principles of logical or scientific thinking to a wide range of intellectual and practical problems.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Physical Demands: Able to use computer for extended periods of time; able to sit for long periods of time.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must have current CPR and First Aid certification.</li></ul>
<p>Excellent salary and benefits. Send resume and salary history EOE</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
</div>
