<h2>Territory Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Stellar Payment Systems, offering merchant services, payment processing, for Visa MasterCard, Amex and Discover throughout the US, Payroll and business software product is seeking to a Territory Sales Manager. This position is responsible for establishing a team and implementing short term and long term sales strategies, objectives and initiatives to attract new sales people and customer. Evaluate effectiveness of current sales programs. Recommend products and service enhancements to improve customer satisfaction and sales potential.</div>
<div>
<br>
Familiar with a variety of the field's concepts, practices and procedures. Rely on extensive experience and judgment to plan and accomplish goals. Lead and direct the work of others. A wide degree of creativity and latitude is expected. The successful candidate will understand what motivates customers to buy and know how to tap into those needs and desires in an effective way.<br>
