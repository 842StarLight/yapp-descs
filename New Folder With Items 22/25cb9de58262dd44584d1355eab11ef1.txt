<h2>Customer Service Agent - Call Center</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b><i>Broaden Your Horizon With Us!</i></b></p>
<p><b>South African Airways, an award winning premier carrier to Africa, is looking for a highly motivated professional to join its Customer Service Center Team as a Customer Service Agent.</b></p>
<p>The <b><i>Customer Service Agent</i></b> <b><i>– Direct Sales</i></b> is primarily responsible for direct sales and to provide full customer service and support to all direct consumers and travel trade based in North America, Canada and South Africa (overflow and after hour calls).</p>
<p><b>ENVIRONMENT: </b></p>
<p>Position is based at SAA’s Customer Service Center for the North America region, in Plantation.</p>
<p><b>MAIN RESPONSIBILITIES: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provide the best customer service to our passengers to ensure their satisfaction with our services.</li>
<li>Handle all calls from the general public and travel agent calling the Service Center to promote direct sales.</li>
<li>Create and fare quote International /Domestic Reservations.</li>
<li>Process Special Service Request (i.e. Unaccompanied minor, medical, wheelchair request etc). Action Queues to/from other stations.</li>
<li>Assist Call Service Centre Special Services with their workload.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>COMPETENCIES: </b></p>
<p><b>Client service orientation: </b>Identifies and responds to clients needs, delivering a high quality, professional service, striving to meet and exceed client requirements.</p>
<p>*<br><b>Attention to detail: </b>Achieves tasks through a continual concern for all areas and aspects of activities no matter how small; displays accuracy and observes finer details.</p>
<p>Drive and goal orientation<b>: </b>Demonstrates motivation and drive in striving to achieve goals; persistent in overcoming resistance and is determined; achieves results with enthusiasm, energy and personal commitment; maintains effectiveness over extended periods of time.</p>
<p><b>Following through: </b>Monitors watchfulness and attention over time, showing continued concern for all areas of activity; tasks are accomplished to defined standards and the client’s satisfaction; completes each and every task.</p>
<p><b>Learning orientation: </b> Demonstrates willingness to improve skills and knowledge through own resources in addition to participating in SAA Americas initiatives; achieves skills/development objectives with view to progressing further.</p>
<p><b>Dealing with pressure</b>: Maintains personal effectiveness and consistency of performance when working under personal and / or work related pressure / stress</p>
<p><b>Oral Communication: </b>Is able to speak clearly, confidently and understandably in order to discuss issues for clarification and explanation; able to answer queries in a concise manner; ability to liaise at all levels; inform and advise others.</p>
<p>Managing own performance<b>: </b>Monitors and reviews usefulness and steadiness of own performance in relation to plans, targets and standards when working under pressure/stress; pro-actively ensures that objectives are achieved to required standards.</p>
<p><b>Managing relationships: </b>Shows an understanding of internal and external clients needs and requirements; is conscientious and responsive; shows honesty and integrity in dealing with people.</p>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>High School Degree required.</li>
<li>Minimum two (2) years airline and/or travel industry experience.</li>
<li>Proficient knowledge of computer reservation system(s) and ticketing.</li>
<li>Basic knowledge of Windows based software including Microsoft Outlook, Word, and Excel.</li>
<li>The ability to learn and adapt to the new products and procedures that the company may introduce.</li>
<li>The ideal candidate should be self-motivated, hands-on, responsive, and customer-focused with solid communication and interpersonal skills. A sense of urgency is key to providing optimal service.</li>
</ul>
