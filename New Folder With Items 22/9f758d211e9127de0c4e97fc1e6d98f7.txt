<h2>Production Floater</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>PURPOSE</b></p>
<p>To assist production staff as needed, including working in the riveting department, leather department, fabric cutting department and quality control department based upon the needs of the business. This involves working with production staff to make sure quality and production standards are met in a timely, accurate and safe manner.</p>
<p>Essential Duties and Responsibilities include the following: Other duties may be assigned.</p>
<p><b>KEY RESULTS AREAS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Uphold stringent quality production standards of all Duluth Pack manufactured products. Attention to detail and following standard production standards is critical.</li>
<li>Work as a team member in a changing environment; promote flexibility and teamwork by being a team builder and encouraging change.</li>
<li>Identify any bottlenecks in the production flow and notify Production Manager/ Supervisor to help resolve issues in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Establish and maintain excellent working relationships with all levels of staff within the organization, as well as exhibiting excellent customer service in any interactions with customers.</li>
<li>Participate in the design and implementation of lean manufacturing principles and techniques to streamline the production process.</li>
<li>Monitor levels of raw materials and notify Production Manager/Supervisor of re-order needs to proactively meet production demands.</li>
<li>Assist with researching and resolving any recurring or significant inventory variances of raw materials.</li>
<li>Assist with general maintenance, repair and upkeep of production equipment.</li>
<li>Research, development and design of new products and enhancements to existing products.</li>
<li>Maintain compliance with all internal and external safety rules and regulations.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned by Production Manager/Supervisor to meet business needs.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>PHYSICAL DEMANDS </b></p>
<p>The physical demands described here are representative of those that must be met by an employee to successfully perform the essential functions of this job. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</p>
<p>While performing the duties of this job, the employee is regularly required to sit; use hands to finger, handle, or feel; and talk or hear. The employee is regularly required to stand; walk; reach with hands and arms; climb or balance; and stoop, kneel, crouch, or crawl. The employee must lift and/or move up to 25 pounds and occasionally lift and/or move up to 50 pounds. Specific vision abilities required by this job include close vision, distance vision, color vision, peripheral vision, and ability to adjust focus.</p>
<p><b>WORK ENVIRONMENT </b></p>
<p>The work environment characteristics described here are representative of those an employee encounters while performing the essential functions of this job. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</p>
<p>While performing the duties of this job, the employee is frequently exposed to moving mechanical parts and risk of electrical shock. The noise level in the work environment is usually moderate. The work environment will be stressful at times.</p>
<p><b>JOB QUALIFICATIONS</b></p>
<p><b>Required Education and Experience</b></p>
<p>High school diploma or equivalent education, preferably with prior experience in a manufacturing setting, or a combination of education and experience providing equivalent knowledge.</p>
<p><b>Knowledge, Skills and Abilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Knowledge of general manufacturing principles and their practical application to the manufacturing and retail sales industry.</li>
<li>Knowledge of and skill in using a personal computer and related desktop software including Microsoft Excel, Word and Dynamics GP/HMS manufacturing software.</li>
<li>Ability to write legibly and communicate effectively with all levels in the organization, including skill in preparing written materials such as correspondence and reports to meet purpose and audience.</li>
<li>Knowledge of the various services, products, policies and procedures of Duluth Pack and knowledge of the various departments within the company and how their respective functions interrelate to the Production department.</li>
<li>Ability to prepare work in timely and accurate fashion and to keep work area in a neat and presentable manner.</li>
<li>Ability to apply common sense understanding to carry out instructions furnished in written, oral or diagram form.</li>
<li>Ability to address problems in a professional manner and work toward an appropriate solution.</li>
<li>Ability to communicate with unhappy and/or dissatisfied customers and/or co-workers</li>
</ul>
<p>and work toward a calm and reasonable solution.</p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to communicate thoughts and ideas to others, both verbal and written.</li>
<li>Ability to read and interpret documents and manuals and apply this understanding to job functions.</li>
<li>Ability to make a decision based upon the best information available at the time and to react as new information is presented to adjust decision making accordingly.</li>
<li>Ability to work independently and also as a productive member of a team.</li>
</ul>
