<h2>Sales Development Representative</h2>

<div>
<p><b>About Datameer:</b></p>
<p>
Datameer is a well-funded, global software company headquartered in San Francisco with global offices across Europe backed by top tier investors including KPCB, Redpoint Ventures, ST Telemedia, and Next World Capital among others. Our customers include the who's who of the fortune 100 and the list is growing!</p>
<p>
We are an agile enterprise big data platform that fuels faster, trusted data-driven insights anywhere. Datameer's advanced data preparation, exploration and governance functionality makes it easy to transform big data into curated data sets to feed any analytics initiative. Using a hybrid architecture that spans on-premises and the cloud, Datameer helps organizations deliver new insights in days and operationalize them immediately, increasing the agility and responsiveness of the analytics that drive the business. Datameer is available on Amazon Web Services as well as Microsoft Azure.
</p>
<p><b>The Role:</b></p>
<p>
We are looking for a motivated and enthusiastic Business Development Representative to come aboard! Our BDRs will work hand in hand with our marketing and sales to generate leads and build customer pipeline. This is an entry-level role with a dedicated growth path and the training to be a great technical sales professional. We are looking for self-starters with a passion for technology and people.</p>
<p><b>
Key Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>
As a Business Development Representative at Datameer you will</p>
<ul>
<li>Generate new sales leads through a mixture of outbound phone and email prospecting, following up on marketing campaigns and inbound inquiries</li>
<li>Schedule meetings and product demos for Account Executives</li>
<li>Accurately enter, update, and maintain daily activity and prospect information in hubspot</li>
<li>Work directly with marketing to discover opportunities from leads, and set appointments from those leads.</li>
<li>Use of strong selling and influencing skills to set up qualified appointments.</li>
<li>Understand our Datameer solution to guide prospective customers and provide them with a great introduction to Datameer.</li>
<li>Leverage taught sales techniques to maximize customer interactions.</li>
<li>Log, track, and maintain outbound and inbound activity.</li>
<li>Work closely with Sales Directors and attend customer meetings as required.</li>
<li>Attend sales meetings, local trade shows to keep current with technology.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Skills:</b></p>
<p>
Our Ideal Business Development Representative Will Have</p>
<ul>
<li>Strong in-person, phone, and written customer communication skills.</li>
<li>Resilient. Can smile in the face of adversity and talk to anyone.</li>
<li>Must be able to interact and communicate with individuals at all levels of the organization.</li>
<li>Understanding of workflow systems and their application to customer business process improvement.</li>
<li>Ability to make formal and informal presentations to staff and clients.</li>
<li>Ability to prioritize work assignments and shift work efforts based on the needs of the department or business goals.</li>
<li>Ability to manage time effectively, work independently, and be self-motivated.</li>
<li>Prior track record of achievement in positions with significant accountability.</li>
<li>Interested in pursuing a career in technology.</li>
<li>Ability to thrive in fast-paced startup environment.</li>
<li>Pro-active, independent thinker with high energy/positive attitude.</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication, presentation, and relationship management skills.</li>
</ul>
<p>We are located in South Park near AT&amp;T Ballpark, minutes from public transportation. We offer catered lunches, lots of snacks, plenty of coffee, and a collaborative friendly work environment.</p>
