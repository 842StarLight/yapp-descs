<h2>Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>TITLE: Account Manager<br>CLASSIFICATION: Exempt-Salaried<br>DEPARTMENT: Agency Services<br>REPORTS TO: Director of Agency Services</p>
<p><b>POSITION DESCRIPTION </b></p>
<p>The Account Manager is responsible for facilitating and expanding client relationships, while also managing/overseeing ongoing account activities. They interact directly with the client and provide support to any additional associates working on the same accounts. They are expected to work with all agency departments to manage client projects, while ensuring a quality and on-time deliverable. He or she is also responsible for the execution of the internal communications strategy for SMGII as directed by the Director of Agency Services.</p>
<p>This position requires open lines of communication between team members, departments and clients, strong attention to detail and organizational skills, expert ability to multi-task, ability to follow projects from beginning to end adhering to tight deadlines/due dates and demonstrate excellent communication skills. Strong business acumen as well as the ability to maintain creative, production and technical goals set forth by teams and clients is a must.</p>
<p><b>PRIMARY RESPONSIBILITIES </b></p>
<ul><li>Main contact for clients; responsible for managing day-to-day interactions and execution of projects from start to finish for new and existing accounts</li></ul>
<ul><li>Create job jackets, develop creative briefs, proof copy, review design at all stages, monitor job progress and invoice utilizing Advantage software</li></ul>
<ul><li>Provide daily support for current SMGII promotional and marketing programs and other agency projects; including scheduling of resources within Advantage, provide ongoing status updates and ensure all deliverables are met according to timeline</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manage multi-channel marketing campaigns including, but not limited to email, social, PPC, SEO, display, website, newspaper, magazine, outdoor, in-store collateral, direct mail, merchandising, special events, promotional products and partnerships.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Attend internal status meetings, capture and record notes to ensure action items are completed promptly; follow-up as necessary</li></ul>
<ul><li>Demonstrate a high level of communication between team members and departments, identifying opportunities, understanding client objectives, listening to clients, strong attention to detail, consistent follow-up, the ability to schedule multiple activities and a desire to exceed expectations.</li></ul>
<p><b>OTHER RESPONSIBILITIES </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Review agency presentations, decks, materials, promotional items, etc. for style, consistency and spelling</li>
<li>Light to moderate administrative tasks as needed (i.e. binding, shipping, errands, inventory management)</li>
<li>Contribute to concept development for meetings and campaigns</li>
<li>Procurement of promotional products and services as needed; strong understanding of purchase orders and invoice processing is required</li>
<li>Assists Director of Agency Services with various agency tasks</li>
</ul>
<p><b>WORKING CONDITIONS </b><br>Office environment with some travel required</p>
<p><b>KNOWLEDGE AND EXPERIENCE REQUIREMENTS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Undergraduate degree. Preferably in Business Administration or Communications</li>
<li>3-5 years in communications, marketing, sales or general business</li>
<li>To be successful, you must be highly organized, a multi-tasking problem solver, flexible and able to work well with others, but firm on deadlines</li>
<li>Must possess strong computer skills, including proficiency in Microsoft Word, Excel, and PowerPoint</li>
<li>Must demonstrate sound professional judgment and a high degree of poise and professionalism when interacting with internal and external clients or prospects</li>
<li>Strategic thinker</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills</li>
<li>Ability to work independently and multi-task, and prioritize a high volume of projects and tasks at one time</li>
</ul>
