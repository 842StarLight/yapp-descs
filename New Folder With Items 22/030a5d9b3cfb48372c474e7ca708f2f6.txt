<p><b>JOB DESCRIPTION</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<div>
<b>
Summary/Objective</b><br>
Provide auxiliary operational support for technical support organization including routing, dispatching, GPS reporting and work order management. This position is responsible for attaining maximum utilization of field technician resources through daily dispatch of service requests.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<p><b>
Essential Functions</b><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
Schedule and dispatch workers, work crews, equipment, or service vehicles to appropriate locations according to customer requests, specifications or needs, using email, text, ticketing or telephones.</li>
<li>
Arrange for necessary repairs in order to restore service.</li>
<li>
Relay work orders, messages, and information to or from support, supervisors, and stores using email, text, ticketing or telephones.</li>
<li>
Prepare daily work and run schedules.</li>
<li>
Receive or prepare tickets.</li>
<li>
Communicate with customers as necessary; informing of incident progress and notifying them of impending changes or agreed outages.
</li>
<li>Monitor personnel and/or equipment locations and utilization in order to coordinate service and schedules.</li>
<li>
Record and maintain files and records of company fleet van maintenance, work or services performed, inventory, and other dispatch information.</li>
<li>
Work with project coordinator and field technician supervisor to manage and schedule new store buildouts, call center buildouts, and remodels.</li>
<li>
Utilize Excel and other software/systems/paperwork to track and maintain ticket quantities, equipment lifecycles (working and non-working machines), and hours needed to complete a project. Track and determine required time needed for specific project completion by a field technician.
</li>
<li>Execute and utilize reporting to ascertain man hours, travel time, vehicle mileage/repair, etc. Apply report information to build metrics that are communicated monthly/quarterly as needed.
</li>
<li>Escalate service requests that cannot be scheduled within agreed service levels.</li>
<li>
Report the utilization of IT Support resources and successful completion of service requests to the Field Tech Operations Supervisor.</li>
<li>
Enter all work as service tickets into Helpdesk Ticketing System.</li>
</ul>
