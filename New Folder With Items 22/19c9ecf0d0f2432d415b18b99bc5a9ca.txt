<br>
<p>
As the outdoor industry leader, Camping World is always looking for new and better ways to enhance your RV and outdoor experience. Camping World is a big place, just like the great outdoors. We make it our mission to equip you for it. We've got an awesome team already, but there's always room for more!</p>
<br>
<p>
We are currently looking for an Escalation Representative for our Island Lake office. In this newly created role, the selected candidate will handle inbound calls, emails, and chats relating to any concerns, challenges, or issues one of our customers may be experiencing. Your role is to understand and document the customers concerns and help find resolution for the customer. Providing a high level of customer service is the primary function of this role.</p>
<br>
<p>
Essential Job Functions:</p>
<ul>
<li>You are responsible for the intake and routing of customer complaints that come into the organization through various channels (phone, email, social media, etc.) and logging the case in Salesforce</li>
<li>You will contact customers with issues and ensure the customer feels heard and seeks to understand issues/challenges as they work toward resolution</li>
<li>You are good listener and seek to understand the full scope of a customer's issue/challenge/concern</li>
<li>Provide exemplary customer service on every customer engagement, including but not limited to: calls, emails, social media and any other type of communication; paying attention to detail and responding with first class customer service</li>
<li>When possible/appropriate, you will offer a resolution on the first call. In more complex matters, you will research our systems and partner with our location to gather all relevant data and attach it to the Salesforce case so that a Lead/Manager can partner with the proper internal resources to execute an appropriate remedy for the customer</li>
<li>You will be responsible for keeping accurate records of all interactions with customers in Salesforce. You will serve as an evangelist for CW and the outdoor lifestyle we support</li>
<li>This role can be either Full-Time or Part-Time</li>
</ul>
<p>Education/Years of Experience:</p>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma as appropriate for age, or working towards a high school diploma, GED or equivalent is required; any additional school preferred
</li>
<li>2 to 4 years of experience with escalations/customer complaint resolution preferred</li>
</ul>
<p>Other Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>Strong computer skills with the ability to effectively maneuver in a Windows environment and through Microsoft Office products; ability to learn new software as needed</li>
<li>Minimum of 45-50WPM</li>
<li>Experience working in Salesforce or other enterprise level CRM systems preferred</li>
<li>Have a passion for the outdoors with personal experience and proven knowledge participating in outside activities like RVing, Camping, etc.</li>
<li>Proven ability to engage customers and deliver unparalleled customer service every day; possess a positive, can-do attitude and enjoy connecting and interacting with customers</li>
<li>Ability to communicate, both verbally and written, clearly and concisely in English is required</li>
<li>Must be willing to work a flexible schedule based on business needs, including evenings, weekends and holidays as needed</li>
</ul>
