<h2>Accounts Receivable A/R Clerk</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>About Us:</b><br>
Groundwork Coffee specializes in batch-roasted, certified organic coffees and artisanal teas. From our humble roots as a rare book store and cafe back in 1990, Groundwork would go on to become one of the first certified organic coffee roasters in Southern California, as well as the largest organic coffee roaster in Los Angeles. A truly local pioneer in fair trade and fairly traded coffee and tea sourcing, we remain committed to sustainable business practices, including maintaining direct relationships with growers and importers who promote responsible coffee and tea production. From the ground up, Groundwork is steadfast in its commitment to the values of quality, sustainability and community.
<br><br>
<b>About You:</b><br>
We are looking to hire an upbeat, detail-oriented, and highly organized Accounts Receivable Clerk to join our growing team and jump-start their career. You’ll wear many hats and be exposed to various facets of the coffee business from sourcing and production to retail and wholesale distribution as you take on the responsibility of ensuring accurate and timely billing of goods and services sold by the organization. If you love a great a cup of coffee (or tea), embody a positive attitude, with a keen ability to problem-solve and prioritize workloads, then please apply today!
<br><br>
<b>What You’ll Do:</b><br>
In this entry-level accounting role, you will collaborate cross-functionally and with the other members of the Admin/Finance department to resolve A/R issues, update and reconcile sub-ledger to G/L, process A/R invoices and payments in a timely, accurate, and professional manner, and resolve invalid customer chargebacks.
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Assist customers and Customer Service Reps with questions/issues;</li>
<li>Contact customers w/ past due balances; collect / resolve past due amounts in a timely manner;</li>
<li>Download bank feed transactions to record bank transaction in SAP.</li>
<li>Ensure correct entry of invoices/credits (understanding of basic accounting is preferred);</li>
<li>Ensure new customer accounts are set up correctly (terms, billing info/contact info, credit card if necessary);</li>
<li>Perform credit checks on prospective customers;</li>
<li>Enter distributor/grocer chargebacks into ERP system; dispute invalid chargebacks and collect money;</li>
<li>Generate weekly chargeback reports to review with the Grocery sales team</li>
<li>Maintain chargeback reporting for Sales team;</li>
<li>Process Customer Payments (checks/credit cards/cash) into ERP system;</li>
<li>Record journal entries for daily store revenues;</li>
<li>Review and confirm PODs (proof of delivery);</li>
<li>Review past due food service accounts with sales team;</li>
<li>Review past due wholesale customer accounts;</li>
<li>Contact customers w/ past due balances, in order to facilitate payment;</li>
<li>Send monthly AR aging reports to sales team;</li>
<li>Send weekly AR aging reports to partners;</li>
<li>Assist accounting team with duties and project-based assignments as needed;</li>
</ul>
<b>What It Takes To Rock This Role:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>AA or BS/BA degree in Accounting, Business Administration, Public Administration or related field preferred</li>
<li>2+ years of experience in an A/R role preferred</li>
<li>Solid understanding of Accounts Receivable (AR)</li>
<li>Strong organizational skills</li>
<li>Microsoft Excel experience and PC Proficiency</li>
<li>Knowledge of ERP software programs for processing invoices and recording data; knowledge of SAP Business One preferred.</li>
<li>Accurate Data Entry skills</li>
<li>General math and arithmetic skills</li>
<li>Thoroughness and proven ability to follow standard procedures</li>
<li>Articulate customer service and interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Willingness and ability to learn</li>
</ul>
