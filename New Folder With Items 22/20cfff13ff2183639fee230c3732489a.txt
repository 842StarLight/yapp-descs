<h2>Solution Architect</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>***No Sponsorship will be provided***</b></p>
<p><b>Job Summary: </b></p>
<p>As the Solutions Architect you will lead management, coordination and completion of logistics technology projects, to meet and exceed internal objectives and customer requirements on-time and on-budget. You will develop operational processes, set deadlines, assign responsibilities, and provide regular status of project progress. Much of this individual’s success will be based on their ability to work closely with customers and internal stakeholders to deliver business value.</p>
<p><b>Role Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Works with project team to ensure smooth and efficient implementation and integration between technical and functional system requirements</li>
<li>Assists Project Managers in planning efforts</li>
<li>Provides advice to customers regarding industry best practices and process methodology</li>
<li>Provides non-technical support during the "go-live" period</li>
<li>Provides primary user support and verifies that the system provides the required business functions and maintains data integrity</li>
<li>Communicates and/or supports the business cases to internal product development team about trends for future product functionality and enhancements.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Conducts/leads business process analysis or design/construction phase of the project that leads to writing functional specifications</li>
<li>Creates and modifies procedural and system documentation throughout the project</li>
<li>Creates detailed tasks on overall project plan (i.e. during sales cycle, after the project plan is complete, or based on enhancements)</li>
<li>Develops and executes test scripts to ensure software consistently meets the customer's business objectives</li>
<li>Develops functional specifications for modifications, interfaces, reports and screens that meet the customers' business objectives and are of sound design</li>
<li>Ensures knowledge transfer is conducted with key customer project team</li>
<li>Recognizes and highlights incremental revenue opportunities with existing customers / projects.</li>
<li>Understands how the solutions within area of focus can be configured and delivered to better facilitate customer business processes (ensuring ability to design, execute, and deliver)</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualification &amp; Skill Requirements: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>This position requires approximately 25% travel.</li>
<li>5 - 7+ years of hands-on experience in warehousing and distribution or as a consultant providing WMS (Warehouse Management System) solutions for customers</li>
<li>3+ years of specific experience working with a WMS like Manhattan Associates, JDA, Oracle, Infor, or 3PL Central.</li>
<li>Strong business analysis skills, understanding and usage of statistical algorithms, inventory optimization concepts, awareness of tactical planning on overall supply chain required</li>
<li>Strong verbal, written and interpersonal skills required</li>
<li>Strong domain knowledge to understand how the supply chain software within the area of focus can be configured and delivered to better facilitate customer business processes.</li>
<li>Experienced in defining problems, collecting data, establishing facts and drawing valid conclusions</li>
<li>Ability to work independently and be effective/proactive in a relatively unstructured environment</li>
<li>Ability to develop and maintain strong, productive relationships with customers, partners and consultants</li>
<li>Advanced degree in Supply Chain, Operations, Logistics or Operations Research preferred. A combination of Bachelor’s degree and relevant industry experience may be considered in lieu of advanced degree</li>
</ul>
