<p>Company &amp; Position Summary</p>
<p>Rand McNally has been helping people discover, map and navigate for over 160 years. While most people know us for our best-selling Road Atlas line, we are also the leading provider of truck and RV navigation devices, one of the fastest-growing mobile fleet management companies and a key education supplier with products in more than 90 percent of US schools. We are currently experiencing significant growth in our Commercial Transportation Business and looking to scale accordingly!</p>
<p>The <b>Sales Account Representative</b> will be in charge of <b>closing new software</b> as a service and fleet management solutions business opportunities. This individual will have an <b>understanding of the transportation industry</b> or relevant experience with <b>SaaS (Software as a Service) sales</b>. Reporting to the Vice President of Sales, the selected candidate will pursue, develop and build subscription and technology device revenue within the Commercial Transportation business unit of Rand McNally. This highly visible position will be responsible for the <b>execution of a B2B direct sales strategy</b>. The Sales Account Representative will be responsible for <b>closing warm leads generated</b> by the business development team. The candidate will also be responsible for <b>forecasting, cross company collaboration</b> and <b>customer relationship management</b> during the installation phase of new customer acquisitions. The candidate’s primary responsibility will be in closing B2B sales opportunities for long haul fleet management solutions, local fleet management solutions, and mobile asset tracking solutions. See https://fleet.randmcnally.com/ for more information on the products and services.</p>
<p><b>Candidate Requirements</b><b>: </b></p>
<p>A successful candidate will be a proven leader with well-developed <b>prospecting and sales presentation skills</b>. The ability to identify needs, <b>create presentations</b> specific to the business needs identified, manage leads, conduct WebEx demo’s, and achieve and surpass revenue and subscription targets. Ideally the candidate will have experience in B2B <b>technology related sales</b> and/or transportation and fleet management experience. The individual must have strong communication and interpersonal skills. In addition, must be an effective negotiator, problem solver and internal collaborator to positively impact business. The candidate must have strong organization skills and a financial aptitude. Experience with SalesForce.com, and a history of achievement in direct sales is required.</p>
