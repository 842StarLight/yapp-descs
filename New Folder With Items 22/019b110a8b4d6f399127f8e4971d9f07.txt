<h2>Field Clinical Manager - Chicago</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div><div>
<p>The primary objective of the Field Clinical Manager is to provide field-based clinical and technical
</p>
<p>training/support for the Bioness’ StimRouter product line. The candidate will be responsible for training
</p>
<p>health care professionals in the use of the medical devices and support associated surgical procedures;
</p>
<p>programming devices and providing technical expertise in the use of the StimRouter/Implantable product
</p>
<p>line. The candidate will perform these duties and develop the necessary training skills in compliance with
</p>
<p>FDA regulations, Good Clinical Practices.<br>
</p>
<p><b>SPECIFIC DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:
</b></p>
<p>The responsibilities include (but are not limited to):
</p>
<ul><li>Surgical case coverage.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Follow-up support and troubleshooting of Implantable Products.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Conducting patient programming’s.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Conducting required training for new and existing centers.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Represent StimRouter from a clinical perspective at tradeshows, local meetings, group dinners etc.
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p>Key Responsibilities<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><i>Clinical and Technical Support
</i></p>
<ul><li>Represents Bioness during surgeries, implants, and device programming.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Demonstrates expertise in all aspects of implanted medical devices and troubleshooting
</li></ul>
<p>techniques.
</p>
<ul><li>Provides support and follow-ups in hospitals and clinics.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Receives technical inquiries by telephone.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Researches solutions to questions or problems.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Provides on-call support as needed for surgeries, reprogramming, troubleshooting, follow-up, etc.<br>
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p><i>Educational/Research Support
</i></p>
<ul><li>Educates and trains physicians, hospital personnel and office staff on technical matters relating to
</li></ul>
<p>Bioness products.
</p>
<ul><li>Builds professional and technical knowledge by engaging with customers, attending scientific</li></ul>
conferences, educational workshops, delivering presentations and training to field personnel and<br>
reviewing professional publications
<ul><li>Regularly educates and updates physicians/clinicians and clinic follow-up staff on new product
</li></ul>
<p>releases of device systems and features, including both hardware and software modifications.
</p>
<ul><li>Aid physicians with technology – literature searches, engineering, Bioness technology.<br>
</li></ul>

<div><div>
<p><i>Internal Support
</i></p>
<ul><li>Educates and trains Bioness personnel on technical and clinical matters relating to Bioness
</li></ul>
<p>products.
</p>
<ul><li>Maintain open, effective communication with all Bioness functions, including Regulatory Assurance,
</li></ul>
<p>Quality Assurance, and Product Development, as well as with employees.
</p>
<ul><li>Assist sales to identify and target physicians.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Perform other related duties as assigned or requested.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must be able to operate with little direct supervision.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Travel may be extensive, up to 80%, with two to three overnights a week.</li></ul>
<p><b>EDUCATIONAL REQUIREMENTS:</b>
</p>
<p>Bachelor's degree in health care related field or a four-year degree with health care/clinical experience.
</p>
<p>Advanced degree in health sciences or engineering preferred.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>EXPERIENCE:</b><br>
Prefer prior surgical and/or OR support experience as a field clinical engineer, technical representative,<br>
technology trainer or clinical trainer in medical device field.<br>
</p>
<p><b>KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS AND ABILITIES:
</b></p>
<p><i>Candidate must have:
</i></p>
<ul><li>Ability to communicate effectively with health care professionals.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to instruct/coach others on use of products and clinical applications.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong interpersonal skills and professional demeanor.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Effective problem solving and trouble shooting skills.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Excellent organizational skills.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to prioritize under pressure.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Solid MS Office skills including PowerPoint.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>The ability to manage multiple tasks simultaneously.<br>
</li></ul>
