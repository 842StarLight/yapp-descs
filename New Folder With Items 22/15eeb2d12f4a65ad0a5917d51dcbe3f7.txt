<p><b>Position Summary:
</b></p>
<p>Provides support to the HVAC sales team for Commercial design/build and value engineered projects from the take off and bid process stage to customer finalized quote.</p>
<ul>
<li>Provides support to sales engineers, consulting engineers, contractors or technical buyers for system and product selection, design and pricing during the design and fulfillment stages.</li>
<li>Responsible for timely completion of projects and maintaining owner, engineer and/or contractor satisfaction.</li>
<li>Take-off and assemble competitive bids from site visits, construction bid documents including architectural, structural, mechanical drawings and specifications using estimating software.</li>
<li>Stays current with material costs in order to address escalation during budgeting of long-term projects.</li>
<li>Assist in maintaining existing estimating data bases, obtaining and tracking current commodity material cost, analysis and develop pricing trend charts for various materials.</li>
<li>Develops relationships with represented manufacturers, maintains competitive advantage and stays abreast of industry trends.</li>
<li>Communicates with subcontractors and suppliers by obtaining/negotiating prices with vendors for materials and evaluates vendor submittals.</li>
<li>Documents work by maintaining files for each job including, but not limited to, internal and external customer correspondence to mitigate project risk. Keep accurate database of prospective projects.</li>
<li>Responsible for holding job meetings such as; cost reviews.</li>
<li>Meets deadlines for proposals and other assigned tasks.</li>
<li>Responsible for timely completion of final bid and preparation of any submittals.</li>
<li>Simultaneously handles a large and diverse number of projects and issues with tact, cooperation and persistence.</li>
<li>Demonstrates strong leadership skills; identifies each estimator’s strengths and utilizes those strengths to maximize the potential of the individual and the department. Identifies each individual’s opportunities for growth/improvement and coaches the individuals to improve.</li>
<li>Responsible for identifying and developing processes to improve the quality and efficiency of the estimating group. Responsible for assisting staff in resolving complex or out of the norm issues.</li>
<li>Flexibility to work outside normal work hours, as required.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Electrical/Mechanical Engineering, Construction, Business, or other related areas strongly preferred.</li>
<li>3-5 years of HVAC Project Management, Estimation, or relevant experience required.</li>
<li>Familiarity with HVAC Systems and/or Temperature Controls preferred.</li>
<li>Ability to effectively present information and respond to question from groups of managers, clients, customers, and the general public quickly and effectively.</li>
<li>Intermediate computer proficiency, particularly with Word, Excel, Outlook and the internet.</li>
<li>Working knowledge of Mechanical Estimating software (Trimble, FastDuct, Blue Beam, QuickPen).</li>
</ul>
