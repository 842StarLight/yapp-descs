<h2>Marketing Intern (Fall 2019)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Canvs is the industry standard for topical analysis and emotion measurement. Our patented AI technology allows our clients, including major research agencies and enterprise media companies, to turn text into powerful insights in seconds. We've automated the ability for any organization to analyze and better understand the emotions and sentiments of their customers - so they can drive significant improvement in their product and brand experiences.</p>
<p>
As the <b>Marketing</b><b> Intern </b>for the 2019 fall semester at Canvs, you’ll tackle all things marketing and support core departmental functions; from creating press collateral and organizing email campaigns to coordinating company events and managing digital assets.</p>
<p>
The ideal candidate is a current graduate or undergraduate student based in New York City, can commit <b>at least 16 - 20 hours per week</b>, and available to start in <b>early September</b>. This is a temporary, part-time internship for hourly pay and academic credit.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
What You’ll Do</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Create collateral, infographics, PowerPoint decks, blog posts, and more.</li>
<li>Help manage company's brand and presence on social media (Twitter, Instagram, etc.).</li>
<li>Partner with Sales on strategy &amp; execution of email marketing campaigns and other digital initiatives.</li>
<li>Assist Client Services with maintaining company's CRM and other databases.</li>
<li>Identify interesting trends, stories, and data points through the Canvs platform.</li>
<li>Support cross-functional collaboration on event management and external networking opportunities (conferences, webinars, product summits, etc.).</li>
<br>
</ul>
