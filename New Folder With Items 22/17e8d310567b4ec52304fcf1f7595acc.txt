<h2>Senior Solutions Architect</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>About NCR
</b></p>
<p>NCR Corporation (NYSE: NCR) is a leading software- and services-led enterprise provider in the financial, retail, hospitality, telecom and technology industries. NCR is headquartered in Atlanta, Ga., with 34,000 employees and does business in 180 countries. NCR is a trademark of NCR Corporation in the United States and other countries.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>NCR is looking for an experienced Senior Solution Architect within the NCR Global Retail Professional Services Team. In this key role, you will be a senior level architect for the overall integration and deployment of a new NCR virtualization solution within new and existing retail customer environments. You will work closely with customers’ technical and business leaders, third party solution engineers, and NCR personnel (including management and subject matter experts) to bring this new solution to fruition in customers’ environments. You will lead the definition and execution of an architectural vision that will be tailored to each customer’s unique environment. You will influence, train, and support other team members in the integration and deployment of this new solution to other customers.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Areas of Responsibility
</p>
<ul>
<li>Understand the customer’s retail business domain quickly and align architectural design with the customer’s existing environment.
</li>
<li>Work as technical liaison between all solution engineers (both third party and NCR), customer business and technical leaders, and NCR management during design, implementation, and deployment of this new virtualization solution.
</li>
<li>Identify and assist customers with their network infrastructure modifications required to deploy this new solution.
</li>
<li>Ensure PCI requirements are maintained in the customer’s deployment of the solution.
</li>
<li>Work as consultant for this solution to all NCR and third-party sales organizations.
</li>
<li>Assist team members with initial customer deployment of solution.
</li>
</ul>
<p>Basic Qualifications
</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science and 7+ years’ experience
</li>
<li>Linux Ubuntu &amp; Debian Operating systems experience.
</li>
<li>XEN Virtualization, ZFS, DRBD, Shell scripting, networking, security, operations, hosting, and public and/or private cloud experience.
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Natural at identifying, troubleshooting, and coming up with a plan of action in a quickly moving product organization.
</li>
<li>Must have good verbal, written, and presentation skills.
</li>
<li>Must be able to travel 50+% (will include international travel, which could be 1-2 weeks away at a time).
</li>
</ul>
<p>Preferred Qualifications
</p>
<ul>
<li>Experience virtualizing operating systems on a Linux host.
</li>
<li>Familiarity with PCI certification requirements.
</li>
<li>Experience configuring and troubleshooting network configurations within a Linux environment.
</li>
<li>VPN configuration experience (OpenVPN).
</li>
<li>CouchDB experience.
</li>
<li>Experience with high-availability clustering of virtualization hosts.
</li>
<li>Experience with documenting systems, physical, logical, and module level architectures.
</li>
</ul>
