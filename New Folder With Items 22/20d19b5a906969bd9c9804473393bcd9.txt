<h2>Community Operations Manager - Headquarters by WeWork (Evergreen)</h2>

<p><b>COMMUNITY OPERATIONS MANAGER - Headquarters by WeWork - Tri-State</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>
The Community Operations Manager reports directly to the Community Director and supports the overall growth and sustained performance goals of the <b>Headquarters by WeWork</b> business line. The role will primarily focus on development and day-to-day operations of <b>Headquarters by WeWork</b> locations, while also serving as a ground control for all teams that deliver services within the Building Operations department including: Safety &amp; Security, Facilities, Technology Services, Community Services, Member Experience and a variety of HQ partners. Responsibilities vary from project management and execution to gathering data and analytics, budget tracking, member relationship management, vendor management, coordination and communication to all internal and external stakeholders.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Operations and Community Key Responsibilities</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>
Manage day-to-day operations of multiple (10-15) locations to ensure a positive member experience and develop operating efficiencies. Day-to-day operations include but are not limited to:</p>
<ul>
<li><p>
Vendor management</p></li>
<li><p>
Consumable management: managing inventory stock levels, ordering consumables</p></li>
<li><p>
Liaison with Facilities team</p></li>
<li><p>
Request and escalation management</p></li>
<li><p>
Landlord operational issues &amp; escalations</p></li>
<li><p>
P&amp;L ownership</p></li>
<li><p>
Communication with member to drive engagement with WeWork Community and services offered</p></li>
<li><p>
New member onboarding: working with logistics and new members, assist in smooth handoff between Development &amp; Sales teams to Community &amp; Account Management teams</p></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>
In addition to day-to-day responsibilities, this individual will play a key role in building out the operational framework and launch for this new vertical. This includes:</p>
<ul>
<li><p>
Develop standard operating procedures</p></li>
<li><p>
Create operating expense efficiencies</p></li>
<li><p>
Develop best practices for Opex reduction strategies related to but not limited to supplies ordering, services provided, move-out process and management<br>
</p></li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Building Operations Key Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>
Project manage key initiatives related to enhancing community experience, creating operating efficiencies and/or incremental revenue streams related to any of the main building operational pillars: Safety &amp; Security, Facilities, Technology Services, Community Services</p></li>
<li><p>
Leverage data to develop dashboards to analyze the business and identify opportunities to create efficiencies
</p></li>
<li><p>Liaise with vendor management team to drive accountability with top vendors
</p></li>
<li><p>Aid and assist in identifying opportunities and deficiencies within the new building opening/handover process
</p></li>
<li><p>Assist in monthly operating expense review to look for opportunities to resolve challenges and create new processes to drive profitability</p></li>
<li><p>
Build and maintain relationships with internal team members and external resources</p></li>
</ul>
