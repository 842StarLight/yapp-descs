<h2>Scheduler</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>We have contract opportunity in Mobile for a Scheduler. This position will assist in the planning and assigning future dated work utilizing technologies to ensure manpower is appropriate and proper work leveling and routing occur so that customer related requests, compliance targets, and new business tasks are completed in a timely manner and routing is properly managed with available manpower. Primary duties: Using the system technologies to perform the following tasks:
<br>
<ul>
<li>Perform duties as assigned by Workload Manager or Administrator/Lead Scheduler.
</li>
<li>Monitor daily assignments of service, leak or maintenance work to ensure both compliance and non-compliance work is completed in a timely manner.
</li>
<li>Utilize and exploit Work Scheduling and Enterprise Asset Management systems
</li>
<li>Ensure assigned work is completed and manage work assigned, but not completed.
</li>
<li>Monitor and adjust availability plans daily.
</li>
<li>Manually and systematically assign work types for next day routing.
</li>
<li>Evaluate and recommend adjustments to routes on a daily basis.
</li>
<li>Answer calls from Service Technicians Regarding situations
</li>
<li>Communicate emergency and non-emergency orders to Regional Maintenance Supervisor and/or Maintenance Crews
</li>
<li>Review all logged leaks during normal business operating hours to ensure consistency and provide guidance concerning leak criteria
</li>
<li>Review and provide locations for all ACI repairs
</li>
<li>Coordinate with CSR’s concerning the status of leak repairs, turn-on’s, and other work in C&amp;M
</li>
<li>Plan daily/weekly work and communicate plan with regional supervisors.
</li>
<li>Call line locates for planned gas system work and document accordingly. Keep line locates current.
</li>
<li>Receive and manage monthly compliance report from Operations Shared Services and ensure compliance orders are completed on time.
</li>
<li>Ensure customer service levels are maintained through availability adjustments and coordination with work location supervisors on manpower adjustments.
</li>
<li>Create and maintain Deployment Plans (field employee vacation scheduling and changes, time off, meetings, training, weekend duty, holiday duty, etc.) for all areas days/weeks/months out.
</li>
<li>Daily coordination with all areas’ field supervisors to ensure manpower schedules and availability plans are accurate for the next day.
</li>
<li>Schedule resources and coordinate new business orders in the customer service system for service line and meter set orders.
</li>
<li>Contact new business builders and customers to coordinate service line installation and meter sets.
</li>
<li>Monitor and updates reports related to service line installation and meter set timeliness.
</li>
<li>All other duties as assigned Qualifications:
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>A minimum of 3 years of experience in Field Operations Construction and Maintenance, Customer Service, Sales and/or equivalent experience</li>
<li>Ability to lead by example in support of the Company’s essential characteristics and values: strong ethics and integrity, dedication to a safe work environment, commitment to a diverse workforce that recognizes and respects every individual’s unique skills and perspectives</li>
<li>Ability to work under pressure and meet tight deadlines</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal skills with the proven ability to build and establish rapport with a diverse array of people</li>
<li>Ability to work independently and manage time effectively in order to meet individual goals and deadlines</li>
<li>Ability to work as part of a team and display a positive attitude for this dynamic environment</li>
<li>Performs duties by using good judgment and sound decision-making skills in accordance with established practices and procedures</li>
</ul>Education and experience: Bachelor’s degree is required or 2+ years of equivalent experience Work environment:
<ul>
<li>Shared office environment</li>
<li>Limited travel, primarily to local field operations locations</li>
<li>Work schedule alternates between M-F, T-Sat,</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
