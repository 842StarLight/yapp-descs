<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Develop Envision customer relationships that promote retention and loyalty.</b></h3>
<p>Envision helps businesses such as Costco Travel, Nike and Alaska Airlines deliver legendary customer service to their customers. Our solution Click2Coach, whether installed on premise or hosted in the Microsoft Azure cloud, allows our customers to deliver exceptional customer experiences and turn their call center into a profit center. We do this by recording interactions, recording agent screen and workflow, providing agent and performance analytics, trending and speech analytics. All of this allows our customers to better understand why customers are contacting them, which products and services they like and dislike, and to ensure the agents responding to the interaction are well coached.<br>
<br>
This position is for a Customer Success Manager. The successful Customer Success Manager operates effectively in a fast paced environment, demonstrates leadership, maintains composure, and has superb communications skills.<br>
<br>
