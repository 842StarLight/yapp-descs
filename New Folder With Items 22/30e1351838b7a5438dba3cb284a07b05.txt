<h2>Regional Technical Support Manager-Material Handling Industry</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>The Organisation</b></p>
<p>Are you determined, focus driven and up for a challenge? If the answer is ‘yes’ to any or all of these questions, you could be the person we’re looking for to join our ever-growing team.</p>
<p>Combilift is the largest global manufacturer of multi-directional forklifts and an acknowledged leader in long load handling solutions supporting our number one market in North America. Our US market has doubled in size within the last three years and our growth strategy is to double again within the next three years, adding to our strong North American team.</p>
<p>Our employees think logically for themselves and have actual conversations with our customers. We have won numerous awards including the Ernest and Young Entrepreneur of the year, Deloitte best managed award just to name a few.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities: What are you expected to do?</b></p>
<ul>
<li>In line with our commercial expansion, devise, build and adjust the appropriate structure, competence and approach to facilitate and drive a reputation of best in class service to support Combilift’ s reputation and development</li>
<li>Lead and manage a team of highly skilled professionals to drive a culture and structure of cohesive and best in class service provision.</li>
<li>Provide advice and leadership to your team, your peers and the Senior Leadership Team in North America on the progression of the service agenda in line with commercial development</li>
<li>Ensure, on a continual basis, that you’re after sales structures are in line with the commercial imperatives of the senior team.</li>
<li>Manage the co-ordination and delegation of duties within your team structures ensuring that work is balanced reasonably and consistently amongst the team and in a manner that allows for maximum efficiency and motivation.</li>
<li>Provide support, insight and practical leadership input to a wide variety of stakeholders from operations to customers.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Skills, Competence &amp; Experience necessary: </b></p>

<li>Ability to work both, as an individual and as part of a team is essential.</li>
<li>Must be a quick learner and have a willingness to learn new skills.</li>
<li>Must be willing to work additional hours to complete the job.</li>
<li>Must be able to work well under pressure.</li>
<li>Excellent MS Office Skills is required.</li>
<li>Mechanical Experience is an advantage but not essential.</li>
</ul>
