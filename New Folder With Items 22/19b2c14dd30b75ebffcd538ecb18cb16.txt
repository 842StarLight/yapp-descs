<h2>Director, Enterprise Exception Management</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>If you want to achieve more in your mission of health care, you have to be really smart about the business of health care. Challenge yourself, your peers and our industry by shaping what health care looks like and doing <b>your life's best work.(sm)</b><br>

<div>
<br>
This position is an Enterprise Operations Position, leading the Exception Management critical team and process. Join us to manage the process of identifying exceptions to our current product, operations and process standards. Outcomes of the exception vetting include analyzing, forecasting, trending, and presenting information for business planning. Support short and long term operational/strategic business activities - by developing, enhancing and maintaining operational information and processes. Develop and implement effective/strategic business solutions through process re-engineering, governance, and analysis to drive strategic decision-making. Works cross-functionality to establish key business processes, ownership, and define service level agreements while maintaining operational excellence and efficiency.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>You’ll enjoy the flexibility to telecommute* from anywhere within the U.S. as you take on some tough challenges.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
</div>
<div><b>Primary Responsibilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Ensure all exceptions are identified and vetted to support critical product functionality, and on-going management of the operation</li>
<li>
Communicate non-standard approvals to implementation and develop a close the loop process to track how many of these are implemented.
</li>
<li>Drive continuous productivity within Optum’s product and services to optimize execution across onboarding/implementation through operational support.</li>
<li>
Embedded as part of the on-going operations to drive a 365 day readiness program, driving cross-functional accountability in a matrix partnership environment</li>
<li>
Utilize data and technology solutions to streamline processes and automate operations across existing product portfolio</li>
<li>
Provide leadership to maintain SLA and performance metrics to excel Optum operational readiness process across the product portfolio</li>
<li>
Work cross-functionally across the Front Office and leadership teams to drive visibility to customer issues and drive process improvement projects to reduce defects and continue to improve processes to reduce implementation cycle time and ongoing operational support costs</li>
<li>
Take ownership of position by proactively leading business process change and driving performance improvements and transformation projects within Optum’s existing product portfolio. This includes improving implementation times, making service repeatable and reliable, ensuring reporting and feedback rigor with the customer and internal stakeholders</li>
<li>
Evaluates available data to identify and develop practical alternatives to complex situations. Summarizes conclusions and recommendations. Present pros and cons of recommendations to leadership</li>
<li>
Gains a deep understanding of specific product areas across Optum to drive cross-functional decision-making to ensure sound business decisions</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div>This position requires sound ability to facilitate through customer issues, service provider issues, and business process decisions. This position should also be focused on gaining consensus with key team members to identify the best business solution possible to improve our onboarding process. The role will be required to make resource and organizational design decisions as they develop the future state of the business.</div>
