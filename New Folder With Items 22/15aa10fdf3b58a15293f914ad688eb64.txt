<p><b>Business Development Associate </b></p>
<p><i>Location: Northern California </i></p>
<p><b>About Scale Microgrid Solutions</b><br>*<br>California is in the midst of an unprecedented energy crisis. Over the past several years, the state’s status quo electricity infrastructure has ignited thousands of wildfires resulting in billions of dollars in damage and hundreds of deaths. At a time when climate change is exacerbating wildfire related threats, operators are now being forced to de-energize large portions of the electric grid in order to ensure safety; and there is no short term fix. This situation is not sustainable, and innovation is the only way forward.</p>
<p>Scale Microgrid Solutions (SMS) designs, builds, and finances the world’s most innovative distributed clean energy solutions. We’re expanding our operations to ensure that all Californians have access to sustainable, affordable, and reliable electricity. Be part of the solution.<br>*<br><b>The Position</b><br>*<br>The Business Development Associate is a dynamic role, best suited for a person who is highly ambitious, independent, and personable. This person is responsible for developing a robust pipeline of project opportunities by building relationships with channel partners and potential clients. In addition to managing his or her own day to day schedule meeting with clients and analyzing opportunities, this person will work closely with the Business Development Manager for California to structure a platform for future growth. He or she will be a key stakeholder as SMS grows it’s presence in California. Our ideal candidate should possess a deep understanding of energy technologies and the energy landscape in the US, as well as foundational business and sales acumen.<br>*<br><b>Primary Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul><li>Developing a robust pipeline of microgrid opportunities in line with SMS’ overarching California go to market strategy</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strengthen relationships with channel partners through consistent engagement</li></ul>
<ul><li>Bring thoughtful, innovative ideas to strategic discussions</li></ul>
<ul><li>Perform initial analysis of microgrid opportunities to determine baseline feasibility, work with BD Analyst and BD Manager to further scope and refine project opportunities</li></ul>
<ul><li>Work with clients through the entire project development phase to ensure the project flows continuously through the pipeline from lead generation through commissioning and operational phases.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Work collaboratively across disciplines within SMS to improve processes and drive overall business efficiency</li></ul>
