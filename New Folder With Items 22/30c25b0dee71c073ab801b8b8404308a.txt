<h2>Senior Enterprise Sales Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Since founded more than 30 years ago, Yorktel has been a dependable IT partner to government and private enterprise around the world. Yorktel today is a leading provider for the connected digital workspace, an SCN Top 50 Integrator for IT-enabled AV solutions, and a supplier of private and public cloud services for managed Unified Communications and Collaboration (UCC) with over 20,000 rooms under active management. We offer a portfolio that includes consulting, design-build-operate, content management and media production and a 24hr helpdesk that provides nationwide dispatch and next day service in selected cities. Yorktel is headquartered in the US, and has offices around the USA and the world. If you have what it takes to be part of this rapidly growing company and dynamic, fast-paced industry, we want to hear from you.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Yorktel is currently seeking a Senior Enterprise Sales Executive to achieve maximum sales profitability, growth and account penetration within assigned accounts. The position is responsible and accountable for overall sales processes from initial contact and understanding client needs to the closure of the product/service delivery. Promotes/sells/secures orders from existing and prospective customers through a relationship and solution based approach. The Senior Sales Account Manager must present existing products and services to current and prospective clients and is accountable for ensuring client requirements are understood and communicated to service teams within Yorktel efficiently and effectively.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>This position reports directly to the VP of Enterprise Sales - Americas. This VP will provide direction and support to the Senior Enterprise Sales Executive in regard to the following areas; development and improvement, career development and client escalation issues. This position will also work closely various departments to identify, develop and deploy service offerings and lead strategic engagements with major customers.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>This position must have the ability to establish and maintain strong working relationships both internally and externally. Excellent verbal and written communication is necessary. Strong presentation skills are an essential function of this position. Superior negotiation skills coupled with a net profit bottom line mentality is required. Must have advanced conflict resolution skills. The ability to forecast business accurately and have basic understanding business financial principles is essential.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The Senior Enterprise Sales Executive will represent customer requirements to Yorktel and will play a major role in directing how Yorktel products and services are customized and deployed in based on the client solution. This position will make recommendations and be actively involved in the design and development of Yorktel products and services.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Internal and External Relationship Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>This position regularly works in a team of individuals with varying degrees of expertise and influences internal and external customers to achieve a mutually desirable outcome.
</li>
<li>This position will make recommendations to internal parties and customers in regard to concerns or issues, and ensure resolution of any problems working closely in conjunction with assigned clients.
</li>
<li>This position requires close interaction with peers in EMEA and Asia Pac.
</li>
<li>This position must regularly communicate answers to complex questions and respond to detailed inquiries.
</li>
<li>This position handles and is expected to protect all internal and external confidential information and must utilize discretion and judgment based on company policies and procedures.
</li>
<li>Responsible for interaction with all Operations and Corporate Services departments including, Sales, Purchasing, Accounting, Engineering, Production and Logistics to ensure the successful execution of all products.
</li>
<li>Drives Revenue through recognition and realization of opportunities within accounts.
</li>
<li>Works with customers and Yorktel resources to leverage Yorktel’s products and services to enhance customers’ business and grow opportunities.
</li>
<li>Develops Sales annual account action plans.
</li>
<li>Identify all customer contract renewals and build plan for the renewal of those contracts.
</li>
<li>Drives profitability revenue growth year over year in assigned accounts.
</li>
<li>Meets and exceeds monthly and annual revenue goals by executing an account growth strategy.
</li>
<li>Works with Marketing to create Sales Programs centered on OEM endorsements.
</li>
<li>Conducts regular Business Reviews and reporting with major accounts.
</li>
<li>Manages relationships with Manufacturers (OEMs), distributors and resellers to further sales opportunities and to ensure continued customer satisfaction.
</li>
<li>Tracks and maintains all account activity in the company CRM application (Vision)
</li>
<li>Provides input into pricing, positioning and strategy within assigned verticals or markets.
</li>
<li>Provides sales forecasts and other reports to manager as required.
</li>
<li>Develop and conform to company technical standards and practices.
</li>
<li>Research, prospect and establish leads for corporate relationships followed by an introductory needs analysis/discovery meeting utilizing a consultative sales approach
</li>
<li>Prepare and present proposals to corporate decision makers, build interactive components for all renewal and new business proposals, build interactive presentations that outline features, benefits and unique selling points of interactive properties.
</li>
<li>Generate a pre-determined level of new business revenue for Yorktel with special attention to net revenues.
</li>
<li>Maintain current knowledge of interactive products and industry trends.
</li>
<li>Prepare weekly sales reports, activities and revenue reports.
</li>
<li>Other Duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
