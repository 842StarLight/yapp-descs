<h2>Pricing/Data Integrity Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Title</b></h3>
Pricing/Data Integrity Analyst<p></p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Job Description</b></h3>
<p><b>
In this role, you have the opportunity to</b></p>
<p>
Serve as a core GBS team member, providing exceptional service to all customers and business partners, as well as be a subject matter expert for all pricing, billing and finance related questions and processes.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
You are responsible for</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Capturing timely, accurate, and thorough documentation of processes, procedures, and reporting standards of all aspects of the pricing and billing functions in both manual and electronic platforms</li>
<li>
Serving as the single point of contact for Salesforce and PeopleSoft</li>
<li>
Managing and controlling contractual compliance tasks</li>
<li>
Generating and analyzing pricing reports and compliance related risks; as well make recommendations for mitigating threats and resolving issues for all business models</li>
<li>
Exhibiting knowledge and skill in general operations of the accounting department, and comprehension of functions of other departments within the organization as it relates to the position</li>
<li>
Facilitating teach backs related to enhancements to the department</li>
<li>
Collaborating closely with inter and intra departmental teams such as Finance, Sales, Marketing, Innovation and Accounting to ensure smooth project implementation, and the resolution of pricing discrepancies.</li>
<li>
Managing and analyzing incoming and outgoing pricing and contractual communications with both internal and external customers.</li>
<li>
Monitoring and ensuring pricing and billing data integrity for all business models and products to ensure accurate and timely billing</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
You are part of</b></p>
<p>
 The Aging and Care Giving division within our Global Business Service hub leading the financial service operations for the team.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
To succeed in this role, you should have the following skills and experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree preferred</li>
<li>
2+ years financial reporting, data analysis, data integrity assurance and customer service experience
</li>
<li>Strong Customer Service Philosophy. Demonstrates exemplary customer service through demeanor, approach to relationships with employees and customers, and through leadership style.</li>
<li>
Strong Excel Skills</li>
<li>
Ability to learn quickly, function in fast paced environment and work on multiple tasks simultaneously.</li>
<li>
Able to think independently, have strong problem solving skills, and have a continuous improvement mentality.</li>
<li>
Planning, organization and effective time management skills</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
In return, we offer you</b></p>
<p>
We offer you a dynamic working environment in an innovative business, which offers you excellent opportunities to further develop and fully exploit your talents. This job comes with a competitive salary, excellent benefits and up-to-date training.</p>
