We are currently looking to add an Accounts Receivable Clerk to our Accounting and Finance Team. The Accounts Receivable Clerk will be responsible for the following:</p>
<br>
<p></p>
Required Skills<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Posts customer payments by recording cash, checks, and Wire transactions.</li>
<li>Updates receivables by totaling unpaid invoices.</li>
<li>Maintains records by microfilming invoices, debits, and credits.</li>
<li>Verifies validity of account discrepancies by obtaining and investigating information from sales, trade promotions, customer service departments, and from customers.</li>
<li>Resolves valid or authorized deductions by entering adjusting entries.</li>
<li>Resolves invalid or unauthorized deductions by following pending deductions procedures.</li>
<li>Resolves collections by examining customer payment plans, payment history, credit line; coordinating contact with collections department.</li>
<li>Summarizes receivables by maintaining invoice accounts; coordinating monthly transfer to accounts receivable account; verifying totals; preparing month end reports.</li>
<li>Prepare and gather supporting documentation for monthly management statements.</li>
<li>Research problems/projects under the direction of the immediate supervisor as required.</li>
<li>Use Excel and other automated processes to prepare required documentation and reports. Must be familiar with how to use formulas and sorting of spreadsheets.</li>
<li>Prepare and assist controller with audit documentation records</li>
<br>
</ul>
