<p><b>
Opportunity</b></p>
<p>
Muncie Power Products, an industry leader in Power Take-Offs and mobile power components to the truck equipment industry, has an immediate opening within the Manufacturing Department for a <b>Material Handler </b>- 1st Shift. Reporting to the Senior Industrial Engineer, this hire<b> </b>will serve as an invaluable contributor to the machine shop at our flagship manufacturing plant in Tulsa, OK. This teammember will partake in the movement, unbinding, and sorting of raw material and assist with the consolidation of parts. If you enjoy fast-paced, hands on work, this position may be for you!</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Safety Notice</b></p>
<p>
This position is considered a safety sensitive position due to "the operation of a motor vehicle, other vehicle (fork lift), equipment, machinery or power tools and/or repairing, maintaining or monitoring the performance or operation of any equipment, machinery, or manufacturing process, the malfunction of disruption of which could result in injury or property damage."</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Essential Duties &amp; Responsibilities</b></p>
<p><i>
To perform this job successfully, the hire must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Retrieves all necessary materials for saw operations</li>
<li>Moves material to facilitate loading material racks as needed using various industrial material handling methods</li>
<li>Unbinds and sorts incoming material after receiving process</li>
<li>Sorts parts from multiple containers into a single container</li>
<li>Sorts parts from old or damaged containers to new containers</li>
<li>Assures ISO and quality standards meet company requirements</li>
<li>Ensures that Company standard practices and procedures are followed in accordance with the department functions</li>
<li>Promotes and administers safety awareness on a daily basis</li>
<li>Interprets written, oral, and digital instructions and specifications</li>
<li>Performs other duties as assigned</li>
<br>
</ul>
