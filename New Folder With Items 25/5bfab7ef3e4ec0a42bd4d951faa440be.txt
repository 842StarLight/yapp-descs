<h2>Manager, Event Planning</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Summary of Major Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div><p>The Exact Sciences Manager, Event Planning – Employee Amenities’ main duty is to provide exemplary customer service at the Exact Sciences Conference and Dining Centers with multiple conference rooms accommodating up to 900 guests in the largest space. This position will provide best in class customer service and planning of events including the management of event logistics such as catering, conference room set-up, equipment set-up, AV, room reservation and facility logistics. In addition, the Manager, Event Planning – Employee Amenities will assist the Director of Wellness and Amenities with contract management, accurate reports detailing catering and room reservation requests and billing of services to business cost centers. This position will lead a team primarily responsible for internal company events.
</p></div></div></div>
