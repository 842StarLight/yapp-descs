<b>Position Summary:</b><br>
The Amobee Senior Platform Account Manager owns the client-facing relationship post-sale and ensures superior client service through premium campaign management, relationship development, account retention and upsell activities. In addition to regular and proactive client communication, Senior Platform Account Managers interact across Amobee internal departments as well as the partner landscape on behalf of their clients. The Senior Account Manager maximizes client retention, is responsible for education of new offerings and solutions, and generates upsell revenue by delivering best-in-class reporting, insights, and results. The Senior Account Manager advises, mentors, and helps train other members of the team and is responsible for the most complex platform accounts typically including partnerships with an agency or brand direct..
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Cultivate relationships with existing clients to maximize client satisfaction, retention, and growth</li>
<li>Grow revenue across the accounts in your book of business through product adoption and upselling</li>
<li>Work as liaison between client and internal departments to coordinate campaign execution, upsell opportunities, and troubleshoot delivery and performance issues</li>
<li>Manage the campaign launch process from start to finish inclusive of client kick off call, strategy recommendation, asset collection, optimization and delivery of performance reporting</li>
<li>Utilize Salesforce and other internal systems to ensure all account information is updated and accurate</li>
<li>Provide accurate revenue forecasts and account plans on required cadence</li>
<li>Have deep understanding of Amobee’s entire product offering, basic optimization and campaign design knowledge, and best practices for campaign effectiveness and cross-selling</li>
<li>Proactively use data, trends, market insights, and logic to develop tailored campaign recommendations and opportunities for continued success.</li>
<li>Deliver insights and reporting using basic queries from DataMine</li>
<li>Present account level Business Reviews to client on a regular cadence including strategy, insights, activation, and product updates.</li>
<li>Support the client training team when a platform client goes to self-service</li>
<li>Train, mentor, and provide advice and direction to the Account Managers</li>
<li>Book of business can consist of 5-15 regionally named accounts</li>
<li>Tasked with supporting regional growth goal QoQ and being primary point of contact for the customer on day-to-day account needs</li>
<li>Performs work directly under the supervision of Manager</li>
</ul>

<ul>
<li>BA/BS degree</li>
<li>4-6 years experience in digital media and/or account management</li>
<li>Track record of delivering exceptional ROI on complex online advertising campaigns.</li>
<li>Self-motivated to dig in to learn about products, technology, customers, and competitors</li>
<li>Process oriented with a strict attention to detail</li>
<li>Strong organizational, analytical, and project management skills</li>
<li>Proven verbal and written communication skills in a consultative, client-facing environment</li>
<li>Powerpoint and Excel skills, including pivot tables, chart marking, and manipulation of large data sets.</li>
<li>Proficiency in G-Suite a plus</li>
<li>Strong cross-functional skills, including ability to motivate and influence immediate team members and non-reporting groups to meet business objectives</li>
<li>Highly skilled with launching and managing complex campaigns and larger client relationships</li>
</ul>
