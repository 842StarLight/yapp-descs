<h2>Sales Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>The Sales Coordinator is responsible for sales and back-office support for the Business Rental Sales teams. This position will create and/or maintain customer reports, customer contracts, profit and loss models, presentations, mailings and various pieces of correspondence. This position is also responsible for leading key pieces of information into the sales CRM tool to provide detailed activity records for business accounts.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
This position is located at 14002 E 21st St. Tulsa, OK.</div>
<br>
<b>
Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Create customer reports as needed by sales</li>
<li>
Create profit and loss models by account for sales</li>
<li>
Create presentations for sales group and management as necessary</li>
<li>
Support sales with mailings and other correspondence</li>
<li>
Facilitate the loading of key pieces of information into the sales CRM tool; provide detail activity records for business accounts</li>
<li>
Work with the domestic sales force team to secure contracts for short term car rental usage worldwide</li>
<li>
Maintain paper and electronic files</li>
<li>
Answer incoming phone lines</li>
<li>
Extend BA rental types as requested by sales</li>
<li>
Complete Method of Payment changes as requested by sales</li>
</ul>
