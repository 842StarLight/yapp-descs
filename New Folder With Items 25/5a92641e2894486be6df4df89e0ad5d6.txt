<h2>Marketing Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">We're looking for a smart, dynamic, curious thinker to join the Marketing team. This role will work closely with the Segment Demand Generation Manager to help the team reach and exceed our core revenue driving KPIs. He/She will also support the larger marketing team on a range of projects spanning across product marketing, content, and field events. Individual responsibilities include copy writing, administrative campaign support, pre-event coordination and sales followup support, SEO/content optimizations, and new content management.
<br><br>
The ideal candidate will be hands-on with a can-do attitude for all tasks big and small. Day-to-day, he/she will be collaborating with a variety of Marketing (Digital, Product, CRM), Creative, Business Intelligence, Analytics, and Sales teams.
<br><br>
<b>About this Role:</b><br>
You will...
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Lead Generation // Program Management</li>
<li>Assist in the execution of cross-channel marketing programs and integrated marketing campaigns designed to generate sales qualified leads</li>
<li>Partner with the Demand Generation Specialist to launch monthly sales blitzes</li>
<li>Email copywriting for owned and paid promotional activities</li>
<li>Work with product marketing and the brand and comms team to rollout new content / case studies across the sales floor</li>
<li>Core operational tasks including but not limited to––Salesforce campaigns, tracking URLs, Salesloft cadences, email list creation/segmentation, and HubSpot landing pages</li>
<li>Field Event Support</li>
<li>Support the events team with pre-event coordination and onsite sales activation</li>
<li>Draft pre-event and post event outreach cadences for the sales team</li>
<li>Partner with marketing operations to ensure sales has accurate reporting and views needed for event outreach/followup</li>
<li>Support the team prepare for and launch the Collaborative–– Classy's annual conference for leaders across the social sector</li>
<li>Cross Marketing Support</li>
<li>Support Classy's SEO manager with onsite optimizations and updates</li>
<li>Assist the content team with operational tasks including Salesforce campaign and CID creation</li>
<li>Update Classy's Resource page to ensure the latest webinars, guides, videos, etc. are accessible to clients and prospects</li>
<li>Work to standardize and document the team's operational processes</li>
<li>Partner with the team to build out quarterly programs within Asana</li>
</ul>
<b>About You:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>1-2 years of marketing experience, preferably in an environment with a B2B sales team</li>
<li>Self-motivated, diligent, and hungry for more responsibility</li>
<li>Passionate about results-oriented marketing with a balance of right brain analytics and left brain creativity</li>
<li>Extraordinary multi-tasker who thrives in managing several projects simultaneously in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>Great time management skills and ability to prioritize projects and workload to meet deadlines</li>
<li>Excellent project management, presentation and interpersonal skills with ability to interact well cross-functionally</li>
<li>BA/BS in marketing, communications, or other relevant field</li>
<li>Experience with Salesforce preferred</li>
<li>Ability to take little instruction and run with it</li>
<li>Comfortable with change and a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>Able to effectively assess, organize and manage multiple priorities, projects and functions under pressure to meet short deadlines within a dynamic environment</li>
</ul>
