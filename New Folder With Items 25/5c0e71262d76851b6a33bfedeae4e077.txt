<h2>SAP BPC Systems Administrator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p><b>PURPOSE</b></p>
<p>
Responsible to govern the end-to-end planning cycle of business users through Sales, Cost, HR, Income Statement, and Balance Sheet planning. Also is responsible for the documentation, communication, and management of the EPM (BPC) 10.1 system.</p>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p><b>KEY RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Demonstrates a commitment to communicating, improving and adhering to safety policies in all work environments and areas.</li>
<li>
Manage and maintain master data in BPC and all facets of the BPC web client including data and information requests through use of the BPC application.</li>
<li>
Communicate status and document system changes including new BPC functionality, enhancements, and improvement for financial reporting.
</li>
<li>Train users, provide financial analysis, and troubleshoot data discrepancies.</li>
<li>
Translate business needs into BPC solutions and communicate BPC technical matter in business terminology.</li>
<li>
Support various monthly, quarterly, and annual reporting requirements.</li>
<li>
Assist in financial planning activities with respect to monthly rolling forecasts and mid-term plan.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
RELATIONSHIPS WITH OTHER JOBS</b></p>
<ul><li>
Provides support to Finance group including Senior Management</li></ul>
