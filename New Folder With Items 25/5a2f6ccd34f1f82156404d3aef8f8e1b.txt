<h2>Technical Services Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Turnkey Technologies is dedicated to solving business challenges with our software solutions and our team of experts. We're looking for a Technical Services Manager to work alongside our team at our headquarters located in St. Charles, Missouri.</p>
<p>As the Technical Services Manager, you will lead the technical experts on customer projects implementation teams and responsible for the delivery of project tasks designed to implement Microsoft solutions. You will take ownership of technical project delivery and client satisfaction through close communications with the client, project delivery, and team management.</p>
<p>This client-facing role requires extensive experience with managing technical projects and excellent interpersonal/communication skills. You must be client-focused, team oriented with exceptional organizational skills.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Oversee and manage all aspects of technical projects, as well as ongoing cloud operations within client environments.</li>
<li>Manage project timelines, resources, tasks, risks, budget and scope.</li>
<li>Attend all project-related meetings, providing feedback and guidance based on long-term strategic vision.</li>
<li>Build a learning culture in your team to build deep skills on core technologies, broad knowledge of adjacent solutions and professional skills.</li>
<li>Coach the team to actively address the technical and architectural issues that block customers' technology decisions and usage.</li>
<li>Generate and deliver presentations to executive and c-level stakeholders.</li>
<li>Think strategically with business objectives in mind, while staying on top of tactical execution.</li>
<li>Ensure that all deliverables are delivered on-time and are aligned with target goals.</li>
<li>Be responsible for personal billable delivery of project management, integrations, development, or data migrations with Microsoft Dynamics of at least 50%.</li>
<li>Communicate issues proactively with project teams and management.</li>
<li>Build strong relationships with key stakeholders as a Relationship Manager with specific accounts.</li>
</ul>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<ul>
<li>5+ years of experience managing technical projects and/or production datacenter operations as a technical lead, project manager or program manager.</li>
<li>Expertise in strategic communication, management, budget management and data analysis.</li>
<li>Experience with integrations, development, or data migrations in Microsoft Dyanamics ERP and CRM solutions
</li>
<li>Ability to communicate effectively and efficiently over the phone, during face-to-face meetings and via written communications.</li>
<li>Experience managing standards compliance of systems, applications and processes.</li>
<li>Ability to manage client expectations and drive business for the team in partnership with the sales and marketing teams.
</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Project and Visio.</li>
<li>Highly responsible, self-motivated, and able to work with minimal supervision.</li>
<li>Consulting organization experience is preferred.</li>
</ul>
