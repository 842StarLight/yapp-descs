 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">

<div><b>Project Manager</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>Monarch Landscape Companies, the fastest growing commercial landscape company on the West Coast, is offering an excellent opportunity for a Project Manager to advance their career. The Project Manager will manage client interaction and expectations surrounding long term planning and high-level value-added services.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Primary Responsibilities</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for client retention, annual increases, and enhancement penetration</li>
<li>Serve as point-of-contact for high level site and project issues to expeditiously and proactively identify, address, and resolve client needs</li>
<li>Act as liaison between operations and client, ensuring clarity in communication and expectations</li>
<li>Conduct walk-throughs with client and other company representatives as appropriate</li>
<li>Evaluate accounts and develop an effective and on-going landscape asset management plan</li>
<li>Build a strong knowledge base of each client’s business, organization, and objectives</li>
<li>Collaborate with Area Managers to maintain a holistic understanding of client sites</li>
<li>Stay up-to-date on day-to-day site operations and issues to identify trends in need of comprehensive solutions</li>
<li>Create, manage, and control scope of work, ensuring projects are completed on time and within budget</li>
<li>Manage all project communication between internal and external clients</li>
<li>Analyze and understand job cost reports and budget variances related to contract projects</li>
<li>Prepare monthly forecast of labor and materials, and accurate job costing of work orders</li>
<li>Meet or exceed annual sales goals</li>
<li>Gather, calculate and compile take off data for use in bid estimates</li>
<li>Accurately prepare bids and landscape designs &amp; renderings, by utilizing field collected data and computer programs, to present to client</li>
<li>Visit jobs in progress to relate actual estimate; review questions or problems that may arise</li>
<li>Add and update project information in ERP software daily</li>
<li>Utilize ERP reports to keep estimate priorities and workload organized</li>
<li>Manage billing process, including communicating change orders, lien releases, progress billing, etc.</li>
<li>Develop and maintain mutually beneficial relationships with vendors and subcontractors, insuring accountability and quality for price</li>
<li>Ensure safety on every project by enforcing safety training and meetings,as well as consistently monitor job sites for potential safety hazards</li>
<li>Constantly promote and encourage adherence to company policies and best practices</li>
</ul>
<div>Supervisory Responsibilities</div>
<ul>
<li>This position may have supervisory responsibilities</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Qualifications</b></div>
<div></div>
<div><i>Education</i></div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Landscape Architecture or Horticulture,Construction/Project Management, or related field; or at least 5 years related work experience</li>
<li>At least 5 years of experience in the commercial landscape industry</li>
<li>Extensive knowledge of regional plant life</li>
</ul>
<div><i>Field Experience</i></div>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 3 years of verifiable field experience with project estimating or design-build work</li>
<li>Strong estimation background, with working knowledge of construction/installation &amp; irrigation</li>
<li>Familiar with commercial landscape equipment and their functions</li>
</ul>
<div><i>Other Knowledge, Skills &amp; Abilities</i></div>
<ul>
<li>High aptitude for driving sales</li>
<li>Strong customer service skills, with internal and external clients</li>
<li>Effective oral and written communication</li>
<li>Advanced knowledge of scheduling, budgeting, document management,and cost forecasting</li>
<li>Ability to read and interpret constructions plans/drawings</li>
<li>Experience working the commercial property managers, vendors,consultants and architects a plus</li>
<li>Ability to prioritize multiple tasks and perform well under deadlines</li>
<li>Organizational skills</li>
<li>Flexibility</li>
<li>Advanced presentation skills</li>
<li>Intermediate to advanced understanding of SalesForce, Microsoft OfficeSuite, and Project Management Software</li>
<li>Intermediate to advanced experience using design software, including</li>
<li>Photoshop, Adobe Creative Suite, AutoCAD, and ProLandscape</li>
</ul>
