<p>
Superlite, a CRH company, is a division within Oldcastle APG. APG is North America’s leading manufacturer and supplier of concrete masonry, dry mix, and hardscape products. With over $2B in sales, APG operates across 36 states and 5 Canadian provinces through a network of over 195 operating locations and more than 6,500 employees.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Job Description / Summary</b><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
Operate 8,000 to 10,000 Lb. forklift to move product into inventory/onto and off truck</li>
<li>
Load trucks with finished product for delivery</li>
<li>
Label product and package product for inventory</li>
<li>
Ensure packaged quantities are correct and quality is up to standards</li>
<li>
Complete daily paperwork including inspection reports for all equipment</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Requirements / Education / Experience</b><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
High school diploma or GED equivalent and 1+ years' related work experience</li>
<li>
Must be able to work any shift</li>
<li>
Ability to read and interpret loading slips, safety rules, operating and maintenance instructions, and procedure manuals</li>
<li>
Ability to carry out instructions written and oral</li>
<li>
Mathematical skills including addition, subtraction, multiplication and division, and understanding of units of measure</li>
<li>
Lifting up to 75 lbs</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Preferences</b><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
Fork Lift Certified</li>
<li>
Ability to work in a team environment</li>
<li>
Previous experience in a manufacturing environment</li>
<li>
Comfortable working majority of shift in outdoor environment</li>
</ul>
