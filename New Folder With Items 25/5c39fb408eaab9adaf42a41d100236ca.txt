<h2>Escrow Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Escrow Assistant</b></p>
<p>Desirable escrow office seeking a FULL TIME self-motivated, accountable, trustworthy, friendly experienced escrow assistant. Must have escrow experience and strong working knowledge of the RBJ escrow processing system.</p>
<p>Daily responsibilities will include but not limited to:</p>
<ul>
<li>Prepare escrow instructions, open orders, packaging loan documents, setting up files, closing out sale and refinance transactions, daily banking, disbursing daily mail and answering phones.</li>
<li>Act as a liaison and maintain communication between customers and all parties involved in the transaction</li>
<li>Submit post-closing documents to lender to obtain funding and disbursement approval</li>
<li>Request documents from Title Companies, Lenders and Insurance Companies.</li>
<li>Assist Escrow Officer in all tasks related to client’s transactions.</li>
<li>Ensure all parties including the buyers, sellers and any agents receive copies of final settlement statements prior to closing, after receiving lenders final approval</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Qualifications</p>
<ul>
<li>The ideal candidate will have strong working knowledge of escrow procedures / regulations.</li>
<li>Strong knowledge of RBJ Software System</li>
<li>Must have strong phone and communication skills</li>
<li>Self-Motivated and Positive attitude</li>
<li>Deadline orientated</li>
<li>All potential hires will require a background check.</li>
</ul>
