<h2>Estimator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Primary Duties</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Experience and knowledge of General Construction with an emphasis on Commercial Exterior Architectural finish materials.</li>
<li>Review of Design Intent Documents or Construction Documents provided to understand scope and specification of opportunity.</li>
<li>Ability to complete on screen take off using Bluebeam or similar software to determine area or quantity requirements of materials or services being estimated.</li>
<li>Develop estimate workbooks with detailed quantities of products and the costs required.</li>
<li>Develop installation costs and other services costs to complete the scope required.</li>
<li>Ensure all working documents and data are maintained to back-up estimate figures.</li>
<li>Advanced knowledge of estimating techniques and cost control.</li>
<li>Estimating skills with ability to understand price and explain estimate content and approach.</li>
<li>Ability to multitask and prepare multiple estimates and proposals with minimum supervision.</li>
<li>Experience with Aluminum Composite Metal (ACM) panels or other Architectural Exterior Finishes including but not limited to, Corrugated Metals, Insulated Metal Panels, Fiber Cement, Wood Grain, etc.</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Job Specifications:</li></ul>
<p>
Education Requirement – Degree in Construction or Engineering or Associates degree (or equivalent experience).</p>
<p>
Experience – 3-5 years construction estimating. Experience in a fast-paced work environment. Thorough knowledge of estimating techniques, engineering disciplines and cost control systems required. Demonstrate excellent verbal and written communication skills. Ability to read and understand construction documents and specifications. Proficiency with Microsoft Office and Bluebeam Revu required. AutoCAD and Sales Force a plus but not required.</p>
<p>
Job Type: Full-time</p>
