<p><b>
Position Overview</b></p>
<p>
We are currently searching for an Operations Analyst with strong, analytical, process improvement and project management skills. This high profile, high impact position will support key areas of the Commercial &amp; Industrial (C&amp;I) teams including: sales, customer service and category/merchandising. The role will rely on effective collaboration with IT, inventory management, purchasing among other departments. The primary responsibility of this position is to lead tasks/projects around sales reporting, sales and margin effectiveness and process improvement. Additionally, this position will play a role in further developing reporting and business analytic tools for the C&amp;I groups.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities Include:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Document IT requirements to capture the voice of the business and manage pipeline of enhancements, includes acceptance testing and change management.</li>
<li>Lead project management and implementation of operational change management projects with key stakeholders across business.</li>
<li>Work with previous process owners to obtain a thorough knowledge of current practices and procedures.</li>
<li>Analyze the as is process to identify pain points and areas for improvement in direct order management.</li>
<li>Aid in business requirement definition, testing and idea sharing on process improvement.</li>
<li>Manage BI tool within the division including development of ad hoc reports.</li>
<li>Work closely with the Director of Information Technology and the IT team to improve current tools and develop new processes.</li>
<li>Co-Lead deployment of E-Commerce system and functionality with the Director of Sales Operations including owning user experience.</li>
<li>Aid in business requirement definition, testing and idea sharing on Sales Driving Technology Suite for North American.</li>
<li>Work with previous process owners to obtain a thorough knowledge of current practices and procedures.</li>
<li>Analyze the as is process to identify pain points and areas for improvement in direct order management.</li>
<li>Aid in business requirement definition, testing and idea sharing on process improvement.</li>
<li>Document and lead training across applications and processes within divisions.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>The ideal candidate will have:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>2 - 5 years’ experience in business analytics, change management and/or process improvement.</li>
<li>0 - 2 years of customer facing formal presentations.</li>
<li>1 - 3 years of technology development experience.</li>
<li>Advance Microsoft applications experience with reporting tool experience.</li>
<li>Strong communication skills across all levels of organization.</li>
<li>Ability to drive a cross-functional internal team through the change management process.</li>
</ul>
