<h2>Account Director, Brand Partnerships</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Complex Networks</b> formed from the acquisition of Complex Media Inc. through a joint venture by Verizon and Hearst. Today, it is a global lifestyle brand and media company and one of the most influential voices in popular culture. <b>Complex Networks’</b> digital channels, which include <i>Complex News, First We Feast, Pigeons &amp; Planes, Sole Collector</i> and more, are the Top 10 publishers in the U.S. for social engagement on channels like Facebook and YouTube. A diversified and profitable media business with premium distribution partners such as Netflix, Hulu, Pluto TV, Roku, and iFlix, Complex Networks’ original content exemplifies convergence culture, exploring topics that span across popular culture from music to movies, sports to video games, fashion to food.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Complex</b> seeks an experienced, motivated, and sales-driven <b>Account Director </b>to join our <b>Los Angeles Brand Partnerships</b> <b>team</b>. You will be a part of a top-notch sales team, selling integrated marketing programs across Complex’s network of sites, custom and sponsored video, branded content and ComplexCon sponsor packages. This solution-oriented individual will be responsible for creating partnerships across multiple brands and initiatives while maximizing revenue.</p>
<ul><li><b>
This role is based in Beverly Hills</b></li></ul>
<p><b>
Complex</b> is the perfect environment for team players with a passion for never-been-done-before programs, branded content creation, building custom ad units and developing long-term partnerships. This is an excellent opportunity to be part of a growing brand and team while earning a very competitive compensation.</p>

RESPONSIBILITIES:
