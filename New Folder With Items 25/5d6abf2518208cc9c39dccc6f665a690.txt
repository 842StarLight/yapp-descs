<p>For more than 75 years, Grifols has worked to improve the health and well-being of people around the world. We are a global healthcare company that produces essential plasma-derived medicines for patients and provides hospitals and healthcare professionals with the tools, information and services they need to deliver expert medical care.</p>
<p>Grifols has been a leader in the healthcare industry since 1940 by creating innovative products and services based on the values of ethics, quality and responsibility. Grifols' activities focus on fulfilling the needs of its patients as well as healthcare professionals working in therapeutics, pharmacy, diagnostics and blood banking. For more than 75 years, Grifols has developed, manufactured and marketed product designed to improve human health. Grifols manufactures plasma derived biopharmaceutical products of proven efficacy, quality and safety. For more information, please visit our website: http://www.grifols.com

<p><b>Position Overview:</b></p>
<p>The Associate Director is a key member of the Consumer/Patient Marketing team focused on consumer and patient marketing, providing support and coordination of key initiatives that are critical to helping the IG and alpha-1 franchise achieve their business objectives. This individual will manage existing tactical marketing programs, provide support at the direction of the consumer/patient marketing leads during the development of new projects and initiatives, as well as serve as a the patient advocacy liaison between Consumer/Patient marketing team and other key cross-functional teams (e.g. HCP Marketing teams, Sales, Public Policy, Commercial Leadership and Patient Advocacy Groups).. The Associate Director must be an organized multitasker able to handle many diverse projects at once and meet tight deadlines.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities include, but are not limited to the following:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Maintain a working knowledge of product features/benefits, competitive activity, and current customers</li>
<li>Collaborate with Consumer/Patient marketing leads to develop agreed upon tactics that align to Consumer/Patient marketing strategy and executional plan including GRP submission, review and approval, interaction with MCM team members (where applicable), material development oversight</li>
<li>Participate in key strategy discussions with Consumer/Patient marketing team members as well as market research development and execution</li>
<li>Act as marketing coordinator for Patient Advocacy groups for prioritized key disease states (alpha-1, CIDP and PIDD - initially) in coordination with key internal stakeholders (HCP marketing teams, public policy, sales, nurse educators). Work with marketing leadership and marketing representatives who have built relationships with patient advocacy organizations to ensure awareness across the marketing team and begin to build a comprehensive plan.</li>
<li>Manage patient event materials inventory and work with sales teams to ensure support for local events</li>
<li>Agency and/or vendor management of assigned projects; project brief development and approval, timeline and budget project management, KPI identification, measurement plan development and on-going monitoring</li>
</ul>
