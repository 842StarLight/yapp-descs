<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Description</b></h1>
<p>Title: Regional Vice President – South</p>
<p>Job Type: Permanent</p>
<p>Location: Lake Mary-FL or Other Sungard AS Office Location</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Opportunity:</b></p>
<p>The Regional Vice President is a strategic leader in the Sungard Availability Services Sales organization and is responsible for the enhancement and management of a defined territory. The Regional Vice President operates with a “pipeline mentality” to encourage transparency and visibility in the organization and will utilize a standardized set of key performance indicators to measure performance and ensure attainment of target revenues and other sales goals. This position has prominent visibility both internally and externally with senior level executives. The Regional Vice President is responsible for working with his/her peers and his/her team of Account Executives on sales initiatives to drive growth, retention, and overall client satisfaction through account planning, opportunity development, and revenue retention.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ensuring that his/her sales team is skilled at selling total solutions, which entails identifying and closing qualified prospects that have needs for the complete complement of Sungard Availability Services.
</li>
<li>Maintaining the highest quality sales team possible by continually ensuring the entire team is trained in all products and services offered by Sungard Availability Services.</li>
<li>Ensuring that all new hires to the sales team are brought up to speed and are productive in the shortest time frame possible.</li>
<li>Managing the volume and distribution of leads in the assigned territory. The Regional Vice President must ensure that every lead generated in the assigned territory is contacted and qualified in a timely and professional manner.</li>
<li>Actively managing the measurement and performance of all team members to achieve organizational objectives against KPI’s, conducting quarterly reviews of achievements, mid-year reviews, and end of year reviews of performance vs. targets.</li>
<li>Work supportively with colleagues, operating in a collegiate manner at all times and in line with the Company’s values.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>An undergraduate degree is required, and a graduate degree is preferred, with at least one degree in a technical field. A minor in Finance would be helpful.
</li>
<li>Minimum of 5 years of successful sales and/or sales leadership experience within a large technical account environment: previous proven expertise in managed services.</li>
<li>Experience selling high availability solutions, telecom, consulting and hardware/software solution sales highly preferred.</li>
<li>Previous management or supervisory experience required.</li>
<li>Able to motivate the team to reach assigned sales and performance goals.</li>
<li>Able to cultivate and coordinate key internal and external relationships.</li>
<li>Proven ability to hire, train, develop, and retain a team of top sales professionals.</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills.</li>
<li>Exceptional presentation skills.</li>
<li>Basic skills in the Microsoft Office (Outlook, Word).
</li>
<li>Travel is required.</li>
</ul>
