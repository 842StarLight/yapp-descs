<h2>Material Handler - Order Puller (1st Shift)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>AFL provides industry-leading fiber optic products and services across the globe. Our company was founded in 1984 with a single fiber optic cable and now we manufacture over two thousand products, employ over 5,000 associates and consistently generate annual sales in excess of a billion-dollars in revenue. With our commitment to professional growth and employee development, let AFL “Connect” you to your next career opportunity!</p>
<p>
The Material Handler - Order Puller is responsible for assuring that manufacturing discrete jobs are completed accurately, orders packaged according to customer and / or stock requirement, and picks ran to meet customer deliveries.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Complete correct quantity for discrete jobs into inventory</li>
<li>Package inventory according to requirements</li>
<li>Perform correct picks and inventory transfers in Oracle to support customer deliveries and maintain inventory accuracy</li>
<li>Keep inventory loss at a minimum</li>
<li>Correctly complete discrete jobs to deliver manufactured accessories to customer(s) or stock</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Personal Qualities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Communication – possess strong written and verbal skills</li>
<li>Attitude – maintains positive outlook and response under pressure.</li>
<li>Problem Solving – ability to diagnose and recommend solutions to resolve issues</li>
<li>Detailed – able to keep detailed and accurate records</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Familiar with MS office software</li>
<li>Oracle Software experience preferred</li>
<li>Fork lift certification</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Working Conditions</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Exposer to loud noises or fumes.</li>
</ul>
