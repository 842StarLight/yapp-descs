<p>The requisition is for a Server Customer Program Manager role in the Enterprise Solutions Engineering Organization. This individual will be responsible for end-to-end support for assigned North American Server OEM program(s), from initial development through End-of-Life. Responsibilities include defining and managing key product deliverables/schedules and driving cross functional activities to achieve both AMD and customer goals.<br>
<br>
The applicant must be a team player with a commitment to meeting deadlines and an aptitude to thrive in a fast paced, multi-tasking environment.</p>
<div>Job Responsibilities:<br>
<ul>
<li>Primary support contact, in development and sustaining, for server customer program teams</li>
<li>Understand the customer’s business goals and requirements; Foster relationships and manage customer expectations to ensure needs are aligned with AMD business goals</li>
<li>Be a strong advocate for the customer internally</li>
<li>Align with AMD cross-functional teams (BU, Sales, Engineering, …) on customer engagement, with emphasis on driving technical enablement/communications, technical support, and debug/issue resolution</li>
<li>Maintain awareness of, and provide status reports on, customer program schedule, status and key issues</li>
<li>Identify program risks and develop mitigation plans</li>
<li>Manage customer critical issues and escalations</li>
<li>Manage customer sampling requirements in development</li>
<li>Drive for highest levels of customer satisfaction</li>
</ul>
<br>
Preferred Experience :<br>
<ul>
<li>8+ years of experience in the computing industry, at the OEM, ODM, or technology provider level</li>
<li>5+ years of experience managing and delivering programs</li>
<li>Experiencing leading cross-functional and cross-regional teams to deliver program goals</li>
<li>Outstanding written and oral communication skills</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Results driven</li>
<li>BS, or higher, in Engineering (Computer or Electrical) strongly preferred</li>
<li>PMP certification a plus, but not required</li>
</ul>
