<h2>Graphics Production/Client Services Coordinator - InDesign</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>JOB SUMMARY</b><br>
<br>
Responsibilities include preparing offering memorandums/multipage booklets, proposals, presentations and communication materials related to commercial real estate sales. The position prefers advanced experience in Adobe Creative Suite with an emphasis on InDesign. Will focus on the layout design and print production to produce quality design. The individual will coordinate the integration of design components with photography, text copy, and financial information provided by internal team members, clients, and third-party vendors.<br>
<div>
</div>
<div>
<br>
<b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b><br>
<br>
Produces graphic design artwork to support team effort using current technology and Adobe Creative Suite (InDesign, Illustrator and Photoshop) for both print and web<br>
<br>
Designs offering memorandums, brochures, advertising, postcards, proposals, and presentations within corporate brand guidelines for both print and web<br>
<br>
Can handle multiple projects within clearly defined timelines with quality results<br>
<br>
Confers with team members and external clients to discuss and determine design goals and project direction<br>
<br>
Works with vendors to manage project timelines and oversee production of in-house and outsourced materials to 3rd party vendors.<br>
<br>
Researches, compares and recommends local vendors, including printers, paper suppliers and online services to complete projects<br>
<br>
Liaises, delegates, and assigns responsibilities to internal and external design resources<br>
<br>
Participates in the brain storming sessions, strategy, and branding meetings for marketing properties<br>
<br>
Acts as single point of contact for all design materials coming in from team members, including material related to marketing, research and financial analysis that will be added to layouts<br>
<br>
</div>
<div>Other duties may be assigned.<br>
