<div><b>
Position Description</b></div>
<div>
Tenna is searching for an experienced Customer Success Manager to join the team. The Customer Success Manager’s two primary focuses are to engage directly with customers and to assist existing customers in maximizing the Tenna platform. The Customer Success Manager will work closely with the Software Engineering Team and Sales Team to provide top notch customer support and fuel our customer growth efforts. Successful candidates will have a passion for the delivery of SaaS solutions and the IoT revolution!</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Provide exceptional customer support to all end users as well as Sales Personnel.
</li>
<li>Develop a deep understanding of the Tenna platform and take ownership of customer engagement, retention and satisfaction.
</li>
<li>Maintain processes to manage our customer on-boarding process.</li>
<li>
Set-up and on-board new customers and users upon request.
</li>
<li>Provide swift and polite communication with customers who call or email Tenna support.
</li>
<li>Ensure day-to-day customer requests and issues are handled in a thoughtful, urgent manner.</li>
<li>
Deliver customer training sessions, providing answers to customer questions and resolving user issues.</li>
<li>
Trouble-shoot and resolve customer issues via phone (or in-person) using our diagnostic tools.</li>
<li>
Submit support tickets and escalate issues in a timely manner.</li>
<li>
Work with Engineering Lead (or Technical Support Lead) to address customer tickets including bugs and enhancements.</li>
<li>
Provide training to current customers, Sales Personnel, and other Business Managers on new functionality and features as they are released.
</li>
<li>Provide User feedback to Sales Managers, Marketing Managers, Product Managers and the Engineering Team.
</li>
<li>Engage existing Customers to retain and renew Tenna memberships.</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
