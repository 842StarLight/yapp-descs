<h2>Copywriter & Social Media Content Creator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>SUMMARY: </b>The Copywriter &amp; Social Media Content Creator will be a part of the marketing &amp; digital team and will be responsible for creating and publishing digital content that aligns with established style guides, brand voice and merchandising practices to support the social and e-commerce channels. This individual will help drive awareness, engagement and product education through the creation of compelling blog, social media and e-commerce content.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Core Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Has a passion for writing—we will generate a lot of content.
</li>
<li>Proofread and edit your copy so it’s typo-free and matches our style and brand voice across all channels to ensure accurate and consistent tone of voice.
</li>
<li>Understand different campaign initiatives and write clear and compelling copy.
</li>
<li>Partner with marketing, merchandising, online sales and production teams to ensure copy is brand appropriate, optimizing for each channel/touchpoint.
</li>
<li>Responsible for maintaining the tone of voice and copywriting for all channels including email marketing, push, social, website, store materials, etc.
</li>
<li>Explore different ideas and concepts for both the visual and verbal elements with the Social Media &amp; Content team and Brand &amp; Creative Services team to help develop integrated campaigns and concepts.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Desired Skills &amp; Experiences:</b>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 1 to 3 years relevant work experience in copywriting</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree in English, Journalism, Business Writing, Advertising or related discipline, minimum 1 to 3 years relevant work experience in copywriting.</li>
<li>Strong attention to details, team orientation and communication skills along with at least 1 year of experience in a social media copy role</li>
<li>Meticulous editor with exceptional command of English grammar, spelling and punctuation</li>
<li>Experience in storyboard &amp; script writing for video content a plus
</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to generate content quickly and efficiently
</li>
<li>Possesses a can-do attitude and is self-motivated
</li>
<li>Must submit a variety of writing samples
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>COMPETENCIES:
</b></p>
<p><b>Written Communication-</b>Writes clearly and informatively; Edits work for spelling and grammar; Varies writing style to meet needs; Presents numerical data effectively; Able to read and interpret written information.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Teamwork-</b>Balances team and individual responsibilities; Exhibits objectivity and openness to others' views; Gives and welcomes feedback; Contributes to building a positive team spirit; Puts success of team above own interests; Able to build morale and group commitments to goals and objectives; Supports everyone's efforts to succeed.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Quality-</b>Demonstrates accuracy and thoroughness; Looks for ways to improve and promote quality; Applies feedback to improve performance; Monitors own work to ensure quality.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Adaptability-</b>Adapts to changes in the work environment; Manages competing demands; Changes approach or method to best fit the situation; Able to deal with frequent change, delays, or unexpected events.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Innovation-</b>Displays original thinking and creativity; Meets challenges with resourcefulness; Generates suggestions for improving work; Develops innovative approaches and ideas; Presents ideas and information in a manner that gets others' attention.</p>
</div></div>
