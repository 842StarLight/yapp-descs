Summary
<br><br>
The Sr. Sales Manager role is focused developing, hiring and managing Inside Sales Managers who oversee the Inside Cloud Communication Consultants (Account Executives). The Sr. Sales Manager will provide strategic direction to the inside sales teams and drive increased sales revenue. The Sr. Sales manager will exhibit strong self-motivation, self-discipline, project management skills, ability to influence.
<br><br>
The Sr. Sales Manager will manage the relationships with operations leadership, develop and implement strategic initiatives, drive projects to completion and provide counsel to all levels of leadership and management employees. They will be involved in many diverse functions: from managing, coaching and expansion of the sales team, to the development and implementation of repeatable, predictable sales and operations processes, to organizational design and development, and ever evolving growth of the business.
<br><br>
<b>The ideal candidate will possess the following:</b> Solid understanding of fundamental root cause analysis, expertise in providing necessary training, coaching developmental opportunities; excellent oral and written communication skills; proven ability to formulate and implement strategic initiatives, and creativity to develop innovative solutions to drive revenue; and excellent ability to foster and grow relationships from line level employees to executive management.
<br><br>
Key Responsibilities
<br><br>
<b>Core responsibilities of the Senior Sales Manager include:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Hire, train, mentor and manage performance of Inside Sales Managers</li>
<li>Provide daily coaching and guidance to direct reports (first-line managers)</li>
<li>Responsible for daily huddles with their team to provide day-to-day direction and business updates.</li>
<li>Responsible for conducting 1 on 1's with their team members throughout the month to discuss, performance, trends, development, ETC.</li>
<li>Ensure clarity and execution around Nextiva's business objectives and goals for their team(s)</li>
<li>Conduct structured and effective pipeline management review</li>
<li>Lead hands-on live coaching and involvement in deal progression, pricing and deal escalation</li>
<li>Actively engaged in Employee Relations and personnel discussions related to teammate separations, etc.</li>
<li>Effectively guide talent identification and development processes for the group</li>
<li>Working across functions with peers in other groups to ensure collaboration for shared goals (cross functional leadership alignment)</li>
<li>Works with senior leadership and other peers for strategy execution planning</li>
<li>Ensure delivery of monthly, quarterly and annual sales goals; manage a weekly revenue forecast to Director of Sales</li>
<li>Communicates goal and results and key performance indicators to direct reports</li>
<li>Facilitates goal-level creation for the broader function and working with managers to ensure the goals cascade to all inside sales teammates</li>
<li>Conduct detailed quarterly business plan presentations / reviews to analyze the state of their business</li>
<li>Manage to sales business rules, ROE, practices and policies</li>
</ul>
Qualifications
<br><br>
<b>Key qualifications of the Senior Sales Manager include:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's Degree in Business Administration or a related field, MBA a plus</li>
<li>Minimum 5 years of successful previous experience as a Sales Leader (or similar), managing a team, and consistently meeting or exceeding targets</li>
<li>Experience with Salesforce</li>
<li>Strong written and verbal communication skills: the ability to speak knowledgeably, clearly and persuasively to a diverse range of audiences</li>
<li>Experience in UCaaS/VoIP industry</li>
<li>Data-driven, analytical, and fact-based approach to making business recommendations and decisions</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to lead, coach, and manage a team using metrics to drive sales performance</li>
<li>Effectively presents data and business insights to Executive Leadership</li>
</ul>
