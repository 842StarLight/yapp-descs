<h2>Modern Workplace Specialist (Education)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>US Education Classroom Experience Team is looking for an elite and experienced Education Solution Sales Solutions Professional to join our East Region team. In this role you will be responsible for uncovering and identifying, qualifying, closing and deploying strategic Classroom solutions within our largest accounts with the backdrop of the One Microsoft story. This role will focus on both K-12 and Higher Education customers in the territory. You will collaborate with the ATU, STU, and various key US and WW EDU stakeholders to accelerate the building of our device and productivity selling capabilities with customers in both K-12 and Higher Education. You will partner with field and partner resources to increase Windows and O365 usage in the classroom, grow the adoption and usage of O365 (through applications like OneNote, OneDrive, and Office Web Apps) with emphasis on TEAMs usage while growing share against fierce competition. You will work directly with both business and technical decision makers, partners and local ATUs to sell and achieve quarterly Windows Device Activations and O365 Monthly Active Usage, revenue and metric accountabilities.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The ideal candidate will have significant experience in customer facing sales roles, be an industry subject matter, and have experience in hardware and operating systems and will have had success in delivering complex mobile solutions to customers. As a Classroom SSP, you will be responsible for leading the end to end sales strategy, development and execution for select states within the West &amp; Central Region. This includes direct accountabilities for all selling motions, achieving sales metrics and business scale to grow Windows unit shipment, activation, and share.</div>
