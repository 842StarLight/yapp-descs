<div><ul>

Develop our Global Sales / Revenue Strategy (Revenue target, channels, sales plan). Accountable for developing sales goals, quotas, compensation plans for the development of sales training
</li>
<li>Establish highly repeatable (and scalable) sales processes, automation and reporting

<li>Oversee sales team productivity and track critical KPI’s</li>

Understand the drivers of effective retail chain, mass merchant, membership club, brand direct, licensing and enterprise sales planning and implementation</li>
<li>
Create and maintain detailed models and forecasts to support both short and long-term strategic planning</li>
