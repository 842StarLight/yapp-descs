<h2>Tax Analyst II</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Under supervision from the Tax Director, prepares tax returns and other related filings for Dawn to comply with applicable federal, state and local tax laws. Communicates internally with other Dawn departments and externally with customers, vendors and government agents on issues of tax compliance and processes. Responsibilities:
<br><br>
Prepares federal, state and local income tax returns, supporting schedules and documentation.
<br><br>
Prepares federal, state and local income tax estimated payments and tax return extensions.
<br><br>
Prepares state and local sales tax returns, supporting schedule and documentation.
<br><br>
Prepares documents computing US income tax provision and audited financial tax footnote disclosures.
<br><br>
Prepares personal property tax returns and processes real and personal property tax payments.
<br><br>
Prepares annual reports, business licenses, government surveys, and miscellaneous tax returns.
<br><br>
Reconciles various tax accounts within the general ledger.
<br><br>
Demonstrates the ability to assist in planning and preparing for, and executing, comprehensive projects from beginning to end.
<br><br>
Demonstrates the ability to research complex tax questions.
<br><br>
Provides support for tax audits.
<br><br>
Performs other related duties as assigned or requested.
<br><br>
Demonstrates the "Dawn Values" of Team Dawn and supports the Circle of Excellence.
<br><br>
Occasional travel may be required to Dawn manufacturing plants and Dawn distribution centers.
<br><br>
<b>Qualifications:</b><br>
Bachelor's Degree in Business Administration, Finance, Accounting or related field
<br><br>
Advance certification preferred - CPA
<br><br>
A minimum of 4 years of related work experience with direct experience in public accounting or Tax or Finance Department in industry.
<br><br>
Strong oral and written communication skills, time management skills and organization skills.
<br><br>
Ability to provide strong attention to detail and accuracy of work.
<br><br>
Good multi-tasking ability.
<br><br>
Ability to work within required time frames or due dates.
<br><br>
Ability to work independently without immediate supervision.
<br><br>
Good working knowledge of tax and accounting principles and procedures.
<br><br>
Ability to research complex tax issues, efficiently navigating internet tax research services, income tax software and property tax software.
<br><br>
Proficiency with Excel, Word and PowerPoint and knowledge of SAP and SharePoint.
<br><br>
Positive attitude and strong teaming skills, both as a team leader and as a team member.
