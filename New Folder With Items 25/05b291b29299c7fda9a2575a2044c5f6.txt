Roles &amp; Responsibilities</div>
<div><div>
<div>
<p><b>Vox Mobile is seeking a Financial Accountant to work closely with the Controller to complete the month end closing process and financial statements, and other Accounts Payable/Accounting projects as assigned.</b></p>
<p>The <b>Financial Accountant</b> will work closely with the Controller to complete the month end closing process, review and update financial statements, perform general accounting duties such as processing vendor invoices, monthly customer billing activities, payroll processing, collection activities and other projects as assigned. The role is an integral part of the Vox Mobile Finance team providing key assistance to the monthly financial reporting process and ensuring the integrity of the financial data.</p>
<p><b>Location:</b> 6100 Rockside Woods Blvd Suite 100, Independence OH 44131</p>
<p><b>Schedule:</b> Monday-Friday 8:00am – 5:00pm, may require work after hours and/or on weekends to meet deliverables.
</p>
<p><b>Company Overview:</b></p>
<p><b>Vox Mobile</b>, a leader in enterprise mobility management is headquartered in <b>Independence, Ohio</b>. With offices in the United States and Canada, Vox Mobile is a Gartner-recognized leader in Managed Mobility Services (MMS) providing world-class support and services to organizations throughout the globe.</p>
<p>At the core of our success is a high energy, talented staff with diverse skills and a unified focus on delivering product excellence and customer value. We enjoy a modernized, open concept work environment with a tech-casual dress code.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b><b>
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Follow our companies established accounting processes</li>
<li>Process Payroll bi-weekly</li>
<li>Calculate and process commission payments</li>
<li>Process month end journal entries</li>
<li>Assist in completing the monthly financial statements</li>
<li>General ledger analysis of accounts</li>
<li>Interface with the NetSuite ERP system and Sales Force CRM systems</li>
<li>Daily A/P and A/R functions
</li>
<li>Perform monthly bank reconciliations
</li>
<li>Prepare sales tax returns for multiple states</li>
<li>Process expense reports for employees</li>
</ul>
