Job Description
<br>American Water Works Service Company, Inc. (Camden, NJ) is seeking Sr. Technologists - SAP Functional Analyst - Finance (multiple openings); Lead and support projects across multiple business functions specializing in the SAP Finance module of FICO. The position requires a minimum of a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Computer Applications, Information Technology, Electrical Engineering, or a related Engineering field, and 5 years' progressive, post-baccalaureate experience in the job offered or any related occupation, to include, 5 years' of experience gathering requirements, analyzing, designing, developing, testing, debugging, and enhancing ERP computer software applications in the SAP system; 5 years' of experience automating financial processes in the area of general ledger accounting, accounts receivable, accounts payables, cash management and integration of SAP FICO module with sales and distribution module etc.; 5 years' of experience configuring, building, and initial testing and establishing configuration related to organizational structure, business processes and master data; and 5 years' of experience testing the integration of the solutions with other applications. Employer will accept any suitable combination of education, experience, and training. Experiences may be gained concurrently. The position requires up to 15% domestic travel.
