<h2>VP of Sales</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Digital Remedy is a digital media execution company, that is capable of saving you time, money, and resources because of our multi-partner model. We are not committed to any single inventory source, though work within all of them.</div>
<div></div>
<div>We are experts in all digital media disciplines, and our workflow platform builds efficiencies through-out the entire media cycle. Our clients can leverage our 20 years of experience alongside an entire industry of tech.</div>
<div></div>
<div>We are seeking a VP of Sales to play a critical role within our energetic, talented, client services team. The VP of Sales will lead sales development and oversee the programmatic sales team for our enterprise channel.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Responsibilities</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Hire, train, and manage sales team in the US</li>
<li>Assist sales reps to hit quarterly sales goals through mentoring, introductions, and training</li>
<li>Conduct 2-3 structured meetings/calls each week with each sales person</li>
<li>Weekly meeting with the entire sales team, updating on best practices, collateral, company news and next steps</li>
<li>Weekly meeting with EVP of Sales to update on progress</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Our ideal candidate</b></div>
<ul>
<li>8+ years of experience working at media sales organization selling brand advertising campaigns, mobile experience a plus</li>
<li>1-2 years of experience sales management in a media organization</li>
<li>Experience managing a team of 3+ sellers in multiple regions</li>
<li>BA/BS degree preferred</li>
<li>A strong set of existing relationships with, brands and agencies at all levels</li>
<li>Proven track record of building relationships with the media/advertising industry</li>
<li>Deep understanding of the programmatic advertising and advertising ecosystems</li>
<li>2-3 years of experience selling programmatic advertising or educating brands, agencies and media companies about programmatic advertising preferred</li>
<li>Experience in or a passion for advertising</li>
<li>Exceptional consultative and solution-driven relationship skills</li>
<li>High energy, self-motivated and passionate about working in a fast-paced, ever-changing environment</li>
</ul>
