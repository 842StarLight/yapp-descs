<div><div>Account Manager<br>
<br>
QUALIFICATIONS:<br>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Marketing or Business Administration.</li>
<br>
<li>Proven “top down” selling experience and relationship management with Senior Executives.</li>
<br>
<li>Solid sales and marketing experience in the automotive industry sector.</li>
<br>
<li>Technical – Must be proficient in Microsoft Word, and Power Point software. Testing systems fro skill, and knowledge.</li>
<br>
<li>Communication – Excellent written and oral communication skills. Strong presentation and facilitating skills.</li>
<br>
<li>Analytical – Ability to interpret numerical information for costing and analysis for feasibility to quote on business.</li>
<br>
<li>Problem Solving – Solid conflict resolution and problem solving skills.</li>
<br>
<br>
RESPONSIBILITIES:<br>
<li>Identify short and annual targets for future customer business opportunities using market intelligence and new contact leads.</li>
<br>
<li>Establish a consistent prospecting pipeline of business leads and confirm potential opportunities through needs analysis of leads.</li>
<br>
<li>Develop an annual action plan to support business group revenue goals and maintain quarterly revenue forecasts against annual target.</li>
<br>
<li>Identify client needs and provide effective solutions based on Linamar’s core business offerings and services.</li>
<br>
<li>Create and deliver business solution presentation to internals and external business executives.</li>
<br>
<li>Develop a sales strategy and implementation plan to close new business to achieve annual financial targets.</li>
<br>
<li>Develop a solid understanding and relationship with the various Group business staff to support full understanding of Linamar’s overall business capabilities.</li>
<br>
<li>Resolve conflict situations as needed, to ensure the most beneficial application of business opportunities are achieved within the Linamar long term business objectives.</li>
<br>
<li>Ensure all pertinent information for quotes and unique customer requirements are presented and addressed in a timely manner.</li>
