<h2>Account Manager (Supplier/Vendor Members)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>HARDI (Heating, Air-Conditioning, Refrigeration, Distributors International) is currently seeking a Membership &amp; Sales Representative to serve as one of the Association’s primary member-facing team members. This role’s primary function will serve as the Association’s membership engagement and sales arm. This role's main responsibilities will be current-member feedback, membership inquiries, program sales, sponsorship sales, new member recruitment and other various related sales tasks as required. The Membership &amp; Sales Rep must be member-obsessed, organized and detail-oriented as they will be responsible for building and maintaining relationships with current and potential members. This individual will also have a key role in database management and will be well versed in all facets of association programming.</p>
<p><b>Specific Accountabilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provide consultation and expert advice to membership about HARDI programs and services through various interactions both in-person and remotely</li>
<li>Research, prospect and establish leads for new members and corporate relationships ensuring an introductory needs analysis and discovery meeting</li>
<li>Prepare and present proposals to corporate decision makers and potential partners</li>
<li>Creation of pre and post contact/visit reports to document member interactions and feedback from members after outreaches and interactions and implement follow up strategies</li>
<li>Secure annual membership renewals and maintain accurate records in the association management software</li>
<li>Grow revenue streams through promotion of association to new and prospective members</li>
<li>Prepare weekly sales reports, progress updates and innovative plans for revenue growth</li>
<li>Responsible for trade show booth strategy in collaboration with marketing and internal client teams</li>
<li>Manage expense budgets related to the execution of trade show booth strategy</li>
<li>Manage and lead webinars to help promote and sell the association’s programs/services</li>
<li>Develop, implement and maintain methods to conduct and document member satisfaction outreaches to program/service participants</li>
<li>Create awareness of the association through developed community outreach and connectivity strategies</li>
<li>Actively engage in social media platforms to promote current association initiatives and activities</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned to meet association goals and needs</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Minimum Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High School diploma or equivalent required. College degree in business, marketing preferred.</li>
<li>Strong networking and interpersonal skills are a must</li>
<li>Strong communication skills – writing, speaking, presenting</li>
<li>Be able to build rapport easily and establish trust, leading to lasting customer relationships</li>
<li>Must be highly motivated, have a strong work ethic and enjoy the selling process</li>
<li>Sales/membership experience is preferred, especially focused on B2B selling</li>
<li>Prior work with a CRM system is highly desired</li>
<li>Up to 30% travel required</li>
</ul>

<li>Competitive base salary + incentives</li>
<li>Salary is contingent upon level of experience</li>
</ul>

<li>Health, vision, dental coverage</li>
<li>401(k)</li>
</ul>
