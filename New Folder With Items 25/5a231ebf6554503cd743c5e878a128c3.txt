<b>Licensed Health Insurance Agent</b><br>
<br>
The healthcare insurance industry is growing faster than any other insurance sector in the United States. If you are seeking a career where you will make a difference, earn a great income, and find ample job opportunities, this is it.<br>
<br>
Bloom is a healthcare focused insurance agency located in Bloomington, Indiana. We are growing and transforming the healthcare insurance industry through our sales automation technology platform. Our systems optimize the member experience from the first moment a client considers a health plan membership.<br>
<br>
With insurance sales agents licensed in 48 contiguous U.S. states, we are always looking for talented, passionate people to help shape the future of our company. Currently we have an opening for a full-time Health Insurance Agent with an immediate start date. The Health Insurance Agent is responsible for providing customer service, advice and coverage recommendations by enrolling our clients into the appropriate health insurance plans that match their needs.<br>
<br>
No insurance license? No problem! Bloom provides paid training and covers the cost of instructor-led pre-licensing classes, licensing exam, and certification.<br>
<br>

<ul>
<li>Competitive compensation with uncapped earnings</li>
<li>Paid training, licensing and certification</li>
<li>No prospecting or cold calling – customer leads provided</li>
<li>Flexible full-time office hours</li>
<li>Comprehensive health benefits package</li>
<li>Long-term career growth and mentoring</li>
<li>Incentive programs</li>
<li>Collaborative environment with team events and lunches</li>
<li>Community involvement and volunteering</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Develop a working knowledge of different health insurance plans and general Medicare information and share that knowledge with prospective members</li>
<li>Assist callers quickly and professionally by fielding inbound calls to answer their questions and enroll them in a health insurance plan</li>
<li>Reach out via outbound calling to current and prospective plan members who have requested information</li>
<li>Meet sales goals to earn incentives in a friendly call center environment</li>
<li>Provide accurate information to prospects while adhering to Bloom’s quality and compliance standards</li>
<li>Communicate effectively with prospects and plan members over the phone, in-person with co-workers and management</li>
<li>Comfortably navigate between websites and software to assist callers requesting information</li>
</ul>

</ul>
