<h2>Shipping and Receiving Clerk</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Addictive Desert Designs, </b>a leader in the automotive aftermarket industry, is hiring a hardworking Shipping Clerk. This person must pay close attention to detail, be organized, and must have previous shipping experience.</p>
<p><b>Summary of Key Responsibilities: </b></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities and essential job functions include, but are not limited to the following: </b></p>
<p>· Timely preparation of rates and quotes for customers and inside sales agents</p>
<p>· Coordinate the routing and shipping of domestic and international shipments with LTL freight carriers, airline, and ocean carriers</p>
<p>· Support the development of existing and new customers by working closely with the General and Production Managers and the entire sales organization</p>
<p>· Collaborate and build relationships with customers, carriers, and agent representatives regarding shipping, air, ocean and ground to strengthen service competitiveness</p>
<p>· Prepare all necessary shipping documentation related to freight and shipping bookings and process all import/export documentation to clearing agents to include: (HAWB's, MAWB's, AES's, Manifest, Shipping Instructions, Haz Cargo Decl., Bill of Ladings, and Certificate of Origin, etc.)</p>
<p>· Dispatch pick-ups to authorized agents and companies, shipping lines, airlines, trucking companies, clearing agents, warehouses, and all other parties involved in the shipment</p>
<p>· Track and trace shipments, researching and resolving any issues regarding billing or shipping discrepancies, communicate timely correspondence back to both customers and vendors</p>
<p>· Act as a liaison with Custom Brokers and agents for all shipments, along with Ocean and Air Terminal Operators</p>
<p><b>Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities: </b></p>
<p>· Ability to work in a blended operations and customer development role</p>
<p>· Self-motivated, assertive, and responds to production metrics</p>
<p>· Reads, analyzes and interprets highly complex shipping and international regulations and procedures</p>
<p>· Manages complex transactional or emotional customer situations promptly and professionally</p>
<p>· Ability to multitask in a fast paced environment with rapidly changing priorities</p>
<p>· Contributes to building a positive team spirit and puts success of team above own interests</p>
<p>· Ability to read, write, and speak English fluently</p>
<p>· We are a drug free workplace so all applicants must be able to pass background check and drug test.</p>
<p><b>Summary of Experience and Education</b></p>
<p>· High school Diploma or GED</p>
<p>· Preferred but not required, a Bachelor's Degree in Business Administration or Business Process Management with emphasis in Supply Chain Management, Transportation and Logistics, Sales and Marketing, or International Business</p>
<p>· Minimum of two years Shipping and Freight Management with International Forwarding Experience a plus.</p>
<p>· Detailed knowledge of operational procedures regarding freight shipments, air, ocean, import business</p>
<p>· Strong customer service, customer development, or business development experience</p>
<p>· Prior experience with QuickBooks software system preferred but not required</p>
<p>· Experience in Microsoft Office Suite and other spreadsheet applications</p>
<p><b>Compensation: </b></p>
<p>· Compensation $12-$16 depending on experience</p>
<p>· Hours are 8:00 to 5:00 pm week days with minimum after hours requirements</p>
<p>· Benefits available after 60 day probation period</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Salary: $12.00 to $16.00 /hour</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul><li>Shipping and Receiving: 1 year (Preferred)</li></ul>
