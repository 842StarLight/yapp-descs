<p>Corning is one of the world’s leading innovators in materials science. For more than 160 years, Corning has applied its unparalleled expertise in specialty glass, ceramics, and optical physics to develop products that have created new industries and transformed people’s lives.<br>
<br>
Corning succeeds through sustained investment in R&amp;D, a unique combination of material and process innovation, and close collaboration with customers to solve tough technology challenges.<br>
<br>
Our Optical Communications segment has recently evolved from being a manufacturer of optical fiber and cable, hardware and equipment to being a comprehensive provider of industry-leading optical solutions across the broader communications industry.This segment is classified into two main product groupings – carrier network and enterprise network. The carrier network product group consists primarily of products and solutions for optical-based communications infrastructure for services such as video, data and voice communications. The enterprise network product group consists primarily of optical-based communication networks sold to businesses, governments and individuals for their own use.</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Purpose of Position</b></h2>
<p>Meaningful role where you will implement a global Price and Deal Desk structure to drive global consistency. A centralized team will define standard processes and policy development while driving regional execution of a deal process. Main objectives are the definition of the pricing strategy (as per go-to-market strategy), support the commercialization process of new products, the communication of price information to channels, the development and maintenance of pricing policies and thresholds and setting of T&amp;C’s. This also includes transaction approvals and the critical issue process. Are you a self-starter and passionate with a high level of technical curiosity?</p>
<p>Are you dedicated, open to new ideas and recognized for your flexibility to adapt and change?</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Major Responsibilities</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>We lead regional and segment-specific price strategies, in line with the overall corporate strategy and regional Sales Team strategies</li>
<li>Own and nurture the continuous creation and modification of linkages between the various market access strategies and regional specific product strategies into segment specific price strategies and define/ maintain segment specific rules, including consideration of competitive environment data</li>
<li>Committed enabler of deal/opportunity formation by providing system driven target price recommendations for specific opportunity categories, customer segments by implementing and maintaining pre-defined price structures, enabling high system automation on standard deal requests</li>
<li>Build and drrive the implementation of set rules in coordination with PLM &amp; Marketing &amp; Sales that lead to a deep understanding of pricing strategy within all business functions</li>
<li>Drive the definition and roll out of standard business processes around programs such as discretionary discount, opportunity registration and others to enable smooth pre-sales operations</li>
<li>Utilize Data analytics and pricing dashboards and reports for indications of price adjustment opportunities to maintain pricing levels within the defined pricing strategy</li>
<li>Lead price communication towards channels on a regular basis by defining a repeatable process around Price files</li>
<li>Support the development of a roadmap towards system automation in regard to Deal responses, Price data communication.</li>
<li>Lead contract creation and build clear set of Approval matrix for CALM &amp; Tender Review</li>
<li>Act as a main contact towards finance in order to set accruals related to rebates and price support programs</li>
<li>Ensure significant functional linkages are in place throughout the Global Sales and Marketing groups and Customer Care team, PLM, Legal and Global Supply Chain organizations</li>
</ul>
