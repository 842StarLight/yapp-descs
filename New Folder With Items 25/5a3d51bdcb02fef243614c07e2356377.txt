<h2>Field Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>As a project manager at TAC, you’ll drive the installation of audio/video, control, lighting and security systems to successful completion. You’ll be the on-site leader and face of the company while projects are underway</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities include:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ensure that individual installation projects are completed on time, on budget, and meet customer requirements</li>
<li>Work with clients, contractors and sales to determine project scope and requirements</li>
<li>Coordinate the scheduling of equipment, installers, technicians, programmers and subcontractors to meet project timelines</li>
<li>Communicate with clients, staff and other contractors to ensure smooth daily operations</li>
<li>Determine and communicate daily tasks related to individual projects</li>
<li>Resolve conflicts &amp; propose solutions to on-site difficulties</li>
<li>Identify risks ahead of time, and take proactive steps to reduce or eliminate their impact</li>
<li>Propose and implement effective quality control procedures to make sure we’re providing excellent results to clients</li>
<li>Propose and track change orders as the need arises</li>
<li>Keep the TAC databases and staff updated on project statuses and projects</li>
<li>Assist with as-built system documentation</li>
<li>Assist with installation, set-up and testing activities, as needed</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Supervisory Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Run a small crew of 2-5 installers and/or technicians at job sites</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Great communication and interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Excellent customer service and leadership skills</li>
<li>4+ years experience as a project manager in the custom A/V market</li>
<li>4+ years experience working with Crestron, Savant, RTI and/or Lutron control systems (and a willingness to learn those you do not know)</li>
<li>Strong knowledge of A/V and control system products – switchers, cameras, TV’s, speakers, wiring, etc</li>
<li>Ability to travel, sometimes for multiple days in a row. You will be out in the field most days</li>
<li>Strong working knowledge of Microsoft, Google, and/or Apple computing products (hardware, software and cloud)</li>
<li>Experience with residential retrofit construction projects is a big plus</li>
<li>Familiarity with D-Tools is a plus</li>
<li>Clean DMV and able to pass background check</li>
<li>Professional behavior, dress and responsibility at job site and in the office</li>
</ul>
