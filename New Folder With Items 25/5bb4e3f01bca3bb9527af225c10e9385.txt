<h2>Communications Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<div><div>
<p><b>Expand your communications career at Tetra Pak in this dynamic, broad-reaching role.</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>To deliver on our strategic goals, we are seeking a <b>Communications Coordinator </b>to help further Tetra Pak’s brand and reputation in the United States and Canada.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>In this position, you will work across functions with colleagues from communications, sales, marketing and other areas to help implement Tetra Pak’s communication strategy in the United States and Canada. You will report to the communications director for Tetra Pak U.S. &amp; Canada and sit within the communications function in <b>Denton, TX</b>.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>We are looking for an energetic candidate with a passion for learning and a drive for results</b>. In this role, you will work across a variety of communications disciplines and subject areas and communicate regularly with a wide range of audiences, including Tetra Pak employees, key opinion leaders in the food and beverage industry and Tetra Pak’s customers.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>The successful candidate will:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>have broad experience working on communications activities across a variety of practice areas;</li>
<li>be resourceful and always seeking new opportunities to learn;</li>
<li>thrive in a fast-paced, diverse environment;</li>
<li>excel at executing many simultaneous projects;</li>
<li>be adaptable and comfortable in an ever-changing environment.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>You should be familiar with the latest communications tools, channels and methods and be able to demonstrate success in using these to enhance brand and reputation.</p>
</div></div>
<div>
<div>
<b>
Main Responsibilities</b><br>
</div>
<p></p>
<div>
<ul>
<li>Create, edit and refine a wide range of communications including corporate statements, media interviews, videos, speeches, internal communications, articles and press releases</li>
<li>Act as liaison to various departments to provide communication support and to identify/create opportunities to enhance Tetra Pak’s reputation with internal and external stakeholders</li>
<li>Update and maintain intranet content for the U.S. and Canada</li>
<li>Continually track, measure, evaluate and report achievement of internal and external communications goals</li>
<li>Distribute press releases, internal announcements and other communications</li>
<li>Coordinate media interviews and prepare briefing materials</li>
<li>Support execution of tradeshows, corporate events and internal employee meetings</li>
<li>Assist in the preparation of executive speakers for company events, including talking point development or research, PowerPoint development or other production support as required by the event</li>
<li>Work with agencies to develop plans and materials in alignment with established communications strategy.</li>
<li>Ensure consistent, coordinated approach to communications activities in the U.S. &amp; Canada in alignment with agreed cluster communications strategy, priorities and guidelines.</li>
<br>
</ul>
</div>
</div>
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<b>Qualifications</b><br>
</div>
<p></p>
<div>
<ul>
<li>A Bachelor’s degree in communications, journalism or a related field.</li>
<li>3-5 years’ relevant experience.</li>
<li>Proven track record of successful communications projects and activities.</li>
<li>Familiarity with digital communications tools, methods and trends.</li>
<li>Strong proficiency in Microsoft Office; experience managing websites and familiarity with HTML preferred.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>You should be:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>A highly-driven and motivated individual with excellent communication skills.</li>
<li>Quick to establish good working relationships with cross-functional and cross-cultural teams.</li>
<li>Able to navigate and influence a multi-cultural and global organisation and have the mindset to constantly challenge and disrupt the status quo.</li>
<li>Able to synthesize large amounts of information and craft succinct, relevant and impactful messages.</li>
<li>Able to speak and write in English with native proficiency.</li>
<li>Highly proficient in Microsoft Office, particularly PowerPoint; experience managing websites and familiarity with Adobe InDesign, Adobe Photoshop, or other photo/design software preferred.</li>
<li>Able to travel about 15% of the time.</li>
</ul>
</div>
