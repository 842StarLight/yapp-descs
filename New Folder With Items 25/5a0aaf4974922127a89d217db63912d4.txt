<h2>Macy's Locker Room Assistant Manager Full-Time</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div><div>
<p>Generate Sales</p>
<ul>
<li>
Produce sales gains, by providing customer service.</li>
<li>
To meet or exceed Company Objectives in all individual statistics.</li>
<li>
Learn to provide consistent, documented appraisal of an associate’s sales performance. Provide support by giving feedback on areas of strength and opportunity while keeping in line with Company Objectives.</li>
<li>
Adhere to current visual guidelines includes: proper merchandising, signage and store cleanliness.</li>
<li>
Maintain a professional appearance consistent with Company Dress Code Policy.</li>
</ul>
<p>
Control Expenses</p>
<ul>
<li>
Protect Company assets within guidelines of LIDS Retail policies.</li>
<li>
Assist in preparation of store work schedules that provide proper store coverage and are within the Company guidelines for wage control.</li>
<li>
Follow all policies to accurately manage store inventory including receiving, transferring, completing price change and conducting product counts.</li>
<li>
Complete accurate product counts in a consistent and timely manner.</li>
<li>
Perform proper documentation and record keeping per LIDS Retail policies as well as state and federal laws.</li>
<li>
Open and close the store as required following the procedures per the Operations P&amp;P Manual.</li>
</ul>
<p>
Supervise Associates</p>
<ul>
<li>
Participate in LIDS Training Programs, adhere to set goals (for sales and tasks) and regular follow up.</li>
<li>
Assist in recruiting and training store personnel on proper store operations and procedures.</li>
<li>
Encourage store associates’ direct compliance of established company policies, procedures and guidelines including (but not limited to) safekeeping of company inventory, funds and property.</li>
<li>
Perform work of subordinates as needed.</li>
<li>
Communicate with employees at all levels of the company.</li>
<li>
Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
</div></div>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Requirements</b></h2>
</div>
<div>
<p>EDUCATION AND/OR EXPERIENCE</p>
<ul>
<li>
High school diploma or equivalent plus one year relative experience.</li>
<li>
Established ability to produce sales results while minimizing loss.</li>
<li>
Strong interpersonal skills and the ability to communicate verbally in a clear professional manner.</li>
<li>
Ability to operate a computer, as well as maneuver relative software programs.</li>
<li>
Ability to lift up to 50 pounds.</li>
<li>
Ability to climb a ladder and work with hands overhead.</li>
<li>
Standing required for up to 100% of the work time.</li>
<li>
Ability to work unsupervised.</li>
</ul>
