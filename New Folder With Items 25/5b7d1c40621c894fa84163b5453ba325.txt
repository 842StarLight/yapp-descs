Summary of Job</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
The IT Mountain Applications team is a technology management team within Vail Resorts (VR) that is focused on enabling operational processes with a suite of vendor-supported applications and solutions. We are business leaders, delivering solutions that enable Food &amp; Beverage, Parking, Digital Signage and Snow Surfaces.</p>
<p>
Vail Resorts is looking for an experienced Senior IT Business Analyst that has worked in a fast paced multifaceted environment. A Senior BA on this team is a leader, subject matter expert and partner to VR business operations and vendors.</p>
<p>
This role will manage and participate in projects as well as provide support and maintenance to Mountain Application applications and solutions. Other responsibilities include liaising with operational and technical teams, leading the gathering, documentation and approval of business requirements, solution design, test script development and execution, and quality assurance for a variety of technology solutions.</p>
<p></p>
