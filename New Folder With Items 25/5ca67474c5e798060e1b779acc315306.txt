<h2>Network Engineer, Level 2</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>High Availability, Inc. </b>is currently seeking an experienced <b>Network Engineer </b>to join our team in Audubon, PA office.</p>
<p><b>About Us: </b></p>
<p>High Availability, Inc. is a systems integrator and managed service provider responsible for implementing, monitoring, managing, and maintaining multiple clients’ IT environments. We are currently looking to hire a mid-level Network Engineer to join the network engineering team in our managed cloud services division. Our ideal candidate is a self-motivated, detail oriented, security-minded individual with an eagerness to learn and thrive in a team atmosphere.</p>
<p>We value having a great company culture, where casual dress is common and employees are key. Team outings are practiced with activities like Pumpkin Derby Racing, Cooking Competition (Iron Chef-style), charity run and bike rides, family team buildings, take your child to work day, and more. That’s not to say that hard work isn’t done here, it’s rewarded. We offer a competitive salary and extensive benefits package for all full-time employees. Work hard, play hard - all in the name of High Availability Inc.</p>
<p><b>Job Summary: </b></p>
<p>This position will provide network engineering support to clients of High Availability, Inc., while operating a variety of on-premise and cloud-based network technologies. As a mid-level Network Engineer, you will primarily be responsible for responding to device and client generated support tickets. These range from service requests (move, add, change, delete) to troubleshooting and break/fix tickets. Additional duties may include assisting with the network specific components of client onboardings and disaster recovery tests.</p>
<p><b>Job Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>80% ticket-based service requests and break/fix</li>
<li>20% other activities (onboarding clients, assisting with DR tests, infrastructure planning, etc.)</li>
<li>Part of team on-call rotation, one week per month</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<p><b><i>Education and Experience: </i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 3 years of experience in a network engineer role, preferably in a managed service provider environment</li>
<li>Prior help desk support and/or customer service experience</li>
<li>Working experience with TCP/IP route/switch technologies</li>
<li>Working experience with network security technologies such as firewalls, routers, and VPNs including Cisco, Palo, and FortiGate firewall products</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>Skills and Abilities: </i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Basic understanding of operating system network administration (Windows, Linux, vSphere)</li>
<li>History of clean and consistent network racking, cabling, and labeling practices</li>
<li>History of creating and maintaining robust network documentation (diagrams, port maps, etc.)</li>
<li>Excellent logical troubleshooting skills</li>
<li>Ability to contribute to a team as part of an on-call rotation</li>
<li>Ability to lift objects up to 50lbs</li>
<li>The following are not required but a plus:</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Experience with formal change control process or ITIL (or similar) service delivery framework</li>
<li>Industry vendor certifications (Cisco, Palo, FortiGate, etc.)</li>
<li>Experience with Cisco UCS hardware</li>
<li>Experience with NAC products (ISE)</li>
<li>Experience with VMware vSphere</li>
<li>Experience with PowerShell, Python, or similar scripting languages</li>
</ul>
