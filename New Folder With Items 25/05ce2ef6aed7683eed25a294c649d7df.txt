<div>
<b>Work you’ll do</b><br>
</div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li><div>Maintains a solid knowledge base of the functional capabilities of Salesforce clouds - Sales/Service/Marketing/Chatter and has proven experience in delivering Salesforce projects.
</div></li>
<li><div>Partners with the Engagement Manager in leading, directing, and reviewing all activities associated with delivering technology products. Responsible for product quality, project timeliness, and customer satisfaction.
</div></li>
<li><div>Defines and analyzes business and user requirements for new and/or enhanced products; Author detailed functional and non-functional business requirements / user stories and acceptance criteria, personas, journeys, KPIs, process flows, wire frames and feature descriptions user guides. Ensures that deliverables align with the business strategy and support customer demands.</div></li>
<li><div>
Serves as an advisor on functional processes, ensuring that both business needs and end-user needs are understood and reflected in every solution we deliver, distinguished by world-class quality.</div></li>
<li><div>
Responsible for planning and conducting Design Thinking sessions to gain insights into end user and customer needs</div></li>
<li><div>
Manages the Product Backlog for a Product, guiding clients through prioritization sessions and working with the Engagement Manager to define and manage scope/sprints accordingly.
</div></li>
<li><div>Provides operational support to practitioners and firm clients. Engages with various ITS organizations to resolve problems as they arise.
</div></li>
<li><div>Engages business owners and other ITS resources to respond to requests for new applications and/or enhancements. Collaborates with the Engagement Manager in creating and presenting the appropriate project on-boarding processes and materials (e.g., Charter, SOW).
</div></li>
<li><div>Leads and is responsible for all Testing and QA activities within the project lifecycle. Works with and manages USI QA resources to develop and execute Test Plans, Test Scripts and all other deliverables and activities related to Quality Assurance of the Products.
</div></li>
<li><div>Works with the Engagement Manager and clients to plan and execute User Acceptance Testing.
</div></li>
<li><div>Participates in reviews of plans associated with project execution.
</div></li>
<li><div>Assists Engagement Manager with status reporting as required by the Deloitte Applications Studio leadership.
</div></li>
<li><div>Responds proactively to both business and project issues and escalates appropriately.</div></li>
</ul>
