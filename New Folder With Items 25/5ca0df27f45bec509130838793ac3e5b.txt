<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>About the team</b></h2>
As a part of the Zillow Home Loans team, you will play an active role in providing a variety of loan options and best-in-class customer service to help home shoppers become homeowners! The Loan Processing team reviews loan file documentation (i.e. credit, employment, income, and assets) within a Mortgage Loan being processed, directs all efforts related to driving the fulfillment of loans from the point of submission for Initial Underwriting Decision to Final Approval.<br>
<br>
As a Senior Mortgage Loan Processor, you will work out of our Overland Park office, where you will carry processing, team leadership, and mentoring responsibilities. In addition to reviewing documents and data integrity to ensure each loan is eligible, and that the borrower qualifies for the specified loan program (Conventional, FHA, USDA-RD, or VA), before submitting the loan for underwriting, you'll also step in to assist with escalated issues and act as a go-to resource for the team.<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
About the role</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>
Maintain and update key data points into the loan origination software (LOS) and point of sale system (POS) in a timely and efficient manner</li>
<li>
Expertly maintain a pipeline of files in various stages of the loan process</li>
<li>
Ensure that all loan documentation is complete, accurate, verified and aligns with company policy</li>
<li>
Effectively communicate with Loan Originators and Underwriters to document and complete loan file for underwriting submission(s) while working towards a full underwriting approval</li>
<li>
Work with third-parties such as credit reporting agencies, Appraisal Management Companies (AMCs), title companies, employers, landlords, etc. to minimize the cycle time from conditional to final approval</li>
<li>
Brief customers appropriately of new document or information requirements via that customer portal with the POS</li>
<li>
Maintain company service level and turn-time standards; specific measurables include return-call times, return-email times, and task completion times, and percentage of files that can be decisioned at first UW submittal</li>
<li>
Provide support as necessary to facilitate the origination and fulfillment of mortgage loans</li>
<li>
Work closely with Closers to document file adequately for closing consummation</li>
<li>
This position sits in our Kansas City, KS office</li>
</ul>
