<b>Job Summary:</b> (Overall purpose and objective of the job)<br>
<br>
The Research Analyst works as part of a team supporting commercial real estate brokers, appraisers and property management employees with their daily research needs by being a main source of industry-leading information. The major function of this role is to maintain current property data from which to provide reports to the brokers, appraisers and property management to use in their marketing efforts.<br>
<br>
<b>Essential Functions:</b> (The reason this job exists is to perform these functions. These functions are performed regularly, seriously impacts other jobs, and there are a limited number of employees whom performance of the function can be distributed).<br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Research background information/ contact information about clients, companies via TLO and Hoover’s</li>
<li>Conduct in-depth data analysis using traditional and advanced methods</li>
<li>Train brokers, appraiser and property management employees how to use our resources (CoStar, LandVision, run availabilities, comp reports, etc.)</li>
<li>Develop trend reports, economic reports, employment reports, market reports, report on recent major transactions for marketing purposes</li>
<li>Develop submarket/city reports for marketing materials accurately and on time</li>
<li>Develop tour reports and quarterly market reports accurately and on time</li>
<li>Prepare documents and presentations in PowerPoint, Excel, Word and Power BI</li>
<li>Compile broker presentation material following KM branding standards</li>
<li>Generate demographic reports accurately and on time</li>
<li>Conduct basic lease or investment analysis</li>
<li>Create zoning and land-use reports, and explain allowable uses/densities</li>
<li>Collect out of area market information as requested</li>
<li>Use proprietary, online and subscription resources to research and validate accuracy of internal data</li>
<li>Maintain a local client list for email blast of market information</li>
<li>Prepare, participate and follow up on broker/specialty sales meetings</li>
<li>Present at quarterly sales meeting that quarter’s market report and discussing storylines/key trends in the market</li>
<li>Update and maintain tenants in the marketplace spreadsheets for the different groups</li>
<li>Email KM branded monthly tricks/tips information to brokers</li>
<li>Track industry news and be knowledgeable about large real estate projects, developments, trends and local geography</li>
<li>Network with other researchers in the market</li>
<li>Manage client expectations regarding the depth of analysis contained within deliverables and on the timeliness of deliverables</li>
</ul>
