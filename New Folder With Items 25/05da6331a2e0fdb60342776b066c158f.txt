<h2>GROUP EXERCISE COORDINATOR</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<b>Summary:
</b><br>
</div>
<p>The Group Exercise Coordinator manages, directs, and coordinates the fitness staff and group exercise instructors in order to provide specialty fitness programming and group exercise classes.The Group Exercise Coordinator ensures that the fitness and group exercise department is operating efficiently to provide safe and effective classes and specialty fitness programs.The Group Exercise Coordinator effectively communicates with the Sales and Marketing department to ensure staff is properly informed and marketing/advertising is distributed in a timely manner.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The Group Exercise Coordinator also works with the general fitness center membership to instruct in group exercise classes, provide wellness management services, and provide general manager-on-duty responsibilities.<br>
</p>
<div></div>
<p><b>Experience/Education Requirements:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<div><ul>
<li>College graduate in Exercise Science, Kinesiology, or a related field is highly preferred.
</li>
<li>The Group Exercise Coordinator must have experience in customer service, group exercise and program implementation, client motivation, and fitness sales.
</li>
<li>Fitness and/or sports background is required.
</li>
<li>The Group Exercise Coordinator must have great communication and leadership skills, positive interactions, sound judgement, and a positive work attitude.
</li>
<li>Experience working with special populations is highly preferred.<br>
 </li>
</ul></div>
<p></p>
<br>
<br>
<p><b>License or Certification Requirements:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Exercise Science, Kinesiology, or a related bachelor’s degree
</li>
<li>A Nationally recognized personal trainer certification (ACSM, NSCA, NASM, etc.) is highly preferred. CPR/AED certification required.
</li>
<li>Nationally recognized group exercise instructor certification required within 90 days of hire.
</li>
<li>At least one special population exercise certification is required within 90 days of hire.
</li>
<li>Annual requirement of one workshop or certification.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>

</b></div>
<div><b>FTE: 0.5
</b></div>

</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>Responsibilities:
</b><br>
</div>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Ensures that seasonal changes to the group exercise schedule are completed and distributed in a timely manner.
</li>
<li>Effectively handles group exercise instructor call-offs and substitutions.
</li>
<li>Consistently comes up with new and exciting paid programs for RHFC membership and special populations.
</li>
<li>Effectively communicates group exercise and specialty program information/changes with the Front Desk employees, sales and marketing employees, and the fitness staff.
</li>
<li>Works with the sales and marketing team to ensure that marketing for group exercise and specialty programs is distributed and taken down in a timely manner.
</li>
<li>Assists the Fitness Manager in the betterment of the department through innovative ideas to improve policies, procedures, and customer service.
</li>
<li>Assists the Fitness Manager in departmental cost control and revenue generation of the Group Exercise, Fitness, and Aquatics departments.
</li>
<li>Assists to improve the member experience through instructing group exercise classes, specialty programs, wellness management coaching, sales duties, etc.<br>
</li>
</ul>
