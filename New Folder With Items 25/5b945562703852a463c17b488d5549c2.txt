<p><b>
The Digital Operations Team:
</b></p>
<p>We support all day-to-day Ad Operations for assigned accounts, aiding campaign planning, scheduling, implementation and tracking. This role involves managing relationships with third party vendors and media teams to ensure proper delivery of Digital ad campaigns. As an Associate, you would be responsible for helping provide various tagging and technical recommendations internally.</p>
<p><b>
Overview:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>You are a self-motivated, analytical individual who is willing to be challenged; work in a close-knit team environment while at the same time take a leadership role and work independently with limited mentorship and direction when needed</li>
<li>
You will have a penchant for problem solving, and can demonstrate excellent time management, as well as oral and written interpersonal skills</li>
<li>
This is an excellent opportunity to get a base understanding of the groundwork needed to launch successful digital campaigns</li>
<li>
We are passionate about the inner workings and essential building blocks of Digital Media. Join the team and you'll be surrounded by these ideals.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Responsibilities and Development: Within 1 year…
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>You are trained in the most up-to-date and latest in Trafficking and Media industry trends</li>
<li>
Understand and become fluent in the various terminology, acronyms, and industry phrases</li>
<li>
Act as liaison with media, creative agencies and publisher sites regarding tracking, scheduling and delivery of campaigns</li>
<li>
Serve as a point of contact between ad server and internal business units</li>
<li>
Screen incoming creative according to ad server specs and technical requirements</li>
<li>
Work with reputable fourth-party Ad tech companies and vendors such as: Double Verify, MOAT and Integral Ad Science</li>
<li>
Handle the implementation, scheduling, and rotation of ads, within online advertising campaigns</li>
<li>
Execute creation of HTML tags for site partners using various ad-serving systems such as DCM and Sizmek</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Within the following year(s)
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Advise &amp; consult with Media teams and Clients regarding advertising technologies, tracking and campaign performance</li>
<li>
Understand the dynamics and specifics of Site Mapping and Strategy</li>
<li>
Train other Team members and Media Teams in the Digital Operations process</li>
<li>
Help build process documents, in coordination with the Digital Operations Team, to ensure all parties in the Media Life cycle are in check</li>
</ul>
