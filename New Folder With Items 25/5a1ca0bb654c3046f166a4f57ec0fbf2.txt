<h2>Warehouse Order Selector</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Job Title: Warehouse/Order Puller</b></p>
<p><b>Reports to: Warehouse Manager</b></p>
<p><b>Position Type: Hourly </b></p>
<p><b>Role: </b>This position is responsible for accurately preparing orders for delivery or pickup according to customer sales orders.</p>
<p><b>Job Duties: [core responsibilities]</b></p>
<ul>
<li><b>Pulling and completing orders in a timely manner for customers based on sales orders for either delivery or pickup.</b></li>
<li><b>Packaging product in a way that ensures product will not be damaged [lighter product is not placed on bottom of box].</b></li>
<li><b>Package product by ensuring box is not over 30 pounds.</b></li>
<li><b>Safely operate warehouse vehicles and equipment, inspecting equipment prior to use, notifying the warehouse manager/supervisor when repairs are needed.</b></li>
<li><b>Attention to detail to ensure accuracy of all orders pulled. </b></li>
<li><b>Ensuring buddy lifting when product is over 50 pounds.</b></li>
<li><b>Act with a sense of urgency; complete on-site customer orders (“will-calls”) with priority </b></li>
<li><b>Assist with all other duties as assigned, including stocking of product, general order selecting, will-call’s, and overall warehouse cleanliness.</b></li>
</ul>
<p><b>Desired skills/experience: </b></p>
<ul>
<li><b>Ability to interface well with all departments</b></li>
<li><b>The ability to multi-task and meet established deadlines and sales orders </b></li>
<li><b>Can and must maintain confidentiality and sensitive company material or information</b></li>
<li><b>Is thorough when performing work and conscientious about attending to detail</b></li>
<li><b>Ability to work well in a team, displaying friendliness, courtesy, tact, and politeness to others</b></li>
</ul>
<p><b>Minimum Qualifications: [Education, Credentials, Certifications]</b></p>
<ul><li><b>Provide satisfactory evidence of your ability to work in the United States</b></li></ul>
<p><b>Preferred Qualifications: [Education, Credentials, Certifications]</b></p>
<ul>
<li><b>Prior experience in warehouse or like field </b></li>
<li><b>Previous experience operating powered equipment/warehouse vehicles</b></li>
<li><b>High School Diploma or equivalent preferred</b></li>
</ul>
<p><b>Physical Demands: </b></p>
<ul>
<li><b>Must be able to remain in a stationary position 50% of the time</b></li>
<li><b>Ability to operate electrical motorized equipment with close proximity, close quarters</b></li>
<li><b>Worker is subject to environmental conditions both inside and outside</b></li>
<li><b>Worker is required to work in warehouse conditions with aisles or passageways.</b></li>
<li><b>Climbing, Ascending or descending ladders, using feet, legs and/or hand and arms</b></li>
<li><b>Lifting objects from a lower to higher position weighing 50 lbs.</b></li>
<li><b>Grasping with fingers to lift product</b></li>
</ul>
<p><b>Training Requirement: </b></p>
<ul>
<li><b>Operation of Electrical Stock Chasers</b></li>
<li><b>Training on use of Inventory Headsets/Software</b></li>
</ul>
