<h2>Customer Service Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>General Job Description </b></p>
<p>A customer service specialist will act as a customer liaison, provide product information, technical proposals and resolve any emerging problems with accuracy and efficiency. Duties will include communication via phone, email as well as face-to-face meetings. Position requires excellent communication skills, detailed work, an excellent attitude, a desire to help customers and teammates and to increase sales through solid customer relationships.</p>
<p><b>Main Job Tasks and Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Communicate with customers regarding products, order status, technical issues, and any other needs as required by each customer.</li>
<li>Manage customer service inquiries on a timely basis and provide accurate information to customer regarding order status, etc.</li>
<li>Identifying and assessing customers needs to achieve satisfaction.</li>
<li>Write technical product proposals per customer requests.</li>
<li>Develop good working relationship with customer, suppliers and teammates.</li>
<li>Conduct follow-up communication with clients to maintain good relationship</li>
<li>Process orders in the company information and accounting system.</li>
<li>Work closely with product suppliers and manufacturer representatives.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Education and Experience: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or GED as a minimum</li>
<li>1-2 years of computers experience including relevant software applications including Word, Excel, PowerPoint, etc.</li>
<li>1-2 years of customer service experience</li>
<li>Keyboarding skills for data entry.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Key Competencies: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Mature, reliable and customer focused</li>
<li>Ability to learn and understand technical product line</li>
<li>Able to read and understand drawings and technical documents</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Appropriate personal appearance for business environment</li>
<li>Customer service experience and orientation</li>
<li>Information management skills</li>
<li>Organizing and planning skills</li>
<li>Attention to detail</li>
<li>Multitasking needed. Must be flexible to deal with changing priorities on a daily basis</li>
</ul>
