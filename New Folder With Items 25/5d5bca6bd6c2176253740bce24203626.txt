<h2>COMFORT INN FRONT DESK</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Responsible for the reception/information for the hotel. Performs a variety of clerical and accounting duties. Is responsible for reserving future bookings, in-house guest accounting, and cashier duties. Acts as switchboard operator.</p>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Performs check in and check out of guests. Posts payments and charges as necessary. Responsible for funds received.</li>
<li>
Responsible for answering the hotel switchboard in a timely, efficient, and courteous manner.</li>
<li>
May be responsible for additional guest services which may include, but are not limited to, airport shuttle, minor maintenance and additional room supplies.</li>
<li>
Handles guest problems and complaints. Serves as an information agent for hotel guests.</li>
<li>
Responsible for accurate accounting of all guest rooms. Required to communicate pertinent information to co-workers and to management.</li>
<li>
Performs a variety of clerical and office duties.</li>
<li>
Responsible for daily execution of reservation check to insure that no revenue is lost due to rooms which are not sold or are overbooked.</li>
<li>
Must have first-hand knowledge of the physical layout of the entire building.</li>
<li>
Should be thoroughly familiar with all emergency procedures with regard to accident, fire, bomb threats, and illness of guests, etc.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
NON-ESSENTIAL DUTIES:</b></p>
<ul><li>
Any other duties as assigned by the Front Desk Manager, Assistant Hotel Manager, or Hotel Manager.</li></ul>
