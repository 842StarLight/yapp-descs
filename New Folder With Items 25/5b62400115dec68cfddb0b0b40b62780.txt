<h2>Junior Desktop Tech</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>The <b>Desktop Support Technician I</b> provides support to end users on a variety of issues; this includes identifying, researching, and resolving technical problems. The Desktop Support Technician II will rely on instructions and pre-established guidelines to perform the functions of the job and may involve use of problem management databases and help desk system. This position works under immediate supervision and primary job functions do not typically require exercising independent judgment.</p>
<p><b>Key Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Maintain a Windows-based PC network and support upwards of 5000 call center users</li>
<li>Handle both physical and software aspects of workstations</li>
<li>Configure phones to work with telecommunication system</li>
<li>Provide both user and front line helpdesk support</li>
<li>Support of user base on internal and 3rd party applications such as MS Office and phone system</li>
<li>Monitor ticketing system and all inbound ticket queues</li>
<li>Responds to telephone calls, email and personnel requests for technical support.</li>
<li>Documents, tracks and monitors the problem to ensure a timely resolution.</li>
<li>Participate in on-call/after-hours rotation</li>
<li>Assist the Server and Network teams with hands-on needs for the local infrastructure</li>
<li>Troubleshoot network devices, printers, and business specific applications</li>
<li>Maintain technology proficiency standards as required for the systems to be supported</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications and Skills</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Experience or equivalent certifications demonstrating proficiency supporting Microsoft products</li>
<li>Associates degree preferred or combination of industry certification and practical</li>
<li>5 years of experience in the field or in a related area</li>
<li>Previous Help Desk experience preferred</li>
<li>Knowledge of commonly-used Help Desk concepts, practices, and procedures</li>
<li>Ability to demonstrate knowledge in applicable areas of technology</li>
<li>Intermediate knowledge in hardware and networking, with particular emphasis on Remote Infrastructure Management</li>
<li>Ability to provide excellent customer service interdepartmental communication</li>
<li>Proven ability to effectively manage multiple priorities and meet deadlines</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills with a demonstrated ability to make difficult concepts easy to understand to non-technical clients</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to excel both independently and as a team member in a diverse and challenging environment</li>
</ul>
