<h2>Customer Service Professional</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<div>
<b>General Purpose</b>
</div>
<div><p>Contribute to a team of Customer Service Professionals to deliver a full range of customer service by preparing reports and correspondence, provide customer service by phone, make and close requests, comply with customer guarantees, data entry, SAP and RCMS work, distribute mail, review and pay invoices, payroll entry and perform other work as assigned. The Customer Service Professional will focus on providing full service to the customer and delivering customer satisfaction, exceeding each customer’s greatest expectations while working to achieve company goals.</p></div>
</div>
<div>
<div>
<b>
Responsibilities</b>
</div>
<div><ul>
<li>Provide world-class customer service via telephone to existing and future customers by delivering services such as billing, credit and collections, service requests, energy efficiency and customer account management.</li>
<li>
Assist customers via telephone in regard to service outages, restoration of electric services, and electric service billings.</li>
<li>
Provide specialized advice in regard to regulatory billing requirements and company processes via extensive phone time and electronic means.</li>
<li>
Resolve disconnect or account delinquency issues by discussing options available with the customer and agreeing to an appropriate plan.</li>
<li>
Coordinate customer’s requests, when necessary, with the appropriate company department.</li>
<li>
Prepare written communication with the use of templates.</li>
<li>
Communicate with field personnel via phone or electronic means to process customer work orders.</li>
<li>
Demonstrate adherence to identified PCCC performance measures as outlined in the Customer Contact Center Guidelines.</li>
<li>
Navigate through multiple computer software programs to address account information specific to customer inquiries.<br>
</li>
</ul></div>
</div>
<div>
<div>
<b>
Requirements</b>
</div>
<div><ul>
<li>High School Diploma or GED Certificate; or the equivalent combination of education and work experience.</li>
<li>
A minimum of two years non-sales customer service experience where one-on-one problem resolution was demonstrated, and/or two years of non-sales call center experience.</li>
<li>
Ability to gather necessary information from internal and external customers, determine appropriate course of action and provide information to customers.</li>
<li>
Communication and interpersonal skills to interface with team members and customers in a fashion that promotes positive customer relations.</li>
<li>
Active listening, conflict resolution and problem solving skills.</li>
<li>
Proficient with the use of personal computers to access and input relevant information on customer accounts.</li>
<li>
Ability to perform basic math calculations.</li>
<li>
Ability to type a minimum of 30 WPM.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of general Company policies and procedures as well as those specific to billing, credit/collections, service and account management is preferred.</li>
<li>
Ability to work scheduled shifts and meet attendance requirements.</li>
</ul></div>
</div>
