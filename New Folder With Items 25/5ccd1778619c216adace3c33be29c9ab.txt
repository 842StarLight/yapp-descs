<h2>Sales Engineer (Accounting ERP)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Summary/Objective</b></p>
<p>You are a partner for the Sales team and a trusted advisor for the prospect. As product expert in ERP GovCon accounting solutions you will answer business and technical questions, deliver custom demonstrations, articulate business value propositions and provide technical competitive differentiation.</p>
<p><b>Essential Functions</b></p>
<p>· Understand customer requirements and demonstrate PVBS solutions to prospects.</p>
<p>· Deliver sales presentations, present technical information about PVBS products and services, and conduct product demonstrations.</p>
<p>· Be a thought leader for pre-sale engagements including assisting customers in defining</p>
<p>specifications that drive the most business value.</p>
<p>· Provide prospect and customer feedback to internal stakeholders.</p>
<p>· Deliver insight in a dynamic and high growth sales environment.</p>
<p>· Articulate the value of our ERP platform and how it differs from our competitors in presentations and written communication.</p>
<p>· Deliver informative, high-quality web demonstrations.</p>
<p><b>Competencies</b></p>
<p>· <b>Adaptability</b>: Adapts to changes in the work environment; manages to compete for demands; changes approach or method to best fit the situation; able to deal with frequent change, delays, or unexpected events.</p>
<p>· <b>Planning/Organizing</b>: Prioritizes and plans work activities; uses time efficiently; plans for additional resources; sets goals and objectives; develops realistic action plans.</p>
<p>· <b>Analytical</b>: Collects and researches data; designs workflows and procedures; identifies data relationships and dependencies; synthesizes complex or diverse information; uses intuition and experience to complement data.</p>
<p>· <b>Sense of Urgency</b>: Displays a matter of utmost urgency; understands the importance of making critical business decision in a timely manner with strong attention to detail; realization that efficiency and prioritization is critical to success.</p>
<p>· <b>Customer Service</b>: Manages difficult or emotional customer situations; meets commitments; responds promptly to customer needs; solicits customer feedback to improve service.</p>
<p>· <b>Problem Solving</b>: Identifies and resolves problems in a timely manner; gathers and analyzes information skillfully; develops alternative solutions.</p>
<p>· <b>Written Communications</b>: Edits work for spelling and grammar; presents numerical data effectively; varies writing style to meet needs; writes clearly and informatively.</p>
<p>· <b>Oral Communication</b>: Demonstrates group presentation skills; listens and gets clarification; response well to questions; speaks clearly and persuasively.</p>
<p><b>Required Education and Experience</b></p>
<ul><li>Bachelor of Science/Arts in business administration or accounting (or equivalent) with exposure to Information Technology is required.</li></ul>
<p>· 5+ years of Enterprise Technical Pre-Sales / Sales Engineering experience for software products with external clients, in a government contractor environment.</p>
<ul>
<li>Extensive experience in working with high-performing sales individuals and software partners is required.</li>
<li>Experience showing a proven track record of quota attainment in a B2B or B2G environment.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Preferred Education and Experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Experience of selling Microsoft software solutions.</li>
<li>Experience with ERP accounting systems.</li>
</ul>
