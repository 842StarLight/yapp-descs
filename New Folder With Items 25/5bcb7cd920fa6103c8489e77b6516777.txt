<h2>Product Stewardship Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Summary
</b></h1>Plan, direct and manage a comprehensive product stewardship strategy to assure compliance with all applicable laws, regulations and management system requirements as they apply to the product lifecycle of the N.A business portfolio. Lead, develop and mentor regulatory professionals within the Product Stewardship function. Establish and manage a process to identify, monitor, analyze and communicate significant business opportunities or risks arising from current and future legislation, industry trends and company-wide objectives. Establish and maintain policies and procedures to assure enterprise-wide training is in compliance with industry product stewardship standards, governing bodies, regulatory and internal requirements.
<p><b>MAJOR JOB DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES (List in order of importance)
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Work with the defined businesses, regulatory agencies, corporate partners and consultants to ensure product-specific regulatory compliance.
</li>
<li>Develop and maintain SDSs (Safety Data Sheets), labels and other hazardous communication information for the business portfolio.
</li>
<li>Create and maintain product stewardship records relating to company products, intermediates and raw materials for the business portfolio.
</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Oversee Label Design team and coordinate with site Quality Assurance Managers.
<ul>
<li>Oversee the application and support of Labeling Software.
</li>
<li>Provide technical support regarding the revision or implementation of the documentation of these systems, including procedures, work instructions, and forms.
</li>
</ul>
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Conduct assessments and ensure proper implementation of the product safety/product stewardship systems, programs and practices.
<ul><li>Lead product stewardship portion of customer audits, including audit preparation, documentation, follow-up, and closure as it relates to supply chain compliance.
</li></ul>
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Create and oversee product stewardship information as it relates to N.A. Quality Management Systems.
<ul>
<li>Provide technical support regarding the revision or implementation of process documentation, including procedures, work instructions, and forms.
</li>
<li>Have working knowledge of and support compliance with ISO 9001, ISO 13485, ISO 14001, OHSAS 18001 and FDA Quality System Regulations (QSR).
</li>
<li>Evaluate and advise management on current product stewardship regulations and standards, new regulations and/or standards and proposed changes. Provide enterprise-wide strategic insight to senior management.
</li>
</ul>
</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>Provide training as requested. Prepare, conduct, and advise necessary training on product stewardship concepts and tools.
</li>
<li>Consult with Sales, Product Development and Quality Assurance on new products to create low hazard product to the extent possible.
</li>
<li>Oversee TSCA compliance for the business.
</li>
<li>Respond to internal and external requests for product safety information on the business portfolio.
</li>
<li>Maintain broad technical expertise as it relates to FDA, TSCA and other global substance control regulations.
</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Other duties as assigned.
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS (Education/Training, Experience and Certifications)
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Engineering, Life Sciences or related discipline
</li>
<li>8+ years applicable experience
</li>
<li>3+ years leadership experience
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>KNOWLEDGE SKILLS AND ABILITIES (Those necessary to perform the job competently)
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Strong interpersonal and people management skills
</li>
<li>Knowledge of ISO Management Systems, SDS software generation.
</li>
<li>Strong knowledge with SDS software systems and implementation.
</li>
<li>Working knowledge of ISO 9001, ISO 13485 and FDA Quality System Regulations (QSR).
</li>
<li>Ability interact with all levels of the organization
</li>
<li>Functional knowledge of Microsoft Outlook, Word, and Excel, (or equivalent), highly desired. Previous exposure and experience with automated inventory control systems strongly recommended.
</li>
<li>Demonstrated knowledge and experience with software systems and integration with the quality system.
</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills – verbal and written
</li>
<li>Significant project management experience
</li>
</ul>
