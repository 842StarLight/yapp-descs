<div>
<div><b>VibraLife is a Rehabilitation Center and Senior Living Community, and is the area's newest senior housing development, comprised of an assisted living facility with a secured memory care unit. Located in Mechanicsburg, Pennsylvania, VibraLife is a beautiful, one-story, state-of-the-art facility that includes the Vibra Rehabilitation Center and Vibra Senior Living.</b></div>
<div></div>
<div><b>VibraLife of Mechanicsburg is seeking a Full Time Clinical Liaison - RN, LPN, PT, OT - to join our team!</b></div>
<div></div>
</div>
<div><div>
<div>The CL is responsible for educating, problem solving and building relationships with referral sources, obtaining referrals, completing SNF assessments, and participating in planning, organizing and executing the Business Departmental plan. Frequent day travel with reliable transportation and occasional overnight travel is required. Responsible for establishing and maintaining effective working relationships with their assigned accounts as well as those of their team members.</div>
<div></div>
<div>Functions as a key source of market intelligence providing direct communication between the community, referral sources and physicians to their Vibra Facility Team. Often serves as the “face of Vibra” in the community and is responsible for developing a positive, professional image conducive to building long lasting relationships with our customers.</div>
<div></div>
</div></div>
<div>
<b>Qualifications</b>:</div>
<div>
<ul>
<li>Current, valid, and active license to practice as a Registered Nurse or Licensed Practical/Vocational Nurse in the state of employment required.</li>
<li>Recent healthcare experience in the SNF, acute care hospital, rehabilitation setting or home health care agency/company required.</li>
<li>Current, valid, and active driver’s license required.</li>
<li>Ability to travel using publicly available air and ground transportation.</li>
</ul>
</div>
