GENERAL SUMMARY OF POSITION
<br>This position is responsible for member outreach and administrative tasks for the Fitness Director. Administrative tasks should be completed in a timely manner. Tasks include marketing flyers, audits, and member outreach communications. Member outreach responsibilities include communications via phone, email and newsletter communications.
<br><br>
Nothing in this job description restricts management's right to assign or reassign duties and responsibilities to this job at any time.
<br>PRIMARY JOB FUNCTIONS
<br>Administrative
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Complete administrative tasks assigned in a timely manner</li>
<li>Create, Develop Audit Member outreach tracking programs</li>
<li>Create and Execute Marketing</li>
<li>Keep Updated marketing flyers around fitness floor and club areas</li>
<li>Track new member and birthday outreach efforts</li>
</ul>
Member Outreach
<br><ul>
<li>Complete, record and follow-up on Member outreach calls and emails</li>
<li>Responsible for new member and birthday efforts</li>
<li>Create member outreach communication programs</li>
<li>Keep members " in the know" with event invitations</li>
</ul>
Communication and Relationships
<br><ul>
<li>Effectively and professionally interacts and communicates with senior management, managers, other Associates, vendors, members, senior staff, and guests.</li>
<li>Promote harmonious relations among members, staff, vendors, and the community at-large.</li>
<li>Communicate timely and follow up, as appropriate.</li>
<li>Active participation in required events, required programs, required meetings and required training's.</li>
</ul>
Corporate Goals &amp; Objectives
<br><ul>
<li>Promote the company's safety goals and actively work towards ensuring a safe working environment.</li>
<li>Interpret and ensure compliance with company policies and procedures.</li>
<li>Actively further Fitness objectives and continuously support improvement and positive change, which includes participating on corporate designated committees to improve the company and business operations.</li>
<li>Make professional development a consistent priority.</li>
<li>Active participation in events, programs, meetings and training</li>
</ul>
OTHER JOB FUNCTIONS
<br><ul>
<li>Flexibility in work schedule, as job may require day and evening shifts, weekends and holidays</li>
</ul>

EXPERIENCE &amp; EDUCTION
