<p><b>
About this role:
</b></p>
<p>Provide support in the administration of the worldwide Sales, Client Partner Group, Consulting, Event and Specialist Compensation Plans.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
What you’ll do:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Member of one of four sub-departments (Service, Design, Payment/Calculations, Quota)</li>
<li>
Increase the effectiveness and efficiency of the transaction to payment process</li>
<li>
Produce Ad-hoc analyses for various business partners and internal clients</li>
<li>
Conduct worldwide sales support through education and training</li>
<li>
Perform recurring compensation plan design and documentation</li>
<li>
Administer initial and ongoing quota setting for a global sales force</li>
<li>
Communicate and coordinate between multiple financial, sales and operational departments</li>
<li>
Complete recurring &amp; ad hoc manual tasks for processes not systematically automated</li>
<li>
Interact with Sales associates to explain and assist in the understanding of sales performance, commission calculations, quota assignment, and reporting</li>
<li>
Review monthly commission payments, confirm commission calculations by reconciling data to source systems, and sign-off on payment integrity</li>
<li>
Maintain commission system by performing a variety of data inputs and ensuring the accuracy of processes by analyzing data outputs</li>
<li>
Understand multiple internal areas of business (Sales, Finance, Territories, etc) to research, analyze, and resolve potential issues</li>
<li>
Perform validation and data quality checks on both data imports and exports</li>
<li>
Identify and research inconsistencies to determine root causes and offer proposals for remediation</li>
<li>
Suggest, implement and test system enhancements as needed</li>
<li>
Prepare recurring formal and ad hoc reporting for Sales and Management</li>
<li>
Coordinate and complete additional projects as requested</li>
</ul>

<li>
Bachelor's Degree required; Finance, Math, Statistics, Economics, Business Management</li>

3-5 years business experience with the ability to design and build moderately complex business/analytical models and possesses the ability to effectively communicate analyses and/or recommendations with senior stake holders</li>
<li>
Excellent excel skills</li>
<li>
Ease with technology</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to work in both a team and individual contributor environment</li>
<li>
Unwavering professionalism and executive presence</li>
<li>
Professional writing/communication excellence</li>
<li>
Innate problem-solving ability, digging deep, persevering, overcoming obstacles to solutions/analysis</li>
<li>
Ability to efficiently gather data, perform quantitative and qualitative data analysis, and succinctly communicate analyses in a language understood by business leaders across multiple disciplines within an organization</li>
<li>
Ability to self-prioritize responsibilities, possibly by end goal, result, and manage to completion</li>
<li>
Takes complete ownership in areas of key responsibilities and has a strong desire to be the subject matter expert of your domain</li>
<li>
Strong collaborative and interpersonal strengths</li>
</ul>
