<h2>Senior Tax Accountant (Contract)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Trustwave is a leading cybersecurity and managed security services provider that helps businesses fight cybercrime, protect data and reduce security risk. Offering a comprehensive portfolio of managed security services, security testing, consulting, technology solutions and cybersecurity education, Trustwave helps businesses embrace digital transformation securely. Trustwave is a Singtel company and the global security arm of Singtel, Optus and NCS, with customers in 96 countries. For more information about Trustwave, visit https://www.trustwave.com.</p>
<p>
As the Senior Tax Accountant within the Revenue team, you will have a direct impact on the organization through the management of the Sales Tax journey within our Revenue cycles and provide hands-on support as we build complex product offerings and in turn determine the appropriate Sales Tax processes.</p>
<p>
This will be a great opportunity to work with the business as a trusted adviser, as you will be working with sales, operations and other members of the finance team. In turn, Trustwave will offer support for continued professional development and mentoring from the wider accounting team who have worked across the globe.</p>
<p>
Candidates should be a self-motivated professional, happy to have a hands-on role and you will get involved in a wide range of processes in order to understand them and implement improvements. As a mentor to junior members of the team, you will also be assisting with evaluating and developing team members so they may become stronger professionals within the organization.</p>
<p>
Key tasks for this our next team member will include the following:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Utilize Avalara for preparation and filing of state sales and use tax returns</li>
<li>
Coordinate sales and use tax audits</li>
<li>
Serve as the designated tax liaison for each government jurisdiction</li>
<li>
Coordinate other local and state tax filing requirements</li>
<li>
Maintain Sales Tax Software accounts and tax codes</li>
<li>
Maintain and update Customer Sales Tax exemption certificates via Avalare</li>
<li>
Assist with nexus review and sales tax registration for multiple states</li>
<li>
Sales Tax Payable reconciliations</li>
</ul>
<p>
<br>
Requirements</p>
<ul>
<li>
Minimum 3 years of sales tax experience with in-depth knowledge of sales tax return preparation &amp; reporting</li>
<li>
Ability to read, interpret, and apply general state and tax regulations, and return instructions.</li>
<li>
Highly organized with excellent written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>
Experience with Avalara is highly desirable</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
Education:<br>
We prefer college-educated applicants, but at minimum, high school diploma or equivalent is required for employment.<br>
