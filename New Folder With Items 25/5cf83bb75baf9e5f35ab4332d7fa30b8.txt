<h2>Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Account Executive Job Description</b></p>
<p><b>Hours: Full Time - 40 hours; Mon-Fri (8 am - 5pm)</b></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities - </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Generates, closes, and onboards new business.</li>
<li>Manages the relationship and growth of existing accounts.</li>
<li>Focuses sales efforts by studying existing and potential volume from all clients.</li>
<li>Keeps sales manager informed by submitting activity and results reports, such as daily call reports, weekly work plans, and monthly and annual territory analyses.</li>
<li>Monitors competition by gathering current marketplace information on pricing, products, new products, delivery schedules, merchandising techniques, etc.</li>
<li>Recommends changes in products, service, and policy by evaluating results and competitive developments.</li>
<li>Resolves customer complaints by investigating problems; developing solutions; preparing reports; making recommendations to management.</li>
<li>Maintains professional and technical knowledge by attending translation educational workshops; conferences, reviewing professional publications; establishing personal networks; participating in professional associations.</li>
<li>Ability to create and generate sales for all services provided by Linguava on top of document translation, on-site interpretation, telephonic interpretation, and video relay interpretation.</li>
<li>Contributes to team efforts by accomplishing related results as directed by sales manager.</li>
<li>Embody an Above and Beyond customer service mentality to provide outstanding customer service with all clients. Create a raving fan culture with our clients, office staff and interpreters.</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Uphold Linguava’s Core Values, Mission and Vision statement.</li></ul>
<p><b>Requirements (Preferred Minimum)</b></p>
<ul>
<li>5 years of B2B sales experience</li>
<li>Ability to meet sales goals and possess strong closing skills</li>
<li>Business bachelor’s degree or additional relevant years of experience</li>
<li>Professional communication skills; verbal and written</li>
<li>Proficiency with standard software applications (Excel, Word, PowerPoint, Salesforce), and familiarity with other web based applications.</li>
<li>Ability to travel to trade shows, conferences etc.</li>
<li>Ability to excel in a demanding, dynamic, and team based working environment</li>
<li>Flexibility in schedule with a general 8am-5pm schedule</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Benefits </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Medical, Dental and Vision Insurance (After 90 Days)</li>
<li>Paid Holidays and Sick Days; PTO after first year</li>
<li>Employee 401K Program</li>
<li>Incentive Program &amp; Bonus Structure Available</li>
<li>Mentorship program</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Compensation</b></p>
<p>DOE</p>
<p>Salary plus commission</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Additional Compensation:</p>
<ul><li>Commission</li></ul>
<p>Work Location:</p>
<ul><li>One location</li></ul>
