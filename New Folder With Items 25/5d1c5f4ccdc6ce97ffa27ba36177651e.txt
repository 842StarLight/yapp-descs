Founded in 2006 by Langley Steinert (co-founder of TripAdvisor), CarGurus is a technology company with a passion for data and its power to simplify every aspect of the car shopping experience. Using proprietary technology, search algorithms and innovative data analytics, we provide unbiased validation on pricing, dealer reputation and vehicle history.

The Field Account Manager (FAM) role works with our largest dealer partners and is responsible for post-sale onboarding of dealerships as well as supporting them throughout their customer lifecycle. The FAM role has a strong relationship with both internal (Field Sales Directors) and external (dealer partners) stakeholders. As the primary contact for dealers and dealer groups, FAMs are responsible for providing an effective and communicative support experience along with proactive, consultative feedback to ensure dealers are getting the most out of their subscription. FAMs work closely with Field Sales Directors to drive growth within the existing customer base through renewals, product upgrades, and new product sales.
<br><br>
<b>What You'll Do:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Manage account base of roughly 350-400 existing field accounts (75-100 groups)</li>
<li>Become a subject matter expert on all CarGurus products (listings, display advertising, SEM, and new product initiatives)</li>
<li>Complete post-sale onboarding training for new dealerships and dealership personnel</li>
<li>Provide dealership and group-level performance analysis; identify areas of potential improvement</li>
<li>Educate dealers on how to maximize the value of the CarGurus platform by leveraging tools, reports, and best practices</li>
<li>When appropriate, work with customer to follow-up on technical, billing, and content issues</li>
<li>Advanced cross-departmental collaboration (support, billing, content, product and engineering) to convey key issues to the rest of the CarGurus organization.</li>
<li>Maintain strong relationships with Field Sales Directors and support the progression of MRR growth opportunities via back-office support, ongoing customer engagement, and leveraging existing relationships to recognize growth opportunities</li>
<li>deep and wide-ranging relationships with dealership decision makers and staff, increasing our span of influence and the number of CG Champions within the dealership</li>
<li>Prospect, identify, and qualify up-sell/cross-sell opportunities and renewal candidates to directly support MRR expansion efforts</li>

In summary, Field Account Managers will provide ongoing support to maximize the overall level of client satisfaction and minimize customer churn. To ensure the best customer experience, combined with ongoing business requirements, Field Sales Directors will tightly partner with their FAM counterparts to create a seamless spectrum of CarGurus engagement for any customer. From initial onboarding through steady-state proactive and strategic support, FAM will serve as the face of CarGurus to customers throughout the post-sale partnership.
