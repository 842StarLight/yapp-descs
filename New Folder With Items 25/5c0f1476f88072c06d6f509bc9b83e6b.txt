<h2>Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>We are currently seeking experienced sales professionals to join the Account Management team in our rapidly growing promotional products company! This is a fun industry and our company prides itself on having an amazing culture that is reflected, by our representatives, to all of our current and potential clients. We promote an environment that is dedicated to excellence through customer satisfaction, teamwork, and personal growth.</p>
<p>
The Inside Sales Rep/Account Manager position is responsible for managing and building recurring revenue on existing accounts. Success in this position relies on your ability to build long-term relationships with business leaders and marketing managers, establishing their trust, and offering advice on how our services can increase their customer acquisition and retention rates.</p>
<p>
We are lucky to have some unique competitive advantages in the market, which are helping our business grow faster than we can ramp up staffing. However, someone coming into the industry for the first time has a lot to learn and we invest a lot in hiring and training. Therefore, our ideal candidates are people who want to grow within our company, as well as the industry; a stable work history is preferred. Experience or interest in advertising, marketing, event planning, graphic design, supply chain management, and/or creative sales is also desirable.</p>
<p>
 Job Duties:</p>
<ul>
<li>Use a consultative sales approach to understand the customer's marketing goals and obstacles</li>
<li>Visit high potential and top clients to cultivate and maintain long term relationships (5-10% travel, company paid)</li>
<li>Work with our designers and suppliers to create and deliver the custom products your clients need, on schedule</li>
<li>Eventually build and manage an existing book of accounts and generate repeat business through relationship building and client engagement</li>
</ul>
<p>Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>Outgoing and engaging with the ability to build rapport quickly with business leaders at all levels</li>
<li>Sales Professional who has a passion for a consultative sales process and understands the value of relationships in the world of sales</li>
<li>Understands different sales cycles as well as building and managing a pipeline</li>
<li>Polished, professional appearance and comfortable meeting with clients/suppliers</li>
<li>Adaptable problem-solver who can think outside the box to create solutions for complex client marketing needs</li>
<li>A positive “go-getter” attitude, with experience setting and achieving personal goals, as well as being a team-player</li>
<li>Success driven, with strong attention to detail and excellent organizational/time management skills</li>
</ul>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul>
<li>Sales: 3 years (Required)</li>
<li>B2B or Marketing/Promotional</li>
</ul>
<p>Education:</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree (Preferred)</li>
</ul> <p>Employment type: Full-Time</p>
<p>
All Applicants will be subject to a background check and drug screen</p>
<p>
ProImprint is an Equal Opportunity Employer</p>
