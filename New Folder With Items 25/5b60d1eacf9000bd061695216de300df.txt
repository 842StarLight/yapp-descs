<h2>Quality Engineer II - Exton, PA</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>DSM – Bright Science. Brighter Living.™
</b></p>
<p>Royal DSM is a purpose-led global science-based company in Nutrition, Health and Sustainable Living. DSM is driving economic prosperity, environmental progress and social advances to create sustainable value for all stakeholders. DSM delivers innovative business solutions for human nutrition, animal nutrition, personal care and aroma, medical devices, green products and applications, and new mobility and connectivity. DSM and its associated companies deliver annual net sales of about €10 billion with approximately 23,000 employees. The company is listed on Euronext Amsterdam. More information can be found at www.dsm.com .<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<div>
<div><b><i>Are you an experienced Quality Engineer accustomed to working in highly regulated environments? This valued resource would work cross-functionally with a variety of departments to lead qualifications through process development scale up activities. Join our growing team in Exton, PA!<br>
</i></b></div>
<div></div>
<div><b>The Challenge
</b></div>
<div>To support the BU regarding engineering activities for all product lines; leading qualifications, designing, leading, and reporting validations, data analysis, statistical process control (SPC), CAPA, KPI’s, Review, and Trending. Responsible for product-related root cause analysis, process mapping and impact assessments for change control.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>The Position
</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Expected to serve as primary contact with business unit customers of all sizes within platform expertise; including communicating data, reports, conclusions and changes.
</li>
<li>Develop, document, and implement QA Procedures.
</li>
<li>Analyze non-conforming products and returned goods and recommend disposition and corrective actions.
</li>
<li>Create suitable data analysis tables and report findings based on statistical evidence and trending.
</li>
<li>Design and specify gauging and test equipment for quality assurance functions
</li>
<li>Design own experimental work, analyze data and generate summary reports.
</li>
<li>Design, execute and report validations including Operational Qualifications, Process Qualifications, Measurement System Analysis and Test Method Validations.
</li>
<li>Review documentation and reports for soundness of technical judgement, accuracy and conclusions
</li>
<li>Create, assess and process production documentation, test methods, product drawings and other written documentation in accordance with GMP requirements to support product development and manufacturing.
</li>
<li>Work within design controls to assist product development activities.
</li>
<li>Review new product designs (internal and external) and make recommendations for quality requirements.
</li>
<li>Plan, write and implement DOE’s and test protocols required to evaluate new and existing products.
</li>
<li>Work closely with internal teams to coordinate, plan, and execute project requirements. Under guidance, lead multidisciplinary teams.
</li>
<li>Adhere to Quality System procedures, ISO13485, 21CFR part 820 and current regulatory standards.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>The Ideal
</b></div>
<ul>
<li>BS Degree in Engineering
</li>
<li>ASQE or Six Sigma Black Belt Certification a plus.
</li>
<li>4-6 years Quality Engineering within a QA/QC department at a FDA-Regulated, Medical Device, Pharmaceutical, Biotech or other regulated industry.
</li>
<li>Proficient use of statistical techniques and software (i.e – Minitab).
</li>
<li>Understanding of GMP and standards.
</li>
<li>Knowledge of medically useful biomaterials (degradable and non-degradable).
</li>
<li>Knowledge of physical and chemical processing techniques. (ie- melt processing, compression molding, foaming, lyophilization, aseptic processing, cross-linking, sterilization)
</li>
<li>Working knowledge of analytical and mechanical test equipment.<br>
</li>
</ul>
