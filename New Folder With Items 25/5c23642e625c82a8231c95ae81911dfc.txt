Job Description
</div>
<div>
<div>Manufactures sufficient quantities of quality products at or below budgeted production costs in order to meet marketplace demand. Sets up equipment operation feed, speed and temperature. Follows established procedures for operating various conventional and/or CNC (computerized numerical control) manufacturing equipment for tasks such as cutting, punching, forming, drilling, tapping, turning, milling, and stamping to fabricate, form or shape manufactured parts, tools or parts assemblies from raw materials. Selects and measures raw materials and parts to specifications to prepare for production processing. Resolves issues related to the organization's production, machinery, processing and/or packaging operations. Responsibilities are within Manufacturing/Operations Function as a generalist or in a combination of Disciplines.</div>
</div>
</div>
<div>
<div>
Job Description Cont.
</div>
<div>
<div>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
Interpret and use production schedules, data cards and MOTs<br>
Load and unload rolls of plain and printed material<br>
Inspect incoming material for visual and measurable attributes<br>
Perform minor maintenance and cleaning as required<br>
Thread materials through printing press<br>
Perform quality checks on press materials and troubleshoot inconsistencies<br>
Continually monitor quality while printing press is in operation<br>
Assure materials and equipment are available in advance<br>
Daily interaction with computer software including data collection<br>
Communicate with Production Control regarding production schedule, move-ups, and throughput as needed<br>
Load and unload cylinder sleeves and anilox rolls<br>
Clean printing parts i.e., cavities, ink kits, etc<br>
Change ink cavities<br>
Process rolls of rejected material<br>
Operate specialized equipment necessary for proper function of the printing press<br>
Self-Manage and work harmoniously in team environment<br>
Perform other duties as requested or needed to support the team environment<br>
Perform Product and Facility Sanitation duties as required<br>
Serve on a Team Resource Committee as required</div>
</div>
