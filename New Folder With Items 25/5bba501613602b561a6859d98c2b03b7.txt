<h2>Senior Customer Briefing Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<b>What you will do</b><br>
Provides strategic consultation and project management required to develop and implement consistent world class customized showcase visits and events.<br>
<br>
<b>How you will do it</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Consults, plans, implements and facilitates customer visits and events with internal sales team to drive revenue and grow customer relationships. Customized visit should meet both the visiting company and internal customer’s requirements, this requires a solid knowledge of entire Buildings business.</li>
<li>Ensures the center delivers a favorable impression of Johnson Controls; manages all logistical aspects of entire visit</li>
<li>Conducts tours at Milwaukee Showcase and other facility to educate internal and external participants on Johnson Controls, Inc. offerings.</li>
<li>Present and delivers message of value of customer visit program to internal groups (sales training, VM kick offs, new employee orientation, etc.)</li>
<li>Locates, evaluates and manages internal and external resources needed to execute customer visits and special events.</li>
<li>Interacts with appropriate BE business executives, directors and managers to execute and assure customized experiences and messages includes coaching and developing visit, event and presentation strategies.</li>
<li>Works with sales management to support business and sales strategies and drive activity through customer visit locations.</li>
<li>Conducts project planning, including budgets and execution for special events as needed.</li>
<li>Manage customer visit pre-engagement arrangements through follow up, which includes cleaning and set up (agenda, feedback forms, name tents, badges, AV set up, meals, logistics, custom collateral, etc. )</li>
<li>Manage BMO Harris and Miller Park process, calendar and ticket allocation. Work with Brewers and Bucks to operationalize and ensure flawless execution.</li>
<li>Performs other duties as required</li>
</ul>
<br>
</div>
<p></p>
<div>
<b>
Qualifications</b><div>
</div>
<b>
What we look for</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s in Marketing, Communications or equivalent work experience.</li>
<li>Minimum of 5+ years’ experience in business to business marketing or communications role or relevant business experience.</li>
<li>Minimum of two years’ experience with excel, PowerPoint and sales management tracking software program is required.</li>
<li>Business etiquette savvy, working knowledge of Johnson Controls business landscape, products and speaker resources, as well as the ability to interact and communicate effectively with senior executives, sales teams and customers from around the world.</li>
</ul>
<br>
Capabilities and Success Factors<br>
<ul>
<li>Customer-driven sense of urgency</li>
<li>Proven ability to effectively manage very demanding workload while achieving strong results.</li>
<li>High attention to detail</li>
<li>Exceptional ability to execute, collaborate, work cross functionally and multi-task in a fast-paced, high stakes environment.</li>
<li>Ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously and turn ideation into action</li>
<li>Collaborative team-approach, working effectively with multiple cross-functional groups</li>
<li>Ability to influence others to complete tasks without direct management</li>
<li>Ability to implement continuous improvements</li>
<li>Self-motivated individual who can work independently, but is eager to collaborate</li>
<li>Solve unforeseen situations as they arise in a calm manner</li>
<li>A fast-learner, who can quickly absorb and adapt to new tools and technologies</li>
</ul>
