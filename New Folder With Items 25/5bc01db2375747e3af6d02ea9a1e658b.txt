<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Position purpose</b></h2>
<p>
The Campus Visit Coordinator (CVC) is responsible to schedule and coordinate prospective student events including personal visits, group visits, Preview Days, NCU Days, Summer Orientation days, and other visit events. In addition, the CVC is responsible to greet and serve those requesting information from the Admissions Office, greet campus visitors, process information inquiries to the university, and process mail and email communications. In addition to these responsibilities, the CVC supports the office with any additional requested tasks.</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Responsibilities include but are not limited to</b></h2>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
(80%) Schedule and coordinate campus visits for prospective students</b></h4>
<ul>
<li>
Answer requests for campus visits</li>
<li>
Schedule and communicate with Admissions counselors and appropriate departments for requested meetings for prospective students</li>
<li>
Prepare all materials for each visit</li>
<li>
Schedule overnight guests with student hosts</li>
<li>
Prepare appropriate information with Security for guests arriving after hours</li>
<li>
Plan and execute visit events, on campus and online</li>
<li>
Provide reporting on all visits and visit events</li>
<li>
Keep financial records of all visit costs</li>
</ul>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
(20%) Support office functions</b></h4>
<ul>
<li>
Serve as department receptionist: greet guests who physically enter the office and either serve them personally or notify the appropriate person they should meet</li>
<li>
Answer phone calls on the admissions and financial aid hotline and either personally answer questions or transfer the call to the appropriate office or person</li>
<li>
Manage the main Admissions email account and forward e-mails to appropriate Admissions team member</li>
<li>
Provide front desk coverage when necessary</li>
<li>
Maintain order, neatness, and beauty in the office to create a warm and welcoming atmosphere for all of our guests</li>
<li>
Launder tablecloths as needed</li>
<li>
Input prospective student data acquired from various sources, including online or over the phone, into Salesforce</li>
<li>
Utilize Salesforce, Outlook, and Textaim for external and internal communication</li>
<li>
Be able to utilize Admissions use of NCU’s Meeting Room Manager reservation software</li>
<li>
Work with the Admissions Coordinator to keep office supplies stocked and order supplies as needed for the Admissions Office</li>
<li>
Create and update guest information packets</li>
<li>
Responsible for copying, filing, faxing as needed</li>
<li>
Update processes through OneNote</li>
<li>
Follow directions from and help with other tasks and projects as assigned by the Director and Associate Director of Admissions</li>
</ul>
