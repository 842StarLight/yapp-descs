We’re seeking a flexible IT Support Administrator to help manage and support Affirm’s technology infrastructure. For our ideal candidate, troubleshooting and problem-solving are beloved pastimes, and they come with a healthy respect for compliance and documentation.</div>

<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What You'll Do</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Maintain enterprise systems and company IT assets such as servers and desktops</li>
<li>
Plan, deploy, and configure new systems to keep up with changing needs</li>
<li>
Support our end users in leveraging technology to optimize their workflows</li>
<li>
Monitor for security threats and malware, staying ahead of the curve to protect our users</li>
<li>
Educate on and enforce our IT compliance and policy obligations</li>
<li>
Physical and facilitative tasks as needed (Moving desks, assembling furniture, relocating users, etc.)
</li>
<li>Oversee office security and access. (Issue keycards, maintain security cameras, etc.)</li>

</div>
</ul></div>
