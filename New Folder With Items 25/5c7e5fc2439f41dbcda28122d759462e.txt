<p><b>Our Mission</b></p>
<p>
At Palo Alto Networks® everything starts and ends with our mission:</p>
<p>
Being the cybersecurity partner of choice, protecting our digital way of life.</p>
<p>
We have the vision of a world where each day is safer and more secure than the one before. These aren’t easy goals to accomplish – but we’re not here for easy. We’re here for better. We are a company built on the foundation of challenging and disrupting the way things are done, and we’re looking for innovators who are as committed to shaping the future of cybersecurity as we are.</p>
<p><b>
Your Career</b></p>
<p>
You will work with your sales partner to establish relationships with customers with the goal of helping them detect and prevent advanced cyber attacks and breaches by advising them on what applications to deploy from the Palo Alto Networks platform. Our systems engineers have a deep technical understanding of cybersecurity products, integrations, and critical cyber threats facing our potential customer’s environments.</p>
<p>
We need these technical guides to work with our sales teams, providing training and technical support. You build trust with our clients and teams, establishing strong rapport as a trusted advisor, and build an environment where our clients feel (and are) secure. Additionally, you'll be called upon to offer feedback to our product management team on new feature requests and product improvements based on what you learn from your customer base.</p>
<p><b>
Your Impact</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Establish yourself as a trusted advisor to prospects and customers working with your Account Manager and the local partners within your territory</li>
<li>
Identify and document specific problems with prospective and current customers which can be solved through the deployment of integrated solutions</li>
<li>
Architect and propose validated solutions which address the identified cybersecurity problems in each individual environment</li>
<li>
Ensure ongoing customer happiness, support, and adoption</li>
<li>
Continuous self-improvement and learning to maintain technical leadership of applicable technologies (data center, SDN, public cloud, security, networking, etc.)</li>
<li>
Understand and effectively differentiate against our top competitors</li>
<li>
Act as the customer advocate for any issues that require technical assistance and follow up with the customer until the issue is resolved</li>
</ul>
