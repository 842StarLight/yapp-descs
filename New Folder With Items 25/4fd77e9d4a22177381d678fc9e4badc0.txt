<p><b>Position Description:
</b></p>
<p>Samsung Electronics America is looking for a dynamic and accomplished (RAN/Core) Systems Engineer (SE) to help drive growth for our Pvt LTE solutions. As a global leader in Mobile Infrastructure solutions, Samsung Networks offers carriers and enterprise customers a broad portfolio of hardware and software solutions to build a compete LTE/ 5G network using licensed spectrum or shared spectrum frequencies (CBRS).
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Within Samsung Networks our Enterprise group delivers CBRS based Pvt LTE solutions to Fortune500 companies. The Enterprise Networks group has an exciting opportunity for you to join this team. We are seeking a Systems Engineer to work with major Enterprise accounts to drive the adoption and rollout of Pvt LTE networks and 5G technologies. 2 Positions are open and will both be based in Dallas.
</p>
<p><b>Essential Duties &amp; Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for working with Technical Account Managers for Pvt LTE &amp; 5G customer opportunities: presenting the RAN/ EPC portfolio, conducting products demos at a customer site, setting up and conducting a trial and providing all pre sales technical support on Samsung CBRS and 5G product lines.
</li>
<li>Responsible for technical enablement activities like Cellular RF Design &amp; Deployment Best Practices, developing technical whitepapers and solution briefs on CBRS/ 5G.
</li>
<li>Develop/ Deliver CBRS/ 5G technical training and competitive differentiation presentations that show the value of Samsung network solutions, outlining technical differentiation and highlighting the key benefits.
</li>
<li>Conduct benchmark performance testing of CBRS / 5G products (RRH/ DU style, Compact Small Cells, EPC) including competitive testing and bakeoffs. Hands on technical experience with Carrier Grade Radio products from major mobile infrastructure vendors is required.
</li>
<li>Evaluates, validates, creates network diagrams, and presents the design of technical solutions to customers.
</li>
<li>Understand customer pain points with existing wireless networks and explain how Samsung solutions can enhance / replace Outdoor WiFi, LMR Push-to-Talk Radio networks and other legacy wireless technologies with a CBRS based Pvt LTE network and B48 enabled smartphones.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Skills and Qualifications
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Background/Experience required:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's Degree in engineering with 12+ years of Sales Engineering or Systems Engineering (SE) experience with LTE /5G Network solutions.
</li>
<li>Hands-on technical experience in LTE/ 5G RAN, LTE Core, CBRS (3.5GHz) required.
</li>
<li>Deep domain knowledge of customer challenges with Indoor / Outdoor Wireless Connectivity, LMR Systems, Push to Talk, PTP &amp; PMP Wireless Broadband systems.
</li>
<li>Ability to setup and conduct demos/ PoCs and do RF design, cell capacity planning, Radio configuration.
</li>
<li>Solid knowledge of layer 3 through 7 network stack (IP Routing, VPN) to assist customers to implement and integrate the Samsung Mobile Network in their LAN/ WAN environment.
</li>
<li>Requires understanding of Virtualization, NFV and Cloud based systems for Operator environments.
</li>
</ul>
