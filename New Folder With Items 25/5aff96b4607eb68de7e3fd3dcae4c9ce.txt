<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Responsibilities: We are seeking an experienced information security sales professional to drive business development for our growing cybersecurity practice nationwide focused on state and local government clients. S/he will develop and manage a pipeline, build and manage relationships with key stakeholders, support marketing events, drive the end-to-end sales process (pre-sales conversations, proposal development, client presentations). This roles will be supported by our cybersecurity practitioners and subject matter specialists to enable growth and successful delivery. Strong interpersonal and communication skills.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Key Responsibilities<br>
<ul>
<li>Create and execute strategic sales plans and build and manage pipeline</li>
<li>Be active in the field to identify prospective clients, discover market needs, and shape opportunities</li>
<li>Engage in value solution-centered dialogue with key decision-makers to understand their needs and offer relevant services/solutions</li>
<li>Facilitate successful customer meetings and presentations</li>
<li>Stay on top of market trends and changes, be aware of new technologies and how it plays a role in our practice development and partnerships with technology vendors</li>
<li>Identify and support key marketing events relevant to state and local information security leaders</li>
<li>Build and nurture relationships with clients and prospective clients</li>
<li>Work collaboratively with cybersecurity practitioners on deals</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Qualifications Required:<br>
<ul>
<li>BA/BS or equivalent experience</li>
<li>Proven 5-7 years sales track record experience selling information security related services/products</li>
<li>Understanding of state and local government procurement process (preferred)</li>
<li>Ability to work independently and as part of a team</li>
<li>Understand the landscape and current needs of CISOs and IT buyers</li>
<li>Comfortable with demonstrating and presenting information security services</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal and communication skills</li>
<li>Passion for delivering value to clients and driving client success</li>
</ul>
</div>
