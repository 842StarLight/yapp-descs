<h2>Landscape Business Developer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Scott Byron &amp; Co. is looking for a highly motivated landscape sales professional to work in the territory of Chicago to grow and develop our maintenance sales.</p>
<p><b>Daily Tasks: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop relationships with existing clients, new prospects or new leads; both residential and commercial, with the goal of generating new maintenance sales opportunities. This may require time outside of normal business hours.</li>
<li>Develop relationships via community, business, charity, industry or other type of event in our core identified target markets to generate new maintenance sales opportunities. This may require time outside of normal business hours.</li>
<li>Seek out opportunities within existing Construction Sales to advance Maintenance services.</li>
<li>Meet key performance indicators specific to Maintenance sales generation, profitability and retention.</li>
<li>Assist and coordinate efforts with marketing to advance maintenance marketing strategies.</li>
<li>Communicate routinely and consistently with all clients, both internal and external, via phone, text, email or other appropriate technology to maintain good communication, high standards, and positive relations.</li>
<li>Responsible to drive daily in a company vehicle with or without passengers.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Sales experience is required, preferably in a green industry related position.</li>
<li>Experience working in a green industry related position is required.</li>
<li>A self-starter with great communication.</li>
<li>Demonstrated proficiency in Microsoft Office and basic computer skills is required. Experience in Boss LM or other cloud-based management software is a plus.</li>
<li>Candidates must have a valid local driver’s license and good driving record.</li>
<li>Required to pass a DOT medical physical according to DOT guidelines.</li>
<li>A pre-employment physical and drug test is required after offer of employment.</li>
<li>Will be subject to random drug screening.</li>
<li>Must be 21 years or older.</li>
</ul>
