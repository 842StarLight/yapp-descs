<h2>Payroll Implementation Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>BerganKDV is currently looking for a Full Time Payroll Implementaton Specialist to join their new and growing HCM team!
</p>
<p>The Payroll Implementation Specialist is responsible for the successful conversion of all new clients to BerganKDV’s Payroll systems, processes and procedures. They will work with the BerganKDV sales, Implementation Coordinator and client to ensure a smooth transition from the previous payroll processor. The Implementation Specialist will help coordinate all transition activities, set transition schedules and ensure conversion deadlines are met. They will work to develop a strong relationship with the client and set relationship and performance expectations for the future.
</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ensures the data population, operation procedures, features, and enhancements of the implemented system meets the end user’s needs and specifications
</li>
<li>Maintains the integrity of the system; assists in data testing and acceptance
</li>
<li>Creates user training programs and documentation
</li>
<li>Verifies and analyzes payroll and data from various formats and determines requirements for the conversion; including data entry of company setup, employee and tax data into the new system.
</li>
<li>Keeps all system documentation up to date.
</li>
<li>Provides guidance in the payroll software and makes recommendations for system changes/enhancements.
</li>
<li>Informs clients on regulatory changes that may affect their HR or Payroll policies, programs or procedures
</li>
<li>Participates in Sales presentations; demonstrates Payroll system features and interface capabilities.
</li>
<li>Assists in the resolution of client issues and problems
</li>
<li>Develops and implements data integrity measures and testing.
</li>
<li>Notifies management of issues, and make suggestions for corrective action<br>
</li>
</ul>
