<div><b>Intent Strategy Group (ISG) Meetings Management Consultant</b></div>
<div>The Meetings Management Consultant is responsible for project management and consulting around meetings management technology and SMMP (Strategic Meetings Management Program) initiatives. Typical projects include new Cvent implementations or transitions from Lanyon to Cvent, optimizing or refining M&amp;IW’s or our clients’ use of existing Cvent accounts, strategic initiative deployments such as SMMP roll-outs or global expansions, business process/workflow automation design, event policy development, and guidance around system integrations/the Cvent ecosystem.</div>
<div></div>
<div>Focus areas include evaluating current systems and SOP’s to identify opportunities for strategic project management and prioritizing client engagements, with new account onboarding being of the highest priority. New projects originate from two primary channels - business development of new prospects, and the Strategic Account Team identifying opportunities within M&amp;IW’s existing client portfolio. This role often provides guidance and training to Sales and Account Management Teams, so they can be more knowledgeable of strategic and technical aspects of client and prospect engagements. This position reports to the VP of ISG.</div>
