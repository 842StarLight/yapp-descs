<h2>Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Elixir is an innovative software company focused on advancing the flow of digital business and solutions. In a world where data is ubiquitous and stakeholders expect to work digitally, businesses are reinventing their operations. We empower users by connecting them with the rights tools to help organizations with their customer communications and content creation in a seamless process that helps them achieve their goals.</p>
<p>Elixir is expanding a new sales team to focus on mid-market opportunities to sell our SaaS customer communications platform and solutions. We are seeking a dedicated and passionate Account Executive (AE) to join the Sales Team at our Ojai, CA headquarters. We are looking for a proactive and energetic self-starter with experience working in a growing technical sales environment who will focus on our US regions. The AE is a high-level sales role that supports Elixir’s sales goals by establishing and developing a strategy for identifying and closing new software subscription opportunities. The AE reports directly to the VP of Business Development (VPBD) and collaborates with a team of sales development representatives. We are looking for someone with proven experience in building relationships and closing sales. A collaborative and strategic thinker who is excited to learn new technology and share its benefits will thrive in our environment.</p>
<p>Essential functions with or without reasonable accommodations include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Align all sales activities with Elixir initiatives and adjust activities as necessary</li>
<li>Collaborate, lead and accomplish successful sales activities with internal cross-functional teams, senior management and corporate counsel, through contract negotiation and signed contracts</li>
<li>Maximize revenue through direct sales</li>
<li>Deliver compelling presentations and support product demonstrations to prospects and clients</li>
<li>Strategize and collaborate with the VPBD to solve sales problems and achieve quota</li>
<li>Execute on leads generated by Elixir’s marketing and sales development activities</li>
<li>Provide accountability for formal responses to customers, proposals, presentations and demonstrations</li>
<li>Maintain a working knowledge of all Elixir solutions and their relevance and value to the markets</li>
<li>Maximize potential customers through use of in person visits, web meetings, conference calls, trade shows, etc.</li>
<li>Actively listen to customers’ pain points and articulate how Elixir’s solutions will help them achieve their goals</li>
<li>Build a future opportunity pipeline that is 3-5x the annual quota for your region and maintain daily updates to Salesforce or other CRM</li>
<li>Collaborate and provide input on customer agreements such as subscription agreements, statements of work and sales orders</li>
<li>Provide feedback on customer requests to senior management</li>
</ul>
<p>Qualifications &amp; Skills</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in related field</li>
<li>3-7 years of account or sales experience</li>
<li>1-3 years of subscription-model sales experience</li>
<li>Experience with business-to-business commerce and customer communications management software desired</li>
<li>Passionate about customer success</li>
<li>Proactive approach and self-starter mentality to accomplishing goals</li>
<li>Advocacy for systems and tools such as DiscoverOrg, Hubspot, Growbots, Woodpecker and other modern sales enabling tools</li>
<li>Experience using Salesforce or other CRM</li>
<li>Ability to relate well to decision makers at all levels</li>
<li>Articulate and have great communication skills, including writing</li>
<li>Excellent organizational and time-management skills</li>
<li>Adaptable to new challenges and changes</li>
<li>Proficient in MS Office 365 apps</li>
<li>Ability to travel onsite to US customers ~20%</li>
</ul>
