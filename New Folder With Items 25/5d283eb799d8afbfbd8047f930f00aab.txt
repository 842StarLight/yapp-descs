<div>Position Overview
</div>
<div>At Siemens Digital Industries Software, our Solution Partner vision is to build partner businesses that thrive through collaborative partnerships. The global Solution Partner sales team leads, designs, and executes a world class global partner ecosystem through scalable and repeatable programs, that deliver an exceptional partner value-add experience to our customers to radically strengthen mutual business performance.</div>
<div>The Director, Global Cloud and SaaS Partner Development is a strategic role responsible for the strategy and execution of programs to expand the global partner ecosystem for Mendix and other Siemens cloud and SaaS based solutions, reporting to the VP of Global Solution Partner Sales. Candidates are expected to work cross functionally and collaborate with the sales, marketing, services and training/enablement teams to build a fully integrated partner ecosystem. A successful candidate will understand in depth the cloud market, cloud operational process and how partners drive customer adoption, consumption and deliver customer success. Candidates must be able to define a strategic vision for the business and identify programs for growth leveraging partners.</div>
<div></div>
<div>Responsibilities
</div>
<ul>
<li>Identify opportunities and programs to accelerate Mendix and cloud SaaS growth through partner ecosystem expansion to drive scalable growth and profitability</li>
<li>Work cross functionally to develop Mendix, cloud and SaaS partner programs including partner contracting, pricing, marketing, certifications and training, services and customer success to maximize sales revenue and influence revenue from across all partners</li>
<li>Develop a cohesive plan to help activate Siemens partners on Mendix, cloud and SaaS products and solutions</li>
<li>Collaborate with field partner sales teams to increase quality partners that add value to our current and prospective customers</li>
<li>Ensure execution in the partner ecosystem of joint campaigns and sales plays driving examples across joint Mendix and PLM solutions.</li>
</ul>
