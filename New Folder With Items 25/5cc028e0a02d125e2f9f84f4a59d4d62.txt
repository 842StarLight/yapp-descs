<p><b>
Job Summary</b></p>
<p>
Reporting directly to the Revenue Manager, Americas, this position is in our Tempe, Arizona office and will play an integral part in the Global Accounts Receivable team. Candidate must be sharp, disciplined and determined to deliver high-quality results in a timely manner. The right person will build collaborative relationships within the organization and exhibit a commitment to continuous improvement.</p>
<p><b>
Key Tasks and Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Undertake all phases of large company invoicing and customer support activities such as:</li>
<li>
New customer onboarding,</li>
<li>
Customer service and support,</li>
<li>
Managing statements and regular customer communications,</li>
<li>
Follow-up on collections of past due invoices, including escalation process.</li>
<li>
Handling remittance questions,</li>
<li>
Handling credit note reconciliations and questions,</li>
<li>
Contract management for renewals</li>
<li>
Sales team support,</li>
<li>
Customer invoicing portal setup and management,</li>
<li>
Other related duties as assigned by supervisor.</li>
</ul>
