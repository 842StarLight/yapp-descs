<h2>Group Product Manager Director - Cyber Risk, Analytics, and Insights</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>Title: Group Product Manager, Director – Cyber Risk, Analytics, and Insights</b></p>
<p><b>
Location: Herndon, Virginia</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Description:
</b></p>
<p>Exostar is seeking a multi-talented self-starter to lead Product Management and Product Marketing for its Cyber Risk Management, Analytics, and Business Insight portfolio of products developed for the Aerospace and Defense, Life Sciences, and Healthcare markets. The Group Product Manager, Director (GPM, Director) will report to the VP of Product Management and will be responsible for creating and leading the execution of a long term product strategy for the Cyber Risk, Analytics, and BI components of Exostar’s broader market offerings. He/she will be accountable for the successful performance of the product portfolio including product vision, product roadmap, product development, and delivery of solutions to customers which meet or exceed their expectations.</p>
<p>
This position will also be responsible for the ongoing development of the Product Management Group. He/she will manage the Product Managers and Business Analysts within his/her group, and will act as the product subject matter expert within the Exostar organization. The GPM Director will play a key leadership role to help the company grow and achieve its business objectives. She/he must be motivated, take initiative, be a team player, and be able to scale in a fast-growing, rapidly changing environment. The successful candidate will have a strong technical background with experience in Cyber Security and Risk Management, strong business acumen, outstanding communication skills, and the ability to collaborate cross functionally and with partners. He/she will be recognized as a product, technology, and business leader.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Responsibilities Include:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Create a product vision, technology strategy, and roadmap for Exostar’s Cyber Risk Management, Analytics, and Business Insight products</li>
<li>
Own and drive a product priorities on both operational and strategic levels; develop and manage plans for enhancements as well as new products and services to leverage related market opportunities</li>
<li>
Understand and own product strategy, developing business requirements and functional specs; clearly and effectively communicate product strategy, functionality, and requirements to internal and external stakeholders</li>
<li>
Develop product positioning strategies, value propositions, marketing content, and appropriate pricing strategies</li>
<li>
Manage multiple product owners/managers; provide leadership and direction to the team including recruiting, hiring, goal setting, resource allocation, utilization, performance management, professional development, coaching, inspiring, career pathing, succession planning and organization effectiveness</li>
<li>
Work with customers and potential customers to gather feedback on how to improve and extend our offerings; understand customer use cases and align with product marketing strategies</li>
<li>
Strong understanding of user experience and the ability to drive product usability</li>
<li>
Research competitive landscape, proactively gather market information and competitive intelligence, and adjust product roadmaps to address the relevant markets</li>
<li>
Measure product line success by customer acquisition, cost structure, profitability</li>
<li>
Manage appropriate customer user and advisory groups</li>
<li>
Work closely with customer support team to monitor and resolve customer escalations along with Engineering leadership</li>
<li>
Conduct sales training and support the development of sales enablement tools, including training materials, battle cards, and value messaging guides</li>
<li>
Support demand generation activities as needed including customer meetings, demonstrations, RFP responses, and business case development</li>
<li>
Ensure Exostar's products and services are delivered to market resulting in the highest levels of customer satisfaction</li>
<li>
Manage key vendor relationships as needed to ensure appropriate coordination of future releases of technology, go-to-market efforts, and on-going delivery to customers.</li>
<li>
Analyze new technologies and maintain close working relationships with industry experts and leading technology providers</li>
<li>
Perform other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
