<p>Power Test is growing! We are looking for a strong technical Project Manager to join our Sales and Service Department. You will be the central point of oversight and coordination for customer projects overseeing assigned projects from receipt of order through commissioning, delivering cohesive, on-time, and profitable execution that satisfies customer expectations. You will lead planning, implementation, and tracking of projects that are tied to a specific customer orders and inspire/manage cross-functional teams to work successfully in delivering expectations from initiation to completion.
</p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Overall responsibility for completion of assigned projects. Specifically, lead and facilitate cross-functional efforts to deliver high quality, profitable products and services as defined by the agreed upon order.</li>
<li>Establish mutual agreement between the customer and applicable project team members on initial product and service definitions, including costs and timelines. Obtain written acknowledgements of initially agreed upon project scope. Update accordingly throughout project timeline.
</li>
<li>Prepare, schedule, and facilitate project kickoffs, communicating expectations and responsibilities with the identified project team.
</li>
<li>Communicate project timelines and milestones to managers, other internal stakeholders, and customers for assigned projects.</li>
<li>Engage in frequent customer interaction to keep project on track, including but not limited to: project changes or updates; timely design reviews with the Project Engineer; and visiting customer as needed.
</li>
<li>Anticipate and identify potential hurdles and risks to delivering on the order as defined, including manpower issues and changes to original order scope and specifications. Communicate and implement timely contingency plans to ensure cohesive execution.</li>
<li>Ensure shop leaders are aware of project milestones and have information to ensure timely and efficient production.
</li>
<li>Communicate and ensure product shipment requirements are identified proactively, including necessary documents.
</li>
<li>Schedule factory acceptance test that delivers on objectives and project success.
</li>
<li>Implement process improvements identified along the project. Deliver timely project updates to leadership.
</li>
<li>Contribute to the company's Lean and Safety initiatives.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Knowledge and Skill</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor's Degree in Engineering or related field plus 5 years' minimum managing people, or equivalent combination of experience.
</li>
<li>Knowledge of and experience in applying accepted Project Management methods in a technical environment.
</li>
<li>Influential in driving teams, results and accountability, while earning respect for accomplishing goals through teams.
</li>
<li>Proven ability to prioritize and finish within deadlines, excellent organizational and multi-tasking skills with outstanding attention to detail.
</li>
<li>Highly self-directed with ability to identify and implement process and personnel improvements.
</li>
<li>Outstanding client-facing and internal communication skills with people across multiple disciplines.</li>
<li>Solid judgment and decision making skills, including those involving complex cost and quality matters.
</li>
<li>Strong mechanical aptitude, knowledge of powertrain products and component testing a plus.
</li>
<li>Basic understanding of electrical and software systems is necessary and ability to read engineering prints/BOMs required.</li>
<li>Strong skills with MS Suite; CAD, ideally SolidWorks/Composer; and a Project Management software.<br>
</li>
</ul>
