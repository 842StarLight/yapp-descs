What You’ll Get to Do:</b></p>
<p><b>
Sales Process</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Promotes/sells/secures orders from existing and prospective customers through a relationship-based approach.</li>
<li>
Achieve sales and profit targets for area of responsibility and responsible for developing demand forecast for assigned customer base.</li>
<li>
Demonstrates products to existing/potential customers and assists them in selecting those best suited to their needs</li>
<li>
Establishes, develops and maintains business relationships with current customers, manufacturer’s representatives and prospective customers in the assigned territory/market segment to generate new business for the organization’s products.</li>
<li>
Responsible for personal visits and presentations to Builders, Architects, Engineers, Contractors and Installer targets in support of the sales plan to meet the needs of the company and region.</li>
<li>
Researches sources for developing prospective customers and for information to determine their potential.</li>
<li>
Develops clear and effective written proposals /quotations for current and potential customers.</li>
<li>
Participate in trade shows, seminars and field events as directed.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Planning &amp; Training</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Analyzes the territory/market’s potential and determines the value of existing and prospective customers’ value to the organization.</li>
<li>
Creates and manages a customer value plan for existing customers highlighting profile, share and value opportunities.</li>
<li>
Plans and organizes personal sales strategy by maximizing the Return on Time Investment for the territory</li>
<li>
Keeps abreast of product applications, technical services, market conditions, competitive advertising and promotional trends through reading of pertinent literature and consulting with marketing and technical service areas.</li>
<li>
Demonstrate a thorough understanding of all Panasonic products, features, functions, and benefits and competitive advantages</li>
<li>
Provide on-the-job training /mentoring to new distributor sales employees.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Customer Service
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Expedites the resolution of customer problems and complaints.</li>
<li>
Coordinates sales effort with marketing, sales management, accounting, logistics and technical service groups.</li>

Administrative
</b></p>
