<h2>Receptionist/ Office Admin</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Job Description</p>
<p>Title: Receptionist/ Office Assistant</p>
<p>Report to: Supervisor/Manager/Director/VP/CFO/CEO</p>
<p>Primary Duty: Coordinate and Supporting immediate office, agent and employee needs</p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities include the following: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Greeting and Answering Phones and direct clients to the appropriate person</li>
<li>Provide a friendly and positive working environment</li>
<li>Maintaining clean and professional Office Appearance</li>
<li>Maintain and update open houses and floor time agent’s schedules</li>
<li>Maintain and monitor daily &amp; weekly inventory reports for executives and supervisor (i.e. sales logs, inventory, web Leads, sales leads &amp; mail leads etc.)</li>
<li>Maintain and update company’s ads on various multiple listing services, and other advertising websites.</li>
<li>Assist agents, employees and executives with various clerical needs (i.e. typing, copying, faxing, emailing, ordering disclosures, reports etc.)</li>
<li>Monitor accurate information and keep agents, supervisors &amp; executives aware of home available, pending &amp; sold status through company reporting system</li>
<li>Participate in Company weekly transaction meetings</li>
<li>Maintain and updating Files thru closing (Filing, Photo Copying, Faxing, etc..)</li>
<li>Order office supplies and keep cabinet stocked at all time</li>
<li>Distributing mails and Preparing Federal Express for mailing</li>
<li>Assist Home warranty service department with emails, updates, reports, etc..</li>
<li>Other Duties/Projects as required and /or assigned</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Types: Part-time to Full Time</p>
<p>Job Type: Part-time</p>
