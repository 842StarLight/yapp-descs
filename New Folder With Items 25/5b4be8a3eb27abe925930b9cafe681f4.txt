<h2>Account Executive, Mid Market - Duo Security</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Duo Security, now a part of Cisco, is the leading provider of Trusted Access security and multi-factor authentication delivered through the cloud.</p>
<p>
Duo’s mission is to make security simple for everyone. We were born from a hacker ethos and a desire to make the Internet a secure place. We believe in empowering people to follow their passions inside and outside of the office and enable every employee to bring their whole self to work.</p>
<p>
Our team is our secret weapon. We run the spectrum from artists to analysts, low-key to high energy, and bring together a diversity of skill sets, experiences, and perspectives to solve what we consider to be the world's most pressing geopolitical challenge — transforming the security industry as we know it. Together we build solutions that are easy, effective, trustworthy, and enduring. And that’s why we are the most loved and trusted company in security.</p>
<p><b>
What you’ll do…</b></p>
<p>
As an Account Executive, Mid Market, you will have a major impact on our business by evangelizing and selling Duo Security’s Trusted Access Platform to Mid Market prospects (companies with 501-5,000 employees). You will work alongside other hunters to generate revenue from sales leads, outbound prospecting calls, and building relationships with existing and potential customers. Additionally, your ability to manage, and close multiple deals at any given time will be an important factor to success in this role.</p>
<p><b>
Skills you have…</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
University or college degree</li>
<li>
2-5 years of SaaS sales experience carrying a quota AND hitting (or exceeding!) your number within the Mid Market.</li>
<li>
Delivering compelling sales presentations and product/service demonstrations</li>
<li>
An athletic approach to your work and can artfully balance pre-sales activities with a high volume of deals that are in various stages of the sales lifecycle</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
3 Reasons why you should apply…</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
You are humble and collaborative</li>
<li>
You are looking to be a part of a sales team that believes in team selling</li>
<li>
You want to sell a best in class solution. You’re driven to work at one of the most loved security organizations in the world.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
This job may not be for you if...</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
You have never carried an aggressive, but achievable sales quota, and consistently hit/exceeded your number</li>
<li>
You get frazzled by juggling multiple deals at once</li>
<li>
You get nervous about, or break into a cold sweat thinking of making cold calls</li>
<li>
You have a “lone wolf” kind of personality</li>
</ul>
