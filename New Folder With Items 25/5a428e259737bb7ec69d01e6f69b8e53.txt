<h2>Product Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>JOB SUMMARY:</b> This position is responsible for a select group of veterinary point-of-care testing and diagnostic products. The key objective of this position is to achieve the budgeted sales of brands within the designated portfolio. Improving product marketability and profitability by researching, identifying and capitalizing on market opportunities are also essential skill elements for this position. This person is considered the product expert and will ensure field and internal team training to the highest level and standards of expertise, precision and accuracy of information.</p>
<p>Responsibilities include providing input to the design and execution of all activities related to the annual marketing plans of the Product Manager’s product; identifying marketing opportunities, customer requirements; customer use cases, competitor share, competitor advantages and weaknesses; forecasting business; and establishing targeted company share. Managing key product development projects and identifying new product opportunities for the future are also core requirements for this position.</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:
</b></p>
<p><b>Product Management:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Acts as the company expert on and champion for the assigned and complimentary product line(s).</li>
<li>Achieve financial objectives by preparing and managing annual promotional budget for assigned product line(s) that improve contribution margin growth of the Product Manager’s product(s).</li>
<li>Establishes product marketing plans by developing marketing and pricing strategies, objectives, and action plans. Executes all tactics related to product launches and ongoing product support.</li>
<li>Manages, monitors, and tracks ASP (average selling price), and margins.</li>
<li>Consistently monitors and tracks sales performance and reacts appropriately to trends to drive sales performance.</li>
<li>Manages product inventory levels and provides sales forecasts for proper ordering from the operations teams of products and supplies, taking into account lead times, shelf life, temperature freight requirements, demand, upcoming launches, anticipated volumes, and other data.</li>
<li>Manages product launch and support materials including product brochures, sales sheets, sales reference materials, customer presentations, demo and training videos, webinar materials and sales training materials.</li>
<li>Grows market share by planning, developing, implementing, and evaluating all product advertising and promotional programs.</li>
<li>Creates and develops strong relationships with key opinion leaders and other Heska customers.</li>
<li>Evaluates and communicates market opportunities to provide strategic direction for the company.</li>
<li>Designs and analyzes business models and coordinates new product development with research and development associates.</li>
<li>Conducts field observations via tradeshow exposures and traveling with assigned field personnel in order to collect “Voice of the Customer.”</li>
<li>Prepares reports by collecting, analyzing and presenting information.</li>
<li>Maintains professional and technical knowledge by working with internal R&amp;D, reviewing professional publications, and establishing networks of technical information from manufacturing partners, which are monitored and updated quarterly, with information disseminated to internal stakeholders.</li>
<li>Develops, communicates, champions, and evaluates effective marketing strategies and programs to sales representatives and sales managers with a key job goal of training, in-house and on monthly field ride-along(s) scheduled with Regional Managers to work with target field representatives and key accounts.</li>
<li>Works closely with the Training Department to present programs for new hires and to improve product technical knowledge and professional product positioning skills of the Heska Sales force.</li>
<li>Spend one week per month in the field with field sales representatives and key accounts as the expert in the product(s) managed.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Other duties as assigned: </b>Conducts other projects and duties as assigned.</p>
