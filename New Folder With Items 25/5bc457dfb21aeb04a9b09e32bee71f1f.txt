<h2>Senior Manager, Strategic Sourcing–Directs</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Beachbody, the leader of in-home fitness programs and nutritional products, is on the lookout for top-notch talent. We’re an equal-opportunity employer who offers competitive compensation, incredible benefits and perks, not to mention a laid-back office filled with smart, friendly people and positive energy. We also provide free Shakeology, onsite gyms, flexible work hours, and cool product discounts. Are you ready to help us achieve our goal of helping people live healthy fulfilling lives?
</p>
<p></p>
<p>At Beachbody, our mission is to help people achieve their goals and enjoy a healthy, fulfilling life.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The <b>Senior Manager, Strategic Sourcing – Directs </b>is responsible for holistic and strategic management of Beachbody’s annual spend with several hundred direct materials suppliers in several and varied categories, including Nutritional ingredients, Fitness products, Packaging, and Printed collateral. The Senior Manager, Strategic Sourcing – Directs is responsible for managing his/her owned spend categories, with the intent to simultaneously build a robust and flexible global supply chain for owned commodities, as well as to structurally and permanently reduce cost where possible. This senior role is expected to independently manage owned spend, senior executives at Beachbody’s strategic suppliers, and senior stakeholders across the company. In doing so, this individual will work closely with cross-functional groups including Research &amp; Development Product Development and Quality Assurance, Global Supply Chain, and Beachbody’s business leaders.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Drive best in class procurement strategies and programs across Beachbody, globally and enterprise–wide.Manage multiple, simultaneous cost reduction and strategic sourcing initiatives, and cross-organization procurement efforts
</li>
<li>Lead senior stakeholders and suppliers in the sourcing, negotiating and contracting of multi-million dollars in annual Direct Spend. Lead cross-functional, cross-division teams to identify, qualify, negotiate and implement suppliers for various programs and projects
</li>
<li>Work in a highly collaborative manner, in a matrixed environment, to represent Global Procurement with senior stakeholders and senior executives from Beachbody’s strategic suppliers
</li>
<li>Responsible for developing and driving spend category strategies, optimizing spend aggregation leverage globally
</li>
<li>Responsible for driving supplier innovation and other strategic supplier development activities with Beachbody's suppliers
</li>
<li>Harness vendor expertise, conduct due diligence on product trends that provide solutions to meet the company's changing business requirements
</li>
<li>Provide rigorous analysis of spend data and industry dynamics to prioritize and launch cost reduction initiatives
</li>
<li>Responsible for supplier contracting and negotiations; supplier qualification; and establishing quality processes/resolving quality issues.Manage the contracting process for procurement transactions ensuring appropriate contractual language to reflect the business interest and policies of Beachbody
</li>
<li>Develop procurement strategies, policies and tactics to achieve objectives. Primary objective is to reduce the cost of supply and meet demand requirements, business and quality specifications, and revenue goals. Adds value by managing performance aspects of major contracts and subcontracts
</li>
<li>Monitor &amp; improve existing prices and service levels. Identify and drive process improvements, root cause analysis and corrective action implementation
</li>
<li>Interface with customers and members of legal, financial/revenue recognition, business unit, sales and other stakeholder teams to bring deals to closure
</li>
<li>Analyze business, financial, and legal risks to develop and communicate creative solutions and mitigation strategies for key negotiation points. Implement rigorous supplier scorecards to measure suppliers
</li>
<li>Respond to supplier service and/or quality issues to mitigate negative impact on the company
</li>
<li>Participate in the approval process to assist business owners in making fully informed buying and cost reduction decisions
</li>
<li>Comply with audit, financial, and system requirements and assists in resolving post-execution nonstandard contract modification requests and disputes
</li>
</ul>
