<h2>Sr. Software Engineer - SAP Basis Administrator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div><div>
<div>
<div>The SAP BASIS administrator will be a part of the Moody’s Shared Services team and will have several key responsibilities:<ul>
<li>
Implement SAP application installations, upgrades, enhancements, patches as needed and coordinate with technical, functional teams and business users.</li>
<li>
Participate in design sessions to understand business needs and functional / non-functional requirements and apply appropriate patterns and practices.</li>
<li>
Facilitate infrastructure architecture working sessions with network and operations teams.</li>
<li>
Implement appropriate level of monitoring and automation for proactive support of the SAP S/4 environment</li>
<li>
Proactively analyze SAP environment to detect deficiencies, recommend and implement solutions for improvement.</li>
<li>
Tune SAP environments to ensure integrity, stability, and optimal performance</li>
<li>
Demonstrates the ability to translate business needs into technical architecture requirements and understands the long-term and short-term perspectives of an IT investment situation</li>
<li>
Support and promote integration relationships between IT and business functions</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>
<div>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<div>Candidates are expected to demonstrate experience in each of the above responsibilities and focus areas, with the following specific qualifications:<br>
<ul>
<li>
BS in Computer Science, Mathematics, Business, Statistics or related technical field required.</li>
<li>
5+ years related work experience in infrastructure architecture in specific product stack that includes S/4 HANA, SAP SuccessFactors, S/4 BW, SAP Solution Manager, SAP BODS, SAP Fiori.</li>
<li>
Extensive experience in ABAP development, with both custom development and customizations to existing processes, including use of IDOCs, RFC, BAPI, BADI, ALV, LSMW, ABAP Workbench, Reports, SAP Scripts</li>
<li>
Deep knowledge of SAP Architecture with Hands-on experience with system installations and administration in both on-perm and cloud.</li>
<li>
Strong technical skills designing, developing, implementing applications through agile and waterfall SDLCs.</li>
<li>
Experience building and supporting mission-critical applications, running in a highly distributed manner</li>
<li>
Knowledge of business process technology implementation with regards to SaaS products for Sales, Customer Management, Product Management, Contract Management, Procurement, Billing, Finance, HR, and Financial Planning &amp; Forecasting.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of leading Business Intelligence/Reporting tools or reporting database design is preferred (Tableau, Microsoft BI, SAP Analytics Cloud)</li>
<li>
Experience with application security technologies and approaches</li>
<li>
Strong experience in developing services using SOAP, REST and messaging platforms</li>
<li>
Knowledge of SOA application development using ESB such as MuleSoft</li>
<li>
RDBMS experience developing and designing databases and SQL a plus</li>
<li>
Experience developing reactive distributed applications, real time data pipelines &amp; streaming applications, large-scale data processing applications, and Microservices</li>
</ul>
</div>
