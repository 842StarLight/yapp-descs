<h2>Accountant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Are you interested in healthy nutrition and learning more about natural healing using nutritional supplements? New Beginnings Nutritionals is a respected international company which serves customers (including physicians) world-wide through distributing high quality, cutting edge nutritional supplements that offer natural remedies for anxiety, depression, ADHD, autism, immune system dysfunction, memory loss and much more. Be a part of a team that can make a difference to help improve the health and lives of children, adults and families through nutritional intervention.</p>
<p>As an Accountant you will:</p>
<p>Manage all aspects of A/R, A/P</p>
<ul>
<li>Account Payable will encompass the processing of all accounts payable transactions including bills, credit card charges and invoicing client bills, ensuring accuracy of each bill and proper classification</li>
<li>Accounts Receivable will encompass the accurate posting and classification of the daily receivable transactions, preparing deposit slips when needed, and maintaining accurate and comprehensive records of each day’s entry.</li>
<li>Handle all payroll information using Inspire services for payroll entry</li>
<li>Figure and pay monthly sales commissions</li>
<li>General ledger maintenance</li>
<li>Reconcile accounts monthly</li>
<li>Assist with monthly financial reports for management</li>
<li>Advise the Director in all aspects of financial analysis, business process improvements, and budget analysis to help the company become more profitable</li>
</ul>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS: </b>To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. Two or more years experience as an accountant, and at least an associate’s level degree in accounting preferred. The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill, and/or ability required.</p>
<p><b>EDUCATION and/or EXPERIENCE: </b>Bachelor’s degree in accounting or a combination of education and relevant work experience.</p>
<p><b>LANGUAGE SKILLS: </b>Ability to read and write in English. Professional verbal and written communication skills and phone etiquette.</p>
<p><b>MATHEMATICAL SKILLS: </b>Excellent Math aptitude required.</p>
<p><b>OTHER SKILLS AND ABILITIES: </b><b>Must have experience in Great Plains Dynamics </b></p>
<p><b>Accounting software.</b>Must possess a working knowledge of Microsoft Office software, Outlook and Internet. Knowledge of office equipment, printer, copier, fax machine, telephone and calculator. Able to work as a team member with other staff members. Detail oriented, highly motivated, well-organized and problem solving skills are prime skills necessary for successful achievement in this position.</p>
<p><b>REASONING ABILITY</b>: Ability to apply common sense understanding in order to carry out resolution of problems involving customer situations.</p>
