<h2>Graphic Artist/Sign Production Leader</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Graphic Artist/Production Associate to assist in running our production department. This position will work closely with the Operations Manager in running all facets of the department. Graphic design knowledge is a must, and general sign shop experience is preferred.</p>
<p>Primary duties will include designing signs and sign layouts, operating and maintaining our large format printer, vinyl plotter and three axis router. When necessary, you will be needed to assist in any aspect of production. This may include anything from weeding vinyl graphics, laminating digital prints, applying vehicle graphics or painting a routed HDU sign.</p>
<p>Duties and Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Design and create professional signs, graphics, and production ready artwork.</li>
<li>Assist sales team and customers with creative input as well as being able to communicate production methods and limitations.</li>
<li>Assist and direct production employees when needed</li>
<li>Operate and maintain large format printer.</li>
<li>Operate and maintain vinyl plotter.</li>
<li>Operate and maintain three axis router.</li>
<li>Maintain print and vinyl media inventory</li>
<li>Complete design work, print and vinyl materials/components and completed sign projects in a timely manner based on production schedule.</li>
<li>Maintain clean and organized work space, as well as professional personal appearance.</li>
<li>Learn and adhere to safety standards</li>
<li>Help maintain a clean and organized production department.</li>
<li>Arrive to work and time and maintain regular attendance record.</li>
<li>Bring a positive attitude with you to work every day.</li>
</ul>
