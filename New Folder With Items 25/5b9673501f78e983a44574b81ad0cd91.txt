<div>"<p></p>
<p><b><br>
Tax Training School Field Instructor</b></p>
<p>
Seeking seasonal instructors to prepare, facilitate, and train for a variety of courses and students at H&amp;R Block. Qualified candidates will exhibit strong tax preparation, communication, and presentation skills.</p>
<p>
H&amp;R Block is the industry’s largest consumer tax services provider¹ and we have been focused on client service for over 60 years.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Tax Training School Field Instructor duties include:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Effectively teaching various courses and training classes while maintaining respect for students with different levels of experience and comprehension.</li>
<li>
Utilizing course materials, including Instructor Guides, Participant Guides, handouts and other teaching aids to deliver course content as designed.</li>
<li>
Presenting and facilitating training classes, using a variety of techniques such as exercises, case studies, skills practice along with question and answer sessions.</li>
<li>
Recording attendance and scores in a timely manner, as directed by District or Franchise General Manager</li>
</ul>
"</div>
</div>
<div>
<p><b>Minimum Qualifications</b></p>
<div>"<p><b>
Required Skills &amp; Experience:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Sufficient experience as a tax professional or education to master content of all courses being taught. Mastery is demonstrated by answering student questions in a comfortable/confident manner and per all tax law or factual information.</li>
<li>
Ability to read, write, and clearly communicate in English and/or other languages as required by needs of the district.</li>
<li>
Effective presentation and facilitation skills as demonstrated in their words, voice, pace, delivery and non-verbal communication. Instructor should have the ability to teach in an energetic and enthusiastic manner.</li>
<li>
Excellent interpersonal skills demonstrated through conversation with, and preparation of tax returns of, H&amp;R Block customers.</li>
<li>
Ability to explain complex laws and regulations in an easy to understand manner.</li>
<li>
Above average reading and math skills to understand information and explain course information to students.</li>
<li>
Travel to training locations in and out of district as scheduled/assigned by District General Manager.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Preferred Skills &amp; Experience:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Two to three years of combined experience as an H&amp;R Block Tax Professional or Client Service Leader (one year of experience within the most recent tax season).</li>
<li>
Understanding and demonstrated proficiency of BlockWorks and other applicable software.</li>
<li>
Additional state and local requirements may apply.</li>
</ul>
<p><i>
H&amp;R Block is an equal opportunity employer.</i></p>
<p>
1<i> Based on annual revenues for sales of tax preparation products and services.</i></p>
"</div>
