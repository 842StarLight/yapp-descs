<h2>Strategic Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b><i>Hey future #Allstar,</i></b></p>
<p><b><i>We're Allbound, a fast-growing, innovative, tech startup in Central Phoenix. At Allbound, our goal is to make businesses and partnerships run smoother and more efficiently through technology. The Allbound platform empowers companies to train, onboard, collaborate, and track deals and opportunities with partners. Simply put, we make it easy to work with partners, helping everyone stay on the same page.</i></b></p>
<p><b><i>Now that you know what we do, you're probably wondering, what's our culture like? We're passionate about providing the best solutions for our customers, and that could not be accomplished without our team of #Allstars. We celebrate wins and loses together, and take every opportunity to embrace challenges with optimism. Overall, we're here to make a difference the way people do business, all over the world.</i></b></p>
<p><b><i>Think you've got what it take to be an #Allstar too?</i></b></p>
<p>Allbound is seeking an #AllStar Strategic Sales Executive to evangelize the Allbound value proposition and strategically sell both Allbound's software and its channel sales and marketing methodology. You will be a key member of a small team building focused on reaching goals and growing our startup rapidly.</p>
<p>To be a good fit for the position, we have to make sure we align on a few key things. The ideal candidate for this role is eager to work for a Phoenix-based SaaS startup that is poised for continued growth. Our Strategic Account Executive #AllStar understands the complexities of an enterprise's sales ecosystem, particularly the channel sales environment that's woven with multi-tier partnership levels, value added resellers, master distributors, agents, franchises and the like.</p>
<p>Does this sound like you? If so, read on.</p>
<p><b>Primary Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Building relationships with our buyer personas at enterprise and mid-sized companies.</li>
<li>Mastering the demonstration of Allbound's software solutions and services and clearly identifying and communicating how our features and benefits align to prospect needs.</li>
<li>Guiding and providing expert advice to companies looking to accelerate partner sales.</li>
<li>Closing new business consistently at or above quota level.</li>
<li>Flexing your muscles as an expert in channel sales and marketing, as well as inbound marketing strategies.</li>
<li>Managing the complete sales cycle, from initial discovery to live software demonstration and the creation and delivery of a custom statement of work and proposal, as well as the critical handoff to Allbound's Customer Success department.</li>
<li>Working collaboratively with marketing and product development departments to execute sales strategies as Allbound introduces enhancements to existing solutions and/or releases new products.</li>
<li>Acting as a conduit between the market and Allbound to identify trends in (or not within) the market that Allbound can capitalize on.</li>
<li>Plus, you'll bring your thinking, strategies, and ideas to advance our company's values, unique culture, and vision for the future.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Required Experience:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Experience within a sales team that produces and exceeds their goals consistently</li>
<li>Experience in a startup environment, if possible</li>
<li>2-7+ years of selling experience operating within a web technologies or innovative online product environment</li>
<li>Experience selling to or working with director-level sales, business development and marketing professionals.</li>
<li>The ability to navigate through our prospects' highly unique and complex sales ecosystems and identify a strategic solution that serves their technological, philosophical and operational needs.</li>
<li>The ability to advance Allbound's vision and unique Partner Sales Acceleration methodology</li>
<li>A powerful work ethic and the ability to thrive in a lighting fast-paced, demanding environment</li>
<li>Tons of energy, passion, humor, compassion, and enthusiasm</li>
<li>Superior communications skills</li>
<li>Exceptional closing skills</li>
<li>Bachelor's Degree</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Intangibles</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Detail-oriented</li>
<li>Life-long learner</li>
<li>Business-minded thinker</li>
<li>Process-driven</li>
<li>Team player who loves to share knowledge and credit</li>
<li>Loves a startup environment</li>
</ul>
