<h2>Business Development Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">TapClicks, Inc. is a leading marketing technology company for agencies, media companies, brands, and enterprises. Its integrated Marketing Operations Platform includes sales enablement, workflow and order management, analytics, and automated reporting - all within a single intuitive user interface available on demand in the cloud. TapClicks has delivered over 1,000,000 dashboards to over 5,000 brands and over 500 media companies and agencies worldwide. The TapClicks platform leverages over 150 native API integrations with leading marketing and advertising platforms and rounds out its solution with ImportWizard, which enables the platform to use data from virtually any source. TapClicks was founded in 2009, is headquartered in Silicon Valley, has development and sales offices in Boston and Canada and sales offices in Europe and Latin America.
<br>We are seeking an entry level Business Development Representative (BDR) with experience in the MarTech and SaaS industries to join our growing North American team. This is a highly visible, collaborative position with quota-carrying responsibility for generating net-new sales pipeline each quarter. This position based in our Wakefield, MA office. A successful BDR is a motivated self-starter who is hungry for a challenge and who has experience in the high tech space. Your high-energy and professional persistence will drive your targeted, outbound prospecting activities into a set of assigned accounts. The BDR's main focus is identifying, developing and creating new business opportunities for field salespeople in specific territories of North America.
