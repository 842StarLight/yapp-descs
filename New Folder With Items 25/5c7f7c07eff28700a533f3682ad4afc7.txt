<h2>Recruitment Administrator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Mpower Direct is currently looking for an experienced Recruitment Administrator in the Pittsburgh, PA area. This position will play an integral role within our operations team by recruiting talented individuals to join the firm. The Recruitment Administrator will provide administrative support, greet and direct visitors and answer and respond to calls and emails. Qualified candidates will have impeccable verbal and written communication skills, a strong ability to multi-task and a friendly demeanor.</p>
<p><b>About Mpower: </b></p>
<p>Mpower Direct is a retail energy provider selling 100% renewable energy to residential customers in states that have energy deregulation. Currently, Mpower conducts business in New York, New Jersey, Pennsylvania, Maryland, Ohio, and Connecticut, and is quickly expanding into new territories.</p>
<p><b>Recruitment Administrator responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Greet visitors and direct them to the appropriate offices and/or contacts</li>
<li>Conduct clerical duties, including filing, answering phone calls, responding to emails and preparing documents</li>
<li>Provide administrative support to the sales team</li>
<li>Review and analyze resumes using a recruitment software program</li>
<li>Scour job search websites for potential recruits and contact those candidates personally</li>
<li>Contact candidates to determine qualification and abilities</li>
<li>Interview potential sales staff members using industry-standard techniques</li>
<li>Introduce new-hires to the Company and walk them through the hiring and training process</li>
<li>Complete all vetting paperwork with candidates who meet the expectations of the job</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Recruitment Administrator skills</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma required (Associate degree preferred)</li>
<li>Experience working in an office setting</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Strong knowledge of Office Suite, primarily Microsoft Excel</li>
<li>Ability to multitask and prioritize projects</li>
<li>Able to complete complex administrative tasks with minimal supervision</li>
<li>Ability to thrive in a dynamic, deadline-driven environment</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal and communication skills</li>
<li>Demonstrable experience in sales recruiting</li>
<li>Familiarity with online job hunting websites</li>
</ul>
