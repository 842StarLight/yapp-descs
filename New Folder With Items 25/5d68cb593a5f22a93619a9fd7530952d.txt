<h2>Solutions Analyst</h2>

<div>

From R&amp;D to portfolio strategy, customer insights, marketing and sales strategy, operations and technology, we leverage our deep industry expertise and leading-edge analytics to create solutions that work in the real world. <p><b>
ZS's Integrated Solutions Group</b> supports ZS clients and local client teams with their ongoing business analytics and operations needs. The group typically works from a local client location.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
SOLUTION ANALYST</b></p>
<p>
Solutions Analysts work directly with our clients at their facilities to assist them with their business analytics and operations questions, as well as reporting needs. Solutions Analysts leverage their quantitative skills to gather necessary client data sets, then formulate and conduct analyses with that data to provide our clients with key insights on business analytics and business operations support. Solutions Analysts also create specific communications to convey analysis results to our clients.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<div>
<b>
Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Leverage quantitative skills to derive answers to clients' ongoing business analytics and reporting questions;</li>
<li>Work collaboratively with clients to ascertain the client’s specific business information needs;</li>
<li>Execute a specific plan for analyzing the data necessary to secure solutions to clients’ particular business information need(s);</li>
<li>Discuss and determine with clients which specific data may be best utilized in order to perform the necessary analysis;</li>
<li>Evaluate, assess and analyze the data necessary to provide solutions to the client’s particular business information needs;</li>
<li>Communicate the data results to the client, with an emphasis on answering the business question as opposed to the analytical and reporting processes used;</li>
<li>Work closely and in collaboration with teams in remote locations to support client’s analytics needs;</li>
<li>Gain immediate familiarity with client’s internal processes;</li>
<li>Develop expertise within a client analytics area.</li>
</ul>
</div>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degrees in Engineering, Information Technology/Systems, Statistics, Economics, Mathematics, Operations Research or a related field with a record of academic success;</li>
<li>Experience in a previous business analytics (financial, marketing, sales, pharmaceutical, industry, etc.) role strongly preferred</li>
<li>Good work ethic, maturity and personal initiative;</li>
<li>Attention to detail, with a methodical approach to work</li>
<li>Self-discipline for planning and organizing tasks;</li>
<li>Accuracy in executing tasks efficiently;</li>
<li>Strong communication skills that enable clear explanation and resolution of client's information needs;</li>
<li>Strong client service orientation.</li>
</ul>
