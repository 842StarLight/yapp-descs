<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Amify is a four-time Inc. Magazine award-winning Amazon strategy partner, focused on maximizing the potential of brands on Amazon. Amify uses industry expertise and a data-driven approach to monitor and prevent unauthorized sellers, optimize and enhance product listings, protect brands from unauthorized sellers and price violations, and drive sales through advertising. With tact, innovation, and a dedication to brand authenticity, Amify has emerged as one of largest brand partners on Amazon.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
We are currently hiring for a <b>Brand Protection Analyst </b>to support our Amazon Brand Services Team.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Serve as primary brand protection point of contact for internal and external stakeholders</li>
<li>
Lead brand protection team to align with client’s strategies, targets, and priorities</li>
<li>
Responsible for the data integrity of all brand protection reports, trackers, scorecards, KPI’s, and database</li>
<li>
Manage relationships with service providers and agencies</li>
<li>
Generate and analyze MAP Enforcement/monitoring reports and prioritize communication with MAP violators and/or unauthorized sellers</li>
<li>
Ensure that best practices and processes are updated and in place for all tasks related to brand protection</li>
<li>
Track researching and inbound/outbound communication tasks with unauthorized sellers</li>
<li>
Assist in the crafting of weekly reports for internal and external stakeholders</li>
<li>
Ability to understand client catalogs, best-selling items, authorized sellers, biggest offenders, and create an appropriate brand protection strategy</li>
</ul>
