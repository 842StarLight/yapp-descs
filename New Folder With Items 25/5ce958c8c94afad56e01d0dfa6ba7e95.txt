<h2>Membership Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div><div>
<p>Description:
</p>
<p>The Boys &amp; Girls Clubs of Metro South was formed in 2019 through the merger of the Boys &amp; Girls Club of Brockton<br>
and Taunton in order to maximize our impact in each community and bridge the service gaps throughout the region.
</p>
<p>Under the direction of the Unit Executive Director, the Membership Director is responsible for driving, leading and<br>
managing a seamless and meaningful Club experience, from an individual’s first point of contact with the Club to the<br>
daily engagement of current members. The Membership Director must exude professionalism and confidence in<br>
knowledge of Club programming, implementing the Club’s membership strategies of welcome, connect, support and<br>
invite during all member and guest interactions.
</p>
<p>This position requires a deep knowledge of sales, customer service, process creation and data analysis while being<br>
able to continually develop and motivate staff. This position will focus on the member and parent experience by<br>
managing strategies in marketing/communication and facility cleanliness/maintenance by which we attract and retain<br>
members, positioning the Club for continued growth.
</p>
<p>The Membership Director should have strong knowledge of fiscal management skills, business operations, sales and<br>
data analytics. This person must be able to increase member units, as well as, increase retention, engagement, and<br>
appreciation of current members.
</p>
<p>Qualifications:
 </p>
<p>Four-year degree from an accredited college or university, or equivalent experience
 <br>
Must be at least 21 years old.<br>
 4 years supervision, sales, customer service experience.<br>
 Strong interpersonal, organizational and oral and written communication skills<br>
 Previous experience working with diverse populations.<br>
 Demonstrated ability in personnel supervision, facilities management, and the recruitment and retention of
</p>
<p>key personnel
 <br>
Strong communication skills, both oral and written
 <br>
Ability to recruit, train, supervise and motivate staff
 <br>
Demonstrated ability in working with young people, parents and community leaders.<br>
</p>
<p><br>
Essential Functions:<br>
 </p>
<p>Implements strategies that support recruitment of new members and retention of existing members. Ensures<br>
a strong utilization of Club software and technology to engage and retain members.
 </p>
<p>Creates a member-focused culture and models relationship-building skills in all interactions. Fosters a<br>
climate of innovation and resolves problems to ensure member satisfaction.
 </p>
<p>Develops, implements, and manages operating plan(s) to promote membership growth and retention.
 </p>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<p>Develop, implement, and sustain a branch-specific member experience training program, executing<br>
strategies to ensure that members and/or program participants connect with one another and connect with<br>
the Club.
 </p>
<p>Recruits, hires, trains, develops, schedules and directs personnel and volunteers as needed. Reviews and<br>
evaluates staff performance. Develops strategies to motivate staff and achieve goals.
 </p>
<p>Delivers a comprehensive and dynamic staff development and training program.<br>
 Observes all staff and provides regular feedback to staff, spending 30% of time is spent on coaching and
</p>
<p>mentoring staff focused on membership and retention strategies.
 <br>
Collaborates with Executive Director and Advancement department in internal/external marketing strategies
</p>
<p>and production of material to promote internal/external member experience and retention.<br>
 Creates and monitors budgets and anticipates changes to revenue and co-creates strategies to meet
</p>
<p>acquisition/financial goals with team and supervisor.<br>
 Creates, monitors and adjusts departments’ goals and objectives in service to vision, mission and financial
</p>
<p>climate.<br>
 Helps teams act as liaisons with the youth development program staff, member services staff and
</p>
<p>leadership staff to respond to membership inquiries and improve processes.<br>
 Evaluates and recommends new policies and procedures as necessary.<br>
 Establishes strong communication systems with all Club staff and facilities.<br>
 Develops and maintains effective working relationships within the community. Ability to establish and
</p>
<p>maintain collaborations with community organizations.<br>
 Analyzes membership statistics and financial results. Assess and recommend management decisions that
</p>
<p>impact membership and program growth.<br>
 Advocates for members and effects change to influence and improve the member experience.<br>
 Participates in the planning of the annual budget; manages and implements the approved budget for
</p>
<p>membership department<br>
 Ensures proper implementation of Membership Services procedures. Reviews and updates procedures and
</p>
<p>communicates changes to staff. Coordinates with the administrative services team, as necessary, on<br>
financial transactions.
 </p>
<p>Supports coordination of special events and activities.<br>
 Provides data and reports as required for assigned programs.<br>
</p>
