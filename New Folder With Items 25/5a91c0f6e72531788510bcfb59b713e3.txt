<b>Job Summary:</b><br>
This position will be responsible for maximizing client relationships and services for specific accounts by serving as the primary point of contact for the client as the internal liaison between Media Sales Consultants Media Planners, Research Director and other resource personnel. This position will report to the Local Sales Manager and is the key owner of the post-sale client experience and the key overseer of campaign implementation and performance.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities include but are not limited to:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Oversees the daily management of assigned accounts that meet specific criteria</li>
<li>Executes regular touch points with clients at campaign inception and throughout the lifetime of the client relationship.</li>
<li>Identifies up-sell opportunities within an account’s marketing strategy and passes them along to the Media Sales Consultant for presentation to the client.</li>
<li>Retains client base through exemplary service and positive ROI for the client</li>
<li>Targets accounts not meeting account management criteria and recommends strategies for the account to meet that criteria, thus growing the Account Managers account base</li>
<li>Through the identification of new opportunities, grows average revenue per account (ARPA)</li>
<li>Evaluates new and existing products to determine optimal fit for each account and makes recommended marketing changes as necessary</li>
<li>Develops and maintains working relationships with key contacts and team members in all functional area execution of the client’s advertising campaign</li>
<li>Receives, compiles and presents campaign performance reports to client; makes recommendations as necessary</li>
<li>Stays abreast of client’s marketing needs: strategy, audience, objective and ROI expectations.</li>
</ul>
<b>Key Traits:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Proven self-starter with a natural curiosity toward business solutions and customer positioning</li>
<li>Mix of analytical ability to see trends in data and ability to influence customer behavior using results</li>
<li>Passionate about improving and innovating the customer experience</li>
<li>Desire for future growth and advancement in media sales and customer solutions</li>
<li>Minimum 2+ years of customer engagement experience in the area of marketing/advertising</li>
<li>Minimum 2+ years of project and customer management experience. Matrix or Sales Force experience, a plus</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to communicate clearly with decision makers with various talk tracks depending on expertise level</li>
</ul>
