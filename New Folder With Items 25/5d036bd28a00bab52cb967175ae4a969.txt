<h2>Video and Motion Graphics Designer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Compensation:</b> Salary depending on skill level and experience</p>
<p><b>
Location:</b> Expert TA’s headquarters are in Tulsa, Oklahoma.
</p>
<p>Expert TA is a young Educational Technology company based in downtown Tulsa, Ok. Our clients are major colleges, universities, and high schools. These include Ivy League schools, as well as dozens of institutions in the U.S. News and World Reports list of top Colleges and Universities. On a local level, Expert TA is utilized by every major university in the state of Oklahoma.
</p>
<p>If you like working on a team, appreciate a good challenge, are willing to learn new things, and want to do something that makes a difference in a positive way — Expert TA may be the right place for you.</p>
<p><b>
Job Purpose And Responsibility:</b></p>
<p>
Expert TA is seeking a full-time, permanent employee to add to our team. This individual would help provide support for our existing customers, support marketing and sales, create new educational content for our end users, and advise on the technical and aesthetic aspects of the Expert TA application.</p>
<p>
The initial responsibilities for this position would be to join our video production team and help with educational content development. Expert TA is currently producing a series of Introductory Physics videos that are based on existing lectures. The current videos consist mainly of screen-captures from a tablet where the instructor is writing and drawing by hand. Based on this existing content, Expert TA is creating highly produced versions of these videos, with formatted math, typewritten text, and vector graphics and animations.</p>
<p><b>
Project Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Create polished PowerPoint presentations based on existing videos</li>
<li>Create enhanced graphics inside PowerPoint</li>
<li>Web search for appropriate images to include in videos</li>
<li>Image Editing
</li>
<li>Editing of Audio and Video</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Skills and Qualifications:</b></p>
<p><b>
Required</b></p>
<p>
Advanced/Expert PowerPoint skills including complex animations, Technically proficient with ability to learn new software quickly, Organization and time management, Web research, Ability to meet deadlines, Capable of independent work with limited direct supervision, Professionalism, Problem Solving, Ability to work on a team (“People Person”).</p>
<p><b>
Desired Skills</b></p>
<p>
Experience with audio/video editing (examples: Adobe Audition or Camtasia Studio), Graphical design skills including: Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator or In-Design, Mathematics background, Physics background, Project management and organization</p>
