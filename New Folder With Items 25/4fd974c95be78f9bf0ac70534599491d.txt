<h2>Retail Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>J<b>ob Summary:</b> This position assists in the daily operations of the Museum Store, Bookstore and/or Web-store to ensure outstanding customer service, assist in maintaining a neat and orderly visual presentation in the stores and enforcing museum guidelines with part-time staff.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Principle Qualifications:</b> Ability to work independently and to follow directions: self-motivated, ability to work under time pressure in a fast-paced environment, as well as at a slower pace as needed. Clean and neat appearance is a must along with the ability to interact with supervisors, co-workers and the public.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Position Type and Expected Hours of Work:</b></p>
<p>This is a 1,000 Hour year round part time position. Must be available 16-40 hours per week as museum needs dictate. Must be available to work a flexible schedule which will include evenings, weekends and holidays. Position will be required to work approximately 34-48 weekend days per year or one weekend day per week as staffing dictates. Summer hours will be primarily evening hours.</p>
<br>
 <p></p>
<p><b>Primary Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Assist in the daily operations of the Museum Shop, Customer Service/Web-sales Operations and/or the Bookstore and to comply with the duties assigned by the Museum Shop Manager.</li>
<li>Supervise, schedule and train store personnel in all aspects of the Retail operation.</li>
<li>Work with Museum Store management to ensure per capita sales targets and other financial goals are met.</li>
<li>Ensure retail customers receive an outstanding customer service &amp; shopping experience.</li>
<li>Assist Museum Store Manager in maintaining a neat and orderly visual presentation in the shop, while also executing merchandising initiatives.</li>
<li>Address and resolve any customer service issues that may arise.</li>
<li>Proper and professional handling of phone inquiries.</li>
<li>Adhere and enforce museum guidelines with part-time staff .</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Secondary Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Assist with web-store/mail order fulfillment</li>
<li>Assist with year end physical inventory process</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Physical Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Work involves almost constant movement including walking, bending, stretching, and standing, carrying, pulling and lifting.</li>
<li>Ability to lift up to 50lbs.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Typical Equipment Used:</b></p>
<p>Computer, 2 and 4 wheeled carts, tagging equipment, cash registers, phones, other display equipment.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Required Education and Experience:</b></p>
<p>High school diploma or GED, or equivalent years of experience.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Preferred Education and Experience:</b></p>
<p>One year of prior retail experience preferred. Computer and/or retail software experience a plus.</p>
<br>
<p></p>

</b></p>
<p>Please note this job description is not designed to cover or contain a comprehensive listing of activities, duties or responsibilities that are required of the employee for this job. Duties, responsibilities and activities may change at any time with or without notice.</p>
