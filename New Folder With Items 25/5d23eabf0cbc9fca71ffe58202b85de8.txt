<h2>Director, Business Development- Retail</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>
<div><b>About this Role</b></div>
<div>
</div>
<div>
Goals</div>
<div>
<div>
Lead the selling activities for a geographic or account based sales territory.</div>
<div>
Generate new revenue by managing several enterprise sales deals to closure on an annual basis.</div>
<div>
Develop as a thought leader and respected “expert” in the ad technology industry so that you are viewed as a consultative partner and not a transactional seller.</div>
</div>
<div><b>
</b></div>
<div><b>
Responsibilities</b></div>
<div>
<div>
An individual contributor driving significant revenue growth by using general business acumen to uncover prospect business needs and identify how Conversant solutions can help prospects achieve their business goals</div>
<div>
Coordinate internal resources for the preparation and delivery of new business presentations.</div>
<div>
Create and deliver compelling, customized sales presentations that address the core business issues and opportunities faced by the prospect.</div>
<div>
Effectively monitor day-to-day management of accounts in your assigned territory and build relationships with key marketing, merchandising, and media decision makers</div>
<div>
Stay current on industry trends and personnel moves among prospect organizations.</div>
<div>
Negotiate and close contracts with client marketing and legal stakeholders</div>
</div>
<div><b>
Skills and Competencies</b></div>
<div>
<div>
Demonstrate experience developing sales in the digital or agency business, have a strong network of prior relationships and contacts (senior level), and show leadership skills in developing the right tools to get the job done with our creative team.</div>
<div>
Senior-level hunter that enjoys prospecting, cold calling and needs based selling.</div>
<div>
Strong knowledge of Ad-Tech: DMP, DSP, Data Onboarding, Device Matching, 3rd party ad-server, attribution vendors</div>
<div>
Understanding of complex and lengthy sales cycles and how to create achievable milestones and deliver meticulous follow-up</div>
<div>
Excellent communication skills with the ability to make compelling presentations to an audience of diverse and senior business stakeholders</div>
<div>
Proven ability to negotiate and close deals</div>
<div>
Must be strategic, flexible, tenacious, results-oriented</div>
<div>
Adept at dealing with a constantly changing environment</div>
</div>
<div><b>
Requirements:</b></div>
<div>
<div>
Bachelor’s degree, MBA a plus</div>
<div>
8-10 years’ experience in a sales/hunter role</div>
<div>
5+ years of sales experience in media, marketing or advertising related fields</div>
<div>
5+ years of B2B business development experience, with a successful close rate</div>
<div>
5+ years of display marketing/interactive advertising experience</div>
</div>
