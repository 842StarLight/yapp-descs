<h2>Snowsports Administrative Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>GENERAL DESCRIPTION:
</b></p>
<p>The Snowsports Administrative Assistant reports to Snowsports School Manager, Supervisors, and Snowsports Office Manager and assists with the planning, staffing, and scheduling/reservation operations of the resort’s two snowsports schools. The position will train candidate to be knowledgeable and experienced with the many aspects of Snowsports School and Treehouse Children’s Program operations. Candidates must be computer proficient and will extensively operate in a variety of computer platforms to include Siriusware (POS software) and Microsoft Office.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Job Availability: November 2019-March 2020<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>SPECIAL JOB REQUIREMENTS/QUALIFICATIONS:
</b></p>
<p>Knowledge and experience in ski school operations a plus. Motivational skills and leadership qualities are important. Must be able to manage multiple tasks and personalities and have outstanding customer service skills. Candidates will work closely with the snowsports school instructors, guests of the resort and other supporting operating areas to include equipment rental department, ski patrol, ski office, and food &amp; beverage.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>PHYSICAL REQUIREMENTS:
</b></p>
<p>The candidate must be able to sit or stand for prolonged periods of time, and be able to lift up to 30+ pounds if necessary. Ability of intermediate skiing/riding a plus.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>SPECIFIC DUTIES/RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Must work with the Snow Sports and Treehouse Children’s Program Staff to maintain staffing levels for all lessons.</li>
<li>Responsible for cash handling and deposit procedures that allow for proper handling of all cash at the sales station.</li>
<li>Responsible for fielding and managing in-person and on-phone reservation calls and following proper protocol.</li>
<li>Ability to consult with parents or resort guests without being offensive or rude.</li>
<li>Must follow all written rules required in the Wintergreen Employee Handbook and individual departments.</li>
<li>Works with office staff to ensure all aspects of guest reservations are running smoothly.</li>
<li>Assist with daily registrations.</li>
<li>Knowledge of safety issues concerning inclement weather, lost children, injured guests, etc.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>BENEFITS AND COMPENSATION:</b></p>
<p>In addition to working in a beautiful resort setting, Wintergreen offers competitive compensation and a full array of benefits that includes and a plethora of free or discounted resort activities, services, and products for you and your immediate family members. Relocation for this position is not available.</p>
