<p><b>ACCOUNTS PAYABLE SPECIALIST</b></p>
<p>We are looking for a reliable and precise professional to help us properly maintain our payable information. The accounts Payable Specialist will be responsible for processing all vendor invoices and non-payroll payments. We are seeking an experienced candidate who can analyze information in order to assign payments to the proper cost centers, work with vendors to resolve past due invoices and reconcile monthly statements with vendors.</p>
<p><b>DAILY AND MONTHLY RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<p>Perform Invoice data entry</p>
<p>Pays vendors by monitoring discount opportunities; scheduling and preparing checks; resolving purchase order, contract, invoice, or payment discrepancies and documentation; insuring credit is received for outstanding memos</p>
<p>Charges expenses to accounts and cost centers by analyzing invoice/expense reports; recoding entries</p>
<p>Pays employees by receiving and verifying expense reports; preparing checks</p>
<p>Sales taxes by calculating requirements on paid invoices</p>
<p>Accomplishes accounting and organization mission by completing related results as needed; Review invoices for appropriate documentation prior to payment</p>
<p>Print and obtain signatures on all checks</p>
<p>Verifies vendor accounts by reconciling monthly statements and related transactions; federal ID numbers and Insurance certificates</p>
