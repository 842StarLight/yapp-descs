<p><b>Position Overview</b></p>
<p>
This Manager-level position, suitable for a skilled professional with exceptional attention to detail, represents an opportunity to work in a revenue-generating position for an innovative financial technology services company. While the company is headquartered in San Francisco, CA and serves companies in Silicon Valley and worldwide, this position will be based on our Lehi, UT office.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Teleport Background</b></p>
<p>
Teleport is a fast-growing, technology-driven provider of turnkey accounting &amp; financial operations as a service. Our leadership team has a combined 65-years of experience and has helped build $5.2 billion of enterprise value at leading technology companies. Our core solution automates and streamlines bookkeeping, monthly reporting, customer invoicing, collections, payroll, and payments. We also cover any client needs that require customized support in areas such as cash management, collections, and HR.</p>
<p>
We use a standardized mix of proprietary and cloud-based technologies to dramatically reduce manual processes related to tracking and reporting financial and related operational data. Our systems are designed to provide accurate results quickly and easily. Our platform includes robust customer communication and project management tools, and streamlines the functionality of powerful cloud-based technologies that handle accounting, billing, payments, payroll, cost tracking, and HR &amp; compliance issues.</p>
