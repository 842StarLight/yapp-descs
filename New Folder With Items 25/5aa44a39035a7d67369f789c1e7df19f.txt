<p><b>
POSITION SUMMARY</b><b>:</b></p>
<p>
This is a 3 month Temporary assignment to potential hire opportunity.</p>
<p>
The role of the Customer Service Representative is to provide a superior level of customer service to all customers. This person must be enthusiastic, patient and have excellent problem solving and follow through skills while working in a fast-paced environment. He/She will strive to improve customer relations with every interaction whether it be with the customer, sales team, or an internal team member</p>
<p><b>
 </b><b>RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Answer phone calls</li>
<li>
Responds to inquiries by email, phone and postal mail concerning order status, price, availability as well as the application of products, services, promotions, and programs within 1 business day</li>
<li>
Responsible for ensuring order entry is complete and accurate within 1 business day of receipt or as indicated by assigned deadlines</li>
<li>
Monitors orders are releasing to the Distribution Center in a timely manner</li>
<li>
Tracks and communicates order progress up to and including delivery to customers and/or the sales team using various reports</li>
<li>
Issues and enters Return Authorizations, shipping labels, and/or credits</li>
<li>
Thoroughly researches customer issues, complaints and/or comments and provides complete and timely resolution</li>
<li>
Assist customers with navigating the website or B2B platform with the intent to promote self-service</li>
<li>
Understand and document customer requirements for assigned accounts</li>
<li>
Works with Inventory Management and/or Sales to process reserve requests and maintain existing reserves as assigned by manager</li>
<li>
Process hard PO’s against reserve orders as assigned by manager</li>
<li>
Works with AR to ensure pre-payments are received and processed accordingly to allow the release of held orders</li>
<li>
Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
QUALIFICATIONS:</b></p>
<p>
To perform the job successfully, an individual should have the following credentials:</p>
<ul>
<li>
High School diploma, or equivalent</li>
<li>
1 – 3 years prior experience in Customer Service preferred</li>
<li>
Bi-lingual – English/Spanish required when working with International Accounts</li>
<li>
Experience with Microsoft Outlook, Word and Excel; 10 key by touch</li>
<li>
Working knowledge of Dynamics AX or similar ERP software preferred</li>
<li>
Strive to continuously build knowledge and skills</li>
<li>
Great attention to detail</li>
<li>
Contributes to building a positive team spirit; asks for and offers help when needed</li>
<li>
Meets productivity standards; looks for ways to improve and promote quality</li>
<li>
Accepts responsibility for own actions; treats people with respect and consideration</li>
<li>
Works with integrity and upholds organizational values</li>
<li>
Adheres to 5.11 Policies and Procedures</li>
<li>
Observes safety and security procedures; reports potentially unsafe conditions to manager</li>
</ul>
