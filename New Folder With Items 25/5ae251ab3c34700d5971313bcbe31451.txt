<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>The Director of Digital Marketing &amp; Growth Role:</b></h3>
<p>We’re looking for an experienced marketing leader to own full funnel acquisition and retention efforts across both B2B and B2C customers. You will lead a team of high-performing and multi-talented individuals with a strong emphasis on digital advertising, conversion rate optimization, lead generation, pipeline creation, nurturing, lead scoring and analytics used to refine marketing programs. The ideal candidate is a hands-on marketer and dynamic leader who loves data and a fast-paced environment, and is highly in-tune with A Cloud Guru’s brand voice and customer following. This role will report into the VP of Marketing &amp; Growth.</p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
As the Director of Marketing &amp; Growth, you’ll get to:</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>Lead the strategy and execution of digital marketing, lead generation, and growth efforts</li>
<li>Lead digital marketing across channels – including SEM, SEO, social, web, and email – to drive acquisition goals for B2B and B2C and ROAS (Return On Ad Spend)</li>
<li>Lead one or more squads of high-performing growth, engineering, data and UX teammates that are responsible for driving user acquisition and retention through web and product properties</li>
<li>Marry testing and iteration with the overall brand and business vision</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>We are looking for someone who can:</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>Inform the development of marketing content and collateral through performance data</li>
<li>Create and maintain metrics reports on marketing and sales activities, effectiveness, and business impact</li>
<li>Closely work with Sales to ensure alignment on strategy, resources, and KPIs</li>
<li>Actively measure and track engagement, lead volume, and sales funnel conversion rates; proactively test and optimize programs to improve performance</li>
<li>Hire, motivate and retain top talent across multiple functions: marketing, product, engineering, data analytics and UX design</li>
<li>Keep it fun!</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>If you have the following experience we'd love to hear from you!</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>Deep experience in B2B and B2C marketing and digital marketing channels</li>
<li>A growth and data-driven mindset, with strong expertise in testing and measurement strategies, methodologies, and tools to manage programs across multiple channels at scale</li>
<li>Proven success in launching lead/demand generation marketing programs for high-growth B2B SaaS companies</li>
<li>Deep experience working with modern marketing tools such as Hubspot, Salesforce.com, Segment, Drift, etc.</li>
<li>Strong understanding of the Marketing and Sales funnel and processes</li>
<li>Ability to influence and work cross-functionally across multiple internal teams</li>
</ul>
