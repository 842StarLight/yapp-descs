<h2>Indirect Sales Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Altice USA is one of the largest broadband communications and video services providers in the United States. We are a subsidiary of Altice NV, a convergent global leader in telecom, content, media, entertainment and advertising founded in 2001 by entrepreneur Patrick Drahi. We are driven by a philosophy to always challenge ourselves. We question everything so that we can find the best way forward for our customers. And in a world where continuous innovation is the only way forward, we are redefining the vision we have for our customers, enterprises, advertisers ‐ and our people.</p>
<p></p>
<div>
<p>The Indirect Retail Sales Agent engages customers primarily in person in a partner retail environment to provide information about Optimum products and services. Achieves sales objectives by identifying needs and selling services.
</p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrates a strong passion for selling Company products and services</li>
<li>
Meets and exceeds sales targets</li>
<li>
Provides quality internal and external customer service by representing the Company in a consistently courteous and professional manner. - Demonstrates analytical proficiency and diagnostic skills in resolving customer issues on first contact, including exercising empowerment (taking ownership), and appropriate follow-through</li>
<li>
Performs all work-related tasks in a manner that follows all Optimum policies and procedures in a multi-product environment.</li>
<li>
Assists other Retail Sales Agents and Managers as required</li>
<li>
Serves as a mentor to other team members, providing instruction, support and direction</li>
<li>
Communicates with other departments to troubleshoot system problems</li>
<li>
Determines customer needs and educates customer in order to sell or upgrade services Processes customer payments accurately and balances daily transactions according to established cash management procedures</li>
<li>
Collects delinquent accounts
</li>
<li>Demonstrates integrity while accessing customer databases and/or paper reference material to locate customer records and research account history
</li>
<li>Exercises positive and cooperative behavior that promotes teamwork</li>
<li>
Provides input and suggestions related to process improvements</li>
<li>
Demonstrates proficiency in Customer Engagement, fostering an environment of continuous improvement.
</li>
<li>Delivers exceptional customer service by providing the overall value, features and benefits of all products and services offered by Optimum.</li>
<li>
Promotes and represents the Optimum brand in all customer interactions.</li>
<li>
Maintains a respectful and professional behavior, putting the customer first; creating experiences that enhance Optimum loyalty and enrich customer’s lives.
</li>
<li>Communicates customer feedback on current needs and trends to management.
</li>
<li>Demonstrates professional work ethic and adherence to all policies and procedures.</li>
</ul>
