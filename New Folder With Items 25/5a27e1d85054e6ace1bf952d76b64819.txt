<p><b>As an Global Investor Sales Executive at SimilarWeb you will:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>The Investor Sales Executive will be responsible for generating new revenue for SimilarWeb: creating, cultivating and developing accounts and increasing market share. S/he will be expected to exceed revenue goals by planning and executing successful sales within the Global Investor Vertical.</li>
<li>On a typical day you will:</li>
<ul>
<li>Meet and exceed monthly sales quota through outbound leads - strategically selling the company’s various propositions to new prospects in a consultative manner</li>
<li>Manage the entire sales cycle from prospecting, discovery, solution proposing, presentation, negotiating and closing</li>
<li>Work closely with Sales Development and Marketing teams to target appropriate accounts</li>
<li>Leverage Investor Solutions team to build targeted value propositions for investor accounts</li>
<li>Build cross functional relationships within the prospect in order to penetrate the account further by focusing on director/ C-level engagement</li>
<li>Work with channel partners to identify, support and close opportunities</li>
<li>Develop accurate forecasts and manage sales activity in CRM (Salesforce.com)</li>
</ul>
</ul>
