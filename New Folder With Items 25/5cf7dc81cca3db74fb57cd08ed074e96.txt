<h2>Bilingual Customer Care Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>ABOUT AGILIS SYSTEMS, LLC</b></p>
<p>Agilis Systems is a fast-growing SaaS provider who develops location-based software solutions for businesses and consumers. Our GPS fleet management solutions serve the needs of thousands of small business customers. We market our services through highly recognized brands such as Linxup.com and MOTOsafety.com.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>ABOUT THE POSITION</b></p>
<p>This role is a specialized form of customer service in which service and technical skills are used to focus on what is best for the customer. Customer Experience Associates serve as the face of the company and are responsible for solving customer issues, documenting each contact for the business and ensuring the customer remains loyal to our brand. Associates will be responsible for accurately responding to customer inquiries that occur via inbound telephone calls, chats, emails and/or cases efficiently from our English and Spanish speaking customers.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Communicate effectively with customers via phone</li>
<li>Answer all contacts with a warm and courteous greeting to ensure customer service satisfaction</li>
<li>Clarify customer requirements; probe for and confirm understanding of requirements or problem and provide first contact resolutions</li>
<li>Confirm customer understanding of the solution and provide additional customer education as needed to prevent call backs on the same issue</li>
<li>De-escalate calls by utilizing active listening and positive positioning statements to ensure positive resolution for both the customer and the company</li>
<li>Provide superior technical support on all branded products and build successful long-term relationships with both internal and external customers</li>
<li>Save customers that are looking to end their relationship with Agilis</li>
<li>Look for ways we can increase revenue on accounts by educating customers on new or existing products we offer</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Basic Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Proficient in Gmail</li>
<li>Fluent in both English and Spanish language required</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills (English &amp; Spanish) required</li>
<li>Exceptional customer service skills</li>
<li>Strong technical aptitude and/or background in triaging technical issues</li>
<li>Ability to learn fast in a non-traditional training environment</li>
<li>Organized with proficient attention to detail</li>
<li>Must possess a strong work ethic, with the ability to adapt to changes quickly and think conceptually</li>
<li>Ability to type well and at a fast pace</li>
<li>Flexibility on schedules</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Preferred Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>1+ years of experience in a call center or support role</li>
<li>Sales or cross-selling experience</li>
<li>Salesforce knowledge</li>
<li>Retention experience</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Work Environment</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Call center environment</li>
<li>The hours of this position are Monday through Friday from 10am – 7pm and every Saturday from 8am – 5pm (Off days are Sunday and one day during the week.)</li>
</ul>
