<p><b>Position Title: Regional Sales Manager</b></p>
<p><b>Description Summary:</b></p>
<p>China Telecom Americas, a fast growing company, is seeking a strategic sales professional who enjoys developing new prospects and interacting with existing customers. Regional Sales Managers work prospecting and qualifying clients, ultimately building a sustainable pipeline to increase sales of China Telecom Americas' products and services. Successful Sales Managers are familiar with a variety of marketing concepts, practices and procedures. This position relies on broad experience (sales or telecom) and judgment to plan and accomplish sales goals.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Major Tasks &amp; Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p>Reporting directly to the Sales Director, the Regional Sales Manager will be responsible to develop business for China Telecom Americas? focused on Fortune 1000 companies, and ASPs in the North American markets.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Focusing on large multi-national organizations and other companies requiring such services as contained in China Telecom Americas? product portfolio, Regional Sales Managers will be expected to meet, and also encouraged to exceed aggressive revenue targets while accomplishing the following:</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Responsible for selling IP, IPLC / IEPL, MPLS VPN, IDC, ICT and other data products in the US market, meeting quarterly and annual sales targets</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Develop long-term relationships with our enterprise customers, especially multi-national corporate customers</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Identify sales leads, provide sale support (including working with engineering and operations for the successful cutover of sold services), manage sales processes, close sales deals, and negotiate terms and conditions</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Manage against branch budget goals relating to direct and indirect expenses</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Review and organize business case documentation for Bid Reviews and/or other requests requiring approval from senior management</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Take overall leadership position on contract negotiations and coordinate Executive, Legal and Operational resources to drive process to timely completion</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Develop and document formal territory plan that clearly analyzes and defines the target customers, and articulates the strategies and tactics to develop business with them</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Manage and document adequate sales pipeline (estimated 5X annual revenue target)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Provide accurate weekly activity, and various monthly and ad hoc reports to senior management</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Act as an escalation point for customers to remove any and all obstacles to developing business in the target markets</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>About Us:</b></p>
<p>China Telecom Americas, a wholly-owned US-based subsidiary of China Telecom Corp. Ltd. (NYSE: CHA), is an international telecom provider for Data, IP and Voice Wholesale services to multinational companies, organizations and international carriers requiring China domestic services and International access to China &amp; Asia Pacific.<br>
<br>
With headquarters in Herndon, Virginia, and offices in Chicago, Los Angeles, Dallas, New York, San Jose, and subsidiaries in Toronto, Canada, and Sao Paolo, Brazil, China Telecom Americas continues to expand its strength and reach.<br>
<br>
China Telecom Americas provides locally-based, one-stop-shop, turn-key solutions for everything from China domestic and international data circuits to IDC services, network management, equipment management, system integration, and much more.</p>
