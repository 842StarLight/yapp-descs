<h2>Senior Recruiter (corporate recruiting experience preferred)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">A HireRight recruiter is responsible for sourcing, screening, testing, scheduling interviews, hiring and referring applicants for available positions throughout the company. Our recruiter facilitates the sourcing, attraction, recruiting, selection process and ultimately extends offers of employment. HireRight is a very dynamic company and is reflective of the rapid growth and market aspirations of the organization.
<br><br>
<b>Summary of Responsibilities:</b><br>
Partner with leadership to design and deliver effective recruiting strategies and use best practices to source, attract, screen, recruit, select, negotiate with and hire quality candidates for HireRight positions
<br><br>
Source candidates via cold calling, networking, referrals, boolean logic and Internet searches as well as other creative methods for attracting high performers
<br><br>
Assist in the development of the Employment Brand and a positive community impression of HireRight as an employer of choice.
<br><br>
Partner and actively communicate with hiring manager’s and candidate’s as appropriate to fill requisitions in the ATS
<br><br>
Follow the candidate workflow process for Ultipro and input necessary activities timely in the system for accurate reporting
<br><br>
Analyze high volume and niche hiring processes and recommend improvements
<br><br>
Ensure a consistent recruiting experience for the hiring manager and the candidate
<br><br>
May be assigned to work on HR projects as needed
<br><br>
Ensure job offers are consistent and based on internal equity and company salary structure
<br><br>
<b>Education:</b><br>
High School diploma or GED required
<br><br>
BA/BS Degree
<br><br>
An equivalent combination of education and/or experience is acceptable
<br><br>
<b>Skills:</b><br>
Skilled at developing and implementing creative recruiting solutions including strategic and tactical sourcing plans; Active communication with hiring managers and applicants
<br><br>
Ability to understand and articulate a hiring leader's resource requirements, culture and other compatibility issues that are critical to making a successful hire
<br><br>
Proven ability to develop and maintain hiring manager and candidate relationships across multiple types of positions and industries
<br><br>
Thorough knowledge of the entire recruiting life cycle coupled with solid candidate presentation to offer acceptance ratios
<br><br>
Solid business and financial acumen coupled with a good understanding of policies, procedures and employment labor laws regarding employment practices
<br><br>
Ability to communicate, influence and build credibility and confidence at all levels
<br><br>
Demonstrated experience filling diverse types of jobs, such as technical, operations, sales etc.
<br><br>
Solid working knowledge of internet-based boolean search techniques and recruiting tool
<br><br>
<b>Experience:</b><br>
2 to 5 years of related recruiting experience with a proven track record
<br><br>
Experience in behavioral interviewing and competency models
<br><br>
Experience with distance interviews, such as using Skype or other alternative interview solutions
<br><br>
Competent with Applicant Tracking software to achieve results
<br><br>
Experience evaluating effectiveness of pre-employment processes, including applicant testing
<br><br>
Preference for recruiting experience in a fast paced corporate environment
