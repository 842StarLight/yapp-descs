<h2>Packing Clerk (Shipping/Warehouse)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Pick and Pack shipping clerk available at an ingredient distributor in Huntington Beach. We are looking for a sharp and enthusiastic efficient yet detail oriented worker who can be an amazing “right hand” to the Warehouse supervisor and support our growing company. The company has less than 10 employees and the atmosphere is relaxed professional.</p>
<p>
 <b>Main Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Picking and packing daily customer orders</li>
<li>Organizing warehouse, keeping it as clean as possible</li>
<li>Mix ingredients per recipes (You will need to have or get a Food Handlers/ServSafe Certificate)</li>
<li>Pack and ship for trade shows (occasionally attend and set up displays).</li>
<li>Answering calls, interfacing with sales and shipping departments, vendors, and customers.
</li>
<li>Demonstrate initiative, professionalism, integrity, and accuracy.</li>
<li>Miscellaneous ongoing admin tasks assigned.</li>
<li>Assist the General Manager and Sales Manager as needed.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Excellent communication skills.</li>
<li>1+ years of warehouse or food handling experience.</li>
<li>Must be able to lift/carry/push/pull up to 75 lbs.</li>
<li>Must have color vision and ability to read fine print.
</li>
<li>Understanding measurements (metric system).</li>
<li>Understanding how to read sales orders, purchase orders, and invoices.</li>
<li>Knowledge of computer systems principles and procedures, including knowledge of Microsoft Office, Outlook, Excel, Word, Email, and other relevant software, typing 50 wpm+.</li>
<li>Work hours can be a little flexible, but primarily morning to early afternoon so long as it is consistent on a daily basis. Part or full time available for the right candidate.</li>
<li>Higher education is a plus.</li>
<li>Must pass background check and reference verification if job offer is accepted.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Preferences:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Willing to learn and take on new tasks as you grow and become comfortable with current tasks assigned. Show ability to grow in the role.</li>
<li>Someone who can take initiative and ask to be taught new duties.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Compensation:</b> $13-15 per hour dependent on experience.</p>
