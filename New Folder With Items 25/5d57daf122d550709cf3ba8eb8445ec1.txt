<h2>Rental Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Southwest Idealease is seeking a qualified, outgoing individual for the position of<b> Rental Account Manager </b>in the Dallas area.<br>
<br>
You must be a self-starter, be self-disciplined as well as possess excellent customer service skills. You need an energetic drive, professional appearance, outstanding work ethic, excellent communication skills and a strong desire to succeed.<br>
</p>
<p>As a sales professional with Southwest International Trucks you will be provided not only the initial but continuous training and the tools as well as the support you need to be the best sales professional in the industry. This is an excellent opportunity with continuous growth within a very people oriented company for an individual who is motivated to succeed.<br>
</p>
<p><b>Duties and responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Prospect, qualify, quote in the market</li>
<li>Call on potential and existing customers</li>
<li>Follow up with customers throughout the selling process</li>
<li>Assist with all documentation with customers</li>
<li>Assist in formulation of marketing plans for the location</li>
<li>Stay trained and current in the ever changing industry</li>
<li>Make company services available to customers</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned by manager</li>
</ul>
<p>Required: High School diploma or equivalent and 2 or more years minimum experience in the Trucking/Transportation Industry or a related industry . Preferred: Associates Degree or equivalent experience, and 4 or more years' experience in the Trucking/Transportation Industry or a related industry.<br>
<br>
General Competencies:</p>
<ul>
<li>Build Relationships</li>
<li>Demonstrate Professionalism</li>
<li>Produce Results</li>
<li>Focus on Customer/Quality/Detail</li>
<li>Know the Business</li>
<li>Influence and Negotiate</li>
<li>Manage Work/Time</li>
<li>Proficient with Computer and Software Applications</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Required Licenses/Transportation:</p>
<ul>
<li>Valid driver's license and clean driving record</li>
<li>Personal and dependable transportation</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Eligibility Consideration
</p>
<p>If you are an experience sales professional with a proven track record for high performance in a fast paced, high dollar value environment, and you re interested in working for a growing, dynamic and financially sound organization, then this could be the opportunity for you!</p>
