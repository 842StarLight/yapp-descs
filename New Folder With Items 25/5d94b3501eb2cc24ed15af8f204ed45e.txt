<b>Overall purpose of this position:</b><br>
<b>The Corporate Accountant position requires strong accounting experience along with analytical and decision making skills. The ideal candidate will possess dedicated work ethics and sound organizational skills to be able to work within team environment and also interact well with other departments. This position supports various aspects of the accounting close cycle including but not limited to the following: Inventory reconciliation, freight accruals, various capitalization processes, review inventory costing, gross profit analysis and general accounting. This position will report to the Corporate Accounting Manager and will have significant interactions with other departments.</b><br>
</li>
<li>
<b>Essential Job Functions, which must be performed in order to carry out the purpose of the position as described above:</b><br>
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Ensure accurate completion of monthly account reconciliations.</li>
<li>Be able to understand, analyze and reconcile inventory cost layers.
</li>
<li>Be able to review and research book vs. physical inventory variances.</li>
<li>Prepare Balance Sheet and P&amp;L variance analysis.</li>
<li>Be able to understand intercompany sales and transfers.</li>
<li>Participate in the month end, quarter-end and year-end closing processes.</li>
<li>Support interim and year-end external audit process, inventory appraisals and bank collateral exams.</li>
<li>Record journal transactions in accordance with US GAAP.</li>
<li>Follow all internal policies and procedures and communicate exceptions to management.</li>
<li>Perform other accounting and reporting-related projects as assigned.</li>
<li>Serve as a member of the Accounting team providing assistance as needed.<br>
 </li>
</ul>
