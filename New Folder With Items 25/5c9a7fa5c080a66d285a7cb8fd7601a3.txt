<h2>Strategic Account Executive</h2>

<p>Allocadia is a recognized leader in Marketing Performance Management, serving more than 150 customers and thousands of users worldwide, and manages billions of marketing dollars for companies like Microsoft, Juniper Networks and VMware. In this role, you will be responsible for managing sales cycles from lead to close with Strategic accounts (&gt;$5 B revenue) in a geographic territory.</p>
