<h2>Jr. Call Monitor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Americor is an innovative finance technology company, focusing on consumer loans and debt resolution. We are looking for a detail-oriented Jr. Call Monitoring Representative to join our growing team!</p>
<p>
As a Jr. Call Monitor, you will audit calls based on a strict set of guidelines and procedures to ensure all compliance and regulatory requirements are upheld. You will communicate relevant feedback to management to continually enhance the customer experience.</p>
<p><b>
What you will do:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Daily call monitoring per the predefined sampling criteria.</li>
<li>
Analyze quality of calls by focusing on regulatory requirements, sales and service procedures, accuracy of information given and collected, and delivering on Americor's Customer Experience standards</li>
<li>
Communicate findings with relevant mangers and team members</li>
<li>
Other activities as assigned</li>
</ul>

What we provide:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
A fun and energetic work environment (Ping-pong, Music, Lunches, awesome Co-Workers)</li>
<li>
Health and Dental Benefits</li>
<li>
Growth Opportunities</li>
<li>
Free gym access</li>
</ul>

What will make you successful:</b></p>
<ul>

At least 1 year experience in a call center environment</li>
<li>
Exceptional listening and analytical skills</li>
<li>
Strong knowledge of customer care processes and techniques</li>
<li>
Great focus on customer satisfaction</li>

Excellent oral, written and interpersonal communication skills</li>
