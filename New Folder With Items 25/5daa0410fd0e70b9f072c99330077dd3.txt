<p><b>
Position Summary</b></p>
<p>
ALL Crane Rental of Pennsylvania, LLC is seeking a Parts Runner. This is a non-exempt part-time position. Local candidates only please.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Functions</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Perform tasks involving the safe driving of vehicles</li>
<li>
Deliver equipment parts to customers and/or other divisions of the company</li>
<li>
Miscellaneous tasks around the yard: maintenance and clean-up of equipment, buildings and general area; parts inventory; small assembly and repairs</li>
<li>
Positive interface with fellow employees, supervisors, and customers</li>
<li>
Maintain conformity to safety requirements and other regulations</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Skills and Experience Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Valid driver’s license with an acceptable driving record</li>
<li>
Must be able to work 25 hours a week</li>
<li>
Strong initiative required; ability to work independently with minimal direct supervision</li>
<li>
Ability to work safely around moving machinery; to work in conditions with marked changes in temperature &amp; humidity</li>
<li>
Able to lift 50 lbs.</li>
</ul>
