<h2>Parts Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><b>Position Overview</b></div>
<div>
The Retail Parts Manager is responsible for running a profitable and efficient parts department. The Retail Parts Manager will accomplish objectives through the use of proper purchasing procedures, inventory control, staff utilization, pricing, merchandising and advertising.
</div>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>What are my day-to-day responsibilities</b>:</div>
<ul>
<li>
Forecast goals and objectives for the department and strive to meet them</li>
<li>
Ensure parts and accessories are available for resale
</li>
<li>Strive for harmony and teamwork with all other departments</li>
<li>
Work with the Service Manager to ensure a timely turnaround of parts needed for Warranty and Customer Pay jobs</li>
<li>
Establish and maintain purchasing policies including purchase orders, inventory guide figures, order codes</li>
<li>
Prepare and administer an annual operating budget for the parts department</li>
<li>
Understand, keep abreast of, and comply with federal, state, and local regulations that may affect parts sales</li>
<li>
Establish and maintain programs designed to train, develop and motivate department employees</li>
<li>
Direct and schedule the activities of all parts department employees</li>
<li>
Provide technical assistance to parts department employees</li>
<li>
Monitor parts department employees' payroll records</li>
<li>
Develop, enforce and monitor guidelines for working with customers to ensure maximum customer satisfaction</li>
<li>
Establish and enforce a pricing policy that balances volume, customer loyalty, and the cost of doing business</li>
<li>
Monitor and adjust inventory to minimize obsolescence</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>
What are the requirments for this job?</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
A high school diploma or the equivalent College degree or equivalent experience.</li>
<li>
Two years experience as a Parts Manager at an Automobile Dealership or wholesale parts department</li>
<li>
Ability to read and comprehend instructions and information
</li>
<li>Two year of supervisory experience</li>
<li>
Five years of experience in an automotive parts department</li>
<li>
Excellent communication and managerial skills</li>
<li>
Must have a valid drivers license</li>
<li>
Ability to operate an automobile</li>
</ul>
