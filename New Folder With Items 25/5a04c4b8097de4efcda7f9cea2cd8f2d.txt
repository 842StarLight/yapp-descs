<h2>IT Business Development Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Job Description:</b><br>
Stambaugh Ness TechSolutions is searching for professionals with a passion for excellence in client service and in life-long learning. We are a firm who has embraced technology and who strives to develop the strengths in each of our valued staff. We are headquartered in York, PA, and work with Project Based Professional Services Firms throughout the United States.
<br><br>
The IT Business Development Consultant works to improve Stambaugh Ness TechSolutions market position and achieve financial growth. This person defines key customer relationships, identifies business opportunities, negotiates and closes business deals and maintains extensive knowledge of current market conditions.
<br><br>
<b>Job responsibilities include, but are not limited to:</b><br>
Cultivate client relationships through primary contact and by collaborating on industry specific strategies and efficiencies
<br><br>
Maintain and develop relationships with existing and new customers in person and via telephone calls and emails to build a robust pipeline
<br><br>
Analyze customer base to identify potential sales opportunities within CRM
<br><br>
Document daily activities into CRM and manage those activities and opportunities from development through product implementation to maintain the highest level of client delight
<br><br>
<b>Job Requirements:</b><br>
The ideal candidate for the IT Business Development Consultant position should possess the following:
<br><br>
Technical Proficiency
<br><br>
Strong client-facing and communication skills
<br><br>
Proven sales record
<br><br>
Associates degree in Information Technology, Business Management or equivalent with 3 years of industry experience
