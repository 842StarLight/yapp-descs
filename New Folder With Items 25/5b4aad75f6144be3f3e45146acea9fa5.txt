<h2>Marketing Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Canvs is the industry standard for topical analysis and emotion measurement. Our patented AI technology allows our clients, from research agencies to major media companies, turn text into powerful insights in seconds. We've automated the ability for any organization to analyze and better understand the emotions and sentiments of their customers - so they can drive significant improvement in their product and brand experiences.</p>
<p>
As the <b>Marketing Coordinator</b> at Canvs, you’ll help drive all things marketing, from creative direction and branding to PR and demand generation with an emphasis on creative content generation/marketing. Through the execution of a diverse range of programs, you’ll assist the team in managing its day-to-day activities while caring for the long-term strategy.</p>
<p>
The ideal candidate is a formidable writer excited about the SaaS and B2B technology space. You’re data driven and have a passion for developing a brand identity that really stands out. This is a full-time position based in the NYC office and reporting to the Director of Marketing.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
What You’ll Do</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop email marketing plans to increase sales prospects and promote brand awareness.</li>
<li>Create sales &amp; marketing collateral and manage content distribution across a variety of channels.</li>
<li>Manage demand gen functions in HubSpot.</li>
<li>Partner with Client Services and Product to create messaging, assets, and branding that successfully engage targeted prospects.</li>
<li>Manage SEO strategy and campaigns on paid social media accounts.</li>
<li>Assist with the development and execution of product launch initiatives, including email campaigns and webinars.</li>
<li>Help lead creative direction for client-facing marketing materials, including one pagers and presentation decks.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Why We’ll Love You</b></p>
<ul>
<li>At least 1 - 2 years of marketing experience in SaaS and/or with B2B software.</li>
<li>Familiar with HubSpot or comparable CRM platforms.</li>
<li>Strong understanding of inbound and outbound marketing strategies, SEO/SEM, etc.</li>
<li>Knowledge of creative branding and direction.</li>
<li>Outstanding written and verbal communication skills.</li>
<li>Comfortable with the tech startup environment.</li>
<br>
</ul>
