Job Description</b></p>
<p><b>
This is a position that requires time in the field, as well as data collection (audits/surveys) and entry. Energy or Lighting auditing experience and a technical background with some of the systems mentioned herein is required.</b></p>
<p>
The <b>Lighting and Energy Auditing positions </b>will work closely with the Energy Engineering team, sales team, project management team, and clients and partners to design, manage and develop building energy improvements to reduce energy costs. The technologies include all facets of energy reduction, including lighting, HVAC, EMS controls, lighting controls, renewables, compressed air and other energy solutions.</p>
<p>
A construction, electrical, maintenance, property management, or similar building or building materials knowledge or an energy/construction related degree or industry experience is required.</p>
<p>
Past energy auditing, and experience performing calculations is strongly preferred.</p>
