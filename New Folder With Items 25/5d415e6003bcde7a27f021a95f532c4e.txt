<div><b>
Overview</b></div>
<div>
Criteo, a leader in commerce marketing technology, is looking for a new Senior Corporate Counsel based in New York, NY. In this role, you will support Criteo’s global operations and report to the Senior Legal Director Corporate based in Paris, France, providing advice on all US securities matters as well as being the lead U.S. M&amp;A lawyer for the Company’s strategic growth plan. Your role will be critical to ensuring consistency and compliance with all applicable regulations and Criteo policies.</div>
<div>
</div>
<div>
This critical position represents an incredible career move for a lawyer seeking a challenging opportunity that combines technical legal knowledge with practical experience, interacting with business clients in a demanding, international, and fast-paced environment. We are looking for a talented lawyer who can work independently and take ownership of matters, but also can take direction and collaborate within a collegial environment.</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What will you be doing?</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Overseeing all U.S. securities reporting, including but not limited to, Forms 10-K, 10-Q and 8-K, the Proxy filings and Forms 3,4,5, 13D, 13G and S-8</li>
<li>
Advising on corporate governance matters</li>
<li>
Supervising U.S. paralegal responsible for SEC filings
</li>
<li>Advising on mergers and acquisitions, organizing and directing due diligence, drafting and negotiating US M&amp;A purchase/sale agreements, and related deal documents (NDA, LOI, engagement letters, etc.), advising on deal structure and negotiation strategy</li>
<li>
Working with People Experience on equity plan design, implementation and administration</li>
<li>
Developing and maintaining internal processes</li>
<li>
Reviewing all earnings-related documents (press releases, scripts and investor presentations), press releases for the Americas and other public disclosures</li>
<li>
Advising on a variety of business, operations and risk management issues</li>
<li>
Leading the review of documentation related to any company debt or equity transactions</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What do we NEED?</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s Degree plus a law degree (JD) from an ABA accredited law school</li>
<li>
Current Bar admission to practice law (NY Bar preferred)</li>
<li>
5+ years of experience in US Securities (both SEC reporting and transactions) and M&amp;A</li>
<li>
At least 3 years of experience from major, nationally-recognized law firm</li>
<li>
Prior in-house experience at a fast growing and international company preferred</li>
<li>
Excellent interpersonal skills to create strong business relationships inside the Company</li>
<li>
Self-confident, with persuasive oral presentation and negotiation skills</li>
<li>
Independent, with a high degree of business and legal judgment, demonstrating the ability to lead projects</li>
<li>
Self-starter, able to spot issues &amp; develop paths to business-oriented resolutions</li>
<li>
Experience in the Tech or Advertising industry is a plus as is international experience (legal and/or business)</li>
<li>
Fluency in foreign languages (including French) is a plus</li>
</ul></div>
