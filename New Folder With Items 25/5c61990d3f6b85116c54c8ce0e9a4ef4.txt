<h2>System Support I</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Summary of Role: </b></p>
<p>Installation of all PC Hardware/Software. Installation, configuration and support of all network services and associated hardware/software. Ensure all production requirements are completed in a timely manner.</p>
<p>Performs beginning level shift procedures and provides basic problem determination, resolution, and records all trouble situations reported to I.T.<br>Provides beginning level support functions on all platforms including but not limited to: AS/400, PCs, Telephony, Enterprise Networks, Windows Server, Active Directory, UNIX and on all applications including but not limited to: CMS, SDS, Point of Sale, Time and Attendance, and video conference systems.</p>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<p>Ability to read, write, speak and understand the English language clearly to ascertain and document important information, to follow written and/or verbal instructions and to provide clear direction/guidance.</p>
<p>Ability to relate to all levels of management and employees in verbal/written form.</p>
<p>Ability to organize/prioritize work, meet deadlines, work with minimal supervision/multiple interruptions, exercise judgement and adapt instructions/directions from one assignment to another.</p>
<p>Remain calm and alert, especially during emergency situations and/or heavy hotel activity, serving as a role model for other employees.</p>
<p>Resolve complications and complaints by conducting thorough research of the situation and the most effective solutions.</p>
<p>Make decisions and take action based on previous experience &amp; judgement, sometimes revising procedures to accommodate unusual situations.</p>
<p>Social skills as demonstrated by the ability to deal with internal/external customers, some of whom will require high levels of patience, tact and diplomacy to diffuse anger, collect accurate information and resolve problems and concerns within guidelines.</p>
<p>Communicate effectively both verbally and in writing to provide clear direction, instruction and guidance.</p>
<p>Assign and instruct all personnel in details of work.</p>
<p>Observe performance and encourage improvement.</p>
<p>Monitor workload and make staffing adjustments accordingly.</p>
<p>Ability to grasp, bend, lift and/or carry or otherwise move goods weighing a maximum 50 lbs. on an intermittent basis and sufficient dexterity of hand in order to use office equipment including: computer keyboard, calculator, fax machine, general office equipment and multi-line telephone.</p>
<p>Ability to stand, move and walk throughout hotel property including walking up to 5 miles, climbing stairs and/or sitting at desk workstation for the duration of the shift.</p>
<p>Ability to exercise judgement and implement control over the performance of others.</p>
<p>Ability to deal with a number of problems requiring initiative and good judgement.</p>
<p>Ability to maintain attendance in conformance with standards.</p>
<p>Ability to maintain a neat, clean and well-groomed appearance.</p>
