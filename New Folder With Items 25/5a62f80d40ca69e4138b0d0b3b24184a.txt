<h2>401K Retirement Plan Education and Enrollment Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Responsible for coordinating and providing bi-lingual enrollment and education meetings (virtual and in person) for 401 k retirement plans focused on encouraging employee participation in the plan. Conduct ongoing education sessions to reinforce the benefits of plan participation, focus on retirement readiness and increase participation in the established plans, in Spanish and English</p>
<p>The ideal candidate will have retirement plans/401k experience.</p>
<p>Bilingual ( English/Spanish) a must</p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Schedules and conducts initial enrollment meetings aimed at educating plan sponsors and potential plan participants, focusing on the value of participating in their retirement plan, as well as maximizing plan participation levels.</li>
<li>Delivers ongoing education and re-enrollment meetings to existing and potential plan participants. Works to educate plan participants on retirement readiness, ongoing through the various phases of their career. Focuses on increasing participation levels of eligible employees and increasing deferral percentages of the plan participants.</li>
<li>Partnering with the Plan Consultant, continuously helps ensure participants have the tools and resources to prepare for retirement.</li>
<li>Provides training to Plan Sponsors and Participants of the tools and resources on the Internet Service Center.</li>
<li>Assist with participant calls; not limited to: investment education, plan provision explanations, website navigation and password resets</li>
<li>Collaborates with Plan Consultants in creating bi-lingual marketing materials and sales presentations</li>
<li>Interacts with Plan Consultants to accomplish sales and enrollment level targets</li>
<li>Performs other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications: </b></p>
<p>Excellent presentation/verbal and written communication skills. Proficient in using technology to present content/meetings. High attention to detail, learning agility, time management, technical and negotiation skills; ability to learn and execute processes and procedures. Proficient in using computers and related common office software and other applicable business applications.</p>
<p><b>Experience: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Three to five years in the financial services industry, with a preference of time spent in retirement plans.</li>
<li>Must have in-depth understanding of the retirement plan business and working knowledge of ERISA, plan documents and investments.</li>
<li>Experience with Relius Administration</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Education/Licensing</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Series 65 desired, must be obtained within 120 days</li>
<li>Designations such as AIF and other Retirement Plan designations are strongly encouraged.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Working Conditions</b>: Normal office environment. Travel required.</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
</div>
