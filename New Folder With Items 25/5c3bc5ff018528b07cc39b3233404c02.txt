<b>Job Description:</b><br>
<div>

<div>Mobility in the United States is in forefront of a technological revolution. <b>Siemens Mobility</b> Inc., Intelligent Traffic Systems (ITS) is an innovative partner to agencies in their transformation by combining existing infrastructure with forward-looking, application-driven technology that meets tomorrow’s needs.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>We at ITS help make public agencies smarter by providing a path to a tailor-made transportation ecosystem that is more digital, connected, and integrated and deliver solutions in the areas of Central and Adaptive Control, Advanced Traffic Management System (ATMS), and Smart Lighting. Also providing Connected Vehicle solutions (V2I) and Mobility-as-a-Service – we are at the forefront of Smart Mobility solutions.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>In this exciting environment, an opportunity is available for a Senior Project Manager to join our team in Columbus, OH. Additional locations may include: Billerica, MA, Austin, TX, Tampa, FL, Miami, FL, Riverside, CA. We could also accommodate a remote office with 50% travel to the project sites.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<b>Position Overview</b>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>Siemens ITS is recruiting for a Senior Project Manager to execute complex projects in ATMS and V2I projects across the United States. This vital role will support existing solutions install base for ATMS projects and guide Siemens’ resources to execute business strategy and meet customer satisfaction and business goals. The Senior Project Manager will report to Manager of Project Management and the position is located in Austin, Texas.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>In this role, you will be a subject matter expert focusing on ATMS and Smart Cities solutions - including how ATMS interfaces with Connected and Autonomous Vehicles, Traffic Management, and Adaptive Traffic Control activities. You will lead project teams during project execution and guarantee meeting business goals and customer satisfaction by executing complex projects on time and in budget. This role requires thought leadership in the respective solution space, applying best practices in ITS industry, leadership and communication skills as well as a strong background in customer relationship management and project management.</div>
