<div><b>
The Role:</b></div>
<div>
</div>
<div>
Collective Medical is seeking a talented Salesforce Administrator/Developer that will help us take healthcare to the next level. We are looking for an individual who likes to be innovative and think outside the box. Your primary responsibility will be to develop/maintain workflows within the Salesforce platform.
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Who you’ll work with:</b></div>
<div>
</div>
<div>
You will be working closely with Sales, Marketing, Product, Customer Success, and IT. This position will report to the Director of IT.
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>What you’ll be doing day to day:</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Perform system administration, configuration and support for Salesforce</li>
<li>Manage the design and programming of new functionality within Salesforce</li>
<li>Assist in analyzing current business processes, gathering requirements and suggest improvements</li>
<li>Document current Salesforce functionality and help create future functionality</li>
<li>Work closely with different teams to develop Salesforce functionality and workflows</li>
<li>Help with Data Science team on reporting through Salesforce and Tableau</li>
<li>Provide users with technical support related to the Salesforce platform</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
</div>
<div><b>
Skills and experience we’re looking for:</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<ul>
<li>At least 3 years experience working as a Salesforce Administrator</li>
<li>Experience with Salesforce Admin required</li>
<li>Data management abilities</li>
<li>Working knowledge of Salesforce development</li>
<li>Jira/Confluence administration is a plus</li>
</ul>
