<h2>Spa Concierge</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div><div>
<p><b>SUMMARY:</b>
<br>
Spa Concierge work closely with the Lead Concierge and additionally report to the Spa
</p>
<p>Supervisor and Spa Director. Spa Concierge work to ensure that the overall spa experience of
</p>
<p>each guest is optimal by focusing on ensuring that all guest service standards are upheld.
</p>
<p>Responsibilities include providing excellent guest service during all guest interactions to include
</p>
<p>phone calls, email correspondence, and in person interactions through the booking of spa
</p>
<p>reservations, completing confirmations, conducting retail sales, the processing of payments, and
</p>
<p>while assisting each guest during their spa visit. Spa Concierge are also responsible for
</p>
<p>confirming service provider schedules, assisting with guest recovery, opening &amp; closing the spa,
</p>
<p>and communicating all spa happenings via the daily recap.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b>
 </p>
<p>Ensuring consistency of service and safety protocols and guest satisfaction at all times
 </p>
<p>Provide continuous communication throughout each day, providing constructive information<br>
to the back of the house team members, other Spa Concierge, and Spa Director
 </p>
<p>Manage all phone and email reservations and all guest inquiries
 </p>
<p>Operate point of sale system to ensure accurate transactions are made
 </p>
<p>Actively sell retail and upsell spa services with a soft sales approach
 </p>
<p>Provide detailed, personal care with each spa guest to ensure a 5 star guest service experience
 </p>
<p>Manage spa appointment books for errors, rooming issues, or potential staffing needs
 </p>
<p>Communicate scheduling issues and appointments with service providers as they are booked<br>
and during call confirmations
 </p>
<p>Ensure all verbal and written communication is completed and follows spa standards
 </p>
<p>Assist in maintaining the tidy appearance of the retail boutique, concierge desk, and all other<br>
spa areas as able to do so
 </p>
<p>Complete all specified shift tasks noted on the Concierge “pass-off” and complete drawer<br>
counts
 </p>
<p>Handle cash with care, ensuring cash balance is always accurate
 </p>
<p>Conduct opening and closing tasks by following all points of the SOP’s related to each
 </p>
<p>Complete shift recap
 </p>
<p>Support safe work habits and a safe working environment at all times
 </p>
<p>Other duties and jobs may be assigned as needed by supervisor<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS:
</b></p>
<p>To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty
</p>
</div></div>
<p></p>
<div><div>
<p>satisfactorily. The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill, and/or
</p>
<p>ability required.
 </p>
<p>Preferred 2 years resort/hotel or salon/spa experience
 </p>
<p>Previous reception and point of sale experience
 </p>
<p>Excellent verbal, written, and communication skills
 </p>
<p>Must be adaptable to change and be able to multitask calmly and efficiently, with an<br>
emphasis on follow up, cooperation, and courtesy
 </p>
<p>Knowledgeable of the spa environment and experience
 </p>
<p>Outstanding interpersonal, guest service, listening, and problem-solving skills
 </p>
<p>Must be highly organized and detail oriented
 </p>
<p>Knowledgeable of computer booking software, POS, and Microsoft office programs
 </p>
<p>Must meet legal age requirements for the position.
 </p>
<p>Must be able to work independently
 </p>
<p>Must provide valid document(s) to work in the US<br>
</p>
<p><b>EDUCATION/EXPERIENCE:
 </b></p>
<p>High school diploma or equal to a GED; two years’ experience in related field<br>
<b>
</b></p>
<p><b>PHYSICAL DEMANDS:
</b></p>
<p>The physical demands described here are representative of those that must be met successfully to
</p>
<p>perform the essential functions of this job. Reasonable accommodations made to enable
</p>
<p>individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.
 </p>
<p>Must be able to lift 25 lbs.
 </p>
<p>Must be able to stand and walk for long periods of time.<b><br>
</b><br>
<br>
</p>
<p><b>LANGUAGE SKILLS:
 </b></p>
<p>Must speak and communicate in English.
 </p>
<p>Must be able to clearly communicate with guests, management and coworkers.<br>
</p>
<p><b>CERTIFICATES &amp; LICENSES:</b><b><i>
 </i></b></p>
<p>Valid California Driver License is required.<br>
</p>
<p><b>WORK ENVIRONMENT</b>:
</p>
<p>The work environment at Bernardus Lodge ranges from winter time lows of 20 degrees to
</p>
<p>summer time highs to 100+ degrees. While performing these duties the employee may be
</p>
<p>exposed to outdoor and inclement weather conditions.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>GROOMING &amp; UNIFORM:</b>
<br>
Employee must wear uniform at all times during scheduled hours. Uniforms are the property of
</p>
<p>Bernardus Lodge &amp; Spa. Uniforms that have been tampered or destroyed will be the
</p>
<p>responsibility of the employee and disciplinary action will apply.
 </p>
<p>Hair must be clean and kept at a reasonable length.
 </p>
<p>All Food Servers, Culinary and Spa personnel must wear their hair tied back.
 </p>
<p>No extreme dying or bleaching of hair is permitted.
 </p>
<p>Fingernails must be clean and well-trimmed at all times.
 </p>
<p>Antiperspirant or deodorant must be worn.
 </p>
</div></div>
<p></p>
<div><div>
<p>Any self-inflicted marking (tattoo) on the body cannot be observed by the public.
 </p>
<p>Cologne or perfume may be worn at a minimum.
 </p>
<p>Nametags must always be worn, unless your department has a specific policy to the contrary.</p>
