<h2>Director of Marketing / Business Development / Administrat</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Company Overview</b></p>
<p>
We are Long Island home improvement problem solvers. At United Water Restoration Group, our first responders handle 24/7 emergency damage service calls for hazards such as water, sewage, fire, smoke, mold, etc. From that point on, we can manage residential &amp; commercial projects (and stress levels) from the initial mess all the way to success.</p>
<p><b>
Mission of this Position</b></p>
<p>
As a business developer, you represent the face of the company to the public by illustrating the level of service and professionalism our clients and strategic partners will associate with the UWRG brand.</p>
<p>
By cultivating a strong network of referral sources – both direct and indirect – you open the channels for business opportunities to flow through our doors.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Duties &amp; Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Business-to-Business Relationship Building.<ul>
<li>
Strategically identify, prospect, and grow current and potential account relationships by coordinating meetings, events, etc.</li>
<li>
Interact daily with a broad array of people, companies, and associations to generate referral opportunities and job lead sources.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Convince potential partners of the unique capabilities of our company.<ul>
<li>
Educate them on the services UWRG provides by approaching prospects in places where they might frequent, such as supply houses, building/contractor retailers, lunch spots, etc.</li>
<li>
Utilize all avenues of interaction and communication to spread the word: face-to-face, phone, marketing materials, online, social media, text, e-mail, direct mail, etc.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Maintain organized and comprehensive records of all marketing activities.<ul>
<li>
Maximize use of CRM software to track contacts, interactions and relationship status.</li>
<li>
Prioritize high-potential relationships over smaller capacity prospects and allocate resources accordingly.</li>
<li>
Follow up: early and often.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Be a solution provider.<ul>
<li>
Serve as a professional consultant to analyze Home Improvement needs and make recommendations on solutions that will benefit the customer.</li>
<li>
Foster "Win-Win" business relationships with referral sources within the assigned territory.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Plan events to create opportunities to build rapport with referral sources.<ul>
<li>
Fill the weekly calendar with prospecting, networking events, and client visits, Strategic Partner visits, new prospect presentation appointments</li>
<li>
Participate in community gatherings, sometimes outside of standard business hours.</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
