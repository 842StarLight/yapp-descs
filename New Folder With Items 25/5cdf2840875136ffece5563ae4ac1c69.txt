 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>

<p></p>
<p><b>Position Description:</b><br>
Responsible for the day-to-day customer service calls and support of clients and customers. Responsible for inbound and outbound patient phone calls, prescription refill reminders calls, documentation of patient phone call, referral to clinical Amber staff for consultations, assist patient enrollment services, and obtaining answers for patient needs and requests.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Primary Responsibilities:</b><br>
General duties include but are not limited to:<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>1.Conducts prescription refill reminder calls.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>2.Responsible for inbound and outbound patient phone calls.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>3.Refer patient calls to appropriate clinical staff.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>4.Provides assistance with all patient needs and requests.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>5.Demonstrates expert customer service.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>6.Refer patients calls to appropriate clinical staff as needed.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>7.Keeps records of client/customer interactions and transactions.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>8.Ensure patience questions and request are addressed through available resources.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>9.Identify and report unique questions to supervisors to be sure correct, prompt customer service.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>10. Process routine website prescriptions orders as trained and directed by supervisors.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>11.Works closely with direct supervisor to improve assigned customer experience on the website.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>12.Participates in team meetings.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>13.Shared information with the Sales Team as it relates to assigned customer retention and growth.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>14.Troubleshoots patient request through working with teams to assist customers.<br>
</p>
