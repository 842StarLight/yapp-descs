<h2>Digital Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Are you interested in joining an infectious company culture where accountability, top performance and teamwork are valued and rewarded? A company that was most recently awarded the 2018 Nationals</b><b>℠</b><b> Silver Award by the NAHB National Sales and Marketing Council.</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Winchester Homes, Inc is looking for an experienced, enthusiastic individual to join our talented group as a Digital Marketing Manager in our Potomac, MD office.</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Position Highlights</b><b>:</b> The Digital Marketing Manager’s responsibility is to implement division/brand online campaigns working with multiple digital platforms in alignment with marketing efforts to increase brand awareness, attract and convert new leads as well as engage current homebuyers through marketing communications. The Digital Marketing Manager will be responsible for leading the design, testing, execution, maintenance and reporting of automated online marketing campaigns and website experience. This role will be self-motivated, collaborative, and will be a power user of marketing automation, website CMS systems and Google Analytics.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Position Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Collaborate with the Director of Marketing, the home office marketing team, advertising agencies and third party vendors to create and manage email campaigns, landing pages, website functionality and digital content, SEO, paid search, display, video and social media advertising, leveraging the appropriate technical tools and optimizing user experience.</li>
<li>Solve challenging business problems creatively, efficiently, and independently, delivering on multiple digital marketing initiatives in the same time frame with minimal supervision, especially when faced with operational constraints or ambiguity.</li>
<li>Analyze the performance of online marketing programs, make recommendations and take action to optimize results</li>
<li>Manage project risks, issues/problems, and activity progress to ensure project goals (e.g., deadline, scope and quality) are achieved.</li>
<li>Interact collaboratively with other internal home-building departments to gather input and materials to create digital assets, website and email content.</li>
<li>Track and work within all online budgets, review budget reports and trends with management, adjust plans accordingly and provide recommendations on future budgeting during planning phases.</li>
<li>Lead junior team members by coaching them to both provide leverage on digital projects and grow their technical and communication skills</li>
<li>Assist with troubleshooting technical issues such as XML feeds and errors</li>
<li>Ensure all content adheres to corporate and regulatory compliance standards and secure necessary approvals of content, when required.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p><b>
</b></p>
<p><b>Position Qualifications</b><b>:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Marketing or equivalent business experience required.</li>
<li>A minimum of 5 years’ work experience in digital marketing and demand generation role where you led both the development and hands-on execution of online branding, content development and go-to-market digital strategy, planning and execution with minimal supervision.</li>
<li>Home-building or equivalent real estate work experience highly preferred.</li>
<li>Proficient with marketing automation tools such as Marketo, Hubspot, Pardot, CRM tools such as Salesforce, Microsoft Dynamics, website CMS such as Wordpress, knowledge of HTML, XML, Google Analytics, general understanding of Adobe Creative Suite for graphics maintenance and other relevant tools.</li>
<li>Strong grammatical and creative writing skills, ability to write marketing text for the web, email and social</li>
<li>Advanced knowledge of Microsoft Suites.</li>
<li>Experience in communicating and collaborating with cross-functional teams at various levels.</li>
<li>Solid organization skills and a results-oriented, collaborative management style.</li>
<li>Excellent communicator and creative thinker, with an ability to use both data and intuition to inform decisions.</li>
<li>Self-motivated, highly driven, and fast learner.</li>
<br>
</ul>

</b></p>

<li>There are more than 20,000 Winchester homes in the mid-Atlantic region, with multiple single-family and townhome communities in the Washington D.C. metro area.</li>

<br>
</ul>
