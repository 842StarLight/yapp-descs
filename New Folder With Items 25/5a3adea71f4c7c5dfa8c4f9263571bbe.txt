<p>The Villages Health, located in Florida, is on a mission to become America’s Healthiest Hometown by keeping people healthy and by healing people quickly. Our care model empowers patients to live their healthiest life ever by giving them more 1:1 time with physicians, eliminating wait times, coordinating care with specialists and practitioners, involving patients in the decision-making process and providing complimentary resources that teach disease prevention and management. With 8 care centers and 15 specialties, we are able to provide seamless patient-centered care. As a result we have 98% patient satisfaction rates and consistently perform within the top 1% nationally for quality
</p>
<p>The Villages Health is building a world-class marketing and sales department to spread the word about our exemplary care model, while recruiting new patients to both our primary care centers and specialty care center. We are currently seeking a Marketing Specialist to join the team to promote and grow our multi-specialty care services, especially as we get ready to move into The Center for Advanced Health Care in Brownwood.
 </p>
