<h2>Customer Service Rep</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Be part of a growing, successful company in an exciting and challenging field. Border States is 100% employee-owned, which means you’re empowered to make decisions, find solutions and receive rewards for your hard work.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>This position will be located in: </b>Kansas City , MO<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<div><b>JOB SUMMARY
</b></div>
<div>Supports the operation functions of the city desk. Enters orders, fills orders, processes returns, responsible for maintaining a clean, safe and well-stocked City Desk display area, maintains vendor catalogs and price sheets, informs proper departments of “stock outs”, and maintains assigned inventory stock locations.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>RESPONSIBILITIES
</b></div>
<div><i>Essential Functions
</i></div>
<ul>
<li>Provides customer service functions including, but not limited to, order entry, filling orders, processing returns, expediting, and technical assistance, Will Call, and other functions, as needed.
</li>
<li>Maintains a clean, safe and well-stocked City Desk display area.
</li>
<li>Displays specials and other promotion items to sell.
</li>
<li>Maintains vendor catalogs and price sheets.
</li>
<li>Informs proper departments of “stock outs”, inventory discrepancies and other inventory problems or concerns.
</li>
<li>Maintains assigned inventory stock locations making sure the material put away has the correct locator and the shelves are kept neat and orderly.
</li>
<li>Abides by all safety requirements including wearing the required PPE gear.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><i>Non-essential Functions
</i></div>
<ul>
<li>Participates in the marketing planning and strategizing for BSE product lines, as needed.
</li>
<li>Participates in the implementation and success of marketing plans, as required.
</li>
<li>Attends sales meetings as required and keeps current on new products and applications.
</li>
<li>Assists with receiving, shipping and other warehouse associate functions, as required.
</li>
<li>Ensures all batched managed material is labeled accurately and inventoried, as needed.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>QUALIFICATIONS
</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of a two-year business/trade degree preferred or the equivalent in work experience.
</li>
<li>Minimum of one year of prior warehouse, city desk and/or sales experience with good knowledge of electrical products or systems preferred.
</li>
<li>Requires the ability to read, write and speak in English.
</li>
<li>Prefer strong working knowledge of PC for Windows, Microsoft Office (Excel and Word), Internet, Email and SAP software.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>COMPETENCIES
</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Excellent interpersonal, written and verbal communication, reading skills
</li>
<li>Ability to effectively plan and organize.
</li>
<li>Excellent customer service skills include being competent, accurate, responsive and engaged.<br>
</li>
</ul>
