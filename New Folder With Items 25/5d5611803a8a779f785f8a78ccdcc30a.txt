<h2>SaaS Account Executive - Los Angeles</h2>

<p><b>SaaS Account Executive</b></p>
<p><b>
Los Angeles / New York</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
TrueData’s (http://www.TrueDatadata.com/) mission is Truth in Mobile Data, delivered as a privacy-by-design data platform. For mobile app publishers our tools simplify high-quality data collection and help them publishers enable privacy compliance. For marketers we provide premium onboarding, customer insights, and 2nd-party audiences at a disruptive flat SaaS fee.</p>
<p>
We are experiencing great momentum and are looking to expand our sales team! TrueData is looking to add SaaS Account Executives in New York and Los Angeles, and will report directly to the VP of Sales.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Background</b></p>
<p>
We are looking for dynamic SaaS Account Executives to engage with a fast-moving, cross-disciplinary staff to grow revenue by evangelizing TrueData’s SaaS data platform to brands within assigned territories. This will include fully understanding TrueData’s broad audience, analytics and onboarding solutions, as well as it’s differentiators in the marketplace. In conjunction with the VP of Sales and Solution Architects, this role will exceed revenue goals by building and maintaining relationships,addressing customer pain, and providing the highest caliber customer service.</p>
<p><b>
Requirements</b></p>
<p><b>
What you'll be doing:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Building and maintaining an account book of business. Work directly with targeted brands, as well as lists you develop, to generate adoption of TrueData’s Platform for targeting, analytics and onboarding abilities.
</li>
<li>Run cohesive and comprehensive meetings with leading brands. Utilize TrueData’s marketing materials to deliver a cohesive and comprehensive snapshot of TrueData’s end-to-end offering.</li>
<li>Develop thoughtful targeting recommendations. In concert with account management, you will brainstorm out of the box ideas for segmentation to drive ROAS and exceed specified KPIs.</li>
<li>Be at the forefront of all trends and planning cycles. You will know each account from the top to the bottom, and fully understand each line of business and planning cycles.</li>
<li>Execute focused quarterly strategy. To meet and exceed quarterly revenue, you will work with the VP of Sales to deliver on focused quarterly strategies as outlined at the beginning of each quarter</li>
<li>Always sourcing, always discovering new revenue opportunities. You won’t sit still and will always be reading the trades, checking our sales automation tools, networking amongst their peers, and uncovering new opportunities.</li>
<li>Become a TrueData TrueData Expert. Working in conjunction with our full TrueData team each you will fully understand TrueData’s offering as well as become an expert on everything data-oriented across the industry.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>What we’re looking for:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>SaaS or DaaS Experience. You have spent 2-4 years exceeding targets at a SaaS or DaaS company focusing on Adtech or Martech.
</li>
<li>Tech Chops. You know enough about ad and data tech to be your own Sales Engineer most of the time. You don’t wait for people to explain technology to you, you proactively figure it out before you need to. You have impressed an engineer or two with your tech fluency.
</li>
<li>Drive. You have an unrelenting passion to build, refine, and grow TrueData Data’s position within the marketplace. You are competitive in nature and always push your team to win.</li>
<li>Relationships: You have strong relationships at brands within key verticals – D2C and Traditional Retail, Financial Services, Telco, Entertainment, Auto</li>
<li>Solution Selling: You are an expert solution seller. You know how to break a complex organization into decision-makers, and sell to each individual’s unique pain. You know how to move a SaaS sale through legal, purchasing, privacy, and C-level decision-makers. And you know that a sale happens in phases each of which need to be actively moved along towards an end goal.
</li>
<li>Curiosity: You love to learn about technology and data, you love to think outside the box, and you are eager to be a part of TrueData’s growth.</li>
<li>Organization. You easily manage hundreds of tasks / clients at once and nothing ever slips through the cracks.</li>
<li>Startup DNA: You are excited and motivated to join a startup to see immediately how your hard work impacts the company’s bottom line. This also means being willing to wear several hats based on where the company needs support, earning you experience across various sides of the startup business.
</li>
<li>Impeccable written communication. You can write exceptionally well to convey TrueData’s value proposition clearly and comprehensively.</li>
<li>Unparalleled presentation skills. You are a TED talk.</li>
<li>Teamwork. You are an all-around nice person who wants to work with other nice people. (honestly, no jerks or drama allowed.)</li>
<li>Location. You are excited to work in LA or NYC</li>
</ul>
