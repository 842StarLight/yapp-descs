<h2>BRANCH MANAGER, Beacon / RIS</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>As a Branch Manager for Beacon / RIS, you will lead a team of hard working individuals while overseeing the daily operations of a high volume branch in the building materials industry.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>Complete Job Description</div>
<ul>
<li>Manage and oversee all daily operations of Branch</li>
<li>Provide Regional Branch Manager any requested branch specific information</li>
<li>Oversee all aspects of profit center (operations, sales, admin, etc.)</li>
<li>Determine the nature of profitable versus non-profitable business through precise decision making and trend analysis</li>
<li>Work closely with Branch Credit Manager to determine adequate account base</li>
<li>Deal directly with corporate office on matters that concern day to day branch functions</li>
<li>Develop plans for increased branch profitability</li>
<li>Market Beacon to internal and external customer base through advertising dollars and manufacturer information/incentives</li>
<li>Provide training to all staff</li>
<li>Groom future managers</li>
<li>Oversee all branch personnel to assure that they are reaching their full potential</li>
<li>Responsible for building and maintaining morale in the branch</li>
<li>Demonstrate leadership qualities and setting an example for staff</li>
<li>Act as support line for all departments</li>
<li>Promote teamwork both within local team and region</li>
<li>Communicate regularly with vendors to establish and maintain strong relationships</li>
<li>Responsible for maintaining a high quality staff of the very best available people</li>
<li>Continue to work to improve the skills of existing employees</li>
<li>Act as a safety leader and is responsible for monthly safety committee meetings</li>
<li>Ensure that all company policies and procedures are followed</li>
<li>Prompt communication all vital branch specific information to Regional Managers</li>
<li>Complete all tasks on the Branch Manager's monthly checklist</li>
<li>Maintain ample levels of inventory to meet delivery and service expectations of all Beacon customers</li>
<li>Maintain a working knowledge of Beacon's benefits and policies</li>
<li>Complete all WC/Accident reporting</li>
<li>Understand and utilizing the Trend system</li>
<li>Conduct performance appraisals for all direct reports</li>
<li>Perform other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<div>Requirements</div>
<ul>
<li>Previous managerial/supervisory experience</li>
<li>Ability to build and maintain relationships with customers, vendors, and employees</li>
<li>Experience leading a team of employees towards a common goal</li>
<li>Motivational skills</li>
<li>Ability to work well with people</li>
<li>Ability to work with employees to satisfy individual needs</li>
<li>Ability remain organized while working with multiple tasks</li>
<li>Ability to delegate tasks</li>
<li>Must have a valid driver license</li>
</ul>
<div>Preferences</div>
<ul>
<li>Experience in the building products industry is preferred</li>
</ul>
