<h2>Director of Global Content and Creative Services</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div><div>Emailage is seeking a data-driven Director of Global Content and Creative services with an SMB mindset who is ready to roll up their sleeves when necessary and at the same time pull back and create opportunities for the team to succeed. This person will be responsible for public relations, branding, content strategy/execution, design, and social media marketing to help us achieve our marketing and branding goals across multiple platforms and formats to drive sales, engagement, retention, leads and positive customer behavior. This individual should be a constant learner and be able to drive the conversation for content and channel optimization, brand consistency, segmentation and localization, analytics and meaningful measurement. The position collaborates with the departments including Product Marketing, Demand Generation and Customer Marketing to help define both the brand story and the story as interpreted by the customer.
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Map out a content strategy that supports and extends marketing initiatives, both short- and long-term, determining which methods work for the brand and why.
</li>
<li>Continuous evolvement of strategy is a must.</li>
<li>
Develop a functional content calendar throughout the enterprise verticals, and defining the owners in each vertical to particular persona groups.
</li>
<li>Design creative marketing initiatives to test solutions and prove the validity of benefit to our dealers and company</li>
<li>
Conduct ongoing usability tests to gauge content effectiveness. Gathering data and handle analytics (or supervise those who do) and make recommendations based on those results.</li>
<li>
Facilitate communication and systematization of sales and marketing activity with product development teams and sales teams.</li>
<li>
Help develop and unify our global content marketing strategy to define and evolve our brand story.
</li>
<li>Develop standards, systems and best practices (both human and technological) for content creation, distribution, maintenance, content retrieval and content repurposing, including the real time implementation of content strategies.</li>
<li>
Leverage market data to develop content themes/topics and execute a plan to develop the assets that support a point of view and educate customers that leads to critical behavioral metrics.</li>
<li>
Work closely with the regional marketing teams, product management and sales.</li>
<li>
Lead a team who develops content, across multiple formats, to build brand awareness, thought leadership, prospect engagement, support SEO and lead generation.</li>
<li>
Develop the implementation of global communications strategies to ensure visibility, growth and profitability of Emailage, its products and services.
</li>
<li>Execute effective public relations, analyst relations, marketing communications, and advertising programs and forging positive relationships with key constituencies including: press and analysts, customers, partners, employees and the public</li>
<li>
Be Agile and partner with a can do mind set.</li>
<li>
Build and drive a content buyer journey and create content against that journey</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications:
</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>Bachelor degree in Advertising, Design, Marketing, Mass Communications or a related field and equivalent work experience, Required</li>
<li>
Experience leveraging market data to develop content themes/topics and execute a plan to develop the assets that support a point of view and educate customers that leads to critical behavioral metrics.</li>
<li>
Skilled at both long-form content creation and real-time (immediate) content creation and distribution strategies and tactics.</li>
<li>
A passion for new technology tools (aka, using the tools you preach about) and usage of those tools within your own blogs and social media outreach. Social DNA a plus!</li>
<li>
Clear articulation of the business goal behind the creation of a piece (or series) of content.</li>
<li>
Leadership skills required to define and manage a set of goals involving diverse contributors and content types
</li>
<li>Project management skills to manage editorial schedules and deadlines within corporate and ongoing campaigns.
</li>
<li>Ability to work in a 24 hour project cycle-utilizing teams or contractors in other countries.</li>
<li>
Familiarity with principles of marketing (and the ability to adapt or ignore them as dictated by data).</li>
<li>
Excellent negotiator and mediator. • Incredible people skills. • Basic technical understanding of HTML, XHTML, CSS, Javascript, web publishing, Flash, etc
</li>
<li>Fluency in web analytics tools, social media marketing applications and leading social media monitoring platforms.
 </li>
<li>A willingness to embrace change and adapt strategies on the fly</li>
<li>
Great powers of persuasion and presentation</li>
<li>
Experience creating a resource or library of content organized indicating SEO, translations and version control</li>
<li>
Needs to be continually learning the latest platforms, technology tools and marketing solutions through partnerships.</li>
<li>
Able to screen out sales pitches and look for the relevant brand and customer story.</li>
</ul></div>
