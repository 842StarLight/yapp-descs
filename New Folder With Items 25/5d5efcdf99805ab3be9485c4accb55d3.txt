<h2>Assistant Center Manager / Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Code Ninjas at Catalina Foothills Tucson AZ is looking for an Assistant Center Director to join our strong team.</p>
<p>Our ideal candidate is a self-starter, punctual, and reliable. Path of advancement available. Must be personable and great with kids!</p>
<p>Must pass full background check and employment verification.</p>
<p>We are looking for an Assistant Center Director to join our team of dynamic, energetic, forward thinking minds, working toward our common goal: providing a fun and safe learning environment for children.</p>
<p>Are you a passionate, ambitious, dependable, business-minded leader? Do you enjoy taking responsibility for a project and seeing it through to success? This could be the role for you.</p>
<p>Code Ninjas is a national provider of children education services for computer technology, specifically computer programming. Coding is the new literacy. We believe that we are creating the problem solvers of tomorrow.</p>
<p>Along with learning critical thinking and problem-solving skills, the children have the opportunity to learn responsibility, teamwork and leadership. We try to foster a cooperative environment where students can build new friendships and extend their social skills.</p>
<p>Our curriculum consists of four years of content progressing through nine levels of achievement. Specifically, we teach children age targeted software programming tools, for gaming, web-development, 3-D graphics, robotics and autonomous drones.</p>
<p>Responsibilities:</p>

<ul><li>Coordinate flow of enrollment, registration and student visits</li></ul>
<ul><li>Engage with children and families in the center</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ensure that parents understand how their child is learning and progressing</li></ul>
