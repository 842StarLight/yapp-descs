<h2>VP of IT Consulting Services Sales (Remote Position)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Job Description</b></p>
<p>Elevate and expand your career path with SCApath working with the latest innovations in robots, automation, AI, digital and supply chain. Be a part of a growing, vibrant, innovative culture that is helping Global Retailers transform their businesses. We hire the brightest, most dedicated candidates and provide leadership opportunities working with companies at the forefront of the omni-channel commerce frontier.</p>
<p>SCApath, LLC is a leading consulting firm that specializes in evaluating, designing and implementing supply chain solutions that enable multi-channel commerce for global consumer companies.</p>
<p>SCApath’s clients are focused on improving business performance in an increasingly disruptive global retail environment. We offer management and technology consulting services to help a diverse set of clients around the world deliver a seamless customer experience through singular commerce.</p>
<p>We are currently looking for a VP of Professional Services Sales to grow sales across our OMS/Omni-channel, WMS/Distribution, and TMS/Logistics Practices. This position has responsibility for sales pipeline management, sales operations, marketing and strategic partnerships. He or she will be a member of the executive leadership team and expected to provide key input into our overall business strategy and go-to-market strategy as we continue to grow. The ideal candidate will have a proven track record closing enterprise level consulting contracts; strong domain expertise in retail/ecommerce and supply chain; mastery of the complex sales process, and strong desire to be in an entrepreneurial and high performance environment.</p>
<p><b>What We Are Looking For</b></p>
<p>We are looking for a proven sales professional from an IT Professional Services background, not a product sales background exclusively (although it would be considered complementary.) Candidates must understand the concepts and key messages behind omni-channel supply chain solutions and enterprise business system applications and have sold services for the complete life cycle of a supply chain solution (business case development, system evaluation, project planning, design, build, test, train, deploy, support). Candidates should also have a network of contacts and experience of selling to one or more ofthe following industry verticals: Retail, E-commerce,Technology or Supply Chain. Candidates must have demonstrable sales figures and a record of achieving sales targets year over year for a consistent period of time.</p>
<p><b>Primary Duties</b></p>
<p>We are looking for a senior sales executive to join our team who is able to quickly understand and leverage our experience and has the same hunger for growth that we have. We feel to do this, the right candidate must acquire and care for their customers professionally and with passion.</p>
<p>Responsibilities include, but are not limited to:</p>
<ul>
<li>Develop and cultivate business relationships with prospects as a trusted advisor through phone, email, and meetings (online and in-person) to articulate and sell the value proposition of our professional services and custom supply chain solutions capabilities.</li>
<li>Effectively manage relationships with a range of stakeholders including C-level executives.</li>
<li>Engage in discussions with prospects to understand business objectives and requirements, consult on technical requirements, recommend appropriate solutions by demonstrating the key differentiators; desired outcomes and associated business benefits.</li>
<li>Propose and negotiate cost estimates for PS projects in alignment with internal resources, project scope/complexity, and the derived value of the solution.</li>
<li>Respond to RFP’s and support sales meetings.</li>
<li>Work with the PS delivery team to ensure a smooth transition of requirements, engagement expectations, and other key business elements of sold services.</li>
<li>Internal coordination of the support of other teams, also abroad.</li>
<li>Manage overall marketing strategy and execution to align with sales and services objectives, working with strategic partners to build relationships, cultivate new opportunities and establish a strong pipeline.</li>
<li>Create satisfied and referenceable customers.</li>
<li>Participation and moderation of industry events.</li>
<li>Provide vision and architect business development artifacts including but not limited to white papers, case studies, blogs, campaigns, website, social media, solution overviews, and sales presentations.</li>
<li>Manage the PS sales opportunity pipeline and process consistently to ensure proper forecasting and reporting of deals from project development initiation through completion.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Skills Required</b></p>
<ul>
<li>10+ years experience in management and systems consulting sales with a thorough understanding of retail supply chain and system implementation principles and practices.</li>
<li>Must have experience with leading enterprise supply chain packages for omni-channel retailers including Manhattan Associates, IBM, JDA, Oracle, Infor and other best of breed applications.</li>
<li>Experience in managing the complex sale and delivering client-focused solutions based on specific customer needs.</li>
<li>Understands and demonstrates a systematic approach to problem solving. Communicates fluently and effectively in business writing and present information to both technical and non-technical audiences.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to communicate, present and influence credibly and effectively at all levels of the organization, including C-level.</li>
<li>Able to plan, schedule, and monitor own work within deadlines.</li>
<li>Maintain awareness of developing technologies and takes some responsibility for personal development.</li>
<li>Possess technical and industry background, persistence, and persuasiveness needed to succeed in a sales role.</li>
<li>Close deals consistently to meet or exceed monthly and quarterly quotas, key performance indicators, and other departmental objectives.</li>
<li>Familiarity with business proposals: Methods and techniques for preparing and presenting proposals, business cases, and statements of work.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Desirable</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Practical experience in management or IT consulting role.</li>
<li>Experience with the full array of omni-channel applications required to execute singular commerce.</li>
<li>Strong CIO or CXO relationships.</li>
<li>Retail or Digital experience.</li>
<li>Experience selling implementation services of business applications.</li>
<li>Passion for professional services and a love of sales.</li>
</ul>
