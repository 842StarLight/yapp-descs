<h2>Regional Sales Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>FSS, LLC is seeking an experienced outside <b>Regional Sales Executive</b> who thrives in a fast-paced, performance-based, competitive atmosphere. The successful SE will have the ability to assess market opportunities, penetrate new accounts, and achieve aggressive sales targets through the introduction of the company's products/services to the general market.</p>
<p>In this role, you will utilize your expertise in engaging decision-makers to promote the TrusteSolutions and CORE product offerings. We promote an entrepreneurial culture where as a Sales Executive you will have the opportunity to mold and build your own territory while taking advantage of career development and training opportunities.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Excel in a quota-bearing role with responsibility for a geographic territory and ownership of the sales cycle from prospecting through to close</li>
<li>Meet and exceed quarterly quota objectives, monthly targets, prospecting activity and pipeline goals that you are expected to meet and overachieve</li>
<li>Responsible for driving primary business within a defined territory with cold calls, campaigns, in-person meetings attending industry conferences, etc.</li>
<li>Responsible for forecasting and activity updates and managing your territories in CRM system</li>
<li>A clear understanding of all TrusteSolutions and CORE services/products and business benefits, with in-depth understanding of the services/products you are focused on selling</li>
<li>Provide in-person, on-line demos and presentations to prospects/customers.</li>
<li>Manage market generated leads in a timely manner.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Experience/Skills</b></p>
<ul>
<li>3 - 5 years of quota carrying, direct sales experience</li>
<li>Previous technical sales experience may be helpful.</li>
<li>Experience with various CRM tools and reports.</li>
<li>Experience with selling to a mature market with 6-12 month sales cycles.</li>
<li>Ability to lead in-person and web-based prospect demonstrations.</li>
<li>Ability to strongly influence decision-makers at all levels.</li>
<li>Proven track record of consistent goal achievement.</li>
<li>Available to travel nationally 40-50%.</li>
<li>Associates degree or equivalent education or equivalent related experience.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Success at FSS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Deliver Outcomes – Demonstrate a bias for speed and execution that serves our shareholders and customers.</li>
<li>Operate as a Team Player – Work together to drive solutions for the good of FSS.</li>
<li>Build Strong Partnerships – Demonstrate integrity and build trust with others.</li>
<li>Strive for Excellence – Motivate yourself and others to achieve high standards and continuously improve.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>About Financial Software Solutions, LLC</b></p>
<p>TrusteSolutions and CORE are divisions of Financial Software Solutions, LLC (FSS), a 17 year old Houston-based software company that provides cloud-based enterprise software to professionals across the United States. FSS is dedicated to providing enterprise-level software that is easy-to-learn and easy-to-use, helping businesses do more with fewer resources for enhanced productivity.</p>
<p>FSS offers paid vacation and sick days, 401k participation after one year of service, medical and dental benefits and profit sharing after the first full year of employment.</p>
