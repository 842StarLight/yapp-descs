<b>
Job Description Summary</b><br>
<br>
Provides support to the Closing Department, handling an array of responsibilities focusing on providing any type of support necessary to complete the closing functions. Not limited to, but may include: collection of application fees; handling change requests, change circumstances; any other functions to assist the closing functions.<br>
<br>
<b>Essential Job Functions</b>
</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Reviews system data and documentation based on procedures. Determines accuracy and completeness. Determines appropriate payments and/or refunds.</li>
<li>
Reviews and runs credit card or e-check transactions for collection of required appraisal deposit.</li>
<li>
Receives change requests and reviews for approval or rejection according to guidelines. Provides required disclosures.
</li>
<li>Monitors reports for satisfactory compliance disclosures in regard to loan changes.<br>
Read and respond or forward e-mails received in department mailboxes.</li>
<li>
Handle any denied or returned credit card or e-check transactions.</li>
</ul>
