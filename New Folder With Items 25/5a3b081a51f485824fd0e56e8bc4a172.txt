<h2>LIHTC/HUD Experienced Regional Manager - South Los Angeles</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>FPI Management is currently looking for a <b>Portfolio Manager</b> to join our Corporate team located in South Los Angeles!<br>
<br>
The Portfolio Manager (PM) is directly responsible for managing a group of assigned assets and is accountable for all operations of the assets in their inventory, all on-site employees and enforcing all Company policies, procedures and internal operations, with the overall objective of meeting the expectations of each Client, based on the respective operating budget and management agreement.<br>
<br>
<b>OUR IDEAL CANDIDATE</b> has experience in a multi-site Property Management role. Is a dynamic leader with supervisory experience, possessing the ability to positively influence, mentor and lead a large dispersed team. Must have knowledge of various compliance regulations. Has competent financial and analytical skills, and excellent reporting ability. Is technologically savvy and proficient in MS Office (Word, Excel), Google Apps (Gmail, Drive, Docs, Sheets) and property management software (i.e.: Yardi). Has excellent people skills, is highly motivated and organized with a positive attitude and has a desire to provide quality customer service. Must have a flexible schedule and the ability to travel.<br>
<br>
<b>MINIMUM REQUIREMENTS:</b> High School diploma or equivalent, 4-year college degree preferred, or equivalent combination of education and work experience in the field of property management. Certification or designation from an accredited organization (ARM, CAM, CAPS, COS, CPM, MPM, RMP), preferred. English language proficiency (read, speak and write). Ability to pass a full background check and drug screening (<i>Note: As it relates to marijuana use, FPI will consider any applicable state law when dispositioning test results</i>)<br>
<br>
<b>Why look at FPI for your next career step?</b> We are one of the largest third-party property management companies in the Nation, an industry leader in both the Conventional and Affordable Housing spaces, with over 800 properties (over 100,000 units) across 15 states under our management. We offer comprehensive compensation and benefits packages, on-going training and professional development, and plenty of growth opportunities. Come join Team FPI!<br>
