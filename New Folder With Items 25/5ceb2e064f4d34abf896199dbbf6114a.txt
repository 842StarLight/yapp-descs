<p>PRINCIPAL RESPONSIBILITIES AND DUTIES:</p>
<p>
Full Stack Developer</p>
<p>
Do you want to work with a group of badass designers, technologists and writers to create innovate websites and web applications? We are seeking a strong Full Stack Developer who has a passion for building websites, single page applications and working with API’s to make extremely fluid and functional software. You will be responsible for building the front-end and back-end design of websites and software applications, and creating design plans for the user-facing side of applications, which controls look, feel, and function.
</p>
<p>This role will be an integral part of the Development team and you will collaborate with designers, marketers, and the entire company to solve problems and achieve meaningful results. As our team’s first full-time Full Stack Developer, you will be expected to pick things up quickly, work efficiently, and contribute to the direction and future of our Development team. You’ll work on multiple client accounts in varying industries. You will have the opportunity to make a big impact both for your clients and on the company. Red Caffeine takes pride in making sure every team member’s voice is heard and that everyone has the ability to shape their and the company’s growth path.<br>
</p>
