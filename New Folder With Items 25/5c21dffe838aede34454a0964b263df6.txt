<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What You Will Do
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>We are looking for a Junior Buyer to work in our Jericho, NY corporate headquarters. The Junior Buyer is responsible for maintaining adequate inventory levels across their product lines, resolving discrepancies related to inventory and communicating results to the warehouse, sales team and vendors through a collaborative approach.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Purchase finished goods in a high volume, multi-warehouse environment.</li>
<li>
Analyze and monitor inventory to ensure optimal levels are maintained at all times</li>
<li>
Work in conjunction with our distribution centers to manage purchase order discrepancies such as shortages, overages and damages.
</li>
<li>Review backorders and communicate with vendor and sales team directly
</li>
<li>Coordinate with vendors to ensure timely deliveries and inventories match demand requirements</li>
<li>
Expedite past due or rush orders as required.</li>
<li>
Manage cost and MSRP changes for assigned product lines.</li>
<li>
Light vendor relations within the lines being managed/purchased.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
