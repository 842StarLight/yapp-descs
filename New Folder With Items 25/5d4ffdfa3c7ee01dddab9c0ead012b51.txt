<h2>Business Development Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Fisher Unitech is fundamentally improving manufacturing in America by delivering, supporting, and training our customers on the best product development software, additive manufacturing, and subtractive manufacturing solutions available. We are one of the world’s largest providers of Stratasys 3D Printers and SolidWorks.
<br><br>
We are seeking a Business Development Manager to sell the benefits of SolidWorks and other 3D solutions. Your sales activities will include prospecting customers that include “C” level executives, qualification of opportunities, demonstrations, requirements analysis, ROI studies, and executive-level presentations.
<br><br>
<b>To Be Successful:</b><br>
You will work daily with Fisher Unitech personnel and SolidWorks representatives to achieve assigned sales quotas. Your primary responsibility is to develop new client relationships in the assigned territory while growing existing relationships at assigned clients. You will establish customers' needs and explain and demonstrate products to them, which may involve providing technical descriptions of products and how they may be used.
<br><br>
<b>You Have:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>2-5 years of experience in technology solution sales.</li>
<li>Proven ability to identify potential clients, and the decision makers within client organizations. Track record of successful solution selling and the ability to listen to customer requirements and present appropriately to close deals.</li>
<li>Understanding of the manufacturing industry.</li>
<li>Excellent presentation/communication skills.</li>
<li>Effective time management and organizational skills and entrepreneurial mindset.</li>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree or equivalent experience in Engineering or Manufacturing.</li>
</ul>
<b>You Might Have:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Knowledge of Engineering Software, specifically CAD.</li>
<li>SaaS sales background.</li>
<li>Experience ensuring that data is accurately entered and managed within a CRM system.</li>
</ul>
