<div><b>POSITION OVERVIEW</b></div>
<div><b>Programmer Analyst
</b></div>
<div>The Information Technology (IT) department at National Instruments (NI) is looking for a Programmer Analyst for its corporate headquarters in Austin, Texas. Our Programmer Analysts support critical business areas by developing and implementing software applications for use globally and by coordinating with business users to solve complex problems. This Programmer Analyst will have the opportunity to quickly become an important contributor to our Marketing IT Applications team, supporting cloud-based platforms and internally developed applications.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>The IT department at NI uses the latest application and database technologies to support our business. We are constantly researching new developments in the industry to improve existing systems and integrate those technologies into a single vision that connects NI’s corporate website, Sales &amp; Marketing operations, financial systems and many other departments. Our Programmer Analysts work with teams around the world and participate in the full software development lifecycle.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Core Job Responsibilities</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Serve as part of a highly-skilled team, providing technical and business solutions to extend and enhance our marketing business applications</li>
<li>
Partner with IT Architects, System Analysts, Quality Analysts, and Product Owners to craft creative solutions for complex problems</li>
<li>
Analyze, identify, research, and recommend solutions that meet business requirements and adhere to IT standards</li>
<li>
Become a sought-after, technical expert in the area of Marketing Operations and Effectiveness
</li>
<li>Provide effective written and oral communication within and outside of the IT department, including interactions and knowledge sharing with all levels of staff from peers around the world to executive-level management</li>
</ul>
