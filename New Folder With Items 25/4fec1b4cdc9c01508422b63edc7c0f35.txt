<div>
About the Role</div>
<ul>
<li>
Hands on Big Data experience - installation, operations, troubleshooting, tuning (examples of key platforms/tools: CDH/HDP/MapR/EMR/HDI/Databricks/Dataproc/etc., Hive, Spark, Kafka, HBase, HDFS, oozie, Airflow, YARN, etc.)</li>
<li>
Focused on troubleshooting directly with customers, interacting via multi-channel issue/case management. Examples include installation and upgrade issues, configuration and environmental issues, end user operational and app/job tuning issues</li>
<li>
Helping define/improve support and knowledge processes, in particular contributes to central knowledge management solutions to improve troubleshooting efficiency for all technical teams in the field</li>
<li>
Works closely with all pre+post sales/technical engineers in the field as well as PM/Eng to improve experience for customers - serves as conduit for roadmap/futures requests from customers</li>
<li>
Minimal travel except for team events and HQ visits w/ eng, majority of work is remote in front of terminal/machine/laptop</li>
</ul>
