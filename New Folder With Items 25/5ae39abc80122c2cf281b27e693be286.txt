<p><b>Overview</b></p>
<p>
Dealmoon is a high-end, elite, but bargain-oriented, e-commerce website with millions of online customers. Our central strategy is to offer luxury, brand-name products in various categories such as Beauty/Fashion, Travel, Electronics and Baby products. As one of the largest deals and coupons sites we operate the leading social shopping community for Chinese expats around the world, as well as an e-commerce advisory platform for global consumer brands seeking to reach Chinese-language shoppers. In 2016 alone, Chinese shoppers engaged with Dealmoon more than one billion times from the U.S., Canada, Australia, Japan, Korea, France, Germany, Italy and Spain, as well as China. Are you ready to discover great products and services to help people live better? If so, join us!</p>
<p><b>
Summary</b></p>
<p><b>
 </b>As such, we are seeking a smart, dynamic and creative Director of Account Management who is passionate about the retail sector and has an innate understanding of a ‘good deal’. This individual must be a creative collaborator who works well in a fast-paced environment. He/She can prospect, develop and grow strategic partnerships with brands focused on engagement and connecting with their consumers. Meetings, entertaining and traveling to outer markets are required.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Building strategy and implementing tactics to produce revenue, optimize relationships with current partners, meet and exceed annual targets, and build strong client-direct relationships through exceptional customer service, integrity and professionalism.</li>
<li>
Optimize current relationships, ensuring that Dealmoon gets the best ROI with all relationships</li>
<li>
Complete proposals and help negotiate commission rates and/or flat fees for programs</li>
<li>
Drive increased efficiency of team by serving as liaison with interdepartmental teams including Editorial and Finance</li>
<li>
Manage respective affiliate network/merchant information, including keeping track of any program changes, and any other account management items</li>
<li>
Manage/assist colleagues and other departments as assigned or as needed</li>
<li>
Respond effectively to sensitive inquiries or concerns</li>
<li>
Travel to conduct scheduled and non-scheduled meetings, conferences and training</li>
</ul>
