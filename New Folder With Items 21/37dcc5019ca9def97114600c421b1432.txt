<h2>Director of Creative Services</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<div>Bazaarvoice connects brands and retailers to consumers, so that every shopping experience feels personal. From search and discovery to purchase and advocacy, Bazaarvoice’s solutions reach in-market shoppers, personalize their experiences, and give them the confidence to buy. Each month in the Bazaarvoice Network, more than a billion consumers view and share authentic content including reviews, questions and answers, and social photos across 6,000 brand and retail websites. Across the network, Bazaarvoice captures billions of shopper signals monthly - data that powers high-efficiency digital advertising and personalization with unmatched relevance.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
</div>
<div>
Bazaarvoice completed the acquisition of Influentster in August 2019 and with the 2 companies coming together we are able to create an even more attractive proposition for our clients.
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>Influenster is a digital destination where consumers discover products and reviews that enable them to make well-informed purchase decisions. With over 6 million members who have written over 38 million product reviews, we believe the consumer is the expert - everything we do within Influenster is centered around this philosophy.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Reporting to the President, the Director of Creative Services is an accomplished leader who will bring a strong sense of brand and product centric design inspiration to Influenster’s design assets. The ideal candidate has an established track record of interactive design experience with branding, the creation of advertising materials, and assets for Web, Mobile, and Apps. In addition, this individual will have experience working with large consumer websites and online marketing campaigns with the ability to translate business objectives into creative expression.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
The Director of Creative Services will oversee design, art direction, production, videography, photography, the social team and freelancers focused on creating high-performance marketing campaigns to drive member acquisition, engagement, conversion, and repeat.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Develop and execute strong design vision for Influenster brand across our app, mobile site and desktop site as well as for our marketing campaigns.</li>
<li>
Partner with Sales and Marketing teams to translate business goals into strong design elements that can deliver on key business metrics, elevate Sales and Marketing collateral and enable a strong brand identity.</li>
<li>
Develop strategic brand and marketing creative executed with excellence and leveraged across all touch points (including mobile and offline advertising); creative programs must meet KPI’s and advance our brand strategy.</li>
<li>
Ensure designs and interfaces communicate consistent, effective experiences and messages across a wide range of deliverables; ensure design across all mediums are in alignment with core site and branding strategy.</li>
<li>
Work with Accounts team to design print collateral such as VoxBoxes and other materials.</li>
<li>
Identify, sharpen, and evolve brand vision and value through creative communications.</li>
<li>
Lead style guide development with product, brand, and engineering partners to enhance design and enable more consistent user experience across the web-site.</li>
<li>
Own brand guidelines; manage and oversee development and sharing of identity assets and templates; development and management of all new and existing brand marks.</li>
<li>
Provide quality control over concepts and projects.</li>
<li>
Raise creative excellence and help infuse brand identity into corporate culture, embodying "Influenster Everywhere".</li>
<li>
Manage and elevate the Design, Social Media teams as well as freelancers. Provide coaching while ensuring each team member’s KPIs are being met.</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Desired skills and experience:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Minimum of 5 years of experience leading and managing teams.</li>
<li>
Experience with branding and the design/creation of advertising and promotional materials, including - but not limited to - digital and social ad formats/templates; OOH, print and video creative; logo lock-ups and award seals; etc.</li>
<li>
B.A. or higher in fine arts, graphic design, interactive design, or other design-related program.</li>
<li>
Strong understanding of best practices related to email designs, responsive UX, and key social platforms.</li>
<li>
Expert or advanced skills in Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign).</li>
<li>
Portfolio that demonstrates previous UX and interaction design work.</li>
<li>
Background in branding and digital marketing.</li>
<li>
Familiarity with CSS, HTML and web typography (Typekit etc.) and experience designing for mobile, tablet, and desktop.</li>
<li>
Positive manager and team player with clear communication skills while working with cross-functional counterparts</li>
<li>
Experience with still photography, video, rich media formats like GIFs.</li>
<li>
Previously worked in Media, Marketing or Tech focused organizations.</li>
<li>
Experience working with clients - ability to translate business objectives into creative expression.</li>
</ul></div>
