<p><b>
J</b><b>ob Summary</b>: The Director of Marketing will research, develop, plan and execute multiple marketing strategies to include: digital, print, media, and tradeshows/conferences. The following Lines of Business fall directly under the scope of this position: Horizons' Edge Sports Campus, Camp Horizons, Camp Up with People, The Outdoor Learning Center at Horizons', NROCKS Outdoor Adventures and Horizons' Hospitality.
 </p>
<p><b>Duties:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop and implement annual marketing plans for Horizons Edge, Camp Horizons, NROCKS Outdoor Adventures and The Outdoor Learning Center and other future subsidiaries.</li>
<li>
Develop marketing through leadership strategies for Horizons companies</li>
<li>
Assist in hiring/training of marketing team members</li>
<li>
Develop annual operating budgets for the above marketing campaigns.</li>
<li>
Develop and implement brand standards across our different lines of business.</li>
<li>
Measure and report marketing/advertising campaign analytics to measure ROI, efficiency, and effectiveness of campaign activity.</li>
<li>
Provide input and establish consistency in regard to website, graphic design, merchandising, and promotional materials.</li>
<li>
Establish and sustain high quality, best industry standards across all company marketing content</li>
<li>
Administer internal and external communications to include social media, and CRM</li>
<li>
Facilitate business growth by developing and implementing CRM and lead mining strategies</li>
<li>
Provide support for our group sales Initiatives to include market segmentation, sales goals, budgets, and annual sales plan.</li>
<li>
Develop, plan and promote annual events schedule.</li>
<li>
Provide oversight of internet marketing plans, promotions and advertisements.</li>
<li>
Support program operations as needed.</li>
<li>
Develop partnerships with local, state and regional agencies to promote Horizons' outdoor and hospitality programs.</li>
<li>
Identify grant opportunities and submit state-sponsored grant applications.</li>
<li>
Research and present marketing and business trend data and growth analysis.</li>
<li>
Identify and forecast business opportunities, and targeted market share.</li>
<li>
Coordinate new product development.</li>
<li>
Sustain rapport with key accounts via scheduled visits to explore specific needs and anticipate new opportunities.</li>
<li>
Perform other miscellaneous duties as assigned or deemed necessary.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Qualifications</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor's Degree required, preferably in Marketing, Business or Communications. Master's preferred.</li>
<li>
Minimum of 5 years of high level marketing experience related to the outdoor and/or hospitality industries preferred.</li>
<li>
Advanced knowledge in using Microsoft Outlook, Word, Excel, and PowerPoint.</li>
<li>
Skillful in Adobe Creative Suite, specifically in InDesign and Illustrator.</li>
<li>
Ability to develop content and write with company voice in mind.</li>
<li>
Able to identify and create opportunities or promotions to deliver revenue goals.</li>
<li>
Willingness to travel.</li>
<li>
Point of Sale POS and Reservation Software experience preferred.</li>
<li>
Google AdWords certifications preferred</li>
<li>
Expert knowledge of HTML, CSS, programming language and blogging</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Experience</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Minimum 5 years of experience in online marketing, PPC and SEO work is required</li>
<li>
Experience in preparing and management of a marketing budget.</li>
<li>
Prior experience in marketing research to gain consumer insights locally and regionally to identify key selling points for attracting corporate/organization relationships including sponsorship and financial partnerships, and business development activities.</li>
<li>
Experience with management of corporate websites in the WordPress and other content management platforms.</li>
<li>
Experience in writing press releases and managing media relationships.</li>
<li>
Experience in managing business accounts on a variety of social media platforms. Including, but not limited to, Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter.</li>
<li>
Extensive experience in CRM, product design and pricing strategies, marketing/sales management to include market analysis and trend projections, internet-based marketing, promotional strategies and quantitative data analysis.</li>
<li>
SEO and Google Analytics Experience as well as knowledge of Word Press highly desired.</li>
</ul>
