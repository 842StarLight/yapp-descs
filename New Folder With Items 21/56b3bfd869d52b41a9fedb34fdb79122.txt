<h2>IT Corporate Recruiter</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">At Memorial Hermann, we’re about creating exceptional experiences for both our patients and our employees. Our goal is to provide opportunities for our diverse employee population that develop and grow careers in a team-oriented environment focused on patient care.
<br><br>
Every employee, at every level, begins their journey at Memorial Hermann learning about the history of the organization and its established culture built on trust and integrity. Our employees drive this culture, and we want you to be a part of it.
<br>Job Summary Memorial Hermann System Talent Acquisition is currently looking for a Corporate Technical Recruiter to support strategic growth by finding the best talent regardless of industry. In this role, you will to use your sourcing savvy, sales skills, business acumen and networking panache to identify and attract superior IT talent that will take us to the future. You will partner with business and hiring managers to conduct intake, source and screen candidates, schedule interviews, formulate and deliver offers, and close deals. As a member of the corporate recruiting team, you’ll carry a moderately heavy requisition load, and ensure the successful delivery of full-lifecycle recruiting support for the health system.
