<p><b>Job Title: Sr. Buyer</b></p>
<p><b>
Department: Procurement
</b></p>
<p><b>Classification: Exempt</b><br>
<br>
</p>
<p><b>Summary:</b> The Buyer is responsible for creating and managing Purchase Orders to support operations and the organization’s ability to consistently ship Customer Sales Orders on-time and accurately. The buyer will use the MRP system to purchase materials from suppliers to meet manufacturing and customer schedule requirements.
</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:</b> As a Sr. Buyer, you will:</p>
<ul>
<li>Prepare and process requisitions, quotes and purchase orders for supplies and equipment.</li>
<li>Participate in the development of specifications for equipment, products, or substitute materials.</li>
<li>Partner with stakeholders to ensure clear requirements documentation.</li>
<li>Negotiate price, payment terms, delivery schedules and freight terms.</li>
<li>Determine quantity and timing of deliveries.</li>
<li>Monitor and forecast upcoming levels of demand.</li>
<li>Evaluate bids and make recommendations; based on factors, award bids.</li>
<li>Keep a constant check on stock levels.</li>
<li>Liaise between suppliers, manufacturers, relevant internal departments, and customers.</li>
<li>Work with Quality and Engineering to select, qualify and approve new suppliers.</li>
<li>Maintain and track purchasing records, including PO’s, invoice payment and PO status.</li>
<li>Responsible for cost savings targets.</li>
<li>Maintain positive working relationships with suppliers and internal customers.</li>
<li>Work to achieve department and company goals.</li>
<li>Work with MRP output to place and revise Purchase Orders to meet customer demand.</li>
<li>Follow environmental and safety regulations and act in compliance with U.S. laws.</li>
<li>Perform other duties, as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<p><b>You’re a great fit for this position if this describes you;</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Business, Technical or Engineering Associate’s Degree or equivalent experience.</li>
<li>3-5 years’ minimum experience in a manufacturing organization.</li>
<li>Previous experience in Supply Chain and/or Strategic Sourcing a plus.</li>
<li>Excellent written communications skills, including the use of proper spelling, punctuation, and grammar to produce error-free copy, particularly composing letters, reports and documents used for decision making.</li>
<li>Outstanding Oral communication skills including tact and diplomacy which would be utilized during customer service, conflict management, development of relationships with internal and external contacts.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to manage multiple and diverse projects including, analytical thinking, planning, prioritizing, problem solving, decision making and carrying out assigned projects with minimal supervision.</li>
<li>Ability to define complex problems, collect data, establish facts, draw valid conclusions, evaluate options and, considering the relative costs and benefits of potential actions to choose the most appropriate one, implement solutions.</li>
<li>Able to perform in a fast-paced, team environment, under pressure with time constrains while responding and adapting to change favorably and quickly.</li>
<li>Ability to think out of the box and work without excessive supervision.</li>
<li>Proficient in current computer software: MS Applications Word, Excel, and understanding of ERP systems. (Syteline is a plus)</li>
<li>CPIM or CSCP certification a plus.</li>
<li>Safety is at the forefront of your mind when you are working and you gladly follow all safety policies to promote a safe working environment at USSC.</li>
<li>You bring with you a positive attitude and the ability to work cohesively in a team environment.</li>
<li>Help USSC operate effectively by adhering to USSC’s attendance and punctuality standards and does not burden co-workers and supervisors unfairly with unnecessary and unplanned absences and lateness.</li>
<li>Understanding that you are part of a growing company, you have no concerns with and able to work extended hours/over-time as needed as well as some Saturdays to support that growth.</li>
</ul>
