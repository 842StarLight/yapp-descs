<div><b>Summary/Objective:</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>Rogers Corporation is seeking a Maintenance Team Leader as the primary contact for leadership, supervision, and oversight of the Maintenance department. The Leader assists and provides direction to the team to ensure achievement of daily safety, quality, productivity, and inventory goals. This person must be a role model for safe behavior, and must ensure team members are following safe work practices and wearing required PPE.</div>
<div></div>
<div><div>
<div>
<b>Essential Functions:</b><b><br>
</b>
</div>
<div></div>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader">Essential functions are representative of the knowledge, skills and abilities required for the position. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform these functions.
</h1>
<ul><li> Assists Maintenance Supervisor in leading, motivating and monitoring maintenance team activities.
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Conducts regular maintenance activities to ensure manufacturing/facility equipment is maintained and department goals are met.
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Schedules and monitors preventative and repair maintenance for the facility and the equipment used by the division, including managing inventory of spare parts and tools and follow up on part orders.
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ensures that all safety codes are met for all equipment and that safety guards and interlocks are maintained in good working order. Advises supervisors, engineers and operations management.
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Assists in the installation of new equipment and modification of existing equipment.
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Assists in coordinating activities of outside contractors for services and special projects.
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Effective communication with multiple personnel and departments regarding department metrics, quality concerns, team objectives, etc.
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Assists in implementation and ongoing revisions to dashboards (metric documentation).</li></ul>

<div><b>Required Education and Experience:</b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>High School Diploma or equivalency.</div></li>
<li><div>8 to 10 years’ prior experience general maintenance or related experience.</div></li>
<li><div>Maintains a strong working knowledge in at least one related trade, including knowledge of applicable codes, and is able to demonstrate such knowledge, and is able to demonstrate a reasonable working knowledge of at least four related trades; or ability to demonstrate a reasonable working knowledge of at least six related trades.
</div></li>
<li><div>Internal applicants must currently work in a Maintenance Technician capacity, with above average attendance record, and demonstrated safety record.</div></li>
<li><div>Must be able to demonstrate strong leadership skills, including demonstrated ability to mentor less-experienced maintenance personnel within given areas.</div></li>
<li><div>Must successfully complete Hazardous Waste (RCRA) Intermediate level training, which includes introductory and recurring training, and abide by state and federal regulations regarding handling and management of hazardous waste.
</div></li>
<li><div>Excellent verbal/written communication skills and effective interpersonal skills.
</div></li>
<li><div>Certified to operate a forklift, mobile elevated work platform, and power industrial trucks, or the ability to become certified.</div></li>
<li><div>Must have basic professional tools and toolbox on-site.
</div></li>
<li><div>Strong organizational skills and the ability to multi-task effectively.</div></li>
<li><div>Minimum of 5 years’ experience troubleshooting and programming using Allen Bradley PLC5’s and SLC5’s.</div></li>
<li><div>Able to take initiative and follow-through on assignments.</div></li>
<li><div>Able to operate PC based software programs and operate automated business systems including previous experience with CMMS systems.
</div></li>
<li><div>Must be respirator certified, or able to become certified.</div></li>
</ul>

<div><b>Physical Demands:</b></div>
<div>
<br>
The physical demands described here are representative of those that must be met by an employee to successfully perform the essential functions of this job.</div>
<div></div>
<div>The Maintenance Team Leader must be able to stand on their feet for long periods of time; capable of lifting up to 50 lbs routinely; able to walk up ladders and work on an elevated platform. This position involves bending, stooping, standing and walking on a regular basis throughout the duration of the shift. The employee is frequently required to use hands, arms and fingers to reach, lift, pull, push and move materials with or without use of material handling cart, as required. The position requires hearing of no less than a 40 db loss @ 500 Hz, 1000 Hz and 2,000 Hz with or without correction.
</div>

<div>Rogers Corporation (NYSE:ROG) is a global leader in engineered materials to power, protect and connect our world. Rogers delivers Power Electronics Solutions for energy-efficient motor drives, vehicle electrification and alternative energy; Elastomeric Materials Solutions for sealing, vibration management, and impact protection in mobile devices, transportation interiors, industrial equipment and performance apparel; and Advanced Connectivity Solutions for wireless infrastructure, automotive safety and radar systems.
