<h2>Veterinary Receptionist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Compassion. Consistency. Community. These are just some of the core values of Noah’s Ark Animal Clinics.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Noah’s Ark Animal Clinics now boast three full-service, state-of-the-art clinics, caring for over 45,000 dogs and cats in Greater Cincinnati each year. From laser-assisted surgery, ultrasound, digital x-ray, to a full in-network lab for quick results – we have everything needed to care for your pet
</p>
<p></p>
<p>We currently have a RECEPTIONIST position open at our Colerain location. We are a fast paced vet clinic looking for an outgoing friendly individual 8:30 am-3:30 pm Monday through Friday, including every other Saturday, to serve our clients, extend hospitality, provide clear communication and knowledgeable sub-professional advice, and to ensure every client has a good experience.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Major duties include but are not limited to:</p>
<ul>
<li>Promptly receive incoming calls.</li>
<li>Greet clients and check in vet visits by obtaining necessary data concerning patient and owner as well as preparing required forms.</li>
<li>Oversee discharge of animals from the clinic.</li>
<li>Maintain a clean and orderly reception area and waiting room.</li>
<li>Collect client fees, make change, process credit card transactions and provide accurate daily accounting of cash drawer.</li>
<li>Perform over-the-counter sales of merchandise and preventative medications.</li>
<li>Exercise knowledge of products sold and demonstrate salesmanship abilities.</li>
<li>Fill veterinary prescriptions with appropriate medication; provide routine instructions to owners concerning Rx for medications.</li>
<li>Collect lab specimens from pet owners, match patient record to the sample and submit samples to veterinary technician.</li>
<li>Complete a high volume of medical records into the computer system, retrieving and modifying computerized records as needed, and navigating our software system, Intravet.</li>
<li>Schedule appointments for surgeries and boarding, providing the necessary information completely and accurately.</li>
<li>This position requires basic computer skills, proper use of the English language including: speaking, spelling, and grammar, as well as a positive upbeat attitude. Some experience in a veterinary environment is preferred. Salary will be determined based on experience and education. This position does include benefits after eligibility period, and paid vacation after a year of employment.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Noah's Ark offers a competitive benefits package which includes the following: medical, dental, vision, disability, 401(k) retirement savings with company match, paid leave (holidays, PTO) and discounts on our products and services.</p>
