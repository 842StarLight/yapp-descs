<h2>Account Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>SUMMARY</b></p>
<p>
Provide customer service, sales correspondence, site visits, meeting minutes, resolve issues with U.S. Customs, HTS codes as needed. Provide effective telephone &amp; written communication with customers, suppliers, brokers, freight forwarders.</p>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES &amp; RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Generate quotations, perform calculations &amp; mark-ups, negotiate pricing, resolve pricing issues with customers &amp; suppliers, and approve sales invoices under 40k</li>
<li>Generate purchase orders based upon customer PO, forecasts, inventory levels, safety stock, and lead times</li>
<li>Monitor incoming &amp; outgoing shipments, monitor container status, ensure delivery control to customer</li>
<li>Issue RMA numbers, resolve rejected &amp; damaged inventory, process insurance claims</li>
<li>Analyze monthly sales &amp; gross profit numbers</li>
<li>Analyze customer AR Aging &amp; collection of AR</li>
<li>Analyze &amp; monitor Inventory Aging</li>
<li>Provide explanations for section manager reports</li>
<li>Provide information for compliance related issues</li>
<li>Research customer’s financial status periodically</li>
<li>Seek new business opportunities</li>
<li>Support other sales &amp; administrative staff</li>
<li>Assist with other projects as needed</li>
<li>10% travel</li>
</ul>
