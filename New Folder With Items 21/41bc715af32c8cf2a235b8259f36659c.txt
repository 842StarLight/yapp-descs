 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div><b>Client Contact Representative</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>The Client Contact Representative I is responsible for answering incoming communications for Cordell Practice Management Group and its clients, scheduling initial appointments for prospective clients, and maintaining a neat and professional office environment for the CPMG office. Do you have superior customer service skills and enjoy working in a fast-paced, collaborative environment? If so, then this you may be the right fit for this position.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>About Cordell Practice Management Group</b></div>
<div>Cordell Practice Management Group is a professional services company that provides 360 degrees of support to our clients. We are dedicated to a radical client-centered approach to the services we provide, backed by quality people who can help us succeed. In order to provide a service that is focused on people first, we hire and reward employees who also take pride in creating a world class experience for our clients and our co-workers. Cordell Practice Management Group liberates law firms to do more of what they do best.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Responsibilities</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Answers incoming calls/chats/emails with positive customer service.</li>
<li>Provides answers to potential client inquiries based on location and needs.</li>
<li>Aligns potential clients with services using a consultative sales approach.</li>
<li>Schedules initial consult appointments and perform conflict checks.</li>
<li>Checks general email and voicemail as appropriate.</li>
<li>Helps maintain the office break room and other common areas of the office to ensure they are tidy at all times.</li>
<li>Signs for deliveries, notifies the recipient via email, and keeps a log of incoming deliveries, as needed.</li>
<li>Alerts management of issues or concerns that require escalation for complete resolution or which may indicate a larger, underlying problem.</li>
<li>Enters data into our legal software for online appointments and scheduling.</li>
<li>Updates job knowledge by participating in up-training opportunities.</li>
<li>Handles sensitive client data with utmost discretion, care, and accuracy.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Minimum Qualifications</b></div>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or general education degree (GED)</li>
<li>0-2 years previous experience in call center, customer service, or other service-based industry</li>
<li>Communication Skills-communicate effectively over the phone and in person</li>
<li>Computer Skills, such as basic keyboard/typing skills. Must have a basic understanding of the Internet including how to receive and send email</li>
</ul>
<div>
<div><b>Preferred Qualifications</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Call Center, phone sales experience</li>
</ul>
