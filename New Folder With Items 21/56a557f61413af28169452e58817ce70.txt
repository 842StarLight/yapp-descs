<p><b>Job Description Summary:
</b></p>The Home Appliance Installer is responsible for the loading, unloading, and installation of gas and electric household appliances such as washers, dryers, range ovens, countertop stoves refrigerators, and ventilation hoods into new and existing residential units. This position ensures the installed appliances are damage free, properly installed according to the manufacturer’s specifications, and are fully functioning.

<p><b>Qualifications:
</b></p>
<p>This position requires the ability to deal effectively and tactfully with customers and employees and the ability to make decisions in a timely and professional manner.
</p>
<ul>
<li>Previous appliance installation experience preferred
</li>
<li>Great customer service skills
</li>
<li>Must be able to physically lift or move appliances up to 100 pounds and occasionally move appliances over 100 pounds
</li>
<li>Able to read and interpret documents such as safety rules, operating and maintenance instructions and procedure manuals
</li>
<li>Meet current ability to deal effectively and tactfully with customers and employees.
</li>
<li>Ability to make decisions in a timely and professional manner, plus have the analytical ability, initiative, and problems solving ability.
</li>
<li>Must have a valid driver license
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities </b>:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Load and unload appliances from delivery vehicle using a hand-truck.
</li>
<li>Moves and places unit(s) into the designated installation spot.
</li>
<li>Install unit(s) using simple hand tools supplied by the company.
</li>
<li>Inspects final product for proper operation and damage
</li>
</ul>
