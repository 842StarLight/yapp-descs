<p><b>SUMMARY OF RESPONSIBILITIES
</b></p>
<p>The HCM Systems Specialist will be the client’s first line of contact. This position will coordinate and facilitate all benefit-related functionality and be responsible for the build and importing of all initial employee data into the system. Along with benefits build responsibilities, the HCM System Specialist will also handle annual Open Enrollments, ACA support and reporting, and EDI setup and support. The HCM System Specialist will respond to client emails and calls in a timely manner. The HCM Systems Specialist will maintain high levels of customer satisfaction and work continuously to build trust and strong relationships. This position will be responsible for the monitoring and distributing of system and operational issues to the HCM Systems Team. This position should have general knowledge of the HCM software system.<br>
</p>
<p></p>

</b></p>
