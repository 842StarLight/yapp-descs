<h2>Technical Sales Support Specialist - Instrumentation Products</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div>
<p><b>Join ABB and work in a team that is dedicated to creating a future where innovative digital technologies allow greater access to cleaner energy.
</b></p>
<p>ABB's instrumentation product line includes flow instrumentation to measure fluid movement in process applications. Our product line is the most complete on the market and includes the following technologies: Electromagnetic, Swirl and Vortex, Coriolis, Differential Pressure, Variable Area and Thermal Mass. Our customer base includes: Water and Waste Water, Oil &amp; Gas, Petrochemicals, Fine Chemicals, Power Generation, Food &amp; Beverage, and Pulp &amp; Paper. Customer types include: end users, engineering firms, system integrators and OEMs.
<br>
<br>
ABB Inc., is currently seeking a Technical Sales Support Specialist for its location based out at Warminster,Pennsylvania . This position must sit in Warminster PA working remotely is not an option. The responsibilities include to provide application and post sale support to our customer base via phone and/or other electronic communication methods . The primary function is to provide assistance to our customer base and sales channels to aid in their application and use of our flow products. As part of a team, your group will be the first line technical support for our customers and sales force. Typical customer requests include: help with proper wiring and programming instructions, aid with product selection based on a customer’s specific application and troubleshooting of installations that are not providing the desired outputs. The successful candidate would become one of the primary knowledge base for the practical application and maintenance of our flow products. You will work closely with our Customer Support, Product Management, and Engineering Groups to ensure our customer’s success with our instrumentation products. Most of the assistance is provided via telephone in response to a customer inquiry. Occasionally, very light travel to customer sites may be required. .
</p>

</ul>
