<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
POSITION SUMMARY</b></h2>
<div><div><div><p>
The Junior Graphic Designer designs and creates marketing pieces (Including but not limited to graphics, logos, images, promotional and branding material) for advertising, internal documents, print, digital and web. This position works closely with the Marketing Department team members to ensure standards are maintained and innovative creative solutions are successfully delivered on time and budget.</p></div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>
Maintain image library for all printing/web needs including the ability to edit and refine images
</li>
<li>Work with marketing team in the creation of print and online sales and marketing collateral</li>
<li>
Follow established styles and guidelines to ensure corporate branding is consistent throughout the organization
</li>
<li>Proofread to produce accurate and high quality work
</li>
<li>Meet with managers to discuss objectives and scope of project and then develop conceptual solution to meet project needs</li>
<li>
Ensure deadlines are met on all projects in accordance with project timelines
</li>
<li>Design, lay-out and produce various graphic projects such as signs, brochures, posters, logos , displays promotions, flyers, etc
</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned<br>
.</li>
</ul>
