<h2>Commercial Ops Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div><div>
<div>
<div>
<b>Job Summary:</b><br>
Manage and assist with projects and initiatives focused on driving revenue. This position communicates with the field teams regarding new field sales tools and operational processes related to selling activities. Act as a conduit between the field teams and all other internal departments for operational initiatives. Assist the field sales team, inside sales team, and the corporate accounts team, in reaching their respective territory, regional, area and national sales goals determined annually by the TIS executive leadership team.<br>
<br>
<b>Job Details:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>
Designs, develops, coordinates, and facilitates the execution of specified projects and initiatives focused on organizational goals such as: Ad hoc analysis, report development, business change readiness and data quality management. Establishes project priorities and schedules, in line with strategic plans and operating objectives.
</li>
<li>Support day-to-day operational needs/requests of the sales and marketing teams, Customer Care and Accounts Receivable.</li>
<li>
Ensure all organizational design and change management actions are addressed so that implementation and execution is successful and sustainable.</li>
<li>
Collaborate cross functionally to complete and achieve successful results for operational projects.</li>
<li>
Provide sales and operational support for the Corporate Accounts Team as needed.</li>
<li>
Interact with Product Managers on the Marketing Team to define and streamline commercial operations process improvements or initiatives, such as product promotions.</li>
<li>
Proactively identify automation opportunities and recommend process improvements to improve system efficiencies.</li>
<li>
Work with Commercial Operations team to assist in development of metrics, KPIs, analytics and dashboards to provide performance measurement and drive business performance for various sales and marketing programs.</li>
<li>
Assist with execution of Commercial Operations initiatives in collaboration with the Commercial Operations Manager</li>
<li>
Participate in Regional, Territory and National sales meetings to assure strong alignment and collaboration with Sales and Marketing.
</li>
<li>Demonstrate a commitment to patient safety and product quality by maintaining compliance with Quality System requirements and all other documented policies and procedures including prompt reporting of adverse events and product performance reports. Fully adhere to all aspects of Terumo’s Interaction with Healthcare Providers policy and AdvaMed guidelines.
</li>
<li>Perform other job related duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>

Knowledge, Skills and Abilities (KSAs)
