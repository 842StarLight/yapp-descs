<h2>Landscape Field Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Field Specialist</b></p>
<p>Job Description</p>
<p><b>Job Summary: </b></p>
<p>The primary duty of this position is to complete a variety of professional assignments. Majority of work is performed by visiting upcoming and completed job sites and previous customers for follow ups. This position requires an independent person who is self-motivated in order to complete work in an efficient manner and maintain customer relationships through the course of their career.</p>
<p><b>Principle Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>In the mornings ensure all crews have tools and material for daily jobs before leaving</li>
<li>Schedule site visits with customers for any warranties, inquiries, possible sales leads, and follow ups with recently completed construction</li>
<li>Use Quickbooks for creating estimates, working orders, looking up customers information, etc.</li>
<li>Read and follow all work orders, designs, materials lists, guidelines, document all add-ons, and follow paperwork procedures</li>
<li>Create client list of previous customers for additional sales in areas schedule to visit already</li>
<li>Learn company formulas to correctly create estimates</li>
<li>Take photos, measurements, and site inventory of client’s property as needed</li>
<li>Record dated and detailed notes during client visits, include customer wants and needs, site conditions, initial ideas, etc.</li>
<li>Be able to identify plants, troubleshoot basic problems, and assess warranty work needed</li>
<li>Up-sell plants, materials, retail items and design work to customers</li>
<li>Communicate progress of projects to company management and field staff effectively</li>
<li>Conduct final walk-through with client, if sales staff or designer isn’t able to do</li>
<li>Visit job site with Crew Leaders and assist production as needed</li>
<li>When possible, visit upcoming jobs for before and after pictures and to familiarize with job</li>
<li>Complete all assigned duties with quality and efficiency</li>
<li>Perform any other related work as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Position Requirements: </b></p>
<p>To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each of the essential duties satisfactorily for an eight-twelve hour shift. The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skills and/or abilities required.</p>
<p><b>Position Qualifications: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Must be at least 18 years of age</li>
<li>Valid Driver’s License and maintain good driving record</li>
<li>Pass pre-employment drug test and maintain Drug Free Workplace Policy</li>
<li>Reliable transportation to work</li>
<li>Ability to work Saturdays and/or overtime hours if needed</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Physical Requirements: </b></p>
<p>The physical demands described here are representative of those that must be met by an employee to successfully perform the essential duties of this job.</p>
<ul>
<li>Physical stamina: While performing the duties of this job, the employee may regularly stand for long periods of time and use their hands and fingers to handle work materials (plants, tools, equipment, etc.)</li>
<li>The employee is required to have good manual dexterity, bend and reach with their hands and arms, and to speak and hear clearly. The employee is frequently required to walk and stoop, kneel, walk, climb or balance, stoop, kneel, traverse rough terrain, crouch or crawl to perform necessary duties on the jobsite.</li>
<li>Specific vision ability required by this job includes, but is not limited to close vision, distance vision, color vision, depth perception and the ability to adjust focus.</li>
<li>The employee may frequently lift and/or move up to 60 pounds.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Education/Experience: </b></p>
<p>College Degree in Horticulture or related field is preferred. Minimum six months experience in a landscaping, management, or related field. Knowledge of the principles and practices of landscape architecture/design, costs and uses of construction materials in landscape design, environmental planning, design and effects of development. Ability to create cost estimates, provide nursery and retail customer service to a high level of satisfaction, and communicate clearly and professionally with customers and other employees. Preferred experience in several areas of landscape and/or design.</p>
<p><b>Language Ability: </b></p>
<p>Excellent written, verbal, and electronic communication. Ability to read, speak and understand English. Ability to read and comprehend detailed instruction, short correspondence and memos and write simple correspondence and fill out reports. Comfortable answering phone calls with a clear voice. Ability to effectively present information and respond to questions from upper management, employees, vendors, customers and the general public.</p>
<p><b>Math Ability: </b></p>
<p>Ability to calculate figures and amounts such as square footage, area spacing and distance, proportions, percentages, area, circumference and volume. Ability to apply concepts of basic algebra and geometry.</p>
<p><b>Reasoning Ability: </b></p>
<p>Ability to define problems, collect data, establish facts and draw valid conclusions. Ability to interpret an extensive variety of technical instructions in mathematical or diagram form and deal with several abstract and concrete variables. Ability to make critical decisions and exercise mature judgment.</p>
<p><b>Computer Skills: </b></p>
<p>To perform this job successfully an individual should have knowledge of word processing software, spreadsheet software, inventory software, Internet software, and database software. Have ability to make accurate drawings to scale from sketches, drawings, photos, or other communicative media.</p>
