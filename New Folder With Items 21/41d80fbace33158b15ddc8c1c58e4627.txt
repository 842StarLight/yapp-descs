<h2>Production Laborer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Ash Grove Packaging, a CRH company, is a division within Oldcastle APG. APG is North America’s leading manufacturer and supplier of concrete masonry, dry mix, and hardscape products. With over $2B in sales, APG operates across 36 states and 5 Canadian provinces through a network of over 195 operating locations and more than 6,500 employees.</p>
<p><b>Sum</b><b>mary</b></p>
<p>Responsible for assisting designated machine operator or production team in a variety of functions including, but not limited to, maintenance, clean-up, yard work, warehouse, production, utilities, material handling and other duties as assigned.</p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>General</p>
<ul>
<li>Handle assignments in a repetitive and/or sequential order for completing tasks assigned</li>
<li>Keep work area clean</li>
<li>Use security gear at all times and follow all safety regulations</li>
<li>Report any unsafe conditions or defective equipment to the supervisor immediately</li>
</ul>
<p>Clean-Up</p>
<ul>
<li>Clean-up machinery and conveyors, maintain the surrounding area and keep equipment free from debris during down time</li>
<li>Clean floors and pick up reusable scrap for salvage</li>
<li>Transport trash and debris away from production areas</li>
<li>Responsible for keeping pallets neat and orderly</li>
</ul>
<p>Utility / Production Equipment</p>
<ul>
<li>Operate machinery or equipment as trained and instructed following safety guidelines at all times</li>
<li>Assist lead operator in preparing for shift production</li>
<li>Work closely with the lead operator and become knowledgeable of the mechanics and operation of the machines in which you assist</li>
<li>May be required to work with conveyors, ramps, elevators, etc. as assigned</li>
<li>May be required to operate equipment such as hoppers, bagging and sealer machines, forklift, palletizers, hydraulic equipment, powered tractors or vehicles, among others</li>
<li>Internal certification/training is required for these tasks</li>
<li>Operate a variety of hand tools</li>
</ul>
<p>Yard / Warehouse</p>
<ul>
<li>Assist in the loading/unloading of trucks</li>
<li>Assist in completing shipping/receiving documents</li>
<li>Package and label products properly</li>
<li>Handle stockpiles</li>
<li>Verify loads leaving the premises</li>
<li>Maintain, verify, and count product inventory</li>
<li>Other duties and responsibilities related to the nature of the job may be assigned on a temporary or permanent basis as needed</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<p>To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skills and/or abilities required. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable qualified individuals with disabilities to perform the essential duties of the job.</p>
<p><b>Requirements / Education / Experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma/equivalent, or a comparable combination of education, training, and experience</li>
<li>Ability to read and comprehend simple instructions</li>
<li>Ability to apply common sense understanding to carry out detailed but uninvolved written or oral instructions</li>
<li>Ability to follow company production and safety procedures</li>
<li>Ability to read a limited number of two and three syllable words and recognize similarities and differences between words and between series of numbers</li>
<li>Able to pass internal certification training</li>
<li>Ability to print and speak simple sentences</li>
<li>Ability to add and subtract two digit numbers and to multiply and divide 10’s and 100’s</li>
<li>Must be able to demonstrate lock out tag out procedures</li>
<li>Must keep key lock with self at all times</li>
<li>Must attend weekly safety meetings</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Physical Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>While performing the duties of this job the employee will be required to frequently stand on their feet for extended periods of time</li>
<li>Must have the ability to lift and/or move up to 50 lbs. from ground level</li>
</ul>
