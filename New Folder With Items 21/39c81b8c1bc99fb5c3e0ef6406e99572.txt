<h2>Account Manager-Diversified</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Account Manager will work directly with the customer and manage the quality and consistency of product and service delivery. Responsible for providing excellent customer service to all customers including assessing customer needs and processing orders, reviewing inventory availability and ensuring customer satisfaction, while being sure to sell product at the gross profit margin. Must develop new prospects and interact with existing customers to increase sales within assigned accounts and regions.</p>
<p></p>
<div>Essential Duties and Responsibilities include but may not be limited to:<br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Sell company services to current and potential clients.</li>
<li>Prepare action plans and schedules to identify specific targets.</li>
<li>Follow up and develop new leads and referrals resulting from field activity.</li>
<li>Prepare presentations, proposals for current and potential customers.</li>
<li>Develop and maintain sales materials and current product knowledge.</li>
<li>Establish and maintain current client and potential client relationships.</li>
<li>Manage account services through quality checks and other follow-up.</li>
<li>Identify and resolve client concerns.</li>
<li>Prepare a variety of status reports, including activity, closings, and follow-up.</li>
<li>Work effectively with Inside Sales staff.</li>
<li>Participate in marketing events such as seminars, trade shows.</li>
<li>Follow-up for collection of payment.</li>
<li>Coordinate shipping schedules and delivery of merchandise and services.</li>
<li>Provide on-the-job training to customers employees.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<br>
Education and Experience:<br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>5 plus years related experience and/or training within customer service.</li>
<li>Four year college degree from an accredited institution.</li>
<li>Working knowledge of computers and business related software.</li>
<li>Strong verbal, written and interpersonal skills.</li>
</ul>
<br>
Physical Requirements:<br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Ability to lift up to 30 lbs on a regular basis</li>
<li>Stand for an extended period</li>
</ul>
