<h2>Marketing Communications Manager, Healthcare</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Company Overview:</b></p>
<p>
Brady Corporation is an international manufacturer and marketer of complete solutions that identify and protect people, products and places. Brady's products help customers increase safety, security, productivity and performance and include high-performance labels, signs, safety devices, printing systems and software. Founded in 1914, the Company has a diverse customer base in electronics, telecommunications, manufacturing, electrical, construction, medical, aerospace and a variety of other industries. Brady is headquartered in Milwaukee, Wisconsin and as of July 31, 2019, employed approximately 6,100 people in its worldwide businesses. Brady's fiscal 2019 sales were approximately $1.16 billion. Brady stock trades on the New York Stock Exchange under the symbol BRC. More information is available on the Internet at www.bradycorp.com.</p>
<p><b>
Position Summary:</b></p>
<p>
The Marketing Communications Manager, Healthcare leads the strategy and development of marketing communications programs for PDC products in the Healthcare vertical market. The MarCom Manager will drive growth and ROI through a variety of marketing channels, including direct response marketing (e-mail, direct mail, and advertising), digital, trade shows, and product literature development to achieve company objectives, generate leads and revenue, and increase brand awareness.</p>
<p>
We are looking for an energetic, ambitious, and creative individual with outstanding leadership, communication, project management, analysis, and business skills. A results-driven team player who can collaborate with many stakeholders and juggle multiple projects in a fast-paced environment to achieve desired outcomes will be most successful. The MarCom Manager will be part of the Marketing Communications team and will report to the Sr. MarCom Manager.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilites:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop strategy and measurable marketing programs and campaigns from concept to completion, working with stakeholders, to achieve desired results for the Healthcare vertical.
<ul>
<li>Define project objectives; Develop project timeline and provide status updates to stakeholders; Plan and allocate needed resources and budget to complete project. Provide leadership to create and maintain project schedules while directing workflow of the production staff and vendors to adhere to the schedules.</li>
<li>Oversee creative process to ensure branding, messaging, and design are consistent and effectively represent the Company.</li>
<li>Develop content through research, writing, and collaborating with marketing stakeholders.</li>
<li>Develop, produce, and publish print and digital marketing collateral materials including brochures, flyers, emails, ads, and videos in collaboration with product management, marketing, graphic artists, printing companies and other stakeholders.</li>
<li>Manage advertising campaigns through research and selection of appropriate media to reach desired target audiences. Negotiate advertising rates and space allocation with publication representatives. Plan and direct ad concept, copy, and provide direction for graphic design elements. Supervise production of ad artwork materials by creative team.</li>
<li>Leverage social media channels (Facebook, LinkedIn, Instagram, Pinterest, Twitter, YouTube) to generate traffic, subscriptions / likes / followers and conversions.</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Create, design and execute compelling and effective offline and online ads, emails, landing pages, blogs and social media campaigns maintaining a cohesive story across all channels.</li>
<li>Create, write, and distribute company press releases.</li>
<li>Source target audience lists for marketing campaigns using company database and trusted list providers.</li>
<li>Maximize allocated marketing budget to focus on initiatives driving most revenue, profit and brand awareness for the company.</li>
<li>Compile reports and analyze marketing campaigns and channel effectiveness and produce and distribute monthly performance results.</li>
<li>Develop annual trade show program strategy and plan for Healthcare vertical. Recommend new shows to consider based upon fit with company initiatives and target audiences, and shows that should be eliminated based upon inadequate results/ROI, audience fit, or stakeholder feedback.</li>
<li>Collaborate with key stakeholders to define show objectives and strategy, including focus products, demonstrations, partnerships, market research, voice of customer surveys, sales lead goals, lead qualifying questions, etc..</li>
<li>Host planning, pre-show, and post-show meetings to collaborate and align with stakeholders, including sales, product management, and MarCom.</li>
<li>Plan and execute pre-show promotions and follow-up emails.</li>
<li>Coordinate leads management, working with show services, stakeholders, database team, and sales and customer service team supervisors.</li>
<li>Communicate trade show results to sales and marketing stakeholders after each show and through a quarterly review.</li>
<li>Maintain accurate and organized documentation, identify and implement process improvements and opportunities to optimize the trade show program, exhibits, product displays, and PDC attendee training.</li>
<li>Manage working relationships with exhibit houses, show service providers, logistics companies, and other vendors to obtain needed services and goods on time, negotiate favorable pricing, and stay abreast of new offerings that may improve PDC's trade show program.</li>
<li>Review and approve invoices for payment. Coordinate vendor setup, processing and recording of MarCom invoices and credit card statements.</li>
<li>Continuously stay connected and informed about trends and best practices for marketing and trade shows. Share highlights with colleagues.</li>
</ul>
