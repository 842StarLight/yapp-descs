<h2>Senior Manager, Network & Security Solutions</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<div><b>Position Summary</b></div>
<div>We are seeking a Senior Manager of Network &amp; Security Solutions for a multi-faceted team encompassing Network Operations, and IT Security. The Senior Manager of Network &amp; Security Solutions would be the daily direct liaison between the VP of Network &amp; Security Solutions and the direct reports who lead those teams. An exciting role that has you building from the ground up a new Data Center and deploying brand new technologies from Cisco, HP and various other Security technologies.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>Areas of Responsibility</b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>Manage four direct reports whose teams are responsible for Networking, Network Operations, End Point Security, and Threat and Vulnerability Management.</div></li>
<li><div>
Provide guidance to the team of Network Security engineers responsible for NGFWs (Palo Alto Networks, Fortinet, and Checkpoint), IDS/IPS, WIPS, NAC, and security monitoring and network compliance solutions.</div></li>
<li><div>
Assist in keen oversight of the performance, stability, security, and operations of the network security solutions engineered by the team.</div></li>
<li><div>
Participate and partner with Information Security (InfoSec) in the design and implementation of network security strategy including cloud enablement and establishing next generation firewall features and capabilities</div></li>
<li><div>
Ensure the broader Network Services organization is adhering to Security best practices and principles.</div></li>
<li><div>
Drive technical projects and take responsibility for the overall planning, execution and success of complex initiatives.</div></li>
<li><div>
Collaborate with Information Security (InfoSec) and infrastructure teams to develop consistent technical solutions, implementation activities and support processes</div></li>
<li><div>
Participate in the design, lifecycle management, and total cost of ownership of security appliances, applications and infrastructure services</div></li>
<li><div>
Assist with on-call escalation as needed.</div></li>
<li><div>
Influence standards and platforms for future application development.</div></li>
<li><div>
Mentor team members in the network architecture and other solutions being implemented.</div></li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
</div>
</div>
<p></p>
<div>
Qualifications<div>
</div>
<ul>
<li><div>
9+ years in an active Security Engineering role with some of that time spent on large scale corporate networks.</div></li>
<li><div>
Experience designing, implementing and operating large scale corporate networks and network security solutions including NGFWs (Palo Alto Networks, Fortinet, Checkpoint), IDS/IPS, WIPS, NAC, security monitoring and network compliance solutions</div></li>
<li><div>
Thorough understanding of Enterprise authentication protocols (radius, dot1x, kerberos).</div></li>
<li><div>
Strong previous hands-on experience with routing and switching technologies.</div></li>
<li><div>
Solid understanding of site to site and client side VPN technologies and protocols.</div></li>
<li><div>
Proficiency with Network flow collection and visibility tools that lend themselves to Security and overall Network monitoring functions.</div></li>
<li><div>
Detailed knowledge of network (BGP,OSPF) transport (TCP,UDP) and application layer protocols (HTTP, FTP, DNS, SIP, RTP).</div></li>
<li><div>
Experience with SDWAN and cloud network security preferred.</div></li>
<li><div>
Prior experience as a tech lead or Manager for an engineering team.</div></li>
<li><div>
Excellent communication skills and an ability to work collaboratively with other infrastructure teams and developers across the firm.</div></li>
<li><div>
Strong resource and project management skills.</div></li>
</ul>
