<h2>VP, Customer Implementations</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Alogent has just been named to the <b>Georgia Fast 40</b>! This annual list recognizes the <b>fastest growing companies in the state</b> based on revenue and employee headcount, and is a testament to the entire company’s dedication to driving success and advancement within the industry!</p>
<p>
Alogent is a tenured financial software technology company. We provide dynamic cheque-image capture, document management and digital banking solutions to all segments of the banking industry ranging from credit unions and community banks to Tier-1 banks. Our proven solutions are designed to deliver strategic goals of reduced costs, increased efficiency, and enhanced services through any channel—in person, online or on the go. A rapidly growing company, Alogent provides opportunity for significant career growth for talented, dedicated professionals.</p>
<p>
The company is headquartered in Peachtree Corners, GA. Additional offices are located in Henderson, NV, Austin, TX and Carlsbad, CA, with regional teams across the US and internationally.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Job Overview</b><b>:</b></p>
<p>
The VP, Customer Implementations will be a dynamic leader on our Alogent Professional Services team and will oversee a team of project managers and implementation consultants for the implementation of Alogent products. In this role, the VP, Customer Implementations will report to and work closely with the SVP, Professional Services to collaborate on the continued build out of efficient and consistent delivery practices that maximizes results and outcomes.</p>
<p>
The Professional Services team is responsible for all new customer implementations as well as upgrades. As part of this, the role will be responsible for making improvements to the current implementation experience, both in terms of cost to the client as well as the overall client experience.</p>
<p>
This position can be located in one of our offices or remotely.</p>
<p><b><i>
Important Note: Authorized to work in the U.S. is a precondition of employment as we are unable to sponsor applicants for work visas.</i></b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b><b>:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Oversee and track all projects from kickoff to deployment, ensuring that all team members are adequately prepared; managing risk across your project portfolio; and, ensuring customer obligations are met.</li>
<li>Drive the overall execution of the team of delivery personnel including staffing, scheduling, methodology, project delivery, project margin and customer interaction.</li>
<li>Identify any yellow flags on projects and be willing to roll up your sleeves to help wherever needed to ensure customer success.</li>
<li>Establish and maintain strong customer relationships and act as an escalation point for high priority customer issues.</li>
<li>Establish priorities and align efforts across delivery teams for consistent and effective service delivery.</li>
<li>Proven ability to staff appropriately for immediate needs as well as forecast accurately for near term and mid-term resource projections to ensure resources are utilized most efficiently.</li>
<li>Partner with Sales leadership to position service offerings for new business opportunities.</li>
<li>Collaborate with senior leadership in order to meet the company's objectives.</li>
<li>Mentor direct reports and high-potential employees for project delivery, priority assessment, communication, customer service and delivery best practices.</li>
<li>Establish clear, measurable objectives for the team. Monitor, measure and report on these objectives regularly, providing coaching and discipline, as required.</li>
<li>Manage according to a cadence of accountability around operational metrics.</li>
<li>Develop effective processes, controls and measurements to drive improvements in efficiency, accuracy, and quality.</li>
<li>Perform administrative functions for direct reports including performance management and career development.</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>The above statements are intended only to describe the general nature of the job and should not be construed as an all-inclusive list of position responsibilities.</i></b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Requirements</b><b>:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>The role requires a solution-oriented mindset. This is someone who can both think creatively in terms of vision but who is grounded enough, with enough attention to detail, to ensure the vision can get translated to execution.</li>
<li>10+ years’ leadership experience with leading diverse teams in the implementation of software for credit unions or banks.</li>
<li>8+ years’ experience with leading teams in the implementation of online and mobile banking software.</li>
<li>2+ years’ experience with leading teams in the implementation of products in a data center.</li>
<li>Experience in building and streamlining Service delivery methodologies to meet aggressive timelines.</li>
<li>Strong financial acumen.</li>
<li>Technical proficiency that allows for an understanding of development lifecycles.</li>
<li>Strong Project Management skills, PMI certification preferred.</li>
<li>Ability to organize, manage and prioritize activities.</li>
<li>Demonstrated experience in managing distributed, remote teams.</li>
<li>Passionate about business results and quality, with a strong sense of accountability, metrics and ownership.</li>
<li>The ability to work cross functionally within the company in collaboration with sales, product development and support to create effective resolutions which work for both the client and the company.</li>
<li>This role will inherit a solid team and will bring their past management skills to develop further the talent on the team.</li>
<li>A team player with strong relationship building skills</li>
<li>Previous experience providing executive level reporting.</li>
<li>Excellent problem-identifying and problem-solving skills.</li>
<li>Excellent written and oral communication skills and ability to effectively communicate at all levels.</li>
<li>Ability to deal effectively with changing priorities in a diverse organization.</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree required.</li>
</ul>
