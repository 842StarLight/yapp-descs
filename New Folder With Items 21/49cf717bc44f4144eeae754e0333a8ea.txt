<h2>Logistics Fleet Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div><div>
<p>Fleet Manager/Indirect Logistics primary responsibility is managing Commercial Driver DOT Compliance, Private Fleet Vehicle Procurement and all indirect logistics related services while reducing cost YOY
</p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Serve as local subject matter expert for NA state and federal operating requirements for commercial vehicles and drivers
</li>
<li>Manage Commercial Driver qualification process and Commercial Driver Safety Training Program
</li>
<li>Measure violation incident rates for commercial drivers and equipment and develop/implement action improvement plans
</li>
<li>Work with NA manufacturing operations and support functions to forecast vehicle requirements
</li>
<li>Spec vehicles that meet specifically defined business requirements and forecasted needs
</li>
<li>Implement comprehensive vehicle acquisition program that delivers YOY operating cost improvements
</li>
<li>Cultivate and maintain business relationships with existing and new fleet related vendors
</li>
<li>Facilitate the disposal and sale of obsolete vehicles<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelors degree in business, logistics or related field
</li>
<li>Working knowledge of CSA reporting, Commercial Fleet Safety Program and state and federal commercial vehicle and driver laws
</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal skills, highly ethical in their interactions
</li>
<li>Detail oriented, organized and self-assured
</li>
<li>Ability to work with direct and indirect reports to achieve positive operational change<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Experience
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Progressive experience in Commercial Driver Fleet management and Vehicle procurement (minimum 5-7 years at manager level), preferably with a large multi-site manufacture
</li>
<li>Training and experience in managing DOT HOS and Commercial Driver management
</li>
<li>Experience in negotiating Commercial and light duty vehicle purchase, leasing, and maintenance agreements<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Other Knowledge, Skills or Abilities Required
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Working knowledge of Microsoft Office software
</li>
<li>Experience with SAP TM a plus
</li>
<li>Knowledge of industry leading ELD capture and reporting systems</li>
</ul>
