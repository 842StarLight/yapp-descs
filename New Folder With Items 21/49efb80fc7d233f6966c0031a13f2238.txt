<h2>Contract Administrator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Agfa HealthCare, a member of the Agfa-Gevaert Group, is a leading global provider of diagnostic imaging and healthcare IT solutions. The company has nearly a century of healthcare experience and has been a pioneer on the healthcare IT market since the early 1990's. Today, Agfa HealthCare designs, develops and delivers state-of-the-art systems for capturing, managing and processing diagnostic images and clinical/administrative information for hospitals and healthcare facilities, as well as contrast media solutions to enable effective medical imaging results. The company has sales offices and agents in over 100 markets worldwide.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<div>
The Contract Administrator is responsible for two components of the service contract business. The responsibilities by the Third-Party contract role include sourcing underpinning contracts, renewing underpinning contracts and securing underpinning contracts through creation of purchase requisitions and purchase orders. This role also includes SMA quote administration activities where tasks include a review of inventory, asset pricing and proposal creation from within the ASSM ServiceNow application.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>This position should be based in the Atlanta area<br>
</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>Third Party Contract Management</b>:</div>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Source new underpinning contracts for support of Agfa Healthcare products and services and assess against Service Maintenance Contract revenue.</li>
<li>Review assigned underpinning contracts and associated SMA’s to identify and process pending Under Pinning Contracts (UPCs) renewals.</li>
<li>Manage UPC’s within ASSM and ensuring that the organization meets or exceeds our cost guidelines to include the processing of PO need to fund the UPC</li>
<li>Process invoices for work orders from partners that provide T&amp;M services.</li>
<br>
<br>
</ul>
<p><b>SMA Quote Administration:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Create and process Service Maintenance quotations for assigned customers</li>
<li>Coordinate proposal creation in coordination with assigned Service Account Manager</li>
<li>Coordinate customer inventory with Configuration Coordinators to develop accurate SMA quotation</li>
<br>
</ul>
