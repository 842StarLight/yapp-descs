<h2>Associate Ecommerce Manager</h2>

<p><b>Position: Associate eCommerce Manager </b></p>
<p>Earth Inc. is looking for a talented and driven Associate eCommerce Manager to support our B2C business. The ideal candidate should have a firm understanding of e-commerce best practices, desktop vs mobile website design and usability implications, be familiar with content and product management.</p>
<p>You will be working side-by-side with our Senior eCommerce Manager in continuously growing our online business, and you will be supporting and improving our e-commerce website <i>https://earthshoes.com</i>.</p>
<p><i>Duties and Responsibilities: </i></p>
<ul>
<li>Support the e-commerce website developers in adding and reviewing tasks for new feature developments, tweaks or bugs.</li>
<li>Perform daily maintenance and merchandising updates on site</li>
<li>Manage and monitor the online product catalog (add/edit products, prices, sales, categorization) through the Content Management System or backend processes</li>
<li>Collaborate with various departments such as marketing, sales, customer support and operations</li>
<li>Identify and collaborate with best-of-breed service providers in enhancing the customer experience online</li>
</ul>
<p><i>What You Will Need</i></p>
<p>Bachelor's degree in Business, Marketing, Computer Science, Computer Information Systems, or related degree.</p>
<p>Preferred Experience: 2+ years in e-Commerce, Product Management, Content Management, Online Marketing, Web Development, or other relevant experiences.</p>
<ul>
<li>A high degree of internet savvy, with a curiosity and enthusiasm for new technologies, and customer experience</li>
<li>Basic understanding of HTML and CSS</li>
<li>Familiarity with concepts like SEO, SEM, PPC, ROI, RETARGETING, CMS, META TAGS, UX, ANALYTICS, B2C, B2B, UGC, ROAS</li>
<li>Familiarity with Google Analytics, Microsoft Excel, Microsoft Word</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Self-motivated, proactive, positive</li>
<li>Able to think both creatively and critically. Willing to think outside the box and challenge the status quo</li>
<li>Able to multi-task, manage many projects and drive multiple threads of work to completion</li>
<li>Able to quickly learn the necessary technical tools and new concepts</li>
</ul>

<li>learn and execute concepts and considerations to successfully manage an enterprise level online business</li>
<li>discover and master best-of-breed service providers that power features like customer reviews, user generated content, search/social/email marketing, online payments, and much more.</li>
