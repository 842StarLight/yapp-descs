<h2>Administrative Assistant, Part time</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Description:</b><br>
<b>POSITION SUMMARY:</b> The Administrative Assistant is responsible for supporting the duties of the Finance Manager and Human Resources Manager, while adhering to the co-op mission and the seven co-op principles.
<br><br>
<b>DUTIES:</b><br>
Assists FINANCE DEPARTMENT
<br>Post daily sales, payments and deposits.
<br>Record credit card transactions.
<br>Reconcile daily transactions with online reporting from credit card processor and bank.
<br>Assist with Accounts Payables.
<br>Maintain accuracy of vendor files and vendor accounts.
<br>Scan and file invoices.
<br>Maintain electronic filing system of invoices.
<br>Act as back-up in Finance Manager’s absence.
<br>Other duties as assigned.
<br><br>
Assists HR DEPARTMENT
<br>Process weekly payroll.
<br>Maintain accuracy of employee files.
<br>Participate in HR projects and activities.
<br>Attend meetings.
<br>Act as back-up in HR Manager’s absence.
<br>Other duties as assigned.
<br><br>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
<b>EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE REQUIREMENTS:</b><br>
Familiarity with and support of the co-op principles and mission.
<br>Possess strong written and verbal communication skills.
<br>Possess strong organizational skills and pay close attention to detail.
<br>Ability to read, write and perform basic math functions.
<br>Ability to multi-task in a fast-paced environment.
<br>Possess fluent computer skills including experience using Microsoft Office (Word, Excel, Google Docs and Google Sheets, etc.), Peachtree (and/or other accounting software) and Internet.
<br>Accounting/bookkeeping background helpful.
<br>Knowledge of human resources administration helpful.
<br><br>
<b>KNOWLEDGE/SKILLS/ABILITIES:</b><br>
Ability to exhibit professionalism in performance and personal demeanor.
<br>Ability to be a positive, independent, reliable and responsible worker.
<br>Ability to take initiative and to manage and prioritize workload.
<br>Complete understanding of confidentiality.
<br>Meets the flexibility needs for a creative work schedule.
<br>Meets expectations of regular, predictable attendance.
<br>Willingly accepts additional responsibilities.
<br>Strong sense of teamwork and flexibility.
<br><br>
<b>ENVIRONMENTAL FACTORS AND PHYSICAL REQUIREMENTS:</b><br>
While performing the duties of this job, the associate is required to have ordinary ambulatory skills sufficient to visit other locations and the ability to stand, walk, stoop, kneel, crouch and manipulate (lift, carry, move) light to medium weights of 10-60 pounds. Requires good hand-eye coordination, arm, hand, and finger dexterity, including ability to grasp and visual acuity to use a keyboard, operate equipment and read application information. The associate frequently is required to sit, reach with hands and arms, talk and hear.
<br>Regularity and consistency of attendance is essential to ensure high quality service and ultimately a productive department and company.</div>
