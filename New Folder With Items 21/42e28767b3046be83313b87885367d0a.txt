Position Summary
<br>ChargePoint is seeking a California Development Project Manager who will be responsible for accelerating ChargePoint's success building electric vehicle charging sites in California. This is a mid-level position reporting directly to the Senior Director of Construction. The California Development Project Manager will work closely with the Incentives Program Manager, and Incentive Sales Executive to guide strategic incentive projects from design all the way through construction and activation. The position is measured by and expected to support the achievement of all agreed upon project milestones for California based construction projects supported by state and local rebate programs.
<br><br>
What You Will Be Doing
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Coordinate and perform site walks to guide cost effective and expedient site builds</li>
<li>Coordinate and manage construction contractors. This includes cost negotiation, contract management, communication and coordination of project activities. Scheduled weekly meetings and diligent follow-ups are required to ensure construction related activities advance at all locations</li>
<li>Coordinate and manage relationships with key contracts at partner electric utilities. Scheduled weekly meetings and diligent follow-ups are required to ensure utility activities advance at all locations</li>
<li>Evaluate site host properties for the proper placement of EV chargers, considering factors such as site host preferences, where electricity is distributed onto the property, how close electrical supply is to areas under consideration for EV charger installation, availability of enough 3 phase power needed for product installation, ADA requirements etc.</li>
<li>Work with ChargePoint's sales operations and purchasing teams to provide subcontractors with purchase orders and manage shipment of materials</li>
<li>Conduct engineering and construction coordination activities (i.e. phone call with site host and installer) to ensure consistent deliverables and aligned expectations for all parties</li>
<li>Record and track project milestones to ensure installs are completed in a timely manner</li>
<li>Coordinate with internal team and external contractors to review, approve and execute any change orders for charger installations</li>
<li>Ensure that all contractors deliver to ChargePoint's strict standards for quality, safety and professionalism</li>
<li>Maintain project schedules by coordinating between site host, subcontractors, and utility planners</li>
<li>Expedite project close-out requirements including approval on as-built drawings, design revisions, and rough underground/electrical</li>
<li>Provide the project team with feedback to help improve processes and follow through to implement those improvements</li>
<li>This role requires up to 50% of travel in western U.S</li>
</ul>
