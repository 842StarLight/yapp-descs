<h2>Managing Digital Sales Director (Pacific)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>At Lonely Planet we live to travel. Everything we do is designed to inspire and enable travellers to get out there, connect with the world and have great experiences. Across all areas of Lonely Planet, we look for talented people who share our passion.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Position Overview</b></p>
<p>
The Account Manager is a highly collaborative role within the Digital Sales team and works to support the field sales team alongside our new product development and production teams to achieve global targets and objectives. He/she plays a pivotal role in helping to maintain and generate revenue through superior client service and relationship management of their accounts. The AM will be commercially-minded with a willingness to push boundaries in order to drive revenue for the business.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Key responsibilities include various aspects of digital and print media planning, presales strategy, relationship management and campaign operations. Account Managers are responsible for managing and maintaining client and agency relationships in order to align all parties for the purpose of building our client’s business. This position has frequent and on-going client communication and plays a pivotal role in helping generate revenue through digital, print and custom solutions.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Account Managers serve as the main point of contact for assigned accounts through all phases of sales cycle. AM’s may be involved in client pitches where required, on-going account management duties, and provide structure and support between pitch closing and management of delivery, including post-campaign reporting and project post-mortems.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Account management of designated customer relationships throughout RFP response process, which includes preparation, revision and ownership of digital/print media plan input within order management system to be presented to agency and advertiser clients.</li>
<li>Engage production team for creative brainstorming, project creation and delivery to be presented to agency and advertiser clients.</li>
<li>Ability to work well across teams including sales, ad ops, project management, new product development in order to ensure smooth delivery of campaigns.</li>
<li>Work with sales to qualify leads and product/ad ops to identify appropriate product mix to ensure profitable campaigns are sold in.</li>
<li>Campaign delivery and optimization management, working closely with ad ops team to track campaign’s delivery and performance as main point of client contact.</li>
<li>Timely and professional delivery of advertiser reporting including pacing/performance reports, screenshots, proof of implementation, campaign wrap-up decks and internal project post-mortems (as needed).</li>
<li>Communicate client changes or feedback through production team or third party vendors for creative campaign executions.</li>
<li>Work with ad ops/business ops to resolve discrepancy resolution, invoice clearance, flight changes and optimizations for campaigns.</li>
<li>Cross-media integration, understanding all the planning and executional nuances of building and delivering media plans or custom campaigns against all media types from digital to print and mobile.</li>
<li>Be considerate of the wider Lonely Planet business strategic objectives and look for solutions that will help further these goals and strengthen the Client Solutions vision.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>3-5 years’ experience in account management/media planning with a digital marketing or media organization.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Experience working within a fast paced sales environment.</li>
<li>High level of customer service and professionalism, and a natural understanding of how to effectively manage client and agency relationships.</li>
<li>Sound knowledge of the latest trends in the digital advertising industry and associated products &amp; processes.</li>
<li>Solid understanding of the sales environment including targets and success measures and an appreciation of the pressures and drivers associated with working in sales.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Exceptional knowledge of Lonely Planet brand, content, products &amp; platforms and the opportunities that exist for Client Solutions</li>
<li>Excellent organization skills with real attention to detail</li>
<li>Time management skills, with the ability to manage multiple pitches, tasks and projects simultaneously</li>
<li>Previous agency or publisher experience preferred</li>
<li>Experience with OMS OperativeOne, DFP &amp; Nativo Ad Servers, Salesforce, Comscore and Google Analytics a plus</li>
<li>IAB Certification a plus</li>
<br>
</ul>
