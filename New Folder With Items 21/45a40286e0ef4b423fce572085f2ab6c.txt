<h2>Supply Planner - Softwood Lumber (SWL)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>Weyerhaeuser’s Wood Products Supply Chain Organization is seeking a motivated, supply chain focused individual to join our team. This position reports to the Wood Products Supply Chain manager, in our Seattle, WA location. The successful candidate will provide supply chain expertise while building strong working relationships with customer service, manufacturing, sales and suppliers. If you love a fast paced and challenging environment that requires problem solving, analytical thinking and a focus on operational excellence then come join our team.<br>
<br>
<b>The Lumber Supply Planner will perform end-to-end duties in several of the following areas:</b><div>
<ul>
<li>
Supply Planning – Develop and maintain an optimized plan (supply and demand) that meets the businesses profit expectations for an 18 mill system</li>
<ul>
<li>
Monitor Regional Supply Targets and supply mix vs plan</li>
<li>
Ongoing Inventory Review</li>
<li>
Transportation Alignment / Issue Resolution</li>
<li>
Establish monthly sales targets based on supply and inventory targets.</li>
</ul>
<li>
Performance Metrics – develop and maintain a system to track key supply chain metrics</li>
<ul>
<li>
ATP, on-time shipments, Week 1,2 and 3 performance and aged inventory</li>
<li>
Prepare reports for the bi-weekly PIPE calls and monthly business review (MBR) for the Supply Manager</li>
<li>
Measuring value and performance</li>
</ul>
<li>
System Analysis
</li>
<ul><li>Develop processes to understand the root causes of variances and develop plans to address opportunities</li></ul>
<li>
Technology Support
</li>
<ul>
<li>Support Halco area model, PST, and future technology
</li>
<li>Uploads of supply plans into SAP – Quarterly and Annually</li>
<li>
Training PST and Halco
</li>
<li>Infor support
</li>
</ul>
<li>Project work
</li>
<ul><li>Participate in project work affecting SWL as required. Project work includes but is not limited to leading and supporting business driven lean events</li></ul>
<li>
Customs Compliance
</li>
<ul><li>Training and Documentation</li></ul>
</ul>
