<p><b>POSITION SUMMARY</b>
</p>
<p>The Website Administrator is responsible for photographing units and maintenance of those photos online. This position is a key asset to our sales operations. Pay range is $13.00-$15.00 hourly depending on experience.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>PRIMARY DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:
</b></p>
<p>This list of duties and responsibilities is not all inclusive and may be expanded to include other duties and responsibilities as management may deem necessary from time to time. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Photograph RV units and key features
</li>
<li>Download and upload unit photos on websites
</li>
<li>Monitor and maintain photos on various websites
</li>
<li>Ensure online photographs and inventory are accurate and clear
</li>
<li>Create photo inventory reports, as requested
</li>
<li>Contribute, or otherwise assist the team, as requested
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS, AND ABILITIES:</b>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Possess excellent written skills
</li>
<li>Ability to operate a camera and computer
</li>
<li>Methodical, accurate, and consistent attention to detail
</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal skills and able to work independently as well as part of an effective team Proficient in Microsoft Office and comfortable learning new software as needed

</ul>
