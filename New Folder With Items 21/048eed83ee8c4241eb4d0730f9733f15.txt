<h2>Sales Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Job Description</p>
<p><b>SUMMARY</b></p>
<p>Established in 2008 as a trading company in Vicenza, Italy; Teltos has evolved over the years by gaining insight into the desires of its customers and their needs. We have become an international producer of the highest quality quartz slabs. Our new headquarters in Guongdong, China uses cutting edge technology to bring to the market the greatest of durability and Italian aesthetic at an unprecedented value.</p>
<p>The Outside Sales Representative will be responsible for obtaining, building, and managing accounts within an assigned territory while providing exceptional customer service. Territory will be in the San Francisco Bay Area, CA.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Sales of company manufactured products and services to current and potential clients</li>
<li>Identify and contact sales prospects</li>
<li>Prepare presentations, proposals and sales tools.</li>
<li>Develop and maintain sales materials and current product knowledge.</li>
<li>Establish and maintain current and potential client relations</li>
<li>Identify and resolve client concerns.</li>
<li>Prepare a variety of status reports</li>
<li>Advise clients of new products and services.</li>
<li>Participate in marketing events such as seminars &amp; trade shows</li>
<li>Maintain daily records regarding any sales related activity.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p>JOB REQUIREMENTS</p>
<p>Persuasiveness and the ability to influence decision makers -</p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to develop and deliver presentations.</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal sales and communication skills.</li>
<li>Knowledge of advertising and sales promotion techniques.</li>
<li>Maintain a professional appearance and a positive company image to customers and general public.</li>
<li>Valid driver license</li>
<li>Willingness to work a flexible schedule and travel overnight occasionally.</li>
</ul>
