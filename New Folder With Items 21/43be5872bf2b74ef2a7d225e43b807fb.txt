<b>Job Summary:</b><br>
</p>
<p>Responsible for ensuring the execution of merchandise presentation plans and priorities for assigned departments. Ensures an excellent customer experience by engaging and interacting with all customers, and maintaining a clean and organized store.
</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:</b>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Role models exceptional customer service
</li>
<li>Creates a positive internal and external customer experience
</li>
<li>Promotes a culture of honesty and integrity; maintains confidentiality
</li>
<li>Accurately processes and prepares merchandise for the sales floor following company procedures and standards
</li>
<li>Maintains and upholds merchandising philosophy and follows established merchandising procedures and standards
</li>
<li>Maintains current, fresh, and fashionable features
</li>
<li>Takes an active role in training and mentoring Associates on merchandise guidelines, protection standards and established best methods
</li>
<li>Ensures Associates execute tasks and daily activities in accordance with store plan; reprioritizes as needed
</li>
<li>Communicates accurately and effectively with management and Associates when setting and addressing priorities; provides period progress updates
</li>
<li>Processes cash register transactions accurately and efficiently according to established policy and procedure
</li>
<li>Maintains all organizational, cleanliness, and recovery standards for the sales floor and participates in the maintenance/cleanliness of the entire store
</li>
<li>Provides and accepts ongoing recognition and constructive feedback
</li>
<li>Partners with Management on Associate training needs to increase effectiveness
</li>
<li>Ensures adherence to all labor laws, policies, and procedures
</li>
<li>Promotes credit and loyalty programs during customer interactions
</li>
<li>Supports and participates in store shrink reduction goals and programs
</li>
<li>Promotes safety awareness and supports maintenance of a risk-free environment
</li>
<li>Performs other duties as assigned
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements:</b>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Able to work a flexible schedule to support business needs, including nights and weekends
</li>
<li>Superior organizational skills with attention to detail
</li>
<li>Capable of handling multiple tasks at one time
</li>
<li>Ability to respond appropriately to changes in direction or unexpected situations
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Team player, working effectively with peers and supervisors to accomplish tasks
</li>
<li>Capable of lifting heavy objects with or without reasonable accommodation
</li>
<li>Strong communication skills
</li>
<li>Ability to train others
</li>
<li>One year retail and 6 months of leadership experience
</li>
</ul>
