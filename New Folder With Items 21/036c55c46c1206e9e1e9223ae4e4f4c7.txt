<h2>DIRECTOR OF E-COMMERCE</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Overview:</b><br>
The Director of E-Commerce will have direct responsibility for managing the Leesa direct to consumer business in North America (US and Canada), and a team of four. We are in an exciting time where we have recently completed a brand strategy refresh and are working on bringing this to life in 2020, in addition to focusing on our social impact messaging and B Corp status. This role includes direct oversight of the following functions: site merchandising, user experience/design, product development, and site operations.
<br><br>
This individual will own the annual operating plan and P&amp;L for the business, including the day to day management of site enhancements, analytics, optimization, and site refreshes. In addition, the role will be responsible for helping bring to life a new brand strategy through a site redesign and content enhancements. This includes direct accountability for improving key operating metrics such as conversion rate, cart abandonment rate, bounce rate, and average order value (AOV).
<br><br>
The ideal person for this role will be someone that has deep experience with successfully launching and redesigning multi-channel e-commerce businesses from end-to-end while providing a strategy that supports the revenue goals and profitability of Leesa. They also must possess a keen understanding of brand and performance marketing to drive revenue and traffic, be data-driven, customer centric, creative, and forward thinking.
<br><br>
<b>Job Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Develop a vision and strategy for Leesa.com that aligns with the Leesa brand and drives successful business results</li>
<li>Lead the execution and development of the e-commerce DTC business with operations in US and Canada</li>
<li>Manage the budgets and drive the P&amp;L to grow the online business. Work collaboratively within the organization to ensure execution of key initiatives that lead to increased market share and profitability</li>
<li>Manage, mentor, and lead a team across the areas of e-commerce strategy, site merchandising, site experience, web operations, and UX</li>
<li>Oversee the development and implementation of seasonal online marketing calendars to marry the offline marketing initiatives and market-specific strategies. Create online only content and events throughout the seasons which enable a tailored experience for the consumer while meeting/exceeding the weekly sales plan. Develop and manage the advertising and promotions budget for all such programs. Lead team in executing seasonal vision from concept to launch</li>
<li>Promote and execute the best online user experience methodologies that drive engagement and conversion. Develop an always on testing program for introducing new features and functionality, and ongoing conversion rate enhancements</li>
<li>Promote and execute quality assurance testing in order to minimize customer service issues and time to market</li>
<li>Manage external vendors, contracts, relationships and technical requirements to continue to develop and enhance the e-commerce platform</li>
<li>Lead a team that partners with the Technology team to implement roadmap strategy for Leesa and advocate on behalf of the brand</li>
<li>Partner closely with the Brand Marketing team to manage the content calendar and digital marketing initiatives for e-commerce, including home page, landing page, email, search, affiliate, performance and social media to support the brand and business objectives</li>
<li>Manage the end to end digital customer journey merging content and commerce to provide customers with the ultimate shopping and brand experience</li>
<li>Build brand recognition and social impact awareness for Leesa as the online destination amongst target customers, the industry and the media through increased onsite merchandising and marketing initiatives</li>
<li>Capitalize on evolving industry trends and technology that supports the brand vision and goals</li>
<li>Analyze and communicate results on key drivers on overall business performance; utilize findings to develop both tactical and strategic recommendations and execute against those strategies</li>
<li>Foster an innovative environment to ensure the brand obtains position as forward-thinking leaders in the e-commerce space</li>
</ul>
