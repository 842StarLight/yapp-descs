<h2>SAP Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>What you’ll be doing...</b>
</p>
<p>Verizon is Seeking SAP Security consultant that has in-depth knowledge and experience working with SAP enterprise software.</p>
<p>
In this role, you’ll be responsible for:</p>
<p>
Delivery &amp; Consulting</p>
<ul>
<li>
Leverage in-depth knowledge and experience working with SAP software.</li>
<li>
Provide the lead in major delivery projects serving as a security design authority and team lead, ensuring that solutions remain in scope and are aligned to agreed business requirements, appropriate delivery methodologies are followed and that effective governance is applied.</li>
<li>
Work with internal and external stake holders to understand business requirements and prepare appropriate technical solution designs at both high and low levels.</li>
<li>
Leverage SAP cross-component integration knowledge.</li>
<li>
Work with the Verizon sales teams to help define end-client business requirements and associated security services solutions.</li>
<li>
Support the definition, scope and estimation of PSS security services.</li>
<li>
Attend client meetings where applicable.</li>
<li>
Support the preparation of RFP responses and SOWs in areas that relate directly to PSS security services;</li>
<li>
Support and actively participate in the business development of the practice by identifying additional business opportunities within the current client base.</li>
</ul>
<p>
Additional responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Maintain day to day records of project work to allow accurate billing and reporting of consultancy time.</li>
<li>
Maintain effective network of business contacts within Verizon and their partner community.</li>
<li>
Maintain and grow their own knowledge to ensure this maintains fully relevant to the security services practice and its clients.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
What we’re looking for...</b>
</p>
<p>You’ll need to have:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree or four or more years of work experience.</li>
<li>
Four or more years of relevant work experience.</li>
<li>
Two or more years of experience with SAP User and Role Management, Authentication and SSO, Authorization.</li>
<li>
Technical experience with very strong skills in at least two or three of the technologies below:<ul>
<li>
SAPFinancialAccounting(FI)</li>
<li>
SAPControlling(CO)</li>
<li>
SAPSalesandDistribution(SD)</li>
<li>
SAPProductionPlanning(PP)</li>
<li>
SAPMaterialsManagement(MM)</li>
<li>
SAPQualityManagement(QM)</li>
<li>
SAPHumanCapitalManagement(HCM)</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Experience with building, maintaining, reviewing all configurations/settings/policies on chosen technologies;</li>
<li>
Experience of multi-vendor, managed outsourcing environments.</li>
</ul>
<p>
Even better if you have:</p>
<ul>
<li>
A degree.</li>
<li>
Strong documentation skills.</li>
<li>
Strong troubleshooting skills (technical and non-technical).</li>
<li>
Ability to review processes and procedures – and improve them.</li>
<li>
Ability to utilize excellent analytical and collaborative skills.</li>
<li>
SAP Certification.</li>
<li>
CISSP or relevant</li>
<li>
CISM</li>
<li>
Willingness to travel.</li>
</ul>
