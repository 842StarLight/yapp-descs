<h2 class="jobSectionHeader">
<b>Progression Fitness is searching for the next member of our team. We are a growing company in the fitness industry, </b><b><i>located</i></b><b> in Rochester, MN.</b>
</h2>
<p>We are looking for a compassionate, energetic individual who is capable of forming meaningful relationships with clients interested in improving their health. This position requires a strong ability to coach, lead, and inspire members in an individual setting.</p>
<p>
Ideally, this team member wants to fulfill 20-30 hrs/wk of Personal Training.</p>
<p>
Progression has an amazing facility: over 15,000 sq ft and three separate gym spaces under the same roof. We offer Personal Training, Group Classes, Nutrition Coaching, Hybrid Nutrition/Fitness Challenges, Olympic Lifting, Kettlebell, Yoga, and Obstacle Course Race training.</p>
<p>
We are a tight team of fitness professionals all driving toward a set of common goals. As a member of our coaching team, this position will have the opportunity to earn a free membership as well as additional income-earning <i>opportunities</i>.</p>
<p>
The purpose of the Personal Trainer position at Progression Fitness is to build a client base that contributes to revenues <i>by</i> maintaining an existing client base, marketing programs and services, and utilizing the highest quality of customer service which promotes member wellness and creates a safe, enjoyable, and positive environment.</p>
<p><b>Essential Functions and Responsibilities.</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Maintain a personal commitment to meeting the needs of members and guests.</li>
<li>Implement safe and effective exercise protocols for client programming</li>
<li>Being on time (15 minutes prior to start) and prepared for all sessions</li>
<li>Follow sales protocol (for individuals and groups)</li>
<li>Utilizing our software system for scheduling and servicing appointments</li>
<li>Energize, inspire, lead and coach members of all skill levels through workouts</li>
<li>Participate in all <i>staff</i> related meetings and other required activities.</li>
<li>Behave as a role model <i>by</i> leading and promoting a healthy lifestyle.</li>
<li>Actively market and promote personal training to acquire new members and retain existing members.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
<b>Required Qualifications and </b><b><i>Experience</i></b><b>:</b><br>
</div>
<ul>
<li>NCCA-accredited and/or nationally recognized Personal Training Certification such as: NETA, ACSM, ACE, NSCA. CrossFit Level 1 Certification is accepted. Other approved and accredited certifications will be considered.</li>
<li>Certifications required within 60 days of hire: CPR/PR, First Aid, AED.</li>
<li>Demonstrated customer service skills and verbal communication skills.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to <i>work</i> with various age groups and ability levels.</li>
<li>Capable of responding to safety and emergency situations.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>

<li>1 <i>year</i> <i>experience</i> in the health and fitness field.</li>
<li>1 <i>year</i> <i>experience</i> Personal Training and Group Training.</li>
</ul>
