<h2>National Accounts Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Customer Acquisition, Sales, and Retention:</p>
<ul>
<li>Launch a new HD truck service product nationally</li>
<li>Pursue and close established sales opportunities from OEM approvals</li>
<li>Train, educate, and support both new and existing customers on product use and technical functionality</li>
<li>Coordinate delivery, training, and travel schedules for new sales</li>
<li>Keep management informed by submitting activity and results reports such as daily call reports, weekly work plans, and monthly and annual territory analyses</li>
</ul>
<p>OEM and Technical Market Development and Sales</p>
<ul>
<li>Consult with HD truck manufacturers for production and aftermarket approvals and sales</li>
<li>Work with consultants on OEM sales and technical approvals</li>
<li>Represent company and products in relevant trade organizations and trade shows</li>
<li>Network with industry leaders to establish technical standards relevant to product line</li>
</ul>
<p>General Marketing and Sales:</p>
<ul>
<li>Assist senior management with pricing, sales goals, and profit metrics</li>
<li>Update management on market size, competitive products, and other market forces</li>
<li>Create sales and marketing materials needed for new product line</li>
<li>Work directly with customers to find potential product improvements and opportunities</li>
<li>Establish and document product demonstration and training procedures</li>
</ul>
<p>Competitive Environment and Product Development &amp; Quality</p>
<ul>
<li>Communicate and provide information on any marketplace changes affecting our products (ex.: competitive situations, pricing, new products, product improvements, etc.)</li>
<li>Report to management immediately any quality issues heard in the field</li>
<li>Review RMAs monthly to identify quality problems and/or recurring product issues</li>
<li>Assess problems areas, then provide and suggest solutions, potentially including existing product changes or new product development</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Job requirements: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>2 years’ automotive or heavy duty/diesel truck service industry experience</li>
<li>Technical sales experience, including hands-on product demonstration experience</li>
<li>MS Office competency and excellent written communication skills</li>
<li>Excellent verbal communication</li>
<li>Available for frequent travel</li>
<li>Mobility—specifically, the ability to spend most of the day on your feet, bend, and squat repeatedly</li>
<li>Ability to lift up to 50 lbs.</li>
<li>Clean driving record and background check</li>
<li>Authorized to live and work in the US</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Preferred Qualifications: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>4-year degree, 2-year technical degree, or military service technical experience</li>
<li>Spanish fluency</li>
<li>CRM software experience</li>
<li>Commercial Driver’s license (CDL)</li>
<li>Experience with HD/diesel alignment systems or other garage equipment.</li>
<li>New product development experience</li>
<li>Proven sales closer</li>
</ul>
