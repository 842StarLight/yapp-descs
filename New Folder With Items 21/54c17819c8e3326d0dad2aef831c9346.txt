<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities include the following. Other duties may be assigned.</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Plan night shift work assignments and equipment allocation to meet production goals.</li>
<li>Schedule associates across case pick, bottle pick, truck loading, and replenishment responsibilities for optimum workload balance.</li>
<li>Release and monitor waves as products are picked and loaded, responding quickly to any issues in case or bottle pick areas.</li>
<li>As staffing needs require, step in quickly and effectively to cover warehouse worker responsibilities.</li>
<li>Where applicable, oversee the daily use of warehouse technology (WMS) as well as picking technology (voice picking) to ensure proper use and compliance with picking standards.</li>
<li>Ensure warehouse safety and housekeeping procedures are followed.</li>
<li>Plan and execute ongoing cross-training program to ensure workforce is flexible, and able to respond to unexpected shortages during the shift.</li>
<li>Continuously improve knowledge and skills. Stay current with developments within the manufacturing and distribution field and share expertise with others.</li>
<li>Assist in the setting of process improvement goals for warehouse employees to improve accuracy and efficiency.</li>
<li>Provide safety tips and recognize associates for working safely.</li>
<li>Oversee inspections of material handling equipment; ensure equipment is properly maintained. Promptly escalate any mechanical issues in order to minimize downtime.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Supervisory Responsibilities</b><br>
Manages subordinate supervisors and associates in warehouse. Carries out supervisory responsibilities in accordance with policies and applicable laws. Responsibilities include interviewing, hiring, and training employees; planning, assigning, and directing work; appraising performance; rewarding and disciplining employees; addressing complaints and resolving problems.</p>
