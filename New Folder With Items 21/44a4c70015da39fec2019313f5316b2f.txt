<h2>Security Engineer I</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>Expedia<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Position Overview</b></h2>
<p>
As a Security Engineer, you will be a key member of the Information Security team, who will guide in designing, building, and deploying security solutions for Expedia Partner Solutions (EPS), a B2B brand of Expedia Group. You will work hands-on with our software engineers and operations team to actively identify and address security risks. You'll be responsible for automating vulnerability scans; assessing, prioritizing and driving remediation of vulnerabilities; driving next generation security operations approaches/tools and producing automated dashboards to measure the effectiveness of the program. You share a real passion for engineering solutions to complex security problems, while minimizing friction and improving productivity.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
As Expedia Group’s B2B brand, our mission is to create new opportunities and work relentlessly to fuel our partners’ growth. We are the fastest-growing affiliate network ($4 billion in sales and counting), we work with over 10,000 partners in 33 countries to turn their web traffic into hotel bookings and happy customers.</p>
<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
What you'll do:</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>
Work closely with and encourage engineering teams to establish, implement and promote security standards to properly secure systems, applications and networks</li>
<li>
Responsible for developing and documenting security related processes and procedures to help secure, mature and/or improve the security of the environment</li>
<li>
Respond to alerts from security tools and take necessary actions to mitigate the security risk</li>
<li>
Perform network and application vulnerability scanning activities, helping to ensure full coverage of environments and assets</li>
<li>
Review, prioritize, and mitigate vulnerabilities including patching on systems and environments including guiding teams towards automation</li>
<li>
Perform log reviews, correlate suspicious activity, identify false positives and alert on key security metrics</li>
<li>
Promote security awareness across the organization</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Who you are:</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor's degree in Information Security or related field</li>
<li>
2+ years of hands on or working knowledge with Endpoint protection, Application security, Malwares, File Integrity Monitoring and Logging &amp; Monitoring</li>
<li>
2+ years' experience in at least one high-level programming language such as Python, Ruby or Java</li>
<li>
Experience with containers, scripting, automation and cloud security is desired</li>
<li>
Knowledge of Information Security frameworks, processes, technologies, and practices, including OWASP, PCI DSS, NIST and ISO27xxx standards</li>
</ul>
