<p><b>Who We Are: </b></p>
<p>Cardinal Financial is a nationwide direct mortgage lender whose mission is to prove that homeownership is possible for everyone. By bringing an open-minded approach to an often closed-minded industry, we're able to embrace every unique financial situation differently in order to craft the best possible loans for our borrowers. We pride ourselves on providing excellent service backed by our groundbreaking technology, and these two components of our process come together to complete a simple, personalized mortgage experience. We tell our customers and our partners that anything can be reimagined. So why not your career? Looking to join a company that values its people, innovates and expands on its proprietary technology, and is growing at a ridiculous rate?! Apply below!</p>
<p><b>Who We Need: </b></p>
<p>The Outbound Marketing Agent will support consumer direct LO’s by working with mortgage consumers, driving qualified consumers to Loan Officers, and by reaching out to customers who have inquired online and/or who have been pre-screened for certain mortgage products. Agents will help drive a consistent flow of quality leads to mortgage Loan Officers. After completing the Outbound Agent introductory period, the successful associate will be promoted to the Associate Loan Originator program, where he/she will receive training and licensure with the eventual step of attaining a position as licensed Mortgage Loan Originator.</p>
<p><b>What You Will Do: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Make outbound calls and answers incoming calls from consumers who have expressed interest and are inquiring about mortgage loans and/or home equity lines, and determine customer eligibility.</li>
<li>Maintains a high volume of outgoing phone calls on a daily basis.</li>
<li>Transfers qualified prospects to mortgage bankers</li>
<li>Effectively builds rapport with consumers on the phone; conveying confidence and presence on the phone; consulting with consumers to identify needs, create urgency, overcome objections, and identify the best options for the customer.</li>
<li>Conveys strong understanding of mortgage offerings and home lending guidelines, Cardinal products and services, and the advantages of each to potential clients.</li>
<li>Displays a strong command of the Company systems, communications and software utilized as an Outbound Marketing Agent</li>
<li>Effectively handles “problem” applications and escalations.</li>
<li>Remains positive, focused, and even-tempered in a fast-paced, time sensitive environment and in challenging situations.</li>
<li>Meets or exceeds team performance goals.</li>
<li>Participates and comprehends industry and company training and instruction as needed.</li>
<li>Utilizes CRM, chat, and outbound dialing systems along with other company tools.</li>
<li>Maintains excellent working relationships with all members of the Cardinal Financial Team.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>

<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Excellent telephone, communication (oral and written), interpersonal and organizational skills.</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal skills with demonstrated customer service skills.</li>
<li>Excellent computer skills; Microsoft Office, Excel and Google Apps experience.</li>
<li>Ability to navigate between multiple computer programs, dual monitors, while speaking with clients.</li>
<li>Ability to work well with a team environment and take constructive criticism, coaching and feedback on a consistent basis.</li>
<li>Highly detailed and organized with the ability to multi-task and work in a very fast paced environment.</li>
<li>Ability to exercise discretion on sensitive or confidential matters.</li>
<li>General Mortgage and Cardinal Product offerings knowledge.</li>
<li>Experience in phone sales / telesales position and/or mortgage industry experience- or combination of both is preferred, but not required.</li>
</ul>
