Requirements:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Responsible for growing your individual Book of Business by proactively generating new qualified leads.</li>
<li>
Identify, qualify, sort, and track leads.</li>
<li>
Aggressive, energetic self-starter who enjoys working in a quota-driven environment</li>
<li>
Minimum of 100 outbound prospecting calls day</li>
<li>
Exceptional cold-call prospecting using consultative phone skills, including the ability to clearly articulate, inspire, project confidence, influence, overcome objections, and remain resilient throughout</li>
<li>
Research and document relevant contact information for existing leads/keeping contact information update in Salesforce</li>
<li>
Utilize Salesforce to ensure activities are documented properly, opportunities are forecasted accurately and adequate information regarding the prospect and/or opportunity is communicated.</li>
<li>
Maintain knowledge of market conditions and competitive activities.</li>
</ul>
