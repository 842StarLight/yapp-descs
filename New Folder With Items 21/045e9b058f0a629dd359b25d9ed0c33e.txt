SUMMARY</div>
<div><div>
<div>
<p>KTGY's Retail Studio is searching for a Project Manager to manage our national retail account projects. This studio is a tight knit group that works collaboratively with all KTGY offices and studios to deliver neighborhoods of the future. We're seeking an experienced PM with a depth of experience moving projects through design development, construction documentation and construction administration. Coordinating with clients and consultants, the ideal candidate is organized, articulate and confident managing a project team. If you would describe yourself as someone who is meticulous about details and enjoys working to ensure project quality, send us your qualifications!</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Additional Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 7 years of work experience in a professional architectural firm, with 3+ years as a Project Manager.</li>
<li>Experience in retail architecture, specifically tenant prototype rollout and retail developer work.</li>
<li>Ability to lead and mentor a team through QA/QC of CD’s.</li>
<li>Proficient knowledge of code requirements.</li>
<li>Good documentation and organizational skills.</li>
<li>Strong written and verbal communication skills.</li>
<li>Understanding and proficiency using the following software:
<ul>
<li>Revit</li>
<li>AutoCad</li>
<li>Sketchup</li>
<li>Adobe Suite</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>

<div>COMPANY OVERVIEW</div>
<div><div>
<br>
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Founded in 1991, KTGY Architecture + Planning is one of the nation’s foremost residential and mixed-use design firms, combining design and technical excellence to provide superior service. KTGY seamlessly delivers innovation, artistry and attention to detail across multiple studios, ensuring that clients and communities get the best the firm has to offer no matter the building type or location. From city streets to master-planned destinations, we design the neighborhoods of the future.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>KTGY Irvine is a large multi-studio office with work in the areas of planning, retail, high-density, mixed-use, age-restricted and service enriched, affordable, modular and for-sale. KTGY is a highly collaborative environment and you can often find our offices or studios working together to provide superior solutions of immediate and lasting value. Here you may find people playing ping pong in the founder’s room or riding bikes to the local lunch spot.</p>
