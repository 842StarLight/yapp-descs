<h2>Senior Application Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>ESTECO North America, Inc. has an opening for a <b>Senior Application Engineer</b> to perform data analysis for various engineering datasets from simulation and testing. Visualize data for engineers in the automotive and aerospace engineering domain.<br>
</p>
<p>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The main responsibilities will be:<br>
</p>
<ul>
<li><p>provide application support for ESTECO software platforms (modeFRONTIER and VOLTA) to ESTECO North America customers (industry sectors include Automotive, Aerospace, Consumer Appliances, Oil &amp; Gas);</p></li>
<li><p>
understand customer domain problems and develop and implement engineering optimization solutions using ESTECO technology;</p></li>
<li><p>
provide application support to customers and guide them on Multidisciplinary Design Optimization (MDO) strategies and Design Exploration methods;</p></li>
<li><p>
work with customers to integrate third-party Computer Aided Engineering (CAE) tools in modeFRONTIER in order to enable CAE process automation;</p></li>
<li><p>
assist and conduct technical training and workshops for new and existing customers;</p></li>
<li><p>
assist and conduct product demonstrations for new customers and contribute to case studies;</p></li>
<li><p>
assist and conduct pre-sales support activities;</p></li>
<li><p>
collaborate with colleagues in other ESTECO offices and partner companies.</p></li>
</ul>
<br>
<p>
The ideal candidate will have:</p>
<ul>
<li><p>
a Bachelor's or Master's Degree in an engineering or applied science discipline;</p></li>
<li><p>
3 years working experience in this field;</p></li>
<li><p>
experience of commercially used CAE and CAD tools;
</p></li>
<li><p>excellent written and verbal communication skills.<br>
</p></li>
</ul>
<p>Candidates with the following skills will have a distinct advantage:</p>
<ul>
<li><p>
knowledge of Optimization and Statistical Methods;</p></li>
<li><p>
programming and scripting skills;</p></li>
<li><p>
a proactive attitude and willingness to learn.</p></li>
</ul>
