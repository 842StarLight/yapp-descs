<div><b>Job Summary</b></div>
<div>The National Account Manager is primarily responsible for managing strategic customer accounts at a national level.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>CORE RESPONSIBILITIES:
</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Maximize revenue by selling all facets of the hotel, both orally and in written form to previous, current and potential clients.</li>
<li>
Set and monitor sales targets for regional account managers</li>
<li>
Network with key customers to identify and address specific needs</li>
<li>
Develop and maintain relationships with customers to ensure long-term success</li>
<li>
Act as the liaison between customers and internal teams ensuring clients’ requirements are met</li>
<li>
Identify and attract prospective strategic customers by making presentations, developing contracts, negotiating and closing sales.</li>
<li>
Ensure brand consistency</li>
<li>
Maintain high customer satisfaction ratings, according to company standards</li>
<li>
Liaise with Sales and Marketing departments to set and implement strategies for new products</li>
<li>
Stay up to date with internal and external developments and suggest new ways to increase sales</li>
<li>
Prepare information for, meet with and entertain clients as deemed appropriate by potential business from that account.</li>
<li>
Research destinations and available services for accommodations to find travel deals and evaluate services to find the best quality for the price</li>
<li>
Resolve any problems with travel or accommodations by using company resolution protocol and communicating with all relevant parties</li>
<li>
Handle credit card programs and charges</li>
<li>
Process T&amp;E reports and handle reimbursements</li>
<li>
Communicate both verbally and in writing to provide clear direction to all departments in the hotel to ensure high quality of service to customers.</li>
<li>
Participate in daily business review meeting, pre-convention meetings, training and other sales-related meetings as required.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Job Requirements</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Must have experience in national account manager at similar size and quality of hotel.</li>
<li>
Experience in Pharmaceutical, Healthcare, Medical, Vertical Markets preferred.</li>
<li>
3+ years of past sales experience preferred</li>
<li>
Advanced knowledge of hotel operations, including marketing plans, budget forecasting, quality assurance programs, and long-range planning.</li>
<li>
Excellent interpersonal and sales related skills.</li>
<li>
Ability to work long and irregular hours, weekends, Holidays and Evenings</li>
<li>
Delphi Sales and Catering Computer Software, MS Word, Excel, and ability to use e-mail and the Internet.</li>
<li>
Ability to handle multiple tasks and make decisions in a fast-paced client driven environment.</li>
<li>
Excellent communication and organization skills.</li>
<li>
Basic computer skills, including, but not limited to, Microsoft Word, Excel and Delphi.</li>
</ul>
