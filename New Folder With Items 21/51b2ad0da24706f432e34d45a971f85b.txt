GENERAL SUMMARY: The Marketing Strategist is responsible for the day-to-day conduit between Resource Advancement (Marketing, Donor Relations, Creative Studio, Faith Engagement) and CRISTA Camps. Strive to meet the marketing and fundraising needs of Camps to support their financial goals, and effectively communicate the mission, vision, and values of each ministry. Collaborate with ministry staff to identify prioritized marketing, fundraising, and operational needs, then work alongside visual designers, writers, videographers and web developers in CRISTA’s Creative Studio to create compelling, effective communications that will inspire respective audiences for CRISTA Camps.<br>
<br>

<p><b>
DUTIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Work collaboratively with Camps personnel and Resource Advancement leadership to develop fluid strategic plans for the marketing and fundraising of each ministry. Identify joint goals, operational tactics and action steps to meet those goals, full production timelines, and Key Performance Indicators to measure progress throughout the fiscal year.</li>
<li>
Write and edit compelling sales and fundraising communications for different audiences to increase conversion, support and awareness to ensure viability through resources including but not limited to: sales collateral, donor-centered fundraising appeals and newsletters, brochures, social media, website content, feature stories, interviews, video production, photo production, and emails.</li>
<li>
Monitor progress toward the annual marketing/fundraising plan on a weekly basis, and communicate progress with leaders from Camps and Resourced Advancement. Formulate contingency plans when metrics indicate revenue and/or production goals are in danger of not being met.</li>
<li>
Serve as the daily contact with CRISTA Creative Studio to ensure that production needs are on track. Ensure unity across different platforms: web, print, mobile, etc. Facilitate the review and approval process for appropriate projects between Camps/Senior Living and Resource Advancement.</li>
<li>
Participate and contribute to appropriate staff meetings, campaign creative planning meetings, production meetings, devotions, etc.</li>

Work collaboratively with supervisor, coworkers, and customers.</li>

Perform other related duties as assigned.</li>
