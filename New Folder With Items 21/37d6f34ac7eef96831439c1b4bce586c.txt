<h2>Front Desk Agent-Comfort Inn & Suites</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>The Comfort Inn &amp; Suites South Burlington is now hiring for a Full-Time and Part-Time Guest Service Agent, responsible for providing courteous and professional care in all aspects of guest reception. Our Guest Service Agents are the first point of contact upon arrival and the last person to wish them well on their way upon departure. We strive to provide excellent customer service and hope to offer a happy and comfortable stay for every guest we serve. We are looking for organized, attentive, and career oriented individuals to join our team.</p>
<p><b>Primary Responsibilities Included, but not limited to: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Books guest reservations and/or coordinates with reservation center</li>
<li>Registers guests into the hotel in a prompt and courteous manner; prepares for group check-ins, check-outs, and VIP arrivals.</li>
<li>Greets, registers, and assigns rooms to guests- Handles confidential information, including guest records, with a high degree of integrity-</li>
<li>Answers and directs calls as appropriate; takes guest messages with accuracy</li>
<li>Offers and properly handles requests for wake-up calls</li>
<li>Records pertinent guest information in the pass on log</li>
<li>Checks guests in and out of the hotel; processes customer payments according to established policies and procedures.</li>
<li>Answers phone in a prompt, efficient, and friendly manner.</li>
<li>Promotes hotel services, facilities and outlets and becomes informed and knowledgeable of upcoming events/ functions in the hotel and in the surrounding area.</li>
<li>Demonstrates thorough knowledge of the property and amenities including: room categories, room rates, packages, and promotions in order to accurately serve the guest's needs.</li>
<li>Is knowledgeable and enthusiastic about VT and the surrounding areas and is able to refer guests to local attractions and outlets.</li>
<li>Arranges for the appropriate transportation of guests to and from the hotel when necessary.</li>
<li>Uses creative reasoning to overcome guest objections and concerns and to ensure 100% satisfaction.</li>
<li>Resolves minor guest complaints to the satisfaction of the customer; informs supervisor of major problems, complaints, disturbances or unhappy guests.</li>
<li>Respond quickly to guest requests or complaints in a friendly manner and appropriate action is taken.</li>
<li>Follows up to ensure guest satisfaction.</li>
<li>Completes daily paperwork to include cashing out, documenting all adjustments, miscellaneous postings, paid outs, charges and check-outs.</li>
<li>Balances cash bank at open and closing of each shift.</li>
<li>Provides a professional image at all times through appearance and dress</li>
<li>Follows company policies and procedures at all times</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Skills and Qualifications: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Excellent Customer Service and Communication Skills</li>
<li>Positive, Energetic Attitude at all times</li>
<li>Employs empathy and understanding to guest needs</li>
<li>Proficient in the use of Windows based office software</li>
<li>Sales Orientated</li>
<li>Exhibits good judgment and decision making skills</li>
<li>Excellent Knowledge of surrounding area</li>
<li>Prior Hotel Experience Preferred</li>
<li>May be asked to work overtime</li>
<li>Will be required to work weekends and holidays</li>
<li>Will be required to work in fast paced environment</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Types: Full-time, Part-time</p>
