<p>Overview:
</p>
<p>Reporting to the Revenue Director, this newly created position is a critical and highly visible role within the organization. This individual will support the ""quote-to-cash"" cycle on a global basis, review and approve non-standard terms per the company's policies, and ensure all sales contracts are accounted for under U.S. GAAP (ASC 606). An important aspect will be identifying and implementing process improvements in the quote-to-cash cycle to minimize friction while supporting the company's order acceptance and revenue policies. The successful candidate will be able to work and communicate effectively in a team environment, interacting with our sales, legal, and product departments during the quote and customer negotiation process, and with accounting department colleagues’ post-sales. Further, this individual will be instrumental in helping manage a global accounting team in support of the company’s strategic goals around the globe.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Responsibilities include but not limited to:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Manage and operationalize the revenue recognition and accounting aspects of the quote to cash business processes including month-end related close activities (account reconciliations, journal entries, preparation/review of estimates/reserves, etc.)
</li>
<li>Ownership over the company’s revenue management system and related month-end processes
</li>
<li>Understand business drivers and requests for non-standard terms, and their effect on financial results to assist in attaining the best accounting and business outcome
</li>
<li>Participate in contract negotiation in collaboration with sales, legal, and business operations (deal desk) teams
</li>
<li>Ability to resolve complex accounting problems with minimal supervision
</li>
<li>Evaluate complex sales contracts to determine the appropriate treatment in accordance with U.S. GAAP (ASC 606), documenting management’s conclusions with appropriate references to accounting codification
</li>
<li>Actively drive revenue related process improvement initiatives
</li>
<li>Assist the Director of Revenue on various projects on an ad hoc basis.
</li>
<li>Collaborate with Internal Audit team to establish and ensure compliance with SOX requirements
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Qualifications:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Accounting or Finance
</li>
<li>Certified Public Accountant (preferred)
</li>
<li>2+ years with a Big 4 or mid-tier public accounting firm
</li>
<li>4+ years’ experience with revenue/technical accounting within a public company in software or technology (preferred)
</li>
<li>Strong technical accounting background. Experience with ASC 606 a plus
</li>
<li>Excellent written, and verbal communication skills with the ability to work cross functionally
</li>
<li>Very high attention to detail
</li>
<li>Ability to meet tight deadlines, and prioritize contending workloads
</li>
<li>Familiarity with Salesforce, RevSym and /or NetSuite preferable, but not required
</li>
<li>Excellent written and oral communication skills and the ability to communicate effectively across all levels</li>
</ul>
