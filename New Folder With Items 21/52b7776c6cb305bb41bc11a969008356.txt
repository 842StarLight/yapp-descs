<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Business Need / Purpose of Role<br>
</b></h2>
<div></div>
<div><div><div><div>Under the direction of the Director of Sales / Technical Support, the System Optimization Engineer is responsible for identifying Brownfield opportunities for all products and services to all clients within the defined territory in accordance with company policies and procedures. The System Optimization Engineer will engage in close collaboration with Product Management and Area Sales Managers. The System Optimization Engineer is primarily a process analyst which will work with plant operation managers and metallurgists and identify how implementing new equipment / technology will improve the plant performance and lower operational costs and improve plant efficiency.<br>
</div></div></div></div>
