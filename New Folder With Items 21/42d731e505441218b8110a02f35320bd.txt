<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities and Qualifications
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>The Internet Manager is responsible for ensuring customer retention, unit sales and profitability through online vehicle sales.
</div>
<div>The Internet Manager will work directly with employees and customers to develop relationships and help bridge the disconnect in the sales process via online.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Forecast annual and monthly goals and objectives for the Internet department</li>
<li>
Coordinate Internet promotions and advertising</li>
<li>
Research auto-buying services on the Internet and acts as liaison with any services the dealership uses to promote its site or advertise</li>
<li>
Update the Web site frequently to attract new and repeat visitors</li>
<li>
Explain, implement, and enforce dealership policies, emphasizing customer satisfaction and positive dealership image</li>
<li>
Ensure proper follow up of all potential buyers by developing, implementing, and monitoring a prospecting and sales control system</li>
<li>
Promote teamwork in a customer-focused sales environment throughout the sales, delivery and follow-up process</li>
<li>
Follow up on all Internet leads daily</li>
<li>
Build rapport with customers to build a base of referrals to establish customer network</li>
<li>
Provide training and support to the sales staff on Phone &amp; Internet skills and assist in closing sales</li>
<li>
Help manage inventory online and incentives</li>
<li>
Work with third party vendors such as AutoTrader and Cars.com to generate and boast lead counts</li>
<li>
Prior Automotive sales experience required</li>
<li>
Be able to create and maintain customer relationships</li>
<li>
Strong computer &amp; phone skills (Internet, MS Outlook, CRM)</li>
<li>
Be able to coach, development and motivate sales staff</li>
<li>
Desk deals for sales professionals and take T.O.’s to maximize deal potential</li>
<li>
Deal with local and national vendors and manufacturer representatives</li>
<li>
Must be at least eighteen years of age</li>
<li>
Must have a valid Driver’s License
</li>
<li>Must be able to pass pre-employment screens (background &amp; drug test)</li>
</ul>
