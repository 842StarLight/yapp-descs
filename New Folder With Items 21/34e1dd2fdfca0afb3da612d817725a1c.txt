<h2>Senior Case Designer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Talented people. Ideal location. Demonstrated core values—Innovation, Excellence, Collaboration, Leadership, and Advocacy. We are a community of driven, high achievers who care about each other, our communities, and the clients we have the opportunity to serve.</p>
<p>
Our history of creativity and differentiation sets us apart. Our LEED certified offices are in the heart of Portland’s Pearl District. We show our appreciation for one another, especially when it comes to serving our Member Firms and their clients.<br>
Our proprietary products and process solutions are innovative with unique features and flexible options, disrupting the life insurance industry as we advocate for individuals, families, and businesses who rely on M Member Firms to help them achieve their goals. We understand that we succeed only when our clients succeed.</p>
<p>
As a Senior Case Designer, you will proactively partner with your external Insurance Specialist to provide support for life, disability, and long term care insurance products, including M Financial’s 25+ proprietary offerings, across a multi-carrier platform to assigned financial advisors within your territory. Along with the Insurance Specialist, you will be responsible for driving sales by marketing M Financial as a premier provider of insurance and protection products.</p>
<p><b>
Day To Day, the Senior Case Designer Will</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Provide exceptional customer service while acting in the client’s best interest at all times.</li>
<li>
Assist the Insurance Specialist in identifying and developing sales opportunities.</li>
<li>
Assess, prepare, and recommend a given product/solution based on client’s stated objectives, as well as be prepared to answer any product specific or technical questions.</li>
<li>
Present basic life insurance sales ideas/concepts via Skype, GoTo Meeting or over the phone.</li>
<li>
Distribute sales marketing materials and generate specific sales illustrations.</li>
<li>
Explain the life insurance application process in a clear, concise, and accurate manner.</li>
<li>
Utilize illustration tools, CRM systems, and other identified tools to track daily activities and report on open opportunities.</li>
<li>
Coordinate with internal teams to help ensure a smooth end-to-end client experience.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Key Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Drive territory sales and development of Insurance Specialist/corporate relationship.</li>
<li>
Perform due diligence and act as a fiduciary when performing case design duties and making product recommendations.</li>
<li>
Represent M Financial’s portfolio of insurance products knowledgeably and effectively so that the client can clearly identify the benefits of the products relative to competitors.</li>
<li>
Coordinate marketing programs within assigned territory with Insurance Specialist.</li>
<li>
Effectively transition the pre-sale process to appropriate internal liaison for application fulfillment.</li>
</ul>
