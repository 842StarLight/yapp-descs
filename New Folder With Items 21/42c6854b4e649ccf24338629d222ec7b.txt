<h2>Office Furniture Warehouse Associate- 7am-4:30pm - $13.85/hour</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Description</b><div><b>
Position Overview:</b></div>
<div>
Performs manual and system tasks to support inbound and outbound warehouse furniture operations. Identifies and resolves issues in various functional areas; put away, staging, loading, shipping. Supports and assembles “make ready” furniture to support efficient fulfillment processing and maximize fulfillment/delivery space usage.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>
What You’ll do (in order of importance; other duties may be assigned)</b></div>
<ul>
<li><b>Performs the following processes to support inbound and outbound warehouse operations.</b></li>
<ul>
<li>Utilizes power tools to assemble RTA (Ready to Assemble) furniture and stages for delivery.</li>
<li>Monitors and ensures CFS task completion within shift.</li>
<li>Separates and labels product received, stocks product into identified locations verifying SKU and quantity accuracy.</li>
<li>Trained and MHE certified to operate power material handling equipment i.e. Forklift, Electric Pallet Jack etc.</li>
</ul>
<li><b>Performs the following nonproprietary software system tasks</b></li>
<ul>
<li>Performs inventory counts and location lookups</li>
<li>Follows processes to report discrepancies or issues (i.e., shortages, damage) to supervisor or order management.</li>
<li>Communicates system issues to Application Support and/or management.</li>
<li>Completes Receiving Discrepancy Tickets when vendor shipment is inaccurate and informs Sales Support.</li>
</ul>
<li><b>Shipping and outbound activities</b></li>
<ul>
<li>Picks product with SKU and quantity accuracy</li>
<li>Re-packages, palletizes, and wraps orders as needed.</li>
<li>Sorts, stages, and loads customer orders by route and delivery order.</li>
<li>Picks outbound orders as necessary.</li>
</ul>
<li><b>Responsible for inbound freight activities</b></li>
<ul>
<li>Utilize proprietary software systems (CFS, Sunrise and Alpha) to perform functional duties and generate reports</li>
<li>Receives vendor purchase orders, stock product, special orders, or customer returns.</li>
</ul>
<li>Required at times to be a helper for 2 delivery man routes</li>
<li>Participates in all scheduled physical inventories.<br>
Performs basic housekeeping in assigned areas of warehouse.<br>
Adheres to all safety procedures in the performance of job duties.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>
Primary Interactions:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Daily interaction with furniture co-workers and fulfillment/delivery management, Application Support, Inventory Control, Furniture Sales Support, vendors/suppliers for scheduling deliveries and obtaining return authorizations for vendor returns.</li>
</ul>
