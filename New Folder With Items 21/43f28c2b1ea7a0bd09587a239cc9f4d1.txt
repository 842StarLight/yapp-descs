<p><b>
Position Objective</b>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Konica Minolta currently has an exciting opportunity for an<b> ECM Help Desk Support Specialist Onbase!</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
The <b>ECM Help Desk Support Specialist </b>provides first-line support for clients within the timelines set forth in the Master Services Agreements, assisting with general troubleshooting, bug resolution, upgrade support, maintenance questions &amp; renewals, training registrations, basis functionality training, and providing backup to the System Engineers, as needed. All client interactions will be tracking in the Case / Project Management System.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Job Functions</b>
</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Delivers technical support to contracted customers, based on the SLA requirements set forth in the Master Services agreement.</li>
<li>
Provide proper documentation of all client interactions in the Case / Project Management System.</li>
<li>
Acquires and maintains current technical knowledge of relevant product/ service offerings as it pertains to document capture, storage, retrieval, electronic workflow, data and systems integration.</li>
<li>
Updating the knowledgebase from Case Issues and other troubleshooting actions. As specific solutions are found to bugs, deficiencies’, modules, etc., the Knowledgebase will be updated to ensure that these solutions are available for future issues.</li>
<li>
Directly responsible for all maintenance renewals, and all supporting activities related to the client’s maintenance, including new license file handling, test system licensing, providing new release upgrade files, review maintenance bills, etc.</li>
<li>
Supports and manages the development of the internal ECM system, including upgrades, issue resolution, new scope, and project management.</li>
<li>
Maintain the customer-facing Portal, ensuring data is reported accurately to clients.</li>
<li>
Provide backup to the System Engineer role, supporting implementations as needed.</li>
<li>
Assist Pre-Sales in the development of customized demos and proof-of-concepts, as required during the sales process and for events.</li>
<li>
Participates in Sales and Training events, as required.</li>
<li>
Participates in technical/ end user training classes in support of the installed document management solution.</li>
<li>
Performs other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
