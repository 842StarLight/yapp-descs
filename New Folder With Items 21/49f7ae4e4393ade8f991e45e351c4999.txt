<h2>Product Line Billing Manager and Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<div><b>Job Responsibilities/Accountabilities</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Support the On-sites Plant Product Line to achieve Airgas AMG’s Vision, Mission, Values, Strategic and Financial Objectives.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b><i>
Supervision of Monthly Billing, Contract Management and Analysis Activities 45%</i></b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Develop a detailed understanding of the different contract terms and conditions.</div></li>
<li><div>
Manage the contract and billing administrators / analysts and ensure accurate and timely invoicing and price increases to customers.</div></li>
<li><div>
Assist the contract administrators / analysts in development of his/her job skills.</div></li>
<li><div>
Drive billing and contract management process improvements.</div></li>
<li><p>
Communicate with the business managers and customers on various billing and analytical details</p></li>
</ul>
<div><b><i>
Business / Financial Analysis and Reporting 40%</i></b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Support VP On-sites Business with timely and accurate financial information and analysis.</div></li>
<li><div>
Consolidate financial results of the business and prepare detailed P&amp;Ls by product line on a monthly basis.</div></li>
<li><div>
Provide financial analysis and view of current performance versus target and prior year results and clearly communicate drivers for variances.</div></li>
<li><div>
Work closely with Operations Control and Planning Group to prepare financial results for the business.</div></li>
<li><div>
Assist business managers in optimizing and reporting on the sales and profitability of assigned accounts.</div></li>
<li><div>
Develop and maintain an updated and accurate repository of data for the On-sites Business including plant locations, age, technology, capacities, plant efficiencies, power costs, and contract terms, etc.</div></li>
<li><p>
Ensure customer and competitive databases and files are maintained.</p></li>
</ul>
<div><b><i>
Business and Project Development 10%</i></b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Manages department’s Commercial Business Process and insures the appropriate application of the process.</div></li>
<li><div>
Work closely with Asset Accounting to manage movement of assets</div></li>
<li><div>
Assist in special projects and other duties as assigned.</div></li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b><i>
Forecasting and Planning 5%</i></b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Develop annual target and reforecast as requested by the management.</div></li>
<li><div>
Assist in long-term business plans for onsite business</div></li>
<li><p>
Demonstrate an understanding of the business drivers for onsite business and suggest strategy and tactics to achieve short and long term objectives.</p></li>
</ul>
<div>
<b><i>
 </i></b>Develop beneficial working relationships with key interface groups in order to maximize knowledge base and identify opportunities</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Assure personal accident and incident free conduct</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>

<div><b>General Requirements:</b></div>
<div><b><i>
Skill Set/Knowledge/Expertise</i></b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Must have a minimum of 5+ years of successful, results-oriented experience in Finance/Operations Control, Business or Operational functions.</div></li>
<li><div>
Excellent verbal and written communication skills for dealing with internal and external groups.</div></li>
<li><div>
Strong operation-business analytical skills.</div></li>
<li><div>
Project management skills (managing initiatives from idea generation to full execution).</div></li>
<li><div>
SAP knowledge and experience is required.</div></li>
<li><div>
Extremely proficient with Excel, Word, PowerPoint, Google platform, and a high degree of ability to effectively utilize a variety of internal software resources to gather and analyze a wide range of data.</div></li>
<li><div>
Capability of developing a deeper theoretical/practical business and financial knowledge generally associated with an MBA or comparable experience.</div></li>
<li><div>
Knowledge of industrial gas industry and markets served.</div></li>
<li><div>
Knowledge of Airgas products, services, technologies and applications.</div></li>
<li><div>
High level of initiative and desire to continuously improve process and performance.</div></li>
<li><div>
Must be organized, possess strong ability to multi-task, be an adaptable team player.</div></li>
<li><p>
Must possess the energy and drive to execute under pressure and meet tight deadlines.</p></li>
</ul>
