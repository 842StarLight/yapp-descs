<p>Dennemeyer has been setting the standard in the IP industry for more than half a century. We are the world’s most comprehensive provider of Intellectual Property services, offering a suite of services: legal (Dennemeyer &amp; Associates), software systems (Dennemeyer IP Solutions), and administrative and management (Dennemeyer &amp; Co).</p>
<p>
U.S. Sales have grown at over 50% a year for four years in a row. As we are quickly growing, we are currently seeking a Marketing Specialist to join our U.S. team. The U.S. Marketing Team plays a key role in our growth and is charged with defining the right go-to market approach for further growth. Marketing works closely with Sales and balances U.S. market needs within a central global campaign.</p>
<p>
This position is located in our downtown Chicago office and reports to the Marketing &amp; Communications Manager (U.S.). Grow your career and challenge yourself in a supportive team environment!</p>
<p>
If you match our profile and are looking for a new and interesting challenge, please e-mail your resume to raustell(at)dennemeyer(dot)com. Please also indicate your salary requirements/expectations.</p>

<div>
<div>Your tasks
</div>
<p></p>
<div><div>
<p><b>Digital Marketing</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Plan, lead, and execute all digital marketing campaigns (content, e-mail, social media, Pay Per Click)</li>
<li>Develop and launch e-mail marketing campaigns, conduct A/B tests, capture feedback, track metrics and refine successive campaigns</li>
<li>Lead audience-targeted, optimized and constantly tested paid social media campaigns</li>
<li>Create, monitor and engage with audience on social media channels as it relates to organic U.S. posts</li>
<li>Execute U.S. regional digital campaigns; recommend digital advertising opportunities focused on driving sales leads</li>
<li>Execute all digital aspects of event management; creation of landing page / registration page, creation of emails and workflows, creation of mailing lists, tracking of email performance, and track and pull registration lists.</li>
<li>Produce webinars; create and administer surveys</li>
<li>Contribute to and execute the US marketing plan</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Events</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Work closely with Event Manager Business Development Managers to establish proper planning, communications and direction for more than 25 events annually across the US</li>
<li>Plan, manage and track KPI’s for events that drive sales leads</li>
<li>Assist Event Manager in all aspects of event planning as needed including securing panelist participants, keynote speakers, and conference booth shipments etc.</li>
<li>Preparation &amp; distribution of event attendee communications including registration monitoring through marketing automation platform HubSpot</li>
<br>
</ul>

<div>Your profile
</div>
<p></p>
<div><div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor degree in marketing, communications, advertising or related field</li>
<li>3+ years of demonstrated experience in marketing, digital marketing, website management and social media</li>
<li>Proven experience in paid social media advertising (LinkedIn Campaign Manager &amp; Twitter Ads)</li>
<li>Event management experience; experience in tradeshow and/or exhibition marketing</li>
<li>Experience implementing successful email marketing campaigns</li>
<li>Experience using content management systems; knowledge of HubSpot is a plus</li>

<li>Excellent knowledge of MS Office products (Excel, PowerPoint, etc.); demonstrated experience using Adobe Photoshop is required</li>

<li>Deep knowledge of the digital marketing and advertising technology ecosystems, including digital agencies, and ability to employ the optimal mix of various digital marketing channels, techniques, and operating models.</li>
<li>Ability to successfully coordinate projects with global stakeholders and manage communication with internal and external customers.</li>
<li>Willingness to travel approximately once a month for 2-day trips required.</li>
</ul>
<p>Your portfolio includes 3-5 years of demonstrated success in each of the following competencies:</p>
