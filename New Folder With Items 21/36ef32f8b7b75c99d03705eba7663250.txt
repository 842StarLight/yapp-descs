<h2>General Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>NO JUDGEMENTS</p>
<p>Some may call it a tagline, but for us, it’s a way of life. It’s our Monday-thru-every-day mantra. An unfiltered philosophy that drives us to create a community and a gym for all.</p>
<p>No judgments means room for everyone, regardless of shape, size, age, race, gender or fitness level. No matter your workout of choice, we want you to feel good while reaching your goals. Join the fun!</p>
<p><b>CRUNCH</b>is seeking a dynamic and well qualified <b>General Manager</b>for one of our latest state-of-the-art expansion locations in <b>Silverdale,</b> <b>Washington</b>.</p>
<p><i>General Manager</i>will ensure the members receive the highest quality service and facilities. She/he will exhibit an ability to achieve the financial targets for the club as outlined in the annual budget by recruiting, motivating, leading, supervising and coordinating the activities of employees engaged in servicing our members’ needs. She/he will demonstrate an aptitude and command of all companywide policies and initiatives to ensure the integrity of the Crunch brand.</p>
<p><b>Administrative/Organization</b>: Orchestrate a team to drive the business leveraging standard operating procedures and policies. Requires excellent communications skills, ability to motivate and encourage, decision making abilities and recruitment of top caliber staff.</p>
<p><b>Sales/Revenue Management</b>: Demonstrate the ability to lead, motivate, and manage a team to achieve comprehensive club sales goals.</p>
<p><b>Fitness Management</b><b>: </b>Responsible for delivering all Personal Training, Small Group Training and Group Fitness Classes relating to departmental and financial goals.</p>
<p><b>Operations</b>: Lead the team to deliver superior customer service to every member while driving a relentless focus on facility cleanliness. Coordinate and work within club support functions of Fitness, Sales and Marketing, Accounting, Information Technology and Human Resources. Ensure rigorous maintenance and management of facilities and equipment.</p>
<p><b>Financial</b>: Exhibit an understanding of budgets and income statements. Display an ability to keep expenses and payroll at or below budget and articulate variances in revenue/sales/expenses versus budget.</p>
<p><b>Leadership/Motivation</b>: Serve as a role model for employees. Communicate effectively by holding periodical departmental and individual meetings with all key club personnel and provide an inspirational environment that welcomes honest feedback and takes action to ensure a quality working environment. Oversee, support, direct and develop department heads.</p>
<p><b>Optimal Requirements</b></p>
<p>4 year college degree, 4 year management experience and fitness management experience preferred.</p>
<p><b>Additional Information: </b>As a member of the Crunch Team you will receive competitive compensation to include base salary and robust commission/bonus program, complimentary club membership and career growth opportunities.</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Salary: $40,000.00 to $80,000.00 /year</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul>
<li>General Management: 2 years (Preferred)</li>
<li>Fitness Management: 2 years (Preferred)</li>
<li>Fitness General Manager: 2 years (Preferred)</li>
</ul>
