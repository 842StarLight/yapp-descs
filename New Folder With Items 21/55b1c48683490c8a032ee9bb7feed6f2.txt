<h2>Senior International Project Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>The Business Application Group International Engineering (BAG IE) team is looking for an experienced and dynamic IPE to join its growing team of expert engineers contributing to making Business Applications products global in the Cloud!<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>As part of the centralized Customer and Partner Success business group, BAG IE supports a wide range of Business Application products such as Dynamics 365 for Finance and Operations, Talent, Retail, Marketing, Sales, Customer Care, Mixed Reality apps to name a few.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The IPE will work closely with the product teams driving globalization and localizability solutions implementation as well as with our external localization supplier to produce world-ready localized versions of our products user interface and content into multiple languages.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Responsibilities</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>
Partner with product teams to review features from an internationalization support perspective, contributing to design</li>
<li>
Provide ongoing localizability and globalization support and guidance to product teamsâ€™ engineers and PMs</li>
<li>
Become an expert of assigned features and content systems</li>
<li>
Define and drive in partnership with International PMs the most suitable localization process for the different product and content systems</li>
<li>
Help create and drive the localization planning from an international engineering standpoint</li>
<li>
Expert contribution to BAG IE localization engineering system continued optimization process through partnership with localization architect and other IPEs</li>
<li>
Support external localization supplier (define/communicate processes, answer queries, bug fixing, etc.)</li>
<li>
Triage and drive international issues to resolution pertaining to areas of ownership</li>
<li>
Partner with the build &amp; content publishing teams to drive and enable loc builds</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
Engage with worldwide ISV and partners to enhance overall localized product and content quality</li>
<li>
Partner with product to define an international QA plan and run world readiness validation</li>
</ul>
