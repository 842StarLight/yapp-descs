<h2>Sales Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Position Title: </b> Sales Representative<br><b>Reports to: </b> Chief Operating Officer of United Services<br><b>Classification: </b> Exempt (plus commission)<br><b>Position Type: </b> Full-time</p>
<p><b>Position Summary: </b></p>
<p>The Sales Representative is responsible for promoting product lines for United Services, Inc. and securing orders from existing and prospective customers through a relationship-based sales approach.</p>
<p>The Sales Representative is responsible for achieving maximum sales growth and profitability by effectively selling products and tracking results. The Sales Representative will treat customers as a priority, being proactive in anticipating current and potential future needs, and is responsible for building relationships with both business and residential customers. The Sales Representative works as an integral part of a team across all departments and must be a team player. Additionally, customer focus is the number one priority of this position.</p>
<p><b>Essential Functions: </b></p>
<p>Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</p>
<p>1. Establish, develop, and maintain business relationships with current customers and prospective customers in the assigned territory to generate new business.<br>2. Make telephone calls, in-person visits, and presentations to existing and prospective customers.<br>3. Attend sales events and community functions as scheduled, including nights and weekends as necessary.<br>4. Generate business through the process of door-to-door sales.<br>5. Research sources for developing prospective customers and for information to determine their potential.<br>6. Develop clear and effective written proposals/quotations for current and prospective customers.<br>7. Resolve customer complaints by investigating problems; develop solutions; prepare reports; make recommendations to management in a timely manner.<br>8. Coordinate sales effort with customer service, marketing, management, accounting, construction, engineering, and technical service groups.<br>9. Analyze the territory and market potential and determines the value of existing and prospective customer’s value to the organization.<br>10. Identify advantages and compare organization’s products/services.<br>11. Plan and organize time to promote efficient and effective sales strategies.<br>12. Supply management with oral and written reports on customer needs, problems, interests, competitive activities, and potential for new products and services.<br>13. Keep management informed by submitting activity and results reports, such as daily call reports, weekly work plans, and monthly and annual sales activity.<br>14. Keep abreast of product applications, technical services, market conditions, competitive activities, advertising and promotional trends.</p>
<p>15. Maintain professional and technical knowledge by attending educational workshops; review professional publications; establish personal networks; participate in professional societies. Overnight stays will be required.<br>16. Be responsible for accomplishing customer sales and service functions as needed or assigned.<br>17. Attend training and/or courses relating to area of work (overnight stays may be required). Daytime hours may fluctuate and some evening and Saturday hours may be required to support the business needs of United Services.<br>18. Provide efficient, courteous service to consumers and the general public.<br>19. This position is safety-sensitive and the employee must learn and comply with all of the Corporation’s safety policies, procedures, rules and regulations, in addition to all other policies, procedures, rules and regulations related to the position of employment.<br>20. Perform all other duties as may be assigned by the CEO, COO, or designee.</p>
<p><b>Competencies: </b></p>
<p>The knowledge, skills, and behavior required for successful job performance.</p>
<p><i>Core Competencies </i><br><b> * Business Acumen: </b> Integrates business, organization and industry knowledge to one’s own job performance.<br><b> * Personal Effectiveness: </b> Demonstrates a professional presence and a commitment to effective job performance.<br><b> * Interaction with Others: </b> Builds constructive working relationships characterized by a high level of acceptance, cooperation, and mutual respect.<br><b> * Problem-Solving: </b>Exercises good judgment to make well-informed decisions and to develop and implement effective solutions.</p>
<p><i>Role-Specific Competency </i><br><b> * Customer Focus: </b>Treats customers as a priority, being proactive in anticipating their current and potential future needs; effectively educates customers about services provided; maintains composure at all times, even after difficult interactions; displays empathy in difficult situations, treating each customer individually based on the situation; builds and maintains positive relationships with both internal and external customers; maintains the "United" image, providing a uniform message without placing blame on team members.</p>
<p><b>Required Education and Experience: </b></p>
<p>1. Education: High school diploma or equivalent<br>2. Experience: Entry level 0 to 12 months sales experience<br>3. Skills: Must have strong verbal and writing skills to provide effective customer presentations, written proposals/contracts; excellent customer service skills; have an effective working knowledge of basic computer skills; acquire a strong knowledge of United Services products and services; must meet sales goals while providing accurate and timely reporting information to Management; have or acquire a thorough knowledge of first aid and CPR; must have and maintain a valid driver’s license.</p>
<p><b>Preferred Education and Experience: </b></p>
<p>1. Education: Bachelor’s degree in related field<br>2. Experience: 12 months of sales experience with telecommunications products<br>3. Skills: Must have strong verbal and writing skills to provide effective customer presentations, written proposals/contracts; must have and maintain a valid driver’s license; excellent customer service skills; have an effective working knowledge of basic computer skills; acquire a strong knowledge of United Services products and services; must meet sales goals while providing accurate and timely reporting information to Management.</p>
<p><b>Supervisory Responsibilities: </b></p>
<p>This position has no supervisory responsibility of personnel.</p>
<p><b>Physical Demands: </b></p>
<p>While performing the duties of this job, the employee is frequently required to stand, walk, kneel, crouch, and/or sit for prolonged periods of time; employee must be able to lift and carry up to 50 pounds. This position must also be able to work inside and outside in inclement weather, including extreme heat and cold.</p>
<p><b>Working Conditions: </b></p>
<p>This is a full-time position. This job operates in a professional office environment as well as out in the field selling products and services offered by United Services. This role routinely uses standard office equipment such as computers, phones, photocopiers, and filing cabinets. This position will report to the Savannah office but may be required to commute to the Maryville office and/or Excelsior office on occasion. This position will be required to travel during the work day throughout the service area.</p>
<p>Standard days of work are Monday through Friday. This position must be able to work flexible hours outside normal business hours, including weekends, events and holidays as required.</p>
<p>This position is also required to attend company-sponsored events as well as community functions that occur after regular business hours, including nights and/or weekends, to support the business needs of United Services, Inc.</p>
