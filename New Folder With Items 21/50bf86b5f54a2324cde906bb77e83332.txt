What You'll Do
<br><br>
This is a great opportunity to become the hub of activity at Cherwell. Solutions Consultants at Cherwell take on a variety of roles, primarily acting as the key technical contact for customers by providing pre-sales advice and consulting. In this fast-paced, high-energy position, consultants interact primarily with sales, but also with co-workers in other departments like Client Relations, Marketing, and Professional Services.
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Quickly come up to speed on Cherwell's products and underlying technology.</li>
<li>Work with Sales to provide prospects with product advice during pre-sales discussions.</li>
<li>Deliver technical presentations that demonstrates how a solution meets client needs.</li>
<li>Answer complex questions and demonstrate use cases to close business deals with prospects and/or customers.</li>
<li>Support Industry trade-shows, customer events, RFx responses, Proof of Concepts and customer demos.</li>
</ul>
