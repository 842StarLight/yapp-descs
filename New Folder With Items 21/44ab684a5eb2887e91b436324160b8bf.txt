<h2>Systems Architect</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>At Jabil, <b>we empower the brands who empower the world </b>– it’s our reason for being and the guiding force that’s driving us to become the <b>most technologically advanced manufacturing solutions provider on the planet. </b>Whether we’re serving one of the world’s biggest and best known brands or the coolest tech startups, our resolve never wavers. We share common desires with these brands: to make the world a better, safer and cleaner place.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Summary
</b></p>
<p>Architects conceptualize products, provide leadership and drive development of a wide range of technology based products. A key job focus is analyzing technical specifications and system requirements to determine best approaches and feasibility of development within time and cost constraints of project expectations.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Duties &amp; Responsibilities
</b></p>
<p>LEADERSHIP AND TEAM RESPONSIBILITIES
</p>
<div>Technical Responsibilities:
<br>
<ul>
<li>Work with Subject Matter Experts and other System Architects to innovate connected, machine learning, data mining, predictive maintenance, and self-monitoring electronic and electromechanical systems and systems-of-systems with disruptive value propositions to Jabil’s customers.
</li>
<li>Drive architectural concept designs using numerical trade studies and methods
</li>
<li>Perform requirements management and flowdown, driving the detailed requirements specifications for multiple disciplines (mechanical, electrical, software, thermal, optical, etc)
</li>
<li>Lead architecture reviews and design reviews, ensuring suitability and adequacy against requirements
</li>
<li>Support creation of R&amp;D roadmaps for both products and technology competency.
</li>
<li>Drive test strategy planning from system-level requirements
</li>
<li>Oversee integration, verification, and validation of complex systems
</li>
<li>Drive the development of Intellectual Property, i.e. Patents, proprietary tools sets, etc.
</li>
<li>Proactively drive technology re-use to accelerate solutions to similar design challenges; propose and lead R&amp;D projects to leverage commonality, economies of scale, technical familiarity, etc.
</li>
<li>Identify internal and external resources that can support the product development cycle and architecture cycles.
</li>
<li>Know and learn component, circuit, product, and process level technologies to support the overall direction of product design and development.
</li>
<li>Define Product Realization Proposals, System Specifications, Product Architectures, PRD, MRD, design reviews, test plans, integration plans, etc., in collaboration with senior technical staff and Customers.
</li>
<li>Architect and guide analysis and simulation activities necessary to determine performance, reliability, bandwidth and other system design characteristics.
</li>
<li>Work with specific regional design centers to enhance product and technical development overall capability.<br>
</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>
<br>
Communication:
<br>
<ul><li>Communicate design technical directions and host technology forums.
</li></ul>
</div>
<div>Technology Industry Mastery:
<br>
<ul>
<li>Identify vendors and suppliers whose technology solutions can meet Jabil design needs
</li>
<li>Maintain broad industry knowledge about those vendors’ product portfolios and product matrices
</li>
<li>Build relationships with new suppliers and host training activities (lunch and learns, pre-sales technical training seminars) to familiarize the Jabil design engineering team with new microprocessor, wireless, IoT, and sensor product families<br>
</li>
</ul>
</div>
<p><br>
BUSINESS AND DEVELOPMENT RESPONSIBILITIES
</p>
<div>Business Strategy and Direction:
<br>
<ul>
<li>Know and understand the business product development team strategic directions.
</li>
<li>Provide regular updates to BUD, BUM and DMS Engineering management on the execution of the strategy.
</li>
</ul>
</div>
<p></p>
<div>Cost Management:
<br>
<ul>
<li>Identify creative ways to reduce cost by streamlining processes and systems (i.e. modification of responsibilities or consolidation of tasks, elimination of non-value-added processes, or complete re-engineering of processes and systems).
</li>
<li>Utilize tools to monitor project costs and cost trends, striving continuously to improve value and tools.
</li>
<li>Provide feedback to peers, BUDs, BUMs, and DMS Engineering management on cost and cost trends.
</li>
<li>Manage creation and submission of project proposals and quotations.
</li>
<li>Help maintain and drive technology roadmaps and product development strategies for business sector.
</li>
</ul>
</div>
<ul><li>Flexibility to travel 25% of the time.
</li></ul>
<p></p>

</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree, in Engineering &amp; MS Eng preferred
</li>
<li>Minimum of Fifteen (15) years’ experience required
</li>
<li>Or an equivalent combination of education, training or experience.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Has 7+ years domain knowledge and design experience in the following: power supply and distribution, graphical intensive LV electronics, human-machine interface design, materials / fabrication and related tooling / production technologies and electromechanical systems.
</li>
<li>Embedded SW development experience or good understanding of it.
</li>
<li>Has 5+ years’ experience in a lead technical role
</li>
<li>Has 5+ years’ experience working with manufacturing facilities to bring products to production.
</li>
<li>Has 3+ years’ IoT / connectivity experience
</li>
<li>English speaking. Located in US. Preferred location is STP.
</li>
<li>Flexibility to travel 25% of the time.
</li>
</ul>
<p>Preferred: Experience in the appliances/intelligent home electronics sector
