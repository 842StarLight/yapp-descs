<h2>Used Trailer Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>The Used Trailer Manager is responsible for evaluating, maintaining and remarketing the company’s local used trailer inventory. The manager must be self-reliant, organized and possess the ability to influence the customer and our employees to close the sale. This position coordinates with the branch manager, local sales representatives, perspective service and parts departments in order to facilitate the acquisition and disposal of used trailer equipment.</p>
<p><b>KEY RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<p>· Perform weekly updates for the local sales team to include a current used trailer inventory, sold trailer status, incoming trades or purchases and any other available inventory</p>
<p>· Maintain an updated used trailer customer list for inventory acquisition and disposal</p>
<p>· Send out a weekly/monthly email or inventory mailer to potential customers</p>
<p>· Establish lead sheets for local sales representatives for follow up</p>
<p>· Check in all inventory to ensure it meets our trade terms and conditions</p>
<p>· Sell Used trailers to fleets and individuals</p>
<p>· Be familiar with the market at all times</p>
<p>· Forecast the market to control inventory</p>
<p>· Be familiar with location of all inventory</p>
<p>· Manage overage inventory</p>
<p>· Be active and aware of auctions and successfully work to our advantage</p>
<p>· Be active and organized in refurbishing inventory while controlling costs</p>
<p>· Work with Sales Team to familiarize them with all inventory and pricing and availability.</p>
<p>· Manage advertising to best utilize sales</p>
<p>· Provide accurate information to management as needed.</p>
<p>· Buy inventory thru other trucking companies/auctions etc.</p>
<p>· Establish used trailer values based on market value not book value</p>
<p>· Ensure that all monthly reports and used trailer surveys are performed in a timely manner</p>
<p>· Maintain a manageable supply of inventory and ensure we have a minimal amount of aged inventory</p>
<p>· Prepares equipment for re-sale to include lot display, detailing and any needed repairs</p>
<p>· Assist the company in disposing of corporate trades in a profitable manner</p>
<p>· Reviews sales records and current information to determine accurate used trailer values</p>
<p>· Meet with potential customers to demonstrate and explain equipment features</p>
<p>· Obtain credit information on perspective customers and forward it to financial institutions</p>
<p>· Investigate and resolve customer complaints</p>
<p>· Accurately estimate and cost equipment for re-sale</p>
<p>· Write reports on sales and sales performance</p>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<p><b>KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS AND ABILITIES </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Office, especially Excel, Word, Power Point and Outlook</li>
<li>Have the ability to communicate effectively with all manner of customers ranging from an owner operator to a company president</li>
<li>Motivate, develop and direct people in assisting of the disposal of our used trailer inventory</li>
<li>Develop constructive and cooperative working relationships</li>
<li>Must have the ability to influence the purchasing decision process</li>
<li>Observe, receive and obtain the information needed to close the sale</li>
<li>Possess the ability to accomplish ones work by organizing and prioritizing</li>
<li>Have the ability to enter, transcribe, store and maintain information written or electronically</li>
<li>Provide relevant information on equipment to assist other sales staff to close a sale</li>
<li>Must have an established used trailer network for the acquisition and disposal of equipment</li>
<li>Must be knowledgeable about all types of used trailer products. To include, vans, reefers, flatbeds, refrigeration units, suspensions, ABS, PSI, etc.</li>
</ul>
