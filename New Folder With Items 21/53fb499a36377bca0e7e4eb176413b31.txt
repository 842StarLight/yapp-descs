<h2>Product Desk Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Purpose</p>
<p>The Manager, Product Desk will be responsible for presenting solutions to sales by recommending products suitable for their borrower’s unique circumstances, offer alternative solutions to loans declined and maintain surveillance on pipeline to suggest FNMA’s Home Possible and Freddie Mac’s Home Ready as the better solution where applicable.</p>
<p>Essential Functions &amp; Responsibilities:</p>
<p>Product solutions (55%)</p>
<ul>
<li>Sales personnel often lack the knowledge of the entire array of products and their features at their disposal. Hence, at times they may be unable to fit a prospective client in to a product. While serving as a repository of the vast array of products offered by Academy and their features, the Product Desk Manager will entertain calls/emails from sales at such times to guide them to a suitable option for their clients.</li>
<li>Since this is the first time this position has been created, the Product Desk Manager will utilize multiple forums, including but not limited to direct emails to sales and Work Place, to create and maintain awareness of the availability of this support for sales.</li>
<li>Create and maintain analytics to enable tracking and reporting of solutions offered that consequently lead to a loan creation. Also, the number of sales personnel leveraging the Product Desk and their frequency of utilization.</li>
</ul>
<p>Decline Desk (25%)</p>
<ul>
<li>Review loans declined and select those which can be fitted in to another product and/or restructured so that Academy’s mission of providing sustainable homeownership is successfully achieved.</li>
<li>Focus not on the credit decision to explore ways to reverse it. Rather, explore alternative product solutions available.</li>
<li>Design and maintain analytics to enable tracking and reporting of alternative solutions offered that ultimately led to a loan being created.</li>
</ul>
<p>Pipeline Surveillance (20%)</p>
<ul>
<li>Monitor conventional conforming loans in the pipeline to identify those eligible for FNMA’s Home Possible and FHLMC’s Home Ready loan program instead. Quantify the benefit afforded to the prospective borrower and communicate it to the LO and their manager.</li>
<li>Design and maintain analytics to enable tracking and reporting of loans converted to Home Possible/ Home Ready and the related savings incurred by the borrower.</li>
</ul>
<p>Other Functions &amp; Responsibilities:</p>
<p>1. Understands key financial drivers, enterprise goals and strategies.</p>
<p>2. Is sensitive to rapidly resolving issues that arise among products and platforms in a way that balances product and enterprise goals.</p>
<p>3. Establishes support from key stakeholders to identify and achieve product objectives and outcomes.</p>
<p>4. Takes actions that directly or indirectly influence others to create support, gain trust and motivate actions in others, or win concessions without damaging relationships.</p>
<p>5. Engages business units and enterprise management on resolving trade-offs of scope, priority, business and technical risk, and business impact.</p>
<p>6.Effective communication of product vision,messaging requiring action in a way that brings people on board with the vision.</p>
<p>Job Qualifications</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<p>7 – 12 years'experience in the mortgage industry;primarily in Credit Risk and related function.</p>
<p>Education:</p>
<p>Bachelor’s degree preferred</p>
<p>Knowledge, Skills&amp; Abilities:</p>
<p>The ability to motivate, influence or persuade others is a material or major part of the job, requiring a significant level of diplomacy and trust. Obtaining cooperation (internally and/or externally) is an important part of the job and a high level of interpersonal skills is critical to the success of this position. Work frequently involves contacts requiring considerable discussion of problems, material presentations, and resolving issues impacting departments or divisions.</p>
<p>Other Skills:</p>
<p>Basic word processing, power pointand computer skills are required, in addition to working and presentingmaterials in a group setting.</p>
