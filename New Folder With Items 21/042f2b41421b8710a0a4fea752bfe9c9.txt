<h2>Senior Product Marketing Manager [Cloud Workload]</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>About Us:</b></p>
<p>
SentinelOne was formed by an elite team of cyber security and defense experts from IBM, Intel, Check Point, Cylance, McAfee, and Palo Alto Networks. SentinelOne is shaping the future of endpoint security through its unified, converged platform that automatically prevents, detects, and responds to threats in real-time. Our unique approach is based on deep inspection of all system processes combined with innovative machine learning to quickly isolate malicious behaviors, protecting devices against advanced, targeted threats in real time.</p>
<p><b>
What are we looking for?</b></p>
<p>
As a Senior Product Marketing Manager owning cloud workloads, you will be a key player in the transformation of the endpoint security market. You will own new product launch, articulate a clear, consistent and creative value proposition for our target markets while acting as the linchpin between our sales, product teams and go-to-market message. You will have the opportunity to develop compelling, go-to-market initiatives and sales enablement programs while being a part of our product positioning and messaging. You will work closely with the leaders of this organization - across product management, product marketing, sales, demand generation, corporate communications and customer success, to define and execute the strategy.</p>
<p><b>
Key Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Be the expert on the key capabilities, differentiators, benefits and use cases for target customer segments.</li>
<li>
Develop compelling messaging, product literature, website, competitive positioning, customer presentations and event presentations.</li>
<li>
Develop, gather and disseminate technical information internally and externally.</li>
<li>
Deliver content, assets and tools including blog posts, whitepapers, presentations, demos, videos, social assets, webinars, ROI tools and other market-facing materials.</li>
<li>
Identify thought leaders and topics - including research, information gathering, technical investigation, testing and publishing.</li>
<li>
Work closely across marketing teams to develop demand gen and awareness campaigns.</li>
<li>
Enable the sales, SE and channel organizations on SentinelOne capabilities and positioning.</li>
<li>
Understand sales/channel requirements and communicate the value proposition of the products to the sales team and develop the sales tools that support the selling process of the products.</li>
<li>
Regularly evaluate competitive capabilities and technologies to understand features, pricing, positioning,and packaging.</li>
<li>
Serve as a thought leader as needed with a variety of audiences including customers, analysts, field and partner teams driving a deeper understanding of the SentinelOne value proposition and our differentiation.</li>
<li>
Work closely with product management, sales and executives to develop &amp; influence product strategy, GTM strategy.</li>
</ul>

<li>

<li>
Knowledgeable about the cloud market, including buyer personas, key speakers, engagement opportunities and conference, understanding the risks knowledge about the challenge of securing cloud workloads</li>

</ul>
