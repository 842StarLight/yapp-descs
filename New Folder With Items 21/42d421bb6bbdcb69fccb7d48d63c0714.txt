Responsible for handling financial activities for selected Consumer Electronics partners and establishing best practices to ensure full of Disputes with focus on customer satisfaction through process improvements and effective relationships with our business partners.
<p></p>
<p><b>Role and Responsibilities
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage current ATB (Aged Trial Balance), all transactions for assigned account
</li>
<li>Analyze accounts, attention to quality and accuracies is of paramount importance in this role Dispute Resolution
</li>
<li>Conduct reconciliations; identify and investigate problems within the process and system provide solutions to resolve the issues
</li>
<li>Work with appropriate departments to ensure credits are issued for valid customer chargebacks
</li>
<li>Reconcile and resolve credit balances and request refunds if necessary
</li>
<li>Advise sales force of outstanding chargeback issues. Alert them of issues and enlist support for resolution of disputes
</li>
<li>Partner with Logistic partner, Samsung America Logistics (SALs), to ensure POD issues to resolve outstanding CB and past due invoice issues
</li>
<li>Partner with sales team and sales executives on problem issues to ensure timely resolution Operational/Functional Review
</li>
<li>System updates- including but not limited to proper coding for clearing and matching of chargeback’s and credits in SAP
</li>
<li>Maintains accurate customer information in system according to company procedures
</li>
<li>Process and submit all clearings prior to monthly closing
</li>
<li>Effectively manage client relationships
</li>
<li>Customer Meetings via Phone and in-person
</li>
<li>Relationship building (internal &amp; external)
</li>
<li>Prepares and communicates various weekly, monthly and quarterly financial reports to management. Various Ad Hoc Reporting &amp; Analysis
</li>
<li>Ability and willingness to jump into project and workloads as needed
</li>
</ul>
