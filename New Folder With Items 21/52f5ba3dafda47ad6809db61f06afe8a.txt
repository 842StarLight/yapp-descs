<h2>Logistics Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>Raytheon Space and Airborne Systems (SAS) Supply Chain Logistics team is seeking a Logistics Specialist to serve as a team member responsible for shipping of government owned assets in accordance with Defense Contract Management Agency Transportation rules and regulations. You will perform a wide array of duties from preparation of government bills of lading using government software from Surface Deployment and Distribution Command.<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities include, but are not limited to:</b><ul>
<li>
Authorizations for shipment are Wide Area Work Flow Receiving Reports, DD1149s and DD1348s.</li>
<li>
Accountability to the government’s many rules and regulations specified in the Defense Transportation Regulations, Military freight traffic unified rules publication and others.</li>
<li>
Foreign Military Sales preparation using the Defense Contract Management Agency transportation tools and Mil-STD-129 for the preparation of Military Shipment labels.</li>
<li>
Handle and ship items listed in the Defense Transportation Regulation Part II, sensitive and classified materials.</li>
<li>
Coordinate and monitor workflow, assist in prioritizing activities in assigned areas to improve upon service level commitment.</li>
</ul>
<b>
Required Skills:
</b><ul>
<li>Experience shipping Military assets using government shipping software systems</li>
<li>
Experience coordinating the shipment of classified and sensitive items including Arms, Ammunition, and Explosives (AA&amp;E)</li>
<li>
Ability to work additional hours and occasional Saturdays</li>
<li>
Experience keeping auditable records</li>
<li>
Experience with loading dock activities</li>
<li>
Experience with Microsoft office suites (Word, Excel, PowerPoint)</li>
<li>
This position requires a U.S. person or the ability to obtain an Export Authorization from the appropriate Government Agency for non-U.S. Persons</li>
<li>
U.S. Citizenship status is required as this position will require the ability to access US only data systems.</li>
</ul>
<b>
Desired Skills:
</b><ul>
<li>General industry experience and understanding of protocol in a defense company, including work with Government Furnished Material (GFM)</li>
<li>
Working knowledge of SAP, Wide Area Work Flow (WAWF), and Defense Transportation Regulations</li>
<li>
Import/Export Compliance experience</li>
<li>
Experience working within a diverse team environment</li>
<li>
Effective time management</li>
<li>
Possess a solid reputation for safety, professionalism, and ethical responsibility</li>
<li>
Excellent customer service and problem solving skills</li>
<li>
High data entry speed and accuracy</li>
</ul>
<b>
Required Education:
</b><ul><li>Bachelor’s Degree in Logistics, Supply Chain Management, Business, or other relevant function</li></ul>
