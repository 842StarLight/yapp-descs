<div>
<p><b>Web Merchandiser</b></p>
<p>Lakeshore Learning Materials is one of the most innovative manufacturers of educational materials in the country. Specializing in top-quality products for the home and classroom, we are a thriving multichannel organization with a robust web business, multiple catalogs for early learning and elementary programs, 60 store locations and a sales force that is second to none. Thanks to the daily contributions of the amazing people who make up our team, Lakeshore has experienced continuous growth since the day it was founded in 1954.</p>
<p><b>Could you be the one?</b></p>
<p>Are you an analytical product guru with a love for e-commerce? We’re looking for an experienced Webb Merchandiser to join the E-Commerce Merchandising Team at our Carson headquarters. We’ll admit – we’re looking for a bit of a unicorn! Along with the other members of your team, you will optimize the customer experience and ultimately build product traffic and sales on Lakeshore’s digital properties, including LakeshoreLearning.com, an award-winning Top 500 Internet Retailer website. The ability to accurately evaluate and communicate sales trends and histories is essential.</p>
<div>
<b>A day in the office looks like this.</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Performing general e-commerce merchandising activities for our B2C and B2B websites, including product management/merchandising and taxonomy management</li>
<li>Analyzing sales reports, communicating histories and trends, and creating action plans for your categories</li>
<li>Optimizing internal search results by applying attributes and keywords to all products</li>
<li>Managing new product launches through product setup and review of PDPs, categories, and search results</li>
<li>Planning for category assortments and selecting featured products for marketing campaigns</li>
<li>Preparing and delivering product presentations to executive management</li>
<li>Collaborating with cross-channel teams including Marketing, Purchasing, Editorial and Art</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>
<b>Got the skills and experience? Here’s what we’re looking for.</b><br>
<ul>
<li>2+ years experience in e-commerce merchandising or product management</li>
<li>A creative problem solver with the ability to think on your feet</li>
<li>Superb analytical skills with a strong work ethic and attention to detail</li>
<li>A knack for thriving in a fast-changing work environment and adapting to shifting priorities</li>
<li>A self-starter who can work independently as well as collaboratively in a team environment</li>
<li>The ability to juggle multiple priorities, stay on schedule and meet deadlines</li>
<li>Top-notch written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Advanced proficiency in Excel - experience with if statements, VLOOKUP and pivot tables</li>
<li>Experience with exporting data from a BI software, and manipulating this data to build product sales reports</li>
<li>Experience with attributes and keywords, including a general understanding of how they affect internal search results and SEO value is a plus</li>
<li>Experience with web analytical tools such as Adobe/Omniture and Google Analytics is a plus</li>
</ul>
</div>
