<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>The Opportunity
</b></h2>
<p></p>
<p>This position is under Marvell's Security Solution group. Key products of the group include crypto offload solutions, PCIe HSMs and Network HSMs. This position focuses on HSM business. Selected candidate will be responsible for developing ecosystem for the HSM products. The ecosystem will include solution providers serving various verticals/applications such as Financial, Technology, Automotive, Govt, Healthcare, IOT, Smart Manufacturing, Key Management, CA and other HSM use cases. Selected candidate will also be responsible for developing new business opportunities among verticals/applications mentioned above. Selected candidate being part of a small team should be self-motivated, self starter and be able to work with and motivate AE and Sales team members. Perfect candidate will have the right mix of market knowledge and business and technical acumen.
</p>
