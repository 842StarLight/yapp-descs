<h2>Lead UX/UI Designer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Job Title: UX/UI Design Lead</p>
<p>Department: Research &amp; Development</p>
<p>Reports to: EVP, R&amp;D</p>
<p></p>
<div>Job Summary:<br>
Responsible for creating powerful, compelling user experiences across Calero’s suite of offerings. This includes best practice research, user research and analysis, interaction design and usability testing. The UX/UI Design Lead will define and lead the creative process from start to finish, delivering an outstanding user experience and evolving the UX/UI vision for future Calero products.<br>
<br>
Duties and Responsibilities of the job:<br>
<ul>
<li>Leads UX/UI design, user discovery, research, visual design and usage analysis across Calero’s entire product portfolio to help Calero produce the industry’s most intuitive and appealing software experiences</li>
<li>Develops and maintains documents that capture UX/UI requirements for product development to use as reference.</li>
<li>Partners with Product Managers and Software Engineers to sketch concepts, epics and user stories in wireframes.</li>
<li>Partners with Marketing to assist with brand collateral development in pallets, fonts, styles, logos, graphics, document templates, and other design support.</li>
<li>Provides final designs and specifications to developers to support product workflow.</li>
<li>Ensures consistent look and feel throughout existing and newly developed products to support brand recognition and consistency.</li>
<li>Collects user intelligence through interviews, usability testing, research, participatory design workshops, and/or other methods to understand and improve the user experience.</li>
<li>Defines and improves the toolsets, processes and deliverables for all aspects of UX/UI design to support continuous improvement.</li>
<li>Mentors, coaches, and supervises other UX and visual designers to support their growth and development.</li>
</ul>
<br>
Education:<br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelors’ degree in Human Computer interaction, Visual Design, Computer Science or related field required. An equivalent combination of education and experience will be considered.</li>
<li>A minimum of 5 years of experience in user interface design, usability testing, human factors, information architecture, or other related disciplines within a corporate environment.</li>
</ul>
<br>
Experience and Training:<br>
<ul>
<li>Experience in leading significant UX projects</li>
<li>Collaborative leadership experience in matrixed organizations</li>
<li>Ability to work in a fast-paced, dynamic environment</li>
<li>Excellent communication, organization, time management and interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Experience in Agile development</li>
</ul>
<br>
Technical Knowledge:<br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Familiarity with HTML5, CSS, web standards and cross browser compatibility</li>
<li>Familiarity or experience with JavaScript frameworks like jQuery or Angular is a plus</li>
<li>Strong working knowledge of one or more UX tools (Adobe Creative Suite, Axure, Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign and associated design tools)</li>
</ul>
<br>
Contact with Others<br>
<ul>
<li>Calero customers</li>
<li>Calero Senior Leadership Team</li>
<li>Product Management, Software Engineering, Sales, Support and Marketing</li>
</ul>
<br>
Confidentiality:<br>
This position will be exposed to confidential information and discretion is paramount. A confidentiality, non-compete, and non-solicitation agreement is required.<br>
<br>
Management Responsibility<br>
This position supervises the work of other team members and performs other management responsibilities, including hiring, professional development, performance management, and participating in senior leadership team activities.</div>
