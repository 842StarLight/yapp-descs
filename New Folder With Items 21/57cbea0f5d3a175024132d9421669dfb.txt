<p><b>Job Description:
</b></p>
<p>Help CoreLogic lead its journey to the Cloud
<br>
<br>
The Enterprise Business Systems - Application Architect will assist CoreLogic build application, data centric, and integration solutions with a heavy emphasis cloud architecture for the company’s back office functions. The successful candidate must have skills to manage both a development team as well as work with all levels of customer to explain concepts, maintain continuity across the different work streams of complex data projects and programs. Individual must be able to solidify current initiatives as well as observe customer needs and expand legacy projects and programs. In addition, the individual must have hands on expertise with Oracle technologies including Cloud offerings.<br>
<br>
<br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Lead discussion, design and architecture for strategic cloud, integration and on-premise enterprise solutions, engaging various CoreLogic business and technology teams as necessary
</li>
<li>Work closely with other peer IT solutions architects in developing short- and long-range plans and reviews these plans with senior IT leadership as well as other organizational management.
</li>
<li>Validate and future proof solution designs and software vendors, internal service providers, application business analysts, and application developers
</li>
<li>Prepare, conduct, and evaluate proof-of-concept integration solutions per business use cases. Quantify the business values and articulate the value proposition to application teams and senior management
</li>
<li>Document and diagram current and future state integration processes across business applications.
</li>
<li>Inventory and maintain an Integration repository across segments in order to promote reusability. Create or review and approve Integration designs for projects
</li>
<li>Initiate both group and one-on-one meetings with application owners and enterprise architects for clear understanding of integration on current and future needs and in terms of business drivers and their application roadmaps
</li>
<li>Research and monitor technology trends and methodologies to simplify and to deliver faster integration services
</li>
<li>Understand and articulate how and what application and integration services can enable digital transformation in business functions, including smart manufacturing, mobility, supply chains, etc.
</li>
<li>Engage in the full cycle of project delivery process i.e. requirement, design, build, test, go live, and support
</li>
<li>Expand integration monitoring and alert capability
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
