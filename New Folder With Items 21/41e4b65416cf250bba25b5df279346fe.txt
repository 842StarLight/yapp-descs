<p><b>About Us</b></p>
<p>
Infinity from Marvin is The Marvin Companies’ premier line of fiberglass replacement windows and doors – marketed to, sold to, and installed for consumers by local window experts who are authorized Infinity dealers. We are currently looking for a hardworking and detail oriented Lead Window &amp; Door Installer to work in our Columbus, OH location. Infinity from Marvin is a world-class manufacturer of made-to-order windows and doors that relies on our experience, advanced technology, and the excellence and entrepreneurial spirit of our employees to continually grow our business. This is a great time to join Infinity as the brand is transforming to drive increased growth which creates a unique and exciting opportunity for you to participate and contribute to our success.</p>
<p><b>
Position Overview</b></p>
<p>
The Lead Window &amp; Door Installer will be responsible for installing window &amp; door projects sold by our sales team.
</p>
<p><b>Essential Job Functions</b></p>
<ul>
<li><div>Execute window and door installations sold by the Infinity from Marvin of Ohio sales team utilizing various trade methods standard within the industry.
</div></li>
<li><div>Provide superior customer satisfaction through professional and courteous communication and treatment of our customers.
</div></li>
<li><div>Guide and manage one or more helper over the course of an installation project.</div></li>
<li><div>Cross-functionally collaborate with Infinity of Ohio operations team to assist in product service in addition to installation.</div></li>
<li><div>Communicate all product and install issues to appropriate parties including customers, project managers and sales people.</div></li>
<li><div>Hours will vary and will be influenced heavily on the wants and needs of our customers.</div></li>
<li><div>Execute job close activities</div></li>
</ul>
<p><b>Additional Job Functions</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Assist in display setup and tear down at various shows and events</li>
<li>Perform maintenance and remodeling on showroom displays</li>
<li>Meet with homeowner to confirm specifications, assist in contract confirmation, and perform technical measurements</li>
</ul>

</li>

<li>Strong work ethic
</li>
<li>Ability to effectively lead and manage helpers</li>
<li>Cohesive interpersonal and relationship-building skills at multiple levels</li>
<li>Solution-oriented and innovative thinking abilities</li>
<li>Strong communication skills
</li>
<li>Aptitude to work with required technology, software applications, CRM</li>
