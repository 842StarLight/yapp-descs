<h2>Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Join the world leader in thermal mass flow meter technology. FCI began in 1964 by pioneering the development and application of thermal dispersion flow and level sensing technology. The Company is the world-recognized leading manufacturer for solving flow and level measurement applications for industrial process and plant applications using patented thermal dispersion flow measurement technology.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>From off-the-shelf product solutions to custom engineered products and systems, FCI provides world leading experience and a record of unequaled innovation to meet or exceed our customer application demands. Complimenting FCI product leadership is a world class Flow and Level Calibration Facility in which all calibrations are performed in liquids or gases utilizing only N.I.S.T. traceable equipment and instrumentation.
</div>
<p>Currently, we are seeking a Project Manager for our Sales Administration department. The level of the position will depends upon previous related experience.
</p>
<div><b>Position Purpose</b></div>
<div>
Under the general direction of the Director of Sales, this position is responsible for managing all aspects of detailed engineered product contracts, interfacing with end-user customers, engineering consultant offices and our sales team to successfully implement and complete assigned projects. As part of your responsibility, you will work closely with the sales team to review and quote new business opportunities, with input into proposal quotations.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Essential Job Functions (primary duties and outputs of position)</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Responsible for managing and coordinating the operational aspects of one or more project(s) and serves as the liaison between the customer and FCI.
</li>
<li>Interface with all internal departments depending on the scope of each project.</li>
<li>
Serve as primary after sales commercial point of contact with customers.</li>
<li>
Continually reviews status of the project(s) and budgets to ensure project(s) are completed on time and within budget.
</li>
<li>Assesses project issues and develops solutions to meet production, quality, and customer-satisfaction goals and objectives.</li>
<li>
Help create detailed quotations that allow the company and customer to proceed with an order in alignment with all technical and commercial capabilities.
</li>
<li>Manage and monitor project schedules, timelines, and milestones from initiation to delivery to meet or exceed goals.</li>
<li>
Actively participate in the planning, estimating, scheduling, resource loading and project procurement activities.</li>
<li>
Evaluate and monitor contract performance to determine the need for changes and to ensure compliance with contractual obligations.
</li>
<li>P&amp;L on all major projects and notify Director of Sales of potential program overruns. Prepares corrective action and cost containment recommendations.
</li>
<li>Help develop strategic plans for industry growth and product development. Review product obsolescence issues and proposed ways to meet installed base requirements.
</li>
</ul>
