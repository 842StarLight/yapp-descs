<h2>Marketing Communications and Trade Show Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Las Vegas Based Position Reports to Zircon Campbell, CA</b></p>
<p><b>Trade Show and Marketing Communications Manager (REMOTE POSITION)</b></p>
<p>The Trade Show and Marketing Communications Manager, under the general supervision of the Director of Global Online, will work closely with and assist the Marketing team who are responsible for building Zircon’s global brand awareness. This position will manage Zircon’s trade shows and oversee the creation of the company’s merchandising and marketing collateral. Experience in managing domestic and international trade shows and marketing collateral projects in a technical, autonomous environment are desired.</p>
<p><b>Key Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>Trade Shows</p>
<ul>
<li>Create and execute an annual trade show and event strategy in coordination with marketing and business leaders</li>
<li>Plan and organize trade shows from start to finish with attention to planning and time constraints</li>
<li>Organize all logistics for the trade show including booth, product, and display set-up, organization, and dismantling</li>
<li>Lead promotional activities, including in-booth events, giveaways, talent, and speakers for the events</li>
<li>In collaboration with marketing, create awareness for the event, including public relations, advertising, social media, and email marketing.</li>
<li>Prepare budgets and ensure adherence</li>
<li>Source and negotiate with hotels, vendors, and suppliers</li>
<li>Oversee event staff</li>
<li>Analyze the event’s success and prepare reports</li>
<li>Coordinate operations for up to 35 trade shows a year including logistics, staffing, organization, and developing business objectives</li>
<li>Work and attend trade shows and oversee the set-up and dismantling activities</li>
<li>Manage booth properties and vendors</li>
<li>Track all aspects of each trade show including financial and success metrics</li>
</ul>
<p>Marketing Communications</p>
<ul>
<li>Acts as a brand champion and ensures brand consistency in all program deliverables</li>
<li>Develop news releases, advertorials, datasheets, marketing brochures, and other marketing materials</li>
<li>Edit and proof as needed Customer service posts and content</li>
<li>In collaboration with marketing and sales, develops merchandising, displays, and other point-of-purchase materials</li>
<li>In coordination with Director of Global Online, execute email marketing campaigns across targeted audience segments to drive awareness and conversion</li>
<li>Track and report on campaign metrics</li>
</ul>
<p>Required Skills</p>
<ul>
<li>Must have valid Passport and be available to travel 25% of the time to international and domestic trade shows</li>
<li>Excellent writing and editing skills</li>
<li>Good organization and communication skills</li>
<li>3 - 5 years of marketing experience</li>
<li>A flexible, can-do attitude</li>
<li>B.A. degree in marketing, communications, or similar</li>
</ul>
<p>Desired Skills</p>
<ul>
<li>1- 3 years prior trade show management experience</li>
<li>DIY and home improvement enthusiast</li>
<li>Multilingual</li>
</ul>
