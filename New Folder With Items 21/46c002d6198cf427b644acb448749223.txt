<h2>Manager - Pricing Analytics</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><div><div>
<div>The Manager - Pricing Analytics leads a team responsible for analysis, modeling, and reporting on factors that impact new loan pricing, profitability, and cash flow projections. This position will use complex analysis, forecasting, and modeling concepts to mitigate risks and identify opportunities relative to pricing and profitability of new loan originations. This team member is responsible for summarizing and reporting this information to a variety of internal clients. This position will interact with many other departments in the interest of achieving the overall company objectives.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div><b>JOB DUTIES</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Lead a team responsible for the development and support of complex models, analysis, and reporting related to new loan pricing, profitability and cash flow projections</li>
<li>
Organize and lead ad hoc research projects incorporating project design, data collection and analysis, summarization of findings and presentation of results</li>
<li>
Develop recommendations for operational policies and procedures</li>
<li>
Gather and analyze data to determine impact to business operations</li>
<li>
Present analyses to management in a clear, concise, convincing, and actionable format</li>
<li>
Direct and review the work of two to five Financial Analysts</li>
<li>
Perform other duties as assigned</li>
<li>
Conform with all company policies and procedures</li>
</ul>
