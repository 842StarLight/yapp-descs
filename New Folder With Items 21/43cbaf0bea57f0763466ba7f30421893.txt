<p><b>What You’ll Get to Do:</b></p>
<p>
Demand Planning Forecast Management:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Actively seek out designated customer forecasts &amp; enter and maintain forecasts within SAP/ TM1. Analyze forecast data to determine variances and trends.</li>
<li>
Advise sales of any concerns with regard to forecast inconsistency and seek input for forecast adjustments.</li>
</ul>
<p>
Supply Chain Management:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Configure new stock materials and new stocking programs to ensure appropriate systems integration.</li>
<li>
Take ownership of the supply chain for assigned customers and resolve supply chain issues.</li>
</ul>
<p>
Sales &amp; Operations Planning:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Facilitate monthly meetings with Account Managers, Sales Administration and Inventory Planners.</li>
<li>
Interface with Account Managers to ensure collaborative sales forecast for designated customers is maintained in TM1 on schedule for monthly PSI reporting.</li>
<li>
Advise management of any known issues which will affect budgeted inventory turnover days.</li>
</ul>
<p>
KPI Management:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Measure customer forecast accuracy and factory performance. Present data to management.</li>
<li>
Inventory Planning Purchase Order and Delivery Management: Run MRP and seek appropriate purchase approvals in accordance with PIDSA guidelines.</li>
<li>
Interface with factory contacts to expedite, defer and cancel orders as appropriate.</li>
<li>
Ensure correct method of shipment is utilized and seek approvals for expedited freight charges in accordance with company policy. Alert Customer Service when purchase orders will not arrive in time to meet customer demand.</li>
<li>
Track shipments from the factory and interface with logistics contacts to facilitate on-time warehouse delivery.</li>
<li>
Post goods receipts into offline warehouses, if applicable.</li>
</ul>
<p>
Inventory Management:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Collaborate with Sales, Accounting, Administration, and Factory personnel as appropriate to resolve inventory concerns.</li>
<li>
Supply analysis for slow-moving/obsolete inventory to ensure timely disposition.</li>
<li>
Process scrap orders as appropriate, in accordance with PIDSA policy.</li>
</ul>
<p>
Supply Chain Management:</p>
<ul><li>
Review and maintain SAP master settings to optimize inventory levels, purchase order quantities and customer satisfaction. Maintain purchase price and purchasing vendor-material info records in SAP.</li></ul>
<p>
Sales &amp; Operations Planning:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Participate in monthly meetings with Account Managers, Sales Administration and Demand Planners.</li>
<li>
Advise management of any known issues which will affect budgeted inventory turnover days.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Education and Experience:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree preferred</li>
<li>
Minimum 3 years’ experience in demand planning, inventory management or related field required Intermediate PC proficiency with advanced aptitude in MS Excel required Proficiency in ERP system software required (SAP preferred) Proficiency in BI reporting tools (ex: Cognos)</li>
</ul>
