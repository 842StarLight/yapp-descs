<h2>Account Services Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>This position plays a lead role in membership retention and second-tier (elevated) customer service. They are responsible for assisting current and new members as well as the membership sales team in managing membership status, information, refunds, and benefits. They work closely with the Business Systems team to resolve system issues.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
As part of the Customer Service Team, they may be called upon to train front-facing staff on general front desk functions, software and customer service, and to cover front desk shifts when needed.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
They are also a key participant in Box Office Operations, supporting Box Office hours and services, offering a knowledgeable ticket sales experience to patrons.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<p><b><i>
Systems and Front Desk Support</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Be a power user of the JCCSF business systems and procedures, including testing processes, providing feedback and training Customer Service users.</li>
<li>Process Program Registrations, Membership Sales and Box Office Ticket Sales using applications including SalesForce, PatronManager, Traction Rec and MindBody</li>
<li>Assist members and non-members with program registrations, login problems and community memberships.</li>
<li>Provide front desk coverage as needed for short periods during regular business hours.</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>Customer Service and Support
</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Assist membership team by providing input and finding solutions to member account challenges and assisting in creating a positive member experience from initial sign-up to cancellation.</li>
<li>Review membership contracts to ensure the billing information is correct, and account access is set up and enabled.</li>
<li>Process Membership Hold/Cancel/Refund/Credit/Payment Updates requests</li>
<li>Answer Account balance questions via email, phone, &amp; walkup in coordination with Accounting and Membership.</li>
<li>Fulfill customer requests for usage, billing statements and reports (upon request and for monthly batches).</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>Box Office Support
</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provide expert customer service for our members and the public.</li>
<li>Staff and support all Box office needs in the 2-3 hours prior to events.</li>
<li>Ensure timely response to Box office emails and voicemails</li>
<li>Be available to support Box office walk up and phone sales and questions during Box office hours.</li>
<li>Qualify contacts and ticket orders</li>
</ul>
<p><b>REQUIREMENTS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Relevant work experience with a minimum of 3 years’ progressively responsible experience in a customer service environment.</li>
<li>Experience in a multi-business/product environment preferred.</li>
<li>Business systems proficiency and computer savviness, including sales, reconciliations, registrations, and member management.</li>
<li>Salesforce experience strongly preferred.</li>
<li>Mind Body Online or major CRM platform experience a plus.</li>
<li>Strong customer service orientation.</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal and communication skills.</li>
<li>Extremely organized and detail-oriented.</li>
<li>Ability to handle multiple tasks and responsibilities.</li>
<li>Proficiency in Microsoft Outlook, Word, Excel and Power Point.</li>
<li>Must be able to sit for extended periods of time (up to 8 hours) and occasionally stand as needed.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>STATUS: </b>Benefited, non-exempt, collective bargaining position</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>COLLECTIVE BARGAINING UNIT CLASSIFICATION</b>: Admin 2</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>HOURS:</b> Full time, 40 hours</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>SCHEDULE: </b>Monday – Friday</p>
