<h2>Transition Consultant- Saint Petersburg, FL / Saint Louis, MO / Southfield, MI</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Description</b></h2>
<br>
<div><b>
Job Summary:<br>
</b></div>
<div>
<br>
Under limited supervision uses specialized knowledge and skills obtained through experience, specialized training or certification to facilitate the creation and implementation of systems needed to provide new offices, satellite offices and financial advisors (FAs) with a successful transition to Raymond James and Associates (RJA) and Raymond James Financial Services (RJFS). Fosters and collaborates in the development of relationships with new offices and FAs. Identifies, researches and works with home office associates to resolve problems associated with transitions. Guidance is provided to perform varied work that is somewhat difficult in character requiring some evaluation, originality and ingenuity to make moderately complex decisions.</div>
<div>
<br>
<b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities:<br>
</b>
</div>
<div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Understands and educates new branch personnel on the policies and procedures relating to operations, compliance, marketing, technology, investment products and general business issues.</li>
<li>Ensures all operational features of the office establishment are arranged and coordinated prior to start date.</li>
<li>Provides consultative educational orientations to all new FAs, offices and their associates on policies and procedures via on-site or telephone meetings.</li>
<li>Supports branches with general questions on all aspects of their business during their transition to Raymond James.</li>
<li>Acts as Branch Manager on an infrequent basis as business warrants (after obtaining Series 24).</li>
<li>Acts as a liaison and ensures follow up and completion of all transition responsibilities for new FAs and new offices with Raymond James home office departments to resolve problems.</li>
<li>Assists with new FA and branch paperwork, including but not limited to, opening accounts, transferring accounts, setting up files and following up to determine incomplete information.</li>
<li>Collaborates in development of new educational ideas and systems.</li>
<li>Acts as a liaison to Business Development, Private Client Group Education and Sales Management.</li>
<li>Acts as the primary point of contact while team members are away from office.</li>
<li>May conduct home office visits for new financial advisors joining RJA and RJFS offices.</li>
<li>Performs other duties and responsibilities as assigned.</li>
</ul>
</div>
