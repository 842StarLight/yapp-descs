<h2>Account Executive</h2>

<div>
<p>Are you an experienced and successful sales professional who is passionate about helping businesses grow and achieve their marketing objectives? Entercom Communications in Rochester is looking to add to our team a dynamic sales professional who is truly one of the BEST of the BEST!<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Entercom’s Radio and Digital Sales Consultants connect local businesses (Rochester area territory -no travel required) with potential customers using mass media (5 top rated radio stations* and online station brand assets), digital agency marketing services, sports marketing (ESPN, Buffalo Bills and Buffalo Sabres) and local event marketing. Sales Consultants are supported with new business appointment setting by Inside Sales team members. In addition, with our extensive reach nationwide, you will have access to sell on a national level, across all 48 Entercom markets, and be handsomely compensated for it!</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Entercom offers an attractive base salary, bonus compensation plus lucrative commissions beyond quota attainment. Matching 401k, benefits. Student Loan Payment Assistance Program offered.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Why you will want to join our sales team at Entercom Rochester and what can we offer you:</b></p>
<ul><li> You would be able to create highly successful multi-platform advertising campaigns including the broad reach of broadcast radio, internet radio plus targeted digital solutions, with killer lifestyle events and e-commerce
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Access to selling on a national level within 48 Entercom markets</li></ul>
<ul><li> On-going coaching and training and development
</li></ul>
<ul><li> We give our sales team the resources and support they need to be successful and earn a great income</li></ul>
<ul><li> You have the opportunity to bring innovative marketing ideas to life that make a difference for your clients and their businesses</li></ul>
<ul><li> <b>Company Mission:</b> We are a mission driven company. "Entercom Moves People". Emotionally connecting with listeners and moving people into action on behalf of our advertisers. Making a difference for local business drives our local economy, keeps our work force strong and keeps our community thriving. Local is at the core of everything we do.</li></ul>
<ul><li> <b>Entercom Culture</b>: Focused on employee engagement which in turn drives great customer experience. Team atmosphere providing many opportunities for team building with and without clients. (Concert VIP areas at station events, sales club celebrations and contests, community team volunteering, etc) Offers a work/life balance philosophy yet performance oriented. Sales management philosophy is that sales managers are focused on supporting sales consultants in the field and behind the scenes acting as coach.</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Build strong relationships with advertisers to ensure their success and repeat business</li>
<li>Proactively prospect and develop new business by using all of our advertising and marketing assets</li>
<li>Create marketing campaigns and strategically sell to local and regional decision makers</li>
<li>Close business and oversee the execution of the marketing campaign and exceed client expectations</li>
<li>Provide exceptional customer service</li>
<li>Achieve sales objectives and goals</li>
<li>Continuously build your knowledge of industry trends, opportunities, and innovations</li>
</ul>
