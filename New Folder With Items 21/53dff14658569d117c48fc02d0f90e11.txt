<h2>Business Development Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>We are looking for an energetic and hungry Business Development Representative ready to launch their sales career in the exciting Construction Tech space! This team is the front lines of the sales funnel and main focus is to get the foot in the door for our field sales organization. With a regional approach at selling into the construction industry, we are looking for someone to be the expert in their territory as we look to break into new accounts.<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Call and educate prospects about PlanGrid. Your goal is to qualify leads and create pipeline growth</li>
<li>
Work within Salesforce to update and maintain leads as appropriate</li>
<li>
Learn the ins and outs of our product and help with competitive analysis</li>
<li>
Learn how to demo the platform and potentially give in-person and online demos</li>
<li>
This position is designed for you to build the tools and skill sets you need for a career in SaaS sales</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
Minimum Qualifications</b><br>
<ul>
<li>You have experience in an outbound, cold calling sales environment</li>
<li>
You are hardworking &amp; action-oriented</li>
<li>
You have unparalleled communication skills</li>
<li>
You quickly establish rapport with people and establish value</li>
<li>
You are results-focused with a past track record of exceeding quotas or other comparable goals</li>
<li>
You are customer-focused</li>
<li>
You want to build your career in sales</li>
<li>
You have a sense of humor and are a fundamentally good person</li>
</ul>
<br>
