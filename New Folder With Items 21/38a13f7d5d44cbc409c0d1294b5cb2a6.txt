<h2>Google Cloud Architect</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<p>Deloitte is an industry leader in Digital Transformation. We recognize that the backbone enabling our clients’ highest aspirations for analytics and cognitive solutions is next-gen Cloud infrastructure. From cloud consulting, to implementation, and ongoing managed services we offer a full scope of Cloud services and solutions. We need talent ready to innovate cloud services and empower our clients to leap forward in their digital capabilities. Building your career at Deloitte offers the opportunity to work with cutting-edge Cloud technologies to deliver next-Gen Cloud solutions. We embrace the tech pioneer mindset and always enable our talent to keep their skills evolving at the speed at which the technology marketplace is evolving.<br>
</p>
<div></div>
<p>Cloud has transformed the way technology enables the business. It represents a radical shift in how IT services are obtained, used, and managed. Companies are navigating through the various cloud options and deciding between private, public, or hybrid combinations across different and dynamic provider ecosystems. Our employees are empowered to think outside of the box and provide innovative solutions to our customers.</p>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<p></p>
<p><b>Work you’ll do</b></p>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<p>In partnership with other leaders in the firm, you will focus on selling and delivering cloud solutions using AWS, Azure and Google Cloud and, as a key member of Architecture group, ensure success in building and migrating applications, software and services on public cloud.</p>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<ul>
<li><div>Promote public cloud internally to sales and delivery teams to drive growth.</div></li>
<li><div>
Educate customers of all sizes on the value proposition of Public Cloud and participate in deep architectural discussions to ensure solutions are designed for successful deployment in the cloud</div></li>
<li><div>
Capture and share best-practice knowledge amongst the public cloud solutions architect community</div></li>
<li><div>
Author or otherwise contribute to public cloud customer-facing publications such as whitepapers</div></li>
<li><div>
Build deep relationships with senior technical individuals within customers to enable them to be cloud advocates</div></li>
<li><p>
Act as a technical liaison between customers, delivery teams and support.</p></li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<p>Required:</p>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Self-starter with an in-depth hands-on work experience with large-scale implementations of Google Cloud Platform (GCP), AWS or Azure.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of J2EE, .NET, and cloud technologies development languages and tools, to continue with development</li>
<li>
Direct experience migrating applications from the enterprise to public cloud providers (e.g. Amazon, Google, or Microsoft)</li>
<li>
Experience helping development teams build and deploy microservices based applications in public cloud. Experience with Docker, Kubernetes and other Container Orchestration and API exposure platforms.
</li>
<li>Experience with Agile methodologies and tools. Continuous Integration &amp; Continuous Deployment tools and processes</li>
<li>
Understanding of emerging technologies and end-user needs.</li>
<li>
Strong analytical skills with the ability to learn new information quickly</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
</div>
<p>Preferred:</p>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<ul>
<li>5+ years of overall experience, to include a minimum of 5 years of enterprise, full-life cycle in Architecture in the professional IT/Software Development services industry.</li>
<li>
3+ years of experience with Java / J2EE technologies and Architecting solutions using Web technologies</li>
<li>
2+ years project experience migrating and deploying cloud based solutions (GCP, Azure or AWS)</li>
<li>
2+ years architecting, implementing and supporting Microsoft Azure or Amazon Web Services infrastructure and topologies</li>
<li>
Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science, Electrical Engineering or equivalent work experience. MBA preferred</li>
<li>
Google Certified Professional – Cloud Architect or Azure, AWS equivalent preferred</li>
<li>
Understanding of Infrastructure as a Service, Infrastructure as Code and related concepts on public cloud.</li>
<li>
Experience with distributed computing, cloud security, IaaS &amp; PaaS architectures, knowledge of big data/analytics, ci/cd, DevOps, SRE and networking concepts.</li>
<li>
Understanding of Digital Advertising, Analytics and the impact of the combination of Cloud and Analytics over businesses, end-users and cloud providers</li>
<li>
Proven experience as a successful senior engineer and/or cloud engineer highly desirable</li>
<li>
Container experience with Docker and Kubernetes or other container orchestration platforms</li>
<li>
API experience with APIgee or other API exposure platforms</li>
<li>
Understanding of Identity and Access Management, user and system authentication methods and certificates, tokens and certificate management
</li>
<li>Understanding of Agile, SCRUM and Continuous Delivery</li>
<li>
Demonstrated skills in leadership, communication, coaching, analysis, problem solving and short term and long term planning.</li>
<li>
Experienced, persuasive and effective presenter, both written and verbal.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of standard selling disciplines, specifically Solution Selling and RFP management.</li>
<li>
Experience leading high performance, results driven teams with a focus on client satisfaction.</li>
<li>
Excellent interpersonal and organizational skills, ability to handle diverse situations, multiple initiatives and rapidly changing priorities.</li>
<li>
Must be a self-starter who requires minimal supervision.</li>
<li>
Ability to travel up to 80%</li>
</ul>
<div>
Limited immigration sponsorship may be available.</div>
