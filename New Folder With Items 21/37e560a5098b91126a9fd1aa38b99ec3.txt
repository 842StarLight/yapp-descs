<h2>Sales Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Position Summary:</b></p>
<p>
Large Automotive Dealership Group seeking a <b>Sales Manager.</b> This position is responsible for assisting the Dealer in planning, organizing, and controlling the sales of the New &amp; Used Sales Department. The Sales Manager also measures the performance of the employees in the department and provides assistance to them.</p>
<p><br>
<b>
In this role, you'll:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Forecast goals and objectives for the department and strive to meet them.</li>
<li>
Strive for harmony and teamwork with all other depts.</li>
<li>
Attend managers' meetings.</li>
<li>
Assist the Dealer in forecasting controllable expense elements for the new and used vehicle depts.</li>
<li>
Understand, keep abreast of, and comply with federal, state, and local regulations that affect new &amp; used vehicles sales.</li>
<li>
Hire, train, motivate, counsel, and monitor the performance of new and used sales employees.</li>
<li>
Report all vehicles sold.</li>
<li>
Directs New and Used salespeople to enhance the image and customer satisfaction ratings of the dealership.</li>
<li>
Communicate with and mentor new and used salespeople to ensure that dealership policies and procedures are understood and followed.</li>
<li>
Set sales and gross objectives for salespeople.</li>
<li>
Guide all salespeople in setting their objectives on a short and long-range basis.</li>
<li>
Review all sales people's performance.</li>
<li>
Develop programs of improvement for those sales people who are failing to reach their objectives.</li>
<li>
Will supervise the salespeople to ensure that they follow their owners on a planned contact basis.</li>
<li>
Will evaluate each quarter gross performance of the salespeople.</li>
<li>
Will conduct a weekly sales meeting.</li>
<li>
She/he will keep accurate sales records, both daily and monthly.</li>
<li>
Will insist that the salespeople follow the company's prospecting program to obtain maximum results.</li>
<li>
Comply with manufacturer's training for self and salespeople.</li>
<li>
Recommend to the Dealer the inventory to be carried by color, model, and equipment, based on a variety of customer and market analyses.</li>
<li>
Help salespeople close deals when necessary.</li>
<li>
Confirm salespeople's appointments are in Elead</li>
<li>
Maintain standards for the delivery of vehicles to customers.</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
<b>
Other Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Will coordinate the function of the salespeople with the Service Dept., Parts Dept., and business office.</li>
<li>
Assist Dealer in the development of advertising campaigns and other promotions.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
For this role, you'll need:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Automotive sales experience is required</li>
<li>
2 Years of experience working in sales management</li>
<li>
Experience managing groups and teams</li>
<li>
Mentality that no job is too small</li>
<li>
Ability to communicate effectively to multiple levels of the organization</li>
</ul>
