<h2>Engagement Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Title:</b> Engagement Manager
<br><br>
<b>Location:</b> San Francisco, CA
<br><br>
<b>What we are looking for:</b><br>
CityBase is looking for an Engagement Manager to lead client and partner relationships and ensure the successful implementation and adoption of a SaaS platform with goal of 100% retention and reference ability. You are responsible for providing excellent client service and helping to identify new opportunities to add value within the market. You are known for your ability to build consensus, establish trust, communicate effectively, and foster culture change with a variety of internal and external stakeholders. This position reports to the VP of Operations and is based in San Francisco with 10% travel required.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Serve as single point of contact for client account maintenance, crisis communication, technology updates, and business development</li>
<li>Participate in meetings and provide responsive, targeted communication to clients to build trust, understand strategic goals, monitor satisfaction, and anticipate future needs.</li>
<li>Collaborate closely with CityBase project management, product, and customer support teams to ensure strategic alignment, drive platform innovation, and capture market opportunity.</li>
<li>Build and maintain relationships with a variety of stakeholders in the San Francisco region including elected officials, GovTech leaders, and universities and research organizations to elevate civic engagement and capacity for future engagement.</li>
</ul>
<b>Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>At least 3-5 years of client management experience with business applications and/or digital services; payment processing, utility, and/or public sector experience preferred</li>
<li>BA/BS in business, operations, technology, or related field; Master's degree a plus</li>
<li>Energized by solving problems and finding opportunities to make customers successful</li>
<li>Experience communicating technical concepts to non-technical audiences</li>
<li>Strong process orientation and excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Familiarity with Slack, Jira, Confluence, Zendesk, or related tools helpful but not required</li>
</ul>
Benefits
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Comprehensive benefits package including health, dental, vision, 401k, Flexible Spending Account, Dependent Flexible Spending Account, Pre-tax Commuter Program, company-paid short-term and long-term disability and life insurance</li>
<li>Unlimited PTO</li>
<li>Paid parental leave</li>
<li>A multitude of snacks and beverages</li>
</ul>
<b>About CityBase:</b><br>
CityBase is a government technology company that transforms the way cities connect and do business with constituents. CityBase works with local governments, agencies, and utilities to integrate payment functionality, business processes, and communications onto a central, cloud-based platform that consumers can access through the web, mobile, kiosk, or point-of-sale.</div>
