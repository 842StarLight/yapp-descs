<h2>Color Producer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Reporting to the Executive Producer of Color, our Color Producers are responsible for the project management of color jobs, shepherding projects from beginning to end to ensure that we deliver on time and at or under cost while attaining client satisfaction and making sure that MPC's high standards are met with each and every job.</p>
<p>
Color Producers are also responsible for managing relationships with our clients, building even greater trust and expanding existing relationships within the Advertising and creative industries to find new business.</p>
<p>
The ideal candidate has experience in post-production, a strong business sensibility and a genuine enthusiasm to present MPC's outstanding color projects to new clients in the US.</p>
<p>
Specific Responsibilities include:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Managing projects from beginning to end, from bidding through delivery.</li>
<li>
Working with the Scheduling team to allocate necessary resources for each project and tracking this data within CETA.</li>
<li>
Adjusting cost efficiencies for each project and calculating P&amp;L on individual jobs. Delivering within pre-approved profit margins.</li>
<li>
Assisting with financial forecasting in predicting revenues for future color projects.</li>
<li>
Identifying business opportunities and managing new business presentations, pitches and reels.</li>
<li>
Developing new client relationships and successfully managing existing ones.</li>
<li>
Building a contact portfolio within relevant Advertising and Creative industries.</li>
</ul>
<p>REQUIREMENTS</p>
<p></p>
<p>The Moving Picture Company (MPC) is a global creative studio with a single goal: to create experiences that move people. With decades of storytelling experience, we are perpetually pushing the limits of what is technologically and artistically possible to tell visually stunning stories. MPC continues to lead in an ever-evolving industry, building authentic and long-lasting connections with audiences across all channels.</p>
<p></p>
<p>QUALIFICATIONS</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>2+ years of experience Producing for post.</li>
<li>
Extensive knowledge of the color grading process.</li>
<li>
Experience in sales, pitching and presenting reels to clients.</li>
<li>
Good Excel and Word skills.</li>
<li>
Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.</li>
<li>
Experience with bidding and scheduling.</li>
<li>
Ability to work in a team and on own initiative.</li>
<li>
Ability to work under pressure and to meet deadlines with grace.</li>
<li>
Experience in Advertising and/or Visual Effects a big plus.</li>
</ul>
