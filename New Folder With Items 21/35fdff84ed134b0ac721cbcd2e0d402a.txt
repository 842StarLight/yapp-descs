<h2>Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>The Position</b>: The Bio-Optronics Project Manager will plan and lead system deployments alongside the customer’s project team, including maintaining a schedule, aligning company resources, communicating to all involved and synchronizing activities from sale to deployment completion. In this position, you will have responsibilities inclusive of:<br>
<br>
• Leading and owning the planning and/or implementation of projects • Defining and developing project scope statements, communication plans, and deployment timelines • Implementing and managing project changes and interventions to achieve project outputs • Managing project budgets and resource allocation • Providing written and oral reports defining plans, problems, and resolutions to appropriate levels of management • Advocating on behalf of the clients’ needs • Following a defined project management methodology<br>
<br>
<b>Requirements</b>:<br>
• Advanced degree in Business Management, or a related field • 10+ years’ project management experience • Deployment/Implementation experience with complex IT solutions, inclusive of team leadership • Experience working with C-level executives and key decision-makers • Leadership attributes in the area of people management and process definition • Familiarity with CTMS, EDC and other clinical trial information systems • Knowledge of software development and database design<br>
</p>
<p><b>What We’re Looking For</b>:<br>
• Someone with a thorough understanding of project management phases, techniques and tools • Impeccable spoken and written communication in English • Uncompromising integrity and ethics • Team player • Flexibility to occasionally travel for extended periods of time • An individual who is cultivated, compassionate, professional, exuding a credible personality • You must be compatible with a small company culture</p>
</div></div>
