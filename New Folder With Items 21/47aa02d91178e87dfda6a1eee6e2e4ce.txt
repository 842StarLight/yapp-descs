<h2>Yield Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<p><b>Position Overview:</b><br>
As a Yield Manager, you will be responsible for the performance optimization of client campaigns, utilizing our technology platform in an RTB environment. A main goal is to develop a deep understanding of human behavior on devices to drive engagement and awareness with brands.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities Include:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Become a subject matter expert in programmatic advertising</li>
<li>Combine data analysis and client feedback to execute and optimize advertising campaigns based on client objectives</li>
<li>Develop actionable insights and make recommendations to internal Sales and Account teams which will be communicated externally to clients and agencies</li>
<li>Work with Data Science team to gather trends and campaign specific insights to improve performance for campaigns and technology platform</li>
<li>Contribute to the development of best practices for Yield team (e.g.; campaign management and performance optimization)</li>
<li>Provide and monitor reporting on a frequent basis to ensure optimal delivery and performance of campaigns</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>1-3 years of experience in online media, search, affiliate, or high-frequency trading</li>
<li>Degree in Engineering, Mathematics, Economics, Finance, Physics, Statistics, Computer Science, Business, or four years experience in a quantitative role</li>
<li>Fluent in excel with a proven background of creative problem-solving techniques</li>
<li>Understanding of SQL and data extraction is a plus, willingness to learn is a must</li>
<li>Exceptional and proven skills to solve quantitative puzzles</li>
<li>Desire to effectively communicate and collaborate across departments</li>
<li>Ability to effectively manage multiple priorities and deadlines with strong attention to detail</li>
<li>High level knowledge of ad server technology, user-level data, media attribution, and general digital media tracking/data landscape</li>
<li>Working knowledge of campaign management via RTB and exchange environments is a major plus</li>
<li>Ability to learn and adapt in dynamic, high-growth environment</li>
<li>Experience in six sigma or statistical control processes is a plus</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p>We offer full health coverage, generous PTO, great office culture.</p>
<p>
We are an Equal Opportunity Employer.</p>
