<h2>Junior Business Development Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Company Overview
<br>----------------

Arundo is a software company based in Oslo, Norway; Houston, TX; and Palo Alto, CA, with presence in 7 locations in Europe and the Americas. Our software enables advanced analytics, machine learning, and IoT applications for more efficient, safer, and more effective physical systems in heavy industries such as energy, chemicals, and shipping. <br><br>
Job Description
<br>---------------
<br><br>
We are looking for a Junior Business Development Representative to accelerate our sales effort in the North American market. This individual will work with our Americas sales and marketing lead-generation teams to play a fundamental role in achieving Arundo's ambitious customer acquisition and revenue growth objectives in the region and to continue to build on our existing relationships. You must be comfortable making dozens of calls per day, working with channel partners, generating interest, qualifying prospects, and closing sales.
<br><br>
Responsibilities
<br>----------------
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Drive lead generation and source new sales opportunities through inbound lead follow-up via email and phone</li>
<li>Work with marketing and lead generation to build digital outreach and social media programs as it relates to sales (e.g. email, LinkedIn, Twitter)</li>
<li>Understand customer needs and requirements</li>
<li>Research accounts, identify key players and generate interest</li>
<li>Maintain and expand your database of prospects within your assigned territory</li>
<li>Close sales and achieve quarterly quotas</li>
<li>Route qualified opportunities to the appropriate account executives for further development and closure</li>
<li>Team with channel partners to build pipeline</li>
<li>Perform effective online demos to prospects</li>
</ul>
Qualifications
<br>--------------
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Proven inside sales experience with at least 1+ years of experience in enterprise software sales</li>
<li>Track record of achieving quota</li>
<li>Strong phone presence and experience dialing dozens of calls per day</li>
<li>Proficient with productivity and web presentation tools</li>
<li>Experience working with Salesforce.com or similar CRM</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communications skills</li>
<li>Strong listening and presentation skills</li>
<li>Ability to multitask, prioritize, and manage time effectively</li>
<li>BA/BS degree or equivalent in sales &amp; marketing, engineering, computer science or data science-related field (e.g. physics, applied math, etc.)</li>
</ul>
Desired Additional Experience &amp; Skills
<br>------------------------------------------
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Experience in upstream, midstream, downstream oil and gas, shipping, utilities or process-related industries is advantageous</li>
<li>Experience in data-intensive and/or data science-related fields also advantageous</li>
<li>Experience with Big Data analytics and visualization beyond classic business intelligence capabilities</li>
<li>Layperson's understanding or better of data science as applied to industrial operations</li>
</ul>
