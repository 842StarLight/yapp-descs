<p><b>
Job Overview</b></p>
<p>The Senior Director of US SMN (small, midsized and nationals) New Business Marketing is responsible for creating demand with prospective customers and supporting in-year revenue growth targets for SAP Concur’s largest addressable market.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>You as a leader of US SMN New Business Marketing will formulate the growth strategy for the three SMN divisions by leveraging analytics, deeply understanding the target audience, and partnering closely with sales leadership. You’ll execute these growth strategies by way of integrated campaigns through your team of field marketers, and through regional and corporate marketing partners including acquisition marketing, channel marketing, events, digital/web, and advertising.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Communication and leadership are vital in this role in order to align stakeholders, cross-functional partners, and to galvanize your own team of direct reports in pursuit of a coordinated GTM. Ultimately, the core challenge of the role is to drive double-digit growth targets while delivering breakthrough return on investment and an outstanding customer experience.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>This is an exciting challenge and a great opportunity to help take SAP Concur’s US SMN division into a new customer experienced-based era where touch points are personalized, marketing engines are scaled, and breakthrough growth is delivered. Other divisions within SAP Concur and other SAP lines of business will surely take note of the innovation that you spearhead.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The position reports to the VP of US SMN Field Marketing<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Develop and manage integrated demand generation and funnel acceleration strategies to meet and exceed pipeline targets.
</p>
<p>Lead regional Initiatives, including market expansion, campaign support in market, product launch support in market, pricing and offer strategies.</p>
<p>Forecast, measure, analyze and report on the impact and performance of marketing performance and demand creation activities on sales pipeline, revenue and sales cycle length (demand waterfall). Benchmark and compare against other geographies/segments for response, conversion rates and pipeline.</p>
<p>Work cross-functionally with SMN International Field Marketing, external vendors and central marketing to manage digital marketing programs designed to maximize website traffic and web leads.</p>
<p>Forecast, direct and adhere to marketing budgets to achieve pipeline objectives including the tracking, monitoring and reporting of marketing spend, relative to sales and marketing funnel performance.</p>
<p>Evaluate, select and manage outside vendors that participate in the development and execution of demand creation programs.</p>
<p>Work cross functionally to develop coordinated strategies for driving customer acquisition and growth<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Position Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>10+ years field marketing experience in B2B cloud software industry
</li>
<li>Relevant work experience at the Region and/or Market level unit</li>
<li>BS required, MBA preferred</li>
<li>Experience creating and implementing proven successful, thought leadership, demand creation, lead nurturing and pipeline acceleration programs</li>
<li>Ability to leverage digital marketing including SEO/SEM</li>
<li>Experience in brand management, PR, marketing content development and management</li>
<li>Experience with MS Office applications, CRM (Salesforce), marketing automation (Marketo or a similar tool), and business intelligence tools (Tableau)</li>
<li>Experience forecasting and managing a marketing budget; building and executing marketing plans; analyzing and reporting on closed-loop, marketing program performance including ROI and ROMI, and running planning and change management processes.</li>
<br>
</ul>
