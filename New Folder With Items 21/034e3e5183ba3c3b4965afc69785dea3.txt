 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">

<p><b>Position Summary:</b></p>
<p>
The Leasing Consultant would be responsible for responding to all prospect calls, walk-in visits, and internet inquiries in a professional manner while presenting the apartment community in a positive light.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Showcasing the property to prospects and converting them to qualified residents</li>
<li>
Answering telephones</li>
<li>
Recording traffic</li>
<li>
Marketing and outreach</li>
<li>
Demonstrate strong professionalism and integrity while representing Dominium</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
1 years of previous leasing or sales experience</li>
<li>
Ability to work evenings and weekends</li>
<li>
Ability to work on-call</li>
<li>
Section 8 experience</li>
<li>
Section 42 experience</li>
<li>
Market Rate experience</li>
<li>
Yardi software experience</li>
<li>
Ability to work independently and demonstrate strong judgment</li>
</ul>
