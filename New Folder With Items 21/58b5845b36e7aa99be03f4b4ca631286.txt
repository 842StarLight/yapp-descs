<div><b>
Corporate Functions – Marketing &amp; Communications</b></div>
<div>
As part of this team, you’ll play a key role in helping Accenture execute its strategy. You’ll work with a diverse team of innovative marketing experts who focus on the following areas: global brand management and advertising; global events and sponsorships; internal communications; marketing research; media and industry analyst relations; corporate and financial communications; internet marketing; recruitment marketing; client-centric marketing; and industry, service line and geographic marketing campaigns.</div>

YOUR ROLE</b>: Communications, Media, Technology (CMT) North America Marketing Senior Manager</div>

<div>
Client Centric Marketing Senior Manager for Communications, Media and Technology clients in North America. Accountabilities include,</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>
<div>Work closely with account teams and clients to develop innovative and impactful marketing programs designed to help win specific large projects</div>
</li>

</li>
<li>
<div>Provide value-added input into concept development, budgeting, implementation and performance measurement of marketing programs</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Define and track metrics to assess effectiveness of marketing programs</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Manage resources and third-party service providers (e.g. approved agencies, designers, writers and media / PR agencies)</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Work collaboratively with industry, segment, and business service offerings managing directors to support their business plans through creative and targeted marketing programs</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Provide marketing support to several key Communications and High Tech accounts in North America</div>
</li>
<li><div>Design and drive marketing programs to deepen our relationships with clients</div></li>
</ul>
