<h2>Bookkeeper</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>Located in North Raleigh, our fast-paced office is now hiring a full-time bookkeeper to add to our growing team. Working at The Bookkeeper provides the best of both worlds. We offer the stability of business knowledge and professionalism gained from decades of corporate experience, in a fun, fast-paced young company with start-up culture. We always promote from within and our people are empowered to effect positive change within the organization, whether that's in company practices, or in providing increasingly stellar service to our clients.
<br>
<br>
This position requires the accurate and timely financial recordkeeping of approximately twenty client accounts, including the recording of financial transactions and reconciling bank and credit card statements. While duties will be performed primarily using Quickbooks, both desktop and online, other accounting software platforms such as Xero may be utilized as well depending on client and industry.
<br>
<br>
<b>Primary responsibilities:</b>
<ul>
<li>Prepare financial statements and reports, including the profit and loss statement and balance sheet</li>
<li>Bring current all banking, credit card, and all other financial transactions for each client no less than every other banking day</li>
<li>Assist with budget preparations</li>
<li>Record payment of bills and maintain ledgers</li>
<li>Record receipts of paid receivables</li>
<li>Keep track of client assets.</li>
<li>Record all client payroll activity</li>
<li>Communicate with clients on questions pertaining to bank and credit card activity</li>
<li>Reconcile all prior month bank and credit card accounts by the 10th of each month</li>
<li>Prepare sales tax returns (primarily NC)</li>
<li>Prepare ad hoc reports as needed for clients</li>
<li>Work across various software systems</li>
<li>Generate 1099's</li>
<li>Prepare appropriate schedules and reports as requested by clients and partners</li>
</ul>
<b>Benefits:</b> PTO, 12 paid holidays, IRA</div></div>
