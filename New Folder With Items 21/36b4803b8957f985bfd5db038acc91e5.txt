<p>
About Ingenium Schools</p>
<p>
Ingenium Schools transforms education by restoring the right to joy and meaning in learning. Since 2009, Ingenium Schools has helped students build confidence, leadership, and a lifelong love of learning with the Ingenium Learning System, an easily replicable, standards-based educational model that promotes personal mastery. Ingenium Schools currently operates four charter schools serving students in the Los Angeles area.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
POSITION PURPOSE/SUMMARY</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Enrollment Recruiters function as a mouth-piece for Ingenium Schools, generating enrollment and facilitating the enrollment process. Enrollment Recruiters must form a deep knowledge of all Ingenium Schools so they can teach families about Ingenium and encourage them to enroll. Enrollment Recruiters distribute information about all Ingenium schools through flyers, in person, and by phone. Enrollment Recruiters must be outgoing, willing to speak with and approach the public to talk about Ingenium Schools, and have a unique ability to encourage new families to join Ingenium schools. Enrollment Recruiters must to travel to Ingenium School sites as needed (Compton, Canoga Park, Winnetka, Maywood), must have reliable transportation, and must be willing to work weekends as needed.</p>

<p>WORKING CONDITIONS</p>

Environment:</p>
<p>
Work is performed outdoors in varying weather conditions (heat, cold, rain, dust, wind, etc.), and sometimes in an office environment. Employees are exposed to weather and temperature changes depending on the seasons. Exposure to moderate and loud noise, seasonal temperatures, dust and wind.</p>

<p>
Physical Demands:</p>
<p>
Enrollment/Recruiters are physically active and must be able to lift at least 40 pounds. In addition, this job requires at least 8 hours of continuous standing and/or walking outdoors in minimal shade, stooping, crouching, kneeling, squatting, and twisting. Campus Leaders must have adequate vision and the ability to communicate orally.</p>

<p></p>

Work Schedule/Logistics:</p>

The working hours vary according to the needs of the organization. Work days include weekdays and weekends as needed for recruitment and enrollment efforts. The employee must have a car or get his own transportation to carry out and complete requested assignments.</p>
