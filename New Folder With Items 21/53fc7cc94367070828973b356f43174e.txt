<p><b>Job Description: </b></p>
<p>We are looking for our first ever team member who will be entirely committed to generating sales through new business development and managing our marketing efforts. The role will have a lot of responsibilities handed over which the business has used throughout its history, many of which are proven and when practiced consistency, yield high results. But then this individual will have to create new plans and strategies to implement to grow the business. The role is exciting in many ways, most of all I think because its new! But also, because we are a great group of people and the business is growing without any formal plan or dedicated individual committed to growing it. We were just honored with being named to the Inc 5000 list for fastest growing private companies in America for the second year in a row. This role will be responsible for delivering 10X growth. And they will do that by managing all of but not limited to the list of responsibilities below:</p>
<p>· Building our first ever marketing strategy for the business.</p>
<p>· Manage a wide range of account based marketing campaigns.</p>
<p>· Reach out consistently to prospect and target customers by way of phone calls, emails, cold canvassing, trade show, networking groups.. you name it! This role is about getting after it.</p>
<p>· Deliver Pizza’s to Prospect Businesses in an effort to get a “Slice of their time”</p>
<p>· Create and develop blogs for the business website</p>
<p>· Send engaging presentations to current customers and prospects</p>
<p>· Reach out to and prospect old leads and previous customers of the business. Reach for that low hanging fruit!</p>
<p>· Manage our email marketing campaigns</p>
<p>· Pick up the phone and call prospect businesses and leads</p>
<p>· Have a target list of prospects, and be ruthless to convert those people and businesses into customers of the business.</p>

<p>· Your first priority will be to learn our business, know our customers and have an interest in all the products we can make and offer our clients. We’ll teach it to you everything you need to know! But for one to have success in this role, it’s imperative they can articulate easily and accurately what we do and who we are.</p>
<p>· That you will be the master of follow ups. Follow up Follow up Follow up!!! We need a tenacious SOB.</p>
<p>· That you have an Always Be Closing attitude</p>
<p>· That you will use every tool, tip and trick available to you to make your process the absolute easiest and most effective.</p>
<p>· That you learn everything there is to know about our business and industry. Because we will not have anyone representing our business that doesn’t know what we do, who are customers are and how everything works.</p>
<p>· Have a target list of prospects, and be ruthless to convert those people and businesses into customers.</p>
