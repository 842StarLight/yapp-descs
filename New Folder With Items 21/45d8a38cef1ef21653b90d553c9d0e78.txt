<p>
KPMG is currently seeking a Director in Enterprise Solutions for our Management Consulting practice.</p>
<p>
Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Participate on multiple client service delivery teams in the active execution of engagements in compliance with firm and practice unit service delivery standards</li>
<li>
Utilize process, technical, and industry knowledge to identify and solve complex client issues</li>
<li>
Lead and contribute to internal initiatives to develop leading practices and comprehensive analytics solutions</li>
<li>
Build long-term business relationships with key executives through engagement delivery and networking in professional organizations</li>
<li>
Lead business development by identifying new opportunities and assisting with proposal development</li>
<li>
Participate in the mentoring and development of staff as a performance management leader for one or more junior resources</li>
</ul>
<p>
Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>
 A minimum of eight years of Oracle Reporting and Analytics related experience with a professional services advisory firm including experience leading the sale and delivery of full life cycle implementation projects utilizing Oracle Business Intelligence and Analytics products including Oracle Analytics Cloud</li>
<li>
Bachelor's degree from an accredited college/university; MBA or MIS from an accredited college/university preferred</li>
<li>
Experience in process reengineering, designing and implementing business performance management applications, and integrating these applications into business processes to enhance decision making</li>
<li>
Strong understanding of governance, controls, change management, conceptual data modeling, master data management, data standardization and stewardship, information integrity and compliance, and data quality management</li>
<li>
Knowledge of information access and delivery methods, analytics applications and tool portfolio, data sourcing and integration methods such as OLAP / ROLAP / HOLAP and knowledge of data warehousing/mining, ETL, EII, data cleansing and architecture</li>
<li>
Experience with enterprise-level and department-level analytics including assessments and strategy development</li>
<li>
Travel may be up to 80-100%</li>
<li>
Applicants must be currently authorized to work in the United States without the need for visa sponsorship now or in the future</li>
</ul>
