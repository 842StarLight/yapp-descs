<p><b>JOB SUMMARY – Assistant Manager</b></p>
<p>
If chosen for a Restaurant <b>Assistant Manager</b> position below is a list of JUST SOME of the responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Be a key support person for the GM as a Brand Ambassador for Chicken Salad Chick.</li>
<li>
Understand &amp; adhere/comply to all policies, procedures, standards, specifications, guidelines and training programs.</li>
<li>
Ensure that all Guests feel welcome and are given responsive, friendly and courteous service.</li>
<li>
Ensure that all products are consistently prepared and served according to CSC standards.</li>
<li>
Achieve identified objectives for sales, service, quality, appearance of facility and sanitation and cleanliness through training of employees and creating a positive, productive working environment.</li>
<li>
Control cash and other receipts by adhering to cash handling and reconciliation procedures.</li>
<li>
Make employment and termination decisions consistent with Restaurant Management guidelines.</li>
<li>
Operationally fill in as needed to ensure guest service standards and efficient operations.</li>
<li>
Continually strive to develop staff in all areas of managerial and professional development.</li>
<li>
Prepare all required paperwork, forms and reports in an organized and timely manner.</li>
<li>
Ensure that all equipment is kept clean and in excellent working condition through personal inspection and by following the restaurant’s preventative maintenance programs.</li>
<li>
Ensure that all products are received in correct unit count and condition and deliveries are performed in accordance with the restaurant’s receiving policies and procedures.</li>
<li>
Oversee and ensure that employee performance appraisals are completed on a timely basis.</li>
<li>
Always adhere to Brand Standards for uniform appearance and personal grooming.</li>
</ul>

<p><b>
REQUIREMENTS:</b></p>
<p>
Regular and reliable attendance and punctuality<br>
Reliable transportation during entire shift<br>
Ability to clearly and articulately communicate with team members and guests<br>
Ability to interact professionally and appropriately with all team members and guests<br>
Must have successfully completed formal Restaurant Management training classes provided by the Corporate Training Department</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
PHYSICAL REQUIREMENTS:</b></p>
<p>
Must be able to stand and exert well-paced mobility including bending and stooping for the duration of the workday<br>
Must be able to lift or carry 20 pounds<br>
Must be physically able to work at any duty station in the kitchen or service area</p>
