<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>About the team</b></h2>
Rentals is one of the largest, fastest growing and dynamic segments in real estate. Zillow Rentals (ZR) is on a mission to reimagine renting and become the leading destination Renters love and trust for finding and renting homes, enabled by partners and products that deliver the most properties and end-to-end renter services and an engaging, seamless customer experience. With the largest rental network and over 1 million daily visits from Renters, Landlords and Property managers, we are focused on accelerating business growth by driving adoption, usage and transactions!<br>
<br>
In this role, you’ll partner with product, business and sales teams to define the future of ZR and build marketing strategies and plans to evangelize it. The Director, Marketing, ZR sits within the Product Marketing team in the broader Zillow Marketing organization where we are organized to be the primary strategic partner to each product and business team, and the central hub that brings the customer journey - searching, renting, property management, rental services - together through integrated marketing programs.<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
About the role</b></h2>
<div><div>
<p>
This is a high visibility role that will directly impact both the ZR business and its development as a brand. You will be responsible for:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Identifying what is most needed and most compelling to customers – and sharing that insight back into the organization to influence product strategy, prioritization, marketing investment and related messaging.</li>
<li>
Leading the cross-functional development and execution of go-to-market strategies and plans designed to drive desired product critical metrics (e.g., awareness, consideration, trial and adoption).</li>
<li>
Monitoring, analyzing and reporting on product marketing program performance and making recommendations to improve both the product offering itself, and related marketing and messaging.</li>
<li>
Identifying and delivering new growth marketing opportunities to drive growth for ZR through cross-selling, promotions, partnerships, or other programs, with the potential to deliver customer value at scale.</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
Key Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Partner with ZR leadership to deeply understand the business including vision, near term and long term goals, operations, technology, sales, gaps &amp; opportunities, and the P&amp;L.</li>
<li>
Lead the annual marketing planning process for ZR and continue to optimize and iterate on that plan throughout the year.</li>
<li>
Identify and socialize key customers needs and unmet growth opportunities through partnerships with Consumer Insights, UX research, or 1st and 3rd party research.</li>
<li>
Recruit, cultivate and retain a best-in-class team of product marketers.</li>
<li>
Partner with Campaign Planning &amp; Management and channel teams to develop innovative and effective integrated omni-channel campaigns to drive acquisition, engagement and transactions.</li>
<li>
Lead cross-functional teams in the development of go-to-market strategies and plans designed to drive desired business critical metrics.</li>
<li>
Work with marketing channel owners (media, lifecycle) to support appropriate people and budgetary investment levels, and develop tactical plans.</li>
<li>
Together with the product team, educate both internal and external partners about our product features and their benefits.</li>
</ul>

Who you are</b></h2>
