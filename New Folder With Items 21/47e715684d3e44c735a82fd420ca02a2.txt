About The Role:<br>
<br>
The Enterprise Cloud Data Architect will be working closely – depending on each case - with Oracle CRM cloud Product Management and the development teams to develop new business as well as to position Oracle’s Solutions at the heart of the largest transformations, including a focus on solutions based on Cloud Services. As an Architect, you will lead and manage a variety of projects in the Oracle product development group. In this role, you will introduce new services, technologies, and technical solutions to Oracle CRM cloud solutions.<br>
You will drive technical solutions for large scale, always available, secure, fast and international ready solutions that will have a direct impact on the product.<br>
Applicants should be senior career-level IT architects, with significant experience in helping businesses transform their IT approach – for example by determining a Cloud strategy that aligns to and supports the current and future business and IT strategy, including being able to elaborate on how this will benefit to the cost model of operating IT platforms and systems.<br>
<br>
As an Architect, You will combine a deep understanding of modern cloud technology, industry best practices, business processes, and architectural patterns to deliver IT initiatives that meet customer's business objectives.<br>
<br>
Ideal Candidates will be seasoned senior-level professionals who not only possess deep experience with technology transformation but also significant experience in the cloud.<br>
<br>
Your skills and knowledge make you a natural go-to person, that people will turn to for guidance on Cloud and Sales Cloud Applications, modern application development concepts and technology in general. In these engagements, both prospective and running, you will act as the person providing creative impulses to keep the process going and as the technological conscience for the right architecture for the customer’s needs.<br>
<br>
<br>
Ideal candidates will be seasoned senior-level professionals who have deep experience with technology or experience in (Oracle) cloud topics like:<br>
<br>
Automation and scripting e.g. Infrastructure as Code<br>
Cloud-Native Development, MicroServices, Containerization, and Orchestration.<br>
Multi-Cloud Applications and Integrations<br>
Continuous Integration and Continuous Delivery/Deployment Pipelines,<br>
Business Event Streaming (e.g. Kafka) &amp; Event Handling</div>
<div>OCI (Oracle Cloud Infrastructure) knowledge is a big plus.<br>

Responsibilities:<br>
 Define patterns and best practices for application and infrastructure design, engineering and development.<br>
 Lead, mentor and provide design and architecture for delivery teams. Collaborate with product and engineering leads and senior management on product and delivery requirements, roadmaps and vision, working closely with delivery teams from project inception to implementation.<br>
 Take ownership of design for a product.<br>
 Expert in the latest technologies and techniques, the go-to person for senior developers wanting technical advice.<br>
 Conduct repeatable strategy, architecture, roadmap and planning workshops.<br>
 Provide an assessment of current state architecture and recommendations of future state architecture.<br>
 Define, develop and evolve architecture standards and principles.<br>
 Provide non-routine and complex statements of work, work breakdown structure and/or level of effort and staff plans.<br>
 Leads the architecture and solution design aspects of engagement(s) ensuring high quality and integrated cross-domain business solutions.<br>
 Identify and evangelize the key business and technology benefits of adoption.<br>
 Advise on the organizational impact of solutions.<br>
 Play an active role as part of the Sales Cloud architecture community.<br>
 Remain fully aware of relevant architectural programs and assets from Oracle worldwide.<br>
 Remain aware of all relevant product developments and references – both at Oracle and at competitors.<br>
 Contribute to, drive and deliver large/innovative Oracle Development with a rigorous business development focus.<br>
 Exercise creativity, independent judgment and business acumen in selecting methods and techniques to design non-routine and very complex business solutions utilizing competitive technology to meet customer needs.<br>
