<p>Associate Wealth Management Advisor</p>
<p>Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>FINRA Series 7, 65 or 66, Wisconsin Life/Health Insurance License.</li>
<li>Minimum of 3 years’ experience in a support role – this includes but not limited to experience in brokerage houses, financial services firms, or financial planning and/or wealth management firms.</li>
</ul>
<p>Preferred Skills/ Traits:</p>
<ul>
<li>Red Tail CRM Software fluency.</li>
<li>EMoney Planning and client aggregate software fluency.</li>
<li>Riskalyze asset allocation client facing software fluency.</li>
<li>Morningstar analytical software fluency, preferably Morningstar Office.</li>
<li>Interest in Financial Planning and Investments.</li>
<li>Excellent Interpersonal, verbal and written communication skills.</li>
<li>Goal oriented and hard working.</li>
<li>Knowledge in financial planning, including but not limited to goals-based planning, assets allocation and retirement planning.</li>
<li>Highly organized.</li>
<li>Passionate about their work and enjoys people.</li>
</ul>
<p>Duties:</p>
<ul>
<li>Meeting with new and existing clients that are at or below the $250,000 AUM threshold.</li>
<li>Go over financial recommendations, fees, financial reviews, and hypothetical illustrations with clients in person, net meeting, or over the phone.</li>
<li>Preparing financial plans for new and existing clients.</li>
<li>Preparing for Client Reviews – Performance and allocation reports.</li>
<li>Assisting with paperwork preparation and submission for New and Existing Clients.</li>
<li>Schedule client and prospective client meetings for self and or the Financial Advisor.</li>
<li>Filing paperwork and client documents as required by appropriate regulators.</li>
<li>Process trades.</li>
<li>Research mutual funds as part of our internal investment committee.</li>
<li>Research variable annuity product when needed.</li>
<li>Assist with Insurance product sales when necessary.</li>
<li>Analysis on prospective client accounts and provide feedback to the Financial Advisor.</li>
<li>Work directly with the Planning Consultant/Retirement Plan Specialist on joint case work</li>
<li>Assist clients with financial transactions outside of our office</li>
<li>Assist with procedure writing/video recordings</li>
<li>Prospect and promote Onward Financial Network though EMoney.</li>
<li>Report Pipeline activities</li>
<li>Proper documentation of work for OFN meetings and through Redtail.</li>
<li>Assist in Enrollment or Education meetings with Qualified Plan.</li>
<li>Understanding and working with all OFN processes for new client acquisition and service.</li>
<li>Roll out 2 Corporate Onward Money training each month until project completed</li>
<li>5 New prospect calls weekly</li>
<li>Update and report template through OneNote.</li>
<li>Work with Qualified Plan Team on Census Mining and promotion</li>
<li>Work with WMA Drip Campaigns for new business and service.</li>
</ul>
