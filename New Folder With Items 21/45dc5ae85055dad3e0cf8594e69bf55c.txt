<h2>Warehouse Associate-Lead</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>Carrier Enterprise (CE) is a subsidiary of the largest independent HVAC/R distribution company in the world, WATSCO, Inc. CE proudly distributes the Carrier, Bryant, and Payne and Gree brands of HVAC equipment, as well as a full-line of aftermarket parts, supplies and accessories. CE has over 150 locations throughout 28 states, Puerto Rico, the Caribbean, and Latin America, with over 1600 employees ready to serve you, providing the best products with best-in-class customer service. We currently have an exciting opportunity for a <b>Warehouse Associate-Lead at our Little Rock, AR location.
</b>
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Job Responsibilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Shipping and receiving
</li>
<li>Stores materials/equipment
</li>
<li>Controls Inventory
</li>
<li>Upholds environmental health and safety standards
</li>
<li>Works closely with internal customers (Store Associates and Sales Managers)
</li>
<li>Provides excellent customer service to external customers (mechanical contractors; dealers, owners)
</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Education:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>High School diploma or equivalent GED required</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Experience/Qualifications:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 2-3 years of warehouse operational experience with 1 year with lead warehouse duties preferred</li>
<li>HVAC experience is preferred, but not required
</li>
<li>Strong attention to detail and accuracy
</li>
<li>PC skills and inventory and shipping/receiving systems experience required
</li>
<li>Excellent communication (written and oral) and interpersonal (Teambuilding) skills</li>
<li>Proven commitment to customer service and a safe work environment
</li>
<li>Must be team-oriented
</li>
</ul>
