<h2>Biology Product Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Want to be part of a company whose goal is to be the best in the world and the best for the world?</p>
<p>
Apply today!</p>
<p>
The purpose of the Product Marketing Manager is to drive marketing initiatives, projects and strategic market research designed to promote, support and grow current GENEWIZ service lines. Post-docs with interest in business encouraged to apply!</p>
<p>
Title: Product Marketing Manager, Biology</p>
<p>
Location: South Plainfield, NJ</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Analyze market trends in Synthetic Biology or Next-Generation Sequencing and characterize the competitive landscape to identify target markets and develop lead generation strategies for the GENEWIZ Gene Synthesis or Next Generation Sequencing service lines</li>
<li>Identify opportunities for increasing customer and business value through new Synthetic Biology, Gene Synthesis, Next-Generation Sequencing, or Protein Engineering services or service differentiation by representing the voice of the customer</li>
<li>Evaluate and communicate competitive threats, strengths and weaknesses, and fine-tune service offerings and forecasts accordingly</li>
<li>Work with business leaders and stakeholders to define pricing strategy, service positioning and differentiation within the Gene Synthesis, Synthetic Biology, Protein Engineering, or Next-Generation Sequencing markets</li>
<li>Support enterprise and business unit planning and forecasting</li>
<li>Work with marketing communications to develop content and strategies for outbound communication and sales collateral for all GENEWIZ synthetic biology products and services.</li>
<li>Develop and implement company-wide go-to-market plans for new services and work with all departments to execute</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Master's degree in Business or Life Sciences required</li>
<li>Ph.D. in Business or Life Science preferred. Post-docs with interest in business encouraged to apply!</li>
<li>3+ years of post-graduate school experience in academia or in the biotech/pharmaceutical industry with a focus on genomics research, marketing, product management, or market research required.
</li>
<li>4-6 + years of post-graduate school experience in academia or in the biotech/pharmaceutical industry with a focus on genomics research, marketing, product management, or market research preferred</li>
<li>Experience in customer-facing position within the biotechnology or science industry preferred</li>
<li>Strong understanding of genomics, synthetic biology, gene synthesis, next-generation sequencing and molecular biology products and services</li>
<li>Must have excellent interpersonal skills; both verbal and written skills are essential</li>
</ul>
