<h2>Account Executive - Social Media Management and Marketing Automation SaaS</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Account Executive – Cross Sell/Upsell</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
The ClickDimensions<b> Account Executive (AE)</b> role is a sales position responsible for generating cross sell revenue from new products to ClickDimensions installed base of over 3,500 customers. Duties of the AE include:</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Meeting or exceeding monthly revenue targets by managing a pipeline of direct, cross sell/upsell opportunities into the ClickDimensions base of over 3,500 existing customers. This is a high velocity job and in a typical month you will be expected to close between 8 to 10 deals with an average ARR over $10K each.</li>
<li>Demonstrating the ClickDimensions social management and marketing analytics add-on that enables customers to integrate their social media marketing into their existing ClickDimensions marketing automation platform.</li>
<li>Work closely with Customer Success Managers and new logo sales team to maximize pipeline and sales for cross sell and upsell.</li>
<li>Keep CRM current for all deals and generate accurate forecasts for in-month and in-quarter sales</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<p>
The ideal ClickDimensions AE candidate will have the following:
</p>
<ul>
<li>4 or more years’ experience selling B2B SaaS/Martech software (specifically Social Media Management and Marketing Automation SaaS)</li>
<li>Verifiable record of on target sales performance, preferably in a SaaS software</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to communicate with mid to senior-level marketing executives in small and medium sized companies</li>
<li>Ability to navigate around low-level blockers to get to key decision makers</li>
<li>Ability to communicate clearly and effectively in person, through email and via telephone (this position is primarily phone-based with little to no travel required)</li>
<li>Track record of disciplined use of CRM to track account and pipeline</li>
<li>Ability to give a world class demonstration of a software-based product both live and via webinar</li>
<li>Strong written, verbal and organizational skills</li>
<li>High energy, can-do attitude</li>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in related field preferred</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p><b>
</b></p>
<p><b>Additional Information</b></p>
<p><b>
“Day in the Life”</b></p>
<p>
On any given day the AE may be conducting multiple online meetings with existing customers to pitch and discuss our catalog of cross sell/upsell products, product demonstrations, following up on numerous pipeline opportunities, meeting with the CSMs to discuss pipeline creation, updating records in the CRM, meeting with management to review progress toward goals and more.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Travel</b></p>
<p>
The AE role is performed mainly from our Atlanta office near the intersection of GA 400 and 285 and little to no travel is required.</p>
