<h2>Business Analyst Student</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader">
<b>Responsibilities</b><b>
</b>
</h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Every day, in nearly 100 countries around the world, millions of people depend on Daktronics scoring and display systems for information and entertainment. Our displays provide vital travel information in airports and train stations and along highways. They advertise the price of gas, store specials, and more as you make your daily commute. They also enhance spectators’ experiences at sporting events of all kinds, from Little League to the major leagues.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Position located in Brookings, South Dakota. Candidates must be enrolled and registered as a full-time student. Preferred candidate should be available to work 15-20 hours per week during the academic year and at least 20 hours per week during the summer.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
A Business Analyst student at Daktronics is typically focused on data needs throughout our business but may also assist with product management responsibilities.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
In more detail, you will:</div>
<ul>
<li>
Assist with data and costing needs for products and services, by entering, gathering and organizing data, analyzing costs, and responding to requests in a timely manner. This may also include working directly with internal customers.</li>
<li>
Ensure data integrity for product components and custom pricing entries.</li>
<li>
Assist with maintenance and testing of pricing and quoting tools.
</li>
<li>Request, run, manage and analyze reports using internal programs such as reporting services Glovia, Pipeline, Excel, or other.</li>
<li>
Develop and maintain standard dashboard reports that will show historical product trends such as failures, orders, win/loss, custom quotes, etc.
</li>
<li>Manage part number/shop drawing requests from sales.</li>
<li>
Assist in SOP activities such as maintenance of spreadsheets and print outs.</li>
<li>
Analyze and correctly assembly packet costs and codes.</li>
<li>
Assist in product launch activities such as quoting, tools, spec sheets, costing, etc.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
