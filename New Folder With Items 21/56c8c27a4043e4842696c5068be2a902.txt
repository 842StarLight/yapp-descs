<h2>Office Administrator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>We're recruiting for an enthusiastic and detail-oriented <b>Office Administrator </b>to provide support to our Sales and Production teams – as well as our valued customers – at our Tuff Shed Factory Store located in <b>Chicago.</b></p>
<p><b>
About TUFF SHED:</b></p>
<p>
Founded on an entrepreneurial spirit and an unwavering commitment to quality, Tuff Shed was established in 1981, and has since developed into America's largest manufacturer and installer of storage buildings and garages, having built more than one million buildings for satisfied customers. We utilize cutting edge green technology in our materials and manufacturing processes, hold multiple U.S. Patents, and are an approved GSA vendor.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>What makes a successful TUFF SHED Office Administrator?</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Strong, proven <b>customer service skills</b>, working directly with customers, vendors, subcontractors, and Sales Consultants to coordinate and schedule Tuff Shed installations. Ability to predict and resolve all levels of issues.</li>
<li>
<b>Attention to detail</b>, preparing the daily "packs" required to install our quality products. This includes <b>verifying orders, requesting materials, and creating purchase orders and waivers.</b>
</li>
<li>Acting as the <b>first contact with our "Big Box" partner</b>, responding to queries, <b>managing the</b> <b>database</b>, and providing assistance to our Area Sales Managers.</li>
<li>Consistently understanding and meeting the needs of our customers, <b>recognizing special needs</b> and <b>recommending appropriate upsells</b>.</li>
<li>
<b>Experience working in "trades / industries"</b> and or the ability to learn technical aspects related to <b>basic product design and functionality</b>.</li>
<li>Ability to assist the General Manager with <b>various front office needs as they may arise, including</b> HR processes such as pre-employment checks and documentation, benefits management and monitoring of the employee time-tracking system.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Skills / Experience:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Successful <b>prior experience working in logistics, coordination, project management,</b> or in a similar capacity.</li>
<li>Proven experience working in a <b>fast-paced environment</b>, meeting <b>tight deadlines</b> on multiple projects.</li>
<li>Hands-on computer skills including <b>Word, Excel</b> and <b>Outlook</b>. Experience in <b>JD Edwards</b> software highly preferred.</li>
<li>Ability to take on a role with a <b>steep learning curve</b>. You'll be expected to know the details about our products and discuss offers and options with our partners and customers.</li>
<li>High school diploma or equivalent. This role will require <b>strong math and communication skills.</b>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
What's in it for you?</b></p>
<p>
As a Tuff Shed Office Administrator, you'll have the opportunity to join a successful company, and be part of the growth team! We offer a competitive salary and benefits including paid vacation, paid holidays, medical/dental/vision and life insurance, and a 'Safe Harbor' 401k plan.</p>
<p></p>

</ul>
