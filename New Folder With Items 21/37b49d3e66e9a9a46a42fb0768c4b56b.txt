<h2>Customer Relationship Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div><div>
<p>We are looking for a Business Development professional to seek new business opportunities by contacting and developing relationships with potential customers.</p>
<p>
To be successful in this role, you will use your previous experience developing leads and outstanding communication skills to cultivate strong relationships with customers, from first contact until you pass the qualified lead to our technical experts. If you are motivated and results-driven and enjoy working in a team environment, we’d like to meet you.</p>
<p>
You:</p>
<ul>
<li>Systematically qualify leads from various sources including but not limited to event lists, membership lists, and your own market research.</li>
<li>Relentlessly contact potential clients through cold calls and emails.</li>
<li>Are eager to present our company to potential clients.</li>
<li>Identify client needs and suggest appropriate products/services.</li>
<li>Build long-term trusting relationships with clients.</li>
<li>Proactively seek new business opportunities in the market.</li>
<li>Set up meetings or calls between (prospective) clients and our technical experts.</li>
<li>Have proven, successful work experience in a B2B business development role.</li>
<li>Have experience with CRM software.</li>
<li>Have a degree in Marketing, Business Administration or relevant field or work experience.</li>
<li>Enjoy meeting commitments and working independently to not just achieve but beat goals.</li>
<li>You were among the first chosen for the kickball team in grade school.</li>
<li>You’ve never met a stranger.</li>
<li>You believe everything in life is a competition.</li>
</ul>
