<h2>DIRECTOR, FP&A (PROJECTS)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>THE COMPANY:
</b></p>
<p>Founded in 2012 and headquartered in Houston, Texas, Sunnova is the leading privately held U.S. residential solar + battery storage service provider. Sunnova’s goal is to provide the choice of low-cost, worry-free solar energy and battery storage technology that generates long-term value and savings for customers and facilitates continued business growth for its network of trusted partners. Our mission is to power energy independence for our nearly 60,000 customers across the US and its territories.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>THE POSITION:
</b></p>
<p>As the Director, FP&amp;A (Projects), you will work with the CEO, CFO and other executive management, playing a key role, you will serve a pivotal role in financial planning and decision support, working across all departments to provide a wide range of financial and decisions support information to senior management and department VPs</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>JOB RESPONSIBILITIES:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Leads the budgeting, forecasting, long-term planning, implementation, and business strategic analysis of the financial team.</li>
<li>Conducts financial analysis, modeling, forecasting and reporting efforts to ensure efficient financial operations.</li>
<li>Reviews analysis results, develops insightful suggestions for investment, sourcing, cost reduction and leads strategic business decisions.</li>
<li>Prepare and maintain 13-week liquidity forecasts</li>
<li>Month-end reporting and analysis, including preparation of monthly portfolio reports, board reports and other ad-hoc requests</li>
<li>Operational and management reporting</li>
<li>Trend analysis</li>
<li>Ad hoc analysis to support management decision-making for new initiatives</li>
<li>This will include preparing and reviewing a variety of complex financial data and will maintain and update the marketing/sales financial models and forecasts.</li>
<li>Review actuals vs. forecasts, investigate material variances, develop tools for improving the forecasting and actual results and roll them out to the executive and management team.</li>
<li>Supporting capital markets initiatives that may include analyses related to raising debt and equity capital, preparation of investor/lender books, covenant reporting, and other tasks as needed.</li>
<li>With respect to business Intelligence, partner with Data &amp; Analytics team to create and automate a business intelligence framework to support various company objectives.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Critical Responsibilities</b><br>
<br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Planning process – Direct the compilation of business unit plans and present analyses of forecasted amounts in relation to actual results and previous forecasts, and expectations.</li>
<li>Highlight and understand major assumptions and their effects on the budget/forecast.</li>
<li>Perform sensitivity analyses to assess reasonable range of outcomes.</li>
<li>Analyze past results and future outcomes in conjunction with market analyses to develop recommendations for changes in model assumptions. Perform sensitivity analyses to assess reasonable range of outcomes.</li>
<li>Financial analysis – Present analyses on forecasts and trends. Analysis for forecasts includes identifying, understanding and communicating changes in forecasts. Trends must also be identified, often by thorough data analysis.</li>
<li>Cost Savings, including financing savings opportunities for the Company.</li>
<li>Financial analysis also encompasses leveraging tools to quickly access data and present meaningful, concise information.</li>
<li>Business analysis – Prepare executive summaries for topics such as market analysis, macro-economic conditions and financial metrics. These types of analyses require an ongoing understanding of key markets, competition and economic conditions. They also require an understanding of how these factors affect various aspects of our business, as well as our relative position in markets. Identifying and conveying key influences on our businesses and trends is expected.</li>
<li>Process improvement – Continuously develop systems and processes. Evaluate current processes, identify best practices, develop plans to reach goals and implement improvements.</li>
<li>Project support and ad hoc requests – Projects may include return on investment analyses for major projects and other analysis. Competencies: MBA or CPA preferred, with a bachelor’s degree in finance or accounting. Job Requirements</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>JOB REQUIREMENTS:<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>14+ years of experience</li>
<li>Bachelor’s in Accounting or Finance</li>
<li>MBA/CFA highly preferred</li>
<li>High proficiency and mastery of Excel</li>
<li>Significant expertise with enterprise cloud financial reporting software</li>
<li>Significant expertise with Hyperion/ESS base/Vena or similar financial reporting systems</li>
<li>Strong financial and accounting background, have a good understanding of overall business operations and will enjoy working on very complex and critical projects.</li>
<li>High proficiency and mastery with advanced Excel capabilities including advanced pivot table design, VBA applications, and query languages essential. Tableau a plus.</li>
<li>Able to master new financial systems quickly and very adept at information systems</li>
</ul>
