<h2>Travel Reservation Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Job Locations</b> US-LA-Lake Charles<br>
<b>Category</b> Customer Service/Support<br>
<br>
<b>Overview</b><br>
<br>
This is a remote, independent contractor role that provides personalized customer service through phone, email and/or live chat support. Identifies and resolves problems, as well as provides a consultative sales approach regarding various options to guide callers to the right solutions for their individual needs. Works in a fast-paced, work-at-home, customer-service environment.<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities</b><br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Practicing excellent verbal and written communication skills.
</li>
<li>Providing top-notch, customer-service skills while guiding callers to the right solutions for their specific needs.
</li>
<li>Having a positive attitude; being flexible and adaptable to change.
</li>
<li>Researching, navigating and independently locating answers from webpages and resources in a variety of situations.</li>
<li>
Communicating effectively, based on a customer's mindset.</li>
<li>
Exercising strong, multitasking skills while navigating through multiple websites and being available for incoming calls.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>Qualifications</b><br>
<br>
<b>Education and Experience:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>At least 1 to 2 years of experience in a high-volume, customer-service role (i.e. call center, retail or similar environment).</li>
<li>
Having achieved a satisfactory level of technical skill or knowledge. Familiarity with operating systems, including Windows OS versions and/or Mac OS versions. Ability to install and uninstall applications. Ability to download products and applications using various browsers.
</li>
<li>Experience with complex problem resolution a plus.<br>
</li>
</ul>

Windows 10 ONLY</li>
