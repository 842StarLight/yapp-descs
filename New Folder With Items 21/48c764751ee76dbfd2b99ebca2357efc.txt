<h2>Customer Service Team Lead for Newspaper Call Center</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Sound Publishing, Inc. is currently accepting applications for a full-time Call Center Customer Service Team Lead. The position will be based out of our Everett office and will report to the Director of Audience Development. The primary duty is to manage the call center staffing, training, regular reporting, staff contests, subscriber retention activities and assuring great customer service for The Daily Herald and our community newspapers. Call center experience preferred.</p>
<p><b>
 Candidate will:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Set &amp; amp; communicate CSR schedules two weeks of advance of work date.</li>
<li>Create a fun, but business-focused environment for the customer service team.</li>
<li>Assure that call center is fully staffed and promote/hire to keep staffing at 100%.</li>
<li>Create monthly sales contests focusing on areas like stop saves EZ pay conversions.</li>
<li>Assign retention calls and make sure all calls are made.</li>
<li>Create weekly stop reports for President, Director, and STC.</li>
<li>Have monthly 15-minute one-on-ones with each CSR for training, employee feedback &amp; coaching.</li>
<li>Assure that Daily Herald Dayend processing is completed accurately and in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Attends bi-weekly STC meetings to provide customer care concerns, delivery problems, and subscriber feedback.</li>
<li>Attend bi-weekly circulation staff meetings and report on the status of the home delivery operations and subscribers.</li>
<li>Prepare weekly retention reports for Audience Dev Director and monthly CSR commission payments.</li>
<li>Create call center volume reports by month.</li>
<li>Must be a pro-active individual who works well with co-workers, delivery, dealer site staff, and carrier contractors.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Required Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Exceptional history of providing great customer service.</li>
<li>To be comfortable using Microsoft Office, intermediate skills with Word and Excel, and able to quickly learn new proprietary software.</li>
<li>Must possess reliable, insured, a motor vehicle; and a clean and valid Washington State driver’s license.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What we offer you:</b></p>
<p>
We offer competitive compensation and a benefits package that includes medical, dental, vision and life insurance; paid time off (vacation, sick, and holidays); and a 401k with employer match.</p>

TO APPLY</b> – please e-mail to careers@soundpublishing.com a resume including at least three professional references. Make sure to include <b>Call CenterTL in the subject line</b>. Please also include your salary requirements.</p>
