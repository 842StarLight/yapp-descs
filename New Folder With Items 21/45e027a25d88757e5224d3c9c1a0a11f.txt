<div>Cove is a startup company in the home security marketplace aiming to revolutionize the billion-dollar industry. It is focused on providing its customers with excellent, easy, enjoyable and economical home security services with nationwide coverage. Its aim? To protect every home in the United States. With a hard to beat product and business model, we are positioned to disrupt multiple categories in the security world.<br>

Cove is looking for an experienced Amazon Marketplace leader to join our team and lead the expansion and optimization of Cove throughout our partner channels. You will have the experience to lead and grow Coves expansion within online marketplaces.<br>
