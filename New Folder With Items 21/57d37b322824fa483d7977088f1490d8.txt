<h2>Fleet Supervisor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Overview of the Fleet Supervisor position</b></p>
<p>
This position is responsible for the management of the Fleet Mechanic team, monitor their effectiveness as a team, and work with operations to assure all assets are available when needed.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provide direct supervision of Fleet Mechanics.</li>
<li>Monitor on-time performance and lane effectiveness and recommend changes when necessary.</li>
<li>Ensure compliance with all DOT Regulations and all internal requirements.</li>
<li>Identify best practices for traffic patterns, asset security and safety.</li>
<li>Work with Area and Production Manager to control the Fleet Department annual expense.</li>
<li>Work with and monitor associates to ensure that the fleet is maintained in accordance with the established Standards of Condition and that all established policies and practices are properly followed.</li>
<li>Ensure that all PM's are performed timely.</li>
<li>Monitor labor efficiencies of all Fleet Mechanics.</li>
<li>This position will ensure high quality standards with regards to fleet maintenance and repairs at the lowest achievable cost while protecting and maintaining company assets.</li>
<li>Management and training of the fleet technicians: vehicle maintenance, internal customer relations, management of fleet vendors, and fleet related record keeping and invoice processing.</li>
<li>Work closely with Route Operations to make sure that all vehicle and equipment needs are met on time.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<div>
<div><div><div><ul>
<li>High School Diploma required.</li>
<li>A minimum of 3 years in fleet maintenance and logistics and at least 2 years in supervision and/ or management.</li>
<li>Proficiency in PC Applications; word processing, presentations, logistics software and databases.</li>
<li>Must have familiarity with DOT regulations, governing the use of Commercial Vehicles, as well as strong mechanical aptitude and working knowledge of vehicle systems, components and design characteristics.</li>
<li>Must possess a Class A Driver License.</li>
<li>Must possess excellent communication, coaching, and leadership skills, as well as have the ability to influence and make decisions by applying independent judgment.</li>
<li>Experience with the EAM <b>Fleet</b> Maintenance System is preferred.</li>
<li>Ability to meet Department of Transportation (DOT) driver qualification policy requirements.</li>
<br>
</ul></div></div></div>
