Your Opportunity: Vectra Visual, a Taylor company, is adding a strong Business Development/Sales Executive to support our California market by focusing on selling in-store signage and retail display programs to national retail brands and retailers utilizing Vectra Visual’s in-house capabilities including design services, wide and small format digital printing, fabrication, and installation.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Your Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Prospect for new clients, initiate calls and meetings, conduct presentations, submit quote requests, present/deliver proposals, and delegate work to an Account/Project Manager to facilitate customer transactions.</li>
<li>
Leverage existing relationships to on-board new retail signage and display programs that fit Vectra Visual’s core capabilities.</li>
<li>
Drive innovation by bringing internal and external teams together to deliver industry leading solutions to clients and prospects.</li>
<li>
Stay abreast of industry trends and materials, plus other forms of advertising media.</li>
<li>
Responsible for client relationships from inception through retention.</li>
</ul>

<ul>
<li>
Ability to analyze data and develop appropriate strategies to address a variety of situations.</li>
<li>
Ability to exercise judgment, with regard to established procedures and practices in determining an appropriate action to situations.</li>
<li>
Solid independent decision making capabilities.</li>
<li>
Strong organizational skills and good attention to detail.</li>
<li>
Experience with Microsoft Office Suite and general computer skills.</li>
