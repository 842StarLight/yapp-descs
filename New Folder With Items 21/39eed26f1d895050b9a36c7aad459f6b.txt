<h2>Property Administrator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Property Administrator supports the on-site, day-to-day property management functions of a commercial office building(s) by performing tasks in the areas of administration, tenant relations, property appearance, leasing, reporting, and construction management.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Functions</b></p>
<p>Essential Functions include but are not limited to:</p>
<ul><li> Enter and track work order requests and coordinate with vendors such as janitorial, signage, landscaping, painting, and security.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Provide administrative and general office support for Property Management and Engineering staff such as filing, providing front desk back-up, processing mail, document preparation, records maintenance, coding and processing invoices, assigning and maintaining logs of purchase orders, confirming and auditing visa statements, ordering supplies, preparation/delivery of mail, and maintaining office equipment.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Track vendor and tenant insurance certificates.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Track data such as LEED and EnergyStar maintenance and administrative license and certification renewals.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Support day-to-day tenant activities including coordinating keys, issuing access cards, writing tenant bulletins, coordinating tenant move in/out, organizing special events, and scheduling freight elevators and conference rooms.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Assist in building marketing and new and renewal leasing activities including lease administration, reporting, and events.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Maintain property appearance and condition by coordinating and overseeing the quality of vendor’s delivery of services such as janitorial, landscaping, and signage.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Assist with close out of tenant improvement or building capital projects.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Assist with compiling information and writing reports.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Assist with Accounts Receivable calls and collection of tenant rent.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Assemble monthly reporting and annual budgets for distribution.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Support Unico’s investment teams with due diligence for building acquisitions and sales.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Build positive business relationships, and promote a culture and demonstrate leadership that is committed to excellent service and sustainability.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Track and assist in negotiating market common service contracts.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Assist and track market common budgets.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Assist in monthly tenant bill backs and utility recovery billings.</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Skills</b></p>
<ul><li> Excellent customer relation skills and experience, with ability to interact with the general public</li></ul>
<ul><li> Excellent communication skills</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ability to analyze moderately complex administrative details, such as planning, scheduling, reporting, and displaying data</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ability to operate various computer software programs, specifically Microsoft Office Tools</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<ul><li> High School diploma or equivalent</li></ul>
<ul><li> 1-3 years’ experience in a customer service and/or administrative position</li></ul>
</div></div>
