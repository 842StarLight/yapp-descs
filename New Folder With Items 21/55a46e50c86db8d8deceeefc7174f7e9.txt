<h2>Salesforce Architect</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Job Duties</p>
<ul>
<li>Effectively deals with a multitude of ambiguity by confidently walking through an undefined situation and drawing out key requirements and political constraints</li>
<li>Conducts facilitated working sessions with technical executives and experts</li>
<li>Participates in hiring resources Conduct hands-on development, configuration, or system setup</li>
<li>Present technology architecture and solution overviews to executive-level audiences</li>
<li>Create presentation materials with emphasis on simplifying complex ideas into digestible topics</li>
<li>Provide leadership across multiple accounts as well as support sales and business development activities</li>
<li>Apply technical aptitude and design patters across multiple industry verticals and use cases</li>
<li>Conduct assessments and define enterprise roadmaps for enabling digital transformation</li>
<li>Travels to client locations</li>
</ul>
<p>Requirements</p>
<ul>
<li>5-7+ years of SFDC experience</li>
<li>Practiced at leading teams of 2-10+ technical resources</li>
<li>Experience with the Salesforce platform, including template solutions like Sales Cloud, Service Cloud, Marketing Cloud</li>
<li>Experience with custom application development in the Salesforce Platform, utilizing Visualforce and Lightning Components/Framework</li>
<li>Experience with designing and implementing custom applications on the Salesforce platform using Apex programming</li>
<li>Experience with object modeling in Salesforce and understanding of fundamental database concepts (including SOQL)</li>
<li>Background in application development using technologies like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript</li>
<li>Experience with integrating with the Salesforce platform (inbound/outbound) using the REST/SOAP APIs, MuleSoft, or Data Loader</li>
</ul>
