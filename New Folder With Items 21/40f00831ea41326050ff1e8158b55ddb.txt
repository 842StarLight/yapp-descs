<h2>Construction Accountant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div><div><div>
<p>RJM Construction is a fast growing, high energy, commercial general contracting company located in Minneapolis. Voted as one of the “100 Best Companies to Work For” by Minnesota Business Magazine, we invest in our people and attract, develop and retain talented professionals in roles where they can make an impact, as well as grow personally and professionally.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>As our Construction Accountant, you will be responsible for providing accounting, analysis and financial support to the construction project team. You will serve as a key financial liaison for the team as it relates to customer billings, vendor/subcontractor invoice processing and financial project closeout.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Specifically, your job duties will include:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Process subcontractor applications for payment and vendor invoices for project manager approval</li>
<li>Gain an understanding of the sales/use tax implications for assigned projects</li>
<li>Prepare monthly billings for assigned projects and ensure all cost are billed in a timely manner</li>
<li>Work with project managers to ensure timely collection of customer invoices
</li>
<li>Provide assistance to the project team in complying with various contractual requirements</li>
<li>Subcontractor lien waiver tracking and collection of final statements of contract</li>
<li>Project audit and annual audit support as needed</li>
<li>Respond to subcontractor/vendor inquiries</li>
<br>
</ul>
