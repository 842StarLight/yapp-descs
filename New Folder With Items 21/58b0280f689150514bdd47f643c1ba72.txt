<p><b>What will you contribute?
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>The Marketing organization is responsible for raising the brand awareness of Finastra in the market and driving the demand for Finastra software and solutions. The Marketing team covers Regional marketing (Americas, EMEA and APAC) supported by Corporate functions of Solution Marketing; Brand and Communications, Customer and Partner Marketing; Digital Marketing and Operations.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The Global Marketing Program specialist will:
</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Report to the Global Head of Regional Marketing
</li>
<li>Support a management system &amp; cadence to run all regional Marketing Demand Generation efforts
</li>
<li>Build and manage best-in class reporting and insight for all territory (Analytics)
</li>
<li>Collaborate closely with the Marketing Operations team and our Marketing Center of Excellence
</li>
<li>Work on or contribute to specific projects or programs related to these functions:
<ul>
<li>Marketing innovation
</li>
<li>Digital Marketing journey improvement
</li>
<li>Demand Generation efficiency
</li>
<li>Regional Marketing operations
</li>
<li>Account Based and Target account Marketing
</li>
<li>Marketing technology implementation
</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<ul><li>so that Regional marketing management &amp; teams and other functions (listed above) can operate at the highest levels of effectiveness and efficiency.
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities &amp; Deliverables:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Support a management system &amp; cadence to run all regional Marketing Demand Generation efforts
</li>
<li>Coordinate the Marketing plan, budget and finance reporting on a monthly / quarterly basis with all regional marketing directors and their teams
</li>
<li>Orchestrate the regional Marketing management reviews and regional all hands calls
</li>
<li>Articulate a strong system to keep and track actions and deadlines from programs, meetings and reviews
</li>
<li>Manage some key programs and initiatives on behalf of the Global head of regional Marketing and the Regional Marketing directors.
</li>
<li>Understand and run the existing Marketing reporting model, KPIs and Vista best practices in collaboration with the Operation Marketing team and the Marketing Center of Excellence. Contribute to the CMO dashboard
</li>
<li>Report regularly on execution and performance to the Marketing management, Marketing teams and regional sales and sales operation stakeholders
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Deliverables to include:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Marketing plans, budget plans and finance updates
</li>
<li>CMO dashboard contributions (Demand Generation and specific programs)
</li>
<li>Program status report
</li>
<li>Action trackers
</li>
</ul>

</b></p>

</li>

</li>
