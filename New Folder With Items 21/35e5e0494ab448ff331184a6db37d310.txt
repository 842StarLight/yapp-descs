<p><b>Company Overview</b></p>
<p>KARL STORZ Endovision (KSE) develops, manufactures and services fiberoptic and digital video endoscopes. KSE services all flexible endoscopes distributed in North America, as well as, many globally distributed endoscopes. The facility also manufactures and services a variety of electromechanical devices -light sources, insufflators and other electronic accessories that provides high quality medical care. To become part of this dynamic team, we invite you to learn more about our opportunities.</p>

<p></p>
<p><b>Role Overview</b></p>
<p>The purpose of this position is responsible for performing various Quality Engineering Activities whose main purpose is to facilitate continuous improvement for both products and processes.. Activities include executing, overseeing, and improving audit and CAPA processes as well as other processes within Quality Assurance.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Key Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Execute, oversee, and improve CAPA process</li>
<li>Preparation and analysis of periodic Quality Reports.</li>
<li>Execute, oversee, and improve internal audits process and results.</li>
<li>Support facility during external audits and subsequent action as needed</li>
<li>Selection, Qualification and monitoring of outside suppliers.</li>
<li>Preparation of Engineering Changes.</li>
<li>Provide training and direction to manufacturing and QA personnel.</li>
<li>Create, sustain and improve elements of Quality Management System</li>
<li>Perform system and component defect analysis in conjunction with the continuous improvement program.</li>
<li>Ensures that appropriate Risk management systems and programs are in place and effective.</li>
<li>Must be an independent worker, capable of meeting goals and objectives as defined by Management.</li>
<li>Support quality/manufacturing related improvement efforts.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Key Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>BS Degree in a Technical / Engineering discipline.</li>
<li>Two years minimum experience within manufacturing environment, with experience in manufacturing, quality, or process engineering preferred. Medical Device experience preferred. CQE preferred</li>
<li>Must have knowledge of ISO and FDA/QSR Regulations</li>
<li>Strong computer skills with software applications for Microsoft Windows including Excel, Word and Minitab.</li>
<li>Knowledge of Six Sigma and Lean manufacturing preferred.</li>
<li>Ability to communicate effectively with personnel at all levels both verbally and in writing.</li>
<li>A self-starter able to function independently with minimum direction.</li>
<br>
</ul>
