<h2>Assistant Property Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>We are currently looking for a part-time Property Manager to manage one of our properties in Los Angeles, CA. This position requires living on-site at the property.<br>This position is responsible for effectively managing the day-to-day operations of the said property and attending to the needs of our residents.<br><b>Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Marketing and leasing of vacant apartment units</li>
<li>Conducting property tours for prospective residents</li>
<li>Rent collection</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Posting on notices</li>
<li>Enforce community rules and policies</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Oversight of maintenance team and all work orders. Ensures that maintenance tasks are done in an efficient and timely manner</li>
<li>Resident relations</li>
<li>Maintain curb appeal of the property</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Other responsibilities as may be required by the position and/or assigned by the immediate supervisor.</li></ul>
<p><b>Requirements: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Two or three years of property management experience in operations/resident services</li>
<li>One year of working knowledge using AppFolio and Yardi software.</li>
<li>Sales and/or leasing experience</li>
<li>General office experience</li>
<li>Good computer skills - proficiency in GoogleSuite required</li>
<li>Ability to interact effectively with prospects and residents</li>
<li>Excellent customer service skills</li>
<li>Extremely organized, oriented and able to multi-task.</li>
<li>Ability to follow through and carry out the work schedule</li>
<li>Good problem-solving and customer service skills and the ability to stay organized</li>
<li>Good verbal and written skills</li>
<li>Hardworking and energetic</li>
<li>Must be able to climb stairs and lift up to 10-lb</li>
<li>This position requires the ability to sit and use office equipment and computer</li>
<li>Must reside at the designated property and be available to work approximately 24 to 28 hours per week</li>
<li>Reliable transportation with a valid CA driver's license</li>
</ul>
