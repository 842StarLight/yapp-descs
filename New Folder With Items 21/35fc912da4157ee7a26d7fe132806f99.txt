<h2>Account Supervisor - HCP, Professional Pharmaceutical Advertising</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<p>GREY is seeking an inspiring, insightful, industrious Account Supervisor to join an integrated team serving a national-level, high profile pharmaceutical client. GREY's responsibilities for this brand include: DTC, HCP Hispanic and Digital communications. Although the role is specific to HCP, success requires an understanding of the non-HCP work and collaboration with those responsible for that work in order to mirror the client's approach to brand-building.</p>
<p>
Responsibilities include being a:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>
BUSINESS EXPERT</b> The AS must become an expert in the client's business in order to develop the foundation that informs recommendations;</li>
<li>
<b>
STRATEGIC THINKER</b> The AS must be an active participant in research design, analysis, and strategic development, supporting and learning from agency strategic planners, client research managers and outside suppliers;</li>
<li>
<b>
CREATIVE DRIVER</b> The AS must develop and defend a point of view in order to encourage effective, hard-working creative for our the brand â€¦ and for the agency's own reputation;</li>
<li>
<b>
INTEGRATED EVANGELIST</b> The AS must be an effective collaborator not only with the HCP team but also with the broader agency- and client-based brand teams;</li>
<li>
<b>
CHANGE AGENT</b> The AS must embrace shifting circumstances and use those as opportunities to improve the agency's offerings and the clients' processes;</li>
<li>
<b>
MENTOR/MENTEE</b> The AS must have an eye on preparing junior staffers for their next roles while working with the AD to overtake that role.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Specific Assignments:</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
The AS is the bridge between designing the future of the brand and the daily blocking-and-tackling. Responsibilities include:</p>
<ul><li>
Leading the day-to-day management of specific projects as well as overall account management including:<ul>
<li>
Running internal and external meetings and ensuring agreements are met;</li>
<li>
Ensuring that projects meet client-, agency- and regulatory requirements;</li>
<li>
Managing budgets and timelines effectively.</li>
<li>
Owning TV production;</li>
<li>
Providing light, but meaningful, supervision of multiple AE-led print projects;</li>
<li>
Participating in strategic initiatives with the objective of developing expertise;</li>
<li>
Managing, guiding and training one AE and one AAE;</li>
<li>
Supporting and learning from VP-Account Director;</li>
<li>
Fostering productive relationships with other partners who serve the client but may not be part of GREY or WPP groups.</li>
</ul>
</li></ul>
