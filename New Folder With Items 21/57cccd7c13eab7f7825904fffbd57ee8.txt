<h2>Membership Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>POSITION SUMMARY:</b></p>
<p>This position supports the work of the Y, a leading nonprofit committed to strengthening community through youth development, healthy living and social responsibility. Delivers excellent service to all members, guests, and program participants. Responds to member and guest needs, promotes and sells memberships and programs, and maintains cleanliness and organization of the lobby area.</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provides excellent service to members, guests, and program participants in the Y and on the phone, contributing to member retention.</li>
<li>Welcomes and scans in each member in a pleasant and friendly manner (i.e., greet, smile &amp; make eye contact).</li>
<li>Conduct interviews and/or tours responsive to the needs of prospective members; sells memberships.</li>
<li>Builds relationships with members; helps members connect with one another and the YMCA.</li>
<li>Be able to actively listen to questions, concerns and feelings of members and staff. Be able to deal with members and staff in a honest, respectful and positive manner.</li>
<li>Handles and resolves membership concerns and informs supervisor of unusual situations or unresolved issues.</li>
<li>Applies all YMCA policies dealing with member services.</li>
<li>Performs other duties as assigned.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>LEADERSHIP COMPETENCIES:</b></p>
<p><i>Advancing Our Mission &amp; Cause</i><i>:</i> Engaging Community, Volunteerism, Philanthropy, Change Leadership</p>
<p><i>Building Relationships</i><i>:</i> Collaboration, Communication &amp; Influence, Inclusion</p>
<p><i>Leading Operations</i><i>:</i> Critical Thinking &amp; Decision Making, Fiscal Management, Functional Expertise, Innovation, Program/Project Management</p>
<p><i>Developing &amp; Inspiring People</i><i>:</i> Developing Self &amp; Others, Emotional Maturity</p>
