<h2>Marketing Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Company Overview</b></p>
<p>
Founded in France in 1970, Fiducial is a major provider of financial services (Business Counseling, Bookkeeping, Payroll and Tax Preparation &amp; Advisory Services) to small businesses and their owners. As a trusted advisor to entrepreneurs, Fiducial provides the financial back office infrastructure to clients, allowing them to focus on what they do best: serve their customers.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Position Overview</b></p>
<p>
The Marketing Associate will work in an exciting team environment managing all aspects of employer branding initiatives, including content marketing, digital strategy, lead generation, social engagement, and customer retention in support of Fiducial’s corporate goals. The successful candidate will also be responsible for creating dynamic written, graphic, web, and video content while utilizing search engine optimization (SEO), to drive the development and delivery of measurable, multi-channel marketing campaigns and to support content campaigns for brand awareness.</p>
<p>
Additionally, the Marketing Associate will be responsible for employing marketing analytics to gather important data (social media, web analytics, rankings, etc.), and maintaining and updating our company and career websites. In addition to engaging new clients, the Marketing Associate will also work on projects related to finding new businesses to acquire and other work as assigned.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Desired Skills/ Experience/Attributes</b></p>
<ul>
<li>2 + years of experience in marketing and creating brand awareness</li>
<li>2 + years of experience administering social media platforms</li>
<li>Experience with SEO, HTML, Microsoft Office Suite, Google Analytics, and other creative tools</li>
<li>Understanding of and ability to report on social media engagement and advertising metrics such as CTR, Conversion Rate, Bounce Rate, CPC, CPM, and CSat</li>
<li>Lead projects from beginning to end for marketing materials of various types from social media to email campaigns to landing pages to sales collateral</li>
<li>Deliver marketing campaigns that are aligned with the brand guidelines and are compliant with the firm and division's risk and legal guidelines</li>
<li>Excellent writing skills with the ability to persuade and engage an audience</li>
<br>
</ul>
