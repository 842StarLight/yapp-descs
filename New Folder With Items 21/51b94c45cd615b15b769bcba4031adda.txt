<h2>Administrative Support Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div><div><div>
<p><b>JOB SUMMARY:</b> Provides administrative support to managers of a busy truck leasing facility.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Rihm Family Companies, Inc. (RFC) is a 4th generation family-owned and operated business specializing in the heavy-duty truck industry. RFC employs 360 people in 22 locations, and includes Rihm Kenworth, a Kenworth truck dealer; RMC Truck Parts, a parts sales company; Rihm Leasing, a truck rental, financing and leasing company; and Rihm Global Sales, which handles international truck and parts sales. Founded in 1932 under the name Rihm Motor Company, Inc., Rihm Kenworth is the second-oldest continually operated Kenworth dealer in the world. Rihm is a growth-oriented company, focused on doing the right thing always, while providing exceptional service with fairness to all stakeholders.</p>
<br>
</div></div></div>

Qualifications</div>
<div><div>

<p><b>ESSENTIAL JOB FUNCTIONS/RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul><li>Enter parts invoices into Enrich software</li></ul>
<ul><li>Enter Road Rescue invoices into Enrich software</li></ul>
<ul><li>Enter National Account invoices</li></ul>
<ul><li>Enter weekly vehicle mileage for Preventive Maintenance reporting</li></ul>
<ul><li>Assist with parts inventory control and parts location assignment</li></ul>
<ul><li>Track warranty and parts returns</li></ul>
<ul><li>Roster special order parts for future repair tracking</li></ul>
<ul><li>Answer incoming phone calls</li></ul>
<ul><li>Scan and forward Pre-Delivery Inspection invoices</li></ul>
<ul><li>Collect and distribute month-end mileage</li></ul>
<ul><li>Other duties to meet business requirements</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL EDUCATION and EXPERIENCE QUALIFICATIONS:</b></p>
<ul><li>High School diploma or GED equivalent</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experience in truck shop environment</li></ul>
<ul><li>Knowledge of truck parts/truck industry</li></ul>
<ul><li>Enrich software experience</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>KEY ATTRIBUTES / SKILLS / CHARACTERISTICS:</b></p>
<ul><li>Proficient in Microsoft Office Suite (Word, Excel, Outlook)</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to multi task and prioritize in a fast-paced environment</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must possess organizational skills</li></ul>
