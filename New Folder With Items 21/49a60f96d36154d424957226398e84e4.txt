<h2>Sales & Marketing Assistant (Food Manufacturing)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Description: </b></p>
<p>An established foreign food manufacturer expands business in the United States and is currently looking for an enthusiastic, detail oriented and well organized Marketing Assistant to join the Sales &amp; Marketing Department. The incumbent will report to the VP of Sales.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Keep up to date on social media trends and tools, such as research how emerging social networks and features can benefit our company, connect and manage influencers</li>
<li>Create contents and manage the company’s Social Media platforms (Facebook, Instagram, and Youtube), at least 5 posts per week on each account</li>
<li>Ensure timely responses to customers and followers’ queries and comments on social media platforms</li>
<li>Handle administrative tasks associated with all digital initiatives and analyze data to provide key insights</li>
<li>Coordinate with VP of Sales and Marketing to design and execute advertising campaigns</li>
<li>Assist in putting together monthly marketing plan and keep track of each event’s status</li>
<li>Handle customer complaints and follow through with customers</li>
<li>Create promotional graphics, company flyers and all other necessary marketing materials</li>
<li>Coordinate with outside marketing vendors with materials creation and events</li>
<li>Translate information and documents into Chinese or Spanish as needed</li>
<li>Assist in conducting product research and market research for competitor analysis and new product launching evaluation analysis</li>
<li>Attend food shows and other local events. Travel as needed</li>
<li>Perform other tasks as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>2 year minimum experience managing social media platforms (Facebook, Instagram, Youtube, Twitter, Snapchat etc.)</li>
<li>Bachelor’s in marketing, communications or a related field preferred</li>
<li>Experience with social media management tools</li>
<li>Ability to identify target audience preferences and trends</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills</li>
<li>Time management and multitasking skills</li>
<li>Creative, outside the box thinker that can execute results-oriented marketing plans</li>
<li>Ability to work independently</li>
<li>Be comfortable creating blog posts</li>
<li>Be willing for website and marketing email campaigns</li>
<li>Bilingual in Mandarin and English preferred</li>
<li>Willing to travel offsite</li>
<li>Familiar with editing tools and programs</li>
</ul>
