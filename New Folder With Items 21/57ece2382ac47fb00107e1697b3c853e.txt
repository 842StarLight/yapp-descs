<h2>Associate Web Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>With millions of page views each year, managing the Amerisleep website is no small task. We're looking for an experienced Associate Web Manager who can handle a wide range of technical responsibilities from integrating new SaaS tools to optimizing page speed and project managing large-scale development initiatives.</p>
<p>
In addition to managing the technical aspects of the website, you'll identify products and technologies that can enhance the user experience and improve our e-commerce sales. You'll work with our in-house and outsourced development resources as well as collaborate with our design and marketing teams to plan out and execute company directives and goals.</p>
<p>
Please apply if you're obsessively detail-oriented and relentless about hitting your goals and deadlines. Our websites operate on Bigcommerce and WordPress and we hope to put them in your capable hands to see how you can make the Amerisleep experience even better for our customers.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Manage our e-commerce site on Bigcommerce</li>
<li>
Build pages in multiple CMSs and use modern HTML, CSS, PHP, and JavaScript</li>
<li>
Update and maintain WordPress sites</li>
<li>
Work with version control and avoid merge conflicts</li>
<li>
Utilize a build system for generating optimized code</li>
<li>
Troubleshoot reported errors and implement permanent solutions</li>
<li>
Identify and evaluate opportunities to improve the digital experience for our users</li>
<li>
Reasoning about APIs and how to best connect them to other business systems</li>
<li>
Develop custom elements for new website use cases</li>
</ul>
