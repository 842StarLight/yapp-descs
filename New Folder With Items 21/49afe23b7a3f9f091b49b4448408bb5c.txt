<p><b>DTN is recognized as a leader in providing exceptional living experiences at the properties we develop, own and manage. For 45 years DTN, through its talented team members, has positively impacted those we serve. We welcome you to join us on our journey in being the housing provider of choice in Michigan.

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Summary</b></h2>
<p>The DTN Marketing Coordinator is the brand liaison to all of DTN Management employees, residents, future residents, and the local communities we reside in. As a part of the marketing team, you will be engaged to help grow the DTN brand and individual property brands.
</p>
<p><b>As the Marketing Coordinator, your primary responsibilities include:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Collaborating with the marketing team to create innovative and engaging marketing campaigns</li>
<li>Coordinate the execution of marketing campaigns, events, and outreach promotions</li>
<li>Assist in the creation and managing of new marketing and advertising campaigns</li>
<li>Creating and delivering timely materials</li>
<li>Meet with current and prospective vendors to ensure the excellence of the DTN brand</li>
<li>Use approved technology and operating software for organization of and access needs for department
</li>
<li>Storyboard, create, and final video production with agencies</li>
<li>Logo design as needed
</li>
<li>Schedule and perform photo shoots</li>
<li>Order and inventory of promotional materials</li>
<li>Assist department with various projects as needed</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Core Candidate Qualities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High school education or equivalent</li>
<li>Preferred 6+ months of marketing/graphic design experience
</li>
<li>Well organized with ability to prioritize and multi-task</li>
<li>Experience with or the ability to learn all technology and operating software used at DTN communities including Yardi, Microsoft Office Excel, Power Point, Photoshop.
</li>
<li>Experience with or ability to learn social media and reputation management websites including Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, YouTube and Snapchat.</li>
<li>Motivation to learn and consistently improve</li>
<li>People skills required to educate, support and overcome objections
</li>
<li>Reliable transportation to get to work daily and to conduct daily job responsibilities</li>
</ul>
