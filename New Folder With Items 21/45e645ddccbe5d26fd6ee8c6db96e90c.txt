<br>Implementation Consultant Digital Banking
<br>*****************************************
<br><br>
*******
<br>Summary
<br>*******
<br><br>
The primary responsibility of the Implementation Consultant is extensive customer interface at all stages of a project facilitating a positive customer experience and the successful implementation of our Digital Banking solutions. The Implementation Consultant works with the sales staff, the Project and/or services management and other delivery consultants, partners, and/or sub-contractors and is responsible for contributing to all facets of multiple Bottomline implementation projects for the Digital Banking line of business. This may include but is not limited to: installation and configuration of Bottomline software products, client communication, maintaining the timeline for project completion, solution design, business process consulting, custom program implementation, troubleshooting, issue tracking and resolution, training, and other tasks necessary to execute the contracted services.
<br><br>
The Implementation Consultant may/is called upon to assist in pre-sale review and documentation of a client's current systems and business applications, establishing technical business requirements and identifying the new or enhanced business processes and Bottomline software and services that meet these requirements.
<br><br>
The Implementation Consultant will have experience and an advanced level of knowledge and performance of the tasks described herein. Additional responsibilities of the Implementation Consultant include team leadership and mentoring, documenting best practices, and leading business process improvement initiatives.
<br><br>

About Bottomline Technologies
<br><br>
Bottomline Technologies provides collaborative payment, invoice and document automation solutions to corporations, financial institutions and banks around the world. The company's solutions are used to streamline, automate and manage processes involving payments, invoicing, global cash management, supply chain finance and transactional documents. Organizations trust these solutions to meet their needs for cost reduction, competitive differentiation and optimization of working capital.
<br><br>
Serving industries such as financial services, insurance, health care, technology, communications, education, media, manufacturing and government, Bottomline provides products and services to approximately 80 of the Fortune 100 companies and 70 of the FTSE (Financial Times) 100 companies.
