<h2>Hardware Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Job Description<br>
<br>
<p><b> </b>Providing local development support for existing and newly nominated business</p>
<p>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES include the following. Other duties may be assigned.</p>
<ul>
<li>
Provide support in development of electrical HW, application and testing for customer projects</li>
<li>
Understand, interpret and negotiate HW related customer requirements with the OEM.</li>
<li>
These requirements concern electrical circuits, EMC and/or electrical motor and other associated components.</li>
<li>
Plan, specify and implement HW related changes to a platform ECU in order to fulfill specific requirements of the customer.</li>
<li>
Mediate between central hardware development in Germany and customer.</li>
<li>
Communicate customer project HW information and requirements internally with various groups such as purchasing, sales, engineering, etc.</li>
<li>
Responsible for maintaining an Open Issues List for the assigned project/s and attend regular meetings with the project core team to review the project Open Issues List.</li>
<li>
Lead problem solving resolution, root-cause analysis, and drive continuous improvement efforts for engineering as well as support continuous improvement for safety and quality during the program</li>
<li>
Provide support for the manufacturing facility</li>
<li>
Assure compliance with automotive quality standards such as TS16949 and other internal/external policies and procedures</li>
<li>
Manage/support testing and validation according to customer milestones and document required results in accordance with internal and customer requirements.</li>
<li><b>
Other:</b></li>
<li>
Occasional travel to Germany will be necessary depending on project demand.</li>
</ul>
</div>
