<h2>General Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Pure Barre is the national leader in barre fitness with more than 500+ franchise locations in the United States and Canada. We are currently expanding our team in Pure Barre Swampscott, MA and seeking a qualified General Manager that is focused on driving sales and service excellence for our luxury fitness brand. This is the perfect opportunity to interact with clients on a daily basis to create a supportive and meaningful community!<br>
</p>
<p><b>
POSITION:</b> The General Manager will oversee all studio functionality from sales to studio operations.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>REQUIREMENTS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
2+ years of fitness sales or relevant sales experience preferred
</li>
<li>Confident in generating personal sales and training Sales Reps
</li>
<li>Ability to manage and drive multiple revenue streams including memberships and retail
</li>
<li>Previous management or supervisory experience required
</li>
<li>Must be fluent in English and have excellent communication and strong interpersonal skills in person and over the phone
</li>
<li>Ability to excel in a fast changing, diverse environment
</li>
<li>Must be solution-based and results oriented, competitive spirit
</li>
<li>Ability to recognize areas of improvement and make changes using good judgement</li>
<li>An affinity and passion for fitness</li>
<li>Solid writing and grammar skills</li>
<li>Highly organized, proficient in data management, ability to prioritize and meet deadlines</li>
<li>Professional, punctual, reliable and neat and organized
</li>
<li>Strong attention to detail and accuracy</li>
<li>Trustworthy and ability to handle confidential information
</li>
<li>Ability to work harmoniously with co-workers, clients and the general public</li>
<li>Proficiency with computers and Studio software
</li>
<li>College Degree Preferred</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>This is a full-time position. Candidate must be able to work a flexible schedule with nights and weekend availability. Travel to California for initial training required.<br>
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>DUTIES</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Lead generation including Grass Roots Marketing and networking
</li>
<li>Implement sales process to schedule prospects into introductory classes</li>
<li>Membership and retail sales
</li>
<li>Manage staff schedule
</li>
<li>Works with the Lead Instructor on class schedule and instructor evaluation</li>
<li>Directly coordinate with all vendors, ensuring that studio retail/products are stocked with accurate inventory counts
</li>
<li>Supervise Sales Representatives
</li>
<li>Proficiency in gym management software and POS to include revenue reports, attendance reports, etc.
</li>
<li>Independently make decisions related to high level customer service
</li>
<li>Collect outstanding dues
</li>
<li>Maintain cleanliness and organization of the studio
</li>
<li>Enforce studio policies and procedures
</li>
<li>Ensure all forms, administrative supplies, and studio literature is stocked and visible
</li>
<li>Schedule and participate in networking/community events and studio promotions
</li>
<li>Strategically manage marketing campaigns to generate leads for the studio
</li>
<li>Any other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p><b>COMPENSATION &amp; PERKS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
This position offers a very competitive base rate</li>
<li>Commission paid on sales</li>
</ul>
