<h2>Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>SUMMARY</b></p>
<p>Maccura Biotechnology (USA) LLC, a newly established subsidiary of Maccura Biotechnology Co. Ltd., is seeking an experienced Marketing Manager to be responsible for market research, marketing strategic planning, brand promotion and product promotion plan implementation. The Marketing Manager reports directly to the president. The company is currently located in Rockville, Maryland.</p>
<p><b>RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<p>Primary responsibilities include, but may not be limited to:</p>
<ul>
<li>Understand company vision, work with company executives to determine marketing strategy. Research and analyze markets, brand performance, potential business opportunities and provide recommendation for new product development, product enhancements, or business expansion.</li>
<li>Develop and implement marketing plans and objectives. Control and coordinate the process of strategic implementation of marketing plan.</li>
<li>Be fully responsible for marketing, brand promotion and business operation, assist sales to achieve the company's marketing objectives through various marketing means.</li>
<li>Organize to collect and report market sales information, new technology product development information, user feedback information, market development trend information, etc.</li>
<li>Understand related product registration regulations, formulate product registration plan and registration implementation management.</li>
<li>Be responsible for planning product marketing strategy marketing activities, establish industry product recognition;</li>
<li>Be responsible for the training and assessment of subordinates of the department;</li>
<li>Finish other tasks assigned by superior.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS AND EXPERIENCE</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor degree or above in biotechnology, marketing, medicine or related fields.</li>
<li>Minimum 3 years working experience in medical device industry;</li>
<li>Proficient in office software;</li>
<li>Excellent in both English and Chinese listening, speaking, reading and writing skills;</li>
<li>Strong communication, coordination, emergency handling and interpersonal skills;</li>
<li>Good professional ethics, high professional dedication and sense of responsibility;</li>
<li>Ability to work under pressure; Strong team player</li>
<li>US Citizen or Green Card required</li>
</ul>
