<h2>Mechanical Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">

<p><b>Mechanical Engineer Stevensville, MD</b></p>
<p>
Miltec UV is seeking a full-time, hands-on Mechanical Engineer to join our team in Stevensville, MD.</p>
<p><b>
Job Summary:</b></p>
<p>
Design, develop, and qualify electromechanical components and sub-assemblies used in UV Curing systems. Works with reasonable independence at the sub-assembly or system level.</p>
<p>
DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</p>
<ul>
<li>Design, develop, and qualify UV Curing equipment (components, products, systems) under supervision.</li>
<li>Apply engineering fundamentals to solve complex mechanical design problems.</li>
<li>Develop product requirements documents (PRD) based on input from Customers, Sales, Manufacturing, Field Service, and Process Engineering.</li>
<li>Assist Custom Projects Engineer in documenting and releasing designs as directed.</li>
<li>Prepare and execute test plans to validate product performance</li>
<li>Coordinate and direct the creation of all documentation required to support the project.</li>
<li>Work with electrical and software engineers as required to develop machine control systems.</li>
<li>Use industry-standard guidelines to develop sheet metal, machined, extruded, cast, welded, molded, chemically machined, and thermoformed components which result in cost-competitive products that meet our customer's requirements.</li>
<li>Work closely with Purchasing, Manufacturing, and Field Service to facilitate the production, testing, and commissioning of UV Curing products</li>
<li>Travel as required (approximately 10% domestic and 5% international)</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p><b>MINIMUM REQUIREMENTS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>BSME degree and 2-4 years of pertinent experience</li>
<li>Demonstrated design creativity and the ability to operate independently</li>
<li>Proficient in 3D CADD software (Solidworks preferred) to create models and documentation</li>
<li>Proficient in MS Office, especially Excel, Word, PowerPoint, and Project</li>
<li>Able to lift 30 lbs., install subsystems into process equipment, and make adjustments within equipment</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills (written and verbal)</li>
</ul>
<p><b>ADDITIONAL ATTRIBUTES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Must be a self-starting, "hands-on" engineer with the ability to function with minimal supervision</li>
<li>Must be able to quickly adapt project activities in response to fast-changing priorities</li>
</ul>
<p><b>COMPENSATION:</b></p>
<p>
Competitive salary is based on experience.<br>
Benefits package includes medical, dental, vision, 401K (with match), paid vacation, holidays, sick and personal leave.<br>
