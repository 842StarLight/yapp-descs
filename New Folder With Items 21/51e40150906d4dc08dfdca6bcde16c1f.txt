<h2>Sr. Technical Recruiter/Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Overview</div>
<div>PROLIM Global Corporation (www.prolim.com) is currently seeking <b>Sr. Technical Recruiter/Account Manager </b>for location <b>Detroit</b><b>, MI </b>for one of our top client.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Job Description:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Accountable for developing a comprehensive sourcing strategy to source for top talents by methods of cold calling, direct sourcing and creating name/talent lead generation</li>
<li>Utilize a variety of proven talent platforms and resources such as Social Media, Major Internet Job Boards, and Industry-specific blogs/discussion boards, onsite/offsite social engagement events, collaborating with trusted professional networks and establishing active presence with local Colleges/Universities for alumni</li>
<li>Execute talent-sourcing strategies to fulfill key metrics such as direct candidate contact time, time to fill per requisition and candidate to new hire conversion</li>
<li>Continuously engage in proactive dialogues with both passive/active candidates throughout the talent sourcing process</li>
<li>Intelligently prescreen all identified candidates and partner with hiring managers keeping all interested entities engaged, involved and providing professional consultative next steps throughout the candidate experience process</li>
<li>Support the recruitment lifecycle by sourcing qualified candidates, reviewing resumes, phone screening and generating a flow of appropriate resumes / candidates who meet the job requirements and preferences</li>
<li>Experience handling multiple clients and Vendor Management Systems (VMS) systems
</li>
<li>Accountable for generating leads by leveraging relationships, referrals, social media networks, career fairs, cold calling, job boards, outreach events, and other emerging sourcing best practices</li>
<li>Foster a strong customer service orientation with clients by working collaboratively with teams, and consulting effectively at all levels within the business</li>
<li>Track and report on staffing activities to ensure Talent Acquisition achieves key hiring milestones Accountable for following Talent Acquisition processes in accordance with policies, practices, EEO, other legal requirements</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Skills Required:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Prior Experience handling multiple accounts and regular interaction with clients</li>
<li>3-5 years of Technical recruitment experience with specific knowledge of effective sourcing strategies and techniques</li>
<li>Ability to work in a fast-pace, high energy, high volume workspace without sacrificing quality over quantity.
</li>
<li>Ability to proactively and successfully introduce new top talents to fill key job requisitions within defined benchmarks</li>
<li>Socially talent-sourcing perceptive utilizing a variety of current and next generation talent resource platforms such as applicant tracking systems, (ATS), Talent Network Communities such as LinkedIn, Facebook and Twitter</li>
<li>Strong verbal and written communication skills with the ability to relate and influence individuals at all levels</li>
</ul>
<div>
<div><b>Education Required</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree in Business Administration, Human Resources, or a related field is preferred.</li>
</ul>
