<p>POSITION DESCRIPTION</p>
<p>
POSITION: Project Manager
</p>
<p>REPORTS TO: Director, Professional Services</p>
<p>
ABOUT OHIO, USA-BASED IQS SOFTWARE, PART OF THE CORITY GROUP:</p>
<p>
Originally founded in 1988 and acquired by Cority last year, Ohio-USA based IQS is our Enterprise Quality Management System currently being used by leading companies to successfully manage and improve their business and performance, assure higher levels of quality compliance and manage risk. The IQS software model provides an enterprise-wide framework to manage compliance with multiple government regulations, quality assurance standards and compliance mandates.
</p>
<p>LOCATION: North Olmsted, Ohio.
</p>
<p>SUMMARY</p>
<p>
The Project Manager is responsible for providing dedicated project management for global and complex implementations of IQS’ Enterprise Quality Management Software (eQMS) The successful candidate will be a self‐starter with a background in Project Management, preferably with software experience in a client facing role, who can leverage these skills to successfully manage the end to end implementation of our software solution.</p>
<p>
PRIMARY RESPONSIBILITIES:</p>
<ul><li>Manage multiple large and complex projects with multiple diverse stakeholders.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manage all phases of a project in accordance with the IQS’ Implementation Methodology</li></ul>
<ul><li>Take the responsibility to complete all deliverables required as defined by the IQS’ Implementation Methodology and customer project plan</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manage and promote effective key strategic internal and external relationships with project stakeholders, management and delivery teams</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manage client expectations especially in the initiation phase
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Independently work with IQS sales team to identify customer project requirements to support accurate project scoping</li></ul>
<ul><li>Facilitate creation of Statement of Work (SOW) and associated effort and cost</li></ul>
<ul><li>Independently coordinates multi-disciplined team of Implementation Consultants, Developers and Client Resources to successfully implement eQMS software for clients</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manage change process and effectively communicate the impact to the client</li></ul>
<ul><li>Develop detailed project schedules in collaboration with team members. Self-sufficient in the creation and maintenance of project schedules which reflect realistic and unique project requirements</li></ul>
<ul><li>Monitor and control project deliverables ensuring quality</li></ul>
<ul><li>Control project time and budget and escalate if budget or schedule is at risk</li></ul>
<ul><li>Identify and mitigate project risks and issues</li></ul>
<ul><li>Hold status meetings and effectively and efficiently communicate project status to internal and external stakeholders</li></ul>
<ul><li>Perform project closing activities including post review sessions</li></ul>
