<div>
Reporting to the EVP, Business Leader, Core DMS, the Sr. Director, Network and Telephony Products will be tasked for setting the strategy to drive this Segment (which includes Network LAN hardware, WAN, Telephony (active handsets with voice traffic), Wireless (AP’s), Network Security (GRE Tunnels, etc.). This position sits on the Core DMS Leadership team. His/her objective is to create a blueprint for these businesses to drive customer loyalty, revenue, and growth. This role is accountable for strategic planning and execution and P&amp;L responsibility.</div>
