<h2>Spa Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Bella Santé, meaning ‘beautiful health’, is one of the most recognizable spa brands in New England. We are seeking a* <i>Spa Director</i>* for our <b>Boston </b>location.</p>
<p>In business for over 20 years, our ‘Best of Boston’ awards span over a decade. We are proud of our numerous awards and accolades as they are a testament to our talented team and our collective commitment to delivering world class service to our guests. We are looking to bring on a strong, gritty, and enthusiastic leader to join our familial culture.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities of the Spa Director include: </b></p>
<p>Ensures that service standards are kept at a high level for guests by maintaining the standards set in Bella Santé Spas SOP's and service standards and Core Values.</p>
<p>· Assesses all employees' progress continually, trains/mentors’ employees with positive reinforcement and disciplines fairly and consistently.</p>
<p>· Provides excellent guest service and monitors guest feedback. Supervises and follows up on guest requests and comments.</p>
<p>· Ensures fiscal responsibility through efficient scheduling of staff.</p>
<p>· Ensures that financial goals are attained by developing and implementing retail sales plan.</p>
<p>· Develops and maintains accurate maintenance procedures and checklists through routine preventative maintenance and repair.</p>
<p>· Creates a team of service providers to meet all aspects of professionalism and service demands.</p>
<p>· Ensures appropriate Spa supervisory coverage through use of a Manager on Duty (MOD) system.</p>
<p>· Specifies and controls efficient usage of professional products for all services by completing a service cost analysis using an inventory control system.</p>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<p><b>EDUCATION/EXPERIENCE</b></p>
<p>· Bachelor's degree or comparable spa management experience</p>
<p>· Knowledge of professional spa services and treatments.</p>
<p>· Knowledge of retail operations and inventory systems.</p>
<p>· Effective leadership skills and a strong work ethic.</p>
<p>· Excellent customer service skills.</p>
<p>· Efficient, well organized, and able to handle a variety of duties simultaneously.</p>
<p>· Sales oriented.</p>
<p>· Energetic, enthusiastic and motivational.</p>
<p>· Professional manner, discretion, and appearance.</p>
<p>· Excellent verbal and written skills.</p>
<p>· Normal work hours: Varied to include nights, weekends, and holidays.</p>
<p>· Proficient in spa software (Harms Millennium) and Microsoft Office products a plus.</p>
