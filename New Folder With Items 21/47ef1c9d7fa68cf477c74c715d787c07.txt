<h2>Technical Product Manager (SSO)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">The Role
<br><br>
As Product Manager, you will be responsible for defining and managing the requirements and user stories in the functional area of SSO to deliver the associated features.
<br><br>
We are looking for a strong product manager with technical background in security, good business sense, and an ability to partner closely with other Product Managers, Engineering, User Experience, Sales Engineering, Customer Success, Product Marketing, and Technical Marketing teams to drive this team and align with overall product portfolio. We are looking for a problem solver with creativity and passion and an ability to execute to provide the most value to our customers.
<br><br>
What you will do
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Lead product prioritization and definition for our Workplace platform features</li>
<li>Be the owner for a designated area of the product (Single Sign-On) across heterogeneous environments (Windows, MacOS, Android, iOS)</li>
<li>Constantly engage with our customers and engineering team and be their voice internally</li>
<li>Define specification for feature enhancements and releases</li>
<li>Develop and maintain a product roadmap and ensure delivery of it</li>
<li>Drive delivery of our products and specific features</li>
<li>Present to leadership on the latest feature sets and requirements</li>
</ul>
What you will bring
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>5+ years of relevant work experience in technical product management</li>
<li>2+ years of managing Customer Identify and Access Management implementations</li>
<li>Experience working with solutions that cover Single Sign-On (SSO) and Multi-factor Authentication</li>
<li>Familiarity with authentication protocols such as OAuth, OpenID, SAML, RADIUS, and Kerberos</li>
<li>Experience coordinating complex product development cycles and SDLC schedules</li>
<li>Background in SaaS products</li>
<li>Proven track-record of taking ownership and driving results to ensure product success</li>
<li>Experience writing user stories, preferably in Confluence or similar</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal skills</li>
<li>Strong attention to detail and organizational skills</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree or higher</li>
</ul>
