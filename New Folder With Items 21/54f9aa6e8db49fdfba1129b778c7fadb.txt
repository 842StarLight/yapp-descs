<h2>Advanced Compliance GBB</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>The Cybersecurity Solutions Group (CSG) empowers enterprises to confidently move to the cloud and modernize their platforms by delivering the security solutions, expertise and services needed to keep their data safe. CSG is looking for a passionate, experienced and credible Compliance and Information Protection professional to join our growing team of Global Black Belts (GBBs). Your main goal in this role will be to win the technical decision of customers to purchase and use Microsoft Information Protection, Advanced Data Governance, Advanced eDiscovery, and supporting solutions. In this role, you will be expected to both own and win the customer’s technical decision, as well as find new opportunities to pursue in partnership with the broader CSG sales team.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>The role involves working as part of a global team, and although regionally based, the person hired will also support colleagues in other geographies. Successful candidates will be self-motivated, customer obsessed and will work closely with the CSG Enterprise Security Executives, the CSG Detection &amp; Response Team, local sales &amp; services account teams, product engineering, and applicable partners to pursue corporate priorities and business opportunities.
</div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities</b></h2>
<div>
You will:</div>
<ul>
<li>
Lead technical presentations, demonstrations, workshops, architecture design sessions, proof of concepts, and pilots to explain, demonstrate, and prove to our strategic customers the capabilities of Microsoft’s Advanced Compliance solutions, including Microsoft Information Protection, Advanced eDiscovery, Advanced Data Governance, and Compliance Manager.</li>
<li>
Own winning the technical decision at customers for sales opportunities and usage scenarios, through tailoring your message, bringing ideas to the customers, engaging with them to show our technology differentiation, and guiding them in decision making.</li>
<li>
Help our customers meet their compliance and legal obligations by clearly communicating the shared responsibility model, explaining the compliance of our platform with key regulations and frameworks, and advising customers on meeting their compliance and legal responsibilities regarding our platform.</li>
<li>
Partner with product management and engineering to uncover and prioritize improvements and enhancements to Microsoft’s Advanced Compliance Solutions.</li>
<li>
Support partner and field sales training and enablement.</li>
<li>
Be a thought leader in the governance, risk, compliance, and privacy space by participating in internal Microsoft technical communities and in broader industry events and by publishing blogs, whitepapers, and reference architectures.</li>
<li>
Help identify and remove technical, architecture, and competitive blockers for your respective solutions.</li>
<li>
Influence the Advanced Compliance Solutions go-to-market strategies by providing feedback to sales, marketing, and engineering on current and future product requirements and sales blockers you encounter.</li>
</ul>
