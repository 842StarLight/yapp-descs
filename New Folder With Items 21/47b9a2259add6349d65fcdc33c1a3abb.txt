<div><b>
Responsibilities:</b></div>
<p><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Serves as the main point of contact for all service customers to ensure customer expectations are met.</li>
<li>Proactively follows up with customers after completion of service visits.</li>
<li>Manages decisions regarding the scheduling and movement of staff and materials.</li>
<li>Schedules and dispatches field labor force to meet customer needs and expectations based on the nature of the call (urgency, contractual obligations, available resources, and customer needs).</li>
<li>Supports material procurement for customer part sales (pricing part sales, processing orders, and invoicing) as well as internal requests in support of Service projects.</li>
<li>Performs invoicing for Service Time &amp; Materials calls.</li>
<li>Effectively utilizes SAP in the delivery and management of service activities: opening service calls, supporting and scheduling planned preventive maintenance visits, updating customer information, creating purchase orders.</li>
<li>Delivers outstanding customer service.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
<br>
