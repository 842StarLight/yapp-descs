<h2>Technical Support (CTS)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div><div><div>
<div>Tessco, a value-added technology distributor, manufacturer, and solutions provider, serves commercial and retail customers in the wireless infrastructure and mobile device accessories markets. The company was founded more than 30 years ago with a commitment to deliver industry-leading products, knowledge, solutions, and customer service. Tessco supplies more than 50,000 products from 350 of the industry’s top manufacturers in mobile communications, Wi-Fi, Internet of Things (“IoT”), wireless backhaul, and more. Tessco is a single source for outstanding customer experience, expert knowledge, and complete end-to-end solutions for the wireless industry.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
As a Customer Technical Support Representative in our Infrastructure Product Business Unit, you will use your technical expertise to provide support by phone and electronically for the Infrastructure product lines.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Support sales teams to select the correct radio towers, base station antennas, mobile antennas, cabling and steel infrastructure products from our vast and deep Infrastructure product offering</li>
<li>
Assemble complete configured solution from applicable product lines</li>
<li>
Assist sales team to select the total solution and uncover areas of new opportunity with customers</li>
<li>
Maintain expert status by learning new products and applications as required</li>
<li>
Periodic travel for customer visits and training</li>
</ul>
