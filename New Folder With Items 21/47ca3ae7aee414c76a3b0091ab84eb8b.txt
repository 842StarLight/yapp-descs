<h2>Reservation Administrator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>POSITION SUMMARY:</b></p>
<p>
The Reservations Administrator will work collaboratively with all employees, guest, owners, and other FLPP stakeholders to support the company’s goals through superior guest and owner service. Supports an efficient reservations process that allows guest to easily find and book their accommodations in the Finger Lakes. Delivers personalized service that quickly and accurately attends to every guest’s needs; meets or exceeds all established customer care standards. During the off season tasks may include marketing research projects, booking maintenance, various periodic administrative tasks and/or housekeeping services as needed.</p>
<p><b>
SUPERVISES:</b></p>
<p>
This role has no supervisory responsibilities.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:</b></p>
<p>
These duties can be performed with or without an accommodation:</p>
<p>
Operational Support</p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for the efficient day-to-day post booking and pre-arrival activities of the Reservations department, and ensures consistently high quality guest satisfaction.</li>
<li>Communicates effectively with guests via phone, email, and fax prior to arrival to ensure a smooth, seamless guest arrival process.</li>
<li>Ensures each guest is provided with personalized, high quality concierge services as requested; promotes local events, attractions, restaurants, etc.</li>
<li>Assists upcoming guests with arrangements as needed to ensure a highly positive and rich guest experience.</li>
<li>Resolves all guest inquiries and complaints in a professional and timely manner; adheres to all established policies and standards; coordinates with Reservations Manager as necessary.</li>
<li>Ensures welcome packages, including concierge needs if applicable, are prepared and ready for all guest check-ins; ensures guests are aware of and understand all major provisions.</li>
<li>Answers multi-line telephone system; closely monitors voice mail and ensures immediate responses to all messages.</li>
<li>Responds quickly to all emails.</li>
<li>Brings issues of concern to RSM for assistance with resolutions.</li>
<li>Assists RSM with guest relocation as necessary.</li>
<li>Follows all relevant policies and standards.</li>
<li>Maintains Streamline per standards.</li>
<li>Ensures correct lockbox codes are available for upcoming guest arrivals.</li>
<li>Ensure keyless access codes have been generated by Streamline as needed for upcoming guest arrivals.</li>
<li>Ensures workspace is clean and organized.</li>
<li>Constantly monitors reservations (post-booking) email account.</li>
<li>Receives all incoming signed guest rental agreements received by email, fax, or postal service; includes review for accuracy and completeness and electronic filing of documents and requirements.</li>
<li>Ensures all guest pre-arrival rental requirements are received including completed rental agreements, identification, etc.</li>
<li>Ensures that all post-booking/pre-arrival guest correspondence is generated consistently, accurately, and in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Ensures guest payments are collected and processed accurately and when due.</li>
<li>Manages daily processing of all new online reservations including phone ‘thank you’ contact with guest to ensure procedures and requirements are understood; processes payments and generates confirmation correspondence to guest and owner.</li>
<li>Performs clerical tasks such as calculating rent percentages, typing, data entry, and file management.</li>
<li>Supports the promotion of Best Western PLUS’s and FLPP’s service accommodations and packages to area businesses.</li>
<li>Other duties and tasks as required.</li>
</ul>
<p>Safety Support:</p>
<ul>
<li>Aligns with the FLPP Safety Program to maintain a safe and clean work environment.</li>
<li>Keeps all required safety records and documentation current and up-to-date.</li>
<li>Maintain a safety first work ethic.</li>
<br>
</ul>

<li>Associates Degree or equivalent experience in reservation sales or hospitality industry, preferred.</li>
<li>Strong sales background in non-hospitality industry will be considered.</li>
</ul>

<ul>

<li>Customer Focus</li>
<li>Interpersonal Communication</li>
<li>Personal Energy &amp; Enthusiasm</li>
<li>Results Focus</li>

<li>Organizing Skills</li>
<li>Team Orientation</li>
