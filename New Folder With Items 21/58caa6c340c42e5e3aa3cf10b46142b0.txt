<h2>Invoice Processing Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div><div><div>
<p><b>Summary: </b>The Invoice Processing Specialist analyzes vendor and supplier invoices for accuracy regarding quantity, unit prices and other shipping or handling costs against purchase orders or other confirmed order documentation. Enters new invoice data into American software systems to maintain accurate records of merchandise and supplies stored at numerous locations. Provides follow up and contacts vendors regarding errors and inconsistencies or refers such issues to supervisors for resolution. Processes vendor and supplier invoices for payment..</p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities </b>include the following. Other duties may be assigned.</p>
<p>Analyze purchase order documents, including verification of those entered remotely, and enter new purchase orders.</p>
<p>Calculate merchandise costs, based on vendor invoices to voucher for payment, match freight to inventory receipts and differentiate between merchandise and miscellaneous costing.</p>
<p>Enter receipts for American owned inventory and verify and receive customer owned inventory.</p>
<p>Troubleshoot in areas such as costing, inventory quantity, order entry errors on purchase and sales orders, receiving errors, errors generated by the system and provide appropriate solutions.</p>
<p>Process quantity and value adjustments upon completion of inventory reconciliation and/or when additional costs have arrived after purchase order has been vouchered.</p>
<p>Monitor prepay and uncosted accounts for inventory item that donâ€™t have an invoice attached for less complex sales hubs and individual sales associates</p>
<p>Other duties as assigned.</p>
