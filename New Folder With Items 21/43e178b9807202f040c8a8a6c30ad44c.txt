<div>
<b>Electrical Engineer</b><br>
Willoughby, OH, US<br>
<br>
<b>The Role:</b> Responsible for production engineering, sustaining engineering, enhancements to existing products and new product development. Provides customer service in the form of technical support to both sales and end users. Works to maximize production through improved production processes. Designs products for compliance to UL and CSA standards. Works with UL and CSA to achieve &amp; maintain product certification.<br>
<br>
<b>The Company:</b> Ericson Manufacturing engineers and manufactures temporary power, lighting and safety electrical products for your workplace. Family owned and operated since 1918, our mission is to safeguard your jobsite with quality and innovative solutions with a commitment to safety, ease of use and reliability.<br>
<br>
<b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Evaluates requests by customer or sales/quotes team(s) for modification to existing products or requests for new product development.</li>
<li>Researches concepts for new product development that will meet customer needs and provide competitive advantage in the market.</li>
<li>When product is to be modified or a new product to be developed, builds a detailed drawing of the product solution including all specification information, sizing and other relevant information using CAD computer systems.</li>
<li>May conduct or supervise tests on new or modified products to ensure performance and production feasibility.</li>
<li>Stays current on all regulatory compliance or certification requirements as relating to Ericson products.</li>
<li>Evaluates and approves the use of materials or sub-assemblies that are received from outside vendors to be used in the production of Ericson product.</li>
<li>Defines testing procedures and determines appropriate lab fixtures or equipment to be used in product testing. Writes clear and concise reports to document the finding of tests on product.</li>
<li>Provides input and answers questions from sales/customers on technical specification, components, function, benefits, features and use of products.</li>
<li>Evaluates customer application needs for Ericson products and determines ways to design new products, possible modification to existing products, or recommends catalogue items to meet application needs.</li>
<li>Evaluates processes, equipment and skills required to produce product efficiently and at the lowest cost.</li>
<li>Designs custom test fixtures, tools, and software to be used in the production of product.</li>
<li>Completes all ISO documentation as required for production engineering and new product development</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Minimum Qualifications, Education and Certifications:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelors degree in electrical engineering, engineering technology or related field required</li>
<li>Minimum of 5 years total work experience in a manufacturing environment</li>
<li>Must have had solid experience in the design of new products, implementing appropriate manufacturing processes, and providing technical support to the sale of products</li>
<li>Must have demonstrated ability to utilize AutoCAD and/or ProE and/or other product engineering/manufacturing software packages.</li>
<li>CREO 4.0 a plus!</li>
</ul>
