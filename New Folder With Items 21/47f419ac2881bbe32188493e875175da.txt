<p>PEVONIA INTERNATIONAL, LLC</p>
<p>POSITION DESCRIPTION</p>
<p><b>Pevonia International, LLC is a smoke free, drug free workplace</b></p>
<p><b>JOB TITLE:</b> Digital Marketing Strategist</p>
<p><b>DIVISION/DEPARTMENT:</b> Marketing</p>
<p><b>REPORTING RELATIONSHIP:</b> The Digital Marketing Strategist reports to the Global VP of Marketing &amp; Communications with daily oversight &amp; collaboration with the Internet Marketing Manager</p>
<p><b>LOCATION OF POSITION:</b> Daytona Beach, FL</p>
<p><b>SALARY RANGE</b><b>:</b><b> </b>Based on Experience</p>
<p><b>FLSA Classification</b><b>:</b><b> </b>Part Time</p>
<p><b>Summary of duties and responsibilities:</b></p>
<p>Daily collaboration with the VP of Global Marketing and the Internet Marketing Manager in order for you to devise, develop and implement an appropriate digital and e-commerce marketing strategy with a focus on customer acquisition and retention with a significant emphasis on performance, measurement and continuous improvement. Ensuring e-commerce sites are strong revenue generating channels in line with overarching brand Marketing Plans.
</p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities (not all-inclusive):
</b></p>
<p>The primary duties of this position involve work along specialized or technical lines requiring special training, experience, or knowledge, and the duties directly relate to the general business operations of the company. These duties include but are not limited to:</p>
<ul>
<li>Experienced producer with established track record in generating sales thru digital and e-commerce marketing for profitable sites</li>
<li>Experience in digital marketing, brand awareness, CMS, social media, email marketing, affiliate marketing, paid advertising and use of technology</li>
<li>Daily oversight of ecommerce sales and digital advertising providing analytics and reporting</li>
<li>Devise strategies to drive online traffic to company websites</li>
<li>Develop and manage digital marketing campaigns</li>
<li>Utilizing and driving a range of techniques including paid search, SEO and PPC</li>
<li>Responsibility for planning and budgetary control for digital marketing</li>
<li>Ability to continually evaluate and present revenue driven strategies, paid services, best practices, and recommendations to Executive and Leadership team</li>
<li>Ability to support and advise e-commerce team</li>
<li>Evaluating customer research, market conditions and competitor data</li>
<li>Staying current on software/data trends and online trends including reviewing and recommending new technologies to keep the company at the forefront of developments in digital marketing</li>
<li>Develop and oversee affiliate marketing programs</li>
<li>Create and maintain an online business plan</li>
<li>Knowledge of mobile and responsive design and how it relates to customer experience to improve site performance</li>
<li>In depth understanding of web analytic software and data analysis</li>
<li>Tracking conversion rates and making improvements to increase conversion on websites</li>
<li>Knowledge of A/B testing, running digital ad campaigns, and Google Analytics</li>
<li>Coordinate monthly coupon offers to eligible customers across mail and paid ad platforms</li>
<li>Landing page evaluation and campaign testing</li>
<li>Segment ad target audiences and allocate budgets daily based on response/source with performance/ROI in mind</li>
<li>Source new channels for email subscribers with ROI in mind</li>
<li>Maintain, update and create automation introductory processes for mailing database in MailChimp</li>
<li>Set up, testing and scheduling of emails in MailChimp</li>
<br>
</ul>
