<h2>Sales Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>This is an exciting opportunity for a Sales Manager to join Storyful. The role of Sales Manager is to drive and grow revenue with Storyful’s key publisher partners. Storyful works with the world’s largest publishers, from the Washington Post and The Wall Street Journal. Our products range from software-as-a-service style Newswire products to bespoke, consultative partnerships including our News Intelligence and Misinformation Training initiatives.</p>
<p>We are looking for a dynamic sales entrepreneur who is client focused with a passion for journalism and in-depth knowledge of user-generated content, social media and digital video. The ideal candidate will be able to demonstrate a track record of building new business as well as growing business with existing clients. Above all else, we are looking for someone that is passionate about selling content and enthusiastic about the prospect of building a new, high-growth business.</p>
<p>Reporting directly to the Director of Sales, News you will drive and develop long term client relationships and lead negotiations alongside Storyful’s News Intelligence, Editorial, Partner Success and Product teams to deliver customer-centric solutions.</p>
<p>Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Consistently meet and exceed your revenue targets through renewals of annual contracts, upselling of new products and identification of new partners.</li>
<li>Prepare client negotiations, presentations and proposals for your portfolio</li>
<li>Identify gaps in revenue and implement relevant strategies to drive revenue</li>
<li>Seek and identify new business prospects for the Storyful News division</li>
<li>Prospect, pitch and close sales</li>
<li>Maintain an awareness of competitor activity and make recommendations to the leadership team</li>
<li>Build and maintain client relationships and ensure high levels of sales service and a customer-centric approach</li>
<li>Actively participate in the building of sales products based on client needs</li>
<li>Provide management with weekly client updates and accurate sales forecasting</li>
<li>Open doors with key decision makers at publishers and media organizations</li>
<li>Bring an entrepreneurial spirit that adds to the culture of a start-up (Storyful), but also an ability to think of the broader context of how business can be expanded within the larger organization (News Corporation)</li>
</ul>
<p>Skills and Experience Required</p>
<ul>
<li>4 years experience in a revenue-focused sales role within the digital marketing/advertising or social software industry</li>
<li>Proven to thrive in a startup environment</li>
<li>Proven record of prospecting, growing and managing revenue</li>
<li>Expertise in the digital marketing ecosystem, including but not limited to UGC, video, brand content, and social insights</li>
<li>Bachelor Degree or equivalent experience favourable·</li>
<li>Strong computer skills, including Excel, PowerPoint, Google tool-set and other cloud-based business tools</li>
<li>An expansive network of publisher and media connections</li>
<li>Experience using a CRM system (salesforce preferable)</li>
<li>Strategic solutions seller with a consultative style</li>
<li>Expert networking abilities</li>
<li>Ability to build an extensive list of prospects</li>
<li>Exceptional business writing skills</li>
<li>Some travel may be required</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul>
<li>Sales: 3 years (Preferred)</li>
<li>Customer Service: 3 years (Preferred)</li>
<li>outside sales: 3 years (Preferred)</li>
</ul>
<p>Education:</p>
<ul><li>Associate (Preferred)</li></ul>
