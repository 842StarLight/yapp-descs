<h2>Home Lending Post Closing Specialist-Tigard</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>JOB PURPOSE</p>
<p>
Responsible for Home Lending post-closing process by ensuring loan files meet investor requirements. Ensure documentation and file are fully complete and in compliance.</p>
<p>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for ensuring loan files meet investor requirements and documentation and file are fully complete and in compliance. Collaborate with Funding, Secondary, and other operational and sales associates to facilitate delivery of complete and compliant loan packages to investors.</li>
<li>Perform downloads from electronic loan files to imaging and depository systems. Responsible for issuance and guarantee of mortgage loans using external websites and activation and payment of mortgage insurance.</li>
<li>Responsible for delivering excellent data integrity and accuracy while maintaining service level agreements and regulatory compliance.</li>
<li>Partner and collaborate with all HL associates to ensure open communication, timely responses, expedient and accurate trailing condition gathering and clearing, and a superior customer experience.</li>
<li>Responsible for compliance with all bank regulations for assigned job function and applying to designated job responsibilities. Keep up to date on regulation and policy changes. Follow all Bank policies and procedures, compliance regulations, and complete and stay up to date on all required annual or job-specific training.</li>
<li>Identify opportunities for continuous improvement.</li>
<li>Assist with coaching, mentoring, or training others; teach coursework as a Subject Matter Expert or acting as a resource for others Home Lending associates as needed.</li>
<li>Demonstrates compliance with all bank regulations for assigned job function and applies to designated job responsibilities - knowledge may be gained through coursework and on-the-job training. Keeps up to date on regulation changes.</li>
<li>Follows all Bank policies and procedures, compliance regulations, and completes all required annual or job-specific training.</li>
<li>Maintain a working knowledge of Bank's written policies and procedures regarding Bank Secrecy Act, Regulation CC, Regulation E, Bank Security and other regulations as applicable to this job description.</li>
<li>May be asked to coach, mentor, or train others and teach coursework as subject matter expert.</li>
<li>Actively learns, demonstrates, and fosters the Umpqua corporate culture in all actions and words.</li>
<li>Takes personal initiative and is a positive example for others to emulate.</li>
<li>Embraces our vision to become "The World's Greatest Bank."</li>
<li>May perform other duties as assigned.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p>SUPERVISORY RESPONSIBILITIES None</p>
<p>
REQUIRED KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS AND ABILITIES</p>
<ul>
<li>High School Diploma or GED.</li>
<li>3-5 years' experience in mortgage lending, processing, funding, title, escrow or related field.</li>
<li>Strong knowledge of mortgage loan documents and title, escrow, and general mortgage flow and functions or related banking or financial field.</li>
<li>Strong understanding of regulatory requirements or ability to understand and comply with regulatory requirements.</li>
<li>Possess strong verbal communication and personal interaction skills, with ability to work effectively with individuals and groups across the company to manage internal and external customer relationships.</li>
<li>Possess strong organizational and reading comprehension skills.</li>
<li>Proficient with personal computer and related software packages, including MS Office Word, Excel, and Outlook, and LOS, and other business machines. Proficient with data entry and possess that ability to balance production standards with data integrity and accuracy.</li>
</ul>
<p>PHYSICAL AND ENVIRONMENTAL DEMANDS</p>
<ul>
<li>Office environment - no specific or unusual physical or environmental demands.</li>
<li>Occasional travel.</li>
</ul>
