<p>Job Description</p>
<p>The Restaurant Manager is responsible for operating a profitable and enjoyable restaurant, where the quality of service and ambience are continually challenged and improved, and where an environment that fosters creativity, free thinking and intelligent decision-making is encouraged. The Restaurant Manager is responsible for maintaining an effective program for training and developing the FOH team. The Restaurant Manager is responsible for assisting in creating and maintaining an upbeat, productive and educational environment. The Restaurant Manager projects a positive attitude, and visible respect for all employees. The Restaurant Manager provides feedback and direction to the FOH line employees to continue their development. The Restaurant Manager is an important member in the management team and therefore participates in meetings to discuss the business and operation of the restaurant.</p>
<br>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>DUTIES &amp; RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<br>
<p><b>
</b></p>
<p><b>Leadership</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Inspirational with managers and employees</li>
<li>Practices empathetic and educated listening</li>
<li>Runs effective, concise, inspirational line ups consistently</li>
<li>Runs effective pre-shift meetings</li>
<li>Is decisive</li>
<li>Acts and follows through with integrity</li>
<li>Knows when to act and when to stand back</li>
<li>Upholds and enhances the culture</li>
<li>Sees the future and is a student of the business</li>
<li>Proactive attitude regarding all aspects of the business</li>
<li>Makes decisions that are in the best interest of the guests and employees</li>
<li>Alert to all aspects of the operation throughout the shift</li>
<li>Recognizes potential problems and solves them quickly and efficiently</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Integrity</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Able to make appropriate decisions without personal emotion</li>
<li>Separates self from hourlies in and out of work</li>
<li>Runs restaurant as if owner</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Coaching, Teaching and Confronting Incompetence</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Can perform one minute management</li>
<li>Uses "See it, Say it" approach to coaching on the floor</li>
<li>Practices "Praise in public, punish in private"</li>
<li>Holds hourly and management team accountable</li>
<li>Fosters an environment of continuous learning with new information, testing, and reading</li>
<li>Confronts incompetence in a mature and timely manner</li>
<li>Follows up on feedback with confirmation of the change in behavior</li>
<li>Documents all performance feedback in a timely manner</li>
<li>Assists in recruiting, hiring and scheduling a crew of quality employees</li>
<li>Determines and maintains staffing needs to ensure guest satisfaction based on current and projected business levels</li>
<li>Implements company systems</li>
<li>Trains staff in service, guest relations, safety, use of equipment, etc.</li>
<li>Works with the General Manager and BOH to provide on-going training and understanding of menu items and preparation to service staff</li>
<li>Provides consistent and frequent feedback to staff</li>
<li>Teaches and coaches staff on a daily basis</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Service Management
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Personally greets the staff as they arrive and takes an interest in them</li>
<li>Holds service staff accountable</li>
<li>Constantly pursues great service while coaching the staff</li>
<li>Enjoys other restaurants service and learns from the experience</li>
<li>Promotes an environment of teamwork</li>
<li>Gives inspiring and informative line ups</li>
<li>Can write an effective schedule avoiding overtime, while maintaining proper staffing levels</li>
<li>Actively works the floor looking to improve service, help the staff, and/or speed up the turn times</li>
<li>Effectively runs the front desk and quotes the wait</li>
<li>Makes an impact by driving the shift</li>
<li>Maintains acceptable levels of inventory for front of the house operation</li>
<li>Ensures the strict companywide adherence to Sauce’s standards for quality of food served and quality of operations</li>
<li>Commitment to having a knowledgeable staff who are proud of the quality of our products and who believe in our concept</li>
<li>Attention for detail</li>
<li>Maintains high standards in all areas of safety, security and sanitation</li>
<li>Reports cleaning and repairs to the General Manager in a timely manner</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Bar Management</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Can effectively order what is needed</li>
<li>Manages to appropriate inventory levels</li>
<li>Ensures that pour cost is managed and controlled</li>
<li>Manages purveyor relationships appropriately</li>
<li>Manages service at the bar to ensure it is up to the same standard as the dining room</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Kitchen Management</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Can have open and intelligent dialog with the chef without intimidation</li>
<li>Is comfortable doing a mid-shift walk through of the kitchen to validate prep levels and food quality for the dinner shift</li>
<li>Pushes food back and demands perfect execution from the line</li>
<li>Participates in and follows up on EcoSure and Health Inspections</li>
<li>Helps ensure acceptable levels of inventory</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Floor Management</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Capable of running the front door on a busy shift with a wait</li>
<li>Exudes hospitality at the front door</li>
<li>Coaches the host team and continuously educates that staff about the restaurant as a whole</li>
<li>Controls the flow of business to set the team up for a successful shift</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Facilities Management</b></p>
<ul>
<li>General monthly maintenance programs are adhered to</li>
<li>Cleanliness of physical plant is noticeable; regularly scheduled programs are in place</li>
<li>Knowledgeable of water/sewer, gas, and electric shut off locations</li>
<li>Fire extinguishers are serviced prior to or upon expiration</li>
<li>Works with the Building Maintenance/Management Company/Landlord to constantly maintain the back dock, trash areas and front sidewalks as well as any communal trash cans</li>
<li>All pest control is performed on a regular basis</li>
<li>All plants and flowers are maintained</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>

<p><b>Guest Connection</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Knows and practices the "art" of touching tables</li>
<li>Uses great guest communication, both verbal and written</li>
<li>Executes guest recovery and follows up appropriately</li>
<li>Gives support to staff decisions (i.e.: cutting a guest off)</li>
<li>Excellent problem solver</li>
<li>Builds guest loyalty</li>
<li>Cultivates a regular clientele</li>
<li>Works to build food sales by interacting, greeting, seating and making the guests feel at ease</li>
<li>Solves problems with an understanding of the importance of each guest being determined to come back</li>
</ul>

<li>Is aware of the local market business environment</li>
<li>Participates in area business groups or affiliations</li>
<li>Knows neighboring businesses and their operators</li>
<li>Contributes to charities that are tied to our company or your guests</li>
<li>Joins networks that further our place in the business community</li>
<li>Rewards repeat guests with VIP treatment</li>
<li>Participates in media events such as wine tastings and food events, including buy outs</li>
<li>Works with the rest of the management team to maximize sales and optimize profits through the management of food, labor costs and FOH Controllable</li>
</ul>

<li>Does not air out any inappropriate company business with hourly employees or guests</li>
<li>Does not gossip or spread rumors or initiate drama</li>

<li>Is aware of sales and labor budgets and is able to discuss then intelligently at all times</li>
