<h2>Sales Development Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Topbox is seeking a Sales Development Rep with a passion for prospecting, qualifying inbound leads, and employing creative tactics to overcome objections and set meetings for our account executives. Topbox is a B2B SaaS application, and our customer list is impressive and growing. Please see our website for a deep dive of what we do: topbox.io
<br><br>
You will have click-to-dial, local number dialing, recorded voicemails, automated email sequences, and access to the DiscoverOrg contact database all in your arsenal, but ultimately what will set you apart is your Sales spirit of relentlessly pursuing the win.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Make 100+ purposeful, outbound dials per day and send targeted emails to prospects to initiate and nurture meaningful business relationships and to uncover new opportunities</li>
<li>Conduct pre-call research to gather company and contact intelligence</li>
<li>Coordinate and participate in qualified product demos</li>
<li>Develop and nurture lead lists</li>
<li>Work closely with the marketing department to support marketing campaign efforts in conjunction with your own sourcing of leads.</li>
<li>Work with Sales Leadership and other critical company resources to meet or exceed company revenue goals</li>
</ul>
<b>Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Some SDR experience, preferably in B2B technology</li>
<li>High energy - High professionalism</li>
<li>Intensely goal-oriented and motivated by a strong compensation plan</li>
<li>Proven track record</li>
<li>Excellent written and communication skills</li>
<li>Familiarity with CRM platforms or related software - HubSpot preferred</li>
</ul>
<b>Personal Attributes and Values:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Fits with a hard-working, innovative, and customer-focused culture</li>
<li>Self-starter comfortable operating autonomously, with full support but minimal structure and hands-on direction</li>
<li>Articulate, with a proven ability to communicate effectively in person and on the phone</li>
<li>Smart, decisive, organized, methodical, biased toward action</li>
<li>Can think on your feet and keep a cool head under pressure</li>
<li>Creative thinker and high aptitude for solving problems</li>
</ul>
<b>Compensation:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>$64,000 On-target earnings</li>
<li>$40,000 base + $250/qualified meeting, with a target of 8 meetings/month</li>
<li>There is no cap on your commissions! If you set 20 meetings/month all year, your total comp would be $100,000</li>
<li>Full medical, dental, and vision, and a 401k plan</li>
</ul>
