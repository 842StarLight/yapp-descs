<p><b>Vice President of Sales – Denver, CO</b></p>
<p>Are you a successful enterprise level sales professional who has experience selling into data centers and clients like them? Do you enjoy managing a highly-skilled sales team while also hunting for new enterprise clients? Do you want a role where you have a seat at the table and can affect change for a growing, well-funded organization in the IT infrastructure space? Do you want to work with cutting-edge technology that is paving the way in composable infrastructure? If so, we may have the perfect position for you…</p>
<p>We are recruiting for a VP of Sales that will be based out of beautiful Denver, Colorado area. The VP of Sales will be tasked with leading a team of highly skilled enterprise sales professionals, breaking into new verticals, while creating key processes and procedures along the way. You’ll be providing strategic thought leadership to help our client become a world-class sales organization. This position is perfect for someone who has been managing a tenured sales team across the US and knows how to remove barriers, streamline efficiencies and has demonstrated success selling IT infrastructure solutions to different verticals. You’ll love this job if you want a combination of hunting and managing.</p>
<p><b>Here’s what our client needs: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>You’ll be managing a small team of highly skilled sales representatives that are spread across the United States. The sales team needs help in improving closing ratios, managing the sales process and breaking into new verticals.</li>
<li>You’ll need to have demonstrated success in selling IT infrastructure solutions to one of the following verticals: media and entertainment, financial services, government sector or wireless/telecommunications. You need to have previous success selling into one of these verticals where you can bring your contacts and connections.</li>
<li>Our client is a growing organization with sales as their primary route to success. They will need your help in developing a successful sales process, forecasting tools and the like.</li>
<li>You’ll be part of the leadership team, representing sales to the board, so you’ll need to have a deep understanding of your pipeline with poise and ability to clearly articulate your pipeline to senior leadership.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Job requirements: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>10+ years successful track record in selling enterprise, highly complex IT Infrastructure solutions in either media and entertainment, financial services, government or telco/wireless</li>
<li>6+ years leading and being accountable for an enterprise sales team</li>
<li>Demonstrated experience building a best-in-class sales process</li>
<li>5+ years selling complex technical solutions to large enterprises (either servers, chips, HDD or other datacenter solutions)</li>
<li>Salesforce experience</li>
<li>Be willing to work and live in Colorado</li>
<li>Travel 50% nationally and occasional international travel</li>
</ul>
<p><b>More about this opportunity: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Our client offers an incredible compensation plan where you will receive a competitive base, overrides on your team’s success as well as a lucrative commission structure on your own sales, plus stock options.</li>
<li>Additionally, they offer top notch benefits that include health insurance (employer pays 100% of your health care premiums) and unlimited PTO.</li>
<li>They support a diverse and inclusive work environment and believe that everyone has a gift to give. Being part of their team means being around of some of the smartest people in the industry.</li>
</ul>
