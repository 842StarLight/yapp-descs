<h2>Senior Training/Education Manager (Nationwide)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>
<h5 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>POSITION PURPOSE</b></h5>
<br>
<p>
</p>
<p>The Sr Training/Education Manager, Americas CEP will report directly to the Director, Commercial Enterprise Programming.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>This position will support the Medtronic Commercial Enterprise Programming strategy. Working directly with business unit commercial operations, business unit sales training colleagues, and other key stakeholders, this individual will oversee implementation of large-scale sales training programs across the Medtronic organization. This role is responsible for providing interface, guidance, and direction to Medtronic business units to ensure successful program execution. Managing people, training plans, resources and technology are critical needs for successful execution and implementation of the CEP strategy. Build strong relationships with key business personnel including commercial operations, sales training, and marketing partners to effectively obtain required support.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS</b></h1>
<br>
<p>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Establish program objectives, implementation plans, and budget to ensure Commercial Enterprise Programing and business unit sales training needs are met.</li>
<li>Serve as Americas expert on CEP strategy and programs across Medtronic as well as advise other geographies where requested and appropriate.
</li>
<li>Direct curriculum deployment, including online and classroom content for Selling and/or assigned programs.</li>
<li>Partner with business unit sales training and marketing teams to align and execute support activities for Selling and/or assigned programs.</li>
<li>Partner with Training Ops and business unit resources to ensure logistics planning and execution of programs.</li>
<li>Responsible for analyzing program effectiveness and adjusting where necessary or needed.</li>
<li>Responsible for identifying the need for program strategic alterations and advising global specialist team.</li>
</ul>
<div>8. Participate on cross functional business unit teams that are responsible for integration and delivery of key programs</div>
<ul>
<li>Drive program acceptance and implementation within business units.</li>
<li>Identify support activities necessary to drive skill/knowledge development and adoption related to programs.
</li>
<li>Manage appropriate tracking of results and ROI for assigned programs.</li>
<li>Regularly make presentations to business unit key stakeholders.</li>
</ul>Perform other duties as assigned.</div>
