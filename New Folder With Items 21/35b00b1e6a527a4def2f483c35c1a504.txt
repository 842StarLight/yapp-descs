<h2>Client Technology Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Position Summary</p>
<p>A Client Technology Manager (CTM) is responsible for technical oversight, guidance and consultative services to the Eze Castle customer base. CTM’s act as a “Virtual CTO” for clients, providing strategy aligned with industry best practices. This includes proactive management of client technology, resolving high priority escalations and contributing standards and procedures to the global technology community. The candidate will interact with executive level resources and should operate with the highest level of presentation, communication and professionalism. CTM’s are expected to provide guidance, leadership and mentorship, to all levels of ECI service staff. Along with Customer Success Managers (CSM), CTM’s will be an integral part of technical translation in the process of business development and sales opportunities within the existing client base.</p>
<p><b>
Position Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>College diploma or University degree in the field of Computer Science or equivalent field</li>
<li>5-10 years of experience in IT management/leadership</li>
<li>Exceptional written and oral communication skills</li>
<li>Exceptional interpersonal skills, with a focus on rapport-building, listening, and questioning skills</li>
<li>Motivated to pursue technical certifications and further education</li>
<li>Highly self-motivated and directed, in a fast-paced team environment</li>
<li>Ability to delegate tasks and manage to completion</li>
<li>Sharp attention to detail with proven analytical and problem-solving abilities.</li>
<li>Effective time management in working on internal tasks as well as with external vendors</li>
<li>Able to design, lead and execute project plans</li>
<li>Capable of working cross-departmentally to achieve service delivery</li>
<li>Can interpret customer needs and translate to technical deliverables</li>
<li>Able to articulate technical needs and requirements to non-technical audiences</li>
<li>Capable of providing strategic guidance on technical budgets</li>
<li>Experience with team management and career development</li>
<li>Competent in industry oriented regulatory and cyber-security standards</li>
<li>Able to independently conduct quarterly Technical Business Reviews (TBR) with clients</li>

</ul>
