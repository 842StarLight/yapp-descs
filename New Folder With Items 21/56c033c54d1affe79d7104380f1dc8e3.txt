<h2>Corporate Council - Retail Electricity</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Reports to</b><b>: </b> CEO</p>
<p><b>Start date: </b> Negotiable - Sometime during Q4-2019</p>
<p><b>Salary: </b> Negotiable – Depending on experience</p>
<p><b>Location: </b> Houston, Texas – Flexible remote working possible</p>
<p>An established retail electricity provider (REP) in the Texas market, is about to embark on its next phase of growth. This will be a technology-driven strategy, through partnership with an Indo-Dutch energy-software company, and a syndicate of VC investors.</p>
<p>This is a new strategic leadership position. The experience the Corporate Counsel brings will be an important part of realising the company’s growth ambitions, and this is deemed a key hire.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities include: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Directing and overseeing regulatory and legal projects, processes, and compliance.</li>
<li>Ensuring that suitable process controls exist for all types of acquisitions, and acquisition channels.</li>
<li>Reviewing new acquisition programs to address any legal and regulatory concerns.</li>
<li>Ensuring accurate, complete and timely regulatory filings for state-level, and federal-level organizations.</li>
<li>Providing input for regulatory and compliance audits of the company’s sales, marketing, and customer service activities.</li>
<li>Review and oversight of various contracts including; supplier contracts, customer retail contracts, employment contracts, third-party vendor contracts</li>
<li>Review of contracts related to sales, marketing, billing, and IT services.</li>
<li>Manage all external counsel-related relationships and strategy for litigation management, mediation, and settlement.</li>
<li>Assess and respond to internal legal and regulatory inquiries.</li>
<li>Assess and respond to inquiries from the state-level, and federal-level commissions.</li>
<li>Keep abreast of state-level and federal-level policy developments and issues which will impact the company.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications and experience: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>J.D. degree from an ABA-accredited law school.</li>
<li>License to practice law in Texas, with a record in good standing.</li>
<li>10 years of transactional experience in a law firm, or a corporate in-house team.</li>
<li>5 years of experience in the Texas retail energy space.</li>
<li>Experience of setting up a REP from scratch will be significantly valued.</li>
<li>Track record of leading successful high-value projects.</li>
<li>Experience of scaling companies through M&amp;A.</li>
<li>Retail energy experience in other states of the USA with deregulated electricity markets ideal, and will be valued.</li>
<li>Previous experience working with Public Utility Commissions ideal.</li>
<li>Analysis and interpretation of applicable tariffs, regulations and laws.</li>
<li>International experience will be valued.</li>
<li>General corporate and commercial expertise, including drafting and reviewing of retail and commercial contracts.</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills, as the role will entail interaction across several countries.</li>
</ul>
