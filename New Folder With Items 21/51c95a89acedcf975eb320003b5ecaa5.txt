<h2>Global Campaign Manager – Clinical Genomics (Germantown, MD)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div><div><div><div>
<ul>The Campaign Management department is part of a full-service internal marketing agency that works with the molecular diagnostics, life science research and bioinformatics global business areas at QIAGEN. Each Global Campaign Manager is a business partner and project manager on a research-focused or clinical-focused Global Business Team. He/she consults on marketing strategy and channel mix of annual marketing plans, and leads the development and execution of global and regional marketing campaigns.<br>
This role reports to the Director, Campaign Management, MDx.<br>
<br>
Role Summary:<br>
<li>Manages the planning and implementation of projects that support the marketing function for the Clinical Genomics team.</li>
<li>Provides consulting expertise to 2 global business areas (Molecular diagnostics &amp; Bioinformatics) and regional marketing on strategies and marketing channel mix during annual planning, and on a regular, ongoing basis throughout the year.</li>
<li>Leads a cross-functional global project team.</li>
<li>Develops and executes multi-channel marketing campaigns for clinical diagnostic market segments in genomics, personalized healthcare, and bioinformatics.</li>
<li>Promotes marketing content through a combination of digital channels including website/microsites, online webinars, email, account-based marketing, social media and video.</li>
<li>Develops campaign strategy &amp; tactics for trade shows/events.</li>
<li>Supports training &amp; coordination with marketing &amp; sales for lead processing &amp; follow-up.
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<b>Position Requirements</b>
<br>
<br>
Education:<br>
<li>Masters’ degree or PhD in life sciences or bioinformatics required or a Bachelor’s degree combined with 5-8 years of relevant work experience in a biotech, pharma or diagnostics company required.</li>
<li>MBA required or 2-5 years’ experience in project management or managing direct reports required.</li>
<br>
Work Experience:<br>
<li>Extensive expertise in marketing, advertising, marketing communications OR product management normally acquired through 2-5 years’ work experience in marketing.</li>
<li>Digital marketing experience required.</li>
<li>Marketing automation experience preferred.
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<b>Personal Requirements</b>
<br>
<br>
- Highly organized and able to manage multiple projects simultaneously.<br>
<li>Strong leadership skills required and ability to lead by influence.</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal skills with an ability to work effectively with local and internationally-based project team members.</li>
<li>Proficiency in English required.</li>
<li>Ability to travel up to 20%.</li>
<li>Ability to use computer and telecommunications equipment and to sit for extended periods of time.
</li>
</ul>
