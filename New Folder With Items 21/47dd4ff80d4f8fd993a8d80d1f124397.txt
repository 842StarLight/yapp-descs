Your day to day</div>
<div>
<p>As Director of Sales &amp; Marketing you’ll develop and implement sales and marketing strategies for the hotel, create and implement tactical plans and ensure revenue goals are achieved.</p>
<p>
•Direct and manage the day-to-day activities of the sales department, plan, organize, and assign work, develop and communicate strategies and goals.•Create, train, and motivate a sales team that is responsible for developing new accounts, maintaining existing accounts, and implementing sales strategies to achieve revenue goals and maximize profits for the hotel, while maintaining guest satisfaction.•Develop and maintain relationships with key clients to produce business•Achieve budgeted revenues and maximize profitability within all areas of responsibility. Produce monthly reports and sales forecasts.•Monitor sales activities/performance to ensure actual sales meet or exceed established revenue plan.•Identify operational problems that impact the effectiveness of marketing activities and overall hotel sales performance and work on solutions.•Analyze current/potential market and sales trends and coordinate all activities to maintain and increase revenue and market share through added business volume and increased rate. Create and develop special events and sales blitzes to showcase the hotel to potential clients.•Review advertising, print and other media for the hotel. •Attend trade shows and major market events locally and nationally to promote new business and increase sales for the hotel.•Promote teamwork and quality service through daily communication and coordination with other departments.</p>
</div>
<div>
What we need from you</div>
<div><p>•8+ years of sales and marketing related experience•Bachelor’s degree in sales, business administration, marketing or communications•Possess prior leadership experience in directly managing a department within similar hotel scope and display strong leadership competencies•Possess strong creative and analytical skills to create and assess impact of sales and marketing programs and be detail-oriented.•Must be able to travel to attend workshops, tradeshows, conventions, etc. and may be required to work nights, weekends, and/or holidays.•Computer proficiency is required. •Highly developed written and verbal English communication skills to effectively negotiate, persuade, influence, and sell are critical•Proven track record of business milestones</p></div>
