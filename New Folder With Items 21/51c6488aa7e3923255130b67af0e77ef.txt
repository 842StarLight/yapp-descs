<h2>Marketing Communications Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div><div><div>
<p><b>POSITION DESCRIPTION:</b></p>
<p>The Marketing Department develops strategies and implements solutions in support of Service Credit Union’s business services, consumer, and military market segments in the US and Germany, as well as corporate initiatives. The Specialist manages the execution of projects that promote SCU’s products and services within your assigned line of business. Position relies on business acumen and project management expertise to ensure the development of high-quality materials that are produced on time, on budget, and on strategy.
</p>
<p><b>POSITION RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<p><b><i>Business &amp; Brand Knowledge</i></b></p>
<p>Responsible to understand the target audience(s), product offerings and competitive positioning associated with your assigned line of business at Service Credit Union.
</p>
<p>Maintain awareness of corporate sales and marketing initiatives within your assigned line of business, keeping Marketing colleagues informed as it relates to potential points of integration across lines of business and/or projects.</p>
<p><b><i>Project &amp; Process Management</i></b></p>
<p>Responsible for day-to-day project management and team collaboration in support of your assigned line of business, ensuring all materials are produced on time, on budget, and on strategy.</p>
<p>Projects are appropriately scoped in advance of initiating work,</p>
<p>Project initiation, estimating and scheduling processes followed,</p>
<p>On-going trafficking of active projects as well as discrete deliverables,</p>
<p>On-going prioritization of project deliverables, especially when scheduling/resource conflicts occur,</p>
<p>Regular communication with Assistant VP, Marketing Communications and Marketing team colleagues, including project timing, status reports, meeting summaries, etc.</p>
<p><b><i>Communication &amp; Collaboration</i></b></p>
<p>Develop and encourage team brainstorming, surfacing creative ideas and solutions that enable Marketing to not only meet the needs of the business but exceed expectations.</p>
<p>Work collaboratively with Marketing Department colleagues, communicating with one another and sharing information to enable individual and team performance—operating in a unified, cohesive manner.</p>
<br>

<div>
Qualifications</div>
<div><div>
<div>
<p><b>SKILLS AND/OR QUALIFICATIONS</b><b>:</b></p>
<p>Minimum of five years in marketing communications and/or advertising, preferably in a fast-paced, deadline-driven environment; agency experience is a plus.</p>
<p>Military experience a plus.</p>
<p>Proven understanding of advertising and marketing project types and accompanying processes, as well as the job functions that comprise a marketing communications team.</p>
<p>Strong organizational and time management skills with a focus on quality and attention to detail; ability to manage multiple tasks simultaneously while consistency meeting deadlines.</p>
<p>Strong interpersonal communication skills (written and verbal); ability to interact successfully with individuals at all levels.</p>
<p>Positive, collaborative, flexible disposition; equally adept at working independently or as part of a team.</p>
<p>Customer oriented with a commitment to proactive problem solving to enable member satisfaction and team success.</p>
<p>Proficiency with Microsoft Office (Word, Excel and PowerPoint).</p>
<p><b>CRITERIA</b><b>:</b></p>
<p>Bachelor’s degree in business, marketing, or related field of study required</p>
<p>Hours are specialized to support our overseas efforts which would be 6 am – 3:30 pm EST.</p>
<p><b>PHYSICAL REQUIREMENTS:</b></p>
<p>Ability to lift approximately 20 lbs as deemed necessary.</p>
<p>Ability to use computers for up to 8 hours</p>
<p>Ability to sit for up to 2 hours</p>
<p>Ability to travel to other work locations as required, if needed</p>
