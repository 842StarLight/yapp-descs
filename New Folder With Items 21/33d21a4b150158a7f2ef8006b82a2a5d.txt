<br>Description
<br>PleasureLand RV Center is the largest RV dealership in the Upper Midwest. We have been ensuring customer satisfaction and helping people select the right RV for their needs since 1971.
<br><br>
<b>POSITION PURPOSE:</b> PleasureLand RV Center is currently hiring for a part time Administrative Assistant to work out of our St Cloud location right off of I94. This position provides a friendly welcome to customers as they walk through the doors and directs them to the appropriate department as well as assist in a variety of tasks. The hours for this position would be afternoons and some Saturdays but can be flexible to try to accommodate other and personal schedules.
<br><br>
<b>ESSENTIAL JOB DUTIES:</b><br>
Professionally and pleasantly greet and direct every customer to the appropriate department.
<br>Answer the main phone line and transfer caller to the proper department.
<br>Maintain inventory for office supplies and computer software.
<br>Receive and distribute incoming mail and coordinate outgoing mail.
<br>Maintain and update as needed the brochures and brochure racks.
<br>Insure proper presentation and cleanliness of reception area.
<br>Assist and support other departments as requested.
<br>Perform other duties as assigned by General/Sales Manager.
