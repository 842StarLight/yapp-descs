<h2>User Acqusition Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">

<div>User Acqusition Manager-PRO01446</div>
<p></p>
<div>
Description<div>
</div>
<div>
As the User Acquisition Manager on the User Growth and Adoption team (“UGA”), you will have a unique opportunity to work with a team situated at the intersection of product, design, data, marketing, sales and broader strategic efforts.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
As part of the team, you will have the opportunity to directly impact user growth for our new and existing products and services, as well as serve as a thought leader to the organization on key strategic initiatives.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
About the Team</b></div>
<div>
Asurion’s User Growth &amp; Adoption (“UGA”) team is growing quickly to support the needs of various product teams within Global Product Development. Acting as internal consultants, UGA partners with existing Product teams to devise the best growth strategies for their product or service. We move quickly to understand the customer need, product/market fit, and create marketing plans to increase new users and/or improve existing user adoption.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
You are a great match for us if….</b></div>
<div>
You’re equal parts analytical and creative. You geek out over data, have hands-on experience managing AdWords accounts, can craft witty Facebook ad concepts and have proven experience driving consistent growth in paid channels.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
You should have a high level of creativity, attention to detail, willingness to take risks, strong project management skills, and an ability to roll-up your sleeves to get the job done. You should thrive in fast-paced and feedback-driven environment and strive to be a key contributor to a rapidly-evolving team.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
What you’ll do:</b></div>
<div>
You will own and manage paid advertising channels including Google SEM, Social Media ads (Facebook/Instagram), Display Advertising, and more. You will help design the user acquisition strategy, define channel mix, roll out tests to establish CAC per channel, and build out new campaigns. You will work in collaboration with other stakeholders in data and design to come up with new ad creative and copy, optimize our advertising spend.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
Responsibilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li> Develop innovative strategies to increase new paying customers, decrease cost per acquisition and outrank our competition.</li>
<li> Perform in-depth audits of our ad accounts to find quick wins as well as long-term optimization opportunities.</li>
<li> Implement innovative tactics in account segmentation, quality score optimization, account automation, bid optimization, keyword expansion, and ad copywriting.</li>
<li> Create, maintain and optimize campaigns in Google AdWords and paid social platforms such as Facebook.</li>
<li> Stay informed of new betas, tactics, and data trends within the paid search industry.</li>
<li> Have a solid understanding of Google Analytics with the ability to analyze and interpret GA data, setup annotations, understand conversion goals, track performance and review paid search impact.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
Required:</b></div>
<ul>
<li> 4-year bachelor’s degree</li>
<li> Minimum 3 years of experience in a B2C digital marketing role; minimum 5 years total work experience</li>
<li> Proven experience and success managing user acquisition campaigns and associated budgets across Google, YouTube, Facebook, and Instagram</li>
<li> Proficiency with Google Analytics</li>
<li> Well-versed in performance marketing, conversion, and online customer acquisition</li>
<li> Strong analytical skills and experience generating ad performance reporting</li>
<li> Desirable:</li>
<li> Experience with A/B testing, multivariate testing, and statistical significance</li>
<li> Experience with Amazon’s ad platform and Adobe Analytics.</li>
</ul>

<div><b>
About Asurion:</b></div>
<div>
Simply put, Asurion helps people stay connected. As the global leader of connected life services, we provide over 300 million consumers around the world with simple, intuitive technology advice to help them get the most from their devices; support to fix their issues and connectivity crisis, and device protection to ensure they receive a replacement or repair. When a product is missing or simply doesn't work properly, Asurion’s 19,000 employees are focused on solving the problem with people and processes operating 24 hours a day, seven days a week, speaking six languages, and working across any device, platform, or provider. By partnering with leading retailers, mobile carriers and pay-tv providers, Asurion helps customers enhance their lives through their technology. For more information about Asurion, please visit www.asurion.com</div>
