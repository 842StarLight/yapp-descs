<h2>Azure Cloud Architect</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Our CI&amp;E practice focuses around the common foundational components required in a Cloud first world. Network, Storage, Compute, Security, and Automation all play an ever-increasing role in our clients' businesses today. Whether it be a myriad of connected devices, business critical applications and data, real time cognitive/analytics, end to end automation to support CI/CD pipelines these services are core to support their operations. As these areas continue to grow and this space continues to rapidly evolve and become more software-centric it also needs to continuously be aware of reducing the threat surface and adopt a zero-trust model. Our CI&amp;E practice help guide our clients through multi-year transformations that are necessary in the wake of technology disruptions such as cloud and IoT.<br>
</div>

<div><b>What we do</b></div>
<div>Our practitioners work collaboratively within integrated, cross-functional teams to deliver leading edge, business relevant, technology solutions to our clients. Our practitioners will advise, guide, architect and implement cloud-based solutions. Teamwork is crucial as teams are composed of a diverse group of specialists across multiple disciplines from both within our consulting practice and from our clients, helping drive towards a successful outcome. Our professionals are well known for their technical depth and breadth in the cloud space.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<p><b>Cloud Engineering</b></p>
<p>Cloud has transformed the way technology enables the business. It represents a radical shift in how IT services are obtained, used, and managed. Companies are navigating through the various cloud options and deciding between private, public, hybrid, or a combination across different and dynamic provider ecosystems. Our employees are empowered to think outside of the box and provide innovative solutions to our customers.</p>
<p>Our Cloud Engineering team focuses on enabling our client’s end to end journey from On-Premise to Cloud, with opportunities in the areas of: Cloud Strategy, Op Model Transformation, Cloud Development, Cloud Integration &amp; APIs, Cloud migration, Cloud Infrastructure &amp; Engineering, and Cloud Managed Services. We help our clients see the transformational capabilities of Cloud as an opportunity for business enablement and competitive advantage.</p>
<p>Cloud Engineering supports our clients as they improve agility and resilience while identifying opportunities to reduce IT operations spend through Cloud-based automation. We accelerate our clients towards a technology-driven future, leveraging vendor solutions and Deloitte-developed software products, tools, and accelerators.</p>
<p><b>Work you’ll do</b></p>
<p>In partnership with other leaders in the firm, you will focus on selling and delivering cloud solutions using Azure and as a key member of our Architecture group, ensure success in building and migrating applications, software and services on public cloud.</p>
<ul>
<li><div>Leverage your deep technical Cloud skills in compute, storage, network and security to craft leading-edge Azure based solutions for our clients.
</div></li>
<li><div>Educate clients on the value proposition of Public / Hybrid / Edge Cloud solutions and participate in deep architectural discussions to ensure solutions are designed for successful deployment in the cloud and to maximize value to our client’s businesses.</div></li>
<li><div>
Capture and share best-practice knowledge amongst the public cloud solutions architect community around Azure and cloud in general.</div></li>
<li><div>
Promote public cloud and Azure internally to sales and delivery teams to drive growth.</div></li>
<li><div>
Author or otherwise contribute to public cloud customer-facing publications such as whitepapers</div></li>
<li><div>
Develop deep relationships with senior client technical individuals to enable them to be cloud advocates</div></li>
<li><p>
Act as a technical liaison between customers, delivery teams and support.</p></li>
</ul>
