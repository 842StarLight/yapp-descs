<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Company &amp; department description
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Are you interested in the opportunity to enable and influence our commercial and product teams? As a Compliance Product Specialist you will deliver expert advice to our members across a broad product portfolio. Our products range from KYC, AML and Sanctions through to Fraud and Cyber Crime services.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>As part of our Latin America team, you’ll collaborate with account managers and provide specialist compliance knowledge/advice to our customers. The aim is to enable our customers to identify the right compliance solutions for their business.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Our (wider) product specialist team acts as the go-between for our commercial and product organisations, bringing together the best of both worlds.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
With over 40 years of experience enabling the financial community to exchange secure standardised financial messages, SWIFT is uniquely positioned to provide financial crime compliance teams with relevant, simple to use and hosted solutions, enabling banks to be well informed, react quickly and be prepared for regulatory scrutiny.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
We are a global member-owned cooperative and the world’s leading provider of secure financial messaging services. Our messaging platform, products and services connect more than 11,000 banking and securities organisations, market infrastructures and corporate customers in more than 200 countries.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Financial crime compliance is critical to every institution operating in today’s increasingly regulated financial markets. The work we do in the compliance space address industry-wide challenges – in close collaboration with our customers making this an exciting and unusual opportunity.</div>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Working collaboratively with colleagues from across both product and relationship management disciplines you will be accountable for:<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Help us to position and expand SWIFT’s varied compliance offering into the financial services and corporate sectors across Latin America.</li>
<li>Support the management of a revenue and opportunity number by product stream by market and take ownership for its being met</li>
<li>Represent SWIFT, promoting the company’s message, products and services to grow our business (i.e. participate in, and speak at workshops, business forums and conferences as a thought leader)</li>
<li>Continue to develop your market expertise and credibility as a product spokesperson and domain expert across the various compliance disciplines (customer and transaction screening, list management, KYC, data analytics, sanctions filter testing and back office intrusion) and the relevance to the wider industry and SWIFT business.</li>
<li>Communicate relevant market and sales intelligence to management and senior stakeholders (ie. as client feedback, required updates or development items), in informative and engaging formats.</li>
</ul>
