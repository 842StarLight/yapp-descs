<h2>Business Development Manager - Loyalty and Engagement Management</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Main responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Being responsible for the full sales cycle of Comarch’s loyalty and engagement management products;</li>
<li>Developing and source a strong pipeline of prospective clients and qualified leads;</li>
<li>Meeting or exceed sales and profit goals;</li>
<li>Facilitating pre-sales process for new leads, including first contact, discovery phase, meetings and contract negotiation;</li>
<li>Actively managing existing customer accounts, addressing key objectives and strategy, concerns and escalations, overall status and new sales opportunities;</li>
<li>Delivering in-person sales presentations with prospective clients;</li>
<li>Representing Comarch by attending conferences and industry events.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum Bachelor’s Degree;</li>
<li>2+ years of experience working in software/IT sales (preferably B2B);</li>
<li>Fluency in English;</li>
<li>Strong oral and written communication skills;</li>
<li>Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite;</li>
<li>Self-sufficiency and ability to prioritize tasks in a fast-paced environment;</li>
<li>Willingness to travel (25-50%).</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>

<ul>
<li>Competitive salary;</li>
<li>100% paid health insurance (upon permanent employment);</li>
<li>401(k) match;</li>
<li>Flexible Spending Account option (health, daycare, commuter);</li>
<li>Wellness Reimbursement Program;</li>
<li>Exciting work in a rapidly growing department working with leading world brands;</li>
<li>Opportunity to learn from leading specialists to develop professional IT career.</li>
</ul>
