<h2>VP of Product Management</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>Savioke is building a fleet of autonomous robots that are revolutionizing the hospital industry, building on our experience with our robots delivering small items to guests in hotels. We are excited to be building robots that are doing useful work, are freeing up clinicians to be effective, and are touching people’s lives on a daily basis. Our robots have completed more than 500,000 deliveries to guests in hotels, have driven hundreds of kilometers autonomously, and have delighted everyone who sees them.</div>
<div>
</div>
<div>
We are looking for a strong addition to our senior leadership team, a strategist who will translate the company’s high-level vision and goals into actionable plans, who will relentlessly represent the voice of the customer into the company and who is passionate about delivering products and experiences that delight users.</div>
<div>
You are a disciplined and energetic leader striving to identify high value use cases in hospitals, and guiding product development teams to create products that meet them. You will set product strategy, prioritize features, build consensus, and coordinate product schedules with your design and engineering teammates. You are the consummate team player, adept at managing relationships, collaborating and influencing. You have a holistic view of the product, from product market fit, to ROI, to understanding the competitive space, to ensure that the products and solutions are easily installed and supported. You show an eagerness to learn and absorb new data points to continually grow our knowledge of the customer’s needs. You ruthlessly prioritize in order to stay ahead of the competition, while delivering quality products and solutions.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
</div>
<div><b>
The Work</b></div>
<ul>
<li> Serve as an advocate for the customer and "messenger of the market" to the internal teams (exec staff, engineering, operations, sales).</li>
<li> Develop, own and evangelize the product vision, roadmap and specific requirements, and ensure they are fully communicated to internal teams. Artifacts include business case, MRDs, PRDs, use cases.</li>
<li> Gather and analyze customer requirements and feedback through in-person engagement, as well as by leveraging sales and operations teams’ interactions via an efficient and organized process.</li>
<li> Use market, competitor and customer discovery research to develop products and enhancements that drive business outcomes for Savioke.</li>
<li> Lead interdisciplinary teams to execute complex projects, ranging from early development to product delivery.</li>
<li> Foster cross-functional collaboration with Hardware and Software Engineering, Field Ops, Finance, Production, Sales and Marketing, to ensure the roadmaps, priorities and requirements are aligned and achievable.</li>
<li> Manage programs and projects through a combination of Agile work practices and schedule-driven project management methods.</li>
<li> Create highly targeted and relevant communication platforms that address customers’ business objectives and technical needs while providing a clear and concrete ROI.</li>
<li> Participates in and supports sales engagements to evangelize product offerings.</li>
<li> Know why we are winning and losing in the market, and drive changes to our product and processes to increase competitiveness and stickiness.</li>
<li> Keep a finger on the pulse of our competition and develop strategies and tactics to sustain Savioke’s leadership position</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
</div>
<div><b>
Minimum Qualifications</b></div>
<ul>
<li> Bachelor’s degree in engineering or sciences required</li>
<li> 10+ years progressive product development &amp; product management leadership experience in healthcare companies, in areas such as medical devices, diagnostic equipment, or medical software and IT products.</li>
<li> Successful track record of designing, building and launching new products and scaling existing products in an agile environment.</li>
<li> Has an in-depth understanding of the hospital environment, including processes, performance and business metrics, decision making, regulatory constraints.</li>
<li> Proficient in complex, intelligent systems, have an understanding of machine design and analysis, mechatronics, and software.</li>
<li> Must have a strong understanding of configuration management in system design. Demonstrated ability in making system-level tradeoffs.</li>
<li> Must be a strong writer and public speaker, comfortable presenting to groups of senior executives.</li>
<li> Possesses both a strategic mindset and the ability to be detail oriented with experience cultivating ideas into marketable products.</li>
<li> Demonstrate strong customer focus with the ability to produce results in a fast paced and fluid business environment with competing priorities</li>
<li> Navigate effectively between being strategic and tactical as well as creative and disciplined; enjoys working at the big picture and detail levels.</li>
<li> Strong project management and leadership skills required. Able to lead multidisciplinary teams, Create and assign tasks, supervise senior and junior level engineers, plan and manage projects including tasks an budgets</li>
<li> Demonstrates strong interpersonal skills and builds strong relationships across functions.</li>
<li> Takes ownership and drives a sense of urgency; highly accountable.</li>
<li> Proven ability to influence cross-functional teams without formal authority.</li>
</ul>
