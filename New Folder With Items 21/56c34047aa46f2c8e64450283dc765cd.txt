<b>
Summary</b><b>:</b>
</div>
<div>The Design Engineer position is responsible for working closely with the prototype or model shop in creating of products by engineering solutions in the form of 3D computer models. PERMANENT, RETAIL, POP, DISPLAY EXPERIENCE REQUIRED. Design retail store fixtures; sheet metal, wood or plastic.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities include the following:</b></div>
<ul><li> Creates engineering drawings for the purpose of building prototypes and instruction sheets through the use of hand sketches and/or computer generated models.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Transition projects from concept to production using innovative techniques, materials, and processes.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Works closely with internal sales, design, estimating, marketing, model shop, production and project management to create prototypes representing the final production quality which can function as working engineering specifications.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Partner with clients, agency vendors, and others involved in the development and implementation of projects.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Discuss manufacturing ides, feasibility, timeliness, and costs of designer’s concept with internal stakeholders.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Create costing information and provides cost/manufacturing analysis.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Routinely gives presentations to clients and internal groups.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Researches and investigates competitive displays and solutions in order to improve on the final engineering solution.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Must be able to work the required number of hours to ensure successful project completion.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Some travel required to meet with prospective and current clients.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Will attend workshops for professional development or to gain knowledge on new materials or manufacturing techniques.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Required to periodically lead meetings and make presentations.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Responsible for the overall Safety and cleanliness of equipment and area.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Work in a safe and efficient manner in accordance with company policies and procedures.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Perform other duties as assigned.</li></ul>
