<h2>Director of Sales Operations</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Title</b>: Director, Sales Operations</p>
<p><b>Work Location: </b>Santa Ana, CA</p>
<p><b>Reports to</b>: VP Premises Sales</p>
<p><b>Who We Are: </b></p>
<p>Identiv, Inc. is a publically traded company listed on NASDAQ [INVE]. Our products, software, systems, and services address the markets for physical and logical access control and a wide range of RFID-enabled applications. Customers in the government, enterprise, consumer, education, healthcare, and transportation sectors rely on our access and identification solutions. Our mission is to secure the connected physical world: from perimeter to desktop access, and from the world of physical things to the Internet of Everything. Find out more at identiv.com.</p>
<p><b>Summary</b>:</p>
<p>Participate as part of the leadership team reporting to the VP Sales for the Premises Business Unit - personally responsible for leading the Sales Operations efforts with a team of 5 focused on Inside Sales, Sales Reporting and Analytics, and Demand Generation. The key priorities for this role include being a player/coach in driving our Corporate Sales Operations team to be a strategic advantage for Identiv, through developing the team and personally leading efforts to optimize the effectiveness of the company’s Sales organization through the development, implementation and maintenance of a range of sales operations functions including forecasting, deal and contracts management, pipeline management and outbound demand generation. The primary goal is to improve business function, sales efficiency and productivity and overall sales process effectiveness.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Drive improvement in the area of Sales Operations by collaborating with Sales Leadership and GoToMarket teams</li>
<li>Lead the Inside Sales team to provide a high level of customer and partner service, and drive the operational cadence for Sales.</li>
<li>Partner with Sales leadership to improve sales forecasting and accuracy. Direct the sales forecasting process</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Deliver key performance metrics, dashboards, and reports that help Sales focus on performance drivers and results</li>
<li>Manage inside sales team members to become SMEs on management information, questions and coordination point for the businesses outside sales team, channel partners and end users</li>
<li>Drive inside sales team efforts focusing on revenue impacting activities, SSA’s, outbound lead follow-up post marketing campaigns</li>
<li>Support the building and review of compensation planning</li>
<li>Identify and manage escalations and internal objections related to pricing and other commercial terms * Optimize sales processes and programs as it relates to “Quote to Cash”, Deal review, forecasting. Sales Pipeline &amp; Forecast Management to drive forecast accuracy and predictability against bookings targets</li>
<li>Manage sales technology strategy &amp; deployments. Develop strategies to optimize SFPC as a tool and data quality improvement</li>
<li>Collaborate with Marketing to ensure proper lead management</li>
<li>Proactively work with Sales to optimize the deal structure for opportunities in accordance with accounting guidelines while optimizing the Company’s revenue objectives</li>
<li>Identify and implement operation-related process improvements</li>
<li>Lead reoccurring and ad-hoc analysis to enable well informed executive decision making</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications/Attributes: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>A self-starter with a strong work ethic and ability to navigate and thrive in fast-changing work environments.</li>
<li>8-10 years experience in sales operational roles, or related experience in management consulting. Experience working with sales and account management teams is a plus.</li>
<li>Strong problem-solving skills. Ability to break down complex problems into the key levers that need to be fixed.</li>
<li>Significant experience using a CRM system, preferably SFDC (Salesforce.com)</li>
<li>Superior analytical and problem-solving skills</li>
<li>Proven project management skills, with success owning complicated cross-functional programs and projects to timelines and budgets.</li>
<li>Collaborative approach to getting things done; ability to work cross-functionally and align stakeholders around common goals. Facilitation and peer leadership skills</li>
<li>Executive-level presence with excellent written and verbal communication skills at all levels in the organization.</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Preferred Qualifications: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Understanding of revenue guidance as it relates to bundling multiple elements, future functionality and other complex accounting requirements</li>
<li>Experience with contract negotiation, revenue guidelines</li>
<li>Understanding of Perpetual, SaaS, subscription, and other revenue models</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Style / Cultural Mindset: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Focus and execution … understand, commit and deliver the results</li>
<li>Data driven</li>
<li>Decisive action … including failing productively and always learning</li>
<li>Valuing the team, roles and clear responsibility … trust and respect</li>
<li>Open communication</li>
</ul>
