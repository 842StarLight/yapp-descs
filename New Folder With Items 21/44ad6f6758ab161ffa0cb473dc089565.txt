<h2>Event Services IT Technician</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>EVENT SERVICES TECHNICIAN</b></p>
<p>Have you ever been to a concert and wondered what happens backstage? Are you passionate about IT and want the opportunity to start your career, learn and grow? Do you want to be a part of a team that uses IT expertise to help make concerts and other events happen?</p>
<p>Our company is expanding through a growing reputation for quality and customer satisfaction. After going through a change in our culture, we’re ready to add new staff while raising the expectations of our existing team. This is an <b>ENTRY LEVEL POSITION</b>for our Event Services IT Department.</p>
<p>You will help our customers with their service requests, assist venues with their IT systems before and during events, and help your team keep a high customer satisfaction rate through great customer service and quality technical work.</p>
<p><b>SUCCESS FACTORS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Answer calls by second ring during business hours</li>
<li>Respond to after-hour requests within 30 minutes</li>
<li>Assist our venue customers with sales process, making sure their customer needs are met</li>
<li>Maintain 100% punctuality for all service calls</li>
<li>Maintain at least 80% of working hours as billable</li>
<li>Maintain customer satisfaction score above 90%</li>
<li>Keep customers’ documentation up to date</li>
</ul>
<p><b>OBSTACLES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Event Services is a very fast-paced industry. This job requires you to have high availability, and work needs to be completed on time, which can add to pressure.</li>
<li>You may be required to work after-hours, weekends or holidays according to event schedules.</li>
<li>Our customers use multiple different third-party applications and we are the liaison between the customer and vendor.</li>
<li>We are always implementing cutting edge technology for our customers and you will need to continuously learn and stay up to date.</li>
<li>We have very aggressive SLAs to ensure our customers are getting the best customer service. Meeting these SLAs is challenging but necessary.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS, EXPERIENCE NEEDED</b></p>
<p>To be successful in this position you will be a quick learner, self-driven, disciplined and thoughtful, natural with follow-up and follow-through, detail-oriented and out of the office 90%-100% of the time, traveling between facilities. Most importantly, you have to keep a positive and proactive attitude, showing our customers you have a genuine interest in helping them succeed.</p>
<p>The ideal candidate for this position must be able to stay organized, multi-task and effectively manage their time. We are looking for an individual who has a natural skillset of strong presentation and the ability to relate to individuals within minutes of a conversation and build credibility with individuals at all levels in an organization.</p>
<p><b>REQUIRED</b></p>
<p>This description is intended to provide only basic guidelines for meeting job requirements. Responsibilities, skills and working conditions may change as needed.</p>
<ul>
<li>Network basics – you must know how basic switching and routing technologies. The job will require you to dynamically change VLAN assignments on switch ports, maintain and switch analog phone lines, run cables from switches to terminals on demand and troubleshoot network connectivity. You also need to have knowledge on DHCP, DNS and WiFi basic troubleshooting.</li>
<li>Everyday IT work – you must be comfortable with installing, configuring and troubleshooting Windows workstations (local or remote), troubleshoot basic hardware issues and network connection problems. You must know how to configure device drivers (especially printers), remove malware and keep workstations at optimal performance levels.</li>
<li>Possession of a valid driver’s license with valid automobile insurance</li>
<li>Ability to lift or carry 30-40lbs</li>
<li>Regular sitting and standing associated with travel in accordance with a normal outside IT environment.</li>
<li>Manual dexterity needed for using a cellphone, tablet, and computer keyboard.</li>
<li>Ability to travel to customer locations throughout the workday. May require some night and weekend work.</li>
<li>Perform other duties as requested by management.</li>
<li>Attend meetings/training as required by management.</li>
<li>Enforce compliance with company policies and procedures; forward all non-compliance issues to HR Manager.</li>
</ul>
