<h2>Reservations Agent - Evenings and Weekends</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>About the Company</b></p>
<p>The Fountain Spa, voted best spa in Bergen County for 12 consecutive years, is an upscale day spa servicing two locations in northern New Jersey. The Fountain Spa offers a variety of services including facials, massages and body treatments, along with a full menu of salon services.</p>
<p><b>About the Position</b></p>
<p>The Fountain Spa is looking for energetic, friendly, articulate Reservation Agents to book spa appointments via telephone and internet for both of our locations. This is a permanent part time position and will be based in our central reservations department located in Ramsey, NJ. The Fountain is open 7 days a week and many evenings until 9pm, therefore shifting may vary.</p>
<p>Basic computer skills and stellar customer service experience are a must; sales experience is a plus. Applicants must have reliable transportation and be available to work nights, weekends and holidays. Experience with Millennium software is a plus.</p>
<p>Job Type: Part-time</p>
