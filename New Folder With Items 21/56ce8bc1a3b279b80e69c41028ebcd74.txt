<h2>SAMD Logistics Management Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>Salary (Range Based on Experience Level): Negotiable<br>
<br>
Position Description:<br>
1. <b>PRIMARY DUTIES. </b>Serve as a Logistics Management Specialist responsible for the overall development, coordination and control of assigned Apache Foreign Military Sales (FMS) programs.
<div>
<br>
a. Plan, coordinate, and direct the requirements determination process for assigned international logistic program requests.
<p>b. Determine the country's unique requirements for equipment, software, system interfaces, technical assistance, OEM training, and documentation based on knowledge of system configuration and technical characteristics.
</p>
<p>
c. Participate in planning phases at international logistics program presentations and Program Management Reviews to foreign government representatives in CONUS and OCONUS.
</p>
<p>
d. Develop and coordinate long-range plans for the conduct of the international logistics programs and ensure visibility within the project organizational elements.</p>
</div>
<br>
2. <b>Specialized Experience. </b>One year of specialized experience which includes:
<div>
<br>
a. Analyzing and evaluating policies and procedures that are outlined in the Security Assistance Management Manual (SAMM);
<p>b. Experience utilizing programs such as Defense Security Assistance Management System (DSAMS), Common Operating Picture (COP)/Case Execution Analyzer (CEA), Integrated Major Pricing Analysis and Reporting Tool (IMPART), E-Collaboration (E-Collab), Standard Operation and Maintenance Army Research &amp; Development System (SOMARDS) and the General Fund Business System (GFEBS);<br>
</p>
</div>
<br>
3. <b>Attributes.</b>
<div>
<br>
a. Strong background in DSAMS Case Writing.
<p>b. Strong Financial Management skills.
</p>
<p>
c. Able to work independently and/or as part of a team.
</p>
<p>
d. Able to travel CONUS and OCONUS.</p>
</div>
<br>
