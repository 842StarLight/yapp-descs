<div><b>
JOB DESCRIPTION</b></div>
<div>
The Client Implementation Consultant will be on the frontlines in driving adoption after a new client joins FieldEdge. S/he is required to act as the single point of contact for new clients in representing the entire FieldEdge team.</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Project manage implementation processes that are often complex</li>
<li>
Use tact and diplomacy to set proper timelines and expectations throughout the implementation process, including both verbal and written communication</li>
<li>
Manage multiple client implementations simultaneously, and prioritize effectively to ensure on-time Go Lives that meet all of the client’s specifications</li>
<li>
Coordinate internally between the Sales, Data, and Product teams to provide customers with a single point of contact</li>
<li>
Exercise attention to detail and technical proficiency to ensure proper migration of customer data from competitor platforms</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
