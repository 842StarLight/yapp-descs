<div>
Position Summary</div>
<div><div>
<div>
<p>Through monitoring, auditing, evaluation and coaching, the contact center QA &amp; Customer Experience Specialist will work with contact center leaders to ensure that all representatives offer consistently outstanding service in all customer interactions. It is the responsibility of the QA/CX Specialist to help plan, coordinate and direct quality assurance activities designed to promote outstanding, world-class customer service. The QA/CX Specialist will also assist with monitoring and reviewing CSAT and NPS data, helping to chart and improve the customer experience.</p>
<p><b>Duties and </b><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>Duties and responsibilities include the following. Other duties may be assigned to meet business needs.</p>
<ul>
<li>As directed by the QA/CX Manager, executes and administers the contact center’s quality assurance (QA) program.</li>
<li>Reviews and scores agent’s interactions with customers (using QA form) to ensure correct processes are followed and accurate information is provided professionally, courteously and consistently.</li>
<li>Works with leaders to ensure that appropriate coaching and feedback is provided to each agent.</li>
<li>As directed, leads and facilitates effective calibration sessions.</li>
<li>Supports, documents and maintains quality policies, procedures, and monitoring forms.</li>
<li>Tracks and maintains individual and team QA scores and trends.</li>
<li>Tracks and maintains individual and team CSAT/NPS scores and trends.</li>
<li>Works with QA/CX Manager to ensure QA program and initiatives are aligned with the goals of the overall contact center and organization generally.</li>
<li>Actively and regularly reviews customer feedback from surveys and other feedback channels, ensuring that customers receive an appropriate response/resolution, and that any needed coaching is provided to the agent.</li>
<li>Ensures that effective inter-department communication loops are in place and that customer feedback is regularly and consistently presented to all impacted departments across the organization.</li>
<li>May act as liaison between the contact center and other departments within the organization to ensure customer satisfaction levels are consistently met.</li>
<li>Maintains a comprehensive working knowledge of policies, procedures, products and communicates the same to all team members.</li>
<li>Partners with training team and other contact center leaders to develop innovative quality training initiatives.</li>
<li>Documents and shares necessary information with leadership to support effective performance reviews.</li>
<li>Acts as a backup to contact center leaders.</li>
<li>Helps with escalated calls as needed.</li>
<li>In periods of excessive call volume, may assist with incoming calls, emails, chats, etc.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree preferred (or equivalent experience).</li>
<li>3-5 years of quality assurance experience (in a customer service contact center setting).</li>
<li>Previous coaching and training experience.</li>
<li>Deep understanding of contact center QA best practices (with call/chat/email/text monitoring, calibrations, scoring).</li>
<li>Proven success increasing individual and team performance within a contact center setting.</li>
<li>Experience with the following tools/applications highly preferred:
<ul>
<li>NICE QM (Nice quality monitoring application)</li>
<li>inContact</li>
<li>Zendesk</li>
<li>NetSuite</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Experience auditing a sales team preferred.</li>
<li>Ability to quickly learn new software and applications.</li>
<li>Excellent oral and written communication skills and the ability to effectively communicate with a diverse audience and at every level of the organization.</li>
<li>Ability to effectively lead and facilitate meaningful calibration and coaching sessions.</li>
<li>Ability to effectively perform in a fast paced, deadline oriented work environment.</li>
<li>Lead by example with exceptional interpersonal skills and brand representation.</li>
<li>High computer literacy. Proficient in Word, Excel, Power Point.</li>
<li>Ability to work independently on projects and as a member of a team.</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task and organize and prioritize workload.</li>
</ul>
