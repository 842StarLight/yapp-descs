<p>
The Business Development Representative is responsible for prospecting, qualifying, and generating new sales opportunities to help support the Federal, State and Local Sales teams. They will partner with Sales Executives to set up sales meetings and opportunities with key decision makers in the government. The majority of these opportunities will be generated through qualifying inbound marketing leads via phone and email.</p>
<p>
This role is for a highly-motivated, ambitious, and results driven individual. Through strategic partnerships with various members of the Sales and Marketing Teams, Business Development Representatives will be exposed to a variety of roles and experiences that will develop the skills and business acumen needed to become a top-tier sales professional and build a successful career.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
What You Get To Do:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop new business via phone, email and social media to educate the prospect, introduce our solution, and identify appropriate buyers within the target market</li>
<li>Follow up on inbound marketing leads through a strategic touch plan to generate additional interest in Granicus and qualify for a potential opportunity</li>
<li>Conduct research to identify potential opportunities and understand challenges facing government agencies today</li>
<li>Conduct initial qualification and determine prospect's current environment, pain points, challenges, and potential projects to determine how Granicus' solutions will speak to their needs</li>
<li>Identify key buying influencers within these prospects to determine budget and timeline</li>
<li>Build and cultivate prospect relationships by initiating communications and conducting follow-up communications in order to move opportunities through the sales funnel</li>
<li>Work with the key sales drivers to develop and grow the sales pipeline to consistently meet quarterly revenue goals</li>
<li>Use Salesforce to track all sales activities properly and manage contact/lead data for accuracy</li>
<li>Analyze sales pipeline reports and dashboards</li>
</ul>

</ul>
