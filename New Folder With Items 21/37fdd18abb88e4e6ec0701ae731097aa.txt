<h2>Sr. Business Analyst-MES (Manufacturing Execution Systems)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Sealed Air Corporation is a knowledge-based company focused on packaging solutions that help our customers achieve their sustainability goals in the face of today’s biggest social and environmental challenges. Our portfolio of widely recognized brands, including Cryovac® brand food packaging solutions and Bubble Wrap® brand cushioning, enable a safer and less wasteful food supply chain and protect valuable goods shipped around the world. Sealed Air generated $4.5 billion in sales in 2017 and has approximately 15,000 employees who serve customers in 122 countries. To learn more, visit www.sealedair.com.</div>
<div>
<div>
<div>
<b>
Job Description</b>
</div>
<div><ul>
<li>The IT organization of Sealed Air Corporation is responsible for the life cycle planning of business applications through selection, operation, refresh, migration, optimization, retirement and disposal.</li>
<li>The <b>Sr. Business Analyst </b>builds/configures, maintains/enhances, and eventually decommissions relevant manufacturing applications through that life cycle. This position requires a deep technical and comprehensive business process understanding of manufacturing environments with a focus on Manufacturing Execution Systems.</li>
<li>The position reports directly to the Executive Director of Supply Chain and will be based at our headquarters in Charlotte, NC.</li>
</ul></div>
</div>
<div>
<div>
<b>Responsibilites</b>
</div>
<div><ul>
<li>Work with Executive Director of Supply Chain and other IT leaders to understand and support the IT and Business strategies and offer innovative suggestions to improve processes.</li>
<li>Form a strong bond with the Global Supply Chain Business Partnership group within IT and with the supply chain business leaders and key users in order to facilitate idea generation and delivery of solutions.</li>
<li>Develop and enhance methodologies and practices for the application lifecycle management in line with best practice and practical experience of continuous improvement.</li>
<li>Ensure that applications processes (including those of external service providers) are conducted in line with corporate social responsibility, environmental and technical policies and applicable standards and legislation.</li>
<li>Partner with our managed services provider to insure their employees are properly trained in order to support our business. Be responsible for making sure that proper documentation exists to support our business processes.</li>
<li>Oversee the successful delivery of key projects, such as the implementation of digital manufacturing initiatives.</li>
</ul></div>
</div>
<div>
