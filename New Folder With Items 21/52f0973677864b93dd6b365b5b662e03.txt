<h2>Patient Account Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Our associates enjoy a lucrative compensation plan that includes a guaranteed pay rate, monthly commission incentives, extensive benefit options and retirement plan.</p>
<p>Job Description</p>
<p>FUNCTIONS</p>
<ul>
<li>Patient contact through automated dialer system</li>
<li>Maintain appropriate daily call volumes and meeting collection goals on a monthly basis while properly documenting account activity</li>
<li>Analyze patient accounts and bringing to completion as soon as possible to lower client accounts receivables</li>
<li>Requires the ability to negotiate account resolution through firm but professional talk-offs with patients in accordance with State and Federal regulatory guidelines and company requirements</li>
</ul>
<p>QUALIFICATIONS</p>
<ul>
<li>Strong data entry skills and experience working within Windows and web-based applications</li>
<li>Communication, organizational and prioritization skills</li>
<li>Effectively interact with peers and other department personnel to make a positive impact</li>
<li>Utilization of active listening skills and develop a rapport with customers</li>
<li>Ability to work independently</li>
<li>Strict adherence to company's Business Conduct Statement and Code of Ethics maintaining the highest level of workplace behavior</li>
<li>Computer literate - Microsoft Office software a plus.</li>
<li>Candidates must pass a pre-employment criminal background search</li>
<li>Excellent oral and written communication skills</li>
<li>Knowledge of Predictive Dialer operations helpful</li>
<li>Ability to handle multiple tasks and fast paced environment</li>
<li>Excellent people skills with strong leadership skills</li>
<li>Minimum typing speed - 40 wpm</li>
<li>Ability to analyze internal processes and results</li>
<li>Familiarity with FDCPA regulations and enforcing compliance helpful</li>
<li>Organized and detail oriented</li>
</ul>
