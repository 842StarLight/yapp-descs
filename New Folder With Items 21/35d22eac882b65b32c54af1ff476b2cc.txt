<h2>Sr. Associate, Digital Investment</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Job Description<br>
<br>
<p>The Digital Investment Senior Associate will focus on buying and activation of digital media plans for our client, a leading legacy CPG, to drive sales. The ideal candidate for this position will have digital media buying and project management experience. The Senior Associate is responsible for managing all aspects of a fast paced, high volume digital investment desk that will work cross-functionally with various internal and external teams. This team member must feel comfortable with a high level of direct client contact, therefore, must have strong written and verbal communication skills.</p>
<p>
The ideal candidate for this position is a proactive, creative thinker, who loves data and has an ongoing curiosity of the ever-changing media landscape and keeping ahead of digital marketing trends. Successful candidates will be collaborative, communicative, and eager to learn. This team promotes an environment that fosters professional growth, mentorship, and innovation. This is an energetic, collaborative team that partners with an analytics-driven and media savvy client to provide guidance as they continue to grow and evolve their brand.</p>
<p><b>
Specific Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Assist with campaign set up and execution including order letters, specs, creative rotation and billing documentation</li>
<li>
Ability to handle multiple campaigns (10-15) at one time</li>
<li>
Daily client communication (email, calls, and multiple status per week)</li>
<li>
Understanding of digital investment practices, marketplace, and technologies</li>
<li>
Create and communicate RFP needs based on client/retailer program briefing</li>
<li>
Evaluate proposals based on primary, secondary, and tertiary KPIs</li>
<li>
Assist with tactical recommendation building</li>
<li>
Create and distribute client-facing reports</li>
<li>
Ensure campaign performance delivery weekly w/ vendors</li>
<li>
Assist with compiling and troubleshooting monthly draft billing</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Desired Skills and Experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Strong digital investment knowledge</li>
<li>
Strong project management skill due to volume of campaigns in flux at various stages at any point in time</li>
<li>
Analytic/attribution experience or familiarity due to measurement requirements on every campaign</li>
<li>
Ability to think clearly under pressure and instill calm confidence in group</li>
<li>
Self-starter and ability to work independently</li>
<li>
Technical proficiency across Microsoft Office Suite with heavy focus on Excel</li>
</ul>
</div>
