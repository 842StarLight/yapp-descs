<h2>Sales Executive - NY</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Job Description</b><br>
<p>We currently have a need for an Sales Executive within our USA market. We are looking for an individual who is passionate about a career in sales. This is a hunter position where the selected candidate will be focused on driving business objectives to obtain commercial targets within a respective territory. While working in a fast-paced / entrepreneurial environment, you will be challenged with proactively searching for leads and generating sales to help continue to develop this fast-growing business unit. This is a team-oriented group that promotes innovative thought and idea sharing.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Major Responsibilities / Accountabilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Directly responsible for finding and building opportunities within commercial accounts in a defined territory (geo or named).</li>
<li>
Utilize complex sales skills to execute and educate prospects/clients to logical decision to engage in partnership with TR and buy software and applicable services in said defined territory.</li>
<li>
Manage sales cycle from identifying prospects, working with numerous internal teams to coordinate appropriate resources, all the way through closing the deal.</li>
<li>
Manage relationships of customers post initial sale for upsell and renewals.</li>
<li>
Maintain regular cadence with management and update all activities in our CRM tool.</li>
<li>
Meet/exceed quarterly and annual quota targets.</li>
<li>
Travel expectations – 25% to 35%</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Essential Skills &amp; Experience Required:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>6+ years’ experience selling major enterprise software plus any proven success with small start-up companies.</li>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree or equivalent a must</li>
<li>
Selling into large commercial entities</li>
<li>
Command knowledge of the enterprise software industry</li>
<li>
Record of sustained and significant quota achievement.</li>
<li>
Experience working in a fast paced, team-oriented, collaborative environment.</li>
<li>
Strong experience with email, SFDC, MS excel, word, PowerPoint, webex and other communication platforms, expense management and travel systems.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Desired Skills:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Engage with a consultative sales approach.</li>
<li>
Must be articulate with strong writing and presentation skills.</li>
<li>
Proven analytical and problem-solving abilities.</li>
<li>
Able to work under stress and handle multiple critical priorities.</li>
<li>
Very strong work ethic with “can do, roll up sleeves to figure it out” attitude.</li>
<li>
Ability to leverage supporting team of Sales Consultants, Lead Generation, Professional Services, Product Development and Marketing.</li>
</ul>
