<p>Symphony SummitAI is a portfolio company of SymphonyAI. SymphonyAI is a group of companies that provide leading AI-centric solutions for transforming the business enterprise by driving revenue growth and operational excellence, for the retail, CPG, healthcare and industrial verticals, and for finance, IT and other key enterprise horizontal functions.<br>
Symphony SummitAI’s flagship product, SummitAI, is an advanced, modular solution certified by ITIL and Pink Verify. SummitAI unifies service management, asset management, and operations management in a single, easy-to-use offering. Enterprises and service providers use SummitAI to dramatically reduce cost and complexity of their IT management while improving efficiency, productivity, predictability, and control. Leading enterprises across business, financial services, insurance, healthcare, IT and consulting, automobile, manufacturing, aviation and education verticals are delivering better user experience while lowering the cost of managing their IT using Symphony SummitAI. Website: www.symphonysummit.com<br>
Symphony SummitAI is seeking a Customer Success Manager (CSM) responsible for developing customer relationships that promote retention and loyalty. He/She needs to work closely with customers to ensure that they are satisfied with the SummitAI Products, the services they receive and to improve upon areas of dissatisfaction. The CSM will work closely with all the internal SummitAI teams – Sales, Delivery and Development to ensure that the customer’s concerns are addressed</p>

<b>
Job description</b><br>
<ul>
<li>CSM must build an in-depth knowledge of SummitAI product suite.</li>
<li>They will work closely with the customers to understand the known and unknown business needs of the organization. They should be able to identify the optimal deployment of the SummitAI product that will address these business needs.</li>
<li>The Customer Success Manager will focus on educating our customers on the flexibility and capabilities of SummitAI Software so that the customers are encouraged to continue using SummitAI products and services.</li>
<li>He/She will work closely with the delivery teams (Implementation, Support, professional Services), development team, Sales and pre-sales teams to understand the customer requirements and address the same.</li>
<li>He/She will engage with the senior management at the Customer organization to chart out long term engagements</li>
</ul>
Skills Needed<br>
<ul>
<li>Customer Success Managers should possess excellent interpersonal skills and be strong leaders.</li>
<li>They need to be highly organized and have experience directing others.</li>
<li>They should be technically competent with deep domain knowledge in ITSM/ITOM</li>
<li>Strong presenters and exceptional communicators</li>
<li>ITIL Expert Certified</li>
</ul>
Qualification:<br>
<ul><li>Bachelor’s degree in computer science or a related field</li></ul>
