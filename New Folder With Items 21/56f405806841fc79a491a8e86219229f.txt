<p><b>
Summary</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
The Print Production Coordinator is a full-time position and a critical team member responsible for providing support for print production team members. This individual is also responsible for supporting our clients and vendors by handling print, kitting and fulfillment, direct mail and promotional item projects, order inquiries, overseeing various campaign packages, list data programs and marketing program campaigns. Strong understanding of the print and mail process, communication skills and ability to multitask.</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Challenges</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li><p><b>
Manage order inquiries through Zendesk and work cohesively with customer service and online vendor(s).</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
Process custom print, promotional item and direct mail orders .</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
Perform project, campaign package and data management programs for clients through various platforms.</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
Manage inventory transactions in Zendesk and Sproutloud application.</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
Monitor all online order delivery statuses with Fedex and other logistical partners.</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
Manage, facilitate and list procedures including purchases and automated list format processes.</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
Provide support for Vehicle Graphic vendors on order inquiries and offline installation charges.</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
Effectively use ZenDesk, Sproutloud App, Excel and Harvest software.</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
Learn Basic knowledge of P3 Purchase Order and Invoicing Software</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
Learn all SproutLoud's applications and software.Learn and define cost-effective production workflows.</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
Setup Price Model requests that come through zendesk ticket requests.</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
Facilitate communications between Vendor contact(s) and Client Services Team through Zendesk tickets pertaining to online production order inquiries.</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
Manage oneself and represent SproutLoud professionally.</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
Provide and document Vendor KPI feedback to Director of Production.</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
Assist Director of Production with Network Programs.</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
Place Online/Offline orders and manage vendor portal</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
Open Offline Purchase Orders through P3 (Job Quoting)</b></p></li>
</ul>
<p></p>

<p></p>
<ul>
<li><p><b>
5 - 7 years of print production experience.</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
Working knowledge of traditional offset sheet-fed and web printing as well as on demand digital printing.</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
Must be fluent in English which includes the ability to read and write.</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
Must display a customer service focused demeanor.</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
Strong communication and organization skills</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
Strong data management skills.</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
Ability to troubleshoot and problem solve independently and quickly.</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
Detail oriented.</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
Strong ability to manage many tasks at a time.</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
Ability to learn new technologies, processes, software and computers quickly.</b></p></li>

Must learn and be familiar with SproutLoud's production workflows, vendor capabilities, Sproutloud Vehicle Wraps portal and manufacturing process.</b></p></li>
