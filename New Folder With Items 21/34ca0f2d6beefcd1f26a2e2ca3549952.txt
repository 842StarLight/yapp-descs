<p>Trimmer Job Description
</p>
<p>Title:Trimmer
</p>
<p>Reports to:DTCP Manager
</p>
<p>Department:Dry, Trim, Cure, and Packaging (DTCP)/Cultivation
</p>
<p>Required Permits/Licensing/Registration: Marijuana Establishment Agent Card
</p>
<p>FLSA Status:Non-Exempt
</p>
<p>Supervisory Responsibilities: N/A<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Overview:
</p>
<p>Following policies set forth by the Company, assist in the overall operation of the Dry, Trim, Cure, and Packaging Department through uniform, sanitary, and compliant manicuring of Deep Roots Harvest flower..
</p>
<p>Essential Functions:
</p>
<ul>
<li><p>Support the operations of the DTCP Department by safe and efficient execution of operational strategies and processes.
 </p></li>
<li><p>Fulfill obligations to the satisfaction of supervisory staffs’ short and long-term performance goals.
</p></li>
<li><p>Thorough communication of any obstacles which limit performance and any conditions observed that may affect the success of the department.
</p></li>
<li><p>Maintain strict and accurate daily/monthly logs &amp; reports for tracking purposes in order to remain compliant with state regulations and satisfy the Company’s reporting needs.
</p></li>
<li><p>Communicate daily output including processed plant counts, trim weights, and produced waste totals.
</p></li>
<li><p>Completion of daily directives &amp; goals
</p></li>
<li><p>The efficient and sanitary breakdown of cannabis plants in order to separate unusable waste from usable product
</p></li>
<li><p>Ensure stems and fan leaves are added to waste weights for disposal and tracking by managers
</p></li>
<li><p>Manicure dried cannabis flowers to satisfy department standards of appearance and overall quality
</p></li>
<li><p>Must be able to adapt to varying strains, styles, and types of marijuana being trimmed
</p></li>
<li><p>Adjust techniques to maintain a steady pace without sacrificing the quality of output
</p></li>
<li><p>Exhibit ability to take constructive criticism from management to maintain quality standards and production goals
</p></li>
<li><p>Demonstrate a clear comprehension of quality control guidelines, checkpoints, categories, and responsibilities.
</p></li>
<li><p>Supply Storage &amp; Organization
</p></li>
<li><p>Assist in the sanitization, organization, and proper storage of all tools
</p></li>
<li><p>Assist in the organization and sanitary storage of all input materials.
</p></li>
<li><p>Ensure all machinery, DTCP Room &amp; surrounding area is properly sanitized &amp; tools are stored upon completion of daily tasks.
</p></li>
<li><p>Assist in the execution, completion, and maintenance of all cleaning logs
</p></li>
<li><p>Completion and review of all matters related to compliance with State and Federal regulations.
</p></li>
<li><p>Fulfill assigned responsibilities in line with procedures set forth by all department Standard Operating Procedures and subsequent training.
</p></li>
<li><p>Other duties as assigned
</p></li>
</ul>
