<p>Job Posting Title:
</p>Dairy Technical Sales Consultant- Kansas/Colorado
<p></p>
<p>Job Description:
</p>
<p>We’re hiring a Technical Sales Consultant to join our Enterprise Dairy Sales Team! This position will focus in the high plains region (Kansas, Colorado) with a primary focus in young animals and feedlot heifers. In this role, you will be responsible for consulting with large dairy producers and calf ranch owners to improve production capabilities and profits through sales of feed components &amp; programs. Your responsibilities include:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Developing strategies to increase the market share and impact of proprietary technology and products.
</li>
<li>Monitor and document production and profitability improvements for producers implementing program and product recommendations
</li>
<li>Maintain a high level of proficiency and competency in nutrition, production management, and sales
</li>
<li>Provide strategic and tactical planning skills necessary to provide solutions-based selling of value-add technologies
</li>
<li>Ability to work independently while contributing and participating in a team-oriented environment
</li>
<li>Build relationships with dairy influencers (vets/independent nutritionists)
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Experience/Education:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Master’s, PhD or DVM required; preferably nutrition focused
</li>
<li>5+ years’ experience in dairy production-young animal growth to adult cow performance desired
</li>
<li>Priority given to candidates with previous sales/consulting experience with large influential dairies
</li>
<li>Requires daily travel in an assigned geography with up to 40%-70% overnight travel depending upon candidate location
</li>
<li>Demonstrated experience using a dairy management/records system
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Required Competencies: </b>Action oriented, drive for results, customer focused, team orientation, functional/ technical skills, interpersonal skills, self-development, time management and problem solving/decision making.
</p>
