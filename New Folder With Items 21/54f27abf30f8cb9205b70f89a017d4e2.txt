<h2>Customer Success Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Customer Success Associate will provide proactive support to new and existing OnShift customers. In this role, the CSA will not only provide proactive outreach, but will also communicate best practices and provide additional training to ensure customer's success.<br>
<br>
<b>WHO WE ARE</b><br>
Software is one of the most interesting industries to work in today and healthcare is the place to be in Cleveland in 2019- combining those, as we do, is what makes our business so compelling. We’re growing fast, we love what we do and we do it with passion.<br>
<br>
OnShift is a B2B Software Company headquartered in the theater district of Cleveland, Ohio. We provide cloud-based human capital management software designed for long-term care and senior living. You’d recognize our clients as retirement communities, nursing homes, assisted living communities and others in the post-acute healthcare spectrum. OnShift has secured a range of awards highlighting our technology innovation and business growth. Notable awards include: 2014 OHTec Tech Company of the Year, 2015 OHTec Best Software Product finalist, 2016 OHTec Best Healthcare Tech Product Finalist, 2016 Deloitte Technology Fast 500 winner, and 2016 Weatherhead 100 winner.<br>
<br>
As an organization, we value people who are dedicated and innovative and we reward them with challenging work, competitive pay, solid benefits, equity participation, career growth, and personal development.</p>
<p><b>
A DAY IN THE LIFE</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Work to ensure that all customers are fully utilizing the system and achieving success</li>
<li>Provide clear guidance in managing and monitoring customers to ensure that any issues are being addressed and resolved in a timely and effective manner</li>
<li>Use internal reporting tools to ensure accurate representation of customer system utilization</li>
<li>Follow department processes and procedures to ensure consistent account management and documentation</li>
<li>Work closely with engineering and product management to ensure customer enhancements and defects are resolved in a timely manner</li>
<li>Perform onsite or webinar based training on the OnShift suite products to clients, when needed</li>
<li>Communicate scheduling and labor management best practices to customers</li>
<li>Understand and effectively communicate long term care industry terms and concepts to customers</li>
<li>Also responsible for continued learning in regards to the long-term care industry</li>
</ul>
<p><b>DO YOU HAVE WHAT IT TAKES?</b></p>
<ul>
<li>1 – 2 years’ experience in a customer service, account management, or sales setting</li>
<li>Demonstrated success working directly with customers in a customer support type role</li>
<li>Must be able to work independently and proactively to resolve customer issues by researching, determining an alternate solution or escalating following defined processes</li>
<li>Basic technical aptitude with more advanced Microsoft Office skills (Excel, PowerPoint, and Word)</li>
<li>Ability to effectively manage multiple tasks</li>
<li>Organized and detail oriented</li>
<li>A team player that is able to take direction and ownership of individual work</li>
<li>Must be able to demonstrate clear communication skills, patience and a goal oriented focus while assisting customers on support calls</li>
<li>Ability to travel up to 10% – 15%</li>
</ul>
