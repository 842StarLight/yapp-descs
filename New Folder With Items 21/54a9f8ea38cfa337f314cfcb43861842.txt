<p><b>Job Description</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>Join Reynolds Consumer Products…a world of opportunities! At Reynolds Consumer Products we are passionate about achieving results and have fun winning as a team! We provide amazing job opportunities for growth with competitive salaries and benefits in an exciting, dynamic, fast-paced, and fun workplace environment. Are you looking to build a strong career? Then we have an opportunity for you! We currently have an opportunity for a Customer Account Representative at our fast-paced Customer Service Office in Canandaigua, NY.</p>
<p><b>Your Role:</b></p>
<p>As a Customer Account Representative, you will be responsible for assuring that customer orders are processed correctly and in a timely manner. You will have the opportunity to:</p>
<ul>
<li>Identify process problems and seek solutions</li>
<li>Collaborate with other teams and resolve internal conflicts</li>
<li>Train and mentor new employees</li>
<li>Participate in, and even lead, short-term projects</li>
</ul>
<p>As a passionately curious individual, you will play a valuable part in our company by investigating customer concerns and assuring that our customers are always happy and satisfied.</p>
<p><b>You will love it here if…</b></p>
<ul>
<li>You are a natural people-person</li>
<li>You are searching for an opportunity in which you have room to grow</li>
<li>You pay great attention to details</li>
<li>You enjoy a challenge in solving problems</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>We need you to have:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
BA/BS degree</li>
<li>
Knowledge of Transportation, Production Planning, Warehousing, and Credit</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Icing on the cake:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
2+ years of Customer Service experience</li>
<li>
Computer software skills</li>
</ul>
