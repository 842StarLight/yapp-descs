<h2>Help Desk Agent</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>About the Role</b></p>
<p>
Club OS is expanding our Customer Support department and looking to bring on a customer-focused support agent to our Help Desk team. We’re looking for individuals who can be a critical part of a high-performing team of problem solvers, while being committed to delivering a world-class support experience for our amazing customers. This position will be located in our Austin, TX office where you’ll have the opportunity to work alongside some very talented people.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
About Us</b></p>
<p>
Club OS is a brand and platform designed to support health and wellness businesses in streamlining their operations and engaging their customers. Our products provide sales &amp; marketing automation, customer relationship management, scheduling and payment services for health clubs, gyms and studios. We are backed by one of the largest private equity firms in the country and are looking for talented, energetic, and motivated people to join our team and help us grow.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Own customer issues from identification to resolution and maximize one-touch resolutions</li>
<li>Efficiently and effectively gather information for all reported issues, perform triage, and perform root cause analysis</li>
<li>Engage with customers seeking technical support, training/education, and contract/billing issues via a multi-channel toolset (e.g. email, chat, phone).</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Experience/Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 2 years experience navigating and resolving various customer inquiries, preferably in a SaaS environment</li>
<li>Experience supporting customers via phone and email</li>
<li>Passion for customer service and ownership of the customer experience including comprehensive issue resolution</li>
<li>Able to effectively tailor communication and style to various audiences</li>
<li>Able to work independently in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>Thrives on a team where expertise is shared and feedback is welcomed</li>
<li>Experience with ticketing systems (e.g. Zendesk, Salesforce.com) and developer tools (e.g. JIRA), helpful</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
</ul>

</ul>
