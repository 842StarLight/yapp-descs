Why Vacasa Real Estate?
<br><br>
Vacasa is the largest vacation rental management firm in the US, and we're bringing our extensive industry expertise and data-driven approach to the specialized market of vacation rental real estate. We're obsessed with providing an amazing end-to-end experience for consumers looking for an income-generating vacation property.
<br><br>
<b>What We're Looking For:</b><br>
Vacasa's Real Estate Team is looking for a sales leader to join our team. This person will develop partnerships with the best real estate brokerages in Vacasa markets throughout the USA, helping hundreds of their clients find their right vacation home. Responsibilities include, building a sales pipeline, meeting with prospective agents/brokers, and closing property management sales.
<br><br>
Successful candidates will be strong communicators who are comfortable contacting brokers over the phone and in person, can learn and adapt quickly, are grounded in ethical business practices, and find reward in building a strong business. Consistently meeting departmental goals while maintaining high customer satisfaction is a must. You will need to demonstrate a track record of excellence as a team player who is also self-motivated, reliable, and coachable.
<br><br>
As our ideal candidate, you are a self-starter with experience in an account executive/closing role. Your strong communication skills and superior listening skills enable you to understand what the client needs and have strong problem-solving skills in order to meet those client needs. You close deals, celebrate teamwork, and are comfortable with the ambiguity of working in a start-up culture.
<br><br>
You will be managed by our Director of Real Estate Partnerships who will work with you to advance your career goals in line with the goals of Vacasa. This role will be based in California with emphasis on working with local brokers.
<br><br>
<b>What You'll Do:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Create new partnerships through active prospecting and communicating with real estate brokerages.</li>
<li>Collaborate with team to identify opportunities, plan and execute portfolio growth.</li>
<li>Leverage Vacasa tools, proprietary data, and subject matter experts to develop value proposition for brokers and their agents.</li>
<li>Oversee the on-boarding of new clients and developing strong relationships through regular contact, providing a stellar customer experience.</li>
<li>Develop long-term strategic relationships with key accounts.</li>
<li>Consistently exceed quota and key performance metrics.</li>
<li>Ensure customer satisfaction.</li>
<li>Expect moderate travel (30-40%).</li>
<li>Carry a valid real estate license in Arizona.</li>
</ul>
