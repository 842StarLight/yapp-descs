<p><b>
Position Overview:</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><br>
Technical and quotation sales support of Flender sales channels (Flender direct external sales team, Sales Reps, Distributors, Repair Centers), which includes:</p>
<ul>
<li>Selects, prepares and presents technical and commercial proposals of gear units, couplings, spare parts and services utilizing internal selection tools.</li>
<li>Reviews customer technical specifications and offers gear units, couplings, spare parts or services for different types of industrial markets, such as Minerals/Mining, Oil &amp; Gas, Material Handling, Extrusions, Pumps etc.</li>
<li>Resolves technical problems to meet customer requirements.</li>
<li>Reviews and clarifies technical &amp; commercial requirements of customers prior acceptance of customer purchase orders.</li>
<li>Prepares / clarifies requests for vendor supply components (transmitters, switches, monitoring system etc.).</li>
<li>Technical write-up of orders after acceptance of customer purchase orders.</li>
<li>Prepares internal quote / purchase order related documents for management approvals.</li>
<li>Communicates with customers on all product related topics (incl. technical aspects, order documentation tracking, quote development etc.).</li>
<li>Coordinates with local or HQ teams Flender technical or commercial solutions.</li>
<li>Supports internal order management or procurement teams from technical point of view.</li>
<li>Maintain internal sales management documents or databases.</li>
</ul>
<p>Supports Flender sales channels (external sales team, sales reps, distributors) in a flexible and adequate way.</p>
<p>
Prepares and presents demonstration materials for internal or external parties both locally and at offside locations.<br>
</p>

<p></p>
<p><br>
Good knowledge in a technical field or business method including the basic theoretical background.
</p>
<p>Develops basic business understanding.
</p>
<p>Contributes to team effort, awareness of expected value added.</p>
<p>
May improve processes, business methods or technical components.</p>

Required Knowledge/s, Education, Skills:</b></p>

5 + years of sales / customer service / application engineering experiences (inside or outside sales) or MSc preferably in Mechanical Engineering, Electro-Mechanical Engineering, Mechatronical Engineering or Technical Degree in related field preferred.
</p>
<p>Communication skills required to be able to clarify with customers technical or commercial conditions.
</p>
