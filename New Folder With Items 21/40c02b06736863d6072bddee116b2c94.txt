<h2>Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>Our Account Executives work in an innovative, customer centric environment that secures business from prospective customers across the United States. They are true hunters who love the thrill of new business and reaching sales targets. They are solutions driven, ambitious, and forward thinking. If this describes you, you might want to join our successful team.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>What You Will Be Doing</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Develop and execute a solution-oriented business plan that will achieve sales quota and budget as defined by the company</li>
<li>
Identify sales opportunities through direct prospecting, lead follow up, product demonstrations, conferences &amp; events</li>
<li>
Accountable to achieve 80% or greater of monthly, quarterly, and annual revenue quotas
</li>
<li>Effectively negotiate price, delivery, and policies and procedures to generate profits and greater customer satisfaction</li>
<li>
Accurately and consistently track sales activities and processes within internal the Customer Relations Management (CRM) tool</li>
<li>
Keep current on product features, advantages, benefits, and selling points for effective communication</li>
<li>
Assist in the development and creation of new sales and marketing ideas</li>
<li>
Performs other related duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
What We Require of You</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree in business administration, marketing, mass communications, or related field, or any equivalent combination of experience and training that provides the required knowledge, skills and abilities
</li>
<li>Minimum 1 + years sales experience, preferably in business development, lead generation, outbound calling or equivalent
</li>
<li>Valid Passport</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
What We'll Love About You</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
3+ years of Business to Business (B2B) revenue quota attainment sales experience
</li>
<li>Experience in Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) solution sales is a plus</li>
<li>
Proven track record of delivering measurable results</li>
<li>
Proven ability to be a business advisor by creating valuable business solutions</li>
<li>
Strive to learn and develop personally and professionally
</li>
<li>Strong collaboration and teambuilding skills</li>
<li>
Excellent, time management, organizational and planning skills</li>
<li>
Ability to multi-task and adapt to a fast-paced environment
</li>
<li>Knowledge of and/or experience with CRM tools</li>
</ul>
