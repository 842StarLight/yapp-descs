<h2>Merchandising Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Overview</b></p>
<p>TheMerchandising Coordinator works with the sales department and suppliers inorder to provide ample amounts of merchandising support and POS for theaccounts to be serviced properly.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Maintains supplier POS and merchandising inventoryby managing inventory status and organizing merchandise.</li>
<li>Provides desktop publishing support by designingShelf Talkers, Bottle Neckers, Case Cards, Wine Lists, Table Tents and allother merchandising materials.</li>
<li>Supports sales and brand management teams byassisting in the creation of Information Sheets and Presells, retrieving andformatting reviews, news and other merchandising information to salesteam.</li>
<li>Installs and builds displays by working withcustomers and sales management in the coordination of timing and location ofdisplays.</li>
<li>Maximizes pull through of products by stockingshelves and displays (where legally permissible).</li>
<li>Provides graphic design support by image and photographediting, image creation and conversion, preparing image files for printing,creating custom artwork for POS and merchandising campaigns and optimizingimages for the use on websites.</li>
<li>Maintains professional and industry knowledge byattending educational workshops, reviewing professional publications andestablishing personal networks.</li>
<li>Supports website by assisting with the preparationof materials, converting and formatting all merchandising materials for theweb.</li>
<li>Maintains professional and industry knowledge by attending educationalworkshops, reviewing professional publications, establishing personal networksand participating in professional societies.</li>
<li>Maintains customer confidence and protects operations by keepinginformation confidential.</li>
<li>Maintains a safe and clean working environment by complying withprocedures, rules and regulations.</li>
<li>Contributes to team effort by accomplishing related results as needed.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in related field and/or equivalent training and work experience.</li>
<li>Must have a valid driver’s license and be able to operate a motor vehicle.</li>
<li>A minimum of 2 years graphic design or desktop publishing experience.</li>
<li>Proficient PC skills using MS Office, Adobe CS6/CC Indesign, Photoshop, illustrator, Dreamweaver (these are core programs and are required.) and other various computer systems including presentation software.</li>
<li>Experience in using various hand/power tools a plus.</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task, work independently and/or within a team, pays attention to detail and meet deadlines.</li>
<li>Must be a results oriented professional with excellent verbal/written communication skills using diplomacy and discretion as well as strong customer service skills.</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>

<ul><li>One location</li></ul>
