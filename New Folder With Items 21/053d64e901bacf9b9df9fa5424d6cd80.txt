<h2>Key Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>· Act as the interface between the Key Accounts, Sales, and Operations organization and assume global responsibility for the customer partnership to assure satisfaction and growth (Gross Profit - GP).</p>
<p>· Develop account in line with the corporate targets and meet the growth and profitability objectives.</p>
<p>· Establish long term, tactical and strategic relationships within a portfolio of key accounts. Main functions which must be executed:</p>
<p>o Retention (expanding client relationships via multi-year deals)</p>
<p>o Expansion (grow client revenue and transactions)</p>
<p>o Tactical: (customer service delegation and escalation)</p>
<p><b>Tasks</b></p>
<p>· KAM KPI:</p>
<p>o Retention (35%) – Keep customer business we already have while building rapport. Meet with customers at least 1 time minimum a month in person</p>
<p>o Growth (35%) – Find ways and opportunities to grow more business with new lanes, modes, products, projects.</p>
<p>o Strategic / tactical (20%) – Create stronger ties to customer through continuous improvement projects such as Kaizen or other special projects. Lead strategic calls to offer best practices, strategic advice, consulting etc.</p>
<p>o Personal Development (10%) – Continue to learn and grow as a person; whether learning more about excel, ocean product, public speaking and so forth.</p>
<p>· Continually review Key Performance Indicators (KPI) and assist with managing reports and client drive IT solutions (such as EDI requirements).</p>
<p>· Identification of new and additional business with the key accounts to achieve targeted sales growth at a rate and profit margin consistent with group targets.</p>
<p>· Build trust-based relationships with key contacts at the client; strive to independently build deeper and wider relationships within and across client organization by building a comprehensive understanding of client and leveraging prior experience and client service acumen.</p>
<p>· Secure existing business by assuring reliability and increasing customer satisfaction.</p>
<p>· Assume responsibility and coordination for dedicated projects (e.g. new traffic lanes, logistics projects, etc.) for all phases of the project (proposal phase, negotiation discussions, project initiation and development, implementation and post-implementation) at a global level.</p>
<p>· Develop strong business relationship at customer across levels of their organization and create awareness of Apex within the customer’s organization and vice versa.</p>
<p>· Acquire industry knowledge and create awareness for the account and the clients industry within the Apex group by proper communication with relevant positions.</p>
<p>· Focal point for escalations and problem solving.</p>
<p>· Build client presentations that capture the client core business issues and the matching solutions that APEX capabilities can bring.</p>
<p>· Prepare Monthly Business Review (MBR), Quarterly Business Review (QBR), or Annual Business Review (ABR) process.</p>
<p>· Conduct and attend client and industry specific meetings to build and maintain relationships.</p>
<p>· Act as a mentor to more junior talent on the team.</p>
<p>· CRM (Customer Relationship Management) utilization – timely updates of Activities, Opportunities and Documents (PPT, SOP etc).</p>
<p>· Complete Monthly Report Cards of overview of Gross Profit (GP), Billing/Rates, Transit Time, Volumes, Value Added Services, Action Items, SOP, BI Tool etc.</p>
<p>· Conduct one-on-one meeting with the Sales to discuss strategies and provide a summary of account performance on a monthly basis.</p>
<p><b>Skills &amp; Requirements</b></p>
<p><b><i>Management Skills</i></b></p>
<p>· Organizational skills (prioritize, plan, assign and control).</p>
<p>· Decision-making capabilities.</p>
<p>· High attention to detail.</p>
<p>· Ability to handle pressure, juggle multiple responsibilities simultaneously, and work well with a diverse client base and various internal teams.</p>
<p><b><i>Personality Traits</i></b></p>
<p>· Excellent communication (verbal, written, listening and presentation) skills.</p>
<p>· Analytical and process skills (creativity/flexibility/strategic thinking).</p>
<p>· Strong network and relationship building capabilities.</p>
<p>· Able to assist in the sale and close a sale by effectively negotiating with decision makers to maintain and expand business opportunities.</p>
<p>· Able to read, interpret, and analyze documents, contracts and procedures in a timely manner.</p>
<p>· Ability to define problems, analyze facts and determine valid solutions to resolve the issues.</p>
<p>· Proven ability to be able to manage multiple work streams at a time with high attention to detail.</p>
<p>· Pro-active self-starter, strong interpersonal skills, highly motivation toward client relationship.</p>
<p>· High intellectual curiosity and hunger to learn in ambiguous environment.</p>
<p>· International mindset.</p>
<p><b><i>Educational Background &amp; Work Experience</i></b></p>
<p>· BA/BS Degree (Business, Finance, Economics, Logistics, Engineering).</p>
<p>· Working experience minimum 5 years.</p>
<p>· Industry experience minimum 3 years.</p>
<p>· Fluent in English and preferably other languages.</p>
<p><b><i>Travel</i></b></p>
<p>· Ability to travel to meet customer for in person visits domestically.</p>
