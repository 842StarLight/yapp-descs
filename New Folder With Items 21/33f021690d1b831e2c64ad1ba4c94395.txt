<h2>Business Development Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>Business Development Managers are expected to Insure and increase profitability of assigned product lines and support local efforts. Product Managers are expected to spend approximately 80% of their time each week outside of their office to enhance business communications and perform their assigned duties.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Assigned duties</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Access the overall filtration business and work with management to implement our growth plan</li>
<li>
Work with seasoned sales force with varying degrees of filtration experience<ul>
<li>
Access each salesman’s territory and develop account plan for filtration sales at key accounts</li>
<li>
Access each salesman’s filtration expertise and provide product and application training</li>
<li>
Work with each salesman to execute the account plan</li>
<li>
Make joint calls to support each salesman and to provide application expertise</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Provide sustainable filtration growth to the company</li>
<li>
Support EADS team members in quotation preparation and evaluation</li>
<li>
Track and measure assigned projects and products</li>
<li>
Make technical product presentations and share product updates, changes and additions</li>
<li>
Analyze sales figures to establish areas needing improvement</li>
<li>
Training coordination and presentation</li>
<li>
Develop new advertising tools (mailers, brochures)</li>
<li>
Communicate inventory needs to purchasing</li>
<li>
Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Skills and background required</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree or equivalent professional experience required</li>
<li>
10 years of proven filtration sales experience and success</li>
<li>
Strong application knowledge (preferably in pulp and paper, chemical, and power plant applications). Aviation fuel filtration experience is a bonus.</li>
<li>
Willingness to travel is a must. Travel will be primarily in GA, AL, TN, FL</li>
</ul>
