<h2>Principal Mining Engineer - Key Account Management</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Deswik are world leaders in a competitive market for technical mining applications - our software is used all day, every day, by 9 of the world’s top 10 mining companies. We solve complex technical mine planning problems in ways that deliver huge value to our customers.</p>
<p>We are searching for an <b>experienced Senior or Principal Mining Engineer</b>looking to take the next step in their career, into a <b>Technical Key Account Manager</b>role. In this role, you will couple your <b>extensive </b><b>mine planning experience</b>with your <b>great interpersonal skills</b>to strengthen Deswik’s relationship with your Key Account – a major global mining house.</p>
<p><i>Given Deswik’s global reach, you may be required to travel up to 50% of the year.</i></p>
<p><b>The role</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Finding new ways to apply Deswik technology to solve challenging mining problems</li>
<li>Managing the overall delivery of mine planning solutions to the Key Account customer – assisting with critical requests and issue escalation, coordinating internal resources and communicating progress</li>
<li>Exceeding the customer’s expectations,delivering increased value from existing investments and building a partnership</li>
<li>Developing new business, drafting proposals and negotiating contracts</li>
<li>Finalizing and closing out sales opportunities,then handing over to the Implementation team</li>
<li>Engaging with stakeholders ranging from frontline site teams to general managers and corporate executives</li>
<li>From time to time, working in the capacity of a Principal Mining Engineer on major projects</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What’s in it for you?</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Work/life balance and flexibility</li>
<li>There will be no ‘playbook’ for your role – just a great company culture and high-capability delivery team to back you up</li>
<li>Our business is successful and growing – make your mark on our success story</li>
<li>An opportunity to build and display your capability with both people and technology</li>
<li>Build on your stellar reputation in one corner of the mining industry by branching out across different mining methods,geographies and business functions.</li>
<li>Downtown Denver (CO), Calgary (AB) or Vancouver (BC) location – enjoy the flexibility and autonomy that allows you to balance this role with your life outside of work.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Who are you?</b></p>
<ul>
<li>You are degree-qualified in Mining Engineering or Geology</li>
<li>You’ve worked in underground and surface metalliferous operations and consider yourself a generalist with some specialist skills that set you apart</li>
<li>You have a solid site operations grounding and know what it takes to get things done on site</li>
<li>You have a deep understanding of mining engineering and operations</li>
<li>You know how to assemble people, technology and process to create mine planning systems</li>
<li>You have a restless desire for things to be done better</li>
<li>In your work, you use both EQ and IQ to solve problems and are known for doing what you say you will do</li>
<li>You are great with technology and will become literate in the use of Deswik tools. If you are already fluent that is a bonus!</li>
</ul>
