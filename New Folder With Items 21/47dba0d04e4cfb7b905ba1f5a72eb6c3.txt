<p><b>
PURPOSE AND OBJECTIVES</b></p>
<p>
The Principal Customer Success Manager is a client-facing role, ensuring customer success by delivering ongoing guidance around the overall cloud business strategy and transformation topics after the sales cycle has completed. The role focuses on executive relationship-building and establishment of proactive success plans that in turn promote overall customer satisfaction, product adoption and ultimately subscription renewals. The role manages diverse, high profile customers and/or key accounts in close collaboration with the respective account team.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
EXPECTATIONS AND TASKS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Work onsite with local customer and implementation partner in parallel to set up support, collaboration and governance as per SAP’s best practices</li>
<li>
Proactive and reactive response to Early Warning System alerts for adoption and support derailers</li>
<li>
Define and drive execution of a targeted goal-based support engagement plan to support key business goals and milestones (e.g. solution rollout and cycle planning)</li>
<li>
Plan and manage the delivery of relevant proactive support checks and enablement offerings as part of the support engagement plan</li>
<li>
Drive close collaboration with implementation partner during the launch phase to facilitate successful handover to support</li>
<li>
Enable key customer stakeholders by providing guidance on the appropriate best practices (e.g. System Administrator)</li>
<li>
Provide Quarterly Release Review to support customer to develop an appropriate strategy that minimizes impact on existing solution and assists in maximizing feature adoption</li>
<li>
Orchestrate Top issue management and provide trend analysis for proactive risk mitigation</li>
<li>
Facilitate access to product and functional expertise for certain topic areas via Empowerment Sessions</li>
<li>
Provide scorecard to track goal achievement</li>
<li>
Drive Customer Success Management onsite for key SAP Cloud customers across all cloud solutions</li>
<li>
Partial on-site presence with customers required, with local travel expectations up to 75% (customers will be assigned based on your home location)</li>
</ul>
