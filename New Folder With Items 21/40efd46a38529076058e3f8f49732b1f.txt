<h2>IT Facilities Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>We are looking for an IT and Facilities Manager to be responsible and accountable for the smooth running of our computer systems within the limits of requirements, specifications, costs and timelines. You will supervise the implementation and maintenance of our company’s computing needs as well as ensure that facilities are safe and well-functioning.</p>
<p>
This includes:</p>
<ul>
<li>Managing information technology and computer systems</li>
<li>Controlling and evaluating IT and electronic data operations</li>
<li>Managing an IT Team</li>
<li>Optimize the use of space and equipment while reducing operating costs</li>
</ul>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Customer Satisfaction</b></h1>
<p>Customer focused view of IT operational excellence. 99.9% peak time availability and critical systems uptime. Respond to any urgent open ticket within an hour.</p>
<p>
Prepare equipment and classroom for all online students, in-person classes and room rentals. 99,9% equipment functioning for class without technical issues nor server downtime.</p>
<p>
Monitor users’ satisfaction: focus on feedback and perspective. Monitor the students’ surveys on the topic “Environment”. Report and address any issues.</p>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
IT Equipment and Processes</b></h1>
<p>Align IT solutions with overall Business Strategic Goals.</p>
<p>
Manage risk and future readiness. Anticipate and prepare for IT problems that could arise, continuously upgrade IT skills through training and development, upgrade IT applications portfolio, upgrade hardware and software. Define a Business Continuity Plan.</p>
<p>
Hardware and computer equipment purchasing and maintenance for internal and external clients.</p>
<p>
Handle annual budget and ensure cost effectiveness.</p>
<p>
Audit systems and assess their outcomes.</p>
<p>
Improve and document processes for the use of IT across all departments.</p>
<p>
Design, develop, implement and coordinate systems, policies and procedures.</p>
<p>
Identify problematic areas and implement strategic solutions in time.</p>
<p>
Drive and complete IT Projects and Initiatives: ticketing system, video conference, Active Directory, ConnectWise…</p>
<p>
Ensure security of data, network access and backup systems.</p>
<p>
Conduct regular hardware, computer and computer accessories inventories, and report results to the Director of Operations. Keep inventories updated.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
IT Support to internal clients</b></h1>
<p>Own Level 3 helpdesk to end users.</p>
<p>
Manage Data back-ups.</p>
<p>
End-user Security Training: keep the entire Team Cybersafe certified.</p>
<p>
Ensure regular communication with the Team on IT and Facilities updates.</p>
<p>
New User set up that includes: hardware purchasing, system, hardware and workspace set up.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Phone System Management</b></h1>
<p>Maintain a high-quality and performing telephony infrastructure to support sales and lead generation efforts.</p>
<p>
Ensure call forwarding, routing and voicemail system effectiveness.</p>
<p>
Manage cost of data communications per analysis.</p>
<p>
Establish optimized call flow between Training Centers.</p>
<p>
Ensure availability of calls statistics reports.</p>
<p>
Eliminate phone system points of failure and downtime.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Facilities</b></h1>
<p>Oversee the company’s facility operations, manage office renovations or moves and manage alarm system, air conditioning and janitorial duties, all while remaining in compliance with local, state and federal regulations on environmental, health, safety and security standards.</p>
<p>
Coordinate with Property Managers in each facility.</p>
<p>
Negotiate bids and contracts for third party workers and vendors then coordinate maintenance services.</p>
<p>
Allocate office space according to needs and ensure best utilization of classroom space in coordination with the Operations Manager and Facilities Team.</p>
<p>
Update evacuation plans, and New Horizons South Florida Safety Standards and Emergency Plans included in the Team Member handbook.</p>
<p>
Handle insurance plans and service contracts.</p>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Manage IT and Facilities Team</b></h1>
<p>Manage IT Team by recruiting, training and coaching the IT and Facilities Coordinators, communicating job expectations and appraising their performance.</p>
<p>
Manage and develop talents, Team’s satisfaction and retain high potential Team Members.</p>
<p>
Manage the IT Team to own user support Level 1: password reset, printer, sound, monito. Level 2: network related issues, video conference set up. while keeping a high cleanliness of the training centers.</p>
<p>
Identify team trainings, certification plans, and skills advancement.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Requirements</b></h1>
<p>Proven working experience as an IT manager or relevant experience.</p>
<p>
Excellent knowledge of technical management, information analysis and of computer hardware/software systems.</p>
<p>
Expertise in data center management and data governance.</p>
<p>
Hands-on experience with computer networks, network administration and network installation.</p>
<p>
Great communication skills and experience working in a team</p>
<p>
Analytical and problem-solving skills.</p>
<p>
Ability to make decisions, lead and manage teams and projects.</p>
