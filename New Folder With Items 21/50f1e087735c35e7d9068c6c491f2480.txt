Job Summary
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The Salesforce Business and Technical Design Analyst will focus in satisfying business requirements &amp; business process flows while participating in the development and software delivery of Salesforce Customer Relationship Management (CRM) solutions for LA Care.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>The Salesforce Business &amp; Technical Design Analyst participates in full software development life cycle from UI/ UX and technical design to development, testing and deployment of the client solution on Salesforce platform leveraging, fully Salesforce out-of-the-box capabilities. This position may use APEX and Visualforce to create unique customer-based solutions, and participate in the estimation and timeline processes.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Duties
</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Understand and translate business requirements, business process flows etc. to software oriented solutions based upon Salesforce architecture.</li>
<li>Analyze the business processes and data requirements to come up with mockups. Documents current baselines, collects new requirements, and writes user stories, storyboards and future state.</li>
<li>Leverage out-of-the-box Salesforce UI/UX and functions and features.</li>
<li>Participate in translating business requirements to solutions on the Salesforce platform. Provide thought leadership to influence business logic to improve user experience/ interfaces, navigation, and design.</li>
<li>Interview, investigate and probe through multiple layers to better understand business drivers and business conditions. Participates in the business process redesign and documentation. Actively participates in the development of detailed design structure after understanding the design requirements.</li>
<li>Actively participates in the translation of engineering/ technical realities into simple terms for non-technical stakeholders. Provide such translation, adjusting the level of detail depending on the audience. Acts as the interface between the team and the management.</li>
<li>Act as a technical analyst to evaluate, assess, coordinate and verify development work. Participate in developing APEX and Visualforce code.</li>
<li>Participate in developing and delivering Web solutions using HTML, JavaScript, CSS.</li>
<li>Understand technical requirements and implement solutions leveraging Salesforce native functionality based upon in-depth understanding of Salesforce object model and architecture.</li>
<li>Configure workflows and approvals processes, Create test classes and deployment sets. Work with Quality Assurance Testing team to verify test cases and assist business unit. Work with project teams to ensure that the testing approach is sound and appropriately executed in all SDLC phases and across all Salesforce based apps and systems interfaces. Provides inputs into roadmap planning, system investments and operations performance.</li>
<li>Participate in creating SOAP/REST web services for integration.</li>
<li>Perform data integration and/or migration.</li>
<li>Collaborate with team members to identify processes and raises concerns about dependencies or impacts. Work with SOA Bus (Oracle Bus).</li>
<li>Work with ETL (Extract, Transform Load) tools such as Informatica</li>
<li>Perform other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
