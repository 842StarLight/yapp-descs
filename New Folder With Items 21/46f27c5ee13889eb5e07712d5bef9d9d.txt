<h2>Sr. Manager, HR Business Partner</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Summary</b></p>
<p>
GumGum is nothing without its people. Our People Operations team partners with leaders, managers and employees to develop strategies that enhance our innovative culture and employee experience. From hiring outstanding talent to ensuring that GumGummers find their work motivating, engaging and fulfilling, the People Operations department strives to create a high-performance, creative and inclusive environment across our global offices.</p>
<p>
The Sr. Manager, HR Business Partner (HRBP) position is responsible for aligning business objectives with employees and management for departments and teams across U.S. GumGum locations (Santa Monica, New York and Chicago). The position serves as a consultant to management on human resource-related issues. The successful Sr. Manager, HRBP acts as an employee champion and change agent with an eye on culture. The role assesses and anticipates HR-related needs. Communicating needs proactively with our HR department and business management, this person seeks to develop integrated solutions. The position formulates partnerships across the HR function to deliver value-added service to management and employees that reflects the needs and culture of the organization. Additionally, this person maintains an effective level of business knowledge, its financial measures, culture and competition. This role reports to the VP (Head of), People Operations (HR).<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>
Business Alignment &amp; Partnership:</p>
<ul><li>
Lead and support the alignment of business initiatives with employees and management</li></ul>
<p>
Coaching &amp; Consulting:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Conduct regular, pro-active meetings with employees, managers and leaders to develop, assess, and anticipate HR related needs</li>
<li>
Provide consultative guidance and support to the business as they evolve HR and people practices</li>
<li>
Consult with management, providing HR guidance on a variety of issues and topics</li>
<li>
Provide day-to-day performance management guidance to management (e.g., coaching, counseling, career development, performance improvement)</li>
<li>
Help manage the annual performance management and compensation planning, as well as quarterly goals process</li>
</ul>
<p>
Org Design, Talent Management &amp; Planning:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Analyze trends and metrics in partnership with the HR group to develop solutions, programs and policies</li>
<li>
Provide guidance and input on organizational design, workforce planning, talent management and succession planning</li>
<li>
Leverage metrics and insights to inform recommendations on people practices</li>
<li>
Deliver insights and reports to leadership as needed</li>
<li>
Draft and edit job descriptions, advise on compensation, and collaborate with talent acquisition and hiring managers on interviewing and advising on selection</li>
</ul>
<p>
Employee Relations:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Manage and resolve complex employee relations issues</li>
<li>
Conduct effective, thorough and objective investigations when needed</li>
<li>
Counsel employees and proactively facilitate conflict resolution between employees, escalating issues as appropriate</li>
<li>
Maintain in-depth knowledge of employment law matters related to day-to-day management of employees, reducing legal risks and ensuring regulatory compliance</li>
<li>
Consult with external employment law attorneys as needed/required</li>
<li>
Provide HR policy guidance and interpretation</li>
</ul>
<p>
Employee Engagement, Development &amp; Retention:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Work closely with management and employees to improve work relationships, build morale, and increase productivity and retention</li>
<li>
Design and manage change management and communications plans</li>
<li>
Identify training needs for business units and individual executive coaching needs; may deliver training programs as needed</li>
<li>
Facilitate effective on-boarding and exit processes; provide tools to managers for effective onboarding</li>
</ul>
<p>
HR Expertise:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Establish leadership presence, credibility and trustworthiness across U.S. offices</li>
<li>
Partner with GumGum HR professionals across benefits, HRIS, training, compliance and communications to deliver HR solutions</li>
<li>
Maintain strict confidentiality and adhere to a high standard of professional conduct</li>
<li>
Keep pulse on HR and industry trends</li>
</ul>
