<p>Now hiring a Design Engineer II in East Peoria, IL! Temp-to-hire or direct hire position available immediately!

<div>Trillium Staffing, a leader in the staffing industry, is currently seeking an experienced Design Engineer in the East Peoria, IL area. The Design Engineer II designs and develops engineered solutions for our customers’ material movement requirements.<br>
<br>
Duties and responsibilities will include:<br>
<ul>
<li>Analyzing customers’ requirements and developing cost effective design solutions.</li>
<li>Collaborating with the sales team to develop conceptual design prints and renderings.</li>
<li>Reviewing product designs for compliance with engineering principles, company standards, customer contract requirements, and related specifications.</li>
<li>Utilizing Creo software to produce 3D models and prints.</li>
<li>Utilizing advanced analytical tools to develop and validate designs.</li>
<li>Processing and publishing shop prints, designing changes, specifications, and drawing releases.</li>
<li>Providing production support to the operations department.</li>
<li>Creating screenshots or renderings as requested by Sales, Marketing, Engineering, or Operations.</li>
<li>Hands-on Operations &amp; Sales support including, but not limited to tool design, manufacturing / weld sequencing, pallet design, and shipping procedures.</li>
<li>Designing for manufacturability while understanding machine and material capabilities and to utilize that knowledge to design for cost effective manufacturing processes.</li>
<li>Provides guidance and technical assistance to design staff.</li>
<li>Other jobs and / or duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
</div>
