<div>CLS Generic Position Summary</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
As a member of the professional staff, contributes general knowledge and skill in a discipline area (e.g. Accounting, Finance, Human Resources, Information Resources, Operations Planning &amp; Support, Sales &amp; Marketing) to support team and/or department objectives. Generally works under close supervision or within well-established guidelines to complete routine tasks.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Specific Job Summary</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
As a member of the Shared Services department, the Senior Staff Accountant contributes specialized knowledge in the shared services accounting process including inter-company and inter-unit transactions. The Senior Staff Accountant supports the period close process as well as prepares and interprets results for business operations during the period, quarter, and year-end close process as well as coordination and consolidation of financial data needed for use in these processes.</div>
<br>
<div>
</div>
<div>CLS Generic Expected Contributions</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Performs the following types of basic tasks: analysis, auditing, budgeting, forecasting, programming, research, report generation, and software integration.</li>
<li>Responds to, solves and makes decisions on standard/routine business requests with limited risk.</li>
<li>Responsible for own work and contributing to team, department and/or business results. May direct work of non-management staff.
</li>
<li>Assists more senior associates in achieving business results by:
<ul>
<li>utilizing technical knowledge and skills to enhance business processes.
</li>
<li>establishing priorities for self and, where appropriate, others.
</li>
<li>allocating own time effectively to meet goals in a manner that does not disadvantage other associates or groups.</li>
<li>contributing to department/unit budget as appropriate (i.e., input, following guidelines, etc).</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Demonstrates an awareness of personal strengths and areas for improvement and acts independently to improve and increase skills and knowledge.</li>
<li>Performs other duties as appropriate.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<div>
</div>
<div><b>Specific Expected Contributions</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Reviews financial data to ensure it is in compliance with MVCI's policies and procedures.</li>
<li>Completes period end closing process, including journal entries, financial statement activity and reporting for all appropriate departments.
</li>
<li>Performs reconciliations, journal entries, and related research.</li>
<li>Ensures necessary adjusting journal entries are completed in timely manner.
</li>
<li>Holds periodic review meetings with department managers to identify and resolve outstanding issues.
</li>
<li>Prepares period end operating statement and balance sheet management reports.</li>
<li>Responsible for MIP-66 account reconciliations</li>
<li>Assists department managers in the completion of their budget models and forecast requests.
</li>
<li>Responsibilities also include providing ad hoc reports for management.</li>
<li>Assists with preparation of audit work papers</li>
<li>Performs special projects as required.</li>
<br>
</ul>
