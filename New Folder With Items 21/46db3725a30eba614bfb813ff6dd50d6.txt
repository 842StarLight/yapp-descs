 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Back End Software Engineer

TixTrack is re-inventing the way live entertainment companies and attractions sell tickets to their events. Our software is used by industry leaders such as The Broad Museum, The Los Angeles Clippers, The Walt Disney Company and Cirque du Soleil. Our products have been driving tremendous growth for our customers' sales and, as a result, TixTrack is now experiencing our fastest growth ever. Were looking for a great Back End Software Engineer to help continue our success!
