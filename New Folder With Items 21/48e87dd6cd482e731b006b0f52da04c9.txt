<h2>Data Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>iLendingDIRECT is seeking a Data Analyst to join our team. The Data Analyst will build or use existing contemporary data tools and technology that will provide data analytics and insights to all departments to increase revenue and grow profitability. The role will be responsible for maintaining and improving existing Data Analytics processes and reports, as well as serving as a resource on a broad variety of special projects.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p><b><i>
Analysis
</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provide sales and senior leadership team with scenario analysis utilizing the sales model</li>
<li>Contribute to business development and growth (both new client and organic growth) using analytics in business plan proposals and project development</li>
<li>Review monthly results and implement monthly variance reporting</li>
<li>Assist with the preparation of departmental budgets</li>
<li>Provide prompt and accurate responses to numerous ongoing one-off analysis requests</li>
<li>Perform thorough analyses to uncover actionable insights or strategic opportunities related to goals like business growth, operational efficiency, and cost/resource optimization</li>
<li>Collaborate with the IT team (your technical counterparts) to discuss and prioritize data needs, instrumentation/tracking, and other key inputs required for your analyses</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>Ongoing reporting</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Complete ongoing reporting in a timely and accurate manner</li>
<li>Maintain and enhance the sales model, including monthly updates to actual, budget to actual and forecast</li>
<li>Maintain and enhance staffing models for various departments</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>New report development &amp; process enhancement</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Enable data-informed decision-making across the entire company through the development and deployment of dashboards/reports, standardized KPIs/operational metrics, and training/awareness</li>
<li>Utilizing Tableau, Excel, and SQL to continue development of a full suite of operational reports from multiple systems</li>
<li>Provide a high level of responsiveness in developing new reporting to address needs or questions from the senior leadership team.</li>
<li>Assist in the creation of a new suite of reports to assist business owners in providing inputs to the annual budget process.</li>
<li>Partner with senior leaders (e.g. Sales, Marketing, Product, Finance, Technology) to understand business goals, advise on data strategies, and re engineer business processes to produce repeatable, data-driven outcomes</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements and Skills</b>:</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b><i>
Required:</i></b></h2>
<ul>
<li>2-5 years of finance and/or data analytics experience</li>
<li>SQL query building experience</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree from a 4-year accredited college or university; Finance, or other analytical/technical majors strongly preferred</li>
<li>Advanced level Excel skills, expert level strongly preferred</li>
<li>Hands-on experience building reporting solutions in Tableau or similar tool</li>
</ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b><i>Preferred:</i></b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Predictive modeling experience</li>
<li>Advanced SQL knowledge</li>
<li>Experience building staffing models</li>
<li>Experience with financial reporting, budgeting and forecasting a plus</li>
<li>Experience in the financial services industry preferred</li>
</ul>
<p>Attributes</p>
<ul>
<li>Excellent written and communication skills</li>
<li>Keen analytical, organizational and problem-solving skills</li>
<li>Experience in effectively communicating key data, including presentations to senior management and other stakeholders</li>
<li>Ability to translate analytical concepts to individuals at all levels in the organization</li>
<li>Ability to work in a fast paced, team environment with rapidly changing priorities</li>
<li>Meticulous attention to detail – demonstrates accuracy and thoroughness, organized, monitors own work to ensure quality</li>
<li>Independent problem-solving ability</li>
<li>Challenges current practice constructively and gives feedback that improves performance</li>
<li>Customer-oriented, positive attitude</li>
<li>Commitment to iLendingDIRECT s core values</li>
</ul>
