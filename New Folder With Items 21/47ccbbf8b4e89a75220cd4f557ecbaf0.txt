<h2>Systems Business Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>Renaissance Life &amp; Health is seeking an experience Insurance Systems Business Analyst to join our growing team!
<br>
<br>
The Systems Business Analyst is responsible for supporting third-party and internally developed software. You will support and maintain internal admin systems, external portals and various ancillary systems as well as solve organizational information problems and requirements and assist in the design of computer programs, testing and recommending system controls and protocols.
<br>

<li>
Technical liaison between business users, IT, and technology teams</li>

<li>
Communicates user prioritization to IT and operational teams in alignment with Roadmap</li>
<li>
Discover, define, and implement data and workflow mapping across people and systems</li>
<li>
Technical resource on internal application/module roll outs to all users</li>
<li>
Maintains production configuration documentation on all internal systems and integrations</li>
<li>
Work closely with BU Stakeholders and System Architects to define business case and system requirements</li>

Facilitates functional design with and IT operations teams</li>
<li>
Coordinate and track requirements from inception to development, to testing, users sign off and final delivery</li>

Lead deployment and implementation of large-scale system initiatives, including but not limited to automating current processes, implementing new systems, and upgrading current systems</li>
<li>
Understand and adapt to internal and external customer needs during initial sale, product implementation and support, and renewal processes</li>

Manage external system vendor relationships. Monitor and negotiate vendor contracts and work orders</li>
