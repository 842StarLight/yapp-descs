<h2>Global Healthcare Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Description Summary</b></h1>
A superb, wide-ranging Global Healthcare Consultant career development role with BD, a global leader in Medical Technology.<br>
BD is a global medical technology company focused on improving drug therapies, enhancing the quality and speed of diagnosing infectious diseases, and advancing research and discovery of new drugs and vaccines. BD provides an environment which enables our highly talented workforce to be the best at their professions. We are always seeking people who have a passion and commitment to join our Company on its journey to ‘helping all people live healthy lives’.<p></p>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Job Description</b></h1>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
As Global Healthcare Consultant reporting to our Global Manager Solution Discovery &amp; Workflow Optimization you’ll operate across the Center of Excellence department</li>
<li>
The Global Healthcare Consultant will engage throughout the customer lifecycle for the Kiestra Platform providing customized solution design, lean workflow optimization and key outcomes measurement for targeted accounts. This role will continue to expand (FY20 and beyond) to include other DS platforms (i.e. COR) and be part of an established Center-of-excellence (CoE) around workflow optimization and design</li>
<li>
The Healthcare consultant plays a key role in enabling customers to achieve an optimized integrated solution combining leading edge technology with best in class processes.</li>
<li>
Through collaboration with the Sales, Marketing, Scientific and Services teams the consultant will demonstrate improvements in patient care, quality and staff utilization resulting DS Solutions.</li>
<li>
Through collaboration with the Sales, Marketing, Scientific and Services teams the consultant will demonstrate improvements in patient care, quality and staff utilization across the entire patient pathway.</li>
<li>
Train and coach regional LEAN specialists during their workflow &amp; statistical analysis engagements to assist laboratories worldwide throughout the implementation of automation and best practices in service (“LEAN”) solutions.</li>
<li>
Teach Lean curriculums to aspiring Lean Practitioners and Lean Leaders within the organization and mentor and coach them during their certification projects.</li>
<li>
Manage and develop activities required to implement service (“LEAN”) strategy across the entire BD DS solution offerings. Identifies and drives services (“LEAN”) to ensure competitive differentiation for the complete BD DS solution offering.</li>
<li>
Establishes and maintains a worldwide communication network with regional LEAN Specialists for global best practices in service (“LEAN”) offering.</li>
<li>
Primary assignment to any BD office in the US and/or home based. Field-based activities approximately 60% of the time.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Educated to a Bachelor's degree (Medical, healthcare or engineering preferable), you’ll have at least 3 years of experience in Microbiology/ Clinical Diagnostic with at least 2 years designing and executing implementation of customized automated laboratory solutions. And:</li>
<li>
Must be skilled in Lean / Six Sigma methodologies, change management, team development and project management</li>
<li>
Experience in sales, capital sales or account ownership with strong documented track record of success strongly preferred</li>
<li>
Experience with Antimicrobial Stewardship Committees is preferred</li>
<li>
Strong strategic planning and organizational skills</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to develop and sustain strong customer relationships</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to understand, interpret and communicate complex subject matter</li>
<li>
Proven interpersonal and communication skills, both verbal and written are required</li>
<li>
Proven ability to work independently as well as in a team environment, with all levels of BD employees and customer contact</li>
<li>
Strong technical product knowledge</li>
<li>
Proven Project Management skills</li>
<li>
Strong Microsoft Office skills (EXCEL, PowerPoint, Word, Visio) are required, as well as a working knowledge of statistical software</li>
<li>
Must be willing and able to travel up to 60% (domestically and international)</li>
<li>
Six Sigma Black Belt Certification or Lean Agent Certification are required</li>
<li>
Healthcare consulting experience preferred</li>
</ul>
