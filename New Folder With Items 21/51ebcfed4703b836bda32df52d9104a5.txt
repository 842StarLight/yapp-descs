As a Territory Sales Manager, you will build and maintain relationships with existing B2B customers and develop new prospects to increase sales of our products and services. In this role you will develop business with key or named accounts and lead dedicated account teams. You will also partner with our customers, developing sales strategies and marketing plans. The ability to build solid relationships and sound business strategies with key business partners is critical to your success.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<p>
Additional Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Assisting B2B customers in making product selections based on customer needs, product specifications and applicable regulations.</li>
<li>
Negotiate prices, terms of sale and service agreements.</li>
<li>
Ensure high post-sales satisfaction by facilitating positive, long-term relationships and high potential for repeat business with customers.</li>
<li>
Learn and utilize our sales techniques.</li>
<li>
Report daily or weekly activity.</li>
