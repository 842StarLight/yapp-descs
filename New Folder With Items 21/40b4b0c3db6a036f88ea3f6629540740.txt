<h2>IT-Technician</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>OVERVIEW:</b></p>
<p>
Vitabyte offers an excellent opportunity for motivated and skilled technicians throughout the country. Our Field Technicians provide on-site field support to customers in the food service and retail industries, performing cabling, installation, repairs, upgrades and maintenance of Point-of-Sale (POS) hardware and software. Strong troubleshooting and customer service skills are what we desire! Some jobs require overnight work some travel and occasionally weekend work. Training will be provided for all positions however, basic technical skills are a must.</p>
<p><b>
REQUIREMENTS:</b></p>
<p>
Network Wiring – Cat5 and Ethernet Cabling<br>
Network Hardware – Hubs, Switches, Network Cards, etc.<br>
Advanced Knowledge of PC’s (Hardware and Software)<br>
Windows and DOS Knowledge<br>
Knowledge of Computer and Electrical Test Equipment<br>
Excellent oral and written communication skills with superior problem-solving and analytical expertise<br>
Good communication and excellent customer service skills, “customer friendly” attitude<br>
Detail-oriented with ability to work independently and handle multiple tasks<br>
Valid Driver’s License with clean driving record</p>
<p><b>
EDUCATION:</b></p>
<p>
B.A. in Computer sciences<br>
A+, N+, Cisco.<br>
VITABYTE is an Equal Opportunity Employer m/f/d/v.</p>
