What you'll be doing…
<br><br>
As our Vice President of Channels, you will be responsible for designing and executing partnerships in close coordination with functional leaders across the organization. This team leads all aspects of identifying, evaluating, and executing large-scale partnerships and transactions with the potential to have a significant impact on the overall direction of the company. Our ideal candidate has proven experience managing complex partnerships, setting strategy, driving business and technical alignment, and communicating with both internal and external executives. You'll be tasked with leading our team of channel sales professionals managing our value and volume resellers in North America. You'll also be responsible for collaborating with cross functional teams to modernize our channel strategy for North America.
