<h2>Retail Supervisor WA</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Our Supervisors are Leaders. They are sales driven and are able to consistently develop team members through one on one selling and coaching. You’ll be a hands-on role model for your staff, providing continuous training and guidance. This is a full time position with 37-40 hours per week.<br>
What you will do in this job:</p>
<ul>
<li>Drive sales. All the time. If you want a non-selling job, this isn’t it.</li>
<li>Proven experience leading a team to consistently meet or exceed sales and service goals. We obsess over results, so be prepared for us to validate your results.</li>
<li>Minding the details, delivering exceptional customer service while ensuring processes and policies are followed in a professional environment.</li>
<li>Provide a terrific engagement experience to our customers on a consistent basis.</li>
<li>Be able to understand and complete business reports and communicate results to the team and Corporate partners.</li>
<li>Proficient in excel use and inventory management software.</li>
</ul>
<div>
What Castle needs from you:<br>
<ul>
<li>Be a part of a team that wants to win and earn added incentives and bonus.</li>
<li>Experience working with advanced point-of-sale computer systems. Ours is pretty complex, so you’ll need to be quick to learn and really good at multitasking.</li>
<li>Ability to work a flexible schedule that meets the needs of the business, including, the day before and after major holidays, evenings and weekends. This includes availability to work opening and closing shifts.</li>
<li>We also embrace change, so be ready to take on new challenges and learn new things regularly.</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>
This position qualifies for the following benefits:<br>
<ul>
<li>Competitive pay, shift differential, spiff opportunities, contests, and monthly performance bonus opportunities</li>
<li>Ongoing career and Leadership Development supported by our Castle University Training Program</li>
<li>Ongoing product knowledge training</li>
<li>Security in a financially strong Company</li>
<li>Medical/Dental/Vision for Full time Management and Associates</li>
<li>100% Company Sponsored Life Insurance for Full time Management and Associates</li>
<li>Paid Time Off</li>
<li>401K with Generous Matching Contribution</li>
</ul>
</div>
