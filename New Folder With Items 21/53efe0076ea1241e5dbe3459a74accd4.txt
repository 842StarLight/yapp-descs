<h2>Inside Sales Rep</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>HackerEarth provides enterprise software solutions that help organizations in their innovation management and talent assessment endeavors. HackerEarth Recruit is a talent assessment platform that helps in efficient technical talent screening allowing organizations to build strong, proficient teams. HackerEarth Sprint is an innovation management software that helps organizations drive innovation through internal and external talent pools, including HackerEarth's global community of 3M+ developers. Today, HackerEarth serves 750+ organizations, including leading Fortune 500 companies from around the world. General Electric, IBM, Amazon, Apple, Wipro, Walmart Labs and Bosch are some of the brands that trust HackerEarth in helping them drive growth.
</p>
<p>We are a rapidly growing Technical Recruiting Software (Saas) company looking to hire an Inside Sales Manager who enjoys hunting and opening accounts. You should be fast paced, self-motivated, relational sales reps who can find new prospects and close deals. You should be good with managing your own time, comfortable tracking activities in a CRM (Salesforce) and be goal and $$$ oriented.
</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities:
</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Develop and aggressively identify sales opportunities through prospecting, cold calls &amp; relationship development for the US market.
</li>
<li>Doing Outbound calls to develop non-active partners, or partners who have a good business potential
</li>
<li>Conducting targeted email campaigns to reach key decision makers in target companies.
</li>
<li>Identifying opportunities by speaking to customers, understanding their requirements, educating them on HackerEarth’s offerings and narrowing down possible opportunities of partnering.
</li>
<li>Present/Demo product solution to prospects and clearly convey offerings
</li>
<li>Develop and maintain relationships with prospects and partners
</li>
<li>Responsible for Lead Generation as well as closing the sales online or by phone.
</li>
<li>Prepare status reports, share updates with the leadership team, record all calls and sales activity in CRM, and propose new business strategies.
</li>
<li>Develop a complete understanding of HackerEarth’s products and collaborate with the management team to provide consistent customer and prospect feedback to help improve the product offerings.
</li>
</ul>
