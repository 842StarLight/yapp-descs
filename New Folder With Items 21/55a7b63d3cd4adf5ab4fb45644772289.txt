<h2>Team Leader</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>The Team Leader, (TL), brings their passion and love of pets and uses it to drive their team to create an exceptional customer experience. Our dedicated and knowledgeable TL?s are responsible for leading their teams to success through coaching, educating, training, and developing and inspiring their teams in all areas required to meet Bentley?s standards. The TL ensures delivery of all standards and the meeting of company goals. By being a part of the Bentley?s Pet Stuff family, the TL shares our exciting culture while upholding all procedures and policies and building relationships with customers and the community. The TL ensures merchandise is properly priced, displayed and stocked according to the appropriate inventory levels for the store. The TL must be able to hold the team accountable, provide education and on-going support to the team. They are responsible for meeting and exceeding sales plans, managing budgets, hiring and firing decisions. The TL embodies the Bentley?s standard of excellence by ensuring the team maintains the highest level of customer service, merchandise presentation, inventory and store cleanliness standards. The TL fosters a team-building environment. They manage, train, coach and lead to ensure Bentley?s Pet Stuff expectations are met daily. TL?s ensure that the team is celebrating and supporting our culture of all natural pet nutrition. We look for leaders that inspire others, believe in integrity and are not afraid to take a risk.</p>
<p><b>Job duties include but are not limited to:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Makes final decisions regarding hiring, training, and developing store team. Develops team succession plan to prepare for turnover and to support store partners in their career path according to their skill set and ambition.</li>
<li>Effectively coaches and counsels team, to improve performance and resolve partner conflicts. Partners with District Leader and Human Resources regarding corrective action and separation. Completes performance reviews of team members.</li>
<li>Motivates and directs store team on product knowledge, engagement and consulting skills to successfully achieve sales budgets</li>
<li>Control expenses, inventory, and profitability to meet or exceed P&amp;L budgets</li>
<li>Manages labor schedule for adequate store coverage and customer service. All schedules must be completed in Paychex and available to team 2 weeks in advance.</li>
<li>Edits and approves as team timecards on a weekly basis, no later than Tuesday at Noon CST for input into payroll.</li>
<li>Interacts professionally in both verbal and written communication, both with in-store partners as well as external partners and professional contacts</li>
<li>Prioritizes and organizes company initiatives; plans, delegates, supervises, monitors and directs store partners to ensure projects are completed by deadlines</li>
<li>Responsible for ensuring that entire team understands, follows and effectively implements operating procedures, processes and are in compliance with company policies</li>
<li>Ensures that all partners complete training programs on time</li>
<li>Provides quick and courteous customer service to all pet parents. Motivates team to engage customers to learn pet parents needs and consults and educates regarding products that meet those needs. Observes, monitors and provides coaching feedback to store partners, related to customer service.</li>
<li>Coaches team on performance including product knowledge, nutrition knowledge, loyalty sign-up, consultation skills to increase sales, execution of policies and procedures, community marketing.</li>
<li>Train all store staff on counterpoint system.</li>
<li>Responsible for ensuring that all orders were submitted as needed within budget guidelines.</li>
<li>Review and correct negative inventory daily.</li>
<li>Manage cycle count process for store.</li>
<li>Responsible for management of inventory receiving</li>
<li>(future) Omni-channel /pet zoom/subscription sales</li>
<li>Ensures merchandising standards, visual presentation standards, advertising signage and promotional displays are implemented according to instructions</li>
<li>Aggressively markets Bentley?s brand in the community, including events in-store and out of the store in the community. Ensures promotion of events by having flyers created to invite customers back to the store and by posting on store?s Facebook page. Ensures execution of required number of store specific events as well as Bentley?s national events.</li>
<li>Builds and develops relationships with adoption agencies to increase adoption events and number of animals finding new forever homes.</li>
<li>Builds relationships that create trust, open dialogue, and full transparency</li>
<li>Maintains a clean, safe, and healthy environment for store partners and pet parents</li>
<li>Job seeker should be reliable, confident, and courageous enough to initiate decisions and take relevant actions if they consider something violates professional ethics.</li>
</ul>
</div>
