<h2>Software Development Manager, Public Safety</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Do you want to lead efforts creating software that supports first responder safety and day-to-day operations for Law Enforcement and Fire agencies? The Software Engineering Manager position suits individuals who thrive in dynamic, collaborative environments that provide significant opportunity for knowledge and career growth. The Public Safety Enterprise Records team is a group of passionate, thought leading technologists who engage with end users regularly to craft best-in-breed Public Safety software. These products are used directly by first responders to save time and optimize their daily efforts. As Software Engineering Manager, you would be responsible for directly leading and mentoring a team of Engineers and collaborating across QA and Product Management in scoping, design, development, and release activities. This role is a key part in the total product delivery process.
</p>
<p>Work with our integrated solutions that help courts and public safety organizations of all sizes better protect and serve the public. By helping provide solutions that improve efficiency and response time, you can help serve our citizens and make communities safer.
</p>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities</b></h2>
<b>Scope and Impact</b><br>
The Software Engineering Manager is expected to lead the production of product release deliverables on time and with a high-level of application quality and performance for a web-based product that spans both on-premise, cloud, and mobile.Output impacts revenue and organizational growth, which in turn supports our Sales and Services organizations. The Enterprise Records team works in a fast-paced environment to support growing business objectives with a high degree of accountability. The Software Engineering Manager directly contributes to this effort by owning the execution and delivery of releases and fostering a strong engineering practice. This role works within a larger Product team that includes QA and Product Management functions.<b>Complexity</b><br>
The Software Engineering Manager must be able to:<ul>
<li>
Be a leader who can inspire, motivate, and positively guide the work and careers of the engineering team.The growth and success of the team is your success.</li>
<li>
Possess a strong and diverse technical background to coach and assist with product design activities.</li>
<li>
Believe in software craftsmanship and quality as part of their DNA</li>
<li>
Gain fulfillment in the building of technology/product for a societal purpose (public safety)</li>
<li>
Proactively listen and be empathetic with directs through daily communication</li>
<li>
Understand and prioritize requirements and information, based on input from a variety of sources.</li>
<li>
Support and collaborate well with a team of leaders toward common organizational goals</li>
<li>
Demonstrate an excellent work ethic</li>
<li>
Communicate technical and non-technical information clearly and effectively/professionally with subordinates, peers, management, clients, and other departments within the company.</li>
<li>
Manage multiple tasks and initiatives while exhibiting calm and focus</li>
<li>
Inspect, adapt, and iterate effectively in a dynamic work environment and as business drivers change year over year.</li>
<li>
Remain flexible to changing product and technical environments.</li>
<li>
Enjoy technology and learning</li>
<li>
Have and share great ideas</li>
<li>
Want to be part of ""green field"" product development and initial release to the market</li>
</ul>
