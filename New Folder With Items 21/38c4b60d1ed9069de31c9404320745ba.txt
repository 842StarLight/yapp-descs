<h2>Seasonal Customer Service Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul><li>Things Remembered Fulfillment Center in North Jackson, Ohio is seeking Seasonal Inbound Call Center Reps. The Teammates in this role is responsible for processing orders, processing returns, up-selling/cross-selling, responding to inquiries and building relationships with customers who shop via the internet or catalog.</li></ul>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities (Essential Functions)
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Utilizes order entry software to process customer orders that are received by phone, fax, mail or via e-mail.
</li>
<li>Maintains a high degree of accuracy when processing orders, reporting corrections, submitting order change requests, etc. Completes all paperwork in a timely manner.
</li>
<li>Responds to inquiries regarding order status, order changes, returns, billing, shipping charges, etc. in an efficient and professional manner.
</li>
<li>Maintains a high degree of product knowledge and maximizes each sales opportunity.
</li>
<li>Builds relationships with customers by following up post sale to ensure customer satisfaction and places outbound calls to promote special sales events.
</li>
<li>Assists customers in resolving technical issues/questions that may be encountered when shopping on-line.
</li>
<li>Performs stock lookups to search for inventory within warehouse or at a store.
</li>
<li>Meets quality, sales and shrink goals.
</li>
<li>Adapts to business needs through willingness to cross-train to other channels and performs other tasks as needed.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2>
<div><div><div><ul>
<li>High school diploma or GED</li>
<li>
Previous customer service and/or sales experience</li>
<li>
Computer literate with the ability to learn customer service software applications</li>
<li>
Professional verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>
Ability to calculate figures and amounts</li>
</ul></div></div></div>
