<h2>Sales Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div><div>
<div>
<p>TEAM UP WITH US!</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
The Sales Manager for SITECH Texhoma, leader, and coach. Manages sales activities of organization by performing the following duties or through subordinate supervisors.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>DO YOU HAVE WHAT IT TAKES?</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
WHAT YOU'LL DO</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Sales, Rental and Marketing of construction technology</li>
<li>Directs staffing, training, and performance evaluations to develop and control the sales program.</li>
<li>Coordinates sales distribution by establishing sales territories, quotas, and goals and advises dealers, distributors, and clients concerning rental sales and advertising techniques.</li>
<li>Assigns sales territory or market segment to sales personnel.</li>
<li>Analyzes sales statistics to formulate policy and to assist the dealership in promoting sales.</li>
<li>Reviews market analyses to determine customer needs, volume potential, price schedules, and discount rates and develops sales campaigns to accommodate goals of company.</li>
<li>Represents company at trade association meetings to promote SITECH Texoma and Trimble.</li>
<li>Coordinates liaison between sales department and other SITECH departments.</li>
<li>Analyzes and controls expenditures of division to conform to budgetary requirements.</li>
<li>Prepares periodic sales reports showing sales volume and potential sales.</li>
<li>Coordinates Program planning with customers, departments, and sales reps to ensure identification of appropriate lead times, goals, design criteria, cost and similar consideration.</li>
<li>Identifies market potential, establishing pricing and market strategies, estimates potential sales, introduces markets, distribution/channel issues, defines promotional activities and manages mature product through phase out.</li>
<li>Provides product information and market exploitation with sales team to ensure maximum market penetration.</li>
<li>Determines sales training needs for the group and develops and directs training plans, programs, and procedures.</li>
<li>Handles customer complains in a timely and resolution manner.</li>
<li>Responsible for customer satisfaction / experience.</li>
<li>Demonstrate a positive and professional behavior, flexibility, and effective communication skills in their interactions with others both internally and externally.</li>
<li>Pro-actively establish effective relationships and support others in achieving high performance.</li>
<li>Demonstrate professional behavior, Influences people rather than demand others.</li>
<li>Responsible for exhibiting professional behavior with both internal and external business associates that reflects positively on the company and is consistent with the company’s policies and practices.</li>
<li>Supervisory Responsibilities: Must have leadership skills that require respect from subordinates, peers, and public at large. Must be proficient at recruiting, training, placing, and retaining personnel. Have delegation and communication skills that ensure success of entire division. Must motivate employees to reach objectives in a professional manner while setting the example for the division. Must set objectives and rules to create and maintain an open and honest environment free form distractions of everyday life. Directly supervises departments that service agricultural customers.</li>
<li>Other duties may be assigned.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p>WHAT YOU'LL NEED</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Master’s degree or three to five years related experience and/or training; or equivalent combination of education and experience. Must have experience in heavy equipment territory.</li>
<li>The ability to read, analyze and interpret documents, procedures, general business periodicals and professional journals.
</li>
<li>Ability to write routine reports and correspondence. Speak effectively before groups of customers or employees of organizations.</li>
<li>The ability to understand, explain and present complex issues to a diverse population.</li>
<li>Ability to make effective and persuasive presentations on complex or controversial topics to a diverse internal or external population.</li>
<li>Ability to solve practical problems and deal with variety of concrete variables in situations where only limited standardization exists.
</li>
<li>Ability to interpret a variety of instructions furnished in written, oral, diagram, or schedule form.</li>
<li>Basic math abilities including addition, subtraction, multiplication and division.</li>
<li>Ability to calculate figures and amounts such as percentages and discounts.</li>
<li>Must be able to quickly apply the above math concepts to practical situations.</li>
<li>Ability to review summary figures, identify trends and analyze impact relative to areas of responsibility.</li>
<li>Knowledge and experience in budgeting and cost/revenue projections.</li>
<li>Ability to independently solve practical problems and deal with a variety of concrete variables.
</li>
<li>Develop and apply a variety of instructions furnished in written and oral form and from multiple sources.
</li>
<li>Must be able to define problems, collect necessary information, establish facts, draw conclusions and take appropriate action.<br>
</li>
</ul>
