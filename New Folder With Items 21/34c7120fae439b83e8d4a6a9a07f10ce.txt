<h2>Business Development Manager - Federal</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Overview</b></p>
<p>Tyto Athene (formerly Black Box Government Solutions) is a full service integrator focused on helping clients accelerate their ability to make decisions by providing ubiquitous and secure access to enterprise information throughout their operating environment. Tyto Athene uses a myriad of technologies, innovative thinking, and proven processes to deliver successful outcomes for its clients worldwide.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>The Business Development Manager Prime responsibility will be focused on expanding and securing new Professional and Managed Services business to new Federal and DOD</p>
<p>customers.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to Identify Qualified Opportunities in the Managed and Professional Services space</li>
<li>Successful candidate will be the Subject Matter Expert in assisting the development of Proposal and Quote response</li>
<li>Knowledge and use of successful contract vehicles in this space is required</li>
<li>Commitment to continuous sales process improvement (spends significant amount of time with customers; travels to see customers; and participates in events involving customers). Develop short and long-term strategies to grow Tyto Athene brand equity.</li>
<li>Build and foster partnerships with manufacturer and integrator team partners;</li>
<li>Develop and cultivate strategic relationships with key stakeholders acting as Tyto Athene’s primary contact. Identify major influencers and decision makers to help drive sustainable long-term partnerships</li>
<li>Provide Customer inputs regarding market conditions, competitor strategies and industry trends that drive real-time feedback and contributes to the solution by articulating Voice of Customer and User.</li>
<li>Fully participate in capture and proposal management process</li>
<li>Utilizes CRM to effectively self-manage activities to insure success with respect to revenue and growth. Responsible for building and managing an active pipeline of new</li>
<li>Professionally represent Tyto Athene at all applicable tradeshows, meetings, trainings, industry associations, public events, etc.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>8 years recent and relevant work experience in selling professional and managed services with specific concentration on voice and data network solutions with a record of sales success within Federal Government;</li>
<li>Knowledge of voice, data, and/or networking solutions/services/ is required</li>
<li>In addition, knowledge of Services in the Security Management, Critical Information Protection, Networks and Cyber Mission IT solutions for select Federal/Civil customers is a plus</li>
<li>Understanding of industry leading partner offerings from demonstrated experience</li>
<li>Proven aptitude for aggressively calling on new customer prospects and building relationships based on trust and integrity;</li>
<li>Knowledge of capture management processes and practices within the Federal space</li>
<li>Understanding of the methods behind Consultative solutions selling in the Federal Market place and focus on Key Decision Makers</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Special Technical Skills/Knowledge Required:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Knowledge of Government contracting requirements as it relates to selling professional and managed services</li>
<li>Ability to develop cost baseline in a competitive environment for customer requests</li>
<li>Proficient with written and verbal communication with, and to, internal and external stakeholders;</li>
<li>Experience leading capture efforts, developing win themes, evaluating win probability, coordinating internal resources, and writing executive summaries;</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal skills, and the ability to work well within all levels of an organization;</li>
<li>Oral Presentation skills; including development using Microsoft related products</li>
<li>Ability to effectively interface with senior command and executive civilian personnel;</li>
<li>Competitive mindset;</li>
<li>Knowledge of Shipley Proposal methods;</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communications skills;</li>
<li>Travel as required – the successful candidate will be in Sales with a broad territory mostly Northern VA/DC based but additional travel will be necessary</li>
<li>Education: BS College or Technical Equivalent/ Years of Service</li>
<li>The successful candidate will be required to submit to a background investigation, and may be required to apply and pass a government security clearance investigation.</li>
<br>
</ul>
