<p><b>Lab/Processing Plant Manager </b></p>
<p><b>Tree of Life Seeds- North Little Rock, AR </b></p>
<p>Tree of Life Seeds, an industry-leading manufacturer and distributor of CBD products, is looking to rapidly expand. This position will be responsible for maintaining the production schedule, meeting production goals and deadlines, managing a team of extraction technicians and support team members as well as maintaining records for compliance and internal reporting.</p>
<p><b>Essential Duties Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul><li>Coordination of technical and support employees at the processing lab facility</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintains and distributes daily tasks/goals to extraction and production departments.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Assists in the development and monitoring of master production schedules within the lean manufacturing environment.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Responsible for developing production plans as required to achieve the sales plan.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ensures unit production plan and production schedule remain clearly defined.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Escalates issues as necessary to ensure that forecasted sales and unit production plans are properly integrated.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Continuous coordination between production and finished goods department.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Works closely with finished goods department to ensure the production plans fulfill customer requirements with the appropriate balance of throughput and inventory requirements.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Applies and monitors safety stock (pars) levels and develops and coordinates the production plans associated with sales initiatives.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Drives Continuous Improvement in all processes within areas of responsibility</li></ul>
<ul><li>Coordinates multiple projects at the same time.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Resolving product issues when they arise in order to protect production goals.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Perform general clerical duties to support and direct extractors and production specialists.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manage day to day tasks within the department.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Orders equipment and supplies as needed.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Performs conversions of starting material to concentrates to finished goods.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Accurately logs and tracks production on a day to day and weekly basis.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Participate in short and long-term planning aligned with goals set by management.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Proficiency in Leaf Logix (or other tracking software)</li></ul>
<ul><li>Create and Implement Good Manufacturing Practices (GMP) procedures and Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs)</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ensure compliance to ISO and NSF Certification(s)</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong managerial skills: Training, mentoring, driving, evaluating performance of the team</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to understand P &amp; L and manage multiple departmental budgets.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to lead an extraction team to production goals.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to help where needed to maintain production schedules</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintains and conducts various Research and Development projects</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ensures OSHA compliance and safety training</li></ul>
<ul><li>Drives and sets examples to all company policies</li></ul>

<ul><li>Strong verbal and written communication skills – works well with others</li></ul>
