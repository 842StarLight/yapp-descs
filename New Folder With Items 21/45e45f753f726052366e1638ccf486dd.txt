<h2>Implementation Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>
<div><b>Job Summary</b></div>
<div>
Manages brand partner and/or internal cross functional teams responsible for delivering defined project outcomes on time and within quality specifications. The Implementation Manager's primary responsibility is to manage and orchestrate the implementation process in order to onboard new brand partners The Implementation Manager plans, organizes, monitors and oversees two or more implementations or projects at a time to meet defined requirements. Reporting directly to the Manager or Director of New Business Implementations they have the responsibility to define, plan and manage projects and new brand partner implementations as assigned.</div>
<div>
Reports to Sr. Manager, EPMO</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
Essential Job Functions</b></div>
<div><div>
<div>
Define and create the project charter, scope and project plan for projects or implementations and drives the overall project progress and quality of the delivery</div>
<div>
Drives the day-to-day coordination of the project efforts while adhering to standards and sponsor expectations to manage the timeline, scope and requirements to meet the project expectations</div>
<div>
Manages the relationship with project stakeholders including internal and brand partners, keeping all informed of progress and issues in order to manage expectations, uses problem-solving, critical thinking and independent thinking skills to overcome challenges and resolve issues and problems</div>
<div>
Organizes work into manageable and logical groups (work breakdown structure) and determines an effective approach to complete the work</div>
<div>
Works effectively with the brand partner's vendors who may support their network, POS, back office systems and web interfaces</div>
<div>
Responsible for identifying conflicts and seeking assistance in resolution to ensure team works well together to meet goals</div>
<div>
Understands and executes all brand partner and vendor contractual agreements to ensure successful implementation of the program, including but not limited to Program Agreements, Purchase and Sale Agreements, Interim Servicing Agreements and vendor statements of work</div>
<div>
Effectively works with all levels, including the brand partner and internal sponsors, which may include delivering status updates to senior level executives</div>
</div></div>
<div><b>
Education/Certifications</b></div>
<div><div>
<div>
Bachelor’s degree in Business, Computer Science or related field; or equivalent education and/or work experience</div>
<div>
PMP Certification or ability to quickly acquire PMP Certification within a year</div>
</div></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
Work Experience</b></div>
<div><div>
<div>
6 to 8 years of related work experience with a minimum of 5 years in a formal project management role following a standard methodology or process</div>
<div>
Ability to utilize project management tools and techniques to create solid project plans and project artifacts</div>
<div>
Experience leading projects and project teams</div>
<div>
3 to 5 years of experience in the credit card industry or other proof of learning and working with diverse and complex project types highly preferred</div>
<div>
2 to 4 years of experience with brand partners and/or external partners</div>
<div>
Proficient in Microsoft Office suite of tools and Microsoft Project</div>
<div>
Demonstrates 4 to 5 PMP Competencies (focusing on Quality, Time, Cost and Stakeholder)</div>
<div>
Demonstrates 5 to 6 of the Alliance Data Leadership Competencies</div>
</div></div>
<div><b>
Knowledge, Skills and Abilities</b></div>
<div><div>
<div>
Ability to create and maintain appropriate project documentation with clarity and quality</div>
<div>
Ability to plan and facilitate meetings with key stakeholders and executive level staff</div>
<div>
Excellent time management skills</div>
<div>
Detail-oriented, able to multi-task and prioritize</div>
<div>
Ability to lead in ambiguous and high pressure situations</div>
<div>
Ability to apply Project Management Principles to projects and implementations</div>
<div>
Understanding of the Strategic and operational issues of the project team and Card Services</div>
<ul>
<li> Strong integration skills and ability to coordinate and lead all aspects of an implementation, or project</li>
<li> Strong leadership and interpersonal skills</li>
<li> Self-starter with strong initiative</li>
<li> High level of skill in project risk identification and execution of mitigation strategies</li>
</ul>
