<h2>Travel Center Supervisor (Twin Arrows Navajo Casino Resort)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div><div>
<p>Under general direction from the Travel Center Manager, the <b>Travel Center Supervisor</b> safeguards the assets of the casino by overseeing the cashiers and Travel Center functions to ensure that monetary transactions are executed for guests in an accurate, professional and courteous manner. Ensures all Travel Center functions operate in accordance with established policies, procedures and gaming regulations.</p>
</div></div>
<div>
<div>Requirements</div>
<div><div>
<p><b>Minimum Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Associates Degree in Business Administration or related field plus <b>three (3) years of experience retail sales to include one (1) year experience of cash handling or customer service, plus one (1) year in a supervisory capacity.</b>
</li>
<li>
Must meet all knowledge, skills and abilities.</li>
<li>
Must be twenty-one (21) years of age.</li>
<li>
Must be able to successfully pass a pre-employment drug/alcohol screen, background investigation, obtain and maintain gaming license.</li>
<li>
Navajo preference.</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
</div>
