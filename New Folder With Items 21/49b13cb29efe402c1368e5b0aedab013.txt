<div><b>Client Portfolio Solutions Analyst</b></div>
<div>
The AIMS Client Portfolio Solutions team is responsible for managing client interactions across the AIMS investment businesses. These responsibilities include identifying and researching prospective investors, analyzing the role of alternative investments in a client’s portfolio, and working with clients and the investment teams to develop unique solutions. Functionally, the Analyst/Associate role supports the team’s client interactions through the development and production of marketing materials, analysis and review of client portfolios, collection and analysis of relevant market data, as well as participation in AIMS strategic initiatives. Client reporting responsibilities include data management and review, preparation of monthly, quarterly and annual client reports, including both quantitative and qualitative information such as performance, diversification, and portfolio holdings. The team works closely with other areas of AIMS and the broader firm including AIMS Risk Management &amp; Analytics, Client Service teams, Fund Accounting/Controllers, Operations and Technology, Legal and Compliance.</div>
