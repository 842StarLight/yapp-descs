<h2>Senior Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Understand and translate statistics to address client business problems</li>
<li>
Structure business problems and design solution</li>
<li>
Develop and implement analytical methodologies, processes, and technological solutions that integrate diverse information solutions and generate analytical insights</li>
<li>
Leverage technology to enhance the competitive position of clients by identifying appropriate solutions and/or products and designs</li>
<li>
Effectively provide consultancy to clients on various aspects of their business using data analytics</li>
<li>
Lead and adhere to project management timelines and quality of delivery, forecast people requirement, other business needs, manage and communicate with stakeholders, ensure that the teams follow operational discipline</li>
<li>
Be a thought leader in the focus area, go to person for internal teams</li>
<li>
Coach &amp; groom the team on gaining knowledge &amp; skills on first principles of analytics techniques, problem solving, project management, client relationship management &amp; team work skills. Conduct regular reviews with team members.</li>
<li>
Actively focus on opportunities to grow the client along with the senior Engagement Managers. Support the sales team when required for RFPs/ pitches</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Qualification &amp; Experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
4+ years of professional work experience in the field of business intelligence and/or Advanced Analytics</li>
<li>
Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills, including the ability to identify root causes and recommend solutions</li>
<li>
Adept in forecasting, regression analysis and segmentation work</li>
<li>
Industry experience in Financial Services/Telecom/Retail/ Computer Software a plus</li>
<li>
Statistical programming software experience in SAS, R or equivalent. Strong SQL skills are required</li>
<li>
Excellent written and verbal communication skills &amp; strong interpersonal skills</li>
<li>
Ability to interact in a fluid, demanding and unstructured environment where priorities evolve constantly and methodologies are regularly challenged</li>
</ul>
