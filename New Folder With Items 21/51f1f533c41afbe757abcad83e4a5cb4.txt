<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>The Job Responsibilities</b></h3>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>
<p>In accordance with Company guidelines and requirements, provides world class customer service in the Draeger Call Center (Triage), supplying products and services to customers in the US. Work hours - Monday - Friday 9:30 AM - 6:00 PM ET.</p>
<ul>
<li>Identify and assess the needs of customers who call the Triage Customer Service Center, asking probing questions as needed. Supply product, pricing and availability information to the customer; enter orders into SAP; and check status of orders. Provide order confirmations with close attention to accuracy and detail. Determine if call should be escalated; complete service tickets for all escalated calls.</li>
<li>Resolve customer complaints, conducting research to determine an appropriate solution. Follow up with customer on a timely basis to ensure effective resolution of the complaint.</li>
<li>Coordinates closely with Credit and Collections to determine credit status of the customer and facilitate order releases as appropriate. Issues order credits and debits as needed.</li>
<li>Follow policies, procedures and guidelines to drive Customer Satisfaction and to ensure compliance with company objectives.</li>
<li>Ensure proper prioritization of orders, providing high priority service for problem solving and other urgent calls. Coordinate with Order Fulfillment for resolution of out of stock items.</li>
<li>Backs up Order Fulfillment and Credit as needed.</li>
<li>Performs other duties as needed and assigned.</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>
<div>
<div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Your Qualifications</b></h3>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>
<p>High school diploma or equivalent combination of education and work experience.</p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum three (3) years customer service experience required; call center experience preferred.</li>
<li>Must be adept at using a computer and specialized software</li>
<li>MS Word and Excel experience required</li>
<li>SAP experience preferred</li>
<li>Must have excellent customer service skills &amp; demeanor</li>
<li>Strong verbal communication skills required</li>
<li>Basic problem solving skills required</li>
<li>Office environment. Must remain at workstation to take calls and process customer requests</li>
</ul>

<p>Our US headquarters located north of Philadelphia in Telford, Pennsylvania is our US manufacturing site for our Neonatal product line supplying many global customers as well as production support for Draeger’s Safety products.
