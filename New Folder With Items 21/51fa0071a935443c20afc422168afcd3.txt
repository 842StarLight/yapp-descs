<p></p>
<p>This is an entry level role and will include on-the-job training for the first 4 weeks of employment. Employment is expected to begin in December.

</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Description Summary
</b></p>Typically SM accounts - Accepts, retains and prices low to moderate exposure new, renewal and endorsement commercial business policies based on granted authority. Provides other underwriting technical support as directed. Proactively partners with agencies and field sales underwriters to consult on and develop business opportunities.
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Description
</b></p>
<p><b>Reporting Relationships: </b>Reports to supervisor or manager; no direct reports.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Core Duties and Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Gathers information from multiple sources according to company guidelines, in order to perform underwriting analysis. May require gathering verbal information or data through reports or other electronic means.
</li>
<li>Reviews individual risks for acceptability and handles those within authority based on company underwriting guidelines and standards. Underwriting analysis may include review of total relationship leveraging all available information pertaining to the book of business.
</li>
<li>Manages workflow and intricacies of underwriting complex accounts utilizing company tools and systems. Ensures file handling quality and workflow efficiency.
</li>
<li>Underwrites new business, renewals and endorsements. May negotiate price and coverage as appropriate to book business. Reviews for acceptability and handles those within authority using company underwriting guidelines and standards.
</li>
<li>Plans completion of assignments to meet agreed upon timelines and service level agreements.
</li>
<li>Consults with agents and internal partners. Understands capabilities and opportunities and drives maximum value of relationships. Identifies and consults on business development opportunities.
</li>
<li>Communicates with agents and other internal and external business partners by requesting and providing requested information.
</li>
<li>Strengthens agency relationships through quality contact on underwriting matters, promoting profit and new business growth, renewal retention and service capabilities. Participates in the management of business performance as part of the underwriting team environment.
</li>
<li>Responds to requests from agents and underwriting staff with resolution within established time and service standards in order to facilitate and expedite service needs. Routes calls and higher level issues to appropriate individuals. May handle escalated customer service issues.
</li>
<li>Educates internal and external partners on underwriting process, practice and tools. May help train less experienced underwriting staff.
</li>
<li>Performs other duties as assigned.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Typical Skills and Experiences:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Education: </b>Bachelor's degree or equivalent work experience preferred.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>License/Certification/Designation: </b>Completion of INS, AU or similar designations is preferred.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Experience: </b>Typically one year of underwriting or equivalent experience preferred. Will consider undergraduate degree in lieu of experience.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Knowledge: </b>Basic commercial underwriting policy, practices and procedures. Rating plans and plan concepts. State and Federal insurance laws and regulations. Commercial insurance processing systems. Working knowledge of company operations and products.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Skills/Competencies: </b>Verbal and written communication skills necessary to interact with all levels of internal and external contacts. Ability to evaluate and recommend solutions to work-related problems. Ability to analyze insurance risks to determine the degree of acceptability and pricing. Effective time management skills and competencies preferred. Ability to operate PC and related software. Ability to advise agents and agency staff on commercial contracts, procedures and business practices.
</p>
