<p><b>About Mirabel Technologies</b></p>

<p><b>Software Consultant: </b></p>
<p>Mirabel Technologies is conducting a highly selective search for a Software Consultant to join our team in Fort Lauderdale, Florida. The Software Consultant will work externally with clients and internally with our technical team to successfully implement and train clients on our SaaS products. This is a perfect opportunity for a person that is two to four years into their career. In addition to the initial implementation and training, the Software Consultant will be responsible for the ongoing education of system enhancements through webinars and Go-to-Meetings. The ultimate goal is to take clients from contract signing to go-live (and beyond) â€“ and to ensure that clients are avid users and raving fans of our Mirabel products! Opportunities to travel to conduct onsite implementation and training for larger clients is available. A successful candidate will be technically savvy (able to grasp technical concepts of web-based software and integration quickly), be an excellent communicator, possess the ability to effectively transfer knowledge in a consultative manner, be customer-focused and operate with a sense of urgency.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Complete an accelerated training and qualification program</li>
<li>Coordinate installation and training schedules with clients</li>
<li>Train client end-user personnel in the utilization of the software product</li>
<li>Configure software to provide the options and features requested; including customized reports and services</li>
<li>Act as liaison between the client and the Mirabel Technologies technical staff and senior leadership</li>
<li>Document, track and monitor problems to ensure timely resolution</li>
<li>Travel onsite as needed to conduct client training</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications &amp; Experience: </b></p>

<li>Strong writing, telecommunications, and public speaking skills</li>
<li>2-year degree or equivalent experience in Computer science, IT, Marketing/Sales</li>
<li>Minimum 2 years in a software (preferably SaaS) implementation/ training role</li>
<li>A high level of customer interaction as a software trainer via remote tools like WebEx or Go to Meeting and Face to Face</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to work well with customers and resolve customer issues</li>
<li>Strong sense of ownership/urgency. Self-starter with ability to work with minimal supervision support capacity</li>
<li>Highly organized with the ability to work in a fast-paced environment.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Pluses: </b></p>
