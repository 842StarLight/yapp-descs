<h2>Marketing Review Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Position Description
<br><br>
SEI is looking for a Marketing Review Analyst to join the legal department. The Marketing Review Analyst will be part of a team who provides compliance marketing review services to SEI’s distribution clients, mainly related to mutual funds and exchange-traded funds. The analyst will also serve as the primary contact with FINRA regarding filings. The position provides the individual with a comprehensive understanding of SEC and FINRA regulation and exposure to a broad array of issues within the legal team and which impact the company and our clients. The marketing review analyst will be a subject matter expert in the issues that affect clients’ investment products and solutions through multiple distribution channels.
<br><br>
<b>What you'll do:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Reviewing marketing communications for clients, such as advertising, sales support materials, client communications, websites and social media.</li>
<li>Staying abreast of changes to laws and regulations affecting the management, operations, and product offering of the company and clients</li>
<li>Coordinating with legal counsel and business unit to ensure adherence to laws and regulations relating to new or revised products and service offerings</li>
<li>Coordinating issues and projects with other teams as required</li>
<li>Participating in client and business unit initiatives and projects</li>
<li>Keeping abreast of industry standards and knowledge to be able to communicate with both internal and external clients</li>
<li>Interacting effectively with clients and other SEI teams</li>
<li>Provide support to clients in various time zones</li>
</ul>
<b>What you bring to the table:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>BA/BS degree in business, finance or a related field; or the equivalent in education and work experience</li>
<li>A minimum of 5 years related industry experience</li>
<li>FINRA Series 7 and 24 registrations</li>
<li>Experience with marketing materials in financial services and knowledge of investment products or legal/regulatory environment</li>
<li>Minimal travel (&lt;20%) may be required</li>
</ul>
<b>Attributes we value:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Excellent communication and negotiation skills</li>
<li>Must be willing to work effectively independently, as well as in a team, in an open floor environment</li>
</ul>
