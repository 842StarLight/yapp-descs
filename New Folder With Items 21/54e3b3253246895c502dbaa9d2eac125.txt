<b>Description</b>
<p>As a <b>Mentor Recruiter</b> with<br>
California MENTOR Family Home Agency, you’ll be one of those extraordinary<br>
people. We<br>
seek a passionate, proven recruiter who<br>
can successfully identify families in the Burbank, Glendale, Pasadena and Los Angeles area to become adult foster care<br>
providers, who we call “Mentors.” Bring your creative ideas and your<br>
passion for success; we’ll train you on the rest. We’re the leader across<br>
California in matching adults with developmental disabilities with amazing<br>
families who want to make a difference.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Do you have experience working in sales<br>
or recruitment and you’re seeking more meaning in your work life? Leverage your skills to make an impact in<br>
people’s lives. Build and enhance an<br>
active network of family homes through the strategic recruitment of qualified<br>
Mentors and guide them through the certification process. Join our dedicated,<br>
dynamic and fast growing team today!</p>

</li>

<br>

<li>Review applications, medical examinations and<br>
