<h2>Global Salesforce CRM Program Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>The Salesforce CRM Program Manager is responsible for facilitating discovery, translating business requirements into well-designed solutions, leading business process and technical design sessions, and delivering and documenting solutions aligned with business objectives. The individual is an advocate for new features and functionality as they are released and acts as the subject matter expert for the Salesforce platform.
<br>
<br>
The Salesforce CRM Program Manager breaks down large-scale initiatives to manageable workstreams and will play the role of liaison between the customer and various technical teams to deliver solutions to highly ambiguous and complex problems.
<p></p>
<p><b>PRINCIPAL RESPONSIBILITIES AND DUTIES:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Initiate and lead discussions with business partners to gather, analyze, prioritize, and refine business requirements
</li>
<li>Create business analysis documentation including user stories, usage scenarios, process flows, data dictionaries, requirements traceability matrices, design documents, and project schedules
</li>
<li>Work directly, in a hands-on way, with the system/data, lead multiple technical projects or portions at one time, as well as provide solutions that are highly usable, scalable, extensible, and maintainable
</li>
<li>Create and maintain objects, fields, field sets, record types, page layouts, permission sets, approval processes, user roles, profiles, groups, sharing rules, workflows, and validation rules
</li>
<li>Integrate Salesforce with other enterprise systems, such as ERPs, BI, and Outlook, etc.
</li>
<li>Perform data cleansing, data uploads and data exports
</li>
<li>Develop and create customized reports and dashboards
</li>
<li>Produce training materials and lead training sessions
</li>
<li>Perform all other duties as assigned.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>EDUCATION:
</b></p>
<p>Bachelor’s degree or equivalent experience. MBA is a plus
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>MANAGEMENT: </b>This position has no direct reports
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>EXPERIENCE AND REQUIRED SKILLS:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>10+ years of experience leading delivery of large enterprise software solutions. Consulting experience is a plus.
</li>
<li>5+ years of experience leading delivery of Salesforce-specific programs
</li>
<li>Strong knowledge of Salesforce.com – sales, service, and marketing clouds – particularly Service Cloud and Sales Cloud. Experience with Partner Community and Customer Community, as well as sales analytics tools, e.g. Einstein.
</li>
<li>Solid understanding and knowledge of Sales and Marketing Operations
</li>
<li>Solid understanding and knowledge of SFDC including configuration, data and governance, and can articulate these to users and technical resources
</li>
<li>Proven ability to design and optimize business processes and integrate processes across disparate systems
</li>
<li>Strong verbal/written communication and data presentation skills, including an ability to effectively communicate with both business and technical teams
</li>
<li>Results-oriented person with a delivery focus and strong organizational skills
</li>
<li>Certified Salesforce.com Administrator (ADM-201), Advanced
</li>
</ul>
