<div><b>
Job Summary:</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Reputation.com is looking for an experienced Customer Success leader to optimize the overall customer experience by defining and driving the overall strategy to build a sustainable, customer-obsessed culture. The Sr. Director of Customer Success will collect and maintain a rich set of customer data, issues, feedback and insights to empower all Reputation.com associates to adopt a customer-centric approach in their day to day and strategic activities.</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Set strategy and goals for the global Customer Success team and manage the team to meet and exceed these goals through a continual improvement methodology.</li>
<li>
Define the metrics and success criteria for our Customer Success function, owning outcomes inclusive of Customer Satisfaction, increased adoption and renewals and operational efficiency.</li>
<li>
Provide real-time guidance to the executive and management teams regarding customer status including wins to celebrate and potential issues to be addressed.</li>
<li>
Build effective relationships across Reputation.com's Executives and stakeholders.</li>
<li>
Develop success plans for customers that outline their desired outcomes, critical success factors, metrics for success, potential issues, and recommendations.</li>
<li>
Coach, train and develop the team into top performers through goal setting, planning, and frequent one-on-one interaction</li>
<li>
Attract and retain high potential individual contributors to the team.</li>
<li>
Lead Critical Incident Management function and serve as the executive escalation point for customer escalations.</li>
<li>
Interpret and provide recommendations around the results of the Reputation.com platform and services and continually confirm our customers are achieving their desired business outcomes.</li>
<li>
Lead the customer executive sponsorship program.</li>
<li>
Lead change initiatives, develop staffing plan, and identify, implement, and manage tools related to superior customer services</li>
<li>
Build strong relationships with Senior Executives (C-level, VP and Director) of Reputation.com customers.</li>
<li>
Develop processes and playbooks that will delight customers in a repeatable and scalable way.</li>
<li>
Be the voice of the customer within Reputation.com to influence product direction and other strategic decisions.</li>
<li>
Collaborate across the organization with Sales, Marketing, Product, and Engineering to address key churn risks and increase customer retention and satisfaction.</li>
<li>
Systematically monitor client health and create predictive models for at-risk customers.</li>
<li>
Upsell clients to purchase more robust product functionality and where possible, expand their current solutions.</li>
<li>
Map customer journey &amp; define segmentation of the customer base and varying strategies for success based on this journey and segmentation.</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
