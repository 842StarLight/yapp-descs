<h2>Associate Manager, Product Marketing</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><div><div>
<div>Metagenics is a global lifestyle medicine company, offering products and programs exclusively to health care practitioners. For over 30 years, we have been driven by a scientific concept: How the right nutrition can help people realize their best health possible. We don't compromise on safety, efficacy, or quality - to make sure that we deliver the best results possible. Time after time. Bottle after bottle. We carefully monitor all products to assure purity and safety, from formulation and ingredient selection all the way to the final product. We offer more educational support for health care professionals than anyone else in our field - keeping them in touch with the latest research and nutritional approaches to help manage today’s most common illnesses. Metagenics is dedicated to helping people live a happier, healthier life by realizing their genetic potential.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Position Overview:</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The Associate Manager, Product Marketing works with the Product Marketing Manager(s) to assist in growing the sales and profitability of a specific category or categories of products in the Metagenics product line. The Associate Manager, Product Marketing assists in in creating, coordinating and executing the strategies and tactics for product development, marketing and sales of the products in the portfolio. This position is involved in a broad range of marketing activities including market research, market analysis, planning, scheduling, budgeting, product development, advertising, sales support, PR and communications activities for the product categories. The Associate Manager, Product Marketing will assist in specific project responsibilities and products within categories, under the direction of the Product Marketing Manager(s) and Directory of Product Management.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
This role requires strong partnering with functional leads and key stakeholders in Marketing, Regulatory, R&amp;D, Sales, and several other technical departments to drive product development. Must have the ability to juggle multiple projects of varying scope under tight deadlines.</div>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div><b>What you'll do:</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Assist in developing the strategy for the product group in support of the corporate strategic objectives and marketing strategy</li>
<li>
Strategize in developing the appropriate tactical plans necessary to execute the product strategy</li>
<li>
Manage specific projects and products in specific groups or categories, including assisting in market research, marketing analysis, planning, positioning, budgeting, product development, collateral development, advertising, PR, product training</li>
<li>
Work with Product Marketing Managers to conduct market research on a product management to determine trends, opportunities, create product positioning and pricing, create forecasts, understand the life cycle of the products in the category and develop marketing programs</li>
<li>
Work with the sales force to develop competitive product analysis, materials, strategies and marketing programs to help combat the competition</li>
<li>
Work with Product Marketing Manager assisting in overseeing specific products to plan, measure and manage the overall advertising, PR, communications and collateral literature production</li>
<li>
Work with Product Marketing Manager to help with budgeting responsibilities for the various marketing areas relative to the categories involved to ensure that overall profitability requirements are met</li>
<li>
Work with the Product Marketing Manager and R&amp;D to support the creation of new products in the line, to stay ahead of the competition and provide the customer with the best in health care nutrition</li>
<li>
Collaborate with R&amp;D and to offer marketing guidance for product development and product improvements for the products in the group</li>
<li>
Contribute to the execution of the marketing plans as assigned efficiently; on time and coordinated with new product development, so as to optimize sales of all products and services</li>
</ul>
