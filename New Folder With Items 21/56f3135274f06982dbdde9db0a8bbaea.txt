<h2>Accounting Administrative Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Job Description<br>
<br>
<p>We are currently looking for a unique skill set to fill an <b>Accounting Administrative Assistant</b> role. This position involves assisting the accounting department, taking customer service calls and compiling sales data for reporting. Ideal candidates for this role will be customer oriented, have some basic accounting experience and will be advanced in the Microsoft Office suite (Outlook, Word, Excel).</p>
<p><b>
What you will be doing:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Assisting the accounting department with varies tasks and projects</li>
<li>
Assist customers with product issues including basic troubleshooting and software issues</li>
<li>
Provide order assistance to customers over the telephone and through email</li>
<li>
Run sales reporting and analyze/manipulate the sales report data</li>
</ul>
</div>
<br>
<div>Qualifications<br>
<br>
<p><b>Job Qualifications and Skills:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
3+ years of hands on administrative or business support experience</li>
<li>
1 year of experience in an accounting related field</li>
<li>
Strong computer skills including Microsoft Office, must be advanced in Excel</li>
<li>
Ability to perform in a high-energy high-expectation environment</li>
<li>
Strong attention to detail and work ethic</li>
<li>
Excellent communication (both written and verbal) and interpersonal skills</li>
</ul>
<br>
Additional Information<br>
