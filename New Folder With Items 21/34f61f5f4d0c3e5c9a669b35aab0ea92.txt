<h2>Field Systems Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<p>Location:
</p>
<div>Chicago, Illinois</div>
</div>
<div>
<p>The Field System Engineer (FSE) is responsible for the proper performance of all information technology based applications and systems within the stadium/arena environment. The focus is on ensuring the point of sale (POS) systems in the respective location maintains maximum uptime. The FSE also acts as a liaison between the Home Office Information Systems teams, vendors, partner IT contacts, and all other Levy Restaurants departments and personnel to ensure the systems at the location are functioning properly as required and technical assistance is provided for their locations and team members in a timely fashion. The FSE will report to the Regional Information Technology Manager (RITM) and will participate in regularly scheduled conference calls and/or meetings.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Key Job Responsibilities</b><br>
<b>
</b><br>
</p>
<ul><li> Provide Level 1 and 2 support for stadium specific applications including POS, retail and catering software and hardware (e.g. hand-held terminals touch screens, digital menu boards, etc). Diagnose issues and ensure they are addressed.</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>Provide event based support and assistance at in stadium/arena or similar environment and act as the escalation point for the support of all IT related systems</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Serve as primary contact for systems support and Information Technology related inquiries within their region</li>
<li>
Coordinate system and application training through the Home Office IT team with vendors or internal resources as requested</li>
<li>
Consistently follow Information Technology service priorities to ensure the best support and communication to their locations, regional support teams and the Home Office</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Coordinate on-site support for location events as requested/needed</li>
<li>Ensure all technical support issues are logged in through Levy ticketing system</li>
<li>Maintain inventory counts of all field based systems by location and service offering within their respective region</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Ensure systems and servers are properly functioning, on a consistent version of software, and running in a stable environment</li>
<li>Assist in the planning, coordination and execution of system installations and upgrades</li>
<li>Develop hands-on, in-depth knowledge of products and maintain technical and operational analysis of strengths and weaknesses</li>
<li>Provide technical support and act as an escalation point for local IT support (local IT managers, or hourly IT supervisors/technicians)</li>
<li>Lead and follow up with vendor resources, on-site support, or location personnel teams while working on any given project</li>
</ul>
<p>Ideal Candidate</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
<br>
 BS/MS in Computer Science or related field preferred; or equivalent combination of education and experience, equivalent skill</li>
<li> Minimum of 2 years’ experience running/supporting POS systems in the restaurant/hospitality/event based business.</li>
<li> Solid understanding of networking concepts and troubleshooting including TCP/IP, VLAN’s, wireless LANs</li>
<li> Eagerness to work in a rapidly changing, diverse environment and a willingness to accept ownership and responsibility of the IT infrastructure to ensure its 24x7 availability.</li>
<li> Well organized, able to handle multiple tasks at the same time independently without close supervision</li>
<li> Excellent written and verbal communication skills
</li>
<li> Travel Required – up to 50%</li>
<li> Able to analyze issues and problems with escalation plans as needed</li>
<li> Quest/Micros/Appetize experience is preferred.</li>
</ul>
