<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Summary:</b></h3>
<p>
As an Inside Sales Representative <b>(a.k.a. </b><b>Financial Technology </b><b>Advisor)</b><b> </b>you are the heart of our sales teams and the liaison between Marketing and Sales focusing on Business Development. You will be responsible for two main things:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Manage HighRadius’ inbound leads from a global perspective.</li>
<li>Profile accounts and build highly customized messages to penetrate new accounts with the goal of setting up qualified meetings for HighRadius teams.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>In other words....</b><i>"You will have the ability to connect to multi-million dollar business to business (B2B) companies both on the phone at events, to listen to the prospect's pain points or current situation, and advise which of HighRadius solutions would be the best fit for their needs."</i></p>
<p><i>
~Current Financial Technology Advisor</i></p>
