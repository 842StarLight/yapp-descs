<h2>Loan Processor II</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>To act as a representative of SCCU and provides exceptional quality service for members; to prepare/transmit all approved loans originated through the Express Services function and ensure a timely closing in order to exceed member expectations; to support all sales efforts of the Express Services staff.</b></p>
<p><b>Principal Duties and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Prepares the loan for closing and transmits the closing checklist to the Branch and ensures all closing documents are sent to the member via Docusign, in order to guarantee the timely receipt of all required loan documents or the checklist.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Ensures that Express Services loan approval stipulations are met prior to loan closing and that coversheets are transmitted in order to meet established time standards.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Resolves member problems, obtains information, researches records, follows up on details, assesses member needs timely and efficiently and reports the results to the inquiring party to develop and sustain profitable member relationship.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Provides timely response to branch questions concerning loan documents; ensures prompt response to member calls by being in the call queue; prepares corrections as necessary in order to provide branch and member support and ensure accurate loan documentation.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Adheres to established productivity and loan quality standards to meet quality service standards.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Supports the sales efforts of all Express Services Associate staff by providing the member with appropriate product and service materials in order to enhance the overall SCCU cooperative/member relationship.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Attendance and punctuality are essential functions of this job.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Requirements:</p>
<ul>
<li>1-3 years prior experience in consumer lending or mortgage loan experience, with emphasis in loan processing/closing functions.</li>
<li>Ability to communicate clearly and effectively, both verbally and in writing, o ensure complete understanding of the intent and content of the communication.
</li>
<li>Ability to prioritize and organize job tasks.</li>
<li>Ability to quickly master and utilize software applications, including Spectrum and C32.</li>
<li>Ability to perform multiple tasks simultaneously while under strict time deadlines without sacrificing quality of output.</li>
<li>Ability to carry out detailed written and oral instructions.</li>
<li>Demonstrated knowledge of loan systems and Microsoft software such as Word and Excel.</li>
<li>Knowledge of consumer and mortgage lending, criteria, products, rates, processes, procedures, policies, and regulations.</li>
<li>Knowledge of government regulations and compliance issues.</li>
</ul>

</ul>
