<h2>Associate Software Developer Co-op</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>OVERVIEW</b><p>
At Sentry, we realize that getting valuable, hands-on experience is important for you and your career. We offer a great opportunity to partner your ongoing education with a highly successful and well compensated job all while actively enrolled in college. Our co-op program is designed to provide you the opportunity to gain valuable experience in your major while working in our downtown Stevens Point location.</p>
<b>
WHAT YOU'LL DO</b><p>
As a member of Sentry’s Stevens Point IT Co-op program you’ll be part of an Agile/Scrum team responsible for finding new and exciting ways to apply technology and creative technical solutions to Sentry’s systems.</p>
<div>
You Will<br>
<ul>
<li>Work hand-in hand with industry professionals on systems/projects across Sentry (there are lots)</li>
<li>Define and design systems including UI layouts, database structures, design methodologies</li>
<li>Provide interactive feedback on designs, testing, maintenance, and technical approaches</li>
<li>Participate in code-reviews with experienced software engineers</li>
<li>Build technical skills in advance and/or beyond what you receive in general coursework</li>
</ul>
<br>
</div>
<b>
WHAT IT TAKES</b><ul>
<li>Are a current full-time student pursuing an undergraduate degree</li>
<li>Majoring in Computer Information Systems, Web and Digital Media Development, or a related field</li>
<li>Have a desire to learn new technologies</li>
<li>Have exposure to or experience with Java or .NET Framework programming</li>
<li>Have exposure to or experience with data structures &amp; algorithms and/or web design &amp; development</li>
<li>Have the ability to work between 12-19 hours per week during the school year with a minimum of 2-3 hour increments</li>
<li>Have the ability to work 40 hours per week in the summer</li>
<li>Our Co-Op provides opportunities for associates to meet challenges head-on. As such, our top candidates are curious, hardworking and always looking to improve. Our Co-Op students typically possess and maintain a GPA of 2.70 or higher</li>
<li>Sentry does not offer employment in this program to holders of F-1, J-1, and H-1 Visas for the purpose of obtaining practical experience</li>
</ul>
