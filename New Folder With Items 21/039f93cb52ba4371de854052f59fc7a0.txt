<h2>Construction/ Flooring Estimator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Baker’s is not just a place to work! When you join us, you become an extended member of the Baker family. We offer a dynamic, collaborative environment and build enduring relationships, as we innovate our business and support professional growth. We are not just a specialty architectural finish company, we are a family that takes care of each other, the community, and our clients. We are expanding our team and looking for an experienced Estimator to help us realize our short, mid, and long term goals.</p>
<p><b>Is this role for you?</b></p>
<p>The Estimator supports the Operation team communicating with the client, understanding the scope of the project and the planning / execution side in our maintenance division. You must be great with numbers, have expert computer skills, extraordinary attention to detail and accuracy. You excel at the skills needed to prepare and submit bids for projects, present solutions and pricing for potential customers, and manage commercial projects from inception to completion.</p>
<p>Your excellent communication skills and time management helps keep the technicians on task, the clients happy, and the internal team informed. You thrive on fast paced, ever changing priorities and put customer service at the top – always. This could be a perfect opportunity for you if you are hungry, humble, and looking to grow and expand your skillset and career.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Using proposal templates, develop them to support the services we provide, prepare and submit estimates per client specifications.</li>
<li>Submit and enter proposals into our CRM/ Project Management software – SimPro.</li>
<li>Manage projects to ensure installers are completing quality work per specs and timeline.</li>
<li>Track and manage customer interactions, recording all aspects of the sales and proposal process up to and including closing the sale.</li>
<li>Maintain a professional, ethical, and positive disposition as a primary representative of the company in the community.</li>
<li>Successfully manage a tremendous volume of work, moving quickly and accurately to support the business growth and excellence in work.</li>
</ul>
<p>· Excel at balancing in-depth project knowledge and administrative oversight, all while communicating with subcontractors, clients and owners. Involved in the day-to-day operation of the estimating/quoting, material ordering, material dispatch and delivery, installation scheduling, coordinating of all documentation, subcontractor and project needs, and job follow-up with the customer.</p>
<p>· Communicate with site project managers, technicians, and architects, with a working knowledge of re- such as plumbing that can effect flooring and maintenance estimates</p>
<p><b>Qualifications and Requirements: </b></p>
<p>· Ability to read Construction Blue Prints, architectural plans and Spec Books.</p>
<p>· Balance in-field measuring and desk top take-offs to find and determine the scope of the project.</p>
<p>· Excels at prioritization, working independently, and under deadline with exceptional accuracy</p>
<p>· Strong computer skills, and working proficiency in MS Office and Project Management Software. Very strong Excel skills/ formula’s</p>
<p>· Proven high level of efficiency, attention to detail and exceptional organizational skills</p>
<p>· Meticulous attention to detail in reviewing, updating, and collecting information</p>
<p>· A proactive approach to anticipating and solving problems. You are willing to think beyond the normal and are a driven go-getter who brings energy, enthusiasm with unshakable honesty and integrity.</p>
<p>· Experience/ Knowledge with BlueBeam Revu or another OST is helpful.</p>
<p>· Experience in commercial flooring and/or maintenance is a big plus</p>
<p>· Able to work in a fast-paced environment with a demonstrated ability to juggle multiple competing tasks and demands.</p>
<p>· Prior experience in an estimating role is a plus</p>
<p>· Bachelor’s degree (preferred) or equivalent combination of education and/or experience</p>
<p><b>Personal Attributes: </b></p>
<p>· Solutions and results oriented</p>
<p>· Punctual, polished, and professional</p>
<p>· Reliable and has sound judgement and discretion</p>
<p>· System and process oriented</p>
<p>· Advanced knowledge of MS Office</p>
<p>· Strong interpersonal skills and relationship builder both internally and externally</p>
<p>· Positive attitude is a must; a good sense of humor is good too</p>
