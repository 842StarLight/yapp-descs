<h2>Business Solutions Representative (FT)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<b>We are looking for...</b><br>
A Business Solutions Specialist, to join our team to generate, develop and close new b2b business within an assigned territory or named account list.<br>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>What We Do…</b></div>
<div>
Akucast is a high-tech software company that develops and sells a Sales Forecasting and Pipeline Management app for the Salesforce platform. Typical customers are in the tech industry and mid to large in size.<br>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>Being Successful In The Role…</b></div>
<div>
Sales cycles are 2-4 months so a BSR that knows how to develop relationships and nurture them thru this time period is crucial to being successful. Being organized and prioritizing quality over quantity is a must.<br>
<br>
</div>
<div>Listening is key to this position. Understanding, digesting, and aligning product fit with customer problems is key to happy customers and a rapid close rate. This is more of a solution sale rather than a quick transactional sale.<br>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>You are...</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Friendly and can carry a conversation.</li>
<li>
You listen more than you talk.</li>
<li>
You’re more interested in developing relationships than acquaintances.</li>
<li>
At a party or networking event, you’re comfortable striking up a conversation with a complete stranger.</li>
<li>
You are a natural teacher, someone who likes to help others learn something new.</li>
<li>
Hunter-gatherer-farmer, you’re all of them but mostly a gatherer.</li>
<li>
An excellent communicator with a high emotional IQ. You strive to be articulate and empathetic in your interactions. You have a way with words—you can write a great email and carry on a good conversation.</li>
<li>
You are a team player who values outcomes over effort—you are someone who is driven to succeed both as an individual but also as a team.</li>
<li>
You care about quality, wanting to get the job done as well as you can. Hungry to learn, genuinely curious and know how to get the answers to your questions.</li>
<li>
Gutsy and self-directed when it comes to problem-solving and working on multiple projects at once. You're looking to work with a team of high performers where you'll be responsible for the impact you make and the freedom to explore your creativity. Working with folks in different time zones will be standard issue for you around here, so being able to stay determined, even when the answers aren't in front of you, is essential.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
Meaningful work you'll contribute</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Create new sales opportunities via cold calling &amp; inbound lead follow-up.</li>
<li>
Take those prospects, nurture them, and close them for a win.</li>
<li>
Research firms that fit Akucast’s ideal customer profile (ICP) in your assigned territory.</li>
<li>
Speak with key executives/buyer personas/positions to create and develop an interest in Akucast.</li>
<li>
Nurture and close opportunities.</li>
<li>
Responsible for pipeline growth and forecast accuracy in accordance with expectations set by sales leadership.</li>
<li>
Conduct high-level Akucast product demo’s via Zoom or Google Meet and demonstrates how Akucast can solve customer pain points</li>
</ul>
<div>
</div>
<div><b>
Valuable experiences and skills you'll bring to Akucast…</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
4-year University degree (any discipline) is greatly preferred.</li>
<li>
Experience with Salesforce is a huge plus but not required.</li>
<li>
Open to learning and coaching and can take constructive criticism and turn it into positive outcomes.</li>
<li>
Strong and articulate telephone personality and strong ability to create rapport over phone, email, social media, and online chat.</li>
</ul>
