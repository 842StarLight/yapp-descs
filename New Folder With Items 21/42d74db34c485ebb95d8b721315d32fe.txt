<br>
<div>Oracle is a founder of the Information Technology industry, and is bringing new tools and technologies to market that leverage data to disrupt and transform how the world works. Oracle Cloud Business Group is seeking a dynamic team-player to help showcase compelling stories of change and innovation with our newest products and our most exciting customers.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>Aligned to Oracle Cloud Infrastructure and Oracle Analytics, Big Data, Data Science the Customer Reference Manager will work through an extended global team to identify, develop, and grow a list of stories where our customer's employees are the real heroes. This role requires a highly motivated, organized individual with a good eye and a good ear for story, who can curate a community of diverse customers across a wide range of industries and from organizations large and small. These customer advocates are invited to participate in a range of co-marketing activities that highlight their successes, such as advertising, print and digital storytelling, videos and in-person events, as well as involvement in key networking opportunities.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>The Customer Reference Manager works closely with Oracle's various content and event teams to execute on this. Coordinating efforts across a global team, and working closely with a variety of internal stakeholders, this individual will also be responsible for identifying gaps versus goals and to track and report on overall progress against objectives to those stakeholders. Prior experience in Customer Marketing, Referencing, PR, or Communications is a plus.</div>
<br>
<div>
</div>
<div>
The ideal candidate will possess both a business background that enables to engage at the CXO level as well as a sales background that allows to easily interact with enterprise customers and sales/field reps. He/she should also have a demonstrated ability to think strategically about business, product, and technical challenges, with the ability to manage customer engagements strategies at a worldwide level.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
 <div><b>Detailed Description and Job Requirements:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Define, build and execute a detailed plan that delivers a steady stream of customer success stories and references to support awareness, demand generation and sales cycles. The success of this role means higher customer satisfaction, retention and expansion of Oracle business.</li>
<li>
Manage relationship with customer stakeholders to ensure they are leveraging our solution to achieve maximum business value. Acquire a deep knowledge of our customer base, existing customer story content, mine and use that content to increase its effectiveness, and manage that content to increase usability and visibility.
</li>
<li>Understand and support the needs of internal and external stakeholders, and communicate their needs.
</li>
<li>Match sales reference requests to appropriate references in a consultative model. Manage relationship with accounts to speak to press and analysts, take sales reference calls, and participate in marketing activities (case studies, awards, videos, reference forums, speaking opportunities) Job duties are varied and complex utilizing independent judgment.
</li>
<li>Minimum 5+ years related experience in a business, marketing, or sales role</li>
<li>
Moderate technical and strategic understanding of Technology, Applications, and Industry solutions being marketed. Sales and Marketing experience in the IT industry. E-business + Internet practical know-how.
</li>
</ul>
