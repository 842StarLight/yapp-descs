<h2>Field Learning Consultant - Remote Southeast</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Summary:</b> Leadership responsibility for the strategic and tactical field training agenda for Denny’s supporting 400+ restaurants. This role has a direct responsibility for influencing the growth and profitability of the region through the training, onboarding, development and coaching processes. This critical role will collaborate with the Learning teams and business leaders to build and implement programs, processes, with measurable results related to: training effectiveness, culture change, learning and development, career growth, performance management, and leader &amp; employee development. Reporting directly to the Director of Field Learning and Development, this role will have significant impact transforming the organization toward a culture of accountability and coaching. This leader must be a highly effective, influential change agent, with a passion for innovation and creativity in designing and implementing solutions that develop talent and increase organizational performance for the Denny’s of today and tomorrow. The is a critical position responsible for setting regional learning strategies, executing learning programs, leading flagship and approved training restaurants, leading all field module rollouts, and all field-training programs with exceptional quality and consistency. With responsibility of over 400 restaurants and cultivating multiple franchise owner relationships, the ability to build relationships is a mandatory skill. Provide guidance and strategic support so that priorities that are best for moving the brand forward are identified and appropriate goals are set among all stakeholders.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Functions (Key Responsibilities)</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Responsible for the development and execution of L&amp;D regional strategies to achieve sales growth and profitability through consistent execution in serving high-quality products and providing a differentiated guest service experience.</li>
<li>
Provide input to the creation of Regional goals to minimize or eliminate goals that conflict with the strategy and objectives of the Denny’s of tomorrow.</li>
<li>
Analyze and apply this information in the development and execution of strategies and determine learning solutions needed to transform formal and informal learning into a performance advantage for the organization.</li>
<li>
Partner with other training and operations colleagues as a consultant and coach to provide insights, best practices, and cross collaboration to ensure best in class enterprise-wide learning initiatives.</li>
<li>
Accelerate organizational performance through leadership – linking strategy to development, building transformational change approaches and facilitating complex organizational challenges.</li>
<li>
Create and align training goals with the Company’s strategic and annual operating plans; work closely with Regional Operations Director to improve performance of all under performing markets.</li>
<li>
Conduct necessary review of existing practices and systems to identify points of leverage for training objectives, and to remove/minimize existing barriers to training implementation and impact.</li>
<li>
Oversee the development of all learning and development programs in the region.</li>
<li>
Act as a leadership role model while consistently exhibiting Denny’s Vision, Mission and Guiding Principles.</li>
<li>
Demonstrate a competitive spirit and “hungry to win” approach by creating training and leadership that serves as a competitive edge.</li>
<li>
Has a joint responsibility with the Regional Operations Director to identify, nurture, develop and support Approved Training Restaurants, inclusive of their General Managers, Management teams and team members.</li>
<li>
Demonstrate the value of training to the bottom line by showing return on training investment and direct impact to key performance indicators through continuous measurement.</li>
<li>
Measure impact of executed training strategy against company dashboard of key performance indicators.</li>
<li>
Responsible for the successful execution of the Menu Gold Standard Training within the respective region.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with Regional Director to drive behavior to positively influence the financial and guest satisfaction tracking systems, and fully utilize the data to drive improvements and engagement.</li>
<li>
Advance the training initiatives to the priority list for the organization by providing a strong connection of training to key outcomes.</li>
<li>
In collaboration with Regional Directors, ensure that all initiatives are properly executed through thorough planning, all-inclusive training solutions, and extensive follow up.</li>
<li>
Influence and engage the Regional Director and franchise owners at a high enough level to insure that they actively support and successfully execute all learning programs.</li>
<li>
Demonstrate ownership of field learning programs to ensure field-learning programs address necessary benefits to the business and realign as necessary.</li>
<li>
Takes ownership of guest satisfaction tracking system and fully utilize the data to drive improvements and engagement.</li>
<li>
Responsible for teaching, coaching and driving behaviors around the three operational tenants: Breakthrough Leadership, The Denny’s Way, and Share the Love.</li>
<li>
Role model leadership practices that influence and inspire engagement and performance, such as development and performance coaching; hold self and others accountable for results and actions.</li>
<li>
Other duties as assigned<br>
</li>
</ul>
