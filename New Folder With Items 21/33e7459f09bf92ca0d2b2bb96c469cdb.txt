<h2>Office Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div><div><div><div><div>
<p>Our client in the Brattleboro area is looking for an <b>Office Assistant</b> to join their team! In this role you will provide administrative support to the Director of Sales and Sales Manager as well perform sales support functions.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Process quotes as necessary; process incoming orders, make copies of prints and quotes and distribute to proper personnel</li>
<li>Compose and type correspondence from outlined information received verbally or from written data</li>
<li>Establish and maintain files; have material available as required</li>
<li>Correspond with customers</li>
<li>Make and schedule appointments, travel arrangements, etc.</li>
<li>Prepare, maintain and distribute reports or other data as required</li>
<li>Maintain area, records and files in neat, orderly condition</li>
<li>Observe all company policies, rules and regulations including good housekeeping, safety and security</li>
<li>Perform other duties as required, assigned or directed</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Knowledge of standard clerical procedures - typing, filing, distribution, computer usage and office software</li>
<li>Able to fill out and maintain standard company forms</li>
<li>Ability to operate office equipment</li>
<li>Must act on own initiative, use judgment in clerical functions</li>
<li>Excellent communication and interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Must exercise tact and discretion in all contacts</li>
<li>Excellent attention to detail and accuracy</li>
<li>Ability to deal with various executives, customers, and officials tactfully and effectively</li>
<li>Ability to coordinate activities and schedules and offer administrative support to various departments</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Hours:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>1st Shift | Mon-Fri | 7:30am-3:30pm</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Pay Rate:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>$16.00/hour</li>
</ul>
