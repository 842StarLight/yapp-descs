<p><b>Regional Buyer</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>We are seeking a Regional Buyer located within our North East Region, including but not limited to: Philadelphia PA, Pittsburgh PA, Baltimore MD, Washington DC, Northern VA, Rochester NY, Salem NH, Wallingford CT areas.</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Aramsco, a family of companies, is a nationwide distributor of environmental, restoration, emergency preparedness, professional cleaning, carpet cleaning, and surface preparation equipment and products. With over 50 retail and service locations throughout the United States our company is considered the leader in our industry. We are currently looking for a full time Regional Buyer to lead a team of Buyers to provide efficient customer service for all internal and external customers by utilizing excellent, in-depth knowledge of company products and programs.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Full Benefit Package!</b></p>
<p><b>Paid Vacation!</b></p>
<p><b>Paid Holidays!</b></p>
<p><b>Opportunity to work from home if Aramsco local branch is not within (25) mile radius.</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES include the following. Other duties may be assigned.</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manages a team of Buyers within the North East Region.</li>
<li>Monitor team performance and report on metrics.</li>
<li>Negotiate purchase agreement with vendors assuring acceptable quality with adequate deliveries at the best price.</li>
<li>Analyze price proposals, financial reports, and other information to determine reasonable prices</li>
<li>Work out agreements with suppliers, such as when products will be delivered</li>
<li>Evaluate and monitor contracts to be sure that vendors and suppliers comply with the terms and conditions of the contract and to determine the need for changes</li>
<li>Meet with staff and vendors to discuss defective or unacceptable goods or services and determine corrective action</li>
<li>Assess quality of stock received and escalate any discrepancies to suppliers and management.</li>
<li>Attends monthly tele/video conference with Regional Managers and Executive Level Managers to provide site reports and updates.</li>
<li>Must stay current with market conditions for existing products and materials.</li>
<li>Ability to recommend substitute product/material for cost savings or more efficient delivery schedule.</li>
<li>Develop, qualify and expand vendor base for all assigned materials and maintain continuous contact with these sources.</li>
<li>Review and adjust incorrect invoices, shipping errors, replacement of materials not meeting company specifications, cancellation of orders and engineering changes.</li>
<li>Update product file with pricing changes in a timely manner in conjunction with Purchasing Manager.</li>
<li>Manage regional transfers.</li>
<li>Ability to travel 20%.</li>
<br>
</ul>
