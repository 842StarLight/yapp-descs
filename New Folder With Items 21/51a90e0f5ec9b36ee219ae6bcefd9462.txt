<h2>Customer Care and Operations Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">We have an immediate opening for a front office client facing manager who will share in the running of a new and rapidly growing lash salon with 7-day operations. If you are a client oriented dynamic person with high energy our environment and culture will challenge you. Are you ready to take it to the next level as part of an accomplished team. This position has great work/life balance and affords weekends off and limited on call responsibilities. You will work closely with our stylist and back-bar manager in a high-end lash salon.
<br><br>
Position Overview
<br><br>
The Customer Care and Operations Manager (CCOM) will represent the owner’s on-site and be responsible for the overall customer experience and salon success. The CCOM will be responsible for ensuring all day to day operations and salon front activities are attended to, focus on the customer experience and business growth.
<br><br>
The CCOM will ensure daily franchise operational policies are adhered to, operational procedures are followed, and brand/quality standards are met by all staff.
<br><br>
The CCOM will have the self-initiative to drive exceptional customer experience and follow through on the tasks associated with the customer lifecycle, the advocation for exceptional client service and satisfaction.
<br><br>
The CCOM will be an expert in customer care and successful salon operations, they will assess, track and recommend actions to improve quality, performance and efficiency to the ownership on a regular basis.
<br><br>
To ensure quality brand delivery and successful results, the CCOM shall provide exemplary customer service to Lash Lounge clients, contribute to the positive reputation, and cultivate overall culture among the staff. The CCOM will have the overall responsibility for day to day operational decisions in the salon concerning staff and customer transactions along with the tools, services or general status of the salon facility. The CCOM will provide periodic reporting to ownership and communicate with them on the overall daily status of the business and will be responsible for supporting and ensuring all Lash Lounge and Premium Service Group standards of salon operations and management are followed.
<br><br>
<b>The CCOM will have 4 primary areas of responsibility:</b> Salon Operations, Sales &amp; Marketing, Sales Accounting and Employee Management
<br><br>
This position has responsibility for the salon facility and all activity associated with opening, operating and closing the site on a daily basis.
<br><br>
Salon Operations
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>The CCOM coordinates with Stylist Manager to ensure stylist scheduling properly supports and adapts to client scheduling and appointments.</li>
<li>Resolves issues such as scheduling conflicts to ensure a stellar customer coverage approach I maintained.</li>
<li>Oversee the appointment scheduling for Lash Lounge clients and proper handling of incoming and outgoing calls with the expected “Gold Standard” customer service level.</li>
<li>Must become proficient and educated in working of key salons systems such as POS software, and manage issue resolution.</li>
<li>Stay up-to-date with organization’s systems and new roll-outs. Consistently follow all organization’s policies, practices and procedures.</li>
<li>See to an organized work area, the preparation and filing of the client charts, intake forms, consultation forms, after care hand-outs, package purchase agreements, and all other forms as needed.</li>
<li>Coordinate through Stylist manager to ensure staff member input and feedback are heard and adopted when appropriate to help create solutions for improved practices going forward.</li>
<li>Handle all customer related issues such as product returns, customer complaints and disputes and turn strive to make negative scenarios into positive outcomes.</li>
<li>Manage retail inventory, see to stock count, enter inventory into the system, order products and supplies to maintain correct inventory levels.</li>
<li>Oversee janitorial and maintenance services to include everything required for salon to keep running and looking its best.</li>
<li>Responsible the cleanliness of the salon including, picking up trash, sweeping, cleaning, dusting, towels or whatever is required to keep the salon looking top-notch.</li>
</ul>
Sales and Marketing
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Assist in marketing ideas based on salon trends and needs, provide feedback and observations.</li>
<li>Manage assigned marketing tasks such as social media posting, attending events, B2B cross-promoting, and more as required.</li>
<li>Preform gorilla marketing by engaging local businesses, clubs, associations and community.</li>
<li>Coordinate with and support the marketing leaders social marking posts and responses, keep web-based marketing up to date and current to include posts, tweets, uploads and updates.</li>
<li>Assist with promotional events at the salon or outside the salon to help market the salon and build the brand.</li>
<li>Educate clients on retail products’ key benefits, ensure staff provide clients with “White-glove” service in presenting offered services, options and discount programs.</li>
<li>Ensure the expected “white-glove” customer service experience is communicated and being implemented by all salon staff members.</li>
<li>Be intimately involved in the client retention, feedback and loyalty program. Create internal process and ensure it is consistently followed.</li>
<li>Oversees accuracy of sales transactions of clients for services and retail items and manage the end of day cash drawer reconciliation.</li>
<li>Aid stylists in building client book retention and building word of mouth campaign.</li>
</ul>
Salon Accounting
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Manage the proper tracking and input of client records into the salon software with correct client lead source and promotional codes.</li>
<li>Manage and track: sales, client counts, comparable sales, donations, gift cards, waste, overtime, costs of products, repairs and maintenance, and other data as needed.</li>
<li>Responsible for weekly bank deposits and salon supply runs.</li>
<li>Coordinate any salon repairs and purchases with owner.</li>
<li>Monitor budget on a monthly basis to ensure expenses are within forecast; note exceptions and potential problem areas and follow up as needed.</li>
<li>Provide expense projections for salon related costs to ownership.</li>
</ul>
Employee Management
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>CCOM will be take a lead on the recruiting, hiring, training, development and assessment of salon front desk staff and coordinate with Stylist manager on stylist hiring to ensure a high-performing team with strong retention.</li>
<li>Oversee staff development and training and responsible for timely successful completion of all required courses based on the employee’s role. Keep staffwell informed and hold regular meetings to communicate, educate and build teamwork.</li>
<li>Will be responsible for cultivating and reinforcing company culture which includes communicating job responsibilities and performance expectations to all staffand holding staff accountable for desired results.</li>
<li>Maintain a positive culture within salon. Ensure that all staff members know and are honoring the core values of The Lash Lounge always.</li>
<li>CCOM will run staff huddles to ensure goals, trends, training, marketing and any other communication is disseminated to salon staff.</li>
<li>Maintain staff updates on salon goals and ensure staff is equipped with all required items they need to contribute to the overall success.</li>
<li>See to continual improvement of stylist service such as better service time and quality.</li>
<li>Conduct or participate in all performance reviews and provide a balanced mix of positive and constructive feedback to all staff</li>
</ul>
</div>
