 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Digital Marketing Intern; She Grows It Brands</p>
<p>POSITION DESCRIPTION<br>We are looking for an enthusiastic intern for the Marketing and Communications Department for She Grows It. This position is responsible for all aspects of marketing activities of assigned products to achieve global sales and margin goals. The Digital Marketing Intern is a strategic thinker who can articulate SGI story and capabilities with respect to vertical industries and use cases and assist to execute content creation for demand generation campaigns designed to dramatically increase the commercial customer base. You will craft relevant, credible and differentiated positioning and messaging for our products and chart the customer journey/decision criteria in partnership with our Communications teams. The position requires a sharp appreciation of the relevant product needs of all markets. This position is also responsible to collaborate in the development of data-based business strategies, development of departmental budgets, and collaborating in the development of advertising, sales promotions, and merchandising tactics associated with assigned products. The ideal candidate is an experienced content developer who has skills in visual graphics, an understanding of social media content, is resourceful, is a good communicator, has leadership skills, can work well with cross-functional and multi-cultural teams.<br>INTERN OF PRODUCT MARKETING RESPONSIBILITIES INCLUDE:<br>· Develop product positioning, value proposition and messaging that differentiates our products in the market.</p>
<p>· Create content such as web pages, white papers, press releases, social media posts and presentations as they correspond to industry verticals such as, but not limited to, financial services, retail, and government agencies</p>
<p>· Communicate the value proposition of the products to our sales team and develop the marketing materials/tools that support the selling process of our products.</p>
<p>· Plan the go-to-market launch strategy for new products and releases, and manage the cross-functional implementation of the plan.</p>
<p>· Work closely with customers, partners, analysts, journalists/media, and internal SGI teams to gauge market trends and innovations, and work closely with R&amp;D to ensure the priorities from the market are properly synthesized and included in the roadmap and development schedule.</p>
<p>· Drive competitive analysis and positioning.</p>
<p>· Develop written &amp; visual product assets and provide subject matter expertise and support for demand generation (whitepapers, eBooks, case studies, webinars, event-content and support), communications (analyst relations, PR content review) and social media campaigns.</p>
<p>· Be extremely comfortable in a highly agile iterative environment, where rapid learning is valued over large monolithic plans and processes.</p>
<p>· Delivers to category strategy to ensure innovative product concepts, unique product positioning, ingredient and formula storytelling, technical innovation, and on time delivery.</p>
<p>· Leads the new product development process from concept through finished product, with specific support on product specifications for formula, shades, timeframes, cost, quality, and packaging.</p>
<p>· Capitalize on new growth/white space opportunities through analyses of competitors in terms of breadth and depth of product range and innovation.</p>
<p>· Actively seeks out, researches and synthesizes new trends in category product format, ingredients, delivery systems, and technology that aligns with the Brand Strategy.</p>
<p>· Introduce new product innovations/concepts and relevant solutions to customer pain points and assortment gaps that support the brand.</p>
<p>· Create product briefs including product concept and benchmarks.</p>
<p>· Develop formulas and create product claims in tandem with CMs, regional R&amp;D.</p>
<p>· Maintain a strong understanding of the global cosmetics business and entire SGI portfolio and strive to develop and advance category products.</p>
<p>· Resolve production/relationship issues that arise by partnering with vendors/CMs to ensure deliverables are met.</p>
<p>· Monitor and evaluate vendor performance.</p>
<p>· Review and approve primary and secondary artwork.</p>
<p>· Drive necessary course correction to ensure deliverables will be met.</p>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS: </b></p>
<p>· Majors in Business, Marketing, Communications, Graphic Design, Art or other related fields.</p>
<p>· A strong sense of consumer trends, style, taste, and an entrepreneurial approach to business</p>
<p>· Knowledge of marketing theory, principles, and technique.</p>
<p>· Excellent written, oral, and presentation skills, and strong ability to interface externally.</p>
<p>· The ability to handle a multitude of tasks and meet project deadlines in a fast-paced environment.</p>
<p>· The ability to work in a team-oriented environment is critical. People management experience is required.</p>
<p>· Strong acumen about new technologies.</p>
<p>· Highly collaborative with cross-functional stakeholders and peers.</p>
<p>· Must have the ability to create engaging written and visual content</p>
