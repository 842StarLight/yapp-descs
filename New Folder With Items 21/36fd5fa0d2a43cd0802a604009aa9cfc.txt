<p>Moody National Management Company is seeking a Property Manager for our <b>150</b><b>-unit</b> apartment community located in Houston, TX â€“ <b>The Village at Bunker Hill!</b> We offer competitive pay, flexible scheduling opportunities, paid time off, and comprehensive benefits, including medical, dental, vision, and 401(k) with Employer Match.</p>
<p>
Ideal candidate will have some Property Management experience, excellent customer service skills, a professional demeanor, and a desire to grow and advance with Moody.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Functions:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Effectively manage property staff; hire, train, develop, empower, coach and counsel, conduct performance and salary reviews, resolve problems, provide open communications, discipline and recommend termination of employment as appropriate.</li>
<li>Implement company programs and develop and implement property programs to maximize occupancy and revenues, and comply with federal state laws and regulations, and the policies/procedures of the company.</li>
<li>Contribute to the development of an annual property budget and track results; accurately report property income, expenses and other financial data in accordance with budget and policy guidelines</li>
<li>Make daily deposits of income, maintain accurate and current petty cash records, and safeguard all property funds; complete month-end closings as scheduled.</li>
<li>Collect and analyze competitive marketing data; recommend marketing plans to achieve leasing and revenue goals; develop and execute effective advertising; ensure vacant units are market ready.</li>
<li>Develop and implement an effective resident retention plan; resolve resident complaints and issues in a timely and cost-effective manner; host property-sponsored social functions.</li>
<li>Ensure timely collection of rents and fees from residents; issue eviction notices and attend court eviction hearings, as necessary.</li>
<li>Ensure the property is maintained in a manner consistent with company goals and allocated budget; protect, secure and enhance the assets as appropriate; visually inspect all grounds and buildings on a regular basis; identify and report apparent operational or safety issues and ensure repairs are conducted as soon as feasible,</li>
<li>Negotiate with vendors to procure cost-effective contracts for services and products used by the property; monitor the work of contractors to ensure contract provisions and requisite standards of product/service are complied with.</li>
<li>Monitor employees to ensure compliance with safety policies and procedures; provide employees with related equipment and clothing items in accordance with Company policy.</li>
<li>Develop, implement, and maintain a disaster preparedness plan for the property.</li>
<li>Maintain inventories of necessary supplies and equipment; order supplies and equipment as appropriate.</li>
<li>Prepare and submit financial and other reports requested by management.</li>
<li>Participate in special projects, task forces and committees and carry out other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Thorough knowledge of all related laws and regulations pertaining to managing the property and all applicable company policies and procedures.</li>
<li>Knowledge of financial statements and sales and marketing concepts and techniques</li>
<li>Management/supervisory skills.</li>
<li>Strong customer service, communication, negotiating, conflict resolution, and organizational skills.</li>
<li>Fluent in writing and speaking in English; fluency in Spanish desirable.</li>
<li>Intermediate proficiency with MS Office, including Excel, Word, and Outlook and Yardi accounting software.</li>
<li>Excellent time management skills and ability to multi-task and prioritize work</li>
<li>Exceptional problem solving skills</li>
<li>Ability to maintain customer focus</li>
<li>Professional in-person and phone presence</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Experience and Education:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High School Diploma or equivalent experience required</li>
<li>1+ years in a Manager/Supervisor position in one or more multi-family residential properties</li>
</ul>
