<p><b>
Your Career</b></p>
<p>
As a member of the Palo Alto Networks sales leadership team, you will report to the Vice President of Sales for America’s Service Providers. You will be responsible for building and driving the Verizon sales team to exceed company objectives and grow the district according to plan. You will be responsible for managing the Verizon sales team on a day-to-day basis including- pipeline review, Demand Gen review, Channel Review, and overall performance management of the sales team.</p>
<p><b>
Your Impact</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Manage and lead a team of sales professionals, the relationship with Verizon and exceed revenue targets</li>
<li>
Develop and maintain a joint business plan with Verizon which covers all businesses including infrastructure, managed security services, and resale.</li>
<li>
Work closely with Sales Engineering and Professional Services teams to build complete solution based proposals</li>
<li>
Deliver well-inspected, accurate weekly sales forecasts and pipeline management</li>
<li>
Ensure Verizon team has well-documented account plans aligned with the value proposition and differentiated strengths of the company</li>
<li>
Disciplined inspection of team activities on account, leveraging relationships to drive revenue</li>
<li>
Assure Managed Service Go-To-Market team members partner with our Enterprise teams to build pipeline and close joint business with Verizon</li>
<li>
Assure Managed Service Creation team members partner with Verizon Product Management to embed our Palo Alto Networks Security Platforms into their managed security service offers</li>
<li>
Assure Infrastructure team members drive Palo Alto Networks Security Platform preference into mobile and wireline infrastructure</li>
</ul>
