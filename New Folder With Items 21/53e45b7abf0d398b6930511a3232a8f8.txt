<p><b>Description:</b></p>
<div>
<p>
Join the amazing team at Thrive Internet Marketing Agency! We are a high-powered digital marketing agency growing rapidly and looking for a customer-centric PPC Account Manager to work with a cohesive team of outstanding PPC experts to help grow the business to the next level.</p>
<p>
Founded in 2005, we are a high-performing team, creating custom WordPress websites and digital marketing strategies for businesses. Thrive is committed to cultivating a culture of exceptional people who create outstanding results for our clients. We are a close-knit team that is motivated, smart, disciplined, organized and committed to turning ideas into action. We are professional, honest and passionate about our work.</p>
<p>
We already have many happy clients and are adding more every day. Grow with us as we address the business marketing needs of clients in the United States and around the world.</p>
<p><b>
Position Summary</b></p>
<p>
This full-time position on the Thrive PPC Team will serve as support for the paid advertising work for Thrive’s clients. This client-facing position will become a key part of our Paid Search services, ensuring that our clients have consistent activity and results on paid search channels, through the integration of strategic marketing tactics.</p>
<p>
This role is ideal for someone that has hands-on experience with paid advertising, but wants to get out of the day-to-day management of campaigns. The individual will be meeting with clients daily, presenting campaign results, discussing strategies, and translating the client’s feedback to our internal PPC Team.</p>
<p><b>
This is a full-time position with expected work hours between 8:30 am – 5:30 pm which offers you the opportunity to work remotely from home.</b></p>
</div>
