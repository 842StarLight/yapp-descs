<h2>Managing Director- South East</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>JOB SUMMARY:</b> The Managing Director (MD) provides strategic direction and progressive leadership for the South East region. The MD is responsible for both supporting Pinkerton’s SVP CANAM and guiding Directors in their respective areas including; profit &amp; loss, brand enhancement, client retention/penetration, delivery of quality services, and effective and efficient operational management.<br>
<br>
<b>Essential Functions:</b>
<ul>
<li>The functions listed describe the business purpose of this job. Specific duties or tasks may vary and be documented separately. The employee might not be required to perform all functions listed. Additional duties may be assigned, and functions may be modified, according to business necessity.</li>
<li>All assigned duties or tasks are deemed to be part of the essential functions, unless such duties or tasks are unrelated to the functions listed, in which case they are deemed to be other (non-essential) functions.</li>
<li>Employees are held accountable for successful job performance. Job performance standards may be documented separately, and may include functions, objectives, duties or tasks not specifically listed herein.</li>
<li>In performing functions, duties or tasks, employees are required to know and follow safe work practices, and to be aware of company policies and procedures related to job safety, including safety rules and regulations. Employees are required to notify superiors upon becoming aware of unsafe working conditions.</li>
<li>All functions, duties or tasks are to be carried out in an honest, ethical and professional manner, and to be performed in conformance with applicable company policies and procedures. In the event of uncertainty or lack of knowledge of company policies and procedures, employees are required to request clarification or explanations from superiors or authorized company representatives.</li>
</ul>
<b><i>The Essential Functions Include:</i></b>
<ul>
<li>Represent Pinkerton’s core values of integrity, vigilance, and excellence.</li>
<li>Prepare, submit, and operate against approved annual budget;
<ul>
<li>Monitor progress against short and long-term business objectives.</li>
<li>Manage the Profit and Loss statement of the assigned area.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Develop and manage a progressive, aggressive, and innovative sales and marketing program to continue to grow profitable base of business;
<ul>
<li>Responsible for regional brand enhancement.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Evaluate industry/business trends for potential impact on the organization;
<ul>
<li>Identify key business opportunities as well as potential revenue threats.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Direct the implementation of best demonstrated industry and company practices as well as corporate, field, and TQM initiatives.</li>
<li>Manage the selection, orientation, training, development, and retention of Directors within the region;
<ul>
<li>Provide on-going coaching and mentoring to ensure the achievement of expected revenue goals and Company initiatives.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Provide billable services directly, as appropriate.</li>
<li>Provide counsel in areas of technical expertise;
<ul>
<li>Direct and manage major projects.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Coordinate with Marketing on business development priorities and key sales actions;
<ul>
<li>Partner with Pinkerton counterparts on cross-selling efforts, referrals, and client contract negotiations.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Maintain on-going contact with client customers;
<ul>
<li>Plan and participate in client service calls for the purpose of obtaining feedback critical to maintaining and improving account relationships.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Build and maintain a network of prospective organizations for the purpose of developing long-term quality business opportunities.</li>
<li>Analyze key financial performance indicators;
<ul>
<li>Focuses on on-going improvement and performance.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Delegate workload effectively to ensure client requirements are met, staff members are utilized appropriately, and labor costs are contained.</li>
<li>Authorize appropriate expenditures including equipment, supplies, advertising, automobile maintenance.</li>
<li>Focus on prompt and effective resolution of legal, financial, human resources, and administrative issues.</li>
<li>Facilitate teamwork and the implementation of progressive change.</li>
<li>All other duties, as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<b>Minimum Hiring Standards:</b><br>
Additional qualifications may be specified and receive preference, depending upon the nature of the position.
<ul>
<li>Must have a High School Diploma or GED.</li>
<li>Must be willing to participate in the Company’s pre-employment screening process, including drug testing and background investigation.</li>
<li>Must be at least 18 years of age.</li>
<li>Must have a reliable means of communication (i.e., email, cell phone).</li>
<li>Must have a reliable means of transportation (public or private).</li>
<li>Must have the legal right to work in the United States.</li>
<li>Must have the ability to speak, read, and write English.</li>
</ul>
<b>Education/Experience:</b><br>
Bachelor’s degree with at least ten years of progressively responsible security and/or business management experience.<br>
<br>
<b>Competencies (As Demonstrated Through Experience, Training, and/or Testing</b>):
<ul>
<li>Security and/or business management experience.</li>
<li>Demonstrated understanding of investigations or security consulting.</li>
<li>Proven business development and financial management experience.</li>
<li>Able to synthesize complex business/financial data and develop innovative solutions.</li>
<li>Executive-level presentation skills.</li>
<li>Excellent customer and service orientation.</li>
<li>Strong project management skills.</li>
<li>Able to interact effectively at all levels and across diverse cultures.</li>
<li>Serve as an effective team leader.</li>
<li>Able to facilitate progressive change.</li>
<li>Results orientated.</li>
<li>Computer skills; Microsoft Office.</li>
</ul>
<b>Working Conditions (Physical/Mental Demands):</b><br>
With or without reasonable accommodation, requires the physical and mental capacity to perform effectively all essential functions. In addition to other demands, the demands of the job include:
<ul>
<li>Must undergo and meet company standards for background and reference checks, drug testing, and behavioral selection survey.</li>
<li>Maintain composure in dealing with authorities, executives, clients, staff, and the public occasionally under conditions of urgency and in pressure situations.</li>
<li>Exposure to sensitive and confidential information.</li>
<li>Regular computer usage.</li>
<li>Ability to handle multiple tasks concurrently.</li>
<li>Must be able to see, hear, speak, and write clearly in order to communicate with employees and/or customers.</li>
<li>Manual dexterity required for occasional reaching and lifting of small objects and operating office equipment.</li>
<li>Close and distance vision and ability to adjust focus.</li>
<li>Travel, as required.</li>
<br>
</ul>
