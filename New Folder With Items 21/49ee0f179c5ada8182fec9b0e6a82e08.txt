<h2>Senior Manager of Patient Services Operations and Vendor Management</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>About Pear Therapeutics:</b><br>
Pear Therapeutics is the leader in prescription digital therapeutics. We aim to redefine medicine by discovering, developing, and delivering clinically validated software-based therapeutics to provide better outcomes for patients, smarter engagement and tracking tools for clinicians, and cost-effective solutions for payers. Pear has a pipeline of products and product candidates across therapeutic areas, including severe psychiatric and neurological conditions. Our lead product, reSET®, treats Substance Use Disorder and was the first prescription digital therapeutic to receive marketing authorization from the FDA to treat disease. Pear's second product, reSET-O™, for the treatment of Opioid Use Disorder, received marketing clearance from the FDA in December 2018.
<br><br>
<b>Position Overview:</b><br>
The Senior Manager of Patient Services Operations &amp; Vendor Management is responsible for the ongoing oversight and operational excellence of reSET Connect, the branded patient services organization that delivers customer support for patients that have been prescribed reSET and reSET-O.
<br><br>
The successful candidate is a skilled operational leader, who has successfully managed day to day patient services centers for the pharma / biotech / specialty pharmacy industries. The role requires a keen awareness of what operational excellence looks like across people, process and systems for all parts of the patient journey.
<br><br>
In addition to having patient-centric operational experience, the Sr. Mgr of operations &amp; vendor management must demonstrate an ability to understand the criticality of the provider support experience vis-à-vis the operational model, have a track record of managing vendors, conducting QBRs with partners, assessing the quality of customer phone calls and working with field-based representatives to reduce barriers to improve patient pull-through. The candidate should be adept and comfortable presenting the Patient Services offerings and operational overview with select key customers in partnership with field-based representatives. Additionally, the candidate must have experience working closely with internal Quality Management teams to ensure operational quality excellence, robust change control management, and experience with handling inspections / quality audits.
<br><br>
Strong candidates for this role must have excellent understanding of how integrated CRM / CTI workflow, automation and pushed work, effective user interfaces, and robust metrics / reporting streamlines operations, improves agent effectiveness, operational efficiency and drives improved agent behaviors. Experience in utilizing multi-channel communications and electronic Rx intake are differentiation factors for eligible candidates.
<br><br>
Critical to this role's success is the ability to work cross functionally with office-based, field based, and external partners to execute operational excellent and meet the high demands of digital therapeutic customers. The successful candidate will be responsible for the operational improvement of patient conversion and adherence rates and the day to day effective support of field-based escalations of key customers.
<br><br>
<b>Key Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Oversight of the day to day Patient Services operations for reSET and reSET-O, including direction of operational staff, customer engagement success, priority escalation, and overall operational performance management.</li>
<li>Assess the need for and development of improved operational materials such as job aids, talking points, agent training, etc. Assess existing process / systems workflows and user interfaces for operational improvements and scalability recommendations.</li>
<li>Present and collaborate with internal Pear commercial leadership to better understand operational performance.</li>
<li>Partner and collaborate with sales and field reimbursement managers to improve patient pull-through success and improved provider experience.</li>
<li>Present to key customers the Patient Service offerings, provide and operational overview and best practices to ensure customer engagement success.</li>
<li>Analyze day to day operational reports and metrics to understand the health of the business / operations and make recommendations for new and improved analytics.</li>
<li>Help foster a department culture that places patients first, providers and payers as critical customers and ensures operational excellence through effective internal and external partnerships</li>
<li>Support the VP of Patient Services and provide Commercial leadership in Patient Services delivery models, goals, KPIs, organizational and technological platforms and practices.</li>
</ul>
<b>Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree required. MBA degree preferred.</li>
<li>Minimum 5-7 years' experience in managing / leading operational teams developing patient services programs and detailing process workflows in the healthcare or pharmaceutical/biotech industry.</li>
<li>Solid understanding of the healthcare environment specifically in the area delivering services to Health Care Professional and Patient customers.</li>
<li>Solid understanding of health insurance and benefits, co-pay programs, Medicare / Medicaid, and coverage determination processes.</li>
<li>Experience with integrated Customer Relationship Management (CRM) and CTI systems utilizing multi-channel communications is critical.</li>
<li>Experience with partnering with SPPs and having a deep understanding of SPP business &amp; dispensing processes.</li>
<li>Experience in new product launches.</li>
<li>Excellent project management skills with demonstrated ability to lead multiple projects and priorities.</li>
<li>Excellent communication and presentation skills.</li>
<li>Possess strong critical thinking, problem solving, and analytical skills with attention to detail.</li>
<li>Ability to work independently and manage competing priorities.</li>
<li>Desire to innovate and work at an ultra-fast paced, quick decision-making energetic environment with little bureaucracy.</li>
<li>Ability to travel up to 30% for key customer presentations and vendor oversight.</li>
</ul>
