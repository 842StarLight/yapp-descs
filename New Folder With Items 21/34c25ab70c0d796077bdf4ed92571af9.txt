<p><b><i>About Us</i></b> –</p>
<p>BlackHouse is an event production agency founded in 2016 with a focus on corporate and experiential events. We bridge the gap between ideation and execution and provide seamless event services to our clients. We live to turn four blank walls into immersive experiences. Our industry recognizes us for our fresh thinking and comprehensive design-to-execution event production. We don’t just plan and produce events - we spark emotional connections to them.</p>
<p>This is an opportunity to learn from a group of creative professionals who love the grind of production life and thrive here in this great city, where we get to be a part of some of the dopest experiences for today’s consumers.</p>
<p>We are seeking a highly motivated Marketing &amp; Social Media Intern to join the team in our NYC office. You will also assist with the planning and production of internal events for the BlackHouse Tribe. You will need to communicate clearly within our team project, be consistent and concise in our brand messaging.</p>
