<h2>Associate Director, Data & Analytics Global Procurement</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Bristol-Myers Squibb is a global Biopharma company committed to a single mission: to discover, develop, and deliver innovative medicines focused on helping millions of patients around the world in disease areas such as oncology, cardiovascular, immunoscience and fibrosis.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Join us and make a difference. We hire the best people and provide them with a work environment that places a premium on diversity, integrity, collaboration and personal development. Through a culture of inclusion, we create a better, more productive work environment. We believe that the diverse experiences and perspectives of all our employees help to drive innovation and transformative business results.
</p>
<p>The Associate Director Global Procurement Lead for Data and Analytics will be responsible for creating a sourcing and procurement strategy for <b>Data </b>(across the enterprise such as sales data, claims data, health care data, and real world data), <b>Analytics </b>(analytics services including use of advanced decision analytics and artificial intelligence). This role will:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Represent Procurement with the Global Business Partner as the business-facing and strategic arm of the function driving optimal relationship management.
</li>
<li>Work collaboratively and drive awareness of the strategies across Global Procurement and ensure that the teams executing sourcing projects do so in a manner consistent with our strategies.
</li>
<li>Bring and develop external perspectives and insights aligned to business objectives and maintains an awareness of market developments.
</li>
<li>Develop the data and analytics a 3-5 year supply plan, sub-category plans, multi-year productivity plan, budget and financial projections, integration with buying channels and service delivery, sourcing projects, supplier negotiations, and supplier relationship management.
</li>
<li>Identify and drive transformational opportunities and to provide overall value, including revenue and savings projections
</li>
</ul>
<p>This role reports to the Global Procurement Director of Business Insight (Analytics &amp; Market Research Sourcing)
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Desired Leadership attributes:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>ENTERPRISE MINDSET – Capability to make decisions, set priorities and create strategies based on what will benefit the whole. Build and maintain networks within and outside of the organization to both strengthen their understanding of the big picture and leverage diverse perspectives, experiences and expertise to maximize performance
</p>
<p>• Uses knowledge of external and internal business factors to inform decisions.
</p>
<p>• Ensures teams are responsible for contributing beyond their own function.
</p>
<p>• Proactively connects teams, networks and individuals in order to increase organizational performance.
</p>
<p>• Creates integrated, long term strategies that have the potential to transform BMS and the industry.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>CHANGE AGILITY – Capability on constantly thinking ahead and scanning the environment for opportunities. Able to navigate ambiguity for themselves by demonstrating smart risk taking and personal resilience. Lead others through change by continuously creating the context and engaging individuals.
</p>
<p>• Identifies and creates distinctive, game-changing opportunities that lead to higher organizational performance.
</p>
<p>• Has the data and rationale to see the future of the industry frames it in an understandable way and wins support for change in a fast-paced environment.
</p>
<p>• Thinks 3-5 years ahead, develops contingencies plans for multiple scenarios.
</p>
<p>• Hires, develops and rewards change agents, welcoming and encouraging those who challenge to the status quo and test their own thinking.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>AUTHENTIC LEADERSHIP – Capability to build legitimacy through cultivating honest relationships. Generate trust by demonstrating the highest level of consistency between their words and their actions, especially under pressure. Role model and promote an environment of openness.
</p>
<p>• Fosters a climate of self-awareness and open dialogue; speaks to personal struggles and how they deal / overcome them.
</p>
<p>• Understands others’ styles and approaches and knows how to leverage and value them.
</p>
<p>• Holds others to account for doing the right thing, even when it requires some personal risk.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Position Requirements:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>10+ years of business experience with 5-8 years’ experience in procurement of data, analytics, marketing services, customer and patient engagement services, or broadly indirect services
</li>
<li>Experience in healthcare and biopharma industry is required.
</li>
<li>Procurement process knowledge, category management, contract management, financial analysis, market analysis, supplier analysis, supplier management, technology utilization and strategic planning
</li>
<li>Internal stakeholder management, data/information management, category management, process redesign and continuous improvement expertise, project management and performance measurement skills
</li>
<li>Leadership experience in a matrix environment with ability to influence those outside your direct reporting line, inclusive of shared performance expectations and objectives.
</li>
<li>Requires excellent interpersonal, communication, presentation, negotiation, problem solving, analytical and organizational skills.
</li>
<li>Demonstrated high degree of independence, requiring minimal supervision from senior management
</li>
<li>Must also be highly motivated, results focused and act with a high sense of urgency. Must be able to create contingency plans and refine action steps to address shifting priorities or changing objectives.
</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree or higher in Marketing, Life Sciences, Business, Supply Chain
</li>
<li>Certified Purchasing Manager (CPM) or APP Certification a plus.
</li>
</ul>
