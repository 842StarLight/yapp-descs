<h2>Cloud Business Strategist/Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>US Based - preferred Bay Area, Seattle<br>
</div>
<p><b>
Why MobiledgeX:</b></p>
<p>
MobiledgeX is a creating a marketplace of edge cloud locations, capacity and services that will connect developers with the world's largest mobile networks to power the next generation of applications and devices. In order to do this, we are bringing together a community that includes application developers, device makers, mobile network operators, equipment manufacturers, systems integrators, and more.</p>
<p>
MobiledgeX already has a live edge cloud today in several global regions, and we are committed to worldwide coverage of these highly distributed edge resources and services to enable the present and rapidly evolving future demands of consumer and enterprise XR (AR/VR/MR) experiences, autonomous everything and next-generation content delivery use cases as well as other compute intensive, latency sensitive and pervasive &amp; immersive applications.</p>
<p>
MobiledgeX is uniquely positioned to be a catalyst in the rapid engagement and revenue opportunity realization for all members of the edge value chain.</p>
<p>
We are anticipating unprecedented growth in the coming 12 months, so there is no better time to join the team at MobiledgeX.</p>
<p><b>
Who we are looking for:</b></p>
<div>
We are looking for a Business Development Analyst to conduct modeling, field testing and optimizing the strategic blueprint for building a predictable, repeatable and scalable business. This will include a continuous and comprehensive analysis and understanding of the addressable market and its trends, changing conditions, competitive threats, opportunities and compelling value proposition to guide prioritization of focus areas for customer and strategic partner acquisition.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
A significant portion of the role will be outward facing alongside business category teams, with an emphasis on thoughtfully drilling down past surface descriptions of wants and needs to get to root characteristics. The role will require development of complex strategic and commercial propositions across complex stakeholder structures that capture cause, effect, desirable solutions and the value of what MobiledgeX can deliver. Findings will be clustered into themes and prioritized based on various, ideally quantified impacts to the business, and they will be leveraged in close collaboration with Product, Engineering and Marketing to influence functional priorities.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Given the nascent stages of the edge computing landscape where MobiledgeX operates, it is crucial to move fast and execute with a mature understanding of the adjacent domains of cloud, infrastructure, operators, etc. This role will heavily inform the definition and shape of use cases and value propositions tailored towards specific audiences and strategic pursuits. The ideal individual for this role is a creative and resourceful self-starter who can operate in a rapidly evolving environment without heavy-handed management and guidance.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
This role will also own structuring and evolving P&amp;L and KPI reporting for various segments of the business.</div>
