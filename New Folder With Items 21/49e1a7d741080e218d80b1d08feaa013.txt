<div>Job Description</div>
<div></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<br>
Begin your future with a company who has been focused on people for more than 100 years. They operate with more than 7, 000 employees in 50 countries every day who help people maintain or regain their freedom of movement with innovative, technology-driven prosthetic and orthotic devices and industry-leading services. As a result of continued growth and success, we have an opportunity on our Service Team for a Fabrication Customer Service Coordinator in Salt Lake City, Utah<br>
<br>
The successful candidate will support the Design of 3D models in CAD and assist with operating the different CAM systems. Perform fabrication tasks such as digital modification of orthotic and prosthetic devices, operation of 5 and 7 axis carving equipment and support activities of running SLS 3D printing.<br>
<br>
<b>Specifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Schedule: Monday Friday 6: 30 AM to 4: 00 PM</li>
<li>
Pay: $24.00/hour</li>
<li>
Temporary to Hire
</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Follows established work instructions and work orders.
</li>
<li>Assures quality of work by correctly using and understanding job quality check sheets.
</li>
<li>Participates in departmental meetings, process improvements, and required training.
</li>
<li>Digitize and design files for custom orthotic and prosthetic devices.
</li>
<li>Creating tool pathing for carve files.</li>
<li>
Support programming of 5 &amp; 7 axis carving equipment.</li>
<li>
Support design of production tooling.</li>
<li>
Demonstrates positive attitude toward safety, product quality, product schedule, and product cost.
</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.
</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Associates/ bachelor's degree in CAD Design/Engineering Technology or similar.
</li>
<li>1-4 years of experience in the field or in a related area.
</li>
<li>Experience with CAM operating CNC of a 5 or 7 axis carver.
</li>
<li>Knowledge of Anatomy preferred.
</li>
<li>Strong 3D modeling skills.
</li>
<li>Familiar with surfacing techniques.</li>
<li>
Experience with engineering drawing/drafting standards and practices.
</li>
<li>Efficient CAD ability.</li>
<li>
PC literate in Microsoft applications. (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, OneNote)
</li>
<li>Experience with Creo (preferred), Solid works, or other design software.
</li>
<li>Highly self-motivated and self-directed with the ability to work with teams cross functionally.
</li>
<li>Knowledgeable in drawing standards and tolerance stack analysis.
</li>
<li>Ability to communicate ideas verbally and through sketches and mockups.</li>
</ul>
<br>
Candidates will be required to pass E-Verify, background check and drug screen.<br>
