<p><b>Staff Accountant</b></p>
<p>Envoy Global is on a mission to disrupt the global immigration process! Our patented online platform integrates the best of people and technology to streamline, simplify and expedite the immigration process for employers and individuals.<br>
</p>
<p>Working alongside our affiliated law firm, Global Immigration Associates, we are giving companies the tools they need to hire the best talent from around the world, send talent where it’s needed, and help support foreign workers and immigrants throughout the process.<br>
</p>
<p>We’re a fast-growing, award-winning technology company, a leader in our space, and are backed by some of the country’s leading venture capital and growth equity firms.<br>
</p>
<p>Envoy is looking for a <b>Staff Accountant </b>to join our fast-paced work environment. This person will manage month end close, treasury, AR, and AP. The perfect candidate will be a self-starter, excited to learn, and willing to adapt to constant change. This role will report directly to the Senior Accounting Manager and will have great visibility cross-departmentally across all functions of the organization.<br>
</p>
<p><b>You Will:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Assist with month end close by preparing and posting journal entries, including accruals, prepaids, allocations, and cash transactions</li>
<li>Perform monthly account analysis and reconciliations, including balance sheet and certain expense accounts</li>
<li>Assist with treasury responsibilities including managing our daily cash position, preparing international wires, and assisting with cash forecasts</li>
<li>Prepare and send consolidated monthly client invoices and account statements for enterprise customers including supporting documentation for any unique customer requests</li>
<li>Coordinate client and foreign national refund process to ensure timely and accurate refunds</li>
<li>Prepare monthly analysis summarizing cash trends for distribution to finance leadership</li>
<li>Assist with external audit and tax requests</li>
<li>Collaborate with other teams including Sales, Customer Relationship Management, and Product</li>
<li>Support accounting team, senior accounting manager, and CFO with ad hoc projects</li>
</ul>
<p><b>You Have:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree, preferably in Accounting or Finance</li>
<li>CPA or CPA Candidate preferred but not required</li>
<li>1-3 years of experience in a related position
</li>
<li>Proficient in Excel, experience with Intacct a plus</li>
<li>Strong written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>High attention to detail</li>
<li>Strong analytical skills</li>
</ul>
