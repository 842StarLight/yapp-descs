<h2>Team Supervisor, Sales & Service- GROW/Seasonal</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">The GROW/Seasonal Sales &amp; Service Supervisor will report to the Manager of Customer Experience and will work closely with their team to deliver Excellence Plus service to all customers. "To help our customers express themselves and connect with the important people in their lives"
<br><br>
The GROW/Seasonal Sales &amp; Service Supervisor will assume responsibility for the daily functions of the Sales &amp; Service Team Members, driving team and enterprise performance KPIs and will have direct responsibilities of coaching, mentoring, driving performance, and troubleshooting the needs of their teams.
<br><br>
The GROW/Seasonal Sales &amp; Service Supervisor will be responsible for a team of up to 20 customer service agents.
<br><br>
Minimum knowledge, skill and/or ability required to perform job functions:
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Preferred - bachelor's degree and/or 2 years of experience in deployment of Operations / BPO outsourcing / Service Centers</li>
<li>Demonstrated team leadership and development capabilities with the ability to lead by example</li>
<li>Excellent leadership and organizational skills; attention to detail; willingness to work in a fast-paced environment and effectively meet aggressive timelines.</li>
<li>Advanced communication skills</li>
<li>Skilled in Microsoft Office Tools</li>
<li>Flexibility in schedule to support the business during peak season</li>
<li>Has the capability and flexibility to motivate their team to meet customer/business demands</li>
<li>Strong business knowledge; ability to set clear expectations for team, manage through metrics and SLAs</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to be hands-on; get into the details to drive issues to resolution</li>
<li>Strong partnership and team mindset</li>
<li>Currently meeting Attendance &amp; Performance expectations (For GROW roles)</li>
<li>Currently not on an active disciplinary plan (For GROW roles)</li>
</ul>
