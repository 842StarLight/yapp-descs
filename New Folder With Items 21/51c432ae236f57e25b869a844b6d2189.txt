<h2>Application Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Brainlab is currently seeking an Application Consultant to service our surgery clients based in San Antonio, TX.</p>
<br>
<p><br>
Brainlab’s Application Consultants perform custom installations and provide consultation and guidance for customers utilizing the company’s product portfolio.<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Conduct on-site clinical evaluations for new and existing customers to demonstrate the use of products and services to increase the frequency and quality of IGS (Image Guided Surgery) product usage</li>
<li>Provide on-site consulting, guidance, and assistance to physicians and OR personnel by evaluating options and problem-solving during surgery in order to maximize IGS (Image Guided Surgery) efficiency and usage</li>
<li>Lead installations and establish functionality at new and existing customer sites</li>
<li>Educate/train physicians and hospital personnel, including OR staff, Radiology, Central Sterile Processing, Biomedical and Information Technologies staff, on the use of IGS (Image Guided Surgery) products and services</li>
<li>Utilize engineering expertise to contribute to the quality improvement process by recommending product modifications</li>
<li>Act as primary customer liaison, maintaining site visits and regular contact with customers, assessing satisfaction and usage levels, and working to increase revenue via the sale of service contracts, spare/wear parts, upgrades, etc.</li>
<li>Regularly strategize with the Account Manager in your region, evaluating customer situations, open sales projects, and identifying and referring sales leads</li>
<li>Resolve product issues through detailed analysis and utilization of engineering expertise</li>
<li>Keep abreast of new developments regarding Brainlab and competitive IGS (Image Guided Surgery) products and services via ongoing training and individual research and analysis</li>
<li>Attend IGS (Image Guided Surgery) Continuing Education annually and maintain annual product certification competency</li>
<li>Promote IGS (Image Guided Surgery) products and services to surgeons at trade shows</li>
<li>Maximize customer satisfaction</li>
<li>Report and document complaints per Complaint Handling SOP</li>
<li>Perform other duties and responsibilities as required</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Required Experience:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>B.S. in Biomedical Engineering, Biomechanical Engineering, Bioengineering, Clinical Engineering, or Nursing required</li>
<li>1-3 years of experience in a technical support role for a healthcare company or provider preferred</li>
<li>Knowledge of surgical procedures, anatomy and medical terminology preferred</li>
<li>Proficient in the use of PC software applications including Microsoft Office</li>
<li>Self-motivated, detail oriented and a proven history of ability to make independent decisions</li>
<li>Travel 50%+</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Additional Comments:</b><br>
In return for the experiences listed above we will provide a business casual environment, competitive compensation package including medical, dental, life, LTD, STD, and matching 401k benefits and much more.<br>
<br>
Visit us at: www.brainlab.com</p>
