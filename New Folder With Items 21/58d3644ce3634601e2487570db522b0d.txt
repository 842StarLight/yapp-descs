<h2>ENTERPRISE SALES CONSULTANT</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div><div><div>
<p>With brilliant people and innovative technology, Joveo is driving next generation recruitment technology solutions for employers around the world. Harnessing machine learning and industry expertise, our platform delivers the most relevant candidates in the shortest time, giving our customers a competitive edge.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
We are passionate people building a value-based culture based on integrity, courage, empathy and humility. We are looking for a strong sales leader to join the Joveo team in our Silicon Valley headquarters in Redwood City, CA.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
YOUR MISSION</b></p>
<p>
As a key member of the Joveo Sales team, you will work cross-functionally with Account Management, Engineering and Marketing at Joveo, as well as with our external stakeholders to expand the Enterprise market in the United States and evangelize programmatic recruitment ad buying.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
CORE RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>
Be responsible for new business development and expanding spend with current clients as per defined sales targets.</p></li>
<li><p>
Proven track record of selling complex technology solutions.</p></li>
<li><p>
Create and manage the pipeline.</p></li>
<li><p>
Monitor account production, growth, future needs.</p></li>
<li><p>
Identify account trends and training needs.</p></li>
<li><p>
Supporting customers during the implementation of contracts and throughout the relationship; seeking customer feedback; taking responsibility for customer satisfaction and loyalty.</p></li>
<li><p>
Provide inputs to the strategic sales planning process.</p></li>
<li><p>
Connect with key influencers and decision makers in the potential client organization and develop relationships.</p></li>
<li><p>
Develop a strong understanding of our product offerings and be able to articulate a convincing value proposition for the client based on their business needs.</p></li>
<li><p>
Ability to work with product, customer success and marketing</p></li>
<li><p>
Demonstrating business acumen, cultivating a network with the result of earning the respect to be viewed as a trusted business advisor.</p></li>
<li><p>
Help evolve a robust roadmap for our product offerings by identifying trending in the digital marketing arena.</p></li>
<li><p>
Work closely with the internal Clients, Client Services, Product &amp; Delivery teams to drive sales of offerings.</p></li>
<li><p>
Act as the voice of the customer across the organization and champion our products and solutions with the customer.</p></li>
<li><p>
Have a strong growth mindset by relentlessly pursuing new opportunities with clients.</p></li>
