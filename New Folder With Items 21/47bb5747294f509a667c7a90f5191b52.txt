<div><b>
YOUR ROLE: Application Architect</b></div>
<div>
While there will never be a typical day at Accenture Interactive, we’ve highlighted a few of your exciting responsibilities.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Lead large-scale eCommerce implementations, utilizing Salesforce Commerce Cloud, providing both oversight and hands on contributions to the software design, development, and integration
</li>
<li>Independently lead the estimation effort for a project
</li>
<li>Participate in the development of conceptual and logical architectures
</li>
<li>Design, develop and maintain application architectures that support client’s business requirements
</li>
<li>Resolve integration and interfacing issues between various back-end systems
</li>
<li>Optimize application performance and scalability
</li>
<li>Assist Project Leadership conduct project risk identification and mitigation planning
</li>
<li>Provide support to the sales effort on new business development pursuits, assisting with RFP responses, architecture assessments and technical qualifications
</li>
</ul>
