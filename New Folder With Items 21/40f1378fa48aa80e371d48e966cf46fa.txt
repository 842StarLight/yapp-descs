Role Overview
<br><br>
This position is for an experienced professional with deep knowledge and highly developed technical expertise who can lead the Electric Power Systems Military Spares supply and demand planning process and be responsible for making improvements to processes and systems to enhance performance of the team.
<br><br>
<b>Primary Responsibilities:</b><br>
Lead the development of strategies for materials planning and SIOP.
<br>Spearhead the development of standardized processes within materials planning and SIOP to optimize the utilization of business resources and meet customer expectations for delivery.
<br>Lead the development of indicators that enable visibility to short and long-term market trends and demand.
<br>Analyze demand streams and lead in the establishment of data relationship linkages.
<br>Collaborate with product development leaders and sales and marketing team leaders to develop demand forecasts.
<br>Analyze and support the development of new technologies to improve materials planning and SIOP capability, sustainability, and reliability.
<br>Guide the establishment of clear and robust metrics to measure progress through process improvements for SIOP.
<br>Manage monthly SIOP process, action plans, issue resolution, and escalations
<br><br>
Qualifications
<br><br>
<b>Basic Qualifications:</b><br>
This position requires a Bachelor’s degree in the appropriate discipline and 5 years of relevant experience or an Advanced degree in the appropriate discipline and 3 years of relevant experience.
<br>In the absence of a degree, 9 years of relevant experience is required.
<br><br>
<b>Preferred Qualifications:</b><br>
Experience with SAP
<br>Experience with background in Business Objects or comparable analytics software
<br>SIOP Certification is preferred.
<br>APICS certification is preferred.
<br>Lean and/or Six Sigma certification is preferred.
<br>Experience with making improvements in forecasting accuracy, planning availability, OTD, inventory, and past due.
<br>Experience presenting to mid to executive level leadership
<br>Experience with implementation of Lean principles, tools and processes
<br>Business analytics experience with demonstrated analytical skills specifically in demand forecasting utilizing MAPE, Bias and Forecast Error
<br>Experience in ACE and/or Lean principles, tools and processes.
