<h2>Manager, Sample Operations</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>The Manager, Sample Operations is responsible for the Sample Accountability/Management under the PDMA guidelines to ensure FDA compliance implemented for any sampling through personal and non-personal promotion. The Manager is also responsible for leading and maintaining sample shipment programs (Representative and Direct to Practitioner) operations and administration with internal departments and the vendor. Vendor management to ensure all guidelines and processes are implemented to adhere to PDMA requirements and Company policies.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Manage and continuously improve sample operations for optimal and operational performance</li>
<li>
Partner with brand managers to provide all sample allocations and direct to practitioner program details</li>
<li>
Ensure that field-based and internal department actions are in compliance with PDMA governing regulations</li>
<li>
Work with Compliance to ensure that Bausch Health complies with federal (Sunshine Act) and state law reporting requirements impacting interactions with Health Care Professionals (HCPs) and Sales Representatives as it relates to samples</li>
<li>
Set up and run status meetings with business units, vendors, etc</li>
<li>
Partner with Supply Chain for sample shipments to vendor</li>
<li>
Partner with Sales Operations (CRM Veeva team) to implement any changes/additions in Veeva</li>
<li>
Inventory management with all stakeholders, vendors, supply chain, etc</li>
<li>
Maintain diligent adherence to operating metrics, including accurate sample allocations, and potential upcoming issues and opportunities</li>
<li>
Keeps up to date with legislation and other developments that affect sample operations</li>
<li>
Work with internal stakeholders and vendors to coordinate sample programs, develop work instructions</li>
<li>
Manage Sample Management/Accountability vendor and implement processes: Closeouts , Monthly inventory , Implement SLTs, Annual inventories, Signature Audits, For Cause Audits – report to Compliance, Random Audits, Transfers/Adjustments, Manage expired/damaged/short dated products, Support Field Sales teams with any issues with sampling activity, Reports – Rep inventory , Manage approve/create work directives with vendor</li>
<li>
Manage shipping invoices for each program, submit to Accounts Payable</li>
<li>
Manage sample DTP Program to effectively sample HCPs through non representative channels (i.e. web, fax back, etc.)</li>
<li>
Reporting and Analysis, monthly, weekly, any frequency per program requirements</li>
<li>
Support Field Sales team on sampling functions, inquiries, etc</li>
<li>
New hire representative training</li>
<li>
Partner with IT for any system enhancements, reporting, etc</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
