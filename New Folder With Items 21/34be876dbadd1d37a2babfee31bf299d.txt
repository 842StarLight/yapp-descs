<h2>Data Center Design Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>POSITION: Data Center Design Engineer</b></p>
<p><b>
LOCATION: Position to train in NYC and then to be located in SF</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Opportunity Snapshot</b></p>
<p>
Primary responsibilities include record maintenance for all assigned facilities; Assign customer’s space and power; Issue Work Orders to instruct facility technician on task to be performed and for project tracking; Create Project Bill of Materials for customer installations, cable ties capacity augments and facility expansion projects. Assist sales with customer’s technical requests; Aid operations with testing and activation of new products and services.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
The Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>/B.T. College degree related to technology, electrical or mechanical.</li>
<li>Minimum 2 years’ experience in telecommunications environment.</li>
<li>Minimum of 2 years of engineering and planning experience.</li>
<li>Must be detail oriented with excellent communications, interpersonal and oral/written presentation skills.</li>
<li>Advanced skills with ACAD.</li>
<li>Proficiency with Microsoft Office Suite (Access, PowerPoint, Word, Outlook, Explorer, Visio) and or/equivalents.</li>
<li>Proven organizational ability and attention to detail is a must.</li>
<li>Strong analytical skills.</li>
<li>Must be a self-starter, proactive, flexible, and able to work within deadlines.</li>
<li>Experience working in a Data Center or Colocation environment.</li>
<li>Work in central office environment with hands-on experience.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p><b>
</b></p>
<p><b>The Role</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Create and updated facility floor plans using ACAD.</li>
<li>Update space, power and cross connect databases.</li>
<li>Maintenance of monthly facility capacity reports for space, power and cross connects.</li>
<li>Assignments of new customer racks, cabinets, cages, panels and conduits.</li>
<li>Creation of engineering packages for the installations of ladder rack, fiber raceway, cable ties, power and grounding.</li>
<li>Creation of engineering packages for space and connectivity assignments.</li>
<li>Preparation of the Project Bill of Materials for all installation projects.</li>
<li>Creation of Work Orders to instruct facility technicians on task to be performed and to track installation projects and sub-Contractor’s work.</li>
<li>Assist sales team with customer’s technical requests and support operations.</li>
<li>Visitation to multiple DLR sites throughout the work year.</li>
<br>
</ul>
