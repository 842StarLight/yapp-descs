<h2>National Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>SUMMARY: Manage existing business as well as developing and executing the company’s strategies for our West Coast key national retailers. <b>Candidate must have experience with key accounts that include Krogers, Safeway, Savemart, and Albertsons. </b></p>
<p><b>This candidate must be based in (CA, WA, OR, AZ, UT or NV) </b></p>
<p><b>MAJOR DUTIES: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Effectively manage all areas of business responsibilities including: sales, customer profit, forecasting, internal and external communication, reporting, administrative and customer service.</li>
<li>Monitor sales and analyze performance trends to identify issues and opportunities.</li>
<li>Plan and execute customer focused sales functions, sales presentations and business reviews.</li>
<li>Understand and evaluate category segments and potential expansion<i>.</i>
</li>
<li>Develop and maintain an active relationship and dialogue with the customer(s) at all levels of the organization.</li>
<li>Direct the collaborative planning process to improve customer planning and alignment.</li>
<li>Provide analytical support to senior management in planning new product launches and existing product expansions.</li>
<li>Drive development of daily, weekly and monthly analytics to support business using Retail Link and SDS.</li>
<li>Work closely with a business/replenishment analyst to monitor the business daily</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>EDUCATION: </b></p>
<ul><li>BA/BS Degree in business, economics, marketing, or related field.</li></ul>
<p><b>EXPERIENCE/SKILLS: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 5 – 7 years sales and key account experience in CPG with a strong track record of success.</li>
<li>Strong leader with excellent communication, influencing, negotiation, and analytical skills. Minimum college level statistics / computation math skills.</li>
<li>Exhibit ability to develop high quality relationships based on trust and integrity with the appropriate customer contacts to drive growth.</li>
<li>Demonstrate flexibility to adapt to quickly changing business environment and varying priorities.</li>
<li>Must be able to coordinate and prioritize high work volume and pressing time requirements.</li>
<li>Strong verbal, written, and interpersonal skills. Second language a plus.</li>
<li>Ability to work well in a team environment.</li>
<li>Strong Microsoft Office skills (specifically Excel) required computer skills using the Microsoft software (word, excel, PPT). Working knowledge of ERP forecasting, planning systems preferred.</li>
</ul>
