<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"></h2>
<p><b>Responsibilities
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p><b>May define and communicate vision for analytics and analytics priorities, ensuring alignment with business unit priorities and IT's commitment Data Definitions &amp; Integrity
</b></p></li>
<li><b>Confirms data definitions with key stakeholders (including global and regional functional leads, Finance, IT and other functions)
</b></li>
<li><b>Coordinates with the business unit to fix identified data integrity and inconsistency issues Tool Selection and Development
</b></li>
<li><b>Identifies tools for an automated analytics platform
</b></li>
<li><b>Partners with:
</b></li>
<li><b>Business unit SME's to determine data requirements/constraints - Information Technology for support and resources - Internal Communications for roll out of tools and updates Reporting
</b></li>
<li><b>May supervise executive level reports such as quarterly results Data Analysis
</b></li>
<li><b>Mines data for patterns, relationships, issues and consistency on a regular basis Business Planning/Recommendations
</b></li>
<li><b>Uses insights from data mining exercise to build advanced analytics reports like org planning, cost analysis, predictive analytics along with implication of data (advanced and predictive) Client and Stakeholder Management
</b></li>
<li><b>Builds relationships and partners with stakeholders (i.e. IT, Finance, business leaders) to understand requests, make recommendations and implement solutions
</b></li>
<li><b>Coaches client group to present data and insights to business leaders for better adoption and use of insights Thought Leadership
</b></li>
<li><b>Brings value to each ask and provides thought leadership on how to develop and use analytics capability
</b></li>
<li><b>Provides support and development for Analysts
</b></li>
</ul>
<p></p>

</b></p>
<ul>
<li><b>10+ years related experience preferred
</b></li>
<li><b>Bachelor's degree or equivalent in Statistics, Mathematics, Economics, Finance, Data Science, or relevant engineering discipline preferred
</b></li>
</ul>
