<h2>Construction Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Position Summary
<br>OCF Construction LLC is in search of a Construction Project Manager to join our growing team. The primary role will be to proactively oversee and manage all aspects of designated construction projects. We are looking for someone who is highly organized, has a solid understanding of residential construction and a strong background in project management. OCF Construction LLC offers a competitive benefits package including health insurance, 401k and bonus opportunities.
<br><br>
<b>Essential Functions:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Work directly with vendors, architects, engineers, government agencies and project owners to determine the specifications of the project</li>
<li>Negotiate contracts with external vendors to reach profitable agreements</li>
<li>Determine needed resources (manpower, equipment, and materials) from start to finish with attention to budgetary limitations</li>
<li>Execute timely and detailed buyout of all project trades, materials, and equipment.</li>
<li>Proactively oversee and manage construction projects to achieve budget and timeline goals, plans and specifications, safety, building codes, and corporate standards</li>
<li>Oversee Site Superintendents, ensuring they have current project information/drawings</li>
<li>Ensure adherence to all health and safety standards and report issues</li>
<li>Determine all necessary permits (by project) and manage the process through procurement</li>
<li>Prepare all Owner Change Orders and Subcontractor Change Orders for review and approval</li>
<li>Review, audit and approve requests for subcontractor payments and collect corresponding lien waivers</li>
<li>Prepare monthly invoices for review and submit to Owner/Bank</li>
<li>Provide monthly cost analysis for all active construction assignments</li>
<li>Define workflow and associated timelines (in conjunction with Sales team) for the efficient management and coordination of homeowner selections, walk-throughs, and delivery against settlement targets</li>
<li>Oversee the pricing of homeowner upgrades and customizations as presented/defined by the Sales team</li>
<li>Oversee projects to 100% completion, including punch lists and warranty issues, providing closeout documentation to both OCF Construction and homeowners in an organized and timely manner</li>
<li>Build and maintain relationships with key subcontractors to enhance performance and understand pricing trends on an ongoing basis Post-Construction</li>
<li>Maintain client communication and satisfaction during and after the project</li>
</ul>
<b>Personal Contributions &amp; Attributes:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Ability to work in a fast pace environment while maintaining professionalism at all time and meeting strict deadlines</li>
<li>Ability to act independently, prioritize tasks, problem solve and exercise good judgment</li>
</ul>
