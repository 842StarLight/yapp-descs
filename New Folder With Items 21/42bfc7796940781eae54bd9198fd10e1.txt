<p><b>B2C Sales Specialist</b></p>
<p>
Wayfair (NYSE: W) is one of the world's largest destinations for home goods. We are looking for a self-motivated individual with a closer mindset to join our Specialized Sales team. This position will report into local sales managers. Compensation includes a competitive hourly wage, great benefits, paid time off, and a monthly bonus plan. If you love to connect with customers and help them discover more amazing products, then Wayfair is the place for you to start a rewarding career!</p>
<p><b>
What You'll Do/Who You Are</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
You listen to your customers and use your expertise, creativity, and passion to discover more products and inspire their confidence to buy</li>
<li>
You are engaging and can leverage your personality to quickly connect with others</li>
<li>
You build trust and rapport with your customers</li>
<li>
You meet or exceed all targets for revenue, close/conversion rate, CSAT, and more</li>
<li>
You can simultaneously navigate multiple software applications and technologies</li>
<li>
You efficiently manage your time and always put the customer experience first</li>
<li>
You are able to overcome any objection and don t take no for an answer</li>
<li>
You believe relationships matter, love being part of a team and are focused on achieving both team/individual goals</li>
<li>
You promote appropriate product solutions and inspire customers to buy from Wayfair by seizing the opportunity for the sale on every call</li>
<li>
You play to win and have a positive outlook</li>
<li>
You solve problems by thinking critically and creatively</li>
<li>
You strive to always do the right thing</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
What You'll Need</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Excellent communication and relationship building skills</li>
<li>
2+ years of sales experience, preferably in a solutions-based selling environment (Retail, SaaS, Account Management, etc. )</li>
<li>
You demonstrate the ability to think like an owner of the business</li>
<li>
You demonstrate the ability to be coachable and open-minded to feedback</li>
<li>
You demonstrate the ability to sound confident in your product recommendations</li>
<li>
You are reliable and always strive to be on time</li>
</ul>
