<h2>Senior Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Job Description<br>
<br>
<p><b>Why is this role important at Infobip?</b></p>
<p>
Imagine having the opportunity to develop business in a local market from scratch while at the same time being supported by a global organization with an established local presence in more than 60 countries worldwide. We are on the lookout for an enterpreunerial spirit to help establish our business operations in the US market. Onboarding you with extensive experience and industry knowledge, we are adapting our organization to think locally. We believe this role will be crucial to onboarding local enterprise clients that will boost the overall success of the US market, one of the key markets in Infobip's expansion.</p>
<p><b>
You know you are doing a good job when you:</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>
Prospect and develop sales opportunities from the target accounts list and Fortune 1000 list</p></li>
<li><p>
Manage complete and complex sales-cycles often presenting to C-level executives the value of our full suite of services</p></li>
<li><p>
Complete regular and consistent capture of activities on active accounts within the Salesforce CRM</p></li>
<li><p>
Forecast sales activity and revenue achievement while creating satisfied customers</p></li>
<li><p>
Evangelize the product / core vision through product demonstrations and account specific initiatives</p></li>
<li><p>
Close new business according to targets, objectives, and plans</p></li>
<li><p>
Conduct territory / vertical identification and research to formalize a go-to-market strategy and create qualified target accounts</p></li>
<li><p>
Develop your pipeline through a combination of cold calling, email campaigns, and market sector knowledge / intelligence</p></li>
<li><p>
Manage the end-to-end sales process through engagement of appropriate resources such as Sales Engineers, Professional Services, Executives, Partners etc.</p></li>
<li><p>
Engage in the analysis of competitors and business intelligence activities to improve and shorten the sales process</p></li>
<li><p>
Attend industry events in order to expand your network and boost our business relationships</p></li>
<li><p>
Collect product feedback and convey ideas for improvement to our technical teams</p></li>
<li><p>
Support the team leader in developing new team members</p></li>
</ul>
</div>
<br>

<ul>

BA/BS degree or equivalent experience</p></li>

6 years of experience quota carrying and selling software or technology solutions</p></li>

Account management experience</p></li>

Experience managing the sales cycle from business champion to the C-level</p></li>

Experience managing and closing complex sales cycles and demonstrated success owning all aspects of territory management</p></li>

Track record of success in reaching quotas and closing Fortune 1000 deals</p></li>

Successful history of acquisition of new business sales, with the ability to prove consistent delivery against targets</p></li>

Strong and demonstrated written, verbal, and presentation skills</p></li>
</ul>
