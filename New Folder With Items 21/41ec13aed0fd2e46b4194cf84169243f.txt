<h2>Technical Applications Scientist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Position Summary:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>Provides technical direction and support to customers on operation and maintenance of company products. Serves as customer contact on technical and service-related problems. Demonstrates uses and advantages of products. Works closely with sales and marketing, QA/QC, research and development to address customer concerns, determine corrective action and customer follow-up. May be responsible for a designated region.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Professionally, efficiently and conclusively triage, troubleshoot and resolve customer’s technical questions
</li>
<li>Assist customers with the design and interpretation of gene expression, genotyping and “Next-Generation” sequencing experiments
</li>
<li>Provide technical guidance regarding the choice and use of the appropriate Illumina product to existing and prospective customers
</li>
<li>Assist customers with the selection of SNPs for custom genotyping projects by utilizing public and private sources and proprietary software applications
</li>
<li>Triage and dispatch issues requiring field support to Illumina Engineers and Applications Scientists
</li>
<li>Log and manage customer issues using Illumina’s issue-tracking database
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Listed responsibilities are an essential, but not exhaustive list, of the usual duties associated with the position. Changes to individual responsibilities may occur due to business needs.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Requirements:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Hands-on laboratory experience using, developing or troubleshooting a wide range of molecular biology protocols such as PCR, sequencing, gene expression analysis, genotyping, and related procedures
</li>
<li>Teaching experience desirable
</li>
<li>Excellent analytical, problem solving and interpersonal skills required
</li>
<li>Outstanding written and verbal communication and training skills a MUST
</li>
<li>Experience working in any service role desired
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>All listed requirements are deemed as essential functions to this position; however, business conditions may require reasonable accommodations for additional task and responsibilities.
