<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>MapAnything - RVP Sales
</b></h2>
<p></p>
<p><b>About MapAnything, A Salesforce Company:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>MapAnything is a pioneer in Location Intelligence software for field sales and service, enabling more than 2,000 global enterprises by combining location intelligence, integrated asset tracking, and route optimization to drive productivity and better customer experiences. MapAnything is a Salesforce Premier ISV Partner who has received IDC’s Innovator Award in Field Service for Manufacturing, and G2 Crowd’s Leader for Field Service Management and Field Sales Tools. To learn more, visit www.mapanything.com .
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The RVP, Sales will be responsible for setting and executing the MapAnything strategy for our Commercial Sales team. This executive will assume leadership of a growing sales organization, including first line managers, and account executives to help drive complex enterprise deal transactions.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>Set and execute an aggressive customer acquisition strategy to generate annual growth in revenue and bookings for the region
</p></li>
<li><p>Maintain key customer relationships and develop and implement strategies for expanding the company’s customer base
</p></li>
<li><p>Provide detailed and accurate sales forecasting
</p></li>
<li><p>Manage overall sales process, set appropriate metrics for sales funnel management
</p></li>
<li><p>Plan and manage at both the strategic and operational levels
</p></li>
<li><p>Position the business to grow successfully beyond its current targets, increasing the revenue of the business significantly year on year.
</p></li>
<li><p>Operating in a high-growth environment, by leading the enterprise sales organization, developing and expanding our footprint.
</p></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Required Skills/Experience:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li><p>7+ years experience leading teams in enterprise strategic sales
</p></li>
<li><p>Strong track record of recruiting, developing and retaining a high performing enterprise sales organization
</p></li>
<li><p>Consistent over-achievement of quota and revenue goals
</p></li>
<li><p>10+ years in software and/or applications sales
</p></li>
<li><p>Proven track record of building satisfied, loyal and referenceable customers
</p></li>
<li><p>C-suite level resources, aligned with regional executives, AEs, and internal leadership teams to present a single front internally and help represent a single vision for our customers
</p></li>
<li><p>Proven success working within a highly matrixed organization and establishing strong relationships across all functions
</p></li>
<li><p>Strong operational and analytical abilities
</p></li>
<li><p>Strategic enterprise sales experience and revenue achievement selling multiple enterprise software offerings
</p></li>
<li><p>Experience selling cloud based enterprise applications is strongly preferred
</p></li>
<li><p>Bachelor degree strongly preferred
</p></li>
<li><p>Passion for nonprofit organizations
</p></li>
</ul>
