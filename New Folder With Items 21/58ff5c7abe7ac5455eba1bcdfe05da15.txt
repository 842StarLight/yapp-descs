<p>Job Description</p>
<p>This is an exciting role overseeing advertising campaigns from brief to completion. You will assist the Account Director in a wide spectrum of display advertising focused on Digital and Print.</p>
<p>
You’ll also liaise with internal departments across Haymarket, to ensure campaigns reach their full potential. You’ll also start to build external relationships with contacts at some of the UKs largest spending agencies to enable you to put them at the heart of what you do.</p>
<br>
