<h2>Marketing Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div><div><div>
<p><b>Summary:</b></p>
<p>
We are looking for an enthusiastic and adaptable professional to create and execute marketing strategy, organize and create promotional agendas and campaigns. The Marketing Specialist possess great written and organizational skills. The position will organize marketing efforts by creating a link between internal and external marketing materials, The Marketing Specialist work closely with internal training team to ensure employees hear the same messages that are sent out to the marketplace., trade shows, and other customer meetings. The ideal candidate will have knowledge of best practices in digital marketing, web copywriting and graphical skills.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Implements marketing and advertising campaigns by assembling and analyzing sales forecasts; preparing marketing and advertising strategies, plans, and objectives; planning and organizing promotional presentations; updating calendars.</li>
<li>Tracks product line sales and costs by analyzing and entering sales, expense, and new business data.</li>
<li>Prepares marketing reports by collecting, analyzing, and summarizing sales data. Keeps promotional materials ready by coordinating requirements with graphics department; inventorying stock; placing orders; verifying receipt.</li>
<li>Supports sales staff by providing sales data, market trends, forecasts, account analyses, new product information; relaying customer services requests.</li>
<li>Researches competitive products by identifying and evaluating product characteristics, market share, pricing, and advertising; maintaining research databases.</li>
<li>Plans meetings and trade shows by identifying, assembling, and coordinating requirements; establishing contacts; developing schedules and assignments; coordinating mailing lists.</li>
<li>Monitors budgets by comparing and analyzing actual results with plans and forecasts.</li>
<li>Create and execute lead generation programs.</li>
<li>Analyze data to determine campaign efficiency and ROI.</li>
<li>Ensure creative marketing materials undergo regular review and adhere to brand guidelines. Liaise with external vendors to execute promotional events and campaigns</li>
<li>Collaborate with marketing and other professionals to coordinate brand awareness and marketing efforts Plan and execute initiatives to reach the target audience through appropriate channels (social media, e-mail, TV etc.)</li>
<li>Implement tactics developed in the marketing plan.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>General Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
BA in Marketing, Communications, Business or equivalent</li>
<li>2-5 years of relevant marketing, strategic communications and/or graphic design experience Interest in technology or high-tech equipment is a definite plus</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Office software (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook) Knowledgeable in Adobe software (Illustrator, InDesign, Photoshop)</li>
<li>Strong organizational skills and ability to multi-task while maintaining attention to detail</li>
<li>Strong verbal and written communication skills Ability to work in a team environment.</li>
<li>Ability to represent LasX in a professional manner to all callers/visitors to LasX office</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>
Physical Requirements</p>
<ul>
<li>
Extensive time working at a computer workstation</li>
<li>Ability to travel 10%</li>
</ul>
