<h2>Senior Staff Accountant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>One Health Solutions, LLC (“OHS”) is seeking an ambitious Senior Accountant to fill a challenging role with opportunities for professional growth and advancement within a group of growing technology companies. OHS is a healthcare management platform that provides the conduit for both human and animal health, located in the South Hills of Pittsburgh. The position reports to the Corporate Controller with daily interaction with the CFO and other members of Executive Management. Primary initial responsibilities are:</p>
<ul>
<li>Maintain General Ledger according to GAAP.</li>
<li>Generate and maintain closing schedules and related back up.</li>
<li>Record journal entries and conduct balance sheet reconciliations.</li>
<li>Oversee transactional data entry process for accuracy and completeness.</li>
<li>Reconcile bank accounts, monitor cash balances and maintain cash forecast.</li>
<li>Prepare payables analysis and process bill payments to vendors.</li>
<li>Advise and assist Staff Accountant with data entry and resolution of discrepancies.</li>
<li>Prepare and file quarterly Sales and Use Tax returns.</li>
<li>Keep books tax and audit ready.</li>
<li>Oversee biweekly payroll submission for completeness and record related journal entries.</li>
<li>Assist Controller with preparation of monthly financial reporting package.</li>
<li>Provide timely information and special reports as requested.</li>
<li>Assist with implementation of new tools and technology, including new ERP.</li>
<li>Streamline procedure and develop policy.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Related Skills and Qualifications: </b></p>
<p>Knowledge of GAAP, Technical Curiosity, Ability to Gather, Analyze and Metabolize Information, Identify and Solve Problems, Ability to Work Independently and on a Team, General Business Acumen, Attention to Detail, Confidentiality, Strong Communication and Responsiveness, Exceptional Organizational Skills, Ability to Meet Deadlines, Interest in Technology Systems and Commitment to Process Improvement</p>
<p><b>Experience: </b></p>
<p>7+ years of accounting experience (3+ general ledger focus);</p>
<p>Industry experience in veterinary medicine, human healthcare, and/or software technology a plus;</p>
<p>NetSuite and QuickBooks system experience preferred;</p>
<p>Progressive technology use, highly proficient in Excel;</p>
<p>Education Requirements: Bachelors degree in Accounting, Finance or related degree; Master’s degree preferred.</p>
