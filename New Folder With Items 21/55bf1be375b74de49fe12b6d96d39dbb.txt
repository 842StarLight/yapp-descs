<h2>Customer Success Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>UNION is looking for a Customer Success Director to lead and scale our customer support team as we rapidly expand our products to new markets all over the nation.</p>
<p>Our company makes bars and restaurants smarter, faster, and more profitable by providing them with the best point of sale system the hospitality industry has ever seen, paired with an unparalleled free 24/7 support team.</p>
<p>If you love the art of problem-solving, have a natural affinity for technology, enjoy digging into hard work, and thrive on making the impossible happen, you may be the support director we’re looking for.</p>
<p>Responsibilities</p>
<ul>
<li>Become an expert on the UNION Point of Sale System and related products</li>
<li>Proficiently and consistently respond to and solve issues ranging from basic to advanced</li>
<li>Manage a support team to accurately capture and log case notes, document all activities, and manage cases in a way that is clear, concise and actionable by other internal UNION teams</li>
<li>Manage support team to properly document all cases after each shift and debrief UNION team</li>
<li>Analyze and discuss cases upon ticket closure to help inform root cause of merchant contact</li>
<li>Build a close relationship with staff at many UNION venues</li>
<li>Author and manage internal and external knowledge base content</li>
</ul>
<p>Experience</p>
<ul>
<li>3-5 years experience in customer support management</li>
<li>Point of sale and service industry experience</li>
<li>Exceptionally personable approach to problem-solving</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to be diplomatic, and tactful in dealing with diverse customers with varying skill levels and understanding</li>
<li>Excellent verbal, written, and interpersonal communication skills in English</li>
<li>Authorized to work in the United States</li>
<li>Austin, Texas based</li>
</ul>

<p>UNION is a fast, intuitive, and customizable iPad-based point of sale software designed to handle high-touch, high-volume hospitality locations. We’ve traded the bulky POS registers of yesterday for a user-friendly system that caters specifically to the needs of this industry.</p>
<p>UNION is spearheading the revolution against all current POS solutions out there, and is continuing to gain momentum with every supporter who joins the movement. We are committed to continuous innovation, and we're looking for the best and the brightest to help us solve tough challenges for our customers.</p>
