<h2>Sale Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>POSITION SUMMARY</b>: The Fuel &amp; Lubricants Sales Representative will grow fuel and lubricant sales and gross margin to meet and or exceed quota and objectives for the assigned territory. This is a revenue generating position that interacts daily with customers/prospects and a variety of employees. A strong sense of organization and self-motivation is required. This person will be expected to organize, manage, and develop a relationship with customers and prospects in their territory. They will gain knowledge of industry, products, and services. This position will represent and communicate Heritage Petroleum products, services, policies, procedures, and programs to the customer/prospect.</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Meet and/or exceed sales and profit objectives</li>
<li>Develop call schedule for territory</li>
<li>Maintain and utilize CRM software to record sales activity for all prospects/customers</li>
<li>Attend and participate in sales and training meetings</li>
<li>Develop relationships with customers/prospects</li>
<li>Organize and efficiently manage sales territory</li>
<li>Implement company policies and initiatives</li>
<li>Work with supplier representatives</li>
<li>Provide technical information to customers/prospects</li>
<li>Assist with credit collections</li>
<li>Secure and document competitive information</li>
<li>Continued product and general skills education</li>
<li>Submit all required reports and information in a timely manner</li>
<li>Work with all company personnel in a spirit of cooperation and teamwork</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>

Ability to perform basic calculations and mathematical figures. Ability to exercise sound judgment in solving practical problems.
