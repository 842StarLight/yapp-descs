<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Description</b></h2>
<p></p>
<p>
Pick up and deliver freight between Service Centers and customers and support the selling process.<br>
<br>
<b>ESSENTIAL JOB DUTIES/RESPONSIBILITES:</b><br>
<br>
1. Operate tractor-trailer combination, including doubles (and triples, where applicable) and/or straight trucks<br>
2. Perform daily pre-trip and safety inspections on equipment<br>
3. Hook/unhook trailers and converter dollies to/from a tractor and/or trailer<br>
4. Perform freight handling using appropriate motorized and manual equipment, including but not limited to: forklift, pallet jack and hand truck<br>
5. Secure freight inside trailers using appropriate tools and supplies, including but not limited to: pallets, straps and rope<br>
6. Recoup/repair damaged freight when necessary<br>
7. Verify and complete required documentation and reports<br>
8. Comply with hazardous material regulations and procedures<br>
9. Collect cash or checks for freight charges, as required, and maintain required documentation<br>
10. Follow dispatch instructions and communicate with dispatch as required, including but not limited to: delays, arrivals and equipment problems<br>
11. Communicate with customers to determine pick-up or delivery needs and solicit additional business<br>
12. Demonstrate internal and external customer service<br>
13. Ask for additional business from customers, and provide leads to sales for potential new opportunities<br>
14. May be required to perform chaining of vehicle tires<br>
15. May be required to perform job duties of a road driver or a dock employee where operationally necessary<br>
16. Comply with all applicable laws/regulations, as well as company policies/procedures<br>
17. Perform other duties as required</p>
