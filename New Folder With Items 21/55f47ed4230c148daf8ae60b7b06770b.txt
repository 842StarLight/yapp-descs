<p><b>
Inventory Manager</b></p>
<p>
FUNCTIONAL DESCRIPTION OF WORK</p>
<ul>
<li>
Maintain accurate stock count..</li>
<li>
Communicates with co-workers, Plant Managers / Supervisors to ensure accurate reporting, material usage, and labor reporting.</li>
<li>
Creates new procedures to become more efficient and to respond to business changes; heavy emphasis in automated processes.</li>
<li>
Balances inventory between all divisions through our inventory system.</li>
<li>
Reviews and audits inventory reports and transactions electronically.</li>
<li>
Research, Analyze, and Reconcile all variances reported</li>
<li>
Conduct random Inventory Audits</li>
<li>
Identify any procedural/process trends that negatively impact inventory and communicate accordingly so proper action can be taken</li>
<li>
Perform necessary inventory reconciliation and adjustments for items</li>
<li>
Rectify Receiving, Picking, and Replenishment errors in a timely fashion</li>
<li><p>
Completes requests from Purchasing, AP or Production Manager</p></li>
<li>
Verifies that materials are received in accordance with Bill of Material.</li>
<li>
Works with Accounting / Shipping to resolve shipping and receiving discrepancies.</li>
</ul>
