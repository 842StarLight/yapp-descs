<h2>DATA ANALYTICS SPECIALIST I</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Summary</b>:</p>
<p>
Data Analytics Specialist I (DAS-I) is responsible for coordinating, analyzing and reporting database marketing initiatives based on marketing data pulled from numerous data sources and analytic tools.</p>
<p>
The DAS will communicate data informed insights in easy to understand formats to a broad base of individuals and functional groups.</p>
<p>
This position supports marketing strategies and direct marketing program development to achieve KPI goals for our database-driven clients.</p>
<p>
This individual is expected to be highly analytical and is proficient in communicating results to a broad audience.</p>
<p>
The Role will report directly to Analysis Manager and work frequently with CEO to support client marketing efforts.<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Analyze marketing campaigns for effectiveness and develop data informed actions that lead to improvements in campaign sales and other s KPI's.</li>
<li>Develop client performance and KPI forecast for a broad range of marketing campaigns and direct marketing initiatives</li>
<li>Develop and oversee A/B marketing test models. Measure, optimize and report outcomes of marketing tests</li>
<li>Use data mining techniques and analyze marketing campaigns and client performance trends to improve client margins and other KPI's</li>
<li>Translate raw data and marketing results into easily to understand findings including preparation of executive summaries and monthly standardized reports</li>
<li>Create Ad-hoc reporting and support of new report development as needed</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<i>2+ </i>years<i> (preferred) </i>of practical analytics experience, preferably in a retail marketing/analytics environment</li>
<li>Advanced proficiency in MS Office Suite, primarily numbers-based component Excel</li>
<li>Working knowledge of MS SQL Server coding/syntax required</li>
<li>Experience in Python, R and supportive analytics software a plus</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree in market research, business, communications or related field</li>
<li>Proficient with mathematics, statistics, and analytical processes</li>
<li>An excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Motivated self-starter who is organized and detail-oriented</li>
<li>Excellent oral and written communication skills</li>
<li>Ability to work under pressure, manage frequent overlapping demands and show high attention to detail</li>
</ul>
