<h2>Event Marketing Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Innovid is the leading independent advertising technology platform with TV at the core empowering brands, agencies, and publishers to personalize, deliver, and measure high-performing omni-channel ad experiences. Innovid partners with all facets of the digital advertising ecosystem, serving a global client base with offices in New York, Chicago, Los Angeles, Detroit, London, Tel Aviv, Sydney, Tokyo, and Singapore. Please visit www.innovid.com ( http://www.innovid.com/ ) for more information.
<br>-
<br><br>
As the Event Marketing Coordinator, you will work closely with the Director, Event Marketing to produce hosted and sponsored marketing events. This role requires experience handling event logistics, managing vendor relationships, promoting attendance, and generating leads through events. You will need to be detail oriented and enjoy coordinating every aspect of events––from ordering catering, booking travel, to collaborating with sales on post-event follow-up. You are able to execute multiple events at a time without compromising on the details and have a passion for events and marketing.
<br>-
<br><br>
<b>What you'll do:</b><br>
-
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Help plan and execute all marketing events, including trade shows, conferences, panel discussions, happy hours, workshops, and more.</li>
<li>Own local/regional event activations for sales team such as roadshows and in-market parties, as lead point for digital and F&amp;B needs.</li>
<li>Manage pre-event logistics such as timelines, vendor agreements, invitations and promotion, and internal staffing.</li>
<li>Handle all day-of event logistics such as setup, vendor relations, staff requirements, and breakdown.</li>
<li>Research and identify potential trade shows and conferences to sponsor and attend that will support our marketing goals.</li>
<li>Collaborate with the Director, Event Marketing and marketing team for all outreach logistics, including email invites, landing pages, and post-event follow-up messages.</li>
<li>Support with collecting and importing lead information from events to accurately distribute data to sales for outreach.</li>
<li>Coordinate with the team on event related collateral, swagordering, and distribution of speaker gifts..</li>
<li>Research and schedule site visits.</li>
<li>Track invoices and reconcile event-related budgets.</li>
<li>Travel to events to provide on-site support and logistics (as needed).</li>
</ul>
<b>What you'll need:</b><br>
-
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>2-4 years in event marketing</li>
<li>Ability to execute logistics for multiple events across various cities</li>
<li>Exceptional project coordination and attention to detail is a must</li>
<li>Knowledge of marketing, sales, and events tools such as Hubspot and Splash that a plus</li>
<li>Occasional travel may be required</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to work independently in a fast-paced environment, and within a cross-functional team.</li>
<li>An understanding of the ad tech, marketing tech, video management, media and/or TV is a plus.</li>
<li>You are eager and willing to learn and are willing to roll up your sleeves and get your hands dirty.</li>
</ul>
