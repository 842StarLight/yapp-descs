<h2>Patient Services Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div><div><div><div>
<b>Patient Services Representative:</b><br>
Are you ready to help clients feel and look their best?<br>
As a Water’s Edge Dermatology Patient Services Representative, that’s just what<br>
you’ll do! Every day you’ll build lasting relationships with our clients, by helping<br>
to find a provider in an office near them that will best fit their needs while answering customer inquiries, resolving customer<br>
issues, and maintaining a customer database of information.<br>
<br>
<b> Company Overview:</b><br>
Water’s Edge Dermatology is Florida’s leading premier<br>
medical and cosmetic dermatology practice with over 34 locations and over 60<br>
providers throughout Central and South Florida. Our comprehensive services<br>
cover every aspect of dermatology, and we treat patients of all ages.<br>
<br>
<b>Who is right for the job?</b><br>
We are looking for energetic, hard-working, dependable, multi-tasking customer-focused team players who are passionate<br>
about helping others and making a difference.<br>
<br>
 <b>General Summary:</b><br>
The primary function of the Patient Services Representative is to effectively<br>
handle all inbound New and Current Patient calls for all of our Water’s Edge<br>
Dermatology offices. Representatives will schedule appointments for the caller<br>
and their family by providing excellent customer service.<br>
Responsibilities include but are not limited to responding to new and existing<br>
patient inquiries, handling patient concerns, rescheduling requests while<br>
meeting performance metrics and quality service goals in a team atmosphere.<br>
<br>
<b> What we offer:</b><br>
Competitive hourly base rate with optional overtime opportunities along with a benefits package including<br>
medical, dental, vision, and ancillary insurances, 401(k), flexible spending accounts, paid time off and paid holidays.<br>
<br>
<b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</b><br>
Respond to incoming calls efficiently and effectively with the highest level of quality service<br>
Handle inbound calls with the goal of increasing business through customer satisfaction, and customer retention<br>
Answer general questions from patients with courtesy and patience<br>
Schedule, confirm, cancel and reschedule appointments as necessary<br>
Handle 70-90 inbound calls daily in a fast paced environment<br>
Enter data accurately, multitask and navigate several systems at the same time<br>
Deliver proficient over-the-phone quality/customer services<br>
<br>
<b>Required Skills:</b><br>
High school diploma or GED<br>
Proficient in Microsoft Word and Excel<br>
Computer literate and confident learning new software systems<br>
Ability to type 35 WPM<br>
Professional and confidential work ethic, strong attendance, and a positive attitude<br>
<br>
<b>Preferred Skills:</b><br>
At least two years’ customer/sales experience working in a fast-paced call center environment, preferably in the medical or insurance industry<br>
Bilingual (English/Spanish) a plus<br>
