<b>About Splash:</b><br>
We believe the most powerful marketing happens in-person. Our mission is to develop the most innovative event technology on the planet to help marketers reach and engage their target audiences in-person. From simplifying event asset creation to capturing attendee insights and measuring event impact, Splash enables marketers to efficiently scale event programs and connect event-driven engagement to business results. More than half of the Fortune 500 use Splash to streamline event marketing execution and optimize performance.
<br><br>
<b>About the team:</b><br>
Our sales team helps the world's leading brands transform their in-person marketing programs by providing technology solutions that make it easier and more efficient to create, execute and measure the impact of events at scale.
<br><br>
<b>About the role:</b><br>
You're a fit for our Account Executive if you've blown away sales goals at a previous role, and are looking for an entrepreneurial sales environment. Ideally, you are a creative problem solver, looking to master and promote event marketing best practices, and help build a world class organization. Experience in the B2B SaaS space is a BIG bonus. You must be comfortable navigating complex and consultative sales processes (Fortune 500-1000 companies), handling deal sizes averaging $50k-$100k. This is an opportunity have an immediate impact: Come build with us!
<br><br>
