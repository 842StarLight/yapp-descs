<p><b>Job Title
</b></p>
<p></p>2020 Summer Intern Program (New York)
<p></p>The Mastercard Summer Intern Program is a 10 week comprehensive program where students will gain hands on real work-life skills while having fun and contributing to a global company. As an intern, you will be challenged with completing projects that will enhance your skills as well as contribute to the department’s goals and objectives.
<br>
<br>
Program Dates &amp; Components:
<br>
<ul>
<li>June 1, 2020 – August 7, 2020
</li>
<li>Impactful assignments that drive business results
</li>
<li>Networking sessions and leadership speakers series
</li>
<li>Volunteer and team building experiences
</li>
</ul>
<br>
Program Qualifications:
<br>
<ul>
<li>Current students pursuing a Bachelor’s or Master’s degree graduating between December 2020 and May 2022
</li>
<li>Evidence of strong academics, cumulative GPA of 3.0 or above
</li>
<li>Innovative thinking and thoughtful risk taking
</li>
<li>Ability to partner across the organization
</li>
<li>Sound communication skills
</li>
</ul>
<br>
Opportunities may be available in:
<br>
<ul>
<li>Operations and Technology (Software Engineering)
</li>
<li>North America Markets (Sales)
</li>
<li>Product (Core Products, Digital Payments &amp; Labs, User Experience, etc.)
</li>
<li>Law and Franchise Integrity
</li>
<li>Human Resources
</li>
<li>Finance<br>
</li>
</ul>
