<h2>Software Developer/Analyst</h2>

<p>Professional Plumbing Group ( PPG ) manufactures and distributes high quality plumbing products and expert solutions to create best-in-class customer experiences. The PPG umbrella has three national brands with Speakman, Wolverine Brass and PlumbMaster and a history stretching back 150 years in the plumbing industry. While distinct in customer base and value proposition, the three brands share a passion for innovation and customer service excellence. The company is headquartered in Conway, SC, and operates in Glen Mills, PA as well. PPG has started a transformation process into an agile, solution-oriented organization with best-in-class sales capabilities. Professional Plumbing Group is a portfolio company of Dunes Point Capital (DPC).</p>

<p><b>POSITION SUMMARY DESCRIPTION</b></p>
<p>Professional Plumbing Group (PPG) is seeking a skilled Software Developer / Administrator to help with the development and integration of our current projects. Your duties will primarily revolve around building new software and software interfaces, as well as modifying software to fix errors, adapt it to new hardware, improve its performance, or upgrade interfaces. You will also be involved in directing system testing and validation procedures, and also working with customers or departments on technical issues including software system design and maintenance.To be successful in this role, you will need extensive knowledge of programming languages such as SQL, ASP.NET, C++, X++, C#, VB.NET, Java and Java script and the software development life-cycle including agile. Our environment is Microsoft Dynamics AX, Dynamics CRM, Power BI, EDI, Hybris e-commerce, O365 and other software/tools.</p>
