<h2>Senior Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">-
<br>The opportunity
<br>-
<br><br>
Pursuing the objective of building the highest quality team in the industry, Coveo is seeking a Senior Account Executive to grow our ever expanding account base. With a strong technical team already in place, great existing customers and a game-changing technology, this is the ideal next career move for a highly-energetic, self-driven individual who wants to grow a client portfolio that already has some solid bases yet unlimited potential.
<br><br>
<b>Typical duties include:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Passionately represent our company, share our vision and develop relationships. As an individual contributor, you will also participate to key events to generate business opportunities.</li>
<li>Build and maintain, along with our alliances managers, quality relationships with key Coveo partners to grow our business from the West, Southwest and part of the Midwest regions.</li>
<li>Build creative account strategies that focus on delivering the highest outcomes for our customers based on their very own operations.</li>
<li>Create comprehensive and compelling business proposals that challenge status-quo and convinces decision makers with crystal clear added-value.</li>
<li>Manage named accounts through the pipeline from doing the right qualification and scoping of opportunities to closing them in no time!</li>
<li>Assess the resources required, the chances of closure, the process &amp; timing and the financial benefits. This is crucial to the company's success and so is the ability to report sales activity and track results at all times.</li>
<li>Step up in the final stages, get the P.O. and consistently achieve quarterly and annual sales quotas.</li>
</ul>
-
<br>Your "success profile" should reflect
<br>-
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>B2B software experience: Your previous targets were in the tech industry, financial services or manufacturing.</li>
<li>Knowledgeable and ready: This is a senior role and our space is complex: experience in Search, Knowledge Management, CRM or ECM is a must to hit the ground running.</li>
<li>Hunter profile: You're driven and self-motivated. You are hungry and you have a track record of over-achieving quota to testify</li>
<li>Seasoned: Experience managing complex software sales cycle from business champion to the C-level is key.</li>
<li>Energetic: You thrive in a fast-paced environment</li>
</ul>
