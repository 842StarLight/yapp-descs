<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>The Role</b></h2>
<p></p>
<p>This position is to fulfill The VOID Field Operations Manager. The Field Operations Manager will be responsible for staffing, training, and operating a successful Field Operations organization. The position is located in Lindon, Utah and is currently responsible to provide assembly, configuration, and deployment of The VOID centers in North America and Europe.
<br>
<br>
The Field Operations Manager will oversee and assign work to Field Operations staff. Establishes travel work schedules to cover upcoming deployments. Establishes and assures adherence to budgets, schedules, work plans, and performance requirements. Ensures that all issues are logged in the ticketing system. Establish process and procedures for Procurement, Assembly, and Staging of equipment. Establish reports to executives as to the status of readiness for deployments. Establish a site certification for new and existing sites. Will maintain the quality of deployed assets through either site visits or remote reports. Will work closely with the Technical Operations Center to help resolve issues and maintain sites. Will contribute to and maintain the Run Book and Knowledgebase documents. Will maintain the Technical Bill of Materials and assembly documents. Will provide feedback into other groups on improvements to all aspects of the products deployed in the field.</p>
<p>Responsible for all reporting aspects of Field Operations for both site deployments, site inspections, and individual performance. Strong coaching and mentoring skills is a requirement. The position requires excellent customer service skills. They must be effective in providing feedback in a timely manner. They must possess excellent communication skills. Must be able to maintain professionalism during high-stress situations.</p>
<p>Will lead teams within the Field Operations in the following areas:<br>
</p>
<ul><li>Procurement/Inventory – Requisition, track inventory and meet delivery deadlines for all equipment required for new deployments. Prepare and package items for shipment. Receive and inventory incoming equipment.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Configuration/Assembly – Assemble and configure proprietary hardware, server racks, networking equipment, and software. Test and assure readiness for deployment.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Site Deployments – Provide onsite installation of all technical aspects which include Infrastructure, proprietary VR experiences, Audio Visual, Point of Sale and all other technical areas. Work closely with the Technical Operations Center for turnup of the new site. Certify readiness of new sites and handoff to local GM and technical staff. Travel to existing sites to assist in troubleshooting issues and help maintain uptime of the VR Experience stages. Escalate issues to other teams and help close the loop on outstanding issues and requests. Perform site inspections to help maintain the quality of our gear deployed in the field. Provide feedback on enhancements. Facilitate other repairs as needed in cooperation with the local GM.<br>
</li></ul>
