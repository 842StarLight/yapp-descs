<h2>Digital Marketing Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>We are looking for an SEO/SEM expert to manage all search engine optimization and marketing activities to join our team! You will be responsible for managing all SEO activities such as content strategy, link building and keyword strategy to increase rankings on all major search networks. You will also manage all SEM campaigns across various platforms and sites in order to maximize ROI.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Responsibilities
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Plan, develop, and implement our SEO and SEM strategy</li>
<li>Work towards organic search optimization and ROI maximization</li>
<li>Perform ongoing keyword research including discovery and expansion of keyword opportunities</li>
<li>Identify and monitor key SEO KPIs, such as redirects, click rate, bounce rate, etc.</li>
<li>Optimize copy and landing pages for SEO/SEM</li>
<li>Research and analyze competitor keywords and advertising links</li>
<li>Prepare and present reports regularly on campaign success</li>
<li>Identify our buyer persona to better target identified audiences</li>
<li>Identify problems and deficiencies and implement solutions in a timely manner</li>
<li>Suggest improvements for process and productivity optimization</li>
<li>Work with web developers and marketing team to properly implement SEO/SEM best practices</li>
<li>Execute tests, collect and analyze data and results, identify trends and insights in order to achieve maximum ROI in search campaigns</li>
<li>Stay up to date with the latest SEO/SEM and digital marketing latest trends and best practices</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>3-5 years of proven SEO/SEM experience</b>, including experience with planning and implementing successful SEO strategies</li>
<li>In-depth experience with web analytics tools, marketing, and business development</li>
<li>Experience with A/B testing and other testing metrics</li>
<li>HTML, CSS, JavaScript, a plus</li>
<li>Ability to analyze data and provide evidence-based recommendations</li>
<li>Sense of ownership and pride in your performance and its impact on company's success</li>
<li>Critical thinking, problem-solving skills, and a passionate team player</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal and communication skills</li>
<li>Solid understanding of performance marketing, conversion, and online customer acquisition</li>
</ul>

<li>Monthly perks and social activities</li>
</ul>
