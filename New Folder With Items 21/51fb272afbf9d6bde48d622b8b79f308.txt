<div><b>
The Role</b></div>
<div>
As a Software Engineer at Medallia, you will be joining our Reporting team, whose responsibility is to rebuild (and scale) the face of our flagship reporting product - the platform that tells stories out of the insane amount of data each client receives, and incites action to impact customer experiences. The most exciting part is that Medallia is just at the beginning of our journey! You’ll specialize in building responsive and elegant user interfaces that scale to hundreds of thousands of users. Be a part of the movement that is driving Medallia to even greater heights!</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Work closely with Product and Design teams to design and implement complex user interfaces and user-facing features for the next generation CEM product.</li>
<li>
Develop new user-facing features, interactive visualizations, and customer-driven enhancements to our Core Platform.</li>
<li>
Create fast, easy to use UI components that can be implemented quickly and seamlessly to support diverse clients.</li>
<li>
Participate in a thriving Front-End Engineering community, helping shape mid/long-term technical roadmap and FE architecture patterns for the global Engineering organization.</li>
<li>
Learn from a team of senior to mid-level engineers through their mentorship and technical leadership.</li>
<div>
<br>
</div>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Our Engineering Culture</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
We don’t expect to be perfect, but we are always proactively seeking out ways to help ourselves and our teams to minimize pain points within our infrastructure and code base.</li>
<li>

<li>

<li>
We build strong relationships with team members around the globe.</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Minimum Qualifications</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Bachelors in CS, Engineering, or related technical field. In lieu, relevant equivalent work experience.</li>
<li>
1+ years of delivering customer-facing web apps using a modern web stack. Our enterprise applications are built in React, with modern libraries like Apollo, Typescript, and JEST.</li>
<li>
Proven proficiency in Javascript - functional programming, promises, closure.</li>
<li>
Experience building microservices using Node, preferably supporting GraphQL.</li>
<li>
Experience ensuring the technical feasibility of UI/UX design.</li>
<li>
Previous experience with agile process, inclusive of ability to work in fast-paced and distributed teams.</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Preferred Qualifications</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Masters in CS, Engineering, or other advanced degree.</li>
<li>
Experience with: ES6/7, NodeJS, GraphQL, Docker, CSS-in-JS techniques.</li>
<li>
Bring an ownership mindset with quality orientation and DevOps experience.</li>
<li>
Demonstrate strong software engineering fundamentals.</li>
</ul></div>
