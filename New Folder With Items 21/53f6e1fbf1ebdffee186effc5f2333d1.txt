<h2>Brand Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Position Description:</b></p>
<p>
The Brand Manager will drive the development, implementation, and management of strategic and tactical marketing initiatives for the hand2mind brand and product portfolio to deliver revenue growth and improve profitability. This includes management of all elements of campaign and product launch marketing – digital advertising, PR, social engagement, influencer partnerships, trade advertising, consumer events, etc. This position defines the go-to-market strategy for new and existing products and develops the appropriate tactical plans by market segment and customer type. Collaborating with the broader Marketing, Product Development, Sales and Operations teams, this individual will be responsible for short and long-term success of the brand.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Our ideal Brand Manager has a passion for marketing, along with a proven track record of building brands and successfully launching products.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Scope of Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p>
The individual in this role will:</p>
<ul><li> Lead the charge as the brand steward, ensuring consistent execution against brand strategy and rollout of our brand experience across all customer touchpoints.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Develop and execute actionable marketing plans to drive performance and build brand equity based on understanding of brand position, product offering, market and consumer trends. Analyze results against KPIs to understand strength of plans, return on investment and future modifications.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Establish new and manage various partnerships (agencies, influencers, etc.) to push for big ideas, tap into the latest trends and improve overall marketing efficiency.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Lead key projects to identify and execute new business development strategies, margin improvement efforts or overall cross-functional process improvement.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Lead development of successful new product launch plans, collaborating with Customer and Product Marketing, Product Development, Sales, and Operations teams.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Drive the development of positioning and messaging for new products and with Ecommerce to establish promotional schedules to maximize sales in key selling seasons or coordinate timing to support new product launches.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Establish a thorough understanding of the target consumer and competitive insights for categories, brands and market segments to drive sales and marketing strategies; staying abreast of market changes, conducting necessary competitive reviews and trend analyses, gather VOC data and determine points of differentiation.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ensure marketing collateral and product packaging features appropriate content and meets brand requirements.</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Other duties for this role will be assigned as necessary/appropriate.</p>
<br>
<p></p>

Requirements/Preferences:</b></p>

<li>Minimum 2 years’ experience as a Brand Manager</li>
<li>Data Driven with excellent analytical skills</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills</li>
</ul>
<ul><li> Intermediate or advanced MS Office skills, proficient learning internal enterprise systems</li></ul>
<ul><li> Consumer goods background preferred</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Ideal attributes:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Motivational communication skills: leads the charge for brand transformation and drive for consistent brand experience across all customer touchpoints</li>
<li>Drives change for the better: offers ideas to increase efficiencies and adds to bottom-line results.</li>
<li>Connects inside and outside the team: shares information, transfers knowledge and seeks diverse input from others within the team and across the organization.</li>
<li>Takes Action: proactive and resourceful in solving problems, making solid decisions, and executing on goals.</li>
