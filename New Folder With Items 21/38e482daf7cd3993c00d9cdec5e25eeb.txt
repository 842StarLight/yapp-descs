Hillrom is a $2.8B leading worldwide manufacturer and provider of medical technologies and related services for the health care industry, including patient support systems, safe mobility and handling solutions, non-invasive therapeutic products for a variety of acute and chronic medical conditions, medical equipment rentals, surgical products and information technology solutions. Description</b><div>

<div><b>
JOB SUMMARY:</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
This position will be responsible for promoting Hillrom Direct, the official site for Hillrom OEM parts and accessories, to targeted U.S. commercial customers of the Hillrom brands.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Following a strategic call plan, perform a high volume of outgoing B2B sales calls targeting potential buyers of Hillrom OEM parts and accessories.</li>
<li>Using a One Hillrom approach, educate customers on Service Products and OEM parts available on Hillrom Direct as required, transition medical device leads and service agreement leads appropriately across the Global Business Units.</li>
<li>Enroll customers in Hillrom Direct Plus</li>
<li>Meet or exceed targeted performance goals on a consistent basis</li>
</ul>
