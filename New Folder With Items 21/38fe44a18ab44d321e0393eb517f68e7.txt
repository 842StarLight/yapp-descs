<p><b>Job Summary</b><br>
Under the general direction of the Manager of Solutions Architecture, the Solutions Architect will be responsible for designing data center infrastructure solutions. This is a highly visible and critical position within the organization.
</p>
<p><b>Description</b><br>
The Solutions Architect is a architecture role that works very closely with Sales, Project Management, and Field Engineering in our organization. The ideal candidate will have a strong technical background, but also possess solid communication and consultative skills. We are seeking individuals with a strong work ethic and the desire to contribute to the growth and success of our organization.</p>
<p><b>
Common Responsibilities for this position include:</b></p>
<ul>
<li> Develop solutions to solve highly complex technical and business issues</li>
<li> Maintain a high level of proficiency and knowledge in data center technology products</li>
<li> Participate in project planning and preparation</li>
<li> Assist with knowledge transfer to the field engineering organization</li>
<li> Participate in strategic meetings with senior leaders both internally and with customers</li>
</ul>
