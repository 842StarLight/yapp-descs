<h2>Marketing Manager, Customer & Partnership Marketing</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>
<div>At Emailage, we believe that fighting fraud is a team sport and our customers are our most valuable players. We are looking for an experienced Customer &amp; Partner Marketing Manager to lead our engagement, re-engagement and product expansion efforts across our growing customer and strategic partner base. Working closely with our Customer Success, Sales, Partnership and Product Development teams, you will build programs to scale the sharing of best practices, evangelize new fraud fighting strategies and highlight new product capabilities to drive adoption and stickiness of our products.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Under the direction and supervision of the Global Director of Marketing, this highly visible role will be responsible for driving revenue, gathering product insight and building brand loyalty by nurturing advocates and creating a memorable customer experience.</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Primary Responsibilities:
</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>Own the strategy and end-to-end execution of customer lifecycle campaigns to drive adoption, retention and expansion through a mix of traditional and modern marketing tactics (including email, direct mail, user events, customer advisory board meetings, webinars, paid ads, etc.).</li>
<li>
Align with Partnership team to create new partnership enablement program; includes partner events, partner tiering plan, sales enablement materials and promotions, training and establishing partnership success via KPIs.</li>
<li>
Identify and manage the creation of persona-based content that communicates the value of Emailage products, how customers get the most return out of their investment and tips to customize product use to address unique challenges.</li>
<li>
Own customer communications including monthly customer newsletters, new feature announcements, focus group / user event promotion and product training webinars.</li>
<li>
Build and manage a customer referral program to increase organic growth.</li>
<li>
Become the go-to expert in existing customer data &amp; stories to help create effective marketing assets (case studies, testimonials, videos) that can be leveraged in sales pitches and marketing campaigns; includes maintaining an internal library of up-to-date stats, quotes and references.</li>
<li>
Partner with Sales and Customer Success to identify top reference accounts and cultivate champions; includes owning relationship building efforts with our Customer Advisory Board.</li>
<li>
Manage customer satisfaction / NPS surveys to capture critical insight and insight change throughout the organization.</li>
<li>
Analyze the customer base to determine churn predictors and build proactive programs to prevent churn.</li>
<li>
Serve as a strategic voice in the planning and content development for the Emailage annual user conference.</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Required Experience:
</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in marketing or related discipline.</li>
<li>
3+ years creating and executing marketing campaigns and programs that drive customer and partner engagement and advocacy (in a B2B SaaS based environment preferably).</li>
<li>
Ability to communicate effectively strategies &amp; results across multiple levels within the organization.</li>
<li>
Technical knowledge of the key sales and marketing tools, including Salesforce and Marketo.</li>
<li>
Demonstrated success in creating and executing marketing campaigns and programs that drive customer engagement and advocacy.</li>
<li>
Ability to write effective copy using proper grammar.</li>
<li>
Familiarity with B2B account-based marketing, lead generation, and sales processes.</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Position Competencies:
</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>Strong passion for serving, understanding and nurturing customer and partner relationships.</li>
<li>
Detail oriented with a proactive attitude.</li>
<li>
Strong project management skills to ensure projects are completed on time and on budget.</li>
<li>
Strong interpersonal skills to work with cross functional and C-level team members effectively.</li>
<li>
Enjoy “thinking outside the box” and A/B testing creative ideas.</li>
<li>
Analytical skills to determine marketing effectiveness and ROI of efforts.</li>
<li>
Strategic thinker that can see beyond the day-to-day grind to help reach marketing and sales goals, create a stronger brand and improve the customer experience.</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div>

<div>
We are a hyper-growth SaaS company based in downtown Chandler, Arizona, with offices in San Francisco, Brazil, United Kingdom, Mexico, Australia and Singapore. We work with financial institutions, e-commerce merchants, and other organizations around the world to make digital transactions easier, more efficient and more secure. We’ve helped businesses around the world prevent over $2.7B in fraud in 2018 by providing an intelligent upfront layer of protection with our innovative fraud prevention solution.
</div>
<div></div>
<br>

We combine fraud data from our contributory consortium model with proprietary machine learning algorithms and email history data. Emailage uses patented machine learning algorithms, along with a global consortium of data, to identify new fraud trends and provide real time intelligence for risky transactions, which allows us to stay ahead of the evolving fraud landscape.</div>
