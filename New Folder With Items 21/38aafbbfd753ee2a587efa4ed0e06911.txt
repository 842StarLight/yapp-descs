<h2>Customer Solutions Specialist - Networks</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The leader in real-time TV ad data and analytics is currently seeking a Customer Solutions Specialist who will support the pre and post-sale support for the media partnership team, this means being able to quickly iterate on “ways in”, e.g. insights to move deals forward. We are seeking a talent who will share our obsession for data accuracy; someone with an insatiable appetite for learning new things and accomplishing challenging goals in a fast-paced (and not always structured) environment. Self-driven individual with a bias for action and a passion for analytics. This person will architect and deliver world-class platforms leveraging iSpot tools and services. The individual in this role will have a strong business acumen and be able to translate customer use cases and stories into efficient solution design.</p>
<p>
We have a product that is changing how brands, agencies and networks measure and assess the impact of TV advertising campaigns. Our software uses proprietary audio and video analysis to monitor and extract TV commercials, movie trailers and other promotional content from TV. At the same time, our software analyzes consumer interactions with TV ads on the digital screen across search, video and social. Interactions are matched with specific ads and attributed back to airings on TV. Our platform collects and analyzes massive amounts of data in real-time.</p>
