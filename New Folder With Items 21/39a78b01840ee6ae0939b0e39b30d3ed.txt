Hewlett Packard Enterprise has an opening for a NA Business Development Manager – Compute Options.</p>
<p>
A NA Business Development Manager – Compute Options is responsible for driving business growth by developing new ways to grow revenue of existing and new business. Leads in preparing and communicating the customer business case/proposal for emerging markets, existing markets, and specific deals. Guides HPE &amp; Channel account teams &amp; Technical Teams through strategic planning, qualifying mega deals, and developing and substantiating various HPE / HPE vendor product value propositions. Leads the development of innovative principles and ideas, and provides the business value selling expertise for specific solutions. Devises and executes a growth plan that meets business goals for a given geographic and/or service business area; including accountability for achieving revenue &amp; order targets. Builds high-quality funnels that generate customer demand in partnership with marketing &amp; sales.</p>
<p>
Responsibilities -</p>
<ul>
<li>
Leverages market and product research to intelligently focus pursuit time, resources and effort on winnable opportunities.</li>
<li>
Rigorously qualifies large opportunities for active pursuit based on the probability of success and the intelligent allocation of the company's resources.</li>
<li>
Drives development of client relationships and interest over the span of lengthy large deal cycles and continually helps build &amp; reinforce client's perception of the company as a credible partner through active persuasion and education.</li>
<li>
Consultatively &amp; proactively positions the company early in deals to influence client strategy and shape deal (RFP) requirements towards the company's strengths along with strategic HPE product vendor strengths.</li>
<li>
Develops and champions the business justification for the company services to ensure a successful engagement based on critical case review.</li>
<li>
Actively educates &amp; engages with others (EAMs, CBMs, Inside Sales, Account teams, Solution Architects, PBMs, iPBMs, iPSMs, etc.) on various business solutions, deal requirements &amp; challenges to facilitate effective RFP/RFI responses, successful closes and client satisfaction.</li>
<li>
Leads across the geographic area to drive the business development activities and report on a regular basis the progress on growth.</li>
</ul>
