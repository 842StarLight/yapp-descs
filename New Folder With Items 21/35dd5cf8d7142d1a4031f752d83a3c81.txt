<h2>Channel Sales Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p>The Channel Solutions Engineer (SE) is responsible for pre-sale technical support of Masergy’s portfolio of Network, Security, and UCaaS services. The SE also maintains ongoing life-cycle support for evolving/emerging technologies and client consultancy.</p>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>The Solutions Engineer is responsible for pre-sales technical support of Masergy’s portfolio of services.</li>
<li>
Client presentations and proposals.</li>
<li>
WAN-LAN integration, architectural design and validation</li>
<li>
Function design and product selection.</li>
<li>
Client data gathering, documentation and technical service order entry</li>
<li>
Joint participation in client implementation planning and related activities to ensure seamless integration or turn-up of Masergy services for both new and existing customers.</li>
<li>
Mentoring and teamwork with new and associate SE’s.</li>
<li>
25-50% travel</li>
<li>
Available for partner training, Webinars, Events</li>
</ul>
