<h2>Principal IT Business Relationship Partner (Experience in Logistics Sector)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div><div><div><div><div>
<div>Job Summary:</div>
<div>
<b>CEVA</b> Logistics is currently hiring a <b>Principal IT Business Relationship Partner</b> located in <b>Houston, TX</b>. The Business Relationship Partner will be a trusted senior technology advisor to Customer, customer partner’s and CEVA management on Customer’s IT issues. This is a knowledge expert on business and technology issues collaborating with CEVA Business Development and operations to close and win opportunities and to relay information critical to customer needs including reviewing and approving Statements of Work for key and critical customer engagements and being the focal point for all technical-related sales activities.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>In this highly visible role, the Principal IT Business Relationship Partner will:</div>
<ul>
<li>Facilitate the process to gather and establish high-level Customer requirements for purposes of winning opportunities</li>
<li>Partner with the global IT engineering/product development, Business Development, GKAMS, and Operations business unit leaders to drive strong revenue growth, widespread customer acceptance, and technology leadership of CEVA products and solutions thru the development of strategic account technology roadmaps</li>
<li>Ensure the appropriate support to Business Development and IT Pre-Sales function in delivery of world class solutions in a timely and cost effective manner</li>
<li>Provide expert oversight on multiple customer accounts ensuring the appropriate resource planning and staffing to fulfil in-bound demand including new customer projects, as well as long term customer care for all Triple C Century customers</li>
<br>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div>Summary of Key Responsibilities:</div>
<ul>
<li>Drives and participates in strategic and operational planning for customer(s) supported including the analysis and development of customer strategy, work plans, and budgets</li>
<li>Uses expert knowledge on customer and customer partner portfolio functions, IT direction, and Customer strategies to provide expert guidance and assistance in prioritizing technology needs</li>
<li>Manage through intuitive, holistic understanding of the most complex system designs and performance in unstable/dynamic or crisis situations based on broad and deep applied professional experience in a primary Team with broad exposure to multiple segments to ensure effective technology integration</li>
<li>Lead and facilitate Customer partners and service providers conversations regarding priorities, hardware/software selection, and vendor selection</li>
<li>Develop and manage Project Pipeline Portfolio and Capital IT Project Portfolio</li>
<li>Assist with development of customer’s annual operating and capital IT budgets</li>
<li>Prepare Project Concept Proposals, Customer Cases, and related Project Initiation documents</li>
<li>Identify and document Customer strategies, objectives and drivers; prepares monthly/quarterly/SABR documentation</li>
<li>Calculate/estimate costs/benefits of alternative actions or solutions to establish their ROI</li>
<li>Proactively seeks to expand relationships with customers and identifies strategic Customer needs and aligns major technology initiatives accordingly</li>
<li>Provide technical and non-technical expertise in identifying, evaluating and developing systems and procedures that are cost effective and meet user requirements</li>
<li>Ensure required service level alignment providing assistance in problem and root cause analysis and exception management on services including the implementation of changes</li>
<li>Facilitate cross-functional team steering committee responsible for technology governance activities such as project approval, tracking, budgeting, roadmap, and prioritization of competing Customer requirements</li>
<br>
</ul>
<div></div>

<ul>

<li>10+ years of experience developing and/or managing budgets</li>
<li>10+ years of implementation and delivery services, preferable for large complex projects and customers</li>
<li>10+ years of experience management of pre- and post-sales customers support</li>
<li>Strategic and enterprise-wide management experience including people and processes</li>
<li>PMI certification is preferred</li>
<li>ITIL certification is preferred</li>
<br>
</ul>
