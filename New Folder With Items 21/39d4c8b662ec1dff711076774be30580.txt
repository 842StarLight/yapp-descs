<h2>Marketing/Social Media Intern</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Description</b></p>
<p>Company is looking for a qualified intern to join our team. One Love Productions produces high-quality wedding films for couples in North Carolina and surrounding areas. We are looking for an intern who can participate hands-on with various aspects of our marketing, including print and online marketing campaigns.</p>
<p>This intern should be prepared to work in a fast-paced team environment and will finish the internship having gained broad experience in various aspects of marketing.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>· Assist in the distribution or delivery of marketing materials</p>
<p>· Perform analysis of marketing and sales data</p>
<p>· Seek and analyze competitor marketing and sales materials both on and offline</p>
<p>· Enter contact information into contact management systems</p>
<p>· Provide support to social media efforts</p>
<p>· Monitor social media for trending topics to utilize on social media channels</p>
<p>· Maintain tracking report of public relations activity</p>
<p>· Contribute to website redesign project</p>
<p>· Monitor and post on blogs, forums, and social networks</p>
<p>· Assist with online outreach and promotion using Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, Twitter, and more</p>
<p>· Optimize website and social media</p>
<p>· Perform keyword analysis</p>
<p>· Perform cost/benefit analysis</p>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<p>· Excellent verbal and written communication skills</p>
<p>· Extensive knowledge of social media</p>
<p>· Driven and hard-working</p>
<p>· Ability to take direction and multi-task</p>
<p>· PowerPoint, Word, Keynote, and Excel experience a plus</p>
<p><b>Benefits</b></p>
<p>· Practical experience with current marketing and advertising techniques</p>
<p>· Shadowing, mentoring, and training opportunities with successful video and marketing professionals</p>
<p>· Opportunity to participate in networking events and company meetings</p>
<p>· Flexible schedule for students</p>
