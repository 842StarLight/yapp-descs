<div>As a Traffic Coordinator<b>,</b> you will be joining The Studio- the nation’s largest in- house marketing agency serving the real estate industry and work exclusively for Coldwell Banker NRT. You will be a part of more than 400 innovative marketing professionals who are driving success for our affiliated real estate agents by focusing on product development, creative design, branding and communications. Recently launched, learn more about their exciting work here: https://thestudiorealestate.com/.<br>
<br>
Coldwell Banker NRT is seeking a Marketing Traffic Coordinator to join our in-house marketing agency. This position is responsible for trafficking projects through a fast-paced, in-house studio, which includes a creative, product, and strategy field team that creates internal and external marketing and advertising to ensure quality and on-time delivery of products.<br>
<br>
This is an ideal opportunity for an organized multi-tasker with a natural sense of urgency. If you are an agency traffic coordinator, a recent college grad with a few solid marketing internships under your belt, or an experienced admin/executive assistant with career aspirations in marketing, we want to hear from you!<br>
<br>
Job duties include:
<ul>
<li>Overseeing the project workflow to ensure materials are produced with accuracy and on time</li>
<li>
Acting as a departmental liaison within the marketing teams</li>
<li>
Entering, updating and closing projects in project management system</li>
<li>
Effectively communicating with print broker, publications and creative services using printer terminology (quotes, software programs, etc.)</li>
</ul>
