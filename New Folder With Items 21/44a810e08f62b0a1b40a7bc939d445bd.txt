<h2>Branch Manager - Home Services</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Position Summary</b>: Oversee all aspects of Service Center operations including the management of revenue, sales, profit, human resource activities, training, and compliance. Ensure uncompromised service to customers and Team Members with a commitment to our Vision, Mission and Core Values.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Functions</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Ensure compliance with regards to product label, licensing, recurring training requirements and existing company policies</li>
<li>Foster and enforce a consistent culture of safety in line with our core values</li>
<li>Drive commitments within your team to provide uncompromised customer service</li>
<li>Resolve customer complaints and issues promptly and professionally</li>
<li>Create and achieve monthly and quarterly revenue and sales budgets</li>
<li>Daily oversight of expenditures that impact profit</li>
<li>Manage daily and monthly production goals</li>
<li>Create a positive sales culture that facilitates achievement of Service Center goals</li>
<li>Adhere to company pricing and sales guidelines</li>
<li>Manage accounts receivable process</li>
<li>Process accounts payable invoices and check for accuracy</li>
<li>Manage cash funds, company credit card(s), and expense reports</li>
<li>Assess and negotiate customer warranty claims</li>
<li>Manage payroll and HR required activities</li>
<li>Maintain effective staffing levels by hiring for open positions and forecasting for future needs</li>
<li>Regularly lead performance feedback and reviews</li>
<li>Explain &amp; train on compliance, inspection, treatment, materials and sales procedures</li>
<li>Conduct routine Check-ins of Team Members</li>
<li>Maintain records within company and government guidelines for Team Members, the Service Center and customers</li>
<li>Manage and maintain fleet, equipment, and material inventory</li>
<li>Other duties as required</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Minimum Qualifications</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Industry experience (preferred)</li>
<li>Demonstrated leadership abilities</li>
<li>Effective verbal and written communication</li>
<li>Conflict resolution</li>
<li>Effectively operate internal applications and software (Paycom, PestPac, Applicant Pro, Outlook, Word, Excel, Power Point, other web based portals)</li>
<li>Proven track record in achieving sales results</li>
<li>Proven ability to multi task</li>
<li>Strong organizational skills</li>
<li>Basic math skills</li>
<li>Ability to pass any state regulatory agency required licensing/certification exams</li>
<li>Able to work a 40-hour (minimum) work week,</li>
<li>Accessible after hours (including weekends/holidays)</li>
<li>Possess current Driver License in the state where you work</li>
<li>Ability to pass background and drug screening tests upon offer of employment</li>
<li>Meets company driving standards</li>
<li>High school degree or equivalency, college preferred</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Physical Demands</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>
The work environment and physical demands described below is representative of those that an employee encounters while performing essential functions of this job.</li>
<li>Constant mobility including walking, crawling, stooping, bending, kneeling, squatting, lifting, looking, probing, climbing, pushing, pulling, balancing, stretching and standing for long periods</li>
<li>Climbing into attics and crawling in crawlspaces</li>
<li>Daily physical labor</li>
<li>Lifting up to 50lbs</li>
<li>Driving of automatic vehicle</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Environmental Factors</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Work both indoors and outdoors, in various weather conditions</li>
<li>May withstand extreme conditions such as heat in an attic in Summer or chill of crawlspace in Winter</li>
<li>Exposure to vegetation, dust, dirt, insects and animal remains</li>
<li>Some work in cramped spaces</li>
<br>
</ul>
