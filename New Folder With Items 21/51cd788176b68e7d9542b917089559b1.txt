<h2>Customer Service Account Manager</h2>

<p>Rave Decor Group, is a leader in the home goods market place and provides the broadest product line, most extensive distribution network and largest builder/contractor clientele in the industry. We are currently experiencing explosive growth, and as a result are searching for an addition to our operations department.The right candidate must have at least four years of experience in shipping, order procurement, transportation or sales support role. We are looking for someone smart, energetic, with a fun dynamic personality.</p>

<p><b>Job Tasks and Responsibilities Customer Service Account Manager</b></p>
<ul>

<li>Provide updates on pending, placed, and potential orders</li>
<li>Schedule carriers to ship materials</li>
<li>Provide product availability information</li>
<li>Place orders with our vendors and distributors</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Education and Experience Customer Service </b> <b>Account Manager</b></p>
<ul>

<li>Knowledge of computers and relevant software, including Outlook, Excel, Word, CRM a plus</li>
</ul>

<li>Impeccable professionalism, upbeat personality</li>
<li>Complete information management — always know what is going on</li>
<li>Thorough and intuitive -- you must anticipate things before they happen.</li>
<li>A quick wit, sharp and consistent with a creative ability</li>
<li>Persistence and follow through- always stay 3 steps ahead of everyone else</li>

</ul>
