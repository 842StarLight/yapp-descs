<h2>Manager, Inside Sales - Durham</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Are you a seasoned sales professional? A leader, who excels in a fast-paced and dynamic growth environment? Are you ready to showcase your background by leading a sales team in the development and execution of rapid growth sales initiatives?</p>
<p>
We are looking for an Inside Sales Manager to join our world-class team in Durham, NC. Come and join us!</p>
<p>
You will be joining a unique team. People who succeed at Nutanix have a sense of urgency, a lot of initiative, a commitment to customer success, and enthusiasm about pushing the edge… as well as a sense of humor. We provide interesting work, a great product, new things to learn, and the freedom to make the job work the way you like it. A strong work ethic and a drive to produce results are essential.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b><br>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Build, manage and drive a team of high caliber inside sales professionals</li>
<li>
Work closely with Nutanix Field Sales on a “Divide &amp; Conquer” sales approach</li>
<li>
Meet and exceed new and growth bookings targets for the identified market segment</li>
<li>
Motivate and manage Inside Sales professionals to drive pipeline development and revenues through Value Added Resellers</li>
<li>
Work with Nutanix marketing to build and implement campaigns focused at various target market segments</li>
<li>
Provide accurate weekly forecasts and track sales activity leveraging SFDC</li>
</ul>
<p></p>

Required Competencies/Skills</b></p>

5+ years sales experience and 2+ years management experience</li>

Experience in enterprise sales required, Storage/Networking/Virtualization or Infrastructure industries strongly preferred</li>
<li>
Proven consistent achievement of goals through the leadership and motivation of an Inside sales team</li>
<li>
Experience hiring, developing and retaining talent</li>
<li>
Exceptional command of enterprise sales methodologies and the ability to coach to it</li>
<li>
Excellent communication skills and the ability to build cross-functional relationships.</li>

Excellent Microsoft Excel and Salesforce.com skills</li>
<li>
Travel 1 to 2 times a quarter required</li>
