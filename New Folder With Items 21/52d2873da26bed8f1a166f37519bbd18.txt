<h2>Retail Store Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Who we are:</b><b> </b>Atlantic Healthcare Products &amp; Medical Supply - The Hardware Store for the Human Body ®.</p>
<p><b>
What we do:</b><b> </b>Our business is centered around exceeding our customers' expectations while fulfilling their healthcare and medical equipment needs. Our product categories range from walkers, wheelchairs, mobility scooters, power wheelchairs, Bathroom Safety products, orthopedic braces and supports, Compression Stockings, ADLs, lift chairs, pool &amp; vehicle lifts, ramps, and much more. Store hours M-F 9am-6pm; Sat 10am-3pm.</p>
<p><b>
Who are we looking for:</b><b> </b>We are seeking a career minded, outgoing personality with positive energy who has initiative, drive and dedication. Someone who is willing to learn and grow in a fast-paced environment. We are willing to train, but you need to be willing to learn and grasp new concepts easily. Anyone who is transient, lazy, afraid to get their hands dirty, or who does not read this job posting entirely - <b>need not apply.</b></p>
<p><b>
Job Summary: </b>The Retail Store Manager oversees and participates in patient flow and the daily operations of the retail store, ensuring company goals are met as set by management. This position requires knowledge and experience in all facets of patient/client processing cycle. Accurate data entry, attention to detail and customer service skills are required as well as demonstration of strong leadership skills in managing a retail sales team..</p>
<p><b>
Job Responsibilities include but are not limited to:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Oversee the daily store operations, including customer processing, ensuring excellent customer satisfaction, managing inventory levels</li>
<li>All activities involved with processing patient orders</li>
<li>Completing regular financial reports to determine profit per deliverable</li>
<li>Drafting reports, quotes and researching products</li>
<li>Lead generation and sales/marketing support</li>
<li>Complete tasks for company re-accreditation</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Job Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>3+ years of business or related experience</li>
<li>Strong verbal and written communication</li>
<li>Valid Driver’s license with clean driving record</li>
<li>Excellent customer service skills</li>
<li>Present a professional demeanor</li>
<li>Knowledge of office administrative procedures</li>
<li>Proficient in relevant software applications</li>
<li>Must be able to pass a thorough background check, FBI Fingerprint test and provide documentation of ability to work in US.</li>
<li>DME, Complex Rehab, Home Modifications and/or Assistive Technology and bi-lingual in English &amp; Spanish a big plus</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Minimum Key Skills &amp; Abilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Must be able to demonstrate strong leadership skills</li>
<li>Proven Stable work history</li>
<li>Passion for excellent customer service, "second to none"</li>
<li>Concern equally about the client and personal achievement</li>
<li>Detail Oriented</li>
<li>Computer &amp; Smart Phone Literate</li>
<li>Ability to quickly learn &amp; grasp established procedures.</li>
<li>Proven record of strong personal relationship development</li>
<li>Problem analysis and problem solving</li>
<li>Demonstrated ambition and passion</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Physical Requirement:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Must be able to lift 50 pounds</li>
<li>Must be able to stand on feet most of the work day</li>
<li>Must be able to climb a ladder on a regular basis</li>
<li>Must be able to stoop, bend, kneel and/or reach much of the day</li>
<li>Must be able to convey information via the spoken word to other workers, customers loudly, quickly and succinctly</li>
<li>Must be able to hear sounds at normal speaking levels, with or without correction</li>
</ul>
