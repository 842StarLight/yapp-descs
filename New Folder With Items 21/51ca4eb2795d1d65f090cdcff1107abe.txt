<h2>Assistant Install Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b><i>The Role</i></b></p>
<p>As an assistant installation manager, you will work as part of a dynamic and passionate team to maximize capacity, efficiency and profitability. You will assist with staff and exceed customer expectations by accomplishing the highest quality installations every time. By working closely with installation technicians, sales manager, and fleet and facilities manager, you will assist with completing jobs on time and within budget, helping achieve the company’s financial goals. The position will be based in our our service area with office work as needed.</p>
<p><b><i>Essential Duties and Responsibilities</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Show technicians how to establish customer rapport to ensure highest levels of satisfaction.</li>
<li>Assist Install Manager with forecast requirements for daily, weekly, monthly, and annual installation goals for each team.</li>
<li>Set expectations for each installation, including safety and code requirements.</li>
<li>Ensure jobs are properly ordered; coordinate and inspect sales work order form and results.</li>
<li>Assist with training to ensure on-time, high-quality installations.</li>
<li>Assist QA Specialist in monitoring performance of installation technicians and advise them on how to improve.</li>
<li>Promote enthusiasm and brand loyalty during daily huddles, team meetings, and 1:1’s.</li>
<li>Adhere to the company’s plan for resolving customer complaints quickly and favorably.</li>
<li>Assist with analyzing and controlling job expenses to meet budget.</li>
<li>Complete random job site inspections.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>Requirements</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Proven track record in installation, with 7-9 years of lead installation experience.</li>
<li>Knowledge of metal fabrication.</li>
<li>Good presentation skills; able to convey confident and decisive messages.</li>
<li>Multitasker who enjoys working in the field with diverse teams on varied projects.</li>
<li>Detail-oriented and highly organized with exceptional follow-through abilities.</li>
<li>Top performer who can instill pride of workmanship in self and others.</li>
<li>Passionate about Arlinghaus and loves the brand.</li>
<li>Outgoing personality that blends well with a fast-paced, goal-driven environment.</li>
<li>Highly motivated, flexible, and great attitude on life.</li>
<li>Skills in standard office applications like Excel, Word and PowerPoint as well as dispatching software.</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>Culture</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Dependably maintain excellent attendance and punctuality to adhere to work schedule.</li>
<li>Collaboratively participate in team/huddle meetings.</li>
<li>Carefully work to ensure adherence to safety and quality of work standards.</li>
<li>Steadily improve both quality and speed over time.</li>
<li>Consistently live out company core values daily.</li>
<li>Enthusiastically motivated to learn the HVAC industry.</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>Physical Demands</i></b></p>
<p>Ability to climb and work on ladders.</p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to lift heavy objects.</li>
<li>Ability and comfort in working in tight and confined locations.</li>
<li>Ability it work in attics and crawl spaces.</li>
<li>Ability to work in in climate weather, high heat, and extreme cold.</li>
</ul>

Company provides transportation, cell phone and gas card. Pre-employment drug testing and background screening are required.</p>
