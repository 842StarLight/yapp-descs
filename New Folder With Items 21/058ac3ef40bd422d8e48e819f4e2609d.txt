<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Company Overview
</b></h2>
<p>At Motorola Solutions, we create technologies our customers refer to as their lifeline. Our technology platforms in communications, software, video and services help our customers work safely and more efficiently. Whether it’s helping firefighters see through smoke, enabling police officers to see around street corners, or reliably keeping the lights on in homes and businesses around the world, our work supports those who put their lives on the line to keep us safe. Bring your passion, potential and talents to Motorola Solutions, and help us usher in a new era in public safety and security.<br>
</p>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader">
<br>
<b>Job Description
</b>
</h2>
<ul>
<li>Lead, collaborate with, and motivate cross-functional teams of user experience designers, business analysts, developers, testers and others to develop, execute, and release cloud-based services in alignment with major program/portfolio and/or product roadmaps.
</li>
<li>Identify, engage, influence, and lead business stakeholders and Subject Matter Experts across programs.
</li>
<li>Work with business and enterprise stakeholders to develop and validate business strategy and lead shaping the vision and customer needs.
</li>
<li>Partner with engineering, sales, program management, and technical services groups to facilitate, define and document relevant customer and businesses processes, gaining an understanding of the needs, measures, and risks &amp; controls for the improvement area.
</li>
<li>Serve as the voice of the customer within the organization, representing/reinforcing the customers' perspective throughout the product realization cycle.
</li>
<li>Works with the business process owners to develop product, project, and program level metrics to manage and track success, product adoption, and risks to operations.
</li>
<li>Assist with portfolio, program and project level plans related to schedule, scope and resources.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Key Accountabilities
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Willing to learn and develop skills and knowledge.
</li>
<li>Starts and finishes tasks with minimal management oversight.
</li>
<li>Maintains a positive attitude throughout typical work-related challenges.
</li>
<li>Interfaces well with individuals outside of engineering.
</li>
<li>Communicates effectively with peers.
</li>
<li>Organizes time and workflow to maximum productivity.
</li>
<li>Demonstrated aptitude in recognizing and solving problems.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader">
<br>
<b>Basic Requirements
</b>
</h2>
<ul>
<li>Undergrad, Masters and PhD in computer science, business administration, or related field.
</li>
<li>Solid understanding of software development life cycle models as well as expert knowledge of both Agile and traditional project management principles and practices and the ability to blend them together in the right proportions to fit a project and business environment
</li>
<li>Experience of successfully implementing software or web development projects using Agile SCRUM/Agile methodologies with enterprise-level application development projects
</li>
<li>Strong, analytical background with experience in one of the following fields- finance, strategy, product marketing, operations, or product management
</li>
<li>5-7 years’ work experience in one of the fields noted above preferable
</li>
</ul>
