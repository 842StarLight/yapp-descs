<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Description</b></h1>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Position Title: Branch<br>
Manager (Classification I, II, III, IV)</p>
<p>Division: Retail/Operations</p>
<p>Reports Directly To: Community<br>
President<br>
</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>BASIC PURPOSE:</b></h2>
<p>This position is responsible for,<br>
but not limited to, directing and administering the operational and lending<br>
function of the respective Branch. Holds<br>
responsibility for growth and profitability as it relates to sales calls,<br>
generating Team leads to subsidiaries, monitoring successful programs and<br>
managing expenses. Manages the income side of the Branch revenue<br>
in relation to fee income, deposit growth and loan growth. Oversees the customer service experience for<br>
all CenterState Bank customers and maintains an ongoing recruitment effort to<br>
hire, train and retain competent individuals. Exercises executive and administrative control<br>
over the functions of the Branch.</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:</b></h2>
<p>This<br>
position is expected to perform the specific duties, tasks, and<br>
responsibilities as outlined below:</p>
<p>Achieve Branch Deposit, Loan and Investment<br>
goals</p>
<p>Actively<br>
participates in Sales Calls to new and prospective customers</p>
<p>Supports<br>
promotion success within the branch to achieve deposit and income goals</p>
<p>Monitors<br>
the customer service experience within the office</p>
<p>Actively<br>
participates in and drives business for the lending initiatives of</p>
<p>Branch,<br>
including consumer, commercial and mortgage lending</p>
<p>Generates<br>
sales leads for CenterState subsidiaries</p>
<p>Oversee a variety of Banking Center Activities:</p>
<p>Holds<br>
final responsibility for management of the functions of the Branch</p>
<p>May<br>
open and close the Branch as necessary</p>
<p>Approves<br>
checks and overrides as needed</p>
<p>Directs<br>
lending activities as defined</p>
<p>Holds<br>
final authority and decisions concerning customer problems or complaints</p>
<p>May<br>
counsel customers with special requests or needs</p>
<p>Ensures<br>
Branch compliance with Community Reinvestment Act and Equal Employment<br>
Opportunity regulations</p>
<p>Will assist the Assistant Manager in the<br>
selection, development, and motivation of a competent work force:</p>
<p>Establish<br>
and clarify responsibilities and authorities of staff</p>
<p>Ensure<br>
established training goals are achieved</p>
<p>Monitors,<br>
supports and manages toward success of the bank</p>
<p>With<br>
input from Assistant Manager, will evaluate job performances, recommend<br>
promotions and salary increases for staff</p>
<p>Participates<br>
in bi-monthly sales meetings with Team members</p>
<p>Ensure that the Bank’s policies and procedures,<br>
code of conduct, and regulatory guidelines are strictly complied with
</p>
<p>Provide assistance to other employees by<br>
liaising with them through healthy and positive interactions</p>
<p>Be involved in performing marketing<br>
endeavors/efforts
</p>
<p>Continuously update skills by participating in<br>
professional training</p>
<p>Seek opportunities to improve skills through<br>
cross-training offered by the Bank</p>
<p>All other tasks, responsibilities or duties, as<br>
directed by management</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>OTHER SKILLS/QUALIFICATIONS:</b></h2>
<p>Strong financial acumen
</p>
<p>Pro-active, self-starter, takes initiative,<br>
ambitious and work independently</p>
<p>Problem solver, resourceful, independent</p>
<p>Must be able to work at a faster than average<br>
pace and able to multi-task.</p>
<p>Must have the ability to perform mathematical<br>
calculations necessary to execute the duties of the job.</p>
<p>Must be able to make sound decisions, have the<br>
ability to recognize potential problems, and take corrective action.</p>
<p>Regular attendance is necessary.</p>
<p>Decisive action, strategic thinking, able to<br>
delegate</p>
<p>Optimistic, enthusiastic, persuasive</p>
<p>Confident, competitive, communicative</p>
<p>Must have strong communication skills<br>
characterized by excellent writing and speaking skills</p>
<p>Proficient<br>
with general office machines and programs to include Microsoft Office Word,<br>
Excel, Outlook, and other software that might be utilized in the department</p>
<p>Ability to use the computer efficiently and the<br>
capacity to learn new software programs as they are rolled out by the Bank</p>
<p>Must have good knowledge of business English,<br>
including spelling and punctuation</p>
<p>Must possess basic English language skills in<br>
order to write and speak clearly, and effectively with coworkers and customers</p>
<p>Must be well-organized, accurate, and attentive<br>
to detail</p>
<p>Must be cooperative and willing to assist<br>
coworkers and customers on a regular basis</p>
<p>Must have effective listening skills demonstrated<br>
by the ability to listen to others talk (without interruption), understand<br>
them, and then propose solutions or make contributions based on the points made<br>
by others</p>
<p>Must possess excellent multi-tasking skills and<br>
be able to function well under pressure</p>
<p>Must be able to remain composed under pressure<br>
and respond to customer and coworker concerns regularly
</p>
<p>Must have a keen eye for detail and follow<br>
instructions to the letter</p>
<p>Must be patient and willing to help others in<br>
solving problems while maintaining a positive attitude at all times</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>ESSENTIAL PHYSICAL DEMANDS:</b></h2>
<p>Must<br>
be able to remain seated or work standing in a confined area on a computer for<br>
a minimum of four (4) hours each work day</p>
<p>Must<br>
be able to constantly use hands and fingers to enter data through or on a<br>
computer or answer a phone throughout most of an eight (8) hour shift</p>
<p>Must<br>
be able to walk frequently throughout the day</p>
<p>Must<br>
be able to hear and communicate with coworkers and customers throughout the day</p>
<p>May<br>
need to lift up to 25 pounds one to two times a day to perform job</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>The physical<br>
demands described here are representative of those that must be met by an<br>
employee to successfully perform the essential functions of this job. Position<br>
may require regular before/after normal business hours, or regular out-of-town<br>
travel.</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>WORK ENVIRONMENT:</b></h2>
<p>Must<br>
be able to function in a professional, business office environment and act and<br>
dress appropriately for same, consistent with the Bank’s grooming and dress<br>
codes</p>
<p>Must<br>
demonstrate excellent interpersonal skills with customers and coworkers</p>
<p>Must<br>
be willing to function as a team member</p>
<p>Must<br>
be willing to demonstrate commitment to CenterState Bank’s mission and goals</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>MINIMUM QUALIFICATIONS:</b></h2>
<p>Bachelor’s Degree or equivalent work experience</p>
<p>One to Two years’ experience in a Financial<br>
Institution</p>
<p>Excellent written, verbal and supervisory skills</p>
<p>Results oriented Desired</p>
<p>Lending experience</p>
<p>Sales Management experience<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>These<br>
specifications are general guidelines based on the minimum experience normally<br>
considered essential to the satisfactory performance of this position.<br>
The requirements listed above are representative of the knowledge, skill and/or<br>
ability required to perform the position in a satisfactory manner.<br>
Individual abilities may result in some deviation from these guidelines.</p>
