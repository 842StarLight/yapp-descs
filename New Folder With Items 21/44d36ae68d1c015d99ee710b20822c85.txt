<h2>Finance Associate Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Gatorade Commercial Finance Associate Manager helps ensure that the Gatorade Business Unit delivers topline and profit growth. The role partners with Sales Strategy and Sales Finance to develop Gatorade and Propel pricing strategies and evaluates potential investment opportunities for the Region Grocery accounts. The Associate Manager also provides insight into business performance drivers and leads the Gatorade topline AOP process.<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities include:</b><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Provide insight into Regional Grocery results and expected future performance, partnering with Sales Strategy and Sales Finance to understand potential risks/opportunities and recommended actions</li>
<li>
Evaluate potential trade investments, including customer-specific incremental funding requests</li>
<li>
Support the development of Grocery pricing strategies, including specific Region Grocery tactics</li>
<li>
Lead the modeling of the Gatorade topline AOP and channel targets, including managing inputs from Finance partners and ensuring alignment with overall BU algorithm</li>
<li>
Support the BU forecast process through the development of detailed volume forecasts, leveraging inputs from Field Sales Finance and macro trends</li>
<li>
Assist Propel Marketing team in communicating drivers of business performance and potential actions through the period POR process</li>
</ul>
