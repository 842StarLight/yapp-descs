<h2>Master Scheduler</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Master Scheduler</b></p>
<p><b>Department: </b> Materials</p>
<p><b>Reports to: </b> Materials Manager</p>
<p><b>JOB DESCRIPTION</b></p>
<p><b>Summary/Objective</b></p>
<p>The Master Scheduler reports to the Materials Manager is responsible for the management and administrative tasks relating to plant scheduling. A solid understanding of material concepts and procedures, applicable to the manufacturing industry is required.</p>
<p>All duties are carried out in the spirit of promoting a co-operative work environment where fairness, equity and non-discriminatory practices are the standard.</p>
<p><b>Essential Functions</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Reviews blueprints and product specifications to accurately determine amount of materials needed.</li>
<li>Documents overall material need, including all codes, brands, and contact information necessary to process orders.</li>
<li>Adjusts amounts for existing inventory and for orders of conventional sizes.</li>
<li>Manage demand and production scheduling for single unit with multiple customer(s) / product(s).</li>
<li>Works closely with customers, team leaders to determine daily production requirements.</li>
<li>Leads improvement and cost reduction initiatives.</li>
<li>Works with Materials Launch team for new product / program launch.</li>
<li>Formalizes Weekly Forecasts/Sales and Operations planning</li>
<li>Build a master schedule to establish sequence and lead times of each operation to meet shipping dates according to the sales forecast and customer orders</li>
<li>Streamlining of the scheduling process with Continuous Improvements and recommendations</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Competencies</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Communication Proficiency, written and oral</li>
<li>Problem Solving/Analysis</li>
<li>Organizational Skills</li>
<li>Teamwork Orientation</li>
<li>Experience using SAP AS-400 System</li>
<li>Forklift training</li>
<li>Knowledge and experience in a Lean Manufacturing environment.</li>
<li>Knowledge and experience with Kanban Inventory Systems.</li>
<li>Understanding of FIFO Principles</li>
<li>Understanding of supplier planning schedules and tracing supplier-on-time delivery.</li>
<li>Experience with computers using Microsoft Office, specifically Excel and Outlook</li>
</ul>
