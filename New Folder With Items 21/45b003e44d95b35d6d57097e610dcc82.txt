<h2>Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>(No Recruiters or Staffing Agencies Please) | www.affect.com</p>
<p>
Affect is a public relations, social media and marketing firm that specializes in technology, healthcare and professional services, we combine smart people, cutting-edge thinking, creative ideas and hard work to provide exceptional service and produce tangible results for our clients.</p>
<p>
Affect is seeking a Marketing Manager to lead marketing initiatives for our agency as well as our clients. This position requires a senior manager with strategic insights into marketing, public relations and sales enablement. The Marketing Manager will plan, develop and execute a variety of programs that are critical to building awareness and driving demand for the firm's service offerings. At the same time, this role will strengthen Affect's reputation and position our executives as subject-matter-experts in a variety of arenas and industries in which we operate. The role requires close collaboration with other team members and leading program and project initiatives that are interdepartmental.</p>
<p>
We are seeking a self-starter who is highly motivated, resourceful, creative and growth-minded. Someone who can be hands-on but also think strategically about the business' goals and objectives and develop result-driven programs designed to achieve them. This individual should be passionate about marketing, dedicated to self-improvement and constant learning as well as a team member with shared values and a vision for accelerating the growth of the firm.</p>
