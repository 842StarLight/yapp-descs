</div>

<div></div>
<div><b>JOB SUMMARY:</b></div>
<div>
This position is responsible to conduct assessment related activities within Academic Affairs including statistical analysis and support for medical education research activities.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
This position is required to conduct business in a professional and cordial manner that will uphold the integrity and reputation of Geisinger Commonwealth School of Medicine (GCSOM). This position maintains a thorough working knowledge of and adheres to the policies, regulations, and procedures of GCSOM.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
MAJOR DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></div>
<p></p>
<div><div>
<br>
<p><b>
1.</b><b> </b>Scheduling of all evaluations related to the MD1, MD2, and graduate programs curriculum.</p>
<p><b>
2.</b><b> </b>Scheduling various evaluations in support of the MD3 and MD4 curriculum as assigned by the Senior Education Specialist (SES).</p>
<p><b>
3.</b><b> </b>Data analysis using Microsoft Excel, SPSS, NVivo, or other data tools.</p>
<p><b>
4.</b><b> </b>Analysis of academic program assessment and evaluation data.</p>
<p><b>
5.</b><b> </b>Provides support for data entry or other curriculum mapping and assessment activities required by accrediting bodies.</p>
<p><b>
6.</b><b> </b>Assist in developing and generating reports for Academic Affairs to support accreditation activities related to educational programming.</p>
<p><b>
7.</b><b> </b>Assist in the administration of One45, MedHub, and current GCSOM educational database.</p>
<p><b>
8.</b><b> </b>Provides Course Directors statistical analysis and consults on examination questions using methods of item response theory.</p>
<p><b>
9.</b><b> </b>Manages the data requests for Educational Research activities.</p>
<p><b>
10.</b> Manages requests for data needed for Educational Research Activities following proper IRB protcols.</p>
<p><b>
11.</b><b> </b>Other duties as necessary assigned by supervisor to support the day-to-day operations of Academic Affairs.</p>
<p><b>
12.</b><b> </b>Train GCSOM users in evaluation protocols.</p>
<p><b>
13.</b><b> </b>Support data analysis for L-CHIP and QUICC projects in the MD curriculum.</p>
<p><b>
14.</b><b> </b>Attend required meetings and trainings.</p>
<p><b>
15.</b><b> </b>Provides administrative support for the Medical Curriculum Committee.</p>
</div></div>

<p><b>EDUCATION AND/OR EXPERIENCE:</b></p>
<div>
Master’s degree (e.g., mathematics, epidemiology, statistics, economics, education, higher education administration) or equivalent is required.</div>
<div>
3 - 5 years’ experience in statistical analysis, assessment, and evaluation of programs or equivalent is required.</div>
<div>
Proficiency in Microsoft Office products and statistical programs (e.g., SPSS, SAS, EpiInfo) is required.</div>
<div>
High level of comfort with complex statistical analyses, both qualitative and quantitative, is preferred.
</div>
<div>Experience with curriculum and assessment at the post-secondary level is preferred.</div>
