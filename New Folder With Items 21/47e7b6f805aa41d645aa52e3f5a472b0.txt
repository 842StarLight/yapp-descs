<h2>Product Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Summary:</b></p>
<p>The Sales Executive is responsible for driving revenue to the business through sales via cold calling, direct leads through marketing and attendance at trade shows to showcase solutions to retailers.</p>
<p><b>Essential Duties &amp; Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Drive demand for value offerings via cold-calling and trade show attendance.</li>
<li>Meet or exceed established revenue goals each month.</li>
<li>Accurately and thoroughly record sales activities in CRM each day including sales, calls, emails and other activities.</li>
<li>Facilitate resolution of customer issues when they arise and find the appropriate people to assist with resolution.</li>
<li>Manage the life-cycle relationship of the account from pre-sale through installation, training and for the life of the account.</li>
<li>Cross-sell and upgrade clients to other emerging sales offerings.</li>
<li>Provide weekly updates and status on business, projects, sale leads and other relevant sales activities.</li>
<li>Comply with CRM policies, procedures and rules of engagement.</li>
<li>Ensure minimum cold calls, booked demos and sales revenue generated meet or exceed set minimum quota levels.</li>
<li>Prepare proposals and contracts for client procurement.</li>
<li>Attend trade shows as assigned as a representative of Petrosoft to increase exposure and to allow for lead gathering.</li>
</ul>
</div>
