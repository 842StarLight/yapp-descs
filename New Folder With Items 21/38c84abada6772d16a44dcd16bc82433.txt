<h2>Sales Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Intrinium is looking for a dynamic Sales Executive to obtain and retain client relationships within the IT services industry! Competitive salary and bonus plan, employer-paid health benefits, unlimited PTO, and very little travel (less than 25%, if ever). Must have at least 3-5 years of sales experience with some focused experience in the IT services industry. Full job description is below: </b></p>
<p><b>Who we are: </b>Intrinium is an Information Technology and Security Solutions company, providing comprehensive consulting and managed services to businesses of all sizes since 2007. We have decades of experience working with a multitude of businesses and hundreds of networks across various verticals internationally. We specialize in healthcare, finance and retail businesses within the United States. From the server room to the board room, we will secure, transform and manage your IT.</p>
<p><b>Job Summary</b></p>
<p>The Sales Executive is responsible for proactively identifying and targeting new customers through cultivating relationships and following up on leads from marketing initiatives and requests received from company website and through partner portals. This position will have the support of engineers, consultants and sales support staff in putting together proposals and getting all agreements signed. Also responsible for selling of all Intrinium’s product and service offerings. This is a quota-carrying position that targets SMB and mid-market customers with up to 1,000 employees.</p>
<p><b>Summary of Essential Job Functions</b></p>
<p>Successful candidates will demonstrate the ability to:</p>
<ul>
<li>Manage the entire sales cycle from initial client contact through final contract negotiations.</li>
<li>Unearth new sales opportunities through networking and research to convert to long term partnerships</li>
<li>Present products to prospective clients</li>
<li>Keep up with industry trends and competitive landscape</li>
<li>Proactively target new customers through cultivating relationships and identifying new opportunities</li>
<li>Work effectively with sales engineering to ensure prospective customer needs are met</li>
<li>Manage pipeline</li>
<li>Present Intrinium services and solutions to information security leaders (CIOs, CFOs, CISOs, VPs, Directors, Managers)</li>
<li>Maintain current knowledge of Intrinium services and solutions</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Minimum Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in business, marketing, sales or related field preferred.</li>
<li>3 to 5 years of experience in sales with a proven track record of obtaining and retaining new clients and fostering client relationships.</li>
<li>Knowledge of market research, sales and negotiating principles</li>
<li>Excellent communication/presentation skills and ability to build relationships</li>
<li>Organizational and time-management skills</li>
<li>Experience selling technology and information security related services</li>
<li>Must be a self-starter with a highly entrepreneurial spirit</li>
<li>Excellent problem-solving skills and business acumen</li>
<li>Proficiency with Microsoft Office programs and experience working with a CRM</li>
<li>Familiar with information security technologies &amp; industry</li>
<li>Must possess understanding of sales performance metrics</li>
<li>Must be deadline and detail oriented</li>
<li>Additional training in the technology field is not required but is a plus</li>
<li>Sales certifications are not required but are a plus</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Abilities Required</b></p>
<p>Ability to lift 25 pounds, drive for work use, sit and stand for long periods of time, ability to kneel and reach, and vision including focus adjustment. Must successfully pass a Washington State background check. Some travel required – up to 25% on the high side.</p>
