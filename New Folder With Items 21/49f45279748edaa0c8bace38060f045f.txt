<h2>Senior NetApp Engineer with Secret Clearance</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Strategic Business Systems, Inc hiring a Senior NetApp Storage Engineer to provide onsite Engineering services and guidance to the customer in Ft. Bragg, NC. He/She needs to be based out of Fayetteville or Raleigh/Durham, NC with the possibility of travel to other customer sites. Expertise with core NetApp, cDOT, and ONTAP knowledge are required skills for resolving issues in this role.<br>
<br>
We are seeking for a strong Engineer who is proactive and can lead a team. It is important for the Engineer to have ownership, tackle any challenges or issues, and find solutions. The person must have a growth mindset and be able to partner with the customer and sales team.<br>
<div>Skills/proficiency required:</div>
<ul>
<li>Secret Clearance</li>
<li>NCDA or other NetApp certification</li>
<li>Security + CE or comparable (CCNA Security, CySA+, GICSP, GSEC, SSCP)</li>
<li>Core NetApp, cDOT, and ONTAP knowledge</li>
</ul>Preferred Skills (not required):
<ul>
<li>FlexPod experience</li>
<li>Cisco UCS</li>
<li>VMWare</li>
<li>Automation/Orchestration</li>
</ul> Specific responsibilities may include but aren’t limited to the following:</div>
<ul>
<li>Storage management and optimization: upgrades, audits, performance monitoring and troubleshooting, volume configuration and provisioning, SAN</li>
<li>Documentation: create and maintain documentation of the NetApp environment according to best practices</li>
<li>Service management: interact and integrate with the end client’s service management personnel to ensure smooth, continuous storage operations</li>
<li>Customer management: serve as a trusted adviser to the end client, providing technical mentor-ship on NetApp technology to customer personnel</li>
<li>Vendor relations: collaborate with NetApp technical team and management to help the end client leverage NetApp hardware and software solutions</li>
</ul>
