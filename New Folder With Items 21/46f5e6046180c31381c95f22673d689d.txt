<p></p>
<p>As a <b>Digital Marketing Specialist </b>you will be responsible for supporting and enhancing Premera's brand identities through the development and execution of digital marketing and social media campaigns. You will support the company's integrated marketing efforts through the development of targeted digital strategies that support acquisition and retention, as well as content for the organization's social media channels.
<br>
<br>
<b>What you'll do:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop custom audiences on social and online channels to attract desired consumer segments and members. Optimize campaigns and audiences to gain efficiencies on marketing return on investment.
</li>
<li>Work with team to implement marketing campaigns utilizing digital tools and social channels to support overall marketing campaigns for the organization.
</li>
<li>Collaborate with company stakeholders to identify and develop original company stories to tell through the organization's social media channels.
</li>
<li>Use syndicated tools to monitor Premera and competitor mentions to create social listening reports that highlight market insights, themes and brand sentiment.
</li>
<li>Create campaign dashboards that convey metrics on social media and digital campaign performance.
</li>
<li>Develop engaging content for the organization's social media channels that aligns with the company's overarching social media strategy, and supports specific marketing and communications initiatives.
</li>
<li>Develop content strategies to support paid sponsorships and events.
</li>
<li>Support brand management team as needed, including copywriting, proofreading, market research, and media planning.
</li>
<li>Assist with creative planning process including development of story charters for content marketing channels.
</li>
<li>Consistently seek out innovative opportunities to promote the company's brand and product messages through social media and other digital channels. .
</li>
<li>Perform social media site and key word monitoring, including Search Engine Marketing (SEM) planning as needed.
</li>
<li>Respond to questions and comments made by customers on social media channels and triage issues to designated responders in the organization.
</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
<b>What you'll bring:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree in communications, journalism, public relations, or marketing or four (4) years’ work experience.
</li>
<li>Two (2) years’ experience in integrated marketing specifically social media field.
</li>
<li>Outstanding written and oral communications skills.
</li>
<li>Self-driven with the ability to meet deadlines and manage multiple projects.
</li>
<li>Sophisticated knowledge of social media platforms and audience targeting.
</li>
<li>Strong marketing analytical skills.
</li>
<li>Knowledge of Adobe Creative Software (Photoshop, Illustrator) and web publishing tools preferred.
</li>
<li>Ability to work with people at all levels in the organization to implement initiatives.
</li>
</ul>
