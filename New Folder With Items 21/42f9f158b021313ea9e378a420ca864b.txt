<h2>Move-In Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Anthology Senior Living of Town and Country located at 1020 Woods Mill Road Town &amp; Country, MO 63017</b> <b>is currently</b> <b>seeking a Move-in Coordinator to join our community.</b></p>
<p>The Move-in Coordinator is responsible for assisting the Director of Sales and Marketing in actively acquiring and managing short- and long-term leads. These responsibilities include handling telephone, walk-in and inbound mail inquiries; qualifying prospects, converting qualified leads to tours; converting tours to deposits; and, assisting with move-ins in accordance with established procedures. The Move-in Coordinator is also responsible for the smooth transition of a new resident to the community.</p>
<p><b>Reports to: </b> Director of Sales and Marketing</p>
