<h2>Business Development Proposal Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div><div><div><div><div><div><div><div><div>
<div><div>
<div>
<p><b>POSITION SUMMARY</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Serve as a key member of the Inside Sales team supporting the full pursuit process, from positioning and capture planning through post-proposal submittal debriefing</li>
<li>Primary focus is the opportunity tracking, planning, scheduling, development, and finalization of responses to client requests for qualification, proposals, bids, and presentations that result in contract and/or project awards</li>
<li>Must be able to multi-task, function effectively under multiple deadlines and time constraints, and manage time efficiently in a high-pressure, fast-paced environment while demonstrating an energetic 'can do' attitude</li>
</ul>
<p><b>PRIMARY RESPONSIBILITIES</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Serves as lead proposal manager and coordinator to:
<ul>
<li>Review and interpret RFPs and lead routine proposals, Q&amp;Es, SF254s/255s/330s, and similar documentation based on requirements</li>
<li>Develop proposal management plans</li>
<li>Develop materials in response to solicitation-specific requirements</li>
<li>Develop/manage/respond to data calls</li>
<li>Develop kickoff and other meeting agendas</li>
<li>Coordinate proposal team activities and meetings</li>
<li>Develop/drive schedules</li>
<li>Participate in reviews, i.e., Red Team reviews, editing and general QA/QC of statements of qualifications, proposals, presentations, etc.</li>
<li>Drive RFP compliance/requirements</li>
<li>Ensure timely and complete proposal production and delivery</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Collaborate with engineering staff and Outside Sales Managers to support opportunity-specific market research, competitive analyses, etc. on leads, opportunities, clients, projects, and competitors</li>
<li>Participate in capture planning/win strategy development with key role of identifying proposal needs (e.g., resources, materials, information, expertise) and in carrying out win strategy tactics</li>
<li>Plan and conduct research of internal capabilities and resources to meet opportunity-specific requirements, collect information/materials, and work with capture/proposal teams to develop new or tailored information/materials</li>
<li>Write new text and/or tailor existing materials (general text, resumes, project descriptions), which may include interviewing staff/technical experts, to incorporate win strategy/capture plan information into text, to meet RFP requirements, and provide the most competitive RFP responses</li>
<li>Provide clear, concise written and verbal instructions and directions for proposal team action items and follows up on progress</li>
<li>Develop proposal-specific templates and data call requests</li>
<li>Attend client pre-proposal and debrief meetings; share lessons learned and best practices across teams</li>
<li>Work with graphic design and other proposal team members to communicate requirements, needs and product expectations</li>
<li>Perform a variety of data and knowledge management activities such as updating, organizing, cataloging and storing information for current and future proposal needs using a shared Inside Sales team network drive</li>
<li>Support sales pipeline tracking, maintenance, training, reporting</li>
<li>Support development of client interview presentations</li>
</ul>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in English, marketing, communications or related field</li>
<li>3 years of experience in proposal management and coordination in A/E/C industry</li>
<li>Proposal development experience for broad range of RFPs/solicitations</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal and communications skills with an emphasis on persuasive writing, editing and presentation development</li>
<li>Team player with the ability to work well with all types and levels of professionals</li>
<li>Strong graphic and presentation experience</li>
<li>Proficiency in Word, InDesign and PowerPoint software</li>
<li>Outstanding organizational and logistical skills, ability to work under pressure and ability to work overtime</li>
<li>Demonstrated initiative and problem solving ability</li>
</ul>
<p><b>PHYSICAL DEMANDS</b>:</p>
<p>
Typical physical demands associated with working in an office environment. Occasional visits to other offices, including international travel may be necessary. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</p>
