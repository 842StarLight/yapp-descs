<p>
KPMG is currently seeking a Director in Technology Enablement for our Management Consulting practice.</p>
<p>
Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Responsible for business development and sales support to Cloud transformation pursuits including guidance to clients on how to move applications to Cloud
</li>
<li>Identify skills and grow team to support cloud migration engagements</li>
<li>
Build proof of concepts for Cloud solutions and support Go-To-Market activities for cloud solutions</li>
<li>
Contribute to thought leadership and development of cloud transformation marketing collateral</li>
<li>
Work with KPMG Global services to hire and grow team with cloud capabilities as well as manage teams, projects and clients</li>
<li>
Create assets to leverage as accelerators for clients in their Cloud Transformation journey</li>
</ul>
<p>
Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>
A minimum of eight years of customer interaction in a technical, technical sales, or technical leadership role</li>
<li>
Three plus years of Azure/AWS architecture and software development on Azure platform (Azure/AWS certification a plus)</li>
<li>
Advanced knowledge of at least two in the following technology areas; Core infrastructure, virtualization and migrating systems to cloud, Cloud Solutions, Information Management</li>
<li>
Exceptional communication, presentation and interpersonal skills with an ability to extract, translate and communicate meaningful information at all levels of executives, senior managers, and associates across business and technology organizations
</li>
<li>Bachelor's or Master's degree in Information Technology, Business, Computer Science or a related field from an accredited college</li>
<li>
Experience and desire to work in a technology consulting environment; travel may be up to 80-100%</li>
</ul>
