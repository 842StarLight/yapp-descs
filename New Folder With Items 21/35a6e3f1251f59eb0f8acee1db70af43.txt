<h2>Store Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Store Manager</b><br>
<br>
Responsible for directing the daily operations of a single store. Operates in accordance with prescribed policies and practices. Ensures compliance with standards for customer relations, food costs, safety, sanitation, and product preparation and merchandising. May participate in image-building activities within local community. As highest management position within unit, is accountable for the unit and its operations at all times whether physically present or not.<br>
<br>
<b>Principal Duties and Responsibilities:</b> Majority of duties performed, but not meant to be inclusive or to prevent other duties from being assigned.<ul>
<li>
Responsible for professional growth &amp; development of assistant managers, shift leaders and sales associates.</li>
<li>
Responsible for assessing all store employees performance;
</li>
<li>Responsible for partnering with GM and share shift crew best practices and drive store growth and performance.</li>
<li>
Responsible for monitoring performance/customer service and morale of store employees</li>
<li>
Responsible for handling disciplinary action and terminations of store staff (when necessary)</li>
<li>
Responsible for interacting with guests, monitoring store performance based on guest feedback and following up on consumer care compliments and complaints.</li>
<li>
Provide ongoing training as needed regarding new products, promotions, policies etc.</li>
<li>
Compliance with all federal, state and local labor laws.</li>
<li>
Ensure store budget is met per district manager/franchisee</li>
<li>
Ensure proper execution of new product introductions and marketing programs within store</li>
<li>
Complete weekly food and paper inventory</li>
<li>
Complete DCP and other vendor orders weekly or as needed</li>
<li>
Complete temperature &amp; coffee calibration logs, and provide product order and throwaway sheets to central production facility daily. Also complete the red and blue book.</li>
<li>
Complete daily cash management functions (cash reports, deposits etc. in its entire)</li>
<li>
Complete weekly schedule for store personnel</li>
<li>
Responsible for achieving satisfactory and passing scores on all Mystery Shop visits, store visits, ROR, Retail Food Safety &amp; Sanitation Inspections follow up is required on all network and regulatory visits.</li>
<li>
Responsible for creating store action plans to drive store performance around areas of opportunity</li>
<li>
Responsible for the daily maintenance of all equipment, stock areas, sales areas, building and grounds</li>
<li>
Conduct random morning, afternoon and evening store visits to ensure compliance with store policies and procedures.</li>
<li>
Ensuring proper staff levels at all times for optimum scheduled times.</li>
<li>
Conducting regular travel paths throughout your shift and taking action on there results</li>
<li>
Make sure that on every shift the pic in charge is running the operations by shift coaching</li>
</ul>
<b>
Minimum Age</b><p>
21+ years old</p>
<b>
Additional Job Specifications:</b> Knowledge, skills and abilities normally required for competent performance in the job.<ul>
<li>
2+ years in a food service/retail environment</li>
<li>
Strong interpersonal and leadership skills</li>
<li>
Judgment, tact, and diplomacy to effectively resolve conflicts</li>
<li>
Intermediate PC skills - ability to use basic word processing and spreadsheet software for internal communications and data analysis.</li>
</ul>
