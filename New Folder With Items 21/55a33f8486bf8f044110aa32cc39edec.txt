<h2>EDI/Order Management</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Provide administrative and customer support to the wholesale division. This position will require the preparation and submission of purchase orders, the management of incoming orders, order entry, returns, credits, inventory accuracy, and general inquiries. The Order Management/EDI person will be responsible for but not limited to the following duties:</p>
<ul>
<li>Manage all functions of the product catalog, including initial upload and periodic updates.</li>
<li>Receive, review, process, and transmit all EDI Documents related to EDI Sales Orders.</li>
<li>Identify and resolve any issues resulting from EDI functions in a timely manner, including price and quantity discrepancies per product.</li>
<li>Provide support in relation to vendor mapping and routing.</li>
<li>Ensure the flow of data via all EDI channels is operating properly and without error.</li>
<li>Maintain accurate and organized company records.</li>
<li>Organize and execute multiple tasks by the respective deadline.</li>
<li>Provide immediate wholesale customer support via telephone, email, and fax when prompted.</li>
<li>Monitor customer orders from start to finish and process them punctually.</li>
<li>Ensure that all system changes are processed according to the change control guideline.</li>
<li>Solve EDI production technical problems.</li>
<li>Solve unresolved customer issues and investigate chargebacks.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications and Skills</b></p>
<ul>
<li>1-2 Years working experience with EDI Software and SCMS.</li>
<li>Knowledge of QuickBooks Desktop A+</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Office (Word, Excel, Outlook, etc.).</li>
<li>Ability to work independently and as a team.</li>
<li>Ability to multitask, prioritize and problem solve while providing excellent customer service.</li>
<li>Must have strong interpersonal skills, both verbal and written. <i>Please include cover letter with desired salary when applying for this position</i>
</li>
</ul>

</ul>
