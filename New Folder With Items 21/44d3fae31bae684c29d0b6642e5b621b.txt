<h2>Director of Sales Development</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>Upserve's Business Development Representatives are on the front-line of our business and are responsible for prospecting and scheduling demo presentations for our Account Executives. In this role you will oversee and motivate the managers of this competitive team with a focus on delivering a winning strategy and coaching. You will report into our Chief Marketing Officer and own the execution of our company’s outbound prospecting efforts to drive pipeline for the organization.</div>
<div>Day to day, your responsibilities will include:<br>
<ul>
<li>Using technology (Salesforce, Outreach, and Marketo) to track progress against goals and improve productivity</li>
<li>Meeting and striving to exceed weekly and monthly team goals</li>
<li>Developing and executing our Outbound prospecting strategies, including script updates and targeted campaigns</li>
<li>Working closely with Marketing, Sales, and Operations teams to create the tools and processes that will enable your team’s success</li>
<li>Coaching your team on sales best practices by analyzing quantitative metrics as well as recorded call reviews</li>
<li>Joining calls with important customers to help educate them about Upserve’s platform, knowing our product inside and out (we’ll train you of course)</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
</div>
<p>Requirements</p>
<ul>
<li>Proven track record of attaining 100% of quota for consecutive months</li>
<li>Minimum of 5 years of sales experience, preferably within a B2B SaaS environment</li>
<li>Prior team lead or people manager experience required</li>
<li>Proven experience executing sales strategy using a data driven approach</li>
<li>Ability to hire, coach, train, and mentor successful SDRs</li>
<li>Experience with Salesforce, Outreach.io (or similar power dialers) and other tech to report and analyze sales funnel and sales cycle metrics</li>
</ul>
- An entrepreneurial spirit - our sales team is hard-working, well organized, self-motivated and confident<br>
<ul><li>A passion for sales and technology that helps restaurateurs succeed</li></ul>
</div>
