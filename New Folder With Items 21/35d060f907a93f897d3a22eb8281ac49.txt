<h2>Sales&Catering Administrative Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><i><b>We are looking for highly motivated employees who bring their all to work every day. We have a high productivity and high engagement culture that is not for anyone seeking a comfort zone. If you are ready to rock, we are a place for you.</b></i></p>
<p><b>JOB SUMMARY </b></p>
<p>Assist the Sales Team in the administrative functions required for effective hotel sales and management.</p>
<p><b>CANDIDATE PROFILE/ JOB FUNCTIONS</b></p>
<p>Experience</p>
<ul>
<li>Hotel experience preferred.</li>
<li>High School Diploma, Bachelor’s Degree preferred.</li>
</ul>
<p>Skills and Knowledge</p>
<ul>
<li>Basic administrative knowledge such as business letters, formats and telephone etiquette.</li>
<li>Ability to effectively deal with internal and external customers, some of whom will require high levels of patience, tact and diplomacy.</li>
<li>Ability to access and accurately input information into a computer using Microsoft Office, Opera, Merlin.</li>
<li>Ability to follow written and verbal instructions.</li>
<li>Ability to stand and/or sit and continuously perform essential job functions for duration of shift with or without reasonable accommodation.</li>
<li>Ability to set-up and maintain filing systems.</li>
<li>Command of the English language both written and verbal.</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Functions <i>(Learning and Applying Personal Expertise)</i></p>
<p>The following are specific contributions that must be maintained in order to successfully maintain the Sales &amp; Marketing department:</p>
<ul>
<li>Act as Liaison between Sales Manager(s) and clients in Sales Manager's absence. Take booking inquiry information via phone and prepare proper paperwork.</li>
<li>Uses various computer word processing and other software packages to enter data, produce contracts, correspondence, forms, memorandum and other documents.</li>
<li>Answer telephone and respond to caller inquiries speaking in a clear and pleasant manner. Exercise decision making skills to direct caller’s request. Accurately record messages for staff and distribute the written messages to the staff members in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Distribute documents to appropriate persons and locations to ensure prompt processing of time sensitive information to the staff. Transport documents weighing up to 10 pounds to offices, mailrooms and other locations throughout the hotel building. Retrieve documents from and place documents into proper receptacles located up to 6 feet high, such as mailboxes, file cabinets, etc.</li>
<li>Handle individual reservations for VIP clients. Ensure that all arrangements, including upgrades, are handled properly, coordinating with other departments through oral and written instruction.</li>
<li>Organize, file and retrieve documents in appropriate binders in order to maintain essential records used in the department operation.</li>
<li>Prepare for all sales &amp; marketing trade shows and participate with the sales team when required.</li>
<li>Assist with hotel accounting as requested.</li>
<li>Assist with Front Office functions as requested.</li>
<li>Comply with attendance rules and be available to work on a regular basis.</li>
<li>Perform any other job related duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
