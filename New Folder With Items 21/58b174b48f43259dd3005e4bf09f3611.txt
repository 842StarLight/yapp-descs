<h2>Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Spark Hire is an award-winning video interviewing platform (Software-as-a-Service) used by more than 5,000 customers in 100+ countries. Our industry-leading platform is recognized as the gold standard by esteemed software review site G2 Crowd. We’ve also been recognized as one of the best startups to work for (nationally) and a best small business to work for in the Chicago area.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>As an Account Executive, your sole focus is to demo the Spark Hire platform and close deals. There's no cold calling. There's no prospecting. There's no appointment-setting. You're at the end of the sales funnel. Your day gets booked with demos by our Sales Development Reps so you can focus on what you do best: close business.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Key Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Conduct virtual product demonstrations with prospective clients</li>
<li>Showcase the value of Spark Hire’s platform and personalize this value-based approach by becoming a subject matter expert (e.g. market segment, job titles, industry, etc)</li>
<li>Close new client accounts employing a consultative sales approach</li>
<li>Hand off new clients to the Client Success Team ensuring a smooth onboarding experience</li>
<li>Master the tools in our sales stack to efficiently nurture and close deals in your pipeline</li>
<li>Be organized and diligent in your research, note-taking and correspondence</li>
<li>Maintain a strong pipeline with a minimum number of top accounts and corresponding contract value</li>
<li>Exceed monthly revenue targets as a product of maintaining healthy metrics and KPIs</li>
<li>Willingness to work in a collaborative work environment</li>
<li>Polished communication and writing skills</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>If you're looking for an exciting place to work and a chance to continue the development of your skills as a sales professional, apply today!</p>
