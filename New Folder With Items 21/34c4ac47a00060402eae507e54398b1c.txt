<div><b>Responsibilities</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>Our Medical Devices Research &amp; Development Leadership Development Program (RDLDP) is a two-year dynamic rotational program that offers exciting and unrivaled assignment experiences supporting a wide variety of highly specialized, cutting-edge R&amp;D teams, such as: New Product Development, Mechanical Design, Computational Modeling and Simulation, Materials Development, Robotics, Research and Testing, Manufacturing Project Management and Design Quality. As an RDLDP, you will:</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li><div>Work in a fast-paced cross functional, technologically advanced corporate environment in a rotational program focused on developing individual engineers capable of pursuing careers across medical device businesses and high-volume manufacturers.</div></li>
<li><div>
Have the opportunity to learn about anatomy, disease states, and deformities and the surgical procedures and products used to address these conditions.</div></li>
<li><div>
Lead and/or support an entire project or a large portion of a major project that may include the design, development, manufacturing and commercialization of new products or product modifications.</div></li>
<li><div>
Develop advanced materials and/or medical devices, improve processes, manage and mitigate project risks, and/or perform test method validation and mechanical testing.</div></li>
<li><div>
Develop and deliver automated medical devices including requisite instruments, advanced imaging, and user interface / experience.</div></li>
<li><div>
Leverage your technical expertise to assist with product development from concept design to commercialization with exposure to cross functional departments like design quality, manufacturing, supply chain, marketing, and regulatory.
</div></li>
<li><div>Utilize solid modeling to design products, create drawings and tolerance analyses of mating components.</div></li>
<li><div>
Become familiar with and create design control documentation required by regulatory governing bodies.</div></li>
<li><div>
Participate in internal developmental training programs, as well as, externally recognized qualifications such as Process and Design Excellence.
</div></li>
<li><div>Present to leadership your learnings and development experience.</div></li>
</ul>
