<h2>Senior Customer Success Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>About NECI</b>
<br>
<br>
NECI, an Emerson Impact Partner, is the leading Digital Automation solutions provider transforming manufacturing, lab operations, process development and process control across a range of process industries in New England. NECI relentlessly seeks to drive the outcomes that <b>change the game</b> for our clients and is seeking team members to join in our mission.
<br>
<br>
<b>The Role</b>
<br>
<br>
We are currently looking for a resourceful, driven, team player with a competitive spirit, and strong industry or business acumen to help us penetrate new customers with our industry solutions.
<br>
<br>
<b>How Youll Be Successful</b>
<ul>
<li>Curiosity for solving business problems</li>
<li>Passion for making your customers successful</li>
<li>Ability to articulate the ROI of technical solution across multiple stakeholders</li>
<li>Experience doing deep levels of discovery and objection handling at entry level</li>
<li>Engage with customers at entry level of the sales cycle (account planning, development of the deal, close of the business, and beyond)</li>
<li>Trusted advisor to the C-Suite and the end users</li>
<li>Not only selling the best industry solutions; but also, cross sell our entire suite of products and services such as Manufacturing Operations, Lab Operations, Reliability, Energy, and more</li>
</ul>
<b>What Youll Do</b>
<ul>
<li>Generate new business within one of NECIs largest strategic accounts with a large global footprint, and play a key role to drive strategic Manufacturing Operations Solutions</li>
<li>Forecast sales activity and revenue achievement while creating satisfied customers that can be referenced future sales cycles</li>
<li>Develop long term account strategies within the assigned accounts to forecast and plan 1-3 years out</li>
<li>Engaged with global and site business unit sales leads to ensure unified account plan aligns with the customer site business objectives</li>
<li>Contact client directly and be able to gain access to all areas of the account to pursue and develop new opportunities</li>
<li>Uncover incremental opportunities that align with NECI offerings and can provide client significant business value</li>
<li>Work closely with cross functional teams to define and execute on the key account plan</li>
<li>Leverage and expand sales in existing accounts and manage profit margins in support of the companys business plan</li>
<li>Network and build strong relationships to maximize sales territory growth; directly manage client relationships and support NECI personnel in establishing and maintaining relationships important for overall health of the account</li>
<li>Work with the product &amp; services teams to accurately maintain sales forecast, achieve quarterly and yearly orders/revenue targets</li>
<li>Ensure highest standards in client interactions (solution positioning &amp; presentations, proposal response, etc.) Align with broader organization (Marketing/Service) to ensure customer needs are met to maximize NECI business value</li>
</ul>
<b>What Youll Bring</b>
<ul>
<li>Bachelors Degree desired</li>
<li>10+ years of solution sales experience selling Solutions or similar business applications</li>
<li>Life Sciences industry business knowledge</li>
<li>Experience selling Software (Cloud/SaaS/Edge on premise) or Service Solutions to a select few large strategic enterprise accounts</li>
<li>Ability to sell C-Level and across both IT and business units</li>
<li>Ability to sell both an application and deployment of a platform</li>
<li>A proven track record of driving and closing solution deals</li>
<li>Strong track record and history of carrying and exceeding a sales quota</li>
<li>Consistent overachievement of quota and revenue goals</li>
<li>Understanding of/accountability to sales behavior metrics including managing business opportunity in a CRM</li>
<li>Knowledge of the unique business opportunities and solutions within the Lab/Manufacturing Operations account space</li>
<li>Track record selling Enterprise software solutions</li>
<li>Account planning and execution skills</li>
<li>Strong technical aptitude</li>
<li>Strong attention management skills</li>
<li>Proven consultative sales solution skills, including the ability to articulate a clear, concise return on investment value statement</li>
<li>Ability to maintain a high level of productivity, manage multiple competing priorities, and work effectively under the pressure of time constraints in a fast-paced, complex, collaborative, and team-oriented environment</li>
</ul>
