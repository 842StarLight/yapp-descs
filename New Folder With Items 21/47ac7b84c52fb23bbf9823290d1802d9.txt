<h2>Retail Territory Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div>
<p><b>Join ABB and work in a team that is dedicated to creating a future where innovative digital technologies allow greater access to cleaner energy.
</b></p>
<p>ABB is seeking a Retail Territory Manager for the state of California. Los Angeles is preferred location. Retail Territory Manager will be responsible for the sales to Home Depot and Lowe’s - Home Depot and Lowe’s have over 3700 store locations nationally that stock and sell ABB electrification type products.
</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Your responsibilities</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Typical duties/responsibilities may include, but are not limited to, the following:</li>
<li>Responsible for analyzing sales trends and implementing strategies to drive ABB sales by market. ABB has two major product lines in the retail channel - ELIS (residential type electrical products) and ELIP (installation or Thomas &amp; Betts type products).</li>
<li>ABB ELIS has over 1200 market driven store plan-o-grams.</li>
<li>ABB ELIP has 6 product lines in various retail markets /stores.</li>
<li>Primary focus is to drive sales through Regional Merchandise Manager interaction. Each RMM is responsible for sales in approx. 100 stores and empowered to drive regional mix and off shelf promotions.</li>
<li>Investigate scrap and field issues – customer and internal ABB. Work with the plants and distribution center - benchmark, root cause, monitor.
</li>
<li>Understand and articulate quarterly forecast by product line.
</li>
<li>Gain and maintain strong relationships throughout all departments at Home Depot and Lowe’s (buyers, store support people).
</li>
<li>Work in conjunction with the current National Account Manager, responsible for NA sales.</li>
</ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Your background</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Basic Qualifications:</li>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree from an accredited university or college with minimum 2 years of experience in sales, preferably in retail</li>
<li>Candidates must already have a work authorization that would permit them to work for ABB in the US</li>
<li>Preferred Qualifications:</li>
<li>Bachelor's Degree in Engineering/Industrial Distribution</li>
<li>Experience with complex contact negotiations</li>
<li>Demonstrated knowledge of electrical industry and ABB products</li>
<li>Ability and willingness to possess and maintain a valid driver's license</li>
<li>Ability and willingness to travel within the United States</li>
<li>Experience working with contractors &amp; channel partners</li>
<li>Strong technical application, take-off, and proposal development skills</li>
<li>Customer-focused mindset with proven ability to respond quickly to customer needs</li>
<li>Knowledge of customer financial drivers / needs</li>
<li>Six Sigma or equivalent Quality training</li>
<li>Ability to utilize analytical software and sales tools</li>
<li>Strong oral, written communication skills and presentation skills</li>
</ul>
