Alice helps small business owners launch and grow. Since launching in May 2017, our goal is to help 6 million owners succeed through our free digital platform,
<br>HelloAlice.com [http://www.helloalice.com/]. Alice serves all entrepreneurs and we wholeheartedly believe that if underserved business owners -- women, people of color, veterans, the LGBTQ+ community, entrepreneurs with disabilities, people in small towns, and immigrants -- are provided better access to resources, they can change the world. Our vision is to be financially successful while making a positive impact on economies and job creation.
<br><br>
Alice is growing a diverse team, and we’re looking for a Director of Marketing to help us fulfill our mission of connecting all entrepreneurs to the resources they need to grow and scale their businesses. We’re leading a movement to connect every founder, regardless of geography, capitalization, prior experience, or cultural constraints, to the experts, tools, knowledge, and communities that will propel their companies forward. Alice is a Series A-backed company with teams in Houston, TX and San Francisco, CA. We are helping hundreds of thousands of owners a week in all fifty states. Led by co-founders Elizabeth Gore and Carolyn Rodz, we are hiring self-starters who can build the plane while flying it.
<br><br>
Alice's Director of Marketing will be responsible for driving marketing decisions to achieve both short and long term strategic and financial objectives with a single cohesive customer experience. You will directly lead owned marketing channels and platform teams - including Email, Push Notifications, Promotions, and Social Media. We are looking for someone who is excited to engage our audience of small business owners through a strong product (
<br>www.HelloAlice.com [http://www.helloalice.com/]), SEO/SEM, content and social media presence. The candidate will be responsible for developing solutions to meet business objectives based on insight and data. Spotting trends and technologies that play key roles in the lives of our core target audiences, as well as deep understanding with SEM/SEO/Social strategies that drive engagement, demand and ultimately revenue, is critical. This role will work closely with our CEO and President, to develop and evolve ideas to bring together company goals, sales objectives and customer engagement. We are looking for a team player who will build a long term digital strategy and transform the digital landscape for small business owners.
<br><br>
<b>Required Skills / Experiences/ Tasks:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>6+ years of experience in digital marketing; general management and strategy consulting experience preferred</li>
<li>Digital brand planning experience, with a broad grasp of digital strategies across Content, Video, Inbound, Demand Gen, Web, Social, Influencer Strategy and Digital PR.</li>
<li>Experience in working with, or for, startups, and a preference for lean, accelerated startup culture</li>
<li>Willingness to work from Houston company headquarters or San Francisco office</li>
<li>Translating business priorities into marketing strategies and tactics in conjunction with other teams to achieve broad financial and strategic goals</li>
<li>Develop and execute strategies, concepts and approaches for new client initiatives across all digital platforms/touchpoints Content, Video, Inbound, Demand Gen, Web, Social, Influencer Strategy and Digital PR</li>
<li>Build amazing, diverse teams and a winning culture that encourages innovation and mentorship. Establish motivating stretch objectives for team members that align with company objectives. Encourage coaching and mentoring, focus on the development needs of employees and ensure collaboration and teamwork.</li>
<li>Lead analysis across all platforms and channels to measure overall performance drivers, including calendar optimization, steering decisions, and conversion optimization. This includes solving unstructured problems using complex data and moving past simple insights into actionable recommendations.</li>
<li>Establish best practices in digital marketing</li>
<li>Evaluate data to provide insights that inform &amp; support multichannel digital strategies</li>
<li>Set digital marketing strategies using all necessary tools (e.g. website, emails, social media and blogs)</li>
<li>Research products, services and current strategies to identify new opportunities</li>
<li>Analyze web traffic metrics and suggest solutions to boost web presence</li>
<li>Monitor SEO/SEM, marketing and sales performance metrics to forecast trends</li>
<li>Build strong clients relationships through social media interaction</li>
<li>Keep up-to-date with our audience preferences and proactively suggest new campaigns</li>
<li>Liaise with Partnerships, Community Impact, Product, Design and Content leads to optimize client experience and ensure corporate brand consistency</li>
<li>Identify advertising needs</li>
<li>Stay up-to-date with digital technologies developments</li>
</ul>
