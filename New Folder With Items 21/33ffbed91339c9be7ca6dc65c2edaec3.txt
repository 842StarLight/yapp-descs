<h2>Drift (Inbound) Sales Development Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Description:</b><br>
<b>WHO WE ARE HIRING:</b><br>
We’re looking for a Drift (Inbound) Sales Development Representative to join our fast growing Sales organization. This role will manage the inbound channel, qualifying leads and connecting leads with the appropriate team and serve as a product expert for external inquiries. Ideal candidates should be eager to advance in our organization by demonstrating their ability to be tenacious self-starters every day. .
<br><br>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
<b>WHO YOU ARE:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree or equivalent practical experience</li>
<li>1-2 years experience in a phone sales role as part of a SaaS or B2B company</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication, listening and negotiation skills</li>
<li>Self-motivated, goal oriented, ideally with proven history of meeting or exceeding sales goals</li>
<li>Strong organizational and time management skills, with the ability to prioritize competing tasks</li>
<li>Eager and driven to learn and thrive in a fast-paced, constantly changing environment</li>
</ul>
<b>WHAT YOU’LL DO:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Leverage Drift Software and Webchat to establish contact with prospective clients</li>
<li>Working amongst cross functional teams to drive revenue (SDR’s, Account Executives, Customer Success)</li>
<li>Understand the impact our products have on our customers</li>
<li>Serve as a product matter expert to answer an array of questions from prospective clients</li>
<li>Articulate to prospects how our tools can help their organizations get to the next level with real-time data</li>
<li>Manage prospecting status, data integrity, and forecasting in Salesforce.com</li>
<li>View this opportunity as a career, not as a job.</li>
</ul>
<b>WHAT WE OFFER:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>A competitive salary</li>
<li>A rich benefits package including medical, dental, and vision</li>
<li>401(k) plan</li>
<li>Stocked kitchen complete with coffee, snacks, and Friday lunch catered</li>
<li>Flexible work hours</li>
<li>All of the opportunities inherent in a fast-growing organization!</li>
</ul>
