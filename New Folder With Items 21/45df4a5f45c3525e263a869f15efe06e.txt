<h2>Assistant Circulation Operations Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Sound Publishing, Inc. is currently accepting applications for a full-time Assistant Circulation<br>
Operations Manager. The position will be based out of our Everett office and will report to The<br>
Circulation Operations Manager. The primary duty is to manage the timely pick-up and distribution of<br>
the Daily Herald newspaper, and other publications assigned. This position primarily works in the field<br>
during the days, with occasional nights and weekends when the business requires. Newspaper and/or<br>
Merchandising experience preferred.</p>
<p><b>
Candidate will:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Works directly with retailers to perform sales, service and collection activities.<br>
Perform route management and oversee the timely and orderly pick up and distribution of The</li>
<li>Daily Herald and other Sound publications.</li>
<li>Learn route deliveries to back up potential down routes, report retail site location changes and<br>
potential impact to site sales effectiveness and profitability.</li>
<li>Learn the methods and process to adjust draw using Newzware software</li>
<li>Be able to learn and apply specified route management for a successful AAM Audit.</li>
<li>Must be flexible to work nights and weekends as needed.</li>
<li>Merchandise newspaper single copy sites for maximum sales results.</li>
<li>Learn key business metrics for The Daily Herald single-copy business and report<br>
underperforming locations to Circ Operations Manager.</li>
<li>Install Sound Video Network sites and work with SVN staff for upcoming installation sites and<br>
Q/A issues.</li>
<li>Recommend new and profitable single copy and 3 rd party sites.</li>
<li>Must be a pro-active individual who works well with co-workers, delivery, dealer site staff, and<br>
carrier contractors.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Required Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Position requires the ability to operate a motor vehicle in a safe manner; to occasionally lift<br>
and/or transport bundles weighing up to 25 pounds from ground level to a height of 3 feet;</li>
<li>To communicate with carriers and the public by telephone and in-person;</li>
<li>To operate a personal computer, and possess a cellphone;</li>
<li>Must possess reliable, insured, a motor vehicle; and a clean and valid Washington State driver’s<br>
license.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What we offer you:</b><br>
We offer competitive compensation and a benefits package that includes medical, dental, vision and<br>
life insurance; paid time off (vacation, sick, and holidays); and a 401k with employer match.</p>
<p><b>
Job Type:</b> Full-time</p>
<p><b>
TO APPLY –</b> To apply, please e-mail to careers@soundpublishing.com provided (1) a resume including at least three<br>
professional references (2) a cover letter addressing the specific job requirements we’ve outlined, and<br>
(4) three references. Make sure to include Asst CircMgr in the subject line. Please also include<br>
your salary requirements.</p>
