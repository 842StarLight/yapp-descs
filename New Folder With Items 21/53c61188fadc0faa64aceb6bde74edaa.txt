<b>Main Objective</b><b>:</b>
</div>
<div>Work in conjunction with other HALO staff to facilitate and assure quality/timeliness of full life-cycle transactions (order entry, billing, credit/collections, etc.) for assigned strategic accounts. This may include data collection, reporting, analyzing data, and providing creative solutions to meet the needs of our Account Executives and their strategic accounts.</div>
<div><b>Essential Duties &amp; Responsibilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Facilitate the movement of assigned account’s orders through the billing and collection processes, by addressing questions on vendor invoices and customer purchase orders.</li>
<li>Liaison for questions regarding assigned strategic accounts.</li>
<li>Collaborate with sales team to establish service level expectations for assigned accounts.</li>
<li>Assist with resolution of freight and accounts payable issues related to assigned accounts.</li>
<li>Monitor key operational performance indicators for assigned accounts, assuring that defined service levels for the account are consistently met throughout each order’s life cycle.</li>
<li>Provide guidance to the sales team for operational escalations related to assigned accounts.</li>
<li>Other projects and duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<div>
<b>Required Education/Experience</b><b>:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree or equivalent work experience</li>
<li>A minimum of 3 years work experience in a professional services environment, preferably in general office management or project management.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Required Skills:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Exceptional interpersonal and communication skills</li>
<li>Capable of understanding the full business process flow, from order entry to collections</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Office software such as Outlook, Excel and Word</li>
<li>Organized, detail-oriented, and project a positive and professional attitude</li>
<li>Exceptional keyboarding/data entry skills</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task and manage multiple priorities in order to work well under pressure and meet deadlines</li>
<li>High level skill in problem solving and decision making</li>
<li>Strong teamwork skills</li>
<li>Available to work overtime when required</li>
<li>Possess and project a positive and professional attitude at all times</li>
<li>Able to advocate process enhancements and change</li>
<li>Ability to travel</li>
</ul>
