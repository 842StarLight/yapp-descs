<p>Are you passionate about crafting content people just love to read and share?</p>

We’re currently looking to add a talented senior content writer to our marketing team. This position would serve all companies under the James Scott Farrin umbrella, which includes multiple law practices, a consulting service, and a software company.</p>

<li>Testimonials</li>

</li>

</li>

<li>Has at least 5 years of professional writing experience, marketing/sales copy preferred (SEO and content marketing knowledge is also a strong plus).
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Excited? If so, please reply with:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>A cover letter</li>
<li>Your resume</li>

<li>Three writing samples, ideally showing your ability to: simplify a technical concept, write creatively, and write effective sales copy.</li>
</ul>
<p>We offer a competitive benefits package, and salary is determined based on experience.</p>
