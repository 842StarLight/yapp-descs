<div>
The Graphics Depot Coordinator is responsible for entering data and maintaining the accuracy and integrity of various aspects of Order Tracking. They are required to generate various reports to management and support personnel, as well as to verify job numbers and work order numbers, and to follow up with necessary personnel to ensure the accuracy of the information given on the work orders. The Graphics Depot Coordinator provides consistent, timely, and accurate documentation of job activity related to each show and work order. This information is used by project managers, account managers, graphics supervisors, and shop supervisors in analyzing and improving the project execution process.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
This position requires a high level of customer service skills. The ability to communicate professionally with all levels of staff and with external clients is a must. The Graphics Depot Coordinator must be able to work well under pressure while maintaining professionalism during stressful peak business cycles.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The Graphics Depot Coordinator must be a team player and must be able to shift gears when necessary. Must be highly organized and have the ability to prioritize multiple tasks. Flexibility in work schedules is needed in order to accommodate the workload. Proficiency in Data Entry, Excel (understanding formulas), Word, Access and Outlook is required.</div>
