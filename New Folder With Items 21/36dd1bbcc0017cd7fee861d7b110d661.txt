<h2>Car Sales General Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Car Sales General Manager</b></p>
<p>
Manages the location team to achieve the operational and revenue performance goals of an assigned retail car sales location.</p>
<p><b>
Duties and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible to consistently and profitably meet new vehicle sales quotas through expert management and leadership of the sales team.</li>
<li>Forecast sales, maintain proper inventory levels in terms of amount and vehicle types, and motivate the sales team.</li>
<li>Responsible for hiring, training, and monitoring team member performance. He/she is responsible for the resolution of customer complaints and to assist in closings when necessary</li>
<li>Train the sales team on sales methodologies that support established sales targets</li>
<li>Train sales team to become subject matter experts on all vehicles and vehicle specifications/features in the inventory</li>
<li>Establish sales quotas for each sales representative and provide continuous feedback in order to manage to overall sales goals</li>
<li>Continuously monitor and adjust inventory to optimize customer choice</li>
<li>Measure and report sales metrics; monitor actuals-to-plan</li>
<li>Continuously and consistently promote the dealership, its values, and its products/services</li>
<li>Finalizing Purchase, Trade-in, and Sales agreements, contracts, etc.</li>
<li>Utilizing e-commerce tools, i.e. social media, listing services, inventory systems, etc.</li>
<li>Executing advertising and promotional plans</li>
<li>Assisting with various Remarketing initiatives</li>
<li>Work closely with ABG Car Rental facilities</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications/Experience:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma required, Bachelor’s degree preferred.</li>
<li>Minimum of 3 years of experience in a Retail Car Sales Management position.</li>
<li>Valid driver’s license with a clean driving record.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Skills:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>General business knowledge to successfully operate and manage a corporate retail store.</li>
<li>Excellent customer service skills.</li>
<li>Strong organizational and analytical skills to track all appropriate operational measures and analyze and forecast operational performance results.</li>
<li>Strong communication and coaching skills. Ability to interact with both internal and external customers at all levels. Ability to manage and motivate staff in a team environment.</li>
<li>Requires problem solving and decision making skills.</li>
<li>Strong understanding of marketing programs, events and promotions.</li>
<li>Must be proficient with various computer applications including social media and mobile applications.</li>
<br>
</ul>
