<h2>Training Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Job purpose</b></p>
<p>The training administrator updates and organizes company-training programs for personnel. The training administrator prepares, monitors, evaluates, and documents training activities and provides overall support to the training department.
</p>
<ul>
<li>Manage multiple channel and organization training calendars</li>
<li>Process all training requests (ex. external from AT&amp;T; internal from DSI) immediately getting them on the appropriate calendar once received</li>
<li>Provide calendar reporting and printouts for management</li>
<li>Strategize, plan, and map out training plans/schedules for trainers</li>
<li>Ensure training content is accurate, up-to-date, and deadlines are met</li>
<li>Track and report on training outcomes</li>
<li>Create and manage zoom webinars, recordings, and track attendance</li>
<li>Compile and archive course evaluation data and supporting materials for all training events.</li>
<li>Respond to questions and requests for information</li>
<li>Implement and monitor programs as directed by management</li>
<li>Monitor and keep Field Sales Representative tool kit up-to-date</li>
<li>Suggest, develop, track and traffic training aids from start to completion to delivery</li>
<li>Create and manage Dropbox users, folders, and content</li>
<li>Create forms/documents/guides for training in general</li>
<li>Ensure trainers have all materials needed for training events (ex. brochures, handouts, evaluations, participant guides, etc.)</li>
<li>Ensure RMs/FSRs have all materials needed for field events (trainings, table days, morning huddles, off-campus meetings (ex. event kits, brochures, acrylic stands, etc.)</li>
<li>Manage logistics of training events</li>
<li>Assist field support with internal requests</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Manage internal training processes
</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>Evaluate and make recommendations on training content and delivery</li>
<li>Assist with new hire onboarding efforts, logistics, planning, execution</li>
<li>Research and evaluate training software</li>
<li>Provide support to trainers and management as needed</li>
<li>Assist in managing software applications/packages such as Flow, Slack, Yapp, Talent, and others as needed.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Planning and organizing</li>
<li>Verbal and Written communication skills</li>
<li>Data gathering and analysis</li>
<li>Problem analysis and problem solving</li>
<li>General understanding of training development</li>
<li>General understanding of curriculum development</li>
<li>Judgment</li>
<li>Facilitation skills</li>
<li>Adaptable</li>
<li>Dependable</li>
<li>Team player</li>
<li>Multitasking</li>
<li>Microsoft Office, particularly Powerpoint</li>
<li>Project management software – ex. Flow</li>
<li>Communications software – ex. Slack</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Benefits</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Medical &amp; Dental insurance plan</li>
<li>401k Plan</li>
<li>Paid vacation and personal / sick days</li>
<br>
</ul>
