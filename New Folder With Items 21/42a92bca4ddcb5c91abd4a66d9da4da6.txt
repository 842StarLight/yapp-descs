We are dedicated to the fight against iffy, mothbally, shoddy software. We make better, more usable tools for teaching and learning (you know, stuff people will actually use). We accomplish all this by giving smart, creative, passionate people opportunities to create awesome. So here’s your opportunity.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Within the role you will need to be able to help develop a revenue-generating website that will promote Instructure, it’s varying brands/products, attract new visitors, convert leads into sales, and produce repeat business. The manager would be responsible for managing, tracking and testing marketing campaigns, using pay per click (PPC) advertising, writing compelling ad copy, and doing keyword research.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
In this role you will work side-by-side with other web experts, such as web designers, SEO specialists, and digital content managers to enhance website traffic through a variety of paid mechanisms.</div>
</div>

<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What you will be doing:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
AdWords Advanced Search Certification</li>
<li>
Strong technical background, with at least 5-7 years of experience managing PPC campaigns.</li>
<li>
Highly analytical and data-driven, capable of owning growth metrics, running tests to quantify impact and interpreting results.</li>
<li>
Works well in a fast-paced team environment and enjoys creating rewarding relationships with the people that you work with.</li>
<li>
Combination of technical ability and excellent communication. Would be just as comfortable writing enticing ad copy as you would be manipulating keyword research in an Excel spreadsheet.</li>
<li>
Attention to detail and strong analytical skills.
</li>
</ul></div>

<li>
Stay informed of new betas, tactics, and data trends within the paid search industry.</li>

Run optimization tests, Interpret the results to drive strategy.</li>

Regularly update marketing leaders on strategy and performance.</li>
