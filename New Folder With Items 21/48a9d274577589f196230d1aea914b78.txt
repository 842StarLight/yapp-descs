<h2>Business Implementation Advisor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>
<div>The Enterprise Business Analytics group at SVB is responsible for providing actionable analytical solutions and products to help SVB sales executives and leaders achieve their initiatives and goals. Typical examples of initiatives include acquiring clients, retaining clients, deepening client relationships and helping our clients to succeed.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>We are looking for a Business Implementation/Product Manager who will help us create and deliver key insights and products using our platform. Your primary focus will be to design, implement and improve our existing platform that helps us identify top prospects, produce relevant benchmarks and identify key trends in the Venture Ecosystem. We’ll be looking for someone who can leverage Finance and Venture Capital knowledge to cross-functionally deliver analytical data products. There will be multiple datasets to integrate and multiple channels in which to deploy our analytical solutions. We’ll be looking for someone who can think big, motivate cross-functional teams, and evangelize the products created.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>Responsibilities</b>:<br>
<ul>
<li>Build and execute a roadmap to support existing stakeholders and create innovative analytics products</li>
<li>Work with business partners and technical teams to build new algorithms and enhancements into the platform</li>
<li>Use experience and knowledge of the Venture Ecosystem to identify relevant insights to incorporate into our analytical products keeping up to date with the latest changes in the market</li>
<li>Craft compelling, data-driven stories that educate, excite, and empower our partners with actionable insights</li>
<li>Keep multiple projects and work streams on track at one time. These projects may be multi-month engagements and have multiple stakeholders across business groups</li>
</ul>
</div>
<br>
</div>
