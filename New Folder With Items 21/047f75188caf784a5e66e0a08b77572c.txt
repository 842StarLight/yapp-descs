<p>The Program Manager (PM) is a critical positions that has 5 major components at AllyO:</p>
<p>1. Shield: PMs are the point-contact for the sales and customer success teams for latest status on features and timelines. They collaborate with customer facing teams to reprioritize work with the least churn and distraction to the engineering teams as well as coach the customer facing teams in managing expectations.</p>
<p>2. Prioritize: PMs collect and facilitate resolving the entire end-to-end prioritization of work for the company and track the status of all projects in process and drive actions to move features from concept to GA utilizing a product maturity process.</p>
<p>3. Visibility: PMs provide the complete visibility of the status of all projects, timelines and next steps for the entire company. Managing complex frameworks in Jira is required to create the visibility for everyone.</p>
<p>4. Accountability: PMs hold internal and external resources/customers accountable for program deliverables.</p>
<p>5. Predictability: Taking a complete view of all planned and in progress work, PMs develop and communicate timelines considering the available resources and risks. The timelines provide sales and customers high confidence in deliverables meeting expectations in scope and quality.</p>
<p>Additionally, the Program Manager (PM) considers roadmap features as well as specific customer deliverables. The PM works across functions and geographies with internal and external resources. The PM also create SoWs for customer engagements as well as outsourced projects.</p>
<p>The PM will be working with state of the art technology for a SaaS application, so it is key to have experience with software development, agile processes, web applications, APIs, REST, etc.</p>
