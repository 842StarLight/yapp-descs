<p><b>LEASING PROFESSIONAL NEEDED IMMEDIATELY!</b></p>
<p>Tribute Properties is a dynamic multifamily real estate company, and we are looking for a <b>Leasing Consultant </b>for one of our brand new townhome rental communities in <i>Wilmington</i><i>, NC</i>.</p>
<p>If you're ready to grow in your career, please consider this opportunity!</p>
<p>Minimum Education / Experience Requirement: High School Diploma or GED Equivalent. Additional vocational training or a four-year college degree is preferred. Prior experience in leasing, sales or hospitality a plus.</p>
<p>Our Leasing Consultant professionals will be responsible for maximizing occupancy by possessing exceptional customer service skills and sales talent. The ideal candidate will demonstrate strong verbal and written communication dexterity along with high levels of organization and multi-tasking ability. Leasing Professionals will also be savvy of Fair Housing regulations and overall business practices. Candidates for this position must be able to work flexible hours and weekends.</p>
<p>Extensive knowledge of property management software and general computer literacy (e-mail; Internet; Microsoft Office programs) is also required.</p>
<p>Background and drug screen are conditions of employment.</p>
<p><b>Primary Duties and Responsibilities </b>include but are not limited to:</p>
<ul>
<li>Successfully meeting goals and achieving results by leasing and marketing vacant and upcoming apartments</li>
<li>Obtaining and maintaining competitive market data</li>
<li>Tour prospective residents and adapting sales techniques to overcome objections, build rapport, create excitement and close</li>
<li>Accurately entering all prospect data into software in a timely manner</li>
<li>Daily inspections of the model, market readies and property to ensure standards and curb appeal</li>
<li>Maintaining positive resident relations by:</li>
<li>Participating in the planning and preparation of resident functions</li>
<li>Assisting with resolving resident issues as they arise</li>
<li>Entering and following up on service requests</li>
<li>Working with the Community Manager to assist determining property operating objectives along with plans to execute them</li>
<li>Being knowledgeable of Risk Management and how to address emergency situations</li>
<li>Rent collection and working to reduce delinquency</li>
<li>Regularly inspecting the community to uphold company standards</li>
<li>Complying with and enforcing company policies and procedures</li>
<li>Handling general office administrative tasks such as auditing of lease files, overseeing A/R and A/P, completing leasing forms, compiling / processing applications and other required paperwork essential to everyday business</li>
</ul>
<p>Competitive benefits package includes: Medical Insurance, Dental, Vision, Paid Time Off, Discounted Housing available, and 401K Retirement Plan with company contribution. COMPETITIVE PAY.</p>
<p>Please send your resume in response to this ad to Attn: Hiring Manager.</p>
