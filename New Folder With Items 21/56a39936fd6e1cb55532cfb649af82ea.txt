NTT DATA Services currently seeks a Strategic Offering Marketing Senior Specialist Advisor to join our team in Plano, Texas (US-TX), United States (US).</p>

<br>
<div>
Strategic marketer who defines and develops messaging and positioning for solutions based on analysis, competitive evaluation and client needs. Executes demand generation and marketing programs to help our business grow and meet our clients’ requirements. Drives marketing strategy, including budget recommendations, roadmap planning, recommendations on content theme &amp; vertical relevance. Brings that strategy to life through hands-on execution of marketing plan. Holds domain expertise in IT Services market space and has a proven track record of driving campaigns and projects that result in success for their company.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Description</b></div>
<div>
The Offering MARKETING SENIOR SPECIALIST ADVISOR is an individual contributor who creates and activates Marketing &amp; Communications programs that support our strategic offerings and successfully position our company in the market to set NTT DATA apart from competitors.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
As an organization that is highly matrixed and values teamwork, you will collaborate across internal groups including: fellow marketers across our organization, business stakeholders, sales and proposal teams, subject matter experts, and content experts to implement marketing strategy, campaigns and programs on behalf of the NTT DATA Services business.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Messaging, positioning, communicating (verbal, written, digital and in the universal language: PowerPoint) are the key skills required. In this is fast-paced environment, pragmatic creativity, teamwork and flexibility are the attributes that will help you thrive in this role.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Key Responsibilities</b></div>
<div>
This is a strategic marketing role tasked with increasing NTT DATA Services awareness, driving preference, building pipeline by:</div>
<ul>
<li>
Translating business strategies and offerings into key marketing messages</li>
<li>
Creating and executing demand generation programs designed to generate awareness and build sales pipeline: events, email campaigns, online promotions, social media and digital ads, webinars, content for our NTT DATA Services website: www.nttdataservices.com</li>
<li>
Ensuring tight collaboration between business stakeholders and other areas of the business
</li>
<li>Creating and maintaining online and offline sales enablement assets</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Basic Qualifications</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Undergraduate degree, preferably in marketing or journalism.
</li>
<li>10+ years in marketing and communications experience working with a global company.
</li>
<li>Excellent writing, communications, project management and analytical skills with ablity to forge internal and external relationships.</li>
<li>
Strategic and creative thinker who can execute tactically with a proven track record of driving results in the following:<ul>
<li>
Online demand generation through promotions, direct mail, customer relations, and advertising.</li>
<li>
Event experience from content and amplification strategy and execution</li>
<li>
Creation and amplification of thought leadership and SME expertise through webinars, blogs, speaking opportunities and PR
</li>
