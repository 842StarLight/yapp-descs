<p><b>JOB SUMMARY:</b> This position is available with the Department of Revenue, Property Tax Oversight program, and is based out of the Orlando office, located downtown. The position is responsible for the appraisal of commercial real property in surrounding counties to determine the just or market value for assessment purposes. Duties include research, compile and analyze market, cost and income data in the development of estimates of value for commercial/industrial/multi-family properties; property inspections which take into consideration market value, location, and building or replacement costs; assessing tax according to prescribed tax tables and schedules; read, analyze, and reconstruct rent rolls, balance sheets, P&amp;L statements and other financial documents; research and analysis of property, and sales data, and submitting commercial property appraisal reports. Provide aid and assistance to County Property Appraisers, conduct and participate in interviews and reviews with the County Property Appraiser and staff. This position requires travel.</p>
