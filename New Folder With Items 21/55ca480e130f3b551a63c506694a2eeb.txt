<h2>Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Qeexo’s mission is to enable intelligence at the edge, utilizing machine learning to rapidly and economically equip devices with the intelligence needed to analyze and interpret the vast amount of data collected at the edge. Our company brings together a unique combination of expertise in machine learning, human computer interaction, and hardware integration to create revolutionary AI and ML solutions.</p>
<p>
Qeexo is looking for an excited and entrepreneurial Project Manager. This Project Manager will manage Qeexo product implementations and maximize customer success. In addition, the Project Manager will be involved in a variety of Business Operations, Product Management, and Business Development initiatives.</p>
<p>
This individual should be a highly motivated, passionate, super organized and should possess a unique blend of business and technical savvy. The ideal candidate will be able to learn quickly and be flexible, as this role requires tackling a number of projects in a variety of disciplines. In addition, you must be able to communicate with everyone from engineers to our executives.</p>
<p><b>
Your responsibilities will include:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Executing and managing Qeexo’s implementation projects at customers and partners ensuring customer success with Qeexo solutions</li>
<li>
Managing Qeexo’s engineering teams in the US and Asia during projects</li>
<li>
Interfacing between customers, partners, engineering and business teams</li>
<li>
Juggling multiple priorities and managing tight timelines.</li>
<li>
Executing other Sales, Marketing, PR, Business Development, Product and Operations initiatives</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Skills &amp; Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree (Computer Science or Engineering degree preferred)</li>
<li>
2+ years of work experience in mobile, hardware, SaaS, information technology, or other tech-related area (at least 1 year of experience in project management or working on customer or client implementations preferred)</li>
<li>
Demonstrated success in working with cross-functional teams</li>
<li>
Superb organizational skills and attention to detail</li>
<li>
Strong problem-solving skills</li>
<li>
Self-starter and versatile</li>
<li>
Excellent written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>
Ability to travel</li>
<li>
US work authorization</li>
<li>
Business-level written and verbal Chinese (a plus but not required)</li>
</ul>
