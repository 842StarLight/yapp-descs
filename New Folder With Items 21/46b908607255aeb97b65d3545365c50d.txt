<b>Job Summary:</b>
</div>
<div>
<div>Supermicro is currently seeking for an Associate Product Manager to promote Toshiba HDD/SSD products. Experience in marketing, promoting HDD/SSD, or related products is preferred. The primary responsibilities will be to collaborate with sales to identify, and secure business opportunities, negotiate, forecast, and execute procurement of Toshiba HDD and SSD. The Associate Product Manager must be able to work under high demand and fast paced environment. This position involves interactions with multi departments in meeting customer satisfaction and revenue growth. In addition, must build and maintain vendor relationship.</div>
</div>
</div>
<div>
<div>
<b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</b>
</div>
<div><ul>
<li>Collaborate with sales to identify and secure business opportunities</li>
<li>Responsible to increase business on Toshiba HDD and SSD</li>
<li>Able to do price negotiation, forecast, execute procurement and inventory management</li>
<li>Work with lab to get new HDD and SSD qualify in SMC platforms and perform new product release</li>
<li>Study supplier road map and new technology for future product development</li>
<li>Interface with system PM/Sales to promote Toshiba HDD and SSD products</li>
<li>Develop and enhance the relationship with suppliers</li>
<li>Able to work under high demand and fast paced environment</li>
<li>Support both Pre-sales and Post-sales as well as training to SMC PMs and Sales</li>
</ul></div>
