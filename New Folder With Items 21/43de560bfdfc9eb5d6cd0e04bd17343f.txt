<h2>Restaurant Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Job Description:</b></p>
<p>
Responsible for managing the overall operations of a Jack in the Box unit. Uses discretion in<br>
daily management decisions with accountability for ensuring effective execution of the Service<br>
Profit Chain (SPC) and Brand Promise. Develops team to provide excellent internal service,<br>
external service, and build restaurant sales and profit while ensuring compliance with policies,<br>
procedures, and regulatory requirements.</p>
<p><b>
Key Duties/Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p>
Internal Service:</p>
<p>
Recruits, selects, trains, develops, and evaluates restaurant employees. Monitors staffing levels<br>
to ensure sufficient development and talent; ensures systems for training employees on<br>
workstations are fully implemented and adhered to by management and crew; identifies and<br>
develops internal candidates for management and Team Leader positions. Works with<br>
restaurant management team to ensure all facets of "My Promise to You" and the Service Profit<br>
Chain are executed; creates a restaurant environment that is "employee friendly," fun, clean<br>
and safe; takes accountability for motivating and inspiring employees to achieve high<br>
performance; treats all employees with respect and dignity; and regularly recognizes and<br>
rewards employees. Understands and utilizes JIB systems, processes, and tools; and complies<br>
with all state and federal labor laws and regulations.</p>
<p><b>
External Service:</b></p>
<p>
Manages daily activities to achieve excellence in restaurant operational performance. Provides<br>
an exceptional experience for the guests by ensuring proper training and holding restaurant<br>
team accountable for consistently delivering excellent guest service and food quality in<br>
adherence with JIB systems, procedures, and food safety requirements. Reviews practices and<br>
modifies as needed to continuously improve the guest experience; maintains visibility and<br>
interaction with guests; responds to guest concerns and complaints in a timely and professional<br>
manner, and ensures positive resolution. Ensures management team and crew understands and<br>
operates all systems correctly. Maintains the brand image of restaurant cleanliness,<br>
maintenance, and excellent service. Serves as a role model for excellent guest service.</p>
<p>
Sales &amp; Profits:</p>
<p>
Utilizes management information tools to analyze restaurant operational and financial<br>
performance each Period, including the l&amp;E, quality and service reports, health inspections,<br>
HACCP, etc; identifies trends and implements action plans for improvement; uses data to<br>
analyze business results and consults with regional and CSC resources as needed. Focuses<br>
efforts on increasing restaurant sales and profitability by executing the Service Profit Chain and<br>
understanding its impact on the overall business. Considers cost/benefit impact of financial decisions and works to protect the JIB brand. Monitors costs and adherence to budget and<br>
restaurant goals.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Requirements:</b></p>
<p><b>
Selection Skills/Qualities:</b></p>
<p>
Fostering the Culture:</p>
<p>
Demonstrates a passion for the business and pride in Jack in the Box; ability to manage<br>
professionally with integrity, honesty, and trust that promotes the Jack in the Box culture and<br>
values and embraces the Service Profit Chain and "My Promise To You"; demonstrates high<br>
ethical standards; treats employees and guests with respect; and actively listens and<br>
communicates timely, clearly, and accurately with management team and crew. Remains calm<br>
when challenged or placed under pressure; calms others who are confronted with a difficult<br>
situation or task; and effectively manages conflict.</p>
<p>
Training/Coaching/Development:</p>
<p>
Serves as a strong role model who motivates and inspires employees; effectively trains,<br>
coaches, and provides time for employees to learn; identifies employees' potential and fosters<br>
development for promotion to the next level; and demonstrates patience and commitment<br>
toward development of employees. Values effective job performance and ensures restaurant<br>
team receives recognition and expression of gratitude. Understands the importance of, and<br>
provides employees with quality and timely performance feedback and reviews.</p>
<p>
Guest Focus:</p>
<p>
Is passionate about providing a high quality guest experience that is evident to our guests.<br>
Understands guests' perspectives and focuses efforts on ensuring consistent, quality service<br>
that exceeds guests' expectations. Demonstrates guest service techniques and ability to<br>
manage in a fast-paced environment.</p>
<p>
Food Quality/Safety:</p>
<p>
Demonstrates a strong awareness and concern for food quality and safety, and restaurant<br>
cleanliness; and is dedicated to consistently serving great food to guests and conveys<br>
importance to restaurant team. Demonstrated ability to utilize systems and perform duties<br>
within established structure.</p>
<p>
Business Management:</p>
<p>
Is willing and able to adjust to multiple demands, shifting priorities, ambiguity and change;<br>
understands the importance of change and implements, manages, and supports change<br>
initiatives; maintains a strong sense of urgency; and works toward achieving goals. Consistently<br>
makes high quality decisions based on experience, policy and procedure, or knowledge of the<br>
setting; and exercises discretion and independent judgment on important restaurant business matters. Attends to priorities, delegates work, and systematically conducts follow up;<br>
demonstrates attention to detail; and is well organized in all aspects of job performance.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Qualifications:</b></p>
<p>
Education - Internal Promote: High School Diploma required; working toward AA or BA/BS<br>
degree preferred. External Recruit: AA degree acceptable; BA/BS in Business or related field<br>
preferred.</p>
<p>
Experience - Internal Promote: Minimum of 1+ years experience as an Associate Manager<br>
and/or 2 years experience as a First Assistant Manager; must be 100% certified in all<br>
workstations. External Recruit: Minimum of 3 years experience managing a service concept<br>
with full P&amp;L responsibility.</p>
<p>
Knowledge/Skills/Abilities - Must be at least 18 years old; must complete Restaurant Manager<br>
training classes; must be ServSafe certified. Requires ability to speak, read, and write effectively<br>
in English; excellent interpersonal skills; ability to perform and understand basic math concepts<br>
(addition, subtraction, multiplication, division); proven analytical skills; and good organization<br>
and planning skills. Is a self-starter who takes initiative and willingly accepts responsibility.<br>
Proficient knowledge of personal computers and related software applications. Must possess a<br>
valid driver's license, insurance, and use personal vehicle to make bank deposits and travel to<br>
other restaurants/business locations as required. Demonstrates integrity and ethical behavior.</p>
<p>
Physical Requirements - Ability to stand and walk approximately 85%-95% of shift; ability to lift<br>
and carry 10-65 lbs; ability to move freely throughout the restaurant; ability to operate<br>
restaurant equipment and drive a motor vehicle.</p>
