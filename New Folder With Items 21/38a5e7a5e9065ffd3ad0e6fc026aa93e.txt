<h2>eCommerce Channel Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Position Summary</b><b>: </b></p>
<p>Responsible for the maintenance of all aspects of eCommerce (third party channels and owned websites); optimization of product presentation, and improvement of websites’ performance<b>.</b></p>
<p><b>Job </b><b>Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Audit and optimize product listings across all eCommerce channels</li>
<li>Collaborate with Product Managers to optimize product photos, marketing copy, etc.</li>
<li>Maintain data integrity across all eCommerce channels</li>
<li>Provide direction to the Creative department to establish marketing graphics</li>
<li>Perform keyword discovery and competitor analysis to aid paid search strategy</li>
<li>Optimize Amazon third-party listing through improving customer service, maintaining product listings, providing competitive pricing, and creating appropriate product advertisements</li>
<li>Optimize on-site search tools like meta tags, page titles, alternate image titles and tags</li>
<li>Utilize existing and new content to build bank of Amazon A+ Content</li>
<li>Maintain and improve positive customer relations through review management</li>
<li>Investigate/evaluate feasibility of new content channels, programs and platforms</li>
<li>Create reports to assist in the analysis of sales data</li>
</ul>

<li>3+ years’ experience with web analytics tools such as Google Analytics</li>
<li>3+ years’ experience with search engine optimization tools such as Conductor, Moz, Screaming Frog, DeepCrawl, Ahrefs, SimilarWeb, etc.</li>
