<b>
Description</b><div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
JOB SUMMARY</b></div>
<div>Manage the sale of products and/or services across domestic and international regions ensuring attainment of revenue objectives and maximum exposure to new business prospects.</div>
<div>
<br>
<b>PRIMARY RESPONSIBILITIES</b>
</div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Manage and coordinate the activities of staff including training, employee development, performance evaluation, hiring decisions, and the establishment of goals and performance objectives for assigned department</li>
<li><div>Provide continual evaluation of department processes, methods, and activities to assure the most effective use of resources and equipment.</div></li>
<li><div>Manage budget and expenditures.</div></li>
<li><div>Manage coordination of customer orders, track market and customer trends, and build and maintain customer relationships.</div></li>
<li><div>Develop, implement, and coordinate the development of sales objectives, strategies, advertising, and promotional programs and ensure their execution to accommodate corporate goals.</div></li>
<li><div>Direct sales forecasting activities and set performance goals accordingly.</div></li>
<li><div>Review market analyses to determine customer needs, price schedules, and discount rates.</div></li>
<li><div>Develop and implement customer requested proposals and quotations.</div></li>
<li><div>Identify and pursue growth opportunities for each region and/or market.</div></li>
<li><div>Meet with key clients, assisting sales representative with maintaining relationships and negotiating and closing deals.</div></li>
<li><div>Advise dealers, distributors, and clients concerning sales and advertising techniques.</div></li>
<li><div>Responsible for periodic sales report showing sales volume, potential sales, and areas of proposed client base expansion.</div></li>
<li><div>Deliver sales presentations to key clients in coordination with sales representatives.</div></li>
<li><div>Analyze and control expenditures of division to conform to budgetary requirements.</div></li>
<li><div>Perform other work related tasks as assigned.</div></li>
<li>Comply with all NOV Company and HSE policies and procedures.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
<b>FACILITY/GROUP SPECIFIC RESPONSIBILITIES</b>
</div>
<ul><li>Manage the sale of NOV ICT products and/or services across West Texas region ensuring attainment of revenue objectives and maximum exposure to new business prospects.</li></ul>
