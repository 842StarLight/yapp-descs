<p><b>
Job Purpose:</b></p>
<p>
Under the general guidance of the Front Office Manager and/or Front Office Supervisor, perform all duties connected with arriving and in-house guests, ensure that all our regular and VIP guests receive high quality, personalized service, an Engaging, Dynamic Guest Experience and maintain our guest recognition program.</p>
<p><b>
Duties &amp; Functions:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Actively welcome, greet and check guests in</li>
<li>Inform guests with savvy knowledge of hotel, its services, the city, and local ‘happenings’</li>
<li>Ensure all requests are dealt with accurately and they receive the appropriate service, attention and follow up</li>
<li>Differentiate between guest types and handle them in the appropriate manner; guests with confirmed or claimed reservation, walk ins, VIP guests, groups, etc.</li>
<li>Follow up on all arrivals using the prescribed procedure, modification of registration cards, special requests, rate changes, room changes and account inquiries, reservation inquiries</li>
<li>Deal with all guest requests, accidents and/or thefts promptly, no later than within a 20 minutes response time, record all matters in Opera or hotel specific recording process</li>
<li>Update and maintain the reception hand over book, pass on all guest feedback to the Manager on duty so appropriate action may be taken</li>
<li>Ensure guest privacy and security, any confidential guest information is not disclosed and processes are aligned with sbe confidentiality standards</li>
<li>Ensure the correct procedure and policy standards are adhered to</li>
<li>Ensure all necessary supplies are available for the front desk, manage par stock and order supplies</li>
<li>Comply with hotel credit policy as it relates to cash payments; credit card payments; account to company; voucher payment; third party payment, international currency, etc.</li>
<li>Encourage up-selling in order to maximize rates</li>
<li>Ensure work areas are cleaned and maintained at all times</li>
</ul>

<p><b>
SPECIFIC JOB KNOWLEDGE, SKILL AND ABILITY</b></p>
<p>
The individual must possess the following knowledge, skills and abilities and be able to explain and demonstrate that he or she can perform the essential functions of the job, with or without reasonable accommodation.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree preferred. High School Diploma or equivalent required</li>
<li>One (1) to two (2) years in a public contact position, preferably in an upscale or lifestyle brand hotel</li>
<li>An intermediate to proficient understanding of Computer systems such as: Opera, GoConcierge, Nuvolo, Microsoft Word, Excel &amp; Outlook is preferred</li>
<li>Enter and locate work related information using computers and/or point of sale systems</li>
<li>Ability to spend extended lengths of time viewing a computer screen</li>
<li>Possess a gracious, friendly, and fun demeanor</li>
<li>Ability to multitask, work in a fast-paced environment and have a high level attention to detail</li>
<li>Maintain positive and productive working relationships with other employees and departments</li>
<li>Ability to work independently and to partner with others to promote an environment of teamwork</li>
<li>Must be able to stand or walk a minimum eight-hour shift.</li>
<li>Must be able observant and quick to respond to various situations while also multitasking and handling stressful situations.</li>
<li>Must be able to twist, tow (push or pull), reach, bend climb and carry objects as necessary.</li>
<li>Must have excellent communication skills and be able to read, write, speak and understand English.</li>
