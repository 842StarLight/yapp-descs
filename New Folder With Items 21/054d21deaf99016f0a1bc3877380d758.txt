<h2>Paid Social Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">BigCommerce is disrupting the e-commerce industry as the SaaS leader for fast- growing, mid-market businesses. We enable our customers to build intuitive and engaging stores to support every stage of their growth.
<br><br>
BigCommerce, named a "Best Place to Work" in Austin 2019, is looking for a Paid Social Marketing Manager. At BigCommerce, you'll have the opportunity to positively influence the trajectory of a billion dollar company in a trillion dollar industry. You must have a proven track record of consistently exceeding goals driving MRR and revenue growth through competitive and high-performing Paid Social efforts. This role will allow you to work across the entire marketing group; including the Digital, Product, Automation, Creative and Content teams.
<br><br>
<b>What You'll Do:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Drive, build and grow our paid social program with a relentless focus on creating a targeted, high-value, revenue generation funnel.</li>
<li>Own the strategy, launch, administration, optimization and budget for all paid social media efforts.</li>
<li>Lead day-to-day operation and management of all paid social efforts.</li>
<li>Partner with organic social media and digital content leaders to develop campaigns, offers, audiences, and funnels that resonate with our targeted personas.</li>
<li>Support buildout and implementation of all campaign related social media tactics for BUs, Partner Marketing and international regions</li>
<li>Build strong and relevant audiences within social platforms and leverage them to drive growth.</li>
<li>Gather and analyze data to make informed decisions for bidding strategy, creative testing, and landing page performance.</li>
<li>KPI's - focus on improving metrics that move the needle toward specific goals.</li>
<li>Manage the reporting and communication of results within the marketing and product teams and to relevant campaign stakeholders.</li>
<li>Test, test and then test some more. Ongoing audience, geographical, behavioral and creative tests will be a priority and a part of a consistent and structured testing schedule.</li>
<li>Forward thinking and experimenting with the latest algorithm changes and beta-testing opportunities</li>
<li>Ensure accomplishment of goals, this includes lead volumes, revenue, margins, and related performance metrics.</li>
<li>Strategize cross-functionally with marketing, sales, design, and product for maximum impact.</li>
</ul>
<b>Who You Are:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Undergraduate degree in marketing, business, math, finance, communications, or a related field</li>
<li>5+ highly successful years direct experience in paid social marketing, ideally with 2+ years experience in demand generation for software vertical</li>
<li>Technically Experienced</li>
<li>Tracking (eg. Pixel configuration &amp; implementation)</li>
<li>Audience Building &amp; Data Mining (eg. Facebook, Adwords, Linkedin, Twitter)</li>
<li>Project Management (JIRA)</li>
<li>Analytics (eg. Google Analytics, Segment, MixPanel)</li>
<li>Data APIs (eg. Clearbit, Datanyze)</li>
<li>CMS (eg. WordPress, Craft)</li>
<li>CRM (eg. Salesforce, Marketo)</li>
<li>HTML, CSS, JS knowledge preferred</li>
<li>Strong Process and Project Manager - You can readily design practices, processes, and prioritizations, implement multiple campaigns simultaneously, and report on results.</li>
<li>Strategist - You understand how paid social can be used to drive growth across different buyer segments and different parts of the customer journey from acquisition to revenue.</li>
<li>Customer Focused - You want to deliver the best experience possible for future and current customers, as helping our customers' businesses grow is the #1 goal at BigCommerce.</li>
<li>Problem Solver - You can see hidden problems and probe a variety of sources for answers, able to look beyond the obvious, and can identify and creatively solve complex problems, including matters related to systems and processes.</li>
<li>Systems Thinker - You are dedicated to providing high-quality services, committed to continuous improvement through empowerment and management by data, willing and able to re-engineer processes from scratch, open to suggestions and experimentation, and efficient and effective work processes.</li>
<li>Communicator - Excellent written/verbal communication and customer relationship skills are critical both working with people internally and externally.</li>
<li>Collaborative - You have demonstrated success working cross-functionally in larger organizations, and have the ability to forge strong bonds and work collaboratively with key external and internal partners.</li>
<li>Composed - You know how to execute consistently in a fast-paced, high pressure, dynamic environment and carry out self-directed responsibilities.</li>
<li>Personable - You are seen as positive, energetic, and exude a strong sense of teamwork by working effectively, respectfully, and efficiently with all team members.</li>
<li>Technical Learner - You pick up on technical things quickly, enjoy learning new skills and knowledge, and are adept at staying ahead of the curve on relevant industry, company, product, or technical knowledge.</li>
<li>Ethical - You believe in our mission, share our values and understand that it's not just about what you get done, but also how you do it.</li>
</ul>
