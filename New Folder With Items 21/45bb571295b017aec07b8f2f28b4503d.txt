<p><b>As a Settlement/Escrow Closer you will:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to communicate via phone and email with all parties involved in closing transactions which is not limited to borrowers, sellers, lenders and real estate agents.
</li>
<li>Prepare and determine the escrow requirements for the transaction.</li>
<li>Prepare necessary closing documentation to complete the sales transaction.</li>
<li>Clear all title requirements needed for transfer of title.
</li>
<li>Deposit and disburse the funds from closings.</li>
<li>Construction Disbursement
</li>
<li>Business development and company promotion by attending civic and Chamber events
</li>
</ul>
