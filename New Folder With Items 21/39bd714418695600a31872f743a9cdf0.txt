<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>The person in the position performs service activities at job sites and customer locations for customer and/or sales and rental fleet equipment.<br>
</div>
<ul>
<li>
Perform the following service operations on AGCO, Caterpillar and allied equipment with occasional supervision:</li>
<li>
Analyze and diagnose equipment malfunctions.</li>
<li>
Repair, replace or rebuild engines, transmissions, fuel systems, final drives, steering clutches, cross-shafts, hydraulic components including pumps, motors, valves, and all related components, electrical and air systems.</li>
<li>
Write service reports that accurately document diagnosis and repairs performed.</li>
<li>
Prepare required documentation including work orders, parts lists, and time cards in an accurate and timely manner to enable other employees to do their job in a timely manner.</li>
<li>
Ensure assigned company vehicle maintains a clean professional appearance and is in proper working order.</li>
<li>
Perform assigned tasks in a timely, accurate, and efficient manner.</li>
<li>
Follow manufacturer and company service guidelines and procedures to ensure quality work and to preserve and document component failure information for accurate failure analysis.</li>
<li>
Project a positive professional image with customers.</li>
<li>
Develop and maintain a strong customer service relationship with all customer contacts.</li>
<li>
Work overtime as needed within labor law guidelines.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Associate's degree in a related field and six (6) years heavy equipment service experience or equivalent combination of education and experience. Maintain an acceptable safe driving record.</li>
<li>
Hold a current Commercial Driver’s License or be able to obtain one shortly after hire</li>
<li>
Lift truck operator certificate</li>
<li>
Maintain a tool inventory sufficient to perform assigned tasks</li>
<li>
Must have excellent proficiency on menu driven main-frame computer systems and with AGCO and CAT PC and windows driven software</li>
</ul>
