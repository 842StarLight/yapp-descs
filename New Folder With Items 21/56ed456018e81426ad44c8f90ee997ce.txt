<b>About Us:</b><br>
Want to join a company on the cutting edge of technology and travel? Want to be part of a fantastic and fun company that’s revolutionizing the online travel advertising space?
<br><br>
Built on a decade of expertise analyzing the complete traveler path to purchase, Sojern drives travelers from dream to destination. The company delivered $13B in bookings for its clients to date by activating multi-channel branding and performance solutions on the Sojern Traveler Platform.

Recognized on the Top Company Cultures list by Entrepreneur Magazine, Sojern is headquartered in San Francisco, with teams based in Dubai, Dublin, Hong Kong, London, Mexico City, New York, Omaha, Paris, Singapore, Sydney, and Istanbul.
<br><br>
<b>The Role:</b><br>
You are a driven, self-motivated, and hungry to learn individual, who is looking to build a career in digital advertising. As part of the entry-level Business Sales Development Program, you will have the opportunity to work with small and mid-sized travel businesses that may benefit from Sojern’s media products and solutions in the fast-paced, online, travel-advertising environment. You will play a critical role within the company as a talented hunter that excels at researching, qualifying and prospecting leads. This role is intended to prepare you to grow and succeed in our Inside Sales environment.
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Identify and investigate leads, qualifying them using online research to establish prospects and corresponding contact info.</li>
<li>Engage qualified and interested leads through outbound multimedia communication to book appointments and drive sales presentations for the Sales Executives.</li>
<li>Meet monthly goals by excelling on the phone…in both activity and presence.</li>
<li>Perform operational marketing tasks including; performing prospective email campaign, creative client gifting solutions, lead nurturing activities and managing a full schedule of follow up tasks while leveraging Sojern technology.</li>
<li>Build an organizational foundation for the sales process.</li>
</ul>

<li>Bachelor’s degree in Business or related field.</li>

<li>Excellent written and verbal/phone communication skills.</li>
<li>Strong resolve and unwavering persistence.</li>
<li>High energy partnered with a positive attitude.</li>
<li>Entrepreneurial spirited with a forward-thinking mindset.</li>
<li>Ability to multitask, prioritize and manage time effectively.</li>
<li>Desire to grow, learn and advance within the Inside Sales team.</li>
</ul>
