<h2>Software Sales B2B Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>*<br><b>B2B Independent Sales Rep for an Emerging Startup!</b></p>
<p>IntellyDoc is a SaaS startup focused on contract and workflow automation for mid-size and enterprise companies in various key industries. We are looking for a highly motivated sales representative to join our budding startup team, and here's who we’re looking for to fill this role:</p>
<p>A hunt &amp; kill mentality - you are eager to talk to cold and warm leads and know how to close deals.</p>
<p>A desire for uncapped commissions and a personal drive to always exceeding your goals.</p>
<p>A reliable and strong work ethic: you show up on time, high degree of integrity and honesty, focused, , beat your numbers while having fun.</p>
<p>A can-do attitude: you like process, but embrace a fast moving and agile startup environment.</p>
<p><b>WHAT YOU’LL BE DOING: </b></p>
<p>-Outbound calling &amp; email prospecting to identify, qualify, and close new business opportunities - from end-to-end.</p>
<p>- Create and prioritize target prospect lists.</p>
<p>-Utilize a consultative selling approach to provide value for our customers.</p>
<p>-Perform online product demonstrations to prospects.</p>
<p>-Update CRM system daily with all sales activity. (Call, Demos, Meetings)</p>
<p>- Testing different outreach methods (email subject lines, value propositions, etc) to find the best ones that work for you</p>
<p>-Accurately report activity and opportunity on a weekly basis to team leader.</p>
<p>-Be accountable for a monthly quota of sales.</p>
<p><b>WHAT EXCITES US! </b></p>
<p>- 3+ years of B2B software sales quota-carrying experience and a strong track record of closing deals with customers with between 100 - 1K+ employees.</p>
<p>- Strong prospecting, qualifying, and negotiating skills; consultative sales approach with a product-centric mindset.</p>
<p>- Experience working with sales development and deploying a vertical focused revenue strategy.</p>
<p>- Interest or experience in workflow, automation, document management and.or collaboration products and a desire to create strategic relationships that help our customers succeed.</p>
<p>- Excitement to work in a high-growth environment and to help build processes and tools as needed.</p>
<p>- Desire to get (more) technical and the ability to learn the ins and outs of IntellyDoc’s product roadmap.</p>
<p><b>DESIRED SKILLS</b></p>
<p>- Has grit and mentally tough - leaving the excuses at home!</p>
<p>-High energy and positive attitude.</p>
<p>-Passionate about winning; focused on energizing client relationships</p>
<p>-Ability to multi-task, prioritize and manage time effectively</p>
<p>-Highly motivated, self-starter who is able to work well independently</p>
