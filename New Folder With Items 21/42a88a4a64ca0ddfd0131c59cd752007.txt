<div><b>
Position Summary</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Reporting to the Vice President of Global Client Onboarding, the role of the Sr. Director, GCO Technology is responsible for the execution of the strategy of the Global Client Onboarding organization. This position will have the responsibility of managing all key projects assigned by the VP, Global Client Onboarding in support of these goals. The candidate for this position will be responsible for planning, resourcing and tracking the completion of all major initiatives. This position will be responsible for managing key client escalations and the process design for all new products and services offered by Rimini Street.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Essential Duties &amp; Responsibilities</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Drive projects in support of GCO Strategy</li>
<li>
Be able to deliver articulate and compelling data to key clients in support of the Rimini Street organization</li>
<li>
Manage key client escalation issues liaising with Service Delivery and Global Client Engagement team</li>
<li>
Collaborate with team members in a virtual environment to manage client issues. This will require the flexibility to work across multiple time zones including evenings, weekend and holidays</li>
<li>
Manage the implementation into Global Client Onboarding for new products and services being offered by Rimini Street</li>
<li>
Determine how new technologies can be leveraged to drive efficiency and excellence with the Global Client Onboarding team</li>
</ul>

<div><div><div>
<p>Rimini Street, Inc. (Nasdaq: RMNI) is a global provider of enterprise software products and services, and the leading third-party support provider for Oracle and SAP software products. The Company has redefined enterprise software support services since 2005 with an innovative, award-winning program that enables licensees of IBM, Microsoft, Oracle, Salesforce, SAP and other enterprise software vendors to save up to 90 percent on total maintenance costs. Clients can remain on their current software release without any required upgrades for a minimum of 15 years. Over 1,700 global Fortune 500, midmarket, public sector and other organizations from a broad range of industries currently rely on Rimini Street as their trusted, third-party support provider.</p>

<br>
<p>
The company is widely recognized by clients, leading industry analysts (including Gartner, Forrester, Constellation Research and Nucleus), and financial analysts as the leader in a large, fast growing market with tremendous upside and an extremely strong value proposition that resonates with CIOs and CFOs. With tight IT budgets and flat global economic growth, IT and procurement professionals are actively looking for proven methods to cut costs while improving service. Rimini Street enables organizations to free up budget for strategic business initiatives by delivering savings of up to 90 percent on total maintenance and support costs, and to date, the company has saved organizations over $3 billion in maintenance costs.</p>
