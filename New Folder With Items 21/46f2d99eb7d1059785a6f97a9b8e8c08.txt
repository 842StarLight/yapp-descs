<h2>Solutions Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Solutions Assistants at Psychology Software Tools are the first people that new customers interact with when working with PST. On a daily basis, you will be responsible for the entire sales life cycle for our customers. This includes: responding to customer requests and questions regarding existing PST products and services, finding creative solutions to allow our customers (primarily neuroscientists and psychologists) to use our products for their research, preparing quotes, processing orders, and working with our global team of distributors and resellers to do the same for their customers. Solutions Assistants must possess a positive, outgoing, and friendly personality and must be comfortable and excel working as part of a small team (3-4 people).</p>
<p><b>Primary Duties</b>:</p>
<p>· Organize personal daily schedule to effectively manage customer meetings, department meetings, and daily tasks.</p>
<p>· Communicate effectively with customers via Zendesk, phone, tele/videoconference, and in-person site visits.</p>
<p>· Follow-up on customer inquiries and leads in a timely, professional, and knowledgeable manner.</p>
<p>· Work closely with Solutions Team and Solutions Manager to prioritize tasks to ensure reaching quarterly sales goals.</p>
<p>· Provide product information and sales assistance to global network of partners, resellers, and distributors.</p>
<p>· Quote and track customer interactions and sales cycle via Zendesk Sell / PandaDoc CRM platforms.</p>
<p>· Estimate shipping quotations and track packages using UPS Worldship</p>
<p>· Process orders via GP Dynamics</p>
<p><b>Secondary Duties: </b></p>
<p>· Attend industry conferences in a sales capacity.</p>
<p>· Assist users with returns, lost, or damaged merchandise.</p>
<p>· Assist in completing necessary paperwork for conference registration by the appropriate date.</p>
<p>· Assist in making necessary updates to marketing materials (website, product sheets, social media).</p>
<p>· Any and all other duties/tasks which may arise in the short term that are deemed critical or beneficial to the company, especially as they relate to the success of assigned project(s).</p>
<p><b>Requirements: </b></p>
<p>· Bachelor’s degree or equivalent and 0-3 years of experience in the field or in a related area.</p>
<p>· Must be able to gain knowledge and become proficient in the applied use of company products (software and hardware) and to explain them to a potential customer. Must possess a professional and personable manner.</p>
<p>· Must possess excellent telephone, email, and conversation etiquette.</p>
<p>· Must exhibit professionalism, courtesy, and salesmanship when dealing with customers.</p>
<p>· Must be highly organized and able to prioritize effectively.</p>
<p>· Must maintain a positive attitude, follow instructions and pay attention to detail.</p>
<p>· Must be a quick study, flexible and willing to take on new and different tasks.</p>
<p>· Must be proficient with Word, Excel, Outlook and the Internet and other desktop applications as assigned.</p>
<p>· Ability to communicate effectively with acting supervisor.</p>
<p>· Ability to lift 35 lbs.</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul><li>relevant: 1 year (Preferred)</li></ul>
<p>Education:</p>
<ul><li>High school or equivalent (Preferred)</li></ul>
