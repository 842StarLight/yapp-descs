<h2>Product Line Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>PRODUCT LINE MANAGER</b></p>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL PURPOSE OF THIS POSITION: </b>The Product Line Manager is the product champion, product expert, and market expert. The Product Line Manager is responsible for all aspects of the product lines including P &amp; L responsibility, financial analysis, performance to forecast, performance to plan, sales team support, selling &amp; pricing strategy execution, product promotions, new supplier identification, and alternate product identification. The PLM leads the product category team which supports development and execution of programs that improve product line sales and profitability performance.</p>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop and execute a comprehensive business plan for the market segment for in-house fabricated and outside purchased products. As part of the strategy, develop the core positioning and messaging for each assigned market segment and product category.</li>
<li>Coordinates costing functions and recommends best manufacturing practices for in-house fabricated products.</li>
<li>Recommends capital equipment investments based on product caps or required expansions.</li>
<li>Analyze competitiveness of each product offering and product trends in the marketplace. Use this analysis to develop strategies for assigned products.</li>
<li>Conduct market research and customer interviews to identify technology and innovation trends in the assigned market segments. Monitor market segment trends and identify opportunities for new products and services. Understand customer needs in the market by visiting customers.</li>
<li>Perform market segmentation analysis for each assigned segment: identify market size, prepare and maintain the competitive landscape.</li>
<li>Develop go-to-market strategies for new products. Plan the launch of new products, develop product literature/training materials as needed and manage the cross-functional implementation of the product launch plan.</li>
<li>In collaboration with inside sales, outside sales, and pricing team, develop pricing strategy to balance profit margins with customer satisfaction and meet revenue goals.</li>
<li>Coordinate all elements of marketing communications for assigned market segments and product categories. Collaborate with the Marketing Specialist and other Product Line Managers to execute a well-coordinated plan.</li>
<li>Coordinate development and deployment of sales training materials to support new product introductions and existing product lines, including value-selling material.</li>
<li>Report and Analyze key product line metrics including sales growth, gross margin, fill rate, inventory turns, new item performance, supplier operational performance, performance to forecast, and performance to plan etc.</li>
<li>Works closely with the inside sales, outside sales, &amp; product line manager on product line selling strategies, discounting guidelines, training initiatives, field sales support from suppliers and more.</li>
<li>Perform other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE REQUIREMENTS:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's Degree in Engineering, Management, Marketing or equivalent</li>
<li>5+ years of experience managing industrial product lines required</li>
<li>Prior Industrial distribution experience preferred.</li>
<li>Prior experience with manufacturing specifications, including product expansion and capital equipment investment preferred</li>
<li>Experience in successfully supporting sales teams with business insights, sales tools, product specification/selection materials, training, and product presentations preferred</li>
<li>Experience in creating and executing successful B2B product marketing campaigns to the industrial, energy, and commercial market segments via print and digital media preferred</li>
<li>Prior experience managing direct reports preferred</li>
<li>Electrical market knowledge preferred.</li>
</ul>

<li>Ability to effectively present information to top management, customers, and vendors.</li>

<li>Ability to define problems, collect data, establish facts, and draw valid conclusions.</li>
<li>Ability to effectively and efficiently manage and keep projects on-track.</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal and negotiation skills.</li>
<li>Ability to build and maintain relationships.</li>
