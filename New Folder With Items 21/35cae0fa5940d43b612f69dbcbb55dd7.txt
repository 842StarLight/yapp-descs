<h2>Account Manager - Property & Casualty</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div><div>Responsible for managing the internal service process and relationships for assigned property and casualty client companies on our Commercial Risk Unit.</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>GENERAL INFORMATION</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
This position is a salaried, exempt and full-time position.</li>
<li>
This position is located in our San Mateo office.</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>ESSENTIAL TASKS, DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES include the following steps to provide simplicity and service to our clients. Others may be assigned.</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Independently manage client relationships and accounts up to $1MM in volume revenue.</li>
<li>
Act as first and primary client point of contact to provide day-to-day service by responding to client questions and requests.</li>
<li>
Develop, analyze, and implement competitive insurance programs, providing various alternatives for client consideration. Prepare market, quote rates for renewals and new business.</li>
<li>
Manage the onboarding process of new clients.</li>
<li>
Manage the marketing process for prospect companies.</li>
<li>
Prepare and review client communications and finalize for delivery.</li>
<li>
Communicate and collaborate with producers, clients and underwriters to obtain information, quote rates, prepare proposals, or service existing accounts.</li>
<li>
Maintain current files and prepare policy renewal questionnaires, recommending coverage enhancements. Review, evaluate, rate and market Commercial Insurance applications and documents for accuracy.</li>
<li>
Compile and organize all client information necessary to process new, renewal, cancellation and endorsements.</li>
<li>
Perform thorough contract reviews for clients.</li>
<li>
Perform database file management and input information. Accurately manage and maintain documents in agency management systems.</li>
<li>
Provide training to Account Coordinators.</li>
<li>
May manage Account Coordinators and Account Assistants.</li>
<li>
Perform other duties and special projects as assigned.</li>
<li><b>
To perform this position successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform these essential functions.</b></li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS AND ABILITIES that demonstrate professionalism, passion and ways to use technology for good.</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Excellent communication skills: written, public speaking and presentation preparation.</li>
<li>
Excellent telephone etiquette and customer service skills, professionalism and tact.</li>
<li>
Advanced knowledge of Microsoft Office and its more complex features.</li>
<li>
Ability to be resourceful, take initiative, solve problems and offer solutions.</li>
<li>
Works well with others in a fast paced environment and be responsive to co-workers and colleagues. Ability to train, delegate, provide oversight and give feedback.</li>
<li>
Adaptability and flexibility to address issues and client needs.</li>
<li>
Excellent time management skills.</li>
<li>
Ability to learn and adopt and train use of technology systems and software applications.</li>
<li>
Excellent at multi-tasking and flexibility to manage ever changing priorities and deadlines.</li>
<li>
Ability to review internal/external deliverables to ensure accuracy.</li>
<li>
Intermediate project planning and management.</li>
<li>
Ability to forge relationships and build trust.</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Minimum of 3 to 5 years of Property and Casualty insurance experience in a brokerage required.</li>
<li>
High School Diploma required. Bachelor's degree preferred.</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>CERTIFICATES, LICENSES, REGISTRATION</b></h3>
<div><ul><li>
CA Property and Casualty Insurance license within 3 months of hire.</li></ul></div>
</div></div>
