<p>Business Analysis Department Manager</p>
<p>
Kamis is searching for a candidate to run the BA department for a growing software company in the Frederick, MD area. This is a full-time, onsite position in Frederick. Salary is in the market range, based on experience.</p>
<p>
The Manager will manage the growing department of Business Analysts, UX Architects and QA/Testers and also work independently on projects. The main deliverable is a set of documents written by the UXA or BA to illustrate the solution to the client and serve as the blueprint for what to build by the development team.</p>
<p>
The candidate should be well versed in managing team members of all levels in a growing department that strives for scalability and efficiency. The candidate should be an expert in creating documentation/deliverables for custom software applications in an agile environment for use by developers and client approvals. Experience transitioning from waterfall to agile processes is a plus.</p>
<p>
Candidates should have experience with most of the following tools or their equivalents:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Prototyping/Wireframing tools such as Moqups, Balsamiq, Adobe XD, InVision, or Figma</li>
<li>
Diagraming tools like Draw.io, Visio, or Whimisical</li>
<li>
Google Suite for day to day work (Gmail, Calendar, Google Docs/Sheets, etc.)</li>
<li>
Browser Stack, Redmine, or other testing devices for quality assurance and issue tracking</li>
<li>
Basecamp for client communication</li>
<li>
Other assorted tools for research and testing</li>
</ul>
