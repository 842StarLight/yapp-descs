<p><b>Revenue Analyst</b></p>
<p>
</p>
<p>Corestream (corestream.com) is seeking a Revenue Analyst that will play an important role in the Company's business growth by helping maximize revenue and bringing to attention any issues regarding accounting, business procedures or finances. This person will serve as a point of contact within the company and relay information to the executive team. Revenue analysts regularly review a company's financial transactions, sales contracts and business practices. In addition to providing guidance for improvement, they identify revenue control risk and may develop and implement revenue control plans. They also monitor and evaluate the effectiveness of their revenue control plans. If this sounds like you, read on…</p>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Location</b></p>
<p>
Corestream is headquartered in Tampa, FL</p>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Your Mission</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Perform monthly revenue analysis and reporting to management.</li>
<li>Determine monthly revenue forecast and review revenue figures with management.</li>
<li>Work in compliance with company financial policies and regulations.</li>
<li>Develop standard revenue recognition policies and suggest revenue generation activities.</li>
<li>Generate revenue reports and financial statements for management review.</li>
<li>Maintain documentations of all revenue related activities for reference purposes.</li>
<li>Develop new strategies to achieve revenue maximization.</li>
<li>Analyze and resolve issues related to revenue analysis.</li>
<li>Work with revenue team in developing and implementing revenue management initiatives.</li>
<li>Assist in performing monthly account reconciliations and General Ledger (GL) activities.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Characteristics</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ideally has strong analytic, communication, multi-tasking, and team work skills</li>
<li>Ability able to produce reports detailing revenue control plans and policies, and do so by analyzing financial statements, contracts, and business practices</li>
<li>Takes full ownership – issues we deal with may be complex, so this person must see the next move when resolving an issue</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>3 to 5 years of accounting, financial, analytic or auditing experience required</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree is required</li>
</ul>
