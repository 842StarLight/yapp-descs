<h2>Sr. Manager of Marketing Strategy</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>Marketing is one of the largest teams at SharkNinja. The team includes Digital, Brand, D2C, Media, PR and Creative. Each team focusing on how we give our consumers the ultimate product experience. Marketing is for the person that is not afraid to dive deep and show a genuine interest into the world of Consumer Goods. These are the people that are consistently asking themselves what can we do to we make things even easier for our consumers. Marketing at SharkNinja is for the person who is never satisfied with creating anything less than the best product on the market.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
As the Sr. Manager of Marketing Strategy and FEI (Front End Innovation) you will act as a Brand steward and product marketing strategist for the Shark brand; help to drive consistency in voice, tone and message across all channels (i.e. Packaging, Advertising, Infomercial, PR, Social/Content Marketing and Web Properties, collateral, trade etc.). Contribute effectively to the development of brand strategy, help shape the marketing plan development, and ensure excellence of execution in market.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<div><b>
Responsibilities:</b></div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>
Be the category expert: understand price, positioning, competitive landscape and market needs</li>
<li>
Work with category management and sales to provide a consistent pulse on the business</li>
<li>
Digest insights, connect the dots, summarize conclusions and recommend a comprehensive product assortment and roadmap that will drive a high rate of sale
</li>
<li>Drive front-end innovation by working with a cross-functional team to develop and bring to life product concepts
</li>
<li>Support product media plan development based on best practices and competitive insights
</li>
<li>Manage creative development process of relevant messaging and assets that deliver a clear consumer understanding of products and brand (i.e. timelines, evolution/iteration, leadership reviews)</li>
<li>
Leads insight plan needed to inform critical decision making</li>
<li>
Conduct post evaluation and analysis of activities, recommending changes for future
</li>
<li>Participate in brand strategy discussions</li>
</ul>
