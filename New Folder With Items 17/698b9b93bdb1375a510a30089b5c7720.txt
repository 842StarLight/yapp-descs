<p></p>
<p><b>PURPOSE/SCOPE:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>This is a key position in the Tennant Enterprise Technical Infrastructure Team that provides foundational management of critical components of the enterprise.
</li>
<li>The primary focus and area of support for this position will be around Implemention, Upgrade and Management of Backup Tools (CommVault), VMWare, HP-Ux, Suse Linux software
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>RESPONISIBILITIES
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Experience in configuring, managing backups using CommVault for Linux, Unix, Windows, Databases etc.,
</li>
<li>Experience in configuring, managing and administering VMWare Environments
</li>
<li>Experience in configuring, patching and managing HP-UX, SLES Systems
</li>
<li>Experience in Storage products like EMC, PureStorage for storage provisioning, expansion and maintenance
</li>
<li>Flexible to support Global teams for Project and support activities as needed
</li>
<li>Unix Scripting Knowledge with PGP Encryption will be needed
</li>
<li>Exposure to Job Scheduling tools will be an advantage
</li>
<li>Familiarity with Apache Administration will be beneficial
</li>
<li>Solves complex problems which may require detailed investigations and collaborating with other teams
</li>
<li>Must follow established company processes, including change management, incident management, and problem management processes
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Education and Experience
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science, IT or related areas
</li>
<li>Minimum 5 years of strong experience working with VMWare, HP-UX, Linux and Backup tools
</li>
<li>Familiarity with ITIL concepts
</li>
<li>Certification in Backup Tools, VMWare, Storage Concepts will be an advantage
</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to organize and accurately prioritize work, be detail-oriented, understand when urgency is required and use good judgment in varied situations
</li>
<li>Ability to concentrate on tasks
</li>
</ul>
