<b>
Brief Summary of Role</b></p>
<p>
Appirio works with companies to address key challenges through the implementation of Appirio application-based business solutions. This individual contributor position is solely responsible for Appirio business development in the Energy &amp; Utilities / Engineering &amp; Construction vertical (ENU), working in a team environment. The successful candidate will be responsible for building relationships with our partner, Salesforce, and internally with Wipro’s (our parent company) Strategic Business Unit (SBU) for ENU, to acquire, grow and nurture new business opportunities in the US.</p>

<li>
