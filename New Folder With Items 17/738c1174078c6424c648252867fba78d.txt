<h2>Senior Consultant – Process Safety (PSM) (Mid-Senior Level)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>ERM </b>is looking for a motivated, hands-on <b>Senior Consultant – </b><b>Process Safety (PSM) </b>to join our growing client-focused team in <b>San Francisco or Walnut Creek, CA </b>. Working under the direction of and in collaboration with project managers and partners, the successful candidate will work on a variety of process safety compliance and risk management projects and client activities. Projects are primarily in the Gulf Coast area, though may extend throughout the U.S. This is a growth position with increasing responsibility for task/project management duties and sales activities.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>RESPONSIBILITIES:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage projects related to OSHA process safety management (PSM), EPA risk management plan (RMP), California accidental release program, BSEE Safety and Environmental Management Systems (SEMS), process hazard analysis (PHA) programs and general process safety risk management.
</li>
<li>Perform and manage multiple projects within scope/budget/schedule expectations and ensure quality standards on project deliverables.
</li>
<li>Develop process safety and risk management programs and procedures.
</li>
<li>Conduct PSM, RMP, CalARP, and SEMS compliance audits.
</li>
<li>Facilitate Process Hazard Analyses (PHA). Participate in safety workshops (i.e., HAZIDS, HAZOPS), including workshop preparation, scribe, and client reporting.
</li>
<li>Conduct Mechanical Integrity (MI) audits and gap assessments.
</li>
<li>Develop key performance indicators for client programs.
</li>
<li>Support, as needed, environmental, health and safety (EHS) compliance, environmental management system, sustainability, due diligence transaction, contingency plans, and multi-media permits.
</li>
<li>Interact with other ERM business units and technical communities to build strong collaborative relationships.
</li>
<li>Prepare technical proposals and participate in business development activities with existing and new clients to expand client relationships that help generate repeat business and grow Process Safety services.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>REQUIREMENTS:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>BS in engineering or process safety related field.
</li>
<li>7+ years of experience in process safety engineering and/or management, preferably as a consultant in this field.
</li>
<li>Experience with development of written PSM programs, Process Hazard Analysis (PHA), PSM/RMP/CalARP auditing, and SEMS (preferred).
</li>
<li>Solid understanding of OSHA PSM and EPA RMP regulations.
</li>
<li>Interest and willingness to travel for both domestic and international travel assignments between 50 to 65% of the time.
</li>
<li>Great client relationship skills; established reputation within industry, preferred.
</li>
<li>Ability and desire to meet with existing and potential clients, build relationships and grow ERM’s process safety services and capabilities through project and program sales.
</li>
<li>Strong verbal communication, technical writing, meeting facilitation and standard computer usage skills (Excel, Word, PowerPoint; familiarity with PHA software a plus).
</li>
<li>Ability and willingness to travel; must have a valid driver’s license.
</li>
<li>Commitment to safety, including following established Health and Safety protocols.
</li>
<li>Ability to maintain flexibility and work independently with minimal supervision.</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
