<p><b>About Us:</b></p>
<p>Market Traders Institute Inc. (MTI) is an industry leader in trading technology and investor education for the past 25 years; based in Orlando, Florida. We help investors make better, faster, and smarter trading decisions by using our proprietary strategies and trading technology. MTI has been recognized as an INC. 5000 (2015 &amp; 2016) fastest growing employee oriented culture companies in America and has received several awards for being a “Best Place to Work” in Central Florida.</p>
<p>Copy link into browser to learn more of MTI -https://www.markettraders.com/mti-story/</p>
<p><b>Summary:</b></p>
<p>As an Affiliate Marketing Coordinator, you will foster relationships with our affiliate partners through daily communication, while actively seeking new partnership opportunities. You will be a "company within the company," a do-it-all team of 1 with the full support, resources and backing of our 20 person marketing department.</p>
<p>As the ideal candidate, you are bright, talented, diligent, and a quick thinker. Your communication skills will be superior, your organizational skills will be top-notch, and you will have a passion for digital marketing. You’ll interact actively with a variety of departments and will work closely with other areas of business, including promotions, business development, and business intelligence teams.</p>
