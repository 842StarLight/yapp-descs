<h2>Full Time Graphic Designer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Track 7 Brewing Company is looking for a hard-working and dedicated individual to fill a Graphic Design position. This position is responsible for creating and maintaining design related to packaging, merchandise, taproom and restaurant visual materials, and sales support tools under the direction of the Marketing Coordinator. This position works with members of the marketing department, sales, taproom and restaurant managers, and other internal partners to ensure visual continuity in the company’s marketing message.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Functions</b><br>
<b>
</b><br>
Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</p>
<ul>
<li>Creates and maintains packaging design, merchandise design, and corporate support materials</li>
<li>Creates multi-media advertisements such as short videos and animations for social platforms</li>
<li>Prepares graphic production files for various types of commercial printing</li>
<li>Creates sample designs for internal design review and collaboration</li>
<li>Create sales marketing material such as sell sheets, banners, promotional items, posters, brochures, and calendars</li>
<li>Produce attractive and effective designs for all media under supervision of the Marketing Coordinator</li>
<li>Works closely and collaborates with the Marketing Coordinator to execute timing and resources for projects to complete on time and on budget</li>
<li>Takes design direction via brief written and verbal communication.</li>
<li>Works closely with Marketing Coordinator to adhere to company branding guidelines for all graphics.</li>
<li>Interacts with vendors and printers.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Archive and organize print and digital assets</li>
<li>Stay current with design and industry specific trends.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Completes all tasks within expected deadlines.</li>
<li>Performs other related duties and assignments as required.</li>
</ul>

<ul>
<li>Communication Proficiency.</li>
<li>Customer/Client Focus.</li>
<li>Flexibility.</li>
<li>Organizational Skills.</li>
<li>Teamwork Orientated</li>
<li>Attention to detail</li>
<li>Time Management.</li>
</ul>

<p><b>
Required Education and Experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or equivalent, or G.E.D. certificate.</li>
<li>Must be proficient with Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop programs, and video editing software</li>
<li>Must have excellent organizational and communication skills</li>
<li>Experience creating graphics for a wide range of print and digital output is required.</li>
<li>At least one year of experience working under the direction of a manager or on a design team. Freelance work is not relevant for this requirement.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>

<li>Associate’s degree or higher in graphic design or related field<b>.</b>
</li>
<li>Experience with web design and digital photography</li>
</ul>
