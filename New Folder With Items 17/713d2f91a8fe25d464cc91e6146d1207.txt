<p><b>Job Description</b></p>
<div>
<p><b>What you will be doing</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Create and execute catering sales action plans to impact need periods and/or specific function space revenue production.</li>
<li>Measure and achieve direct sales activity goals for client solicitations, outside sales calls and property site inspections.</li>
<li>Effectively utilize sales and marketing system for change log, TBA function space, BEO layout and consistency, menus, average checks, meeting space capacity and diagrams.</li>
<li>Maintain accuracy of any third party diagram software. Ensure diagrams are standard in client proposals and provided to banquet operations with Banquet Event Orders (BEO's).</li>
<li>Efficiency in the use of solicitation tools such as Knowland Group for identifying prospecting targets.</li>
<li>Ability to solicit seasonal, traditional and non-traditional wedding and other specialty menus.</li>
<li>Maintain budgeted sales/revenue booking goals on a monthly basis.</li>
</ul>
