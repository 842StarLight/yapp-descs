The Role
<br><br>
Reporting to the CFO, you will interact with all levels of company management. This role will lead efforts related to general and technical accounting, reporting, internal processes and controls, including billing, payroll, equity management, payables, taxation, international expansion, and other key accounting functions.
<br><br>
The seasoned professional will set the strategic direction of the Accounting team and will be responsible for staffing, professional development, and scaling of the function and its members.
<br><br>
Cool Things You'll Do
<br><br>
<b>General and technical accounting:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Manages the monthly and quarterly general ledger close processes.</li>
<li>Ensures that Pantheon is in compliance with GAAP and oversees the development of technical accounting memos relating to Pantheon's business activities.</li>
<li>Ensures proper revenue recognition under ASC606, including reviewing sales agreements, purchase orders, and other related sales documents.</li>
</ul>
<b>Reporting:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Reviews monthly and quarterly financial statements, including analysis of monthly trends and flux analysis.</li>
<li>Ensures the integrity and accuracy of information provided to the external auditors in relation to the annual audit and quarterly reviews.</li>
</ul>
<b>Internal processes and controls:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Manages other finance-related activities, including oversight of accounts payable, collaboration with payroll, and oversight of equity plan administration.</li>
<li>Coordinates the preparation of schedules requested by external tax advisors for corporate income tax returns and tax provision calculations. Reviews tax provision calculations and disclosures for completeness and accuracy.</li>
<li>Leads all finance and accounting activities related to international expansion</li>
<li>Develops, maintains and ensures ongoing compliance with accounting policies and internal controls.</li>
</ul>
<b>Executive, cross-functional leadership:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Develops a culture of mentorship, continuous improvement, training and success in the accounting organization.</li>
<li>Collaborates with Finance, Operations, and Sales leadership on setting the technology roadmap for the Finance function in respect to key initiatives, interoperability, and highest and best use of resources</li>
<li>Manage external relationships with auditors, legal firms, banks, insurance brokers, Board of Directors audit committee, customers and vendors.</li>
<li>Provides finance and accounting input as requested during negotiations of complex and long-term agreements.</li>
<li>Provides cross-functional support to the Company as needs arise, including special projects, system improvements or ad-hoc financial or tax-related analyses.</li>
</ul>
