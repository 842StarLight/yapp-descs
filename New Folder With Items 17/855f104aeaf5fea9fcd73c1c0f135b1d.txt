<div><b>What This Position Is All About:</b></div>
<div>The Beauty Specialist will be responsible for interacting with customers and attending their beauty needs. This individual must model all principles such as Greet, Relate, Exceed, Attend and Thank the customer.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Who You Are:</b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>Focused on attention to detail, taking initiative, and working in a positive working environment</div></li>
<li><div>Ability to work well with others, as well as the ability to work independently to drive a successful business</div></li>
<li><div>Demonstrate professional make-up and treatment consultation and application</div></li>
<li><div>Ability to work well in a fast-paced, team oriented environment that requires a high degree of multi-tasking with minimal supervision</div></li>
</ul>
<div><b>You Also Have:</b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>Minimum 1 year retail experience, preferably in a customer focused, cosmetics environment</div></li>
<li><div>Previously demonstrated successful sales experience, exceeding quota</div></li>
<li><div>Ability to learn and utilize online clienteling tools and software</div></li>
</ul>
<div><b>As the Beauty Specialist, You Will:</b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>Focus on achieving and exceeding individual and vendor line sales goals, event sales goals, and department daily monthly sales goals.</div></li>
<li><div>Maintain a consistent high level of customer service by creating and developing excellent customer relationships as the brand expert and department representative.</div></li>
<li><div>Maintain a positive image as a liaison between the company and your designated Account Executive.</div></li>
<li><div>Utilize online clienteling resource tool to record and maintain client information and preferences to drive your business.</div></li>
<li><div>Focus all efforts on providing an exceptional customer experience from approach, to application, to closing the sale and following up with the customer.</div></li>
