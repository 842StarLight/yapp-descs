<h2>Sales Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>SecureW2 develops best-in-class tools for enterprise wired and wireless networks. We specialize in securing wired and wireless networks and ensuring an organization’s users and data are protected from credential and data theft. Our customer list is rapidly growing and includes Fortune 500 companies, top-tier institutions and universities, and SMB's around the world. If you are a recent graduate, it’s likely your university security system is backed by us. To keep up with high demand, we are looking for energetic professionals to join our small sales and marketing team.</p>
<p>We have an opportunity available for someone to join our team as a Sales Coordinator. We are growing fast and need additional help with inbound lead and existing account management. The person who fits this role is goal-oriented and self-motivated. They will utilize their excellent communication skills to manage new customers and follow-up with our existing accounts. The person we are looking for has a passion for cybersecurity and technology topics and wants to begin a career in tech. If you’re looking to join a collaborative team and enjoy working in an environment stocked with LaCroix, you will succeed at SecureW2!</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities and Duties</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Managing existing accounts to ensure customer success.</li>
<li>Arrange technical reviews to maintain customer relations and develop upsell opportunities.</li>
<li>Help manage Salesforce CRM database.</li>
<li>Work with sales assistant and account executives to process purchasing documents.</li>
<li>Work alongside the marketing team to follow-up on leads via email automation software.</li>
<li>Prepare daily/weekly sales reports to management.</li>
<li>Communicate customer complaints/feedback to technical engineering team.</li>
<li>Support senior sales staff.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications and Skills</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Marketing, Business Communications, or Management preferred</li>
<li>1-3 years experience preferred</li>
<li>Team Player</li>
<li>Self-motivated, organized, and persistent in managing leads/accounts</li>
<li>Excellent oral and written communication skills</li>
<li>Excellent time management skills</li>
<li>Extraverted</li>
<li>Goal-Oriented</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
