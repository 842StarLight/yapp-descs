About Us
<br><br>
MediaMath's mission is to help innovative marketers delight their customers and drive real business outcomes. Over 9,500 marketers in 42 countries use our enterprise solution to reach the right user with the right message for the right price, wherever they are. These are the leading brands and agencies in the world who rely on MediaMath to provide technology, services and leadership to help them continue to transform their marketing practices and compete in a quickly changing industry. Founded in 2007 as a pioneer in "programmatic" advertising, MediaMath has been recognized as a leader in the space in four independent evaluations over the last two years. MediaMath has offices in 16 cities worldwide and is headquartered in New York City.

Key Responsibilities
<br><br>
We are seeking a highly motivated Senior Account Manager with a proven history of leading and managing large enterprise clients, in a competitive, dynamic, and evolving marketing space.
<br><br>
As an Account Manager at MediaMath, you are the strategic lead on existing Tier 1 accounts, developing strategies to grow the partnership.
<br><br>
In close partnership with other internal teams, you will engage with senior technical, operational and executive-level contacts at the client, and be expected to establish, develop, and deepen the relationships that will allow our partnership to grow.
<br><br>
<b>You will:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Partner with decision makers across your clients to design, iterate, and evolve joint, multi-year roadmaps that address their priorities and challenges</li>
<li>Articulate value propositions MediaMath can offer to the client's business to drive outsized revenue growth for both companies</li>
<li>Work closely with the Account Director, you'll provide strategic input into the overall client strategy and future roadmap</li>
<li>Serve as the strategic lead and point of escalation for clients and your team across your book of business and pipeline</li>
</ul>
<b>You are:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>An excellent communicator; specifically, strong listening, negotiation, and presentation.</li>
<li>Able to establish meaningful relationships throughout all levels of client organizations with a track record of retaining key clients</li>
<li>Able to coordinate internal resources with an uncanny ability to rally peers to resolve problems quickly, efficiently and under pressure and/or time constraints</li>
<li>An effective project manager – ability to prioritize and methodically meet deadlines.</li>
</ul>
<b>You have:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>5+ year experience working as an Account Lead / Senior Account Manager on Tier 1 accounts – brands of agencies</li>
<li>History of hitting and exceeding revenue goals by adhering to a Customer-Focused Sales Methodology.</li>
<li>Experience engaging with decision makers at Manager level and above</li>
</ul>
