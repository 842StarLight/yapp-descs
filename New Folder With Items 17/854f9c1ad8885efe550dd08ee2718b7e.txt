<h2>Leasing Consultant I</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">WinnCompanies is looking for a skilled and motivated Leasing Consultant to join Elements at 1600 in Fairfield, CA. Your remarkable sales and/or customer service skills set you apart from your peers, and you thrive by making genuine connections. You’re interested in a career in property management and love the thrill of closing a deal. No two days will be the same with this fast paced opportunity, but here’s what you’ll be responsible for:
<br>Responsibilities
<br><ul>
<li>Rent apartments and renew leases at a 120 unit property</li>
<li>Interact with prospective and current residents to achieve maximum occupancy for the property</li>
<li>Prepare lease documentation and complete move-in paperwork</li>
<li>Ensure that property interior and exterior are to standard</li>
<li>Review, prioritize, and delegate resident service requests</li>
<li>Assist the Property Manager in execution of operational and administrative tasks</li>
<li>Establish relationships with area corporations and real estate brokers to generate new business</li>
<li>Remain current on local events, hiring trends, and any matters that may impact the property</li>
<li>Maintain knowledge of competitors, submarkets, and customer demographics</li>
<li>Maintain knowledge of Federal, State, and other regulatory requirements</li>
<li>Perform all duties while generating interest and enthusiasm about the property by creating an exceptional customer experience</li>
</ul>
Requirements
<br><ul>

<li>1-2 years of sales, customer service, or hospitality related experience</li>
<li>Outstanding verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Experience with computer systems including Microsoft Office Suite</li>
</ul>
Preferred Qualifications
<br><ul>

<li>NALP or other industry-related certification</li>

</ul>
