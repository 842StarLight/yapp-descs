<h2>Harley-Davidson Service Writer/Advisor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>No Cages Harley-Davidson </b>is looking for an experienced, self motivated, outgoing, and enthusiastic sales / service professional to join our <b>Service Department </b>team as a <b>Service Writer/Advisor</b>. Good organizational skills, willingness to learn and a positive outlook are a must. We offer excellent benefits including competitive pay, health care, vacation and holiday pay and a fun work environment. <i><b>Must have Harley-Davidson knowledge</b></i>!</p>
<p><b>Summary Description</b><br>Intakes customer vehicles, diagnoses problems and service needs, schedules work, assigns jobs to service technicians and communicates job status to customer.</p>
<p><b>Major Duties and Responsibilities: </b></p>
<p><b>1)</b><b>Service Department Operations</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Assist Service department manager in maintaining a smooth running efficient service department with a high degree of customer satisfaction.</li>
<li>Diagnose problems accurately, and clearly describe them on the repair order.</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Estimate costs and completion times at point of sale. Clearly communicate them to customer.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Assign jobs to technicians based on skill level and current resource utilization.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Review work-in-progress to ensure quality and timeliness.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Occasionally assist technicians when they are having difficulty performing service work.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Determine correct part numbers on repair orders and assist the parts department with picking and posting of parts used before job starts.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Perform cashier functions, as needed.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Make estimates for internal and wreck repairs.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Open &amp; close all customer paid, warranty, and internal repair orders.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Follow procedures for quick &amp; efficient handling of warranty items, including tagging and proper storage of these items.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Become familiar and efficient with all phases of the computer system required for service &amp; parts management.</li></ul>
<p><b>2)</b><b>Customer Service</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provide superior customer service to both internal and external customers.</li>
<li>Greet customers immediately, in a courteous and friendly manner.</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Handle telephone transactions quickly, and courteously.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Notify customers ASAP of any changes, delays, or additional work needed. Avoid setting high customer expectation levels in situations where expectations are not likely to be met.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Sell additional services by pointing out service specials or additional work needed.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Handle customer complaints reasonably, showing empathy and a positive attitude, and demonstrate our commitment to “Make Things Right”.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Give special attention to repeat repairs to ensure the situation is corrected.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Avoid making commitments which can not (or likely will not) be met. Set customers’ expectations to a level where they will likely be met.</li></ul>
<p><b>3)</b><b>Other Duties - As Assigned</b></p>
<p><b>Commitments: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Treat all employees and customers fairly, courteously, and with dignity.</li>
<li>Model superior customer service behavior for all interactions with customers and employees.</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Be prompt and available for flexible scheduling.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintain up to date technical qualification (PHD and others) by completing all training programs assigned by the Service Manager.</li></ul>
<p><b>Qualifications &amp; Job Requirements: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Outgoing, friendly personality and superior communication &amp; customer service skills.</li>
<li>Knowledge and experience with servicing of Harley-Davidson motorcycles, and other products sold by the dealership, or the demonstrated ability to quickly learn them.</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Experience with Point-of-Sale and Parts &amp; Service management computer software or the ability to quickly learn due to general knowledge &amp; experience with computers.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Current Motorcycle license.</li></ul>
<p><b>Physical Demands: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>The noise level in the work environment is usually loud.</li>
<li>Occasionally required to bend, stoop, crouch, reach, and handle tools.</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Occasionally requires the ability to lift 40 pounds of material.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Occasionally requires the ability to balance and push a 600+ lb. motorcycle.</li></ul>
<p><b>Working Conditions: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Frequently works near moving mechanical parts.</li>
<li>Is potentially exposed to battery acid, gasoline, chemical cleaning materials or other toxic materials commonly found in a motor vehicle service department.</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Occasionally exposed to exhaust fumes or airborne other particles.</li></ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul>
<li>Harley-Davidson dealership: 1 year (Required)</li>
<li>Service Writer/Advisor: 1 year (Required)</li>
</ul>
<p>License:</p>
<ul><li>Motorcycle Endorsement (Required)</li></ul>
<p>Additional Compensation:</p>
<ul><li>Store Discounts</li></ul>
<p>Work Location:</p>
<ul><li>One location</li></ul>
<p>Benefits:</p>
<ul>
<li>Health insurance</li>
<li>Dental insurance</li>
<li>Vision insurance</li>
<li>Retirement plan</li>
<li>Paid time off</li>
</ul>
