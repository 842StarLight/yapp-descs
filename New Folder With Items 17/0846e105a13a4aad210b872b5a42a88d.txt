<h2>Sales Development Representatives</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>JFrog is looking for talented and motivated Sales Development Representatives (SDRs) to join our Sales team. As an SDR, you will be responsible for researching, contacting, and qualifying inbound &amp; outbound prospects as potential customers.</p>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Responsibilities</b></h4>
<ul>
<li>Initiate contact with inbound leads</li>
<li>Outbound sales to targeted accounts</li>
<li>Research accounts, identify key players, generate interest and develop relationships with important stakeholders</li>
<li>Provide potential prospects with information about JFrog and our product value</li>
<li>Conduct conversations with decision makers from SMB to Mid &amp; Large Enterprise organizations</li>
<li>Ensure prospects meet predefined qualification criteria</li>
<li>Schedule qualified customers for sessions</li>
<li>Build and maintain a profitable sales pipeline</li>
<li>Disseminate opportunities and educate Sales Managers as necessary</li>
<li>Update salesforce to ensure efficient lead management and record-keeping</li>
</ul>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Desired Skills &amp; Experience</b></h4>
<ul>
<li>At least 2 years Sales or Sales Development Experience</li>
<li>Outbound Selling Skills</li>
<li>Ability to conduct in depth research via various online tools - must</li>
<li>Self-starter with a track record of self-management in order to get the job done</li>
<li>Pre-call planning and time management skills</li>
<li>Proficient with standard corporate productivity tools (email, voicemail, MS Office,Google docs)</li>
<li>Sales training and Salesforce experience is a plus</li>
<li>Positive and energetic phone skills, excellent listening skills, strong writing skills</li>
<li>Excited to work in a high-energy sales team environment</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
