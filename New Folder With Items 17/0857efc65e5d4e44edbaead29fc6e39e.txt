Who is Caldera Medical?
<br><br>
Caldera Medical is a growth stage medical device company dedicated to improving the quality of life for women! In 2018, Caldera was ranked one of Inc 5000’s fastest growing private companies in the United States and #38 fastest growing private company and #3 fastest growing Healthcare company in Los Angeles by the LA Business Journal. We develop and market differentiated surgical implants specifically for the treatment of Stress Urinary Incontinence and Pelvic Organ Prolapse. Our products are used by Urogynecologists, Gynecologists, and Urologists worldwide.
