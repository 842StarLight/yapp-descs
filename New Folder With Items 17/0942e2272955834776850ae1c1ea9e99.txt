<b>
Job Description:</b></p>
<p>
As our <b>IT</b> <b>Business Systems Manager, </b>you will be a hands-on techno-functional leader with high visibility and ownership in driving a number of key programs cross functionally. You will primarily be responsible to serve as the point of contact and subject matter expert in supporting, maintaining and managing our G&amp;A applications ecosystem. This is a full-time position at RingCentral HQ in Belmont, CA.</p>
