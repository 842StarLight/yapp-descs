WarnerMedia is looking for a smart, analytical and socially-savvy individual as Social Discovery Analyst. Launchpad is a global social agency within Ad Sales that handles advertiser campaigns across WarnerMedia properties (CNN, Great Big Story, Bleacher Report, Adult Swim, Team Coco, etc.). The candidate will use a suite of innovative social tools, access to WarnerMedia premium social handles and data to find the right audience on social media, develop strategies to target and reach them on the right social platform and deliver tailored content to them at the right moment. - Be a member of the Ignite Social team within Ad Sales - Implement, plan, and measure paid social media campaigns for sponsored content across CNN and Great Big Story - The ideal candidate has experience running marketing/advertising campaigns, analyzing data, and using it to craft a strong sales story.<br>
<br>
<b>The Day-to-Day</b><ul>
<li>
Plan social media campaigns across WarnerMedia networks, utilizing responsible media mix, audience insights tools, and historical performance knowledge.
</li>
<li>Support campaigns on social platforms including Facebook, Instagram, YouTube, Twitter, etc., working within the native tools on each platform to execute and optimize.
</li>
<li>Analyze campaign performance; could include reporting as often as daily, weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly, to ensure reporting ladders back to strategy and business objectives.
</li>
<li>Participate in discussions on tracking, tagging, and targeting related to social media.
</li>
<li>Create, find, and leverage custom audiences across 1st party DMP and 3rd party lists.
</li>
<li>Keep campaign team well-informed of performance and tracking against KPIs.
</li>
</ul>
<b>The Essentials</b><ul>
<li>
1+ years working experience in paid social, digital marketing or social insights at an agency or media publisher. Education level: Bachelor's Degree or higher.
</li>
<li>Experience executing and managing campaigns using Facebook Ads Manager &amp; Power Editor, Google AdWords or Twitter Ads.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of paid social landscape, including optimization techniques and strategies, bidding models, tracking techniques, targeting options, KPIs, and ad units across variety of social platforms (Facebook, Twitter, YouTube, Instagram, Snapchat, etc.)
</li>
<li>Strong quantitative skills, with the ability to translate deep learnings into actionable recommendations.
</li>
<li>Working knowledge of one or more social analytics &amp; insights tools a plus –
</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Office, with emphasis on Excel and PowerPoint.
</li>
<li>Strong verbal and written communication skills.
</li>
<li>Strong analytical and critical thinking skills.
</li>
<li>Exceptional attention to detail and organizational skills.</li>
</ul>
