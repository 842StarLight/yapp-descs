<h2>Chief Marketing Officer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Job Context and Summary:</b></p>
<p>Healogics is the largest provider of advanced wound care services in the United States, treating more than 300,000 chronic wounds patients annually across over 600 sites. For several years, Healogics has utilized iHeal, its proprietary, wound-specific Electronic Medical Record, to capture patient, treatment, outcomes and other data. As a result, the company now maintains the largest database of longitudinal wound care outcomes in the world, with research-ready clinical data on over 1.5 million wounded patients.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Healogics is now looking for an innovative, entrepreneurial and inspiring leader to lead our Marketing organization. This critical role will be responsible for our go-to-market strategy for all our products and services, marketing execution, and brand strategy across all markets and Healogics offerings, and marketing communication. As a member of the Senior Leadership Team, the EVP, Marketing is responsible for partnering with business leaders and embedding strategic marketing across all functions to truly enable growth and realize the strategic vision and mission of the Enterprise.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>All Healogics employees must perform their job responsibilities according to all Healogics policies, Hospital policies, as well as to accrediting organizations, federal and state regulation, and to the Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services (CMS) guidelines, as applicable.</p>
<br>
