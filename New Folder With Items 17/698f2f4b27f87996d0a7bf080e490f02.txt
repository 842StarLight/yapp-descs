<h2>IT Business Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>The IT Business Analyst will be working directly with customers, vendors as well as interfacing with and providing support to sales, buying, business operations, and supply chain teams. Time will be split between supporting existing production systems and designing/deploying new functionality within these systems. Support tasks will include working with business users on questions and requests around how to best utilize current functionalities as well as helping troubleshoot system issues and problems. Project tasks include working with business partners on future system needs, documenting requirements and helping design related processes. Vendor relationships will be a critical key to success as they?ll play a role in both support and new system deployments.</p>
<p><b>Duties &amp; Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Will participate in design, assembly, and documentation walkthroughs</li>
<li>Effectively communicate status, changes, and issues to the relevant parties and stakeholders</li>
<li>Quickly address and /or escalate project issues and support their resolution</li>
<li>Serve as a liaison between the business and the development team</li>
<li>Assist in the creation and execution of test plans and test cases</li>
<li>Assist in management of issue and change management process</li>
<li>Responsible for all activities related to supporting and enhancing our corporate systems</li>
<li>Act as an authority and subject matter expert corporate systems - understand the business user?s function and help them develop more efficient process/procedure</li>
<li>Anticipate and identify issues that may negatively impact business and develop/implement technology based solutions</li>
<li>Support all aspects of IT projects including analysis, design, development, installation and implementation</li>
<li>Work with IT vendors and other outside contacts on development and service relationships</li>
<li>Must be able to multi-task and juggle multiple priorities while ensuring successful completion of all items</li>
<li>Providing suggestions to the development team during the development stage of product to meet the client's business needs</li>
<li>Preparing accurate and detailed requirement specifications documents, user interface guides, and functional specification documents</li>
<li>Documenting the acquired results of analysis and workflows as well as obtaining sign-off from the appropriate client</li>
<li>Designing and executing the test scripts and test scenarios</li>
<li>Coordinating with the groups of business unit who test, validate, evaluate new applications and functions, and determine issues in services and software</li>
<li>Managing any change requests related to the working project plans daily to meet the agreed deadlines</li>
</ul>
</div>
