Key Responsibilities
<br><br>
MediaMath seeks a thoughtful, high-impact contributor with exceptional analytical, communication, program management, and client management skills to join our Client Services team! The Client Services (CS) team serves as the primary point of contact for service delivery across Professional Services, as well as the main conduit back into the Accounts team on sales. They diagnose and coordinate with necessary MediaMath resources in order to achieve the client's goals. In partnership with the CS Director, the Client Services Manager contributes to the execution of programmatic campaigns and evolution of strategic/technical roadmaps for high-value clients. You must possess the ability to develop and deepen relationships with both operational and executive-level contacts.
<br><br>
<b>You will:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Support Manager/Director in owning and developing strong relationships with the client and key stakeholders at all levels</li>
<li>Project manage client onboarding and implementation process including product, channel and partner activation, campaign launches, SDGs, and ad-hoc projects</li>
<li>Coordinate resource management and allocation and control activities across Professional Services teams to ensure projects are delivered on-time and on budget</li>
<li>Serve as the main point of contact for delivery and activation questions from the client and issue resolution as needed. (i.e. communication and expectation setting, raising tickets)</li>
<li>Help facilitate change management between self and managed service</li>
<li>Understand and communicate relevant new products, features, and capabilities as they become available and ensure successful deployment and activation with your clients</li>
<li>Creating and streamlining processes and workflow</li>
<li>Creating client staffing plans across Sales and Professional Services</li>
<li>Client Training (facilitating educational programs)</li>
</ul>
<b>You are:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>An experienced with managing enterprise level clients, dealing with key stakeholders and focused on account retention and growth</li>
<li>Experienced in online advertising account management, marketing consulting and/or brand management or yield optimization</li>
</ul>
<b>You have:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>A Bachelor's degree; (Preferably in quantitative discipline Mathematics, Economics, Engineering, Statistics, Physics, etc.)</li>
<li>3-5 years experience working within the Programmatic industry specifically within a client services team</li>
<li>Excellent listening, negotiation, presentation, written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Strong analytical skills. Must be comfortable with MS Excel, data analysis, and internet technologies.</li>
</ul>
