<p></p>
<p>SAP Concur is seeking a dynamic and innovative inside sales professional to prospect and sell directly to companies with 1 to 99 employees via the telephone, internet presentations, and through partners in a defined geographic territory. This role will be responsible for the entire sales cycle along with supporting the customer experience. The Regional Sales Executive reports to the Regional Sales Director.<br>
<br>
<b>In this role you will…
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Achieve sales objectives through sales to new customers.</li>
<li>Identify sales opportunities through direct prospecting, lead follow up, networking, and partner relationships.</li>
<li>Manage sales process through qualification, needs analysis, product demonstration, negotiation and close.</li>
<li>Work closely with internal resources and cross-functional partners to drive events, promotions, and partnering.</li>
<li>Develop and maintain a high level of knowledge about Concur’s products and services.</li>
<li>Develop and maintain an understanding of the territory, marketplace, competitive offerings, and other business issues relevant to the position.</li>
<li>Increase opportunities through relationships with Concur partners and other third parties
</li>
<li>Document daily sales activities in Salesforce, prepare accurate reports and forecasts, manage pipeline, and perform other tasks necessary to drive sales revenue and communicate activities to sales management.</li>
<li>Be an active team player on the sales team, with business partners, and throughout Concur to help achieve wider company objectives.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>You will bring…</b></p>
<ul>
<li>1-3 years of successful B2B sales experience with a strong emphasis on acquiring net-new customers</li>
<li>Experience to selling to “C” level Executives</li>
<li>Documented success achieving and exceeding assigned quotas</li>
<li>A consultative, solution-based sales methodology</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills with an emphasis on persuasion &amp; influence</li>
<li>Strong business acumen and hunter mentality</li>
<li>Excellent PC and Internet skills including: Microsoft Office, Outlook, Salesforce, and web presentation applications (Webex, Placeware, etc.)</li>
<li>Sales experience with financial/accounting applications and/or hosted software solutions is a strong plus although not a requirement</li>
<br>
</ul>
