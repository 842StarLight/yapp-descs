<h2>Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<div>Vevo is the world’s largest all-premium music video provider, offering artists a global platform with enormous scale through its distribution partners. Vevo connects artists with their audience globally via music videos and original content, working directly with them to find unique ways to bring their music to life visually. Vevo also works with emerging artists, providing them with a platform of global scale and reach, to find and grow their audience. Reaching 26 billion monthly views globally, Vevo has over 400,000 music videos in its catalogue.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Take a look inside Vevo</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Vevo is looking for a rock star salesperson to sell Vevo’s music video programming platform to both clients and agencies. The Account Executive is responsible for building relationships, driving revenue and closing business. This seller is the day-to-day point of contact for agencies and clients and is responsible for selling Vevo’s value proposition effectively in the media marketplace, with special attention to Entertainment, Tech, CPG and QSR clients.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
If you believe Bono that “music can change the world because it can change people”, then you believe in the power of music. Bring Vevo your ability to balance creativity, focus, determination, and teamwork so that, together, we can bring the power of our music video and entertainment platform to the world.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
This is a full-time position and is based at our office in Times Square</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>As a member of our team, you will:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Drive revenue, close business, and build relationships</li>
<li>
Consult with clients on their advertising needs, and present innovative video solutions to meet their objectives</li>
<li>
Negotiate deal costs and terms to close client programs that results in a win for everybody</li>
<li>
Forecast/track/report sales performance, including budget, dates, and probability to close</li>
<li>
Travel throughout a territory (once/twice monthly)</li>
<li>
Collaborate with Vevo teams (Client Services, Marketing, Programming) to lead proper execution of campaigns and insure alignment with customers</li>
<li>
Provide actionable insights and solutions to clients based on their objectives and Vevo’s opportunities</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>This describes you:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
You have established relationships with New York-based agencies and client-side teams</li>
<li>
Ability to lead, collaborate, and work autonomously in a dynamic start-up team environment with a positive demeanor</li>
<li>
Problem-solving skills, specifically the ability to assess situations, evaluate options, make decisions, draw conclusions, and exercise judgment</li>
<li>
Ability to analyze clients’ business marketing objectives, brand or ROI goals, media buying cycles, metrics for success, etc.</li>
<li>
Proven capacity to utilize analytics to drive campaign optimization</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>

<li>
Strong negotiation &amp; presentation skills</li>
<li>
Basic understanding of viewability and data tags</li>

Excellent organizational, analytical, interpersonal, and communication (written and oral) skills</li>

Advanced skills with Microsoft Outlook, Excel, PowerPoint, Word</li>
<li>
Knowledge of SalesForce and Operative preferred</li>

Expert familiarity with online media planning, research and trafficking tools; proficiency with @Plan, comScore, DAR, Atlas, Free Wheel</li>
