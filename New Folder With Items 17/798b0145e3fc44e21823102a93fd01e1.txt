<h2>Sales/New Business Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Open position for a salesperson for a Chicago-based media content company offering commercial photography and video production services for a variety of businesses and brands including manufacturers, catalogers and retailers.</p>
<p><b>Job Responsibility: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop sales strategy/plan to identify and target new business prospects (primarily) and existing customers (secondarily)</li>
<li>Develop and maintain a customer database</li>
<li>Plan and organize daily, weekly and monthly schedule of calls/emails etc. to prospects</li>
<li>Generate and qualify leads</li>
<li>Identify and secure pitch opportunities for the company on an ongoing basis</li>
<li>Source and develop client referrals</li>
<li>Respond to all sales inquiries by phone, electronically or in person</li>
<li>Review, adjust and maintain sales and promotional materials. Develop new material as needed.</li>
<li>Plan and conduct direct and digital marketing activities</li>
<li>Research the competitive marketplace, gathering information on pricing, clients, services, staffing, etc.</li>
<li>Keep management informed by submitting activity and results reports, including daily call reports, weekly work plans, and monthly and annual analysis</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Experience</b></p>
<p>The ideal candidate will have:</p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of 5 years sales experience in the advertising/marketing industry</li>
<li>A proven ability to achieve sales targets, please provide examples</li>
<li>An understanding of the principles and practices of sales</li>
<li>Experience in making presentations</li>
<li>Knowledge of relevant computer applications</li>
<li>Knowledge of customer service principles</li>
<li>
<b>Key Competencies</b>Planning and strategizing</li>
<li>Self-confidence and persuasiveness</li>
<li>Prospecting skills</li>
<li>Closing skills</li>
<li>Customer service/client management skills</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Negotiation skills</li>
<li>Adaptability, resilience and tenacity</li>
<li>Goal-driven, results-oriented</li>
</ul>
