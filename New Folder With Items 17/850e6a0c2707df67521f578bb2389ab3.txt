<h2>Senior Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Has your paper napkin business plan turned into a billion-dollar reality? Ours has, and our employees have helped us get to where we are today; and as we continue to scale towards being the incomparable leader in our industry, we are looking for a Sr. Project Manager to help write the next chapter with us.</p>
<p>
What started as a sketch on a napkin is now our world-class software platform that combines cutting-edge technology with outstanding client service and premier wealth management capabilities. Shareworks by Morgan Stanley is designed to simplify the complexities of equity plan management, while helping employees realize the full potential of their benefits.</p>
<p>
If you’re sitting there scratching your head while reading this and wondering how you could possibly be part of “simplifying the complexities of equity compensation”, keep reading to the bottom and you’ll find out!</p>
<p>
We currently have a full-time, permanent opportunity for:</p>
<p><b>
Senior Project Manager</b></p>
<p>
Located in the <b>Eau Claire neighbourhood of downtown Calgary, AB</b>, the Sr. Project Manager will support the larger internal Migrations’ team responsible for the successful transition of over 250 clients from the existing Morgan Stanley legacy set of applications to Shareworks. This role will guide clients through the full lifecycle of migration phases and activities for the client’s Equity Plans (Stock Option, Stock Appreciations Rights, Restricted Stock and Units, Performance Awards, Stock Purchase Plans, Cash Awards, and Long Shares) onto the new platform.</p>
<p>
We foster a collaborative work environment and work closely with other company teams involved in the migration project lifecycle, ‘doing what it takes’ with the ultimate goal of ensuring the successful completion of each project. Successful candidates will be those who are rewarded by finding solutions to difficult problems, get a sense of gratification from finding things in the details, and the satisfaction of successfully transitioning the client into live operations.<br>
<br>
<b>Skills/Abilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Leadership: Ability to take charge and lead projects with minimal supervision or follow-up. Leads by example and models the way for the broader team.</li>
<li>Business Knowledge: Depth of knowledge relative to equity plan administration and recordkeeping software applications.</li>
<li>Analytical Skills: Well-developed problem-solving skills necessary to research and trouble shoot inquiries through continual interaction with all departments.</li>
<li>Software: Strong knowledge and use of MS Office (Project, Word, Excel, Visio, PowerPoint, Access &amp; Outlook); Knowledge of Stock Plan software.</li>
<li>Communication: Excellent written and verbal communication skills necessary to Interact with external clients and internal stakeholders.</li>
<li>Project Management/ Support: Strong organization skills and project management disciplines. Ability to work under periods of high pressure with tight timeframes. Flexibility to work weekends.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for senior management reporting of current status and daily updates.</li>
<li>Responsible for implementation of standard project management disciplines to properly align with new platform including introduction of new development through an agile approach, meeting management, project plan creation and tracking, action item tracking and resolution, escalation management, coordination of testing activities, client readiness assessments, and pre and post go-live coordination.</li>
<li>Expert in prioritization management through multiple initiatives/projects for multiple clients</li>
<li>Expert communications and coordination with all key external and internal stakeholders including multiple home office departments and various office locations.</li>
<li>Responsible for guiding corporate clients through all phases of migration activities for all stock benefit plans</li>
<li>Expert in leading meetings and coordinating complex corporate client discussions in person and via teleconference. Ability to manage client and key stakeholder expectations in a dynamic and changing environment.</li>
<li>Individual must work closely with assigned client base to provide world class service and high client satisfaction.</li>
<li>Ability to transition lessons learned and skillsets to future new client onboarding pipeline</li>
<li>Assist in the RFP and sales presentations process as required.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Strong project management track record and migrations/implementations of enterprise wide solutions</li>
<li>PMP Certification a plus</li>
<li>In depth knowledge of Equity Compensation, CEP a plus.</li>
<li>Shareworks and/or relative administrative software knowledge a plus</li>
<li>Strong client engagement skills with fortune 500, midcap, microcap, and IPO companies</li>
<li>Proactively manages potential problems to mitigate and minimize risk associated with people, product types, and projects and ability to take corrective action.</li>
<li>Exceptional working knowledge of Microsoft Office suite</li>
<li>Jira application knowledge a plus</li>
<li>5 - 10+ years’ experience in the project management, benefits and/or financial services.</li>
</ul>
<p><i>While we thank all applicants for their interest, please note that only those individuals selected for an interview will be contacted.</i></p>
