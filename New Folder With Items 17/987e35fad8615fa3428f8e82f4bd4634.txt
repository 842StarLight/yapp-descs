<h2>Street Team Promoter | Sports Marketing</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Our company culture embraces originality. We eschew traditional advertising in favor of campaigns that let people observe, sample, and form their own opinions. Exp Inc fresh way of operating is the reason that our partners flourish.</p>
<p>At Exp Inc. we seek out problems in order to solve them. Our mission is to build awareness for local major and minor sports, hospitality, and other entertainment businesses. Our highly-trained representatives know how to connect with people on the ground. Their instinct for introducing the public to experiences they might otherwise miss ensures immediate return on investment for our clients.</p>
<p><b>Qualifications: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Must possess excellent interpersonal communication skills</li>
<li>Maintain a high level of professionalism &amp; integrity</li>
<li>4-year degree preferred, not required - ideally in marketing, management, or business</li>
<li>1-2 years of customer service, sales, retail sales, marketing, or management experience</li>
<li>Demonstrated leadership ability - not required to be in Sales and Marketing field</li>
<li>Demonstrated critical thinking and problem solving skills</li>
</ul>
<p>Our hands-on-training is designed to focus on the individual, using your strengths to help build up your weaknesses. Promotions are based on how quickly you grasp information and your ability to mentor other individuals, not how long you have worked in our offices.</p>
<p><b>Our philosophy is that we would rather have 5 people that can do 5 things that 25 people that can only do 1. </b>As there is no one that does what we do, our promotions are only from within.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Actively participate in brand building awareness and promoting the designated services through sponsored events, seasonal marketing campaigns, products, and other marketing events.</li>
<li>Coordinate with sales, marketing, and event organizers to ensure marketing objectives and needs are being addressed</li>
<li>Have a working knowledge of all clients and services, focusing on education, and brand knowledge.</li>
<li>Coordinate marketers for each event, with the assistance from the management team.</li>
<li>Participate in weekly meetings or conference calls with the marketing team, to review recent campaign outcomes, and prepare for upcoming campaigns.</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
