<h2>Account Strategist II</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><b>Role Summary</b></div>
<div>
<br>
As a member of the Account Strategy team at Sendoso, you will be blazing trails at the world’s first Sending Platform. You will make an impact on the company’s growth and retention efforts by targeting key accounts that have strategic value to develop a deeper, more meaningful, and mutually beneficial relationship .<br>
<br>
More specifically, you will be a key player in continuing Sendoso’s tremendous company growth through strategic efforts that drive success through customer utilization of the Sendoso Platform. This role will work hand-in-hand with the Customer Success, Finance, Marketing, Solutions Engineering and Enablement teams to enhance customer relationships via a data-driven approach. The Account Strategy team utilizes a tailored approach to understand, at a high level, the histories, competitive landscapes, industry trends, and growth projections of our customers to gain a deeper understanding of how they can leverage Sendoso to be successful.<br>
<br>
Additionally, you will be part of a team that focuses on enabling internal partners on the Customer Success and Sales teams with data-driven insights and research that will help them target specific opportunities for growth, retention, and churn risk mitigation within the Sendoso book of business.</div>
<br>
<div>
</div>
<div>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Partner with Customer Success Managers to do quarterly deep dives with our customers</li>
<li>Provide customers with tailored recommendations to help augment their marketing strategies through Sendoso</li>
<li>Work with internal partners to craft strategies targeting retention, growth, and expansion efforts</li>
<li>Contribute to the team’s buildout of departmental and company-wide enablement resources</li>
<li>Analyze customer data to identify trends, opportunities, and risk indicators</li>
<li>Serve as an ambassador of Sendoso for offsite events, trade shows, and customer visits</li>
<li>Report on an ongoing basis on KPIs</li>
<li>Work with the Directory of Account Strategy on best practices to reach out to, engage, and grow our relationships with internal partners</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
<b>Qualifications</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>4+ years experience in a relationship-driven, customer-facing role</li>
<li>Experience working in the Software as a Service (SaaS) industry</li>
<li>Experience working with cloud CRM, sales engagement, and/or marketing platforms</li>
<li>MSExcel or Google Sheets skills a plus</li>
<li>Willingness to work on a wide range of tasks, from high strategy to nuts and bolts operations</li>
<li>Willingness to engage directly in customer strategy calls</li>
<li>Willingness to become a Sendoso software expert and Super Sender</li>
<li>Ability to present ideas effectively to individuals or groups, including the ability to change direction of presentation to meet the needs of the audience</li>
<li>Ability to build relationships and take responsibility for the success of both our internal and external customers</li>
</ul>
