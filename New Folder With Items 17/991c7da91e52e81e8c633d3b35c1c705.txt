<h2>Training & Documentation Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>The Vistex Training &amp; Documentation Specialist is responsible for defining, developing, implementing, and coordinating all aspects of training for Vistex Go-to-Market Suite® (GTMS®) solution application. As part of the Training GTMS team, you will develop and conduct training for both internal and client training engagements.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Define, develop and organize training materials (presenter decks, participant guides, exercises, job aids) for GTMS software. Materials will include the business need for the software as well as the technical steps to implement and utilize the system at the customer’s site.</li>
<li>
Work closely with product specialists, customers and trainers to create delta classes and documentation.</li>
<li>
Teach virtual and onsite classes to varying audiences and skill levels on the business processes to implement and utilize GTMS software.</li>
<li>
Create templates and other tools for reusable content for end user and customer tailored classes.</li>
<li>
Analyze training needs and develop strategies to improve project management of implementation projects.</li>
<li>
Proactively manage, report and troubleshoot issues that occur in system during course material development and pre-training testing.</li>
<li>
Handle related duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2>
<div><div><div><ul>
<li>Minimum Bachelors degree.</li>
<li>
At least 3 years of software training and material development experience.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of SAP or ERP software products; Sales and Distribution (SD) SAP module experience preferred.</li>
<li>
Ability to train both technical and non-technical people.</li>
<li>
Independent thinking with strong problem solving skills.</li>
<li>
Ability to adapt to changing schedules and multiple work plans.</li>
<li>
Excellent verbal, written and interpersonal skills.</li>
<li>
Ability to learn new software and processes quickly.</li>
<li>
Travel required to customer facility depending on training requirements (20-30%)</li>
</ul></div></div></div>
