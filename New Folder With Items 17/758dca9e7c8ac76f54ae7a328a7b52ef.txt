<h2>Account Manager / CSR</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Our clients, backed by a nationally recognized and leading insurance agency, are seeking talented professionals to join their team across the Greater Baton Rouge area. If you are a recent grad or established professional looking for a career change, who is energetic, self-motivated, and has a strong desire to win, this may be the opportunity for you
<br><br>
As a Customer Service Representative, you will be part of a winning team and will play an integral role in helping the business, and our customers, through providing remarkable customer experience. If you are outgoing and enjoy helping others, this may be the opportunity for you.
<br><br>
<b>Position Highlights:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Valuable experience: you will work for an independent agent that has the backing of one of the largest insurance agencies and a Fortune 100 company, with extensive tools and training to be set up for success</li>
<li>Helping Others: you’ll have a unique opportunities to help others both in proactively seeking insurance coverage, as well as in times in need, when they need you the most</li>
<li>Growth Potential: You will have the opportunity to work alongside a great team, who will provide training and mentorship, leading to growth opportunities in the agent’s office.</li>
</ul>
<b>What you will do:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Establish customer relationships and follow up with customers, as needed.</li>
<li>Provide prompt, accurate, and friendly customer service. Service can include responding to inquiries regarding insurance availability, eligibility, coverages, policy changes, transfers, claim submissions, and billing clarification.</li>
<li>Use a customer-focused, needs-based review process to educate customers about insurance options.</li>
</ul>
<b>What you will need:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Customer service experience (in any field) preferred</li>
<li>Effective communication skills and ability to connect and build strong relationships with current and future clients</li>
<li>Must have or be able to obtain Insurance Producer's licenses in Property, Casualty, Life &amp; Health</li>
<li>Must successfully complete and maintain all company, state and federal requirements including licenses to sell and service designated products.</li>
<li>Strong listening, oral, and written communication skills</li>
<li>Detail oriented, with effective problem solving skills</li>
<li>Dedicated to providing stellar customer service</li>
<li>Experienced with popular computer software (Microsoft products especially)</li>
<li>Motivated to work in a team environment</li>
</ul>
