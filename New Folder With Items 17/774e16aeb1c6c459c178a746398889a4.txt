PRIMARY PURPOSE OF POSITION
<br>
Provide deep technical expertise to support delivery and maintenance of IT solutions. Provide IT teams and business personnel with technology solutions by weighing advantages of technology standards, market availability of products, and risks and benefits of technology to meet business/IT needs. Maintain significant customer engagement, work planning, project management services provided to IT customers.<br>
<br>
PRIMARY DUTIES AND ACCOUNTABILITIES<br>
<ul>
<li>Undertakes complex database projects requiring specialized technical knowledge. Makes well-thought-out decisions on complex or ambiguous database issues. Comprehends the big picture.</li>
<li>Provide technical database expertise and/or solution delivery support for maintaining performance and reliability of database solutions.</li>
<li>Maintain and enhance engagement with business and IT partners to determine requirements in support of service levels and project commitments. Establishes partner relationships with clients, internal and external. Visible and accessible to clients.</li>
<li>Build and maintains knowledge of new technologies or technology opportunities and assists in development of analysis for business and/or IT clients.</li>
<li>Participate in relevant IT Architecture Domain Groups to appropriately review and plan the introduction of new database technologies. Ensures appropriate implementation of technology both within the production and the development environments. Establishes and communicates common technical goals/direction for team.</li>
<li>Provide performance evaluations, career development feedback, and recognition. Acts as a source of direction, training and guidance for less experienced staff. Approachable and leads by example.</li>
<li>Finds ways over, around and through barriers to success. Self-starter. A results-oriented doer.</li>
</ul>
