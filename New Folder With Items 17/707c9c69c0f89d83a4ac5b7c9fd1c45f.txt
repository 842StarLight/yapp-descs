<div><b>
The Role:</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
The Delivery &amp; Activation Instructional Designer will be responsible for increasing the team’s ability to effectively move our clients up the learning curve. They will work with all groups within Delivery &amp; Activation, along with Product, Marketing and Sales to ensure consistent and effective training and messaging for products, solutions, and methodologies. The instructional designer will support the materials needed for onboarding clients, as well as material related to reinforcement, expansion, and remediation.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
Who you'll work with:</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
This position will report through the Delivery &amp; Activation team, and will be closely connected with other groups including Sales, Marketing, and Product.
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>What you’ll be doing day to day:</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Create engaging and compelling content, integrating adult learning theory into course/curriculum design .</li>
<li>Work with subject matter experts to identify target audience’s training needs and select training modalities.</li>
<li>Provide coaching to other members of the delivery team on effective content delivery</li>
<li>Collaborate with other members of the broader collective team focused on education to establish and evolve company standards</li>
<li>Design and implement methods to measure effectiveness of training content</li>
<li>Improve training effectiveness by developing new approaches and techniques; making support readily available; integrating support with routine job functions</li>
<li>Update job knowledge by participating in educational opportunities; reading professional publications; maintaining personal networks; participating in professional organizations</li>
<li>Deliver training to internal stakeholder teams, as well as occasional virtual training for client partners.</li>
<li>Partner with the rest of the delivery team to decrease our clients time to value, and increase long term customer engagement.</li>
</ul>
