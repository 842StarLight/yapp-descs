<p>
Job Overview:</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Concur is seeking a proven sales professional to prospect and sell directly to State &amp; Local Governments via the telephone, internet presentations and face to face meetings within a defined geographic territory. The Sales representative will be essentially responsible for the entire sales cycle, from prospecting through booked sale but will also have support from extensive Field Marketing and Lead Generation activities. We are looking for an aggressive, success-oriented individual with a proven record of exceeding sales targets, a high level of commitment and a strong desire to be part of a top-notch sales team. Position reports to the Senior Director of Sales.<br>
</p>
<p>
Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Achieve sales objectives through sales to new customers</li>
<li>
Identify sales opportunities through direct prospecting, lead follow up, networking and partner relationships.</li>
<li>
Manage sales process through qualification, needs analysis, product demonstration, negotiation and close.</li>
<li>
Work with pre-sales team when technical or product support is required.</li>
<li>
Develop and maintain a high level of knowledge about Concur’s products and services.</li>
<li>
Develop and maintain an understanding of the territory, marketplace, competitive offerings and other business issues relevant to the position.</li>
<li>
Use effective time and territory management to maximize results.</li>
<li>
Maintain effective collaborative working relationships with Account Development and Client Services teams.</li>
<li>
Develop and maintain relationships with Concur partners and other third parties to increase opportunities.</li>
<li>
Respond to RFPs/RFIs, coordinating and organizing input from multiple sources within Concur.</li>
<li>
Document daily sales activities in sales force automation tool, prepare accurate reports and forecasts, manage pipeline and perform other tasks necessary to drive sales revenue and communicate activities to sales management.</li>
<li>
Be an active team player both on the sales team and throughout Concur to help meet company objectives.</li>
<li>
Provide feedback from field experience to internal cross-functional teams with regard to product, selling, competitive activity matters.</li>
<li>
Interacts with sales, marketing and external customers.</li>
<li>
Operates under moderate supervision with frequent reviews of work.</li>
<li>
Be aware of, and comply with, all corporate policies.</li>
</ul>
