<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>About Entertainment One
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>
<b>Entertainment One Ltd.</b> (LSE:ETO) is a global independent studio that specialises in the development, acquisition, production, financing, distribution and sales of entertainment content. The Company’s diversified expertise spans across film, television and music production and sales; family programming, merchandising and licensing; digital content; and live entertainment. Through its global reach and expansive scale, powered by deep local market knowledge, the Company delivers the best content to the world.</div>
<div>
<br>
<br>
Entertainment One’s robust network includes international feature film distribution company <b>Sierra/Affinity; Amblin Partners</b> with DreamWorks Studios, Participant Media, and Reliance Entertainment;<b> Makeready</b> with Brad Weston; unscripted television production companies <b>Whizz Kid Entertainment</b> and <b>Renegade 83</b>; live entertainment leaders<b> Round Room Entertainment</b>; world-class music labels <b>Dualtone Music Group</b> and <b>Last Gang.</b>
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The Company's rights library, valued at US$2.0 billion (as at 31 March 2018), is exploited across all media formats and includes about 80,000 hours of film and television content and approximately 40,000 music tracks; innovative music platform <b>Audio Network</b>; and award-winning emerging content and technology studio <b>Secret Location.</b>
</div>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Position Summary
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>The Executive Assistant will provide professional and confidential support to the SVP Production/Post Production in the Film and Television department. As an individual committed to excellence, you are a proven high-achiever, polished professional, having exceptional time management and organizational skills. The successful candidate will be resourceful with an energetic demeanour and a proven track record of providing outstanding executive assistance, with a passion for television and entertainment. The executive oversees post on all eOne productions and production on some television projects, experience and interest in physical production and/or post production would be an asset.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Provide professional and confidential executive support</li>
<li>
Screen incoming requests to determine priority level, including visitors, phone calls, and incoming mail</li>
<li>
Arranging conference calls and scheduling meetings</li>
<li>
Managing executive’s calendars</li>
<li>
Manage monthly expense reports for executive</li>
<li>
Manage travel arrangements and proactively check on cancellations or delays to ensure alternate arrangements are made</li>
<li>
Reviewing Outlook email and responding to immediate requests on executive’s behalf and flagging urgent emails</li>
<li>
Work closely with other assistants</li>
<li>
Conduct research on specific topics</li>
<li>
Create high caliber presentations for Town Halls and Board Meetings using PowerPoint or other software packages</li>
<li>
Reach out to BTL agents and draft appropriate availability lists of crew members for projects</li>
<li>
Maintain internal production department calendars/show binders.</li>
<li>
Manage authorizations and distribution of eOne projects via digital distribution platforms.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Key Competencies
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>1-3 years of entertainment administrative experience, including time at a major agency.</li>
<li>
Highly organized and dynamic, with ability to manage multiple tasks under tight time frames</li>
<li>
Proficiency in MS Office Suite, in particular Outlook and PowerPoint</li>
<li>
A self-starter who is able to work both independently and function well within a team</li>
<li>
Demonstrate strong independent judgment and a high degree of interpersonal effectiveness to be able to deal with stakeholders across various levels of the organization</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to work in a team environment where flexibility, keeping others informed and anticipating needs is essential</li>
<li>
Relationship builder and able to interact respectfully and confidently with internal executives, executive assistants and external contacts</li>
<li>
Ability to manage efficiently in a multi-tasked environment and ensure deadlines are met by prioritizing work flow</li>
<li>
Demonstrated discretion and confidence dealing with highly sensitive matters</li>
</ul>
