<h2>PROPERTY TRANSFER SPECIALIST</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Goodhue County is actively recruiting qualified applicants for the Property Transfer Specialist position in the Finance &amp; Taxpayer Services division.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The Property Transfer Specialist performs a variety of high-level technical appraisal support duties involving a high level of decision-making responsibility under relatively minimal supervision. Responsibilities include technical work, which involves specialized knowledge of procedures, software programs, land transfer documents and statutory requirements. This position plays a critical role in county property sales and classifications along with maintenance and understanding of the countywide property valuation records system.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The Property Transfer Specialist must possess a strong understanding of tax law, property classification and statuses as well as thorough knowledge of legal descriptions, deeds and title transfer procedures. The Property Transfer Specialist must be able to tactfully communicate with the public both verbally and in writing.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The successful candidate will have demonstrated knowledge, skills and abilities in:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Property linkage laws and related coding requirements;
</li>
<li>Analytical math skills and ability to perform complex calculations;
</li>
<li>Operating modern office equipment and related software including proficiency with the Microsoft Office suite; and,
</li>
<li>Adapting to frequent changes in environment and schedule in a fast paced work setting.
</li>
</ul>
<p>For specific job duties and additional position information, please review the job description here.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Minimum Qualifications:</b>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Associates degree in real estate, business administration, math or related field or four years of a progressively responsible position
</li>
<li>Experience with various specialized software programs
</li>
<li>Experience in real estate appraising, title or abstract work as well as government preferred
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Salary &amp; Benefits</b>
</p>
<p>County employees receive competitive pay and a generous benefit offering. The starting salary for this position is $22.81-23.94 per hour.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The County's comprehensive benefit package includes medical and dental insurance, health savings account, employer paid life insurance, short and long term disability insurance, pre-tax flexible spending accounts, retirement plan, tax-deferred compensation, generous vacation and sick leave and 12 paid holidays per year. County employees are eligible for the Public Service Student Loan Forgiveness (PSLF) program.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Application Process:</b>
</p>
<p>Interested candidates must submit a Goodhue County Employment Application. This posting will remain open until filled with preference given to applications received by <b>Wednesday, October 2, 2019</b>.</p>
