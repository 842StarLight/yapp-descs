<h2>Operations and Project Manager for Call Center</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Are you a business systems whiz? Have you helped develop sales teams or call centers? Ready to make your mark at an ascending startup and create your own career path? Join the Airflow Sleep team! Become part of a dynamic, growing company that is revolutionizing the sleep industry with breakthrough solutions for people with sleep apnea and snoring, and athletes seeking a natural performance edge.</p>
<p>We’re currently seeking a candidate who has created business workflows, deployed new software platforms and complex system integrations, and created Standard Operating Procedures.</p>
<p>Help us build the framework of our Sleep Advocate Center and support and advance the daily operations, productivity, performance and standards of the Sleep Advocates – a team of certified specialists who will help our clients make optimum use of our sleep solutions. Responsibilities include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Perform business and systems analysis work; conduct requirements gathering/analysis via information gathering sessions with Advocate Center business users. Develop supporting process flows</li>
<li>Organize the various components needed to initiate, run and conclude major projects, including developing and documenting business and functional requirements, creating user acceptance test scripts, and managing user acceptance test sessions</li>
<li>Define, manage and implement enhancements to processes and systems to create efficiencies and improve the customer and employee experience within the Advocate Center</li>
<li>Develop and update Standard Operating Procedures and Knowledge Base content</li>
<li>Conduct gap analysis between application and stated requirements and collaborate to develop new solutions</li>
<li>Support the development of training materials and training sessions with business users to improve adoption and efficient use of all systems related to the Advocate Center job functions</li>
<li>Produce summary and detailed reporting for key performance indicators, utilizing data housed in multiple databases/systems</li>
<li>Support Intraday and long-term forecasting and scheduling analytics</li>
<li>Produce ad hoc reports to answer business questions quickly and thoroughly</li>
<li>Analyze complex data, identify anomalies, and provide useable insight to business leaders</li>
<li>Maintain comprehensive inventory of all reports and associated data sources</li>
<li>Maintain and publish standard reports for management team</li>
<li>Lead and/or participate in ad-hoc projects to support efficiency, effectiveness, and growth objectives for the organization that contribute to overall business results</li>
