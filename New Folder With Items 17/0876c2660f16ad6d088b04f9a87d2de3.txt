<h2>Marketing Specialist - Partner Marketing</h2>

<div>
<p>We are a <b>young</b> and <b>unconventional</b> software company created in 2011 by experienced pricing software and pricing consulting professionals. Our <b>vision</b> is to <b>become and stay the leading SaaS pricing and CPQ solution provider</b>. We are <b>agile</b> company that brings good ideas to life quickly and is committed to <b>market driven innovation</b>. We are very flexible so our customers are from diverse industries and sizes with brands like, Avery Dennison, Cox Automotive, Iron Mountain and many others. We are a solid and sustainable company <b>which is and will be strongly growing globally</b> for the years ahead. Great time to join us and grow with us outside the borders.</p>

<p><b>
Your mission?</b></p>
<p>
Our Partner Marketing Specialist will be a versatile, multi-disciplinary marketing pro, with a special skill for understanding our business model and marketplace objectives as they relate to and align with those of our growing ecosystem of partners and strategic alliances. Co-branding, partner invested paid advertising and event sponsorship, joint content development, and co-branded sales enablement materials are all tools in the kit for this swiss army knife marketing professional!</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
What will be your responsibilities?</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Marketing team member embedded to the global Partner and Ecosystem business unit</li>
<li>Partner with Sales and Ecosystem teams to effectively enable joint programming and impact of partner efforts</li>
<li>Develop strategy for partner marketing campaigns, activities, organization, and execution</li>
<li>Develop and deploy both brand marketing and demand generation programs in collaboration with ecosystem partners</li>
<li>Maintain high quality of Pricefx brand and messaging across ecosystem partners</li>
<li>Collaborate to achieve Ecosystem business unit goals for partner portfolio and revenue growth</li>
<li>Maintain clear and consistent communication between corporate marketing and the global Ecosystem business unit.</li>
<br>
</ul>
