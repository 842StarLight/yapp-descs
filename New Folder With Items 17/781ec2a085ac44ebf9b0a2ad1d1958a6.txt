Job Description
<br><br>
The Project Manager is at the center of the customer engagement team and provides it with project execution leadership. Reporting to the Senior Practice Director, you will plan and execute projects on time and under budget, meet stakeholder expectations, and communicate risks, issues, and status. You must demonstrate those basic skills, have a strong technical aptitude and extensive experience across multiple industries. You will bring real business value to the organization by building strategic partnerships with Neal's customers.
<br><br>
Primary Responsibilities
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Collaborate with the Sales Team (Account Executives) to win the client's business: work with the client to understand their requirements, identify key solution drivers, determine scope and price</li>
<li>Conduct workshops, focus groups, interviews to educate the client and establish trust in the value of Advanced Analytics / Big Data / Machine Learning / IoT</li>
<li>Utilize project management frameworks (e.g. Agile Methodology, PMI, CRISP-DM) to plan and manage deliverables</li>
<li>Work with the Resource Manager to plan resourcing and to assign appropriately skilled team members to the project</li>
<li>Assign team members to specific tasks within the project and deploy resources from other teams (e.g. specialist consultants) to work with the client</li>
<li>Be the main point of contact for the Client, manage risk, and act as the primary escalation point for any problems in the project/relationship</li>
<li>Develop and execute a communication plan – regular team meetings, client updates and team feedback, and internal management reporting</li>
<li>Ensure the creation/implementation of deliverables meet and exceed client expectations</li>
<li>Update tools and trackers required to manage the project (e.g. hours tracking, budget tools)</li>
<li>Manage the project timeline and budget to meet client and internal profitability expectations</li>
<li>Secure references and testimonials that are critical for winning new customers</li>
<li>Guide the Accounting Department to ensure timely and accurate billing of the client</li>
<li>Expand the Company's book of business by keeping an eye open for other opportunities with the same Client and/or positioning the Company's broader portfolio of offerings</li>
<li>Enhance the Company's capabilities and knowledge through post-project analysis and recommendations</li>
</ul>
