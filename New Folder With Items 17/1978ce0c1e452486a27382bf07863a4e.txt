<h2>Optician/Patient Care Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Total Vision is a group of highly successful optometry practices that have joined together to thrive. These practices provide superior eye care results to patients throughout Southern California.
<br>We are looking for our next great Team Member to join our practice, Eye Gallery Optometry in Solana Beach, CA.
<br><br>
<b>CORE DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Assists patients with proper frame and lens selection</li>
<li>Educates patients on various options for both clear lenses, sunglasses, and any other needs the patient may have</li>
<li>Ensures proper fit and measurements for all lens types including single vision, bifocal, progressive and others</li>
<li>Educates patients on the benefits of having multiple pairs of glasses to fill all their eye care needs</li>
<li>Verifies patient insurance and explains their benefits</li>
<li>Dispenses glasses and makes sure patients knows about adaptation to new prescriptions, lens types, etc.</li>
<li>Helps patients with repairs and adjustments</li>
<li>Ensures patient phone calls are returned in a timely manner and patient is informed of any delays with eyewear purchased</li>
<li>Assists with any other tasks that need to be completed in the office</li>
</ul>
<b>POSITION REQUIREMENTS:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or general education degree (GED); and two to three years related experience and/or training; or equivalent combination of education and experience</li>
</ul>
<b>OTHER SKILLS:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Customer Service – Must exhibit excellent customer service skills, ensuring each patient interaction exceeds their expectations</li>
<li>Sales – Demonstrate strong relationship building skills with customers and ability to educate patients on their options resulting in successful purchases at the practice</li>
<li>Computer - To perform this job successfully, an individual should have knowledge of: Manufacturing Software; Payroll Systems; Spreadsheet Software (Excel); Inventory Software; Order Processing Systems; Project Management Software; Word Processing Software (Word); Electronic Mail Software</li>
<li>Language - Ability to read and interpret documents such as safety rules, operating and maintenance instructions, and procedure manuals. Ability to write routine reports and correspondence. Ability to speak effectively before groups of customers or employees of organization</li>
</ul>
<b>PHYSICAL REQUIREMENTS:</b><br>
The physical demands described here are representative of those that must be met by an employee to successfully perform the essential functions of this job.
<br><ul>
<li>Must be physically able to sit for long periods of time, read a computer screen for long periods of time, reach, and lift up to 20 lbs. Hand dexterity, and eye and hand coordination critical. Employee is subject to inside environmental conditions. Employee’s schedule could include after hour projects, as needed. Travel, as needed.</li>
</ul>
