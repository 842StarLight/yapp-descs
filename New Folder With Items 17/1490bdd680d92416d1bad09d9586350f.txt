<h2>Senior Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div><div>
<p>Position Summary</p>
<p>This role will drive key performance indicators to enhance organizational focus on contracting and pricing strategies/execution necessary to support the US Device Sales Organization and US Device Business. The Device GPO Senior Analyst is accountable for price and contract strategy development and evaluation of ICU Medical’s US Devices (including hardware, disposables, software, and services) as well as IV Solutions, and Consumables pricing execution and contracting strategies through agreements.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Essential Duties &amp; Responsibilities</p>
<ul>
<li><div>Key performance indicators will inform the business regarding contract turnaround time, most frequently negotiated contract clauses, effectiveness of pricing strategies (quote tool), the robustness of the opportunity pipeline, the effectiveness of operational improvements (example: MedNet and ESA billings), and overall performance of the contracting effort.</div></li>
<li><div>
Accountable for working with the Device Sales Organization, the US Device Business Unit, IV Solutions Business Unit and the Major Accounts Executive team to provide analytical support and identify strategic exceptions and/or maximize opportunities to grow sales and margin within defined parameters.</div></li>
<li><div>
This collaborative role will need to interface effectively with leadership in sales, services, finance, legal, and other functions to ensure optimal cross-function communication and cooperation.</div></li>
<li><div>
Work on special projects as they arise</div></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Knowledge, Skills &amp; Qualifications</p>
<ul>
<li><p>Significant knowledge base in and experience with the US medical device business, ICU Medical Customer base, and GPO landscape.</p></li>
<li><p>
Ability to communicate effectively with sales, Customers, and internal functional departments to ensure Customer centricity and speed in support of the US device and Major Account Executive sales teams.</p></li>
<li>
<p>
Strong analytical skills and creative approach to developing effective sales tools to support the business. Strong negotiation skills.</p>
