<h2>Senior Manufacturing Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>Summary</b></p>
<p>Prepare and Maintain production documentation in compliance with Design Requirements, Quality Manual and Regulatory Authorities. Manage aircraft assembly and support program development using knowledge, principals, and practices of Mechanical/Electrical/Aerospace Engineering. Evaluate and implement solutions for manufacturability, product quality, ground/flight test requirements, and system compatibility. Design and develop tools, test benches and Jigs. Develop and manage the annual capital investment plan for operation purposes. Disposition non conformities in the production process, and represent Manufacturing Engineering in the MRB (Material Review Board). Recommend and develop design improvements. Co-ordinate internal and external customer inputs in order to prepare and optimize flows, sequences and layouts pertaining to the aircraft production cycle. Guarantee technical support to all departments and suppliers. Integrate schedule, cost and resource availability factors, applying Lean Manufacturing practices assuring continuous Improvement.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Review part number procurement requirements for manufacturing processes and raw material usage to:</p>
<ul><li>consolidate raw material usage where possible to streamline US procurement</li></ul>
<ul><li>assist standardization between European and US specifications for special processes and raw materials</li></ul>
<ul><li>work with CoE to standardize testing requirements and qualification requirements for US sources for both materials and special processes</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Serve as focal point for the following activities:</p>
<ul><li>New Supplier Development</li></ul>
<ul><li>Supplier sponsored improvements in materials, processes and technologies involved in part manufacture, and coordinate cost/benefit/ROI analysis with Supply Chain and Design Organization</li></ul>
<ul><li>Project Management</li></ul>
<p>- Analysis of Plant Capacity - Budget Build Cycle</p>
<ul><li>Systems Compatibility Engineering</li></ul>
<ul><li>Departmental Budget &amp; Capital Expenditures</li></ul>
