<p><b>About Us</b></p>
<p>
Acumed is a global leader of innovative orthopaedic and medical solutions developed to improve patient care. With over three decades of experience in the orthopaedic industry, our mission is to aid the afflicted through the ingenuity of our minds, the labor of our hands and the compassion of our hearts. The company is headquartered in Hillsboro, Oregon with a global sales and distribution network supported by offices worldwide.</p>
<p><b>
PC Support Technician</b></p>
<p>
Under general supervision, the PC Support Technician provides first-line support to company employees to maintain software and hardware.</p>
<p><br>
<b>
Duties/Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provide routine analysis and problem solving of network and PC software, hardware, and peripherals.</li>
<li>Maintain courteous customer service and professional manner.</li>
<li>Contact and coordinate with hardware and software vendors for service if necessary.</li>
<li>Install computer hardware, software, and peripherals for new employees or for employee relocations.</li>
<li>Install new software packages and upgrades.</li>
<li>Aid in the development of system documentation and procedure manuals as required.</li>
<li>Conduct training sessions for company employees in the operation of software/hardware systems.</li>
<li>Assist the network team in daily network administration.</li>
<li>Oversee the uncompleted technical requests and ensure open communication with users.</li>
<li>Oversee the inventory, configuration, and maintenance of all workstations.</li>
<li>Oversee the inventory, configuration, and maintenance of Acumed's library of laptops for employees' business use.</li>
<li>Request quotes from approved vendors and submit purchase requisitions for all hardware, software and IT supplies company-wide.</li>
<li>Troubleshoot and maintenance of fax, printer and copy machines company-wide.</li>
<li>Purchase and perform inventory control for all toner cartridges used in fax, printer and copy machines throughout the company.</li>
<li>Oversee and coordinate the administration, creation, and changing of company passwords.</li>
<li>Create and modify network profiles and Outlook mailboxes. Provide training to maintain mailboxes.</li>
<li>Maintain anti-virus and Spam applications across the network.</li>
<li>Perform PC equipment and phone relocations for end users.</li>
<li>Maintenance of Active Directory</li>
<li>Administration of telephone system</li>
<li>Other duties / projects as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
<b>
Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree in Information Systems, Computer Science, or equivalent professional experience.</li>
<li>Minimum two years related experience or training.</li>
<li>A+ Certification preferred</li>
<li>Microsoft Certified System Administrator MCSA a plus.</li>
<li>Experience with MS Local Area Networks.</li>
<li>Must have good written and verbal communication in English.</li>
<li>Must have the ability to multi-task, work independently, and be a willing and cooperative team player.</li>
<li>Must be able to work patiently and cooperatively with users that have varied technical abilities/knowledge.</li>
<li>Experience with Active Directory</li>
</ul>
