<h2>Associate Digital Marketing Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Reporting to the Director of GI Marketing - Promotions, this position will uniquely span all CABOMETYX brand activities in the digital sphere. They will be responsible for setting, executing, and measuring the digital and media strategy that takes into consideration both RCC and HCC indication needs. They will be coming in at an exciting time of continuing the strong momentum of CABOMETYX in the two indications, but preparing for more to come.
</p>
<div>Position Requirements
<br>
<br>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:<br>
<ul>
<li>Set digital marketing strategies integrating multiple channels</li>
<li>Manage the media agency so that the overarching media plan accomplishes the key brand goals across each indication</li>
<li>Be the project manager for digital marketing campaigns – ensure objectives and timelines are met, and that we have the resources needed</li>
<li>Partner to develop e-learning / video programs that can be leveraged in both personal and non-personal channels</li>
<li>Propose CRM, web and other infrastructure requirements for success and manage vendors to ensure delivery</li>
<li>Monitor SEO/SEM and optimize sources of traffic</li>
<li>Explore unique CRM opportunities in both patient and physician channels</li>
<li>Analyze web traffic metrics and suggest changes to web pages to boost KPI performance</li>
<li>Manage approval of digital marketing materials with Regulatory, Legal, and Medical</li>
<li>Liaise with Marketing, Sales, Clinical and Reimbursement to ensure consistent messaging and customer experience across all media and digital assets</li>
<li>Always assessing new opportunities as they come to see overarching fit to our strategic media plan</li>
<li>Partner with analytics teams to provide a comprehensive view into all aspects of the digital / media mix</li>
</ul>
<br>
SUPERVISORY RESPONSIBILITIES:<br>
<ul><li>There are no supervisory responsibilities for this job.</li></ul>
<br>
EDUCATION/EXPERIENCE/SKILLS:<br>
Education:<br>
<ul>
<li>Master’s degree in related discipline and 8 years of related experience; or</li>
<li>BS/BA degree in related discipline and at least 10 years of related experience; or</li>
<li>Equivalent combination of education and experience</li>
</ul>
Experience:<br>
<ul><li>Experience in product marketing, field sales, or market research/analysis in the pharmaceutical or biotech industries</li></ul>
Knowledge/Skills/Abilities:<br>
<ul>
<li>Proven work experience as a Digital marketing strategist or Digital marketing manager</li>
<li>Demonstrable experience with SEO and SEM and CRM software</li>
<li>Reporting and analytics experience across media and digital channels</li>
<li>Hands on experience with online marketing tools and practices</li>
<li>Talent for blending innovation with pragmatic execution</li>
<li>Skills to network internally and externally</li>
<li>Ability to draw insight from market research, the sales force, and other sources to optimize messaging, tactical plans, and promotional activities</li>
<li>Demonstrated strategic thinking, initiative and creativity</li>
<li>Proven track record for consistently meeting or exceeding financial and/or other quantitative targets, as well as qualitative goals</li>
<li>Strong presentation, verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Understanding of FDA Regulations of pharmaceutical drug promotions and experience with Legal, Medical and Regulatory review processes</li>
<li>Some business travel is required</li>
</ul>
Job Complexity:<br>
<ul>
<li>Works on problems of diverse scope where analysis of data requires evaluation of identifiable factors. May also work on complex issues where analysis of situations or data requires an in-depth evaluation of variable factors.</li>
<li>Exercises judgment in selecting methods, techniques and evaluation criteria for obtaining results.</li>
<li>Networks with key contacts outside own area of expertise.</li>
</ul>
