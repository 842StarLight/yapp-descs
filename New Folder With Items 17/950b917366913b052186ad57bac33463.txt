<p><b>POSITION DETAILS</b></p>
<p><b><i>Position Summary</i></b></p>
<p>The Motor Design Engineer supports the design effort of motors and electro-mechanical actuation devices and works under technical supervision of a Sr. Principal Engineer or Engineering Manager.</p>
<p>This position requires interactions with other engineering associates, suppliers, and applicable departmental assignees (production, sales, quality, program management, etc.) in Concurrent Engineering environment to develop producible and economical design solutions.</p>
<p>Hands-on participation in the test laboratory and related design validation, prototype development, as well as product preparation activities.</p>
<p><b><i>Principal Activities</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Performs DC Brushed and Brushless Motor Design (Mechanical and Electrical), with types such as Frameless, Gear-motor and Brake-motor.</li>
<li>Reviews customer motor RFQ and specifications, develops preliminary design concepts and prepares technical proposals for new business opportunities</li>
<li>Prepares Design 3D models, 2D drawings, Bills of Materials (BOMs), and all design related documentation or reports.</li>
<li>Performs tolerance stack-ups, fits and clearances, and stress, strain and flow calculations as required.</li>
<li>Performs analytical and/or experimental testing as necessary to demonstrate soundness of the design.</li>
<li>As defined in the CIRCOR New Product Introduction procedures, the Design Engineer works with Sr. level Engineers, Manufacturing Engineering, Quality Engineering, Procurement, Operations and Program Management to ensure designs are efficient to manufacture and inspect, and within cost targets.</li>
<li>Investigates production problems when called upon and searches for solutions; advises and assists other department members on design issues.</li>
<li>Completes forms and relevant documentation within the Engineering department for change management, procurement, fabrication and testing.</li>
<li>Generates applicable test and assembly procedures and reports for design verification and manufacturing.</li>
<li>Initiates requests and supports fabrication of prototypes and order special materials when required, as well as any fixtures and tooling needed.</li>
</ul>
