<h2>Business Development Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Business Development Rep/Appointment Setter wanted! Team One Chevrolet, Buick, GMC Truck of Charlotte is seeking an outgoing, motivated individual to become part of our team!</p>
<ul>
<li>Full-time position</li>
<li>Benefits (Health, dental, vision, short term/long term disability, 401k with employer match)</li>
<li>Close to interstate 69, and centrally located in the mid-michigan area!</li>
<li>Paid time off, including holidays and vacation(s)</li>
<li>$40,000 average annual salary (Hourly plus commission)</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Job Title:</b> BDC Representative</p>
<p><b>SUMMARY:</b> This position answers directly to the Business Development Center Manager (BDC) while resolving various customer sales inquiries, conducting sales or service related marketing campaigns, receiving incoming phone calls, scheduling appointments. BDC Representative may be assigned duties related to either Sales, Service or the Parts Department depending training path.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES </b>include the following:<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Communicate with BDC customers and prospects via phone, internet, text message, in person and social networking sites.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Conduct telemarketing and internet related sales/service campaigns.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Schedule appointments for prospects through BDC Manager.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Meet and exceed daily goals and objectives.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Maintain accurate records of daily activities.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Make comprehensive notes in the dealership CRM reflecting all customer/prospect communications.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Maintain working knowledge of all dealership products and services.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Check e-mail frequently and provide a quality lead response immediately.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Deliver messages intended for other departments promptly.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Coordinate internet promotions with other dealership sales promotions and advertising.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Maintain an ongoing customer database to capture repeat business.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Know and understands the federal, state, and local laws which govern retail automobile sales.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Keeps abreast of new products, features, accessories, etc., and their benefits to customers.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Attend product and sales training as requested by the BDC Manager.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Attend sales meetings.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Maintain professional appearance.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
