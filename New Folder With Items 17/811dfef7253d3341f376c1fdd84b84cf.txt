Position Description
<br>-
<br><br>
We're looking for a high performing Senior Enterprise Account Manager with 8+ years experience retaining and growing revenue while delivering an exceptional level of customer service. As a Senior Enterprise Account Manager you will be responsible for profitably running a high volume of current accounts by enthusiastically ensuring client satisfaction/retention and increasing revenue.
<br><br>
<b>What You'll Do:</b><br>
-
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Manage and grow a large book of business through a high-deep and wide sales approach</li>
<li>Maintain account retention while providing exceptional customer service and developing new client relationships</li>
<li>Successfully communicate and work with third-party agencies to deliver enhanced metrics and over proportional growth</li>
<li>Build client value by providing support, information, and mentorship; researching and recommending new opportunities; recommending profit and service improvements</li>
<li>Partner with the Client Success Team to ensure clients are receiving accurate program expectations and deliver impactful QBR's</li>
<li>Exceed monthly/quarterly retention and growth goals</li>
<li>Thrive on change while remaining highly organized, optimistic, and coachable</li>
<li>Identify product improvements or new products by remaining current on industry trends, market activities, and competitors</li>
</ul>
<b>What You Bring:</b><br>
-
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>8+ years of Account Management experience (prior sales or advertising experience desired) with a consistent track record of success</li>
<li>Experience working within a team environment to over-deliver on desired results</li>
<li>Experience with high-level (Director or above) contract negotiations and the ability to successfully navigate complex organizations</li>
<li>Ability to prioritize and manage large Enterprise Accounts (3k+ employee size organizations) with a strong focus on retention and growing your customer base</li>
<li>Excellent time management and interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Proven ability to balance tough conversations with clients in a professional, customer-focused manner</li>
<li>Strong belief in a proactive rather than reactive approach to balancing clients</li>
<li>A passion for excellent customer service</li>
<li>Technically savvy and specifically skilled in SFDC &amp; Marketo</li>
<li>HR or recruiting industry experience preferred</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal communication (both verbal and written)</li>
<li>Bachelor's Degree or higher</li>
</ul>
