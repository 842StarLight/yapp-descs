<h2>Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>The Accreditation Association for Ambulatory Health Care (AAAHC) is a non-profit organization formed in 1979. We are the leader in developing standards to advance and promote patient safety, quality care, and value for ambulatory health care through peer-based accreditation processes, education, and research. A certificate of accreditation is awarded to organizations that are found to be in compliance with AAAHC standards.</p>
<p>
We are currently seeking a Marketing Manager to develop strategic marketing plans, execute their successful implementation and grow the long-term revenue, profit and market share position for assigned products and services within the context of the overall AAAHC corporate strategic plan that strengths AAAHC's brand equity and reputation. This position is the AAAHC marketing enterprise lead for and subject matter expert in the health care market landscape impacting assigned market segments. This role directs outreach through interaction across multiple marketing channels including clients, partners and internal AAAHC departments and requires competency in marketing utilizing clinical, regulatory and Standards knowledge, and health economic and outcomes research to leverage the value proposition which may include the design and launch of innovative product/service and marketing solutions. Through use of an explicit or implicit business framework, the individuals in this role link the objectives of key stakeholders, the competitive strategies required for success, the people and activities needed to deliver intended results, and the business processes that support the Marketing Manager's ability to deal with complex market and product/service scenarios.</p>
<ul>
<li>
Direct market development including identifying high-opportunity markets, securing and understanding voice of the customer, and ensuring the successful execution of market development plans to achieve growth targets</li>
<li>
Create, implement and measure the success of marketing, communications, and public relations objectives, enhancing and extending brand identity, internal and external communications, content development and art direction, conference exhibits, social media, digital marketing, vendor and contractor management, as well as website content and usability</li>
<li>
Drive market growth by guiding development and execution of AAAHC's market penetration and expansion strategy designed to drive brand awareness of AAAHC's unique value proposition and profitably grow revenue.</li>
<li>
Develop go-to-market strategy for designated segment responsibilities. Execute strategic and tactical initiatives including:</li>
<ul>
<li>
Promotions - Initiate and execute channel promotional plans, set calendar of events and conduct pre- and post-promotion analysis</li>
<li>
Tradeshows/Meetings - Attend and support business development meetings where appropriate and relevant tradeshow calendar</li>
<li>
Sales Collateral/Education - Working closely with Marketing Communications and Learning &amp; Development develop compelling core marketing collateral materials and on-going education initiatives and assessments.</li>
</ul>
<li>
Collaborate with the SVP, MBD and other team members to develop and implement a brand strategy and integrated strategic communications plan to advance AAAHC brand identity, broaden awareness of its products and services, and increase the visibility of its impact across key stakeholder audiences</li>
<li>
Coordinate and implement market research efforts and analyze market trends, integrating findings into marketing and business development, analyze existing products, services and markets in terms of the ability to compete, gain market share and grow revenue</li>
<li>
Develop and manage expense and revenue budgets for key campaigns and assigned market segments; generate AAAHC's revenue and volume forecasts</li>
<li>
Organize, develop and present current and prospective client presentations on AAAHC services, hot topics, and client performance</li>
<li>
Develop and maintain a high level of AAAHC product knowledge. Seek and analyze information on competition, market and industry trends and discern how this could impact the business and adapt to changing conditions</li>
<li>
Communicate proactively to direct manager and when needed upper management and relevant stakeholders</li>
<li>
Provide regular, detailed status updates to manager</li>
</ul>
<p>
Requirements:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor's Degree</li>
<li>
At least eight years marketing experience in positions of increasing responsibility in health care industry</li>
<li>
Ability to understand highly complex specialty products/services with a long sales cycle and develop strategic plans to find opportunities to maximize performance and growth</li>
<li>
Excellent communicator (verbal, written, presentation)</li>
<li>
Occasional evening conference calls and weekend meetings; travel &lt;25%</li>
</ul>
