Administrative Coordinator</b></div>
<div><b>
Job Description</b></div>
<div>
The Madison, Wisconsin branch of Otis Elevator Company is searching for a administrative coordinator. The successful candidate must be an ambitious, self-reliant, and focused individual to perform a wide range of functions that require excellent organizational and problem solving skills.<br>
<br>
<b>The fundamental responsibilities will include:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>
Supporting field associates with payroll, uniforms and other essentials</li>
<li>
Handles permits, purchase orders, check logs and drafts</li>
<li>
Migrate new buildings for conversion</li>
<li>
Manage fleet vehicle</li>
<li>
Provide administrative support to branch sales and operations staff</li>
<li>
Handle 3rd party /procurement company billing websites for Madison, Milwaukee, and Green Bay (ex: Roundy’s Department of corrections, Housing Authority)</li>
<li>
Assist Green Bay and Milwaukee office with some of the tasks listed above from Madison</li>
<li>
Handle Illinois state inspections / coordination with 3rd party testing agencies</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Other administrative duties may include the following:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Accounts payable</li>
<li>
Ordering office supplies</li>
<li>
Logging and mailing checks</li>
<li>
Sort/distribute incoming mail</li>
<li>
Setting up new vendors in the system</li>
<li>
Filing and departmental organization</li>
<li>
Provide administrative support to branch sales and operations staff</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Education:</b></div>
<ul><li>
High School diploma require. Bachelor’s degree preferred</li></ul>
<div><b>
Experience/Qualifications:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Excellent computer skills, with proficiency in Microsoft Office - Word, Outlook, Excel, and aptitude to learn company-specific software (LINX, mainframe, JDE)</li>
<li>
The ideal candidate will have exceptional written and verbal communication skills, be detailed oriented, and organized</li>
<li>
Would possess skills and experiences related to the elevator industry, construction trades or property management</li>
<li>
Demonstrated proficiency in planning, organizing, prioritizing and multi-tasking is required</li>
<li>
Must be able to manage workflow efficiently, be self-reliant, show initiative, good judgment, be a team player and have the ability to handle sensitive and confidential information.</li>
</ul>
