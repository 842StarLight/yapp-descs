<p>Content Marketing Manager
</p>
<p>Berkeley, California</p>
<p>
About us</p>
<p>
Headquartered in Berkeley, CA, Bellwether Coffee is a venture-backed, high-growth tech company working hard—alongside other game-changers—to positively transform the coffee industry, making it more accessible and sustainable for our planet and our communities. We’re a diverse team of coffee industry veterans, entrepreneurs, engineers, technicians, human-centered designers, and people-focused rule-breakers who have a passion for making the “impossible” a reality.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
What we do</p>
<p>
Our revolutionary electric, ventless, zero-emissions commercial coffee roaster uses clean technology and does not require gas lines or expensive ventilation. It’s the most consistent and controllable roaster available, the lowest carbon footprint commercial roaster ever made, and was designed by coffee people who want a better future for coffee roasting. Our roasting platform allows Bellwether customers to easily and responsibly source green coffee from incredible coffee farms around the world, expertly roast using artisan-crafted roast profiles (or create their own), and share fresh, delicious coffee just the way their customers like it.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
About you</p>
<p>
You are a crucial member of the team. You’re an integrity-driven person who enjoys solving problems, rapidly adapts to change, and thoughtfully shares your progress and ideas with the team. You have a sense of urgency with everything you do without compromising quality and detail. You’re naturally empathetic and deeply committed to the equitable treatment of all people everywhere.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Our Content Marketing Manager is responsible for continuously producing exceptional content and deliverables to support internal and external stakeholders. This role includes strategy + design + copywriting + curation of visual assets (photo, video, etc.)</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
The person who joins our team will embody the Bellwether brand and share our drive to make the coffee industry more fair and equitable while improving the carbon footprint of an entire industry. They will be an outstanding writer/editor, highly organized, persistent, naturally optimistic, enjoy working as part of a close-knit team, thrive in an open and collaborative environment, and have experience managing others.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
About the position</p>
<ul>
<li>Report directly to our Vice President of Marketing</li>
<li>Work alongside our Growth Marketing Manager, and will work with our Product Marketing Manager, once hired</li>
<li>Opportunity to help build Content Marketing Team. Future hires to manage:
<ul>
<li>International Content Marketing Specialist</li>
<li> Content Marketing Specialist</li>
<li> Blog Manager</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Key Responsibilities – What success looks like</p>
<p>
Continuously produce exceptional content and deliverables to support internal and external stakeholders, including but not limited to:</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Marketing:</p>
<ul>
<li>Produce content regularly to create new legit leads: Website additions (ie. new pages) + updates to existing pages; social posts + campaigns + ads; press support; influencer content; print + digital advertising; email marketing; educational resources (eg. e-books); customer case studies; nurture marketing of existing leads</li>
<li>Work with designers and copywriters to develop most relevant content for stakeholder + persona + product</li>
<li>Develop strategy to scale output of content. Work with Growth Marketing Manager to understand, through data, what content is working and what’s not working</li>
<li>Keep team up-to-date on content marketing trends</li>
</ul>
<p>Sales: Produce collateral relevant to product + persona + channel; content support for different leads statuses, email marketing, etc.</p>
<p>
Product: Product Marketing materials for new + existing products; support bringing new products, features/benefits, and/or services to market, etc.</p>
<p>
CX: Content support for customer on-boarding, training, new product introduction, support materials, etc.</p>
<p>
Day-to-day</p>
<ul>
<li>Supports internal and external marketing stakeholders: Meets with fellow Marketing team members, Sales, Product, and CX teams to understand what content resonates, what doesn’t resonate, and what content is needed or might be missing</li>
<li>Fuels PR and influencer campaigns</li>
<li>Continuously generating exceptional, relevant content, and updating existing content, for all personas + products across all channels</li>
<li>Assisting with driving new legit sales leads by understanding what content attracts visitors to our website and social followers</li>
</ul>
<p>About you. You:</p>
<ul>
<li>Completely embody Bellwether brand</li>
<li>Demonstrate a high level of creativity, and a proven ability to scale</li>
<li>Take pride in your superior attention to detail, and being extremely well organized</li>
<li>Have great project management skills and a long history of completing projects with aplomb</li>
<li>You’re comfortable working with cross-functional teams, and being the person others will rely on every single day (will always have a heavy workload)</li>
</ul>
<p>Qualifications</p>
<ul>
<li>Currently or recently working as a Content Marketing Manager in an intensely high-volume, fast-paced environment and enjoy that velocity</li>
<li>Minimum of 7 years experience in a related area as an individual contributor; 1-to-3 years supervisory experience is preferred</li>
<li>Top-notch Project Management skills: You’re well aware of the number of content campaigns you’re working on and are open to sharing how you’ve optimized your favorite P.M. software</li>
<li>You use both the left and right sides of your brain: creative writing and coordinate your material in a highly organized way
</li>
<li>In a word, you’re brilliant: Smarter than most; a clever and fast writer/editor with a long list of sample projects to discuss</li>
<li>You’re curious: You keep your finger on the pulse of the latest/greatest tactics; you’re willing to investigate, innovate, and take calculated risks</li>
<li>Bonus points for candidates with coffee industry knowledge</li>
</ul>
