<li>We have an opportunity for an experienced <b>Apartment Community Manager in Las Vegas NV!</b> The Community Manager will be responsible for the overall management and success of a 390 + unit asset. Our National Property Management and Development Company, WM Builders specializes in the development and management of luxury multi-family mid-rise and high-rise buildings. If you are a dynamic individual, undaunted by day to day challenges in our industry, with an exemplary work ethic, we would like to hear from you!<br>
<br>
<b>RESPONSIBILITIES:</b><br>

<li>Efficiently manages 5 Day notices, 30-day notices, evictions, move-ins and move-outs.</li>
<li>Manages vendor’s scheduling and billings. Coordinates with maintenance and vendors to ensure timely turnover of apartments after move-out.</li>
</ul>
</li>

<li>Reports all liability and property incidents to the corporate office immediately.</li>
</ul>
</li>

<li>Excellent closing skills.<br>

</ul>
</li>
