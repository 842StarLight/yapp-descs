<h2>Premium Seating & Partnerships Coordinator (Part-Time) - Angel Of The Winds Arena</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>This position is based at our <b>Angel of the Winds Arena</b>. Angel of the Winds Arena is a multi-purpose complex, in Everett, Washington, designed and developed by the Everett Public Facilities District. The 10,000-seat venue has hosted a variety of concerts and other types of performances. Angel of the Winds Arena’s mission statement is to “consistently be the perfect partner, through the provision of flexible gathering spaces by an award-winning team dedicated to delivering extraordinary fan and client experiences.” You can learn more about our venue here: http://www.angelofthewindsarena.com/default.ashx?p=1097</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The <b>Premium Seating &amp; Partnerships Coordinator</b> will assist the Spectra Partnerships team in the servicing and retention of all current and future Club, Premium Seating, Suite, and Advertising clients at Angel Of The Winds Arena in Everett, WA. The Coordinator is responsible for timely and accurately processing all ticket orders/payments for Premium Seating clients. The position will oversee logistics with in-house building partners to ensure exceptional service for our premium/group clients in all areas of their visit. This position will also oversee the contracting, fulfillment, and tracking of advertising agreements. Additionally they will assist in prospecting new business clients and sales opportunities for Premium Seating areas, sponsorships, and partnerships.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Spectra, an industry leader in sports, entertainment and venue management, is dedicated to recruiting and developing individuals with the skills, experience, desire, and values to contribute to the continued growth and success of our organization. Together, with our 250+ sports &amp; entertainment venue partners, we transform events into experiences! These experiences create excitement, turn heads, and make memories. Come Join Us! You can learn more about Spectra at <i>www.spectraexperiences.com/</i>
</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Act as representative of the Spectra Partnerships team for all current premium seating accounts</li>
<li>
Assist in overseeing premium seating areas during events at the arena</li>
<li>
Coordinate VIP will-call for all events and ensure smooth distribution of tickets</li>
<li>
Building liaison between all premium seating clients and Spectra Food Services &amp; Hospitality personnel</li>
<li>
Responsible for creating and sending event announcements to premium seating clients</li>
<li>
Process all ticket orders and payments for premium seating clients in an accurate and timely manner</li>
<li>
Facilitate distribution of event tickets to premium seating clients; as well as season tickets coordination/distribution for in-house tenants (Everett Silvertips)</li>
<li>
Pursue and book suite rentals, process payments and provide information regarding upcoming rental opportunities to the rental contact list</li>
<li>
Assist in new client prospecting for both premium seating and corporate sponsorship</li>
<li>
Create and administer contracts as directed for all department clients</li>
<li>
Summarize advertising deal requirements on compliance forms and share with relevant in-house building partners</li>
<li>
Work with advertising partners and sign companies or otherwise along with in-house building partners to fulfill all sponsorship contracted assets</li>
<li>
Update Sales Tracker with all contracted business</li>
<li>
Track and monitor client relationships with CRM</li>
<li>
Maintain list of available sponsorship inventory</li>
<li>
Follow up with department clients on outstanding accounts</li>
<li>
Manage and appropriately file all department data flow</li>
<li>
Track usage of all department tickets and suites</li>
<li>
Communicate problems and propose solutions to the Director of Partnership Sales for proficient client servicing</li>
<li>
Submits regular activity reports and performs related duties as required</li>
<li>
Develop and maintain productive relationships with both established clients and foster relationships with prospective and new clients</li>
<li>
Create and maintain Premium Seating files, handbooks, and templates</li>
<li>
Exercise discretion and utilize independent decision making during events that will best represent Angel Of The Winds Arena and serve the client</li>
<li>
Provide excellent customer service assistance to internal/external clients and exemplify the mission and ethics of Spectra Venue Management and Angel Of The Winds Arena</li>
<li>
Perform other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
