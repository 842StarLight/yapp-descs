<b>JOB SUMMARY:</b><br>
Responsible for managing the activities of construction projects, ensuring construction schedules, safety, SWPPP, quality standards, and customer satisfaction are maintained throughout the construction process.<br>
<br>
<b>PRIMARY RESPONSIBILITIES</b>:<br>
<ul>
<li>Validate schedule progression and adherence.</li>
<li>Work with team to share feedback and improve planning activities, including, but not limited to:</li>
<li>Vendor coaching and performance feedback through schedule and quality recordables</li>
<li>Design quality, materials management, budget accuracy, and take-off accuracy</li>
<li>Manage the customer experience throughout the construction process through close of house</li>
<li>Collaborate with trade partners throughout construction process to improve quality and efficiency</li>
<li>Build sustainable relationships of trust with the homeowner through open and interactive communication</li>
<li>Interface with Sales personnel to manage neighborhood and customer activities and referrals</li>
<li>Ensure job sites adhere to company safety and SWPPP standards</li>
<li>Assist in resolving issues/conflicts related to daily construction activities (e.g., vendor contracts, work orders, job progress, design)</li>
<li>Authorize payment for materials received and work completed</li>
<li>Ensure trade partner work is completed on time and within defined standards for quality</li>
<li>Collaborate with Process Improvement, Resource Planning and Purchasing in construction processes</li>
<li>Inspect/validate workmanship and product quality to conform to Company standards.</li>
</ul>
