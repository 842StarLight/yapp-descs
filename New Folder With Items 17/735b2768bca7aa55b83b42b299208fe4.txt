<div>Position Summary</div>
<div>Director, Product Management will report to the Sr. Director Product Management for Wireless Products. Candidate for this role should demonstrate passion for product management, very strong technical, communication &amp; leadership skills, creativity, cross functional team work to deliver competitive product while keeping customer first attitude.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>This person will be directly responsible for managing WLAN controllers (physical and virtual), data plane gateway and infrastructure services that are focused on Enterprise and Service Provider customers, primarily from software features perspective. It means maintaining a well-defined roadmap, very close interactions with engineering to clearly define the product requirements via PRDs and in person discussions, and managing the entire product lifecycle through idea inception, product execution, and end of life.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>In addition, this role requires strong collaboration on positioning, content development &amp; presentation, go-to-market with wireless and wired product management, product and technical marketing, training, business development, Support and the Sales teams. This person will be the product evangelist and champion throughout the company. Additionally, this person will have frequent interactions with customers and partners and/or some amount of travel to their locations worldwide.</div>
