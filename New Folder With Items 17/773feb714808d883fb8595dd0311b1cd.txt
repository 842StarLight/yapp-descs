Position Detail:</b></p>
<p>
Collections Representatives receive paid training and on-going training in the aspects of performing recovery of past due balances owed to our clients. Position entails managing an inventory of accounts, Skip Tracing, and Contacting the Past Due Parties to negotiate repayment, or determine if Litigation will be needed to ensure repayment to our clients.</p>
