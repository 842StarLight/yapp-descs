<h2>Senior Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
Act as a contributor to the consultative sales of security products and services in the Northeastern Region. Identify and qualify prospects by networking, office/site visits, and cold calling. We also create lead generation through direct mail and partner relationships. Build and maintain new revenue within established account base and through newly acquired accounts. Maintain a pipeline of qualified prospects sufficient to meet monthly goals. Manage entire sales process, from initial point of contact to close and implementation.</p>

<li>5+ years of successful experience in technology sales</li>

<li>Minimum 5 years consultative, solutions based sales experience in information technology, network/data security, or wireless markets.</li>
<li>Experience in proactive outbound telephone sales with aggressive close ratios</li>
<li>Formal solution sales training</li>
<li>Entrepreneurial spirit – takes pride in ownership, doer, thinks outside the box, welcomes new challenges</li>
<li>Detailed oriented and extremely organized, yet flexible enough to adjust style to a fast paced environment</li>
<li>Competent computer skills a must; Contact management software experience preferred</li>
<li>Fluent with MS Word, and Excel</li>
<li>Proven track record of consistently exceeding quota selling into the enterprise market.</li>
<li>Experience in developing new business and a multi-million dollar pipeline quickly.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to build, nurture, and leverage relationships with C-level executives, using a consultative approach.</li>
<li>Superior networking, negotiation, and relationship management skills required.</li>
<li>Highly motivated overachiever with the ability to work independently and manage long sales cycles</li>
