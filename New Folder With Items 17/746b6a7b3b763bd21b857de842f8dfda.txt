<h2>Beverage Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">The Inn at Pound Ridge in Westchester Seeking Beverage Manager!
<br><br>
The perfect Beverage Manager candidate will possess "hands-on" fine dining service skills, attention to detail, commitment to hospitality, positive team-player attitude and the proven ability to be a leader.
<br><br>
<b>Prerequisites and characteristics:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Advanced knowledge of food &amp; beverage, Avero, Compeat (or other restaurant management accounting inventory software), Open Table/Resy and Aloha required.</li>
<li>Candidates must possess at least three years of fine dining experience; and at least 2 years experience as Beverage Manager.</li>
<li>FULL AVAILABILITY- daytime hours, evening hours and weekends.</li>
<li>Must be friendly, engaging, gracious and have the ability to focus and connect with all guests at all levels, as well as with employees, and exceed their expectations.</li>
</ul>
<b>Responsibilities (these are not all inclusive):</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Attends and contributes to the weekly/monthly Food and Beverage departmental meetings.</li>
<li>Oversees and assists in service in accordance with the requirements and practices of the restaurant.</li>
<li>Responsible for taking the lead in growing wine/beverage sales in the restaurant.</li>
<li>Maintains awareness to customer focus and attention to detail.</li>
<li>Work the floor during service with an emphasis on beverage sales. Up-sell at all possible opportunities.</li>
<li>Train staff on quality level of service regarding body language, eye contact and proper verbiage and seek to positively enhance the guest experience throughout service.</li>
<li>Maintain thorough knowledge of all wine, spirits, coffees, teas, etc.</li>
<li>Responsible for ordering all bar products-liquor, beer, wine and consumables to par, as required.</li>
<li>Perform weekly inventory of all beverage department products, as required.</li>
<li>Research and conduct comprehensive beverage trainings for FOH staff.</li>
<li>Oversee maintenance/cleanliness of all decanters, wine stations and all storage areas pertaining to beverage.</li>
<li>Open &amp; close the restaurant as needed.</li>
<li>Performs other related duties and special projects as assigned.</li>
</ul>
Look forward to hearing from you!
<br><br>
"Chef Jean Georges introduces a casually elegant restaurant ensconced in the picturesque town of Pound Ridge, New York. Here, Jean Georges cultivates a truly seasonal, farm-to-table cuisine, with ingredients sourced from the Hudson Valley and New England, whenever possible. Constructed in 1833 as a residence and serving later as an inn, the building has been painstakingly renovated to preserve as many of the original materials as possible, including four working fireplaces."
