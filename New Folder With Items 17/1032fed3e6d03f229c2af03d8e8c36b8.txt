<h2>Market Development Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>COMPANY OVERVIEW:</b></p>
<p>
AAMCO is the leading provider of transmission, engine, and drive system automotive repairs in North America with a network of approximately 600 stores under the nationally recognized AAMCO brand. The Company has over fifty years of recognized leadership and is extremely well positioned for exciting growth in the coming decades.</p>
<p><b>
POSITION OVERVIEW:</b></p>
<p>
ADS's growth plans have created the need to hire a new Market Development Specialist ("MDS"). This role will be responsible for sourcing and driving franchise sales. The MDS will play a critical role in growing the Company's store footprint by helping new franchise owners gain the opportunity to build their own business.</p>
<p>
This Market Development Specialist position represents an exciting and challenging opportunity to join a team with a large growth opportunity in front of it. The successful candidate will receive outstanding experience, work directly with the Company's leadership team, and make a dramatic impact on the Company's growth and development.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
JOB RESPONSIBILITIES AND DUTIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Achieves all goals/objectives of franchise sales and development plan within a targeted market</li>
<li>
Follows a disciplined process to close franchise deals according to expected timelines</li>
<li>
Cultivates expansions and/or resale opportunities for existing franchisees</li>
<li>
Maintains accurate and timely records in CRM/pipeline reports</li>
<li>
Performs and/or hosts meetings with prospective candidates</li>
<li>
Responds to all requests for additional franchise information according to required timeline</li>
<li>
Serves as the key market development functional expert</li>
<li>
Positively and professionally represents the AAMCO brand and value proposition</li>
</ul>
