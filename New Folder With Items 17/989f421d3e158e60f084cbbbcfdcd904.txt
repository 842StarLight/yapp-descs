<h2>Field Technical Support Rep V</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Within the family there is a range of technical and managerial customer on-site functions. The family of jobs is focused on managing the onsite delivering of software services, pre-sales, post-sales or service delivery support, installation and configuration for customer environments. The jobs solve various business systems, networking and applications problems for standard industry servers, specialized or complex clustered environments. Deliver reactive and proactive activities according to Service Level Agreement to ensure customer satisfaction and loyalty. Provides direct post-sales systems on site technical support for reactive and proactive customers. Each position maintains a high level of customer satisfaction by ensuring clear business and technical solution alignment, clarifying customer needs and ensuring that they are met. Jobs are responsible for company Total Customer Experience as well as the revenue growth, profitability, and account retention.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b><i>
Responsibilities:
</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ensure high level Customer satisfaction by clarifying Customer needs and ensuring that they are met.</li>
<li>
Handle Customer-relation problems promptly and appropriately, provide guidance, escalate issues according to established procedures.</li>
<li>
Provide software service, pre- sales, post-sales or service delivery support.</li>
<li>
For Mission Critical Customers, provide proactive and some reactive services.</li>
<li>
Monitor operation performance and work on corrective action plans (if needed).</li>
<li>
Provide input about Customers business and industry trend information into Account Support Plan.</li>
<li>
Mentor /Coach new hires on work process and procedures.</li>
<li>
Responsible for account growth and retention.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>

<li>
Experience with support of full range of company products in Customer base.</li>
<li>
Proactive account management experience must meet HA/MCS qualification</li>
<li><div><div><div><div><p><b>

</i></b></p>
