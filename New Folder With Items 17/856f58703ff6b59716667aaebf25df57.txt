<h2>Marketing Automation Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>As the Marketing Automation Specialist, you will work collaboratively with Marketing and internal When I Work team members to own the planning, architecting, and successful execution of marketing automation processes and programs in support of When I Work's acquisition, retention, and expansion efforts. You are the marketing technology &amp; systems expert and enjoy making sense of complex data, systems, &amp; processes. Your curiosity inspires you to experiment, test, tweak and you aren't afraid to try things that may fail quickly. You see ambiguity as an opportunity to create and have a desire to lean into a high impact position. You are motivated by a strong, innovative, and passionate work culture and are constantly searching for ways to improve, inspire others, and add value.</p>
<p><b>
What You'll Be Doing</b></p>
<p>
This role is responsible for leveraging marketing automation tools to plan, design, execute, and optimize marketing campaigns. You will build and launch programs in Marketo ensuring campaigns target the correct audience at the right cadence with proper scoring on behalf of Program Managers (lifecycle, paid media, content, etc.). You will execute on the marketing automation strategy, roadmap, and vision.</p>
<ul>
<li>
Surface opportunities to improve marketing operational effectiveness based on funnel data. Own &amp; update lead scoring and lifecycle stages</li>
<li>
Master the tools and processes, become the primary contact and subject matter expert for all Marketo-related questions across the organization including training and enabling cross-functional teams to leverage Marketo's ability to connect to other systems &amp; tools</li>
<li>
Manage and support Salesforce / Marketo integration</li>
<li>
Deliver &amp; analyze actionable dashboards for all marketing initiatives (acquisition, conversion, and expansion efforts)</li>
<li>
Maintain &amp; optimize the Revenue Cycle Model in collaboration with the Sales team</li>
<li>
Work collaboratively with Sales leadership and sales team members on a continual basis, including weekly reviews.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with Sales and Marketing team members to optimize lead scoring and ensure scoring accuracy across all channels, programs, and initiatives</li>
<li>
Ensure data accuracy within Marketo and help investigate issues as they arise</li>
<li>
Contribute to the strategy, management, and performance of our marketing automation ecosystem</li>
<li>
Contribute to process improvement projects to drive team efficiencies</li>
</ul>
