<h2>Digital Marketing Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>BeverageFactory.com is a family owned business founded in 1997 and based in San Diego, California. We offer unique products to make, store and dispense your favorite beverages! We bring nearly two decades of experience and knowledge from the brewing and beverage industry online. From our extensive line up of kegerators and wine storage units, to our water dispensers and draft beer parts, you'll find our specialized selection of beverage equipment second to none.</p>
<p>Our digital marketing specialist will embody our company values, possess digital marketing expertise, leadership skills, team work, problem solving skills, excellent communication skills and be a mastery of SEO.</p>
<p>Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Set digital marketing strategies using all necessary tools (e.g. website, emails, social media and blogs)</li>
<li>Research products, services and current strategies to identify new opportunities</li>
<li>Analyze web traffic metrics and suggest solutions to boost web presence</li>
<li>Monitor SEO/SEM, marketing and sales performance metrics to forecast trends</li>
<li>Build strong clients relationships through social media interaction</li>
<li>Keep up-to-date with our audience’s preferences and proactively suggest new campaigns</li>
<li>Liaise with teams to optimize customer experience and ensure brand consistency</li>
<li>Identify advertising needs</li>
<li>Establish best practices in digital marketing</li>
<li>Stay up-to-date with digital technologies developments</li>
</ul>
<p>Requirements:</p>
<ul>
<li>Proven work experience as a digital marketing strategist or digital marketing manager</li>
<li>Demonstrable experience with SEO/SEM and CRM software</li>
<li>Experience implementing and optimizing Google Adwords campaigns</li>
<li>Solid knowledge of web analytics tools like Google Analytics</li>
<li>Hands on experience with online marketing tools and practices</li>
<li>Familiarity with web design and HTML</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Strong analytical and project management skills</li>
</ul>
<p>Qualifications</p>
<p>1-2 years’ professional experience in a digital or traditional marketing agency or 2-3 relevant internships in a similar environment.</p>
<p>Experience with SEO tools such as SEMRush, Ahrefs, AdWords (keyword tool), and Google Analytics.</p>
<p>Knowledge of online retail (eCommerce) businesses and website migrations or re-platforms a huge plus.</p>
<p>Knowledge of SEO and the “white hat” way.</p>
<p>Knowledge of Search Engine Optimization (SEO) strategies and techniques.</p>
<p>Ability to set and execute strategies to improve the organic rankings of Visiture's SEO clients.</p>
<p>Onsite optimization related to page titles, URLs, redirects, internal link structures, SEO copy editing, sitemaps review/maintenance.</p>
<p>Keyword research to identify and discover new targeting keywords and explore content strategy.</p>
<p>Conduct market research and competitive analysis to assess strength, weakness, opportunities and threats of online marketing strategy.</p>
<p>Monitor, track, and analyze ranking performance on an ongoing basis. Ability to pivot/optimize SEO campaigns as necessary.</p>
<p>Keep abreast of the latest SEO tools and techniques.</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Salary: $50,000.00 to $75,000.00 /year</p>
