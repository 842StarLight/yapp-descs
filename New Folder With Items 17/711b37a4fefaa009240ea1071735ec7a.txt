<h2>Theatre Operations Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>
<div>Come join the Marcus Team! We are looking for an enthusiastic and engaging <b>Theatre Operations Manager </b>to enhance our fun, fast-paced theatre environment by ensuring the best possible entertainment experience for our guests.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>A successful candidate will be able to lead a staff of associates dedicated to providing guests with fun and memorable entertainment experience. As a theatre operations manager you will manage, train, and develop Theatre and Restaurant staff to ensure prestigious service resulting in high sales, profit, and returning guests in a fast-paced entertainment complex. Now is the time to join the growing Marcus Theatres Organization!</div>
<br>
<div>
<br>
<b>Your Future Responsibilities as a Member of our Team:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Providing service that is friendly, helpful and fast</li>
<li>Maintaining facilities that are clean, safe and in good repair</li>
<li>Providing an experience that is comfortable, distraction-free and picture-perfect</li>
<li>Serving fresh, appetizing and properly prepared food and beverages</li>
<li>Guaranteeing associates meet and exceed guest-service standards</li>
<li>Ensuring proper staffing in each area of the theatre</li>
<li>Performing daily opening and closing operational duties</li>
<li>Reviewing financial numbers on a regular basis and make operations adjustments, as necessary</li>
</ul>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<br>
At Marcus Theatres we strive to create Magical Movie Memories for our guests. As a <b>Theatre Operations Manager at our Marcus Ridge Cinema location in New Berlin, WI</b> you will become a part of our family! All ready to get started? Ok! You will need to demonstrate the following abilities:</div>
<br>
<div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Associates or Bachelor’s degree in hospitality, culinary arts, business or commensurate experience.</li>
<li>Two years’ experience working in a restaurant</li>
<li>Ability to obtain any necessary alcohol service and licensing requirements</li>
<li>Three years of experience at a national chain or restaurant group preferred</li>
<li>Effective written and oral communication skills</li>
<li>Strong analytical skills</li>
<li>Comfortable communicating and working with guests, supervisors, peers, subordinates or vendors</li>
<li>Experience using Microsoft suite in business setting</li>
<li>Experience using Taleo Recruitment software and Kronos scheduling preferred</li>
</ul>
