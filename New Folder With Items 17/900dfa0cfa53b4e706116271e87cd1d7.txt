In this role, you will be responsible for creating professional quality plans and technical drawings in support of proposals and RFP responses by collaborating with ChargePoint engineers and project managers, as well as taking input from outside parties. You will be a product expert and ensure that the material produced accurately incorporates product specifications.
<br><br>
<b>Typical drawings include:</b> site layout and site engineering, electrical (one-line and three-line), operational workflow drawings (parking layout, traffic flow, vehicle parking orientation and vehicle charging inlet location, EVSE placement and cable reach)
<br><br>
The applicant must be self-motivated and able to effectively manage their time.
<br><br>
<b>Travel requirement:</b> up to 25% travel within the United States and Canada.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Collaborate with sales team, engineers, surveyors, project managers, and outside contractors to collect site and operational data to produce professional quality drawings, schematics, and maps.</li>
<li>Prepare and review rough sketches (concept drawings) and review them along with the engineering team</li>
<li>Create detailed designs with computer-aided design (CAD) and 3D modelling software based on the approved concept drawings</li>
<li>Identify potential operational or product-related issues and propose solutions</li>
<li>Ensure final designs comply with regulations and quality standards</li>
<li>Maintain expert knowledge of ChargePoint products and specifications as well as general knowledge of standard electrical equipment, such as transformers and switchgear for inclusion in plans and drawings</li>
<li>Maintain knowledge of the National Electrical Code (NEC) and relevant state and local codes, specifically related to Electric Vehicle Supply Equipment (EVSE)</li>
<li>Visit jobsites to perform first-hand assessments to incorporate into plans and drawings</li>
</ul>
<b>Desired Skills and Experience:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>BSc in Engineering, Architecture or appropriate degree from a technical college</li>
<li>Certification from the American Design Drafting Association (ADDA) is a plus</li>
<li>Proven work experience as a drafter or similar role</li>
<li>Hands-on experience with computer-aided design (CAD) software and 3D modelling tools</li>
<li>Must be detail oriented, creative, and have strong math and technical skills</li>
<li>Ability to meet deadlines</li>
</ul>
