<p><b>Call Center Specialist</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>We are looking for a candidate that has experience working in the travel industry, preferably travel agents, to join our call center as a travel consultant. This is a great position for eager sales and service candidates looking for exciting and interesting work.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Duties and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Handle incoming calls and email in support of our online websites and printed brochures</li>
<li>Provide superior support and guidance to our customers and passengers</li>
<li>Learn and build destination knowledge of Europe, Latin America and other destinations</li>
<li>Focus on selling vacation packages</li>
<li>Build customer loyalty by cultivating relationships and providing outstanding support</li>
<li>Exceed the customers' expectations through excellent customer service and attention to detail</li>
<li>Perform other work-related duties as required and assigned
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Strong sales and service skills, required - 2 years' experience</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills- both written and verbal, required</li>
<li>Call center work experience, preferred</li>
<li>Knowledge of travel and tourism</li>
<li>Experience in travel industry and using Amadeus, a plus</li>
<li>Strong computer skills, required</li>
<li>Ability to pay attention to detail and keep organized while working in a busy office environment</li>
</ul>
