<h2>Account Manager (Small Market)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>About NCR
</b></p>
<p>NCR Corporation (NYSE: NCR) is a leading software- and services-led enterprise provider in the financial, retail, hospitality, telecom and technology industries. NCR is headquartered in Atlanta, Ga., with 34,000 employees and does business in 180 countries. NCR is a trademark of NCR Corporation in the United States and other countries.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Consistently reach and exceed assigned sales quota each month.
</li>
<li>Sell core HR solution bundles including; Payroll, HR, Hiring/Onboarding and Time &amp; Attendance to target accounts.
</li>
<li>Execute effective cold calling and lead generation strategy on a daily and weekly basis, meeting or exceeding daily/weekly KPIs as assigned by sales leadership.
</li>
<li>Develop a network with key decision makers in an assigned/designated territory
</li>
<li>Generate referrals by identifying key centers of influence and developing relationships; uncover the needs and challenges for them and their business clients.
</li>
<li>Effectively position and recommend the right NCR products and solutions.
</li>
<li>Collaborate with implementation and service counterparts, establishing and maintaining exceptional customer relations.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Experience You Need:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree or commensurate experience
</li>
<li>0-3 years direct inbound or outbound sales, or related, experience
</li>
<li>Excellent systems literacy knowledge in MS Office
</li>
<li>Goal-oriented drive and demonstrated ability to reach and exceed monthly/annual sales quotas.
</li>
<li>Disciplined approach to your day and week; focused and well-organized with highly effective time management skills
</li>
<li>Demonstrated cold calling sales ability, with assertive, positive and persistent style
</li>
<li>Proven customer facing and relationship building skills
</li>
<li>Strong verbal and written communication skills
</li>
<li>Ability to work independently but also as a collaborative member of a team
</li>
<li>Self-confident and Professionally competitive
</li>
<li>Capacity to work under pressure
</li>
<li>Strong work ethic
</li>
<li>Committed to building a career in Sales and/or Sales Leadership
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Preferred Qualifications:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Prior business-to-business sales experience with a proven track record in selling and presentation skills, prospecting, and territory management - particular interest in experience selling iSolved or other full-suite HCM solutions.
</li>
<li>Experience with Salesforce.com or similar CRM system
</li>
</ul>
