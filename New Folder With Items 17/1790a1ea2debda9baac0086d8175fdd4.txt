<h2>SEO CONTENT MARKETING SPECIALIST</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Hanes Supply, Inc. is a well-established full -line industrial manufacturer of slings and contractor supplier specializing in lifting solutions. Hanes Supply offers a comprehensive and diverse product line to its customers including: rigging, fall protection, power tools, fasteners, hand tools, construction and safety equipment. Headquartered in Buffalo, NY, Hanes has five branches located throughout the Northeast.</p>
<p><b><i>
Position Summary</i></b> <b>:</b> SEO/Content Marketing Specialist will be in charge of maintaining SEO/SEM for several websites and social media channels. This will include creating and publishing content for the web, social media and email on a regular basis, as well as driving sales through pay-per-click campaigns. Additional responsibilities will include managing cooperative advertising funding from third-party vendors/supply partners.</p>
