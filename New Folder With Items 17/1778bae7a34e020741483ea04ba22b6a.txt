<h2>General Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Operating since 2003, Mina Group currently manages more than 35 concepts around the world. At Mina Group, impeccable service is not just our ideal, it is ingrained in everything we do. Rooted deep in our foundation is the concept of Kaizen, the art of making great and lasting change in small steady increments. By embodying our values of adaptability and fluidity, we cultivate a whole new sense of hospitality that is uniquely Mina. #ZR</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
General Manager</b></p>
<p>
Responsible for all aspects of the management of restaurant StripSteak in Honolulu, Hawaii (the "Company"). Work in conjunction with MG-Hila, LLC and Host Partners policies. Oversee and coordinate the planning, organizing, training and leadership necessary to achieve stated objectives in sales, costs, employee retention, guest service &amp; satisfaction, food quality, cleanliness and sanitation.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
GENERAL EXPECTATIONS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Act with integrity, honesty and knowledge that promote the culture, values and mission of restaurant StripSteak Waikiki and our Partner.</li>
<li>
Ensure that all guests feel welcome and are given responsive, professional, gracious service at all times</li>
<li>
Performs all aspects of position with efficiency, respect, friendliness, professionalism, teamwork, knowledge, patience, awareness, compassion, courtesy and initiative.</li>
<li>
Understand philosophy of management and owner. Displays consistent attention to detail and follow through of all restaurant policies.</li>
<li>
Represents the restaurant professionally through effective communication, cooperation and relationships with the all business partners.</li>
<li>
Collaborates with team to create a culture and restaurant work environment based upon respect; fosters opportunities for team to learn, grow and develop their abilities.</li>
<li>
Leads the Management Staff, ensures that service standards are on par with all outside accreditation sources: including but not limited to Zagat, Gayot, Relais &amp; Chateaux, Mobil 5 star, AAA Five Diamond, San Francisco Chronicle 4 star, Wine Spectator, and Michelin 2 star establishment and in keeping with the service foundation and philosophy established at restaurant StripSteak Waikiki.</li>
<li>
Understand completely all programs, procedures, standards, specifications, guidelines and training programs.</li>
<li>
Offer positive solutions to problems or issues and be a voice that is part of the decision making team.</li>
</ul>
