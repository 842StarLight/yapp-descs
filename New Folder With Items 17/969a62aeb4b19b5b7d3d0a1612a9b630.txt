<h2>Sr. Global Product Manager - PM&C Next Gen</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>At Spacelabs Healthcare, we are on a mission to provide continuous innovation in healthcare technology for better clinical and economic outcomes. Our scalable solutions deliver critical patient data across local and remote systems, enable better-informed decisions, increase efficiencies, and create a safer environment for patients.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Why work at Spacelabs? Because lives depend on you!</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The Product Manager holds primary responsibility for driving and enabling sales growth for key Spacelabs monitor products, software and services. Specific responsibilities include product development research, pricing, launching new offerings, positioning new products in a competitive market, and developing synchronization of capabilities across Anesthesia, Cardiology, Supply &amp; Accessories and defibrillation product lines. Requires close collaboration and interface with R&amp;D and Sales.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Work with the cross functional team to release our next generation of patient monitoring products</li>
<li>
Work with UI designers to build a UI that is intuitive and provides our users with a workflow that will enable Spacelabs patient monitoring products to one up the competition.</li>
<li>
Develop the business and marketing plan for our next generation Patient Monitoring Surveillance Station.</li>
<li>
Ensure that the product requirements for our next generation product will not only hit the mark but provide users with a workflow and experience that gives them a reason to want to come back for more.</li>
<li>
Develop value based pricing strategies for next generation patient monitoring products.</li>
<li>
Drive innovation and inclusion of unique capabilities to address viable market requirements.</li>
<li>
Develop key opinion leader relationships to create unique clinical positioning through the use of white papers, case studies and focus groups.</li>
<li>
Provide comprehensive product training to downstream leaders and distributors, and act as product expert for sales and clinical employee training sessions.
</li>
<li>Communicate all relevant customer information including product and/or customer problems or opportunities to appropriate Spacelabs personnel. Support Spacelabs business, mission and goals.</li>
<li>
Develop and track metrics, benchmarks and success criteria to measure the efficiency and effectiveness of new product enhancements (ROI success).</li>
<li>
Uphold the Company’s core values of Integrity, Innovation, Accountability, and Teamwork.</li>
<li>
Demonstrate behavior consistent with the Company’s Code of Ethics and Conduct.
</li>
<li>It is the responsibility of every Spacelabs Healthcare employee to report to their manager or a member of senior management any quality problems or defects in order for corrective action to be implemented and to avoid recurrence of the problem.</li>
<li>
Duties may be modified or assigned at any time to meet the needs of the business.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Marketing or related field, or in field related to specialty requirements. MA/MBA preferred.
</li>
<li>Experience in capital device product management. Medical device experience preferred. (Overall experience determines level; minimum 5+ years for level 3 and 8+ years’ experience for level 4)</li>
<li>
Clinical experience in a critical care setting a plus.</li>
<li>
Global product experience in EMEA, China, India and/or South America a plus.<br>
</li>
<li>Ability to manage multiple, complex priorities within demanding timeframes.</li>
<li>
Strong project management and decision making skills.</li>
<li>
Proven ability to deliver results through others, both direct and matrixed. Proven ability to lead, develop, motivate and hold others accountable.</li>
<li>
Strong presentation and communication skills. Extensive public speaking experience as a key opinion leader.</li>
<li>
Specialty knowledge may be defined and required, based on the assigned products/product line.</li>
<li>
Strong computer skills: MS Office applications, Visio and enterprise database systems; Agile, MFG Pro experience a plus. Use of computer: 80%
</li>
<li>Must travel and be able to acquire all necessary documents for international travel 25%.</li>
<li>
Work with business areas within their local time zones as required (EMEA, China, etc.) Work various times as necessary for successful installation and training. 15%</li>
<li>
Successful completion of Spacelabs training required (including training done at Company location). Must become fully competent in the application and operation of all products in all care areas within 180 days of employment.</li>
</ul>
