<h2>Talent Acquisition Partner</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Merchants Fleet is the fastest growing fleet management company in the country, with over 50,000 vehicles under management. Our mission is to provide our clients with the most innovative fleet management experience nationally. We partner with our clients to optimize the mobility of their organization, through leasing, fleet services and customized solutions. Through innovative thinking and specialized teams, Merchants elevates the fleet performance of its broad range of clients.<br>
Merchants is an award-winning place to work with accolades including; Best &amp; Brightest, Best Company to Work For 2015-2019, Great Place to Work and Coolest Company for Young Professionals.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b><i>
Purpose:</i></b></p>
<p>
Merchants Fleet is adding a full-time Corporate Recruiter to our Talent Acquisition Team! Help us attract the best and brightest by joining one of the fastest growing companies in Fleet. Lead recruitment efforts for assigned business units to build a strong workforce. Develop recruiting plans, network through industry contacts and utilize innovative sourcing strategies. Interview, evaluate and refer internal and external candidates for a variety of roles to include management, finance, sales, operations, logistics, client experience, technology and administration.</p>
<p><b><i>
Key Responsibilities:</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Achieves staffing objectives by recruiting and evaluating job candidates; partners with managers to make effective hiring decisions</li>
<li>
Anticipates future recruiting needs by creating a strong partnership with hiring managers and solid understanding of the business</li>
<li>
Builds consultative relationships of trust across all areas of the business</li>
<li>
Creates job ads that attract quality candidates and stand out from the competition</li>
<li>
Develops applicant sources by building rapport with community resources, educational facilities, employment agencies, recruiters, media, and internet sites</li>
<li>
Establishes applicant requirements utilizing intake &amp; attributes assessment, job description and team feedback</li>
<li>
Determines applicant qualifications by interviewing applicants, analyzing responses, verifying references, and comparing qualifications to job requirements.</li>
<li>
Evaluates applicants by discussing job requirements and applicant qualifications with managers and interviewing applicants on a consistent set of qualifications.</li>
<li>
Facilitates proactive communication with hiring team and applicants throughout the recruitment lifecycle</li>
<li>
Delivers offers of employment and manage negotiations according to established practices</li>
<li>
Works with Talent Coordinator to meet all pre-employment requirements with candidates</li>
<li>
Avoids legal challenges by understanding current legislation, enforcing regulations with managers, recommending new procedures, and conducting training</li>
<li>
Stays ahead of trends by maintaining an awareness of new recruiting technologies, job boards, etc.</li>
<li>
Tracks competitive offerings, market data, etc. and makes recommendations for team to consider</li>
<li>
Updates job knowledge by participating in educational opportunities, reading professional and trade publications, maintaining personal networks, and participating in professional organizations.</li>
<li>
Partners with Marketing Team to develop advertising programs to enhance company’s image and presence in the talent market</li>
<li>
Manages candidate activity in the applicant tracking system</li>
<li>
Participates in staff meetings for assigned business units to develop expertise and insight</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>
Skills:</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Detail oriented, organized and tech savvy</li>
<li>
Strong work ethic, commitment &amp; determination</li>
<li>
High energy level, self-motivated &amp; willingness to learn</li>
<li>
Impeccable core values and desire to serve others</li>
<li>
Desire to work in a professional, high-paced environment</li>
<li>
Approachable &amp; ability to openly receive feedback</li>
<li>
Team-oriented with the ability to work independently</li>
<li>
Excellent communication &amp; interpersonal skills, effective with front line staff, managers, peers, and candidates</li>
<li>
Proficiency with MS Office including Excel, Word and Outlook</li>
<li>
Proficiency with LinkedIn and other recruiting sites as well as social media</li>
<li>
Proven ability to develop a strong understanding of the business and culture of the organization</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>
Experience:</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Prior experience working in an applicant tracking system (ATS) required, UltiPro preferred</li>
<li>
Expertise in sourcing candidates from job boards, niche sites, social networking, etc.</li>
<li>
Minimum of 7 years recruiting experience at all levels in a Corporate environment</li>
<li>
Prior experience recruiting IT positions required</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>
Education:</i></b></p>
<ul><li>
Bachelor's degree, preferably in human resources.</li></ul>
