<h2>Director of Technology, Clearing and Prime Services Division</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Job Description:</b><br>
<p>Since our founding in 1955, Wedbush has been a leader in the financial industry providing our clients with a wide range of services; including institutional sales, correspondent clearing services, equity research, corporate and municipal finance, equity market making, fixed income trading, prime brokerage service, and wealth management.
</p>
<p>Headquartered in Los Angeles, with nearly 100 registered offices, the firm focuses on dedicated service, client financial safety, continuity, and advanced technology. Wedbush Securities is the largest subsidiary of holding company Wedbush, Inc which also includes affiliated firms. We are seeking a Director of Technology to oversee all aspect of software development for our Clearing &amp; Prime Services division within Wedbush Securities.<br>
<br>
Responsibilities include, but are not limited to:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Define project scope, goals and deliverables in collaboration with stakeholders of Clearing &amp; Prime Services Business Unit
</li>
<li>Engage key business leaders in order to determine requirements and translate those requirements into software solutions</li>
<li>
Analyze and verify business requirements for completeness, accuracy, comprehensibility, scalability, and conformity to best practices</li>
<li>
Establish, build, and maintain working relationships with stakeholders, technology team members, vendors, and other departments as needed</li>
<li>
Encourage team work and enable effective collaboration</li>
<li>
While working with the PMO group, plan project timelines and milestones within each phase and estimate resources needed to achieve project goals</li>
<li>
Maintain and enhance complex trade processing systems
</li>
<li>Work closely with the Client-Onboarding team to assist with IT solution delivery in order to successfully onboard broker/dealer clients as well as migrate existing clients from old securities processing system to new one</li>
<li>
Work with vendor and in-house teams to build system functionality needed to onboard new clients</li>
<li>
Provide innovative solutions to solve business problems in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>
Develop, maintain and execute user acceptance test plans</li>
<li>
Lead testing efforts and approve for deployment after successfully conducting various test packs (smoke, regression, and/or volume) where applicable</li>
<li>
Ensure that issues are identified, tracked, reported and resolved in a timely manner</li>
<li>
Develop and manage deployment plans with the solution delivery team</li>
<li>
Develop and deliver progress reports, proposals, requirements documentation, and analysis to the senior management of Clearing &amp; Prime Services division</li>
<li>
Perform other duties as required and assigned<br>
</li>
</ul>
