<div><b>QUALIFICATIONS</b></div>
</div>
</div>
<br>
<div><div><ul>
<li>Bachelor's Degree in Marketing, Communications, New media advertising or related field; or equivalent combination of education and experience required.</li>
<li>5 - 8 years of related marketing experience required; mortgage lending industry experience strongly preferred.</li>
</ul></div></div>
<br>
<div>
<div>
<div><b>What you’ll do</b></div>
</div>
</div>
<br>
<div><div>
<p>As a <b>Product Marketing Business Partner </b>for Banner Bank, you will have the opportunity to:</p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for developing and implementing a social media program for client-facing mortgage loan officers. This will include collaborating with vendor partners, writing or curating content at a cadence to support ~100 production sales people in multiple markets, sourcing supporting material (i.e., imagery), seeking compliance approvals and posting to approved channels via appropriate SMMS platforms.</li>
<li>Responsible for creating content (digital and print), and designing accompanying creative pieces, including managing mortgage loan officer content on company website, within the CRM system(s), print ads, fliers, signage, infographics, social media posts, email content, etc. to disseminate through various outlets and channels in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Responsible for developing and maintaining a content calendar for the mortgage channel and individual mortgage loan officer participants, updating as needed. Keep business unit leadership apprised of unit and overall bank marketing calendars (i.e., schedules for statement messaging, new branch marketing, etc.)</li>
<li>Partner with business unit leaders, marketing director and compliance to ensure adherence to all compliance standards.</li>
<li>Facilitates development and subsequent management of a program through which marketing budgets can be allocated to production staff. Analyze business unit marketing expenses to help leaders make informed trade-off decisions for their investments.</li>
<li>Partner with other marketing and communications colleagues to ensure the overall department effectively meets the needs of all business units throughout the organization, including graphic design and layout support, advertisement creation, media buying, and sponsorship support.</li>
<li>Partner with business unit leaders to increase portfolio retention, including strategy development and tactical execution to retain client accounts.</li>
<li>Responsible for managing the marketing elements associated with Bank Investment Services program offerings, including ensuring all marketing content is in keeping with our investment management service provider's approved guidelines.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>
</p>

<div><b>Who you’ll work with</b></div>
</div>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<div>
<div>You will be part of our Marketing team, responsible for supporting Banner's mortgage lending and investment services business units. Responsibilities include developing programs that grow with the Bank, including developing and implementing digital and traditional marketing programs in support of the business units, working closely with business line leaders as well as individual production team members (i.e., mortgage loan officers and financial advisors). You will develop and manage best practices in an effort to increase overall external awareness of, and preference for, Banner's abilities for these business units, create/curate content for sharing via social media channels, manage content for various CRM applications, maintain individual representatives' personalized web pages, and develop/track budget performance.</div>
