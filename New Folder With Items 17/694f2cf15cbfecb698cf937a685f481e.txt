<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>About The Position</b></h4>
<div>
<p>We are looking for an experienced <b>Client Success Manager</b> to be a key member of the Product &amp; Client team, working closely with our clients throughout the implementation of our mobile-first platform and post-implementation to help them maximize the value they realize from Yaro. You will be getting in on the ground floor to not only build best-in-class consumer digital products but also build our company.</p>
<p>
This is not your average client success manager role – and it’s not for the uninspired. This is life in the venture-backed start-up world – the fast lane. It’s next-generation digital healthcare. It's innovative and rich with opportunity, as you drive better healthcare and financial benefits for a portfolio of Yaro’s clients and their members.
</p>
</div>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>About Yaro</b></h4>
<div>
<p>Yaro is building digital products that put consumers in the center of their healthcare. We’re an experience transformation engine putting the consumer first, so that it’s simple to make smart benefits choices, shop and pay for care, and establish financial well-being. We work with health plans, providers, and health systems to serve members as consumers, delivering better health outcomes, increased transparency, and lower costs.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
We are:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Guided by <b>clarity</b>. We start with people - our clients, our users, and our team. By approaching our work with focus, transparency, and intention, we are clear on our priorities and what needs to get done and when.</li>
<li>
Building with <b>intensity</b>. Our passion has purpose, and that purpose drives us to build and sustain momentum. Staying agile and efficient gives us the ability to build and adapt quickly and securely. We want to get things done: built, launched, and making a positive impact.</li>
<li>
Working in <b>community</b>. As team members, we radically collaborate in trusting relationships and create something we’re proud of. Bringing diverse experiences, perspectives, and skills, we build together as a company, with partners, and as part of the healthcare community.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
In your role as a client success manager, you’ll be the trusted advisor to our clients. Part coach, business analyst, project manager, and strategic adviser, you are continually focused on helping our clients and users improve the benefits they reap from working with Yaro’s platform and digital products. If you’re passionate about creating delightful cross-platform mobile applications along with building team and strategy on a mobile-first consumer product, we would love to talk to you.</p>
</div>

<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>About Your Responsibilities</b></h4>
<div>
<ul>
<li>Project manage our client implementations and continue the relationship post-launch as the main point of contact for our clients and users to triage issues, answer questions, and position them for success</li>
<li>
Collaborate cross-functionally with our Product, Engineering, and Sales teams to ensure project timelines are met, resolve issues, and identify new opportunities to add value for our clients</li>
<li>
Assist clients with marketing and communications to their members that encourage adoption of the Yaro platform</li>
<li>
Become a Yaro product expert, knowing every user journey, capability, and feature like the back of your hand so you can coach clients/users in getting the most out of our product. You’ll even get to assist with product strategy, product roadmap, and competitive analysis as needed.</li>
<li>
Constantly innovate and identify ways we can consistently delighting our customers</li>
<li>
Success looks like maintaining high levels of client engagement, satisfaction, and loyalty</li>
</ul>
