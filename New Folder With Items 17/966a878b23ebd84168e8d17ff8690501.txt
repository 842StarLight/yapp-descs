<h2>Product Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Eurotech, a global company that designs, creates and delivers full Internet of Things (IoT) solutions, including services, software and hardware, is seeking a <b>Product Marketing Manager</b> to be added to its marketing and PM team.</p>
<p>
The <b>Product Marketing Manager</b> is responsible for all things related to the positioning, promotion and lifecycle of a set of products from concept through end-of-life, ensuring continuity of the product portfolio to support company sales goals.</p>
<p><b>
Duties</b></p>
<ul>
 <li>Responsible for managing a portion of the global product portfolio, including performing competitive analysis, product positioning and driving promotional activities.</li>
<li>Develops global market requirement documents based on research, collaboration, and understanding of company portfolio and strategic direction.</li>
<li>Provides insights to improve the value proposition, competitive differentiation, and product positioning to ensure strong competitive advantage. Monitors market trends and competitive dynamics.</li>
<li>Attends tradeshows and client visits as needed to support sales and marketing activities.</li>
<li>Requires ability to collaborate across time zones and cultures to facilitate communications and understanding throughout the entire global organization.</li>
<li>Will work with corporate product management team to maintain the product documentation repository.</li>
<li>Will support channel partners and sales team in answering customer questions about product specifications and/or requirements.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>At least 2 years of technical product marketing and/or product management experience.</li>
<li>Market knowledge of embedded systems, wireless, cloud and/or IoT technologies.</li>
<li>Must be fully functional with Microsoft applications including Word, Excel, and PowerPoint.</li>
<li>Demonstrated interpersonal communication skills with the ability to work within cross-functional groups.</li>
<li>Must be able to travel up to 30%, including international travel.</li>
<li>Italian language skills are a plus.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Office Location</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Columbia, MD</li>
</ul>
