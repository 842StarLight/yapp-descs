<h2>Systems Engineering Business Process Design - Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div><b>Business Process – Manager</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>Are you a talented professional that focuses on the planning, execution, and monitoring of project initiatives? Are you a resourceful problem solver that thrives in the identification and definition of creative, effective technology solutions? As a Project Management/Functional Senior Consultant, you help clients set-up, manage, and operate their most complex projects, define functional requirements for technology solutions, and design and derive transformative approaches for technology systems.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Work you’ll do</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>As a Manager you will lead work threads within an engagement team to create software solutions that help our clients solve problems, expand market share, improve operations, and grow their business. You engage in:</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Project and Program Management</li>
<li>
Requirements Elicitation and Management</li>
<li>
Joint Application Design Sessions</li>
<li>
Creating Robust, Effective, Scalable Solutions
</li>
<li>Being the lead person to interface between the clients and our Deloitte teams</li>
<li>
Serve as an integrator between business needs and technology solutions, helping to create technology solutions to meet clients’ business needs.</li>
<li>
Developing &amp; reviewing system requirements, designing the solution, redefining the business, presenting the prototype, and leading the testing of our custom technology solutions</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
</div>
<div><b>The Team</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Systems Engineering</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>Our Systems Engineering team delivers large-scale software applications and integrated systems, and assists clients with architecture design, assessment and optimization, and definition. We develop service-oriented architecture (SOA) and other integration solutions to enable information sharing and management between business partners and disparate processes and systems, and we deliver technology enablement to support CIO services transformation.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>Systems Engineering professionals focus on key client issues that impact the core business by maximizing operational value, driving down the cost of quality, and enhancing technology innovation. Our teams deliver a wide range of services, including application architecture design and optimization, application modernization, business process management and solution integration, custom application design and development, and large-scale technology delivery program efforts.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Business Process Managers are required to have:</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>11+ years of relevant consulting or industry experience;</li>
<li>
5+ years working in requirements engineering, with specific expertise in functional and business analysis on custom software development type projects;</li>
<li>
Experience working on projects related to custom software development with a deep knowledge of SDLC;</li>
<li>
Experience leading teams and translating requirements into optimized designs and identifying alternatives for clients;</li>
<li>
Deep background and experience in requirements planning, requirements engineering and management, functional design, business process modeling, and enterprise business analysis;</li>
<li>
Must possess industry certifications like CCBA, CBAP in the business analysis area;</li>
<li>
Deep management and leadership skills for requirements analysis and guiding projects throughout the SDLC on requirements, change requests and successful delivery;</li>
<li>
A willingness to travel 80-100% of the time;</li>
<li>
A Bachelor’s Degree in IT or a related discipline.</li>
<li>
Must be legally authorized to work in the United States without the need for employer sponsorship, now or at any time in the future.</li>
</ul>
<div>
</div>
<div><b>In addition, successful Managers will have the following preferred background:<br>
</b></div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Experience translating requirements into test scenarios;</li>
<li>
Experience leading requirements processes including conducting interviews and documenting technical requirements;</li>
<li>
Experience leading fit gap analysis and helping with impact analysis;</li>
<li>
Knowledge of and experience with DevOps, Agile, CI Methodologies;</li>
<li>
Experience managing design activities including design sessions and defining future state processes;</li>
<li>
Experience leading teams across shores – onsite, nearshore and offshore;
</li>
<li>Strong oral and written communication skills.</li>
</ul>
