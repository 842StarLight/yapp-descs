<h2>Bilingual (Spanish) Field Technician Representative - Fort Myers - 42774</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Nielsen is known for our work in TV and Radio Ratings. Since 1923, we have studied consumers in more than 100 countries. We give a complete view of trends and habits worldwide on what people watch, listen to and buy.</p>
<p>Being a Nielsen Field Representative allows you to make your friends with boring jobs jealous and work with the best because no company in the world knows more about consumers than Nielsen. That means you never stop learning because our world is your classroom.</p>
<p>The Nielsen Field Representative position provides the opportunity to combine technical aptitude with outstanding people skills while working a <b>non-traditional schedule including evenings and weekend hours</b>. Field Representatives are available to take customer calls and are responsible for maintaining a designated customer base of up to 100 Nielsen Families/Households.</p>
<p><b>What does a Field Representative do?</b></p>
<p>Field Representatives build and maintain relationships with Nielsen Families/Households and educate Panel Members on the proper use of our equipment. Field Representatives install, maintain and troubleshoot proprietary hardware and software in our sample homes to capture three screen audience measurements - Mobile, Online, and TV.</p>
<ul>
<li>Establish and maintain long term relationships with assigned Nielsen Families/Households</li>
<li>Install Nielsen measurement equipment in newly assigned Nielsen Families/Households</li>
<li>Drive to selected homes within a designated territory, using company-provided vehicle</li>
<li>Identify problems and troubleshoot data/equipment</li>
<li>Ensure collection and transmission equipment is properly installed and configured to transmit necessary audience data</li>
<li>Respond to calls from assigned Nielsen Families/Households and ensure equipment is maintained and operating effectively</li>
<li>Prioritize, schedule and complete all work in compliance with established policy and guidelines in assigned territory</li>
<li>Accurately collect and report demographics and audience information using Nielsen proprietary software</li>
<li>Educate and coach Panel Members on proper operation and usage of Nielsen equipment</li>
<li>Negotiate and maintain cooperation of Panel Members, solicit additional participation in Nielsen samples, such as PC measurement, portable video, and others</li>
</ul>
