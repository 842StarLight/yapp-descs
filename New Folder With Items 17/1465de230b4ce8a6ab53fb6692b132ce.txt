<h2>Corporate Counsel, Privacy & Product</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">This person will be a key member of Okta's Privacy &amp; Product Legal team, and provide support across the organization globally, as follows:
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Support our Sales and Professional Services teams by working with customers, prospective customers, and partners on privacy and product-related questions regarding Okta's services;</li>
<li>Counsel and work closely with Okta's Engineering and Product Management teams regarding privacy-by-design principles;</li>
<li>Assist the company in identifying patentable inventions and work with outside counsel as necessary to secure appropriate patent protection;</li>
<li>Partner with the Commercial Legal team in customer negotiations, including supporting the drafting and negotiation of privacy-related agreements and addenda;</li>
<li>Help facilitate Okta's ongoing compliance with all applicable global privacy and data protection laws, including the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), NIS Directive, E-privacy Regulation, Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act;</li>
<li>Partner with members of the legal, security &amp; compliance, technology, marketing and human resources teams, and outside counsel to help ensure the company's compliance with laws, regulations, compliance frameworks, and customer contracts;</li>
<li>Iterate on and maintain internal privacy policies and procedures, including Okta's internal privacy compliance program;</li>
<li>Develop training materials and provide training to employees on privacy-related obligations;</li>
<li>Work with Okta's other legal and public policy stakeholders to help influence privacy and data protection policy, laws, regulations, and frameworks.</li>
</ul>
<b>Required Skills/Experience:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Law degree from a highly-regarded school and California bar admission;</li>
<li>Comfortable with the culture of a fast-paced, rapidly-growing enterprise software company;</li>
<li>4+ years' experience (combination of law firm and in-house experience strongly preferred), including at least 2 years specializing in privacy and data protection matters;</li>
<li>Excellent written/verbal communication, presentation, drafting and negotiation skills;</li>
<li>Experience in negotiating technology contracts;</li>
<li>Highly-responsive and service-oriented approach to driving projects to closure;</li>
<li>Team-oriented and collaborative: a self-starter with an insatiable intellectual curiosity regarding legal and regulatory aspects of data privacy and security;</li>
<li>Ability to organize, prioritize and manage deadlines in a fast-paced and demanding work environment;</li>
<li>Experience in and/or knowledge of software-as-a-service and internet industries and technical concepts;</li>
<li>Certified Information Privacy Professional (US and/or EU) is a plus.</li>
</ul>
Okta is an Equal Opportunity Employer.
