<p>Are you looking for a full-time position that offers you excellent pay and benefits? Are you passionate about learning the exciting, in demand world of Transportation and Logistics? <p>We are seeking a motivated, high-energy Customer Service Representative (CSR) to join the team! A role as a CSR is a gateway to a career of interesting challenges, celebrated victories, and limitless opportunities.</p>

<li>Verify orders for accuracy of all information</li>
<li>Provide back-up support to team members in order to balance workload</li>
<li>Understand the tools and technology used to perform daily tasks</li>
<li>Accountable for meeting key factors in areas such as service and phone etiquette</li>
</ul>
