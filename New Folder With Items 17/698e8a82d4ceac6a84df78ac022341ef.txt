<h2>Employment Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Primary Responsibilities </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Equips individuals with disabilities with job readiness skills to secure employment</li>
<li>Creates an inviting, exciting, innovative, learning environment</li>
<li>Takes lead in developing and implementing activities and employment opportunities focused on personal and professional development</li>
<li>Produces weekly supported employment schedule</li>
<li>Consistently connects with community employers in order to identify or create employment opportunities for individuals</li>
<li>Establishes opportunities for job shadowing and training with local community businesses</li>
<li>Develops job leads and cultivates employer relationships to enhance community partnerships</li>
<li>Monitors individual job performance, attitude, and behavior for appropriateness</li>
<li>Provides individualized support to individuals when necessary, both during programming and 1:1 job coaching</li>
<li>Proactively communicates with families over employment progress and needs</li>
<li>Provides supports to individuals and assists in developing natural supports to ensure integration and increased or maintained independence in the community</li>
<li>Trains and develops independence skills related to employment, such as travel training, hygiene, self-advocacy, etc.</li>
<li>Provides transportation for groups or individual career seekers to interviews, employment assessments, job searching, and other employment activities in the community</li>
<li>Collaborates with Career Services/Work Force Development departments to execute supported employment jobs, including production goals</li>
<li>Properly trains and manages direct care staff working supported employment sites to meet employer goals</li>
<li>Communicates in a professional manner with employers and trouble shoots issues at supported employment sites with all relevant parties</li>
<li>Works with Case Management team to ensure individual’s ISP goals are being met</li>
<li>Participates in ISP meetings with Department of Developmental Services, participants, and their families or other members of their support team as needed</li>
<li>Takes direction from Site Manager to collect accurate data as needed for ISP’s, behavior plans, monthly progress notes, etc.</li>
<li>Submits billing and related progress notes on a daily basis utilizing Penelope software</li>
<li>Contributes to monthly ISP goal tracking notes</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Associates or Bachelor's Degree preferred</li>
<li>One to three years of Human Services or Sales experience preferred</li>
<li>First Aid and CPR or willingness to become certified.</li>
<li>Massachusetts Driver's License and clean driving record required</li>
<li>Personal vehicle required</li>
</ul>
<p>Must clear state and federal background check</p>

</ul>
