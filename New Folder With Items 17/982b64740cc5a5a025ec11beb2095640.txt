<h2>Property Manager Spring Branch, TX</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Candidates applying for this opening will also need to complete a Move It Management video interview and will receive an email with instructions after submitting their resume.</div>
<div></div>
<div>Move It Self Storage is seeking a Property Manager eager to offer outstanding customer service to our tenants. Come and join the Move It team where we offer a competitive salary, medical and dental benefits, 401k and bonus opportunity. Strong sales skills and customer service experience a plus!</div>
<div>
<br>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities include the following. Other duties may be assigned</div>
<ul>
<li>Provide exceptional service to our customers</li>
<li>Recommend appropriate sizes and services to customer’s needs</li>
<li>Maximize conversion of telephone and walk-in inquiries into rentals</li>
<li>Understand and explain the leasing process to customers</li>
<li>Take payments, accurately manage cash and balance receipts</li>
<li>Sell merchandise including packing and moving supplies and insurance to protect our customer’s goods</li>
<li>Maintain accurate records for each customer including contracts, insurance and change of address and correspondence to or from customers</li>
<li>Conduct daily visual and unit audits of entire facility as well as perform lock checks</li>
<li>Prepare and make daily bank deposits</li>
<li>Perform light maintenance inside the facility and on the grounds including: sweeping, changing light bulbs and removing debris in storage units</li>
<li>Effectively utilize computer systems in the Microsoft Windows environment, including accurate working knowledge of SiteLink and other operating software</li>
<li>Utilize social media as instructed for marketing purposes</li>
<li>Assist customers with truck rentals</li>
<li>Consults the TSSA Goldbook as well as Company and industry standard publications regarding proper operating procedures</li>
<li>Responsible for collections of delinquent accounts</li>
<li>Implement marketing strategies as instructed</li>
<li>Attend all webinars and required training sessions</li>
<li>Supervise Associate Manager at the property</li>
<li>Ensures Company standards are maintained at all times and complies with Company’s Policy Manual</li>
</ul>
<div>A High School diploma/GED equivalent and at least two years of related experience and/or training in direct sales, retail and/or customer service is required. Leadership skills and experience supervising others is required. A valid driver license and reliable transportation is required (local travel approximately 10%)<br>
<br>
Employees will generally split time between an office environment and outside work tasks. These outside tasks may need to be performed in either extreme cold or extreme heat and must be able to climb in and out of U-Haul trucks.<br>
<br>
Applicants should be computer literate with standard typing skills, and able to work a 10 key.<br>
<br>
Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.<br>

<br>
