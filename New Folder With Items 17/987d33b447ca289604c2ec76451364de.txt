<h2>Estimator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>You'll support the sales and ops team in the creation, review, and completion of accurate and timely pricing &amp; cost information on our homeowner's projects.</p>
<p>In addition to this, you'll be responsible for developing and managing day-to-day relationships with vendors, reviewing and managing subcontracts and helping select and purchase a variety of products, materials, and services.
</p>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities</b></h1>
<p>Based in New York City, you will work closely with, and receive support from, Bolster's sales, finance, and admin teams to fulfill the following responsibilities for a growing volume of qualified inquiries and simultaneous projects:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Analyze blueprints and other instruments of design to prepare time, cost, materials, and labor estimates and act as a resource for teams in need of quick unit costs</li>
<li>
Assist in vendor and subcontractor contract negotiations and liaise with engineers, architects, homeowners, contractors, and subcontractors on changes and adjustments to cost estimates</li>
<li>
Prepare estimates used by management for a variety of purposes including pricing, budget planning, and scheduling</li>
<li>
Assess the cost-effectiveness of products, projects or services and tracking actual costs relative to budgets as projects are delivered</li>
<li>
In collaboration with the finance team, set up cost monitoring and reporting systems and procedures and help prepare a variety of management reports</li>
</ul>
