<h2>Edtech - Sales Development Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>The Role</b></p>
<p>Our client is a Boston based company that leads the industry in quality customized on-demand executive coaching programs to develop better leaders. They are a Techstars ’19 company, with a strong management team from Harvard and MIT.</p>
<p>They are looking for several Sales Development Representatives to expand their growing team. This is an opportunity to grow with a professional coaching organization that is changing the way companies support their people. Their SDRs lead the way in researching prospective clients, optimizing outreach, and developing new opportunities.</p>
<p>Your role is to engage the key decision makers in a variety of organizations that are responsible for and passionate about the development of their team members. You are comfortable operating in a fast-paced, dynamic start-up environment, explaining value proposition over the phone, setting up sales appointments, and interacting with prospects at all levels within an organization.</p>
<p>If you’re looking for an opportunity to join an elite team in a high-impact role with a clear promotion path, and deliver incredible value to partners and businesses, then this is the opportunity for you. This position has a base salary, incentive compensation, and stock options.</p>
<p><b>What You'll Do / Learn</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Create new opportunities for the sales team via outbound emails, phone calls, and other social touches</li>
<li>Contact (call and/or email) a high volume of leads per day to ensure that they are fully aware of our clients capabilities and that they are qualified to move on to a demonstration of the platform</li>
<li>Work closely with the sales team to schedule demonstrations of our client’s platform with qualified leads</li>
<li>Collaborate with sales and marketing to align the quantity of leads with each month’s goal</li>
<li>Become an authority at using lead generation tools to routinely extract contacts and build accurate and targeted lists of prospects</li>
<li>Collaborate with sales and management team to build your process and pipeline – learn from regular training sessions but also bring your own creative ideas to help evolve the role of Sales Development at our client</li>
<li>Rapidly test and iterate messaging to refine communication with prospects</li>
<li>Make at least 50 calls a day, in addition to robust email campaigns and social outreach, that will result in at least 15 or more qualified leads on a monthly basis</li>
<li>Meet or exceed weekly, monthly, and quarterly and annual targets</li>
</ul>
