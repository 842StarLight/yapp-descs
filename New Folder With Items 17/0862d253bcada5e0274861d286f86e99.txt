<h2>Sourcing Project Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>TableCraft is looking for an energetic go getter to join the supply chain team. If you enjoy interacting with a broad range of domestic and international suppliers in a fast-paced environment, you’ll love this position. This high-spirited individual will play an essential role as the Sourcing Project Coordinator.</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manages all necessary communication on new product development and sourcing projects.</li>
<li>Facilitate the sampling and product approval process for all projects.</li>
<li>Plans, directs, and coordinates the work activities and resources necessary for manufacturing products in accordance with cost, quality, and quantity specifications.</li>
<li>Manages new product launching, consulting with appropriate teams on the new product approval processes.</li>
<li>Work as part of a team, multitask, and problem solve; maintain a high quality of work.</li>
<li>Effectively communicating specifications for each piece of the required project.</li>
<li>Work with Vendor/Suppliers for sourcing of outside quotes needed for timely completion of a project.</li>
<li>Ability to work with a large domestic and international supplier base.</li>
<li>Provide and maintain accurate product / vendor information within ERP system.</li>
<li>Make contributions to project development, including but not limited to product idealization, pricing request, vendor audit, sample evaluation, PO follow-up, quality control inspection</li>
<li>Primary resource to the Sales and Product Development Team with weekly meetings</li>
<li>High level of accuracy and attention to detail.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Other Skills/Abilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication.</li>
<li>Proven work experience within a sourcing or procurement capacity.</li>
<li>Experience with managing international suppliers.</li>
<li>Strong project management and leadership skills.</li>
<li>Familiarity with sourcing and vendor management relevant software.</li>
<li>Excellent organizational skills with the ability to effectively work on multiple projects.</li>
<li>Strong analytical and decision-making skills required to successfully interact with all teams and levels of business within organization.</li>
<li>Good facilitation and influencing skills required.</li>
<li>Document all critical and pertinent information that may impact projects</li>
<li>Proficiency in Microsoft Office.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Education/ Certificates: </b></p>

</ul>
