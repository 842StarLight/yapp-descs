Unlike most companies, CCU offers a pension plan that is fully funded by the credit union and provides up to 35% of an employee’s salary upon retirement. <p>If you have a passion for providing exceptional service in a dynamic environment and have excellent teller and member service as well as sales skills and experience, consider joining our team at CCU. We are currently advertising for an open Teller position at several branches: Rockledge, Port St. John, and a Floater Teller position that covers the north area branches.</p>
<p>This position is responsible for:</p>
<ul><li>Performs routine member transactions including deposits, withdrawals, cash advances, transfers, money orders, within policy guidelines and with a consistent balancing record; confirms all necessary documents are properly authorized, are in proper form and are within authorized limits. Receives payments for mortgage, installment and share secured loans, ensuring that payments equal the amount due and that all late charges, if applicable, are collected.</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>Assists with night depository duties; logging bags, processing deposits, making change orders, issuing receipts and returning bags to members.</li>
<li>Processes ATM deposits and balancing duties. Processes coin deposits and balance coin machine. Remove and replace coin bags as needed.</li>
<li>Provides information on credit union services including eligibility for membership, types of credit union accounts, interest rates, current dividend rates, locations, office hours, and telephone numbers. Actively promotes and cross-sells new account products and services by providing recommendations and assisting members in whatever capacity necessary.</li>
<li>Assists members through the lobby management with services including, but not limited to, check orders, stop payments, wire transfers, coin machine, statement copies, records research, pinning or re-issuing debit cards, accessing safety deposit boxes and maintaining records and notary as needed.</li>
<li>Assists with telephone support by responding to member inquiries and problems in a professional, timely manner; follows up on inquiries with informational materials and/or phone contact; investigates and corrects errors and resolves problems or other issues; helps members to utilize electronic delivery channels for convenience.</li>
<li>Adheres to security controls policy and procedures to aid in detection and prevention of fraud, theft, robberies, threats of violence, fires, accidents, facility safety, etc. Maintains maximum security over cash, operating procedures, and other valuables on hand according to established security policy. Balances cash drawer daily and verifies cash being returned to vault.</li>
<li>Updates member information and verfies identity, processes mail or reports and open and perform end of day balancing including checks as requested.</li>
<li>Performs all other related duties as assigned and special projects as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p>Position Requirements:</p>
<p>These specifications are general guidelines based on the minimum experience normally considered essential to the satisfactory performance of this position. The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill, and/or ability required to perform the position in a satisfactory manner. Individual abilities may result in some deviation from these guidelines.</p>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or general education degree (GED); or six (6) months or more related experience and/or training; or the equivalent combination of education and experience. Work related experience should consist of a cash handling and exceptional customer service and sales background. Educational experience, through in-house training sessions, formal school or financial industry related curriculum, should be business or financial industry related.</li>
<li>Basic skills in computer terminal and personal computer operation.</li>
<li>Basic typing skills to meet production needs of the position.</li>
<li>Basic math skills; calculate interest and balance accounts; add, subtract, multiply and divide in all units of measure, using whole numbers, common fractions and decimals; locate routine mathematical errors; count currency, coin and negotiable instruments in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Effective oral and interpersonal communication skills with the ability to apply common sense to carry out instructions, interpret documents, understand procedures and to communicate clearly to members and employees.</li>
<li>Ability to deal with difficult problems.</li>
<li>Effective organizational and time management skills with ability to multi-task, the ability to work with close supervision while performing duties and be a strong team player.</li>
<li>Ability to handle simple problems in nature requiring some analysis or research to determine best solution.</li>
</ul>
<p>The incumbent must be able to perform this position safely, without endangering the health or safety to the individual or others.</p>
