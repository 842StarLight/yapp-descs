<h2>Macy's Montgomery Mall, Bethesda, MD: Sales Supervisor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><b>Job Overview:</b></div>
<br>
<div>
As a Sales Supervisor, you will support My Macy's and drive sales for a specific selling area in partnership with a Sales Manager. You will be responsible for assisting the Sales Manager by supervising all daily selling activities and leading a selling team in your selling area. Responsibilities include, assisting in the selling process; driving customer service through coaching and monitoring selling behaviors; teaching product knowledge and communicating policies and procedures. Perform other duties as assigned. This position leads to placement as a Sales Manager.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>
Essential Functions:</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
Sales:</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<ul><li>Drive and exceed sales goals by executing Macy's initiatives.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Lead the push toward selling through coaching and recognition.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Review reports, identify deficient areas and partner with Sales Manager to implement action plans.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Identify best sellers and key items; communicate merchandise needs to optimize the My Macy's process.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ensure accuracy of promotional presentation; communicate advertising and sales information to Associates.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strengthen attendance and weekend hours compliance among staff</li></ul>
<ul><li>Motivate Associates to solicit our Star Rewards Program by reinforcing the benefits of new accounts.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Review your personal Selling Area Scorecard monthly for your Associate Turnover, Sales &amp; Star Rewards results.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Establish high level of shortage awareness and effectiveness; ensure that all policies and procedures are implemented.</li></ul>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
Customer:</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<ul><li>Coach Associates to consistently deliver effective selling behaviors resulting in high customer engagement.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Review &amp; utilize Associate Scorecards to provide recognition; partner with Sales Manager to develop and communicate strategies to improve results.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Observe Associates twice daily through formal observations focusing on selling behaviors; ensure Associates are providing an outstanding shopping experience to all customers.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Coach to Associate Scorecard metrics weekly through daily informal observations defining one focus metric and behaviors to improve an individual metric.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ensure optimum sales floor coverage and lead selling initiatives throughout the store as required.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintain high customer readiness standards; deliver a clean, neat and easy to shop store environment.</li></ul>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
People:</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<ul><li>Partner with Sales Manager to interview and employ selling focused Sales Associates.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Engage in Macy's recognition program; reward Associates with recognition cards.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Provide input to performance evaluations; ensuring Associates participate in the development of individual performance improvement plans.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Make recommendations on Associate promotion, advancement, talent development and termination.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Address Associate concerns in a reasonable and fair manner, consistent with the values expected by the Company.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Create a positive, inclusive work environment resulting in retention of the best people and turnover reduction.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Coordinate Associate vacation scheduling and paid time off for Sales Manager's total area of responsibility.</li></ul>
<br>
<div></div>
<ul><li>Regular, dependable attendance &amp; punctuality.</li></ul>
