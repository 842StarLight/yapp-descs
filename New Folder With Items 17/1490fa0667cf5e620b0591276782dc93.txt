<h2>Grocery Truck Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>The Mobile Grocery Manager will be responsible for implementing an exciting new project that seeks to create new avenues of healthy food access in high need communities. This project?s unique quality is its ability to bring a large variety of high-quality, affordable, healthy foods into neighborhoods with limited grocery store access.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>This candidate will be responsible for all aspects of the day-to-day operation of the grocery truck project. Successful candidates have the proven ability to independently move a project forward with professional maturity. The manager will work closely with the Grocery Truck driver at each market stop to service the community with affordable, healthy market basket of foods. The manager will be the face of the project in the community.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Functions</b><b>:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Communication &amp; Customer Service:</p>
<ul>
<li>Displays excellence in customer service through embodying a welcoming and charismatic presence</li>
<li>Ability to grow a strong customer base through relationship building</li>
<li>Creates outreach materials that increase the number of users of the grocery service and advertise promotions</li>
<li>Represents the CAFB in community meeting, on panels and through outreach opportunities.</li>
<li>Incorporates feedback from the community and works with the DC Director to adjust and update the Mobile Grocery route and stop locations to best serve community needs and ensure customer and staff safety</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Operations:</p>
<ul>
<li>Ensures accurate accounting of grocery inventory</li>
<li>Coordinates food storage, arrangement and display for Grocery Truck Project</li>
<li>Leads the set up and breakdown of the market at each location</li>
<li>Load and unload grocery items for safe food storage</li>
<li>Ensure food safety standards are met throughout market process</li>
<li>Restock products, and maintain the market?s inventory and produce quality</li>
<li>Conduct transactions, handling different payments</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Reporting:</p>
<ul>
<li>Monitors product demand and uses customer feedback to improve product offering and customer experience</li>
<li>Tracks and reports on client feedback and sales outcomes</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Supervisory Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Directly supervises employees and carries out supervisory responsibilities in accordance with the organization?s policies and applicable laws.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Works with team to develop annual programming plans and metrics to measure success and tracks performance against plan</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Responsibilities include interviewing, hiring and training employees; planning, assigning and directing work; appraising performance; rewarding and disciplining employees; addressing complaints and resolving problems.</li></ul>
</div>

<div>

</ul>

Other Skills / Abilities:</b></p>
