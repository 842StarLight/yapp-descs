<h2>Revenue Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Revenue Manager - (19000A1N)</p>
<div>
<b>
Description</b><div>
</div>
<div>
Caesars Entertainment is the world’s most geographically diversified casino-entertainment company. Our core mission is to inspire grown-ups to play. Focusing on building loyalty and value with its guests through a unique combination of great service, excellent products and technological leadership.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>Caesars has an exciting opportunity as a <b>Director of Revenue Management</b> in our Finance division, located in Las Vegas, Nevada. Under minimal supervision, this position is to ensure the enterprise is receiving maximum return on every room sold based on: detailed forecasts of the multiple revenue streams of our customers, booking patterns by distribution outlets, seasonal and competitive dynamics impacting demand, and other key drivers of the business.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>Key Functions</div>
<div>Work directly with Executives across all properties and departments to develop the strategies, tactics, and tools necessary to maximize the yield on each hotel room, including providing timely forecasts and recommendations to identify and maximize revenue opportunities.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>Support and Reporting</div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Provide daily support to the Directors and VPs of Revenue Optimization</li>
<li>
Provide weekly and ad-hoc Revenue Management executive reports</li>
<li>
Research additional revenue-generating processes and implement new procedures</li>
<li>
Develop analytical skills and contribute to department’s continuous improvement</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div>Business/Functional Results</div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Manage hotel performance for a hotel or portfolio of hotels with goal to grow market share, beat cash revenue targets while maintaining lodging contribution to casino gaming targets.</li>
<li>
Must approach work in a structured manner to ensure ad-hoc requests or analyses do not interfere with execution of daily, weekly and monthly critical tasks for each hotel.</li>
<li>
Ensure full optimization and utilization of critical RM systems.</li>
<li>
Produce weekly forecast and ensure accuracy for effective segment yielding and pricing.</li>
<li>
Identify need times and partner with Casino, Hospitality Marketing to ensure profitable events and promotions are in place to drive incremental demand over need dates.</li>
<li>
Partner with group sales to align demand trends and To-Be targets to ensure targets are met.</li>
<li>
Ensure blocks for Group, NCM, and Tour segments are managed appropriately.</li>
<li>
Be the gatekeeper to prevent needless discounting that promotes trade down, cannibalization or displaces higher value customers. Inform VP of Revenue Management of conflicts.</li>
<li>
Set criteria for casino discounting based on demand levels. Set NI criteria and work with casino marketing to ensure Event and DM offers map to demand needs.</li>
<li>
Monitor room pace and pricing; adjust and align strategy as needed to achieve goals.</li>
<li>
When hotel performance falls below expectations, identify root cause of shortfall; leverage other analytic groups as needed. Socialize root cause and align stakeholders on mitigating strategies to correct performance problems.</li>
<li>
Develop annual lodging budgets and align stakeholders on budget strategy.</li>
<li>
Meet deadlines and communicate with VP pro-actively to renegotiate deadlines at risk.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div>Leadership / Managing Execution</div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Anticipate Market demand environment and collaborate with VP of Revenue Management to develop strategies that deliver the most profitable mix of customers across Casino Comp, Casino Cash, FIT Retail, FIT Discount and Group segments.</li>
<li>
Ensure Casino, Marketing, Leisure Sales, Group and other critical departments are aligned on strategy to execute flawlessly. Leverage VP of Revenue Management as needed to accomplish this task.</li>
<li>
Recognize shifts in market demand environment and pivot quickly to adjust strategies. Align critical departments to ensure flawless execution.</li>
<li>
Proactively communicate strategies with key stake holders to ensure alignment. Build comfort when taking risk, establish credibility and confidence in RM discipline. Leverage VP of Revenue Management to accomplish this task.</li>
<li>
Lead effective bi-weekly Hotel/Pod meetings to communicate performance, forecast risk/opportunity and actions needed to meet/exceed targets.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div>Building Relationships</div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Foster strong relationships with key departments: casino marketing, hospitality marketing, marketing analytics, ecommerce, distribution, leisure sales, group sales.</li>
<li>
Build effective working relationships with property and pod leadership. Ensure leadership understand the value of revenue management activities and are aligned on strategies to achieve goals.</li>
<li>
Sell ideas persuasively and influence without direct authority.</li>
<li>
Keep VP of Revenue Management aware of conflicts and decisions that have material impact on results.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div>Learning/Applying Personal Expertise</div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Actively pursue learning and self-development to enhance personal, and professional growth.</li>
<li>
Seek and welcome feedback. Take action to enhance performance based on experiences &amp; coaching. Admit to and learn from mistakes.</li>
</ul>

<b>
Qualifications</b><div>
</div>
<div>
Required</div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in Business, Economics, Engineering, Finance, Revenue Management, Hospitality Administration or in another quantitative field preferred; or equivalent work experience in related disciplines</li>
<li>
At least a minimum of two(2) years’ of experience in revenue management, marketing, forecasting or pricing in hospitality, or the equivalent with quantitative market-related experience and demonstrated ability to understand the nuances of a market and marketing operations</li>
<li>
Hospitality, Analytics or Marketing Professional with revenue management experience</li>
<li>
Experience in developing and interpreting statistical models, and in using statistics to explain patterns to colleagues with little knowledge of statistics</li>
<li>
Experience in using predictive and historical models in multi-segmented markets with dynamic pricing</li>
<li>
Advanced Excel knowledge and experience with automated systems and spreadsheets</li>
<li>
Experience in database management—knowledge of LMS, AS400, VBA, and SQL preferred</li>
<li>
Project management skills and ability to complete multiple time-sensitive projects with competing demands for resources</li>
<li>
Excellent communication skills Executive-level communications skills</li>
<li>
Knowledge of the gaming and hospitality industries</li>
<li>
Need to be a seasoned revenue management and analytics professional capable of building industry-leading tools and processes while leading an organization with potentially broad geographic coverage.
</li>
<li>Must have strong Executive presence, business acumen and presentation skills</li>
</ul>
