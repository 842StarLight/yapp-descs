<h2>The UPS Store Retail Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Job Summary</b></p>
<p>The Center Associate is responsible for the day-to-day operations of this retail location. Our associates are trained to deliver world-class customer service to all customers. The ideal candidate has a post high school education (college coursework or a degree), at least a year of retail store operations experience, strong leadership skills, good computer/internet/software knowledge, the physical ability to perform this job (lifting, bending, etc.), and knows how to “listen and lead.” Owner is willing to train the right candidate to Manager's position.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities and Duties</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Delivers outstanding customer service to walk-in customers and telephone inquiries</li>
<li>Maintains a clean and safe working environment</li>
<li>Empathizes and manages demanding, impatient, and/or frustrated customers to turn a stressful event into a positive experience</li>
<li>May open and/or close the store and perform end-of-day accounting, etc.</li>
<li>Packages shipments for safe arrival, including assembling stock boxes, building custom boxes, and properly cushioning contents</li>
<li>Takes ownership of customers’ packing, shipping, and/or print problems and offers viable solutions</li>
<li>Expertly operates POS equipment</li>
<li>Helps with other Center functions as needed, such as faxing, copying, laminating, and binding</li>
<li>Maintains strong attention to detail when proofing all materials</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications and Skills</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or GED required</li>
<li>Retail/customer service experience</li>
<li>Good communication and people skills</li>
<li>Strong computer skills, including Microsoft Office and Adobe Suites</li>
<li>Prompt, reliable, and responsible</li>
<li>Able to lift 40+ pound</li>
<li>Notary Public is a real plus</li>
</ul>
