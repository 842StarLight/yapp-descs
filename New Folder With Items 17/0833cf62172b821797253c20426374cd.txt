<h2>Senior Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div><b>Position Objective</b></div>
<div>
Responsible for servicing and marketing the needs of Lockton clients under the general guidance of the Unit Manager and/or Account Executive and in Accordance with Lockton procedures. Will support the unit in renewal process as other technical initiatives as assigned.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Essential Duties</b></div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Request and review updated exposure information from client</div></li>
<li><div>
Create coverage specifications</div></li>
<li><div>
Determine and collaborate on market selection</div></li>
<li><div>
Create underwriter transmittal with submission</div></li>
<li><div>
Partially complete applications for client</div></li>
<li><div>
File copy of submission in shared drive</div></li>
<li><div>
Review loss projections and collateral analysis (at renewal and midterm as needed)</div></li>
<li><div>
Analyze/compare loss and exposure information to expiring year</div></li>
<li><div>
Coordinate international network where needed (at renewal and midterm as needed)</div></li>
<li><div>
Review and negotiate quotations from underwriters</div></li>
<li><div>
Form review/coverage analysis/checklist</div></li>
<li><div>
Translate specifications into proposal, including overall program comparison</div></li>
<li><div>
Review loss forecast and collateral analysis with client</div></li>
<li><div>
Obtain written order to bind from client</div></li>
<li><div>
Submit written orders to bind to insurers</div></li>
<li><div>
Review binders from insurers for accuracy</div></li>
<li><div>
Help facilitate online quotes</div></li>
<li><div>
Obtain signed binders from insurers</div></li>
<li><div>
Mark up Lockton binder—when needed</div></li>
<li><div>
Mod projections</div></li>
<li><div>
Transmit renewal documents to client</div></li>
<li><div>
Obtain signed program agreements (and check for accuracy)</div></li>
<li><div>
Review and sign off on renewal certificate masters</div></li>
<li><div>
Complete client billing sheets</div></li>
<li><div>
Manage marketing file</div></li>
<li><div>
Prepare policy digest</div></li>
<li><div>
Prepare major coverages chart</div></li>
<li><div>
Create request to underwriters for policy corrections</div></li>
<li><div>
Perform policy review</div></li>
<li><div>
Convert final and bound to summary/update workbooks</div></li>
<li><div>
Premium allocations</div></li>
<li><div>
Process premium audits/check for accuracy and update</div></li>
<li><div>
Review deductible and/or retro adjustments</div></li>
<li><div>
Loss source analysis as needed</div></li>
<li><div>
Provide feedback from client to underwriters</div></li>
<li><div>
Self-insured filings, if applicable</div></li>
<li><div>
Respond to coverage questions from client</div></li>
<li><div>
Review client contracts</div></li>
<li><div>
Contribute ideas to risk map</div></li>
<li><div>
Establish and maintain underwriter relationships</div></li>
<li><div>
Provide market update to client</div></li>
<li><div>
Identify opportunities for expanded business</div></li>
<li><div>
Participate in claims reviews, if applicable</div></li>
<li><div>
Partner with Claims on coverage determination and intent</div></li>
<li><div>
Review client’s program structure to identify opportunities and gaps (ongoing)</div></li>
<li><div>
Provide recommendations for insurance program improvements (ongoing)</div></li>
<li><div>
Review prospect information</div></li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div></div>
<div>
<div>
<b>Qualifications</b><br>
</div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Bachelor’s degree in business or equivalent education and/or experience.</div></li>
<li><div>
Minimum of three to five years’ working knowledge of commercial property and casualty coverages and services required.</div></li>
<li><div>
Experience in marketing and servicing the insurance needs of commercial accounts.</div></li>
<li><div>
Understanding of commercial rating concepts.</div></li>
<li><div>
Successful completion of Insurance 21, 22, and 23.</div></li>
<li><div>
Organizational and time management skills to prioritize heavy workloads to meet time sensitive deadlines.</div></li>
<li><div>
Strong knowledge of Microsoft products (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook) or similar software applications.</div></li>
<li><div>
Strong presentation skills.</div></li>
<li><div>
Demonstrated analytical and problem-solving skills, including research and investigation.</div></li>
<li><div>
Demonstrated interpersonal communication skills and ability to interact with Associates, Producers and Clients at all levels of responsibility.</div></li>
<li><div>
Willingness to delegate as appropriate.</div></li>
<li><div>
Demonstrated understanding of when to proceed alone and when to involve the Unit Manager, Producer, and others.</div></li>
<li><div>
Demonstrated interpersonal communication and interaction with company personnel for favorable relationship management and account outcome.</div></li>
<li><div>
Demonstrated professional, tactful negotiation and persuasion skills to achieve objectives.</div></li>
<li><div>
Cognizant of workflows on a proactive basis without AE interaction and in accordance with QA guidelines, and demonstrated initiative to support AEs working in tandem for an efficient and effective product outcome.</div></li>
<li><div>
Ability to coach and mentor and train other Associates.</div></li>
<li><div>
Personal presence and appearance to interface with top management of other businesses including insurance carrier representatives and clients, representing Lockton in the highest professional manner.</div></li>
<li><div>
Wholly understand the concepts, issues, and overall impact of market pricing.</div></li>
<li><div>
Ability to analyze quotes for coverage adequacy.</div></li>
<li><div>
Ability to use internet and intranet as resources for information.</div></li>
<li><div>
Ability to travel by automobile and aircraft, and stay for more than one day and night.</div></li>
<li><div>
Firm, fluent grasp of English language with above average verbal, written, and interpersonal skills to interact with Associates at all levels of responsibility.</div></li>
<li><div>
Ability to speak into and adequately hear voices from telephone equipment.</div></li>
<li><div>
Legal right to work in the United States</div></li>
</ul>
