<h2>IT Help Desk Support</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>About SEM</b></p>
<p>Security Engineered Machinery Co., Inc. (SEM) is a market leader in providing innovative destruction and sanitization equipment at the highest security level to the U.S Federal Government and its various agencies.SEM’s products include high security paper shredders, disintegrators, hard drive destroyers, and multimedia sanitization systems.SEM is headquartered just outside of Boston.Please visit www.semshred.com for more information.</p>
<p><b>About the Position</b></p>
<p>SEM has an outstanding opportunity in Westboro for an IT Help Desk professional to support SEM’s IT network, hardware, and software needs. The position reports to the Manager of Computer Services.</p>
<p><b>Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Respond to requests for technical assistance in person, or via phone, chat, or email.</li>
<li>Maintain all server/work station, hardware, and software related to SEM’s business.</li>
<li>Diagnose and resolve technical hardware and software issues.</li>
<li>Resolve technical IT issues with various SEM staff including customer service, engineering, accounting as well as outside sales and service personnel.</li>
<li>Coordinate network issues with outside VARs.</li>
<li>Research and recommend hardware and software upgrades and replacements as needed.Install all needed software on new systems for all new and existing users.</li>
<li>Assist employees with training and educating on software and media.</li>
<li>Manage phone system, including setup of new users, maintaining active directory, and scheduling required service.</li>
<li>Backup to administrator of SEM’s NetSuite enterprise system.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Required Education, Experience and Skills</b></p>
<ul>
<li>A vocational/associate education with emphasis on IT related topics.</li>
<li>1-3 years’ experience in direct helpdesk support.</li>
<li>Experience with Microsoft Office, NetSuite, and Microsoft Operating System software.</li>
<li>Able to demonstrate strong verbal and written communication, interpersonal skills, and build relationships with end-users and portray a professional image.</li>
<li>Organized, productive, self-motivated, and multi-tasking professional who is comfortable with many areas of responsibility and who has a solid work ethic.</li>
<li>Must be a U.S citizen and successfully pass a government background check.</li>
</ul>
