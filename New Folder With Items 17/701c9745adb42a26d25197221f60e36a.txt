<p>Would you like to increase customer adoption of AWS cloud in the Oil &amp; Gas Industry? Do you have the business savvy, technical knowledge, and leadership skills necessary to help AWS take its transformational messages to our Global &amp; Strategic account business customers? Do you have the experience to help customers envision the possibilities that our services and solutions can create for them, and then help them unlock that potential value for their businesses?<br>
<br>
We are hiring an accomplished Go to Market (GTM)/Biz Dev lead for Global accounts in the Oil &amp; Gas industry. As AWS grows its presence within this industry, the GTM-BD lead will assist our sales teams focusing on solving business problems that ultimately drive greater AWS adoption and customer value. Cloud services include the full range of AWS offerings across workloads and platform services that are most relevant for the industry. This person is accountable for developing the strategy and executing it to expand AWS market share in our top accounts in Oil &amp; Gas.<br>
<br>
This person will work closely with industry sales leadership and their teams to drive measurable goal attainment across the full suite of Global &amp; Strategic account goals. A proven track record of developing and executing sales play and initiatives with tangible results is a must. The ideal candidate possesses a business development / sales background whom has led tactical and strategic initiatives with proven, measurable results.<br>
<br>
As the GTM-BD lead your responsibilities will include partnering with AWS corporate platform services and industry teams to identify business solutions that address customer needs. Then you will bring the solutions to market, leveraging AWS programs, initiatives, funding, and best practices. You will own the necessary business and technical relationships with both our partners and proserve organizations, enabling the sales team to drive their day-to-day interactions with prospects in order to build long-term business opportunity. Your responsibilities will include acting as a business trusted advisor to the sales teams, diving deep into data, inventing &amp; simplifying to create sales plays, and tracking progress against goals &amp; ROI to measure effectiveness. You also must be a leader of virtual teams. You will guide the Global Accounts sales teams &amp; leverage cross-functional resources to drive AWS adoption in the Oil &amp; Gas Industry.</p>
