<h2>Sales Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Location: NY, NJ, PA tri-state area</p>
<p><b>
COMPANY OVERVIEW:</b></p>
<p>
Precise Continental is an award winning specialty printer located just outside NYC in Harrison, NJ. We provide our clients with exceptional quality specialty printing, dedicated customer service, environmentally sound business practices, and a true business-minded approach to our projects. We strive to earn our reputation — and our client’s respect — with every project they entrust to us.</p>
<p><b>
POSITION SUMMARY:</b></p>
<p>
We are currently seeking an energetic, self-starter and results-driven sales person to sell our portfolio of print solutions to organizations in the financial services and luxury goods marketplace. We have a base of accounts that you’ll work with as well as look for additional growth opportunities in the marketplace. If you are looking for an exciting consultative sales career that lets you help these businesses grow their business, we may be a perfect match!</p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
What We Are Looking For:</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>4 year college degree and/or equivalent experience</li>
<li>At least 3-5 years of Outside Business-to-Business Sales Experience</li>
<li>Proven Successful Sales Track Record (provide method and historical results)</li>
<li>Experience in direct marketing, digital and/or local advertising sales or as an advertising agency account executive a plus</li>
<li>A ‘real world’ understanding of the variety of marketing options for financial services and luxury goods marketers</li>
<li>Excellent communication (oral and written) and strong customer follow-up skills</li>
<li>Computer Literate with proficiency in MS Office (i.e. PowerPoint, Excel and Word) and the Internet for research</li>
<li>Solution Based or Consultative Sales Training or Experience preferred</li>
<li>Detail-Oriented with the ability to follow-up on tasks</li>
<li>Experience tracking customer data in CRM, weekly activity and sales pipeline management</li>
<li>Ability to prioritize and work independently with minimal supervision</li>
<li>Valid driver’s license, good driving record and dependable transportation</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Position Responsibilities</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>We have a base of accounts in the financial services and luxury goods marketplace that you’ll work with to maintain the existing business, and identify and close new opportunities for growth through new products and increased volume.</li>
<li>New business development via identifying, qualifying and selling new prospects. Will also join and participate in industry associations and participating in networking events.</li>
<li>Meet and exceed all sales quotas for existing accounts and new business clients</li>
<li>Retain and grow existing account base via cross-selling additional services</li>
<li>Conduct needs-based consultative meetings with prospects and existing accounts including purchasing departments, heads of marketing and C-level decision makers</li>
<li>Build relationships with new and existing clients</li>
<li>Prepare and present ideas and proposals and follow-up on all quotes</li>
<li>Develop solution-based presentations that address clients’ business needs</li>
<li>Prepare and submit weekly activity call logs and actual-to-target status</li>
<li>Open and available for ongoing training / education from both self-study and corporate training</li>
<li>Close sales and provide excellent customer service and follow-up</li>
<li>Enjoy working in a face-paced, dynamic, team-oriented environment</li>
</ul>
<p><b>COMPENSATION:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Competitive base salary and unlimited commissions/bonuses</li>
<li>Competitive benefits package</li>
</ul>
