<h2>Senior Brand Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div><div><div>
<p><i>California Olive Ranch Inc. is transforming the olive oil landscape. With our two brands, California Olive Ranch and Lucini Italia, we are focused on bringing high-quality products to the everyday supermarket consumer. We are a values-driven company who believes in breaking down silos, being a pioneer, enriching our environment and never ceasing to grow. Our employees are constantly presented with opportunities to learn, be creative, and bring ideas to life. We offer competitive wages, benefits and paid time off. No day is ever the same. If you are looking for a chance to learn, grow, and establish a rewarding career… Come join our dynamic team!</i>This position is responsible for developing California Olive Ranch into the most recognized and admired brands of olive oil in the world.</p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for building, managing and executing the brand’s marketing strategy and plan across channels and segments in support of the company’s strategic plan</li>
<li>Manage planning of, execution of and track effectiveness of marketing tactics such as blogger relationships, brand ambassador partnerships, in-store retail opportunities, foodservice marketing efforts</li>
<li>Manage planning of, execution of and track effectiveness of digital strategy, social media platform growth, and digital advertising for purpose of attracting new customers while retaining brand loyalists</li>
<li>Manage outside agency partners in support of marketing plan: PR, design, digital, research and others to perform to or in excess of KPIs</li>
<li>Represent as brand spokesperson as needed in PR efforts, media tours, events and harvest tours</li>
<li>Oversee the Marketing Coordinator role, in partnership with the Lucini Marketing Manager, in all day-to-day activities and training as well as ensuring the role meets assigned annual objectives</li>
<li>In partnership with Channel Marketing Specialist, support sales team; oversee preparation of marketing PPT sales materials, advertising assets, broker newsletters, and other materials in support of sales’ efforts.</li>
<li>In partnership with Events Manager, support trade shows, harvest tours, media events, as well as local marketing efforts and grower marketing efforts</li>
<li>In partnership with Marketing Chief of Staff, support other cross-functional departments as needed.</li>
<li>Develop and manage annual marketing budget to execute to or under annual spend, including monthly review of BVA, and ad-hoc reporting as needed to maintain budget</li>
<li>Develop and manage integrity and aesthetic of all brand assets, including logos, product imagery and photography assets used in companywide, sales support and marketing activities</li>
<li>Participate in New Product Innovation team by managing and supporting new COR-branded packaging designs, change to existing design, of all COR packaging in our New Product Innovation process; Coordinate consumer testing, and launch plans</li>
<li>Help develop ideas for new products, working with Marketing, Sales, and consumer research experts</li>
<li>Communicate to Marketing Chief of Staff as well as other senior management, companywide and key stakeholders, the results of marketing programs
</li>
<li>Communicate a positive image of the company to fellow staff members, customers, and the public.
