<h2>Data Center Application Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Eaton’s North American Sales division is currently seeking a Data Center Application Engineer to join our team. This position is based at our Hanover, MD location.</b><b> </b><b>Relocation assistance will be provided if residing outside 50 miles of this location.</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>The primary function of the Application Engineer is to achieve acceptability of Eaton’s Electrical products in the data center / mission critical marketplace, while providing technical leadership and mentoring for sales engineers, outside rep agencies, VARS, and to both internal and external customers. This position will be responsible for leading the design, development and validation of Eaton products and service applications. This position will be familiar with competitive product offerings in the data center segment and be able to position Eaton products to best compete against competitive products. The primary customer target industry is the Multi -Tenant (Co-location), Enterprise, and Cloud data center market, working directly with the end user, as well as front-end Consulting Engineering firms and the installing/procuring General Contractors/Electrical Contractors as needed.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>The primary functions include; providing efficient and accurate pre-sale application and technical support by analyzing and recommending solutions to meet customer’s needs; manage daily phone and email activity while providing directions, solutions, and recommendations; effectively communicate product value proposition and product line strategies; review and provide feedback on product literature developed by product line; provide feedback to product marketing and engineering regarding information, cross reference data, renewal parts information, technical drawings, and any other technical documentation found in hard copy or on the website; serve on special teams to enhance team performance and drive process improvement , productivity, customer satisfaction, and high performance work culture; and provide training to Technical Support Engineers.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Making what matters work at Eaton takes the passion of every employee around the world. We create an environment where creativity, invention and discovery become reality, each and every day. It’s where bold, bright professionals like you can reach your full potential—and where you can help us reach ours.</p>
<p><b>In this function you will:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>

Provide support on major mission critical projects including submittals drawings and interfacing with the customer and project consultant</p></li>
<li><p>
Work with institutional end users to gain acceptance and promote Eaton products</p></li>
<li><p>
Conduct product seminars and training on Eaton products and solutions to: end users, consultants and sales engineers</p></li>
<li><p>
Assist customers with design build opportunities.
</p></li>
<li><p>
Assist your area sales engineers in customer relationship building</p></li>
<li><p>
Provide technical submittal packages for customer approval</p></li>
<li><p>
Work in conjunction with Power Distribution sales teams and application engineers</p></li>
<li><p>
Provide technical support for your sales engineer teammates in your assigned area (Hanover, Maryland)</p></li>
</ul>
