<b>Job Description</b><br>
<p><b>Job Title: Director, Product Management</b><br>
<b>Requisition ID: 103062BR</b><br>
<br>
When you join us at Thermo Fisher Scientific, you’ll be part of a smart, driven team that shares your passion for exploration and discovery. With revenues of $22 billion and the largest investment in R&amp;D in the industry, we give our people the resources and opportunities to make significant contributions to the world.<br>
<br>
<b>Division: </b>Clinical Next Generation<br>
<b>Location: </b>South San Francisco, CA<br>
<br>
<b>How will you make an impact?</b><br>
<br>
The Director, Product Management will provide leadership and execution to manage the Instrument and Software portfolio in the Clinical Next-Generation Sequencing Division’s (CSD) in the Life Sciences Group (LSG) of Thermo Fisher Scientific. This person will help define the overarching Instrument and Software strategy and go-to-market plan in collaboration with the broader executive leadership team. This includes defining the product roadmap, the market access strategy (i.e., reimbursement), and informatics strategy. Lead team focused on developing and managing the complete portfolio of NGS instruments and software.<br>
<br>
<b>What will you do?</b><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Manage and lead the Instrument and Software product management team and drive market readiness in delivering new and existing products for the global market</li>
<li>
Work collaboratively with CSD commercial team (sales and market development) to drive commercial strategy and growth for the business. This includes defining and developing instrument and software engagement strategy with key opinion leaders</li>
<li>
Responsible for worldwide supply, demand, revenue, margin, and pricing for product portfolio; update and present forecasts regularly to management, finance, and manufacturing teams</li>
<li>
Lead and leverage analyses of business and market trends to define customer requirements, competitive strategy, and business opportunities for increasing customer and business value</li>
<li>
Manage the overall portfolio roadmap and lifecycle for new Instrument and Software development and corresponding business cases, with due emphasis on revenue growth, install base, and profitability for the portfolio. This includes balancing the investment profile across the portfolio to deliver IVD and RUO sales strategies</li>
<li>
Lead teams through new product development processes from concept through commercialization, including voice of customer research, definition of customer and product requirements and value proposition, manage product launches and sales training to achieve timely launches and commercial objectives</li>
</ul>
<b>
Leadership Responsibilities:</b><ul>
<li>
Provide thought leadership to overall CSD Instrument and Software product strategy and direction. Collaborates with CSD leadership to successfully commercialize new products, enter new markets, and achieve share and profitability goals by product category and market segments</li>
<li>
Lead and represent CSD business from a product management perspective in critical customer and precision medicine efforts to drive commercial success and key account efforts</li>
<li>
Lead major commercialization and/or product development initiatives driving multi-functional efforts to deliver revenue objectives and key strategic market positions</li>
<li>
Effectively represents Product Management and key internal leadership meetings, communicating with colleagues, global leadership team and senior management on progress against objectives and future plans for driving performance</li>
<li>
Collaborate with other Thermo Fisher organizations to leverage existing capabilities and resources.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>How will you get here?</b><div></div>
<br>
Bachelor’s Degree required, Master’s Degree, or higher preferred<br>
<br>
<b>Experience:</b><br>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>10+ years of direct product management or marketing experience in the diagnostics, genomics, or medical devices industry; Next generation sequencing experience required</li>
<li>
In-depth Instrument and Software market experience, with familiarity of the diagnostics and research industry</li>
<li>
Must have proven ability to build relationships and guide teams which include representatives from multiple functional areas, including R&amp;D, Sales, Program Management, and Manufacturing</li>
<li>
Must have experience in managing multiple stakeholders and resolving conflict within teams</li>
<li>
Must have excellent communication skills including oral, written and presentation techniques</li>
<li>
Ability to travel up to 40% including international</li>
</ul>
