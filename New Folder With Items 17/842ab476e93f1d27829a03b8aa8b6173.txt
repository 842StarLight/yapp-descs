<h2>Client Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>JOB SUMMARY</b></p>
<p>The Client Specialist (CS) is a full-time, on-site position to support testing department client account operations. This position will work closely with Pacific BioLabs Testing Department Heads and Business Development in ensuring that Client/Sponsor testing needs are met. The duties of the CS include 1) supporting the Client Account Manager in quote creation and quote follow-up and 2) department log-in and study file preparation.</p>
<p>The Client Specialist reports to Testing Department Management for testing department activities and to the Client Services Manager and Director of Business Development for all activities associated with client account operations and sales, respectively<b>.</b></p>
<p><b>JOB DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<p>SERVICE RESPONSIBILITIES TO CLIENTS</p>
<ul>
<li>Follow-up on non-binding estimates and formal quotes with clients via e-mail on behalf of Client Account Managers</li>
<li>Prepare formal quotes on behalf of Client Account Managers for potential and active clients</li>
<li>Assist in the invoicing and billing process</li>
<li>Deliver highest level of customer service to promote client retention</li>
</ul>
<p>INTERNAL SERVICE RESPONSIBILITIES</p>
<ul>
<li>Perform sample verification process at sample receipt per PBL SOPs</li>
<li>Perform department log-in and prepare study files according to regulatory treatment and PBL SOPs</li>
<li>Process and send samples out for subcontracted work when applicable</li>
<li>Assist in protocol preparation for routine tests</li>
<li>Track status of testing department studies (project management) for timelines (study completion)</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Assist with facilitating the completion of agreements (MSAs, NDAs, and SOWs)</li></ul>
<p>TECHNICAL RESPONSIBILITIES</p>
<ul>
<li>Maintain active database with current client information</li>
<li>Acquire proficiency in database searches and data exports</li>
<li>Maintain testing department document templates for study-specific protocols (if applicable)</li>
</ul>
<p>ADMINISTRATIVE RESPONSIBILITIES</p>
<ul>
<li>Adhere to PBL personnel policies as specified in the <i>Employee Handbook</i>
</li>
<li>Adhere to relevant PBL SOPs governing documentation</li>
<li>Comply with PBL SOPs pertaining to the handling of client complaints</li>
<li>Participate in general staff training sessions as these are scheduled</li>
<li>Participate as needed in company-wide projects designed to improve the overall effectiveness of PBL.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>JOB QUALIFICATIONS</b></p>
<p>EDUCATION/EXPERIENCE</p>
<ul><li>Four-year undergraduate degree (e.g. B.S. or comparable) with 1 year experience in a client-facing role preferred; degree in a relevant field (e.g. life sciences, communications) is a bonus.</li></ul>
<p>TECHNICAL SKILLS</p>
<ul>
<li>Must be proficient with computer database software (e.g. MS Office).</li>
<li>Must be proficient in typing.</li>
</ul>
<p>INTELLECTUAL APTITUDE/CRITICAL THINKING SKILLS</p>
<ul>
<li>Must have excellent organizational skills; must be able to prioritize diverse tasks.</li>
<li>Must possess solid critical thinking skills; must be able to anticipate and solve problems in a systematic manner.</li>
<li>Must be detail oriented; able to detect minor discrepancies in study related documents.</li>
</ul>
<p>INTER-PERSONAL SKILLS</p>
<ul>
<li>Excellent communication skills (written and verbal) required. Must be able to communicate complex ideas in easily understandable terms. Must enjoy meeting and interacting with people when necessary.</li>
<li>Professional telephone manner essential.</li>
<li>Must be able to handle confidential information with discretion.</li>
<li>Must be able to work well with others and function as part of a team.</li>
<li>Must be able to work with persons of diverse background.</li>
</ul>
<p>PHYSICAL ABILITIES</p>
<p>Reasonable accommodations will be made for persons with disabilities.</p>
<ul>
<li>Job requires mobile use of hands and arms.</li>
<li>Must be able to work at a computer terminal for up to three hours at a time, and for up to eight hours on a given day.</li>
<li>Must be capable of spending up to three hours at a time on the telephone.</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>

<ul><li>relevant: 1 year (Preferred)</li></ul>

<ul><li>Bachelor's (Preferred)</li></ul>

<ul><li>One location</li></ul>
