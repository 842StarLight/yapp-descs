<h2>Sales Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>About this opportunity:</b><br>
<br>
If you are a top sales performer with a love for technology and a consultative selling process, this position is a dream career. Working as part of the sales team in conjunction with our solutions consulting team, this is your chance to do strategic work with our clients and gain valuable experience as you sell cutting edge software and deliver to our clients the full spectrum of benefits from Model Based Design, Digital Prototyping and Simulation technology and consulting services. Take this opportunity to join a smart, driven, growth-oriented company where you will constantly be on the forefront of today’s latest design and technology trends!<br>
<br>
Our customers love us and you will be the anchor of our relationship with them. Owning a geographic region, you will be the consistent point of contact the customer can rely on. We operate in a rapidly evolving industry, and M2 Technologies is a fast-paced, upbeat environment. If you are passionate about sales, fanatical about details, a relentless problem solver with excellent written and phone communication skills, we should connect now.<br>
<br>
<b>What Kind of Candidate are we looking for?</b><br>
<br>
Candidates experienced in software, finance or technology sales (and/or services) with a strong knowledge of the architecture, engineering, construction and owner/ operator industry preferred. Candidates should have prior experience in sales leadership.<br>
<br>
The 'Sales Executive' should be comfortable meeting with individuals at all levels within the clients’ business, from executives to end users, in order to understand business issues and discuss solutions while evaluating areas of opportunity for expanded implementation to create sales.<br>
<p><b>RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Develop and implement strategic goals and tactical planning for assigned sales territory</li>
<li>
Strategically develop new customers</li>
<li>
Maintain, develop, and become a business partner of the key personnel in all customer and prospective accounts</li>
<li>
Develop strong working knowledge of client business processes and common challenges, and how M2 Technologies products and services can address our clients’ business needs</li>
<li>
Meet and exceed sales volume and gross margin quota expectations</li>
<li>
Oversee all sales activities within defined region</li>
<li>
Assist with development and coordination of marketing initiatives within region</li>
<li>
Track and report weekly on all sales activity within region using CRM and other reporting tools</li>
<li>
Take complete advantage of internal training, research and outside education resources to maintain and continually enhance your knowledge of the industry and the utilization of CRM and other resource management tools</li>
</ul>
<p>
 <b>REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS</b></p>

<li>
Excellent verbal, written, presentation, and computer skills</li>
<li>
Demonstrated history of sales success and sales management</li>

<li>Proficiency in client requirements gathering and technical discovery,</li>
