<h2>Manager, Indirect</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>At KPMG, we are reimagining tax with you in mind. Become an integral part of a dynamic team within a collaborative, future forward environment at one of the world's top tax firms. Be surrounded by deep tax knowledge and equipped with the latest in cutting-edge tax technologies. And join an extensive network of functional, industry, legislative and technical specialists with access to our Ignition Centers, where technology innovation accelerates. We welcome people with a passion for tax, a spirit for new thinking, and a commitment to providing outstanding client service today, and tomorrow.</p>
<p>
KPMG is currently seeking a Manager to join our State and Local Tax (SALT) practice.</p>
<p>
Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Review sales, use, and telecommunications tax data received on a monthly basis from KPMG compliance clients to determine if it is in the proper format for input into KPMG's mechanized tax processing systems</li>
<li>
Arrange the monthly data received into the proper tax location codes, tax types, tax categories, authority levels, taxpayer entities, and tax amounts</li>
<li>
Apply client specific business rules to the tax data received from tax compliance clients to modify the data for input into KPMG mechanized tax processing systems.</li>
<li>
Once the data is ready for input, process the data for the returns using one or more of KPMG's mechanized tax systems</li>
<li>
Identify, document, and recommend client technological business requirements for sales, use, and telecommunications tax compliance processes, and system improvements
</li>
<li>Design, test and troubleshoot indirect tax technology solutions developed using Microsoft Access, Excel or SQL Server</li>
</ul>
<p>
Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor's degree in business, accounting, or a related field of study from an accredited college/university</li>
<li>
Licensed CPA, J.D., LL.M., MST (Master's in Taxation) or a Certified Member of the Institute for Professionals in Taxation (CMI) designation for non-CPA eligible candidates (Please note that any candidate hired by KPMG into this position that doesn't currently possess one of the aforementioned credentials/designations will be required to secure one within one year from the commencement of employment)</li>
<li>
Development knowledge using Visual Basic for Applications in Microsoft Access, and experience developing in .NET is preferred</li>
<li>
Proficiency in Microsoft Excel, Access, SQL Server, XML, PowerPoint, Project, Visio, or process mapping software and strong technical awareness and basic knowledge of global indirect tax issues</li>
<li>
Strong skills in indirect tax information systems, including strong project management skills and the ability to analyze client business needs</li>
<li>
Experience in reviewing and working with transaction tax data received from the billing systems of wire line/wireless telecommunications companies or cable/broadband companies, and a working knowledge of federal, state, and local telecommunications transaction taxes imposed in the United States</li>
</ul>
