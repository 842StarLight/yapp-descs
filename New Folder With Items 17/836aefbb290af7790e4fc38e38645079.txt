<div><b>SUMMARY:</b></div>
<div>
<div>Using discretion and independent judgment, the Case Manager/IRF-PAI Coordinator, coordinates the process of providing accurate and timely data to the appropriate entities involved in the Prospective Payment System for Medicare patients.</div>
<div></div>
<div>The Case Management/IRF-PAI Coordinator is a licensed clinician. The responsibilities for this job are approximately 40% of the time spent in a case management role, and 60% of the time spent in the IRF-PAI coordinator role, as directed by their supervisor.</div>
<div></div>
<div>In the IRF-PAI Coordinator role, this position coordinates with the Director of Quality/Risk to ensure IRF-PAI completion with the interdisciplinary team, gathering and input of data, transmission of data. The IRF-PAI Coordinator is responsible for coordinating the process of providing accurate and timely data to the appropriate entities involved in the Prospective Payment System for Medicare patients. The IRF-PAI Coordinator oversees all aspects of data collection on the IRF-PAI, ensures the presence of supporting documentation and provides staff education to ensure compliance with CMS guidelines.</div>
<div></div>
<div>In the Case Manager role, this position is responsible for coordinating the care and services for an assigned unit-based population. The Case Manager is responsible for promoting quality of care, supporting effective resource utilization, and facilitating the patient's progression of care to proactively plan for the patient's safe and timely discharge to the most appropriate level of care. The Case Manager serves as the key resource for the patient's multi-disciplinary care team and works collaboratively with all members of the patient's health care team and patient representatives to achieve desired clinical, financial and quality outcomes.</div>
<div></div>
<div>This job directly affects all members of the clinical team through accuracy of data from medical chart to CMS transmission.</div>
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></div>
<div>May include, but is not limited to the following:</div>
<ul>
<li>Collaborates with rehab liaison and admitting physician to determine the anticipated case mix group (CMG) to be assigned to each patient admitted.</li>
<li>Collects pertinent demographic, clinical and FIM data and enters it on the IRF-PAI.</li>
<li>Communicates with coders within 4 days of admission to determine etiologic diagnosis and co-morbid conditions present on admission.</li>
<li>Communicates estimated length of stay (LOS) to case manager for discharge planning and discussion at case conference.</li>
<li>Participates in all case conferences and reviews documentation concurrently throughout the stay to ensure accurate documentation of team conference discussion.</li>
<li>Reviews codes as provided by health information management for capture of documented conditions.</li>
<li>Tracks and reports regularly on 60% rule compliance</li>
<li>Provides guidance and ensures efficient performance in accordance with overall company and hospital goals and objectives as it relates to the quality / case management services departments.</li>
<li>Provides support, communications, and holds accountable data collection processes between hospital departments, and external entities.</li>
<li>Able to communicate effectively with individuals who have varying levels of education in order to accomplish goals.</li>
<li>Maintains compliance to all safety protocols and procedures as required by</li>
<li>Ensures completion of supportive documentation for FIMs in medical record</li>
<li>Completes admission and discharge IRF-PAI within CMS designated time frames.</li>
<li>Serves as a system-wide resource regarding state/federal regulations/laws and Joint Commission standards for all healthcare facilities.</li>
<li>Coordinates and provides care that is safe, timely, effective, efficient, equitable and patient centered.</li>
<li>Communicates regularly with patients and their families about Plans of Care, PT, OT and other treatment protocols.</li>
<li>Coordinates referrals both to and from our facility.</li>
<li>Assists with formulating a plan of care including expected outcomes for each patient based on diagnoses derived from patient assessments.</li>
<li>Educates and trains staff, including physician, nurses and therapists on documentation, to ensure that the plan of care and supporting documentation reflect the need for intensity of services provided, medical necessity of services provided and need for 24-hour rehab nursing.</li>
<li>Maintains a current knowledge of PPS regulations related to participation requirements for inpatient rehabilitation facilities.</li>
<li>Creates as needed and monitors educational modules on educational software.</li>
<li>Provides support to quality and case management areas via email, phone calls, and monthly phone conferences.</li>
</ul>
