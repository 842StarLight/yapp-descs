<h2>Staff Accountant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div><div><div>
<p><b>Brown Distributing Company</b> is a leading beverage distributor throughout the states of Virginia and Florida. Since 1919, Brown Distributing has grown into a beverage distribution business with combined sales of over 11 million cases annually and serving in excess of 8,000 retailers with over 500 employees.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Brown Distributing offers their employees competitive compensation, complete benefit package, including medical, dental, vision, company paid life insurance, company paid short term disability, long term disability and a 401K employer match.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<div>
We are currently seeking a <b>Staff Accountant</b> to join our team in<b> West Palm Beach, FL.</b> The ideal candidate is a self-starter and team player with the ability to multitask and perform a cross-functional role in supporting the Accounting team. An ideal candidate will have a business related degree, ideally in finance or accounting. The position will focus mainly on a backup for route settlement, beverage tax, daily cash reconciliations and monthly GL account reconciliations.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<b>
Compensation:</b> Will commensurate depending on experience.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The position will go through a 4 week training program as follows:</div>
<ul>
<li>
<b>
Week 1 &amp; 2</b> – Cross Departmental On-Boarding</li>
<li>
<b>
Week 3</b> – Route Settlement Training Cash Reconciliation and other various balance sheet account review/reconciliation training</li>
<li>
<b>
Week 4</b> – Beverage Tax</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p>Primary duties include but are not limited to:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Bank reconciliations</li>
<li>
GL Account reconciliations</li>
<li>
Various tax forms i.e. 1099s (sales and use tax)</li>
<li>
Prepares monthly account reconciliations and audit work papers</li>
<li>
Prepares journal entries for appropriate adjustments to balance sheet and income statement accounts</li>
<li>
Review and Post daily sub ledger transactions</li>
<li>
Fixed assets and depreciations</li>
<li>
Assist with year-end Financial audit and others</li>
<li>
Cross functions between team members are assumed as necessary in order to meet department deadlines and goals</li>
<li>
Develops and maintains, procedures to stream line processes within the department/organization and provides recommendations and tools that can increase integrity and accuracy</li>
<li>
Performs other related duties as required and assigned</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>

</b></h2>
