<p><b>Who We Are</b></p>
<p>
The purpose and passion of DATIS is to serve the Health and Human Services industry with a best-in-class, industry-specific software that can help these organizations better manage their workforce and achieve their challenging missions with greater efficiency. Our Health and Human Services clients serve more than 500,000 children and adults annually, across more than 35 states. Their missions are to serve their communities by providing a vast spectrum of services for primary care, behavioral health, substance abuse, homelessness, intellectual developmental disabilities, children and families, veteran's services, and many more. DATIS employees are problem solvers at heart and feel that if we can improve the operational processes of our clients through technology, we can, in small part, help our clients serve their clients, fulfill their missions, and improve our communities across the country. Our clients inspire and challenge us every day to build better solutions to meet their unique and ever-evolving business needs. We are looking for driven, passionate, and dedicated employees to join us in delivering cutting-edge solutions that enable our clients to change the world.</p>
