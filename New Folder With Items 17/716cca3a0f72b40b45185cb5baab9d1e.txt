<h2>Senior Digital Inventory & Yield Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Description</b></h2>
<div>
The Senior Inventory &amp; Yield Analyst will play a crucial role in maximizing value and monetization of our digital inventory. The primary focus of this role is to proactively identify positive and negative revenue and inventory trends on a line item, campaign, advertiser, agency, and sales team level to optimize delivery and mitigate risk to maximize Digital ad sales revenue.<br>
<br>
What will you be doing?<br>
</div>
<div>
<br>
Core Responsibilities:<br>
<ul><li>Yield Management: Understand seasonal inventory/revenue marketplace and forecasting trends as they relate to ad delivery, pacing, and yield optimization to mitigate risk and maximize revenue for all Digital properties</li></ul>
</div>
<div>
<br>
<ul><li>Analytics: Manage daily/weekly/monthly analytics reporting to enable the business to operate transparently and efficiently while capitalizing on product insights to track revenue success/opportunities</li></ul>
</div>
<div>
<br>
<ul><li>Reporting: Manage recurring and ad hoc reports to internal stakeholders and communicate insights in a clear and concise manner</li></ul>
</div>
<div>
<br>
<ul><li>Communication: Present findings to all appropriate audiences on a consistent basis via email, verbal, in-person, and other communication methods as deemed appropriate</li></ul>
<br>
Additional Responsibilities:<br>
<ul><li>Responsible for reviewing and approving advertising campaigns by managing ad inventory levels to assist in campaign optimization</li></ul>
</div>
<div>
<br>
<ul><li>Present recommendations to a variety of audiences, such as Senior Management, Ad Operations, and Sales Planning in a verbal or written manner</li></ul>
</div>
<div>
<br>
<ul><li>Generate reports to monitor inventory trends and translate into actionable insights</li></ul>
</div>
<div>
<br>
<ul><li>Work closely with cross-functional teams to be able to understand the inventory, reporting and workflow needs</li></ul>
</div>
<div>
<br>
<ul><li>Identify and implement Inventory Management related workflow and technology enhancements to support scaling the business</li></ul>
</div>
<div>
<br>
<ul><li>Collaborate with various constituents and business groups to gather reporting/analysis requirements and to drive a data-driven culture</li></ul>
</div>
<div>
<br>
<ul><li>Prepare, manage and distribute standard daily/weekly/monthly/quarterly reporting for Senior Management teams, using various reporting tools</li></ul>
<br>
</div>

Qualifications</b></h2>
<ul><li>Superior Analytical Skills</li></ul>
o The ability to analyze performance data to drive campaign recommendation and troubleshoot issues; experience manipulating large data sets, interpreting data trends and reporting results in a clear and effective manner via Excel pivot tables and/or Business Intelligence tools.<br>
o Strong organizational skills and ability to multi-task; detailed oriented; demonstrated ability to manage multiple projects at once.<br>
<ul><li>Experience with Sales</li></ul>
