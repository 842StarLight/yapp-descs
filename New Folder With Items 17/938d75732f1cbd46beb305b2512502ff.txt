<h2>Sr. Software Engineer, RPG</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Agilysys provides enterprise software, services and solutions for the hospitality industry in North America, Europe and Asia. Products include points-of-sale, property management, inventory and procurement, analytics solutions, and mobile and wireless solutions that are designed to improve efficiency and enhance the guest experience. We serve casinos, resorts, hotels, foodservice venues, stadiums, and cruise lines.</p>
<p>
Agilysys is looking for an experienced Senior Software Engineer to join our team responsible for delivering our Lodging Management Solution (LMS). This position will be part of a cross-functional team located in our Las Vegas, NV.</p>
<p>
The ideal candidate has a great attitude and is team oriented but also able to work well independently on one or more projects. We are looking for someone who is self-motivated with a strong desire to improve their knowledge/skills, is passionate about delivering great products for our customers, thrives in an agile development environment, and emphasizes quality code in our products. We are looking for someone with solid coding skills who understands how specification, design, coding, documentation and quality assurance pieces fit together to create a great product.</p>
