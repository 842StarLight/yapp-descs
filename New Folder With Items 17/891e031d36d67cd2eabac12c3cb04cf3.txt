<h2>Sales Development Executive – B2B Lead Generation</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Pycube is a fast growing company that provides innovative products and services that are shaping a new age for managing enterprise assets in any healthcare facility. Our expertise in managing assets helps our customers deliver care to their patients more effectively and efficiently at a lower cost.
 If you are a Superstar Sales Development Executive with a Hunter mindset and proven track record of qualifying leads and looking to build your career with a fast growing company that’s invested in your personal development, a solid compensation package and a huge opportunity to move up within the organization, see below job description and apply.</p>
<p> Job Type: Full-time, Commission</p>
 <p>Roles and Responsibilities</p>
<ul>
 <li>Responsible for prospecting, qualifying and generating new sales leads to support the Sales Directors in the healthcare industry</li>
<li> Design successful inbound and outbound lead generation campaigns</li>
<li> Source new customers in the market of company services and solutions</li>
<li> Follow up on leads and conduct research to identify potential prospects</li>
<li> Build and cultivate prospect relationships by initiating communications and conducting follow-up communications in order to move opportunities through the sales funnel</li>
<li>
 Work with Sales Directors to develop and grow the sales pipeline to consistently meet quarterly revenue goals
</li>
<li> Report KPIs and sales status to management and the sales team.</li>
</ul>
 <p>Skills</p>
<ul>
<li> Minimum 3 year of experience in Sales or Lead generation (preferably in the healthcare industry</li>
<li> Demonstrated ability to design successful inbound and outbound lead generation campaigns and qualifying leads</li>
 <li>Demonstrated ability to probe pain points, extract information and business requirements from prospects</li>
 <li>A proven track record of meeting and/or exceeding sales and activity quotas</li>
 <li>Organized and ability to multitask, manage multiple assignments and prioritize tasks in a fast paced, deadline driven and team oriented environment</li>
 <li>Strong written, communication and presentation skills</li>
 <li>Outgoing personality with outstanding phone mannerism and expertise at developing relationships</li>
 <li>Excellent Knowledge of CRM systems</li>
 <li>Be a great team player, have high self esteem and show empathy to customer</li>
</ul>
<p> Candidate Attributes Needed:</p>
<ul>
 <li>Work ethic: proactively pursuing the company mission with a high degree of energy and daily activity</li>
 <li>Persistent and positive attitude: will not take NO for an answer. Burning desire to succeed and will do whatever it takes to make it happen</li>

 <li>Curiosity: the ability to understand customer’s context through effective questioning and listening</li>
 <li>Coachability: the ability to absorb and apply coaching</li>
