<h2>Customer Service Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>We believe that the skills you bring to OUP are just a starting point. We want you to have the opportunity to expand your abilities, and innovate and develop in areas which both interest you, and support your aspirations</p>
<p>
This role will require that the successful candidate understands the customer service environment and can confidently convey customer focus to all aspects of the organization.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Support the sales effort by handling customer calls and recording wrap code information into the system.</li>
<li>Quickly and efficiently perform all functions of the department to ensure work is processed in a timely manner. This will include processing orders and adjustments, handling customer claims, and customer service inquiries.</li>
<li>Manage and resolve complex customer issues; respond promptly to customer needs; solicit customer feedback to improve service, and resolve problems and follow through until the customer is satisfied with the results.</li>
<li>Work diligently to meet commitments; communicate diplomatically and tactfully with a broad and diverse base of customers via inbound and outbound phone calls, e-mail, fax, and written correspondence; gather and analyze information skillfully, and develop alternative solutions.</li>
<li>Works well in group problem-solving situations; displays willingness to make decisions and exhibits sound and accurate judgment. Importantly, provide customer support at the point of sale for billing problems, the status of the order, and account queries.</li>
<li>Compile and track data to make recommendations to improve customer relations and fully understand the customer service environment and exhibit exceptional customer care.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>An ability to learn various OUP publishing divisions, including specific products and best-selling titles.</li>
<li>Develop knowledge of conducting business in various market segments, i.e. school market requirements, retail, wholesale, and international Candidates must possess excellent verbal and written communication skills, and the ability to successfully handle high-pressure situations.</li>
<li>Exceptional organizational and sound decision-making skills. Perform duties in a self-managed environment and be able to balance team and individual responsibilities.</li>
<li>Proficient computer experience, which includes Microsoft Office software, and a typing proficiency of at least 35 wpm.</li>
<li>An Associate’s degree or equivalent experience plus one year of Customer Service experience is required.</li>
<li>Call center experience preferred. Must possess problem-solving skills and be detail oriented.</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>Hourly Pay: </i></b><i>~$14.25</i></p>

GJC Level: S2 (for internal purposes only)</i></b></p>
