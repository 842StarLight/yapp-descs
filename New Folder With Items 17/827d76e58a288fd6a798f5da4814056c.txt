<h2>Advisor Engagement Manager - Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>About this role</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
About the role</b>:</p>
<p>
You can work with us at one of the world’s top FinTech companies. We sell our Aladdin Wealth Technologies to some of the top domestic and global investment and financial firms. The US Advisor Engagement Team is passionate about engaging with and supporting the financial advisors of our partners. We want to help our partner’s financial advisors be successful and help our internal and external sales groups bring BlackRock digital technologies and investment services to Financial Advisors across the country.</p>
<p>
As an Advisor Engagement Manger (AEM), your role will focus in a few exciting areas. Firstly, as a Financial Advisor, and as part of a team, you will be a client relationship manager and provide financial advice to nearly five thousand clients who represent nearly one billion dollars in retail client AUM. Your second focus will be to engage with the home offices, centers of influence, and the Financial Advisors of BlackRock’s Partners to champion our Digital Technology Platforms.</p>
<p>
We work with internal and external teams to collaborate on our platforms, product enhancements and priorities, the overall go-to-market and in-market technology efforts, advisor engagement, and adoption strategies for our partners. This will include an emphasis on creating thoughtful training plans, training materials, videos, sales support resources, pitch decks, and other partner activation and engagement planning that will support our partners and their advisors.</p>
<p>
We are looking for people who want to join a start-up-like team within the greater BlackRock business, who can focus on several projects at once, who can wear several hats, learn many technologies quickly, and who is excited by new and different work each day.</p>
<p>
If any of this excites you, we want to talk to you.</p>
<p><b>
Basic Qualifications</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>2+ years job-related experience providing investment advice and/or managing client relationships</li>
<li>Series 65 or equivalent</li>
<li>1+ years experience developing and delivering SAAS sales/training</li>
<li>Strong work ethic and attention to detail</li>
<li>Current market acumen</li>
<li>Ability to learn new technologies quickly</li>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Preferred Qualifications</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Any operations experience at an RIA</li>
<li>Any experience designing training and sales materials</li>
<li>Any experience working with/on selling SAAS</li>
<li>Proficiency in design and light data analytics</li>
</ul>
