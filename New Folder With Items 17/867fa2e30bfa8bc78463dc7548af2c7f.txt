<h2>Account Executive, News Digital Group</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div><div>
<div>
<div>
<b>ROLE SUMMARY:</b><br>
Work with a driven and highly motivated team to support the sales operations of NBCUniversal, one of the world’s leading media and entertainment companies. As an Account Executive at NBC Universal (NBCU), you will be responsible for developing, generating and maintaining new and existing business for Digital properties of TODAY, Nightly News, Meet the Press, MSNBC, and CNBC. Nightly News and MTP are both housed on NBCNews.com<br>
<br>
<b>RESPONSIBILITIES:</b><ul>
<li>
Generate/increase revenue of new and existing accounts from an established list of advertising agencies and clients to meet annual revenue goals</li>
<li>
Generate additional revenue from untapped sources</li>
<li>
Own, manage, and develop relationships with clients, agencies and advertisers through day-to-day interactions; actively grow industry relationships in order to gain marketplace intelligence</li>
<li>
Be well versed in digital advertising marketplace and trends including knowledge of Programmatic, Social, Mobile monetization, Video Distribution, Branded Content, and Rich Media solutions.
</li>
<li>Prepare and Present all Digital media opportunities
</li>
<li>Collaborate with linear counterparts helping to activate key linear accounts
</li>
<li>Manage all digital advertising from pre planning to post sale execution and optimization.
</li>
<li>Work with all networks within NBCU portfolio and use leverage in order to grow corporate share and ensure success of all properties</li>
<li>
Lead, develop, motivate and mentor support team of sales planner and sales assistant by developing strong team alliance, inclusive participation and structured training</li>
<li>
Communicate effectively within assigned office, as well as with other sales offices and various operations and sales support groups within the company</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>
<div>

<div>BASIC QUALIFICATIONS:<br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree</li>
<li>Minimum of 5+ years direct sales or sales support experience in Digital advertising industry</li>
</ul>

ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS:<br>
<ul>

<li>Must be willing to work in New York, NY</li>
<li>Must be willing to submit to a background investigation</li>
<li>Must have unrestricted work authorization to work in the United States</li>
<li>Must be 18 years or older</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>

<p><b>Desired Characteristics</b></p>
<div>DESIRED CHARACTERISTICS:<br>
<ul>
<li>Proven experience in prospecting new clients, generating sales leads and closing the sale</li>
<li>Tactical knowledge of the digital advertising monetization stack including digital video, branded content, mobile, ad targeting, programmatic and display.</li>
<li>Strong Organizational and Territory Sales Activity planning skills</li>
<li>Well read and Knowledgeable on industry trends affecting Digital ad monetization</li>
<li>Agency and client contacts in NY advertising market. Strong consideration given to contacts at Group M.</li>
<li>Ability to travel as necessary</li>
<li>Well Organized</li>
<li>Self Starter/ Reliable</li>
<li>Understand brand &amp; marketing objectives.</li>
<li>Strong communication, negotiation &amp; presentation skills</li>
<li>Capacity to work and analyze numeric data.</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal and client relation skills</li>
<li>Able to multi-task in a fast paced environment</li>
<li>Ability to build and maintain relationships at the senior level</li>
<li>Strong technical skills adept in the use of Salesforce CRM and Microsoft Office Suite.</li>
</ul>
