<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>About the team</b></h2>
Zillow Group is seeking a Sourcing Recruiter (Non-Technical) to proactively identify and assess passive and active talent for our growing Revenue Organization. As a member of our team, you would work closely with our Recruiters and Hiring Managers to develop creative strategies to find the best talent to support multiple brands under Zillow Group with a large focus on Biz (Ops, Strategy, Sales Managers, etc.) candidates. We are creative problem solvers who invest in strategic approaches to identify leading talent. We invest a lot of time into engaging passive and active candidates through a highly engaged approach and an awesome candidate experience. You are the first point of contact for many candidates. Are you up to the challenge of bringing a level of motivation, curiosity and scrappiness to our team?<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
About the role</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>
You will partner closely with aligned recruiting team to understand specific talent needs to successfully build a pipeline of active and passive top talent through various channels</li>
<li>
Interview and assess for technical and business skills</li>
<li>
You will evaluate candidates on Zillow Group core values</li>
<li>
Develop and execute on a sourcing strategy to build a solid pipeline of candidates and other talent segments as needed, utilizing various channels such as: applicants, referrals, LinkedIn, use of Boolean, personal network, blogs, forums, advertising, and researching to select sourcing tools to attract passive talent</li>
<li>
You will strategize different ways to build talent pipelines and execute on tactical research, referral generation, events and sourcing campaigns</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Who you are</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>
Minimum three years of sourcing and/or recruiting experience within a fast-paced organization</li>
<li>
Experience with innovative direct sourcing techniques with the ability to build and maintain a network of top-tier talent</li>
<li>
You are a great teammate and have a collaborative nature: We are a fast-growing team, and everyone needs to pull for each other, share best in class sourcing practices, and take on team projects to make the entire organization better</li>
<li>
Expertise in both traditional and advanced talent sourcing techniques including full proficiency with Boolean searching, experience with a wide array of specialized sourcing tools, and a deep understanding of the primary sourcing methodologies</li>
<li>
Experience building and developing pipelines of qualified, diversity candidates</li>
</ul>
