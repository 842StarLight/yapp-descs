<h2>Staff Accountant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Summary Performs accounting functions according to established procedures or as directed by the Accounting Manger - Loans for Origin Bank, or other Bank accounting management by performing the following duties.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Duties and Responsibilities </b><b>include the following</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Perform daily reconciliation of the Loan Control and/or Clearing General Ledger Accounts applying appropriate accounting policies/practices in a consistent and timely manner; identifying outages and ensuring necessary entries are made to bring the account into balance.</li>
<li>Monitors the Interface Balancing Report daily to ensure loan applications and related general ledger accounts are in balance; researching outages and ensuring necessary entries and loan corrections are made to bring the account(s) into balance.</li>
<li>Monitors the fee related error codes on the Loan Maintenance Cross Check report; performs a review of the loans identified to determine what entries caused the error and ensuring corrections/adjustments are made to the fees to resolve the errors.</li>
<li>Records daily purchases and sales of mortgage loans, utilizing mortgage software and information systems to ensure entries are posted accurately.</li>
<li>Balances purchases and sales to incoming and outgoing wire transfers and funds remittances.</li>
<li>Records daily warehouse lending activity, utilizing the warehouse lending software and information systems to ensure entries are posted accurately.</li>
<li>Utilizes technology systems such as Blackline, IBS, and Peoplesoft.</li>
<li>Reconcile and monitor other general ledger accounts as requested.</li>
<li>Assists with month-end accounting entries as requested.</li>
<li>Works on other accounting tasks and projects as requested.</li>
<li>Works on ad-hoc projects which require a fundamental accounting knowledge, at times with little supervision.</li>
<li>Backs up other functions within accounting as needed.</li>
<li>Prepares reports and requests as needed for examiners and auditors.</li>
<br>
</ul>
