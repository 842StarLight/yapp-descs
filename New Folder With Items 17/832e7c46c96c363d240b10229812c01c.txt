<p><b>Founded in 1970, CGB Enterprises, Inc. is known as an innovative and progressive leader in the grain, transportation, and risk management industries. Today, CGB operates a global enterprise with nearly 100 locations and over 2,500 US employees overseeing a diverse family of businesses that provide an array of services for producers and logistics services for an international base of customers. The combination of our assets provides a growing organization that is celebrating over 45 years of strength and stability!

<p></p>Imagine yourself as a future leader at CGB!

<br>
CGB is looking for inspired individuals to join the team as Management Trainees for our Terminals who aspire to be in leadership and make a meaningful impact on the future of our company! Our 12-14 month trainee program can help you grow your career.
<br>
<br>
Consolidated Terminals and Logistics Company (CTLC), a subsidiary of CGB Enterprises, Inc., began as a transportation company in 1970. Today, CTLC possesses one of the largest and most sophisticated networks of assets and manpower in the U.S. It has become one of the major logistics providers and handlers of bulk commodities and products throughout the middle and lower United States. In addition, CTLC now serves the global marketplace; capable of moving customers’ products from anywhere in the world to its final destination in the United States, and vice versa. From trucking to ocean freight, CTLC is an organization capable of providing world-class service with a small company attitude.
<br>
<br>
This job is primarily responsible for training to assume a management role (i.e. Superintendent or Operations Manager) and will learn the day to day operations and various roles within the business. The most important responsibility is guarding employee safety. Responsibilities include, but are not limited to, learning terminal operations, financial profit &amp; loss, safety program(s), marketing and sales, staffing activities, and how the different business units function together. This job will involve immediate decision making, building and maintaining relationships via phone and in person, and making decisions or participating in decisions that affect profitability for the company and/or for our customers. This role will learn and utilize leadership skills and assume responsibility to ensure terminal operations function in an efficient and profitable manner while facing different and new challenges every day. The job has direct input on personnel issues, financial decisions, construction projects and proposals, as well as the daily operations of the facility.
<p></p>
<p><b>In this job, you will:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Openly communicate with assigned mentor on a regular basis; involves interactive discussions, discussions around learned experiences, etc.
</li>
<li>Learn and work in various divisions to learn the day to day operations; may include Stevedoring, Marine, Terminals, Logistics, and Merchandising.
</li>
<li>Manage the location operation for efficiency and profitability; this will include learning how to independently plan staff daily work.
</li>
<li>Participate in, lead, and/or manage the company's safety initiative: work with management and assumes authority to lead, direct, communicate, and enforce the company's safety program at assigned location(s).
</li>
<li>Lead the employees to accomplish the work at hand and improve the location; this may include job duties such as working with employees to coach and manage them to complete necessary tasks, assisting in planning the incoming/out-going commodities.
</li>
<li>Provide recommendations for location and equipment repairs and construction projects.
</li>
<li>Work with management on staffing activities and provide input and/or take action on such issues (hiring, staffing/scheduling, training/coaching/mentoring, employee performance management, promotions, corrective actions, terminations, etc.).
</li>
<li>Work to learn accounting function of the division, department(s), and/or location(s) supported.
</li>
<li>May rotate through various departments at a location or locations; dependent on business needs.
</li>
</ul>
