<h2>Demand Planner</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Department:</b> Operations
<br><b>Reports to:</b> Demand Planning Manager
<br><br>
Summary
<br>This Demand Planning position is responsible for all forecasting activities associated with customers and products. The Demand Planner creates and maintains forecast models for their customers, incorporating business intelligence and forecast information gathered from sales, marketing, and finance. Responsibilities also include set up and maintenance of material master records.
<br><br>
This description is not intended to be construed as an exhaustive list of responsibilities, duties, or skills required for the position. This job description may be changed or assume additional duties at any time. The employee may be requested to perform different or additional duties as assigned.
<br>Duties and Responsibilities
<br><br>
<b>Essential:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Develop demand forecasts (operational forecasts) at multiple levels of aggregation for multiple time horizons as part of a demand planning function.</li>
<li>Review historical sales trends, research demand drivers, prepare forecast data, develop statistical forecast models, and evaluate forecast results.</li>
<li>Coordinate cross-functional research activities to reconcile significant variances and refine the forecast model to reflect updated sales and marketing assumptions.</li>
<li>Interact with sales, marketing, and customer finance to understand demand forecast drivers.</li>
<li>Utilize a collaborative and consensus approach by working with Sales, Marketing and Finance to obtain and ensure that current and accurate information is used for demand forecasts.</li>
<li>Use and maintain the Demand Planning software as the primary forecasting system tool.</li>
<li>Provide input to the Supply Planning organization in developing inventory strategies on existing items, new products, and product phase-outs.</li>
<li>Closely coordinate and communicate customer action plans with supply planning</li>
<li>Support management with risk assessments and mitigation activities.</li>
<li>Propose and implement solutions to improve demand forecast accuracy.</li>
<li>Address demand-related issues in a timely and effective manner.</li>
<li>Perform regular system data maintenance for products, materials and vendors.</li>
<li>Coordinate with internal departments to ensure accuracy of material master data.</li>
<li>Participates as a team member on a minimum of one company project per year.</li>
<li>Performs other duties as assigned by supervisor.</li>
</ul>
Supervisory Responsibilities
<br>This job has no direct supervisory responsibilities.
