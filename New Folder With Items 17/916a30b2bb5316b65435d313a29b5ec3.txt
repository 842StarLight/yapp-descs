<p>The Software &amp; Analytics Team is seeking a Revenue Cycle Management (RCM) Advisor. This role is for a client and service focused professional with a proven track record of completion of large and complex efforts in tight deadlines developing and enhancing partnerships among clients, business and technical personnel. Strong problem-solving capabilities with demonstrated innovative thinking; strong communication skills; strong project delivery methodology; and proven experience in stakeholder management skills are required for this role.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Location: </b>Remote/Telecommuter role. Tulsa, OK, Alpharetta, GA, or Nashville, TN preferred. Must live close to a major metro airport and be willing to do 60% overnight travel.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities include:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Maintain and develop a high degree of expertise in revenue cycle operations, technical processes, and reports
</li>
<li>Meet with customers to determine areas of focus to maximize their investment
<ul>
<li>Lead design sessions with customers to determine goals and objectives resulting in the assembly of a framework for effectively implementing and utilizing RCM solutions into the customer’s operations
</li>
<li>Analyze and communicate financial and operational data to clients for decision-making and strategic planning as a result of design, assessment / health check and/or optimization during a transformation engagement
</li>
<li>Execute on plan to increase customer adoption of solutions to achieve desired outcomes
</li>
<li>Provide best practices on usage of the RCM Solutions owned and ability to position other solutions to achieve maximum outcomes
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Utilize keen problem-solving skills to drive positive outcomes for the customer
</li>
<li>Work closely with cross functional project team on all aspects of each project to ensure milestones are met according to the project plan
</li>
<li>Provide and present executive summary presentations based on recommendations and findings which may include monthly reports regarding progress of engagement reflecting trend in achieving outcomes
</li>
<li>Collect and analyze customer utilization data to identify opportunities for increased adoption
</li>
<li>In conjunction with Client Management work with staff at all levels of a customer organization, including director, VP, and C-suite for implementation design sessions to proactive reviews of utilization
</li>
<li>Engage in nonpaid and paid engagements to drive better customer utilization of owned RCM Solution assets
</li>
<li>Be involved with Sales, Client Management, Customer Care, Education and Product Management where appropriate to aid in client success
</li>
<li>Develop and refine internal team processes for maximum efficiency and success
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Minimum Requirements </b>:
<ul><li>5+ years of experience with large provider in revenue cycle management
</li></ul>
</li>
<li>
<b>Education:

 </li>
</ul>
</li>
