<p></p>
<p>We are searching for an experienced a Regional Field Installation Operations Manager to support the Northern California subregion, based in San Francisco, California.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
Otis, a United Technologies company, is the world leader in reliable, efficient and technologically advanced elevators, escalators and people-moving systems. Our revolutionary Gen2® elevators, energy-saving ReGen™ drives and NCE “green” escalator have clearly set the industry standard for innovation, safety and performance. More than 2.4 million Otis elevators and escalators are currently in operation throughout 200 different countries<b>. </b>We are proud to have a global team of 60,000 employees that continues to rise to the challenges of a fast-moving company. Together, the people of Otis are creating new ideas and opportunities by collaborating across time zones, geographies and cultures. So just imagine where we can take you!</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Job Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
The Field Installation Manager will provide technical direction for field installers and Superintendents and has active control of and responsibility for the quality of all new equipment and modernization projects in California</li>
<li>
Responsible for establishing installation metrics and ensuring projects are completed timely, within estimate and while maintaining Otis Safety and Quality standards</li>
<li>
Reports on and presents recommendations on product design or performance requirements that continue to drive installation efficiency</li>
<li>
Work closely with Sales and Project management team(s) to establish estimating guidelines (labor / field purchases), conduct pre-bid reviews on all modernization projects, monitor field capacity and schedule management</li>
<li>
Conduct routine backlog reviews to monitor field progress and establish recovery plans when needed</li>
<li>
Provide technical direction to branch installers and adjusters by working with them on job sites and distributing necessary written information to familiarize experienced installers/adjusters with new equipment</li>
<li>
Provide training on all types of equipment</li>
<li>
Have control and responsibility for the quality of all new equip/modernization projects, monitoring first inspection reports and Field Test and Data reports and resolving modernization problems</li>
<li>
Make regular inspections to consult with Construction Superintendents, Constructors, Adjusters and Examiners on problems encountered with product performance</li>
<li>
On a proactive and emergency basis, offer assistance to the Region on the malfunctioning of equipment, determine needed corrective action, see that it is implemented and advise personnel</li>
<li>
Utilize the CRC / Crex websites data to resolve problems with the highest callback units in assigned territory</li>
<li>
Determine from patterns of complaints when a problem is repetitive and warrants a change in design or manufacture, seek the solution through the respective organization and see that it is completed within a reasonable time frame</li>
<li>
Assist in designing and delivering field education classes</li>
</ul>

Good analytical/problem solving skills and good communication skills</li>
</ul>
