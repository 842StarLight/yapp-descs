<b>Position Summary</b><br>
The candidate must be an expert in Engineering PLM and CAD areas, with primary skill set being the PLM system PTC Windchill. Responsible to lead delivery in the areas of Engineering PLM for both projects and support. Capable of managing relationships with customers, vendors, and fellow IT groups. Develop a clear understanding of the current SBD PLM and CAD toolset. Serving as both role model and trainer, the candidate must demonstrate the attributes of excellent application service and assists team members in developing technical and professional competency. The candidate must be able to identify areas of IT risk in the SBD PLM and CAD environments and identify opportunities to help improve information security, and business processes.<br>
Possesses extensive technical breadth. Exhibits the Leadership Qualities of Accountability, Integrity, Respect, Speed, Stretch, Vision and Boundarylessness.<br>
