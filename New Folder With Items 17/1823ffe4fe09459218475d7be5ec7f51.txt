<h2>Campus Recruiter</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Midwest Dental is growing and adding a Recruiter to act as our liaison with universities and professional associations to help us recruit dental &amp; hygiene students in our Midwest region. (Target locations: Des Moines, IA or Milwaukee, WI metro areas)</p>
<p>
Position Summary</p>
<p>
The Campus Recruiter is responsible for ongoing Doctor recruitment including successful onboarding and retention efforts within the organization. The Campus Recruiter sources, qualifies, recruits, screens and places Doctors in short and long term opportunities throughout the organization. Sourcing efforts will focus heavily on campus &amp; events.</p>
<p>Responsibilities</p>
<p>
Recruitment/Sourcing</p>
<ul>
<li>
Travel within assigned regions as directed to attend company seminars, trade shows, vendor fairs, campus lunch and learns, and socials<br>
Build and maintain good relationships with universities, other educational institutions &amp; dental industry professional associations.</li>
<li>
Plans and executes recruitment strategies to enhance profitability, productivity and efficiency throughout the company’s operations</li>
<li>
Maintains regular communication with potential candidates via email, phone and social networking</li>
<li>
Schedules and tours candidates through practices; provides exceptional communication and follow-up with appropriate Vice President (VP), Regional Director, Doctors and staff at the practices<br>
Continuously evaluates and recommends process improvement measures<br>
Supports hygiene focused events as needed<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p>
Administrative</p>
<ul>
<li>
Ensures accurate background checks for all perspective candidates</li>
<li>
Manages information flow for recruits and acquisition doctors throughout Midwest Dental Support Center (MDSC) to ensure timely credentialing and licensing</li>
<li>
Maintains and updates Salesforce application</li>
<li>
Coordinates Orientation process for new doctor hires with MDSC staff</li>
<li>
Ensures Midwest Dental postings and literature is distributed quarterly at each dental school nationwide</li>
<li>
Manages advertising for the recruitment and development teams</li>
</ul>
<p>Qualifications</p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree and/or one or more years of relevant experience.</li>
<li>
Previous sales, customer service, and/or recruitment experience in the dental industry or health care is preferred</li>
<li>
Interpersonal skills required to collaborate with different levels of the organization</li>
<li>
Proficient in Microsoft Office, internet, and social media applications</li>
<li>
Highly detail oriented
</li>
<li>Superior customer service and candidate follow-up</li>
<li>
Relationship building skills</li>
<li>
Strong business acumen</li>
<li>
A valid Driver’s license</li>
</ul>
<p>
 Working Conditions: Work is performed in a home office. Travel to dental offices &amp; events in division. Position requires up to 50% travel, including overnight.</p>
