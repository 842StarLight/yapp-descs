<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Introduction</b></h1>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader">This is a great opportunity for a high energy, career minded individual that has a passion for education and working in a SaaS environment. As an Inside Sales Representative you will be responsible for developing an assigned sales territory and working with the customer through the entire sales and renewal process (prospecting, product demonstration, sales order, implementation and contract renewal). You will work with K-12 schools, public, private, charter and virtual, to provide digital content solutions for credit recovery, blended learning and intervention.</h1>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>About Edgenuity</b></h1>
<p>Imagine a world where all K-12 children have an equal opportunity to acquire an excellent education. That's the Edgenuity mission. Founded in 1998, today Edgenuity partners with schools in all 50 states to provide award-winning curriculum to millions of students across 3500 school districts, and we’re growing! As the leading provider of digital curriculum and instructional services, Edgenuity empowers students, educators and school districts to achieve success in a variety of online and blended learning models.
</p>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>A Day in the Life as an Inside Sales Representative</b></h1>
<p>An Inside Sales Representative is responsible for making 50 outbound customer outreaches daily, 25 outbound calls and 25 emails. You will be responsible for managing both new business opportunities as well as an assigned current account book of business.
</p>
<p>An Inside Sales Representative is responsible for full understanding of the initiatives and opportunities for their assigned territory. This will include state and school funding, District performance and turnover, and growth within the assigned territory.</p>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications</b></h1>
<ul><li> Sales knowledge and sales and education acumen; including lead generation (cold calling), account management, and advanced customer problem solving skills.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Previous experience with SaaS, PaaS, or software sales is highly desirable.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Demonstrated history of landing new business for a SaaS offering.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Outstanding interpersonal and relationship-building skills with a high degree of responsiveness and integrity.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Self-starter with the ability to work in a fast-paced environment.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Exceptional verbal and written communication and interpersonal skills.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Strong analytical skills to assess complex situations and exceptional creativity to provide and execute on solutions.</li></ul>
<ul><li> A comfort level with presenting over the phone and via the web.</li></ul>

But even more importantly, we’re a great place to work!</p>
