<h2>Real Estate Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Description/Job Summary</b></p>
<p>
The Real Estate Coordinator will assist with the overall goals of the Real Estate and Development Departments in helping to find and secure new 84 Lumber store, truss and/or door shop sites, to assist in acquisitions of excess properties, to assist in the sale of real estate desired to be disposed of by the Company, and to maintain and track all Lease Agreements. Candidate will be reporting directly to the Director of Real Estate and Development and must possess general knowledge of how to understand and interpret commercial real estate agreements, appraisals, transaction documents and reports. The candidate must have strong negotiating, organizational, computer and written and verbal communication skills. The candidate must be able to multi-task and work on several projects simultaneously to meet project deadlines.
</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities/Duties</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Assist in all aspects of real estate disposition, includes marketing, negotiating, contracts and closings.</li>
<li>
Produce and organize real estate and site investigation materials and reports.</li>
<li>
Responsible for continuous updates to Real Estate Catalog.
</li>
<li>Responsible for assisting in leasing of real estate and maintaining and updating leasing data.</li>
<li>
Maintain and create files for new and additional acquisitions and dispositions.</li>
<li>
Assist where needed in Development and Construction contract management.</li>
<li>
Provide support for needs of Director of Real Estate &amp; Development.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Required Skills</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Spreadsheet/Internet Software<br>
</li>
<li>Word Processing
</li>
<li>Ability to read, analyze and interpret general business reports, technical procedures and/or government regulations; write reports, business correspondence; effectively present information and respond to questions from groups of managers, clients, customers and the general public.
</li>
<li>Ability to solve problems and deal with a variety of variables in different situations; interpret a variety of instructions furnished in written, oral, diagram or schedule form.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Required Experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor's degree or 3-5 years of experience in real estate.</li>
<li>
Willingness to travel less than 25% on business needs.</li>
<li>
Willingness to acquire real estate license, if not already obtained.</li>
</ul>
