<h2>Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Summary</b></p>
<p>The central mission of an Account Executive is to increase revenueby acquiring new business from a nationwide territory in accordance withspecific set sales goals. Goals will be monthly, quarterly, and annual. Theproduction will come by executing against RTI’s defined sales process to opennew accounts and develop a subsequent strategy for ongoing growth anddevelopment of accounts with RTI Account Managers.</p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b> include the following. Otherduties may be assigned.</p>
<p>- Actively and continually prospect and maintain growing salesfunnel using Company sales methods including cold calling</p>
<p>- Understand and execute the sales process to open new accounts</p>
<p>- Maintain a portfolio of growing accounts by working with AccountManager team to develop business continuity strategy and excellent customerengagement.</p>
<p>- Meet or exceed growth goals and quota set by Director of BusinessDevelopment or another leader</p>
<p>- Negotiate pricing and contracting agreements</p>
<p>- Electronically track and maintain leads and contacts using CRMsoftware</p>
<p>- Participate in Company training for skill and competency growth</p>
<p>- Display a strong ability to multi-task</p>
<p>- Represent RTI’s mission and values</p>
<p>- Maintain positive attitude and can-do spirit</p>
<p><b>Competencies</b></p>
<p>JobKnowledge - Competent in required job skills and knowledge; keeps abreast ofcurrent developments; requires minimal supervision. Have a comprehensiveunderstanding of the transportation brokerage industry.</p>
<p>CriticalThinking/Problem Solving - Think strategically around customer problems todeliver value to clients.</p>
<p>Business Acumen -Havekeenness and quickness in understanding and dealing with business situations ina manner that is likely to lead to a good outcome.</p>
<p>Negotiation –Use solid negotiating skills to influence others.</p>
<p>Teamwork- Balances team and individual responsibilities; exhibits objectivity andopenness to others' views; gives and welcomes feedback; contributes to buildinga positive team spirit; puts success of team above own interests; able to buildmorale and group commitments to goals and objectives; supports everyone'sefforts to succeed.</p>
<p>Useof Technology - Demonstrates required skills; adapts to new technologies;troubleshoots technological problems; uses technology to increase productivity;keeps technical skills up to date.</p>
<p>Communications- Expresses ideas and thoughts verbally; expresses ideas and thoughts inwritten form; exhibits good listening and comprehension; keeps othersadequately informed; selects and uses appropriate communication methods.</p>
