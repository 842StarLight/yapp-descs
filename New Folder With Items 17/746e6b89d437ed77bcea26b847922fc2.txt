<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Job Summary</b></h3>
<div>
Oversees and manages the global demand generation program, including three categories of responsibility or key functions: management, strategy and execution. Management responsibilities include goal alignment, project management and administration, as well as acting as demand generation liaison to other functions such as field marketing, sales operations and product business units. Strategic responsibilities include program strategy, monitoring and analyzing, governance, training and playbook deployment and managing marketing tele qualification processes and partners. Execution functions includes program development, system development, quality assurance, measurement and template execution.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Roles and Responsibilities</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li><div>
Work with marketing leadership to evolve and develop coordinated, enterprise-wide demand waterfall framework and marketing automation strategy.</div></li>
<li><div>
Manage email marketing and landing pages to support digital campaigns, including copy writing, landing page and email creation, list management, analysis of results and recommendations for improvements. Collaborate with the product business units and/or field marketing to ensure there is a cohesive creation, execution and testing/optimization strategy to increase engagement of campaigns.</div></li>
<li><div>
Work with field marketing, product business units and sales organization to create dashboards that measure, analyze and report on the impact of demand creation activities on sales pipeline.</div></li>
<li><div>
Train and manage an external tele-prospecting firm on lead qualification by writing calling scripts, evaluating qualified leads and reassigning them for further follow-up.</div></li>
<li><div>
Train the global sales organization on lead management processes as they evolve, including playbook creation.</div></li>
<li><div>
Collaborate with event managers on lead capturing strategies to ensure a smooth lead capturing process for key events.</div></li>
<li><div>
Drive data cleansing, integration, data mapping, segmentation, automation, and lead scoring in marketing automation and CRM platforms.</div></li>
</ul></div>
