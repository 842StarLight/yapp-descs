<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Summary</b></h3>
<p>
The Customer Service Representative is responsible for handling customer service related transactions via a two-way video conference with our customers. The Customer Service Representative will create sales opportunities by engaging customers in conversations and product demonstrations. Individuals occupying this position will be assigned annual loan deposit referral goals. Performance expectation is to meet or exceed those established goals.</p>
<p>
Flexible scheduling options available! See SCHEDULING &amp; TRAVEL below.</p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Scheduling &amp; Travel</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>
Must be able to work on a flexible schedule that at times may include early or late and regular weekend hours.</li>
<li>
Days of the week and hours will vary, but we generally schedule between the hours of 6:45 am to 7:05 pm.</li>
<li>
Must be available on Saturdays. Work on every Saturday is not required.</li>
<li>
Must be able to attend new hire training and orientation in Lexington, Kentucky Monday through Friday 9:00 am to 4:00 pm for a one to two week period.</li>
<li>
Ability to travel to other locations as needed.</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Essential Duties &amp; Responsibilities</b></h3>
<p><i>
To perform this position successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty and responsibility satisfactorily.</i></p>
<ul>
<li>
Take a proactive approach to determine service needs, suggesting appropriate services to customers and generating sales leads.</li>
<li>
Assist and engage customers with financial transactions via two way video conference. This includes:<ul>
<li>
Cashing checks within proper authorization levels and paying out money after verifying that signatures are correct, that written and numerical amounts agree, and that accounts have sufficient funds.</li>
<li>
Receiving checks and currency for deposit, verifying amounts, and checking accuracy of deposit slips.</li>
<li>
Recording transactions and issuing computer-generated receipts.</li>
<li>
Placing holds on accounts when appropriate.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Issue cash advances on credit cards for customers with proper identification.</li>
<li>
Accept loan payments.</li>
<li>
Accept customer check orders and enter order on-line.</li>
<li>
Sell or refer Bank services to customers and potential customers to include: DDA accounts; Savings Accounts; Certificates of Deposit; Individual Retirement Accounts; and, Safe Deposit Boxes</li>
<li>
Perform other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Supervisory Responsibilities</b></h3>
<p><i>
This position does not have supervisory responsibilities.</i></p>
