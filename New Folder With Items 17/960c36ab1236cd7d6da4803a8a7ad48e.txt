<p>Graphic Web Designer</p>
<p>- Please include a link to your online portfolio to be considered for the Graphic Design position.</p>
<p>The Designer must be self-driven, operating effectively with minimal oversight - yet welcome collaboration and feedback from other team members. Strong communication skills are a must, with a high emphasis on articulating creative ideas and expressing ones opinions clearly and in a straight-forward manner.</p>
<p><b>RESPONSIBILITIES: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Design and create assets across multiple channels, i.e., web, email, marketing</li>
<li>Partner with various team members to develop and produce content/materials that support the brand strategy</li>
<li>Ensure consistent branding and messaging</li>
<li>Attention to detail in design, preparation and presenting</li>
<li>Work collaboratively to ensure that all design solutions meet deadlines, objectives, and established parameters</li>
<li>Research market trends and translate into brand appropriate graphics</li>
<li>Design display and video ad creative that will be used for Google, Facebook, Instagram, and other media outlets.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>REQUIRED EXPERIENCE/SKILLS/EDUCATION: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>3+ years' graphic design experience</li>
<li>Extensive knowledge of Adobe Creative Suite, specifically with Photoshop, InDesign and Illustrator</li>
<li>Great with layouts and typography</li>
<li>Understanding of site flow &amp; website layout &amp; design for e-commerce</li>
<li>Strong, current portfolio reflecting relevant work</li>
<li>Working understanding of Macintosh work environment and how to use Microsoft Outlook, PowerPoint and Word</li>
<li>Ability to design around concepts</li>
<li>Ability to think creatively and justify concepts to: executives, designers, marketers, sales and other internal staff</li>
<li>Committed to the professionalism of visual communication, passionate for design and creative problem solving</li>
<li>Excellent presentation skills</li>
<li>Understands brands' target consumers and how to reach them through design</li>
<li>Able to multitask and take on multiple projects at once.</li>
<li>Possess a positive attitude and be a team player</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Types: Part-time, Contract</p>
<p>Salary: $30.00 /hour</p>
