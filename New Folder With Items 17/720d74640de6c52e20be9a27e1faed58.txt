<div><b>Description</b></div>
<div>The primary responsibilities surround management of location intelligence and firmographic data and will require strong SQL, Salesforce, and GIS skills. The ideal candidate will have experience with backend Salesforce data, working with firmographic data (e.g. Dun and Bradstreet, Acxiom, etc.), and familiarity with the telecommunications industry. This position will support the Enterprise Sales organization by ensuring that prospect and existing customer location data housed in Salesforce is up to date, accurate, and actionable for location based prospecting.</div>
