<h2>Product Manager, Cards</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>ACE Cash Express, Inc. is a leading retailer of financial services, including short-term consumer loans; check cashing, digital bank accounts, prepaid debit cards, and bill payment services, and the largest owner and operator of check cashing stores in the United States and the second largest owner and operator of short-term consumer loan stores in the United States. ACE focuses on serving consumers, many of whom seek alternatives to traditional banking relationships in order to gain convenient and immediate access to financial services.<br>
<br>
About the Position<br>
<br>
This is an exciting opportunity for an entrepreneurial product manager looking to make an impact quickly. This position will to walk the line between the technical and analytical by utilizing razor sharp math and technology skills to create solutions out of real-world problems. ACE Cash Express is positioning its resources to place its Digital Card Product offering at the forefront of its product offering in the upcoming years. You will be charged with implementing and enhancing the Digital Card product roadmap and be instrumental to the present and future success of the product. This position will report to the VP of Card Products.<br>
Duties and Responsibilities<br>
<ul>
<li>Overall accountability for the design and implementation of Card initiatives across multiple IT systems.
</li>
<li>Manage the product throughout the full product lifecycle.</li>
<li>Identify opportunities and uncover untapped needs through industry and portfolio analysis, customer research and interactive design.</li>
<li>Able to deliver on insights by driving product through the SDLC process:</li>
</ul>
o Work day-to-day with internal and external development teams;<br>
o Write and review requirements and interactive wireframes;<br>
o Integrate product across multiple technological platforms, both internally and externally.<br>
o Simultaneously act as the Product Manager, Project Manager and Business Systems Analyst;
<br>
o Identify compliance concerns and shepherd product through Legal and Compliance approval.<br>
o Ultimate accountability when it comes to User Acceptance Testing.<br>
<ul>
<li>Perform financial modeling and identify key opportunities through the analysis of data.</li>
<li>Develop and implement company-wide go-to-market plans, working with all departments to execute.
</li>
<li>Develop business plans and frameworks with Finance team to accurately measure success of new initiatives.
</li>
<li>Drive field adoption of systems through training, weekly communications, management reporting, tools, etc.</li>
<li>Execute marketing campaigns in print and internet marketing campaigns.</li>
</ul>
</div>
