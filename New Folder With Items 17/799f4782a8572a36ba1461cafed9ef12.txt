<h2>Director of Business Intelligence</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>AAA is looking for talented, innovative, and forward-thinking Director of Business Intelligence to join our team!</b> The Director of Business Intelligence will build AAA WCNY’s information management and business intelligence function. The Director will act as an advisor to our leaders to help them drive higher levels of engagement with our members and grow market share using data as a strategic asset. This leader will develop a strategic BI road-map to ensure that we have the proper tools, infrastructure, process and resources to drive success. The Director will prioritize our business intelligence efforts and align resources to AAA WCNY’s strategic goals.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b><i>
This position is located in Williamsville, NY.</i></b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
BENEFITS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Health, Dental, and Life Insurance</li>
<li>Competitive PTO package</li>
<li>401K with match after 6 months!</li>
<li>Free <b>AAA</b> Membership</li>
<li>Tuition Reimbursement</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p><b>
</b></p>
<p><b>RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Define the data and analytics strategy and project priorities in partnership with cross-functional leaders</li>
<li>Create infrastructure to proactively gain insights from the data and inform sales growth across all business lines</li>
<li>Translate business requirements to support analytics projects</li>
<li>Lead a team of subject matter experts across business lines to establish a common data framework for the various teams to leverage.</li>
<li>Build and manage a team that performs analysis and build data products. Mentor and develop growth plans for those associates.</li>
<li>Oversee and manage vendor partnerships with an eye on ensuring compliance to SOW terms, quality of work, and adherence to cost structure/budget.</li>
<li>Build rapport and manage relationships with other business stakeholders throughout the organization.</li>
<li>Weigh the priorities of our business leaders to shape the Data &amp; Analytics road-map.</li>
<li>Promote visibility into the work of the data team to demonstrate the value of data-driven decision-making throughout the organization.</li>
<li>Collaborate with business units to identify and prioritize value creation opportunities to grow market share and revenue.</li>
<li>Act as thought leader within AAA to drive a deeper use of data and member / customer insights</li>
<li>Oversee creation of Annual Budget to support Data Analytics and Business Intelligence program and lead vendor negotiations to optimize spend.</li>
<li>Direct continuous improvement of data management practices to ensure that data resources are integrated, understandable, accessible, and secure.</li>
<li>Implement a communication strategy and process to review metrics, track milestones, report issues, and update key stakeholders and executives on program status.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree required.Prefer MBA or MSA with coursework in data science</li>
<li>5 or more years of experience applying advanced analytics using structured &amp; unstructured data with 3 or more years of experience in a senior leadership level role
</li>
<li>Demonstrable understanding of enterprise data warehouse principles and common business intelligence tools and technologies.
</li>
<li>Understanding of Master Data Management (MDM) principles, technologies and their role in enterprise data integrity.</li>
<li>Experience with Extract Transform Load (ETL) methods; especially in a hybrid cloud-based environment.</li>
<li>Extensive knowledge and experience in analytics, big data, data warehousing, information architecture, data modeling and/or master data management.</li>
<li>Ability to manage team of varied experience levels and independent individuals, nurturing talent, hiring and goal setting is required.</li>
<li>Strong communication skills and ability to communicate across multiple levels and communicate technical information to non-technical audiences.</li>
<li>Presentation experience to both small and large groups</li>
<li>Strong business acumen, with the ability to build a compelling business case for change.</li>
<li>Able to create support and buy-in across a wide range of stakeholders; create a strong network of relationships among peers, internal partners, external constituencies, decision makers and stakeholders.</li>
<li>Strong organizational skills and the ability to manage multiple projects and priorities.</li>
<li>Experience with negotiating contracts &amp; managing vendor relationships</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to lead and manage change</li>
<br>
</ul>
