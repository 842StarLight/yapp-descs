<h2>Marketing & Events Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>About Us: </b></p>
<p>What’s your game? ATX Sports &amp; Adventures is here to make it happen. Our leagues, tournaments, and events are designed for fun-loving athletic Austinites who want to experience the chills and thrills of a sporting life. Get connected to a rec sports community built for friendly competition, peak performance, magical moments, epic celebrations, and lasting friendships. We take your fun seriously. If you have a team or are looking to join one, we’ve got a game for you. Let’s Play!</p>
<p><b>The Role: </b></p>
<p>ATX Sports &amp; Adventures is looking for a Marketing &amp; Events Coordinator to join our growing team and help us achieve our next stage of rapid growth. In this role you will coordinate the Company's day-to-day marketing efforts, including newsletter, email, social, paid media, and content marketing, partnering directly with our Operations and Management teams to grow ATXSA’s brand awareness and drive conversion. You will also assist with the sales, coordination, and account management of ATXSA’s corporate and social events.</p>
<p>You’ll have the opportunity to:</p>
<ul>
<li>Lead marketing, advertising, and promotional planning and execution</li>
<li>Manage clients and design plans for ATXSA corporate and social events.</li>
<li>Help build a culture and brand that is growing rapidly and wants to bring more people together through sports, lifestyle and fun.</li>
</ul>
<p>We're looking for a high-energy, dedicated professional who is a versatile team player, someone as determined and excited as we are to build up an active, athletic, and social culture and community in Austin. If that describes you to a T, we’d love to hear from you!</p>

<p><b>Marketing</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Conduct market research to deepen our understanding of customers, potential customers, competitors, and market opportunities</li>
<li>Participate in marketing strategy development, including distillation of brand identity and voice</li>
<li>Create and execute a marketing plan spanning earned, owned, and paid media:</li>
<li>---Budget development and management</li>
<li>---Platform/channel identification</li>
<li>---Audience segmentation</li>
<li>---Campaign creation and management</li>
<li>---Thorough and specific calendaring</li>
<li>---Report compilation, marketing activity analysis, marketing plan refinement, and funnel optimization</li>
<li>Develop marketing content and copy:</li>
<li>---Website</li>
<li>---Newsletter and e-mail</li>
<li>---Blog</li>
<li>---Press Releases</li>
<li>---Print Collateral</li>
<li>---Paid Media</li>
<li>Manage social media platforms</li>
<li>Design and create marketing collateral</li>
<li>Coordinate event marketing</li>
<li>Assist with sponsorship sales and activations, as well as marketing partnership development and execution</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Event and Account Management</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Assist with event sales and related outreach, including post-event cross-selling</li>
<li>Manage relationships and communications with corporate clients</li>
<li>Transform client vision into viable event design</li>
<li>Collaborate with event operations team in planning and execution of custom events</li>
<li>Process contracts, prepare invoices, and track</li>
</ul>

<li>---Growth marketing and experimentation</li>
<li>---Conversion rate optimization</li>
<li>---Google, Web, and Ad Network Analytics</li>
<li>---Event Planning and Management</li>
<li>---Sales and Account Management</li>
<li>Photography and design knowledge/experience preferred</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills, both written and verbal. Copywriting or content marketing writing experience preferred</li>
<li>Resourceful with the ability to quickly grasp new concepts and ideas with a strong marketing and business acumen</li>
<li>Self-starter who is able to think creatively and work independently in a fast-paced, shifting environment</li>
<li>Strong organizational skills: ability to effectively manage multiple projects simultaneously. There are many hats to wear!</li>
<li>Ability to work some evenings and weekends</li>
<li>Team Player!</li>
