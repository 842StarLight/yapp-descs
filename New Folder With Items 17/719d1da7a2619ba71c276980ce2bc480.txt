<h2>Customer Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Description:</b><br>
POSITION SUMMARY
<br>The Customer Account Manager is responsible for developing mutually beneficial transportation-customer relationships for our company’s largest clients. The CAM will build trust with key customers by providing unparalleled service, communication, and solutions.
<br><br>
Essential Functions Statement
<br><ul>
<li>Perform routine solicitations and follow up with designated account contacts in person, via email, or other electronic forms of communication, even when no freight is available.</li>
<li>Build relationships through daily interactions with current customers, operations and logistics personnel, shippers, consignees, support departments, and sales.</li>
<li>Collaborate with Load Planners and Driver Managers to ensure customer and company needs are communicated and met.</li>
<li>Monitor freight commitments and on time service for key accounts to ensure they are being met by both Hill Bros Transportation and our customers.</li>
<li>Oversee order entry accuracy and customer website updates and accessorials.</li>
<li>Act as a liaison between Sales and Operations to negotiate rates with current customer base for new lane opportunities.</li>
<li>Offer Logistics as a solution when Assets is unable to cover to satisfy customer capacity needs.</li>
<li>Maintain EDI requirements for acceptance or rejection of freight, EDI214 communications or holds, and updates of loads.</li>
<li>Oversee trailer pools in the respected region and/or shippers and consignees for assigned customers.</li>
<li>Compile and organize customer notes and daily checklists, as to ensure the assigned region and/or customers are covered in the event of an absence or vacation.</li>
<li>Assist in training Customer Service Reps (CSR) and Assistants (CSA).</li>
<li>Provide billing team with required documents to submit invoices to customers; i.e. rate confirmations. Complete required customer tasks so that billing can process; i.e. customer website maintenance.</li>
<li>Prepare and distribute daily and weekly reports to the customer service department.</li>
<li>Act as a substitute in the absence of other Customer Service associates which are seamless and transparent to our customers</li>
<li>Communicate unresolved customer grievances or problems with Customer Service Manager for further investigation.</li>
<li>Consistently report to work as required and complete assigned or required tasks during normal work schedule.</li>
<li>Comply with all company policies and procedures.</li>
</ul>

SKILLS &amp; ABILITIES
<br>Skills
<br><ul>
<li>Able to control and express emotion, able to handle relationships judiciously and with empathy</li>
<li>Able to do thorough, accurate and consistent work, free from errors</li>
<li>Able to effectively maintain a balance of freight in assigned region</li>
<li>Able to clearly communicate with customers, co-workers, and support staff departments</li>
