<p><b>The Role
</b></p>
<p>Do you have a knack for providing excellent client service and an innate interest in digital media and advertising? If so, please continue reading!
</p>
<p></p>
<p>We are seeking a <b>Manager </b>to be based in our Chicago, IL office. As a TAM Team Manager, you will provide unparalleled support to our portfolio of Agency, Media and Advertiser clients across the US as well as help build, shape and transform a TAM team of 3-5 team members.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>With communications between brands and consumers changing daily, it’s important to bring in hard numbers to validate advertising dollars and ensure companies are communicating effectively. At Kantar we tell the story behind advertising campaigns’ effectiveness, and our Technical Account Management team serves as the projects technical point person for each campaign. From fielding pre-sale inquiries to managing aspects of the campaign execution process, TAMs are involved in the day-to-day operations of our campaigns to ensure all ads are tracked properly. Ideally, your experience with project management in the digital media space will enable you to prioritize tasks, articulate complex ideas, and work closely with a diverse list of clients, which happen to include some of the best-known brands in the world.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Our Technical Account Management group is responsible for ensuring the successful execution of projects that evaluate online advertising campaigns. With a customizable and holistic approach which works across devices and tactics, we provide the bigger picture of effectiveness to our clients. TAMs make it all happen by managing project workflow, tracking key milestones, and communicating with both internal and external clients to keep them updated on project status.
</p>
