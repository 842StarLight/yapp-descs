<h2>Staff Accountant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Miami Air International, with its modern fleet of Boeing 737 aircraft, is the premier provider of private group air transportation, commonly called chartered flights. It’s not just a flight. This is a travel experience that is tailored to delight your group. We understand that each group is unique and personalizing the right services and flight amenities is critical, and at the heart of why you are considering a charter.</p>
<p>All we do is charter. And we do a lot! Our business is built to only serve groups like yours, and you are our number one priority. In addition to concentrating our efforts on being the best in those things you want from any airline – like industry leading safety-programs and over 25 years of proven reliability and financial stability – we provide a suite of services unparalleled by any other airline.</p>
<p>Sound like a match? You’re in luck. Miami Air is currently seeking a highly motivated and personable individual to join our Accounting/Finance team as a Staff Accountant to Maintain financial records and ensures that financial transactions are property recorded. Assist in preparing the balance sheet, profit and loss statements and other financial reports including statement of cash-flow, daily cash.<br>
</p>
<p><b>Functions &amp; Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Prepare, examine or analyze accounting records, financial statements, or other financial reports to assess accuracy, completeness, and conformance to reporting and procedural standards.</li>
<li>Assist in the development of accounting processes, budgets, forecasts, and business planning programs.</li>
<li>Assist in processing monthly, quarterly and annual financial closings.
</li>
<li>Reconcile sub-ledger to general ledger account balances.</li>
<li>Compute taxes owed and prepare tax returns, ensuring compliance with payment, reporting or other tax requirements.
</li>
<li>Maintains account analyses that are clearly organized and easily accessible to management.
</li>
<li>Compile monthly or quarterly reports for local, state, or federal authorities as required.
</li>
<li>Provide monthly performance comparisons including sales, .
</li>
<li>Assist in preparing audit schedules for financial/governmental audits.
</li>
<li>Responsible for maintaining and generating accruals.
</li>
<li>Maintains chart of accounts.
</li>
<li>Facilitate and complete monthly close procedures.
</li>
<li>Recording of semi-monthly payroll.
</li>
<li>Analyze statistics and utilize past and present financial operations in order to estimate future revenue and expenditures. Develop and manage forecast models using this information.
</li>
<li>Compare and analyze actual financial performance to various KPIs benchmarks, forecasts and historical results.
</li>
<li>Provide trends to support improvement of operations.</li>
<li>Assemble and summarize data to present in-depth reports on financial results and risks including reliable conclusions that management can use to implement more effective operational strategies.
</li>
<li>Ad-hoc assignments and special projects.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications &amp; Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree in accounting, finance or equivalent. Certified Public Accountant (CPA) preferred
</li>
<li>Minimum 2 years, up to, and including 4 years Accounting and/or Finance.
</li>
<li>PC literate with intermediate proficiency in Microsoft applications accounting software.
</li>
<li>Must have advanced knowledge in Excel.
</li>
<li>Excellent written, verbal and interpersonal communication skills.
</li>
<li>Strong attention to detail.</li>
</ul>
