<h2>Service Delivery Executive - AV/VC and Events</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Milestone Technologies is a top provider of IT support services and shared services for leading internet, social media, enterprise software, and biopharmaceutical companies. These customers are household names and Global 50 players – an impressive logo list! Milestone enjoys stable recurring revenue from this elite installed base—and is growing quickly both domestically and internationally. They are headquartered in the Bay Area and serve clients globally. Milestone’s Business Services include Contact Center, Business Process Management, Infrastructure Operations [Network and Data Center], Applications Support Operations, IT Professional Services and others.</div>
<div>
<br>
Historically Milestone was an IT staffing company – labor intensive and (compared to today) low margin. But with a private equity investor, H.I.G., taking ownership in August 2015, the company has added a new leadership team (CEO, COO, CFO, three VP Line of Business) and a broader vision.<br>
<br>
The mix of services is changing at Milestone and becoming more sophisticated. While preserving the base of on-premise labor services, Milestone is adding remote (off-premise) services and more holistic managed services. These new services allow Milestone to not only provide talented labor, but also innovative, proprietary processes and best-in-class technologies. With these new services, Milestone expects substantial growth at higher margins.</div>
<div>
Milestone is seeking an experienced leader to drive this strategy, focused on AV/VC, Events related accounts/services. Preferable candidates include experienced leaders with demonstrated ability to shape new service offerings and translate them into a sustainable business portfolio, as well as having strong delivery model transition and transformation background.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Reporting to the Vice President, Customer Experience and Infrastructure services, the AV/VC and Events Service Delivery Executive (SDE), is a leader within Milestone’s Customer Experience &amp; Infrastructure team, serving as the Business Service Owner (BSO) for the AV/VC and events Accounts practice.</div>
<div>
Focus will be a subset of Milestone existing customers as well as new customers, all with AV/VC and Events related activities.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
AV/VC and Events SDE will also have to oversee all Customer Experience and Infrastructure related Pre-Sales and transformation AV/VC and Events related activities, acting on behalf of VP Customer Experience and Infrastructure services, ensuring that the right scope definition is clearly defined, the right resources allocated and proper follow up executed.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
As the business evolves, AV/VC and Events SDE will be requested to define an associated Go-To-Market strategy, Service Offering Portfolio definition, support Sales efforts, ensuring proper account onboarding and to drive resulting growth.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>AV/VC and Events Accounts Service Delivery ownership - manages directly (or with his SDM team directly reporting to her/him) some accounts (either existing or new) where AV/VC and Events Services is implemented or targeted to be implemented.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Client Relationship Management – overall responsibility for AV/VC and Events related accounts. Lead internal/external efforts to ensure contractual obligations are met. Provide oversight of day-to-day operations with a focus on delivering exceptional customer service that meets or exceeds client Service Level Agreements (SLAs) and/or Key Performance Indicators (KPIs).</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Existing Accounts transformation – owns the transformation of existing on-premise labor services Customer Experience and Infrastructure AV/VC and Events related accounts to a managed services or transactional model, with associated SLA, customer contract negotiations resulting in an updated Statement Of Work (SOW).</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Manage Financial and Operational Performance - Attainment of AV/VC and Events account / managed services targets and client specific metrics are the key performance measurement criteria for the position. This will be facilitated through Sales Enablement, development/maintenance of a world class Business Managed Services transformation Portfolio and associated Operational Delivery/Execution.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Go-to-Market Strategy for Managed Services – owns the development and execution of the Managed Services Strategy related to AV/VC and Events services, which includes setting the strategic direction of Milestone’s Account transition and transformation towards a Managed Services model. This activity is done in close alignment with CTO and Professional Service LoB.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
New Accounts onboarding – owns and drive the onboarding, associated transition and transformation of new AV/VC and Events accounts. This activity is done in close alignment with CTO and Professional Service LoB.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Engagement with the Sales Team – Enable AV/VC and Events managed Services knowledge within the Sales team, working with Sales VP, and individual Sales, to ensure the Sales team is adept at discovering Managed Services model and leverage the SDE/SDM as necessary to position and close Managed Services related business. Similarly, she/he is responsible for enabling the Solution Architect(s) to handle AV/VC and Events related Services opportunities properly – positioning and scoping those solutions to meet Client needs, and effectively partnering with Sales to close related business.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Pre-Sales and Transformation related activities: ensuring Pre-Sales and Transformation activities for Customer Experience and Infrastructure AV/VC and Events scope are handled in a consistent way, having a proper Go/No Go and scope definition defined upfront, proper resources allocated, follow up in place.</div>
</div></div></div>
