<h2>Director, Event Technology I</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>The Director, Event Technology I (DET I) is responsible for achieving the revenue and profitability goals of an assigned location(s). This position ensures the team delivers World-Class service while cultivating and maintaining relationships with key venue personnel to enhance the overall business relationship with the venue. The DET promotes high performance, accountability and continuous improvement in the team and fosters a collaborative environment that values learning and a commitment to quality. This role will report to the Area Director, Venues or RVP, Venues.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Financial Management and Reporting</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li><p><b>
Drive Results</b> by managing an efficient and profitable operation with a focused approach on revenue maximization and profitability using cost control measures in accordance with the company’s standard operating procedures.
</p></li>
<li><p>Achieve PSAV’s financial goals for the location(s) by effectively managing labor, equipment sub-rentals and other costs.
</p></li>
<li><p>Understand and utilize operational and financial reports and completes standard and ad hoc reports accurately and on time.
</p></li>
<li>Utilize the Company billing system to coordinate invoicing activities and makes certain that billing is reviewed and approved by clients.
</li>
<li>
<b>See the Big Picture</b> by completing the sales forecast at home location, ensuring they are accurate and submitted timely.
</li>
<li>Participate in business review presentations as needed, in collaboration with regional management.</li>
<li><p>
Review and manage location P &amp; L and develops action plans to address deficiencies/grow the business</p></li>
<li><p>
Confirms venue partners process all payments to PSAV in a timely basis.</p></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
 </b><b>Operations Management</b><b><br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li><p>Perform daily floor operations including scheduling and equipment setups, operation, and strikes. Assist on the floor as needed.</p></li>
<li><p>
Ensure that all equipment and services are delivered on time, in good working condition and as specified by clients and the venue, and in accordance to brand standards.</p></li>
<li><p>
Anticipate equipment challenges and changes in a timely and professional manner.</p></li>
<li><p>
Use inventory control procedures to maintain proper inventory levels while also maintaining safety, security, and quality assurance of equipment.
</p></li>
<li><p><b>See the Big Picture</b> by efficiently sharing labor and equipment within the local market.</p></li>
<li><p>
Attend all operational venue meetings such as daily stand up meetings with venue operations staff.<br>
 </p></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Sales Management</b><b><br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li><p>Work with Regional Sales Director to create effective sales strategies to maximize revenue per-event for assigned customers including upsell and cross-sell opportunities.
</p></li>
<li>Effectively prepare and present quotes and proposals in a timely manner to customers, including those submitted through www.psav.com, venue booking system, or other sources as assigned</li>
<li>
Attend customer meetings, understanding their goals and responding to their questions, concerns, and challenges.
</li>
<li>
<b>Do the Right Thing</b> by ensuring all customer information is up to date and accurate in the Customer Relationship Management system (CRM) at all times.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Customer Service</b><b><br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Deliver World-Class Service</b> by cultivating and maintaining relationships with customers, venue personnel and various PSAV supporting functions and departments.
</li>
<li>Position will have oversight to personnel to assist with event execution.</li>
<li><p>
Exceed the expectations and needs of internal and external customers.
</p></li>
<li><p>Meet with guests on site to confirm that their needs are met and the equipment setup is accurate and working properly.
</p></li>
<li><p>Monitor small to medium size events and check in on customers throughout the day.
</p></li>
<li><p>Enforce the guidelines in the Employee Guide and/or venue standards for the team to portray a polished and professional image.<br>
</p></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Technical Ability</b><b>
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>Understand and oversees the technical aspects of the job and applies basic to advanced troubleshooting and problem solving skills to guide technicians in resolving equipment and software issues in a timely and professional manner.
</p></li>
<li><p>Effectively utilizes applicable company computer systems.
</p></li>
<li><p>Act as on-site technical expert as needed for events.</p></li>
<li><p>
Assist on the floor with operations as needed</p></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
 <b>People Development</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li><p><b>
Value People</b> by promoting a culture of high performance, accountability and continuous improvement that values learning and a commitment to quality.
</p></li>
<li><p>Manage performance, addresses employee concerns, maintains adequate staffing levels, and facilitates team development.
</p></li>
<li><p>Manage the human resources activities including selection, performance management, and learning.
</p></li>
<li><p>Provide focused and continued coaching to develop the skills of team members.
</p></li>
<li><p>Train employees on PSAV standards for service and equipment, and verifies they are properly trained on company computer systems.
</p></li>
<li><p>Recommend team members for training opportunities, as needed.</p></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Job Qualifications</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li><p>
High School Diploma is required. Bachelor’s degree is preferred.
</p></li>
<li><p>3+ years of audio visual experience</p></li>
<li><p>
1+ years of supervisory experience</p></li>
<li><p>
2+ years of customer service or hospitality experience is preferred.</p></li>
<li><p>
Sales experience is a plus.</p></li>
<li><p>
Working knowledge of audio visual equipment in a live show environment</p></li>
<li><p>
Proficiency with the use of computer hardware</p></li>
<li><p>
Proficiency with computer software and programs, including the Internet and Microsoft Office</p></li>
<li><p>
Effective leadership abilities and customer satisfaction focus.
</p></li>
<li><p>A valid driver’s license is required for team members who may operate Company vehicles.<br>
</p></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Work is performed in a venue/convention center environment with moderate exposure to outdoor temperatures and to dirt, sand and/or dust. The working conditions will vary between moderately quiet to noisy volumes. Team members will use high-end audio visual equipment and electrical components, and will be exposed to heights via lifts and ladders. Team members may be asked to work in multiple venue locations. Working times will include irregular hours and on-call status including days, evenings, weekends and holidays. Team members must adhere to appearance guidelines as defined by PSAV based on an individual venue or a representation of venues in that city or area.<br>
</p>
