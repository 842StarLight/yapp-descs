<p>Responsibilities
</p>
<div><div><div>
<div>InComm is adding a DBA Manager to our team who will be responsible for the management of database administrators and systems. This individual should possess strong competencies in Oracle and SQL database troubleshooting, configuration, support and setup, backup/recovery, and related tasks with multiple instances of Oracle databases. You must have a solid understanding of other inter-related database, storage, operating system, networking, and other technologies commonly found in the architecture of larger enterprise class environments. A "can do" attitude is critical as well as ability to be flexible, level-headed under pressure, strong team work sensibilities, and a general all around hard worker.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
As the Mgr. I, DBA, you will lead our organization’s data management, security, and maintenance initiatives while providing day-to-day direction for our team of database administrators. You will work with the many other information technology professionals in our organization to ensure the security, availability, and speed of our databases. This individual should possess excellent Technical skills as well as excellent Soft Skills. The ability to professionally communicate with leadership and peers is just as important as the technical skills required in this role. The ability to multi-task with excellent Time Management is a core requirement. Proactiveness and Accountability are keys to operational excellence in this position. The ideal candidate for this position will have strong PL/SQL development experience in Oracle environment. You will be sharp, motivated, hardworking, and well versed in enterprise reporting tools, frameworks, and best practices. Experience writing testable, scalable solutions is very important to us.</div>
