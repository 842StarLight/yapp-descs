<p><b>Job Description
</b></p>
<p>Manage the implementation of 1 - 2 simultaneous projects,
</p>
<ul><li>Demonstrate competency in the Workday product suite - HCM, Payroll, and/or Financials.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Participate in the sales cycle when requested to gather requirements, formulate delivery approach and develop a proposal/SOW.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Provide guidance and mentoring to those Professional Service resources working on their implementations.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ensure projects are properly planned and staffed.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Work with the client project manager to identify/resolve all issues that could impact project scope and/or timeframes.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Work with Delivery Assurance to ensure compliance with agreed to checkpoints.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Present at Executive Steering Committee meetings.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ensure the client takes advantage of Workday best practices.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Be a liaison for professional services when interacting with sales and/or development.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Identify opportunities to position other service offerings.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ensure the client can serve as a reference upon completing their implementation.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Assist in the transition of the customer from professional services to product support.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ensure the project is delivered within the budget outlined in the SOW.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Present client with change orders in the event they're asking Workday to perform tasks that are outside the scope of the SOW.
</li></ul>
