<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities &amp; Requirements
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p>The Director of Clinical Products and Solutions, leads the clinical program design and implementation strategy for clinical products working in close collaboration with the Director of Clinical Services, Care Management Operations and the Consultation and Clinical Analytics team led by the Executive Medical Director. Responsibilities include assessment of customer clinical solutions needs and design of clinical solutions to meet customer needs; interface with the Product, Sales and Finance teams to price and market new clinical products; establishing and monitoring clinical products performance and financial targets; oversight of product implementation process. Serves as a liaison with Health Care Services partners on national account program design and RFPs, and as the primary clinical interface with the Blue Cross Blue Shield Associations for population health and clinical program initiatives. Leads design of comprehensive clinical programs that provide a competitive advantage in the national marketplace. Ensures programs offered are market competitive, compliant with regulatory and accrediting standards, and designed to manage health care costs while achieving quality outcomes. Serves a key member of broader Clinical Services Leadership Team to establish business objectives, allocate resources and drive results.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
General Functions and Outcomes:</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Provides leadership in developing, implementing, and communicating short and long-range plans, goals, and objectives for clinical teams. Aligns team goals with the organization’s vision and strategy.
</li>
<li>Develops strategies and tactics to effectively manage healthcare costs, improve clinical quality and enhance the member experience for assigned lines of business.
</li>
<li>Collaborates with Medical Directors, Healthcare Informatics, Network Management and other departments as needed to collect, analyze, and report on effectiveness of programs and address quality of care issues. Uses data to guide the development and implementation of health care interventions that improve value to the member.</li>
<li>
Manages the organization by ensuring clear performance expectations along with appropriate skills sets. Fosters an effective work environment and ensures employees receive recognition, feedback and development. Participates in organizational talent management and succession planning.</li>
<li>
Determines appropriate staffing levels and resource needs, creates and manages department and/or project budget, allocates resources, and approves expenditures.</li>
<li>
Oversees the development of effective and efficient clinical and operational policies, procedures and standards. Monitors results and implements process or system changes as needed.</li>
<li>
Ensures clinical programs meet federal and state regulations, accreditation standards, quality metrics, client requirements, and evolving models of care (e.g. accountable care organizations, patient centered homes).
</li>
<li>Makes recommendations as to the use and selection of external vendors. Provides clinical leadership and operational oversight to vendors.</li>
<li>
Manages 3-5 FTE's.</li>
</ul>

<div><b>
Minimum Requirements:</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s Degree in Nursing or other Healthcare Related Field with a minimum of 7 years of progressively responsible clinical care experience in designing and leading health plan care management programs and clinical products and solutions to serve large fully- and self-insured employer groups., Masters degree is preferred.</li>
<li>
Current unrestricted Registered Nurse (RN) license, or other healthcare related licensure or certification. Certification in care management and/or population health management strongly preferred.</li>
<li>
Demonstrated experience in designing and delivering comprehensive care management programs and clinical solutions for large, national fully- and self-insured employer groups. Includes experience with clinical solution design and marketing/pricing, demonstrated experience working with customers to design and deliver solutions; reporting clinical outcomes, value propositions, and return-on-investment analyses. Direct experience with consumer-focused programs and clinical management of programs serving employer groups.</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to lead high performing teams, manage managers, and direct vendors.</li>
<li>
Strong communication and facilitation skills with all levels of the organization, including the ability to resolve issues and build consensus among groups of diverse stakeholders.</li>
<li>
General business acumen including understanding of market dynamics, financial/budget management, data analysis and decision making.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of health insurance industry trends and technology.
</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to create and execute care management and utilization management programs and drive results across internal teams and/or external vendors.</li>
<li>
Extensive knowledge of CMS and state regulatory requirements and demonstrated ability to manage compliant operations. NCQA, URAC and/or HEDIS accreditation experience strongly preferred.</li>
<li>
Ability to decipher and interpret clinical data and deliver presentations to various internal/external audiences related to clinical outcomes.</li>
</ul>
