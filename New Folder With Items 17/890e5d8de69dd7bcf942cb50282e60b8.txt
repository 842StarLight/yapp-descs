<h2>Accounts Payable Administrator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Want to work for a company that is reimagining distribution? Join Univar Solutions, the premier global distributor of chemicals and ingredients. Our goal is to function as a direct extension of our customers’ teams; to serve as an ally in their quest of business success.<br>
</p>
<p>At Univar Solutions, we’re building on our more than 90 years of experience and drawing on deep resources – inventory and logistics experts, scientists, PhDs, procurement, customer service, sales and marketers-to deliver consultative services and an unmatched portfolio to solve customers’ formulation and operational challenges. When you work for Univar Solutions, you’ll play an important role in shaping how we deliver more than chemical and ingredients.</p>
<div>
<b>
Primary Purpose:</b>
</div>
<div>The primary role of the FSS Accounts Payable Administrator is to monitor, review and update approver tables for DCI and SAP. This role is also responsible for creating and running the payment runs and manual one-off checks. Works in a team environment as a part of the Finance Shared Services Department.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>Financial Responsibility:
</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Ensuring integrity of master data and proper delegation of authority under delegation of authority policy</li>
<br>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Education/Experience/Training:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Associates Degree in Accounting or Finance required, Bachelor’s degree preferred</li>
<li>2 plus years Accounts Payable experience required</li>
<br>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Specialized Knowledge/Skills:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Technology proficiency in Microsoft Office and creation of Excel queries a plus</li>
<li>Proficient knowledge and experience working in SAP or other major ERP system a big plus</li>
<li>Sharepoint experience is a plus</li>
<li>Experience with workflow and automation</li>
<li>Experience with Visio and Access to maintain and update reports.
</li>
<li>Ability to utilize 3-monitor setup for batch scanning, data entry and importing into accounting software</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills-has mastered business communications in English</li>
<li>High volume data entry</li>
<li>Excellent attention to detail and accuracy, ability to adhere to multiple deadlines and multi-task</li>
<li>Cross functional team collaboration</li>
<li>Must possess excellent organization, time management and prioritization skills</li>
<li>Ability to be flexible and adapt to changing policies and procedures</li>
<li>Excellent customer service (internal &amp; external) from clerical level to C-suite</li>
<li>Experience with and understanding of distribution business is a plus</li>
<li>Foreign language (French, Spanish) is a plus</li>
<br>
</ul>
