<div>Responsibilities</div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<div><ul>
<li>Understand the goal, audience, and message of a campaign and create copy to suit.</li>
<li>
Write advertising copy for use by publication or broadcast media to promote the sale of goods and services. Research, write, edit and proof for all online, offline, external and internal needs.</li>
<li>
Build and implement social media programs that ensure appropriate messaging is executed online, to support corporate goals, incorporating Facebook, LinkedIn, Twitter, YouTube, Slideshare, Google+, etc.</li>
<li>
Monitor the company’s social media performance usage reports and tracking search engine results.</li>
<li>
Use creative thinking and initiative to analyze site performance metrics and make recommendations on content, site design or features to improve the effectiveness of web content and/or applications.</li>
<li>
Provide support to other team members on online promotions (such as Hubspot/Wordpress, Google, AdWords campaigns and email marketing).</li>
<li>
Assist with core team development of an integrated marketing communications plans that align with industry strategies leveraging the most effective balance of media.</li>
<li>
Execute website and social media updates at the direction of the management.</li>
<li>
Identify website functionality needs for commercial content delivery and effectiveness.</li>
<li>
Act as liaison for supporting organizational functions for Website maintenance.</li>
<li>
Support the Marketing team in the building of media relationships to create the PR and editorial opportunities that will enable a consistent cadence in targeted channels.</li>
<li>
Aid in the development of press releases, scripts and editorials.</li>
<li>
Act as coordinator, editor, and execute internal newsletters.</li>
<li>
Measure and analyze results/effectiveness of marketing programs/initiatives/campaigns and recommend changes based on performance.</li>
<li>
Assist with the execution of organizing trade event logistics, which will include booth setup/take-down, customer social events, sales collateral, ensure electrical and WIFI capabilities, etc.</li>
<li>
Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul></div>
