<h2>Executive Sales Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>About Aceyus:</p>
<p>Since 2002, Aceyus has provided software solutions to companies with enterprise-level contact centers. Our advanced product portfolio and range of services cover reporting, computer telephony integration (CTI) and resource management solutions for the Cisco ICM, Cisco UCCE, Avaya and ASPECT environments. With a foundation of expertise in the telecommunications market, we develop products specifically for the contact center environment that work in harmony with established market-leading solutions.</p>
<p>We are a mid-sized company that is quickly expanding, with corporate headquarters are in Charlotte, NC, a Support &amp; Development Lab in the Chicago area. As an organization, we have a reputation for bringing personality to an otherwise colorlessless industry -- so we like to find employees that will bring their unique sense of character to work with them each day. We value hardworking, out-of-the-box thinkers with positive attitudes.</p>
<p>About the Position:</p>
<p>Aceyus is looking for an experienced, reliable and task-oriented Sales Assistant. The Sales Assistant will work directly with senior-level executives and will be responsible for performing a number of sales administrative duties. The ideal candidate is highly self-motivated, professional, and capable of managing their workload and prioritizing tasks in a fast-paced corporate environment. This is an excellent opportunity to join a growing company with competitive compensation</p>
<p>Essential functions and Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Provides advanced, diversified, and complex administrative support to the various senior sales leaders</li>
<li>Coordinate executive communications, including taking calls, responding to emails, managing travel itineraries and interfacing with clients</li>
<li>Manage all aspects of travel arrangements such as booking, preparing travel itineraries, making changes as needed, and preparing expense report submissions</li>
<li>Manage reservations, meetings, and other group activities</li>
<li>Manage a complex and dynamic calendar</li>
<li>Assist with budgetary planning; balancing general ledger lines and/or expense accounts; researching general ledger issues</li>
<li>Establish and implement office procedures and controls</li>
<li>Coordinate facilities support, floor administration, space planning</li>
<li>Manage onboarding and off-boarding processes for associates and submit service requests for equipment and system access.</li>
<li>Manage executive calendar and schedule</li>
<li>Prepare and proof correspondence, reports and other written material and prioritize work assigned to meet individual goals of the executives.</li>
<li>Schedule various in-house and outside meetings (including luncheon meetings) as required.</li>
<li>Assist with special projects, reports and activities.</li>
<li>Miscellaneous duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p>Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrated intermediate skills with the standard features of various personal computer word processing and spreadsheet software (Microsoft Word and Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook, Internet, OneNote, and other proprietary software)</li>
<li>Ability to relate well and cooperate with others to effectively coordinate activities and accomplish goals</li>
<li>Superior interpersonal and oral/written communication skills</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to learn and master new software packages as needed</li>
<li>Strong attention to detail and creative mindset to generate efficiencies and new ideas for getting things done</li>
<li>Ability to work independently</li>
</ul>
<p>Education/Experience:</p>
<ul>
<li>Associates degree required; Bachelors degree preferred</li>
<li>Minimum 2 years related experience supporting executive and senior leaders</li>
<li>Desire to grow and succeed in a fast-paced growing organization</li>
<li>Experience working in for sales executives a plus</li>
</ul>
<p>Benefits:</p>
<p>Other than being a supportive, team-oriented and a fantastic place to work, Aceyus offers a competitive benefit package with multiple plan choices of Medical, Dental, Vision, Short-Term Disability, additional Life Insurance, and a Flexible Spending Account plan. Basic Group Term Life and Long-Term Disability are fully paid for by Aceyus. Aceyus also offers a 401k plan with a 4% company match, paid holidays, and a generous vacation policy with 16 days of PTO in the first year and higher accrual rates with tenure. Our full-time employees also have access to an education assistance policy.</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul><li>Sales Assistant: 2 years (Preferred)</li></ul>
