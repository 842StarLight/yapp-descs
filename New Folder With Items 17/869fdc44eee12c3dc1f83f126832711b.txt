<p><b>BUSINESS</b></p>
<p>
ESCAPE CAMPERVANS is a unique and fast-growing travel company that rents custom-built campervans across North America. We provide a budget-friendly, functional and fun vehicle for weekends away, cross-country road trips, and everything in between. Perfect for couples, families, and friends, we strive to inspire adventure and exploration off-the-beaten path. Our teams have local knowledge and expertise to help our renters get the most out of their vacation, and all of our vans are hand-painted by local artists in LA. It’s about the freedom and spontaneity the van gives you to explore the beauties of our planet in a safe and fun way.
</p>
<p>Founded in 2009 in LA, we now have 450+ vans across 12 locations – LA, San Francisco, Seattle, Denver, Las Vegas, New York, Phoenix, Miami, Atlanta, Portland,Vancouver, and Calgary. We are an energetic, inspired, and entrepreneurial team. We have a passion for travel and believe in the power of adventure. And we all wear a lot of hats.</p>
<p><b>
POSITION SUMMARY</b></p>
<p>
Escape Campervans is growing very quickly which means we get a LOT of inbound interest. We need the right person to be our first touch point, the first friendly face, to answer questions and excite customers about their upcoming trip. We also need this person to solve customer problems and energize potential customers about booking an amazing trip with Escape.</p>
<p>
Responsibilities include but are not limited to complaint &amp; problem resolution, handling incoming phone calls &amp; e-mail requests, expediting/routing reservation and roadside assistance calls. This person will be the first and primary point of contact for most Escape customers.</p>
