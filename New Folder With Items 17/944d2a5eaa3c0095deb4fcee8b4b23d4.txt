<p><b>Job Summary
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>The Data Scientist Analyst Co-op position involves working as part of a dynamic team of Data Scientists, Sales Strategists, and Market Data Analysts within the Technology and Distribution departments. This team works with internal business partners within Distribution to develop models and provide analytics to improve decision-making.
</p>
<p></p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Principal Responsibilities
</b></h3>
<p>Primary functions include, but are not limited to:
</p>
<p></p>
<p>1. Working with domain specialists in Distribution and Technology to integrate business needs with data analytics technology solutions. These solutions will be delivered by:
</p>
<p></p>
<p>a. Defining the problem and identifying business requirements.
</p>
<p>b. Identifying, collecting, loading, and preparing the data.
</p>
<p>c. Building analytical models using descriptive, predictive and other advanced analytical techniques on transformed data sets.
</p>
<p>d. Analyzing and interpreting the results of the analytical models.
</p>
<p>e. Communicating results clearly and concisely using visual formats.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>2. Working with other Data Scientists and Technology domain specialists to identify opportunities and deliver novel process improvements to the Distribution business units, leveraging:
</p>
<p></p>
<p>a. Data from internal systems including CRM, Sales, and MFS.com
</p>
<p>b. Third-Party Market Data
</p>
<p>c. Other identified data sets as required
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Pursuing a Bachelor's or Master's degree in a related area. Data Science, Statistics, Computer Science, Mathematics, Economics, Analytics, or Applied Mathematics are preferred.
</li>
<li>Experience in applying Advanced Analytics and/or Data Science Modeling Techniques. This could be project experience as part of a class or work experience.
</li>
<li>Strong expertise utilizing Python or R for machine learning and statistical analysis.
</li>
<li>Basic knowledge of the following – Supervised and Unsupervised Learning including Classification Models, Cluster Analysis, Neural Networks, Non-parametric Methods, Multivariate Statistics, Reliability Models, Markov Models, Stochastic models, Bayesian Models as well as NLP (Natural Language Processing)
</li>
<li>Comfort manipulating and analyzing data in various formats. Experience with complex, high-volume, high-dimensionality data from varying sources is a plus.
</li>
<li>Understanding of relational, XML and NoSQL database models with and associated data access methods (SQL)
</li>
<li>Strong communication skills
</li>
<li>Proven analytical skills and demonstrated ability to take initiative and help with developing creative solutions to meet business needs
</li>
<li>Experience working on a team, preferably on projects
</li>
</ul>
