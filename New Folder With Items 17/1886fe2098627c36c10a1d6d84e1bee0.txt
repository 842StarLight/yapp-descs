<h2>National Operations Director - VIP Services</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Job Summary:</p>
<p><b>
Unity in Diversity
</b></p>
<p>Our team members are as diverse as our music venues. With its owned/operated/managed venues, Live Nation Clubs and Theaters, a division of Live Nation Entertainment, is wide-ranging, including the legendary Fillmore venues and the intimate House of Blues clubs throughout the United States. Live Nation Clubs and Theaters is the country’s preeminent group of intimate music venues, featuring superior sound and lighting technology in one-of-a-kind custom-designed environments aimed to bring fans and artists together in unparalleled musical environments.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
Our mission is to create a profitable and principled global entertainment company. To celebrate the diversity and brotherhood of world culture. To promote racial and spiritual harmony through love, peace, truth, righteousness and non-violence. Our venues bring fans as close to artists as possible and our aim is to create teams that are just as close.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Who are you?</b></p>
<p>
Passionate and motivated. Driven, with an entrepreneurial spirit. Resourceful, innovative, forward thinking and committed. At Live Nation Clubs &amp; Theaters, our people embrace these qualities, so if this sounds like you, then please read on!</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
The Role:</b></p>
<p>
The National Operations Director of VIP Services is responsible for assistance in the overall direction, revenue and management of Foundation Room Operations and VIP Musical Hall operations including business development, market competitiveness, pricing, packaging, compensation and marketing strategy .</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
What You Need:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s Degree in General Business categories, Hospitality, or like field.</li>
<li>
Minimum 8-10 years in high end F&amp;B or Entertainment in a senior management role</li>
<li>
Minimum of 5-7 years in a senior leadership role, preferably in multi-unit management, upscale food and beverage, nightlife, or live music premium operations.</li>
<li>
Knowledge in operating reservation platform systems, point of sales systems and proficient in excel and other office suite knowledge</li>
<li>
Position is based out of Hollywood, CA Home Office or Las Vegas, NV</li>
<li>
Proven effective leadership skills</li>
<li>
Ability to interface with people at all levels of the organization</li>
<li>
Experience with Microsoft Office products: Word, Excel, PowerPoint</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
What You’ll Do:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Operational liaison for Foundation Room operations and local management</li>
<li>Oversee Execution of all directives and national initiatives for revenue growth, customer satisfaction and operational efficiencies</li>
<li>Assist in driving teams to achieve revenue goals, CM targets and guest satisfaction against annual budgets</li>
<li>Provide analytics and goal tracking per venue and identify deficiencies and provide solutions and guidance</li>
<li>Help in training and continued development of all positions within Foundation Room and Night of operations of VIP MH teams</li>
<li>Supervise and audit systems for accurate and timely data such as Hot schedules, Cruchtime, Avero and Micros</li>
<li>Assist in oversight of operational expenses, scheduling, marketing deadlines and overall operational deadline requirements of the team</li>
<li>Supervise operational execution against all marketing campaigns and directives by venue</li>
<li>Assist in finding new avenues for sales, ideation of events per room and overall continued evolution of department and services.</li>
<li>Overview of all programming calendars and work in tandem with local management for proper marketing support and tactics</li>
<li>Work with F&amp;B team on new product roll outs, system improvements execution and menu analysis</li>
<li>Review all guest facing websites, social sites and collateral for brand consistency and accurate messaging or content</li>
<li>Organize and coordinate touch point meetings by venue and national department</li>
<li>Assist in coaching opportunities through secret shops and venue visits</li>
<li>Assist in oversight of best practices for MH VIP ops</li>
<li>Assist in financial audits, P&amp;L and GL analysis by venue</li>
</ul>
