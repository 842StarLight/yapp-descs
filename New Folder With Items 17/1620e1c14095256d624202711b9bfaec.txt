<p><b>PRINCIPAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b><b>:</b></p>

<li>
Participate actively in industry training programs; strive to complete industry and supplier certification programs beyond entry level</li>
<li>
Willingness to learn new equipment and materials</li>
<li>
Promote sales and repeat business by practicing positive customer service skills with clients</li>
<li>
Troubleshoot and solve problems to rectify problem situations with clients</li>
<li>
Work as an effective team member, freely sharing knowledge and expertise with other technicians</li>
<li>
Demonstrate competency in professional installation of ADAS calibration assuring the quality of workmanship</li>

<li>
Willingness to learn new installation techniques, equipment and materials</li>
<li>
Communicates JNP’s glass values to customers</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
<b>
DAILY TASKS INVOLVE THE FOLLOWING:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Technicians with morning dispatches are expected to depart by 8:15 am</li>
<li>
Driving a company vehicle to and from each job location</li>
<li>
Driving a customer vehicle to complete the calibration process</li>
<li>
Operating a hand-held electronic device to calibrate the ADAS systems, not larger than an iPad</li>
<li>
Operating a cell phone and/or tablet utilizing camera and e-mail functions</li>
<li>
On static calibrations, will be required to measure out targets and target stands and place them in specific locations to calibrate the ADAS system utilizing common measuring devices, plumb bobs and laser levels</li>
<li>
Operating a computer or laptop to perform system updates, data transfers and patch installations</li>
</ul>
