<h2>Keyholder for Indie Plush Toy Company - Squishable @ Westfarms Mall</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Squishable, an established indie plush toy company, is seeking an enthusiastic team to staff our pop-up store at the Westfarms Mall in Farmington, CT. Our Westfarms location will open in early November and will run thru the end of December. We are looking for experienced keyholders for immediate work.</p>
<p>Qualifications are below.</p>
<p><b>Role</b>: Temporary, P/T, 10-30 hrs/wk<br><b>Compensation</b>: $14</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b>:</p>
<p>- Set and maintain exceptional brand and operational standards within the store<br>- Perform all POS transactions in an efficient and courteous manner<br>- Engage with customers and create a fun and memorable environment within the store<br>- Process inbound shipments<br>- Maintain efficient organization of inventory and ensure that all merchandise is displayed correctly as per brand visual guidelines<br>- Open and close the store<br>- Foster a passion for and willingness to become Squishable ambassadors--to learn about our unique products and the communication skills to translate that into the highest level of customer service</p>
<p><b>Requirements</b>:</p>
<p>- 18 years old or older<br>- Reasonably open availability with flexibility to work both evenings and weekends<br>- Previous retail keyholder experience, preferably in a pop-up, promotional or boutique environment<br>- Leads by example and served as mentor to sales associates<br>- Familiarity with Square or similar POS software<br>- Ability to lift cartons up to 20 lbs.</p>
<p><b>For consideration please submit a resume and brief cover letter.</b></p>
<p>Job Types: Part-time, Temporary</p>
<p>Salary: $14.00 /hour</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul>
<li>relevant: 1 year (Preferred)</li>
<li>retail sales/customer service: 1 year (Preferred)</li>
</ul>
