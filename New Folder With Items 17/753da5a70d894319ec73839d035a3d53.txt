<div><b>Position Overview</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>Our Support Group is a dynamic Support Organization for EBS and Fusion/Cloud Support of existing DAZ customers. The group includes Support Managers, Functional consultants and Developers along with an offshore Support staff to help with 24x7 situations. The Team is very diverse in skills and needs to be a quick study as well as know their subject matter extremely well. The support group frequently implements new orgs, new modules, handles upgrades, new interfaces and other customer projects. Consultants build relationships as named resources, attend weekly meetings, some on site work as needed, and attend to raised issues. The Team also plays a strategic role in each customer’s use of Oracle products by providing guidance, attending steering meetings and being a liaison for Accenture. Support is a great spot to continue to build skills, relationships.
</div>
<div>The Oracle Cloud/Fusion HCM Senior Principal Consultant will provide functional leadership for assigned project role with focus on implementing, maintaining and supporting Oracle Cloud/Fusion HCM applications and any relative solution design, business process analysis, and system integration. Provide functional Oracle HCM or Oracle Cloud/Fusion HCM leadership and guidance to client regarding “industry leading practices” and the functionality of the E-Business Suite software or Cloud /Fusion HCM modules. Create and maintain effective client relationships. Participate in, and support, profitable business development.</div>
<div><b>Essential Job Functions</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Identify business requirements and align them to Oracle Cloud/Fusion HCM functionality.
</li>
<li>Configure the Cloud/Fusion HCM Application based on client requirements.
</li>
<li>Provide functional application leadership and guidance to client regarding “leading practices” and the functionality of the software modules through all phases of an implementation.
</li>
<li>Work with client to develop business solution designs to ensure that business requirements are met.
</li>
<li>Provide thought leadership around industry leading practices for improving operational efficiencies and profitability of client business.
</li>
<li>Document and perform application set ups in test and production instances.
</li>
<li>Thoroughly review application set up and configuration with the appropriate client lead to obtain sign off.
</li>
<li>Raise issues as they come up and work on resolutions in a timely manner to ensure adherence to the project schedule.
</li>
<li>Work with the respective client functional lead to ensure knowledge transfer.
</li>
<li>Participate with the respective client functional lead throughout system testing.
</li>
<li>Provide guidance to the respective client functional lead in the preparation of training materials and class agendas.</li>
</ul>
