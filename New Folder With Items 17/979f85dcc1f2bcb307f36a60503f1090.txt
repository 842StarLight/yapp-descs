<h2>Senior Wireless Network Engineer, Managed Services</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>Seeking an experienced network engineer who is passionate about cutting-edge wireless and security technologies such as 802.11ac and is ready to take the next step in their career. You will join a growing technical team as a Sr. Wireless Network Engineer (Wireless Network Engineer II). In this role you will touch all aspects of the network (LAN, WLAN, WAN and security) as you help to realize successful execution and total customer satisfaction with our managed services customers to satisfy real business needs for today and tomorrow.<br>
<br>
To succeed in this role you will have a proven track record of independently and jointly delivering results on-time and within budget to the delight of your customers across a variety of networking projects (WiFi network design, heatmap creation and development, core switch cutovers, and security best practices). You will have experience working for Managed Services Providers in an engineering role. You will have, at minimum, an active CCNP Wireless certification with a concrete plan to acquire your next level certification. You will have no fear of discovering and surveying unknown network environments and producing detailed remediation plans, as well as delegating tasks to other engineers as appropriate.<br>
<br>
You must be self-disciplined, work well on individual tasks in a team environment, and be able to work well and communicate clearly with both technical and non-technical personnel in high-stress situations.<br>
