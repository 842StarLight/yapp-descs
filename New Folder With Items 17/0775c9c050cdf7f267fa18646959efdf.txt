<br>Microprocessor Technician
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
Maintain electrical, electronic, and computer systems throughout the plant.
<br>Maintain documentation on various systems throughout the plant.
<br>Assist operators and process engineers in solving quality problems.
<br>Participate in one safety sub-committee.
<br>Assist contractors in the installation of new equipment or upgrading of existing equipment.
<br>Maintain spare electronic equipment and parts.
<br>Assist control equipment technicians and or other associates with troubleshooting electrical problems.
<br>Help train other shop associates as needed.
<br>Participate in on or off-site training as required.
<br><br>
<b>Schedule:</b> Available for call-ins (emergency) , Weekend rotations as scheduled , Available to work overtime or other shifts if needed
<br><br>
<b>Knowledge/Skills:</b><br>
Understanding and troubleshooting of electrical (AC and DC) and control systems
<br>Power distribution
<br>Relay logic
<br>Electrical print reading
<br>PLC programming and troubleshooting
<br>Drive wiring and programming
<br><b>Control equipment including:</b> RTDs, flow meters, D/P cells, I/Ps, load cells, positioners, compensators, etc
<br>Voltages up to 600VAC
<br>Troubleshooting mechanical systems
<br>Understanding of hydraulic and pneumatic systems
<br>Knowledge of general safety procedures including proper PPE and 70e
<br>Documentation and root cause analysis (5 Why)
<br>Organization of spare parts inventory
<br><br>
<b>Programs/Systems:</b><br>
Microsoft Office (Word, Excel, etc)
<br>PLCs (Allen Bradley, GE, Siemens, etc)
<br>PLC software and ladder logic
<br>Bus systems
<br>Drives
<br><br>

Microprocessor Associates degree or equivalent
<br>5 years of experience in field
