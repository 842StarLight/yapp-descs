<div>Are you ready to help Amazon build a fast-growing business? As a Sr. Marketing Manager, you will help build, communicate, and grow Amazon's vision of delivering a new premier online services marketplace, Amazon Home Services (http://www.amazon.com/services). Our goal is to make the find, buy, and fulfill experience for services as trusted, easy, and reliable as it is for products.<br>
<br>
This leader will own the strategy and implementation of our scalable campaigns and email marketing automation to drive seller acquisition and seller success. This person is responsible for developing and leading the implementation of our automation platform strategy to support seller acquisition and multi-channel campaign management. This leader will be responsible for scaling a new ecosystem across cities and ensuring active engagement through automation. This is a unique opportunity to influence a new business and to grow an entire new segment of the Amazon portfolio.<br>
<br>
We’re looking for a marketing rock-star to join our team—someone who will drive awareness and engagement. This person thoroughly understands the mechanics of business-to-business marketing and has successfully built and maintained a well-oiled, lead generation machine. This role will be responsible for demand generation and sales enablement — particularly presenting Selling Services on Amazon as an important service that will help pros grow their business.<br>
<br>
The ideal candidate will be a self-starter with a passion for independent, creative problem-solving, have proven data analysis skills, show strong ownership/commitment, have proven leadership delivering large scale marketing automation. Customer obsession is a must.<br>
<br>
Roles &amp; Responsibilities<br>
<ul>
<li>Define and implement marketing automation strategies to nurture leads, improve registration and retention. This will include defining engineering requirements where needed to implement strategy.</li>
<li>Create innovative marketing programs, campaigns and promotions across various channels, such as email marketing, events, online advertising, webinars, social media, SEM, co-marketing with partners, etc.</li>
<li>Develop targeted messages based on the key value propositions and use cases.</li>
<li>Partner with sales management to identify needs and execute lead generation programs.</li>
<li>Work closely with account service teams, sales/business development, PR, product management, evangelists, and other marketing and product managers to achieve goals.</li>
<li>Create marketing collateral to support your campaigns including online, print and video media; manage internal &amp; external agencies and creative partners as needed.</li>
<li>Use compelling and comprehensive data to measure performance of programs and drive innovation.</li>
<li>Identify opportunities in service provider life cycle to automate communications to improve engagement</li>
<li>Design flexible campaigns to support personalized content based on different lead sources/ professions/geographies</li>
<li>Champion an iterative test-and-learn philosophy on the team. Includes holding back control groups, conducting A/B test, and report on results to team.</li>
<li>Contribute to the creation of progress dashboard and key performance indicators to measure ROI. Monitor and analyze performance of email activities to measure impact.</li>
</ul>
Key Capabilities<br>
<ul>
<li>Deep knowledge of business-to-business marketing methods, tools and strategies; ability to plan, develop and execute complex multi-platform campaigns that drive leads.</li>
<li>Strong analytical and quantitative skills; ability to use customer research, hard data and metrics to back up assumptions, generate credible forecasts, and assess the efficacy of marketing programs.</li>
<li>Strengths in problem solving and issue-resolution, ability to work effectively in a deadline-driven work environment, attention to detail, and ability to multitask.</li>
<li>Outstanding skills at initiating, managing and growing strategic relationships with third parties, vendors and internal stakeholders.</li>
<li>Experience working with Marketo and Salesforce.com or comparable marketing and CRM automation platforms.</li>
<li>Team-oriented</li>
</ul>
