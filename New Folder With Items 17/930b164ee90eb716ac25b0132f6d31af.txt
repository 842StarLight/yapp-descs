<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p>The role of the <b>Director of DOD Business Development</b> is to identify, qualify and capture opportunities across the U.S. Department of Defense. More specifically, in the Army, Air Force and Defense Agencies. This position is currently an individual performer role and will report directly to the Senior Vice President of Business Development. Qualified candidates will have a strong understanding of the programs and acquisition methods used by the U.S. Department of Defense, including a deep understanding of their mission, goals and objectives, as well as their IT infrastructure, and mission solution requirements and challenges. This role will be responsible for developing Account Plans, Call Plans, and associated execution strategies which will result in the successful identification and ultimate capture of opportunities that align with IntelliDyne capabilities and advance the mission of our clients. We are looking for an “intrapreneurial” mindset, someone who can individually build their own portfolio of business that can quite possibly grow into something more over time.</p>
