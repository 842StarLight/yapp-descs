<h2>Sales Strategy Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Who are we?
<br><br>
At Intersection, we are at the forefront of the smart cities revolution. Our mission is to improve daily life in cities and public spaces, with products that bridge the digital and physical worlds by delivering connectivity, information and content to enrich our everyday journeys and elevate the urban experience.
<br><br>
We pair our human-centered methodology with groundbreaking technology to design, develop, deliver, and maintain unique products and experiences in public space that deliver value to advertisers, cities, and consumers. Whether partnering with urban transit systems to revolutionize commuting and travel, with cities to transform how they connect with residents and visitors, or private developers to craft unforgettable experiences in neighborhoods and districts, our solutions are scalable platforms on which our clients can build the future.

<br><br>
What is the role?
<br><br>
Intersection is looking for ambitious, data-driven and analytical individual to take on an exciting challenge and join one of the highest-performing revenue teams in advertising. We pride ourselves on setting aggressive goals and have a ton of fun achieving them together.
<br><br>
Intersection's Sales Strategy Coordinator will be responsible for contributing to revenue growth by helping sales leaders track against KPIs, supporting sales initiatives, and identifying areas of growth by fielding inbound leads and researching prospects. We foster a performance based culture and invite optimistic, talented, and motivated people to join our team.
<br><br>
<b>As a Sales Strategy Coordinator at Intersection, you will:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Research prospects externally and internally to ensure leads are qualified</li>
<li>Use Excel, Salesforce.com and other data tools to create reports and derive insights &amp; trends in an effort to help sales leaders make decisions for their business</li>
<li>Have the autonomy to operate beyond your role and help the team continuously improve and optimize our processes, training, use of technology, and other aspects of our business</li>
<li>Support the Sales Strategy &amp; Enablement team with ad hoc projects and ongoing initiatives as we work to help drive revenue growth and remove roadblocks for the sales organization</li>
</ul>
To excel in the role, you should
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Have a strong desire to pursue a career in sales strategy, operations and enablement</li>
<li>Have a strong will to achieve - Call it what you want: grit, competitive spirit, hustle</li>
<li>Be flexible and adaptable - your day to day will vary!</li>
<li>Make others around you better while achieving your own goals</li>
<li>Be committed to lifelong learning and continuous development</li>
<li>Be innately and insatiably curious</li>
<li>Be coachable (don't just do what we say, but rather be self-aware and reflective, and digest feedback critically and adapt to overcome challenges)</li>
<li>Be friendly, enthusiastic and demonstrate empathy and thoughtfulness in your professional communication.</li>
</ul>
What We Expect
<br><br>
<ul>

<li>Relevant experience in analysis &amp; research is a plus</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to take initiative and see projects through from beginning to completion</li>
<li>Ability to effectively work in a high-performance sales environment &amp; work cross-functionally across the organization</li>
<li>Impeccable attention to detail is a must</li>
<li>Excellent time management skills ability to meet deadlines</li>
<li>Excellent and professional communication and presentation skills</li>
