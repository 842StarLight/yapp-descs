<b>Engineering Manager - Sales Engineering</b><br>
<br>
The B2B Sales engineering team within the Sales Engineering department is looking for an experienced Engineering Manager to build and design new systems, scale existing systems, mentor Engineers , and help set coding and platform standards across our teams.<br>
<br>
<b>About B2B Sales Engineering</b><br>
<br>
The B2B Sales Engineering team is part of the Sales Engineering group that supports the Salesforce CRM platform for Wayfair business units including B2B / B2C / Media Sales / etc. The B2B team owns the workflows and tools including business lead identification, business account creation and enrollment, and data synchronization between Wayfair and Salesforce and workload distribution using Round Robin. The B2B Sales Engineering team works to support the approximately 1,000 Business Account Managers (BAMs) that sell Wayfair's products to businesses.We write our own playbook for success and working cross functionally with everyone from Engineering and Product to Merchandising and Category Management. We pride ourselves on delivering world-class pre- and post-sales support while always raising the bar for ourselves and the industry.<br>
<br>
<b>Current Initiatives include:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Provide a simple, easy and streamlined way to BAM's and Sales Agents to create Business Accounts and Contacts in Salesforce
</li>
<li>A Commissions platform for the BAM's to provide them real time information about their sales and commissions. The objective of this tool is to take out the anxiety about commissions from Agents mind so that they can focus solely on delivering a world class service to customers
</li>
<li>Creating a scalable and real time Freight pricing platform for providing the BAM's a way to get accurate pricing information about shipping in near real time
</li>
<li>Work to build systems to achieve cross channel sentiment analysis.Create a toolset to take advantage of data science models that can give our account managers more information about the customer, so we can better know what they need and when
</li>
<li>Investigate new technologies to improve our systems and engineering proofs of concept (POCs)
</li>
<li>Build out our automated testing suite through unit and integration tests to ensure that the team can iterate quickly with confidence
</li>
<li>Sales funnel real time streaming service, every lead is processed in real time through a distributed and scalable platform that ensures no leads are left behind
</li>
<li>A custom-built communications platform including a Salesforce-based phone system, SMS and chat capabilities - with the ability to integrate call data into Salesforce and Wayfair systems
</li>
</ul>
