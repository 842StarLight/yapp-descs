<h2>General Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>General Manager Job Description</p>
<p>Reports To Owner</p>
<p>Job Summary:</p>
<p>Oversee, direct, and coordinate the planning, organizing, training, and leadership necessary to achieve stated objectives in sales, costs, employee retention, guest service and satisfaction, food and beverage quality, cleanliness and sanitation.</p>
<p><b>Primary</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Promote, work, and act in a manner consistent with Company Mission.</li>
<li>Establish standards for personnel performance and customer service.</li>
<li>Ensure that all restaurant policies, procedures, standards, specifications, guidelines, and training programs are followed and completed on a timely basis.</li>
<li>Monitor compliance with health and fire regulations regarding food preparation and serving, and building maintenance.</li>
<li>Achieve company objectives in sales, service, quality, appearance of facility and sanitation and cleanliness through training of employees and creating a POSITIVE, PRODUCTIVE working environment.</li>
<li>Monitor food preparation methods, portion sizes, garnishing and presentation of food to ensure that food is prepared and presented in an acceptable manner.</li>
<li>Ensure compliance with all federal, state, county and municipal regulations that pertain to health, safety and labor requirements of the restaurant, employees, and guests.</li>
<li>Control cash and other receipts by adhering to cash handling and reconciliation procedures in accordance with restaurant policies and procedures.</li>
<li>Count money and make bank deposits.</li>
<li>Investigate and resolve complaints regarding food quality, service, or accommodations.</li>
<li>Coordinate assignments of cooking personnel to ensure economical use of food and timely preparation.</li>
<li>Monitor budgets and payroll, and review financial transactions to ensure that expenditures are authorized and budgeted.</li>
<li>Schedule staff hours and assign duties.</li>
<li>Perform some food preparation or service tasks, such as cooking, clearing tables, and serving food and drinks when necessary.</li>
<li>Keep records required by government agencies regarding sanitation and licensing.</li>
<li>Test cooked food by tasting and smelling it to ensure palatability and flavor conformity.</li>
<li>Organize and direct worker training programs, resolve personnel problems, make hiring and termination decisions, and evaluate employee performance.</li>
<li>Be knowledgeable of restaurant policies regarding personnel</li>
<li>Be knowledgeable about beer, cider and spirts and ensure staff is properly trained on all product offerings.</li>
<li>Administer prompt, fair and consistent corrective action for any and all violations of company policies, rules and procedures.</li>
<li>Use reward reinforcement to promote positive employee behavior.</li>
<li>Continually strive to develop staff in all areas of managerial and professional development.</li>
<li>Order and purchase equipment and supplies.</li>
<li>Review work procedures and operational problems to determine ways to improve service, performance, or safety.</li>
<li>Assess staffing needs and recruit staff.</li>
<li>Attend all scheduled employee meetings and offer suggestions for improvement.</li>
<li>Coordinate with and assist fellow employees to meet guestsâ€™ needs and support the operation of the restaurant.</li>
<li>Fill-in for fellow employees where needed to ensure guest service standards and efficient operations.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Supplemental</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Monitor employee and guest activities to ensure liquor regulations are obeyed.</li>
<li>Estimate food, liquor, wine, and other beverage consumption to anticipate amounts to be purchased or requisitioned.</li>
<li>Take dining reservations and manage catering.</li>
</ul>
