<b>Job Summary:</b><br>
Gray Vertical Sales provides marketing and advertising support for healthcare and automotive clients. The Vertical Sales team works directly with national accounts and local station sales teams to develop incremental and new revenue for these two important revenue categories.
<br><br>
Gray Vertical Sales seeks an ad operations specialist who is responsible for executing cross-platform solutions on the Gray Television platforms, with a clear focus on the healthcare &amp; automotive industries. The ad operations specialist will work closely with the sales teams to ensure proper placement of advertising campaigns. The ideal candidate will have extensive knowledge of media, healthcare and automotive industries.
<br><br>
<b>General Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Execute work orders for digital campaigns</li>
<li>Launch display, video, social, search, native and email campaigns across multiple platforms</li>
<li>Manage workflow for campaign execution</li>
<li>Troubleshoot order entry errors</li>
<li>Communicate with managers and media planners to update and traffic campaigns, ensuring approval, delivery and pacing</li>
<li>2+ years experience in multi-platform advertising operations desired (related internships and education are applicable)</li>
<li>Bachelors degree or equivalent work experience</li>
<li>Strong knowledge of digital media industry. Knowledge and understanding of the healthcare and/or automotive marketing industries is a plus</li>
<li>Must be able to work and thrive in a team environment, and work well with other departments, especially the product, sales and content teams</li>
