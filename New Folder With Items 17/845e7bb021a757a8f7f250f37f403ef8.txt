<h2>Business Development Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<p><b><i>self-driven, results-oriented, and fun to work with Business Development Manager who is experienced in sales.</i></b></p>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
WHAT WE’RE AFTER:
</b></h4>
<p><b>Basically, we’re looking for someone different. </b>We want a person that potential clients trust from the moment you enter the room. Someone that can find opportunities, discover challenges, and communicate solutions that will inspire action. <b><i>Ideal candidate is smart, organized, quick-thinking, good in a room, a self-starter, works well with a team, and has an understanding of process/reward.</i></b></p>
<p>
While the goal of the BDM is to generate new clients through networking, industry events, and direct marketing – <b><i>the emphasis should always be on discovering opportunities.</i></b> In addition to creating strategic sales plans, mining for potential prospects, and managing client proposals, our BDM should always be working to build relationships and maintain regular communication with contacts.</p>
</div>
<p></p>
<div>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Key Position Requirements:</b></h4>
<ul>
<li>3-5 years of sales experience in addition to the following: <i>project management, CRM, strategic planning, research, and an understanding of advertising and brand development.</i>
</li>
<li>Strong analytical abilities, both quantitative and qualitative</li>
<li>Excellent oral, written, and interpersonal skills, both internally and with a client</li>
<li>Inspired by brands and what it takes to build great ones</li>
<li>Likes to work hard and has fun doing it</li>
</ul>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Duties:</b></h4>
<ul>
<li>Prospect for potential new clients through research, networking, community involvement, attending industry events and direct sales.</li>
<li>Identify and establish contact with potential clients and the decision makers within the organization.</li>
<li>Research and build relationships with new clients, as well as maintain those that already exist.</li>
<li>Set up meetings between decision makers and company leaders and principals.</li>
<li>Work with creative team to develop proposals that speak to the clients’ needs, concerns, and objectives.</li>
<li>Handle objections by clarifying, emphasizing agreements, and working through differences to a positive conclusion.</li>
</ul>

<li>Health insurance</li>
<li>Vision insurance</li>
<li>Dental insurance</li>
<li>Unlimited vacation</li>
<li>Retirement match program for employees of one year or more</li>
<li>Relaxed office environment</li>
</ul>
