<p>
Enterprise Finance drives financial management for the company and maintains and enhances risk and financial controls. Key functions within Enterprise Finance include finance and accounting; Treasury; corporate development, mergers, and acquisitions; Data Management and Insights, the Customer Remediation Center of Excellence, Enterprise Shared Services, Business Process Management, and Corporate Strategy. Enterprise Finance informs shareholders, regulators, taxing authorities, team members, and leaders of the company’s financial performance through earnings releases, investor meetings and conferences, and meetings with regulators and credit rating agencies, following appropriate reporting guidelines. They also maintain and enhance risk and financial controls and lead many of the company’s shared services functions including corporate properties, security, and global services.</p>
<p>
The Securities Operations &amp; Accounting group (SOA) in Enterprise Finance is responsible for the settlement, clearing, custody, accounting and regulatory reporting for securities within the available-for-sale and held-to-maturity investment portfolios, as well as the Wells Fargo funding products (debt) and equities. SOA is responsible for the maintenance, control and data integrity of the related accounting systems of record.</p>
<p>
This Middle Office role will primarily support Wells Fargo debt issuance funding desk. This group issues and manages Wells Fargo third party debt and funding of all Wells Fargo subsidiaries. Debt can be issued in a variety of currencies and the interest rate and Fx may be managed with derivatives. The ideal candidate will have a solid understanding of the debt markets, US and international settlements, banking rules, and experience with derivatives. This team member will provide operational support for trade entry, settlement, general ledger, and cash reconcilement. This team member will work closely with stakeholders and supervisor to support the trade processing and foreign/domestic cash flows. This role will be have primary responsibility for coordination, cash mapping and funding flows to the ultimate Wells Fargo subsidiary fund user. This team member will also be required to perform additional duties in support of the funding team as it pertains to system and workflow enhancements and projects.</p>
<p>
This role requires knowledge of debt issuance, pricing protocol, USD and FX cash operations</p>
