<p><b>
About Datacubed Heatlh:</b></p>
<p>
Datacubed Health is a pioneering technology company making better science and healthier communities a reality. We apply individualized solutions for the capture of data, including smartphone apps, wearable, in-home, and environmental sensors. We specialize in remote engagement with patients and virtual clinical studies. Focusing on healthcare and life sciences, Datacubed Health offers software and services based on behavioral science to improve patient retention and compliance, resulting in better data and positive health outcomes. We believe that the best way to unlock the insights that have eluded scientists is understanding the holistic life of an individual.</p>
<p><b>
Key Duties and Job Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<p>Datacubed Health is seeking a Director of Business Development to engage pharmaceutical industry leaders, to build awareness, and to lead efforts to secure licensing contracts. This is a senior role within Datacubed’s Commercial division. Duties include duties and responsibilities that are typically associated with such positions as further summarized below:</p>
<ul>
<li>BUILDING RELATIONS:
<ul>
<li>Build and nurture strong relationships with senior executives within an R&amp;D function of companies responsible for clinical, RWE, bioinformatics and/or data sciences</li>
<li>Establish CRO partnerships that drive significant contributions to Datacubed revenue and customer growth</li>
</ul> </li>
<li>DRIVE AND CLOSE PIPELINE:
<ul>
<li>Collaborate with Datacubed’s C-level executives on evaluating new initiatives, opportunities and deals for strategic alignment</li>
<li>Present product solutions overview</li>
<li>Drive the deal process from initial prospecting, pipeline development, use-case discussions, technical review, proposal creation, contract drafting, negotiation, and implementation &amp; post-sales satisfaction</li>
<li>Understand key endpoints and decision criteria within a clinical study protocol</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Given the civic mission of the company, it is especially important that employees uphold the business’ principles honestly and ethically; strive to improve the quality of our services, products, and operations and maintain a reputation for privacy, honesty, fairness, respect, responsibility, integrity, philanthropy, trust, innovation, exploration and sound judgment; and adhere to high standards of business and personal integrity as a representation of our business practice.</p>

<li>Strong portfolio of previous professional work with clear understanding of your role and contributions on each project</li>
<li>PaaS (or similar “as-a-Service”) experience</li>

<li>Strong interpersonal skills; especially, self-confidence, critical thinking and problem solving, time management, and an ability persevere through deal cycles &amp; ongoing relationship &amp; trust building</li>
<li>Creativity to brainstorm &amp; discuss new use-cases for data in any industry vertical</li>
<li>Excellent English writing, speaking &amp; presentation skills</li>

</ul>
