<h2>Lube Technician</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>POSITION SUMMARY:</b><br>
Performs service and installation activities associated with store products and services in an accurate and timely manner per Jiffy Lube JTSS procedures.<br>
<br>
<b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Drives vehicles with automatic and manual transmissions.</li>
<li>
Drains oil and replaces oil filter.</li>
<li>
Lubricates vehicles.</li>
<li>
Cleans windows.</li>
<li>
Inflates tires.</li>
<li>
Vacuums vehicles.</li>
<li>
Checks and fills various fluids.</li>
<li>
Checks and replaces various filters.</li>
<li>
Writes work orders.</li>
<li>
Look up information in PMM system for various applications.</li>
<li>
Explains services and products to customers.</li>
<li>
Looks up information for various applications.</li>
<li>
Operates point of sale hardware and software.</li>
<li>
Displays positive attitude at all times towards customers and fellow employees.</li>
<li>
Wear PPE equipment, including safety glasses, when required.</li>
<li>
Maintain JTTS certification as directed by Company standards.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
MARGINAL FUNCTIONS:</b> In case of short-staffing, as required</p>
<ul>
<li>
Answers telephones.</li>
<li>
Stocks shelves.</li>
<li>
Cleans facilities, tools and equipment.</li>
<li>
Greets customers.</li>
<li>
Rings out customers.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
PREREQUISITES:</b><br>
Valid driver's license; ability to reach and work in awkward postures; able to transport oneself from one<br>
work level to another; able to move materials from one height to another; able to read and write; good verbal communication skills.</p>
<p>
This position description in no way states or implies that these are the only duties/functions to be performed by the incumbent. Employees will be required to follow any other job-related instructions and to perform any other job-related duties/functions by their supervisor. Najjar Lube Centers, Inc. may change any of the above duties at any time, without notice.<br>
