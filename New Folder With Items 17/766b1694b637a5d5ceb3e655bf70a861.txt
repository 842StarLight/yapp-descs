<h2>Customer Service Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>As a Customer Service Associate, your patience, good listening skills, attention to detail and overall positive presence are at the core of your character—and the foundation for providing an outstanding customer experience. You’re our number one ambassador. With an undivided focus on the customer, you offer support every step of the way, from being a helpful voice on the phone to taking orders and helping to resolve customer service issues with ease. Armed with a deep knowledge of current merchandise and promotions, you are confident in answering any question that may arise. Behind the scenes, you demonstrate your knack for organization and efficiency, managing office activities and procedures under the direction and mentorship of the Team Leader, Customer Service.</div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Maximize company sales growth and profitability by assisting the Team Leader, Customer Service with all office activities and procedures in order to deliver an engaged experience to every customer, every time.</li>
<li>
Demonstrate company standards in selling, customer service, and teamwork.</li>
<li>
Maintain an awareness of all product information, intranet communications, current advertising, promotions and catalogues, and the status of merchandise.</li>
<li>
Maintain an awareness of all available resources, including customer service standards and guidelines, as well as additional furniture resources, as applicable.</li>
<li>
Complete and process phone sales orders and book all deliveries, both local and long distance.</li>
<li>
Coordinate with Distribution Centers and vendors regarding return and repair authorization and chargebacks.</li>
<li>
Process all customer correspondence and returns and credits, including UPS returns and breakage cards.</li>
<li>
Answer all telephone calls immediately and courteously.</li>
<li>
Perform all follow up communication with the customer, and respond to customers within the time frame promised.</li>
<li>
Support and model excellent service by exhibiting a positive attitude and enthusiasm toward the job and company to both internal and external customers in all forms of communication.</li>
<li>
Communicate on a regular basis with the Assistant Store Manager, Sales or Team Leader, Customer Service concerning all aspects of customer service issues.</li>
<li>
Communicate effectively and efficiently with all company associates in both written and verbal form.</li>
<li>
Actively participate in weekly customer service meetings, store meetings and training sessions.</li>
<li>
Support the sales team by assisting on the sales floor as directed by the Assistant Store Manager, Sales.</li>
<li>
Support sales team by assisting with all aspects of the sales process, including booking/rebooking deliveries, following up on estimated time of arrival (ETAs), backorders, transfers and all customer service issues.</li>
<li>
Identify and coach sales associates on appropriate internal and receipt notes, sales skills and recovery options.</li>
<li>
Participate in ongoing training regarding technology upgrades and new systems.</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
