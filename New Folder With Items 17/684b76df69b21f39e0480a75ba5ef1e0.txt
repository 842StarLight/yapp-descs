<p></p>
<p>A Quality Specialist plays a vital role in the consumer buying experience by preserving the quality and fresh taste of draft beer through timely line cleaning service, prompt response to service calls, and backup support for keg delivery and equipment installation. This role is part of the On Premise Sales team.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>WORK TASKS
</b></p>
<p>A Quality Specialist can be expected to perform the following tasks in this role as a routine part of their job:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Services all draft accounts and performs scheduled maintenance to include cleaning of beer lines, inspection and cleaning of draft equipment. (85%)
</li>
<li>Monitors and maintains temperature, pressure and the freshness date; maintains current records of line cleaning and service calls for each account. (10%)
</li>
</ul>
