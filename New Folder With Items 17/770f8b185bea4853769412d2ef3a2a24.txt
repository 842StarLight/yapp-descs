<h2>Payment Services Administrator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Incumbent serves as the Payment Services Administrator in Disbursements &amp; Travel -a unit of Integrated-Procure-to-Pay Solutions (IPPS) housed within Business &amp; Financial Services (BFS). This position provides front-line customer relations, the disbursement and administration of payments which accounts for over 111K payments annually. Serve as the point of contact for campus departments and suppliers needing assistance with payment status and discrepancies.
</p>
<p>Incumbent uses a variety of processing applications and a customer relationship management platform to provide rotational support across multiple sections as assigned. Serves in a lead or back fill capacity and assists in cross-training efforts of staff and students which may include the following units: 1) Payments &amp; Expense, 2) Travel and Expense, and 3) Supplier Management providing cross training as needed.</p>
<p>The incumbent practices risk monitoring activities and confidentiality in dealing with sensitive banking and personally identifiable information. Supports managers and other team members to ensure accuracy, efficiency, quality customer service, and policy compliance. Participates in special projects as needed.</p>
<p>You are encouraged to review additional information about our organization at:</p>
<p>IPPS - http://ipps.ucsd.edu/</p>
<p>UCSD - http://ucsd.edu/about/index.html</p>

<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>QUALIFICATIONS</b></h1>
<ul>
<li><p>Bachelor's Degree with a major in business administration or related field and two (2) years experience in a professional working environment with similar functions relating to customer support, payment processing, or banking; or an equivalent combination of education and experience.</p></li>
<li><p>
Demonstrated experience processing payment request documents and dealing with customers.</p></li>
<li><p>
Strong analytical and research skills. Proficient math knowledge required.
</p></li>
<li><p>Proficiency with Microsoft Office software applications including Outlook, PowerPoint, Excel, Word and Visio.
</p></li>
<li><p>Excellent interpersonal, verbal and written communication skills. Knowledge of correct English grammar, spelling, and punctuation.</p></li>
</ul>
<p><b>Preferred</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>Experience with Customer Relationship Management (CRM) Platforms (i.e., Salesforce)</p></li>
<li><p>
Knowledge of California State Board of Equalization Sales and Use Tax Regulations.</p></li>
</ul>
