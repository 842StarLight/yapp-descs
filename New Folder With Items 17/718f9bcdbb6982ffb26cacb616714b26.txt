<h2>Leasing Consultant II</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">At a lively and active multi-family, Tax Credit property, located in Loma Linda, CA, this focuses on leasing and marketing of a 215 unit Apartment Community. The Leasing Consultant’s responsibilities include, but are not limited to, renting apartments, renewing leases, administrative functions, and providing outstanding service to our customers, residents and clients while also maintaining budgeted occupancy at the highest attainable
<br>rents at all times.

Essential Functions
<br><ul>

<li>Follows up with prospects and leads to increase occupancy.</li>
<li>Promotes positive resident relations and responds to resident issues and complaints.</li>
</ul>

</ul>
