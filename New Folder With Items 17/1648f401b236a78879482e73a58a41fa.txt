<h2>Project Manager- Creative and Brand Management</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Morgan, Lewis &amp; Bockius LLP, one of the world's leading global law firms with over 4000 lawyers and staff in 31 offices, is seeking a Project Manager for our in-house Creative and Brand Management team. The Project Manager is responsible for the overall project management and resource allocation of the Creative team and creative projects of varying size and complexity from initiation, planning, design, and monitoring, through to completion of a project and reporting.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Using strong planning and organizational skills, the Project Manager coordinates project initiation for design and collateral materials, social and digital content, video, events and environmental branding while maintaining the firm’s style and guidelines and keeping within allotted time frames and budgets.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Candidates must be creative, responsive, self-motivated, a team player, committed to excellence in the workplace, and have unparalleled attention to detail and strong client service skills. Candidates must be a self-starter with the ability to think strategically. This position requires a very high energy level, a positive “can do” attitude, and flexibility with working hours.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities include:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Manages creative project assignments and workflows; making project assignments, setting the pace of the departments deliverables and balancing assignment of project resources.
</li>
<li>Manages the overall planning, control, status reporting and delivery of projects from start to completion to ensure that project goals and objectives are met within the agreed upon time, scope, budget and resource requirements.
</li>
<li>Maintains an accurate and up-to-date record of all projects and their status using appropriate technology.
</li>
<li>Determines risk and contingency planning for projects.
</li>
<li>Analyzes work plans to identify issues and barriers.
</li>
<li>Communicates consistently with project stakeholders and executives as appropriate, ensuring deliverables are being met by all team members at all stages of the project.
</li>
<li>Identifies all assets and resources necessary to successfully meet client expectations and develops and maintains all project deliverables including project charters, budgets, project plans, issues logs and change orders.
</li>
<li>Leads internal project team meetings and client status meetings.
</li>
<li>Ensures that all project documentation is produced in the standard format and is approved prior to delivery.
</li>
<li>Ensures issues requiring escalation are routed to the appropriate contact and resolved.
</li>
<li>Advises clients and senior management/executives on devising solutions for specific project issues and problems.
</li>
<li>Implements, maintains, and manages creative asset management system.
</li>
<li>Provides design support as needed.
</li>
<li>Develops quarterly forecasting reports.
</li>
<li>Conducts post-mortem reviews on key projects.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Experience and Qualifications:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Must possess a Bachelor's degree (Fine Arts, Graphic Arts or a related field).
</li>
<li>Demonstrated working knowledge of fundamentals with project management approaches, tools and methodologies. PMP certification preferred or in progress
</li>
<li>Minimum of five years of relevant, directly related professional experience. Equivalent combinations of education and experience will be considered.
</li>
<li>Working knowledge of design, photography, video, print and/or graphic production workflow.
</li>
<li>Strong leadership skills with the ability to influence effectively without direct authority, balancing between competing priorities while remaining flexible and creative.
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Proven ability to identify and resolve issues, manage risk, develop detailed work plans and specifications, perform resource allocation and run team meetings.
</li>
<li>Ability to find optimizations to process and deliverables that drive quicker turn-around times while upholding creative quality.
</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal and negotiation skills, interacting with senior executives and creative teams alike.
</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to prioritize and balance competing demands and meet deadlines in a fast-paced professional environment.
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Quick and flexible problem solver, estimating design &amp; production project time and schedules.
</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills including basic editing skills.
</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal skills, leadership skills and the ability to work well within a team
</li>
<li>Budgeting experience.
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Ability to read, analyze, and interpret business and professional publications, prepare business correspondence, proposals and reports, effectively present information to groups of employees, and respond to questions from employees, managers, clients and vendors
</li>
<li>Advanced arithmetic skills (compute rate, ratio, percent; able to draw and interpret graphs and charts).
 </li>
<li>Ability to define problems, collect data, establish facts, and draw conclusions.
</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Law firm or other professional services experience in in-house Creative is preferred.
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Successful candidates must have a basic technical proficiency in the following:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>PC and Mac Computer Platform
</li>
<li>Document Management software including Microsoft Office (Word, PowerPoint, Excel), iManage, and Adobe Acrobat
</li>
<li>Accounting Software including Chrome River
</li>
<li>Design Software including Adobe InDesign, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Acrobat, Dreamweaver, Flash, Fireworks (optional), basic HTML, Extensis, Suitcase, and Extensis Portfolio
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>This position will reside in our Philadelphia or New York office.
</p>
