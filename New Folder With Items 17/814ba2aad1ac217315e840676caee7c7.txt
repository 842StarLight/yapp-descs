<div>
The Upwork Sales team brings our flexible workforce solutions to mid-market and large enterprises to help them adapt to the changing workforce. As a Business Development Representative, you will prospect for new business by phone and email, qualify existing leads, and book sales meetings for the Account Executive teams. You would work closely with a group of lead gen specialists, sales account executives (AEs), and marketers. This position offers advancement opportunities and is an excellent way to build a career in sales.</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Your Responsibilities:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Make outbound calls to connect with marketing-generated inbound leads</li>
<li>
Identify Enterprise opportunities within current book of business</li>
<li>
Be responsible for monthly critical metrics to drive quarterly and yearly business objectives</li>
<li>
Provide weekly forecasts to your sales leadership</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What it takes to catch our eye:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
You have 1-3 year of sales, human resources, or staffing experience</li>
<li>
You have successfully owned and grown an account base in a revenue quota carrying role</li>
<li>
You are an excellent communicator, writer, and articulate over the phone</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>How to really knock our socks off:</b></h3>

Knowledge of the Upwork platforms</li>

A quick dialing finger, experience with cold calling</li>
<li>
A strategic &amp; creative mindset</li>
<li>
Proficiency with CRM &amp; prospecting tools, e.g. SFDC, Salesloft, Zoominfo, and LinkedIn Sales Navigator</li>
<li>

</li>
