<h2>Recruiter</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>LEAP leads the world in the development and sale of cloud based legal practice management software for small law firms on a Software as a Service (SaaS) model. We employ more than 300 people, have offices in the USA, Australia and the UK with more than 12,000 law firms using our software worldwide. We are currently recruiting for a Recruiter to join our highly motivated, high velocity and growing team around the world, and based in Jersey City. LEAP will provide you with the best training, equipment, mentoring and support in order for you to be successful.
</p>
<p>We employ people who are smart and can get things done.</p>
<p><b>Job Specification</b>
</p>
<p>The purpose of this role is to create the strongest workforce by attracting the highest calibre, missionally aligned talent and ensuring that all applicants have a world class candidate experience. This is a new position within the business as high scale growth is anticipated. Working with our Director, People &amp; Culture this role is responsible for full life-cycle recruiting to source, select and hire top talent for LEAP in our New Jersey, Boston and NYC offices, and reviewing the market for expansion in further US states. In addition, the role is responsible for establishing and maintaining effective recruitment metrics/key performance indicators, processes, systems and tools. The position models our LEAP values, professionalism and discretion and champions energy, innovation, attitude and excellence in everything that we do here.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b>
</p>
<p>Full Cycle Recruiting</p>
<ul>
<li>Owning the full cycle recruiting function to maintain a continuous pipeline of excellent candidates in accordance with overall recruitment forecasting</li>
<li>Devise sourcing strategies to capture the brightest wave of LEAPsters, (staffing plans and event strategies) and improve efficiency of the recruitment and selection process</li>
<li>Source, screen, interview, refer applicants to hiring managers and partner with hiring manager to ensure all steps of selection process are completed</li>
<li>Post internal and external jobs and maintain information in our ATS and on our website to ensure quality, effectiveness and meet EEO standards</li>
<li>Perform all recruitment responsibilities in accordance with LEAP values (and keeping within employment legislation)</li>
<li>Collaborate with Global HR teams throughout recruitment process to attain staffing goals</li>
<li>Partner and manage recruitment agencies and other vendors associated with recruitment; ensure vendor contracts are in place and approved by appropriate parties</li>
</ul>
<p>Recruitment Process and Record Keeping</p>
<ul>
<li>Schedule interviews with candidates and internal employees; keeping all parties informed of scheduled interviews and all pertinent information</li>
<li>Ensure timely completion of offer, promotion, not qualified and transfer letters, with support from the local HR Team</li>
<li>Ensure entry and maintenance of candidates through appropriate hiring stages within applicant tracking system and review of resumes and placement in the appropriate file</li>
<li>Maintain confidentiality of all applicant information</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>2+ years of solid recruiting experience</li>
<li>Demonstrates an ability to take action and responsibility and works well with little supervision</li>
<li>Develops good working relationships with all key parties and is service oriented</li>
<li>Obsessed with getting stuff done to an excellent standard
</li>
<li>Works brilliantly in a team, but also responsible enough to self-manage</li>
<li>Has superb problem-solving skills – can gather and analyse data, recommend solutions and follow through on all projects to the LEAP standard of excellence. Thinks BIG</li>
<li>Resilient and can adapt to any situation with a responsive attitude</li>
<li>Articulate, authentic and always demonstrates excellent judgement and decision making</li>
<li>Travel between our local offices to source the best talent</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree or equivalent qualification</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Prior experience in a research capacity for candidate generation.</li>
</ul>
