<h2>Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Who we are and what we do?</b></h2>
<p>Alchemy Systems (www.alchemysystems.com) is the global leader of innovative technology solutions that help food companies engage and train their frontline workforces. Over three million food workers at 50,000 locations use Alchemy’s learning management, performance management and communications programs to drive operational excellence.</p>
<p>Alchemy was named to the Inc. 5000 list of America’s fastest growing private companies. The Austin American Statesman has named Alchemy as one of Austin’s Top Workplaces for five consecutive years. Alchemy is now part of Intertek family since it’s acquisition in August 2018. Intertek is headquartered in London with over 40,000 employees throughout the world completing Assurance, Testing, Inspection and Certification services.</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What are we looking for?</b></h2>
<p>Alchemy is seeking a highly motivated Account Manager to develop and maintain post-sale contact with medium to large customer accounts in order to facilitate positive and productive long-term relationships. The Account Manager will serve as an internal advocate and client liaison throughout the customer life cycle. Accounts Managers are ultimately responsible for ongoing product success and adoption in all assigned accounts.</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What you’ll do:</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Identify opportunities to cultivate organic sales within assigned accounts</li>
<li>Successfully manage client relationship at executive levels</li>
<li>Assist with the implementation of Alchemy products and services</li>
<li>Manage client satisfaction with the services and products</li>
<li>Handle escalated customer concerns and account issues</li>
<li>Establish a deep knowledge of an organization’s business goals and objectives</li>
<li>Leverage industry knowledge, applications, and product knowledge in order to find and propose business opportunities and client solutions</li>
<li>Acquire industry knowledge related to general trends, emerging technologies &amp; competitors</li>
<li>Partner with internal resources to proactively identify opportunities or issues that drive customer retention</li>
<li>Ability to prioritize and manage key accounts with attention to detail</li>
<li>Accurately forecast upsell and renewal business</li>
<li>Travel independently throughout the Americas, and occasionally to other international locations</li>
</ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What you bring to the table:</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree (B. A.) or three to five years related experience and/or training; or equivalent combination of education and experience</li>
<li>Strong analytical skills and data-driven thinking</li>
<li>Ability to build and maintain strong relationship with large and small organizations</li>
<li>Ability to work autonomously under pressure</li>
<li>Strong written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Bilingual (Spanish/English) a plus</li>
<li>Understanding of food industry processes and SaaS a plus</li>
<li>B2B Sales and Leadership experience a plus</li>
</ul>
