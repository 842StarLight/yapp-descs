<p><b>Job Description<br>

<p></p>
<p><b>Title: Machine Controls Designer / Drafter AutoCAD<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Company: I-Tech LLC</b>- An assembly builder of electrical control panels which provides<br>
modifications, assemblies, panel building and other services needed to support the<br>
parent company Tri-Phase Automation. A growing company that focuses on innovative<br>
solutions to help our partners solve difficult application problems
</p>
<p><b>Location: </b>Tri-Phase Automation Headquarters – 1251 East Wisconsin Avenue, Pewaukee, WI
</p>
<p><b>Compensation structure: </b>Salary will be commensurate with experience
</p>
<p><b>Benefits: </b>401k (w/company matching, Health/LTD insurance, Vacation/Sick days available to full time<br>
employees
</p>
<p><b>Full/Part Time: </b>Full Time<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>General Description: </b>We are searching for a hard-working, extremely detail- oriented person to join our innovative team. This<br>
individual must be willing to follow through on a design, from concept through manufacturing. As a controls designer you will work<br>
closely with our sales engineers and design team to design and modify drawings per customer specifications to be utilized directly on<br>
the assembly floor.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities Include:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Perform adjustments and develop designs in AutoCAD</li>
<li>Evaluation of components needed and cross over to current vendors</li>
<li>Support labor quoting and auditing of assemblies</li>
<li>Processing new orders<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications</b>: A person applying for this position should have the following capabilities/experience:<b>
</b></p>
<ul><li>A high school diploma or more<b>
</b>
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Basic knowledge of various software ie. Microsoft Suite, Adobe Acrobat, etc….<b>
</b>
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must have a firm understanding of electrical schematics and the basic workings of PLC’s, Servo’s, VFD’s, etc.<b>
</b>
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Well versed in using AutoCad…. creating assemblies in a 3D format<b>
</b>
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Willing to work with manufacturing to improve design and/or efficiencies<b>
</b>
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experience in manufacturing (wiring control panels, machine design or assembly) a plus
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Conscientious and attention to detail
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Excellent communication skills and the ability to work well in a collaborative environment
</li></ul>
