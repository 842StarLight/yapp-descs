<h2>Manager, Small Business Sales</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">The customer journey starts with a question. And consumers expect answers. Yext puts businesses in control of their facts online with brand-verified answers in search. By serving accurate, consistent, brand-verified answers to consumer questions, Yext delivers authoritative information straight from the source — the business itself — no matter where or how customers are searching. Taco Bell, Marriott, Jaguar Land Rover, and businesses around the globe use the Yext platform to capture consumer intent and drive digital discovery, engagement, and revenue — all from a single source of truth. Yext's mission is to provide perfect answers everywhere.
<br><br>
The rapidly growing Small and Medium sized Businesses (SMB) Division is a team responsible for growing Yext's revenues. Yext is seeking a Regional Manager - West Coast (RM) for our SMB Division who can help us continue to deliver best in class performance and availability to clients across the Western United States. The role will be responsible for managing a team of sales reps generating revenue by closing sales directly with clients and working with the account management team to secure renewals and upgrades, mainly via telesales contact.
<br><br>
Responsibilities
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Build, oversee and help drive a team of sales representatives that maximize sales revenues, attain corporate objectives, and exceed sales goals</li>
<li>Provide coaching, advice, support, motivation or information to help individuals meet and ideally exceed their defined sales objectives</li>
<li>Work cross functionally with Account Management and Business Development Resources to drive new and incremental business, while retaining existing business</li>
<li>Forecast annual, quarterly, and monthly sales revenue streams accurately</li>
<li>Identify, develop, and implement effective sales plans and strategies</li>
<li>Develop specific revenue plans for sales teams as needed to ensure growth in all products/services targeted by the organization.</li>
<li>Train and coach sales representatives to structure deals that exceed revenue expectations</li>
<li>Serve as escalation point for issues beyond team authority; resolve team conflicts as necessary</li>
<li>Perform sales activities to achieve or exceed assigned revenue objectives</li>
<li>Develop and test new sales strategies and tactics, replicating those that are most successful</li>
<li>Keep abreast of and disseminate new product and industry knowledge to the team</li>
<li>Maintain open communications and collaborate with cross-functional partners, including Client Services, Product Management, Consulting and Marketing to ensure timely and appropriate client solutions</li>
<li>Work closely with Product Marketing on go-to market strategies and see them through to successful completion</li>
<li>Participate in and run sales team meetings, sales management meetings and quarterly business reviews</li>
<li>Perform other duties as needed/assigned</li>
</ul>
Minimum Requirements
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>BA/BS degree or similar college level education. In lieu of degree, relevant skills or equivalent experience</li>
<li>4+ years of experience exceeding quota as an individual contributor and/or leader in field sales</li>
<li>Strong sales, recruiting, leadership, organization, planning and prioritization skills</li>
<li>Results oriented with multiple years of meeting or exceeding quota</li>
<li>Experience in bringing innovative / market defining products to market</li>
<li>Good interpersonal skills to align and foster positive working relationships across the organization (internal and external)</li>
<li>Exceptional Project Management skills</li>
<li>Ability to develop and critically analyze a sales pipeline and forecast</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Highly motivated and flexible individual</li>
<li>Ability to travel</li>
</ul>
Preferred Requirements
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>1+ years of demonstrated success leading and managing a field sales team and exceeding team quotas</li>
<li>Experience in a marketing performance environment with a solid understanding of SEO and SEM</li>
<li>SaaS, Local, Display and/or Search market experience</li>
</ul>
