<h2>IT Product Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Our ideal candidate will have a strong technical / IT background, interpersonal skills, and a passion for teaching and guiding our customers to success.</p>
<p>Liongard is a fast-paced tech startup based in Houston, Texas. We built Roar, a software automation platform, and it’s changing the way that IT service companies manage their customers. Our team and customer base are both growing rapidly, and we’re looking for a seasoned professional and A-player to join us as a Product Specialist.</p>
<p>Our platform interacts with uniquely configured IT stacks which are inherently varied and complex. Our customers are information technology professionals, and they rely on Liongard to train them on deploying their Roar platform. Your role requires working directly with our Product Educators, Account Managers and Product Onboarding team members. As a Product Specialist, you will be a part of the Product Education team which is responsible for teaching partners and our internal staff.</p>
<p>This position requires a strong ability to listen and learn, an information technology background, high attention to customer success, and excellent customer education skills.</p>
<p>You'll encounter varied partner environments, expertise, and understanding of our platform.</p>
<p>You’ll be responsible for stepping in to interact with partners post onboarding to develop, present, and deliver material that educates them on how and why the platform can improve their business.</p>
<p>This is a full-time position in Houston, Texas.</p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul><li>Have at least 2 years’ experience teaching users on how to adopt and implement software.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Quickly gain an understanding of our SaaS software product, the key components, and how our users interact with it.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Understand the partner journey by monitoring the health of the relationship to improve engagement, satisfaction, and retention.</li></ul>
<ul><li>You will need experience in leveraging tools, techniques, processes and procedures to guide and teach partners on the value and best practice use cases of the product.</li></ul>
<ul><li>You must be excellent at writing and contributing to the customer documentation, creating video-walkthroughs, and providing content for internal and external knowledge bases.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Provide feedback and assist in the development of educational content to improve the one to many onboarding processes.</li></ul>
<ul><li>You will need to be comfortable teaching partners in one to many online video webinars</li></ul>
<ul><li>You will need to be comfortable presenting education in 1:1 calls and video calls</li></ul>
<ul><li>You will work alongside Account Managers, as their technical counterpart, to drive strong healthy partner relationships and ensure customer success.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Serve as an escalation point for partners with concerns about support &amp; onboarding.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Review, help create, and be accountable to Liongard’s success metrics and KPI’s, in order to maintain the standards of the company’s goals and allocate resources as needed.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Effectively communicate with partners and colleagues through e-mail, Slack, and verbally to make sure that problems are visible to the rest of our team when that need arises.</li></ul>
