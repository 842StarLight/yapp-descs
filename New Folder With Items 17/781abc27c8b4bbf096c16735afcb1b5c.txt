What you'll be doing?
<br><br>
Fieldwire is growing fast, and we'll need you to help continue to fuel it by building and leading our growth marketing team. We'll be counting on you to continue to strengthen our current lead funnel and to further untap growth by activating new channels and campaigns.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Build the team and infrastructure to drive a growing number of qualified leads to Fieldwire's sales team.</li>
<li>Drive the strategy and execution for demand generation campaigns across all channels, including paid, organic, email, radio, and print.</li>
<li>Unite the Sales and Marketing team to conceptualize and execute account-based marketing initiatives.</li>
<li>Develop a culture of experimentation by making testing a standard practice of all growth efforts at Fieldwire.</li>
<li>Maintain a feedback loop to continuously optimize the marketing-to-sales hand-off process.</li>
<li>Effectively communicate and evangelize demand gen's strategy and performance to founders.</li>
</ul>
