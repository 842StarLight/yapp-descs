<p>Lucrative uncapped plan - $100,000+ potential year 1</p>
<p>
Are you searching for an organization that will hold up their end of the bargain when you hold up your end of the performance plan? Are you looking for a compensation plan that's tied directly to your hunger, efforts and results? Do you love the thrill of the hunt? How about selling services that customers truly need and appreciate? Are you committed to a proven sales methodology (i.e. Sandler, Miller Heiman, etc)? <p>
R.J. O’Neil is looking for an Account Executive that will sell preventative maintenance plans and service projects to commercial and industrial accounts. We seek someone who is accountable for the entire pipeline without territory restrictions. Are you hungry to join a team that offers coaching, feedback and support to help you achieve your goals? If this sounds like you, and you thrive in a fast-paced, collaborative team environment, then let's talk.</p>

<li>

<li>

<ul>

<li>Sell profitable preventative maintenance plans and service projects that meet our customers' needs.</li>
<li>Build, develop and maintain positive business relationships.</li>
<li>Represent RJ O'Neil at industry events, networking events and in our community.</li>
<li>Continuously improve through feedback and coaching.</li>
<li>Fully leverage our internal systems to accurately report on progress and results.</li>
<li>Participate in project kickoffs and retrospectives to improve our project and sales processes.</li>
</ul>
<p>What you bring to the table:</p>
<ul>

<p>About Us:</p>
<p>
R.J. O’Neil is a full-service mechanical contracting firm. We build long-term relationships with our commercial and industrial customers by offering custom, comprehensive preventative maintenance programs and complete mechanical services, including system design and layout, installation and emergency repair. We specialize in process piping, heating, air conditioning, plumbing and refrigeration. We are signatory with Plumbers Local 130, Pipefitters Local 597 and Sheet Metal Local 265. We’ve proudly served the Chicagoland area since 1926.</p>
