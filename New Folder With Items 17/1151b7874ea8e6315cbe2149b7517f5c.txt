<h2>Customer Service Team Lead</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">LOCALiQ is the marketing solution from the USA TODAY NETWORK providing businesses the smarter way to reach local customers. As local marketing becomes more complex, LOCALiQ empowers local businesses with unique insights from 125 million consumers in our network of sites, apps and experiences and learnings from millions of local leads delivered each year. We couple that with end to end service from our experts using local marketing best practices proven across the country. LOCALiQ simplifies local marketing and provides our clients the confidence of growing.
<br><br>
<b>Our core values:</b> Community, Progress through Passion, Action with Purpose and Belief in People. If you share these values, come join our family!
<br><br>
The Customer Service Team Lead role requires a highly personable, motivated individual with the ability for directing the behaviors of his or her team. They will create and maintain open communication and lead by example. The Team Lead will forge solid relationships and empower team members, laying the foundation for a high performing team.
<br><br>
We are dedicated to helping our clients grow based on their unique needs. When they win, we win!
<br><br>
As a Marketing Solutions, Customer Service Team Lead, you will:
<br><ul>
<li>Assist in answering calls and providing outstanding service to customers looking to place advertisements, upsell into multiple newspapers in multiple states as well as maximizing the look of each ad to improve the ads reach.</li>
<li>Coach and develop team members in all aspects of performance.</li>
<li>Handle elevated phone calls.</li>
<li>Draft and help conduct performance evaluations, may assist in the employment decision, set performance goals and objectives.</li>
<li>Communicate policy updates through team meetings.</li>
<li>Document all training/coaching sessions.</li>
<li>Interact with other teams within the department to support and help manage the day to day operations.</li>
<li>Additional administrative duties as needed.</li>
<li>Support Sales Team and overall Department in meeting annual revenue goals.</li>
<li>Effectively prioritize tasks and accomplish responsibilities to product quality results in a deadline driven environment.</li>
</ul>
<b>What you bring to the table:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Sales ability with a passion for the selling of print and digital products.</li>
<li>Proven leadership skills to effectively develop, lead, inspire and motivate.</li>
<li>Work cooperatively and collaboratively with all levels of employees, management and customers to maximize performance, creativity, problem solving and results.</li>
<li>Background in advertising preferred; not required.</li>
<li>Desire to learn and understand both the print and online advertising industry.</li>
<li>Outstanding customer /client focus.</li>
<li>Strong work ethic and ability to multi-task</li>
<li>Problem solving skills.</li>
</ul>
