<p>We create smart innovations to meet the mobility challenges of now and the future. We design and manufacture a complete range of transportation systems, from high-speed trains to electric buses, autonomous subways, signalling and digital mobility solutions. Joining us means joining a truly global community of 36,300 people dedicated to solving real-world mobility challenges and achieving international projects with sustainable local impact.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Purpose of the Job</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Manage Development Center Safety Assurance resources (SAMs and SAEs) and is accountable for general Safety Management activities associated with all Signaling Products at the Rochester site.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Signaling Product solutions are composed of Electronics, Software, and Electro-mechanical design that utilize technical safety concepts such as vital fail-safe design, and inherently safe design</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage the workload of Safety Resources and allocate the resources in accordance to DC Director and manage/approve QCD commitments for Bids to Projects and S&amp;P Development</li>
<li>Manage and lead the team in Project and Program Safety Planning.</li>
<li>Develop and Maintain Safety Resources Skills</li>
<li>Deploy Safety Process, Method and Tools in accordance to Central rules and propose improvements based on REX (Return on Experience)</li>
<li>Support the Safety Process Correspondent</li>
<li>Ensure convergence between System/Products and System/Projects for safety-related topics; escalation and arbitration will be managed between ADM Safety Director and/or ADM Platform Safety Director</li>
<li>Provide Technical Expertise to Projects for System &amp; Products Cross-Acceptance to assure</li>
</ul>
<p>Customer Safety Acceptance</p>
<ul>
<li>Review safety issues and support root cause identification and mitigation measures</li>
<li>Implement the Safety Management Cycle defined centrally by ADM Safety Director Staffing: Total Capacity versus Workload. Analysis and action plan</li>
<li>Provide Quarterly Dashboard in accordance to Central rules, and provide weekly status reports.</li>
</ul>
<p>Review/validate key Safety Analysis in their Domain. Perform Validation of the following Safety Outputs produced and verified by SAM/SAEs.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Educational</b></p>
<p>Bachelor's or Masters Degree in Engineering, Electrical, Electronic or Software</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Skills</b></p>
<ul>
<li>A proven background in Product Development or System Design microprocessor based system for highly regulated industry (Rail, Defense, Nuclear or Space…)</li>
<li>Self-starter able to work independently with a sense of urgency and network effectively to gather data from different sources.</li>
</ul>
