<h2>Creative Team Leader</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader">
<b>Summary:
</b><br>
<b>• To direct and facilitate the activities of a diverse group of Integrated Marketing Communications creative personnel providing strategic planning, creative development, execution, financial management and measurement of programs. Aspects of the marketing mix included are: advertising, promotion, and PR. The Team Leader reports to and receives work direction, support and performance evaluation from the Associate Director of Integrated Marketing Communications in Roswell.
</b><br>
<ul>
<li><b>Oversees, develops, and provides direction, work assignments and performance evaluation to the Creative team members and manages Communications planning and activities at the Enterprise level
</b></li>
<li><b>Serves as the point person to oversee, manage interaction and development of items as requested by the Marketing and Sales Team for urgent selling/communication materials needs.
</b></li>
</ul>
</h3>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Key Responsibilities
</b></h3>
<p>In a player/coach role, the Team Leader is expected to:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Champion Kimberly-Clark Professional, its platforms and its Brands in an impactful and professional manner, and drive consistent implementation of these initiatives by the Team.
</li>
<li>Oversee, manage and prioritize the creative development, production, and execution of communication materials by the team ensuring creative excellence. Team to deliver creative reviews a minimum of four times per year.
</li>
<li>Manages the creative development of the materials needed by stakeholders and ensure creative excellence and cost efficiency
</li>
<li>Manages the cloud-based server to ensure new materials are uploaded and the tool is kept up to date
</li>
<li>Possess strategic vision and sufficient understanding of the Company and the business to provide creative insights and recommendations leading to growth.
</li>
<li>Participates with the Associate Director of Integrated Marketing Communications to set the tone for department staffing needs, recruitment, on-boarding and daily coaching of team members.
</li>
<li>Monitors, evaluates and actively champions the quality of creative output of the department, including brand identity guidelines and recommends and implements methods or steps to continually improve and enhance creative team performance and work product, especially in the digital space
</li>
<li>Ensures standardized work processes are established, adhered to, and kept evergreen by team members.
</li>
<li>Ensures branding and graphic standards are being adhered to and regularly requires a brand audit by team members. Position Marketing Communications Creative Team as brand stewards in the organization
</li>
<li>Ensures career development activities occur for self and Team members.
</li>
<li>Communicates fully with superiors, subordinates, and others who have a need to know. Be informative without being obtrusive or vexatious. Communicates in a way that is timely, yet prompt; complete, yet concise; candid, yet accurate; clear, yet responsive.
</li>
<li>Contributes to an environment in which all team members are respected regardless of their individual differences and are motivated to improve both their individual and team contributions to achieve desired business results.
</li>
<li>Ensures individual actions are consistent with safety standards and corporate policies.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in Advertising, Design, Marketing, Journalism, Communications or Graphic Design
</li>
<li>10+ Years of experience in marketing/advertising industry
</li>
<li>10+ Years of experience in Developing and eliciting visual communications for a variety of media and background in marketing, graphic design or copywriting- including B2B
</li>
<li>3+ years of experience in Proven problem solving skills and generation of creative solutions that improve the way we do business
</li>
<li>5+ years of experience in Organizing and managing multiple projects with overlapping schedules and deadlines; prioritizing projects and directing work
</li>
<li>Supervisory experience of groups of three or more employees or extensive project team supervision experience
</li>
<li>Demonstrated knowledge of all aspects of the team’s core business (i.e., communications planning, development, execution, overall project management, management of creative teams and creative development).
</li>
<li>Demonstrated organizational and relationship skills, with ability to work effectively across
</li>
<li>Knowledge and understanding of graphic preparation, file management and production processes, plus experience in coordinating photography, illustration, and production resources, for both traditional and electronic/interactive applications.
</li>
<li>Design skill in Macintosh graphics and supporting software systems, including current releases of Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign, PowerPoint, Dreamweaver and coding in HTML or CSS are required. Pardot and Sitecore experience is a plus.
</li>
</ul>
