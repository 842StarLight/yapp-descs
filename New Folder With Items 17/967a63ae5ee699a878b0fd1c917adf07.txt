 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">32 hours per week-Sunday through Wednesday-$20/hr + bonus opportunity

Availability to work every Sunday through Wednesday 8:30am to 5:30pm is required.

<br><br>
<b>PURPOSE:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Provides administrative support to the Sales team.</li>
<li>Works with families to coordinate all move-in logistics.</li>
<li>Coordinates post move-in follow-up programs to ensure residents and families are satisfied.</li>
</ul>
ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Administrative Support:</li>
<li>Maintains minimum supply level of Promotional Packets and Move-In Packets using online ordering system.</li>
<li>Maintains Apartment Inventory and Readiness list current each day.</li>
<li>Provides data entry support using our internal proprietary resident information system.</li>
<li>Converts working files to permanent files.</li>
<li>Updates resident files quarterly.</li>
<li>Audits resident files quarterly for accuracy and required documentation.</li>
<li>Ensures resident files are up to date by filling all new documents weekly.</li>
<li>Pre Move-In Support:</li>
<li>Contacts family via phone or in-person immediately after sale for introduction and coordination of Move-In process.</li>
<li>Assists family and resident with all Move-In logistics</li>
<li>Performs pre-check of the apartment and follow-up on any readiness issues.</li>
<li>Post Move-In Support:</li>
<li>Greets resident and/or family on Move-In day.</li>
<li>Ensures staff awareness of new resident; notify nurses and caregivers.</li>
<li>Provides a high level of individualized customer service for resident and/or family as required.</li>
<li>Provides resident and/or family a brief orientation to the apartment and surroundings.</li>
<li>Provides next day follow-up as needed.</li>
<li>Coordinates Welcome Committee members.</li>
<li>Hosts official orientation program for new residents.</li>
<li>Updates with the family within 24-48 hours of resident move-in.</li>
<li>Provides back-up support for Community tours, including knowing the features, advantages, and benefits of the Belmont Village facility and service programs well enough to present this information and tour customers.</li>
</ul>
<b>SUPERVISORY RESPONSIBILITIES:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>None</li>
</ul>

<ul>
<li>High School diploma or equivalent</li>
<li>Minimum of 2 yrs Marketing/Sales Assistant experience</li>
<li>Proficient in written and verbal English</li>
<li>Excellent telephone skills</li>
<li>Proven organization, time management and attention to detail skills</li>
<li>Interest in working with the older population</li>
<li>Computer experience and ability to use or learn to use database software</li>
</ul>
<b>PREFERENCES:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Associates degree in Administration or Marketing or related field</li>
<li>More than 3 years of marketing/Sales Assistant experience</li>
<li>Experience working with the older population</li>
</ul>
