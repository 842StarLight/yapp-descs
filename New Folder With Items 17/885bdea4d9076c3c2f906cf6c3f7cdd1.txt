<h2>Marketing Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>REPORTS TO: General Manager and Vice President of Property Marketing</p>
<p>POSITION SUMMARY: Develop, direct and drive all marketing efforts in the property resulting in increased traffic, sales and income.</p>
<p>JOB DESCRIPTION:</p>
<p>I. To develop an annual marketing strategy consistent with annual budget.<br>II. To execute marketing programs that drive traffic and sales to the center and tenants.<br>III. To operate within local budget guidelines.<br>IV. Develop sponsorship and event opportunities to increase income.<br>V. Work in conjunction with advertising agency to develop creative campaigns for all marketing programs, collateral pieces, advertising, email, social media and website.<br>VI. To nurture and develop ongoing retail partnerships with tenants.<br>VII. Initiate and manage community relations and media outreach in conjunction with designated PR agency. Develop and maintain social media influencer program.<br>VIII. To strategize and capitalize on target markets specific to the property (i.e. office, college).<br>IX. Develop and maintain tourism program including relationship building (i.e. area hotels, group tour operators, area attractions), tourism advertising, traveling to conferences, etc.<br>X. Maintain website and email program.<br>XI. Maintain and grow social media program. Develop and post content/campaigns.<br>XII. To develop signature programs geared to target markets which are highly visible and generate large traffic and sales increases.<br>XIII. To oversee the marketing functions of Marketing Assistant and delegate related responsibilities.<br>XIV. Supervise internship program and delegate responsibilities.<br>XV. Manager on Duty – Supervise all property functions on assigned dates and holiday periods. This position requires flexibility in working hours.</p>
<p>QUALIFICATIONS:</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree with concentration in marketing, communications or business.</li>
<li>5+ years of experience in marketing, advertising, sales, sponsorship, event management and budgeting.</li>
<li>Knowledge of retail management, marketing, advertising and media.</li>
<li>Tourism and event planning experience.</li>
<li>Shopping center or retail experience preferred, but not required.</li>
<li>High degree of attention to detail</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task and work in a team environment</li>
<li>Working with outside marketing vendors a plus (i.e. advertising agencies &amp; PR groups)</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
</div>
