<h2>Consumer Care Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>JOB DESCRIPTION: </b> <b>Consumer Care Specialist</b></p>
<p>The Fulham Group is small and fast-growing consumer products manufacturer based in Newton, MA focused on designing, developing and marketing quality products in the consumer home and lifestyle industries. The company has an exclusive license with <b>Cuisinart®</b> for grilling accessories and outdoor grill and cooking appliances.</p>
<p>The Fulham Group is a seeking a motivated and customer service minded individual to play a supporting role in the Consumer Care department. The ideal candidate should have previous call center, customer service and/or retail experience along with strong interpersonal skills. Providing a high level of consumer service to create positive, long lasting relationships with consumers is a top priority for this position.</p>
<p>Strong verbal communication, organizational and problem-solving skills are needed to support our fast-paced growth environment.<br>The right individual understands the challenges and excitement of working in a small company and will have the ability to multi-task while being part of a dynamic team. This position reports to the Consumer Care Manager.</p>
<p><b>RESPONSIBILITIES: </b><br>Consumer Care Specialist (80%)</p>
<ul>
<li>Assist customers with product and technical issues via phone and email</li>
<li>Pack and ship spare parts and replacement products as needed</li>
<li>Maintain and update consumer inquiries via Fresh Desk online portal.</li>
<li>Help report on customer service issues to product teams through quarterly reviews</li>
<li>Manage spare part inventory and request replenishments as needed</li>
<li>Contribute to improving product manuals and trouble shooting</li>
<li>Pivotal role in helping identify and communicate any product quality issues to Engineering/Product Teams</li>
<li>Monitor online product reviews for emerging issues and respond to consumer review questions on Amazon and through</li>
</ul>
<p>Bazaar Voice review portal</p>
<p>General Office Duties (20%)</p>
<ul>
<li>Help in assembling new versions of our product and competitors’ items</li>
<li>Test grills and accessories to share feedback to product teams</li>
<li>Assist office needs and other duties as required</li>
<li>Help with recycling and sample disposal – small office we all pitch in</li>
</ul>
<p><b>KNOWLEDGE &amp; SKILLS: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Strong communication and interpersonal skills, ability to be self-motivated and work within a team environment.</li>
<li>Strong problem solving and complaint resolution skills with the ability to troubleshoot on technical/functional product issues.</li>
<li>Experience with a Consumer Support Software System (Fresh Desk, Sales Force, Zen Desk, etc..) and Quick Books a plus.</li>
<li>Strong project management skills (detail oriented) with the ability to manage multiple projects and tasks.</li>
<li>Prior experience with outdoor grilling and/or kitchen electrics is preferred</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Office (Word, PowerPoint, and Excel)</li>
</ul>
<p><b>EDUCATION &amp; EXPERIENCE: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>1-3 years of previous experience in a consumer care or sales support role</li>
<li>A BS degree in Business Management, Communications and/or similar preferred</li>
<li>Growth potential for the right individual into product marketing or sales</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Salary: $35,000.00 to $40,000.00 /year</p>
