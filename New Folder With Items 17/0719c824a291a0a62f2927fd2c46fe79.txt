<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>About the job</b></h2>
<p>
At Google, we build products that organize the world's information and make it universally accessible to our users. These products are the lifeblood of who we are. As part of the Product Management team, you work closely with our engineers to guide products from conception to launch. You connect the technical and with the business, working with teams across Engineering, Sales, Marketing and Finance. With your technical expertise, you identify a project’s setback and generate solutions for resolving them quickly. As a Product Manager, you help shape the future of Google products.</p>
<p>Google One is a subscription plan that gives users more storage to use across Google Drive, Gmail, and Google Photos. It also gives users extra benefits and allows them to share their membership with their family.<br>
<br>
As a Product Manager within Google One, you will helps us build a brand new business and shape future customer experiences. You’ll engage in business and technical problems, collaborating with a wide variety of partners to define the user experience, solution capabilities, and operational processes and deliver amazingly great experiences for users worldwide.<br>
</p>
<p>
Google's mission is to organize the world's information and make it universally accessible and useful. Our Devices &amp; Services team combines the best of Google AI, Software, and Hardware to create radically helpful experiences for users. We research, design, and develop new technologies and hardware to make our user's interaction with computing faster, seamless, and more powerful. Whether finding new ways to capture and sense the world around us, advancing form factors, or improving interaction methods, the Devices &amp; Services team is making people's lives better through technology.</p>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Responsibilities</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Drive and manage product execution from ideation to launch, adoption, and growth.</li>
<li>Define requirements, initiate and prioritize projects within engineering, assist in determining the best technical implementation, track product development, and develop product launch plans.</li>
<li>Work with research teams to test hypotheses and prioritize product concepts and features.</li>
<li>Work jointly with our user experience teams to create design concepts and work with engineering to implement and iterate.</li>
<li>Influence peers and leadership, leveraging insights from various sources, including product performance metrics, research, customer support, and competitor/industry research.</li>
</ul>
