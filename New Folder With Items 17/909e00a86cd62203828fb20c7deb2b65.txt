<h2>Sales Support Specialist - Northern CA</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><b>Sales Support Specialist</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>TITAN School Solutions is seeking to hire a Sales Support Specialist to assist and support the sales team. You will partner with our Sales team in answering customer inquiries, contacting lead prospects, cold calling prospective customers, coordinating advertising, assist with trade shows and user meetings, and much more of the sale process. The Sales Support Specialist will work closely with the Outside Sales branch personnel and Account Executives.<br>
<div><b>WHERE YOU WILL BE IN ONE YEAR:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
<b>EXPANDED INTO PREVIOUSLY UNTAPPED MARKETS</b> – You will have built up a book of business that isn’t normally targeted by other competitors</li>
<li>
<b>PROVIDED PREVIOUSLY UNDERSERVED MARKETS WITH NEW TECHNOLOGY</b> – The smaller and less recognized, but equally deserving, groups will LOVE you for helping them utilize cutting edge technology to improve their lives</li>
<li>
<b>GROWN A COMPANY’S SAAS SALES EXPONENTIALLY</b> – Your contribution to an extremely fast-growing company will be recognized and greatly rewarded and your career will grow at an exponential rate</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<b>The Sales Support Specialist’s responsibilities will include:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Cold calling, email campaign, and more to prospective school districts to generate leads.</li>
<li>Have consistent follow up communications with prospective leads.</li>
<li>Contact existing customers on a regular basis for the purpose of learning customer’s satisfaction, new opportunities, problems, and maintaining accurate contact information.</li>
<li>Schedule and coordinate online and onsite presentations.</li>
<li>Receive lead inquires via phone, mail, email, trade shows, etc., and enter them into the HubSpot CRM. Forward lead inquiry to sales representative.</li>
<li>Generate quotes and ensure they are completed in an efficient, accurate and timely fashion.</li>
<li>Work with sales team to revise quotes based on new or changed specifications and requirements to provide customers the most accurate quotes and solutions for their project.</li>
<li>Respond to general inquiries from customers or leads for information (examples: computer specs, references, sole source letters, etc.)</li>
<li>Work with other departments to maintain accurate customer and prospect lists for e-blasts, mailings, marketing, HubSpot CRM updates, and more.</li>
<li>Generate sales reports requested by sales team</li>
<li>Advise sales team of any trends in the market. Engage in competitor intelligence and share results with sales team.</li>
<li>Help plan travel arrangements.</li>
<li>Perform such general assigned duties that are assigned by the sales team.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>The successful candidate must have the following experience and capabilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>2-4 years proven experience in a support role, or in other sales/customer service role.</li>
<li>Hunter-mentality to be able to build a sales pipeline as well as pursue leads by Senior Regional Representatives</li>
<li>Previous experience with HubSpot or other similar CRM tools</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Office: Excel, Word, Outlook, and PowerPoint.</li>
<li>Proficient with the use of the internet and LinkedIn for data research.</li>
<li>Strong phone and verbal communication skills along with active listening, presentation and negotiation skills</li>
<li>Have the ability to perform all duties in an independent manner with little supervision.</li>
<li>Must have valid driver’s license.</li>
<li>Ability to travel when needed.</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree preferred.</li>
</ul>
