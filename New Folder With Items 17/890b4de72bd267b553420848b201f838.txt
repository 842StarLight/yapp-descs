<h2>Strategic Advisor (Pre-Sales)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Businessolver delivers market-changing benefits administration technology supported by an intrinsic and unwavering responsiveness to client needs. Our clients trust Businessolver to take care of them and their employees with a configurable and secure SaaS platform and a culture of service, all aimed at total and measurable success and our clients' complete delight.
<br><br>
We work with some of the most recognizable brands in the U.S. We look to our rock-star employees to help these clients maximize the investment in their benefits program, minimize their exposure to risk, engage their employees with our easy-to-use solution and full suite of communication tools, and empower their employees to use their benefits wisely.
<br><br>
At Businessolver you will have opportunities for individual development through our common language: Trust through transparency. Assume positive intent. Be real. Live a growth attitude. Embrace the reverse golden rule.
<br><br>
We are seeking a highly motivated, organized and professional individual to act as a Strategic Advisor in our innovative sales process.
<br><br>
<b>The Gig:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Work with field sales representatives to generate and follow-up on leads for sales of Businessolver products/solutions</li>
<li>Work with marketing to identify sales opportunities and create sales strategies for target markets</li>
<li>Work opportunities within Salesforce</li>
<li>Conduct product/system demos via web and face to face</li>
<li>Work to support Operations through implementation and support on-going requests involving Sales Support</li>
<li>Foster and support the progression of opportunities from the beginning of the process through implementation</li>
<li>Solution and Strategize with prospects and consultants on services and product</li>
</ul>
<b>What you need to make the cut:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Competitive nature, ability to thrive in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>Excellent time management, communication and organizational skills</li>
<li>Benefits and/or technology background a plus, but will train the right candidate</li>
<li>Candidates with a bachelor's degree strongly preferred</li>
<li>Life and Health Insurance licenses a plus</li>
<li>2-4 years selling experience, phone sales preferred</li>
<li>Ability to learn new technology</li>
</ul>
