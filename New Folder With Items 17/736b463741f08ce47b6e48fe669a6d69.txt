<h2>Automotive Technician</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>This position is a mid-level Automotive Technician who provides high quality vehicle maintenance and repairs for fleet vehicles with a focus on exceptional customer satisfaction in a cost-effective manner. This Technician may work independently or in a team environment. The Automotive Technician II will report to the Area Fleet Maintenance Supervisor, Area Fleet Maintenance Manager, or the Senior Area Fleet Maintenance Manager.</div>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Job Responsibilities:</b></h4>
<div><ul>
<li>
Perform Preventive Maintenance: change oil, oil filter, fuel filter, transmission flush, and inspection of vehicle for mechanical deficiencies. Perform light repairs as required.</li>
<li>
Based upon certification levels, perform various repairs and maintenance which may include: OBD II systems, brake components, tires, tune-ups, suspension components, aerial lifts, electrical systems, State Safety Inspections, interior/exterior components, engine and drive line components, etc.</li>
<li>
May maintain/repair equipment. (cable trailers, laptop docking stations, etc.)</li>
<li>
Keep shop area clean and tidy. Perform work in accordance to safety standards. Dispose of all fluids in a safe manner and in accordance with all regulations.</li>
<li>
Create and maintain vehicle history and maintenance records. May up-fit new vehicles with necessary equipment.</li>
<li>
Performs some more complex repairs, advanced diagnostics, and some fabrication.</li>
<li>
Assures prompt repairs by interfacing and coordinating with vendors for parts and repairs. May perform roadside assistance as needed.</li>
<li>
Provides custom equipment when needed by engaging in equipment design and moderate fabrication tasks including welding.</li>
<li>
Assures availability of specialized trucks by troubleshooting and repairing complex hydraulic systems, generators and electrical systems, hybrids, and on-board computer systems</li>
</ul></div>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Qualifications:</b></h4>
<div><ul>
<li>
High School Diploma or equivalent preferred.</li>
<li>
Minimum of 3 years of vehicle service and repair experience; 1 year of customer service experience preferred.</li>
<li>
Must currently possess or be able to attain minimum of 4 A.S.E. certifications within 18 months of employment.</li>
<li>
Must continue training to maintain current A.S.E. status and to meet with technology and fleet changes as they occur.</li>
<li>
Must have own tools.</li>
<li>
Ability to drive vehicles with standard and automatic transmission.</li>
<li>
Ability to work safely in a fast paced environment.</li>
<li>
Ability to enter and exit vehicles frequently.</li>
<li>
Regularly required to stand, walk, reach, talk and hear. Frequently required to stoop, kneel, crouch, bend, squat and climb.</li>
<li>
Vision abilities required include close, distance and color vision, depth perception and ability to adjust focus.</li>
<li>
Must be detail oriented.</li>
<li>
Ability to lift and carry up to 75 pounds (tools, equipment).</li>
<li>
Must have a working knowledge of DOT and CDL requirements for medium and heavy truck applications.</li>
<li>
Must be able to obtain Class A or B Operator’s License if required.</li>
</ul></div>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Work Environment:</b></h4>
<div><ul><li>
Frequent exposure to outdoor weather conditions. Moderate noise level.</li></ul></div>
