 </b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div><b>Customer Service Representative</b></div>

<br>
<div>
<b>
The Opportunity</b><br>
</div>
<p></p>
<div>
Daikin is seeking a professional, skilled individual for our Customer Service Representative position. This position is responsible for order management by handling all the details from purchase order to shipment of product. Responsible for maintaining effective customer service for all internal and external Daikin customers by utilizing excellent, in-depth knowledge of company products and programs. If you meet the qualifications listed below, then we invite you to apply for our open position by visiting our website http://careers.daikincomfort.com, and submitting your resume.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div><i>May include:</i></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Review purchase orders for correct pricing, delivery information, ship dates, item numbers as well as terms and conditions</li>
<li>
Responsible for processing customer purchase orders in MAS 500, by verifying inventory availability, ship locations and transportation of product to shipping destinations</li>
<li>
Create order acknowledgements and pick list, release orders for shipment</li>
<li>
Monitor inventory availability, maintain backorder status</li>
<li>
Work with Sales Managers and/or Account Managers to obtain completed Discount Authorization Requests (DAR) forms</li>
<li>
Check shipping invoices for accuracy and provide any special shipping instructions</li>
<li>
Process any change orders</li>
<li>
Process re-consignments</li>
<li>
Initiate orders for replacement equipment/accessories and/or for correcting errors (shortages, wrong item shipped, etc)</li>
<li>
Provide tracking information to customers</li>
<li>
Maintain detailed files for documentation</li>
<li>
Receive, investigate and respond to all customer inquires regarding shipments, returns, product availability, and pricing</li>
<li>
Process Return Good Authorizations and Damage Claims</li>
<li>
Establish a working relationship with our customers by communicating via email and telephone</li>
<li>
Troubleshoot orders that require special handling</li>
<li>
Process warranty orders</li>
<li>
Review Back Order reports</li>
<li>
Create weekly reports</li>
<li>
Serve as a liaison with inventory, accounting, etc.</li>
<li>
Research invoicing issues, Issue Credit/Debit Memos</li>
<li>
Obtain working knowledge of Daikin products and accessories</li>
<li>
Perform additional duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
