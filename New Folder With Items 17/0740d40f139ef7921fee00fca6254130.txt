<p><b>
JOB SUMMARY:</b><br>
We are currently seeking a SFCC/Demandware Architect to work as part of our team. In this position, you will work collaboratively with the sales team, other delivery consultants, partners and sub-contractors to successfully scope and implement customer solutions.</p>
<p><b>
RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>As part of pre-sales and post-sales phases, architect and design leading ecommerce solutions that support industry leading best practices in technology, features, performance and scalability.</li>
<li>Lead teams of Implementation Consultants comprised of SFCC/Demandware, and customer resources. Provide technical direction and guidance, development oversight, and support the successful delivery of projects.</li>
<li>Responsible for the technical design of client solutions. This includes a lead role in project discovery and definition workshops, creating scope and development estimates, and documenting requirements and designs.</li>
<li>Coach and mentor customers and developers as they learn to implement on SFCC/Demandware.</li>
<li>Provide technical problem resolution to complex issues.</li>
<li>Contribute to on-going process and methodology improvements within the team.</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
<b>REQUIREMENTS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Previous Solution Architecture experience designing and implementing complex enterprise applications in B2C eCommerce, ERP, CRM, or similar.</li>
<li>The ability to quickly learn new concepts and technologies and convert them into customer solutions.</li>
<li>Direct customer facing and implementation delivery experience in a Consulting or Professional Services organization.</li>
<li>Strong documentation skills.</li>
<li>BS or BA (In Computer Science or related field preferred.</li>
<li>7+ years work experience; with 3+ years in professional services/consulting.</li>
<li>Prior experience leading and working with geographically dispersed development teams.</li>
<li>Experienced with web technologies, J2EE, Java, SOA, application servers, portal servers, middleware, ecommerce, enterprise application integration, databases, security, performance &amp; scalability.</li>
<li>Experience with SFCC/Demandware, ATG, or other e-commerce platform a plus.</li>
<br>
</ul>
