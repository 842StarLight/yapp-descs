<h2>Partner Marketing Manager, Global Distribution, Google Cloud</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Minimum qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>
BA/BS degree in a technical or marketing field or equivalent practical experience.</li>
<li>7 years of experience working in channel marketing for a technology company.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<p>Preferred qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Experience marketing with cloud computing enterprise software, virtualization, application development, containers, and developer-oriented products.</li>
<li>Experience in building creative assets and marketing content.</li>
<li>Experience in planning and executing multiple, complex marketing programs including marketing campaigns.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>About the job</b></h2>
<p>
As the Partner Marketing Manager, you’ll be interacting with key internal stakeholders and partners to develop, orchestrate, monitor, and report out on joint marketing strategy and execution for Google Cloud products and solutions. You will collaborate on high impact content and campaigns. You'll generate high-impact ideas, manage multiple projects simultaneously and drive them to completion. You will also build relationships with partners, senior leaders, sales leads, channel leads and other product and marketing groups across Google.</p>
<p>
Google Cloud helps millions of employees and organizations empower their employees, serve their customers, and build what’s next for their business — all with technology built in the cloud. Our products are engineered for security, reliability and scalability, running the full stack from infrastructure to applications to devices and hardware. And our teams are dedicated to helping our customers and developers see the benefits of our technology come to life.</p>
</div>
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Responsibilities</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Co-develop and drive joint marketing strategy with distribution partners and DMRs across Google Cloud portfolio of products and solutions.</li>
<li>Collaborate with Google Cloud marketing teams and partners to develop multi-touch demand generation campaigns (which may consist of digital marketing, webinars, direct mail, in person events, etc).</li>
<li>Develop and track key performance indicators for all MDF programs; responsible for the optimization of spend; Drive communications of metrics to key stakeholders.</li>
<li>Ensure all supporting sales training and tools (joint solution datasheets, whitepapers, sales battlecards, pitch decks, customer stories, etc.) are available to support the success of marketing campaigns.</li>
<li>Lead all to-distribution and to-DMR partner marketing communications, and support the content and strategy for thru-to channel partner communications.</li>
</ul>
