<p><b>Estimator Position Summary </b></p>
<p>The Estimator position is focused on providing timely and accurate proposals to Ore’s customer base on catalog and custom inquiries. This individual will work closely with the Project Management team to provide guidance to the client base in terms of products and solutions.</p>
<p><b>Estimator Required Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Assists in trim take-offs as requested</li>
<li>Estimates the amount of materials needed for projects</li>
<li>Assists sales department as needed with customer orders and materials lists</li>
<li>Under minimal supervision analyzing blueprints and plan designs</li>
<li>Qualifying project scope and product needs from client base inquiries</li>
<li>Working in conjunction with other teams to qualify solutions and manage custom projects</li>
<li>Balancing numerous Estimation projects simultaneously</li>
<li>Maintaining data in CRM and work files to track job details and correspondence, including archiving job information, drawings, and estimates</li>
<li>Demonstrating strong interpersonal skills for internal and external communications</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Required Skills</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to read blueprints and other technical materials</li>
<li>Ability to operate networked computer systems and specialized software application programs and to instruct others in effective use of application software. Bluebeam experience helpful.</li>
<li>Must have excellent oral communication skills and positive interpersonal skills.</li>
<li>Understanding of measurements and basic math principles.</li>
</ul>
