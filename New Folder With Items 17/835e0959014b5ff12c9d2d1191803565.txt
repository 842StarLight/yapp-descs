<h2>Solution Architect - West</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>For over 10 years, Zscaler has been disrupting and transforming the security industry. Our 100% purpose built cloud platform delivers the entire gateway security stack as a service through 150 global data centers to securely connect users to their applications, regardless of device, location, or network in over 185 countries protecting over 3,500 companies and 12M employees.</p>
<div>
We work in a fast paced, dynamic and make it happen culture. Our people are some of the brightest and passionate in the industry that thrive on being the first to solve problems. We are always looking to hire highly passionate, collaborative and humble people that want to make a difference.<br>
<br>
<b>Position:</b> Solution Architect<br>
<b>Location:</b> West Coast or South Central, US<br>
<br>
The <b>Solution Architect</b> is a director level pre and post sales role. It is responsible for educating customers on why a network centric application access approach is no longer ideal and requires a complete reimagining of how users access applications and information.<br>
<br>
This role requires a deep understanding of security and networking technology, along with the ability to understand and interpret organizational dynamics and the ability to engage and positively influence key technology and enterprise architecture decision makers.<br>
<br>
<ul><li><b>Change Mindsets</b></li></ul>
Challenge our customers to evolve their strategy and change their mindset from Network Centric, to App and User Centric. Lay the foundation for the sales teams to prove Zscaler offers the best solution in this new paradigm.<br>
<br>
<ul><li><b>Architecture Workshops</b></li></ul>
Lead transformation workshops across security, network, application, authentication, communication, and Enterprise Architecture teams to challenge, educate, design, and plan for a world where users, applications, and data can reside anywhere.<br>
<br>
<ul><li><b>High Level Design</b></li></ul>
Create 1-3 year Transformation Roadmap with the customer that maps out business objectives, technical requirements, and Zscaler technology required. Lays out the Deployment &amp; Upsell framework for the Sales team.<br>
<br>
<ul><li><b>Industry Expert</b></li></ul>
Present on stage at industry events &amp; conferences. Educate internal teams on industry trends (SD WAN, 0365, etc.) Act as an interface to the Product Management &amp; Cloud Ops teams for prioritization, planning, and architecture.<br>
<br>
<b>RESPONSIBILITIES:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>
Challenger Sale approach who can lead customer to define modern technical architecture requirements</li>
<li>
Demonstrate strong understanding of networking and security architectures and engage in technical discussions with network and security architects of large enterprises.</li>
<li>
Present both industry and Zscaler technical architecture as it relates to customers’ and prospects’ environment and technical requirements.</li>
<li>
Own the room and coordinate high-level technical workshops to gain agreement from diverse stake holders and agendas.</li>
<li>
Leverage relationships to accelerate the sales cycle.</li>
<li>
Ensure the sales and customer success organizations is fully supported and has the necessary resources to be successful.</li>
</ul>
