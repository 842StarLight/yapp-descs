<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Position Summary
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>The Sales Account Executive generates advertising revenue by calling on established agencies, cold-calling new prospects and convincing potential clients of the merits of television advertising.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>WWLP-22News, (NBC), has a full-time opening for a <i>Television Account Executive. </i>The qualified candidate will be responsible for selling comprehensive media solutions to one or more local/regional/national accounts in a geographic territory to achieve sales targets and maximize revenues. Principal Duties &amp; Responsibilities include:</div>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
 Identify, develop, and expand existing and new client relationships &amp; key accounts.</p>
<ul>
<li>
Maintain close communications at various stages in the sales cycle including account updates, weekly projections &amp; forecasting of future advertising.</li>
<li>
Conduct research on target markets and trends, and adjusts sales strategy accorgingly.</li>
<li>
Ensure clear understanding and interpretation of client needs, and recommend appropriate solutions, in order to meet requirements.</li>
<li>
Develop short and long range plans for revenue growth.</li>
<li>
Negotiate advertising rates, create oral &amp; written presentations; generate packages and monitor accounts receivables.</li>
<li>
Research, analyze and monitor competitive media for new leads.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<p>
Specialized Knowledge/Skills/Abilities:</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
 Knowledge of online/digital and interactive advertising tools in order to advise advertisers and peer advertising sales representatives on the effective use of online/digital and interactive advertisements.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
 Strong oral, problem solving, communications, writing, negotiation and presentation skills.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
 Must be well organized, display a professional demeanor, exude high energy and show attention to detail.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
 Must be a confident, flexible, multi-tasker, and be able to work independently.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
 Knowledge of Comscore ratings is helpful.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
EXPERIENCE REQUIRED: Bachelor's degree preferred with an emphasis in Business/Marketing or Communications. Experience in MS Office including Word, Excel and PowerPoint required. Experience in Marketing and advertising background preferred.</p>
