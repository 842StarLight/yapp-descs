<h2>Job 1785, Digital Sales Specialist - Park Record</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>The Park Record and Parkrecord.com are located in Park City, Utah. We are a division of Swift Communications, a multi media company that offers print and digital solutions. Swift Communications reaches over 25 million unique viewers and over 127 million page views a year on our websites. Parkrecord.com reaches more than a million unique visitors and over 5 million page views a year and more than a million unique viewers a year. We are looking for a key individual with drive and desire to help small, medium and large businesses grow by utilizing our unique digital marketing products.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Our application process is two parts. Please start here and complete the questionnaire. When complete, return to this page and click apply to complete your application.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities Include (but are not limited to):</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Generate new business by being our digital expert.</li>
<li>Work in conjunction with the Advertising Director to shape digital strategy at a property level. Also develop audience targeting throughout our Swift network and beyond to identify revenue opportunities.</li>
<li>Develop fluency and leadership in our digital space</li>
<li>Educate our internal sellers and clients on the power of our digital footprint.</li>
<li>Creatively work to bring awareness of online opportunities in synergy with our printed products</li>
<li>Utilize our digital assets to develop creative and compelling solutions for our clients.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of 2-4 years sales/ digital experience</li>
<li>Ability to exceed set revenue and customer acquisitions on a monthly, quarterly and annual basis</li>
<li>Work with agencies both locally and nationally</li>
<li>Able to generate new business</li>
<li>Confidence in pitching and closing business to senior level executives and clients</li>
<li>Exceptional communication skills</li>
<li>Ability to sell comprehensive marketing solutions</li>
<li>Work well in a face paced, deadline oriented environment</li>
<br>
</ul>
