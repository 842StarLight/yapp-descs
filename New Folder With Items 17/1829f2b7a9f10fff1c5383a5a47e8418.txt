<h2>Director, Business Development</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">The DoorDash Business Development organization is responsible for building a partner portfolio that enhances the growth, operations, and reach of our marketplace. As the liaison between external and internal stakeholders our team focuses on developing and testing new strategies for partner engagement. Our current areas of focus include (but are not limited to) software, growth marketing, new verticals (grocery, alcohol, retail) and real estate. We need a highly motivated self-starter with an engaging personality who wants the individual responsibility to create and own business relationships and the leadership qualities to build a team. The ideal candidate will have a strong ability to think strategically and analytically about business and product opportunities, demonstrated a record of executing high-value partnerships, and experience managing a team of high performing individuals.
