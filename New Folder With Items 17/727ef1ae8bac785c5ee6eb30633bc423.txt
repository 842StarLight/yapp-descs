<p>MEMBER SERVICE SPECIALIST (CALL CENTER) - Currently Dallas, Texas but Relocating to Allen in Sept 2019</p>
<p>
We are team CUTX, and we exist to deliver an unexpected experience. We were chartered in 1931 to serve our communities better than the banks. Our focus is on teaching our members to take control of their finances. To join team CUTX, you must value Caring, Understanding, Teaching and eXceeding expectations at every interaction. We empower our employees to succeed. Most of all, you must be ready to have FUN!</p>
<p>
Currently, we are seeking a Member Service Specialist (Call Center) who's primary purpose of this employee is to assist CUTX in living out its vision to deliver an unexpected experience. To achieve this goal the employee must provide outstanding service to both internal and external members while answering calls and assisting members with their requests in a high-volume contact center. A key component of this service is to identify the financial needs of the member and recommend an appropriate CUTX solution. This is an ideal position for candidates who thrive on providing exceptional customer service by phone! This position requires ability to work weekdays and Saturdays. Deliver outstanding service to both internal and external members that meets or exceeds our core values: o We are interested, not interesting. o We are empowered. o We have fun. o Deliver outstanding service to both internal and external members that meets or exceeds our creed: o Care o Understand o Teach o eXceed o Meet or exceed established sales and service goals. o Obtain and communicate effective knowledge of the Credit Union’s products and services. o Complete transactions within members’ accounts while keeping errors to a minimum. o Keep work area neat and clean. o Complete daily assignments. o Demonstrate enthusiastic support of CUTX mission, core values and long-term objectives.</p>
<p>
Performs job duties within our bylaws, regulations, Board of Directors policies, established internal and external service standards and our work procedures. A Service Specialist handles a variety of Contact Center functions. These functions include (but are not limited to):</p>
<p>
1. Answer general questions relating to CUTX products and services.</p>
<p>
2. Assist members with their requests such as balance inquiries, account research, product knowledge, etc.</p>
<p>
3. Resolve member complaints concerning handling of their accounts.</p>
<p>
4. Educate members about the features and benefits of CUTX products and services.</p>
<p>
5. Assist members in accessing and understanding mobile features such as Mobile Banking, Online Banking, Remote Check Deposit, ATMs, etc.</p>
<p>
6. Respond to member requests to update accounts, including address, phone, email changes; adding and removing joint owners and/or beneficiaries; opening or closing additional accounts; ordering debit cards, etc.</p>
<p>
7. May perform other duties as directed by the Contact Center Leadership to maintain credit union service and operations.</p>
<p>
We offer an excellent compensation and benefits package including: medical, dental, and vision insurance; life insurance and AD&amp;D; 401(K); flexible spending and health savings account; and free and discounted credit union services. If interested in applying please visit our website at: www.cutx.org .</p>
<p>
Please note that all prospective candidates are subject to a credit check. Credit, if established, must be in good standing to be eligible for hire.</p>
