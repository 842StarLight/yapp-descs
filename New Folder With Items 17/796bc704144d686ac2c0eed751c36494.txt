<h2>Manager Regional Health Information Management</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Description</b><br>
</p>
<div></div>
<div>The Manager, Regional Health Information is responsible for oversight of Health Information Management (HIM) Departments and assigned Ministry wide work stream. This position will control and coordinate activities related to the compiling, processing, and maintaining records of hospital patients in a manner consistent with medical, administrative, ethical, legal and regulatory requirements. Supervises and maintains the department with regards to financial management and supervision and competency of team members. Designs and evaluates functions, processes, and workflows of designated work stream. Manages release of information, scanning, identity management, and deficiencies in accordance with the standardization set by the Ministry Work Stream owner.<br>
</div>
