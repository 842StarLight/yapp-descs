<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>As an Inside Sales Executive you will:</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Run and manage the full sales cycle, from placing outbound sales phone calls, managing sales pipeline, to negotiating and closing out the sales process.</li>
<li>
Work to achieve monthly sales quotas and revenue goals and drive interest in SiteMinder products.</li>
<li>
Learn and utilize sales processes, maintaining set sales standards at the highest level.</li>
<li>
Serve as the initial point of contact for the SiteMinder sales department and builds SiteMinder's brand.</li>
<li>
Become knowledgeable of SiteMinder products and target market to facilitate sales efforts.</li>
<li>
Provide information to potential and existing customers in order to educate and inform new opportunities about SiteMinder products and services.</li>
<li>
Utilize CRM database (Salesforce) to track activities, update data and competitive information.</li>
</ul>
</div>

What you will bring:</b></h2>
<ul>
