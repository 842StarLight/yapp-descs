<h2>Project Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Project Coordinator</b></p>
<p>Sunpro Solar is seeking a Project Coordinator for our<b> El Paso, TX.</b> office. The Project Coordinator will be in charge of assisting our Project Managers in organizing our ongoing projects.</p>
<p>The Project Coordinator will create an exceptional customer experience while supporting all facets of the solar project. The correct candidate will be responsible for handling multiple projects in a fast-paced, high-volume environment.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities &amp; Duties:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provide friendly and professional customer support – you are the primary customer advocate.</li>
<li>Provide continuous customer interactions throughout all phases of the solar installation project.</li>
<li>Prepare and submit all documents and permits required to support the project.</li>
<li>Ensure timely collection of supporting documents.</li>
<li>Work and communicate with all company personnel, including: Management, Sales, Accounting, Field and Office staff.</li>
<li>Maintain relationships with utility companies, REP’s, municipalities, and HOA’s.</li>
<li>Ability to prepare and submit all documents required for permits, utilities, and HOA approvals.</li>
<li>Fill out/file rebate applications.</li>
<li>Create PV Watts Report.</li>
<li>Organize and schedule installations, inspections, etc.</li>
<li>Review contracts for accuracy.</li>
<li>Maintain customer job packets.</li>
<li>Update project management software with appropriate status.</li>
<li>Manage any exceptions to the process.</li>
<li>Ensure timely achievement of permission to operate.</li>
<li>Create necessary reports.</li>
<li>Follow all office policies and procedures.</li>
<li>Maintain a positive work environment.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements:</b></p>
<p>It is not required that you’ve worked in the solar industry; however, experience in a construction or engineering company is a plus.</p>
<ul>
<li>Must be confident and comfortable with heavy phone interaction with customers.</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Attention to detail and problem-solving skills</li>
<li>Must be proficient in MS Office (MS Word, Excel) and CRM software. Salesforce experience is a plus.</li>
<li>Knowledge in file management, transcription, and other administrative procedures.</li>
<li>Basic finance knowledge (companies, plans, etc.)</li>
<li>Knowledge of licensing/insurance requirements (in current state/territory).</li>
<li>4-year BA or equivalent work experience in project coordination.</li>
<li>Valid driver’s license and clean driving record</li>
<li>Must be a motivated team player with a positive attitude.</li>
<li>Excellent organizational and time management skills</li>
<li>Strong work ethic and grit.</li>
</ul>
