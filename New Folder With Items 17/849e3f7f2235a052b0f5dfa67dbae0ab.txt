<h2>Center Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>You are intelligent and like to use your brain to solve problems. You enjoy meeting challenges head on. You really like to work with friendly people and have your work noticed. You aren't sure what you are doing with your life, but you know you have plenty to offer a unique, challenging and rewarding work environment.<br>
<br>
The Center Associate delivers world-class customer service to all retail customers by receiving and processing packages for courier shipment and operating copiers, fax machines, binding equipment, laminating machines, and point-of-sale devices. The Associate expertly advises our valued customers by providing accurate information on our array of products, services, and best-value options and exhibits confidence by knowing best practices related to the industry.<br>
<br>
The ideal candidate has previous retail sales experience, strong computer and internet skills, a high school diploma or GED, a friendly and genuinely helpful demeanor, and a professional appearance. He or she must be a fast learner who makes an effort to learn all aspects of the business in the shortest possible time.</p>
<br>
