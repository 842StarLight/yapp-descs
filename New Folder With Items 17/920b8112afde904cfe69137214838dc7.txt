<div>Company Overview
</div>
<div>
<div>Grainger is a broad line, business-to-business distributor of maintenance, repair and operating (MRO) supplies and other related products and services. More than 3.2 million businesses and institutions worldwide rely on Grainger for products such as safety gloves, ladders, motors and janitorial supplies, along with services like inventory management and technical support. These customers represent a broad collection of industries including commercial, government, healthcare and manufacturing. They place orders online, on mobile devices, through sales representatives, over the phone and at local branches. Approximately 5,000 suppliers provide Grainger with more than 1.6 million products stocked in Grainger’s distribution centers and branches worldwide.</div>
</div>

<div>
Position Description
</div>
<div>
<div>This role develops and refines Grainger’s diversity recruiting program philosophy, designs, execution, and analytics. Working in partnership with Talent Acquisition, University Relations, and business leaders, develops programs and external partnership to increase diverse candidate slates and enhance Grainger’s employer brand as a great place for diverse talent to build their careers. Discovers, evaluates, and manages external partnerships by building deep relationships with service organizations, community partners, and other partners to attract talent aligned to diversity recruiting programs, deliver impact and support our diversity recruiting strategies. Partners with the Talent Acquisition and University Relations team to develop align branding and communication materials for internal and external audiences. Designs specific campaigns to drive candidate awareness and engagement. Builds toolkits and collateral materials.</div>
<ul>
<li>Manages Grainger’s strategic alliances and builds relationships with key partners. Integrates strategic alliance partnerships across enterprise-wide D&amp;I programs and regularly assesses ROI of partnerships.
</li>
<li>Partners with Talent Acquisition, University Relations, HR business partners, BRGs, and business leaders to drive adoption and grow diversity recruiting programs.</li>
<li>Regularly represents the Diversity &amp; Inclusion team at recruiting conferences, conventions, and targeted recruiting events. Partners with the Diversity, TA &amp; University Relations Events Coordinator to prepare for on-site events.
</li>
<li>Tracks progress to goals to ensure impactful results of diversity recruiting programs. Partners with the Analytics team to track diversity recruitment metrics, provide reports to track progress, evaluate investments, and identify opportunities for improvement.</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>
<div>
<div>Position Requirements:
</div>
<div>
<div>Candidates for this role will have:</div>
<ul>
<li>7+ years prior experience in recruiting and/or building and managing recruiting programs.</li>
<li>Proven track record of creating and executing diversity recruiting strategies for a global organization. Experience building processes and programs from scratch.</li>
<li>Experience building business relationships with community organizations, professional associations and diversity partnerships.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to influence and partner with business leaders to assist in the development and guide the direction for the growth of the diversity programs.</li>
<li>Ability to partner, influence, and work effectively with all levels of an organization.</li>
<li>Proven ability to think strategically, translate strategies into actionable plans, develop performance metrics and analyze results.</li>
<li>Effective communicator (verbal, written and presentation) with the ability to influence stakeholders</li>
<li>Physical requirements include ability to operate office equipment and ability to work in a fast paced environment</li>
<li>Travel up to 30%</li>
</ul>
