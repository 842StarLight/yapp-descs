<h2>Marketing Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Job Summary:</p>
<br>
<div>
<br>
This position reports directly to the Director of Marketing. The Social Media &amp; Marketing Coordinator will assist in all areas of the Marketing Department to include, but not be limited to: creating formal and creative copy, social media marketing, collateral creation and implementation, special event marketing and online advertising. The Social Media &amp; Marketing Coordinator will work in collaboration with other members of the Marketing Department, Business Development, Member Education, and Branch Operational Staff. The position has a heavy focus on social media marketing/management, analytics and graphic design.<br>
<br>
Primary Duties and Responsibilities:<br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Keep inventory of all marketing items</li>
<li>Maintain and update the company's website</li>
<li>Support credit union management and Board of Directors</li>
<li>Create, schedule and implement email campaigns to membership</li>
<li>Work creatively to establish marketing campaigns for the credit union</li>
<li>Maintain editorial calendar and write content for social media channels</li>
<li>Evaluate success of digital marketing through monthly analytical reports</li>
<li>Assist in developing short and long term objectives for social media's overall strategy</li>
<li>Assist in set up, breakdown and participation of all credit union sponsored events/activities</li>
<li>Perform marketing department and branch inventory, replenishment and mailing of marketing items</li>
<li>As needed, work with Business Development to assist with trade show set up/break down, presentations, membership events, etc.</li>
<li>Assist with the coordination of production and distribution of various communications, such as printers and mail houses, to meet the designated due date</li>
<li>Assist in the creation and distribution of all communications, including, campaigns, product enhancements, direct marketing programs, sales tools and general marketing updates</li>
<li>Other duties and responsibilities assigned</li>
</ul>
<br>
Position Specifications/Requirements<br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Strong attention to detail</li>
<li>Must be reliable and demonstrate a positive ‘can-do’ attitude</li>
<li>Good, sound, and practical judgment</li>
<li>Excellent organizational and time management skills</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal skills and customer development skills</li>
<li>Frequent local travel required. Must possess valid driver's license</li>
<li>Efficiently work under pressure to meet deadlines and complete assigned tasks</li>
<li>Attendance may be required before/after regular business hours at various events</li>
<li>Ability to work effectively and collaboratively with colleagues and diverse personalities</li>
<li>Ability to utilize individual and group instructional techniques. Must be able to effectively read, write, and listen as well as communicate verbally, electronically and in written form</li>
</ul>
<br>
Education and/or Work Experience Requirements:<br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Must be able to work under pressure and meet deadlines, while maintaining a positive attitude</li>
<li>Must have experience using Instagram, Twitter, Facebook, YouTube and video recording software for social media</li>
<li>Intermediate/Advanced computer proficiency in MS Office (Word, Excel, Outlook); Adobe Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign) and video editing software</li>
<li>Ability to work independently and to carry out assignments to completion within parameters of instructions given, prescribed routines, and standard accepted practices</li>
</ul>
