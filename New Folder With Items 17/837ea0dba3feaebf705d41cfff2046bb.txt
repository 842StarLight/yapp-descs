<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p><b>How you make our customers SMILE…</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Deliver “5-Star” experience for every customer</li>
<li>
Be the face of SmileDirectClub, explain our product, and guide customers through timelines and expectations</li>
<li>
Engage and form a genuine connection with our customers to keep them excited about their journey to a more confident smile</li>
<li>
Use cutting edge technology to create a 3D scan of customer’s teeth for our Licensed Dental Professionals to review</li>
<li>
Help our customers select the best payment plan for their budget</li>
<li>
Provide our customers with complimentary whitening, and guide them through the “do’s and don’ts”</li>
<li>
Work with your teammates to create a positive, high-energy atmosphere</li>
<li>
Additional responsibilities as needed to set your SmileShop up for success</li>
</ul>
