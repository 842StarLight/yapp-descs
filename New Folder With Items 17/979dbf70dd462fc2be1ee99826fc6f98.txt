<h2>Maintenance Purchaser</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Purchase materials, equipment, supplies and services. Supports company site by reviewing and analyzing purchase requisitions, investigating and developing sources of supply, reviews quotations, interprets purchasing procedures to departments and vendors, and serves as a liaison between the company and suppliers/vendors.</p>
<p><b>Position Requirements: </b></p>
<p>· Must have working knowledge of pc, spreadsheets and word processing software programs.</p>
<p>· Must understand the purchasing (requisition, approval, purchase order, account coding, receiving notification, account payable) cycles.</p>
<p>· Must be familiar with typical food processing equipment parts and supplies.</p>
<p>· Must become familiar with equipment specifically used in the fresh cut produce industry.</p>
<p>· Understand and be able to work with maintenance parts manuals.</p>
<p>· Be able to measure parts and materials in S.A.E and metric standards.</p>
<p>· Must have skills to deal with outside vendors &amp; others necessary to carry out purchasing function.</p>
<p>· Have knowledge of chemical used in the facility.</p>
<p>· Must be able to work in cold environment.</p>
<p><b>Safety: </b></p>
<p>· Must be knowledgeable in forklift and scissors lift safety.</p>
<p>· Must have working knowledge of basic protective equipment requirements as they apply to maintenance personnel.</p>
<p>· Must be knowledgeable in the proper use of portable fire extinguishers.</p>
<p>· Must have working knowledge of OSHA, FDA and GMP requirements as they pertain to maintenance activities at a food processing facility.</p>
<p>· Must have working knowledge of basic Protective Equipment Requirements as they apply to maintenance personnel.</p>
<p>· Must be knowledgeable in the proper use of portable fire extinguishers.</p>
<p>· Must use slip-resistant footwear due to nature of environment: Wet floors, cold and icing/debris.</p>
<p>· Must use/wear/follow PPE, and GMP compliance as needed, including but not limited to: Hair/Bear net, Boots, Clean Smock, Waterproof Rubber Gloves, Plastic sleeves, Cut resistant gloves, eye &amp; hearing protection, face shield, PPE to handle Chemicals when applicable, etc.</p>
<p>· Must be aware of potential hazards as: Paper/knife cuts, Slip/falls due to wet floors, Pinch Points/Contusions, Chemical exposure (chlorine), Back/Arms/Legs sprains.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities: </b></p>
<p>· Meets daily or as needed to order parts or clarify purchase requisitions with maintenance supervision.</p>
<p>· Use the work order system to meets daily or as needed the needs of all customers.</p>
<p>· Managed the tool crib parts/supplies needs or orders.</p>
<p>· Meets as needed to resolve maintenance budget reporting problems or any issues concerning the maintenance purchasing cycle.</p>
<p>· Must keep accurate and accessible purchase order file. Track of parts from arrival to end-use and keep customers informed of any changes.</p>
<p>· Manages receiving of items delivered to maintenance but ordered by others.</p>
<p>· Manage the procurement processes with both domestic and foreign sources as it relates materials, parts and equipment needed in the maintenance department.</p>
<p>· Identifies and selects vendors to procure goods and services based on several factors, customer demand, inventory, quality, delivery, economic order quantities and pricing.</p>
<p>· Must have knowledge of purchasing practices and procedures in the maintenance industry.</p>
<p>· Must have the skill set to prepare and analyze complex technical specifications and bids.</p>
<p>· Examine and analyze vendor sales contracts for completeness and accuracy.</p>
<p>· Serve as a leader between vendors and company site to communicate requirements and any foreseeable issues.</p>
<p>· Identify cost reductions and negotiate discounts with vendors.</p>
<p>· Research and resolve any accounts payable issues related to assigned commodities.</p>
<p>· Take daily action to meet KPI’s defined by Management.</p>
<p>· Monitor inventory for assigned commodities to optimize working capital.</p>
<p>· Participate in the review and identification of root cause analysis for all issues prohibiting the timely delivery of products to customers.</p>
<p>· Must work well in a team environment and with other cross-functional groups. Must feel comfortable working in a dynamic, team based environment where priorities are continually changing.</p>
<p>· Demonstrates consistent, dependable, on-time performance.</p>
<p>· Ability to communicate, effectively with customer, Operations, and other Team Members.</p>
<p><b>Miscellaneous: </b></p>
<p>· Able to read/write in English and communicate in Spanish.</p>
<p>· Able to work flexible hours and overtime as required by workload.</p>
<p>· Able to function in a team environment where continuous improvement is expected through communication and cooperation.</p>
<p>· Must be willing and able to contribute to and implement department programs, procedures and systems as needed.</p>
<p>· Must be able to qualify for forklift, and scissors lift driver’s license.</p>
<p><b>FOOD SAFETY / QUALITY, FOOD SECURITY: </b></p>
<ul><li>· Enforces and adheres to Good Manufacturing Practices and complies with Regulatory Requirements and SQF System.</li></ul>
<p>· Supports Foreign Object Prevention Program.</p>
<p>· Ensures compliance to product specifications.</p>
<p>· Reports food safety / quality problems to personnel with authority to initiate action.</p>
<p>· Reports concerns / issues pertaining to food security to leadership staff.</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Salary: $16.00 to $18.00 /hour</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul><li>relevant: 1 year (Required)</li></ul>
<p>Language:</p>
<ul><li>Spanish (Required)</li></ul>
