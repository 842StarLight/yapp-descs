<h2>Independent Travel Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Ever After Travel Boutique, LLC is a full service boutique travel agency serving clients nationwide. We specialize in upscale Disney destinations, Cruise Lines, All Inclusive Resorts and European vacations. We are hiring talented independent travel agent professionals to join our team. If you're excited to be part of a growing company and have a passion for travel/helping others, Ever After Travel Boutique is a great place to grow your career.</p>
<p>Responsibilities:</p>
<p>~Book air and ground transportation for clients.</p>
<p>~Research to locate the best pricing and options according to client's specifications</p>
<p>~Work to meet weekly and monthly sales/business goals as established by the company</p>
<p>~Promote tours and vacation excursions through local media and PR channels</p>
<p>~Coordinate with other business partners in the travel community to facilitate a positive experience for clients.</p>
<p>~Book hotel reservations for guests in their desired location, ensuring their specifications for their stay are met</p>
<p>~Organize and provide materials pertaining to potential locations and local tours to clients.</p>
<p>~Plan and promote accommodations and travel services</p>
<p>Qualifications:</p>
<p>~Proven experience organizing travel for business or personal use.</p>
<p>~Exceptional organization skills are essential</p>
<p>~Fluent in English, bilingual a plus.</p>
<p>~Customer service experience is preferred.</p>
<p>~Personal experience with both domestic and international travel is considered advantageous.</p>
<p>~Ability to understand clients travel goals and needs and exceed their expectations.</p>
<p>~Proficient with data entry and internet software.</p>
<p>~Experience processing payment information and working with confidential information.</p>
<p>~Ability to locate the ideal travel options for clients upon a scheduled meeting detailing their travel aspirations.</p>
<p>~Must be a self-starter, goal driven and business minded.</p>
<p>*Commission Based Position with opportunity to grow. Training included with opportunities for FAM trips. Arizona Based headquarters. Please fill out initial application to see if we are good fit! www.everaftertravelboutique.com/application</p>
