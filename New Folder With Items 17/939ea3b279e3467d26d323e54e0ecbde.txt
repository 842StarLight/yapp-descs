RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>

<li><b>
Utilize all software and texting tools and company required word tracks</b></li>

Maintain a minimum of 75% Email Capture Rate</b></li>

<li>

Oversee and participate in quality control</li>
<li>
Participate in the development and documentation of Standard Operating Procedures as appropriate</li>
</ul>

Ability to concentrate and accomplish tasks despite interruptions.</li>
