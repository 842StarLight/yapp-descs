<h2>Brand Innovation Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>WE are Self-starters , WE are Passionate , WE are Relationship builders , WE are Innovative , WE are Nimble , and WE are Transparent …WE are SPRINTers ! We believe in challenging you, then providing you the room and flexibility to deliver.</p>
<p>
Are you passionate about technology and design innovation?</p>
<p>
Reporting to the VP of Business Development, Ink Jet strategy, the Brand Innovation Manager, will be driving the education and adoption of digital printing at major brands (Consumer Product Goods, Fashion brands, etc), advertising agencies and prepress houses. The Brand Innovation Manager will work to funnel new jobs to EFI’s network of digital printing customers and develop innovative strategies to solve production and market challenges.
</p>
<p>The Brand Innovation Manager will be responsible for:</p>
<ul>
<li>Identification of key brand events, conferences, tradeshows, and represent EFI speaking at events and networking with brands.</li>
<li>Developing a robust funnel of brand key contacts covering each of EFI’s main ink jet businesses in textile, packaging and display.
</li>
<li>Work with existing EFI customers, sales people, and management to generate lists of brand prospects.</li>
<li>Present the value and capabilities of digital printing to key executives at brands, conferences and trade shows. Strong capability for public speaking.
</li>
<li>Evaluate how brands, pre-press and agencies work and the flow of packaging design to create successful strategies leveraging EFI software and hardware.</li>
<li>Work with EFI groups to deliver and support brands print trials and sample production.</li>
<li>Work internally to assist in creating content, case studies and proof points to market the success and impact digital printing has for brands.</li>
<li>Manage the database of all contacts, and activities in Salesforce. Develop KPI’s to track successful implementation.</li>
<li>Responsible for presenting updates on success to date to senior management.</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Requirements:</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree minimum
</li>
<li>Creative and independent in looking for opportunities</li>
<li>Highly motivated, influential and capable of understanding the EFI value proposition within the brand environment
</li>
<li>A rigorous and structured strategic thinking mindset</li>
<li>Comfortable with public speaking
</li>
<li>The ability to work independently as well as part of a team</li>
<li>Proven success interacting with executive level management to determine solutions, resolve issues, and present recommendations at a senior level</li>
<li>Ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously and handle time sensitive deadlines</li>
<li>Cross-functional and interdisciplinary leadership and knowledge</li>
<li>Ability to travel up to 60% worldwide</li>
<li>Be an EFI Ambassador!</li>
</ul>
<p>The successful candidate will thrive in a fast-paced, results-driven, informal, and open environment that is highly collaborative and communicative at all levels . He/she will have strong intellect, be analytical, and effectively communicate through reasoning and fact. He/she must be able to develop opinions yet be open minded, able to listen and willing to change course. Being comfortable in a culture that promotes debate and discussion and having the patience to push through that to get results are essential for success. The candidate will be adept at balancing strategy and execution, continually driving toward the intended results.
</p>
<p>Work Experience</p>
<p>
The ideal candidate would have business development experience, in the packaging sector. He/she will have experience with selling to brands, understand packaging workflow inside of a brand environment, have a background in print technology.
</p>
<p>We move quickly, with the purposeful intention of solving a problem, accomplishing a goal, and ultimately making a difference for our customers, partners, colleagues and shareholders, EFI SPRINTers take action and adapt to changes in order to transform our customers businesses to be more productive and profitable through innovation in a digital print business.</p>
