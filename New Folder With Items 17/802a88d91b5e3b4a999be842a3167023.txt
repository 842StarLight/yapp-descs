<h2>Senior Technical Support Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Position Summary</b>
<br>
<br>
DMA is seeking a critical thinking, self-motivated resource to provide industry leading companies with technical consulting services to enhance the functionality and automation of their sales tax processes. Position will also enable the consultant to gain and further expand existing experience with an array of cutting edge technologies. Consultant must possess the skillset and desire to communicate with clients and provide best in class consultative services.
<br>
