<h2>Copier Technician</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Pacific Office Automation is currently seeking technicians who are skilled in repairing/maintaining <i>Canon, Ricoh, Sharp, HP, Konica Minolta </i>or any other brand of copiers/printers for our Bay Area region.</b></p>
<p>If you are a self-driven, service expert who is detail-oriented and capable of multi-tasking, we want to talk to you! If you are a skilled leader, have experience managing employees and are looking for the rare opportunity to make an impact leading a group of employees who already have a successful process in place, we want to hear from you!</p>
<p><b>As a Field Service Technician, your duties will include: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for a full range of repairs and service procedures on high speed digital copiers/printers, full-color copiers/printers, facsimiles.</li>
<li>Diagnostics, installation, troubleshooting, machine modifications, software upgrades, removal and retrofits on a wide range of machines. (Canon, HP, Sharp, Lexmark, Konica Minolta, Oce, Ricoh etc.)</li>
<li>Repair, maintain</li>
<li>Providing high quality technical support and expertise by complete each service call according to service agreements and requirements</li>
<li>Effectively complete all territory assignments and developing options to resolve unexpected problems</li>
<li>Participates in training to remain up to date with new products and procedures.</li>
<li>Provide specialist service to other field service technicians for unresolved technical problems/issues</li>
<li>Provide technician support to our sales team members to effectively maximize equipment leads and upgrade opportunities</li>
<li>Working in fast paced environment</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications: </b></p>
<ul>
<li><b>Must have experience with servicing and repairing office equipment such as copiers, printers, fax machines, mailing machines etc.</b></li>
<li>Applicants with any advanced <b>Canon, Sharp, Oce, Konica Minolta </b>certifications are highly preferred.</li>
<li>Strong computer knowledge</li>
<li>Excellent customer service communication skills are essential</li>
<li>2-6 years minimum of relevant experience</li>
<li>Strong problem-solving and mechanical/electrical skills</li>
<li>Strong working knowledge of Windows 10 applications are required</li>
<li>Highly motivated and capable of working independently in the field</li>
<li>Must possess a valid driver’s license with a clean driving record. A company car will be provided.</li>
</ul>
