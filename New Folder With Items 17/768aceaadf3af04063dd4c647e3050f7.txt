<h2>Remarketing Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Let's Talk! If you are a good communicator and enjoy working in a close-knit team, then this is the right opportunity for you! As a Remarketing Specialist you will provide quality and efficient customer service by performing administrative duties related to the remarketing of vehicle returns. Every call is an opportunity for you to use your communication and negotiation skills while working with customers, auto dealerships, vendors and financial institutions concerning retail and lease accounts. You will works with other team members to ensure that the daily workload of the center is completed. What is Remarketing? We prepare and manage the inventory of Ally's U.S. pre-owned retail and lease-return vehicles, which includes:· Vehicle Return Management· Transportation Logistics· Inspection - Title Coordination· Online and Physical Auction Sales· Direct-to-Dealer Sales· Direct-to-Customer Sales Visit <b>www.ally.com</b> to learn more. The purpose of this position is to provide quality and efficient customer service by performing administrative duties related to the remarketing of vehicle returns. This position works with other team members to ensure that the daily workload of the center is completed.</p>
<p><b>
Job Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Supports strategies to minimize net credit losses by maximization of dollars recovered from written-off accounts (outside collection/repossession agency/attorney management). This includes assisting with on-boarding new vendors by requesting and organizing information associated with the RFP process.</li>
<li>Delivers continuously improving year on year expense results by improving processes and increasing productivity.</li>
<li>Builds solid peer-to-peer relationships in a team environment to meet operational needs.</li>
<li>Communicates effectively and clearly on a daily, weekly, monthly basis with outside vendors to provide exceptional customer service regarding routine requests.</li>
<li>Supports rigorous quality, control and compliance programs to include an ongoing control/compliance self-assessment process.</li>
<li>Performs data entry, system coding, inventory audits, reconciliations, etc. to ensure alignment between vendor systems and Ally systems.</li>
<li>Responds promptly to vendor requests (information, data, letter generation, etc.) on an as needed basis.</li>
</ul>

<p><b>Ally Overview</b></p>
<p>
Ally Financial Inc. (NYSE: ALLY) is a leading digital financial services company and a top 25 U.S. financial holding company offering financial products for consumers, businesses, automotive dealers and corporate clients. Ally's legacy dates back to 1919, and the company was redesigned in 2009 with a distinctive brand, innovative approach and relentless focus on its customers. Ally has an award-winning online bank (member FDIC), one of the largest full service auto finance operations in the country, a complementary auto-focused insurance business, a growing digital wealth management and online brokerage platform, and a trusted corporate finance business offering capital for equity sponsors and middle-market companies.</p>
