<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Contract Estimator</b></h2>
<div>
<b>Job Description</b>
</div>Marathon Staffing is seeking candidates for a Contract Customer Service Representative position available immediately in Bessemer, AL!!<br>
<br>
The CSR will work in a Manufacturing Facility, providing support to the Sales Department and Customers by providing professional specification review, advice, and training.<br>
<br>
Essential Functions:<br>
Creates quotation by entering bill of materials into a computerized quotation system.<br>
Sets up price basis for sales to negotiate scope of supply.<br>
Provides training to sales reps, customers, and customer service, plant employees in the area of products and application thereof and other areas of expertise.<br>
Coordinates all assigned orders to include all tasks required.<br>
Experience in SAP, preferred but not required.<br>
Previous customer service experience a plus.<br>
Performs other duties as may be assigned by managers.<br>
<br>
Skills, Knowledge, Abilities and Traits:<br>
<br>
Must have strong ability to meet deadlines, manage information from multiple locations.<br>
Ability to work with accuracy and be detailed oriented.<br>
Excellent computer skills and experience.<br>
Excellent oral and written communication skills and ability to communicate on all levels.<br>
Excellent problem solving/troubleshooting skills.<br>
Strong sense of urgency in meeting internal and external customer needs.<br>
Must have strong working knowledge of Microsoft Excel, Word and Outlook software.<br>
Strong data entry skills<br>
Requires diligent presence in work area to be responsive to customer calls and e-mails.<br>
Ability to prioritize and manage multiple tasks/projects while meeting all required deadlines.
<br>
<br>
Contract expected to last approximately (4) months.<br>
<br>
Hours are Monday through Friday, 7:30 AM to 4:30 PM.<br>
<br>
Pay range of $15.00 to $17.00 per hour, depending on experience!<br>
<br>
Interested candidates should forward resumes to this post for immediate consideration!!! To apply online, go to http://www.mwebconcepts.com/seekers/application.go?app_office_id=80.<br>
