<h2>Associate GIS Demand Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Please review the job details below.

<p></p>The Associate Demand Analyst is responsible for supporting tasking, ordering and tracking our internal demand. Aggregating our demand in manner that maximizes our constellations efficiency. The Associate Demand Analyst identifies data required for product generation to include commercial imagery source, ground control, elevation data and other sources. Provides interdepartmental interfaces between operations, customer service, sales and marketing for production status and order processing while managing and collaborating our demand. The Associate Demand Analyst utilizes a combination of proprietary and commercial-off-the-shelf software suites to perform work required.
