<p><b>Technical Operations Manager</b></p>
<p><b>JOB DESCRIPTION </b></p>
<p>Page 1 of 2</p>
<p><b>Job Title: Technical Operations Manager</b><br><b>Department: Service</b><br><b>Region/Location: </b>Resides in Atlanta GA<br><b>FLSA Status: </b>Exempt<br><b>Prepared Date: </b>7/19/19</p>
<p><b>Position Purpose</b><b>: </b></p>
<p>The Technical Operations Manager works closely with the Service Area Manager supervising technical aspects of implementation and repair of complex electrical, electronic, hydraulic, pneumatic and mechanical equipment, development of protocol and procedure, training and scheduling of staff, and troubleshooting; serves as technical expert in connection with production issues and process improvements.</p>
<p><b>Essential Functions: </b>including but not limited to the following:</p>
<p>- Manages daily operational service issues and technical problems with customers, internal departments and colleagues and ensure all daily technical matters are (1) properly tracked; (2) timeously resolved; (3) satisfactorily concluded in customer’s opinion; (4) comprehensively communicated to Italy for investigation, resolution and reporting to R&amp;D department if needed</p>
<p>- Consciously manages warranty budget with respective staff to ensure efficient and optimal warranty expenditure</p>
<p>- Consciously adheres to service travel expense budget with respective staff to ensure most efficient T&amp;E spending as well as staff education if necessary in this regard</p>
<p>- In conjunction with the Service Area Manager, plan technical resources training, time, interventions and needs to fully cover customer needs and satisfaction</p>
<p>- Troubleshoot machine problems in conjunction with customer on-site technical personnel and SIPA technical helpdesk</p>
<p>- Find appropriate remedies and repairs with customers’ on hand spare parts or coordinate with SIPA parts department to obtain parts in a timely manner</p>
<p>- Advise and train customer on proper care and maintenance of their machine.</p>
<p>- Complete and issue detailed technical reports of the intervention or open issues and submit for approval and signature of customer or distribution to internal departments, and ensure all reports and expenses are submitted to office in timely manner</p>
<p>- Continuing education of technical knowledge for department to keep up with technological solutions</p>
<p>- Provide after-sales technical service and support at customer locations and remotely for all SIPA machines if necessary</p>
<p>- Promote and support SIPA in all areas, which will include regular and frequent customer visits</p>
<p>- Interprets customer production, layout, and processing data and advises on options and alternatives for product installation and operation</p>
<p>- Provides analytical engineering consultation and technical training to internal and external operators</p>
<p>- Provides technical input into quotations and analysis to secure increases in service and parts revenue</p>
<p>- Coordinates pre-project testing, validation and customer training plans in conjunction with product shipment or installation</p>
<p>- Reviews and approves repairs and maintenance procedures in consultation with SIPA warranty manager</p>
<p>- Assists with creation of developmental technical course materials</p>
<p>- Installs, replaces, maintains, troubleshoots or repairs mechanical, hydraulic, pneumatic and electrical or electronic equipment including but not limited to robots or programmable controllers, numeral controls, pipe systems, gauges, valves, pressure regulators, power supplies, drives, and related equipment; PLC programming and analysis where necessary</p>
<p>- Other duties as assigned</p>
<p><b>Qualifications: </b></p>
<p>- Bachelor’s degree or equivalent in a related field or 10 years of progressive experience in electronics and mechanical environments; or equivalent combination of education and experience</p>
<p>- Previous experience in an industrial environment with an understanding of manufacturing restraints</p>
<p>- Project management experience</p>
<p>- Managerial experience</p>
<p>- Specialization in relevant technical systems and software applications</p>
<p>- Ability to analyze engineering documents including electronic schematics and assembly drawings</p>
<p>- Ability to interpret technical instructions in mathematical or diagram form</p>
<p>- Ability to define problems, collect data, establish facts and draw valid conclusions</p>
<p>- Strong problem solving and decision making skills</p>
<p>- Ability to draft technical reports detailing technical or design flaws</p>
<p>- Ability to multi-task in a fast-paced environment without compromising efficiency and accuracy of work</p>
<p>- Travel extensively (50%+) both domestically and internationally and be able to leave for remote intervention with no or little advance notice and stay on site until project is completed;</p>
<p>- Possess or eligibility for a US driver’s license and passport</p>
