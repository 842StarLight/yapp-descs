<p>
Lux Global Partners is launching Lux360 Solutions, an energy efficiency services company serving our client base and beyond. We require an experienced Energy Sales Representative with a proven energy efficiency sales background who will be responsible for introducing our client’s disruptive technologies in various market sectors.</p>
<p>
The Lux360 Solutions group is part of Lux Global Partners that develops and helps implement strategic initiatives for our client’s emerging technologies. The candidate will be responsible for delivering best in class sales performance in multiple key markets, such as industrial, healthcare, commercial buildings, and so forth. The ideal candidate will be accountable for driving revenue growth, profitability, and market expansion in line with our company vision and values.</p>
<p>
The ideal candidate must be a self-starter who can work independently, from their virtual home office thereby can be located anywhere in the continental United States.</p>
<p><i>
Please note that this not a traditional salaried position. There is an equity position for the right candidate and a generous bonus/commission plan with benefits.</i></p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</b></h3>
<p>This role will perform, but is not limited to the following responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Strong direct energy sales that seek out, targets and closes new customers</li>
<li>Ability to sell energy solutions to executive level decision-makers</li>
<li>Cultivate, develop and maintain long-term customer relationships</li>
<li>Develop and execute an annual sales plan that meets the Lux profitability objectives</li>
<li>Ability to conduct preliminary infrastructure energy assessments for initial report generation and proposals</li>
<li>Provides timely feedback to customers to ensure high customer satisfaction</li>
<li>Prepares and present quality proposals and presentations</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Experience &amp; Qualifications:</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in an engineering related field</li>
<li>5 years+ experience in energy efficiency sales</li>
<li>Strong understanding of customer and market dynamics</li>
<li>Ability to update and maintain CRM for customer management and forecasting</li>
<li>Strong executive presence with a C-level mentality and solid communication and influencing skills</li>
<li>Basic knowledge of multiple vertical markets</li>
<li>Exceptional business and analytical skills</li>
</ul>
