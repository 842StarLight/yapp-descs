 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Receptionist</b></p>
<p>Data Age Business Systems - Clearwater, FL</p>
<p>Dynamic growth oriented computer software company seeking a Receptionist who is accustomed to a fast paced environment. This position will be based at the corporate office in Clearwater, FL. The position requires the candidate to have superior communication skills, presentation skills, and exhibit a self-motivated and proactive work ethic. Work schedule is Monday-Friday 9 a.m. - 6 p.m. This is a full time position offering a benefit package which includes Paid Time Off, Paid Holidays, Medical Insurance, Life Insurance, 401(k) and optional Dental and Vision Insurance.</p>
<p><b>Position Summary</b> Responsible for relaying incoming telephone calls, greeting visitors, performing diversified clerical duties and providing organizational support for the office.</p>
<p><b>Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Answers a multi-line phone system and schedules or transfers calls to the appropriate individual, division or department.</li>
<li>Receives and logs all technical support tickets.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Types a variety of forms, letters, and reports.</li>
<li>Receives and distributes mail and received faxes.</li>
<li>Logs all complaints and tracks lead generation.</li>
<li>Assesses needs of Clients and transfers them to appropriate services throughout the company.</li>
<li>Performs general clerical and administrative support for the sales department and executives.</li>
<li>Processes office supply orders, maintains and stocks inventory, and monitors use of supplies and postage in the office.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Knowledge, Skills and Abilities Required to Successfully Perform Position</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite.</li>
<li>Excellent customer service skills.</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication, problem solving, interpersonal, and time management skills.</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task in a fast paced environment.</li>
<li>Maintain professional appearance and attitude in all settings.</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul><li>Receptionist: 3 years (Required)</li></ul>
<p>Location:</p>
<ul><li>Clearwater, FL (Required)</li></ul>
