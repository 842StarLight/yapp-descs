<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>The Opportunity
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div><b>We are currently seeking an Contract Support Specialist (CSS) for the Transportation-Construction Management Practice to support our team in the Tampa and Lakeland, FL areas.</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<p>
The Contract Support Specialist must be familiar with the Department’s Procedures and governing documents covering project related duties. Candidate must exhibit excellent communication skills (written and verbal) as well as advanced proficiency in the use of computer software such as Word, Excel, ProjectSolve, SiteManager, and MAC, to help monitor and track contract quantities, materials, and conformance with the contract. Additionally, shall become proficient in Trimble Business Center - Heavy Construction Edition (HCE) or approved surface to surface comparison software and Engineering Menu. The candidate will also demonstrate an ability to exercise independent judgment in planning work details and making technical decisions related to the office aspects of the project such as:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Prepare and process progress and final estimates,
</li>
<li>Process Contract Change Orders and Supplemental Agreements,</li>
<li>
Interpret contract documents,</li>
<li>
Ensure engineering calculations conform with contract requirements,</li>
<li>
Interpret work details from the field and make technical decisions related to office operations.</li>
<li>
Monitor EEO compliance, etc.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<div><b>
Qualifications:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
High School Diploma or equivalent</li>
<li>
12+ years’ experience in LIMS entry, progress and final estimates, EEO compliance, processing Construction changes, etc.</li>
<li>
Valid Florida Driver License</li>
<li>
Good driving record</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Preferred Qualificiations:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Minimum 4 years of FDOT experience</li>
<li>
Excellent communication skills (written and verbal)</li>
<li>
Advanced proficiency in the use of computer software such as Word, Excel, ProjectSolve, SiteManager, and MAC, to help monitor and track contract quantities, materials, and conformance with the contract</li>
<li>
Proficient in Trimble Business Center - Heavy Construction Edition (HCE) or approved surface to surface comparison software and Engineering Menu.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Certifications:</b></div>
<ul><li>
CTQP Final Estimates Level II</li></ul>
