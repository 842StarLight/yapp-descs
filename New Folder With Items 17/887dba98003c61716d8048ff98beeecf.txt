<b>Overview:</b><br>
A Shift Lead is responsible for the overall shift operations, sales performance and execution of brand excellence in a store in the absence of Assistant General Manager and/or General Manager. Shift leads typically supervise a staff of 1 to 4 team members and carry out management responsibilities consistent with Jamba Juice policies, procedures and legal requirements.
<br><br>
Essential Functions
<br><br>
Lead and manage shifts while acting as manager in charge of store. Deploy team members as required to meet business demands and make necessary staffing decisions.
<br><br>
Communicates clearly, concisely and accurately in order to ensure effective shift operations. Execute Jamba's customer service standards at or beyond acceptable level. Clearly communicate with customers and team regarding Jamba's products and brand.
<br><br>
Coach, counsel and direct team members. Lead team in execution of Jamba standards through BOOST. Train new team members as required. Provide input to general manager and asst. general manager on team member performance issues.
<br><br>
Shift readiness. Evaluate staffing and food pars to ensure proper financial accountability during each shift.
<br><br>
Open and/or close store as required. Perform all safety and security tasks, i.e., alarms, two-person rule, as required to open and close the store.
<br><br>
Working the line. Perform all tasks related to Jamba operational standards. Be able to effectively work and act as a role model at each station in the store.
<br><br>
Accountable for the customer engagement experience on the shift.
<br><br>
Follow all food safety, cash handling and operations procedures and policies.
<br><br>
Coordinate and participate off site program customer visits and deliveries.
<br><br>
Participate in the interviewing and hiring process for new team members.
<br><br>
Ensure proper financial accountability during each shift. Hand off shift to next manager in charge or ensure the store is closed properly for next day's opening manager.
<br><br>
