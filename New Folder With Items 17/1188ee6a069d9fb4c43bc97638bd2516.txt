<li>Contributes to sales growth through product selection, execution, pricing and creative development</li>
<li>Achieves purchasing, scheduling and culinary standards</li>
<li>Achieves all assigned cost goals</li>
<li>Works within budget guidelines</li>
<li>Effectively uses purchasing system</li>
<li>Teams location to meet operational needs</li>
<li>Effectively uses all team training and development tools</li>
<li>Hires hourly team who can deliver Levy operating standards and philosophies</li>
<li>Promotes a cooperative work climate, maximizing productivity and morale</li>
