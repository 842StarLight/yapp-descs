<h2>Assistant Business Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>JOB TITLE:</b> <b>Assistant Business Manager</b>
</p>
<p><b>AA/EEO CODE:</b> 5 ~ Administrative Support</p>
<p><b>
FLSA STATUS:</b> hourly non-exempt</p>
<p><b>
General Function:</b> Ensure prompt and accurate payment of all bills, accurately record all Resident Trust Fund transactions, on-time completion of payroll, and assist the Business Manager as needed. Provide an abuse/neglect free environment for all Individuals served. Supports the agency’s Vision, Mission and Core Values.</p>
<p><b>
Reports To:</b> Business Manager</p>
<p><b>
Duties &amp; Responsibilities:</b>
</p>
<p>1. Enter new hire information, raises, deductions and other changes into time clock and payroll systems, run reports for Business Manager and/or Executive Director to review; maintain time clock and payroll databases.</p>
<p>
2. On a daily basis, inputs approved exception sheets and leave requests into time clock software, review all timecards for missing punches. On payroll Monday, run timecard report for Executive Director to review, make changes as needed.</p>
<p>
3. Help resolve issues and complete exception sheets as needed. Problem solve for issues with staff and their hours, help staff understand what they are paid and why.
</p>
<p>4. Update spreadsheets for net payroll, hours worked by department, work comp audit, full time hours, and yearend audit after each pay period.</p>
<p>
5. Track full-time status for insurance and benefit eligibility, review hours with the Business Manager when eligible for benefits.</p>
<p>
6. Ensure proper processing of all invoices for all companies and Resident Trust Fund accounts.</p>
<p>
7. Ensure proper scheduling and payment of accounts payable.</p>
<p>
8. Record Individual financial transactions in trust fund using accounting software, and generate quarterly reports for all guardians.</p>
<p>
9. Prepare and disburse activity funds. Ensure proper return of receipts and change.</p>
<p>
10. Complete deposit tickets for all accounts, make copies of all deposits for verification.</p>
<p>
11. Reconcile all change and receipts, and maintain cash in and out for all checking and petty cash accounts. Balance petty cash account as often as needed.</p>
<p>
12. Maintain all payroll, accounts payable, Resident Trust Fund, and Individuals’ financial files.
</p>
<p>13. Run errands for business office which includes trips to post office and bank, or other errands as necessary.</p>
<p>
14. Order office supplies for all companies.</p>
<p>
15. Maintain strict confidentiality of all information relating to Individuals, employees, and Business Office operations.</p>
<p>
16. Serve on various committees as assigned.</p>
<p>
17. Attend all in-services and training sessions as required.</p>
<p>
18. Participate in emergency evacuation and emergency procedures to ensure the
</p>
<p>Safety of the Individuals served.</p>
<p>
19. Be knowledgeable and thoroughly adhere to and implement all policies and procedures of all companies.</p>
<p>
20. Ensure the rights and confidentiality of the Individuals served are protected at all times.</p>
<p>
21. Produce or assist with other projects and reports as requested by the Business Manager or Executive Director.</p>
<p><b>
Educational Requirements:</b></p>
<p>
High School Diploma or GED required.</p>
<p><b>
Special Requirements:</b></p>
<p>
No disqualifying criminal convictions</p>
<p>
Minimum one year experience with accounts payable and money handling</p>
<p>
General knowledge of office machines, 10-key adding machine and data entry experience preferred.
</p>
<p>Working knowledge of word processing and spreadsheet software</p>
<p>
Knowledge of accounting software, Peachtree preferred</p>
<p>
Experience in a multi-company environment beneficial</p>
