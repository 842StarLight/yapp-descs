<h2>Marketing and Branding Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>
<p>VSE Corporation is in search of a<b> Marketing and Branding Specialist</b> to supports the Chief of Staff with defining and deploying communications that increases brand awareness, enhances marketing campaigns, and exemplifies corporate communication strategy.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities </b><b>include, but are not limited to:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Supporting the development, coordination, publication and maintenance of all company-wide communications for a variety of mediums, i.e., social media, internet, intranet, print and verbal.
</li>
<li>Designing and developing of internal materials and graphics to support operating initiatives, recruitment and cultural development.</li>
<li>Utilizing social media to exchange information about VSE directly with shareholders, candidates, employees, key audiences and influencers.</li>
<li>Creating and refreshing marketing communications to support the changing marketing strategies set forth by the Chief of Staff.</li>
<li>Assisting with the content and brand cohesion for VSE subsidiary marketing materials, trade shows and newsletter.</li>
<li>Editing and proof-reading all presentations and marketing materials.</li>
<li>Coordinating with various departments on a variety of projects to obtain relevant content in support of company-wide communication strategies.</li>
<li>Supporting the Chief of Staff and executive team on all internal and external presentations.</li>
<li>Creating branded content for web and print distribution.</li>
<li>Ensuring flawless execution and timely delivery of projects.</li>
<li>Collaborating and assisting in website projects (optimization, functionality, content).</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
