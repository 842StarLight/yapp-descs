<h2>SharePoint Practice Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Job Description</b></p>
<p>The SharePoint Practice Manager must come from a technical background with experience in SharePoint. The SharePoint Practice Manager will work with team members and clients in the development and delivery of customizations generally related to various Microsoft applications and technologies, including SharePoint, Microsoft SQL, Business Intelligence, Dynamics GP, and Dynamics CRM. The SharePoint Practice Lead must have the ability to lead a client engagement and team of technical resources through the entire life cycle of a project, creating the opportunity to sell services and software. SharePoint experience with SharePoint online and the latest SharePoint platforms is a must for this role.</p>
<p><b>Desired Skills &amp; Experience</b></p>
<p>The Practice Manager is primarily responsible for building and overseeing a team of resources to deliver customizations and projects as well as assisting, developing and closing business. They apply well-honed consulting, project management and functional skills in the management of projects. Further, Managers are involved in business development such as project diagnostics and proposal development. This is also a hands-on role and the Practice Lead is expected to work on and lead client projects.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:</b><b>
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Team development and management
</li>
<li>Demo SharePoint and be part of sales meetings</li>
<li>Lead the development of a conceptual design for an overall business solution</li>
<li>Develop detailed work plans and then lead a team of resources to deliver the solution</li>
<li>Identifying and resolving issues critical to the clients' strategic and operational success
</li>
<li>Project execution
</li>
<li>Presenting conclusions and recommendations to senior client management
</li>
<li>Providing solution implementation assistance as required
</li>
<li>Managing client relationships</li>
<li>Working with offshore team</li>
<li>Active involvement in firm building activities including recruiting, training, performance management, capacity planning, etc.
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>6+ years working with SharePoint, with at least 3 years in a SharePoint consulting role</li>
<li>3+ years working in a management role preferred</li>
<li>Understanding of strategy and operations related to technical consulting and delivery of projects
</li>
<li>Possess successful team leadership qualities
</li>
<li>Possess successful project leadership abilities
</li>
<li>Outstanding client relationship skills
</li>
<li>Outstanding verbal and written communication skills
</li>
<li>The ability to effectively assist with generating new business
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Technical Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Consulting experience with SharePoint and Office 365</li>
<li>Development experience in SharePoint
</li>
<li>Experience with Microsoft Power Platform (Flow, PowerApps, Power BI)</li>
<li>HTML/DHTML/JavaScript/JQuery/VBScript/CSS experience preferred</li>
<li>Microsoft SQL server, including SSRS and SSIS experience, preferred</li>
<li>XML/XSL/Web Services</li>
<li>Experience with Microsoft Dynamics 365 a plus</li>
</ul>
<p>In addition to the qualifications listed above, the firm places high emphasis on relevant personal qualities: Innovative, Passionate, focused on Customer Service Excellence, strong Ongoing Communication and Collaboration, and a high level of Integrity.</p>
