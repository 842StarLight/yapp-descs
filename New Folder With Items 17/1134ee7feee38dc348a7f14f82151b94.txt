<h2>Southwest Rewards Line of Business Leader</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div><b>Summary of Role</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<p>The Rewards Office Line of Business Leader (OLL) role is an integral part of the leadership team spanning the Southwest market, the Western US and our North American Rewards Leadership Team. The OLL is a team leader and senior Rewards Consultant serving as strategic advisor to clients concerning their Reward related issues; including Executive Compensation, Broad Based Rewards, Sales Compensations, Data and Software. AS OLL, you will manage P&amp;L delivery, drive local sales and marketing strategy, manage and expand large client relationships, and lead and develop the local team – including staffing and recruiting strategy. You will contribute your thought leadership to the overall development of the Rewards LOB.<br>
</p>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>Role Priorities</b></div>
<p></p>
<div><ul>
<li>
<b>Sales and marketing strategy: </b>Primary accountability for leading and executing a business strategy to penetrate the market, introduce new clients to the firm and expand relationships with current clients in order to meet annual revenue goals. To help achieve these goals, you will work with senior sellers across the Human Capital &amp; Benefits Segment and Corporate Risk &amp; Broking to position the Rewards LOB to:
</li>
<ul>
<li>Identify opportunities to cross-sell new products and services</li>
<li>Collaborate with Client Management Group and sales teams to identify, develop and close strategic sales opportunities</li>
<li>Build relationships with internal and external sources to maximize the penetration of key target accounts</li>
</ul>
<li>
<b>Operations management, P&amp;L responsibilities: </b>Contribute to development of plans and budgets, delivering planned performance and ensuring both local market and regional units generate revenue, profit and market share growth in accordance with the local, regional and geography LOB plan</li>
<li>
<b>People management and development:</b> Develop and execute on strategic staffing plans for the office, including all people processes (e.g., recruiting, training and development, goal setting and performance management, employee engagement and compensation/bonus recommendations)</li>
<li>
<b>Lead people management process:</b> Establish People Management team and ensure all aspects of the performance and pay process are executed effectively for your staff</li>
<li>
<b>Collaboration:</b> Collaborate across Region (s), Geography, LOBs and Segments to effectively pursue and deliver on multi-faceted global opportunities</li>
<li>
<b>Client relationship management and delivery:</b> Responsible for all aspects of local delivery of Willis Towers Watson advisory, data and technology solutions for the LOB. Collaborate with the Rewards LOB Practice Leaders to develop client strategies to deepen, broaden and build profitable relationships, including the management and deployment of staff to effectively deliver client work</li>
<li>
<b>Strategy, new product, IC development:</b> Support the development, commercialization and dissemination of IC in cooperation with IC leadership and be the Rewards LOB face in your market</li>
<li>Ensure appropriate governance and work quality process are adopted on all work carried out
</li>
</ul></div>
