<p><b>POSITION TITLE: </b>Information Systems Support-Support role</p>
<p><b>
DEPARTMENT:</b> Information Systems</p>
<p><b>
________________________________________________________________________</b></p>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>GENERAL PURPOSE:</b> The Support, Information Systems position is responsible to perform and maintain the day to day activity on the floor to support developers as directed by manager or director. The position is also responsible to design web pages, upload and download daily files from CMS or vendor sites, distribute these files to various departments using the front end utilities, perform document classification and data entry in Cardiff, and format Excel spreadsheets and reports.</p>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Designs web pages for the company intranet, adds and updates various P&amp;Ps and other documents using applications, i.e. Dreamweaver, in various software languages such as HTML, PHP, etc.</li>
<li>Uploads and downloads files to and from the various vendors FTP, Gentran Mailbox, OTC system.</li>
<li>Daily classification for the documents scanned in Cardiff by departments such as Enrollment, Sales, UM, OTC as well as data entry in Cardiff for OTC diabetic forms.</li>
<li>Performs other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS AND REQUIREMENTS:</b> To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill, and/or ability required. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</p>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>EDUCATION and/or EXPERIENCE:</b> High school diploma or general education degree (GED) required; minimum of six months to one year related experience required; HTML, PHP, Microsoft Office, Cardiff, OTC, Core FTP, Gentran Mailbox, Data Control Center experience preferred; or an equivalent combination of education, training and experience.</p>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>NECESSARY KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS AND ABILITIES:</b> Ability to: work from designated office for collaborative/interactive fulfillment of duties; concentrate in an open-office “cubicle” environment; appropriately and directly interact with other staff members and/or outside associates; perform telephone and/or computer work for extended periods of time; operate office equipment such as computer, telephone, and copy/scan/fax machine; attend and participate in meetings via phone, computer, or in person.</p>
<ul>
<li>Knowledge and skills in customer service practices.</li>
<li>Related skills and training in Microsoft Windows environments.</li>
<li>Excellent oral and written communication skills including good grammar, voice and diction.</li>
<li>Adaptability.</li>
<li>Attention to detail.</li>
<li>Stress tolerance and flexibility with schedule.</li>
</ul>
