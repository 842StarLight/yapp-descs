<h2>Fundraising and Grants Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Employer Information</b></p>
<p><b>Company Name: </b> Soukup Strategic Solutions, Inc.</p>
<p><b>Company Web Site: </b> http://www.SoukupStrategicSolutions.com</p>
<p><b>About Our Organization: </b> At Soukup Strategic Solutions, we have privilege of advising our community’s top leaders in the nonprofit sector. Our clients have selected us to guide them in making their organizations more effective at achieving their missions. We are proud of our strong reputation as one of our community’s most trusted resources in strategic planning, fundraising, nonprofit management, and Board development.</p>
<p>Our clients are large and small nonprofits that positively impact our world in countless ways. Their leaders are some of the most talented and generous people in our community. Through our work with them, we make a measurable impact on thousands of lives. Our clients depend on our expertise, and we see it as an honor to deliver high quality service to them.</p>
<p>Our employees are self-motivated, life-long learners who value being members of a team and are dedicated to providing our clients with the highest level of service. We are seeking new team members to help us build our clients’ capacities to fulfill their missions and achieve their goals. We are looking for talented professionals who share our love for working with nonprofits and who aspire to make a difference in the world. The ideal candidate has experience in the nonprofit sector, preferably in the areas of fundraising and/or nonprofit management.</p>
<p><b>Job Location: </b> Work will be performed primarily at the Company office. Meetings will be required at client offices and other locations throughout the Southwest Florida community. Some travel outside of Southwest Florida is required.</p>
<p><b>Time Commitment: </b> This is a full-time position of 40-hours per week. Some evening and weekend hours will be required to fulfill client needs.</p>
<p><b>Starting Salary Range: </b> $40,000 - $55,000 per year</p>
<p><b>Purpose: </b> Responsible for planning, developing and coordinating the Company's fundraising and grant writing activities on behalf of nonprofit clients.</p>
<p><b>Job Tasks: </b></p>
<p><b><i>Fundraising</i></b></p>
<p>· Research information on clients, their programs, and goals for use in grant proposals, appeals and case statements</p>
<p>· Write annual appeal letters and emails on behalf of clients</p>
<p>· Coordinate the creation of donation remittance envelopes for clients</p>
<p>· Manage the printing, mailing and online distribution of fundraising appeals</p>
<p>· Provide content and direction for the creation of online fundraising pages using each client’s unique brand</p>
<p>· Assist clients with the development of annual and multi-year fundraising plans</p>
<p>· Connect fundraising strategies to each client’s strategic plan</p>
<p>· Assist clients with the development of annual fund campaign plans</p>
<p>· Provide generalized support for clients in implementing their fundraising plans, capital campaigns and/or annual fund campaigns</p>
<p>· Perform research on prospects; prepare and deliver reports to clients on results of research</p>
<p>· Write case statements</p>
<p>· Assist clients with donor recognition, including thank you letters, phone calls, events, gifts, displays, etc.</p>
<p>· Manage data entry for clients in their donor databases as necessary</p>
<p>· Provide coaching and support for clients on fundraising strategies</p>
<p>· Assist Boards in understanding their role and fundraising and striving for their fundraising potential</p>
<p><b><i>Grant Research</i></b></p>
<p>· Develop a deep understanding of clients’ programmatic offerings, innovations and data to drive and inform grant writing strategy</p>
<p>· Research and monitor grant opportunities at the national, state and local level which match clients’ priorities for funding their programs and services</p>
<p><b><i>Grant Writing</i></b></p>
<p>· Work closely with the Client to determine goals, objectives and budget, related to specific funding requests</p>
<p>· Prepare grant proposals on behalf of clients</p>
<p>· Prepare budgets and gather letters of support and required materials needed from the Client to complete funding requests</p>
<p>· Ensure compliance with grant submission guidelines</p>
<p>· Submit grant proposals on behalf of clients, including all required attachments, prior to the deadline and maintain documentation of submission</p>
<p><b><i>Grant Reporting</i></b></p>
<p>· Maintain grant records, including but not limited to, files of all proposals, contracts and reports</p>
<p>· For each client, create and maintain a deadline-driven calendar of grant deadlines and activities, including cultivation activities and report requirements</p>
<p>· Track and report funders’ responses to requests</p>
<p>· Submit quarterly and annual reports on behalf of the Client to funders in the manner specified by each funder</p>
<p>· Act as central support figure to grantors on the Client’s behalf from application through implementation</p>
<p>· Provide excellent customer service to clients by offering technical support, tools and problem resolution for grant-related activities</p>
<p><b><i>General</i></b></p>
<p>· Provide supervision and support for interns and volunteers engaged in fundraising and grant writing work for clients</p>
<p>· Emulate the Company brand, provide leadership, and serve as a role model for others</p>
<p>· Meet with supervisor</p>
<p>· Communicate with clients, vendors and Company employees by phone, email and in person</p>
<p>· Prepare reports on work activities and progress toward Company objectives</p>
<p>· Keep track of and report time on Company timesheet</p>
<p>· Collaborate with other Company employees and interns</p>
<p>· Participate in professional associations in fundraising</p>
<p>· Stay informed on the latest developments within the fundraising field</p>
<p>· Other general duties in support of the Company’s fundraising and grant writing strategies</p>
