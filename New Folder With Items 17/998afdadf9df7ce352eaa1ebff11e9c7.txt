<h2>POS Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Requisition ID: 13496</p>
<p>Functional Area: Sales</p>
<p>Posting Locations: Akron</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
The Space Management Point Of Sales (POS) Clerk will be responsible for overseeing the daily operations to include ordering POS, managing POS and fulfilling POS orders submitted internally. This position will also require some knowledge of inventory processes to aid in the receiving, tracking, routing and monitoring of POS items. The POS Clerk will participate in inventory counting and reconciliation processes and will be responsible for pre-assembling inventory items for shipment. The POS Clerk must have good interpersonal skills since they will work with a variety of people including managers, warehouse personnel, vendors, and customers. The POS Clerk will work on multiple projects simultaneously, which will require efficient use of time management and organization skills.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Managing POS for multiple branch locations</li>
<li>Performs the binding and assembly of POS materials as necessary</li>
<li>Performs other activities of packaging, storage and transportation of goods as required</li>
<li>Receives POS materials from suppliers and assigns to storage locations as defined</li>
<li>Maintains accurate counts and records inventory appropriately</li>
<li>Pre-assembles POS items as needed for use in the market place</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Working knowledge and proficiency in industry desktop software</li>
<li>Reliability and attention to detail</li>
<li>Ability to operate warehouse pallet moving equipment</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Motivated, sense of urgency</li>
<li>Excellent Driving Record</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Must be able to work flexible hours</li>
<li>Some overnight travel may be required</li>
</ul>
<p>Coca-Cola Consolidated, Inc. is an Equal Opportunity Employer.</p>
