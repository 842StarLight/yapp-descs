<h2>Artist Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>*REMOTE POSITION*<br>COMMISSION-BASED POSITION</p>
<p>Compensation: 10%, plus incentives</p>
<p>Motif Artists is a creative management agency representing and supporting artists within the beauty and fashion industries. We represent creative artists such as makeup artists, photographers, hair stylists, fashion stylists and more.</p>
<p>Motif Artists is seeking Artists Managers to work with us remotely. The artist manager will be responsible for the management of artists on the Motif Artists roster. This person must be able to maintain a positive attitude, respond efficiently to any inquiries, work collaboratively with others at the agency, and communicate with people effectively at all different levels. The candidate must work to develop current relationships as well as create new opportunities and business strategies for their artists.</p>
<p>Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Representation, development, and marketing of Motif artists.</li>
<li>Manage artists’ calendars, and support their budget negotiations, productions, logistics, and accounting matters.</li>
<li>Plan artists’ career development and establish strategies for their professional growth.</li>
<li>Generate new business and revenue opportunities for artists.</li>
<li>Retain and nurture existing relationships with luxury brands, agencies, and other potential clients.</li>
<li>Work closely with other agents and divisions internally on artist projects, future opportunities, and company objectives.</li>
<li>Assisting with negotiating contracts and budgets.</li>
<li>Manage artist invoicing and payment in timely manner.</li>
</ul>
<p>Requirements:</p>
<ul>
<li>Must my 18 years of age.</li>
<li>Must be a US resident.</li>
<li>Must have a high school diploma or GED.</li>
<li>Strong sales skills</li>
<li>Social media marketing skills preferred.</li>
<li>Ability to create leads, negotiate, manage and close deals</li>
<li>Some knowledge of the fashion and advertising market</li>
<li>Existing Industry relationships</li>
<li>Strong connections in the beauty and fashion industries</li>
<li>Knowledge of contemporary photography, art, high end fashion and publishing</li>
<li>Proficient negotiation skills, familiarity with standard rates and usages</li>
<li>Personable nature, with ability to develop lasting and productive relationships with artists, clients and colleagues</li>
<li>Strong written and verbal skills</li>
<li>Detail oriented and organized</li>
<li>Team player</li>
<li>Ability to multitask</li>
<li>Ability to identify and manage priority requests, calls and meetings</li>
<li>Proficiency in Microsoft Office, Google Drive, Google Docs, Google Sheets, Google</li>
<li>Ability to work productively under pressure, think ahead and be a consistently proactive problem solver</li>
</ul>
