<h2>Senior Manager, Innovation and Product Development</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Company: </b> Bragg Live Food Products, LLC</p>
<p><b>Position: </b> Sr. Manager, Innovation and Product Development</p>
<p><b>Location: </b> Santa Barbara area, CA</p>
<p><b>Reports To: </b> Vice President of Marketing</p>
<p><b>Innovation Manager Position: </b> The Senior Manager, Innovation and Product Development is responsible for driving new product initiatives from idea creation to launch. In this new role, the successful candidate will lead cross-functional teams responsible for delivering platforms that provide long-term enterprise value by optimizing brand strategy, consumer insights, supply chain strengths and technical capabilities. He or she will exhibit a strong balance between entrepreneurial creativity and an enterprise, best-practice-based approach, grounded in strategy. This position will work cross-functionally to ensure strategic alignment, coordinating and driving innovation with multiple stakeholders, to include supply chain, technical services and sales teams, as well as suppliers and customers.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Engage with marketing team and senior leadership to develop innovation strategies, including multi-year platforms.</li>
<li>Recommend and develop new products that position Bragg for future growth, in partnership with the Technical Services team.</li>
<li>Generate a healthy innovation pipeline, with a minimum two-year horizon.</li>
<li>Maintain detailed knowledge and understanding of consumer and customer needs, competitors and food industry trends.</li>
<li>Mine data and consumer insights in order to keep innovations consumer-grounded and to ensure high profit potential for items.</li>
<li>Work with Vice President Marketing, Director of Technical Services and senior leadership to launch and implement a stage-gate product development process that ensures strategic alignment through the organization.</li>
<li>Translate vision and strategy into explicit plans and action.</li>
<li>Ensure that proper financial assessments are completed on all innovation projects and that proper approvals occur in accordance with process.</li>
<li>Drive all aspects of new product platforms, as well as the broader, cross-functional organizational effort to meet initiative objectives and timelines.</li>
<li>Work closely with the marketing, sales, operations and finance teams to ensure that new items are incorporated properly into annual plans and capacity models.</li>
<li>Work with marketing and sales to develop sell-in stories and promotional support plans to ensure a successful launch.</li>
<li>Track new item performance and report results.</li>
<li>Manage post-launch requirements, including product enhancements and optimizations as needed.</li>
<li>Effectively communicate status and performance at all levels of the organization and with outside suppliers to ensure organizational alignment and support.</li>
<li>Play an active role in presenting multi-year innovation pipeline to key customers, securing early feedback and buy-in to upcoming launches.</li>
<li>Provide continuity and support to marketing colleagues as product management responsibilities transfer post-market-launch.</li>
<li>Own P&amp;L line items for new product lines, recommending marketing investment, pricing strategies, and trade programs in order to meet financial launch hurdles and deliver optimum enterprise value.</li>
<li>Manage the budget for innovation platform development and new item launch support.</li>
</ul>
