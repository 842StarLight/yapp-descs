<h2>Workflow Services Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">At MIM Software, our Workflow and Services Team provides solutions to everyday clinical needs and also solves complex clinical problems. Utilizing MIM’s GUI based programming language, Workflow and Services Team members build “MIM Workflows” to automate routine tasks and expand the usage of the software in the clinical setting. As a member of the Workflow and Services Team, you will create MIM Workflows that are requested by our current customers and also serve as a technical expert for new customer implementation. These requests can range from simple adjustments of how images are being viewed to more complex problems involving quantitative analysis of images for specific studies. No day is the same, nor are the problems you will be solving. We are looking for people who love to solve problems and take their own creative approach to these problems.
<br><br>
A GREAT PLACE TO WORK
<br><br>
Be Flexible – We have a relaxed office setting that will allow you to build your own goals and learn at your own pace. If you’re looking for an office that will let you get ahead without anything holding you back, this is the place to be.
<br><br>
Challenge Yourself – Medical imaging is what our company is based on, and we want you to succeed. It’s an extensive learning process and a challenge to constantly stay on top of the latest developments.
<br><br>
Make a Difference – While we don’t always directly converse with patients, we do assist their doctors. The goal of our company is to enhance patient care. This position directly affects how the physicians and technologist interface with the software. Your work here will be used at different facilities all over the world, every day.
<br><br>
RESPONSIBILITIES
<br><ul>
<li>Creation of custom MIM Workflows for different institutions across all markets</li>
<li>Develop new MIM Workflows for new version releases</li>
<li>Assist sales and new site implementation for technical assistance</li>
<li>Take part in customer MIM Workflow Workshops</li>
<li>1+ year training as a Clinical Support Engineer on the Customer Service team to develop proficiency with our software products while assisting users with questions or problems</li>
</ul>
