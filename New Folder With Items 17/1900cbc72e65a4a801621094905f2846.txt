<h2>Contact Center Customer Service Representative *Oct Start Date*</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>The Customer Service Representative (CSR) interacts with customers to provide information in response to inquiries about products and services and to handle and resolve complaints. He/She also ensures the efficient and accurate entering of all requests using proprietary software as well as documenting complaints and issues while providing superior customer service to members.</div>
<div>ESSENTIAL JOB FUNCTIONS<br>
Assist customers with transportation questions and concerns<br>
<ul>
<li>Schedule, modify and cancel reservations as requested</li>
<li>Obtain and document details of any complaints being filed</li>
<li>Receive and document customer concerns</li>
<li>Ensure that all relevant customer information is documented</li>
</ul>
Policy Adherence<br>
<ul>
<li>Attend all required meetings</li>
<li>Adhere to and ensures all company policies and procedures are maintained</li>
<li>Maintain an acceptable attendance and tardiness record</li>
</ul>
Customer Service<br>
<ul>
<li>Ensure accurate trip data input</li>
<li>Maintain a polite and courteous manner at all times</li>
<li>Demonstrate sincere personal commitment to producing high quality work</li>
<li>Refer unresolved customer grievances to designated department for further investigation and resolution</li>
<li>Promote a positive working environment</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>POSITION QUALIFICATIONS<br>
Competency Statement(s)<br>
Accurate - Ability to perform work accurately and thoroughly<br>
Communication, Oral - Ability to communicate effectively with others using the spoken word<br>
Communication, Written - Ability to communicate in writing clearly and concisely<br>
Customer Oriented - Ability to take care of the customers' needs while following company procedures<br>
Interpersonal - Ability to get along well with a variety of personalities and individuals<br>
Patience - Ability to act calmly under stress and strain, and of not being hasty or impetuous<br>
Reliability - The trait of being dependable and trustworthy</div>
<div>Education<br>
High School Graduate or General Education Degree (GED)</div>
<div>Experience<br>
Six months to one year prior contact center experience and/or prior customer service experience with heavy phone volume and data entry strongly preferred</div>
<div>Skills<br>
Excellent customer service and people skills and must be able to work independently or with a team<br>
Ability to quickly learn new technology and processes<br>
Must be able to understand and follow complex instructions<br>
Read, write and understand English fluently<br>
Ability to accurately type 35wpm<br>
Ability to answer a high volume of calls up to 100+ calls per day</div>
