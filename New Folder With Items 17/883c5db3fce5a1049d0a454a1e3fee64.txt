<h2>Bartenders</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>BARTENDER JOB DESCRIPTION</b></p>
<p><b>General Purpose</b></p>
<p>Prepare, mix and serve drinks andbeverages correctly to either customers directly or through serving staff.</p>
<p><b>Main Job Tasks and Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>greet customers and present beverage menus</li>
<li>make recommendations and answer all related inquiries</li>
<li>check identification of customers to verify they are of legal drinking age</li>
<li>take orders from serving staff or directly from customers</li>
<li>record drink orders accurately into register system</li>
<li>prepare and serve alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks in accordance with standard recipes</li>
<li>peel, slice and pit fruit for garnishing drinks.</li>
<li>mix and garnish cocktails according to standard specs</li>
<li>upsell drink and snack items to customers</li>
<li>respond promptly to requests from customers in a polite and professional fashion</li>
<li>maximize quality of customer experience</li>
<li>attempt to limit problems related to customers' excessive drinking by following established procedures</li>
<li>process transactions using the POS system</li>
<li>prepare checks for customers</li>
<li>process customer payments</li>
<li>arrange glasses and bottles into attractive and functional displays</li>
<li>ensure clean glasses, bar equipment and working areas</li>
<li>comply with all TABC and company health, safety and hygiene standards and policies</li>
<li>balance the cash register at the start of each shift</li>
<li>monitor inventory</li>
<li>order and restock bar inventory as necessary</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Skills and Experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High School Diploma preferred</li>
<li>minimum age requirement of 18 years</li>
<li>working knowledge of standard drink recipes</li>
<li>working knowledge of spirits, wine and beer</li>
<li>understanding of classes of alcohol, different glasses, brand names and garnishments</li>
<li>working knowledge of bar equipment</li>
<li>knowledge and education in mixology an advantage</li>
<li>basic math and cash-handling skills</li>
<li>able to work flexible schedules</li>
<li>able to work in a standing position for long periods</li>
<li>be able to reach and bend and frequently lift up to 30 pounds</li>
<li>quality customer relations</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Key Competencies</b></p>
<ul>
<li>customer service orientation</li>
<li>communication skills</li>
<li>interpersonal skills</li>
<li>team member attitude</li>
<li>high energy level</li>
<li>handle a fast-paced work environment</li>
<li>reliability</li>
<li>honesty and integrity</li>
</ul>
