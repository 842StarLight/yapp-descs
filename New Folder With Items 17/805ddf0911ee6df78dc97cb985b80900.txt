<p>
MoistureShield, a CRH company, is a division within Oldcastle APG. MoistureShield has pioneered the use of recycled polyethylene plastic in the manufacture of composite building materials. With its constantly evolving portfolio of patented and proprietary recycling technologies, MoistureShield has been widely recognized as a leader in resource conservation innovation and received the EPA Award for Environmental Excellence for its process of converting scrap plastic to composite outdoor decking.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
The <b>Regional Sales Manager </b>will be the face of Oldcastle for our MoistureShield Decking. The position with work closely with the Sales Team and implement processes to develop more business distributors and increase sales.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Job Summary</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s Degree or equivalent combination of education and experience with 5+ years related experience in Sales</li>
<li>
Valid Driver’s License with clean driving record</li>
<li>
Excellent Customer Service and interpersonal skills</li>
<li>
Proficient in Microsoft Office with advanced knowledge of excel and ERP systems</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Improving and leading the assigned territory to increase sales at Retail lumber yards and assist with PK’S and special order of MoistureShield throughout the territory</li>
<li>
Leading and coordinating sales functions</li>
<li>
Coordinating and conducting training on our products for store personnel</li>
<li>
Represents MoistureShield and attends trade home shows to promote our product</li>
<li>
Investigating and, if applicable, resolving customer claims to reinforce relationship between customer and local store.</li>
<li>
Coordinates liaison between sales department and other sales related units</li>
<li>
Prepares periodic sales report showing sales volume, potential sales, and areas of proposed client base expansion</li>
<li>
Monitors and evaluates the activities and products of the competition</li>
</ul>
