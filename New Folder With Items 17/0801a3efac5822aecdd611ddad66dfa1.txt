<h2>Quality Assurance Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Through the responsible development of innovative and sustainable building products, CertainTeed, headquartered in Malvern, Pennsylvania, has helped shape the building products industry for more than 110 years. Founded in 1904 as General Roofing Manufacturing Company, the firm's slogan "Quality Made Certain, Satisfaction Guaranteed," inspired the name CertainTeed. Today, CertainTeed is a leading North American brand of exterior and interior building products, including roofing, siding, fence, decking, railing, trim, insulation, drywall and ceilings.
<br><br>
A subsidiary of Saint-Gobain, one of the world’s largest and oldest building products companies, CertainTeed has more than 6,300 employees and more than 60 manufacturing facilities throughout the United States and Canada. www.certainteed.com
<br><br>
<b>Summary:</b><br>
Under the direction of the Regional Quality Manager (West), The Quality Assurance Manager is responsible for the leadership and direction of quality initiatives along with performance of products manufactured in the Roofing plant of Portland, OR. The Quality Assurance Manager develops and implements Quality Management Systems, ISO certification, as well as maintains quality certifications. They will ensure quality in all products, resulting in a strong product reputation, reliable performance and thus, leading and/or growing market-share.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
Supervises quality direction and day-to-day production utilizing the systems and processes established to ensure product quality meets standards and tolerances established.
<br>Collaborates with R&amp;D, Manufacturing and Marketing to ensure quality standards are integrated into new products so production performance and output is to the standards required.
<br>Conduct, score and publish formal quality audits at raw material and 3rd party suppliers.
<br>Interface (and travel) with distribution customers and sales force to address product quality issues or to advise on quality standards or initiatives.
<br>Oversee implementation of measurement tools that enable product evaluation.
<br>Conduct product audits to ensure finished goods conform to specified requirements.
<br>Implement and track all quality measurement systems and Key Operating Indicators for Roofing Production.
<br>Monitor certification and compliance to applicable building codes.
<br>Manage system improvements, process capability studies and design of experiments.
<br>Develop methods to prevent quality defects, reduce mistake and improve production ability.
<br>Supervise Quality Department staff.
<br>Staffing and resource determination.
<br>Support customer quality claims investigation and short term claim administration.
<br>Training, development and coaching of department members to ensure strong performance and morale within quality departments.
<br>Make selection and termination decisions.
<br>Ensure safety goals are achieved and regarded as priority.
<br>Serve as a member of the plant Leadership Team to support our operations and business objectives.
<br><br>
Bachelor’s degree in business, engineering or other technical field.
<br>3-5 Experience in quality engineering, production management or project management.
<br>Prior supervisory experience desired.
<br><b>Member of/Certified in:</b> ASQ disciplines and ISO 9001 lead auditor, Six Sigma Belt a plus
<br>Knowledge of extrusion processes preferred
<br>Analysis skills – including Excel, Access, Mini-tab and Data Management software
<br>Must be able to effect positive and professional interaction with internal groups and customers.
<br>Strong verbal and written communication skills
<br>Very good conflict resolution skills, ability to diffuse volatile situations
<br>Strong presentation skills
<br>Very good facilitation and influencing skills; good coaching skills
<br>Ability to represent the plant and company for external audits, inspections and field inspection.
