<p><b>
About Our Company</b></p>
<p>
Founded in 2003, Aquatic Informatics provides software solutions that address critical water data management, analytics, and compliance challenges for the rapidly growing water industry. Aquatic Informatics is the trusted provider of water management solutions to over 1,000 municipal, federal, state/provincial, hydro power, mining, academic, and consulting organizations in over 60 countries that collect, manage, and process large volumes of water data.</p>
<p>
Aquatic Informatics’ platforms include Aquarius analytics software for natural environments, WaterTrax software for water and wastewater compliance, Tokay software for backflow prevention, and Linko software for the management of industrial pre-treatment and FOG programs. From source water through to receiving environment, our interconnected data management platforms drive the efficient management of water information across the water cycle to protect human health and reduce environmental impact.</p>
<p>
Aquatic Informatics is headquartered in Vancouver, Canada and has offices in the US and Australia. Aquatic Informatics is one of the fastest growing clean technology companies in Canada. We value independent thinking, initiative, teamwork, a relentless pursuit of quality, a playful spirit, and a sense of humor. We like smart people – IQ and EQ – who care about the environment and want to do good in the world.</p>

<p><b>
About the Opportunity</b></p>
<p>
As an Account Manager, you will serve an existing customer base located in North America. You will help providers of drinking water, usually municipal water utilities, improve their backflow prevention operations and use of Tokay software, by learning how they are using the Tokay software they already own and identifying areas for improvement. You will use solution and consultative based selling to outfit them with additional Tokay software and services.</p>
<p>
Additionally, you will ensure that Tokay customers renew their annual support/subscription services. You will also look for opportunities to solve additional customer challenges with our other software solutions (WaterTrax, Linko and Aquarius).<br>
(www.Tokay.com)</p>
<p>
Responsibilities will include:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Work with existing customers to understand their usage of Tokay software and identify opportunities to improve their experience through additional software and services.</li>
<li>
Ensure that recurring support/subscription contracts are renewed each year.</li>

<li>

<li>

<li>
Travel up to 30% of your time mostly to conventions and trade shows in the U.S.</li>
</ul>
