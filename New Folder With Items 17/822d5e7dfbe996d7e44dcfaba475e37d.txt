<h2>Office Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b><i>The Office Manager keeps the office spinning right round by keeping our office up to date, informed, and organized!</i></b> If you’re a master of office procedures, we have a great opportunity for you! Our Kansas Interstate Logos office in Topeka is now hiring a new management team member to help us enhance the Interstate Logo Program in the state of KS.</p>
<p><b>
Company Overview:</b></p>
<p>
The Interstate Logos division of Lamar Advertising is the largest provider of contract logo signing and tourist-oriented directional signing (TODS) programs, partnering with State Transportation Agencies since 1988. Interstate Logos is a wholly owned subsidiary of Lamar Media Corp., which is a wholly owned subsidiary of Lamar Advertising, one of the largest outdoor advertising companies in the world. We live by the Golden Rule, and we operate with honesty and integrity in every aspect of our business. We are open with our employees, transparent with our customers, and loyal to the communities in which we serve.</p>
<p><b>
Benefits Overview:</b></p>
<p>
We offer a competitive and comprehensive compensation &amp; benefits package for all full-time employees. Some of our benefit offerings include, but are not limited to, multiple medical plan options, dental and vision insurance, paid vacation/sick time, 401K plan, and incentivized wellness programs.</p>
<p><b>
Compensation Overview:</b></p>
<p>
The hourly range for this position is <b>$18 - $20 / hour</b>, dependent on relevant experience and skills.</p>
<p><b>
Position Purpose:</b></p>
<p>
The purpose of the Office Manager is to oversee all office functions and responsibilities, supervise clerical staff, maintain payroll hours, work closely with Human Resources (HR) on company-wide policies and regulations, and oversee the overall paperwork and administrative duties associated with the Interstate Logo Program.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Functions and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Maintain accounts receivables (AR) for the company. This includes making calls, sending correspondence, updating the ATB (Aged Trial Balance), and communicating with the sales department about past due accounts.</li>
<li>Responsible for updating the billing system with client information, accounts, and contracts.</li>
<li>Maintain accounts payable (AP) for the company. This includes processing invoices, recording pertinent information (company, date, amount, etc), coding invoices to the correct department, and sending invoices to Corporate for payment.</li>
<li>Act as a liaison for corporate Human Resources (HR). This includes assisting in the hiring and Affirmative Action process, maintaining employee information, assisting employees with benefit questions, worker’s compensation, and assisting with any other human resource requirements needed.</li>
<li>Provide administrative assistance to all employees as needed, such as assisting the sales department prepare contracts, answering collections questions, providing invoice copies, assisting operations employees with payroll, etc.</li>
<li>Provide information, including compensation, to corporate departments (Payroll, Human Resources, Credit, Accounts Payable, Accounts Receivable, etc) as needed.</li>
<li>Run errands as needed. For instance, going to the post office for stamps, sending certified mail, making deposits, etc.</li>
<li>Complete any special requests/projects from General Manager, Sales Manager, or Operations Manager</li>
<li>Maintenance of OSHA (Occupational Safety and Health Administration) logs</li>
<li>Handle Operations Manager and General Manager’s expense reports upon request</li>
<li>Miscellaneous Truck Mileage reporting</li>
<li>Petty Cash</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities Requirements:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Working knowledge of Microsoft Office Suite (Word, Excel, etc.) and computers, with a high level of proficiency and experience using MS Access or similar database program</li>
<li>Requires an excellent command of the English language, both written and verbal.</li>
<li>Ability to manage bank records</li>
<li>Skill in speaking with persons of various social, cultural, economic, and educational backgrounds</li>
<li>Requires continual attention to detail in composing, typing and proofing materials, establishing priorities and meeting deadlines</li>
<li>Strong work ethic – someone who takes great pride in professionalism, responsibility, and is proactive</li>
<li>Must be able to work independently and as a team member</li>
<li>Ability to use fax machine, copiers, scanners and binding machines</li>
<li>Requires the ability to handle different challenges each day and adequately prioritize those demands</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Education and Experience Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>A college degree in business, business administration, or related field is preferred.</li>
<li>A minimum of 1 year experience in an administrative/office setting is required.</li>
<li>Training in office management education preferred</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Physical Demands and Work Environment:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>The primary work environment for this position is an office.</li>
<li>The physical demands for this position include light lifting (10 to 20 lbs.) pushing, reaching, seeing (with a focus on reading, acuity, and depth perception), and talking.</li>
<li>Nights spent away from home traveling are less than 10%.</li>
<br>
</ul>
