<h2>Technical Support Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<div><b>What you will do</b></div>
<div>
The Technical Support Representative will work with Field Services, Applications, Sales, Engineering, manufacturing, customer service, QA, etc. to provide pre and post technical support to customers for a variety of Indoor Air Quality products. Trion IAQ supports (5) product families; Trion Commercial/Industrial, Trion Residential, Herrmidifier, Envirco, and Military. Products include Electrostatic Precipitators, media air cleaners, humidifiers (electric steam generator, atomizing, evaporative, etc.).</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
How you will do it</b></div>
<ul>
<li> Becomes quickly informed of products, their needs, and applications.</li>
<li> Work in a call center environment handling technical support calls from external service technicians, installers, homeowners, customers, etc.</li>
<li> Handle parts order calls and emails including part look up, part identification and part order processing.</li>
<li> Provide pre and post sales technical support including equipment installation, troubleshooting, and applications assistance via phone, and email.</li>
<li> Upon request, provide information such as submittal drawings, wiring diagrams, installation recommendations, or other information as needed.</li>
<li> Approve and process warranty orders as needed.</li>
<li> Log results of all calls into CRM system.</li>
<li> Follow up on customer inquiries to ensure job completion and customer satisfaction.</li>
<li> Must demonstrate excellent customer service and a professional demeanor at all times.</li>
<li> Provide recommended literature revisions, corrections, improvements, etc.</li>
<li> Follow and comply with company work and safety rules and policies.</li>
</ul>
