<h2>Producer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div><div><div>
<div><div>
<p><b>Summary:</b> Responsible for selling new business to ensure continued profitable growth for the agency. Will prospect for new clients, sell additional coverages to existing clients, and retain existing accounts to achieve personal production goals by performing the following duties.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities include the following</b>. Other duties may be assigned.</p>
<ul>
<li>
Call on clients/policyholders to deliver and explain policy, to analyze insurance program and suggest additions and/or changes</li>
<li>
Customize insurance programs to suit individual clients, often covering a variety of risks</li>
<li>
Contact prospects, schedule appointments, and make sales presentations</li>
<li>
Develop prospecting programs and follow-up for maximum results</li>
<li>
Maintain prospect list of clients for target business</li>
<li>
Balance production to achieve desired mix of business. Promote products of carriers that the firm has identified as important</li>
<li>
Coordinate production efforts with Operations Director, Marketing Director and account service personnel, as requested and needed</li>
<li>
Interview prospects/clients to obtain data about their financial resources and needs in order to accurately and thoroughly complete survey and application forms before marketing or submitting to be marketed/re-marketed.</li>
<li>
Conduct periodic meetings with Account Managers to provide updates and ensure policies and procedures are understood</li>
<li>
Calculate premiums and establish payment methods. Arrange payment terms for all accounts in accordance with agency credit and collections policies. Responsible for collection of all outstanding premiums.</li>
<li>
Deliver renewals and/or binders following agency guidelines</li>
<li>
Perform administrative tasks, such as maintaining records</li>
<li>
Keep abreast of trends and techniques to maintain a competitive status for the agency within the industry</li>
<li>
Attend sales seminars, company sales meetings, or educational activities needed to improve sales techniques, stay up to date on the latest developments in the marketplace, and maintain required licenses</li>
<li>
Review all agency activities relating to the public, customers and companies to avoid issues involving potential errors and omissions</li>
<li>
Participate in industry meetings, providing subject matter expertise.</li>
<li>
Meet or exceed performance goals for client and employee satisfaction and retention, as well as revenue generation.</li>
</ul>
