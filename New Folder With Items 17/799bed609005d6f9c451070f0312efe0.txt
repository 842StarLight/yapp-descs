<h2>Sales/Marketing Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Join a company that offers employees a dynamic environment with the ability to travel, encourages learning and growth and is committed to providing superior products and services to local advertising customers.<br>
</p>
<p>
Be a hero for small business and join our full service marketing team! Sales/Marketing Consultants work with our clients to assess their business needs and develop effective marketing and advertising strategies. Successful candidates have an awareness of both print and online marketing solutions, are well organized, self-motivated and enjoy building relationships as they explore the communities we serve.</p>
<p>
 <b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Meet with new and existing business clients to determine an effective marketing and advertising strategy based on objectives and budget.</li>
<li>
Educate customers on the value of a strong marketing plan and the benefits to each of the various marketing and advertising solutions we offer.</li>
<li>
Maintain assigned accounts as well as develop new accounts</li>
<li>
Process necessary documentation to complete contracts</li>
<li>
Communicate with clients through email, phone or face to face presentations</li>
<li>
Develop a market strategy to prioritize, organize and accomplish goals</li>
<li>
Overnight travel to our various markets to discover what's great about each of our clients</li>
<li>
Valid driver's license in good standing required</li>
</ul>
