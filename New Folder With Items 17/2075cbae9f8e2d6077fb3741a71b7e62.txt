<h2>Community Outreach Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Pieces Technologies provides a dynamic information exchange platform enabling health systems to extend their network of care to community service organizations. Health Systems refer, connect and share information with community services organizations for seamless care coordination. Pieces also uses artificial intelligence (AI) to predict patients at-risk for adverse outcomes, applies machine learning (ML) with clinical human augmentation to ensure high model accuracy, and utilizes proprietary natural language processing (NLP) technology to surface insights from unstructured data such as free text progress notes and dictated notes. Pieces supports the care team across the entire journey - in the health system and in the community.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Pieces Tech is looking for energetic, team oriented individuals with strong communication and organization skills that learn quickly and enjoy frequent travel to fill the role of <b>Community Outreach Representative.</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
The responsibilities include but is not limited to the following requirements:</p>
<ul>
<li>Support the Client Engagement Manager in establishing community contacts and resources in various regions across the country</li>
<li>Develop and maintain relationships with Community Based Organizations (CBO) and key stakeholders</li>
<li>Understand organizational missions, community network connections, and key community stakeholders</li>
<li>Coordinate events and educate organizations on Pieces Iris’ case management and referral platform</li>
<li>Produce accurate and achievable timelines, estimates, and statements of work to customers while coordinating with Sales and Client Engagement team to fulfil these goals</li>
<li>Under general supervision, support requests that arise from key stakeholders and end users</li>
<li>Deliver monthly KPI reporting and routine status updates for each account</li>
<li>Provide feedback to the Client Engagement Team on system enhancements, feature improvements, and alternate uses for the software</li>
<li>Assist Client Engagement Team with setting up meetings, organizing agendas, and scheduling travel arrangements</li>
</ul>
<p>Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</p>
<br>

</ul>
