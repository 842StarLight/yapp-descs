<h2>Senior Analyst - Trade Compliance & Transportation</h2>

GoPro is seeking a top-tier candidate for its Sr. Analyst Trade Compliance &amp; Transportation position. The role offers deep international exposure in transportation sourcing, service provider performance management and import/export trade compliance. Reporting to the Senior Manager of Transportation, Trade Compliance &amp; Asia Logistics, the candidate will leverage freight and trade analytics to identity opportunities that improve efficiency and optimize network operating costs throughout GoPro's global supply chain.
<br><br>
Responsibilities
<br><br>
The candidate should have a strong working knowledge in the key areas of trade compliance including tariff classification, country of origin determinations, and principles of valuation and is confident in interacting with local and international customs administrations, legal counsel, customs brokers, and internal stakeholders. The Sr Analyst position is a coordinating position that is expected to collaborate cross functionally and establish effective and positive working relationships with Operations, Sales &amp; Marketing, Finance, Legal, and Administrative &amp; Workplace Services so that internal compliance with Import/Export Regulations are properly followed and maintained.
<br><br>
The candidate will also manage GoPro's freight bill audit &amp; payment (FBAP) program to ensure freight payables compliance with GoPro's contractual payment terms and rates. In addition the candidate will use this system to analyze key freight performance metrics and will develop, maintain and product key reports on freight execution and costs. Understanding of transportation costs will ensure the candidate delivers the required level of support and value. The ideal candidate possesses in-depth experience developing logistics analytical models and tools.
<br><br>
Trade Compliance
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Maintain GoPro's parts dictionary of trade compliance designations inclusive of US and foreign import tariff codes (World's Customs Organization "WCO" Harmonized System "HS" codes) and US export control classifications numbers (ECCN).</li>
<li>Support functional lead with product classification (i.e. Import HS; Export ECCN/Schedule B) for finished goods and components.</li>
<li>Partner with trade compliance partners (i.e. customs brokers and/or trade attorneys) to resolve shipment delays, initiate correction action and develop procedures to prevent recurrence.</li>
<li>Assist internal functions with import/export guidance and preparation of trade documentation (i.e. commercial invoice, packing list, product technical information) for the international shipment of GoPro product and sourced commodities via carriers and freight forwarders.</li>
<li>Support and lead import and export trade management initiatives that focus on minimizing risk, improving efficiency and reducing costs.</li>
<li>Assist functional lead with investigation of trade risk events to include exposure analysis, risk remediation and development of mitigation controls.</li>
<li>Analyze, monitor and communicate pertinent policy changes to US/foreign customs regulations and US export controls laws that may impact business operations.</li>
<li>Manage the process to prepare and submit GoPro's annual assists calculation on products imported for the EU and US markets.</li>
</ul>
Transportation
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Champion the freight bill audit and payment (FBAP) program for global freight payables.</li>
<li>Lead monthly transportation metrics reviews in collaboration with key functions – Operations, Finance and E-commerce.</li>
<li>Develop/present a dashboard of transportation metrics showcasing costs, defined KPIs, forecast variances and summary highlights by month, quarter and year.</li>
<li>Manage freight loss &amp; damage (FL&amp;D) claims program administered by GoPro's FBAP provider.</li>
<li>Assist in the identification of transportation saving opportunities.</li>
<li>Assist functional lead in development of freight forecasts.</li>
<li>Assist functional lead with the logistics service provider (LSP) performance management process (e.g. Scorecard preparation, Business reviews, SOP enhancements, etc.).</li>
<li>Assist functional lead with the LSP sourcing process (e.g. RFP package development, LSP assessment, tariff/contract negotiation, etc.).</li>
</ul>
Skills We're Stoked About
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Familiarity/experience with trade compliance including: country of origin &amp; tariff classification determination, Free Trade Zone (FTZ) operations, and Customs Assists reporting.</li>
<li>Direct experience managing freight payables for US domestic and international transportation – Americas, EMEA and Asia-Pacific.</li>
<li>Working knowledge of pricing structures for core transportation modes: Air, Ocean, Truck (less-than-truckload and full truckload) and Small Package.</li>
<li>Ability to articulate findings, concepts and ideas in a concise manner.</li>
<li>Familiar with EDI implementation and management for freight invoicing.</li>
<li>Familiar with International Air Transportation Association (IATA) and International Maritime Organization (IMO) regulations for the transport of dangerous goods (DG) – emphasis on lithium ion products.</li>
<li>Experience with import (WCO HS) and export (US ECCN) product classification.</li>
<li>Familiar with US trade laws for import (US Customs) and export (US Department of Commerce, US Bureau of Industry &amp; Security and US Census Bureau).</li>
</ul>
Requirements
<br><br>

<li>BA/MBA in Logistics/Supply Chain management or Data Analytics; equivalent experience may be substituted for degree requirements.</li>
<li>Excellent communication and problem-solving skills.</li>
<li>Highly effective interpersonal skills and organizational skills.</li>
<li>Dangerous goods certification preferred.</li>
<li>Licensed Custom Brokered designation preferred.</li>
<li>Ability to travel approximately 10%-15% (US/international).</li>
</ul>
