<div><div>
<p>Salem Media Group in Nashville, is offering an EXCEPTIONAL opportunity for a qualified <b><i>Media Strategist </i></b>for our Nashville’s publishing division and the <i>Singing News</i> radio network. This involves representing and selling our print, digital and radio products to national advertisers. You will be a key part of our dynamic publishing advertising sales group and will work as a team member to achieve both individual and team goals. Our magazines include <b><i>Family Fiction</i></b> (digital), <b><i>CCM </i></b>(digital) and <b><i>Singing News</i></b> (print and digital). The <b><i>Singing News </i></b>radio network is a Southern-Gospel music network and was the winner of the <i>2018 Southern Gospel Radio Impact Award</i> from the GMA Dove Awards!</p>
<p>
Salem Media Strategists are high-energy, proactive marketing and sales professionals who are “black belts” in using digital, print and radio advertising for the good and growth of national businesses.</p>
<p>
The Media Strategist will engage in all levels of sales activity, personally making direct sales calls on national marketing directors, publishers, authors, author agents, record labels, artists and group management with the goal of offering solutions designed for growth. You will learn about their goals, needs and challenges and then will develop and present effective solutions for growing their businesses through advertising campaigns to our high quality audiences.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities will include:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Generate revenue for the publishing brands and the <i>Singing News</i> radio network and meet monthly goals through effective sales techniques and new ideas</li>
<li>Create and present advertising strategies and ideas for national products and businesses incorporating digital, on-line, on-air and print mediums</li>
<li>Retain current business and develop new business contacts</li>
<li>Attain budgeted revenue goals through effective solicitations, promotions and service</li>
<li>Understand the business objectives and advertising strategies of clients and find ways to help them achieve their objectives through effective advertising</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Expertise and Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrated history of advertising sales campaign success</li>
<li>A proven track record of meeting and exceeding sales goals</li>
<li>Proficient in Word, Excel and PowerPoint</li>
<li>Strong organizational, written and presentation skills</li>
<li>Ability to build and maintain positive customer relationships</li>
<li>Competitive, persuasive, self-motivated</li>
<li>Ability to overcome objections</li>
<li>Working knowledge of new media and digital interactive initiatives</li>
<li>Digital/print/radio media sales experience preferred</li>
</ul>
<p><b>This position needs to be based out of the greater Nashville, TN market.</b></p>
<p>
Salem Media Group offers countless opportunities for advancement and growth. Compensation is commensurate with experience. We offer a comprehensive benefits package including medical insurance, life insurance, paid holidays and paid vacation.</p>

Apply online with a resume and cover letter. </i><i>Salem Media Group is an Equal Opportunity Employer and is a certified “Best Place to Work”.</i></p>
