<b>Job Description:</b><br>
<p>Carter's, Inc. is the largest branded marketer in North America of apparel exclusively for babies and young children. The Company owns the <i>Carter's </i>and <i>OshKosh B'gosh</i> brands, two of the most recognized brands in the marketplace. These brands are sold in leading department stores, national chains, and specialty retailers domestically and internationally. They are also sold through more than 1,000 Company-operated stores in the United States, Canada, and Mexico and online at www.carters.com, www.oshkoshbgosh.com, and www.cartersoshkosh.ca. The Company's <i>Just One You </i>and <i>Genuine Kids</i> brands are available at Target, its <i>Child of Mine</i> brand is available at Walmart, and its <i>Simple Joys</i> brand is available on Amazon. The Company also owns <i>Skip Hop</i>, a global lifestyle brand for families with young children. Carter's is headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia. Additional information may be found at www.carters.com.
</p>
