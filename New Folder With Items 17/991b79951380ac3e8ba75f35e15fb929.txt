<p><b>Account Executive</b></p>
<p>Long Beach, CA</p>
<p>Orient Overseas Container Line” and “OOCL” are trade names for transportation provided separately by: Orient Overseas Container Line Limited (“OOCLL”) and OOCL (Europe) Limited respectively and both are wholly-owned subsidiaries of Orient Overseas (International) Limited, a public company (0316) listed on the Hong Kong Stock Exchange. OOCL is one of the world's largest integrated international container transportation, logistics and terminal companies. As one of Hong Kong's most recognized global brands, OOCL provides customers with fully-integrated logistics and containerized transportation services, with a network that encompasses Asia, Europe, the Americas, Africa and Australasia.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>OOCL is well respected in the industry with a reputation for providing customer-focused solutions, a quality-through-excellence approach and continual innovation. OOCL is one of the leading international carriers serving China, providing a full range of logistics and transportation services throughout the country. It is also an industry leader in the use of information technology and e-commerce to manage the entire cargo process.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>We have an immediate opening for a virtual Account Executive in the Long Beach, CA area.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>In this position, the successful candidate will be responsible for developing new business, maintaining and improving the value of existing business and customer relations in the designated territory based upon trade and regional sales plan objectives. The Account Executive also develops strategies and negotiates bids and contracts, solves problems and/or elevates issues as needed and keeps accounts updated on service, pricing and policies. The successful candidate will maintain a professional appearance, demeanor and display excellent interpersonal skills.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>The ideal candidate must have excellent negotiation, analytical, research and problem solving skills. Applicants must possess excellent communication skills (oral and written) and have strong PC skills (including MS Office). The ability prioritize and manage multiple projects is a necessity. A Bachelor's Degree or higher is preferred and only applicants with a minimum of 2 years industry and sales experience is required.</p>
