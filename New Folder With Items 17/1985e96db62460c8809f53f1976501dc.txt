Essential Functions:</p>
<br>
<p></p>
</div>
<p>Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<li>Constantly seek out, gather, learn and analyze information throughout the company with an eye towards making things better</li>
<li>Partner with Product Management and key stakeholders to foster innovation by translating rough business requirements into tangible, easy to understand user stories</li>
<li>Participate in cross-functional innovation sessions, design discussions and showcases to build new services, redesign processes and solve business problems</li>
<li>Capture clear acceptance criteria for user stories to ensure software meets the business needs</li>
<li>Ensure backlog is groomed and developers have a solid pipeline of stories</li>
<li>Identify when and where communication breakdown exists and offers recommendations.</li>
<li>Explains issues and problems to the non-technical business users and/or additional support staff</li>
<li>Identify differences in how the system was designed and how it is used.</li>
<li>Assist Sales, Implementations and other parts of the business with informal training and demos</li>
<li>Build and maintain strong relationships with all aspects of the business</li>
<li>Aid business users in resolving unusual problems related to enterprise applications (e.g., Finance not understanding all the results of a new special report)</li>

<div>
<p>Summary</p>
<div><p>
The Senior Business Analyst will report directly to the VP of Product and Strategy Management and is a strategic role that will liaise between all aspects of the Business and the Technology organizations. This position will be a subject matter expert and thought-leader in the company to drive revenue and reduce operating expense with new technology, product solutions and services. The ideal candidate can translate between business and developer speak, has experience as a business and systems analyst, is a creative problem solver and can help lead our transformation from legacy technology to a next-generation payment processing platform.</p></div>
