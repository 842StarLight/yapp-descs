<div><b>PURPOSE OF THE JOB</b></div>
<div>
This role is part of the Composites Infrastructure Business and is accountable for performing daily inbound and outbound transportation tasks and carrier related communications for the Blythewood manufacturing facility. The TSS (Transportation Support Specialist) will assign daily freight to the Owens Corning carrier base to meet delivery dates of customer orders in the most cost-effective manner. The incumbent will collaborate very closely with Customers, Carriers, Blythewood Rebar team, Customer Service Specialists, Material Planners and other OC organizations as necessary to identify and troubleshoot issues to ultimately deliver our product on-time and damage free to a growing business. This role will require the TSS to embrace and actively display the Owens Corning values, stands and purpose.</div>
<div>
A key competency for the successful candidate will be a demonstrated history of enrolling and influencing others across a new organization to drive superior results. The successful candidate must have exceptional analytical and communication skills, with a predisposition for initiating action.</div>
