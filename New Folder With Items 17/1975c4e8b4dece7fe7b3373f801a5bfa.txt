<h2>Physician Contract Specialist - Managed Care Contracting</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Physician Contract Specialist is responsible for negotiating and executing Physician, hospital and/or other provider contracts and agreements for the organizations capitated HMO population.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>The Physician Contract Specialist is also responsible for negotiating and executing Letters of Agreements, specialty agreements and ancillary contracting (purchased services) as may be applicable for a patient that Hoag Hospital or one of their affiliates may have a risk for.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Job Functions:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Identify and recommend potential provider contracting strategies to reduce costs, limit financial risk and ensure adequate provider network to service members.</li>
<li>Negotiate provider agreements (physician, hospital, ancillary) within the financial and strategic guidelines established.</li>
<li>Review and analyze proposed contract terms and conditions of physician contracts and amendments to determine which terms are acceptable and which terms require further negotiation.</li>
<li>Monitor contract performance against negotiated terms and conditions.</li>
<li>Maintain a consistent timeline for evaluating contracts and renegotiations.</li>
<li>Monitor medical group utilization of non-contracted providers and develop action plans as needed to re-educate or obtain contracts</li>
<li>Monitors and updates contracts required to comply with state and federal law, regulatory or plan requirements.</li>
<li>Maintain physician contract database (SharePoint) to ensure contracts are archived and available to Hoag Executive Leadership as applicable.</li>
 <li>May assist with identifying and recruiting providers based on network composition and needs.</li>
<li>Innovates improves and problem solves to further the goals and achieve the operating and strategic plans of the organization. Identifies opportunities for physician contract improvement.</li>
<li>Performs other duties as assigned.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Education, Training &amp; Experience:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Required</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of 5 years experience in managed care operations within a hospital, IPA or health plan environment with atleast 3 years of experience in contract negotiations.</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree in Business Administration or related field or an equivalent combination of education and experience.</li>
<br>
</ul>
