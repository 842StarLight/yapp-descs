<h2>Presales Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Vista is looking for technology professionals with 6 or more years of engineering experience to join our sales team. Put your skills to use and advance your career by joining a growing technology company with vast presales, marketing and technical resources to help enable your success! Be on the front lines of helping customers and our experienced Account Managers leverage Vista's experienced team, logistical resources and successful vendor relationships.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
POSITION RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Answer Account Manager and customer technical questions related to server, storage, network, service and software solutions</li>
<li>Design and deliver custom server, storage, network, services and software solutions to our customers</li>
<li>Work closely with our technicians and technical resources to resolve technical/support issues</li>
<li>Manage customer account satisfaction from a technical perspective</li>
<li>Interface with customers at a technical level to improve and accelerate sales cycles</li>
<li>Achieve and maintain necessary certifications</li>
<li>Continue professional development and knowledge of IT products and solutions</li>
<li>Provide structured technical sales training to Vista Account Managers</li>
<li>Willing to occasionally travel as needed</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
EDUCATION &amp; EXPERIENCE</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Minimum 6 years’ experience with proven technical sales success a plus</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree from accredited institution or preferred</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
DESIRED SKILLS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Microsoft office including Excel, Power Point, Word, and Visio</li>
<li>Relevant industry certifications such as A+, Network+, Server+, Storage+, VSP, VTSP, CSE</li>
<li>Ability to execute exceptional consultative sales practices</li>
<li>Exceptional communication skills</li>
<li>Strong presentations skills on the phone, in person, and in group settings</li>
<li>Interpersonal and customer service skills and ability to translate technical terms into non-technical language</li>
<li>Analytical/troubleshooting, organizational skills and ability to resolve hardware and software issues</li>
<li>Documentation and reporting skills</li>
<li>Flexible and can adapt in a fast-paced, dynamic environment</li>
<li>Organized with the ability to maintain time management and expenses</li>
<li>Highly motivated and the ability to work independently and within a team environment</li>
<br>
</ul>

VMware ESXi</li>
