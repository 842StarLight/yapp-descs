<p><b>Principal Duties / Responsibilities</b></p>
<div><ul>
<li>Become the product subject matter expert on assigned product lines by understanding the market, supply chain, technical product details and sales channels.</li>
<li>Create business plans for each product line with short-term and long-term product line strategies and defined sales and margin goals for each brand.</li>
<li>Develop primary positioning and messaging for product lines and brands.</li>
<li>Maintain integrity of the brand promise.</li>
<li>Maintain database of competitive intelligence, including pricing and features analysis and recommendations to support the development of strategic plans and programs for assigned product lines.</li>
<li>Participate in developing marketing budgets for each brand.</li>
<li>Lead product launches with sales, marketing and operations personnel.</li>
<li>Participate in sales calls and perform product demonstrations.</li>
<li>Preserve trademarks and registrations by monitoring competitive products.</li>
<li>Provide support regarding all aspects of the product life cycle.</li>
<li>Provide direction to Marketing on all sales tools: literature, displays, websites, video, promotions, packaging, installation instructions, press releases etc.</li>
<li>Work with plant management and legal department to create warranties when applicable.</li>
<li>Train salespeople and customers on products feature/benefits and differentiation attributes.</li>
<li>Collaborate with product development on development of new products.</li>
<li>Performs other duties as required.</li>
</ul></div>

<div><b>Position Overview</b></div>
<div>
<p>The Product/Brand Manager is responsible for planning, developing and directing marketing efforts for assigned product lines and brands. Coordinate activities of production, sales, advertising, promotion and packaging to maximize sales of products lines and brands.</p>
<p>
This position works under general supervision; relies on experience and judgment to plan and accomplish goals; and requires a wide degree of creativity and autonomy to perform the job.</p>
</div>
