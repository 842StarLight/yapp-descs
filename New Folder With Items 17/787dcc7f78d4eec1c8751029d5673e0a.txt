<h2>Marketing Director (My Gene Counsel)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Job Description<br>
<br>
<p>The <b>Marketing Director</b> will be responsible for overseeing the company’s brand marketing, Internet marketing, and sales support functions, and will be a key contributor toward all product definition and specification efforts with an eye toward optimizing customer adoption, activity and satisfaction. He/She will be a confident, self-starter with excellent communication and interpersonal skills, and passionate about technology and its ability to impact peoples’ lives. Candidates should be comfortable creating and then executing to marketing calendars and measuring marketing performance on a frequent basis. Candidates will have demonstrated experience leading multichannel marketing campaigns that drove product demand and were executed within a firm budget and will have managed internal teams as well as multiple third party vendors. Motivating and getting the best out of the internal team and vendors will be essential. Given the importance if this position and the breadth of responsibilities, candidates must be comfortable working and being available outside of traditional business hours and must be available for meetings at our New Haven offices at least three times per week.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Join a fast-paced, and rapidly growing company and work with a diverse, highly motivated team in a dynamic industry that involves genetics, personalized medicine, and direct to consumer healthcare.<b> My Gene Counsel</b> is a disruptive digital health care subscription based platform that saves lives. Our online platform provides genetic counseling information for individuals and families at risk for genetic diseases, and keeps our customers informed of all relevant developments associated with their genetic test results.</p>
</div>
<br>
<div>Qualifications<br>
<br>
<p><b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Lead all facets of brand marketing aggressively with an emphasis on demand generation. Constituents to include potential customers, partners, and fellow My Gene Counsel colleagues. Responsibilities include but are not limited to:</li>
<li>
Developing and managing the branding messages for the company in a way that properly demonstrates the value the company and its products provide</li>
<li>
Developing and maintaining the content on all online properties, including the company web site and any landing pages, blogs, and social media sites</li>
<li>
Overseeing QA for all marketing materials before send-out, ensuring all copy is correct, and that all links, videos, and buttons are operating and functional.</li>
<li>
Work with the company’s Founder/CEO to drive the company’s public relations strategy and making sure it aligns with and drives the product and sales goals of the company. Oversee the calendar for and execution of all press releases, speaking engagements, press opportunities, corporate newsletters, and advertising.</li>
<li>
Overseeing the creation of any corporate collateral, brand names or logos.</li>
<li>
Assist as needed on any corporate presentations, such as speeches or investor decks.</li>
<li>
Lead all facets of Internet marketing with an emphasis on achieving customer acquisition goals at desired cost per acquisition. Continuous improvement of all conversion metrics will be key, and this role will work closely with finance team to review all metrics and will be expected to use best practices to test different approaches to maximize performance. Responsibilities include but are not limited to:</li>
<li>
Ensuring that the company’s web site, brand marketing, and social media efforts are maximizing organic search results.</li>
<li>
Overseeing all paid Search Engine Marketing and online advertising efforts, managing toward a targeted cost per customer acquired. This includes determining where, how and when to spend marketing dollars and designing the most effective Internet ads.</li>
<li>
Track, understand and inform the rest of the company about when and how our customers use our product to better inform product development, marketing, and customer communication efforts.</li>
<li>
Coordinate, conduct, analyze and summarize user focus group sessions or feedback to improve the product.</li>
<li>
Support sales and new revenue in the following ways:</li>
<li>
Develop and manage any sales collateral such as brochures, PowerPoint decks, marketing emails, white papers, customer testimonials, videos, and case studies.</li>
<li>
Oversee any demand generation programs that in addition to a coordinated multichannel brand marketing effort may include email marketing, direct mail, and telemarketing.</li>
<li>
Manage any trade show participation, including the exhibit space and booth design, event communications, speaking opportunities, giveaways, sponsorships, and any pre and post event analysis</li>
<li>
Assist as needed with any RFPs or other proposals</li>
<li>
Oversee any campaigns to measure customer satisfaction or improve customer retention</li>
<li>
Contribute to decisions regarding the product roadmap, feature prioritization, and product pricing based on what will most increase customer demand and retention and what will most lower customer acquisition cost.</li>
<li>
Report closely to the Leadership Team on progress, problems, and final decisions in campaigns and projects and closely oversee marketing agencies, interns, and other marketing staff.</li>
<li>
Create a center of excellence with your team that serves as an example of enthusiasm and partnership to the rest of the company.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
 <b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
BS/BA with 5+ years of experience in a supervisory marketing role for brand marketing, Internet marketing, and sales support</li>
<li>
Experience with marketing both B2C and B2B2C products a must. Experience with subscription products and/or healthtech businesses a plus.</li>
<li>
Solid knowledge of SEO, SEM, and Google Analytics</li>
<li>
A self-starter with ability to work in a fast paced environment bringing new ideas and methodologies into the business</li>
<li>
Demonstrated experience managing and nurturing an internal team as well as third party vendors</li>
<li>
Excellent communication skills, keeping the CEO and Team up-to-date on ongoing projects and reporting urgent issues in a timely fashion.</li>
</ul>
