<b>The Role:</b><br>
To lead SnapLogic’s future success, we seek to hire a Chief Marketing Officer (CMO) to help set and adjust the strategic direction of our company and our associated marketing programs (positioning, messaging, digital marketing, customer acquisition/retention, content, social media, SEO, and all customer/partner marketing). This position is reporting to the CEO and is accountable for achieving company objectives with regard to revenue, customer acquisition, expense management, and profitability. It requires cross-functional collaboration with multiple internal and external business stakeholders including members of the board and agencies to drive incremental sales, customer engagement and loyalty. This role requires a strong balance of project management ability, communication skills, analytical thinking, brand expansion, and creative problem solving with the ability to influence and introduce innovative and impactful marketing programs.
<br><br>
<b>What You’ll Do:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Identify marketing strategies and compelling campaigns and partnerships to accelerate revenue growth, drive traffic to our website, and increase sales pipeline, customer acquisition, and retention rates.</li>
<li>Identify market opportunities for growth based on customer feedback, market data, and analyst or industry leader input.</li>
<li>Develop KPI’s and marketing reports for the executive team based on annual marketing objectives that demonstrate contribution to new and add-on revenue goals.</li>
<li>Lead cross-functional campaigns for product launch or demand generation and specify messaging and offers as well as desired outcomes like responses, MQL and ROI.</li>
<li>Identify best practices and opportunities to optimize messaging and content to maximize campaign effectiveness and share key insights with across the organization and members of the board.</li>
<li>Improve existing, and identify new, acquisition channels including PR/AR, paid search engine marketing, (SEM), search engine optimization (SEO), account-based marketing (ABM), and affiliate marketing.</li>
<li>Leverage third party channels and data to implement online and offline programs to broaden reach and grow our customer and prospect databases.</li>
<li>Lead our customer/partner marketing program including overall strategy, communication planning, analysis and optimization.</li>
<li>Monitor CRM/MA trends and innovations across all industries including competitors to identify business opportunities.</li>
<li>Create and manage quarterly and annual budgets along with headcount requirements working closely with our VP of Finance and Administration.</li>
<li>Lead our social media strategy and expand social media presence by identifying new channels and opportunities.</li>
<li>Collaborate with Development and Product Marketing teams to provide input to the product roadmap in order to take advantage of market opportunities.</li>
<li>Lead, manage and develop direct reports and create an inspiring environment that results in improved productivity, team collaboration, creativity, and results.</li>
<li>Partner with CEO and HR leaders to support company culture initiatives.</li>
</ul>
