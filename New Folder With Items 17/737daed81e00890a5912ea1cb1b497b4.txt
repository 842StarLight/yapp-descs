<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>ISYS Technologies delivers emerging technology solutions through our diverse and talented employees who are dedicated to our customers’ success. We empower our teams, contribute to our country and operate responsibly. We are a reputable award-winning WOSB providing Engineering and Enterprise Information Technology (EIT) services to the Federal government. Headquartered in Colorado, ISYS serves key national customers throughout the United States with a presence in more than 16 states.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<p>
We are seeking a Program Manager to be responsible for managing and supporting our accounts to maximize growth through a structured alignment of resources, coordination of the customer facing teams, and internal stakeholders.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Cultivating relationships with key customers and decision makers through stakeholder meetings and client site visits</li>
<li>
Planning and reporting requirements associated with all stages of our contracts with the Federal Government Client</li>
<li>
Providing project management support through budget monitoring, schedule development, scope management, and stakeholder communications management to guide projects toward completions</li>
<li>
Managing and supporting our employees at client locations</li>
<li>
Partnering with recruiting team in reviewing and interviewing candidates along with onboarding new candidates</li>
</ul>
