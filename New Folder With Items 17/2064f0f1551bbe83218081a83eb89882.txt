<h2>Warehouse Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>
<p>We're looking for the best and brightest to take our Warehouse department to the next level. If you have passion and expertise in distribution, shipping and receiving, forklift operation, or picking and packing, UBC Group USA is the company for you. We offer competitive pay, full benefits, great and fun work environment, career opportunities within the company. We are looking for a long-term employee.</p>
<p><b>
POSITION DESCRIPTION</b><br>
</p>
<p>The Warehouse Associate is responsible for storage, picking, assembling, handling, shipping and control of products being inventoried and sold.</p>
</div>
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>
Position Type and Classification:</b> Full-time position/Non-Exempt.<br>
<br>
<b>Hours:</b> Monday- Friday, 8:30am – 5:00pm (may be switched to 7:00am – 3:30pm during summer heat). Infrequent over-time stays are needed during high season, paid accordingly.</p>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Location: </b>O’Fallon, MO.</p>
<p><b>
Training: </b>In-house training is provided.</p>
<p><b>
Probation and Orientations Periods: </b>3 months.<br>
</p>
<p><b>DUTIES AND REPSONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<p>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Develops and maintains knowledge of products.</li>
<li>Develops and maintains knowledge of stock location within the warehouse.</li>
<li>Receives and puts up/stores materials being inventoried in a safe and appropriate manner.</li>
<li>Participates in warehouse organization. Assists in the general maintenance of the warehouse.</li>
<li>Records damaged or defective materials and immediately reports it to designated team members for proper handling.</li>
<li>Product repairs and servicing.</li>
<li>Inbound and Pre-sale inspection of beverage equipment.</li>
<li>Assemblies of various products as needed.</li>
<li>Picks and packs orders for shipping.</li>
<li>Obtains shipping quotes. Prepares bills of lading for shipment.</li>
<li>Verifies outgoing shipments for accuracy.</li>
<li>Loads and unloads material from truck in an accurate, efficient, and safe manner, utilizing proper body mechanics and proper equipment (e.g., forklift).</li>
<li>Assures proper documentation accompanies all material being loaded onto or unloaded from the truck.</li>
<li>Greets and interacts with customers in a cheerful and helpful manner.</li>
<li>Completes stock checks for the Purchasing Department as requested.</li>
<li>Reports suspicious activities immediately to a member of the management team.</li>
<li>Follows all safety requirements and procedures.</li>
<li>Delivers and picks up merchandise locally as needed.</li>
<li>Performs other duties as requested or required to successfully complete the job.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>SKILLS AND QUALIFICATIONS:</b></p>
<p>
Attention to Detail, Results Oriented, Strong Work Habits, Team Player, Problem Solver, Organized, Product Knowledge, Customer Service, Dealing with Complexity.</p>
<p><b>
MINIMUM ESSENTIAL QUALIFICATIONS</b></p>
<p>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Authorized to work in the United States for any employer (please do <b>NOT</b> apply if you are <b>NOT</b> authorized to work in the United States for any employer).</li>
<li>No criminal records and/or drug issues in the past.</li>
<li>High school diploma or equivalent.</li>
<li>Basic familiarity with the use of a personal computer, telephone, printer, scanner.</li>
<li>Understands measurements and measurement tools including use of scales, tape measures and micrometers.</li>
<li>Valid Driver License.</li>
<li>Ability to learn quickly and thrive in a fast-paced environment.</li>
<li>Strong attention to detail.</li>
<li>Commitment to and demonstration of high ethical standards governing professional behavior and interactions.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to communicate clearly and concisely, especially verbally.</li>
<li>Ability to work well alone and in a team environment.</li>
<li>Strong customer service orientation.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>DESIRED QUALIFICATIONS</b></p>
<p>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Experienced Forklift Operator.</li>
<li>One-year experience working in a warehouse environment (general labor roles).</li>
<li>Technical skills.</li>
<li>Higher education degree/technical degree.</li>
<li>Experience of using QuickBooks software (or similar).</li>
</ul>
<p><b>PHYSICAL REQUIREMENTS</b></p>
<p>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to sit, stand, and walk for prolonged periods of time.</li>
<li>Ability to bend, climb, push, and pull, including the ability to maneuver at heights and in tight or small places.</li>
<li>Ability to routinely lift, carry, and otherwise transport work-related materials that frequently weigh up to 75 lbs. and that may occasionally weigh in excess of 75 lbs.</li>
<li>Vision abilities: close vision, distance vision, color vision, peripheral vision, depth perception and ability to adjust focus.</li>
</ul>
