<h2>Software Engineer - Third Party Tools</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Linode is looking for a software engineer to join our team to develop integrations between our platform and Infrastructure as Code cloud automation tools.</p>
<p>As a Software Engineer on our 3rd Party Tools (3PT) team, you'll be building products and services that enable our customers to manage their Linodes using solutions like Terraform, Chef, Ansible, and Salt. You'll get a chance to ease the accessibility of our services through third-party platforms and open source projects. This position sits at a really interesting intersection of our business: you'll have an active voice in shaping our open source community, help sales technically evaluate business partnerships, and build tools that will be internally useful.</p>
<p>We're a highly collaborative team with a focus on agile development and innovative solutions. We encourage and reward curiosity, ingenuity, and technical skill. If you enjoy working on ambitious projects that have a huge impact, you'll enjoy working at Linode!</p>
