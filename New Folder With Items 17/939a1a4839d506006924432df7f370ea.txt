<div><b>Executive Director of Engineering</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
About Us</b></div>
<div>
Built by some of the most innovative, experienced people in the business, FreeWheel Advertisers’ platform powers the advertising campaigns for over 7,500 media buyers at 1,200 agencies and advertisers across the U.S. and Europe, processing more than $50bn per year. Our scalable, open architecture gives our clients the flexibility to handle all types of media – from print to TV to digital. As part of FreeWheel, a Comcast Company, we provide the technology, data enablement, and marketplace integrations to allow buyers and sellers to transact across all screens, all data types, and all sales channels.</div>
<div>
As we expand into new markets and further develop our product suite, we are looking for a Director of Software Engineering to join our team in our Chicago HQ.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
About the Opportunity</b></div>
<div>
We are looking for an experienced leader to provide strategic technical direction, executive oversight and strong execution leadership across our agile engineering teams. You will work in coordination with product leadership to align on business objectives and establish a strategy for execution. You’ll ensure best practices are set and that appropriate metrics are in place to track performance and progress. You'll have the responsibility of day-to-day management of a group of technical architects and engineers of varying levels (entry-level to senior), spread across multiple agile product teams. You’ll be expected to be able to communicate well with all parts of the business, including senior leadership, and be as comfortable explaining and advising on deeply technical issues as you are on organizational management, resource and budget planning, to successfully deliver against the business strategy. As a leader within the Engineering organization you’ll be expected to be highly technically proficient and able to both empower and inspire others. Your role will primarily be based in Chicago, but you will work closely with our global teams with offices in New York, London, Paris and Singapore.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Responsibilities</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Maintaining a healthy, motivated, and effective team</li>
<li>
Assisting the members of your team with defining and achieving their individual performance goals and development plans, as well as objectives for the group as a whole</li>
<li>
Maintaining a technical roadmap that provides organizational level vision and strategic guidance to our Product and Engineering teams</li>
<li>
Evangelizing engineering best practices and other company initiatives</li>
<li>
Driving Key Performance Indicators across all areas of the Engineering function to ensure that all projects are delivered on cost and on time to the highest quality standards.</li>
<li>
Working closely with our heads of Product and Engineering to deliver game changing technology against an ambitious Product roadmap</li>
<li>
Serving as a liaison between your office location and the engineering staff and senior management in our other locations around the world, as well as facilitating effective communication between the members of your own team</li>
<li>
Taking the lead with the teams when issues arise, providing guidance, and proactively leaning in to help the teams avoid this</li>
<li>
Lead in the engineer interview and hiring process in Chicago</li>
<li>
Using the resources we provide to continuously upskill and better yourself, as well as mentor engineers within the team and help them grow</li>
<li>
Establishing the organizational structures that enable us to scale our engineering to match our global ambitions, and playing an integral role in resource planning</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
About You</b></div>
<div>
The linchpin of a high-functioning engineering team is its leader, and you will play a key role in building and maintaining such a team. Our engineers love a challenge, strive for continuous improvement, and seek to make world-class software – and your job will be to make them achieve their highest potential as individuals and as a group. Successful candidates for Director of Engineering should have the following:</div>
<ul>
<li>
14+ years' experience managing successful engineering teams, across geographies and timezones</li>
<li>
Flawless communication skills in English, both written and spoken</li>
<li>
Understanding of web-based, service-oriented software architecture, ideally using a modern Microsoft technology stack. Direct experience of the following will all be beneficial: C#, SQL Server, NServiceBus, ReactJS</li>
<li>
Demonstrated evidence of understanding organizational goals and successfully managing the trade-offs between speed, quality and long-term implications.</li>
<li>
Evidence of leading an engineering group in successfully delivering large scale projects. Experience working on global projects is required</li>
<li>
Proven experience in successfully leading a large scale transformation of legacy products/code to a modern platform architecture while meeting business objectives.</li>
<li>
Experience with negotiating and managing large offshore teams, augmenting FTEs</li>
<li>
Developed and implemented team metrics and reporting capabilities for transparency of engineering activities, alignment to roadmaps, and ability to manage multiple product efforts simultaneously</li>
<li>
Working experience as a software engineer, ideally in an enterprise business environment</li>
<li>
Knowledge of and experience with agile development methodology</li>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related technical field</li>
</ul>
