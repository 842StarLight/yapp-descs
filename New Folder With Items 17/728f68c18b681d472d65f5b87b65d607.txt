<h2>Field Service Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Provide specialist level support within the Nordson ASYMTEK Field Engineering &amp; Technical Support Departments for both internal &amp; external customers:</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
Field Service Engineering: Installations, billable services, and warranty.</li>
<li>
Technical Support: working directly with customers.</li>
<li>
Product Support: interfacing with new product teams.</li>
<li>
Training customers and fellow service engineers.</li>
<li>
Applications Engineering: developing and implementing solutions.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
Flexible and able to travel 50%.</li>
<li>
Provide installation services and customized training to customers.</li>
<li>
Document customized lists of spare parts.</li>
<li>
Provide application engineering services in either an installation or billable services environment.</li>
<li>
Confidently troubleshoot any problem remotely or on-site and stay with the issue to resolution.</li>
<li>
Provide customers with quotes for billable services and implement solutions with their dispensing equipment, application, and training.</li>
<li>
Respond to incoming requests for technical assistance originating via phone or email.</li>
<li>
Enter cases in the CRM system and actively manage the case and solution workload.</li>
<li>
Assist in the global technical support environment to provide direct support to both internal and external customers.</li>
<li>
Responsible for collecting technical information &amp; documents relating to new products, options, and features, that are being shipped within the region.</li>
<li>
Prepare for field visits to understand every option and feature of an order and be prepared to make customers successful with the product in every aspect of their ownership experience.</li>
<li>
Recover from field visits by closing any open issues.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with applications, software, electrical, factory, integration &amp; mechanical design engineers and technicians as resources for troubleshooting and delivering solutions to customers.</li>
<li>
Keep current with the latest Application practices, challenges, and best known methods.</li>
<li>
Support commercial success in the pre-sale environment by defining technical solutions and ensuring delivered solutions meet customer expectations</li>
<li>
Serve as technical-commercial liaison supervising complex projects, reviewing customer specifications, and setting product performance expectations.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Education and Experience Requirements</b></p>
<ul><li>
Requires BS degree in Mechanical or Electrical Engineering.</li></ul>
<p><b>
Preferred Skills and Abilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Good communication skills and the ability to deal effectively with customers.</li>
<li>
Ability to travel 50% of the time.</li>
</ul>
