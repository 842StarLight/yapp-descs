<h2>Box Office Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Job Summary:</p>
<p><b>
Unity in Diversity
</b></p>
<p>Our team members are as diverse as our music venues. With its owned/operated/managed venues, Live Nation Clubs and Theaters, a division of Live Nation Entertainment, is wide-ranging, including the legendary Fillmore venues and the intimate House of Blues clubs throughout the United States. Live Nation Clubs and Theaters is the country’s preeminent group of intimate music venues, featuring superior sound and lighting technology in one-of-a-kind custom-designed environments aimed to bring fans and artists together in unparalleled musical environments.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
Our mission is to create a profitable and principled global entertainment company. To celebrate the diversity and brotherhood of world culture. To promote racial and spiritual harmony through love, peace, truth, righteousness and non-violence. Our venues bring fans as close to artists as possible and our aim is to create teams that are just as close.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
The Role:</b></p>
<p>
The Box Office Manager is responsible for all aspects of ticket office operations and phone system administration.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
What You’ll Do:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>I. Supervise the Department</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Assist and support box office staff in any of their job functions as needed</li>
<li>Maintain positive and creative team environment within the department</li>
<li>Facilitate open communication with the supervisors and staff</li>
<li>Facilitate proper inter-departmental communications and organization</li>
<li>Conduct staff evaluations (training, 90-day, annual). Set development and performance goals and monitor progress</li>
<li>complete and satisfactory on-going staff training</li>
<li>Ensure appropriate staffing levels. Recruit and hire staff and supervisors and uphold hiring/disciplinary/termination standards that adhere to all Live Nation Clubs &amp; Theaters guidelines.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
II. Financial Performance</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for financial settlement and accountable for all revenue collected from ticket sales and ancillary income</li>
<li>Conduct band settlement on shows with split point deals, or when deemed necessary by talent department</li>
<li>Complete and distribute accurate daily ticket counts</li>
<li>Responsible for scheduling and labor cost control</li>
<li>Balance and reconcile all seller cash drawers</li>
<li>Track all service charge and ancillary income</li>
<li>Organize department and financial information for presentation at weekly management meetings with Corporate Director of Ticketing</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
III. Other Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Create, modify and maintain inventory for all ticketed events in a computerized ticketing environment</li>
<li>Modify, maintain and update all customer database files</li>
<li>Maintain ticketing computer systems</li>
<li>Primary representative from Live Nation Clubs &amp; Theaters in all venue interactions with Ticketmaster</li>
<li>Coordinate lunch and dinner restaurant reservations with operations as needed</li>
<li>Develop and maintain superior customer service both in-person and over the phones</li>
<li>Develop and maintain call center phone system, and keep current all phone menus and calendars of events</li>
<li>Support phones system for the administrative offices</li>
<li>Supervise brunch sales/reservations</li>
<li>Organize nightly will call tickets</li>
<li>Interact with artist, record label, management and band tour accountant</li>
<li>Ensure an accurate and timely flow of show, dinner, gospel brunch and general venue information both intra-departmentally, and to the general public</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
What You’ll Need:</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Required:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>2 Years of Box Office/Ticketing Management experience</li>
<li>High School Diploma</li>
<li>Flexible Schedule (days/nights, weekends, and holidays)</li>
<li>Tolerance of all cultures, music and art forms</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Preferred:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>College Degree</li>
<li>Experience verifying and recognizing valid identification</li>
<li>Experience in a live music environment</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Physical Demands/Working Environment:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Working environment is fast-paced and often loud and stressful</li>
<li>Must be able to lift or move up to 75 lbs using proper lifting techniques</li>
</ul>
