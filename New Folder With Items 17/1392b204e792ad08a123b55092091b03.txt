<h2>Senior Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div><b>Position Objective</b></div>
<div>
Responsible for the servicing and marketing needs of Lockton clients under the direct supervision of the Unit Manager/Team Lead and in accordance with Lockton procedures.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Essential Duties</b></div>
<ul>
<li> Effectively manage service/project plan timeline for each client as developed by the Account Executive</li>
<li> Support Account Executive in the scheduling and preparation of meetings</li>
<li> Request and manage receipt of all renewals from carriers</li>
<li> Prepare and manage “Line of Coverage and Revenue Summary” for assigned accounts</li>
<li> Prepare RFPs for bid solicitation</li>
<li> Manage carrier/vendor responses and questions during bid solicitation</li>
<li> Clarify questions with carriers and Lockton Account Executive</li>
<li> Review and summarize proposals in Presentation Master template</li>
<li> Notify carriers in a timely manner of final decisions once client has made final decisions</li>
<li> Initiate and manage all administration activity relative to implementing a new carrier</li>
</ul>
<div><ul>
<li> Contract reviews</li>
<li> Implementation meetings/calls</li>
<li> Carrier introductions</li>
</ul></div>
<ul>
<li> Ensure all necessary contracts/documents are executed in a timely fashion</li>
<li> Review SPD, Certificates, Policies and Contracts for accuracy</li>
<li> Prepare annual compliance calendar for clients</li>
<li> Provide updates and responses to employer compliance issues/questions as needed</li>
<li> Ensure internal compliance issues are addressed, including but not limited to:</li>
</ul>
<div><ul><li> Service Agreements</li></ul></div>
<div><ul>
<li> Broker of Record Letters</li>
<li> Business Associate Agreements</li>
<li> Compensation Disclosures</li>
</ul></div>
<ul>
<li> Prepare routine claim summary/agg reports</li>
<li> Prepare and manage the employee communications material, including but not limited to:</li>
</ul>
<div><ul>
<li> Enrollment Guides</li>
<li> Annual Enrollment Meeting PowerPoint Presentation</li>
<li> Announcements</li>
<li> New Hire Guides</li>
</ul></div>
<ul>
<li> Conduct employee meetings as needed</li>
<li> Prepare initial claim projections when appropriate</li>
<li> Work cooperatively with Financial Analysts and/or Health Risk Solutions team</li>
<li> Assume additional responsibilities from Unit Manager/Team Lead/Account Executives as appropriate</li>
<li> Ability to operate independently with clients on certain initiatives to relieve or create capacity for Team Lead or Account Executive</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
<div><b>Qualifications</b></div>
<ul>
<li> Bachelor's degree or equivalent education and/or experience</li>
<li> Five+ years’ working knowledge of employee benefits or related experience</li>
<li> Experience in marketing and servicing the employee benefit programs</li>
<li> Organizational and time management skills to prioritize heavy workloads to meet time sensitive deadlines</li>
<li> Strong knowledge of Microsoft products (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook) or similar software applications</li>
<li> Develop and maintain strong working knowledge of Presentation Master</li>
<li> Demonstrated analytical and problem-solving skills</li>
<li> Demonstrated interpersonal communication skills and ability to interact with Associates at all levels of responsibility</li>
<li> Strong public speaking skills to present during Enrollment Meetings</li>
<li> Willingness to delegate as appropriate</li>
<li> Demonstrated understanding of when to proceed alone and when to involve the Account Executive, Unit Manager, Producer, and others</li>
<li> Demonstrated interpersonal communication and interaction with company personnel for favorable relationship management and account outcome</li>
<li> Demonstrated professional, tactful negotiation and persuasion skills to achieve objectives</li>
<li> Managing workflows proactively without AE interaction and in accordance with QA guidelines, and demonstrated initiative to support AEs working in tandem for an efficient and effective product outcome</li>
<li> Ability to present proposals to clients in a professional manner</li>
<li> Ability to coach and mentor other Associates</li>
<li> Ability to consult clients on other Lockton services and insurance needs</li>
<li> Ability to analyze quotes for coverage adequacy</li>
<li> The desire to take on a leadership role both internally and externally at Lockton</li>
<li> Personal presence to interface with insurance carriers’ representatives</li>
<li> Ability to travel by automobile and aircraft, and stay for more than one day and night</li>
<li> Firm, fluent grasp of English language with above average verbal, written, and interpersonal skills to interact with Associates at all levels of responsibility</li>
<li> Legal right to work in the United States</li>
</ul>
