<h2>Senior Consultant - Point of Sale</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Manhattan designs, builds and delivers market-leading supply chain technology solutions for leading companies around the world. We help drive the commerce revolution with unmatched insight and unrivaled technology, connecting front-end revenue and relationships with back-end execution and efficiency—optimized on a common technology platform. This platform-based approach is enabling leading companies across the globe to Push Possible® by getting closer to customers and achieving real-world results.
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>Senior technical point of contact throughout the implementation of multiple supply chain solutions software packages. Provides senior level design for client specific enhancements, proactively troubleshoots and resolves issues that arise during the implementation, and updates and maintains the project issues list, which includes prioritizing the issues. Works with customers to test and implement interfaces between MA products or external systems and works directly with the customer project team during configuration, testing, training, and implementation.
<br>
<br>
MINIMUM REQUIREMENTS
</p>
<ul>
<li>Four-year bachelor’s or foreign equivalent degree in computer science, engineering, business or related field
</li>
<li>3 years of experience working in a client or customer facing role within a supply chain or software organization
</li>
<li>Up to 50% travel required
</li>
<li>1 year of experience with database troubleshooting (SQL)
</li>
<li>3 years of experience deploying software
</li>
</ul>
