<h2>Accountant - Revenue</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Aethon Energy Operating LLC (“Aethon”) is a privately owned Dallas-based energy company with operations in Wyoming, Texas, and Louisiana. The company has a successful acquisition and development strategy, building on a 25-year track record of success, and has strong financial footing. Aethon is looking for a talented and motivated person to contribute to our continued success and growth.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>At this time, Aethon is accepting applications for the <b>Revenue Accountant </b>position at our headquarters located in <b>Dallas, Texas.
</b></p>
<p><b>POSITION SUMMARY:</b><br>
</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>The Revenue Accountant is primarily responsible for the processing of revenue from oil and gas sales including royalty distributions and preparation of ONRR and State tax reports.
</p>
<p><b>DUTIES &amp; JOB RESPONSIBILITIES:</b>
</p>
<ul><li>Prepare revenue sales allocation schedules in Excel as needed to process revenue</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>Prepare ONRR, Wyoming, Louisiana and Texas tax reports</li>
<li>Update gas imbalance schedules</li>
<li>Book non-operated revenue sales information into Enertia accounting system</li>
<li>Process state severance tax</li>
<li>Report Federal royalties</li>
<li>Perform account reconciliations for revenue, royalty and tax accounts</li>
<li>Complete revenue general ledger account reconciliations and journal entries</li>
<li>Complete other special projects and assignments as needed</li>
</ul>
