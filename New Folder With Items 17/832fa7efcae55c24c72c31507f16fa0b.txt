<p>Installation Technician I, eCamSecure</p>
<p>Houston, TX</p>
<p><b>JOB SUMMARY: </b>Design, install, service, test and maintain all Mobile Surveillance Units (MSUs), CCTV, alarm and related equipment.</p>
<p>If you are interested in this position please follow the link:</p>

<p></p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:</b></p>
<p>Understand and communicate the seriousness and responsibility of protecting CPS customers whether that protection is engendered with security officers or video surveillance.</p>
<ul>
<li>Work with eCam operations personnel to ensure that all projects are completed on time and on budget.</li>
<li>Assure that MSUs and commercial sites under maintenance agreements are in perfect working order, clean and properly documented. Conduct preventative maintenance on a weekly basis.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Assist subcontractors, other eCamSecure ™ installers, project coordinators, engineering manager and sales persons with site surveys.</li>
<li>Maintain regular updates with eCam staff.</li>
<li>Prepare daily activity reports.</li>
<li>Maintain high level of knowledge in the eCam and related technologies.</li>
<li>Prepare and maintain MSU and job site inventory records.</li>
<li>Maintain a good, positive relationship with clients.</li>
<li>Assure that Account Executives and eCamSecure ™ personnel are informed of any and all issues on customer sites.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>SECONDARY FUNCTIONS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Transport MSUs to, from and around customer sites.</li>
<li>Conduct reviews of eCam video files as requested by operations; assure that eCamSecure ™ operations is aware of all requests to review video files.</li>
<li>Assist with assigned eCam projects.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>EDUCATION/EXPERIENCE:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma, plus vocational training or equivalent.</li>
<li>One year’s experience in CCTV or alarm installation.</li>
<li>Technical background in CCTV, access control and security; understanding of basic electricity (low voltage) and electronics.</li>
<br>
</ul>
