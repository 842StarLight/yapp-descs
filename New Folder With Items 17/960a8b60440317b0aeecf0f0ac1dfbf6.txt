<h2>Business Development Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>POSITION OVERVIEW</b></p>
<p>Webbula seeks a candidate with an entrepreneurial mindset who loves a fast-paced, fun environment and loves to win. This person enjoys working within a team yet takes full ownership of their individual role and goals. Our company has an ambitious growth goal that requires a lot of hard work, mental toughness, and grit. Those who thrive in this type of environment will earn substantial rewards, both monetarily and in a sense of accomplishment in building something of their own. This person must challenge the status quo, get people to act when doing nothing is an option.</p>
<p><b>JOB DESCRIPTION</b></p>
<p>A Business Development Executive (BDE)is responsible for meeting or exceeding expected revenue targets generated from new logos and current customers as assigned. One will use strong prospecting, online presentation, and closing skills to both self-generate and convert marketing sales leads into a minimum of new meetings per week resulting in closed-won revenue per month supporting the overall revenue target. In addition to excellent prospecting, negotiating, and closing skills, the BDE must utilize good account management skills leveraging a CRM tool to maintain current long-term client relationships.</p>
<p>Sales cycles and client needs vary. So, one must have the ability to shorten the sales cycle while having the skills to increase average revenue per invoice and close business in a larger more complex sales process at the enterprise level.</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES &amp; RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Strong closing skills to meet and exceed established sales &amp; revenue goals.</li>
<li>Develop and drive a strategic business plan to create new revenue by building and managing a pipeline of customer prospects from identification to negotiation to execution. You will be expected to aggressively develop your own prospects, supplemented with direct leads/prospects for development.</li>
<li>Identify prospective client’semailHygiene and Data Append marketing needs and present solutions that meet those needs. Through consultative selling, you will outline an ongoing multi-product solution to increase ROI for your prospects.</li>
<li>Build a workable and forecastable pipeline of business- moving each opportunity through the Webbula sales process.</li>
<li>Write effective proposals, which address the needs of the prospects and outline a profitable solution for both prospect and Webbula.</li>
<li>Provide valuable contribution to the account planning process to ensure the successful development of clients.</li>
<li>You are expected to be “in the market” and a visible force at tradeshows, conferences and other industry/social events.</li>
<li>Meet or exceed aggressive initiatives and timelines. You must have a strong sense of urgency.</li>
<li>Fully document engagement with clients in Salesforce and other Webbula systems including activities, needs, services discussed, services provided, pricing, timelines, budget, etc..</li>
<li>Be well organized, detail-oriented, and able to delegate responsibility as needed.</li>
</ul>
