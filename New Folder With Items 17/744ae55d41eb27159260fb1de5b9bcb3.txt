<h2>Partner Manager, Channel Partnerships</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">We're looking for a Partner Manager, Channel Partnerships to join our Partnerships &amp; BD team.
<br><br>
mParticle is the customer data platform for the world's most respected brands. We are helping businesses across a variety of verticals orchestrate amazing, customer-first experiences. Our clients include marketing and engineering functions for some of the largest apps in the world and our platform processes nearly one third of the world's smartphone traffic. We care about building a company that not only has the best solution on the market, but also provides the white glove experience to our customers and partners alike.
<br><br>
We are looking for a Partner Manager, Channel Partnerships to accelerate the growth and adoption of the mParticle Platform within a select group of Channel Partners by driving joint training, enablement and relationship-building initiatives with the primary objective of generating leads. The Partner Manager, Channel Partnerships will play a key role in mParticle's growth strategy ensuring that Digital Media Agencies, Consultancies and Mobile Development Shops endorse mParticle as the defacto CDP for D2C Enterprise Brands. The Partner Manager, Channel Partnerships will create market awareness by evangelizing to and enabling our partners, ultimately creating Win (Customer) - Win (Partner) - Win (mParticle) situations that accelerate our ability to secure jointly identified target accounts. The Partner Manager, Channel Partnerships will craft, deploy, execute and manage an array of tactics and strategies where the goals and associated KPIs are net new pipeline development leading to measurable ARR impact.
<br><br>
From day to day, the Partner Manager, Channel Partnerships will engage with key partner stakeholders across multiple functions at varying seniority levels. This requires the individual to have a dynamic skill-set that ensures the partner walks away from every interaction with the impression that mParticle is constantly setting a new standard of excellence for what it means to be a preferred technology partner.
<br><br>
This individual will need to be strategic and leverage influence to drive outcomes in situations where they have little direct control. As conflict inevitably arises, the Partner Manager must have an emotional intelligence and innate confidence to turn conflict into opportunity, ultimately forging deeper and more trusting relationships with our partners. The individual should be a challenger who is proactive and tenacious, eagerly rolls up their sleeves to drive the outcomes needed to achieve their Pipeline Generation goals.
<br><br>
<b>What you will do:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>The primary responsibility of the Partner Manager, Channel Partnerships will be to generate new leads from our partner's customer base and to ensure partners positively influence in-flight deals.</li>
<li>Ensure that our partners have a best-in-class experience as an mParticle partner. Act as the day-to-day contact for key Channel Partner contacts, ensuring timelines are met, partners are receiving the support and resources they need to influence deals in mParticle's favor, and ensure that our partners have a best-in-class experience as an mParticle partner and are successful in our joint business goals.</li>
<li>Establish, maintain and grow relationships with those businesses best positioned to provide leverage in mParticle's ability to meet our revenue and retention goals.</li>
<li>Collaborate cross-functionally with our sales, customer success, solutions and engineering teams to create Win-Win-Win scenarios and be the champion for your partners both internally and externally.</li>
<li>Champion programs that create further opportunities for engagement with partners and customers (e.g. rules of engagement processes, joint messaging, campaigns)</li>
<li>Develop a strong understanding for the different segments in our ecosystem, key players, competitors, the goals of their business, functionality provided, value of joint solutions.</li>
</ul>
This role might be for you if you have
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Experience generating new business through inbound and outbound opportunities</li>
<li>You are fluent in technology, and a bonus if you have had exposure to mobile marketing and growth stack technologies</li>
<li>You know your customer profiles inside and out</li>
<li>You solve problems and understand how to demonstrate the true value of a product</li>
<li>You are comfortable in a high growth environment -- in fact you thrive on it</li>
</ul>
About mParticle
<br>---------------
<br><br>
Founded in 2013, mParticle is the leading customer data platform that unlocks the full power of data for businesses. The company empowers brands to accelerate their growth strategy to keep pace with their customers by providing the most advanced data platform for web and apps across all devices in the marketplace. A trusted partner among renowned brands such as NBC Universal, Spotify and Airbnb among many others, the mParticle platform has grown to manage over 1 billion mobile users each month, capturing over $5 billion in e-commerce transactions and processes over 250 billion API calls. Recognized as one of Crain's 100 Best Places to Work three years in a row and named to Gartner's "Cool Vendors in Mobile App Development" list, mParticle has 100+ employees and is headquartered in New York City with offices in San Francisco, Seattle, Florida, and London.</div>
