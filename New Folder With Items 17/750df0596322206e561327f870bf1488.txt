<div><b>Position Objective</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>GENEX is seeking a Reproductive Program Technician in <b>Wausau, WI </b>to provide personalized A.I. service and breeding program assistance to customers that will result in improved profit potential for them.</div>
<div></div>
<div>This position is <b>90% dairy, 10% beef</b>.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Major Areas of Accountability</b></div>
<div>
<ul>
<li>Provide reliable service and sales support to the Breeding Program Specialists by completing all farm records accurately, maintaining and developing high non-return rates, and develop heat detection skills and an understanding of synchronization programs</li>
<li>Provide reproductive management expertise to members that will improve reproductive performance in their herds</li>
<li>Represent GENEX in a professional manner while carrying out daily activities</li>
<li>Assist in achieving desired herd and profit improvement for customers</li>
<li>Develop market analysis and support the direct sale of semen and Genex products</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Qualifications</b></div>
<div>
<ul>
<li>Associate's or Bachelor's Degree in Agriculture or equivalent practical experience and knowledge is preferred</li>
<li>Must be an aggressive sales and service person who is self-motivated with the ability to work independently and as part of a team</li>
<li>Experience in heat detection and synchronization programs is helpful</li>
<li>Must be dependable with the desire to develop personally and professionally</li>
<li>Strong communication and people skills is required</li>
<li>Have strong computer skills is required; ability to work with herd management software is a plus</li>
<li>Preference will be given to applicants with previous A.I. and large herd experience</li>
<li>Must have the ability to pay attention to detail and your surroundings to adhere to safety protocols</li>
<li>Applicants must have a valid driver's license for this position</li>
</ul>
