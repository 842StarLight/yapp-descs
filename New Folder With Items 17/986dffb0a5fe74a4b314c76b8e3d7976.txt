<div><b>Position Summary</b></div>
<div>The Senior Revenue Analyst will work closely with other Finance staff, as well as inter-departmentally, to ensure accurate recognition of revenue in accordance with ASC 606. This position will play a key role in our month-end close process, including preparation of journal entries and account reconciliations. Their primary responsibility will be ensuring that we are recognizing revenue within our required guidance. This position will play a key role in reporting revenue, and will also assist in commissions processing.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>What You Will Get To Do:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Perform revenue accounting allocations and deferrals to sales orders in Revstream and SAP systems, based on established revenue accounting guidance and internal control policies</li>
<li>Become familiar with the new Revenue Recognition guidance outlined in ASC 606 and prepare for implementation of the new standard</li>
<li>Make recommendations to improve efficiency and accuracy within the revenue accounting and reporting process</li>
<li>Perform monthly/quarterly SSP analysis for all business units, products, and geographies</li>
<li>Review underlying SSP data and make recommendations for compliance improvement, where needed</li>
<li>Perform monthly/weekly/ad hoc reporting of revenue and deferred revenue, with focus on process, systems and data improvement</li>
<li>Monthly reconciliation of all systems, to ensure completeness and accuracy of bookings, billings, and revenue data</li>
<li>Assist in monthly accounting close procedures, including journal entries and balance sheet reconciliations</li>
<li>Assist with Services revenue accounting, as needed</li>
<li>Assist in preparation and analysis of Sales Commissions</li>
<li>Focus on improvement and operating effectiveness of internal controls</li>
</ul>
