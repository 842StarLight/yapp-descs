<b>About SuperLumin Networks</b><br>
SuperLumin provides sophisticated Web and Enterprise Content Delivery Network (eCDN) solutions. We are part of the STRATACACHE family of companies which focuses on digital signage, media displays and marketing solutions. Our network technology increases network performance, reduces congestion by decreasing the overall volume of content moving over the network. Our technologies identify frequently requested data and stores or caches it locally. Caching eliminates the need to repeatedly transmit the same content over and over again from the web server through the cloud to the client improving web content delivery and performance.<br>

<b>Marketing Manager - SuperLumin</b><br>
The STRATACACHE family of brands is seeking a Marketing Manager of an advanced expertise level for the SuperLumin brand and company to join our Salt Lake City- based team. This position requires a detail oriented, creative, highly motivated and self-directed individual with a strong technology marketing background, who can manage multiple projects and deadlines simultaneously and be an active contributor within a fast-paced, flexible, and fun marketing team in our offices in the US and Europe.<br>
<br>
Reporting to the Marketing Director of STRATACACHE (based in the Philadelphia suburbs), the Marketing Manager will work closely with the Salt Lake City-based Senior Sales and Management team of SuperLumin alongside the centralized STRATACACHE marketing team. The marketing manager will collaborate with Digital Marketing, Product Marketing, Communications teams, and other internal subject matter experts to develop and execute marketing campaigns and initiatives to generate leads and increase brand identity &amp; awareness with the goal to increase revenue and market share.<br>
