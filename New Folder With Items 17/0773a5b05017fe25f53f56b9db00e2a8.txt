<h2>Retail Channel Operations Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Position Summary
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Role and Responsibilities
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>Our mission in Retail Operations is to make things easier for our in store teams so they can invest more time with the consumers that experience Samsung mobile products every year. As a Leader of Retail Channel Operations you will own the operational strategies, processes, and tools that enable our teams to deliver premium consumer experiences at scale, across all retail formats around the country.
<br>
<br>
Leading a team of Operations Managers dedicated to assigned Channel accounts, you will collaborate with all Retail Account Channels, Marketing, Design, Merchandising, Technology, Service, Finance, HR, Field, and Strategy teams at both Global and North American levels to bring operational execution and services to life in our stores that elevate the Consumer Experience while improving productivity and consistency to each account and Samsung.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Together we make the impossible possible. Because at Samsung, we Own the Unknown.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Are you ready to #OwnTheUnknown? Join us.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>If you have ambitions to be a part of a Best in Class sales organization, Samsung’s Carrier Sales Account is the place to be. We use deep consumer insight, experience, and market-driven innovation to develop new product and solution concepts to sell out in retail. Bringing new hardware, software and services to market, we work directly with key product planning groups for each business unit for our customer to get Samsung products into users’ hands. We provide new ways to bring Samsung products to market at one of the globe’s leading carriers.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The dynamic culture at Samsung offers both great challenge and great reward. If you want to work among the very best talent in the industry, working on the most innovative products in the world, Samsung is the place to be.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Position Summary:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>As the Channel Operations Manager, you will be the operational voice of our accounts internally.
</p>
<p>You will collaborate with all Retail Account Channels, Marketing, Design, Merchandising, Technology, Service, Finance, HR, Field, and Strategy teams at both Global and North American levels to bring operational execution and services to life in our stores that elevate the Consumer Experience.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Role and Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>You will partner closely with the carrier cross functional teams to ensure tools and resources function correctly. In addition, you will be instrumental in the annual business planning processes, influencing operational-strategy roadmaps and process improvement planning in support of current and long-term business plans.
</li>
<li>You will work directly with corporate retail account teams both locally and nationally to help set the operational process and measure the overall execution of Samsung’s success.
<br>
You will partner in the creation of operational materials that drive continuous improvement and establish key benchmarks and performance measures to assess operational effectiveness with key accounts.
</li>
<li>You should exhibit a proven ability to convey complex ideas in simple, effective ways that influence, inform and educate audiences at all levels in the organization.
</li>
<li>You will interact with Executive Leadership across Samsung, globally and within cross functional areas, to help move Samsung’s retail experience forward. This will include being a fierce advocate for the team, driving repeatability, stability, and intuitiveness into everything we do, so that they can focus on serving consumers instead of trying to use inefficient tools/processes. Additionally, you will be required to explain complicated, detailed, and quickly changing facts to Samsung’s retail leadership team in a succinct, easy to understand manner.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Skills and Qualifications:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree required
</li>
<li>6+ years of relevant retail experience required
</li>
<li>Demonstrated experience working with multiple accounts and operations
</li>
<li>Strength in working cross functionally, harnessing a collaborative team environment and being able to lead project work within a large dynamic team and drive end to end planning
</li>
<li>Adaptability to move at the pace of consumer and work in a constantly evolving environment, making decisive decisions when needed
</li>
<li>Experience in developing process and creating measurements
</li>
<li>Understanding of Retail development and store systems/equipment highly desired
</li>
<li>Proficiency in Power Point, Word, and Excel
</li>
<li>Outstanding written and communications skills, time management, and organization skill
</li>
<li>Ability to drive and improve business cadence (planning, budgeting, business reviews), setting and improving processes, monitoring execution and driving accountability and visibility of progress against goals and objectives
</li>
<li>Assess and provide insights on operational market opportunities for various channels, geographies and service levels to boost sales performance including informing go-to-market strategies, improvements to our account/internal team’s capabilities and performance, and product enhancements
</li>
<li>Ability to travel approximately 15-25% of the time
<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Necessary Skills &amp; Attributes:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop and maintain excellent working relationships with all assigned levels within and outside the company.
</li>
<li>Plan, organize, and prioritize multiple assignments and projects.
</li>
<li>Review detailed product development, marketing documents and media materials as needed.
</li>
<li>Demonstrated competency in both oral and written communication modes for both internal and external personnel at various levels.
</li>
<li>Work independently and in a team environment in order to achieve customer business objectives, personal and team goals and complete assignments within established time frames and specifications.
</li>
<li>The ability to develop tasks and work assignments, based on sometime vaguely defined objectives.
</li>
</ul>
