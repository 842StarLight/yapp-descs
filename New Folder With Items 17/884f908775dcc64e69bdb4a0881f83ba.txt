<p><b>SUMMARY</b></p>
<p>This position is responsible for providing exceptional customer service to current and potential customers. Accurately processes financial transactions and maintains cash accuracy in cash drawer. Cross-sells bank products and services by looking for sales opportunities while assisting customers and non-customers.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>SUPERVISORY RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<p>None.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul><li> Provides exceptional customer service to current and potential customers in a professional manner. Processes customer transactions accurately and efficiently and responds to customer inquiries in an efficient, timely and courteous manner. Uses down-time on Teller line advantageously.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Meets balancing standards, maintains cash drawer limits, and exercises processing and proofing practices that ensure accuracy. Follows teller cash counting rules, such as counting cash three times.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Maintains sound practices regarding fraud and follows all procedures to ensure necessary precautions are taking place to prevent loss to the bank (i.e. monitoring alerts on accounts; checking for proper endorsements, stop payments, holds, etc.) Communicates with other staff, remaining aware of customer or non-customer activity which could put the bank at risk.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Has knowledge of bank products and services and is able to educate customers on basic checking and savings accounts. Actively looks for opportunities to cross sell products and services including making referrals to meet customer needs.</li></ul>
<br>
