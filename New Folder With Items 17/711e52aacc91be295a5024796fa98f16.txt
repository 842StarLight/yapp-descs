<p>
Eagle Creek has over 2,500,000 hours of project experience spanning over 300 engagements. It is one of the only companies in the world with the experience and capabilities to successfully deliver multi-million dollar engagements remotely. <p><b>
Duties</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Participate in the development of Salesforce applications based on client needs for integration, implementation, and customization projects</li>
<li>Mentor and train, formally and informally, Eagle Creek’s Jr. and mid-level resources</li>
<li>Foster and maintain development and integration standards, best practices and related deliverables with the EC team</li>

<li>Additional duties as required</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p><b>
</b></p>
<p><b>Required Technical Skills</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in computer science, related field, or equivalent</li>
<li>3+ years experience with end to end Salesforce.com implementations, integrations, and enhancement projects</li>
<li>2+ years APEX and VisualForce development for custom Salesforce functionality</li>
<li>Experience integrating business processes across disparate systems and multiple Salesforce instances using web services, SOAP, XML, REST, MSMQ, workflow, XML, HTML, UML and JavaScript</li>
<li>Development of custom objects, fields and formulas, custom reports, and dashboards</li>
<li>Integration experience with other Salesforce Applications and External systems</li>
<li>Development of SOAP and REST based web services used for custom development</li>
<li>Solid understanding of SaaS and CRM development processes and methodologies</li>
<li>Create supporting technical documentation and detailed design specifications</li>
<li>Develop and maintain validation rules and custom workflows</li>
<li>Salesforce development certification (highly desired)</li>
<li>Hands-on development skills</li>
<li>Knowledge about OOTB functionality and the ability to discern when to configure over code</li>
</ul>
