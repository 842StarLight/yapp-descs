<h2>Office Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<p><b>Date: </b>Sep 12, 2019
</p>
<p><b>Location:</b> Kansas City, MO, US
</p>
<p><b>Company: </b>Mobile Mini
</p>
<div>
<div><div>
<div><div>
<p>The primary focus of this position is to provide administrative support and assistance to the branch and Branch Manager. Work directly with the yard staff to support sales and yard efficiencies. Work with the Branch Manager to assist in defining and implementing office/administrative procedures and ensure that Mobile Mini provides superior internal and external customer relations.</p>
<p><b>
Principal Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Assist the Branch Manager in maintaining and promoting a positive working environment.</li>
<li>Promote “Safety First” culture in all areas of branch.</li>
<li>Adhere to all Mobile Mini procedures/paperwork.</li>
<li>Supervise yard operations and yard team:</li>
</ul>
<p>1. Work order processing</p>
<p>
2. Parts and Container Inventory control</p>
<p>
3. Direct parts ordering</p>
<p>
4. Work with sales team to provide inventory availability</p>
<ul>
<li>Communicate with customers to assist in scheduling service orders and delivery items.</li>
</ul>
<p>1. Inform customers of delivery/pick-up times</p>
<p>
2. Answer customer questions about delivery/pick-up</p>
<ul>
<li>Coordinate and perform office services, such as:</li>
</ul>
<p>1. Time Keeping/Payroll</p>
<p>
2. Accounts Payable</p>
<p>
3. Accounts Receivable</p>
<p>
4. Purchase Orders</p>
<p>
5. Filing</p>
<p>
6. Reception</p>
<p>
7. Clerical Functions</p>
<ul>
<li>Ensure all daily/weekly/monthly paperwork is completed, recorded and filed.</li>
<li>Complete purchasing processes for the branch to include stock for the Yard, office supplies and trucking purchase orders.</li>
<li>Work with Credit Control/Purchase Ledger to resolve customer invoice queries.</li>
<li>Responsible for completing branch invoicing, including, but not limited to: sales invoices, relocations, damages and rebills.</li>
<li>Manage, assign and monitor office services for the branch including time keeping/payroll, accounts payable/receivable, purchase orders, filing, and reception.</li>
<li>Define and maintain office/administrative procedures.</li>
<li>May manage a team of Branch Administrative employees to assist with the administrative duties, if not, will be main point of contact for Branch administrative duties and responsibilities.</li>
<li>Be an active member of the Branch Leadership Team.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Equipment Used and Responsibility:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Computer, telephone, radio and electronic office equipment</li>
<li>MS Office Suite</li>
<li>GPS and tracking software</li>
<li>Salesforce</li>
<li>SAP</li>
<li>Decartes</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Supervisory Responsibility:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Yard employees and admin staff if applicable</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Education and Experience:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High School Diploma or equivalent, some college preferred</li>
<li>1-2 years’ experience dispatching preferred</li>
<li>Standard concepts of scheduling and dispatching</li>
<li>2-3 years of Customer Service Experience</li>
<li>Management experience preferred</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task effectively</li>
<li>Knowledge of state specific safety regulations (OSHA)</li>
<li>Attention to detail and ability to problem solve</li>
<li>Communicate clearly: verbal &amp; written</li>
</ul>
