<h2>Mechanical Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Simmers Crane Design &amp; Services is a leading, highly technical and growing overhead crane service company. We are searching for a highly motivated Entry Level <b>Mechanical</b> <b>Engineer</b> for the Rock Island, IL area. We are a fast paced, heavily customer service based, organization. Our projects range from a 250# hoist repair to million-dollar control and capacity upgrades and modernizations. We work in many different industries such as steel, automotive, power generation, etc. The Mechanical Engineer provides engineering support through data collection in the field, and creates drawings, designs and calculations as required. This position will perform various “reverse” engineering work as well as project management of total engineered systems.</p>
<p><b>Job Responsibilities for the Mechanical Engineer: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Calculations for design and analysis of crane supporting structures, affiliated components, and buildings evolving around steel and concrete design</li>
<li>Coordinate with sales team to propose work</li>
<li>Coordinate and manage project from start to finish</li>
<li>Direct and supervise subcontractors and technicians to complete projects</li>
<li>Planning and preparation of material orders and delivery</li>
<li>Maintain deadlines and budgets to demands of the customer</li>
<li>Ability to collect and interpret data</li>
<li>Abide by safety policies of Simmers Crane Design and Service, the customer, and OSHA standards</li>
<li>Prepare documentation such as invoicing and project closeout</li>
<li>Act individually and in a group environment</li>
<li>Manage time to work on multiple projects at one time</li>
<li>React in a timely matter to problem solve stressful situations</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Mechanical Engineering or equivalent engineering degree</li>
<li>Preferred experience in engineering, and project management</li>
<li>Strong sense of organization and attention to detail</li>
<li>Strong technical knowledge and computer literate; FEA software (Finite Element Analysis) Microsoft Word, Excel, Outlook.</li>
<li>Strong communication and customer service skills</li>
<li>Must have the ability to work in an office environment as well as in the field</li>
<li>Travel Required</li>
<li>Ability to work inside, outside in all types of weather, and work from heights</li>
<li>Must be eligible to work and free of any non-competition agreements.</li>
<li>Must be able to pass drug screen and background check.</li>
<li>Simmers Crane Design &amp; Services is an Equal Opportunity Employer</li>
</ul>
