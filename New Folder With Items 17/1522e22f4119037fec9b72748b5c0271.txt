<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Job Description</b></h2>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
This position is responsible for increasing the effectiveness of Thrivent’s field directors in the area of financial professional recruitment. The individual in this position is a thought leader who is responsible for field director skill development, one-on-one coaching, recruiting joint field work, recruiting training, and execution of Thrivent’s recruiting playbook. The Recruiting Leader is knowledgeable about industry trends and best practices. This individual also influences through others, and partners closely with many home office and field stakeholders, including: Field Leader Development, Regional Vice Presidents, Regional Financial Office (RFO) staff, Human Resources, and other home office leaders.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>This position can be remote/located anywhere within the United States.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Responsible for leading the development of a strategy to upskill field directors across the enterprise on the attraction and selection of high-quality financial professionals (FPs) candidates.</li>
<li>
Recruiting Leader spends the majority of their time proactively upskilling leaders of all tenure levels using a variety of leadership styles and approaches, including one-to-one coaching sessions, on the ground joint field work, accountability conversations, role play, and formal training sessions.</li>
<li>
Responsible for leading the development of critical recruiting skills, including:<ul>
<li>
Telling the Thrivent Story</li>
<li>
Asking for introductions/referrals</li>
<li>
Conducting effective Career Overview Meetings</li>
<li>
Conducting effective Center of Influence meetings</li>
<li>
Describing the financial professional career opportunity</li>
<li>
Developing a geographic/demographic market growth strategy</li>
<li>
Talent attraction/lead generation strategies</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
Establish credibility and trusted relationships with field directors and regional vice presidents and diagnose recruiting strengths and opportunities.</li>
<li>
Partner closely with the Leadership Development team, RFO Business Consultants, local Regional Vice Presidents and Career Network leaders to ensure a consistent and effective approach to recruiting consulting and development.</li>
<li>
Obtain and communicate industry recruiting trends and best practices.</li>
<li>
Lead additional recruiting initiatives as needed.</li>
<li>
Provides work direction to the Career Network Recruiting Sr. Specialist.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>
Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>
College degree required; graduate level business degree preferred.</li>
<li>
Proven success in a financial services field leadership role</li>
<li>
Proven success recruiting and leading independent contractors</li>
<li>
Professional experience of at least 5 years in distribution, marketing or sales required.</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to influence others from across the organization, including executives and others at higher levels.</li>
<li>
Prior success negotiating “win/win” arrangements with strategic partners and vendors.</li>
<li>
Understanding of different distribution channels for financial products and services.</li>
<li>
Demonstrated success in achieving results.</li>
</ul>
