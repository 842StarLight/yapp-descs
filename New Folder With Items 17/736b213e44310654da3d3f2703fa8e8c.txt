<p><b>Job Description Summary:
</b></p>The Account Manager is responsible for the overall management of one or more Dedicated Contract services (DCS) accounts within a geographic region. The Account Manager looks to optimize the company's assets by monitoring revenue and costs through effective P&amp;L analysis. Other essential duties include building and maintaining customer satisfaction through problem solving, communication and relationship building. This position has supervisory responsibilities for onsite managers, support personnel and drivers and is usually located on site at the customer's facility.
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Description:
</b></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Serves as the main point of contact with their assigned customers in order to provide exceptional service and trouble free account operation.
</li>
<li>Makes regular site visits to observe the accounts for safe, efficient and profitable operation.
</li>
<li>Responsible for the selection, hiring, training &amp; development of all office personnel and drivers.
</li>
<li>Monitors driver safety and takes proactive steps to create a positive Safety Culture at their accounts. Ensures drivers have completed required periodic company safety training.
</li>
<li>Conducts P&amp;L analysis to determine trends in costs to maximize profitability.
</li>
<li>Ensures customer billing and employee payroll are accurate and timely.
</li>
<li>Coordinates with Maintenance personnel to balance equipment maintenance schedules, customer requirements, and expenses. Ensures all equipment meets Federal and state requirements.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree from a four-year college or university and 2 years transportation related experience and/or training
</li>
<li>Or five years transportation experience
</li>
<li>Or a combination of education and experience.
</li>
<li>Candidates should also have working knowledge of transportation supply chain management and prior P&amp;L responsibility.
</li>
<li>Ability to speak effectively before groups of customers or employees of organization.
</li>
</ul>
