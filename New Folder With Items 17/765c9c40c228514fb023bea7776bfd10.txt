<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>About this Position
</b></h2>
<p></p>The DevOps Manager will manage the DevOps team that develops and maintains the build pipelines and continuous integration tools; as well as being responsible for the cloud infrastructure for databases, services and applications.
<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities &amp; Qualifications
</b></h2>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop, implement, and manage the software development lifecycle build, deployment, and release processes in order to achieve Continuous Integration / Continuous Deployment (CI/CD) objectives
</li>
<li>Ensure compliance to Company policies, processes, procedures and security requirements within the development life cycle; as well as enforcement within the pipeline as necessary
</li>
<li>Develop, implement, and manage the automation of testing, security scanning, and approval workflow activities
</li>
<li>Lead, grow, and maintain a team of DevOps Engineers
</li>
<li>Keep a customer focused mindset, prioritizing developer productivity and application uptime
</li>
<li>Champion DevOps processes and procedures across the organization.
</li>
<li>Prioritize build tools and automation to accelerate software development and increase code quality.
</li>
<li>Provide high-level system monitoring with an understanding of reliability, availability, and failure management/mitigation needs.
</li>
<li>Ensures required logging and proactive availability and performance monitoring solutions are in place.
</li>
<li>Selects, develops, implements, supports, and manages all aspects of the DevOps practice.
</li>
<li>Participates in strategic and tactical planning for Business initiatives and projects that have DevOps involvement and create roadmap with milestones. • Demonstrate ownership of problems, issues, or requests from start to finish and track through resolution
</li>
<li>Participates in the budgeting process to make recommendations for future acquisitions related to DevOps
</li>
<li>Communicate, present, and champion ideas concerning areas such as the build process, tools, and cloud service providers in a productive and diplomatic fashion
</li>
<li>Develops and maintains continuation of business (COB), Disaster Recovery and failover plans.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL SKILLS &amp; EXPERIENCE
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>5+ years DevOps or highly related experience, including 3+ years managing teams and projects
</li>
<li>Understanding of source control, branching and merge strategies and experience with source control tools such as SVN, GitHub , Artifactory Pro, Nexus etc
</li>
<li>Working knowledge of virtualization technologies, such as VMware and Containers / Kubernetes.
</li>
<li>Working knowledge of Configuration Management tools such as Ansible, Terraforms.
</li>
<li>Working knowledge of Build and Integration systems, such as Jenkins, VSTS, Maven, Ant.
</li>
<li>Familiarity with Deployment and Release management tools
</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal skills, with the ability to work well with subordinates, peers and management.
</li>
<li>Ability to work under pressure and adapt quickly to shifting priorities
</li>
<li>Possesses excellent verbal and written communication skills
</li>
<li>Sets team goals then develops plans, assigns responsibility, and sets timelines to achieve those goals.
</li>
<li>Provides regular status reports to management concerning team activities.
</li>
<li>Work collaboratively with peers, management, executives and members of other business units
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>A Bachelor’s degree or equivalent experience
</li>
<li>A degree major in an Information Technology discipline is preferred.
</li>
<li>Minimum of 5 years work experience in Information Technology
</li>
<li>Azure experience strongly preferred
</li>
<li>Participation in major system projects with proficiency in at least one development discipline/platform
</li>
</ul>
