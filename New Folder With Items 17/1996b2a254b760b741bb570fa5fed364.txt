<h2>Marketing Manager - Graphic Design</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Business Unit:</b> Corporate
<br><br>
Summary
<br>Under direction of the Director of Marketing, the Marketing Manager is responsible for assisting in developing and directing the company’s marketing and brand development activities that are in alignment with the company’s vision and sales/growth goals.
<br><br>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities
<br><b>Duties may include the following:</b> (other duties may be assigned)
<br><ul>
<li>Participate in the development of branding and implementation of corporate brand identity strategies and initiatives.</li>
<li>Ensure sales team has the appropriate promotional collateral, presentations, etc. that enable them to meet their sales objectives.</li>
<li>Oversee branding and visual identity of the business including signage, asset branding, promotional materials and related vendors.</li>
<li>Design, create, and distribute print ready materials as necessary including sell sheets, media kits, presentation slides, mock-ups, signage, swag and more.</li>
<li>Build and manage advertising and communications assets including copywriting, design, layouts and more as necessary for each campaign.</li>
<li>Work with social media manager to direct programs that improve social media reputation and recognition.</li>
<li>Design and setup regular email campaigns, manage leads, setup nurture automation, and report on performance.</li>
<li>Oversee the company websites and provide analytics reviews.</li>
<li>Perform market research related to upstream oil and gas and relevant industry segments.</li>
<li>Coordinate community events and involvement.</li>
<li>Manage digital library of marketing assets.</li>
</ul>
Qualifications
<br>To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill, and/or ability required:
<br><ul>
<li>Minimum of 5 years marketing experience.</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree.</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft applications including Excel, Word, PowerPoint, etc.</li>
<li>Proficiency in Adobe InDesign, Photoshop and/or Illustrator with a thorough understanding of digital marketing and digital production.</li>
<li>Applicants must supply a strong portfolio of previous work.</li>
<li>Must have the ability to work well with people and be a team player.</li>
<li>Must be a self-starter and detail oriented.</li>
<li>Must possess good reasoning, analytical, and mathematical skills.</li>
<li>Excellent oral and written communication skills.</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task, prioritize, and manage time effectively.</li>
<li>Must be willing to travel.</li>
<li>Experience in photography and videography a plus</li>
<li>Experience in Salesforce a plus</li>
</ul>
Physical Demands
<br><ul>
<li>Ability to lift up to 25 lbs.</li>
<li>Involves sitting at a desk for extended periods of time.</li>
<li>Manual dexterity to operate specialized equipment.</li>
<li>Needs high degree of concentration in a busy area.</li>
</ul>
Work Environment
<br>This position works inside a climate-controlled environment in the corporate. The Company will supply adequate training and equipment to perform the functions of the job.
<br>Monday – Friday, 8am-5pm.
