<h2>Marketing Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Summary<br>
Broadway Across America’s Mountain Region presents 20-30 touring Broadway titles per year in Albuquerque, Boise, Salt Lake City and Monterrey, Mexico. The marketing assistant is a vital part of the team assisting the Director of Regional Marketing in the tactical implementation of advertising, promotions and public relations in these markets.<br>
<br>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities<br>
<ul>
<li>Executes advertising plans with direction from the Director of Regional Marketing.</li>
<li>Works with internal and venue partner accounting teams to manage client billing. Responsible for collection and creation of appropriate back up, accounting forms and reporting.</li>
<li>Prepares final advertising documentation for settlement with shows and partners.</li>
<li>Maintains communication with media reps, vendors, press agents, and venue partners.</li>
<li>Traffics all electronic, print and interactive ads for all engagements with the creative team in New York City.</li>
<li>Assists in creating and maintaining planning calendars for multiple aspects of single ticket and season ticket marketing.</li>
<li>Interacts with ticketing staff on promotional and trade ticket requirements. Assists in communications with ticketing team about marketing initiatives.</li>
<li>Facilitates approval process with shows and our New York City headquarters.</li>
<li>Maintains email blast schedules with venue partners and facilitates any outside email blast requests from local arts groups; works with Director of Regional Marketing on creation of content.</li>
<li>Maintains brand consistency across all marketing collateral.</li>
<li>Assists Group Sales and Sponsorship teams in execution of collateral and promotional needs.</li>
<li>Supports, with guidance of Director of Regional Marketing and Regional Executive, a healthy partnership between BAA, venue partners, and TMD in NYC.</li>
<li>Interacts with shows, partners and printers to coordinate proofs and approval of all show programs/playbills.</li>
<li>Attends partner marketing meetings/special committees to represent marketing team.</li>
<li>Assists with securing and execution of special events, such as cast parties, promotional events and subscriber promotions. Manages invitations, RSVPS and guest lists.</li>
<li>Attends all events and opening nights.</li>
<li>Works with TMD staff in NYC and shows to develop new partnerships and third party promotions.</li>
<li>Liaises with current grassroots promotional contacts and develops new partnerships for non-media promotional ideas</li>
<li>Assists the director of regional marketing with occasional press interviews or events, including escorting talent and overseeing interviews.</li>
<li>Maintains and/or coordinates all social media campaigns for the Broadway Series including consistent presence over all social media platforms and cross promotions; incorporates national Broadway and show updates to local Facebook, Twitter and Instagram accounts.</li>
<li>Writes copy and conceives posts with an keen sense of brand voice.</li>
</ul>
<br>
Qualifications and skills needed:<br>
Bachelor’s Degree in Communications, Marketing, preferred.<br>
Proficient in Excel and Word.<br>
Experience with digital marketing and social media platforms, KPIs, scheduling tools, tracking tools and analytics.<br>
Attention to detail, proof-reading, copy-editing skills<br>
Ability to work on multiple projects at a time with good time management skills in order to meet deadlines.<br>
Ability to take a positive team approach to working with coworkers.<br>
<br>
Special considerations:<br>
Must be willing to work nights and weekends as needed.<br>
Reliable transportation to and from venue is essential<br>
