<h2>Storekeeper (Order Picker/Shipping and Receiving)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>KHS USA Inc.</b>, a global leader in the packaging industry, has a career opportunity for an experienced Storekeeper. We offer challenging work with a congenial team. Our company has competitive wages and full benefits package including health, dental, vision, disability and life insurance, paid time off, holidays and 401K with matching contribution. Our employees enjoy a variety of duties, worldwide contacts, excellent training and development opportunities.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Summary:</b> The Storekeeper receives, stores, and ships materials and parts in KHS USA stockroom and compiles stock records in the Enterprise Resource Planning system.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>REPORTS TO: </b>Director of Inventory Control</p>
 <p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities include:</b></p>
 <p></p>
<ul>
<li>Pulls pick list, assembles parts for shipment; packages parts using boxes, packing materials etc.; creates correct shipping label and generates packing slip for invoicing.</li>
 <li>Counts and sorts to verify items on requisition or invoice;</li>
 <li>Enters data into ERP system; stores stock in correct location</li>
 <li>Receives purchase orders and shop orders</li>
 <li>Manually pull parts from stockroom for daily parts requirements; assemble parts in kit form for daily production requirements and deliver to appropriate area on production floor; support lead production personnel in requests for parts in a timely manner; Keeps accurate records</li>
<li>Processes and returns to correct location in the stockroom all production floor returns;</li>
<li>Performs inventory cycle counts and records results accurately; Inspect physical condition of warehouse and equipment.</li>
<li>Coordinates warehouse activities with activities of other functions such as production, parts sales, production control, and purchasing.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>The ability to work quickly and accurately in storing materials, selecting parts for production, supporting production needs, organizing stockroom and keeping records.</li>
 <li>Ability to operate a powered industrial truck safely.</li>
<li>Computer Literate and strong math skills</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Education &amp; Experience:</b></p>
 <p></p>
<p>High school or GED; six months related inventory experience; or equivalent combination of education and experience.</p>
