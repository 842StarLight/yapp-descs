<h2>Front of House Staff (Servers, Expo, Host, Busser...) Lunch & Dinner</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>SERVER<br>Provide friendly, responsive service to create an exceptional dining experience for all of our guests. Each server’s primary objective is to show our guests such a marvelous time, they will want to return again and again.</p>
<ul>
<li>Welcome and greet guests. Make all our guests feel comfortable and let them know you’re there to personally take care of them.</li>
<li>Inform guests of specials and menu changes.</li>
<li>Make recommendations you genuinely feel your guests will enjoy.</li>
<li>Answer questions about our food, beverages and other restaurant functions and services.</li>
<li>Take food and beverage orders from guests, enter orders in our point-of-sale system which relays orders to the kitchen and bar.</li>
<li>Deliver food and beverages from kitchen and bar to guests in a timely matter.</li>
<li>Perform side work at the start and end of each shift as required by service station assignment.</li>
<li>Maintain clean service areas.</li>
<li>Monitor and observe guests dining experience. Ensure guests are satisfied with the food and service. Respond promptly and courteously to any requests.</li>
<li>Prepare final bill, present check to guest, accept payment, process credit card charges or make change (if applicable).</li>
<li>Be ready and willing to assist fellow servers as situations arise.</li>
<li>Be ready and willing to assist busperson with clearing and resetting tables.</li>
<li>Thank guests for their visit and invite them to return.</li>
<li>Be available to fill in as needed to ensure the smooth and efficient operation of the restaurant as directed by the restaurant manager or immediate supervisor.</li>
</ul>
