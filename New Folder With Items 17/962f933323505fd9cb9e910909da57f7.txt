<h2>Field Office Corrdinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Rudolph and Sletten</b> is looking for a Field Office Coordinator to provide accounting, administrative and clerical support to the project team for the day to day activities on the project including: administration of subcontracts and all associated construction compliance, documents, and documentation; electronic processing of subcontractor and vendor invoices and payroll; job site safety within a fast paced environment. Refer to experience required below.</p>
<p><b>Supervisory Responsibilities: </b><br>N/A</p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Type, distribute, and electronically file all project correspondence originating from field office</li>
<li>Receive and distribute all correspondence at the site</li>
<li>Act as liaison for project; Respond to requests, answer phone and greet visitors</li>
<li>Work together with the Project Engineer to prepare and maintain all project logs including: shop drawings, submittal logs, equipment rental logs, change orders, back charges, and supplementals</li>
<li>Type subcontracts and monitor for signature</li>
<li>Monitor subcontractor insurance certificates for compliance with contract requirements and advise subcontractors of corrections needed</li>
<li>Prepare monthly billings utilizing field records and information provided by the accounting department.</li>
<li>Process monthly billings and subcontractor payments</li>
<li>Submit draft of all billings to Operations Accounting within required deadline</li>
<li>Provide accurate Owner billing within required deadlines</li>
<li>Demonstrated knowledge of the Owner payment procedure</li>
<li>Corresponds with Owner in accordance with contractual terms</li>
<li>Follow up with subcontract payables and subcontractors to ensure timely payment of subcontract work.</li>
<li>Assist field management team in carrying out safety program, including administration of safety meetings, accident reports, order first aid and safety supplies, and conduct new employee slide program safety orientation</li>
<li>Assist project management team with project close out documentation</li>
<li>Assemble and file necessary documentation for archiving</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Required Job Skills: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Working knowledge of control estimate and cost accounting to maintain Budget Summary and assist with preparation of the Monthly Project Status Report.</li>
<li>Ability to effectively communicate both verbally and written</li>
<li>Manage multiple tasks, produce quality work on time sensitive deadlines, while maintaining a positive attitude at all times</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Computer Equipment and Software: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Strong computer skills with the ability to use MS Office Suite with proficiency</li>
<li>Advanced MS Excel and Prolog Software skills required</li>
<li>Experience using Bluebeam, a plus</li>
<li>Adept in learning new software quickly and able to navigate and use to review, track, and upload various documents</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Education and Experience: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Associate’s degree preferred</li>
<li>Minimum five years’ experience; construction industry preferred.</li>
<li>Demonstrated knowledge and understanding of contract management.</li>
<li>Experience with sales tax computation.</li>
<li>Processing Union payroll experience.</li>
<li>Certified Payroll, plus!</li>
<li>AP and/ or bookkeeper experience required.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>We Offer</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Industry Leader Salaries</li>
<li>Extensive Benefits Package</li>
<li>Training, growth opportunities, and reimbursed education assistance</li>
</ul>
