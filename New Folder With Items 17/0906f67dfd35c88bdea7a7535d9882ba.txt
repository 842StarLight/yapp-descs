<h2>Yearbook Sales Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Jostens is seeking a sales professional with an entrepreneurial spirit who has the desire and ability to own and manage a business that provides great career rewards. These rewards include the incredible opportunity for you to work with students, parents, teachers, principals, administrators, coaches and school communities to provide the best experience in the industry.</p>
<p>As a Jostens independent sales representative you will be proud to represent the Jostens brand and share our values of accountability to our customer, always acting with integrity, responsibility and respect for those we serve.</p>
<p>To be successful in this role, you inherently understand that you are in control of your future. Your success will come by having great customer focus that connects with and supports the missions of organizations and people we serve. You are a leader and will develop great partnerships through your ability to offer the best products and educational programs in the industry. You will lead and embrace change that comes with Jostens commitment to continuous innovation and market leadership.</p>
<p>Jostens will offer you the best industry training experience to support your sales and business leadership success. This is a great opportunity to build a business backed by the unparalleled advantages of Jostens.</p>

<p>- The Industry's best products and educational programs</p>

<p>- Reliable, effective E-commerce systems</p>
<p>- Competitive compensation and incentive structure</p>
<p>- Personal satisfaction of having a direct, positive impact helping your local community of students, teachers, principals, administrators and educator</p>
