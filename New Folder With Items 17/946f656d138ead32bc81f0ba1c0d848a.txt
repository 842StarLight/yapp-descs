<p>Are you eager to tell everyone you meet all about the different types of skis you ride? Are you a powder hound who loves getting people into and set up with the gear that fits them best? What about laying trenches down perfect corduroy groomers with all your buds? you belong here!</p>
<p>The Bootfit Team Lead is responsible for the daily operations of the department and is a customer-facing position. This is a FT, hourly position that reports directly to the Location Managers.</p>
<p>Please review the job description below. If you want to join an awesome team and meet the requirements as a qualified candidate, submit your cover letter and resume now!</p>
<p>General description of Position</p>
<p>The Bootfit Team Lead is responsible for management and leadership of the Next Adventure Bootfit Program. This is a seasonal position with full-time hours, that oversees all of the customer bootwork for the winter departments. The Bootfit Team Lead is accountable to the Ski Department Team Lead and reports directly to the Location Managers.</p>
<p>Job Responsibilities</p>
<ul><li>Working closely with the Ski Department Team Lead and the Winter Sports Buying Team, you will lead the scheduling and managing of boot fit appointment process/program, product/supply needs, and assist with scheduling and delegating bootfitters’ appointments.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Influence Next Adventures 5 KPIs through add-on sales, promotions, sell-through strategy, conversion rate, marketing initiatives, and stock level management.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Assisting with development and execution of Winter Sports product assortment plans.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Identify opportunities to improve the customer experience through floor plans, product assortment, promotions, merchandising, etc.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Daily Progress Sheets – Support Ski Department Team Lead by creating, collecting, and communicating prog sheets and projects.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Supervise staff through team building and leadership to accomplish both short and long-term department goals set forth by Location Managers and Buying Team.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Basic aptitude in all winter departments.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Learn Retail Pro (RPro) and EasyRent functions.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Learn basic GSuite (Google) applications, Asana project management, as well as any other relevant software.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Assist with layaway and special order fulfillment and customer requests.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Opening and closing of Ski Department register</li></ul>
<ul><li>Other duties as assigned</li></ul>
<ul><li>Working with the Staffing Manager and Buying Team, organize and facilitate brand clinics for staff.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Work with Staffing manager and Location Manager to identify department training needs and facilitate scheduling accordingly.</li></ul>
<p>Qualifications</p>
<ul><li>3-5 years of documented industry experience fitting/selling winter goods or equivalent</li></ul>
<ul><li>Custom boot fitting experience</li></ul>
<ul><li>Display ability to think critically and problem solve</li></ul>
<ul><li>At ease with multitasking under pressure</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong verbal and written communication skills</li></ul>
<ul><li>Able to stand, crouch, and walk for extended periods</li></ul>
<ul><li>Able to lift up to 40 pounds</li></ul>
