<h2>Product Designer I</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Job Duties</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Analyzing tax forms and tax rates to map customer data to a tax form.</li>
<li>
Assisting the design team with research necessary for defining tax form requirements.</li>
<li>
Reviewing tax data collected and providing other teams a solution for reporting on forms.</li>
<li>
Understanding the technical implementation of the tax forms, including the architecture and data model.</li>
<li>
Working with Quality Assurance to help in understanding requirements and needed test cases for enhancements and updates.</li>
<li>
Investigating tax form issues reported by clients / technical support and proposing solutions and/or enhancements to improve the application.</li>
<li>
Interacting with the team to understand processes and capture business requirements.</li>
<li>
Supporting the tax product design team in achieving their goals and objectives.</li>
</ul>
