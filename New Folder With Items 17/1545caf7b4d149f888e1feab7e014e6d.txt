<h2>Software Architect</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>The <b>Software Architect</b> is accountable for being intimately involved with all technical activities, and for providing architectural direction and leadership on assigned project teams.</p>
<p>
Job duties include, but are not limited to, developing and documenting high-level solution architectures, scoping, approach, estimation, research, tool evaluation, documenting patterns and standards, defining technology strategy, technology evaluation and recommendation, mentoring through hands-on assistance as well as formal/informal training, and some technical development all while adhering to NetJets technology standards and best practices. Architects must be able to take high level direction, formulate a plan to achieve the goals and execute the plan. Additionally, Architects must be able to work across project teams, Product Owners, and Enterprise Architects and will be expected to work on multiple tasks and initiatives concurrently.</p>
<p>
50%: Be intimately involved with all technical activities and provide architectural direction and leadership on assigned project teams. Additionally, remaining current with regards to architecture and technology trends and help drive change based upon emerging opportunities.</p>
<p>
20%: Drive the scoping, estimation, and the development/documenting of high-level solution architectures for new projects.</p>
<p>
15%: Effectively interact/communicate with business users regarding requirements, design reviews, prototype presentations, etc.</p>
<p>
15%: Provide technical and process coaching/mentoring of project team members regarding the understanding of technologies and processes, which may include leading a Center of Excellence.</p>
