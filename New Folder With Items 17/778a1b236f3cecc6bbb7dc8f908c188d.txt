<h2>Engineered Systems Sales / Applications Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Summary: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Will become the company’s specialist for Clean Agent Engineered Fire Suppression Systems.</li>
<li>Representing the Original Equipment Manufacturer (OEM) for a Global market.</li>
<li>Will use your technical skills to explain the benefits of our products to potential customers and how these products and services are better than our competitors' products.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Duties and Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Confer with customers, to assess equipment needs and to determine system requirements.</li>
<li>Respond to functional and technical elements of Customer and/or Approval Body requests.</li>
<li>Provide design and specification development support to consultants, integrators, and distributors.</li>
<li>Provide Quotes for designed systems and follow through to secure orders.</li>
<li>Collaborate with sales teams to understand customer requirements and provide sales support.</li>
</ul>
<p>· Interact directly with clients and through regional/International Sales Reps in selling products.</p>
<ul>
<li>Maintain accurate records of target accounts, opportunities, quotes, projects, contacts and correspondence.</li>
<li>Resolve customer issues or difficulties in a manner that is consistent with the company mission, values, and financial objectives.</li>
<li>Provide status of ongoing projects and future projects.</li>
<li>Identify customer's needs and sales opportunities to help future growth.</li>
<li>Prepare and deliver technical presentations explaining products and services to customers, prospective customers and 3rd party Service Technicians.</li>
<li>Help clients solve problems with installed equipment.</li>
<li>Travel to project sites to preform start-up, integration, and training services.</li>
<li>Help in researching and developing new products.</li>
<li>Perform market research on industry trends and competitors’ products.</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul><li>relevant: 3 years (Preferred)</li></ul>
