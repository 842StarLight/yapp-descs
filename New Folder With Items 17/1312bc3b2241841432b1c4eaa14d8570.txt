<h2>Assistant Branch Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div><div>
<div><div>
<div><b>SUMMARY</b></div>
<div>Assist manager with any and all duties, and act in his/her place when he/she is not present</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b> include the following. Other duties may be assigned.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Must be able to produce a level of sales required by management and Havertys policy by serving customers, selling products, and performing all duties required of a Sales Associate.</li>
<li>May assist in sales training of sales staff</li>
<li>May supervise the sales department and hold sales meetings. Follow-up prospecting and scheduling</li>
<li>May communicate with inventory control and assist in ordering</li>
<li>May perform code changes on floor to include retagging merchandise</li>
<li>May oversee customer service and handle difficult complaints</li>
<li>May oversee display of floor and supervise merchandise on and off floor</li>
<li>May assist in moving furniture around the showroom floor</li>
<li>May help sales associates close sales</li>
<li>May assist with credit applications and credit memos</li>
<li>May assist manager in personnel matters and discipline</li>
<li>May assist manager with advertising plans and help to execute them</li>
<li>May assist manager with merchandising, customer service, office duties, delivery operations and scheduling</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
<div>
<div>Job Requirements
</div>
<div>
<div><b>REQUIREMENTS</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>Education and/or Experience</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>High school diploma or general education degree (GED); or one to three years related experience and/or training; or equivalent combination of education and experience.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>Other Qualifications</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Must have sales and furniture knowledge.</li>
<li>Computer literacy required with Microsoft Office experience preferred.</li>
<li>Must be detail-oriented and excellent communication skills.</li>
<li>Must be able to follow oral and written instructions</li>
<li>Must be able to work independently while using discretion</li>
<br>
</ul>
