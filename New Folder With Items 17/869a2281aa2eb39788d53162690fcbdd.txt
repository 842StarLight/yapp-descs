<h2>Trademark Paralegal</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Huntsman currently has an opportunity for an Trademark Paralegal at our Corporate Administrative Headquarters located in The Woodlands, Texas. This position provides substantive and organizational support to Huntsman’s Intellectual Property Law Department primarily in connection with the docketing and maintenance of Huntsman’s global trademark portfolio.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential responsibilities of this position include but are not limited to the following:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Assisting Huntsman IP attorneys with managing Huntsman’s international trademark portfolio including:
<ul>
<li>Conducting Preliminary Searches
</li>
<li>Assisting with the preparation, prosecution, and maintenance of trademark applications
</li>
<li>Assisting in instructing and facilitating the renewal of trademark registrations
</li>
<li>Reviewing Trademark Watch notices
</li>
<li>Maintaining Huntsman’s Trademark Infringement database
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Docketing all incoming and outgoing correspondence, ensuring that all renewal and official fees and third-party invoices are processed and paid, and maintaining electronic and physical files
</li>
<li>Communicating with internal business associates, third party service providers, and outside counsel regarding trademark matters
</li>
<li>Preparing various reports for IP attorneys and internal business managers and assisting in projects as requested
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>General Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul><li>Follows instructions and performs other duties as may be assigned by supervisor.
</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>Assists other employees in accomplishment of Company goals.
</li>
<li>Participates in Environmental, Health and Safety initiates as set forth by the Company
</li>
<li>Participates in and completes company-required training programs
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Education and Work Experience Requirements:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Must possess at least five (5) years of IP paralegal experience, with at least two (2) years of international trademark experience or five (5) years domestic trademark experience.
</li>
<li>Experience managing a large trademark portfolio (docket size of at least 1,000 filings worldwide) preferred
</li>
<li>Experience with IP docketing software (preferably, IP Manager)
</li>
<li>Working knowledge of documents used in domestic and foreign trademark prosecution and an understanding of relevant filing procedures
</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree preferred
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Special Qualifications (Training, Technical skills, etc.):
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to perform detail-oriented work and to work independently with minimal supervision
</li>
<li>Self-motivated and ability to proactively assume responsibility over assignments
</li>
<li>Ability to handle multiple assignments while working well under pressure
</li>
<li>Excellent written, oral communication, and organizational skills
</li>
<li>Must be proficient with Internet, Email, and Microsoft office programs
</li>
<li>Ability to professionally interface with all levels of management, internal business teams, and outside counsel
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Certificates, Licenses, Registrations
</b></p>
<ul><li>Paralegal Certification not required, but preferred
</li></ul>
