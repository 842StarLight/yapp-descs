<h2>WAREHOUSE WORKER(FIREARMS) EAFB MAIN STORE</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div><div>
<div>
<div>UNDER DESIGNATED SUPERVISION, PERFORMS A VARIETY OF CLERICAL AND MANUAL DUTIES RELATED TO RECEIVING AND PROCESSING MERCHANDISE AND SUPPLIES, TO INCLUDE RECORD KEEPING, DOCUMENT PREPARATION AND SELECTING STORAGE/SHELVING LOCATIONS IN THE FACILITY OR STOCKROOM, AS APPROPRIATE.<br>
<br>
1. INSURES THE PROPER RECEIPT, VERIFICATION AND DOCUMENTATION OF MERCHANDISE/SUPPLIES. AS REQUIRED, POSTS SELL PRICES ON DOCUMENTS. RECORDS DISCREPANCIES OR DAMAGED SHIPMENTS, INITIATES APPROPRIATE CORRECTIVE ACTION AND COMPLETES NECESSARY DOCUMENTATION.<br>
<br>
2. SIGNS RECEIVING REPORTS AND OTHER ACCOUNTABLE DOCUMENTS ANDPROCESSES RECEIPT THROUGH APPROPRIATE SYSTEMS USING A VDT/PC OROTHER EQUIPMENT.<br>
<br>
3. PROCESSES DAMAGED/DEFECTIVE MERCHANDISE RETURNED FOR REPAIR OR REPLACEMENT. MAINTAINS RECORDS AND TAKES FOLLOW-UP ACTION, AS NECESSARY.<br>
<br>
4. COMPLETES VARIOUS MERCHANDISING DOCUMENTS AND REPORTS, AS REQUIRED, EITHER MANUALLY OR BY APPROPRIATE SYSTEMS USING VDT/PC OR OTHER EQUIPMENT.<br>
<br>
5. AS NECESSARY, ASSEMBLES AND SEGREGATES MERCHANDISE (BICYCLES, GRILLS, ETC.) FOR DELIVERY AND PREPARES APPROPRIATE DOCUMENTATION.<br>
<br>
6. ASSISTS IN TAKING STOCK COUNTS AND OFFICIAL INVENTORIES.<br>
<br>
7. AS REQUIRED, MAY OPERATE MOTOR VEHICLES NOT TO EXCEED 10,000 POUNDS GROSS VEHICLE WEIGHT. MAY OPERATE FORKLIFT NOT TO EXCEED 10,000 POUNDS LIFTING CAPACITY.<br>
<br>
8. MAINTAINS COMPLIANCE WITH FIRE, SAFETY, SANITATION AND SECURITY REQUIREMENTS WITHIN STOCKROOM AREA.<br>
<br>
9. PERFORMS OTHER RELATED DUTIES AS ASSIGNED.<br>
<br>
10.RECEIVE AND MAINTAINS DOCUMENTATION REGARDING RECEIPT OF FIREARMS.<br>
<br>
ACTIVELY COMMUNICATES/PROMOTES THE BENEFITS OF THE IN-STORE CREDIT CARD TO CUSTOMERS IN ORDER TO DRIVE SALES GROWTH AND INCREASE CUSTOMER LOYALTY.</div>
</div>
