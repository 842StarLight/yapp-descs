<h2>Customer Support Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Job Description</b></p>
<p>Our firm is a retailer of surgical instrumentation to physicians’ offices, surgery centers, hospitals and other healthcare providers. The vast majority of our product offering is stainless steel, hand-held instrumentation used in various surgical specialties. The company is based out of the Chicago suburbs and serves customers across the United States, and internationally.</p>
<p>We are seeking a customer support specialist whose primary responsibilities include ongoing customer satisfaction, improvement in processes and issue resolution, processing customer returns, quotations, and other administrative tasks. The role will require business development support in conjunction with the sales team; in particular, developing comprehensive pricing and value-added proposals for health systems during the pre-contracting stages.</p>
<p>The role may also require some routine customer service responsibilities, including fulfilling support-related requests, answering product questions, processing orders, invoicing, and administrative support; however, primarily it will require management of these tasks which will be completed by another team member(s). The customer support specialist role is fast-moving, requires liaising with many stakeholders (management, warehouse team, purchasing, customers, sales representatives, corporate and clinical customer contacts, etc.) and necessitates a holistic understanding of the company’s operations.</p>
<p>The role has fairly little oversight and the candidate must be able to instill in senior management the confidence to manage others, prioritize tasks, drive projects to completion by deadlines and deliver customer-ready work products in final form. The role is full-time with standard hours from 8:30 am to 5:15 pm, Central time, with occasional work outside of normal business hours.</p>
<p><b>Primary Duties</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Customer Service</li>
<li>Answer customer calls</li>
<li>Assist customers making purchasing decisions</li>
<li>Provide information on products, pricing and availability</li>
<li>Complete price quotations based on company price lists</li>
<li>Create tools for customer communication during roll-out phase</li>
<li>Enter sales orders into accounting software</li>
<li>Assist customers with online purchasing on the e-commerce website</li>
<li>Communicate with customers regarding order confirmations and ship dates</li>
<li>Perform stock inquiries/availability checks</li>
<li>Invoice orders which have been shipped</li>
<li>Issue return authorizations and process returns</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Administrative</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Write official corporate correspondence</li>
<li>Specials projects assigned by management</li>
<li>Front-office management (ordering office supplies, mailings, etc.)</li>
<li>Administrative support for management</li>
<li>Issuing purchase orders</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Business Development</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Reach out to potential sales leads and track communication history</li>
<li>Organize promotional/marketing mailings</li>
<li>Assist business development teams in preparing proposals for contract and other large sales opportunities</li>
<li>Design and implement a customer onboarding strategy for large institutional health care systems</li>
</ul>

</li>

</ul>

<li>Professional attitude and outlook</li>

<li>Extreme Detail orientation</li>
<li>Self-direction, independence and leadership</li>
<li>Strong work ethic</li>
</ul>
