 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<div>
<div>
<b>Summary/Objective</b>
</div>
<div>
<p>The Marketing and Engagement Coordinator provides extensive administrative and functional support to WAMU’s Marketing &amp; Engagement Department. The position is responsible for providing administrative support to the Senior Director, Marketing and Engagement in terms of scheduling, travel arrangements, expense processing, and document processing as necessary. Additional functional support for the Marketing &amp; Engagement Department includes processing vendor contracts, handling monthly trade and in-kind reporting, budget support, handling all billing and reporting requirements unrelated to events management, newsletter formatting and distribution, and project management of key initiatives throughout the year. Additional requirements supporting creation of marketing and communication materials and providing additional on-site support for large events will be expected on an as-needed basis. The coordinator reports to and receives assignments from the Senior Director, Marketing and Engagement, but also supports the Director of Marketing &amp; Communications.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
The Marketing and Engagement Coordinator interacts with all members of the Marketing &amp; Engagement Department and will have the authority to work on sensitive documents on behalf of the Senior Director, Marketing and Engagement as directed. This person will have regular interactions with the Underwriting Department to ensure monthly trades and in-kind donations are accounted for accurately. The coordinator will also interact with key personnel across Content, Development, Membership, Business Administration and News as required.</p>
