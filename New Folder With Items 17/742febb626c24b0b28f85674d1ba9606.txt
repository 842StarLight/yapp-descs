<h2>Data Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><div>Outreach is a leader in sales engagement software and one of the fastest-growing technology companies in the country. In order to sustain our hypergrowth, we must continue to drive operational excellence for our go-to-market teams. The Data Quality Specialist team is responsible for ensuring quality and accuracy in the Outreach portfolio of accounts within Salesforce.com. The Data Quality Specialist provides programmatic and project-based enrichment of leads, contacts, and accounts and is focused on maximizing our data completeness, quality, and integrity overall. The goal for this role is to provide data excellence and thus to enable our Marketing, Sales Development, Sales and CS teams to operate with increased productivity as they engage with their respective counterparts in the community. Another key objective is to improve our focus and our effectiveness as we drive new models for our GTM strategy. This role is based in Seattle and reports into Outreach’s Revenue Operations team.</div></div>

</li>
