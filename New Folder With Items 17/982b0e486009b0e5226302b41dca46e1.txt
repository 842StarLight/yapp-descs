<h2>Applications Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>JOB SUMMARY:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>The primary function of the Applications Engineer is to provide support to the Applications Department for our sales and customer application needs. The ability to produce Cycle Time Analysis using various software tools and customer data.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul><li> Scrub incoming data from customers provided formats to usable data format for importing into Fuji SW.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Research component details via internet and other means to identify and confirm component specifications and its packaging.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Learn and understand Fuji programming software packages (Fuji Flexa and NEXIM).</li></ul>
<ul><li> Importing of formatted data into Fuji software systems for program job creation.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Learning all aspects of creating output data in order to generate Cycle Time Analysis studies.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Support machine demonstrations by presenting of data results</li></ul>
<ul><li> Assist in machine testing of custom solutions and new technologies/processes.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Minimal Travel approximately 10% to support customer projects on-site at their facilities.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Other duties as assigned by Manager.</li></ul>
<br>
