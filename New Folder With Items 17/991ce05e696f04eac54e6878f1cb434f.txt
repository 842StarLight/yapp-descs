<h2>Commercial Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>At Orkin, our purpose is to help protect the world where we live, work and play.</b></p>
<p>Our Sales Representatives are committed to this purpose.</p>
<p>Are you a <b>Sales Professional</b> with a <b><i>Hunter Mentality</i></b>?</p>
<p>Then check out our <b>Commercial Sales Representative</b> position that can turn into an amazing career.</p>
<p>As a Commercial Sales Representative with Orkin, you will be provided the opportunity to <b>grow your sales career</b> and <b>control your financial opportunity</b>. Our sales professionals earn top wages, recognition, and have the opportunity earn annual awards trips! We offer extensive <b>paid training</b> and a rewarding career.</p>
<p><b>The Successful Commercial Sales Representative Will . . .</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Prospect and develop new business in a designated territory</li>
<li>Achieve sales performance objectives through prospecting new business and assigned leads</li>
<li>Develop a daily schedule of productive activity from creative sources and qualified leads</li>
<li>Schedule sales appointments and meet with potential customers to explain Orkin products and services</li>
<li>Conduct a thorough inspection of interior and exterior areas of potential customer’s property</li>
<li>Serve as a problem solver for customers by utilizing the in-depth training provided to decide on the most efficient and best overall pest solution for each customer's needs</li>
<li>Make sales presentations to customers based on inspection and issues identified by addressing any questions, explaining the process and setting expectations</li>
<li>Complete proper paperwork to begin service for the customer and leverage your hand held device (iPad) provided for thoroughness, speed and accuracy</li>
</ul>
