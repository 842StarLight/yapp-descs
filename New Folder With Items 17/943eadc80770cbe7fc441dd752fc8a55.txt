<h2>Sr Manager - Hub C</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Oversees and manages the staff managers in the efficient operation and maintenance of a large size hub facility, and ensure the hub is operating to optimize safety, security, and quality measures, productivity, and service expectations. Supports the FedEx Ground (FXG) Safety Above All Culture.</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Essential Functions</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Serves as a champion for FXG’s Safety Above All Culture and supports all related initiatives.</li>
<li>Management responsibilities include, but are not limited to: hiring, terminations, coaching, skills development, recognition and rewards, performance management and staff productivity.</li>
<li>Plans, organizes, staffs, directs and controls the day-to-day operations of the department; develops and implements policies and programs as necessary.</li>
<li>Develops and manages the department budget; ensures compliance and addresses variances and other budget concerns.</li>
<li>Coordinates with other departments and FedEx Operational Companies (OpCo) in all aspects of Hub operations (e.g., Linehaul, Engineering, Maintenance, Administration, Human Resources, district staff and Pittsburgh General Headquarters).</li>
<li>Oversees the integrity of all licensing and permit requirements for the facility; including special guidelines and/or procedures to ensure federal, state, and local governmental compliance.(i.e., adhering to Environmental Protection Act (EPA) guidelines)</li>
<li>Oversees department audits, Control Self Monitoring (CSM), dispatching, and other audit processes.</li>
<li>Participates in the development of and responsible for administering company safety strategies and programs.</li>
<li>Oversees the education and implementation of employee safety and accident/injury programs and prevention.</li>
<li>Participates with the district staff in the implementation of improvement strategies designed to enhance the overall performance of the facility.</li>
<li>Leads and participates in company marketing and sales efforts (i.e., acts as operational liaison between the customers and FedEx Sales department).</li>
<li>Oversees a system of controls to ensure that service levels and operational performance goals are met within prescribed operational goals established on the Hub Operations Summary.</li>
<li>Oversees and facilitates meetings within the facility (e.g., safety and engagement meetings)</li>
<li>Participates as a member of the District Quality Counsel supporting the Quality process; facilitates the Hub Quality Team utilizing the Quality Driven Management principles.</li>
<li>Leads and supports community involvement efforts and drives corporate citizenship.</li>
</ul>
<br>
In some areas within the department, the following may apply:<br>
<ul><li>Oversees and monitiors the Air Gateway responsibilities for the Hub; completes necessary certification and training requirements.</li></ul>
<br>
NONESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS<br>
<ul><li>Performs other duties as assigned</li></ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Minimum Education</b></h2>
<ul><li>Bachelor’s degree in Business, Logistics, Engineering or related area required; in addition to Minimum Experience, high school diploma or GED and four (4) years experience in logistics management is accepted in lieu of degree.</li></ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Minimum Experience</b></h2>
<ul><li>Eight (8) years experience required as a manager, Assistant Hub Manager, or other like upper-middle level management experience with at least two (2) of those years working within FXG.</li></ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Required Skills, Abilities and / or Licensure</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Software skills, including use of Microsoft Office software and web-based applications.</li>
<li>Verbal and written communication skills necessary to explain complex and/or confidential information.</li>
<li>Presentation skills necessary to facilitate training and/or deliver informational sessions to groups and/or individuals.</li>
<li>Time management and organizational skills necessary to manage multiple projects, appropriately prioritize workload, plan for resources to meet deadlines and goals, and respond to day-to-day functional needs.</li>
<li>Ability to anticipate and identify problems and use sound judgment and fact-based analysis to develop effective and efficient solutions.</li>
<li>Ability to inspire a shared vision and empower and motivate a team.</li>
<li>Ability to mentor, coach and act as a knowledge resource to other employees.</li>
<li>Knowledge of concepts, practices, policies and procedures related to FXG field operations, linehaul, and contractor relations.</li>
</ul>
