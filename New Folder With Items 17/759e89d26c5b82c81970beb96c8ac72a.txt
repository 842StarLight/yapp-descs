<div>Matillion, a company with all 5-star customer product reviews on AWS Marketplace and all 5-star employee reviews on Glassdoor; are expanding the teams across all functions globally. <br>
<div>Responsible for driving revenue and customer acquisition for Matillion by prospecting outbound and inbound leads and qualifying opportunities within assigned geographic territory.</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Key Areas of Responsibility</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li><b>
Prospecting Leads</b></li>
<li>
Responsible for meeting/exceeding individual quota of meetings held with qualified prospects per month to drive revenue and customer acquisition.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with aligned Account Executives and Account Managers to identify and build out target Enterprise accounts to actively prospect for new customers.</li>
<li>
Effectively manage all inbound leads from assigned territory and actively prospect for new customers.</li>
<li>
Prospect and develop outbound and inbound leads, overcome objections, understand prospects’ needs, qualify opportunities.</li>
<li>
Prospect into existing Matillion customer accounts to expand adoption of Matillion ETL across company with new business units.</li>
<li>
Increase attach rate of users of Amazon Redshift, Snowflake, or Google BigQuery.</li>
<li>
Make a high volume of outbound phone calls and emails sent (50+ calls per day).</li>
<li>
Follow up on demand-generation activities such as webinars, conferences, field marketing events, partner events, etc.</li>
<li>
Analyze customer needs in regards to current business obstacles, identify opportunities and determine potential for Matillion solutions.</li>
<li>
Build and maintain relationships with decision-makers &amp; influencers at Enterprise accounts in assigned geographic territory.</li>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<li><b>
Salesforce administration</b></li>
<li>
Diligently maintain Salesforce CRM environment by regularly updating lead records and activity.</li>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<li><b>
Teaming for success</b></li>
<li>
Build strong working relationships with all teams within Matillion; sales, marketing, solution architecture.</li>
<li>
Share best practices with other SDRs, collaborate with other members of sales organization to maximize productivity.</li>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<li><b>
Culture</b></li>
<li>
Live the Matillion values; reflects Matillion’s values through demonstrating positive behaviours.</li>
</ul></div>
