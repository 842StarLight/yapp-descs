<h2>Marketing Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Description:</b><br>
Saturday Properties is seeking its next brand champion and marketing strategist. If you’re interested in building a data-driven marketing program and working in a fast-paced, flexible start up environment…keep reading!
<br><br>
The Marketing Account Manager is a full-time position that oversees all of Saturday Properties’ marketing efforts to drive sales/traffic and NOI growth for its 12+ properties. This position leads and manages the marketing team in developing annual marketing plans and setting strategy to ensure desired outcomes are both measured and achieved. Reporting and making data-driven decisions are critical in this role, as well as alignment with a dynamic sales team.
<br><br>
Ideal candidates have 6+ years of digital, social media and/or marketing strategy experience. Must have expertise using SEM, Google, display/retargeting, social media and traditional advertising channels to drive leads, raise brand awareness and meet annual goals.
<br><br>
Create marketing strategy for multiple properties
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Develop marketing plans for Saturday and its individual properties, to include brand management, strategy, audience segmentation, messaging, channels and KPIs.</li>
<li>Work directly with site, sales, executive, ownership and marketing teams to align on goals and strategy.</li>
<li>Responsible for marketing budget and producing greatest ROI.</li>
</ul>
Lead and manage marketing team of two staff
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Manage day-to-day operations, workload and priorities for the marketing team.</li>
<li>Set annual strategy and share vision with team, holding each member accountable to goals and KPIs.</li>
<li>Responsible for recruitment and hiring of a growing team; encourage and support a healthy team.</li>
</ul>
Marketing project management
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Ensure marketing plans are executed on time, and refining strategy with results and data as needed.</li>
<li>Manage content development, campaign design, reporting, events, websites and all marketing channels.</li>
<li>Must be capable of managing multiple property sites with many moving parts.</li>
<li>Manage declining budgets.</li>
</ul>
Reporting
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Monitor campaign performance, analyze data and optimize strategy to achieve KPIs. This includes PPC campaigns, Google Ads and Analytics, social media ads, CRM, traditional marketing, etc.</li>
<li>Collect key data, prepare and clearly articulate performance reports for property sites, ownership and executive team</li>
</ul>
Desired candidate should have working knowledge or fluency with:
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Setting and executing marketing strategy</li>
<li>Creating ad campaigns and meeting deadlines</li>
<li>Social media channels</li>
<li>Consumer review platforms</li>
<li>Direct response advertising and/or CRM</li>
<li>Manage DM and EM databases</li>
<li>Optimizing paid search</li>
<li>Website analytics</li>
<li>Landing page optimizations</li>
<li>Creative and effective ad copy</li>
<li>Project management.</li>
</ul>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
<b>Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>BS, ideally in advertising, marketing, analytics or communications</li>
<li>6+ years of relevant experience (marketing department or advertising agency)</li>
<li>Strong analytical skills and data-driven thinking</li>
<li>Relocation not provided, ideal candidates are established in the Minneapolis/St. Paul area</li>
</ul>
