<h2>Senior Project Manager Strategic Initiatives</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>When you join Kyocera Document Solutions America, Inc. you are joining an organization that is deeply rooted in the Philosophy of “doing what is right as a human being”. Through this Philosophy, our employees are passionate about providing best in class customer service through efficiency, accountability and a sense of urgency all while putting knowledge to work to drive change.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
As a Senior Project Manager, you will be a key member of a new organization that has been given the charter of driving the digital transformation of Kyocera Document Solutions of America (KDA). You will drive a core set of projects across several functional areas including: Finance, IT, Corp Strategy, HR, Logistics, Sales/Marketing, and Customer Operations. You will provide these organizations with structured project management support pre and post M&amp;A activities. You will help assemble project teams, lead integration meetings, develop integration playbooks, create tailored project plans, identify critical dependencies, and actively manage the integration of people, processes, and systems so that the promise of these M&amp;A’s can be realized quickly and seamlessly. You will be responsible for the identification and scoping of specific integration initiatives and the development of associated project plans, action registers, work breakdown structures, project presentations, and the other project management tools which will help ensure accurate knowledge of each integration as well as the assignment of resources where extra support is needed. You will also ensure that a superior customer experience is as core to your projects as productivity gains and revenue growth. You will deliver projects on time, resolve technical issues through collaboration and sound decision making, develop aggressive project plans that are realistic and achieved, and actively controls development’s costs, scope, and scheduling. You will also ensure your projects’ statuses are well understood through accurate tracking of actual project performance relative to the plan of record. Finally, you will ensure all levels of the organization are properly informed through excellent documentation and regular communication of your strategic and tactical initiatives. Likewise, you will provide regular quantification and tracking of the business benefits of your projects (ROI).</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
To be successful in this position you have a winning attitude, a positive problem-solving approach, an aptitude for critical thinking, and enjoy working in a team environment. You also are a self-started, are meticulous about details, have an aptitude for the bigger picture, and have an intellectual curiosity for different business models. In this newly created role, you will have the ability to make significant and long-lasting impact on the entire KDA organization by leveraging project management disciplines, change management methods, and strategy formulation methodologies. As a result, you will be one of the critical change agents within KDA responsible for the achievement of strong organic growth and radically more efficient operations.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The chance to do something meaningful, to challenge yourself, to take others where they would not go on their own, to be a part of change in an industry, and to drive change - doesn’t come around every day!</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Lead multiple integration initiatives across Legal, HR, IT, Sales, Marketing, and Operations in order to accelerate revenue and reduce operating costs while dramatically improving the customer experience.</li>
<li>
Develop for each project detailed project plans, timelines, action registers, work break down structures, and presentations that accurately describe the state of the integration at any given time.</li>
<li>
Develop and provide new operational and financial understandings of people, processes and systems across both organizations pre and post integration.</li>
<li>
Develop and maintain standards for project scheduling practices that ensure work is completed on time and within budget.</li>
<li>
Manage a team of that ensures work is completed on time and under budget.</li>
<li>
Develop and maintain standards that ensure project activities comply with company policies, contracts, and schedule requirements.</li>
<li>
Develop, implement and administer procedures and other work controlling documents to ensure clarification of roles and responsibilities.</li>
<li>
Represent KDA, its projects and/or departments during client and project management meetings with the highest ethical standards and clear communications.</li>
<li>
Develop and maintain all job reporting and logs to ensure corporate and project compliance.</li>
<li>
Collect and share market insights and identify significant market trends as a result of new technologies and competitive actions.</li>
<li>
Provide forward thinking that is disruptive to the competition and thrilling to our customers, suppliers, and partners.</li>
</ul>
