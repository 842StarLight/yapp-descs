<p><b>Position Summary:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>We’re searching for a Product Marketing Senior Manager to join the Value-Tier Smartphone team within the HHP (Handheld Products) Product Marketing group at Samsung Electronics America.
<br>
The Value-Tier Smartphone team is organized under the Senior Vice President of HHP (Handheld Products) Product Marketing. This team is tasked with developing and executing the go-to-market strategy for all Smartphone devices below flagship in the US market. This includes product planning, value proposition definition, situation analysis, creating business driving go-to-market plans, and leading cross-functional teams to execute those plans.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Role and Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>The Product Marketing Senior Manager will lead product marketing responsibilities for the value tier products. As product marketer, the Senior Manager will lead interactions with HQ around planning of future product as well as communication with key accounts to secure sell-in of newly developed smartphones.
</li>
<li>The position will require a blend of analytical skills, strategic thinking, and organizational influence. Key success factors in this role include strong project management skills, attention to detail, statistics/finance acumen, enthusiasm, a self-starter attitude, practicality, agility, collaboration and communication. Our business is evolving—we are looking for a creative and dynamic individual to bring a fresh approach to help drive the business.
</li>
<li>This role will report to the Director of the Value and Mid-tier Smartphone Product Marketing team. You will have significant interaction and work collaboratively with the business planning, supply chain management, marketing, retail, finance, legal, product development, customer support, HR teams, and frequently interact with Senior Leaders within the organization.
</li>
<li>Play key role in development of future smartphone products for US market and positioning those products to key customers
</li>
<li>Work with SEA Account teams to ensure sell in of new products •Formulate GTM strategies for all new products and execute /project manage launch operations
</li>
</ul>
