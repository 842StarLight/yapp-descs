<div><b>The Role: Commercial Coordination Advisor<br>
</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<br>
<br>
<div>
<b>Purpose
</b><br>
Provide commercial coordination between various groups within Engie Energy Marketing to ensure efficient and error-resistant workflows. Provide staff augmentation to the ISO Settlements group, the Power Scheduling group and the 24 hour desk when needed.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<br>
<div>
<b>Location/Department
</b><br>
Houston, TX / Engie Energy Marketing NA – Commercial Operations
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<b>Status
</b><br>
Exempt<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<br>
<div><b>Essential Job Functions
</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Propose changes to processes and software associated with Engie Energy Marketing’s wholesale operations and the interactions between the ISO Settlements group, the Power Scheduling group, the 24 hour real time desk, Middle Office, Trade Control and IT.
</li>
<li>Provide high level project management of changes to processes and software that have been agreed upon
</li>
<li>Closely and proactively coordinate across groups to ensure seamless transitions during process changes
</li>
<li>Develop and maintain written documentation for new and existing processes associated with ISO Settlements, Power Scheduling, Real Time Operations, and the interactions, data flows and software that support those groups.
</li>
<li>Provide in-person training and review of new and modified processes.
</li>
<li>Provide staff back-up to the ISO Settlements group, the Power Scheduling group and the 24 hour real time desk as may be needed
</li>
<li>For the ISO Settlements group; perform typical daily, weekly and monthly settlements functions.
</li>
<li>For the power scheduling group; handle daily submission of load bids to various ISO, submission of bilateral schedules, administer ISO portal access rights.
</li>
<li>For the 24 hour real time desk; perform the functions of monitoring and directing power plants and coordinating with ISO dispatch desks in real time.
</li>
<li>Review new renewable off-take agreements associated with Engie’s wind and solar plants, and develop the conceptual design of the internal work flows and data flows that are needed to implement the agreements.
</li>
<li>Iterate and build consensus with the involved groups to finalize the detailed design.
</li>
<li>Incorporate new generation and demand response service agreements into new and existing processes
<br>
</li>
</ul>
</div>
