SUMMARY
<br>The essential functions of this position are to complete analysis and communications to support continuous production and engineering. Builds, installs, and repairs electrical and electronic circuit wiring used on machine tools, plant equipment, and building. Test and troubleshoot faulty equipment and circuits within the facility.
<br>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS AND RESPONSIBILITIES
<br><ul>
<li>Analyzes, diagnoses, and determines extent of repairs. Performs dismantling, fitting, assembly, repair or replacement of parts on production machinery, building and related equipment.</li>
<li>Maintains and repairs all electrical equipment, inside and outside of plant grounds</li>
<li>Facilitates movement or installation of any equipment and machinery as required</li>
<li>Replaces faulty electrical components on machines such as relays, switches, and motors</li>
<li>Plans layout of wiring and installs wiring, conduit, and electrical apparatus in buildings</li>
<li>Able to diagnose minor mechanical problems and make necessary repairs</li>
<li>Maintain a 5S work area and environment</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
