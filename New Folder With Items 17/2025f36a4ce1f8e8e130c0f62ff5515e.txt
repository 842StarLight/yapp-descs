<h2>Marketing Consultant, Marketing Operations</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Job Description<br>
<br>
<p><b>WHAT IS A MARKETING CONSULTANT ANYWAY?</b></p>
<p>
Our job is to make our clients happy, and that means helping them succeed as marketers. The Marketing Consultant (MC), under the direction of Engagement Managers will work with a team of marketing experts focused on delivering end-to-end campaign/demand generation projects for clients. The MC will own and execute on project segments, such as:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Assessing current state of the client’s marketing execution or technologies</li>
<li>
Creating strategies and plans for marketing campaigns</li>
<li>
Creating operational processes to support marketing needs</li>
<li>
Enabling and optimizing software within their tech stack to facilitate and measure campaign performance to produce uncommon results (hyper-value).</li>
<li>
The MC will be expected to manage multiple project segments for a variety of clients at any given time to ensure success of LeadMD’s client organizations.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
EXPERIENCE:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
3-5 years of experience in integrated B2B and/or B2C marketing campaign planning &amp; management and demand generation, including:</li>
<li>
Experience in developing marketing strategies and tactics by audience segmentation and targeting</li>
<li>
Experience working with sales/sales op teams to manage sales campaigns or enablement</li>
<li>
Experience in A/B testing campaigns to trial new ideas for effectiveness</li>
<li>
Reporting on campaign performance/effectiveness, analysis, and optimization recommendations</li>
<li>
Two (2) or more years using Marketo (or similar system; such as Eloqua, Pardot, HubSpot) in a B2B or B2C environment, specifically in an advanced/admin capacity</li>
<li>
Experience with client-facing work and client management is a plus!</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
JOB RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Owns assigned work and understands the correlation with project outcomes</li>
<li>
Able to perform day-to-day tasks with minimal supervision</li>
<li>
Contributes to direct client communications and project deliverables</li>
<li>
Communicate routinely with Project Managers on task-specific timeframes and blockers</li>
<li>
Performs and documents requirement gathering based on client and project specifications</li>
<li>
Creates and socializes documentation throughout projects</li>
<li>
Assists in strategic workshops for clients and recommend strategic direction for engagements</li>
<li>
Helps to prioritize initiatives in relationship to greatest impact on client revenue goals or KPIs</li>
<li>
Works on several customer projects at the same time, and must have the ability to constantly shift gears, solve problems, synthesize ideas, and listen to changing needs</li>
<li>
Trains and empowers client-side resources to take over our work and leverage frameworks that set them up for success</li>
<li>
Maintains a strong working knowledge of current sales and marketing trends</li>
</ul>
