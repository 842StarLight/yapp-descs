<h2>Production Specialist-Premedia 2nd Shift</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Description</b><div><b>
Position Summary:</b></div>
<div>
Works with Sales and internal teams to preflight, process, proof and output digital art files for the Retail Copy &amp; Print Centers and Digital Production Facilities. Impose files, print hard copy proofs, develop binders and kits, and post print-ready PDF files utilizing online proofing software and other output platforms for approval from the customer and/or sales team. Prepares documents for print and/or press by utilizing the Adobe Creative Suite (InDesign, Illustrator, Photoshop) and other Prepress software, as needed. Will have experience in print production while working with external vendors including but not limited to customers, designers, printers and manufacturing plants. Ability to produce superior quality in all areas of prepress. Able to work effectively with all levels of management and team members. Requires the ability to be flexible in different settings and managing priorities effectively, while delivering on time in a deadline-driven production environment. Passionate about accuracy with a high degree of personal accountability. Goal oriented at an individual and team level. Accountable for ensuring that projects are delivered in line with style, quality, and accuracy expectations and within the Industrial Standards Organization guidelines to achieve corporate goals.</div>
