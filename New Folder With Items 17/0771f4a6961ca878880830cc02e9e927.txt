<p><b>Job Description Summary:
</b></p>The position will play a key role in ensuring that change initiatives meet objectives on time and on budget by creating content that supports adoption and usage. This person will focus on the people side of change by developing eLearning and Help content that enables J.B. Hunt employees and customers to effectively use and benefit from new business processes, systems and technology. The primary responsibility will be to design, develop and deliver eLearning modules, task and reference content and other forms of user documentation to drive faster adoption, higher utilization and greater proficiency of the changes that impact employees in the organization while increasing benefit realization, value creation, return on investment and the achievement of results and outcomes.
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Description:
</b></p>
<p>Key Responsibilities:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Applies instructional design principles in the development of training curricula and documentation plans.
</li>
<li>Assesses the change impact as it affects content needs.
</li>
<li>Partners with Organizational Change management to create a content strategy that dovetails with communication plans and technology development.
</li>
<li>Consults and collaborates with project teams.
</li>
<li>Supports knowledge management at the organizational level.
</li>
<li>Coordinates with and manages content contributors, reviewers, and approvers throughout project plan.
</li>
<li>Creates eLearning modules and knowledge checks as well as task, reference, and concept Help.
</li>
</ul>
