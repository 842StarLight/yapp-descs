<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p>RKL is looking to expand its Senior Living Services Consulting practice (SLSC) and is searching for a Consultant to join our group. SLSC is a national practice and serves clients in the post-acute healthcare industry. Financial members of the SLSC practice work with clients to increase operational and financial performance, analyze financial transactions and development projects, assist with gaps in finance leadership, and prepare annual cost reports for Medicare and Medicaid.</p>
<p>
We are RKL, a Pennsylvania based professional services firm that taps into a wide range of services – tax, accounting, data analytics, human capital management and more – to deliver solutions that prepare companies and organizations for real, sustained growth. Recognized as a “Best Places to Work in PA” we are also one of the nation’s fastest growing firms that offers an entrepreneurial culture that values the contributions of each team member.</p>
