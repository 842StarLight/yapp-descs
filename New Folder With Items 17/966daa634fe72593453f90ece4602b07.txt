<h2>General Accountant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>POSITION SUMMARY:</b></p>
<p>
Responsible for performing basic, professional accounting activities within Omni Glass &amp; Paint, Inc. Accounting activities performed may include posting, summarizing, reconciling, auditing, ledger maintenance and statement and/or report preparation.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Prepares statements and reports in accordance with Generally Accepted Accounting Principles.</li>
<li>
Entering invoices into the ERP system both thru Accounts Payable/Purchase Order.</li>
<li>
Entering customer payments into the ERP system thru Accounts Receivable.</li>
<li>
Post Labor and Burden to Jobs.</li>
<li>
Set up jobs in Job Cost Module/Sales Order Module.</li>
<li>
Invoice Jobs through Job Cost Billing Module/Sales Order Billing Module.</li>
<li>
Creates statements for customers.</li>
<li>
Calculates sales tax on all taxable purchases.</li>
<li>
Will train as a back-up to the Payroll function.</li>
<li>
Performs special projects and other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
QUALIFICATIONS:</b></p>
<p>
To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill, and/or ability required. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor's Degree in Accounting or equivalent work experience preferred.</li>
<li>
Basic understanding of GAAP.</li>
<li>
Two years of experience using ERP Systems, including accounts payable / accounts receivable / job costing in a Construction and/or Manufacturing environment.</li>
<li>
Experience with MAS-90 System preferred.</li>
<li>
Ability to work with a minimum of supervision and prioritize tasks.</li>
<li>
Strong verbal and written communication skills.</li>
<li>
Strong attention to detail.</li>
<li>
Proficient 10-key data entry skills.</li>
<li>
Must be able to meet the attendance requirements.</li>
<li>
Must be able to work professionally with others.</li>
<li>
Proficient use of Microsoft Office Suite.</li>
<li>
Experience with accounting software applications.</li>
<li>
Able to follow instructions.</li>
<li>
Able to operate office equipment copiers, scanners, fax machine, postage machine, etc..</li>
<li>
Ability to multi-task will be required to be successful.</li>
</ul>
