<h2>Customer Experience Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Description:</b><br>
Have you worked in customer service, accounts receivable, or collections and want to look at the job from a different perspective? Are you passionate about ensuring great customer experiences? Do you often find yourself exploring every option and menu in a software program? Good news — you just might be who we are looking for!
<br><br>
As a Customer Experience Account Manager, you will be our customer’s main point of contact and be responsible for engaging with them proactively after implementation. You will ensure a positive customer experience through outreach and own any customer follow-up actions they have requested. You will also provide improved customer utilization and satisfaction through your knowledge of the Esker product suite.
<br><br>
What’s in it for you? Of course, Esker will pay you a competitive salary, but you’ll also love coming to work every day. With a laid-back culture that encourages employees to keep a healthy work-life balance, Esker makes sure all of our employees have time to do the things they love — including playing ping-pong in the office! With benefits like paid vacation and a reimbursement program that can be used every year towards your fitness or home office, we want you to be able to commit to your passions! .
<br><br>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
What do we think it will take to succeed as Customer Experience Account Manager?
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Background in account management, customer service, training, accounts receivable, or collections</li>
<li>Proven experience managing and cultivating positive working relationships with customers and coworkers.</li>
<li>Outgoing individual with strong communication and presentation skills.</li>
<li>Excellent time management skills, with the ability to complete assigned tasks in a fast-paced work environment.</li>
<li>The technical skills to quickly learn and configure the Esker on Demand solution including; dashboards, reports, tabs, views and templates.</li>
<li>Ability to travel; the Customer Experience Advocate may spend up to 20% of his/her time visiting customers on-site.</li>
<li>Strong knowledge and experience using Microsoft Office products, as well as GoToMeeting, Salesforce.com, and other web-based applications.</li>
<li>Entrepreneurial/problem-solving mindset, with an emphasis in making our customers feel valued, understood and engaged.</li>
</ul>
