<p><b>
Description:</b></p>
<p>
The Product Specialist applies scientific knowledge and principles to provide in-depth training and assistance to Anton Paar sales staff and customers on the proper use of highly complex measuring and laboratory instruments. The Product Specialist also represents the company as a technical expert with respect to the assigned product line.
</p>
<p>This position will form part of our Business Unit Solutions team, and will work with a variety of materials characterization and analytic chemistry instrumentation. Examples include Anton Paar's Multiwave 7000, MCR 702 Multidrive, and AutoFlow BET+, and ChemBET Pulsar instruments.</p>
<p>
The starting salary range for this position is $50,000 - $90,000/yr depending on technical knowledge and industry experience, with specific instrumentation experience preferred. Anton Paar USA offers profit sharing bonuses; and we offer a $10,000 anniversary bonus every five years.</p>
<p>
This position requires anywhere from 40% to 60% travel. Travel is typically intermittent, and usually entails a two or three night trip to a client site within the region for demos, installations, and/or trainings. Anton Paar provides company cars when needed as well as Amex and gas cards for travel expenses.</p>
