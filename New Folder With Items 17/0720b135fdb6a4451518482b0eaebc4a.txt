<p><b>Join our Growing Team!</b></p>

<p></p>
<p>Summit is a growing natural gas utility providing safe, reliable and clean burning natural gas service to homes and businesses in Arkansas, Colorado, Maine, Missouri and Oklahoma. Being part of the Summit team means embracing excellence, diversity and innovation, committing to safety each and every day, and doing all that we can to serve each other, our customers and the communities where we live. We aim to bring warmth and energy to everything we do.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>We have an exciting opportunity open for a Customer Development Representative for our Eastern, CO district,(Strasburg, CO), based out of our Littleton, CO. office.</b> <b>(Our office will be moving to the Denver Tech Center in November 2019).</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>The <b>Customer Development Representative</b> will be responsible for obtaining new and commercial residential customers for Colorado Natural Gas. Meeting with individual customers, building developers and contractors will be an important part of this position. There will also be the development of various projects with the satisfaction of seeing them through completion.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Primary Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul><li> Responsible for achieving company sales goals for new and commercial and residential sales customers by;</li></ul>
<p>o Initiating or responding to prospective customers and scheduling appointments.</p>
<p>o Educating prospective customers regarding the advantages, (savings, environmental, ease of use), to converting their service to natural gas.</p>
<p>o Identifying important customer decision points and providing solutions that address them.</p>
<p>o Finalizing each new customer signup by completing all necessary paperwork including a commercial/residential service application, a drawing sheet and any other forms necessary to facilitate an installation agreement.</p>
<p>o Completing, scanning and following through with all contracts and other documents.</p>
<p>o Providing on-going customer service and follow through with prospective customers including resolving any problems or issues with their conversion or service line.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul><li> In order to successfully market the companyâ€™s natural gas service, the Customer Development Representative will be required to;</li></ul>
<p>o Keep current on all promotions and services available as provided by the company marketing and business development departments.</p>
<p>o Maintain a thorough knowledge of all products and current costs that are available including working with spreadsheets and manipulating fuel conversion software in order to develop customer proposals.</p>
<p>o Maintain contact with builders/land developers regarding potential new businesses.</p>
<p>o Complete and pass required tests for continuing education.</p>
<br>
<p></p>

<ul><li> Comfortable in a fast-paced sales environment requiring multiple commercial and residential contacts each day.</li></ul>

<ul><li> Comfortable with Microsoft Office Suite of computer programs.</li></ul>
