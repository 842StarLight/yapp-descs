Job Description</p>
<p>
<br></p>
<p>
CoStar is the leading provider of information, marketing and analytic services to the commercial real estate industry. Our customers include owners, brokers, lenders, appraisers, investors, and others who need our information and services to help them buy or sell a property, represent a tenant, lease a space, value an asset, underwrite a loan or manage a diverse portfolio.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
CoStar is currently seeking a full time, experienced Marketing Manager to lead our marketing efforts for CoStar analytic product and services targeting these different audiences across the full spectrum of CRE property types.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
Reporting to the Director of Marketing, you will collaborate with a strong in-house team of stakeholders from Market/Business Intelligence, Marketing Services, Communications, as well as work with leaders throughout the company.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
You will be supporting and collaborating particularly closely with stakeholder analysts, economists, and thought leaders in the Analytics department to initiate, drive, and implement marketing strategies to promote CoStar analytic capabilities.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
Job Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Create and manage marketing campaigns including brand awareness and lead generation activities for CoStar analytic services targeted to key audiences, through inbound marketing initiatives, nurture tactics, display and social advertising, content and native marketing, speaking and other thought leadership opportunities at events, and direct marketing.</li>
<li>
Support the Analytics department with marketing and sales materials that layout the value proposition of CoStar analytic services, and ensure they have what they need to promote their efforts.</li>
<li>
Work with Marketing Services to design and execute campaigns; work with Analytics, Communications, Market and Business Intelligence stakeholders on message.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with product, sales enablement, research, and client engagement marketing managers to incorporate insights to the analytics marketing campaigns and ensure an integrated approach.</li>
<li>
Drive CoStar-related/participating events with opportunities for branding, engagement touch-points, and follow-up efforts as they relate to our analytic services.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with Content Development Manager to create and execute content marketing campaigns around key pieces of thought leadership.</li>
<li>
Report on and analyze campaign effectiveness by working with Business Intelligence team; seek out best practices from Market Intelligence team and other stakeholders to improve campaign results.</li>
<li>
Develop marketing briefs including writing the copy, identifying and involving the stakeholders and resources needed, incorporating product information and market/business intelligence.</li>
<li>
Draft customer testimonials (written and video) and case studies, gleaning insight from clientsâ€™ feedback, analysis of product usage and management reports.</li>

<li>
Manage analytics marketing resources on company intranet, adding and deleting materials based on need and relevancy to ensure that all assets are accurate and up-to-date.</li>
</ul>
