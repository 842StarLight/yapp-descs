<h2>IT Governance Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>At Agilysys, Inc. we are proud of our 3,000+ customers including some of the world’s most recognizable resort, casino and cruise line brands. We specialize in market-leading point-of-sale, property management, inventory and procurement, and mobile and wireless solutions that are designed to streamline operations, improve efficiency and enhance the guest experience. We serve casinos, resorts, hotels, food service venues, stadiums, cruise lines, grocery stores, convenience stores, general and specialty retail businesses and partners. With extensive operations, throughout North America, and additional sales and support offices in Singapore and Hong Kong, as well as software development in India, we are growing. For more information, visit <b>www.agilysys.com</b>.</p>
<div>
<b>
Agilysys</b><b> is hiring for</b><b> an IT Governance Specialist</b><b>.</b>
</div>
<p>
The successful candidate will join the global Information Technology team with a primary focus on IT Governance. Responsibilities include documenting and establishing IT standards, Business Continuity planning, monitoring policy effectiveness and implementation, ensuring IT policy/procedural awareness, staying aware of IT management and compliance processes, recommending IT security practices to executive management, plus other IT regulatory requirements, as well as assisting internal and external auditors with audits.</p>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Your duties will include:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Provide expertise by way of multi-disciplinary knowledge and experience working with: Legal, eDiscovery, Internal Audit, Information Technology/Security, Privacy and Compliance/Risk.</li>
<li>
Develops, maintains, and manages detailed project plans, action items, and milestone timelines for all assigned projects.</li>
<li>
Internal and external audit liaison.
</li>
<li>Identify improvements and breakdowns in IT governance elements with significant business impact and feed into IT risk management function.
</li>
<li>Provide inputs into IT disaster recovery process and business continuity.</li>
<li>
Perform review and validation of documentation and evidence of compliance.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with peers and stakeholders on compliance schedules and deliverables.
</li>
<li>Identify and implement process improvements for internal processes and those related to other IT teams.</li>
</ul>
