<h2>CRM Business Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<div><p>Global Business Development is a fast-growing division and in need of a Salesforce business analyst. As an analyst on the CRM team, you will identify and address business requirements as well as work with Salesforce users and other corporate stakeholders to understand and help realize gaps and opportunities for improvement. This position requires strong technical aptitude, problem-solving skills, attention to detail, positive professional attitude, and the ability to work in a matrixed team environment.<br>
</p></div>
</div>
<div>
<p>RESPONSIBILITIES</p>
<div><ul>
<li>
<b>Understand customer needs.</b> Work with stakeholders to gather, understand, and define business requirements.</li>
<li>
<b>
Solve business problems.</b> Develop user stories and process flows to support the design and development of Salesforce solutions for our customers.</li>
<li>
<b>
Collaboration is key.</b> Work collaboratively with team members to design a solution that will meet our customersâ€™ business requirements.</li>
<li>
<b>
Implement solutions.</b> Identify and define user stories to support the development of solutions that leverage the Salesforce platform and AppExchange products or other cloud/enterprise-based technologies.</li>
<li>
<b>
Validation is important.</b> Work with developers to test and verify that solutions will meet business requirements.</li>
<li>
<b>
Engage regularly.</b> Participate in key meetings with clients including requirement sessions, system demos, user acceptance testing, and end user training.</li>
</ul></div>
</div>
<div>
<p>REQUIREMENTS</p>
<div><ul>
<li>3+ years of hands-on experience using Salesforce CRM to develop client or business solutions, configurations (including workflows), validation rules, and security management</li>
<li>
History applying analytical and problem-solving skills in a fast-paced environment, setting priorities, planning, and independently executing tasks in a structured manner</li>
<li>
Strong presentation and verbal and written communication skills with ability to tailor communication style based on audience</li>
<li>
Ability to prioritize multiple tasks and develop strong interdepartmental and cross-functional working relationships within a collaborative team environment</li>
<li>
Proficient in Microsoft Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and Visio or Lucid Charts</li>
<li>
Bachelor's in business, computer science, or a related field</li>
</ul></div>
