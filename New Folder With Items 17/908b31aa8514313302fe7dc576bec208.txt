<p>
Camping World is now hiring a <b>Multimedia Designer</b> to be based in our Lincolnshire corporate headquarters. The <b>Multimedia Designer</b> will be responsible for producing an array of design assets including: Video content, animations, print material, and web content.</p>
<p><b>
Job Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ensure on-brand design and accurate content that align with the overall creative strategy.</li>
<li>Collaborate with team members to produce media for video, print, and web.</li>
<li>Manage, edit, and produce video content and print production projects.</li>
<li>Ensure all projects are completed on time and within budget.</li>
<li>Develop creative, strategic ideas that solve specific marketing challenges.</li>
<li>Execute initial concepts through to completion.</li>
<li>Perform other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Minimum Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's Degree in Multimedia, Graphic Arts, Advertising or relevant experience.</li>
<li>Minimum 1-2 years experience in graphic design, video editing, and motion graphics.</li>
<li>A strong demo reel and design portfolio.</li>
<li>Proficiency in design and video editing applications including Premiere and After Effects.</li>
<li>Advanced Adobe Creative Suite experience.</li>
<li>Advanced graphic design and typography skills.</li>
<li>Must be self-motivated team player, professional, detail orientated, and possess excellent oral and written communication skills.</li>
<li>Comfortable working under pressure with tight deadlines.</li>
<li>Travel, RV and or Automotive experience preferred.</li>
<li>Strong customer service skills with a commitment to superior quality.</li>
<li>Outstanding creative presentation skills.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Capacity to handle more than one project simultaneously</li>
<br>
</ul>
