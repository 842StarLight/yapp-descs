<h2>Hospice Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div><div>
<p>JOB DESCRIPTION</p>
<p>Responsible for achieving sales and/or profitability objectives, within an assigned territory. Working a business plan to effectively grow the territory. Key point of contact for all referral sources; physician practices, hospital case managers, nursing homes, senior housing and community.</p>
<ul>
<li>Educate referral sources about the services available through home health.</li>
<li>Maintain contact with referral sources to update them on changes in Medicare/Medicaid regulations.</li>
<li>Assist agency in maintaining compliance by delivering physician orders to offices and facilitate in obtaining signatures.</li>
<li>Enhance awareness of home health services in the community by interfacing with discharge planners, hospitals and physician staff.</li>
<li>Maintain committed and cooperative attitude with all job duties.</li>
<li>Be available to assist the community with wellness and prevention.</li>
<li>Participate in community-sponsored events and expositions.</li>
<li>Stay current with regulatory changes in the home health industry.</li>
<li>Know your company and the product lines.</li>
<li>Make a minimum of 50 contact calls (referral sources) weekly to include following objectives:</li>
</ul>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Develop relationships with physician offices, discharge planners, and other potential referral sources.</li>
<li>Utilize consultative sales training to manage Top 30 accounts above and beyond monthly expectations.</li>
<li>Partner with CCC and HCC teams to effectively manage time and expand visibility and service to additional referral sources.</li>
<li>Identify decision-makers at various sites and know their names.</li>
<li>Maintain calendar and activities in RSL.</li>
<li>Provide educational information related to the home health industry.</li>
<li>Follow up with referral sources at least once per month: provide outcomes.</li>
<li>Deliver and pick up 485’s and Dr.’s orders as needed.</li>
<li>Discuss problem patients with the ADON, DON or Administrator on potential issues that relate to physicians.</li>
<li>Relay complaints related to any JHS patient to the appropriate ADON, DON or Supervisor.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Performs other duties as directed by the Supervisor.</li>
<li>Maintains availability at all times during schedules work hours.</li>
<li>Generates company minimum of 4 Medicare admissions per calendar week.</li>
<li>Achieves or exceeds MCR monthly admit budget as assigned by RSM</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
