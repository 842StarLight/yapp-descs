<b>Life at Elsevier</b> is about helping researchers, clinicians and engineers tackle the biggest challenges facing humankind. Combining content, data and analytics we turn information into answers. Learning is at the heart of what we do, as we help people in science, research and healthcare expand their knowledge every day. <p>Are you the experienced <b>Account Manager (AM)</b> with a track record in selling information tools, eager to contribute to our Global Sales division? Our Academic Account Manager vacancy can be your next challenge!</p>
<p><b>Purpose of the Role</b>:</p>
<p>The Account Manager develops plans and manages their accounts within major academic research universities. They work with multiple stakeholders in the university research community and research libraries, and are the primary strategic account leader driving the overall Elsevier business (all research products) at the account in his/her territory.</p>
<div>The AM is the main strategic account planner – ensuring other sales roles and Elsevier stakeholders are aligned with the overall account strategic plan and account engagement plan. The AM encourages, facilitates and leverages the other sales roles (Solution Sales Manager, Customer Consultant) in order to optimize Elsevier‘s business at the account –working with Solution Sales Managers(SSM) to drive new sales of specific solutions, and working with Customer Consultants(CC) to ensure customers get the most value out of our solutions so as to secure renewals and future growth.<br>
You will report to the Regional Account Director. This position is Home Based.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>Your responsibilities:</b></div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>You are responsible for customer loyalty and customer satisfaction. This means you are the expert on the customer; you know everything about the customers’ business, strategy and research needs, budgets and competitive products at the customer. You translate this knowledge into implications for the holistic Elsevier value proposition and the solutions we offer.</li>
<li>
As the Account Manager you are responsible for meeting or exceeding the annual revenue targets and achieving long-term growth of overall Elsevier business at the account or territory.</li>
<li>
You will deliver the content value story to the customer (institute, library and researchers) and drive customer value perception of Elsevier content, products and services. Leads or drives high value, complex and potentially highly political negotiations (e.g. at consortia or country level)</li>
<li>
AMs are responsible for successfully meeting current year and longer term revenue growth goals by working together with the other sales roles (SSM/CC) as a tight-knit team in the territory.</li>
<li>
AMs interact with various stakeholders at an account – not just the commercial buyer – and work to transform the value perception of Elsevier‘s core offering with researchers at the account.</li>
<li>
Build long-term Elsevier success in your territory.</li>
<li>
Responsible for new-sales within existing accounts for ScienceDirect Journals, Scopus, ebooks</li>
<li>
Responsible for strategic account planning, ensuring appropriate input from other stakeholders, and focusing on the customer’s business strategy.</li>
</ul>
