<h2>Customer Service Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Cenveo, a world leader in the management and distribution of print and related offerings is seeking an experienced Customer Service Representatives ideally with print experience.
<br><br>
The ideal candidate will have a High School Diploma. An Associate Degree is preferred with a minimum of 3-5 years of relevant experience.
<br><br>
Cenveo offers a competitive salary and a comprehensive benefits package.
<br><br>
Job Summary
<br><br>
Under general direction, acts as a liaison between customer accounts, sales, and operations; advising clients on product availability, pricing; and delivery schedules using established guidelines and procedures.
<br><br>
Primary Duties and Responsibilities (*Essential Functions)
<br><br>
These are functions a qualified incumbent or applicant must be able to perform with or without reasonable accommodation.
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Provides timely responses to internal and external customers.</li>
<li>Maintains active communication between customers, sales staff and manufacturing personnel concerning order status.</li>
<li>Review client specifications and provide efficient and economical production alternatives when necessary.</li>
<li>Expedites or revises job orders as needed.</li>
<li>Receives or prepares customer inquiries concerning current and past orders.</li>
<li>Coordinates preparation of written quotes and proposals.</li>
<li>Reviews manufacturing orders for accuracy and completeness.</li>
<li>Maintains daily updates of production schedule; notes deviations and communicates with customers.</li>
<li>Independently discerns and solves concerns or complaints from customers.</li>
<li>Coordinates and monitors customer releases with the shipping department.</li>
<li>Inventory management of customer accounts.</li>
<li>Promptly inform sales team/ other key personnel of any conditions that may lead to customer dissatisfaction.</li>
<li>May train/direct other customer service staff or direct work flow</li>
<li>May act as designated representative for specific accounts.</li>
</ul>
Other (Non-Essential) Functions
<br>Included functions which are normally performed by persons holding this title. However, these functions may be reassigned to other employees as part of a "Reasonable Accommodation" under the ADA.
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Other duties and assignments as required.</li>
</ul>
Job Requirements
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>High School Diploma or equivalent. Associate degree preferred in business/ relevant field or equivalent work experience.</li>
<li>A minimum of three (3) years of customer service experience required.</li>
<li>Strong computer skills including Office Software packages and Internet.</li>
<li>Exceptional communication, organization, and time management skills</li>
<li>Ability to calculate figures such as discounts, commissions, proportions, percentages, etc.</li>
<li>Ability to read, analyze and interpret general business data, technical procedures and numerical data.</li>
<li>Ability to write simple reports and general business correspondence.</li>
<li>Ability to effectively present / convey information effectively.</li>
<li>Requires contact with employees at all levels, outside vendors, and customers.</li>
</ul>
Physical Requirements &amp; Environmental Conditions
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Environment is typically general office, though the incumbent may spend short periods on the production floor with exposure including but not limited to dust, dirt, high noise levels and variations in temperature.</li>
<li>Occasional exposure to one or more unpleasant or hazardous conditions.</li>
<li>Regularly required to stand, walk, use color vision, grasp, reach, use manual dexterity and sit for prolonged periods of time.</li>
<li>Occasionally required to lift/carry up to 25 lbs.</li>
<li>Occasionally required to travel.</li>
</ul>
