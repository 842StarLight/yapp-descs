<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Position Summary
</b></h2>The Operations Lead, in partnership with the Operations Supervisor, is responsible for the day to day operations of the cash office, cashier counter, and various reporting functions. This includes exacting execution of Operations SOP and high levels of guest satisfaction at the cashier and guest service counters, while delivering a minimum of 9.1 guest survey average through the application of Foundational Excellence (SOP, Talent, and Leadership).
<p></p>
