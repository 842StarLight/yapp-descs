<h2>Manager Customer Marketing</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>Job Description Details
</b></p>
<p>As a Manager Customer Marketing, you will be accountable for delivering results against aggressive goals by hiring the right team, setting the right strategy and rolling up your sleeves when needed. You will be innovative in your thinking, and have proven success influencing cross-functional teams. You will understand both the art and science of customer marketing and advertising, and have proven success in driving substantial results, ideally in B2C eCommerce marketing or customer acquisition. You will be comfortable working with complex challenges, appreciate ambiguity and autonomy, and understand the value of rapid test and learn approaches. You will be savvy about how to leverage large data sets to create sophisticated marketing programs. You will be comfortable working with marketers, product managers, engineers, and teams ranging from customer service to merch to business development.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>What You’ll Do:
</p>
<ul>
<li>The Marketing Manager for Partners and Affiliates will own publisher and partner relationships including influencer and loyalty campaigns.
</li>
<li>A successful candidate will lead a team of Specialists to identify the problem, build creative customer and client programs to impact KPIs.
</li>
<li>This is a very visible role within the company and a successful candidate will own digital advertising campaigns for Zulily.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Qualifications:
</p>
<ul>
<li>5+ years of experience managing large scale and complex affiliate campaigns for lead gen companies (as an agency client or in-house)
</li>
<li>Manage the Affiliate Program by adding new affiliates, optimizing existing affiliates and scoring significant strategic and tactical wins with key partners in the channel.
</li>
<li>Strong negotiation ability to improve overall costs and package deals
</li>
<li>Maintain high level of communication with affiliates, recommend new campaigns for affiliates and provide performance feedback to sales team
</li>
<li>Strong analytical skills and understanding of KPIs for online/digital media for direct response
</li>
<li>Manage programs within acceptable levels of advertising spend, contribution and customer long term value
</li>
<li>Exceptional knowledge of affiliate and influencer marketing
</li>
<li>Exceptional analytical skills, including standard web analytics platform (Google Analytics or similar) as well as ad-hoc analysis skills in Excel
</li>
<li>Ability to think strategically and identify innovative and creative solutions
</li>
<li>Extreme focus on driving business growth through action by being obsessed with results
</li>
<li>Strong written and verbal communication skills, ability to articulate ideas and persuade stakeholders Superior analytical skills including experience with use of complex and diverse data sources and platforms
</li>
<li>Basic ability with HTML and SQL a plus
</li>
<li>Achievement-oriented, detail-oriented and results-driven analytical and data-focused
</li>
<li>Collaborative and enthusiastic about being part of a tight-knit team environment
</li>
<li>Highly organized with a deep appreciation for efficiency
</li>
</ul>
