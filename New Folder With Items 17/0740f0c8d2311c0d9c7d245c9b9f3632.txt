<h2>Sales Development Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Are you a Winner? Competitive? Hate to be defeated? Great communication skills? We want you on our TEAM!!!</p>
<p>
We are Terraboost Media, a national omni channel advertising company focused on assisting small to medium sized business owners leveraging our products and knowledge to grow their business. One of our core products is a sponsorship opportunity on a gigantic hand sanitizing wipe dispenser. This billboard sits at the front door of prominent supermarkets/ retailers all across the country, locally we have advertising inside Jewel, Tony's, Bed Bath and Beyond and other awesome locations.
</p>
<p>We've been in business for 12 years and service over 70,000 kiosks nationwide. We are looking to add dynamic relationship building presenters to introduce our products to them, get them interested, and moved through the sales funnel. In essence our Sales Development Representatives are responsible for qualifying leads at the initial stages in the sales funnel. They must research potential clients, connect with and educate prospects and qualify leads before handing them off to the closers of the sales team. The job is working email marketing campaigns, text campaigns, social media campaigns, and cold calling campaigns all about 25% equally. The goal is to get people to take meetings with our Online Sales Executives and close business.
</p>
<p>Salary: $36,000.00 to $44,000.00 per year plus bonuses!</p>
<p>
*This position is located in our Joliet, IL office.*</p>
<p><b>
Requirements</b></p>
<p>
1. Minimum of 1 year in lead generating role. Advertising background helpful, but not required. Our best people have come from Groupon, Angie's list, Grubhub, and similar small business serving companies.</p>
<p>
2. Very articulate and well-written.</p>
<p>
3. Charismatic personality.</p>
<p>
4. Strong work ethic, Self-starter, Self-disciplined person who can work independently.</p>
<p>
5. A desire to WIN and be coachable. Our best employees are coachable!</p>
<p>
6. Able to work in a competitive work hard play hard environment. Ready to report to an office every day and WIN.</p>
<p><b>
Benefits</b></p>
<p>
Terraboost Media offers a fun, energetic, fast paced environment with the ability to write your own paycheck and grow your career. We offer a competitive base salary with an easy to understand monthly bonus structure. Other benefits include health insurance, Friday lunches, company outings, paid holidays and vacation accrual after 90 days. Most importantly, there are rapid advancement opportunities for high performers.</p>
