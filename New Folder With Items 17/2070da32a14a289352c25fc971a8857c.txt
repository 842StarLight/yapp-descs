<h2>Engine Workshop Technician - Technician Apprentice Program (TAP) - Pacific Region - Sumner, WA</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<p><b>Technician Apprentice Program<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><i>If you want to earn while you learn, develop your career with the industry leader of innovative engine technology and be empowered to deliver a service you can be proud of – then this is where you need to be.<br>
</i></p>
<p></p>
<p><b><i>Extraordinary Technology Requires Extraordinary Technicians!<br>
</i></b></p>
<p></p>
<p>Our Sales and Service business supports our customers in the Trucking, Delivery, Farming, Construction, Power Generation, Mining, Oil &amp; Gas, Marine and Public Transportation industries. Working with industry leading engine technology (2.8 – 95 liters) and cloud-based diagnostic tools, you’ll learn to deliver solutions that build your knowledge base and fuel your career.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Join our emPowered Cummins Technician Apprentice Program! This is the pace to start your career the right way and grow your options as you learn about our technology, our business and our customers. This is a government-registered apprentice program with both on-the-job training to embed skills from real world context and a formal education based in a classroom setting. Then you’ll focus on one of our service specialties:<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<div>o Diesel Engines – Light duty, midrange, heavy duty &amp; natural gas
</div>
<div>o High Horsepower – 19 – 95 liter engines
</div>
<div>o Marine – Pleasure and commercial vessels
</div>
<div>o Power Generation – Consumer and commercial products
</div>
<div>o Service Operations – Business management and process training<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<p>During your apprenticeship, you are a <b>full-time, paid employee </b>who will complete a development program with the support of our coaching technicians and managers. Our agreement with you ensures you receive the following upon successful completion:
</p>
<div>o Cummins Level 1 Technical Competency (minimum)
</div>
<div>o Associate in Applied Science (AAS) degree, Automotive &amp; Diesel Technology
</div>
<div>o No college debt – Cummins pays for your education
</div>
<div>o U.S. Department of Labor Trade Certificates for Diesel Engines
</div>
<div>Course credits that are transferable towards a bachelor’s degree
</div>
</div>
<p></p>
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2>
<p>The <b>success factors </b>for this apprenticeship – and your career as a Service Technician are simple. If these traits sound familiar, we’re ready to help you develop them fully and expand your career choices.<br>
<br>
</p>
<div>o Strong personal values
</div>
<div>o A problem solver – curious about how it works
</div>
<div>o Passionate about working with your hands
</div>
<div>o Embraces new engine technology for power, fuel economy and diagnostics
</div>
<div>o Cares about co-workers, customer service and communications
</div>
<div>o Inclusive of diverse perspectives
</div>
<div>o Desires to develop their skill set and product knowledge through classroom, hands-on, and virtual training.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<p><b>Minimum Requirements:<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<div>o Applicants must meet minimum community college general education course requirement of 2.8 GPA high school (unweighted) or 22 ACT score.
</div>
<div>OR have a
</div>
<div>o GED with one year of college with minimum 2.8 GPA
</div>
<div>o Ability to properly and safely use and maintain common hand tools and electrical testing equipment.
</div>
<div>o Familiar with Windows and Microsoft Office 365 and Outlook (email)<br>
</div>
<p></p>
<p><b>Physical Requirements (times are approximate)<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>Standing 7.5 hours per day · Pushing/Pulling 4.0 hours per day · Sitting 1.5 hours per day · Lifting 1.5 hours per day · Lifting up to a maximum of 80 pounds · Bending 3.0 hours per day<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Compensation and Benefits<br>

Additional benefits vary between locations and include options such as our 401(k) Retirement Savings Plan, Cash Balance Pension Plan, Medical/Dental/Life Insurance, Health Savings Account, Domestic Partners Coverage and a full complement of personal and professional benefits.<br>
</plus></p>
