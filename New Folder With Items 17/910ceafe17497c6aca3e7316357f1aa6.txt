<h2>Retail Center Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Job Summary</b></p>
<p>Retail Center Associate selling customer' shipping, packaging and printing needs.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities and Duties</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Delivers outstanding customer service to walk-in Retail customers and telephone inquiries</li>
<li>Continuously practices good listening skills with customers, UPS Store team members, and leadership</li>
<li>Takes ownership of the customer's shipping needs and offers viable solutions</li>
<li>Takes action to learn all product and service offerings, alternative solutions, and industry trends</li>
<li>Operates all equipment, software, and devices in an expert fashion and is willing to teach others</li>
<li>Maintains a clean and safe working environment</li>
<li>Performs all other activities of a Retail Center Associate like operating cash registers, maintaining a clean and pleasant environment, start of the day activities, end of the day activities.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications and Skills</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Clean Driving License Required</li>
<li>High school diploma or GED required</li>
<li>Retail/customer service experience is not a must but further qualifies you.</li>
<li>Good communication and people skills</li>
<li>Strong computer skills, including Microsoft Office and Adobe Suites</li>
<li>Outstanding phone skills</li>
<li>Strong verbal and written communication skills, including spelling and math</li>
<li>Prompt, reliable, and responsible</li>
<li>Able to lift 40+ pounds</li>
<li>Willing and able to work 25 to 30 hours per week for a 6-day work week</li>
</ul>
