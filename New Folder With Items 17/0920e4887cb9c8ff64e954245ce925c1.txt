<p><b>Senior Exective Assistant </b></p>
<p>Job Description</p> <p>Rich Mark Developers Group is seeking a high performing Senior Executive Assistant for the Chief level leader of the company. The successful candidate shall possess high level management skill and will work closely with the company’s CEO on every aspect of residential and commercial property development. This is a perfect position for someone who has had over 3 years of professional experiences in working with the top management and would like to get highly involved in real estate development process.</p> <p>Responsibilities</p>
<div>
<ul><li>Create and utilize financial models for investment valuation</li></ul>
<ul><li>Provide executive level management administrative support by conducting research and performing clerical functions</li></ul>
<ul><li>Excellent organization and project management skills with keen attention to detail. Handle day to day support duties to include complex calendaring, travel arrangement, expense reporting, etc.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Discreetly handle confidential information of major importance such as company strategy, financial and personnel information</li></ul>
<ul><li>Source, underwrite, capitalize and close new investment and development projects</li></ul>
<ul><li>Compile, summarize and prepare documentation for review by President</li></ul>
<ul><li>Perform due diligence to prepare real estate acquisition/ closing proposals</li></ul>
<ul><li>Assist with the many facets of the sales and marketing effort including supporting sales goals, if needed.</li></ul> </div>
<p>Requirements and Qualifications</p>
<div>
<ul><li>Bachelor’s degree in Finance, Real Estate or related field</li></ul>
<ul><li>Over three (3) years of professional working experience in real estate development.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must have strong analytical, quantitative and organizational skills. Attention to detail and prioritize tasks is the key for success.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Computer proficiency with a strong working knowledge of financial modeling, word processing, spreadsheets, presentation software, database and etc.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Excellent communication skills in English and Mandarin, both verbal and written</li></ul>
<ul><li>Enthusiastic, career minded, individual, with a strong work ethic and manage and execute work independently. Flexibility is also important for this role.</li></ul> </div>
<p>If you are interested in applying for the position described above, please submit your resume to: hr@richmarkdevelopers.com<br>
We thank all applicants for their interest, however, only those selected for an interview will be contacted. Rich Mark Developers Group is proud to be an Equal Opportunity Employer. Candidates applying for this role must be legally able to work in US for any employer.</p>
