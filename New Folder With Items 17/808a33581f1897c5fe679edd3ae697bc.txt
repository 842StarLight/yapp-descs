<br><br>
------
<br><br>
About the Role
<br><br>
We are seeking a driven, innovative and energetic demand generation leader, with a talent for empowering high performing teams and an ability to get things done. The person in this highly visible role will take Punchh's demand generation to the next level, owning the demand strategy and accountable for execution that drives top-line growth. If you are a revenue centric marketing leader that thrives on growth and scaling a modern B2B enterprise demand engine, this role will be the perfect fit for you.
<br><br>
Responsibilities
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Leadership of the global demand gen team with a focus on driving demand from large enterprises in priority markets and segments.</li>
<li>Accountability for meeting/exceeding sales pipeline goals and leading indicators of demand</li>
<li>Forecast, measure, analyze and report on the impact of demand creation activities on opportunities, sales pipeline and closed deals</li>
<li>Continuously evaluate the performance and cost of acquisition, adjusting demand tactics and strategy accordingly</li>
<li>Lead our account-based marketing efforts by working with Sales to identify target accounts and build highly customized campaigns that yield pipeline and revenue</li>
<li>Lead the executive level demand gen strategy and execution, bringing sales leaders together with customers and prospects through engaging experiences</li>
<li>Ensure highly productive relationship with Sales, forging strong communication and aligning on campaign and target account strategy</li>
<li>Work closely with all marketing functions coordinating the demand funnel and ensure message optimization</li>
</ul>
Qualifications
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Authority in enterprise demand gen, with 10+ years of experience in a fast-paced technology company; 3+ years ABM expertise with demonstrated success</li>
<li>Strong understanding of SaaS business models and metrics</li>
<li>Experience in targeting Marketing and IT Executives (B2B2C experience a plus)</li>
<li>A collaborative and passionate leader, able to provide direction, inspiration and experience in assessing, managing and building high performing teams</li>
<li>Ability to work as a player/coach – no task is too big or small if it supports the team or mission</li>
<li>Proven track record of doing account based marketing and industry marketing in partnership with a direct sales team that drives results in large enterprise accounts with executive level buyers</li>
<li>Exceptional written and oral communications skills, effective in both formal and informal settings</li>
<li>Ability to thrive in a dynamic environment in which high growth is the norm</li>
<li>Bachelor's Degree in a related discipline. MBA is a strong plus.</li>
</ul>
