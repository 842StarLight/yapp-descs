<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div><b>That’s Nice, But What’s the Job?</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
In short, as an Associate Systems Engineer, Support the day-to-day IT operations of area DriveTime Car Sales locations with the ability to troubleshoot and diagnose system, Server, and LAN related issues. Strong technical skills relating to Windows Server, Windows 10, Active Directory, PowerShell Scripting, Virtualization and Cisco Networking are required in this role. This is the perfect position for the System Admin and/or budding System Engineer who wants to learn even more, and grow their skills in an advanced environment.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
In long, as an Associate Systems Engineer, you are responsible for:
</div>
<ul>
<li>Windows Server setup, troubleshooting, maintenance and management
</li>
<li>Performing administrative tasks with Hypervisors (ESXi and AHV), to include monitoring and maintenance</li>
<li>
Coordinating with business units and execute system patches, upgrades and scheduled maintenance</li>
<li>
Microsoft Active Directory Administration on a regular basis to include Azure AD, Office 365, DNS, and Active Directory Federation Services (ADFS) support and maintenance</li>
<li>
Working with Enterprise Architects to execute projects and tasks as needed</li>
<li>
Assisting with monitoring and maintenance of Citrix Virtual Desktops</li>
<li>
Maintaining maximum system up time for maximum end-user productivity</li>
<li>
Willingness and ability to learn, and adapt to rapid change</li>
</ul>
