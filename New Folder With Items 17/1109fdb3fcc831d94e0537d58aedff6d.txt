<h2>Director of Guest Experience and Operations</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>POSITION SUMMARY:</b><br>
The Director of Guest Experience and Operations is responsible for leading the housekeeping, front desk, mailroom, facilities, grounds and security teams in guest experience and day to day operations. The Director of Guest Experience and Operations is responsible for maintaining high standards of customer service throughout all departments at Kripalu based on industry standards and in alignment with Kripalu?s mission and values. This position will assist and lead major Capital budgets and improvements on campus. This position includes weekends and holidays.</p>
<br>
<p><br>
<b>Accountabilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop and implement exceptional, consistent, and reliable guest service standards, including setting and exceeding measurable service benchmarks across department.</li>
<li>Continuously evaluate all areas of the organization that touch the guest experience and work strategically with all department leaders to develop and improve a seamless guest experience of the highest quality.</li>
<li>Proactively coach and develop team members within departments</li>
<li>Manage, coordinate, and continually evaluate Front Desk, Mailroom, Housekeeping, Facilities, Grounds and Security departments.</li>
<li>Manage and lead the capital budget alongside the Vice President of Guest Experience and Retreat Center Operations</li>
<li>Evaluate current department standards, policies, and procedures and drive improvements within the area</li>
<li>Involve team members in the planning of the work that they will be performing</li>
<li>Achieve Guest Service objectives by contributing guest service information and recommendations to strategic plans and reviews; preparing and completing action plans; implementing guest service standards and training; monitoring accuracy; resolving problems and making recommendations for improvements.</li>
<li>Effectively and efficiently resolve guest concerns and complaints that rise to the Director level.</li>
<li>Analyze customer feedback and provide strategic direction to continuously improve overall guest experience.</li>
<li>Ensure that all administrative policies and procedures are effectively created and maintained.</li>
<li>Maintains in-depth working knowledge of Kripalu systems and processes, including safety protocols.</li>
<li>Assist department managers in planning departmental budgets, optimizing operational spending and staffing levels.</li>
<li>Work continually towards self-development to stay current on customer service, sales and supervisory procedures and practices.</li>
<li>Provide feedback to the Senior Vice President regarding service failures or customer concerns.</li>
<li>Participate in organization wide discussions and working groups</li>
<li>Complete other projects and duties as assigned by the VP.</li>
</ul>
</div>
<br>
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>Core Competencies:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Possesses strong core ethics, integrity, and values consistent Kripalu’s organizational principles.</li>
<li>Organizationally minded; leading teams in operating under what is best for Kripalu and the guests that we serve</li>
<li>Represents the organization and resolves guest issues above and beyond guest expectations</li>
<li>Demonstrates strong leadership and experience in developing teams</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Builds effective relationships, identifies internal and external customer expectations—sees issues from their point of view; offers practical, mutually-beneficial recommendations, solutions, and ideas.</li>
<li>Demonstrates the knowledge and abilities necessary to perform required job elements to established standards.</li>
<li>Learns and adapts quickly in new situations resulting in successful performance.</li>
<li>Accepts responsibility for own actions.</li>
<li>Embraces experiences as learning opportunities.
</li>
<li>Keeps up-to-date on industry trends.</li>
<li>Demonstrates strong leadership skills.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Essential Skills and Experience:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in hospitality or related field.</li>
<li>As least 5 years experience as a cross department leader (hospitality industry preferred)</li>
<li>Experience leading housekeeping, front desk and security operations</li>
<li>Experience with established hospitality/guest experience programs</li>
<li>Proven effectiveness as an independently motivated, innovative, and results-oriented leader in customer service management for a minimum of two years.</li>
<li>Impeccable character; keeps confidentiality and skillfully and gracefully resolves issues.</li>
<li>Ability to manage emergency situations.</li>
<li>Extensive experience with Microsoft programs, including Outlook, Word, and Excel.</li>
<li>Experienced with hospitality property management software required.</li>
<li>Strong orientation towards Kripalu's mission and core values.</li>
<li>Must be available to work some evenings and weekend hours.</li>
</ul>
