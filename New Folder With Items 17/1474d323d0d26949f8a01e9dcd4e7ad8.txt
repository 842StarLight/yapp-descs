<h2>Retail Assistant Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Job Summary: </b>The Assistant Manager’s role is to functionas the second-in-command to the General Manager, focusing on executing keyoperational responsibilities and in managing freight flow. This role supportsoperational functions related to Human Resources, including recruiting,interviewing, onboarding and scheduling. In addition, this position leads allaspects of visual merchandising, receiving and freight processing.</p>
<p>Primary Job Responsibilities:<br>· <b>STORE OPERATIONS</b></p>
<p>o Manages all aspects of store when General Manager is not present</p>
<p>o Role models Customer service expectations by executing and reinforcingservice model principles</p>
<p>o Drives sales initiatives, discount programs, charitable programs andother company initiatives</p>
<p>o Reviews and understands financial data to support driving profitablesales and decision making</p>
<p>o Verifies and drives store compliance with required online trainingcourse completion</p>
<p>· <b>HUMAN RESOURCES</b></p>
<p>o Assists with recruiting, interviewing, onboarding and scheduling tobalance Customer experience with task prioritization</p>
<p>o Manages sales floor and freight processing Associates, includingperformance feedback, coaching and annual appraisals</p>
<p>o Champion for staff training and development to enhance Associateperformance, develop succession plans and promote recognition</p>
<p>· <b>MERCHANDISING &amp; INVENTORY CONTROL</b></p>
<p>o Manages seasonal sets, transitions and packaway events</p>
<p>o Leads execution of store inventory management process: weekly in-stockprocedures, inventory adjustments and price changes</p>
<p>o Completes required promotional sets and displays for advertising events</p>
<p>· <b>TASK PRIORITIZATION &amp; PLANNING </b></p>
<p>o Reviews all corporate communications and prioritizes/plans with storemanagement team accordingly</p>
<p>o Coordinates completion of company directives by prioritization based onservice balance with task deadlines</p>
<p><i>This job description is not all-inclusive. Additional responsibilities will bedetermined by supervisor, based on store needs.</i></p>
