<h2>VP of Operations</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Scout Curated Wears is a rapidly growing fashion brand and we’re looking for a dynamic VP of operations. Founded in 2015, sales grew 160% last year with annual sales over $2m. We are on track for similar growth in 2019. We’re looking for a seasoned professional to join our team and work in a creative, entrepreneurial environment. This is a great opportunity to play a meaningful role in a growing company.</p>
<p><b>About us: </b></p>
<p>At Scout, we specialize in designing modern, multi use jewelry that is beautifully packaged and easy for gift giving. The brand is currently represented in over 2,000 stores nationally as well as on our own retail website. We operate within both the wholesale and retail channels and partner with a network of 75+ contracted sales representatives. Our team works closely together in an open office located in a beautifully renovated mill building in Easthampton, MA. In 2017 we launched an initiative to give back a percentage of profits to causes that support and promote women. As part of this mission we donate to several local and national organizations.</p>
<p><b>The person we’re looking for: </b></p>
<p>As VP of operations, you’ll be working with a team of four that is passionate about creating and selling products people love. We are looking for someone who thrives being part of an entrepreneurial environment and has experience developing and managing the nuts &amp; bolts of the business. This role is perfect for someone who has the ability to analyze functions and implement solutions in an efficient and motivating way. We are looking for someone who has a positive attitude, is flexible, and is a strong communicator. It’s crucial to have in depth knowledge of diverse business functions for a product based company with wholesale and retail business channels.</p>
<p><b>About the job: </b></p>
<p>The VP of Operations position is responsible for organizing and overseeing the daily operations of the company to ensure the business is well-coordinated, productive, and profitable. The role will manage a customer service team of two and work closely with product development and sales &amp; marketing.</p>
<p><b>Core responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul><li>Direct daily business operations for optimum efficiency &amp; employee productivity. This includes the ability to coordinate, plan, implement, and manage projects based on company goals.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manage customer service department including order processing &amp; customer service.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manage the relationship with third party warehouse including fulfillment, inventory, and shipping and</li></ul>
<p>supplies management.</p>
<ul><li>Analyze financial data to maximize revenue and reduce costs.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Assist CEO with procurement. This includes knowledge of forecasting, supply chain, and inventory</li></ul>
<p>management.</p>
<ul><li>Support the sales &amp; marketing team with reporting systems and ways to improve analysis.</li></ul>
<p><b>Vision/Strategy: </b></p>
<ul><li>Collaborate with management team to drive organizational vision, operational strategy, and development.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Help plan and execute special projects to drive sales and increase brand visibility.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Identify and implement solutions to optimize efficiency where the business can improve with operational</li></ul>
<p>process.</p>
<p><b>Qualifications/Requirements: </b></p>
<ul><li>Bachelor’s degree required; Master’s degree a plus</li></ul>
<ul><li>Proven experience as head of operations or similar position</li></ul>
<ul><li>5+ years experience in the wholesale or retail sector</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong business acuity with solid knowledge of finance, supply chain distribution and employee</li></ul>
<p>management</p>
<ul><li>Cross-functional and multi-channel experience across different markets required</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong computer skills and knowledge of a range of business software programs (e.g. ERP, CRM)</li></ul>
<ul><li>Skilled with Excel and MS Office Suite</li></ul>
<ul><li>Working knowledge of data analysis and performance/operation metrics</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to work independently as well as within a team</li></ul>
<ul><li>Organized and proficient with data and numbers</li></ul>
<ul><li>High attention to detail</li></ul>
<ul><li>Outstanding communication</li></ul>
<ul><li>Positive attitude and strong interest in working in a fast paced entrepreneurial environment</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to multitask, prioritize and focus in an open office environment</li></ul>
<ul><li>Confident and effective decision making skills</li></ul>
<ul><li>Diligent and proactive results-driven personality</li></ul>
<p>This is a full time position. Competitive salary &amp; benefits to be discussed based on experience.</p>
