 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>As a rapidly growing global provider of IT solutions, we’ve expanded from Seattle to Sydney and beyond – with reach in over 80 countries around the world. <b>Position Overview</b><p>
The Cloud &amp; Software Specialist will act as a trusted advisor and an subject matter expert (SME) focused on solutions (software &amp; services) sales for your team, Account Executives, and clients to increase sales and permeate into your assigned accounts. To accomplish these tasks, you will invigorate solution sales in your clients through software renewals and upselling and cross selling to your clients. Driving Microsoft and non-Microsoft revenue to meet partner target goals, selling cloud solutions, and reaching the software GP target. We pride ourselves in providing Five Star Service to all of our customers, and we work around the clock to make sure our customers continue to use us as their First Choice for IT. As such, we need you to successfully drive our solutions offering to your clients, which will include preserving client satisfaction via open communication and identifying business needs as they arise. You will also need to cultivate partner relationships in your territory to stay current with products and solutions for your client, as well as identifying professional service opportunities, and Cloud deployment.</p>
