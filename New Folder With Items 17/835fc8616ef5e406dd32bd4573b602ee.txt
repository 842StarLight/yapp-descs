<h2>Senior Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>Manage Evolution Digital’s engineering team; including hiring, training, staff development, performance management</li>
<li>Manage product development and customer facing solutions within budget and aggressive timelines</li>
<li>Create and implement Agile Groups to target complex business problems by assembling multi-disciplinary teams</li>
<li>Manage complex changes to solutions by creating multiple streams of work, with different teams through good communication, collaboration and execution excellence</li>
<li>Create compelling value propositions for customers by creating customized solutions based on Evolution Digital’s IP Video platform</li>
<li>Develop solution proposals based on thorough understanding of customer requirements</li>
<li>Direct and coordinate resources to support the day-to-day operations, installation, upgrade and migration of IP Video solutions</li>
<li>Coordinate and manage resilient, scalable high-performance products and solutions across multiple locations involving multicultural teams in different countries</li>
<li>Prioritize and manage workloads from multiple sources – customers, partners and development teams and deliver under agreed upon SLAs</li>
<li>Convert product features into key technical requirements and help drive the development across multiple teams</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Track and report progress and key product metrics to stakeholders and management with the ability to develop and improve KPI’s to deliver service excellence</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Proactively identify key risks and develop mitigation strategies. Work with customers and partners and resolve issues by building consensus</li>
<li>Assist with the sales process by articulating salient product features to customers. Decompose complex solutions into lucid components and help customers understand the value of Evolution Digital’s solution</li>
<li>Assess market directions in the IP Video industry and create innovative ideas and review with executive management</li>
<li>Develop and manage financial budgets for projects</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>EDUCATION:</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>Engineering Degree</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>DESIRED EXPERIENCE/SKILLS:</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>10 – 15 years of experience in the Tech Industry. Cable/Telco/Video Industry preferred</li>
<li>Strong technology architecture background is a must</li>
<li>Leadership skills and extensive experience in customer facing role is critical</li>
<li>Knowledge of Agile methodology and processes</li>
<li>Software systems development experience is a preferred</li>
<li>Ability to build consensus with customers and design solutions collaboratively</li>
<li>Experience in presenting and working with all levels of employees, including C Suite Executives</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to learn the business process flows and technical solutions of complex systems and their dependencies</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Financial acumen with knowledge of pro-forma development and budget development/tracking required</li>
<li>Flexible, innovative and the ability to manage responsibilities and priorities in a fast-paced, growth-oriented and time-critical environment</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Should possess know-how to get things done. Motivate and get the best out of delivery teams, partners/peers, including vendor partner engagement</li>
<li>Excellent communication and interpersonal skills to coach and mentor junior employees, sharing technical and customer knowledge in a helpful and timely fashion</li>
<li>Desire to learn new skills and technologies, work well within multicultural teams, strong communication and documentation skills, and seek creative solutions that further the overall goals of the organization</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>LOCATION:</b></h3>

<li>Evolution Digital’s headquarters; Centennial, CO</li>
</ul>
