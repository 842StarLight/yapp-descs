<h2>Business Development and Proposal Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Armand Corporation, an award-winning, M/WBE construction management firm, is seeking a full-time <b>Business Development and Proposal Manager </b>to oversee our Business Development team in Camden, New Jersey. Our Manager will supervise existing members of the team to carry out a comprehensive approach to marketing our firm. Our ideal candidate should have a combination of skills and experience in business proposal writing and professional presentations, in order to assemble winning bids for diverse Construction Management and Professional Services contracts in New York, New Jersey, Pennsylvania and the United States at large.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
This position will be based out of our Camden, NJ office, but will require travel within New Jersey, New York, Eastern Pennsylvania, and other locations as needed.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Roles and Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop and execute a strategy for discovering and securing new business opportunities either locally, regionally, or nationally (government/public/private).</li>
<li>Shepherd the proposal process from Requests for Proposals (RFP) extraction through delivery with follow-up regarding presentations and awards.</li>
<li>Effectively work with technical staff throughout the firm and lead the management strategy of various proposal efforts; coordinate with sub-consultants and teaming; coordinate with potential client/proposal contacts; ensure that schedules are met, and final packages are fully compliant and of the highest quality.</li>
<li>Write and assemble compelling business proposals; edit technical narratives provided by construction teams.
</li>
<li>Train, mentor, and supervise the other members of the Business Development team.
</li>
<li>Foster beneficial relationships with business partners, potential clients, business contacts, and government contacts to attract new business and enhance organization reputation; represent Armand at trade fairs, networking events, and meetings as needed.</li>
<li>Develop and update accurate, powerful, and attractive marketing materials including company profiles, project sheets, advertisements, brochures, and handouts.</li>
<li>Provide oversight for the content of the companyâ€™s website and social media accounts.</li>
<li>Perform additional duties as required.</li>
</ul>
