<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Technical Architect – Network</b></h2>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Who We Are
</b></h3>
<p>Burwood Group is not your average technology consulting firm. We are an innovative, culture-driven, industry leader and we are growing our team by leaps and bounds. Founded in 1997 in Chicago (and still headquartered here), we've grown to be a 200+ employee firm with people and offices spread from Southern California to NYC. Our specialty is closing the gap between business strategy and technology solutions. Our clients view Burwood as a trusted advisor and partner who can help them select the best technology for the job, manage and execute a first-rate implementation, and measure project success by the business outcomes achieved.</p>
<p><br>
<br>
<b>What You’ll Do</b></p>
<p>
The Technical Architect (TA) is a consultative sales, technology, and solution focused specialist who creates client intimacy with their technical proficiency. The TA provides pre-sales technical support and expertise in identifying client requirements, while analyzing the client’s current network and system capabilities, to ensure the technical solutions proposed meet the client’s objectives. In addition to being viewed as a trusted advisor to the client, the Technical Architect also has responsibility for solution design, statement of work development and delivery, and the transition of projects from pre-sales to delivery.</p>
<p>
 In addition to core responsibilities, you will be:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Working alongside our Account Executive to achieve financial goals, pursue appropriate opportunities and provide top notch solutions for our customers’ biggest problems.</li>
<li>
Maintaining strong multi-vendor relationship to ensure an understanding of each vendor’s products, business initiatives, and technology positioning</li>
<li>
Recognizing other opportunities to contribute to client success and development</li>
</ul>

Who You Are</b></h4>

<b>
The Tech Stuff</b><br>
<br>
<b>Network Knowledge:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Highly available enterprise WAN, campus LAN, datacenter networks, and wireless networks</li>
<li>
Design best practices for layer 2 and layer 3 enterprise networks and wireless mobility</li>
<li>
Data center interconnect architectures including VPLS, EoMPLS, OTV, and long distance VMotion</li>
<li>
Cisco network routing and switching platforms including Nexus, Catalyst, ISR, and ASR</li>
<li>
Arista network products and technologies desirable</li>
<li>
Cisco and Aruba wireless products and technologies</li>
<li>
Cisco and Aruba network access control platforms desirable</li>
<li>
CCIE R&amp;S certification is highly desirable</li>
<li>
Prior deployment experience with SDN platforms such as Cisco ACI or VMware NSX is highly desirable</li>
</ul>
