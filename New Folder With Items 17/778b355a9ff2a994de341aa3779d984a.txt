<h2>Field Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">THE COMPANY
<br><br>
PAX Labs is an award-winning consumer technology brand with a mission to establish cannabis as a force for good. Our products set a high bar for quality and, together with the PAX App, offer unique features such as safety locking, adjustable temperature and draw control. As millions of consumers try cannabis for the first time or return to it after a long hiatus, PAX believes that quality, transparency and predictability are the keys to safe, responsible use by adults.
<br><br>
Headquartered in San Francisco, PAX is backed by leading technology investors including Tiger Global and Tao Invest (Pritzker family fund). We are an exceptional team with hardware and software backgrounds in consumer technology, healthcare, and biotech, and are growing rapidly to deliver on our mission. Our internal culture values diversity, integrity, having an impact, and passion for the larger movement that PAX is a part of.

ROLE AND RESPONSIBILITIES
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Build and maintain relationships with key retail partners and field sales team</li>
<li>Become a territory expert with a pulse on all things happening in the market</li>
<li>Interact daily with Regional Manager, retail staff and consumers</li>
<li>Identify key activations in territory &amp; oversee executions that are engaging, scalable, and relevant consumer PAX experiences through B2B &amp; B2C</li>
<li>Book, manage and execute all aspects of events &amp; activations</li>
<li>Maintain calendar of events and of pre-plan development</li>
<li>Create a territory specific activation playbook</li>
<li>Develop recommendations for scaling program with a team</li>

<li>Manage marketing and promotional materials; event flyers, posters, brochures</li>
<li>Manage swag, pos, executional assets and inventory/storage unit</li>

<li>Be on-call to provide on-site operational support</li>
<li>Facilitate pre &amp; post event analysis and reconciliation</li>

<li>Correspond regularly with in market partners</li>
