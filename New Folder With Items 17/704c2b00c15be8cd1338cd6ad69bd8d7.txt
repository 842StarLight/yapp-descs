<p><b>Summary</b></p>
<p>Primarily responsible for managing EBG's Online Reputation by monitoring and addressing Customer Feedback submitted through our Brand Management and Social Media profiles. The Manager, Online Reputation will work to mediate Escalations and Customer Feedback submitted through the Better Business Bureau, Consumer Affairs, Trust Pilot, Facebook, Twitter and Instagram. In addition to the primary role as outlined above, the Manager, Online Reputation will support and assist our Quality Assurance efforts which includes monitoring the quality and content of inbound and/or outbound telephone calls and provide feedback by way of coaching sessions. This will also include providing continual feedback and data to management for improvement of call handling skills across multiple platforms.</p>
