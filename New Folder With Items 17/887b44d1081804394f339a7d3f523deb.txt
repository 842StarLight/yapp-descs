<h2>Office Assistant - Apartments - Fountain Valley</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>WSH Management is currently seeking an Office Assistant to join our growing team in Fountain Valley!</p>
<p>
The Office Assistant will be responsible for assisting the property manager at a 71 unit senior apartment community. This person will complete various tasks such as answering phones, interacting with residents, taking maintenance service requests and assisting with the planning of residents events. The office assistant will also assist with renewals, update the board with resident package information, file resident documents and vendor documents and invoices. Code and enter bills promptly. Assist residents with work orders and concerns.</p>
<p>
WSH Management has managed senior and multi-family properties since we began operations 19 years ago and are very experienced in the management of both Affordable and Market Rate Apartment Communities. We currently manage over 60 properties in California and have over 100 employees. Come join us!</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Receive all telephone calls and in-person visits.</li>
<li>Listen to resident requests, concerns and comments.</li>
<li>Demonstrate strong oral and written communication skills.</li>
<li>Provide general clerical assistance to property offices.</li>
<li>Assist with maintaining compliance with all applicable Affordable Housing programs.</li>
</ul>
<p>The ideal candidate is polished, engaging and energetic. Candidates need significant customer service experience, with a commitment to apply his/her very best in a high-energy environment that rewards your efforts and dedication!</p>
<p><b>
Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High School Diploma or equivalent required; some college preferred.</li>
<li>A minimum one-year experience in a customer service-related industry or one year apartment leasing experience or a combination of accounting skills/education with office support and customer service experience is preferred.</li>
<li>Must possess strong attention to detail and sales ability.</li>
<li>Demonstrate an ability to support and contribute to community team.</li>
<li>Demonstrate strong oral and written communication skills.</li>
<li>Competence in operation of telephone, business calculator, copy machine, facsimile, personal computer/keyboard, Microsoft Office including Word, Excel and MS Outlook and community software.</li>
<li>Bilingual in English and Vietnamese or Chinese a plus.</li>
<li>Candidate will be required to pass a background and drug screening (A conviction will not necessarily disqualify you from employment with WSH Management).</li>
</ul>

Medical &amp; dental; paid vacation and holidays; 401K plan with employer matching contribution.</p>
