<h2>VP Software Development</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provide leadership and direction to all IT teams: Software Development, Quality Assurance, System Administration, and Security</li>
<li>Partner with Sr VP of Product to ensure timely and complete delivery of new features and enhancements</li>
<li>Collaborate with product management, marketing, sales, accounting, and legal to<br>
ensure products are innovative, deliver value, and comply with appropriate regulations<br>
and policies.</li>
<li>Oversee technology infrastructure and security at multiple data centers and the<br>
corporate office to ensure 100% availability</li>
<li>Manage talent acquisition process for IT department and facilitate performance<br>
management reviews.</li>
<li>Ensure department managers are providing development of training materials along<br>
with training coaching and mentoring for their team members</li>
<li>Maintain awareness and knowledge of latest security threats, technologies, and best<br>
practices through formal continuous education</li>
<li>Stay abreast of the latest technology trends and practices and recommend relevant<br>
solutions to senior management</li>
<li>Strive for continuous improvement of IT department processes</li>
</ul>
<p><b>REQUIRED</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree, in Computer Science or related field, required</li>
<li>Master’s degree in Computer Science, Business Administration, or related field desired</li>
<li>15+ years of software development experience</li>
<li>5+ years leading or managing a team of developers</li>
<li>10+ years’ hands-on experience with Java, PHP, and SQL in a Linux environment</li>
<li>CISSP, CISM, or other related information security certification</li>
</ul>
<p><b>SKILLS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to communicate proactively on identified issues, updating status, and analyzing and reporting on alternative design solutions.</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills with the ability to present complex technical information in a clear and concise manner to a variety of audiences.</li>
<li>Good interpersonal skills with the ability to work well in a team environment</li>
<li>Highly motivated and independent worker who is not shy at tackling tasks they do not immediately understand with the ongoing sense of curiosity and desire to learn something new.</li>
<li>Exhibits relentless quality standards</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to build teams, develop talent, lead by example and have passion and discipline on operational excellence</li>
</ul>
