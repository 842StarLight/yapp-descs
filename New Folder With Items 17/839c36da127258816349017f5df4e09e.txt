<h2>Logistics Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Browse the Aisles</b>- We are looking for a Logistics Coordinator to join our team to optimize Ahold Delhaize brands yield management and route planning/design process to produce a route file for Ahold Delhaize business systems to initiate the subsequent fulfillment and transportation execution processes.</p>
<p><b>
What’s Our Dish - About Us</b></p>
<p>
Announced in May 2018, Peapod Digital Labs is an Ahold Delhaize USA company that will serve as the engine that powers U.S. eCommerce and digital strategies. Peapod Digital Labs will accelerate growth in digital and personalization capabilities for the great local brands of Ahold Delhaize USA and will serve as the innovation lab for the U.S. portfolio of companies, driving digital and eCommerce innovation, technology and experience to meet the changing needs of customers of Ahold Delhaize USA’s local brands, regardless of when, where and how consumers choose to shop. Peapod continues to be a consumer-facing eCommerce brand, and is part of Peapod Digital Labs, focused on growing Peapod sales to consumers.</p>
<p><b>
Recipe for Success - What's “in store” for the role</b></p>
<ul>
<li> Optimize every order through routing software providing route design and ETA/ETD information in a dynamic route planning environment (non-standard routing)</li>
<li> Optimize total truck capacity to utilize greatest percentage of available cube per the multiple styles of Peapod trucks</li>
<li> Maintain full logging on all planning adjustments including road closures, construction impact, customer stop times, etc. to produce high accuracy route plans</li>
<li> Responsible for communicating anomalies to facilities and corporate management regarding order capacity, sell-out rates, sell-rate velocity and a myriad of other items related to the customer time windows/orders being processed on Peapod.com</li>
<li> Directly manage and optimize $4.2MM annual spend of detailed slot level incentives to meet requirements of budgeted orders and commercial forecasted sales</li>
<li> Input manual data from RoadNet including GPS interpretation into Peapod business systems feeding fuller automated analytics</li>
<li> Perform ad hoc analytical tasks as requested by the Sr. Manager of Logistics and Transportation Planning including weather related impacts, truck turn compression, etc.</li>
<li> Capture hourly sell rate and provide historical data to commercial team to feed decisions on Value Day, Flash Sales and other ad hoc revenue generation/order leveling initiatives</li>
</ul>
