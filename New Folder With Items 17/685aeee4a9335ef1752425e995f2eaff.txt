<h2>Member Services Associate Seasonal Position</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>As the nation’s premier athletic club, the Washington Athletic Club is not simply a place to be, but a place to belong. The WAC provides jobs in a variety of departments, including administration, athletics, and hotel, food and beverage operations.</p>
<p>The WAC is seeking individuals who are dedicated to being the best and are willing to embrace the Club’s five core values: Leadership, Excellence, Integrity, Service and Sensitivity.</p>
<p><b>This Position begins ASAP and ends in March.</b></p>
<p><b>General Responsibilities: </b></p>
<p>The Member Services team is dedicated to delivering outstanding customer service for a myriad of requests for all WAC Members and guests. The team provides accounting and marketing support, supplies specific club information and processes membership profile updates.</p>
<p>The seasonal Member Services (SMS) Associate serves a critical support role during the WAC's busiest season.</p>
<p>This position provides a warm and welcoming first point of contact for the 9th floor Executive and Administrative Offices Reception Desk and is responsible for administrative projects and process improvements at the Member Services desk and on the 9th floor, as assigned. The SMS Associate will work to identify opportunities for tasks and process improvements, complete projects and establish procedures in observance and in keeping with the WAC's high standard of service excellence.</p>
<p><b>Specific Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Opens and/or closes the 9th Floor Executive and Administrative Offices Reception Desk at 8 am and 5 pm.</li>
<li>Performs administrative office front desk and reception duties such as answering general telephone inquiries, screening and directing incoming calls, providing initial responses to members or visitors, responding to email inquiries, making appropriate referrals, and maintaining conference room and guest office calendars.</li>
<li>Oversees the copy rooms for cleanliness &amp; ordering, the employee break room for upkeep &amp; supplies and the coffee &amp; refrigerator stations for service readiness.</li>
<li>Supports management of the Business Development Team prospecting materials-including packet preparation, inventory management, and reorders-and creates New Member Immersion Packets.</li>
<li>Manages the Legacy program; organizes monthly mailings and follow up to parents whose children will soon no longer qualify for dependent status. The program provides valuable assistance to Membership retention and New Member acquisition efforts.</li>
<li>Assists the Club Programs Team; helps prepare committee meeting packets, assists with ticket sales and mailings, manages lists and registrations and provides in-person support at WAC Tradition Events during the busy holiday season, including party favors and table decor, gifts and handouts.</li>
<li>Apprises member Services Manager of any issues or concerns with members. Observes and recommends opportunities to improve systems, categories, or standard operating procedure, based on experiences gleaned from administering to a variety of people and requests from this important centrally located desk.</li>
<li>Ensures marketing materials are plentiful and alerts Marketing when inventory is low.</li>
<li>Operates telephone, camera, computer, fax machine, copier and other office equipment.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Position Qualifications: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>This position is responsible for providing first class customer service in a positive, professional manner and by exceeding expectations during interactions with members, team members and guests. This person should br friendly, helpful, knowledgeable and well-versed on WAC offerings and policies and present a professional, polished impression at all times.</li>
<li>This person should possess excellent communications skills. They will work closely with the Member Services Manager and should be comfortable both setting and taking directives for projects, service and operating standards for the expanse of the 9th Floor Team</li>
<li>One year of customer-facing office experience</li>
<li>Strong written and oral communication, problem-solving, computer and people skills.</li>
<li>High School diploma or equivalent.</li>
<li>Familiarity with office equipment and Microsoft Office software.</li>
</ul>
