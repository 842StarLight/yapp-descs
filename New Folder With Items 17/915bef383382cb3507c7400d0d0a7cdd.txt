<h2>Assistant Community Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Looking for a career path in property management? Our <b>Assistant Community Manager</b> position is a great first step to a successful career with one of Denver's most respected apartment companies. As an Assistant Community Manager, you'll put your customer service and property management experience to work in assisting your Community Manager in all community operations with an emphasis on accounts payable. You'll also have direct contact with our customers and will serve a key role in leasing apartments and maintaining and enhancing our customer service platform.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Our <b>Assistant Community Managers </b>are critical members of our team. They?re responsible for providing great customer service ? the cornerstone of the RedPeak experience ? and keeping the community running smoothly. Be ready to be busy! You may need to work on weekends, but no weekend evenings.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>This challenging position includes:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Providing superior customer service</li>
<li>Working closely with the Community Manager in the smooth running and operation of the apartment property</li>
<li>Good written communication for correspondence, resident notices, etc.</li>
<li>Understanding and managing budgets, coordinating vendors, service agreements inventory and supplies</li>
<li>Monitoring leases, rent increases, late payments, collections, evictions, tenant move-ins and move-outs</li>
<li>Inspecting community grounds, vacancies, and common areas to ensure they are in top condition</li>
<li>Keeping tabs on the competition and presenting creative leasing and marketing ideas</li>
<li>Building and managing a motivated and trustworthy team</li>
<li>Leasing responsibilities</li>
<li>Completing leases and lease applications, plus maintaining resident records and files for leases, applications, rent deposits, security deposits, bank deposits, etc.</li>
</ul>
</div>
<p></p>
<br>
<div>
<p><b>Essential Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Assist Community Manager in day-to-day community operations</li>
<li>Maintain a positive living environment for community residents through prompt conflict resolution and consistent follow-up</li>
<li>Maintain a stabilized fiscal operation
<ul>
<li>Monthly posting of rent charges including utility charges if applicable</li>
<li>Rent collection and delinquency management</li>
<li>Managing write-offs and collection accounts</li>
<li>Monthly management and payment of community invoices</li>
<li>Approving and coding invoices for payment</li>
<li>Regular bank deposits of funds received</li>
<li>Coordinate w/ Maintenance Team to assess move-out charges</li>
<li>Responsible for creating and managing SODAs</li>
<li>Review and report any variances from annual budget to Community Manager</li>
<li>Regular auditing of accounting reports to ensure accuracy</li>
<li>Processing and balancing of move-in and move-out ledgers</li>
<li>Coordinate w/ Concierge Team to ensure all non-recurring resident services charges are entered</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Regular auditing of new move-in files to ensure accuracy</li>
<li>Increase/maintain occupancy by aiding in leasing functions
<ul>
<li>Answer leasing calls.</li>
<li>Respond to online/email leads in timely fashion</li>
<li>Tour, follow-up with, and close prospective residents.</li>
<li>Prepare and complete all reservation and lease paperwork with future residents</li>
<li>Walk apartments and prepare move-in gift to ensure readiness for future residents</li>
<li>Prepare keys and change locks for future move-ins</li>
<li>Open/close models, inspect tour path and common areas, and publish marketing ads as required by schedule</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Personal Competencies:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Warm, friendly and service-oriented personality</li>
<li>High-energy – this is a busy and hands-on job!</li>
<li>Flexibility and willingness to “whatever is needed”</li>
<li>Strong written and verbal communications skills</li>
<li>Appearance and demeanor appropriate for upscale properties and clientele</li>
<li>Pride in your work and your community</li>
<li>Organized and detail-oriented</li>
<li>Able to multitask</li>
<li>An aptitude for financials and experience working with a budget</li>
<li>Computer skills including Microsoft Office, email and Internet platforms</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
