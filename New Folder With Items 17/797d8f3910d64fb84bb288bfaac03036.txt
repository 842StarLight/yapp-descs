<div><b>Position Purpose</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Whatever job you're looking for, you can probably find it at Cargill. Our diverse partnerships across a variety of industries mean countless career opportunities for you. It's your choice. It's your future. And you can make it happen at Cargill. This position, Merchant Assistant, is part of the Cargill Ag Supply Chain (CASC) in North America. As part of Cargill's global agricultural supply chain enterprise the grain &amp; oilseed group serves customers with a 1,500-member team at more than 100 grain elevators and soybean crush plants across the U.S.<br>
<br>
The Merchant Assistant role will be part of the West Region’s merchandising team and will be based out of our Olathe, KS office. The purpose of this position is to create a positive customer experience while balancing the needs and challenges of the West Region of CASC North America while acting as a coordinator to link merchandising, origination, transportation and accounting. The person in this role will be responsible for assisting the merchants with sales and purchases including: entering and pricing contracts, creating load orders, aligning supply at our plant with customer purchases and general support functions to the merchants and logistics specialist.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
This position is the key point of contact with our carriers and a vital link to our customers. The Merchant Assistant is also responsible for various projects and continuous process improvements as assigned.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
This role will consist of a split between logistics related activities and merchant related activities.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
Principal Accountabilities</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
30% Sales and Purchase contract management. (Enter, price edit, monitor, confirm and file contracts daily as needed, confirm with customers and communicate with merchants, work closely with accounting team to research/resolve any issues relating to contracts and/or payments)</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
30% Answer and make calls to customers; provide a positive customer experience.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
20% Scheduling/Logistics: Communicate with carriers and customers handling all meal and grain scheduling/shipping responsibilities ensuring flawless execution.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
5% Delivery Authorization: communicate both electronically and through mail the authorization of specific shipments or grain. (In conjunction with our merchants)</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
5% Freight: Enter and track all truck and rail orders daily; inform merchants of changing freight issues and monitor past due freight</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
5% Support the Logistics Coordinators to ensure we maintain carrier database with Motor Transportation Agreements and Insurance Certificates and any other necessary compliance documents.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
5% Customer &amp; carrier validation &amp;/or set-up in Accounting System; keeping records up to date. Responsible for running daily reports and communicating information.</div>
