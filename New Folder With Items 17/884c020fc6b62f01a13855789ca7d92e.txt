<h2>VP of Retail Operations</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Curaleaf Holdings, Inc. (CSE:</b> CURA) (OTCQX: CURLF) is the leading vertically integrated multi-state cannabis operator in the United States. It is a high-growth cannabis company with a national brand known for quality, trust and reliability. The company is positioned in highly populated, limited license states, and currently operates in 12 states with over 40 dispensaries, 12 cultivation sites and 11 processing sites. Curaleaf has the executive expertise and research and development capabilities to provide leading service, selection, and accessibility across the medical and adult-use markets, as well as the CBD category through its Curaleaf Hemp brand.
<br><br>
The VP of Retail Operations is responsible for leading our retail operations team in strategizing, defining and delivering comprehensive new systems and processes that support Curaleaf's retail advancement. Cultivate a customer centric operations culture. Ensures the customer experience is constantly evolving to meet our customer's needs and biggest business opportunities aligned with brand priorities. Direct, define, implement, and ensure effective and efficient systems, procedures and metrics are in place across our organization.
<br><br>
Directs the development and enhancements of enterprise OMS and ERP systems, fraud management solutions, benefits processing, payment processing, logistics and supplier integration. Strategic planning, management and execution of Curaleaf's business and retail partnerships. This role will build and maintain a high performing retail sales operation that meets or exceeds sales goals while maintaining excellent quality and relationships.
<br><br>
Responsibilities
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Drive a sales culture to achieve or surpass sales goals</li>
<li>Provide operational leadership to multiple dispensaries, with strong understanding of the market and patient needs</li>
<li>P&amp;L and budget responsibility</li>
<li>Motivate, coach and manage dispensary team members to create a passionate environment and contribute to patient count growth</li>
<li>Overall responsibility of each dispensaries operational start up and timelines-including driving administration and process implementations</li>
<li>Collaborate with Regional Dispensary Managers on hiring and training all dispensary employees</li>
<li>Ensure that the dispensaries operate legally and conform to all established state and local regulations</li>
<li>Ensure proper store inventory and merchandising</li>
<li>Cross functional collaboration with Marketing, Education and Patient outreach, Physician outreach and Compliance on new products and packaging based on patient and market feedback</li>
<li>Evaluate overall employee performance by gathering, analyzing and interpreting data and metrics</li>
</ul>
Qualifications
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree in business or related field preferred</li>
<li>10 years'+ experience in multi-site management of business or medical service facilities operations with at least 5 years in a leadership position</li>
<li>Strong management and retail operations knowledge, including inventory control, loss prevention, retail POS systems, customer service, budget preparation, employee relations and human resource</li>
<li>Solid business and financial acumen and reporting skills</li>
<li>A sense of urgency, high energy, and hands-on approach</li>
<li>A high-touch, visible leader who can establish instant credibility and inspire the team</li>
<li>Demonstrated track record in driving revenue and increasing profitability</li>
<li>Experience in the medical marijuana industry not required, but a plus</li>
<li>Ability to pass a background check and a valid driver's license is required</li>
</ul>
