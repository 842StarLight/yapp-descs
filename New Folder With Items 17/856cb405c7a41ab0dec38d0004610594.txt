<h2>Account Executive, Group Sales</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Los Angeles Football Club (LAFC) is the newest MLS soccer club serving the greater Los Angeles area. LAFC is dedicated to building a world-class soccer club that represents the diversity of Los Angeles and is committed to delivering an unrivaled experience for fans. LAFC’s ownership group is comprised of local leaders and innovators of industry with intellectual capital, financial prowess, operations expertise and success in the fields of entertainment, sports, technology and media. LAFC is invested in the world’s game and Los Angeles with its 22,000 seat Banc of California Stadium and a top-flight training center on the campus of Cal-State Los Angeles.</div>
<div><b>
</b></div>
<div><b>
POSITION SUMMARY:</b></div>
<div>
The Account Executive, Groups Sales is responsible for creating, prospecting, and selling group packages for LAFC matches. Specific focus will be placed on establishing relationships with Southern California businesses, schools, membership organizations, and clubs that result in annual programming. Account Executives will generate group sales through prospected leads as well as internal campaigns assigned within the LAFC database. This role will oversee specific categories (i.e. Youth Sports, Alumni Groups, etc.) as well as leverage various Fan Experience Packages to surpass sales goals. Qualified candidates will have 2-3 years’ experience in ticket sales with a track record of exceeding goals. Account Executives will have the flexibility to sell a full menu of ticket products including season, suite, and partial ticket packages.<br>
<br>
<b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTION:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for selling group ticket inventory as well as building scalable group programs that result in an annual book of business.</li>
<li>Acquire new business and maximize sales by setting up face to face appointments, completing issued metrics per week, developing and executing sales events, etc.</li>
<li>Develop and execute Fan Experience Packages before and after match days at Banc of California Stadium.</li>
<li>Prospect and execute appointments with group leaders in Southern California.</li>
<li>Service current and new customers via phone calls, emails, and visiting key influencers off-site.</li>
<li>Participate in various team and community events, as well as social and civic activities.</li>
<li>Network at various outside events including business after-hours, clinics, speaker series, etc.</li>
<li>Other tasks and duties as assigned by Supervisor/Management.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>
QUALIFICATIONS:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelors Degree from and accredited College/University required, major in Business or Sports Management preferred.</li>
<li>Minimum of 3 years experience in sales required, with a major Sports team preferred.</li>
<li>Exceptional organizational and time management skills with the ability to multitask and execute in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Must be flexible with the ability to adapt in a fast pace work environment with challenging demands and frequent changes.</li>
<li>Ability to maintain high level of confidentiality at all times.</li>
<li>Must possess a professional demeanor and has the ability to interact with all levels of the organization and outside contacts.</li>
<li>Ability to communicate efficiently and effectively – both verbal and written.</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Office – specifically word, excel, PowerPoint, outlook.</li>
<li>Must have own transportation to get to/from meetings outside the office.</li>
<li>Must be able to work nights, weekends, and holidays, as the event calendar requires.</li>
</ul>
