<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>About the job</b></h2>
<p>
Google Cloud teams work with schools, companies, and government agencies to make them more productive, mobile, and collaborative. On this team, you will drive Google Cloud business relationships by analyzing partner business performance, identifying methods of increasing partner and Google returns, pitching ideas to and identifying opportunities within partners, and coordinating across Google teams to deliver.</p>
<p>
Our Online Partnerships team drives our G Suite and Google Cloud Platform (GCP) partnerships. In this role, you'll combine partner management with business development, and you'll manage business relationships with key partners and prospects to develop new areas of collaboration, growth initiatives and new lines of business. In this role, you'll work to define new partnerships and go-to-market strategies. You'll bring experience with managing complex, cross-functional projects to completion, sales and marketing acumen, and attention to detail. You'll use your analytical skills to effectively influence and communicate with all levels of management. You thrive in a team-oriented environment, working cross-functionally with the Legal, Marketing, Product, and Programs teams to develop new ways to drive Google Cloud growth.</p>
<p>
Google Cloud helps millions of employees and organizations empower their employees, serve their customers, and build what’s next for their business — all with technology built in the cloud. Our products are engineered for security, reliability and scalability, running the full stack from infrastructure to applications to devices and hardware. And our teams are dedicated to helping our customers and developers see the benefits of our technology come to life.</p>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Responsibilities</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Manage a set of portfolio partners through acquisition, agreement consultation, onboarding, and other channel program activities to help channel partners grow their G Suite channel business.</li>
<li>Identify and validate opportunities across different channels and/or industry verticals, prioritize ideas by evaluating product market fit, the competitive landscape, potential business models and path to scale.</li>
<li>Lead discussions with partners and prospects to define partnership models. Manage the agreement cycle by closely coordinating with Legal, Finance and other stakeholders.</li>
<li>Develop joint business plans with partners, plan and forecast growth for key channel and partner accounts. Manage partner relationships to drive business growth for Google Cloud.</li>
<li>Ensure business execution by monitoring activities and impact towards goals, pivot partnership/engagement model as necessary.</li>
</ul>
