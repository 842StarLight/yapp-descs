<h2>Customer Support 1, VSP Optics Group</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>VSP Global is comprised of five complementary businesses that combine high-quality eye care insurance, high-fashion eyewear, customized lenses, ophthalmic technology and retail solutions, with employees in over 23 countries. No matter the role, we’re all focused on a singular mission: to help people see. Learn more by visiting https://vspglobal.com/cms/careers/
</p>
<div>
<div>
<div>General Summary
</div>
<div><p>With general supervision, ensure accurate, timely and efficient customer support. Respond to customer inquiries in a manner that supports the achievement of VSP Global’s goals.
</p></div>
</div>
<div>
<div>Essential Functions
</div>
<div>
<p>Responds to customer inquiries ranging from routine to complex from members, clients, providers, and internal/external business partners; inquiries may range from telephone, in person, or via the internet/e-email<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Contingent on the VSP Global Line of Business (LOB), requires in-depth knowledge in the following areas:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Prescriptions, Plans, Products, Services, and Procedures
</li>
<li>Online purchasing &amp; e-Commerce knowledge
</li>
<li>Internet knowledge
</li>
<li>Payment processing/payment collections
</li>
<li>Eligibility verification
</li>
<li>Order Status, Shipping Status, and Stock Availability
</li>
<li>Accurate completion of necessary documentation, letters, and forms processing
</li>
<li>Quality Assurance testing for company software<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Research and resolve issues/complaints and determine appropriate resolution(s)<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Solves problems and assists with issues that may not fit the “cookie-cutter” solution<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Effectively educates and influences customers and prospective customers on the value and benefit that VSP offers through its products, services, and solutions<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Identify customer trends and communicate to appropriate business partners, leaders, and divisions; suggest necessary corrections, changes, or solutions; appropriately elevate situations that could have organization-wide impacts<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Effectively maintains specific line of business Customer Service standards and support level standards<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Remain current on all changes to Customer Service policy, procedures, and product/services/solutions information to accurately and consistently respond to customers inquiries<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Contingent on the LOB, may require attendance at benefit fairs, open enrollment meetings, and client meetings to provide information about VSP to attendees; available for occasional overnight travel<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Contingent on the LOB, may support Sales Representatives for any inquiry related to their customer orders<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Contingent on the LOB, may be the first point of contact for Sales Representatives, not related to customer orders
</p>
</div>
</div>
<div>
<div>Job Specifications
</div>
<div>
<p>Typically has the following skills or abilities:<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>One to two years of customer service experience in handling moderate to complex customer inquiries in call center, insurance, manufacturing, software, telecommunications, retail or eyewear industries<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Available to work any shift, including weekends, holidays, and/or overnight<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Demonstrated internet knowledge and understanding of basic internet browser settings<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Demonstrated ability to work with multiple software programs, simultaneously<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Working knowledge of MS Office package<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Effective verbal communication skills to prioritize multiple tasks and to be available for incoming calls and inquires<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Effective written communication skills to clearly document customer situations, as well as prepare letters to confirm agreements made<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Excellent interpersonal and rapport building skills<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Ability to ask appropriate and relevant questions to identify customer needs<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Proven problem solving, negotiations, and decision-making skills<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Ability to use appropriate discretion and judgment in applying customer/call handling guidelines<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Ability to listen, process transactions and interact with customers simultaneously in a fast-paced environment<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>If this position acts as a third-party administrator (TPA) for processing payments, then candidates will be subject to a credit check
</p>
</div>
