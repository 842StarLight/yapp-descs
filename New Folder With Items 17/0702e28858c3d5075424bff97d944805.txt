<div>
<b>Objective:</b><b>
</b>
</div>
<div>Provide efficient customer support and repair of Fujitsu Imaging Products for warranty, contract, and Time and Material services along with preventative maintenance and Installation<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<div>Essential functions/Duties:</div>
<ul>
<li><div>Perform repair, ScanCare preventative maintenance, user training for scanners, and installation of drivers and software while meeting Service Level Achievement requirements
</div></li>
<li><div>Travel constantly to assigned clients within primary territory; this position does require travel to other territories when primary territories are low in call volume
</div></li>
<li><div>Be fully trained on the repair and service of all Fujitsu Scanner and Competitive Scanner Products for which on-site service is provided</div></li>
<li><div>
Physical requirement to support all variation of weight up to 50 pounds individually lifting, pulling, and pushing as needed. Required to stand long periods of time, crawling, squatting, and constant movement.</div></li>
<li><div>
Maintain 100% utilization each work week as measured by the Weekly Service Level Achievement Report published by Dispatch</div></li>
<li><div>
Communicate in an effective and professional manner with customers, colleagues and management at all times
</div></li>
<li><div>Ensure that the daily call log is reviewed weekly and discrepancies between the call log and Service Book are reported to Field Service Management Ensure all calls are completed within the SLA period prior to contract expiration
</div></li>
<li><div>Be sure no calls are missed or remain unclosed and aging without service being provided</div></li>
<li><div>
All calls over 5 days should have dispatcher notes, and no dispatcher note should exceed 5 days without an updated note</div></li>
<li><div>
Follow the “Deferred Process” for ScanCare PM’s, Cleaning Calls and Single Event’s that cannot be scheduled during the SLA period and note the name of the person requesting that the call be deferred in the “Deferred Note”</div></li>
<li><div>
Follow the “Watch List Process” for ScanCare PM’s, Cleaning Calls and Single Event’s in cases where the customer cannot be contacted for scheduling
</div></li>
<li><div>Calls should not be allowed to age more than 20 Business Days without completing, being deferred by the customer or moving to the watch list if customer contact cannot be made after a minimum of 2 calls along with voice messages (where possible) and an E-mail (where possible)</div></li>
<li><div>
Return spare parts as noted in Service Book Return, BPart or GPart at least once per week and Email Field Service Management with call number, part number, tracking number and date of shipment on the date returned</div></li>
<li><div>
Take full responsibility for all parts shipped to your customers and ensure they are returned in a timely manner</div></li>
<li><div>
Actively participate in resolving any aging spare parts return issues and ensure that all parts have been returned within 20 days of the service call</div></li>
<li><div>
Ensure GPS data is being updated for all stops defined by FS Management including all scanners being serviced daily with the appropriate call numbers into the GPS interface</div></li>
<li><div>
Attempt to improve utilization by setting up call events which are in close proximity to break-fix and expedited PM’s being done so that travel can be minimized and time is utilized efficiently
</div></li>
<li><div>Complete accurate data input and closure of all service calls using Service Book at the time calls are completed to ensure accurate parts replenishment and customer billing
</div></li>
<li><div>Make sure all visits are itemized on your debrief resolution notes separating each visit with dates, repair times, travel times, miles, parts attempted or used, additional parts ordered, troubleshooting steps in detail, and detailing what services were performed per visit.</div></li>
<li><div>
Know inventory level and replenishment status for your trunk kits at all times</div></li>
<li><div>
Maintain an accurate trunk kit inventory</div></li>
<li><div>
Maintain all company assets (i.e; company van, tools, documentation, tablet computer, cell phone, etc.) in your possession
</div></li>
<li><div>
Report any infraction that will affect your DMV record to your manager (company vehicle or personal vehicle) as the primary function of the job is driving from start of shift to the end of shift.</div></li>
<li><div>
Provide accurate detail in a timely manner for all issues with Service that causes the Customer to be dissatisfied with Fujitsu Scanners or service practices</div></li>
<li><div>
Ability to work and handle customer situations with assistance from field service management, as required</div></li>
<li><div>
Complete all company required training before the date noted for required completion</div></li>
<li>
<div>
Confirm and document customer email address in closing debriefs</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
</div>
</li>
</ul>
