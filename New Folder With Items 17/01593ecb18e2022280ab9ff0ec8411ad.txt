<p><b>Who We Need: </b></p>
<p>We are currently looking for energetic, hardworking, friendly, outgoing team members for the position of Associate Loan Originator. This position will provide candidates with industry knowledge and training necessary to become a successful Mortgage Loan Originator. The ideal candidate is someone looking to begin a career in a fast-paced industry with outstanding growth and income potential.</p>
<p><b>What You Will Do: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Call and establish rapport with potential clients to validate information provided by consumer.</li>
<li>Overcome client’s objections.</li>
<li>Transfer live calls to our Licensed Loan Originators.</li>
<li>Provide support to our Loan Originators as needed.</li>
<li>Maintain a high volume of outgoing phone calls on a daily basis.</li>
<li>Prepare and study for the National Uniform State Test (UST) and states licensing exams.</li>
<li>Upon successful passage of the Associate Loan Originator program, National and State Exams, and licensing requirements are successfully met, you will move to a Mortgage Loan Originator.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What You Need: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Excellent telephone, communication (oral and written), interpersonal and organizational skills.</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal skills with demonstrated customer service skills.</li>
<li>Excellent computer skills; Microsoft Office, Excel and Google Apps experience.</li>
<li>Ability to navigate between multiple computer programs, dual monitors, while speaking with clients.</li>
<li>Ability to work well with a team environment and take constructive criticism, coaching and feedback on a consistent basis.</li>
<li>Highly detailed and organized with the ability to multi-task and work in a very fast paced environment.</li>
<li>Ability to forage and maintain excellent working relationships with all members of the Cardinal Financial Team.</li>
<li>Ability to manage large volume of tasks while providing excellent customer service.</li>
<li>Ability to exercise discretion on sensitive or confidential matters.</li>
<li>Expected to obtain Mortgage Originator License within 120 days of hire.</li>
</ul>
