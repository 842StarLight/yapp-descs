<div><b>
POSITION SUMMARY</b></div>
<p>
Under general supervision, the Insurance Support Specialist III, is an advocate for the riders, dealers and Harley-Davidson Insurance. The agent works closely with Harley-Davidson dealers to promote, educate and assist with Harley-Davidson Insurance &amp; Protection Products. The Insurance Support Specialist III sells the Harley-Davidson Extended Service Plan (ESP) to consumers as appropriate. The agent receives and responds to complex inquiries from consumers via telephone using software, product knowledge, tracking using tools such as Excel and agency management systems to complete transactions. This position may be a path to a leadership role and requires leadership skills. The Insurance Support Specialist III will assist with any insurance protection products sold by Harley-Davidson Insurance when required. The Insurance Support Specialist III must ensure compliance with state requirements with all interactions.</p>
<p><b>
PRIMARY RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Makes outbound calls to Harley-Davidson dealers regularly promoting Harley-Davidson Financial Products.</li>
<li>
Makes outbound calls and answers inbound calls to / from Harley-Davidson dealers relative to Harley-Davidson ESP balances and underpayment. This activity can include support of other rider Protection Products and select commercial insurance products.</li>
<li>
Track and document all dealer interactions in agency software.</li>
<li>
Use follow-up techniques for repeat calls to dealers to maintain dealer relations and keep apprised of dealership changes.</li>
<li>
Obtains information from prospective and/or current customers to adequately meet their needs</li>
<li>
Identifies needs, verifies and validates information using appropriate review, probing techniques and reasoning skills. Identifies/clarifies potential discrepancies in information to gain accurate understanding of customer needs.</li>
<li>
Accurately records information in agency management system or through HDIS required documentation process</li>
<li>
Document quotes, sales requests and activities by updating appropriate records in HDFS software or HDIS systems</li>
<li>
Become knowledgeable and assist with additional Harley-Davidson Insurance products when needed</li>
<li>
Uses effective time management to ensure customer service and dealer experience is satisfactory and meets expectations</li>
<li>
Ability to obtain a Property and Casualty Insurance license, if needed.</li>
