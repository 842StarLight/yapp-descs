<h2>Digital Marketing Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Works closely with Marketing Team to create engaging digital content for a company's digital presence, including website, social media and email marketing. Involve in developing digital touch points to drive more website traffic and nurture prospects throughout the customer journey.</p>
<p><b>Job Duties</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Analyze digital data to draw key recommendations around website optimization</li>
<li>Proposes new ideas and concepts for social media content and manages company social media channels, including Facebook, LinkedIn, Twitter, and other relevant platforms</li>
<li>Engages in social media presence creation on new and emerging social media platforms Maintain digital dashboard of several different accounts</li>
<li>Uses timelines and scheduled content to create a consistent stream of new content for audience interaction while analyzing, managing, and altering schedules where necessary to optimize visits</li>
<li>Coordinate with sales team to create marketing campaigns</li>
<li>Works with marketing team members to coordinate marketing campaigns</li>
<li>Assist the team create content that promotes audience interaction, increases audience presence on company sites, and encourages audience participation</li>
<li>Monitor CRM system to make sure all the data is update and assist in creating a nurturing process for inbound leads</li>
<li>Monitor key online marketing metrics to track success</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Skills and Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Marketing or Journalism degree preferred</li>
<li>Digital design experience a plus</li>
<li>Strong writing skills and copy creation skills</li>
<li>Knowledge of video and picture editing software and Microsoft office</li>
<li>Must be able to juggle multiple projects at the same time</li>
<li>Incredible attention to detail</li>
<li>Full understanding of all social media platforms especially LinkedIn</li>
<li>Problem solving skills, Teamwork, Self-Motivation</li>
<li>Knowledge of content management systems and CRM system</li>
</ul>
